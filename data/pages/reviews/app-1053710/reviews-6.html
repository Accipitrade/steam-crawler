\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been meaning to get this game for a long time now. It was on Epic for the first year it came out, so I had to wait a little. Considering that it's now on sale (a little over € 10), I thought this was a good time to try it out.

Tl;dr: this isn't a bad game. It's just really, really short and not worth the price.

The art, environment and graphics are absolutely beautiful. In that way it's hard to not compare this game to the Long Dark (yes, there's auroras!), but it definitely manages to catch the same sorta vibe. Also, Ashly Burch is an amazing voice actress. At times I was worried that it was gonna sound awkward or forced, but it never did. I absolutely love her work. The writing isn't too bad - although there isn't really a "story" per se. It's more of a continuous self reflection of a woman trying to see what she's made of. And the interaction with the dogs is lovely!

So why a bad review? The main problem with this game is that while it's story driven, the story itself is very short. I managed to complete the entire thing in less than 2 hours - and I definitely wasn't rushing through it. The whole story builds up to 'living in the wild' with your dogs, figuring out how to do and growing as a person. It starts with you making your way to a house in the middle of nowhere. After reaching your destination, the suddenly game ends. That's the whole thing. Congrats, you've won the game. Imagine my surprise. There's a few things you can do after completing the game (which they've added later - I assume because people had the same criticism I have), but it's really just not that interesting because the story is already over.

It's a shame because it definitely has potential, but it looks like the studio is working on a new game already and not actually updating this one anymore, besides bugfixing. I guess this is what to expect from studios with Epic deals.

This is not worth € 10 and definitely not worth the full € 21. Though it's not really a "half-assed" game, it's just a very small game. I'd recommend it if you could get it for like € 5, otherwise it's not worth your money.\t\t\t\t\t

One more small thing, it would be great to see the dogs interact more, even if it was just them playing at home/camp. Minus a few moments they all feel pretty separate from each other, it would be great to get a better feel for them as a team.
