\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhilst I did enjoy having adorable dogs with me - and listening to the main character talking here and there, the exploration part of this game is what let me down.

Still, what I did enjoy was: the ambience, being able to choose my own team of dogs (there's quite a number to pick from), the dialogue spoken by the main character (although she can at times talk a lot) and the art style of the game overall.

What I disliked however is: that you can't make any movements yourself - which I would have enjoyed rather than yelling "Mush on", "haw" and "gee" constantly, it is quite difficult to ration out everything you have resource wise (sometimes after dying you will get more resources added - but not necessarily the ones that you feel you really need to make a run), the map isn't the easiest to read at all and only seems to update once you've reached a marker.

Still it is a fairly good game, but I just wish there had been more movement allowed as it felt so restrictive. So those of you who are thinking it might be like The Long Dark, it might not be what you're expecting - still worth a shot though even though it wasn't my cup of tea.\t\t\t\t\t

But the game softlocked itself when I reached the end. It showed me the cutscene - without the voicelines of course - but no matter which button I pressed, the textbox did not close. Game didn't save, didn't recognize I reached the cabin, nothing. Had to force quit it and lose my progress.

So, right now, in the state it's in now.. I can't honestly recommend it, which is a shame.\t\t\t\t\t

I'm a huge sucker for atmospheric games, and this was perfect for me! For those who like a lot of action, or a fast paced game, perhaps this is not the game for you. But as someone who loves story, and loves dogs, this game is ideal!
The only drawback to the game is the repetitiveness of the runs, but for me it is forgivable. I also love how the different dogs you choose will impact how your runs go!
Probably my favorite part of the game besides the beauty is; the encounters! the way you approach each encounter, the way each one affects your trip! its so great! i look forward to seeing how this game progresses! I recommend you give it a try!\t\t\t\t\t
