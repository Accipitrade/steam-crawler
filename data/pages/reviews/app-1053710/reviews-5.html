\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has lots to recommend it - beautiful visuals and soundtrack, and an interesting and unusual premise - you are a sled driver (musher?) traversing a frozen wilderness with a pack of sled dogs. You do get to choose the dogs. Yay for dogs!

The whole game plays like a visual novel. There are choices but also many unskippable cut scenes. The story feels a bit sparse. The game reminds me of 'in the long dark' but there is much less content.

You drive your dogs through the wilderness (on a path, occasionally choosing left or right) and events happen, both good and bad. You decide what to do. You must also look for various things to keep you and your dogs in good condition to continue.

The game is good, but repetitive. I've played for about 1 hour and 30 mins (2 partial run throughs) and I'm bored. I would say that the game is better suited to people who enjoy repetitively playing through the same game (with some different procedural events) in order to win. I feel there could be more content - the price tag even on sale is too high imo. I doubt I will play this again unless there are more updates.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy this game. I know it's going to be one I 100% complete. The art style, the dogs, the organization needed, it's all very enjoyable. I would not pay the full price for this game- I waited for it to go on sale, as it's a shorter game that's fairly repetitive. It takes a good few playthroughs to see everything it has to offer, which allowed me to sink a good 7 hours into this game to get all of the tools, but still not 100% the game.

Although I ran into some issues with loading the game, like some others have, the support was very friendly in troubleshooting with me over 10 days. Zak, the person who I was conversing with over email, helped me find the files I needed to delete to reset my saves and make the game playable again, while staying patient with me having absolutely no idea where to start, as well as me having issues finding the right files to send so that the rest of the team could try and find out what's wrong.

Overall, my very non-concise review wraps up into: it's a very pretty and amusing game to sink some hours into. It runs too expensive without a sale, though, so wait for one. Although it still has some issues, the support team is very friendly. if you're into any of the tags listed in the story page, it's definitely a nice game to check out, and I was overall happy with my purchase even with some issues.\t\t\t\t\t
