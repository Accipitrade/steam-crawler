\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly I'm bad at words so perhaps I won't be able to capture how good this game is, but honestly its a real treat!! :'D
I'm a sucker for any games with dogs in it, and especially about the sport of sledding, so when my brother sent it to me I had to try it.

The dogs all have unique personalities, the journey has beautiful sights to see with the graphic style, and overall I adored it personally!

Pros:
- Dogs. Do you need more
- The more attempts you take on the the runs, the better your next chances are. I appreciated the fact that any gear you found carried over! It feels rewarding being able to make another attempt and actually feel like you know what you're doing
- Back on the dogs; they do all have their own personalities and perks to the team, learning about them is really sweet actually
- The graphics and music are gorgeous and I love the stylistic choices the team made!

Cons:
- As stated in another review; the runs can get a bit repetitive, and the monologue's do replay sometimes. Luckily mine actually muted so I didn't have to hear it again, not that I minded though, but keep this in mind if you're easily annoyed by repeated things.
- You won't always get chances to bond with your dogs. In my run I picked up Stilton, Barkley, Fin, and Noodle. While I had chances to learn more about Barkley and Fin.I actually bonded with Fin enough to pet her hehe. I didn't get as many chances nor prompts for Stilton and Noodle. Perhaps they come later on, but it seemed a bit strange to me
- Another note about bonding with the dogs; this does not carry over on new runs, which is understandable, but can be a bit annoying- especially if you become dependent on some of the things they can do once bonded
