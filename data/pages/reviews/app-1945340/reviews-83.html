\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't really understand why people are calling this the worst dlc. This is honestly the most fun I've had in destiny in a while. Here are my thoughts.

Pros:

Campaign was very fun to play on legendary.

Strand introduces new and interesting mechanics that are fun to build craft around.

Probably some of the best post-campaign exotics I've ever gotten my hands on. Some for the new strand subclass, and others to further enhance or familiar subclasses.

The new tormentor enemies are awesome! They're fun to fight and are even more satisfying to take down. They genuinely feel like a whole new enemy type in the game due to their badass appearance and mechanics , unlike the hive guardians in witch queen which felt more like re-skins with a new attack.

Cons:

Story was defiantly lacking and we learned nothing new outside of what was already revealed during the marketing. As a pint of comparison, witch queen dropped a lot of new info on us during the campaign which was really satisfying for all the build up prior. Lightfall just didn't have that sadly. The story wasn't horrible, just kinda meh...

The cloud striders weren't very interesting in my opinion and it was hard for me to connect with these characters during the campaign. Nimbus is kinda cringe not gonna lie.

The new shadow legion cabal are basically just re-skins of the cabal we already knew. They just have new backpacks that explode with shields.

I personally found the final boss very aggravating to fight solo. I understand it's supposed to be challenging, but it seemed like all it took was the smallest mistake to send 20 min of progress down the drain, and there's a point where it's so touchy it's not fun anymore. I recommend bringing a fireteam for this fight.

I think the dlc was overpriced for what you get. Almost a full AAA game price for a dlc. However I play the snot out of this game so I'll defiantly get my moneys worth.

Conclusion:

Did this dlc have a lot of pitfalls? Yes. Was it perhaps overpriced for what you get? Yes. However, I don't believe this dlc deserves the rating it's been given. Destiny continues to be one of the most fun games I've ever played and this dlc brought even more features to play with! If you're a regular D2 player, I recommend this dlc. If you're more casual and only pick up the game on occasion, you might want to wait until it goes on sale. Witch Queen still takes first place as best dlc imo, but I still had lot's of fun with this one!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you come in to lightfall expecting Witch Queen level of story and content, it's a huge letdown. It's obvious that Bungie cut out the most important part of the story to be able to put it in The Final Shape. In terms of campaign story, it's as bad as D1 vanilla campaign, you could even argue that it's worse because D1 Vanilla had this sense of mystery and exploration which Lightfall lacks. To build up that level of hype for Lightfall and ship the game in this state is a joke. We are around 9 years into Destiny and still Bungie fails to deliver on the story. I'm actually really tired of the teasing that something big is about to happen and then nothing happens and the cycle keeps repeating.

In terms of gameplay, it's Destiny, so expect fun mechanics and gunplay. But Bungie managed to drop the ball even in this. Weapons are mostly old weapons with new skins, 1 new strike (it's amazing that expansions used to have 3-4 strikes and we are paying more than before).

Bungie needs to start respecting it's playerbase. The game is monetized to oblivion with seasons and dungeon passes (we used to get these as part of the expansion and Bungie is not an independent studio anymore). They really need to cut down on the greed and start shipping the game that the loyal fanbase deserves. As it stands, Bungie has burned through most of the goodwill they generated these years.
If you are not a dedicated D2 player, or you are considering returning or want to start, do yourself a favor and just skip. For us who invested in this game from the beginning, I hope Bungie just wakes up and show us some respect.\t\t\t\t\t
