\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe mass review bombing is a HUGE overreaction by the community. This expansion is not bad - in fact it isn't even close to being bad.

Is the Lightfall campaign better than the Witch Queen Campaign? No. Is it still the second best campaign we've ever had? Yes lmao. The return of the Witch Queen format (and legendary campaign) and the gameplay itself is brilliant. Love the tuned-up enemy density for most sections, especially in the final mission when you have the huge battle alongside Caiatl. I do think the final fight with Calus felt like an oppressive spike in difficulty, however, especially when doing the legend campaign solo. I think the campaign just needed to be a little bit longer. That being said, these are relatively minor gripes. The campaign is still like, a 7/10 at worst.

The story does fall flat in places but I feel optimistic for the year ahead because I feel splitting Lightfall + Final Shape into two expansions was to give Bungie a year to work with and 4 more seasons to build up and tie up loose ends.

Strand feels really fun, needs a little bit of tuning on the grapple cooldown post-campaign, but otherwise is fine. Already finding some strong builds, especially Strand Titan. Not everything needs to be meta anyway - if Strand was the strongest subclass in the game people would just whinge about being forced to use the new thing.

Neomuna is awesome, big step up from the Throne World which was visually impressive but I rarely visited it post-campaign because of how actually empty it felt. In Neomuna there's frequent mini-events, good public events and the new activity (Terminal Overload) is a wonderful balance of a shorter Altars of Sorrow and a more diverse and interesting Nightmare Containment.

Anyone complaining about Nimbus' dialogue not fitting in with the mood of Destiny clearly hasn't played the game while Cayde-6 was around lol. If Nimbus was just called Cayde-7, silly as it would be, the community response to the character would be way tamer.


I think Lightfall had clear flaws, but the overwhelmingly negative response to it has been ridiculously overblown. It's still a very solid piece of content that I enjoyed and will continue to enjoy playing. Looking forward to the new raid!\t\t\t\t\t
