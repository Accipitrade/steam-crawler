\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll get the elephant in the room out the way, Lightfalls narrative was rough. The campaign missions were great and the difficulty kept us on our toes but the story ends so abruptly and you're left saying "what now?". Leaning off of The Witch Queen and all its narrative success, this feels like a real blow to the community, BUT we still have the raid and the season to aid our critiques.

Other than that, the DLC in it's first week has been great fun. Strand, the new darkness subclass is a blast to play, with its grapple to throw you into enemies or get you out of sticky situations. The build crafting for this subclass seems like really deep and the more we explore, the better it can become.

Neomuna is beautiful and its challenge is a great addition in my opinion, although, I do wish we had more enemies roaming around. Other than that, I am really enjoying my time in this city and I am currently on the hunt for Nimbus dolls...

With the rest of the year to come, I remain hopeful and optimistic for the future of Lightfall. Bungie have pulled 180s in the past, look at the likes of CoO and Shadowkeep. The raid is upon us and new narrative avenues can open up for us. While I don't expect the community to forgive Bungie for this, I do hope they can bring us what we all want.

Overall, Lightfall gets a 7.5/10. While the narrative fumbles, the quality of life changes and the new additions in the game are all welcome.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo i recommend Lightfalll?
Depends on what you want out of the game. The huge amount of backlash its getting is not because its "bad" but more so because its disappointing, especially compared to the quality of the Witchqueen.

The expansion is "fine", still better than beyond light and shadowkeep. But it should have been more than fine,. It should have been at the same level of the previous expansion, but now it feels like filler so we have something to do until Final Shape. The campaign is still really fun but storywise it will leave you confused and frustrated at the end because nothing is explained and nothing of consequence really happened. Although this could turn around with a surprise story drop after the raid which comes out in a couple of days.

The new systems, subclass and tweaks are great. The new patrol space is gorgeous (although not as deep and interesting as Savathun's throneworld), there is new guns, new exotics. Plenty of reasons to get the expansion if you just want more Destiny.

So, if you simply want "more" Destiny, just get light fall and have a good time. Just know that the campaign and story are very mid compared to what we got last year. Still a recommend for avid destiny players, but if you value a solid campaign story more over the more grindy mmo elements of the game, skip for now until you can get it at a discount, because the "story" of the campaign aint worth its full price if thats all you care about.\t\t\t\t\t
