\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLightfall is a very mixed bag. There are some highlights, but overall I'd have to say I don't recommend it, which really saddens me to say as someone whose been playing consistently since Season of Arrivals (Season 11). I don't think I've ever been as disappointed in the game as I am at the moment. It's not any one thing but the cumulative effect of a bunch of areas where Bungie let the player base down:

  • The story on it's surface is just OK. It's kind of confusing and leaves a lot of unanswered questions. But it's made WAY worse by Nimbus' character being insufferably cheesy and just killing the tone of an otherwise serious and dramatic situation
  • Strand is fun as a novelty but I can't see using it in anything but the easiest content. It's also very tricky to master and the campaign forces you to be an absolute Strand god to progress which makes the campaign needlessly frustrating in places. I hope you like sailing off the map to your death.
  • The new armor mod system is maybe the biggest let down of all. It strips out a bunch of mods that were previously available and the ones that are left are now armor-slot-restricted. Bungie hyped the new system as more freedom for buildcrafting, and I personally was really excited for it. Now instead of mods being tied to energy types (Solar, Arc, ect) they're all tied to armor slots, which somehow feels even MORE restrictive, not less.
  • Guardian Ranks are completely meaningless
  • Neomuna, the new location, as a lot of others have said, is underwhelming
  • Overall, there's just less overall, and more of what there is is recycled, and it just doesn't feel worth the asking price

That said there are some bright spots:

  • Outside of Nimbus, the voice acting and character cutscenes and renders are excellent
  • The new enemies (Shadow Legion) are really cool and have some fun new mechanics.
  • The bump up in difficulty was a good move and definitely feels more balanced especially in lower-tier content

My recommendation is to skip Lightfall and just play Season 20. The new seasonal story is EXCELLENT. You'll still be stuck with the new armor system - which sucks - but the story, activity, and most of the weapons are fun and certainly less disappointing than Lightfall.\t\t\t\t\t
