\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will say this one thing before I rip into this dlc. The gameplay during the campaign was pretty solid. Don't have many complaints on that part. Strand is a welcome addition to the game. Its very fun to use. Neomuna is also fun to roam in. Its a great addition the the game and the environments were gorgeous to look at. Also they of course nailed it with the music. Now for the negative.

WHAT A WASTE. This was the time for us to finally get answers. To the witnesses plan, the final shape, and what on gods green earth is the veil. NONE of these where answered. Nothing but more questions. We wasted WAYYYYY too much time on Strand, a stupid vex incursion side plot, and introducing new and unlikable characters. Seriously do we not have enough already? I have no clue what happened at the end. None of the main characters seemed upset about what happened and nothing changed in the system other then there is now a purple triangle next to the Traveler. It seems like Bungie is too afraid to have lasting consequences in this game. The silver surfers where a disappointment. Didn't really care for either of them. Didn't get enough time with the witness and Callus. The end fight was a massive let down. I've been so invested in the story since the Red War. I was super excited to see what was in store. Who was gonna live or die, but now i feel nothing but disappointment. I cant believe this is what we get after the Witch Queen. One of my favorite dlcs next to Forsaken. Bungie, this was your time to go BIG and show people what y'all are capable of, but no. You wasted it. Nice work.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's horrible. Such a letdown after the amazing Witch Queen expansion. Other reviewers probably have highlighted most of the shortcomings, but in short: the story structure is awful, Strand is underwhelming, new patrol zone does not deliver on the promise of being THE Strand traversal experience (in fact, it's rather dull and flat), the story tone is all over the place with you going from "this may be the end of us all" to ill placed humor and tone deaf wisecracking. The new character personality I found to be unlikable (some have likened it to 80s surfer culture, which feels way out of place in the Lightfall), the Neomuna seems like a tone deaf dystopia that writers somehow mistook for a utopia (most of population is frozen in cryosleep and exists in their analogue of the Matrix). Strand subclass progression is timegated AND you have to rebuy all of the fragments on each character, so enjoy that grind. It feels weak in the campaign, where it gets a significant "story" buff, feels even weaker once that is gone. The story ends abruptly without any explanation of the implications, the why, the how, nothing. Not in the likable "ala dark souls" way, where you can piece some meaning together. It's like a B-movie version of the Infinity war, but nobody ever explains what the infinity gauntlet is, and Thanos doesn't do anything of consequence throughout the story, and you only get to know his name anyway. It's that bad.

New exotic guns are underwhelming, lots of reused old guns with a fresh-ish coat of paint, this really feels like a season or 2 squeezed together and release all at once rather than a full-sized expansion. Definitely not worth the $50 they are asking for it.\t\t\t\t\t
