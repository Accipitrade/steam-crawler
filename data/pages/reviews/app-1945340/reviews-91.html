\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere do I start with this? Well lets first look at the upside of this dlc (what was considered "good") This is purely my own opinion. You are warned this contains spoilers.

1. We got the promised loadouts, it works as intended. It's incredibly convenient and I as well as my clanmates use it regularly.

2. Neomuna, at a base look its a very beautiful city. It had a lot of time and effort put into it for it to look the way it is. The futuristic neon vibe I get from it everytime I load into Neomuna is very nice to see.

I'm sorry I am genuinely trying to find what else was good that came from this dlc and I cannot find it at this point in time. Now let's look at the downside of this dlc. Still my own opinion.

1. The length of the campaign, for $60 (the base dlc, not the dlc + annual pass) I expect better coming from the creators of the Witch Queen campaign. Not very cowabunga of you bungie. It felt like I went out to mow the lawn and I did one pass and left the rest of the lawn. It was far too short for what was suppose to be something that was humanities and earth's greatest threat. The witness shows up first mission in the cutscene and like 5 missions later he glides into the traveler and dissapears. Seriously? What in the hell was that? No direct dialogue, No explanation. He plays Fruit Ninja with like 4 ships and stares at the traveler like a 5 year old stares at the TV watching Dora. I don't get it.

2. Nimbus (AKA Nimrod), "The uglier they are the harder they fall! Up High!" -_- (That is all)

3. Strand, ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ I have absolutely no words for this green eggs and ham looking pile of stinky green swamp ♥♥♥♥ on my screen. You give and you take, you give and you take, you give and you take. Can you make up your mind on the give and take for the subclass? I felt like I was playing ping pong with this damn subclass and I couldn't even hit the ball back before I got it snatched mid-air from me. I feel like this could have been better set up for us instead of the 1/3 of a mission we got to use it for for the whole campaign.

4. The class action lawsuit level of abuse we obtained from Osiris, why the hell do I have a old man that has Dementia yelling at me every 5 minutes on the intercom. He tells us off like we are a 16 year old teenager that took money from grandma. Osiris has turned into a straight up ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ for no reason. Don't even get me started on his relationship that is held together with painters tape with Saint-14. Saint-14 had better have signed a prenup when they got married.

5. Calus, first of all they did my diaper baby dirty. The witness gave him powers, helped him out and got him out of the hospital bed he was in. THEN gives him a Super Soaker gun that shoots water slugs and said "Go have fun little Timmy!" The boss fight was lack luster and his phase two was piss poor in terms of difficulty and challenging. He was extremely weak and he smacks you with the equivalent of a wet, sloppy, day-old noodle.

6. WHAT THE ♥♥♥♥ IS THE VEIL

I could have added more but I would have been beating dead Calus with my Khvostov. For the TLDR folks, This dlc is not worth the money you want to/ already have paid for it. I highly recommend waiting for it to go on sale. From the same exact people (to my knowledge unless they replaced their whole story writing team from underneath our noses) that made the gem we call Witch Queen. I'm not sure how to process this rest of my feelings for this campaign. I've been with D2 since the beginning and its had its ups and downs but I hold out hopes that the new raid that comes out tomorrow will attempt to distract us long enough to wash away the trauma this dlc has caused for us all.

P.S. in the wise words of my friends and the community (they finally caught on) GREEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEEN\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere is my Review for Lightfall. I wish it was more hopeful, truly I do.
I was SO EXCITED about Lightfall, It was talked about being tantamount to a Destiny 3, and I was ALL IN. Truly the last time I was this excited about a release, was Halo 3 in 2007. I even bought the $100 edition that included the extra Lightfall Neomunian style goodies. I got into the game and settled in for a good story, challenging new enemies, and some classic looter-shooter fun. Most of all I was excited for a conclusion to the story and hopefully some seasonal content or cliffhangers pointing at the exciting "whats next".
What I GOT, was disappointment. I got the same enemies with the same combat logic just re-skinned, with exactly one new enemy that is not "scary" it is not "tough" it is just flat out annoying. The vanguard leaders are just sitting sulking in their tower, none of the characters are interested in actually manning up and getting at it.
The last time I was this disappointed was when Mass Effect Andromeda turned out to be a flop, like I feel like the release of Cyberpunk 2077 was more fleshed out than this.
Gacha games have better storylines than this. I can't even get my money back now. I will be uninstalling immediately.
I know it makes me sound pathetic, But I was actually pretty sad and discouraged, I was hoping for a capstone of an epic story, but I feel less like a player and a "Guardian of Light" and more like a cow in a milking rack.
I wanted so bad for Lightfall to be amazing, but I think with this one I'm just done. I can't take toxic cycle of bad games anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
