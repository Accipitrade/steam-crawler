\r\n\t\t\t\t\textremely bad story, I wanted to write an actual full review why it's bad, but when I was writing it I was just constantly getting stuck trying to make sense of this whole thing.
buy it for strand and the loot, not for the story.

the way the killed off Calus, the last true emperor, the shadow emperor, the golden emperor, disciple, herald of second collapse is very disappointing.
5 years he's been around in destiny, only to be killed off so underwhelmingly. especially after he's been empowered with darkness? REALLY bungie? so you are telling me that his automatons were raid bosses but himself, especially when he's been empowered with darkness, is not? it's plain stupid. In my opinion he should've been given the oryx treatment: he should've been beaten in a final mission, but still alive, so to finish him off we'd need to do so in a raid. that, I think would've been a worthy send off for calus. 5 years of character building, wasted. this upsets me a lot since I loved Calus' character, I am a huge cabal fan and seeing this expansion being themed about them made me very happy. Though when I actually played it, I was extremely disappointed.
Can't help but mention the fact that, among the Shadow Legion there are Red Legion soldiers, while having a couple incendiors or collosi of the red legion be fighting alongside them isn't necessarily too bad(they could be deserters that proved themselves to be worthy of serving alongside or whatever), having red legion ships(harvesters) be on the typhon imperator when they were preparing for a siege is not fine. I hope that this will be changed in the future, although it doesn't do much, it's just inconsitent, bungie is always inconsistent with such things. the fact that lightfall and final shape were originally supposed to be one expansion really shows, it feels somewhat unfinished in some aspects.

btw, in what way does the Light fall? and what the hell is the veil?

anyway enough ranting. story extremely bad, rewards good, bungie forgot what made witch queen good and made an expansion that completely does the opposite of what witch queen did.
bungie just does not stop disappointing the community.\t\t\t\t\t

And yes, I am absolutely happy that not every question gets answered here. Destiny is an evolving story line and Lightfall is not the end. I'm sure Bungie has the story in the upcoming seasons thought out to prepare us for the Final Shape, just like the Witch Queen seasons also were great story telling.
