\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCan not recommend buying, the whole expansion was very underwhelming for how much it cost and how Bungie hyped it up.

- Swinging around with the grapple and using the new buffs and debuffs is fun, but you have to finish the new campaign to unlock your Strand subclass. And even then you only get the "skeleton" of it, you'll still have to unlock all of the fragments to get into buildcrafting. The grind for that is very slow and not fun, at least for a casual player.

- I personally find the new Hunter Super very frustrating. The light attack has a sweet spot at the very end of the rope, where the blade is, so to deal the maximum amount of damage on a boss or large enemy you have to constantly reposition yourself because your character moves forward with every attack. The tracking against airborne targets also sucks.

- As a lot of people have already said, the story is very "floaty". The stuff that is interesting is not explained anywhere, at least not to my knowledge. The last bossfight in the campaign was dissapointing for me: no unique moveset, he is basically a larger version of 2 different mobs in both his phases. And at the end when you talk to Zavala and Ikora they act as if the Traveler is dead, when its clearly still there and we clearly still have our Lightpowers. Very confusing.

- After finishing the campaign in about 3 days solo partially on the easiest, partially on legendary difficulty i was around power level 1750 (which seems to be the softcap). For Neomuna activities you have to be around 1800. The exotic lost sectors are set at 1830. The new seasonal activity is at 1770, and while that is doable it is still really annoying to be underpowered. Especially because there are adds just constantly spawning. You clear out a few to get some breathing room, and immediatly a new group spawns. I appreciate Bungie trying to make activities more interesting, but this is just obnoxious.

- I expected the city of neomuna to be a lot bigger and more vertical.

- I could be wrong about this, but i'm pretty sure when i bought the lightfall dlc a day before it launched it only gave me the option to buy the bundle with the annual pass which is priced at 99,99€. And now Lightfall is available on its own at 49,99€. Very disappointed because of that, feels like i have been scammed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPerfectly fine DLC. Story is pretty par for Destiny. They've always had you chasing after unexplained space stuff anyway - the Light, the Traveler, the Black Garden, whatever. Maybe it's a little more ambiguous than usual but it just seems like they're pacing it out for the upcoming seasons.

There's some corny VA here and there but it's far less noticably grating than at least three specific games that dropped in the past few months alone. If anything, they needed a second take for some funky sounding Osiris lines (or just let him rest in peace).

As for the stuff that actually matters- the new activities are pretty fun, the QoL changes are great, and the new subclass has immediately worked its way up to my top two for titan. This is the stuff that got me to actually come back after barely playing the last two seasons. I'm also actually using new guns for the first time in a while, and not just the exotics.

The experience may be different for other classes and playstyles, but overall it's a nice freshening up, in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
