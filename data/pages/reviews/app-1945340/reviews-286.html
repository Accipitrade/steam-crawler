\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWill Update As I Progress:

Lightfall is certainly worse than Witch Queen and Red War, but also certainly better than Shadowkeep and Beyond Light.

Pros:
- Some of the best cutscenes in the history of Destiny.
- Destructible environments are a great addition.
- First and final campaign missions are very good.
- Music is banger as always.
- Strand is fun but cooldowns are too long post campaign.
- Witness and Calus dynamic is very cool.

Cons:
- Did not feel like the 'Infinity War' of Destiny other than that we lost.
- Veil, Radial Mast, and other buzzwords thrown around without explanation.
- Nimbus.
- Who thought Nimrod was a good idea. Terrible voice acting and lines.
- Nimrod design makes me uncomfortable.
- Nimrod is planet vendor.
- Neomuna feels empty, and not in the awesome ODST way.
- Neomuna has so many reused assets from DSC.
- Strand missions in campaign are buns and waste of a mission.
- No crucible maps.
- Only 1 strike.
- Not enough loot. (This is a looter shooter.)
- Legendary mode too easy.\t\t\t\t\t
