\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore I get all negative, let me say this expansion isn't bad, it just didn't deliver what we were expecting given the trailers and such. Strand is easily one of my favorite classes and far outclasses Stasis. Not to mention Neomuna is an excellent setting that I am very pleased with. However, the story is the blatantly lacking area of this expansion.

Lets start with the big man himself: Calus. He's by no means bad in this story. In fact I thoroughly enjoyed his presence in cutscenes and the final fight. I do feel however they didn't know exactly what to do with Calus, or perhaps they didn't feel any pressure to expand on his character in the same way as Savathun given that we've had him since early D2. They push this concept throughout the campaign he's been changed since he joined the Witness, however they never really expand this. He's still a selfist, bloated despot and I fully expected this is remain somewhat similar, but he shows a very odd resistance to the Witness throughout the story. I can't criticize him too much as, honestly, I don't know myself how to approach this character other than ripping out his personality and turning him into a fanatic of the Witness.

Now for the most terrifying villain in D2 yet, the Witness. Like Calus, the Witness is awesome in every cutscene. Given this is the second-to-last expansion for this saga however, I had expected some exposition on it and its motives. All we're given is that it wants to end out "pain", but this is never explained. We got no information on why it wants the Traveler AGAIN, and moreover the big thing we spend the entire campaign hunting for, the Veil, is never explained to us. We have no idea what it is, why its important, or how the hell Savathun hid it and yet it seems like its been powering Neomuna since the Collapse. The prescence of the Witness is and always will be awesome, but they failed to give us an explanation on anything involving it and the story.

Before this gets any longer, I'll wrap it up with a general summary of my opinions on the story: its nothing. Literally nothing. I spent hours going through this campaign, only to be left with no answers, and only more questions. No plot point is explained, Strand is just there, Neomuna's history seems questionable at best, and every point that they had a chance to make us actually CARE about anything in this story they proceeded to fumble. I truly wish I could say I loved this thoroughly, but I can't. Despite everything they've set up, despite the countless hours they have had to make this story, they failed to make me feel like anything in this campaign that I did was important. I went to Neomuna, I found Strand, I beat Callus, and then the Witness proceeded to jump through a portal to god-knows-where. I'm fine with losing, I WANTED TO LOSE, but worst of all is that despite the fact we lost, there are no stakes to this. The Traveler is still here, we still have the Light, and humanity is honestly in a better place than it was when we started this campaign.

(I lied about not making it longer, sorry.) To the devs at Bungie, if you do read this, know I'm upset, but I don't feel like you guys failed me here. I don't know who pushed Strand so much into the main story that there was no actual story, but I truly believe you all wanted us to have a great experience. For years me and my friends have hypothesized on what "Lightfall" is and you all created an excellent idea of it up until release. But despite that, I am disappointed. I was one of the first people to preorder this campaign back when it was announced, and I do feel betrayed. You all can't afford to fail like this, not anymore. There's been plenty of times in the past and Destiny has a history of failing in terms of the main story, but you don't have that luxury anymore. This is the second-to-last expansion in a saga in the making for almost a decade now. There's no do-overs, no second chances, this is THE END. Don't fail us when you just showed us how incredible you all can be with Witch Queen. I wish you all the best, I hope you don't receive any hate for this, but please I beg of you to take the time to think on The Final Shape. Blow us away and make it the best thing Destiny has seen in its 10 years.

Eyes up, Guardian.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tfirst thing first, Witchqueen is said to be the first dlc for destiny 3, or in the current situation, the first chapter of the new saga, so lightfall is suppose to be the second chapter, which means catering the needs for new comers shouldnt be the priority of this campaign but what i can see is the whole walkthrough is full of basic mechanics such as dunking balls, shooting vex cubes etc and makes the campaign even more boring.

in the campaign, strand is introduced and bungie just decided to walk the good old path of beyond light again, which is the new subclass is the key to beat the enemy and make the campaign just a damn tutorial of the strand in a very boring way. we just go into an arena, use stand to create balls to break shield, kill enemy, rinse and repeat. only in a few encounter we are forced to make good use of strand to keep moving and keep us alive. but the point is with strand the gameplay can be done so much better. just like in the last mission we need to turn off the anti air guns with the use of strand grapple hook, the whole spiderman feel is pretty neat but somehow even tho grapple hook is one of the main element of this subclass bungie made this feature very underwhelming. while we can swing across buildings and roof and one mistake gonna cost us to replay the whole mission, most encounter require using the strand is held on the ground so what we can do the using the strand to hook on enemy and melee them. imo making good use of movement skill is more important than ever in this dlc but bungie just somehow overlooked that. another lethal decision is in all 3 classes, strand is a melee based subclass, and cabel shield is counter melee perfectly, especially with the shield bash and boss stomp, pickin a fight with cabel in close range is a very stupid move but with strand its the only thing we can do. in 2 story missions we need to escape the area after clearing wave of enemy, one require strand but what makes it stupid is even without strand we can just jump onto those platforms, and with no count down, the whole escaping move is just blend. in the lightfall trailer we saw guardians running away from an cabel army and jump off the building using strand, yet there is no such encounter in the actual dlc while imo this is a prefect way of letting us getting use to evade to strand. all in all with a subclass weigh heavily on movement it had so much potential yet bungie absolutely wasted it.

story wise ( spoiler alert)
I understand that bungie wanna keep things in secret so the player base can discover new secrets every season, which is a good approach but the problem is they have over done it. at the start of the campaign, with no background info or anything, we just followed osiris into a cabel ship heading to neomuna, no understanding of what the veil is ( even after the campaign we still dont), and the funny thing is everyone in the story keeps empathasizing the importance of the thingy we have little to none knowledge of it. which is one of the reason i dislike this campaign, having no idea what i am doing, the more we go deeper into the story, more questions came up and none get answered, it just feels like yea go get strand and fight, whats the objective? why are we defending this? why we destroy this? oh coz the npc in neomuna said so. bungie just digged many holes for the secrets waiting for an answer but when it comes to explanation all i saw is a bunch of " somehow palpatine returns " level of lazy writing. we dont know what we are doing and everything just feels so pointless. and in the end, our guardian, killing everyone in the way carefully, just ignore our ghost being possessed by withness and let it ruin everything, we are literally being the destiny version of starlord in infinity war, being a dumbass so the story continues. after that, the ending is um, baffling, i watch the cut scene three times and read the dialogue but i still dont know what in the actual has happened, and this really pissed me off.

now for the new destination, neomuna, neptune, a futuristic city, a civilized world instead of a random planet with alien design and post apocalyptic feel, right? my ass. bungie reuse the season of splicer design and put some vex platform across the destinition, which is good, it fits, i have no problem with reusing environment design, but once again bungie over done it, they might just copy and paste half the deep stone crypt into the indoor landscape of neptune, and its already boring. and, the city is empty, everyone just link themselves to the soulkiller and live in the net, which makes the need of a whole city weird, especially when there were only 2 cloudstrider defending the city. it will be nice if they locate the hall of heros to another fast travel point with a bunch of npc instead of a damn hologram. and with that said, the city dont even feels like a futuristic city, its just a damn ghost town.\t\t\t\t\t

The hype for this expansion was insane and is honestly why so many people were disappointed. I think overall if you ignore the haters, it is a solid DLC and fun to play. Lightfall wasn't absolutely amazing, but I wasn't really expecting that and I think others were. If any DLC should blow me away, it should be the Final Shape, Lightfall would've just been icing on top.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoes anyone else feel like the trailers for Lightfall are nearing false advertising? Almost all the trailers show off cinematics that are literally just from the introduction cut scene to Lightfall. The story is excruciatingly bland taking place in a beautifully bright neon, yet somehow completely void of life narrow cookie cutter city that looks like it was trying to be Bungie's far better New Mombasa from Halo 2 and ODST.

After riding the high that was The fantastic Witch Queen expansion I and my fireteam were beyond excited to jump into Lightfall. Only to be met with Nimbus, The most annoying character this franchise has ever seen. The sighs that could be heard through Discord every time Nimbus would speak was harmonized. Rohan was a neat character that is used to try and make you feel any emotion during this sludge of a story. And obviously it falls flat, How is anyone supposed to care for a character they knew for what...3 missions?

This is all without even mentioning the utter confusion you'll experience when you try to follow the story. The Witness needs "The Veil" To get at the Traveler for whatever reason, Problem is. The game not once ever explains what this "veil" is or what it does. Not via dialog, and not even shoved into a text log. We have no idea what the veil is, why the Witness wants it. And not even what happens at the end. This whole expansion at every corner leaves you saying. "Wait... What??"

Strand is so disappointing. The grapple hook is clearly the eye candy of this whole expansion, Well don't get too excited because the grapple shares the same cooldown as your grenade. Bungie clearly wanted to emulate Halo Infinite's Grapplehook but how am I supposed to enjoy this new dimension of traversing when I have to wait 3 minutes (Not a joke literally 3 minutes) For my grapple to recharge???

The story is less of a cohesive story and more of a extremely prolonged Strand tutorial, not even letting you have access to the subclass until you unlock the secondary side quest after you beat the entirety of the Lightfall expansion... Who's bright idea was this?

Avoiding boss spoilers, what a shame. There's a surprise second phase once you defeat the boss but the game doesn't recharge your lives, nor give you a chance to place a banner for heavy ammo or your super. So have fun celebrating with your squad once you finally kick the bosses ass using all your supers, and heavy ammo. Only to wipe seconds later to said second phase in which you are woefully unprepared.

Overall very disappointed with Lightfall and feels like it should've been at least $30 but $50... for this? This is robbery.
Wait for a sale or skip all together and buy something good like the Dead Space Remake.\t\t\t\t\t
