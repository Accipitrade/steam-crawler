\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely underwhelming.

The locations are cool, but a solid 30-40% of all content is reused assets so it feels annoying to play again.

Strand is okay at best (needs a big buff).
- With how power crept the light subclasses have gotten over the last year it makes strand terrible to use, slow ability charges are the main thing, plus its difficult to build off of.
- In the campaign the grapple recharges super fast when you are on the ground which feels super nice, but as soon as you unlock it it is on a minute long cooldown.

Score is fantastic as always, nothing bad to say about it.

Story is abysmal,
- Nimbus is so annoying and his jokes do not land at all.
- Rohan is cool but he dies too early for us to even care about him
- Calus barely has any time in the story and he is written like we know how he'll act even though he's barely had anytime in the main story.
- The exotic missions are laughably bad the exotic machine gun mission is just GARDEN OF SALVATION BUT BACKWARDS. It is probably the laziest thing I have ever seen in this game.

The main story just felt like a small push for The Final Shape and honestly with how lightfall looks I probably will not be buying it.

Only buy this awful expansion if it is on sale, otherwise do not waste your time.
