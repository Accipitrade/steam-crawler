\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis seems like a rushed production... The story is nearly non-existent in the campaign. There are more gaping holes, inconsistencies and analogies that literally don't make sense in the story than coherent, useful, storytelling. I am a bit of a lore buff, and I am left scratching my head trying to make sense of the story arc.

Nimbus (one of the new NPCs/vendors) is incredibly annoying. Their voice is oddly modulated and their dialogue is excessively cheesy.

There are some positives, though. The gameplay in the campaign was enjoyable, although not exceptional. It felt more like doing random patrols than progressing through a story, which made it less cohesive.

The raid is one of the easier raids, but it is a lot of fun. The planets encounter seems out of place, and although the encounter is a lot of fun, it doesn't really add much to the story.

If you are a new destiny player, this campaign isn't going to be enjoyable for you. Work through the older content (particularly Witch Queen) and decide if you are willing to continue playing for the sake of the game before purchasing this campaign.\t\t\t\t\t
