I have 803.3 hours played on Destiny 2 on PC. Not counting the PS time and D1 time.

To make this semi short and semi simple.... not only is this the most god awful attempt at an add-on/story addition DLC whatever you wish to call it... It has totally ruined my faith in Bungie and the Destiny franchise. I was so excited to see the new addition coming I did the pre-order option in game where you got the exotic rifle and all the extra goodies for 100usd and wish i would have just lit the money on fire instead.

The story is COMPLETE trash... rushed... the dialogue in the story feels like a 2nd grader wrote it. The overwhelmingly beautiful and dark cut-scenes with "the witness" tease you into thinking the story is going to progress to something better and darker and any second you'll finally get the new power to fully use through the main story... but no... all this time and hype and marketing for a story that you can finish in less than 3 hours...

The new planet environment looks like Fortnite with skittles and diarrhea set in an atmosphere of what seems to show the devs of any new game series and Fornite what NOT to make their games look like. The new NPCs ... ugly... talk as if they have the education of a pre schooler... and seem to have something shoved in their pants as if they are striving to be a mechanical magic mike Ai on acid. Though their appearance goes truly right along with the most annoying voices you will ever hear and you will hear it enough to make you feel as if you would rather be listening to cats or monkeys during mating season.

The ONLY good thing about this add on content is the new subclass as it is pretty cool to use... but like stasis it is going to take 15 years and bounties and quests to get all the aspects and fragments you really want to have. Basically I wish I could have just paid 9.99 for the new subclass and that be it. Sad thing is... you HAVE to play the entire new story to get the subclass... upside is it will only take you a couple of hours.... truly a couple\t\t\t\t\t
