\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUsually big expansions like these see the game taking a step in the right direction but sadly, that's not the case this time. Instead of Bungie taking one step in the right direction and 3 steps back, they don't take any step in a right direction but rather 4 steps back. Just to highlight the many cons we have:
-Less build variety
-A very meh subclass (Titans once again get shafted this time)
-A very lackluster story
-Annoying/Unlikable new and existing characters
-Unexplained plot points
-Weak plot points
-A dead and boring new zone
-No more comp freelance

Sure there are some good pros to this expansion like the new loadout system, the removal of armor energy types, and artifact mods being applied permanently without the need to slot them. But that's it. That's all the good new ♥♥♥♥ that was delivered. Only one new strike too? I feel like Bungie doesn't even know what kind of game they want Destiny to be at this point.\t\t\t\t\t
