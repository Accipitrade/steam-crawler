\r\n\t\t\t\t\tday 1 impressions:
so much hype for such a disappointing payoff :(

post raid overall thoughts:
the good: The campaign is more or less on par with WQ, maybe a little worse but plenty enjoyable going on gameplay alone. Strand is goofy fun. Like, holy hell I love this subclass. the raid weapons are also interesting and cool, and I like all of the new exotics. Don't even get mt started on Tormentors. Really interesting and terrifying on Legendary.

the not so good: The story was a considerable step down from the quality that I've come to expect over the last few years. the raid feels buggy/inconsistent, which is a bummer given that it's the best content D2 DLCs have to offer. The marketing content set up the DLC to look like the "Infinity War" of the saga, but fell flat and ended up being just a side quest for a macguffin that I still don't understand, making the stakes feel absent as a result, and pushing back the allegedly imminent threat of the Witness back *another* year. Like seriously, why in a DLC called "Lightfall" is the Traveller seemingly harmed/dead, and nothing has really changed other than there being a portal looking thing in it now. It just gives me the same sense of disappoint cliffhanger that Shadowkeep left me with after our first encounter with the Witness.

I love the game, and I desperately want to see it succeed. After WQ and a year of *decent* seasons, I felt confident recommending Lightfall to friends. After this, I'm not confident I know what to expect anymore. Following the drop in story quality, it feels like Bungie is starting to phone it in, and I'm not likely to expect anything that will blow me away in the following seasons or in The Final Shape. My biggest fear right now is that the saga ends in the same way as Game of Thrones, letting major important questions go unanswered, and leaving a bad taste in my mouth.\t\t\t\t\t

One level is the "designated training arc," but the way the campaign is designed, almost all of the levels are just strand playgrounds. On the strand topic, it's fun, but it's out of place. If strand was taken out of the campaign, most of the plot would stay the same. The voice acting is mediocre to be as positive as possible. Voice lines were pretty meh, voice acting was okay, and the audio effects on Nimbus' voice was atrocious. There also wasn't enough time to learn about the characters, and Rohan dies without us caring about him. Probably missed a couple of things, but those are the main complaints. I hope we can learn more from the raid. All in all, I think strand was fun, and if you like funny weapons, then get the game. If you don't care about the weapons, I don't recommend getting Lightfall.\t\t\t\t\t
