PROS
Loadout System is great
Music & Set pieces are awesome
New guns, exotics feel fresh and fun to use as always.
Legend campaign was a satisfying content

CONS
Story and writing were terrible, a HUGE step back from witchqueen. There werent any "big" reveals and overall story beats missed the mark. Characters were poorly written tropes and stereotypes and the quality of the writing for them took us all the way back to the red war. Absolutely nothing like the "Empire Strikes Back" story we were promised from the marketing.

Im sure the raid, and seasons leading up to the final shape will be worth it, and for 100$ you get a TON of content over the next year to play and enjoy. Ill update the review if anything changes.

*EDIT*
The new raid is excellent. There is some discourse around the difficulty of challenge mode, but its one of the best raids from a sound and visual design. The mechanics are simple and it has its place as a good raid to bring new players through. The armor, and weapons from the raid are top notch.

All in all ive updated the review to recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
