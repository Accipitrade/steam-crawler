\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll amend this review as the season goes on, but so far it feels like I've been duped. Lightfall was hyped up by Bungie as being the tipping point of the story. Everything from last year's seasons and the Witch Queen were pumping it up to be an insane unforgettable campaign. Building up to the end of the series. What we got felt like the original Destiny campaign and D2 Red War. The campaign missions were unimaginative, and what's more disappointing is that bungie has told these stories before.

See Halo Reach, a story where the main villain of the series makes it's move unknown to the protagonist. Thought the first mission was learn what's going on and it's scope. We meet the team, and work together to try and foil the plot of the covenant. Spoilers for the old game. While we fail to save Reach, we sacrifice everything, litterally staying on a doomed planet to give humanity one last chance.

In Lightfall, Spoilers for lightfall, we see the stakes, then are pulled away from them. We then go on a side mission to gain an ability that's only purpose in the campaign is to out arms race the enemy cabal's anti ability field. And then we fail, we get the new subclass in the end, but other than that there's seemingly no other impact.

Other missions outside of the campaign are much higher quality, and it just makes me question how bungie can even imagine they'd be prepared to send of the series in final shape. They couldn't replicate the success of witch queen through storytelling, mechanics, or lore. It's a near absolute failure.

The only saving grace is the seasonal missions, which I hate grinding out. I just want to play and see the story play out with me.

My conclusion is Bungie screwed something up they've already done before. And returned to their old bland ways of not giving us anything of meaning in the story.

I'm a sucker who bought the annual pass, so I guess I'm in for the long haul. And as much as I'd like to say I won't make that mistake twice. I'd like to see and play out the end of Destiny's story. I'm just incredibly disappointed\t\t\t\t\t
