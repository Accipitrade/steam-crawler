\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall, an incredibly negative experience. Yes the difficulty changes across the board are great and strand is amazing to play with. But the story, despite this being the penultimate dlc, is more confusing than ever, has so many plot holes and odd thoughts.
- Im sick and tired of random characters being introduced in dlcs for them just to be used in the campaign and then just become a destination vendor. It happened with variks, happened with fynch and i can guarantee itll happen with Nimbus.
- Neomuna is not what anyone was expecting. Even looking at the initial trailers for the destination i, along with many others were expecting giant skyscrapers that you can jump off of and grapple into the air, swinging between these buildings, but we got another very flat but decent enough destination
- Calus, despite having a beautiful overall of his character design, is a completely underwhelming boss even at legendary difficulty.
- The story telling for this dlc is horrific, leaving me with so many questions and plot points that weren't even explained. Characters talk about the veil like they all know exactly what it is, bringing it up in every sentence possible, but never ever explaining what it is. Even after you've visited the veil, you still have absolutely no clue what it is and after completing all the post dlc content, you will still have no answers.
- Strand, although great to play with, you're teased with it throughout the entire dlc and only get it after randomly meditating at some place we've walked past so many times before? We even had a training montage and a time trail Osiris made for us but we still cant wield it until after the campaign has finished?

The list of things can go on and on but overall this is a massive disappointment for the money, confusing story line, horrific final boss, underwhelming open world. This is bad bungie. You made everyone happy with Witch Queen but then go and do this? A complete 180 and one of the most underwhelming and worst expansions that this company has put out.\t\t\t\t\t
