\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed since Destiny 1 Beta. Bungie has proven they can salvage their reputation by occasionally knocking things out of the park on an expansion. They did this with The Taken King and Forsaken. However this has been 3 disappointing expansions in a row now, and my hopes aren't high for a return to form. I hope the art and sound teams find a new home to display their talent in new original franchises.

Good:
As always, the art direction and sound team are flawless.
The environment and music of the last campaign level was cool.
Calus looked cool.

Bad:
New destination advertised a bustling neon downtown city. There's nobody in it.
The new class, probably the biggest selling point of the DLC, is timegated and will only drip-feed new abilities over time.
Bungie still wants you to grind the same content in the same way for the same rewards that have not been evolved upon in 4 years.

Butthurt:
New class for Titan is just another recolored punch. Arc = blue punch, Stasis = dark blue punch, void = purple shield punch, solar = orange hammer punch, Strand = green punch.

About $15 of content in a $50 package.
I regret my purchase.\t\t\t\t\t
