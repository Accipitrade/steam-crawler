I'll paint the picture: A DLC with good core content, that is content locked behind a ♥♥♥♥ campaign with a lousy narrative. Now imagine doing this on more than 1 class. The campaign offers brief uses of Strand. This should be a teaser, it should make the player yearn for more, but no, it forces you to use the terrible grapple grenade revolving around obstacle courses of some kind. Every Strand encounter, including the final boss fight revolves around this miniscule, uni-dimensional aspect of Strand, that is offensively weak, and boring. I hated every moment I had to use Strand grapple. The campaign is nothing more than an unpleasant chore with how unbalanced it is.

Constant waves of enemies and many enemies whose gimmick is to teleport, be invulnerable, or heal ? Red bar enemies who have half a magazine of health ? I don't know what's worse: If the campaign was play tested and approved, or if it wasn't play tested at all, both are dismal conclusions. The average gamer is a middle aged man with a job, and perhaps a family. Very few people are willing to spend this much time unlocking Strand on more than 1 character. How such an experienced company has such a poor understanding of their market is bewildering.

The characters lack depth, the dialogue is weak, the narrative is laughable. Consistent references to " The Veil " whose significance, or what it even is, is never explained.

Lightfall is like a paper 20% of which was written by a graduate and the remaining 80% by a toddler. It could've been good, hell, it could've been great, and it would've been easy to do so, but nah, that'd be too logical. Mind-bogglingly stupid.\t\t\t\t\t
