\r\n\t\t\t\t\tsurprisingly, i can recommend this DLC for anything but its story. (any ratings or evaluations i give will be marked out of 10 and fall into 3 main categories: loot, story, gameplay, )

the story of Lightfall deserves a 4/10, it is unable to fill the shoes of Witch Queen's wonderful campaign, even which got me interested and invested! The story is incredibly basic, and has lots of filler dialogue with poorly written characters and poorly written events with no significance or buildup. The only thing which was decent was the narrative, where the traveler was under threat, and the guardian must stop armies of combatants to save the very thing that gives him his powers

the loot of Lightfall gets a 7/10, there are many fun and powerful pieces of loot to chase after! the weapons in the Lightfall weapon set are all unique, and powerful! however, some loot may require lots of grinding, which can be a hassle to deal with to casual players.

the gameplay for Lightfall earns a 9/10, i found the new enemies and version of cabal to be lots of fun to fight! i liked the vehicle sections too! the strand subclass is a blast to use! it gives a sense of incredible power to the player, the rope swinging, dashing and suspending enemies is an exhilarating experience to enjoy.

overall, lightfall is incredibly fun, but not for those who like a good story to follow (side note: i chose to be vague to avoid giving any spoilers to people who don't take too well to spoilers).\t\t\t\t\t

It seems that they are stretching their developers far too thin in terms of support for multiple projects. Along with that, I think the cadence for the seasons and expansions are harming the quality of the game. If The Final Shape needs a delay for us to receive anywhere near a competent ending of a story we've followed along with for years, delay it.
