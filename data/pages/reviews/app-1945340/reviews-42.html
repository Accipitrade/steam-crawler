\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLots of reviews mention stuff like the current PvP meta, lack of playlist armor refresh, changes to the mod system, the Neomuna patrol zone, difficulty changes... all of those things are free, or are just evolutions of the game which are the same whether you buy Lightfall or not. So I only want to highlight what you actually get from purchasing this expansion.

The narrative itself isn't great, and feels like a side story that's barely related to the main plot thread.
But the campaign missions themselves are good - unique encounters, an interesting new enemy type, some new mechanics, and there are quite a few missions. The story may be lacking but the missions themselves are very fun to play.

The new Strand subclass is very fun to use (and actually balanced at launch).

Lightfall also comes with the first season of seasonal content, which is quite good. The story is even more interesting than Lightfall even if it's shorter - although it's not finished yet. And the gear is cool, new seasonal activity is pretty fun too.

I haven't played the new raid yet but everyone is saying it's very easy but fun. The gear from it looks amazing though imo.

Anyway I would definitely recommend getting this expansion last, after you've completed all the other Destiny campaigns and you're looking for more to do. It's a shame about the narrative, but putting that aside the gameplay and the new gear are just as good as Bungie always does, and the new subclass adds some fun variety.

I had fun with Lightfall, and I'm still having fun with it. Hope Bungie can get the story back on track though.\t\t\t\t\t
