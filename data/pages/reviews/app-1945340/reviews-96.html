\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely dramatic backlash about Lightfall due to the poor storytelling, especially in comparison to Witch Queen. Where the expansion excels is the QoL changes and gameplay.

- Saved loadouts is a much needed addition
- The new Strand subclasses (with all the fragments unlocked) are extremely fun, creative and powerful
- Increases to difficulty are good and make build crafting feel impactful
- Neomuna is a bit lifeless with digital citizens, although enemy density is good. It is a nice change of scenery and the Lost sectors are really well designed .
- Post campaign missions are good in terms of enriching the story of Neomuna, although the overly long sessions and boring requirements in patrol zones could have been done better
- The secret exotic mission was great and felt like a mini dungeon, with a blend of unique mechanics and good enemy density

Overall the campaign story is a big let down, but everything else is an absolute win. Hopefully over the rest of the seasons leading to next year we will actually get to tie up loose ends and unravel some big mysteries before what I imagine must be a blockbuster finale to the Light and Darkness saga.

For the people who could care less about the writing and just play for the game-play loop and the player vs. player experience, this expansion has been pretty solid so far. People are still figuring out strand and the new build-crafting system but the overall reception from people I know who have played since the double primary days think it's a net positive.

Get the expansion for the additional mechanics and loot. If you are here for the story, I have no clue who pointed you toward Destiny.\t\t\t\t\t
