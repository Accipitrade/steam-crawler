\r\n\t\t\t\t\t*sigh* Okay… well I don’t usually write reviews but looking at some of the backlash for this expansion I think a few things should be clarified.

Let's start off with the bad.
This campaign is really not delivered well at all. New characters are kinda mid and unenjoyable and old characters feel worse than they have in the past. Both Osiris and Calus in this expansion do not feel like enjoyable characters or that their characters received enough justice for how long they have been built.
The trailers and teasers for this expansion felt a bit like a lie. In the trailers we saw a lot of the Witness and their power both individually and as an army leader. In the campaign the Witness is barely present and Calus is the main antagonist. Which was not portrayed well in the trailers.
Strand also feels particularly bad in the campaign. On each mission you get little glimpses of this power expect… it doesn't feel strong in the campaign. You also have to complete the whole campaign to use it even though you get it every mission.
You are chasing after an object called “The Veil” in order to prevent the Witness from obtaining it but we are never told what The Veil is or what it is going to help the Witness achieve. Instead we chase it blindly while the Traveler which we are supposed to protect is under siege by its greatest enemy yet.

TL:DR Campaign is portrayed poorly in almost every way. Worse the Witch Queen even though it costs more

Now the good.
Overall gameplay is super fun with this expansion.
Increasing the difficulty makes the player have to be more aware during PvE in most instances which is enjoyable.
Strand is actually super fun to play with in both PvE and PvP
Neomuna is enjoyable to play around with. The new events like Vex Incursion and Terminal Overload are fun.
The few exotics this expansion does have seem cool to play with. Except one is locked behind pre order still for some reason.

TL:DR Gameplay outside of the story is actually quite enjoyable.

Because most of my issues reside in the story and storytelling I think this expansion is still good. I would wait for a sale to buy this expansion because $50 is a bit much imo.

The raid has yet to come out so the raid experience and level of loot is not considered here.
