The story ends with a disappointing moment of realizing the entire story you just played was meaningless, and the characters you speak to following the ending are all, "Well, guess the best we can do is hope for the best". Strand itself is super cool and the visual/audio design for it is wonderful and enjoyable. They also didn't remove anything other than the obvious seasonal activities from Witch Queen that were expected to leave, so that's nice too. Honestly I give this DLC a 6/10.
