I love the loadout system and the mod page, both are great quality of life additions. the story was acceptable, my jaw was dropped through the entire thing. Like The Witch Queen, I did Solo the legendary Campaign; and had a lot of fun doing it. The missions on legendary are perfectly difficult, and had me on my toes without feeling unfair. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tSeriously, this is the "ultimate answer to what fans are asking for"?
The base game and expansions up to season 19 are decent to really good (fun dungeons/raids/crafted weapons). When this was announced, the big answer I was expecting was a group finder. We've been promised this forever now and its still not here. With this expansion its badly needed. Content is soloable for high tier players, but not the everyday "I just wanna relax and game" types. No, I don't mean it can't be done solo, I'm just saying its overly complicated.
If you go in thinking you'll just clear the area and burn down bosses or champions like season 19 and earlier, you'll be the one burned.
Honestly speaking, Lightfall can easily be fixed: Add group finders, give a longer time between mob spawn after kill, cut the damage output of mobs in a few areas- easy. Too much of Lightfall right now is kill, die, respawn far from where you were, get immediately sniped by OP elite, repeat. Worst thing is the player count seems below average, even for Destiny 2.
I love the game. Hate the expansion as it is early on. Hope it gets better.\t\t\t\t\t
