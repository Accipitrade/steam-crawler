\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I’ve been playing Destiny for a long time so I’m going to recap what Lightfall brought to the table that is passing over so many peoples head. First, the quality of life fixes. Movement is a lot smoother, you feel like you’re able to glide around and run without feeling like something is dragging you anymore. Fall damage is reduced. They reworked the mod system and made it much more approachable for new players and simplified it for the veteran players. You can now save classes for different situations and preload load outs and armor. Champion mods are now applied to your character and no longer a mod so it no longer takes up slots for other mods. They added more fragments to the classes giving customization to fit your playstyle and buffed pretty much every class and type this DLC.

On the story side, (no spoilers) it was amazing, calus was amazing, neomuna was amazing, I don’t get what was so hard for people to understand and the story is not over yet, this campaign was around calus with the story of the watcher in the background and that’s ok. Overall the dlc is amazing and by far the best one we’ve received since forsaken.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was certainly a DLC hinged on expectations. Here were my main 3:
- The Traveller would be under threat (hence ‘Light fall’).
- We’d get to use Strand throughout the campaign.
- There would be a showdown with Calus.
All 3 of these were met, and in-between were amazing gameplay sequences, cool set pieces, and more of the triumphant orchestral scores we’ve known to love.

The campaign missions were consistently fun and engaging. I was mostly enthralled by the cyberpunk aesthetic of Neptune’s Neomuna, and loved every new location we saw. I do think that the overall plot was neither as well written or as revelatory as ‘The Witch Queen’, but I enjoyed each part regardless, despite some goofiness here and there.

I feel that, ultimately, this is the beginning of a long journey, as all DLCs for Destiny are, and my initial experience was mostly positive. It’s not perfect, but I had fun and I’m excited to see what’s next. So far, the Season of Defiance plot and missions are a great start.\t\t\t\t\t
