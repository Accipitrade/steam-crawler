\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing Destiny since D1 release. I've never bothered to write a review because, for all the bad stuff Bungie did in the early years of D2, it was never bad enough to take time to warn others away. No longer. Lightfall is a sham. The developers straight-up lied to us and gave us false promises. The game was infinitely better before Lightfall's release. Let's break it down.

The mod system? Gutted. Infantile. Insultingly simple. Limiting. Boring. This isn't a revamped mod system, it's sunsetting in disguise. Thank you devs for lying to your player base. You're clearly out of touch.

Story? Shallow. Childish. Not compelling. BORING. Seriously devs? This is the best story you could come up with, even if Lightfall is a "filler" expansion? Insulting. Nimbus is horribly boring and poorly written. Osiris has been reduced to a grumpy old man.

Map? Neomuna is dead. A whole city and no citizens because "they're digital". Pathetic. I'd rather roam around the Helm than explore this empty, bland city.

Strand? HA. I didn't think it would be possible to deliver such a bad subclass. Y'all really postponed its release during Witch Queen only for this to be the final product? YIKES, I cannot imagine what it was like a year ago then. It's horribly repetitive,

Pay your marketing team more because they did a GREAT job of selling what would be the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ expansion yet. I would rather play through all of Shadowkeep and Beyond Light (as boring as they were) before I replayed this horrible campaign. How do you make this after doing such a lovely job with Witch Queen? Did your B team make this expansion? Honestly, fire them. They don't deserve to touch Destiny if this is what they are going to do with it.

There is too much to hate for me to cover it all. Guardian ranks are insulting, weapons are uncreative, and dialogue is worse than if George Lucas wrote quippy lines for the latest Marvel movie.

Instead of bringing up all the bad stuff, I'll save time by mentioning the few good things:
Mods don't require elemental affinities. That is the only good mod change.
First Cut Scene was good and actually felt like "Lightfall". No other part of the story felt like that. I don't know how the devs messed up so badly.
Loadouts are a nice addition that only took them 9 years. Honestly I don't know if they deserve points for this.
Yup that's it. Those are the redeeming things. Insubstantial compared to the steaming pile of ♥♥♥♥ that Lightfall is. Well Done Devs. I couldn't have imagined a worse expansion if I tried. Clearly you seriously missed the mark on this one judging by the significant negative feedback and reviews Lightfall is receiving. I hope some of you lose your jobs over this because job security relies on good performance and this just is not it. Wake up and listen to the community.

0/10. Don't buy it. Don't buy it full price, or on sale. Honestly don't even download it for free. It'll taint your hard drive.
Would not recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
