\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPast Experience with Destiny
As a D1 player that played and quit in early D2 due to lack of content, ♥♥♥♥ gameplay, ♥♥♥♥ strikes, no power fantasy, and no cool weapons I came back for Lightfall. I played through Red War and quit in Curse of Osiris I believe.

Lightfall
Straight up the story isn't worth the price tag if you only play Destiny for the story. It's pretty typical Destiny story from what I'm used to some good characters, some ♥♥♥♥ characters, and the storytelling is okay with some cool stuff they don't explain properly because they want you to pay for them to explain it. The best way I can sum it up is you pay $50 to see a 20 minute trailer for a 3 hour long movie that will never be written and you'll be able to pay to see the highlights and important parts of the movie through seasonal purchases. Destiny 1 never had good story so keep in mind when I say it's okay I don't believe it is at all at a D2 level of storytelling. From what I've heard Bungie generally is better at this but Lightfall they kind of ♥♥♥♥♥♥ it up. The story has maybe one moment they try to make you care and they don't even take that moment seriously.

Strand
New subclass is a lot of fun the Hunter is apparently broken and just deleting stuff so it's busted. Haven't seen Warlock yet. Titan is cool and almost a mix of a ranged sunbreaker type subclass, cc, and bladeddancer. I've seen one extremely busted pve build with the new Titan subclass.\t\t\t\t\t
