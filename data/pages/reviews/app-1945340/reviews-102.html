\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis expansion in no way deserves the amount of hate that it's getting. Don't get me wrong, the campaign execution was poor, but is it worse than Shadowkeep? No, ain't even close. The only reason is because this is following Witch Queen, which was a masterpiece. The campaign just misses the mark. It didn't hit quite the same and things felt a little disjointed. The raid and exotic mission aren't out yet, and I'm expecting those to add to the story in a positive way. Outside of that, everything else is quite good. Strand is a fantastic subclass and the new fragments add some new flavor into the other subclasses. New buildcrafting is much easier, which is great for more casual players, but it's somewhat lacking in depth. It really just need more mods, but I'm guessing they played it conservative for the first iteration and will be adding back many of the old mods and adding new ones as they monitor the impact on gameplay. The new expansion activity is good and the vex strike force in the incursion zones is a nice addition over the similar Beyond Light version on Europa. New difficulty options in other activities is nice, but some tiers are lacking in rewards. They've said they are monitoring the reward situation and I'm expecting this to improve.\t\t\t\t\t
