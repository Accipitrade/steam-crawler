\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWitch Queen ran so this could fall on it's face. I have never been as disappointed in a game as I am with this content drop. We have 24/7 lore specialists that have no idea what is going on in this 'campaign', with characters reverted to basic Marvel level writing, complete with one liners, millennial humor, and the worst character since Jar Jar Binks! Yes I mean you Nimbus. I refuse to believe that this was a planned DLC. The rumors of this being part 1 of the total story of final shape seem very true, there is a correct way to walk into a story and leave with more questions than answers, but this? This is just lazy. Putting aside the fanfiction level story, everything just sucks. Neomuna feels empty and hollow, like the Cosmodrone but with a better skybox. Almost all assets (enemies, guns, etc.) are reused and reskinned assets from other DLCs. And the poor build crafting system. We got rid of elemental wells, charged with light, and warminds for this? And don't even get me started on the guardian rank system. With the exception of the cutscenes (which to be fair are excellent and really do tell an amazing story when they are there), some strand abilities which are a breath of fresh air in movement, and some minor balance changes, this DLC is a complete failure and will leave a black stain on Destiny's record for many years to come. Do better Bungie.\t\t\t\t\t
