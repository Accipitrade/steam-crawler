\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI pre-ordered the collectors edition, so I was excited when it arrived and I downloaded this expansion. Except going into this, it was made abundantly clear that the seasons would continue the story instead of racing through the DLC campaign and using seasonal content as "just something to do"

The campaign was great, again knowing more of the story would be in the seasonal content, but filling in time between the DLC and the seasons made for a very boring time. The "intro to Strand" was more frustrating and a bigger let down for me than the actual story. An even bigger grip is the DLC weapons. I hate the majority of them.

The skill rebalance, I feel was needed. No low light should be able to demolish yellow bar enemies, and with the story focused on Cabal being more powerful, running headlong into Neomuna netted zero damage until you gained higher light. I like this approach.

I don't care about "vaulted stuff I paid for" considering it plainly tells you, and nearly every one of the games DLC's have vaulted some to add others. If people actually paid attention to the lore and the story, the "vaulted" areas were pulled out of this dimension by Savathun, and since we killed her in the Witch Queen, obviously large areas will be gone.

Bungie has stated they will be bringing back vaulted locations - so review bombing a DLC because you have no clue, after all this time, how the story and game work.

Is it perfect? Hell no. It's playable. It's boring solo, but a blast with friends or randos.

But here's where my disdain will show. Season of the Deep. The fishing is relaxing and the chance at random exotic armor is great. Deep Dives and Salvage ops suck. Why? Well, they're actually fun and challenging when you get people who know how to play them. But one person misses one of the "Toland" spots, it kills the chances at extra loot and everyone else just leaves. Guess what? NO ONE CAN JOIN IN ONCE THESE HAVE STARTED! Get kicked from the session, too bad, start over and those left in, have no help. WHY WOULD YOU KEEP PEOPLE FROM REJOINING given Destiny has a long history of persistent connection issues?

Would I recommend this at full price ($100 with the season pass) NO. One sale to include the Season Pass at 50% off, yeah, I think it's worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
