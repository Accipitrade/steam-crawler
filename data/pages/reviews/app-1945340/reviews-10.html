\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAside from the typical game loop of grinding activities to get gear to tackle on even harder activities a big proponent that continues to drive me back to this game are the environments it creates. These environments are crafted by gameplay, locations and most importantly storyline. I believe Lightfall under delivers in every aspect mentioned above. With the introduction to Neomuna, a mostly empty world in which once again Bungie decided to reuse assets albeit somehow making it fall in line with a previously used boss dating back from years ago, we see the potential of a new location wasted. Put aside the flashy synthwave look and you have something completely unrelated to the Destiny world that has been made over the last decade. Introducing characters which apply major plot holes with little to no background to them and hoping to create something new and interesting when we still have hundreds of unanswered questions. The gameplay misses the mark by continuing to lack any new and exciting ways to course through activities by continuing to use similar tactics we were using early on in D2. As for the storyline... the veil, disciples and the witness all lacking depth. The only snippets we get are from Osiris and others constantly spoon feeding us how dangerous they are yet with nothing to show for it but empty ships, reused races and a cutscene months later from the expansions release that was originally behind a paywall until the community complained enough that Bungie put it on their weekly update website.

The sting that Lightfall gives feels more painful coming down from Witch Queen's immaculate storytelling in comparison. I really hope Bungie manages to salvage what little is left of this expansion with the Final Shape on the horizon.
