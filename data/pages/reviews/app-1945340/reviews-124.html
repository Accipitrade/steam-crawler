Not as bad as most reviews are making it seem

And I really don't think the negative review bombs are justified considering the expansions release is less than one week; There are no real performance issues, or game breaking bugs; We haven't reached raid release; We are still in the first week of the seasonal content/story

Campaign introduces a lot;

  • Amazing new destination with a lot of new kinds of post-campaign/open world activities
  • Good characters with new concepts
  • A lot of great cut scenes compared to previous expansions
  • Halo-esque kinds of missions including vehicles, friendly ally AI
  • ANOTHER insanely good soundtrack (change your music volume to be higher than the game volume)
  • Updates to a number of older strikes which feels refreshing
  • A new destination strike which I consider one of the best I've played in Destiny
  • Great new cosmetics, which is ofc optional but is a big part of the game for a lot of players like myself
  • A new subclass which I think is being negatively reviewed way too early..
  • A number of other new UI / feature updates such as the Journey screen, commendations etc.

It does however come with a few minor cons

  • Campaign story feels like it's missing some crucial information around some objects, and characters you encounter during the campaign; Leaving it feel a little unfinished despite being very fun
  • Bungie wants to push this information to be attained throughout seasons 21, 22, 23 etc. which is gonna cost players the annual pass to conclude the story..
  • Campaign pace feels off at times, and honestly I think the campaign should have had a better storyboard written up for it. For example, the opening of the campaign is chaos, you're given the feeling of absolute urgency to chase someone (avoiding spoilers) and beat them to the destination; But suddenly you're introduced to a lot of new elements that slows everything down too much (new characters, new subclass, side missions that don't feel incredibly important)

Overall I would currently give a positive, and this is before the raid and seasonal story has even gone past a week
