\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny itself has its problems, but Lightfall really takes the cake.

This expansion feels like it was written as a bad anime filler season by an entirely different company. Neomuna is an empty lifeless neon city where you're always lower light power than what you need. The story is an extremely poor tutorial mission written for the new Darkness subclass of Strand. You end up with more questions and no answers to what they were building to for the last several seasons.

The attempt to bring back the challenge just made everything feel like bullet sponges, last season I had no issues running Lost Sectors and they were fun and challenging. This season everything hits hard and takes obnoxious amounts of special/heavy ammo to taken down. I haven't cleared a lost sector yet and I probably won't until it's fixed... so I'm locked out of several exotics for my two alt classes. It's like True Vault Hunter Mode from Borderlands where everything just got turned into a bullet sponge, it is not fun.

Honestly Lightfall feels like they took bad Borderlands writing and combined it with Curse of Osiris while trying to monopolize on the Cyberpunk aesthetic. The game feels broken and uninteresting.

The only fun part of the expansion and so far for Season of the Defiance has been the raid. Yes lots of complaints about it are abound, but honestly I had a lot of fun with it. My clan didn't clear the contest mode, we got stuck on the Macrocosm (3rd Encounter), but we had a blast. Post-contest mode it is quite easy once you figure out the mechanics.

I would save your money for Season of the Deep to see if it has improved... as of right now I wouldn't even let my friends get it to come play with me.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeing a new Destiny 2 Player who has only joined less than 2 month ago, It's already disappointing to me as a new player seeing on what direction the game is heading towards.

Starting with Neonuma itself, the place itself lacks a lot of content outside of the campaign. Only one part of the map has a public event, with the rest of the map lacking content besides maybe a lost sector or two. (lost sectors are still dogwater anyways)

The campaign itself is... rushed. Practically speaking you can get this done around 2-5 hours if you really wanted to, being able to quite literally cheese Calus in what was already a boring boss sequence at the end of the campaign. Most of the characters introduced are given barely any development are are generally uninteresting to me. The new enemies named Tormentors are bullet sponges and are buggy. I feel like Bungie just rushed this out at the last second and did little testing from whatever QA Team they have.

I will say that strand is a pretty interesting subclass, and Caiatl is given pretty interesting character development. The music and visuals are great, but the actual content and gameplay doesn't make this DLC worth $50.

If you really want to buy Lightfall for the content (why??), just wait for a sale.\t\t\t\t\t
