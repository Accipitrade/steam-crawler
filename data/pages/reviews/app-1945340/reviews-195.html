\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is clear that Bungie did not invest the same care and resources into this expansion. you are treated like you are meant to understand whats happening at certain points in the story, without any prior education on what the importance of things are.

Luckluster, careless, and rushed writing aside, it was still a fun and enjoyable campaign, just nowhere near being a quality one. The biggest day to day issue that we will come to experience with Lightfall is the mod system, which has been simplified down into a game of run around and try to generate orbs, with a timer on when you have to use them by before they expire. More egregious than this is the fact that so many interesting mods could have been given the time to shine, and were cut from the game. Want to get half your melee back, whilst playing the new subclass for the Titan who's entire toolkit is geared towards melee? no, that mod doesn't exist anymore, because why should it right?

Not only was the campaign poorly written and the mod system an oversimplified shell of what it was, the "new" loot is terrible. The Neomuna weapons are reissued Shadowkeep Moon weapons, and the Season of Defiance weapons are re issued from Season of the Lost. Any good perk is in the right column for about 80% of the weapons so good luck having any fun at all.

Overall this was a complete letdown of an expansion. What has been done well has admittedly been executed well, such as the load out system and improvements to activity difficulty. But adding sprinkles to a piece of crap still makes it a piece of crap.

4/10, Bungie knows they can do better and I seriously hope they understand the gravity of what they've done.\t\t\t\t\t
