Yes. It's fun to play. It's a really fun and semi-challenging campaign that I really enjoyed. Strand is a very fun subclass and way better to actually get than Stasis.

But that's really about it. Lightfall is all action, but seemingly missing a cohesive story.

The story feels downgraded from Witch Queen. I'm honestly really disappointed with how absolutely disconnected the story is in Lightfall, seeing how much of an important step in the game's story line this DLC was meant and hyped up to be. It goes by way too fast and just glosses over things really quick before you have much time to process anything really.
Although Witch Queen did have a tad bit too much dialogue and lore drops for me in ratio to action, it did actually give me time to process what was actually happening. I get that the events currently in this universe are urgent to our protagonists, but holy crap was I left just purely confused of what the hell was going on story-wise.

I hope Bungie realizes how absolutely disjointed the narrative is in Lightfall, and makes a better balance like Witch Queen for the Final Shape.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Second Strand type game.

This is not $50 (USD) worth of content. They have a seasonal plan to drip feed some additional content that will only come with the $100 version, or by purchasing individual seasons.

The campaign story is not at all compelling, and does not answer any questions you might have at the start of it.

The gameplay is fine but nothing particularly groundbreaking for Destiny. The introduction of Strand is done poorly and will rip away your current subclass just to have you play with it. And then Strand itself is ripped away for the duration of a short walk just to get it back again. It feels unnecessary for its introduction. Strand doesn't fit the theme and feels shoehorned into the plot at the very last moment.

Strand on its own is a fun subclass. But it does not make up for the $50 price tag. If $50 was for the content plus the season then I would be more on board. The campaign and Strand should have only been $25.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThey went for a classic action movie vibe and they nailed it. Nimbus' dialogue is good for a cocky rookie who is a bit dumb and a bit headstrong, love that for them. Strand feels undertuned but like it has potential. Post campaign lore is great.

The writing around the Veil and the Radial Mast could have been better. The post-campaign missions sorta flesh out stuff about the veil but I would have liked to know more about it before going on this offensive. The Radial Mast feels like a huge McGuffin. Overall, the campaign writing feels a rushed, and is a step down from Witch Queen. That being said, still enjoyable.

Buildcrafting feels good with the new system. Bungie made us aware there would be fewer options at the outset compared to before, so just be patient.\t\t\t\t\t
