\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Lightfall" is not terrible. But it isn't good either. It's just very lackluster.

In this expansion the player has to protect a thing (and it is never explained what this thing actually is) because otherwise the bad guy will do bad stuff (again, not explained what this bad stuff actually is) with it.

Well the player failed, and the bad stuff happens. Probably. Not so sure since nothing seemed to change.
At the end of the expansion I had the feeling that my involvement was completely useless and everything I did was inconsequential. My actions really didn't matter, a 2 minute cut scene would have been just as effective.

The scenery is... okay I guess. Not the grand vista like in the trailer, but again, also not terrible. It does feel very empty and devoid of any inhabitants though.

There is one thing that actually is bad though, and that is the dialog of two people you have to work with. Was really not a fan of that.

The whole expansion feels as if only half of the effort of the previous expansion (which was really good in my opinion) was spend on it.\t\t\t\t\t
