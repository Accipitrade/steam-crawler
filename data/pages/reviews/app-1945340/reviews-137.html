The attempted 80's hero vibe falls extremely flat against the extensiveness of the calamity that is supposed to be occuring. It leaves the tone disconnected from the reality of the events happening. It's also much easier than the WQ campaign, even though I did legendary.
I like the new mod system, the new input system for having loadouts saved, the new armour system is great, the quality of life changes are remarkably great but the actual campaign is a complete failure.\t\t\t\t\t
