\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWe were spoiled by With Queen incredible narrative, thus Lightfall narrative feel like a disappointment. It's all over the place, more questions than answers. Like what the hell is "The Veil"?, the characters in the campaign keep talking about it but never explained it.

Gameplay is pretty fun, the legendary campaign is a slight upgrade from Witch Queen. The Tormentors are fun to fight against, Tormentors are that type of enemies that you can't just nuke the crap out of them with something like gjallarhorn.

Strand is fun, but not that powerful compared to other sub-classes. The grappling hook ability is very fun to use, definitely tons of exploits can be made with the grappling hook although it suffers with long cooldown. So far Strand is inferior compared to other sub-classes in terms of power, maybe with more updates in the future could make Strand a viable choice for endgame contents.

Post-Lightfall campaign content is pretty cool, you get to explore the new destination Neomuna and it secrets, get new loots that are interesting especially exotics, new public events and new strike. Somehow the post-Lightfall campaign story is more interesting than the actual campaign, which makes me wonder why Bungie don't include it in the campaign instead?

The quality of life improvements in this Lightfall update are pretty nice, it's free and you don't need the DLC to access it. With the new buildcrafting changes, it makes Destiny 2 feel new and fresh in someway. You have guardian ranks which determine your mastery with the game, and commendation system that tells you how experienced you are in the game.

Overall i think Lightfall DLC is in a rough spot because how Bungie delivers the narrative in this one. Bungie marketed Lightfall soo hard that after playing it feels like a clickbait. It definitely did not live up to the hype, and it's not as good as Witch Queen. If you're a new player or returning player that is going to play Lightfall, then you're in it for a wild ride. Although if you're in it just for the gameplay and feel, i think you'll be having fun in Lightfall.

I just hope that the new Raid and Dungeons could explain more about Lightfall story, perhaps the questions about The Veil would be finally be answered.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've almost 2000 hours in Destiny as a largely solo player. I play, and love, this game for the unparalleled gunfeel, the story and engrossing lore, and those characters I've spent nearly ten years with. I was never as hyped for as a campaign as I was for Lightfall.

And yet, here we are. My first ever negative review for a Destiny product.

No spoiler bullet-point thoughts:
- Narratively empty. A HUGE step back from the Witch Queen, the narrative and characters here are perfunctory at best and downright missing at worst. It's a MacGuffin hunt where you never find out what that MacGuffin is. The rest has the feel of a mid-tier, mid-phase Marvel movie with quippy sidekicks and cringeworthy dialogue. Oh, and they turned Osiris into the Old Man Yells At Cloud[Strider] meme. It's the worst campaign in this regard since Warmind.
- Unbalanced. The campaign is either pathetically easy or teeth-grindingly frustrating. The Witch Queen campaign absolutely nailed the Legendary difficulty but here it's borderline broken. Add on a terrible checkpoint system and you've a play experience that's amongst the worst Bungie have ever delivered.
- Dead Zone. Neomuna is boring. It's empty. For some reason, it's a graphical downgrade from every other zone (just compare its flat, dull surfaces to the detail of the Dreaming City). It's full of dead-drops and confusing street layouts and the teased verticality is completely absent
- Strand-ed. Strand IN the campaign is fine. But when you unlock the REAL Strand, the Strand you'll be using for the next X years... it's a huge letdown. The campaign gives you a grapple hook on a 5 second cooldown. The actual game? That cooldown is closer to *2 minutes*. Bungie gives you the power fantasy, then they rip it away. AND you have to unlock the class separately for each character, and then each aspect and fragment after that.

Lots of words, but they don't really do justice to how disappointed I am.

A complete letdown.\t\t\t\t\t
