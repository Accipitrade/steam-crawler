\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt hurts to say this, but this ain't it. I love Destiny 2 and I'm still going to play all the upcoming seasons, but Lightfall itself and the campaign that came with it, is just mediocre at best. This was supposed to be THE moment in Destiny 2 where we finally learn about the Veil and the Witness' ultimate goal, but no. Nothing. Bungie hyped this up to be something that it isn't. I remember reading something about how Lightfall and The Final Shape were originally supposed to be just one expansion, and it shows. Nothing was explained. The tone of the story towards the end feels really weird. Did we lose? I know they say so, but it doesn't really feel like it. Lightfall has a forgettable campaign that only serves as a lead up to the actual "big moment" in Destiny 2's story, which is, hopefully, going to be The Final Shape.

Gameplay-wise this is fun as hell though. Strand can be pretty hilarious and the movement it adds is pretty cool too. Quality of life improvements are excellent, but they aren't part of Lightfall, they're free.

Please Bungie, do better. You did such a great job with Witch Queen and now you deliver this? Come on.

Should the raid add anything relevant to the story that might change my opinion, I'll update this review.\t\t\t\t\t
