Sure Lightfall has some flaws and a lot of people are constantly whining and crying about things especially how the story was written. Yeah, and you all would also whine and cry if we killed of Witness in an 8 mission campaign right of the bat at the beginning of the new expansion. Go outside, get that fresh air.

We still have a full year ahead of story-telling and weaving out its course towards the Final Shape. For me this was well-written as a prelude to the main events to come. Lightfall and the new changes are heading towards the right direction rather than making the game worse.

If you're here reading reviews seeing people butthurt, don't make assumptions from just reviews. People will whine and cry every time at everything even at tiny things they can just to be purposefully negative. Just because some people don't like it doesn't mean you won't like it as well. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to love this expansion but for a number of reasons I just can't. Lightfall is ultimately a double edged sword — on one hand, the ideas and systems put in place in this expansion are great. Guardian ranks, the new mod system, crafting changes, etc. On the other hand, the execution and the utilization of these new/changed systems nullifies any benefits they may have brought.

Guardian ranks are a good idea but the current tasks for each rank makes them trivial and meaningless. The new mod system is a step in the right direction for customization but removed half of the interesting or useful mods from the game which ultimately killed build diversity. The new campaign has interesting and fun levels, but terrible story writing that leaves much to be desired. Using strand throughout the campaign is really fun and engaging but absolutely sucks the second you don't have a 3 second cooldown on the grapple anymore.

From a gameplay perspective there's a ton of good. The new destination is interesting, looks good and has lots to do. Gaining materials to level up strand int eh patrol space as a core means of unlocking the subclass is fun. The new loadout system is basically perfect — wherever gear can be changed, it happens instantly and is easy to understand / use.

It only takes a couple of things to ruin the rest of it though. Having no good mod options because combat mods were entirely removed and consequently having to choose between outdated orb mods or kickstart mods for ability energy generation sucks — especially considering the latter don't work in most builds due to specific wording of how the mods work.

There's a lot more on my mind but these are the biggest contenders. Hopefully the season has better writing, I haven't played it yet.

tl;dr: Bad writing, dead build diversity, strand sucks outside of campaign, guardian ranks are pointless. The systems themselves are a step in the right direction though and loadouts are perfect.\t\t\t\t\t
