\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd say yes and no to the recommendation of this DLC.
Why?

Yes
Absolute yes on strand, it has brand a whole new way to play the game and is a very nice addition, literally changing how traverse or play the game, and is carrying this DLC so hard.
Grinding strand fragments is nowhere near as bad as stasis fragments. (not that this is a valid excuse)
Most new exotic weapons in this DLC specifically are very good and unique SO FAR. (I've obtained everything released this season for weapons, only one im not particularly liking is the Deterministic Chaos) Absolutely loving the Glaive, Sidearm and Strand Quicksilver Storm.
The timegate was removed from the strand fragments which is quite surprising to be honest.
There was a particular mission after the story i really liked to be honest; you had to essentially race against the timer on a sparrow to a specific checkpoint, kill enemies to increase your timer and defuse the bomb on each checkpoint. PLEASE add more sparrow activities, this sort of reminded me of sparrow racing back in Destiny 1.

No
Story is very bad, aspecially as to what was promised. (very abrupt)
Focusing the story on being a Strand tutorial was an absolutely horrible idea, ontop of straight up blue-balling you from unlocking the subclass until the very end of the campaign. I can understand it was to build story up in a specific way, but in my opinion it should have been a side mission to introduce strand to us without interrupting the story and granting us the ability to use it.
Overall the new planet is pretty damn boring in terms of liveliness.
There was ALOT of re-used maps/assets used for the story being;
Lots of pre-existing assets from previous raids.
The mission for the Exotic Glaive is quite literally the Garden of Salvation (Raid) backwards with a few gimmicks.

For the price mark; this is not worth it at all; and i can definitley understand everyones fustrations,
Personally, I am loving this DLC and having a blast but i just cant recommend this for the price.
I do understand that this is a whole years content worth, and it only being 1 week, but the main story certainly didn't deliver from the community's expectations.
So i'd probably recommend this if it was discounted by a fat 30% or something along those lines.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYes, the campaign story is a hot mess, and yes, they struck the wrong tone with Nimbus, and yes, I'm mad about it, but literally everything else is a pretty good time:

  • The campaign has some great set pieces, gameplay-wise.
  • Strand is fun. It's FUN. Swing-punch-throw-swing is a good feel. Throw on whatever gets you Wraith Mail and enjoy your new career as an unkillable live missile.
  • Neomuna is cool. It's like the Arcology on Titan if it hadn't been ruined in the collapse. Great use of vertical space, lots of secrets, good enemy density if you keep moving around. It's cool and I like it.
  • The post-campaign missions are great. They have time to explain (cough) the campaign lore, they send you through some old environments that I missed, there's a great Preservation replacement, it's all been very satisfying. They even take Nimbus in a different emotional direction that landed WAY BETTER with me.
  • The seasonal activity is really excellent. It's fun, it's engaging, it's challenging, it's in some cool environments, and it's the story that probably should have been the campaign.

That's not even touching the loadout system, or all the quality of life improvements. Don't trust the Reddit bandwagon. Lightfall is good.\t\t\t\t\t
