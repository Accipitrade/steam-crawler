The game play 8/10 strand is fun and the campaign itself was fun, although being bottle necked into using strand without any build crafting was annoying, it would have been better if they just gave you strand a third of the way into the campaign.
The raid is a 5/10.
Visuals of the raid were 10/10.
The actual "mechanics" of the raid revolving around 4 people constantly on ad clear was disappointing.
Legend lost sectors are near impossible for new players especially if they are not up to power.
Titans got nerfed into the ground recently (they are still usable but that glaive nerf to synthos was uncalled for). Heart of innermost light post nerf is still one of the best exotics, I kinda wish they would buff other titan exotics to compensate because titans don't have much.
Day 1 difficulty for most players was fine.
new build crafting system and load outs 8/10.
Trials remains painful, I remember being excited for a non flawless route for adepts (spoiler it didn't happen).
PVP and gambit players get nothing.
There is 1 new strike and the gm rotation is looking rough as the battle grounds are not balanced around being nightfalls.
Neomuna is a really pretty location that once you get your loot you will probably never go back too. In my mind a fix would just let us replay the campaign start to finish as we first got it as deleting a character is too much work.
New season 8/10 they reduced the grind and can get progress by doing any of the activities.
The new focusing options are a 7/10 great for new players.
adept focusing 2/10 neat idea but trials has not changed and for gms it takes too long, but I guess its a route for weapons not in rotation.
commendations 0/10 it should get removed from rewards and be a thing that people give you because either you played well or were nice.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReal questions is always, should you buy this? Will you enjoy it? I think the answer to that really depends on what you want from Destiny, though for the casual enjoyer of the game its a pretty mixed bag.

The story is basically impossible to follow if you have no reference to what's been going on, and even then it'll leave you with more questions than answers by the end. That's pretty concerning since this storyline is wrapping up within the next couple of years, but there is at the very least confirmation that the major questions will be followed up on in the coming seasonal releases. That being said if you're playing for a good story experience through the campaign, I don't think you'll enjoy Lightfall.

Gameplay wise though I'd say that Lightfall has been a pretty solid. Strand is a super fun subclass to play with in PvE and is the first subclass in the games history to not break PvP at launch. The grapple feels amazing, and the effects of strand on the pve sandbox are really fun to build into. You can really feel your builds firing on all cylinders with Strand. In the future I expect that people will recommend people to buy this expansion just for Strand in a similar way Beyond Light was with Stasis. If you're playing for the gunplay and power fantasy of Destiny, I think you will enjoy Lightfall.

The raid is where the package is really weird. I'd say it's worth doing just for the gear, but in terms of experience it's all over the place. If you've never raided before, I'd say that this is the most accessible raiding experience to ever come to Destiny. With the in-game LFG coming later this year I'd suspect that the raiding scene will expand with more players raiding that ever especially given a raid like Root of Nightmares. That being said, it's also incredibly easy and I wonder if it's really representative of the raid experience when instead of climbing a mountain, you're hoping over a mole hill. If you want to get into endgame of Destiny, then Lighfall is a good entry point, but might prove disappointing to enfranchised players.

Overall I'm still enjoying Lightfall, but I really wonder if that simply has more to do with Destiny being a good game overall moreso than anything tied to the Lightfall package itself (excluding the free to play updates that came with it, those were amazing and definitely improve the game).\t\t\t\t\t
