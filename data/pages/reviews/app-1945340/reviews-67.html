\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe campaign is good fun, but the story in this expansion feels as though it is nothing short of filler. The focus was in all the wrong places - who are the Cloudstriders, what is the ArkNet (?), where was the unveiling of the Veil and why did it get there? Poor Nimbus is an easy character to dislike, for their writing did not quite hit the mark, either.
Side note: please do not spread hate for non-binary folk as they are people like you and me.

The campaign could have been much, much better and the post-campaign content shows it.

The raid was much too underwhelming an experience in terms of difficulty. You can do the entire thing with only two teammates focusing on the mechanics. You can probably do it all with two players just fine. Even during the contest period, the most challenging 24 (now 48) hours of a raid's existence, it was a joke. Sub-3-hour first clear (which is much too quick for a raid's first day), high 24-hour completion rate (should've been lower had it been on-par with Vow of the Disciple), and people doing low-man challenges in the latter of the two days.
It still manages to capture the aesthetics that makes Destiny 2 very special, however. The art direction is consistently excellent.

Strand, however, was the single best addition the game has ever seen bar none. The quality of life changes that shipped with this expansion were helpful to all, from endgame-level players to newbies alike. The game has never felt better... but there were no PC performance optimizations shipped with this update.

In summary,
Fun campaign, bad story
Good post-campaign
Raid is a beautiful joke
Game got improved by a grapple mechanic
Quality of life galore

If you love Destiny for how it feels, you can surely find joy in this. If for the story, this will surely miss the mark.
This might change in the future should Bungie revisit the campaign itself and fix the dialogue to appropriately address the game world and explain all the newness introduced to us and not just Strand.\t\t\t\t\t

That being said, the raid is definitely on the easier side for experienced players. My friends and I have still enjoyed it so far.\t\t\t\t\t
