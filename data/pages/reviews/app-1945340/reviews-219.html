\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf I could give a neutral review I would.

Do not buy this expecting anywhere near a coherent story. I have more questions than answers after playing through the campaign. You can tell this was put together with a tighter deadline after they scrapped whatever "darker" story they had. The opening cutscene and ending cutscene can be spliced together to make one full normal opening cutscene. It feels like they took the opening cutscene for what was The Final Shape, cut it in half, gave us a go find the doohickey before the bad guy does story, and slapped the other half at the end.

The campaign itself is pretty good gameplay wise, with some fun standout missions and one really bad tank mission. The constant obsession over Strand during the campaign can be very annoying, as there were multiple points where I thought my guardian had finally mastered the power, and then seconds later they got too sweepy and had to go take a nap again. Strand should have been given to the player in full or in part a few missions in, with mastering it being a side quest the player can complete before finishing the campaign if they wanted to.

Strand itself is a very very fun subclass, especially on hunter. It feels a little under powered right now but it's so dang fun swinging around everywhere. A little upsetting that as of the time of writing, all the fragments that seem like they would make or break the subclass are currently time-gated unlocks for 3/7/23, with more coming after worlds first.

All in all, buy if you want a fun campaign with a pretty bad story, or if you just want to become space spider man. I really hope Bungie can take this next year to properly setup for The Final Shape, because this is not the setup I was hoping for.\t\t\t\t\t
