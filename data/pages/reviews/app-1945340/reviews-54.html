\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere is a casual solo player's takeaway on Lightfall, playing 1-2 hours daily approximately.

Story: At this point in time, I expected Bungie to clear the basics and not create more confusion. But well, they answered less and created more questions which I didn’t like. I don’t want to go into details, but Byf made a video that resonates with what I felt during the legendary campaign.

Gameplay: It’s hard playing solo legendary on all three; I died a couple of times and had to change my loadout multiple times. For the campaign, I liked it, apart from the Calaus fight, which was a bit much, like shoot, run, shoot in a sponge. But in other activities like Strike/NF/Battle Ground, I feel like it's too much, in a sense that I have to keep a certain setup to use all the time. I cannot use things I want to as my teammates might not be prepared for it. Like, I really wanted to play Strand Hunter, but the burn was void, so I had to select a void build just so that I can play BG. Also, Strike and Lost Sector rewards are still meh, or at least for me they are. Also if power level is always capped then it doesn’t make sense going beyond a certain level which is required to enter certain activity.

Strand: It is good, but I felt as a solo player in harder content, Void/Solar works better overall. I have seen all the ‘best strand builds’ and am currently using them on all three. But I feel like other classes have better synergy with exotics and mods better than Strand at the moment.

Overall, it feels a bit more grindy, and sometimes I must get LFG, or I will not be able to do it solo like I used to. But I am too deep into the game and lore now to get out, so I started saving so that I can pre-order the next expansion also.\t\t\t\t\t
