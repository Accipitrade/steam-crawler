Story: a lot of questions and no answers given/10

Okay for real though, if you're only getting this for the story (which is a huge part) you are most likely going to be disappointed, the campaign is very short (and way too overtuned on legendary imo) that the only reason it took me over 2 hours was falling off of the map 90% of the time.

However, i did really enjoy the gameplay, Strand is very fun for the campaign, Neptune looks gorgeous and the soundtrack is as goated as ever.

Is it worth the 100$ i paid? Nope, definitely not. But i'm here for the ride anyway and i still have faith in Bungie that they'll get this right with seasonal content and hopefully "The Final Shape" when it releases.

Still had fun, if you can skip the campaign and can skip having strand as well, just skip buying the DLC and probably just get the seasons. Hopefully the Raid is fun and the seasonal content at least gives us some more story since it was very minimal in the actual DLC.

Recommended only if you're really really dedicated to Destiny 2 as a game and wouldn't like to really miss out on anything (although you won't be really missing out on much).\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a D1 and D2 Veteran with over 2k hours, I must say this is one of the more disappointing DLC's released. I was so pumped for this after seeing how great Bungie did with The Witch Queen. Where do I begin?

Pros:
The new public space is very well done, has a great atmosphere to the game.
Strand is a blast to use.
The campaign was FUN, that's all.

Cons:
The campaign answered almost no questions that it asked.
Poor character development.
The campaign is insanely short, It took me roughly 6 hours on legendary and that was accounting for breaks and whatnot.
The amount of content that came with the DLC was MIN-NUH-MUHL.

Truly heartbreaking as this game is one of my passions. Bungie had an incredible opportunity to blow our socks off, and they fell flat on their face.

Overall, considering I spent $99.99 on the Annual pass version and accounting for the price of the seasons included.....I'd feel safe saying this base DLC should be about $19.99.
