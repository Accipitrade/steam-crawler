The bad: History wise, it was a kick in the teeth. It felt rushed, unfinished, and yes, we can tell that it felt that way. The main quest felt like a poor side story, optional, which has little to no impact into the main history.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSPOILERS!

First and foremost, the story is straight trash. We waited 10 years, and the chase is still going on. Its like they ran out of ideas and are making us chase a ghost or something that doesn't exist until they can come up with something good. Instead we chased strand the whole game. Let's be honest, Destiny campaign, in general, most of it was trash. The cut scenes, music and visuals are what make the game. I can say I was spoiled by Witch Queen Campaign, hyping all this oxygen into a balloon just for it to fart out.

Surprisingly, the new mod system is great. I'm having fun with the new content. Strand is cool, but I don't think it was fair that they forced it on us throughout the entire game. Gameplay is still top tier, I like that they added custom build slots to create and save builds. New ways to stun champions, not having to equip seasonal artifacts. No more elemental mods, slots or armor. Gambit is still forgotten like a stepchild, but I play it. I just hope they can learn from this result, not as a loss but a lesson. We, as a community want other returning guardians to come back to a game where it gave them the sense of excitement coming home from work or school to play. It's not a bad game in general, it's just that it was overhyped for disappointment.\t\t\t\t\t
