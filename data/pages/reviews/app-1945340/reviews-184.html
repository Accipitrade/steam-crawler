\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEveryone crying that the story was bad is chronically online. The DLC is great. The story felt like a Marvel movie.

Sure, they should've went a little more in depth with details about The Veil but people seem to forget that this is a whole year of Lightfall we are getting. It can get explained in late seasons this year or they might even be saving it for The Final Shape.

I only had one serious complaint about the story and it had to do with the Cloud Strider, Rohan. I genuinely wish they would've let him live longer, given his character more development and then killed him off in The Final Shape DLC. He was such an awesome character and I felt like he had so much more potential. His death felt so anticlimactic and I felt like he could've came back to Earth to help with the next few seasons and then in The Final Shape his death would've felt way more impactful.

Other than that, I have zero issues.

The story was great! 6 years of us chasing down Calus and we FINALLY see him in the flesh and get to defeat him for good this time. A WHOLE NEW SUBCLASS that felt so refreshing and fun! A whole new revamped buildcrafting system. A GUARDIAN RANK SYSTEM. Commendations!! An amazing looking new location which is so much fun to explore!!

There is so much I can say about this DLC, I love it so much and if you aren't a crybaby who only believes what the negative people say about things, I think you will enjoy this, too!

Give things a chance and form your own opinions. Have faith in what the developers have planned. Once again, we have a WHOLE YEAR OF CONTENT planned for this year ahead of us which will explain a lot of missing bits we have questions about. If it doesn't, please remember: this is a video game. They added a "Touch Grass" emote for a reason haha\t\t\t\t\t
