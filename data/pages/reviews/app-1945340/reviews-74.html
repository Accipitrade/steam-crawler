\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of a few reviews I have ever written in my life.
Lightfall is not worth more then 10 dollars. Hell i doubt its even worth that at this point.
I got my friend into Destiny a week before LightFall came out, and it was the best time playing with them.
Running Witch Queen, showing them Mod builds, and how powerful you can be.

LightFall took that, and ran it through mud. So stay with me while I recap everything since the raid is finally out and contest is done.

First, The story.
Story? should I even call it that? What happened? I don't know. Whats the Veil? I don't know. What does the Witness want with it? I don't know. Where did the Witness go at the end? I don't know.
Every mission you're guided by the hand, to kill enemies and test out strand. That's it.
The Witness came to our home, killed people and what happened? we ♥♥♥♥♥♥ off to another planet to stop a sad sack of pancakes getting the Macguffin called the Veil. Everyone. I mean mean EVERYONE says how important the veil is or how powerful it is. NO ONE TELLS YOU ANY THING MORE.
There's another Macguffin which does something but who knows what, it doesn't matter cause old dude who said hes one year from dying, kills himself to destroy it. Now you're should feel sad cause he died, Wait are you supposed to? Cause EVERYONE MOVES ON IN ONE CUT SCENE. NO ONE CARED THAT HE DIED. so i don't.
Every mission is just kill these guys, use strand, oh no your body can't take the power of strand. Mission end.
Cant use strand? lets do a training cut scene and now you can use strand better.
Eight missions. Every single mission is just a strand how to.
BTW what is the witness even doing while we are on this planet ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ around? chilling in space? Doesnt attack us or anything?
Legend mode on the story is a joke and you can run it over in 8 hours if your even somewhat good. (The story was harder then the CONTEST version of the raid btw)

Second Gameplay.
Its destiny. That's it. Have you played destiny before? you know the game play, its not really changed.
Mod rework is a 10 steps down then what it was in Witch Queen. I understand its for new players to understand building and bla bla bla. Build crafting took time to learn, but was worth it at the end. You went from some damage to destroying content. It wasn't even hard to learn. Watch one video on how to do it and boom you now can solo "end game" content.
Once you're done with the story, Strand is given to you but ofc you need to unlock more of it. Thats fine. Strand makes every champion in the game worthless. Are they even worth keeping at this point?
Strand is fine. Thats it really.
Also Legend mode anything is just hard cause ♥♥♥♥ you Bungie wants it to be hard. You are -20 levels in legend lost sector, something YOU HAVE TO DO in order to get new exotics. some thing that you have to FARM for hours to get ONE good roll. you now 4x the time and effort for nothing.

Third. The Raid.
Its not a raid at this point. Its a strike with more Loot. Its a Joke of content. Theres no point spending money and time on something that is easier to do then walking around the new planet.
Contest is a joke now. No Hate to the people who did Contest for the first time. Im glad you joined us.
Contest is supposed to be the one content End game players look forward to. I dont even wanna run it again. EVER.
Worlds First clear was done in 2.5 hours. Kings fall, a raid that came back and was mostly the same, normal mode took longer. This whole raid is a joke. Not even a joke. At least with a joke, Theres a reason for it.
More people cleared this raid on contest mode then ANY OTHER DAY ONE IN ALL OF DESTINY 1 AND 2.
Glad those players got the emblem, but this isnt "end game" content. Legend Lost Sectors are harder then CONTEST MODE. The whole raid is have 4 people AD clear and 2 people play connect the dots, till you gotta do damage, then reset.
Also Cap your FPS as low as playable, cause they do more damage to you the more frames you have.

Forth. The World.
Neomuna is a cold and worthless world. Thats it really. The NPC's are just glowing lights to save bungie the chore of making models and placing them. Only one new character and hes just a copy and paste of any MCU hero. Delete him and nothing changes. Fighting enemies in patrol is harder then the raid. You are under light for EVERY enemy on this planet for no reason other cause ♥♥♥♥ you. Theres a ship that just one shots you while you play. You have to sink so much ammo into just for it to come back and one shot you later. Nothing about it is fun.

Yea. 2.5/10. Witch Queen was 40 dollars. This is 50.
I lack the words to explain how bad this DLC is. I lack the words to express my sadness about LightFall.
I sat and thought, This is bad but this could save it. I did that with the story, the guns, the gameplay, the raid.
I just can't anymore.
Don't Trust Bungie anymore. Don't Trust them to tell a story they built. Don't Buy Final Shape.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA solid 8/10 in my opinion, with witch queen being 8.5 and beyond light being a 6. The gameplay changes and additions completely outweigh any negative aspect about the writing. The story is something you will play a couple times and visit again for the weekly pinnacle, while the massive sweeping changes to the mods, loadouts, champions, guns, difficulty, 2-day contest mode raid and far more than I'm listing while keep this game going for the long haul. While it might seem like a hefty price, it also comes with the season which is still ongoing as of writing this, along with a superb mission that rewards you a unique void glaive that came out of nowhere with no announcement. Along with the raid which alone I'd say is worth at least half of the expansions price as Bungie yet again doesn't miss when it comes to tone, atmosphere, music, art and general direction when it comes to how each raid is so uniquely different from one another. At first I was completely disappointed that we were heading to yet another pyramid ship for a raid setting but that disappointment vanished the second I saw the colorful mix of white tye-dye aesthetics forcefully blended with the darkness artstyle we've grown accustomed to. Not to even mention the new locations art, music and atmosphere so uniquely different from anything seen in destiny but somehow manages to merge itself into the universe with only minor hiccups narrative wise. As a fan since the beginning, my honest opinion would be its strongly worth the money, but anyone who can see any of the flaws as a deal breaker it's not something I can really refute other than tell you to give it a shot when it comes on sale later down the line.\t\t\t\t\t
