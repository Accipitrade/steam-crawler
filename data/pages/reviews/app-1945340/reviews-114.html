\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think overall this expansion does deserve more praise than its given. Yes the story itself is probably one of the most disappointing things to come out of this franchise since the first year of destiny 2. This expansion is coming off the high that The Witch Queen set and that bar was not even close to being met, at least when it comes to the story telling within the campaign itself.

I beat the campaign solo legendary in around 7-8 hours, and those 7-8 hours were a ton of bombastic crazy fun. The game play elements introduced this time around, along with the vast quality of life improvements to the game are some of the greatest this game has needed for so so long, for new and old players alike. It's just a shame that the poor story telling muddys this expansion so much. I would still rate it higher than Shadowkeep & Beyond Light. If you are a new or returning player thinking about sinking back into the game, I would consider jumping back in maybe once the dust settles here and the expansion has had some time to mature. At least consider it if its on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
