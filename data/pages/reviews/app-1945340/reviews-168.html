\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing Destiny for 7 years, and this is the first time I've really felt inclined to review an expansion. That is because Bungie has somehow completely undone the strides they've taken in recent seasons to improve the narrative.

The gameplay has been and still is decent, and strand is pretty fun to use, but the entire point of this expansion's campaign should have been to address the consequences and severity of this apocalyptic event they've been building to for almost a decade. Instead it's just a drawn out and completely unfocused introduction to the newest subclass which has seemingly appeared out of no where and is apparently super important in taking down Calus in some way they DON'T EVEN EXPLAIN which is a downright lazy way to shove this into the story.

Apart from strand, the primary focus of the campaign is to stop the Witness from getting the Veil. Despite 8 story missions, we still know ABSOLUTELY nothing about what the veil is, how it's being used by the people of Neomuna, where it came from, what the Witness wants with it, and most importantly wtf the Witness did with it at the end???

The most pathetic part of all this is that we're told by every character that we lost despite there being LITERALLY ZERO consequences for us and no indication that things are any worse than when we started this expansion. Supposedly this is the farthest the Witness has ever gotten despite trying this once before during the infamous First Collapse which has been constantly referred to as the most devastating blow to humanity ever. This is apparently the Second Collapse, but with us losing this time and somehow the only "blow" to humanity was losing a few pilots and getting a handful of citizens held hostage by trivial Shadow Legion henchmen which isn't even the campaign story, it's the seasonal story.

I can't stress enough how much of a horrible disappointment the Lightfall campaign was. I honestly don't recommend getting back into Destiny for this. Just wait until the later seasons and the Final Shape I guess. Maybe then you'll at least have 10% of a clue what's going on\t\t\t\t\t
