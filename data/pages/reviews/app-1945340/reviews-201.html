\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're the kind of person who just wants to run around and shoot a gun, you'll probably be fine. But after Witch Queen and even Season of the Seraph, the story content was awful. Yes, there's still more to go before the last expansion, but the campaign brought up more questions than it answered. It might not have even answered any questions at all, because it seems like none of the characters asked about anything at any point. It felt more like a half-baked vehicle to deliver a new subclass than an actual piece of this fascinating story that's been developing for years. I can't even give my full opinion on Strand because half of the Fragments are arbitrarily locked for another week in addition to the ones usually gated by the raid completion. Also, despite being a genuine fan of Cayde-6 and Failsafe, who people are comparing them to, I found Nimbus to be annoying and unlikable. That fist-bump scene...no thanks. Maybe in a year this will just feel like a weird interlude, but having a massive build-up to THE MAIN ANTAGONIST IS HERE and then to go about it the way that they did where absolutely nothing comprehensible happens feels really, really bad.

The armor mod system was simplified, which is great for new players, but functionality was severely limited as a result. A lot of variety is gone, and some interactions that were fun and interesting just no longer exist. Orb generation is slightly more difficult than spawning Elemental Wells (at least in a fireteam when you're competing for kills), there's no Seeking Wells equivalent so you're forced to run around to pick them up, and naturally you no longer get the ability energy that Wells used to provide. In reality it's just a way to reduce ability uptime.

Guardian Ranks was a great idea on paper, being able to see how experienced someone is at a glance, but it's been poorly executed. I have not seen a single person who isn't rank 6. Ranks 1-5 are incredibly simple, which I'm sure is great for the sake of a tutorial, but it means that people who have been playing since day 1 and people who installed the game a week ago or even those who played for a couple days 3 years ago are all the same rank. And with everyone being reset to rank 6 each season, it's a poor baseline that doesn't really show how much a fellow player knows.

Commendations...I like the system, but being severely limited to which "cards" can be given, only being able to give 1 of each per game, and not being able to commend people who have returned to orbit (like most people do when an activity is finished), makes it fall flat.

I sure hope The Final Shape or even the seasons in between are able to make up for this. If this is how the saga is going to end I'm going to be very sad.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR; It's not a masterwork, but the game play is awesome and I'm having lots of fun.

True, the story of the campaign is not the infinity war epic crossover that some people were expecting. But if I'm honest, I don't play destiny for the story; I play it for the game play and Bungie absolutely knocked it out of the park from a game play perspective. Strand is incredibly fun to use, the campaign was the perfect mix of feeling powerful and still being challenged, and the new location is just perfect! Couple that with engaging post-campaign quests and seasonal activities and you get an expansion that is truly a great time.

Is it worth $50? For me, absolutely. Playing with strand is truly something else. It is finally living up to my hunter-go-fast-dodge-things desire that I have had since the beginning of the franchise! I feel like I am rediscovering so many fun activities because of the mobility that strand provides. I will definitely put more than 50 hours into this expansion for sure, especially once the raid drops. From the looks of it, the seasonal content will be a blast as well!\t\t\t\t\t
