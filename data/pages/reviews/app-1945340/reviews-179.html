\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a sad one to write really, i have been a Destiny player for a long time so im used to ups and downs... for example post Forsaken it was rough, Shadowkeep was pretty bad and lacking content but we stuck around and then came beyond light which added improvements but still overall lacking.... Then Witch Queen came out and it was fantastic! It made me very optimistic for the future of Destiny but sadly we are now here.... Probably one of the most frustratingly bad expansions yet... The story was extremely disappointing and rather misleading which ended in (no surprise) a cliffhanger which means another year of waiting....

I will give Bungie credit, the new gameplay mechanics, strand and worlds are very pretty.... New characters however? dear god they are annoying...

Right now i cannot recommend this expansion, especially with the price increase... as of writing this the raid has not been out nor any dungeon (Which btw we got in Shadowkeep lol) so ill edit it to reflect that.....

Cons
- Story is extremely frustrating, boring and not fulfilling.
- Ends in a cliffhanger
- Its a Calus expansion not a Witness one
- Forces strand during the campaign, its annoying
- Terrible voice acting by on of the characters
- Literally recycled previous season into the expansion

Pros
- Strand is pretty nice and different...
- New engine updates for destruction
- I can break glass now!
- Neat amount of new weps
- The season itself alongside is good

The sad truth is i think this year is going to be a rough one, both a bad taste from the example but being confirmed by the Devs the next 2 seasons will be similar to previous ones with boring gameplay loops... Stay safe guardian and put your wallet away... wait for it to be on sale or just wait, i hate to say this after Witch Queen but it is what it is.\t\t\t\t\t
