\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've always hated the parkour in destiny 2 because I feel like it achieves difficulty by playing to the games weakness: Jump physics. The jumping mechanics have always frustrated me because of their inconsistency. Sometimes while falling if you press space it stops your downward momentum and sends you floating forward, while other times it gives you no forward momentum at all and simply slows your fall. And while you are floating in the air, enemy shots knock you off course, sending you into the many bottomless pits that litter every single level in the game. And then there's the problem with steps. If you run into a step or small obstacle on the ground, you can't simply step over it or up onto it like a normal person would, no, it blocks you like a wall and you have to jump to get over it, even if its only a foot tall. And if you're running down a hill and there's a tiny step down, the game considers you to be falling when you go off of it, so if you press jump, you don't actually jump into the air, you simply start floating forward into the pit you were trying to jump over. And in case I hadn't mentioned, this game is FULL of pits. It's like the level designers get bonuses based on many times players fall into pits.

So imagine my horror when I discovered that the lightfall campaign introduces new mechanics that fling you wildly and uncontrollably into the distance and then built a bunch of parkour levels just to ensure that you spend hours falling into pits. Even when you get past a long parkour section and reach a big multi wave gun battle, a death doesn't take you back to the beginning of the battle, it takes you back to the beginning of the previous parkour section. They REALLY REALLY want you to fall into as many pits as possible. It's literally torture.

Combine this punishingly trolly level design with a worthless story and annoying characters and you have lightfall. I don't even think I'm going to finish the campaign because if they're doubling down on the horrible parkour then there's just nothing left here for me to enjoy.\t\t\t\t\t
