\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo we did a thing and the Witness, Calus and all his little baddies did a thing and apparently we lost because the characters that weren't even present in the story said so. Every character knows what's happening but won't tell us like we aren't apart of this story. This is the 9th year to a 10 year story, and we have no idea what is happening. We don't know where it is heading come The Final Shape. It feels like a story that was stripped back so badly that we ended up halfway into Act 1 of a 3-act story.
Whether it was Bungie that wanted to give us more context to what happened in the main expansion through seasonal story content, it is embarrassing to think this is what we got for a MAJOR expansion, and a lead up to the end of a 10-year story.

Story wise, it is the weakest in the Destiny 2 expansions - we spend most of the campaign learning the new darkness subclass and takes up so much of the story missions, you reach the end without even knowing it and leave not understanding what we did see in terms of the narrative. Hence, pacing is very poor.

Gameplay-wise, so far it is a good change but also just feels like the start of their idea of innovating the mod and gear system. The new subclass is okay and has a better upgrade path than Stasis introuced in Beyond Light. The Neomuna world is not as great as Bungie hyped it up to me but if you expected more, you will be let down.

So, with all that, do you want to feel disappointed, confused, lost and uncertain of the future? Find something else because Lightfall doesn't stop there in terms of hyping a real push in the narrative for it to barely get its foot off the starting line.

I have been with Destiny since it first came out in 2014. Throughout it's journey, we saw lots of ups and downs, and we have come a very long way since then. We were excited and immersed in the lore and world that Bungie created and were expecting great things for their planned idea with the Light and Dark Saga. But its 2023 and we are near its end, and we are only left confused and unfulfilled. This is disheartening but I do not want to lose hope on it, even if that is a gamble in itself right now.

We will have to see how they progress the story in the seasonal context throughout the year, but this is not a good start and I can't blame any Destiny fan if they are worried for the future of Destiny.\t\t\t\t\t
