\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis expansion? Akin to eating at a expensive dinner and everything being delicious, but the main course, that was a pile of scat.

The writing and storytelling that we received does show something that existed previously, but the current iteration just exemplifies that a simple poor decision can make something decent into something pretty bad.

Whatever happened to the storytelling and its development, really showcased that the writing direction was shifted, and the person who had such poor decision to change most things about it, really should not be in the position they're currently in.
However, Bungie had enough chances in the time they definitely had to work with the storytelling and its adaptation of Strand into the campaign to make it expansion worthy, instead of having Strand be cool but shoehorned.

Everything else is definitely pretty good: campaign gameplay, Strand subclass, patrol area, post-campaign content, seasonal content, balance and gameplay changes-- most things that doesn't include the campaign storytelling.

That said, negative review because the campaign could've been something else MUCH better, yet it wasn't, and it gives worry to a lot of people in regards to Final Shape and incoming seasonal stories, which I hope nothing worse happens.

Also pray for every PvP player's soul for this first month of the expansion. :pray:\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCampaign doesn't know what it wants to be. Serious? Lighthearted? Fast? Slow? The solar system is about to get wrecked and for some reason we're making friends and joking around, but also, RUSH RUSH RUSH!!! What's going on?!?! I don't know. The devs probably don't know, maybe someone can figure it out and tell them.

As for the legendary campaign, difficulty is mostly okay, until the last couple of missions. Where I just can't help but wonder if they even playtested it. New ability is introduced by drip feed through the campaign instead of just letting you try it and unlock it right away, which, I thought we learned was bad from Beyond Light, but I guess not. I regret spending 50 dollars on this.

I wanted to like this, a lot of mechanic changes were good. But, when everything else is mid to bad at best, I can't really say it justifies more than maybe 20-25.\t\t\t\t\t
