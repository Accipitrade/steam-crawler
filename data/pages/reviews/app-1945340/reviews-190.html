\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlease, for the love of god, don't give the overly negative reviews any merit. At the time of writing, the DLC is barely more than a couple days old, and the Destiny 2 community is already in revolt. I admit the DLC so far is underwhelming, but again, its super young. Its the nature of live service games to start off on a bad foot, but more often than not, Bungie manages to fix most issues.

People think the story is aggressively bad. I disagree. Its VERY VERY MID. Not BAD. While the story itself is a significant step down from The Witch Queen, the gameplay (I played through the legendary campaign solo) is on point. Nimbus is a pain point for the vocal minority of the community, I think he's very endearing. They wrote him to be a cheesy, happy-go-lucky rookie who is just trying to make light of a bad situation. Strand is apparently underwhelming. This one is twofold. One, that is easily fixed with future balance patches and with the release or potential rework of many fragments. Two, HARD DISAGREE. STRAND IS EASILY THE MOST FUN I'VE HAD WITH A SUBCLASS SINCE SUNBRACERS.

Overall, hold off for now if you aren't desperate to get in. Give Bungie time to find and fix the issues the community has, and THEN pick it up. So this review will be a recommend from me. I just hope that Bungie is smart enough to separate the more level-headed players from the people who just want to hate.\t\t\t\t\t
