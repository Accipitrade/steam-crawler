\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI LOVED THIS EXPANSION!!!!! There were so many different parts to this that I loved. There are some faults but I would say that this is a BEAUTIFUL Expansion.

pros:
-This expansion brought us a new power, STRAND(IT CHANGES HOW YOU PLAY THE GAME A TON!!!!!!!!)
-New guns and shaders, the new guns are WAY cool looking and the perks you can get on them are quite interesting and make you think about how you want to maybe use them in each encounter.
-MOD SYSTEM REVAMPED= the mod system has been overhauled and let me say that it has been a MIND BREAKER trying to figure out how to use mods again, I am reading through and slowly starting to piece things together, but OVERALL the system has been simplified compared to what it was before, and mods now make more sense in going to ALL armor pieces and not just specific ones with a certain element type.
-A NEW CAMPAIGN!!! This campain was BRILLIANT in its introduction to a new area, some new characters, and to helping setup the grand finally for FINAL SHAPE!!! Some parts of the story are still left to be wondered about BUT that is usually what happends when something new is added.
-SAVED LOUDOUTS!! You have no idea how much time this has saved! I love being able to save my loudouts now instead of having to memorize which armor pieces has which mods and are for which roles in raids, crucible, or just roaming around in strikes.

cons:
-it costs money.......BUT I am happy to support this AMAZING studio, they have a FANTASTIC game and I want to continue to help them make amazing content LIKE THIS ONE!!!!
-story was a bit short, BUT they introduced some new items and some more BIG THINGS that we need to explore and fully understand. So there is a lot to understand or learn here in the next year before final shape comes out. I hope that some more things are explored and expounded upon. NOT EXPLICITLY, but with some nuance so that the players, me, has to use my brain a bit to try and pick up what is being said, or written between the lines.
-no other cons, just a great expansion\t\t\t\t\t

-Legendary campaign is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ amazing
-Strand is a fun new subclass and OH BOY it feels like being a grapple pilot in titanfall 2 sometimes!
-LOTS of new, non-campaign content to explore
-new seasonal activity is a blast so far!
-Neomuna is BEAUTIFUL.
-Campaign encounters, especially bosses, are challenging, fun, and fairly well balanced.


CONS:
-The story doesn’t make much sense.

Strand
