The story of the Lightfall campaign falls short because it feels like a non-essential sidequest, but lore is still good, the raid is great, and the gameplay changes are excellent.

I couldn't imagine going back to WQ after getting used to the new loadout system and subclass verbs stunning champs and the difficulty changes to activities and patrol spaces. I personally enjoyed the campaign missions a lot, the environments and the big setpiece encounters are amazing and made replaying the legendary campaign on all characters actually fun. The Strand subclasses started off slow but ended up becoming extremely powerful and by far the most fun subclass in the game with tons of buildcrafting and synergy.

Destiny as a game is headed in a great direction, but the narrative has to pick up the pace in the upcoming seasons to start expanding and explaining the Witness' intentions, humanity's response, and the Veil.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEven though this expansion was intended to be something amazing, it ended up being just placeholder content. However, its honestly not as bad as I thought it was going to be.

This was the expansion that I expected to HIT, I expected a LOT from it, coming face to face with The Witness, new darkness subclasses, insane raids and dungeons, crazy weapons and whatnot. This wasn't the case though, it was just a placeholder until The Final Shape comes out, however I think that Bungie made it work well. Ill make a few points in a sectioned list below.


Campaign

This, if i'm being honest, was kind of a letdown at first. A completely new planet with a futuristic almost vapor-wave type city where on the first mission you're given a taste of this new power that we have no clue about, it kind of just showed up. you keep discovering more and more of this power without any idea of how it works or where it came from, until it's revealed that it's some kind of byproduct from "The Veil". Awesome, now, what is the veil? well, at first, no one knew. All we knew was that it was some kind of thing that was similar to the traveler. You would think that if its similar to the traveler it would be a HUGE deal, we need to find out what this thing is quick! Well, actually, no you don't, all you need to know is that its good, and that the bad guys want it for something, and that it gives you funny green strings to absolutely decimate anything instantly, and hey! Funny rope swinging is fun! Anyways, nothing was explained at all and it reasonably upset pretty much everyone that cared even a little about storytelling. Luckily, they recovered from this with various lore logs, more missions, and other things. The campaign itself was pretty fun, on legendary mode it definitely was harder, and at the end of each mission you really felt like you earned that extra loot, that was a great feeling. Rather than just being thrown exotics and over powered guns, you really worked for them and it felt like it. Plus at the end of each mission I got a bunch of upgrade modules and enhancement cores which was a huge plus from last expansion where you mainly got a bunch of gear that you would most likely throw away pretty quick. Overall ill give it a 5.6/10 for story and a 8.9/10 for content.


Strand

I play hunter only really, but I've tried strand on all classes ill rate each of the classes and give my thoughts
on it.

Hunter - easily a 9.6/10, extra grenade charge, homing dart, suspending dives. Very fun to use, while also dealing absolutely massive amounts of damage. With certain builds this class becomes my favorite by far. throw on just a few mods, and either radiant dance machines or the 6th coyote with the shackle grenade and you become the absolute peak of ad clear. 2 suspending grenades along with potentially 3 more suspending dives from radiant dance machines but typically 2, and you get 4-5 dives, as well as having an aspect that gives you your class ability back for killing suspended enemies? My god, I haven't had this much fun in a WHILE. grapple builds are great too, see all those people well skating or shatter skating but its too hard to learn? Just throw on a few mods and boom, you can go pretty far with just that, its not AS good, but its great if you either don't feel like using stasis hunter just to skate. Maybe not the highest DPS subclass, but easily one of the most fun and you can make loads of different builds for it. Only problem I had was the dart, doesn't do great damage and it can be a little hard to catch. However in season 21 its being fixed, 55% more PvE damage, longer range, more energy refunded for hits and kills, and some other tweaks to make it easier to catch. After that hits I would give it a 10/10 easily.

Warlock - Basically a bunch of mini nova bombs, it does good damage, arcane spike is pretty fun too, you get 3 charges with it which makes it nice. Threadlings spawning from your rift is also really nice. basically, you turn into a little worm god. Personally, I just like it overall, not too much I can say other than its just fun. 8.8/10 just because 1. well skating lol and 2. feels like there could be improvements such as some cooler fragments or something, pretty solid though.

Titan - I kinda just feel like its stasis, but green, and instead of crystals, its just strings. still fun though, you can have suspending barricades which are great in high difficulty activities for quick shielding and to suspend whatever is shooting you. Very visually interesting with the knives on your hands. Melee ability is just another dashing attack, pretty basic but still cool. I would like to see cooler titan supers in general, they just all feel kind of basic, just different elemental punches, or some hammers and a bubble. I would like to see something ranged like the hammers again, titans don't have to be only punches, i'd like to see something just insane. (can't think of anything specific rn, its getting late) Regardless, feels fun to use, pretty powerful, ill give it a 8.3/10.


Weapons

The new strand weapons feel very unique, yet I cant help but feel like the stasis and strand weapons are getting a little too far from the light weapons, in the sense of they just feel more powerful with their cool abilities, they're not in reality, the meta weapons at this moment are almost all light based, but I would like to see a little more abilities to flow with 3.0 classes more. I have seen some like jolt weapons, volatile rounds, and some solar doing scorch damage, but I can't explain it well, I kinda just wish there were more gimmicks and tricks to them. Like with solar make a perk on the gun that could be like the new raid shotgun, landing a bunch of hits on an enemy could make them have a solar explosion, cant say much about void weapons, they feel nice to use with volatile rounds, or do something with arc like thunderlord, landing rapid precision hits could strike the enemy with lightning. I may be speaking way out of my like here, those kind of powers could be too over powered, but overall new weapons ill give a 7.2/10. Solid, but I feel like there could be better.

(side note, I recently went into a strike using old sunset weapons, and I REALLY miss some of them. I can understand that some may have been too good for their own good, *cough* *cough* mountaintop, recluse, randy's throwing knife, and various others *cough*, sorry, but I can see how some became a little TOO good, but for ones like black armory weapons, they weren't entirely too over powered, I was using The Ringing Nail and it honestly just felt like any other auto rifle, but the sound of the gun, not to mention the ~looks~ of them... all ill say is I really hope they consider bringing some of those guns that weren't OP back.)

Exotics - As for armor, its armor, I mean, It helps with your new strand subclasses, they're cool. 8/10
But as for weapons, these things are really cool. A heavy ammo GLAIVE??? That was COMPLETELY broken and would MELT bosses? AND freeze hoards of enemies? A shotgun wielding stasis AND solar? that can freeze or ignite enemies? and does big damage? A sidearm that tracks your enemies? An auto rifle that is also a grenade launcher and homing rocket shooting arsenal? These things are very fun, love them 10/10.


The Raid

Oh yeah, personally, this might be one of my new favorite raids. The huge areas for skating through, visuals, weapons, armor, and that exotic, just unbelievable. not much to say about it, just a VERY solid raid. 9.5/10.

OVERALL, TL;DR, WHATEVER

I can recommend this expansion, its very fun, and with the events to come such as potentially titan coming back and a new dungeon there, as well as guardian games and whatnot, and this is only the first season, I can hope that this is only gonna get better and better toward the final shape where its all gonna be the biggest era of destiny 2. 8.5/10. I won't be seeing sun for the next 20 years.\t\t\t\t\t
