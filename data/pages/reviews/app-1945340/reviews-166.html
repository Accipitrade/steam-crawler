\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've seen Lightfall get a lot of hate, but I actually really liked the DLC. Listening to the devs interviewed about it, I think they did a great job executing what they envisioned for it.

I played through the story with a fireteam on legendary, and I'd personally recommend doing this campaign that way. The devs wanted the ledgendary campaign to be a challenge, and compared to my experiences with Beyond Light and even the Witch Queen at times, it certainly was. It was frustrating, but it was powerful.

The music team did an incredible job breathing new life to what we think makes Destiny music Destiny music.
The artists did a splendid job with the new world design. Neomuna is so different from what Destiny has been before. It's a breath of fresh air, and I think it's well done.

Ofc, this DLC was released recently at the time of this review, so there's still things they need to work out. Bungie has been getting a lot of ideas from the community on how to improve the new subclass, and with time I'm sure it will only grow and improve on what I think is an already solid design. I don't think room for improvement should be deciding factor in whether to purchase this DLC or not. Updates will come, there will be fixes. Overall, it's worth it to me- the vision was well executed and this DLC does a great job of expanding and shaping a new perspective of what makes Destiny what it is.

I'm excited to see where Bungie takes the story next!\t\t\t\t\t
