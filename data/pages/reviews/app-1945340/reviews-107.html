\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood: There are some great things about Lightfall. The new subclass, Strand is great and shakes up at this point a fairly stale ability meta. The movement and abilities are really enjoyable. The new destination is interesting and lore bits that we get about Neomuna are really neat and help to build out the world a bit more. The Quality of Life changes are also long overdue and pretty well implemented. There is still room for improvement, but its still pretty good.

Bad: The campaign was just... I have no words. I am an original D1 beta player, so I have been playing Destiny for a very long time and have done every expansion. Destiny has had its hits and misses to be sure, but this expansion was on another level of bad. After the launch of Witch Queen, I really thought Bungie had finally nailed good story telling. With all the build up through Witch Queen and its seasons, I was really looking forward to Lightfall.

Suffice to say, I was incredibly disappointed. The pacing was rushed, we ended up with more questions than answers, Nimbus was incredibly cringe, especially this being the penultimate entry into the Light and Dark saga. We are standing on the precipice of annihilation, and this is what Bungie delivers? I think they went too hard for the Marvel style quippiness and it just does not track.

This is a bad point for both new and returning players to rejoin the franchise. I can't recommend it to anyone. Sadly, the story issues can't be fixed with patches. It's just bad and half cooked. Dunno what else more to say. I was really looking forward to this campaign, and it just failed on so many points.\t\t\t\t\t
