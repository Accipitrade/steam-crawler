\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBungie has managed to build a time machine because somehow we've gone back in time to 2019 and Shadowkeep has just released.

History repeats itself yet again where we have a great expansion Forsaken/Witch Queen followed up by an absolute disaster in the form of Shadowkeep/Lightfall.

The Story is awful and a complete nothing burger. What is the veil? What is the radial mast? Why are they important? What did the Witness even do to the traveller? We have no ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ clue about any of this.

The cloudstriders may as well not exist.
[] Rohan exists purely to be killed off. We barely get to know him so we don't care when he dies. []

Nimbus is awful. His city is being invaded and destroyed by the Witness and all he can say is "cowabunga my dudes, time to own this Calus guy with your green stringy strings"

He feels like a Marvel character spouting off quips being ha ha funny and wacky when the plot should be serious. This is meant to be the start of the second collapse,

Osiris won't shut up about needing to hurry to get to the veil but we spend half of the campaign pissing about learning strand.

Strand is fun to use but it's very underwhelming compared to the other subclasses in terms of power and is basically impossible to use in anything but the most trivial content.

The grapple hook has a minute plus cooldown when really it should be like 20 seconds. Best hope you dont slightly clip the top of an object otherwise it will instantly cancel and go on cooldown.

The grapple hook melee is a bit wonky to use and sometimes doesn't even work. Outside of the most trivial content it is basically an express ticket to dying instantly.

Fragment acquisition is still time gated just like stasis only it's slightly less annoying to earn them.

For warlock the melee is complete ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, you have three charges for the melee but the only time you will ever have all three charges is at the start of a mission or with a rally banner
You either need to get all the charges back at once or get them much quicker.

The new armor modding system is 1 step forward 2 steps back. Builds in general are a lot more streamlined however a large percentage of builds are now impossible due to mods being removed and not replaced with a new equivalent mod.

I hope you didn't like elemental wells because thats gone.

Loadouts are nice to finally have I suppose.

In terms of New content for the core game we get 1 new strike, 0 new pvp makes, 0 new gambit maps, no loot refresh it just reuses gear from previous seasons, 0 new ritual activity armor sets like promised.

The newly updated strikes have somehow been made worse, if the goal of making strikes feel more "rewarding" was to make them more tedious and waste the players time then I'd say mission accomplished.

So yeah all together a pretty terrible expansion that is a massive let down from the high bar they set with Witch Queen.

I'll probably still login and play it everyday because I'm terminally addicted at this point but this just means I get to spend the year whinging about it more than usual.\t\t\t\t\t
