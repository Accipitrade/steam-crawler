\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt hurts to say this but Bungie underdelivered with this DLC.

while Neomuna visually looks pretty decent and the soundtrack is a banger everything else is pretty mid or downright bad.

(spoilers ahead)
starting with the story, the first half is pretty promising and then all of a sudden everything is rushed. the two cloudstriders, Nimbus and Rohan, you don't and won't give a ♥♥♥♥ about them. they kill off Rohan after like 5 minutes of screen time and Nimbus, I don't think many players will find him a likable character he's annoying and edgy. when you think that you're halfway through the story you find yourself fighting the final boss. there is no major plot twist. none. not even at the end. the whole thing feels like a free demo for the final shape which is not worth 50$.

and about strand. I finished the campaign on warlock so this may be a bit different on other subclasses but strand is by far the weakest subclass for warlock. while it is fun, I don't see myself running strand in any form of high-end activity. In my opinion, it needs several buffs before it becomes somewhat viable, especially in the cooldown department. Bungie was really scared of another pre-nerf stasis scenario but this just feels too much.
p.s: at the time I reviewed lightfall, the strand fragments weren't available. strand is pretty decent or dare I say overpowered in the right build so you can ignore that part of the review.

everything feels like a downgrade from the witch queen. both the seasonal and lightfall weapons are re-skins of older weapons, which I don't usually mind but both the seasonal and expansion weapons? the story is lackluster. neomuna feels empty and pointless and strand doesn't feel strong.

what more can I say? I really hope that Bungie won't fumble the raid too but at this point, I don't even care anymore. feels like they are more focused on their new IP instead of destiny. A loyal fan base and having no other competition will only keep your game relevant for so long.\t\t\t\t\t
