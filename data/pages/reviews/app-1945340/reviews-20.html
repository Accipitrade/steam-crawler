\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe main story in this DLC was a joke and a short one at that - we're being told from the beginning that we need to protect the 'veil', but we don't receive any sort of explanation throughout the story of what the 'veil' actually is?
To be honest the horrific story writing isn't even the worst thing about this DLC and game as a whole, the steadily declining art direction takes the cake on that one - I remember playing Destiny 1 and even some of the DLCs of Destiny 2 and feeling like the equipment was worth grinding for, even just for the aesthetic aspect of it.
Now, however, the large majority of the gear in this game looks either hilarious or atrocious, with any somewhat decent looking pieces being locked behind the paywall that is Eververse, and even then most of the stuff there looks ugly at best.
Back to the story and gameplay in this DLC, I don't recall one memorable moment of the story itself after playing through it for the past two days, the strand mechanics and abilities feel extremely tacky/gimmicky like I'm in some sort of low budget Indiana Jones adaptation, game feels more buggy and glitchy than ever, haven't even touched PVP in this update but I already know nothing has been changed or updated in it so there's no point in even trying.
Not entirely sure what this company is doing with this game except for trying their best to drive it into the ground with extremely poor quality expansions like this one. But yes please keep doing more pointless collabs that ruin player immersion and keep turning the game into fortnite, I'm sure that's what everyone wants!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't know where to start. Was this Destiny? The story, imo, doesn't fit, at least the parts with the cloud striders or neomuna or whatever the hell they were called. This is the first time I have not cared, AT ALL, for the new characters. They were terribly written (the cringe) and terribly designed. Their look is just....not Destiny imo. I hate it, they don't fit.

I couldn't wait to get through that "campaign." I wanted to get through it as fast as Osiris did. He knew how bad it all was and rushed you through it. Luckily, it's really short, and that brings you to the only good part of this expansion. Which is the new subclass Strand, is so much fun. Definitely a welcome thing to come to Destiny.

I quit playing right after the Witch Queen, which was great. I ran a few vow of disciple raids, then got burnt out. This expansion, doesn't make me want to come back. I'll probably finish up some small things with getting strand finished, and quit again until the New Shape.

I picked this up on sale at $30. I wouldn't recommend it, even at that price. Wait till it comes down....waaaay down. If you're looking to jump back into Destiny after a long hiatus, like I did, I suggest waiting until the next big expansion. This isn't it.\t\t\t\t\t
