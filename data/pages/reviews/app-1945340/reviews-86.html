\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLightfall is a wasted potential. The campaign is supposed to be the penultimate one, a setup for what will be the finale of Destiny 2.

Great, right? Something epic maybe?

Nope. The campaign is horribly written, and the introduction of Strand was, pun intended, woven into the campaign story. Which is a huge, confusing mistake. I'm not going to comment on the Marvel-level humor of one certain character because y'all didn't complain about Cayde-6 being the same so I'm not taking that to heart.

So, is it ALL bad? No. The Campaign is terrible. But the gameplay... I've been playing Destiny since the first, and I have had my periods of not playing it at all. It's NEVER been as good as it is now. Once you unlock Strand it is SUPER fun to play with the class. The endgame content is super fun, and I will say that the Lore of the game has greatly improved, and Neomuna is honestly a really cool setting, and even the Cloud Striders have cool bits of lore to them as well. But, again, the Campaign pacing and its overall writing is abysmal. But then there are the small details that make it incredible. I don't understand how they fumbled it so bad but they did!

Overall score: writing - 3/10, Gameplay: 12/10 (yes, I think it's THAT good)

Summary: Once again I can't recommend it for the story. But if you like satisfying gameplay; it's peak Destiny.\t\t\t\t\t
