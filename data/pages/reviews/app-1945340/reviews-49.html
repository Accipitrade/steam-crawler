\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA pleasantly average Destiny DLC. If you had fun with Rise of Iron or Beyond Light, you shouldn't be challenged to enjoy this stuff. Campaign story is rushed and stilted, but the missions are fun. The raid is a very good time, albeit a step down from the complexity of its predecessor. The new subclasses are an excellent addition and get much the high point of this DLC.

The increase in difficulty of since activities is actually pretty welcome, in my opinion. It feels more like playing in the Taken King or Forsaken eras, just with far more gear and build opportunities.

The downsides are that it didn't ship with much improvement or addition to core playlist activities, like strikes or PvP. Ball dropped there. The narrative is pretty fairly criticized as shoehorned in and overly enigmatic, and it's fair to wish Bungie would improve in this regard instead of regressing to a mediocre storyline.

As with almost every Destiny expansion ever, it's very easy to get your money's worth in entertainment, so it's pretty easy to recommend. Not great for newcomers, but that's hardly a unique indictment of Lightfall...\t\t\t\t\t
