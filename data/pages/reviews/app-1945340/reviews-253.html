The pacing is extremely rushed. New places new things, and just NPC yelling go faster in your ear with not an explanation in sight or a moment to smell the roses. Only a few second intro to new characters and suddenly you are best friends. So far the new characters are both very shallow, and one is very annoying. The almost complete absence of ambient npcs is more noticeable than ever.

Perhaps worse than the story is the butchery done to the armor mod system. All complexity and nuance has been deleted in favor of an over-simplified system that lacks both variety and build options. They added build templates to the game, but ironically removed most of the reason to use them. The new system was supposed to unify a few different subsets of mods. Instead it largely removed them all. One subclass, stasis, was mostly left without any synergies with the mods at all because the critical mods were removed entirely. Huge step backwards.

The season content seemed much better so far, but that does not redeem lightfall itself.\t\t\t\t\t
