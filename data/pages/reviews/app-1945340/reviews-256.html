\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt this point, I am only here for story and to play with friends. It remains a fun game to play with friends, but story? This expansion feels like it just blew it all. This was supposed to be feel close to final. It was the penultimate confrontation between the Traveler who made us what we are and the big bad Witness, who caused our first collapse. The campaign then proceeded to send us to the fringes of the solar system and try to make us care about some other city that was no more than a myth a week ago when our *own* Last City is facing a literal apocalypse. I will give no spoilers about the ending, but there honestly isn't much to spoil—the final cutscene was just as badly written as the rest of the campaign and a huge letdown for those of us invested in the lore since day 1. And this doesn't even mention the absolutely horrendous character supposedly adding comic relief like Cayde-6 once did.

If you care about story, this is a huge "not recommended." Go watch the trailer, and you basically know or can easily guess everything that happens.

In terms of gameplay, well, I still can't recommend it unless you just want to goof off with friends. The legendary campaign was not nearly as fun as the Witch Queen. The difficulty is easier overall with (often annoying) mechanics added to lengthen encounters and test player endurance. I find long, grindy encounters neither fun nor effective tests of skill; I find them exhausting. And if you just cave and play on normal difficulty? It's brain-dead easy.

I do not recommend this expansion. In fact, if you've been on the fence for a while, this is honestly a good opportunity to finally free yourself from the game.\t\t\t\t\t
