\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIs the story great? No, it's probably worse than the original story back in 2014.
Is everything else great? Yeah, and by quite a bit.
The seasonal mods are powerful and allow for temporary boosts for certain subclasses, and honestly I kinda hope some of the changes stay. The new mod system, while more simple than before, makes it easier than ever to create good builds. The new Strand class is not only a blast, but able to stand up against high difficulty content. The new guns and perks are interesting to play with as well. Finally, the raid, while probably the easiest one, is still fun to run through. The first season story also is worlds better than the main campaign, if not a little short.
I will say however that the PVP content is being left behind hard. No Gambit maps, no crucible maps (at launch, they're apparently adding some in later seasons). That said, PVP seems to be pretty hard to initially get into, and it makes sense since people have had the best guns for a while and it can take time to get them. But still, for these two modes to just be left in the dust is embarrassing.
Overall, I would recommend this for everything but the main story, but regardless I feel like my purchase was justified.
