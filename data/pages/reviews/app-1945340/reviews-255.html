\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFive missions deep myself and while I will probably finish it with a buddy, although I've already seen the final mission to see if it was worth my time. Honestly, if you're playing solo or not simply messing around with friends with spare change / time, then I'd seriously pass and play any other shooter / RPG at this point. Quality of life changes to mods and build crafting were honestly nice and should've been added years ago. That's about it for the pros. The missions in this seem so downgraded from Witch Queen with mission breaking bugs that an indie game developer would blush at. Neptune is a lifeless landmass covered in pretty paint and is so horrible to navigate without a grappling hook. It's extra space with zero reason to exist outside of boring lore that no one actually cares about. The new characters look goofy beyond belief and no one cares about Shadow Cabal or whatever their nonsensical name is. The one new enemy is cool until they make you fight it time and time again to the point it poses zero threat quite quickly. Lightfall is another reskin expansion for the sake of on-boarding fresh meat alongside funding Destiny 3 at the cost of their communities goodwill. Try spending more money on game designers, programmers, and community management rather than psychologists, lawyers, and streamers that drop your game after a single day getting paid. When / if I play the new dungeons / raid then that will be the end of Destiny for myself. This game was full of ups and downs, but the biggest up was walking away from it with a smile. Last Wish is a close second! Good luck, but never expect it to get better.\t\t\t\t\t
