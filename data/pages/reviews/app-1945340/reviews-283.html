\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had very low expectations heading into Lightfall because of all the negative reviews it has received... I've played it for an hour and a half now, and I've gotta say - I have enjoyed every minute of it. A lot of people dislike the storyline, because it supposedly has a lot of holes and a very underwhelming ending. But putting the story aside, everyone seems to collectively agree that the actual gameplay in this DLC is good.
​
Strand is underpowered according to most players, but it's very fun to use. It has unique abilities, one of them being a grappling hook. The abilities are what make it such a joy to play around with.
​
So overall I would say, if you're a very story-focused player, you will likely not enjoy this DLC. But if you don't pay too much attention to the story, and you focus on the actual gameplay more (like I do), then you can have a great time.
​
Don't knock it 'til you try it. If I didn't buy Lightfall based off of all the negative reviews, I never would've played the DLC and found out that I actually really enjoy it. The gameplay is good, and Strand is really fun.
​
Also, if you're a veteran Destiny 2 player, you may see this DLC as "recycled" content from previous expansions... but if you're a newer player like I am, Lightfall feels fresh and like a whole new experience, because I have never even played through the previous DLC's stories. So I would recommend getting Lightfall especially if you are a brand new player.
​
I suggest that you only buy Lightfall when it is on sale, though. (Like you probably should with all DLCs.)\t\t\t\t\t
