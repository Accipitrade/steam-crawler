This is likely a product of the fact it was originally the last expansion until that was then determined top be the final shape instead. The tonal whiplash is a lot. One minute you go from the coming apocalypse to a happy go lucky marvel movie with campy dialogue. To be frank, in any aspect, the story is just terrible, especially coming straight after the masterpiece that is the witch queen. To say bungie fell flat doesnt do it justice.

Gameplay wise, its actually really fun and there some standout moments in the campaign that are frankly awesome. The tormentors are terrifying mini-bosses and really are tricky to kill. The new mod system has potential, even if it is largely dumbed down from what we had, and the new fragments for solar, void and arc are pretty dope. Strand really just sucks and is way too gimicky to be useful. They say its all about mobility but the grapple, strands big selling point, is on a 2 minute ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ cooldown. ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ genius bungie lol.

The season itself is actually rather strong with a great new activity and a superb exotic. The seasonal story arc is shaping up to be good as well, resolving some of crows relationship struggle and potentially seeing him oficially become the vangaurd.

In short, skip lightfall. I just cant in good conscious recommend it due to how terrible the story is. but i do recommend season of defiance.\t\t\t\t\t
