\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLightfall was looking like it was going to continue in the good direction that Witch Queen took. Boy was I wrong. Not only was the ability/mod system overhaul abysmal, but the story was the worst story Destiny has ever put out. Destiny has never been good at putting the story (lore/grimoire) into the game but they didn't even try for this one. It didn't answer any questions, the characters were poorly written and we were left with more questions. Calus was a terrible villain and should never have been brought back as a main antagonist.

The ability system overhaul feels like a kick in the nuts to PvE players. They should have added new mechanics to force the player to think while still allowing players to have their builds. However, like everything Bungie does, they nerf the player's power in order to "create challenge" and "increase difficulty". It's a garbage way to balance games and they continuously nerf player power based on what is popular.

This expansion is essentially Beyond Light 2.0 with it's level of ball-kicking and disappointment. Bungie should be ashamed for demanding $100 for this garbage release and 4 more lazy seasons.\t\t\t\t\t
