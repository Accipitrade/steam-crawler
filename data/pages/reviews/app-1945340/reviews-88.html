\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the first time I've felt compelled to write a review for anything destiny. After close to 1500 hours in D2 on PC alone, well over 2000 in the franchise, I am pretty disappointed.
Witch Queen was amazing, in gameplay and narrative. Lightfall does a lot of stuff right with gameplay but the narrative falls less than short.
Pros:
The new weapons are all pretty cool with the new perks, and there is a much better system in place to earn what you want
New abilities on every subclass.
Strand is fun and feels powerful once you unlock everything
Gameplay Overall is great
New areas are fun
New content is engaging
The game is harder but in a good way
Buildcrafting is simplified by removing armor elemental affinity
Loadout system is great

Cons:
Buildcrafting is SO simplified that you do not feel as powerful and it lost so many of the mods that its just shallow
Loadouts would have made a much bigger difference if we still had all our mods
Some content is just way too punishing for newer guardians who dont have the gear that some of us have.
Textures on Neomuna are kinda blah, opposite of Witch Queen.
Grind to unlock strand takes a long time even if you know how to optimize, once you completely unlock strand on one character that character can no longer earn the materials needed to unlock the perks on other characters, all characters share the same inventory, kinda silly IMO but I get it, they want you to use your other characters.

Narrative:
Id rather listen to my wife farting after too much pepperoni pizza than anything Nimbus has to say. They sound like they have an accordion in their throat. Nimbus has absolutely nothing to say of consequence "ehh they so ugly ima punch em in the gut" WOW ok, that's the writing we get from a $3B company. They do not answer a single question we had going in, its just "YOU MUST GET TO THE VEIL SLOWPOKE" But, Osiris, What is the Veil? "ITS THE VEIL GO THERE NOW".
Bungie wants you to care about the new characters but they didn't try to make them likable and there's no time to get to know Rohan before he gets Cayde-6'd. You get forced into using Strand in the middle of every mission, completely ruining your build, you cant even make a loadout to switch to bc you don't have any strand stuff unlock to put in the loadout...
Its even more sad since all the other teams did a great job, to have the whole expansion brought down by terrible story telling and characters that are uncomfortable to listen to and look at (nimbus has this gross cod piece that i just don't understand)

I want to say I don't recommend this, but, I still play every day, and I want more people to pick this game up since it really is worth it in the end. The best times are when you get a chance to teach a random New Light something that will help them in their journey. This game is supposed to be about community, working together, overcoming obstacles. Not about taking dumps on one another, the game included.\t\t\t\t\t
