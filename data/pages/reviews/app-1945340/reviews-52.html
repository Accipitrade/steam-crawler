\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a player with nearly 1000 hours in this game, I can certainly say that this was imo the biggest blunder in lifetime of destiny 2. The story added nothing but questions when at this point in the light and dark saga we should be getting answers, such as: what is the veil? Why is establishing a link to the traveler so important? What does witness want? The new raid, while beautiful, was way to easy and rather simple, just the fact that it was cleared in under 2.5 hours says that. The campaign was a major let down in terms of story hits, character development, polish, and length. Nearly HALF of the campaign is spent learning about strand which, even though I enjoyed murdering waves of enemies using the new darkness sub-class, is FAR too much time spent on it. Claus didn't even feel like a character in this expansion, with the complete lack of development he got, it feels as though they simply pulled him out of the dcv to come back cause they didn't have a better idea. It honestly felt as though they either didn't have nearly enough time or simply didn't care about it. The Pvp meta is beyond wack, the ENTIRE expansion gets you only 10 exotics, and the so-called pinnacle content is practically a strike at this point. To give credit where credit is due though, in terms of gameplay, there has never been a time where there has been so much content available for both players with friends and solo-players. Strand is really strong and fun, the new buildcrafting and loadout systems are a great addition and simplification to the previously difficult to understand concept of mods, the exotic weapons we got (even though there weren't many) are rather fun and interesting to play around with, the tormentors are a really interesting new enemy to fight against (even though they scare the ♥♥♥♥ out of me when playing solo content), and Neomuna is just beautiful, not quite Witch Queen level good, but good none the less. If you're here simply wanting to shoot stuff and play occasionally, buy it on sale for like 30$ because 50$ is WAY too much, but if you're a new player thinking of getting into destiny, while I still love this franchise, I wouldn't recommend purchasing this, just go find another game like elden ring to sink you're teeth into, since currently, unless you're willing to pay something like 250$ for all the expansions in destiny, this is not the game for you, even then, I would wait until next years expansion to even THINK of getting into destiny 2. Assuming Final Shape is actually any good. Here's to hoping it will be. Cheers.\t\t\t\t\t
