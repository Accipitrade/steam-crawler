\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTwo main things happened to Destiny with this update.
1. Large amount of QOL updates.
2. Lightfall Campaign and it's new environments/characters.

The QOL updates are some of the best I have seen (Umbral removal, Seasonal Artifact passives, new armor mods, loadout slots, the list goes on), while Lightfall is the worst expansion Destiny has ever had. Ever.

The Lightfall story is terrible, nothing is explained about anything happening, and -Surprise!- you are just fighting Calus. Again. Nothing new other then sometimes enemies will be carrying shield packs that deploy when they die and there is a new, immensely annoying-yet-easy yellow bar enemy with a scythe and auto-kills. That's it. That's the entire new expansion.

At a moment where it seemed like Destiny was going to have a big explosion of events/surprises/twists, there's... nothing. Nothing new is learned, nothing is revealed, nothing is really lost either. The opening cutscene was the only source of "wow" in the entire expansion. It was awesome. Then Lightfall really started.

The new characters are amazingly unhelpful and amazingly annoying. Blatant Posiden/Aquaman design rip-offs (though I don't mind the designs too much), one is only in the game for about 20 seconds worth of screen time, and the other is a bro who actually seems to not care about anything that is happening. As if the entire plot and everyone in it is a joke. The most cringe script-writing I have seen in a AAA game, maybe ever? He calls out your public event updates like sports plays, it's horrible. How do you make a "sarcastic/funny/weird" character? They already made one, Failsafe. But nope, there needed to be new character who acts/sounds/has the attention-span/has the maturity of a thirteen year old teenager. Every moment in the new expansion with him is horrible.

The new planet is fun for about 30 minutes until you start to realize how basic it is. It's a college student's environmental design project. I would know, I did one just like it. Great theme, bad/sloppy details. Everything is weirdly stuck together and looks 100% fake. Nothing looks lived in or like something that could be a place used by citizens. It's just... odd. Plus, it's half empty and pain to get around in. Comparing the environment to Witch Queen/Calus's ship is just night and day.

And Strand, the new subclass (Only played hunter strand). It's... not going to work out for now. It's a badly-tuned mobility class that cannot survive in high-end content. To compensate, they put in a mass-dps super that feels very OP yet very boring and uninspired. Half the aspects are locked for now, this is the one new thing that will most certainly get patched and improved over time until it is useful. At least make the grapple useful?

Skip Lightfall. And seeing how badly they messed up one of the most important expansions in the game's history, if you are new you might want to just skip Destiny completely.\t\t\t\t\t

If questions get answered in a reasonable time: 8/10
If D2 vanilla era ptsd continues: 5/10

This is pertaining only to the campaign: the Lightfall campaign is far from what I wanted. That being said, I had an extremely enjoyable time. As I have processed my thoughts about the campaign and went back to watch the Vidoc and other developer commentaries, I see more that Bungie developed this campaign as a story that would affect the story of Destiny overall, but could be contained as a heroic tale of the Guardian actually feeling like they are saving lives framed as a cheesy 80's action movie. Viewed from that perspective, I love Lightfall's campaign.

The character's, especially Rohan, felt a little pointless, while I mostly enjoyed their screentime. As a player of Destiny since Vanilla D1, I really want to see the wrapup begin, since that what was more advertised; however, if these characters and loose threads end up paying off, I will be a lot more satisfied with the campaign. I did love the setting of Neomuna, the atmosphere, the music, the new enemy changes, and more, but as a lore nerd who cares about the Destiny universe more than the real one, I need to have the egregious amount of questions answered that Lightfall's campaign asked that need to be answered in this and the next few seasons.\t\t\t\t\t
