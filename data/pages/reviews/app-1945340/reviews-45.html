The main takeaway, Lightfall isnt perfect, but Destiny 2 currently is in the best shape it has been I would dare say since Destiny 1: Taken King time. It feels like new management got put into the team since Forsaken and theyre really polishing the game, not sunsetting content, making great design decisions such as leaning into "build" based customization, making subclasses feel deeper with more RPG mechanics(Thank GOD they finally did this, instead of the simple, garbage we used to have.)

Theres a reason Destiny 2 is currently hitting all time player records. The foundational game, Destiny 2 is in an amazing shape. The previous expansion, Witch Queen was insanely good, added weapon creation, intriguing lore heavy campaign, and introduced tons of Subclass mechanics. I would say, if you feel that youre not sure about Lightfall, just play Destiny 2 and whatever expansions you have, see if you have fun, work on unlocking exotics, do campaigns, and you'll realize, like I reluctantly did, that Destiny 2 is really fun now!\t\t\t\t\t
