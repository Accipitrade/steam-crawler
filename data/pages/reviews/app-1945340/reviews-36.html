It doesn't have to be a pie which you have to gobble every part of it. Can't have everything all at once.

I think Nimbus is a cool trans character. There's emotion, optimistic attitude and you can find bunch of character in that, but when you have like max 10 years to live, do you have to go super emo and edgelord on it? I like how the character develops in the later part of the story as well and I like their voice.

Gameplay on legend campaign feels great and sufficiently challenging. Strand subclass rocks the more and more you get used to it's feel. Loadout update is a nice addition and more user friendlier than before, but still have to rely on DIM and companion app to save time on inventory management and bounty acquisition... New exotics sound exciting to try out and figure how to use them well in loadouts.

At the moment as of this writing. Busy with the raid since I am doing it blind with clan. We are close to finishing it and so far I like it. It is quite approachable and I like how it ties to story.

My concern is on the amount of content they're expected to churn out... maybe have longer seasons, add more time to develop stuff?

I do think the price increase is a bit much versus previous expansion... is it because of Sony Acquisition? Global price increase because of the war happening in Ukraine...? I don't know but I <3 the game and its developers... so haven't really lost anything on it.\t\t\t\t\t
