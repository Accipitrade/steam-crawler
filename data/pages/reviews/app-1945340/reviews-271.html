\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is like the 5th time I've tried to get into Destiny, because on paper it should be my dream game. I love MMOs. I love First Person Shooters. I like Science Fiction and Science Fantasy. I like games with both PvE and PvP. I like Co-Op shooters. I like grindy loot-farming games.

You know what I like about Destiny 2? The guns. Especially the Sunshot. That's a really neat gun. Has a cool sound effect, and making a chain reaction of exploding enemies is cool.

You know what I don't like about Destiny 2? The entire universe. At least how it's presented in game. Every character might as well be an animatronic robot. Every environment feels like a movie set, especially this new "city". A collection of buildings does not make a city, a population does.

I used to hate not getting any answers about what the important stuff in this universe actually IS. What The Traveler is, what The Darkness is, etc.

I gave Witch Queen a try and it finally seemed like some answers were on the near horizon with the introduction of The Witness, and Lightfall's trailers teasing his confrontation with the Traveler. Yet when we finally get to see this confrontation, we end up bailing on the Traveler(and Earth?) to chase the Cabal to Neptune for... another mystery box McGuffin. I thought "The Veil" was something I didn't understand because I don't play this game consistently, but seeing people that love this game express confusion of what it is just makes me feel exasperated.\t\t\t\t\t
