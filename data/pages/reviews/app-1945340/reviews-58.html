What can I say about Lightfall?
Allow me to clarify who you're hearing this from. I'm a top 15% player in play time in Destiny 2 across all the systems I play and play Destiny 2 nearly every day. I also am obsessed with the lore and I could probably answer any question you have about the lore.

So all that being said. Lightfall is an amazing experience for people who just care about gameplay. But is a pain and a confusing mess for those who want a story. Bungie said this was supposed to be a DLC that helps bring new players into Destiny 2, yet this DLC is easily the most confusing (story-wise) since the original Destiny 1's story. Nothing is answered and everyone seems to know what happened except you. Combine that with Osiris being a whiny baby, Nimbus getting on my nerves with their annoying voice and personality, and nothing being answered, Lightfall just feels unfinished from a story department. It feels like they outsourced this story to a second company to be a filler story. A lot of dialogue feels like it was written by an AI, it's so awkward. Especially with a certain cut-scene later on with Nimbus not being able to read the damn room.

It just feels misguided and ill-focused. Focusing on Strand and Neomuna rather than the Traveler, Witness, and Calus. Combine this with a mid raid in Root of Nightmares and a wasted character in said raid with an awful design (imo) and this DLC's story is up there with Shadowkeep in mid-ness. But then combine that with nothing being answered and it just becomes a pitiful mess that I hope is fixed/addressed in The Final Shape next year.

But gameplay wise? Oh wow Lightfall is fun. As usual the gunplay is unmatched by any other shooter or MMO available right now. It's hard for me to put into words but Destiny 2 just gives that little "DING!" in your head that makes it among the funnest games I've ever played. And with the introduction of Strand adding a whole new dimension to movement and loadouts, it only is getting funner and funner. I genuinely am enjoying playing Destiny 2 more than any other game out right now, bad story aside.

Along with more reworks and quality of life additions like the new Journey tab that gives new and returning players a goal to do. Don't know what to do? Check the Journey tab and bookmark one. There's your goal this session(s). The addition of many unobtainable items in the random loot pool, bringing back that sub-grind for things you might've missed out on due to not playing or just missing them before they were initially unobtainable. Along with Ada-1 in the annex of the Tower selling unobtainable shaders to those who missed out. It feels like that F.O.M.O. is starting to be peeled away item-wise and I'm very glad about that, despite me already having basically everything they're giving out now. Hope they start that with seasonal items, too sometime. And my favorite addition in this DLC, the presets/loadouts. Oh my word I am so happy the have FINALLY allowed me to save loadouts and appearances I enjoy on the fly.

Strand is super fun and has rapidly grown on me and it's a shame it's locked behind this DLC's campaign but trust me it is so worth it. All of the Strand subclasses are fun, especially Hunter. Strand really opens up the game and frankly, just frees the player to do almost whatever they want. I'll always be biased to loving Solar the most, but Strand was my favorite when I just wanted to cut lose and have blind, stupid fun.

TL;DR: Meh gameplay that's not much worth your $50. And an awful story that even a lore nerd like I was confused by.

Edit: While all this is true, the package Lightfall comes with is not worth it. Don't get Destiny 2, and don't get Lightfall. It's not worth your time.\t\t\t\t\t
