\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are good bones hidden inside Lightfall, unfortunately overall the end result is misguided in execution and misleading in communication.

Biggest issues:
Lightfall campaign is just bad. The campaign itself is FAR shorter than witch queen, and a much less polished story. Witch queen told an entire narrative. Lightfall is just the first teaser to bad radio serial. TUNE IN NEXT WEEK, SPACE COWBOYS, FOR THE FURTHER ADVENTURES IN THE VEIL!

Neomuna is very pretty. Its also very small compared to the throne world (which had "off the map" explorable areas that were easily as large as all the patrol areas put together), and more importantly it is just empty. Running around a deserted city looking for anything to shoot at is NOT a good look for a shooter.

And now the absolute worst:
The game loops have all been made more "difficult", which really means more time consuming while having equal or less rewards to past efforts. You could do a season 19 heist in about 10-12 minutes, and if you open the second chest (always an option with ANY amount of effort) it would reward you with 3-5 legendary items. If you do a season 20 battleground (in somewhere between 20-30 minutes depending on the group you get) and open the chest at the end (NOT guaranteed as its a random drop) you get 1-2 defiant engrams and 1 legendary item. Also worth noting, 1 defiant engram does not decode into 1 weapon. FOUR defiant engrams decode into 1 weapon.

So we've gone from 10-12 minutes for 3+ legendary items to instead being 20-30 minutes for 1.25 ish legendaries (only 0.25-0.5 if you dont have a key).

Lastly, the ugly:
All these changes are happening AFTER we were told by Joe Blackburn that they were making the seasonal activities be MORE generous. And that having a key should feel like "a true bonus", but not like they are really required to make it worthwhile.

Bungie may be able to recover the state of the game with work, and if they do then this may be a worthwhile expansion then. Repairing the trust of their playerbase may take longer though.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a veteran player, this campaign left me with more questions than it did answers, and not in the good way. The shock value Bungie tried to deliver at the end of the campaign was entirely overshadowed by the pure amount of confusion and ignorance the story provides. The characters were written poorly and the emotional moments we were supposed to feel had absolutely no effect. Every character acted like we all knew what the topic of the story is and why it’s important, but in reality it was never explained or even hinted at. Additionally, the new subclass was focused WAY too hard and half of the campaign was nothing but training on something that we could’ve done without. Strand should not be the topic of the story, the veil should have been. Even with strand being central to the “story”, even that wasn’t explained. It wasn’t explained how it’s never been seen before, even though Neomuna is flooded with it, it’s never explained how it’s connected to the darkness except for a few vague lines that could’ve literally been used for a light subclass explanation, and again strand was shoved down our throats with no explanation why other then “the witness doesn’t know about it.” That’s not enough to merit an entire subclass being focused way too hard. Despite all the flaws, the gameplay is fantastic and super fun. It’s just there’s no explanation or elaboration on anything we’re doing, why we’re doing it, and why it’s so important to do so. Lightfall did not feel like light fell. It did not feel like our end as it was HIGHLY marketed to be, and probably one of, if not, the biggest disappointments Bungie has put out in a long time. Right after they nailed a mostly solid year from Witch Queen, it’s hard to trust their narrative team after this big of a flop. Granted the raid has not come out, under a third of the community actually raids and even a smaller portion of that regularly raids, so to say the raid can make up for it is not enticing enough to rate this expansion highly. And again, this is all coming from a veteran player. If you’re a new player, all I can say is god help you and good luck.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am simple man. I play simple character and enjoy crayons. Game offered me new green crayon. Eat crayon, crayon tasty, gives me grapple hook and fun melee. It much better than the blue crayon, blue crayon taste bad and was boring to get, green crayon way better.

All of that stupidity aside, I've been thoroughly enjoying Lightfall (have not completed it as of yet). As someone who pays very little attention to the story of Destiny or its story telling quality, this seems about on par with everything else I have played within Destiny. Yes I wish they would tell me what the Veil is, yes I want to know how we never knew about the massive civilization on Neptune (if this is explained in the story then jokes on me), however this isnt taking away the enjoyment I am having playing this through with my friends or how fun Strand is to use. Now I might be forgetting stuff from Witch Queen, but I think they did a lot better job with the cut scenes than Witch Queen did. I also like the new boss enemy thing a lot more than I liked the light charged enemies.

I am enjoying the Strand class. It's fun swinging around and slicing through things. I do think the Titan Strand class is.. a little underwhelming so far. I havent fully unlocked it so havent had a chance to look at upgrades, just campaign base skills. That said I like it a lot more than stasis which just felt ass and I have very little desire to fully unlock everything for it. It was slow and boring to unlock, stasis itself felt slow and boring, and further unlocks would be just more of that. Strand is so far the opposite, I'm swinging around like a mad man, slashing through stuff like there is no tomorrow, and they give it to me frequently and often. This is a wonderful combination at least in my mind.

All of these things combined have left me happy with this DLC and I must say I was surprised when I looked at the reviews and saw it as mostly negative. I hope even the people who are leaving negative reviews can at least find some enjoyment from their purchase.\t\t\t\t\t
