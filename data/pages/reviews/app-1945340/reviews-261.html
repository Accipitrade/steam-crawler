\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTerrible, a huge disappointment after 7 years of buildup. The strongest moments of this story come in the opening cut scene with the traveler facing off the pyramid ship fleet, the mysterious character of The witness hanging over everything, the firing of a massive laser and the Veil being revealed. The stakes are sky high and the key lies on Neptune.

And then we finally get there and ladies and gentlemen the quality of writing falls, It falls off of a cliff into a the mouth of a burning volcano. The story is BAD, incompetent and shows clear signs of being rushed. There are no explanation no discovery no head or tail as to what tf this veil thing even is. One moment the neomunians seem to know everything about the Veil and the next they dont even know where it is and have never seen it before.

All we do is just go here and do sh!t because the game tells you. Bungie managed to fail at writing a griping story or even a barely competent one when the stakes and setup were this good. The only reason we want to defend the veil is because the witness wants it. That's it. We have no idea wtf it is or does.

Calus, holy sh!t he was not the correct choice for the big bad guy of this campaign. First of, the dialogue delivery and writing makes it so you cant take this guy seriously. Knowing the characters history had already made him something you would rather laugh at than fear. They tried to remedy this with a cheap, tapestry like cut scene but it just makes him even funnier. The rest of the characters are even worse. Osiris has been given the role of "remind the player of whats at stake" but he comes of as extremely pushy, and clearly the Voice actor thought being loud and angry will result in a better performance. Yes there are undertones of how he is trying to be more meaningful and overcompensating cause he lost his light but this is not the way to do it, this is some amateur hour stuff.
Nimbus, 0/5 fire the writer. Wtf happened here? First of they gave em a robot voice which really stripped any emotion the dialogue could have had and then the entire personality of this guy is that of a real Git. This is the kind of character that Inspires anger and hatred amongst those who hear it. In the Apocalypse this guy comes off as the happy go lucky moron. No gravitas no expression no emotion no internal motivation just a piece of plastic being pushed around as the plot demands. A soulless box pretending to be a fully realized character. This guy's partner and Mentor dies and the response to that is "ah well, ♥♥♥♥ happens whatever. Skill issue RIP Bozo". Rohan had 3 lines and died (Huge Surprise), so we do not care and neither does nimbus. They were going for a levity and humour with this guy like fynch 2.0 and it has backfired catastrophically, Bungie i want you to see this, GIVE UP YOU WILL NEVER BE ABLE TO WRITE ACTUALLY FUNNY DIALOGUE. This is the brand of humour that you find in corporate glass boxes and company birthday emails devoid of emotion, risk averse and market tested as if written by a man who hasnt talked to a real person in 20 years.

The gameplay is the same destiny we all know if you like shooting guns and space magic, you will like this. The legend campaign does seem a bit easier this time around which is not a very good move, it should have gotten harder as players are more comfortable at contest and underlight content now. Too early to say anything significant on strand, its ok the sound design for the subclass is cool. Whats funny to me is how you go to Neptune and see the green stuff for first time in world btw (brand new thing never seen before by anyone) and immediately other characters start calling it strand like how tf do you know what this is? Theres just a surface level intrigue from osiris and the focus is on "mastering" it instantly than really exploring what it is. ah well not a huge gripe but its still just another example how this dlc is an unpolished and not thought out very well.

Lightfall is not the great battle and the beginning to culmination of the light-dark Saga that its being marketed as, but rather a cheap filler story with a mediocre campaign and fakeout ending that will leave you with a bad aftertaste. Bungie are masters of building stuff up and then under delivering. Witness arrives > some real dumb sh!t happens > witness leaves
That's the story good bye
I give you 1 dolla for this game\t\t\t\t\t
