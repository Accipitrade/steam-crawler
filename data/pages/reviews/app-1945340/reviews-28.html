\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI decided to wait until this season was (almost) over before giving my review of the expansion. Overall, I have to say, I liked this expansion, which I know is going against the grain, but genuinely, I did.

Firstly, I am so glad that they finally added loadouts to the game. That's a feature I've been wanting for years. Can we get character appearance editing and character names please?

The cutscenes and dialogue between the characters were great; in particular, I was surprised by how much I liked Nimbus. I fully expected to dislike the character based on the trailers, because they seemed like a one-dimensional hot-headed rookie. While 'hot-headed rookie' is definitely a part of them, they're also pretty respectful and reasonable, and there's so much fun dialogue that this character has, both in the story and in ambiance. Additionally, Osiris has a really good arc throughout the story.

The new location is fun; I'm a big fan of futuristic, tech-based cityscapes. The color palettes for pretty much everywhere, including the Vex zones, are really well done.

As for the seasonal content, I really liked the new battlegrounds, the exotic mission, and the new weapons, gear, and cosmetics. There's an artifact perk that spawns an Orb of Power whenever you break an enemy shield with a matching damage type. Pretty much all of the shielded enemies in the new battlegrounds have Void shields, so if you have Void weapons on with that perk, you can have your super up every 10-20 seconds, which is mad fun.

I understand why people would dislike this expansion. There's a number of things in the campaign that make it frustrating to play through, there were a number of bugs throughout the expansion and the seasonal content, and the new raid is stupidly easy. But, for me, the things I enjoyed outweighed the things I disliked. Also, anyone complaining that the campaign is "too short" are try hards who play this game for 16+ hours a day - for the vast majority of people who spend a healthy amount of time playing video games, the campaign is plenty long.

I know a lot of people were upset with the ending of the campaign: it leaves us on a cliffhanger, and leaves a lot of questions unanswered... but, IMO, that doesn't ruin it at all. "Oh no, the story is going to continue! Bungy, what the hell is wrong with you?!!?!1!?!?1??1!?" Yeah, sorry, not with y'all on that one lol. The story wasn't completely devoid of any substance as some people are making it out to be. For example, everyone seems to have glossed over the pretty major revelations that Osiris had about the nature of Light and Darkness during the campaign. It put forward some concepts that I found really interesting and enjoyable, and I hope it gets explored more in future storylines.

So, yeah, I enjoyed the expansion. I know, I know, how unimaginable: a player who actually likes the game. Fake fan, I guess.\t\t\t\t\t
