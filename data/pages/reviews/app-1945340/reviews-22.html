\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI only really started playing Destiny 2 in the past year and logging 450+ hours (as of this review) I can confidently say that this expansion's story is dreadful.. but I also honestly can't say that the rest of the game's story isn't on par with how confusing and vague this expansions story is either. Nimbus is probably the most annoying and unlikable character I've seen in this game so far. His dialogue is just... cringe and annoying. And the new boss type is uh... well... read on.

That being said, if you just want the expansion for Strand (like myself) and some new weapons and such, then I'd recommend it. I can't say that I am a huge fan of seasonal content, solely because of FOMO but I do think it is in Bungie's best interest to advance the story and the game through the seasonal content passes that they have going rather then make expansions, which seems like the direction they are going in. I'm perfectly okay with throwing away 10 bucks for the content they release in the season (battle) passes if it means that I don't have to deal with the complete and utter torture that is LITERALLY ANY FIGHT INVOLVING A TORMENTOR. Seriously, who the hell came up with a boss that stops you from doing ANYTHING with your abilities and sends you FLYING into orbit with a subclass that prevents you from flying into orbit?! God why?!?

TL;DR: Don't get this if: you want to have any semblance of understanding the story or just any good story at all. Do get this if: you want cool new abilities that are extremely useful. Seriously, shackle grenade? Never before has it been so satisfying to look your enemies dead in the eyes at point blank and shoot them in the face while they just hang there helplessly.

Side note, why the hell does Neomuna's textures look like that kids rug that we all had when we were little? You remember, the one with the streets and buildings printed on it? Google "rug city" right now and tell me that Neomuna doesn't fit that description.\t\t\t\t\t
