\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gameplay is fantastic as always but the story for this expansion does not hold a candle to the Witch Queen story. If you are not bothered by story and just want to play the game and have fun, I would recommend this expansion. I find "Defiant Battlegrounds" particularly enjoyable. But again, battlegrounds have been around for a while before this expansion was released. I had not yet completed the new raid but from others that I know that had completed it, they all say it is fun and the rewards make it worth your while. The new "Strand" subclass is very fun to use as well and allows for players to take on champions more easily with the suspend feature. It always feels like there is something to do in this game especially if you're in possession of other expansions. But if this may be your first expansion purchase; you may find the content you have access to underwhelming. If you are also/only in it for the story, I could not recommend this expansion. I enjoy the game itself but Lightfall just doesn't really add anything good to the game other than what it already has. It feels more like a filler expansion than anything. The majority of players as of now do not seem to be too pleased with this addition either. So I will have to give this expansion a thumbs down.
I believe the best thing Bungie can do for the franchise right now is to start working on a Destiny 3. The game is stating to feel like it had run its course and the new expansion accentuates this idea as its story is confusing and underwhelming. Placing an annual paywall to play the game and enjoy the content it has to offer makes it unattractive to new players as well. Especially if they want to play more DLC locked stories from the past. The vanilla story is now unplayable as it has been vaulted. New players would just have a confusing experience as they would be thrown half way in the story and would have to pay hundreds of dollars to unlock all the playable content in the game. If a Destiny 3 is in the making, I'm hoping Bungie could use this opportunity to right the wrongs from Destiny 2 and allow for a more pleasurable and less confusing experience.\t\t\t\t\t

As someone who played all of D1 and grinded D2 at the beginning, i have come back off and on when new content is released hoping things have gotten better, and is enjoyable to play.

Sadly this is not the case, the new campaign is essentially a tutorial of the new subclass "Strand" complete with repeated "Hold X to get unlimited Strand super energy/abilities" to allow you the opportunity to spam all of the strand abilities every mission.(Yes even in legendary campaign).

As always the new sub-class make all previous sub-classes inferior, this time with every class having "tangle grenades" that are essentially required to immobilize and suspend all "Champion" class enemies.

After beating the story-line you resume the grind of light, trying to get +2 or +3 "Pinnacle" rewards by doing the usual weekly missions, all to reach the current max base light (1810) for the supposed harder content.

Some changes on this topic are activities like Iron Banner no longer take Light Level into account, meaning the only time your light affects anything is weekend "Trials of Osiris" or the Weekly Night Fall or Raid. (which the rewards from both are pretty awful and your BIS items will come from 160 content)

All in all, it's just not fun and not worth the purchase, you can just watch the cut-scenes on you tube if you are curious about the story, and that will be more enjoyable than playing.\t\t\t\t\t
