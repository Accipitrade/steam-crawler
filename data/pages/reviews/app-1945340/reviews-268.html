\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have a mixed reaction about this Expansion, it feels like they unlearned everything that made Witch Queen great.

Pros:
•Cool cyberpunk setting

•Emperor Calus and The Witness get more screen-time

•New Armor, Weapons, and Exotics. Always a plus 👍🏻

•Strand is actually a great addition to existing subclasses and is fun to use regardless of who you main. There was a lot of controversy surrounding the Titan super but I tried it and it’s honestly pretty fun to use

•Tormentors are a great addition to Destiny’s enemy composition, they instantly flip the power dynamic whenever one enters a fight, they’ll give you a tough time on Legendary

Cons:
•Story quality is a step down, takes a less serious tone even though the situation is extremely dire

•Nimbus will be your main vendor on Neptune, and even though they’ve got a cool borged out bod their corny lines and actor really make them hard to stomach whenever they chime in on a conversation, feels like a major step back from Fynch in WQ

•The Expansion ending makes the whole DLC feel like you could skip the entire story and just watch a recap on YouTube

•Doesn’t feel like things are dire at all when you visit the tower after, Red War did a much better job depicting the damage in the aftermath of its campaign. It doesn’t feel like a truly impactful defeat. Kinda just feels like “oh we lost, oh well back to weekly activities!”

Verdict:
As excited as I was for this expansion, it’s not worth its current price, the content you get in exchange just is a major step back and in some ways feel like a return to the horrible Curse of Osiris days of D2. Save your money and wait for a sale if you reallllyyyy want Strand and the new gear, otherwise it might be best to just wait until next expansion.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCons:
This expansion is suffering the same fate as Beyond Light in which the trailers overhyped everything.
The city feels dead even though it looks pretty.
It doesnt look like theres a war going on except in certain areas.
Weapons look cool but ultimately are just as meh as most other world drops.
Nimbus has their moments but ultimately is the typical arrogant/overconfident rookie to the seasoned veteran.
Strand is fun to use but is not nearly as good as it was shown in trailers.
Trailers showed fighting on top of skyscrapers but the only skyscraper you actually stand on is the vendor ones.
Osiris sometimes sounds like hes doesn't have a voice actor but instead had his voice lines voiced by one of those AI's
Osiris is just a ♥♥♥♥ tbh
Airborne Effectiveness just ruining a lot of the fights when you have Strand.

Pros:
Visually its actually quite nice to look at
Soundtrack has some good songs in it
Tormentors are more threatening than the Lightbearer Hive (although this may change as people become higher light level)
Rohan
Weapons actually look pretty good and shade well.

All in all i was actually quite let down, it started off somewhat decent but slowly got more meh as it went on.
The trailers made this whole war on Neptune seem like a big deal, pyramids in the sky, shadow legion marching in the streets.
Honestly the biggest let down was how Strand is utilized. Trailers were showing these big flashy skyscrapers and that Strand would be used to move around them but instead we got drip fed moments of Strand and spent more time on the ground/in buildings than in the air.
I would say get this DLC at a sale just for access to Strand...such a shame, i had been waiting for months just for an overpriced seasonal questline.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn complete honesty - This DLC is relatively fun. It's not the best one released and you can tell at certain parts of the story it was rushed. Strand however feels super smooth and is hours of fun over 3 characters. If you're a casual player jumping back in, you'll have fun with this expansion.

Pros
- Lots of added content for a fresh new look.
- Loadouts can now be saved which saves a lot of time
- Strand feels 10/10
- Story was well made, felt engaged during most parts
- Armour and guns added look amazing and are fun to use
- New environment is pretty to look at
- Witness is an amazing villain

Cons
- The story could have been longer, not as rushed towards the end
- Hard mode didn't seem as difficult as witch queen did (IMO)
- New city feels quite lifeless
- Lacks true "This is our final downfall" feeling

Overall
3/5 for guardians who play every season
4.5/5 for new lights
