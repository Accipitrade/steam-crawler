\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe story/narrative of the main campaign was, in my brutally honest opinion, pretty bad. So much fell flat for me and I truly felt that the new characters introduced as well as the city itself and ANYTHING to do with the Witness/The Veil/The Final Shape or anything were incredibly under or misused across the board.
But besides that? This expansion absolutely ROCKS.
The Music is PHENOMENAL. Strand is AWESOME. The QOL improvements across the board are things we have been asking for for YEARS. The new artifact perk system alone is huge, let alone an entire mod overhaul/buildcrafting changes/ the introduction of loadouts/the attempt to fix the New Player Experience with the Journey system....and so much more.
The fact that people would say lightfall is "Terrible" is absolutely beyond me. Yeah, the MAIN campaign was a disappointment, but as any avid destiny player would know we pay the entry price for a HELL of a lot more than just the main campaign. The main campaign is just the beginning, and the rest of the expansion unrolls throughout the year so that we as players keep coming back to get another bit of information.
Does this excuse the fact that we got virtually NOTHING in the way of information or answers about anything? No, of course not. And this campaign/expansion is DEFINITELY not perfect, but it's nowhere near terrible.
D2 might be in a weird, confusing, and convoluted spot for narratively...
But honestly in SO many ways there has never been a better time to jump into this game.
Just don't try too hard to understand what happened in the campaign 0_0\t\t\t\t\t
