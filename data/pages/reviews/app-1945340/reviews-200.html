\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA Disappointment.
It’s a huge letdown in every aspect. The story is boring and predictable, the new location Neomuna is bland and empty, the new powers are unbalanced and buggy, and the endgame content is scarce and repetitive.

The campaign is a slog through recycled missions and enemies, with no real stakes or surprises beside one or two moments.
The new location Neomuna is supposed to be a futuristic city, but it looks like a copy-paste of Europa with some neon lights. There’s nothing to explore or discover here, just empty streets and buildings.

The new powers are supposed to be based on Strand, a mysterious energy that connects everything in the universe. But they’re just reskins of existing subclasses with some tweaks.

Lightfall is a staggering downgrade that makes me worry for The Final Shape, the expansion meant to cap off the game’s Light and Darkness saga in 2024. Big story arcs like this are about time and payoff, but Lightfall fails on both counts. It feels like Bungie ran out of ideas and passion for Destiny 2, which is sad.\t\t\t\t\t
