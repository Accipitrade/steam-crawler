If you're a long time destiny 2 player, you will enjoy a lot of things about it like vanguard weapon focusing and stuff but as far as the DLC itself is concerned its garbage. The plot is nonsense, there is no feeling of there being a mystery that you the player can solve, just grandiose nonsense that will require more ridiculous technological mcguffins to figure out in the future. Ultimatley, there is still a lot of creativity in the team at bungie but its being horribly squandered, and the entire writing team should be fired, they have no clue what they're doing, and I have strong feeling they got hired to fill diversity quotas instead of because they have skill and experience at writing cohesive stories.\t\t\t\t\t
