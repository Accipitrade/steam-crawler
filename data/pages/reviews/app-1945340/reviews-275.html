\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnderwhelming expansion overall, not really worth the price tag imo

pros:
Great QoL changes, especially the mod manager. Overall the game is much more streamlined and buildcrafting is much more straightforward

Music is great as usual

Visuals of Neomuna are great


Cons:
Story falls flat hard. We are introduced to the veil and the radial mast without any explanation for what they are at all. You end up spending the entire campaign trying to figure out what you are trying to achieve. The story climaxes by the veil linking with the traveler (which is never explained on what that does, it just somehow means we have lost the war), and the witness ♥♥♥♥♥ off into a portal into the traveler that is never explained where it leads or what the ♥♥♥♥ is happening. Top that off, we get left on a cliffhanger without any of our questions being answered. While you move through the story you do missions in-between that you are forced to use strand and these parts act as basically strand tutorials. The strand missions are not that enjoyable mainly due to how weak strand is, especially against high health enemies

Characters are a mixed bag. Some are great, Osiris as usual is always an interesting character and watching him try to deal with not only the loss of Sagira but also trying his hardest to help save humanity from the second collapse is good. Nimbus on the other hand is extremely annoying, he just comes off as cringy and dumb almost all the time and his voice is grating. He really just ruins every scene he is in.

The location itself I personally find it doesn't fit in Destiny's universe. The Cloudstriders and Neomuna feel out of place with the gritty, crumbling world that is Destiny.

Weapon re-skins. Have not seen this brought up but it is one that pisses me off a lot. Every expansion and season we receive a set of new weapons that are completely original, never before seen models (e.g. throne world weapons for WQ and Seraph guns). For Lightfall and season of Defiance, we do not receive a single new model for a gun, all of the guns are re-skins or slight modifications to existing models. The Neomuna weapons are the moon weapons without the trinkets, they are still the same base moon model. Season of defiance is a series of Season of lost and 30th anniversary weapons that are just re-skins. Lightfall is charging more than WQ but WQ actually had new weapon models which really just does not sit right with me

_______________________

Overall, just wait for the price to be lowered or wait until later seasons when bungie tells us what the hell is going on\t\t\t\t\t
