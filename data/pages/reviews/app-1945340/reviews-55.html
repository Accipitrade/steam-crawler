\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay so hear me out, it's a good expansion... but not a 50 dollar one. So I recommend it just not at its current price.
I'll start with what everyone's talking about and that's the criticisms.
The story needed more for the price and that's absolutely true. Not only that but like many others it felt like there could have been so much more within the story than there was.

There is not enough interaction with the Vanguard for example. For such a huge moment in Earth's history and for such a dire situation back home, not enough is said or done with any of the heroes of the tower. Finding a thriving human colony is a BIG deal, and so is the Traveler being under attack.

Story wise it feels a bit like they didn't want to add more than they did because it would "over step" into the final shape, but what is there isn't all bad. Calus is still as interesting as ever, and the final mission was legitimately fun and challenging. It forced you to use and show off strand, and on legendary difficulty it takes a lot of patience and timing using strand to beat Calus and his tormentors in an awesome arena. Once again, though, Calus feels like he could have had more screen time in this DLC, there should have been more exploration of his invading flagship and interactions between calus and the witness, as well as the player.

Neomuna is a really pretty planet, with a cool city and GREAT music. I think the music really helped carry a lot of the DLC.
But like earlier, it feels like there could have been more. Neomuna has a lot of attention to detail with its hidden areas and lost sectors, and is by no means empty like I see some people say, but it does feel a little small. More world like the main city in the distance would have been really good for the price tag- the map not being as big is forgivable with a lower one.

The Cloudstriders story arc was cool, and the memorial room/grave site was cooler. However like many others it left a lot to be wanted, mainly the fact that again it feels like so many big moments within the DLC are rushed, moments that would have otherwise been more impactful like Rohans actions. Speaking of other characters Caital is well acted and every scene with her as the cavalry in the campaign is very nice. Osiris has always been a cool character (cough despite his lore actions) and continues to help carry this DLC and give an awesome VA performance.

Overall I think it was actually very fun to play, and very aesthetically pleasing. Strand is an awesome subclass and very versatile while still being fluid and original. The campaign, while short is fun and really shows you the ropes of strand.

Where it is lacking is in its story which feels like a condensed version of a clearly great idea they had.

There is still plenty of hours of content, just wish the developers took their time and delayed this so lightfall could have had its full potential realized.\t\t\t\t\t
