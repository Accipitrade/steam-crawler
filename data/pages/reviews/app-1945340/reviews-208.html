Not to mention Osiris and Nimbus are such a pain in the *ss, holy f*ck, Osiris is arrogant almost all the time, only stopping to be a sad f*ck and Nimbus acts like a annoying kid 99% of the time, the one guy who sound cool, died in the middle of the campaing...

On a side note, it wouldn't be Bungie if they didn't break PvP, not that I care, but when you must play to get rewards is kind of annoying having to deal with people exploiting things that Bungie did wrong...

The fact you must pay a good ammount of money and receive something like this with zero accountablity (or at best a "buy the next one, we will really try") from Bungie is laughable...

The one positive thing about all this are the loadouts, but this is a quality of life improvement, not something that should be praised when the rest of the DLC is this mess...\t\t\t\t\t
