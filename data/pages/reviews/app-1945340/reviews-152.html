\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally enjoyed the campaign and the glimpse of strand.
Great way of storytelling with the cutscenes. I like that bungie tries to do stuff different.
I can't comprehend why people are so mad? What was everyone expecting? Has Destiny ever been 100% without mysteries and cliffhangers? Was there ever a time where everything was clear? It certainly was not for the witch queen - but people seem to forget that and blindly jump on the hate train.

It's video game and the gameplay is fun (as usual) - it's not the new top of the line award winning science fiction movie/series (and it never wanted to be).
The music is great. Neomuna looks fantastic. Buildcrafting is superb. I like Nimbus (Cayde vibes). Everything is a little harder which is good (why do people expect or want a game that plays itself and receive free loot?). Overall I recommend the game to every destiny veteran - new lights might want to play with experienced friends to fully understand what's going on (yet I think the new guardian rank system adds a nice touch to the game for all players).

If you grind everything in one go - yes maybe you finish the story quickly (which does not account for the majority of players) but the story is fun to play through and interesting. Yes bungie set the bar pretty high with witch queen and lightfall comes close in some aspects and in others not so much. The tone is not as dark and serious as witch queen - which is still fun and a nice diversion. It's still action packed, adds interesting changes and quality of life updates - and yet so many people judge the whole dlc and season before having played more than a few days of it.

Is it the best experience ever in gaming history? No
Is there room for improvement? Yes
Do I enjoy playing it solo and with friends? Very much so.

Will there ever be anything where people won't have over the top expectations? Will the community ever learn to touch some grass and have a well-considered opinion? I surely hope so, because this DLC is great and whether we wanted it or not, we've stepped into a war with the Cabal on Mars... <3\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLook, i love this game I have 1500+ H on steam and probably 500+ H on console. Witchqueen was a blast i loved the main story, seasonal content and every subclass 3.0 we got each season. Yes, there were good seasons and bad seasons (looking at you nezcafe) also there were some owerpowered abilities and some underpowered ones. Stuff needed to be adjusted. There always will be things to adjust, dials to turn and Bungie will always adjust stuff. This is actually good. I dont wanna sound like complaining in that regard. However there is this notion of taking parts of the game that everyone likes; taking the parts of the gameplay that feels good and making them so overcomplicated and feeling bad. Recent AE changes are good example and they are reverting those back slowly.

And now with Lightfall, the game is straight up not fun anymore.

First of all story, there is no story. Look, i like to write stories, worlds and characters for my tabletop games with friends but I am not a writer at all and I respect writers because writing is hard, very hard. Also i truly think Destiny lore and story has so much good stuff to offer but Lightfall aint one of them

Secondly gameplay! New armour system feels awful, so restraining, some builds players loved to play straight up not achievable. Melee builds, swords builds, etc. We just wanted elemental armour affinities to go away and some tweaks, nerfs and buffs to our current perks inside a good organised UI with quality of life improvements. Not this!

Now, Strand, what exactly happened here? I understand Bungie doesnt want another stasis but thats why they separated PvE and PvP sandboxes, right? It feels so underpowered.

Lets also not forget new difficulty changes, they didnt made the game difficult, no, they just made it frustrating, especially for new light players and this expansion supposed to be the one that made the early game more player friendly? NO! Guardian ranks and thanking people after games, yes those are great additions but if the game feels frustrating why new light players would stick around to see all the other great experiences this game offers.

We can go and complain about everything but lets end it on this note. Lightfall only brought Destiny -the game i absolutely love- more negativity and frustration. And this makes me just sad. I dont write reviews in steam at all but this one, this one was from the heart. Until than i'll help my blueberry friends in witchqueen campaign so maybe they will stay :P
