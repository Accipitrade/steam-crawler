Masks
Much like the Mega City masks, all masks retain their base colors and textures when being customized. This means that coloring already on the mask will still appear after being customized.

This also means that hats with different base colors will use the same materials differently.

Here's the big issue; no material & color mask pattern exists. The existing Material Only pattern removes all color options. This is a huge issue because almost all of the new masks use those color options in special ways.

Outfits
Do you know how weird it is to see outfits that your cousins un-ironically wear? I feel like I'm about to be asked if I can help out down at the farm.

I will point out though that the Trailblazer outfit makes all the female heisters weirdly chunky, like somehow more than the male heisters.
