\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGive me more cats! The main Nutcracker story was a bit of a slog. I was expecting the character I was playing as to narrate the gameplay but most of the voice acting happened during sporadic cut scenes and occasional character introductions. Some of the puzzles fell on more of the difficult and boring side which made it feel like a chore compared to other HOGs I've played. They do have a strategy guide in the game which was a nice and detailed reference for when I would get stuck. Visually, while it isn't high quality, I absolutely loved the weird rat character design/movements and it is actually what sold me on the game. I also enjoyed my various companions in the game and getting to use their functionality, they were weirdly designed but I loved them (looking at you candle stick with live action Sonic teeth). The deciding factor on if I recommend the game is the bonus chapter. The Bonus Chapter was a lot more fun to play through. The bonus is contained to a smaller part of the map that has the more interesting locations, the puzzles are less frustrating/boring, and I enjoyed meeting/using all my cat companions.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn okay HOPA game with the Nutcracker Suite playing in the background and lots of Christmas decorations providing a seasonal feel. I didn't find the game very interesting overall, despite having a variety of puzzles (minigames). If you're looking for something very casual and seasonal, with a bit of a story and some HO scenes, this is probably worth picking up, but look for a good sale.

Pros:
- three difficulty settings, interactive map, integrated Guide
- variety and range of minigames (several challenging)
- cute collectible dolls in each room
- good voice acting
- nice cut-scenes (mini-movies)
- if you like cats, the bonus chapter is for you! [added to review]

Cons:
- older look and feel
- a lot of back-tracking
- unclear who the protagonist is or what is going on (there's a few mentions of Christmas magic, but nothing helpful
- general lack of excitement (my entirely subjective opinion); I tried but never got engaged in the story
