\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, one of my favourite action roguelites - bullet heavens.

Well balanced - keeps you on your toes even when run is going great - whole run feels intense.
Meta progression does not break the game. Getting up to 20 banish points is a good counter to increasing upgrade options pool due to new unlocks.
Many different upgrade options - many interesting builds. Some slightly more dependent on character you are playing, while others can be leaned into depending on what you get during the run.

Movement is smooth, can pull off some very satisfying maneuvers to avoid damage.

Fantastic music, fits very well with game theme.


Minor issues that could be addressed:
- Periodically spawning plasma on orbitals happens instantly rather than over time
- Plasma could be half transparent to help with visibility
- Lasers feel a bit meh compared to other weapon types (in terms of visceral impact feel)
- More color distinction between enemy projectiles and your explosions/orbitals - maybe just add bloom to enemy projectiles to make them stand out even more
- The upgrade that lets you keep one weapon when going ultimate is basically mandatory - every run without it feels bad. Might actually be best to remove it completely, or make it buyable with scrap, or just increase chance of getting it during a run.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the Vampire Survivors / Bullet Heaven style game stripped down to its core. It doesn't have the multiple layers of meta progression some of those games have. But what it does have is a simple game loop that is well executed. 4 Types of weapons with a number of variations each. The upgrades are divided, weapons from bosses, passives from scrap. Despite being simplified, every element is done very well. There's no single weapon that I would outright avoid, nor one that's a must have. The passives provide creative ways for weapon types to interact which in turn provides a lot of build options. If you ever play bullet heaven games and like to zone out because you don't have to fire and can just focus on dodging, this is a great one to try. Not that its easy, some of the characters were more challenging than others, and they have a good variety of individual abilities.\t\t\t\t\t
