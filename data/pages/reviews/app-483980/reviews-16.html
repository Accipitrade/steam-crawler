\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a very well made horror game with the usual old school graphics that has both the orgininal version AND the remake version.

The story is intriguing, mysterious and of course scary.
From horrific human expirements, to ghosts, monsters and traps that are all over the mansion of Aya's father...

Aya must save him and she has to stay alive to do so...

Besides that you can play both the old and the remastered version, the game has replayabilty with the IF mode that you play the game from someone else's perpective and the blood mode that you play a more mature version of Aya.

I wish all games from maker engines were as well made as Mad Father! I only can think of corpse party that was at the same caliber.

If you are a true horror fan, you won't regret getting this game!\t\t\t\t\t
