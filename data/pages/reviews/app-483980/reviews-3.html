Compelling story, good characters, dumb and fun ways to die, good characters. 9/10, I'd say Blood, the New Game + if you will, is a bit... meh, doesn't feel like it makes much sense, just an excuse to play the game again and see the protagonist post the events that happened years later... and feels a bit depressing without spoilers.
