\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMad Father is a game I've heard talked about a lot in the horror RPG Maker community, so needless to say, I was excited to play it! The only game I've played from the game developer Sen was Misao, so now having played Mad Father, it feels like a whole new level of quality.
That's not to say Misao isn't as good, in fact I enjoyed the setting and story in that game a lot more but you can tell the game play of Mad Father is a lot more polished.
I would have wished the aforementioned game-play had more tension or difficulty though as I found myself a bit bored at times. The story is serviceable and the music is okay at best but in the end, where I think this game shine best is it's nice pixel art :)\t\t\t\t\t

Story is dark and disturbing, yet it has been done before
The music is amazing, best part of the game
The artwork is nice but there isn't much of it, could use more anime artwork
