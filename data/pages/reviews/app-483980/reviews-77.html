Mad Father Review
By Brian Dean

Mad Father is an indie horror game that was made in RPG Maker and it is one of the games that defines the horror RPG genre. This game is a remake of the original version of Mad Father. I will go into my positives and negatives of the game

Positives:
-The game is like a puzzle RPG game and it feels good when you solve a puzzle or do something on your own
-The game has updated sprites and art from the original
-The soundtrack and atmosphere can surprisingly keep you on edge for a 2D horror game

Negatives
-The game is really short. I 100% it in 5 hours
-This doesn't really add much content to justify the price tag, I nearly recommend the free original version unless the "updated" visuals really are a thing that you care about and have extra cash to throw for it.

Overall, Mad Father is something that horror fans should experience one way or another, Whether it's the steam version or the original free version.
