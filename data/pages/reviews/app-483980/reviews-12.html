The story throughout the whole game was intriguing, and it kept me on my feet to find out what happened next; Hence why I tried to get every ending and achievements.
This game is very good for those who want to get into scary games without being extremely scared. The jumpscares were mild, and had very good timing. The puzzles were a good combination to the game, and it wasn't overly difficult. The art style is cute and adds a lot to the game. The pricing wasn't at all bad, and to be honest it was definitely a price worth paying for the journey I had while playing.
Altogether, I recommend this game to anyone who wants to give it a try!\t\t\t\t\t
