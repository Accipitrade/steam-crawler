\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was interesting, to say the least.

PROS:
There were a few great jump scares in the game that seriously sent me and chat reeling in our chairs. The artwork of the game was amazing and I loved the pixel theme the Devs went with. The plot-line had me crying at some points, cringing in others, and gasping too! It was a seamless game that was great for someone wanting to dip their toes into horror games!

CONS:
The true ending was really bad. I was so, genuinely disappointed that the only "good" ending was what it was. I spent 5 hours streaming for that ending? It was really disappointing to say the least. Although the jump-scares were good, after a while they just became predictable and became more annoying than anything else. I felt, as well, that the narrative lacked clarity in some parts where I really had no idea what was going on ...

Overall, I'd give this game a solid 7/10!!! I think it really is great for someone wanting to start exploring the horror genre :)\t\t\t\t\t
