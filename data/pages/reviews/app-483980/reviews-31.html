\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game dev and I love this game! Though I will say I liked Misao better, I did enjoy this game too!! The story was very developed and it was actually spooky scary (rather than the whole startling vs disturbing horror seen in Misao). Though I never played the original version, my friend who did told me the differences they saw between the two. In this remake, there are many different endings, as well as the IF and Blood epilogue stories. The IF mode further on a character I loved and believed deserved more screen time, and the Blood mode not only allowed replay-ability but a reflection by the main character. The puzzles were a bit more difficult than Misao's, so I did need a walk-thru pulled up just in case, but it didn't ruin the immersion. All the endings and added content furthered the story along, dragging you in. I was actually scared for a lot of the game. Plus, if there was any game that should be turned into an anime, it's this one (that's all I could think about in my mind). I wonderful experience and I'm tempted to play the original now. Definitely recommend!! :)\t\t\t\t\t
