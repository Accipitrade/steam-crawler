\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis RPG maker horror rpg is one of the more popular games of this type. It's short but definitely worth playihng.

The story is decent and poses some moral questions. Despite having only one ending and being linear the small-dosed way of delivery leaves you with some ambiguity encouraging interpretation .Some characters could have been developed better but considering the low budget of this product I can't complain. Numerous creative puzzles provide enough variety and balance to keep you entertained. Some extra content is a cherry on top.

The art and occasional CGs were done well. The pixel art was done up to standard and so were the locations.
The music is slightly above average. It fits the game well and often enhances events.
No bugs encountered.

Overall, very pleasant game to kill some time with. For genre enthusiast it's definitely worth playing. 7/10
