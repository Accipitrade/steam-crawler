\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere to begin?

This was the first time I tired a game like this yet right off the bat the setting and music caught my eye and had me playing from start to finish in one sitting. It is not a long game by any means and even whilst appreciating everything you can still finish the base story in under 4 hours yet that does not take away from the quality.

Exploring the house felt thrilling and fun, with sprinkled in quick time events that do enough to keep you on your toes yet are not punishing enough to halt your gameplay. Music and atmosphere is immersive and though you aren't truly ever going to find out everything about the background of the characters you meet and come across, you are given enough to form your own assumptions as to why things have gotten to where they are and how said things involve your own character. The new added blood mode also helps answer many of your questions whilst creating new ones that the player can only speculate about.

All in all fun little exploration game that won't take up too much of your time and that you can enjoy if you're in the mood for a horror setting without the plague of consistent cheap jumpscares that a lot of movies and games fall into a habit of.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLove this game, love the jumpscares and love the mysteries! The plot is pretty disturbing and emotional, but that is what makes the plot so interesting! The end almost had me in tears, the developer did a great job with setting out the plot of the game and designing our MC.
HOWEVER, I played the blood mode and I was pretty disappointed that there weren't many differences, sure we find out a little bit more about the Drevis family and we play as the adult version of the MC but I was expecting more such as new enemies, new maps, new quests, new characters etc...but the fact that the developer even bothered to make a remake of this game when it was 8 years old is very good, just goes to show the dev still cares about this game!

I really wish they release a sequel of this game, since the MC ends up like her father we can play as another MC (Robert the blonde guy perhaps) to stop her\t\t\t\t\t
