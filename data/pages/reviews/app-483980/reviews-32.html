\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI remembered watching Markiplier play this game back in his early days of YouTube, I remember first seeing the playthrough when I was in High School and instantly grew to love the game especially for its story. Seeing it now having been not only remastered but remade as well, I wanted to give the game a try. Game feels a bit easier compared to its former variant however, but at least it gives me a bit of a feel for it, I do enjoy a lot of the changes done with the game especially giving more story to characters that needed it including Monika (Aya's mother).

I actually spent time wanting to really succeed at this game and even getting the Gems which wasnt so much a chore at all, I greatly recommend this game to anyone who likes these type of games honestly, including Misao (I have yet to play that one). Overall I enjoyed this game more as I played its remake both on here and on the Switch as well. If there was one thing I could say that I liked but at the same time felt really off about, was while I do like how it was emphasized more on how insane Alfred (Aya's father) was by giving him some more voice lines, making him laugh more maniacally and repeatedly saying Aya's name....at the same time I felt a bit off about him practically moaning her name when he rammed into the door, it felt weird but I guess it still makes up for how insane he really is.

Now if I really want to say something I didnt actually like overall with the game was, while the extra lore was good to add to give some more story especially more about Monika during Blood Mode, some bits of the lore also kind of contradict some bits that were already existent, such as making Monika seem a bit more crazy than Alfred and making it seem like Alfred had some common sense despite him still being practically crazy. The story could use a little bit of work but at the same time maybe I am just missing something.

Overall really loved this game as I have already said before, and would gladly recommend this game to anyone, including some of my own friends as well. I thank Markiplier for getting me into this game.\t\t\t\t\t
