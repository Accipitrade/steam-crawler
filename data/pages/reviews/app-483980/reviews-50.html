\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played the original, free version of this before. I was expecting some changes, because before this i played the remake of Misao (a different game from the same producer) and a lot of things were different. To my surprise, almost nothing has changed. The dialogue is all the same (which is good), the graphics are slightly improved i guess but it's not a big improvement, the OST is the same, and the map and gameplay is exactly the same.

There are a few minor changes, like changing the way you free yourself from a monster that is attacking you, in the original game you had to mash the Z button and here there is a minigame where you have to press 5 random arrow keys a few times in a row, i think it's an ok change. They also changed the location of one gem, but it's mostly the same.

The game is pretty short and i beat all endings in a total of 3 hours, but i already knew the solution to all of the puzzles etc. because i have already played the original game before. I think it would take around 5 - 6 hours for a blind playtrough.

Overall i recommend this game, and if you give it a go and enjoy it you should also check out Misao.\t\t\t\t\t
