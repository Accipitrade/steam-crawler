\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI. Love. This. Game.

I've always been a fan of horror, but horror games have a knack for relying on cheap jump scares and more often than not, the plot is not very interesting.

This is one of those games where there are almost no jump scares, but the atmosphere and setting of the game is enough to keep you on your toes when exploring the mansion (I highly recommend playing with headphones in the dark for full effect). The story itself is great because all endings give you a different
perspective to a character, and the true ending will make you question your choice instead of "ahh this is how it should have ended". More than that, with all endings discovered, the plot is not comprehensive to the point where they lay out the story for you, but rather leaves you enough room to ponder about each character.

I've thoroughly enjoyed my 7hrs experience playing this, and will highly recommend this to anyone looking for a good psych horror game and can appreciate a good plot.\t\t\t\t\t
