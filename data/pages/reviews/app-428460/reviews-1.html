I personally take about 20-30 minutes per game (unless I lose in the first ten). Definitely recommend playing in Custom or Bible Study mode if you don't want to affect your level ranking status! Bible Study mode provides both good and bad consequences to your multiple-choice response, making it worth cracking open your Bible (or clicking the online Bible links provided) for the right answer. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn terms of mechanics this is adaptation of a board game which is very similar to pandemic. It may sound unimpressive but I actually like this game a lot, here is why.

Firstly, I find the aesthetics of this game very appealing. Drawing are very artful, animations are smooth, music serves great as a pleasant background.
Secondly, I believe that this setting of ancient Israel is very well suited to the mechanics and builds a very unique atmosphere.

Some may be attracted to it or might be put off by overly religious aspect of this game. I think both group of people should be satisfied.

It is true that map, the names of places and events are based on stories from Bible, which definitely makes this game very unique. However you don't need to be a religious at all person to enjoy the game. You can completely switch off The Bible trivia questions and reduce the religious aspect and just enjoy the game itself. And if you want to discover the additional layer, then it is there for you to explore and perhaps do some research on your own about places and events mentioned in the game. But that's completely optional. It should be mentioned however that those looking into very deep philosophical or relighous content might be dissapointed.

Overall, a great, relaxing game you will enjoy, well worth the money. I would be very happy to see more games like this or perhaps some DLCs for this game.\t\t\t\t\t
