Aside from the ranged mutations (which are almost required to get past the 2nd stage) the other mutations are just decoration. There are no strategies that become available to you if you luck out and find the "walk slightly faster" ability or the "one extra inventory slot" ability. There's very little that makes this a rogue-like (aside from perma-death) and RNG has very little to do with winning and losing.

I do like the announcer though.
