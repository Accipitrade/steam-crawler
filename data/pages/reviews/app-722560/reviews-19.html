\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRAD is a fun rogue-lite that is full of incredible art and impactful upgrades. The game starts simple, giving you just a bat to attack your enemies with, but the way you attack enemies quickly changes as you gain new mutations. The game takes place in a beautiful apocalyptic world full of mutant life, secrets, and little bits of lore. I spend half my time just exploring the map to look for little hidden areas or new NPCs. The visuals and sounds are bright and energetic, which I have never seen in an apocalyptic game before. Overall, it gives RAD a playful but honest tone.

I think there is too little variety in the game to get infinite replayability, but I expect to get a couple dozen amazing hours out of this game. I highly recommend!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t TL:DR - very nice game!

I had this game on my wishlist for a few weeks or even months now. I first heard about it whilst looking for another roguelike with some kind of mutation mechanic. Sadly, i had to wait until today, 20.08.19, for it to be released, but i think it was worth waiting for.

Comparison
If you played "The Binding of Isaac" or "Enter the Gungeon" this game might be for you. If you also played any of the Borderlands games and liked the art style and the silly humor, this game is almost certainly something for you.

Controls
I play this game with mouse & keyboard and at first the controls seemed a bit finicky, but after i found out that you can change it* so you aim with the mouse (with the cursor to be precise) everything was fine :D
* (you only have to press tab in game)

Game Mechanics
I like the progression you can make within the game by expanding the item range the shop in town offers, unlocking new weapons and learning all about the different mutations. The game is not that easy but i like that it poses at a bit of a challenge - at least for me :P

Double Fine Productions
About the developer i can only say that i also bought "Day of the Tentacle - Remastered" and "Brutal Legend", was quite happy with the games and had a lot of fun playing them.

Complaints
The only thing i would like them to add is the option to change the color of the cursor, because in some areas i find its color to be too similar to the color of the ground and sometimes that makes it hard for me to see where i am aiming at.

Summary
If you want to play a solid 3D rougelike comparable to "Isaac" or "Gungeon" that is also a little bit challenging and contains a lot of funny lines and jokes - this game is definitely something for you.
Also you get awesome synth wave background music and a badass announcer to go with it.
So... what more can you wish for? :D\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is terrible. I really wanted to love it. The voicing is great, the soundtrack is amazing, and the art style is incredible.

The combat is a complete disaster though. If you run into an elite mushroom, your run is over unless you just run away from it or have a ranged mutation. There is no difference in different attacks, aside from the lunge attack, which is the only way to safely engage most things. I used a heatmap and found I used dodge 90% more than any other ability, and it's definitely a REQUIREMENT. Not sure if this is just poor tuning or the gameplay is just not my style, but I shouldn't have to spend 75%+ of my run time dodging abilities that can three shot you. Elites don't get staggered nearly as much as normal enemies, so they are almost unmanageable depending on your mutation RNG. Normal monsters aren't bad once you play enough to learn their abilities and can easily avoid or stagger them. The elites though... What a mess. 2 damage minimum no matter what if you get hit, and may god have mercy on you if one of them hits you with normals around you because it's basically just a gang bang from there and a sure death.

As far as mutation RNG goes, I consistently just got the same mutations, almost the exact same mutations, after 15 runs in a row. Got boring really fast. I requested a refund, but I really hope they get it together and work out the kinks. I am a VERY dedicated player and not even I will subject myself to the broken combat in this game. I hope it gets better so I can return and enjoy it, but for now I gotta say good bye.

I was so excited for this game.\t\t\t\t\t

TL;DR:

1) Will this game be able to compete with the Rogue-Like giants? No
2) Do you crave a new Rogue-Like game / experience? Only your wallet can say yes.
3) Colorful, 80s vibe, humor
4) Variety? Yes
5) Leaderboards? Yes
6) Daily Challenges? Yes
7) Gamepad RECOMMENDED

Intro

Starting off by saying that I normally don't leave down reviews, but for this game, i'm making an exception.
- I only have 3-4 hours put down into the game, and mostly just watched gameplay from CrapAttackTV from the Early Access.

What is it?

RAD has a bit of a sarcastic humor to it considering they experienced 2 apocalyptic events. (Sounds like they really want to be wiped off the face of the earth, huh?)
This game can only be really compared to Binding of Isaac, and i'd have to say that you should only grab it when you're really desperate for a "New" rogue-like experience.

Gameplay

You're forced into mutations quite often, making decisions into a certain build almost IMPOSSIBLE, so you have to make do with what you're given, but this also gives it a bit of a flair of variety when playing and dying over, and over. Sometimes you just don't have any control and just die from bad RNG. If you dislike failing from purely RNG, this game is not your cup of tea.
- However, there are items that can be bought that enhances your passives. Want to be immune to poison, fire, electric, etc? Want to buff your crit chance, or walking speed? There are enough "Endos" that enhances passively "that gives a sense of a build"

What about the rest of the game? Enemy variety comes with experience as you progress further into the game. When I came to World 2-1, the enemy changed to bigger, more dangerous of what you already know, but also added new ones, and new way to get to your objective. I haven't personally seen 3-x world yet though.

The world is bright, colorful, and vibrant. It can almost be blinding for some people, and you will be stuck with an Isometric view that only zooms out when needed for larger encounters.
- The music adds in and gives it an 80s type of vibe with Chill/Synthwave type flow, and the dialog gives me a Transistor vibe to the game.

Outro

Do I recommend this game? Yes, and no.

K+M is iffy as hell, I can only recommend a Gamepad.
RNG can ruin the flow of the game

Only buy if you really crave another new Rogue-Like games and understand what a Rogue-Like is. There are better games out there, but if you're a die hard Rogue-Like fanboi, then I do recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPROS:

So far this game is VERY COOL. Earn your HARD CASH and slay foes with great retro 80's style with a modern twist. Fans of Brutal Legend will find a strange nostalgia here. Gameplay is fluid, the story is interesting even if it is cliche but it is because of its cliche that its interesting because it doubles down and gives you not one but TWO apocalypses! Spread flower power wherever you go and retrace your steps to cover the map in glory. It is great and it is refreshing at least for me but there are some incredible optimization issues.

CONS:

The game is almost completely GPU intensive.. My 8700k rarely reaching over 15% utilization while my 2080ti maxed out just to reach a decent 60FPS in 4K and even dipping down into the mid 40s at times... I feel like with a game with graphics such as this 4K 60FPS should be a tickle and an Anti Aliasing slider would be nice... Although this isnt a game that requires high framerates, it would be nice to sit at 60 or higher comfortably even though a consistent 30fps is just fine if you dont mind some of that peasant life... Ill give it the benefit of the doubt though because this is day one and it could be a lot worse.

With the cons so far only being performance related Id say this is a game well worth its price. Be rad... Get this game and enjoy its colorful visuals and fun gameplay. The soundtrack is pretty rad too.\t\t\t\t\t
