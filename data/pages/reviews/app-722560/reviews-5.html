\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt pains me to rate this game negatively because the artstyle is amazing and it has a lot of cool ideas. The gameplay flows really well in the typical roguelike fashion - venture out, gather stuff, get a bit better every time and go a bit further. The world seems interesting and I just love the overall style.

The problem is the gameplay which has several problems. First of all: it is very repetitive. You attack with the X button and that is about it. With mutations you can enhance your character and get a ranged attack or additional abilities, but the bulk of the damage comes from your melee weapon. Here comes the biggest problem: attacking locks you in place until the animation is finished. It makes fighting extremely awkward and it becomes almost impossible evading the attacks of some enemies, particularly bosses. In the end it comes down to dodging the boss attack, then attacking once, then dodging again because you wont get another attack off in time before you get damaged due to animation lock.

There are some other smaller issues, like it not being much of a roguelike because you dont really unlock much between runs, but that singular issue ruined the entire game for me. I wouldve loved to see more of it, but attacking is so unnecessarily clunky and unfun that it makes the game very frustrating. It is a shame because the potential is definitely here and without this unnecessary animation lock, the game would be a million times more fun. With it, though, it just devolves into frustration. Give the art guys a raise, though, because they did an amazing job - but playing the game isnt very fun.\t\t\t\t\t
