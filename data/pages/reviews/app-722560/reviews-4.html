\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat do I do with this game?
Its a fantastic game. On the other side there is stuff that brings me to rage.
Maybe I recommend it but I start with

WARNING: Fatal Errors!
Here and there you will meet minor but annoying bugs.
But not much is more frustrating than fighting your way through the last level, enter the final Boss and... the game crashes with a Fatal Error. And this has happened more than once.
So be warned, this game is relatively buggy!

On the other hand...
Nevertheless it's a fantastic game.
Though the maps Design is the same for each level-class, the Map shape is different each time.
Also the possible mutations differ from every runthrough, which give the game a stable value for replay.
Its even better since you have some meta progress.

Quick summary of the content
Just in short:
You are a kid of your choice, starting with a club of your choice, entering the levels, beat everything up, collect and buy whatever you may find usefull and try to get your favourite abilities (mutations) to finish a runthrough.
Everything in a funny, post-apocalyptic world.

Frustration
Since the game can get really tricky, mostly depending on your mutations-luck, it can be frustrating to start over and over again after while. The randomness of mutations and items helps a little bit, so it won't get too boring.

The price
I would say the price (20 bucks atm) would be okay for a gold disc status.
Sadly such thing does not really exist anymore nowadays.
If your tolerance level for bugs is high, then the full price is aceptable.
But if you are easily annoyed by bugs, then I would say half price is really perfect to live with this amount of bugs.

Alltogether
Yes... in general Bugs are a real pain and a reason for me to not recommend a game.
But since I like this game so much, I spare the ranting and recommend it. But very close. Next time Double Fine will not get through with it that easily. ;)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell this game could be awesome if not for some problems that completly anihilate all the good point.

I'll resume this review for those who doesn't want to read it in all.

The setup ( music, graphism, etc ) of the game is great and original but the gameplay is averall lacking as well as the roguelite element while the game is made difficult because of the way you have to play it ( need for completed attack animation to do another attack or action ). So overall, the book's cover is attractive, but the content is lacking or even bad at times.

First, the game is beautiful really, it's a bit flashy sometimes but I love the graphism.
It's the same for the OST their are really cool and fit well in the game.

But what pains me is the roguelite element, they suck.

First the money, I know this type of game is not supposed to be tender on us but else you make the price fairer or you raise the drop of money.

Next is the mutations, they are not really that useful. Some are, but since there is not much of them and many are barely useful well sometimes you ask yourself why you try to get them.

Chest are also a bad point to me. I don't think locked chest are the problem, the real problem is that every chest is locked which means you need a key for each one of them and key's drop rate is even less than money. Plus, since you don't know what you'll get, sometimes you feel like you wasted a key.

The difficulty is also something that amaze me. I played The Binding of Isaac, and it is a difficult game but you mostly take damage because you don't pay attention or because you do an error. But here there are mobs that can do you damage without attacking you, they just have to face you for you to take damage...

Some people already said it but attacking need to complete the animation to let you get control over your characer and so, most mutations that would let you do crazy combo don't because you have to wait for every litle animation to end. Then do shorter animation because sometimes you could do massive damage but you do not because you need to evade else it's free damage.

It's just a detail, but there is a plant in the game that do the same things as a flipper's bumper and appart from making you fall a cliff or launching ennemies at you, they do not have any interest at all.

It could have been a really great game and the global set is here but when you take a closer look, it's unpolished and clumsy.\t\t\t\t\t
