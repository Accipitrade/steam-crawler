\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely not!

Pros
.Lovely artwork/graphics/scenes
.Appropriate music/sounds/voice acting
.Four difficulty modes including custom
.Mini-map with fast travel (receive in-game)
.Stationary toolbar
.Intricate to simplistic adventure play
.Variety of hidden object scenes
.Bonus chapter

Cons
.Story very basic (kidnapped relatives)
.Mini-map legend in another language
.No indication when objects in correct position for possible use
,Mechanics were a little slow and clunky
.Easy to annoying mini-puzzles (with vague instructions)
.Very short, even with bonus chapter
.On first run thru, game froze at final boss battle and would not play any further
.On second run thru, game was very glitching and quit twice
.On second run thru, game again froze at final boss battle (but did eventually continue)

Final words (2/10):
I enjoyed the adventure play and hidden object scenes, was often annoyed with the mechanics and mini-puzzles, and disappointed over the length but it had still earned a five out of ten from me. Then it froze. And glitched. And quit. And froze again. So I cannot recommend it. (And I will be deleting it.)\t\t\t\t\t
