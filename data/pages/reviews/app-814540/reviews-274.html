\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDont expect this to be a formal review, its kinda hard to describe what I felt with this game. Id consider myself to be biased because of how heartfelt the dev was, and you could sort of understand his thoughts on designs as you progress through the game, as well as the ending credits. I could relate to it, being a dev myself in a similar situation. The strong philisophical core, great puzzles and story telling really hit me hard and got me stuck in my own head just thinking for a few days. It ignited some of my own passions, and gives me one of those intense feelings to just meet the dev and have a conversation about it. To be blunt, this is absolutely a fetish game. Theres no way around it, as im sure theres plenty of others who have already said that. But its BECAUSE it has sexual content, that it makes me more appreciative of the game. Simply because of the fact that it isnt entirely sexual content. It makes me understand it more as a sort of adulterated realism. Theres plenty of other things that this game has to its credit. The puzzles hit a niche between hard and satisfying thats addicting, and the story, albeit simple on the surface, is very personal and engaging. Nothing comes to mind about what i see as negative to this game, though it just might be me being ignorant. It certainly has flaws, i just cant name any off the top of my head.

Summary: Buy this game if youre either really kinky, or want to feel personally invested and engaged in a world and setting, in which someone very passionately made.
