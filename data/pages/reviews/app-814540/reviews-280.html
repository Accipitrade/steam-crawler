\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is not fun to play. It has some of the stupidest puzzles I have ever seen. They give you absolutely no information and expect you to just figure it out. Being made in RPG Maker also means the chase segments suck. You can't really run away from an enemy that easily if your movement is stiff. RPG Maker is for slower paced exploration, not for fast paced evading enemies. There's "stealth" segments but again due to being made in RPG Maker it's incredibly bare bones and not that fun.

As for things I do like, I think the idea of the story is really interesting. It seems like an intriguing concept. I also quite like the art style, it's a cute looking game. I think this style would look really nice in a more... family friendly title. I really do think this game would have a ton more potential if the puzzles were improved and it wasn't made in RPG Maker. Maybe Game Maker? If this game got a remaster in an engine that allowed for better movement and the puzzles were a bit easier to understand, I'd check that out.

Overall the game had potential, but due to the fact that it's in RPG Maker and the puzzles requiring a guide to solve the game flops. If you're interested in the story and characters, I'd say find a no commentary play through on YouTube. If you're just in it for the furry sex then just go use e621.
