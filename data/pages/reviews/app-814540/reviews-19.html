\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really want to like this game. To be honest, I'll probably keep playing it, if only to say that I finished it and feel somewhat justified for having spent money on it. But it is one of the most infuriating things I have ever tried to play through. The "puzzles" are only challenging because the slightest mistake leads to you dying and having to start over from the last checkpoint, which is usually farther back than it should be and stuck behind a cutscene or unskippable lines of dialogue. Half of the boss fights don't even have save points right after them. This means you have to trudge through the same scenes again and again only to keep dying to the same thing. Almost every single room has the threat of death and losing progress if you misstep or investigate the wrong item, often for seemingly no reason except because they can. The mechanics are not explained well and leave you trying to figure them out yourself most of the time. None of the chases seem to have any sort of consistency. It's purely luck whether or not you are able to succeed at them, and the enemies move so stupidly quickly that even the tiniest slip up causes you to get caught instantly with no chance for correction. Even if you don't appear to make any mistakes, you still end up dying half of the time. The game is difficult--and not in a fun way. It has potential, but it's nearly impossible to focus on the story or enjoying the experience when I'm too busy being frustrated about the gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
