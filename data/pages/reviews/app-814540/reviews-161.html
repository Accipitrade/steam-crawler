\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is really good.

We all know the reason you want to buy/bought this game, but even if you're someone who doesn't care for the weird ass transfurmation fetish bait, or isn't a furry this game is really â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥ good.

Story: 8.5/10 Story is pretty damn good, I loved to walk around and interact with everything, the game has great world building and really allows you to understand where you are and what these areas once were, for a pretty short game the developer did a really good job, especially with Puro

Gameplay: 7/10 The game has these really weird running sections that are fairly RNG based, I swear I will take the same path over and over and each time the enemies will move differently causing a different outcome, this wouldn't be an issue if these sections weren't so difficult and require pixel perfect movement. Some of these are easier than others and they're all so satisfying to complete. Puzzle segments are always fun, well designed, and clever.

Artstyle: 9/10 people say the game looks ugly, and I did too but after getting used to the visuals, its not bad looking at all. The characters are well drawn and cute, and spritework is nice. Only issue I have with the artstyle is the weird, and confusing perspective it creates. The main character is 4 units (spaces squares boxes tiles whatever) tall, and 1 unit wide, this was done to make the character seem reasonably tall in this 3d environment which is fine, but the problem is the boxes which are used for multiple of the puzzles in the game, are half the size of the player, this makes a confusing perspective that is hard to describe but makes these box sections impossible until you get used to it. Another thing is these puddles that you have to avoid that if stepped in "transfur" you, they are 3 units wide which is fine except for the fact that the hitbox is straight in the middle of these puddles, and only one unit. Making some sections look impossible to pass without getting "transfurred". After learnign abou these things though the game was much more enjoyable.

Music:Not much to say, its pretty good, fits the game really well, not something I would go out of my way to listen to like Undertale OST but still, pretty good.

Basically this game is really good, and I would recommend this to all my friends if they were furries, but unfortunately they are sane.

I would make a Youtube video about this game, but my family knows my channel, and I don't want to lose subscriber :(

Overall Score: 8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
