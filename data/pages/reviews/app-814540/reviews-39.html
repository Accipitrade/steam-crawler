A room that you not only have no way of knowing how to access but also cannot deduce that it exists. Or in a part where you have to guess the password of a machine and the note that has it is hidden on the side of the machine (all very fair). Needless to say, anyone serious about a good puzzle will not like this. And if it turns out that you are saving like a freak/hating this game: Your save point brings back with it all your previous progress; If not careful though, you may have to replay an entire section you've already passed because of unfair puzzles or traps placed so they cannot be seen by players who are not good at solving them.
The only thing I took away from the game is the music, which I found retro and enjoyable. The characters are decent but do not have the same level of detail as those in other games. The settings are also below average; although they were colorful and diverse, there were still several things missing from them. Finally, the main incentive to play was the story--although it was interesting and engaging at first, it soon became overshadowed by all of the other problems that I've mentioned before. In conclusion, this game needs to be fixed. Although it entertained me for a while and I congratulate the creator on his creative abilities, it is not easy to make a video game; They should definitely consider making changes to improve the game before releasing it again.\t\t\t\t\t
