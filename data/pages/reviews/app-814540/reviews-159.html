\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI liked it.
I divide this game into two parts:
The Gameplay is fun and challenging. One downside is that sometimes you have to re-watch a cutscene when you "die", which gets annoying (especially when you "die" a lot). This Problem is being remedied in the special-Version though, which is free with the base game but at the time of this review not yet finished.

About the setting: You either find it strange or you like it. When you count yourself to the "strange" section but you still want to play the game you can simply make more use of the "F12"-Key. It brings you straight back to the main-menu so you can reload your safe and try again.
If you like the setting then what are you waiting for? Enjoy!

One last thing: I wish the controls were changeable. Maybe the "F1"-Menu could help, but I can't read the language it's written in.\t\t\t\t\t
