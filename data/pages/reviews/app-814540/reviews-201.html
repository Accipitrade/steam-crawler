Going into this game was nothing but tedium, frustration, input lag up the ass, trollish dev behavior, and straight up ABHORENT game design. Don't get me wrong, the idea, the world, the characters and the latex fetishy theme are all cool. But dealing with this gameplay whatsoever is like poking a steaming turd. Here's some ways the gameplay is aforementioned smoking turd.

AI can just say "♥♥♥♥ you, die," and their is no counterplay. You essentialy must figure out a route then pray to rnjesus that the AI doesn't kill you. Again, no counterplay, you can pick the right route and the AI will still kill you without you being able to do anything about it. This is ♥♥♥♥ game design

If you like exploring, don't. This game heavily discourages exploring anything out of the blue. You may have gotten past a very hard section and decided to go check a box for any cheeky text. Welp nope, ♥♥♥♥ you, the dev just ♥♥♥♥ all over you and punished you for looking at a random box after a hard "boss." Now go back, do the boss again and get back to the area again. This is ♥♥♥♥ game design!!!

Puzzles are bad...lol the puzzles in this game just make you backtrack nonstop and that is the ONLY gimmick to these puzzles. So if you have to do a hard part and a puzzle I really hope you took a screen shot where it matters because if you haven't you got to back track now potentially die then do the hard area all over again. This is ♥♥♥♥ game design

Puro is adorable, game play wise he's an obstical. Your "adventuring companion" has a hit box you can't walk through and can make you get stuck in a restart situation. *This is ♥♥♥♥ game design*

I can go on and on and on about how bad, frustrating, and unfair the "gameplay" is in this game. (Lol if you consider walking only game play) I HIGHLY HIGHLY HIGHLY RECOMMEND just watching this game DO NOT BUY OR PLAY IT. The game isn't fun to play, is frustratingly unfair, has terribly input laggy controls, and is just an absolutely awful game play experience. Watch some other bloke tear his hair out trying to play this game. Enjoy watching all the endings, but whatever you do save yourself the 6 bucks and DON'T PLAY this game.

So i decided to make a Steam account and get this game to see if I could maybe play alongside him. I loaded into the game, picked my character and world and started playing but I was stuck on what you where supposed to do. I asked my Son for help and he hosted a game for me to join. I loved it as it was the best time I had spent with my Son since my wife had died. This game has ever since brought me and my son closer again and now we actually spend time together outside the house together as well.
