in all seriousness, the gameplay is fine, the art is cute, and the music is...there. The main draw is of course the concept of transfurring itself. But there is more reason to play it. The story is nice, and has a good sense of mystery and wonder as you travel through this abandoned building. The difficulty can be a bit too high, but its usually not to bad, since you get treated to a nice little art piece when you die. Deaths are in fact a feature of the game, with many different enemies to lose to, and each giving a unique death animation (mostly).

Only really play the game if youre a furry, or are interested in this sort of transformation thing. If the game was changed to be a bit less...suggestive, id be able to recommend it to a larger audience but as of now its a little stealth adventure game with a bit of funny furry kinks thrown in\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly? Changed is a pretty simplistic looking game that has some challenging puzzles if you're not that much into the puzzle genre. Most of the, well, "bosses" in the game seem to have some level of challenged that involve mechanics used in the area you were just in and require you to learn various patterns and techniques. If you're not used to them, you will fail a lot, but it's never too unrewarding as long as you know how to save.

Aside from that, the whole post-apocolyptic setting told throughout the game alongside some of the scenary you do end up viewing makes the situation much more bleak on whether or not you should really venture back out into the unknown of the world. It makes you think on why you should progress and leave the area when it's probably much worse than the situation in the building you're in.

Game overall is a nice 6.5/10. It's nothing ground-breaking and there's not really much to hold it back in enjoyment, but the game is very niche for players and can definitely cause problems for those who are disgusted by things like latex, transformation, and even furries. I still recommend giving it a go despite all this and enjoying the game out for yourself if you can.\t\t\t\t\t
