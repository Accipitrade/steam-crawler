\r\n\t\t\t\t\tterribly disguised furry goo kink game 10/10

my honest review about the gameplay of this game is that this game is weirdly difficult yet seems easy at the same time, it's sort of got that cuphead effect where you think you're able to beat a boss even though you've died to it like 10 times. i had to restart this game twice because i didn't give the funny black goo animal enough love, it's definitely a lot easier when you know what you're actually doing so a walkthrough is basically essential to playing this game. the puzzles start off not that bad and then become the hardest ♥♥♥♥ ever (in the true ending route that is).

now about the goo animal stuff...

i knew what to expect when i bought this game and sort of jokingly played it, and despite possibly being a furry and enjoying the non-toxic part of the furry fandom, this ♥♥♥♥ was sometimes like a horror game. i kid you not i screamed in fear when i witnessed the first white latex creature suddenly appear, have a love heart speech bubble over its head and then proceed to chase me. also that one balcony scene with the snow leopard has ♥♥♥♥♥♥ scarred me.

overall difficult game but cute (yet questionable) art\t\t\t\t\t
