\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen I first bought the game, I wasn't expecting much of anything, but when I started playing it, I realized that this game was more than what people made it out to believe. To start off, I thought of this as a puzzle game, & only a puzzle game. So I was already expecting high difficulty throughout.

This game I'd say has a detailed story line, a few complex mechanics, as well as some very strange moments throughout the game. The puzzles are generally difficult as you need to constantly die, or "transfurred" as they call it, to find the set pattern to proceed. Additionally, there are some hidden notes & items scattered around the place that give additional insight on the story. Another thing to note is the artwork implemented. This game gives off an 80's or 90's feel to it, & the music gives off an 80's or 90's arcade setting in my opinion.

It's very fast paced & requires you to have quick reflexes. In many cases, I'd have that feeling of adrenaline rushing through my heart while running from the creatures chasing me down. If you like frustratingly difficult & quick paced puzzle games, I'd highly recommend giving this game a try.\t\t\t\t\t
