\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was encouraged to get it by my friends as the archetypal furry game, I am so glad it turned out to be so so much more <3.
The difficulty is definitely there full force and it's not the most casual gaming experience however it's not punishing of failure, only setting you back 2 screens or so for the worst failures. The gameplay is tough but it's tough in being genuinely fair challenge and about learning and adapting to your failures and not about being an endurance test or entirely dependent on RNG. Everything can be repeated until mastery even if it isn't intuitive right away and there is a good variety of puzzles, obstacles requiring thought out strategies and quick thinking scenarios. The art style is detailed and gorgeous and incites that same wonder I feel every time I play modern games that nail pixelated art styles, for me while it is certainly developed on it's own it echoes Undertale and Deltarune, in fact this game reminds me a lot of them. The story and lore are sparse but like everything else it's up to the player to piece it together, you really feel it through the eyes of the Human protagonist. The music reminds me of Undertale and Deltarune once again, it also gives off faint Super Paper Mario vibes. Overall I think it's definitely worth checking out even if you aren't a fan of the anthropomorphic nature of most of the characters and monsters. Warning for those unwilling to work out the puzzles and try many times to succeed, it's a bit of a toughie ;).\t\t\t\t\t
