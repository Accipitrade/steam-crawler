An underrated masterpiece by an underrated indie dev (and also sus [amogus]):

Changed is an RPG Maker VX game made by DragonSnow with a decent 8-bit soundtrack. I wouldn't say that it's that much of a puzzle game, but more of a setup and strategy game. It may be difficult having tile-based movement and no WASD, but with enough practice, you will soon get the hang of it. Watch, read, and learn.

Concept (Minor Spoilers!):

You wake up in some strange laboratory, having slept for five or more years in a containment cell you came out from. You were obviously kidnapped, and you have nothing except a pair of shorts. You must use strategy and notes around the building to figure things out. Get out of this building, alive and not plundered.

Difficulty:

The difficulty is a little close to I WANNA BE THE GUY and its fan-games, but I would say those are way harder than this. The game has an easy mode, but you can't play through the full game unlike normal mode. Some parts are going to take time to figure out, and a drop of luck at times.

Final Verdict:

This is a first release, so don't be surprised if there are any mistakes. Out of everything I said, I would give this a 9/10. It could have gotten a control configuration, but it is good sides that.
