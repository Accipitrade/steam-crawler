\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this because I see the items for it so often on the points store, might as well, right? I'm not a furry and I'm not interested in sexual content, so obviously I'm not exactly the demographic this is aimed at, but I don't have an issue with the content and story to begin with. Honestly, I love the story, it's interesting enough to follow and I enjoy the art style, it's very fresh.
My issue is with the gameplay itself and the tracking of the enemies. You could do something perfectly once, then end up dying doing something stupid and have to do it again. Sure, you'll just do it again, it wasn't hard. Except surprise, it'll now be the bane of your existence for the next twenty minutes because the enemies won't move the same way. Sometimes, they'll jump on you at spawn when you walk into the room, or corner you so fast you barely knew what hit you.
I have three hours on this game and I haven't finished it, and it's not the puzzles that I struggle with, it's literally just being caught by the enemies so much that makes the experience miserable.
Don't recommend if you rage easily or don't have a lot of patience, because you will die, and you will die a lot, and many of those times will be unfair. If you're going for a certain ending, make sure you read a guide ahead of time because if you make a mistake then save, you're not going to want to go back.
I'm determined to finish this game, but I wouldn't play it again. The AIs need a lot of adjusting.

On the positive side, the controls are simple, the story is interesting, and the art is cute. I can't say much on the music, I tuned it out after a while of hearing it loop every 15 seconds from death but it's not grating so I think it's fine.\t\t\t\t\t
