\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo start off: Although this game is a furry tf game, do not think it's an easy experience. This game will kick your ass, and kick it hard. You will die/transform a lot, but if you can get past that you might enjoy it. This game has a really good character in the form of Puro, just be nice to him, trust me on this. Outside of him, there's one other character that goes into spoiler territory, but it's mostly just the two of you in a hostile environment. The game itself does have a lot of character development, and some pretty unique puzzles. One downside is the rng on some of the enemies, it may or may not be possible to clear some of the areas depending on how they move (Three notable examples include a 'stealth' area, a puzzle area, and a water area), and some of the puzzles reach a bit far when it comes to logic. You don't have a health bar in this game, any attack from an enemy will instantly send you back to the save point after a transformation/vore sequence, which can get frustrating at times, even with the f12 to menu function. The special edition allows you to auto save before the enemy encounters, so it is going in the right direction (currently in Chinese only at the moment). A big complaint I have involves the endings, like I said you need to be your kindest to Puro, if not he'll turn you into him during the halfway point of the game, which means if you saved in one slot and had no prior knowledge, you will have to lose a lot of progress to advance.. Said options also are one time only, meaning if you pick another option out of curiosity, you'll lose your chance...

Another thing is that there is the Second part where you can be stopped.. After the part where he wants you inside him, he'll decide against it, and 3 more opportunities come up where if you miss them.. (Ultra Ending spoiler ahead)

(ENDING SPOILERS AHEAD!!!) a scientist named K will smash Puro to death, which obviously is a terrible feeling and makes you feel like a crappy person.. If you get everything right however you can get one of 3 good endings, the third being a secret one.

But other than that, it's pretty enjoyable. The setting is interesting, and despite it being a kinky game, it's a fun one with a genuinely lovable wolf blob. I'd give it an 8.6./10, mostly because of rng being a big factor in whether or not you'll succeed in certain areas, a few major choices being determined that can grind your playthrough to an utter stop (One of which is Easy mode, which to be fair does warn you), and the time it takes to get back to certain areas after you inevitably get turned both out of curiosity, and genuine mistakes. Though seriously, there's like a half hour of transformations in this 8 hour game. If you're good, you might be able to beat it in 2 (Assuming you don't die)\t\t\t\t\t

As well, the way it handles characters is incredible; even the biggest antagonist ends up being helpful if you do the right thing.
