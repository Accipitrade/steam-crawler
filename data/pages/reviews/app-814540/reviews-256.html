\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is little more then weird furry bait. I was told that the game was good by a friend and decided to pick it up because it was cheap, but honestly I wouldn't have spent a penny on this game if I had known how bad it was. So far, I made it into the lab section, about nine minutes into the game according to my save. Getting here was a grind and involved looking up a guide just to figure out what the hell was going on. There are creatures on preset paths that you have to sneak passed, and then you hit a section where you have to run and rely on the random pathing of the monsters to get passed them. If you die, you go back to the previous save and have to sneak passed the first two monsters again, just for another shot to get lucky and win. I have been stuck at this segment for an hour.

The game is not fun, dying to random chance you have no way to mitigate over and over is not fun, being subjected to some weird fetish stuff over and over because you died to something you had no way to control is not fun, and over all I give the game a 3/10, with my honest opinion being that the game has only gotten good reviews because of the massive amount of furry bait that has been put into it. Don't waste your money. If you want to die over and over in a fashion that is actually fun and gives you some control over the situation, go play Darkest Dungeon.\t\t\t\t\t
