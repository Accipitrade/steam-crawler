\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros: Interesting premise, polished style, great story, and likable characters with awesome designs.

Cons: Controls can be wonky to where your character can be stuck going around tight corners or make it hard to gauge whether a hazard is about to hit you or not. These issues will make some puzzles range from being unreasonably difficult to downright nearly unplayable without the aid of a walk-though.

Despite the flaws I still highly recommend this game to fans of challenging rpg puzzle games with good stories and lovable characters. I will add the caveat though that if you are uncomfortable with furry transformation fetish kind of stuff you'll likely not get into this game all that much. But if you're okay with it, the best piece of advice I can give you for this game is to always say THANK YOU when you can, ESCape when you can, and just have patience then everything else will fall into place.\t\t\t\t\t
