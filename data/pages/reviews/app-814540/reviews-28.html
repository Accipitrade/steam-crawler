Even if you aren't a furry and hate all things about them, have fun dodging their slimy clutches and try to escape with your humanity intact. Or embrace the fluffiness and become a cute little fuzz-ball with Puro.
This is a fairly short indie rpg, a run without deaths should take under 2 hours, my first run took about 8 because I was screwing around and insisted on getting every death and text box. Aside from my strange collection obsession, there are 5 main types of gameplay you will experience.
1, chase scenes
2, button-mashing to avoid being transfurred in some scenes
3, boss fights
4, puzzles (mainly figuring out door codes)

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChanged review by Ender

I got to play this game as a dare I took from a discord user. I expected it to completely destroy my sanity. I was suprised. I enjoyed playing this game a lot, comparable to my favourites, like Hollow Knight, Celeste, Terraria, Rain World and many more. I'm going to seriously review this game now. Be careful, some minor spoilers ahead.

The furry aspect of Changed
So this part is probably why you are reading this. And uhm~ fair I guess. The game is clearly oriented around the concept of furries And it's true. Changed gets dangerously close to the "suggestive" border. Before I played it, I expected it to uhm~ cross the border. I hate to clarify it again, but I was DARED to play it, and I take dares seriously. I am NOT interested in any freaky furry stuff. I really hope that's clear to everyne. One of the main concepts of the game - transfurmations is uhm~ interesting. Basically, you lose the game when you get touched by certain enemy creatures. Upon death, you get transformed into an anthro- thing. They all have their own animations.

♥♥♥♥ why is this game fun - the gameplay
The gameplay mainly consists of running away, solving puzzles, etc. Basic rpg stuff. My main point is that Changed is NOT an easy game. I love hard games, and reseting hundreds of time just get to the next part of the story might not be for everyone, but it is something for me. The savepoints are fair and not frustrating. The puzzles are decent, not always very fun, but they are great still.

Im crying rn - the story
As much as the concept of the whole game is uhh~ questionable, the story is just amazing. I love it, the characters, the world, it's dripping with emotions (no pun intended :clyde: ). You can see the motivations behind each of the three characters very well. The aren't much decisions to make in Changed, but when there are, you feel an extreme weight behind them. I loved the ending of the game the most, it felt very emotional and I loved it. I've grown very attached to those characters, and i felt their reasoning very well. This was truly one of the best pieces of storytelling I have ever experienced, along with the games like Celeste or Hollow Knight. Changed is amazing at making you feel emotions they want you to feel. The despair, hopelessness and powerlessness of waking up in an unfamiliar, dead world, with everything trying to "change" you makes you really enjoy those moments when you find someone who you can trust and who can help you.

Artstyle
Cool ig, reminds me of OneShot. I just don't really understand why they had to make multiple animations of the player being turned into furry creatures

Number summary
Gameplay: 8/10 (it was hard)
Story: 10/10 (very emotional)
Graphic design:7/10 (decent ig)
Characters: 10/10 (puro ^w^)
Music: 9/10 (i love 8bit and chiptune)

So, basically I would recommend you to play this game, but be warned of the "transfurmations" uhmm~ they might make you feel a bit uncomfortable , if they dont, then theres something wrong with you.
I can safely compare this game to OneShot, though it isn't fourth-wall-breaking.
Cya.\t\t\t\t\t
