\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay, I played this game because I saw a resurgence in popularity and I was like, why not, lets give this game a shot. And this has been one hell of a game.

----------
Changed (Scroll down for Changed Special)
----------

I'm not the greatest gamer and don't have the quickest response time so I decided to put the game on easy mode. Big mistake. If you put the game on Easy you get the bad ending, no mater what you do, which is very annoying. I didn't know this at the very beginning, I know it gives you a warning saying you wont get to play through the whole game but my dumb ass still played through easy mode and got what was coming. After about 2 hours in, I got stopped and had to redo everything I did, just through hard mode to continue the story which was a bit of a night mare.

This game is unfair, and the controls are grid base, which would be fine if they weren't so unfair. So slight mistakes can make you have to restart a decent portion because saves are not very abundant. So the game might be labeled as a puzzle game, its mostly a chasing and timing game. Even with all the negatives there are some positives about having the game be so tough. The award factor, and the ability to master the game. And when you finally pass a boss or a difficult section, its such a great sigh of relief. And by the end of it, because I had to replay the beginning 3 times in total (1st time on easy, 2nd time because I didn't pick right dialogue, and 3rd time I was able to pass that part of the game) Stuff that use to take me about 40 minutes took me only 15-20 minutes by the end. And that's a very awarding feeling to spend so long trying to find the best path and fastest way to complete sections. And for the story, its alright, a lot better than expected. Is it the best, no. But if you're playing this game your probably not playing it for the story, probably because your a furry or you got dared to play this. In other words if you're not a furry there's a high chance you're not going to like it, but let me get back on that statement later. The "Transfurmations" are a important part of the game. It promotes exploring and trying to get all of the different transforms and picture galleries which I really enjoy to search for. So this game is great if you like to find all the little details within the game.

Now I had to play this game with a guide in a another window because some of the puzzles were just to hard for me, and other times I would get lost and what to do and wonder if what I was doing was the right thing or if I was doing it wrong. And with that being said, only the worded puzzles is where it was difficult like the ending puzzle which I wont spoil. The more visual puzzles and movement puzzles were easy for the most part excluding a few. And there's a decent amount of movement puzzles, which include box pushing (A lot of box pushing which were not really much of puzzles), ice sliding, and movement matching and more. They were fun but didn't have to much of a challenge and was able to pass them within a reasonable time.

Over all I had a wild but mixed experience with the game. But I'm going to recommend it to furries who enjoy the challenge. If you're here for the story, watch a youtuber because you probably wont enjoy the game. I enjoyed the game personally, even after the rocky start because the after the first half, you start getting a hang with how the game works. Even if its unfair you do start to enjoy the difficulty and challenge it beholds. And the game has a charm to it and the story holds well enough for you to continue playing. If I had to give this a rating I would give it a solid 7/10, but if you want the non furry bias then it's more of a 4.5/10. Because you're playing it mainly for the transfurmations.


TLDR; Game is a furry focused game with the transfurmations being the main focused. This element promotes exploration and finding detail in the game. Gameplay is difficult, unfair, and sometimes repetitive but can be awarding for some. The Story holds fine for what it is and overall I enjoyed the game. With that being said, a lot of people wont because of how unfair and challenging it is and if you're not a furry and don't like challenging games I do not recommend this game. If you do, give it a shot you might enjoy it.

------------------
Changed Special
------------------

Changed Special, after playing the first game I was thinking this was just going to be a new paint coat and that's it, I was very wrong. Changed Special fixed a lot of the complaints I had with the original. And the story is so much more held up and has so much more development through out the game. The puzzles are better and have so much more variety. I had a blast, even if it's only a demo, its a huge improvement. And I can state that I could see maybe one or two non furries enjoy this version of the game because its such a enjoyable experience and more forgiving, while still being tough. I love the special edition of the game so far and would rate it a 9/10 because how good it is compared to the original. Can not wait to see where the game goes from here.

TLDR; Big improvement over all, better story and better gameplay. Do recommend to most furries who want a challenging but not over the top game.\t\t\t\t\t
