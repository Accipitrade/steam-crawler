At the very start, you choose a difficulty. The default is easy, so I selected it, only to be told that I can't actually complete the game on this difficulty. OK, fine, I'll go back.... except it then starts the game and doesn't give you the option to go back and change your difficulty after the warning, it just assumes you don't care and starts the unfinishable game.

So that's one restart.

Second time, I check a thing that says a monster is coming, then examine the capsule I spawned from. The monster spawns shortly before I examine the item, and that's when I learn that while you are frozen in place, invulnerable, and unable to do anything at all during dialogue boxes, monsters are unaffected by the boxes. They retain full mobility. So as I'm sitting there, forced to mash my way through far too much dialogue about the flavor of the tank water, the enemy happily sidles over and sits on me until I finally get to the last dialogue box, the dialogue box closes, and I die instantly.

Two restarts, and I haven't even manged to do more than learn that I drank the tank water at some point and that this game's coding is broken as ♥♥♥♥.

I might be willing to try again if there's a patch to fix this, but being able to be effectively killed while trapped in unskippable dialogue boxes is unacceptably bad.\t\t\t\t\t
