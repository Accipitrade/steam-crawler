\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay so, I've played this game through twice, (I got the Bad Ending and True Ending) and all in all, a pretty okay game. The art is very unique, reminding me of a DS game like Rhythm Heaven, and the animation pretty good to boot. The story is quite cool, and the characters are nice too. I do have a bit of a distaste for the difficulty. This game is hard, and it gets really repetitive after dying over and over again, but there's not much you can do about that past letting you skip dialogue, and having closer checkpoints / savepoints so, I guess it's okay. The real problem I have with this game... The death animations. They're really- weird. They're strangely... sexual. It made me very uncomfortable every time the screens came up, and made me feel uncomfortable playing the game in general in fear of seeing another screen like that. The concept was there it really was. The idea of a living gloop running around trying to get you, and when it does it takes over your entire body transforming you into something not human, is terrifying. I thought it would be a horror game based of what I knew going in. And well, this game is a horror game to me, but for all the wrong reasons. The death screens ruin the game for me. Ignoring that fact though, (which is hard) the game was alright. Most of the music was a bop, and the diversity between the different infected people was cool. Just... god the death scenes. This game is gross.\t\t\t\t\t
