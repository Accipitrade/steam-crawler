Unless you are skilled in movement in games you will have a ROUGH time but it has learnable methods to complete the game and each new section is pretty fulfilling with different ways to, well, change you.

The story of the game is alright, it's not like reading a massive novel with thousands of pages and it isn't like a child game where the only bit of story is a characteristic. What I mean by this is that the game gives an interesting plot that doesn't go on forever and the endings are well thought out and go well with the story.

Let me be honest I mostly wanted to play this game because fluffy boys and I LOVE the sharks however, the boss fight is pretty difficult but the AI does have a weakness of a pattern movement (I'm sure you'll find out).

in all seriousness this is NOT a game you should play as your first ever game, this is because of how difficult the game is as a whole. While the pretty style is aesthetically pleasing, the puzzles are hard and progressively get harder. The cat, actual pain I had experienced, I beat the game as a whole in a couple of days (taking breaks out of anger from boss fights as I have anger issues) and when I go back to the start I still get pain because of the cat.

I am pretty hyped for the changed special edition and wish the best to DragonSnow and the future of this lovely game.\t\t\t\t\t
