\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA truly beautiful experience. Honestly it has been one of the best games I have played in a long time, and playing it was an absolute blast. While it was difficult, and VERY frustrating at parts (using a guide was required for some puzzles and chase scenes). The endings... Well.. I'm not gonna spoil them, but the made me feel powerful emotions that will stick with me for the rest of eternity. This is a game that should be enjoyed by every furry (or people that are into puzzle/horror games), and I truly hope that you play this game, and get the same amount of enjoyment out of it as I did.

Pros:
- Beautiful graphics
- Great music
- Well-written characters
- Challenging gameplay
Cons:
- Extremely difficult at times
- Getting the true ending was a pain
- There was slight slowdown, but very rarely did it happen
Overall score: 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
