about a guy who just woke up from a capsule due power shortage.

The game is top view perspective game.
Its a Single player game where you need to solve puzzles and escape from the building,
But watch out because there are a lot of dangers in the building. The enemies are
some kind of a latex monsters (Some of them look like slimes)
If one of these monsters touch you, you turn into a fur beast and the game
throws you back in to the main menu (pro tip: press F12 if you die, its faster to go back to the main menu)
Watch out If your have an opportunity to speak with a character named Puro speak and your first sentence to him always be: "Thank you" its important to have a good relationship with him! After that you can ask him whatever you want but first Thank him every time!

I really love this game! This game reminded me of Undertale. I know that they are completely different games but maybe the perspective is the reason. I have 20 hours in this game Right now and I can say I found everything and did everything that I wanted.

For 5 buck this game has a good price but in winter / summer sale
its usually around 2 bucks which way cheap so its bargain for everyone
who loves these kind of games.

This game got my recommendation and always be one of my favorite games!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game less then 24 hours ago and i already completed all the routes for the base game. That's how much i love it, right off the bat.

The art style is charming and cutesy, a strange little contrast with some of the "spicier" scenes, but generally speaking works really well and sets a strangely convincing tone.
It has some horror elements peppered in ever so lightly, but this game is pumped full of adrenaline at times. Even with the set pace it runs at, it's shocking that i was able to be startled repeatedly by sounds, enemies, and sometimes even interactions.

The story was wonderful. No matter what route you take the characters seem to grow on you, a sense of despair following you like a stray dog. There's a problem here and now it's time for you and your unlikely friend to solve it and escape. I may not have phrased this the best, but it's definitely worth seeing for yourself.

The last thing i'll mention is that it's a little difficult and my only real negative part about this whole game is that there isn't really a skip dialogue option, which can get annoying if you don't save post dialogue. That and the map placement combined with the art style can make seeing what route you're goin down on the map just a little difficult. But that's only two things.

All in all, this was an instant favorite for me. It's definitely worth checking out, and if you don't like it, at least you know you tried. Have fun and good luck if you do decide to buy this game!\t\t\t\t\t

Also shoutouts to the room towards the beginning of the game that you can just straight up softlock in. The save box even says not to save there when enemies are nearby, but if you ask me, that's just bad game design.

UPDATE: I have beaten the game, along with getting all the endings and achievements. The game gets a bit better after act 1, but not by much. It's still just kind of... bad. The worst part of the game BY FAR is the end of act 1, but everything else is either mediocre or somewhat bad. Not worth playing at all.

Also also, the Special Edition runs really poorly on my computer. I don't know how they creator managed to make an RPG Maker game laggy, but they did.\t\t\t\t\t
