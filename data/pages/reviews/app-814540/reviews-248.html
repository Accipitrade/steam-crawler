\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(2022: This review has been greatly edited so that it properly fits my opinion on the game.)

The good:
- The graphics and music have an 8-Bit-ish aesthetic.
- 3 main characters with a decent simple short story.
- Difficult puzzles.
- An actual well-made furry game.

The mixed:
- Original: The transformations are a mix of being NSFW and being boring. The only after pictures in this version are not NSFW. The transformation animations can be NSFW, specifically being caught by the Black Latex Creatures in the office right before the library.

- Special Edition: Almost all the transformations have after pictures, the animations are basically the same, just higher quality. There are few new ones, mainly being combinations of two transformations. The after pictures in this version are mostly NSFW, showing the main character in revealing or sexual positions and/or with sexual facial expressions.

The bad:
- It's not very re-playable, the game doesn't change much, outside of a few dialogue changes based on choices. So having to replay the game for the 5 different endings can be a chore. This is made easier with the save mechanic, however if you don't pay attention or know when to save, you might screw yourself over, meaning you have to replay the game for a different ending.
- The puzzles, in the Original, have serious randomness in them. Enemies move randomly, making routing/planning for already hard puzzles, even harder. You'll often lose because of bad enemy movement, rather than your own mistakes. (Note: This problem is way less prevalent in the Special Edition.)
- Movement is difficult, while it wasn't too hard for me personally, the movement can still be hard to control, it's just not smooth at all, and you'll often overshoot or turn too early.

Overall, this game isn't for everyone. The Original has a lot of problems the Special Edition doesn't have. This makes reviewing the game hard, because I don't recommend the Original, but I very much recommend the Special Edition.
Basically, if you want a good experience, play the Special Edition.\t\t\t\t\t
