This game was a really fun and challenging, but it has a more higher skill criteria and needs a lot of patience for its RNG (that will take many attempts). The puzzles were a bit too difficult in my opinion, needing a walkthrough on a few occasions.

The game can be quite unfair for it's story, I recommend to keep a steam walkthough on the side. If not then this is a warning, keep at least 1 save for every different area with Puro. Also, you have to play on Normal mode to play through the full game.

Overall, this was a really fun experience!
It has a strong story and requires a lot of patience. If all the story flags weren't required and the puzzles can be done without needing a walkthough, this would be an excellent game. As well, a bunch of small changes would really improve this game. I look forward to seeing the updated version for full release, Changed: Special.
