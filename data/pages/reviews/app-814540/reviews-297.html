\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChanged is a game where you play as a human that as been "kidnapped". You woke up in a small room and decide to search an escape and leave the building where you are. Nothing is explained and your only way to find how your ennemies works is to die and restart.

Pros :
- Not too punitive, multiple save point
- 2 endings (or more I only have found 1 for now but it saids there's another one)
- Cute graphisms/arts
- Enigms mecanism
- Nice soudtracks
- Addictive

Cons :
-A bit hard sometimes (I needed to look at a let's play to find how to continue)
-Some things aren't translated but they're not useful overall. Some are badly translated but it's actually funny to read and entirely unserstandable

Overall I loved the game and will replay it for the second ending. It's probably one of my favorite solo game ^^ For the price I would recommend it to anyone who is okay with restarting multiple times and thinking a bit to solve enigms. It has a pretty good lifetime, it took me around 7/8 hours to finish it 1 time so jump on it and buy it now !\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChanged is called a goo fettish and transformation game and is judged so quickly

Not quite

Changed has a unique depth of story and even though it gives you little clue of what is going on, it keeps you guessing and keeps you going.


Gameplay 4.2/5.0 - Simple enough, easy controls once you discover them, puzzles are the task and require you to think. Though, there is stress and hard times to come, you gotta keep calm, think it through and keep hoping.

Plot 4.0/5.0- Again, the game doesn’t fill some gaps in the story, it’s up to you to let your imagination guess and makes you keep going in case you do find your answer, the idea of the plot is eye catching.

Music 3.7/5.0- It has that retro feel which I do enjoy it but I’m giving it a 3.7 just cause I was honestly expecting more music, but nail it for tension, and some part just have background noise but it’s quite peaceful

Difficulty 5.0/5.0- Challenge...oh boy..if you liked tricks and having to go back to the menu screen and opening your save a ton of times, then this game will do that, one touch gameovers which at times show you what happens and a transformation animation, Puzzles test you and it took me time to figure them out and I felt stupid when I didn’t see some of them but though it stresses you, you just wanna keep going.

Conclusion: Changed may not be everyone’s favorite, for some it’s criticized as a goo fetish game and all that but it just needs some time to be actuality seen as what it truely is, a game that provides a unique story with little explanation of what the hell is going on. I truely recommend getting this game for yourself and friends.\t\t\t\t\t
