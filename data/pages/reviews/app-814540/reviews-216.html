\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd say the gameplay loop is like smashing your head through a brick wall and then finding furry art on the other side of the brick wall

the game is super punishing and difficult and unforgiving although it's surprising how fun super punishing, difficult, and unforgiving can be when most games now a days are to worried weather or not their players will rage quit on a first try
it's a ton of trail and error over and over and some puzzles ( like the first one ) seem pretty , but the action scenes where you have to constantly be on edge, knowing one wrong move and your dead as toast, slowly inching along carefully making your way to each save point really makes you feel like you've done a lot of hard work, finally after all of that time and effort you've made it past that enemy
the game easily teaches you with the first room that you should never let your guard down because everything can just one shot you, every step you take is a closer step to death and I love it

the feeling of "Yes! Finally Oh My Lord!" has never felt better knowing that if you fail your screen gets filled with a ton of furry art

and if you actually read everything and go through all the text boxes, there's a large story and lore behind everything to wrap it all up, with it just telling you enough to get a sense of what's happening without ever giving you a straight answer, meaning the player can come to their own conclusion and understanding of the world

if there was a complaint I would have, Tone down the NSFW, sure it implies it and i get that the whole "Goo" and "Furry" Thing is a kink but turning into one is bad enough
and the game seems to lag whenever I go into a big area with alot of scenes or enemies, not sure if that's me or the game

V Possible Spoiler Warning Below V

Its set 5 years after the "outbreak"
You have memories of being kidnapped but at the end of the day its all foggy

You have to fight, run, and escape every enemy without fail or your dead
You've been starved, thirst deprived
You are overly malnourished yet you still keep going in the hope of finding something or someone

Notes that seem to guide you in the right way but you are unsure of its trust worthiness
After slowly and tiredly making your way through each room you can't anymore

After all of that you finally meet whose been leaving the notes
Puro
You have to choose to trust one of the monsters that have been attacking you this entire time or weather or not you can face it alone

Puro is the first character in the entire game that responds to you
He explains that the outside world is gone, its no longer what it was
As you make it along with Puro now on your side you slowly have this thought in the back of your head "what will i do when I escape"

And then one of the best scenes I've seen in a game in a while starts

After finally making it past all the water based monsters you make it to the generator
You power it back up and see that its been
5 years.
5 years and everything is gone, your life before it, everyone
The ruins of the town are real
The newspapers are real
Its all real

The concept of loosing everything, being put through misery the entire time.
The single line "Death may have been the better option this entire time" really just Hurts

Like it just hurts, you really do get an understanding of what is going through your characters mind while playing this game

No time to think about how the world ended while your running for your life
I never thought I'd get such dark themes from a Latex Furry game but it really just puts into retrospective how someone in that type of situation would act
If Changed is worth anything its the story and character development

It tells a wonderful grim and dark story while disguising itself as a fun happy furry game\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinally gonna write a quick review

Wanted to write one once i got to an ending, which was a bad one but still, i personally really enjoyed the game. It brings back memories of when i used to play a ton of these kinda games (like IB or Witch's House). A simple but nice look, nice music (granted not all tracks hit fully home with me) and just a fun game.

My main issue is the difficulty and controls (for the most part), the chases can be really tricky due to room layout and the controls sometimes (for me at least) didn't respond to key presses or keep going in a direction that i let go off (that could be an issue on my end). Hopefully Special from i have seen changed room layout to make it a bit easier. Plus the game pretty much makes you play the normal diff since easy locks out content (thankfully the game tells you about easy not having the full game)

I feel if you don't like game that are more difficult (and sometimes lean to trial and error) i would say wait for special for finish or see if you can get it for free (like getting it gifted) if you are okay with difficult games i recommend playing this game!\t\t\t\t\t
