\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has to be my favorite game of the year, or maybe out of all games I've played on steam. I wasn't expecting so much personality, feel, and gameplay in this game once I first bought it. I adored and adore this game a lot, I was blew away by how deep it got. I'm quite shocked as well, but not in a bad way because this game quite took my heart away. I was sorta slapped in the face how it became after progressing. I always wanted to see what would be happening next in every level. I had a LOT of fun in it. I've just beat the game, all endings and all achievements by heart and determination. I knew it would be a great idea to review it after achieving all endings. I'll try to make this review as 'spoil-less' as possbile, but I will still warn you if I tell a spoiler.

Introduction: (You can skip this part if you want and head straight to the Pros & Cons) At start, I had a friend who told me about the game, telling me about it and I thought it was pretty interesting. So I gave it a buy, but I'll admit that I intentionally bought this game out of irony because of what the game had in it. Personally I was interested in that, not much in general before I played this game. The game felt a little quiet to me at start, but I felt so intrigued finding out to do what next. The game felt like a nice puzzle game after all, it did indeed feel a bit hard for me but I didn't really care. The game became fun to me. However it wasn't until I got sort of deeper in the game finding out that this game has a quite a big emotional feeling to it, and I have clinged to it. This game became more fascinating to me more than just playing it out of irony. It felt sorta as if, 'strong'. It wasn't until around each endings it hit me because they were all quite strong endings, more likely the final ones.

Pros:
- Very challenging levels and bosses, VERY very fun
- Simple gameplay, harmless but also has a frightening feel in lots of levels
- World and graphics feels so proper, graphics are nice but they fit the game so right
- Superb story, completely blown away
- Main characters had a lot of personality to them
- Has a cutesy feel to it
- Great BGM, personally I loved it and just had to buy the album because it was that good
- VERY very strong emotional feel to it, it was played out excellent
- VERY strong tensions in the game, it made me anxious and careful to make a wrong decision
- Strong endings, wasn't expecting them and was hit by them hard
- All the transfurmations scenes were great, liked them all

Cons:
- This game gets HARD, this isn't a bad thing at all but it is definitely not for the easy players
- I wasn't able to take screenshots, I mean this could be my fault even tho I tried to change the key but I missed all the parts where I could've taken some
- 4 saves only, this is quite crucial because making the wrong mistake can lead you to restart the whole game again if you didn't back-up your save (I had made this mistake quite a bit, and after the 4th time it wasn't pleasant). There's even some parts of the game that takes you far away from one and if you're transfured then you have to restart all again back from your last save. This just gets only annoying tho if you've played the game enough
- I was unknown on what to exactly do next after hitting the same (spoiler warning!) Betrayal ending twice after on normal difficulty. I didn't know I had to play the game a certain way to progress to the final endings. I admit that I had to check out a walkthrough guide to help me. That kind-of spoiled me to see what I had to do but if I didn't see the walkthrough I would've hit the same ending a couple more times.
- There is some frame-rate issues on the Water level
- spoiler warning!I feel like Dr. K on the secret ending could have some more story to him. Yeah I mean, he did seem quite like a bad guy a bit but I was hoping to see if he would have a happier ending. He didn't really seem like a bad guy either because he did offer to help the human in the game to survive but I felt like he was sorta thrown away, even after getting quite a lot of plot from him.

I mean, yes, this game is flawed. It has it's downs here and there but overall it is such a perfect game in my honest opinion. The developer and co-developer nailed this game. I can't even stress how much I love the game now. This is game is an absolute 10/10 for me, and I recommend anyone to play it. This game was totally worth buying at full price. If there was a Changed 2 or another update to this game I will happily and undoubtedly play it again. I may even play this game again because I love it a lot. After the final true ending I got I felt so achieved and immediately satisfied. I was so determined beating the game and getting that right ending, and I'm going to admit to you right now that it was all so worth it. Excellent game, 5 stars out of 5.

Just keep in mind tho before you play this game that this game is not that easy. You will experience trial and error. It is also definitely furry. It's not a problem really but as long as you don't mind it.\t\t\t\t\t
