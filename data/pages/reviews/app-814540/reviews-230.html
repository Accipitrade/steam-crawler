\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, basically, I played through the whole game on another friend's account (that's why I have so little playtime yet I have finished the game), but I just HAD to buy it for myself.

This game has some rough edges. But despite that, it has elevated itself has a recognized piece of furry culture. It has brought me many tears of frustration, yet my friend who had completed the game convinced me to keep going, and wow, I don't even have words for it.

The story, even if simple in nature, is greatly amplified by the difficulty of the game; you're a weak human surrounded by merciless creatures, and can die at any moment, and have to retry again and again in order to succeed. I was very frustrated by that, but I think that it's hugely beneficial for the whole plot; it feels like you're trapped in here forever, and that there's no possible escape.

Honestly, I couldn't have finished the game if I played it all alone; if, like me, you tend to play more lighthearted or easy games, I highly recommend getting a friend that has completed the game to help you. An important note: if you plan on really finishing the game, do NOT choose easy mode; you'll be very disappointed if you do so...

Also, don't feel bad for looking up guides if you're really stuck; it's alright, the gameplay itself is hard enough as it is. And keep going.\t\t\t\t\t
