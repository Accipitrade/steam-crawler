\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've seen a few replies about this game. Funny how easily it is just called a furry game. Well... They're not wrong.
But in my opinion, it's much more than just that. So sure, there's lots of that. But just rating and judging it based on that would be a mistake.

To me, this game was a complete emotional odissey. Well-written characters, smooth animations and character expressions, accurate narration... I really liked it. (Unpopular opinion: Dr. K is probably my favorite character.) Not only that, but the art style manages to both look like it was made from MS paint, yet look beautifully attracting. And of course, the music. It's not what I consider the greatest aspect of the game, but still notable. So yes, the game's control might be quite troubling. The game itself might be shown as unfair. Still didn't stop me from getting all 2 bad endings, and all 3 true endings. The whole thing was made by two guys, and one had to take care of the entire gameplay, lore and design side.

In the end, all I'd say is... Very good furry game, but not just that. It's definitely worth your time, furry or not.\t\t\t\t\t
