\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd say this is a pretty sub-par game, Story definitely carries. I'll rate it a 7/10.

Now I'm gonna explain why.




!!!! Spoilers Ahead !!!!




So, Trail and error is a pretty fun concept isn't it.
I can't believe how many times I have wanted to break my table into pieces from either, The game being too difficult too quickly or from the very epic input system.

Secondly, Puro, that is all.

Thirdly, These endings...
Not only is the way to get all the endings so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ specific, but how would you even know that you HAVE to thank Puro every time or else you get a bad ending. It genuinely feels like Puro is the main character and you (as the player) are simply just filler. Plus, why do the endings have to be so negative? There isn't a single ending other than The Best Ending where Puro doesn't get the short end of the stick.

Fourthly, "How was I supposed to know that?"
There are so many parts of the game where if I didn't have someone in a voice call, I would have no clue what to do. For example, One of the very first parts of the game. The room with the flickering lights and the dark goo creatures, You can get through the first room pretty easily if you are fast enough, but the second room... You would be stuck. There is not a single warning or even a piece of dialog that says you can stand still in front of them while the lights are on without being seen! You are just assumed to know this knowledge! I had to have someone come into the voice call I was in and TELL ME that I could do that.

Ultimately,
It's an okay game! I loved playing it (when I wasn't angry at it) and the soundtrack was nice. I just wish there was more thought put into it. Would recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
