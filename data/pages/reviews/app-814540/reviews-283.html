...
Okay, obviously there's more to the game than just that. For starters: It does get a bit fetishy with its transformation scenes and furry scenarios which yeah, it isn't for everyone; but I suppose if you're into that kind of stuff it'll make you love the game even more (But don't worry, the game doesn't really get lewd. Some of the characters are naked, but there's no genitals nor nipples to be seen anywhere. It's pretty "Safe For Work", but you shouldn't play it at work if you don't want to get personally embarrassed if a co-worker were to see them "transfurmation" scenes :v)

Though that being said, this game isn't what I would call a "perfect" game. For starters, this game tends to slap you in the face with its unpredictable difficulty. Some of the rooms tend to go from having an easy to figure out puzzle to becoming fugging Touhou having to dodge every enemy you see and pray the AI will have mercy on you by making a wrong move or find a pattern that'll give you a chance to pass such nightmarish room. And other than the unbalanced difficulty; the English translation which sure, it's far better than the one it had before, still has the occasional grammatical errors, punctuational error, and occasionally has phrases that are too long for the textbox and get deleted, leaving you having to guess what they were meant to say. (I know it's pretty hypocritical of me to complain about that since I'm not a native English speaker and make errors constantly, but my point still stands)

Overall, I'd say if you can look pass the unbalanced difficulty, as well as the occasional errors made within the English translation and just want to play a cute and wholesome game with furry characters and fetishy little moments, I'd say give it a shot! (´･ω･`)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is not what I expected. And what I got did not dissapoint.
Honestly, I just played it for the same reason most people here did, because it looks like, and in some respects is, a furry fetish game. But Changed is so much more than that. The creativity, horror, and character is wonderful and present through the whole game, which I beat in just a single day, I liked it so much. The story of this game is really great, with wonderful characters, and a very unique premise executed amazingly, and with little flaws (Present though they may be). Now those flaws are 1: The sometimes evil difficulty, and 2: The furry ♥♥♥♥. There's a couple places where the furry influence goes overboard, and it pains me, because even though I'm a furry and I can deal with it, transformation fetish often doesn't really belong in a section of a game outlining the horror of creatures that take your body and erase your consiousness, as well as it murders accessibility, even though said stuff is very scarce and pretty mild as it goes.

HOWEVER: I still recommend this game. It's cheap, it's got a good story to tell, and it's just plain good. Just, don't let the weird stuff deter you.

Trust me. The game's great.\t\t\t\t\t
