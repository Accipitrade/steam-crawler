\r\n\t\t\t\t\t A large majority of people have a very common ability of being into something due to the plot and relatability. I however cannot say the same. I've never binged a show, played a full game in one sitting, or watched an entire movie franchise. However this game was special, this game CHANGED me. I love Change because it was something I can relate to and enjoy the plot/character development.
I've always had issues connecting to things, i would not consider myself normal by any means. I'm quite the recluse, I had my true passions from everyone, I hate talking about my sexuality and other personal things. However, after I had started taking this game seriously, I found myself really comfortable. I felt like Puro, I felt like Human. I felt myself portrayed all round. From my more submissive traits portrayed by Human, to my more masculine/dominate lustful traits portrayed by the "enemy characters". Puro especially i related to. leaving the pack to pursue what i wanted, even if it wasn't "Normal" or "Natural instinct". I also related to the bad parts of Puro: a loner yearning for a companion. Around 8 months ago I stopped pursing a chance of finding myself a companion. I had lost emotion for someone and I never wanted that feeling again so I suppress all emotions, almost becoming numb. However deep down, I wanted someone like Human to just stumble into my life like he did into Puro's. I want to trust, I want to feel, I wanted somebody there. This game was like looking into a twisted mirror into myself.
Furthermore, in addition to the extreme and personal connection I have with the characters. It also helped me realize that everything will be fine in the end. Puro goes from being a smart, lonely fella often seen as a monster, to a really good friend/lover (depends on how you look at Human's and Puro's relationship). Seeing him go to what I portray myself as to someone I want to be, it feels me with hope. Hope for the chance to explore a boundless world with my companion, It gave me a hope for difference, it gave me hope for change, I had changed.\t\t\t\t\t
