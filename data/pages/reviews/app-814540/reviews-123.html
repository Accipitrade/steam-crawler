\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like this game, sure the visuals are dated, but it plays into the stylization of the game, which is in itself an homage to what computing in the 90s felt like. The entire game is 170MB so it would fit on a CD, definitely within spec for the computers of the time. Only thing that could be updated to spec would be the music, but I do respect the artist's decision to use an older chiptune spec because it was par for the time for computers with soundcards to sound better using the older spec. I might take a look at the soundtrack myself and use pixitracker to redux the OST as a new composition in a newer standard, because I am just so enamored with it. Though, I will have to give a warning, if you're not a furry, or, if you are, not into the lewd side, might want to uh... take a double-look at the game before purchase. However, myself, I like it, I like the concept, and am not bothered by such things. I love this game, and the special edition (included free with your purchase) is really a work of art. I love games in the heart and art category, like cave story and things like that. Even if you're not a furry, and are not bothered by the somewhat (and sometimes blatently) lewd aspect of it, do take a look from a heart and art game standpoint, because I do believe that even though the game is rather simplistic, the story shines through in spades.\t\t\t\t\t
