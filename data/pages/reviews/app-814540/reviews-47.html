\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a furry fetish game, period. I'll try to review it regardless, but your decision to buy it will probably hinge on this fact.

Changed offers a range of challenges including creative puzzles and action segments. The music is one of the positive aspects, with respectable chiptunes that complement the gameplay without getting in the way.
That being said, it is excruciatingly difficult, with challenges that are hard to solve, sometimes relying on guesswork even. The action segments are often frustrating due to trial and error and input issues (it's made in RPG Maker). The story is shallow and the game is only 6-10 hours long, with a lot of the length depending on puzzle solving skills. The save points are also usually far from challenges, which feels really annoying.
Probably the thing you might be most interested in, which is the art, is actually pretty middling and relies on being a fetish to bring value. The death animations and post transformation art are limited, and the quality isn't outstanding either. It is pretty much what you paid for, at this price point.

I can recommended it only for those who enjoy the artistic content. If you were gifted it by friends, it can be fun with them in voice chat, as they can help with puzzles and laugh at the challenges.
In the end though, it is not recommended for non-furries (and probably even many furries) looking for a good game, as it is often frustrating and just plain not worth the price.\t\t\t\t\t
