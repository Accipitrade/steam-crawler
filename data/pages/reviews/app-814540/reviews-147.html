\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrustration.
...

Oh how much I regret buying it... And how much I regret giving it a second chance... No it doesn't get any better. It remains as frustrating as it always been.

.......

- Sprites are nice, charactes are adorable, transformations have some "lеwd" subcontext behind them, but everything is still very wholesome and cute. Probably a nice story too.

- Would be cool if transformations could be used as a tool to solve puzzles, or in combat. But no, any transformation is just a regular death.

But the gameplay. The gameplay... Oh my f ing god!.. All it gives you - is Frustration...
- That kind of frustration you can get from weird puzzles and labyrinths.
- - That kind of frustration you get from the lack of a Skip Text button.
- - - That kind of frustration you get from too many things being random (enemy movement)...
- - - - Oh god, that kind of frustration from intentionally placed by the dev, the most b-s-tiest "traps".....

And most importatnly, THAT kind of FRUSTRATION you get from the rpgmaker Movement, when the game expects from you, Fast and Perfectly Precise Movement (chase scenes).


/Note: Changed-Special is a WIP remaster, everything looks nicer, there are a lot more death screens, and now story is being told you more actively. But so far, the gameplay didn't really get any better.

==================================================

If you love being frustrated - go ahead. If you believe you have a lot of patience - sure. As long as you don't hate furries, and ridiculously hard and very questionable gameplay elements is what you love the most - you will enjoy the experience.

This game wants me to hate it... And I hate it... Hate... But at the same time... In a weird way... I appriciate the effort... I like the art... I like the idea behind the game... And I like the story...

Be warned, and decide yourself, if it is worth to buy it or not\t\t\t\t\t
