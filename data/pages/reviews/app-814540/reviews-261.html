Changed review

Let´s start with Changed, a old school indie survival horror, made with RPG maker.

You are a human what suddenly wake up in a strange facilities. You don´t know why are there, but here´s two main objetives:
- Survive
- Find out why you are here

With that in mind, you must go ahead and survive no matter what.

Now let´s talk about the game itself, and before you do anything the game warns you about the lack of information about controls, even the HUD. At this point you must search hints to get information about the facilities, and the game´s lore.

The gameplay it´s very simple, the main character (the human) only can walk, run and interact with items; no more. Knowing that, your skills, mind and reflexs will be tested. Are you ready for chalenge?
About the difficult, the game is very challenging, very close to be unfair, and REALLY frustrating sometimes (for saying that it is REALLY hard, and there´s no room for mistakes!) Sometimes the hints are pretty lame, and makes so dificult to understand.

The music and sound efects are pretty "old". It feels you are playing from the begining of 90´s or even older! But despite the "old fashioned" BGM and SF, it fits well on the game.

About the characters and story, ill be brief for not too much spoilers. First you meet Puro, a black anthro wolf made with latex, who wears a misterious white mask. I don´t tell more, about him, but ill say is so adorable, if you play it, you ll understand (and i realize why this character it´s so popular on comunnity, seriously, it´s SO popular!) And the story and lore it´s very interesting, i couldn´t stop to keep playing to see more! Not many games succeed in empathizing and caring for the characters, after read the backstory its hard not feeling something.

Now i´ll finish this review with pros and cons:

Pros:

- Really good story.
- Cool characters.
- Visual section really good worked.
- Nice BMG and Sound efects
- Fully translated in English and Spanish.

Cons:

- Sometimes the game is very frustraing due it´s high difficult.
- Some hints are very confuse.
- The translation it´s full of mistakes and words out of view.

In conclusion, Changed it´s good game with an interesting story, lore, and great characters. I recommend this game? Yes, i do; but be warned cause includes furry (a lot), wierd fetishes, and questionable rating images.

And i give thank to you for read all, this is the first review I have done in English. So, please, be comprensive and, excuse my bad english. If you wanna tell something about my review or this game, go ahead an use the commentay section, but without offense anyone. Thank you again, and see you next time.

Long life to Puro!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe first thing to know about Changed is that the video is deceiving. If you get transformed, you lose. This is completely opposite to the depictions of a happy and curious character in the video, so I am disappointed there. The game is way too hard. The description says the game takes about six hours however, after nine hours I have discovered that I am about half way through. I have also discovered that unless the player does certain actions perfectly, there is no way to get a good ending of the game. This is incredibly frustrating because I have found myself at a point where the only way I can proceed is to start the entire game over. I really don't like needing to read a strategy guide to play through a game. However, a strategy guide doesn't help as the player has to have lightening fast reflexes for certain parts of the game. Unlike traditional puzzle games, enemy movements are not consistent every time, so several playthroughs are required for each room to get lucky enough for things to go right.

I enjoyed what I played, I want to know the ending, however, I cannot recommend this game to any of my friends, even the ones who love transformation like I do, because it is absolutely too punishing.\t\t\t\t\t
