\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdated 9/29/2022 - This game is harder then you expect, i put it on the same level of SCP but a bit harder. There are some suggestive animations, like when you get captured by the black wolf looking latex monster it seems to be doing the dirty and the picture of a house dog becoming a anthro dog seems suggestive but it really isn't. Other then that i haven't really found anything else that stands out thats suggestive. Please note i couldn't get past the black latex wolf looking creatures. Thats how hard the game is. There are clear signs that this was made with rpg maker based on the hud, and some of the sounds. It might not be and just used the same sounds but thats up for you to decide.

---
Not a bad game, while it has a RPGMaker vibe too it. I think its on the same level of undertale.
---
PS: I was afraid of streaming this at first because i thought it had some suggestive stuff in it but it doesn't. It has latex furry transformations but there not as bad it sounds. Its more of a gooey fur transformation kind of thing.\t\t\t\t\t
