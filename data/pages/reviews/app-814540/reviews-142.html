\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe story made me feel a way I haven't in years, and not in the sense you would think. Of course the concept and plot is original and entertaining, but I haven't been this attached to a video game character since titanfall 2 with BT-7274. The bad endings made me feel like I failed puro and would never recover. The true ending filled me with a bitter-sweet happiness that everything was going to be OK but I would never see it through. I know that this game will always be there and I can return and relive all the wonderful moments whenever I want. But I'm filled with unease knowing that what comes next has not been written and may never be and as the game itself says, "It is pointless to relive the past." The concept of maintaining your humanity in a world overrun by these creatures is interesting and believable to some degree. And if saying I enjoyed it makes me a furry, then so be it, I wouldn't have it any other way. The plot is straightforward but becomes filled with bumps and hills that make way for the development of the relationship between puro and the unnamed human. That relationship was the only thing that kept me going in tough segments like the latex forest and water level, it kept me going because I wanted to see where it went. Once the game finished and I couldn't see where it went after that, it left me feeling hollow. Now I can only hope for a sequel so I can see where it goes. But from the looks of it, that may only be wishful thinking. It's obvious a lot of love went into this game and I definitely felt it. If you're reading this and haven't played the game, I highly recommend it. If you have played it, then you probably understand. And if DragonSnow is reading this, thank you for such an amazing game, I hope you plan to make a sequel. And thank you for everything I couldn't fit into one steam review.\t\t\t\t\t

"Changed" is a very solid 2d pixel-graphic adventure game(which I love) with the furry theme that we find pleasant.

Each boss fight takes up to 20 mins to learn. It is a challenge but sooo far from "frustration". It is when I see reviews saying that the game's fights are frustrating I feel frustrated.

But you see endings can be sudden. You do want to avoid frustration here.

After reaching sudden ending for 2 times, I looked up the guides on steam community. On the third attempt, boss fights take less than 3 attempts(1 min roughly) with those RNGs. Perhaps you dont want to do repeated work(like what I did). Simple, save each game progress separately so that you can revive from the place where you missed something.

Overall amazing game 9.5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
