\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs funny as it would be to write only "Puro." on a 69 hour time at review (with a 420 on record goal), informative reviews are usually better than joke ones.

Graphics: May take a little getting used to when starting out, but it is good. Personally, it took me just a few transfurs to like the style.
Story: Can't be predicted easily and accurately in a good way, which could potentially add an extra layer of immersion. Generally, it's well thought of and put together.
Music: Has a retro-like OST that goes well with the pixel art. It's better in some places than others, but pretty good overall.
Difficulty: Imagine Dark Souls as a 2D furry game, but with minimal health and no ability to fight (QTEs are the only exception). It does get easier with practice, but even then, you must heavily rely on RNG. 100% speedrunable.
Probably the reason this game exists: Transformation. Transformation everywhere. Many consider this a fetish game because of it, which is true. Don't want to see a furry TF after every death? You're playing the wrong Dark Souls. Coming from somebody who actually has the fetish, I love almost every transfur and keep replaying the game just for them. Latex is also another (intentional?) possible fetish in this game, but not like I care.
Recommended to: Anybody with a transformation or latex fetish. Or just furries in general. A friend calls most of the transfurs "skin absorption" which is supposedly a kind of vore, and I don't see how he's wrong. I don't even know how I can cope with him sometimes.

In summary, Changed is an amazing game. Just about everything about it is good, and has no flaws from my point of view. 10/10, best furry game.
Sidenote: Can be suggestive pretty often. It's usually not that bad in a sense (or so I think) and still is a good game. Just keep that in mind.\t\t\t\t\t
