Well in total. I am very mixed on Changed I might go for other endings if I feel like it but in all honesty I felt more confused than anything. If I knew how to make games I would make a more horror themed version of Changed and allow for you to defend yourself more. But Dragonsnow if you are reading this I think your game is great just flawed but I'd still recommend this game its kinda like Silent Hill 4 its got baggage but if you are willing to take it, you'll find good game
