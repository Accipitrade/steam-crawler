\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd like to preface that this is a pretty "furry" game. There's a lot of anthro animal characters, a lot of transformation sequences in to said furry creatures (if you die), and some of them are sexually suggestive (though there is no explicitly pornographic transformations, I think). If that bothers you, you'll be turned off by this game quickly.

This is a weird one to recommend. A lot of the puzzles are cryptic and the enemies have an element of RNG that severely hampers the puzzle gameplay. I personally played the game with a guide, using it to avoid cheap shot encounters and help me out when I got stuck and I would recommend even seasoned puzzle gamers have one handy.

Flawed gameplay aside, the story is interesting. The world isn't built strongly, but it does a great job of making you feel isolated in a strange and hostile place. The game is brief at about 5-6 hours personally for all 5 endings (playtime shown doesn't factor time played offline) and its easy to become invested in the great character you meet on the way. I recommend using a guide to get to the secret ending, it left me with... a feeling I won't soon forget.

TL;DR: Gameplay can be frustrating, but there's a gem of an experience under the latex.\t\t\t\t\t
