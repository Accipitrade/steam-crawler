TLDR I RECOMMEND THIS GAME HOWEVER I STRONGLY URGE YOU TO USE A GUIDE IF YOU DO

Changed is a fantastic story wrapped up in a neat and easy to play package. The game has no combat and is comprised mostly of running from enemies and solving puzzles. Now, I know I just said it's easy to play but fair warning it has some ABSOLUTELY BRUTAL RNG elements, places where trial and error is the only way to proceed and, a fairly archaic save point system that make some parts of the game absolutely agonizing without a guide. Be prepared to fight some bosses over and over again just because the game is as DragonSnow puts it: Flawed. Also worth noting, don't play it on easy mode, it's not easier and it locks you into the games first bad ending.

There is also a remixed version of this game called Changed-Special that is included with the purchase of the main game. This version which I have not yet played includes Updated Graphics and a slightly altered story narrative that DragonSnow recommends playing after you complete the main original game. As of time of writing, it is now accessible though the main menu of the main game. For some reason it used to be tucked away in the tools section of the steam library. I plan to update this review once I have finished Changed-Special, but i have seen other steam reviews saying that it is easier. I personally can't say for sure.

I especially recommend this game to anyone who is a fan of transformation, latex, or furries in general.
Additionally, while this game doesn't feature explicit sexual content, it is highly suggestive and includes kink content
Another really nice thing about this game is once you have completed it, it drops you into a room where you can watch all of the transformation scenes in the entire game with no consequences.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's iffy as to whether or not I'd recommend this game. Given what it is, I'd say yes, but the gameplay is... annoying. Best way to put it.

There's multiple issues I personally have with the game. First and foremost is the fact that all the cutscenes, all the dialogue, it's unskippable. The game wouldn't be as annoying if you could press a button and skip all the dialogue. The game requires a lot of trial and error, and I mean *a lot*, so it's frustrating to have to spam spacebar/enter/Z every time you load to skip to the back to the gameplay to try again.

The unskippable dialogue is just one of the annoying aspects though. As said, the game requires a lot of trial and error, and that's because it's difficult. But not the kind of difficult where you need to try over and over to get the pattern down pat or w/e, no, it's difficult in the sense that the creatures and attacks are semi-random. Oftentimes you get caught because of RNG, because the creature decided to move up instead of left, or because an attack forces you to move backwards, or you're unable to avoid it. It's a second frustrating aspect.

A third frustrating point is the movement. The game's movement is a bit... iffy at times, and it's especially noticeable in chases. You'll tap up to avoid a trap, only for the game to ignore that input and have you walk right into the trap instead. I can't tell you how many times I've tried chases just trying to get the game to listen when I press up or down.

So, again, it's iffy as to whether or not I'd recommend it. I can recommend it for the story and for the graphics - it's cute, and if you're a furry or a transformation fanatic/fetishist it's something to consider. But if you're easily frustrated, don't like difficult games, or don't like repetition, then this isn't the game for you. You're going to be doing a lot of trial and error, you're going to be repeating sections a lot due to failure, and the game is difficult.

Can't change the difficulty either without having things blocked off.\t\t\t\t\t
