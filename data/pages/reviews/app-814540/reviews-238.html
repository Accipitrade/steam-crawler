\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVERY short review: FURRY BAIT LATEX/TRANSFORMATION GAME. NO MORE QUESTIONS.

Standard review: Well... Uh... Hey, at least we still have our past memor- oh wait that's gone too huh? Well, crud. Jokes aside, "Changed" is a rather interesting game that many people are aware of by now. It's a fairly difficult puzzle survival "horror" that have some good story lore tied to it. A game made in RPG maker and for once it's a well crafted game from that engine, not that RPG maker is a bad platform to develop on...

Likes:
-Graphics are simple yet effective, reminds me of the 8/16-bit era of video games. Quite colorful too!
-Puzzles are the "Think outside the box" kind of deal, not too easy nor hard. All the hints I will give you too.
-Music is short but the tunes are quite catchy like in the library.
-...The animations when you get transformed is the best/worst part about this game and I can't help to be curious at certain scenarios on what's going to happen if I touch that goop. "Curiosity killed the cat..." You can be transfurred into a anthro street cat with bling on it. A STREET CAT WITH BLING I TELL YOU! I really like the animations in this game.
-The Story in this game isn't the best thing ever told but it's worth the investment if you look into it quite a bit. A game where society have collapse to an unknown virus and scientist desperately tried to save humanity but to no avail... All you know that you're the last one "alive" in this abandon world and the choices are up to you. It's ♥♥♥**d in the end. You have no choice but to join them regardless if you want to or not... YOU WILL GET TRANSFORMED. IT'S OVER FOR YOU! It's not all bad though, they have oranges!

Dislikes:
-Options for this game are very limited (No Sound/Binding controls/graphics settings) even though this game IS very easy to run even with a toaster. But we still have those people that have very low-end PC's that can't even run Tetris on it.
-Movement is adequate in the game but it feels a bit unresponsive at times.
-At first this game is Trial and error, hope you like restarting every 12 seconds after you got transformed if you're new to this game! And if you're a regular player for this game well... It's going to happen less often. I hope.

This game have some love and care put into it and still to this day, "Changed" received some massive updates that even making a "Remake" of the original game called "Changed: Special!" If you looking for a game that's fit into the weird Indie games that you played before this game will be up your ally. It's a shame that AAA games are less enjoyable now-a-days compared to Indie games now... I'm just talking what I feel right now. Go on, get out of here!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat I thought would be a cute, fun, story-driven adventure is nothing more than a blatant, fetishy, furry fan-service cashgrab, not even worth the 2$ I paid for.

Tedious, annoying "puzzles" riddled with terrible checkpoints and random bullshittery, the game tells you to look for hints to unveil the full story, but at the same time punishes you for inspecting random items. Seriously, taking interest in an odd looking crystal? Game over.

There are no real "puzzles" in the game, the ones that actually require you to think take very little time to figure out, the rest of the "puzzles" being a combination of randomness and endless trial-and-error. It is very easy to get bored of the game when you keep dying and having to read the text, skipping which barely speeds anything up, watch the cutscene and everything else, every time you load a save point. Extremely player-unfriendly environment.

While the characters are cutesy and their writing is alright (although the translation is pretty abysmal), I did not find the story interesting at all. From the very first 20 minutes of playing, it was already obvious where the plot would be heading. I truly expected a decent experience, but the only redeeming quality of the game is that it has cute characters, which I'd assume is the main reason people purchase the game.

Art style isn't really my thing either, the game looks very low-effort, as if it were made in MS Paint.

Soundtrack is alright, but really nothing special.

If you're looking for a game similar to this one, but doing everything this game is trying to, but better, check out OneShot, not only does it have cute characters, but it is truly an incredible experience.\t\t\t\t\t
