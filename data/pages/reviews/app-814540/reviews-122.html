\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEverything about the game is fine except for the gameplay, which is an absolute nightmare of poor design and absurd difficulty. From nearly RNG-dependent chase sequences to the tiniest and easiest to miss clues being the most important, this game expects the player to already know the game inside and out even if they've never heard of the game before. There also appears to be an absurd amount of surprise enemies; some of which punish the player for exploring the world that was crafted for the game. I don't know if these issues are fixed in the Special Edition of the game or not, but the game I played was incredibly rough to get through (and I'm still trying to push through to the end). Don't buy this game; just watch someone on YouTube play it if you're curious.

EDIT: Finally got around to finishing the game. Same issues as before and a bug(?) with the last boss before receiving the bad ending. Story is practically non-existent and I have no desire to go after a better ending, which would involve replaying the entire game again. As a reminder, this is all from the base game purchased through Steam, not the Special Edition.\t\t\t\t\t
