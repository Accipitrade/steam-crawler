\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a -hard- game, and probably only enjoyable if you're interested in the scenarios that unfold in it.
But if you happen to be just that, it's awesome! The difficulty remains, and it can be disconcerningly frustrating at times. But this only enhances the feelings it wants to evoke-that you are alone, surrounded by creatures stronger, faster and more powerful than you, and you are very probably going to fall to them, sooner than you hope.
I've arrived at about what I think is the first half at 5hrs of play. It feels very replayable so far, and seems to have a good bunch of content-and I'm sure after a few times I get over each area, I'll gain better sense of movement, control etc. to help me through those same obstacles with less effort and less time.
Some of the puzzles I've seen so far have a good sprinkle of what in any other game would feel like artificial difficulty (ex. RNG timed movement), but here fits nicely with the tone of powerlessness the game puts on you.
A much-needed improvement is the options menu, not accessible by the regular interface of the game and instead opened with F1. It needs to be translated, and feels very confusing. A nice addition would the the possibility to avoid going back to the menu and loading a save (a "retry y/n" would be the most perfect thing).
Other than that, I would totally recommend this game! Again, be prepared for a hard game, and one that can throw you unknown stuff relatively far from a savespot.\t\t\t\t\t
