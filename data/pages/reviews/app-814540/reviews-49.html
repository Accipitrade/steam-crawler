\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI did not expect to love this as much as I did. I picked this up because, well, I love slime and slime TF. It's why you catch interest in a game like this, and I'm not afraid to admit that.

But time and time again, I kept getting surprised at how much the developer seemed to be one step ahead of me. Do something stupid? Game comments on it. Examine everything? Descriptions of cookie cutter decoration objects become more and more flippant. Be filled with hubris after winning a tough segment, and stand over your defeated foe? Shoulda taken the win when you had the chance, They throw one last spiteful attack. Enjoy doing the boss fight again, sucker.

And then, the actual storyline. Gosh. I grew attached to these characters, and understood their motivations and inner struggles. It feels ridiculous to say this. I must look ridiculous to you, reader, who's too embarrassed to purchase the game, but are finding themselves reading the reviews below the page, waiting for someone to give you the excuse. You should just get it.


I'm going to be thinking about this game, it's characters, and it's overarching story for a long time.

It is pretty hard though, gotta admit. you have 99 save files. I used almost every one.\t\t\t\t\t
