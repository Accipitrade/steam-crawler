\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this sort of as a joke, but ended up playing through it multiple times.

The standard game was fine. I liked some of the puzzles and the art-style and music is nice too, but it got really infuriating in some cases.
Especially the chase sequences annoyed me, due to the controls being pretty clunky. You can get stuck on objects or walk into enemies quite easily which does not help when there is a fast moving creature running you down.
The safe points are sometimes placed decently but can also be located in ways that lead to some backtracking or make you repeat some longer dialogue/ puzzle sequences. It was never a huge issue though.

The story is alright.



Changed-special, the remaster is at the point of writing this not yet finished.
From what is playable right now however, I can say it is a pretty big improvement.
The characters seem more fleshed out, the puzzles are easier to understand (even for idiots like me) and the environments look a fair bit nicer.
Due to the game now allowing you to safe before every encounter it feels a lot fairer and there is far less backtracking and puzzle repeating. It pretty much fixes all issues I had with the standard edition (for example it redesigns some rooms to feel less rng driven and allows for better movement with the still pretty clunky controls). There is a lot new content like puzzles, bosses and a bunch more dialogue.

I'm really looking forward to the full release and think the developer did a great job improving.
