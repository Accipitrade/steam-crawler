\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR; It's a great game; bonus if you want to witness one of the most wholesome monsters in horror games.

Fair Warning: SOME SPOILERS and do keep in mind that it does have so fetishy stuff in it to which I'm immune of because I'm already a Pro-OwO-togen and if by chance in the past if I played this game I too would likely be Changed (badumm-tss) so try not to make a fuss about it, it's weird.

The difficulty is somewhat surprising, the puzzles are okay, but the enemies; heck all of the enemies can outrun you and ambush you really quick if I you don't have a proper plan and it does get repetitive at least at my end (I'm quite stubborn), there are some few quick-time events but they were not much of a threat, they mostly prey on the player's curiosity and some of them did have unique interactions so do interact with everything if you want the full experience. My Original play time was 11 hours half of which was spent vibing on music theme and other was due to my own faults, it plays with my weaknesses a lot I am ashamed I will never learn from those.

I'd say the characters is where this game is at it's strongest. SPOILERS AHEAD, Your greatest ally is a monster named Puro and by gods if he isn't one the best characters I have interacted with, Something about his existence radiates innocence in it's simplest form that outshines his already terrifying appearance as he is a monster, but his goals, dreams and his motivation doesn't exist just because you are the protagonist no-siree he will kill you if he sees an opportunity and reason to do so. As for the Main villain I didn't expect to sympathize with him as it really didn't seem so at first, as to be honest he really just appeared out of nowhere but it did somewhat make sense at the given circumstance and it work quite well in the end. To his credit I am surprise he even is alive given that this doctor literally watched as he lose all of humanity in his fingertips and he had to suffer after it, now that he has a chance to make things right, why wouldn't he do it? Why wouldn't he want not to kill you? For all he know is that he is right but in his heart he wishes that he was doing was wrong, and that is something I can sympathize for.

Overall I'd say this game is great given that this is an RPGMAKER game and that curses sound effects will never leave your mind once you started playing other indie-titles, it doesn't have much replay value other than Puro which is a replay value of it's own right and yeah that's about it.

You may not be Changed, but you would stay for Puro. That's all and have a great day!\t\t\t\t\t
