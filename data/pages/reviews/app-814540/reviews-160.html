With the terrible lag in a lot of rooms, it makes challenging puzzles nearly impossible and very frustrating. Some say it's only a few rooms but, for me, it felt like every other room. Couple that with the save system that limits where you can save, you are left repeating a room and rewatching a cut scene dozens of times. Despite all of this, I enjoy the game. It's very cute and a lot of fun! I loved playing this game, despite the lag. I highly recommend it. :)\t\t\t\t\t
