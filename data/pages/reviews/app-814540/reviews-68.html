\r\n\t\t\t\t\tArt is cute, but gameplay should be better.

This game obviously has some kind of fetishes attached to it, latex and furries, manly. I'm not fond of that, but I know there's people that like it, so whatever.

It was kinda fun to play, although it was also VERY frustrating. Not because it's a hard game, but because there was some gameplay problems. I think the game would be less frustrating if they were to be fixed.

Its perspective is not good. It's really difficult to tell when you're going to hit an object or not, sometimes I see the character stepping on something but it doesn't count, and sometimes it counts. There was a time I thought I couldn't go through a path because it was blocked by a table, but it turned out that there was a LINE between tables that was considered a path... I don't know if it's supposed to be that way to make you try everything, even what it seems impossible, to solve the puzzles, or if it's just a poor design choice. I think it's the latter.

But what truly frustrated me the most was that you can't completely skip dialogs and cut scenes you've already seen (just the ones after you die). It's so annoying when you die (and you do die a lot in this game) and have to manually go through every dialog just to get to the part you were, or watch the same cut scene with no skip button and then die again and repeat and repeat and repeat. I would be REALLY grateful if there was save stations AFTER the dialogs and cut scenes, not before them. Or insert a auto skip button. Pressing the F12 key allows you to go to the menu after you die, why not add something similar for the dialogs and cut scenes? That problem makes this game annoying. I had to play it throught several months, I got that angry.

Don't get me wrong, I didn't HATE the game. If I could, I would give it a neutral review, but steam doesn't allow that. I can recommend it if you like frustrating games and fetish stuff with furries. Maybe you'll like the story. Maybe you'll just appreciate the artwork. I thought it would be more of a cute game with twisted endings, but I think the fetish thing ruined it for me.

In conclusion, I don't know if I can recommend or not recommend, I think this is the type of game that you have to see for yourself if you like it or not. Maybe buy it on sale or watch someone else play it. Giving a thumbs up because I didn't drop it completely, so it's not that terrible.\t\t\t\t\t
