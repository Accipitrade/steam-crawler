It's very nice and difficult puzzle game to which my only complain after finishing and analyzing is lack of checkpoints system. That would allow for re-trying sequences faster as sometimes getting to next try via skipping dialogues can be worse than repeating. Also once you know what to do getting through sequences is way nicer, mostly when you figure out how to do so and that's not easy.

Changed resonated with me on such extreme level after finishing it in every shape and form possible it left me empty, depressed and crying out not sure even why. That's experience I could re-live over and over again, that's how good it is.

So if you don't mind the theme surrounding Changed. Give it a go, get through and you will be super happy you did.

Sometimes I was thinking like: "Why this section HAVE TO include that obstacle?". It was annoying as I wanted to just go forward but nope... repeat same corridor X times... But in Special edition same section was re-worked and completely straigh forward, it turned so easy it was totally unsatisfactioning. So yea, after thinking, if there would be a sensible placed checkpoints what would just skip all the skipping between trying. Then that would be absolute purffection.

I envy you who going to experience this masterpiece for the first time.\t\t\t\t\t
