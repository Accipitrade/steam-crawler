\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI just want to sit through the game and read the story lines and be challenged a bit with puzzles, not plunged into a frustrating mess of near unsolvable enemy puzzles.

It's a shame this game is near unplayable for most players because it has good things to offer, namely the soundtrack and some of the story. I have had to resort to watching a playthrough rather than enjoy the game myself, which really sucks.

Whilst I don't mind difficult puzzles that require you to think etc, So many enemy chase puzzles are near unsolvable unless you're super precise. The 'Easy Mode' (which is a mode I usually always choose knowing how crap I am with controls) is not really a thing that exists - it does not make the game easier, it ends it early, because f you that's why.

I shouldn't need to 'get good' to play through a rpg. Most other rpg's have a mechanic for casual gameplay - not to make the game stupid easy, but making sure that the vast majority players can proceed within a small handful of attempts. It's very easy for any developer to get accustomed to what they've made, which is why the game feels like it was not play tested properly. It SERIOISLY spoils what is otherwise a quite interesting game.\t\t\t\t\t
