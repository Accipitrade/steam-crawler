\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCreative story that tells a fantasy tale of a destroyed world trapped within a research complex. Unfortunately, complex extends to the randomised and unforgiving movements of enemies within the first quarter of the game, and when combined with save-points that feel few and far between, makes for an infuriating introduction.

Luckily, that smoke dissipates and leaves behind a wonderful, Puro. Someone who understands your struggles and foretells a saddening backstory that unveils the determination to prevail and escape. From then, the game is a truly unique adventure with all the ups and downs to ride.

A worthwhile indulgence for anyone with an anthropomorphic fascination that spews a wide spectrum of imagining creativity around every new scene. it is captivating and proof that new realistic graphics aren't needed if the story can stand for itself. 8/10 for me.
