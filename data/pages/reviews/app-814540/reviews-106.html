\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf I pay 6 whole-ass dollars for a fetish game, I'm looking for two things: a good fetish, and a good game.

Let's start with the fetish: great. Biggest point of this game. The graphical style is amazing, the CGs are beautiful, the furries are cute as ♥♥♥♥. I absolutely adore looking at this game, and Puro's soft little face, and I would die for him. Exactly what I came for.

But the game. The GAME. I get that this is the dev's first game. I get that playing the game in a translated language can distort the experience. But it's just too much to ignore. Writing this after 7 hours of frustrated gameplay, staring at walkthroughs for nigh-impossible puzzles, and dealing with the incredibly RNG-heavy conditions, I can't recommend this game to anyone in good faith.

I really wanted to like this game. I love the story and the art so much. But this game is of massive acclaim in the furry community, and something of that high standard I feel should at least be reflected in the gameplay. This game is difficult for all the wrong reasons, and Changed-special (while fixing some of these problems) still doesn't hold the full game's weight.

DragonSnow has a lot of potential to be a good game designer. They have so much already down so well, and putting a game on Steam to such high praise is amazing enough for them. But Changed is just not enjoyable enough of an experience to play yourself, especially not for a price.

Keep a close eye on their further projects, but for now... just watch a playthrough on YouTube. I have a headache.\t\t\t\t\t
