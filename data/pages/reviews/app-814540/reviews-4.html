UwU-y Gooey Transfurmations!

Alright, let's talk about Changed, an action-puzzle game that's definitely not your typical run-of-the-mill experience. It was released back in 2018 and developed by DragonSnow, with some interesting background music by Shizi. But be warned, this game has some kinky, fetish undertones, so brace yourself.

The plot is simple yet intriguing. You wake up in a research facility after being unconscious for who knows how long.

Your goal?

Escape.

But here's the twist, everything in the facility wants to transform you into a latex furry. Yeah, it's as bizarre as it sounds.

As you progress through the rooms, you uncover the backstory from various written materials and meet other characters with their own motivations.

The gameplay is a mix of puzzles and action sequences. Figuring out the puzzles can be a challenge, especially when the rules suddenly change without warning. Fail, and you'll face some rubbery transformations. But hey, that's part of the appeal for some players, right?

Visually, the game is simple but effective. The chiptune-style music adds to the tension during chase scenes and creates a calming atmosphere in other areas. The transformations are a major theme, and while some occur at the sprite level, others are displayed as full-screen images with more detail. Some of the transformations in the Changed-Special version even feature bondage elements, which might interest some players.

So, is Changed worth playing?

Well, if you're into furry, latex transformations, this game might just be your cup of tea. But if you're person that's just curious and want to see what this game is about... It's certainly not without its flaws.

The puzzle challenges can be fun, but there are probably better puzzle games out there if that's your main interest. The real charm of Changed lies in the quirky transformations that happen when you fail. Just remember, it's not your typical game, so approach with an open mind and a willingness to embrace the unconventional.

Overall, its a good game and my curiosity has been satiated. Furry or not, the game does have a charming indie feel and you can definitely tell that this was a passion project at heart.\t\t\t\t\t

You get immersed in the story and ideas of the game, and the horror aspects are definitely there, despite it being somewhat lacking in horror, it still is there. But it doesnt need extreme horror to be good. The style the game uses is great. The remake called "Changed Special" is better, but the original still is good. Just dont fall for the misconceptions about this game! Claims that call it various nsfw things are wrong. While the game is mildly suggestive, and a bit more so in the Special Edition, so is countless things people and even kids enjoy that are more suggestive than this game. The "nudity" warning is there because of the fact the animals are animals and therefore dont have clothes, and the mild suggestive content. A lot of claims about hidden nsfw meanings have been debunked by the creator. The creator rated it 12+ on an interview and I would agree with that. I love this game!
