ABOUT THIS GAME
Game Synopsis:
"Changed" is a difficult, action puzzle game with a lot of chase.
The protagonist of the game awakened in a strange room and came to an unfamiliar laboratory building.
In the adventure, he will encounter all kinds of unknown creatures, each of which can easily “plunder” his body.
He can only make constant explorations and try to walk out of this dangerous building.
In this deserted world, who is secretly guiding him to go out? Why is he trapped here? What happened to the outside world?
All kinds of mysteries are intricate and the truth will slowly emerge from the interaction and exploration.
Of course, be careful not to get lost in this building...
Features Introduction:
* The protagonist can only use simple movements and sprints because he is the prey of all creatures.
* No props, even tools, all problems must be solved with wisdom and action.
* High game difficulty, Japanese fusion RPG and I WANNA games.
* 8-bit style music evokes childhood memories in a retro style.
* Difficult, suitable for players who are good at watching and daring to try.
* Understand the characteristics of creatures in one failure to escape them.
* Interactive traps, text eggs, CG plots, many investigable items for exploration.
* Multi-line outcomes, triggering different outcome lines based on interactions and investigations.
* Approximately six hours of playable length (if possible).

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game definitely has its flaws, but I'm surprised with how much it stuck with me. The characters, Story and World in Changed are surprisingly well written. You even get a pog companion. Each area felt different from the last and had different feels and it never felt out of place. Each had their own enemies and mechanics too which keeps things interesting.

If you've heard things about poor English translation, as of the time I wrote this review I believe was improved significantly over time since I couldn't find any of the old mistranslated.

I do need to address the difficulty however: This game really likes hitting you with a lot of cheap deaths and you cant skip cutscenes, so you might end up repeating a segment over and over due to hidden walls behind obstacles or an obstacle moving in front of you killing you instantly and then you're stuck spamming z through dialog you've already seen several times,
then right after you get through the challenge, the game does not give you a savepoint, followed by hitting you with a QTE using a different button than the last 4 QTE's for some reason sending you all the way back to the beginning of the chase sequence again.
The enemy ai also have you praying to RNGsus that they wont trap you or spam a 3 tile movement speed until it kills you (oftentimes unavoidably).
And for the true ending you've got a scene where you need to wait for a character to walk down with you as a requirement and their is no way to know this beforehand, and you have to exhaust his dialog so I thought it was all I had to do not walk him across, had to spend an hour and a half on a new save just to retry too.

I still managed to enjoy the game in spite of the unfair difficulty though since other aspects like the story and the OST was banging hard so I could bear with it lol.
Despite its issues I still highly recommend this game if you're into RPGmaker stuff and weird furry ♥♥♥♥.
BUT only if you have the patience for a lot of trial and error.

Also this is the first time I wrote a review this long so sry if its not organised to well\t\t\t\t\t
