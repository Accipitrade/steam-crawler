Gameplay:

(3/10)

For the most part, bad. Controls are weird, no diagonal movement, sometimes unresponsive and it’s just plain buggy and frustrating. Also not being able to beat the game fully on easy mode is a really bad move on the developer’s part.

Graphics:

(7/10)

They look alright, fits in with the aesthetic of game, although it could use some shading here and there.

Music:

(4/10)

Average, not good, not bad, however it can get pretty annoying really fast due to it being repetitive.

Technical

(2/10)

The game doesn’t offer you a way to play in 16:9, forcing you to play it in 4:3, it also suffers from frame drops and slowdowns on reasonable hardware.

Story:

(9/10)

The story is the single biggest reason for you to continue playing the game, it's really good and motivates you to keep going.

Conclusion:

Changed really focuses on the story, if you are here just for the gameplay, leave, it’s not a game for you, but if you are interested in the story, go ahead, it will be a unique experience. So overall, I would say that it’s worth buying just to experience it.

Score: 5/10
