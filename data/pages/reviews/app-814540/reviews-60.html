This game actually has a story – a very good story, in fact. You play as a clueless man who has woken up without memory in a post-apocalyptic world and it’s your mission to find out what happened and make it out of the skyscraper alive. I won’t spoil, but it’s a story about greed, capitalism, eugenics and what really means to be human. I’m still amazed this is a 2017 game even though it converses so well with… more recent world events. I would recommend this to any fan of good dystopian writing, if they are willing to overlook (or enjoy) the implacable horniness that permeates it.\t\t\t\t\t
