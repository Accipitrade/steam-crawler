\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend this game to anyone who is a furry, but thats where it ends.
This game, apart from the story line, is very bad.
I'm honestly not sure if its just meant to be insanely hard and annoying, or it's just because the game was made poorly.

Cons:
Save points aren't where they need to be, they're placed basically randomly, after beating a tough boss there is no guarantee you'll be able to save.

Dying is very frequent, however when you die it takes you to the main menu, which is annoying as it takes sometimes more time to get back into the game than time you actually spend 'fighting' a boss before you die again.

Puzzles are very repetitive and just get boring after a while.

Takes a long time to actually begin the story of the game, making you just want to quit as your efforts aren't going towards the story.

There's an ending MID game, in which you will have to restart the game if you get it. (Took me a month to get to that point)

Controls, the controls are laggy and often don't register inputs.

Boss after boss after boss, it seems that you don't get break in this game.

Insanely hard 'filler' mission, that don't contribute to your progression.

Pros:

Great story line, you feel as if you are the character.

Great game to play whenever you want, no long loading times, you can just start it up when you are bored and play for a couple minutes.

Great art, I love how the unlikely deaths are still animated and full of art, there's no corners cut with the art.

Deaths are actually interesting to look at due to the art and animations, sometimes going out your way to die to see what new art there is.

Puro is cute.

I don't recommend you spend hours playing this game at one time, you will get a blood pressure issue from how much you rage.
