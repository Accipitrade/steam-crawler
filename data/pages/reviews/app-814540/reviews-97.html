I feel the only imaginable way to actually enjoy this game is if you're a furry with strange kinks. But even if you have those kinks, you won't enjoy the game. The game sucks; it is unironically horrible. I thought this was a "furry Undertale," but even calling it that is a disgrace to Undertale.

The art style makes it unbearable to play because half the time, you can't even tell if you're on the same tile as an enemy. And not to mention the abhorred gameplay. Most of the game is dying; after about 5-10 deaths, you figure out how to get past the puzzles. Not to mention how all-out terrible the puzzles are as well. They are not the slightest bit enjoyable.

The game tries to use science to make this scenario understandable, but even using the science provided by the game, it makes absolutely zero sense. There is no reason why the virus needs physically rape the main character after catching them. It's such an unnecessary game mechanic.

This game is mediocre at best, and even that is giving it too much credit.

I bought this game for the meme and to see what all the other furries were going on and on about, and I don't regret it.
Changed was annoyingly difficult at times, and basically impossible to get through without a guide, but in retrospect that isn't going to be what I remember.

Because it feels very homemade and genuine in its presentation, and the story and characters is good enough to keep you going. Not that they're the best characters ever made, but like the rest of the game it has an unfiltered indie feel to it.

Which brings me to the "issue" of this being a fetish game. Let me ask you this: if a fanservice heavy anime like One Piece can still have a good plot and characters, why can't a game like this? The, ahem, 'subject matter' is definitely here, and you will notice it, but it's not jarring. The universe this game sets up allows for it to feel natural, integrating the developers interests into the narrative rather than forcing it in.

So whether you should play this or not depends on your comfort level with a technically sfw fetish, and your willingness to see the game that lies within.\t\t\t\t\t
