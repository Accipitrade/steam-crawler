\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite how it is, I quite like this game. However, there were some glaring flaws about it. The game can be very unfair and confusing at times. There is a part in the game where you have to guess a passcode, and in order to get true ending, you have to do specific things. Problem is, if you miss out on one interaction, you have to start all over again. . The controls don't make it any better. I had moments where I ran into an enemy during a chase because of how stiff the controls were. The story, while I did enjoy it, was bare bones and the characters felt underdeveloped (despite how much I loved them).

I quite liked the puzzles, though, and some of the areas were fun to go through. Without being said, I still kept a guide near me in case I was stuck somewhere.

So, would I recommend this game? Hard to say. It depends on your tastes. And if you are willing to reject your humanity and become a furry. Otherwise, I would not recommend this.\t\t\t\t\t
