This game is overflowing with lore so much so that at times it can feel even a little overwhelming. But I promise you that this game is very fun even though some of the controls and A.I. is clunky it is still A very amazing game. the bosses and chase sequences are usually passed with skills but sometimes it relies on RNG. Sometimes the A.I. is moves faster than the last attempt or slower which can throw off your rhythm of how to go about passing the sequence heck sometimes even the movement screws with you by making it so you don't move in the direction you pressed when you are pressing two keys at once even though it worked last attempt. This may cause you to rage quit but even though I have rage quit many times I can't help coming back to try and get past that one part that has stopped me and then I somehow get through. That is to say you can really only pass some parts with time and patience along with a clear mind. This game is overflowing with character and I would recommend this game to anyone with a interest in a furry puzzle game that is very unforgiving. That or you have a transformation fetish either or I don't judge!
