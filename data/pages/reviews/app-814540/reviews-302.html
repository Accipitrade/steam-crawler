\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI do not play a lot of RPG's to begin with, but man this game is really something.

First of all, the gameplay is very simple... controls are pretty much self explanatory.
You have one goal... escape without being transformed into a goo furry form since almost anything that moves tries to transform you.

I never got bored at any point of the game, some parts are a bit more frustrating than others, and it's more of a trial and error and learn from your mistakes to succeed.

I admit, I have gone through the same puzzle multiple times just to see how many transformations you can get (Yes is a bit kinky to see how you transform)

This game itself is relatively short, but since you gotta try hard to succeed... you'll definetly will take a while to figure things out and the experience is very much enjoyable along with the characters.

What I think it could have been improved:

-I would have liked to see a few more characters in the game
-I understand the translation from Chinese is not simple, but It lacks a bit of a better translation, some parts still show the square thingy (unidentified character, chinese I figure) and the english is a bit broken at times, but is very well understood, I'm not really complaining about it, just pointing it out.


Besides that...
I enjoyed every second of this game, every pixel... and every transformation <3

The soundtrack is really nice too!

It is a very cute game which teaches you patience, friendship, perseverance and a little of science I guess?

-----------------------------------
TLDR:

If you like:

-Simple gameplay
-Challenging puzzles
-Trial and error
-Furry
-Transformation
-Goo
-Multiple Endings
-Cute characters

This game is a must for you. Very cheap at the moment and I totally bought the soundtrack.

For me it is worth every cent I spend on this, I expect more from the creator for the next games <3\t\t\t\t\t
