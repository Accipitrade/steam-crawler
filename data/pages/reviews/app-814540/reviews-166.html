\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSpoilerless
If you can't tolerate furry, transformation, latex fetishes, then you will not like this game.
But if you can at least tolerate those things, you are in for a very challenging, charming, and interesting gaming experience.

When I say challenging, I really do mean it. Every single room in this game takes genuine effort to pass while also not being frustrating. Every time I cleared a room it felt extremely accomplishing and that's not something I usually get from games anymore. While the mechanics are a little bit buggy, they aren't game ruining.

The soundtrack was one of the highlights in my opinion. I played this game with my boyfriend and we agreed at many points that the game wouldn't be nearly as playable as it was without the funky, intense, and sometimes mellow music that would be playing in the background. While the characters *are* limited, I think they are done fantastically. My only gripe would be the dialogue being a bit repetitive at times and not very well 'ironed-out'.

I really love stories like the one portrayed in this game, it interested me a lot and was a major motivation for continuing through the rooms. I really don't want to spoil too much of it because it is honestly best experienced blind, but the storyline, and I promise you, does pay off. The quality is consistent all the way through and I was impressed with how the different motivations of the characters intertwined. There are multiple endings, and I love games that do this.

Since I'm writing a review on this game, I need to address the elephant in the room. Yes, it's extremely suggestive, but not explicit.\t\t\t\t\t
