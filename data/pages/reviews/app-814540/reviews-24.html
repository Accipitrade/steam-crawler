Opening Thoughts
Changed is a game, and I might be a furry now.
I did not get this game for free, I bought me and 3 friends this game with my own money.
13.2 hours of pain and suffering wasting my days playing this terrible kink/fetish game.
This game has awakened something within me, and I don't think that is good.
My life is a disappoint because of this crappy furry game on steam.

The Game Itself
And now, my opinions.

The game itself, kind of sucks. The grid system is really limited and makes moving more annoying. Plus, this game is actually difficult, and I hate that it's difficult because I wasted 13 precious hours on this game. The dialogue can be a bit wonky at some points, but it's rare and makes sense since English wasn't the original language for the game. Some text exchanges can get a bit annoying, especially because you can't skip through text.

Next, the characters. I love Puro (i appreciate the design and character i'm not a furry please) and Dr. K is an interesting character and the fact that he's not a stereotypical villain and just wants to restore humanity is a good touch. The human not tallking but still "talking" makes the character feel more real and like the protagonist.

Lastly, the story and endings. I'm not a professional game reviewer but the story is good enough to be satisfactory. Having to restart multiple times to actually complete part of the story (because i forgot to say certain stuff to puro) got annoying but it was (kinda) worth it in the end. The endings don't fully end the story, which leads to player interpretations of the endings. Personally, I think that the ending where the human gets transfurred and leaves with puro is the true ending, but there isn't really a true ending.

Closing Thoughts
Overall, this game is a 7/10. I would recommend this to any furry, and I might recommend this to anybody that I hate and want to see them become a furry. If you are willing to risk the possibility of becoming a furry, or getting a goo transformation kink, you could play this if you want.

Changed is a game, and I might be a furry now.\t\t\t\t\t
