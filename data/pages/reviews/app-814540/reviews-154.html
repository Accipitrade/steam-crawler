\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI changed my mind on this game (pun not intended). With a couple hours of play time I can conclude that this game is extremely mediocre, boring and just flat over all.
I have a couple reasons why I think this =

1. Game play is mostly focused around puzzle solving and chase sequences, while this may be fun for most people, this is all that the game has to offer.

2. Puzzles are not really fun, you will end up seeing yourself watch the same death "cut scenes" over and over, the puzzles are difficult but not in the way that requires thought but mostly just trial and error. For example in some stages, you are meant to navigate around enemies and instant kill traps, but the enemy AI can feel random at times meaning even if you have a solid plan, they will catch you or you will end up running into the trap.

3. Death cut scenes are novel and unique at first, but once you see the same roll of images X amounts of times, they begin to become irritating and annoying. Sure you can skip them by pressing a button but the issue could be easily fixed by allowing you to simply turn them off.

4. The story isn't very good, sure it gives reason for you having to run away from anthropomorphic goo monsters that are "conveniently" shaped like furries. But the issue is the fact that while the story adds a weak backbone and reasoning for the games plot, it isn't very interesting and deep, the story isn't strong enough to keep the player reeled into the game, its just "there".

5. The characters have the same issue as the story, they don't have much to them and end up falling away from what the possibly could be. For example the 3 main characters are just=
-Confused test subject trying to find a way to live and get out of the building
-The nice smart monster that doesn't want to hurt the protagonist
-The evil scientist who ends up being good because he wants to save the world
They end up really falling into common story tropes which end up feeling repetitive after some time.

Alright, I will give the developer credit where credit is due. He is and indie dev working on a game by himself, he really cant put too much work into other aspects of the game so its understandable why some parts of the game fall flat on their face.

In summary, this game is just passable within what it contains, it not bad enough to where its a completely unplayable and horrible experience, but its also not good enough to be really memorable or enjoyable enough to play through the game several times.
While I wont complain about this game being a "furry game" too much, I can say that I believe the fact that this game contains furries may bring in certain audiences who will look past its issues and just focus on the furries.
