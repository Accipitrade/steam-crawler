\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou might like it or you may utterly despise it. Furries will probably be way more likely to appreciate it, though.

Let's start by saying that, while this game is hard, it's not necessarily as hard as people make it out to be. I managed to complete it in 8 hours, having lost dozens of times but ultimately persevering. Most losses are caused by trial and error or just a mistake of you, the player - "fair" losses. However, this game will occasionally throw losses by RNG at you that are totally outside of your control, even if you played the game perfectly. As a result, this game can seem a lot harder than it really is, and I really don't think this is great design. At least this game gives you a hotkey to jump back to the main menu at any time.

Also note that your choices matter, and if you pick the wrong choices, the story may end in an unexpected way (and not in a way you can recover by reloading your last save). So, when you save, don't just save in the same slot - use all the slots at your disposal (so first save goes in save 1, second goes in save 2, etc.).

Anyway, the story is pretty good, but there are some spelling errors here and there in some of the dialogue (and I'm not talking about the notes on the wall). The OST is pretty good, too. The art style is pretty simplistic, but it works well for the game.

All-in-all, it's fine. I don't think I'd recommend it to just everyone, but it's fine.\t\t\t\t\t
