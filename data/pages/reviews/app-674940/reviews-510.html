\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game feels like it's still in beta.
The concept is good, but the gameplay feels rough, GUI looks unfinished, menus are hard to navigate, active button areas overlap with each other. Some options are displayed as if you're able to click left or right to switch to a different setting, even though they're just buttons (maps setting is a good example of that). Ah, maps. Choosing only random online maps does nothing, it still loads the default maps.

The fact that you have to TURN ON showing points in a competitive game makes me question if the author ever played a game before in his life. Blocking rarely does anything. The summary level shows up randomly, you have to wait till it chooses to display random statistics, you have no control over anything.

The game never ends, some could consider this a feature, I consider this a poor design choice. There's no way to set a point limit, either. The only way to win the game is to stop playing, otherwise the winner never emerges and the levels loop.

It's a little poetic, you know? I stopped playing this game and I indeed feel like a winner.\t\t\t\t\t
