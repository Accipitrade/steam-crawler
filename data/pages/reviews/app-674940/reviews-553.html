7.5/10

Simple yet charming, Stick Fight is such a casual game. There is no complex game system and complicated content, the player in the game to do very simple things, quickly get started and then you can carry out in the game to fight. At this point, this work is undoubtedly done quite perfectly.

Its shortcomings are also very obvious, due to the very easy to get started, lack of substance of the gameplay, and monotonous mode, players will easily get bored after a long time playing. The game system does have some unreasonable design, the absurd way of fighting in addition to the technology also makes the luck to a certain extent will be the winner, which will inevitably make players lose the game in the accident, but fortunately, the overall pace is fast, will not leave too much frustration to the players.

As a game to relieve stress during leisure time, Stick Fight is certainly very good, and even now, it still has a certain number of online players, so players can play anytime and anywhere, whether it is a local match or online multiplayer. The pursuit of casual gaming experience and stress relief is not to be missed by players.\t\t\t\t\t
