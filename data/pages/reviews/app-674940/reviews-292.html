For me, and like a lot of people, Stick Fight: The Game with my current 9.6 hours of play-time, has been a game of action. There are initially 1-n players with 1-n weapons around a given map. After the timer goes to zero, all the players have to do is attempt survival. You go grabbing weapons with your flimsy stick-man arms, or you hit players with them. Or you commit suicide. Like in any necessarily lethal defend, fight, or die situation, it's generally plausible that it won't last more than 2 minutes; that is the situation with every match. The movement of more skilled and crafty players is often noticeable off the bat and preserves the games demanding knowledge of all the functions on the keyboard, and the experience of playing. I have personally enjoyed earning achievements in this game. By the way: "мигающий кинжал" in the achievement section means "flashing dagger". There is even an "invite friend" option which I have yet to try, and probably never will. Weather your a person considering buying this game, an experienced player, the game's developer, a random kid that doesn't have a PC, or anybody interested in the game, I highly recommend checking this one out especially if it's on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
