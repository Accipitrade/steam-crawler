\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend this game for people who have nothing else to do that isn't optional. Its a good way to relax and just have fun with friends or random strangers. Its a PVP game so if you're good at that stuff you should definitley buy In my 88 hours of playing i've found that setting framerate to OFF allows you to get a large amount momentum when blocking in the direction of a jump. If you're getting into this game thats probably the best move you could use.
I recommend this game but there are a few issues that can be a dealbreaker for new players, so be advised. There are some pretty weird invincibilty and invisibilty glitches that crop up from time to time, I've only gotten to understand one. And if you have this game installed, be advised. Invincibilty is a GLITCH not a hack, happens when somebody new joins as the last player is killed and the game switches stages, has to do with the hitbox getting displaced. On the opposite end of the spectrum there is the invisibilty glitch where the model gets displaced. This happens for the same reasons as the last glitch. If the player dies before they can move or do anything in a stage, but is still alive during the stage that means their model is somewhere else and the one that dies is a placeholder. There is also a glitch where the player stutters, depending on your or their connection their ragdoll is somewhere other than where the game thinks it is and it causes the game to try and fill in the gaps leading to the ragdoll stuttering.

With all of that said, this game is a 6.9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
