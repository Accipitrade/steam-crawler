Pros:
- Easy combat
- Fast-paced
- High mobility
- Surprising variety of weapons
- Room for creativity in battle
- Lots of potential for development in terms of updates

Cons
- heavily reliant on community workshop stages
- Certain guns too strong (nothing wrong with some being stronger, just that there's one AR and it's a free win)
- Buggy and glitchy
- No chat log, so it's hard to type in game
- Heavily reliant on you having friends
- Not enough developer made stages
- Gets stale quickly

Don't underestimate the value of dumb fun. This game at 5 dollars is okay if you're buying for yourself. If it's on sale it's definitely a good buy. I bought 3 copies at $2.50 and it's easily worth that.\t\t\t\t\t
