The online is bizarre, it's hard to justify playing this game without a group of friends to join you. The system works and is coherent, but extremely unfulfilling, and there's no real solo gameplay.

The different weapons and setups are what really make the game click, and the feeling of terror when your friend of 4 years gets the space laser is a great bonus.

For the price it's offered at, get it with a friend. It's worth it, but comes with caveats.\t\t\t\t\t
