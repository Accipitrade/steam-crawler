\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll start by saying that I love this game. The weapons are all unique and balanced (Except Laser Machinegun. We don't talk about him.) The game controls well and is accentuated by the physics and cool movement tech, and the maps (except the laser ones. We don't talk about them either) are great. Throwing weapons is a fun balanced mechanic, and blocking is completely irrelevant.

All of that said, this game will do anything but lag traditionally. As a consequence, here is a list of all the glitches I have found in my 12 hours playing:
- Fall Guys Tail Tag level desync problems (Extremely Common)
- The game freaks out a bit when a new player connects to the server
- Falling through the stage (Uncommon) [1]
- As a boss, you can't see your own healthbar
- Walking around in the bottom death zone (Rare) [1]
- Dying before the round starts, then haunting the other players as a spooky ghost (Extremely Rare)
- The game doesn't appear to have a character limit when it comes to pasting, as I saw some guy paste what I presume to be the entire bee movie script in chat, freezing my game for about 6 seconds
- 1 Person remains, but the game continues, forcing them to suicide (Rare)
- Same situation, but the game still continues after the last player dies, resulting in a server softlock (Extremely Rare)

[1] Hasn't happened to me specifically but I've seen it happen to others

I've never experienced an outright crash though, and when it doesn't play like a Wii online game, it's hype AF. The music is also great.

TL;DR This game is great, if you're willing to put up with or even embrace all the bullcrap it has in its bad lag compensation.\t\t\t\t\t
