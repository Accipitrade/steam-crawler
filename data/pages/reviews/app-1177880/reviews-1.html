\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Retrowave DLC is great. All the weapons look really good and the Vindicator 2000 (the revolver) is a really good sidearm. You need a base weapon to craft it with and if you use a pistol to do it, you end up with a 15 round revolver. The reload animation only shows 6 chambers in the cylinder so it looks a bit weird. I used a 15 round pistol to craft it and whenever I start a game with it in my inventory it only has 6 rounds loaded. The ammo isn't lost, it's just loaded into the cylinder. The Volkatronix (the rifle) is good too, but not the best rifle in the world. The gunshot SOUNDS suppressed but it isn't. In the weapon description it says that the Volkatronix will "alert less infected" or something similar. It could alert less, but i feel like it doesn't. The sight on the rifle is nice, but the round thing on the left side of it takes up more space on your screen than a regular rifle. The "electric bullets" are really nice, they work pretty well. The damage and accuracy depends on what you use as a base for crafting though. The Blademaxx (the machete) is just a skin, but it looks really good. The stats of whatever weapon you use to craft it will remain the same, it just looks cool now. I haven't used the outfit or the buggy skin yet so I can't speak on those.\t\t\t\t\t
