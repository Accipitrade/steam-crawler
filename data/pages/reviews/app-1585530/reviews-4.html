What does this mean for this game?
Well, it means that
  • Ammo belts are not actually physics based, and grabbing one end of a belt from an ammo box will snap the belt onto the gun and load the gun similar to Pavlov's reload system
  • Revolvers and breach shotguns don't actually physically load into a cylinder or tube. In fact, the shells disappear from the tube as you shoot the gun.
-Game advertises itself as simulating the human hands fully physically, but if you try and stick your finger into a hole on the gun (Like the trigger), your "finger" pushes the gun away or glitches it and sends it launching across the map because your hands are actually a lazy box collider, which is obviously not how a hand obviously works.

The somewhat low quality textures in the game, ugly character models, and limited AI intelligence which can be easily seen with the AI WALKING BACKWARDS TO THE PLAYER IN A STRAIGHT LINE heavily hints to me that this is going to be a Quest game first before PC.
