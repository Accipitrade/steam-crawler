\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've... seen things with this class... I only bought her for a Sand Manipulator class branch, but after seeing some reviews on her, saying she is ♥♥♥♥♥ and has no damage, i would dissagre with that. So, decided to give my thought on her. And yes, im new to the game, don't judge... And if i'm wrong in some ways, please correct me. Thanks in advance!

Both class branches are interesting in some ways:

Sand Manipulator

it's... Pretty fun.
For a solo playthrough to the end.

1) First Ability (Call For War) Sends a big pile of sand to where is your cursor pointed, forming a sand statue to aid you in battle dealing melee damage, but they can't move until you use the ability again, summoning more statues. They disappear over time. For well with a second ability.
2) Second Ability (Cirle of Guardians) Summons a circle of Sand Statues around Illusionist. It works great with first ability, as you can send summon them, and use the first ability to send them on the group of enemies and dealing a really great damage to 'em.
3) Third Ability (Link of Sand) As more levels you get, the statues get more stronger. The third ability, alows Illusionist quickly pulling herself to one of the statues, dealing a big slashing amount of damage, and reducing incoming damage with a passive ability (Spirit Link).
4) Fourth Ability (Combat Order) Much like the third ability, this one doesn't require you to pull to the statues, instead, again, you order your statues to hit a big pile of enemies, killing them in an instant. Depends on the statue damage.
Really fun and balanced, but low survival.

Now... lets get to the weird and funny stuff.

Chronomancer.
It's a damn curse, and a blessing for short.

The start is boring:
1) First ability (Age Proliferation) I think it's bugged, since it's useless. Slowing enemies down and dealing almost no damage to them even over time. Well, she can heal herself all the time with her first ability, and boosting defence, elemental damage and dodging chance for her comrades, but still useless.
2) Second ability (Time Deceleration) is slowing enemies down for a few seconds.
3) Third ability (Dimensional Displacement) is dodging to reduce damage income for a few seconds.Starting as a Chronomancer is boring...
...until level 48 .
4) Fourth ability (Temporal Heroes) Summons a Hero. A summoned hero can be either Pyromancer, Viking or Marksman. With level 545 or more. Here is a catch tho. See... Summoned hero is either broken too, or they just complete given up on fixing this class after a month, but he deals more than 600k damage per second. The Summoned Hero is so OP, that can kill bosses in a nanosecond.

I went with Sand Manipulator, and then after level 48 with Chronomancer just for fun. Great for speedrunning i guess.\t\t\t\t\t
