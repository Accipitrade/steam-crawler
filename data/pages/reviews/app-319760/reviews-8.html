\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaving recently begun to play this game, the number one piece of advice I can give you is not to judge it too soon. When I first opened up this title I saw some rough edges and a few spelling errors here and there. Plus, it was so inexpensive that I was nervous about that feature as well. However...

As the game progresses you start to enjoy the characters a bit more and their conversations. The challenge does increase and the music...my god the music is brilliant. For the low price I paid, I seriously cannot complain.

That said, here are my only two complaints :)

- There is a level editor achievement that is literally impossible to complete because the feature was never built
- The higher resolutions seem to just stretch the game, instead of increasing it's detail. The art would look much better if I could use a larger portion of my monitor.

Still, the title is worth it if you like sarcastic evil or small-scale TD games. Enjoy!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttl;dr :
Don't buy this game, except you are bored af, you like bugs/the dumbest AI ever and if you can get it under 2€.

So, why do I think Oh My Gore! is a bad game?
Let me ask you something first... Do you like cake? If yes, then think about a random person who tries to sell you a f*cking stone which is coated in cake glaze. Yes, this is the game!

Now, don't understand me wrong, the game realy tries to be a good tower defence game. The textures are pretty good, you have 6 different characters and everyone has a different ability, this game has a story and most of it is, at least in german, voice-overed, so, what could go wrong?

Here is a small list why this game is not it's money worth:

  • The storyline is very boring and flat
  • The german voiceacting is not good, especially of the wargod Raelius
  • Very unbalanced towers: You can complete most of the maps with 1-2 fire/gatling towers on hard
  • Unbalanced units: I completed every map, on hard-mode, only with minotaur-units
  • Every single ability is rather useless
  • The game is very repetitive and it feels like work after 10 minutes (almost every mission is the same)
  • Dumbest AI i've ever seen (in a f*cking tower defence game, how?!), most unit's change whole lanes to kill a enemy unit or a tower and after they killed the tower/unit, then they walk the whole way back which can be very time consuming...
  • You can't 100% this game because the developers thought it is a good idea to implement an editor achievement without an editor...
  • The ''3 challenging difficulties'' adjusts only the stats of minions and even if you play on the hardest difficulty, you just have to place 1 gattling/fire tower and it will eat 90% of all enemy units away...
  • The Skillsystem is just a bad joke which has not a big impact on the game
  • There are several small bugs with your and enemy units (Example: there is a chance that every unit, which changed the lane to attack a tower/unit, will get stuck in a loop where the affected units walk up and down until it get's attacked)
  • It seems like the developers abandoned the game (they wanted to add the level editor a whole time ago...)

So, in my opinion this game is barely 2€ or even 5€ worth, you will have more fun with a free tower defence game on your browser than with this sh*t... Just spend these 2€ on a better game or on a prost*tute.

(This is my first review and not in my native language, so please don't kill me)

Edit: Just to be sure, most of my review is just satiric and what I really love about this game is the artstyle but I just can't give this game a good rating, at least in its current state. And don't ask me why this game has such a good like/dislike ratio.\t\t\t\t\t
