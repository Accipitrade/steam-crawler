\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a such low price I didn't expect much. What I found is an interesting Tower Defense game where you also create own units to attack enemy towers so basically a combination of Tower Defense and Tower Offense.

There is 6 different generals to choose (tho not on every possible level) affecting what one spell and what units you can use on selected mission.

There are 20 very unique levels (sometimes you only defend, sometimes only attack, sometimes do both, sometimes you have even different objective)
and quite a lot of different towers and traps to chose.
Every few seconds you get money and food that you use to spend (money on towers/traps, food on units). On some levels you dont even get food or money due specific objective.

Also you gather experience points at the end of every level (you can replay same level for easy exp farming if you want) to upgrade your skill tree and you can read ton of humerous text about hard life of goofy Evil Overlord.
Story is kinda meh and cliche yet charming and funny.

Definitely recommended!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn unusual TD game.

I didn't want to buy this game for a long time. And I bought it last week. I can't say I was excited, but I found some cool features there.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1398189289
First of all, I liked the setting of being a bad guy. Skeletons, Orcs, Mummies, Harpies and etc.
You are bad guy who fights with good guys. There is a lot of humor and sarcasm in the story. Yeah, there is a story.

What about gameplay. Most of the missions are Attack&Defense. And it's fresh. You have one road where enemies attack you and you need to build towers. There is another, you attack. So, there are 2 resources, food for sending units and gold for building towers. There are also Pure TD missions, enemies come from all sides and you just defend.

There are 6 heroes. They stand at the end of the road and fight with leaked enemies. Each has unique attack unit and a spell. Also they are all affected in the story.

There is a very humble skill tree. Just 12 upgrades with 1-4 levels. (+5% damage, health and etc).

The main flaw of this game is graphics. The design is good, but graphics. I can't see it, it makes me suffer. Different resolutions, amount of frames and etc.... arghhh.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1398189266

Pros
- Attack&Defense TD game. Attacking is interesting.
- Six different commanders with special bonuses
- There is a story with nice texts

Cons
- Awful graphics with a good design
- Big numbers for everything. Hard to calculate in mind
- 1 unit can destroy all your defenses

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1398189496

Resume
I have mixed feelings. There are good gameplay features and bad graphics. So, if you can survive this art, i think you will like this game.\t\t\t\t\t
