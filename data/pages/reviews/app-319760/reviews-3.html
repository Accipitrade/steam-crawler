\r\n\t\t\t\t\t'Oh My Gore!' looks like it could've been a very interesting hidden gem. However, when playing it I quickly noticed its poor execution and horrible balance.

* Enemy units constantly get stronger as time passes, forcing you to rush the enemy or else stand no chance later. Because this issue will quickly lead to literally undefeatable enemies.
* A lot of units can walk huge distances off their given paths to attack their targets. This allows enemies to basically skip terrain entirely.
* The AI and pathfinding are very bad. It's really frustrating watching your units backtrack back up the path every time they kill an enemy.
* Traps are worthless. They expire very quickly, don't do anything to most enemies, and even affect your own units as well.
* I believe the layout and allocated build space in a lot of maps could've been designed better.
* The UI is really clunky and slow to respond, and the audio is often delayed. At least in my experience.
* Oh My Gore's writing, dialogue and voice acting are really bad in my opinion. It often made me cringe.

These issues make it nearly impossible to enjoy this game, and they need to be fixed. Not that I expect that to happen since the developers have seemingly abandoned this game. So all in all, this game more than deserves a negative review in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
