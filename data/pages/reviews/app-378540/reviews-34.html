\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrustrating is the one word I'd use to describe this game, for two reasons.
1. Dark Souls gave me a lot of moments where I turn the game off because I was frustrated at myself for making silly mistakes. I turn this game off because I'm frustrated at the game itself- the majority of my deaths in this game have been from falling off ledges that are poorly lit and badly telegraphed. Dying to enemies that killed me because I wasn't playing smart enough is a fun challenge- dying to cheap tricks and poor design is not.
2. I do not want to hear the same sad, boring country song every time I go back to the med-bay. I was sick of it the first time I heard it, I was looking up mods to remove it by the third time I heard it. Double goes for the supremely annoying Don Hackett character- everything about his voice and his appearance make me wish that he was real just so that I could punch him in the face.\t\t\t\t\t
