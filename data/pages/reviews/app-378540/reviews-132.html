\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA strong contender in the souls-like sub genre of games. It takes the FROM-soft formula and adds a lot of new and unique mechanics on top of that, though doesn't always do the best job of teaching how the new systems work. Combat is more interesting with a mix of limb severing, timed blocking, dodges (movement as well as ducking or jumping over attacks), counter attacks, and a basic combo system. Regular enemies are a challenge through out most of the game, often times more so than bosses, which to me were a weaker aspect of the game. That said, looking for the "hard" way to beat a boss and get a unique weapon does given them depth, shame there is a very small number of them to actually fight. I enjoyed the level design, how every area only has one Ops Center (bonfire / hub point / etc) and everything wraps around to it via shortcuts and good level design. Though the second region did look a bit samey, the other regions looked good and had enjoyable (to explore and look at) level designs.

Definitely a niche game, but leaps and bounds better than the devs previous attempt at a souls-like. I wouldn't mind future games having multiplayer (coop / pvp invasions), but this is still a good single player experience and I look forward to what the devs come up with next, be it more content for this game or a new title with similar mechanics.

Good game, though not for everyone.\t\t\t\t\t
