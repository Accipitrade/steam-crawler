\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a pleasant surprise this game turned out to be. I am not even a huge fan of "souls-like" games, and had pretty much lost interest in hack and slash type games but I loved this one and am looking forward to playing number 2. What I liked the most would have to be the aesthetic, a near future/ industrial vibe which was also clearly influenced by the movie Elysium. The gameplay is like a chess game rather than just running in and mashing buttons and swinging like a madman, you have to take your time and make sure you get good at timing, blocking and dodging.
