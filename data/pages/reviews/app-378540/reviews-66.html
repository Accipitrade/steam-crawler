\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have really enjoyed my time with this game.
i've only played the base game, so any dlc is irrelevent to my feelings.
The Surge is a 'dark souls'-esque game, it can be difficult at first, but once you get used to the rules of the game, it gets more enjoyable. The first time I played this game, i had a tough time with it. it was unforgiving, but once watched a playthrough, it became a lot easier and fun. although some might say this is not a good way to play it, i found it to be the best way for myself. it isnt a sandbox game. there is backtracking, which is fun to go back to older areas but gaining access to places that was once blocked off.
the setting can be seen as "very bland and un-changing" but i don't mind that because the game takes place in the same place, although different parts of the complex.
the story is messed up in a good way. i enjoyed the twists, turns and heartbreaks in the story and i can't wait to play the dlcs and the sequel.
With my time with the game, it has gone from a game that i wanted to really like but had a hard time getting into it, to one of my favorite games of all times.

TLDR; The surge is a more forgiving version of the Souls series. A fun game with rewarding gameplay. very replayable\t\t\t\t\t
