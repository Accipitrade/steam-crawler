\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSince The Surge is part of the "souls" genre I have seen many reviews unfairly compare this to the Dark Souls games. While it does have some similarities on the way the game is set out it's a very different beast entirely. Like with my review of Nioh I will let you know what is similar to Dark Souls (for people looking for a similar experience) then the pro's and con's of the game.

Similar to Dark Souls:
- There is a challenging difficulty in which the learning curve is high that is overcome through trial and error and patience
- Tech is this games version of souls and acts as levelling and currency
- Bosses are a headache and will keep you trying until you get them down
- If you die you lose your tech and will have to get it back before you die again
- The story is explained in little hints until you can fit the puzzle together (but it's still pretty vague)

PRO
- Lost tech will expire if you do not get to it fast enough or if you die which adds a new level of haste
- To aquire certain parts and equipment you need to cut off certain enemy limbs in stylish fashion and then upgrade it at stations. There are many pieces of equipment to collect and even more items to use to upgrade.
- Various robotic and undead enemies to fight all with their own move sets and equipment
- While there are only five bosses (not including mini bosses) they still feel well paced and are all different
- The Creo facility is great to explore, really feels like a high tech factory and looks amazing
- It's very creepy and eerie in moments especially when listening to audio logs and messages
- New game + with (I believe) two different endings and many side missions to do and weapons to collect
- There are a few pieces of free and paid ofr DLC, two of which are story related and take place in new areas of the Creo Facility, a theme park and wild west area.
- While your 'core' level doesnt' affect stats you gain lots of implants which do various things from giving you health injectables, increasing maximum health and stamina, allowing you to see enemy life and stamina bars, gaining more tech from kills and many more.

NEUTRAL
- I see people complaining of no multiplayer features. Personally I don't mind that as I don't think it would bring much to it and keeps the difficulty high.
- Sometimes you will be in areas that look the same and get lost easily. When you're hunting for your tech that will expire soon it gets frustrating.
- The main character Warren is pretty much a blank slate. You don't create a character or customise him in any way. He's just a mostly silent 30 year old man with short brown hair. Game designers love them.

CON
- Sometimes the same enemies can go from really easy to hard as balls with seemingly no reason. I get that it's based on skill but fighting 5 enemies in a row with no problem only for the 6th to kick your ass in splendid fashion really gets annoying.
- Stealth doesn't really exist in this game. Sure you can sneak past enemies but if you try to attack them unaware they will hear you as soon as you lift your weapon an inch which will cause them to dodge or attack you first.
- The Big Sister 1/3 boss is the worst as the first two phases are totally fine but the third changes the camera angle and is the only time in the game in which it does causing you to be disoriented and confused fighting against the controls.
- The most important thing to do in this game is to DISABLE THE AUTO LOCK! It will get you killed so many times as it automatically targets enemies for you when one is dead. During boss fights (especially Big sister 1/3) or groups of enemies it will get you killed.

Like with all souls like games, the enjoyment is sometimes blurred with frustration and the forced patience at the difficulty. Even though there were times I had to leave the game alone for a while I really enjoyed this and can't wait for the two DLC's (not played yet) and The Surge 2 which was announced not so long ago.
If you're a fan of games like Dark Souls, Nioh, Bloodborne, then give this a try. It's not the same so don't go thinking it's going to be exactly like them, it's very different but still great.
8/10\t\t\t\t\t
