Well optimized, graphics are pretty good, runs on a lot of old machines. Fun and engaging ways to tackle your build. Works amazing on 21:9 monitors, thanks devs! Lots of configurations as well.

Overall, only buy it at a sale. I had fun but it's not something I see recommending over another souls game. 6/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Surge is an overall positive, if uneven, experience that manages to be a good time despite not keeping up with its more excellent competition. Still, it is decidedly above average in the genre, so worth checking out if you need to scratch an itch.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

It has its fair share of frustration and jank, but I found it to be a generally compelling game, and I had a lot of fun playing it. The boss fights are easily the worst parts of the experience, but there aren't very many of them, fortunately, and the game ends on a high note with a solid final encounter. Overall, the game largely plays to its strengths with its exploration and map design, and I had a lot of fun just wandering around finding stuff, although I, uh, did get lost a few times.

I wouldn't recommend this for a first foray into a Soulsborne game; I do think having experience with the genre will even out the experience for you, and will help you find the fun faster past some of the rougher edges.\t\t\t\t\t
