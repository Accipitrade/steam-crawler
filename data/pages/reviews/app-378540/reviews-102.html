A Solid Souls-Like With A Sci-Fi / Industrial Theme

You start of with the most basicest of the basic character basic designs. Warren. There is no character customisation.

Lots of weapon and armour variety will have you cutting every single limb from the same humanoid enemies you'll be fighting throughout the game. You can mix and match different armour types though equipping a full armour set will give set bonuses, some complete trash, some OP as hell.

Bosses and NPC are okay but often forgettable (I don't even remember their names).

Controls are great except for the duck/hop mechanic which I've used rarely.

Enemies hit HARD, though there are a lot of different healing options from different injectables to different implants.

Level design is great with numerous shortcuts although most locations look the same. The DLC offers 2 very different locations in the game and is a must play.

Overall, a great Sci-Fi/Industrial take on Souls-like and a very good one. Can't wait for The Surge 2.\t\t\t\t\t
