\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor the first few hours, I really didn't get it, but then I realized that's all there is. I can see the niche that would like it, but it really isn't for me.

I was expecting a first person melee RPG where you build and craft and upgrade your gear with stuff you get off the bad guys. I guess that's technically there, and they system they use is good but I can't emphasize enough that Surge is a rouge like version of that.

I didn't find the melee system very fun and I guess that's my problem with this game. There aren't really combos, or abilities, All I'm doing here is left mouse button, arrow keys, space bar. Where the complexity and challenge comes in is the timing of how you dodge. I kind of found that's all I can see in this game. Meanwhile, dying is a huge thing. The levels are super small so they have to be super difficult to progress so a lot of enemies kill you in 3 or 4 hits, and we're talking about a half second window to successfully dodge attacks. What I've been doing so far is go out from the spawn point, kill 3 enemies, return to spawn point to upgrade or stash my resources. When entering a new area, the enemies are so far over your character level, you're getting killed in 2-3 hits (ready 1.5 seconds of not hitting dodge properly). Then over 10 repetitions of killing the same unit, I'll start to explore a little further in the level to kill some new units. Ever return to the spawn point respawns everything in the level too.

If you get off on mastering the perfect dodge, and pwning the enemies in a melee RPG without taking a hit, this is your jam! Surge is a rouge like of That.

I was expecting to move through a LOT more terrain and plot development by now. My character is level 40 and in the plot, my character has woken up, clearly everyone has gone insane without explanation and I've encountered a NPC who's sending me a transmission of "I can't explain, you have to make it to me".- Plot development isn't a thing.


I think without enemy respawn and without getting stun locked and getting killed, What's taken me 10 hrs would otherwise have taken me 30 mins. As you play the game and you get 10 mins in, heads up that's pretty much what your working with. There really hasn't been anything added after the first 10 mins for me.\t\t\t\t\t
