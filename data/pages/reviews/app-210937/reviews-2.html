\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA brand new playable character, that's great?
Well, It's basically just a new skin to play with.
Pretty much a simple cosmetic for extra cash.
I usually just hate cosmetic DLC's like this one.
They truly won't increase the overall playtime.
And therefore, It's kind of pointless to have it.
Sure, It makes you look a bit cooler, whatever.
Unless you are rich, you really don't need this.
Why did I get this? To support the developers.
Killing floor has received a ton of free content.
There's a lot of free events and great updates.
And I think they do deserve that extra money.
Yeah, cosmetics are a very lazy way to earn it.
But supporting those developers is important.
With that being said: I give a negative review.
And why? Because I simply dislike cosmetics.
I can't recommend something I didn't enjoy.
As I said: I bought this DLC to support them.
But that doesn't mean I have to like the DLC.
There's a ton of free characters to play with.
Purchasing a bonus one seems a bit boring.
You really won't gain extra playtime from it.
It's a way to give 'more' money to the devs.
But in the end, It's just a skin. Nothing else.\t\t\t\t\t
