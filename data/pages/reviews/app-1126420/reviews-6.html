\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWas lucky enough to be chosen for the Beta release and loved it from the moment I picked up the controller. Music, sound effects, weapons, levels and graphics are all top notch.

I was NEVER really a scroller, shooter kinda game guy, but the graphics and game play drew me in and once I discovered the various weapons that pulled me in further and then the level difficulty (I started on hard) pulled me ALL THE WAY in and I was determined to beat it.

It's hard enough to be challenging and almost too hard at times, but figuring out the right weapon to use at the various points is KEY and even then it doesn't become easy.

Looking forward to playing the full game and seeing what the other levels have in store.

Kudos to the the DEV on an excellent release. Something to be proud of for sure.

PS. if there was one negative, I'd have to agree it's the voice over, it's NOT enough to be distracting, it just doesn't complement the rest of the game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow, my hands are sweating :D I just made it to the third stage and although normally it is too a hard game for me (anyone remembering R-Type?) I just have to keep on playing.

The art is really really outstanding, very "alien like" and surprises me with new ideas every inch I manage to dive deeper in each level. This is not tile based and you won't feel any kind of recycling, it is - befitting to the setting - very organic!

The soundtrack is also very driving and keeps pushing me forward. There are a lot of different weapons and attachments with great particle animations but the good thing is, I never loose track of my ship in all the visual bombast.

The controls by keyboard are very simple to grasp and aiming with the mouse if very helpful. It has a nice learning curve, where I thought at the start of level 2 I would never get past the first 20%, I learned the new types of enemies (plenty!) and how to react to them.

All in all it is a great shmup not only for retro fans; it is a solid reimagination of a modern shmup while keeping all the things that made them great. I don't know any other game like this out there at the moment and can recommend it very much.

Btw. if you manage to get very far in the game you even get an "achievement" with real life value: a 3D printable art from the game! Guess I have to go back in :D\t\t\t\t\t
