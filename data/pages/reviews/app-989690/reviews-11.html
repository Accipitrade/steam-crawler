\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA long term fan of games like Command & Conquer, Age of Empires or Warcraft I decided to pick the game up during a sale. But I can't get into it. For one, there seems to be no real tutorial. I was left to find out myself what each building would do, which left me reading the descriptions of the buildings while the clock was ticking. There are too many different resources and too little time to actually build a decent supply base that supports your build-up. The games I played so far were basically always just the same: Spam Hovels and Hunter shacks and add a logging camp somehwre for wood, then just spam units as many as you can. Never mind unlocking research, there is not enough time or resources to even use that. Building advanced buildings with enemy waves appearing so fast one after the other is just a gold sink that will not yield any rewards since there is so little time. Just build units, and the support buildings for the units. That way you survive and "win" although the last wave or two will usually trash parts of the base. "Victory!" - except to me it doesn't feel like it when I celebrate over the ruins of my base which wold not survive another wave.
It just doesn't feel satisfying or entertaining although technically the game is solid.\t\t\t\t\t
