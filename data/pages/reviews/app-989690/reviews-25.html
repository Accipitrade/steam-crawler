\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIve seen the game being called a They are Billions ripoff, and while Im not certain where "ripoff" starts and "same genre" ends, Im having a good time with it. It is, however, definitely a pretty similar game, which in my opinion doesnt have to be a bad thing, Id like to see more of these!

BIGGEST CONS:
- on a pricing level the game is pretty greedy with 30 bucks upfront and on top of that day 1 DLC and I do think it asks too much for what is there so far.
- on a technical level the thing the devs should fix as soon as they possibly can(!) is the lag while the game is paused. scrolling around the map while the game is unpaused works fine but as soon as you go into pause you start wrestling with the game for frames and given that you constantly pause and unpause you are reminded of that drastic contrast constantly.

BIGGEST PROS
While it is very similar to They Are Billions, it brings a good deal of cool ideas to the table.
(Note: I havent played TAB in over a year so forgive me if I mention something that is by now in TAB as well)
- Enemy types feel more distinct, with horseriders, giant scorpions and spiders, catapults flying batdemons, and right now Im in mission 4 and it apparently introduces bosscamps instead of only creep camps? Exciting, in general Im a sucker for games that only gradually introduce more and more features in later missions!
- Disease and fire a pretty cool additions
- The sandstorm is a neat idea
- Coop ofc, even though I personally dont care for that
- The comic is genuinely cool and you need to beat missions to unlock more chapters.

- in general it has a lot of cute ideas that allow me to give it a cautious thumbs up overall, even though Id still recommend you wait for a sale. 30€ seems pretty damn steep for the amount of content, graphical fidelity, etc that is currently provided.
I'll keep playing however, and Im not afraid to refund usually.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI stopped halfway through the first mission just to write this.

I chose this over we are billions (which I plan on picking up at some point), and I have no regerts. ;)
The combat is glorious. The graphics decent. The base building easy, but still a need to be resourceful. This game is... well... It's just good fun.

If you enjoy RTS, Defense based games, or even city builders with some action. This game is worth a look. Usually I wait for sales, but this was worth the full price of $30.

Can't wait to dive deeper, and also join in on the Co-op!

P.S.

Thanks to the Devs for giving us an RTS, as it unfortunately seems to be a dying breed. Cheers!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will start by saying that overall I DO recommend this game as a solid, fun, strategy defense title. I unfortunately will leave it with a thumbs down from the practice of locking a hero unit behind a deluxe edition upgrade. Locking skins is fine, characters not so much. This is my primary gripe I have so far in that there is very much a lack of hero variety. Conan and Valeria might as well be the same character and having Kalanthes behind an additional $10 paywall is just laughable.

I think my only gripe outside of the locked hero/limited hero pool is the autosave is juuuuust enough to feel intrusive. This may be an engine issue or something else, not a major gripe, just irksome.

The game has borrowed VERY heavily from They are Billions, which I do not fault it for. The overall gameplay loop is really enjoyable, especially with the ability to play cooperatively, but the lack of hero variety and FURTHER limiting the selection pool behind a paywall on an already premium title is just...what? Absolutely can not give this a thumbs up while this remains the case.

Pick it up on sale if you enjoyed They are Billions, or are really itching for some RTS survival you can play with a friend, but otherwise I would recommend holding off until it is either on sale or they do something about locking a playable character on a premium title behind a paywall. I am contemplating a refund just on this principal. Would absolutely pick the game back up if this changes or more heroes are added to the pool with in-game unlock methods.\t\t\t\t\t
