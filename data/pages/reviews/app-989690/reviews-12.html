\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely steep learning curve at the beginning which is compounded by the lack of any real tutorials. Was difficult to the point that I almost returned it. Decided to go ahead and really try to learn it instead and the payoff was huge. The game is a blast to play. It's definitely challenging, but that makes it rewarding to play. There isn't a ton of content (factions, maps, units) but it also isn't a massively expensive game. What IS there a good amount of buildings, a somewhat complex economy (one of the sources of the difficulty), and a good variation of enemies within the waves. Full games (10+ waves) can easily take a few hours.

I'm glad I stuck it out. It's a lot of fun and I'm nowhere near done. The achievements help stretch things out even further.\t\t\t\t\t
