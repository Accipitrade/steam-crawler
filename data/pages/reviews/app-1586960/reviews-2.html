I could see the exercise instructor being potentially interested in pushing a vampire all the way to a vampire's limits after she had learned was convinced of the truth and not set off by being so strong she walks off thinking we are tricking her somehow.

Job Balance. Or maybe it is just that I would like to make more use of the Animal Shelter job. However, jobs that reduce one of your two energy stats really need something to offset that. In contrast you have some jobs that pay well and don't decrease any stat. This might be offset by energy drain, but I didn't notice a huge difference between the good jobs and the ones that seem to have less going for them.

Speaking of. It would be nice if each job didn't just list what it needs but what it affects positively and what it affects negatively. Right now it just punishes us who can't easily remember the values for each and every job off the top of our heads. Maybe if they want it to be a surprise the first time through have it unlock an option in the options menu after the first playthrough for those of us who struggle with memorizing such information.

So all in a cute and fun game with what I see as lots of potential.\t\t\t\t\t
