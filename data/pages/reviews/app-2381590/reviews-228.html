\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTerribly designed and optimized game. Character movement is way too clunky for a "getting over it" style of game, which isn't even the worst of it's issues. Too many objects/props that require double/triple mounting to get over such as a pipe elbow joint. But most importantly is the seemingly randomly placed invisible walls in paths that look like it could be a route forward, only to block you with an invisible wall which leads you into a funnel of invisible walls that brings you back to the start with no indication or chance of knowing that it was the wrong path. It punishes exploring at no fault of the player, because they were too lazy to finish out said route and just decided to block it with invisible walls and call it a day. Falling because you failed to make a jump or you tried to get cocky and take a risky route is fine, but when you fall because the player didn't develop the game and/or trial and error multiple paths until they found one that wasn't blocked by a nonsensical invisible wall, especially a path that seeming has a route, and gets blocked by something you can't see after the 3rd jump of what looks to be a path of 20+ jumps is unreasonably punishing. Either make the invisible walls visible or marked, or finish the paths.\t\t\t\t\t
