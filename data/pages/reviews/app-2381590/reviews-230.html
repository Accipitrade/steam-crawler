Finally I beat this game.
I won't recommend you guys reading this review to buy this game at full price. IMO, it's not really worth it, it's mostly just a bunch of cheap random models stacked into the freaking high upper stratosphere. It's about bad Value For Money.
TBH, I kinda had some fun playing it. I must say, I like the idea of it which plays like some custom maps of GTA5 for the first few hours. But what I can't stand is falling or getting stuck 'casue of some stupid bugs or lags or model clippings.
Anyway, it's recommended to those who are really idle and like jumping games. It's fun to watch others fall over and over then beat it but it's suffering play by yourself.

Update(2023-06-05):
I checked those latest patches, the author shows great attitude which makes me wanna give it another shot.
But a few minutes later, I found a new bug at this place. I stuck between containers and I tried every direction and action, I just cannot get out.
All right, maybe give it another shot next time.



Update(2023-05-26) recommend→not recommend:
When I reached the end of the ice scene using all medium quality graphics settings(i7-7700 & GTX1066), there's a room slightly ahead of this place with hollowed-out floor and half an orange floating in the air. Even I moved extremely carefully, I couldn't tell there's a trap because of the graphics setting which is limited by hardware. I just went straight ahead and then fell to somewhere very close to the ground. I figure the Contrast and the Saturation of that scene is too low at medium settings to make the player aware of the danger.
Afterwards I checked other players' videos, it turns out it's easier to distinguish that ice stick from nothing at higher settings, maybe the developers should modify those transparent grounds a lit bit to improve the game experience at lower graphics settings.

Also, things like the cuboid at the end of this road, their actual collision volumes are smaller than their appearance, which often leads to model clipping and distance misjudging.
Besides, idk why there're no achievements in this game.

Thx for replying, but I reckon that GTX1060 is slightly better than GTX1650, mine is mobile(Laptop) though. XD



In a word, this game is Aerial Acrobat Simulator.

We are born with only two innate fears: the fear of falling and the fear of loud sounds.
Loud creepy sounds are often used by jumpscares in most horror games. Only Up will tell you what the real horror is in the other way, it'll let you climb into the upper clouds and trick you into falling down without any checkpoints.

Btw, I love those graffiti like the Journey to the West one, those static lovely family models, the city and other scenes, and the music includes the narrator (except when I was falling D:).\t\t\t\t\t
