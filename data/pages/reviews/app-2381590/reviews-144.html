\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has it's flaws, but those flaws matter little for how this game is supposed to be played.

If you are buying this game because you watched your favorite streamer play it, then you should already know how the game plays. It is designed to be difficult and annoying, but also deeply satisfying when you break your previous record. For me, this game is A LOT more enjoying than Jump King.

I play this game every weekend with my friends and we take turns passing the controller on the couch and seeing who can get the highest. We make drinking games out of it, we don't take it seriously and we have a good time. Occasionally we hop on a discord call and use Keyboard and Mouse which is obviously easier than controller, but its really about having fun with good friends and NOT taking the game seriously.

For only being $9.99 the game is worth the price imo. If you are a loner with no one to play with, find a nobody streamer playing it and try to compete with them on either time or total height climbed, because this game gets old immediately if you just play alone and aren't the type to get intrinsic satisfaction for breaking your previous height.

Game with friends 10/10

Solo 5/10 unless you truly enjoy gaming alone and are looking for a mindless grind while you listen to music.\t\t\t\t\t

Also, the settings are not persistent, and you have to config them every time you start the game up.

Happy to turn this into a positive review once they fix the above issues.\t\t\t\t\t
