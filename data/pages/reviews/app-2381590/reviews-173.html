Where do I even begin with the abomination that is Only Up? This game is a prime example of everything that's wrong with modern gaming. From its lackluster game play to its uninspired graphics, it fails to deliver even a modicum of entertainment. Only Up? More like Only Down, because that's exactly where this game will take your gaming experience.

Let's start with the game play, or should I say lack thereof? It's as if the developers took a checklist of all the cliches and tired mechanics in the industry and threw them into a blender. Repetitive jumping, mind-fumblingly simple puzzles, and a complete absence of any innovation make for a gaming experience that feels more like a chore than a form of entertainment. It's a shame that the game's title promises an ascent, because you'll be spiraling into boredom with every passing minute.

And don't get me started on the graphics. I've seen mobile games with better visuals than this so-called "next-gen" catastrophe. The characters look like they were designed by a kindergartner armed with a box of crayons. The environments lack any semblance of detail or creativity, leaving players with a bland and uninspiring world to explore. It's clear that the developers prioritized rushing this game out the door rather than putting any effort into making it visually appealing.

To add insult to injury, the sound design is equally disappointing. Forget about immersive audio or a memorable soundtrack; Only Up seems to have opted for a soundtrack that consists solely of a repetitive loop that grates on your nerves like nails on a chalkboard. It's as if they wanted to torture players and make them regret ever putting on their headphones.

But perhaps the most infuriating aspect of Only Up is the complete disregard for player enjoyment. The game is riddled with micro transactions and loot boxes, turning what could have been a mediocre experience into a blatant cash grab. It's clear that the developers care more about lining their pockets than delivering a satisfying gaming experience. Shame on them.

In conclusion, Only Up is a perfect example of how not to make a game. Its uninspired game play, lackluster graphics, and exploitative monetization strategies make it an absolute train wreck from start to finish. Save your time and money and stay far away from this abomination masquerading as a game. You'll thank me later.\t\t\t\t\t
