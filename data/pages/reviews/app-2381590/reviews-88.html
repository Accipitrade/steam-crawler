\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn the end I would not recommend this game.
I bought this game for me and some friends because I thought it would be a fun experience.
Of the four of us, only two of us beat it (myself included). The other one that beat it did the start glitch to fly to the top after he got rainbow roaded.
Had another friend basically resort to fully dedicating to flying to the top but never managed to get it and he already gave up.
Fourth and last member gave up after losing progress to mantle bugs.

The game is incredibly janky. Sometimes you'll get air control and sometimes you won't. You can hit the same bed (bounce pad basically) and 1/5 times it sends you somewhere else or disables air movement.
Mantling is a core part of the game. Maybe half the platforms in the game are designed to require mantling, and if that fails, you just fall and can lose an hour+ of progress. I get that they made improvements to it but it still feels terrible.
In 4 hours I had countless clips of mantle bugs where I was clearly facing the correct direction, holding the correct keys, and my character would just not mantle the platform which MUST be mantled.

Ignoring the mantling bugs, if things work as intended, the game is relatively easy. The only difficulty would really be the rainbow road which going in blind is one of the most troll experiences imaginable. Scripted return to the start from about 85% of the way up.

One of the actual nice parts about this game was that there were multiple ways up certain areas... until later on when you only have one path up. This defeated the whole idea of finding your own way up. Assuming you don't run into any bugs it just becomes terribly straightforward of normal jumping with mid-air control to land in the right spot.

Assets are all generic free / bundle assets. A bunch of assets were removed because they were stolen.
Voice acting so bad they must have hired a 1star shop from Fiverr. Or promised some exposure for free voice acting.
The commentary of getting over it was much better. In Only Up it's just random and makes no sense most of the time.

Overall, enjoy the game by watching other people play / rage at it. Not worth buying it yourself.\t\t\t\t\t
