\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe only reason this game gets NEGATIVE reviews is because they can't beat it. Nowadays everyone is so used to play games that are easy with infinite restarts and checkpoints where you continue playing from the exact spot you just failed. Giving you infinite number of tries at no cost and no worries to fail while in Only Up! you don't get that luxury. In here, if you do mess up, you might end up losing hours of progress. And if you're lucky, a quick thinker with good reaction you just might not fall all the way down.

Yes. The movement is not perfect and is a bit clunky and weird at times. For example you will go through stairs and then you won't be able to pass 1 stair step and you have to jump that one stair to keep going even though it looks like a normal staircase. Yes sometimes if you don't jump perfectly and not touch the hitbox from its right angle it will not grab and you will fall. But to me, this makes the game what it is. It makes it difficult and keeps you focused. If it was too smooth and every jump would just connect then this game would lose its sweetness. It's a 10$ game. Don't expect it to be too perfect. I like it because it is the way it is. Its flaws make it a good thing.

Only Up! gives you an experience you'll never forget.

It's Happy. It's Sad. It's Scary.

It's happy: with objects like: Ice Cream, Strawberries, Macrons, Oreo's, Skateboards, a Tetris and MANY MORE! I won't write them all as there's hundreds. But i really have to mention the unexpected Turkish SAZ instrument at the end and the Tuong ot spiracha Chilly Sauce (My Favorite) was very surprising and exciting to see in game. Thank you for that. Oh hey.. Did i mention there's a condom in the sky?

It's Sad: with the story and his father. And along the way you will be told to go back. You will fail. You won't make it. You're a weak kid. And once you're back in the beginning. After being so high up in the sky. You will see even a smaller kid than you looking up in the sky dreaming about going there while you just came back.

It's Scary: because you have high adrenaline while playing and you have the fear of not falling because if you fall you might have to do everything all over again! There is this part where your screen gets black & white and you see creepy stuff with creepy music and sounds on the background. It was really creepy.

I loved this game. It will really test your patience and your skills. It has shortcuts and is perfect for Speed Running as well. You can try to play as safely as you can. Or you can run and make long risky jumps to get through objects faster and skip some content to finish the game faster. Only Up! Is an amazing game and it has really cool game developers. Their vision is just beautiful.

I wish there was Controller Support :( . I'm looking forward to Night Mode and Multiplayer! Multiplayer is gonna be AMAZING!!!

Believing in yourself is half the battle. You fell? GO AGAIN! You made it once you'll make it again!\t\t\t\t\t
