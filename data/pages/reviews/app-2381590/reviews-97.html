\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA frustratingly aggravating game. Poorly designed in this way. (But Wemod has a trainer for it!!! Slow down falls. Increase jump height etc.)
1 - It claims you are defaulted to "Epic" settings yet the graphics are VERY BAD. I have a ASUS Tuf laptop and have excellent hardware. It's not MY laptop. Other reviews said to downgrade the graphics settings and they were right. I set most things on High or Med and it looked better. EPIC settings are pointless.
2 - Your character doesn't run straight...ever. I was constantly falling because even if I lined up the pathway or pipes or ramps directly in front of me, my character ran at an angle to the right. You have to constantly angle your path off center. Don't try to jump in one direction directly straight. It won't. It'll go forward-right.
3 - There's no plot. No reason that everyone else is frozen in place. Like, am I supposed to go up and stop some evil machine from freezing time and space? There's some sort of dialogue as you progress but without subtitles, I can barely hear it (even with volume up). I had to change it to almost full 100% volume to hear the voice. Most of the time it's just ramblings.
4 - NO SAVES - They claim it's "coming soon"?
5 - What's the point of a useless elevator? Trolling?
6 - No rewards. No checkpoints. No reason to climb if you'll fall all the way back down. And you don't fall to a lower level or ledges. You fall all the way.
Update after finish:
There are NO REWARDS. After getting thru 2/3 of the game i realized there are things you can collect. When I got to the "winner" false ending, I grabbed the crown. That was the first time I realized there were things to grab like a rose, a ball, a egg and more. You get no rewards for collecting them. Look for floating things or items that look like collectibles but don't kill yourself to get them. It's pointless.
Multiple times I was close enough to easily grab a edge and pull myself up and I still fell to a lower level. Luckily I was using Wemod cheats for "slow fall" and I could angle to a much closer level. I then used level 4X super jump (Wemod) to make my way back up faster.
When you finish, nothing happens to all the frozen people below. It's like you're in this trip for yourself and no one below matters. I found ONE dog that was animated on a train platform.
(After the factory area) DO NOT GET ON THE PLATFORM THAT LOOKS LIKE A STEEL GRATE and IT STARTS TO FLY UP LIKE A DRONE WHEN YOU GET ON IT, IT TAKES YOU BACK to a lower level (back to the factory) and doesn't bring you back up. YOU HAVE TO CLIMB BACK UP. Superjump cheats helped there.
Likes:
I liked the 4 skins at the beginning. It's just a headpiece you get to choose but I felt that there should have been unique skins all over the map as you move around. Like the more you explore, the more skins you find. Like the soccer field should have pads for you to grab or a jersey. A car area could have a biker helmet or something car/motorcycle related and so on.
I liked the godlike themes as you went higher. Asgard, Greek, Japanese Shinto style and Samurai/Zen philosophies, Cthulhu etc.
NEEDS A LOT OF WORK.\t\t\t\t\t
