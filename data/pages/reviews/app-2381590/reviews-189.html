\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is worth $5 at most (probably more like $2.50). Took me about 4 hours to beat it. (Left the game on idle since there isn't a save option.) It's pretty janky and uses a lot of random assets from the unity store and other games.

I think the reason why this game got so popular for streamers is because of the high-stakes game-play; it's really fun to watch streamers lose a ton of progress from one mistake. The level design itself is extremely random and abstract and the dialog is really odd. I love that you can take extremely risky shortcuts that can save a lot of time; I always tried to learn these shortcuts so whenever I fell, the trek back up was quicker.

Normally I would still give this game a thumbs up even with it's flaws, BUT the developer plastered his NFT all over the game as graffiti as well as graphics for your character model. So for this reason I don't recommend buying this game/ad for his nft.
