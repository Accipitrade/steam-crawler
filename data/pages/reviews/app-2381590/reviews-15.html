\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably an unpopular opinion, but this game isn't worth getting unless you have ULTRA patience. There is only one checkpoint in the whole game - at the 50% mark. Above that, if you fall, you can only go as high as the 50% mark. Its a fun concept to stream, but it draaaaaags a lot, especially taking the elevator back up. The game would be much better if there were checkpoints at 25%, 50%, and 75%. Expect to find yourself falling a lot, and spending a LOT of time trying to get back to where you were...which is fun for some, but like I said, requires MEGA patience (sometimes hours to regain your progress).\t\t\t\t\t
