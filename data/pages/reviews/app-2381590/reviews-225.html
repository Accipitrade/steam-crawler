\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs the name indicates, this game is basically find a way up and you can only go up. You might fall down if you are not cautious which add some challenges to it. I play it for less than two hours and finally gave up. I quite enjoyed it at first since the game itself is interesting and finding a way up is both challenging and fun.
One of the reasons I gave up: I changed my mouse sensitivity to a lower one in the setting and made sure I clicked the save button. But when I resume the game, the sensitivity remains unchanged and stays the same high sensitivity. Is this a bug or something? Has anyone encountered the same issue? I found it quite inconvenient when the sensitivity is too high and what annoys me is that I can't even change it. Every time I opens the game, the sensitivity returns to the default highest one.
Another reason is that this game has no saving button, you can't save your progress halfway and need to start from the very beginning the next time. It's quite frustrating for me not to be able to pick up from where I left of last time. So unless you spend quite some time chained to your desk play it through, you'll need to start from the beginning the next time. That's understandable some players like challenging games and the achievement it brings when you finally reach the top is very exciting. But for casual gamer like me who just want to have some fun after a day's work, starting over each time when having a minor error is frustrating.
One suggestion I would really like to propose: Perhaps this game can have two modes, casual and normal. Casual mode is more convenient and can save progress or adding some saving points in the game, allowing players to pick up where they left of last time and can enjoy the game at their own pace. Normal mode is the current mode without saving points for those who seek challenges and want to show their skill in the speed-run.
In general though, it's a good game and I would recommend it especially for those who want to have some fun and challenges at the same time. I just hope it can gets better. <3\t\t\t\t\t
