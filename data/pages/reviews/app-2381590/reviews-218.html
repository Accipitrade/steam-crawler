\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIs this game fun? Yes.
Is this game frustrating? Yes.
Is it a good challenge? Yes.

Do I recommend the game? No.

The game is plagued with inconsistencies in how the character grabs ledges, sprints, and how the character interacts with the various terrain. The game also has some serious bugs one of which where god's hand that carries you to a section of the game completely broke for me and was stuck at the next place (I fell back down to god's hand). Therefore, my game was hard locked and I would have to start all over again as this game has no checkpoints.

The game also lacks a good amount of options in the settings menu and has a forced FPS cap of 90. The "sliders" for mouse sensitivity and sound are not actually silders, they have very fixed points and if you put it between 2 points it snaps to the closest one. So you are not able to set your settings properly.

Good concept, ruined by bad development.\t\t\t\t\t
