The major pro to Only Up! is that it offers a great range of difficult and complex puzzles to navigate. What ultimately does in my recommendation is the broken grab and camera mechanics. Sometimes the player grabs onto obstacles even when you think it wont. Other times, during critical obstacles or jumps, the grab mechanic just 'breaks' and the animation fails to work correctly. Thus, 20-30+ minutes can be ruined; not by player error but straight up the game not working correctly. Until this critical piece is fixed, no one should be paying for this game.\t\t\t\t\t
