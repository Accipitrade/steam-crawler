So at least spawning where you quit the game would be great, in order to not lose hours of playtime because you are taking a break or because you just wanna do something else.
Another thing, the collectible items, like the crown or the brain or the heart, does someone know what they are for? Because i could not find anything about what they are for. Would be sad honestly if they are for nothing and do nothing at all. So if someone knows, please let me know. Other than that it would be worth a thought of adding save points there at these items. Just a suggestion though.
But anyways, great game, love it, but definitely still needs some improvements here and there in my opinion. Other than that i can recommend it if you like challenging plattformer.\t\t\t\t\t
