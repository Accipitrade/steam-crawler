\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is terrible. Yeah, there are fun aspects, the idea itself is incredible, the environment looks amazing and the controls feel pretty good, not much delay or anything like it.

BUT!!

Everything falls apart very quickly into the game when you realize how poor it's made. I'm just gonna explain what I experienced bad that made me to say a big NO NO to this game.

The same model of an object you're jumping on can feel different every time. Sometimes I was able to easily climb something and on another identical object, my character just refused to climb it and I fell.
There are textures that just throw you off when you jump on them for no logical reason, you just slide on them and fall.
There are textures that have no collision at all, I fell on an object and I wanted to reposition myself under a tree, I jumped on the ground near the tree and I simply fell through it because that texture had no collision.

And many more.

I totally understand that this game should be frustrating, but there is a difference between a truly challenging game (this game is actually not hard if you are patient and you focus enough, I finished it in 4 hours) and a game sold under the "challenging" umbrella, but it's just made so poor, that it becomes challenging.
I do not mind challenging games, but I want to be punished only when I make mistakes. I do not consider normal to be punished for an event where I literally had no choice.

If you are truly a masochist, you can go on and play this game, it's gonna make you question your life choices, but sadly, not because it's challenging, but because it's bad.

GL climbing!\t\t\t\t\t
