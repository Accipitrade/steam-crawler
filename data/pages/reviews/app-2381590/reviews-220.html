\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOnly played this after June 14 patch. Some people say it makes the game more responsive, others that it makes the game too easy. Personally I had fun. The game isn't as difficult as Getting Over It or Jump King, but it is also considerably longer, giving you more room to screw up and fall. It took me exactly 3 hours to beat my first climb, but that was with only one fall that cost me about 15 minutes, and I feel there's plenty of other things to find while exploring, so I'm probably gonna play it again.

As for the bad, the game feels a bit rough around the edges, trampolines can throw you in hard to predict directions, causing you to fall, and few times I got temporarily stuck on geometry. Voice acting is pretty bad as well, but I guess it's suppose to be parody or something. Also with the game being as long as it is, it's really unfortunate that there's no save on exit function.\t\t\t\t\t
