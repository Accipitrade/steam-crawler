\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst time writing a Steam review.

Don't know how many people are going to actually read this review, but I'll try to explain.

I hated the game at first, because of the super clunky physics, movement, and hit-box. I swear there were so many unfair falls that I shouldn't have fell. But I just wanted to beat this damn annoying game once in for all, and I did. To be honest, I consider this game as testing your patience more, rather than testing your skill, like Getting over it or Jump King. I think if you don't give up, you are most likely going to beat the game no matter what. It just takes a long, LONG time to.

I also really liked the world view of the game. This dystopian city and a little bit of dark backstory were really interesting. Maybe it's because I commonly like B-games such as Getting over it and Sexy Hiking.

I know this sounds exaggerated and ridiculous, but maybe beating this game kind of encouraged me to never give up, even though it might seem impossible, just like when I looked up and saw the endless-looking way to go.

I'd maybe recommend this game if you legitimately want to beat the game, but not really if you just want to casually run around without the intention of beating it. I think the true satisfaction and charm of this game hides behind after beating it, or else it will just bring you frustration.

I'm sorry if my English was weird, English is not my first language. Thanks for reading this long random ass dude's speech. Hope you can beat the game if you get it.\t\t\t\t\t
