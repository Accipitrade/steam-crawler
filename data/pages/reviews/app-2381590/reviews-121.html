It is true that it has bugs, for example:

-Delay in recognizing the jumps.
-Delay when recognizing that you hold on to objects.
-At times you jumped on an obstacle and you went under the object and ended up at the beginning "Which frustrates a lot."

The positive:

-Good soundtrack "Little, but there was".
-Good graphics.
-Spectacular amount of levels and each one different "Obviously".
-Virtually the whole game, very well detailed.

Important note:

-Do not get carried away by negative reviews, from people who for the sole reason that they are not able to advance or finish it, because it is already a bad game and it is not like that. Give it a chance you won't regret it.

And something else "It does not have Check-Points, nor manual saving", so if you want to finish it, you will have to play continuously or leave your pc on for good hours while you sleep and then continue.

Rating 9/10 (-1 point for certain bugs).\t\t\t\t\t
