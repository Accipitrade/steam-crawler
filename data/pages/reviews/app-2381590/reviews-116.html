\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOnly Up! is an interesting title. I have a bit of patience, and as a streamer I do enjoy games that are fun when I fail at them. The platforming isn't bad, and it does what it sets out to do well. I have not played Getting Over It, but from my understanding this is essentially a very similar 3D platforming version of that, though the "philosophical" aspect of it is genuinely laughter inducing. The translation is bad, and truly I wish I could just mute the main character.

I would give this game a positive review based on gameplay alone, however one thing that should be noted is that this game advertises Goblintown NFTs very heavily. It uses the art, along with what must be other AI generated assets. The main character wears Goblintown merch, and the art is plastered in bizarre places throughout the game. I did not entirely know this until after my time spend playing the game.

As such, due to its use of Goblintown assets, I cannot recommend you spend money on this title.\t\t\t\t\t
