Sound 2/10:
I have heard a couple short loops in this game that are so few and far between you forget they exist. They are also short loops and not particularly great.

Story 1/10:
Nothing about the writing has quality. It reads like a meme but isn't funny. Crude and seemingly added as an afterthought.

Voice 1/10:
It's literally text to speech reading the story. It would be better as subtitles, but that's not an option.

Gameplay 4/10:
This is the single redeeming quality. The platforming is far from the worst I've seen. It still gets a poor score however because mantling objects barely works and sometimes you'll fall when you expect it to grab a ledge.

Graphics 2/10:
Between the terrible godrays, awful textures, crude mapping, and the fact that graphics are swapped from other games in places says there's nothing of value here.
