\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe content this dlc offers in my opinion is not worth the 10$ alone as most of it can probably just be found on google images or through youtube and doesnt actually offer any more gameplay content which i would love. However i feel they could charge upwards of 10$ for the base game itself and i feel they should be making more profit for this in depth and well made game so i decided to support it by buying the dlc. As an added bonus I found the devs notes pretty cool.

My review of the base game below:
V


I went into his game expecting it to be another semi-competently written dating sim with a cliché plot twist that people only enjoyed because they like cute anime girls and they like feeling special by having 4 girls unrealistically obsess over you in completely absurd and unrealistic ways. To be quite honest I hate dating sims and games like Honeypop or whatever and the only reason I even downloaded this game was because I was bored out of my mind and somehow it seemed to keep finding its way on my Instagram feed. So, I downloaded it.

After even the first 5 minutes I could tell this game had some quality storytelling and wasn’t just some terrible English translation that the devs whipped up in google translate in under 3 minutes for more downloads. Saying this though I have absolutely no experience with games like this and therefore cannot compare it to others but from what I can tell this is truly unique. Something about this game caught me though and I continued playing even though even the first hour wasn’t exhilarating to say the least something kept me going.

Now I don’t want to spoil anything but after what happened to Sayori my heart dropped. I realized I actually genuinely cared about the fate of these characters in a way I have never felt for any other game or movie and after that I think I spent the next 10 minutes just staring at my blank screen. After that I realized I just spent the past 4 hours playing and it was already 3 in the morning. The next day I couldn’t wait to continue the story and completed the good ending and any other ending I could find out how to do. This game isn’t by any means long or hard but it will require you to follow guides and can get repetitive. And without really wanting to complete these alternative endings all the research necessary may be slightly discouraging. Also another thing I found was a ton of false information about how you can marry characters and prevent Sayori’s which as far as I know is not possible. Even after completing every way I could find i still feel there is more content to be found and I cannot wait to see if anyone will discover new ways of completing the story. Although the story is simple and has very basic multiple choice questions that can slightly differ the story this game has much more to it than just that as editing game files like deleting a character can change the game and is actually necessary for some parts. I honestly hope team salvato releases more content for this game in the form of free dlc or paid and I’m hoping they eventually release another game similar to this as this was a truly unique game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe pack is pretty decent overall. I got it mainly for the bonus soundtracks, OST, and art booklet. I must say, the extra songs you get are definitely worth it as they are seen nowhere else in the game and are very catchy, however art booklet is pretty underwhelming. It was a lot shorter than I thought it would be, sadly, but it still gave nice insight into the game's development overall. The wallpapers are pretty ok, containing very high definition pictures of each of the character's heartwarming cutscenes from the first "playthrough" before Sayori's incident, plus one wallpaper with the entire gang together with the logo. I'm not one to flaunt this sort of stuff on my desktop or phone screen so those will be mostly untouched for me.

The pack seems a bit overpriced at first glance, but considering the entire game is free, I can overlook the price tag. Just think of this as your "purchase" of the game.

If you enjoyed the game and would like to support the creators and developers, this is a nice way to spread the love and show your support for this game and their future projects to come.\t\t\t\t\t
