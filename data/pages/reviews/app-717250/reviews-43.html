\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoki Doki Literature Club was recommended to me by others as a ''Horror game''.
After one or two hours I thought ''Well, they just tricked me into playing a visual novel/dating sim''
But then it hit, hit hard. The story completely turns on you, in a very addictive and interesting way with a very dark and cruel tone .

The story itself is great, the build up is done perfectly and everything after that.. I can't put it to words.
I thought this game would be three hours of semi-fun, but I now have 13 hours or so on it and it was all a blast.
From the humor in the dialog, with the interesting and eye candy charachters (waifu material is presence in the game) to the great music, with great art and most important of all a very interesting and intriguing story line that sucks you right up in it and doesn't let you go.

After playing this game for this long, and having enjoyed every hour of it I've decided to buy this pack as well.
I'm kind of dissapointed that it misses one picture that I would have gladly seen in there (Spoiler at the end says which one)
But other than that, it includes a lot of other intersting stuff that is worth buying it. And it also worth supporting the creators of this game.

In short, amazing game, amazing pack.

I'm upset that it is missing the picture where you hold Natsuki pressed against the wall during the making of the cupcakes. Although, YURi #1 #1
