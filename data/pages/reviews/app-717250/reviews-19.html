\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat sold me on shelling out $10 dollars I didn't need to shell out for this Fan Pack wasn't really the game itself. I mean, trust me, the game itself is easily worth $10 dollars--I probably would have even paid $19.99 for it, to be honest. But because I COULD play the full version of this game for free--and because I'm a generally stingy person--I didn't really feel compelled to burn any extra cash on this if I didn't unlock any extra in-game content for it, despite how INCREDIBLY DESERVING Team Salvato and this game are of my hard-earned moneys. (I know. I suck).

What sold me on paying extra for this though is the amount of time and attention that went into--not only the actual game-- but the hidden secrets OUTSIDE of the game.

If you don't know what I'm talking about and YOU HAVE ALREADY COMPLETED THE GAME, please see this very interesting video by the capable Game Theorists: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=w9AWvi82uDw Whether or not the underlying titular theories in the video or correct, the stuff that other players have uncovered about the .cfr files in the character folder alone were enough to absolutely blow me away. I can't tell you how excited I am to learn that Team Salvato is creating ANOTHER game that maaaay or may not be loosely tied to the characters of DDLC.

Please, if you enjoyed this game, and if you believe in the power of games as quite possibly the pinnacle of multimedia storytelling when put in the hands of a person or persons who really care, then drop that $10 on this game. Just do it. Skip those two cups of coffee or pack a lunch to school/work instead of spending $10+ at your local deli just this one week. Because Dan Salvato and his team have truly earned it, and you can trust that the money is going to go towards something spookily magnificent.
