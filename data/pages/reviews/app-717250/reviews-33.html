\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDDLC is my first ever VN experience that I play myself, and I have to say I'm very interested in what the developer has to offer in the future.

In my experience of watching other people play popular VN, it has always been about one simple plot that is often easy to figure out. You choose options that you think fits the character, and you can be her gf or something. DDLC is also somewhat like that, but it has twists that I never thought I'd came across. The idea of this game blows me away.
It's radical, it's fun, it always keeps me on the edge of my seat. its like watching a good, horror movie where you have some control over what's going on inside of it. I have sort of gone through 3/4 of the way and there's always something shocking, sadly I got some spoilers of the ending of each characters but even knowing that, it is still awesome. Playing it by yourself is a whole different experience.

Every single image that flashes has always succeeded in scaring me. I got the shivers, I got the goosebumps, I feel the suspense even in a rather calm situation inside the game. I love that this VN has the horrors with none of the stereotypical ecchi stuff. Even the romance with a particular character inside this game melts me.

Dan Salvato et al, you guys are awesome. I hope you all have the support you need from your fans, friends and family so you can bring new things to the table soon.

Cheers!\t\t\t\t\t
