\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis pack is worth it, mostly for supporting the developers of this fantastic game, which I still love to this day.
I suggest only checking the content of the pack itself only after you've completed the game due to spoilers, but that should be obvious.

This pack includes wallpapers for your computer, as well as wallpapers for your phone, they're high quality as you'd expect and perfectly fitting for wallpapers.

This pack also includes the Soundtrack of the original game as well as 2 fan-made extras (Doki Doki by Nick Kaelar and Poems Are Forever by shoji), along with sketches, and song variants not included in the game, you can hear for example the progress between the initial idea ("Daijoubu!" and "Dreams Of Love and Literature" Early Sketches) compared to the final, ingame version which is interesting to hear.

The pack also contains a Concept Art Book where you can see how far the game and it's characters have come starting from the initial concept, to what we have today.

Overall, a good pack with very good content that any DDLC fan will love, and a way to support the devs if you thought the game (which is free) was awesome!\t\t\t\t\t
