\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI doubt I can add anything else to this immense pile of Goodwill and Satisfaction that anyone else hasn't mentioned. Yet in statistics it won't add up. So I'll write this review in all Good Faith.

Doki Doki Literature Club is a work of art, It has far exceeded my expectations as someone who frequents Visual Novels quite alot nowadays. The Development uptil the Climax of the Plot was exceedingly amazing, a fresh and original take on the genre.

The Characters well developed to the Brim, Yuri, Sayori, Natsuki all were amazingly developed each with their quirks and traits. There is no genius of subversion or anything new. The Characters were simply taken at their core and polished until the gleamed of goodness.
We don't only get to see the good but the dark side of each Character.

Oh... and MONIKA

MON FREAKING IKA

<insert immensely huge weeb rant here>

This VN was amazing, it got me into poetry, gave me a splash of nostalgia I have been itching for and all the satisfaction coupled with depression in one bite sized package.
Better yet it was FREE

I cannot Thank Enough the Devs for this piece. It stands as a resounding reminder that Games aren't just another monetised Business Product. They are a Work of Art Themselves.
And Art exists to express oneself.

If anyone is still not convinced they are welcome to try it themselves. It's free and geniusly craft, you will take no more than 5 hours to beat the initial story, far less if you follow walkthroughs or Lets Plays on Youtube.

I 110% Reccomend this. I bought the Fan Pack in a heartbeat as soon as I got the cash on me, Whats 10 dollars for something Crafted with utmost Love.

Just like the Witcher Series, Consider this a "Vote" as a member of the Gaming community, cast it on this piece of Art to show that "You" for one still support the personalities and love involved in crafting this.

Boundless Hugs and Loves to Team Salvato, may they keep making such things as they do in Letter and Spirit.

Tl;dr

Buy first, think later\t\t\t\t\t

Besides, the DLC also has some sweet stuff like all the OST + some bonus tracks not in game, sweet art, and a little bit of development commentary that was really interesting to read.

The game also deserves at least a 2nd playthrough since there are lots os little tidbits and foreshadowing you will only pick-up after finishing it once.

BEST game of the YEAR!

PS: I also nominated this game for the "Haunts My Dreams" category of Steam Awards!
Help Team Salvato get its recognition for this masterpiece and do it too!\t\t\t\t\t
