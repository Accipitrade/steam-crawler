\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTBH, i didnt buy this for the extra content, while it is nice, I think the developer deserves this, and so much more! I'm stingey when it comes to my money, but with this game, i feel as if he really deserves it. I look forward to your later work! That being said i would like to comment about the stuff you get from this pack. While there isnt a whole lot you get from it, you can really feel how much time, effort, and love went into everything!

This holds most true with the concept art booklet. While it was a little short, every inch of it is jam packed with the intresting parts of game development. The first part focuses on the inspiration of the game, the second focuses on the art construction of the game as well as giving credit to the artists. The conclusion expresses interest in other game development which really tells me that the developer is one of the most amazing game devs i've ever seen.

The music is amazing to say the least, its all from the game which really gives that chilling feeling when you remember the music playing from a scene, or have a good memory from the game when listening to the music. My personal favorite is that little "Doki Doki" in the menu music, so cute! The music comes in 2 folders, and there is a pdf which explains which each song is in the second folder. In total, there are 25 songs, 15 in folder 1, and 10 in folder 2. Fun Fact: The game dev composed most of the music in game, himself! Or at least i think, all but 3 songs credit him as the artist. If he is the artist though, TAKE MY MONEY! I'd honestly pay $1 per song, just so i can enjoy that beautiful music and remember the feels.

The wallpapers are all pretty much resized screenshots of the game. As statet in the product description, there are mobile and desktop versions availble. The desktop versions are 1920x1080 and are beautiful! If you dont know anything about resolutions, its the resolution fitting most 27 inch monitors that are 1080p. The mobile wallpapers are aslso 1920x1080, but have been resized to fit perfectly into a smart phones dimentions. I wont describe any of the picture, but they are fireaking cute!!!! AH i love the so much! <3


If you didnt read the poduct description, heres how to access you new stuff: Right click your game in the library, click "properties." Then click the tab that say "Local Files." After that click "browse local files" and it is the folder labeled "DDLC Fan Pack." I'd copy and paste this file onto your desktop or another easily accessable file so you dont forget about it b/c theres alot of really great stuff in here.

I hope this review helped someone out there, and remember, Monika, Just Monika.
