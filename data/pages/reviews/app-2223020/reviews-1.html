Art: consistently good
Music: actually really nice
Plot: A few gimmicky parts but overall damn good
Price: Decent for $20, If you have the money, get this game. I paid full price and I don't regret it at all.
Overall thoughts:

This game truly shocked me. It came out of nowhere and hit me like a ton of bricks. The writing, while some of the medical stuff...clearly not completely realistic, is well done. The voice acting is really great. I have to say that each LI VA had some really good sections where they convinced me (ex. Jay, Tamaki, and Brayson).
I hope this team makes more VNs.

PS, Route order is not terribly important but this is what I would recommend: WILL, ELI/LYFE, NATHAN, TAMAKI, JAY or BRAYSON.

PPS, the VA for Tamaki was my fav. I hope they do more roles! <3\t\t\t\t\t

Another plus is that you can choose a sweet or seductive after-story if you want some spicy moments with your boi. :'>

It's like being personally involved with the best soap opera of all time ft. mystery, thrills, intrigue, power, passion, and two distinct endings for each lover.

Premise, you play as a girl named Meira Lovingston, who was born with a debilitating heart condition and has spent most of her life in the hospital. One day, you are given the news that your heart as stopped functioning at full capacity, and your only option is a risky heart transplant with a low chance of survival. Your goal for the next month is to fulfill a dream of yours, and what ever dream you choose to pursue will put you in the path of a gorgeous, caring, devoted love interest for you to meet, bond with, and eventually fall for. But it will be up to you to determine how your love story ends.

There are 5 main guys to choose from (with a not-so-secret 6th path once the right circumstances have been met), all with wildly different personalities, appearances, and motivations for helping you- but make no mistake, they're all lovely, and they're all worthy of your time. The following is a nonspoiler description of each of the romance paths:

Will: the childhood friend boy-next-door who clearly cherishes you and your time, and the two of you have been best friends for ages. He helps you to come out of your shell and live as a normal girl for the time you have, introducing you to new people and new experiences that you have only fantasized about through the hospital window. He's protective, kind, funny, popular, and a star athlete, with a hidden soft and nerdy side that he doesn't always get the luxury of showing- he'll always let his guard down around you, though.

Tamaki: Soft-spoken and gentle-hearted, he is a florist whom you met in a rough spot in your life who didn't seem to be deterred when you cried within a few minutes of meeting him. Ever since, he won't seem to forget about you, constantly coming to offer you his beautiful bouquets and his company whenever the gaps in his tight schedule allow it. Upon hearing your terrible news, he opens his life up to you to help you experience a normalcy like you have never known, including a part-time job and an easy routine outside of hospital life. He's a wonderful man, but as you begin to learn more facets of his life and see sides of him that you would have never otherwise seen, you will find that there is much, much more to Tamaki than what meets the eye

Nathan: A tall, blonde, billionaire gentleman who endeavors to make your wildest dreams come to life, sparing no expense. It sounds too good to be true, but he vows to bring your dreams to your reality, showing you a slice of the world you'd only ever seen in pictures, providing experiences for you that you had resigned yourself to never seeing. He also inadvertently give you glimpses of a life you would never personally know- a life of intrigue, money, politics, and a bleak mechanical existence that is too cold, detached, and gray to be the right sort of life for a man as complex as Nathan. For beyond his pale green eyes lies a story of hidden passions, fragile hopes, and, above all, a reverence for the precious moments in life that tend to pass us by.

LYFE/Eli: A brash, stubborn, rude, and infuriatingly handsome popstar. He catches you singing an original song one cold and quiet night, and it sets off a chain of events to change the courses of both of your lives. No matter how much his presence may grate on you, or how much his sarcastic attitude makes you want to pull your hair out, it quickly comes to light that you are not the only one among you battling with personal demons. Somehow, he manages to convince you that it is better of you stop trying to fight alone, even if that means you end up fighting alongside each other. It's not like he likes you, though.

Jay: A cardiologist who wears his heart on his sleeve and will toe any line of medicine if it means saving the life of a patient- particularly if that patient's name is Meira Lovingston. You've known him for years, and he was the one who took you by the hand at one of the lowest points in your life and dragged you back out into the light. He is not about to see that light go back out; not if he has anything to say about it. As handsome, kind, and successful as he seems to be, you wouldn't know what to do with yourself if your relationship affects his career. You are not the only one with this fear, as you may discover.

As for the 6th man, you may just have to see for yourself ;)
