\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLoretta's Brand-New Job is the brand-new, totally uncensored (with patch) English-language game published by Kagura Games.

From a gameplay point of view, those familiar with the genre won't feel out of place, except that the creators had the good idea of letting us choose between playing as Loretta or her husband, each with a slightly different scenario.

I've already finished the game playing with Elio and I'm planning to play this time with Loretta to see the differences in gameplay (if any).

If, like me, you decide to do everything in your power to save Loretta. Unfortunately, you'll have very few CGs to unlock, as the relationship with Loretta is very platonic if you don't opt for the NTR scenario.

I won't go into the details of the scenario to avoid spoiling anything, but the game is relatively easy to complete, even if you do get lost at times. I seem to have completed the game in about 3 hours, going fairly quickly.

There are a few places I didn't get a chance to visit, so I'm assuming they'll be included in Loretta's scenario.

This game obviously has miminum 2 endings (a good one and a bad one for Elio). If you're a fan of NTR then I'd advise you to close your eyes and let things happen around you without intervening, but while you'll get your kicks with a few animated CGs, your poor Elio will be helpless in the face of what he's about to discover! I'll let you find out!

I'd advise you to take advantage of the current release promo, or wait until the game is on sale before buying it if you're not sure this genre is for you.\t\t\t\t\t
