I consider myself somewhat of a VR-veteran, but man.. after about 20 minutes even I get nauseous. If you have that friend who'd like to check out VR, don't let this be their first game. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is different from my previous experiences in VR. The dynamics as well as the whole core of the game is quite different than Robo Recall or Arizona Sunshine. We don't see hands, we don'tt fight with hundreds of enemies, we don't shoot everything that moves, we don't interact with objects that can be lifted and viewed on each side. And yet the game will let you sweat and deliver unforgettable emotions by reflecting the conditions in open space!

The state of weightlessness is like a big playground, and the flight near a great piece of rock gives a great impression. With the progress of the campaign, the game is getting more and more challenging until we reach the point where we curse not once. The devs forced the players to face a difficult section, where the task is to pass the chamber inside the spaceship. Little mistake makes us start over again, but after succesfull pass... I felt victorious!\t\t\t\t\t
