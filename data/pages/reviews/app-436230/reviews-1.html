Experienced on the Oculus Quest 2

You can view my review & gameplay here: https://youtu.be/-8hQrJJeNKg

Detached is a zero-gravity space simulation game. It is hard-core. I quit playing this game about halfway through (I watched a full walkthrough). It's not a bad game per se, but it definitely has some negatives you have to overlook to enjoy this experience.

The biggest negative is the control scheme(s). None of the different control methods felt good. You need to spend time in the game to get used to them. Even then, it feels like you're fighting the controls and it adds an artificial difficulty to the experience. The other big negative is the core gameplay loop. You're basically looking for tiny objects scattered around in the vastness of space all while having to keep an eye on your oxygen and fuel levels. If you take a wrong turn, you're almost certainly die. The problem is that you never know exactly where you're supposed to be going, except through trial and error. The game has random checkpoints, some that feel generous and others that feel punishing.

I really wanted to complete this game. It has gorgeous visuals. When you first look out and see all the space debris in the vastness of space, it is jaw-dropping. It also has several sections that are fun & challenging to play. I could imagine with a better control scheme it being very fun to navigate these "courses." Unfortunately, it isn't fun to have to re-do large sections of the game over and over until you master the controls.

Game is running on the Unreal Engine. On my RTX 3080, on high graphical settings and pixel density set at about 192, I was getting almost steady 90 fps with only a few drops / stutters here and there. Unfortunately, I did find that using motion controllers on the Oculus Quest 2 was not feasible. I had difficulties selecting menu items with my Touch controllers, so much so that I had to use an Xbox gamepad. I also found manuevering was much better using a gamepad.

Game is beautiful, challenging, & has a great musical soundtrack. However, the controls & core gameplay loop kept me from wanting to continue playing this game. That's a shame, because there are epic moments in this game.

Rate 6/10. Buy on sale recommendation only.
