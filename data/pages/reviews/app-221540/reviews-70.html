\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLoved the first game, and bought and liked all the DLC for it. Have been waiting for the sequel eagerly. It's the first game I've eveer bought full price on Steam. I have to say it's disappointing. Almost every aspect of the game feels worse than the original for some reason. The graphics are of lower quality, not just visually, but design-wise. The new designs make everything look very similar. Aliens and towers do not look different enough. The scale also seems off... the aliens are too small when compared to the towers. Graphics are very jagged even on "very high" graphics setting. Disappointed also to see that the final release of the game only has 21 levels. Hoping very much that things improve, but right now, it's not a polished game. Seems unfinished. I don't know what they were working on for so long.\t\t\t\t\t
