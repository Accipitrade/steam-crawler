Compared to the original, it isn't as hard, doesn't have as much content, has a weaker story, and just isn't as fun. I played the original for 72 hours. I played this game for 16. I still think fondly of the original. I mostly forget the sequel, but I do hope they'll add some extra content in the future that is better balanced and more challenging.

So, if you love tower defense games you should buy the original and this game. If you only like tower defense games once in a while, buy the original and it will give you enough top quality content to last a long time.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tgood game,

cons first:
-story is a bit silly,
-voice actors are, well, there seem to only be 3 of them (I turned the in-game scenario voices down to a minimum, but I also did that with fallout 3, and Civ 5, so its probably a personal preference)

pros:

+very easy to get into, and actually pretty deep when it comes to strategy, anyone who says "this is obviously the best" hasn't played enough of the game to unlock multiple tower upgrades. (a previous reviewer said that the resource laser is the obviously best choice, I don't think they gave the other lasers a chance, and keep in mind each laser has a different cooldown).

+while the voice acting is repetitive and silly, there are a lot of different lines, each tower has at least 5 descriptors (quite a few "on kill" voice overs, a couple that describe the tower as its being built)

mehs:

ocassionally I get a *missing string* dialog box, I believe there is supposed to be dialog there.... or it could be pulling a Stanleys Parable on me, I'm not quite sure.

is it as good as the original? I'd say it is. Is it as innovative and is the story as unique? Probably not. Think of this as Portal 2 to portal, it's got a lot more to it, but its really just the same mechanics and its driven more by story than original mechanics.

oh and I read a review about difficulty spikes, you're free to set the game to any of the 4 difficulties at any time, and each difficulty, as well as game mode, has its own ranking, so there is a LOT of "perfection tweaking" to be done. I'm happy enough just chasing the achievements.
