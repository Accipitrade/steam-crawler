\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI beat the game in... five hours? I really enjoyed DG1 and it was a lot more challenging. There's only one new tower in this game over DG1, the Boost tower. You'll hear how special it is every time you build one. Pretty much the same strategy works on every map. Place AoE towers near the front to weed out the swarmers, then put the powerful single targetting stuff in the back. Sprinkle temporal towers about and you win.

There's no flying enemies like in DG1. There are some new types of aliens to blow up, but they don't add any extra challenge. For example, aliens that heal themselves, so just make sure they die quickly, which you will want to do with all the aliens anyway.

And they talk about raspberries the whole dang time. In DG1, it was something he mentioned a couple of times as a joke, but you hear it on every map in DG2. It gets old really quick.

I find it hard to recommend this game. It's basically DG1 with one extra tower and new aliens that don't add anything to the strategy. The new graphics look OK, but nothing compelling.\t\t\t\t\t
