\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttl;dr -
Yes, the campaign is easy (beat it in 12hrs). But it's more to get you ready for the gold level and harder challenges. Some people seem to just beat the game and then skip the challenge modes.. that's actually where the real fun is at. Co-op works fine. No you don't 'need' to make an account.

I've done a full play-through of the basic campaign and some other modes slightly. This may be the 2nd best TD game I've tried, if you include the story and the other modes. Obviously Defense Grid 1 is far, far better and more challenging. If you catch this on sale, go for it, but not just for the campaign.. get it for all the game modes and challenges.. and that's what many dislikes here are forgetting..

And no.. you don't need to create an account. The account screen pops up, click skip, and play on. Personally I enjoyed this game.

Pro's -
-Solid Graphics and particle effects, if your system can handle it.
-Fun little story.
-Multiple game modes (yes I play Co-op with my wife and it's fine).
-The campaign is more like campaigns in classic games.. it's a training ground, thus all the other modes are a huge plus.

Cons -
-Lack of any real DLC campaigns (compared to DG1).
-The functionality of boost towers and map designs make the campaign just too easy if that's all you play it for.
-If your system can't handle everything, you may have to turn down the settings (it's still fine, it's just not optimized the best).
-The story didn't really make sense until the last two maps.\t\t\t\t\t
