\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've always had a fondness for tower defense games, and Defense Grid and Defense Grid 2 are probably the best I've ever tried. There's a nice variety of towers and upgrades, plus a decent variety of enemies. And the rewind mechanic is a perfect feature for keeping long and difficult missions enjoyable.

For me, one of the best parts is that unlike a lot of other modern tower defense game, this one keeps the focus solidly on the towers themselves rather than putting a lot of the responsibility for defense on extra "more active" mechanics. While you can take an extra weapon into each mission, its long cooldown assures that it will be more of an emergency measure than a major part of your defense strategy.\t\t\t\t\t
