\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is quite engaging. The graphics are great, the characters are actually fun to listen to. And I enjoy the myriad of different modes and maps. If you like tower defense games, this one is a standard bearer of the genre.

There's only two things I have issue with. Some co-op multiplayer modes feel the need to declare a "winner" and a "loser", which makes my friends feel bad. And when in co-op mode, the ability to engage the 2x speed mode is disabled. Given that some modes take a hair over an hour to complete, it seems like an unfortunate oversight that prevents widespread co-op play of most of the non-story modes.

But those are literally the only flaws in an otherwise spectacularly fun tower defense game. Definite recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
