+ No longer have to deal with Flying Aliens like in DG1
+ Multiplayer options (although at this point I haven't actually tried MP)

- Only 20 Maps
- Terrible Interface
- Two Upgrade systems, that if it was explained how/where to use them, I completely missed it

If you like DG1 there's little reason you won't like this one too.

The Menu Interface is absolutely terrible. I often wonder when I see one like this, if the testers of this game were also the programmers. Someone surely would have spoke up and said this is terrible.

I was probably 10 or 15 hours in before I found out there was a way to do some upgrades that were not the standard yellow and red. I was 40 hours in when I found the other upgrade system. Neither of these did I learn from in game, but from watching You Tube Videos.

Aside from this, the single player is more of the same good ole DG1. Looking forward to trying the MP.\t\t\t\t\t
