Defense grid is not so much good as it`s predecessor was. I like graphics style of DG1, in this game, the graphics is not my cup of tea. The sound is also somewhat weird. Here I miss the powerful BANG of cannons from DG1. It was a pleasing tripple shot from cannon that can rest you assured, that you have dealt a massive damage to an anlien.
I also do not like the maps very much. This game is harder than DG1 was. But I have very weird feeling when playing DG2 that it is just quickly made mobile game with lots of unnecessary modes beside story to keep you playing maps, you played a lots of time before.

Overall I do not like DG 2 very much since for me it is lacking a fun factor.\t\t\t\t\t
