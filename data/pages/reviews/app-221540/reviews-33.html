\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA nice sequel to the one of the top rated TD games on Steam.

The original game was released 10 years ago and it's kinda outdated now. A sequel looks fine now. I especially liked voice acting dialogues and had some enjoyable hours playing different missions.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1379743848
Gameplay is the same as original game. You have core to protect. The core has 20 energy bars. Enemies are trying to reach the core and take an energy bar. If they got succeed they go back to the exit point. If they die, energy bar moves slowly to the core back. BUT, a new alien can pick it up. It's very simple and very addictive addition to the game.

There are several types of enemies and 10 towers to build. Each can be upgraded 3 times. Actually, that was not enough for me. A TD game should have more towers and option for defending. But excellent mission design compensate this flaw. Each map is unique. There are road maps, circle maps, maze maps, bridge maps and others... It was really fun.

There is a score system. And it was a surprise for me. The game saves how you deal with the level in real time for all players. Then a new player starts a mission he can see his current score and his Steam fried score. It's like a competitive single game. It was really cool. With one flaw... looks like difficulty doesn't affect score...

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1379743805
Pros
- Better graphics than original game
- Maps are very diverse
- Very cool voice acting. It is interesting to listen funny dialogues during fighting with aliens
- A lot of modes and 4 difficulty settings.
- Cool challenge score system. You can play and watch how your Steam friend played the same level.
- Cool Fast Forward feature
- Devs are still updating the game.

Cons
- Not enough diversity in towers.
- No info about enemy health / tower attack damage and etc.
- No damage/armor types. All tower are effective against all enemies. Yeah, splash tower better against groups ofc.
- Because of previous point, the game transforms from a usual TD game where you need to place towers depends on enemy types into a Base building game where you need to make a perfect maze with perfect towers placement which could eliminate all threats.
- Not enough diversity in filling of waves.They look all the same.
- Looks like difficulty don't affect at score.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1379743882
Resume
If you liked DG1, you should definitely try this one. It's the same gameplay with new maps, new towers and other cool features. If you didn't play DG1 and you are fan of TD genre I can recommend this game with more than 33% discount. There are lot of gameplay hours.\t\t\t\t\t
