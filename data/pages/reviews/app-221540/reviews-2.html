\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's been a long time since I last played a Tower Defense game and the last ones I played were on my phone. I needed a new game so I finally tried this from my backlog even though I bought it long ago. Despite being an older game the graphics look nice. I do love me some shiny graphics and the weapon effects look great. Nothing like the smoke trails from fully upgraded missile batteries to make me warm inside. You can make this as accessible or as punishing as you want. It's well polished and runs great. I'm playing through the story campaign and each level is a unique map with its own design challenges. My brain isn't great at strategy, but on normal difficulty the game strikes a fair balance between difficulty and fun while avoiding tediousness. I don't mind restarting levels to change up tactics if I want to score as high as I can. I can't complain about the value at this price.\t\t\t\t\t
