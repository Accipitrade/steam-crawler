\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShould you pick up this pack, start with the SU-27 Flanker tutorials, even if you want to fly the F-15C, if you wanna fly the A-10.....neither it nor the F-15c have tutorials, so the A-10 will be a hard sell, but some shared commands and settings will allow you to jump from Flanker to Eagle...just...take some time learning the systems, like in the mission editor vs enemy ground radars (without a SAM battery attached) or AWACS (without escorts) to learn your initial systems after the tutorials.

The pack gives you plenty of planes, but of them, there is a pair that is essentially 3 of the same plane, MiG-29A/G/S and SU-27/33/J-11, SU-33 is an exception in that it is carrier capable and can carry a bit more, but these all fly almost identically between models, the SU-25 is also a practical downgrade vs the F2P -25T.

Personally, if you wanna learn what you like, this isn't a bad bundle, but if you want a jumping off point into the full Clickypits, the F-5E or a trainer jet is a good choice, if you wanna dip your toes into just a single fighter, SU-27 standalone is probably the best way to go.

Whatever your choice....good luck! Learnin' to fly ain't easy, but it's fun!\t\t\t\t\t
