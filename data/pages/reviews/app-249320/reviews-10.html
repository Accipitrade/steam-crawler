\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis reminds me of some of the Janes combat simulators from the late 90's but in modern form. There is a learning curve but that makes this sim great, it's not just an arcade game made to grab money. Anyone who played ATF, USNF, F-15 IAF or the Longbow sims from back in the day would enjoy this. If you are 14 with the attention span of a squirrel stick to COD. Once you figure out how to operate the radar systems and weapons systems this game will bring tons of excitement. Things happen really fast at mach 1.3 Graphics are very good, flight models are realistic. Supports my old Logitech Attack 3 stick.

-Fly toward radar contacts
-Switch to RWS and AIM-120's
-Dump them all at the four targets
-Switch to sidewinders
-End up in a dogfight with 2 SU-27's
-Wingman ejects
-Empty sidewinders on SU-27, he ejects
-Switch to guns, and go after the last SU-27
all that in 120 seconds.\t\t\t\t\t
