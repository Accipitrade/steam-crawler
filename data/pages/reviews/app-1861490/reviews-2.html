Art style: Just amazing as usual, except the second sex-scene (it looked a little strange to me). The character design is simple, but beautiful.

The text and plot: they got much better - although it's still "slice of life", I liked the plot development and the only thing that bothered me was the final part, which seems a little rushed (But I guess it's common for all the "My Furry" Novels)

Sound design: A little dissapointing, some tracks were out of place and lasted too long or too short, that ruined the atmosphere a little :(

Trish` character: Roxy 2.0

In general - it's a solid 8/10,\t\t\t\t\t
