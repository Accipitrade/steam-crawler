\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTired of a nonsense units in RTS games? You always had better idea what a great ship should be like? Design your own (!!!) nonsense ships (so now you have nobody else to blame beside yourself!) from hundreds available parts and throw them to battle! Great idea nicely made. This game is simple but wonderful with a huge multiplayer potential. While designing your ships you will have to take into consideration many factors so design a "perfect" ship is simply not possible. Factors to take into consideration:

- total mass of the ship
- maneuverability (how fast ship turns)
- speed (type of engine)
- energy generated and stored
- type of mount and type of turret
- armor (type, weigh)
- additional support or extending systems

You can design own fighters, med size ships, huge powerfully armored heavy cruisers (with many 360 degrees mounts, many weapon systems and more energy creating potential than Fukushima!), special class of ships such as mobile artillery (or anti cruiser artillery - basically just mount with a powerful artillery cannon, basic engine, basic armor, basic energy generator etc.). It's all up to YOU - you're the designer.\t\t\t\t\t
