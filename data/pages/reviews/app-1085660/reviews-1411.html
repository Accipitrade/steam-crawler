Destiny 2

Free-To-Play As a Free-To-Play player, you're still able to travel to all Planets without restrictions. However some XP caps might get in the way. Thus, you'll get tons of XP throughout your travels unlocking other planets along the way. All Crucible Modes are accessible as well. (Iron Banner, Dawning, etc). Check out the link posted for further details!

Destiny 2: New Light

GRAPHICS - We'll start here. Destiny 2 will work fine with a GTX 970. GTX 1070's/80's work well too. I use a GTX 1650/GTX 1650 Super although anything higher than that is not needed. Theirs a lot of things you could tweak within Destiny's Game Settings, however I will not cover those. I'll wrap this section up by stating that the Graphics Quality is fine, playable even with a GTX 1050 which is what I first used.

MUSIC - Aside from music, there's a lot of ambiance as well which really sets the tone, mood, and atmosphere in many zones. Sometime's even the sound of NPC Dialog can be heard.

WORTH - If you plan on spending time playing Destiny 2 I would recommend buying the Season Pass. If you just want to try the game, it's well worth the try. But for that purpose I would only recommend playing as a Free-To-Play Guardian. Aside from that, I feel like the game is worth it. In more cases than another.

PLAYTIME - I've spent 1200+ hours in this game, which isn't much compared to some others out there. However, that alone should show how much playable time there is. There's always something to do whether that be Daily's, Weekly's, Bounties, or Questing.

STORY - Destiny's story is a long one, but a good one at that. There's moments of dialogue and lore that often leave you scratching your head or feeling some type of way. *cough Cayde-6 *cough.. Nonetheless Destiny has a great story if you care to learn more, I'll leave a link below to a YouTube Channel that covers the story so far.
