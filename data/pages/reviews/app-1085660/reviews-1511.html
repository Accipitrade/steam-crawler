\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSO MUCH CONTENT
Destiny 2 has to be one of the BEST free games ever handed out. I can't believe they made it free.
The gameplay is FPS with some MOBA elements (abilities and superpowers).
You pick one of three classes (you can have up to 3 characters, so you can play all three), and you get a brief FPS-style tutorial and you're off.
The lore is REALLY deep and a bit overwhelming for new players but the gameplay is easy to understand.
The graphics are excellent for mid/high end PC's (i've been able to get max settings 60fps on a gtx 1050ti ssc and an fx6300 for a while, results may vary), and they're surprisingly forgiving for lower end PC's.
If you have a weaker PC, try it. It's pretty well optimized!
Overall, I'd recommend you tell EVERYONE AND THEIR MOTHER to get this game as there are TONS of multiplayer events, pvp, raids, dungeons, boss fights, AND MORE that are better with a pre-made squad.\t\t\t\t\t
