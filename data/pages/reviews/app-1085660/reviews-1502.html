\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you enjoy playing the same levels over and over for your endgame content this is for you.

Your initial journey into destiny 2 will seem fun, exciting and new but you will steadily learn that game play after the main story starts to sour as you run out of story missions. To progress into the game much of the time the game forces you to play strike missions, these missions are long tedious and most importantly REPETITIVE. Bungie only has about 7 of these missions and they love them. They love them so much they want you to also love them, they convince you to love them by making you play them all the time for everything. Want this cool new gun? play 5 strike mission to check the box, on top of that get 50 headshot kills in pvp with a sword......you get the idea. Much of this is because Bungie no longer cares about making new content but padding out what little new stuff they do add. I have bought the season pass, and all the other DLC, not once have I thought my money was well spent for the content I received. The only truly new content is often a new map and a couple story missions on that map (all of which you will be grinding throughout the DLC). The majority is often reskinned guns, armor, and new shaders.

Most recently with new light coming out Bungie has made clear they will be removing the vast majority of pinnacle weapons. These weapons are the most annoying items to obtain, often requiring extensive overly specific pvp work. Get 500 headshots kills with a smg in deathmatch, get 1200 kills in gambit with a sniper rifle, are often only the first of up to 5 steps to unlock these guns. All the work you have put in to get these fun and powerful weapons? gone. I know the community is somewhat divided on this point with concerns of "power creep". The way future seasons appear to be going this will always be the norm. Every season will demand you play constantly to unlock everything before everything is sunset once more for the next season. I consider this game a looter shooter in a similar realm as borderlands, only now the Loot is made useless every other month.

Save your money, don't buy this game. Don't waste your time like I did.\t\t\t\t\t
