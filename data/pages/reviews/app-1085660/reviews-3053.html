\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a fun game at first but after a while gets repetitive. Destiny's story is not very good but the lore for the game is amazing. There are many different planets and they have different activities that you can do on them. While that may be cool and all the activities on every planet are not very different from each other so what happened to me was that the game as a whole became very boring and I would often stop playing and comeback every season to see if they had added anything new. I quickly figured out that there is only so much you can add to a game like destiny and bungie has recently just been rerunning nostalgia through the game. The game has came to a point that everything in it is the same which is not good because nothing "new" was added. I also got tired of grinding the game for a new gun and then that gun gets nerfed so I'm forced to get another one. That was my experience with destiny but I;m going to tell you not to play it. It's a free game so you might as well try it and see if you like it. The game is very fun when you start because you are new and you haven't experienced everything in the game yet. So just give the game a try. Update: They removed many planets in order to re-release old content from the first game. Really bungie? How do people put up with this. Try it if you want but the content of Destiny 2 is slowly turning back in to Destiny 1. I guess there is no need to port the old game now since Destiny 2 is now Destiny 1.\t\t\t\t\t
