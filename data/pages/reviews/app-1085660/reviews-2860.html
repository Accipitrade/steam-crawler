\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, you may have heard of Destiny 2 for a while. In 2017, it was the sequel to what was supposed to be a "10 year journey". In early 2018, you may have heard of Bungie's scummy moves with terrible DLC, like a cashgrab. Later on the same year, you may have heard of Forsaken, that one DLC that swept everyone off their feet and made the game great. And today, you see this game on Steam, currently free to play. So, with such polarizing news of it, why should you try it?
1) It's free. Can't argue with the asking price of 0. Also the first two DLCs(terrible and not too shabby) are included for free too. Forsaken and the newest expansion Shadowkeep require purchase however, which you can decide later after you figured out if the game is for you or not.
2) The grind for better loot. Kill a bunch of enemies, get paid in loot. Redo content at more higher difficulties for better loot. Use said loot in raids and PVP.
3) Raids have a very different feel in this game. In most other games, raids means difficult enemies in large amounts so more people are necessary. In this game, it's more of puzzles that require breaking the team down into their own individual squads to solve multiple parts of a puzzle simultaneously.
4) PvP feels fun. I'm mostly a PvE nut these days, however, Destiny 2 brought me back to PvP. The amount of mobility, strategic use of supers, loadouts with exotics, and the different subclasses makes it feel very varied.
So, if your PC can run it, and you don't have anything to do(don't answer, the backlog looks into your soul) give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
