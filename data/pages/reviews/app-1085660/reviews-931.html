\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played this for 20 hours so far and there's mostly good things. It's a visually striking game with fun combat that is particularly satisfying, especially when playing with a group of friends. At times, the waypoint system can be frustrating and I get lost, but I'm also a bit of a dumbass, so disregard that if you don't have issues with it. Boss fights are challenging and I find that I get decently appropriate loot for killing massive mobs. You'll find yourself getting stronger as you play, and that's the point, and it'll get you wanting to play more. Of course, these expansions are kind of annoying from the perspective of someone who doesn't want to spend $80, but I said ♥♥♥♥ it because I got my stimmy. I'm still in the middle of a bunch of the expansions and the quests are scattered, so you can do them at your own pace. I can't say if the story is particularly enthralling, but it is what it is. The space theme is similar to Halo and it's made by Bungie so if you liked the original Halo games, you'll probably like this. Lots of the guns (Scout Rifle, certain shotguns) feel similar to the guns from those games. Also, check out the Moon when you can. Very cool.\t\t\t\t\t
