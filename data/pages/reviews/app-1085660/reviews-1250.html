\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere's the thing (many hours into the game on Xbox and many many many hours on D1), Destiny has devolved over time in some interesting ways. Loot and gear chasing has been completely ruined by a seasonal approach. Loot and guns don't matter when they're just getting outclassed or left behind in the next season. And if loot doesn't matter, then you should just go for the stuff that looks the coolest. Except that many of the best looking items are locked behind a paywall now.

Destiny, in my opinion, is not worth it for the looter-shooter aspect. However the story is something else entirely. Bungie have created an immersive, lore-filled world that is definitely worth exploring and debating. To me, the story of this game and the concepts it brings to the table are more than enough to justify purchasing the expansions it brings.

I 100% recommend at least trying out the free base game.\t\t\t\t\t
