\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeen playing Since D1 beta back in 2014. Not the best game out there, but if you're looking for an fps/mmorpg/looter-shooter game to play with friends, this is probably the best one out there. Too many times I've heard of something being the next "Destiny Killer" (The Division, Warframe, Anthem, etc.), only for the new stuff to fall so short. sure it's got issues, but what game doesn't? The gunplay and movement feel superb and unique since it's a Bungie game, there's a LOT of content two years in (just some people refuse to play anything older than three months), and the grind/rewards system seems very refined, minus eververse. Overall my most played game, and with the most good memories attached.

Tip: Set your crosshair to console default. Bungie intentionally designs their games for the crosshair to be lowered so less of the screen is filled up with the gun at your side and the dirt at your feet. This leads to more of the top and sides being filled with the gorgeous skyboxes this game has, making the world feel larger, and the immersion deeper. It also takes no time at all to adjust your aim to compensate.\t\t\t\t\t
