\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Eyes up, Guardian."

Now, to write a Destiny (this is for number 2, but really it's just Destiny and everything it is) review, I can't write it as I would write any game review. It might seem quite a pretentious premise, I know, but, really, I have to be honest here, I don't just play Destiny. This is mostly a game I "live".

I know, this Universe is not perfect, but what is ever perfect, really? And what if being perfect wasn't even necessary to be loved? There, I said it, I think Destiny is a game you either end up loving (and living, as I already mentioned) or never truly get into it - which is fine, by the way.
And probably this is all true for a very simple reason: there is no other game like it.
It's not just about being an FPS and RPG (sort of) and so much more at the same time. Let me also say that if you're not that keen on making new friends, cooperating and such, this game will not really be what you're looking for.
I think Destiny grows with you (and on you) if you really let yourself go into it. When my Ghost first told me "Eyes up, Guardian", now years ago, I wasn't really sure I wanted to get into it. I was determined to keep on playing on my own. I was determined to remain a sucker for CoD (which I still love and play, anyway.)
But then things changed, I couldn't help it, and not that much later, Destiny became quite like a second home to me.
I've been living in the Destiny Universe for a while now. It's true, sometimes there are disappointments, I won't lie, but, still, I think it brought me so much joy over these last few years, that I don't really care. I think there are things in life you just love, warts and all, and Destiny is one of mine.

If you're new, or undecided, know it may not go for you as it did for me. Maybe it will stay "meh" to you or something. That's ok, you do you. If you're lucky, though, as I was, you may end up being so engrossed by it that it may even feel like a job, sometimes, but a job you love, nonetheless. You may end up building beautiful friendships, habits and so much fun. And yes, you'll also get angry at times.

"A choice must be made, little light. Even if it's the wrong one."
I guess I made mine and I pretty much know I'll stick to it, heh, I'm that loyal.
You'll make yours and I wish you good luck. And if you decide to join Destiny let me just say you're welcome.

Yeah, let's go full circle, here:
"Eyes up, Guardian."\t\t\t\t\t
