\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty fun! If you like MMO's I'd recommend it! I played back in the D1 days and had a pretty fun time with friends. That being said, this is one of those games that is absolutely more fun with friends. The game can be a grind at times, but for some people that's what makes it fun! There is tons of lore and very creative encounters and boss fights. It can seem like a "hard" boss fight is just a big guy with a big health bar, but at other times there are crazy mechanics in a boss fight where you have to do some weird thing in order to do damage to the boss.

The PVE is where it's at if you ask me, but there is an occult following for the PVP. The only time I get hyped for PVP is during Trials of Osiris. It's fun and I LOVE the way the gear looks!

The gear is something to behold. If you get into the way the gear and weapons will spec, you can do great in the game! It all depends on the RNG from that point on though...

This game is for sure one of those that you have to buy the DLC's for, but that makes sense now that it's free to play. Bungee did a smart move in separating from Activision, since they were making the game a sort of money grab... but Bungee is recovering and trying to make up for past mistakes!

The game is being made more fun with each update, so jump on in!

(btw this is all opinions from my experience in the game, so don't get triggered if it's not like your experience lol)\t\t\t\t\t
