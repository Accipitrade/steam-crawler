\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't play this game for the story. None of it makes any sense.

Gameplay is great. The guns are fun, the platforming is entertaining, and each world is built out with some interesting nooks to explore. It reminds me of the golden age of the FPS. Nothing like finding a secret room with a new weapon in it.

Playing with your friends is well designed. You don't have to work too hard to group up.

In order to love this game, you must enjoy "The grind". You will hit a soft cap of power very quickly, and spend days eking out small upgrades by running the same missions over and over. Weekly challenges try to mix it up, but it's easy to burn out after you run the same nightfall for the 500th time.

TIP: Ask yourself if you have friends playing this already. If yes, you'll have a great time. If no, you'll still have a great time, but you will constantly bug your friends to spend 60 bucks. This game is just better with other people to play with.

Edit: 300 hrs later and I'm starting to change my mind about the story. What started out as a bunch of lore notes that I didn't have time to read, has slowly turned into an interesting evolving world with characters that all seem to have their own agendas. Learning what drives each character has been more fun than expected.\t\t\t\t\t
