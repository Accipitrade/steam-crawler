\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a beautiful and epic sci-fi/fantasy FPS. The huge world building and beautifully detailed open spaces are where Bungie really shines and they are at their best here. I love the fantasy aspect of having elemental abilities and supers and it's a huge reason I'm such a fan of this FPS. The high mobility of the characters makes exploring and batting more fun than normal. The lore is far more rich than it was in the 1st game and the story is ever progressing, mysterious, and interesting. The ability to collect armor and weapons and change the way my character looks and plays is great but, that said, most gear drops with RNG results so having "the best gear" takes luck or lots of grinding. The only con I have is that, due to the huge amount of gear and RNG, PvP is never a truly even playing field. The lack of even ground can be highly frustrating but it rewards those who out in the time and effort. I'm fine with it but think people should know up front. Lastly, the loading times on PC is VASTLY faster than on consoles and makes the game far more appealing. The loading times for PS4 and XBOX SUCK!!! But anyway, I love this game. It's worth checking out!\t\t\t\t\t
