Pros:
- Excellent gunplay, really addictive
- Really enjoyed the PvP ( at the time before I quit ), Destiny 2 was my go-to game for PvP for a long time

Cons:
- Everything else
- Sunsetting content
- Non worthwhile "expansions" - its more along the lines of "Here's the campaign for $40-$60, now wait until our Season content comes out."
