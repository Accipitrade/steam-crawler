The amount of content, hunting for every exotic weapon, grinding for a perfect built, the story (red war and mercury were kinda whack but still), and after all that diving into the raids and all the end game stuff, exploring secrets, it was great.
But since Shadowkeep, it just went downhill, less content, more grindy and un-fun seasonal events, that were getting worse each season so people would buy with MTX.
