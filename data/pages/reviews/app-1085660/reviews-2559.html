\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReiterating my review of the latest expansion, Lightfall:

Having completed the main campaign, I must say it was horrifically terrible. I have no idea how it manages to be so bad when the Witch Queen campaign and seasonal stories were so good.

SPOILERS AHEAD

The plot is your standard McGuffin-driven storyline. The McGuffin has never been mentioned, established, or given any build-up at all and carries no weight.

You're expected to care about the death of an incredibly minor character with a handful of lines and almost no character development other than "grumpy wise role model".

Most of the character dialogue is... Mediocre at best. Osiris though has some fantastic character moments, even if they're sadly brushed off pretty quickly.

Titan's version of Strand is about as fun to use as wet cement would be as a stand-up comedian. It feels floaty and has no weight or impact behind it at all, making it feel like you're bopping the enemy with some (admittedly very lethal) feather dusters.

However, what really absolutely killed this for me was the ending. It, and this isn't hyperbole but rather actual fact, boils down to "Bad guy wins because good guys were too busy having a fist bump." Yes. Really. Literally, in the literal sense.

There are so many ways that the same effect could've been achieved, but delivered in a good ending. Heck, they could just have ripped off their own homework from the ending of HALO: Reach wherein you have an unwinnable fight, but your own skill at the game influences how long you can fight on before you're simply overwhelmed. They could have done something similar here but instead, we kill the bad guy's minion, celebrate with banter and a fist bump at a time where our characters shouldn't have allowed themselves to be distracted by anything at all with the fate of existence on the line, but nope.

Gotta fistbump teh lulz funny man because he speaks like they do in Marvel.

Christ on a unicycle it was bad. My hunter and warlock won't see any play this year as I sure as all hells am not putting myself through this cheesegrater again.

But on the bright side, I could pay Bungie additional money to -SKIP GAMEPLAY IN A GAME I HAVE ALREADY PAID FOR-.

I genuinely don't know how we ended up here after Witch Queen.\t\t\t\t\t
