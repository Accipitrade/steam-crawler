\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLook, i have over 200+ hours or more even in Destiny 2, I bought the deluxe big box edition for 100 euros on ps4, because I’m a naive guy that thought the first two expansion were going to be taken king kind of quality. I was wrong and after a while it felt like the made a 50% good and 50 bad reboot of the franchise.

After two years and seeing them do the same thing over again with finally giving us a worthy sequel with its forsaken expansion, I felt like “Finally, they made my 140 euros investment worthwhile". More good news hearing Bungie went independent and could finally show the public they are a consumer friendly well-respected dev/publishing studio like the old days of halo.

Unfortunately, it already stumbled a bit with its first expansion release shadowkeep, it was extremely short and most of the content will release over the coming year. They basically gave us a polished early access expansion with a battle pass.... Once again, I was naive but I’m also aware that they are a business and going independent costs a lot of money, seeking investors and trying to keep the same quality of servers and content is expensive. So, i swallowed it and bought it without playing it for over a year.

After playing it in its complete form it was worth it, but than it felt like they sh!t the bed with its new business model of making epic story missions, quest chains, loot and locations seasonal only and it's introduction of 'The vault' (not the epic raid from the first destiny). They removed practically the entire base game of destiny 2 and raids that I and many others paid for right up until forsaken. So that was a bitter pill to swallow and i just stopped playing.

Now today here I am typing this review (nobody will probably read) and they already announced their new expansion that looks like they are FINALLY giving us gameplay changing mechanics, a totally new world, new dungeons, new raid, ... and I wanted to pre-order and really said "I'm going back to destiny even if they devalued my investment of 140 euros". But then I read and saw something that completely felt like a letdown.

The once again are going to vault almost half of my other investment of the game and above that devastating news, they are selling the 'Complete' version of the next expansion for 90 euros, do you want the new dungeons? "Give your money". Do you want the 30th anniversary pack for free? "No, pay us money". Will this content remain for years to come like every other MMO/pseudo MMO? "No, you’re investment means nothing to us".

So far, i missed so many epic quests, battle pass content (even though i paid for 4 season but FOMO was enabled with a time limit) and i missed important story bits. I went back for a short time and I didn't even know what was going on and it felt extremely sad to see that most of this epic universe is gone.... In a different universe new players and returning players would have had the biggest, richest universe to dive into with 500+ hours of content to go through and grind.

Sadly, this reality is not possible, until Bungie finds a way to give us back what we paid for, I will probably never touch this game ever again.

O and fix PVP for godsaken...\t\t\t\t\t
