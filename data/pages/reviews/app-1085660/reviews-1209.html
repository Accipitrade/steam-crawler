\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNote- Only 38 hours on pc, but 14 days 23 hours on Xbox worth of play time and some on the ps4.
This is post beyond light release specifically but has affected the core game experience.
I have been playing destiny for years but only recently picked it up on pc. I paid my way through the first game and second game Up to shadowkeep and then rebought both forsaken and shadow keep when moving to pc.
Since the most recent expansion launch some of the core content from destiny 2 year 1 has been “vaulted”. The only planets left from year 1 are the EDZ and Nessus. But even then, the tribute hall and menagerie even though tied to Nessus is gone, and the black armory which was tied to the EDZ is gone. Then the planets IO and Titan are gone from the base game and the first two expansions are gone.
I expect these will be back, but it does seem a bit dodgy to cut all that content when there is a new expansion out. I am sure this helps incentivise people to pay for the new expansion. I know that the “Vaulted” content will be coming back but I wouldn’t be surprised if it will be tied to a new expansion like the moon from Destiny 1 was.
I was sceptical of this expansion after the short and disappointing shadowkeep. So, I have held off on buying it and watched someone stream it to get the story beats. Now all I can say is that I am glad that I held off buying it. More reskinned enemies, more short story campaigns and more reworked content. Since 2014 I have be excusing this kind of stuff because of Activisions involvement or that the next expansion is going to be the real one. I am done with this game. I thought leaving Activision was going to be good for this game but seems Activision has rubbed off on bungie.
Bungie once made good games, now it has 1 good game that is tied down with bad decisions.
Hope the game gets better.
