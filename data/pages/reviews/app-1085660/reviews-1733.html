\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game, but not recommended for beginners.

I will give 3 main points as Yusaku would do, then go over them in details. It is not that this game is poorly made, many levels are interesting, there is also a lot of details, big map, nice graphics and story etc.

1>Guidance and UI is not good for beginners
2>Rewards are not proportional to the difficulty of missions
3>Often requires "Final Blow"

1> Guidance in many missions that is suppose to be easy, is poorly labeled on the empty mini map(I guess they can hide some secret room and stuff this way so): labels for other players often distract me from the actual objective as they are more obvious to notice and have the same color. It took me a while to get use to where to go and what to do. It would be much easier if for example, the objective point has the distance between you and it labeled; mini map shows the level difference between you and the objective point (is it below me or above me?); labeling what you can find on what planet (or an in game wiki can help a lot, it took me a while to know who is vex, which one is fallen which one is the taken, who are the cabals, where do I find them, and their name doesn't labeled what race they are). It is also very hard to find the gathering in the open world.

2> Most bounties and missions have a strong negative feed back, tedious and boring, doing them feels like I am sitting in the office working. For example, there are bounties requiring to achieve 20 kills with a certain weapon without dying and I don't get any good rewards for completing them; the levels meant for 6 players took me and my friends weeks to complete only to obtain some weak weapons and armor. If these difficulty bounties and raids stuff have a chance to drop a piece of armor or materials to the them from a full armor set, which would give you a certain buff on full set, then I probably want to play more.

3> Weirdest thing, why is final blow so important in PVE?\t\t\t\t\t
