\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNearly 8 years I've been playing this game; I've formed such a deep personal relationship with this mess of a franchise that I don't think I can even give an unbiased opinion on it.
The actual game side of the franchise is very fickle, it will be great for a year, and then Bungie will break it somehow for the next year, and then they fix it and make it better, and thus the cycle repeats.

I can however, vouch for the parts of the game that are consistently excellent:
Shooting aliens is always satisfying and fun, guns feel great, as do abilities.
Art direction is easily the best I have ever seen in a game. Seeing the jungles of Venus as a 12 year old blew my mind, and I still have Destiny concept art as my wallpapers to this day.
Music is awesome, though this is Bungie, so of course it is.
Worldbuilding and Lore is some of the best, if you are into that kind of thing.

Even with it's flaws, which I'm sure will be highlighted thoroughly in other reviews, this franchise has so much more quality and passion in it than it's competitors, which is why I think it has been on the top for the last 8 years.\t\t\t\t\t
