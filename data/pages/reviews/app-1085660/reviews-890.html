\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are flaws, but most of what people complain about is microtransaction related cosmetics. Bungie did remove a bunch of old content, so getting certain Triumphs is impossible now, but oh well. Transmog is going to be a pain if you care about fashion in an FPS, but again this doesn't affect gameplay in the slightest so meh. PvP has been hot trash since D1, but now you can be stun locked or locked out of sprinting.

Despite these flaws (and there are more for sure) I still find myself coming back. The gameplay loop is fun, I have friends to play with, and the gun mechanics are some of the best in the business. If you want a looter-shooter, give the free-to-start New Light portion a try. I rate this game 4 out of 5.\t\t\t\t\t
