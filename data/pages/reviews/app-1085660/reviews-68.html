\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat I like:
- I've always been impressed by how Destiny 2 can create amazing environments and atmospheres without having it put a large toll on your system. This is coming from PS4 Forsaken expansion to more recent stuff on PC.
- I've not touched any timed content or raids, however casually, I've enjoyed the dungeons and boss fights.

What I don't like:
- When you return to the game and log on the first time, you'll be greeted with the new expansion cinematic, after that you continue your previous campaign. The second day, however, you will be forced in the expansion content.
- Previous dislike is very disruptive when you start a fresh character and suddenly you're no longer engaged in the timeline.

What makes the game even more fun:
- Find a group of friends or connect with people you play often with.
- Try not to feel overwhelmed with the weapons streamers use, or the missions for legendary weapons you'll receive while logging in. Weapons take effort to obtain and often they require group cooperation. Don't rush it and enjoy the adventure.\t\t\t\t\t
