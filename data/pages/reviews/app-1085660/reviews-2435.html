Ever since Bungie left Activision, they have to monitor this game more carefully than ever before. They have lured veteran players to stay with the fake promises of Armor 2.0 system. This system accomplishes nothing positive for players but accomplishes a single objective for Bungie, creating a 8 fold RNG system for gearing. If you think Korean MMOs are bad, they have nothing on this armor system. It is impossible to target an armor piece with the stats you wanted, you can only feel compromise with what you are given, but never happy on what you get. Kind of like insurance in America. No armor you earned from this game gives you a sense of achievement, you are just lucky to have something to compromise on, that's it.

The new monitization system of this game follows the tradition Season Pass system, this accomplishes nothing but providing useless items for 90/100 of the rewards slots. In addition, other games allow you to get a free season pass for next reason if you are hardcore enough, but you would have to buy a new season pass every season. Certain gears and activities are locked from you if you do not purchase the Season Pass.

Since this game's announcement of Shadowkeep, they market themselves as a MMO. However, the max team size of most activities in this game is 3. You cannot go beyond this limit or you can't start or play most of the activities.

I can also complain all day about balancing issues, but if you are being honest to yourself, no game in history ever got that right. Thus, no point in complaining about it, except the fact that Bungie just so happens to ♥♥♥♥ it up harder. Every single update, there are major ♥♥♥♥♥, scream, cry, and moan from the player base to balance this and balance that.

A word for FPS PvP lovers, DO NOT BOTHER. If your aim is good, especially if your aim is good, DO NOT play this game because it will ♥♥♥♥ up your aim with aim assist (enlarged hitboxes on your target based on your weapon and range, many weapons can aim at air and still hit your target.) Most of the maps were originally created for 4v4, but they changed it to 6v6. Maps and weapons are never going to be balanced or get an overhaul because it has been like this since launch.

These developers never once showed signs of coming up with smart or innovative solutions, they ALWAYS go with easy and lazy solutions when a problem occur.

Finally, A nasty scam recently performed by Bungie was their in-game microtransaction store. While I am not a victim of this scam, you can easily find a post on Reddit regarding the Solstice Armor Scam. I leave the conclusion to your judgement, but for now, I am very done with this game; I cannot see hope or a way out of this hamster wheel besides quitting. It almost pains me to do this because for once, someone is standing up against bad players such as Activision. Yet, I cannot drag myself to continue with this game, I am being sucked dry of my soul after gaining one useless armor after another. I will not participate with this online gambling thing.
