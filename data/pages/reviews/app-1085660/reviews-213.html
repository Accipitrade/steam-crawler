\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI play on PlayStation since my laptop can't run it *sobs*

Anyways, this would be my top favourite game, along with Kirby and Among Us xD

Back when I was 13, my older brother introduced me to the game. Every time I went to his house, I always played it. Every time I saw an add or something to do with the game, I get extremely excited.
During lock down, I found out it had a story line and I discussed it with my brother. During the summer of that year, he gave me his old ps3 to play Destiny (the original, obviously you can't get D2 on a ps3 XD)
Later on, after finding out it couldn't be played on my laptop, my brother and sister helped me purchase a newer PlayStation. And that's where my journey began.

AMAZING story line and DLCs! Fun activities, such as PvP, PvE, and PvP/PvE. I'm more of a story line player and I love to dig into lore, but when I do get the time or I have finished the DLC, I love to take part in PvP, Gambit, Raids, etc.
Lots of helpful people. For years I played with crappy WiFi, and when I joined a raid on better internet the people in the fire team were very patient and helpful. Although I did still feel bad haha

I definitely would recommend this game to any Sci-Fi lovers! It's also a first person shooter, which I find easier than third person XD

If you want to see more in-depth reviews from me, my username on Common Sense Media is PinkFox (it's in the child section even though I'm 18-) and I have a public review on the game in total and all the DLCs so far.

Good luck new Guardians!\t\t\t\t\t
