The DLC is overpriced for what you get, like the Forsaken Pack or the 30th Anniversary Pack. There is also the option of purchasing battle passes / seasons, which don't come with dungeons anymore. In addition to this, Bungie is using the Free to Play tag as an excuse to add more paid cosmetics that can only be bought with the premium currency, Silver.

If you don't, you are going to be locked out of the newest and more difficult content. Every expansion, your power level gets reset and the effort you put into staying on top is gone. For example, I have over 200 hours (I played on battle.net) and all I have to show for it is my unlocked exotics and some cosmetic stuff. A lot of the weapons that I earned were turned into legacy weapons that are completely useless for PVE, as they can't be upgraded anymore. Many of these legacy weapons have been re-released as new weapons, but others have not. If you don't care about this stuff, sure go ahead and try the game, it's sort of free.\t\t\t\t\t
