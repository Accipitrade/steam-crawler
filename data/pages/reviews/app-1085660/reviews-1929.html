*60h into the game*
Good game has everything you need in an MMO that is Raids, Dungeons, PvP, Very few solo missions, Each DLC brings you new division of story arc. just don't play it solo. It has great PvP similar to Halo and Call of Duty.

*207h into the game*
The clan grows richer everyday, Met a few people got new gear thanks to their help, The Gambit is fun which is PvP situation with the other player team trying to invade on your area to ruin your score against AI. The PvP aka Crucible where you fight against other players is really fun and rage quit material if you are into that. I also did some farming in the weekly strike nightfall, was a bit hard at the beginning but as I progressed through the game it became easier and easier. Still a pretty solid game.

*509h into the game* + Beyond Light
turns out the game is not developing as you think a big like this would be, When Destiny 2 came out i thought they will add more game modes such as Racing, Ship dogfights, or mecha fights, but with the new DLC Beyond Light the 4th subclass (super or ultimate) has been added and that is it, Yes it is a big jump from release in 2017 but still this is the first time we see a major change in the game since Destiny 1.
I wish if the core gameplay could change or get more advanced since the game has so much potential to be something more, Specially with +40gb of useless content removed from the game file and this opens a whole new section for the game to add more modes or even mini games.

*Piece of mind* 9th/Dec/2020
After thinking for a long time and re-playing destiny 2 for many times with the latest expansion you won't be able to find a solid endgame to keep on going even the latest Raid I didn't bother playing, if you are new to Destiny you you will like it and most likely enjoy it for a while before you come to the realization that it doesn't offer a suitable endgame materials or endgame gear that is fun to play with rather than spamming it over and over again in the dungeons. what annoys me the most is the lack of improvements with every update, like i mentioned the biggest changes from D1 is the gambit and the new ice subclass. I promise you that we will not see a new addition for a while, we will not see a new 4th class and we will not see a 5th or even 6th subclass and most likely we will not see any new mode. The only thing that still stands is the crucible because of the gameplay. Oh and we will not see new maps.
