PvP is always in a terrible state because Bungie is allergic to the idea that some things should be disabled in PvP and kept only for PvE content. Did i mention that Raids and Dungeons have no matchmaking? So if you want a unique and fun experience in the Raids and Dungeons, have fun in the LFG Discord Server and dumping 50 hours in before you should even attempt doing a Raid or Dungeon. If you truly want to learn and experience the rich lore, have fun reading the lore books in game or going to MyNameIsByf on youtube. Light Fall's story in particular was a big let down because it was marketed in a way that made it seem like something else than i actually was, where we were supposed to lose and figure out how to push forward but spoiler alert! you never actually lose and end up chasing "the veil" which is something that is never explained but is talked about in dialogue throughout the game like you should know what it is. if you think i didnt do a good job explaining the story, thats literally all it gives you to go off of.

with all that being said i cant seriously recommend the game right now.\t\t\t\t\t

I have a lot of friends who play it (many whom are playing less and less). Recent design, development and marketing choices have been bad. Prices are going up for the same amount of content. Things that were previously included in DLC are now behind separate paywalls. More things are being added behind new paywalls. A lot of new content is just reused content from Destiny 1 or vanilla Destiny 2. Previously paid for content is periodically removed. The game is very grindy and is getting worse in some aspects. Most seasonal progression is time gated to keep you coming back. The PvP community is basically ignored and constantly shafted by Bungie. Seasonal content is just a reskinned version of the same thing every season (that you pay for each time). The only thing it really has going for it is that the core mechanics of the game are great. The lack of competition for this style of game is probably why it still has such a large player base and why they feel like they can get away with their crazy pricing and marketing.

TL;DR: Destiny 2's slow steady decline seems to have hit free fall over the last year. Don't buy into the game right now.\t\t\t\t\t
