(UPDATE) It's been a while since I first wrote this review. My feelings remain the same. I recently joined a clan who does regular raid, dungeon and trials runs, and the feeling of learning the raid mechanics and boss fights, grinding out your trials passages, and just having a group of people to play with, makes this game much more enjoyable. Yes, a good majority of the community is worried about the future of this game, but I think everything will turn out just fine in the end. Destiny 2 still gets a thicc thumbs up from me.

(UPDATE 2) We're currently coming up on the next DLC, Witch Queen. Lots has changed in recent months, with sunsetting, sunsetting being removed, Vault of Glass coming back, and other changes. I still feel the game is enjoyable, however seasonal content does get pretty boring after time, but I have a good feeling about Witch Queen. Overall, still recommend Destiny 2, but others would probably not at this state of the game. Try the game out and see for yourself.
