\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is at the top of the mountain for disappointment.

What should be a generational classic will absolutely languish in the shadow of other MMO's, Shooters, Looters, and narrative driven games. It has consistently made the experience for its players more cumbersome, less amenable to portions of their own base, and has even gone so far as to make paid content no longer accessible. They show a truly impressive aptitude for taking the path that satisfies the lowest number of their players.

Things you like to use in game will be pushed in to obsolescence, and a cycle of grinding for things you barely care about will dominate your time and attention. At the end of a grind you will have an inventory full of things that feel pointless to use and a few things that are actually good - maybe even fun - but you'll have to make your peace with the fact that they will inevitably be made unusable and the cycle will begin again. It's the shooter equivalent of a Magic the Gathering Standard block.

Obviously, the game is hella polished. The shooting and abilities have always felt really good and satisfying. The variable being the unique fell of an individual gun that you can't use forever. I'm sure there is still fun to be had, but I couldn't find it. I think it's best to let the bloated corpse of Bungie rot away and hope that someone learns from the very real great things Destiny does, and takes that knowledge to make something that isn't infested with deliberate structures that squeeze players for time and money.

There are lower impact looter/shooters that I think are more worth the time, there are better pure shooters out there for sure, and you could find a better narrative in a child's diary. I don't think Destiny is worth the time, but maybe goalpost moving is just your thing. If so, give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
