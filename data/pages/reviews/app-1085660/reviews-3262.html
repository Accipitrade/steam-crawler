\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSeason of the lost: Pretty good. The repeatable mission is semi-randomized, the weekly quest doesnt force me into the playlist activities, the weekly mission is cool. The weapons are fun, the new stasis trace rifle is sweet. Story is a solid B+ for destiny - far more engaging then the previous season.

One request: If we are going to keep the upgrade system we have had the last three seasons then it needs to have more significant upgrades. I don't want 75% of the upgrades. All the upgrades do is keep me from spending seasonal currency on the umbral engrams. I didn't need any of the upgrades to finish the weekly mission triumphs and it stinks to grind for pointless stuff. Either make em super cheap or make em worth while.

-----------------------------
Season of the splicer: Well... its better then Season of the Hunt was. This seasons public event mission thing is rather boring. With six players we fight for kills, we nuke the bosses. The only hard part is staying awake during the 'hacking' sequence (when we shoot the red orbs). The developers need to raise the enemy numbers to the level of Season of the Chosen. If the enemy count was double, this mission might be difficult.

The story is interesting, perhapes the best seasonal story yet. The slicer missions are pretty cool, though pretty easy. The upgrade system is okay, but half the upgrades don't really matter (ammo buffs, laser damage reduction).

Grind: The grind does not match the content. I have finished the seasonal story, and played hours of playlist and seasonal missions... and have reached rank 40 on the battle pass. The xp nerfs are significant and unnecessary. Bungie had the best kind of battle pass -> the one that you can finish. The xp nerfs hurt people who cant play every day.

Ritual playlists: getting pretty boring. Looks like they will get some reward buff next season. Yay... please incorporate some significant modifers into strikes. The devs could try it out in crucible and gambit too! eg: Gernades recharge 50% faster.

Gambit: Balance it or delete it. Snipers... One phase boss kills... invaders...

---------------------------------------------
Season of the chosen:
This is the best season so far. There has been new things to do each week. The Battlegrounds activity is actually fun with SO MANY ENEMIES :), I feel like there is more action in the first area of a battleground then there is in a whole strike! I hope that Bungie will continue to scale up the number of enemies AND go back to old content (strikes in particular) and double and triple the number of enemies. All in all there are three things that makes this season good
1. Clear, interesting, player guided gameplay loop. The playlist activites complement the seasonal activities and so on.
2. Continous story thread with voice acting and animations. The helm does a great job facilitating this.
3. Good loot.
4. None of the Chosen title requirements FORCE me to use weapons.

Wishlist
- New expansions focus MORE on core playlists. I am sick of doing the same strike in the same way with the same pitiful enemies each week. Please stop adding bubbles of expansions that dont change the CORE activities of the game.
- MORE ENEMIES. Not necessarily more types. I should not be fighting for kills in strikes.
- Greater variety of objectives in pve. Most of the strike areas are 'kill enemies, push button'.
- Revising of current strikes. Change them up, add enemies, change objectives. You have big open world areas to use. Maybe you could randomize the different objectives each time we do a strike?

Roadmap - good.
- Decreased power raising each season is a big plus in my book. It is dishearting to continously be arbitrarily underpowered for activites I could previously do. Raising it only 10 power validates the time spent by players.
- No sunsetting. Thank you. Now it actually makes since to spend time grinding for weapons.
- Delayed next expansion. I approve. Make it great. Set the last three expansions of D2 off right.

----------------------------------------------------
Beyond Light: Mixed bag. If it wasnt for the new subclasses then the only change is we have lost half the game. The core ritual activities were largely ignored, despite losing half of the content.
-- Crucible needs balancing and meta shifts.
-- Strikes need a fresh spin to add complexity (NOT the 1 shot u no matter what mechanics), treat strikes as mini-raids and you have the formula for more fun. At least try it, Bungie please make a complex strike to test it out.
-- Gambit needs some changes, xenophage 1 taps from invaders are pretty old and boring. Maybe the invaders need 'special' weapons to choose from.

Loot is suffering from sunsetting but I dont care about sunsetting nearly as much as others. I understand that we need it. When it comes to loot, I am sick of getting the same old weapons from the world loot pool over and over and over. 90% of what I get feels like garbage compared to what I have. This is indictave of seeking 'the best' gear. I look forward to the return of Umbral engrams, because it is nice to target specific loot.

Now, a word against the player base. We need to change our expectation and our mentality. Destiny used to be a game about grinding to the highest power level and spending hour after hour farming the best possible roll on the Ikelos SMG. The developers are taking a different road, and we need to accept that.

This is my perspective going forward. I only care about completing the triumphs. I will use any weapon. I will use any stat-stick-of-armor. I will kill as many thrall as it takes. But during that time, I will try to do the activities that are fun. If I feel like gambit, I will do gambit, even if it isn't the perfect best awesome boring easy option. I suggest you do the same.

Critics as a bullet point.
* Gambit was consolidated. this was really important. However like the other playlists it is bland.

* The story hasn't included the darkness as much as the last season led up to.

* The new subclasses and the few new game mechanics are great.

* PVP and Gambit did not get new maps for the expansion :(

* Mods are locked behind random drop walls. Most mods still dont feel significant.

* My armor is a stat stick. i can RARELY feel a difference no matter what armor I use. As long as my power level is high enough the enemies die.

Wish list: Remove planatary materials and make vendor tokens useful in the end game - right now they aren't even useful in the beginning of the game. DRASTICALLY EXPAND core playlists, more strikes, more gambit, more crucible. Add a new core game mode, something openworld would be cool.

----------------------------------------------
Season of the hunt: Failed. Pitifully easy. I single handedly nuke the bosses using my sword. "At least make it a challenge for me"
