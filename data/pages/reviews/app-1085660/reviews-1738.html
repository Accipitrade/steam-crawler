\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I like shooting things. I'm a simple person. Is this game perfect 24/7? No, but neither is any online multiplayer game with a persistent game world. Any game where balancing and competitiveness and a large world all exist (especially with RPG elements) is going to have some flaws.

Destiny is no exception.

But.

Destiny is beautiful, has some of the best audio design, a shallow or very deep story, depending on how much you wana dive into it, some of the best gunplay around, hands down, great animations, and is essentially free to try before you throw any money at it. It has huge amounts of content (even with several planets getting removed in the fall), ranging from very easy to very hard for solos or teams and just SO much to do, it's honestly pretty insane.

If you are looking for a looter-shooter with PvE, PvP, group content, solo content, great visuals, and buttery smooth gunplay, then this is for you. I literally cannot stress that enough. I WAS a huge borderlands fan, but after getting into destiny, I can never, ever go back.

Not because Borderlands sucks, but because shooting guns in Destiny is just incomparable to any other gaming experience.\t\t\t\t\t
