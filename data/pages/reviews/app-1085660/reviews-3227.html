With Destiny 2 going free to play, I gave it a chance, and I'm glad I did.

Whats good

The gameplay is smooth and fun.
The story is okay.
Plenty of different PVE and PVP modes and activities to pursue.
Pleasant leveling experience.
Good graphics.
DLC is actually good and reasonably priced.

Whats not Good

Eververse (The microtransaction shop).
Some content is locked behind monthly timegates (You need to play for a month to get and/or upgrade certain items).
You need to use 3rd party apps like Discord to find groups, no ingame group finder.



Overall, the game is free to play now and is worth a try, if you end up enjoying it, the Forsaken and Shadowkeep DLC's offer a good, reasonably priced, addition to the game.
