\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt has been a long time since last I played Destiny 2. I left around late Shadowkeep because I felt like I had done most of the content and felt satisfied with the game, both in PvE and PvP. mostly as a solo player. Lots of content to explore, cool missions to play, and nice weapons to try out and have fun with.

Come back to it today, apparently around 70% of the content in the game has been cut, outright removed. Content previously paid for. Quests, whole planets, you name it. Every previous gear piece in the game has been sunset, as in, gated from increasing its item level beyond what is now the base Light level (1100), making most of them directly irrelevant in current content.

You had some fun Exotic gun or gear piece with some cool talent? Too bad, apparently Bungie doesn't want to bother improving and balancing the game in its current status, including all of the previous content, painstakingly created along these last years, might as well throw it out of the window!

You better get on that loot treadmill, since some enemies found in open world can one-shot you, and some public events and content are straight up impossible, since your Light level is too low, therefore it makes sense for the enemies to be immune to your damage, making you completely useless in multiplayer missions.

I wouldn't care much about this if there was some way around it, but having finished the campaigns and with my current equipment, the main choices were farming public events and praying not to get bumrushed by outright unkillable, oneshotting enemies, or playing dice in PvP, equipped with what amounts to water guns.

Not sure what the **** is going on at Bungie, but at this rate they will be the only ones playing whatever they come up with after Beyond Light.\t\t\t\t\t
