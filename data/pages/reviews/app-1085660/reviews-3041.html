\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2. Beautiful worlds.... That's all I got.

I was going to write a ton of stuff, and I suppose it still a lot but here is a short list.
The graphics are beautiful.
Only the two paid DLCs are good.
PvP is unbalanced and a dumpster fire, but is required if you are going to be a collector.
Weapons can look really cool, but the weapon system is terrible. Most weapons are either the same with small gimmicks, or don't behave AT ALL like the rest of the same weapon type does.
Quests/bounties are repetitive, but that's nothing new for any RPG.
Bungie doesn't give a ♥♥♥♥ about their game. Updates are weekly and if something is introduced in their update that is broken or overpowered/underpowered, they wont fix it until next week (or not for multiple weeks.)

For me Destiny 2 is fun as a collecting game, and there are plenty of things to collect: emblems, ships, sparrows, exotic armor and weapons, etc. There's the explanation for all of my hours.

You need ALL of the DLCs and some friends to play with. There is just way too much content locked until after Forsaken and Shadowkeep. If you don't get them, you wont have the minuscule chance of enjoying the game. Also this is a widely multiplayer game so the chance of you enjoying it without friends is even more slim. The game doesn't REALLY begin until you complete the campaign. The first couple of DLCs (the free ones) are garbage, but Forsaken and Shadowkeep are great overall.

Now, you'll have no clue as to what the ♥♥♥♥ you are doing. Bungie is very poor at explaining EVERYTHING, so be prepared to utilize google a lot.

PvP is unbalanced. Titans dominate PvP followed by Warlocks, then Hunters. There are stats online straight from the game in favor of this so don't just take my word for it. (Even though you'll see an obvious disparity while playing. 11/08/19)

Raids. They're super fun, but you need regular friends to play them with, obviously.

Now, despite how incriminating this looks. Despite how ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ atrocious Bungie is, I do recommend this game; BUT only if you play it with friends. IF NOT, then you are better off not buying it.\t\t\t\t\t
