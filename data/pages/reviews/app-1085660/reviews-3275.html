\r\n\t\t\t\t\tConsidering the base game is free, the game is spectacular. The game is a lot of fun, there's a LOT of content to go through which offers different challenges. The game has a lot to offer for anyone that gives it the time of day and it was overwhelming at how much there was to do. 3 Campaigns at base, multiple planets to explore, tons of dungeons, 4 raids at base, a PvP Game Mode with many variations, and a unique PvP/PvE game mode that is quite in-depth. Numerous ways to modify your character's armor and weapons. Absolutely beautiful game, 10/10 for art styles and vistas, there's a lot to look at on many many planets and many exploration benefits. Little nooks and crannies that contain hidden dungeons.

The game has a few cons - but the pros heavily outweigh these in my opinion. Especially for a -free- game at base. The game is a lot of shooting - if you're not a big fan of FPS this is definitely not for you. The game also does a rather poor job of walking new players through. It's kind of a figure it out for yourself sort of deal - or hopefully you have friends to help out.

Otherwise I have thoroughly enjoyed this game so far. I did purchase both DLC's because I wanted to support the developers and run the new raids. $55 for a game of this size felt more than fair to me. Even at ~100 hours I still have only gone through maybe half of everything.

Give it a shot - the absolute worst thing that happens is you don't like it and it was free so who cares?\t\t\t\t\t
