\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI decided the edit my review after playing playing Destiny 2 for an additional few years and to make it more accurate to the current state of the game.

I love this game, it has a lot to offer and a lot of various activities, the highlight for me is the Raids, as its the first ever game I've seen that has incorporated FPS with Raids and the story of Destiny's Universe. Regrettably, recently or not too recently, Bungie has decided to 'Vault' content that isn't being played as often with promise of possibly bringing it back later on in the future. Bungie basically removed some activities.

For new players this probably doesn't sound too bad, but it is in fact very bad for new players. New players no longer have a story to follow through, they no longer have a story that will tell them what is going on in the Destiny universe and this can be a big problem for new players as it doesn't incentives them to continue playing. It doesn't give players a reason to look forward to the next expansion. If a new player had to buy one of the newer DLCs they would be completely lost and wouldn't be interested in the story. It can have a beautiful cinematic, amazing gameplay but it would be difficult to follow a story mid-way through.

The only way I see a new player sticking with Destiny 2 for a long time is because they really like the PvP but at some point they will feel the difference between paid and FTP payers and will have to fork-out cash just to buy the DLCs so they can have the same weapons, armours and special abilities, just to be on the same level as everyone else.

I love Destiny. I love its campaigns, its armour, its weapons, its abilities, its lore, its customisation, its PvP, I love it all but Destiny has become a game for the the original player base and its difficult for new players to enjoy and get hooked on the story when there is no Page 1. This is sad because I want to see the Destiny player base continue to grow but I don't see it happening at the moment.

Destiny has become a game for the original player base.\t\t\t\t\t
