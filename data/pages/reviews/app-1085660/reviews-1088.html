\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny itself is a good game, it has it's ups and downs. The more I play it the more I feel like the developers are starting to hate working on the game, They removed a huge portion of content from the game that was fun and had replay ability. They released this last expansion (beyond light) but removed years worth of content (Vanilla Destiny 2-some of Forsaken). On launch of Beyond Light the game felt great for PVE, it was fun for sure it wasn't bad but the game felt more empty now that most of the Strikes,Raids,Missions,Campaigns,Destinations, and crucible maps were gone. They game truely felt empty for the first season. Pvp in the first season was horrible with the addition of stasis the new freezing subclass, they did not even balance test the subclass for crucible it controlled crucible for about 2 seasons. They merged the meta weapon archetype with a lesser archetype for "balance" but then buffed the hardest hitting weapon archetype by giving it way more range and increasing the firerate to 120 from 110. When the playerbase cried for to them to balance the archetype they released a Exotic weapon that is a 120 with a catalyst that increases the rate of fire for hipfire to be 150 while also buffing hipfire to have no penalties with the catalyst. Now that they released the Exotic weapon you are forced to buy the season pass to get this weapon, if you didn't buy this season pass for $10 when the season was out you now have to buy the Digital Deluxe edition of Beyond light which is $70. The raid they released was a ♥♥♥♥ compared to the 5 raids that they removed, it had two note worthy weapons and an Exotic that was only wanted because of FOMO(Fear Of Missing Out). Destiny is a good game for the content that it brings to players but when they remove content from the game it just makes the game boring, the playerbase prefers the older content anyway compared to the new because it feels that the developers are tired of the game itself. The replay ability for this game is still there just not as good as it was before, This game is hard to give a yes or no review because I myself can't stop playing it because it's addicting. Only way I can describe it is I love this game as much as I hate it, I love how fun it can be as much as I hate it for being boring. For me it's a negative review because I felt the game was in a better state for newer players and content wise and loot wise it was better, the newer content isn't as good or rewarding you are kinda forced to get it if you don't have it.\t\t\t\t\t
