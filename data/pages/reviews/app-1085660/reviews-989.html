Not only have they fixed that issue, but Destiny 2 has way more content offered with the base game than Destiny 1 ever had.

My big problem with Destiny now is the sunsetting of weapons and armor. Any gear you have that might be fantastic for PvE, may be sunset because it is also unbalanced in PvP. Gear is also constantly nerfed because it is considered too overpowered for the Crucible. In my opinion, balancing the game around PVP is asinine. PvP is argubably not even a quarter of what the game has to offer, not to mention with no pro league direction and no paid DLC, it isn't even what devs are focused on from a content standpoint, and players aren't focused on it from a gameplay standpoint. By balancing the game around PvP you are only hurting the larger portion of the game, a majority of the playerbase, and harming those who pay for DLC (which as I stated contains no PvP content).

This game barely scrapes by with a thumbs up, because the story telling is just so good - way better than D1.\t\t\t\t\t
