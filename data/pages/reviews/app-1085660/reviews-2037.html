Pros: Thousands of Hours to sink your life into, good story, amazing lore if you are into that, and good FPS. People might consider this game an MMO, but the max players you can have in one lobby is 16, but it is a live service game. I have at this current moment 1,849 hours and i still have lots to do but that is only because i try to go for crazy stuff, like getting all the weapons, armor, and seals, but not everyone's into that and there is still lots to do for new players.

Cons: You really have to dedicate money blindly into a the game to get the full experience because it has a pay wall for most of the content. With the launch of beyond light the 2020 DLC they removed almost half the game, but to compensate they added some old stuff from D1 and said they would eventually bring back some of the old stuff. Not sure why they don't want their game to have a lot of stuff to do, but that's that I guess :/

In my personal Opinion I would highly recommend it to literally everyone!
TL;DR
Pros: Lots of stuff to do and an amazing game if you like a mix of FPS and a little bit of MMO.
Cons: You kind of have to blindly spend like $150 into a game you don't know if you will like or not.\t\t\t\t\t
