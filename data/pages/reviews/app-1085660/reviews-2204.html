The graphics are great as are controls. The game feels PC native.

This is basically a live service game, so be prepared to drop money on expansions and season passes periodically to get the most out of the game.

The game itself has a lot of variety to the gameplays and missions. The game adds new things almost weekly. The one annoying thing is that to finish new quests and get new rewards, you have to play the activity/event they add over and over and over again, so new content ends up being the most repetitive.

The game does get somewhat addictive (not in a good way) because there are so many limited time events and rewards that you feel like you have to play in order to not miss out.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter over a thousand hours of suffering, I finally decided to quit the game, and now is 2 months after that. Destiny 2 is a great game, if you only play Destiny 2.
Yes, the game has top-tier story of any online shooters. But I mean, if you are interested in Destiny's story, just go Youtube and watch some content. You really don't need to pay HUNDREDS OF DOLLARS only for the story.
To make things worse, the content you've paid for will be outdated and deleted after a year or two. I enjoyed the Red War campaign and it's gone, I paid for Forsaken and it will be deleted next year. The weapons and armors I spent hundreds of hours to farm are sunsetted. I don't understand why people are still OK with Bungie when they consider your time and money as just NOTHING.
Besides its story, Destiny 2 is a looter shooter. Well, it has a large variety of weapons and armors which look fancy, but comes with a huge price: literally bottomless grinding. You have to farm endless bounties to level up your season pass. You have to farm the same activity over and over again just to get the loot you want. And I hope you're lucky enough to get the perk you want, when someone at Bungie thinks 12 perks for the third and forth column is just fine. The grinding makes me so tired and feel burnout.
Then it has an very unbalanced sandbox, especially in PVP. Stasis is just so ridiculous that becomes P2W. Bungie is charging more and more for their fancy DLCs and season pass when there will be nearly no content after 6 or 7 weeks of the season. The list is long and depressing so I just don't want to write anymore.
DO NOT ENTER Destiny 2 if you haven't already. With all the time and money you can put into this game, you can buy more games that you can play and stop playing whenever you want, you can go out to meet friends or do exercise, you can sit down and have some reading. You can be a better person without Destiny 2.\t\t\t\t\t
