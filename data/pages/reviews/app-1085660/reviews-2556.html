\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is for sure the best of its genera. A good story line, great voice actors and actress and many updates. Also developers pay a lot of attention trying to add quality content and also to help players in tough activities. For them I'd really love to recommend this game.
Now the big problems that now make me wonder to uninstall it every time a complex endgame activities: - there are a lot of issues and glitches and bugs in every features, even in very old ones. One of them at the wrong moment and you'll have to start again an activity from point zero, losing even more than half an hour for nothing.
- this game has literally the most complex activities of this games genera, but absolutely no manual. It's more complex of your car, but without instruction and you have no idea what exactly everything does. So you spend half the time googling, reading forums and watching youtube/twitch videos to learn anything, and everyone is literally giving you different information that most of the time are simply wrong or inefficient. So you are going to lose even more time for nothing trying them all.
- activities don't require a lot of grinding singularly, but really a lot instead for stuff you later won't be even using.
- in-game chat goes offline many time and you have to restart the game to recover, so you have to complete often a mission with your team and zero communication (not many players in matchmaking use a microphone)
- all end game activities requires complex real-time coordination through voice chat with other players; you have no idea what kind of players you are going to meet (in the worst possible way). So sometimes you have to leave a "toxic" team during an activity and start it again from zero, hoping to find a decent group of players.
We could say in the end that if there is really good content at the beginning, what i have found later made me just wonder if I really want to start the game again or simply move on and recover from that bad feeling. So if the last impression is what matters in the end, actually I can't recommend to anyone, in particular not to young players and children.\t\t\t\t\t
