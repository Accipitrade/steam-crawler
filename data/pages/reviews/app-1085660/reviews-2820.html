\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat fun for the squad and even alone. The story isn't super interesting, and it can get a bit grindy at points, but there is always something so satisfying about popping heads and slaying your enemies by the hundreds as an unstoppable force of will. Even in the long treks of grinding for that one exotic quest, its kept interesting by giving you smaller more manageable bounties to complete at the same time.

As with any game, its better with friends, and since the majority of the content is free to play they have no excuse not to join you! (barring the required hard drive space) The only roadblock I constantly hit is having to split up our friend group into more than one fireteam, since outside of the raids or PvP the max party size is 3.

Final Score: 8/10 - Needs more friends\t\t\t\t\t
