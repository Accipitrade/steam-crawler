\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is a little boring honestly... It seems to be fairly well made, but as a only sorta sometimes maybe scfi guy, its really hard for me to connect to this game like I tend to do with fantasy games. With that said, the PVP is good fun. Perhaps instead of making an sorta open world MMO type game, they should just focus on making shooters PVP style games. The 2 classes I played are fairly well designed (Hunter,Warlock) and fun to play. I got bored really fast, but I also found my self wanting to come back. I am not sure if I could "main" this game, but I look forward to exploring it more. I just wish the PVE content was better. I feel its confusing and lack luster. I don't need a clear direction path, but when I play games like this I miss having clear direction. The world events are a bit lack luster also. When I think of world events I tend to think of something more grand, and this is more basic. The Pumpkin Spice Latte of gaming, if you will.
All that being said, I am not throwing the baby out with the bathwater. I will spend more time and update my review if I feel the need. As of right now, I am in no rush.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore anything, if you're going to play in FREE TO PLAY, then I'll definitely recommend you do so as it is indeed a GREAT game. One of the best looter fps lately. However....


I was originally going to put a positive review about this game. It's still a good game, especially if you're playing the free to play up to the forsaken DLC. But I've changed my mind with regards to the paid aspect of the game.

Sadly the devs have been putting a lot of padding to make the game longer (i.e. bright dust changes, Iron banner objectives for season of dawn) wherein the grind takes the fun out of the game (even compared to grind intensive games such as warframe, the division, even ragnarok online). It feels like you're always trying to catch up with the paid season pass while fighting through all the paddings placed all throughout the game with limited time because of work or pretty much everyday life and responsibilities, it's mostly going to be hardcore grind all the time.

Also since the free to play model, there's been more hackers and exploits that's been going on lately. Imagine 3 players doing face to face shotguns, smgs, nades and even supers on 1 player without doing 1 bit of damage. This usually happens only on casual pvps though.

I hope they change how they handle the updates and the anti-cheat algorithm or whatever they use as the game is still great despite all this.\t\t\t\t\t
