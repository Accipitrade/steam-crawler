\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a love-hate relationship for me. I love the world, the lore and the gameplay but the changes Bungie has made and the new content we have been offered no longer seem like the best value for money. Among my complaints include:

- sunsetting: for me with uni et al, it's already hard to farm up to the continuously increasing cap but now sunsetting has made it an even worse pain
- new content: I don't own Beyond Light but I owned Shadowkeep, and from what I hear the campaign is kinda short, but joins up into endgame activities such as the raid. Personally, the raid is not as accessible and I would prefer to have a heftier campaign.
- transmog: when's it coming? Honestly, I don't wanna farm for pretty armour and then chuck it away. Sure there are some universal ornaments now but it's not as extensive as I was hoping.

Nonetheless, I kinda still have to recommend the game. I await the day all those aforementioned QOL issues are resolved and I can play the game and enjoy its vistas.\t\t\t\t\t
