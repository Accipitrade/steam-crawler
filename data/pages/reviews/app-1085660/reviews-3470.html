First off, the servers were to be expected but it was kind of annoying that we had to unpack it before we could play on PC and that took me a good 45 minutes, some people said it took an hour and a half. The game feels great like always, hopefully they have a good endgame. I think the 35$ pricetag is probably a fair pricetag for this DLC, I already own Foresaken but I think 25$ is a little steep not saying its not great but it is a year old (not that big of a deal). Also I think the $60 for the digital deluxe is fair its a little pricey but I understand why and thats why I bought it. Also the season rewards are kind of a cool idea kind of like a battlepass but it seems to be all free, not sure if you can buy ahead but its not pay to win. The eververse store is still there and you can still earn some stuff for free, the max you can spend is now 50$ I believe it used to be $100 but I could be wrong.

Overall, the gameplay is still very fun and looks great like always. The story seems good and I cant wait to play endgame and spend countless hours on the moon! Also the spirits seem really cool, not sure if they are called that sorry if I butchered it lol. Bungie seems to be able to do fine without Activision, the servers can pretty much only get better.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 today is significantly better than its initial launch back in 2017. If this fits within your gaming related interests, then this game should be a pretty enjoyable experience.

I honestly enjoyed this game A LOT after 'Forsaken' dropped. And I look forward to 'Shadowkeep' being an even better expansion. Destiny 2's launch on Steam is a little flawed as of 10/1/19, but all those server error issues will be fixed fairly soon obviously. I played quite a lot on Activision/Blizzard's platform and was pretty happy that it switched to Steam (played couple hundred hours).

To me, it really does look like Bungie is trying to improve Destiny 2 dramatically each year. I'm also glad that Bungie is now their own publisher and broke off from Activision. If you like shooters and/or "MMORPG" style games then Destiny 2 shouldn't disappoint you. Try the free base game first and if you enjoy it then I recommend buying the full thing. Playing with friends in a fireteam is loads of fun too and the raids are pretty challenging but are pretty awesome. Destiny 2 is a pretty cool game though, I think it's future is rather promising.\t\t\t\t\t
