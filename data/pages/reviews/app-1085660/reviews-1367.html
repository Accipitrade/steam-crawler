Basically this game is a grind and a great FPS player along with RPG strategist will enjoy this game but it is not for the casual gamer. Equipment is retired after X amount of seasons and this changes up how you must play. If you are a creature of habit this might be a little frustrating coming into it but if you like your game to stay fresh and encourage you to try new things, this game does this for sure. But do not expect it to hold your hand through any of it. PVP will be discouraging at first but once you learn about buffs, equipment builds and trait rolls along with adjusting to the type of gun play PVP becomes very enjoyable. I am coming from 20+ years of FPS experience along with being a heavy Counter Strike player and I enjoy this alot. Its massive, looks spectacular and rewards you for both exploring and learning the systems. I give this a 9/10 and highly recommend it to the technical player.\t\t\t\t\t
