The story: Way better when witch queen dropped, the seasonal content seemed to be trending towards some crazy climactic event, then boom. Poop humor and plot points that are never explained

The grind: Incredibly tedious. exotics with good stat rolls take too many hours to get. raid exotics have an incredibly low drop rate and are the most interesting weapons, so good luck getting your hands on one. The socializing: nobody uses voice chat, and I'm not even sure team chat works because no one responds almost ever. There is no in game LFG service, so if you don't have an active clan or 5 friends, get ♥♥♥♥♥♥.
