\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame truly shines when playing with friends. I got the base game, Curse of Osiris & Warmind DLC for free(from a giveaway, so it's basically the paid version). They were fun enough to get me to buy both Forsaken and Shadowkeep.
Gunplay is very good. Environments are pretty and the musical score is nice.
I've some server connection issues very rarely and have encountered frame rate drops only twice.
Story is on the same level as Destiny 1 by which I mean that it's garbage and the only way to get seriously invested in it is to go and read wiki page upon wiki page. But let's be honest nobody plays this game for it's worthless story. Maybe they'll get it right when Destiny 3 releases? Doubtful.
Raids are pretty good. Probably the best part of the game.
The PvP is under all forms of criticism. There is an obvious imbalance in the three classes which has yet to be adequately addressed. If you want to play Crucible that badly I'd suggest making a titan or warlock. The only viable hunter's are the fan of knives subspec and the weeaboo naruto shadow ninja spec(both locked behind forsaken/shadowkeep).
Other than that, the game is honestly quite fun. Resources aren't too hard to find. Loot is grindy(as it should be for this type of game) but not to the point where it's unbearable. It's fair.
I'll probably be playing this game for a long time.\t\t\t\t\t
