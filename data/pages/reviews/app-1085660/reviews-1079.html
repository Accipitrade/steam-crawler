Revisiting my review after 2 years:

Level cap has been removed, BUT anything sunset before the removal is still useless for PvE, Iron Banner (PvP) & Trials of Osiris (PvP) gamemodes. So you have to restart the grind anyway and just pray that Bungie doesn't renege on their revocation.

PvP is STILL a joke with P2P connections. They finally released ONE (1) new PvP map after 1000 days. It's beyond the shadow of a doubt that Bungie is not remotely interested in PvP despite the game's potential.

Dungeons, which were part of DLCs (or was it Season Pass? No idea, haven't paid a dime in years), have been separated. You can only access them with Dungeon keys (2000 coins or 20 € -- This is for the 2 Dungeons of WQ expansion, so I assume it's 10 € per dungeon) or via special bundles like Deluxe editions.

To recap all monetization avenues

  • Game was originally buy to play (60 €), turned Free to Play.
  • Game has DLCs (25-40 €), which are lackluster in content (except Witch Queen, which seems to be well-received).
  • Game has DLC bundles (50 €), containing Legacy content. These include largely vaulted activities which are not accessible anymore...
  • Game offers Bungie 30th Anniversary Bundle with Destiny 1 content (25 €).
  • Game has Season Pass (10 €), with 10 additional unlocked levels if you dish out an extra 10 €.
  • Game has Character boosts (10 €), which can boost the Powerlevel of your character to current softcap. This is PER character.
  • Game has Dungeon Keys (10 €) in case you didn't buy the Deluxe edition. From Witch Queen onward, all expansions will require either a Dungeon Key (for F2P players) or Deluxe edition purchase

Destiny 2 also has micro-transactions for the following items:
  • Solstice Upgrade with unique emotes and yadayada (time-limited event) (10€)
  • Illumination Bundle for special outfits (15€). This is PER class.
  • Outfit Bundles (15€). This is PER class
  • Finishers (8€+). This is PER class
  • Exotic weapon ornaments (7€+).
  • Exotic outfit ornaments (6€+). This is PER class
  • Emotes (5€ to 12€).
  • Ghost shells (6€+).
  • Ghost projections (2€).
  • Exotic vehicles (6€).
  • Exotic ships (8€).
  • Transmat effects (lol) (2€+).
  • Synthweave bundles (10€).

I'm very disappointed with how Bungie is putting nearly every predatory practice in effect in this game, all the while "listening to their community".

FOMO is still a thing and strongly enforced, despite claims to the contrary.
Mindless grinding is still a thing.
Convoluted systems for player-requested features is still a thing.

Every new event seems to be pitched as introducing "new" mechanics, but the underlying processes are still the same boring mindless grind fests.

I would recommend it even less right now, although I applaud the story/lore team. They're doing a stellar job and deserve a raise. I'm sure Bungie can afford it with such a heavily monetized game and the recent acquisition by Sony :).

Unfortunately for them, you don't need to play the game for the story though... You can just watch videos about it.

I honestly thought Activision was the problem, but it seems it's just Bungie.
