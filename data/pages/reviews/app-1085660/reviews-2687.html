\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 has obviously been out for awhile and is only new to Steam. That being said, if you are a new player or a returning player then I think the state of Destiny 2 has never been better. Without comparing Destiny 2 to itself there is no doubt that it has some of the best gameplay an FPS can offer. The gunplay is dynamic, fluid and rewards skill at aiming. Movement in the game plays a big role in how well you can do also. Unfortunately, that is all marred by a fairly sloppy ability system and a lack of balance in the weaponry.

Even still, if you are looking for an easy to pickup game to just play for a little while here and there or if you are looking to sink hours into a collectathon this game appeals to both types...\t\t\t\t\t
