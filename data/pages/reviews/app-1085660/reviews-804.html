\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is by far my favorite game of all time. After almost 1,700 hours overall across my main and this alt account i'm still finding new things to do and meeting new people to do them with. My relationship with this game has been rocky at times i will admit but no matter what i always come back for more. The open world is always a joy to explore and i always end up discovering stuff i never noticed before. PvP is constantly going through balancing changes and there is almost always a meta that if not followed can poorly affect your win rate. But overall its still a blast. To be honest i kinda enjoy either playing with or against what most people consider to be "broken", I'm having a blast no matter the circumstance. There are so many different game modes that sometimes i'm honestly not sure what i want to do. My all time favorite game mode has to be Gambit without a doubt though. The combination of PvE and PvP ( PvEvP ) was a genius idea for a game like Destiny and i hope to one day see new things similar to it. Lore... For those people who love story and i mean very very detailed story Destiny 2 is the game for you. The copious amounts of lore in this game is mind boggling and very, very fun to explore and discover. The raids in Destiny 2 are some of the most detailed and intricate works of art I have ever had the joy of experiencing with friends and LFG teams... Bungie puts so much time and effort into Destiny 2's raids and it shows. Destiny 2 is an 11/10 game for me and i highly recommend it to anyone who wants a great game to play and a great community to play it with.\t\t\t\t\t
