The game has its upsides being the build crafting potential as well as all sorts of special exotics to mess around with. The PvE is abundant and there's a decent amount of content for it, but it can get monotonous at times. The PvP is a hit or miss, period. You either love getting stomped by 6 stacks whilst improving skill in those occasional duels you come across, or you hate it since it can be a mess. To be fair, I've played a lot of pvp over the years so Im definitely one to say it can be fun. but it can also be frustrating as hell. Play at your own risk.

Overall a solid game to waste time if Im ever bored.
