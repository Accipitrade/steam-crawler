\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo this is my first review. I'm not going to lie, my initial impressions of the game were less than stellar. I thought it was more of the same bad story writing as destiny 1 and I was right. However after a lot of updates Bungie has put the work in to make an amazing fulfilling FPS looter shooter, comparable to Borderlands in it's loot system, and I have nothing but admiration for them after completely turning this ship wreck around. You don't play this game for the story at all. The stories present are vapid and poorly paced with characters you only know from community memes and a few scattered, well done, cut-scenes. You play this game for it's loot system, getting the best gear to 1-up your friends, the sprawling sandboxes Bungie has made with gorgeous hidden features (the artists really need a raise, the sandboxes really look like the concept art it's insane) and finally perhaps the deep lore that has thousands of pages to read up on, even if tedious to do so. Key features you NEED to try out include the 6 person raids that feel as if you are really contributing something meaningful to the in game world and the crucible PVP system even if it's addictive as it is broken. Overall the game isn't perfect, but I've sunk over 500 hours into this game and that's carrying over from PS4 utilising the crossplay feature (hence why I have 330 hours on steam currently). I'm looking forward to the new additions Bungie plan to do now they have finally left activision. I'd give this game a 8.3/10 overall. I'd even advise buying the DLC's.\t\t\t\t\t
