\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"i like" -Ghae, 97 hours in.

This is definitely my game of choice. A lot of people criticize it though. I'm not gonna say its unjustified, because it isn't. I know I definitely complain/have complained about it too. Now that I'm 1217 hours in, I can comfortably say that I love this game! However, it is more than just a game. It is a universe Bungie has been building for years now. The lore inside and out is astonishing, and just gets better with every new page/book released.

The game itself has some of the best gun play in the industry, and the Raids are world-class. Otherwise, there is the issue of sun setting and balancing. PVP in a collectathon, power based MMO is objectively strange, and I never think crucible will be truly balanced because of it. However, Bungie does what they can. Somehow it is improving, and is dare I say "good".

Personally though, I find the game is enjoyed best if you are immersed in the world and story, and love collecting for the same of collecting/completion. Crucible should be enjoyed in moderation. Destiny 2 has something for everyone though, and this is just how I choose to enjoy it.

But Bungie at least add freelance to gambit. I mean come on\t\t\t\t\t
