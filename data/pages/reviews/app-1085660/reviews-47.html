This game is like an abusive relationship. You'll have some fun times while ignoring the massive red flags. The only thing that kept me here this long was the friends i made in the game and desire to have fun with them but today i finally had enough and uninstalled.

These are just a few of the numerous issues you'll have with the game....

1. It's way overpriced for the content and quality. Most of the "new" story that comes with each xpac can be finished within 4-5 hours. Well.. except for the excruciatingly tiny bits of seasonal content they spread out over 6-8 weeks that might give you 30 min of something "new" that is just old assests being reused. And you'll be paying full triple A price for it even though it's maybe 5% of a normal game size. On top of that, many of us bought previous xpacs for them to be vaulted and then Bungie has the balls to rehash that same content with reskins and make us pay for it all over again. This is on top of being a paid for live service game with seasons AND having a cash shop on TOP of it. The greed and laziness is their driving factor and it shows in every way.

2. The game runs on spaghetti code and you WILL be disconnected and error coded more than any other game you have ever played. The last 2 days alone i have not been able to play more than 5 min before being booted back to title screen. And this happens EVERY SEASON like clockwork. They have next to zero quality control/bug testing and you end up paying to beta test their lackluster game.

3. They care more about politics and "wokeness" than designing good games. I'm all for inclusion and treating others with respect in the real world but these things have zero place in video games. I play them to escape real life, not to have someones lifestyle or opinion shoved down my throat.

4. When there are bugs that benefit the player they are fixed within hours. But anything other than that will be disabled or can take weeks or even go entire seasons without getting a fix.

These are just a few issues and don't even take into account how brokenly unbalanced the game is. Save your money and go play something that will respect your time and money spent. This game is not it.
