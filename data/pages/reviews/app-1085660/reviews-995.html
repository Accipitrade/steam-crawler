\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game overall. Nice main story line, three different classes with unique system of skills(ark, solar, void and stasis), plenty of endgame content, easy to learn mechanics.
Bad thing in this game is that at current state game is lacking real new content. Most of it is either revamped or brought back from Destiny 1(strikes, weapons, etc.). Also there is the problem with PvP - endgame PvP is plagued with people using hacks, which destroys the experience for the ones that play fair. Sadly at this moment Bungie is not doing enough to fence the hackers away.
All in all I would recommend the game to anyone who likes 1st person shooter with RPG elements and futuristic background.\t\t\t\t\t
