\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can't help but recommend people stay away from this game.

This game isn't free. You can play it for nothing, but you're getting a supremely inferior experience. If you want to play the game proper, you'll need the annual DLC ($60) and each individual season ($10x4 per year). If you choose not to pay for those, then you can expect to be kicked in the teeth by paywalls at every turn. If you're not willing to pay that much, don't bother with this. If you are, then I suggest you go and spend it on better games.

Moving on from the predatory pricing, we can talk about the stale game structure. The seasonal grind has been the same for 2 years now. It's just play new raid/dungeon/activity for powerful gear, until it's not fun anymore. The grind never changes. The progress you made last season is all reset at the beginning of the next season. The only thing that changes is the meta, which is interesting for a week or two, then you're left with it for 3 months, and you hate it. Then it'll get changed and the process starts over. There is never any choice, there's only a "best" loadout that you need to run.

Lastly, theres the fear of missing out, or FOMO. FOMO has been a central Destiny 2 theme for 3 years now. Every season has exclusive cosmetics, quests and exotics. Some actual weapons are unobtainable if you didn't play for the season they were around. It's honestly the hardest thing to overcome if you get into the game. It will never improve. Bungie know that FOMO works, and they're going to continue to use it to keep you playing.

Don't make the same mistake that I did.\t\t\t\t\t
