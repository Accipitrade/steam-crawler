\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 has fun and engaging mechanics supported by beautiful environments that serve as a backdrop to a splash zone of effects. Destiny 2's art style lends itself a pleasant picture regardless of your set-up (I currently game on a laptop with an i5 and a GTX 1050 2gb). My favorite aspect of the game is fulfilling bounties and turning them in to Vendors for rewards, especially when they relate to dynamic, changing activities. Vanguard activities are fun but unless you're playing with friends you can sometimes feel out of place, especially when playing with those who have a ton of end game weapons and armor.

Competitive is fun but I haven't kept up with the meta since early on when the Mida Multi-Tool was the gun to have. Speaking of the Mida Multi-Tool I appreciate the endgame content. There's tons of activities to do to keep you busy.Exotics Quests, Bounties, Lost Sectors, Strikes, Nightfalls (weekly strikes with modifiers and extra challenges), seasonal and monthly events and Raids. if you've got the time Destiny has the content for you.

This isn't to say that the game has had a smooth tenure. People have been critical of some sparse expansions (curse of Osiris). around the same time of the Curse of Osiris DLC, Many had noticed XP Throttling. Throttling was significant because after each level above the hard cap players would receive an 'engram' with content only available from the in-game store where much of the items were only available with premium currency purchases or through these engrams and the throttling seemed like a very clear move against the games most avid players.

Following the 'Forsaken' DLC Destiny 2 reset and introduced a lot of changes to the game, many for the better, and Bungie did a lot to regain the goodwill of many players, Bungie also separated from publisher Activision ( I personally felt that a lot, but not all, of the problems in destiny, especially the slimy ones had a lot to do with Activision.

So here we are, The game has had a lot of growing pains but is currently in a pretty good state, I'm enjoying the New Shadowkeep expansion with it's new activities and am interested in how the battlepass style season system pans out. I'd like to see a Destiny 3 in the Near-ish future. I've been Playing on and off since launch and while it hasn't always been smooth sailing, I still really enjoy this game and would definitely recommend it especially as it's more accessible than ever.\t\t\t\t\t
