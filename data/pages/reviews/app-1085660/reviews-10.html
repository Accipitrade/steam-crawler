Shadowkeep was not worth the price for what it offered. it was a clunky campaign and didn't add much to the game, and not to mention them transitioning to a seasonal model. the seasonal stories had a decent basis to make the story great, but 3/4 of those seasons got very boring very quick.

Stasis ran rampant in Crucible rendering the mode nearly unplayable for the casual player.

when WQ came out, it was an amazing story and the content it provided was worth what they were charging for it. it felt like were were back on track trying to save the Solar System from a powerful threat instead of climbing a red tower, or playing in the snow waiting for something to happen. the seasonal content in this year was ok, with one of the seasons (Plunder) being the worst performing time for the game at that point. basically half the steam playerbase left the game that season.

Neomuna was empty and felt quarter baked. Osiris was whiny the whole time, Nimbus felt like a modern Marvel character (all crap humour and no contribution to the story). the only good character Rohan was killed off halfway through for no reason at all.

after the horrid launch and response from the community they promised that they would correct it and give us the answers we wanted, the biggest one being "What is the Veil?". we are almost halfway through the year and despite giving us a weekly story/ lore entries regarding Neomuna and Veil experiments, we still have ZERO clue what it is or what it does. also with the current season (Season of the Deep) the servers have never performed worse, almost daily you'll error code Weasel and DC from the game. servers go down almost twice a week it feels at this point. not to mention that the devs have gotten more and more greedy this season by changing the API to things like the Eververse Calendar no longer is able to tell you when a new cosmetic item is going to be available for Bright Dust.
