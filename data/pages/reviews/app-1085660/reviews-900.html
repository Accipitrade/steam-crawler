\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn exhaustive guide to Destiny 2 for new and returning players.

The best FPS looter-shooter I have played.

The PvP is riddled with cheaters on PC which can be dealt with some baseline paywall to unlock pinnacle PvP activities. Battle-eye anti-cheat was introduced, along with paywall(Yearly base expansion purchase) to play pinnacle PvP activities, it significantly reduced cheating in the game. Battle-eye is well optimized for better performance on low end systems(gtx1060). The battle with cheaters is eternal and Bungie has promised its players to never stop fighting for fair game-play. For PvP lovers there was never a good time then right now to join the game. The PvP gets significantly better each and every season. New maps and reworked player favorite maps from Destiny (Destiny 2's predecessor) are introduced every season. New PvP game modes are frequently introduced which makes choices practically unending.

Destiny 2's Raids and Dungeons are the best Co-op PvE activities you will find in the FPS looter-shooter genre. The PvE is fluid and dynamic as ever. The gun-play is unlike anything you will ever experience. The combination of gun-play along with newly reworked character abilities will culminate in a thrilling experience unrivaled by anything you have ever seen. The shifting Meta keeps the game interesting. New PvE content is introduced each and every season. New dungeons and raids (new and reprised) are introduced every other season throughout the year i.e. you will never run out of things to do and weapons or armor to chase. Weapon crafting is a rewarding experience, level up a weapon by using it to equip enhanced perks which will boost the weapon's capability significantly.

Much of the game can be enjoyed by free to play players. The pinnacle PvP activity (Called Trials of Osiris) requires the current base expansion to play but there are tons of free PvP activities that can be enjoyed by everyone. 80% of all exotic weapons and armor (The META gear) can be acquired by F2P players and almost all of the 'META' legendary weapons and armor can be acquired by F2P players. The game requires players to invest some time to achieve weapons and armor with community favored perks and rolls. The purchase of Expansions is meaningful, it provides players with significant amount of activities to pursue and helps development of a truly great game of this generation.

The story is a gripping experience. It will ever keep you at the edge of your seats. The building story over consecutive seasons culminating in an expansion is truly marvelous. The story-telling is extraordinary and you will never run out of lore to pursue which are hidden in every nook and corner of the Destiny 2 world.

Witch queen is the best expansion of Destiny 2 yet. The upcoming Lightfall expansion(2.2023) is truly promising with new character abilities (called subclass), new location, 2 new dungeons to be released in Season 21 and 23, 1 new raid to be released in Season 20 and one player favorite raid from Destiny to be released in Season 22.

My tips for new players.

1. Choose a platform, any platform you want (except Mac and Linux :p). If and only if you like the game (should play for over 200 hrs before making any decisions), purchase the Witch queen base expansion and the Witch queen dungeon key. Pre-order the upcoming Lightfall Deluxe edition(TBR 2.2023), you will not be disappointed!

2. Join the Fanmade Discord Server for Destiny 2 called "Destiny 2 LFG", it has over 450k members with around 150k members online at any given time, there you will always find sherpas willing to guide you through every activity in the game. With Lightfall finding groups to play with will become even easier as LFGs (Looking for groups) will be added to the game itself.

3. Download the Destiny 2 companion app on your phone to acquire vendor bounties and use "Destiny Inventory Manager" in a browser on your PC to manage your weapons and gears, both are completely optional and are just convenient tools.

4. Destiny 2 community is one of the most active community you will find in the gaming world. You will find guides for everything on Youtube (My personal favorite is "Aztecross"). The lore daddies of the community are "My name is Byf" and "Myelin Games", watch their videos to delve into the story of Destiny in minute detail.

The game defines the term fire-team. With the recent introduction of Cross-play, you can play with your friends no matter what platform you are gaming on! You can also synchronize your progress across every platform with Cross-play. Every activity of this game is meant to be enjoyed with other players whom you need beside you when you delve into the limitless depth that is Destiny 2. So come and enjoy a wonderful game with a great community and I guarantee you will never leave.
