\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo let's get a quick overview :

First off, i played Destiny 1 back in the Days but stopped after 1 Year.
Now after hearing that Bungie and Activsion parted ways and D2 moved over to Steam I thought i'd give it a Shot.

Is this Game perfect and flawless ?
Not in the slightest. There's a lot of Things that could be improved upon.
Questdesigns, the completly butchered Introduction and missing out on a lot of lore that has the potential to bequite intriguing ( since its free you basically skip the whole leveling process and the base story), the options of actual ingame socializing,missing systems to compare Weapons/specs,Stats in a manner ppl understand it (no Dps ?) and so on, just to name a few.

Is this Game a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ of fun and a sinkhole for time ?
Yes indeed, i found myself enjoying the game a lot, like....really.
Not even visited the Moon or t he actual Content yet.
Just grinding PvP and visiting old Content to catch up on the World.
3 Classes, with 3 subclasses each.
Not a lot of Costumization Skillwhise, but enough for a Game thats want to be a Shooter and not an Roleplaygame.
New Armor 2.0 and some Weapon costumization as well.
Raids, Dungeons, different PvP Modes.
Cosmetics, "Achievements", a Lore that is Miles better than the stuff Destiny 1 had to offer.

Sadly the Whole Clan Finding process and ingame communication really are a disappointment.
Ofc Destiny isn't a Roleplay MMO and in itself quite fastpaced, but i'd love to just have a chill conversation while doing some PvP or Strikes, sadly nothing remotley close happened in my 10h playtime. noone tried to talk to me, nobody answered questions or just friendly greetings/comments while doing some Events.

Overall :

Get it ! If you enjoy Lootin.
Get it if you enjoy Shootin.
Get it if you enjoy just getin silly or need something to relax.\t\t\t\t\t
