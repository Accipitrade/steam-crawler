\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwesome game to lose hundreds of hours into. I didn't like the seasonal model at first but after a while I realized that this game is perfect to pick up and put down. I can knock out most of the things I want to knock out in a few hours each week, including seasonal storylines, dungeons, raids, and just plain exploration since the Witch Queen came out (excellent open world addition to the game). Generally about halfway through the season I will only hop in to play any new seasonal content and then I can put it down and play something else for a few weeks. The grind is real and Bungie has lots of ups and downs but overall this is a very fun game to dig into. Plenty of sidequests and weekly rituals to keep you busy and the gunplay is perfect. The game has highs and lows over the years, with many additions being absolutely retarded and some being amazing QOL changes or gameplay additions. Most recently the game is encouraging class builds with weapon synergy so it's a lot of fun to try new things out. There's almost always a chase for a better gun or armor loadout and there is plenty of both to choose from.

Destiny 2 is a game I hate to love and love to hate. Sometimes it feels like a second job when the season starts winding down but Bungie always keeps things interesting with new activities to roll through. Destiny also has some incredibly deep lore if you're a lore buff. You can spend hours reading into it and you'll watch the whole Destiny universe come together if you do. Bungie is focusing on some really solid storytelling with Witch Queen and I can't wait to see what comes next.\t\t\t\t\t
