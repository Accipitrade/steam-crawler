STRAIGHT UP!

At first I was skeptical and disappointed with Destiny 2.

But I kept playing and the game grew on me.

As time has passed, I must admit it is fun!

It's like DOOM with a crap load of content, guns galore and tons of loot!

The people in game have been cool to me.

PVP is fun.

PVE COOP is a blast!

PVE Solo is THE BEST!
