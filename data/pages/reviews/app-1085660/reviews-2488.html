\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttl;dr: It's free. And the free content is better than many games you'll pay to play.

The game's main competition is Warframe, which is a more grindy game with more content, but because this game is less grindy you'll feel like there's definitely less content. There's BIG differences between the two that keeps the two different enough imo to make them "apples and oranges" to me personally, but those differences won't matter to most people, so give them both a shot and form your own opinion. Also, it totally blows me away that Bioware released Anthem with a 60$ price tag and failed to make it even remotely as good as this game or Warframe.

The biggest issue with this game since it launched was the idea that the player would be spending 200$ to effectively play this game and all it's DLC. That's not the case anymore, and it's probably around 45$ to get both major expansions for this game and all it's content. Not bad. So that's not an issue anymore.

My biggest issue with the game today is how neutered the RPG mechanics have become and how they're still prevalent in meaningless ways. You don't level up anymore, and your character stats are all based on the gear you have equipped. The gear you get is usually the same 20 or so items per slot but just "better." The items themselves have a level that increase your damage and decrease your damage based on the level you are in relation to the level of enemies, but your damage is brought down to the level of the area you're in and capped based on the zone in weird ways that aren't ever explained to you well. Honestly, it's kind of a dumb system and Bungie obviously didn't care to balance it or design it well at all or think of how it would really work or how people would feel about it, etc etc etc. Long story short there, I hate it, it's dumb, it's nitpicky as heck I know.

Beyond that, the game feels great to play, great visual effects, great button alignment and control scheme, everything is very satisfying to do, from shooting to critting, to using supers, and acquiring cool new weapons to play with. There's a wide number of different enemies to fight and it's a pretty fun game in general with a lot to do. Did I mention the game costs 0$?

Also there are swords in it. I see swords I press like.
