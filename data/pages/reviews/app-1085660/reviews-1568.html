\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played Destiny since the House of Wolves expansion came out for Destiny 1 in 2015. The game has evolved so much since then, and now that Bungie has gained freedom from the strong arm of Activision Blizzard, they have used their creative talents to improve Destiny 2 even further beyond what it already was.

The game is excellent. It has incredible, stunning graphics, with intricately designed character and gun models, and beautiful skyboxes. The gameplay is, as always with Destiny, very fun, and rewarding. The story of the game has certainly picked up from the nonsense that it was pre Destiny 1 Taken King, and has kept getting better as the game moves through its various and continuing seasons and expansions.

Overall, Destiny 2 is a great game with tons of content to explore, even for those who opt to stay free-to-play. The download size of the game is absolutely massive at around 94 - 100 GB. Keep that in mind before deciding to play the game, because the size of the game is so ridiculously large that most people will not be able to comfortable fit it on their SSD, and this game is quite taxing on most systems which use even the best technology today.

This is an excellent game, I would recommend playing it, and maybe buying some expansions or a season pass if you really enjoy it, and have the free time to play it regularly.\t\t\t\t\t
