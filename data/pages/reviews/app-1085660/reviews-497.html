\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt used to be good. Gameplay is crisp and tight, weapons all feel unique and have reasonable tradeoffs, and everywhere is pretty.

Freemium games fall for the same pitfall every time: Dollar signs fill their eyes, and pricing ramps up. At some point, a developer listens to the vocal minority and balance goes out the window. At first, I felt like I was able to enjoy the same content, but eventually I noticed the paywalls getting higher and higher.

PVE: It's alright, but there's very little in the way of community building. A fair amount of the content has just been outright deleted in place of new stuff, but that's not all bad. There's very little variety: Ride to a place, swoop around and kill baddies, rinse and repeat. PVE has been pretty well benched in the name of PVP.

PVP: If you're not a titan, don't bother. Warlocks got de-fanged, and everyone who fancies themself sniper plays hunter... and I wouldn't say snipers are a real threat. It feels mostly like a Halo reskin.\t\t\t\t\t
