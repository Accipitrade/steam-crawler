\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a game currently managed by idiots. They had a brilliant game. It was called Destiny 2: Forsaken. I would pay good money to play that game again today. Destiny 2, Season of the Free to Plays, will never have that magic. Bungie has straight up told the community, "No, we will not do anything like Forsaken again, but keep buying our battle pass." If you are a new player, you deserve to punch me in the mouth; I got to play this game when it was fun and you never will - it sucks to be you.

I would play with you, if it was possible. If we could load up a Day 1 game of Gambit, before Bungie overburdened the game mode with their hipsterian over-tuning, we would have more fun than you can possibly imagine. Back before it went F2P, you could overlook the rough edges of the beautiful set pieces, you would willingly engage in the illusion. It might shock you to hear this, but it was the story which made the illusion so appealing, but that has collapsed into a witless and shiftless pile incapable of causing any human emotion. Bungie has taken a page from the anime light novel writer and seems to believe that refusing to communicate is somehow mysterious instead of frustrating, and frustration is not an emotion; it is merely the prelude to the rage which will inevitable overwhelm you immediately before you uninstall the game.

It is slow; it is full of glitches; it runs on an engine too old for Jeffrey Epstein; it is managed by money-hungry hipsters marketing to the Fortnight Brigade; and it is claims to be free to play, but you will never be competitive without buying the Darkness Powers expansion and then grinding those all out. Bungie extorts their players worse than Blizzard, just don't do it.\t\t\t\t\t
