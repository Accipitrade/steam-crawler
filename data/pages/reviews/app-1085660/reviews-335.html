\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing Destiny 2 since launch, and it continues to be one of the strongest looter-shooters around. Solid gun-feel, a wealth of different activities, and many, many options for customization, both in terms of abilities/weapons and aesthetics. It is still very well-maintained for a game of its age, still boasting new content every few months. And now that some of the content is free to play, you've got nothing to lose by giving it a shot!

One recommendation though: Don't expect to have a great time playing the competitive multiplayer modes, unless you're willing to dedicate a fair amount of time and effort into learning the meta and tracking down the weapon and armour pieces that are OP right now. For me, playing competitive multiplayer makes me feel like a farmer with a sawed-off shotgun trying to take on an invading alien force armed with lasers and space magic. My gun shoots bullets. Their guns fire wacky grenades that send out a advancing wall of instant-kill fire. Still, if it seems like fun to you, do check it out. Especially to get the good rewards.\t\t\t\t\t
