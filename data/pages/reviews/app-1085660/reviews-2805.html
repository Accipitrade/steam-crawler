While it is super overwhelming at the beginning, thanks to quest overload and the ability to only carry 63 quests at a time, the problem will be gone at some point. Maybe a month in, depending on how hard you go at it.

The quests are also either great or straight garbage. There are no information on most quests in the game, and you have to rely on google to help you through a lot of the game.

The gameplay feels the best out of all the shooters i've played recently.

While the game is free to play, quite a bit of content is gated behind Forsaken or Shadowkeep.
I would suggest just downloading the game and play, it is free afterall. If you enjoy it, and would like some more content, like raids or even new weapons - buy it :)
