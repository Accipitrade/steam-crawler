This game meant so much to me over the years but recently in the last 2 to 3 years its started to cause heartbreak. I am mostly into the PVP aspect of the game as I am sure a large amount of you are as well, and seeing the last few seasons with no big push for PVP, has absolutely killed playing the game for me.

Don't get me wrong, I love the PVE side of Destiny as well, that's the main part of the game and it should be. I have enjoyed ever single raid that has been released (Including Destiny 1) as well as completing strikes and the story with friends. Grinding for guns and loot is always fun because it gives you a chance for those "God Roles" that can give a player a pivotal advantage in PVP.

Destiny 2 in 2021 is just not as it was before and listen change is great but for me, without new maps or new PVP focused weapons and gear it becomes draining and makes the community feel like we're just left out.\t\t\t\t\t
