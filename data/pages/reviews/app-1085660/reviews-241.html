\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat can I say, 1200 hours in and counting. Destiny 2 has been one of the best gaming experiences I've ever had. Overall this game is always a good time, even during the worst expansions. Especially recently, Bungie has done an absolutely excellent job at making this game feel rewarding to play without taking out core aspects of the game. I switched to Destiny after WoW started feeling like a boring slog and I'll tell you that was the best decision I ever made. Price wise, the game is alot cheaper and for the fun that just makes it's value skyrocket.

The only caution I would give is to newbies, this is a tough game to pick up and get into simply because of how much catch up content there is. However Bungie has recently updated this a little to make the early game slog feel less like a grind and more of a progression. I would say if you're new and feel like you're having trouble figuring out what to do, check out a starters guide on YouTube and don't give up! Trust me, you will have catch up in all MMOs and destiny is the only one I know that doesn't punish you as harshly from a gameplay perspective if you're new.

Not only that but if you're a big fan of story and lore, this game is chock full of it. Bungie is quite skilled at story and you would know this if you're a halo fan. Recent expansions have expanded the universe even more and it just keeps getting better.\t\t\t\t\t
