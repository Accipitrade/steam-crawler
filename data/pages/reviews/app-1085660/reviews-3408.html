\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOnce I got into a groove of gathering mats/weapons/quests, the game started to click for me. It sucks that the campaign is buried because it's nice to have context to the action, (and it's actually not too bad as far as story is concerned) but overall, it's not a big deal as there are other systems in place to get you on the path of the loot grind, which is honestly the only reason most people will play this.

The system they've put into place reminds me a lot of Diablo 3, which gives players the option to just skip right into bounties and never touch the main campaign, which is far inferior to this game's.

It's basically a game that gives you A LOT to do, through various tasks big and small, not to mention tons of ♥♥♥♥ to collect, so you always feel like you're progressing toward something, even if it's kind of easy most of the time. It's still a lot of fun, because hey this is Bungie, and they make shooters good. The new Shadowkeep is very well done, good setting, and some neat world events that get pretty crazy.

It's a more sophisticated Borderlands and it's really damn pretty and incredibly well optimized.

If you're even slightly curious, then you owe it to yourself to suffer the 80gb download and give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
