\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(I played a lot of Destiny 2 on another platform, followed the game up to the Shadowkeep expansion.)

While a part of the development team clearly put a lot of soul in the art of the game and deserves recognition, I can't recommend the game itself due to its mismanagement and shallowness.

Destiny 2 is the perfect example of why "Games as a Service" is the bane of gaming. With the expansion "Beyond Light" Bungie decided, out of nowhere, to implement a "Content Vault" and remove previous available maps to "make room" for the new ones. If you loved those maps, now they're gone, reduced to atoms - until Bungie decides to re-release them in a 30 dollars expansion for Destiny 3.

Better save that kind of money for something truly good that doesn't disappear from your library.\t\t\t\t\t
