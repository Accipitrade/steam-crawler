\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my game! I probably have several thousand hours on console too. It is not a perfect game by any means but the farming loop and constant reward system for your time, build crafting, mesh of pve and pvp, co-op activities, and being a space-ninja-mage works for me. It is very intimidating and convoluted for new players. It is true, much of the content has been vaulted to keep the size down but you can get all of the previous story from Youtube and let's face it, many don't play MMOs for story. The best thing about this game is it has content for casuals and sweats both. It has a healthy community that is always willing to help. I've had countless raids and dungeons voluntarily taught to me by strangers that ask for nothing in return. It's hard to find that kind of patience and charity in other MMOs.

If I could improve it, I'd make the open world more open. More dynamic open world content instead of the exact same mission types and public events on each planet. Add some space dragon that takes 100 people to kill that shows up somewhere with big rewards for participation or events like that. I'd also add a large team vehicle based pvp game mode. Seasonal events leave a lot to be desired. You will trek over the same maps over and over again doing similar things to collect relics or data or something monotonous. Seems little effort is put into the seasons.

Other than that this game requires taking a break sometimes. I've taken months to years off. But I always come back eventually. It's pretty much a crack habit I can rehab for some time but the itch always returns. Don't even bother playing if you are limited on time. Like any MMO even if you are casual, it requires some time investment.\t\t\t\t\t
