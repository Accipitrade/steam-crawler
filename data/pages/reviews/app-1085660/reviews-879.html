\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really want to like this game. I love the world, the guns, the dungeons.
What I don't love, is constantly having to inject more and more money into a game that is routinely broken (servers not working, severe rubber-banding on creatures) or just flat out not patched (except when it's something that the community likes or enjoys, then things get patched). There are so many bugs and unfixed issues that its impossible for me to list them here.
Not to mention the fact that a game that you MUST purchase expansions and seasons for, also has a microtransaction shop. Most of the content that I have paid for in this game has been artificially removed for.. reasons?

In short, don't waste your time or your money. There are dozens of other games that respect your time, that respect their players. Bungie doesn't and won't care, but they'll tell you that they are listening.

If I could, I would absolutely refund this game. But I can't, because I wanted to believe that the developers would improve their product. I was wrong.\t\t\t\t\t
