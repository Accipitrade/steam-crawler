\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOddly, not the best looter shooter IMHO. Everyone cites it as right up there with Warframe, The Division games, and the Borderlands series, but I liked The Division 2 and Borderlands 2 much more than Destiny 2.

The world feels very drab and lonely to me. The characters and bosses are uninteresting. The loot is unmemorable. Unlocking the best stuff seems to require a lot of grinding, more than in other games.

There are a ton of features, some pertaining to multiplayer, that I just could not care less about.

I was not enthusiastic to hear that they sunset ("vault") content. Taking away loot in a looter shooter is an odd choice IMHO.\t\t\t\t\t
