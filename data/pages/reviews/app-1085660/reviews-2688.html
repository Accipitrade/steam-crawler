I burnt out so bad that I didn't even play Forsaken (even though I had paid for it).

I've been following the news though, and The Division 2 was getting pretty stale at the time that Destiny 2 was going free to play so I thought I'd jump back in and do Forsaken and soon after I also finished Shadowkeep.
