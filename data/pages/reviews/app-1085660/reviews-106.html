\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt the time of writing this...I can't recommend it. (Lightfall)
1. Difficulty spikes (although not massive by any means) across all activities (yes, including patrol in the newest zone) have just crushed what enjoyment there was for playing. Some people enjoy the changes, but overall, the changes were not needed as no one was bragging about getting the clan together to do strikes or patrol. Some of us (like me) mostly get on to unwind after a stressful day at work and just want to tear through some mobs w/out having to stress over builds. I just want to get some kills w/ my prolonged engagement and turn in bounties! Some of you may say it's a skill issue, but A: It's not, as I have solo flawless dungeon runs and B: The game wasn't marketed as a "soulsborne" game or w/e.

2. The story was weak, but honestly? Has been most of the time with expansions with the exception of Forsaken, Witch Queen annnd one or two missions from shadowkeep. Thats it. Last few seasons had been incredibly weak and rushed narratives, so it was to be expected with Lightfall. No surprises is all I'm saying.

I'm hoping that Bungie will revert the difficulty changes soon because I'm a diehard fan of the game, and weak storytelling hasn't pushed me away yet, but that's because the shooting stuff feels good. Right now, the shooting part doesn't.\t\t\t\t\t
