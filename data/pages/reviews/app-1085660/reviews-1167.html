However, for veterans of the franchise, I can easily say that this game TRULY is a one of a kind experience. From Day 1 raids, to experiencing new seasonal content, the game continues to get stronger. The gameplay is very engaging and enjoyable whether you're defeating a Hive god or gunning down some low tier ads. The loot is the main reason to play this game, as most of the exotics feel unique and fill their niche of the meta. This game has connected me with people all across the world, and I wouldn't give away that experience for anything. 10/10

EDIT-------

I don't know what I was on, but copium was strong. I think that this game is struggling to innovate on anything other than endgame activities and weapons. I will always have a soft spot for this game as I've been playing since D1, but it is to the point where I will just be buying the yearly expansions for the raids and not buy the seasonal content. Hopefully I can come back to this game and reignite that flame, but for now it doesn't seem like it will happen.\t\t\t\t\t
