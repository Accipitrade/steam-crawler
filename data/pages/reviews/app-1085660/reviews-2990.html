First things first
If you are new, make sure to look at some videos about guides/info/tips for newcomers, this game is not clear in what you should focus on, since well you can start where ever you like. Once you start to get the hold of it the games is absolutely fantastic.

Gameplay
Rock solid and very satisfying. The difficulty is just right, also the speed of gear progression is decent.
Tons of stuff to do in the game, I’d say it’s somewhat of a casual game, but you can still do hard core stuff with Raids.

Voice (VoIP)
Personally I have not encountered players using it, that said I also have not touched the hardcore stuff, and when I wanted to communicate I used the chat. Though chat does not seem to get used either.
(Disclaimer: this is during my 37 hours gaming and most of them where in campaign mode)


TL:DR Solid game definitely worth giving it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
