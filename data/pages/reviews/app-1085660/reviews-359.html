\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny has some of the best FPS mechanics, most satisfying weapons to shoot, and some great designs for enemies and locations.

The gameplay itself is passable, however it's constantly bogged down by formulaic, boring missions and even just strange timewasters that feel like fillers because they didn't make as much content as they thought they would.

Some examples are:
- Sit through a loading screen to press E on an NPC in the Tower hub world, then sit through a loading screen to go back to where you were.

- Bosses with health bars cut into three, which once you reach a new section of said bar, will make the boss disappear/become immune/open a door/etc...

- Run from A to B to C, whilst killing enemies that don't pose a threat.


Not to mention the clunky and sometimes even unresponsive jumping / floating / clambering can make some jumps a dice roll.

Eh, game is fun if you don't put to much thought into it.
But all things said, its still certainly the most engaging game in the MMO genre.

With multiple tens of hours playtime on both Destiny and Destiny 2.

Then came Curse of Osiris. It was a lackluster campaign, especially compared to it's commercials. But the game was good enough.

Then came the Warmind expansion. It was filled with a great story, short as it was. Some elements were incorperated which i didn't fully get but i had a good time.

Then came the downfall. Bungee had forsaken us with Destiny 2 Forsaken expansion. They reworked the RPG system entirely. Every ******* thing in the game became a grind fest. Then the game hit bedrock with it's Shadowkeep expansion, with even more grindfest systems. This was not the game i fell in love with all those years ago. The grind was so bad that I for the first time on the franchises history did not complete a campaign. The game had more content than ever but all i ever did was come back to the places where i have unlocked everything and not touching the new grindfest stuff.

Then the pressure of the downfall was to bad that it broke the bedrock and fell underneath it with the worst deal i have ever seen in gaming history. A true deal with the devil. The "expansion" deleted half of the game. My beloved Red War campaign, with it two small Osiris and Warmind expansions were upright removed from the game. All items which i have purchased twice! On both Xbox and PC. All my work, all the money i have put into the game, just one day a big ******* waste. In it place took even more grindfest.
