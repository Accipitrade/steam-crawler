\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is like a toxic relationship. You love it to death, but when you spend too much time on it, you get to see it's true colors.

Let me start off by saying, I highly recommend this game. HOWEVER, there are many things that tend to turn people away, even myself at times.

Pros:
Beautiful Design
Truly one of a kind
Great gameplay mechanics
Raids are a BLAST
Music never ceases to amaze me, always a 10/10
Phenomenal Community feedback compared to most companies.
You will get hooked.
Bungie has SO much potential for this game.
Decent new player experience (Although complicated when you get to the endgame)

Cons:
Buggy at times
The current seasonal model
PvP
Stasis.
Every weapon/armor you get will be useless in a couple seasons.
F2P experience is terrible.
Playing alone is rarely ever fun. (Get a clan!)

I've been playing Destiny 2 since it's release, and Destiny 1 since the Dark Below DLC.
I know what Bungie is capable of... it's just a matter of if they can make a fantastic DLC such as the Taken King in Destiny 1 or Forsaken.

Ratings of each current DLC:

Forsaken (2018) 9/10

Shadowkeep (2019) 5/10

Beyond Light (2020) 7/10

Witch Queen (2021) TBD

Lightfall (2022) TBD\t\t\t\t\t
