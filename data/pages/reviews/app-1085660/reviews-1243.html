\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis review has been updated as it has been one month after Lightfall's launch.

Should you play Destiny 2? Yes but also no. It's complicated.


If you are looking for a compelling story, i'm afraid to say it's not here. Witch Queen and the story after was pretty great, setting up so much excitement for Lightfall. It didn't pay off, It crashed and burned. So on that regard, no you shouldn't play Destiny for the story!


How about gameplay? Basically nothing but banger decisions. All light subclasses are great. Strand feels insanely good. Stasis ain't doing so hot, but i think Bungie knows that and are going to tune it up to fit the new mod system, which btw feels incredible. So in that case, yeah you should play Destiny!

Endgame content? It's uhhhh a bit of a mixed bag right now. New raid is fun, weapons are cool from it, but it's not hard in the slightest. If you can connect the dots, you can do the whole raid. Lot of older content has been pushed back into the spotlight so there's always something to do. And visiting old raids gives you high end gear from time to time! So yeah, i guess you should play destiny!

They also tried to introduce something called Guardian Ranks. Basically how knowledgeable a player is in the game. For a first attempt, you can see it was made with good intentions. Just didn't factor players who have /actually/ been playing a long time. So if you're a returning player after so long, you'll look as if you know as much as a new light who started five hours ago. Maybe that's true in some sense, but imagine it's not the case for 90% of returning guardians.


Overall, the game is in a better state, but with them always having a focus on story, it's a shame to see it do so poorly. If you're looking for a more compelling story game, look elsewhere. If you're looking for better gameplay, you found it! And with everything amazing being behind paywalls, maybe consider picking up something else.


Should you play Destiny 2? Yes, but no.\t\t\t\t\t
