\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very content-rich game, especially for a free one. The DLC model makes you feel like you are not really missing out on that much and by the time you do, it is probably worth buying them anyway.

The community is thriving at the moment and the queue times are somewhat short for most activities.

One of the biggest issues is definitely the quest system, Everything in this game works on an inventory/item system for some reason incuding quests, which are treated like items too (you´ll understand once you play it). Also, there is no hand-holding to the point where as a new player, you might come to the point, where you are overwhelmed by the options. The game promotes "choose your own adventure" playstyle, but sadly, as a new player who doesn´t know the options, creating such adventure might be a bit of a mess filled with confusion. But after few hours, you should get the gist of it and it wo´t be a problem anymore.

Also, if you are not a fan of PvP, you might find the fact, that a lot of quests involve participation in it pretty scary. Thankfully, lot of the PvP modes will grow on you and you might even start to enjoy them, so I´d like to encourage participation, because you might find them surprisingly fun in the end.\t\t\t\t\t
