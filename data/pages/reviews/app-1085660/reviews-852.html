\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is always a hit or miss kind of game but if youre looking for an semi mmo that isnt a monthly subscription or a very solid FPS this the game for you. It takes quite some time to figure out exactly what you are doing as a new player but if you have a group of friends the experience is not comparable.

Avengers - Flopped
Outriders - Flopped
Anthem - Flopped
The Division 1 & 2 - Flopped
Warframe - Not my favorite

All of these were labeled "Destiny Killers" but there honestly won't be, not for a long time. The gunplay in this game is unmatched in my experience. Through all of its issues the player base always speaks up and finds ways around bad monetization. Right now Destiny is in a very good position to hop in and catch up. There is plenty of content to do whether youre a returning D1 Vet or a New Light to D2. Some of the best Experiences from D1 are being added with one just being added days ago. There is so much about this game to praise but with it being Free-To-Play now there is zero reason to not try it.

Here comes the bad.

The dlc's like shadowkeep and forsaken should NOT BE $25 EACH. Especially if they are marked in game as Legacy content. Also with the seasonal content, making the deluxe edition a forced purchase for people who did not get to play the previous seasons of that year is very scummy in my opinion. With the last release of pvp and gambit maps being 600 days ago, there should be a reason the price tag is that high. If not bring it down to half. Also (Especially this season) locking cosmetics being microtransactions is fine, but the level its been taken recently is absurd. Complete the brand new raid for an opportunity to spend real money on a different color for your armor??? stupid. $15 for an emote that is themed off of said new raid instead of making it an earnable drop?? stupid. the forced monetization to feel like your character is allowed to look good is really predatory and must be properly addressed. Dont even start on transmog. again thought that is all optional just sucks to see it.

TLDR;
Get game, buy dlc on sale. Deluxe edition of Beyond Light required for best experience. Dont get sucked into eververse cosmetics. Game good.

Witch Queen Update;
Expensive but this game is so good\t\t\t\t\t
