Very Well Polished

Destiny 2 has had a rough road. Bungie has managed to smooth things out and have come out on top now that Activision is out of the picture. The game play is varied enough to keep you interested for a VERY long time. The PvP has SO many options and is fun, yet frustrating for me since I suck at PvP.

One of the best features of Destiny 2 now, is that it's FREE! Included with the free version is the original story missions, The Red War, and the first two expansions, The Curse of Osiris and Warmind. These will provide you with quite a few hours of story line game play. I enjoyed them very much. Now for the two paid expansions, Forsaken and Shadowkeep, they are well worth the price of admission. After playing through the free story content, you'll know at that point if you'd enjoy the paid expansions.

In a nutshell, Bungie has created a great FPS/RPG shoot and loot game with TONS of content. I look forward to the upcoming content.\t\t\t\t\t
