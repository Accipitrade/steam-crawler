\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game that spawned so many business deals for Bungo.

This game and its success has lead to Bungie changing their game motto "We make games we want to play" to "games made fast" and "don't over deliver."

It's been said that this game gets a lot of criticisms for its under-delivery and price gouging its community/player base. While this could be stated as an opinion on my part; I want to leave a review on Bungie's business practices in terms of Destiny 2. Their servers are very old... they are tailored to a small player-base and whenever you get a few thousand people playing, well... expect to see a queue to get in to play OR the game just being down altogether. There are many KNOWN server issues and many MANY videos floating around describing what they found when "poking" said servers. I believe the most common saying is that the servers are being "held together with hopes and dreams...and some duct tape." Any correspondence from Bungie and their server-side team on their website was from about a year ago... and any updates are done mainly through twitter. (Cause everyone uses twitter, right?)

Any DLC you purchase can be legally wiped and ripped away by Bungie's VAULTING clause in the agreement you sign to play. Same for cosmetics and alike...

This is a very fun game for first time players...You just need a youtuber's help to understand what is happening. I recommend "MyNameIsByfHere". The game is heavily tailored to the current fan-base and anyone new who joins in is just left to fend for themselves.


In short: Fun game. Bad business practices in terms of the life of the game. Company actively wants more money and urges to make less content. Company bashes playerbase who pleads with said company to hear their concerns. Game is not new-user friendly. Servers are likely down when you wish to try the game; issues are known and aren't fixed..mainly just patched.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter The Witch Queen I was hoping for a great dlc and had so much hope for it! it seemed like Bungie was BACK at it! but I hate to say it but this was so bad and I wonder what happened?!

The good is that the design team and art looks nice, the option to have the legendary story mode is great! a new zone was exciting, new open world event is fun, exotics are nice, changes to difficulty is nice, new characters finally but holy cow...

These characters aren't enjoyable or likeable at all, Typical hero vs bad guy surface level stuff and they all lack charisma, depth, good humour, they aren't likeable enough.

-Im getting some strange 80's cheesy vibes with this dlc and not a very focused art-style in this expansion like the Witch queen had a more focused design going on and theme.

- Still fighting the same enemies as the years before, there are some mini-bosses that looks different but that's about it, its getting old.

- strand is powerful but does not feel powerful it feel like a strange spider-man ability that you are forced to use rather then want to use.

- Feels like Nimbus was trying to be the new Cayde 6 funny guy but isn't funny nor likeable the voice acting is very weak in this expansion and I zoned out.

- Story line is very weak, people die without even knowing who they are or why we should care, feels rushed and has no depth, very surface level stuff here.

- New area feels empty and lacks life and backstory / depth, no feeling of mystery here, having static hologram Npc's you cant talk to or interact with isn't helping that either.

- I missed some clever mechanics and unique gameplay designs or clever puzzles in the story mode, mostly shoot and move on until you kill the boss, creativity is lacking.
