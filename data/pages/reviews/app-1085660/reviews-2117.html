\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 can be quite fun, but with some caveats: this game asks for you to routinely invest large amounts of time in order to obtain a great many of its weapons and gear; and that routine will regularly push you into using a slightly random subset of your available equipment, meaning some of the cooler things that you worked so hard to acquire might not be practical to use in the current season's activities or for your tasks for a given day.

For example, progressing in the current season's goal of upgrading bunkers requires you to clean them out first, and three enemies in the bunker can only be beaten by a slightly random pair of the following weapon types: hand cannons, sidearms, sub-machine guns, auto rifles, and swords. Many of your other daily tasks and some of your weekly missions will also require you to use these weapons. You will have to take frequent, prolonged breaks from playing your favorite way if you prefer using bows, fusion rifles, scout rifles, pulse rifles, linear fusion rifles, trace rifles, sniper rifles, rocket launchers, grenade launchers, breech-loaded grenade launchers, or shotguns ... although sometimes these weapons are needed for daily tasks that don't involve the special enemies. The composition of those lists only changes approximately once every three months, at which point some of the weapons you fell in love with in the current season will no longer be the ones that can fight those special enemies. If you can live with this, the game is a lot of fun! You will probably find more satisfying weapons than you can regularly use anyway.\t\t\t\t\t
