Updated version for 2022-2023

My most favourite game ever hands down and been playing Bungie's games since the Original Halo: Combat Evolved and Destiny!


Playing it since
15th of September 2018 (Recently moved to PC Platform)

Is it worth the price?
Yes. This only applies to the DLC as the base game is free to play.

Total playtime
1,954 hours (Counting my console hours)

Score
8 out of 10

The Good
- Multi-platform, is available for PC, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, PS4 and PS5;
- Amazing weapons and gear to chase;
- Plenty of game modes in PVP;
- A plethora of ways to costumize your character;
- Decent graphics;
- Armour Transmog;
- Steam Achievements;
- Downloadable Contents (DLCs).
- Each Exotic is unique;
- Exclusive gear in unique game modes;(Ex. Trails of Osiris)
- Bungie is no longer under Activision;

The Neutral
- Ported game;
- In-game chat;
- No vote system;
- Seasonal content;

The Bad
- Small player base(PC);
- The season silver bundles;
- Re-Skins of Vanguard, Crucible, Gambit, and Iron Banner;
- FPS drops since ported to Steam;
- Lack of Content;
- Most cosmetics locked behind a paywall;
- Slow matchmaking;
- Several annoying bugs and glitches that never got fixed.
- Paying for dungeon activities

Notes/Extra comments
Destiny 2 is far from perfect but it is a good game! However, the thing that bugs me the most is the re-skins. Bungie should go back to their ideas from Destiny 1, adding faction-specific armour instead of re-skins for the Vanguard, Gambit, and Crucible. Re-add the ornaments from Destiny 1's year of triumph back into their raid remasters in Destiny 2. Do not add them to the Eververse store. Taking the time to make something great should be prioritized over rushing. Your player base will thank you for not making more and more re-skins.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts ok I guess.

Ok, now for the actual review now that I hit 1k hours. This game offers you a comprehensive view into a future in which humanity controlled the solar system, and fell from grace. It offers to the player hundreds of puzzles to solve, challenges to complete, bosses to defeat, and overall amazing gunplay in my opinion. There is a level of complexity and thought that was put into the sytem of perks, modifications, and builds that can be added and created to and with guns and armor within this game that is simply unmatched by any other title in the gaming industry. If you are looking for a game that will give you an amazing story to follow, an exciting weapon and armor system to use to your heart's content, and a caring, INSANELY dedicated community to the game and to helping one another out, then Destiny 2 is the game for you to play. There may be some downsides in the eyes of the community such as armor and weapon sunsetting (basically a decrease in the armor or weapon's usefulness) or things such as cheating in PvP modes, but in the end, all of this is what makes Destiny 2, well, Destiny 2. This game will make you not only fall in love with the idea of space exploration, but it will show you the true power and potential of open world games, and provide to you the passion and love for a game series that can only be found in a few other legendary titles. I hope this helped you get a quick view of the game and what it might be like, and I hope that most of all, I'll get you hooked on this game as much as I am. :)\t\t\t\t\t
