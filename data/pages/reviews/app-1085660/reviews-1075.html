\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've really enjoyed the atmosphere and thematic direction Beyond Light is developing. So far the story is definitely still less engrossing and emotionally impactful than Forsaken, but that's gonna be hard to beat; Cayde-6's telling of his relationships with others in the recordings and his introspection in the lore books, The Man They Called Cayde, are some of the best storytelling elements I've experienced in a game to date. But it feels like we could be leading somewhere nearly as powerful. And I've really enjoyed some of the lore/plot surprises this season! Although, because of leaks and speculation one of the reveals was less surprising and more, oh yeah, cool.

The Deep Stone Crypt raid was excellent, and personally sits among my all time favorite raid experiences. Again, the atmosphere and music here was really on point.

Overall Bungie has made some strides in crafting a really fun and enthralling gaming experience, and I believe they've done a better job at cleaning up the new player experience as well.

One quality of life feature that I and many others are still waiting for however, and which Bungie seems unyielding in refusing us, is an in game LFG system. LFGing still remains a minor annoyance at the best times and a real clusterfk at the worst.\t\t\t\t\t
