\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe story is beautiful. Fully enjoyed the story/campaign mode. Beautiful graphics.
When you have completed the story, you can enjoy playing freely throughout what you have already played, through either missions, heroic adventures and other, or just roam around.
I love the PvE of this game. Much to enjoy. Much to collect.

I absolutely hate the PvP of this game, though not exclusively to this game. Online PvP in general.
This is the lousy side of this game. The developers thought PvP could be included in a PvE game, and forces players to participate in PvP through quests, item quest or item upgrade side quest.

PvE is a liniar game type. You will gradually be able to overcome more and more, grow in experience and skill.
PvP is non-liniar, you can be lucky and play against people who are worse than you, in PvP, but then you will make progress and will compete against others with probably more skill than you, and more knowledge of PvP maps, better equipment, better weapons, better addons on equipments. You might even be unlucky and be stuck against PvP nerds from the beginning.
By PvP nerds, I mean the people who engross themselves in the PvP aspect of every game and you will likely never beat them.
It's very random and not fun, especially if you have quests, item quests that needs PvP, it might halt your progression and even detract from your progression.

I'd wish that Bungie would make PvP only about getting additional materials, not a requirement for anything at all.
It would make PvP more enjoyable, because you didn't really need to be in PvP, but choose to play it.

Eleminate the frustration of PvP progression, please.
Until PvP have been removed as a requirement for anything, I will not recommend this game.
It just frustrating needing to play something(PvP), that you don't enjoy.\t\t\t\t\t
