\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would recommend this game, with some reservations.
1st: If you've never played Destiny or Destiny 2 before, it's gonna be very confusing and I recommend you make use of the endless info on the internet to make sense of things because God knows the game won't tell you.
2nd: If you don't have friends to play the game with, it's gonna be a pretty boring, grindy crawl. Especially if you play fresh start free-to-play. Calling it "free-to-play" isn't the way to put it. playing free seems more like a demo than a real free experience. There's not a ton that can be offered to you without having to pay for expansions. Plus there's seasonal loot that can only be accessed with a paid season pass. You still get loot, just not near the amount you would if you pay for it.
I've played these games since the beta was launched for Destiny 1, I've loved and hated it ever since. There's a lot Bungie has done that I've praised and put up with. I think its a fun and addicting experience, but its a very hard sell for someone who didn't play at the very start. I'd say give it a try, but it's definitely not for everyone.\t\t\t\t\t
