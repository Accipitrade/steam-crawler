\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI loved this game to death. I put in 400 hours on Xbox One, 200 hours more on Battle.net, and then the rest here on steam. I then stopped playing for a bit to wait for the next dlc to drop. When I came back after about 4 months I wanted to replay the original campaign on a new character. I created a new character, played through the "new" intro mission and quickly realized that I was not playing the "Red War Campaign" I then googled how to play that again only to realize that it is no longer possible to play it. Bungie Removed the content that I payed for on multiple platforms without giving the players any form of compensation for it. Just a big F You now buy more dlc!!! I loved both the original Destiny and Destiny 2 up until they realized they can make more money by removing old content that people paid for and replacing it with new content that they have to pay for again. This was the nail in the coffin of what used to be a great time sink if you liked the grind. Now all the gear and exotics that I was in the process of getting from the raids are all gone.

After dedicating countless hours of my life to have things ripped away like this. Not even EA is that bad. If you pay for EA's micro-transaction BS then you get the items and guess what you wont ever lose access to them. You pay for a call of duty Campaign from Activision? You get to keep the campaign even after the game loses support at the end of the year when the next copy paste game comes out. So can someone explain why if all these other companies that are horribly greedy can let you keep your content after buying it, Bungie can get away with removing story campaign that people paid for??

Overall impression of the Game: F off Bungie. You wont see another dime of my money again.\t\t\t\t\t
