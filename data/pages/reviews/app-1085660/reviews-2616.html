\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou'll read a lot about how bad Destiny 2 is. I don't care about that, what I do care about is nailing buttery head shots with some of the most rewarding game play of any modern shooter that I've ever played. As they iterate on the game, there is constantly more to do, one more bounty to finish, one more exotic quest to finish. There are definitely longer term goals if you're more casual with the game which I'd say that I am.

So through casual lenses, there is always something to do. You can complete the lions share of the game without match making. If you want a taste of the co-op and pvp modes you can jump into Crucible, Gambit or just run the strike playlist. If you nolife this game, you will run out of content and probably end up in the salt mine that is the Destiny 2 subreddit. I do appreciate their passion for Destiny 2 but as a filthy casual I think Destiny 2 is a great game worthy of a play through. This was true even before it was free to play.

If you're into shooters, you should have this installed.\t\t\t\t\t
