\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm gonna start this off by saying I used to give this game a lot of ♥♥♥♥. The first one as well, as I was under the cult of WarFrame. I didn't play it, I just looked from the outside in and screamed "just play warframe."

As you may be able to tell, the tables have turned. DE (Warframe developer) have shown that they do not care in the slightest bit for their veterans, and prioritize shadows over actual continuing story content. I have since abandoned the game and its overly toxic community, and decided to give Destiny 2 a shot.

Boy, did I just make the best decision in my gaming lifetime.

I spawned in at the cosmodrome after building my warlock and proceeded to foam at the mouth over how beautiful the game looked on my RX 570. I then continued to grab the assault rifle and kill some Fallen. Just like what I remember from watching Datto start Destiny 1 a few years ago. Fast forward a few hours, I run some strikes, start and speed run the Red War, Warmind, and Forsaken. First thing I can say is that the artistic development team at Bungie are gods in my eyes. They know how to take concept art and port it into the game. My favorite vista is that of the Dreaming City and the Awoken Watchtower, with Rasputin and his guns being a second. The Taken and the Shattered Throne is awesome, and I can't wait for Beyond Light. I question why Bungie doesn't let you reload while sprinting as a default, but that's something minor. If you like grinding for rewards, play Destiny.\t\t\t\t\t
