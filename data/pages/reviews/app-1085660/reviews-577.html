\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLots of highs and lows with this franchise since 2014, but I do recommend this game IF you pick it up with the DLC and have someone to play through with you, since currently the new player experience is pretty underwhelming.
For existing players the game is rarely bad, the devs just make questionable choices at times.

The story is currently in a really good place, a lot different than how it was in Destiny 1 and the Seasons model lets you feel like the story is evolving over time, the only downside being when we have delays like right now (the wait for Witch Queen), the story gets placed on a long hiatus.

This game also has some of the best feeling gunplay out there and its pinnacle PvE activities (Raids specifically) are great pieces of content that people find themselves running multiple times over. I don't play Master/Grandmaster content so I can't comment on those.

The PvP side I haven't felt joy in since Destiny 1.

Gambit needs a complete rework to be worth any kind of time investment, but they have mentioned they're working on it.

Solid 8/10 game, I don't have Stockholm Syndrome\t\t\t\t\t
