\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have 266 hours on steam, and I do not know how many hours on Battle.net, I think I started playing a few months after Warmind was released.

Destiny 2 gets a passing grade in my book. It is a great game with a captivating lore and even if the campaigns do not always do service to the rich lore of the Destiny universe.

There is still room for improvement when it comes to balancing the crucible (pvp) and gambit. As a solo player, I feel like when I am against a stacked team, I lose more than when i am paired with one a stacked team. Almost as if i am often paired with stacks who aren't necessarily good.

When it comes to pve, especially the challenging content, there isn't too much room for variety because certain loadouts and mods are required. Now that is not necessarily bad but I think this system needs improvements. For example, I'd love to use a pulse rifle in pve, but the much needed mods don't work on pulses so more often than not, we are stuck with hand cannons, smg or auto rifles. It is not a problem if you enjoy them or if like me, you grinded enough to acquire nice weapons (a god roll kindled orchid or a recluse so you can use Isanagy as primary) in that category but I imagine that it would be frustrating if you do not have them. Also many exotics are excluded from pve activities because they can't be equipped with mods.

I do not know what the right solution would be for this, because I can see why Bungie might have wanted to implement those changes (maybe it is because exotics and pulses were too good and now other weapons have a chances to shine, or maybe those changes will come later with new mods that better fit pulse rifles and side arms) but I think it is worth looking into.

Moving on to a different form of feedback, one which I believe Bungie has admitted, I'd like to talk about the apparent lack of world building. I like the fact that Ikora has been building her vex gate since Shadowkeep came out and we can see weekly progress. That is good, but my point is that despite all their powers, it seems guardians spend all their time collecting loot in the form of weapons and armor that they hoard in the vault. I want to see significant progress in the world indicating the gains or achievement or guardians. I want them to reclaim parts of the earth or solar system, restore them and keep them. (Rasputin and Ana Bray on Mars are a good example)

That's it, my rant is over, I am sure you have read or heard parts of it before, but I suppose it does not hurt for me to post my review here\t\t\t\t\t
