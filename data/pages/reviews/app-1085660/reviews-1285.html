\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll in all, I do really enjoy Destiny. With that being said, this game is not very friendly with new players or with more casual players.
For one, even though the game is "free to play" there is barely anything to do without buying the DLC, which is $100+ for every DLC available right now. You can try the game to see if you like it for free, but what you can do is not good representation of the game as a whole (raids, etc. are not available afaik).
It also takes dozens of hours of grinding each season doing almost the same thing to be a high enough level to do high-end activities. I played something like 50 hours in the 10 days before the newest raid came out, and I still wasn't strong enough to do the raid for at least the first few days. This game is a slog at times, so be warned.
However, I love this game for different reasons. The story building, visual effects, and the game's communities are incredible. There's not another game that I've ever played that is able to emulate Destiny or even really come close.\t\t\t\t\t
