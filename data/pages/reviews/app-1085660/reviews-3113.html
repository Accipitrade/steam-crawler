\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe story overall is very shallow and generic... same missions over and over with different context... intro, shoot, something goes wrong, shoot, kill boss... final boss is very easy and underwhelming in every expansion...

But, if your looking into this game then you must be looking for the rich endgame with a steep grind to the top, with many different raids and dungeons to test your teamwork skills, or possible the competitive and fast paced Player v Player that puts you on high alert at all time, thanks to a plethora of viable load outs.

Note: The raids are easier compared to D1, but if your new to MMO's then it should kill time
enjoyably for awhile.

As a World of Warcraft player, this game sort of serves as a filler for when i am waiting for new content to come out, as D2 receives a multitude of new content updates, events, and armory stuff to sink your teeth into.

Overall, this is for the more casual MMO player who still wants the availability of a grind and deep endgame, but doesn't necessarily want to be the top strategist with 2000 hours spent on teamspeak yelling many welps even side... all day (Idk if you get that reference).

Raid Aesthethics: 10/10
Fun Factor: 10/10
