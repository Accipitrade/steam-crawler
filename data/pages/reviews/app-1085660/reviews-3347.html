\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs of writing this review I've clocked in 247 hours from Oct 6th to Oct 26th and I definitely recommend Destiny 2.

Pros :
-Coop Looter Shooter, you can go play solo but not all fights can be done alone
-Fantastic PvP, that's coming from someone who's trash in PvP btw
-Introductory Quests, I felt this should be noted because the quests that were given to New Light players (you who didn't buy the game) helped a lot for anyone to understand what the game has to offer.
-Difficult but Fun Raids, takes a bit of trial and error when you go in blind but it's a lot of fun with friends.

Cons :
-Loot Grind, to put it simply getting the mythical gear called a "God Roll" is no easy task and this game does want you to play it for only THAT particular gear which has perfectly rolled perks but you can easily just slack off on hitting higher levels once you hit 900 (softcap) since you can easily do the leveling process after 900 through weekly challenges.
-OP Playstyle in PvP, if you haven't checked any videos about One-Eyed Mask then you should it's the only thing that makes the PvP aspect of the game not fun because you have no way of countering a single man with twice your health with or without him using the top tier PvP weapons.
-Grindy Questlines, there are quests that provide gear as a reward for completing a questline but there are some that are quite tedious to do or rather requires a lot of effort to pull off in order for you to accomplish it.

If you plan on continue playing Destiny 2 :
-I do recommend having a feel for what the game has to offer first before you swipe your credit card for Shadowkeep and Forsaken (DLC Content) because Destiny 2 : New Light already provides you almost everything the game has to offer. So clock in a few dozen if not a hundred hours in my opinion checking everything that game has to offer you and buy the DLCs when you think it's the game for you.

9/10 would Yeetus Deletus more people in Crucible and from the mighty words of Calus.

"Take them, and grow fat from strength."



So yes 9/10 would recommend you play it
