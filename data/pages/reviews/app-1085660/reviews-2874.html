\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo... Destiny 2...

I've never seen a game with such an up and down public image. Seems like with each expansion and season the mood shifts. Underneath those turbulent opinions there's a core to this game that is truly special. The gunplay, world-building, achievements, collecting, and RPG elements come together in a unique blend that makes Destiny enjoyable to a variety of tastes.

+ Interesting and visually spectacular world.
+ Great gunplay in both PVP and PVE
+ RPG sub-classes for unique play-styles
+ You like collecting? Don't play this game, you might never stop.
+ New content several times a year
+ An intriguing lore
+ A great community
- Lore isn't very present in normal gameplay (hidden in lore books, grimoires, etc)
- Managing hundreds of pieces of armor and weapons to try and find the best combinations (limited vault space)
- Only a few "types" of enemies with a lot of re-skins
- The grinds don't respect your time (pinnacle weapons / undying title)
- What the hell is this monetization scheme? Paid expansions, paid season pass, and crazy expensive "micro"-transactions for cosmetics. ($10-$15 for a set of armor for a single character type. Note that you CAN get this armor through amassing in-game currency and being very patient)

Destiny 2 gets it right in a lot of areas. Bungie seems committed to making it great and it's got a great foundation.

Give it a try. Play the Red War campaign. It might become your favorite game. It sure did become mine.

9/10 - almost there\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm conflicted if i can recommend it or not. I wish the game was really good and it is slowly getting better with each season, but it's not quite there yet. If you buy the expansions you get a lot to do for a good while. But be careful, you can easily burn out due to repetitiveness. also DON'T WASTE MONEY TO BUY SILVER FOR THE CASH SHOP.

Movement is great, Gunplay amazing, abilities make you feel powerful. PvE is great, despite the community getting shafted from constant nerfs when they find a way to crank up the damage numbers. Get some friends together and run Stikes/nighfalls/dungeons/raids. It's challenging and rewarding.

PvP can be fun at times, but mostly it's frustrating. Stasis subclass can freeze players, Handcanons are always meta, Aim Assist and Bullet Magnetism ruin the skill-based aspect of the game, they're too strong.

Content gets repetitive fast, especially since they effectively removed half the game in a content vault while drip-feeding players the seasonal content. Emotes, Finisher moves, Skins and other cosmetics are mostly locked behind the cash shop.

F2Ps get access to the core playlist activities, the open areas and some other things, the game opens up after buying expansions AND season passes. Earlier seasons were utter garbage, lately they improved a bit. Maybe they will get it right one day.

I recommend try out the F2P part for a bit and see for yourself. If you wanna buy more access, wait for a sale, or Until the next Expansion comes out so the older Expansions get combined into a cheap bundle. This game demands your time and/or your money.\t\t\t\t\t
