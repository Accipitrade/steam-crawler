\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI joined once Destiny 2 released as free to play. I figured I'd check it out and give it some time casually. At the time of writing, I've purchased both DLCs and a Season Pass. This game is a blast.

Pros:
* Tons of content. I have played 80+ hours now and feel like I've only scratched the surface.
* Large variety of content. Some of the PVP/PVE modes feel like they could be full games as is.
* Meaningful sense of progress. The leveling system is a bit tricky to understand at first, but it is just enough of a grind/reward ratio to make it worth it.
* Immersive worlds and interesting varied enemies. The different planets truly have unique themes and the alien races that are warring on them truly leave their characteristic mark on the worlds.

Cons:
* Large learning curve. You don't need a lot to get up and running and start shooting, but there is a lot that is not explained. The way quests and bounties work, the way that resource gathering, lost sectors, public events work is all not obvious.
* Story is lacking. There is some story that adds to it, but make no mistake, this is not a story focused game. You can really play hours and hours without having any idea of the story.\t\t\t\t\t
