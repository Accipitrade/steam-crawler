\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI suppose there is a lot both good and bad that could be said about Destiny 2.

First I'm a casual player. I originally started playing when I got the basic game under the Blizzard app. I had been looking for a new (to me) first person shooter that was not requisite of PvP. Destiny 2 filled that quite nicely. So I paid for my upgrades and enjoyed the game for the most part.

I would tend to say I have few major gripes with the game. For one I have a strong dislike of PvP so the Gambit and Crucible are things I really don't like even with some of the nice weapon quests I could finish if I ever played them. I don't mind some of the quests that have me all over the place or even some long routes to complete, but the PvP is a stop for me.

My other gripe is jumping. When it comes to a quest with a lot of technical jumping such as a lot of the Acsended challenges or the Whispered quest just made me give up on those types of quests.

Still for the most part the game is still fun to play.\t\t\t\t\t
