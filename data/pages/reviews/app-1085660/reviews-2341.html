\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet get the Bad out of the way:
PvP is a roaring dumpster fire. Sure it can be fun to watch and even throw some trash into it, but it's still not an overall great time.
You play for a couple of months(let alone years) and you are always wondering where more vault space is...
The fact that older content is near useless to leveled players is sad and needs to be fixed.

The Medium:
The PvE has a lot of great content for newcomers except for the fact that there aren't any real sign posts to help them through it. Bungie just kinda throws everything at you at once and says "have fun", and the goes and gets a beer.
The Grind is a lot better and more forgiving than what it used to be.

The Great:
The gameplay is at the top of it's game.
The graphics are very nice and even a mid tier laptop makes it look great at 60+fps.

This is a game that will keep you happy if you don't take it seriously. If you aren't good at something, just don't play it or practice and stay patient with yourself and other players. This series has made me laugh, cry, want to punch small children, rage, and right back to laughing and enjoying myself.
It may say I only have a couple hundred hours in it, but I switch on and off between Xbox and PC.
All together I have over 3500 hours in the series. WastedonDestiny.com\t\t\t\t\t
