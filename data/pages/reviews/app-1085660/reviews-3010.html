\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs many others have said, the game has no sense of progression anymore. Everything is unlocked, and all there is to do is try and get your power level higher.

PvE is a lot of fun, especially gambits in my opinion. Would recommend PvE aspects of this game to my friends.

PvP is a different story. Matchmaking and Spawn points are absolutely horrendous. If you don't have a fireteam to play with I do not recommend PvP at all. A lot of the time, you get thrown on a trash team, and the opponent team just decimates you and your team (unless of course you're competitive at the game and can kill the entire enemy team by yourself). A lot of the maps are a bit too big in my opinion, you'll spawn by yourself on the other half of the map, away from your team, and other times you'll spawn in front of an enemy's line of sight, and be dead before you can move. On some occasions, matchmaking will place you in a match that has already started (I've been put in one 20 seconds in, and another less than a minute before completion) but they won't give you any super energy to scale with the time that has passed. So more often than not, I spawn in and get decimated by a bunch of supers in a row, with no hope to retaliate with heavy weapons or a super of my own. It is possible to take down a super without those things, but with the guard some supers have, and the damage supers do, more often than not, if you're alone (which is usually what happens in my case) you'll just get trampled. Also, 90% of your deaths in this game will be from special weapons(Sniper Rifles and Shotguns), heavy weapons (Rocket Launchers and Grenade Launchers), grenades and supers when fighting 6v6 matches.

Long story short, if you use matchmaking, don't expect any actual teamwork, or a team decent enough to cover your back.

I recommended because it is free.\t\t\t\t\t
