\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdit: So now, over 2000 hours later, I STILL LOVE THIS GAME. So much. Love the story & lore, the combat mechanics, the weapons, the environments, the characters, the story - oh man, the story - basically everything about it except that it's so hard to get to end-game content unless you've got a larger clan or a bunch of friends who play. Even without raids it's still awesome.

Original review:
I never played the first Destiny (was there even a Destiny 1? Is it included in 2?), I started with Destiny 2 Forsaken, and I honestly don't understand the story or have any idea what I'm supposed to be doing most of the time or how to find a "clan" or do a dungeon or anything. That does not hinder my enjoyment of this game! I love the voice acting - Gina Torres is my favorite forever, and Nathan Fillion is lots of fun as Cayde -- and once I discovered I could punch aliens in the face, -and- find an awesome sniper rifle and snipe, -and- have running battles against aliens fighting alongside random other players, -and- do interesting single-player quests -AND- get a cool speeder bike to ride? Oh yeah, this game has something for almost any mood I'm in, even if I don't understand it.

That said, I do plan to figure it out, 'cause I would like to know what I'm doing. But right now I can just pick a spot, drop in, and start fighting aliens. Honestly, punching aliens in the face. What's not to love?\t\t\t\t\t
