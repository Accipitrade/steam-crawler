My thorough Review of "Destiny 2"

Howdy Gamer,

So, I've spent a good thousand hours on this game.
You may ask yourself one or more question from the following:

Do you have a Life?
- Absolutely not.

Who hurt you?
- A lot of people, don't worry.

Was it atleast fun?
- for the most part, no.

Would you consider this to be torture?
- Absolutely some form of Torture in Korea.

HOWEVER.

I do not play this game to constantly get my Gear up and grind guns to hop into the Crucible with it.

"What do you do then?"
Good thing you are asking, random reader of this stupid Review.

I do Raids with Friends whenever they are available. The game (In my opinion) atleast, is only really fun and rewarding when doing raids. There is no better feeling than finally killing the boss after hours of trying with your Friends.

Do you really have Friends?
- I do not. Maybe like 2.

I did somewhat enjoy playing this game though.

~Gamer
