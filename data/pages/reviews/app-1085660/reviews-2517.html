\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThousands of hours here btw (PS4 to Steam).

After playing nearly every day and every single week since launch in 2017, I have lived the full Destiny 2 experience. I lived through the broken sandboxes.... though the whisper meta.... through the lack of content in year 1 expansions. But, through the lows and the highs, I have felt that Destiny 2 has delivered an experience that trumps every other game I have owned in the last decade. I have bought 40 or so games since Q4 of 2017 and I find myself always loosing interest in them and coming back to Destiny. The gameplay is just.... amazing. The lore is awesome (check out YT vids). The PvP is.... not my favorite, but its better executed than any other game out there that I have played.

Now that this game is free to play, you are truly doing yourself a disservice by not downloading and trying out this game for yourself. Destiny 2 is an amazing game with an amazing community. Not too sure what else to say other than try it out yourself.

I'm a big fan of the looter-shooter genre and frankly... Destiny just smashes the competition.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMost time-consumingest game ever.

The game is great, however there's only one thing: it's not for casuals AT ALL. It's got great eye candy, great optimization, loading times are a tad long (even with an SSD). The rest is pretty much cookie-cutter MMOFPS with skins and all. The monetization is subtle through the Eververse kiosk and you don't need to spend a dime if you don't want to.

I wouldn't recommend it for lone wolves like me, you really need a clan and buddies to have the most fun in this game. Since Shadowkeep, it seems Bungie doesn't really feel like explaining anything anymore. You end up accepting quests and running around the world to try and fulfilll them. Raids and strikes can be challenging and coordination is key.

Lore is nice, but can be overwhelming for those who don't really care about that. This game is worth it and characters are pretty engaging. I don't get the "Free to play" trend, they should just make a third opus at this point, with even better graphics and a brand new storyline.\t\t\t\t\t
