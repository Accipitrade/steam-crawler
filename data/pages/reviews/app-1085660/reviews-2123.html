\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 was a great game with the key word being was. This isn’t because of my personal preference. The game originally started offering a rewarding experience for those who chose to grind weapons and gear. This gave players the ability to change how they played and what value they held with their teammates in Crucible. It also helped Players find ways to utilize other players who may not be as great at the game.

The reason I rated it 2 stars is because somewhere in the mix they lost a lot of credibility for the amazing gameplay they once had. This was because Bungie as a company wanted to appease all players of the game so in doing so players who pride themselves at having accuracy and great strategy can now be easily defeated by players who can not aim or play the game properly making it fun for the vast majority only. Before they made any of these changes players who were worse could still enjoy the game however they would have to play according to their play style. They wanted to make a truly amazing experience for everyone that played so they could expand their player base. The problem with this is it takes away from those who truly try to be great at the game. Skill and accuracy no longer mean anything, and now all players can adjust their weapons and armor to mask their inability to play the game the way it was originally intended. This way everyone seems to play the same instead of showing any diversity during battle. Bungie has not learned from the mistakes of their past with games such as Halo where they did the exact same things. Where Halo moved on to 343 who seemed to bring back the original feel of the game, Destiny is still very much owned by Bungie who will soak up any profit they can before they move on from that project as well.

True gamers will always look for rewarding experiences. If they can’t find it in a franchise that they were loyal to they will move on to greater and better games. Betraying your original fan base has and will destroy the game, just like it does with movies when hardcore fans feel betrayed.\t\t\t\t\t
