\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I've enjoyed D2 since launch, only now does it really feel like they've found the right balance between Destiny 1 callbacks and new ideas. The new style of progression they've introduced with Shadowkeep and New Light feels more organic and reminiscent of D1, and the new introduction has renewed my hope that Bungie will merge the majority of D1 content into D2 eventually. This game has some of the most fun combat I've ever played, and with the new Armor 2.0 system, the loot grind is top-tier. For those worried that going Free-to-play will hurt the experience, so far microtransactions remain purely cosmetic, and new expansions are still paid-for so it's not Bungie's only source of revenue. So far as I've seen, the new content added with Shadowkeep is top-tier, and making character levels into an account-wide seasonal grind is a stroke of genius. I feel free to play across all character classes without sacrificing potential levels.

In summary, the game wonderful. Alone, with friends, or with randoms, there's fun to be had for near indefinite amounts of time. Give it a shot. It's free.\t\t\t\t\t
