I really wanted to play the original Destiny but never got around to it. When Destiny 2 went free-to-play I was so excited. But for some reason I didn't start playing it straight away. Now I've been playing since about October/November last year and I have to say, I love it! It's got flaws, but honestly, it's all I'm playing at the moment. It's just the kind of game that I love. The main downsides are there's not enough hours in the day to do everything I want to in game... but seriously, the seasons are a little weak, "good" bugs get fixed pretty quickly but "bad" bugs linger and linger.
