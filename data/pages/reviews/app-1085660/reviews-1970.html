\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis review is towards those who are wondering if you should try the game. Not for seasoned players who played the game before it came to Steam.
For new players this game got lots to offer. I mean really lots to offer. I have played 60 hour and still have plenty of content left I havent even touched. And I havent even started the end game grind that I expect to come down the road. Sure som quest I have done have been some what grindy but still it havent taken lots of time to get it done. The game also runs very good with no crashes, frame drops or anything else encoutnered so far.
And since the game is free there is no reaason not to try this. Even if you dont like looter shooters there is a nice campaign that you can just play through for free.
The game is also not very difficult so you do not need to be a hard core fps player to be able to do well in this one.
But is all peachy? no there are some things that are not very good from a newbies pov. First of all you will be flooded with quests and the quest lsit is incredible bad. Its almost impossible to keep track of every quest and you will spend lots of time trying to find quests that can be done at the same time. Also the description in many quests are lacking and for a new player you will often find yourself on google to undertsand what the quest wants you to do. Fortunetely you will get the hang g it after a while and since the game has been out for a long time you will always find the info you need. But still, the quest list should be reworked and have better sorting options. Like sort by game mode, by location etc.
Also the lack of tutorials make it difficult to understand every aspect of the game. After 60 hours I still find myself googling game mechanisms that are not explained well inside the game.
Note that some quests will force you to play game modes you may not want to. For example you will be forced to do both PVE and PVP modes if youy want to complete all quests given. Of course you can just ignore those you do not like since the reward is often gear or weapons you may not use anyways.
As fot the paid content I would recommend to not buy those at full price. Wait for a sale and then buy if you find the base game good enough leaving you wanting more.

In the end I think this game will give new players over 100 hours of content before reaching the repetitive end game that many seasoned players are less happy for. So why not give it a try. It wont cost you anything but some time and maybe it will give you months of fun.\t\t\t\t\t
