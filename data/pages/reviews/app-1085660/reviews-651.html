\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny has always been a really cool concept. Take the foundations from the MMO and FPS genres and mix them together into an FPS Diablo clone.

Destiny 2 had a rough start being perpetuated by activisions crappy leadership, and the monetization of the content is not ideal, However, if you bite the bullet and pay them for all the content, what you get in return is one of the deepest and most rewarding FPS games out there.

Mechanically the game has so much to offer. Multiple sub classes for 3 base classes, with thousands of permutations with gear and mod augmentation. If you like to min max, this game rewards you heavily.

If you've never experienced doing a raid in Destiny, and you love coop experiences, you are doing it wrong.
The raids are the crown gems of the Destiny throne, and seriously, go watch someone doing one. Its ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ wild. Communication, trial and error, working through puzzles, and slaying space gods with your friends. It's really very fun.

PVP is in better shape today than it has been for a while. Trials of Osiris provides a fun endgame weekend competitive tournament for high level guardians to get good gear. 3v3 elimination modes really shine in Destiny 2. The 6v6 game modes are a bit more of a cluster ♥♥♥♥, but can be fun in their own way.

I do get it, its an expensive ass game. No doubt about that. To get all the content, you gotta buy some 80 dollar legacy edition or something (please make sure you buy the correct version with all the content, which may not be the most expensive one mind you). But if you do spend the money, its safe to say the value is there. These guys are the only people who have successfully merged the DNA from the MMO and FPS worlds so well.

A lot to learn if you get it, reach out to an LFG and/or find some friends to learn the ropes with.

Cheers.\t\t\t\t\t
