\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been a Destiny player on an off since the game launched. I have been through all the controversies and all the times the game has "died". While there are decisions that get made that irritate me, the latest of which is Sunsetting, which serves the purpose of increasing the grind while also semi balancing some of the more overpowered aspects of it, (In my opinion it does both very poorly). I always end up coming back, and I believe the reasons are the fact that it (In my opinion) perfectly blends aspects of an MMO with the aspects of a FPS. I always go to First Person shooters because of the gameplay. They feel snappy and reward good plays and reflexes. Destiny and Destiny 2 have some of the highest quality FPS gameplay that I have ever experienced. Partly due to the second part I always come back, which are the MMO aspects and mechanics. Which liven up gameplay as well as making you feel unique in the world that Bungie has created. Which leads into my final statement about the world. The universe that Bungie has created is one of my favorites to explore, right up there with Star Wars, Elder Scrolls, Halo, ...etc. I love learning more about it and how the high quality characters fit into the lore. So in summary I would highly recommend anyone who has never played it, to give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
