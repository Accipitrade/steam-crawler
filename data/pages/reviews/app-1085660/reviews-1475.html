The game's use of a seasonal model also keeps it from becoming stale quickly (at the literal cost of season passes though). All told I'd say that I enjoy the game. Two things to note though: the endgame activities are very rewarding and loads of fun, though given the communication they require they do not have matchmaking. I personally hate grinding and usually avoid it at the cost of taking longer to complete certain quests, though I still advance just fine. If you're prone to a grinding mentality, you can and will spend a large amount of time doing just that. If you keep these things in mind going in you probably won't be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
