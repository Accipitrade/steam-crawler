\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has been out for years and it looks like a lot of changes were made with Beyond Light. As a brand new player you will find that you are far outclassed by other players and the content. The game content is made for those that have spent years farming equipment, weapons, and modifications. (add on items to weapons and gear). As a new player you are pretty much locked out of any higher level content as it takes so long to get the mods. (2 per day as long as you have the materials to obtain them, feels like you only obtain enough materials each week to buy 2) Not sure as there is not much information you get. You can watch youtube videos but once again most of what you get is content for people who have been playing for years.

I bought the package deal with all the old content thinking it would be fun to start at the beginning. As I completed the older content I realized that the rewards you get from them are so low they are useless. You can upgrade the gear you get in the new content but all the older stuff is locked out. Even the Gambit rewards (a pvp/pve game mode) rewarded me with a weapon that was locked out. If you buy the game I would reccomend just buying the minimum game.

To get the higher rated weapons and armor the game sets a reccomended power level. As a new player you can easily obtain the power level but the content is impossible without the mods, gear, and weapons. I understand that for the player base that has been playing for years this creates some amount of challange but creates only frustration for new players.

You can easily get 100+ hours of enjoyment from this game as a new player but set your expectations. This reminds me of playing mobile games where it takes years as a free to play to get anywhere. This is not a pay to win game but they do not have a path for new players.\t\t\t\t\t
