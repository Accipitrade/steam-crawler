\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRedo time for this review.

D2 is a very expansive game. And by this I mean it's expansive in a lot of ways. Different ways to play the game, different ways to run subclasses, many many weapons, and a lot of lore. And I mean, A LOT of lore.

Destiny's gameplay is pretty fun. It can feel repetitive sometimes though. That's why you should never stick to just one exotic, one subclass, etc etc. This game has variety to it. Variety in missions, mechanics, and then some. The grind for max Light gear can be mind numbing, I won't lie. But for more than half the game's activities, your stuff doesn't have to be super up there in Light. All gear has different stats, and the game has a system where you can make armors look like other armors. People that play this really do like looking fashionable. That being said, you'll see some good looking guardians. There is a lot to do in this game. Explore, do missions, heck play Gambit. There are also some good exotics to get too. Some from quests, a kiosk, engrams, or the famous noodle hair man Xur.

The PvP in this game is far from balanced when it comes to Crucible, which is straight PvP. People will say it is, but it is far from. Gambit, a PvPvE mode, is more enjoyable in my experience, even though that can be pretty unbalanced too. But at least you don't deal with actual players as much.

The game has a lot of customization, and looks really good. There's all kinds of hidden details too.

My only complaint, is that it would be nice if Raids had matchmaking or something. A lot of the endgame content needs you to have a squad, but has no matchmaking. I get that you can use for LFGs and whatnot, but at that point, you could just add matchmaking.\t\t\t\t\t
