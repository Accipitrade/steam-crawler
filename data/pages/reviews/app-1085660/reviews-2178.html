\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI cannot recommend this product for the following reasons:

1. Content is locked off by Season passes. A practice similar to Fortnite. Some missions you miss out on, for example, reviving Saint-14. Had to have the Season Pass for that. Want new weapons, exotics, armor, and boots? 10 more dollars. Each Season pass from what I have seen lasts about 2-3 months, after of which you have to pay another 10 dollars. It honestly feels like a subscription service that the Elder Scrolls Online did. If anything, this combined with the annoying grinding makes it to where I don't want to play.

2. Constant grinding. You need to increase your power level in order to do more activities. However the gear you get only allows you to build your power level by about 4-6 at a time. Since the score is based of the average of all your gear, you have to upgrade every piece of your gear before getting to tackle the next 4-6. Doesn't sound bad, however the power level you want is 980, but you start off at 750. Have fun slow crawling to it.

3. Players that had this game on Blizzard had to pay for the base game among other content. With the game being Free - to - play now it feels like a slap in the face to those who had to pay, myself included. You don't receive any item for it except the feeling you got hosed.

4. More micro-transactions. Destiny 2 continues to beat on the dead horse that is micro-transactions with the Eververse store. Each Season pass is $9.99 or 1,000 silver coins. The store also has weapon skins, taunts, emotes, etc... Stores like this remind me of Evolve when it was released.


Until this Season Pass everything fade goes away, I can't recommend this game even at a discount.
