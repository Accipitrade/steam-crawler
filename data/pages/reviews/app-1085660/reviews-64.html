\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStarting with the down side, my biggest complaint is that the dungeons are now locked behind another payment and are not included in an expansion, unless you buy the $100 version of the expansion. That is absolutely crazy to me!
That being said though, the gameplay and feel is probably the best out there and I love all of the options to play with my friends. I don't play much PvP, but there is so much PvE content that I don't care or even notice. The raids are the most fun, but typically need 6 people and some time to commit to them, especially if some people have not done that particular raid before. The dungeons are a good step down, with only a max of 3 people and not taking as long. After that there is still strikes, lost sectors, or just exploring. There is so much to do that I never get bored and I always come back to it!\t\t\t\t\t
