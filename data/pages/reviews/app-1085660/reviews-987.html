\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere's a bit to go through here so TLDR: Its a good game with a lot of improvements that need attending to.

I've played this franchise since Destiny 1 Year 1 and it was my most played game by far on PlayStation and still beats my most played game on PC. That's how intriguing and interesting this universe is. The main problems are as follows:

Destiny is one of those games that you really needed to play from the start. I gave the game a break for a year after playing it for 5 years straight. I was thrown into a universe that was 2 weeks away from a major expansion and had no clue where to start. As a returning player, the game doesn't really give you a clue as to what to do but even on an alternate account, after completing the first quest, I still didn't know what to do. Nevertheless I pressed on (and missed alot). Beyond Light gave me a sense of direction though, so GJ Bungie

Anti-cheat. There is none. I don't experience it too much as I rarely play trials in D2 but from what I have played and seen from my clanmates, it's ridiculous. Bungie, pls fix.

This may be addressed with the recent expansion of the rewards team but for now, in a brand new season, there are about 10 new guns that we currently know of. There is a pretty lacklustre excuse for a ritual weapon (I know Mountaintop and Recluse and Redrix's were completely busted, to name a few, but c'mon Bungie, create something, don't just slap together a weapon with good perks, give us something original), about 3 snipers (because we need to replace our sunset Beloved) and a few boring primaries (haven't gotten them all because RNG). We don't want reskins, we want new weapons and perks that aren't Rampage with extra steps.

The complete disregard to PVP is just atrocious. I'm saying that and I don't play competitively! There are no new maps, no interesting ideas for gamemodes, no balancing (shatterdive) and Crimson Days was stripped!!?! Come on Luke, at least give us 2v2s then in the rotating playlist.

Ugh, this game infuriates me sometimes, but honestly, I still love it. If I was given the chance to do it all over again, I would've done it in a heartbeat.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 has its ups and downs, there's no sugarcoating it. However, it's been my favorite game for many years for a good number of reasons: First, the world building and lore is amazing. I really feel immersed in the world and its characters are fun. Second, the game play and the power-fantasy fulfillment are both fantastic. Bungie is well known for their gun-play and they didn't hold back when it came to Destiny 2. The game has some of the best feeling guns I've ever used, and the variety of weapons to choose from is huge. Ultimately, there's no other game quite like Destiny 2 in all of its faults and achievements and it's a game I think anyone who likes shooters should try, especially because its free. (that being said, F2P is less than ideal, and investing in the seasons and expansions is definitely something I recommend if you're enjoying the game.)\t\t\t\t\t
