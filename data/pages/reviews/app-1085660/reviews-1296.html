\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm sorry to say that after 9 years I am done with Destiny. The ridiculous amount of ♥♥♥♥ that gets ignored in this game by Bungie is almost impressive. The fact that every balance patch some how manages to make it worse, and the blatant disregard for the PVP community is astounding. With the Seasonal formula turning the game into the same 3 steps week after week, season after season I can't play this anymore. The fishing added this season was mildly fun for the first few times, but the fact that the world Public Events disrupt it and reset any progress kills it for me. We are getting a 'new' raid in a few months that is just one from the first game being brought forward. PVP hasn't gotten a truly new map in years, and Gambit is... gambit.

This game has so much potential, but the fact that it still does not have dedicated servers, a rampant cheating problem, barely reheated content, Strikes that get a single new activity a year, and a drip feed seasonal story model that is about 3 minutes of actual story and doing a 3 man or 6 man activity is just destroying it. all of the big content creators are stepping away and it is no surprise why. It truly does feel like Bungie has moved most of the team to creating Marathon, and that sucks. In short, the game is dying, not because some other game is killing it, but because Bungie is through apathy.
