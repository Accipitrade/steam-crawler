\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReviewing based off the perspective of a typical gamer from an average middle class family in a third world Country.

In all honesty, this game is genuinely good when it comes to comparing between massively multiplayer games due to the sheer quality of the game itself (It is literally a FREE MMOFPS game with HALO engine)

PROS
- It has amazing gunplay compared to most MMOFPS that can be really fun and flexible according to your playstyle due to the sheer magnitude of gun/class types

- The graphics are generally amazing and designs of armor/guns are amazing despite technically being an early last generation game

- In addition to the graphics point above, it is a pretty optimized and accessible to the average gaming rig despite having amazing graphics
- The game is popularly played globally. What this means is that no matter what time/which region you play at/from you will constantly get pretty quick matchmaking times and constantly be able to find players randomly in the open world ish aspect of the game . This is not changing anytime soon since this is the only game in the market to cater to these kind of genre/service that is doing it correctly therefore it will not be dying anytime soon much like Fortnite/League of Legends/Dota 2/Valo and so on.

- For Lore enjoyers, the game has an abundance of that though if you're a "New Player", then it might be a problem for you because from what I have gathered, You do not need to know about Destiny 1's lore to understand Destiny 2's lore appropriately. HOWEVER, this is not exactly Destiny 2 anymore as they removed some base game content that sets the foundations of Destiny 2 and replaced it with a some kind of tutorialish prologue expansion [New Light] that is supposed to be "Beginner friendly" which I agree it is because of how short and simple it is. However, really against the whole removing content thing. In retrospect if you are new, think of the lore in the current game as Destiny 2.2/3 rather than just Destiny 2.

- Content wise , the game is generous even in the base game. Amount of guns and worlds are genuinely not what you would expect from a Free To Play game.

All this amazing good points which are no brainers that makes this game a definite must play and the only reason it would be a "Bad game" to you is due to personal bias or matter of preference

CONS

Keep in mind the negative points I am about to talk about the game below does not justify Destiny 2 as a "Bad Game" but more like "Bad Pricing Model" atleast for a MMO game. This is because the game itself is good in quality and anyone could enjoy the game THEIR OWN WAY if they could ignore the negative points i am about to make below".

However the problem stems from the "Free" aspect of it. As in, it takes the term "FREE TO PLAY" too literally. What this means is that, you only have access to Free aspect of the game as in the Base game and some Expansions and that is all. Which might sound fine on paper because there are other MMO'S that do the same thing and is doing fine [FFXIV,ESO,GW2 and etc]. But lemme stop you right there.

- The reason why those others MMO are doing fine despite having almost the same model is because the base content is still relevant

- Those base contents are probably still getting updated

- The base contents in those games are EXTREMELY Way more abundant compared to Destiny 2's base content (which is already kinda huge)

But the main reason difference it makes with Destiny 2, is the fact that the game ABSOLUTELY DO NOT CARE ABOUT YOU if you are playing old content.
- Meta gets easily changed way too frequently

- Implementation of Battle Passes [Paid] which are too game intrusive as in they are literally the only form of progression often. Yet they offer current possible META weapons which are locked behind a paywall literally if you do not own the current expansion. Yes there are Battle Passes and Expansions in the game.

- It does not ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ help that sometimes the battle passes implement new game modes. Which sounds nice on paper content wise, YET you have to PAY to PLAY them. [Think of Fortnite implementing Battle Passes and new seasons but you cant play their new maps, imagine that BS]

- You might be thinking, these are all optional. That is true, if they are only cosmetics. Yet that cannot be said for these season/battle passes as they literally lock mechanics/meta weapons behind a paywall with already a Expansion requirement to even enjoy some of the other possible new content as well.

In short, its understandable the fact that the game is Free to Play and all so they can pull off whatever kind of battle passes/expansion crap they could if they want to cause its their game and not mine. However at this point the game feels more like "Free to Play, Pay to Enjoy" instead of "Free to Play" if you get my gist.

If you really love the game and would love to support the devs go ahead and buy the required Expansions and Season Passes. Its your money therefore you are free to decide what to do with it. However, if you are from a middle class average gamer belonging to a third world country like me I would recommend you keep your paws off this game if you would like to have a decently sized wallet.



The expansion prices combined with season passes which are all REQUIRED content for you to really enjoy the game is too expensive for the likes of us in all honestly. I mean yeah wait for SALE and all that, but remember the sht you buy on SALE is no longer relevant to the current meta to enjoy the game therefore there comes a time where you would need to fork your cash over for a full priced season pass/expansion to stay relevant and enjoy the game properly.\t\t\t\t\t
