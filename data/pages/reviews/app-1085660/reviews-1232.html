Pro-Con, I guess.

Pro: The gunplay still feels just as vibrant as it did in September of 2014. If there's any single thing that brings me back again and again, it how the guns feel.

Con: We've had some serious setbacks... Unfortunately in the form of D2's original release, where there was so little variety due to weapons having locked rolls that it made me seriously consider quitting permanently... Then in the absolutely horrendous concept that was "Sunsetting"... Just some really bad times.

Pro: The abilities and build-crafting systems have just gotten better and better over time, to the point that I'm genuinely enjoying using builds that I had never touched before.

Con: Crucible, mostly Trials of Osiris, makes me feel inconceivable pain.

Pro: We can now custom tailor not only our looks to a very good extent, but also a select variety of weapons with our preferred traits.

Con: Playing this without friends can make it feel like ten times more of a slog.

Pro: This game has retained my attention the longest, only close to Warframe in my actual time playing.

Con: ... I miss my Kindled Orchid.
