\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'mma make this short cause writing these is annoying.
Though the gunplay is fun and the stories can be good, this is not a game you can just jump in and enjoy. You needed to be in on the ground floor and never have done anything else since then.
Though I started on the ground floor I have not been playing nonstop like some other people and have merely several hundred hours in game (200ish before the port 2 steam and whatever is on my account now). Thus I am the PERFECT person to explain what happens when you try to dip in and out of the game instead of spending the rest of your life playing and paying it.
-Fortunately for me it's very easy to explain........ It totally screws you over.
-Because of the way Bungie treats old content and how the game pace is set it is very hard to not even possible to complete parts of the game even just a couple dlcs old much less stuff from the beginning.
My mission log is crammed with quests I will never be able to complete because the components are either gone or the people are because they are busy playing the latest dlc.
-If you do not have a bunch of friends who also play the game nonstop getting the post story content (the vast majority) done is extremely grindy, annoying and in some cases not even possible if the dungeon is one that requires a tight team with is impossible to get queing in randoms (I know cause I've tried SO MANY TIMES)

This game can be fun but it comes with requirements and you need friends willing to all do the same thing.
-you all need discord and the ability to play at the same time
-the ability to sacrifice all your free time when a new dlc comes out
-the willingness to buy basically everything Bungie vomits out

It took me a long time to realize that I wasn't capable of meeting some these requirements so I had to stop playing.
Also it really irks me that content you pay quite a bit of money for just gets erased or made in-accessible.\t\t\t\t\t
