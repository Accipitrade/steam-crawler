\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPROS
-You never are going to have an unfun time doing whatever activity you choose to partake in.
-You also don't have to do EVERYTHING in order to progress and feel accomplished! I play mainly PvP and story stuff and I'm still able to progress with cool armor, cool weapons, and season pass stuff.
-All the time, with any character, you're going to find a playstyle that matches what you want to play with. Bungie has done an excellent job diversifying the class archetypes and fantasies and no matter which "space wizard" you choose to be, you're going to have a blast mastering their abilities.
-The STORY and the LORE are absolutely incredible and captures the imagination, for the most part ;) Of course there are some weak storylines and stronger ones, but overall it's very engaging.
-Sound design again, is phenomenal and unique. You won't hear another game like this in the market!

CONS
--Coolest armor and weapon skins are locked behind a paywall through the "Eververse". In game microtransactions that are, in general, overpriced. No way to earn the currency (silver) in-game so even if you do save up your in-game currency, you'll never be able to purchase things. Really needs an overhaul.
--Story is presented in a fashion that isn't as linear as you might want it to be. You may find yourself on some Destiny wiki sites filling in the gaps sometimes.\t\t\t\t\t
