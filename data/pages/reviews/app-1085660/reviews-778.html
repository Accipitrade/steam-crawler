\r\n\t\t\t\t\tApparently I've put a thousand hours down on this. Must be something to it.

There are lots of games with an endless grind that keep you playing, the thing that sets Destiny apart is Bungie's ability to make the moment-to-moment shooting so meaty and satisfying that the grind becomes something that happens while you're playing, instead of something you do purely for the grind's sake. There's deep and engaging lore, there's pretty cool storylines, there's challenging content and other stuff you can just blaze through with your brain turned off, but none of that matters if the moment to moment gameplay isn't deeply satisfying, and this is. In recent seasons the build mechanics have finally matured to the point where you feel like blazing your own path or copying someone else's setup are both viable options, and while it's hard to "break" the game, you can definitely pull of some crazy stuff with the right setup.

It's far from perfect. Some of the game modes haven't gotten attention in ages. The PvP is a mess. The game's development has taken so many twists and turns that many mechanics are broken, at odds, or just inscrutable. The new and returning player experience is horribly confusing.
