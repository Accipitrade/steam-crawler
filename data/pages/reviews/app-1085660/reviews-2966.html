\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere do I start?

The best way to explain Destiny 2 is that it's a looter shooter with some of the best gunplay out there and there is a lot of stuff to do.

Performance... is weird. So Bungie, while optimising the game really well, decided that we aren't allowed to have nice things like performance overlays and all that. Which is a shame given that the game does have issues:

1) Even if you can maintain over 144fps in most areas of the game there is the Tower and Gambit where you are easily looking at 72fps or even as low as 40fps. For these areas to cut FPS in half for no apparent reason is silly.. but for an activity like gambit to absolutely tank down to less than a third of my FPS while staring at a wall is honestly the stupidest thing. Similar activities like escalation protocol don't have this issue. Temps and clock speed are fine.

2) Peer-2-peer. The second issue you will face is that the game uses p2p for everything which normally isn't too bad... and then there's gambit. (Should be clear by now that Gambit is honestly trash but sadly you need to do it for multiple quests) - Of course, there are still instances in other gamemodes where you are put up against people with bad connections...

3) Connection issues... I mean sure. Getting connection issues is a normal thing from time to time... but when it's just one game and you follow all the rubbish guides they provide you? Yeah no. If all other games work fine while streaming multiple 4k videos and it's your game having issues and a load of people are reporting the same thing... It's not our connections. Please attempt to fix the server issues already.

4) Oh sure... Place me, alone, against a full team of guardians when I want to play survival.. and then punish me for wanting to leave. Honestly becoming stupid now.

Edit- Status of Destiny 2 in 2020.

Performance after the release of Season of Dawn is even worse. Getting 70fps in many activities with major stuttering. Further issues surrounding numerious bugs and other issues. SoD was rushed.

Edit2 - A patch with a few performance fixes came out... It actually made things worse. How utterly incompetent are Bungie?\t\t\t\t\t
