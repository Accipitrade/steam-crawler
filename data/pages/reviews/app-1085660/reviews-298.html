\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEDIT: AUG 2023
Ok that's it, I'm done. I would not recommend this game anymore as it stands. I'm writing this review after the August 2023 State of the Game that Bungie just released, and I cannot in full faith say this game is worth getting into. Sure at a base level it might all seem great, but looking at the timeline it is 100% not the game it deserves to be for the price required to play. Over the years Bungie has been adding price increases to the seasonal model, events, dungeons, and more and more Eververse content; yet the new content has less and less actual content packed into it with broken promises and rewarding activities. The State of the Game proves this with the official confirmation that Gambit is a dead activity, ritual armors (promised to be delivered months ago) not on the docket anymore, and a general lack of awareness of what the community wants from this game. The fact that this State of the Game was supposed to excite the community is sad. Most of the good are very minimum "oh ok thats kinda cool I guess" things: shader favorites tab, medals in vanguard playlists, so on. But most of the things mentioned are negatives surrounded by gamer dudebro lingo to keep the hype train going. They say they're dropping their promise of new ritual armors after months of no words on that past their due date, with "oh no one wanted them anyway" vibes. They cut off major Gambit support and act like the community hasn't been engaged with Gambit anyway, ignoring the fact this is because we lacked any major updates since 2018 with a minor update in Witch Queen. It's the equivalent to cutting an employee's hours and then saying "you're not doing as much work as before, you're fired."

I love Destiny and will still play it personally to ride out the final expansion, but man this game is a shell of what it used. I'm so sick of Bungie's excuses of things being too hard, you are a a huge company now with a game that makes bank; you can GET the resources. Whether this is the fault of Bungie themselves, Sony, or investors, who knows. But there is no excuse to say the basic of basics can't be done. We hit peak with Forsaken, almost again with Witch Queen, but now its spiraling down hard and fast with the mix of greedy freemium practices, lack of rewarding content, and general community awareness from Bungie.

EDIT: MAY 2023
Adding an addendum after Lightfall. It really feels like lately its 1 step forward, 1 step back. The game's technical state has been in a weird limbo, things take forever to load in your inventory, multiple times servers go down for emergency maintenance, and bugs galore. But when it doesn't mess up, the game feel is still amazing and I personally really love strand. I think Lightfall added some interesting gameplay aspects but dropped the ball HARD on story. It's common knowledge at this point but the story feels like we're in a filler mode to Final Shape.
