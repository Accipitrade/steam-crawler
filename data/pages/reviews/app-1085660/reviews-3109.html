\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't love the meta -- a lot of the endgame activities are oriented toward being in a clan and doing planned clan activities (there's no matchmaking for raids, for instance, and activities like that are the only way to raise your power level beyond a certain threshold). And yet, I keep playing this game because the moment-to-moment gameplay is incredibly compelling. Guns have a tactile kick and sound amazing. The environments are nothing short of spectacular. Each of the three classes has a unique double-jump that is crazy fun to just let you bop around the battlefield in its own special way. If you're into customizing your appearance, you have a lot of knobs and dials to really tweak out your character's look (and all the armor pieces have a distinctive and very "destiny" look to them). The lore is super-deep and much richer than your average online shooter. And so on.

If you are looking for an online loot shooter, even if you're pretty casual, Destiny 2 can be really fun. Just don't expect to be able to reach the highest tiers of play without a considerable commitment.\t\t\t\t\t
