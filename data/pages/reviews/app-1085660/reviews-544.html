\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIs it greed, or bad management, or overall incompetence? Regardless, this game is mostly missed potential.

PvP is barely even on life support, and Bungie makes it worse each time they touch it. Play competitive, even though the ranks are meaningless because it uses SBMM with zero transparency! Tune in next season to see what the newest ability spam meta is! Don't forget to play the fundamentally terrible mode of Trials this season, for one of the most obviously OP guns since Recluse! Have fun while the entire lobby suffers the load time of old consoles because crossplay can't be turned off! Hope you enjoy playing people on a different continent with p2p connections and melee lunge desync!

Gambit... exists? It's worse than both Gambit Prime and old Gambit. I'm convinced they only leave it in the game because it would be more work to rip it out. They'd inevitably break the game some other way if they tried to do that anyway.

Lightfall's story is one of the worst stories I've ever seen in a game. Go go MacGuffin time! Oops we tripped over strand on the street! The seasonal stories try to be better, but they're about as predictable as they could possibly be.

So that leaves the PvE. It's decent, but the game is bursting at the seams with power creep and an old engine struggling to keep up. Between myriad bugs and the continuously declining performance of the game - both in FPS and network quality - it often feels more annoying than engaging. Lightfall's raid has 1/4 good raid encounters, and 3/4 encounters that belong in a 3 player dungeon. Lightfall's seasonal activity has 2/3 battlegrounds that are good, and 1/3 that's mostly waiting around for a few enemies to spawn.

The art, music, and sound design are fantastic.

Overall, I've never been more consistently disappointed with a game. I've kept playing because it's always almost great, but they never actually get there - and at this point, I don't believe they ever will.\t\t\t\t\t
