\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played Destiny a fair amount over many years, ever since it was first released. But as I took a break from the game, i now come back to all of the missions i had almost completed being gone. All of the content i had bought for the last many years gone. Not a great feeling when coming back, all those hours trying to complete missions, some of which have rather "insane" requirements.
They then want me to buy the new expansion, if i want to acquire exotics from raids in the old game, because i get those tokens in the new raids.. I already paid for it once... and i was almost done.. But oh well.
I wasn't a big fan of new exotics arriving in a season pass. took the fun out of it for me. And now when i missed a few seasons, I have to earn those back, by playing to rank 55 in the new season passes. You want 2 exotics that you previously missed and that you had the expansion for? well you have to wait until the next season and get 55 in that in order to acquire it.
Oh yeah, and if you missed previous season pass weapons that WASN'T in the pass, but rather a quest from the pass, you have to buy the DELUXE edition of the current expansion. So basically even though i have payed for everything up to beyond light, I am now unable to get a large amount of exotics, and i have to wait a YEAR in order to get 3 of those I missed. And since I don't have beyond light OR beyond light deluxe edition, in PVP (that I normally only play) I am not able to get some of the frequently used weapons, due to me not having (or wanting to) pay for the deluxe edition.
How is this not turning into to some Pay-to-win cashgrab?
"Sunsetting" all of our old gear to make it almost useless, forcing us to pay for new content, and removing the old so that eventually if we want to play, we HAVE to pay, even if we already owned over half the content.
I used to love this game, but if nothing changes, I'll have to go right back to uninstalling it. Can't recommend buying a game that throws your money away, and treat you as if you didn't spend years buying and playing their content.\t\t\t\t\t
