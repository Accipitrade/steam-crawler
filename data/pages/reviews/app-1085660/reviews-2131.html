\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGraphics: 10\\10

Awesome graphics from the moment you start from the farm all the way to raids. The visuals and effects are one of the best I've seen. When you use certain weapons to your class effects the visuals are just what you expect.

Game play: 9/10

The game draws you in from the moment you start. Great story line and great character developments. I haven't finished the all the campaigns yet since the new expansions, but I can't wait to continue it. The player base is very active and you will always see players for the public events. Other players are very helpful and the clan system is helpful. You start with the companion app and find clans you want to join and apply.

Once you join a clan you can begin to form teams to help with raids to achieve better equipment and weapons. Awesome system they have implemented; where you can always find help when you need it. If you like to play solo by all means this game is great for solo players. Play at your own pace and enjoy the game. There is no rush to finish and no rush to get to endgame.

Recommendation: 9/10

You won't regret purchasing this game, but the reason why I won't give it a perfect score is because the game is not finished. There is more to the game that will come in the future, and I can't wait for more expansions. You will be drawn into the story and will enjoy the game play, and who knows by the time you see this review more of the game might be free to play.
