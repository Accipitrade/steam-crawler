\r\n\t\t\t\t\t5/10 ~ I will recommend because they spent a lot of time working on this game and for $60NZD I spend more on a pair of underpants, so I felt it was a cheap date that satisfied.
It was fun at the start, but soon you will be chewing your arm off to get away quietly.

Look I had fun for the 10 or so hours I played but I was falling asleep with boredom by then.
It is very much a kiddie shooter game.
RPG ~ Hardly
Skill tree is so basic and pointless, you don't need any of them unless you zerg with your eyes closed while eating a bowl of 2 minute noodles and talking on your cellphone.
Challenge factor 1/10 ~ Warframe but worse on a paraplegic kind of game play level. Steven Hawkins could have pawned this game it is that easy.
There is an overhead indicator of where every alien is in range, there is no surprises and the aliens only ever spawn in front of you, never multi directional attack formations.
AI is incredibly useless to the point of WTF~
AI will just stand still while you pick them off at range
AI will run directly in front of your gun sight, there is no challenge at all.
Just hide around a corner and they cant even shoot you, they shoot the ground,walls, everywhere but at you.

This game got stale very quickly.
Too much loot ammo, too much loot gear, enemies without guns dropping ammo.
Mini bosses, just move side from side but rarely shoot back, it was dull.
I played Warlock, never used my skill tree, what was the point? You don't need the skills in PVE, just shoot your gun at the incredibly mundane and stupid AI.
I was changing to a new gun every 5 minutes because loot drop rate is just insanely over the top crazy.
You should see all the crap you never pick up, they must think we cant aim or something?
I never bought anything from vendors
Maps I seemed to be running the same maps from previous missions, just zoning in from different entrances.

Your character in cut scenes is this kind of mute that just nods.
It just makes the character look like a moron with no real place in this world.
NPC with ridiculous Superbowl shoulder pads was irritating.
Areas have missions but it is all the same, kill this, go here kill that alien which evaluates to standing offside in a doorway and just shooting everything that impales itself on your gun barrel. You would think with games like Elder Scrolls Online that they could add some imagination to the missions.

Multi player? Think I saw a few in zone, 3 was the max at anyone time.
Hubs have more players but seem to be just standing around circle jerking or deconstructing all the useless loot you get.

On the plus side, some graphics was nice and the lighting has been done well in creepy scary caves but again, stand in one place and range every dumb alien in the face ~ it loses its fear factor really quickly.

Maps seemed to twist and turn for no apparent reason but just to keep you awake.
The Mario jump map of the wrecked oil rigs got really tedious as you went from ramp to ramp with no enemies shooting at you was just pointless.

Did I expect more for my money after playing games like Warframe? Yes I did. Did I get it? ~ Not even close.

TLDR:
Nice graphics and lighting, quite enjoyable lore and cut scenes.
Smooth game play and zone/instance transitions.
Games so easy you could play it while having a heart attack
It is not a real RPG
Felt like a kiddies shooter
AI is the worst I have ever seen in game
Too much loot drops
Equipment is too easy to get and you don't even need it except the gun
Skill system pointless, you don't need any of it.
Tutorial was non existent ~ no explanation of skill trees or skills
Map is a bit horrendous to work out at the start ~ a lot of well WTF do I go? WTF do I do for the first 15 minutes once arriving to the first player HUB.\t\t\t\t\t
