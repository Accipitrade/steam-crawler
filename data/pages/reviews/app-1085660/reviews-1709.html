1.1k hours update: Antaeus Wards can deflect G-Horn. Kinda cool.
1.9(ish)k update: still sucks.


I've been playing this game since early March of 2020, and I gotta say, my experience has been pretty bland overall. This game is really only fun when you're playing it with a few friends. If you aren't, you can expect to be disappointed in the use of your time.

The PvE (campaigns, quests, open world content, etc) is a little lackluster, but it's overall fairly enjoyable the first time around. Unfortunately, this game reuses so much of its content for the sake of the "daily grind". Many activities start to feel like chores the more you experience them.

The PvP is pretty consistent. I won't say much about connection, as I have subpar internet in all honesty. If you've been playing this game's PvP for a while, you might be able to have fun. If you're newer to game, loading a match of Crucible will start to bring some negative feelings. Most of the deaths you'll suffer will come from one-shot mechanics, of which there are all too many. Attempting to improve is nearly impossible.

Levelling your season pass is easily one of the most boring aspects of the game; a monotonous grind for XP that takes literal weeks worth of constant gameplay, all for rewards that sometimes don't even feel worth working towards.

If you're looking for a free game to dink around in, this is a decent choice. If you want a game you can invest yourself into and feel consistently rewarded for playing, brace yourself for heavy disappointment.\t\t\t\t\t
