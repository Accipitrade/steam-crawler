1. If you aint got five friends who also own the game don't bother buying as raids will be off limits as the community(what community) is shocking, I can honestly say that this is the only game I have ever played where not a single person spoke to me(yes I did try to speak to others but no reply).

2. If you wanna level a character to the max and then when an expansion comes out start again then this is the game for you.

3. One of the least intuative games I have ever played, I honestly believe the devs are having a laugh. You need two monitors to play this game because half the time you will be browsing google to find out what the hell you are supposed to be doing.
