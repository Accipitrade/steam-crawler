But they have destroyed the little equality the Hunter had in the first game. They also decided to make all of our gear make us look homeless... so that's nice.

Say goodbye to the old gear! Say goodbye to most of the planets! But hey, they added the cosmadrome back in!... I actually like that part of this update but they changed parts of it and it's not the same so I'm salty in that respect.

If you were someone who bought the old DLC, say goodbye to them! Cause they have since been removed from the game (Where's my credit/refund eh?). If you were someone who had really good gear, be it good from stats or looks, say goodbye to it! So base armor now looks like trash (somehow more than it already did), but you can pay us more to make it look... ok! This game is fun to play, at times, but unless you give no ♥♥♥♥♥ what your character looks like; give no ♥♥♥♥♥ about grinding nonstop just to have another update ♥♥♥♥ all of your progress so you have to grind again; give no ♥♥♥♥♥ that the only cool stuff has now, just recently, been totally locked behind a pay wall; give no ♥♥♥♥♥ that all of your old gear/armor is now 100% useless, don't ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ buy this game.

To be fair, it really wasn't, and I was happy with what they did. Originally, it only had stuff that you could get, or really didn't matter like a stupid ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ emote. But now, actual gear is payed. Now with each update they are just beating a dead horse to see how much blood really is held in that skin sack.
Bungie died years ago when Reach fell. The company we are seeing right now isn't the same one that gave us the goliath that was Halo. It's some washed up uncle that is a fraternal twin to father we all grew up with. It kinda looks like him, but he died in a car accident and the driver of the other car was money and greed who had teamed up with our uncle to take over the empire dad made. ♥♥♥♥ Bungie and don't buy this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeen playing Destiny on multiple platforms since 2014. Between Xbox and PC I have over 1000 hours spent playing both Destiny 1 and 2. It's my most played game ever. I always come back despite the ups and downs for several reasons:

1.) the actual shooting itself feels amazing, especially on PC
2.) Endgame is fun, doing a raid with a good group is a blast
3.) It's fun to explore planets and find hidden chests, lost sectors, hidden lore, etc.
4.) Speaking of lore it's pretty good and interesting
5.) Exploring builds with Exotic armor and weapon combos is a fun sandbox
6.) Lots of challenges and triumphs to strive for
7.) When you get bored of PvE the PvP is pretty fun and it's cool it has both and is (mostly) balanced

The game is advertised as "Free To Play" but it's not. You can technically START the game for free but if you don't want to be gated out of everything, you gotta pay up. It's generally worth it to buy the expansions and seasons.

As far as stories go, the best stories are usually in the season pass. The latest expansion The Witch Queen is very good, however the main story doesn't have many beats that are super surprising or interesting. The seasons on the other hand ramp up the drama and tell an excellent story every week with plot twists and interesting character arcs. It's like tuning into a really good Netflix series every week. The stories don't always last a whole season but so far I haven't been disappointed.

Destiny's biggest flaw is FOMO. Content gets vaulted after a certain amount of time and if you didn't play it, well too bad. You'll have to watch someone else play it on Youtube and honestly it kinda sucks. I understand why they do it from a technical perspective but it feels really bad to see things you paid for get vaulted. I'd like to see a feature like what Call of Duty has where you can choose what to have installed instead of things being taken away forever.

It's my gaming equivalent of comfort food. If I don't know what to play, I'll fire up Destiny and grind the season pass, chase triumphs, experiment with weird builds, grind for gear and generally have a good time. It's also a great podcast game, some of the quests can be really grindy so I like to get caught up on my podcasts while playing. Some people get super sweaty with D2, I just try to have fun. Is it the best game ever? No but even when the meta kinda sucks it's still fun, and I will probably keep coming back to it until they shut the servers off someday. Recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
