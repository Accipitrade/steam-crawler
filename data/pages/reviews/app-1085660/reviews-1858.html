Edit: Help I'm addicted

Double Edit: since writing this review and after about 3.4k hours i have come to realise that this game just isn't all that great. The things that make this game good are the people you play with but even just looking at the game by itself you need to play all year round for 2 times a year where the game is actually fun, those 2 times being day 1 raids. when this game is fun, almost nothing can beat the satisfaction. i have lost my voice around 7 or so times screaming from joy and excitement after finally beating a raid encounter that took 6 or so hours. but playing the game for thousands of hours per year just so you can enjoy those 48 hours among them just doesn't seem worth it to me.

Though, this game is some of the best for your money in the MMO space even with yearly expansions. for about 80gbp its a years worth of content with constant updates, though, these updates can create more stress than they bring joy due to the nerfing of weapons youve come to love or buffing types of weapons you havent saved and have just been dismantling so far so you have to scramble to get one ready.

In conclusion, this game will let you experience the highest of highs and the lowest of lows. but its all up to you if you want to commit the time and energy into mastering this game.\t\t\t\t\t
