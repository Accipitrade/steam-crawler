\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 has been my primary game for several years for many reasons. Mostly though, it's the variety of play types, styles, weapons, abilities and challenges that have kept my interest. It's truly a game for all experience levels, from story to strikes to multi-player gambit and player versus player, you can be a beginner or advanced and enjoy. At the top end of the game, Raids, Grandmaster Nightfalls, and Trials of Osiris provide the highest level of competition for those experts and professionals.

Tired of playing? Switch to a different character archetype completely (nimble Hunter hiding in the shadows, Titan smashing everyone in your path, or mystical Warlock escaping from any situation and bringing down pain).

Tired of playing a character archetype? The game has subclasses of different varieties that can completely change up your playstyle and let you experiment with new exotic armors that enhance your abilities.

Tired of that? Enjoy the variation of armor and weapon loadouts and challenge yourself to accomplish aquiring "God Roll" weapons that set you apart from the competition in Trials, Iron Banner, or Raid's, and use tools like Destiny Item manager to build armor loadouts to maximize your capabilities. Oh, and then start investigating how to use Mod's to further refine your weapon and armor loadouts.

The amount of variation possible, and engaging in fun bite-sized activities 20 minutes at a time - is what has kept me playing this game more than any other. I'm sure you can find aspect's you'll enjoy as-well.
