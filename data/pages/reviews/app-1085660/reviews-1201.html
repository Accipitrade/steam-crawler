\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeyond Light destroyed the game. With this expansion, Bungie removed well over half the content -- places, quests, events. Most importantly, they made close to 75% of the gear useless -- INCLUDING the stuff from the previous two expansions (which they're still asking you to pay money for). This was under the guise of being able to rotate in new stuff, which to me, made sense: keep the game fresh, don't let it all get cluttered and overwhelming. But how much new gear did they bring in with this expansion, you may ask? Oh, approximately 5% of the total. They literally didn't even add one of each weapon type for this expansion.

The Beyond Light campaign was fun, but it took only like 3 hours and really didn't feel like the big game-shifting event they advertised. Instead, it felt like a side quest in any other game. The new subclass element, Stasis, was cool, but it took the whole campaign for you to actually get it. Once you beat the campaign and have Stasis, you realize the vast majority of remaining gear is best used for a different build, so you'll just wind up going back to a different element.

Don't play the game, don't buy Beyond Light, and DEFINITELY don't buy Forsaken or Shadowkeep because they're charging you for basically nothing.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a weird review to write, because the game itself is good. The gameplay, mechanics, and style are there. Theoretically, this SHOULD be one of the best MMOs of all time. But... Bungie is probably the greediest company I've seen to date. I'm talking EA levels of squeezing every possible cent from its players.

I started playing Destiny 2 a few weeks ago before Beyond Light launched. I purchased the "Upgrade Edition" which gave me the two previous DLCs, Forsaken and Shadowkeep, for around 30~ bucks. DO NOT BUY THESE DLCS!!! Pretty good deal for the amount of content I received. BUT WAIT, the release of Beyond Light actually SUNSET most of this content. In other words, buying any of the DLCs besides Beyond Light is pointless because a majority of the gear has been made useless. A majority of my favorite guns are now unusable because I can't level them up anymore. Exotics that I paid 30 DOLLARS to access are now out of the game, unable to be collected anymore. The few that were saved can be bought by pretty much anyone if you grind enough. This may seem like a good idea to some, but seeing a gun you grinded days for on a shelf for maybe like 7~ hours worth of grinding is pretty disappointing.

I can't speak for the quality of the new DLC itself because I have yet to buy it, and I probably never will. The only aspect I can talk about is Stasis, as I have experienced it first hand in PvP. It's broken. There's no incentive to play PvP anymore because all someone has to do is run up to you, throw a freeze grenade and you're dead. Not only are you stunned for like 5 seconds (A lot in a game where every second matters) but you take a ♥♥♥♥ ton of damage. It's hard to justify removing a majority of your game's content and then charging 40 dollars to play new stuff. Most of the reviews for beyond light and negative, and I think you should take ALL of them to heart. People aren't ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ on this game for no reason, people are fed up. I've only been into this game for a few weeks and I myself am already fed up... Take a look at the beyond light reviews if you're curious, they're pretty entertaining.

Oh yeah, and if you're a F2P player? Good luck lol, you basically get to enjoy like 20% of the entire game. Anything else is locked behind a pay wall which is hard to justify. Game launched at 60, now it's ~100 to enjoy "ALL" the content (keep in mind most of the previous DLCs are useless now, yet they're still on the steam store). What the ♥♥♥♥?

There's so much more I can go on about but I've commented on the main flaws, at least in my opinion. Again, the game itself isn't bad at all, it's actually really fun. The movement feels solid and the gun play is pretty tight for the most part. It sucks that they ruined what they had.

TL;DR: A solid looter MMO unfortunately plagued by an out of touch company who thinks its players are stupid. If you value your time and money whatsoever don't bother with this game. There are better FPSs out there for your money. I've already lost most of my motivation to play this atrocity.\t\t\t\t\t
