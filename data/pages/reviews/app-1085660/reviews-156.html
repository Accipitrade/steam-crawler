\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLord, give me strength as I write this.

I've been in this ridiculous, absurd game for 8-going-on-9 years now. Started Day 1 of the first game on a PS3 attached to the lobby of my college campus because mine was broken. I made a character, played a few hours here and there, and started again once I had a PS3 of my own.

This is a common theme in Destiny. Play for some time, put it down, come back when you want. It's easier than ever now with the seasonal model they adopted. That being said...

This game is the worst addiction you can't quit. The gear is amazing (if you get the best stuff to drop). The gameplay is phenomenal (assuming you don't get a random DC or the Architects don't end your life). The stories are unique and have a large breadth of lore (if you go online and do a little reading). The endgame content is EXCELLENT (assuming yhou find a non-toxic group).

All in all, Destiny is a truly amazing franchise and Destiny 2 is a great game (but it needs to be better). If that doesn't some it up, I don't know what would.\t\t\t\t\t
