There are a lot of positives to this game that also rises a lot of negatives in its wake, unfortunately it's very difficult to explain.

PVE fun suffers from the extreme over-balance on PVP content, causing PVE content to be extremely repetitious because of the lack of customization or 'builds'.

The Main story each expansion has been a few cinematic cutscenes of 'catchup' and then plunges you headlong into 3 hours of 'story' with vaguely referenced lore-checks on each quirky dialogue at which point one of the characters breaks it down and explains the 'whole' of 'light vs dark' all over again saying they didn't understand what it meant each time UNTIL NOW. then proceeds to not explain anything ONCE AGAIN and end the expansion's story on a gigantic cliffhanger.

It also features every single type of monetization you can imagine.
An ingame cash shop for micro transactions.
An initial buy-in price for previous expansions (legacy edition) at 60$
Buy-in price for each expansion (yearly) (lightfall this time) 50$
3 month (seasonal) subscription-based battlepass you pay 10$ for to be able to partake in. (40$ a year)

Bungee rides a lot of their fame and glory on halo, they essentially turned it into a live-service MMOFPS and it sucks because they over-balance and under-tune every single aspect that would make the game fun and entertaining while asking PREMIUM dollar on multiple levels for anyone to partake in this absolute soup of a game, with no pronounced flavors, ingredients or spices, just one finely-blended goop that it dripfeeds into your mouth.\t\t\t\t\t
