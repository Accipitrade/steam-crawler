\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLook, I've been on this train for nearly a decade now. I'm getting close to 1000 hours in now. I can say with absolute certainty that I love Destiny. I love this game, I love the story, and I will see this saga through to the end. If you have been following the story, or maybe playing along, Destiny 2 is an unmatched gaming experience.
_______________________________________________________________________
BUT...

If you're new and looking to get into this game, I would advise EXTREME caution. The core story of Destiny 2 is GONE. Gone. When Dominus Ghaul stormed the Last City, the veterans stopped him. When Emperor Calus challenged us on the Leviathan for his own entertainment, the veterans challenged him too. When we avenged our Hunter Vanguard, we could almost transfer our own emotions into our Guardians. You just had to be there.

But you will not have been there. You can't be there anymore, and that's just terrible to me.

So much of the game's story, the CORE OF THIS GAME'S HEART was left to the wayside in the release of Beyond Light. The earliest you can immerse yourself in this game's story is Shadowkeep, an expansion where admittedly very little happens. Yes, you will challenge great foes in the current game: Savathun, Rhulk, Eramis, and more to come down the line; however, you will miss the context and narrative scaffolding that made this game, this universe great .

If you don't care about that, and want to dive into the amazing gunplay, loot system, beautiful raids, and blisteringly tough PvP, go for it. But I'm obligated to caution you that at this point, you are lacking the true Destiny experience thanks to the content removal. You will be missing the wonderfully down to earth seasonal stories that came like campfire tales that we all sat around and absorbed. You won't feel the wrenched hearts of the losses we've had along the way. Yes, old content from Destiny 1 is being brought back like the Vault of Glass and King's Fall, but the story is what makes them matter.


TLDR:
Getting into this game at this stage is a matter of deciding whether you want gunplay, compelling narrative, or both.

If you don't care about story, go for it. If you do, hopping in now will not satisfy you anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
