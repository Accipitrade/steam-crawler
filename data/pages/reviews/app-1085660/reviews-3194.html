\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet's just say it's so good that I dropped out of college, quit my job, gave up my relationship(s), got a warlock bond tattoo, and sold my cats because I'd much rather be spending my time playing this ga.. Lifestyle. 5 stars. Would recommend.

Just kidding, I kept my cats.

In all seriousness though, I've been here since the beta for D1. Sure, there's been some rough patches and content droughts, but Bungie always pulls through for us and have been making the game better and better with each new expansion/season. If you bought D2 at launch and were (severely) disappointed, don't worry, they noticed, and they actually did something about it. Destiny 2 is in a great place at the moment and I'm sure it can and will be even better going forward. Thanks, Bungie, a studio that actually listens and respects it's player base. ...On that note can we increase the drop rate and diversity for armor pieces in the raids? I have gotten so many dang boots I can open a 99% discount shoe store only accepting dogecoin as payment and still turn a profit while providing for 8 kids and paying my extended family's medical bills. Too many boots! Moon's haunted, peace.

5 stars, would play here again.\t\t\t\t\t
