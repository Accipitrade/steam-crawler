\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter putting in 1500 hours into the game, I have realised that I cannot, in good faith, recommend this game to anyone, especially those that have a life outside of video games. It can simply be summarised as "wide as an ocean, deep as a puddle". Initially, I was taken aback by the sheer amount of stuff I had to do. However, 1500 hours later, I can say that the filler content you need to plod through to actually do what you really want is staggering.

And now that weapon sunsetting is a thing, I expect the filler stuff to get even worse.

On the technical front, the game has been plagued with connection issues for more than 3 months now. I have been banned from for leaving PvP activities twice in that time, even though I was getting constant error codes. The 2 month ban that I got a couple of days ago was really the straw that broke the camel's back. I just cannot be bothered with this crap anymore.

The only saving grace is that the game is free2play, though most of the free2play stuff, i.e 3 raids and 3 campaigns are going away in September when the next major expansion drops. If anyone is interested, try the free game as a demo, but DO NOT blow your hard earned money on either Shadowkeep or Forsaken, since those 2 expansions will have most of their content rendered obsolete in another 3 months. Stick with the free bits, and buy the September expansion instead, if you are really interested. Otherwise just stay away.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to love this game. I love the art direction, the characters, the gameplay, the lore, nearly everything about it. But ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, Bungie just doesn't know how game balance works, or how to make a gameplay loop, or how to keep things interesting. They just buffed sniper rifles like 2 months ago because no one was using them. They are now nerfing sniper rifles because they are now the most used weapons in raids. Any new weapon they put in the game is broken good upon release and is balanced down, or is useless and quickly forgotten. The lack of armor and weapon ornaments that are available in game also makes it so you mismatch armor to make your light level go up until you are at the level cap. The shader system to color your weapons and armor is a total step backward from the first game and is clunky to navigate. Overall, it's so very close to being good, but man, when you run out of things to do, it comes to a slow crawl of trying to make a number go up, slowly over the course of weeks.\t\t\t\t\t
