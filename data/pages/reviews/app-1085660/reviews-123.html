\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a game that I've been playing for quite some time now, and I have to say that it's one of the most fun and engaging first-person shooter games I've ever played. The game's visuals are absolutely stunning, and every environment is so detailed that it really feels like you're in another world. The sound design is also incredible, and the music is so well-crafted that it adds to the overall experience of the game.

The gameplay is what really drew me into Destiny 2. It's fast-paced and challenging, but also really rewarding when you start to master it. There's so much to do in the game that it's hard to get bored. I've spent countless hours playing raids, strikes, and public events, and I still haven't done everything there is to do in the game. I also enjoy the game's PvP mode, where I can compete against other players in various game modes. The gunplay is smooth and satisfying, and every weapon feels unique and powerful.

The story in Destiny 2 is well-crafted and engaging, and the characters are likable and well-written. I've really enjoyed playing through the different campaigns and learning more about the game's world. However, I do wish that the story was a bit more fleshed out and that there were more opportunities to interact with the game's characters.

One of the things that does bother me about Destiny 2 is the game's monetization model. While microtransactions are not necessary to enjoy the game, it can be frustrating to see so much focus on monetization rather than on improving the overall experience of the game.

Overall, I would highly recommend Destiny 2 to anyone who loves first-person shooter games. The game's visuals, gameplay, and sound design are all top-notch, and there's so much to do that it's hard to get bored. While the game's monetization model can be frustrating at times, it doesn't detract from the overall experience of the game. If you're looking for a fun and engaging shooter with a great community, then Destiny 2 is definitely worth checking out.\t\t\t\t\t
