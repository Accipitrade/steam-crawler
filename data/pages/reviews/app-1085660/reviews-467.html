\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game, however...
This game feels like a part-time job where you get paid all at the end of the year. The yearly releases (although overpriced) are prime gaming content. Enthralling story, unique gameplay, and so much fun. But seasonal content is repetitive and mind-numbing. And yet I love it. I come home from work every night, turn off my brain, and grind the same ♥♥♥♥.

If you want a game that is always exciting, great PvP content, and worth your money; this game isn't for you. You have to study this game's story like it's a final exam, you have to be there starving for content to enjoy the Last Supper, and then you'll enjoy this game.

9/10 worth the $400 I've spent on this game.\t\t\t\t\t
