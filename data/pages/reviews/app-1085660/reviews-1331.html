Shadowkeep DLC - 6/10. Not as horrible as people say, just reflects Bungie trying to get off the ground after split with Activision (thank the Lord). Worth buying for sure if on sale. The moon is pretty nostalgic from D1 and it has a Raid and some fun story/Patrol stuff going on.

Time of writing this is Season 2 of Witch Queen. Rating may change by the end of Season 4.

Overall, if you ask me, D2 is a must own on PC and everyone should grab the DLC on sale, and if you are a huge fan, its worth just getting it because it is super solid.\t\t\t\t\t
