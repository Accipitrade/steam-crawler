\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty fun game overall, but Season of the undying is almost as bad as Season of the drifter though. The raid is amazing and rewarding but Vex Offensive is braindead and has rewards (more reskins). FOMO is more present than ever with content going away come a season's end. If you are going to play this game hardcore, there is a of content to burn through, and tedious seals to earn. I have 7/13 seals at the moment, but some of them it will be hard to find people to group up with for. I recommend joining the LFG server for your best chances at finding people to play with. PvP is a lot better after the new changes in Shadowkeep, making supers more vulnerable and having many more options as to what gamemode you want to play. The largest issue is the queue times, they are much longer with the recent division of gamemodes, especially in the rotator playlists, Every raid is enjoyable at least once, and most of them have triumphs and rewards exclusive to them that will keep you coming back if you are hardcore. Casual players without any DLC can easily get 100+ hours of fun, and the DLC can get you hundreds of more hours depending on how hardcore you get with the game. Easily the most fun and replayable looter shooter out there with a lot of great community members, I recommend you give this a try if you are looking for an MMO-like game with an active community and fun PvE + PvP content. Spent over 1000 hours on this thing.\t\t\t\t\t
