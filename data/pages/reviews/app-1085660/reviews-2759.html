\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty good overall. Movement is tight and responsive, the looter shooter elements tie more into actual RPG mechanics, having different stats for armor and weapons as well as different damage and resistance types. Doesn't feel like a typical looter shooter in that regard, makes it seem like it actually respects itself a bit instead of just hunting after a higher numbered item. Sound design is good except for the guns, wish they sounded a bit meatier and louder. Story is serviceable, lore is all over the place, but the effort put into the world building is nice to see, regardless. Multiplayer has lots of different game modes, PVE, PVP, different modes therein, can last a while. Leveling and items and all that can seem a bit skinner box, and I don't like the idea of having a season pass, microtransactions, and expansions to pay for. Seems redundant and scummy. Doesn't really factor in to gameplay, but the meta of that kind of weighs on me as I play the game.

Solid 7.5/10, it's okay, could be better in some areas, wish it had stronger RPG elements and less money grubbing.\t\t\t\t\t
