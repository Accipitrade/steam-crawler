\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are into a mix of Halo/Diablo/Wow style games, this is the one for you!

48 hours in and there is a lot of content packed into this game. I would suggest buying the expansions when they go on sale as even the free portion is filled and getting the expansions let you try out the different planets and the alternative spec for your classes. Plus having the tons of planets option can help with the grind (MMO part of the game) by switching it up.

I suggest for new players to do as much of Earth as they can, while looking into the Main quest, Adventure locations, Patrols, Public events, Lost sectors, and Region chests. The game explains most of these as you progress, but a quick Google search of these keywords will bring tons of information for you to get started. (There are also secrets on each Planet to look out for :) )

PvP side of the game is decent, just make sure to grab the PvP quests and bounties from the NPC at the tower before you head out.
I suggest if people who like PvE stuff will fair better with the Gambit PvP mode. Otherwise, find a couple gun types you like and head over to the Crucible. For each mode you might get some loot after each match, so that's nice too.

Early on you want to just focus on keeping the gear with the highest Light value and dismantling the rest. I haven't done so yet, but you can always go back to the Collections menu and buy back any gear you have found thus far, so no big fear there of accidentally getting rid of stuff. (Lock loot with the LShift key).

My only grip with the game so far, is wanting to do strikes or raids and not having a group to play with.
As I believe some of the top tier gear is behind events that require pre-made groups. *Please leave a comment if I'm wrong, as a casual/solo/match-making player, that information will be helpful.*

Extra tip: Google "Destiny 2 Xur Locations" early on :)\t\t\t\t\t
