\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI made the mistake of playing this game on my xbox one originally and when it first released. To be honest, it was a ♥♥♥♥ show and the downgraded graphics and console aiming didn't help me to appreciate the game anymore. So I put it aside and thought I would never pick the game up again. In my eyes it was a bard borderlands without the unique charm that tried to take itself too seriously.

I have some hardcore friends of Destiny 2 who told me to get new light when it came out, but remembering my time with the original I did not want to go down that road again. I was very wrong. You see, after all the changes Bungie has made and with Shadowkeep they had completely changed the game for me. It felt fresher and more open to new players vs the people who had already played Destiny 1. The grind feels challenging, but fair and there is a lot to do. Hundreds of quests for each planet. 5 Campaigns if you own Forsaken and Shadowkeep. Gear to grind for, friends to play with, this is truly going to be a game that I am willing to delve into now. If you have any thoughts about getting it from having played it in it's, in my eyes, unfinished form, don't. The game is EXTREMELY good now with a lot of content and life in it not to mention its huge player base. Good luck Guardians.\t\t\t\t\t
