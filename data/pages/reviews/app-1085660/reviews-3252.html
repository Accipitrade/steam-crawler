\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played this game for countless hours and have the game on console and PC...so expect a biased review.
With that being said, this game is a ton of fun. The developers may not put out a new patch every single day but they are more on top of it than most companies. They put out weekly updates without fail.

The gunplay is a ton of fun. There are inevitably guns that are better than others but a large part of the game is grinding for better weapons that you can use to be an even more amazing Guardian. There are quests that the community unlocks, puzzles that are solved for new weapons, etc. The only issue I can see with this is that some weapons are locked behind certain game modes that people hate (Gambit for me!). It does force you to play a certain game type to get certain weapons at times. While I don't necessarily think that's a good thing, I don't necessarily think it's that terrible either.

The PvP is a ton of fun. The PvP is certainly imbalanced by the guns and the rolls that you get (randomized gun loot) but that does not change the fact that you will have a blast killing other Guardians, training by Shaxx to prepare you for the fight against the darkness.

The raids are beautiful, big, and amazingly fun (especially with a clan!) If you want a blind run raid, you won't find a bigger community for that then Destiny in my opinion. There are plenty of people out there for a speedrun though, so don't worry.

The story is great...but there is an issue that I'll address with that. The story is hidden. What I mean by this is that the story isn't mainly told through cutscenes and quests that you read. For some strange reason, Bungie made the decision to hide the lore in entries you get for completing different tasks and hitting certain achievements. You do have to go out of your way to read up on the lore and learn everything. There are certain YouTube videos that you can go watch to make sense of the lore if you are not a reader or someone who simply doesn't have that much time. This decision by Bungie is mind-boggling and bad but the lore is amazing...you just don't get it all fed to you like you should in my opinion.

All in all, if you haven't played this game, I IMPLORE YOU to try it out. Give it a chance. It's amazing.\t\t\t\t\t
