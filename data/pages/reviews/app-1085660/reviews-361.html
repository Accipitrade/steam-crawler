\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly a great game. Hasn't always been good and it took Bungie a while in order for the game to be good. The strong points I think matter more than the weak points for me because I have the time to put into the game and the cash to put into the game. Not everyone has those both. Below are my take-aways after playing almost 700 hours.

Strong points
- Lore/story is great and accessible.
- Gunplay is great!
- FINALLY new player friendly. (wasn't for a while)
- Progression relevance isn't near as elitist as it used to be.
- Ornaments to look like what you want is nice! Fashion central!
- It is FREE to get a taste for the game...

Weak points
- ... BUT DLCs are required for a full experience. DLCs are worth it the money, however it can get expensive. If you don't have a $60-100 laying around every year for the next DLC for a years worth of content you'll miss out.
- Can be more grindy than people would like for certain gear/rolls on gear.
- Doesn't demand, but passively requests more time for a full experience. You feel like you're missing out if you can't commit the time sometimes (raids, grinding, exotic hunting, etc.)
- Can't access old sunset/legacy content. (Red War, Curse or Osiris, etc.)
- No matchmaking for dungeons/raids. (There is a good LFG system on Discord but not everyone wants to do that)
