\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm being genuine when I say that playing this game (and the previous one) has given me insight into how abusive relationships work in real life.
If you can't resist a free MMO, then play the free content, sit down, and consider just how much you are now invested. Spoiler alert, it's probably not enough to justify buying the expansions.
No, the story never gets good. Sure, Forsaken is much better than the first three campaigns, but keep in mind that a 5/10 is better than a 2/10 and is still not worth it. If you end up really liking the lore and worldbuilding, still stay away, the actual plots themselves are too bad to justify staying for the lore. Just read the grimoire and books online.
No, the gameplay never gets better than the grind. You'll always be playing twenty missions on loop for hours. Maybe you want a relaxing, mindless activity to waste time with, but trust me, this will waste more time than you originally wanted unless you emotionally disconnect yourself from the things that make the game satisfying in the first place (i.e. loot).
Turn around, for this path leads only to madness, pain, and denial. Play literally anything else.\t\t\t\t\t
