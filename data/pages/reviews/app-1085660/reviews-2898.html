\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf there were a "meh" rating I would give it that. The game is great in the beginning, tons of new stuff to do, new loot to grab and all that jazz. It's until you reach endgame content when the grind starts to wear you out, the constant raising of the power level just feels like another number to reach and there's no real meaning to reaching max power level. Season of the Worthy is ok, in my opinion. I feel like the rasputin public events are a bit too samey for my taste. I appreciate that it's not really time gated, and you can speed things up if you do well on them. I couldn't really get into the bunker system, I didn't feel like grinding out the public events all that much. More importantly, going flawless on trials needs to be incentivized more. Maybe not adept weapons, but something exclusive to flawless other than some sparrow or emblem. Similarly, raid loot should also be put in a tier above the standard run of the mill weapons. All i cared about when doing the raids were the raid exotics like 1KV or Anarchy. Other than that, raids just felt like something to check off the list. On an unrelated note, exotics feel too easy to get especially coming from D1. I prefer that system of exotics more, just because they felt more special. As nigh useless as some of the exotics are, I appreciate that the catalyst system allows for small buffs/tweaks to exotics like the Lumina, but of course pretty much 90% of the exotics pale in comparison to the likes of Xenophage, or other meta exotics. My interest in destiny 2 around now is pretty low, and I'm hoping that the coming annual expansion can renew some of that interest in the game. I love Destiny, been a fan since D1 and I understand that that Bungie doesn't have the same amount of resources and manpower available to them as they used to, but something like incentivizing flawless more doesn't seem that hard to do, much less to ask for.\t\t\t\t\t
