\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is personally one of my favorite games. Sure, Bungie has their ups and downs over the last few years, many would argue mostly downs than ups. In my opinion, you either like this game or you don't. If you happen to get this game, I strongly advise NOT joining the Bungie.net forums as it's a cesspool of toxicity that will literally drive you mad. Reddit is more civilized if you have questions are want to talk with the community. I love the story line of this game and where it's currently headed. Stroy wise, I think this game is really good at keeping you on the edge of your seat.

I reccomment this game only to people who would actually invest time in this game. It is not casual friendly, no matter how much Bungie tries, or how many people say it is. FOMO will always be present in this game, even if Bungie tries to say they want to get rid of this feeling. Yea, it sucks, but for me, the story of this game outweighs the FOMO.

If you are new to the Destiny franchise as a whole, I strongly recommenfd that you look up two YouTubers that focus heavily on this game. My Name is Byf and Myelin Games. They have a huge amount of lore videos that are extremely helpful with understanding the story from start, to the present. If you jump in this game blind, you are shooting yourself in the foot and I wouldn't blame you for quitting the game.\t\t\t\t\t
