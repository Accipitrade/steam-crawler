\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like it, I just do not have the time to play it more. The game is really fun and i enjoyed it a lot. However the game is not perfect.

Pros: (nearly everything)
Fun gameplay
Many gamemodes
Fun seasonal events
Cool weapons
Great graphics and cool looking weapons
Fun characters
Fun to play with friends
Cool raids

Cons:
Crucible (I admit that I am bad it as well, you may laugh at my 0.20 k/d :D )
Strikes are fun but when you do not play or legend or higher you are forced with matchmaking
Raids are only playable with a certain number of players and there is no matchmaking you need to make lobbies using the D2 discord. If you want to do raids i can recommend using it.
Season pass is pretty fast paced if you do not play enough you cannot complete it. May be difficult to put in enough time if you have friends, work and family exams etc.

Honestly the most annoying thing for me is Power level. It just keeps going up and its not really well spread throughout the galaxy. You think you finally have a high level piece of equipment you spend a lot of resources buffing your exotic only for the power cap to increase, AGAIN! Back when I started the Power cap was like 800 right now its like 1800. It Gives a grind aspect that I dislike more than the grind in Warframe.

If you do not mind playing with random people every now and again or if you have a few friends who are interested its an amazing game and I can highly recommend it.

If you play many games and have other stuff like exams or work I can recommend this game less.\t\t\t\t\t
