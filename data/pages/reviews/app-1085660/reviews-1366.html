\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall incredible, but definitely not for everyone. Rich atmospheres, addicting gun-play, and loads of content will keep you coming back for more.

Music: Wide varieties and styles of music, each unique to situations and enemy races. Never get tired of it. Perfect 10/10
Story: While sometimes hasty in story-telling, ridiculously deep and engaging lore personally makes up for it. Solid 8/10.
Gameplay: The most addicting way to shoot aliens, with dozens of different ways to show off your power. Each exotic adds a new way to play the game. 9/10

With this game being free-to-play, I believe the least you can do is give this game a shot. Whether you want to relax and explore different worlds, sweat and PvP, or grind out in PvE, this game will keep you coming back. Although content is a lot more spread out due to time-gating, the experience it brings as you're immersed in the world is always worth it. Overall 9/10.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played the first Destiny on PS4 several months after it launched and didn't really understand the concept. I thought I was embarking on a journey optimized for 1 player that would resemble a story. I didn't understand that the game was meant for multiplayer, and because of this misunderstanding I turned the game down.

Several years later I received Destiny 2 for free from BattleNet. I decided to give the franchise another shot and I was pleasantly surprised with what I found. I still thought it was meant to be a single-player experience (I'm dumb, okay) and because of that I found the end game extremely meh and kind of put the game off to the side. I don't remember exactly when I started to play with friends and genuinely enjoy raids and end game content, but I do remember my first raid was Leviathon, and I did it with my friend Cruvian and several randoms. The raid took us 6-8 hours. Genuinely. We sucked lol. But after we finished, at 6ish in the morning, I remember just being blown away by the complexity and intricate mechanics that were required to be learned through repetition or memorization. Sometimes you could brute force your way through encounters and other times a foot in the wrong place could kill everyone.

Destiny 2 has its flaws, just like any other game, but the incredible visuals paired with top tier puzzle gameplay and invigorating fast-paced FPS battles make this game TOP TIER and will stay in my heart for a long time. As an added bonus, the game is lore heavy and takes quite a bit of reading to truly understand the universe that is Destiny 2.

Expect a game that is heavily focused on PvE and that will knock your socks off if you play with friends.

But ♥♥♥♥ Crucible. Honestly, ♥♥♥♥ Crucible.\t\t\t\t\t
