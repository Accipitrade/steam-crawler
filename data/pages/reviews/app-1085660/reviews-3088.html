Even with my issues, i still recommend this game to anyone, especially since it has a free model to run on now!
TOP RECOMMENDATIONS
*DIM - Destiny Item Manager (makes life easier, faster, and the app and website are great and well utilized)
*Destiny Companion App -mobile- makes finding fireteams easier, checking what you have on the go, and works similarly to DIM, but is more user friendly for console players since they dont have the option to alt tab

PvE and its rewords
*Raids
These are fun to learn, tutorials usually come out on youtube within the week of launch if you dont feel like learning mechanics. The weapons and armor are pretty unique to anything else available
*Forges and menagerie
These are where you farm specific weapons, the perks will still be random.
The forges let you choose a weapon, menagerie will let you choose the weapon and masterwork. Both have their limitations

PVP (as of this post 11.5.19) is a mess, however its still being worked on and this is the season they are dedicating a team to it.
The new game mode momentum control is a great thing to give a shot, if you were one of those hardcore mode COD players :P

PVE is fairly fun, they keep changing it up, and keeping it fresh. New seasons bring new things, and the game is moving away from reskining armors to make and release original and unique looks, and even has a transmog system!

If you love pve, this game has it
If you love pvp, this game has it
if you love adjustable challenges, this game has it!
