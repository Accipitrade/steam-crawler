\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBungie was a great game studio. Emphasis on was. Looking at this game, it seems like they’ve entered their twilight years.

This isn’t something I want to write. I’d love to give Destiny 2 a glowing thumbs up, but well, I can’t. The ups and downs are mostly just downs.

-An “MMO” where the only time you see more than 3 other players is in PvP. Be real, this is a first-person shooter with an identity crisis.

-Things like sunsetting old content, including content from paid DLC.

-Bounties bounties bounties YOU KNOW WHERE YOU CAN PUT YOUR BOUNTIES?

-Oh boy battle passes- sorry sorry, "Seasons". More Fear Of Missing Out ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥.

-Great art, lovely lore, ♥♥♥♥ writing, and ass dialogue. The story/world lacks substance or depth.

-Characterization. Who is X, and why should I care about them?
Oh no, Zavala has his panties in a twist about [insert new thing here], if only I gave a damn.
Oh no, the funny guy died. (Yes, that is his entire character…)
This is the same studio that made all the memorable characters in the Halo series, so… why?

-The DLCs don’t offer enough content to be worth the price. I could buy another full game (or multiple during sales) for the same amount of money.

-Unrewarding loot. When you’re getting the same gun with bad rolls for the 50th time, it doesn’t feel exciting. And have fun dismantling armor because it has trash stats, over and over and over again.

-Eververse - the overpriced microtransaction store where all the nice things are.

-Grind to get a random weapon or random piece of armor with random stats that you then have to grind more to level up.

The game doesn’t respect your time.

But if Bungie one day manages to remove their head from their own ass and unscrew this game? I’ll happily come back.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I personally have played this game for a long time. I also am a Destiny 1 player. I have always enjoyed the game, personally, but I have been here since the beginning. Unfortunately the game developers are making the game less and less appealing to play for new players. There are less and less actual quests and activities in the game and they are continuing to take them away, making the game more of a grind then it used to be. They also are taking away a lot of the charm that the game had. Most of the base game is completely gone. They took away one of the dlc questlines.
The positives: I chose to put this in a seperate section so people can see it. I think that the game is cool. I think the story is very rich and interesting, to any that are interested in that. I also think that the game is fun for those that are playing with a friend or in a group of friends. It has some good challenges for those who want one. The triumphs system is nice for those that want to do something for a purpose. Its not all bad. Take all of this how you will, but from my own friends who I have tried to get to play this game, they really made the game not very fun for new players who are just trying the game out and havent yet decided to get any of the DLCs.\t\t\t\t\t
