This was when Destiny 2 first launched, and I had NO idea that we had to play up to a certain point in campaign just to play with people. It just didn't make sense to me, and it became a horribly wasted birthday present to a friend when we couldn't play together originally.

But, after a long time of not touching it, I thought 'why not give it another try with New Light'? Same little issue which was short lived because a seasoned friend properly explained that only the first part of any campaign [minus the brief bits in Red War] needed to be solo.

Since then, I have actually enjoyed this game a LOT. Crucible [even when odds are stacked against you] is a lot of fun. Some of the exotics grinds are annoying, but the rewards are wonderful.

My only complaint is that they messed with the armor system a LOT so that is still a small bit confusing. And that rewards from the old campaigns are gone too.\t\t\t\t\t
