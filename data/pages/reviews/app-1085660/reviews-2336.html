\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been a fan since the Destiny 1 beta, I did not put that much time into the first game after hitting end game.
Destiny 2 is so much fun, I have easily put over 150hrs more into D2 than D1. What keeps me coming back and enjoying my time in this game are the seasons, the emotes and the community.

I am not usually one for seasonal journeys and quests but Bungie has found a way to get me hooked. There can be what seems like an overwhelming amount of activities and quests to do, however if you just focus on one at a time or just swap to a different goal when your current grind gets too repetitive you will have the best time and lose track of the last 5 hours of life. Worth it!
I am starting to finally get exotic weapons as I sit down and focus on specific quests instead of bouncing all over the place.

There is always something to do in this game and that also helps keep my attention on Destiny 2. Whether it is grinding out a title to represent your skill, knocking out quests for the best raid/pvp//pve loot or gathering groups for the raids. endless hours of fun to be had and I love it.

Edit: A lot is changing in the game, if you are new to the game then you will have a lot to do, but as I said above, just follow a specific quest and you'll be making progress before you know it. Yes the vaulting does suck however, there are times when you can get vaulted weapons if you have the DLC for that content.\t\t\t\t\t
