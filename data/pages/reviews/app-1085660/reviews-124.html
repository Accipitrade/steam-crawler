\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis review is solely on Lightfall, which is so bad I felt like I need to try and warn people away.

Lightfall was easily falsely advertised. You aren't the tip of the spear of anything, as the ViDoc before release states. You spend 2/3 of the campaign learning about the new darkness ability, which is cool itself, at least. Half the time you need to spend time between campaign missions looking for better gear to be a high enough level for the campaign mission. Which there were so few of, it's disappointing. Fanciful sci-fantasy terms are thrown at you left and right to make it seem well written, but that's not how it works. The new NPC for the new region is awfully written. They are the literal worst parts of every Marvel character thrown together.

This is NOT a $50.00 expansion. This was the content you'd find in a $20.00 season, if that. The story is non-existent. You could watch the beginning and ending cut scene for the the expansion and you leave with just as much information as if you played through it. The new subclass is great in the campaign for using, because the new grapple feature is sort of fun to use, when it works in a manner that makes sense and doesn't play physics pinata with you. But outside of it, it has an immenselly long cool down even if not targeting an enemy.

Bungie, this isn't it. Give your advertising team a raise, because they pulled the darkness over our eyes with this one.\t\t\t\t\t
