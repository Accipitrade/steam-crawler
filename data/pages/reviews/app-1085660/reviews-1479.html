\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI felt compelled to write this in the off chance that I helped someone else decide not to invest their time in Destiny 2. I played this game for many hundreds of hours before it came to Steam. The gameplay and some of the story elements roped me in. I had a great time for many hours playing with a friend. Upon getting through all content through the Dreaming City, something happened. It became apparent to me that the developers of this game have no respect for the time or sanity of their player base. There will come a point when advancing your power level requires mindless repetition of content that you have experienced over and over again, and there is no way to avoid this. There will come a point when you look at a quest objective and just lose the desire to go through that content even one more time. You will ask yourself, "Why am I doing this? Is the gameplay worth it?" I am trying so hard to get back into this game prior to Beyond Light, as I like the sound of some of the things Bungie is doing, but I don't know if I can handle running some of this content over and over and over and over and over and over and over again in order to chase some arbitrary number.\t\t\t\t\t
