\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have loved this game, and have hated it. It's been a long road from the time spent on the Battle.net Launcher to now on the Steam Launcher. This game has gone through many ups and downs and has been both hated and loved by many. I sadly now can't bring myself to play it, the wonders of the game have been lost. The recovery for Bungie will still be tough, and I expected that. But to say the changes they've made over the last 6 months have been wanted, is well completely polar opposite to how some of the community feels. They've permanently dented a portion of the game for me and have removed the core fun that I found within the game. It's a possibility that they will change it back at some point. As of right now however, I can no longer play this game until things have settled and Bungie has figured out what they need to accomplish.


Update as of coming back during season 19 and lightfall launch.
This game is in a better spot now that content has been added. However, there are still some extremely stupid decisions being made by Bungie. The loot system feels abysmal, they don't QA their patches and updates (recently launched with a massive problem with audio, the seasonal questline is locked if you already have a ship, and a bunch of other ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. They have pushed out content that is nothing close to what they had with Forsaken and Witch Queen. Now yes, Forsaken was a different era as they had the support of Activision, but Bungie has shown they can make a great DLC through the witch queen expansion. Lightfall was an extremely rough DLC, it felt like it added nothing to the game besides a new subclass, which honestly has carried the DLC from now being absolute ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. The campaign was extremely weak, some of the new systems were just not fully complete (Guardian Ranks and the loadout system). The loadout system was a great improvement to the game as now you can swap full gear sets and weapons, even the mods and shaders on the armor in a single click. It has some extremely stupid restrictions though. Firstly you can't personally name them, it also has a weird system for changing the name, color, and image used for it (W/S + </> and some other stuff). I can understand the need for some restriction in terms of censoring words used, however why didn't they just utilize their chat censor? Why did they completely limit what they can be named? Stupid. Guardian Ranks, feel like a bunch of fluff added to the game with no real meaning currently. Once LFG is fully implemented I feel like this will change, however that is considering they don't screw up that system as well. The guardian rank system is made for lfg matchmaking, but it got released now rather than 2 seasons from now when LFG will be released. It has helped to improve the system, but makes no sense to be in currently.\t\t\t\t\t
