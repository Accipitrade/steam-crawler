The problem is for almost all of those years it's been promise after promise that Bungie hasn't delivered on. The PVP has been so heavily neglected that it's just not fun anymore. There's cheaters teleporting through the map with infinite ammo, poor connections, and extremely off-balance match making. You either completely stomp the other team, or get completely stomped. It's almost never an even fight.
If you spend a lot of time solving hard puzzles and grinding a quest for a special item, its likely they'll just give that item to the rest of the player base later on for no work.
I love this game, and if it wasn't for the story and my friends I would uninstall it and never look back. About twice a week I think about uninstalling and just watching the next campaign cutscenes on youtube. It's a game that's so good it's become the only game I play, to where I can't enjoy other games as much anymore, but also so bad and full of broken promises that it makes me regret getting into gaming at all. I really hope this next expansion is better, but I'm not confident in Bungie at all. I feel like I'll probably be saying goodbye to destiny after this one. Bungie doesn't have a healthy relationship with its player base, and if people keep giving them money it's never going to change. : (\t\t\t\t\t
