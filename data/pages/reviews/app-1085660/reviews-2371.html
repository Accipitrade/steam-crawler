\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think this game is amazing. Guns, magic, space pirates, customization options galore, constantly changing gameplay, and extra features added routinely. I have been playing video games of all sorts since 1985 and no game has been able to hook me in and get me to keep coming back like Destiny. So good job Bungie at whatever addictive programming you put into your game. (that's a tongue-in-cheek joke, just to be clear)

Destiny is an open world, online, FPS (first-person shooter) with guns, magic, aliens, puzzles, and space travel. There are multiple customization options for your character, guns, armor, and accessories such as your spaceship and sparrow (which is your land vehicle - looks like a hover snowmobile). You can tailor your character to whatever style of play you wish and you can change it up for various activities whenever you want.

You can play alone or with friends. Form a group or jump into a random matchmaking activity. You can travel to other planets and fight unique enemies while discovering hidden materials and treasures. Random activities can be found everywhere you can join, there is tons of loot to chase, and if you look for it, you will find a rich world filled with history and lore. Choose to be a human, exo, or awoken. Play as a hunter, warlock, or titan... or create 3 characters and play as each one depending on how you feel or what activity you are doing. Choose how you want to play and do what you want to do.

If you enjoy shooters and/or if you enjoy action/adventure games with puzzle/rpg elements to it, I definitely recommend giving this game a go. It's free to try so boot it up and play a bit of the story to see what's up. in the great Destiny universe\t\t\t\t\t
