\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2

One of my favorite games. I absolutely adore this game. Rather I did. Bungie has since made very questionable design decisions and seems to be incredibly out of touch with their player base and what is actually wanted, rather than what they believe players want. Seems more they wish to force their flawed design decisions upon players without accepting community feed back or having the respect to admit an idea they thought was good did not pan out and was more a horrible choice for players which ruined an experience for them they otherwise used to love.

Pve vs Pvp

A large problem with this is that many of my friends and many players do not want to play, take part in, or do anything related to pvp. Who can blame them. Pvp in destiny is a niche cancer. Some love it, some tolerate it, and some are forced to do it while requiring new blood pressure medication by the time you finish your Shaxx weeklies. Many exotic quests, many exotic catalysts. MANY MANY things REQUIRE FORCED pvp participation and quite a LOT of it.

Pvp affecting pve

Another wonderful one all the pve people love. Players find ways to abuse, combo, or downright break things. Something that works fine in pve that is loved, is suddenly trashed and nerfed beyond recognition and permanently changed because something that was done in pvp. Often times the core of the game will constantly suffer and go through many revisions or content will be COMPLETELY disabled all because of pvp. Aggravating pve players constantly.

There were times I could get friends to do gambit with me, it was tolerable enough. However with the new stasis powers, that is all but a distant memory. Oh and for some reason all gambit content except a basic, chopped down version has been removed. No more armor sets, no more gear runs, no more anything but this one thing. Mind blowing why they did so for no reason at all.

Stasis

-Pve
I am not sure where to even start with where this came out as a good decision. Sure. Freezing npc's sounds like a fun idea, and to be fair CC is a classic mmo tactic used for hard trash pulls in many games, boss control, and keeping ones self alive while rezzing downed team mates. It can be a life saver and give breathing room. However. When you allow hard cc to affect a player, it does absolutely nothing but cause aggravation, frustration, and a feeling of being cheated and robbed.

Trying to position yourself away from a boss to cover? Rez an ally? Focus on fighting adds? Doing the myriad of things you normally would do now will often result in your instant death. When frozen. You may be so when jumping. Just to fall to your death. When frozen. Adds will attack you still and likely kill you. When frozen you will have to spend time breaking free while the boss and adds come down on you. All of these are bad enough, however when you break free, from this hard CC, which is bad enough in its own right, for some reason it was deemed being a single hit away from death, when you were full health prior, after coming out of cc, was the perfect choice. The moment you come out be prepared to die instantly. From shots already in the air coming at you, to wound up melee swings from mobs of enemies on top of you. I have seen a guardian rez another just for both to be frozen, then instantly killed after breaking free. This leads to people quitting, unnecessary wipes, and wasted time, all while making the game feel like a painful chore you are forced to constantly slog through to get any kind of reward, which now in this expansion, feels the most mediocre and bottom of the barrel possible.

-PVP

Oh boy where to even begin with this. Crucible, Gambit. Both feel barely worth playing anymore. I used to love gambit. Xenophage was cancer and sure that hunter that always spawns in and taps instantly with xenophage just to tea bag you cause to cool for his youtube highlights was bad enough, now it has gotten far... far worse. You will be frozen. Constantly. By grenades, by melee, by ultimates and you will be powerless to stop it. It is about who can get their ice power off first. The guns and builds do not even hardly matter anymore. Freeze someone, shotgun or snipe them. Move on to the next. Avoid being frozen, and freeze. Gun battles, planning, mobility, accuracy, load out choices. It really doesn't matter hardly anymore. If you are not using stasis in pvp, then you are at a very severe handicap. Especially for players who do not have the expansion, it will completely alienate them. Even to a point players who que with stasis should be put against one another, and those without in their own categories. Nothing is more frustrating then being stunned in pvp from another player and instantly killed with absolutely zero ability to fight back, even if you break free, anything can one tap you at that point before you have a chance to possibly do anything to recover. A group of friends I used to pvp with in crucible refuse to even touch it anymore.

This is also bad considering the fact, for some reason, so many quests and exotics require FORCED pvp participation, and absurd amount of kills or feats done in pvp as well.

Price and content

Another topic that has been beaten to death across forums and reviews but because it is worth mentioning so much. The current latest expansion costs 40 dollars. As many know sunsetting has happened, content was removed, the loot pool massively shrunk and many many things are just flat gone and who knows when or IF they will ever return.

For those who do not know somehow yet, sunsetting means gear has a limited life span. Typically from what is understood is a year after it debuts in content now. Perhaps shorter. So all of your favorite weapons. Trash. Pinnacle weapons? Trash. Even small basic legendary niche weapons that may not have been powerful or the meta but you just really damn well loved using them? Gone. You must now trash everything and regrind the same things again, many of which are no longer obtainable. Exotics however are timeless and can all be used and upgraded without worry. So far.

Many of my favorite weapons are gone. It is a frustration beyond anything. Many of my favorite were gambit weapons. Pinnacle weapons. Breakneck, bad omens with tracking, and my favorite gun (which seems to have survived a little longer and a reason for playing), Python shotgun (Looks amazing as well) are simply just gone now. One of the reasons I played was soley to USE these weapons because they were FUN to use. Now it feels like putting your dog down and being told you have to get a new puppy even though your dog was only a year old.

Lack of content

Another topic beaten to death. But sadly it is true and something to be extremely frustrated over. So much content has been removed, even questionably things such as armory, gambit pve and modes, and much more. Limiting the available resources available, options on how to play, how to obtain gear, and what feels like being told that you MUST do this now and maybe once you do things to our liking and way we might consider letting you do other things in the future. So shut up and go to europa and enjoy not being able to see anything or deal with random wandering hard core mobs that will just annihilate you.

There have been some changes that are nice. The world over view looks really nice and organized. The mod system is fantastic. The new scout rifle is fun to use for now, and the new shotgun can be useful if you can get it to work right for you. But the small good things are just so heavily outweighed by horribly poor design decisions that could have easily be remedied. Player feed back is necessary to keep the life of a game constantly alive. When you deem yourself to be above the level of others and "They don't know what they want" mentality starts to surface, it quickly leads to a death of community, player numbers, and soon revenue for the company.

Thank you for coming to my Ted Talk.\t\t\t\t\t
