\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: Good game, currently bad execution
Destiny has been one of my favorite games since 2014 when I was 10 years old, loved the grind but now the game has grown stale. Everytime a new raid comes out I will plan on returning to the game and probably always will, but if you are new to the series you will have no idea what is going on and about 1 game too late. The main weapons that Bungie is interested in adding are old exotics and the new expansion "New Light" costs 40 dollars for... A terrible story in which the main antagonist is not threatening in the slightest and you can kill in around 5 minutes. And a new raid. And although raids may be my favorite piece of PvE content in any video game ever, it's the first raid in a year and it was... Pretty good? The loot is good and the armor looks sick. But it is. So. Damn. Easy. In the first encounter I have sat in place not shooting anything trying to wipe for 5 whole minutes without dying. In a raid this is unacceptable. I haven't played since the first season of Beyond Light but I'm not sure that they have fixed that. They are adding a raid next season but then again... It's the first raid from the last game which means unless they seriously change something it's going to be very. Very. Easy. There is a ton more that I could cover about what I am disappointed in about this game but I'm sure this is about 5000 characters too long. Anyway, get this game if you want, after all its free but be warned: it's very addicting and as of the current moment, very disappointing.\t\t\t\t\t
