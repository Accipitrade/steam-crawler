\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played Destiny on xbox for varying periods through all three expansions, and continued playing Destiny 2 through year 1 of D2. Within the past 10.5 months my friends and I have gotten heavily back into Destiny 2, and I can safely say that this game is fantastic for anyone who is interested in the PvE aspect of the game. Bungie has consistently been able to capture lightning in a jar in terms of creating satisfying raid and dungeon encounters in an FPS. If PvE is what interested you in D1, D2 is absolutely worth investing your time and money in. In terms of PvP, D2 certainly leaves something to be desired. I feel in love with Destiny because of the Crucible, but due to the lack of anti-cheat software and inability on the part of Bungie to meaningfully adjust aspects of the PvP meta that the community is fed up with (Mountaintop), I cannot in good conscience recommend the Crucible as a main reason to play this game. That being said, if you are interested in the PvE aspects of this game, the downsides of the Crucible can be looked past, and the game on the whole is an incredible FPS experience that is worth all of the time and money you are willing to sink into it.\t\t\t\t\t
