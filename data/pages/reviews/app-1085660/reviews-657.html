\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny has always been near and dear to my heart due to my love of its lore and gameplay mechanics.

The raids are fun, the story is great, and the combat is all inclusive with many different playstyles.

Pros.
-Great story
-The voice work is amazing
-The cutscene animations are top notch
-Music is amazing
-Plenty of 3rd party sources to find groups to run high end content with regardless of your experience
-PVE content in my opinion is top notch. The raids are extremely well done, nothing feels repetitive and you can confidently run in with friends for the first time in a raid and figure it out.
Cons
-PVP is heavily defined by the whims of Bungie and what they buff/nerf. One month you could be an absolute god, the next you could be a nobody because they decided to nerf everything you like to play or buff the very thing you hate.
-The community at its core is amazing, but there is enough toxicity in Destiny 2 to point it out as a warning sign to those that dont want to deal with it.
-Hackers, hackers especially in certain PVP modes infest the game at some points. It comes in waves, right now its lessened due to a new anti cheat being implemented, however give it 3 months and the hackers will be back again before the next ban wave.
-The sunsetting of content has for many players (myself included) felt like a kick in the teeth. They removed half the game to appease SONY and their requirements for games on their marketplace. They have promised that the removed content will come back in time however when that actually is uncertain as there is no official date given to us at this time.\t\t\t\t\t

Each season will cost about 10 dollars with expansions being around 40 dollars. S15 is going to be 6 months long, with the 30th anniversary event and dungeon in December. Because of sunsetting the original campaign that many people had when they originally bought the game at launch is not in the game anymore. At shadowkeep I would call it more of a F2P game. I imagine after witchqueen launches forsaken will become f2p. Despite sunsetting the new additions to the game have been a massive improvement, specifically what has been added to s15 and the roadmap of changes from witchqueen onward. Bungie decided to focus on giving lightbased subclasses a massive overhaul. The trials changes have been great, and I am happy that the seasons after Beyond Light have been more cohesive with their themes. I still is annoying that if you do not get all the rewards from the battlepass you are SOL, but that is how they work unfortunatley. I forgot to collect my rewards from season 8 and the season of the dawn and have missed out on some cool ornaments. I do not like that you buy the season pass for a chance to get rewards you paid for. I think destiny would benefit greatly by implementing what halo infinite is going to do with their battlepass system. It is unfortunate that core activities have been lacking in content for quite some time but hopefully the team will rectify that with witchqueen and subsequent seasons. The game used to be a chore but now it is getting to a much better place. I can't wait to see what weapon crafting and Savathuns throneworld will bring. With this past season it looks like the dev team has been getting more and more transparent with updates and qualms the playerbase has. Great game I wish it were Febuary already!\t\t\t\t\t
