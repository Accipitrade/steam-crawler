\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're someone who loves a long grind, Destiny 2 is for you.

I've had Destiny 2 long before it came out on Steam. It definitely can get frustrating at times, but what game doesn't? The story is amazing including the DLC stories. You never run out of things to do or things to collect. It feels very rewarding at the end of each quest or activity and not to mention, it's outstandingly beautiful. There's literally something in Destiny 2 for everyone. Whether you're a PvP person, or PvE person.. Destiny 2 does a great job providing a fun and never ending experience. With constant events happening in it and new things to do being added, I will gladly continue to play it.

With that said, no game is perfect. Every game has it's minor flaws. Thankfully the only problems I can come up with D2 is ones that I personally don't enjoy but do understand why it's there. Like "why can't I pull everything I collect out of collections? this isn't fair." When in reality, I get it would be unfair if I could pull that really op sniper out at any given second of the game. I also hate that I can't have more than one exotic weapon or armor piece on.. but again I understand it would be kinda.. or really stupid if I could.

But by the end of it, I don't see why anyone wouldn't want to grab a free game that includes 2 free DLCs while it lasts. I know I definitely would. It's worth it, I promise. And if not, it's not like you spent any money anyways.\t\t\t\t\t
