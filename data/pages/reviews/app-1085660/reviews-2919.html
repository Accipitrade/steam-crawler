\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't quite know how to put into words as to why I can't recommend this game. I just feel like I'd be a bad person for telling anyone to play this game. Not even for meme reasons like, "oh this game ruined my life", or whatever. Genuinely, as someone who has put in a combined total of around 3,000 hours into Destiny across both games and multiple platforms, do not waste your time. I only stick around because Bungie stole so much of that time and money from me already. It's definitely Stockholm Syndrome at this point.

New players, if you're looking for something to play, look elsewhere. It's not worth your time, there's so many issues that either get over-corrected, or just never addressed. Not to mention there seems to be this ebb and flow of laziness when it comes to this game. The first two DLCs were lazy, then Forsaken came and it was great, then Shadow Keep was really lazy again, Beyond ligh was also kinda mid, then Witch Queen came out and was fantastic again, but we're seeing that same laziness again with Lightfall. If nothing else, at least the pattern is consistent.

Hopefully in two expansions, it'll be good again, but I still wouldn't waste your money either way. If you're looking for a sci-fi shooter game, they just remastered Metroid Prime, go play that instead.\t\t\t\t\t
