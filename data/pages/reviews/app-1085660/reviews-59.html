If you find or make a build that is fun and does a lot of damage...nerf. If you get a weapon that is overpowered even by a little... straight to nerf. If you kill yourself in PvP (which is always extremely broken) to get a quest chain done for a specific item (Not Forgotten, Luna's Howl, Recluse, or raid weapons to name a few), guess what...also nerf. Maybe one day this game will be good enough to continue playing or come back to, but as it is now with the current in-house leaders killing off anything fun, you're better off sticking appendages into a spinning fan blade as that is more entertaining and fun and less painful. Currently I can't at all recommend this game, as hard as it is to say. Maybe as they keep loosing streamers and players they will get the picture, but by then it's far too late.\t\t\t\t\t
