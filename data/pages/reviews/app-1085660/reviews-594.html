\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game. Still, I can, as of right now, not recommend it.

Why, you ask? Well. I've been playing this when it wasn't free to play. Back then, it had a core campaign. It introduced me slowly to a fun and very shooty game, interesting places to go and do stuff and characters I really came to like. Okay, sure, the tutorial phase was long. Overly long, for some, possibly. But what it did very well was enable me to build a relationship to the game world and the fictional characters that populate it.

This is no longer the case. "The Red War", the first campaign, has been removed, along with about half the characters it introduced. The rest are still there, but if you don't do some research, you wouldn't know who they are and why they are there, doing the things they do. Or even what they actually do at all. Some of my friends picked it up to play along with me and from what they tell me, the tutorial is very brief. Often I needed to explain things to them, that I should not need to. And they'll never really understand why I pour myself a nice cup of black tea and drink to Devrim Kay, why I have a crush on Failsafe and am so sad that Cayde-6 is... gone.

Still, a lot of the things that I loved are still there. It is still a good, enjoyable shooter with a beautiful world. And I am not mad at Bungie for doing what they did. They had very good reasons. I'm having fun playing the game and I will keep doing that, I will continue to buy expansions and follow the story as it goes. This I can do and have a great time, because I've played through all the old campaigns and am invested into the world, but I strongly doubt this would be possible for a new player. Especially when you only have access to the free-to-play part of the game.

If you have a friend who played Destiny 2 for a long time and is willing to tell you about all this or are yourself willing to actually dig up all the story you have missed, things might be different for you. Or maybe those things, that were so important to me, are not what you find so utterly necessary to enjoy a game. Anyway, these are my reasons and I hope they are helpful for you.\t\t\t\t\t
