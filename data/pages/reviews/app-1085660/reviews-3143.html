\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, around 4 years and (almost) 900 hours later, I have a few things to say to whoever is thinking of buying this game.

1. This game is 150% with one or two or however many buddies. Destiny is more than a game, it's an experience and it's one of those experiences where the content you play doesn't matter as much when there isn't someone to engage in with.

2. The game is absolutely stunning. Veterans like me have spent hours just sitting on a planet, looking up at the beautiful skyboxes. If you're a fan of visuals (that being graphics, character fashion and weapon fashion) then this game has quite the fancy package to offer, with every expansion leaving you say "bless the art team"

3. It's a broken, yet pleasant, piece of art. The game FEELS good, it shoots, runs, plays perfectly. The abilities are powerful, well executed and the weapons have some of the tightest and most satisfying controls in any shooter to date (yes, even your favourite shooter) and killing is too damn fun.

BUT. and this is a huge butt... it's a mess. PvP is and has been unplayable for a long while, the game can feel grindy and shallow with not "enough" rewards sometimes, and there will always be more content in the cache shop than you will be comfortable with.

So, if you want the experience, buy this and you won't regret it. But if you're looking for something flawless... stay away.\t\t\t\t\t
