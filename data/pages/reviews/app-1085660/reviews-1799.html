Though the main pve rewarding games require high levels, just roaming a planet and questing is just as if not more fun. Although I have played this game for (at this moment) under a year, I already have triple 5 hours and have been through 3 seasons, so I don't have the most understanding of everything about this game's past, but boy if it ain't fun. The one thing that every hates that I personally love is the low drop rate of awesome weapons and armor. It's the one thing that kept me coming back saying, "I wonder what I'll get next?" Anyway, all in all, absolutely fantastic game. Just, Bungie, please, nerf beaver.
