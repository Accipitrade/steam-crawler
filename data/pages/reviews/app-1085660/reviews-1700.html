It is a looter shooter and a light MMORPG, I stress "light" in this context. So unless you like games that require you to grind to get the best loot you may want to steer clear. That being said, the gun-play is top notch akin to Halo, the lore is very interesting and if you play long enough and get into the endgame activities then there is challenge that will really push your skills. If you have any interest in games such as Warframe, Borderlands etc. than this game is worth checking out. Especially since the first year of content (up to Foresaken which is the first big expansion) is free so ultimately you're not losing anything by trying this game other than hard drive space.\t\t\t\t\t
