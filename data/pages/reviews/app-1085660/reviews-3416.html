\r\n\t\t\t\t\t7.5/10 and that's only because it's free to play and the PvP aspect is unique, if it wasn't free to play easy 6.5/10. This game at its core is a generic mediocre repetitive mostly bland looter shooter, but I can't stop playing it.

Highlights:
-Fantastic Gameplay and gunplay, however there is very little to no story this includes the expansions except for Forsaken.
-A TON of content is included in the free to play version.
-Unique PvP system.
-Exotic items (Equivalent to orange gear in borderlands) are actually... exotic. Being that they're extremely rare drops with the best way to obtain them being from end game content that is incredibly challenging. Some are achieved from quests but most quests are either extremely hard or take a very long time to complete.
-Harder difficulty activities are basically just die in a few hits while killing bullet sponge enemies, however the saving grace is there are mechanics to learn and coordination is needed.
- Biggest gripe of all though is that there is no matchmaking whatsoever for high endgame content (raids, legend difficulty strikes etc) I can see a lot of people quitting because of this.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo not let the bad reviews fool you, this game may start you at 750 light. But its only fair since that this game is so deep into DLC and light (950 max light cap) so its only fair to start at 750. Me seeing the bad reviews just make me cringe because they only have a couple hours into the game as a player who has clocked over 1000 hours counting destiny 1 and 2. Those reviews make me cringe because the people who make them have no damn idea what they are talking about, let me make this bigger


--THE INTRO TO THE GAME--
The intro starts with a ghost finding a dead person (You) and bringing them back to life with supernatural strength (Light). You kill these enemies called "Fallen" Enemies who have taken over the earth. You defeat enemies with guns such as automatic rifles and shotguns etc. Then you reach a ship and you fly over to a place called the "Tower" your hub world and we will get to that next.



--HUB WORLD--
The tower, the place where a lot of guardians spend their time, the tower has vendors and you collect bounties from them and complete them to get rewards. There is a post master where you collect items that you didn't get from activites, and that covers it really.



--THE CRUCIBLE--
Ah, the crucible. The crucible is the PVP place, you fight other guardians there. New players in crucible will think its not balanced, it is not balanced just a little bit, like supers and stuff, besides that they need to just get better at the game and their strats.



--THE GRIND--
The grind is the most fun part of the game and basically the core of the game, you see if you are 750 light you can't get to high end gear straight away. You have to get to 900 light before you get to the high end stuff and you have to stick to blue gear, or I guess if you are lucky enough. Legendary gear


--THE STRIKES--
The core of PVE, strikes are missions with matchmaking. Thats it really


--THE STORY--
The story is lacking a little bit, but just a little little bit.

--OVERALL--
Overall I say that the game is really fun and amazing, not the best game i've ever played but its damn near close

9.8/10\t\t\t\t\t
