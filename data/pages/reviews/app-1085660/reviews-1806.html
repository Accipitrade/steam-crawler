\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is genuinely one of the best games I played for a while- but I cannot recommend it to others. For starters let's talk about the good:
-The gameplay is incredible, the guns feel great to shoot, the classes feel different in PvE and all have roles to play, and the strikes and raids are fun and sometimes challenging.
- There is a decent amount of stuff to do in the endgame but it is mostly just grinding for a better role on a weapon or trying to get the penultimate season weapon.
- The graphics and environment are incredible. Walking into the moon and seeing the cosmodrome in glorious 60 fps was great!

Now the bad:
-This game is quite frankly worse than any EA game. If you want to stay relevant in the game you are forced to buy hundreds of dollars worth of DLC. If you don't want to pay for the dlc you are going to be behind everyone else, not have access to the best weapons, and will be capped at a lower level making PvP situations very hard to be in.
-Speaking of PvP, Bungie has ZERO idea how to balance the game. They always overbuff one ability or one class and then nerf it into hell. They are going down the path that ruined the first destiny for me which was: Play with this type of weapon or lose the game. At the end of D1 it was all shotgun titans everywhere, and D2 Is turning into a similar game.
-Trials of Osiris: Trials of Osiris is a joke! In d1 the matchmaking sucked so you always went against streamers carrying some chump to flawless, and were rarely given a fair fight. They brought back Trials in D2 to the SAME effect, for a penultimate PvP mode you need to have some sort of skill based matchmaking that rewards good play, not punishes it.
-Finally The microtransactions! Dear GOD the microtransactions. I am baffled as to why you need to pay for cosmetics in a game where you have to pay hundreds of dollars for normal content. On top of this they added a battle pass mechanic that is quite frankly offensive. For example: If I want to stay relevant in this game I will have to buy the new 60$ DLC that is coming out later this year. Every 3 months they also release a new "season" where you have to pay 10$ just for that seasons drops. The currency that D2 Uses is at a 100 silver-1 dollar ratio, which means that some of the cosmetics can cost up to 30$ in a game that costs wayy to much, all in if you wanted to catch up right now you would have to spend over 120$!!! It is unbelievable how money hungry this game is.

TLDR- Bungie is a money hungry company that makes a game with good gunplay at the cost of over 120$ plus microtransactions and battle passes. Dont Waste your money, play the free to play warframe.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe menu system and UI is awful. Tracking quests should just be based on the zone you're in. IE if you're in dreaming city all your dreaming city quests should display as tracked. Gambit and crucible aren't that fun with gambit being who gets to drop the most motes and crucible is "mom its my turn on the 1 shot machine". Triumphs and seals are interesting and I would like to see more titles in the game but they're gated behind RNG and again, the menu system is annoying to navigate. Most quests, triumphs, etc are extremely cryptic which would be fine if there was a good wiki but there isn't, quests + challenges should be more clear and easier to track. Instead of for Summoning Ritual the flavor text "complete the summoning ritual challenge" (???????????) should say "complete the Kalli encounter after activating and clearing all 9 plates". The most fun I have is in raids but thats because I have an IRL friend group to do them with. There is almost no in-game community building or way to queue up with others for raiding so I notice a lot of people don't do them. Which is a shame because the raids have by far the most diverse and interesting boss mechanics instead of "spawn random mobs that'll make it harder haha". Most basic enemies are extremely similar to each other in appearance and mechanics with stupidly similar names like taken, scorned, fallen, etc. There isn't enough diversity in most zones aesthetic with many planets looking eerily similar or there just simply not being enough to do on them. The moon and the dreaming city are by far the most diverse with a plethora of things to do. I mean I'm not going to quit because again, my friends play it; but I feel as though there is so much wasted world building and mechanic wasted potential.\t\t\t\t\t
