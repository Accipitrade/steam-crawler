\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFresh Edit for Beyond Light:
[Short version]
Sunsetting gear/locations = bad

[Slightly longer version]
I wouldn't have minded if all that sunset gear and locations were replaced by something useful like weapons tied to the darkness or even better, a pvp area and TRANSMOG (maybe sunsetting should have been postponed until suitable replacements were available, yes?)

But we didn't even get that.

So, now I'm sat here, twiddling my thumbs going "I paid all that money to just have it cut? Bollocks"

But I guess in the end it's Maroon 5's One More Night with me and this game...

[Original review]
We made a video essay on how Bungie and their split from Activision, in our eyes, it was a benefit for the game: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TbWK-mp6CNI

Overall, this is a game I would get if you played Destiny 1. As a player from Destiny 1, I had fond memories of the game - though compared to now, it was somewhat lackluster - the open world was bland, all bounties came from the tower, the raids became repetitive after a while, and the only thing that kept me going back to it was Iron Banner.
This was a major improvement on their first release - we now have seasons with new content, cosmetics (though I won't always buy into that), bounties galore, and tons more multiplayer to do (I wish team clash was a permanent fixture though).
Still, there are things that I should warn you of:
- there are no official servers (can be a good thing at times)
- it can get grindy (though to me, not as grindy as Warframe)
- Things do get repetitive (Shaxx would say it's character building)
Apart from those three points, I have nothing to complain about. For me, this new entry into the franchise is a major improvement on the old.\t\t\t\t\t
