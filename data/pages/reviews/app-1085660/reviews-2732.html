\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very solid mmo looter shooter. The classes are all very fun to play, the guns feel satisfying to use, and there is a decent variation of enemies and environments, spanning from the ruins of old Earth, the hellish homes of the Hive, and the ethereal beauty of the Dreaming City. And the content you get for no cost is quite significant, with the original base game plus the following 2 DLCs (Curse of Osiris and Warmind). Both Forsaken and Shadowkeep I would say are also worth their costs.

All that said, I will note that to get the full enjoyment out of the game, being in a Clan or having friends that also play is strongly recommended. Also, sadly, while shaders only cost in game currency and are at times almost thrown at you (my shader inventory filled very quickly), most universal armor ornaments (items that significantly alter the armors appearance) are currently found in the cash shop (Eververse).

Still, I currently consider both to be fairly minor issues and have no problem recommending Destiny 2.

EDIT: Two years later and I would still have no issue recommending D2. Europa is one of the best areas they've ever had and frankly, Bungies story work this past year has been the strongest it's been in ages. I eagerly await the coming launch of the Witch Queen and to turn Savathun into a gun.\t\t\t\t\t
