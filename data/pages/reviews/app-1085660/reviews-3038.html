\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStarted as a new light:

-Horrible PVP netcode with exclusive weapons gated behind the "competitive" modes.

-No In-game global chat, requires use of external apps or sites.

-No matchmaking for high-end content, so no Raid or Heroic Menangerie matchmaking. Made worse with no way to effectively organize a group in-game due to no chat.

-Eververse shoved down your throat at every opportunity. Artificially extend the grind on long slogs of seasonal quests in the hope that you;ll play longer and'll end up with you caving and buying the useless bull**** contained within.

-In a game that's supposedly about team work, even PVE bounties will frequently pit you against your friends for kills.

-Quest log limit is laughably small for all the quests and bounties they expect you to do.

-Despite buying off TGA2019 for "best community support", they ignore their forums and have a single nobody, dmg04, spam the reddit with "I forwarded your feedback", of course nothing ever changes for the better, in fact it only gets worse.

-Champion mod system grossly limits loadouts. Which is a shame because the champions themselves are an interesting mix up while farming ordeals until your brain goes numb, using the same weapons you probably don't even like or want to use over and over again because it's the ONLY way to consistently get materials to upgrade your gear.

-Armor Elemental Affinities limit builds even further and exist only to extend the grind on upgrade materials.

-Seasonal armors look terrible but you're stuck using them because the mods are gamebreakingly powerful.

-Even if you have an SSD, you'll still spend a 5th of your time in loading screens because you're constantly being matched with people who don't. And sometimes even if you're playing with people who you know 100% have them too, the game will still decide it wants to load for a good 5 minutes. Want to grab a quest? Enjoy your loading screen, want bounties? Gotta go back to the tower. Of what's that, did I hear you want bounties in four other locations for your sundial seasonal that you need to go through another loading screen for each? I love slow moody shooters right, like STALKER, or Metro, so I'm not approaching this from a zoomer mindset when I say this game is legendary at wasting your time for no reason.

-Virtually all of your drops will be garbage, a lot of other games have garbage in their loot too so what's the problem? Well, every other game where you get a ton of garbage loot they typically give you ways to manage it. In Destiny managing gear without the use of external API's is abysmal, it's slow and inconvenient. It completely ruins the pace of the game and coupled with the long loading screens it's a literal waste of time.

-Special mention for the UI because it's 100% garbage, I'm actually impressed that they managed to make something so poorly put together that only achieves wasting your time and space. Whoever made it should be embarrassed.

Good points:
-Uh, I guess the game runs well even on old computers, I managed to pull off 45-60 fps on average during fights with just a gtx660ti and a mix of medium-low settings and the game still looked pretty decent visually.

-There is some cool stuff done as far as art style is concerned, but it's wasted here.

In my first review I kinda recommended it despite some flaws, but now that it's not the shiny new toy and I've thought about things I almost regret playing as much as I have, if you have some friends to play with it's probably okay for messing around, in fact I intend to keep playing if my friends ever want too. But when there is so many good games nowadays why you would subject yourself to a game where the devs allegedly hate their average player is beyond me, and the hatred bleeds through in their gameplay loop.

If anything Destiny 2 showed me it's that Bungie is a shell of it's former self, enjoy your Eververse, Guardian. Because to Bungie that's all that matters now.\t\t\t\t\t
