\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSolo player here and ive been playing destiny since D1. Destiny is a good game sure its had its ups and downs and I have taken my fair share of "breaks" but I always find myself coming back to it and its similar mechanics make it easy to jump back in. If your just picking it up for the first time expect to get hooked if you are into the sci-fi looter shooter MMO type. I will say, that not having a ton of friends on here is a blessing and a curse. Its a blessing in that I still have a life, its a curse in that I may never see raid/dungeon/trials only loot (since it takes a considerable amount of time to run a raid, less so for the newer dungeons). But to be able to hop on for 30 min - 1 hr, knock out a strike or two and a gambit/crucible match, maybe progress on an exotic quest and then go about with life makes it even more enjoyable.

You can get burned out quickly with the grind though. While new seasons sometimes provide new activities and loot it always follows a similar mechanic.

Community is fairly nice, though it has its fair share of toxic players that expect everyone to be at there level. Definitely seems to be the in the minority though and if you ask questions/are honest most will help.

Lastly, the lack of matchmaking for higher tier activities is frustrating ... I only wish they had something in game that you could use and just get rid of relying on the destiny/3rd party apps to find a group. Being have a Match Making tab that scores your equipment for an activity (so that you have the requisite power level) and then matches you with other players would be amazing, but this is strictly a personal problem, cause im kinda lazy.

So BL: I will always recommend Destiny, especially if you like/are looking for a sci-fi looter shooter MMO type game, destiny can definitely scratch that itch for you.\t\t\t\t\t
