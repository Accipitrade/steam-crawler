\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed the game before new light and since release. Took several breaks because of frustrating pvp metas, lack of content or just problems that were created because Destiny was unable to decide whether it was an mmo or not. For example: There was no world chat, clans were also weird (not sure what was going on with those). Lootboxes for cosmetics was also lame as hell, so I just started getting online from time to time (I paid for all seasons and expansions), just to see how the game developed. It became better. But still there were major problems. It just didn't feel right.

Well... Since returning to destiny with new light it is alot of fun. Both in crucible and pve. Yes there are some problems (a pvp and pve meta that is purely defined by the season) and the in game store still exists (however this f2p is the best way to do it, lootboxes are literally a thing of the past and dont give you the newest items, and they do not give you an ingame advantage over other players).

There is a lot of content, while Shadowkeep in itself (the story mission) is extremely short, there are so many bounties, weapon quests, both planetary and interplanetary (story) missions (for new light players) that the content balance swings into the other direction now; there is so much of it, it becomes nearly overwhelming.

tldr: great game, lots of content, maybe a bit of an annoying meta from time to time.
