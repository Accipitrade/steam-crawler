\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo now that most of the DLC is free I recommend playing this game, the dialog and story can get really boring for the most part. The DLC is great though, if you can I highly suggest getting Forsaken and Shadowkeep, those are really, really fun. I admit it's a lot harder to increase power now, but at the same time it's so much more fun with the updated weapons and armor systems. Combat is so much more immersive than it was before. I hadn't played in a long time so when I started playing around with the new system I was like "Wow this is so much more fun!" and I had a great time. I even started using one of the new bows and I find it very hilarious that in a game where enemies are running around with energy weapons I'm slaughtering them with a primitive weapon. Now if I ended the review here you'd probably think that the game is super easy but don't worry I'm not. There is still the option to play through some pretty difficult areas to give yourself a challenge. There's Leviathan Raids, Dungeons, both Forsaken and Shadowkeep which has a lot of great stuff if you're willing to buy it, There's still Firestrikes and all that jazz. And of course if you want to rip out your eyeballs then go ahead and play PVP. Destiny 2 used to be a pretty meh game, but now it's awesome and as long as they keep working on it(Game devs please fix your servers) it's bound to only get better. Can't wait to see what happens after Shadowkeep, feels like it was building up to something. Great game.\t\t\t\t\t
