\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have over 1k hours on PS4 before I was able to link my acount and bring all my stuff over to PC and have been playing since release day. I would say if you're a fan of looter shooters like Borderlands and you also life MMOs than this game is def for you.

3 playable races and 3 playable classes each with 4 different subclasses (as of BL) and each of those subclasses has 3 different branches (Except for the new subclass "stasis" which has its own way to be customized.

Tons of PvE content, 3 player dungeons called strikes, one thing to note is there isnt any traditional dungeon roles like Tank or healer, but there are class support abilities that can heal your group. Raid content is the hardest in game and take 6 players, and its not like other MMOs where you just kill boss, each encounter usually has a bunch of crazy ♥♥♥♥ to do before boss even becomes damagable, for example standing on a small plate which throwing a ball back and forth between each group member to charge it than hit the boss with it to take down its shield. This imo is one of the best aspects of the game especially day 1 for the worlds first race and trying to figure out the encounter because there is no dungeon journal like in other MMOs.

The PvP scene is also really good and fun, lots of different game mode and ranking systems, the not too long ago brought back Trials of Osiris which was a staple of Destiny 1 which is a team based pvp match.

The final mode is called Gambit which is PvEvP basically youre in teams of 6v6 and you dont see the other team at all. Your whole team is in a somewhat large arena where theres tons of small adds, elite mobs, and mini bosses and when they die they drop motes and each team has a bank in the middle and you have to kill ♥♥♥♥ collect motes and bank them and when 100 motes are banked the Primevil spawns which is bascailly a full on dungeon boss first team to bank their motes and kill their primevil get the win and its best of 3 so first team to win twice gets the win, but the little extra PvP element is thattheres a portal on both sides that will open and let 1 person through at different points in the match, 1 person goes through and has 30-45 seconds to kill enemy players, if the enemy players are holding motes when killed they drop them all, also if you invade while the other tear has their Primevil summoned every enemy you kill heals their Primevil. I've had it come down to both tears killing their boss at the same time and only bc one of my teamates invades and wiped their whole tear were we able to win.

Also there are guilds like other MMOs, in Destiny they're called Clans. And my favorite feature is the Destiny 2 companion app that has a build in group finder by linking your Steam account, it makes it incredibaly easy to get groups for raids or trials or what not.

All in all this game is a 10/10 for me and I would highly reccomend it to anyone into FPS looter shooters with an MMO aspect. :-D\t\t\t\t\t
