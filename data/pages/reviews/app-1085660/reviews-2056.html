Destiny 2 is a good game. With many hours on Steam and many more on PS4, I would say Destiny 2 is fun to play.
But I have a feeling that the team behind Destiny 2, does not listen to the community as much as they should. In my personal opinion, some weapons and gear need to behave differently between PVE and PVP.

Beside that, Raids are fun (I guess) for some people, but the most important part of a Raid is that you need a team. What about the players that play alone almost all the time. That whole content is blocked because you are alone. I do not think that Destiny 2 was meant only for "group-players", also for "lonely-players".

The other thing is Gambit, in general is just annoying to the max. If they put for Glory Crucible a non-premate match-up, then why not for normal Crucible and Gambit. Why do I need to play vs 4-premate-team and lose my mind, because at least one of the players in my team is just trash(the ones that want to have always 15 Motes and never deposit). I would be happy if the other team has a bad-player. So at least is balanced. Beat me with your skills not because you have a 4-premate team.

This Season Content (Trials of Osiris) is also mostly for PVP People(Do not mention Bunkers, I know its PVE but meh).
I am a PVE Player, I bought the game in the beginning for PS4 and then later on got it for free in Steam(there was no thank-you for the players who supported the game when you need it to purchase the game. At least a Banner. I know I know, this is how it works, they need it money/revenue bla bla bla. and also I and many others worked(or somebody else did) and with that money I bought the game(and played many many many hours), show some appreciation to the player, the community, that are there. Personal opinion. Everybody has theirs.)

At the end, I like to play Destiny 2. It has brought me joy and fun with friends or alone and also has made me mad and rage.
I will keep playing maybe you should give it a try too.\t\t\t\t\t
