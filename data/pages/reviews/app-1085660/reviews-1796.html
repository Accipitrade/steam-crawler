\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a hard game to summarize because of how many different elements it combines together to make an amazing experience to play. You have rng loot mechanics similar to an RPG, a shared world with other players like in an MMO, and a bunch of different loot that can roll with unique perks like in a looter-shooter. Each of these elements is toned down a bit, and then combined into Destiny 2 as a whole to create an amazing experience to play.

Starting out as a new light during shadowkeep; the game just hooks you in after you finish the extraneous tutorial stuff and makes you want to keep playing all the way through the endgame. The expansions offer so much additional story and content access that their price is well worth what you receive.

The level of mastery you can achieve is ridiculous as well. As of writing I'm 1.2k hours in, and have mastered hunter, working on mastering my titan, and have not even touched my warlock yet.

If you decide to play, be ready for a grind, and prepare to waste countless hours of your life obtaining that sweet sweet loot.\t\t\t\t\t
