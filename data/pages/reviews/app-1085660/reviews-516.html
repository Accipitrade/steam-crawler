\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game may be a bit difficult to get into, but with the right investments it's worth it.

Great Things:
- Gun Play is great and the different mods and abilities for guns give you a feel for how each weapon is played.
-Armor and Stats, Armor, especially Exotics, give you unique abilities that when paired with the right build, can have drastic effects on play styles!
-Class System, the Class System give guardians some good kits and Ultimates to change tides of battle. Each one truly feels like it can be useful in some kind of situation
-Story, The DLC and lore books give such a scale to this game it is genuinely unreal.
-Variety of Gameplay, The different game modes and ways of building your guardians are gloriously fun to use and even PvP modes can have some enjoyable twists to them.

Now, with each good comes some bad, but that's all I want to say, pick up this game, try it, and hey, if you enjoy it, invest in it, it's a fantastic game if you're willing to put the time, money and effort in\t\t\t\t\t
