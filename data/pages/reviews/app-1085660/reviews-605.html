\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a weird one for me.
On one hand the PVE segments are pretty well done and there's a lot of content to do each week for your 3 characters (if you decide to do 3 that is) to keep you busy. BUT a lot of this content you have to shell out money for, it's all gonna get removed after a year or two to make way for new content, and the whole FOMO thing can make people pretty annoyed. The whole thing of a yearly expansion plus 3 addition season passes with now an additional purchasable dungeon coming later this year can drain the wallet quite quickly if you decide to want to experience it all.

Now PVP... well if you're into that sorta thing then there are plenty of people way better than you to try and learn from. Some of these people have made it an art form in the way they can make you look like the softest, most clumsiest player possible. For all it's worth I would love for there to be a way to separate players partial based on skill and their connection because as it is now it's basically a free for all with new PVP players having to carry the weight of going against players who have played more, have gotten PVP equipment from playing that gives them an edge, and having strategies and routes that maximizes their death dealing.

Overall: 6.5/10 - PVE is quite enjoyable but it feels like PVP takes more and more of a backseat for Bungie as that whole segment sorta becomes a game of Meta. PVP is pain, pain, pain.\t\t\t\t\t
