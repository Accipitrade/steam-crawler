In short, if you want the good stuff (i.e. God Rolls, Ritual Weapons and Exotic Weapons) you need to do some grinding, other than that XP is earned from Daily and Weekly bounties, which can be completed either in PvP or PvE. Power Level increases are done with Pinnacle Gear only, the season pass lasts for 3 months and has nice cosmetics which is earned by XP.
Overall its a good game but sometimes in the name of content all we get is MORE GRIND, won't lie got bored of it and will be thinking a lot before buying the Beyond Light DLC + 4 Season Passes. \t\t\t\t\t
