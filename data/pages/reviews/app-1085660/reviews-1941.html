I came back and played a bit of Shadowkeep when that released but only for a few days. I like playing destiny and if you have friends to play with then it's more fun to go through the higher level content and grinding is faster and more fun with friends as well. While it's only been several hours since I expanded my universe, I had already been playing for free for some time in the games free version. Surprisingly, there was a lot of content available to free users, however, for me personally, the allure of access to the full arsenal of exotics was just too tempting. All in all, the game hits the mark as far as RPG elements, smooth and solid feeling combat system, and relatively challenging A.I in PvE mode. Looking forward to experiencing PvP as I hadn't allowed myself to try it until I had geared to the hilt, that's just me.

A solid and enjoyable looter-shooter with quite a bit of content in both the free and buy to play versions. Fun all around no matter how you slice it.\t\t\t\t\t
