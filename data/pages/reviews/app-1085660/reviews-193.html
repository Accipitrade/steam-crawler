\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll jokes aside, not actually fun. What looks to be a cool PvE/PvP looter shooter set in the future solar system is ruined by an experience that makes you want to kill yourself. The PvE game play itself is fun but really repetitive and that's all this game has going for it. The PvP is ridiculously unbalanced and for championing itself as being an "MMO", the customization and build options suck in comparison to other games. They also threw out old content, some of it paid, as a way to highlight the new paid content. Kinda like a Netflix subscription except there's no Stranger Things to keep you paying. The game play is essentially the same recycled garbage over and over again and the game is incredibly grindy. The only reason to grind and get better equipment is to be able to grind even more. Even with all that, the campaign is probably the most lackluster part of this game. The story is always, "bad aliens try to kill people, good guardians stop them." The early parts of the campaign are actually archived and you don't actually have to complete them in order to progress in the game, which I guess in this case is good because it's bad anyway. The various planets seem really interesting but even that gets wasted as Bungie completely squandered the world building over the years. All in all, what looks like a fun premise is destroyed by an awful game play loop, awful monetization strategies, and a general lack of care to create something that's fun.\t\t\t\t\t
