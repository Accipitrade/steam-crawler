\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe "Undying" title you can earn does not, in fact, stop you from dying. 0/10, would die again.

This is one of those games that it's better to look at the hours played for each reviewer rather than the comments that are given by them. I could talk about the cosmetic micro transactions or the seasonal model or the loot system that feels far too stingy at times. But after nearly two thousand hours and nearly five years of playing this game, it's impossible not to recommend. This game has buttery smooth combat, a vibrant and many millions strong player base, continuing expansions and a developing story line that (for an MMO) is actually fun and enjoyable. The game can be grindy, but that's the genre it's a part of. It's also not particularly welcoming to newer players as the introductory missions are painfully in need of a refresh. I would encourage anyone picking up the game to do it with friends as that will at least make the confusion more bearable.\t\t\t\t\t
