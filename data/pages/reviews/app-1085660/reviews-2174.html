\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst time coming back since House of Wolves burned me. The game is vastly improved and the power grind seems endless. It is a little frustrating that nearly half of your primary sources of gear upgrades come from PvP, if you're not a fan of crucible, or PvP in general.

If you're stepping in new, be prepare to be confused to high heaven about what is going on storywise though. Everything is available from the launch of D2 and it can be very difficult to figure out what happened when or why and what or who anyone is and what they're talking about. Your quest logs can be absolutely loaded without even trying and you'll be progressing quest steps you forgot about and it'll just be a giant "huh?".

The fun gunplay is still there, the game is beautiful, and it's super addictive. The story, as told to new players, is a mess and could use for a serious clean-up. PvP, imho, is too central to progression. All-in-all, definitely worth picking up, especially if it's on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
