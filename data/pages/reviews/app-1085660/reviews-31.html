\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPOSITIVES

It's a beautiful game. The environments are always well done. Everywhere you go, you feel life breathed into it. The music is phenomenal. The armour sets are truly fun to customise with shaders and transmogs.

The gunplay is probably the biggest draw to the game. As someone that primary plays FPS games, I can say with confidence that Destiny 2 has the best the gaming world has to offer. The guns feel so good to shoot and they have come up with some pretty fun exotics in the last 5 years.

Raids and Dungeons are always a blast. (Root of Nightmares was a bit of a letdown but they knocked in out of the park with the Deep Dungeon.) Sucks that you can't play the earlier raids like Leviathan and Scourge of the Past though.

NEGATIVES
If you want to play this game, you're going to need to fork out a lot of money. It's labelled 'Free to Play' but take that very lightly as most of the content that's good and fun is locked behind a paywall and they just keep coming. From Expansions, Seasonal Passes, Dungeon Passes and Event Passes, Bungie doesn't hesitate to reach for the money out of your wallet.

They have improved this but you'll be at a disadvantage if you plan on being a 'solo' player. A ton of the end game content that you grind for is meant to play with more people. There is an LFG system on the Destiny 2 phone app as well as thriving Discord community but the game isn't as fun to play alone.\t\t\t\t\t
