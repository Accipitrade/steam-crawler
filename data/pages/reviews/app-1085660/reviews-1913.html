\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a solid NO from me.

I started my journey of love with Destiny 2 toward the end of Season of Opulence, and enjoyed it up to them starting their vaulted content with Beyond Light. They removed a LOT of content, like the grind on Mars that was an absolute joy when you needed to Masterwork an exotic. Then you had to swap to the moon when no one showed up because the moon was the newest place to grind, but I digress.

I got it that vaulting the zones was part of the story, but double backing some of the weapons? Why not ALL of them? I have a Truth in my vault that was used to make me fall in love with hand cannons, as well as despising them in PvP. It's capped at 1100, so I can't use it anymore. Same goes for all the ritual weapons that I spent hours trying to grind for, or the strike based weapons like The Militia's Birthright that were painful, and you had to pray to RNGesus that it'll pop out.

Having spent well over 2k hours, both the original launcher and on Steam, I can honestly say that I'm tired of the game. It was a great place to grind, meet new people, make new friends, and rage at the raids. The game allows for people to drone, and that's always great when you want to shut off your brain for a couple of hours, but they're going to vault two more zones next month, as well as a LOT more weapons and armor.

If you're a new player, or someone returning, wait until February 22 to see how it is.\t\t\t\t\t
