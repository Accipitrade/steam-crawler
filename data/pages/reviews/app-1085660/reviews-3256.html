\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is my most played game and i love it so much. However it is not welcoming to new players. The beginner experience is not great. I've had many friends try this game out while I guide them, and still, they could not get into it. so without someone to give you help I can not see how that many people with get invested.

Also, while the game says free to play, its not, its free to try. All of this games content is DLC and seasonal passes. And I wouldn't say the game is worth playing unless you get it with the 2 newest DLC at the time whatever they may be. Which makes the cost about the same as a AAA title, and if you want all the DLC it will cost more than that. For me who has been playing for a long time i was able to buy the DLC as it came out, which is not a luxury new players have. It is an investment, I pay $100 a year to get all the Content that comes out that year.

However This game once you do get it is great for PVE. there's a lot of fun to be had if you have all the content. However The most fun comes from Dungeons and Raids. But for Dungeons you need 3 people and for raids you need 6 people. so unless you have dedicated friends you will have to use LFG systems to do them and its hard to find groups that are willing to teach. I've seen a lot of people leave teams or kick people from teams because the encounter was not a success first try.

The Build crafting is the most fun part about this game for me, if your gonna play this game, DO BUILD CRAFTING. There are too many people that miss out on a lot of opportunity because they just throw on whatever and don't make a fully flushed out build. Sadly i think Build Crafting got a big downgrade with the Newest DLC expansion making it very one note and dull. But it still remains my favorite part of this game, I highly recommend Void titan btw.

Then there is PVP... this mode got abandoned. I don't blame Bungie fully as they had other projects they wished to work on and it was more important to focus on PVE rather than PVP. But the only people I see in there now days are the dedicated players that have been playing so long that I cant keep up. I've seen new people go into PVP and be so lost on what is going on that they just get frustrated and never go back to it.

I am unbelievably torn on what to give this game, if there was a mixed option I would chose it. I give it a positive just due to my bias. And again its only worth with DLC, it is not free to play it is free to try. if you play without DLC the game is gonna pop so many things you can buy in your face its not even funny.

and lastly on a side note. The music is S tier, play with music on. the music on a raid boss will give you chills.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI personally love this game and will always come back to it, but lately I've realized that this game is a nightmare for new players to try to navigate. There's so many numbers to keep track of and so many resources and items to learn why you need them that for new players it's basically sensory overload. Some of the older quest lines don't do a good enough job of teaching you where to look next so for new players without a guide or someone to help them it can be very discouraging and leave a bad taste. That being said, if you can find the time to learn all you can about the game and get some outside help when stuck this game will always be a top 10 in your library. By no means is it a perfect game but it sure can be a fun one. There's also plenty of challenge for players who are seeking it. If you turned it away at any point I highly recommend giving it a true second chance and try to discover what you couldn't before.\t\t\t\t\t
