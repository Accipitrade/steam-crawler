\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't let my steam playtime fool you, I have ~100hrs from when this game was part of bnet.

If you read my reviews, you'll notice a theme. This game's content is pretty shallow. It's Call of Duty's MP system placed in it's singleplayer campaign with added co-op and PvP lite. This genre of games sets a pretty low bar (Anthem and Division are really the only other games like it) and it meets expectations, but as someone wants more diversity in their grind I find this game lacking. If this game wants to call itself an MMORPG, it should be taking its cues from other games in the established genre, not Call of Duty with its millions of "unique" guns and armors (aka repaints and slight rekins).

I'm being a bit harsh, the game can be fun, but replayability isn't there and I don't see the appeal of cosmetics, so I don't like the idea of grinding for them when they do nothing but affect the appearance of my character that I don't even get to see in most situations.\t\t\t\t\t
