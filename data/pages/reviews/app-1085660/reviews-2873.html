\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is going to be a short review. Destiny 2 is a great game, and although they have had some odd monetization models in the past, the current direction of the series has me looking forward to its future.

Pros:
-----------------------------------
- The campaign is pretty good, nothing too crazy, but enough there to make you want to play.
- Raids are incredibly fun.
- Weapons, and their quests. It is a lot of fun and challenge to complete some of the quests required for certain weapons, and while this might not be everyone's cup-of-tea I certainly loved some of the puzzles and challenges. (Whisper of the Worm is great fun to unlock!)
- Environments, Bungie constantly develops stellar maps and environments. They need a wallpaper / screenshot mode. Excellent architecture and landscape design.

Cons
-----------------------------------
- There is a grind, and now a season's pass. Not the worst, but it can become a bit chore-like when you hit end-game.
- PVP exists, but it still requires polish, and their most competitive game-mode allows level advantage.
- Raids with the wrong group can be incredibly not-fun.
- Time-gating quests (looking at you City of Dreams).

Overall, a ton of great enjoyment to get out of this game. However you might find yourself entering that normal MMO cycle, returning only to complete the newest content before moving on again.\t\t\t\t\t
