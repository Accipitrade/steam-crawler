\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf I was looking for a game today D2 is a great value. But being a Day 1 Destiny 1 player, I have a very different experience. Constant hope that the game that is good will truly become the greatest game ever as was intended.

No FPS ever has felt as good as Destiny. Pulling the trigger is addicting because the sound, response and overall feel is unmatched. It truly is Halo`s successor. But it is very shallow. It launched in a broken state in both D1 and D2. They had almost no story, no in depth endgame, no deep customization....It started so badly that just now it has become what it should have launched as.

Destiny was the first "10 year plan" game, but Bungie at every turn has decided to piece the game up, resell it in small amounts and break promises about what is coming so many times I have lost count. Out of 10 Seasons maybe 2 of them lived up to the hype, and the rest were complete let downs with some straight up robbery that left me feeling guilty for paying for it. I literally bought the seasons just to not miss out on a few guns and armor that the next season made "meh".

They charged me 89$ for a broken game that was 10 steps worse than how D1 ended. No unique weapon perks or armors, if you owned a weapon , you would get 20 more and just delete them all because they were the exact same. The raid was meh compared to masterpieces like Vault of Glass. Exotics were boring and the staples from D1 were gone. Destiny became a casuals game but worse super shallow and lifeless exactly how D1 launched. But D1 turned it around in the next 3 years. SO we were all so sad at how they made the same mistakes and even doubled down on the bad ones.

Then I had to pay for a DLC every 4 months??? So by now I have paid 400$+ so IMO of course it was not worth it. But if you are buying all this content that i digested over 3 years for less than a AAA title I would say its a absolute steal.

Destiny2 was supposed to be the Hybrid FPS/Space RPG GOAT, like Half Life, Whitcher3, GTA but in space with Jedi powers and a story so rich it would take years to learn. The problem is it never came close to the idea of it, but it looks so damn pretty and feels so amazing you can forget about the fact that its story makes no sense and can be played with the sound off skipping cut scenes and still being the same level of enthused.

Its like a perfect looking person who at first seems deep but really is not at all. You get sick of them fast, but after only a few hours you can look at them again and hope you were wrong only to learn a few hours later....You were right. And the cycle repeats over and over. This was supposed to be Knights of the old Republic and Whitcher in story telling but most the story is in lore cards and not the actual game.

The best anology is....Football prospects. D2 was the player who everyone "Expert" says will be this generations Icon. They all talk about him breaking all the records, and before hes even drafted are talking about how much better he is than the pros who actually play in the league. Teams are willing to sink there entire current well being to trade all the draft picks for him etc.

D2 isnt a Ryan Leaf or Jamarcus Russel [although it almost was 5+ times and it took the playerbase leaving the game for them to finally listen.] Its more like a Cam Newton. On paper he is the QB you would build. But looking at only stats he is an average NFL starter. But what makes him so enraging is when he shines for periods he reminds you of what he was supposed to be and he is untouchable, but it never lasts no matter if its his fault or injury etc.

The team who gets him cheap after the original team severs ties [because they were so let down.] is getting one hell of a deal. They are getting a guy who is a middle of the pack NFL QB who will have moments of absolute dominance, and if not you just cut him. But for the team who gave away 3 years of draft picks, and actually believed in there souls it was there time to be a part of greatness.....Well its hard for them to not see only negatives.

TL;DR
If your a new player who isnt expecting the sun and stars and you realize it is not really a RPG but a FPS that has customizations and you have another game in your life that you can alternate with, D2 is probably the best pick there is. But, dont by the hype Bungie sells. The yhave proven time and again to not just be incompotent, but highly dishonest and they are not the same company who created HALO. The yhave been riding that gravy train for years but the creative geniuses left before D1 came out.

Luke Smith has been the most inept game lead I have ever witnessed. He allowed a game that should have been the biggest game in history to slowly die to the point where they had to go free to play on steam. He has made every mistake you can make at every turn.

Dont be to hopeful. Take it for what it is, a beautiful game that is Halos spiritual successor but honestly has never surpassed it and should have. But for pure mindless enjoyment its great.\t\t\t\t\t
