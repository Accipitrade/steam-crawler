\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been meaning to make this review for a long time now, but never knew what to say, after 750 hours you would think i know :/ . This game was a light for me for a long time and i took solace in the raids that present itself in the game. Having 6 people fighting bosses and doing challenges is a blast that still gives a kick even now after so long. I was in it entirely for these raids and i still love them even after what Bungie has been doing since season of the undying back in October last year (abouts). For me, unfortunately there are way too many bad balance changes, and lots of ways damage were changed that made these annoying and difficult, which is why i have stayed away so long.

I will not address multiplayer here, i never liked it but you can discover it for yourself, but bear in mind that some weapons are locked behind pve so there's that i guess. The main campaign is fun but short, and CoO and Warmind are wastes of time. However both Shadowkeep and Forsaken are great until you see the price take that comes with them. All of them have lots of missions so you can spend hours in this game and it has loads of content.

The best thing about this game are the enemies(besides the scorn XD) with most bosses being unique and quirky and with a lot of different factions to fight. The gunplay use to be fantastic, and is still great but changes were made that make it clunky and annoying sometimes. The ui is very friendly and simple to use, but missions take a long time to be rid of along with some materials however this can be solved with 3rd parties like DIM.

It has been a tough decision for the verdict I will give this game. On the one hand i used to love it, maybe one day I will again, this game is a roller coaster after all. On the other, everything I hate about games has happened with fun and useful bugs being removed and bullsh*t ones not, and terrible balancing that makes riot games and blizzard look good. I don't know the future of the game, but I hope they fix their sh*t, otherwise I ain't coming back.

Apologies for any bad grammar I may have missed but English isn't my strong point.\t\t\t\t\t
