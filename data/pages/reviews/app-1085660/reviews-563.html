\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny was to be a full ten year Odyssey for players. A place where we grew and expanded your powers as players while enjoying a rich background of intriguing places and dangerous foes.
While my account shows "only" 397 hours, We actually pre ordered original Destiny on PSN and played for thousands of hours. Raids, daily bounties, all across three characters.. that was EVERY bounty for three characters EVERYDAY then include Raids and strikes etc.
We left the game after Destiny 2 and all the "adjustments" to the equipment, wiping of character progress, and the issues with the game loop/software did not help but we tried to stay but it just was not fun or worth putting any effort into.
So today, 12/7/2021 at 10:48a.m., I finally decided to make this review of the "new" bungie Destiny2.
We do not know the "free to play" experience as we decided to just buy back into the game. I hear it is a lot of dead ends leading a "Free Player" to the "Paywall" to continue the small amount of content that led them to the paywall in the first place.. My experience is with the Splicer content and the small add on required. I was led to a quest paywall.. I imagine it is far worse for the true free player.
Content: We have all current and legacy content. In fact I am writing this post at the very time Bungie is upgrading for the new 30th anniversary pack. which I am sure my wife will make me watch her play.
Thus highlighting the schism about Destiny/Destiny2/Destiny........whatever number they use..
I see the entire affair as a business model, my wife sees it a a fun Sci-Fi world and ignores the vagrant actions of Bungie over time, the End-less grind, the pay to Emote/Dress model, the sub-software products used in "focusing Customer attention to desired in game products, and the entire subscription model Bungie has adopted to remain viable as a source of income for those individuals in the company.
In my role as the "Game Breaker" for my Wifes interests in gaming, I am intimately engaged with Destiny2, and speak with authority to the details of the game and I am a bit mixed.
Bungie may be finally able to tell the story we all expected them to in the first place. The corporate intrigue around Bungie is worth a book/movie deal alone, the fact that they are somehow cobbling together something close to the original vision of Destiny is worth respect.
Do I trust them? no....never,,,
Are they capable of making a good game? sure they are! will they?...no idea!
So as I sit a wait for the servers to reset I wonder what Bungie will ultimately become.
For now, if you wish to play the game I recommend buying the entire DLC set, plan to purchase every little bit of content, and at this rate spend 100s of monies to stay current with Bungies income plan..
For in the end, no matter the talent and execution, all this game is,,, is an INCOME PLAN.....
Plan accordingly.\t\t\t\t\t
