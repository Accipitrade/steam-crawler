\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm here to write a review of the state of this game as of June 29th, 2023.

This game is in a VERY tight spot, the community is upset with Bungie, and they're not doing anything about it.

I'll start off with what I think is the absolute pinnacle of Bungie not caring about their game or community.

PvP. The current state of PvP is absolutely abhorrent. We haven't seen any new maps added into PvP for a LONG while.
We saw the return of Meltdown, but that's it. The PvP meta is also one of the worst I've ever seen. This season (season of the deep) has been the absolute worst time for PvP I have ever seen. This seasons meta is worse than the mountaintop recluse times. And I'll go through each concern I have in a list.

Weapons - If you haven't already gotten PTSD from the noise of immortal alone, I salute you. It's the most broken SMG the game has ever seen, almost everyone is running it, and if they're not they're running something equally as bad or just sitting in the back of the map with Randy's Throwing Knife and a sniper, or using a fusion rifle that has more range than some SMGs. I'm not even going to talk about Le Monarque. You probably already know why it's one of the weapons I'm complaining about.

Armor - My god if I haven't seen a game without a titan running peacekeepers and immortal or antaeus wards and a shotgun or a fusion rifle then I wouldn't even be writing this. Not to say that other exotics AREN'T broken, but these 2 are the most ridiculous. It requires almost ZERO skill using antaeus, all you do is slide and charge a fusion rifle, and whatever is in front of you gets turned into a mist of elemental dust. Along with peacekeepers. Buffed SMGs sounds nice till you realize that also means immortal, which is already broken in the first place, OH not to mention that Bungie also said the 1. Nerfed aggressive frame SMGs, but also nerfed immortal in general staring in season of the deep. I guess they must have just sneezed on the gun because it still DOMINATES all leader-boards in PvP by FAR.

Matchmaking - Here we go... My absolute most hated thing in this game. You like competitive games? Like ranked matchmaking? Well, too bad. The state of competitive in this game is not even worth mentioning. There's NO SKILL BASED MATCHMAKING!!! Matchmaking, they say, is based off of how you played in the week, and connection. Sounds like skill based matchmaking until you go into the game and realize that no matter how bad or good you do, you just go against the same level of people forever. I know when I say this that some people might just say I have a skill issue. I agree, I'm not great at PvP, I know I need to work harder to get better. However. I'm still going to mention the fact that Ascendant is near impossible to get for anyone even decent at the game, unless you get carried. I've been stuck at Adept III for the past 3 weeks. I've been getting better, but you'll just get matched with teammates that don't play with you, feed, or just leave after losing one match. Solo queue is terrible, its 50/50 on your teammates being team players or trying to rush in and 1v3 every round. Duo queue is better, but you're going to get people duoing that are probably already ascendant, or just PvP gods. Trios are in my opinion the worst. I've been known to just have really bad luck with matchmaking. I get around 1300 ELO on trials report, or about top 15-20% of players. But whenever I go against people in comp, they're top 5-0.1% in the world. The skill gap between those ranks is HUGE. I shouldn't be going against these people, I'm nowhere NEAR the skill level of these people who never see sun cause they want to get the most flawlesses in a week, or they're carrying people for twitch views or being paid $20 and upwards of $60 for carries. Matchmaking is terrible, and I have NO CLUE why a COMPETITIVE game mode has NO SKILL BASED MATCHMAKING.


Alright. I ranted about PvP enough. Time for PvE and storylines.

The Season of The Deep - Or as others like to call, Season of the Errors. Bungie for some reason has not been able to keep their servers up. Keep in mind, this is a MULTI-BILLION dollar company. I've seen mobile games with better server uptime than Destiny 2. The story is kind of fun, yipee, we get to go under water, well, methane. Whatever. It looks nice, we got titan back, learned about the enemy of the witness, cool. Now until the next season there's nothing to do. To put in perspective just how dry this season is, I did solo flawless on the new dungeon because I was bored. TWICE. I have NEVER considered myself an insane PvE player, but I got so bored that I decided that it was the only thing left to do this season.

State of the game in general - Destiny 2 has always been a fun game to me. I loved Destiny 1, and have been playing Destiny 2 since it came out. But over time seeing how this game has degraded is honestly depressing... Micro transactions left and right, more things that used to be free now being locked behind paywalls, servers always down, issues not being fixed and so on. I had a lot of hope last year in Witch Queen that Bungie was going to fix the game and make it fun again. I couldn't have been more wrong. I have little hope that this game will continue. I can only feel like Bungie is just squeezing out every last drop from the wallets of loyal players. You used to just pay a flat amount for the yearly expansion and all content added in that year was free. But you now have to pay for the yearly expansion, seasons, dungeons, events, and even simple things like shaders are locked behind expensive paywalls. The dungeon alone is a $20 addon if you don't buy the deluxe edition. Each season, $12. Events, $10 for am "upgraded card". on top of the $50 for the expansion alone. With the amount that each year costs to get all of the content, you could just buy a AAA title game and have everything added for free with updates. However, if you've been playing this game since it's early days, it's almost like an addiction. You really want to see the end of the game, but you need to dish out hundreds of dollars for it and I know Bungie knows what they're doing. They're going to finish Destiny 2, and forget about the game so they can work on their next money hole, Marathon.

Conclusion - If you haven't gotten into this game yet, don't. If you have already, I feel sorry for you. We all wanted more than this, and rightfully deserve much more than what we are given currently. I'm just hoping some kind of miracle happens and they fix all the problems with the game, but I highly doubt they will. I'm dissapointed with this game, and I really wish it wasn't that way. The only thing I'm looking forward to in this game, is the end. I just want the game to be at its end already, no more story, no more expansions, no more seasons. I just want the game to have an ending so I can stop playing and not feel like I'm missing out on something. And I know I'm not the only one...

TL:DR - Don't get addicted to the game, It's GOING to break your heart.\t\t\t\t\t
