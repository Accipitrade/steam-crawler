\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYeah it's pretty good. The grind is pretty well paced and you feel like you can always make progress towards the light cap, until you hit 950 gear score. I'm fine with the slog of a grind from 950 to 960 gear score, but my big problem with it is that there are literally not enough drops in the season to get all the way to 960, unless they introduce a new way to get quite a few more pinnacle drops before the season ends, but other than that, it's pretty fun, easy to play for long sessions, great game to play with your friends, especially because it's primarily PvE, so it's less stressful than playing online multiplayer games with said friends (who may or may not be trash at it). There's tons of stuff to collect, mainly in the form of exotic weapons and armor, and I have to mention that the quests you complete to get these exotics are in my opinion the right amount of satisfying, making you feel like you really earned the exotic while also not burning you out making you do to much. There are micro transactions, but I've had the game since launch, and I have never felt compelled to buy anything, its purely cosmetic, and in my opinion, for better or for worse, generally uninteresting. Also before I end this I have to mention the graphics, 10/10 top notch. Heckin beautiful, fantastic settings and places, probably one of the best looking games I've ever played. And, of course, it's now free to play, so I really recommend you give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
