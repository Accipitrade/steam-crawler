\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a fun game when playing PVE with friends or solo. Lots of activities to keep you busy for hours on end. The PVP is also very fun at times, but Destiny 2 has quite honestly become pay to win. With each big update or seasonal update, Bungie adds new sub-classes for each class. The only way to be able to obtain these new sub-classes however, is to buy the expansion pack, which is a lot more expensive than most AAA titles I've played. That being said, the most recent expansion has introduced the ability to freeze opponents, and there is absolutely nothing that you as a player can do against it. At least with the previous ability updates, you would be able to dodge in some sort of way, and in my opinion, they required a bit more skill to actually use well. My only gripe with the game is, like I said, it's very pay to win, and it gives you the feeling that Bungie just doesn't care about the player. I have completed almost every campaign mission that I could and I've collected a large amount of exotics for the classes that I play(Hunter and Warlock), which for me, is leaving pretty much nothing else to do except for gambit and crucible. Either way, there is some form of pvp, which means that you will most probably be paired with people who own the most recent expansion pack. This review has mostly been about the new abilities, because quite honestly, I hate them. I don't even think that would use them because of how much I hate them. They are completely unfair to people who aren't able to own the expansion. Other than that, destiny 2 is a really fun game, especially when played with friends. I have over 600/700 hours in the game, and I can definitely say that I have enjoyed a really big chunk of it. To summarise, the game is very fun when played with friends, but once you get bored of PVE and start dabbling into PVP, you might start getting annoyed with how unfair the pay to win is.

EDIT:
The servers are complete garbage, even with decent internet connection. I can't even reach the character selection screen, because I keep getting network errors, whether I'm using wifi, ethernet cable, or even mobile data.\t\t\t\t\t
