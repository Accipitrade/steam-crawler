\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen Destiny 2 first came out it was in a weird spot. It looked amazing and ran incredibly well, even on mediocre hardware. The quality of the console port was astounding. However the gameplay itself was a big step back from Destiny 1. Abilities took forever to recharge, and exotic guns were just boring.

So why the positive review? Because now it's actually pretty awesome again. The ability recharge rates are much faster, the exotics feel kinda cool now. It still has it's negatives, PvP is heavily meta-based and some exotic catalysts (minor upgrades to exotic guns) are ludiciously grindy to get but overall it's a lot of fun. Plus the Shadowkeep expansion is more of the serious with bits of fun tone that made the first game great. Opposed to the "well everyone liked how we joked around once in a while in the first game so let's just filled the second game with non-stop jokes!" of the second on release.

I mean, it's free, try it out.\t\t\t\t\t
