\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 somehow manages to always feel fresh, yet so familiar. With tight gun game, smooth controls, excellent optimization, and remarkable visuals, the game never ceases to surprise with yet another great vista, or yet another challenging conflict.

A few drawbacks though - the engine seems to be quite old, as exhibited by a number of corridors that connect the levels. Perpetual loading screens can easily ruin the experience. Personally, I have consistently experienced problems with matchmaking, often playing with Chinese players with terrible ping, while located in Northern Europe.

Still, at its core, Destiny 2 is a great game, and should be in everyone's library!\t\t\t\t\t
