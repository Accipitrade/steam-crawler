\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's fun enough for new players, albiet a little confusing. You are bombarded with so much content as a returning player that it will sometimes take several minutes to process your surroundings and situation. Like initially, I didn't know what to do as I'm used to a fps with some direction. Initially, when D2 released it had a linear progression but after several expansions and other content drops, its pretty much a sandbox now. You can do whatever you want in any order and still get loot for your level.

I really appreciate this because I can tackle the content however I want. After 12 hours or so I eventually got the gameplay loop down but was extremely bored with the repetitive gameplay. One example is how the mission structure works. Go to an area, grab mission, kill a bunch of mobs and then sit in a room and defend it. Nearly every story or capaign mission ends like that.

Other than that, the gameplay is fun. The gun play feels impactful. The AI have good pathing and know how to use cover. All in all, definitely worth the $33 I spent on all the content.\t\t\t\t\t
