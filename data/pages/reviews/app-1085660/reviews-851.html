\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThough it might be entertaining for people that like grindy "looter-shooter" and "sponge-shooter" games (à la borderlands etc), the developers of the game have a perpetual recurring trend of removing content and "reintroducing" it or reusing old content from the previous installation in the series. They even seem to struggle with the most mediocre aspect to get even somewhat right when it comes to the PVP side of the game, and that's lag-reduction and what's known as tickrate. Like dying before the enemy is even around the corner in PVP? Like to die to attacks that are fired off 2 seconds after you died from it? Horribly balanced weaponry that are exclusive to those that are active end-game PVE players and use them in PVP with no remotely comparable PVP-sourced equivalent? This and more like it? Then this might be the game for you.

Sure, it's an ok time-passer, but you basically cannot get any meaningful enjoyment out of it if you have a job and normal life that prevents you from obsessively farming and raiding, but that's most MMO's though, just particularly noticeable in it hidden in a veil of "muh cosmetics!" while you cannot breach beyond a "light level" rank that allows for getting better items because you're stuck in the ouroboros of getting sub-optimal items while never getting any particularly higher "light level" on the items and just keeps you at net-zero most of the time. Tooltips are also often not very helpful, questing often requires you to find help elsewhere outside of the game just to figure out what on earth you're supposed to do.

But, if none of this is a deterrent for you, I'll say it again as I said before; it's an ok time-passer, so it might still float your boat enough to just have a time-passer like this.. because lets be honest, the era of MMO's (especially MMORPG's) is over, and the fact that this manages to still keep a somewhat strong cult-following is testiment to potential it has, even if it often seems like the developers don't prioritize the right things to address.

So since there's only a "yes" or "no" option, I'll just set this as a "no" even though it is in all honesty actually more of a ginormous "maybe".\t\t\t\t\t
