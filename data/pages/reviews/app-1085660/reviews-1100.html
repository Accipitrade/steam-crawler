\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a very tough game to recommend to others. New light offers a semi free to play model, that locks you into, depending on the week, maybe 1-2 hours worth of content. After the opening level, you get a few older locations, vanguard strikes, sometimes a nightfall, and a crucible where you get locked into an underpowered situation without stasis, and lacking the ease of access to exotics found with the season pass and/or the expansion. Putting money into the game can immediately make it a great experience, putting you on par with other players, and unlocking a good amount of different kinds of content to grind and enjoy while you level. I've played this franchise since year 1 of Destiny, and have put money into it for every expansion except for one. I love this game, but can't, at least with the current model, recommend playing destiny 2 with the amount of money you need to put in to enjoy the content. I will always tell people to try it out, and see if they COULD like it. Beyond light however, makes that difficult. Overall, I do not recommend playing destiny unless you can really see yourself putting a lot of time into the game.\t\t\t\t\t
