\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet's start off by saying that my hours on this game for steam is not a representation of how long I've played. I started on console and have well over 1,000 hours, plus my newly acquired PC time. I have enjoyed a lot of the previous expansions for Destiny 2 and although I would get annoyed with the character customization debacle of "new content, new forced blue gear look" ....I pressed on.

Pros:

- Graphically stunning compared to most AAA titles
- Next to NO bugs like many AAA titles
- Good sound and music quality
- Good for that FPS itch you are having without being forced to play PvP
- Typically a year expansion with roughly 3 to 4 events throughout the year for those that like to grind
- New Europa planet is pretty cool. It has weather changing impact so it's hard to see enemies sometimes (GREAT TOUCH BUNGIE!)

Cons:

- Every clothing ornament is locked behind a paywall therefore customizing your character requires more cash
- Exotic Weapons, no way to unlock ornaments in the game to change the look (They've finally got some exotics that you can add color / shaders to now but still, very lackluster)

**NOTE: How do you become attached to your character if they don't represent themselves the way you desire? Well, in Bungies case, it's spend more money in the Eververse shop for something you MIGHT like...because they are very limited. OR you can grind for gear and hope that it actually has a full set.....I'd look under armor first to see if it's worth your time.

- Removing previous content (planets) we paid for from previous DLC or that I paid for when I bought the original base game.

**NOTE: In an article from the lead for Bungie they said "It's too much work for the devs to keep up all he planets and they weren't being used as much as other so we'll remove those planets to possibly come back at a later date and we'll add the new planets in place". Here's the thing. They removed 4 planets and gave us one...that is equal? Either way, we paid for it and it should be ours. This is just one of those moments when you realize that digital goods / services can be taken away from you at anytime. You have the right to that data up until THEY decide to no longer provide it. This goes for digital movies you own on other platforms like Vudu. Anyway, All of us paid for that content and we should have been able to keep the content. <--Not cool!

- So many different types of currency and parts and pieces with literally NO tutorial on what they are all for. Any new player will definitely have to Google how to play this game.

**NOTE: For playing this game I mean upgrading items, where to get the items, how LONG (my GOD...how long will it take) to get said items. Needless to say....it's a very grindy game.

- The CONSTANT re-use of previous material. Instead of making something new they will just use a mission start and end point in the opposite direction to make a "new and exciting mission". !

FINAL:

With all the time I have played on this game with all the expansions on both Xbox, Playstation, and now PC, I cannot recommend this game any longer. You no longer are rewarded clothing ornaments for seasons, you have to pay for EVERY SINGLE THING. They are really milking this. If you really want to play an FPSMMORPG then I suggest you go and play Borderlands. There is a reason those games can still sell for 60 bucks a pop for years and years and years later. Same with Diablo.

Bungie has left from Activision and I had hope which is why I purchased this expansion but it is clear to see that they have adopted the EA and Activision publisher mentality instead of following the developers / publishers such as Gearbox (2k Games) and Blizzard.
