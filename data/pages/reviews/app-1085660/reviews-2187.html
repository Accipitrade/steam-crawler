\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI initially liked this game and didn't understand the hate. But...
Having played nearly a year, and exhausting the major story campaigns... I've come to realize this game hates solo players and punishes progress. New content seems to be focusing exclusively on mandatory team play in events that don't provide matchmaking, pvp without skill based matchmaking, which favors team play, and is actively moving away from solo sandbox opportunities. The only thing solo players can do once the campaigns have been exhausted, is grind for better equipment to use while grinding.

plus, this solstice armor nonsense taught me something that i hate about "leveled" games. The weaker your stats are, the stronger you are. Because the enemies that scale to you (most of them) are not balanced to be on par with their level in relation to yours. The closer you are to the level cap, the stronger they are. but if you're 100 levels low, they're weak as sh*t and you cut through them like paper. So killing baddies gets more difficult the stronger you are, which is baffling.
I'm sure this mechanic is used to suck in new players and make the game more fun, but once you're not new, it's just a source of frustration.

So with the changes being made in the Beyond Light expansion, all of the loot you grind for now has an expiration date. Couple that with being asked to pay $40 for half the sandbox being deleted, and new content being locked behind multiplayer that isn't match-made... I'm out.

I no longer recommend this game.

-----------------------------------------------------

My previous comments on March 24, 2020:
This game is trying to fill a lot of positions at once. It's trying to please the original crowd that paid full price for the game. It's trying to please all the new-comers that started in October. It's trying to please the people that love PvP. It's trying to please the people that love PvE. It tries to please the people with friends that always play together, as well as the people that play solo. So there's something for everyone assuming you ever liked a FPS in your history of gaming. And there's also something to annoy everyone.

Personally I'm a solo PvE kind of player that started in October; and there's plenty of content for that. My only gripes are that as a solo player, there's a lot of top tier rewards locked behind PvP gameplay, or EXTREMELY difficult campaigns that are impossible to solo for the average player. I'm not just referring to the extreme difficulty of 3 simultaneous barrier champions that are 30 levels above you in a legendary lost sector that you're trying to solo and the only way I got past that was to cheese it. However the whole nature of what this game considers a puzzle to solve is poorly done. (Shattered Throne, Whisper of the worm, etc) The game just doesn't do a great job of telling you what you should be doing or where you should be going. And sometimes on top of all that, there's a ridiculous time limit that makes no sense.

I do wish they'd make the reward system a little more accessible to the solo player. The nightfall reward system makes perfect sense to me: harder difficulty, higher level rewards, But nothing exclusive is locked behind the highest difficulty. Unfortunately many of the quests for the gold level loot are impossible for a solo player to achieve. Which sucks.

Hopefully they enable crossplay so I can play with my console peasant friends. But whatever, I'm still playing. It's still fun, the seasonal content and campaigns have kept me busy enough that I don't feel cheated. Granted, I have spent a fair bit of money on the content for this 'free' game.

additional points of interest:

This game hates poor connections. If you live in the woods and have a garbage high speed connection, you probably can't play this game as there's no offline mode and this game is thirsty for that data. It's a big part of why I'm stuck playing solo. My usual team can't play this game.

I see a lot of people complaining about recycled content, but I'm new to the game, so that isn't apparent to me.

If you ever see me in the crucible, feeding the pigeons or sitting in a chair waiting to be killed, rest assured I'm just checking off the mandatory PvP requirements of some exotic quest. Enjoy the free kills.\t\t\t\t\t
