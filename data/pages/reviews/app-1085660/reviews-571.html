\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHEAVILY PVP BIASED REVIEW:
I have over 1000 hours in this game over several consoles and a sleeve tattoo dedicated to how much I loved this game. The key word here is loved because the game as it is now is unplayable. The same weapon metas dominate every single PvP match with all of the matches beings so heavily drowned in skill based matchmaking that it's not even a competition and you'll be losing most games. I had a consistent 1.6-1.7 KD and a 30% win rate so take that as you will. The abilities are oppressive with Stasis being still wildly powerful on hunter classes, or bottom tree striker, or etc.

The raids are great and pretty enjoyable. That aspect of the game is blooming in my opinion. The artificial difficulty can be more annoying than challenging at times but overall not the worst experience out there.

As a PvE game alone, I think Destiny is one of the most expansive games out there. It has tons of lore and story that makes it a joy to play and learn about. As a PvP game, Destiny is one of the most miserable experiences I've ever had. I wouldn't recommend anyone play PvP this season, and I'm afraid based on years of experience and seeing how the balance team works, I don't think that it'll get better with Witch Queen. I canceled my pre-order and I highly recommend that if you wanted to pick a game for PvP then I'd go with Halo Infinite.\t\t\t\t\t
