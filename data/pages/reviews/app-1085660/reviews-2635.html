\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell, actually this is the worst AAA - game I've ever seen. Main problems are servers and especially, as far as I'm concerned - NET-code. The number of problems with Destiny 2 is not huge, it's extremely huge.
1) Competitive game
a) Shots through the wall are common here because in D2 we have shooter's priority. If sniper with ping ~500 will kill you through the wall, don't worry, it is Bungie's game and everything is OK. Peak of my experience was when I killed a guy, played for ~10-15 seconds and got killed by this guy by shotgun from HIS RESPAWN, through 6 walls (later I really counted number of walls). So, OK.
b) Corpses can shoot - don't forget about this thing because in this game you can be killed even by dead body, more often by its melee attack.
c) Aim-assist - bullets change their trajectory depending on your weapon aim assist. Sometimes you can kill not your target but somebody near. For example, here you need to aim higher than at head if you want to headshot with sniper rifle. You can check how it works on Youtube.
d) Matchmaking - NO. Here you don't have matchmaking because in your team can be 2 newcomers while enemies will have ~970 item power. And this problem is inseparably linked with NET-code. Drops out of matches are common thing here too, so you have to play instead of party 3, you have only 2 or even 1 person at your team. This problem is rare for me but my friend had 30 drops per 4 hours. Also because of Bungie's great errors system codes of mistakes are uncommon and there is no recommendations what to do.
There are a lot of little problems which I can't exactly remember now. But I think for competitive it's enough.

2) Gambit - quite interesting game mode consisted of PVP + PVE but because of the 1st has some equal problems as competitive game. Also has it's own great bug. Lags - unreasonable and unexpected, here you can have drops from 60-120 FPS to 4-10. Why? I don't know. You could say that my PC is the reason. I have GTX 960 and Ryzen 5 - 1600 and for minimal settings as I know it's enough.

3) Global problems:
a) Balance is not in this game, it is far far away from here. You have a lot of weapons with auto-aiming (don't mess it with aim-assist), for example in Gambit a lot of people are using rocket launcher "Truth". Only thing that you need - take your target in sight for 1 second than LMB and search for new, rockets will follow your target by themself.
In competitve the same, hand grenade launcher is something that I think should be restricted in PVP because 1 shot and you're already dead.
b) ALT + TAB, those keys are cursed. When I return in game after alt+tab lags in Gambit appears and there is no way to solve this problem. The only solution - restart of D2.
c) PVE matchmaking is also leaves no hope because sometimes you have to pass through dungeon solo.

So that's it for now. Maybe later I'll return to this game but now I don't want to play it at all. Play or not undoubtedly is up to you but I can't recommend this game. PVE is great, story and weapons, visual part, everything is great but bugs ruin everything.

In our party we have a joke about rarity of bugs because each of us has own bug, there are common, rare, epic and exotic bugs.\t\t\t\t\t
