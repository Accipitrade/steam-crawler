\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite a shady history of monetization and grindy quests that can put even WoW to shame, this game keeps me coming back. And part of that is due to the regular content drops and unique worlds, story, and combat situations. The game is visually stunning, and to a casual player, there is more than enough content to keep you busy. I always take criticism with a grain of salt, when coming from people with hundreds of hours of play. Of course with any game, when you reach that point you'll reach a drought. Destiny has fast paced gameplay that makes you feel like a badass, gear that makes you want to quest or grind for it, and a sense of progression that even when stagnated, still has that feeling of victory when you grow in power level. This game does have a history, and it does have its faults. But if you're looking for a fun, beautiful FPS that has some MMO like features, where you feel like a total badass at times... This game is just fine for you. Start with the base game first though, and then decide if it is worth investing for the expacs. And don't let the 40 hr play time fool you, I had this game before steam and played well over 250 hrs.\t\t\t\t\t
