\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust for reference i played Destiny 1 on xbox and ps4 as well as destiny 2 on both of those as well. From the perspective of someone who has played and followed this game since essentially the very beginning: this game is fantastic. Is it grindy? ya. is it ridiculous and annoying. no. If you have to work hard for something in this game 99 out of 100 times its worth it. rather than a pay wall you get better gear/weapons by playing. This game has had issues in the past, things the community felt Bungie "mishandled", etc. most of which I've seen fixed.
NOW. 2 important points.
1.) Taking all my past experience with the game it self i can say on PC 100% recommend even more. Visually stunning and smooth gameplay. Waaayyyy better overall control and makes the whole game feel like it was, dare i say it, made to be played on PC.
2.) This may be the most important part: this is a game that has been around nearly 4 years. With constant maintenance and new content added. I feel its very hard to maintain a game this long and keep it interesting with new engaging content. However; Destiny does this phenomenally.\t\t\t\t\t
