\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like this game, even though it is not perfect. It's incredibly beautiful and many level designs are truly awe inspiring, the art and the graphics are so good it's almost rare to see games reach such pinnacle of beauty, I am not joking here. I went into the sunken pyramid for a mission and holy schmoly that place is just so pretty detailed and alien in its expression and detail. And beautiful. Voice acting is top notch and there's a lot of it. The story and the lore is pretty damn good too. The game runs incredibly well too, very very stable, I havent had a single crash or disconnect. And I get nearly constant 60fps on my somewhat old medium gaming rig, on the highest graphics on 1920x1080 with render resolution maxed to 200%. (my rig: ryzen 5 2600, gtx1080 8GB (not ti), 16gb ram).

The gunplay and the classes is a joy to play, it works really well. I wont shy away from comparing the experience with Borderlands 2 and 3, it's much the same type of doing it and at least as successful and well working as in those games.

It does have some serious issues like incomprehensible game mechanics for skills and abilities that works ambigously in many situations, and much needed central gamebreaking/gamechanging information is simply not there and is entirely omitted. Im pretty tired of having to research all this crap by googling, watching youtube videos and asking reddit questions. It shouldnt be necessary, it should be possible to learn and understand these things from the game itself. When the game makes claims in tooltips about abilities and effects any kind of exceptions to the rules and descriptions laid out MUST be mentioned, but they almost NEVER are in this game. It's a major design flaw and it caused me so much annoyance and wasted time.

The game also isnt really free, I mean it technically is, but good luck trying to get anywhere without buying the latest DLC. You can technically also not buy the season pass, but you'll be at a great disadvantage, it's not like Fortnite where it's cosmetic, you actually get HUGE benefits to xp, gear and ressources. Some important features are also locked behind previous seasons, and those season cannot be bought directly anymore but only acquired by buying the current deluxe edition of the game (which has quite the steep price).

All those negative points being said I cant ignore the immense positive points I mentioned in first paragraph. This game is an amazing experience, I truly enjoy it. It's probably worth buying most of the DLC and the season pass each time a new one comes, at least I feel im getting my worth of content. Deluxe version seems to steep imo. Also note there is at least one of the DLC's that seems to have content removed that people paid for, where they can no longer play the story(?) I havent tried it myself, but that doesnt sound good. So you may want to be aware of this when buying DLC, take care not to buy the one that they gutted (but are still selling!)\t\t\t\t\t
