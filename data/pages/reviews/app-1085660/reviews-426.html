\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 started out on a rough start. Throughout the years Bungie shaped this game into one of the best looter shooters on the Market. Another added bonus is the communication between the devs and the player base. Every week (Almost depending on holidays) they post a blog including upcoming changes, content, and community events. Speaking of the community, depending on where you search you can meet the nicest people you'll know all the way to the most toxic. Although every game has toxicity this community overall loves to help new players learn the game.

A topic I wanted to cover was content vaulting. I understand the frustrations surrounding the subject and agree on some of the matters. Overall, I really don't realize the loss of the content when playing due to the content being added to replace it. If you do not feel the same way, I get it.

All together if you're looking for a looter shooter that will keep you with it, this is the one. I promise you that the content is worth it, the people who disagree are a small percentage of the community. 10$ for a season that keeps content fresh for about 3 months. Then 40$ (Depending on the edition) for a major expansion every year which includes massive changes and content. The game is free, give it a shot and see how you like it.\t\t\t\t\t
