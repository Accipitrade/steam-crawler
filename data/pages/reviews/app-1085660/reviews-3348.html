In comparison from where it was before and where it is now, I'd say the game has blossomed into another new bracket of Shooter RPG genre. May it be in content, game design, lore/story telling, and game mechanic features (especially Raid dungeon design).
Though it is not perfect, in terms of balancing different variable combination (as most class + item based games are) its at least close to balance as players still need to hone their personal skill in order to reach success, as they progress either alone or with friends.

Destiny 2, may have been in a rough patch before but moving to steam is probably one of the best decision they have made yet.\t\t\t\t\t
