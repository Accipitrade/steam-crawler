\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can't say how much hours I did play, but as of my review, it's 35 hours on steam and I guess it's about 200 hours before the PC Move on Battle.Net.

Even though I'm not the biggest shooter fan, Destiny 2 is a blast:

PROs:

- Very good graphics, even now when we are going towards Year 4
- Fantastic run and gun gameply
- Superb soundtrack, one of the best ever in my opinion when it comes to Videogames / Certain activities together with the orchestral sound alone feel Epic and leave you stunned in front of the screen
- Very refined loot shooter mechanics, with many many different gameplay systems interweaved with each other to make you come back again and again
- Since Year 3 features a Seasonal system with a new Season Pass each three mounts (which you must buy to have premium rewards)
- In-Game Shop has no longer loot box mechanics, you can still buy a lot of stuff (and spend considerable amounts of money, but you always know what you get and for what price)
- Weekly Resets for challenges that give you good loot / Tons of Bounties and an endless amount of quests and activities keep you occupied for a long time if you want too
- Nowadays there are a lot of activities that have Matchmaking and allow you to enjoy it as solo player
- Its Free 2 Play and the F2P version is really including a lot of great stuff already

CONs

- If you really want to play the top endgame content like Raids or Dungeons you will have to find a an active Clan which will require Discord 99 % of the time, because the most challenging activities don't have Matchmaking and therefore you can't find other players in-game on the fly for it
- Finding a clan is awkward, you need to do it on the Destiny Homepage, there are no Recommendations or so and you need to read the forums / This is really strange in my opinion, that there is not a better integration into the game
- The game has more or less no chat / You can chat to ppl already in your group (Fireteam) or in your clan, but otherwise you can't simply chat with other ppl and therefore you can't find any to play with you in game / There is a LFG Tool on the Homepage, but I haven't tried it too much and again, in my opinion this is awkward
- The game can be grindy, because if you want to reach the top Power Level you are required to play the Weekly Challenges and complete them before they reset and then on the next Tuesday it begins anew
- There are many quests and they are great, but some of them are really hard to complete, especially the ones that require PvP / So if you are a completionist, then this can burn you out
- Otherwise it's really hard to find things that wouldn't be good about this game

If you have only the slightest interest and did never play Destiny nor Destiny 2, you really should donwload and try the Free2Play version right away. After that you can judge yourself and then you can spend money by either using the In-Game Shop for Cosmetics you like or you can upgrade your account to include the latest expansions and/or the Season Pass.\t\t\t\t\t
