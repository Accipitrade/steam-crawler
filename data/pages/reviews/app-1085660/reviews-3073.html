\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHumble Bundle gave me this game back in May 2018, and man has it been a wild ride. A lot of ups and downs, but the game has come a long way from it's stale, Activision-riddled days. The developers are really pushing to create a story they want rather than a story that Activision wanted to profit off of. Games made 100% for profit are stale, redundant, and dont last long.

Destiny 2 is reaching into its 3rd year soon, and it's release from Activision is the start of a new, bright era. An Era where Bungie is telling THEIR story. A story they love and want us to enjoy. Destiny is slowly turning into the passion project it began as and I love it. Passion projects have more care and detail than any AAA game on the market.

Most people are skeptical of the new Era due to the battlepass-style system, but it's not that bad. The pass comes free with Shadowkeep's purchase, and passes for all 4 of the next seasons is fairly cheap. A lot of people also complain about the changes to the Bright Dust economy, but if players utilize bounties properly, you can make a fortune of Bright Dust weekly.

This game is best enjoyed slow, simply, and with friends. Take your time, absorb the detail put into it, and have fun.\t\t\t\t\t
