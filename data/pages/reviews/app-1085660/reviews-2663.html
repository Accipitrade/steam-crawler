\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have over 1,000 hours on console and will soon have almost 1,000 hours on PC. The reality of the game is this:

Pros

- People often misremember Destiny 1. When compared to its predecessor, Destiny 2 is so much better because the game doesn't feel so empty, has great replayability, build crafting, customization, and Co-op.

- The game looks and feels great, has a lot to offer (especially for arriving players), and regularly receives updates.

- Compared to the previous years, excluding Forsaken, Bungie has really stepped up to the plate to improve the game and curate it around consumer-based feedback. I really feel Destiny is reaching a new peak again especially with the near arrival of the Witch Queen expansion.

Cons

- Like I said before, there is a lot of content in the game right now. Without guidance from an experienced friend it can easily get overwhelming for new players as they may not be sure where to start, or what objectives to follow.

-The PvP and PvE sandbox modes
These modes (crucible and Gambit), are neglected. Because of the type of game that Destiny is, they will never be balanced. The main issue though is the lack of maps. Because Destiny is a live service game, Bungie will remove maps and activities from the game and replace them with other ones. While this has happened with PvE, it has not happened with Crucible or Gambit. Instead we are left with 3 to 4 maps, making the game modes very stale and very boring.

Conclusion

Over all I feel this game is worth it. It will take a bit of time to get into and a slight investment, but if you have a love for FPS fantasy games with RPG aspects that everyone loves, then Destiny 2 is for you.
