\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is a game that you dont ever stop playing, you just leave for varied amounts of time. Bungie has had some growing pains since the Halo days, but the end result is a great MMO-lite experience worth getting into. Im not sure about the future, but being along for the ride is what I enjoy the most. If you love games with a lot of hidden lore then this is a great game for you.

The content loop is PvE (3 player missions such as strikes, 6 player raids with fun mechanics), PvP (Crucible 3v3 or 6v6, etc, or Competitive such as Trials of Osiris), and PvPvE. The season system keeps things relatively fresh with now yearly expansion plans.

Endgame content consists of Grandmaster Nightfalls (3 player strikes dialed up to 10 in difficulty), Dungeons (3 Players with mechanics), and Raids (6 players with mechanics). PVP has pinnacle activities such as Trials of Osiris (3v3 Elimination) and Iron Banner (6v6 control) as well.

Destiny 2 hits the mark for the genre. It plays a middle ground between MMO/FPS/Action Game. I'd give it a try!\t\t\t\t\t
