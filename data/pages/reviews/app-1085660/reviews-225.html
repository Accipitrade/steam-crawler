\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is finally a worthwhile sequel to Destiny 1 after 5 years.
The gameplay is fun, the PvE content is actually good now, and PvP doesn't make me want to rip my hair out currently.
There's a quality main campaign in the Witch Queen with 2 difficulty modes, very fun to play through with friends.

However, Destiny 2 is horribly overpriced. If you want all the content in the game, be prepared to pay $100+ not even including season passes and the in-game cosmetic shop. It's Free-to-Play offering is very bad, not even worth mentioning. It's more a trial than anything. You don't NEED all of the content, though. My recommendation for getting started is to simply grab the Witch Queen + Beyond Light. If you enjoy that, feel free to buy the other content.
If you like Bungie-style shooters, build diversity and loot-chasing, it's a good game. But if you're on the fence at all, wait for a sale.\t\t\t\t\t
