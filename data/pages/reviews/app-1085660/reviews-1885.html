\r\n\t\t\t\t\tComing from never playing any Destiny 1 and jumping into Destiny 2 mid-way through Season 9. I can say I honestly love this game. It's a pretty good FPS with the typical mechanics of an FPS with a sort-of Halo spin. (Makes sense being made by Bungie)

This game actually changed some of my ideals as well. I've always been the Sniper to pick enemies off from afar while Clanmates or Randoms are up in their faces. But now, thanks to some friends' advice and some weapon testing, I actually use things like SMGs and Swords. Quite a bit of variety in the different weapons as well. I mean, all SMGs are similar but it's the perks they can drop with are what make them interesting.

There's also three different classes (Hunter, Warlock, Titan) when making your character and another three different subclasses (Solar, Arc, Void) to choose from and you can switch to any one of the three subclasses at any time you wish, even during a mission. You even have three different abilities; usually in the order of any type of grenade, any type of throwing knife/CQC melee, and a Class Ability dependent on your Class.

Coming from a background of games like the Halo and Gears of War series, the controls seemed very natural to me. I started playing on Keyboard and Mouse before trying out my XBox One Standard Controller. The Xbox controller definitely felt more natural. I did have to remap one or two of the buttons, though.

Overall, this game is extremely fun. Whether you're the lone wolf or a key part to a clan, this game is a lot of fun and EXTREMELY addicting. I started playing in January 2020 and five months later I've put in more time than I ever would have imagined. With the new season starting up as well, I'll be putting in many more hours than ever before.\t\t\t\t\t
