Sure you get some content free but here's where my main complaint comes in:
At the beginning you started off basic and had to work your way up to light power and follow a fantastic campaign giving you an amazing flow of how the game progresses...that's been thrown right out the window and you set at the last seasons max light cap form the start and no explanation on how you got there, why you have these powers, what the direction you're supposed to go in and why you care about any of the characters (RIP Ace).

Here's most of the current problems that could be so easily fixed:
- 30th game event, make it free to enjoy and to celebrate. Why is this gated behind a paywall?
- Make the season passes attainable in game...again why is this paywalled?
- Main campaign progression from before even light powers, bring this back in so new players can experience the story that made the game good...also it gives meaning back to the itemisation. Currently items mean nothing, it's just a big stats stick over another.

I'm leaving my previous review in tact so we can see the difference from when i praised the game to the now disbelief of the current gatcha system. Stop charging us for content that should be free or at least give the content to the people who actually paid for the game when it released. I can't recommend this game as is it now, not unless they ease up on the paywall.

I'd rather have a one time payment for the main game and free content with the exception of maybe some major expansions or give us all the season passes and event content free with the major expansions. It's not difficult and we're not asking for a revamp of the game. Just fix the current marketing scheme.
