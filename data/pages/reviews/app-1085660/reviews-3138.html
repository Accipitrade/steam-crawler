\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI started playing this in shadowkeep and had a blast, But as each season comes and goes the technical side of the game gets worse and worse. It's a point now that server disconnects are a constant thing, lag is very noticeable and it just brings an already tired and old formula down even further. After 3 seasons nothing has really changed in the PvE experience, each season brings back another grindy event that plays much like the last season and it all feels the same after awhile. It's clear that all bungie knows how to do anymore is create another horde mode variant for you to grind through day after day, week after week. Ontop of this bungie keeps pushing the bounty system over and over with it's daily and weekly bounties making this feel just like a cell phone game.

PvP can be enjoyable but it's very inconsistent, With many small issues seemingly punishing you for not playing with a group. All the high end PvP requires light level grinding which just isn't fun and gates out the 'real' PvP experience. This is good for someone who lives on destiny 2 but for anyone who doesn't your put at a disadvantage and bungie knows this and actually wants this. It's very strange given the current FPS market.

And that is what this game feels like now, A poorly run cell phone game that doesn't respect your time investment and wants you to live and breath destiny.

I know everybody says bungie is a great dev but it's clear for this title they can't keep up anymore technically or entertainment wise, It's a real shame because the game has just a metric load of content but it's all just the same thing.\t\t\t\t\t
