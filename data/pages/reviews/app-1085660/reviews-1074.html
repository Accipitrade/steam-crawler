\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR; If you're looking for a fun and fast paced game with a great PVP and PVE, and an enjoyable stoy and set of characters try Destiny for free it wont hurt you're wallet, but trust me the game gets way better with the expansions.

Destiny has had alot of ups and downs, I've played since day one on both D1 and D2 so I know/knew many of the flaws in both games. Recently I built my first First PC and switched off xbox, and Destiny 2 Beyond Light was the first game I bought and let me just say Destiny 2 feels great on mouse and keyboard. With how fast your actions have to be at most points it feels a MILLION times better than on controller.

The Gameplay / Gunplay is easily my favorite out of any game i've played..... well tied with Doom Eternal, and Halo love those games too. Anyway, the gameplay reaches a point where it's not too hard for newcomers to get the hang of to have fun but it also has a point where players can master the various skills and guns to desecrate any player in their way. Unlike most FPS games the core loop of PVP helps eradicate any signs of camping or just mindlessly running around and holding the trigger, every single gunfight in PVP has the potential to become and epic confrontation between two seasoned players. I still have fond memories of my favorite crucible moments from D1 I don't think i'll ever forget my first team wipe in Trials of Osiris. But their are many times where a new weapon is released and it's not balanced one bit, so people take advantage of it till the Devs fix it, it has happend so many times that sadly I can only give the Gameplay for PVP 8/10 I'm also gonna give PVE 8/10 only because you can't change the difficulty of the story missions. But there are many other PVE events such as strikes and raids that blow the story missions out of the water. It's just all around exciting and fun for the whole friend group.

Next up the story. I personally really enjoy the story... for the most part. One Grievance I have with the story is that it is very vague unless you delve into the grimore cards. Without watching youtube videos on the story most people can write it off as verrrrrrry long for no reason. But the only reason for this is Activion forcing bungie to rush Destiny 1's production, in order for bungie to release the game on the deadline they had to cut about 75% of the game and turn it into one base game with tons of expansions. To give you some context destiny one released the main game and the later released the following expansions listed; The Dark Below, House of wolves, The Taken King, and Rise of Iron all very spread out from each other. Destiny one was supposed to have all the content from the listed expansions in the base game other than Rise of Iron was supposed to be the only expansion, know thats alot of left out content. Heck even some content from destiny 2 was supposed to be in Destiny one. I rate the story 6/10 only because Activision screwed everything up.

Destiny was supposed to be Bungie's passion project after leaving halo to 343, it was their chance to prove that they can make yet another great game franchise. Yet corporate greed from Activision seeped it's way in and messed up alot, yet Bungie still pushed through and delivered a very great game, yes it has flaws but what game doesn't. Destiny has grown alot since is poor realease and press, just give it a chance and I'm positive you wont regret it. Final Grade 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
