\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first glance the game is interesting, looks like any other typical fps to anyone the starts playing the game. The mechanics are pretty simple to understand and execute. The story itself is vast and there's so much of it to play through. Like any other co-op game this one too shines and you get the best experience when you pay with teammates or friends. As you progress more into the game you learn of builds, weapon perks, exotic armor perks, this is where everything get so much more interesting and also where the grind begins. Grinding for better power levels, better gear with better stats, weapons with better perks and the never ending grind for materials, glimmer and whatnot. This is where you realize that this not a fps, but a looter shooter, your rewards depend on the amount of time you spent in the game, playing and grinding. Any and all endgame content(raids, grandmaster nightfalls, dungeons) all require a team and people that has an understanding of the mechanics of the activity. Here's problem 2, this is a free to download but pay to play game as most of the content is locked behind Expansions (DLC's).
That being said the game itself is fun and bungie has a way of keeping their player's satisfied.
At the time of writing Ill definitely recommend or suggest the game to anyone and everyone..I would also suggest that you be ready to pay for the expansions, preferably the season pass too (there's content locked behind season pass as well), and if you play right, you can finish the season pass in 2 weeks time.
Haven't even touched the pvp part of the game yet..but its as fun and sweaty as any other comp level game. You will either be destroyed or you will be a pvp God.\t\t\t\t\t
