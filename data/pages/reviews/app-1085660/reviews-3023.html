\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSummary: a satisfying casual game which I jump in every now and then but cannot be attracted and invested to a larger extent

Pros:
1) Extremely satisfying gameplay, including: gunplay, movement, fluidity of abilities and gun swapping
2) Very well optimised - haven't experienced any problems at all
3) Well diverse enemies, AI with some decency of tactics
4) Good selection of weaponry and how diversely it handles
5) Well-done sound effects and great OST

Cons:
1) For some reason I didn't have a tutorial and went right into numerous game systems, which was confusing at the start
2) Feels too much like lobby-based shooter due to fast-travel options, transition between planets and hub as well as the way dungeons are accessed
3) Poor exposure to world lore and story-telling due to unfocused progression (hopping between planets), lack of in-game library (at least I haven't found it) and lack of introduction
4) Missions and progression designs feel repetitive: new planet - meet characters which I have no clue about - do some things with very limited context behind - jump onto another planet - repeat

Disclaimer: obviously I haven't reached the endgame and my opinions are mostly high-level\t\t\t\t\t
