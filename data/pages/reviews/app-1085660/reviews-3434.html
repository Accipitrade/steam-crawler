\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaving one of my favorite games moved over to steam was really amazing news for me, I would 100% recommend playing Destiny to anyone thinking about it. The graphics and even just the background environment is absolutely beautiful, I sometimes find myself just pausing for a minute while playing just to take it in. The game also has an extensive and enormous amount of lore which must really excite some people but regardless of what you're into, this game probably has it.

There are some downsides, the issues I've faced are extensive crashing and server disconnections but this is only the first couple of days the game has been on steam so it's easily excusable. The other issue is that you can be fairly restricted if you don't have the latest DLCs, especially Iron Banner which is a seasonable PVP event. Some players who have a higher Light level will absolutely demolish you easily and you won't be able to do much against that. Regardless of these minor issues, the game is just one of those iconic gems that you can put thousands of hours into.

Overall, should you play the game?
- Yes

Should you buy the DLCs?
- The Shadowkeep DLC has been really fun so far, I'm enjoying the high power level and having the Moon as a location you can explore brings back old memories of Destiny 1. I would personally recommend it if you're an experienced player.
- Shadowkeep Digital Deluxe isn't worth it in my opinion.
- The Forsaken DLC is something I didn't play much of, I've only recently purchased it since I couldn't afford it when it first came out. The DLC has a lot of new items and things to do but I couldn't give a full opinion on it.

Is the season pass worth it?
- That really depends on what you want from the game, I've purchased it but I really don't think it was ultimately worth it in my opinion, it's just an easier way to get a bunch of gear that you'd earn over time. It just comes down to what and how you want to play the game.

For new players, Destiny is honestly really big. When I first started playing I got really lost with what to do. The best advise I can recommend is doing as many patrols, bounties and so on to boost your light level. There are a lot of guides online that will have an easier way to do something and I can recommend keeping up to date with the game using the Destiny companion app and also following the Reddit communities: (r/DestinyTheGame & r/Destiny2).\t\t\t\t\t
