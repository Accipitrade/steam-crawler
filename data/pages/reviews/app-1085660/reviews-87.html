\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExcellent game to kill time with. For long term play, either get familiar with the LFG groups in Discord or bring some friends. Most of the endgame content almost requires 2-5 additional people (groups of 3 and 6 for challenging runs/raids respectively). A lot can be taken on solo, and the campaigns are all completely viable if you are running the game on your own. PvP and Gambit are both pretty sweaty, but they do have their charm.

Only really big downsides that this game really has all seem to surround Bungie's inability to work out all their bugs, especially as they add and sunset content en masse. They do their best, but top runners in this game tend to succeed heavily through exploiting these bugs and that can be a big issue if you like to actually work on getting better at the game.

As a whole, I think the game deserves a solid 7.5/10. I've put in just shy of 1100 hours into this game on PC and about 500-600 on Playstation and I still find the story to be interesting, the lore well developed, and the activities fun enough to keep me coming back. Breaks are always needed here and there to get away from the inevitable grind that comes with all MMOs, but nothing that I think would make me shelve the game permanently.

Get your friends together and get raiding!\t\t\t\t\t
