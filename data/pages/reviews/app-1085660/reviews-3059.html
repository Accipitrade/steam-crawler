\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe PvE is decent. PvP is a poorly balanced mess, and unfortunately, a lot of cool stuff is locked behind PvP activity.

Raids are hit or miss, and they seem to cater to the crowd who plays only one game (and a lot of it). The RNG nature of loot is especially punishing when you sink a lot of time into a raid and get boring rewards.

That said, I've met a lot of really cool people through Destiny 2. Some are skilled, and some are not so much. But, they all have one thing in common: they like to shoot things and get cool gear from doing that. Ultimately, that's what a looter-shooter is supposed to be about. Join the Destiny 2 LFG Discord because no one talks in game voice chat.

Don't take D2 too seriously, and you'll have a blast. It's worth spending money on the expansions only if you love the gameplay loop. If not, plenty of free content is waiting for you anyway.

I'll probably drop it in the next month or so, but I definitely already got my money's worth.\t\t\t\t\t
