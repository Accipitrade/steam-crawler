\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere is no explaining how good this game is, all I can say is be prepared. Be prepared to laugh and cry, to be frustrated and excited, to be screamed at by your friends in discord and to yell at your friend to clutch as the last guardian standing. Be prepared to explore strange environments and discover every hidden little secret. Be prepared to slaughter aliens from every corner of the cosmos and to fight your fellow guardians in PvP.

The mechanics of this game just work. The gunplay is satisfying and every single guns feels awesome to shoot. The movement is also extremely satisfying with double jumps and glides depending on which class you play. All of the gamemodes such as strikes and PvP and Gambit are perfectly crafted so they feel just right with the amount of players to how you play through each mission. The game is technically open world but a lot of your time will be spent in linear missions such as strikes or quests but it doesn't feel like a chore to do any of these and you can go off and explore any time you want.

The loot in this game is something else, there are countless armour sets, guns and cool ships/vehicles for you to discover. You can earn pretty much any piece of loot from just playing the game. There are a few cosmetics which require money but the majority can also be earned through playing. The customisation in this game is endless and there are hundreds of builds for you to use. The cosmetic customisation is also pretty extensive, which includes gun skins, armour skins, ghost shells, shaders (colours for amour/weapon), ships, vehicles, emblems, emotes and last but not least finishers.

Also, grab some friends and do raids without looking them up, they are an otherworldly experience and probably the most unique missions in any videogame ever.\t\t\t\t\t

Enemy variety is big, with most of them playing different from each other. The Scorn are a very gimmick based species and require knowledge of enemy abilities, same goes for the Taken enemies, who each possess a different ability that forces the player to adapt to them.

The class system could be considered basic, but it offers a total of 27 playstyles, which is very high for an MMO. We have dedicated support classes like Protector Sentinel and Well of Radiance Dawnblade. This is paired with DPS Classes like Gunslinger or Novawalker. There are dedicated add clear classes like Striker or Stormcaller.

This game is truly incredible, and going F2P just allows it to be more accessible. I highly recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
