\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGonna state this for the record, I've played this game since release over on Battle-net, and I haven't played the first Destiny Game.

This game is mainly for people who Enjoy a mix of something like Borderlands and CoD, It's a great game to chill out with some friends and mess around in, this goes for the PvP and PvE aspects of the game, Loot drops and Gun perk rolling is something that will keep you very busy if you love building the perfect setup to cause as much carnage as possible.

Here's the catch, The game can be played through very quickly, and since they dropped the main campaign and made the expansions more of a "Do it if you want" things can go very bland very quickly. This leaves you and your friends running through repeatable activities. Public events where other players drop in or out, Strike playlists which are like smaller and quicker raids, Bounties for simple one off tasks, and if all else fails PvP. So if you don't mind a gameplay loop, this might just keep your attention.

Gun-play and PvP feels smooth. Throw in the fact that you can use your "Perfect Build" in there (God Roll guns, Exotic Armor and Weapons, Customized Armor attributes) and you get something where you can really tailor everything to your play-style, but even then, you need to go through a bit of a grind to get to that point.

Speaking of PvP, Balance is something that is a little tough in there. Yes, there is a Meta. Yes, this meta shifts. Yes, Titans will more than likely shoulder charge you into oblivion. No, this shouldn't stop you from trying and experimenting and finding the way you play, otherwise, this too will grow very bland and unappealing. I recommend playing with friends in here, things can get very frustrating if you solo, and communication even if its minimal, will help a ton.

All in all, It's worth a shot and you don't lose anything. You can do everything I listed here without purchasing any expansions, so if you like what you see and decide you wanna sink some money into it to delve a little deeper, go for it!\t\t\t\t\t
