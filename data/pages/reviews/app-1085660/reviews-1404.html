-Lightfall
I have had no issues with the lightfall campaign. The story leaves some details out, but Destiny story telling has always withheld details, and always has been a bit vague to allow for the season by season expansion of the story.
As for the gameplay?
It feels great. In all the legendary campaign I only got stuck for several deaths once or twice and it never felt like bad game design, just like I was going about the encounter the wrong way.
