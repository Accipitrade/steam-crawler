I bought it when it was cool, but now its free and even cooler!

 + General - NO "PAY TO WIN", premium currency for cosmetics, effects and some unique skins
+ Gameplay - "a ton" of well-made game modes (SP/MP)
 + Gameplay - doesn't matter if you have a "party" or you are alone, both can be fun
 + Netcode - almost "LAG-less" experience
 + Weapons - 16 weapons. categories, quite a lot of different unique "gun types"
+ Personal - I really enjoy playing this game with my friends, most of the "games" are fun, even with strangers in team, restricted chat keeps the "toxicity out of society", fun thing is that sometimes I miss the the classical chat system approach, when we could express our feelings about our enemies :))

**** but... on the other hand ****

- Netcode - game invite thru steam "sucks", fire team system sometimes fails for both inv/join
- Netcode - under current loads (Mar/Apr 2020) occasional connection problems
- Gameplay - the game is so big and full of content, that a new player might find him self quite lost for considerable portion of time, but maybe that is just a matter of approach

TIP 4 new players: Find mode you enjoy, don't be afraid of trying different PVP modes, most of them will find you teammates to play with, and finally dismantle what you can materials will come in handy soon!

PS: I am so glad I don't have to level up from scratch after you switched to F2P model, grind was fun in Wow, but current system feels like a nice fit for Destiny :)\t\t\t\t\t
