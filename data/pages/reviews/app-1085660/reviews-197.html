\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe state of Destiny 2 over the years has ultimately shifted. The absolutely terrible Vanilla launch took away the identity that Forsaken DLC had to earn back and coupled with a player base that honestly does not know what it wants has turned this title into the steaming pile of ♥♥♥♥ that has only gotten more moldy and rotten as time goes on. For starters, newer games with far superior graphics run better than a game that has been supported and optimized since 2017, it is embarrassing. I have to hold my computer at gun-point to achieve 140 FPS on specific crucible maps with fairly new equipment.

Gameplay:
The single-player and co-op experience is an incredibly mixed bag that tries to appease to many different types of players yet ultimately fails to succeed in any department. The hardcore gamers complain that there is not enough to do and that using the same strategies makes the game repetitive and easy whilst the RPG gamers have no idea what is going on in the story because you have to read BOOKS in order to understand the VIDEO GAME STORY. Also, the quality of this experience has massively degraded as Bungie’s philosophy has shifted from innovation to reiteration. They add so many old things back into the game with little to no changes and whatnot so it feels new for about 5 minutes. I have used the same 3-4 load outs in PVE for nearly 2 YEARS because nothing new catches my eye. All the new guns tend to be old re-skins, they love to poke at nostalgia. The raids are ok, I tend to feel the worst part of doing a raid is the raid itself. I find the best part of it being the talks with my buddies or just killing your friends with eager edge sword to get a reaction. If you’ve done the encounters once, the replayability diminishes ten-fold. The only raid to not have this happen in my opinion is Last Wish.

Crucible:
Coming from a company who created one of the original competitive FPS multiplayer titles, you tend to wonder how this happened. The servers are terrible due to negligence and the implementation of SBMM hurt the game completely. This was my main aspect of the game I enjoyed and all it does for me now is make me furious. The meta changes constantly because gun balance is non-existent and players love to abuse the easiest things in order to achieve the best results. When I played the game the most this was One-Eyed Mask which would enable even a blind grandma to accidentally kill the 4 people on the enemy team in COMP with one ace o’ spades kill. Nowadays, this crutch has come in the form of Loraley’s Splendor. Bungie also took so many of my favorite maps out of crucible and put them in the “DCS” to come out as payed DLC one day.

I cannot recommend this game, it has shown me that I have done nothing but waste my time hoping that things would change for 8 years now. I hope this will get to people so they do not make the same mistake.\t\t\t\t\t
