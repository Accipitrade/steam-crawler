\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 and the franchise as a whole had a rough start. It seemed clear even to those that loved Destiny that The developers were not sure the direction they wanted their game to go. For years Bungie tested and changed their new IP As an MMORPG Destiny is lacking in some ways and stronger in others. As a looter shooter its a difficult game to get into because of its reliance on late game non matchmaking content for some of its best experiences and rewards. However In its years of ongoing change and development Destiny 2 combinds the best of both an MMORPG and a Looter shooter. In my 1000 hours of play on steam and 800 hours of play before that on Battle.net I have always enjoyed Destiny 2. It is one of the most rewarding and enjoyable games to date to play with friends. The game play is immerisve and impactful the story and lore are phenomenal. The art style and creativity is a product of a passion project by the developers not a cash grab from a big name publisher. However its not without flaws as of the time of writing the game is cross-play but not cross-platform. The base game is free but DLC is expensive and Most activities are not Match making requiring the use of Third Party apps to get a team for what are at times intense coordinated Raids or Dungeons. Overall Destiny two is an amazing and worthwhile time sink. IF you have friends to enjoy it with you.\t\t\t\t\t
