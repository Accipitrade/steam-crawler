\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI previously reviewed the game after it's first month of being moved to Steam and I feel that with time my opinion has changed. I am still deeply fond of this game and its community being reliable at times, but after a while of playing this game and from Season of Dawn to Season of Worthy, it was made apparent that the game has plenty of problems that still need to be sorted, but that will happen in its own time and doesn't make the game any worse. It's a spectacular game for people who like to shoot the first thing in front of you, people who like to find every single piece of armor or every weapon to complete a collection, people who like to follow a story and feel important in that story, and people who want to solve unique problems with friends. There are plenty of activities in the game. There are bounties for you to do ranging from daily to weekly. There are quests and side-quests that have you following side stories for gear or a look at a "bigger picture". There is a PvP playlist for those in competitive nature. And lastly, there are raids, in which you can get together in a group of 6 to take on bigger challenges. Overall a great game. Highly recommend for everyone to at least give it a try\t\t\t\t\t
