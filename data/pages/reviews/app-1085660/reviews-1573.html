I got the game maybe a couple weeks/ a month after initial release and me and my best friend were hooked on the game and once we both got past the initial Red War campaign, we both immediately started grinding for exotics. Unfortunately, we both had school coming back when the new DLC Curse of Osiris came out so we stopped and only started back up around the time of Forsaken/ whenever it was ported to steam and made free. Since I started playing again I can honestly say that all the DLCs are worth the buy (yes even Curse of Osiris) because they each offer something new as they release. My biggest disappointment is that my clan disbanded while I wasn't playing and now I need a new group of people to help me do certain missions and to do raids with cause I don't know many people personally that play D2. Overall, I love this game!!\t\t\t\t\t
