My Beyond Light review for the current state of the game
Below is my review for the Pre-Beyond Light expansion state of Destiny 2 which isn't valid anymore, not by a long shot. Check my Beyond Light review above for the current state of the game.

**

There are lots of things to do, so many it almost feels like a mix of games put together in one package; multi-planetary exploration, raids, strikes, dungeons, PvP, competitive co-op and tons upon tons of quests be they for weapons, exotics or story. Also, the feeling of the weapons when you shoot them is top-tier. They feel absolutely fantastic. Furthermore I absolutely love how all the different gamemodes are connected. You can jump from PvE to PvP by just navigating a menu. You can farm weapons in PvE that are good in PvP or just find one that looks interesting and go and try it out. The way Destiny feels like an All-in-One package of games is very interesting.

Without buying any of the expansions expect a free demo experience.

The only thing I dislike is the armor design. Contrary to Destiny 1's design, in this game armor does not look believable and practical for the battles you will fight. Guardians look more like cosplayers than actual soldiers/warriors. This fantasy-rpg style really does NOT suit Destiny's world. It completely ruins the feeling of "we are the last humans to exist and we cling to survival with every tooth and nail". Destiny 1's sci-fi style was infinitely better.\t\t\t\t\t
