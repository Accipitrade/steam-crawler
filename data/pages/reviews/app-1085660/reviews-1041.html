\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
- Gorgeous graphics, colors, visual effects
- Tons of content, I'm at 1000+ hours between PC+Xbox
- Cross-save
- The story and lore are both wide and deep
- PvE, PvP, PvEvP modes
- Co-op (1-6 player depending on the activity [typically 3/6]) is pretty seamless and very fun
- Completionists and explorers will love this game (rewarding secrets and achievements everywhere)
- 100s of different weapons and armor; weapons are rolled and most items are collectible
- Weapons are fairly balanced (i.e. they're mostly all usable, some are certainly better than others)
- Instanced open-world

Cons:
- Late-game solo play is very grindy (use LFG Discord server for quick team-ups)
- Late-game co-op is pretty much a requirement to reach the higher levels (again, LFG)
- Weapons, armor, locations, activities can be "[content] vaulted" (temporarily/permanently removed from the game) with each new season/DLC

Developer suggestions:
- Open world proximity voice chat
- Longer, more difficult, and large boss-oriented open-world public events with greater rewards
- Raise glimmer cap
- Increase PvE difficulty across the board (slightly)
- Future destinations should be more open (larger landscapes, less linear)
- More exotic armor, weapons and catalysts should be earned via quests and dungeons
- Add mod slots to class items for mods that effect your subclass abilities (class ability, grenade, melee, super)
- Exotic class items

Getting started?
- Start a Bungie account
- Link your Bungie account to Destiny Item Manager (DIM) - 3rd party browser application
- Download Discord (if you have not already) and join the Destiny 2 LFG server
- If you have Twitch Prime, link your Bungie account for substantial bonus rewards\t\t\t\t\t

Most of all I was excited for a conclusion to the story and hopefully some seasonal content or cliffhangers pointing at the exciting "whats next".
What I GOT, was disappointment. I got the same enemies with the same combat logic just re-skinned, with exactly one new enemy that is not "scary" it is not "tough" it is just flat out annoying. The vanguard leaders are just sitting sulking in their tower, none of the characters are interested in actually manning up and getting at it.
The last time I was this disappointed was when Mass Effect Andromeda turned out to be a flop, like I feel like the release of Cyberpunk 2077 was more fleshed out than this.
Gacha games have better storylines than this. I can't even get my money back now. I will be uninstalling immediately.\t\t\t\t\t
