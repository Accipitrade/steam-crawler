\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have over 2000 hours in Destiny 2 since it first released on Xbox One and have done pretty much every activity other than the newest raid.

Pros:
- Gameplay is extremely fun
- The game looks beautiful
- The music is amazing. I can't think of a single bad piece of music anywhere between both D1 and D2.
- There is a tonne of loot to collect
- There is a huge amount of quests and activities to do.
- The Vast majority of the community is super nice and happy to help you if you're confused or just stuck on a quest.
- The lore surrounding the universe is incredibly detailed and extremely interesting

Cons:
- PvP. Balancing affects PvE (which means some things end up nerfed into the ground for PvE but are decent for PvP). Balancing in general sucks in PvP anyway, and it's primarily made up of hunters.
- A good chunk of the loot hasn't been brought up to current standards. A lot of weapons are stuck without random rolls, and some armours cannot be obtained anymore and if you still have them cannot be brought into the new armour 2.0.
- Some of the quest and loot grinds are just painfully long and not fun.
- It can be confusing, especially if you're only just jumping in for the first time.
- The stories told within the expansions can be a bit meh overall (Though Forsaken is the exception and is brilliant, and the greater universe consequences and lore that come from those stories are also really good).

Overall i would have to give Destiny 2 a solid 9/10. The gameplay loop and most activities are just so fun that they tend to override most of my issues with the game the vast majority of the time. Not that those issues aren't there, but the game is so fun that they can be ignored and Bungie are always listening even if they may not be immediately acting on feedback.\t\t\t\t\t
