More about my experience
Ever since I started using a keyboard & mouse, I've looked for a game that I can work towards a greater goal in a world that is constantly changing. At the time, I've only ever known multiplayer only games with no purpose other than to win the match, and Single Player games which are over once you complete the story and find all the collectibles. I picked this game up after it went free to play since I remember seeing it a few years back on YT. After walking out of my grave I was greeted by some really good graphics and an inclusive Ghost that really made you feel like you we're the guardian as opposed to being a player. After completing the starting mission I was dropped into the world. No Shawn Han back then, No tutorial, I wondered what my purpose could be in this game with this many planets and game modes. I eventually figured out how to do the Red War Campaign and I finished that and boy did I have a blast. I also learned of the exotic weapons and armor that could be obtained. This felt rewarding to get since many of them had quests attached to them and each one was memorable since you went on a journey to get it. I eventually learned of these things called raids, and I was a pretty stupid guardian back then since I tried to figure out how to solo a raid. I eventually caved and realized, I'd have to learn the mechanics, and communicate with people to get through this activity. After about 2 days of non stop grinding to get to the highest power level, and studying the raid mechanics, I felt ready, I joined a fireteam of 3 guardians who were teaching kinderguardians like me through the Leviathan raid. These people were really patient and really made sure I learned what I was doing rather than simply carrying me through it. And after about 2 hours we finally beat Calus. It was a feeling I'd never forget nor would ever feel again. I'd did it, I completed a whole raid! I'm not gonna lie, if it wasn't for these group of guardians I would have never continued my journey, this community welcomed me with open arms and told me "You get your ass out there and become a Legend" and I did just that, I slayed gods in the rest of the raids, Unlocked the most elusive exotics and conquered the strikes only a Grandmaster could complete. I've bought every expansion and season pass since then and I've never been happier, now as I write this review I look at myself in the reflection of my monitor and think, what's next? What new journey or adventure will this game send me to next? This game presents itself to you as a fulfilling journey where you can become what you envision to be. This is a game I'd recommend to any and all of my friends. My only complaint would be the removal of planets, raids and activities that came with beyond light, I'm hoping all this can be added back in the future since those memories now only exist within our hearts and minds. Good luck Guardians!
