\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThrough out my journey of playing Destiny 2, I would say that some people will enjoy it and others will say it boring due to the grinding and other stuff.

Here are some things you might want to know before playing destiny 2

(1) First being Destiny 2 is a grindy game and if you want more content you might want to drop 100$ on DLCs to have more things to do. (Check PvP Meta being 3 on the list to know a little more about DLCs in PvP)

(2) The game can get repetitive and may get boring at times. Honestly I would recommend playing with friends.

(3) Destiny 2 PvP: When you get the game for free there are not many weapons that go with the PvP meta making it hard to enjoy PvP. When you get the DLCs you'll need to grind for weapons for PvP and it is best to follow the meta. One other thing is that it might get annoying because people are running stasis subclass (earned Europa DLC missions) because they can free you. It has been buffed a few times.

(4) Destiny 2 Raiding: These are big boss battles that have many mini bosses and then a final big boss. These raids can range from 1 hour (being experienced in the raid) to around 4+ hours (not knowing much about raiding). If you dont have time with raiding then why even do it.

(5) Power Level: your gear has levels that make you tanker and provide benefits when upgrading the gear. For example increasing your mobility to 10+, increasing machine gun reload speed on one piece of armor. There might be a lot of time where you create Raiding armor sets, PvP Armor sets, and/or PvE armor sets. [Note to be honest I am a lazy person and make an all around good set, where i switch the perks (modules) on the gear]

(6) Planets: when you not doing anything , where can you go. You can go to different planets that a mini missions and allows you to free roam and explore. In the planets and Tower/Ship (shops, and basic stuff you'll be there a lot) there are lore to be found.

These are some tips you might want to know about the game. Honestly I like Destiny 2 even though there might be challenges and a lot of grinding. This review is just a general overview of the game. It is your decision to play the game. it is also never bad to try the game to see how you like it before reading review and saying it is a bad game.
