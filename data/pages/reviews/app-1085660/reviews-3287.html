Played about 75 hrs back when it was on BattleNet and have now clocked in another 173 since it dropped on Steam. A great Loot 'em-Shoot 'em with tons of stuff to do. Always. There is so many side missions and challenges to do that finishing the main story lines shouldn't even be considered the tip on an iceberg. More like a speckle. I highly recommend Destiny 2 regardless if you've played the first game or not.

My only criticism of Destiny 2 is the lack of structure. It doesn't really give you a strict route on how to complete main line missions or most things in general, so if you're trying to follow the lore/story or just 100% the game then it's gonna be a tough cookie without some friends to guide you.\t\t\t\t\t
