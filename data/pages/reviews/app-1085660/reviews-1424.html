\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBungie doesn't care about their players; they only care about their players' wallets. Every design decision in this game feels like it was come up with in a completely sterile environment, devoid of any creativity or player knowledge. It's a patchwork of half-baked ideas that, with every new addition, creates a more grotesque monstrosity of a game.

At its core, Destiny 2 is a grindfest. But not just that: you grind for a *chance* to get a piece of equipment you want. Did I say want? I meant need. To play portions of the game, like Nightfall Strikes and Trials of Osiris, you need to constantly be grinding your power level to even be able to access them. So you grind for tens of hours before you can even play portions of the game you want to play. It's like a Fun Tax. And what do you get when you get to play those parts you actually wanted to play? More grinding, and in the case of Trials of Osiris, hackers. And the best part? Every season you get to start over again, because now your light level, that you spent hundreds of hours of your life attaining, is the bottom of the pile. Time to pay the Fun Tax again.

This game is not good. The AI enemies are either dumb as rocks or one-hit-kill you. There's no emergent or interesting gameplay. Most of the armour and guns are hideously ugly. Small things, like changing the colour of lighting effects on equipment when a new shader is equipped, are overlooked. It's a mess.

Bungie used to be the best game developer in the world. Recently I saw a job posting in my field for Bungie online; six years ago I would have jumped at the chance to work with such an esteemed studio. Now, however, Bungie is just a husk. It's no longer the studio that made Halo. It's now responsible for *this*. I let the job listing pass me by, just like you should let this game pass you by.

Oh one more thing: they say it's Free to Play, but unless you have all the expansions you can't be competitive. I played the base game for a year before I got the expansions, and it was like I was playing a completely different game. This is not a free-to-play game, it's a cash-grab grind-a-thon. Enjoy at your own peril.\t\t\t\t\t
