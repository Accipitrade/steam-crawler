Wasn't really a fan of this game for a long time due to it being somewhat empty and soul less then the news that Bungie were breaking off from Activision and I thought they at least deserve another chance for such a big decision. That was like 1.5k hours ago (most spent on Battlenet).

The game went from having little to no content to almost having too much, you will struggle, even at end game to not find something to do.

The free to play model has been mostly a success, however legit new players are kinda dropped straight into the story with very little help. New players getting matched in strikes with people with thousands of hours is never really healthy for either player, one wants to speed run the other wants to enjoy the play through. This is probably the only issue I have with the game and over time I can see it becoming a non-issue.

As for as DLC goes, you get Warmind and Curse of Osiris for free which leaves you with Shadowkeep and Forsaken as the 2 buyable options. If you are wondering which one to get or even if you should, I'd recommend playing through the content you have first then revisit your choice.
