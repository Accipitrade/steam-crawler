That said, the optimisation is perfect, the gunplay is excellent, the music and art are beyond beautiful and the character designs are subtle and enjoyable to flick through as you progress through seasons and content. And yet, playing this game feels like a compulsive habit more than an escape.

Despite its drawbacks, this is an important game in the history of FPSMMO development, and perhaps with a loyal clan by your side, there's plenty of fun to be had in this game, and much of it for free. Worth a try, but bewarned - the grind and RNGesus will haunt your very nightmares.
