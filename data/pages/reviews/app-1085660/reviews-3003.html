\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game with excellent gunplay that can cater to players from casual to hardcore. There's a bit of character optimization that could be polished a bit more, but it's really just a fun game to play with your friends and has plenty of tougher endgame challenges for those who are up to it.

Edit time:

This game has certainly come a long way. The character customization is a lot better (still has some flaws), the storytelling is second to none in a live service game. With Crossplay, there's no reason not to play with your friends of all stripes (as long as they have discord smdh). This game has gotten better, especially within the last year and shows no signs of slowing down.\t\t\t\t\t
