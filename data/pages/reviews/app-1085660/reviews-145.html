\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFollow the light, guardian. But not too bright cause then it will reflect off your screen.

A fantastic first-person-shooter MMO/RPG experience from Bungie. Destiny 2 is an experience unlike anything else.

However, the new-player experience, and this include the returning-player experience, can use some much needed work to guide players into what to do, what to grind, and what directions to go. This game has been out for many years now, meaning there is ample content for Guardians to explore. This can get QUITE overwhelming for new players, and even returning players if they've missed a couple of expansions.

With that being said, Destiny 2 provides gameplay experiences like none other. The atmosphere is engaging and awe-inspiring, the combat is fun, fast-paced, and engaging, and the opportunity to sweat with your friends in Raids, Nightfalls, and PvP while also allowing for some laid-back experiences makes Destiny 2 one of the best games of the decade.

Highly recommend, just make sure to find some friends or YouTube channels that will be able to help you through getting into (or back into) the gameplay experience.\t\t\t\t\t
