This game is awesome to say the least. Sure it has it's little snafoos, but the things it does right heavily outweigh what it does wrong.

Pros:
- Great multiplayer, especially if you are in a clan.
- Really cool gear to get in-game. (exotics and raid gear are dope)
- Fun mechanics.
- PVE is really deep with Strikes, Raids, Menagerie, etc...
- It's free!!!! You can have fun with the base game and not buy the DLC expansions. But I highly recommend you get both Forsaken and Shadowkeep. Adds awesome Exotics and legendary gear.
- PVP is great with a wide range of options and rotating game modes in Crucible, and you have Gambit which is a really cool mix of PVE and PVP.
- Micro-transactions in Eververse do not give you a leg up over anyone.
The scenery is great. Each planet is diverse and differentiated from other planets. It's fantastic level design.
- Obviously graphics are great.
- There are lots more pros but you can all figure them out yourself.

Cons:
- Micro-transactions. Not really a con, most games have them now. But they can suck money quick in this game. I know from first hand experience.
- Exotic weapons that are quest based take a while to get. The quests have a lot of steps and some take quite a while. Not really a con for me, mostly putting in the con section because some people will not like the grind to get powerful gear.
- Multiplayer can be pretty unbalanced at times. But overall it's solid.
- All the different currencies for the different planets can get confusing to the new player. There are so many currencies in this game, lol.
- Upgrading weapons and armor to masterwork requires a lot of said currency and specific items like enchantment cores to upgrade stuff.
- Again this isn't a con but might be to some. You should really join a clan once you get to a higher level. I;m not talking about some random clan, but and active one. I joined a clan called Apex Deft. They have an extremely active discord that is quite impressive. At the time of writing this, they are recruiting. The main reason for joining a clan is the higher level activities like Raids and high difficulty Nightfall; there is no matchmaking for those. You have to create a fireteam with people outside of the game. There is a Bungie LFG webpage that is active, but you never know who you'll get. With a clan like Apex, their Discord is heavily moderated and anyone that is a or is not active in chat will be kicked from the clan. And the organization is just insane with these people. Highly advise joining an active clan.

All in all, this game is worth every penny. Buy the DLC and slowly watch you life slip away.\t\t\t\t\t
