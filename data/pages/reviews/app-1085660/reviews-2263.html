\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is one of my favorite games- a game a come back to over and over again. As a loot shooter, it has a combination of sci-fi, loot, and impeccable gunplay that keeps me hooked. PC is my preferred platform after playing for years on PS4 through Destiny 1 and then 2, mostly for the reduced load times and that I can crank the visuals to 4k (though you could with a PS4-pro.) It's hands down my favorite co-op online shooter. It really shines in puzzle solving and depth of story where others barely prop those elements up.

My only complaint is Bungie's stubborn fireteams rules that require mics for elite content. Don't have time to commit to making online friends or don't have friends playing Destiny? Don't want to chat with strangers? Don't know why they can't explain objectives in co-op modes? They don't care. I play Destiny almost exclusively solo, and always have. The good news is that you can enjoy much of the content minus Raids and Dungeons. You can even dominate in Iron Banner with a 4x kill ratio all season with your equipment- you're not substantially handicapped by being locked out of Raids and Dungeon content.

No game is perfect. Get the game. Try it for free. It's a ton of fun no matter how you play. I just wish they'd open up match making for all events.\t\t\t\t\t
