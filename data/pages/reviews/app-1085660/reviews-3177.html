The PvE aspect is generally solid, although the move to Steam and switch to F2P has ruined it. So it's no longer actually good as enemies will now randomly teleport all around outside of their abilities. Playing the classes in PvE feels pretty incredible. The gunplay in PvE feels pretty good. There's a huge amount of PvE content to sink your balls into and if anyone else tells you otherwise, they're stupid and you're stupid too if you believe them. There's the main questline, side quests, public events, dungeons, raids, black armory, menagerie and loosely gambit. If you can't find something to do PvE, find a different game because Destiny can't offer anything to you.

The PvP aspect of this game is utter garbage. It's not fun in the slightest and there are Exotic quests tied to playing their horrible PvP gamemodes. Why is it bad? Because the time-to-kill is insanely quick, the maps are poorly put together, your abilities in PvP are stupid as ♥♥♥♥, shotguns rule and don't you dare try anything else, the netcoding is equivalent to a waste disposal plant. I can't tell you how many times I've aimed my gun, any gun, at a guy and have done zero damage despite WATCHING my bullets pass through him. It's insanely infuriating and frankly anybody who finds the Crucible entertaining or fun are unfortunately brain damaged idiots who need some kind of introduction of quality to their life. Because they aren't experiencing any. And let's not even get started on the garbage Halo movement you have to tussle through when playing competitively. Halo's movement is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ dated, buggy and only works in Halo because its a DATED game.

I've been playing this game only since Warmind while it was on the Blizzard launcher. It was more fun back then. The PvE is still decent and the PvP has always just been a crapshoot with its Peer-to-Peer netcoding. I'm sorry if you think the Crucible is fun, it must suck to have no brain cells left and to be deprived of quality in your life.

Gambit is stupid for its invasion mechanic. There, I said it.

All in all, Destiny's short of a good game and not worth the pricetag it's offering.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played over 1400hours (from Blizzard and Steam) in this game and a good few in Destiny 1 too. This game is infinitely replayable with endless activities as the game is constantly updated by Bungie, who are very down to earth with their community. It can all seem a bit overwhelming at first but I recommend starting with Redwar and then doing what you want from there on: perhaps start with expanding your collections by completing exotic quests, giving The Crucible a try, Gambit, taking on the other campaigns (Forsaken and Shadowkeep are good) and lastly taking on a Raid. A Raid is a 6 player activity requiring precise coordination and teamwork, tackling puzzles and boss mechanics. The Leviathan Raids are great Raids to start off with, but my favourite raid from Destiny 2 is the Last Wish. Its definitely one of longest raids Destiny 2 has to offer, and is the most demanding in terms of skill and coordination. The last encounter has a cheese but if you truly want a challenge I would try and beat properly. Though my all time favourite Raid would be Kings Fall from Destiny 1.
Lastly this is a game that you will either love or hate, if you enjoy Borderlands or Warframe I would definitely recommend this, and if your looking for something new to play be prepared as this game sucks away your time but provides endless entertainment.
If you have further questions please don't hesitate to ask me.\t\t\t\t\t
