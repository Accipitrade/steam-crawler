\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn all honesty, Shadowkeep is not worth buying (unless it eventually includes Forsaken). The content is minimal given the price and what is there is plagued by seemingly incompetent storytelling. However, the base game, while not critically well written, is enjoyable and the Forsaken expansion is worth every penny.
There's a lode of content for anybody just starting, enough to cover maybe 200 or 300 hours if you take your time and enjoy the different activities. Most of the gripes come from long time players and I will admit, those gripes are almost all entirely sound. Bungie's been messing up. A lot. The in-game real-money store seems to take creative priority and the base stories are filled with problems. However, new players will have plenty of time to enjoy the game for what it is before they inevitably become disappointed with what the game offers longterm.\t\t\t\t\t
