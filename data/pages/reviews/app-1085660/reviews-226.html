\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIs this game enjoyable? Yes. Is this game a great way to play with friends? Yes. Is it worth the continual money dump every year, which are getting more and more expensive? Up to you, I say not really anymore. Game had a decent launch... first two dlc, osiris and warmind were okay but not a lot. Foresaken really took off, and shadowkeep and beyond light each had enough to keep me interested in the game, and witch queen is fantastic so far. However, now they are charging nearly 100 dollars for deluxe editions of DLC when they used to be 70. And the worst one... content vaulting... the game had become "bloated with content" so the devs decided to vault content, meaning removing content from the game that you already paid for... it is so bad that the vanilla campaign from the games launch is no longer available to be played. As well as activities, end game content, weapons, armor, and entire locations effectively removed from the game... everything which I and other players paid to have access to. AND THEN after this... making the game free to play, while a good idea to get people into the game, was a tax on the players in from day one, diminishing the value of money already spent on the game for which new players were now getting content for free. I really, really like this game. I adore the story and love how much they have managed to salvage the unfortunate launch of this game in D1. I love how the community is generally helpful and I offer help to people who are looking to get in or have just started. It's a great way to get them towards end game activities relatively quickly. However, would I offer this game to someone whose never heard of it before? Probably not, just too expensive. The next expansion looks fantastic... great story threads looking to finish up, new locations, and a whole new ability for guardians... but for 100 dollars its setting the bar very high and I hope it hits it. I'm trying to maintain my expectations so i am not underwhelmed by the actual content that will be present in the next expansion.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHas become one of the worst gaming experiences to date.

Destiny 2 started weak but grew very well with Forsaken coming out, not to mention every other campaign and raid added so much new content that allowed you to experience D2 in the best way possible, with the ability to get friends to make their first character and start off the same way we all did. It was a great MMO and one of the only decent FPS MMOs.

However the DCV has ruined that. Having come back from a hiatus, convincing my friend to download and play through all the original campaigns and than try the new ones being released, I quickly noticed the extreme lack of content, not just original, but also free content. Everything was limited to an extreme amount and making most areas, paid areas with little inbetween.

Considering the price we all originally paid for DLC and some of us for D2 itself, we never expected a temporary addition, That is what the seasonal content was for. A removal of content should never have been the decision, not for any game and especially not for Destiny 2 which had so much to do in the free-to-play areas, with optional paid worlds that added a longer playtime.

Maybe I care too much about a decent cooperative experience, or maybe this cop-out truly is one of the worst mistakes in video game history. I never see enough people talk about it, but maybe that is why the world felt so empty when I first launched back in.

Hopefully D2 will return to it's former glory, I hold my doubts. At least the memories will always remain, and I will never forget the shame the corporate has brought to its studio of great writers.

Destiny 2 has truly fallen from light.
See you on the other side, guardians.\t\t\t\t\t
