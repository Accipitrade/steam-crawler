Somewhere along the way from Forsaken onwards Bungie started trying to milk your wallet for more money, while simultaneously giving you less for it. The Free to play is not Destiny 2, it's basically there to show you the few good things, and make you think you'll enjoy the rest of it. Not even joking, most of the original Destiny 2 content, including the story, is gone. They took it out to make room for newer content, and are currently planning to take out the best piece of content they've ever made, Forsaken.

There's no accommodation for anyone who's new to the franchise. They've made the game so jarring to attempt to start as a new player, as you're thrown into the game with practically no guidance, but they've also taken out most of what the older players enjoyed in favor of lackluster content that just isn't worth the trade. Their new business tactic relies heavily on FOMO, and it's done so shamelessly that I can't stand the series anymore.

I used to recommend this game to everyone I knew, and was obsessed with the lore, universe, and gameplay that I practically never shut up about it. This game hooked me like nothing ever has, and I still check back on whats going on because I so badly want it to do that again. It's fallen so far that I not only don't play it, but actively want to discourage people from playing it. It's just not worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
