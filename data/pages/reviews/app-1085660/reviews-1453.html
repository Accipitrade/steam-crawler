BUT THEY CAN'T BECAUSE THEY SHOT BOTH OF THEIR OWN FEET BY MAKING THIS GAME *ONLINE ONLY* MAKING THIS IMPOSSIBLE. I still cannot fathom why they thought this was a good idea when borderlands 2 worked perfectly fine offline.

Don't buy the new beyond light rubbish no matter how fantastic it looks. Wait for the dlc to settle from the new release to see how the game state fares as the only thing you have to go off currently is an E3 trailer minus the E3 part & the promise of new content while having other content *taken* from you and shelved.

You get more lore from forsaken about them. go play that instead.

I just know that for some reason I can play every game in my library without constant disconnects with the exception of Destiny 2.

The player base is pretty toxic which leads you to stick to your own clans. I haven't needed a sherpa in a year but I still join new groups to help them with raids. Its amazing how many LFG discord sherpas have zero teaching ability. In pvp you will be teabagged by players using the instakill weapons so often it becomes normal. Outside of raids you will be running into matchmaking with afkers that are encouraged by bungie's terrible reporting system.

One last note on something that has irritated me for months as I've been trying to get more into pvp..... Destiny 2 allows people with VAC bans to play their competitive pvp circuit. Let that sink it. Bungie has terrible management and programming ability for that to hit the main game.\t\t\t\t\t
