\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOnly fun if you have friends to play with. Other than that, it's just a time killer.
The main campaigns are kind of a slog to get through. The story is interesting enough in most campaigns, but all it amounts to is shooting more aliens. That might be fine for some people; but I've played way too many first person shooters at this point in my life. Just shooting whatever gets in my way has become rote at this point.
There are three character classes you can play as. Player race is purely cosmetic. I would recommend either Warlock or Titan. Warlock has the best grenades, jumping ability, and the Nova Bomb super is a real problem solver. Titans have the best melee. Hunters are the third class, and they get to wear capes.
The real game comes after you complete or ignore the campaigns, and start grinding for loot. There are a few options to reach the level cap, and you'll probably want to do a mixture of them. There's strike playlists where you run through missions from the game at higher levels. Crucible is the PVP mode. Gambit is competitive wave based gameplay where two teams compete to see who can kill the most enemies, summon a boss, and kill it before the other team. As far as I'm aware, there's nothing quite like gambit in any other game. These will all drop loot of a slightly higher level than what you have until you reach level cap. This will take a while if you're starting from scratch.
There are quests for exotic items and particularly powerful weapons. You'll probably complete a few of these just doing what you're doing, but you'll need to go out of your way to do most of them. These are worth doing, because some of them are extremely powerful and will make your life easier. There's really no way of knowing which ones you'll like before you get them unless you look them up.
The most fun part of the game is the raids. They encorperate puzzles and problem solving into the game, and are the most challenging part of the game. You need six people to do them, and at least decent gear. Play the game for a while, until you find guns that feel good and then either join a clan or gather up five of your closest friends.\t\t\t\t\t
