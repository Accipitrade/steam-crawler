\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think this is a great game. I'm an old school fantasy mmorpg player. I played EQ from launch until the release of EQ2, and tried every major title after. I was playing around in ESO, which is a great game, but it doesn't keep me playing every day. This game fills the void perfectly for me. I've always been a fan of first person shooters. In between my mmorpg raiding and grinding, it was always fun to play battlefield, and game like that.

This feels like a great marrying of both genres of games. It has everything that an mmorpg offers. Quest, raids, dungeons. Great pvp in controlled environments. It even has swords. The class system is great. Each being very viable to do anything. I ultimately landed on the titan. I regularly play the other two classes though. I love that there is no death penalty, no repair fees. If you fail, you get back in there and hit it until you get it done.

I believe this is what EA was trying to achieve with that horrible failure of Anthem. Bungie gets it right. Plus, they told Activision / Blizzard to go pound sand. That's the real reason I bought the game. To support them in their telling the second worst gaming company in the world to go piss off. Playing the game, and enjoying it very much has been a very pleasant surprise.\t\t\t\t\t
