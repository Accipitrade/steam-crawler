\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has been in my life since I started 7th grade. I got into the Destiny 1 alpha by some sort of miracle and I just fell in love with the game. I played the Beta and it got me even more hyped for it's release. I played back on the PS4 when the game first came out. I didn't play as much as I would have liked to since it took me a month to complete the main story. I really got back in during The Dark Below where I met one of my closest friends who still plays with me whenever I hop back on PS4 from time to time. The Taken King was awesome since we got new subclasses which are still my most used subclasses in Destiny 2. Rise of Iron better known as Rise of Tapir was fun and amazing and It's where I met most of my current friends today. I still to this day have never beaten Wrath of the Machine. Destiny 2's launch was okay. I compelted the main story within 24 hours and played until Curse of Osiris. I quit Destiny 2 about a week into Curse of Osiris and didn't return until Warmind had been out for at least a month. Forsaken got me so hyped and re-lit the flame under me to play more Destiny. Black Armory was fun but I didn't play it as much as I would have liked to. Joker's Wild was great but getting the triumph was what drove me away since there we way too many armor sets to get. Penumbra was great and I played it a lot mainly to get my godroll Austringer which I still use to this day. Shadowkeep is one of the best if not the best DLC's Bungie has ever released. Thier were some Day 1 problems but every game as them. I have grinded my butt off in Shadowkeep and almost have gotten all the pinnacle weapons except for Mountaintop and Loaded Question. I'm not going after Hush since I'm not the biggest fan of bows. New Light is a great place to start your Destiny adventure. If you want to have the most fun I recommend picking up at least the Forsaken DLC so you can get access to the other 9 new supers. My favorite is Blade Barrage combined with Shards of Galanor.

Final Score: 9/10 Highly Recommended for anyone.\t\t\t\t\t
