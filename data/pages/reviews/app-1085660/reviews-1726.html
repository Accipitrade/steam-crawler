Personally, I enjoy the game and all that comes with it. It deep down is a grindy looter shooter. It tries to appeal to the competitive side of the playerbase but I dont think the PvP in this game can hold someones attention for that long if you're that type of gamer who likes
a methodical FPS. The PvE is where it shines IMOP, every week you will have stuff to do to get more loot, and the PvE activities at first (game launch) were lacluster. But overtime have seemed to get better. I recommend this game to anyone, atleast to give it a try, they have a free to play download and it sure is worth a shot to see if it is the game for you. Having a group of buddies to play this game is always a blast, plus it makes endgame activities not as stressful. But yeah, there ya go.\t\t\t\t\t
