\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTl;dr - great pew pew, crappy menus.

Sometimes it's nice to shut your brain off and shoot a LOT of things. This game accomplishes that handily. It's beautiful and has excellent combat mechanics, and the voice acting is excellent.

There are things that I dislike, and here they are:

1) Much of the best gear requires the player to compete in PvP contests in order to complete the unlock requirements, and they do not seem to be balanced in any meaningful way. Importantly, these PvP contests are not required for any part of the story missions.

2) Your party (fireteam) is limited in size to 3 members in most situations (all story). There are some raids and game modes where you are on teams of 6 and 4, respectively, but in general you are limited to 3.

3) The UI is utterly unintuitive, and many aspects of the game are very poorly explained to the players, if they are explained at all. Fortunately, there exists a very dedicated community that provides guides both written and video for every facet of gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
