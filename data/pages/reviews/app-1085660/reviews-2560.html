\r\n\t\t\t\t\tComing from Warframe, I find this actually better, more polished.

Full disclosure, I paid for Forsaken, Shadowkeep and season passes, but I was hooked with the free version "New Light", and only decided to get the paid editions after I knew I liked the game.

Areas where it shines

Better Quests: There are variety of mini quests called "Adventures", and a nice set of story campaigns - most of which don't require grind.

Optimization: The graphics is pleasant. The optimization is top notch, didn't feel any difference when playing with my laptop with Gtg 1060.

Immediacy: Weapons (or anything in Destiny) don't take days to build - though TBH I didn't find that a big deterrent in playing Warframe.

Postmaster: Missed some resources or weapons that were dropped? Rest assured they were delivered to the Postmaster, for you to pick up once you are out of the level.

Logging in: Destiny doesn't even ask you for an additional password when you launch from Steam!
Can't say how many times I launched Warframe, and didn't remember the password so called it quits. Curiously enough, this simple feature has been asked for and overlooked on Warframe forums, https://forums.warframe.com/topic/972840-auto-logging-in-for-steam-users.

Areas which can be improved
Matchmaking: For some unfathomable reason the matchmaking on some endgame content is absent. You are supposed to only play with friends, or advertise through Discord and what not to assemble a team. If this wasn't the case, Destiny would have been perfect.

Weapon variety: Compared to Warframe, weapons feel very similar.

Overall, give it a shot, the free campaigns are a treat too even if you don't want to get any paid content.\t\t\t\t\t
