\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing this game for a pretty long while, since before I graduated high school. I've always enjoyed it, but I've also been less than satisfied with many different aspects of the game, whether it be a lack of content and world-building, to the shady practices that Bungie facilitated when they were under management by Activision. I have to say though, in it's current state, this is one of my go-to mmo games that I play quite regularly. I think they've definitely improved on the storytelling in their two most recent expansions, Forsaken and Shadowkeep. They've also pulled a stellar move and made the base game and the first two expansions free. The only things I have to say negatively about it is that I still don't think they did an amazing job at creating backstory, as I had to watch a video to understand all of the lore (I'm not exactly keen to reading cards to get lore in a game like this). This game is also not super friendly to new players. I started my titan over again to see what the new light experience would be like, and after the new intro, I had to look up what to do next. Maybe make it more clear what new players should do?

I highly recommend this game. It has flaws, but Bungie seems dedicated to fixing their mistakes, and what is there already is still amazing. A shining example that triple a developers still have a little bit of soul left in them.\t\t\t\t\t
