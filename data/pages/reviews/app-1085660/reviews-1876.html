\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL:DR: Don't buy if you're a returning player (even if you're a new player better wait till September for the next expac) Game is in it's worst state it has ever been.

Don't get me wrong this game at it's core is not bad, that's the reason i have 200 hours in even though I really don't have that much time to play games. It's core gameplay mechanics are fun and have always been. It's gameplay is fast paced dinamic and flashy (if you're into that sort of thing). Now you might be asking yourself why am I then posting this negative review. At the point of writing this i really can't in good conciense recommend the game to anyone. It's grindy and repettive as hell. Plus even if you want to progress you can't access the content at your own pace as most of it is time gated. If for some reason you're a slow player for me that won't be such a problem but if you have let's say 12 hours to play only in the Saturday then my friend you're screwed royally. The Shadowkeep expac is honestly the lowest and most disappointing thing in this games history I've seen. The items are honestly not all that fun. The story is basically non-existent in this expac. Not the mention the sheer lack of content as a whole. We got a dungeon okay a raid okay and in the seasons in this repetitive honestly not fun to slog through world events, which even if you're a great big fan like me would get stale after a week or two of doing them. Let's say after beating yourself to death for 2-3 weeks of grind (like papa buguo wants you to) you've finally reached an item level where you can actually make a build and play the game as intented. Well kind of s**ks to be you since by the time most people get to that point the hardcore player have either exhausted the expac or just don't care about doing anything. So you'd have to wait around and wait for a group (in the phone app) to do a lot of the activities in the game (since for the raids or dungeons there is no in game matchmaking) Only to probably be kicked from said group because you wiped once since you didn't watch a 40 minute dungeon/raid explanation video on youtube. All in all is the game good: YES, Should you buy the game right now: YES (if you've never ever played it before if you're a returning player then NOOOOOO). Is Shadowkeep expansion worth it's money: HONESTLY NOT just buy smth else and hope that the Beyond Light expansion would be better (though my hopes and the communities hopes as a whole are really really low)\t\t\t\t\t
