addendum: after a bit more playing I can definitely say that you will hit a lot of progression walls with equipment after reaching a soft power cap and even more so on the less soft power cap (currently 950). Still recommended, however not without some doubts.

some time later:
rotating content in and out may be fine in theory, but the way they are doing it is effectively cutting off free players from anything useful or fun to do.
