\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI give this game a solid 6/10.

Pros
- Great graphics. Very well optimised for low end rigs.
- Good Gameplay with great gun mechanics and armour customizations.
- Has a good story and story level designs.

Cons
- The grind is real. You can forget about your real life if you want to get anything decent.
- Destiny 2: New light presumes you've already completed the campaign thus, the endgame content is unlocked the moment you install the game.
- Not newbie friendly at all. You complete the intro mission and get thrown into what is called 'The tower'. From there you can do whatever the hell you want.
- The servers can be a real pain to connect to sometimes.

Conclusion
Although the game presents itself well with great gameplay and mechanics along with well thought out lore and details. It fails to be very enjoyable for new players who get thrown into an unknown oblivion not knowing how to proceed from there. Campaign mission should stand as the main highlight of the game thus bringing more people interested in the lore. Yet, Bungie somehow thought it'd be better to unlock the endgame content first and simply render the campaign missions useless.

While I'm giving this game a thumbs up, doesn't necessarily mean I recommend this game to everyone.

Enjoy the game, Guardians.\t\t\t\t\t
