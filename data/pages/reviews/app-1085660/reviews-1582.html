\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a great overall game! However the biggest stipulation is that it takes a little bit to get to the level where you can access most of the game. If you are brand new to the destiny franchise and wanted to try out New Light for free, I'll tell you its a great deal and is fun once you get into it.

That being said, Destiny has a tendency to not tell you what to do or how to do it. Which is great once you get more immersed and get to the endgame, but starting out can be rough if you have no prior experience or no one to guide you. There are many New Light guides on the DestinytheGame subreddit, but outside that, there's not much guidance.

Personally, I have played this game and the one before it for a few thousand hours combined and think its a great game with a lot of fun activities once you get into it. If you find a group of players or friends to play with you, even better. Its definetly game I would recommend, and despite its flaws, its still one of the most fun games I have ever played.\t\t\t\t\t
