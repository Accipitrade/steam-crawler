\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing this game since the beta release. Over the game's lifetime, there have been many ups and downs to say the least, and just a month ago was a huge positive. I had taken a couple year break and I picked the game back up to find a wealth of new content and plenty of things to do, even for F2P players (as I was one at the time, then I bought all the DLC and the season pass because I was pretty impressed and I wanted more content). I knew about the vaulting, but I didn't expect it to get rid of so much content.

I just started to play today to see all the new stuff. I'm very disappointed to say the least. There's not much left, and very little variety. And there is next to no F2P stuff compared to the previous version. I was really looking forward to new stuff to do, but it's all locked behind a pay wall. Granted, this is the way the game used to be entirely, all the new content was pay to play. It was a nice breath of fresh air to be able to play a lot of the game for free, and then move on to buy the DLC to get even more. Now it just feels like Year 1 D2 all over again with the amount of content available.

Honestly, I just came back to use good shooting mechanics to blast aliens. Instead, I was met with a really solid experience and a lot of things to do with some sick looking seasonal armor that sucked me right back in along with my wallet. I thought the new model was the way to go.

As for my review, I can't recommend this game to new players at the moment, unless you have a sort of background to understand what's going on, or you just want to shoot things casually. At the moment, it really seems built for the veterans that have been a round a while, who know what is going on, and who are willing to shell out the money to keep playing.

As for me, I'll still probably play casually to blast aliens. It's just a shame there's not as many places to do it anymore, or as many places to explore.
