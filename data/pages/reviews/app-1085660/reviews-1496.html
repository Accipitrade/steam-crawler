\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played this game a lot. Not nearly as much as many other people, but long enough to start to wonder about sunk cost fallacy. Am I playing because I bought all this stuff? Do I really enjoy it?

Yes and no, respectively. I find myself infuriated at this game more often than not. There's so much grind, and so much of it is unrewarding. There are a dozen negative things I want to expound upon. The jumping and platforming. Getting stuck on floors. Boss stomps. Sunsetting. I won't bore you further, just check reddit, people complain about this stuff all the time over there.

Bungie has made improvements, sure. The umbral engram system is really great. I still can't shake the feeling that all the mats and grind I've done will be made redundant as soon as the next DLC drops. I'll come back and give it another try then. I'll update my review. But for now? Give it a pass.\t\t\t\t\t
