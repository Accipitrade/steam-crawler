\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThank you, Bungie, for bringing this game to Steam. Because I sometimes live under a rock, I didn't even know Destiny 2 had a PC version until it hit Steam. This game is amazing running on my i7-4790k, Radeon 380X Black Edition, 32GB DDR3 system.

Graphics: Pretty
Environments: Amazing in most places.
Sound: Great
Gameplay: For an FPS it is fairly varied making it more enjoyable, with different PvE, PvP, and PvEvP modes.
Customization Options: So many. Especially when it comes to shaders and equipment ornaments.
Storyline: Not too hard to catch onto but wish I could have played the first Destiny.
Other things: I really like variety in the length of missions like the mini-missions or "Patrols", the area missions like the bounties, public events, lost sectors, the main story missions, the vanguard strikes, gambits, and crucible matches.

My only complaint so far: Sometimes it can take several minutes to get just 12 players for a match in Crucible or even 8 for Gambit, with how popular this game is I really hope this gets improved.

Still would highly recommend. Especially for people that like FPS/RPG hybrids.\t\t\t\t\t
