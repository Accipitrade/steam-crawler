\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile Destiny does have its down moments, every content drop develops a hype among its player base that is pretty remarkable. With the new direction the game is headed (season pass with content expiring every season) the player base has become extremely split. The player base also does not like the current iteration of the in-game cosmetic store, Eververse. While Eververse was originally promised to bring new free content into the game, and has produced some pretty incredible content in the past, it appears to have not really produced much aside from disdain since the release of Shadowkeep in Oct. 2019. However, it is very likely Bungie has something up their sleeve for the near future.

Overall, the game boasts excellent shooting mechanics, a decent loot system, and end game content that will keep players entertained for hours (although most end game content requires you to be in a non-matchmade group of 3 or more players). It has personally provided me with over 1500 hours on Xbox and PC.\t\t\t\t\t
