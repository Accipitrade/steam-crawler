\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo should you get destiny? No. It is simultaneously the best and worst game I have ever played. The gun-play is unlike anything else currently on the market, it feels amazing. Everything about this game is great, yet that greatness is marred by some of the smartest and most stupid decisions I have ever seen in a game. It seems like every other week the dev's have the community either ecstatic or outraged over a change.

That being said this is still the best game I have ever played, and if that was its only problem I would totally recommend it. The bigger problem is the new player experience. If you do not have friends who are planning to baby sit you into the game, you will be totally lost. There is no in-game explanation for some of the most important things for a new player to learn. There are far too many materials and activities all with different rarities, caps, and difficulties. There are so many unexplained things I wouldn't be able to list them all here.

However, if you do decide to start playing despite this, you won't regret it. Some day you will look back and say, "why did I let myself get addicted to this game", then laugh, chastising yourself for the ridiculous thought. You didn't get addicted, the game simply controls you now, like it has since the beginning.\t\t\t\t\t
