I'll try and put into words,my feelings as of right now Season of the Hunt(First season after the Beyond Light release).

The game is addicting, trully addicting. I firstly, gave it a shot, because it was free and I was thrilled by all the classes, gameplay mechanics and beautiful open worlds you can get lost into.The graphics are beautiful and the soundtrack is amazing. But after 20ish (amazing) hours you understand the whole system it's built in .Without high power level you are nothing and in order to raise it you have to go through so many hours that I wish I could justify but no. It means nothing every 3 months when the level cap is reset and you are a nothing.Grind all over again. All the effort means nothing and I haven't even managed to reach even the grandmaster level cap. The game doesn't let you matchmake for higher level strikes or raids and dungeons, so without a a good team of 3-6 you can't enjoy those activities. Also you have to play constantly to catch up with the content level up etc which is an investment that will truly disappoint everyone given a month or two .I won't touch sunseting and vaulting (which in short is that you can not use weapons/armor or play activieties of past dlcs) because I'm not as a hardcore player but paying for Forsake and Shadowfall even on a good sale seems like a joke to me now looking back.Thank God I din't jump on the hype and buy Beyond Light, which is required to do anything on Europa as of right now. And even the story isn't as interesting as some present it to be. I'm utterly disappointed and i don't think I'll ever get invested as much into Destiny 2 unless something really big happens. I guess I might stick for some casual gameplay, pvp and maybe do the story every now and then. But that being said,Bungie it's such a shame.I feels so bad for this title. Thankfully i didn't waste so much of my time...\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2.
Playing since D1. Day one.

Audience - Pretty easy for beginners to jump into with the new tutorial, but plenty for expert gamers to sink their teeth into. PvP is fun and the PVE certainly has it's challenges.
☑ Beginner
☑ Casual Gamer
☑ Normal Gamer
☑ Expert

Graphics - Runs well and looks superb. FOV slider makes a big difference to perspective and the inclusion of HDR has added further graphic fidelity. Although the game generally runs well, there are specific areas that are well known for causing frame drops, I have also encountered this.
☑ Fantastic


Music - Brilliant original soundtrack, anyone who is familiar with Halo/bungie games will enjoy this soundtrack for sure.
☑ Fantastic

Difficulty - This really is quite varied depending on what you are doing. You can choose to run things such as public events in easier modes, or switch them to heroic. Strikes (basic dungeons) are generally run pretty quick thanks to expert player grouping. Raids require much more of a tactical approach and dedicated team of players. PvP is challenging and many well practised players frequent the crucible. All that said, the basic story mode of the game plays really well and at whatever pace you are comfortable with.
☑ Easy
☑ Average
☑ Easy to learn / Hard to master
☑ Hard


Bugs - Some errors could be found. Bungie work fast after break up with Activi$ion
☑ Nothing encountered

Story - Amazing. Has a lot of mistery on who´s the traveler.
☑ Good


Gameplay - A good balance of fun and challenge so far.
☑ Fun
☑ Challenging

Game time / Length - Due to the nature of this game and how content is rolled out year on year, the game time is ever increasing. Even with the free base game you have a huge amount of content to play through.
☑ Extremely Long (1000 + Hours)

Price/Quality - It's free! and probably one of the best bits of entertainment both free or paid I've ever encountered. The free game offers so much content and a much more refined new player experience since beyond light released.
☑ Full price\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is not a game for everybody. With that being said, I personally love this game. Yes there is a lot of go here and do this or kill this type of chore quests people complain about, but overall, fun story, fun raids, fun dungeons, great gun play, fun movement, cool abilities. Randomized gun rolls are back, so you never know how good the weapon you get is until you open it up and check it out, they are rarely the same rolls. which makes getting drops of weapons you already have still fun and gives length to overall game life.

The main reason I recommend this game is because there have been few games that can actually bring me in and have me lost for hours, not only playing with actual content but also going through my gear, tweaking things, trying to adjust armor just right to get the perfect stats. The list goes on, buy this game.\t\t\t\t\t
