\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll honestly just give it the most straight-foward review I can

💠Destiny 2 repurposed and restructured alot about how progression worked inorder to switch to a free-to-play model, the starting quest doesn't really guide you well, mostly just telling you reach a set amount of light and doesnt tell you the most efficient ways to do so.

💠Despite the poor initial guiding, I do think this game is definitely worth sinking time into, the real appeal of this game becomes apparent in the end game, chasing exotics is decent busywork, but the secret missions, dungeon(s) and co-op raids really make that grind alot more worth it, there are some really unique endgame missions.

The best way to view it is that you can level to max light without the need for spending money, but if you really want more of Destiny you buy into the expansions for aditional powerful gear sources and activities, the structuring is strange but I've been enjoying it, if you need to run stuff I'm more than happy to help so give it a shot\t\t\t\t\t
