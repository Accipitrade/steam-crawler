\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing Destiny since year the launch of The Taken King. Despite none of my friends playing on the same platform as me, (xbox 360 at the time) Destiny 1 may be my favorite game of all time. I bought an Xbox 1 for Rise of Iron, and a PS4 for D2 launch. This game is wildly inconsistent, you basically have to count on a dice roll to have fun in any given season. When it's fun it's amazing, and when it's bad it's offensively boring. Play it, see if it's your cup of tea.

Pros.
Good timesink/grind game.
Some of the best movement of any pvp shooter.
Extremely well designed raids.

Play endgame PvE and you'll love it.

Cons.
The game can be the worst grind-y on the market when the loot isn't worth the shoot.
PvP is highly meta contingent, same with PvE e.g. Anarchy and Breech GLs.
The core playlists are mind-numbingly boring. I've played on the same maps and the same strikes for 6 years now, New content is constantly subbed out for reuse of old content.

PvP endgame is terrible. Trials is at least.

The FTP experience is not great, so if you're willing to invest any time into this game you're gonna have to pay for it, despite it appearing to be free.

Pay in blood, join the club.\t\t\t\t\t
