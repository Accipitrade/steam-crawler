\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is very inconsistent for people who have completed most of the endgame stuff. If you are playing for the first time, you have a lot of content to play. Like. ALOT for F R E E. If you want to purchase the expansions, that is a lot more new content added onto the game. The main problem with the game right now is sustainability. If you have played through everything and are almost max level, the game feels very underwhelming for those players. The game at this point has a big problem with cheaters/hackers in PvP, especially Trials of Osiris.
PROS: Very smooth gameplay, multiple exotics to grind for, base game up to the first major expansion, Forsaken is free, and for beginning players, there is so much content, it may feel overwhelming
CONS: For players with thousands of hours like me (more on PS4) the game might feel dry after a couple months after major expansion release, At the time I am writing this, there is problems with hackers and cheating in PvP.
OVERALL: I recommend this game to players who haven't touched Destiny in a while or never at all. This game is extremely questionable (right now) to the hardcore Destiny players. Even events like the Guardian Games is extremely lackluster for these kind of players. If you haven't played, pick it up, it's free! If you feel dissatisfied with the game right now, stop playing for a while! Have some fun with some different games!\t\t\t\t\t
