\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing this game long enough to realize a few things--it's badly designed, the game is rather restricted, and I honestly do not have fun with it.

If you're the kind of person who just loves dying to absolutely nothing, or someone who has to deal with annoying game mechanics and boss mechanics that are literally recycled across EVERY boss, and who loves getting the "YOU WERE DEFEATED" every fricking time you try to do an activity in a group? Maybe this is for you, but I avoid Dark Souls-like systems for a damn reason.

In terms of actual gameplay; the game feels like it was designed PURELY for the diehard "You died" crowd, or the crowd that's already in end-game tiered gear, and everyone else can frick right the hell off.

How? Simple. You often have periods where you're getting swarmed by enemies, or being attacked by enemies that are incapable of being attacked back (IE: Enemy drop ships who can nearly one-shot you in some cases, but you cannot fire back at them and they love to throw them at you in areas with no to little cover whatsoever), or enemies are rendered 'immune' to EVERYTHING with no to little visual ques as to why they're invulnerable--this game does a REALLY BAD JOB at trying to tell you WHERE you need to go. Oftentime when it DOES give an indicator the indicator is SO SMALL that you can't SEE it when you're being blasted from like six different directions at once.

And then you have the bosses in NORMAL strikes (read: NORMAL. As in "NOT HEROIC" or "NOT RAID". N O R M A L) whom literally just kick your teeth in from the get-go, and there is no real sense of 'improving' or 'getting stronger' because EVERYTHING scales -WITH- you so you're always going to get destroyed at the exact same rate unless, again, you're one of those few with top-end gear and end-game crap. Oh, and ALL of the bosses in this game have swarms of adds that never stop coming in, almost all of them have some kind of annoying attack that just one-shots you with little to no visual warning that a big bad attack is coming, and all in all it just feels like the game designers were just sloppy and lazy and refused to add in proper warning mechanics or tried to teach their players how this one boss works and it honestly just feels like every boss fights like every OTHER boss.

They all have recycled attacks, and honestly I've stopped giving a damn about this game.

Oh, and did you know that 90% of the game is locked behind paywalls? In order to get the 'good' (air quotes mandatory) stuff you NEED to get the Season PAss which is like 20 USD or some odd number like that, and the game LOVES to try and force 'COME TO THE STORE AND BUY OUR CRAP' labels on the bottom of the damn screen that PULSES BRIGHTLY until you LOOK at it.

And then there's the FORCED GAMEPLAY TIPS that even when I have the GAMEPLAY TIPS toggled to 'OFF' they still keep coming up, forcibly TAKING control away from me until I go to this VERY SPECIFIC WINDOW IN THIS VERY VERY SPECIFIC WAY THAT THE DEVELOPERS WANT ME TO GO. AND THEN the final icing on the cake is that the developers, in their infinite stupidity, decided to go "OH HEY LET'S ADD A FEATURE WHERE IT FORCES YOU INTO MISSIONS DESPITE PLAYERS NOT OPTING INTO THEM, YEAH, THAT SURE SOUNDS LIKE A GREAT IDEA!" where you'll log in and suddenly you're being thrown into content MANDATORIALLY.

Give players a CHOICE to go into a mission, Bungie. Not this forced mission garbage you're using.

Sure, completing the stuff gives you gear--but the rate at which your power rating increases is honestly at a snail's pace, I can go through like 5 strikes and only gain 15 power rating when you need like hundreds of power ratings just to get into better stuff. I'm sorry, but I can't throw myself at this badly designed piece of trash anymore--if I want a grind that feels like it's going nowhere I'll play World of Warcraft, at least I don't randomly get one-shot out of a badly designed game mechanic on that game.

All in all, I cannot give this game a recommendation--there's far too many factors making the game generally unfun, unenjoyable, and unplayable for me.

If you like dying over and over to stupid crap, this might be for you--but I don't have the patience to throw my face at this piece of crap anymore. Sorry, but nah. I'll spend my money on things that don't make me Alt-F4 constantly out of feeling like I've been cheated out of something. When a game stops being fun, then you know the game designers just failed at their damn job.

Peace, Bungie.\t\t\t\t\t
