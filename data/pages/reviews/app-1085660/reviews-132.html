\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlmost 2000 hours... wow!

I started playing this game in Season of Arrivals. (The last little part of the Shadowkeep expansion) and as a new player, I found this game tough to follow. The ever-expanding world, and at times strange jargon from players were discouraging. But thanks to a friend of mine (a Uni-que individual), I had a consistent player to play with you showed me the ropes. I joined a clan and got to know and expand the clan. My clan is so helpful with bridging the knowledge gap associated with picking this game up. And I now enjoy the game even more!

Fair Warning: Though it is "Free" the expansions are paid and are closer to the $60 range and are released about yearly. The "Free" content is engaging enough sure, but you can tell that the paid content (excluding cosmetics) is much more so.

Solo: 7/10
With Friends: 8.5/10
With Friends, as obsessed with the game as you: 9.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
