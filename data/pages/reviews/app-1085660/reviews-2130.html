\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've enjoyed the Destiny series for years across multiple platforms, and while Destiny 2 is far from a perfect game I have enjoyed playing it. My only major complaint with the game would be that the non competitive playlists also have skill based matchmaking, which I feel harms the overall experience of the more casual playlists. While I certainly have other complaints with the game and the series as a whole most if not all of them are small and nit picky. The core gameplay loop is great, just like you would expect from a Bungie game. The world building is also fantastic and while on the surface the story is rather average, if you engage with the deeper narrative and lore of the game it becomes complex, deep, and compelling. The game is also free now so their is no reason not to at least try out playing the game.\t\t\t\t\t
