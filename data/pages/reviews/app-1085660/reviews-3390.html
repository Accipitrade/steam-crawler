\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith the latest move committed by Bungie, Destiny 2 has arrived on Steam and is staying for a long while. (Goodbye Activision)

I have plenty of time playing Destiny 2 and after the recent move, it feels a bit better and smoother here than on Blizzard's
platform, but skipping that comes the pros and cons.

PROS
  • Game is free for everyone to play
  • Access to the first expansions (Curse of Osiris and Warmind), granting access to new areas
  • New tutorial for everyone to experience
  • Did I mention it's free?

CONS
  • No leveling system [All characters start with a power level of 750]
  • Season Pass is rewarding but can't confirm if it's for everyone
  • New players will be confused on where to go next once they finish the tutorial
  • Grinding can get relatively annoying when boosting your power level once you hit the soft cap


Other Thoughts
While this is a great change on Bungie's part, it's still rather confusing and I'd rather see
Bungie allow new players to start from scratch so they can move their way up instead of just forcing all characters to Power Level 750 at start and giving them a guide on where to start.
Overall, the game is fun with friends. So grab a gun and travel into the unknown where
tough enemies await you.

Be brave, guardians!\t\t\t\t\t
