\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think it's safe to say that Destiny has become one of my favourite games of all time. I just love the gameplay and the lore. I started playing when Forsaken launched, so about 2 years ago. I had to find most stuff out on my own, but the game was pretty good at guiding me (back then at least). Once I got into raiding, I really started to love the game. Raids are 6 man activities with multiple encounters and different/challenging mechanics.

The Crucible (pvp) can be pretty fun at times, but the meta is really boring at the moment. Auto rifles are way too powerful and are really easy to use. When I try to use something different/experimental, I usually get smoked by cheesy loadouts that don't require any sort of skill. Really hoping that the meta changes with the next expansion


The game in general:
Unfortunately, since the last expansion (shadowkeep), it has gone a bit downhill. The New Light experience (New Player experience) is in my opinion very confusing. When you first load up the game you have to play a small introduction, but it really doesn't explain much. After you've done that, you get like 20 different quests that you have to do, without any real guidance. I'm guiding a few New Lights myself, and it really helps to have someone explain everything (according to them). However, it's really exhausting for me because I don't really know in what order I should be doing activities with them. But at the same time, I'm having a blast. It's really fun seeing them figure out mechanics and stuff. In the end, it's all worth it.

Do I still recommend this game? Well, it's free so definitely give it a shot. Just know that the game is a lot more fun with friends, especially if you're a New Light. The game gets a lot more fun the more you play it.

I hope this review helped out :)\t\t\t\t\t
