The gameplay is what really shines in Destiny, There's enough gun types to suit anyone's play-style, and the exotics mix things up to make them even more interesting.

The PVP is well balanced and is pretty fun overall. I'm not usually into PvP but I have a lot of fun in the Crucible. Though I'm not a huge fan of the Gambit. It should be cut down to a single round because it's a waste of time.

The Strikes and Raids are where the most fun is. There's enough interesting fights to keep you on your toes. That being said, they can get a little repetitive. That's fine though, since there's enough to do to change it up.\t\t\t\t\t
