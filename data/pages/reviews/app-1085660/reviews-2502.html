\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEvery second this game continues to exist makes me sadder. I play a lot of it, to be sure, but I'm fairly loathe to do so outside the rote "need to get <x> stupid gun" or "must do <y> achievement solo" for no good reason. The story has never been better, and that goes for the entire history of the franchise, but the gameplay and the associated loop has never been worse. Bungie wanted to show us "Bungie's Destiny" apart from Activision. Well they have, and it's absolutely abysmal. The bugs continue to pile up, the meta becomes more cheesy with each passing season, and the core playlists continue to languish in an intermediate hell of repetitious and unchanging boredom to which I would not subject my worst enemy. Everything is in place for this to continue to be a truly and staggeringly excellent franchise, but Bungie's complete and utter lack of respect for its player base seems to get worse with each insultingly lazy season they release. Even the previously infallible endgame content (raids and dungeons) seem to get worse with each iteration. At this point, Bungie should stick to writing science fiction rather than trying to afford the opportunity to its players to live it out, because it just ain't getting the job done, as much as it continues to feel like an actual "job." I implore you, do not waste your time. Just don't.\t\t\t\t\t
