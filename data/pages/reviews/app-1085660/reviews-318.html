\r\n\t\t\t\t\t First off want to say I don't regret the time I put into the game as it still is some of the most fun I've had in gaming, and will always have the most satisfying gun-play and subclass abilities compared to any other game. Unfortunately the game has severely suffered from lack of meaningful content season after season and has not improved as time goes on. A large portion of the game was vaulted to make room for new content and keep expanding the universe, but more content was removed than added leaving a feeling of emptiness. From a lack of new pvp maps/pve strikes/6 man activities, questionable balance changes, and failure to create new unique weapons to chase after the game has just grown stale. One of the biggest things I'm annoyed about is that they vaulted a majority of the raids some of which were the most enjoyable activities in the whole game to run with friends. I hope to one day see this game get the love and care it got during the forsaken days. I know back then they had the help of other studios owned by Activision, so its very unlikely we'll ever get that amount of content ever again in a year, but maybe with Bungie being acquired by Sony recently there's a slight chance we get new thrilling and breathtaking experiences to enjoy. I'll definitely come back to this game in the future but right now both the PvP and PvE are just not enjoyable for me.\t\t\t\t\t
