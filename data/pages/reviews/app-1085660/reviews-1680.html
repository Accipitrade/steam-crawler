\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor some quick groundwork, Destiny 2 is not a true free to play game. The content that is offered to F2P players is closer to a demo or tutorial than a full game. That being said, by purchasing the slew of expansions available, players will have access to a unique, large and engaging MMO/FPS. The expansions and seasons are all pricey however, even if you take advantage of bundles and sales as they become available. Any prospective player should come in knowing that Destiny 2 is a game that will push you at every turn to invest time into it. Just about every system is designed to keep you logging on every week, and it is a serious commitment to stay "caught up" on all the various progression systems (light level, season pass, triumphs, seals and gilds, and a lot more). The grind is real, and its not always player friendly. Overall, Destiny 2 offers a ton of unique and enjoyable experiences, especially with friends, but right now the suite of content is not strong or wide enough to justify the investment of so much time and money.\t\t\t\t\t
