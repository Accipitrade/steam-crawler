\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI thought after all my hours i should say something more than "fabulous" for my review but if you are looking into getting into this game just know it is a bit of time Time investment (Just look at my hours or rather everyone who plays this game) While many will take it as a shoot stuff, and shoot stuff again, it is more than that, this game when coming to endgame and the such is much more of a social game, raids require communication, patience and teamwork, so do dungeons, this game can be played solo, although i wouldn't recommend it. Story is beyond, truly masterpiece with billions of bits that enlighten you into the living universe that is destiny, if you come and are here to stay...just know you are in for a bumpy ride, the game has been living for a while now, much longer than the average lifespan of a looter shooter. While you may beat borderlands in a week, you will not triumph so easy here. (BTW if you are looking for a well constructed review, pursue another besides mine, this review comes from a bias and absolute fanboy, be warned) The setting makes this game all the more interesting, being it the only game i can seem to find to fit that weird niche between Sci-Fi and Fantasty, it has everything. from Daunting wizards of dark magic, to technological magic, to bounty hunting space yee haw, all the way back to Cyberpunk style Cybernetic augmentation. If you value story and experience, this game is for you. If you desire a challenge to fight against with your friends this game is for you. This game is for everyone i'd say but to each their own. Many players that end up leaving is because the Commonly toxic side of the community, where we can observe elitist class of players that will backseat game a whole fireteam into a raid, micromanaging every loadout...only to fail because the general morale is lowered. meanwhile i see teams that go in with not a care on their mind, only wishing to complete the challenge together with whatever fire power they so choose to use as individuals, these teams, they are the ones that prevail, remember always be wholesome. You're a magic slinging space wizard cowboy, everyone else should feel just as magical together (:P) A million lights disperse any darkness guardian.
