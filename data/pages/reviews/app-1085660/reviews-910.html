\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy original review was too hyperbolic and it was the product of burn out and a negative head space. There's still a lot of problems and it's hard to recommend the game for a lot reasons. I want to take this review to list the things the game needs for me to get to recommending it. Bungie has already done good with next year adding ingame LFG and a system that recommends what to do to new players.

Bring back sunset weapons
Add some way to get old armors from past events, iron banner, and raids.
Remove or heavily rework how power levels work, are gained, and effect gameplay.
Fashion loadouts.
Favoriting shaders and ornaments so they appear first.
Character customization without having to make a whole new character.
Sell past season cosmetics in the store archive or using Halo Infinite's season pass system.
Bring back more past season activities as strikes
Bring back removed Crucible and Gambit maps.
Make new game modes for Gambit based on Crucible gamemodes (mayhem Gambit).
Let us use umbral engrams to get a new roll on any weapon we've previously obtained.
Add a separate game like COD has for Zombies, but it's for vaulted content.
Remove power levels mattering in any pvp, only skill should matter.
Customization for where our weapons sit on the screen like Halo Infinite does.
Customization for ui and team colors for colorblind support and personal taste.
Customization for crosshairs beyond colors.
More subtle titan class items and warlock robes that are shorter.
A season about getting Rasputin back up and running.
More ornaments for exotic armors that really need it (ashen wake).
Buff really useless exotic armors.
Give heavy grenade launchers just a little more damage and some more good perks.
Give exotics that should have catalysts their catalysts (bastion).
Matchmaking that doesn't intentionally pair bad teams against good teams.
Bring back bounties giving rank.
Reduce how much enemies teleport, especially for overload champions.
Make anti champion effects activate quicker.\t\t\t\t\t
