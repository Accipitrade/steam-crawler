\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs of today, December 7th, 2019, i've put 310 hours into this game since rediscovering it 4 weeks ago. I first played it on release, but lost interest. After stumbling upon the roadmap for Season of Dawn, i decided to venture back in. Now i regret not having continued earlier! For the longest time, Counterstrike and Overwatch were my favorite games thus far, the former of which i had been playing since i was 19 years old. I have put in 2,400+ hours into CSGO in 4 years but at this rate, i'll surpass that with Destiny in less than a year.

What i love about this game is that it combines everything i love about each genre of games that i enjoy into one experience. Riveting story mode? Check. Competitive PVP? Check. Completing challenging missions with fellow players for those who don't enjoy PVP? Check. A non-toxic environment with people who are genuinely nice and just want to help each other? Check. Gorgeous graphics? Check. Superb gunplay and mechanics? Check. Actually earning rewards and loot instead of just being able to lazily buy them through microtransactions? Check.

Everything is earned. Nothing is given. Sure you get RNG drops of exotics and other items but those are "earned" too. Unlike other games where those with money can flex, exotic and legendary weapons in D2 are a more meaningful because you had to put in the work to acquire them.

By far the most immersive and rewarding experience i've ever had in a game, and the devs plan on supporting this game until at least 2022, so there is plenty of life left until Destiny 3 is released.\t\t\t\t\t
