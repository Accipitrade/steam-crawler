\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game doesn't receive enough credit from the overall product perspective. Most of the complaints on this game come from one section of the game or another, and that there isn't enough "content" when really that term is loosely used in lieu of the word "cutscenes". This game has plenty of content, ESPECIALLY if you are just picking it up, which now that it is free there is no excuse not to. It's a must try for Sci-Fi fans.

This game encapsulates a well rounded approach to a diverse story, engaging PVE, competitive PVP, a double digit amount of end game activities to pursue, plus a metric F*** ton of lore and content to read and consume to immerse yourself into the universe. Does it have it's issues? Of course, all games do. But the Destiny franchise is one of very few games that attracts players from most genres of gaming and brings a strong community management presence that adapt to the feedback thrown at them as well as keep the ENTIRE community going with occasional projects and puzzles to give everyone a buy in. It is in no way the perfect game but deserves way more credit than it currently receives. It's at least worth a few hours just to see what its about and appreciate the creativity that went into it.\t\t\t\t\t
