\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood free shooter (or $40 if you get the dlc on sale). Bungie tightened up the game quite a bit since it's launch for sure (had it on PS4). Good graphics, good music and runs @ ~120 fps on a 1070.

Strikes, which are kinda like dungeons in WOW are pretty fun the first couple times you play them--wish there were more. Gambit (PvEvP) is different,, intense and really fun. Crucible (PvP) is OK...a bit unbalanced with some gun types having a much longer time to kill than others...and of course the Gaurdian Special ability is super OP in PvP like Black Ops 4. Theres a Iron Banner or something Domination type PvP that pops up every so often which is a blast because the loot is so good even the care bears play which is a nice break from the sweaty vanilla Crucible.

I play Destiny with a SCUF controller and it plays really well although the ability to tweak controller settings a bit more would be nice (take a look at Battlefield, Bungie). I'd like to adjust my ADS sensitivity and crank up the general controller a bit more (10 is slightly slow). Controller doesn't seem to put me at a disadvantage to M&K in PvP because the controller aim assist is really, really strong...one of the strongest aim assists I've seen.\t\t\t\t\t
