\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBungie these days might be the greatest studio on the planet if you're judging by the metrics of being bloated, meandering and impotent. Release after release since the renaissance that was Forsaken the state of the game has by and large suffered and any hope for improvement after escaping the clutches of Activision has dried up - if anything, it seems Activision was a positive influence and forced them to meet some kind of standard. In hindsight I can't help but call into question how much work Bungie really did on Forsaken considering High Moon Studios has their name plastered over a not insignificant portion of its concept work; it's inconceivable to think that the studio which made that expansion is churning out the pathetic excuse for content the game considers good enough now.

At any rate the Bungie apologists who insist on waiting and seeing when everyone else can read the writing on the wall have now gotten exactly what they wanted. The franchise might be at its lowest point since D2Y1 and Luke Smith is an endemic plague for trying to get it out of a hole Bungie created for itself and refuses to stop sustaining by for once committing to listening to and acting on the feedback of the playerbase it pretends to value. Enjoy a game stripped of its content wholesale - including content a great deal of people paid full price for several times over - and make no mistake about its status as F2P. Destiny 2 was and is a paid to play game with F2P monetary practices plus practically a sub fee layered on top combined with two expansions which have zero gameplay value since the majority of gear earned in them can no longer be meaningfully carried into the game's future, which I can only imagine at this point will continue to be impressively worse.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDue to the new expansion, I'm forced not to recommend this game and give it the much-despised title of a pay to win game. I will explain how and why.

In Destiny 2, there is a PVP mode. This mode, if you want much of the exotic weapons and armor, is unavoidable. That in itself is bad but not a deal-breaker. The deal-breaker came in a fantastic one-two combo for me.

The first is a gun called 'Felwinter's lie'; this gun defined the meta for months and is still considered best in the slot. The issue? You can't get it. It was only around to be grabbed for about a month. Suppose you were not playing in that month. It's gone, and you are now at a permanent disadvantage in PVP. Remember, you CAN'T avoid PVP. There is no way to get this gun to this date, even though they added a method to get older quest weapons.

The second issue, which makes this game a pay to win, is the new expansion. Let's say that you are not willing to drop 40$ on what amounts to reskinned enemies and one new area? Well then, I hope your ready to have a terrible time in PVP. There is a new set of powers that only people with the expansion gain. The best example of this is to compare the hunter: Nightstalker's area denial super, with whatever the new one is for the hunter.

The night stalker gets to fire an arrow that will then place a trap where it lands. It will then leash enemies and make the day pretty bad. They take extra damage from gunshots, lose their powers, and try to make them look at the trap. This is countered by shooting the trap, thus stopping the hunter from closing a massive area.

The expansion power allows you to drop a massive tornado in an area that appears to seek enemies. Enemies in this massive zone will take damage over time, and then after a bit, will freeze in place. Unable to move, cast any moves, or shoot. The hilarious bit is that to break out of this freeze will also damage you. The cherry on top is that you can't 'shoot' the tornado; it's just there. HOPE YOUR NOT PLAYING CAPTURE POINT.

I would say avoid this game until this is fixed, but instead, I'm just going to say avoid this game. It can be fun, but it's clear Bungie doesn't care about its user base anymore. All the above is the tip of the ice burg in awful stuff the devs have forced on the users.

EDIT: Its been over a year. I'm still mad about this game. Me and my friends will some times break into just ♥♥♥♥ talking it again.

On refection I don't think any game has personally wronged me like destiny has. Which is kinda impressive.\t\t\t\t\t
