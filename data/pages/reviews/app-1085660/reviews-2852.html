\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRighto, so I just hit 1,000 hours in this game. I am going to rewrite this review because I now have a lot more experience than I previously did.


Ill cut to the chase; do I recommend this game? Yep I definitely do.

Its a game that I absolutely love to its core. There is a lot of content to go explore and complete, even if it feels like that there is nothing else to do. I have progressed decently far into the game, so I feel like I am a pretty appropriate person to write a review.

If you are a new player, the New Light experience is..interesting. It will bombard you with quests and objectives that you need to do. My one tip is just to complete them one by one. It will give you some more quests, and then just focus on them one by one. You can do the Red War, Curse of Osiris and the Warmind Campaigns by going to Amanda Holiday and picking up the quests there. These will also power up your character as leveling up at the start is done by completing really anything, and the quests at the end will give you some Exotic Quests to complete.

If you appreciate the story and lore behind particular places and characters, then this game is full of it! Most of the destinations will give you lore books that contain stories and lore about characters, destinations and even some of the weapons have lore as well. This is a game that also has some great looking locations. Some of the ones I can think of are the Last Wish Raid, the Garden of Salvation Raid and the Dreaming City, but there are so many more that you can explore. Some of these places are basically eye candy!

There is something that I need to mention. PVP. PVP can either be a hit or miss experience. You can play modes like the Competitive Mode or even Trials of Osiris and find people who are absolute sweats at the game and just walk all over you like it was nothing. But if you play with people who are really relaxed and nice (like my clan, shout out to The Wackers!) then you can actually enjoy these matches and even come on top!

Which brings me to another point; the social aspect of the game. This game is very social and almost oriented around playing with other people. If you're a bit of a nervous wreck when it comes to socialising, then you can sort of get away with playing only some of the content solo, but it really encourages you to be social and find a clan and play with them, which I cannot recommend enough. Getting to know other people that play the game is not only fun, but it allows you to expand on the harder content that requires more than 1 person to complete, such as the Nightfall and Raids. Raids are 6 Player activities that focus on more mechanically difficult tasks that in the end either lead you to kill a difficult boss, or even finish a puzzle.

My last topic that I am going to cover is the classes. There are 3 to choose from, which each have different abilities and other factors that make each class unique from each other. There is the Titan, which is typically the more easiest class to play because of how simple it is to be rewarded with using your abilities. As a Titan you can throw hammers, run and slam into things like Hulk or even through a purple mystical shield like Captain America, and there is so much more. Hunters are more mobile and have a lot more sneaky abilities, such as smoke bombs that make you invisible. You can wield a high power, fire blasting gun, shoot a bow that immobilises enemies and wield an electric staff and smack people with that. Then there is the last, but definitely not least, class; the Warlock (which I personally main). The Warlock is basically the Space Wizard of the three classes. You can use abilities to heal yourself and make you stronger. You can fly around the skies swinging Flaming Swords at your enemies, turn into Emperor Palpatine and zap everyone until theyre not on your screen, and launch a mystical bomb that does a monstrous amount of damage.

All that I have covered is really just the surface of this game. There is so much more content to explore and so many different weapons to try out. If you're ever stuck, YouTube is your best friend. Honestly, its a blast and you should 100% give this game your all!

8/10 for Gameplay.
9/10 for Visuals
8/10 for its creativity.

Thank you so much for reading, and I hope you give Destiny 2 a shot!\t\t\t\t\t
