\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLots of content for new players and returning players. There is no hand holding here on what to do. There are guides, videos, tips & tricks all out there on the internet. If looking up information about a game and learning how to play it does not appeal to you, do the community a favor, and dont play. The lack of knowledge in this game is blamed on the devs but its ultimately the ignorant players who whine about the game. This game is a lot like going into a dark room. Until you actively look for the light switch youre going to ♥♥♥♥♥ and whine about how bad it is. But the second you flip that switch and see whats inside that room, all that frustration, its gone.

Now if you can muddle through all that stuff that you have at your fingertips, just realize there are two other DLCs out there waiting to add a plethora of quests, gear, guns, bounties & lore. Shadowkeep is a stand alone expansion that doesnt need Forsaken DLC. However the Forsaken gear and guns are locked behind that paywall. Another paywall is cosmetic items. If you want to spend money then do it. If you dont, then save up Bright Dust and buy items on sale for the week. Silver is a premium currency used to buy cosmetic items off of Eververse. There is no sub to buy and as a free to play player you have the option to buy a battle pass for each of the four seasons in Shadowkeep.

Spend money or dont, the game is fun, exciting and worth exploring in and out of the game. To be blunt if you dont want to look up guides or how-to tips & tricks, then go play some more Call of Duty or Battlefield. Cause if you want an empty baseless and boring set of shooting rules, those "tactical shooters" might be more your snuff. But if you want to join forces with other bad ass MFers and kill things like its early 2000s Halo, then I cant recommend any other shooter.

Jump. Shoot. Loot. Repeat.

Played Destiny 1 Year 1 & 3, Destiny 2 only the first two months and quit. Came back for Year 3 of Destiny 2. Ive been an on and off player since Destiny 1 Beta. Destiny is the World of Warcraft of shooters for me. I quit it just to come back. Sometimes its good and sometimes its bad. But Destiny is a game like World of Warcraft and you need to understand there is a lot of content and a lot to learn. Use the tools and information out there to help you succeed in this amazing game.
