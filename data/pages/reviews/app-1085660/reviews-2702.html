\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're looking at the time logged and wondering why this is a negative review, it isn't really one. I genuinely enjoy myself every time I log in and will continue to do so for some time. The problem is that I cannot in good conscience recommend the game. The new player experience is an absolute mess that tells you nothing about what the game is actually like, and the system of locking content away year after year to make room for the new stuff is confusing even for veterans. The devs are too reliant on FOMO to keep players here, and it conversely creates the feeling of having missed out on too much to get into it if you haven't been here since day 1. This game is a chore to get into, and I wish it wasn't, because it's some of the best core gameplay I've experienced in years. I really WANT to stand here and tell all of you that this is the game you need to play, but I just can't. For those of us who have been here forever, this game is a work of art; if you're coming back from a hiatus, or just starting, it's just too much of a mess to get into.

If you're REALLY determined to find something you like, you just might. Give it a whirl, really. But if you're not determined to figure out the mess that is early game, stay far away.\t\t\t\t\t
