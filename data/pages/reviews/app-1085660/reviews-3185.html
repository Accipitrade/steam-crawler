\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to write a neutral review of the game, but unfortunately, I can only make a positive and negative review here. So rather than give an unjustified negative review to the game, I want to point out that this game is not very new player-friendly. I started this game and literally had no idea what I was doing for a few days, there was no real help in-game that shows you where and what you should be doing, it just tosses you into the game and you kinda just figure it out, now to be honest, I'm not sure if that was more appealing to me or a turn off to me that I had actually to play the game and figure out how things worked.

I will say now that I have figured the majority of the game out in 50 hours of gameplay I'm rather enjoying the game. The gunplay is amazing in the PvE side of the game, the PVP side I didn't really like all that much seemed to be some cheaters rampant in that part of the game making it rather unenjoyable, that or the balance of classes is just bad, either way, they need to work more on that.

Long story short, if you're player PVE this is a fantastic game with some learning curves; while I understand it can be frustrating at the start, the game ends up being great in the end. When you shoot those enemies down, you can feel the power of the character as you play, and it feels great!

Another thing I didn't like was purchasing the DLC in different bundles; I don't understand why they didn't include Forsaken in the Shadowkeep digital deluxe version of the game? It seemed like a cash grab, and a kind of shady thing do to do on their end to their players, but that's a personal opinion.

Over-all I do recommend this game even if you play the free to play version at the start; it's worth your time and money at the end of the day, spite some of the less positive things in that game.\t\t\t\t\t
