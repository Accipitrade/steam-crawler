\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really like this game. I have about a little over 2k hours on it and I'm still playing it. This game is purposely repetitive and grindy, so if you hate that type of stuff then this game just isn't for you. However, bungie has implemented a really good combat system. Guns, feel nice to shoot, movement has a lot of verticality to it and even has tid-bits added into it that lets you move even faster (titans and warlocks), enemies are varied and a lot of them actually requires some part of your brain to kill more efficiently (for example cabal phalanxes have a crit spot in the middle of their shield, if you shoot it the shield goes down). Raids are really good (each one of them has a good mix of puzzles and combat), subclasses are also a strong point each one of them serves a different purpose and can drastically change the way you play your character, and of course the loot grind. There’s a lot of BS in it, (pointing at you elemental affinity) but overall it’s good it can change the way you handle your gun, and help you min max your armor stats. However, this is PVE, which is in my opinion in a really good state (well, besides story, but at this point I don’t even care about it).

PVP… ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ is PVP annoying. Don’t get me wrong. I like PVP. I went all the way to legend this season (not like that’s much of an accomplishment these days), but good god is it annoying. Artifact mods that allow you to cheese your way to victory, scavenger mods that gives you enough ammo after like 2 bricks of special to kill the entire enemy team and then some. Heavy… seriously Bungie? We complained about heavy last season, because it gave the user too much ammo (there were a few more but I’d imagine this was the biggest one) and allowed them to ground stomp the enemy team, and essentially forced everyone to play for it. So what’s your solution? Let’s ALLOW THE ENTIRE ENEMY TEAM TO GET HEAVY.. how does this at all fix our complaint? mountaintop that takes all the challenges away from using a normal grenade launcher and at the same time improves upon it by firing in a straight line and exploding on impact. Supers… ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ I hate them. IDK how many times I die to a super and then spawn only to get killed by the same one. I wouldn’t mind supers if it only happened once in a game. So, that way they don’t turn an otherwise good match to basically slower mayhem. Despite all these annoyances, I actually do like PVP in this game. There’s been a lot of moments where I would outplay entire teams, and the satisfaction you get from achieving that is pretty incredible, and being able to show that off (in the form of gold medals) is also really cool. IMO PVP is a good idea and should be in this game. But the best parts of a match is the beginning, where it’s mostly primaries battling each other out. It’s only until the first supers does it really shows it’s flaws.
