\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is bad.
I have 1500 hours in this game. I have done just about everything there is to do in it. I have got gud.
This game is bad. People both love and passionately hate the game because it so good in so many ways and so deeply flawed in so many others. It is visually and viscerally appealing, the world it imagines is mesmerizing. It's easy to fall into the trap of spending hundreds of hours learning just how flawed it really is. So, I suppose In terms of time played for money spent it's easily worth getting. But....
It's fun until it's not. Eventually, you realize it's hollow. The quest rewards are meaningless, the power levels are meaningless. The story goes nowhere. The characters do nothing, have no arcs, no lives beyond their role as the givers of the meaningless quests. The players have no agency because the world never changes. The exotic weapons and armor you'll spend days, or weeks, tracking down won't change the gameplay from the pistols and submachine guns you'll pick-up in the tutorial. There are no decisions, no choices, no strategies, no tactics. Nothing but shoot, over, and over, and over again.
There are zero in game tools to support social play, literally zero. Despite the fact that this is a social game. CONSEQUENTLY, MOST OF THE BEST CONTENT IS COMPLETELY INACCESSIBLE TO ALL CASUAL PLAYERS.
The largest dungeons to explore, and most interesting activities to do require teams that cannot be formed with the tools that exist within the game. This insanity is a core design choice by the developers. They have repeatedly reaffirmed it year, after year, after year.
The competitive multiplayer modes are terrible. Players completely ignore the objectives. Cheating is rampant. The community behaves terribly. Griefing is rampant. The game does nothing to limit the worst behavior, or even to inform players that their behavior is bad. I honestly still don't know if players consistently ignore the PVP objectives because they don't understand how scoring works or because they just don't care. What I have seen in the PVP in this game leads me to doubt if humanity is worthy of Grace.
The awful PVP is unavoidable. Many, many quests will steer you back to PVP whether you can tolerate it or not.\t\t\t\t\t
