\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is one of those games that is going to be around, it's a game with unique story lines, stunning visuals, and the cinema is executed nicely. From release to now, it has came a long way.

Let's start with the good:
*Customization, from creating your guardian, and different shaders, sparrows and ships, you are unique.
*Game play, movement, combat, and abilities are sure to keep you around.
*Activities, there are a lot to enjoy the game, PvE, PvE/P, PvP, there's something for everyone.
*The amount of weapons and armor in this game, is phenomenal.
*Visuals, the game looks AMAZING, simply put. Every planet is beautiful, and plenty of sights to see.
*Great game with friends, raids, lost sectors, dungeons, strikes, take a friend or a few, and go explore the worlds!
*The right amount of grind that doesn't get boring.

The game hasn't always been the polished and definitely had a lot of drawbacks but let's be honest, Bungie improved it and we admire that Bungie has listened to the community granted it took a while for them to listen to us, but they did.

Now let's start with the cons:
*Balancing, mostly in PvP
*The random matchmaking, sometimes you get a good team, a lot of times you get the person who expects you to do everything, or just wanders off and does their own thing
*The main hub is confusing for new players, it's a labyrinth.
*The lack of use for currency

Overall, it's worth the investment, plenty of activities in game to keep you busy and a lot to collect in game, Destiny 2 is going to be around for a good while, and with constant events going, different seasonal items to collect from weapons, armors, sparrows, ships, shaders, and ornaments. Characters to love, hate, enemies to punch, shoot, blow up, slice and dice, and amazing cutscenes to watch. My opinion, ignore the bad reviews and experience it yourself!
