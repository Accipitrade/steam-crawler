\r\n\t\t\t\t\tthe dislikes:
if u do not have actual real life friends who play the game with you, and you wish to join a fire-team, you will need to look for vacancies on bungie.net. also you will need to figure out the voice and or chat system.
Many times upon logging in, you will be forced to auto join an instance. Usually it is not too difficult but every now and then someone at bungie decides to upgrade the difficulty without choice. Honestly I enjoy just doing quests and dungeons and missions. I do not enjoy being forced into a LEGENDARY LEVEL when I have been enjoying normal for years. Dying every few seconds cannot be called enjoyable.

but the good:
as mentioned above solo play is accessible and enjoyable to a point. there are no mandatory quests or missions to complete in order to proceed to more content. for the most part, the main story-line is easily doable while solo.
the game can be played on different formats - i am not certain if all formats can be played together or not. I play on my desktop pc.
I do not belong to a fire-team. Many of the player options can be run solo or auto join a group if needed or required. You can create a max of 3 characters and they all share same bank, credits (money), etc. You get access to a vault where you can store extra items that are found to use on your alternate characters.\t\t\t\t\t
