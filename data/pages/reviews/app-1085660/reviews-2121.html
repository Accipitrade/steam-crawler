\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is an enjoyable game. The game is set in a sifi world within the milky way galaxy. Destiny 2 has many activities especially for new players. There are 3 character classes Hunter, Titan and Warlock each has it's own unique elemental abilities. Weapons and armor come with random perks to make them feel fresh. Special weapons and armor called exotics have their own perks special to them that makes them stand out amongst the rest. There are many modes to play like the pvp called the crucible there are also strikes which are match made three player missions. There are raids which are 6 player non match made encounter based activities that can supply some of the best loot in the game. There is a pve pvp mix mode called gambit and two dungeons but that just scratches the surface. Some of this content requires expansions but they offer a lot to free to play players. At the moment there is a problem with error codes and people being kicked out of activities.

All in all the game is fun to play it is much more fun to play with friends but solo players can still enjoy the game so I recommend people try it out as it is free to play. The game gets lots of updates and has rotating activities to keep the game feeling fresh. I'm Albert Einstein and I approve this message.
