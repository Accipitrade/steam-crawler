\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt has its ups and Downs, yes you need the DLC's to really do anything, so that "Free To Play" Title is lie. The game is more micro transactions than anything else. Can you have fun in the game, yes, the games worthy of recommendation as it has some of the best raids and mechanics, to which no other game to my knowledge has done or come close to re-creating. However if your looking to make friends in this game to do any of the higher leveled content forget about it as the community surrounding the game is mostly toxic, with some exceptions. Help will always be there in the form of players with vast experience and knowledge to help you in the game, but the majority of then have little to no patience in teaching or waiting for new players to experience the thrills and plot twists they themselves once had. All-in-All the game can be fun, but only if you already have friends you personally know who already play and if your alright with spending your hard earned cash on now overly priced expansions, silver, and season passes.\t\t\t\t\t
