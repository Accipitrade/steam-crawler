\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEasily one of, if not the best open world looter shooters out there with enough content to keep you busy for months. The game is very well optimized, has a beautiful soundtrack and comes with polished mechanics. Forsaken was the best expansion for me, Shadowkeep was a bit lackluster but it was still content.

Never really got into Gambit/PVP or Crucible as a casual player and unfortunately never got to experience the joy of completing a raid on day one. Each activity is unique in it's own right and the game is definitely rewarding and fun. Destiny 2, for me was a replacement for Warframe as it suffered content drought but being the same genre - both games felt completely different and I never really got into Destiny as much. Destiny's lore is top notch though. The game just wasn't my cup of tea, so it was fun while it lasted.\t\t\t\t\t
