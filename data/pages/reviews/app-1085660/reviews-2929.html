\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not gonna tell you it's the best game ever created, but it has a few things in it's sleeve to keep you hooked for a long time.
First off, Bungie knows how to handle the feeling of a first person shooter and it certainly proves that here. Everything feels crisp, controlled, well polished. It doesn't feel "clinical", mathemaical or sterile. Things sways a way that makes everything feel somewhat believable but very clean and responsive.
Secondly, the designs are amazing. The environment, the music, sound design the various creatures. It's well realised and original.
The downside is the storyline which, is rather boring and the amount of grinding for certain quests.
If you are afraid of joining and feeling underpowered, don't be. The PVP is mostly level balanced and it's very easy to get good equipment. You'll just need to sharpen your skills.

Overall, this game has tons of content, it's rather innexpensive, beautiful, sounds right and is just great to play. At least make the jump to try it.\t\t\t\t\t
