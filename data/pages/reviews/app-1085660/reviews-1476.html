\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is not a *bad* game, in fact the shooting and movement are it's strengths and clearly carry over from Bungie's days with Halo. But it has become a very much a grindfest and a bounty simulator. You go to the Tower (the alleged social space) where you can't actually *be* social and interact, run around the entire place to find every vendor and grab bounties to do, leave and then complete them. There are no story or quest missions (repeatable or otherwise) to get unless you have the *1* seasonal quest you are working on.
But at this point, just grinding for weapons and armor every season (90 days) that disappear and are now temporary makes working to get that "best" roll for you feel rather pointless. Then of course there is Masterworking weapons and armor which is expensive but now pointless for anything since gear now has a short expiration date.
For me I've put my hours in on XB1 and here on Steam.
But if you don't care what you use or any of that and just want to kill things with guns and space magic, maybe this game is for you. For me, I'm finishing mh time in this season and spending it on other games later as its not worth it spending money or time after that.
