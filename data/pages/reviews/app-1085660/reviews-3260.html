On September 17th 2019 (you bet your ass i looked it up), Destiny 2 went free to play. On the 1st of October, it came to Steam (It's literally right above the review prompt). On that day (or not, I don't really remember when) I installed it, and took a shot at it. The game was nice, with amazing lore, amazing gameplay, just- Overall amazing.

So where does that bring me today, after 130 hours of gameplay (at the time of writing)?

This game is just as, if not better than when I first played it. There's enough content in this game to keep you playing for thousands of hours. I will admit, the start is always a grind. But when you get past that; When you finally reach the soft cap (aka the place where "common" items don't help you level up), when you experience the dozens of hours of campaign, which, sidenote, F2P players have TWO FREE CAMPAIGNS.

Lightfall was disappointing, awful story, strand training montage the campaign, Strand is alright but lead to a great increase in enemy difficulty to deal with Woven Mail and the mobility so now everything else feels not as good without having at least 1 Woven Mail bot.
