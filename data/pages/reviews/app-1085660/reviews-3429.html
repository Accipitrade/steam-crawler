\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am a Shadowkeep era Guardian since I started playing more full-time since I wasn't wanting to install Battle.net back when Bungie was with Activision.

Destiny 2 is an alright game, for me personally an amazing game that I cannot ever get the same experiences in at all. I've made amazing friendships through this game and have encountered a lot of goofy people in this game.

The story and lore is good, great even. That's subjective, never objective. One person will say story sucks while the other says it's good. Don't merit those to the game, people are different in their interpretations of it. The Light and Dark saga has grown and it seems Bungie is back on their original track with D1's original story line before it was forced to be rewritten by the higher ups at Bungie.

Content wise, it depends on who you are. If you're a dedicated PvE player, you're eating good most of the time. PvP player, you've dug a hole, don't complain. Game has gotten hard to balance things out, Bungie is trying, things take time, especially considering that this isn't a PC only game.

Music, no matter what, is a banger. And more so with Lightfall dropping.

Gameplay is what you expect with a looter shooter. You play, you get loot, level it up. The game isn't what many are accusing others of copying. That's more Borderlands and Division type loot. D2 is just perks. If you got the roll you want, you got it. Your perks and armor stats are the biggest thing you'll ever be concerned about. Light Level is an old, archaic system that is slowly fading out but is still part of the Destiny gameplay loop. Grind your LL to powerful then to pinnacle, then grind your artifact (that you get for free at the start of every season, you do not need to pay for the season pass to access the seasonal artifact.)

Three classes, now 5 sub classes, and a bunch of gameplay styles. Don't be afraid to feel like a cheater and look up a YouTube video and see what build gets your eye. FOMO is getting phased out, for the love of god stop listening to detractors of this game. FOMO is one of Bungie's concerns and they're working on phasing parts of the game that causes that. But if you're not playing, you cannot complain. Make time, play at your own pace, don't rush and enjoy the game naturally, not artificially and under an invisible timer.


This game is improving, more so than what it was before Forsaken and during the D1 days. I have thousands of hours in Warframe, used to hate Destiny, Warframe got stale and very extensive content droughts and community drama led me to try Destiny 2 out, been stuck with it since and it has been a freaking blast. This game is fun, like all games should be. I make my own enjoyment and avoid stuff that doesn't bring me joy. Many other gamers should live to this philosophy and mindset. But many choose to still portray Destiny 2 as this incredibly bad and evil game that will take away everything from you and that the story doesn't make any sense and the lore is bad and x,y,z is crap and that Bungie is some evil socialist, commie woke company that wants to spread propaganda or whatever politically brainrot buzzword-fill tomfoolery anyone can conjure up.

If you don't like the game, don't like the game. Genuinely no one cares or will care. If you want to try out the game, there are plenty of discord servers to LFG with other people and have someone to help you out. If you don't like the game, have zero interest in playing it. Just keep your mouth shut, play your games, don't sour other people's gaming experience because you're a miserable garbage person who feels the need to be a constant debbie downer. Chill out and play video games. Get lost in game worlds and have fun.


tl;dr
Destiny 2 is a good game, a very misunderstood and mischaracterized game. Bungie is hard at work to improve this game, and most huge updates and fixes come in yearly expansions. DLCs are good, get all of them during a sale or get the whole bundle. Campaigns are getting better and better. WQ is the best so far, Shadowkeep and Beyond Light struggled behind, but that is because with Shadowkeep Bungie split from Activision and lost their support with two secondary studios, and Beyond Light was with the rona hit and working from home happened.
Gameplay is fun, and engaging. Music is a bop and has many bangers. Characters are great and memorable and I'm glad to see a lot of characters get more screen time and more development and growth. Weapons and armor are top notch and I fashion my guardian up all the time. Got that DRIP.\t\t\t\t\t
