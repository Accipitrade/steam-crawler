Have fun and don't forget to help the odd blueberry in a dungeon or quest! Keep paying the help forward.

This game has been the gateway to meeting many unique players from all around the world and personally, I run in a fireteam which is composed of some people that I really like. They have kept me sane though some really rough times in my life and I owe them for that.\t\t\t\t\t
