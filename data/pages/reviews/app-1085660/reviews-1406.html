\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReview from a Destiny 1 + 2 Player :D

If you're looking for a game that you can play over and over mindlessly, you've chose the right one. There's a lot to do in Destiny 2, and the game keeps expanding. Expansions may be expensive and I've probably spent over Â£200 on the Destiny franchise, however, it's better than most games I've played.

The team aspect of the game is like no other, and completing challenges in raids etc. couldn't be more rewarding (mentally). Sometimes the loot isn't great, but it doesn't kill the feeling of sitting in a raid for hours and finally completing it in those first few days.

I feel this is one game where the devs really care about the community and try their best to make the game as playable and entertaining as possible. However, a lot of the game is carried on the back of YouTube content creators who make raid tutorials etc. I'm sure I wouldn't have played as much if I hadn't found a few videos on "How to complete Vault of Glass" in D1.

The PvP can be annoying sometimes, I played D1 on Xbox and went 19 games flawless on trials with the same problems. The meta always seems to favour certain weapons. Although it's extremely hard for the devs to do, it would be perfect to make a more diverse range of on-meta weapons, instead of just mainly "Hard Light", "Suros Regime" and a couple others. Although I've always mained Hard Light, I remember times when Vigilance Wing could pluck you from thin air within half a second. Most of the time with updates, it seems that the on-meta guns either get nerfed too much, or the off-meta guns get turned into a HUGE DEATH LASER.

Of course there are things to be worked on in Destiny 2, like any other game. But I fully recommend D2, especially if you've got a few friends who play as well. (Be ready to GRIND).

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a beautiful disaster. I'll explain:

THE GOOD
Jawdropping environments, zones, and scenery.

Buttery smooth controls and gameplay.

Excellent optimization.


THE BAD
Everything else.

Overcomplicated mess and terrible for new players. There are so many quests, bounties, materials, vendors, and just horrible nonsense it's nearly impossible to know where to start and where to progress to. And what's the point of it all? Destiny 2 is the type of game you will be opening up your web browser every five minutes to figure out what to do, instead of actually playing the game.

The user experience is horrific. Finding and progressing quests is a nightmare and what little lore is presented in the game, will not be compelling enough for most new players to bother slogging forward.

The entire plot of Destiny 2 can be boiled down to this: Earth got semi-destroyed. We have to keep it safe from a variety of mindless alien hordes that we never really interact with and that have next to zero backstory or context.

I'll play through the story content so I feel like I get my money's worth of this game and then I'm abandoning it for good.

My advice if you're new: just don't bother with Destiny 2.

To Bungee and everyone else creating MMO's: stop wasting time developing unnecessarily complex, unintuitive garbage, and use your time and energy making games interesting and fun. Destiny 2 is a mindless grind that destroys an otherwise beautiful game.\t\t\t\t\t
