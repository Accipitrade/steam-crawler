PVE:
Important Notice: if you're ONLY interested in PVE, than it's a wonderful game. A top quality product, gun play feels solid, animations are top notch, diverse environments, just a lovely experience. You'll sink in many enjoyable hours. Well worth it, up to the last expansion, which is basically a re-skin of all the existing enemy characters. Now, "the witch queen" expansion is coming, which is another reskin of enemies...-_-
There's also the issue of "vaulted" maps (environments) , weapons, basically loads of content that they take out because -insert ridiculous corporate explanation- some excuse that only children can believe.
In reality, it's a cheap tactic to keep the game fresh without ACTUALLY CREATING anything. They just take part of the content out, and then bring it back again another season. I do this with my 5 and 6 year olds. I hide some of their toy boxes and then bring them out again, keeps them excited.
It seems this is how smart bungie think their audience is, the equivalent of 5 year olds.....(the sad thing is, they might be right, but don't tell anyone i said it).

PVP:
Now, onto PVP. if you are like me, and PVP is a must have and the go-to place after you've mastered the pve side, well, this game is highly lacking.

1. First of all, like the title says, ITS FULL OF HACKERS. You won't know whos good and who's hacking, because hackers are in almost every single pvp game. Trias of Osiris should be called The Hacking of Osiris, it's the only place i've seen full hacker teams going against other full hacker teams. Honest.
There is literally NO ANTI CHEAT. Nothing. Zero. Tried and tested, *a friend of a friend* -wink wink- got so fed up one day after being obliterated by hackers in every single game played, that he went online to see how hard it was to hack in this game. Turns out the first link you get on google, is usable, undetectable, you pay for a day or for a month. When *this friend* tried it in-game, he absolutely abused it, he didn't want to seem legit. He just started spraying everyone, simply destroying every map, flying around, making it AS OBVIOUS AS POSSIBLE to see what would happen, if anything.
To this day, nothing happened.
The report system is a gimmick, it's just there to "be there". You can't post hacking videos on their forums to get people banned because Bungie are a sickly leftist progressive company that, like all progressives, defend abuse and sickness, be it p*do*phil*es like joe biden or hackers in their game absolutely abusing their playerbase, Bungie say "It's HARASSEMENT...you can't shame hackers, you might hurt their feelings". So in other words, it's free for all. If you want to hack, you can just do it, nobody is ever getting banned.

2. The current pvp meta is shotgun ape'ing. Basically, for those who dont know, every weapon in this game takes a bit to kill you, not very long, just enough to leave room for strafing, counter-fire and good aiming. Which was why i initially feel in love with pvp in this game. However, a shotgun only takes 1 shot to kill you, doesn't even have to be aimed right, you can aim for his legs, and still get the kill. So what people do, they mindlessly run around into players with a shotgun and 1 shot them, no aim, no exit strategy, just kill or trade. All the tactical play goes out the window. There's also a combo you can do, if you dont kill with 1 shot, you can immediately melee and get the kill since melee takes 50% of HP. (melee again, doesnt not require any aiming). So in other words, the most effective way to kill in Destiny 2 pvp, does not require aiming.

3. One shot Skills. Another one, every class has a skill that can secure a kill with 0 effort, instantly and with no aiming required, just by looking in your opponent's general direction. As you can imagine, these skills exist in every single pvp build and they're used on-cooldown.

4. Hit registration can be pretty bad. Every 1 out of 10 encounters you will have shot's on target that don't register. In the beginning you won't notice cause everything seems chaotic and you're too fired up, but as you play more, you'll notice every shot on target that never registers and that's gonna bother you like hearing nails on a chalk board.\t\t\t\t\t
