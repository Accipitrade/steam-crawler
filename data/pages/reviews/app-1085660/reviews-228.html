\r\n\t\t\t\t\t1471 hours in. I hate the enemies because I have been fighting them for about 8 years now. Actually...I kinda hate this game. But after 1471 hours (on Steam alone), another 1000 or so hours on PS4, and my almost 2000 hours on Destiny 1 on both PS4 and Xbox One, I feel like my opinion is entirely accurate:
I have never had so much fun playing games than when I get to shoot some aliens with Bungie guns and use literal "Space Magic." The story has gotten much much better, some of the guns are fun to shoot. Literally that part on its own is amazing! Boot up the new Halo or the newest Call of Duty and the guns are kinda fun to play with...but Destiny? We have weapons that can shoot black holes, one that we made out of a worm (that talks a little bit too), and even the voice actor for Zavala (the incredible Lance Reddick) has memed about killing GODS and turning their corpses into GUNS. No other developer even approaches the crisp, quality gunplay like a Bungie does.

Especially since the base game is free now, I would absolutely recommend this game to people. I'll even clear up my schedule to play with ya. I think it's worth a download and I disagree with anyone who tells you to avoid it. Give it a shot and worst case scenario, our paths stray Guardian. I hope to see you starside.\t\t\t\t\t
