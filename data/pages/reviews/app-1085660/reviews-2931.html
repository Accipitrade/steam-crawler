\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've owned Destiny 2 since launch, but couldn't really get into it at that time. Came back to it with New Light, and have really been enjoying it, with a few caveats.

The good:
*Armor 2.0 is a good system that lends itself to creating some fun builds.
*The different game modes are fun and entertaining in different ways.
*Exploration is rewarded decently well.
*Shooting and combat feels excellent.
*The game is beautiful on higher settings, and the sound is great.
*As a new (returning) player, there is a *ton* of stuff to do.

The bad:
*The "story" content doesn't seem to scale with more people, and is quite boring if you're not solo, when it becomes a bit challenging at times.
*It gets repetitive pretty quickly.
*Some things are pretty damn grindy. This is either a pro or a con, depending on your thoughts on grinding.\t\t\t\t\t
