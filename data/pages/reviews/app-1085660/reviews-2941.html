\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has a little fun but, lets be honest this game lacks any depth or worthwhile content. Bungie has lost there touch when it comes to developing a well balanced and fun experience when it comes to the multiplayer aspects of the game. They have a large sandbox but, 90% of it is irrelevant as they are completely outclassed by poor weapon design and power creep.

PVP basically, is recluse, LW, snipe or mountain top, with a dash of god rolled spare, luna's to top it off. It gets old very quickly. Nevermind, the lack of dedicated servers will make the game play horribly, as you get sniped by someone who hasn't even appeared on screen. And the constant revolving game design where nothing plays the same ever since D1 and requires you to relearn the "meta" more often then any FPS or MMO has ever made me do and I have played WoW. Overall, If your looking for a well balanced and fun FPS don't play this game.

PVE is on its last legs at all times due to lack of fun and engaging content. Strikes are so dull that everyone who has to do them just runs through them. Bungie shouldn't waste any more resources on this as it just become a relay race to complete them as no one enjoys them. The open world events and patrols are also just a bad and Bungie has used the same events since the first year of D2 and some from D1. The only thing that's fun and engaging is Raids but, they too have been made easier than D1 ever was as new raids have no Hard mode and gear feels pointless due to similar weapons being out in the open world with similar perks. I have deleted about 90% of the gear i have received from these activities. Lastly, Dungeons are cool and are fun again gear is an issue here as there isn't much worth getting in them.

Lastly, this is not a story game as the characters have no meaningful back story and dialog is not great. This game has had multiple writers that have come and gone. This game shows it as the game seems to have no direction where its story wants to go.

Overall, if you are ok with only playing raids and dungeons weekly and do the boring grind to do so then you might be able to get some fun out of this game. Otherwise, skip this game.
