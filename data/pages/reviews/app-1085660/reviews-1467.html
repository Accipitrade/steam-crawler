\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got the free-to-play version as a trial and a lot of the content is locked behind a paywall along with the "good" weapons. General experience was very lackluster. The campaign of Red War was good, but the following campaigns were not too impressive. Crucible (PVP) is a joke in Destiny 2 with grenade launcher spam and many weapons that favor no-skill (tracking projectiles as well as "aim assist" which bends bullets to the player's head or body). Not to mention the massive amount of cheaters in Destiny's PVP that continues to thrive. Strikes are a good way to earn rewards and to test your PVE skills, but they get repetitive over time and the limited inventory space for resources (items which lets you upgrade gear) makes it boring and frustrating respectively. Gambit is a fusion of PVP and PVE which combines the worst of both crucible and strikes but without the rewards. Essentially, you kill mobs to collect motes, and when you deposit a certain amount, you are allowed to invade the enemy's map and kill the other players utilizing those same "no-skill weapons" that either tracks you across the map, or one-shots you across the map. Some might say "Just buy the DLC then" and I did. I bought Shadowkeep and have played it, however the campaign was nowhere as engaging as Red War and its previous campaigns. The weapons included were fine, however the price tag of $30 for one map, one raid and 2 exotics is absurd. I would've gotten the Forsaken DLC, however that content is getting removed along with a lot of other content in the next 2 months which would make it a waste of money. I do appreciate the graphics of the game and the maps as they are gorgeous and I can give praise to the raids that have been developed. The raids are my favorite part of this game, however, you are required to farm other aspects of the game which I dislike to perform well in them. You are also required to play with a team of 6 others to participate in raids which limits the accessibility of it for players.

Overall: Not a good free-to-play game as the "good stuff" is locked behind a paywall. Content takes "no-skill" and gets repetitive over time. Graphics and raids are good with the exception that 6 players are required to participate in them.\t\t\t\t\t
