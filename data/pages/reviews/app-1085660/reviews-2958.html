The PvP can get frustrating if you don't have certain, top of the meta, weapons or abilities. However, the meta changes often enough in my opinion to keep things fresh and draw me into PvP. I also really enjoy Gambit, their PvPvE game mode. This is a very unpopular opinion, but I would say not to get caught jumping on the Hate-Train. Gambit does need some work, but its a fun mode when you learn the maps, invader spawns, and find a role you really enjoy. The Lore is DEEP. You will not understand the story at first because it is told in a semi-non linear way. While a lot of the story is buried in a log on a menu page. Yes you will have to read. Or find a friendly community Youtuber to let you in on the details. Trust me, it's worth a look into. This game is always at the WIP stage and is never really done. But as a Live-Service game, I don't believe it will ever be "Finished". It's worth at least a download and a good hardy try. Who Knows? You might join our Guardian Ranks.\t\t\t\t\t
