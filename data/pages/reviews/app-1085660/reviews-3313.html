I am really glad I got the game and finally played it, and I must say, I have been happy to support it with money by purchasing Forsaken and Shadowkeep as it really is fun - in fact, one of the best in the genre (if not 'the').

I think Bungie will do much better now they have cast off their shackles of oppression and I for one really recommend this title to anyone who might be sitting on the fence - there is so much content in the free variant that I am sure you will end up supporting the product at some point.
