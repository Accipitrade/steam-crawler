Edite I (golden era):
I even don't know what to say...
For a completely new player: give it a try (if you like this setting and you are not a scrooge).

I came into Destiny 2 after Shadowkeep launch and spent lot of time actually having lot of fun and "wow!" moments. Exploring all that was an amazing experience.
And comparing that to BL... It feels like I payed for a real solid DEMO version to test new subclass xD
Sunsetting is one big oof. 96% ? guns is "removed". Power caps. You can't have fun with 'em even in campaign, lost sectors and non end game content. DCV!...
My disappointment is immeasurable.

Edit II (post Witch Queen):
Try it IF: it's on discount and you have nothing else to play. There Shadow Keep and Witch Queen campaigns left. Which gives you not too much in terms whats going on in the game story.
Lightfall? You saw that reviews right?

It might be one of that moments happened before. When the game went downhill but then did a solid comeback. So I'm just waiting for Finale Shape maybe it happened again.\t\t\t\t\t
