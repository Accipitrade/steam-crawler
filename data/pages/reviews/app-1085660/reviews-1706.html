\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLikes: gun-play, story (where it exists), co-op when I care for it.

Dislikes: repetitive grinding, network instability, screen-shake, quest log limits, forced matchmaking on strike playlists.

I have played this franchise since its beta on PS4 and loved it. It has that shooter looter vibe like you get with Borderlands. I am a returning player after taking 2 years off and was convinced by my family to play again when one of my kids showed interest in joining in one of my other games. I'd recommend it for families that like playing video games together. I find it difficult to deal with the screen shake at times (it makes me dizzy), but I deal by using the most stable firearms I can find in the game and taking frequent breaks. I love that there is a quest log, but I find that the 63 quest limit is frustrating as it also limits the bounties. I don't like having to return to the tower just to pick things up. It breaks the immersion. I have noticed that many players like to rush through things now and wish I could turn off matchmaking at times because of it.\t\t\t\t\t
