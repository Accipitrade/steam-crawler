\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSeason 13 is splendid bungie out did a great job delivering story and great activities with crazy enemy density lots of positive changes came from this season and there are many more to come. Sometimes issues and cracks in the system show but no game is perfect Bungie does a weekly blog talking about new things they will bring and issues that are on their radar.

Destiny has always been a favorite of mine since 2014 the game did have its issues but no one can ever top its gameplay.

Gunplay 10/10: The responsiveness and feeling of shooting your weapons is just classic Bungie here Destiny is unmatched.

Movement 10/10: The feeling of moving around with your guardian and their movement abilities feels amazing and are fun to master you really notice it when a warlock times there dashes well and move super quick or if a hunter rolls around you and is on top of you before you notice and Titans... They just fly at mach 10 speeds sometimes.

Abilities 10/10: All the abilities are cool and flashy and feel amazing. The ease of use and being able to time and use abilities right leads to a lower skill floor but a high skill ceiling. You can see and feel the power of your character there's something for everyone here.

Graphics/Effects 10/10: Destiny 2 was always a very nice looking game but its pretty crazy that it stills looks this nice in comparison to new releases and it released in September 2017. The atmosphere of the game and overall space vibe is perfect. Think Star wars and Halo. The level design and Skyboxes are immersive and BREATH TAKING -Keanu Reeves.

Story 10/10: Destiny's Story and lore is magnificent and are super cool to get invested in. Destiny has been following a more seasonal way of telling stories leading to a ever evolving world. When something happens it has physical effects on the world ie: events that happen on a or to a planet or even a characters death. Forsaken was an amazing story Shadowkeep was mediocre and Beyond light was great giving more depth to characters from the past and having clashing opinions between characters. All these DLC stories have raids that have connections to the story and have cutscenes at the end leading to changes in the world or story happening. The story so far is leading to something big and it has me excited.

Music 10/10: Always have music on. The music of Destiny is a crowning achievement.

Raids 10/10: The raids at one point were the saving grace of Destiny and just kept getting better and better grabbing 5 other players and solving difficult and unique mechanics and getting that sick loot is an awesome experience and is something that must be experienced at least once in a lifetime.

Grinding/Loot 10/10: All the loot and the grind for it is always fulfilling grinding out for that one weapon and getting that super good roll is a euphoric feeling using that weapon and its power is super noticeable and you grow an attachment to that weapon and start using it in most content you play. The Exotics are all unique and make super big shifts to your gameplay accentuating the power fantasy changing abilities and unique weapon passives. Getting that sick armor roll is a great feeling and stacking them out with mods further changing your gameplay. Perfecting builds and just destroying everything in your path is an amazing feeling.

Overall 10/10 sometimes its a 9/10 due to issues at times but all the things above will always be great factors.
