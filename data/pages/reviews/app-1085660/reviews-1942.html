The first thing that i must say is you only have 3 slots for characters.
3 classes and only 3 slots...
If you want to make a 4th character you must delete one.
Why make a 4th character? Well, because we are gamers...

When it comes to the "single play + pve" part of the game, i can say it really is AMAZING. You can see the hard work this guys put to graphics, music, photography and the mobs A.I. Its really fun to play by all means.
Beautifull scenes, great music and atmosphere. They really got me there.

But when it comes to multi and pvp (except from Gambit, in my opinion always) this is where the game lacks.

Multi: There are NO players. If you want to progress more and do some dungeons or raids and stuff from the latest expantion, unfortunatelly you are alone. Of course you can always post in forums that you need a group, but this is not a solution. A great solution would be a Groupfinder that works in the backround while you do something else, like in WOW. But not...

Pvp: I was never a great pvp player or fun of pvp in any game so i don't have a lot to say here expect one very important think. There are 3 classes in this game and only one of them is suited for pvp, the hunter. Not that you can not get kills with the other classes, but with hunter you do 1 shoot - 1 kill. Which is amazing but leaves the other classes with no actuall hope against a hunter. The warlock (which i loved most) and the warrior are not pvp classes, but you should be able tweak them somehow in order to able to ENJOY pvp with them too.

I bought Destiny 2 for 60 euros and i gave 25 in Steam a few weeks ago for the rest of the expansions plus a season pass. 85 in total for a game that now is free to play ( i feel betrayed, bungie should give something in return for the trust and 60 euros i paid on release), has no future in dungeon or raids and the only thing that it has to offer after you finish the whole destiny story is... Cosmetics...\t\t\t\t\t

Season of the bounty
This game started out as over-hyped as most games do, they then released two DLCs (warmind and osiris). I thought they were fun, but the community thought they were bounty simulators which I hate to agree with. They gradually made improvements to the game. Then Forsaken an extremely fun expansion with many additions to the world and lore of destiny, it was the most well received expansion since Taken King on Destiny 1. Cool raid and raid lairs, but fairly buggy.
Then the content bomb that was just reskinned D1 and content from Y1 and 2 Destiny 2. The exotic quests were pretty cool with the lore and such and rewarded a unique gun; xenophage, divinity etc. But the seasons and seasonal content has become so dry and broken that players are frustrated. Look at the lie quest. At every part of the quest it was either tedious (500 shotgun kills) or just plain broken. THE DOOR it wasn't even a surprise what the reward was. Players including myself were bored, I mean yeah the guns good really good, the only thing in crucible. So the quest is broken, boring, and the gun that is rewarded is overpowered.
The dorito ships
Every week come on reset excited to see the new developments happening in the world of destiny and... oh a 4 second cut-scene showing the same pyramid ship moving across Saturn. For beings that have the same power as the traveller they have ships that take forever to arrive to wherever, probably Enceladus the moon of Saturn. Then confirmation that it is because their PR team leaked a video of Eris on the moon interacting with a Force field of some kind. Thanks for spoiling next season yet again.

BTW the New light player experience is so bad. Millions of quests for the player to do, they plague the map of every planet drawing the attention of the player away from the story. I'm not even gonna mentions the return of trials.

Thats enough rant, I am still playing the game because I've waited for this next season to appear, they have to do something to the bounties, since they got rid of the drops that mattered. Finally they are removing pinnacle, in fairness they are OP.

I recommend you play if you play just first three DLCs, other than that its not worth your time.
