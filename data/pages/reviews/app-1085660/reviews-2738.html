\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Free2Play base game USED TO provide a lot of solid, enjoyable content. With the "Beyond Light" expansion, though, 99% of the base game has been placed in the "Destiny Content Vault" (e.g., removed from the game entirely for an indeterminate amount of time). The new seasonal content that comes every 3 months is likewise 99% paywalled behind a Fortnite-style battle pass.

The worst change made in Beyond Light, though, is the "sunsetting" of equipment. All Legendary-rarity weapons AND armor from the first two years of Destiny 2 are now worthless and can no longer be upgraded to a relevant power level, while newer gear from year 3 and continuing now have a ticking expiration counter on them (metaphorically speaking), which will render them likewise useless once the player power cap exceeds the caps arbitrarily placed on "old" gear. This change makes gearing up, which is a long, slow grind, as in any Live Service game, feel like a complete waste of time, as putting in the effort to masterwork a complete set of armor or grind up a weapon with the perfect set of random perks on it is no longer something you can keep and enjoy into the future, but something that will be taken away in roughly 6 months.

Even though the gunplay, character abilities, and exotic loot (which doesn't expire, thankfully) are all still fun, Bungie completely ruined the experience for everyone except Twitch-streaming career Destiny players.\t\t\t\t\t
