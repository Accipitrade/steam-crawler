\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a free to play game, I'd say it is probably one of the best out there. The combat is fun and easy to follow. Build potential is almost limitless. The grind can be a bit demanding at times, but definitely more tame than other shooter looters out there. PvP isn't too bad either if you exclude Trails. The only thing I could complain about is the very lackluster story from all the campaigns and DLC which is not something you would expect from Bungie.

But overall if you're thinking about playing this game, then download it. It's free and will only cost you space. And if you enjoy the game a lot then consider buying the DLC and Season Passes. Fair warning. This is a game that if you want to get into, you will have to dedicate time to it. Other than that enjoy the ride and stay clear of Trails\t\t\t\t\t
