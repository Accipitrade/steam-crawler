\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros: Solid shooter game play, cool RPG elements and awesome loot.

Cons: Story needs explanation, especially for new players. The legendary and exotic loot drops need to be more frequent. The game is to grindy, it can a slog and a chore. I don't like the in-game store, and there needs to be a way to earn Silver in the game (even if it has to be hard). They need more enemy variations, they're too predictable.

My thoughts: For what it's worth, I like this game a lot. And I want it to be good, but it needs work. When it comes to longevity, this game stops being fun, and that's not a good thing. I realize that Raids and Dungeons are multiplayer, but why can't they be single player too? Or at least let people join them if they're a player short or something. This game needs more random elements. Like loot needs to be more fulfilling and exciting to get. Create randomly generated Dungeons and unexplored areas, so that way it feels like an adventure again. And make the enemies more interesting, at least give them different weapons or abilities, or maybe even rogue NPC Guardians who have left the light or something. And make the quests fun to play, stop making it feel like a chore. Every single time I play a bounty or a quest, I'm not exciting to do it, it's not fun, it's just work.

Something... just for the love of god, SPICE THIS GAME UP! All it needs is a few slight tweaks.\t\t\t\t\t
