\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTHE REALEST REVIEW YOU WILL READ ABOUT THIS GAME:

Let's break it down, starting with the overall game and the experience you can expect.

This game would be defined as an Open World MMORPG. You have 3 Classes; Warlock, Titan, and Hunter. Each of the classes has different abilities. All come with a set of 3 different element-based abilities; Ark Solar, and VOID. Each Element has different branches within them to define what abilities you will be able to use. For example, One branch of Solar for a Warlock allows you to throw a grenade that explodes into smaller grenades. Great for AOE type fighting / Mobbing. This is just one of the many to choose from, and Like I said before. You're going to get different choices based on the class you choose.

The open-world portion of the game allows you to travel to different planets, all with different quest lines, and weapons that tie to other planets and their missions. For example, one quest could have you travel to several different planets depending on the requirements for completion.

Now let's talk RAIDS. There are several, each one different, and each one coming with its own drops. You can gain achievements for completing them as well as exotic weapons.

Speaking of weapons and armor. This game has different levels of armor. Ranging from Common, and making its way up to Exotic. Which you can tell by the name is harder to obtain. You can get sets of different armor, and every piece comes with its own advantages. Though collecting an entire set is more just for looks than anything else. Though gear stats MATTER. So you can't just pick any gear if you plan on getting stronger. Weapons are the same way. Some of the Exotics look cool but are not as effective. Just like any other game you might play.


Moving on to the Multiplayer aspect. You can do anything and everything with a team in this game. It's required for RAIDS, and some other things as well. There are PVE and PVP, for those of you who don't know what those mean. PVE is Player vs Environment, which is just fighting monsters and things around the open world, and then PVP is Player vs Player, which is exactly how it sounds.

There are several PVP arena's, Available through Crucible and then Gambit. In Crucible there are several different modes you can play. Things like Elimination, Control, Mayhem, and more. Then there is Gambit. Which to make it simple is 5v5. Two teams, killing different monsters to collect Motes (Little triangle things), and bank them into a machine. The first team to 100 spawns a "Prime", and if you kill it before the other team hits 100 and kills theirs, you win. Plain and simple.

In the end, I could keep writing this forever. There is so much in this game that I just can't list it all here. I wanted to touch on some of the key elements you might want to consider before buying the game. In my opinion, this game is AMAZING. I LOVE IT. There is nothing I could say bad about this game. So if you're looking for a game that will keep you glued to the edge of your seat. This is it. You can be a casual player or a competitive player. The game has something for everyone. You cant buy your way into power in this game. So everyone you see has EARNED IT. There are too many games where you can buy your way to the top. Yeah not here.

I WOULD RECOMMEND THIS GAME TO ANYONE AND EVERYONE. 10/10 Five Stars AMAZING. Best experience you will ever have.\t\t\t\t\t
