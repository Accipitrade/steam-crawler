\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHmm, do I recommend this game to others... well... i certainly was more invested in Destiny 1, and this game had an abysmal launch. That being said the game has had many improvements since it's initial release. Most of what you would like from their dreaded store can be grinded for, but is ultimately only cosmetic. With the amount of items in game, it's not really a big deal. If you have a group of friends to play with this game is endlessly entertaining. If you're mostly a lone wolf like myself there is still a fair amount of content to be had. The graphics are superb, the interface is good, and the gunplay is unmatched.

PVP is fun but for certain unbalanced. It's worth that sacrifice to have unique weapons and abilities. Random perk rolls on weapons make repeating content worth your time. This is my go to game when I don't feel like thinking much and just grind out strikes listening to podcasts.

If you haven't tried destiny 2, give the free to play a try and see what you think.\t\t\t\t\t
