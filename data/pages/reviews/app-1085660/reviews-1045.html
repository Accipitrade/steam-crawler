\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter pumping over 5,000 hours into Destiny and over 3,000 hours into Destiny 2 on Xbox One, I made the leap to PC gaming. Destiny 2 is my most played game on PC, and my most played game of all time in general. I love FPS games, and the transition to PC was smooth and seamless. The game runs well compared to consoles, the action is fun, the Raids are exciting and the seasonal events are great but they do lack content. The optional Season Passes offer some cool incentives, there are cosmetics you can buy with real money but you are never FORCED to do so. I enjoy the mixture of PVE and PVP but I tend so stick to the PVE side of things. The game, overall, is worth the time and money I have invested over the years. Pushing 300 hours total on Steam so far and I've only been playing here since October 2020. Do I recommend this game? Yes. But it's not up to me if you are going to enjoy it or not. Different strokes for different folks. The base game is F2P with loads of content available. Give it a try. If you enjoy it, I'll see you in Orbit. Eyes up, Guardians.\t\t\t\t\t
