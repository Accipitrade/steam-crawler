\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat pvp content and good story structure. This is a career game similar to the likes of warframe, wow or other online mmorpg's. Seasonal content is fun too. Some negatives are the repetitive nature of the gameplay and the complexity of errand type quests that don't offer much for the amount of time and effort put into getting the rewards. Network issues can adversely affect your pvp experience too. If you've never played an mmorpg and are looking for a good place to start, destiny will more than fulfill that desire. Just be prepared for a lot of griding and a lot of reading to understand the universe that destiny exists in.\t\t\t\t\t
