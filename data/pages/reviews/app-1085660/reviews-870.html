\r\n\t\t\t\t\tY'know, I really liked this game. The gunplay is fun, the bosses are good, the graphics are pretty. The PVP, while meta-heavy, is enjoyable. However please let me preface that I have not played since early 2020.

But I fundamentally dislike Bungie as a company, due to their repeated and deliberate attempts to harm or paywall the experience. And no, I'm not talking about expansions, that is actually a monetization model that I can accept.

I'm talking about:
Arbitrarily disallowing upgrades of equipment obtained during previous seasons (Of which they backtracked).
Vaulting of pre-PC-launch content (Which was done due to console install-size restrictions. Not sure how that has been doing since.)
And most recently, the Battlefront 2 levels of utter hogwash that is their implementation of armor transmog.

This is the precedent, and they will keep doing stuff like this. This behavior was why we wanted them to get away from Activision, but maybe it turns out that Activision weren't the bad guys.

Edit: Meant to state this in the actual review, but Shadowkeep's main quest being effectively a series of "Do some side-quests while I rant about lore to you" is literally what made me quit.\t\t\t\t\t
