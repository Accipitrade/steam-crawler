\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Played this game for a couple of hundred hours on battle.net as well before it moved to Steam.

First of all I really recommend everyone to at least play through to f2p campaign, its really fun and enjoyable.

The game requires a lot of grinding to reach and stay at the top (gear and achievements as well), and that includes doing a lot of the same things every day / week. If you don't like the long grind then it will get boring sooner or later. That being said, if the gameplay speaks to you there is A MASSIVE AMOUNT of things to do and weapons to grind for, especially with the DLCs. Regarding the DLCs, I advise that you play a fair bit of the game before deciding to buy them or not (after finishing the main campaign). DLCs do add a bit more campaign, but I wouldn't advise spending the money planning to ONLY play through the game and then dropping it.

There are a lot of cool activities that require friends to participate in as well, so beware.
