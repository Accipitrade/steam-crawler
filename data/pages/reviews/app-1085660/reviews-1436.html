\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was one of those die hard Destiny fans going all the way back to the D1 Beta on the 360, played the game through most of it's life and most of Rise Of Iron. With D2 I played it when it first came out and it's first two "expansions" (if you can call them that), after I could tell that it was following D1's roadmap almost exactly I hopped off the train. After I heard Bungie finally got away from the Activision overlords I gave it another try and so far I love what I see. The gun play that was always good is still there, they are going to remove a lot of the timegate locations Activision had them put in, and just generally starting to feel more like a game and less like a huge cash grab. Of course we will see what they do when Beyond Light comes out as it is Bungie's first expansion without any Activision influence, but it's been a long time since I've been excited for anything regarding Destiny and I hope the future is good for the franchise and Bungie.\t\t\t\t\t

For example the DLC came out just a few days ago and I was only a few missions in and I had to go to a location and kill some fallen, however, when I arrived while there was a couple of enemies my level the map was pretty small and all of these super high level events and even just random high level enemies were just tossed about and one hit from even the lowest of enemies would instantly kill me. My honest recommendation is as someone who has played countless hours on multiple platforms is just get the game for free and play the free DLCs that come with it I can easily say that will give you 90% of the fun to be had without wasting your money on mediocre DLCs and battle passes that at this point are really only so you can say "Look I have the new moderately ok looking new gear and my light level is a couple digits higher." that other 10% of fun if I dare call it that will cost you more than just your money but your valuable time that you could be spending playing a game that is genuinely well made and loved by the devs for more than just the money it produces.\t\t\t\t\t
