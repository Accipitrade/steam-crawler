\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been playing Destiny 2 since launch and it is a good game for many reasons.

Visually Destiny 2 is top notch, you can't beat it. It's explody pretty, animations are fluid as all get out and just good to look at.

My only gripe with Destiny 2, and I'm sure others, is that even if you pay for the content, seasonal or expansions, eventually that content will be vaulted and removed. What made Destiny 2, well, Destiny 2 was 'The Red War' the original thematic campaign to show WHY Earth/The Last City is in the state that it is. Removing that in favor of New Light was a bad mistake.

Suddenly waking up 500 years later in the Cosmodrone, (for new players) never experiencing the Red War is a major bummer. Granted, New Light is a PHENOMINAL starting campaing as it's directly ripped from Destiny 1, which still holds up today, 7 years later.

Starting fresh, new, I think it would be a major challenge but a good challenge. I'm even considering starting a second account (however that'll work) to grind from scratch, with no bank, no saved up goods, etc, to see how far or what it's like to start over. The game has kept me busy on and off for years and since a good chunk of it's free, I would highly reccomend giving it a shot.

9/10 Only missing a point because of content vaulting.
