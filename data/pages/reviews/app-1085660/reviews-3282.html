\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played Destiny since it came out on Xbox. I like the overall shooter and story pve. I enjoy it even more on pc. The story line is great and they continue to update the game. For me very few glitches, if any at all. I've played many different games on steam and many of them glitch or at times don't work at all. Destiny has always had beautiful artwork and scenes. One of the reasons I loved Skyrim was the vast beautiful scenes. I could look out from a cliff or mountain top and see this vast beautiful country. Destiny at times has different but beautiful places. Not something you look for in a game but I enjoy it. The community is always active and engaging. Whether you are here for pve or pvp or both it's fun and engaging. I love the gun control and the feedback my controller gives me.
Are there cons to the game of course but what game doesn't have them. For me there is not enough to warrant not coming back and going again. Thanks to the Destiny community for a great time each and everyday.\t\t\t\t\t
