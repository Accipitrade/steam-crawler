1: A newish type of FPS RPG that doesn't have a game that's close to it, A unique experience when first playing.
2: A vast loot Pool with ever changing Meta's so nothing is too OP for long.
3: A (Contrary to popular belief) Large community.
4: Cross play on all platforms.
5: A VERY large lore that can be discovered in both normal game play and in depth inside lore Cards ect.
6: A large amount of abilities to satiate your power fantasy.
7: Somewhat vast and changing mod system to allow players to Fit their playstyles.
8: End game content such as raids and dungeons provide players a much larger challenge with mechanics and bosses to separate them from normal gameplay.
9: A built in store where you can buy skins/shaders on a daily basis at no real life cost.
10: A game that if willing to get there has essentially no end, Always something to do.


Cons
1: 90% of content is locked behind Overly expensive DLC to the point where the average player buys them cheap from a third party website.
2: A ever changing meta that almost always required some super insane hard to get weapon and you'll be kicked if you can't keep up.
3: If you don't like repeating things this game aint for you most of its content it duplicate or recycled.
4: A very easy game once you learn it, Nothing in this game will ever actually stay challenging.
5: Lest you want to spend 6 hours a week getting the Mats for Ornaments you can pay you're left kidney.
6: A community so stupid it'll make you wonder why bungie isn't abusing them more.
7: Nerfs and buffs are made not by need to balance but a want to handicap the players. The devs will Nerf anything popular into the floor solely because its popular.
8: A Horribly unbalanced PVP scene where certain weapons Rule for months on end at random.
9: Despite devs spending A year on content and "Vigorously play testing" bugs game breaking or annoying alike are rampant.
10: End game content is based on knowledge not difficulty, once you learn the Mechanics its as easy as pressing W.\t\t\t\t\t
