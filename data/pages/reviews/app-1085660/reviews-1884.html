\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStarted when the game went 'free-to-play'. Not exactly 'free', if you have to buy the expensive expansions that by themselves are not even close to being worth their, full stand alone game, prices.
but that's not why I can't recommend the game. I'm a filthy casual. I enjoy running around, doing the public events and liked vangard missions. PvP and raids were irrelevant to me and I still found plenty of fun.
The season of the undying public event and sudo-raid was my jam, season of dawn was a riot with the sundials massive quantity of rewards by the end of the season.
Season of the worthy, was unworthy.
The seasonal endgame level cap doubled compared to season of dawn. The new events were unrewarding for the effort you HAVE to put into them, and they were quite boring. It was a massive, "Why should I bother playing?" moment.
So I didn't. Decided to wait for the end of the (un)worthy.
Massive waste of $10.
Season passes are robbery. Come at me.
So the new season came around. No idea what it's called. Don't care. Doing the seasons new mandatory mission. LL 965+ in a 750 recommended (utter lie. 940 at least). Finished that unskippable YouTube ad and checked the new public event.
Recommended LL 990.
I'm done. I'm completely useless and it'll take me over a week at least, WITH the seasonal artifact power, to get near that.
Permanently removed this hardcore gamer fetish dream out of my Steam Library.
400+ hours, wasted to be abandoned by the devs for hardcores again.
Yes, I was in a clan. The same clan that I'm in another game, but they're more, 'day 1 max level', kind of players. They're great guys to play with and would undoubtedly help me, but I had no intentions to use them as crutches. By that I mean for basic light leveling and season advancements. Why should I be playing if it was another players effort that powered me up?




There was no point anymore.
I have no purpose to play anymore.
Despite all my salt, There's no point in saying who's at fault.\t\t\t\t\t
