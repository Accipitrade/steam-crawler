First off, I really like this game. Barring the "PeOpLe StIlL pLaY tHaT?" jokes that were so funny 7 years ago, they're the pinnacle of humor now, the series has come a long way. Much of the game is free to play now, It's the World of Warcraft model, in that you play up to a point and then they've got you hooked.
The story's pretty good, for a looter-shooter RPG and the community surrounding it, for the most part, is too. Bungie's usually very receptive to player feedback and they've an active presence within the community. Since their split with Activision, everyone seems to be less stressed out, since it's no longer 24 hour crunch time!
As of this writing, I have played for 31 hours, but cross-save is a thing, so you can play with your characters on all platforms. 31 hours on Steam. I'll be playing this forever. It's like that line in Hotel California: You can check out any time you like, but you can never leave.
They're constantly adding new content, all the microtransactions are cosmetic and have NO bearing on gameplay, it's a good game to just play with friends.
I'd recommend the game. If you don't like it, fine. That's on you. Do what ya like, play what ya like, don't get eaten by llamas.\t\t\t\t\t
