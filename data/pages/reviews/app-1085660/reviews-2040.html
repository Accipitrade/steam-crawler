\r\n\r\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\r\n\t\t\t\t
\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSolid game if you like the looter/shooter formula. Much more of a dense gearing system than you would expect.

The UI is beyond dreadful though and can be really hard to parse through as all icons for guns, gear, quests, quest items, bounties, materials, and more are just squares with images that only sometimes make it obvious what they are. They all appear in the same place so at a glance its nearly impossible to know what you just picked up if you werent expecting it. I have 80 hours logged and I dont always know what ive got. Also rarely can I find the actual relevant information that I want. Want to know what ornaments are available for a certain weapon? Cant until it randomly appears in the store, or you search unofficial wikis. Want to see what quests you have for a particular zone, maybe while looking at the map? Nope, those are separate, and extremely vague. Its a bit of a slog.

Once you do wade through the fog of menus and squares on squares you have a rich game with tight gun-play that is phenomenally deep for solo play, but especially in a group. There is something for all skill levels that will impact your character progression.

The store is another sore spot were clearly the best stuff is locked behind a the paywall. This is easy enough to avoid, but they definitely make it known. You cant pay2win which is nice, but the only way to earn a specific cosmetic item is through real money, which is unfortunate.

All round great game, with dedicated devs that seem to tirelessly try to improve the game. If this game even remotely interests you, then it will delight you.\t\t\t\t\t

Old Review (still true).
Bungie have been removing quality content from the game, because the game is becoming too big (on your hard drive), no really, they said that. Problem is that they are removing more than they are adding back in with every expansion. Plus what they are adding is of noticeably worse quality. Which is fine, they just remove the better content, so you can not compare it as easily. First it was Red War campaign and the first batch of DLCs before Forsaken. Now with the Witch Queen expansion, they have taken out the best content the game had to offer, Forsaken is gone...
Remember when Bungie escaped Activision/Blizzard everyone thought that is for the better, how they painted it to be this breakaway moment, how it'll get only better from there? I do, I was one of them fools who was excited as well. Now, in retrospect, the content they have removed so far, was the best they had on offer, I mean in the long run, I'm not speaking of novelty, something new is always more fun until its not. New content they have been adding gets old very fast.

Finally, that content they had removed, had exotic weapons in it that you could unlock, now that its no longer possible, Bungie just sells you these guns, Isn't that a dream? Do less, sell for more, promise to bring it back in the future, then just recycle and sell it again. Slap on seasons on top, and you got yourself just another game as a service long term money milking scam. Before you say, just don't buy these guns, they are optional. They really are not, some of these exotics are too good, and without having access to them you just gonna have really hard times in PVE and especially PVP.

Destiny 2 is now just another business, Bungie does not care for the game, they only care to keep you playing long enough to be able to promise you something in the future just so you buy another season pass or next expansion. Their promised improvements take too long to come out, and always come with extra features nobody asked for that do more damage than what was fixed. With every such "improvement" Bungie cramming in more things that they can sell you, because you having to buy expansions and season passes is not enough for them. Even their so called "Free to Play" model is a scam (they are scamming you out of your time), you can't do anything that is fun, only very basic content is open for you, it should be renamed to "Free to Look at".

If you are still planing on getting this game for PVP, do not. This game has THE WORST networking setup, its peer to peer hosting, and its the worst of them all. Even Warframe does it better, that is a free to play game, and its PVP mode is an afterthought.
Frequent disconnects due to NAT conflicts between players, fixing it on your end won't matter when you play with other people with "broken" connections. At which point, what is broken? Right, Bungie's setup.

Another thing to keep in mind is that when you get these zoo code error popups (beaver, baboon e.t.c., have fun looking them up btw.), most of them, are not even on your end or are anything that you can do anything about. But, it is never Bungie's fault for not running this game on dedicated servers like any self respecting game that wants to have good and proper PVP modes would.
Too bad you can't completely ignore it either, you have to participate in both PVE and PVP to get the most out of the game. Some quests force you to do both.

Bungie really wants you to engage with their janky PVP, and that is because, of what I said earlier about what Bungie does with content now. They just need to keep you busy until its time for another promise of things to come in their next expansion.

Had I known what this will turn into, I would've not supported this game with my wallet from day one, throwing money at Bungie. Now I only feel like I am part the reason why it turned out so bad.\t\t\t\t\t
