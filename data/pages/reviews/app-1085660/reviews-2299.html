\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI decided to hold off on purchasing the Lightfall expansion due to uneasiness around the massive price for a DLC, even more for the season, and Bungie's recent habit of splitting off things that used to be included in DLC and charging even more money for them (things like Dungeons and seasonal/holiday events). I even gave them a couple of months to hash things out before trying the (very little) new content that is accessible without the massive price tag. I definitely made the right choice.

First of all, the story. I haven't played through the new story because, again, I didn't buy the DLC, but the community seems to unanimously agree that it's the worst in several years.

The new subclass, Strand, seems underwhelming at best. It doesn't help that it was released alongside yet another "Go Slower" update nerfing ability regeneration all around, and the same update wherein they absolutely wrecked mod options.

Speaking of (combat) mods... gross. It seems like half of the Elemental Well mods in the game were just completely removed with no comparable replacement in the new armor charge mod system. The ones that were carried over in some way are shadows of their former selves, requiring far more investment to get worse results than with the previous mods. They said they were removing element requirements for mods and decreasing overall costs, but what they failed to mention is that they were instead locking all mods to specific gear slots and reducing potency by more than half in many cases. I have never seen this level of homogenization this far into a game's life before.

It seems like Bungie in recent years has begun to regret their focus on space magic, which was one of their main selling points, and now they just want to be a normal FPS game. I've played since D1Y1, and this is the biggest step backwards since D2Y1. I'm so glad I saved my money.\t\t\t\t\t
