\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, this game has more maintenance than any game I have ever encountered by a landslide. They won't even tell you it's coming, they just log you out lol...

Its okayish if you just play PVE. The enemy mechanics are the cheapest on any game I've ever seen. They literally just hide and wait for you to walk out in the middle of an area so they can 1 shot you. The Champion system is so broken and utterly useless, it's beyond head scratching. Instead of being able to play what you want, you are forced to use a certain weapon type you may not like at all. Again though, the enemy mechanics are so cheesy and cheap. Enemies hiding behind corners waiting for you to run by and then they one shot melee or blast you off the side. Picking you off with lobbing shots that track you as you move. Just all in all very frustrating. The idea behind Lightfall was that they wanted it to be harder but instead of introducing mechanics to the game to challenge you they just said, here, we will program all the enemies to be cheap and cheesy, and we will also just toss the bullet sponge Champions in on them over and over and force them to play with weapons they don't want! Just lame.

They also just broke GM Nightfalls. Just stupid stuff that you die to like the one this week having to ride a speeder across the platforms and cannot get hit by any of the balls or you are dead and go back to orbit. I could see taking you down to red and forcing you to stop and recover but to be 1 shot by a ball while riding a speeder after you have cleared all their goofy Champions and enemies up to that point is just utter cringe.

It's a shame because they gameplay in itself is great. The seamless interaction between gunplay and abilities and what little build crafting there is, is nice. Definitely a slick shooter as far as those mechanics are concerned.

The PVP is by far the worst experience you can possibly have. Do not play this game if you like PVP. Go find something else. The amount of cheating/lag/server desync/wtf Destiny moments during PVP are just so abundant, you simply cannot enjoy it. And as usual, they force it on you to play to get your Pinnacle gear for the week.

PVE 7.5/10 It turns to being extremely repetitive once you reach what I would say end game is. As in, running Heroic stuff just becomes so boring you want to just do Master difficulty content which then, get ready for the wheel of cheese they throw at you.

PVP 1/10 and i'm being generous. I wouldn't recommend this part to anyone, ever...

All in all, the thumb is down until they get the game stable (tired of the contacting Destiny servers everyday) and do something about some of the end game content. I elect to keep the Champion system but go another route. Stripping weapons that someone typically likes to use in a map and forcing another on them to kill 4-5 enemies across the hundreds that are on said map is just ridiculous. 4-5 doesn't seem like a big deal but if you've never played it then you wouldn't understand that those 4-5 can literally break your run. The Barriers will heal infinitely, as well as the Overloads. Unstoppables will just charge you non stop and eat 10,000 bullets and still have half health unless you can stun them. Your options are either suspend them or use the weapon that correlates to their stun. Just not enough options there to enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
