\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCons:
Game forces you into pvp a constantly.

I would say PvP is a dumpster fire but that would be an insult to dumpster fires. Why:
1.) All PvP is done on one server meaning you often face off against people with very high latency causing them to rubber-band and be practically invincible, or if they're on your team they usually just disconnect or stand there while you die because they actually can't help.
2.) There are a ton of aim-botters. (Since the majority of the game is PvP people "have to win")
3.) Melee is super glitchy, sometimes you can melee someone from 4 meters away, sometimes it only works 4 cm away... Take a chance.
4.) Movement is clunky... Jump, hit a 1 cm bump on the wall fly 2 meters in a random direction.
5.) Toxic community: "have to win" because of forced PvP content.
6.) If you're not playing a Titan you better use one of 10 weapons or don't bother.... said weapons require quite a bit of effort to get.
7.) Toxic Developer: refuses to accept that PvP is broken and unbalanced. Also refuses to stop putting PvP requirements in season quests/PvE quests.
8.) A few PvP maps are amazing and have good design. The rest are very pretty but damn near unplayable from a PvP standpoint.
9.) Toxic community: (Yes twice) Half the community recognizes that PvP needs to be fixed, other half calls them "stupid, dumb, trash" etc. Also tells those that complain to go kill themselves quite often.
10.) If you're actually good at PvP (not cheating) you will be griefed constantly (i.e. opponents will ignore your teammates and only shoot/kill you.) and you will receive threats as private messages IG.
11.) There is no way to blacklist people meaning you have to put up with all this BS.

If you have decided you just won't play PvP by this point, don't play at all because about half or more of the game is PvP

Pros:
1.) It has Nathan Fillion as a voice actor, and as usual he is awesome.
2.) Raids are pretty amazing.
3.) While there isn't much of a story, it is pretty good.
4.) Very nice PvE maps.
5.) Lots of cosmetics.
6.) Outside of PvP the game handles very well.

What would it take for me to recommend Destiny 2?
In no particular order:
1.) Balance out the classes in PvP
2.) More story/lore
3.) Balance weapons in PvP
4.) Devs need to ban people for threatening/trash talking people who give constructive criticism.
5.) More story/lore
6.) PvP servers on each continent so everyone has a decent ping and stable connection.
7.) Devs need to quit forcing PvP on people.
8.) ALLOW US TO BLACKLIST OTHER PLAYERS.\t\t\t\t\t
