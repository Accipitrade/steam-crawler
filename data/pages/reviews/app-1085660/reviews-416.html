\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSome things keep getting worse, some things keep getting better. Two things that I dislike the most is that as time goes on, the creatives at Bungie are on slippery slope to serve the game to the lowest common denominator (in all regards you can imagine). The second thing is that they actually removed half the game content a year ago. We got something extra (raid from Destiny 1) for free but it doesn't make up for what we lost, places where where we made memories with our friends.

The best thing is the engine - the fluidity of movement and the animations are perfect. Lots of vertical freedom. And the main story has been great too.

If you want to go on epic adventures, use magic and guns, listen to soundtrack that will give you goose bumps and do that with optimally 5 friends, this game is for you.

If you value your free time, have real life, hate grinding and get easily manipulated by sunk cost fallacy STAY AWAY FROM THIS GAME. I have 3000+ hours in this game across Battlenet and Steam and I can't stop even if I wanted to.\t\t\t\t\t
