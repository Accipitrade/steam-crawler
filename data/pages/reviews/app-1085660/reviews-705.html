I understand that the devs need something to profit from, but charging us what's essentially a full priced game once a year, every year on top of 10 bucks every so often for half an hour's content isn't a good business practice, it's a cover up for a scam and people praise Bungie for doing this while shafting any other dev team that does it.

NOW: Destiny 2 does have it's strengths.

There is a lot of places to explore (on the few planets you can go to.) There are plenty of secrets and loot to be had.
The few story missions that are in the game have usually been pretty interesting and maintained my ADHD ass's interest long enough to care to finish, so if that means anything I guess.
There's tons of customization, you can design something pretty akin to anything your imagination has in mind.

TLDR;
The game has it's fun moments, it can be good with friends but scummy business practice that gets defended for absolutely no reason ruins the real experience, new players will never be able to experience the story, thanks to time locked story events. So old players returning be prepared to be confused because it throws you in and tells you to go f**k yourself. and before rando's try to discredit for lack of hours on pc, I have over 300+ on Xbox.\t\t\t\t\t
