\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGeneric, repetitive, bland and gets boring fast. Overall an FPS where any gear that begins to shine is quickly disabled then nerfed if not locked behind a complex raid. Mandatory PVP to complete certain functions and a grind to get anything of worth. all play styles are generic and use one of several available weapon types due to the poor performance of the others. Some weapon systems are so unused to the point you can go days without seeing another player utilizing it.

bog standard and shallow "daily and weekly" task list with a straight to the trash reward. strikes will repeat themselves to the point you'll at times do the same exact one 5 times in a row before you get a different one. There is no purpose to doing said strikes once you reach a certain point anyway due to the trash loot and no possibility of a unique or interesting drop.

exotics the games powerful gear and best reward add a pathetically minor edge to play to the point where receiving one is a meh experience. Older weapons making a second appearance from Destiny 1 are neutered or outright outclassed by a lower tier weapon.

enemies follow the repetitive trend of the bigger they are the more bullet sponge they will be and may or may not have a regenerating shield. (disregarding the hamfisted instant invincibility shield) There are some unique instances that break the monotony of "shoot it in the weak spot until it dies" but thats normally reserved for the strike boss.

graphically the game holds a steady frame rate even when theres a ridiculous amount on the screen which is a feat.

in terms of bugs theres the occasional falling out of the actual game map which doesn't happen too often. The biggest problem is when enemies interact (incorrectly?) with the environment and will instantaneously teleport either in front of you or out of your fire. Sometimes they'll even appear yards away and unlike you and the other players around you the enemies aim is near perfect.

in the end bungie seemingly didn't want people to play solo if they wanted to experience the full game but for individuals without friends actively playing then by yourself you'll remain. There are countless ways they could add variety to play style but at the end of the day they already have your money so it doesn't really matter.\t\t\t\t\t
