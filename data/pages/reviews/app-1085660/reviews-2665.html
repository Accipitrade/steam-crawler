\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt had a rocky start, but after the Forsaken expansion I can confidently say that Destiny 2 is a great game. Of course it has flaws, but what game doesn't? The gun-play in PvE is great and the mouse and keyboard feels amazing. Bungie did a great job bringing this over to the PC. I am more of a casual player and don't play too much PvP, but there's enough in the world to keep you coming back for more consistently. I do not regret purchasing the DLCs, but having it free-to-play (up to the Forsaken DLC) was a great decision. I recommend to at least try the free-to-play content before making any purchases. As a person who loves story (Halo especially) I recommend going through each campaign starting with The Red War (base D2 story). It's nothing too impressive, but I had fun nonetheless. If you want to read a lot, there's plenty of lore unlocks from playing the game that get pretty interesting. Overall, I do enjoy playing and would recommend everyone to at least try it. It's free, so why not!\t\t\t\t\t
