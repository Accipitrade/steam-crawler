\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a million times better than it was a year ago, and infinitely better than it was at release.

The removal of the time gating powerful gear mechanic is very welcome. Now players are more free to play how they want and do what they want to get stronger and no longer feel compelled or forced to play missions and gamemodes they hate. The gameplay is distinctly Bungie, Music is fantastic, and the writing is great.

My negatives about the games would only be a few. The enemy Races feel so similar in gameplay. None distinctly stand out from a gameplay perspective. If I look at a game like Warframe the races are all distinct even in gameplay style. I feel you could honestly replace any encounter with the Vex with any of the other races and they'd largely feel the same. Raids are another big gripe. Raids are at this "half matchmaking" set up. Either you go in as a full group or you can try queing for a "guided game" as a group of 2. This requires a group of 4 already set up and good to go to be willing to que up as a guide. This never happens. Raids do not require some insane level of coordination. Raids should have matchmaking. Many players have already used the destiny 2 app to get a group of randoms together to play Destiny 2. They can clear raids fine. Put a warning about a time commitment, have players leaving mid raid result in a cooldown on being able to que again, and add a vote kick. It's far better than locking a portion of the game's end game content out of player's reach.\t\t\t\t\t
