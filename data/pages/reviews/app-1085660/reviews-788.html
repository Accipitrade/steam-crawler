\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm going to attempt to write a thorough and as objective a review I can. That will not be easy, as Destiny 2 is my favorite game, but even great games have flaws. So lets start with gameplay.

Gameplay: The gunplay itself, the gameplay loop, is in my opinion the absolute best multiplayer PVE shooter experience out there. Even amongst single player games like Doom, it holds it's own and in some ways surpasses.
This your game, your character and you get to play how you want. If you want to be a space cowboy who swaps out their rifle for their pistol then a rocket launcher for the big baddies, feel free. If you want to throw out seeking grenades that split apart and explode your enemies, further exploding other enemies around them, you're a wizard 'arry. There are countless ways to play the game and none of them are objectively best for every situation. You can build your character so many different ways and discover new ways of playing old characters, it keeps them fresh.
The "meta" in PVE changes every season and they are constantly adding new things that shake it up, so it doesn't get stale. (Alt characters help this if you're a neckbeard like me and put 1,000 hours into it with no plan on stopping)
The end game PVE is second to none in this genre of looter shooter, or even looters imo. The raids are fantastically designed, they look beautiful, they have a fun mixture of puzzles and combat, they require personal responsibility making you feel like you're a part of the success (or failure) of the team.

PVP- Honestly, I'm not the best here. I think it can be fun and as competitive as you make it. Just be ready to be killed by less skilled players than you because they're using something a bit cheesy. Luckily cheaters have almost all but been eradicated from the game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing for 440+ hours (and I have some regrets in doing so, but worth it) I can safely say it's an alright game. (and I enjoy myself playing it from time to time)

Beautiful visuals, vast collection of armor and weapons, as well as a wide variety of exotics to choose from for your preferred class. And many activities to try and enjoy playing. Tho I did wish that they didn't sunset the majority of planets (as well as the majority of armor and weapons during the past seasons) as it made the game feel a little smaller (this may be an understatement), therefore less activities to try out. (Europa is neat tho)

It is however, a very (and I mean VERY) Grindy game. some missions may take hours to finish and most activities may get quite repetitive after a while. But if you choose to look past that and you're up for the grind, this game may be worth while for you.
