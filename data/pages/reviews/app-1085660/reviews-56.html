\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gunplay or rather the 'feel' of the guns are great, don't get me wrong... But, the developers for whatever reason decided to nerf the bonus headshot damage which made headshots less rewarding. Which is strange, because the aim assist in this game is absurd. In fact, when I am trying to shoot an enemy and another one runs past... my sight will switch to that target like a really strong magnet. It also doesn't really feel challenging to get headshots with this amount of aim assist, and really doesn't give much room to 'master' the vast majority of weapons. There isn't much to work towards getting better at, and it's even worse on bows and scouts. To make matters worse, the game really doesn't want you to use whatever guns you feel like using. You need to equip a weapon that your artifact is specced into, so you can fight the overload, barrier, or unstoppable champions. On top of this, you need to have specific elemental weapons to break shields or they just get a straight damage bonus OR it synergizes with your class 'build'. By the time you've optimized your build, you're not likely using the weapons that you actually want to use. My next issue is with how primaries(even with the recent buff), feel very weak unless it's an exotic weapon. Ability spam hasn't gone away, and I am finding it difficult to justify using my primary for most of the content. Groups of enemies disappear so rapidly from 3 guardians using powerful grenades and ranged melees and special ammo which is extremely plentiful. Although I haven't done any master level content besides lost sectors(I really didn't use my primary much at all in that), in legendary lost sectors and the story primary usage doesn't feel impactful. I find that I am racing against other players to use my grenades or melee first before they annihilate the mobs and I feel like I'm not contributing much.

Speaking of mobs... and enemies in general. The game makes 'trash' mobs, feel like joke with the amount of available CC. Between the blinding grenade launchers, class ability blinds, strand suspending CHAMPIONS and has spam grenade builds, stasis weapons and classes freezing targets. I find that the game doesn't take this core part of the game very seriously at all. Guardians are way too powerful, and before these CC abilities, we could BS our way out of bad decisions with our supers anyways which was hard enough to balance. As for the bosses, most bosses in this game can be face tanked with a warlock well.. and that is how guardians are expected to fight bosses... standing in a circle most of the time... fun. And to make matters worse, the well lasts way too long and can have a very short cool down. In addition, they brought back the titan bubble when titans already have a shield as a class ability. While hunters can give everyone invisibility... And on top of that, everyone has healing grenades... I have no idea how you even begin to balance all of this even if they knew what balance actually means(they don't). The game gives players so much, but the enemies haven't evolved accordingly, they're still just as stupid and annoying as before and it gets old pretty quickly.

Abilities don't feel very satisfying anymore when they can be spammed. There is definitely some judgement that is 'sometimes' impactful. But, with how easy it is to regenerate them.. choosing the wrong time really doesn't mean much at all. Spamming them doesn't give me a dopamine hit the vast majority of the time....Geez, I wonder why that could be...?

Story is atrocious despite having such an interesting universe, they really ♥♥♥♥ up HARD with the exception of the witch queen. At this point, just replace them with an LLM like chatgpt and it would be an improvement somehow...

The amount of bugs that break the game are ludicrous, and servers feel like they're down or barely functioning every other day.

Playlists are terrible in general. Strikes which were rarely ever good to begin with are actually fairly uncommon to be put into. Instead, you'll likely be placed in a battleground which destiny players have grinded like crazy in the past(and are sick of usually) and were either quite a bit better or usually about the same as strikes. Instead of just bringing back old strikes they just throw a bunch of trash at us, and also give us trash rewards with no unique strike loot. And the nightfall has an absurd number of modifiers you need to remember and a single strike you can grind for an entire week... fun. Oh, and it sucks that you can't choose which strikes you feel like running. Gambit mobs are faceroll the vast majority of the time, but to be honest it feels a bit better than strikes. But invaders have way too much power so the game mode is usually infuriating. PVP lacks maps and game modes and they still haven't separated pvp from pve... COMPLETELY LIKE THEY SHOULD!

The game doesn't make any attempt at trying to fill new players in or explain to them much of anything on what and how they should play.
