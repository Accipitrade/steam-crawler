\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing Destiny for over 300 hours I have mixed opinions with the game. Overall Destiny is a masterpiece. The design of the game is smooth, fluid and the enviroments are built extremely well in terms of traversing around. The atmosphere in this game is the best I have ever seen when it comes to this genre of gaming. The only games enviroment I believe to top Destiny is Subnautica.

The game has numerous modes that will suit everybodies needs. You have multiple main campaigns which offer hours of play time and multiple rewards. You have Vanguard which is a mode inwhich 3 players are thrown into a mission like scenario which requires players to follow objectives and ultimately kill and end boss.

You also have crucible which is a control based competitive shooter inwhich 2 teams of six are tasked with taking 3 zones and keeping control whilst the other team kills and tries to reclaim these zones.

Finally you have Gambit which is a mixture of PVE and PVP inwhich 2 teams of 4 are tasked at killing ads and collecting motes that they drop. You then have to bank these motes until you reach 75 which will then summon a boss which you have to kill. The first team to summon and defeat there boss wins the round. The PVP element of gambit is that whilst players are killing ads and collecting motes. A player from the other team can teleport to the other side and kill players to remove there motes that they have collected.

With all that said there is also a huge endgame. You have raids which are a 6 person activity which is essentially and extremely hard strike with hard and challenging puzzles. Raids are phase based and have between 4-6 steps/encounters. After each encounter you will be able to open a chest which can offer high level endgame armour, weapons, mods or even cosmetics.

The negatives of this game would be that it can be an extremely big grind to get to certain light levels or ranks in the season pass. The main way to level up your pass is to gain XP and the main source of XP is bounties. Bounties are offered as weekly and daily tasks which can be anything from killing 5 enemies with a melee attack on a specific planet to kill 150 enemies with void damage and complete 3 strikes. Whilst for a beginner these seem very fun to do. After 300 hours in game they become extremely tedious. Bungie has said that with the November update they are going to be revamping the bounty system to make it more fun so hopefully that wont be an issue no longer.

This game is one of the best games I have ever played in terms of replay ability. I would highly recommend downloading Destiny 2 especially when it is a free to play game.\t\t\t\t\t
