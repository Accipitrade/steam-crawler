07 / 10

Game version: Season 19


PRO:

+ awesome Dungeons and Raids
+ PvE endgame content (Grandmaster Nightfalls etc.)
+ fantastic replay-value (seen as a whole)
+ big amount of stuff to discover for newbies
+ a lot of things to grind for (f.e. armor / weapons with prefered stats, exotics etc.)
+ weapon forging
+ Transmog system
+ detailed Lore with books etc. for interested players


CONTRA:

- extremely unbalanced and absolutely BAD PvP mode, which doesn't reward skill but luck and ability spamming (still Bungie is talking about a "great experience for every player")
- a lot of great missions and unique content has been deleted in the past 1-2 years
- Bungie is now recycling more storywise content, weapons, armor etc. than ever before
- seasonal PvE content is nothing else than thawed occupational therapy (boring af)
- everything you need for the game (seasonal pass, DLCs, extras) gets more and more expensive (it's already pretty bold, tbh)
- the Lore is pretty badly integrated in the actual game


SUMMARY:

"Destiny 2" is the best Loot-Shooter for Multiplayer, yes - but Bungie didn't make the game better and better in the last years, as you may would expect it from them. The PvP gets worse and worse and the playable stuff for oldschool-players and people with 2000h+ who are used to their PvE endgame content, gets less and less satisfying. It's no wonder that this game is losing thousands of players every month. The statistics are clear - especially the PvP is basically DEAD at this point. But also when it comes to PvE - the amount of long time players is sinking and sinking and Bungie doesn't seem to understand the warning alarm and keeps ignoring all the signals for years now. It looks like they're living in their own parallel-universe and regarding to their announcements and interviews and how excited they sound everytime they speak about a damn boring gun or whatever, they doesn't want to see the reality or the heads of the company are setting everyone on drugs.

The basic idea of the game is great and for new players it's def. fun for hundreds of hourse, but for loyal players / customers, that are part of the community for years, it's simply not exciting anymore and they need to change that.

I'm hoping for better times in "Destiny 2". Would be great!\t\t\t\t\t
