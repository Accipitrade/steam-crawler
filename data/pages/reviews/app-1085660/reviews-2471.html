That being said, quantity does not mean quality. The game will eventually become a grind for end game activity. If you enjoy shooting your way through a grind this is a good game for you. If you enjoy fantasy and space settings, this is a good game for you, if you like shooters that feel well built, this is a game for you.

The only real down side to this is much of the end game content, raids, dungeons, nightfalls, etc. you are forced to find a group to go with you.
