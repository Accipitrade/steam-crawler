\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI use to like this game from the beginning when I was exploring all of the cool planets and fighting all of the vast enemies. That is when I did some objectives in order to get a specific weapon that I really want. It would take me hours if not days of constant grinding that slowly degrades my patience on this game. There would even be some objectives that would look like I made progress but it won't even count because you would have to do it perfectly every single time to just scratch the surface of getting it done. Crucible was a huge mess. (For me) Some people would luck out by getting their objectives done quick. But for me it was a complete joke. So I lost hope on that. Getting exotics is very tedious. Some of the raids were flat out ridiculous to complete. Especially when you get a group of players that have no idea on how to work together. But most of all, the season passes were almost all filler with glimmer, xp, emblems, and it seems very redundant at every rank I go to.

If your the kind of player that likes to grind a whole lot on a lot of weapons you want to fill your vault with, then have at it.
But if your like me that wants to enjoy a game that doesn't drag you into endless grinding and sorting gear, I would look somewhere else like Warframe which I already reviewed on as it's more simpler, you get more classes to choose from, pick any weapons for any classes of your choosing, and so much more that Destiny doesn't quite have. (IMO)

Unfortunately, I'm giving this game a 4/10.\t\t\t\t\t
