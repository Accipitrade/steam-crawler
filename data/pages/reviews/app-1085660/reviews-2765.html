The Forsaken and Shadowkeep expansions still must be bought separately, but since this isn't Battle.net and there's no Bobby Kotick to appease, sales are more frequent.

The gameplay is still solid, with excellent shooting, good enemy AI, rewarding loot (that's how it's done, Bioware!), group activities for all tastes and a gear progression less obtuse compared to the Activision days.

Unfortunately, the new player experience got way worse than before. Being dropped on the Tower without any explanation it's not the best way to greet newcomers, so if you are one of them, go to Amanda Holliday and play the Red War, Curse of Osiris and Warmind campaigns to get the gist of D2's lore.

So, if you were afraid to try Destiny 2 before, now is the time.\t\t\t\t\t
