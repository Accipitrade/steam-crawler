\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: Destiny 2 is in a bad place right now. If you want to play it, go free to play and don't buy any of the old expansions.

I've been with Destiny since Taken King, putting 300 hours into Destiny 1 and over 500 into Destiny 2. I know those aren't the biggest numbers in the world, but it's a lot for me.

Destiny 2's been through its ups and downs. The release was less than stellar, but Forsaken was amazing, on par with Taken King. Shadowkeep was okay, but a step down, and now Beyond Light is the same way. Unfortunately, Beyond Light can't really be looked at with the same lens of the other expansions, as it came with a lot of changes that severely hurt the game as a whole. The newest update to Destiny 2 ended up cutting over half of the loot from the game through a system called sunsetting. This wouldn't be a big problem if there was loot to replace is, but this expansion added so little loot, and if you are playing as a free to player, then you don't get the new loot and are just left with scraps. All of the loot (with exceptions) from Forsaken and Shadowkeep have been taken by the sunset, meaning if new players buy these expansions, they will get nothing from them as the loot from it would be useless to them.

Now Beyond Light is an okay expansion. It adds cool new subclasses and a new Element, new enemy types, a lot of cool stuff. But in reality, you're paying forty dollars for a new subclass, a new destination, and a new raid, while at the same time losing five other raids, as well as a number of strikes and fun activities like the Menagerie. And even if the new raid is amazing, in order to do it, you need to power level, doing the same thing players have been doing since Destiny 2 first came out; Strikes, Crucible, Bounties. None of this has changed, we're paying forty dollars to redo old, free content so we can play the new content.

Destiny is near and dear to me, and I've wanted it to be great for years now, but it's not right now, and I want people to realize this before going in. I hope someday Destiny becomes the game we want it to be, but until then, go in with low expectations.

If you want something that comes close to how Destiny should be, get Destiny 1 with all the DLC on Xbox or Playstation, as that has loads of content to enjoy and is significantly cheaper than buying Beyond Light.\t\t\t\t\t
