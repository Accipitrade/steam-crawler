\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: If you are into games with a lot of cryptic stuff, endless grinds and incredibly well-made raids, this is one for you. It's F2P, so you might wanna give it a shot.
But if grinding is not your thing, stay away. Some missions take 5 weeks of repeating events. You will NOT be happy.

Otherwise, the gun play is awesome. Shooting stuff feels very satisfying. The sound design is great as well, although the OST never seemed memorable to me. Voice acting is on-point, the game has great humour. Graphics are okay. They leave a lot to be desired, although the skybox displays some incredible scenes, from cities to planets.

This game is a loot shooter first, and an MMO second. Progress starts out fast, and gets slower the higher your level gets. That's when you wanna start doing stuff with others. No group finder though, you will need the Bungie-Destiny-app for that.

I love this game, I don't mind the farm when the gun play is THAT good. And with the support over the next couple of years announced by Bungie, I will make sure to come back as often as possible.\t\t\t\t\t
