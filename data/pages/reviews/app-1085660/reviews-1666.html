\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was pretty fun when i first got into it. Personally, I miss season of arrivals, as the fun lasted throughout that whole season. The game lost it's charm after a while, probably because of honeymoon-ing
Met a lot of friends, a good clan which honestly may have influenced me more than i would admit
But the gameplay feels... strange
Everything feels so light and floaty and weak. This is not good.
The recoil feels faked, like the character is deliberately moving it up, creating the sense that the weapons you use aren't actual weapons, but just plastic. It feels so bouncy as well.
The reloads suffer some of this same issue, they're flinging the weapons around as if they're all pistol weight
I understand that the characters are supposed to be really strong, but that doesn't excuse how the weapons feel so strange at times.
The game is claimed to be "open world," but that whole concept is entirely lost when in missions, there's only one path chosen, and in the actual """open world""" sections, there's hardly anything to do besides public events and small objectives, which lose most of their value very quickly as you progress.
Also, throughout my playtime, I've hardly ever felt like I've been "graced by an eternal power" with any of the abilities, even when using ult abilities, because they all come off as so short-lived and weak.
The game does look REALLY ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ pretty tho, so there's that
all-in-all, the experience of the game is like, a 5/10? it's fun at times, especially when you get the feeling of being purely unstoppable, which is rare unfortunately, but at other times it's just mid.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright I'll list some positives and negatives. So you can have your own opinion.

Positives
-Great rewarding for high level activities
-Variety of weaponry that will hook you on the gun-play
-Multiple options to get almost everything
-LFG and overall community of the game is very positive
-Lots to do which will reset weekly including high and low level/skill activities
-Beautiful Graphics
-Can hold steady frames (MOST of the time)
-Compelling stories added
-Seasonal content is very good and detailed including new armour and weapons
-Fun and long lasting events such as Solstice which is an annual event where in a previous (now deleted campaign) guardians vanquished a user of the Darkness, Ghaul. It rewards you with glowing and (good looking :) ) gear!

Negatives
-Poor Servers
-If you are not a completionist you will find the game very over towering and over-encumbering
-Some Apparel glitches (Cloaks on Hunters Clipping and extending incorrectly, Titan marks clipping through armour, Warlocks robes also clipping) though this IS RARE!
-PvP is extremely frustrating with OP METAS and over the top loot which can overpower newbies.
-Gambit can be a letdown if teammates do not help the team efficiently. (Although, from experience play it with some friends, its quite funny so get up to some shenanigans!)
-The game has a LOT of micro-transactions which is heavily encouraged.
-The game is far more difficult as a free to play, player. (I bought the game on console back in Red War so I cannot relate but friends who have played for free, without DLCs, had less opportunities such as raids and dungeons/ or specific Nightfalls.


CONCLUSION

Yes the game is quite an aqquired taste. But if you can enjoy the positives, trust me you will start to ignore the negatives. So get out there Guardian and spread the light where the traveller cannot!\t\t\t\t\t
