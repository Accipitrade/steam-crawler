\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 isn't perfect at all, not by a long shot. Yet, i still find myself enjoying the game. Right now in 2019 it is a pretty swell experience and heading in the right direction. I would not invest too much into the game until it becomes as great as everyone hopes it becomes.

2020-2021 Update:

accounting for the fact I now have 2.5k hours I would say I love the game again. In my eyes, the game is heading in the right direction and I will stick with this game for a while. A fun "MMORPG" that is awesome with friends and an awesome story that you can dive into with the great lore community. I recommend this game to anyone who loves to shoot things and have awesome powers given by the light and now also the darkness. Bring some friends though as I have learned that this game is better with some partners there to share the experience.

I would not recommend this game for someone who is looking for a great competitive experience as the PVP side of things has always been a hot topic for debate. Don't take the PvP seriously and enjoy it for what it is.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith all of the hours I've sunk into this game since back when it was a console-exclusive, I felt like it deserved a more fleshed-out review, so here it is.

Destiny 2 provides a varied amount of content for both free and paid players alike. If PVP is your thing, Crucible is an accessible game mode with plenty of great loot. Once you're comfortable there and feel like flexing some muscle, Iron Banner and Competitive PVP modes are available.

If you're looking to bash giant robots and blast away space aliens, the PVE content is great. Strikes provide quick missions you can play without taking up too much time. For more of a challenge, Night Falls are available with better loot drops. And for the pinnacle hunters- there are Raids.

Destiny's Gambit offers a nice hybrid of the two. A game mode with PVE objectives, where PVP invaders can jump into your game and attempt to shut you down- opening the way for their team to advance ahead.

The game is Free with New Light. If you're an FPS fan, a SciFi fan, an RPG fan, or simply a PC Gaming enthusiast in general, I can't recommend this game enough.\t\t\t\t\t
