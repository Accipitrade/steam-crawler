\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGuns, puzzles, secrets, lore, swords and lots of activities to do.

It doesn't matter if you like PvE or PvP, this games has content for everyone, from casual players to hardcore gamers who'll try over and over to complete very difficult challenges.

You never play alone, you can visit many locations across the Destiny universe and join wandering guardians in the public events. Or find some people to play with using the Official Destiny App. But at the very end, friends with a microphone is the most valuable resource there is since you often need to comunicate and coordinate when facing the endgame content.

You are able to customize your characters choosing from hundreds of combinations possible between armor pieces, guns, shaders, ornaments, and the modifiers that will affect your gameplay.

And don't worry if you can't get any exotic drops. Be sure to look for the Exotics vendor named Xr, as he arrives every weekend from Friday until the last second of the following Monday.
