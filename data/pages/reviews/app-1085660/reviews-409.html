\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll preface by saying i have maybe 1500 hours on xbox before making the jump to PC, also played D1 quite a bit.

Two major points everyone needs to know:

1. This is essentially a subscription game without an actual sub. You're basically paying 8 bucks a month to stay current, though it comes in annual expansion and season pass form (roughly 100.00 a year)
2. This is NOT a solo game. You'll miss out on the the best content in the game if you're intent is to only play by yourself. The most challenging content does NOT have in game matchmaking.

Do you like loot grinds? Do you like FPS? Are you a social person? If yes to all 3 than this is the game for you. There is nothing else on the market that even comes close. Gunplay is top notch with a ton of variety. There is more than enough content to keep players engaged year round. I play around 30 hours a week and never seem to get everything done.

Bungie definitely favors the PvE side of the game over PvP but both are still a lot of fun. Pinnacle PvE content would be six man raids with complex team based mechanics and encounters and 3 man Grandmaster nightfalls that leave you punishingly under-leveled and outgunned. Both offer highly sought after and top tier rewards.

Pinnacle PvP is a 3v3 multi round elimination style mode called Trails of Osiris that is only active on weekends and comes with a lot of bragging rights and high end rewards. Aside from that at all times you have multiple 6v6 and 3v3 PvP playlists to sate your bloodlust.

Don't let the bitter reviews from people that only played solo and barely scratched the surface scare you off. Even if you don't know anyone that plays there are clans constantly recruiting on reddit and discord. Find a group you fit in with and there is no better game.

If you want to see some videos i would highly recommend you tubers like Datto, Fallout Plays, Aztecross (all around game coverage), or Myelin Games and My Name is Byf for some serious lore deep dives. If you want to see top end pvp check out say walluh bruh, ifrostbolt or zkmushroom. I'm sure I'm forgetting some people but they cant all be listed.
