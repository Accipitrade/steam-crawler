\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf your OCD about completing lists and 100% percenting everything in games,
I highly recommend that you DON'T touch this game. It will drive you mad.

I myself, am someone who needs to have everything completed. I gotta collect everything, I gotta do all the side missions, etc. While this game indeed has A LOT of lists to complete and things to collect, the main problem I have with it is the Content vaulting.

This content vaulting, makes a large majority of these check lists simply impossible to complete and leaves incomplete stuff to just sit and stare at you in the title and collections screen if you don't keep up with the game 24/7.

You'll be grinding away trying to do something such as collect all the exotic weapons, only to realize that some exotics are locked behind deluxe editions, or are straight up impossible to obtain because they vaulted the mission that you would acquire it from, leading you to just hope and pray the developers bring it back in some form. This type of situation permeates throughout the entire experience and as the game gets older and more things get vaulted, the problem grows like a cancer.

Realistically, I fully understand why content vaulting is necessary and it doesn't really ruin the core experience for me, and I realize that this is a really niche problem.

Overall, this game has one of the most addictive and satisfying game-play loops I've ever played and if your looking for a fun shooter to play for a bit, your gonna have a great time, however as a completionist and achievement hunter, I would strongly advise my fellow completionists to stay FAR AWAY.\t\t\t\t\t
