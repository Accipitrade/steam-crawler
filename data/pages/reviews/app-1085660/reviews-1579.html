\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI could probably write a dissertation on the pros/cons of this game. Do your friends play this? Then they're the company you should play it with. Solo player? Then maybe not, but I've spoken to many solo players who'd say otherwise.

The gun play is great. The loot system is questionable. The methods in which the F2P model have leaked into the full game is concerning. The raids and dungeons are top tier. The weekly content is repetitive if you're a seasoned veteran. The lore is great. The game's ability to give you the story and lore is abysmal in many ways. PvP is both amazing and insanely frustrating. Game balance seems to be a dart board.

I'd say "never has a more divisive and dual-identity game existed" but that's anecdotal, not fact. Simply put, it's a mixed bag that I've spent 600+ hours on (Steam + Xbox) and that's not even counting time spent in the original game. I have fun with it, especially with friends. Solo? I tend to play for a little bit but don't want to do the weekly stuff on my own given how stale its gotten.

Since this is free, you can give it a try. If you like it, then great! If not, well you've spent a few hours trying something new.\t\t\t\t\t
