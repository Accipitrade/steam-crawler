\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't understand the pricing of the "expansion". It's as expensive as full 20 hour games but offers less than a quarter. The campaign which costs 40+ dollars, that only lasts for about 4 hours, doesn't have an inspiring story or solves any mysteries. It's really flat and predictable. Not even engaging. Well sure, you are also paying to get access to some oThEr GaMe MoDeS which are basically endless reruns of hamsterwheels you endlessly roll on to, I dunno, get good or something? It gets old really, really fast.

The game is fun, I'll give it that. If you can empty your mind and go from one place to another, slapping random mobs just running all over the place- this game becomes fun. Customisation is sort of a thing. You get shaders. You can't choose where to put which colours onto but hey, it still looks nice. Armour variations are also a big thing. Lots of gears and looks you can go for here. Vehicles are also cool. Well, many of the cooler looking ones are up there on the store sold for dollars- but eh, it's up to you to buy one of those.

Oh but wait a second- the new update just took away most of the damn locations you could go to! Well look at that. Even less fun right here. At least before the update you could go for contents you wanted to grind for to get what you preferred more. Now those are all gone. You gotta keep up with the big bois all the time.

Many items have text lores. It's a fun read. But well, many of the newer items don't even have a text attached to them. Bah. Points off from that.

Oh right, PvP is a big thing in this game. If you are into some frustrating games, go for it I guess. It is sorta "skill" based. If you really want a decent PvP game, however- I suggest you go play R6S or something. It is still quite a bad game but at least it feels fairer.

Worst part is that this game has so many stans. Not fans. Stans. Yes, like any other game. Sure. But it seems to me Didoolidi two here is one of those games that has more aggressive stans, you know? SeAsOnEd VeTeRaN gAmErS or something. My point is, these stans allow Bungie to throw at people these underwhelming and overpriced eXpAnSiOn. They know people will buy it anyways. Pretty much like one of those sports games at this point.

4/10, can't really recommend the game. Gets old fast. Wait for big discount.\t\t\t\t\t
