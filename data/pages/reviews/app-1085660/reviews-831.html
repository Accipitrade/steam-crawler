\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think it's time for a new review. Destiny 2 has a wonderful atmosphere with creative gameplay mechanics to match. This is a game best played with others for a chill experience (LFG ADDED TO DESTINY 2:LIGHTFALL) with solo content made difficult and rewarding. Customisation is expansive, weapons have many perks and abilities as well as armor coming in many fashions. In terms of FPS this game has unique and fun gunplay which can be approached by new players quickly.

The free to play portion of the game is lackluster and does not tie into the game well both story and gameplay wise. DLC is a big part of Destiny 2, if you want to get the full experience it will cost hundreds. Gameplay balance for a multiplayer shooter game is somewhat bad especially for a new player. Many abilities have few counters and can slow down combat a lot. PVP can be unreliable to play and if you want to enjoy it don't take it seriously. The PVE content is amazing and where most of the action is. High priority loot is mostly found here and Bungie wants most of the players in PVE.

Overall this game is fun, but the fun comes in short bursts as content flow is over a longer period rather than at once. Bungie needs to keep some of the content in the game instead of removing it for months at a time.\t\t\t\t\t
