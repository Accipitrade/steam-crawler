+Fun instanced events
+The sci-fi vibe is excellent
+Locations are exotic and varying
+Gambit is a BLAST (co op pve with pvp elements)
+Lots of guns, not borderlands but plenty of loot. Some exotics change gameplay entirely, and there are many options to modify your guns
+F2P
+ You can mess around with your loadout, quest journal, etc. during loading screens... masks load times, more games should do this!
+ Constant trickle of new content to keep things fresh.
+ Great Co-Op experience with friends

Cons

- Locations are big, but they don't feel like 'worlds' as we are meant to believe they are... more like big maps
