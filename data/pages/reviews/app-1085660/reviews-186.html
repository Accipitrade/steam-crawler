\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery unfriendly game for new players. To effectively do most content in the game you need mods to put on your armor & guns. Getting the basic mods are easy, as you can get them through the gunsmith. These mods rotate daily & you should have your basic mods within a week. To effectively do different builds however, you need to rely on mods from a different vendor. This vendor has a massive pool of mods & even after playing for a year+ I do not have the all mods. I can effectively do different builds at this point, but for a new player it might take a month or two before they can do some of the simplest things effectively.

This game does not respect your time. I have a combined 2k hours between console & pc. The devs seemingly do not play this game when it comes to high end content. Specifically for pvp they have no idea what they are doing. There will be a glaring issue with a certain class & the response will be a nerf that has little to do with the issue. The result will be like 3-4 nerfs before they finally touch on the issue that they should have addressed from the get go.\t\t\t\t\t
