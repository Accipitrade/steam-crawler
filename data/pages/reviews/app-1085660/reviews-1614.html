At the time of the seperation the decision seemed to be the right one as Activision has a negative reputation for making games worse by over-monotizing them. But in recent months it has become apparent that Activision was doing all the heavy lifting. The game has seen increasingly barebones and poorly made DLC after DLC. The Devs have time and time again showed that their loyalty does not lie with their fans, it lies in their willingness to make a quick buck. Beyond Light so far looks like it has more content than the past few DLCs, but it comes with a WHOPPING $50 price tag, which is significantly more expensive than even the games most expansive DLC packages. It is clear that the game is past its prime, and is doomed to become the very thing it was fighting against, a generic, unfun PRODUCT, not a game, a manufactured PRODUCT.\t\t\t\t\t
