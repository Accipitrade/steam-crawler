TLDR:
Destiny 2 is the biggest waste of time i have ever experienced and i heavily recommend against downloading the game.

My Review
I have been playing Destiny for a very long time, ever since December 26th 2014 specifically. This series means a lot to me and i really think i wouldn't be alive right now if it weren't for this series. So trust me when i tell you that it hurts like hell to be recommending against downloading this game.

Destiny 2 is a game that i have owned since its launch back in 2017, it has had many ups and downs over the years, and i have had much fun during these past few years but as i'm getting older its harder and harder to care about the game as it becomes more and more apparent to me that this game is a GIANT waste of time.

Why is this game a huge waste of time you may ask? Destiny 2 is an MMORPG looter shooter, so you would have to be an outright moron to think grinding would not be involved to a certain degree. But the thing is that this game is such a grindfest to the point that to do anything whether it be leveling up the battle pass, getting better gear, getting gear with the roll you want, you have to grind for hours for all of these. Sure if you are used to grindy games you may think that this doesn't sound too bad, but it seems like bungie is hell bent on making all of it not feel rewarding. Could you imagine spending hours of grinding to the highest possible level just for the new expansion to come out and for the max level becoming the default level for everything in one update essentially resetting you to level 1 every year? Or could you imagine grinding for the best armour and gun rolls to fit your play style only for bungie to casually say that those weapons cannot be upgraded to the max level anymore essentially making you start from scratch, sure they made it so that doesn't happen to gear anymore but they still did this, basically rendering my entire arsenal of gear useless, restarting this cycle of grinding again. So now i barely want to open the game anymore because whats the point of leveling up if every time my progress resets back to zero. I would feel a little better if they added a title or something that showed you reached the max level for a year but i doubt they will.

Then comes my second issue. Destiny 2 went free to play back in 2019 and to keep players interested they made the whole vanilla game plus the two small expansions free of charge which was awesome until you realised that hey! older players? you spent over 100$ on our game and we are making the content you paid for free? How about you get zero compensation whatsoever. Listen i get it, its not that big a deal but i would have loved to get something for buying the digital deluxe version of the original game and all that content going up for free, But then the next year they introduced the Destiny Content Vault. What is that you may ask? It's a system to rotate content in and out of the game every year to keep file sizes down to keep adding more content into the game. Good idea right? yeah on paper that sounds awesome; remove the content that barely gets played to add more space for the dlc and yearly content. So they removed all of the vanilla destiny campaign, along with some vanilla areas, and removed all of the two smaller dlc's and their areas. It's fine i guess... i payed for that content and i still get no compensation whatsoever but at least that was the free content, so that should mean the big dlc's are safe right? Turns out that was not the case, when they announced that Destiny 2 Forsaken would have all of it's campaign and one of it's areas removed, still offering no compensation, they still sold it at full price for months after this announcement which was pretty scummy. All of this to say that it is very hard to want to pay for an expansion every year while i have zero assurances that i will have access to content that i chose to buy a few years down the road. And they probably could have kept this content in the game... "big file sizes" is not much of an excuse anymore now that optional content packs have become the norm for games.

I love the world, gunplay, lore or destiny but its hard to care anymore if playing destiny requires me to buy a temporary dlc and constantly resets me to level 1 after hours of gameplay. I dont want to dedicate my whole life to a game, and even if i would it would 100% not be destiny 2. I might check out the new dlc if its on sale because this series has such a tight grip on me but as of now i am done with this game...\t\t\t\t\t
