\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLets make a review.

I really like this game. I don't know why did I started this game but now I'm quite addicted because of the lore, characters, loots, design, weapons, etc. I found some very good characters what I can really like or even hate :D
I joined this game in Season of Arrival era and god that season was so entertaining and very eventful. I managed to get a very decent amount of gear, and also find a dungeon which is now my favourite (it's Prophecy). In this season, I really get addicted in this game. I was even so hyped when the season ending live event happened. I literally was awake from 1am till 4am just because that event.

HOWEVER!

In beyond light, something changed.
The story itself was cool and entertaining and able to see Ana Bray again is a huge plus, also the whole story is around the Brays so it's not that bad. I'M still waiting Rasputin to return as an Exo lifeform. However the Season of Hunt is just....BORING.
Wrathborn Hunt (or watever what it is) is repetitive and short and way to empty and boring. Loots are not that amazing (but the seasonal SMG is good so that's an exception), weapons are boring and the fact that Bungie brought back the Seven Seraph weapons from Season of Worthy just completely turned those new weapons useless and unwanted piece of trash.

Also Bungie still can't separate the game between PVP and PVE which caused already so much problem like weapon disable, stasis bug which caused Trials of Osiris to delayed untill 18th of December. Iron Banner has only 2 new weapons and that's all. In Trials of Osiris, you cannot achieve all weapons because you don't have time to do it because of those delays. So the PVP content still screwed.

But what about the raid, Deep Stone Crypt.

Now that's a completely different story.
I'm not that Raid type of player but god this raid is so good and bit easy but the lore behind is so good.
I'm not gonna say something from the raid. Just play it and you will understand.
Raid weapons and clothes are so good looking and very strong!
The new raid exotic is just overkill.

Also generic game problem: Bungie for the love of god! Please fix the problem when enemies just popping to somewhere else! It's super annoying when I'm using my super and the boss just disappearing and reappearing somewhere else! Thank you!

Overall, this game still 7/10
+ Graphics
+ Characters
+ Story
+ Loots from raid
- Lots of bug
- Lots of delaying
- Boring Seasonal content\t\t\t\t\t
