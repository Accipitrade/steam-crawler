THE GOOD
- excellent mechanics, gunplay and abilities, very poished
- weapons all feel good - almost addictive. Very unique exotic weapons, very useful legendaries.
- aesthethic designs for characters and weapons are subjectively fantastic
- Looks amazing!
- best microtransaction system (none is best obviously, but these apparently fund extra content - and theres lots of it)
- expansions are always at least good - sometimes fantastic
- Raids and exotic mission / quests are amazing (e.g. Last wish, Garden of salvation, Zero hour)
- fantastic community and support
- regular updates and events so there's always something to do.
- Many of the bad points can (and are likely to) be fixed too as a result of previous 2 points

THE BAD
- poor guidance to unfamiliar activities at times - my cousin, a new light player was so lost when he started.
- occasional awkward balancing of weapon performance leads to certain weapons being overused and minimising diversity. cough recluse cough.
- some activities can be repetitive or RNG based or both (and in a tiring way)
- occasional glitches and bugs - 9.9/10 minor
