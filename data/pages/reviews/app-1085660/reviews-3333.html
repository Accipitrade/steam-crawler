\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEDIT: Short version, game is still a dumpster fire. However, after having gone out of my way to try and understand it better... I suspect that when it was first released (as a console game) it was actually something that was engaging and fun to play. Since that time (especially since its "PC release"), the game has been gutted to the point where it is effectively a constant grind / microtransaction farm. In general the players are helpful (when you can find them), and graphically the game is stunning, but the meat of the game just isn't there anymore. In fact, I doubt it ever will be again. As I said before, if you have a preset group with which to work, this game might be a great "hangout simulator" for you. Otherwise, it's going to be a long, obnoxious grind (or a moderate dent in your very real wallet to buy imaginary things).

Honestly, unless you find a group of people you enjoy playing with (or bring one with you) this game is petrifyingly boring. The story is shot, the lore is all over the place, there's no tutorial or 'get started' mechanism, microtransactions as far as the eye can see, and, frankly, in every way it feels like a game that was HUGE 4 or 5 years ago, but now its only played by nostalgia seekers and newbies trolling the free-to-play bin.\t\t\t\t\t
