\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rarely stray from my simulation/indie/cute game comfort zone, but Destiny 2 is an exception. For reference, I enjoyed the Halo series, which I feel this game can be compared to, but I did not play Destiny 1 and I also am not well versed on the FPS genre as a whole. I played this game with only one other person, so I was never able to do end-game content, which requires at least 3 people for dungeons and 6 for raids.

Story:
This game has a massive amount of lore. If you're into that, there is plenty to sink your teeth into and the story is very interesting. If you're not, it can be a little overwhelming. The intro quests yadda yadda it for you, but you'll probably have to watch some youtube videos if you really want to understand how all the characters relate to one another.

Graphics:
Absolutely gorgeous and extremely varied. This game is a visual masterpiece, and each location has plenty of content, which means you can spend time in whichever landscape you like best and still make useful progress.

Gameplay:
This game feels really good to play. I'm sure there are a lot of words to describe it that aficionados of the genre would know, but everything just feels very snappy and responsive. Each gun feels a little different and you will get to know which are your favorites by how they feel. Customizing the color/look of your gear with shaders is very accessible even early in the game, and you can grind for aesthetics you like better later on.

Bottom line: This game has something for everyone. If you just want to jump in and shoot stuff, you can. If you want to really dig into the lore and feel like the hero as you go through PVE, you have that too. If you just want to explore some truly impressive visual landscapes, there are quests for you and things to do in those places. If you like grinding to collect weapons/gear or striving for perfect rolls, there are fan sites that will help you keep track. Just be aware that if you want to do end-game content and raids, you'll need to find some buddies along the way to do it with.\t\t\t\t\t
