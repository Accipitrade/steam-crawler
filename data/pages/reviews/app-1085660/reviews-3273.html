\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone that’s been playing destiny since its launch in 2014 and then the sequel in 2017 let me tell you right now, it hasn’t always been sunshine and rainbows. Destiny 2 launched in a state most would call boring, slow, awful and most of all, terrible. The core shooting mechanics were still top of their class but just about everything else from supers to even how fast your character could traverse the world were downright gut wrenching. Radom roll weapons had been traded for static perks leaving the end game devoid of any true loot hunt. Fast forward to 12/4/19 and it’s a completely new experience. The supers feel “super” for lack of a better term. Random rolls have come back into the fold with forsaken and random stats for armor with Shadow keep. In short, this game has become the game we wanted it to be when it launched in 2017. The raids are spectacular with random roll everything to keep you coming back, the exotic quests are fun and challenging diversions, pvp is fun and diverse, though ill admit that the crucible and gambit feel a tad ignored right now. Every week there are a dozen things you can do to earn bigger and better loot to “craft your ultimate monster killing machine” and with the new seasonal content coming out every few months there is always something new to experience. To top it all off, the first years’ worth of content is now free to play, so if you’ve been on the fence on if you should give a shot or not, there hasn’t been a better time than now.\t\t\t\t\t
