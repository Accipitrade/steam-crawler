\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a review coming from a player who started playing on launch and has played on multiple platforms. Destiny 2 is a very good, yet very bad game. The main gameplay is very fun if you just like to do simple tasks and kill enemies, and if you like games where there are lots of grind, you will like this game. But, if you want to get very high power level, you have to grind bounties for hours upon hours just to get 1 or 2 power levels. Also, the developer (Bungie) has made some very bad decisions (in my opinion) such as: removing half of the planets and content, locking most of the content behind a pay-wall, and the feature coming soon: armor synthesis (basically a cash grab.) Also, some weapons are very overpowered in the PvP mode (crucible,) and they can take hours of grinding to get. Yet, the reason I gave D2 a positive review is because there is so much to do, and the game is just fun in general. So, if you like grinding and fun gameplay, this game is for you.\t\t\t\t\t
