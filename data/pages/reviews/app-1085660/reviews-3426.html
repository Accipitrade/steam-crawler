\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been playing this game religiously. It has been an amazing experience, but not without the misteps of the devs. Their latest effort with this most recent update has been mostly positive for me. One of the few things that I see have rubbed people the wrong way is how the old campaigns have been relegated to the hangar with Amanda. It just seems like they are a little bit more than after thought despite how they set up the story and the grind for us pre-Shadowkeep veterans. It just seems like new players are expected to be okay with being overwhelmed with everything that us older players already were used to. The tutorials that could make the experience for new players better can and should be addressed by the devs. Some people also might prefer to start off or at least have the option to start off as 0 light level (or at least below 750) in order to make the progression feel more earned.

It will definitely be a completely different experience compared to how we pre-Shadowkeep players versus currently new players starting out. I just think that the new players will be missing a huge piece of the game. Of course, the rationale of balancing the playing field for all players is understandable. However, I don't think all players prefer being leveled up all of a sudden without playing a single second of the game. I wonder if it would be feasible to allow players the option of starting out below versus at 750 base light level.

Aside from that issue, I have enjoyed my time playing post-Shadowkeep. The new changes are welcome as a veteran player and it is nice to see the devs take more risks and to try to change things up. Crucible feels slightly better than before, although I wish there would be an option to choose a game mode without any heavy ammo at all or to at least reduce the frequency of heavy drop rates. I like the direction of where Bungie is taking the game. I hope they keep listening to the feedback of all players and to tune Destiny in a way to balance it while making it more fun. Bungie has convinced me to continue playing and I aim to continue my journey as a guardian.\t\t\t\t\t
