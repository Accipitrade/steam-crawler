It was bad enough when Bungie decided to REMOVE CONTENT at the same bloody moment of a DLC launch which was supposed to kickstart a new era of the game, but now they're continuing to ignore community dissent about the sunsetting of content. They have removed and plan to continue to remove old, DLC, paid-for content as time progresses, replacing it with newer, still paid-for DLC content. This means that, if you bought anything from them, it'll be inaccessible to you in the future and you're pretty much forced to pay AGAIN to replace the content they took away.

Over-priced cosmetics and a paid-for transmog system (which should just be a BASIC FUNCTION of the game) are just more nails for the coffin of this game. I want to tell you to buy it, because it's truly fun, but I can't anymore. Bungie is ruining the game with terrible business practices, and it doesn't seem like it's going to stop anytime soon.

Oh, and, one more thing: it says free to play. It's not. If you want to do anything cool or fun long-term, you have to pay. And pay a lot. It bloody hurts to see what the company that created so many happy memories with Halo has fallen to.\t\t\t\t\t
