Unfortunately the game is super grindy and has holes in it everywhere with story and ideas. Also Honestly the last major update from when the game came to steam was not good. I have hope for this game, I should since I had 300 hours on it when It was on battleNet(crap btw). I hope destiny 2 ends on a high note before destiny 3 becomes a thing. The original destiny just needs to be pc ported and made cross-save capable and then we have one hell of a trilogy pc release possibly!

Overall it is a 7/10 game in my book to cut to the chase.
Good gameplay mostly but too grindy and full of holes in the bad way.

Update: This game has gone through so many changes and I do not approve of them removing content you pay for and replacing it with re-hashed areas. I like the new stasis super and that variks is back but otherwise I am kinda annoyed with the games current state.\t\t\t\t\t
