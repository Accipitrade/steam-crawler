\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe overall experience of Destiny is unlike any other game. The game play, the feel, the mechanics, they're all second to none.

There's an absurd amount of content to keep a new player busy. I've been playing the Destiny franchise since day one and there's still things I haven't done. That's entirely by choice though, which is the beauty of it!

PvE and PvP both exist in this game, one more equally than the other. If you're looking at this game for the PvP, it feels amazing. No other game can compare to the way that Destiny feels in both gun play and ability usage BUT balancing is an extremely hard thing to do for this game so at times it can be VERY frustrating.

Overall, Destiny is one of the best games to kill time with, as you can tell by the number of hours I've put in.
