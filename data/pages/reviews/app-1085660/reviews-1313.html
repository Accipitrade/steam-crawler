\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a very grindy game but I really love this game.
The original Destiny was fun but this is probably better + they're bringing back old content
for nostalgia.

PROS:
- Allows people to play 'legacy' campaign missions from past DLCs.
- Amazing exotic weapons to suit any playstyle. Even the legendary weapons are designed that way.
- Random rolls on guns. Some consider this a con, but grinding for a god roll is a journey on its own.
- Amazing aesthetic ornaments for armor to change the way your character looks.
- Titan, Warlock and Hunter are all designed in different ways to fit different roles.
- Enemies you fight against are fun. And the raids especially are the best to play. Absolute blast.

CONS:
- Raid exploits that Bungie won't patch. Which allows easy cheese over bosses. Which isn't really that fun.
- No way to buy the exact aesthetic you want from the cosmetic store if it's not for sale. You have to buy engrams through the season or grind them. And hope to get lucky with an engram drop.
- Useless sidequests that drop mediocre loot. And don't really matter.

Destiny 2 for me is a 8/10.\t\t\t\t\t
