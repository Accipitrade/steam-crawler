\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game got me hooked from the start.

This is why I like this game.

The gunplay is absolutely fantastic.
There is a lot of loot to chase.
Lots of high-intensity endgame PvP and PvE activities with good rewards.
Item quests and pursuits that are very rewarding.
Lots of cooperation is encouraged, if you are that kind of person of course.

But this game does make me a bit exasperated:

The biggest problem this game has is reoccurring content slumps. There are often many slow times in the game.
There are many other problems that I won't be able to go into because it requires actually playing Destiny 2 such as meta problems, nerfs, etc...

This is an excellent looter shooter, I absolutely LOVE this game. It's not perfect, it has flaws and there are many lows to this game. However, in the end. this is still a game that I find worth playing.

Would recommend 8.5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
