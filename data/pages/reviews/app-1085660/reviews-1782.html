This is such a bad way of doing things, and is just one of the limitations of the tiger engine. With Destiny 3 shelved for the foreseeable future, I hope Bungie is at work behind the scenes porting the game to a new engine (as well as hopefully having been working on this task for at least a year, since this is a huge undertaking), and maybe in year 5 we see a change to a new, better engine.
