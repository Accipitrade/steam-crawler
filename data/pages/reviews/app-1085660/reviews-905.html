As a new player you just get thrown into the game without any good introduction or explanation of what you can do and what you should do. No good explanation on leveling, no good direction to follow, nothing. The story for new players is ♥♥♥♥ considering the old Red War campaign was removed, which was a clear and smooth introduction and tutorial to the game for new players. The old Forsaken campaign was also removed (but they still charge for the content from that dlc, dlc that is years old), having both campaigns being majorly important and constantly brought up in the current dlcs and stories, even though you can't play either of them anymore.

The community allows the game to get worse, old dlcs and content taken away, dlc to become more expensive, and micro transactions to become more predatory. It's only goodish if you're like me and have played the game for years (and addicted).\t\t\t\t\t
