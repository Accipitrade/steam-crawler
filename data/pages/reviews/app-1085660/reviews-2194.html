You are a Guardian...This is your DESTINY

So this will be a different approach on Destiny and feature more an oppinion on the provided Storymission and Lore that suround it.
So i mainly played Destiny for its Campaign. The whole Story is told via pretty good Cutscenes and various Mission which need to be played in order. The Game features 5 Campaign, three of them are part of the base game since the rerelease on Steam and the other two are paid DLC. From all 5 Campaign the fourth Forsaken is my favorite.
For the Stories in general I can state that they are pretty well told. Charakters are appealing and the interaction inbetween you and your Ghost are on point in terms of the combat relationship they have. Perhaps the biggest Question should be for me. Was it worth to buy the game and mainly play the Campaign.
Yes it was. Ok to be fair i play most mission with a friend. And beside the mission I completed a raid, various Strikes and Quests in order to optain better weapons. I do participated in some PvP combat. But I mainly want to point out the Storymissions since those made me play Destiny 2. So honestly I am more of a niche player then most of the Destiny players are. Not realy interest in stats and what weapons to get neither in Events and seasonal stuff. Anyways
For what it is I can recommend it. The Story is cool. Three campaigns are a cool start and beside that Destiny 2 offers way more stuff to do.
This game is best played with friends. But can be done alone.\t\t\t\t\t
