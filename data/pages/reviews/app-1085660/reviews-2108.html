\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played Destiny 1 on PS4 and Xbox One for about a total of 500 hours combined. I've Played Destiny 2 on PC, Xbox One, and PS4 for about 700 combined hours at this point. So I figure I have a good amount of experience and know exactly how I feel about this game, but ill keep things brief:
PROS:
Fun game play
Great art style
Lots of story lines to follow
Fun characters and some good writing

CONS
so for this section I feel i need to have a bit more detail. Destiny 2 introduced a battle pass system they called "Seasons", at first they weren't that bad, but recently they have killed my drive to play this game. All old content is meaningless, no reason to do old raids or do older content. This season introduced only one new armor set you can earn through gameplay and a handful of guns. The other armor set can only be purchased through an ingame store with real money. The current season (Season of the Worthy) feels awful to play with an awful gameplay loop. All you do now is run bounties to level your battle pass and....thats it? There is no new content that actually does anything to make your character stronger. The season has these events on 3 planets, that you can not match make for. These events are group content that require about 3 people to complete. These events take about 5 minutes to do and can be repeated as many times as you wish. That is it. That is all the content in Destiny 2 currently, grind daileys and do boring tower events until the weekly reset.
I love this game a lot, I love the art, the music, the lore, the environments, and most importantly the gameplay. But this game has stripped out the experience and reason to play to experience everything I listed above.
(Edit: I forgot to mention that after this season, the tower events will be removed, so doing them feels meaningless.)
Every new season for Destiny 2 i'll come back and edit my review, but currently, I can not support this game that is putting more effort in paid cosmetics and in actual gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t

V V

Normally I don't have time for games like this, but It was the holidays and now quarantine (covid-19 safety) that have given me the chance to review this game thoroughly. So here we go:

This game is more catered toward people who have too much time to play, and yet at the same time it has an amazing lack of content when you reach the "endgame". Once you finish the story (if you do) it's all basically just a weekly grind of the same 6-10 powerful & pinnacle activities/tasks, with endless amounts of repeatable bounties. At the start you will have many things to do and it will be fun as well as overwhelming at times, but as it goes on, as you collect the items you seek and the "fat loots" / "god roll guns" that you want etc... you will quickly see how empty the game really is.

If you play with friends, and do challenging content with them or play pvp (if that's what you enjoy), then you will get more out of this game then playing solo. However as I said before, you will grow bored fairly quickly and you will see the emptiness.

Also to add insult to injury, the new style of drip fed seasonal "content" (if you can call it that), is spread so thin it is barely noticeable.

Finally, the nail in the coffin for a lot of dedicated and long time fans (currently at time of this review) is the endless amount of bugs, exploits and cheaters (in PVP) that are running rampant throughout the game and are destroying any possible good that the game has to offer for endgame activities, whether you are doing PVP or PVE.

I played destiny 1 and now Destiny 2, I am a fan and I am sad with the direction the game is in.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you have never played Destiny, I would highly recommend the game. The free to play version has a lot of content to enjoy and a community that will help you do and activity you need. Especially the THREE RAIDS you get for FREE. Insane.

This game is not for hardcore players though. I would highly stress to not play this game religiously and get you life sucked into it. The reward pool, currently, is overfilled; There are too many armor pieces and weapons that are simply re-skinned or are the exact same gun. Trying to complete everything, earn everything, do everything is a full time job. Play casually, get on for a little while, and you will enjoy the game.

I am giving this a thumbs down because of how drastically the game has changed. For me, it is for the worst. There is a lot, the bounty system of leveling, the bad connections, the boring raids, etc. The biggest and most important thing is the Eververse store AKA micro transactions. Since the beginning of the store it has killed the inspiration to play. Thousands of cosmetics have been in that store that should be inside the game activites THAT THEY ARE THEMED AFTER. It is maddening.

I highly recommend play the FTP version, but do not give them a dime.

Edit for my additional time playing Beyond Light:

The game is even worse now. All the free to play content that I mentioned above is now gone. The two DLCs that you still need to buy to play are now useless because all the gear from them cannot be brought into the new content. All sorts of content was removed from the game and not even a fraction of that content was replaced. Waste of money, and just a scam at this point.\t\t\t\t\t
