\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 helped me find a game that works amazingly for co-op. My buddy and I always try to find co-op games to play and going back into the expansions and playing the dungeons was amazing. We got to complete the pit of heresy with a little difficulty yet it was a challenge that we both enjoyed. I also play first person shooters like COD a lot, so playing a game as a titan main with abilities to crush your enemies with lightning, to throwing a hammer with a left over damaging sunspot. Destiny 2 is simply amazing. I cant wait for beyond light to release as i'm about an hour away before it does while writing this post. Knowing I started on destiny 1 has made this game so much sweeter, unlocking storylines and lore that have not been revealed yet makes this game that much better. This game had a rough start but its starting to look more and more like D1 since Bungie un-partnered with Activision. Definitely recommend this game for co-op/competitive play as well as a fantastic single player storyline with the DLC's.
