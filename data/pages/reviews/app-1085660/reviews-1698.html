\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI started playing this game via "New Light", the free-to-play subset of Destiny 2. I tend not to play PvP activity in games, mostly preferring to see what story is available and whether the game mechanics are fun by themselves or in co-op modes. Destiny is worth playing on those grounds alone, in my opinion.

At the time of this review (July 2020), it's possible to go to an NPC in the Tower (a central location) straight away and play what would normally be "story mode" in other games - earlier story content released with past expansions. I highly recommend this to everyone, but especially new players as they can decide if they like the story and game mechanics. If you like this game, be warned that there is a lot to digest without any help from existing players.

After 60+ hours I finally ended up buying the unlock for the full game - there was so much remaining that I wanted to see, and some of it was locked behind content which falls outside "New Light" areas. Sometime after that I tested out the PvP arenas and found out that they're pretty fun too.

Plenty of other reviews can tell you the pros and cons, and certainly this game has its share of both. For me, at least, I found everything to balance out fairly well and I've enjoyed playing.\t\t\t\t\t
