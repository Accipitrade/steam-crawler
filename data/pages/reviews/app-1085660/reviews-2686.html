\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has most certainly had its up and downs, but right now i think the game is in a really good spot with plenty of room for improvement in the coming years. This game is seriously fun to play with some of the best FPS action out of just about any game I've ever played. The story can be a bit difficult to follow as the lore is unlocked by finding hidden gems through out the world, or just watch "my name is byf" on youtube to have someone tell you ALL the lore.

Otherwise, this game has literally so much to do that I have a hard time deciding what I want to accomplish when I get on. I highly recommend this game for anyone looking for a time sink and a great experience.\t\t\t\t\t
