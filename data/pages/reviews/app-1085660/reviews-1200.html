\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis used to be my favorite game, but its unfortunate how far its fallen since Bungie's split from ActiVision. Let me preface by saying I'm not usually one to complain about a game, just stop playing it if you don't enjoy it. But from the recent borderline predatory changes to monetization that even leave experienced players like myself confused how we're supposed to buy the new content, to the complete disconnect between the developers and the community recently, I'm very disappointed with the recent (and now foreseeable) content provided. And with the recent State Of The Game, they pretty much reassured us we'll be receiving even less content than the current very expensive drip feed model barely provides in the first place. The seasonal content is pretty much the same activity in a different location for the past 4 seasons. Pvp very seldom receives any new content. They've completely abandoned gambit. And events have been a joke for the past 3 years, all they bring is cosmetics and sometimes a re-hashed weapon that's nothing special. And although the story has never really been a strong point, The Witch Queen campaign gave me hope. I even got into the lore a bit, but Lightfall had EVERYONE confused and its obvious when they were creating the campaign for Lightfall they either didn't have a plan for how it was going to end, or Lighfall was a cut part of The Final Shape. Its very unfortunate how far this game has fell because I've played it since I was a kid, and especially that the developers are willing to spit in the face of the player-base to fund their new game Marathon. I'm sure marathon is going to be great, but at least finish out the story for the community of the game that secured Bungie's $3.6 billion acquisition. After following this game since 2014 I'm very disappointed with the direction this game is headed.\t\t\t\t\t
