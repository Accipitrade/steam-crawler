therefore i havent done any of the raids and cant talk about them but if they are anything like the ones from destiny 1 then i shouldnt have a problem with them either.

at the current point its definately a good game and since its free to play at least worth your time to try it and see how you like it. make your own opinion about it afterwards. but from this player its a good game.\t\t\t\t\t
