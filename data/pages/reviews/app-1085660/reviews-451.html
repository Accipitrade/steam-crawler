When I booted up the game for the first time I was automatically put into the introductory mission for beyond light, which I was already under leveled for (at this point a hadn't played in a season or 2 so my light level fell behind) I completed the mission only to find out they removed at least 90% from the first 2 expansions, although they were minor ones I was still kind of frustrated since I did spend money to get them, they also removed some exotic weapon quests and made you purchase the weapon with in-game currency (note: you cannot by necessary currency with real world money, so that is nice), removing even more content, they also removed several planets making it feel like there was very little content for someone who didn't purchase the expansion. Finally, personally I dislike where the team is moving in terms of story, for a long time it was always light against the dark, but with the last two expansions that line has been grayed. This kind of thing never happened when Bungie worked with Activision but soon after they split, everything hit the fan, Before this was one of the most content rich free to play games out there and now more and more content is locked behind paywalls which makes me sad, it was a great concept of having a triple-a quality game completely free.
