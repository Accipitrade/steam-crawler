Grind - The game resets every 12 weeks and all your stuff becomes useless. You will have to farm weapons and gear all over again. Furthermore, the meta keeps changing so, you cannot build up your character to a certain play-style.

Gameplay - This game is all about bounties. Its is the sole way to level up your seasons pass. The bounties are fun for a time but loses its appeal due to the sheer repetitiveness. The gun-play may be fun but, the repetitive bounties takes away from the experience.

Story/Quests - These are just an amalgamation of different bounties with a cutscene.

Stacked RNG - While some RNG is fine but stacked RNG just feels too much to handle.

Powerlevel System - The grind for powerlevels are just lame and sleep inducing.

Bugs & Glitches - Players are not you bug testers. Just like all live service games, this one has a lot of it.

Heavy reliance on wiki and content creators - Yes, you need wiki for this. The game won't tell you anything.

Overpriced - The content this game offers for the price is not justified. The amount you pay for the repetitive chore of a game is way too much.

The Loot - You will have to grind 25 hours a day to get good loot.

Chat - Chat feature is just there to collect dust.

Guilds/Clan - Good luck finding that.

Raids: Good luck finding a team for raids.

Battle eye anti cheat - it requires administrator level access to run. Furthermore, you wont even be able to ply the PvE part of the game you paid for.

Verdict:
Do not waste your money, this game is just a waste of time.\t\t\t\t\t
