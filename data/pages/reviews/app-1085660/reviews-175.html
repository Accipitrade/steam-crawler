This was a hard game to upvote.
The core gameplay of Destiny 2 remains solid, gunplay is excellent (barring a few weapons)
PvE experience is fun, in my opinion it's even more fun with friends and if anything it is a must to go find an active clan to join.
PvP experience has the tightness of Halo and other arena shooters whilst adding character and flair with each class and various subclasses, some would argue that the meta is pretty strict but I've been doing OKAY whilst using any gun that I wanna use.

I initially quit due to the sunsetting and removal of former paid content. Destiny 2 is also plagued with one of the worst onboarding experiences for new and returning players (IN MY OPINION) there is a plethora of content to do and finding direction takes a bit.

I returned because there is a lack of other games in the genre that really scratch that itch, especially for someone like me who likes having a lot of other players to play with at a moments notice.

If your pockets are semi flexible and you have the time to kill, Destiny 2 remains one of the best looter shooters around to just play regularly.\t\t\t\t\t
