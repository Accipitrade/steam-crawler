\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLets just say this; I normally hate all online co-op games, with a few notable exceptions. When I was first convinced to download Destiny 2 it was only because it was free (I ended up buying) and because it would stop a certain gamer friend from messaging me to download it every 5 minutes (you know who you are).

This game surprised me, the Story is engaging and most missions are scaled so that you can single player it if needed (or is your preference) and the co-op/multiplayer is enjoyable. As a bit of a stats and item ♥♥♥♥♥, it has enough stuff in it to keep me engaged and wanting to play more to upgrade and equip my character(s).

Pros
- Graphics
- Multiplayer Netcode (I havent noticed any lag but seeing this is p2p my Gigabit Connection might help)
- Story
- Amount of content available
- New season incoming

Cons
- Jumping; 3 different styles per class which you may need to swap between in some of the harder missions)
- Classes; there are only 3. Also would make more sense if there was a playable opposing faction (especially in PVP)
- Currently cant co-op the starter mission.
- In Game Store (I suppose its inevitable these days in an online game)

Overall I'd give this game 90%\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really, really wanted to love this game, but there's just a few things holding it back. For starters, the new player experience is garbage, literally a 20 min tutorial and a brief synopsis of the story through a cinematic and there you have it. But that didn't stop me from exploring what else this game had to offer. I surprisingly had a lot of fun with the crucible aka PVP, and this is coming from someone that avoids that sh*t like the plague. Gunplay and jump mechanics are very satisfying. Also, the planets/visuals are absolutely GORGEOUS. The landscapes, lighting, skyboxes, etc., wallpaper worthy. The cool armor sets and exotics give you a nice reason to put some hours into this game. BUT the grind loses its charm very quickly, even more so if you play alone. I was hoping this wouldn't deter me from diving into the endgame content, like trials, but from what I've heard, there's no good anti-cheat system in place, so that's gonna be a hard pass from me, and it's not like i'm going to shoot at countless trash mobs for fun. As of writing this, Beyond Light is not released yet, and I've pretty much done everything there is to do expect raids. But for now, I'll be setting this game aside and cross my fingers that the expansion pack will drastically change the player experience. If things remain the same, however, then I can't bring myself to main a game that boils down to: log in, complete bounties, log out, repeat.\t\t\t\t\t
