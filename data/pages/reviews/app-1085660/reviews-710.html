\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere to start?

This game has had the same curse/blessing ever since the D1 days.

A great time sink the first 1-2 weeks post new content, then a drought until next expansion.
Sure, the Seasons that have become a routine thing now are great and all, but having to play off and on the whole season just to finish the little bit of story that is present, is boring. The seasons have time-gated quests and progression. To me as a player, I'd much rather have everything right up front instead of the illusion of "content" due to having to wait. Outside of this content, I don't NEED to play the game at all. There is no grind worth logging in over once you have reached item cap and have done the endgame progression for 50-60hrs.

Aside from the major issues with droughts, Destiny 2 is a solid game. Shooting stuff feels great. Story is good, could be better. Grind is real and anti-climatic. I like most people, dream of the day, this game finally finds its stride as THE action-MMO to play. Long way to go, but hopefully, this day shall come.

Thanks for reading.
