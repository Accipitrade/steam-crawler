\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you have never played Destiny before, this will be an uphill struggle to like and recommend. The beauty and horror of Destiny is its history. The game I paid for in 2017 is absolutely not the same game I'm playing today. Half the content has been "vaulted" and have been replaced with different content, some bad some good.

As someone who has played Destiny 2 on and off since the beginning, Witch Queen is one of the best expansions to the game. The campaign is fulfilling and challenging (if on legendary). Bungie have really got their seasonal model polished to release compelling story and an evolving world.

If you are new, which you probably are if you're reading this, take it one step at a time. The story is complicated and long, don't worry too much about it right now (there are a lot of great resources like My Name Is Byf if you wanna learn more). Here are a list of things to get a handle on step by step:

- Gun play, just grab whatever you can and try everything out
- Destinations, go explore! this game is gorgeous!
- Armor stats, don't worry about mods yet just look at the stat numbers, understand what they mean and which ones you want to care about
- Ritual Activities, this is a huge part of the game. Strikes are PvE missions, Crucible is PvP, and Gambit is a weird combination of both with extra mechanics (do gambit after you have a good handle of everything and learn the rules). You don't have to do all of them if you don't like it (I hate PvP).

Once you have the basics down

- Campaigns, if you like how the game feels buy an expansion. I recommend going in current order (Shadowkeep > Beyond Light > Witch Queen). This will help you scratch the surface of the lore and get caught up on the story.
- Legendary and Exotic guns. Legendary guns can drop with random perks that make the gun feel different and cater towards your play style, some rolls are good, some are bad, just focus on what feels good and is useful to you, don't worry about "god rolls". Exotic guns are the fun ones, most have static rolls with crazy fun perks so try them all out and see what you love. (Monte Carlo is my favorite)
- Exotic Armor. Just like weapons, there are exotic armor that give you bonus abilities/perks to elevate your game play. These can roll with random stats so keep an eye out for a high number or a high stat that you care about.
- Armor Mods. These are bonus perks to help you out, some mods buff up stats, some help you find ammo for a type of gun, some give you bonus damage, yadda yadda yadda, there are a lot of mods so just take some time to look through them and see what is important to you.

Now that you have what I think are "the essentials", the rest is up to you, there are high level content you can shoot for like Raids, Dungeons, Legendary/Master Lost Sector, Exotic missions to get exotic weapons, there are a TON of secrets for you to uncover.

Every guardian is different, and every guardian is useful. Eyes up, it's a big universe out there.
