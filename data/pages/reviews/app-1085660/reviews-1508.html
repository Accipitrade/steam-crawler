\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gameplay is great but heavily dragged down by daily/weekly chore that forces you to play the game their way to progress. Most of the guns feel the same, even some exotic (legendaries) feel uninspired and boring, some to the point of replaceable by normal guns. Balance is not really good for a game that has quite big focus on PVP, especially in Iron Banner (which is basically PVP without gear score sync), and you have to play that for some high lvl gears. Which bring me back to the "play the game their way" I mentioned before, as I despite this kind of content a lot. A lot of daily/weekly quests (and some quests) are uninspiring and usually become chore in process, for example: kill X enemies with Y weapon in Z mode (and repeat), etc.

The game is worth checking out just for the gameplay itself, especially if you have friends to play with, but I wouldn't recommend sinking too much time and money into it.\t\t\t\t\t
