\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am a destiny 1 veteran to start this review off. I have to say there was originally a very rough launch for this game when it first came out. They have ironed out all of those issues a long time ago and i have had barely any connection issues at all. The gun play feels awesome and they have struggled with several dlc's not quite hitting as well as others, but their main huge updates they do with expansions are typically well worth the money. This game definitely is a grind type game but it is a good grind and makes getting the rewards you were seeking just that much better. They have yet to find a stride with their inbetween major content updates with them having been scattered with their introduction and not flowing super well with the actual story, but I think they may find this stride soon. One major long term issue that is really starting to form is that of power creep. They want to make raids challenging but it is extremely hard to balance a difficulty of a raid when you have god tier weapons like the Wisper of the Worm and ultimates that are mandatory for boss fights. The one biggest thing i love that is unique to destiny is that of their raids. Im not saying raids are unique to only this game, but they definately have not felt any better anywhere else. Raids are hard. They are ment to be a challenge, even to veteran players. This challenge, and overcoming this challenge just feels good. The amount of teamwork and dedication required for them is alot but trust me, when you get that 1st clear on a raid it is one of the best feelings you will ever have.\t\t\t\t\t
