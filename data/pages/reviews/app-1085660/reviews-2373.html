\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoy playing this game, but if my friends didn't play I would've given it up months ago.

PROS:
- Absolutely Great Gameplay
- Fantastic weapon variety
- Fun multiplayer with friends
- Interesting story
- Fun gear progression

CONS:
- Worst UX/UI... like, anywhere, on any platform/game/website I've ever seen... (I'm a web designer)
- Way too many currencies and systems. Puts the most complex MMOs to shame. Ridiculous variety of systems and currencies hinder anyone other than the most hardcore players, and at worst, alienates the hell out of any casual or even regular gamers.
- Confusing everything, purely due to a lack of useful tutorials/instructions. I've literally had to watch hour-long fan-made tutorials to understand what the hell is going on several times
- Storyline -- it's very interesting, but also so effing out-there it's like shoehorning a very high sci-fi novel a nerd wrote in his parents' basement into an 80 hour game... that you still won't fully understand. Too complex, too out-there. Think Warframe on Acid and Ketamine...
- Load times -- why, on a modern computer with a top-tier graphics card, solid SSD, and very fast Internet connection, does it take 2 minutes to load into a new area? I literally have not seen load times this long since the early 2000s... Like, wtf is the hold up?

Bottom-line: The game is absurdly fun to play; the gameplay outweighs any of the hindrances, and it's great for a game you're looking to just jump into and shoot stuff, but if you're actually trying to progress in the game, want to understand the lore, or need to scratch an MMO itch, there are far better alternatives. Like I said, I really like the game, but I would've abandoned it months ago if it weren't for a great crew of friends to play with. Your mileage may vary.\t\t\t\t\t
