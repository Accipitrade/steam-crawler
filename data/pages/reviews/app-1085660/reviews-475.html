\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have many hours between ps4 and PC and I'll be the first to say that this IP has been less than perfect. However, Bungie has continually made good to great content and while this game isn't for everyone, it's at least kept me hooked for almost 8 years.

The pros:

-FPS mechanics second to none.

-Very deep narrative. (it used to not be this way and much of it was restricted to reading but that's changed a lot.)
Environments are gorgeous.

-Raids are the most unique that you'll find in an FPS.

-Abilities are very satisfying.

-Lately, they've doubled down on RPG elements so build-crafting is becoming a much deeper experience.

-The grind used to be REALLY bad but lately it's been worth it and more streamlined.
Bungie has made big strides to eliminate cheating in pvp.

-Main NPC's, most of them, have become household names for players and the meme potential is never ending.

-Eververse used to be a pretty hot topic but really it's become one of the better monetization practices in modern gaming. The battle pass is definitely more rewarding than other games in respect to what you gain from buying it and what you get if you don't.

-I'm not a fan of any in-game monetization but I understand that it's a business and half the time I forget it's even there. Pay to win doesn't exist here. (Looking at you 343. Fix Halo. It's pathetic. Not pay to win but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ take stuff from behind the pay wall)

-The seasonal model has damn near erased content droughts that were so painful in the past.

-They've been making much quicker meaningful updates on a timely basis.

-Very rewarding solo experience if you're into that. Such as soloing some end game content. (dungeons, high level strikes, high level Lost Sectors)

The cons:

-When new content drops, you can almost bet on bugs happening. Small ones I'd be ok with but sometimes it's gamebreaking. Albeit rare nowadays that the servers are shut down temporarily.

-As of late, this hasn't been too much of an issue but Bungie is on the tail end of figuring out their identity crisis with this game. It's gotten a ton better but there's still some gameplay loop, narrative, endgame, and pvp tweaks that need to be made.

-When cheating is encountered, it's really demoralizing but that's true with any online game.

-Vendors don't get refreshed nearly as much as they should. They tend to have the same armor for a year.

-I wish there was more gear earned in game by doing activities. Lately they've been adding a lot of good stuff but still not as much as it used to be with armor.

That's pretty much all that I can think of off the top of my head. The game has made massive strides and it's definitely worth it.
