\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game when it first came out and it was still $60. It did not have a lot of content, and you could get through it very fast. There wasn't much to the story, and most of the lore is stuff you have to seek out and read through in game. Wasn't worth it then.

Fast forward to now, and the game is free! You get a good amount of content that you can play with friends. They have cross-save, so you can also take your progress to console (or vice versa) to play with your friends across multiple systems without having to grind through getting gear again. There is still paid DLC/seasons/expansions which unlock more content and stories if you so choose, but none of it is required to enjoy the game with your friends. The game is no longer massive after the update sending some stuff to the content vault to return later.

Some negatives: If you or your friends have the paid content, and the other does not, you won't be able to play that new stuff together, but there isn't much of that after the content update, but that also means less content in the game.

Overall it's a great Sci-Fi/Fantasy shooter worth at least trying out!\t\t\t\t\t
