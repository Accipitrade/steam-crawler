\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am going to start out this review by saying that I have played the Destiny franchise since the beta of Destiny 1 and have played probably about 4,000 hours combined of both games so far.

This game gives you an experience that I do not think can be found anywhere else. In my opinion there is no other first person looter shooter that provides the PVE and PVP experience as well as Destiny 2 does. I will say that If you can get in a active Clan then you will enjoy the game even more. I have been in a Clan since December 2014 in the days of the Dark Below. To me this game is more than just a game, it has given me Friends and Clanmates that I will have for years to come. this game's influence on my has gone beyond the game itself with my Clan, so truely this game has the potential to give you friends that you will have for years.

the only complaint I have about Destiny as a franchise is the fact that at time it seems like Bungie is not being very communicative about how it balances their game in both PVE and PVP. so there will be times of little to no change for months at a time. So, if that makes you hesitant I do not blame you.\t\t\t\t\t
