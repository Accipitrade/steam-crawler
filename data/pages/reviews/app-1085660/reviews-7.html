\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game and I've been playing since day 1 Destiny 1, but I cannot in good faith recommend this game to a brand new player, unless you're willing to spend the money on all of the dlc's. Even then, I would still caution you. The new player experience is confusing and there's barely anything in place to catch you up with what is going on. From what I've heard and seen, you need online guides and friends to help so things can go smoothly. The base game is free so you can always give it a try, but with out some of the paid content there's barely anything to do. It's truly unfortunate that I have to put it this way, but this is the current reality of the game. It really feels like the ship has left the port and there's no way for someone new to hop on without drowning in the mess of the new player experience.

For existing and new players, we know what's up. It has highs and lows, and I'll always come back for the highs. Bungie will rope us back in and blow our pants off with every hype reveal and I'm all here for it. Take breaks and come back when you feel ready, there's nothing wrong with playing that way.\t\t\t\t\t
