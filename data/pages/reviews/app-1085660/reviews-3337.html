\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I have nearly 50 hours in game already, but I feel like I have no idea what I'm doing still. You're just kind of thrown into the game and start running around doing random events and quests, unsure about which ones matter. I ended up doing some events multiple times before realizing they weren't part of the story at all. Other times I've accidentally started quests, had no idea what I was supposed to be doing, and after an hour of sitting at 0% progress I had to force kill the game in order to do anything else. During some quests you can't queue for things from the menu or leave the planet, so you're just stuck until you either crash the game or finish the quest.

As soon as I figured out how to use the pvp queue system from a menu, I haven't really needed to leave the menus or main city. Login, go to the main city to grab daily quests, then spend the rest of the time in the menu queuing for PvP, Co-op versus PvP (Gambit), and a couple PvE encounters. Certainly not thrilling bouncing between menus and watching loading screens, but the gameplay is pretty good.

Supposedly there are raids, but I keep getting the same two dungeons over and over and it makes PvE incredibly boring. And there's a bunch of planets that as far as I can tell don't really give you anything worthwhile.

On the bright side, the PvP does keep giving you improved versions of gear as you play even after you hit the soft item cap. So it seems like if you want, straight grinding PvP all day every day is a legit play style and you're not punished for it like in other MMOs.

Overall I give it a 7/10. It's fun for a bit, but expect to be completely lost the entire time you play. If you just want to shoot people in the face and get different colors of armor, then you'll probably have a decent time.\t\t\t\t\t
