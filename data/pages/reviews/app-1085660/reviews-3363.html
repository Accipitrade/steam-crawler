\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPRE-LIGHTFALL(around WQ release)

first of all, this game is advertised as "free to play" BUT it should more accurately be called "free to tryout", Destiny 2 has a good amount of expansion, which you have to pay for, keep that in mind when evalutating wether or not you want to stick with this game

second, even after almost 2k hours, i am still very convinced that the story and gameplay are absolutely top tier and there is nothing thats gonna change this for me (for now)

third, if you are someone who enjoys to obsess over fashion/lore Destiny 2 is a really good avenue for both, while fashion is more limited and not 100% free, the lore is and both have quite heavy levels of depth (coming from warframe though, this game is nowhere the fashion levels of warframe, i don't think there will ever be a better fashion game than warframe)

the previously praised character creation, yea well i think bungie could add more availability and improve that system in general

while weapon choice and builds are definitely quite in-depth, we don't have a loadout system (for example like warframe, which does this really well too), which in turn makes making character builds or rather switching between them really annoying

[yes i know there are third party tools approved by bungie for this, but let's be real here something like this shouldn't be third party but rather in-game. of course i don't know all the technical limitations bungie is facing with this but i stand by this]

there are PvE, PvP and PvEvP game modes, of off which PvP and PvEvP need to be improved upon very badly

PvE is holding up better than the other two but also starts to show its age in a lot of areas(vanguard playlists and similar playlists start to become stale more often than not, this 100% is a veteran player problem)

POST-LIGHTFALL

We have loadouts now, PvP and PvEvP are still in dire need of changes

the biggest criticism i think most veterans (including myself) have right now is probably the price point, the game is becoming more and more expensive which in the end is a personal choice (wether or not you want to oblige/care or not)

I think the game is definitely worth checking out and the game is gameplay wise in a decent state i'm gonna keep this as a positive review, might change later\t\t\t\t\t
