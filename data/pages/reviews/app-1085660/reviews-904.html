Lets get into it

I played this game for a long time. a really long time. Before it was here on steam I played this on battlenet about halfway into the forsaken expansion. Back then you pretty much had to do the campaign but this was kind of good. the old red war campaign got you used to the mechanics, allowed you to find your play style, and exposed you to the wonderful lore this game has to offer. It was a grind that started from level 1 and light level 1 but it was a labor of love honestly. The exotics felt earned, pinnacle weapons were a huge flex, your efforts at the highest level of the game felt as if there was a payoff.

Now, slowly but surely, the game has gone into the free to play hell. you have to pay for the season pass, yearly expansions, and most ornaments (cosmetics). your leveling does not matter. the good weapons you got will go the way of your iphone with planned obsolescence, making you grind for more weapons that you know wont last or be as fun as the last ones you got. They don't deserve your time or money. i wouldn't waste either of them on this game.\t\t\t\t\t

As they introduce one thing to keep the players happy the silently take two others away amidst the shadows of patches and "bug fixes". PVE will always stand out for me as one of their strong suites. The stories can be immersive and engaging for years at a time. They have worked out grinding to be somewhat enjoyable with the feeling that you are working towards something.
PVP is the worst experience I have played in years. With the introduction of stasis subclasses the pvp experience was further degraded. Balance issues are overabundant and matchmaking never seems to be a match but a one-sided play with a group or get ground into dust experience. Very seldom are you in a match where teams are evenly matched. Every seasons new meta forces you to skip over enjoying the games extensive weapon cache and does everything but force you to play with the same thing everyone else is just to survive a round of getting one tapped by the said meta.

We shall see how everything is now that they have made some changes to everything and introduced anticheat.\t\t\t\t\t
