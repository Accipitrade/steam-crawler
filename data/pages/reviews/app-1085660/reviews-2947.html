\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith Lightfall I am officially changing my review to a Negative rating. This game has been going downhill for awhile, slowly but surely, and lightfall slammed the nail in the coffin. I can't recommend playing this game anymore, as it has created significantly more questions than answers. There are still unanswered questions from D1 also. I'll try and make this quick:

Pros:
-Gameplay is still fun
-New Mod system is a good quality of life upgrade imo
-Loadout system is great, needs a few tweaks but otherwise good
-Strand Subclass is nice and feels good and smooth, decently powerful also

Cons:
-New Lights (New players who have never played D2 or even D1 before) Will be so lost in the story, who is Calus, who is Cayde, why is Calus "back", where did he go? Who is the traveler specifically? Without Playing D1 or D2 before Forsaken, You will never know who Cayde is or who Calus is besides small mentions or specific strikes, that just make it more confusing.
-Paywalls everywhere
-Incredibly confusing story with not so good narrative
-Short campaign not worth $50 or $99 (if you buy the annual pass also)
-Unless you have a Clan/Discord, finding people to do endgame content will be very difficult and sometimes run into Toxic players in LFG
-After getting to endgame levels, the game becomes incredibly dry with content, especially since they have removed over 80% of the games content, that you can no longer do/experience
-Weapons keep getting sunset (unable to used for any endgame content due to a level restriction) or if they don't get sunset, they get incredibly outclassed by new content, so much so, that they become useless

With all that said, this game is not the worst game I have ever played, in fact, I genuinely liked the game a LOT up until the last few seasons (roughly 6ish months ago), however, it has been going downhill for quite some time and after putting in all the money I have with this game, simply by buying expansions and Season Passes, I no longer feel like I'm getting a positive return on my investment. I love the gameplay, I love doing strikes and raids, I just don't think the game is worth it anymore, as it just doesn't make me feel like my money is being well spent. Everyone is entitles to their own opinions and mine is just that, an opinion, I don't think the game is worth the investment anymore, so I can't recommend the product to anyone, at this time, maybe ever again. Only time will tell if I get lightfall (maybe on a black Friday Sale or something) or maybe the next expansion will redeem the game, who knows. I will probably still play the game from time to time, but since everything "fun" is locked behind Lightfall now, I don't know how much I will be playing.\t\t\t\t\t
