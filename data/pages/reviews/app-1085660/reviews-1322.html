\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy favorite item in Destiny is the "lack" of story. I know, I know, it sounds weird that I like it's often-discussed "biggest" flaw. Here's why I like it and have been hooked ever since I started in 2014.

Consider the whole premise of a Guardian. A human/exo/awoken that wakes up after death with no memory, a ghost, and the inability to die. How confused would any of us be to find ourselves with no context in a world, with what boils down to space wizards with guns, where humanity is on it's last legs with multiple assailants. We wouldn't know anything. So this element of "no story" is completely appropriate to finding out bits and pieces as you go along--who can you trust, is the vanguard a transparent actor, and why are these specific aliens attacking us. I love that we get a little bit at a time and that it leads to discussions with other players, theories, debates, etc--all things that I believe would occur organically if you woke up with space wizards in the future.

I also really like building the character and fighting in teams.\t\t\t\t\t
