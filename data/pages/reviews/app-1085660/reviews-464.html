\r\n\t\t\t\t\t2022 review: This game has everything. I will say, if you are a new player, find a clan or watch some youtube videos, there are a lot of things in the game that don't make sense to new players because of the way Bungie has decided to develop the past few years. They have removed some content so there are some confusing plot holes for some new players. That being said, Destiny is by far my favorite game and I will play it till the day servers go offline.

2023 Update: I still love this game, and I will continue to play it. However, there are some items that I want to bring to the attention of someone looking to play this game for the first time.

1: Bungie has listened the what their dedicated player base has requested and I feel as though it is making the best parts of the game completely unattainable for new players. The skill gap is now so large that the top 5% can solo pretty much anything in the game besides Raids and even those can be completed as a duo. While new players are stuck scratching their heads as the game has changed so much and bungie has done a terrible job of teaching new players how to even navigate the menus.

2: this is a looter shooter. there is a lot of loot that you can obtain. but if you want the best stuff. it is going to come with a heavy time commitment.

3: to me, this is not a solo game. If you are a solo player, look elsewhere. if you are still interested after hearing that, then I highly recommend that you find people to play with. Just about every content creator has their own discord server. If you are not a discord person, then there are Facebook groups, as well as a "Looking for Group" page on the D2 website. Find some experienced people to play with. it will make everything way more enjoyable and help you to not waste your time doing meaningless activities.

Basically, I do still recommend that people at least try out this game. there is a reasonable amount of free content (the best of which are 3 and 6 player activities) and i have never played an FPS game that feels as good as destiny. The build crafting is fun, and they are always having sales on expansions and stuff, so put them on your wish list and wait for them to go on sale. If you want people to play with, message me, My friends and I love helping new people learn the game and complete awesome stuff like raids.

Good luck Guardians.\t\t\t\t\t
