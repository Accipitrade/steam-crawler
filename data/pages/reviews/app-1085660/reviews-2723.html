\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny has had a very bumpy launch back in 2017 and was ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ on by almost every person on this planet who loves the looter-shooter genre. From in-game monetization to the low amount of content available in the game at that time turned off most hardcore D1 players who were expecting great things from the sequel.

In just two years though D2 has become a phenomenal game that is one of its type, a unique game in this era where every others just want to become a looter-shooter regardless of whether it suits the gameplay loop or not *cough Anthem cough*.

People often compare this game to Warframe and say that why should they play this when they get everything for free in Warframe whereas you have to pay for every expansion in Destiny 2? Well, the answer to that would be to just play both games. You can just take a look at my profile and tell that i am saying this from experience. Both games are vastly different from each other and the only thing in common is the sci-fi genre. Both are good at what they do so comparison is kinda dumb. (except endgame, none in Warframe XD)

The game is very rich with lore and loot and have a very solid gameplay loop with one of the best FPS gunplay out there so if you like the looter-shooter genre, there really is no reason for you to not play this game considering the base game is free along with 2 dlcs.
Trust me, you won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t

While the Vanilla's story is really off center, the DLCs really bring the story in.

PVE and PVE has alot of opportunity to play with various builds the the Ladder more then the former as PVP tends to require more Specialized builds.
Long story short PVE can be focused on abilities and gunplay while PVP is almost all Gunplay and Really heavy on using exotics to amp up abilities.

Loot is plentiful and a short grind can get you plenty of parts to upgrade and alter your gear mods.
however some of the quests for exotics and Pinnacle weapons will take some more time. usually straight forward, but quick searches in google or the community hub will get you the info you need.

All in all I say give it a go if you enjoy Loot 'n shoot style games I'm sure you will get dragged in as many before.\t\t\t\t\t
