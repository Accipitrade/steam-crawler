\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wanted to love Destiny 2, I really did. But decisions made on the balance-side of things have made that an impossibility for me. Allow me to tell a story, and perhaps you'll understand why I feel this way: I have paid over $300 USD into this game. 100 at launch, when I bought it, another 100 when forsaken came out, and little bits here and there for cosmetics and season passes. In that time, Bungie has released some primo content, and made some of the worst live-service decisions I have ever witnessed. At one point during the game's lifespan, I can't remember when exactly, Bungie decided it would be a good idea to implement something called "Sunsetting" which basically gave every piece of equipment you purchased an expiration date. This was received by the public about as well as a sulfuric acid enema. Despite this response, Bungie decided it was the best game mechanic ever, and so they kept it in. So now I have a gun, called the Loaded Question. It reloads itself in the backpack, makes enemies explode when they die, and does a lot of bonus damage on hit. It's great! But thanks to sunsetting, it's useless. The current "Power Level" in Destiny 2 ranges from about 1350 to 1560. The release of the Witch Queen expansion boosted the power level of every piece of equipment a player owns to 1350, the new minimum. However, my Loaded Question, a weapon from the game's first season, is still capped at 1060. This means that, although it has been boosted to 1350, it cannot be improved in any way. No amount of upgrades or power infusions will get that weapon's power level from 1350 up to 1351. This means that all of my favorite guns in the game, the ones I worked hardest to earn back in season 1, are functionally useless for anything other than patrolling low-level areas and mopping up trash-mobs. Until this decision is reversed and the level-caps on legacy weapons is removed, I cannot recommend anyone spend any money on this game. A game which punishes its longest-time players is not one that deserves to be profitable.\t\t\t\t\t
