Countless hours on D1

Im in no way a professional reviewer these are just my thoughts and angry words.

I have played this game from release, pre-ordered every dlc and expansion. and they were all given away for free. I dont have a problem with that in general but we hard core players did not get anything in way of compensation. not to mention they gave some dlc away just months after me buying it. I mean you could atleast make the hard earned money I spent go to some sort of cool "unique" item (not some kinda cool shader) Give us something more substantial or refund the money.

I love the F2P model we have more players! but "crucible finding player times can still take up to 5 minutes for a single match. "no its not my wifi or PC" its a big complaint


The game has had alot of ups and downs, dev sometimes listen mostly about unimportant thing and let certain abilities ruin crucible and do nothing. ie "hand held spernova" "shards" "skull fort" just to name a few. if there was a monthly balancing "update" to fix broken items skull fort went unfixed for years not even sure if It was ever fixed. "good ol days" of a striker titan flying around the map insta killing every one with shoulder slam and insta regening it. They dont even use guns at that point.

This game is completely missing the game play loop that would keep it alive. they new "dlc" updates add nothing substantial and the dlc before were so short and non replayable that they are not even worth remembering "dont get me wrong the game a gotten a thousand times better that Year 1" but it doesnt that that spark that Destiny 1 had. I cant even take half the races seriously! oryx was badass a god, but in destiny 2 we get Xol a true worm if I had ever seen one...


I have loved this game and will have fond memories but there is zero reason for me to keep playing. No game play loop! its like they try to make every npc as lame as they possibly can. kills off cayde 6 leaves us with the rest of the vanguard two of the lamest characters ever.


In conclusion if you have never played destiny before please do you might have alot of fun! But I cant find enough of a reason to pick this game up again.\t\t\t\t\t
