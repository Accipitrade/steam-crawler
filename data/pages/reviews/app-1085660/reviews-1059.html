\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreetings as I hope all is well! Below is my Steam Review for Destiny 2, thanks for reading as I wish you well in all your gaming adventures...

STORY/LORE - 9/10

If you haven’t played the first Destiny, it’s okay as with Destiny 2, you can easily jump right in and get what exactly is going on. It’s not required to have played the original to get what is going on in the sequel as you’re a Guardian and are here to save the galaxy from evil! Yes, this has been done many, many times in gaming but once you start getting into all the unique characters, NPCs, and all the great stories of what is going on in Destiny 2, it’s all very well done. The overall story and lore in the universe is fantastic once you start traveling around and doing zone missions and quests. It’s all quite immersive and will leave you wanting more.

GAMEPLAY - 10/10

Looking for an FPS game that has an MMO feel to it while also giving you the option to PvP when you feel like it? Destiny 2 is the perfect hybrid game that checks off a lot of these boxes. Whether you want to be in a huge zone questing, raiding nonstop, or hitting PvP battlegrounds for hours, you can do all that and more in Destiny 2. The gameplay is great as there is a lot of loot to earn with a ton of different stats, abilities, and the option to customize everything nicely as far as fine-tuning to your playstyle. You can also change the color scheme of your armor, weapons, and vehicles which is a nice touch so everything can match as you progress in the game. The gunplay is very smooth as well as unique abilities based on your playstyle. The gameplay is easily a huge part of what makes Destiny 2 amazing!

SOUNDTRACK - 9/10

Considering that Bungie were the original developers of Halo, it is more than enough to know that the overall soundtrack of Destiny 2 is absolutely great. Every zone has its own unique sound and whenever you raid, each strike has its own unique signature sound. Whenever you fight the music also changes so it really adds a nice dimension as get through all the content in Destiny 2.

MULTIPLAYER - 10/10

The decision to bring Destiny 2 to Steam undoubtedly boosted the game as it’s become a main staple in popularity. You will almost always find it within the top 10 of games actively played on Steam at any given time and Bungie has said they will implement crossplay in the future. Once they do that, it’s going to definitely open things up for this to be one of the top FPS/MMO games out there. Yes, you can solo a ton of content if you’d like but once you get to doing raids, the multiplayer aspect really shines. Queues are quickly formed as teams are easily assembled via matchmaking and with all sorts of different modes to enjoy with others (Strikes, Nightfall, Rumble, Gambit, Crucible, etc.), there’s a ton of things to do via multiplayer. Best of all, they have daily/weekly missions to do specifically for multiplayer as you can still level up, earn gear, and everything else in between at the same time.

FINAL SCORE - 10/10

Destiny 2 is an incredible experience and a very unique video game when it comes to a fun hybrid game. It’s mostly an FPS game with some 3rd person view (if you like to use swords as you break faces) but treated as an MMO. The lore, gameplay, and multiplayer aspects are all superb as the community is also very friendly and open to new players that get into Destiny 2.

If you’re looking for an MMO fix but without any monthly subscription fees, give Destiny 2 a shot. They have a ton of in-game events, a battlepass system, and a plethora of content to jump right into. This game will only get bigger and better over time as Destiny 2 gets a 10/10 in my book. With so much content to get into (new expansion Breaking Light just came out as this review was written), fun PvP modes, and a community that loves this game so much to see it enjoy continued success, it’s worth getting into right now. What are you waiting for? Create your Guardian and go start helping the cause! Bring your friends.\t\t\t\t\t
