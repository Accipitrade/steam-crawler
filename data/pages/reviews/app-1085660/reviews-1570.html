\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played destiny since the first game :) easily one of my all time favourites. Time just flies when you play this game. The thing about it for me is my major dislike of fps games. but this one brings a lot of elements into play. first off there's a lot to do. There's a lot of tough quests to complete, some of these quests earn you powerful exotic weapons. Secondly this game isn't all about the pvp, I suck at pvp games but Im slowly getting better. so here's the various offerings that I find notable:

PVE:
Exploration:
explore various planets and moons. without the need to go questing. If you just kinda want to run around and join world events, its easy to join other players. There is also a lot of hidden areas you can locate on the map.
Rating: 5/10
For me its good If im hoping on for 15 minutes

Strike missions:
Short missions, always ending in a boss battle. theres a lot of them. you go into matchmaking and travel in a group of 3 random players. you work as a team together to complete it. at the end you collect rewards (guns armor, currency) .
Rating: 9/10
I love strikes, I like boss battles. some of these bosses are meh to me. the first game had some amazing battles, and there still is incredible battles. Strike missions can be very challenging if your teammates are terrible. but at the end of the day you complete it and grab your rewards :)

PVP

Crucible:
A player vs player free for all or even teams. theres a lot of different options to the type of pvp you plan to play.
Rating: n/a
I cant rate it because I absolutely suck. I completely suck! I get slaughtered in there because I know theres a lot of people who only play pvp in destiny. this is definitely not for me :) everyone in there is a tough cookie


pvp:pve

Gambit:
its a player vs player vs environment

you work on teams of 4 to fill essentially (for unfamiliar/new people) fill a jar full of marbles and once done it summons a monster with a prime evil. the team who kills it first wins.but you can also invade the other teams to put your team in the lead.
rating 10/10
Easily my favourite pvp experience. I highly recommend it :) bring a sword! its easier and quicker to kill other players.

there are more gameplay modes that have been introduced on and off in the seasons. the seasons introduce new stories and lore (If you love lore based games you will love this one). Seriously check it out! the game has brought the first game into it for the players who are new to the game. This game is easily on my top 5 list of favourite games :) the hour count is a little inaccurate as I have played this game forst of other platforms. I have put an easy 800-900 hours in the game total!

See you in game!
