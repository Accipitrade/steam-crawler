\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy very first impression starting the game was "Am I playing 2?". I was literally confused and had to double check. Then there's the 7+ minutes to actually start playing, which is a theme that continues as you play. The load screens seem very slow for what is being loaded.
The story remains something that comes off as being written by a teen who wants to be a writer when they grow up.
I was trying to play coop with a friend. We wanted to do the start to finish campaign. Start playing, no mention of it and no idea who to start it. We had to look it up on the forums. Which is another theme that continues: "WTH, where is this or how does this...?" "I'll google it..."
The whole experience comes off as being shoddy and haphazard. Activision can't be blamed for that, as this is core gameplay elements.
Then there's the 'classes'. I put them in quotes, because as far as my friend and could tell there really very little difference between them. Maybe that changes down the road, but other then 1 ability, everything we had was basically the same. Which rolls into the loot. Where is it? I picked up about 15 pieces of weapons and gear in my four hours and very little of of it was different. The gear was very boring and it felt like we had nothing to work towards. Which is about how the class abilities felt.. underwhelming and boring.
On the overall, I'd have to say this isn't worth my time and I just can't recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
