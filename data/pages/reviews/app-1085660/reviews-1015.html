\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would recommend but you gotta hear me out first, the base game is not worth putting 100+ hours into. Vanilla destiny lacks content. you can do crucible, and strikes. after that point the game is over and you've finished. but after you've purchased the expansions for around 60+ dollars you get a game baby. from raids to the trials of osiris the game is packed full of things to do. this game is a game you grind, you're not just picking up this game for 30 minutes and expecting to be able to run the highest difficulty nightfall or run a raid. every single tuseday we get something in the community called weekly reset. every week we get two new sets of bounties. two more difficult bounties that give more rewards, and 5 easier ones that give exp. how does exp and leveling work? well leveling works by equipping higher "power" gear in order to get even higher "power" gear to drop. the higher the gear the higher the power. every single season (which lasts about 2 months) has two seasonal caps. a soft cap and a hard cap. a hard cap is the level you can get to through gear alone and a hard cap is a even higher level you can reach by leveling up a seasonal artifact. although its only around 15 levels higher than the soft cap it still is a higher seasonal cap. through leveling up through the seasons you can receive rewards through a seasonal pass. the pass has different tiers and two different sets . a premium set and a f2p (free to play) set. the f2p set will get you rewards that can help you hit the seasonal cap, although difficult hitting the f2p seasonal cap is very hard. but with the premium pass you get exotics, which are special weapons and the highest tier weapons in the game and cosmetics. with the premium pass you also get materials to help you level through to the seasonal cap and get to end game activities at a much faster pace. this game is playable solo.. playable... but it really becomes a game you can become immersed by creating a clan and playing with a group of 5-6 people. This is when you really get a great game. with three classes and four sub classes per class you get tons of ways to combine fire teams and create a power to be reckoned with. this is what destiny is, a bunch of space warriors taking down gods and huge robots. its badass. now what about the story??? is it even good? does it even have one?? well yes, more than you even know. the way destiny tells its story is through unlockable texts called grimore. the grimore is not something you need to unlock. if you want the full destiny story lets just say its four hours long. and was read on youtube by "My name is byf". so what are my final thoughts? vanilla destiny is not worth your disk space. but after the expansions its worth 1000+ hours. the devs have work to do but I believe in bungie. they are hard workers. and if you think this game is p2w (pay too win) just know bungie chose to split off with activision in order to make destiny a better game for everyone. if you chose to play this game have fun. and remember 203 on top.\t\t\t\t\t
