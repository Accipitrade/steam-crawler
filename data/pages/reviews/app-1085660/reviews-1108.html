\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is one of the games that I always find myself coming back to. The series has many flaws is not for everyone, or probably even most folks unless they have quite a bit of time to really dive into the game.

A few quick points:

The main stories for each expansion tend to range from meh to just ok. The lore beyond the main campaigns is pretty enjoyable if you you are down to hunt it down and read some in game or via the web.

The combat is pretty enjoyable with each class having a number of abilities to use.

The various raids of the game are quite enjoyable is you can find a good group. Creating a group for endgame content more or less require you to use either have a good clan or find help via methods such as the Destiny 2 fireteam finder, discord, forums, etc...

Weapon variety has been hampered some by the recent "sunsetting" of many items whereby items have basically a lifespan of about 1 year before they can no longer be upgrade or reasonably used in new content. The issues has been eased somewhat as more gear has been added to the loot pool but it is still a concern to be watched and aware of for the future.

There are also many people who dislike the monetization of the game whereby the cosmetic store called "Evervesre" sells various emotes, skins and vehicles for premium currency. This issue does not bother me personally but it can turn some away.

With all the games faults and flaws, I would still recommend that you many folks should atleast consider giving the game a shot. For me personally, the game still quite engaging and always draws me back in. I've been play Destiny 2 since the beta back in 2017 and will probably continue to play in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
