\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo... I got this game for free on Battle.net. I didn't really play it back then, I just saw that a game was free to claim and of course I wanted to claim it, anything to expand my game library. The game, even for free, wasn't much to me at all until Shadowkeep released. Since it released on steam, I have transferred my account over and begun playing with a bunch of friends, wasting away hundreds of hours without even spending a single penny on DLC.

After I had my fun with all the included content on New Light, I purchased Shadowkeep and it truly blew me away. The content available to the player in Shadowkeep is awesome and there is quite a lot of it.

For a player who doesn't plan on buying DLC and wants to try exclusively the F2P model, the game is great and comes with all the year one DLCs for free. From personal experience though, it is a matter of time before you either can't resist buying a DLC or quit the game entirely because you ran out of things to do. Which is an issue for people who are tight on a budget.

Overall great game, but if you are looking for a free game you can spend hundreds of hours on without spending a dime, you may want to look at Warframe instead. (For everyone's information, I love both games equally and find the Destiny vs Warframe debate to be pointless and stupid, they are allowed to and SHOULD coexist.)

Beyond Light slaps also.\t\t\t\t\t
