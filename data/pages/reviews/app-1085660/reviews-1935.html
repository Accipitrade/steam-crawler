\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2... another one of those games i want to like, but it simply frustrates me to hell.
Yes, the gameplay feels good. Mobility, skills, weapon handling and feedback - it is a fun shooter... but the way the content is handled just p*sses me off.

It is another one of those grindfests. Constantly repeating the same activities while constantly feeling underpowered. It feels like i'm roadblocked ALL THE TIME. Oh, here's an open event. Wanna start or join it? You can do... but then you get crushed by enemies which afford power levels WAY above you. And even if i find some nice guys who carry me (at power lvl 850 on an alt-character) through, items drop on lvl 880. Great. Made it so much worth feeling like being a hindrance. The only way to catch up someway faster is by doing the dailies, which either force me to PvP (i hate PvP) or repeat some missions or "dungeons" for the 50th time.

The Beta-guided-system for raids or harder dungeons never worked for me and ended up in pointless waiting.
The story could be great, if it wasn't shredded to tiny pieces and locked behind grindwalls.
What about those ships? Can you do something with them. Manually fly or space-combat. Nah. A Crafting system? Not really. Yeah you can grind material on planets but mostly to deliver them to someone so a bar gets filled and if thats full you get a rng-reward, which may or may not be about 1 point above your equipped item-lvl. Seriously the rng is out of control.

This game is way to rng-frustrating to play it casually and i've got way to few time to play it religously - that is, if i even wanted to. From time to time i get an itch and return, just to be frustrated all over again.

Sorry, but if you look for a looter-shooter, take any of the Borderlands-titles. Way more fulfilling than this random timegating grindfest of a wannabe mmo.\t\t\t\t\t
