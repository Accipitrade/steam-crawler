\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this game for hundreds of hours on Battle.net.

Destiny and I have had a complicated history. I quit Destiny 1 'for good' at least 5 times. Despite the frustrations I had with the game, I loved elements of it. The art, shooting, music, and lore were there. What it needed was a compelling plot and compelling character development for the large cast.

Forsaken gave me that. The story to this game went from non-existent to mind-blowingly thrilling. Finally, Destiny (through Destiny 2) has completely lived up to what Bungie promised back in 2013. I haven't finished Shadowkeep but I'm enjoying it so far just as much as Forsaken.

This game has a lot to offer. It has beautiful art and music, amazing shooter mechanics, and a gripping, extremely complicated plotline. Crucible PVP (your standard team deathmatch type of MP) is terrible, but Gambit (a non-standard PVEVP mode) fills that PVP void extremely well! The raids are a total blast, to boot. This game is gonna go down as one of the greats. It has so much to offer, and don't let your past experiences with Destiny taint this experience. If you go in with an open mind, you'll be very pleased with the game, I guarantee it!\t\t\t\t\t
