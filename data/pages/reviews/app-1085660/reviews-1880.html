\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFPS crossed with RPG think Warframe and Borderlands.

Did not enjoy the game on release (it was bare bones), Forsaken salvaged it then not sure what happened afterwards but it has become a tedious side quest (bounty) simulator. Kudos to them for standing up to Activision Blizzard. Shame on them for going through money like toilet paper.

The design (up until this point) has been FOMO-centric, the idea that if you do not play you will miss out on content be it guns, instances or cosmetics. The game relies on crafted story missions to tell the narrative and bounties and activities to deliver higher grade gear to the player.

However with the new "sunsetting" of content and gear (the grinded gear will become useless, content will be disabled), the game becomes a job and less a way to relax and enjoy the gear you've worked to acquire.

The grind is just not worth it.

Save your money and play Warframe.

Reviewed from the PvE perspective and someone who occasionally dabbled in PvP.\t\t\t\t\t
