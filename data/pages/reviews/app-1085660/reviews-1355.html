\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played Destiny for years, starting on the Xbox 360 then switching to the Xbox One and eventually onto the PC. It's an ever expanding world with plenty to do and to grind for. There are usually lulls in content leading up to the new season, but you can take advantage of these to finish off the grind for the weapons and armor you'd like or even go for a title.

The gun play is unmatched and while the beginner experience may be a little rough, the community is amazing with very little toxicity. I'd recommend this as an amazing place to start for your first FPS game. Especially if you enjoy space wizards, giant bosses, and really awesome guns.

10/10 Graphics
10/10 Gun Play
10/10 Community
10/10 Game Play

My favorite game by far.\t\t\t\t\t
