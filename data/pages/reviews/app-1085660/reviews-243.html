I download the game b/c my nephew loves it and I'm greeted with a cinematic about "Mars returning" (where did it go?) and "Savathun is here" only to be disconnected b/c the anticheat timed out (lol). Log back in and I'm greeted by a totally different cinematic about the Drifter and I'm doing something for them (??) with no context. I'm DC'd for the same timeout issue from anti-cheat. I restart my pc, hoping to resolve the issue (dude I have a monster rig, why is this on ME to resolve??) only to then be greeted by loading screens that don't load (lmao) and a series of disjointed quests that mean absolutely nothing to me. There are icons present in The Tower that despite talking to the flashing objects in mention, never go away, so remains as clutter on my screen.

The gameplay is now systems on systems on systems. Wanna mod your useless talking companion? Wanna mod you abilities? Wanna mod the mods? It's like D2 chased whatever hype train occurred during population spikes in order to retain players and then never addressed that they were creating the most complex shooter of all time (in all the non fun ways; there's no strategy or nuance to these systems-- there are meta and off-meta builds with a ton of obfuscation in between) while making the story so obtuse that it's impossible to link what's happening and why these systems exist.

Not to mention all the ♥♥♥♥ they sunset without any credits or rewards in game. A ton of content I enjoyed is now gone, replaced with 5+ different ways to enter LFG, and a handful of explorable areas.

If not for my nephew, I'd leave this game uninstalled. Decoding all this ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ is going to take a while. This game is unnecessarily obtuse, has a godawful onboarding experience (what is happening? why is X or Y important? why are X or Y doing what they're doing? literally nothing is explained), and a customization system that's entirely too complex for what Destiny 2 is; a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ shooter. I loved the balance at the end of D1 and even the start of D2. But whatever Frankenstein monster this game is now, it's definitely not friendly to new players, and returning players are equally left in the dark.

I regret buying the expansion for this game before turning it on to see what it's become. What a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥.\t\t\t\t\t
