\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust hopped back into this game after beating the main storyline when it first relaunched. I will say I was a fair bit confused going into it as I didn't expect many of the changes that were made, like making the standard power level 750, or removing the leveling system entirely. But nonetheless this game has just tons of content. I'm about 40 hours in and have only experienced a taste of what this game has to offer. From raids to the pvp and pve, this game is really fantastic. If your a fan of games like the Bethesda Fallout series, and Borderlands then you will absolutely love this game. There is dozens of hours of fun to be had for free, and hundreds of hours of gameplay if your willing to buy the upgrade pack. Highly recommended to those who enjoy loot based FPS games. While it may get some hate the vast majority of that is from veterans who've sunk tons of time into this game and are heavily impacted by every mal change. But to the average player this game is well worth it.

8/10\t\t\t\t\t

I CANNOT recommend this game without any DLC if your intent is to just play for free, if you want to try the game out for a bit go ahead its great. But as of right now the game is very bare bones due to how much content was removed with the launch of Beyond Light that was a part of the original free to play package. For new players just getting into the game who purchased all of the available DLC, you will have a blast on this game, especially if you have a group of friends who are also getting into the game. Tons of stuff to do; exotics to grind for that will take you hundreds of hours to collect a decent amount of. The raids are amazing if you get multiple friends to play them with, but if you're a solo player honestly forget about raids, or test your luck in LFG lol. PvP is meh to most but I like it, hackers are talked about a lot but I haven't ran into many because I don't often play competitive PvP. You might run into that one guy with a full auto RPG but not often. So yeah, with no DLC, I cannot recommend this game unless just trying it out, the DLC's if ever on sale is in my opinion a must buy.\t\t\t\t\t
