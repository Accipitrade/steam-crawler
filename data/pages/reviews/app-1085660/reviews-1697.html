Conflicted Review [Updated]:
Destiny 2 is a mixed bag. The environments and sound design are amazing but in regards to story and content, it is really lackluster, especially the end game content. For end game content, you will repeat the same things over and over again. It can get boring. There is no game-play variation. And the campaigns for the other expansions are so short. How are they expansions?! For some expansion missions, you literally do bounties to advance the story.

There are raids to do and strikes; however, there is no matchmaking in regards to raids which I think is stupid.

In a nutshell, Destiny 2 is a gorgeous game but the grind is not worth it when the mission variety is just doing the same thing over and over again,

After a 6 month hiatus I really like what Destiny 2 has become. The introduction of Stasis was nice so was the BattleGrounds. I love them!\t\t\t\t\t
