\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my first negative review, and the honor is all your Bungie:

I was always a fan of D2. Played allot of it, grinded away in the game like mad, loved it.
But over the years, D2 changed for the worse. It started with the sun-setting. This wasn't a big deal for me until recent. Old content has been recycled may come up allot. But to explain the severity of this, listen closely.

The content that was made way back has been amazing. it was very detailed, high quality, and the materials are just beautiful. Now after the sunset the planets containing these assets where "removed". Currently, the asset are re-used again. But there is a problem here.

You pay for the full DLC again and again.
However the price for making the DLC however is no longer the same due to the fact that the assets already where made before the sunset. This is a prime example of corporate greed, and we allow it. It vastly lessened the quality of not just the game, but overall, by repetitive content every single year again. And this is not the first time.. is it?
It lowered it as the development is now more then ever focused on more money. And none of it is pumped back into the game. I would not even be surprised if D3 is gonna be announced soon-ish, and im not gonna pay a dime for that game. Cuz in the end, the disrespect of the leaders that gave these shots is just unforgivable. I have been around and enjoyed the game. The Devs are not to blame, the leaders are. And the sheer GULL to dare to ask to donate to bungie for some event that happens every year in my inbox... i hope you rot alone. The path of this game is a perfect example of what happens if you allow the greed to pray on the ones who stumble across the game. When you get to the point i am at, and you lose the respect for the game, its too late. Its just pure abuse.

So this is farewell from yet another player that "licensed" the game. I never thought idd say this but you where better when activission still owned part of you. And im sad to say goodbye.

And to all new people finding this game now i say this:

This game loves the fear of missing out.. and i'm sad to say.. the good days are gone, and you.. you missed out.\t\t\t\t\t
