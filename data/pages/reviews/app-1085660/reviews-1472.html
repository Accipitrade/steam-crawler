\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game sucks.

The pve side is in decline because all of the things unique to each class have had their efficacy reduced, and then been given to the other classes. Warlock supers still suck.

However, the main issue is pvp. Since there is only so much pve content out there, the replayability of destiny 2 often comes from pvp.
Horrible pvp sandbox balance kills all the fun from pvp. The weapons that rule are pulse rifles, sub machine guns, and shotguns, as per usual. Ability spam, which bungie said they were trying to reduce, actually increased since lightfall launched. The class balance in pvp is horrible, as titans (despite bungie saying they removed them) STILL HAVE ONE SHOT MELEE. Hunters versatility in pvp allows them to almost be equal to titans, but they are weaker. Warlocks, however, have been given trash exotics, had their abilities moved to the other classes, and strengths they did have neutered. Everyone talks about the gun-play being high quality in destiny 2, but one look at the aim assist values and it should be pretty obvious that the gun-play is way too forgiving.
In addition, the pvp anti-cheat is seemingly nonexistent.
The introduction of strand was literally just stasis during it's broken phase but you could shoot while effected. It also has very low cool-downs, adding to the ability spam.

Lightfall expansion was a waste of money. The game-play is almost always trash, but I used to really like the story-line. In lightfall, not even the story-line was good.

There have been no shortage of error codes either. Many high-difficulty activities, like anything "flawless", are ruined because of error codes.

==

I really loved this game. If you look at my hours spent, 5334 to be exact, you can tell that I was dedicated to this game. It is basically the only game I have ever played consistently. It has lost everything that made it good.
I played raids, dungeons, night falls, and campaigns. I played quick-play pvp, competitive, and trials. I played mayhem competitively for some reason. But everything has lost its appeal because of these "new" additions to the game.\t\t\t\t\t
