BUNGIE GOING SINGLE NEVER LOOKED SO GOOD

When I heard my childhood friend & sweetheart Bungie had split from her scumbag of a husband, Activision or as I called him....A**vision. I was ecstatic...I recalled the promise we made to each other when we were kids in 2007, that we would be together forever. But alas, my heart was broken when she settled for him. All he ever did was treat her like trash. Never listening to how she felt, how she thought. When she tried her best to meet his unreasonable demands, he would cuss her out, call her a tramp and order her to buy him another pack of bee--microtransactions. But now he's gone...maybe the spark of romance can once again be rekindled. Nostalgic memories of our time dining on sunset Valhalla played through my mind.
I quickly scrolled through my phone's contact list...

"YES" i thought to myself...I still had her number! I quickly dialed her up
"How long has it been?" I thought as the phone rang.
"5 years? 8 years? She listed herself single on myspace"
"Would she respond?"
"Would she remember me?"
I heard the call connect and a soft voice warm say... "Heeyy"
"Hey.." I said...letting the silence take hold for a sec "Been a long time, I heard about your split...u doing okay?"
I could hear her soft breathing through the phone and my mind instinctively saw her smile on the other end.
In a sly but confident tone I knew all too well from our time together she said
"Good... but I'll be better if you take me out on a raid this friday"
I was back in the game boys.

So I bought D2 back in 2017, played it for a few weeks, on and off with one or two friends every so often , completed campaign, maxed out my hunter and titan to around 300 power level, left my warlock at 37. Stopped playing after the first DLC bombed. It was awful and I didn't touch D2 for well over a year, uninstalled. Fast forward to 2019 and now I'm contacting all my closest friends on discord making plans to do raids, public events, coordinate on Crucible. I'm honestly surprised at how much fun this game has become and is becoming. Shadowkeep is alot of fun, worth getting and playing. I love how they bumped the classes powerlevel to 750 so I don't have to worry about bringing my warlock back up to speed.. Thank you Bungie! I heard some players are complaining of this but I'm not since I know me and many others are now getting back into this game after a LONG time. In my opinion, It was a smart move by Bungie doing this. They knew their divorce from Activision would attract many guardians, old and new, so it made sense.
My advice to you the reader: D2 is free, get it, Forsaken has awesome reviews and Shadowkeep is the icing on the cake. D2 is meant to played with friends, find some guardians to play with. D2 is at its best when its with community. Don't go in alone. (sorry for the grammer, its a late night)\t\t\t\t\t
