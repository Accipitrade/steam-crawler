\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI used to love playing Destiny 2 since i tried Destiny 1 out. It started good but fell miserably. I make this Review from my own Opinion. You dont have to support it but maybe you understand me with that.

The Story just repeats

Destiny 1 had alot of key Figures in the plot to offer such as great Names as Crota, Oryx, Savathun and many more. They have been known to be defeated at the Time so Destiny 2 could just Progress and offered an addition to the Story with its own Antagonist and stuff. That was pretty cool and i liked the Story but now they just bring all those Peoples back from the first Game and make a whole Story about them again. I mean im done with Savathun and her reject Family. I dont want to fight them once again. This becomes pretty boring by now. I know some peoples were happy for the content to brought back as a form of Raid and so on but i dont need a whole Addon based on Savathun again. No thank you.

returning to Destiny 1

With Destiny 2 i had hopes to see just a lot of new additions to Destiny in general with new unique Locations and new races. It gave us Titan (wich was deleted later) and Io (wich also was deleted later). I liked these Locations and especially Io was very Interesting but then came Mars into the Game, wich technically is not new. Still i liked the Content about it. It maintained mostly about Rasputin and the Bray Family wich also were not new Names. We knew about it in Destiny 1 allready. Forsaken was the legit new content they added first in the Game but i get to it in a few Steps. Later on they also brought back the Moon from Destiny 1, also the Trials of the Nine, wich i think was also in Destiny 1 and many more. They started to implement more and more Content in the Game we allready had in Destiny 1. Its not wrong to bring back what was successful in a prior Game but seriously, i could just play Destiny 1 again instead. Deleting Destiny 2 own content and replacing it with Destiny 1 Content just feels wrong.

Lack of new Classes and abilitys

It was always a Thorn in my Eye that you could play only 3 Classes in the Entire Game makes it look poor. It also took us a long time to actually get a new Light that barely anyone used after Beyond the Light was just not new anymore. The 3 Main Lights were thankfully updated and gave you a new Sub Tree to choose but i still wished just for a complete new Light and Darkness, wich sounds just awesome, being only an Light based on Ice, didnt even seem to be useful to use at all while the updated Lights of Arc and Solar just seem to be superior to it. It just takes too long for Bungie to actually do anything new in the Game.

Lack of new Races

No Matter if playable Classes and the 3 Races you can choose for yourself or the Enemies, it lacks on both. Since Destiny 1 you could only choose between 3 Races. Seemed fair enough in Destiny 1 but i hoped by now they added at least one more Race. Same for the Enemies. Im actually sick of fighting only Fallen, Hive, Vex, Taken, Cabals. With Forsaken they started to present to us a new Race but its just the Fallen in 2 different Variations. One of them being the main Enemy Race for the Content. Still its not new. On Mercury (wich also was deleted) they also brought us a new Vex Race but again, its not a new Race, its a Variation. In the Queen Addon they also brought us a new Version of the Hive but still its not a Race.
By now its just getting old.

The same exotic Quest Routine

Every damn Exotic Weapon Questchain has the same format. Collect, Pvp, kill, collect. No Thanks.

Free to Play my A**

In the beginning you had to buy the Game. Fair enough. Destiny 1 had a Price too. but Destiny 2 became free to play later. A Boot right in the A** of many Peoples who spended Cash on it allready and didnt even get anything for it. This leads us just more and more in to the deep dive into our own Wallets because literally everything that gets added in the Game is hooked with a Price Tag and there is just so much Crap by now you can or have to buy. Adding Seasons to the Game? You have to buy a Seasons Pass and not only one. The Season Pass promises you to get access to the first 3 Seasons but afterwards you have to buy a new one. Come on Bungie, srsly? Also every Addon for the Game is hugely overpriced and most of them gives us just one new Location, some Story addition and exotics wich sometimes are worse than the ones before. Also you need to buy Dungeon Keys to get access now in the Dungeons and they are not cheap either. Its just greedy. Also since the last like 3 or 2 Years they bring us Events (no they are not new) like Solstice, the halloween one, the Christmas one and so on. I enjoyed to do them but now they combine the Events also... with a Pass wich gives us extra loot but ofc you have to buy it... it needs alot of greed. Dont call it free to play then.

Recycling just everything

This is the Point in the Game were i drew the Line. As i said before. They added a few Locations in the Game like Titan and Io wich were cool but later they removed it and replaced it with "new" Content. Keep that in Mind. The Moon came back together with Eris Morn. Not new. Mars was later deleted. The Shore from Forsaken got also deleted. Dreaming City got added but still exists for whatever reason. Mercury was deleted (new in Destiny 2). They happen to delete Content from the own Destiny 2 Game and replaced more and more with Destiny 1 Content. Thats not the Point yet. Mars came back later as the new Addon Witch Queen. Titan also came now back readded and both of them hooked with a new Price Tag. I am sure they will do the same with Mercury and Io aswell and no im not rebuying it im sorry. Also they re-added Strikes and Raids from Destiny 1 wich are part of the Strike Playlist while the original Destiny 2 Strikes often are even kicked out of the Playlist. I see a pattern here. Some of the old deleted Content will be brought back as Season Content (buyable Content).
Also the Leviathan came back (like i dont have enough of it) with a renewed Story about it but the Point is, its not new. Its about Calus once again and he got destroyed good enough in Destiny 1 allready. Just leave me alone with that. Bottom Line. Content of the Game disappears and gets added back for a Price with a slight Story change. As i said. Greed.

Literally scammed

I cant just ignore some Points about the Game, were i really felt scammed. One good Example here is the Addon Forsaken. It came for like 30 or 35€. By now you cant even buy it anymore because they deleted it. It brought us the new Location Tangled Shore and a new Story about the Scorn (a crazy Version of the Fallen). This whole Content got deleted and the Money you didnt got refunded. Thanks. They even replaced the DLC with a Forsaken Item Pack for not cheap. Thanks again. i didnt ask. I paid like 30€ for a Content that got deleted and im not the only one. Im sure they re-package this content for a new Price. Sorry but no. The 30th Anniversary is a joke too. They celebrate 30 Years with Content we have to buy. Always a great move. And no this Content is not new again. It gives us Xurs Treasure Hord wich is just a small hub for us to cash in Tokens from the new Bounty Quests and crap for Cosmetics. Thanks again. Also it promised us a new Activity called Dares of Eternity. Dont call it new because its not. It is literally like Gambit: Prime wich was deleted. They cant run both at the same Time because why you need 2 exact same Activities? Dont be fooled here. Also this DLC in a whole is also overpriced. Again i see a greedy pattern here..... Also Lightfall is 99,99€? Absolutely no. It received allready alot of negative Reviews.

Conclussion

It started out promising but ended horribly. It became a mess of Price Tags and paying Contents. Im not letting them fool me anymore and i hope you will understand what i mean.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played Destiny for years. Sometimes, usually near the end of a season, I'll hit a wall and not play Destiny for a month or so.

It's been 5 months.

Lightfall is so bad, I cannot open Destiny without being hit by a wave of depression, knowing how good this game is, and seeing what's become of it. I can't stop thinking about how I could be playing any other game, and actually having fun.

The terrible monetisation. I used to think it was fine, because the devs deserved to be paid for putting out amazing content. You can't even buy the DLC and expect to get access to the dungeons now, which is something that was in every DLC before Witch Queen. Why now? The devs are already getting paid when I buy the DLC, now you're just fragmenting the DLC to squeeze every dime you can out of me.

You can now buy season pass levels. The price to buy every season pass level is over $120 CAD. You can buy a new Triple-A game with that money, a whole world of new content, but they want you to spend that money on a few cosmetics.

They also intentionally give you more Silver from a pack of it. this sounds great, but they intentionally make it so you won't have enough Silver to buy anything afterwards, making you want to buy more silver because "I have some extra to spare, might as well spend it on something small". This is mobile game monetisation, the type of ♥♥♥♥ you see in Gacha games like Genshin Impact.

It hurts that it's come down to this. I never could've seen a day where I can't recommend Destiny to someone. I've loved this game, loved the lore, but I can't even open it without feeling so sad, even with all of the positives that are still in the game.\t\t\t\t\t
