\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst things first. I do play Destiny 2 just for the casual Content since I don't have the time to play another game on Hardcore Endgame Level.

Every time a new Expansion pops I'm back to the game after I played it a Month or two within the "current" Expansion. I can totally reccomend the game. It is actually grindy, but since you got huge amount of Quests and Modes to choose from, you have more than enough to do. This way you can make the biggest grind really enjoyable.

Also you feel like you're always progressing further, even more now than at the early days of Destiny 2. When you reach the softcap of Lightlevel you still get a few LightLevels a Day or Week and since there is an artifact increasing your Light Level, you also get Progress through that.

The update of putting Season Passes and Rewards through out the max Level was a good choice, since this also gives you the feeling of progress every day until you have capped it.

The only raid I was looking at back in the days was the Leviathan Raid and I didn't have the mates to actually compete it. So the Raid Content is stuff I can't talk about but everything else on Destiny 2 is atleast enough to fill up a good 100+ hours of Gameplay every Expansion + the Story of the Game and the Expansions.

If I'd guess, I'd say that you need more than 500 Hours to complete every Quest in Destiny 2, which actually reward decent weapons. So everyone that says "Destiny 2 has not enough content" or "Destiny 2 isn't worht the money" has his/her own opinion, but from my perspective didn't take a close look at the game at all.

I hope this Review was helpful for you and if you decide to go for Destiny 2, have fun with the game. It is really good.
