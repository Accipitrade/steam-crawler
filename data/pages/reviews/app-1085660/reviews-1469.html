\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis Game known as "Destiny 2" will consume everything in your life be it your time, money even your sanity, it has this drive behind it fueled by the greed deep inside all of humanity, Ultimately Leaving you feeling empty and hollow inside, but yet still leaves you with an addiction, driving you back to it again and again, much like certain recreational substances, Unlike the mentioned substances, This is free so 10/10 best game

onto reality this is a great game to piss around in and have fun especially with friends, be it in strikes, crucible or gambit its fun, most of the time at least, the pinnacle of endgame content being grandmaster strikes and Raids, the game is genuine fun and unironically one of if not my favorite game

From its 3 classes, Warlock, Hunter and Titan to the individual subclasses your given an array of customization options to find the perfect gameplay style for you, adding to the options are armour mods which allow you to enhance or adjust certain abilities, such as your melee, grenade or class abilities, in terms of options you have 3 elemental subclasses in base with an addition of a 4th with the Beyond light expansion , this leading to you want to find a balance as those elements carry over to your weapons.

Above mention my like for the class and subclasses, but now im going to mention the problems, firstly being that half the time the balance of the game gets shifted to far in one direction and reduces the other subclasses to nothing as they aren't perceived as meta , this is just poor balancing by bungie which then leads to the broken subclasses to be nerfed to complete uselessness.

onto weapons, as of the current season, the season of the lost, most weapon types are quite valid and useable with a notable one being fusion rifles, which for the longest time were considered trash, but now are probably one of if not the best for damage, with 1K being a top contender for most used weapon of the season well in my eyes at least.

Part of the reason I write good things in this review even though this game drives me to literal near insanity half the time, it means a lot to me since i started playing Destiny 1 when i was 12, and i know that's rather young for a 16s game, but hey that's the past, that game helped me, who was a lonely bullied kid come out of his shell make some really damn good friends and cement my love of games, and destiny 2 is no exception, yes yes the game has had its ups and downs, mostly down more often than not, but i still see it as an amazing experience to play be it solo or with friends, and who knows you might just make some friends on an lfg,i know it happens and its sometimes out of nowhere so until then, Eyes up Guardian\t\t\t\t\t
