\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright 4000+ hours in. I am clearly a fan and dedicated player. So now I'm making a more detailed review. Even a 1000 hours in when I last edited this review, was pretty accurate to my current feelings. But here we go:
The Good:
Bungie has kept this game updated for years, and while some content has been vaulted due to needing space or technical limitations, there is still so much to do for new lights. Including 4 campaigns (one of which will bring you up to the soft cap of power), dozens of strikes, PVP that can be absolutely baller, a variety of weapons to discover and use, as well as build crafting that is really fun to spec into. If you are someone who just loves to collect, you'll be here a while. You love story? Thanks to My Name is Byf and several other creators, you can deep dive into the lore and understand the amazing yet elusive story, even if the most recent DLC has failed in this regard. The power fantasy here is something unparalleled to other games and you will find smiting enemies with guns crafted from eldritch horrors to be more than satisfying.
The Bad:
Bungie can be a confusing bunch... while they do keep the game updated, sometimes it feels the prioritize the wrong things. Fixing exploits that honestly should be features or patching out bugs that really didn't harm anyone. While leaving major balance problems or connectivity issues unattended to. They do get around to them eventually (even if it is a few months) which is better than letting them fester for the games entire lifespan.
The game was designed as if it was a single player adventure but then has multiplayer elements mixed in, which can often make certain decisions regarding difficulty and how levels are designed quite odd or maddening. I would say hands down Destiny 2 is best experienced as a fireteam. Get you and 2 friends to play and you will likely have a fun time for a quite a while.
That being said, as with any online game. Please be wary of the community, cheaters and trolls still exist here and don't give a flying [INSERT PROFANE WORD HERE] about you. Don't join random discord servers via LFG and try to avoid heavily using LFG through there as well. I often ran into very suspicious people, rude, and inconsiderate jerks, and overall stand-offish persons. My best bet would be to again, have friends play with you, or go to the discord of some Youtubers who have the option to LFG. Since they are more moderated for making sure members aren't running around ruining other peoples day (especially because they are smaller than the gigantic PC LFG server). This problem as a whole extends to both PVP and PVE.
Regarding PVP, do not play this game competitively. As in, take it so seriously that you would skip important life stuff. I know this sounds ridiculous but this game is never truly balanced and thus cannot be taken truly seriously. The aim assist compared to other shooters is insane and the rage inducing strats from certain weapon choices are constant. I am not a horrible player either, going flawless several times. I have done carries, and had a 1.56kd via Guardian Tracker. Which is in the top ~10% (at least it was at the time I remember checking it so consistently). So please, if you are looking for an enjoyable, balanced PVP experience, refer to something like Call of Duty, CSGO, Valorant, or even Overwatch. They are much more suited for competitive play than this game is.

Now to conclude...
I realize that the bad section is much more lengthy than the good. However this should not deter you, as I was merely trying to elaborate on WHY I see the bad elements the way I do. This game has a lot to offer for people of varying playstyles and is an absolute blast with friends. Just be wary of the downsides that may be content drought or the community being garbage at times. If you decide to start, welcome! And I hope you thoroughly enjoy what the game has to offer. For those who may be reading this that already play? How do you feel? I've always tried to maintain a positive relation with this game and Bungie via leaving comments and playing the game.
But aside all that, here's hoping that the end of the Light saga is a good one. And that whatever Destiny may have in the future be amazing.\t\t\t\t\t
