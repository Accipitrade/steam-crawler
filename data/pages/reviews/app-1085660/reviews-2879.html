\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish there was a thumbs in the middle option. I like Destiny 2. It's a beautifully crafted game with an absolutely fantastic world and universe to explore full of live and discovery to be made. The lore and story behind the entire game is so well done. I feel myself becoming full immersed in its world and enjoying my adventure through it. The game is a grindfest overall and like progression for end game comes down to gathering materials to upgrade your weapons. It is what it is. I didn't mind it but I know it is contentious for other people.

My biggest disappointment from the game stems from the light level changes. Before, you could have any piece of gear from any time during Destiny 2's past and continually upgrade it so you can use it, but then a change was made to give every piece of a gear a maximum light level for which is can be upgraded to . I started playing December 2019 and I grinded out 250+ hours in Gambit and crucible to get the weapons I wanted to maximize my builds and to get the absolute perfect rolls ONLY to have all that work completely invalidated because all those weapons max out at 1050 light. All that hard work I did completely gone and it really upset me. I'm a PVE player. I don't like PVP, I don't particularly like gambit, I like playing in Destiny's 2 massive world, doing dailies, doing raids, doing public events, etc. The changes to light levels pretty much made the game unplayable for me because I've already put in a tremendous amount of work I don't want to do it again. I know most people will just say get gear and weapons that don't max out so quickly, but again, I've already put in 250+ hours of work, I don't want to have to repeat all that work and it really upsets me that Bungie is forcing me to so it pretty much made me stop playing the game. And that's unfortunate. I really like Destiny 2 but I can't play it so long as this whole max light level on gear and weapons remains in the game.

That's my two cents about the game. Obviously the game is tremendously popular. Beyond Light will be a huge success. I just wish I could be apart of it.\t\t\t\t\t
