\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall this game is well made with a decent amount of content, good stories, and extremely well produced music. I am disappointed with all the content being vaulted, but I fully understand why it must have been done. The way that bungie is taking this game is extremely well, emphasizing world exploration with story building. The three current raid that are in the game are extremely fun, being "Last Wish", "Garden of Salvation", and "Deep Stone Crypt".

There are some problems with this game, but they are problems with many MMOs. This game is free to play, but there is not really anything to do unless you have the paid DLC, which will cost you anywhere from $25 - $40 per DLC, and there are three of them. These DLC used to have a ton of content that was extremely fun and creative, but much of it has been vaulted with past planets. One other problem with this game is the fact that the raids are very heavily dependent on team communication and if you do not have five other friends to the raids with they become extremely hard to do. There are LFG (Looking for Group), but unless you can create team synergy based off of that you are still going to struggle.

Overall score for Destiny 2 would be a 7/10.
*Lots of fun to be had, but struggles from standard MMO problems.*\t\t\t\t\t
