\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 can best be described as a Disney ride. You watch and listen as the animatronics dutifully complete their prescribed routines behind the Plexiglas, plink some targets, listen to the robots exposite some more, plink more targets, overwrought final boss, next please! You are given the illusion that what you are doing has consequence, that you have any agency, but it's a Disney ride. Potemkin Village: The Game.
It's also a game that at once takes itself too seriously and not seriously at all. I wish the world had half the intrigue or mystique that Destiny 1 has prior to its release. I wish it had an ounce of interactivity or depth beyond the samey patrol quests and raids and the damned monetization. As it is, Destiny 2 is not for me at all, even in lieu of a price tag.\t\t\t\t\t
