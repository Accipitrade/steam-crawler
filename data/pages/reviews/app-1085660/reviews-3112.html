\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun game, with excellent sound design, HUD, levels, enemies (mostly) and missions. With the introduction of F2P, along with Cross-save, I'm now able to bring characters from separate consoles to steam. Most weapons are fun to use, and combat is excellent. The game has many bugs, some apparent and most others not, but for the most part Bungie listens to feedback. Lore is all in-game, but requires a lot of time to collect. There is a lot of grinding in this game, but nearly every activity can be played with a friend. Armor looks good, and guns usually look great. Microtransactions are all cosmetic, from what I've seen.

The gameplay has always been one of the strongest points in Destiny's favor, and across the years it has only gotten better and better. With newly updated subclasses that allow for more customization than before, an ever increasing supply of weapons that don't go away, and fun seasonal content and modes, Destiny 2 is more fun than ever.

In the past, one of the biggest complaints about Destiny was that it had no story. That argument now holds no water, and will continue to lose weight as Bungie keeps taking this game in the direction it's going. Every few months we get a new season, with its own story that ties in to the larger narrative. Each year, we get a new expansion that adds more features and rounds out the overall world even more. I've been here since D1Y1, and with what has been going on the past few years, and what is going to happen soon, I can't wait to stay.

Destiny 2 is a science fantasy game where you wield the powers of light and dark, protecting the remains of humanity against alien invaders that had wrought terror across out Golden Age. Now, with allies among the other races we prepare to do battle to defend our home from a voice in the dark, an enemy.

This is all coming from a casual player, one who enjoys the game but doesn't engage in the higher tier, harder content, but I feel Destiny 2 is in a very good spot right now, and with what we've seen of the next expansion, Lightfall, this game is only getting better and better.\t\t\t\t\t
