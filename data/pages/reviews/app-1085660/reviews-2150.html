I've also seen it multiple times but I want to ask: how the hell is it possible for a new light to be matched with a dude that has flawless or unbroken? It's a great game worth trying if you liked Diablo 2, Far Cry series, Horizon Zero Dawn - but it's always room for improvement and we all want it to succeed.\t\t\t\t\t
