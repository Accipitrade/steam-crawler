\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy the game. At first I didn't want to give it a shot as I was quite the "just play Warframe" ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥.
But a couple of months before Shadowkeep I actually tried it. And MY GOD did I enjoy it!
The universe is somewhat sucking, even the lore! The grind is...well, less than Warframe but at least it's fun.
I also dare to say that even solo-ists such as myself can enjoy the game with people. Here it feels natural. You don't even feel like the game's forcing you to socialise.
The customisation (with or without the cosmetic shop) is also really neat in my opinion! The styles are not too many or too little, and there are A LOT of shaders to try.
Now, if something sticks out for me its how the classes are handled.
Here there are no roles (DPS, Tank, Support), or at least not in the sense that most games use it. here all 3 classes can do these things in a different way. I personally enjoy being a Hunter with a Golden gun that refunds bullets for each enemy (DPS Solar Hunter). Someone may want to become invisible, dodge next to an enemy and then throw a second some to make a teammate invisible (Support Void Hunter). I can only give example with a Hunter because I only play that, but I've seen the other 2 classes in action and they're just as unique!

So overall, I'm surprised by how polished and fun the game is. I recommend it!\t\t\t\t\t
