\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRealistically, its probably the best "Free to Play" game you will ever play. Providing your coming into the game blind with no previous Destiny 1 or 2 play time.

Pros & Cons for a New player:

Pros:
A well optimised game FPS wise and graphically beautiful.
A lot of content with multiple story lines and in game lore.
Insane amounts of different armour sets and weapons with customisation included perks, stats, levels, rarity and cosmetic.
Really good animations and gun play along with fluid character movement and abilties.
Multiple large Open world locations across multiple planets with side quests, patrols, weekly quests and daily bounties.
Great community as well if your look on Bungies website or using their bungie app. Also multiple clans on discord as always.
Large 6 man Raids and small 3 man dungeons.
PvP is good as long as your not a competitive PvP gamer. The game is aimed towards casuals when it comes to PvP game modes.

Cons:
If your really into PvP you will run into cheaters on PC, its a huge problems at the moment. Also they removed skill based matchmaking with will hurt new players. Its great for me dropping 40+ kills per game but their is not protection for new players.

There NetCode within the game needs work. The error codes will drive you insane but with the recent updates these have overall subsided but lag and bad connection is still a problem within PvP.

Basically PvP is the main downfall within this game but that is only because I hate the casual approach they have taking towards PvP.
