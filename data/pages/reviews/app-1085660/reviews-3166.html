\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI personally love Destiny 2. There was a ton of hype surrounding it's release because of Destiny 1. With the release of the game, there was a lack of content, even with the expansions Curse of Osiris and Warmind. However, Foresaken and Shadowkeep have brought much needed changes to the game, letting hardcore players grind to their hearts content while allowing new players to enjoy the game, even as a casual player. Destiny 2 combines the fast paced action of popular titles like COD or CSGO with the Sci-Fi feel of Halo, and also features a unique take on the MMO/Looter-Shooter experience. This is a game which you can lose yourself for hours in the scenery, gunplay, and story. Even after over 1.5k hours, this game still is amazing.

Side note:
This game is best with friends, playing by yourself will not be nearly as fun so keep that in mind.\t\t\t\t\t
