Negatives aside, it is a great game with amazing replay value, great gun-fight, and stunning visuals. Story and lore are extremely interesting but their delivery is lackluster. Characters keep you company through "radio", it'd be awesome if they actually stepped-in more often and aided you in battle (the Vanguard did this at the very beginning and then Osiris did it very briefly later on). It'd be much more immersive and fun if these NPCs joined in battle more often.\t\t\t\t\t
