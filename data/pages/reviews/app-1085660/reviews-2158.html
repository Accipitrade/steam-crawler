\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLook, I love this game. That's why I'm giving it the recommendation. It's got the grind, it's got the fun/fast maneuverability with double jump combos, it's got hundreds of different weapons and weapon types and its got the bugs and memes to go with it.
However, this game requires patience outside of the grind. The PVP meta needs to be less prevalent. The netcode is horrendous and laggy as hell as the game does not have centralised servers. On top of all that there is basically no anti-cheat.
So if you are willing to put yourself through a disgusting playerbase of meta users, disconnects after long queue times and hackers if you somehow make it past the baseline of ♥♥♥♥♥♥ netcode, then give this game a go.
Personally, I can see this game dying due to the DEV team having some kind of strange blindness to their biggest issues. That's sad for me. So I recommend trying the game, for the game's concept, because space aliens with borderline magic powers, shooting eachother with futuristic weapons is awesome.
Not sure I can recommend sticking around for the game itself though. At least for the moment.\t\t\t\t\t
