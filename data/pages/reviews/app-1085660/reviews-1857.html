Since then I've purchased the DLCs and participated in a season pass and I am proud to say that Bungie has made their game much better since the schism. While it's not perfect, no game is. But you can already see that Bungie has tried their damnedest to make this what they always wanted.

It's definitely worth redownloading if you hated the release back in 2013.\t\t\t\t\t
