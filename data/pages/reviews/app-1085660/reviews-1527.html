And there are story elements from 2 years ago that people would love a follow up on that the writers have left at the bottom of the ocean. The only characters that are interesting enough for you to care about are relegated to side roles (Shaxx, Drifter, Saint-14, etc) or are just left as daily quest giving NPCs (Asher Mir, Eris Morn, Ada-1, etc). World building is nice but when most of the writing in a video game is world building, just write a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ book instead at that point.
