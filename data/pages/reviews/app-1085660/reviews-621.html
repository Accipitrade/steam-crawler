\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI hate this ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ game so much. I actually really love it.

Okay but on a serious note, all jokes aside for a second, Destiny 2 is a masterfully crafted game, in terms of progression and gameplay. You've got simple-to-understand statistics and perks on your weapons and armor, making this an RPG title, but then you add fast movement and first person shooter systems, making it an FPS. On top of that, you've got a crazy long and cohesive story, which if you sit down and read about, is one of the best reads in my opinion.

My only gripe with the game is how tricky it may be to get into. In terms of story but also in terms of pure gameplay. Then, you've got the unfortunate content vault. New players will plop down somewhere in Beyond Light or Witch Queen, four to five years ahead from where the story begins. New players won't understand a single thing unless they either watch videos on what happened in the past, or have been playing since Beta Destiny 1 (like me). But if those new players move past that and dive balls deep into the game, level themselves up, and play endgame content like it's nothing, then who am I to judge?

Overall though, good game. I'm not gonna say it's for everyone, but it's definitely a title worth trying if you're into RPG's and FPS's, also if you enjoy space terrorism.\t\t\t\t\t
