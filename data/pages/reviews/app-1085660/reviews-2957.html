\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou probably already know what the game is about (Shooting and Looting with some crazy story in there), so we'll focus on the gameplay itself.

- Great shooting mechanics all around. Few if any games can match it
- Runs great. My computer is pretty old (I5-750 and Radeon HD 7950), yet it runs impressively well on medium settings
- Story is pretty good, but you need to go outside the game in many cases to really understand it.

The main bad point is the endgame, where once you hit 950 light you can no longer advance without doing major activities. I enjoy these, but if they aren't for you then this will be a hard stop. There is also an incredible grind around gear once you hit 950 (and before, but it is more managable) with the random drops and limited drops you can get.

Worth it to play to 950 light at the least, which will take some hours and will make it worth your money!\t\t\t\t\t
