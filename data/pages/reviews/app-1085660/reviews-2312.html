\r\n\t\t\t\t\tObviously I like this game and I've played a lot, but it certainly isn't perfect and can be annoying sometimes. I have played on and off since the game first came out but I'm definitely not a pro or expert and these are just my thoughts.

Pros:
Graphics so pretty wow

Lots of different cool destinations

Runs smoooooth (though i will say it seems to be more bit buggy now than when it was on blizzard)

Dynamic weapons and abilities, lots of awesome combo possibilities, each weapon makes a different sound and has its own unique feel- I've got to where i can know what weapon somebody else is using just by the sound it makes which is pretty cool

Just feels so nice to play, the mechanics are very pleasing other games will feel clunky and and clumsy after playing destiny.

Lots and lots of different activities, you don't really run out of content

Cons:
As a person who has played since destiny 2 first came out, I can't help but feel sad that almost all of the original content is completely buried underneath the new stuff and is now obsolete, to sound like a boomer, it's weird to see newbies hopping around with 750 power when I started from the bottom up lol.

The grind is just unnecessary!! It's impossible to get some random roll weapons and armor no matter how long you play, also I feel like there is a ton of useless boring filler content and some of the tasks become repetitive.

Your work just doesn't pay off. You can work for hours and hours and get lousy weapons.

All in all, you have to play destiny because you like the actual gameplay and activities, but you have to accept that this game can be frustrating and unrewarding.\t\t\t\t\t
