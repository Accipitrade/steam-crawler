\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is a bit of an oddity. The game can hit so many good points but in the same way hit the bad. The grind, while enjoyable with friends to a degree can become insufferable the moment you're forced to work on what you solo. The PvP experience can be fun, and exciting when you start carrying your team or start poppin off. But as quickly as that happened the reverse can happen. you get paired with sweats who have played 2 to the point that ever weapon is a god roll with scummiest loadout they can come up with to slay with. Raids can be a blast when a team together and just rinses the enemy, but if a team doesn't sync get ready to sink hours into your raid. The lore is deep but only to a surface level as its read or heard via exposition dump, never felt. The visual and music are stunning but the world its for is meh at best. Don't get me wrong I love Destiny, but as it stands I don't think it will ever compare to joy I feel when playing Halo.\t\t\t\t\t
