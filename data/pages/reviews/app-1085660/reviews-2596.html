Pros:
* Gun-play is top tier in its genre and is what mostly brings me back to play over and over again.

* Raids are top notch and the best in its genre.
* Forsaken campaign is great.
* Game still looks beautiful and UI is amazing.
* Game does MOSTLY cater to both casual and hardcore players.
Cons:
* New player experience and quest navigation is awful in general.
* PvP and Gambit are awful for new players and deters them away.
* Game doesn't have bad luck protection so you can run 10's or 100's of end game content such as raids or nightfalls for a specific weapon and still not receive.

Game good but new player experience bad\t\t\t\t\t
