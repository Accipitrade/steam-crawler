\r\n\t\t\t\t\t8.5/10

This was my first go with Destiny 2 ever. Was late to the Destiny 1 train as well, and let this game simmer for a bit before I tried it out.

Was a little bummed that I wasn't able to catch up on the original story now that its been removed from the game. Got air dropped into learning that Cayde-6 was dead (whoops)

Started with the forsaken campaign to catch up as much as possible. Really enjoyed it and look forward to catching up on the next campaign.

By the time I was done with Forsaken, I had weaved in enough other stuff (strikes, patrols, ect...) that I hit the soft 1260 power cap in about 20 hours (10 days of gameplay about) It took me a little while to realize this, which was when I finally jumped into the moding/gear upgrading.

Overall, the new player experience was good, but a tad overwhelming. I didn't even realize I could mod my weapons cuz I skipped the into quest that helps you with it. Also, I jumped in the middle of the season. This caused the seasonal stuff to be kinda in the way tbh. Added to the overwhelming feeling, so just stuck with catching up on the story and trying Nightfall Strikes.

My other main criticism is the quest/bounty menu. Im such a frigging completionist, that I reaaaaally struggle with having so many quests all under different categories. By the time I had talked to everyone and snagged all that stuff in the tower for the first time, the quest menu became a pain. Some of those quests are gonna stay there till the season ends, and thats a bit triggering.

Not sure if I'll make it to end game (I suck at crucible) but I've enjoyed everything well enough.

Still confused about armor skins/mods and such. Like it took me forever to reach the armor smith and its super confusing. Ill get to that eventually lul

Would Recommend
