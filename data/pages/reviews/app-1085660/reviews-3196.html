(a personal vehicle that helps you get across the maps faster) armour and weapons the possibilities are near endless.

With 8 planets to free roam at your will and with that 8 coming to a 9 with the moon being added there is so much to do in destiny, there has never been a better time to play this truest breathtaking game.\t\t\t\t\t
