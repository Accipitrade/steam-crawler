Seasonal changes every few months is great for keeping game play from getting stale.

Holiday events are fun and give players new activities to participate in.

PVP could use a little work
Match making could use some work as far as matching players geographically and based on skill.
A lot of times you jump into Gambit or Crucible solo and get pit against a fire team.

RNG could also use some work
Certain items drop so commonly it's frustrating while other items drop so rarely that it's very difficult sometimes to get the item you are after. Mainly pertains to RAID activities.

PVE Match making would be extremely nice for all raids.
It is currently on a few of the raids (kind of), it would be nice if ALL the raids had standard match making queues for any solo players or less than 6 member fire teams.
If solo players could get match made in with fire teams that are a few players short it would really improve community play, it would also help clans find new members that have been playing solo for lack of interaction with other players.
