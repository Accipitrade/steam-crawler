\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI started playing Destiny when it went F2P and since have purchased both expansions. I usually am not a fan of FPS games at all, but Destiny is just so, so good. Especially the gunplay is top tier - and if you're a fan of loot games like Diablo or Borderlands, you'll love Destiny. Everything is just high quality, AAA and it's plain fun to play and hunt equipment!

Pro:

* Great gunplay
* AMAZING art and landscapes. The worlds and mission / environment design is some of the best I have ever seen.
* The minute-to-minute gameplay in general is super fun
* Grinding/getting new weapons and armor, making builds, there's always something to do
* You get A LOT of content for free. I played around 100 hours now and I barely touched the expansions
* You have a ton of different gamemodes. From Crucible (PVP) to Gambit (PVP + PVE) to Strikes, to Forge Ignitions, to various public events, missions, campaigns, quests, etc.
* There are around 300-400 weapons in the game you can go for with different rolls, mods and customization
* You can use "shaders" (a bit more than "coloring" equipment, the shaders also change the material of the gear - e.g. metal to plastic or have various effects) to customize your gear.
* No pay-to-win mechanics or loot.
* Especially Forsaken campaign is really good.

Cons:

* The new player experience is horrible and the quest management isn't the best. You don't get what you're doing, you're getting LOADS of quests to do with no plan on what to do first. I recommend to read some guides, get a clan and ask for help (generally very nice community) or checkout the subreddit.
* The first campaigns "Red War", "Warmind", "Curse of Osiris" (which you get for free) are.. not that memorable or great. Not terrible, but not great either. Forsaken is MUCH better.
* There are some bugs sometimes. Not game breaking, but small bugs happen occasionally with fireteams/matchmaking/starting missions. Leaving the current fireteam and re-inviting your pals always fixes it. Bit annoying, but you can work with it.
* Many cosmetic things are behind a paywall.
* You'll grind your time away without noticing it.\t\t\t\t\t
