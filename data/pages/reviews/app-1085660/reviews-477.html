\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe mechanics of shooting and moving are excellent. It's unfortunate that this is in service of a game that is otherwise incredibly obtuse. Most of the game is not done well, which is probably the result of being crippled by the need to make it work for consoles.

Large swaths of the game (which you must pay to access) are regularly removed. This includes guns, armour, maps/dungeons.

The inventory is laughably bad. Inventory management doesn't exist in game at all and needs to be done on a phone app.

The UI in general is garbage. It feels like the only goal was to make it simple for controllers.

Virtually all progress is time-gated, with plenty of daily/weekly quests and lockouts. I personally find it toxic when a game punishes the player for taking any time off.

The claim that it is an MMO makes me laugh. It is just a regular multiplayer game with (mostly) seamless matchmaking. You cannot play with more than 6 people (whether grouped or solo). Some PvP is 6v6 for a technical 12. You generally can't join your friend's group if they are in the main social area because the Tower instance is population capped at 10ish people which prevents you from joining. You can never leave a group without aborting whatever you are doing and being kicked back to orbit.

The game is full of bugs. Missions will randomly soft-lock (enemies fail to spawn), in case you were enjoying yourself.

I give it a 5/10, which is approximately the same as the ratio of time I am enjoying myself to time it is making me angry. It's definitely possible to have fun, but on the balance the headaches are just not worth it.

I recommend skipping it.\t\t\t\t\t
