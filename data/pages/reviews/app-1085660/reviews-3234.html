====
1.\tA "Free to play" version with massive content (excluding Shadowkeep and Forsaken campaigns).

2.\tFlashy guns, especially the Exotics.

3.\tBeautiful background / sceneries.

Bad
====
1.\tLFG needed for Raid, Dungeon, or any non-matchmaking activities.

2.\tSeasonal Title, too much for casual players; Non-seasonal players might experience the dryness after trying almost everything unless you like the repeatable activities (Strike, Gambit, Pvp).

3.\tResource Economy - Ascendant Shards tied to non-matchmaking high difficulty Nightfall.

Overall
======
This game is suitable those that love Loots (like Cayde), as well as the “Endless Grinding” for more loots, title, Exotics, bounties, quests.\t\t\t\t\t
