-Good shooting gameplay pvp and pve wise
-A lot of areas different planets to explore
-Lots of interesting weapons and armors but they are not as crazy as in Borderlands
-Lots of things to do from activities, to pve events, to dungeons etc.
-Cool Bikes to ride
-No pay to win. Game story only has vanity items

Cons:
-Story wise I would say 3 out of 5 campaigns are not great
-Very very grindy tasks to obtain gear that are not worth spending time on, imo
-Many planets look samey. Extremely toxic community full of cheaters and trolls
-Not acceptable UI for 2020 (you cannot sort items by types in vault, for example)
-Not intuitive about many things like certain quests and descriptions tend to be inaccurate
-Peer to peer so you gotta open ports and then once in a while you will get a disconnect still but not as bad as Red Dead Online
-Not enough variety in enemies but they are not too bad.
-I could go on...

Despite outweighing amount cons, I still like it. Try to play it casually, do not ruin your life by getting all the gear and then seeing it nerfed or lvled down.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is pretty good and is becoming an great fps + rpg game. The main problem with the game is how dry it becomes toward the end of a season. Most people have done everything they wanted to and are now waiting for new content. New players should have a lot of fun for a while, that is if they can understand the UI and progression/quests system. Another problem with Destiny is how co op based it seems. If you have a team everything will be so much easier. Endgame is pretty much just raids which is bad because there are so many activities to do and besides farming for good weapons, Raids are impossible without a group to play with and that causes people to resort to LFG which isn't necessarily a bad thing but their are a lot of people who wont play with you unless you have a certain gun or use a certain class. Now that brings me to balance, the game is not balanced, now it is probably the most balanced it's ever been but mainly pvp is the same weapons again and again. Competitive is terrible, its people abusing the most broken abilities and weapons as well as a good amount of cheaters, if you are good at the game tho, it can still be super fun, or if you use all the op stuff. If you grind for the meta guns and high roll armor than pvp will be very fun. It is also fun just playing with friends. Balance in pve isn't as bad but the main dps weapons are all pretty hard to get and nothing really comes close to those guns. Destiny can be very fun but also super annoying. The game has some bugs but I can't think of many that are game breaking and are not patched fast. This sounds like a rant but i still love the game and I keep coming back. people who've played destiny since 2014 still play today and the game will keep getting better. Destiny 2 is not a very casual game but if you learn the game and know the meta and grind for the good gear, than you will keep coming back even if its only when a new season starts or when a new exotic quest comes out. Destiny will keep you playing

art: 9/10 great. If you have a good PC this game will look awesome.

sound: 9/10 Incredible. The music is incredible but Destiny 1 had a better soundtrack. The voice acting is also very well done.

story: 7/10 good. People don't play Destiny for the story but it does have a very large and ongoing story that probably wont make sense to new players that have not played the first game, but if you follow the lore than you will know how much thought and writing goes into this game.

combat: 7/10 good. A lot guns and abilities are not very viable tho. Pvp will always have a strict meta that will be hard to win if not used, but The gun play is nice and all the abilities are super fun to use.

endgame: 4/10 bad. The only way to get pinnacle gear is through raids and dungeons, both get boring doing the same thing over and over again without variation. If every activity had a way to give pinnacle gear, the endgame would be fantastic

Replayability: 5/10 average. once you get to the level cap, pvp is the only thing that is fun and has variety, but even that will get old once you are at max comp rank

conclusion: Destiny is a game that is not for casual play, but if you invest into the game and have friends to play with, it can be super fun at times
overall: 6.8/10 pretty good\t\t\t\t\t
