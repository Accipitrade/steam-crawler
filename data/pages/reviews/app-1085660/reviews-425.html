The rewards this game give you might feel lackluster since most weapons you want will require a combination of perks to be considered "good" but if you are very casual like i am any gun would be able to get you through the playlist activities with matchmaking. However, PVP wise i feel like its a very pay to win aspect since most seasonal meta exotic would mostly be behind paywalls.

Gameplay feels very simple to understand just that more complicated stuff like hot swap and mod combinations to make a build in the game may required a lot more like what weapons work with the build and what is the sequence to do your max dmg combo.

Fashion is true endgame, and there's a lot of shaders(colour schemes) to fit your armour and weapons. and now that you can transmog armour pieces allows for better and easier designing. although exotics armour have limited options some exotic ornaments look absolutely stunning.

Overall the game can be quite addicting and its very much better with friends for both PVE and PVP parts of the game and if you can get a group of 6, go and attempt the raids. they will require alot more coordination than just mindless shooting in playlist activities.\t\t\t\t\t
