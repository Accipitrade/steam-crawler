Review
Best feeling shooter I've played, with light mmo aspects (dungeons/raids/gear farm). Great PvE & mostly great PvP. Gorgeous art design & sound is fantastic. Due to how the content vaulting works, you'll miss a lot of story, still worth playing without it because it feels so damn good.

Gameplay revolves heavily around creating builds and getting good gear for those builds. This can be pretty involved and will take new players a while to get used to. Find someone who knows what they're doing and ask them. The game has easy -> raid level PvE encounters, there's something for everyone. PvP is good, but is in a fair amount of upheaval pretty regularly due to changes in balancing, mechanics, and matchmaking changes. PvP has it's own build ecosystem, so another layer of stuff to look out for.

GUNS. LOTS OF GUNS. WEIRD GUNS, BIG GUNS, SHINY GUNS, GUNS THAT ARE OTHER THINGS TOO. SHOOTY GO BOOM MAKE ME SMILE *cough* the loot game is great :)

Story is fine but I mostly engage on the level of character interaction, a lot of the background went over my head because I missed a bunch of old content that I can't replay. Some seasonal story has been fantastic, some has been mediocre. You probably won't play this for the story if I'm honest, but it's there and it's pretty good.

Purchasing
Try this game for free, see if you like how the gameplay feels. If you do, I'd recommend you buy the latest deluxe edition (Lightfall, at time of writing), so that you get a full year's worth of seasonal content. Older expansions are worth having but I would say most people would say not at full price now because of how content vaulting and the seasonal model works.

Story Caveat
If you're joining as a new player, you're best off thinking of this as a looter shooter with light mmo elements. It will be very difficult for you to fully engage with the story, without you spending a lot of time outside the game catching up. The writing and characters are done well and are enjoyable as they are, you'll just be missing a lot of background.
