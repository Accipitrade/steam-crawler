\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAccording to timewastedondestiny, I've played this game for approximately 215 hours. Destiny 1 for a little over 400 hours.
The Good:
Gunplay. Hands down, the best gunplay I have ever experienced in any FPS. The different guns feel unique, are satisfying to use, and have great designs. This is the best part of the game. Movement is fluid as well.
Raids: All of the raids and raid lairs in this game are well thought out and fun to play.
Power Fantasy: The game rides the line between being borderline overpowered while still offering challenging encounters.
Exotic quests: while some are a huge pain (izanagi's) there are quite a few memorable exotic quests, such as the one for Last Word.
The Decent:
Story: Destiny's story has never been great. It's been serviceable. D2 is a step up from D1, best it still won't blow your mind. The one exception is Forsaken, which is absolutely fantastic.
Endgame content: Getting past 960ish is a pain in the ass and kind of boring. Your only options are Nighfalls, GoS raid, and Iron Banner. Gets stale after a while.
Crucible: While many tweaks have been implemented that have improved PVP, it's still an unpolished mess in a lot of ways. Meta or bust for comp. Does not encourage unique playstyles or loadouts.
The bad:
Eververse. All of the cool cosmetics are locked behind and absurdly high paywall. Nothing cool looking unlockable in game through challenges/gameplay. Bungie, please make bright dust more availible through challenging activities such as Garden of Salvation and other raids, 950/980 nighfalls, and going up Glory ranks in comp. Additionally, transmog for all legendary gear should be a thing.
Balancing: D2 has a perpetual problem of balancing guns. Meta loadouts (izanagi's/recluse/Wendigo) are almost required for endgame activities. More diversity of loadouts would be appreciated.
Irrelevant activities and gear: Old raids, menagerie, and other fun activities no longer dole out powerful rewards. These are super fun, but not worth doing any more. Additionally, older armor sets should be given Y3 mod slots so they can be considered viable. This however, could be fixed with transmog for all legendary sets.

Overall, this is a solid looter shooter with fantastic FPS mechanics. If you don't like looters, don't buy. If you don't like grinding, don't buy. Eververse sucks.\t\t\t\t\t
