\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright so I have about 616 hours in this game starting as a New Light player most of which was from afk farming for seasonal events i have decided to write a review.

Here are the pros and cons i have seen so far

PROS:
The lore is interesting

You have an actual end game like raids

Bugs make some (not all) of the grind faster/ not a pain aka lots of cheese

Fun game play alone or with friends

Has something for both pvp and pve players

Fast level grind to end game content (you almost hit the soft cap just by doing the three story dlc's as a new light player)

Ways to show people you have no life while still working full time and going to university full time

Cosmetic only in game store so no pay to win

Tons of guides on the internet if you get lost

You don't have to be min max to do most content

You can do some nutty stuff and theory craft builds

If you are a number cruncher like me you have a lot of ways to min max or make builds

You have access to some of the best pvp load outs and good enough pve load outs in the game as a new light player
You can do all of the three main game modes as a new light player

Active clans and lfg

Good third party apps like destiny item manager to make life easy

No trading everything has to be earned aka no bad economy or ptw problems/ currency trading

You don't need to hit the hard cap

All crucible gambit and strike weapons are not tied to dlc

People will carry you through hard quests if you ask in the right places

CONS:
This game is held together with duct tape and super glue and moves with a huge amount of dw-40 some thing is always breaking in this game and it has a ton of bugs some help the player some don't

You need to look up guides if no one is teaching you this game or you will have no idea what to do

You need discord or some other third party app to find random players or a clan to do stuff that doesn't have matchmaking

You will run in to toxic players every now and then in lfg (I have only had this issue every now and then and only with streamers because it gets views i guess and that's fine with me you go get that bread but I'm not playing with you. Others have told me they run into it all the time )

If you don't have friends or a clan you will have to run a meta load out to get lfgs for end game content

The in game store is over priced but is cosmetic only and you can get cosmetics for in game currency but that is a pain to grind or you have to wait a season for it to randomly drop form a engram and very few cosmetics can be earned other ways

Most of the base armor looks like garbage the ones that look good are a pain to grind and if you do grind them it's almost impossible to get a high stat roll

This game has a lot of rng most things are easy to get but to get them with good perks or high stats are a pain some things make it less of a pain but it can take days to weeks to get good non exotic items

Most exotics are trash but they get broken every now and become good but they get fixed back to being trash but all are fun to use

You need dlc to hit the hard cap in any reasonable amount of time

Things are time gated if you miss it you miss it this includes lore and items

From season to season the content can be dry if there is no dlc

Most new items are repeats or a minor change to and old item

Long times between patches takes a long time to fix stuff or nerf/buff stuff can be good by giving you time to do/use stuff that is bugged in a good way or can be bad by not letting you do/use stuff that is bugged in a bad way

This game is not optimized for pc at all but it is slowly being fixed patch to patch

you are locked out of dlc nightfalls which means if you can only get gear needed to reach the hard cap if the nightfall is non dlc that week

You are locked out of the mid skill tree on all classes and sub classes if you don't have dlc making it so some people won't lfg with you if you have a nightfall you can do

You are locked out of the two meta weapons for high level nightfalls if you don't have dlc making it so some people won't lfg with you

The story can get bland if you play the dlc after it's release period "very much a you had to be there"

Zavala & Ikora

TL;DR: Over all this game is great and i highly recommend it. It has it's gripes but they are slowly being fixed. The Player base is pretty chill but can sometimes be toxic. You need dlc if you want to do all the cool pve stuff but completely unnecessary for pvp.\t\t\t\t\t
