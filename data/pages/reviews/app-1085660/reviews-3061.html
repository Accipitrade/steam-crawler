\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has been around for a while, so I doubt that this review is mind-blowing, BUT I WILL DO MY BEST!

The game-play and gunfeel of this game are bar none some of the best on the market. This isn't due to the story, but rather the framework/engine that Destiny uses. There are a lot of great things this does right. The quests feel solid, the world feels complete, and the story-line is compelling. That being said - there are some challenges. You seemingly rush through the non-DLC content and it gets a little confusing if you don't do them in the right order with NPCs suddenly existing again or being talked about as if they were gone.

I had already played through this game prior to it coming to STEAM - so nothing was lost for me, but I warn you... if you are a story person - read the release dates on the DLCs before you jump in or it'll be weird.

Since they pushed everyone's light level up - you kind of rush through the DLCs. The main bosses aren't too difficult because you so outlevel everything. But don't worry - the DLCs aren't needed if you want to play the latest expansions - they are just there to help you level / gear and get some lore.

The latest expansion (ShadowKeep) is AMAZING. I loved it quite a bit. The story is wired in nicely, the bosses feel difficult and the lore rolls out nicely. The gearing is quite a bit of fun - and you will likely find yourself sucked in a little. The PVP element is just... terrible. Sorry, I'll never like Destiny PVP - so it might be me, but it is so out of balance and weird. If you have a super-high light level and 2 friends, PVP is fun. If not... good luck being farmed. :)\t\t\t\t\t
