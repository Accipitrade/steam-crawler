\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's hard for me not to recommend Destiny, but in its current state I can't truthfully say you should spend money on this game. I only have 80 hours on steam, but on xbox my Destiny 1 and 2 hours are probably nearing the thousands. If you want to enjoy Destiny 2 you can do so F2P for maybe 30-50 hours, but you you really aren't playing Destiny. To unlock the full experience you have to spend a minimum of $50 on a game that came out years ago. The graphics are good the run and gun looter shooter aspect is fun, but the cons at this point completely outweigh anything good I can say about it. Content is limited and the "new content" they bring in is typically just reskinned versions of old content. The story is utter garbage. 99% of the time all you will do is follow a quest marker and kill things. The crucible is littered with cheaters now that the game is F2P, and The season pass is just there to take your money and give you exclusive gear you don't need. When Bungie split from activision everybody thought Destiny 2 was going to comeback and really make a stand. It hasn't happened yet, and the fact that Bungie is practically ignoring veteran players is unexcusable. This is not Destiny 1. Do not expect it to be Destiny 1. Bungie is driving this game into the ground, and they are relying on us to keep it from crashing.\t\t\t\t\t
