First the network issues have been alleviated a ton in my region so the game now runs stable 99% of the time.

(I did previously not reommend this game because of the numerous networking issues at launch and the poor newbie experience).

Now that's sorted out lets talk about the experience for new players, it totally sucks to be perfectly honest, maybe not if you come from the first game but as a brand new player having purchased Shadowkeep I ended up playing Shadowkeep campaign BEFORE the previous 3 (4 if you count Forsaken which I don't own).

The campaigns are tucked away at the ship vendor under Legacies which you have to manually take. This is pretty sad as this is pretty decent content (curse of osiris might be sub-par if you have paid for it but as free content it is good).

The content as a free to play is pretty good but I feel the shadowkeep campaign to be a little on the pricey side considering what you have in the base game, I would wait until you get a discount personally (at the current Forsaken price it would be a better deal).

I am still happy with my purchase and Destiny 2 is a game you might end up sinking countless hours into so I do recomend it.\t\t\t\t\t
