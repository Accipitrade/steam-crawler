\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst things first, this game is horrible for new players, genuinely terrible, particularly if you go free 2 play (this game is not really f2p given the content it offers, think of it more like a free demo or trial). There's no real structure or guide on what to do, all the activities are old and have no real leveling structure. You'll need to do a lot of research and ideally have an experienced player guide you.

However

Once you've broken through the wall, and bought the DLCs the game opens up to an extremely fun mmo/fps hybrid. I particularly recommend you play the raids, you'll need to find a group yourself either with friends or through an LFG website/discord. But the raids are the best and most rewarding and interesting content I think I've ever played in a game.

Right now the pvp is not in a good spot and won't be for the foreseeable future, however when it works IT WORKS.

The game has a more casual feel difficulty wise, though it is quite grindy, its more about doing different activities and getting some loot in a more laid back sense than it is a proper MMO grind. Unless you are a hardcore player ofc.

Recently they have added a lot of build-crafting options which are very fun and spice up gameplay a fair bit, with more changes coming soon when lightfall releases.

Overall I would say that the game is extremely difficult to get into, but once you do you will find it extremely fun, particularly when played with friends. And I also highly recommend you look up a synopsis of the story so far and buy The Witch Queen and/or Beyond Light DLC.\t\t\t\t\t

Update after update, season after season i wanted D2 to reach it full potential but it never did. There is alot to enjoy in D2 and if you can find something you love more power to you i just cant with good conscience recommend this game to anyone i know and i wish i never started playing it in the first place. I could go into what really is wrong with the game but there is just too much to cover.
Ill list a few here: 1. Skill based match making in every PVP game mode with a really bad algorithm. 3. Ability spam in PVP. 4. Lack of vision and support for PVP. 5. Strange balance in PVE where guns are there to shoot marshmallows at the enemies while your abilities cool down. 6. Ability-centered PVE where there really is no power fantasy focused mainly on gunplay. 7. Reissuing old D2 and D1 content as new content. 8. Lacklustre optimisation (for what the game looks like). 9. Over monetisation. 10. Overpriced expansions when looking at total content they contain.

Note: (2023/02/01) Credit where credit is due, Bungie has reverted some of the movement nerfs in PVP and has reworked to AE system. This is a step in the right direction.

Sorry Bungie but this game keeps getting worse and worse.

The campaign i cannot comment on as i was not one to by Lightfall however, from the first mission it doesnt seem to be on the same level as WQ was. The Dungeon pass is yet another pain point so i feel that this game is worth shelving for the time being. Maybe final shape will bring better more innovative content. Nice try Bungy but i think you lost the plot some were along the line.\t\t\t\t\t
