\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThank you Bungie, for helping me stop my addiction.


I have played this game religiously over the last few years. It came out when I was in Uni and after playing just once on a friends PS4 I fell in love with it.
Bought it as soon as I was able to and had more than 2k hours on battle.net and then 450+ hours on steam after the port.
Most of my current online friends are all from Destiny. I have met some really cool people and created a lot of fun memories through it. I have seen guys get married and becomes fathers all while playing this game.


BUT, I quickly realized that it was unhealthy. Maybe I just have an addictive personality but I could not put this damn game down.
I even lost a very good job opportunity because of it. Seeing that new content drop was like getting a fresh dose injected straight into my heart.

I missed holidays, family gatherings, birthdays, friend meet-ups, just because I wanted to play a little more of good ol' destiny.

And then one day, I just stopped. The hackers, the lack of content, the repetition FINALLY got to me and my friends. We all decided to stop together.

It was good for close to 8 months. We tried dozens of others games until a few weeks ago when out of no where one of them jumped back into the game, "just to see if it had changed".

It had not, but that did not stop him and almost all others from joining him as well. They started playing every single day just like before, the clan was alive again. Guys I hadnt seen in over a year popped back up.

They gathered and played once again, complained, and farmed the exact same old sh*t day and night. Whined every time so much as touched crucible, but through all that noise I could see time turning backwards.

It was like a sirens call coaxing me back into hell, towards a most certain doom.

My steam timeline became full of people preordering the witch queen dlc and I knew deep within my heart that I would not be able to stay true. That I would inevitably return once again to the tower, back into Zavala's warm embrace.

It beckoned me with the same old Destiny 1 reskined exotic drops with almost zero new content other than a handful of cutscenes and reused models, and I was okay with it.

That is until i saw the moth*rf*ck*ng price.
Are you people out of your damn mind? Who the F*CK charges THAT much for THIS little content THIS often?
Its like the 5th time I've had reused content shoved down my throat in the name of "DLCs".
I could (and will) buy full AAA titles in the same price instead.

So thank you for proving me right, bungie. Thank you for showing me that no matter how much time passes, you won't change. Nothing in life is certain other than a company's greed.
Thank you for giving me a swift kick to the groin so I could see the reality.
Hope you all burn in the deepest pits of hell, you greedy c*nts.\t\t\t\t\t
