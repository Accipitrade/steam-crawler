While there used to be much more free content available there's still plenty to do and there are many sales on DLC if you get bored of that. Destiny has an engaging story spanning many years which allows new players to be eased into. The unique way this game is set up makes it easy to connect to other players. It is open world with many different destinations. Destiny 2 balances PvP with PvE in that you have to go out and earn your weapons. This creative system makes it so that your never tethered to one activity and can take your loadout anywhere. The PvP is pretty sweaty but easy to get the hang of. Gambit is a game mode that combines PvP and PvE if thats more what your after too. So, weather if you're a new player or a sweat theres plenty to enjoy. There's something here for everyone but be warned it can feel a grindy at times but I think that adds to the weight of reward. With destiny 2's open world hundreds of free activities and weakly updates it almost seems too good to be true. Bungie has created a community of what I find are very welcoming people who will often work with you if you ask and of course you'll be running into them on the daily pretty much anywhere you go.\t\t\t\t\t

We STILL have overpriced dlcs that add a few hours of content and require you to grind for hours just to play the actually interesting content (raids) that most people won't want to play anyway due to their extreme difficulty There is STILL no way to change the difficulty of missions, and the difficulty is extremely easy (why is there such a difficulty jump between solo content and raids???). The biggest thing we've learned is that the darkness takes the form of foreboding triangles. But I often get the sense that even Bungie has no idea where they are taking their story, they very clearly don't want to commit to anything and leave everything open-ended (over 6 years of content too). Some of the characters' lines are really, really bad, which makes them difficult to relate to, especially when they take themselves so seriously.\t\t\t\t\t
