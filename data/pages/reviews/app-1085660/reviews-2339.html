\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter 231 hours, i can say some things. Yes, this game has it's own magic.
The tons of weapons, armors, items, ships, sparrows and cosmetics (Shaders) are mostly farmable, but other cosmetics (universal weapon and armor arnaments, emotes etc.) buyable with farmable Bright Dust and Buyable Silver for a acceptable price.
The Destiny's world is great have tons of huge maps where you can fight with that six species.
In short:
-Cabal: Heavily armored type enemies with huge and slow weapon. They have snipers, dogs, big shielded "You will not kill me with one-shot. Oh sh*t he has a rocket launcher!" and MORE bigger super heavy bosses.
-Hive: Tipical ranged and Melee rusher type enemies with some slow explosive "Do not melee them" mobs
-Vex: An ancient robotic race. Mostly they have smaller and bigger (Giant minotaur) ranged and air units. "BE careful with the one-eyed (one-shotter in some mission/raids) cyclops"
-Fallen: Ranged agile scavangers with some robotic big balls and flying one shotable bugs.
-Scorn (Forsaken Required): these bad guys are ranged and melee. Explosive rusher enemies ahead, don't let them jump on you.
-Taken: Others species combined here (except Scorn) just in fully black edition. "Be careful with the taken phalanx when you are standing at the edge of a gap.
The game has a tons of activity: missions, quests and seasonal quests, raids, PVP and plus weekly fun match rotations, Gambit and it's prime edition and Strikes.
So yes this game has a lot to offer in gaming but... This GAME has a nice atmosphere with beatiful places, characters are well designed and fully voiced who gives daily and weekly bounties and huge missions what are challenging enough to play a few hours for it and at last the Campaigns are made the game whole, the story of the Red War, Warmind, Curse of Osiris, <Forsaken and Shadowkeep> (DLC required) are having a Cinematic quest line what gives you a not bad storytelling.
So long story short: I love this game. It gived me a lot of fun and i will not stop playing with it.
10/10 for me.
Last thing: PVP can be a bit balanced finally, some weapons are... critically breaking the laws of the Ranked.
For some reason... i still go back to this game...\t\t\t\t\t

But it is a good game overall, compared with other FPS games. Guns feel great, there is a lot of things to do (though this is a downside sometimes too).

What it really needs to me, is to refocus away from the typical AAA Garbage, and have enough time away from big Publishers to git gud... AKA, get away from the same garbagio that typical AAA companies crank out these days. More game, less fluff, less ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ you about.

I think Destiny 2 can get there, and Bungie may be willing to do so. But the game is good enough as is, just has a lot more promise if Bungie goes back to listening to their player base more, and actually understanding the challenges of people who actually play their game.\t\t\t\t\t
