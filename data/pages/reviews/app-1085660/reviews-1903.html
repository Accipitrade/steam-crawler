\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's definetly worth a play but you can burn yourself out fairly quickly if you constantly play. I played probably far too much Destiny1 on Xbox, Destiny2 on PS4, and now more Destiny2 on PC.

It's more of a game where you play on/off whenever you feel like it. But it's ironic that the game design isn't based around this because certain quests and especially content falls out of favour (or outright powercreeped) so it becomes harder to find people for content older than the most relevant release. The newer content is also very counter-intuitive to this because it's designed around desperately trying to get you to play somewhat consistently which can end up feeling more like a chore.

TL;DR - Don't treat it like a job and feel free to stop playing whenever you want. Don't let the game try to dictate for you.\t\t\t\t\t
