\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start out by saying this game is not for everyone. This is a game that has some of the most beautiful scenery I've ever seen in a game and possibly my favorite character in gaming period, that being Cayde-6 (may he rest in peace). But you have to live for the grind because this game is full of it. Exotic quests in this game tend to take a while to complete with some tasks taking a couple hours of Crucible to complete. This isn't inherently bad but if you are someone who gets inpatient easily this will make you want to pull out your hair. Another thing is this game is best enjoyed with people, preferably friends. When I first started out playing I really only had 2 friends that played and they weren't on much so I ended up playing my first 100 or so hours alone. This really sucks the fun out of the game as the only thing you can really do completely solo (like not using LFG) is the PvP modes and campaigns. This just makes the game feel bland and like a grind fest just to get a decent roll on a weapon so you can use it in Crucible. Now finding people to do raids with isn't horribly hard thanks to LFG; however, raids are best experienced with friends yelling at you because you did the encounter wrong and now you need to wipe and being deemed the team ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. The story of this game is actually really cool even though most of it comes from lore rather than the Campaigns. Overall this is a very good game and I highly recomend trying it out if you are unsure about it (its free so what can you lose?).

Edit: Considering the time I have put in this game, its alright\t\t\t\t\t
