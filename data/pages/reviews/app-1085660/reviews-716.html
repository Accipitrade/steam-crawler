\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is by far my favorite game. if you've never played, don't be overwhelmed and skip just because there's a lot of content for it. the community is awesome and LFG'ers are generally kind to newcomers and there are a ton of people willing to help you out.

off the cuff positives:
- Light/Dark Abilities make you feel like a God-killing force of nature. Power fantasies for the titan warlock and hunter classes synergize well with the available abilities for each class. Tank, Caster/glass cannon, Rogue

- If you love a deep and engaging story, Destiny has it. check out Lore content creators on youtube like myelin games and my name is byf.

-if you like gunplay, destiny has it. Destiny probably has the best feeling gunplay, creative weapon design, variety and overall fun factor out of any shooter i've played.

The graphics are beautiful and customization options are amazing. when you unlock a cosmetic from doing a challenging activity, it makes you feel proud and badass and other people recognize it

Amazing community - there's a weekly report called "This week at Bungie" or "TWAB" (Twahb) that drops every thursday. having a weekly communication from the devs makes the game feel more engaging. there are also annual charity events where hundreds of thousands of dollars are raised and donated to sick kids every year.

- fun fact, its the only game I know of that incorporates dying/respawning into the lore of the game. the dev's are intentional about immersion

a few points of criticism:
PVP needs some attention, but the Dev's have shared their vision for where they want it to go and I am excited to see it develop.

- it sometimes takes a while for the Dev's to patch issues. the non-game breaking bugs add to destiny's charm.

I have been playing destiny for many years and I plan to play for many years to come. Eyes up, Guardian!\t\t\t\t\t
