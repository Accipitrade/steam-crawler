\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's ok.

No but seriously. I got the game when New Light dropped (played vanilla D1 and dropped it, no other Destiny experience) after burning out on Warframe. I think Bungie really nailed the moment to moment gameplay experience and managing how leveling and endgame interact. What I mean is, instead of how Warframe just scales everything infinitely, Destiny has a "soft cap" on all activities so that no matter how high a level you are, functionally you can only be 30 light level above the activity level, meaning you can never really just steamroll through content by breathing.

Big world and lore too. I still wish more of it were presented in game but it's so very much better than D1's "I don't even have time to explain why I don't have time to explain." It's the most current sci fi game that delivers anything like an emotionally investing storyline and characters like in Mass Effect or Titanfall 2.

Do what I did, grab the free to play version and give it a spin, if you like it and want more, grab the expansions. The Forsaken expansion alone is a full game's worth of content.\t\t\t\t\t
