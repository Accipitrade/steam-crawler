\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore I start, I just want to say that I have no issues with the game itself.
The gunplay is fun and exciting. The story (even though quite a bit of it has been removed) is interesting as well. Its like a good mashup of Halo and CoD, with a bit of the mythical and magical side of Star Wars.

The reason why I am leaving a negative review is because I have not been able to enjoy or play this title. While other players are able to enjoy the game, I am always reverted back to the main menu because Destiny 2 fails to find my internet connection. This issue never happens with any other online multiplayer game I play. Even after changing my ethernet cable, I am still getting the same results.

I do not believe this issue is on my end of things. My PC was able to run this game fine during its launch. And I have never messed with the files or anything, other than reinstalling the game after not playing it for a while.

If Bungie fixes this issue, I will change this to a positive review.\t\t\t\t\t
