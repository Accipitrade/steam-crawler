\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLots of content for everybody. There are 3 base classes which are Hunter, Titan, and Warlock which are all great for their own reason so I would make and play all 3. The base story is really good for people into lore and like finding themselves immersed in the world. If you like PvP then there's a whole section for that with the base competitive game mode or other modes that rotate each week with even fun modes like Scorch, Mayhem, or Momentum Control. Then there's lots of PvE content out there for all types of players. If you like puzzles or harder activities that requires teamwork then raids are there for you. There are also many other matchmade activities like Strikes, Seasonal content like Menagerie, Reckoning, and Forges. There's even in between with Gambit which is PvEvP where you fight ai enemies and sometimes you can go to the other team to kill them or one might come to kill you and at the end you need to be the first team to kill the boss. There are many quests for exotics or pinnacle/ritual gear which are the highest tier weapons and armor in the game that you can farm for. I have many hours on PC and Xbox and I still love playing this game so I recommend playing it to anyone who likes first person shooters and the MMO playstyle to play more and get more powerful. This game should be game of the year 2019.\t\t\t\t\t
