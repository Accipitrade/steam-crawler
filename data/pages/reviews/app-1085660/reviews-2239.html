\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is most fun with friends, as a primarily solo player I've learned that fairly quickly but even then I manage to find plenty of enjoyment with matchmaking activities and LFG when I need it. However, this is a game that for most will require a lot of time and dedication to in order to progress.

As of the release of Beyond Light, i must be honest. If you're looking to get into this game as a free to play player I don't think this will provide much satisfaction. However, if you have friends willing to guide you through and maybe if you buy at least Forsaken then you might start to see why so many people still enjoy this game.

As of the release of Witch Queen i must say that i think this is a good starting point for people looking for good content/gameplay. There's so much good, challenging content to be enjoyed here and the story, especially for those of us familiar with the lore, is fantastic. This has been building since Beyond Light and now its as great as its ever been.

As of Lightfall, the story was rough. That first week, everything was on fire. The community was in an uproar and honestly rightfully so. This expansion was nothing more than a dragged out version of Beyond Light. Everything is about strand, learn strand, play with strand and so on but where Beyond Light kept me engaged with the Bray story and being our first real introduction to the darkness Lightfall just fell flat. Sure, the post campaign stuff is really good, from partitions and quests it all added to what was otherwise just nothing. I had hope for season 20, it started off pretty strong but after what happened with Amanda it just lost alot of goodwill. I get if this year is going to be all about loss but the way it went about it just seemed so rushed at the end and incoherent just like the whole of the Lightfall campaign. In terms of gameplay, Strand is great once you have it unlocked and have built around it the Legendary Campaign while almost completely devoid of any worthwhile narrative beats was a good challenge. This "expansion" is worth it for strand alone, but pick it up on a sale, don't buy it at retail and especially if you're not planning on buying the annual pass. Season 21 looks promising but well, we've been made fools of before. Guess we'll have to wait and see.\t\t\t\t\t
