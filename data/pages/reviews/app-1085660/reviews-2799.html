Rating differss with combinations =>

Not recommended if solo.
Overall piece of crap without DLC (3/10)
DLC is meh due to its crazy price and what it unlocks (2/10)

Recommended with friends.
Awesome with a consistent group of friends (8/10)
- Funny jumping puzzles (watch friends rage and die)

Game's mechanics work fairly good in PvP, not sure about balance though due to a large scale of unique weapons and armors with upgrades (Rewards people who grind hard instead of pure skill unlike CSGO?).

PvE (Dungeons) is very complex and requires looking up YouTube videos at minimum, which adds challenge and fun with the right group. "Look for group" works alright with the Destiny 2 companion app. The app itself works surprisingly well even while you're in game ( swapping weapons in and out of storage using app, instant transfers while in-game).

The grind (maybe 4 hours / week) is not that bad to increase power levels in-game (Acceptable for a full time working person).
You need a high power level to participate in Dungeons at an acceptable difficulty.
The grind is very repetitive, just replaying the same campaign bosses with different difficulties (strikes).

The micro-transaction prices are really ridiculous, costs more than the DLCs itself, and are just skins =|
Like... $15 to $50 for weird stuff then people don't even see often in PvP or whatever.
Meh, you aren't forced to buy, so at least its available if you like being special and stuff.\t\t\t\t\t
