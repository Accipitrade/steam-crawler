\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is in a good place these days. The only thing that really bugs me about it is the aggressive monetization, but for the most part, what you get when you spend money is worth it (aside from the Eververse, but that's all cosmetic anyway). If you're entirely new to the game (ie, F2P), I'd suggest just getting Shadowkeep and worrying about Forsaken later if you feel like going back to experience the story. You will be a bit confused in terms of what's going on, but you'll have everything you need to reach the end game relatively quickly. Forsaken also has some neat side classes for the three main ones that you will probably want at some point, but I'd wait for it to go on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
