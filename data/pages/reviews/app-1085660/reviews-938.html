\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDESTIYN 2:
Destiny 2 is one of the best MMO loot shooters out there. Following it's predecessors strong performance, it's finally available on PC. The game had a decent release back in 2017 with bugs and loot problems. By the time 'The Forgotten' expansion (the best expansion in my opinion) rolled in the game had been well polished and looked very much like the successor of Destiny. I joined Destiny 2 just before Forgotten's release, so I never experienced the early problems of launch.

BEYOND LIGHT:
Separating from Activision was the best move for Bungie (the underwhelming 'Shadowkeep' expansion shows that). Otherwise we would have got Beyond Light as Destiny 3. The dark side powers is a welcome change though the sunsetting of contents is kind of a down side even if understandable. Right now too much off the content, especially weapons, have been removed from the game limiting player choice. No doubt by the time 'The Witch Queen' expansion rolls out we will have more content to enjoy.

Above all else it's FREE!!! So what are you waiting for? Try the game now. You will absolutely love it. Free players also get access to every new planet that is released. Go and conquer the darkness!\t\t\t\t\t
