\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo clarify some misconceptions: This game is free to play but do consider that a demo. There will be little to do as a Free to Play player and if you like what you see, know that you would be investing in an MMO-lite experience (aka Live Service Game). Expansions are $40 when new, but do go on sale from time to time. and they come with 4 'seasons' of content that are $15 dollars each (or all included if you buy the deluxe edition of an expansion). These seasons tended not to add much in the past, but this past year they have been very enjoyable in terms of adding good story content and interesting new rewards and activities.

Other than that, the gunplay in this game is immensely satisfying. The design philosophy of the game has flaws that show up, but I personally stick around because I really enjoy the universe, and because these flaws are relatively minor. It is grindy, as you would expect of a looter shooter/mmo type game. Overall this has been the best year overall for destiny and things are looking up for the next expansion. Give it a shot on the free trial (demo) and see if you enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
