\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's fun and you should play it if...

If you like well designed shooting gameplay and fun abilities, you'll probably enjoy playing this. The game runs well, it looks beautiful (except the anti-aliasing). PvE content is robust and wide ranging. You get the base campaign and first 2 expansions for free, which is about 20 hours of campaigns and a few dozen more hours of additional content. You can hit max level without buying any DLC. You might be locked out of some exotics and weapons from later DLCs but you'll be functionally equal.

PVP on the other hand will likely evolve meta-wise and leave you behind. It's also pretty robust with new game types every week. If you're the type to get upset at losing or are not adept at FPS stuff, you may suffer a bit in PVP and have less fun. I personally stick to PvE unless I have a quest that requires playing in the Crucible (the name of the PvP arena).

You should play with friends and join a clan, if you can. There's a mobile app and reddit communities to help you find people to play with. The game is at it's best when you're playing with friends and messing around.

Try the free version. It's pretty. It's good. It's pretty good.\t\t\t\t\t
