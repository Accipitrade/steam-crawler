\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI feel the need to edit my review a bit. I still very much like the game.

If you are a PvE player. Get this game. You won't regret it, PvE multiplayer is wonderful.

If you are a PvP player, be wary. If you like balanced sandboxes and for the most part fair PvP, this is not for you. There is no balancing between PvE and PvP. Especially with the introduction of Stasis, you're almost required to have Beyond Light in order to play PvP. The other included abilities just won't do it. If you like competitive PvP, this is not it. HOWEVER, I still play crucible regularly, and it often annoys me but I do enjoy it.

It's not to say the PvP isn't fun, it just isn't balanced - at all.

Original Review:
I'd like to start this review by saying I am a casual Destiny 2 player. I don't switch between other guardians much, and I main my Titan.

That being said, here's my take on the game.

Pros:
*A lot of content, and a lot of it offered for free.
*If you have a fireteam, it is a rewarding experience to complete trials together.
*It looks very pretty if you take time to look around
*Map areas are MUCH bigger than you first think.

Cons:
*Seasons operate almost on a FOMO metric. You can definitely miss out on things never to see them again.
*Finding a weapon you like, with the perks you want can take ages.
*There are some things you cannot do as a casual player. You must invest a lot of time. Does not play nice with work.
*Fireteam limitations can be annoying if you have many friends.

All that aside, even though Destiny has it's share of frustrations, I find myself coming back for more. It's gameplay is addictive, and with Armor 2.0 it's even easier to make a unique Guardian.\t\t\t\t\t
