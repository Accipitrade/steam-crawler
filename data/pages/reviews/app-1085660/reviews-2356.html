\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have a love/hate relationship with Destiny. However, for all of its faults, it has been consistent with the content flow, the seasons have been gradually getting better (to the point where I would honestly say they are worth coming back for alone), and Bungie seems to have remembered how to tell a cohesive story (as the stories being told in the seasons are ACTUALLY GOOD).

So, those are the improvements... now the Eververse is still a thing and will continue to be a stain on the game AND Bungie as a whole. The new transmog system is laughably (you can cry while laughing, right?) horrendous. And if grinding for gear and power levels isn't your thing... you'll be sorely disappointed after the initial WOW factor wears off.

All said, it still BEST IN CLASS FPS mechanics and the game runs like a dream. When the game is firing on all cylinders, it's an absolute joy to be a part of, but it's a game of extremes... it's going to take your breath away... either for how wonderful it is or how horrible it is at that very moment.

11/10 Would recommend trying, it's free to play (with paid expansions and seasons) so you really don't have anything to lose other than a few hours of your time to try it out.
