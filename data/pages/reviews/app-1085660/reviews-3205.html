\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI hate this game beyond belief, yet it is still my favorite.

In all honesty, you see all these reviews of people mad about removal of content. Replacing or making new reviews on previous dlcs and making the game look bad. Yet either way, were you really going to go back and do all of it anyways? I agree it is odd that they did it, pretty sure it was for too large of file-size, yet that doesn't really matter. People still buy cod ya know.

The dlc prices aren't that bad, except for 30th anniversary and the separate dungeons coming up. Deluxe editions are literally the normal edition but prepaying for all the year's seasons and the current, with a few extra goodies. And 10 dollars per season for it's content isn't too bad either, remember most games charge 10 dollars for only a reward pass.

Lastly, contrary to what people say, the game is still fun. Even with the removal of old content that a veteran player should have completed years ago. There are still plenty of things to do, early game to endgame there are so many unique exotics and gear to collect and almost each one feels unique in its own way. The build crafting you can eventually get to is insane, with elemental well mods alone you can become a "nearly invincible" god of death. Sure, it gets rather repetitive, but at that point, change your weapons or go do something else in the game. There's more than just Strikes, Crucible, and Gambit.

Also, contrary to more opinions, this game still has better releases than a good portion of the brand-new games that have been coming out recently. Oh and, all the games that were made to compete, "destiny killers," have all failed.

TDLR: If you're having fun, you'll have more fun with the dlc. And stop whining about the game, yes things that bungie do are a bit odd, but stop making a perfectly good game look bad.
