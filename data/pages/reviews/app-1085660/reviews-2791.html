\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell... This game is flawless! I've played since the Beta of this game back on console. Now the game has come a long way from were it all began. I started from being a noob and sucking ass.

I hated the game at first because i was bombarded with Missions & Quest etc. I was like "No way will i complete this heap of " I didn't care about the weapons.

But i soon realised that the game was a Grinding game. Later on i decided to actually have a go. I started grinding the game like crazy. In the long run i acquired exotics weapons & armor. I did various quest and bounties to acquire loot. Tbh it was worth it.

Now the campaign of destiny 2 is meh it was fun i enjoyed it sometimes it got boring towards the end but other than that it was fun. The original campaign of this game was called The Red War. When you started the game you would be automatically loaded into it. But later on Bungie removed it (Not Fully) So instead they used the original earth cosmodrome (Load up) but instead of doing The (Red War) they added the New Light A Quest. This would introduce new players to the basics of the Game. So instead of being bombarded with missions and quest they would have a slower experience learning everything.

If you are new or old and you still want to do the Red War go to Amanda Holiday in the tower in the hangar.

I've Put so much effort into this game and i'm not bored one bit.
The dlcs are fun and introduce a new experience to the game with new quest and exotics as well as a campaign. You may of noticed that the dlcs are quite expensive. Go to .com - to buy cheap games, dlcs etc. Make sure to choose steam.

Can't stop playing the game one bit Right Now i'm waiting on Beyond the Light A new dlc coming to Destiny 2!!

I would definitely recommend this game it is 9/10 for me because why not.
Best part of the game it is FREE TO PLAY.

If your into that, cool, your gonna have a blast but if your looking for moderately enjoyable story telling your not going to have a very good time, in my 47 hours playing this I have not once stopped to listen to what anyone had to say because absolutely none of the content is particularly engaging this game suffers from very poor writing; for this reason alone I do not recommend it to anyone looking for something to enjoy there but hey ya enjoy grinding a quest 50 times for a small chance to get a slightly better weapon than the one you had before? this is going to be your jam.
