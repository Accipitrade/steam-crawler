\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros
+ Blues are finally going away. Took them a few years!
+ Very addictive gameplay since D1. Each class feels unique
+ One of the best communities out there (LFG, API apps, friendliness, Lore videos, PVP guides)
+ Hands down the best universe to immense yourself in lore wise
+ Activision split + Cross save + Stadia/GeForceNOW/Project xCloud + Cross Play
+ Game is finally going back to it's roots with more RPG elements. Vanilla was very bare bone in comparison and shipped as a corpse
+ Ability to transmog eververse armor gear finally so you can be more unique and not a generic clone

Cons
- Craftable weapons shouldn't have superier versions of perks, should be the opposite.
- Melee in PVP is broken, teleporting galore
- Bullet magnetism (When a 2nd hitbox passes by it messes with aim). Can't disable it.
- Sniper quick-scoping brought back
- Story presentation and dialogue is sub par. Felt D1 story telling was better.
- Bounties should be always active, full time job being a mail courier
- Postmaster does not notify you that it's full until you are in Orbit or Tower
- Can't swap classes using the same character. 3 different classes require new character creation
- Seasonal mods being temporary + RNG on armor = No care. Not all armor was updated to accept the seasonal mods. Older armor can drop that can't use the new mods. This makes no sense.
- Armor mods section is useless now
- Seasonal badges and seals do not give enough time to complete them. Some are now unobtainable.
- Some triumphs are locked behind seasons and even have points attached to them
- Farming for items yields better results than actually playing the game. EX: Baking cookies to get 2X EXP and delivering them to vendors
- PvP balance is broken with some one hit kill abilities, they should be a way to counter them with higher resilience or lowering their damage; Shoulder charge, handheld supernova, Warlock warp, and weighted Knife
- Sunsetting old content. Why?\t\t\t\t\t
