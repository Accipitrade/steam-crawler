\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am reviewing this game as a yes but not going to say what is good about it because I don't know what it is. It is addictive for some reason I can't figure out, which is why I've played quite a bit. Most of the time I think of things I hate about the game.

1) The quests are super vague. A lot of the time you need to google something to find out if others figured things out. Sometimes I wonder if many people happen on things by chance and then just share it. In my mind a game that is made well shouldn't require a person to constantly google things. So likely just stupidity on the developers part.

2) Games where things zig zag and teleport constantly is just annoying. I know it adds a challenge but I think other tactical ways could have been made into a game to add challenge. Again stupidity on the developers part.

3) I'm tired of games with jumping challenges. There are so many times you run through areas just jumping from block to block. The loot sucks at the end so it is again just another annoying pointless part of the game. Again stupid development again.

4) The campaign stories were interesting though. I enjoyed it. Was sad when Nathan Fillion wasn't the voice anymore for Forsaken DLC introduction.

5) another grind game. After playing grind games since mid 90's I'm tired of rinse repeat. So I played mostly for story because it had voice actors I liked from TV.

6) PvP is ok. I'm no longer a pvp person so much but have to do some for some exotic unlocks or catalyst unlocks. If your into pvp matches then this games pretty good.

Overall I think a lot of people will enjoy the game. But personally I think the game should have less development of jumping challenges and more development of quest / mission descriptions. I may come back each time the company adds new story content though.\t\t\t\t\t
