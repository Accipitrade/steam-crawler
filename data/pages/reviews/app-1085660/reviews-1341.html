\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played the XboxOne and PS4 versions of Destiny2 several years back. Now trying it out on the PC in order to game with a friend. Have to say I do like the PC version much better. I like being able to use my XBoxOne controller PLUS the keyboard. This creates a much more natural flow for me.

Love the layout of the interface and menus. Takes a bit to learn them all and to be able to navigate them all. Impressive variety of story lines, plots, and battles to navigate and conquer. The fun keeps building as you dive deeper and deeper into this universe, meet new characters and take on more an more objectives. I would like a bit more story driven interaction with PCs. But that is a minor issue.\t\t\t\t\t
