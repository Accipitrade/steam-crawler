Awesome potential ruined by technical issues and laziness.

Pros:
  • Graphically, the game is gorgeous. Lots of partical effects and shaders that render fine on a non-top-of-the-line card.
  • Lots of fun loud out customization in different forms.
  • The gunplay is smooth and guns have clear niches (mostly).

Cons:
  • The story is laughably poorly told.
  • The controls and available key bindings are clearly made for console players. For example, your standard melee and melee ability are bound to the same key, so you're often forced to use your melee ability when you just want to stab someone.
  • SO. MANY. HACKERS. And they don't get banned. You're more likely to get a PVP match with someone that is hacking than someone that isn't.
  • Purely connection based matchmaking outside of competitive PVP.
  • Lots of strong guns are unattainable.
  • The tooling is terrible and what little there is is community driven. No clan search, no group search, no loud out management.
  • Grindy.
