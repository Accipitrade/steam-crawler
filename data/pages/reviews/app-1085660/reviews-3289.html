\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played vanilla for a while. Went through the campaign and Leviathan raid, but soon lost interest due to it feeling like an unfinished beta with a weak story. However, Bungie's split from Activision and the resultant Shadowkeep is a triumph. I now enjoy this game much more than any other multiplayer shooter, by far.

The Good: Very pleasing color palette, masterful art, and great music. Excellent PvE gunplay. The fact that you can work towards unlocking top-tier items via quests instead of obtaining them via RNG is fantastic. If only ALL the items were like that...

The bad: PvP is extremely gear-dependent and leaves much to be desired. If you don't have a meta loadout/class then you are at a severe disadvantage. There are RNG exotics like One-Eyed Mask that make this mode extremely frustrating.
I have a gigabit connection, yet hits will often register late or not at all. Players will pop into existence out of nowhere (not invis or blink, just bad optimization). PvP connections need to be optimized and the fights more skill-based than gear/class-dependent. Items like OEM need to be disabled in Crucible until properly balanced.

The Ugly: Not being able to modify our character's appearance even after all this time (things like hairstyle, etc.). You better like that facepaint or whatever, cause for some reason you're stuck with it. Please add an appearance change vendor for glimmer or something.

8/10. Would be 10/10 with better PvP, more story, and QOL additions like an appearance change vendor.\t\t\t\t\t
