\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is great. Amazing story, graphics, gunplay, characters, lore, and settings, these are the best parts of the game and why I love it so much. The biggest problem isn't the grind, or the anti-cheat, or the weapons being broken, or the glitches. It is the content. They have, for the past two years been so inconsistent with content drops. When Forsaken launched, I think it was what saved this game, but then we had Season of the Drifter and (to an extent) Black Armory. While the other two seasons added a variety of content, these two consisted of grinding boring content for guns you would brag about then never use once something better came out. This got much more prominent recently, and the season pass seemed like a placeholder instead of actual content. Essentially this game is 50/50 on whether a season is actually good or boring, but I do appreciate how PvP doesn't change too much (even though people take every gamemode in it too seriously, like their social rep depends on every match). I do recommend this game, but be prepared to take 3 months off when a bad season comes out.

Edit: Beyond light first impressions.

Beyond light has improved on the game significantly, in visuals, content, and gameplay. Stasis adds an entire new way to play that isn't just a gimmick (however op it may be right now), story and quests are interesting and don't entirely just feel like a grind, and they have improved how they make and fix the game to allow for faster fixes and more content per update. Although it doesn't yet live up to year 2 (forsaken), I think that if they keep on improving like this it will reach that point by the next expansion.

Edit: Witch Queen First Impressions

After the overall B+ experience that was BL, I am glad to see that WQ has made almost everything in teh game way better. There are still issues with core playlists being boring, but that might be because I have alot of time in this game. Overall WQ makes me very hopeful for the future of this game. Solid A.\t\t\t\t\t
