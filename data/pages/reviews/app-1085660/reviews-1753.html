\r\n\t\t\t\t\t*Easily my favourite game of all time.*

Positives:
-Extremely fun as a player who wants to completely submerge themselves into the game's universe and experience all of its content whilst perfecting builds and min-maxing loadouts.
-I could imagine the game would be less enjoyable to a casual player, but still fun nonetheless :)
-I find the PvE side to be incredible when it is at its best, and the PvP side to offer intense situations, a high skill ceiling and many unorthodox ways to play when bored.
-Massive, in depth lore world (although not much lore is given to the player directly. I reccomend a lore youtuber such as mynameisbyf).
-Plentiful content during the yearly september expansions.

Negatives:
-There to tends to be dry spells of content around the last month of each season but the beginning month of each season is usually very good and the yearly expansions are always amazing.
-Very very difficult to get into if you are not being guided by a friend for a few reasons: there is so much to do and such a specific meta yet not much information is conveyed to the player directly, the most enjoyable content is endgame and behind a paywall, there is no skill-based matchmaking in casual pvp (an extreme positive for skilled players but probably quite bad for new players), with each season comes more content but a lot of the previous season's content remains (this is being addressed soon, however). I believe this is destiny 2's largest problem.
-Rampant cheaters in high-end PvP

TL;DR: I really do recommend this game to anyone but only as a primary game, and not one to play casually between other games.\t\t\t\t\t
