I'm gonna keep this short if anyone is eagerly interested in this game, so heres what it offers:

- Vast content and storylines from Curse of Osiris to Shadowkeep DLC
- Competitive and Casual Enviroments
- CRAP TON OF QUESTS AND ITEMS TO OBTAIN AND COMPLETE
- And constant updates including new upcoming seasons!

Only Cons to this game is that its VERY grindy and that the pvp can be quite a headache.

I mean the game is free so it won't hurt to try it out. If you have been thinking about getting this game then do so now! It is currently quite a very actively played game across steam and new content is constantly getting released. Also I would reccomend buying both DLC's as they offer most recent content and raids that you may enjoy doing with friends or randoms! Apart from that the game is fantastic, tho IMO I don't really like the PVP side of it as it can be very unbalanced and BS quite often and you will find your self dying a lot if you don't have decent weapons to go with.

9/10 - Would grind more, but life hates me - Reccomended for those who are looking to try something different or are simply interested in what this game can offer.
