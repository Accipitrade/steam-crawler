\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall, this game is an 8/10.
The overall PVE experience is fantastic and the gun-play is some of the best in all of gaming. The content can get stale but if you play it casually you will enjoy it.

PVP is another story. Overall it is good, but the strength of special weapons (Snipers and Shotguns specifically) does hurt it. Stasis in it's current form is way to op and needs to be tuned, but if you play it casually then you will be fine.

Overall, phenomenal game and one of the most fun experiences with other people. I have easily sunk more than 3000 hours into Destiny 2 alone and still enjoy it somewhat. Also, it's free. Why not get it?
