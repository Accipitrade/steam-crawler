Pros
Very responsive FPS gameplay.
Things to do every week, keeps you hooked.
Good campaign missions which can be played with Friends. (4 Right now)

Cons.
Servers and error message. Being booted out of a game and random crashes,
Sometimes long wait times for the match to fill

This is one of the games I have played the most.

I dont know how LONG I will keep playing this game, but with the season coming to an end, I am eager for new content and more good times!\t\t\t\t\t
