\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe definitive FPS MORPG looter-shooter experience. Gameplay is intuitive and easy to pickup, while more nuanced features allow for more advanced in-depth play. The player community that has been built up around the game over the years has propelled it's popularity. It has allowed for an entertaining, if not enjoyable endgame as you work towards optimizing load-outs and signature look of your Guardians. if you are not having fun while playing the game, look to the player community and you will eventually find those who are willing and able to aid you in your Guardian's journey.

The game can be grindy and very challenging at certain places in the end game as some end game content requires cohesive cooperative group play in order to complete. The initial frustration can feel disheartening at first until you find your preferred group of players in which to work with on the more challenging content. You can play 100% solo, however the true enjoyment of the game comes from having bonding experiences with other friendly players that keep things fun and fresh even while playing the same ol' content for the 100th time.

Your mileage may vary, but I can assert that you will definitely find something to enjoy about the gaming experience as you progress your Guardian's Journey.\t\t\t\t\t
