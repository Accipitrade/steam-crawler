The keyword here is boring

I think it's just me because a lot of my friends seem to really like the game but I find it very uninteresting.
It's a game where the developers obviously spent a lot of work and time on but they forgot one important thing, the most important thing when you make a game: Fun gameplay.
The gameplay is just so bland and boring, there isn't really any fun to be had.
All you're doing it just shoot bland looking enemies so you get new gear. Then you use that new gear to kill bland looking enemies so you get new gear. This is basically the trap that most multiplayer games fall into.
People who haven't realized this yet will probably want to keep grinding the game so they get really good loot and they might have fun doing it. But I can't enjoy games like this anymore. Other people may want to keep playing because they are engaged in the story. But I find the story horribly uninteresting and just can't get into it. I finished every campaign except for shadowkeep because I wasn't invested in the story or any of the characters and the gameplay became even more dull than it usually is. The "Red Legion" campaign was one of the worst stories I've ever played through in a game. Curse Of Osiris is about 2 hours long but feels like 12 hours because you have to keep doing the same stuff in the same environment over and over again and the boss battle was really easy and simplistic. Warmind was maybe one hour long and honestly I can't remember anything about it except for fighting a giant worm. Forsaken is the best campaign out of the bunch but sadly by default. I liked some of the boss fights but that's about it. I didn't care at all about Cayde because I never got to know him, I saw him in maybe 4 cutscenes throughout all campaigns and that's about it. You never fight side by side with him through a level and his character doesn't get any development so I don't really get why people got so emotional over his death.
In the end I simply cant recommend this game because every time I play it I have to put on some movie or youtube video to not get horribly bored. At least a video game should be fun to play and this one isn't...for me.\t\t\t\t\t
