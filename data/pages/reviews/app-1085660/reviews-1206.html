The end of a Journey
I'm a fairly new player to the world of Destiny, only started my journey when Shadowkeep released; alongside with the Steam release. Still trying to figure out the lore and stuff. Took some breaks here and there, missed about 2 seasons. Had my Ups and Downs with this game, but mostly had a great time experiencing all of the content that I paid for. Bought all DLCs and Seasons. Really enjoyed the raids especially. Be a shame if it got removed to be replaced by a DLC full of reskins and old content? Oh wait . . .

Requiescat in Pace
This game was amazing, emphasis on "was". Like many, I am deeply concerned in the direction Bungie is going with their game. If they are going to remove content then what are we paying for? Am I just watching a movie, I thought I'm playing a game? I have done the grind, while I haven't done everything which . . . I won't be able to now as most of the content has been removed. It's a real insult to players who put the time and effort for Bungie to just take it all away or make the said items obsolete. Why not just fix your game? Your pretty quick at patching "cheeses". The game is becoming stale and unnecessarily tedious. Yes, that's the point of the game "you grind for better loot" etc. However, while the gameplay is somewhat fun it can get old quick. Like many, Beyond Light is my last token of support for the game. It has been great, such a shame to end like this. I'll still play to get my money's worth but at the same time I feel like what's the point? What's stopping Bungie from removing Beyond Light content in a future update? Goodbye dear friend.\t\t\t\t\t
