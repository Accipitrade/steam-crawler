Gone was the original Destiny 2 campaign (starting with the attack on the Last City), as well as Curse of Osiris/Warmind (with Forsaken soon to be removed). So imagine you own the original Star Wars trilogy, but you can only watch the 1st and 3rd film, without being allowed to watch Empire Strikes back (that you've paid for). You're missing arguably the best film in the trilogy and you really need to have experienced it to make sense of the 3rd film.\t\t\t\t\t
