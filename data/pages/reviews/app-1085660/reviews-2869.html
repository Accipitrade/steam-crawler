\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you enjoy looter-shooters such as Warframe, The Division, Borderlands, etc., then this is definitely a must-play as it absolutely earned its place as one of the elites of the genre.

Visually, the game is gorgeous and has a great sense of style that really makes everything feel like a proper sci-fi world.

Gunplay feels very polished and tight. General gameplay feels very rewarding. There is definitely a ♥♥♥♥ ton of grinding in this game, but gameplay is varied enough that it keeps everything feeling fresh. Plus, there are so many different grinds and gameplay loops in the game that if you get bored grinding for one thing, there is always something else to do instead. Importantly, all these different grinds feel very unique, so you never feel like you're doing the same thing for very long.

The story is probably the biggest weak point for this game. All of the campaigns are just pretty forgettable and don't really push any barriers when it comes to the writing. Many in the community seem to really like characters such as Cayde-6, but even he felt like a stale, canned stereotype of comic relief.

All that being said, even if the story they tell you through the main campaigns is lacking, the grander lore behind the game is actually really interesting. All of the little tidbits of lore you can find anywhere really make the game feel like its a living world with a full history. My advice, just watch MyNameIsByf on youtube for synopses of all the lore in the game instead of trying to figure out the story on your own because it is pretty convoluted.

Overall, I still think Warframe is the best game in this genre, but Destiny 2 is a solid 7/10 game. If you specifically want a first-person looter-shooter, I don't think you'll find any better.\t\t\t\t\t
