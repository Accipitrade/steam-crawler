\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is an amazing Sci-Fi themed game, set in a future where the remnants of humanity are under constant attack from ruthless aliens. This game is very good, however, there are a few things one should keep in mind before taking the plunge. If you want to get the full experience out of this game, including the story, best weapons and armor, and different activities, you must buy the expansions. After a few years, some of the locations from these expansions are put in the "Destiny content vault," where they might come back in the future, at the behest of the developers. This means that after that point, you can still use weapons and armor from that location, but you can no longer obtain items from the location, as it is gone. Another thing to keep in mind is that if you want to get the most out of Destiny 2, you must put a lot of time into the game, and you must put even more in if you want to play on the maximum of three possible characters (I would recommend making one for each class -- Hunter, Titan, and Warlock). This game can be overwhelming at first, but once you really get involved, it becomes very fun.\t\t\t\t\t
