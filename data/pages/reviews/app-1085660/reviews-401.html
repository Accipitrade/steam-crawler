\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave you ever wondered what it was like to be on a hamster wheel? Sure you feel like you're going somewhere, but that somewhere never seems to come. This game provides just enough enjoyment to keep playing but gets old really fast. Let's break it down into its basic parts.
First: the campaign. It's fun! i really enjoy the core campaigns this game puts out. the challenge if you want its can be there, but its not too much where the goals feel out of reach. i have nothing terrible to say about these campaigns.
Second: the PvE specifically in the form of the strikes playlist. It's boring, repetitive, and I that's all that you're playing; gets stale. Weekly you'll need to complete at least three of these "Vanguard Ops" in order to level up in any form of efficient way. Which i think is a fare amount to play. the issue i have is you are more likely have to do the same strike over and sometimes even over again. now this isn't always but when it does happen its really disappointing. makes me question why given the number of strikes that are in the game. and the majority of them you'll run through is just that, you run right through them, boring.
Third: Raids, these are fun, when you have a team to complete them. they take a fair amount of team work and knowing how certain mechanics work. what really bothers me isn't the mechanics them selves but when a certain encounter requires every member to be doing something but theres that one person who never pulls their own weight. its not always terrible, like if you are new to the game, most teams will explain whats going on (when they have the time) but its when players who do know or should know, thats when i can get frustrating.
Fourth: PvP, this game has a really funny way of how PvP can go. it can be very satisfying and fun. or it can be a toxic, sometimes hacker, filled cesspool. METAS rule this area in the game and even if you think about deviating from the norms you are playing a very risky game with how your match will be. and you might be thinking "oh this must just be the end game and top level game play, this sounds like what would be ranked" wrong, this is baked into ALL of your Crucible, Trials/ Iron Banner(when those are around), and even Gambit.
Now all of these on their own is not that big of a deal, but in order to avoid the monotonous game loop you'd be going through you'll need to be doing all if not at least more than one of these activities, and believe me, after doing three of the vanguard ops, three of the PvP Crucible matches which are a crap shoot weather or not youll have a good time, AND three of the Gambit matches, which are in my opinion even more of a crap shoot. week after week just to progress even the tiniest bit, its easy to get burnt out on.

Over all, I am conflicted, on one hand I enjoy playing this game and have meet some very good people from it. they really helped me get back into this game and honestly with out them i propably wouldnt have as much time as i do. On the other hand i am frustrated with both the lack of longevity in some of the content and with the attitude of how certain activities are ran.
