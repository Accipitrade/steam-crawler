\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe shooting mechanics feel good and the game has an addictive quality that brings you back. I don't enjoy PvP modes, but there's plenty of PvE to keep you busy.
On the bad side of things, there are some huge bugs like one I ran into with the final boss of one of the expansions. The expansion had been out for at least a year or two and it still wasn't fixed. That's pretty incompetent. No other way around it.
Also, it's hard to figure out where I'm supposed to go. The sometimes there, sometimes not white dot just doesn't cut it. Sometimes I lose my fireteam and have to wait until they auto-rejoin me to the group on unfamiliar strikes.
Overall, I definitely recommend this to fps / looter fans.\t\t\t\t\t
