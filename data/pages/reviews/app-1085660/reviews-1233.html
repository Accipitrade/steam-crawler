\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gameplay itself is a ton of fun. Guns and space magic are a blast to use. Co-op, raids and PVP (even though its very meta heavy) are all lots of fun with friends. However, Destiny 2 has been on a downwards trend for a while now and we are starting to see why very clearly with the newest expansion. We kept getting spoon fed recycled content, recycled enemies and generic storylines. The story has fallen stagnant and doesn't give the player a reason for why they are killing stuff. They were heading in the right direction for a moment, but instead, as of the writing of this review, have regressed back into generic "go kill bad guy cause they bad" stories. Both the lack of engaging new content and stale story can clearly be seen in the new Beyond Light expansion. Something else that came with the new expansion was the removal of older content in the game, that had to be PAID for to have access to. They cut the game in half with the promise of adding new and exciting content, but we have yet to receive it.

There is a great game underneath all the bs that Bungie has added over the years and hopefully they make their way back to what made Destiny a great game. Hopefully they wont continue down their current path, but if they do this game will die very quickly.\t\t\t\t\t
