\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe new player experience is godawful and you'll find yourself googling everything to figure out what to do, where to go, and what means what because it's so unintuitive. Once you get past those hurdles, though, you'll be in for one hell of an experience.

Pro tips:
- Google the order you should complete missions in
- Stats only mean a damn thing every 10 points. So that "upgrade" might actually be a downgrade. Basically, the stats on the gear aren't what you need to be looking at, but rather the "tier" that those points put you in for that particular stat. Your stat tiers are displayed in a column just to the left of your armor in the character menu. mouse over them and you'll see how they work. Note: the more you invest in a single stat, the bigger the bonuses it provides (i.e. it's not a linear increase).
- Start with the Red War questline. IIRC, the woman that sells you ships (Tower hangar) starts it.
- You do actually have a bank. Your vault is located to the right of the big gap when you spawn into the main part of the Tower.
- To adjust your spec, click to see details on the subclass you've chosen.

Enjoy Destiny 2, it's a lot of fun, but sweet baby yoda, they need to invest some dev time into smoothing out the NPE.

8.5/10, B+++, would play again.
