\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA promising combat system and an interesting array of aliens with lore to boot.

It's such a shame that the game absolutely and undeniably sucks.

All gameplay is repetitive and is either "defend this" or "find this" in nature. Ever since shaking off Activision, Bungie has turtled inwards and refuse to explore any new fronts or introduce any new characters, instead choosing to focus on stories and old characters. This would be great, if it wasn't for the ham-fisted approach.

Shadowkeep feels like a cash-grab with the Moon as free advertising. Season of the Undying promised a return of a fan-favourite boss, only for it to be a cloned fight from earlier in the SAME SEASON. Season of Dawn ♥♥♥♥-teases the return of another fan favourite character, only for them to appear very few times and have one small questline for. Season of the Worthy is the worst by far, the same new area copy-pasted onto old planets adding absolutely nothing story-wise and introducing a "new threat" that honestly ends up being a joke.

I understand many people are excited that Europa is likely coming with the new season, yet another "new old" thing players have been asking for since D1. But to be perfectly honest, I'm only expecting a bait-and-switch tactic, maybe a new Gambit map and nothing else for about a year until Bungie decide it's profitable to exploit the fanbase again.

Not to mention the greedy cash-grab of Eververse (micro-transactions) and season passes AND DLC, ALL IN THE SAME GAME. Hell, some seasons straight-up WON'T FUNCTION without the DLC, so I hope you have money!

To clarify, I got the game at launch being a long-time D1 fan. Had my frustrations with D1, but ultimately it was decent. D2 threw all decency out of the window. After the main questline, everything became pointless and ultimately I quit after SEVERELY misjudging my sanity and buying both DLC 1&2 (which are now part of the base game). The DLC were TERRIBLE, so I left. I then came back for Shadowkeep as it seemed promising, but again, it was all seemingly just a front.

DON'T FALL FOR IT. This game SUCKS and Bungie are still trying to salvage the absolute mess it turned into, instead of just starting over from scratch like they should. Their marketing tactics are questionable and you'll ALWAYS end up paying more than you expected or wanted to. ALWAYS.

Don't make the same mistakes I did.\t\t\t\t\t
