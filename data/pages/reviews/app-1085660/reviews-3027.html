\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played both the first destiny and I have also finished destiny 2 twice, once on ps4 and now on PC. I had bought the game and all it's expansions originally on Battle.net so my current gameplay time on record isn't representative of my total playtime. While I generally hate RNG Loot I have to say they have massively improved the rate at which you pick up new weapons and armour, though I feel that they have nerfed the drop rate of legendary items. I'm glad Bungie has split from Activision so they can have creative freedom and implement their vision properly without Activision looking over their shoulder.
The re-release of this game (expecially being free to get almost everything) was a stroke of genius BUT whoever designed the start of the game needs sacking, if you have played the game already then you know who the traveller is and why everything is the way it is. However if you are brand new to the game everything is just thrown at you without much instruction. there's simply too much content thrown straight at you as soon as you start to create context and make you actually care about the world. I would have suggested maybe not giving access to the tower straight away and have a new player have to do the main campaign first to access everything else as you did originally.
If you're playing it with friends who know the game in and out you will definitely be OK.
Gun mechanics are amazing, they feel weighty and that they are doing actual damage, there is also some real potential for some awesome builds.
I think everyone should try this game at least once because it is some serious fun!\t\t\t\t\t
