\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is a great game if you like grinding for Loot.
I've been playing Destiny 2 since launch 2016 on PS4, but I switched to pc about 2 months ago so I hope I can say that I've played the game enough to truely rate it. This game manages to be diversified but still monotonous. Dont get me wrong, I love this game, but especially the new players without the DLC's could have trouble playing this game for longer periods of time without it becoming boring. Of course there are many things you can do even without owning the DLC's but once you finish the Story of the 'Red War', 'The curse of Osiris' and 'Warmind' the game quickly gets boring and repeated. Some people may like running through strikes daily or play crucible the whole time but farming for weapons and armor is only fun until you hit power lvl 1000, which you can achive in 2 days, cause bungie slows the progress down by limiting the ways you can get more powerful gear, but that doesnt matter.
I think you should try the game for yourself cause well... its free. The game can be really fun, especially with friends, and you shouldnt just listen to reviews.\t\t\t\t\t
