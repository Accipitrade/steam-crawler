\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed it when it first came out and played the Year 1 DLC and was pretty disappointed. I loved the world, and the aesthetic rocks, but it didn't seem worth my time. Jump forward to Forsaken, I thought, eh what the hell, let's try it again. And it was incredible. The Cayde-based storyline was great and really made me feel for a character that I originally thought was just comic relief. The new bounty system made me feel like my time doing random stuff was worth, and Gambit, for all of its ups and downs, was an inspired new gamemode. Jump forward again and Bungie separated from Activision and somehow managed to keep Destiny in the divorce. Since then, between the sudden transparency with the developer and its fanbase, the launch of New Light, and with *most* of Shadowkeep's new content, I gotta say: it's worth it.
I've gotten friends into New Light and with Cross-Save I've managed to keep playing with my PS4 friends as well (Crossplay sometime soon I hope). It's a great space shooter with long-term goals for those who want to invest the time, and that good ol' Bungie minute-to-minute gunplay for those that just want to blast a space rhino in the face with a shotgun.
Edit: Beyond Light is incredible so far, as well. Well done, Bungo, keep this up.\t\t\t\t\t
