I just can't help but feel like my free time is being completely wasted when I play Destiny 2. I have played D2 for over 400 hours (including Blizz.net), and I can't even remember one specific moment, let alone a moment like getting my first exotic in Destiny 1.

I have spent at least $480 on this franchise since the launch of Destiny 1. All I can say is that I regret every single dollar.\t\t\t\t\t
