The basic premise is this: You are awoken from death and reborn as a Guardian, humanity’s last line of defense against a multitude of extraterrestrial threats. As a Guardian, you wield the Light, a power given to you by the Traveler, a great machine which now hangs over the last safe city on Earth. While the story and characters are all extremely well written, it has become much harder to comprehend without an external source, especially for players joining during this “New Light” era. However, on the opposite end of the spectrum, the different facets of Destiny’s game-play have all become much more accessible to a more casual audience and have improved drastically since launch. In the end though, it is purely Bungie’s dedication to the game that allows for its constant evolution. While the game has obviously had its fair share of rough spots (and still very much does), Destiny still stands against the raging storm.
