\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe main advantage of Destiny 2 is that, although its entire gameplay is reduced to shooting in all directions, the developers managed to diversify this process as much as possible. The long shelf life of the project was provided by a perfectly working shooter mechanics, many types of weapons and enemies, as well as a social component. Destiny-fun is primarily multiplayer: without a battle group, you will not only be able to start certain tasks, but also will not experience the full range of emotions.

Sky Atlas-clear, and most importantly, convenient.
The game finally officially has clans. Completing tasks, their members improve the banner and get various bonuses. The innovation made life easier for lone Guards, because in "Twilight raids" and" raids " there is an option to pass with a mentor — advanced members of a clan can take a novice with them on a task.

PvP battles have undergone minor changes. The number of team members decreased to four, and the maps became more compact, which increased the dynamics. Added several new modes, "sharpened" for team interaction. In General, without communication with party members, the chances of winning are significantly reduced. Proper use of the abilities of the three classes and coordination of movements mean much more than the ability of an individual player to make "headshots". Although this skill is quite important — especially with a gamepad in your hands.

The presence of many other Guards significantly enlivens the world — each of them is engaged in their own business. Anyone can be invited to a group, show them a gesture, or study their possessions.

But it is worth admiring not only the contents of the inventory running around the Guards, because the locations themselves are just incredibly beautiful. Technologically, the second part went only a little further than its predecessor, but the design here is so magnificent that the eyes do not cease to rejoice — each planet stands out with color and is ready to satisfy your aesthetic needs with a majestic spectacle.

Huge destroyed ships that cover the horizon, massive and mysterious buildings on The IO satellite, or dead landscapes of the Earth-large-scale locations not only give space for battles, but also inspire the user to explore. There are secret places, hidden chests, and new "Abandoned territories" with useful things and strong enemies, and just curious from an aesthetic point of view views. The map is clear and easy to use.

It rained, and the cabal Empire fell.
***
Destiny 2 is definitely better than the first part. However, it is difficult to call it an ideal game. In a sense, it has become a hostage of its own setting. A setting in which the characters are races, peoples, planets — but not individual characters with their stories and characters. In this situation, it is extremely difficult to create a story, drama, and dialogue that takes heart. Namely, their Destiny 2 is not enough: to make meaningless skirmishes turn into a struggle for independence, the development of the Guardian - the birth of a legend of this world, and empty chatter on the radio-in fascinating and important conversations of heroes with original views and biographies.

Pros: great shooter mechanics; an infinite variety of weapons and clothing; majestic landscapes; a lot of activities that provide dozens of hours of gameplay; the appearance of clans, acceleration of the "grind", simplification of "pumping" and character settings, as well as many other small details that were missing in the first part.

Cons: with the plot and characters — not fate.
I recommend it to everyone!\t\t\t\t\t
