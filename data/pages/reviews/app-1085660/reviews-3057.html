\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor solo players this game only has a small amount of replay and longevity value; but if you have friends to join you more nights than you will remember can stretch into the early morning. I am not a huge fan of MMOs, and as an MMO light, this game shares many of the industries problems. I avoid the PvP like the plague, even going so far as to not complete some quests because of it, but the PvE is often well thought out and engaging. Not a perfect game by any stretch, not even a great game - but I would call it a very fun game, and as I have grown older, I find that fun is more what I look for in a game than anything else.

If you want something fun, with a large player base, and don't mind meeting new people around every corner than this game could certainly be worth your time. If those things don't interest you and you find that mechanics and world intrigue are what hold your attention, then I would recommend looking else where.\t\t\t\t\t
