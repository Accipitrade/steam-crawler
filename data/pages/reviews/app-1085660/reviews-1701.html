\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe same rock solid gunplay you can expect from Bungie with more of an actual story than the first and more overall content.

Suffers many of the same issues as the original though. Poorly designed menus with very few quality of life features and clumsy functionality. The UI (particularly player names and icons) and the sheer amount of special effects can be very annoying in competitive multiplayer, often obscuring the screen and your vision of the opponent.

The multiplayer still remains an unbalanced ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. It's fun, but ultimately broken and cannot be taken seriously as a truly competitive mode with how uneven the playing field often is. The level design doesn't help with many small and often congested feeling maps. Very disappointing because the Destiny has such tight controls and great feeling gunplay. The AI is still laughable bad as well.

I give Destiny a positive review because the gunplay feels great and it's overall great fun, it could be so much better.\t\t\t\t\t
