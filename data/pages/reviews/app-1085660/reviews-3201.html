\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExcellent time waster.

The gunplay is a reminder that halo and doom are still incredibly well designed games to this age.

Customization is low this time around, The rarest gear is outdated and unnecessary. Some are incredibly strong while others are useless.

The raids are incredible but require 6 people to get them going and a lot of patience. They can take an absurd amount of time depending on group skill.

Don't expect anything from the writing or the story, graphics are amazing and you're able to play this even in low-spec machines thanks to the PC options. Tons of loading screens can get annoying, the planets are very small but have a good bunch of things to do.

Lots of grinding, plenty of challenges to complete, tons of shooting to do. It's pretty fun. Free to start, expansions are kinda cheap too.\t\t\t\t\t
