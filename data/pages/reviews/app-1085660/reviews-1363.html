\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed 180 hours and still enjoying it. I could always end up not enjoying it down the line but if i've put this amount of time without much complaints then i think that it worth the purchase. Sometimes the servers kinda wonk out but not enough to be drop that much fun from the gameplay.

It is a looter shooter to its core so it can be very grindy but with the solid gameplay and multiplayer, it can be enjoyable. As to all looter shooters, until you have a solid build and some nice weapons, the game can feel dry so don't give up if its boring but you only have very basic weapons.

But i should also talk about its issues. Unsurprisingly, this popular shooter game has a lot of hackers. While hackers are a plague that will never die, Bungie is trying to fight back but sadly this game with probably never not have hackers.

And to the reviews who are complaining about it costing a lot, the base game used to cost money to play but they made it free. Yes the expansions cost money but thats not unreasonable. The expansions are excellently made and add so much content to play. Yes theres season pass but i personally like the system. As this game is basically a mmo, they require constant money to run servers. Instead of making you pay to just play the game online like other mmos, they added a season pass which gives you more content and helps keep the servers up instead of trying to shove microtransaction cosmetics down your throat. Each season comes with new armor set and weapons and the season pass will give access to cosmetics from the season and gives you buffs to obtain the new armor and weapons easier. Yes you could say its somewhat pay to win as it increases your chance to get new gear but each season is 10 weeks, you have plenty of time to grab the gear you want. The fact that the alternative is paying JUST to play the game, i welcome the season pass whole heartedly.

This game has an amazing story and characters with extremely satisfying gameplay. The music brings emotions and the sound design is so well crafted. There is so much lore to dive into. They work so hard to tailor very unique quests. Yes it can be a grind fest but it makes you deserve the things you grind. This is a role model of what a AAA (triple A) game should be.\t\t\t\t\t
