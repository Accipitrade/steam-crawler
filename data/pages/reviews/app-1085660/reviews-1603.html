\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is either a lot of fun, immersive to the point your dreams are about the game, or it is so god awful that you forsake it for months. PvE is great, the gunplay is amazing, and there is enough variety to entertain new players for months. With buildcrafting becoming a larger part of the game there is seemingly no limit to what one can do when it comes to builds. Want to run around and one-shot melee everything, you can build into that. Want to spam grenades everywhere, you can build into that. Want to one shot a boss by bonking it? There used to a build for that (RIP wormgod.) Sunsetting did damage the game, however the content that was removed was largely boring and the loot wasn't good from it. Additionally, Bungie claims to not remove any dlc content going forward, preventing this issue from happening again. Overall, PvE is amazing and you can sink thousands of hours into the game in just this alone.

PvP is a mixed bag.
Balance is questionable at times, with Bungie making dubious decisions regarding the top PvP playlists. SBMM polarizing the community, on one hand the less-skilled players are able to play PvP without being constantly stomped by the top 10%, on the other the top players who spend the majority of their time in PvP now don't have a casual playlist to enjoy. Overall, if you like PvP, play it, if you don't like the changes Bungie has made to it, then don't, and spare yourself the mental toil of ramming your head against a wall for hours on end.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a whole, it's a solid game and quite a bit of fun.

My main gripe is that there is no real way to change the difficulty for most of the base game and it starts to feel stale pretty quickly. Most of the content is set at a flat difficulty that cannot be modified and most of the more challenging content (e.g. raids, nightfalls, high-level missions) requires a pre-made party. I want to feel rewarded for all the grinding for exotics and maxed-out legendaries, but most of the game hardly puts up any resistance, regardless of your light level or your mastery of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
