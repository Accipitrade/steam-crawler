The most toxic relationship I have ever been in.
(TL;DR is below)


I am in a love/hate relationship with this game. I love Destiny. But I hate what Bungie has done to this game over the years. Between my console and PC accounts, I have over 2000 hours in D2. My friends are the only reason I keep playing this game. We met through Destiny 1 and have been through hell together, both online and in real life, and we continue to play and raid together. Those friendships make it worth it but it's beyond frustrating to watch Bungie slowly destroy everything I loved about their franchise.

When it comes to mechanics and gameplay, Destiny is easily one of the best-feeling FPS games out there. However, as a company, Bungie seems to only care about micro-transactions and pleasing the Youtubers and streamers who play their game for a living. Bungie needs to realize that the bulk of their community is made up of normal people.

Even if we can pour thousands of hours into their game, we are rewarded with them deleting content that we paid hard-earned money for, and them making our favorite activities, weapons, and abilities irrelevant after a season or two. Want "new" re-skinned weapons and activities that are worse than the originals? Gotta pay again...and again...and again.
(Free game by the way. LMAOOO).


I can't in good conscience recommend this game to new players. The Bungie we knew from the days of Halo and Destiny 1 is gone. Not only because employees have come and gone, but because their values have changed. If only they knew how many players are just tolerating them until something similar/better comes along.

TL;DR
I love Destiny. I hate Bungie. Don't give them your money.
(Please, for the love of all that is still good in this world, can some other game company make a sci-fi FPS game that can compete with the gameplay of Halo and Destiny??) \t\t\t\t\t
