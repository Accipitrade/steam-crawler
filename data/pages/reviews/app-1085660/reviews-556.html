\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdated 3/19/23:

There's been a lot of great QoL updates to the game. The new strand subclass is amazing and the mod system rework is simpler to use. However, the Lightfall campaign story is not good. The commendation system is not working as intended; you shouldn't need to "farm" for something like this. Guardian Ranks needs a rework. I like the idea but the execution is not good and I have no desire to target farm for them know a good amount will reset next season.

So do I finally recommend this game to new players? Nope. It's still not new player friendly and at this point I'm beginning to think it never will be. Not until after the next expansion is over. It's just so hard to get new players invested in the game when a good portion of the game has been removed (Red War, seasonal stuff, etc.).


Old Review:

Let me start out by saying I have a love/hate relationship with Destiny 2. Most D2 players know what I mean. I have many hours played on both PC and PS4 (way more on the PS4 before jumping to PC). It's a really good FPS. Some of the best feeling gunplay out there actually. It's also very difficult game to recommend to friends and new players in general. It's super confusing. I've tried several times (unsuccessfully) to get some friends to play it but it's just so overwhelming for them. Bungie definitely needs to improve in this area. It's for this reason I don't recommend this game to new players. At least not in the state it's in. Maybe next year.

There will definitely be a ton of content for you to do and plenty of loot to chase if you do decide to pick it up. Once you've caught up though, things will get stale quickly. I find it best to play for a while when new content is out and then forget about it until the next big content drop. There are certain things I don't like about the game like the feeling of FOMO when a new season arrives, and I think the whole bounty system needs a rework (which I believe is coming in the future). A lot of good to come but just moving in a snail's pace.\t\t\t\t\t
