\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMixed Opinion.
If you want to enjoy destiny, play the game at your own pace, don't do everything straight away.
Take your time.
Destiny is one of those games that are very grindy, so if you don't link extremely grindy gameplay, this game definitely isn't for you.
If you like cosmetics, you will certainly like them, as they are all unlockable for free. (most of them)
If you have a group of friends you want to do raid activities with and form a clan, you will have a blast, but be informed the hardest part of playing a raid as a group is getting everyone online at the same time, its a pain haha, but it pays off.
I played destiny 1 alot, then i hated it.
Then i played destiny 2 alot, then i hated it later.
Now i've returned during the 30th anniversary event, and im enjoying it again.
Make sure to give yourself light goals you can achieve easily, as it gives you a sense of accomplishment in the game, don't give yourself goals that are ridiculous like doing a Witherhoard Catalyst in 4-10 days, otherwise it will make you lose interest very fast.
Its not a 10/10 game, but it is still pretty good, and is very much still alive due to bungies efforts.
