\r\n\t\t\t\t\t8/10 A commendable effort by Bungie.

+ Pros:
-Gunplay is top notch, as an offshoot of Halo of course the gun play is fluid and engaging
-Some planets are really well-populated and beautiful in term of level designs.
-Memorable NPC's, Lord Shaxxx is da man
-Active development
-Progression grind fest

+ Cons:
-Story is straight crapshoot, you get a good story here and there but most of the time they are terrible. This is partly dued to the time constraint they had during the Activision days.
-Quest lines are chores: literally fetch quests all of them, you go to A, kill B to get C then go D to defend E to spawn F then kill F to get G
-Bungie don't understand loot, unbalanced loot pool and outdated year 1 loots. This is the biggest offender, for a loot game the "useable" loot pool can be counted with 1 hand.

I could ♥♥♥♥ on the loot system all day as the current state. Since armor 2.0, getting new armor pieces is useless, and yet none of them are counted as skins. Year 1 guns are instant trash because they roll only 1 random instead of 2. Even the random rolls are complete trash unless you roll 2 out of the 5 useful ones. And even when you get the Rampage/Outlaw/KillClip..... rolls that you need, those are still not as impactful as you make them out the be, simply because your gun loadout is literally consist of less than 5 guns in ALL situations: Inazagi, Recluse, Mountain Top, PvP filler, PvE filler. Hand Canons are trash in pve since the new damage revamp, cannot one shot white mobs with headshot anymore.... A lot of work to be done.

Overall 8/10. The gameplay is there, the graphics will hold up for many years and the community is a dedicated bunch. Just get your ♥♥♥♥ together and hire a real ARPG game dev or two, maybe that person could shed some lights on how to do loots the correct way.\t\t\t\t\t
