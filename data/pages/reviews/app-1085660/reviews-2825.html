\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFeel like a re-review is needed since they've changed so much.

So they've decided to remove the main story, for whatever reason. And remove their first two DLCs, so if you were unlucky enough to purchase them. You've technically lost money. I understand it's the lore behind the story, but you should be able to at least play them on Characters/Guardians you didn't the first time around.

They have however released loads of new* content with their Beyond Light DLC.
*I say new because technically all they've done is add the Cosmodrome (from Destiny 1), a new strike (from Destiny 1), Variks (from Destiny 1) and new forms of bad guys on Europa. Plenty of recycled and polished old content. It's like they're running out of ideas.

I understand the game is now FTP, but that shouldn't be an excuse to add stale content from a previous game. I still play it and I still enjoy it. I just feel like those who actually spent money and have now lost things, should get more things in return. But if you've bought the game recently, since their Beyond Light expansion and bought that expansion, then you'll have plenty of hours grinding away and getting the best loot you possibly can.

This game is pointless to play with just their FPT content as there's no story, so all you have are Strikes, PVP, Gambit and Public Events. Their battle pass/season pass is however extremely balanced. Their free version is one of the best I've ever seen, so good job to them for doing that.\t\t\t\t\t
