Personally, I've never truly liked MMORPG's, mostly because the grind is absolutely unbearable. In Destiny 2 the grind is way more managable and even the meta weapons don't have to take long to get. Although some of the new Ritual weapons are a bit overkill (looking at you RTK).

Fooling around with friends or random people is really fun. The Destiny 2 community is an absolute treasure (apart from the comp pvp community IMO), I've made many friends playing the game.

Now that Destiny 2 is free, they've made a bunch of great changes that I thoroughly enjoy. Even the new light players get a bunch of stuff that they can spend hundreds of hours on.

Thanks Bungie for not giving up on this used-to-be mediocre experience and changed it for the better.
