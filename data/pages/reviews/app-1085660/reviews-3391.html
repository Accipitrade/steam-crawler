\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(This review comes from someone who's 960 with all three characters and has reset his Crucible rank like the 3rd time now and reached Fabled 3 in Competitive)

If you're looking for a fun PvE Experience, look no further. It's free, a lot of fun, - especially with friends, grinding PvE is fun, the story is alright, gameplay wise it's also pretty good and the graphics are good.

(TL;DR below)
If you're looking for a fun PvP Experience, you better pass this game because from all my years, this game has without doubt the worst PvP Experience I have ever witnessed and suffered through. An inconsistant, unbalanced complete mess, but what can you expect from a looter shooter? Those with the best loot, the ones you have to grind literally for days completly ♥♥♥♥ over anyone who don't have them and don't have the time nor motivation to grind through the awful and very lengthy quests and challenges. It's very clear the game tries to have some competitive modes in it but fails MISERABLY with all the one hit exotics and pinnacle weapons. From what I've seen so far the meta of the game is pretty simple : Use one hit exotics/shotguns to kill your enemy before they can one hit you. - Oh you don't have any good exotics or weapons that can achieve that? Well good luck wasting half your magazine into the enemy when all they have to do is only hit you once. The game just punishes you very badly for not using those meta weapons and it's really disappointing, and let's not talk about the inconsistancy and weapon mods. I can use a sniper and get only hit once in the torso and I get staggered completly and can't aim AT ALL, but when I spray an entire magazine into someone's face their accuracy is completly untouched and they still manage to one tap me with 100% accuracy, or that one time I sprayed an entire magazine into a Titan's face in his Super for him to tank me and all my teammates around me and one hit melee me but when I am the titan in my super I get melted in two seconds by a single guy just because he has basically a CSGO r8 revolver but in the form of a one hit rocket launcher. And if all that isn't bad enough, Bungie literally FORCES you to play PvP for the exotics and weapons you want. Fun fact, The Mountaintop and Recluse (Some of the best weapons for PvE)? - You need to get 100 Crucible wins for the Recluse and get a ♥♥♥♥ ton of grenade launcher kills and multikills (killing 2 enemies within seconds). I just hate how I am forced to play a mode I utterly DESPISE just to get the items I want for an entirely different mode... And as you can guess, the Mountaintop and Recluse are some of the best weapons in PvP too(one is a grenade launcher that uses special ammo, instead of the very limited heavy ammo, and the other is considered to be the best SMG in the game), so whilst you're trying to get these top tier weapons you'll get absolutely destroyed by sweaty veterans using these top tier weapons. Just.. Bungie, for the love of god stop forcing people to play your awful PvP mode by hiding god tier rewards behind unbearable, awful designed PvP Modes.

TL;DR:
-Unbalanced
-Too many one hit kill mechanics
-Inconsistant
-Grind to win
-Items over Skill
-Awful matchmaking
-Some very good items locked behind PvP and endless, painful grinding
-Very strict metas


Without the PvP the game would be a solid 9, but with all the exotics and top tier weapons hidden behind an unbalanced wreck, I can only give it a 7/10 , even For Honor had a better PvP.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe basic controls and mechanics are pretty polished overall, as one would expect from Bungie. The graphics and sound design are both above average, and the level design is very good.

But... that's it. There's nothing more to this game. PvE content lacks any meaningful challenge, so that entire part of the game is just a grind. The story is decent, but nothing to write home about. The one possible redeeming feature would be PvP, and Bungie should know how to get that right thanks to plenty of experience with the Halo franchise - but they fail, utterly. Class and weapon imbalance is rampant enough to make matches feel unrewarding and the matchmaking system is a complete joke, throwing newbies in with overgeared veterans round after round.

It's worth the price of admission - free - only if you don't value your time and have nothing at all better to do.\t\t\t\t\t
