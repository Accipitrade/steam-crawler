\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeyond Light DLC Review - 13/11/2020 (3 days post expansion)

The campaign was a bit anti climatic. A long season of build up to the pyramids/darkness only for it to be a fallen campaign. The campaign itself is great and finally does a good job for the first time in Destiny of actually telling the story.

Europa is great as a planet, one of the best they have ever added. I can see it getting boring over time as its all snow and does not vary much. Stasis is super fun in pve, broken in pvp. Content overall feels lacking with vaulting of the planets/raid as does the choice of weapons as so much has been sunset. Hopefully the content and loot issue will pick up when Season Of The Hunt starts.

PvP weapon wise is good minus a few weapons that need a balance. At the time of writing this the Stasis in pvp is just so broken with amount of crowd control that abilities leave when they freeze/chill players. It's overall broken pvp to the point where trials has been pushed back two weeks. The grenades however are amazing (ice walls etc) as it adds a new tactic and is great vs the sniper meta. Despite Bungie sending C&D’s the hacking situation in crucible has not improved at all.

Difficulty wise a step in the right direction overall with all PvE content so far. Empire hunts are basically nightmare hunts, but the bosses tend to be more interesting. The quality of nice changes are very much needed, especially in regards to the player UI overhaul and npc vendors. Triumphs have also been updated and feel less of a chore and more of an achievement.

Gambit is now only gambit prime, but you only have the initial wave of envoys and no prime buff. There is no reckoning or ‘gambit classes’ anymore. The boss has less hp than before, which as a result the tactic is just summon the boss asap and melt it. Invading after the initial portal or two is pointless due to how quickly the games finish which will only shorten as players gear up and minimax. Gambit is essentially a DPS race.

Lost sectors unlock a master difficulty after first completion If you complete a run solo you have a chance at a new exotic at high ll. The playlists have been condensed with now only Nightfall the ordeal instead of the five of cards version, unpopular crucible and the amount of playlists displayed have been reduced for the better.

Summary – Worth a purchase, but don’t rush out and buy the expansion. The game isn’t in a great spot right now, but with that being said if they balance stasis in pvp, add a decent anti-cheat and bring in more loot/content when the season starts it has the potential to be better than it’s ever been.\t\t\t\t\t
