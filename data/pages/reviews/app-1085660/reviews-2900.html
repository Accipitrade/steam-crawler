\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is really good. There is not another first person shooter with better gun-feel. You get to play dress-up with a badass superpowered space mercenary. There's a massive variety of activities to complete and objectives to work on - and if you run out of those, many of the activities (see: Gambit) are engaging on their own and have long-term progression like titles. Like any good MMO, you can expect to pay an additional fee every couple of months to have access to the newest, most interesting content. That said, if you're a new player who feels intimidated by the amount of DLC-locked content, try the free version of the game out and pick-and-choose which DLCs you care about. You won't be missing out on anything by not owning them - there will be activities you're locked out of, but it's not like other players in crucible or gambit will have a distinct advantage over you.

As a long-time MMO lover since my early WoW days, I can definitively say that Destiny 2 is the best action MMO out there. FFXIV might have them beat in story, but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, action bar MMOs just feel bad to play. Destiny 2 delivers on a crisp, polished experience that's fun solo or with friends. Can't recommend enough.\t\t\t\t\t
