\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game... I love it, according to the 'hourswastedondestiny.com' said that i invested about 700 hours into the franchise as a whole (xbox for D1 and pc for D2). There's something strange about this game that makes it so addictive and easy to log in and play a bit for a while after school or work.

In terms of the monetization of the experience, you can pay for the 'forsaken' and 'shadowkeep' expansions independantly of each other, i initially just bought shadowkeep but found myself drawn to forsaken to unlock the rather sizeable amount of content that is restricted to that specific expansion.

The newest expansion, 'shadowkeep' has both the shadowkeep content and, dependant on which of the editions of the expansion you bought, between 1 and 4 season passes which promise to add additional content and keep the game fresh. The current season, 'season of the undying' has provided quite a steady stream of content alongside the shadowkeep expansion content, however i think that next season will be the deciding factor on whether or not the season passes will be worth all that much for the overall content of the game.

So overall, this game is great, sure, it has its flaws, and at times i hate it, but i can never draw myself to put the game down. the initial game is free and provides a nice chunk of content to sink your teeth into before you even need to consider buying either of the expansions. so i'd reccomend that everyone at least try it at some point in their lives.\t\t\t\t\t
