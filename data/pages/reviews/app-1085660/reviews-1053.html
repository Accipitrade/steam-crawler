The gunplay is fantastic, the art team is second to none (at least as far as sci-fi is concerned), it manages to balance RPG and action-shooter elements well, and the lore is possibly the most interesting lore out of any entertainment medium I've come across. It's not a bad game to play solo, but with a group of friends (or even strangers) to play with it becomes something truly unique.

If you're reading this and skimming reviews because you're on the fence about trying this game, jump in and give it a shot. It's been 6+ years since Destiny launched, and while the franchise has had its fair share of highs and lows, there's still nothing else out there quite like it.\t\t\t\t\t
