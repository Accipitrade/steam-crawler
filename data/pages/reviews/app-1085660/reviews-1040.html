\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI started playing Destiny 2 one week before the release of Beyond Light and I would have loved to recommend it to friends but at its current state they wouldn't last long.

Pros:
- deep lore content
- gun play is solid and smooth
- different class build options
- customization including stats, star ships, land transportation, and many more
- Beyond Light Campaign is decent
- DSC raid is fun and enjoyable
- Beyond Light soundtrack is superb
- Lost Sectors are a welcome addition to farm exotic armor pieces
- opportunity to make friends through clan and make your game sessions more meaningful

Cons:
- grind checkpoints placed in the quests to artificially extend content
- forced to redo the campaign 2 more times for the other classes if you wish to unlock Stasis
- 1 year rental for hard earned legendary weapons before getting sunset
- limited weapon rosters after sunset has occurred
- Xur is a shadow of his former self based on the options he is selling in Destiny 1
- strike and Nightfall can get dull and repetitive especially with the poor loot drops in the final chest
- power level is existing for the sake of padding content through weekly bounty rewards
- most exotics are boring due to the existence of Pvp. Bungie should add a ban system for weapons in Pvp or auto adjustment features when certain weapons are used Pvp content.
- daily bounties are required to earn certain in-game currency like mod components and more
- extremely difficult to earn missed armor mods due to Banshee only selling 2 mod components per day
