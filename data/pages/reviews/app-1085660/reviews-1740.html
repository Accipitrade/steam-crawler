\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a good game with a bad direction.

The GOOD
1. It's one of the best shooting games around.
2. Nice graphics.
3. Cool guns and hero powers.
4. Has decent content for both PVE and PVP players, both solo or with friends.

The BAD
1. Not much new content was being added for the past 1 or 2 years. Most of the missions, guns, stories are just recycled stuff from the past (even Destiny 1).
2. Even less original content will be added later, as the company plans to start using a Content Vault from September 2020. In this vault they'll lock parts of the game and then release them a year later as "new". Basically, they'll just cycle content instead of creating new one.
3. The game can be/become very boring, as most things to do are about repeatable bounties (killing 25 of this or 25 of that, and when you're done, just rinse and repeat).
4. The rewards that can help your progress are forced to be duplicated so you can't progress easily and just grind endlessly. Basically, you get the same kind of items with the same kind of power several times in a row.
5. The bug fixing process is all about what makes money for the company. If it doesn't help with that, the bugs will stay there forever.
6. The connectivity is pretty bad, as they don't have dedicated servers, but a sort of P2P connection system. There are lots of random disconnects, at least on my side.

In conclusion, I don't recommend buying this game for now. In my opinion, based on how things are at the moment, Bungie is incapable of delivering quality content. They'll just keep milking the things that were created in the past just to make more money, so the game will become even more repetitive and boring. Sooner rather than later the game community will give up on them.\t\t\t\t\t
