\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSeason 10?
More like Season of Bounties. Exclusive Exotics only for content buyers, ♥♥♥♥♥♥ loot pool for the basic playlists (more than thousands of coins for ♥♥♥♥♥♥ gear), hackers on competitive pvp, hackers on GAMBIT. Only redeeming quality is playing with friends, and even that, might as well play Minecraft. And maybe raids, those are cool enough to keep me playing.

Season 11?
It's way better than what the hell last season was. Interesting exotic (Witherhoard is fun), actually rewarding and worthwhile grinds (Greatsword go spin spin, boss shredder), New dungeon (and a really cool one at that). Easier grinding too with this season's new engram.

Do I recommend this game to new players? I'd say give a shot, it's grindy yet it can be fun for new players. For players thinking if this season is worth buying, I'd say yes. Bungie now allow players to get previous season pass weapons (I think one season to get one old one?). With the upcoming new expansion, I'd say give it a try. If you don't like it, that's fine too!\t\t\t\t\t
