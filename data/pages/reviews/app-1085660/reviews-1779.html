\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy favourite game to play of the modern era of games

After my 1000+ hours on Xbox, and over 100 on PC, I can easily recommend this game. Its free too, so you might aswell!
Its been one of my favourite games since it came out, only being topped by 2 other games, one of which is its predecessor. By yourself this game is fun. The amount of free content Bungie gives you to toy around with is IMMENSE. If you have the DLCs Instealled, good for you, you just unlocked super mega fun mode. Where this game really picks itself out of the crowd is when you play through with a buddy or a group of friends. The memories you will make on this game: killing your first raid boss, destroying your first 6-stack in PvP- will stick with you for years to come. If you like community, this is the perfect game.

The area where this game doesn't outshine the rest is its constant barrage of bugs and glitches. Most of which are fixed effective immediately, but can still be annoying for some. This is a sort of hybrid MMO game, leaning more into that area with the more recent content drops. If you have played games such as TERA, World of Warcraft, games like this, definitely give this one a try.

Edit: This game is about GRINDING. If you don't like grinding, then don't pick it up.\t\t\t\t\t
