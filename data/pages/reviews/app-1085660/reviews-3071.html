Vanilla D2 6/10 "Feels a bit hollow."
Curse and Warmind D2 (New Light) 7/10 "I can play Escalation Protocol for hours. Short stories."
Forsaken D2 8/10 "Story!"
Shadowkeep D2 8.5/10 "Main story progress, new amour system, moon nostalgia, and gradual new content."

I started on PS4 and cross-saved to PC at Blizzard and now to Steam.

As a free game excluding those two expansions its really good, but Forsaken and Shadowkeep are what makes D2 worth playing. I highly recommend finding a good clan for the friends, bonuses, and raids.

I'm hoping that now that Bungie is free from Activison/Blizzard they continue this Shadowkeep new content roll out. In previous expansions story and content were released mostly at the same time, raids exempted, and that was that. The new plan seems to be a gradual roll out of story and content to keep things fresh month to month.

I hope they put in even more voice acting for all characters including ours because I'm tired of literally being a silent protagonist and I want to feel more of the emotion I felt initially in Forsaken from all characters.\t\t\t\t\t
