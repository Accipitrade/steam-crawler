Destiny 2 looks great, everything moves very smoothly and the gameplay is superb. The voice acting is fantastic and the music is still great on the surface the game looks and plays great, raids are cool to

The bad
Destiny 2 is a game that doesnt do anything to drawn in new players and if you havent been playing the game since launch. If you cant spend countless hours on it nonstop the game you will fall behind and almost never catch up if you cant put the 100 hours it takes to catch up. Destiny 2 made every thing more simple and easier than destiny 1, in PVE the mobs all fight the same in destiny 1 the mobs all had different ways of fighting and how they approached you that is gone in destiny 2, most of the time you can either run past them or cheese them and sometimes the game adds some simple mechanic so you cant do the ladder. The story feels like it was written by 30 different people, almost everything you learned in destiny 1 is almost never used or is changed so all those hours you spent on the lore was a waste. You'd think that they would make it worth it in Destiny 2 but it isnt, its ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ boring and you might get a interesting plot point here or there just for it to be dropped and you'll need to READ lore to get anything more of the slightly interesting plot point or wait for the DLC or season pass drop that might expand on it. Everything is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ vague but not in a cool way or in a interesting way its vague in the sense that they dont know what they are going with the story. It feels like an adventure anime where every episode is a completely different adventure but here the adventure is stupid and boring. The game had stream lined the mechanics to make it more generic and easily to play which isn't always a bad thing but in Destiny 1 it already wasn't that hard its just offered some funny/cute things to try when you customized your class ( you cant customize your class anymore in destiny 2 you have 3 presets in each of your class) so you could be a vampire void walker among other things. Ill admit they are bringing some of it back by armor lowering the cool down of abilities and grenades but that already was a thing in destiny 1 so it isnt something new. PVP is almost a nightmare since you didnt drop the 1000 hours you need to get the wide range of guns needed to succeed in high rank play you will lose, destiny PVP has never been balanced since 2014 and that hasn't changed in Destiny 2 and it never will.

This game just feels so cut up its weird, i dont know but these season passes need to go as well ♥♥♥♥ is gross\t\t\t\t\t
