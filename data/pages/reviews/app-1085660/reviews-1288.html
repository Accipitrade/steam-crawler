\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI dont write reviews. IDK why I'm even writing this one, I just saw the button to recommend or not and started typing?

I strongly recommend you have friends or are willing to join a guild. There's not a lot here to do solo that is challenging.
If you're fine with grinding for grinding's sake then yeah it's rewarding and has a good gameplay loop.
But if you want to engage in the gameplay and be challenged, all of that happens in group level content.
PVP solo is no different than cod or halo really, the special abilities are important, but at a macro level you're just fragging to get to the end of the match or test new gear.

Raiding brings something I've only seen in one other game, which would be WoW. If you're looking for a cooperative experience against the computer, nothing is better than raiding. If you're looking for a similar experience to WoW raiding but dont like the 20 man requirement, even if you're not great at FPS games - this is it for that specific experience.

There is depth to everything though. It's a flushed out system of gearing and role playing. It is a top tier loot game. Balance and META aside, I stopped caring about that stuff after I hit 30 and only had so much time each week.

This game doesn't work if you're not willing to make it your main game though. If you dont login as frequently as your friends, you fall behind and make things difficult to run together.\t\t\t\t\t
