\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot as legendary as the original but still pretty decent and a nice alternative to Warframe.

There is still a solid player-base despite all the content cuts and as far as F2P it has a lot of material to keep you occupied for hundreds of hours and the game is still be constantly updated.

Half the time you are preying to the digital gods for the loadout that you want though.

If you like the "base-game" enough you can always grab the expansions cheap during sales.

The grind in this game is one of the things keeping it alive alongside the complex lore.

It doesn't hurt to try it and you may end up getting addicted to it.

Kudos to Bungie for cutting out all the content oversaw by Activision / Blizzard.

Can't wait for future content. I know the business practice is ♥♥♥♥ but at least we have a solid game that still stands the test of time.\t\t\t\t\t
