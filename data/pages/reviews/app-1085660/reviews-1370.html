\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo be completely honest, the game is going in such a bad direction. I can not recommend D2 to anybody anymore just because its a complete mess. Gonna keep this short, there is plenty of info out there if you want anything expanded on.

-To start off, the current narrative is just really bad. Essentially, you have to pay for the 'Lightfall' DLC, but you won't even understand half the story because you actually have to play the seasons associated with the DLC to actually have things make sense.

-This brings us to another 2 points. Not only is the narrative bad, but because over half of the game has been removed, the story is so confusing for new players that you have to actually go on YouTube to search up the story to understand it.

-The other point to this is the monetization of the game. Destiny is now riddled with microtransactions. For a free game, that is expected. See, the issue is that D2 is NOT a free game. Despite what the store page says, the game has a VERY limited amount of things you can do without paying for DLC and/or seasons. In fact, they are still charging money for the 'Forsaken Pack', despite half of that content being REMOVED from the game. Yes, you are paying a decent amount of money to access a raid and a dungeon basically. The raid is good, but there are better. The dungeon provides you with almost nothing, being one of the easiest dungeons in the game, you don't get much from it aside from the experience. There are so many things you have to buy in this game that everything just gets convoluted. You would think that dungeons would actually come with the DLC that you paid UPWARD OF $100 FOR, but no. Dungeons are actually sold separately for $20 unless you buy the deluxe edition of the DLC.

-This brings us to another point. CONTENT monetization. Season passes used to be 1000 silver. Silver being the premium currency. This translates to $10.00. However, bungie changed this, seasons are no longer only 10 dollars, they are now 12 dollars. This would not be an issue, except you can not directly buy seasons from the store page, you are forced to buy seasons with the in game currency. The best part is, there is no 12 dollar option to buy silver, the next lowest one would be 15 dollars. So, the price did not increase by 2 dollars, it increased by 5. 5 dollars isnt a lot, but its the concept of the shady tactics that have thrown me and a lot of others off. Also, this is one of those rare games where you can not buy back the season pass with the premium currency it offers, because it does NOT offer premium currency in the pass.

Greed.


I could go on about so many more issues, but these are the ones that stand out to me the most.

Sad to see the game going to such a bad state.\t\t\t\t\t
