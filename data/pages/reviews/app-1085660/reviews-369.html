\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs much as I enjoy Destiny 2, I cannot recommend it. The new player experience is terrible and has been since Shadowkeep, leaving even experienced players confused. Paid content only being available for a few years is an understandable turn off for new and returning players. The lack of an in game LFG is embarrassing, and in game loadout tools haven't kept up with the game's expanding gear system. And the biggest problem is that the leveling system can be an actively push you to not have fun. For example, I personally think Destiny 2 has some of the worst PvP I have ever played. Being able to grind out better weapons and armor to have a measurable advantage over other players, instead of all players starting on equal footing is the last thing I want from PvP. Add in a lack of skill based matchmaking and I find all forms of Crucible, and to a lesser extent, Gambit to be actively un-fun. The fact that the game then pushes you to do those activities, to not have fun, means it is not something I can recommend. Bungie is one of the most talented studios out there, and I refuse to believe that any of it's developers got into game development to make skinner boxes. Until there are changes to either leveling or level requirements, give Destiny 2 a pass.\t\t\t\t\t
