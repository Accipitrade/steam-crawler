\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game for me is a love hate relationship. It keeps me busy trying to upgrade my armor and weapons through a large library of missions, quests, and bounties. The bounties come in the form of daily and weekly varieties that raises your reputation with that person allowing you to get better gear. The quests usually have multiple steps and each step reveals a little more about the story/lore. Even if you're not doing quests or bounties, there is still a lot to do to keep you busy. There are public events, and raids you can do to earn income and resources. The biggest problem I have with this game is the lack of information they give you. For example, I had a mission where I had to find 5 feathers but they don't tell you which planet their on (and they are on different planets) they don't even tell you the approximate area. If I didn't have an experienced player with me who knew where they were, I probably would have given up on it.\t\t\t\t\t
