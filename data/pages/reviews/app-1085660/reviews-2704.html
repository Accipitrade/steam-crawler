\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst game on Steam I've hit the 300-hour mark on. Amazing gunplay that never gets old. Planet designs are fun, pretty, and unique between each one. Story missions could be more fleshed out (pretty short overall), but there are some real gems both in missions and music. The soundtrack is 11/10, absolutely outstanding. Some of the strikes are extremely well done and Bungie has done a great job of really tapping into sci-fi space fantasy and creating a balance between being a super-powered killing juggernaught while still vulnerable enough to die if you screw up. 10/10 would recommend to everyone to play for free. DLCs are both great, very happy with both- some interesting new exotic weapons (Ace of Spades and Izanagi's shoutout) , some fun mission, more 11/10 music and cool planets (parts of which are F2P). Absolutely love the game. Love it even more playing with some friends. 300 hours and still haven't touched a raid so there's plenty more to discover! Also, being able to inventory and loadout manage in loading screens is the greatest QOL feature ever and every game needs to adopt this ASAP (although the space loading screens are pretty in their own right).\t\t\t\t\t
