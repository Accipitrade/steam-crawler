Neomuna itself just doesn't have a mysterious new feel to it for it being a city that was shrouded in secret and the cloud striders are.... just no. The developers had a chance to inject some seriousness to allow us to connect personally with these new characters but they just threw it out the door.

I don't know whats happening with my beloved Bungie but this is no longer the Bungie of old. Welcome to the new bungie... one that scams its people and can fix their mistakes just to stab us in the back again when our trust has been regained....\t\t\t\t\t
