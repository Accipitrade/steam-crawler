\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny is, at face value, a looter-shooter with some nice gun-play and abilities. With my current time with the game (1696.9 hours, nice) I found it to be so much more than that. It is an essay to the cosmos with it's rich lore and story, exploring morality and intention within an ever-changing and dangerous world. It explains the causes of war, love, how emotions can influence politics and governing humanity's final bastion. If you ever get the chance to play this game, and I mean really play it, read the lore and purchase the grimoires, research all of the metaphors behind the sword logic and the flower game, the final shape, do it. Behind every weapon, every specially named enemy, every guardian there is a story that runs so deep and surprisingly, nearly every one of these stories connect through an over-arching narrative. I do hope the developers get enough of a budget and time to finish this story and tie up any loose ends they can. I cannot express how well done this game is. I know this review has been mostly about the story but it has so many amazing qualities. Its music has so far been the only game to make me cry, with the Orchestral version of Journey. The gun play is so unique and satisfying, even when you are doing horribly, that I honestly shy away from other shooters as I know they won't be what I am looking for. Of course, everything has its flaws. PVP has had a considerable lack of updates though the future looks promising with Bungie getting a ton better with communication to the community. We just need new maps, please Bungie! But seriously I understand how difficult it is to make a game. If destiny were just a PVP focused game, people would have so much more of a right to complain. The outliers in the community might be my LEAST favourite part of the game. If you can stick to certain creators you enjoy, do it! This game feels rewarding, fun, exciting and is infinitely better with friends. I will continue to play this game for the foreseeable future, to The Final Shape and beyond. Keep spreading kindness guardians!
-Arcuz\t\t\t\t\t
