\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has fallen far from what it used to be. The story is dry and the game play even drier. The pvp match making is varies heavily from great games to absolutely being stomped on. Stasis, Felwinter's Lie, and similar game breaking aspects and weapons have turned crucible into a joke. Don't making me mention the cheating. The in-game grind is hardly immersive and repetitive. They have removed much of this game's great content including the great raids of Leviathan and provided ample replacement. The community has grown tired and I've witnessed my friends one by one lose interest in the poor story telling and boring season to season filler content. I pity all who have paid more for less over this past year and I hope they all are able to find a better outlet for their time and interest than this den of broken promises.

UPDATE: As Iron Banner comes around, I must write yet another scathing complaint about Bungie's gross incompetence in game balance. While they finally and rightfully managed to nerf stasis(to the great detriment of pve gameplay) , felwinter's lie, and the range of hand cannons, they have released an equally disgusting volley of slide shotguns. Bungie, surely there is a way for you to intelligently determine the consequences of game changing mods, weapons and more. I have some hope given the recent nerfs but more can be done.\t\t\t\t\t
