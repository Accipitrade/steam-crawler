You WILL have a problem adjusting to the different atmosphere and way the game flows, but honestly, you'll improve before you even know it. It's one of the best when it comes to Lunar Looter Shooters, as it also allows for solo leveling (no manhwa pun intended) to a good degree if you're a lone wolf, but also favors those who bring their friends along with them.
