\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPersonally can't recommend this game for new players looking to get in. Starting with the Beyond Light expansion, Bungie started a practice of removing older content in the game, as at The Witch Queen Expansion they have removed 5 main locations in the game (Titan, Io, Mercury, Mars, and the Tangled Shore) along with the 3 player PvE strikes tied to these locations, the base games story, the Forsaken expansions story, and 5 of the endgame 6-man raids. As a free-to-play player, you get absolutely nothing story-wise and only have basic access to the remaining locations in the, plus access to Strike (PvE), Crucible (PvP), and Gambit (PvPvE) modes. If you want access to a wider array of weapons (namely the meta relevant ones), story, and endgame activities such as most of the raids you have to fork out for the expansions (keeping in mind there is a high likelihood that most of the content in the expansions will also likely be removed at some point in the future).

The game still has it's positives, the gun-play, the classes, abilities, and the enemies you fight are all fun, and it can be a really enjoyable experience, especially with friends. But again, I can't recommend this new players looking to get into it.\t\t\t\t\t
