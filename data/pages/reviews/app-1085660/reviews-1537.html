\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of a kind game. It has a diverse world that offers a plethora of activities for new people.

Once you play a little more, you can identify certain issues that start popping up during your playthrough. The game is made with a story in mind with rich and deep lore. The problem is that the game does not revolve around that, but mostly around gunplay and grind.

There are lore cards that you can farm by doing certain activities, but I think it would be better to search for those in Google and read them up instead of spending time trying to earn them.

Quests are fine as long as you spend time in your browser looking up the next steps you have to undertake. The game does a very poor job of allowing you to keep a track of your quests. Markers shown on the map are not always helpful as the next step could be an activity on the planet which is not marked. This may prove to be frustrating and annoying to new players. I certainly find it very annoying.

The visuals, sound and design is out of this world. I mean it is amazing. Abilities and weapons feel powerful and impactful, especially paired with an exotic item that enhances them.

On the gameplay front this game could seem awesome for the first 20-30 hours, but it quickly dries up. One of the main marketed activities are raids and dungeons, but those do not offer in game matchmaking. Instead, you need to have a group of people to be able to play these activities. I consider this a gross oversight on Bungie's part as it is not as easy to locate groups and you need to find discord servers or third party sites that alleviate the difficulty of organizing a full party.

The game has no in-game world chat, so as mentioned previously, you either need to find someone in game that has a clan, to which you will be subsequently invited, or you need to take the initiative yourself and ind an active group/ clan.

PvP is one of the better modes that offer a lot replayability since there are multiple activities and you can create your own loadout and min-max it. The worse thing about PvP is that unless you are playing competitive, you are not receiving anything. You are not rewarded with powerful loot for performing better in game. What you get is mediocre items that only fill your inventory, and then you need to clean it out. So, if you play this mode, you either do it because of bounties or you simply love it.

Most of the activities (excluding raids and dungeons) revolve around bounties, which can be daily or weekly that give you tokens for the respective NPC, which you then spend on randomized loot.

Needless to say, the loot is rarely good, unless you are looking to increase your power level fast early in the beginning of a season.

New content does not come often and you will be stuck doing the same activities over and over. If you like to grind for loot and better rolls, this would be great, but most player burn out easily from the game, which does not speak well or its longevity.

One other downside of the game is that each expansion costs full price. This is simply preposterous since the game is old enough that it is free to play, but still gatekeeps new content behind a paywall. If you have no issues paying, then good news, but honestly I think it's an overkill and I personally do not support this tactic.

Overall 5/10, the best looking game with the most wasted potential of all time. Play this with friends as it will increase the longevity tenfold, otherwise I cannot recommend this product.

Edit 1: After putting double the time in, Bungie removed like 70% of the content, they are now re-introducing it as 'new' content every so often, but your old weapons are unusable, but you can grind them out again. This time with different perks. I would advise you to not waste your time with garbage ass game.

Bungie is a lazy developer and this game does not value your time since it seeks to have you sinking as many hours as humanly possible to get gear. Sometimes it's gear you already had, but re-introduced, like we're seeing it currently happening. Stasis is the laziest ♥♥♥♥ I've seen and the most boring ♥♥♥♥ to date.

Vendor rewards are better I guess, but the activities are the biggest pain in the ass ever, since you have to play over and over and over and over the same content since the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ don't release anything new. 3/10\t\t\t\t\t
