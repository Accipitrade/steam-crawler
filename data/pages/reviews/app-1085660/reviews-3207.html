\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGunplay is good.
Story is pretty solid in the expansions (base game is kinda meh imho).
PVP is fun though some people say it's imba. Played a lot of shooters over the years, and haven't noticed anything imba, save maybe Fusion Rifles.
Hard content is hard, so there's a challenge.

My only complaint is that loot is a little bland compared to D1/BL, but there's enough variety to keep it interesting.

If you've played Warframe, I personally think the harder content in this is a bit harder than WF (assuming you use the right builds in WF), the grind is a great deal lower, the guns feel better to me (though there's a bit less variety in some aspects, WF has more interesting weapons), but WF has overall more content I'd say, though you could argue WF is inflated by the grind walls, whereas that is a bit less of an issue with D2.

Story in D2 is good overall, but has low points. WF does better here imho (though it can be confusing af in comparison).

At the end of the day D2 and WF are pretty similar but also very different. If you like one, you'll probably like the other.

The main reason I would play D2 over WF would be because D2 actually has active PVP, whereas WF it's not very active at all.

Also, if you like how gunplay feels in Halo, it's pretty similar in D2 for obvious reasons. The gunplay really is almost perfect imho.\t\t\t\t\t
