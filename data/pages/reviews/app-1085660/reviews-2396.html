\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI returned to this game after Bungie separated from Activision. I have enjoyed all the content released post-Activision. The current Saint-14 content, in my opinion, is my favorite. Highly recommend to play with friends to further increase the fun of the game. If you are someone who enjoys lore in a game, you'll love the twists, turns, and teases that this one has.
Now, for someone who is probably one of the highest anti-PvP players in the world, this game actually makes PvP fun. The different modes and the way the characters are balanced out, regardless of light level, allows even the most casual of players to have a chance at winning matches unlike other FPS games. My most favorite thing about PvP in this game is that even though Camping is still a thing, it doesn't benefit the team. So you don't normally see it often.
However! There are a couple things that bother me about this game that friends have had to help me with. Some quests are ungodly grindy with rewards that seem like it was a complete waste. Example; Symmetry Catalyst quest. Also, quests that force you into PvP matches and to do objectives that can be way too difficult for those who don't normally PvP.
But the biggest complaint that I have that really sucks the fun out is the amount of hacking/cheating going on right now due to this game going 'free to play'. And not just in PvP, but it's also being increasingly leaked out into PvE as well.
Now, with all of that, I still recommend this game because it is a lot of fun, especially with friends. The PvE has great story and challenging fights. The PvP can be a lot of fun if you give it a chance, especially when you show hackers they still can't win matches.
For the sake of this game, I just hope Bungie does have a plan, moving forward, to slap the ban hammer on those who are abusing this game and ruining the fun for those of us who just want a good clean game to play.\t\t\t\t\t
