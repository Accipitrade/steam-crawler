Bungie removed 90% of the game, the 90% that I put those 3500 hours into. But being so bogged down by trying to be an MMO with a constantly evolving meta negatively affects the game. Destiny is at its best when it's a co-op story based FPS with loadouts functioning like deck-building games, not when it obsesses over balance and "pinnacle" content and inventory management and RNG.\t\t\t\t\t
