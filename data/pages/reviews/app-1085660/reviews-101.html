Now you need to grind to level up to even get access to the ritual playlists, unless you like dying constantly and your weapons doing no damage...even patrol on the new planet is seemingly more difficult than the newly released raid. Some of my clanmates grinded for 12 hours a day for 2 weeks for the raid, and even now with the level adjustments in certain activities they're getting one-shotted from across the map.

Lets not even mention the bug where enemies do more damage the higher your framerate is - so, if you don't want to die instantly you have to run at 30fps...makes my PC I spent a fortune on to get 200fps a bit pointless, eh? Oh, this bug has been around for YEARS in certain activities, but seems much more prevalent now. PS5? Yeah, crashes all the time.

After 3 years it's a hard NOPE from me...I'm done.\t\t\t\t\t
