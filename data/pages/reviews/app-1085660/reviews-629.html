\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start by saying that this Game has evolved from what was launched at the start of its launched. There are so many things that have been done right as well as other details that can be better but, overall its a game I would definitely recommend. The following comments are, by no means, meant to discourage users from playing the game but more of a personal opinion of mine which you do not have to agree. Since the implementation of buying separate expansions in destiny, the game feels like its always forcing you to buy the expansions in order to play the game. Yes, this is obvious since the idea is to have all the xpacs but, a compilation package could be offered like what is done in Warcraft. What I mean by this is, you can have a bundle where not only do you receive ALL the past expansions but the most recent one as well. I spent quite some time off destiny 2 and when I returned, I did not have Beyond light and it felt like the game was always pushing for me to buy it, which is totally annoying. When I talked to my friend about it, he joined the fray to fight back the darkness but, he was discourage to see that the only bundle available at the moment of this post, was Forsaken with the previous xpacs. That meant he had to buy the forsaken bundle and the beyond light bundle which adds up in $ when bought all at once. While he did buy the Beyond light xpac, he does not have the previous content before beyond light. There are others that do not join the fight and become a guardian because of this $ hunger from Bungie.

Hope this review helps a bit and remember, the game is SUUUPER good in my personal opinion. It has been a while where I have wanted to leave my job early in order to play all day.\t\t\t\t\t
