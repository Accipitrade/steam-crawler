\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe community is largely toxic, people will kick you because they don't like the weapon you have even if you have no better choices, or if you use a controller, or if you have a bad round, and THE GAME IS BROKEN WITHOUT HELP FROM THE COMMUNITY. None of the guides are up to date, and the game gives you no way of listing perks and sorting or looking for perks AT ALL. If you try to look stuff up on youtube, streamers desperate for subscribers will make God Roll videos for everything.

Trying to sort through your vault is a joke, and considering the amount of time you spend in there, this alone is frustrating enough to make the game not worth playing. The only way to play this game is with third party apps that help you manage the UX nightmare that is Destiny 2. If you don't make space you'll lose guns and by extension chances to level up and have good/consistent games. And if you don't get lucky enough to farm something at the top of the guides that are out there, there is no guidance as to what you're supposed to run with or use or aim for. As a result, you're either dominating everything with people who don't know what they're doing or massively outgunned. Often out of frustration people will spam guns that Bungie has put in to make the grinds worth it, that the community immediately revolts against while using at the same time. The number of people screaming bloody murder because they got killed by a Recluse or a shotgun for the thousandth time is ridiculous, and you will get kicked or reported for your playstyle, which to be fair hasn't happened to me but has happened to other people.

Once it comes to rolls for perks on weapons, you barely get help from anyone. Looking it up on a third party website doesn't help because they're not up to date, or the ratings are wildly unreliable and asking on a Discord or the like makes no difference because people will tell you your gun is trash and your roll is good or your gun is good and your roll is trash or shrug unless its something everyone agrees on which everyone hates because it's too overpowered, or it'll it's something so rare it'll take you dozens of hours to farm. Your job to play this game now is to parse through all this advice, most of it contradictory, and figure out if its maliciously misleading (which has happened to me and resulted in me sharding some of my best gear), completely idiotic, or people pushing personal preference as dogma.

I picked this up because I wanted to play a Bungie game. It is incredibly disappointing and stressful, and there was no point putting extra hours in to try and understand it better.
