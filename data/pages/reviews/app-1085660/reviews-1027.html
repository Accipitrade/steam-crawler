\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was decent up until recently.
Then they put the stupid gear sunsetting in the game.
It´s basically a carrot on a stick. You grind hard to get the best gear possible, and then after a while, poof! It´s gone, useless.
You have to grind new gear back again. It was a half-assed attempt to solve power creep but it ruined the game.
Also they started doing the same thing that many games do when they think they´re being smart: reshaping the game world.
In order to keep the client file size "small", they started to remove things from the game (RIP Mercury).

The PVP they ruined by allowing hackers to run wild (when you´re lucky enough to last a full match without being disconnected).
They also released dumb weapons like the Witherhoard, a grenade/missile launcher that turns pvp matches into a "the floor is lava" game. You spend more time dodging damage fields on the ground than actually fighting people.

Overall, I can´t in good faith recommend this game to anyone.
Bungie had a good game on their hands, but squandered it badly.\t\t\t\t\t
