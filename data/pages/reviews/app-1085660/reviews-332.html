\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 has very fun gunplay, great story (if you're a veteran of the series; new players will be lost), amazing encounter design and fantastic visuals.

However, I cannot recommend it to new players due to the declining quality of the game and what seems to be an ever increasing focus on engagement over player experience by Bungie. Bugs go unfixed for weeks/months/seasons (unless they benefit the player in some way). Promised bad-luck-protection systems never materialize or are broken, leading to absolutely punishing grinds for some essential items.

Additionally, the PvP side of the game is utterly broken for anyone who isn't in the topmost echelons of players. Players are not matchmade on skill, so new or casual players will be matchmade against players with the best gear/skill, meaning many crucible matches are a landslide victory for the better equipped team. Additionally, the game makes no consideration for solo-queuers vs stacks, leading to even more one-sided matches.

I believe Bungie can fix the game... but I don't know that the executives and management want to.\t\t\t\t\t
