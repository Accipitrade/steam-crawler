\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I figured I would write an actual review for the game while I waited for MW2 to download so here we go. A No BS review of destiny 2.

First off do not that this game has a huge investment cost it is highly recommended to buy all the expacs to get the most out of the game the best versions of expacs to buy always being deluxe. This will easily run you almost $300 USD and for most that is a very high cost to sink into a game they don't know if they will like.

The free to play version of this game is more of a trial of the game rather than actually playing the game for free, It isn't a good trial but a trial none the less.

All that being said there is a reason I personally have over 1000 hours in this game the game is very good if you like shooters it has some of the best gunplay in the business using abilities feels very good and movement is very very smooth. The raids and dungeons are extremely fun and while they def require lfg or a friend group to play they are well worth it if for the experience alone. (raids are 6 man pieces of content where you essentially solve puzzles and kill big bad guys with your friends. Dungeons are smaller scale version of raids with 3 players its a bit reductive but that's an easy way to explain it.) your general day to day content is also quite fun I always enjoy playing strikes and pvp gambit I enjoy sometimes when I play with friends. Nightfalls are fun and very rewarding the higher difficulty you go especially on double nightfall loot weeks.

So your most likely also wondering if the story is good, The answer to that question is yes and no let me explain.

The story they are telling is actually quite good and is very gripping and I am hooked in when I actually pay attention. However the way they tell it sometimes and the fact that as a new player you are missing alot of context you can only get through things outside of the game make it hard for new players to enjoy. So if you actually have all the context it's good but getting the context is kinda a tall task unfortunately. That being said you will not be coming to this game for story but there is plenty to dive into if you want to go to outside sources.

Lets now talk about new player experience well as I stated earlier story wise it sucks, and the on-boarding process honestly is not much better it does not do a good job of teaching you ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ now this might change in lightfall but I can't review something that isn't out so uh for now it sucks. You will very often be confused on what your supposed to do or where you should go after the first few quests. It will bombard you with a ton of quests that you don't really learn anything from. I will straight up say that you should really have a friend that plays the game already to go through this with you, Let them teach you because for now bungie won't. Bungie is the swim instructor that drives you on a boat 12000 miles into the ocean and then on your first day trying to learn to swim they pick you up through you off the boat and say good luck gamer try to get back to shore, Then proceed to sail away. Be prepared for that and know that if you brave the learning curve there is a beautiful game with a ♥♥♥♥ ton of depth to sink your teeth into.

Now the thing that actually matters in an mmo, community its about 50/50 tbh most interactions with lfg and stuff will be fine or great, there is of course bad apples and sometimes you will get some ♥♥♥♥ thrown at you but eh its apart of a multiplayer game at this point. However avoid reddit and twitter at all costs aside from like bungie help on twitter. It is a cesspool over there, a constant circle jerk of "content creator bad" and unnecessary whining about bs that doesn't matter in the slightest. Sometimes complaints are valid but most of the time it gets drowned out by hate for content creators or bungie for ultimately no reason.

Sun Setting

Lets talk about it since most negative reviews will mention it. Yes it happened yes we had some parts of the game we paid for like 3 or 4 years ago deleted yes it sucks. Will it matter for you the new player? no no it won't they actually said in the most recent lightfall reveal that no more stuff would be sunset so you have nothing to worry about. Did we actually lose anything of value no not really tbh most of the things we lost were things no one and I mean literally ♥♥♥♥♥♥ no one played. Still sucks of course but it really won't effect you as a new player at all in GAMEPLAY. Story wise, not having the launch story, warmind and forsaken do affect your understanding of the story which sucks. That being said they have basically replaced all of that content with new content that is honestly better in most cases.

How should you approach this game?

Now I'm gonna give some advice on how to approach this game,
1, Play with friends please for the love of god it will be 1000x better if you do even just one buddy will make the world of difference this game is 100% a social experience and it is best enjoyed that way.

2. Try to look into guides for stuff you don't understand there is a million of them out there and they will help alot.

3. Bookmark: Destiny Item Manger(DIM), Bungie,net , Light.gg , and D2 Armor Picker

4. Sub to: Datto, Aztecross, My name is byf, Fallout Plays and Ekuegan they are all very very helpful content creators.

5. Play around with all subclasses and see what you like

6. Know that you need to make your own goals in the game its kinda free form like that.

That's about it tbh not much else needs to be said hopefully this was helpful. Also Hope that if you do end up getting the game based on this review that you enjoy it. I'm usually down to help new lights find their footing so HMU if yall need help getting into the game.\t\t\t\t\t
