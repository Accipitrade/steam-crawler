\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was never a hardcore player, but I loved to play. I loved the exploration, and the story. Mainly I’d run around playing missions with my friends.

I’m really, really disappointed with the removal of the original content. The removal of the main campaign, and two DLCs is so frustrating. I never had issue with going free to play. I was excited for the chance to pull more friends in to experience the game. But now it feels like all the money I spent to access the original content is wasted. There’s so little access to certain NPCs too, so the story and their development is all gone too which is sad.

But this just makes me hesitant to ever purchase another expansion for fear that it’ll just disappear too.

“Free to Play” felt like a really cool move to draw in more people and let them experience the game, and now it feels like a scam to encourage players to buy expansions only to remove the content entirely.

It’s made me really disheartened with the game as a whole, which is really upsetting since I loved this game. But as someone who enjoys replaying, and re-experiencing the game I just feel like there’s not much to keep me around anymore. Not into PvP, and there’s not enough content of anything else to make replaying/restarting worth it.

I loved the gameplay, the aesthetics, the visuals and music were phenomenal. Really bummed I can’t share with friends why I loved this game- cuz it’s literally not there.\t\t\t\t\t
