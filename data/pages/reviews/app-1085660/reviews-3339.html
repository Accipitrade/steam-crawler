\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is not a game for everyone. To be clear, this is a looter shooter that still needs a lot of work. It can be really enjoyable if you get down and dirty into the mechanics of each class. I have more than 106 hours in this game, coming from playing on ps4, then to Battle.net, and the transfer to Steam, and I can still come on at least once or twice a week and play this game HARD. With the Season pass system, its now less enjoyable imo, but is still fun. If you are going to play this alone, I recommend playing the game for a while before making a purchase, and if you see yourself enjoying the game, get Shadowkeep. Maybe even get Forsaken, as Exotics (extremely powerfully gear, sometimes) are expansion locked. This is a good game that I can come back to and play with friends and do end game missions and have a great time.
There still is alot of work to be done with this game, and as soon Bungie fixes it, I'd gladly force people to download this game and play it.\t\t\t\t\t
