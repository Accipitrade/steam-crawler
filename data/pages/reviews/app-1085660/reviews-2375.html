I like that instead of leveling up your character as you defeat enemies, you have to increase your light by getting better gear. Another thing I like is the feeling that there's more going on here. More places to explore, more missions and more interaction. I don't really interact with other players that much, but it is still, in my opinion, better than the first Destiny
