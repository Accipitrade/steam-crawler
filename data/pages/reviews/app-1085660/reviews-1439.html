\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnlike what the game's plot, story and lore wants to show. Its almost like Forza Horizon, not in the sense of genre since both are very different but more so on what they basically are meant to be, enjoyable experiences where you can friendships and rivalries, where while forsaken and shadowkeep being good stories, the main game is about bettering yourself or as the marketing puts it "Become your own Legend".

The community is rather chill and enjoyable to hang around with and there's always that one person who wants to help ya out or just be chill.

Gameplay is simple but what i found to surpass what it is, which is going against enemies with not many distinctions, is how you can feel like a badass. Being a hunter, chaining kills. Being a Warlock and being a full fledged force and titans, being versatile in most situations. There's a lot of potential here to make gameplay fun and addicting.

Story is alright, only forsaken and shadowkeep having either decent or down right great stories. Though the base game's story isn't a slouch either as it does have its moments of pure awesomeness such as A1U, which is still my favorite mission in destiny 2.

Honestly, i would say pick it up if this interests you at all since the game has a lot to offer and is just all around fun.\t\t\t\t\t
