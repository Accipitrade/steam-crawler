If you're looking for a game that you can sink serious time into, grind for the best gear. Join up with friends to get exclusive gear, or hunt for the best exotic weapons and quests you can, this game is one that you would enjoy. Whether you prefer PvE or PvP, this game has both, incredibly fleshed out, balanced, and well rounded. Numerous game modes in either catagory depending on what you could like.

Personally, I love the story and lore of this game. It is EXPANSIVE. If you're like me and make it a point to read every lore entry you unlock as you complete various things in the game, you'll find yourself constantly wondering who the lore tab is referring to. They aren't always named, and it can be a fun/ exciting guessing game until further entries are unlocked. Its like waiting for the next episode of your space opera to be released.

10/10. If you haven't, give this game a shot.\t\t\t\t\t
