\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not a big fan of spawning into a holding pattern when loading into the game, that being said Destiny 2 makes up for it with solid game play and robust FPS mechanics. There's a variety of events for each zone along side short "patrol" and longer adventure style missions. Each zone includes a number of lost sectors, mini dungeons with a boss and loot box at the end, and this is all part of the FTP game. If you have the money for the DLC sets and season pass you can add a good amount of story mission content along with a number of exotic weapon missions + the current seasons rewords. I played around 60 hours before buying anything. Now 177 hours in I still have 2 full DLC story arcs untouched, the open world content is quite satisfying. I have been holding off on story while working towards more powerful exotic weapons. You will want a mouse/keyboard control setup to take advantage of Destiny 2's fast paced combat! If you crave endgame content there are a decent number of raids found in the DLC. 3 player strike teams and 6 player raids plus a number of difficulty modifiers like super strength shields and or brutal melee attacking enemies you have to bypass or disrupt in heroic mode with another level beyond that. Moddable weapons and armor with a creative level up option allowing you to bring you're favorite gear along as you progress. I would go on but I need to post this so I can play! Hope to see you in game. PS. PvP is fun but intense! If you have nerves and steady aim along with a good weapon load out you can compete with meta weapon wielders. But they can often 1 or 2 shot you. Then you have the pvp gods with season reword weapons. May they have mercy on us mere Immortals. Anyway off to play! >8D\t\t\t\t\t
