Destiny is a fun game for everyone to play however it can be annoying for newer players due to the fact that they are spammed with missions and quests to do. I would recommend any new players to watch a play through on the best way to complete the quests and missions.

It is also nice that Bungie has allowed players to transfer there accounts between platforms for those that want to move to PC as you don't have to make a new character to do all of the stuff you all ready have such as collecting all of your exotics again.

New review - 876 hours played

Pretty much nothing has changed from my original review however I would like to say that the game has gotten even more difficult for newer players with the release of new DLC. Lightfall is a mediocre DLC that doesn't offer anything new other than a few new unique weapons and a new subclass that you might or might not like depending on your play style.

The newer season as of 03/06/23 however has made it easier for newer players to get more exotics weapons and materials through weekly missions, fishing and decrypting them at the tower making it easier to make the builds that you want.\t\t\t\t\t
