\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny was my favorite game of all time.

The gameplay feels good, the raids are awesome, and it is an awesome way to spend hours with your friends just having fun.

However......

Bungie has absolutely slaughtered this game into the ground. I put nearly a thousand hours into D1 and another thousand hours into D2 before it was launched on steam. I have been here through the good and the bad, but as of recently, the amount of bad has outweighed the good by a substantial mark. The amount of reused content for dlc you have to PAY for is staggering and in the end you find yourself doing the same thing over and over again with no reprieve even though you handed over money to get "new" activities to do. Not to mention that Bungie will snuff out anything the community finds fun to use or play in the name of 'balancing' even when no one has complained about anything.

I want to recommend this game. I want to tell people to spend their money, but in all good conscience, can't. If you have never played Destiny before, then by all means try out the free to play platform of it, but eventually you will come to understand a community that just wanted to fall in love with this game, but couldn't because at every turn, they were nothing but disappointed by bungie time and time again as they ran their perfect game into the ground.

I will forever miss this game, because I truly believe their will never be another like it. I am sad that it came to this state.\t\t\t\t\t
