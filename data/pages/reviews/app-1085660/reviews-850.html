\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2.

I love destiny, I have put thousands of hours into both destiny 1 and 2 over the past decade so it really leaves me conflicted while making this negative review.

Lets get into the good:
- Snappy gunplay
- Unique weapons and armor and loot
- Multiple enemy factions that are enjoyable to fight
- Really addicting combat loop (thanks to the enemies and gunplay)
- Subclass customisation has improved drastically opening up much more build crafting.
- Fantastic soundtrack (like most bungie games)
- The BEST first person PvE content of any game I have played or been aware of. (referring mainly to the raids and dungeons)

Thats just some of the great things about destiny but after all that you may be wondering why I gave it a negative review.

lets get into some bad:
- Atrocious servers with multiple hours of downtime a week
- The PvP experience in this game is stale and bland as it has been long neglected.
- Content recycling, bungie LOVES removing content and then either A). Selling it back to you OR B). Releasing it again and calling it new.
- Drip fed content; as with any live service model game there will be drip fed content.
- This game requires a fair bit of attention if you want to get involved in the pinnacle endgame content such as master difficulty raids, grandmaster nightfalls, trials of osiris etc.

There is certainly more that can be added to that list but I really don't feel like writing a novel.

Next lets get into the worst offender MICRO TRANSACTIONS!
Destiny 2 love their micro transactions, from buying armor ornaments (changes the way your armor looks) to something as silly as a ghost projection.

I honestly can't even properly convey just how egregious the micro transactions are in this game so I will link a video made by Aztecross (A prominent destiny content creator) that I STRONGLY URGE new players watch.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nsIdPWB2_JA

Just know what you're getting into.\t\t\t\t\t
