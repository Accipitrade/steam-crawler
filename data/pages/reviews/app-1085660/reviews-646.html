\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI used to love this game.

But then they began to lock story-related content behind PVP. They tried to sunset weapons. They started puling content out of the game, making certain achievements and triumphs inaccessible. Hell, I was even cheated out of a whole set of triumphs at the end of a season because their servers didn't recognize my account as having completed them- despite submitting photo evidence to Bungie Support proving otherwise. And the story itself, while initially very good, is becoming ham-fisted. Nothing separates Guardians from any other enemy now. Everyone can use the Light. Everyone can use the Darkness. You could slap a Hive Knight, Captain, or Legionnaire skin on a model and nothing would change. Guardians are now just walking fashion shows (and they even decided to make a "fashion magazine". I love looking good as much as anyone, but come on.)

And let's not get into the hackers, or Bungie's refusal to institute any sort of anti-cheat mechanism or bans. Even basic strikes are riddled with them. If you have to cheat during one of the easiest forms of PvE in the game, you're not contributing to a good game environment, and Bungie just... lets it happen.

Was it a good game? Yes. Is it a good game? Maybe. Will I ever play again? No. And I can't recommend it anymore. I would have, a year ago. Not now. Maybe if they would stop pandering to the streamers and the hyper-aggressive try-hards, it could be good again. I have very little hope that that will happen.\t\t\t\t\t
