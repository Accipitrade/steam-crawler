\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMost poor reviews of this game are dated.

Destiny 2 has, over the past 2 years, gone through transformative updates to the player experience. The base game, the free-to-play title New Light contains everything a player would need to jump into the game for 15 hours minimum (including PvP and PvEvP modes) without feeling the need for any add-ons. It will also be enough for people to begin to WANT some of the additional content, without feeling necessary. Certain weapons are locked behind certain DLCs (Ace of Spades, for example, is found in the Forsaken DLC story), but there are base weapons that can fill the niche-metas in PvP that are easily attainable (Bygones, the pulse rifle from Gambit for example). While the DLCs do serve to enrich the story and add slews of new guns, quests, and activities, they will only be notably missing to people who have otherwise completed the free-to-play aspects of the game.

Destiny is, IMHO, a great franchise. If you are unsure, try the F2P version, New Light, and see for yourself!\t\t\t\t\t
