\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 had a rough start. The campaign itself was great but then it felt that there wasn't that much to do in the online part and the 2 first DLCs were too short for the price.

With this Free 2 play version you not only get all of these for free (you, at a minimum, get 20 hours of single player content, which you can do in co-op with friends if you want) but you also can get access to the Forsaken and Shadowkeep extensions, which I recommend.

Destiny 2 has outstanding gunplay, think of it as a great FPS with non intrusive online parts. There is a store in the game with paid content but it's mostly cosmetic stuff and you can perfectly ignore it.

Also the game engine itself is amazing and runs great (60FPS+, 1440P, medium to high settings on my GTX 970). It has some of the most beautiful lighting effects I've ever seen and some fights can get several dozens of enemies on screen for a big splattering mess.

The only drawback is that the UI is unintuitive sometimes and some game mechanics aren't obvious (like leveling up, infusions, ...) so it's worth investing 10 minutes of reading up the basics online.

The perfect game to play every day when you have a busy schedule as you can quickly chose any activity.\t\t\t\t\t
