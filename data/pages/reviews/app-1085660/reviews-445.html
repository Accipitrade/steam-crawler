Pros

Cross platform
Caring devs that put time and effort into making the game enjoyable. (they listen to feedback heavily and will revamp modes and other things based on feedback )
One of the best stories to a game that i have experienced . Genuinely pulls in the player and makes you a part of their world despite your ghost doing most the talking (LOOK UP BYF on YT and LEARN the LORE. Its really makes you feel like youre a part of the game)
Allows for a lot of creativity in builds with the new subclass system where every part of your subclass is up to you and the exotic weapons / armor that you use can really change the way you play (they also added weapon crafting which is amazing. you need to watch videos on it before playing)
Also allows a lot of creativity in LOOKS which is really important to me. They consistently add new costume sets (which you can get for FREE with bright dust. <- not hard to get) and you can transmog gear (also watch videos on transmog please if you like fashion)
The end of season events are amazing. Example, there was this one season where the cabal (an enemy race) who in the red war campaign had this giant ship that could latch on to the traveler (the planetoid thing that gives us our powers) and take our powers from us. We beat the campaign etc and get our powers back but then a few seasons down the line they fly the giant ship towards the last city on earth to destroy it then destroy their own thrusters etc so we cant stop it and the entire season we're powering up this super AI named Rasputin so he can save us. Then at the end of the season if your standing in the tower you can look up in the sky and see Rasputin shooting missiles up at the ship (The almighty) then its a huge explosion etc. (Just look up d2 end of season events)


I want to get into secret missions and raid races a lot more (literally the best thing in this game imo) but this thing will literally be a blog if i do. Instead i urge you to look up evanf1997's "The Wildest 24 Hours in the History of Destiny" and just go through his video's. he shows a lot of the Destiny communities craziest moments from raid races and secret missions and everyone coming together to solve GIANT puzzles that require an entire community to work together to solve. Being a part of stuff like that is what I enjoy most.

CONS

You can customize your characters face and stuff at the start but like after playing for a few years you really want to change it but cant.
You need to buy the dlc to enjoy a lot of the content in this game and season passes every 90 or so days.
Towards the end of a season it gets pretty dry if you have already done everything. (There are events for holidays but sometimes its just the previous years event on repeat.)
It can get really grindy for certain exotics or weapons. (grinding spoils for tarrabah still)
Some content you need friends for which can be a good thing or bad thing depending on the person (JOIN D2 DISCORD SERVERS)

Honestly thats it. The whole thing about needing to buy dlc isnt even that bad when you consider this game which is one of the best games of all time is f2p.

Do some research while its downloading (on transmog, weapon crafting, lore (byf), evanf1997' raid/secret mission videos etc) This game is an experience. I really suggest playing with friends and following youtubers cause they make it 10x better. I also suggest playing with someone who knows what they are doing. If you need people to play with i have a few communities. My Discord is CEDER#7791 do not be scared to add me. I will respond instantly)
