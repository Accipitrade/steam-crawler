\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet's get this straight: Destiny 2 used to be a fantastic game. It was unbelievably fun with minor problems here and there. But over time they've made mistakes, released guns that aren't balanced in the slightest until weeks later, they have server problems on the regular and the meta is stronger than ever. This isn't an attack on Bungie though, they recently split from their parent company and it'd be hard to do anything after that. They even released a DLC while going through it which, although mediocre, was still a large DLC. I can't recommend this game, true. But I'm also not putting it down. It lived it's life, paid it's dues and we all had fun with it. But the grind has gotten extreme, the weapons feel repetitive and most of all - it just seems to be dead. Not player wise, don't get me wrong. This game is still played by many people. But the community, the memes, the fun that we all enjoyed feels gone. The fire is burnt out. I got into Destiny 2 on battlenet and enjoyed every waking second of it. After 79 hours on steam I can confirm Bungie is just doing what Rockstar is doing with GTA V. There is no new game, no new adventures...it's just rehashed, recycled nonsense. It's time for Destiny 3, a huge step in the story. Stop asking for money, gauging people that just want to have fun by forcing them to grind for not hours but days and return to that raid haven we knew and loved.


And I swear if I crash or disconnect again I will find you and do nothing but hand you a strongly written letter.\t\t\t\t\t
