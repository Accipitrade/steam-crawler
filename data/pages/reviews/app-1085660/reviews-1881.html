\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is pretty great. Running around killing baddies for hours on end is pretty fun to me! I recommend this game for anybody that likes loot pinata type games.

There are even jump puzzles! Certain classes *cough* Hunters *cough* have it easier for a lot of them, but all classes can do them.

Armor 2.0 rework was something that needed to be done. I think overall it was a good thing. I like being able to customize what my armor gives me (faster grenades, more speed, etc)

There are a few things I absolutely abhor about this game;

The need to exploit early and exploit often. For example; In the newest season (arrival) the best way to farm gear is to literally AFK in a certain area. If you didn't do it/ are not on board, you are significantly behind. It could take weeks to catch up depending on RNG. This feeds into the next point;

Gear is not normalized in all PvP. While I dont expect perfect balance, if you are not 'meta' you can be at a huge disadvantage. Yes, you can PvP with whatever gear you happen to have; but expect people to make your day suck. PvP is also 'snowballey'. More kills means more super power, more super power means more kills. For these reasons I stay away from PvP as much as I can. I cannot stress enough, if you are a casual player avoid doing Trials of Osiris. Get good gear before going in!

Some missions that require groups (Like Shattered Throne) are not in the Matchmaking rotation. You *can* solo these with enough patience and or 'God Rolls' on your weapons or with great exotics.

You need to get the Destiny 2 app on your phone. It has some QoL features the game should have but simply does not.

It has done well for me as a replacement to Warframe, so if thats your kind of thing give Destiny 2 a shot!
