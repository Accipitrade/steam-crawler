\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChanged my review from a negative to a positive, due to some choice design changes being implemented on Bungie's side, however I will warn anyone reading this and is not sure if they are willing to get into the game yet.

This game has roughly two expansions worth of story that has been deleted. Be aware of that. The story of the original Destiny 2, and Forsaken is not playable, it has been removed and streamlined into the 'New Light' experience, which I played through on one of the my characters to see what it is like.

The story of the Red War is gone, Forsaken is gone, Shadowkeep's story is probably going to get gutted soon at some point.

For what content there is, it is good, however you will notice that just about every reprisal decision being implemented now, demonstrates that Bungie screwed up with sunsetting, now if they were to bring this system back in any form, I think they would lose the last bit of goodwill they have with the playerbase, don't let the current game deter you.

There is fun to be had, and if you have mates and a clan to join in on, then all the better. The LFG experience for higher level content is relatively good, I have experienced little to no issues in that regard.

If you go into PvP though, beware that you will play against cheaters, and that currently Bungie has just about no desire or action that they implement against them. Unless you are a streamer, than they get banned during the match funnily enough.

I would give the game a rating a 76%, the gunplay can't be beat, the loot is good, and getting better, but you can sense Bungie is scared to make major leaps and bounds at times. Something that I hope they will overcome before the next expansion releases, people want to have a power fantasy. Should a powerful build be easy to achieve? No, but should it be achievable and powerful when done so? Hell yes.\t\t\t\t\t
