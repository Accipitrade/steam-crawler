\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCONS:

pvp crucible and iron banner are required to obtain powerful gear drops to raise light level. i myself am a player who receives no enjoyment at all from any form of pvp. it isn't fun. it is stressful to have a mission REGRESS because of people who are outfitted better (or are using cheats) which is quite common. it is also common to run into kill rooms, full of a squad in a free for all setting that will only target you, the outsider and not each other. can it be enjoyable? sure for those that enjoy the pvp competitions against other players. i don't care for it. yet i have to endure it just to enjoy participating in a raid at a decent light level due to equipment locks behind those modes. not fun at all.

RNG is no good.

The RNG factor is a monster and for some quests needs to be properly addressed. Grinding can be tolerated if there is a decent time frame and reward in sight. Leaving up to chance can be exciting in some circumstances and just ridiculous in others.

aside from that THE GRIND, good god the grind is real. Sometimes it is ok. sometimes it is ridiculous, often times it is ridiculous. The rng lock for some quests is just absurd. I mean literally, there are people who have been grinding for months for a change at a rare bounty drop just to complete an exotic quest for a specific weapon. very much NOT FUN.

The RNG is just terrible.

I love the exotics, i'm not super thrilled that i can run pve equipped with what i earned through hard work. you can equip ONE armor piece (head, chest, arms, legs, character specific special) and ONE weapon (out of 3 available weapon slots, primary secondary and power) i can understand limiting this for pvp because it can become stupid good with the builds and op quite quickly. higher tier raids or strikes with the modifiers on are quite ridiculous and it would just be nice to have the ability to wear what you enjoy and run a build you grinded so long for. but, you have to play it smart, which is understandable. but also frustrating. it's my gear. i should be allowed to wear and use it to optimize myself. how can we truly be guardians if we arent outfitted to take on these trials with the best equipment. you don't go into a gun fight with a pack of rubber bands and expect to win when you have an anti tank rifle just waiting to be used. it doesnt make sense in that regard. i wish they would change this but i know they wont.

God i hate the RNG.

it would be nice to do strikes and story missions at 4 players instead of 3 limited :/ most cooperative squad based shooters already figured that out but destiny has only 3 characters to choose and locks a fire team to that.

RNG SUCKS. Did i mention that?


PRO:
Strikes, Raids, Cooperative story, sometimes with friends, sometimes with great players who understand what teamwork is all about, B

Bully bosses or enemies, punishing them for trying to punish you. You can push your way into a higher tier (within reason) whilst being lower and feel amazing when your coordination and teamwork pulls off a win of the ages. The good regular people who meet who aren't toxic elitists are worth quite a bit of strife. and for me that speaks volumes.

I love the pve aspects and cooperative nature of the strikes, story and raids. Change your ships, your color, your armor!

there is so much room for improvement. much i dislike, through it all i still enjoy playing the game with my friends and can recommend it for the cooperative pve experience alone.

I don't recommend any of the seemingly FORCED pvp or the TERRIBLE RNG ratio.
