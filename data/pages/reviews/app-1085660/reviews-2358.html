Long version: It's a lot different, but the core gameplay is the same. But hey, if it ain't broke right? Speaking of broke, PvP. It's not great. At least not at the time of this review. It's... Bearable. Just be prepared to become well acquainted with the business end of a Spare Rations/Mindbenders Ambition. (Play it for yourself, you'll see.) Sadly, the meta stands strong in the PvP aspect, although it gets shaken up every once in a while. It usually settles back to a single loadout for a good few months. The PvE game is pretty solid. At this point, there's nothing that can really improve what's already amazing. The raids are back, with a strong enough variation to give each it's own experience. A few of the key systems are very different. (Shaders, armor system, weapon perks, etc.) But Destiny is Destiny. And honestly, it's in the best state it's been since launch. And now that it's free, the only thing you have to lose by trying it is some hard drive space and some free time.\t\t\t\t\t
