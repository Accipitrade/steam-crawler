\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2 is not fun to play when playing most of the content does not get you anything.
PVP
-Crucible games and the tokens you get are useless, the gear is always worse than what you have, it was made to be that way. If you are someone who really likes competitive pvp do not look for it here. The number of hackers and ddos'ing jerks is INSANE to me in a game with a name as big as Destiny has. And no its not salt, getting teleport spawn camped is just ridiculous is a RANKED game mode.
PVE
-The few strikes that are currently in the game aren't worth spamming when there aren't any new exotics in the loot able even after an full price expansion. The only other reason to run strikes is to get farmable materials that you can upgrade your gear with. For whatever reason, they heavily limit the amount of these farmable materials which makes it much less desirable to do unless you have a specific piece you want upgraded right now.
-Vendors are literally just bounty boards that for some reason are spread throughout a huge area
-Although not immediately apparent for new players most content is just recycled. Whether you want to talk about entire worlds/load zones, or weapons or even events. Some recycled from previous seasons that used to be considered "limited time gear" which when re-released makes both those who spent lots of time farming for it feel bad but also those who thought something new was coming out feel bad. And no this hasn't happened to me personally since I only started 1 season before Beyond Light and already have done all the content this game has to offer.
Raids
There is only ever 1 raid worth anything and you can only play this slot machine with extra steps 1 time per week per character. Unfortunately unlike other MMO style games the pure randomness of loot drops has left players I know with subpar gear for an entire season with nearly daily play. In other games these high tier pve events that require getting a group and lots of teamwork and communication will have vendors for their content where even if you didn't get what you want you got a little currency that would get you one step closer to "pity buying" the gear.
Lost Sectors are a stab at solo pve content and fails terribly. The game was built with lots of support abilities and weapons with the idea you would be playing with friends making tons of builds and play-styles obsolete.

Currently I do not suggest buying Destiny 2 Beyond Light with the idea that this game will be your main game to play with your friends. And don't even think about playing this solo, its amount of content you can do is cut immensely.
That combined with Bungie having literally no idea what the community wants and there being little to no feedback ever taken from the community ever taken seriously.
I was gonna write more but its not even worth the time.\t\t\t\t\t
