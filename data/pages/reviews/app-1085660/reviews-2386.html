\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed hundreds of hours on the Battle.net launcher. Destiny 2 is a great game and I highly recommend it, but it's also not without flaws.
I am very disappointed in Shadowkeep. The story was confusing and the ending felt unsatisfying and unfinished, like "what? that was it? seriously?" It does not hold up to forsaken at all.
I am also disappointed in the fact that you no longer earn a bright engram upon every "level up" now.
and I don't mind the new Armor 2.0 for the most part, I think it's great you have better control over your stats and I love the way the mods work now. However, Armor affinity has GOT TO GO. I shouldn't have to grind the perfect stats and then realize that the piece has the wrong affinity for the mods I want to use.
There are never any new enemies and always endlessly recycled and re-skinned enemies.
Besides these things I have enjoyed playing and have had a blast playing with friends. Destiny has some of the smoothest and most fun gun play I have ever experienced.\t\t\t\t\t
