\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny has embodied the "Maximize Time Played" metric and not the "Fun" metric. Everything is time-gated. Each season is a repeat of the last season where they roll in a new armor set, up the arbitrary power number to "strive for" by a few numbers, and introduce a couple new weapons When the next season rolls out you do the literal same thing again. It's World Of Warcraft with 1/500th of the lore and actual content. If you want a breakdown please enjoy this list.

1. If you like lore and story missions: get ♥♥♥♥♥♥, nerd. I assume thats their message as they've deleted so many actual story missions and areas from the game to "save space' that you have no remote CLUE as to what the hell is going on unless you have a friend guiding you or a youtube video that will spell it out for in some boring narrative instead of having fun by..I don't know...playing through it...you know...like a video game would do.

2. PvE: I hope you like seeing the same five dungeons getting completely blasted through on rapid repeat because that is what this game is. The "end game" of this is to get the same weapons over and over and over until you get a "God Roll" which means it's randomized stats are just slightly better than other rolls it can drop with. Hurray! Your gun will kill 0.0001% faster than everyone else's gun. You're so cool. It was worth the 1800 runs of the same dungeon to get it...right? After that just do this again with the "raids" but with 6 people instead of 3 and the raid takes an hour + instead of a couple minutes and you will somehow get LESS loot. Amazing. BOSSES! Every single boss in the game has immune phases. It isn't fun. It's LITERALLY just annoying. Because the bosses are so easy. They go immune because if that wasn't the mechanic you would kill them immediately. Also, difficult modes tend to hit way harder but mainly just means everything is a bullet sponge now. ...Hurray.

3. PvP: Same thing as PvE really. Everyone uses the same 6 weapons (same can be said for PvE) because all other weapons are so ♥♥♥♥ compared to them that they may as well not exist in the game...at all. For real. Even in PvE everyone uses the same 2 heavy weapons because nothing else is even acceptable. PvP gets the added bonus of never having the classes AND weapons being balanced so they end up shutting down an entire playlist or LITERALLY locking out your preferred class loadout because they don't know how to balance it's abilities.

4. User Interface. I could go on all day but let me be concise. It's a flaming pile of ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. It's built for controllers and if you're on PC with M&K you're SOL. It's like if Bill Gates had a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ stroke while designing windows 8 touchpad menus. It's so cumbersome, incoherent, and borderline useless, that you spend more time ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ with the menus than you do actually accomplishing anything in said menus. Everything is laid out in the worst possible way they could have designed. Truly a masterpiece.

5. Bugs. How? How have you had such a huge company working on this game for so long to have so many absolutely broken glitches in this game? You've got time and resources to introduce 15 more items to the real money shop in game every other week but you seem to be just a little short handed on working out the issues of functionality.

I got this game to mindlessly blow stuff up with friends who already have the game but I can't motivate myself to play it anymore because it is LITERALLY just a repetitive circle of weak ass dopamine hits that works on people who are easily pleased by shiny colors and numbers. They want you to stay in the game longer so that you fall into the sunk cost fallacy and buy their in-game ♥♥♥♥ that they pump out relentlessly. Activision made this exact same player engagement model for WoW and it's moving towards losing half it's remaining player count, if that says anything. I'll probably hop on it just to spend some time with friends who now live across the world but I sincerely hope they choose to play a better game soon because this one is a chore.

Edit: I tried to pick the game back up to give it another chance and see if it was worth it. lol nah\t\t\t\t\t
