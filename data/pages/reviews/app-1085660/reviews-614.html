\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNow to start, I never leave reviews, this is the first review I've put on a game in over 3 years and I think it's due time.
Now i like Destiny 2, I think the gunplay is excellent, the loot game is strong and the game can at times be really great value.... BUT and there is a massive BUT, if you plan to buy anything from this game, just know you are essentially almost renting DLC. They have removed so much content in a small amount of time its sickening, content that we as consumers paid FULL RETAIL PRICE FOR! (and some on sale) and they have the gall to remove it. I was not told my purchase meant ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ 2 years from the purchase date, it's only come to light recently that the money you spend on this game is only temporary.
I love this game, hell I love destiny 1 aswell, (and at least that game still has all the content that we paid for) most of my hours were spent in Vanilla Destiny 2 and most of that is gone.
To summarise, if you are interested in this game give it a try but please know that any money you spend on this game will leave you with nothing in 2 years.\t\t\t\t\t
