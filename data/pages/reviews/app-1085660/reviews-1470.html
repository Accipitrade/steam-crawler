There are community made tools to find people to play with, The PC LFG discord, and Bungie Forums/lfg off the top of my head.
They have slightly solved this issue with Vault Of Glass and Prophecy, Free to Play Raid and dungeon respectively, but to get access to the 4 other raids you must buy every expansion (Forsaken - Last Wish, Shadowkeep - Garden Of Salvation, Beyond Light - Deep Stone Crypt, and Witch Queen- Vow Of The Disciple). If are desperate to find a game you'd want to play, and are willing to spend money to get the full experience, and force your friends to come along with you, or make new ones, this is the game for you.

I want this game to do well, but, please think of yourself before playing.
