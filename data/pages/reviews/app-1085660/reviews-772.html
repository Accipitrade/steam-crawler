\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed it a little. It's nice. Transmog is a little clunky, just like the shader system. Gun play and movement is good, hitreg, especially in pvp, can be a little streaky and I think the balancing in pvp in general needs a rework, but Bungie has already hinted that they will fix some things in the coming months, so I have hope.
Graphics are great, just like the music. Dialogues are a little cringe from time to time and large parts of the story are in lore books, which I don't like that much, but I bet there are ppl out there who like it. Other than that, pretty good game that managed to hold my interest for a long time. You can probably add another 1000 hours to my play time, since the game used to be on battle.net.

Firm recommendation, if you are looking for a sci-fi FPS with satisfying gun play, smooth movement (not titanfall-level smooth, but it's pretty simple and allows for a moderatly fast pace of gameplay) and an evolving storyline.\t\t\t\t\t
