For myself, Iron Banner and Trials are awful due to there being a massive gulf between those who are amazing at it and everyone else. They reward those who are amazing whereas the majority of players cannot even attempt them and ask for groups to jump and kill themselves rather than play.

Raids similarly need a little more help for those less experienced to get into as again players expect other players to be expert straight away. I have ended up trying to solo whatever I can rather than face getting kicked for not being really good at something straight away.

After 1300 hours my crucible play is getting a bit better, but not great. Maybe this year I will persevere with raid groups and get some knowledge there too.

Despite the Trials, Banner and Raids being downsides for me, overall the game is fantastic as there is plenty to do even if there are some parts that do not agree with you.\t\t\t\t\t
