\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played D2 on Xbox when it was first released and quit after two weeks because i simply got bored. I am now returning on PC as it is free atm and i have to say, its in a worse state then when i left. Lets talk about the things destiny does right because its a much shorter list.

1. Graphics & Sound:
The game is gorgeous. The environment vistas literally take your breath away. Musical scores envoke all the emotion they are designed to. 10/10

2. Gameplay:
Game play is smooth and well refined. Expected from a AAA developer responsible for the halo series. What i didn't expect was the addictive interactions you have with your friends chaining supers and skills together to create memorable moments that keep bringing me back to this abusive relationship. 8/10

That unfortunately is where the brilliance dries up. Lets touch on the draw backs.

1. Story:
Strictly speaking from a new player point of view, there is none. i spawned into the game max level with no idea what my character abilities were. The game threw about 50 random bounties at me telling me to go to places i've never heard of for no particular reason and all the missions while interesting feel like they have no purpose other then to shoot stuff. Which needless to say is very unrewarding. This was a massive disappointment because when i first started there was a campaign which at least it gave some context. They went and removed it and we are right back where D1 was with no story. 1/10

2. Progression:
This is probably the biggest draw back because it is a fundamental aspect of a looter shooter. As mentioned the game spawns you in at max character level which is 750. This might be great for existing players who just want to power level their 2nd or 3rd characters but for new players it completely ruins any sort of connection you have with your character as there is no development time. You then are just told to do random missions to get gear to level your gear score to soft cap which is 950.

You then have to complete specific quests to farm "powerful Items" which are locked behind time walls (you can only do them once a week) THIS IS THE ONLY WAY TO GET PAST SOFT CAP.

ALSO congratulations i have just explained the entire game to you. That's all there is to it and a lot of the time you are farming the same quests over and over again because they are the allocated quest for that week.

The progression in this game is ultimately flawed and has been since D1 was released in 2014. 6 years of development and they cant figure out how to make a rewarding gameplay experience that doesn't involve locking content behind time walls.

Which brings me back to the abusive relationship comment. The progression in this game is so bad that i physically makes me angry, but i keep coming back because the gameplay is so good and it just frustrates the hell out of me.

My advice is to give the game a go. Its free you will enjoy parts of it. DONT buy the season pass. its a rip off and you get nothing out of it and i would hold off buying the expansions till you decide where you like the game or not.
