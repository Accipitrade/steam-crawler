UPDATE: Witch Queen!

I took a long break and WOW Beyond Light was a bit of a bummer, Unbalanced subclass, piss poor villain and main plot writing; HOWEVER! the surrounding stories where brilliant! Banshee's exotic, and the deep stone crypt lore is really interesting, and the exo stranger is utilized brilliantly, being a fully fleshed out character now.

As the seasons moved forwards into witch queen I noticed a pretty drastic uptick in how the story was being written and how the characters were being explored. They have started to utilize their seasons to tell micro stories that are engaging and fun, expanding the world a little bit and giving you something to do that doesn't feel tacked on and totally repetitive like in season of the worthy. Each season has a unique event, that, while repeatable, gives you an extra thing to do to expand the battle pass and gives you seasonal loot, a chance at a new title, etc.


The thing that has gotten me back into the game however, is the class changes. They have fully revamped and reworked the base classes, and I gotta say, this is better than ever, including Destiny 1's system.

The choices on offer are really intricate and allow for actual build-making that I never expected Bungie capable of. Their game design strategy seems to have shifted from over-simplicity and frugality to generosity and complexity, giving players a whole bunch of build options without making us over powered.

all in all, the changes have allowed me to use The Last Word in pve without feeling like an ingrate. id honestly give the game a 3/5, solid title. Price of entry is steep, but with a good group of friends, like any multiplayer game, i may add, this becomes a lot of fun. cheaper than ffxiv if you do the math anyhow.\t\t\t\t\t
