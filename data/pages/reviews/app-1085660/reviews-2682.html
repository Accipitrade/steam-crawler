\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSolid shooter with MMO elements, great gunplay and varied modes of play. Technically the game is impressive, graphics and sound are top-notch, with expansive game environments with some fantastic level design. Game is mostly enjoyable, although there is serious grind for most quests and equipment - which could be seen as a positive/negative depending on what you're looking for.

Major gripe with the game is Bungie seem to be confused on how to monetise the game. The best looking gear require real-money transactions in the Eververse, which is the norm for a Free to Play game, however is kick in the balls if you've already paid $100 to buy both expansions. The in-game store is constantly being filled with new loot, yet the rest of the game is crying for more carrots to chase from a loot perspective.

Definitely worth checking out via Free to Play, however I'd struggle to recommend buying the expansions.
