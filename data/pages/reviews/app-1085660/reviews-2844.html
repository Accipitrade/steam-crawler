\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Update Review

The game was fun and I ended up buying all of the dlc (at the time) and all of the the season passes for Year 3. I had a lot of fun and put a lot of time into the game. Some seasons were better than others but I felt like I got my money’s worth out of it. Then towards the end of Year 3 the content vault was announced and that more than half of the content was going away. I decided to hold off on purchasing the new dlc, Beyond Light, to see if it would reconcile the lost content. I am glad I did.

Beyond Light dropped and with it half of the activities in game disappeared, activities I previously paid for to be able to play them. I would have understood making them free to play but completely removing them from the game making them completely unplayable seemed dirty to me. I decided to stick it out a bit longer as a free-to-play player before making any final judgement. But what I kept finding was the bulk of the content removed was the content that was available to free-to-play players. The game became completely lifeless for me with nothing left to do. This almost got me to purchase Beyond Light (probably the goal) but after looking into the content added and planned to be added it was not even close to the content removed and with the price increase compared to Shadowkeep it felt like a slap in the face.

I can no longer stand by my original review as the game is basically empty for free-to-play players with little to do. I do not play the game anymore; I wish that weren’t the case as I really enjoyed it before but the way everything was handled left such a bad taste in my mouth I had to stop. Maybe one day I will pick up the game again and see what I missed but that is contingent on things still being available down the road and not stuck into the vault.
