\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've had a lot of fun with Destiny 2 in the beginning but I feel the game has gotten worse over time. which is strange. To summarize here are a few issues I have with Destiny 2:

- Navigation of multiple menus is a huge mess

-Netcode is terrible causing random disconnects and can even block friends from joining your party

- Armor 2.0 There is nothing like grinding for armour every season just to get the newest socket with random stats on the armor. Very grindy and not rewarding in the least.

- Sunsetting of weapons forces you to not use weapons that you have spent a lot of time grinding to get. Many of these items will be reintroduced into the game in future seasons and you will have to grind to unlock the same gun with same stats you already had to get the new seasonal socket and ithe new item level cap.

TTK - The time to kill in this game is insane now often under a second. It is faster than even COD. In the beginning the game was slower paced ideally having your fire team focus and targets to bring them down using teamwork.

These are just a few issues that I have with Destiny 2. I really enjoyed the game for a while but too many other games do things better than this game.

Purchased Witch Queen on sale having a blast again. PvP is still messed up but I am having fun again,\t\t\t\t\t
