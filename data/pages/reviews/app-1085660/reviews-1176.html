PvP can and often is a major sweat fest. The removal of content; strikes, raids etc is a bummer as well. I certainly wish Bungie would have simply stated that they are removing the content with the intent of reworking it and it returning at a later time. The player base should not have to deal with the results of developers not doing thorough testing on weapons/armor they release. Recluse and Mountaintop are prime examples of this.

All that being said, would I recommend this game to others? As a PvE game to be done with friends, absolutely.\t\t\t\t\t
