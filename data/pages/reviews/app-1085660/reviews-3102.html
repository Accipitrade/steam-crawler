\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: 8/10
FFO: Diablo 3, Halo, Borderlands, WoW

Imagine this game as owning a hot-rod. Its a constant grind to improve your characters and get them loaded up on gear. But is well worth it when you get to take them out for a spin with shiny new weapons. Once you get into the flow of the game it is super addicting and rewarding. Definite elements of grind and RNG but that's what action RPG looters are all about right? PVE is really fun and at times extremely epic (Raids). PVP is unbalanced but if you have the meta weapons its fine.

Story: 5/10 is VERY mediocre and forgettable. Lore is typically picked up via in game achievements. So if youre a sucker for reading up on lore this is definitely better than the actual in game story.

Multiplayer: 4/10 is horrendously unbalanced and never seems like it will ever be balanced. You really do have to be a meta-slave in order to remain competative.

PVE: 8/10 is really where the games has its most epic moments. While the year one content (New Light) is the weakest and certainly the most tedious. The newer content is extremely enjoyable.

Graphically: 9/10 this game is gorgeous. And is very well optimized. However I have noticed since the switch over to Steam that there have been an increase in strange bugs in game. Cannot confirm steam to be the source.

Audio: 10/10 is amazing. Great scoring. Guns sound incredible.
