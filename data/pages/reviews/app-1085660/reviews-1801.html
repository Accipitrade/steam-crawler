\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDestiny 2,

It's a great game if you played it while it was growing into what it is now; Witch Queen. Unfortunately playing it as a New Light player is pretty harsh. Since it doesn't follow Destiny 2's true campaign; Red War, because of the Content Vault that Destiny 2 had introduced to mitigate disk size and resources to maintain it at the developers side of things at Bungie.

Now, because of this New Lights' missed out on great content of Legacy Content: Red War, Curse of Osiris, Warmind, and then Forsaken. So now if you want to want to understand the story of what's been going on you'll need to watch a Synopsis or a Game Movie with only the cutscenes on until Shadowkeep and then you go from there.

Other then that I believe the story, music, and game design is very beautiful and sounds very pleasant and intense at the right time. Then the story is quite good that goes and hits all great elements that make a story great. Great loss and great wins are made in Destiny 2 making it one of my favorite games.

The game is also great to play with friends, because everyone in your fireteam is fighting for the same rewards. I started with a group of my IRL friends and we wanted to do more content, but need more people so we LFG and made a cool community clan that we can all play together.

Customization is also pretty good and you can run almost any build in PVE and PVP (Enemy AI's and Player vs Player) gamemodes. Then they added this universal ornament system that you can wear almost any armor you get from loot and rewards. Which can in return make you look very cool or very goofy!

9/10 - Is my review score and would definitely recommend this game!\t\t\t\t\t
