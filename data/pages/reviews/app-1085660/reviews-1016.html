\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am very much a casual Destiny fan, so the meta and grind does not really come into play for me for the most part. I enjoy the solid moment to moment gunplay and exploration.
The environments still look fantastic even with Destiny 2 getting higher in years. The sound and visuals are all very well done. Plenty of wow moments with large set pieces.
There are lots of group events to join everywhere and group stuff is where I think this game shines. Seeing 3 guardians rushing in together and slaughtering the enemies is always fun to watch.
I do recommend this game but for me this is a game I would only buy on sale. The content does feel segmented with how it is monetized but overall not terrible.
Give it a shot if you can find the game with it's DLC on sale.\t\t\t\t\t

Cons
The game play loop is vapid. It's extremely repetitive and you notice it.
dozens of currencies, endless materials and currencies
If you made a new character you can't turn off tutorials or skip the tutorial quests
The levels you gain are wiped out every few months with a new DLC
Gear system is overly simplistic. 99% of items don't need to exist.
The mods you can add to your gear become mandatory to participate in higher level content so there's no meaningful choice
The combat mechanics revolve around a series of your opponents being IMMUNE often
Splash damage from enemies completely negates cover.
Your character level depends entirely on your gear, not what you've completed or accomplished.
The level of the gear that drops for you is based on what gear you currently hold so you end up holding on to crappy gear because it's a higher level. What a stupid loot system. So lazy.
Quests and bounties often require you to use weapons and armor you don't want to so you can't stick to a build
Campaign quests often require you to do normal weekly quest/bounty equivalents so there's no real difference.
Nearly all armor that isn't 'exotic' is pointless because it's just different RNG rolls with no meaningful characteristics
PVP revolves around slide + shotgun combo and the meta has been this way for what seems years
The classes players can choose from don't serve different roles they're just slightly different play styles
The stats on armor only have slight impacts on abilities
Voice acting is mediocre at best and the story feels bland because the characters are boring.

I can't emphasize enough how much I felt like there was ONE 'correct' way to play the game and how I couldn't make a build and stick to it. trademark of a good RPG is being able to actually execute a good build and bungie makes you use every gun and change your build on an extremely regular basis just to complete the bounties / quests that are available. it's the laziest bounties(repeatable minor quests) and quests I've ever seen in a triple A title.

The Pros
Music is amazing
graphics are pretty good
Some of the exotic gun mechanics are clever

TLDR Bungie made a very shiny turd.\t\t\t\t\t
