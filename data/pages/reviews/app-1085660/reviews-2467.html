+1800 hours on PS4, moved to PC November 2019 and it was one of the best decisions I've ever made. The game on PC is silky smooth, looks stellar on my monitor, and is an absolute joy to play.
The game is definitely best enjoyed with friends or others, as you'll get access to the endgame such as raids, nightfalls, and trials of Osiris.
I did take a break for a few months from the game earlier this year as I thought the game was lacking in content (and if you're new to destiny this happens a lot, so it's quite normal to feel drained at times), but with the new release of Beyond Light in September I'm loving the game again and can't wait to see how it will evolve.
My final score is 10/10 without question, the story, gameplay, and soundtrack are all amazing and the game is an absolute must-play.\t\t\t\t\t
