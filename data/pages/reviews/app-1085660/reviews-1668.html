\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI need to make one thing clear: I have given years of my life to this game. I've been on this train since the Destiny Beta and just been trucking ever since. I love this game and will play until the day it dies. All that said... it isn't perfect.

I love it, but I loved it a lot more when it was a fresh-ish space RPG. I drank in the lore and devoured every drop of the universe I could get. Destiny 2 came along and the whole thing kept going. It was different, but it improved. Still not perfect, but a blast. However, for me, the game spoiled when they went to a games-as-service model. I don't mind this for many games, but I didn't want it for Destiny. I watched as the emphasis on world building and interesting stories gave way to a constant stream of "CONTENT". By that I mean that it didn't really add to the game. It was just more stuff to do. For me, this didn't bring new air to the sails, but just made it feel like a never-ending slog. I love a game that makes me desire it to never end, not one that tells me it never will.

At the end of the day, though. I still love it. I'll still play day in and day out, I just wish it wasn't the slog it's become.\t\t\t\t\t
