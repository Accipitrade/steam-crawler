\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, game play is great, the guns feel fantastic and have a huge amount of variety in their form and function, the environments are large and beautiful with a lot of attention to detail. Sound design is also great, with a lot of weight purposely thrown into your actions.

But the reason I can't recommend this game is because who knows what it'll be like in 6 months.
Bungie's inconsistent stance on balance and whether or not to sunset equipment, constant use of FOMO to pressure you to spend more money on the shop. (It doesn't matter if you bought the expansions or not.)
And the complete lack of basic, quality of life features or decent tutorials to explain its myriad of game mechanics.

Prime example, they recently introduced a method to make transmog armor pieces, a feature that's been highly requested for half a decade now, it has 5 different currencies to keep up with for this one cosmetic activity. So even when they give you something you want, they still try to twist the knife into you to go pay more real money on garbage.

My recommendation, try it free to play and do your homework on whats a trap or not, but don't pay for any expansions until you've decided you can see past the flaws and shadowy economy. Outside of that, save your time and go play another shooter, maybe one that has a Master Chief in it.\t\t\t\t\t
