\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright after over 1000 hours spent in this game, across console and pc, I can definitely say this game can be amazing and has the potential to be even better, but if ur a hardcore player you will definitely be disappointed with a lot it has to offer as its endgame is ultimately very weak and has a lot of balancing issues that often take way too long to fix.

Quick note, this game does have PvP but it is widely disregarded and I would only recommend you get this game for PvP if you are willing to deal with a lot of balancing issues and grinding in order to get the best builds and gear. There is a small competitive playlist and a hardcore all out Trials game mode that rewards specific loot, but it is very annoying to play unless you have 3 very competent friends or teammates who are willing to go through the ringer as it is a very sweaty playlist.

If you're new to the game then I would definitely recommend trying it now that it's free, as many core aspects of the game are great and the game just feels very smooth and great overall on pc. Just be aware that the game is live service and you are expected to pay for dlc to get the most out of it. Theres a lot of stuff to experience and going in as a new player is one of the best experiences you can have, but I will say you're gonna want a friend or another experienced player to be with you as the game can get confusing for a new player.

I both love and hate this game but since it is free I don't think I can say that you shouldn't at least try it out for yourself so I recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
