+ Promising stories and good dialogues
+ Good weapons choice (in theory)
+ Many interesting ideas and some mechanics (mostly in old content)

Cons:
- MANY network errors (drop you drom activity or doesn't let to start/join one)

- Lousy netcode in total (especially affect on PvP).

all above makes you to waste MUCH of time.

Same time they continue to drop even being disabled (I found 4 of them while they was disabled. You can pick it up but can't equip as well)

- many, MANY, MANY inane grind.

Still, it's a good experience to get a free version and to play story line (mostly solo). As it's the only thing that doesn't force you to load 16 tons.
