\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGetting to play the classic we know and love as Klonoa again was great, I never played the wii version myself but it was a well done remaster of a fun game, if you've never touched the series before it follows a...uhhh cat? I was never sure what Klonoa was really but I'll guess a long eared cat, still you follow him through a unique land filled with monsters of whom you grab and throw, usually at other enemies, bosses or even to progress through the level in general dood.

Both games have a nice set of levels of which have both vibrant and dark colours to them, making them a treat to traverse, the games can be hard but not the hardest, that said they have an easy mode for those who are struggling, both games pretty much play exactly the same bar one thing in the second game which they added in which is decently fun and around four new ways to traverse through levels via (unless I'm forgetting one lol) new enemy types dood.

It's really hard to go into the stories of both, especially the first one without spoiling it for first timers but the long and short of it is bad thing/guy appears, gotta stop them, it goes into an interesting direction ya kinda don't expect and done lol I still enjoyed them as simple as they were and you should just have fun with it for sure but not entirely expect the most amazing writing ever, it is kinda a kids game after all, even with the darker tones it could hit dood.

Only thing left to talk about is the port itself, seen a lot of talk about stuttering and such, they aren't wrong from my experience, it's not the absolute worst, it seems to effect cutscene's more than anything but it did catch me once on the first Klonoa during a jump making the input invalid, that said only that one time and 2 was much better in that regard, never having it during gameplay and rarely having it in cutscene's outside of the initial loading up of the game dood.

Final words? it's an okay port of the first game that could use a liiittle tuning up and a great port of the second game, if you just want a semi challenging to challenging sidescroller with some nice visuals, music and simple gameplay the this game is for you for sure, thanks for reading dood ^-^7\t\t\t\t\t
