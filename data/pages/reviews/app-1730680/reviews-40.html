\r\n\t\t\t\t\tKlonoa Lunatea's Veil was one of the first 4 games I ever got in a PS2 bundle back around 2001. Of those 4 games Klonoa was my favorite and I have played that PS2 game several times since then. I have a very strong nostalgia for this game, and it was great to find out that this franchise is getting another breath of fresh air for a new audience. Though in classic Klonoa fashion Namco hardly advertises it so this release will probably suffer the same fate as the other releases.

Now this game isn’t that hard, honestly if you don’t care for platformers or want a challenge I wouldn’t recommend it. But if you do enjoy early 2000s platformers that also went the extra mile with a good setting, memorable soundtrack that uniquely uses alot of wind instruments, and a decent story. Well this game became a cult classic for good reason.

The remake is good, it has some quality of life stuff like the ability to skip cutscenese and all graphical options are always accessible. Something some remakes make you have to restart or backout to change. The sound mixing for the first game could be a lot better, voices were very soft compared to music. For the second game I really wish the baked lighting wasn’t so strong, it takes away from the mood the original was setting. Thankfully the sound quality for the second game is great which I am glad cause I’ll say again Lunatea's Veil has a fantastic soundtrack.

Overall while I really wish both games would let you play with original graphics. I can’t hate this remake too much. It honestly is great to have the franchise in the spotlight again, even if just for a little while. This remake/remaster works well enough that it isn’t just a cash grab (though it shouldn't be $40, that's a little too much). I would recommend, though again if you care for platformers that are on the easier side.\t\t\t\t\t

WAHOO! Klonoa Is Back!
Good remastered, but there is some problem

Notes : I still try to finish this game, even I already finished from the old PS1 + Wii (Klonoa 1 Door to Phantomile) and PS2 (Klonoa 2 Lunatea's Veil a.k.a Klonoa 2 Sekai ga Nozonda Wasuremono for Japanese name)

If you have stuttering issue with the game, even with minimum requirements. (For Klonoa 1)
I suggest do this
- Set to Window mode
- Downgrade anti aliasing from 8x to 4x (I don't know why this game suddenly enable this setting)
Because Unity Engine sometimes hogging your memory

The Good
- Two games inside
- Klonoa 2 was better port than PCSX2 (So you could kiss goodbye for buying expensive copy of Klonoa 2 and PS2 console + you could stay away for glitching water on PCSX2)
- Original PS1 voice + With vibrant color (Again, F*** you Wii version!)
- No Sh**ty Mario voice on Klonoa 1 Intro (If you have US Nintendo Wii copy of the game, you know that)
- Easter eggs intro Klonoa 2 before title screen
1. Klonoa Running (Don't press anything)
2. Popka ruined Klonoa (Press LB + RB for Xbox Controller, L1 + R1 for Playstation Controller, Press L + R for Nintendo Switch Controller, Press Q and E from Keyboard user)
3. Lolo fell off (Press LT + RT for Xbox Controller, L2 + R2 for Playstation Controller, Press ZL + ZR for Nintendo Switch Controller, Press Z and C for Keyboard user)

The Problems
- This game doesn't have Demo version for Steam, even Nintendo Switch user needs Japan account to try the demo. For PS4/PS5 and Xbox One/Xbox Series S|X got the demo (What the ****!!!)
- Even The Pre-order was not coming on Steam and US eShop Nintendo Switch but coming on JP eShop was another BAD MARKETING from Bandai Namco
- Klonoa 1 used Wii mechanic which is a problem If you PS1 player before
- Klonoa 1 hit box was not good, even this have problem with PAON version a.k.a Nintendo Wii version, Especially For jumping platform which is a big problem for Dream Stone Perfectionist Collector
- Camera issue problem since Wii version
- Moo a.k.a enemy Sometimes never coming back (Minor issue, resolved with go to the door, and back again)
- Hitbox entering the door

The Missing
- No Mirror mode just like Wii version
- Soundtrack you need buy a DLC or Deluxe version, while the original you unlocked the soundtrack by collection Perfect Dream Stone
- No demo gameplay for Klonoa 1 after Title Screen, and it just goes to first cutscene

Did I still recommend this?
For Standard version -> Yes
For Deluxe version -> No\t\t\t\t\t
