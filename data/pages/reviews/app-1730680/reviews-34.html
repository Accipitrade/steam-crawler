klonoa 2 is amazing in every way, and this remaster gives it a beautiful graphic overhaul that just makes the game all the more enjoyable. the story will pull you through all kinds of emotions, and will probably make you cry by the end. it's something everyone needs to experience for themselves. with an addition of difficulty modes anyone can play this masterpiece now.
