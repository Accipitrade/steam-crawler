\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's one of those ''If steam had a Mixed rating'' instead of negative/positive.
Since it is on sale, i decided to buy it again and give it another chance after i refunded on day release. I still witness stutters within game, although less frequent you still get them, but not enough to detract from the game play like on day 1. Aliasing is still rather bad, but if you go to the options and enable the pixel filter, it does help a lot to make the game look more natural and it even adds more detail to the game. The games don't pack the same punch or detail they once did on previous consoles, but if your new to the franchise you will likely enjoy it.
