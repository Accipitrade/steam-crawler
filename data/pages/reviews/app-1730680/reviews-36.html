BUT, if you want the best experience, I would recommend playing the PS1 version of the first Klonoa before playing this remake. The presentation, cutscene animation, and level design take considerable steps backwards compared to the original, which is a damn shame. There's still fun to be had here, and it can definitely be enjoyable, but the PS1 original is a near perfect game imo, so I would recommend firing up an emulator to play that version (which is incredibly easy to do). Klonoa 2 in this collection is great though, highly recommend!\t\t\t\t\t

really well done on adding those features Bandai-Namco


Is the Remake a good Remake? Yes, this remake is 100000% better than whatever than anything Wii version was attempting to do
The Characters, Game play, OST, Map design are all on point!
Its a cute adorable game with an Amazing story makes me cry every time
Little upset not all the cutscenes are pre rendered videos like the original as I feel some of the emotion is lost but i'll live with it, it's not the end of the world

How well does the Remake Play?
It plays fantasy, unbelievably well it's buttery smooth and is a game anyone can pick up and understand and I would Highly recommend to anyone


Is the remake perfect? No, it's not it has some really odd issues

Audio setting can not be accessed until you're in-game? And even if you do adjust them the title screen is still LOUD! (ones of the reasons i hate Sonic all stars racing transformed)

It ruins great game design
Perhaps have the Boss Tutorial Pop-up if the player has died alot and/or alot of time has passed with no progress being made
Instead of the second the fight starts
