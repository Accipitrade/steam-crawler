Greetings! A quick DLC Review for the Wey-Yu Armoury Pack on Aliens: Fireteam Elite a small DLC that mainly comes with cosmetics and minor items. This is one of the best of the cosmetics DLCs in my opinion but is it worth it? Let’s Discuss.

Gear Up Marines: Yet another heavy cosmetic DLC that includes new armor skins, head accessories, weapon skin / decals and some emotes. It also comes with some in game currency and consumables. I do really enjoy the more armored and high end look that these skins provide and found myself opting to use these skins until I unlocked some of the rarer ones. As usual I never found myself using the decals but some of the colors were nice and the in-game currency / consumables were nice.

Although I’d never pay full retail for this DLC it does have my preferred helmet and the outfits are good. Wait for a sale or grab it with the Deluxe Edition but not a bad purchase all things considering. Enjoy!
