\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is nearly 6 years old now... and is almost universally beloved... I've never once heard a bad thing said about it.

BUT NEVER FEAR, FOLKS!
I, and my massive ego, will somehow write a review that is going to help those on the fence and change the hearts of the those who hate the genre. Here goes!

This game is a Walking Simulator. I hate the name for the genre... but others have spoken.
What they mean is you're going to walk through this story as it unfolds. More of an exploratory movie than a game.

The narrators are all unreliable, so don't expect a ton of resolution. What you WILL get is a ton (metric!) of story that keeps you invested. Everything you see will be a clue and foreshadowing and also completely immaterial... and also essential.

It's gonna bring the Interactive.
It's gonna bring the Feels.
And boy, will those Feels be Interactive!

If you are remotely curious about the genre, this is about the best gosh darned place to start!
If you want to shoot and punch things, consider it sometime when you want a change of pace? But otherwise avoid.

It is a great 2-3 hour investment for those who want a rich story.

And... since I fear I may have oversold things a little, let me say: This game sucks.
