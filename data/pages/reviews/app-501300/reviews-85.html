\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat can I say about this game that hasn't already been said? In short, it is absolutely incredible. I first played this game on PS4 the year it launched, and in my entire life as a gamer there has never been a game that I went out of my way to purchase on two different platforms. Until now. When I saw it on sale in the Steam store, I immediately jumped at the opportunity to experience it again, and to support the extremely talented team who created it. And after 5 years, it absolutely still holds up.

At its core, What Remains of Edith Finch could be described as a walking simulator and a visual novel, but it is so, so much more than that. This games unique method of storytelling had me immediately immersed, and engaged me emotionally like very few games have before. From the very start I was completely invested in the history of the Finch family, and my desire to learn more about their fate simply kept growing until the game's climax.

Bottom line, I have absolutely nothing bad to say about this game, and I cannot recommend it enough.\t\t\t\t\t
