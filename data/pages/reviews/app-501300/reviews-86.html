\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR, "What Remains of Edith Finch" is good for what it is, but I don't think this is what you want.

To start, I would not call this a "game." If there was a style for this, maybe I would call it an interactive novel. I also would say this is most likely not what most people are looking for. This is a walk through/ interactive story more than anything. Its not bad at all, just not what I was looking for. The art is is fine. The sound is fine. The controls are fine. It never crashed. I was not hunting anything down though. Unless you wanted to get all of the achievements, I don't think it really has any replay value outside of wanting to hear the story again... It's a tragic story that does not resolve. I find this to be a problem in almost every form of entertainment these days. Nobody just ends a story these days. This title is neat for what it is, but I would not call it a game. If there was a proper classification for it, than could recommend "What Remains of Edith Finch."\t\t\t\t\t
