I totally applaud the concept of this game, good job, but at the end... It's not like I wanted to keep playing some more, it's more like I wish the game had developed the characters to a deeper level to allow me to feel more of a connection. 11-11 and Painscreek set the benchmark for me. Both with this game and a real cemetery, I feel that the whole story, it just isn't there! Well done on the powerful ending.
