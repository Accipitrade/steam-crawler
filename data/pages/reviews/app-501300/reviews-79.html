The game takes about 3h or more to finish, depending on how much you like to explore and look at all the details :)
It is pretty much a walking simulator without any puzzles to solve, where the main story line unfolds itself as you progress.

If Outer Wilds (and SOMA) was 10/10, I would give this game 5/10. Not great, but not bad either. Just something to experience.

I don’t usually review the price for games, but for 20 € and about 3 h of gameplay, and considering not a very memorable story line, it is too much however.\t\t\t\t\t
