When Edith grew older (16/17), she returned home with a key that she had inherited from her late mother. Edith hoped to find some answers or clues about what her mother had been hiding from her growing up. Playing through the eyes of Edith, you are encouraged to explore your childhood home. To sum it up, this game consists of short stories about the Finch family, which are interactive and quite clever/fun. The graphics are pretty as well.
