I paid 10€ for it and believe it was worth the price. It took me 2 hours to get through the game and it's an appropriate length for the game. Pretty much a perfect walking simulator, but it's ordinarily priced at 20€ and I don't believe it's worth 20€. Given Steam's binary review system I have to vote this game down.

It's very much recommended on a sale, or if you don't care about the price. If games are to be compared and valued in some form, 15€ for these 2 hours would be pushing it, but anything lower is recommended by me.\t\t\t\t\t
