\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch has a story to tell. It tells that story in a way unmatched by any other story-driven game. It's polished, beautiful and immersive and you'll enjoy every second of it.

The developers invested greatly on perfecting the story, which clearly shows in the quality of this game.
However, this is also it's main drawback, as the story is not a long one.
In fact, you will likely only play two hours before completing the game, and at that point you might aswell uninstall, as you probably won't play it for a very long time.

Conclusion
If you want to be immersed, I thoroughly recommend this game. Especially when it's on sale. But not if you're looking for a long-term investment.

+ Stunning storyline
+ Excellent visuals
+ Controls

- Very short (2hr)
- No replay ability\t\t\t\t\t
