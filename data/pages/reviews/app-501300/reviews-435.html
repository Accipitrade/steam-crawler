\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood stuff:
It's an interesting story, to say the least. The visual storytelling, the musical selection, and the general direction of how the player interacts with the plot is so on point. This game really blended the interactivity of a video game and the observing type nature of a movie.

Still good, but could do better stuff:
I do, however, feel this game is a little over-hyped. It's certainly good, but I'm not sure about 10/10. I'd probably give it a solid 8.5 as the story was great, but the ending left me wanting more. The game filled me up with a million curiosities, and then when I got to the end and a bunch of those questions got answered, there were a still a few important ones that never got fully addressed. Perhaps that it'll-never-fully-be-understood type feeling is what the game is going for, so if you like that, you'll like the game more then I did. It's certainly intersting enough that I'd want to play the whole thing again to re-experience the story.\t\t\t\t\t
