\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot what I expected but I am so glad that I played it. 8/10.

Like 'Inside' and 'Firewatch', this experience has been very carefully and lovingly crafted for our enjoyment.
The music is beautiful, the house that you will explore is full of surprises and, even though the walking sections are intentionally slow, I am glad that I stuck with it and discovered the sad and tragic stories of the Finch family.

This is a hard game to write a review for, so I'll keep it simple - if you have kids, play it, if you don't and you enjoyed 'Inside' or 'Firewatch', play it.

I played with the Xbox PC controller and the game felt designed for that kind of controller.

I don't want to single out music or graphics or gameplay because this game successfully combines all of the key elements of a great game so well that it would be unfair to focus on one part of the experience.

Enjoy it.
