\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch can be described as a walking simulator with a great story, but leaving it at that would sell this game short and deprive it of the praise that it truly deserves.

To me, this game can be described as Awe-Inspiring, Whimsical, Fantastical, Mildly Depressing, and most importantly, Clever. The way this game tells its story was clever in every way. So clever in fact that even something as simple as subtitles were taken to the next level. Instead of static lines of script that pop up at the bottom of the screen, when someone talks, they in corporate the appearance, movement, and disappearance of every line of text into the story. For the commentary, this helps to tell a more visual tale than just an audible one.

The story and writing is downright amazing as well. I couldn't stop playing, because I wanted to see what happened to the next family member. And although it is a walking simulator, there are still some gameplay elements in it that are intriguing, clever, and fun. Even if you are not into "walking simulator" type games, I'm sure this one will change your mind about them.

Every now and then a game comes along that is so amazing where I just HAVE to right a review about it. I cannot recommend it enough. Please do yourself a favor and buy this game it is amazing!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really don't know what to say about this game. Like this game is one of the few games to have left me speachless at the end of the first playthrough. What Remains of Edith Finch is to me an excellent example of story telling and environmental exploration through broken narrative.

Pros:
+An amazing story, with an intersting plot and compelling narrative that keeps the player quessing
+Great, well orchestrated soundtrack
+Wonderful and creative world design and level design
+Interesting characters and good voice acting

Cons:
-The game is only around two hours long and is maybe worth one or two playthroughs after the first
-The game is great, but the twenty dollar price may be to much for some, and especially for two hours of gameplay
-Not much in gameplay, the story is the center piece of the game, so most of the game is walking around and taking in the story

This game is worth the time and investment put into it, but I would wait for a sale. What Remains of Edith Finch is an excellent game that I recommend anyone that is a fan story-driven games.\t\t\t\t\t
