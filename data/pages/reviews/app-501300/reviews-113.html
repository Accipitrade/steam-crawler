A must play for walking simulator fans

If you enjoyed other similar games like The Stanley Parable, Firewatch, Gone Home and Dear Esther, you will love this game. If you are interested in the genre, I highly recommend this one to start.

I started playing without much expectations but I have to admit that I understand why it won so many well deserved awards.
Without providing any spoilers, the plot is a bit like Gone Home but much more elaborate and with a story that is beautifully told in many very interesting ways, let's say it's many stories inside the story. It makes the game brillant as there is much imagination involved and it never gets boring.
Curiosity and exploration are rewarded as the house you visit is full of little details.
There is almost zero difficulty and no puzzles so it's much more like an interactive movie and you can never get really stuck so you don't lose the immersion.

The music is great and quite catchy in some parts, it sets the mood really well without never making it a horror game.

The game can be finished in 2-3 hours hence it might be a bit expensive for some. I would recommend it to anyone looking to enjoy an interactive story that is well crafted and a wonderful adventure to experience.\t\t\t\t\t

A Generational Mystery.

Graphics/Performance

Graphics are pretty nice. It runs well in Linux on a RTX 3090 with 4k resolution. The house if full of interesting lore and detail.

Gameplay

This is mostly a walking simulator. There isn't much gameplay except for some mini games.

Sound

Great voice cast, the main theme has a haunting solitude to it. Very much like a movie's soundtrack.

Final Thoughts

I'm really liking the games published by Annapurna. This one is no exception and was a solid experience. It's basically an interactive movie and about the length of one too. You can probably finish this in one evening of gaming. The story is great and really pulls you in for the ride till the end of the game. This review was intentionally vague to not ruin the plot.

1 thumb up.\t\t\t\t\t
