\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best horror games of this decade!

I really loved the creepy dark old house, a bit similar to the one in Resident Evil 7, the atmostphere was amazing, reminded me of John Carpenter's 'Halloween' (1978) which is one of the best horror movies in my opinion. I also love how the game doesn't rely on jumpscares, just pure psychological horror. Game likes to use a lot of colours and happy music, but that's an irony, I love it.
The level of detail is insane, there are tiniest details in level design like books/tapes/notes each having different text on them and there's just tons of stuff/things scaterred around on every corner.
The storyline is fantastic. Loved how the game proceeded. I love the characters and the great protagonyst, it's very extremely rare to see a great female protagonyst, but this game finaly brings a fantastic female protagonyst who has great depth and character.
The game is also very sad too, especiallly ending

Cons:
- Controller doesn't work, though it should, many people experienced this issue.
- The walking speed is ass
- Way too short - around 3h long
- Final sequence is lacking something
- Wish there there some kind of puzzles.




A must play horror walking simulator.

9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
