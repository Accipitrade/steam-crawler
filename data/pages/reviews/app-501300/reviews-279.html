Short quick warning to anyone who might be like me:
If you have any problem at all with hunting or killing animals, regardless if the killing and hunting is "just an hallucination" or "a dream" or actually (ingame) real... this game is sadly not for you.

Under normal circumstances I love story-telling games and most of the time I have no problem with killing people in games but as soon as it comes to killing cute or cuddly, innocent souls, talking about "gobbling them up" and "seeing the fear in their eyes" I am just out.

This was just a small scene in this Game so far (and I didn't played it much) and I am sure "it gets better" and I just "have to play through this" but I can't. I tried, I felt sick, I cried, I quit the game.

So if you are anything like me, fair warning: this Game is not for the cuddly-hearted.

Why do I still recommend this game?
Well, the game itself doesn't feel like it deserves a bad review. I trust in the recommendation of some friends (who apparently forgot that scene was in there) and just look at the good reviews, the critics and the screenshots alone. This game looks gorgeous and special.
Sadly .. not for me. :(\t\t\t\t\t

I bought this game in June of 2020 (yesterday at the time of writing this post) and ran into a glitch in the story that has apparently been around since release. During one character's story, a wall that is supposed to be broken down just . . . isn't. The game glitches and I'm stuck in a room with no way to move forward. I wanted to be immersed. I wanted to go on a journey with this game, but this glitch really ruins the experience. Until they fix it, I can't in good conscience recommend Edith Finch. How can I complete the story if one section just stops my progression in learning about this family? I wish the developers would fix it, but after learning the bug's been around since 2017, my hopes are not high.\t\t\t\t\t
