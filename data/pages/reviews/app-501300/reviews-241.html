\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are many people who look at this genre of games and call them "non-games."

Man, I have been playing video games my entire life. I have played every genre under the sun. I have played the huge AAA blockbusters, the skill-based multiplayer, the soulsborne stuff (or whatever it's called). No shade on any of them, but-

This game is not what games should be exclusively, but it is what games should aspire to be. There should always be games that challenge the notion of what is possible in an interactive experience. The whole "games are art" debate is a meme at this point but...yeah, they kinda are. This thing is one of the best examples of that. It's also one of the greatest achievements in interactive media to date.

Is it the best game ever made? Wrong question: it's impossible to pick just one. Is it one of the best games ever made? Absolutely.\t\t\t\t\t
