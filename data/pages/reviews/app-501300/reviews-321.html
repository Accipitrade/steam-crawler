\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a brilliant and highly detailed game! I can't imagine the passion and dedication this game required of the development team! An amazing amount of thoughtfulness in the decorations of the house. It really blew my mind!

Calling this a walking sim is a great disservice to this game, because it's so much more! Your character morphs into different beings, you explore a house filled with secret passages, and secrets, and the past lives of an unusually eccentric family. Great imagination was used to tell the many stories of the house's past residents.

My only real disappointment, and it's a big one, is the lackluster and depressing ending. The game had been so imaginative and unique up until the last few minutes, and then it all just kind of stopped. IMO, this game deserved a much more exciting, twisting, unique, or imaginative ending. A wonderful game, that dies in a quick fade. But before the end - it's a very nice ride! Get it on sale, and enjoy the interesting and imaginative storytelling techniques!
