\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBasically this is a game where you walk through a crazy house reading about how all your relatives died. You'd expect it to be a sad game from that summary, but all the deaths are so ridiculous that they're way more funny than sad. I laughed pretty hard at a few of them.
The ending also completely lacks resolution to any of the stuff in the game. I know that's intentional but I still don't like it, it's unsatisfying and stupid.
Gameplay-wise it's pretty terrible, most of the game is holding W or LMB to advance while listening to narration. There are a few things in the environment you have to click on to discover, but it's like 75% totally linear. In addition, the times when you aren't just holding W or LMB you're likely to accidentally trigger the wrong action and spend an unreasonably long time waiting for an animation to complete and then waiting again for it to reverse once you get out of it.
There was exactly one segment in the game where game mechanics were used in an interesting way (the fish/imagination part). The rest would have been improved by removing player interaction and turning it into an animation or something.

Overall: 5/10, play it if you have 2 hours to spare, want some laughs, and don't care about a satisfying ending.\t\t\t\t\t
