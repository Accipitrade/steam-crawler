\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a beautiful, haunting, surreal experience--not really a game but more of a story that you wander through. It took me only a few hours to go through it, but there's a lot about it that I have no doubt will stick with me for a long time.

You take on the perspective of Edith Finch, the last surviving member of the Finch family, as she returns to her deserted childhood home to reconstruct what happened to her relatives dating back three generations. Each relative has their own unique perspective and story, each happening in a different time period and many having distinctive styles to match the era they come from.

There's many aspects of the game that are especially strong. The voice actress who does the main narration as Edith is superb and ties the various individual stories together with a beautiful mix of wistfulness and stoicism. The variety of visual styles and most of the individual stories are captivating and tragic, each in its own way. The music is beautiful. The weakest part of the game is the actual interface, which is sometimes a bit awkward. The price point is approximately right--I bought it on sale and feel like I got a pretty good deal for my money.

If you like games for the story and immersion, this one's definitely worth checking out.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDifficult game to review. The graphics are lovely, with great attention to detail for the most part. You definitely get drawn in to the story, and some of the meta-stories are captivating.
I got it on sale so I'm happy - but it is only 3 hours long. Not much then... but so beautifully crafted it's hard to complain.
Then there's the story of the family... while there are some imaginative elements - they are all quite fantastical - and i don't feel like i know this family any better by the end. The mini-stories don't form a meaningful grand narrative that is in any way coherent. This makes the stories enjoyable but, at least for me, ultimately unsatisfying.
I will give it a big thumbs up overall, because it has clearly had a lot of thought and attention paid to create something very interesting and at times immersive. I'd buy a part two, but I'd be hoping that there is a little more reality to ground everything so i can genuinely get invested in a deeper way.\t\t\t\t\t
