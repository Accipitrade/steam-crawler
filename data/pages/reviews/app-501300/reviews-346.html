You are taking the role of Edith Finch, who is coming home after a long time to learn about her family history. You go trough each family member's story one by one, which are presented in an breathtaking, unique way, and in the end you learn something valuable about yourself.
The game is dealing with issues on multiple psychological levels (themes such as fantasy prone personality, loss, dealing with overwhelming emotions of anger, guilt, fear..) and invokes great sense of empathy, providing you with understanding about something you may or may have not experienced, teaching a lesson not only to Edith, but also the player.
