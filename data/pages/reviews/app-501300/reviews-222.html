WOW! SO MUCH TO DISCOVER UNDER ONE ROOF!

Death is a topic that many cultures tend to tone down with the use of euphemisms or avoid altogether. It is a topic that is seldom embraced or celebrated (although Mexico celebrates Dia de los muertos – Day of the Dead) due to its morbidity and people’s fear of it. I believe the Finch Family feared it. But are curses real? They become real when we believe they are. When we begin to fear them, we give them power to take over.
In the movie “Big Fish,” a man on his deathbed tells his family stories of the grand life he led in the form of tall tales (or are they?). So why not use the same approach with the topic of death? Why not use a bit of fantasy and creativity to remember the way we exited the world? How would you like to be remembered?
PROS: beautiful scenery and music, interesting stories and creative ways of letting us find out what happened (each story has a different type of game-play), relatively easy game play, emotional stories
CONS: sometimes the game is too dark and it’s hard to see (but maybe that’s the point metaphorically), some stories are too sad and leaves a hole in my heart, some stories should have more follow up (game-play is a bit short), some game lags, a person could get easily lost in such a big house and miss some stories (I missed some the first time I played!)

Every family has its deep, dark secrets, I suppose….

Questions and feelings I have at the end of the game:
----------------------------------------------------------

1.\tWhich death hit you hardest? For me, that would be Gregory and the bathtub scene. Poor baby.
2.\tWhich story did you find most interesting? For me, I really enjoyed how they did Barbara’s death sequence in the form of a Halloween comic. Reminded me of “Creepshow.” I enjoy things like that.
3.\tIs the “curse” broken? I was sad that Edith died during childbirth. I had hoped that uncovering the unfortunate deaths of the Finch family members would finally end the curse because the fear would be gone. But, I guess Edith feared it as well. I hope her son Christopher will live a good life because his mom did not hide the truth from him. (Btw, who is his dad?) What else remains?
4.\tAnyone else felt confused trying to figure out the Finch family tree? So Edith is the daughter of Dawn and Sanjay. Dawn is the daughter of Sam. Sam is the daughter of Edie. Edie is the daughter of Odin?
