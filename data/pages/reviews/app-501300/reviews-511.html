\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdith Finch is an imaginitive game and fills you full of questions and intrigue. Then after just over 2hrs, it ends.

What the game sets out to do, it does well. It does have plenty of plotholes though unfortunately. The biggest issue is the audacity to charge $20 when it was new. For what little you get, 2-3hrs max, it shouldn't have cost more than $10 new and saw reasonable sales within a few months of release.

Had the game been a few hours longer, I would be willing to understand the praise and hype it's been recieving. Unfortunately, it doesn't even compare to many Telltale titles since, along with amazing storytelling, they actually have reasonable length.

Wait for it to drop to $5 so you won't feel ripped-off.\t\t\t\t\t
