\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStellar graphics bar none. Game play a little clunky. Spend quite a bit of time trying to find the path the game wanted me to find. Story line was average or better. In-game arcade game like tasks got to be a bit too much. Found both common bugs in the Walter and Lewis stories. Had to restart game, drop graphics options to low and replay. Found that tip in the forum otherwise I'd probably have just quit the game and not returned. Not at all worth the $20, but if you can get it for free, it's a fun little (though just a bit morbid) storyline game to spend a few hours on while enjoying some really great graphics. I guess I was willing to give it a try considering the highly recommended rating and noted awards the designers received. Somewhat surprised by that, but had some fun none the less.\t\t\t\t\t
