\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne word to describe this game "Amazing"

This game isn't exactly a game..
It's more like a interactive and immersive story which was being told beautifully and majestically..

The story of this game is really unique, it was compiled with bunch of stories.
What makes it unique is that each story have a different mechanic. One time you are a cat, then you are an owl, a shark and etc.

Although i must say the game mechanic from each stories isn't that good but it's amazing to see how fresh and different from each story.

The voice acting from this game is also very good, the sound effect felt really real, and the background music really help capture the mood of the moment for us.

After all being said there are some things that isn't so great about the game too, first of all the interaction mechanic is somewhat annoying. Like when you interract with them, sometimes they are annoying to be done.

Solid 8/10 game, buy it if you can.
This game isnt filled with generic story where it end in a happy ending but the story was rather touching and sad.\t\t\t\t\t
