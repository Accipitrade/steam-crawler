By far this is the crowned king on my list of the genre and i doubt any other game will surpass this masterpiece. Even if you dont like the genre that much i recommend you give it a try and treat it like youre "playing a movie or a book".
The story is fenomenal, it revolves around the Finch family and a "curse" that goes on through the generations.

Overall i give this game a 10/10 and leave the remark that its nothing like i've ever played before, the story telling is just in another level and the game is just... just try it :D\t\t\t\t\t
