\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is a masterpiece. Truly a work of art.

The stories are compelling: dark but funny, both frightening and comforting, tragic and upbeat at the same time. I found myself at times both laughing and crying within the same moment and for the entire game I had a permanent smile glued to my face. The atmosphere is beautiful, the house design is amazing, the soundtrack is perfect and the story delivery mechanisms are never the same twice. I really have never encountered a game like this. This goes on my shelf as one of the greats.

One thing to you should know, WRoEF is not so much a game as it is an interactive story and one that is not particularly long (I am a completionist and I finished it in ~3-4 hours). Still... the experience is more than worth the time investment and asking price. Even if this style of game is not your typical cup of tea, I would still recommend giving it a whirl. And for those who love a good story, you are in for a treat.

10/10\t\t\t\t\t
