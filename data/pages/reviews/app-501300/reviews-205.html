\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay, wow. This is already one of the greatest games I've ever played. I bought it around the time I was really into The Vanishing Of Ethan Carter, I listened to TVOEC's soundtrack and got this game's soundtrack in my recommended (It's excellent btw!) - long story short, if you liked Ethan Carter you'll LOVE thi. There is so much thought, love and care put into this game it's simply astounding. It's somewhat what you would expect, a walking simulator that has you uncovering secrets - it's not the first of its kind, but its visual and narrative style, as well as its sense of humor are by far the most refined of any of these games I've played. I was surprised how dark this story got at times, and some of the sceneries were simply breathtaking. Voice acting was absolute top notch, and it never got boring just because how interwoven narrative and gameplay are.

I recommend just getting and playing it. Don't read up on it, play it entirely blind like I have been doing. That's generally the best approach to this kind of game there is, and it's served me very well so far, and for this game it definitely boosted it beyond anything it could have been if I had known what I was getting into.\t\t\t\t\t
