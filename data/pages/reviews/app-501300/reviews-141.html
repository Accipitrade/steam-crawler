And if that's what you're looking for, then it's worth a walk-through if you find it on sale. You walk from here to there, and there are instances where you might open something or pick something up or move something around, but they're all prompted and the "game" doesn't really progress until you do it - and doesn't really change depending on whether you do this or that. You have about as much effect on the outcome as you do when you turn the page of a book. If you're interested in an interesting story told in a neat way, then do it up. Just don't expect too much of a "game".\t\t\t\t\t
