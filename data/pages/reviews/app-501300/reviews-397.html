\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSeeing that the reviews are at Overwhelmingly Positive I will give it a thumbs down for the ending.
The game itself is beautiful. The house is fantastic, the atmosphere is great and the individual stories (the game consists of multiple sub-stories) are well told. The narratives are fantastic and each story has it's own style, which is great.

It's a walking simulator that is very similar to Gone Home, with the same strange, eerie house and uncertainty about what will happen and how scary it will all turn.

All of it is fantastic and captivating, right up to the end. The ending, sheesh. It's horrible. Not story-wise-horrible, but story-telling-wise horrible. It left me deeply unsatisfied. It just doesn't end, the ending story gets ripped in half (literally) and is then abandoned (literally). End credits. What?

If you're here for the views, the atmosphere and the individual stories, go ahead, they're great. But the overall story was a big let-down for me.\t\t\t\t\t
