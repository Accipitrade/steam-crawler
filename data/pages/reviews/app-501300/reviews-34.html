\r\n\t\t\t\t\t5 Stars - wow.

This game is a masterpiece. From the beginning you are curious, unsure on the plot, or how it might unfold. Piece by piece, you explore the lives of several fictitious characters who share a key moment in life, and their untimely end. Slowly as each character is explored you piece together the family tree and unravel the curse of the Finches. This unfortunate, dysfunctional and tragic family, force you to confront emotions of despair, joy and sorrow simultaneously.

I spent a good hour playing this and I do not think the short play time detracts from the price, although I did buy on sale. For me, it is rare a game tugs on your heart strings and takes you on a true journey. For that, I think this game is worth every penny.

Thank you to the developers, this game is a bite sized work of art.\t\t\t\t\t
