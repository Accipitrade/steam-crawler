\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere really needs to be a "neutral" option for reviews.

The overwhelmingly positive reviews made me have really high expectations, and although I can't say I didn't like the experience, the game fell way short of said expectations.

It is very artsy, beautiful to look at, great audio and background music, has a poetic feel to it and the narration is excellent.

BUT, it's very short, it has very little interaction and is extremely linear. Also, while the story does invoke an emotional response, it feels incomplete and rushed. Since the dev focused so much on the storytelling, the story itself should have had way more depth.

Overall, not a bad game by any means and it's worth a try provided you know what you're getting into (a walking simulator with little to no interaction). But in my humble opinion, it's way overrated even for what it is and the price tag of $20 doesn't help.

6/10. Not recommended for its price unless it's on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
