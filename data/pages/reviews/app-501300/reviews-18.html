What Remains of Edith Finch has a unique way to narrate a story, making you go through many different places with different feelings with different artstyles and a great soundtrack! But honestly, if you watched the trailer, thats the whole game, it literally shows all it has and I didn't really find it emotive.
It is a book made game, and it's well done! It definitely FEELS like it incorporates the player more into the story than other games like Gris, but in my opinion, it's still not great, it lacks interactions. Maybe I expected a story with more depth, with more optional secrets in each corner that helps the player unveil the secrets of the Finch family, but I found nothing.
It's worth buying at a sale and it's probably worth playing accounting on the 2 hours that it lasts, 3 if you go for all achievements, but it's not the almost-life-changing experience that I thought it was with the argument I said at the first paragraph.\t\t\t\t\t
