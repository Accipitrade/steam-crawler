\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd like to begin with one word: Wow!

This story enthralled me and I couldn't help but finish it within one sit-through. Apart from the gripping story, the sheer atmospheric feel immerses you right into the story from the get-go along with the directed narration. I couldn't help but think that it wasn't just her story, but also mine...

Pros:
Story-driven
Well narrated with a wonderful voice
Atmospheric
Interesting
Abstract
Unique
Individual story replayability (Upon completion)
Easy achievements

Cons:
Story-focused (i.e. Interactions are limited)
A bit expensive for just over 2 hours gameplay
There's no option to run... (This was a weird one for me but I'll include it)

Overall:
Based on the above, clearly, the pros far outweigh the cons. Furthermore, I can easily say it has been one of my best buys (On Special) and it would make a great addition to your library. Moreover, I believe that by buying this gaming and supporting the development studio that we could expect more masterpieces like this in future. However, if you're not into a slow-paced interactive and narrated story then this may not be for you.\t\t\t\t\t
