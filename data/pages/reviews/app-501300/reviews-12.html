\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone that really enjoys story-rich games, this is definitely one that I would recommend to anyone looking for good storytelling. It's a very unique recount of Edith Finch and her family using different medias. Most of the story is told from Edith Finch's point of view, though it does change for some of the characters. I did really enjoy the subtitles that were placed in the game also, which makes it more accessible to people that are hard of hearing or deaf. While there are a few questions left unanswered with the plot that I wish had answers, many of the questions raised did eventually have answers.

The music was atmospheric and matched whatever plot point was happening in the game. Sometimes it was too overwhelming, and I did think some of the background noises didn't quite match the theme of "old house that's settling". However, for the most part, it didn't take away from the storytelling and enhanced it at times, especially in a couple of the stories.

While I do enjoy this game, I do have a couple of issues with the game. I think the biggest issue I had with the game was the lack of a tutorial. Maybe I'm just stupid, but I couldn't figure out which button was the interaction button for controllers (usually it's the A button or the X button, but it was neither here). It took me a long time to figure out it was the right trigger button, so I would have appreciated some form of tutorial, even if it was super basic. Considering the content of the game, I also thing that the game is a bit overpriced. This game easily could be finished between 2-4 hours (I only took close to 7 hours because I would leave the game running on my ROG Ally and leave it running while I did something else, and I had to redo some sections because I didn't realize where the auto-save was) and the game really is only designed for one playthrough. Sure, you can play more than once, but there isn't anything new to gather from the game for any new playthroughs.

Despite this, I believe the game is very well designed and has a unique style that makes it memorable from the first moment. I would recommend getting this on sale, but it absolutely would be worth playing!\t\t\t\t\t
