This game is a prime example of games being an artistic medium rather than mindless fun and shooting.

Lewis's section in particular was absolutely amazing and is a perfect example of some things working 100x better in a game over other types of media, the way the chopping off the fish becomes second nature to you as it did with Lewis's character is a top example of ludonarrative harmony in games and i dont think it can be topped even though it was so simple. such a small thing but so immersive and this game is full of stuff like that, absolute hats off to the developers, they have created a damn near masterpiece of video game storytelling, fully utilizing the interactivity of the player and advantages of telling this story through a game rather than lazily just making us walk around and watch the story unfold.\t\t\t\t\t
