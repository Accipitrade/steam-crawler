\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe reviews were over the top. It said it was an atmospheric adventure game, similar to "The longest journey", which I adored. I bought it for its full price.

I was MASSIVELY disappointed. Why?

1. The story does not make sense at all. It's just the "ramblings of a mad person", as somebody in the forum called it. There is no consistency, no logic, nothing is even happening, it's just random events that are not connected at all.

2. I wished there was an ending that would at least explain all of the incoherent pieces of "stuff" happening. There is absolutely not. The story just ends, and there you go.

3. As a bonus, the story ends in the most depressing (but still completely unreasonable) way you can imagine. Yay.

4. There is basically no gameplay at all. It is an interactive movie, whereas the interaction is limited to the player opening a door or a book every 5 minutes. That is it. There are no other characters, no different paths to take, no choices to be made, not nothing. You basically just walk through the one path of the game, listening to those insanely incoherent bits of info and then that's it.

I wish I never played this game, really. All it did was give me a sense of despair for its gloomy setting and the hugely depressing mini stories and the frustration at the end as I could not believe that this had been it.

I feel like this was prank of the developers to see what they could actually get away with. I'm not usually this negative, I can enjoy a game that is not perfect without any problems, but that was both a waste of money and of time, as I now feel worse than before I had played this.

At least wait until it's massively reduced in price if you feel the need to check this out for yourself.\t\t\t\t\t
