\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think that video games can be a great way to tell a compelling story. Letting you be a part of it and, more or less, shaping it is wonderful in its own.

Well, this game throws that out the window and tells it to ♥♥♥♥ off. It's a walking sim, I hate walking sims. This is basically a movie, and thankfully when it's on sale, it's priced like one.

Otherwise, I think "What Remains of Edith Finch" is fairly charming. The environments and voice acting are on point, it is a beautiful looking and sounding game, they help elevate what is otherwise, in my opinion, a fairly bland story. It didn't pull my heartstrings much. But at the very least it was presented well, and when I was done with it, it left me thinking. Not so much about the game, but more about life. But then my cold, shriveled black heart might be less disposed to crying than yours, so I would recommend the game.\t\t\t\t\t
