\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh my god!! Where do I even begin.... This game is absolutely phenomenal!!! I have only played the very first part of the game (I have finished reading Molly Finch's diary), and that section alone is enough for me to right the most positive review I have ever written about a game!!

To begin with, the graphics in this game are absolutely AMAZING!!! I have a gaming PC which can run this game with every single graphics setting on Ultra, and still get the maximum of 120 FPS, and I can honestly say, hands down, that What Remains of Edith Finch is one of the best-looking games I have ever seen or played (from a graphical point of view, at least!).



Secondly, the soundtrack to this game fits the atmosphere amazingly well. All the music in this game is both mesmerising, and epic-sounding, all at the same time!! It really does fit the theme and atmosphere of the game, and I can't say anything bad about the music at all!!



So, we are two-for-two with the positive comments, for both the graphics and the soundtrack. But can we go for the hat-trick? 1000000% yes!!! Finally, the cream of the crop, the thing that makes this entire game what it is... The story. Now where do I begin with the story... Well, "I guess I'll start at the beginning, with the house..." (see what I did there? ;) ). But all jokes aside, What Remains of Edith Finch honestly has one of the best stories a game has ever had, in m,y opinion! It is captivating from the very, very beginning, it is also immersive, gripping, enthralling, mysterious, emotional, moving and a straight-up masterpiece!! I especially love how that, even though you primarily play as Edith Finch, because you have gone back to the house to solve the many mysteries hiding within it, you are also discovering more and more about the other members of the Finch Family, and also more about their enormous wealth and fortune. And because of all this, you also actually get to play as the different family members!! I have only completed Molly Finch's diary at the moment, but I can honestly say I cannot wait to play more!!! I. AM. HOOKED!!!

So, If you are into story-driven games, and want one which will keep you captivated, and leave you intrigued, filled with mysteries and wonder, then What Remains of Edith Finch is one I cannot recommend enough!!! Huge props to the developers!!!!\t\t\t\t\t

To be honest, I almost gave up on it early on; the controls are a little clunky at times, and in the first major story about a family member, I couldn't control the movements of the character very well, and I got dizzy and a little motion-sick. I'm really glad I tried again the next day, though, and it was better to play at a desk instead of trying to play while in bed. If you struggle with the controls and the gameplay style like me, it may help to quickly look at a play-through. I had to check one because I was getting so dizzy and frustrated, and after I saw someone else do it, it helped me get a handle on how I was supposed to play. Without that, my own poor gaming skills would've made this a really tough game.

It really is such a magical game. The amount of detail in the house is astounding! I spent a long time just wandering around the house, looking at family photos and the books on the wall. It's an incredibly rich environment. And the way the family members' stories are shown to us is so cool. Each story has its own unique method of delivery, and sometimes you have to figure out how to get the story to move along through some specific immersive detail.\t\t\t\t\t
