\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this game back on the Xbox One, years ago. I forgot some of the plot, but I still remembered that it was good. Every story for each member of the Finch family is interesting and keeps you moving forward to find out more. Overall, the tone of the game is a somber one, as the story is about the misfortunes of the Finch family (sort of like a generational curse on their entire family tree). The stories of Gregory, Barbara, Lewis Molly and Calvin are the best. After completing the game, I walked away feeling melancholy and reflective about life, family and the legacies we all leave behind. This is a short game (around 3 hours total) but, overall, it's a great game. It's short enough to not overstay its welcome, yet long enough to make its point and leave a lasting impression on the player. I highly recommend this game to everyone who like a to engage in an emotional, thought-provoking and emotional journey. The topic that many of the stories in the game revolves around, is anout death. So keep that in mind. It's not always an easy subject to approach.\t\t\t\t\t
