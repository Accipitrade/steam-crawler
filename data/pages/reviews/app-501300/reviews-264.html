\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is quite the evolution of the "walking simulator" genre. In a way it is a "fixed" Gone Home. You are still experiencing the narrative by walking through the weird house that in a way makes sense and feels more natural than other environments I have played over the years. Letting the player interact with doors, hidden passages and all that gives an interesting sense of agency that the genre is usually lacking, even though in reality it is barely there. The little story vignettes were all great and some of them were surprisingly gruesome and weird. The whole game was a fun and unique experience. While it's not the best thing I have ever played I'm sure it will stick in my mind for a bit nonetheless.\t\t\t\t\t
