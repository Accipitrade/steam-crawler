\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery weakly recommended...

There are two things because of which I can recommend this game, and a whole lot that caused me to end up disappointed:

1. The graphics are amazing! Sure it is not Red Dead Redemption 2 level, not by far, but there is something very atmospheric, very captivatiing in the scenes, the house, the entire world! And shoutout to the music, because as subtle as it was, it was very good.

2. You should judge for yourself, as this is definitely a unique gaming experience. I was very confident purchasing this game, because of the outstanding reviews it got and even the negative reviews weren't that bad. I understand why it got the positive reviews, for me personally it wasn't that big a whoop...

The game mechanics change enough so that it doesn't become boring (like the Painscreek Killings, a game I can highly recommend if you liked this one, it has two issues though the walking simulation, which becomes incredibly tedious after a short while (and the other issue shall remain unnamed)), it took me a total of three hours to finish ( I made dinner in the meantime too :)

However, be warned, this is a highly narrative/linear game, there is little action and basically no freedom to branch off or explore!\t\t\t\t\t
