\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaunting.

I loved every minute of this. It hits right in the feels and is now one of my all-time favorite games.

It's got a hell of a plot. Every vision has unique mechanics and the music is lovely.

I've never before ended a game emotional and needing to compose myself. It's not straight up depressing, it's ... delightfully depressing. It somehow feels uplifting while making you experience one tragedy after another. That's hard to do.

I mean, it portrays the accidental death of a baby as gut-wrenching and beautiful at the same time, and at no point does it come across heartless. They don't make writers like that anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
