\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is amazing. I quite enjoy walking simulators, and aside from Firewatch, this is the best yet. Here are some pros and cons without spoilers:

Pros:
1) Great story - The story in this game will keep you going, and is quite interesting.
2) Sound - The sound effects that accompany the story are on que and perfect
3) Environment - The environment is beautifully designed and goes great with the story. Nothing seems to be out of place.
4) Simple - Theres not a thing in this game that makes it difficult. It takes you on a ride and lets you enjoy it.
5) Plot - The story ties everything together perfectly, and doesnt let anything go astray.

Cons:
1) Too Short - Same thing with Firewatch, this game shares its problems. The story is fantastic; however, it leaves you wanting more and with that, the short 2 hours of gameplay makes it difficult to justify the price point.
2) Price - I bought this game when it went on sale, and I would recommend doing that. It's an amazing game, but not enough hours of gameplay to justifiy the price point IMO.
3) Not enough clarification - If you purchase this game and play it you'll quickly learn that some of the stories might urge you to google for more descriptive content. The game does a pretty good job on setting a story line; however, it doesn't do a great job at describing exactly for some content.


Overall this game is amazing and I would definitely recommend it to anybody that loves story driven games or likes walking simulators.\t\t\t\t\t
