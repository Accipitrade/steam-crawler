This game includes loads of unique gameplay moments that constantly switch up for each Finch family members story. Each diary brings a new and interesting mechanic to play and have fun with. These mechanics allow this game to avoid many issues with story based games: the gameplay itself is very fun and not just pure plot.
The game is short (around 1 hour 40 mins for me) and thus I do not recommend buying at full price but in steam sales.

This game does not require any mechanical skills and can be enjoyed by any individual despite skill with mouse/keyboard.\t\t\t\t\t
