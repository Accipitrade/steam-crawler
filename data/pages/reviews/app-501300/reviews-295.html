\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe good
- The gameplay (if you can call it that) is non-repetitive
- The art style (narration/presentation/graphics) work really well for what this game sets out to achieve

The bad
- This isn't a video game. Its more of a walking simulator + audiobook
- The story. Its simply an artistic telling of what is otherwise, narration of someone's family history
- The story is far too predictable and honestly, without a point. There is an absolute lack of investment for a player except empathy.
- The story ( A third time since that's what this game is all around) is absolutely flat without all the presentation around it.
- The gameplay. There is practically none outside a walking simulator. Most of the times I'd be bored and wanted to get through the game faster so I could encounter some gameplay hinted by the undertones of the game, which unfortunately, is a red herring.\t\t\t\t\t
