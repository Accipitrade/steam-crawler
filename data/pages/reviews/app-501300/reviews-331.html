First impressions

I am, in fact, mesmerized. My expectations were too low, considering how I see the game now. I thought it wouldn't be so captivating nor interesting, but it is. The game itself has the most interesting gameplay I've seen and/or met till now, you can interact with so many things and control so much! You don't have to just lay on your back an see how the game plays itself, the player is pretty much involved in the game/story.
It's not all about walking forward and exploring everything in a linear way; you have to discover everything by yourself and explore as much as you want.
I bought this game as it was on sale, but to be honest, it deserves the full price. But if you feel like that's too much, buy it on sale, you won't regret it, I promise.

Now...

I played a bit of the game again a short while ago, as I streamed a friend some of the stories in it, because I really felt like I had to share it with someone. I don't know if I'm overreacting, but I really have so much love for this game.
To add more to my initial review, I have to say that the graphics are extremely smooth and beautiful. The subtitles come as you walk and they go as you explore further. I found this one aspect to be very awesome, as I've never actually saw this in another game, yet.
Some actions require you to move in one direction/pattern (for example, if you open a book, you'd have to make a movement with your mouse from right to left) and this is basically what I meant through what I said above regarding you, as player, being involved in the gameplay.
The game itself is fantastic and everything is well put together; each story is unique.
Therefore I still recommend this game and I hope it'll get more recognistion. :)\t\t\t\t\t
