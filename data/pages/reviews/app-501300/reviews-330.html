You start to wonder about the Finch family and creepily scare you into how these situations can be you, your closed ones or your friends.

I am not the kind to find happy endings but this game leaves many questions hanging (in a good way). The psychological impact really makes one feel it is a thriller/ horror-like genre(the theme of the stories) when it is all just reminiscence. Although it is not my cup of tea, if you enjoy these kind of games, I highly recommend. The graphics are magnificient, everything is done to the finest detail (storywise and graphics).\t\t\t\t\t
