\r\n\t\t\t\t\tQuick review for a quick game everyone has played already anyway :-)

This is how storytelling in a video-game can be. This is why video-games are considered art by some.
It's not much of a game itself. You don't influence anything, you don't decide anything, you can't die, you can't fail (unless you just refuse to move forward).
You will not like this if you expect any kind of "real" game here.
However, you will like it if you're up for a nice story been told in an interesting and captivating way (most of the time) with yet no big suprises and no unexpected plot-twists either. All covered in adequately moderate graphics with a fitting soundtrack. And a lot of love for tiny details.

I completed it in about 4 hours. Yet totally worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
