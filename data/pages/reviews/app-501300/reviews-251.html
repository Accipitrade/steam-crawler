\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I'll be honest, I wasn't 100% sure what to expect when I got this game. My base review is: Good game, short (about 2-3 hours), interesting story, recommended on sale.
Longer Review: It was on sale, it has great recommendation rating and so I figured- Why not? That being said I find it easiest to break it down into pros and Cons and I will try really hard to be vague but helpful.
Pros- I think that the Visuals are great, loved the atmosphere and music. I prefer Controllers and didn't have any problems with their config. I found the story to be very interesting, I liked learning about the family members and was very invested by the end of the story. I actually liked that it wasn't a terribly long game but that might fall under Con for some.
Cons- I wanted more: It took a minute to get into and while it is more of an interactive story than a traditional game, I think that it would have made the game better to enhance player involvement a bit more. I would also note, if I had paid full price, I might feel like I didn't get the full bang for my buck. My favorite story is Lewis's because it is one of the more interactive stories. Left me unsatisfied: Fun mysteries abound, various questions arise and answers are supplied for I'd say 80% but that other 20% is buggin me...I need Answers! O_o
Will I play it again?...maybe...outside of achievements, unlikely.
Did I like it? Yes
Recommended? Yes, on sale\t\t\t\t\t

I bought it like a day ago and decided to play it that night. I wasn't expecting the experience I got when I first went into the game.
