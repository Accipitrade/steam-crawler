It is a really well told story that almost made me feel completely immerised. Unfortunetly I said almost, because I would have recommended this game if it did not have one single issue. It is poorly optimized for pc.

I have a computer that easily surpases the recommended specs for the game, and In can only play it with it only looking slightly pixely (with mostly medium settings) I have about 25-18 fps (depending on what scene was happening). This game should be able to run smoothly at its highest settings because it is the kind of game you get full immersion from a beautiful looking world with responsive controls. This is extremely disapointing.

It feels really werid to recommend this as a pc player, but if you can play the game on console, do it. It is a great game that deserves the beautiful looking world that, during this time, pc verison cannot give.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was a neat little movie.

Not much of a game, but I guess that's the point really.

Hell, it's even as long as a movie.

I'd recommend this game for what it is, but not for what it's being hyped and priced as. Everybody is like "this is one of the best games of this generation". It's hardly even a game!

Do I like it? I did, but just as much as I like going to the playground with my son, and I did that for free. I can't see this being worth $20 or being hyped up as ground breaking. It's really just a silly story involving a bunch of interesting characters that die. That's all this is.

Best I can say is that this game is very gorgeous and the setting and atmosphere, and just generally experienciencing this house even tops Resident Evil 7's house. But unless you're a person who are easily appealed by something truly on an emotional scale, you're probably going to be left feeling very underwhelmed.

Also, I must address the narritve. I don't think it beats Nier Automata, just saying.

This is hardly anywhere near GotY quality because it's hardly anywhere near a game. It's an experience, so with that said, if you can get this game for $5, go for it, otherwise, skip this one.\t\t\t\t\t
