\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rate this game NEUTRAL.

This "game" provides a linear, but very unique and creative narrative experience. If you like story games, I recommend this game.

HOWEVER, playing this game consistently gave me extreme motion sickness. It is because of the very low Field of View coupled with the tight interiors/claustrophobic nature of the setting. I wanted to continue, but I could not finish playing it. The reticle option in the game settings is supposed to help with that issue, but it didn't help for me. I watched the rest of the thing on Youtube, and I felt sick even then.
If you are sensitive to that sort of thing and navigating in games like Dead Space made you want to throw up, then there's a chance this game will affect you too.

It's probably a good idea to try the game as soon as you buy it (and not wait months like me) to see if you get motion sick within 10 minutes, so you can refund it if it's unbearable.\t\t\t\t\t
