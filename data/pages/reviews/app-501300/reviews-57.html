Had some weirdness first time I launched, as the cursor wasn't locked to the window and I accidentally ended up accidentally sending my partner a bunch of "W"s over Discord because it was on the other monitor lol. Fixed when I restarted though, and I then got to play the game properly and have a good time!

A bit disappointed with two stories as they're kinda left open which, to me, was a bit unsatisfying. The rest were enjoyable though!

I like that the game had some solid achievements, but they were all ones that you could get without too much trouble.

One thing I disliked, though, was some of the movement controls. It got a bit annoying when I was trying to close a book and it would't register, so I'd have to do the same motion like five times. Part of the problem is that I'm limited in my movements due to a mouse pad, something the devs probably didn't factor in. It wasn't impossible, just annoying.

Overall, really loved the game! Would absolutely recommend. I've been a big fan of the game for years and I'm glad I finally got a chance to buy it and play it for myself now that it was on sale. Not that it isn't worth the full price, it is! I'm just poor and didn't see a need to pay full price when I had seen it played by five different people.

On that note, shoutout to CallMeKevin! He uploaded his playthrough of the game and it made me wanna play for myself. I checked the page and perfect timing, it was on sale! If it weren't for CMK, I probably never would have played it myself, and now I'm so glad I did. Thank you Kevin!\t\t\t\t\t
