There is no doubt there was much thought and work put into the presentation and some of the story elements genuinely surprised me. So, in that sense I did not hate this game, but the real point of most walking sim games, at least in my opinion, is enjoying a story that draws you into the world. It makes you feel some level of investment or emotion in the characters, but this game never did that for me and for that reason I cannot recommend it.

I really think this boils down to one major issue, that being the games length. It took me 2.3 hours to finish this game and that was with tabbing out and taking my time. By itself, 2.3 hours might be fine, but the other glaring issue is they have essentially doubled the length of this game by slowing your characters movement speed.\t\t\t\t\t
