Finished the game in one sitting. Wasn't really long, but well worth the money. The story, artworks/graphics and the gameplay were surreal. Kinda annoying that runnin/pacing wasn't an option, but i think that would've ruined the game atmosphere. Other than that, the gameplay/controls we're mcuh better than i expected, and the voice acting and dialogue setup was perfect almost everywhere.
I wouldn't concider this a horror game tbh. There were some moments where the atmosphere got dark and spooky, but i expected jumpscares and freaky ♥♥♥♥ since it's a "Horror" game.

Conclusion: Im very picky on singeplayer games and am allways on the hunt for a new game to amaze me (Story is really important). Anyone who is on the lookout for a game with a great story should definetly check this out!
I usually lose interest pretty fast if the game is too misleading or if i cringe from the voice acting or somthing, but i played this without stop.

I could go on and on about this, and i never write game reviews unless im blown away. (did one for Ori in the blind forest)
But this? I can't just get amazed by a game like this and not spread the word.\t\t\t\t\t
