\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was instantly drawn into this game. It was so unique in it's way of story telling. You just wanna keep going until you've finished the game because you always want to see what will happen next. It wasn't scary, but some parts of it were bizzare, but in a good way. Sort of like "Alice in Wonderland." Things don't always seem as they appear. The writer definitely had an interesting imagination.

The only bad part for me personally I suppose would be, (besides it being a little too short) is that you can't seem to run. You have to walk. It's not a bad thing, but I feel that if the game sped up the walk just a little bit, it would be way better. If you went the wrong way and had to go all the way back, you have to endure that walk.

The voice over was also very well done. I would definitely like to see more games with this style.

I will definitely do another play through because I did end up missing a couple of stories, and so this means you can complete the game without needing to do them all. But with steam achievements and those two stories from my family tree still outstanding, its worth playing again because it is a fun game, and the graphics are gorgeous.\t\t\t\t\t
