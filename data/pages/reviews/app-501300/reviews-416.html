\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn intense, incredible, colourful masterpiece about finding out who your family was, who you are and what is the curse which is connected to your destiny. Will you find that thing out? Probably yes. Will you survive it? Well, that's difficult question. And will you like it? Of course yes!

The main aspect and quality of the game is the story. This is hardly surprising when even the game describes itself as story rich. The game is composed of the main story of your character and bunch of smaller partial stories of your family members so basically you gradually uncover the whole and compact story of your family. Nevertheless, the story, or better to say it in plural - stories, are quite good, elaborated and full of surprises which you would hardly expect and which I would not like to spoil here. The only thing I can say to illustrate it is that I am sure you will be shocked when your character transforms into a cat, and I mean that positively, because later you may find that out thanks to the well-made story. It is also worth noting that the main theme of the story is death - the topic which all of us more or less fear. However, this topic should not scare you! The story is written so well you understand the characters and their deadly situations and then somehow get over those destinies much more lightly. This is something we may get used to in our real lives and real situations. But that is different story.

What actually is surprising about the game, is its gameplay. I have previously written about those surprises in the story. But those surprises develop into the real gameplay situations. I have outlined one situation previously - the one you transform yourself into a cat. Then you play as the cat. For a while. Then comes another story and another surprising gameplay. Just...WOW!

A few words to the aesthetics. The soundtrack is perfectly fitting, Jeff Russo (e.g. Fargo, Star Trek: Discovery, Legion) did a great job. The soundtrack is also worth listening alone - on Spotify or YouTube. The graphics are also cute and relevant to the atmosphere, mood and setting of each story. Hopefully you will run the game smoothly and without any issues. I have had only one issue with entering the partial story but that is not something you cannot handle.

It takes ca. 3 hours to complete the game. Personally I think it is okay because the story has developed at the right speed. It was not too slow, nor too fast - you will not mind those 3 hours. However, I am concerned about the replayability. Maybe I would like to play some of the stories in the future or would like to experience the whole game again. But now? For me it is not the kind of the game I wish to restart immediately after I have seen the last line of the credits. I rather wish to keep it in my memories for a while.

I got the game for 10 Euros during the sale which I consider as a completely fair price worth paying for this game.

To sum this up, I would like to recommend the game to everyone who would like to experience an intense story with quite surprising gameplay. You only have to have a heart capable of emotions. 93/100.
