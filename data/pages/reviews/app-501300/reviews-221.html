\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros: Beautiful artwork. Interesting story-telling mechanics. Amazing and engaging details in the environment.

Cons: With a mouse and keyboard, the gameplay mechanics can be really obnoxious, even exhausting. This game might be played better with a controller.

The worst: The individual stories and overall story are devastating, even disturbing, and not in a fun horror movie kind of way. The developer claimed in the discussion board it was not intended to be depressing, but it is, and I imagine for some it could be "triggering." I had a hard time getting through one of the stories, and almost stopped playing altogether. Do a little research on the story before committing to playing this game.\t\t\t\t\t
