\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best stories in a game. What Remains of Edith Finch is about the deaths of each member of a family that believed themselves to be cursed. I won't say more than that, as it would give away too much of the plot. This game is technically a walking simulator, but doesn't feel that way at all, as you explore this gargantuan house full of secrets. In between the exploration, you experience the final moments of each of the Finches by playing through them. These range from somewhat interesting (Calvin), to heartbreaking and unnerving (Gregory), to the best sequence I have ever experienced in a linear game (Lewis). I highly recommend this game.

Score: 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
