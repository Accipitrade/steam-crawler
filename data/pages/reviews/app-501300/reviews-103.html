\r\n\t\t\t\t\ti don't think ive ever played a game like What Remains of Edith Finch.

What Remains of Edith Finch is a game of a young woman finding out what happened to her family but it is also so much more than that. as you travel through the wonderful and over the top house of the Finch family you learn a story about a family who isn't your normal, cookie cutter family.

every character is unique to the extreme ranging from 'hilly-billy'ish hunters to movie stars who are trying to regain their spotlight but somehow these characters all feel so real, so in tune, and so... like you. the player playing the game may not live in a huge house with a lot of relatives but they can relate to the impact death has on a person.

as the story drives forward you realize that this is not a game about a girl learning of her past, it is a woman coming to terms with her future. i believe that this game is a metaphor for terminal illness or cancer and the slow march towards eventual death.

you as the player know that the character is going to die at the end of the scene but there is nothing you can do about it. you can take as long as you want and explore every aspect of what is around you but in the back of your mind you know for a fact that you are going to die.

as someone who knows what it is like to watch the slow march towards death and suffer from degenerative issues, this game perfectly sums up how i feel.

play this game. it's beautiful.\t\t\t\t\t
