The constant strain of my heart to keep me alive. It pumps fast.

I was scared this game would be boring based on this advertising page. Really, it's just trying not to give anything away. As small as it looks as a little tab on a steam page, when you're playing it, it feels huge.\t\t\t\t\t

The game subtly leads you through- not so much that it's annoying, but you don't have to scramble to figure things out either. I really appreciated the ease of play so I could focus on the graphics and story. Speaking of graphics, I really enjoyed the vibe and style of the scenery and house. The entire play of the game is leading you to this big answer, this big reveal and you never get it. I really would have enjoyed a neatly summed up ending with a bow tied around but that was not the case. That being said I would still play this game again and highly recommend it to anyone who likes mystery stories.\t\t\t\t\t
