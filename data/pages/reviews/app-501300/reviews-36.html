From the gameplay perspective it is amazing, very imaginative and artistic.
From the story perspective... I just.. dislike it? If that's the right word.
This game is supposed to be an interactive story, they did the interactive part very good but the story part is very lacking.

Some deaths are incredibly stupid and I actually whistled the tune of "dumb ways to die" more than once. The house is also huge and full of hazards (surprised more of them didn't die , and who the f makes a swing over a big ass cliff ).

I really wish there would be more to the story.
Also I couldn't "feel" most of the character stories - even the main character.
For example I noticed she is pregnant from the beginning because I was looking around and all I could think is how stupid she is to climb all around on ancient cartoon made house and crawl to weird passageways/basements all on her own while being (later revealed) 22 months pregnant. .

Maybe I'm missing something but I don't really know if there was any point to the story, other than maybe enjoy what you have or some similar thing?

Again, gameplay is amazing, story is meh.\t\t\t\t\t
