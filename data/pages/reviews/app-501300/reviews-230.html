\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is a beautifully crafted story and experience I highly recommend to other players. This game is about a girl named Edith who is exploring a house that she and her deceased family used to live in. As you explore, you learn about the family's story and why you are the sole remaining family member. If you enjoy a detailed and tragic story, definitely give this game a try.

Another fantastic aspect of this game is its design. For the majority of the game, you are walking through and around a house, but you experience a greater variety of gameplay as you play as the deceased family members in flashbacks. Each flashback is a unique experience that has you as the player control different aspects of each story. Instead of describing each of them, I recommend that you play for yourself to get the most out of the experience.

I think many who are afraid of buying this game are concerned with the primary gameplay being walking. This is understandable, especially since the game is only about 2 to 2.5 hours long. Although I paid the price of $20.00, I recommend waiting until the game is on sale if you are concerned with being underwhelmed by the gameplay. I was happy to pay full price since the experience met my expectations and that I wanted to support the developers.

This game may not be the fast-paced first person experience that many are accustom to, but I suggest giving it a try. The story is certainly worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
