\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA fun game if you like walking simulators intertwined with good storytelling. There is one overarching storyline throughout the entire game, and a bunch of smaller stories that are cradled within the same larger narrative. After completing the game, here's a very brief pro/con list:

+ Graphics are decent, art style is great
+ Storyline plot quality ranges from underwhelming to good; however the storytelling is consistently good
+ Good voice acting and soundtrack

- Length and replayability: The game can be completed within two hours (I took slightly over 3, and that was to get every single achievement), and there is essentially no replay value whatsoever

While the overall game was an enjoyable experience, £15 for three hours of content is absolutely ludicrous and as a result I simply cannot recommend this game at full price (I bought it on sale for £3.75, which is a price point I'd consider far closer to appropriate). Buy on sale, avoid at regular price. 5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
