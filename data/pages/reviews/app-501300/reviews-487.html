\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got to this game way late but I'm extremely happy that I got around to it eventually. I stayed away from any spoilers and am extremely happy I did. It's for that reason that I want to keep my review short and to the point.

The only gripe I have about this game is that it does not support my native monitor aspect ratio of 21:9 and therefore looked a bit janky running in a non-native resolution. Other than this very specific and small issue I thought that "What Remains of Edith Finch" is one of the best games I have ever experienced. I was thoroughly caught up in the story from start to finish, which it is important to note is not very long. However, this game made me feel a wider range of genuine emotion in a single 2 hour session than many other games have been unable to illicit in 140 hour playthroughs. I loved this game and I think you likely will too.

It is important to note that it is rather pricey for a 2 hour game but in my eyes it was completely worth it (not to mention if you can pick it up on sale). Anyway, this is a glowing recommendation of "What Remains of Edith Finch", in my opinion one of the most overlooked game of the year contenders for 2017.\t\t\t\t\t
