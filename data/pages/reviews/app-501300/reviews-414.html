\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust sat through a single playthrough, about 2 1/2 hours laughing and crying and completely drowning a hand towel at the end I was blubbering so much.

What an astonishing game, the emotional ride i have just been on, I am still crying whilst writing this. It is beautiful, it is creative, the scenes are so different, the house and the forest surrounding it are sublime. The stories suck you in, just suck you in and then they drown you. I will not spoil any of them here but have to say that the baby bath scene was the most adorable thing I have ever seen in any game.

I saw many YouTubers review this and all gave it accolades, I delayed playing it, I needed to be in a mood to play what i thought was going to be a walking simulator of sorts, well, it is a walking simulator in the barest sense and a story, and an emotional rollercoaster and a delight to have played it.

Cannot recommend this enough, you have to see this at least once in your life to see true games development and if it does not make you cry, well then I guess I am just a soppy cow or you are a robot. (I suspect the former)\t\t\t\t\t
