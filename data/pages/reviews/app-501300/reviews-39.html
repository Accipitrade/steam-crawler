\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA bit overrated. Mysterious, trippy, with a lot of things ultimately left for interpretation. I enjoyed the design of the house and secret passages, and found the idea of leaving rooms as sort of "memorials" inspiring. I had fun trying to piece together the true stories, but was disappointed by the ending, though I guess I wouldn't have liked everything to just be revealed to me either.

It felt like the sort of literature that would be taught in high schools, but lacked a sort of profound impact -- at least not on me anyway. Also, due to poor connection with my family, I couldn't connect on an emotional level. I didn't know I could experience motion sickness this bad either before play this game, which negatively impacted my experience. Didn't feel like it was worth the praise or the price, but it was an experience.\t\t\t\t\t
