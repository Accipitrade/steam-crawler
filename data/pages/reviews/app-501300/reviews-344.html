\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI liked this game, a lot.
You uncover the story of the Finch family, and learn what happened to every member of it one by one. The stories themselves arent really what makes this game, but the way they are presented is.
I think this game is more like a book in terms of storytelling. In a book, a bloodthirsty warlock and an adventurous explorer are gonna see, lets say a random tree, very differently, and that will give us information about their psychology. I think this is a rare game that does that kind of storytelling well.
The music was solid but not breathtaking. The voiceacting was good and believable.

Finish it in one sitting, and you will get your money's worth.\t\t\t\t\t
