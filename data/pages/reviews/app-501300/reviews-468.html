What Remains of Edith Finch follows the experience of Edith Finch, one of the members of the Finch family. Edith Finch travels back to her old family house to explore and discover the stories of other members in the family.
.
Storywise, thsi game doesn't hold a special place in one's heart. However, how the stories are told and the environmental design are what make this game so great. There are so many moments in this 2-hour experience that will make you go "What the hell?!? Wow!". Every story you uncover has its own unique artistic style and emotional impact. The general tone of the story is dark and tragic.
.
Afterall, What Remains of Edith Finch is one of the best very good walking simulator games, in my opinion. With superb beauty of the settings and lighting technique, short stories with twists and unique gameplay elements, and well composed soundtracks, What Remains of Edith Finch can prove worthiness out of 2 hours of your time.\t\t\t\t\t
