\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is a captivating and emotionally charged exploration game that tells the story of the Finch family, this game offers a unique and memorable experience that stays with you long after you've finished playing.

The game's premise is simple: you take on the role of Edith Finch, the last surviving member of her family, as she returns to her childhood home to uncover the secrets of her ancestors. As you explore the house and surrounding areas, you'll discover each family member's story, told through a series of interactive vignettes.

One of the game's biggest strengths is its storytelling. Each vignette is told in a different style, ranging from comic book panels to first-person narratives, and they all offer a glimpse into the lives of the Finches. Despite their often tragic fates, the stories are presented with a sense of wonder and beauty that makes them feel more like bittersweet memories than grim tragedies.

The game's visuals and sound design also deserve special praise. The Finch house is a marvel of environmental storytelling, with each room offering a unique glimpse into the family's history. The music and sound effects are equally evocative, adding to the game's emotional impact.

That being said, What Remains of Edith Finch is not a game for everyone. It's a relatively short experience, clocking in at around 2-3 hours, and it's more focused on narrative than gameplay. If you're looking for a fast-paced action game or a puzzle-solving adventure, you might be disappointed.

However, if you're looking for a game that tells a touching and poignant story, What Remains of Edith Finch is a must-play. It's a game that will stay with you long after you've finished playing, and it's a testament to the power of storytelling in video games.\t\t\t\t\t

I could have absolutely done without the death of baby Gregory. I know that many of the others died young, but the drowning death of an infant in a first person view was a bit much for me. .

I had to AFK part way through, but the game is only about 3 hours long or so. It is a quality game, don't get me wrong...but $20 for 3 hours of content is a bit steep. Grab it on sale, but definitely give it a shot.\t\t\t\t\t
