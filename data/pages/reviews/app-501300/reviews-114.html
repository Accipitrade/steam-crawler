\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter thinking about this game a few weeks after playing, I cannot recommend this game. It gave me nothing to chew on in my mind. Offered no replay value, and honestly, I don't think I can imagine recommending this game to anyone, even new-to-gaming players.

I almost exclusively play single player and story games, but honestly this game is extremely over rated.

Are the graphics, narration, and playable world great? Yes, by far.
However, for this kind of "game", (more an interactive movie), the story really has to pull through, but it never does. It's a tragedy story, but no lessons to be learned, and don't see how anyone really could think otherwise.

I didn't get stuck at any point in the game, nor any bugs, which is a plus. It FEELS polished, but by the somewhat abrupt ending, I felt that the project size probably exceeded the developers budget, and they had to end the game early, possibly cutting out major plot development or possibilities.\t\t\t\t\t
