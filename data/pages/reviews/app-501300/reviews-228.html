\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is an amazing game. It has an extremely melancholy vibe, which stops from being too depressing due to the up-beatness of the narrator, along with the dark humor scattered throughout. The visuals are very good, and the drastic changes in scenery while viewing each characters story keeps the game fresh. If you are a fan of the type of vibes the movie Coraline gives off, you'll probably enjoy this.

The only real downside is the price vs playtime. At most this game takes 3 hours to finish at a leisurely pace with achievements complete. So as far as that goes, I don't think the 20$ tag is worth it. Aside from play time though, I definitely think more than 20$ worth of work went into the game, so it kind of comes down to what you expect out of it.

Personally I had a great time with it, and I think it's worth picking up.\t\t\t\t\t
