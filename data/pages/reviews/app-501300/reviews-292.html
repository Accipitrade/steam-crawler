\r\n\t\t\t\t\ti honestly didn't wanna write a detailed review but oh well, here we are.
everything about what remains of edith finch is insanely creative. the way they connect stories -characters- is pro-level.
during the first half an hour or so, you might be like "wtf is going on here" as i was, but the way the story unties is so on point that there will be nothing left to explain.
there are really good stories; some touching, some ear-ie (see what i did there?), some cute but saddening.
it had by far one of the best gameplays i've ever seen (i don't even need to say but ahem- LEWIS) especially considering it's a walking simulation, it was ingenious & somewhat terrifying AS ♥♥♥♥.
the details of the game are well made: colour palette changes depending on the characters and the stories, little notes you find here and there... one can easily see how much thought was put into this game.
the art style is really nice, not super gorgeous but quite unique.
unlocking every member of the family is definitely not hard, just pay attention to what edith has to say.
i gotta point this out, i love her voice and how clean she speaks. i believe that it's a very important element for story-driven games.
it's a very short game indeed. around 2 hours (almost as if you're controlling a movie) but it's one of those games that will make you come back to it. in those 2 hrs there won't be a single second that you'll be bored, so this is def worth a shot.\t\t\t\t\t
