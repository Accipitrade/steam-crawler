\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWHAT REMAINS OF EDITH FINCH

Overwhelmingly positive? Well, if you haven't played this amazing narrative story then you have to ASAP.

The graphics are unbelievable on ultra, smooth and colorful, fully lived in and real feeling.. yet sadness fills this games driven story line.

Music and sound is great as well, mix of haunting and happy which plays to create the right amount of feels.

The finch family seems pretty unlucky, the overall story had me moved for sure, very sad at times but a feeling of relief to find out what happen to these people.

As you play you will be taken into different characters stories which play out in theses completely new and unique settings with cool control movements or comic book style story telling.

I can go on and on, games amazing truly.

Pick it up at full price regardless, its a must experience, good job Giant Crow Studios!
