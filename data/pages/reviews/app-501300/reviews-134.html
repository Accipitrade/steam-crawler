What Remains of Edith Finch is not only a game that tells a story, it tries to bring you the same emotions and feelings that the people in game experience. Each are so specific in its own character that you'll always want to discover something more, you want to know about them more.

The story was also wrapped perfectly in something melancholy but not bleak. It was beautiful and heartwarming, but it also shown me fears that i never knew i would be scared of.

10/10, would recommend to ANYONE.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBack when this game came out in 2017, I watched ChaoticMonki (a.k.a. Cryaotic, or simply "Cry") play this on YouTube. At the time, I really was struggling in life and repressed most of those memories, but for some reason, I felt the need to return to this game-- even after 5 years of reflecting. To be honest, I forgot most of the story. I never got the full experience that it offers as a player. The most notable example would be playing through Lewis's story where you need to multitask using one hand to chop the fish and the other for playing in his imaginary world .

Now, what are my thoughts on it now?

I fully enjoyed the experience as a player rather than a watcher. It was refreshing and interesting enough that I replayed a couple moments. It kept my attention and gave me a unique experience within a walking simulator. I even went and 100% completed the achievements because it's now a habit that I do with either games I really enjoyed or ones that I find are short enough to 100%. This game was because of the former reason-- so don't worry. lol. I wouldn't be giving it a thumbs up if it was most likely the latter.

Is the full price worth it?

Personally, no. If you want to pay full price, you'd be spending $8USD/hour. It's still less than the cost of going to the movies on a weekend night, so if that's a good enough reason to pick it up-- then go for it! But, the game is on sale often for 70%+ off sometimes, so you'd be spending $2.40USD/hr if you wait for the seasonal sales. To be honest, this was a wonderful experience, but as someone who's watched the game played already and wanted to play it, I wouldn't have paid full price. I do hope this helps!\t\t\t\t\t
