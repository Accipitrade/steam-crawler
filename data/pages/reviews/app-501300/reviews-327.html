\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe only reason I came across this game was because I was looking for another award winning game with powerful story. I stumbled across this game as it had won a number of BAFTAs so gave it a try. Given I don’t really like the look of “cartoony” graphics and have stayed away from these games in the past, I’m so glad I bought this game.

The story as you work your way around the family tree and the old house is brilliant, it’s very in-depth but easy to grasp as it doesn’t leave the player trying to work out tonnes of conflicting clues for hours on end.

The graphics are brilliant; it’s thoroughly gorgeous and the sound track is great and blended in well with the gameplay.

The only thing I think could be improved is the length of the game, I guess you could argue that there is a massive amount of detail in the short story which compensates in the end. But for what you get I wouldn’t change anything else as the devs have mastered it.

I hope there is more games like this from the developers.\t\t\t\t\t
