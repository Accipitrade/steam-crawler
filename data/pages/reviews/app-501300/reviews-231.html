\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is very enchanting with rich storytelling and the most varied gameplay I have ever experienced. Each different
piece of the story has a different theme and different types of puzzles that you must solve to progress. I played the whole game through in just over 2 hours. Don't look at the dates on anything, they don't check out and it will drive you insane. There isn't any replay-ability, however, and there is only one right way to do anything so I have struggled to determine whether it is worth $20. However, given that I played this game years ago and have continued to think about it to the point that I am now writing a review, I would say so, especially if you have a family-share and someone else can play it. This game is a gold standard for me, and I always wish that there were more games like it.\t\t\t\t\t
