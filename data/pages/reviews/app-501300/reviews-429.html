\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a short game, about 2 hours of play in which you learn about a rather unusual family through storytelling and exploration. I got this title at half of its release price and I feel like it was totally worth it.

Based on reviews, I came to this game with pretty high expectations, and then, as I started playing, I felt like those expectations aren't gonna get met. The game seemed nice and polished but I conceded that I might have expected too much. But then, as I made peace with the fact that the game might not be that great, I started enjoying it more and by the time I finished it, I got something really extraordinary from it.

I'd recommend this game especially to those who tend to connect with people and are more on the empathetic side. If you can’t or don’t enjoy putting yourself into other people’s shoes to feel and see the world through their eyes, you won’t like this game. So, if you fit the profile, get this game, it’s as good as a game in this genre can get. Just drop your expectations and let the game carry you.\t\t\t\t\t
