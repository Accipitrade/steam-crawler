The curse of the Finch family is quaint. Yet within this quaint story, there is such powerful emotion, intrinsic mystery, and poetic tragedy... It forces you to rethink your perspective on life. It shows you how important a quaint story truly is. But most of all, it shows you how beautiful, strange, and short your time on earth is... You will never be able to experience all life has to offer. All you can do is open your eyes.

This is a beautiful game. It combines the poetic storytelling of a novel with the interactive nature of a video game. This, in my opinion, is the best of both worlds. I suggest you buy this game. You will fall in love with the story, become emotionally attached to the characters, and have the best of fun exploring the beautiful world around you. All I can wish you now is good luck!\t\t\t\t\t
