\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTheres a lot to be said about What Remains of Edith Finch but not so much without spoiling so I'll try to keep it short. Its a good game but extremely inconsistent and highly overrated.

The story involves around Edith exploring the Finch mansion, trying to find out what happened to her entire lineage/family and how they died. The inconsistency part is that, it hides under a supernatural thing at first and how each of the family members death is portrayed but then it gets clear as to what really happened. You dont really know if it truly is a supernatural curse, bad luck or if some of the family members are just plain idiots. Whichever the case, the way its told is quite good and I didnt expect some of the things happening to the members. It ranges from emotional to "seriously?". I appreciate quality story telling, and the whole presentation is well done but in the end this can be a hit or miss with some of the players.

Gameplay is as limited as you'd expect from such a genre. You basically dont do anything besides interact with a few things and walk forward. Thats it, sadly. It could've been better, like have actual puzzles to solve in order to find out what happened, and allow for a more free non linear exploration. Not a fan of its gameplay.

Sound design, is good. Nice soundtrack, good voice acting, atmospheric sound effects all add up quite nicely to create a thrilling atmosphere, hindered by gameplay.

Graphically speaking, the atmosphere and each room has huge amount of details and I quite enjoyed walking around the house to discover a different kind of personality in every room you explore. It fits well with the story and music.

Overall I'm not a huge fan of 2 hour interactive story games but once in a while I try to appreciate some of them and What Remains of Edith Finch is a good one but dont expect some incredible plot unless you're easily impressed or emotional in which case, you'll probably appreciate the story of each member more than I did. 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
