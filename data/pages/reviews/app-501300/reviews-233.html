\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs dark the tale is the light shines at the end.

A story about loss, but also perhaps over-parenting, and the "live for the moment" mentality.

Where most games in this genre offer a slow paced, fetch-quest/A-to-B amount of gameplay, WROEF offers constantly evolving gameplay which keeps you at the tip of your toes.

This game is short. It took me 3 hours. But this game also offers something which a lot of games don't. It's a melange of ideas, emotions and fast paced story. It's capturing, fun, adventurous and heart wrenching at an astounding rate. And when the credits roll, and the story ends, you'll feel a sense of satisfaction which is truly difficult to find.

9/10.
