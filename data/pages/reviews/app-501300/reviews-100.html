\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoyed my time with the game! Telling a story through different little mini-game snippets was novel to me, with some standing out more than others.

When playing through it I unfairly compared it to the Outer Wilds and Return of the Obra Dinn in my head, purely because those are the only remotely similar games I've played recently. What Remains of Edith Finch is an almost stiflingly linear game, but once I adjusted my expectations away from those two games this I was able to enjoy this game a lot. I'd imagine the game's length also keeps it from being grating, with me suspecting that my 4.6 hours is at the higher end of what people take to complete the game.

It also helps that the music is excellent and the visuals are gorgeous. I loved seeing the house at different points in time. The emotional notes of the story worked for me, not to get into it any further because writing about that takes more effort than I'm willing to give right now lol

Give it a try if the music and screenshots are appealing to you, and if you're cool dropping money on a game you can complete in an afternoon.\t\t\t\t\t
