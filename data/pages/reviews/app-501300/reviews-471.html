\r\n\t\t\t\t\t What Remains of Edith Finch shouldn't be overlooked as just another indie game, what is here is a very beautifully crafted story driven experience. You play as Edith Finch, a 17 year old girl who travels back to her childhood home to learn more about her mysterious family that she really doesn't know a whole lot about. You explore the house in a linear fashion without much difficulty thrown your way. The point of this game is the story, not gameplay, difficulty, or mechanics. There are some slight puzzles here and there, but the majority of the game is walking the house and it's surrounding land and learning about Edith's family just as she does. It sounds simple, and that's because it is, but it is so well done and the environments are packed to the brim with things to look at. The voice acting from the main character and all the other characters are very well done and believable. It only took me roughly two hours to complete, so it is a shorter game, but it is definitely worth the price on a sale. Just don't forget to bring tissues.\t\t\t\t\t
