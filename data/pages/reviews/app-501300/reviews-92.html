\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA beautiful story that deals with death in a thoughtful way. The way in which the story progresses is well paced and it's especially interesting to find all of the hidden passages into the rooms that you've only seen through peep holes at first.

I wanted to add that apart from the characters who we hear and meet, there is a character found in the house as well. Something about the house feels more alive with each story you discover. And the details in the the abandoned house, how everything was left, are all visually stunning and intriguing to say the least. You get a good grasp of the Finch family just by looking at what remains.

And the ending...I'd say it's a good thing to have to play through all of the other stories first before you reach Edie, Dawn and then Edith herself. A lovely way to end a happily sad game.\t\t\t\t\t
