\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is less of an adventure game with puzzles and more of an interactive story. There are no alternate endings. It is really just follow the storyline.

I found there were one or two frustrating parts in this game. The game offered little chance to move from the plot and explore. For instance, once the game is completed, you can return to any part of the story. I really wanted to return to Edie's story in the library. Unfortunately, the game does not allow it. To return to any part of the story means that you just replay that part of the story.

Another frustration was that I did find a bug during Walter's story. The bug has been well documented in the Steam community. The fix is to lower your graphics settings to medium or lower. I would recommend lowering you graphics settings at the beginning of the game so that you do not encounter the bug at all.

Having said all that, the game itself has a rich and immersive story and I thoroughly enjoyed playing it.

If you enjoy reading stories, then I would recommend this game.

BTW, it is worth paying the extra $2 to 3 for the soundtrack.

Enjoy\t\t\t\t\t
