\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch is a narrative focused title developed by Giant Sparrow. The basic premise is your a young adult female exploring your old childhood home, while diving into the past generations of your family. Edith Finch really took me back by how well told its narrative was presented, having quite a few powerful moments throughout the game. But what shocked me most was how well developed most of these characters were. A lot of information can be found just by looking around their rooms, and that's not even mentioning the fantastic story segments that develop these characters more. The story is the main focus of the title, and that would be the main reason to play Edith Finch. There is no gameplay here, and if your looking for that kind of experience, you are going to be sorely disappointed. Overall would I recommend What Remains of Edith Finch to a friend? Yeah I think this game is very strong emotionally and is well worth the asking price. I give What Remains of Edith Finch a solid 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
