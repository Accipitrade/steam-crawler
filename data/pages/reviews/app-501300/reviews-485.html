\r\n\t\t\t\t\tImagine a game with the mechanics of Firewatch, the story of Gone Home, the visuals of Ethan Carter, and the atmosphere of Dear Esther. What Remains of Edith Finch is a worthy successor to the walking simulator throne. Its unique way of adapting the gameplay to convey each story more profoundly proves that the devs at Giant Sparrow understand the Video Game medium to its full extent. I look forward to their next project.

As a side note, one thing many walking simulators disregard is the danger of the player losing immersion due to distractions within the game. WRoEF managed to evade this pitfall by sticking to a very linear (but not limiting) journey so that you can simply experience it without having to simultaneously explore or complete achievements (the few available achievements are fun to complete and can more easily be done after finishing the game). As such, I was completely unaware of reality whilst playing, and I recommend only starting a playthrough if you have nothing planned for the next 3 hours. Enjoy.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrom the beginning of the so called "Game" I only expected it to be bleak and boring. little did i know i will spend the next hours eyes glued to the monitor listening to beautifully narrated story that gives you chills and a hint of bitterness knowing that it's all about the story of how they died.
Small yet on point narration glimmers around every corner to both guide you to the story and mesmerize you about the beauty of the game. It just feels natural with how the game progress and the placement,format is just on point. Surreal depiction of each character stories but yet carries each unique tone that corresponds to each of the character age/stories.

The DETAILS!!! IT S AMAZING!!! you may only have a small window to explore. however, every nooks and crannies is filled with stuff that is never out of place and interesting. I was mind blown by the sheer beauty of every room and the details in it. you can tell that it's quality when even an empty room painted ocean blue with whales blow my minds.

It's a very satisfying Game. Definitely a must buy!!\t\t\t\t\t
