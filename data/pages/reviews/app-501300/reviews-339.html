I do give this game my recommendation. It is a BEAUTIFUL game, from the writing to the storytelling to the characters and all around. It was enticing, When it was just starting out I was slightly worried it would be a bust, not live up to the hype, but a couple stories in and I was loving it. I definitely think it is something you should try for yourself, I definitely think it is an amazing game that is up in my top favorites...

BUT

The ending felt rushed. I was enjoying going through and having each individual story to play through, but in the end, the final 3 (including your own) is all thrown together and are left with many unanswered questions. There were things I wanted to know more about, something that was cut short that I was hoping would be able to be continued so I could know what was happening.

Then there's the glitches. Two of the stories are so glitched they're practically unplayable, they even are unplayable to some. When I experienced them myself, I found a forum where the developers claimed there would be a patch to fix the issues, but that was in 2017 and they are still very much there. I tried turning down all graphics settings to low and managed to get past one of the glitches, the other was absolutely annoying and ruined the story for me while trying relentlessly to play through it and get it to work. They even said to just skip one of the stories and watch how it plays off on YouTube. Now, I don't know about you, but when I'm paying $20 for a game that is 90% walking instead of actual gameplay and only lasts for 2-4 hours depending on your personal speed of play, I'd prefer the developers actually take the time to fix the glitches that are causing such major problems for players. It ruined the immersiveness of it all for me.

I still want to recommend the game through the problems I had with it. It had potential to be incredible but was downgraded to great. I recommend trying it, but only trying it when it is on sale. I paid full price and didn't get to the issues in it until past the two hour play mark, when the game becomes unreturnable.\t\t\t\t\t
