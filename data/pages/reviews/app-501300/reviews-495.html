\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat Remains of Edith Finch presents itself as the story of a girl discovering how the last five generations of her family died. At first, the stories seem disjointed, but subsequent playthroughs reveal a clever continuity between each death and the true story of the Finch family. While the true cause of many of the deaths is obscured, players are given the details to find out what really happened themselves. The result is that the first two-hour playthrough merely gives players the pieces to the puzzle, but it takes another playthrough or two to piece them together. Altogether, What Remains of Edith Finch is a wonderful game with a deceptively large amount of content for what is a two-hour game at first glance.\t\t\t\t\t
