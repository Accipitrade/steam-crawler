\r\n\t\t\t\t\ti watched jacksepticeye's playthrough of what remains of edith finch all the way back in 2017. although i never rewatched that series of videos, this game has stuck with me. i always thought of buying it, but never did because i thought i would never get the same enjoyment out of it as i did the first time. thankfully, i was wrong!

although it lacks puzzles, it does things to force you to interact with it and immerse yourself in the story, so even though i knew most of what was coming, i still found myself really enjoying it. the 2.4 hours i spent playing it went by really, really quickly, and i'd really recommend this to anyone who enjoys story-heavy, walking simulator-adjacent games.

pros:
-the art is lovely!! nothing ever felt bland or boring. the house really seemed to be alive.
-the sound design/music was also wonderful.
-i really liked the voice acting too. edith's voice was nice to hear.

cons:
-that one track from the main menu did start to grate on me a bit.
-the controls during barbara's story glitched out? i wasn't able to walk straight and had to resort to walking backwards until it was over.\t\t\t\t\t
