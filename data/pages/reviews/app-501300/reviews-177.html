\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI know it's been stated in multiple reviews thus far, but What Remains of Edith Finch was an amazing and enthralling story. The graphics were astounding, and the audio gave the perfect atmosphere to listen and experience the story. But that's the thing; it's a story. For me, personally, I found this "game" under the "puzzles" category in steam. I felt a little misled. Obviously I should have looked more closely at the top game tags such as "walking simulator," but there was nothing puzzling about this "game" except for why it is called a puzzle game. To me, a game is something you play, more so than something you watch happen in front of you. Obviously it has great execution for its purpose, I was just hoping that it would be more interactive, or better yet, more difficult like an actual puzzle game. I guess I should have just been more thorough when browsing. I hope everyone who buys this enjoys it for its actual purpose! (and isn't let down due to some mildly false advertising.) :)\t\t\t\t\t
