\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is easily my favorite walking sim I've ever played. The gameplay was unique and the story was engaging. It was genuinely a work of art. I can not recommend this game enough, even if you don't like walking sims, it felt more like a compilation of mini games all tied together by story and I wasn't finding myself wandering aimlessly or worried about getting lost.

My only complaint is that I wanted MORE and I had unanswered questions... which I suppose is the whole point of the game. I felt like it ended a bit too abruptly and I was posed with so much mystery and a really rich story, only to have it end suddenly. Still incredible, but I wish I had some sort of catharsis at the end of it. I'd recommend picking it up on sale, only because I got 2 hours out of it and I don't know if that's really worth full price, but it's a story you will be happy to have been a part of.\t\t\t\t\t
