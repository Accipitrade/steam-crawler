\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is....extraordinary. A sad, poignant story of a family living under an alleged curse, which tells of the lives and final moments of five generations of its members. This is more like a visual story than a game in many ways, as its fairly 'on rails' but the story telling is just so good that you don't care. The environments are beautiful, the music haunting, the stories as interesting as they are tragic. It is only 2 hours start to finish, but I honestly doubt there is a better use of 2 hours to be found.

Just get it, dive into it and let it permeate you. This really is something special.\t\t\t\t\t
