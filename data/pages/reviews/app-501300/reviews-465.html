\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHmmmm... Yeah it's pretty good. The game has some very strong highlights, that stand out above all others (Milton and Lewis' stories for example), but a few times I felt myself drifting.

In comparison to Firewatch and Virginia, two (in my opinion) very interesting stories with "choose your own interpretation" endings, I can't help but feel What Remains is the weaker. The soundtrack was less memorable, I only felt emotion towards a few of the family's stories, and it didn't have a unique thing to grab me. Firewatch was a joy to navigate the beautiful Shoshone. Virginia had fantastic art direction and a frankly cinematic soundtrack.

I'd still strongly recommend this game, it has some great moments, with 1.9hrs being perfect length.\t\t\t\t\t
