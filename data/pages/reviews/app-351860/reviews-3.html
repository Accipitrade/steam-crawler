The equipment options are nice, but outfitting every individual soldier with three or four items takes way too much time. It can take half an hour to outfit your army, at which point they are all hungry and/or sleepy, and you curse the game for not implementing a "feed all" button.
The micromanagement is just exhausting, so when after four hours of grinding through the first mission a critical event just doesn't trigger, I am eager to abandon this game forever.\t\t\t\t\t
