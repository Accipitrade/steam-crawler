\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere are the Japanese Shaman Girls, Lintahlo? ahem, uhh, I mean, if you like the original STEINS;GATE, then just read this one. 10/10 recommended. If you haven't read the original STEINS;GATE yet, go read that frist.

Seriously, I do like STEINS;GATE 0 more than the original STEINS;GATE. Most heroines from the original story, say, Mayuri, Faris, Suzuha, and even Lukako, are more mature, understanding and considerate. They understand (more or less) the situation, contribute considerably to the world, and make decisions on their own, without relying on Kyouma. These characters are far more likable and relatable in this rather dark story in my opinion. The new characters, especially Kagari and Maho, are really well written. The bond between Mayuri and Kagari and the feelings from Maho are pretty well written and touching.

In short, this is the choice of Steins;Gate. El Psy Congroo.\t\t\t\t\t
