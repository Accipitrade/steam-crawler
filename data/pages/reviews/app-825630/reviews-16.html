\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt took me quite a while to get around it, but after finishing Steins Gate 0 completely, I can say that this game is absolutely worth it for Steins Gate fans.

The story was great, even if parts of it were hard to follow due to all the moving parts happening behind the scenes. Its a much grimmer tale than the original VN, but that doesn't mean it doesn't have its lighter moments; they're just not as frequent. The characters are still a treat to watch as always, but with the addition of development from characters you wouldn't expect; I found myself loving one or two characters alot more after playing through this VN. The new characters are pretty interesting and have their own personality to carry their interactions and story without feeling like they're just filling a certain niche missing in the main cast. There's at least one character that's especially flat however, but they do their role in the story fine; they're just not incredibly interesting outside of it.

Its an absolute blast of a VN overall. Highly recommend.
