\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSteins Gate 0 is a translated Japanese VIsual Novel (VN). It is sort of a 'midquel' to the original Steins Gate VN. If you have not read Steins Gate then you should start with that instead. Steins Gate 0 starts during the original Steins Gate true ending where the main character fails and goes into PSTD and describes what was required for the happy ending in Steins Gate to come about.

The general plot concerns the lead up to World War III with a power struggle over time travel technology between various factions and is set in a region of Tokyo. As in the original, there's very little interactivity. Interactivity involves using your cell phone to respond to various events. Unlike the original, you don't need to respond to random emails in certain ways to unlock achievements or events and it's much simpler to complete the true ending.

The overall story is good but not quite as good as the original. The alternative endings through are much more interesting and fleshed out. They're not short off-shoots from the main story but more a branching path.

On the downside, the art style has changed from the unique original look to a bit more of a generic look and feel. Some of the CGs are pretty poor and CGs from the previous game are shown in flashbacks in 0. As the old CGs have different art style it kind of clashes with the new style. The true ending is also kind of lackluster compared to the original's.\t\t\t\t\t
