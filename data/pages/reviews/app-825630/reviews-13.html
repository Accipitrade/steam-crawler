\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start off by giving context: I love Steins Gate. I've seen Steins Gate, I've played through all endings of Steins Gate. I love Steins Gate.

I like Steins Gate;0. It's not as phenomenal as Steins Gate, but it adds on and creates a necessary depth to the story. I played Steins Gate;0 and finished it only to about 80%, before I ran out of motivation to play it.

So why would I recommend a game, that I dropped?

I only recommend it to Steins Gate fans. But unless you really really love Steins Gate, don't waste money on this.

In my honest opinion, the endings of Steins Gate;0 are all way less interesting than the ones from Steins Gate. Almost disappointing. Obviously that doesn't go for the true ending, but the true ending is also worse relatively to Steins Gate.

There is no reason to play this game. I personally think the anime adaptation did a great job at portraying the backstory, much more interestingly and better paced, than the game.

If you consider buying this game and you aren't a diehard ultra fan of Steins Gate, don't. Just watch the anime adaptation, it will suffice.

8/10

Playable and enjoyable, but nowhere near as good as its predecessor.\t\t\t\t\t
