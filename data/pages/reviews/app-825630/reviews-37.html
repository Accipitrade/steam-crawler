\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI finished it a while ago already, but I've never actually written a review for it, so here I am.

I really got interested in the story, and despite Reyes not being an interesting character as she doesn't get any character development, she's only here to play as the bad "guy" and the lack of explanations on some important plot points (they're not plot holes, just poorly explained), I think I liked this game more than the original Steins;Gate.

The OST's awesome, the sprites are pretty great (even though the artstyle's slightly different and I feel like some sprites have their characters squint a bit), it introduced some interesting characters in addition to the already existing ones and again, the story's freaking amazing. Just be sure to not get lost because of all the stuff happening.

I'll personally recommend watching the anime after playing the game, especially if just like me, you didn't get why the events of the true ending were happening, why Okabe was doing what he was doing. \t\t\t\t\t
