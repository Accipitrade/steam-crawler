\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNo, it's not as good as Steins;Gate, but I still enjoyed it. The new characters are actually all very likeable and I enjoyed seeing them interact with the old characters. There are some very emotional moments between characters in this game that are just as good as the ones in the first game. The main issue is that the story is very disjointed and seems a bit aimless at times, parts of it will really make you say, "Why is this in the game???" If you find it annoying, I'd recommend watching the Steins;Gate 0 anime instead- this is actually one game where I think the anime is better, as it cuts out a lot of the fluff and attempts to tie things to the first game/anime better. But worth playing imo if you don't mind some slice-of-life and craziness.\t\t\t\t\t
