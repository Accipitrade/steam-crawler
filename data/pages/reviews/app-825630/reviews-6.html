\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Steins;Gate 0 Visual Novel and the anime actually encompass each other in the sense that the anime built upon what the visual novel brought forward, whilst the visual novel included lots of extra story lines on different routes. If you felt like the Steins;Gate 0 anime was missing something then I highly recommend picking up this visual novel because it provides a lot more content and character development for numerous more characters.

If you haven't watched the Steins;Gate 0 anime but have either played the Steins;Gate visual novel or watched the Steins;Gate anime then this visual novel will be a real treat for you if you enjoyed the cast of Steins;Gate and/or the core concepts that make Stein;Gate great.

You'll get to witness the transition between mad scientist to sad scientist which certainly contributes to the success of Okabe Rintaro as a character as he demonstrates more human qualities. Also, there are a few new characters in this visual novel that aren't present in the og Steins;Gate but they are all very much developed as characters in the visual novel, which was something I personally thought the anime lacked in a bit.

Overall, you should have a good time with this visual novel if you enjoyed the original Steins;Gate in either format, but if you haven't played the original visual novel or at least watched the original anime then make sure to do that before playing this visual novel or you'll be in for a world of confusion and spoilers for the og series since this is technically a midquel of sorts, albeit near the end of the original series. Other than that, enjoy! :D
