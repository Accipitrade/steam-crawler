\r\n\t\t\t\t\tContinuing with the trends set by the original novel, all CGs are beautifully made, a stellar soundtrack is incorporated, and a riveting plotline exists that will grip your heart in the end. Though this novel is not up to the original, it is still a great experience. Overall, I believe that Steins;Gate 0 is definitely worth the purchase.

Quick Opinions:

- I believe the plot is somewhat convoluted and difficult to follow at points, some are never clarified completely either.

- I believe that it is required to play this visual novel to fully appreciate the anime adaptation, as it is able to effectively remove unnecessary plot points

- This novel also expands on the original cast of characters very well, as well as adding characters that all play a role in the plot. The exception of Kagari, her character design seems like a quick and cheap at tug at Kurisu heartstrings.

- There is an increase of fan service in this novel compared to its predecessor, an unfortunate fact, but not done to an overbearing amount.

- Personally, I liked the anime adaption better than this VN, but playing this first allowed me to get a better grasp on the world of the Zero and 0kabe's personality in this worldline, as well as experience each ending that better flesh out characters such as Leskinen and Maho\t\t\t\t\t
