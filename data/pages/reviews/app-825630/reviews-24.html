\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMan...Man oh man,
I didn't think it was possible really i just didn't think i would enjoy this VN as much as i had.

I've been a pretty big fan of this franchise for about more than a year now and after watching the anime adaptations for both OG and 0 i at different points ended up buying the Visual novels for both on top of playing the spin offs.

Now let me just appeal to a few questions that would come up?

If you're a fan is this for you? especially if Visual novels aren't your thing?

I'd say if you really find yourself remotely infatuated with the series no matter where you start(anime and the likes) it's worth the effort to get through it, i myself picked up again after having it for ages and while the original VN was on and off for me i couldn't help but binge on this

How long is the base game on it's own? and how does it compare to the other VNs in both length and quality?
depending on whether you're thorough or not it should be roughly between 15 to 30 hours,
As for how it compare's in terms of length i think you'd spend just about the same amount of time with OG(though accessing the other routes is far easier as theirs much more so playtime may vary),
I've only played Linear bounded phenogram outside of these two so i'd say if you're slow about it you'll probably get roughly maybe just a bit more of that time out of it all.

Can i just watch the anime?

I think it's specifically more important to note that compared to the first game's anime adaptation this is vastly superior to the adaptation that was made for this game, like....the original had more dialogue but here for 0 my goodness there is so so so so much that is skipped over or gone through so fast you miss it completely.
If you're on a budget go for it but again even if you cop on a sale i think you'll enjoy what's here, it starts a little slow but just like the OG VN when it runs it just don't quit.

Love it or hate it there's many great things here, i may not replay for a while since i did just finish it but man do i wanna dive deeper wherever else i can.

I'd give it a 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
