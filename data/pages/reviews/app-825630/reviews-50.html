\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I absolutely love the original Steins;Gate, and I really like this VN, it leaves me with the sensation that this game is an afterthought.

Don't get me wrong. The story is really interesting, characters (returning and new) are fantastic, and it leaves you guessing for every turn. I fully reccommend this game to those who liked the first one. Wholeheartedly. But some things just don't seem to click into place just right to make me think that this alternate story line was planned from the beggining.

Completely reccommended for fans of the original, but when it ends it doesn't leave the same "magic" sensation as that one. But then again, how can you possible follow up one of the (if not the) best VNs of all time?\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tS;G 0 fills in a gap left by the original, and will actively spoil you if you haven't played through that already, so make sure you have.

With that said I can't recommend this enough for fans of the first - it's emotionally... darker than last time, without the long lead-in of lightheartedness. It starts dark and it only gets darker. But that means that the emotional high points hit so much harder.

You know the characters by now, and they're just as good here. Mr JAPANESE SHAMAN GIRLS's intonations can be a bit grating but beyond that it's all good.

One thing I can say is that Daru seems to have been written to more closely match his anime representation rather than his last VN appearance - his voice is different but you get used to that quickly enough.

Other than that, you know where the story begins, and where it must inevitably end. Enjoy the ride.\t\t\t\t\t
