\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe "sequel" to the amazing SciADV visual novel: Steins;Gate. Does Steins;Gate 0 live up to the name? Short answer? No. No it most certainly doesn't.

Despite what my listed time on steam is, I have read this VN 100% start to finish from the patched DMM version.

Steins;Gate 0 is the result of rushed production from MAGES. after the cancellation of Antimatter. Due to it being so rushed, its story lacks polish in nearly every way. Several plot points left completely contradictive, emotional threads left hanging, a really stupid and cringeworthy final twist, etc.

It's CGs and Music can range from amazing to "WTF WERE YOU THINKING?!?"

Overall, I rate Steins;Gate 0 a 4.5/10. The worst SciADV VN, aside from maybe C;C LCC. I can't recommend it to anyone unless they just want the full SciADV story.\t\t\t\t\t
