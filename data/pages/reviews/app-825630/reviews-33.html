I’m really glad that this game isn’t a direct sequel to the original because that would ruin the charm of the original (although episode 25 and the load region of deja vu movie weren’t terrible, they weren’t canon.) Without spoiling anything, it’s a midquel and tells the story of how the true ending was achieved. Overall, I really liked this game. Of course I didn’t love it as much as the original, but I really did enjoy reading through this. At first I was a little put off by it’s anime adaptation, but this VN fully explains the plot and characters in the time it needs to have. The decision making in this game is a great improvement over the original, it changes the plot in a completely different direction and actually makes sense, but sometimes you don’t know which choices affect the plot at all. I’d suggest playing this game in a specific order according to a guide because it can be really confusing. Well, even after reading through it fully it still doesn’t make sense and a lot of questions come up after playing, but you can watch the anime if you really need clarification.\t\t\t\t\t
