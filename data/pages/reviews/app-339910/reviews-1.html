\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis can be a fun physics-based platformer, but the physics are definitely questionable at times and sometimes the lava tiles aren't actually active (especially in the last level that I finished). Occasionally, there are areas you can get into that won't let you get back out (typically areas with the blue squares that change gravity). Checkpoints allow you to resume from there when you get killed (and you will get killed often). The jetpack that you use to get around only refills when you hit the ground or when you pick up a star icon that refills it or go through a glowing purplish field that refills it as long as you are not actively thrusting. Plenty of timers, I have seen the game not refill the jetpack when you land and take off immediately, which is a problem in the some of the tighter precision-platforming sections. 3 treasure chests are in each of the main stages, which give you money to buy entrance to an area where you can get extra characters to use (and they typically cost "3 monies" to open up).

Overall, this can be fun but prepare yourself for a good challenge if you want to 100% the game.\t\t\t\t\t

Uncraft World - Nice "Story"

*A sequal to an old game however with more levels, content and more challenge. Now, I won't be going over the story in this game because frankly that is not why you are buying this I would assume, if it is, I'm sorry to disappoint you.*

Gameplay
The original game which for those so inclined to search it up and play that too it is called "Uncraft Me", it's basically this game, just less expansive. The game is a 2D platformer focused around different obstacle courses. You are equipped with a jet-pack that allows for ascension and flight for a few seconds. This will prove to be very, very useful in some of the later levels but not so much in the first few ones since they don't prove much of a challenge and are more focused on teaching you the basics of the games elements. Every level ends with you revealing a picture of an anime girl and basically that is the whole premise of the game. It's pretty simple but thankfully the game ramps up it's difficulty quite a bit later on and the levels become more complex and harder to complete levels which is a great thing of course.

Additional Features
The game also has workshop support that allows the community to post their own custom pictures to the game that other can download and implement into the levels instead of the normal images. This will result in the custom picture showing up at the end of a level instead of the original image. And for those curious, yes it does allow you to have more "naughty" images if that is your thing!

Conclusion
Not bad. It's pretty simple and bare-bones but what is here is enjoyable to play and fun to play through some of the harder levels. I recommend if you don't mind a bit of a challenge! I don't know if the price is a bit too steep. I would probably take it down a couple euros, to 8€ instead of 10€ so if you can, buy it on sale to get more value out of it but overall, I recommend!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUncraft World is a side scrolling plat-former. It is in the pixel art style and resembles a bit of minecraft with the look of the characters and the maps with the block like design and solid colours.

The game has a unique mechanic where in the backdrop of each level is a picture which is covered by a brick wall. As you make your way through the level, you will hit checkpoints which will respawn you if you die. When you hit these checkpoints, part of the covered picture will be revealed. When you hit the end of the level and the final checkpoint, the whole picture will be revealed for your viewing pleasure.

It makes you want to complete the level and gives you a sense of a goal when playing through the level and achievement when finishing it. The pictures are colourful and have a high quality in terms of the character designs and are quite lewd but not nude. However this can be over come thanks to the great modding community which have posted lewd, nude, funny ad
and meme CG's which can be replaced easily for the in game counter-part. You can even add your own through the in game explorer.

Overall its a good fun game to sink a few minutes for a rewarding side scrolling platform experience.\t\t\t\t\t
