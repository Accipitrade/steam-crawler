\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter seeing Rooster Teeth do a video on this, I bought the first two Uncraft Me! games on the Xbox 360 a couple years ago and found them addictive, despite (or because of) the challenge and the "reward" (*wink wink*) at the end of each level. So, you could imagine my happiness when I found this game had finally been ported to Steam.

I have found that the game plays easier when using a game controller, like that for the Xbox One. I would not recommend using a keyboard to play this game, or you will find yourself having a much harder time controlling your character, thus causing you to die multiple times, and not beat the level until 20-30 minutes later.

However, I have noticed a major bug in this game. When I am playing a session of this game, the game will crash unexpectedly. This has happened multiple times without warning. Since this game is still in early access, I can understand that there are bugs that could crash the game. So I call upon Team Shuriken to fix them as soon as possible.

Overall, despite some bugs needing fixing and heightened difficulty when playing with a keyboard instead of a game controller, I find this game to be very enjoyable, especially with the added challenge of finding treasure chests. I would definitely recommend this game to anyone who likes a challenging platformer to play. Keep up the good work, guys!\t\t\t\t\t
