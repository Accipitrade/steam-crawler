\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was a blast to complete, picked it up on sale when it was dirt-cheap and I regret not paying more, it's trial and error hell, everything must be executed perfectly to move on, if you think this game is about the boobs, you're badly mistaken, the artwork is bland, albiet funny at times, but you should only buy this for the game that it is.

Remember playing Sonic and learning the fastest route through a level by replaying it hundreds of times?

Well this is like that, except there's only one route, with lava everywhere and rockets chasing you through it, tons of different hazards, tons of difference in each level, it's frustrating but it's a damn good feeling when you finally complete it, it is a real challenge.

"But why does it have to have scantily clad anime women in it?"

Well, they could just have you run through the levels and it would still be fun, but it's nice to see your progress as you move forward, as the picture is gradually revealed you KNOW you're almost at the end and so the final challenge (which is usually the hardest) is the last one before you can rest and quit stressing over making that...turn...just...freaking...right...don't...hit...the...lava-DAMMIT.

Did I mention that it has a guy screaming "WHAT THE ♥♥♥♥" "DAMMIT" "ARRRRGGGHHH" among other things every time you die?

If you like a challenge, buy this, if you're easily frustrated and just want boobs, go to Google.

9/10 The hardest challenge I've come across in a video game since Mario Frustration.

The only downside is it has a physics glitch that is negligible, but makes landing and turning a bit problematic if you take off immediately afterwards, a few minor bugs here and there but nothing that will make you hate it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first the game seems funny and cute, but after the first 5-10 level the difficulty makes the hole game collapse, it's a rage game in sheep's clothing. As soon as it gets really hard some problems begin to shine through you didn't see on the lower difficulty:

1. At first the checkpoints seem reasonably set, but as harder the level get the longer they get, but they don't get more checkpoints. So as the courses get harder you have to play ever growing bits again and again.

2. The game lets you geuss where you have to go. The only clues you get are some arrows pointing in a direction at the checkpoints, but because the course goes up and down, left and right it doesn't help you much as soon as the levels get a bit more complicated. Sometimes even in the easy levels you can't tell what's in the foreground and what's in the background and as soon as the levels get more complicated it's nearly impossible to tell. These two things then change the game from a funny little platformer to a trial and error mess, where you guess the right way and die a million times until you find the right way.

3. The character slides a little when he lands. This will kill you many times when you have to land on a surface that's even smaller than you, because even if you hit the surface you will still slide off and die.

4. I had the problem that for some reason the game didn't fit in my computer screen, so the edge was cut off, but I couldn't find any ingame options menu to change something at the graphic settings.


These are the things that in my opinion destroy the game. There are a few other things like the music that is good at the beginning, but as soon as the levels get harder and longer it gets really nerve-wracking because it loops the hole time. And the cursing of the character when he dies is also pretty annoying, but you can turn it off, so no damage there as soon as you realise it.
So yea, in my opinion it's pretty much youtube gold and probably some hardcore platformer will enjoy the game for more than 2 hours, but I can not recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
