\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere's not a whole lot of stuff to say about this game. It's pretty simple.

PROS:
+The gameplay itself is decent. It's your standard, run of the mill hard-but-fair platformer. You can move left and right, and propel yourself through the air with a limited jetpack that resets upon landing. Levels are short and tough, with plenty of hazards that are integrated pretty well for the most part. The game usually introduces new hazards in an easy and fair way. Quick respawns upon death allow for trial-and-error gameplay to help you learn.

+Boobs. The game comes with SFW boobs. You can import your own SFW/NSFW boobs. You can use the Steam Workshop to download other people's boobs. Other games have no boobs. This game has boobs. They're the best boobs. They're tremendous boobs. Everybody loves them.

+Level editor. It's a thing.

CONS:
-The game feels incredibly cheap and unpolished. Even $10 feels like a lot for this game. The UI is terrible, to the point where it feels like you're playing a Flash game. The sound design is awfully weak, and the Minecraft-esque design is an excuse to get away with very low quality graphics. Graphics which have absolutely zero settings you can change in-game, mind you. No way to change resolution, AA, shadows, textures, none of that. If your pc can't run the game as is, tough cookies. If it can, you'd better like how the game looks because you aren't gonna be able to make it look any better.

-Extremely weak progression system. There's nothing to unlock except more levels, boring SFW gallery pictures, and occasionally a new ugly character model. The game has no overarching goal or any other rewards to work towards. You just kind of unlock levels until you don't anymore and then you're done. Thanks for your $10, we hope you had fun.

CONCLUSION:
A decent platformer that's good at killing a little bit of time, loosely wrapped in what is currently a very unpolished package. I know it's an early access game, but it's been out for well over a year and this is still the current state it's in. There will probably be improvements but I wouldn't expect it to change a whole lot from here. Price is a little high in my opinion.

Everybody likes boobs though.\t\t\t\t\t
