\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love hidden object games, but played this for less than half an hour and think I've got the most out of what this game has to offer.

There aren't any specific levels - it's more an arcade mode where finding all the objects extends the timer. Some people may enjoy this, but it got kinda boring and repetitive very quickly.

There are 3 difficult modes. But in each, after a few rounds the game just adds so many items to the screen that it has to zoom out quite far to display them all. There doesn't seem to be any option to zoom, and the anti-aliasing on the items seemed quite poor, so it quickly reached the point I would lose due to the graphical performance. Perhaps I just had the wrong screen settings, but there doesn't seem enough content in the game for me to want to spend time fixing this.\t\t\t\t\t

The game has three different difficulty levels and wonderful graphics with bright colors. You can give the game to your child if you want to teach him how to use a computer mouse. Find This! can be played as an adult during a short break in the office to relax your head. The investment was worth it and I was positively impressed.
