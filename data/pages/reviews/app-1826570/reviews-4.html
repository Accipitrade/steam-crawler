\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed the original First Class game a year or so ago, really enjoyed the atmosphere of the game and the challenge of some of the puzzles. Was excited to see a second First Class game was now available!

This game is quite similar to the first in nature. The plot/storyline is that you're chasing down spies aboard a 1930s airship but that's wholly irrelevant to the game (and honestly a little nonsensical, given the environment you're in and the nature of some of the puzzles) . You just progress through the "airship", solving puzzles.

Controls are largely point-and click with some keyboard usage needed when reading books and other things. Like the original game the controls can be aggravating at times and occasionally interfere with solving puzzles. Opening doors and putting down items can take a few tries sometimes.

As for the puzzles themselves, some of the puzzles were fairly easy but several took a fair amount of headscratching and thought to figure out. Sometimes there are clues as to how the puzzle works, other times there are no clues and you're left to figure out WTF you're looking at (which is a good thing). None were over-the-top difficult (IMHO).

As of this writing the game is a new release and there were some glitches. The game crashed on me 45 minutes into it and I lost all of my progress, so manually save the game now and again. There were some issues with items passing through other items and some minor grammatical errors in the English version but none of this really detracted from the gameplay.

Overall I really enjoyed this game and if you enjoy puzzle games I recommend it highly.\t\t\t\t\t
