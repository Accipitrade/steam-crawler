\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe story of First Class Escape 2 - Head in the clouds happends in an airship, high above the ground. You're an agent, you need to find out about your co-workers doings, while trying to escape!

As the previous game, this was also a very entertaining and mindboggling game, and I once again tried my best to do as much of the game as I could on my own; without help...and I would say it was a success for the most. I came to a point though, where I got a puzzle I found was pretty much ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. It was not right, and I didn't understand it even using a walkthrough. For another puzzle the game also introduced a new way of gathering information which I had not seen before.

As I had played the game for many hours, I started getting tired of it, and even though I didn't use much help through the entire game; not even for collectibles, there were around 3 things I just got the answer for. In the end I really felt it was good to be through the game, but it made me occupied in a good way for many hours, where I did everything else myself, and that is the reason I will definitely recommend this game.

If you want to do everything yourself, without help, I recommend taking a longer break from the game, so you can try it again with a fresh mind.

For those wondering if this is a game for them or not, it's pretty simple. If you don't give up that easily, and you like puzzles, you should defintely try the game. My opinion about finding a solution is that it's not a bad thing, but I try to exhaust every possible way until it feels like ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ and my patience runs dry. Even doing this game with a walkthrough would probably be entertaining, but it feels so much more fullfilling when you're doing it yourself ;)!

The game is not really easy, but it's doable, at least to a certain point. Buy the game!\t\t\t\t\t
