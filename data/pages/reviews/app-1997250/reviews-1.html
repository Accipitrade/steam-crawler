\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite what I'm about to write this game had me going back and forth.

Eventually I decided on giving it a thumbs up but please read the rest first.

I give this game a thumbs up because TECHNICALLY it does what it sets out to do.

It's a simulator about living life on a mountain and all the fixings that go with that.

But the game is unfortunately rather boring. Now if you're looking for a game where you can build as many houses as you want in as many different ways as you want with absolutely nothing else, then this game is right up your alley.

But it's basically like someone said "hey remember all the most boring grinds of ARK? Or Rust? Or Raft? Or 7 days to die? Let's make a game out of those grinds!"

because this game consists of chopping wood, mining stone, hunting deer, fishing, and building houses and that's it. Now if they wanted it to be more of a relaxing game then why give us a food meter? Why not give us ambiance in the forest where we spend all day? Why not give us certain benefits to adding more stuff to the house? Why give our truck a gas gauge? Why make the truck hard to drive? Why make it so damn dark that you can barely see even during daytime?
This game has a lot of issues and problems and if it had been released in early access I might have actually given it a bit of slack, but it released fully finished. Yet playing it feels like an unfinished empty game.

It's buggy, it's glitchy, it's sluggish, it's hard to see, most of its sound effects are awesome as is the music but the gun sound effects are awful. The contrast and the lighting are awful, the gameplay loop is the single most boring part of other games and this one is focused around it.

I suppose if you're just looking for a simple building game and you don't like the freedom of minecraft then sure...you could play and enjoy this game. And even though they did what they set out to do, it still feels empty and pointless.

It's just my opinion but I don't undrestand the appeal here. I didn't see the fun while playing it. ANd if you'd like a closer look at the game then check out the video on my YouTube Channel (link below).

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d-CdWFJOHnk

But for me personally, I give it a thumbs up because it does exactly what it set out to do and nothing more. But this is a game I will probably never touch again. It was just empty, boring and repetitive.

Thanks for reading and I hope I managed to help some of you out there to spend your money wisely.\t\t\t\t\t
