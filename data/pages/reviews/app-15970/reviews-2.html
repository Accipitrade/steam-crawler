If the 'snake' reaches the end before you've destroyed all of them, you will lose. Also, there are a few powerups to help you in your quest. That was about the simplest way to describe this game :)

Luxor is a fun little game and it will most likely enjoy anyone who enjoys casual games but the downside is that, on a regular day, this game will cost you 10 bucks, which is way too much. Also, like most Mumbo Jumbo games, there are (more ugly) other versions of Luxor available online for free.\t\t\t\t\t

I find the power-ups that do drop in Luxor to be very unsatisfactory and lacking in excitement. The map design appears to be rehashed continuously throughout the estimated ~8 hour play through.
