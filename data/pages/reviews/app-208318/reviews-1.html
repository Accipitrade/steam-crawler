\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis DLC is quite old at this point, but there is a lot hidden under the price tag. The amount of features included in this are incredible, and I feel this goes for all Reppo products. While £17.99 is quite steep compared to the normal price tag of £14.99 for these sorts of products, the amount of detail more than makes up for the price, provided you buy in a sale. There are reskins available on Railworks America which expand the scenario oppurtunity as well! One of my favourite features is the fact than when you sit in the passenger view, there is the odd chance that a waiter will walk down the aisle. There is also old fashioned (50s?) music playing in the background which really adds to the ambience. My only major criticism is that the cab sounds are mediocre at best and there is some sort of bug when you open a cold start scenario, the engine shutdown audio will repeat over and over. You can fix that by turning on and off the battery but it is mildly annoying.

Overall Score: 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
