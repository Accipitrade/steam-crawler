\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA fantastic follow up to an already fantastic route. The stars of the addon are the two locomotives. The gas-mechanical locomotive is very well done. I have actually run a similar locomotive and it handles exactly as I expected. The gearbox and clutch is fully functional, as is the hand crank to re-start the motor when you stall out! It's spot-on. The little battery mine loco is a very nice model too. The developers modeled all 4 controls the prototype has, these being the reverser, throttle, handbrake, and light switch. The package also includes a few new wagons, all of which look great. The included scenarios are good, offering a bit of a challenge as you shunt wagons around and move materials around for the upkeep of the railway. I highly recommend this pack, and the base route too. Both are high quality and very affordable. Well done Skyhook!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been waiting for this pack since it was anouced (a bit more than a year I beleive) and it blew my expectations out of the water. This pack brings an electric and an early gas mechanical locomotive to the Corris Railway, and adds much needed variation to the route. Although the electric is nothing amazing (these locomotives are simple in nature and the simulation is done great none the less) the gas mechanical is where this pack shines. This little engine is insanely detailed, and brings a new type of challenge to train sim. The addition of a clutch and gear system makes this engine a fun challenge (more challenging than most steam locomotives in this game) and learning this system will take alot of trial and error. If you are into the types of equipment in this DLC, or just want some high quality content for the allready great Corris route, then you should pick this up, and at $10.99 US dollars, its a steal.\t\t\t\t\t
