So when I booted this up, my expectations were high and this game met them.
There some fiddly things, however. Trying to get to the fuse box in the electrics and figuring out the trick to get derailed engines back on the track were a cause of frustration.
But in the main, I liked how the designers added complexity with each engine type and l also appreciated that the missions weren't repetitive unlike other mechanic simulators.
Good job!\t\t\t\t\t
