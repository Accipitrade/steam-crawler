\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBought the game discounted for £0.86. For that price, despite the poor reviews i figured it was worth a look and I was pleasantly surprised, even taking into account i had very low expectations. Ive just played 1.5 hours or so and really enjoyed it.

The game looks great for a start. The graphics are excellent and the scenery in the outdoor portions is really quite beautiful.
The gameplay itself is interesting... fun even. Ofc you have to have an interest to begin with otherwise dont even buy this game.

Seeing the different components on the trains, removing them and re-fitting them, was really satisfying. Its obviously well researched and quite detailed. I would have liked to see a more interesting animation for the repairing process rather than simply a rapid 0-100% progress bar, but other than that i cant really fault the game in the time i have played it.

In summary i would say if the title grabs your attention its really worth taking a look. You too might be pleasantly surprised.These kinds of games arent for everyone but for those of us interested I would recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
