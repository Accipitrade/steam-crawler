\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a bit on the fence about this one but I ultimately did enjoy my time enough to say it's positive. Microgames are always a very fun style so it's hard to go wrong with them. The ones here are fun and can offer a challenge too.
The reason on the fence though is there are a lot of minor annoyances. The biggest one is how slow the game can be. I think there's like 5-7 seconds between each microgame where the arm slowly shows you which control sceme to use. Compare this to warioware where it's like 3 seconds max. Not to mention some of the microgames themselves are really long, like 20-30 seconds it feels like. That doesn't sound like a lot but it's long for a simple minigame.
I also had some issues with one particular achievement (3x2) because it took forever just to get to that level sometimes, and it also just felt completely random when it actually happened. It took me like half my time to get that one achievement. I think 5 would've been a lot more reasonable for that.
This isn't inherently an issue, more a personal taste thing, but this game is also really memey. You can probably tell from the trailers, but a lot of them really are just like memes and references. Again not inherently an issue but you'll know if you'll be annoyed by that or not.
Like I said, ultimately had a positive experience. It's pretty short and cheap so feel free to try it out. My time is for 100%, I think to beat it took me an hour.\t\t\t\t\t
