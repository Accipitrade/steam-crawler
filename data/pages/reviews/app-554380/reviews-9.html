This is a review I honestly wish that there was a Neutral option for because I am split between Positive & Negative.

Positives:
The Trapper masks look great. Plus, 1 of those masks was made by a community member
Hillbilly items are looking good and by far are the most diFFerent
The fact it adds more customisation options is always nice
The price is pretty neat for what you get.

Negatives:
Both Wraith items are just terrible re-skins
All the Killer items come under a single customisation option out of the 3 they have. So there are no full outfit changes
The 80's Suitcase had more items (and better IMO) for the same price

Like I already said, I am split with this review. However, I am going to leave it positive because the content is nice but at the same time somewhat disappointing.\t\t\t\t\t
