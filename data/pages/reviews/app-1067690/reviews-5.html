\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHi Devs,

You have managed to do something remarkable in 2019. A serious expansion to your core game while maintaining quality of gameplay and art!

I love fighting the new barbs. The camps are utter chaos. The starts are pretty great, and you're already ironing out some of the jankier backgrounds.

However, many of the players feel that the barb loot is underwhelming. What's the point of fighting barbs if you are getting worse weps and armor, while sustaining higher amounts of armor damage?

After a campaign exclusively int he north, I realized i still had no gear so I tried to pivot to the south to fight brigands and promptly wiped.

The fact is that barbarians don't offer a competitive progression of acquiring weapons and armor that will prepare you for increasing difficulties. This is unfortunate because it incentivizes us to continue grinding Brigandquest and not experience the new content.

I would love for the northern wastes to offer the same ability to prepare for mid-late game as fighting brigands.

Thank you!
