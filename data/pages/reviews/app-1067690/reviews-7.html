\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend this DLC:

Pro
+Barbarians are nice human enemies. Not too weak but not to strong either. A relief from all those overpowered enemies.
+The new northern/rus helmets look awesome
+More music is a good thing
+The company origins add A LOT of flavour and replayability to the game
+New weapons like the whip are useful (I was skeptical of the whip first, but after testing it i have to say it is really awesome!)

Con
- Why is there no barbarian endgame crisis???? It should have been there!
- Alps are still broken
- The Northern Raider Origin has you actually play not in the north but in the south because the northern houses are always your enemies. Which means that you cant get barbarian/nordic/rus equipment which means your company actually has the least amount of barbaric flavour of all the origins!!! That needs to be changed somehow.
