\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic. Another great addition to the already solid base game. A whole new faction of enemies, bridging the gap between early game bandits and mid game orcs and undead etc. Barbarians are a great early-midgame opponents that pose an unique threat, but are not nearly as menacing as 12 armored Wiedergangers and a Necro fortress in your early days.

Not all of them seem to be groundbreaking and lose meaning fairly quickly in a campaign, but subtly affect the game. Others make your whole run around them like the lone hero or cultist ones. They might need a little refining donw the road, but overall the addition is incredible for the replay value of this title.

More great soundtrack, more weapons, more famed weapons, more events, more legendary locations, more of EVERYTHING!
It's wonderful and the pricing is very fair at full price. Great value.\t\t\t\t\t
