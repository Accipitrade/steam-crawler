The new sound theme for the warriors of the North is awesome, I put my headphones on to better listen to it (still, the hexe one is my favorite), I started with the Deserters origin, the one I was really waiting for. Now I have a band of poor hungry fellows, badly equipped and with few stocks, trying to survive and make a name for themselves while being hunted down by a cruel noble house that wants to hang them, dodging brigands, spiders, witches and every kind of danger. During their roaming they encountered some fierce barbarians of the North and managed to dispose of them, also stole some of their equipment. A peaceful juggler recruited in some southern town is now entering battle brandishing a wooden club with an animal claw fixed on top of it.
All in all, great DLC, looking forward to discover its full potential in the next days. Congrats to the developers and consider to keep working on this game with more DLC.\t\t\t\t\t
