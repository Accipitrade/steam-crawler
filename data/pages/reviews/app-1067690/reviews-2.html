\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo you enjoy:

- getting rekt by RNG even on "beginner" level?
- see your guys miss and miss and miss? (basically, think bad Xcom)
- savescum a lot, and I mean dozens of times, as recovering from a few losses is almost not possible in this game?

Then this is the game for you!

Everything is random and it's wrecking the game, especially in the beginning and endgame. In the beginning, if you have a couple of misses and the enemy has a couple of hits, it's game over. You can reload, reload and reload, until you get the hits and the enemy misses - bang, you win the fight. Sound like fun? Now, in the beginning you might still recover as new mercenaries are cheap and you don't need them high level. Think endgame however, after you spent dozens of hours leveling up your guys. Yes, you have more capabilities to find strategies to try and cheese the game, but losing even one of those guys quickly sets you on a path you can't recover from...

Obviously the game attracted a cohort of players who love the game and they want it to be as tough as nails. In that way it is succesful. However, it means it caters to a small group of hardcore players, who think casuals should "git gud". Now, I am not a casual, having been gaming for over 35 years. So I believe this game is flawed and broken in many ways, making it very frustrating. And I played almost 200 hours now...

Spare yourself the frustration!

It's a shame really, also for the developers, as they are missing a lot of extra purchases. But of course, this might have been their vision and they might be content with a game that is pretty much unknown, has a couple of thousands of players at peak levels, but has good reviews with that group.\t\t\t\t\t
