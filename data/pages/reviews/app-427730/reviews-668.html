\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-Overall Review-

Who's Your Daddy is an interactive game about a father attempting to prevent his baby from certain death. Although the description of the game seems bland, Who's Your Daddy is a very fun and addictive game with depth added into its every design. This game is one of the few games that have lots of potential, and Who's Your Daddy takes advantage of that from doing everything possible to show its true form, a well-rounded, polished, fun game. From the many different items that are used both for the baby and daddy and the realistic house, it all adds into the feeling of saving your baby or dying a horrible death. I personally enjoyed tricking my daddy by closing doors to make it look like I wasn't in the room and then grabbing a hammer, rushed to the glass table in the TV room and smashed the glass and roll in it, gloating in my glory. You can be stuck for hours in this game without realizing how much time has passed because of the little details and Easter eggs that are hidden in the game. Figuring out how to eat batteries and Clorox Bleach is just scraping the surface. From vents in the house to finding car keys to hiding in a cabinet, Who's Your Daddy distances itself from other games because of the detail to every single thing possible to see and still maintaining the fair price and continuous weekly updates that make the game better. However, all games aren't perfect. Glitches forcing you to restart the game and minor setbacks here and there are always looming above you, however Who's Your Daddy somehow distracts the player from these imperfections by giving engulfing you in every game of 1 versus 1, making you want to play more, thus creating the best experience possible. If you aren't sure about getting this game, get it now! You won't regret it.

--Pros--

Easter eggs and small details continue to pull you into the game.

Whether you are the baby or daddy, the suspense of saving the baby or hiding from the daddy is always around the corner.

Early Access continues to bring new and improved features into an already great game.

--Cons--

Often times you find yourself waiting for minutes at a time for someone to join or someone to join you.

The "servers" are not fully functional and may glitch at times.

Frustration is always there when the biggest glitch of all occurs: You and someone else start a game, and the timer does not start, forcing you to exit and restart the game.

---Verdict---

Overall despite some minor setbacks and glitches, Who's Your Daddy provides players with a plethora of items to toy around with, and the exciting baby versus daddy episodes are sure to last.
