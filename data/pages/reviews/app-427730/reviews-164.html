Yeah First Of All I Have No F**king Idea Why I Bought This Game But I Remember Me Playing It When I Was Like 6? Who's Your Daddy Is A Game About A Dad And His Baby, The Dad Has To Protect The Child By All Means Possible Because As A Baby You Try To Commit Suicide, (Sounds Just Like My Childhood) Yeah The Games Pretty F**cked To Be Honest But I Guess It Can Be Fun For Like A Round?

Gameplay
So I Played The Game Like A Month Ago Because I Was Going Down Memory Lane Of Childhood Games And I Decided To Play It, Suprisingly There Were 1000 Players Online Now That Impressed Me, I Don't Know If It's The Same Deal Here Tho, And As Far As I Know Everyone Wants To Play As The Baby So Yeah Maybe Matchmaking Is Going To Take A While. The Gameplay Is Fun And Janky, It's Kind Of All Over The Place Really, I've Already Told You What It's About, The Dad Has To Protect The Baby And The Baby Tries To Commit Suicde Pretty Simple, There's Things Around The Map That Can Give Both The Dad And The Baby Advantages, There's Also Different Skins For The Baby And Dad And They Actually Give You Like Boosts And Sh*t, BUT It's Not Pay-2-Win In This Case It's Play-2-Win Which Makes It Way Better, Still Won't Play It Again Tho, There's Different Gamemodes And When You Launch The Game You Can Choose Between The 2016 Version Or The 2020 Version And Each Is Very Different But The Objective Is Still The Same. I Saw That There Was New Gamemodes But Idk What All Of Them Are, I Just Know There's A 4 Vs 4 Mode And 2 Vs 2 I Think? Idk.

Thoughts
Yeah I Really Don't Know How To Feel About This Game, I Bought It When I Was Young And I've Somehow Played For 24 Hours And I Don't Know How, If I Mention This Game To Anyone They'll Prob Think I'm Like A Freak Or Something Just Because Of The Name Alone And I'm Pretty Sure None Of My Friends Would Wanna Play "Who's Your Daddy" With Me, And To Be Honest I Wouldn't Want To Either, It's Cheap And Sure It Could Be Fun Playing With Friends But Randoms I'm Not Sure About Cause I Got Bored Pretty Quick And I'm Sure Even If I Played With Friends They Would Also Get Bored Really Quickly Plus Matchmaking Is Just Not Great.

Would I Recommend?
Yeah No. 4 Euro's To Play A Game That's Fun Once Isn't What I Would Go For Unless You're Rich. There Really Isn't Much To Say Here FFS The Game Is Repditve And Boring, Matchmaking Is Dead And I Recommend Spending 4 Euro's On Something Else.
