\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-Quick Review-
Who's Your Daddy is genre I'd like people to start using called "Creepy Simulator", on par with the games: "What's under your blanket !?" and "Shower With Your Dad".
If we're speaking specifically, it's a competitive, co-op game between a baby and father, the person controlling the baby is trying to kill himself or herself, while the father frantically runs around trying to keep the baby from death's grip.

-Detailed breakdown review-
Story There's not much to say about the game's story, however *I* have a number of stories from playing the game. I purchased this game to play it with my younger brother, and then laughed until I cried at all the hilarious events that took place.
To all of my friends who sent me messages saying "oh god, that game"; instead of judging me from afar, you should have joined my twitch stream and laughed along with me (because my brother is so much more funnier then me).
From setting myself on fire as a baby, predicting what my brother was going to do, and hearing his frustrated screams of "don't give me those pills! Keep that apple away from me!", it really was a hilarious stream.

Gameplay: WARNING!!!! I'm not going to spoiler tag my whole review, but lets say this game is way more fun when you don't know very much about the mechanics of the game. Grab two copies of this game, grab a friend, skype, and enjoy. Read on if you don't mind having basic gameplay spoiled.

I have already explained the gist of the game. If you're the father: you try to keep your child alive by moving dangerous objects away, and giving pills and fruit to your child to keep them healthy until mom gets home! As the baby your objective is to get yourself killed in one of the very many MANY ways of doing so, drowning, freezing, burning, poison, power outlets, and more that are still being added (03/07/16) as the game updates.

Some may criticize the game on it's graphics, or glitches that may happen while playing. Naturally in most games this would be a negative quality, but there's a hilarious novelty about the game, and it's not over priced for the entertainment.

Achievements: There are no steam achievements at this time. This is a game which would be fun to have "dumb achievements", just so achievers can grab an easy 100%, since the game at this time is purely 1v1 co-op, an achievement for each death would be very easy to teach people the various ways you can die, or power ups you can unlock as the father.

Price: Who's Your Daddy is priced at $4.99, now I normally grade games by 1 dollar for every hour played, but for this game I make an exception. The quality of laughter and company of my brother over ruled the standard rule.
At this time I've spent two and a half hours playing the game. I have no doubt as the game is updated we will continue to play it occasionally, so even in price and hour the game will probably make it!

Conclusion: There's not much else to say about the game, the developer has already made a great game, and we can all look forward to seeing what other madness will be added to the game, and hopefully the developers don't get picked up by child support services before the game comes out of Early Access.
I recommend this game for anyone to play with another friend (if you have a funny friend, it's even better), if you're generally a solo gamer this is a skip for you.\t\t\t\t\t
