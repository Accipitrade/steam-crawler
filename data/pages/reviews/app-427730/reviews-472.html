-=-Overall-=-
This game is about a baby trying to kill itself, while the dad tries to prevent that. The graphics look like ♥♥♥♥, and the gameplay isn't very smooth, but that's the fun part of the game: the awful graphics and downright absurdity of the gameplay. The baby can die in various ways, including but not limited to eating batteries, frying oneself on a stove, overdosing on pills, driving the car into a wall which makes it explode, and toaster bath. The dad has to hide dangerous objects, lock cabinets, and otherwise monitor the baby and keep him healthy. This game can't be enjoyed without a friend, so make sure someone else you know wants to play this too. It does get boring after a while unfortunately. All in all, this game is weird, wacky, and absurd, but that's the best part.
