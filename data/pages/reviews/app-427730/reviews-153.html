\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile the classic version was quite good, their successive updates have ruined the initial concept.

I remember days when, with great effort, the father character could win. Those days are long gone it that is why the game is on its deathbed. Playing as both the dad and the baby is now boring due to the fact that the baby can die within 30 seconds of the start of the game in some cases. The shear number of ways, and the effectiveness of them, renders the dad character obsolete. In the classic version, the father had time to both stop the baby from dying as well as mitigate future attempts, and while the latter still applies, it is severely limited. Not to mention that the ability to eat almost everything enables a permanent means of death.

All told, the game is now pointless, as the only time you actually get to play is if the child effectively chooses not to and instead toys with the dad for an extended period of time before finally killing themselves. At this point the only means for fun I can see is as a split screen party game.

While I don't regret my initial purchase, I strongly advise you to reconsider buying it in 2022. While the option to play classic is there, no one plays it, and even that had already been ruined to some degree, so the purchase is still not justified in that respect.\t\t\t\t\t
