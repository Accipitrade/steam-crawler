\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this for a while the other night and immediately fell in love. I hope this stays simple though, I think the simplicity is what makes it great. I love the idea of being able to jump into multiplayer and be playing...seemingly anonymously in seconds.

The pros:
Great Concept, it's funny while keeping you focused and the action lively.
Clean Graphics, makes it easy to identify what you're doing and where you're going.
Good menu, easy to understand and easy to navigate.
Small install size, doesn't seem to be a lot of bloat here, it was something like a 111MB download?

The cons:
strange physics glitches, it felt jittery and unstable - it was hard to reliably climb the stairs especially.
there might have been some lag issues, it was hard to know what was a network hiccup or a physics issue
the in-game HUD is minimalist, which is nice, but it's so minimalist I'm wasn't sure what I was looking at most of the time

things to add?:
more weird music, the current track is neither good nor bad, but if it's on a playlist with other weird songs I think it'd work well.
bigger maps - the current map is small enough it's hard to hide away as the baby and get out of the father's view.
multiplayer mode? - multiple babies, multiple dads something like this might be fun for team 'deathmatch' type play
ways for the baby to trip/temporarily disable the parent
ways for the parent to put the baby in the crib temporarily


Overall, a great start though, I'm really looking forward to seeing how silly this gets.\t\t\t\t\t
