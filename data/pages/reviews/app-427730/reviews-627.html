\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd have to say, if you're interested in this, do not buy it, at least not now.

Bought it a while ago for my son and I to play, after he was watching a let's play of it. Seemed like a goofy game to mess around with.
It is, actually.
For maybe a round or two.
When we first got it, it had issues, but hey, it's early access, right? Well, as each update came out, it seemed like there were more and more issues. Weird lag, objects not working, getting stuck, random people joining our password protected custom game, etc.
Then, out of nowhere, it's 'released'. I'm sorry, but this game is NOT ready for release.
Not sure where to begin.
How about performance? Pretty abysmal. Can be running smooth, but then the framerate just plummets, mouse movement is all jerky, and it just feels rough.
Graphics-Quite poor. I'm not one to judge a game by it's graphics by any means, but overall, the game just looks bad. Looks like the devs just slapped it together and called it good. Character models are atrocious (hats and glasses are humorous though), textures are all over the place. Some look fine, but others don't make any sense. Go outside and look at the achievement room. The room is flat, but the 'bricks' taper off. Good example of it being slapped together.
Controls-Serviceable I suppose, although picking up items and using them is a major chore.
Balance-absolutely ridiculous. It's not a matter of who is going to win? The baby or the daddy? It's a matter of if the baby dies in one minute or two. The daddy has only a couple things he can do to stave off the baby's death, but there are sooo many options for the baby to off itself, it quickly turns into a race to see just how fast the baby can kill itself, especially with warping through vents.
Idea/concept-brilliant. Actually a really good idea for a game, but there are so many issues right now, I'd stay away. We'll probably keep playing it off and on for a while to see if things improve, but it's quickly becoming tiresome.
One thing I forgot about, and just remembered. The soundtrack. One of the most annoying and grating soundtracks I've ever heard.\t\t\t\t\t
