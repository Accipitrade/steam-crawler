\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played this game quite a bit, even if just currently 2.2 hours, I've killed myself in every way possible as the baby and would proclaim that I can be a great dad who can block his baby from killing him/herself, equally so - I can kill myself anytime as the baby. One could say babies capabilities to kill itself is OP. Sometimes it's true, sometimes not.

Review: The game is 1v1, Online-only Dad Vs Suicidal Baby. Items randomly position themselves everytime in select locations that are meant for the dad, or for the baby. Things ranging from a Padlock for the dad to lock cupboards so the baby has harder time accessing poison and dangerous objects, but also things like the Baby Key which can unlock the cupboards if found and used. Other items may range from Fruit, or meat in the fridge which can heal the baby if 'wounded'.

The point is to keep your baby alive obviously, or to die if you're the baby, it's fun whether with a stranger or with a friend.

I make Let's Plays in my spare time and I've currently made 3 videos. The first is my first experience with the game and the troubles of settling in. The 2nd was me showing off my 'Skill as the Dad' and the 3rd and most recent at the time of writing this was me being the most miserable little ♥♥♥♥ as possible as a baby. A link to the gameplay videos will be below for those who wish to help me and view gameplay to learn the ins and outs of the game! :>

My only wish is that the game of course, continues to be updated, not only will there be new rooms in the future and fixes to the game already, but I hope later from there we'll receive different areas like Outside, a Park, a backyard, or a new house layout to continue expanding the game. :D

My only Cons about the game is that after 1-3 hours, you may already be done the game. I don't see this being a Long-term issue though as long as new layouts, levels, obtsacles and items continue being made though. I'd love to see more ideas/games like this in the future to appease my sadistic nature :>
