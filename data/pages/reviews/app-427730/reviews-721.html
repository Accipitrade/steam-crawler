\r\n\t\t\t\t\tV0.4 REVIEW**

Holy mother of children, this game is hilarious. At first glance it looks a little shallow but once you figure out some of the mechanics it's actually pretty deep. Lot of strategy.
Totally recommend. At this point there is a lot to improve on but the game was super entertaining for the first hour.
Tips for first timers:
- The fruit heals the baby but once the heal runs out damage over time continues
- Look for the pills (green bottles), they will stop damage over time but won't heal
- Be mindful of all utensils as baby can get electrocuted
- baby can use the vent system
- hammer can break glass table so be wary

Overall I found being the dad much much harder but I think there is a higher learning curve. After about 45 mins me and my friend figured out different strategies for each so the dad was winning a bit more.\t\t\t\t\t
