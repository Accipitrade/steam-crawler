(Written as of....Alpha V0.3.0. Can't wait for the update this friday!)

The game right now is fun but has issues. The gameplay is rather simple. One player is a baby who must die before time runs out (I think it's 4 minutes.) while the dad must keep him safe.

Now while the gameplay has evolved over the versions (Pills used to heal baby too and were infinite.) the game has some serious balance issues. I was able to play for about an hour as baby (I'd leave and make a new lobby each time so I'm always baby.) and was able to win every time, often before the first 30 seconds. Forks spawn near the crib and you can easily jump up, grab it, and use it on an outlet to die instantanously. If a hammer is on the TV stand you can smash the coffee table and eat the glass to die quick too.

Daddy gameplay is fun too but it seems very easy to shut baby away from harm if you know where to look. (Put knives and forks on sofa, etc.)

The main ways to die as Baby are either electrocution by outlet or poisoning (Glass works the fastest.) drowning or cooking yourself in the oven takes too long and can be easily stopped by Daddy, which should be made faster.

The game also has technical flaws. Often the countdown before a match starts glitches and I have to alt F4, but it's rare. (I get it often enough I'll complain on the review though.)

The house has alot of rooms locked (WIP stuff not added yet.) so I can't say for the finished state yet. It might be more balanced and fair if more rooms are involved (there's literally no reason to go upstairs currently. Nothing dangerous spawns up there aside from power outlets you can use to zap yourself, and there's no falling damage. There is a funny dildo hidden in the bedroom though.) but it's hard to say. I know the next update adds ventilation shafts and more chores Daddy can do.

Speaking of the chores, they're not easy. Putting all toys away takes a good deal of time and by the time you're distracted doing that Baby often offs himself. Finding the smoke detector battery is iffy. Sometimes I swear it doesn't spawn at all, which might be a glitch. (Or maybe I'm not looking hard enough.) The next update adds more chores and daddy powers. Doing the chores gives you daddy powers like being able to see Baby through walls or see objects through walls, or be invisible to baby.

Now for the main question, is it worth the 5 dollars? Honestly, yes. The game has serious potential to be amazing with more features which are coming soon.


EDIT: It's been a long time since I came back, but here I am! This section was written after the outside update was released.

The game was expanded. Baby's crib was moved to the bedroom, then it was moved to the baby's room which was previously locked upstairs.

New rooms include a garage where you can blow yourself up with a car or set yourself on fire with gasoline, an office room upstairs, a shower upstairs with chemicals for baby to drink or burn in the shower, a laundry room with a washer and dryer for baby to die in, and an outside area with a pool.

They added weapons for the baby and daddy like a taser or a ball blaster, etc. You can now drop the toaster in water and get electrocuted, or blow up a fork in the microwave. New chores include cooking for the baby, washing him, etc.

Family Gathering mode was added, which allows 8 players. You now have achievements and can get hats for doing them.


However, the game is now out of early access. They added music which many people have complained about, but I actually like the music just fine.

I have one major issue. The game wasn't ready for release yet! With the newest update, the game is broken! After Baby kills himself, the game doesn't seem to recognize it. You're forced to spectate until time runs out, and Daddy basically wins by default after time runs out.

Also, some achievements are broken. I swear I've spent an entire match just playing with the piano and the achievement didn't pop.

Is the game still worth the money? Maybe. But the developer needs to get his act together and fix these bugs! How did this pass playtesting? The "Game doesn't recognize Baby dying." bug literally happens every time, and it breaks the game! it's been a few days (At least 3) and the developer didn't release a hotfix or anything!

It shouldn't have left early access yet.

Buy it if you want, but keep in mind in it's current state it still feels early access, and has game breaking bugs.\t\t\t\t\t
