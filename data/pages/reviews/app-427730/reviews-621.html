\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game needs a lot more work to be considered complete. The idea is fun but it's not balanced which is so important for a asymetrical multiplayer game.

EDIT (11/24/2017):
Looking through my reviews I decided to revisit this game. It was in early access and I thought maybe the devs made some major changes to fix the game's balance and general unfinished vibe.
Well, not much has changed. The gameplay is still clunky, the models still look like placeholders, and little has changed gameplay-wise. There are now a few additional gamemodes, however I couldn't try them out. I could barely find a 1 vs 1 game, let alone an 8 v 8 because the game is dead. You might get lucky and find 100 people online, but the matchmaking system must be broken because 9 out of 10 times I still couldn't find a game. I tried searching as both the dad and the baby, but could almost never connect to another player. I think if you want to play this game online you will need to set up a custom game with a friend or spend a lot of time "matchmaking" and not actually playing a match.
I stand by my earlier review. Don't buy this game.\t\t\t\t\t
