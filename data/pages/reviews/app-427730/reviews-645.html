You can play the game as either a dad, or a baby.

If you are the dad, you do your best to prevent the baby from killing itself.
If you are the baby, you do your best to kill yourself.

I honestly only find it fun to be the baby, being the dad is kind of a bummer as it is very very easy for the baby to kill itself, once they learn where they can quickly kill themselvs.

Pros:
Funny
Good for a quick game / time killer

Cons:
some bugs still in the game, as far as i know the game developer is still working on ironing those out.
Repetative music / sounds can get annoying for those of you who are easily annoyed.\t\t\t\t\t
