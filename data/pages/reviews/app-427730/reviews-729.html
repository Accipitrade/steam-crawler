\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst impressions:

The concept alone made me pick up this game. I'm not sure if laughing at it makes me a sick bastard or not, but I was definitely intrigued.

They're not kidding when they say this is early access; it has "proof of concept" written all over it. Still, unlike a lot of early access games, this one can (in my thus-far very short experience) be counted on to work as intended.


Currently, there seems to be a major balance issue. Whatever the cause(s), the game seems heavily in favor of the baby. I've played seven games thus far, three as the baby, four as the dad, and in all seven, the baby won. A few things I think could help:

-The baby needs to move considerably slower. I believe his speed is already slower than the dad's, but it needs to be slower still. There's also a "sprint" button, albeit with limited usage, that makes the baby move impossibly fast.
-The baby might need more health. All it takes is one metal object to a power socket and the game is over immediately.
-The dad needs to be able to carry several items at once. There are a lot of baby-proofing items around, but you can only hold one at a time, and the time spent fiddling with them is time the baby is using. I'd like to be able to go around with electrical socket covers in my pocket, a few cabinet locks hanging from belt loops, with hands free to move the poisonous windex to the top shelves.
-This is a very iffy suggestion, but I'll put it out there anyway: Perhaps allow the dad to pick up the baby. The baby can then respond by wriggling free. The baby might counter this by finding something slippery and covering itself in it, making it impossible to grab. The dad could counter that by hosing the baby down.

I can see this very easily turning into a stealth-based game. The baby, with his small size, has access to routes the dad doesn't, and if the dad can't keep track of the baby, it'll kill itself. In short, the game should play out like this: "So long as the dad and baby are in the same room, it is extremely difficult for the baby to kill itself. But if the baby can shake dad for long enough, it'll win."\t\t\t\t\t
