\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, all of the bad reviews almost stopped me from buying this game. I'm glad I took a leap of faith. The game runs perfectly on my PC and it's never been laggy. It is a ton of fun and there is only one downside. Being the baby is marginally more fun than being the daddy. I've put a lot of thought into how to level the gameplay out and I think that some sort of reward system for winning as the daddy would create a more even game. The real frustration comes from the fact that there are usually a maximum of 100 people online at any given time which makes the gamemaking take forever. Again, this could be fixed by offering a sale or recreating the matchmaking algorithms. I'd really like a way to let the devs know about little bugs and minor frustrations that seem easily fixed.

Now the fun part, it's so simple and so perfect as a multi-player game that I truly believe it will only become more popular. The game is hilarious. The baby controls exactly as it should, the environment is beautiful and the game is addictive. I wish more games like this existed. This is a perfect example of Unity's broad platform. Someone had an amazing idea and now I get to enjoy it. Thanks for making this.\t\t\t\t\t
