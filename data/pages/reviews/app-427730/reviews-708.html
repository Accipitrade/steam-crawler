I would like to see possibly more rooms in the house, but that may be an update for future games considering how many locked doors there currently are. I hope to see more modes that have been mentioned such as one dad and 3 babies, as well as multiple dads and multiple babies. It gets a little repetative after a while, so maybe in future updates, different environments can be created (e.g. outside, in a store,) which may also be fun to have the baby spawn at a random location.
