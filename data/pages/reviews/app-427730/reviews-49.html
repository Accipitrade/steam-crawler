\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWho's Your Daddy?! is the ultimate game for anyone who's ever wanted to experience the joys of parenthood without actually having to change a diaper. This game will have you laughing, crying, and maybe even wetting your pants (but hopefully not the baby's).

The graphics are top-notch, the gameplay is smooth, and the sound effects are spot-on. The controls are easy to master, even for those of us who can barely operate a toaster.

The best part of the game is the tension between the Daddy and the Baby. Will the Daddy be able to keep the Baby alive long enough to win the game? Or will the Baby's constant crying and need for attention drive the Daddy to insanity? Only time will tell.

Overall, Who's Your Daddy?! is a must-play for anyone looking for a good laugh and a dose of realism when it comes to the joys of parenthood. Just don't blame me if you end up wanting to adopt a baby after playing. Trust me, it's not as easy as it looks.\t\t\t\t\t
