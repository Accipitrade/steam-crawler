\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game simply because A. It was cheap, and B. I had seen a few YouTubers playing it, and I thought it would be fun.
In all honesty, it's not that bad of a game. The idea is good, there are plenty of gruesome ways to die, and the graphics are decent. But there are a few issues, some that might be out of the developer's control, but issues notheless.
1. It's imbalanced. It's much, much easier to kill yourself as a baby than keep a baby from dying as a Daddy. You can't even pick the kid up or pull things out of their hands without dropping the item you had first, which is far too buggy and difficult to do. Can you even do that? Daddy is an adult, he should be able to at least decently protect a small child.
2. Nobody plays this game. Nobody. The servers are almost completely empty. And as a person without a lot of online friends plus a busy home/school life, my only option is the vacant servers. The solution to this might be a singleplayer mode, in which you battle against an AI. Or maybe just address the first issue, and this one might just fix itself.
Overall rating: 5.5/10. Has the potential to be something worth buying/playing, but needs some more work.\t\t\t\t\t
