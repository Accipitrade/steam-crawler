\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would like to begin my review by stating that I was inspired to review this game at all because the devs released a new patch. Honestly, when I first got this game, I knew that it was minimal on the features, and I anticipated that I would have to wait a long time for the game to attain any substance. Well, the devs took my five dollars and put it where their mouths are and proceeded to make an asskickingly fun game. The game itself is great with a simple concept, one which has circulated on the internet for a while, but the way that these guys have done it, and the creative ways in which the baby can kill itself, give this game a polished sheen. It is far from complete, but the developers have certainly impressed me with their community outreach, and I commend them for not just taking people's money, going dark for 2 months, then releasing a lackluster patch. They consistently add new content into the game, and the game's expanse is ever-growing. Devs, if you're reading this, keep up the good work; I really appreciate it. As to all you reading who aren't the devs, you should buy this game solely based on how active the team is. In the past 2 or 3 months, they've taken a game where the only ways to kill yourself was to put a fork in a socket, drink bleach, or drown, and turned it into a game where killing yourself is more of an artform than anything. If the basic description of this game caught your interest, then go ahead and buy it. It's only $5, and it may very well be the best $5 you've ever spent.

TL;DR: Good game. Devs are active. Good game. Best game.\t\t\t\t\t
