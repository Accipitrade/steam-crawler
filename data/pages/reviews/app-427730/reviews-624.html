As of today the baby wins and then the baby flies around until the dad wins, WTF? As the dad you have no other option than to watch the baby grab things and murder themselves with no recourse other than blocking things. If there is an option to remove items from the babies hand I haven’t found it, or at least it’s not available on the 360 controller. It seems that more thought was put in for these hats than actual game functionality and intuitive play. The most annoying thing is the interact dot….it’s white…everything is bright and you have to be too exact for the controller to walk up and grab things. I was hoping to play a game with the kids but this is just lousy, I’m convinced they only play because they saw it on YouTube, otherwise they would get frustrated.
