\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is too glitchy to be fun.

As the baby, when outside, the Daddy could hold me on their screen but I was released on mine. I would glitch threw walls and be able to die while still being held by the Daddy. This happened multiple times.

The characters get caught on random objects and corner. The Daddy also feels like he walks oddly and his camera view prospective feels too high.

If you are Daddy, you can hold the baby almost the entire game. And everytime the baby gets loose you can easily pick it right back up. This makes the game very easy to win for the Daddy and the game overall even less fun. This feature should be removed. Or maybe make it so if the baby is being held it easily falls once it hits any of the movement keys. Something along the lines of that.

There are many more problems with the game but these were the big annoyances. This game feels like it is a very early developement game that still needs a lot of fine tuning.

Even if this game is on sale for less than a dollar, I would he hesitant to recommend it even then.\t\t\t\t\t
