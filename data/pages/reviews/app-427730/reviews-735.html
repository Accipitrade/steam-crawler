\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn it's current state, Who's Your Daddy is a little too unbalanced to be as fun as it's capable of being. I definitely think that the role of the father should be a little bit harder than the baby, but as it stands the player controlling the father simply doesn't have enough time to child proof much of anything before the player controlling the baby bee-lines to the batteries and fork in the kitchen and then kills themself almost right away with one electrical outlet or another. I had seen another review suggesting 30 seconds to a minute for the father to baby-proof the house, but I'm not sure I entirely agree (although I think it's absolutely a reasonable suggestion) because during that time the baby would be forced to just sit idly. I think the answer to the imbalance may be in some randomized elements. Instead of all outlets being uncovered, maybe a few random ones could be covered at the beginning of the match. Additionally, I'd really like to see randomized item location and less of them in general, because as I had mentioned earlier the batteries and fork and bleach jug/cleaning supplies are all very close to each other and always in the same place. Other than the frustrating imbalance at the moment, I think this has the potential to be a unique, strategic, and incredibly enjoyable 1v1 game after a couple of tweaks.
(Note: Although my total play time is low, I did play more of the alpha before it had been released to Steam)\t\t\t\t\t
