(Real review below if you actually want)
I see most of the negative reviews coming from people saying that the game is very buggy. While this is true, most of the glitches (mostly seeing through walls for brief moments and clipping through walls,) in my opinion don't really effect the game that much. The seeing through walls glitch only happens for a brief moment and you can still interact with any object. The clipping through walls glitch can be annoying, but it rarely ever happens. Even if it does, it really only happens as dad and will not move you very far allowing you to simply just walk back.

Another thing people don't like about this game is that it "is much easier for the baby to win." While this can be true. If you just play dad more you will quickly get much better at keeping the baby from dying. Also, this really doesn't matter if you are just messing around in a custom lobby, which is, in my opinion, the most fun thing you can do in this game.

Additional Info

Game runs smooth
Fps will rarely ever drop that much
Games are usually found in about 1 minute (May be a bit more if you don't live in North America)
Visuals are good and funny
A lot of customization

If you have any questions or things you want to add, feel free to comment them
