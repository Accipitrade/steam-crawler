\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is genuinely funny, especially when I decided to stop being the actually good father, and just team up with the baby to wreck the house. But, there are issues that I will list, as well as some suggestions to improve the game.


There are definitely clipping issues, especially with the walls, as I have lost several matches due to the locks or the plug covers going through a wall. Hopefully those will be fixed relatively soon.

Lag issues. From time to time, despite having a pretty high-end PC and 75mbps connection, I experiance catastrophic lag whenever I play in a room that I, myself, am not hosting. I am unsure if this is a stability issue, or I have consistantly joined people with high latency. It would be nice to see the latency of each person hosting games, in order to avoid the latter, hopefully the former is being looked in to.

Some items do NOT spawn at all at times, or sometimes spawn behind drawers, where they are unreachable. This makes winning as the father impossible in those situations.

The one tune playing on the radio gets old, pretty quickly. More variety in the music, possibly let people use their own music in it, would be good.

The area, while containing a diverse range of stuff to hide from baby, is kinda small. It would be nice if we had more rooms. Maybe allow for different areas of the house for different playthroughs, I would LOVE to see a laundry room, since the idea of the baby being able to go into a washer or dryer (or both) is hilarious. Maybe even add an ironing board. Trying to keep the baby from danger in a garage, or yard would be fun, too.

Edit: Seems most of those have been implemented. Good joh, dev. Only problem is that there seems to be a glitch in the garage that makes characters move sideways, almost as if I am walking against an invisible wall that is forcing me to strafe. This is the only room that does that.

While the one on one aspect is fun, having only one game mode does get tedious. It would be fun to have other game modes, maybe one where dad has to compete against twins, or mom AND dad, or two dads, have to go against a faster version of the baby. Maybe a survival mode with different rooms in the house steadily unlocking, bringing even more dangers for dad to move out of reach for baby.

Overall, I would recommend playing this game, especially with friends. Though, I would suggest maybe waiting until it is further along in development, so more of the kinks are worked out.\t\t\t\t\t
