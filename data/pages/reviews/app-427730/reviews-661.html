Who's Your Daddy is a fun and random game, very silly to play. Especially in Family Gathering mode. Here's my main review of the game.

First off, here are the pros:
  • You can decide to play the game however you want; but the game reccomends it as a >insert number here< VS >insert number here<. It's still fun that way, but do whatever other things you can think of!
  • The baby and dad are balanced. The baby has shortcuts to areas using air vents, but the dad is faster and can carry large objects and is taller to reach higher objects. This helps to balance the game.
  • Many ways to die for the baby. Death by microwave, oven, toilet, bath, washing machine, dryer, the list goes on. I could list even more if I wanted to.
  • Low price. 5 dollars sure is enough for a very random game like this!

Cons:
  • LOTS of bugs. Yeah. I know, it's still in alpha or something (as of this was written), but hopefully most will be fixed by the time the full game is released.
  • Somewhat innapropriate. (maybe this should be rated T or something, idk.)
Yeah, that's really all to my review of this game. Overall: 8/10, not the best it could be, but still, very good. Well job creating this, hope there's even more when it's released!\t\t\t\t\t
