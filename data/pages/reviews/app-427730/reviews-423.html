\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a long time fan of the game and super excited for the remastered version at the end of the year. If I could voice my thoughts on the main problems with the current version of the game:

1. Knives and forks can spawn in the baby's closet and there's an outlet right outside his room, so when that happens to spawn there it's near impossible to stop.
2. Pets destroy whatever room they're in by throwing everything around. The worst is the dog in the kitchen which poops like crazy, knocks over the trash, and sets up chair for the baby to jump on to the counter. This makes the dad's job pretty hard.
3. If the baby gets the car key the game is over because it's impossible to take it from his hand (you can sometimes get it if he sprints and you pick him up, but he could just not sprint) and if he puts the key in the ignition it falls through the map, so the dad can't take it out. Maybe there's a mechanic with the car that I don't know to stop it from going, but if not you need to be able to take the key from the baby or from the ignition.
4. Parkour with the baby is very fun, but impossible a lot of the time when the dad is around because you need to sprint to knock things like chairs over and you need to sprint to jump from place to place. Maybe make it so he can only pick up the baby when the baby is at <75% stamina (or some other threshold that isn't 100%).

Love the game and thanks if you actually read this!\t\t\t\t\t
