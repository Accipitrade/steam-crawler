\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet's start with the positives. The music in this game is great, and often resulted in a few laughs within our group. (especially the "Daddy's gonna die here" song in the Daddy's Nightmare mode) The game is also obviously not to be taken seriously, and will likely provide some laughs the first few times you play it, especially if you have a group of friends to play with.

That is, assuming you're able to play it properly.

Unfortunately, this game has some game-breaking bugs that pretty much kills off the fun aspect after a few rounds. For example, four of us played last night (two babies + two daddies), and one of the babies could not be picked up or interacted with at all. (neither daddy could interact with them) Basically we were powerless to stop said baby from winning each and every round. As a baby myself, I proceeded to jump into my watery doom in the pool outside, only to find myself in the 2nd floor of the house a moment later, with no explanation as to why. Finally during a round of Daddy's Nightmare, the daddies would randomly win sometimes when clicking on the generator outside, despite it still needing gas. It's true that some of our issues might've been a result of lag, but our connections were all pretty decent.

Game balance is also way off. As other reviews have mentioned, it's ridiculously easy to win as the baby in regular and Family Gathering modes.

Overall, this could've been a good game if the devs had put more work and time into it. As it stands right now, it generally gets stale after about 30-40 minutes of gameplay, and that's assuming the bugs don't drive you into quitting before that.

Interesting game concept, but executed poorly.\t\t\t\t\t
