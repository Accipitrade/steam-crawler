\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's truly unique, to be honest. I've never played a game where the goal, as a baby, is to kill yourself.

The baby truly has the easiest job here, it takes a lucky dad to be able to remove the knife (baby stabs you, slowing you) and all of the edible hazards while keeping an eye on the little bastard.

Honestly, its a truly hilarious play but it gets old very quickly, simply because theres no chat system. In this game, it is almost required you have a friend to play with, because otherwise there's no interaction other than the baby jumping around as you slowly advance as the Dad, desperately trying to feed it a pill, tears rolling down your face as the baby grins in delight as its little body gives in to the Bleach, Soap, Trash, Dishwashing fluid and foam balls it has gorged upon.

This game is fun, and very cheap, but without a friend or a system being implemented to be able to partake in banter with your opponents then the game shall remain a short lived experience, albeit a funny one.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, where to start?
The graphics are nothing to be disapointed with, it is a simple take on what a house and people look like then again last time I looked you don't buy a game with the main objective is to ether save the child or kill yourself and think 'You know what would be good? High def!' So it is perfectly suitable for the game. The animation could be a little better, I noticed that when 'daddy' crouches he just snaps at the waist and instantly becomes half his height but that does not overly bug me.
The game play is good, one on one who can do what the fastest. Baby eats a battery, Daddy feeds a pill to Baby. Baby sits in oven, Daddy turns off oven and turns on the lock. Baby takes knife and heads to the power socket, Daddy ether takes all knives away or puts socket protectors in them. It is rather comical, I played as both and found that good fun can be had with next to no hastle.
The learning curve is nothing daunting, as Baby your 'job' is to be a hazard, you need to drink poison *soap, window cleaner, bleach* you need to eat bad things *raw meat, batteries, trash* you need to get into bad places *in the bath, in the oven* you need to put things in sockets *Knife or fork* and lastly you have the hammer to break the glass. As Daddy you not only have to keep Baby safe from that by detoxing Baby with Pills but you also have to stop it from happening again by locking cabinets, placing dangerous things up out of reach, putting in socket protectors etc. Finally there is a reward, if you put all of Baby's toys away you gain ESP and can see Baby's health and location through walls, I never earnt this but it is infinitly useful.
The price is completely worth it, I certainly feel that when I bought this game I have managed to get a game that can be played over and over without getting dull.
Finally, would I ask my friends to get this game? Yes, yes I would.\t\t\t\t\t
