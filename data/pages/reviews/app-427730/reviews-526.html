-Fun to mess around with friends
-Isn't demanding on your pc
-Not very serious and fun to relax while playing
-Buggy (adds for hilarious things)

Cons:
-Buggy (can be game breaking at some points)
-No depth to the gameplay
-Kinda need friends to play with otherwise it is boring
