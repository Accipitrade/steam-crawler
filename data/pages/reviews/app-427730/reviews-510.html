\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is an excellent simulation of being a single father.

As a single father I can't praise this game enough for its accuracy in the portrayal of raising kids.

I'm not joking this is literally what raising kids is like. All they try to do is find new ways to unintentionally kill themselves and you spend every waking moment trying to corall them and stop them from killing themselves.

Don't believe me? Just wait 'till you have kids. Then you'll wish you were just playing this game.

This isn't a huge game, but it's definitely a fun one. The goal of the game is to either kill youself as the baby or stop your little ♥♥♥♥ of a kid from killing himself as the father. There can be multiple fathers and multiple babies making the game not completely impossible. Raising twins I can attest to how impossible it is to look after more children than there are of you. *Whisper* they used to be triplets*

The babies have the more fun job as they wander around the yard and the house trying to find new and intricate ways to commit suicide. It can be something as simple as a knife in an electical socket or super complicated like driving the car through the garage and watching as it explodes. It's fun to use your imagination and find new ways to end yourself. As far as I can tell if you think it'll kill you then it will. The devs put a lot of thought and content into the game so there are a multitude of ways to kill yourself. As a bonus, when you find a new way to kill yourself you can get something to customize your baby or daddy with encouraging you to find weird ways to die and not just rush for the electrical socket LIKE SOME PEOPLE WHO ONLY CARE ABOUT WINNING I'm looking at you Jeremy!

Being the daddy isn't boring, like real life, it just isn't as fun as being the baby. Even so, there is a sick sense of fun that comes from watching someone trying to kill themselves and standing over them constantly stopping them. To win as the daddy you have to stop the babies from dying, but thankfully you only have to make sure that they all don't die, just like real life. You have a couple of different tools to stop them like outleg plugs, cabinet locks, and magic pills that cure all types of chemical poisoning. Also floaties for the sneaky babies that try to desparately throw themselves in the pool.

The game is pretty well balanced with multiple ways for the babies to get away from the daddies like crawling throught he vents and the daddies to prevent the babies from getting away like child restraints and the aforementioned cabinet locks.

This is a game that's better played with friends especially with voice chat which will lead to many quoteable moments, but playing alone is a bit underwhelming. The game is definitley enhanced by playing with your friends.

Eventually the game does get a bit repetitive and boring after only a few hours even with your friends. There are only so many ways to kill yourself and you eventually dread being the daddy.

All same the game's only five bucks so you can't really ask for much more. Five bucks gave me five hours of entertainment which is 495 dollars cheaper than a hooker and 55 dollars cheaper than 2 movies with snacks.

...

...

It gets an 8/10 It's worth the asking price. If you have friends go buy it. If you don't have friends... cry yourself to sleep like the rest of us.\t\t\t\t\t
