\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI discovered this VN completely by accident, and I am now officially and proudly a dedicated fan!

Things I Loved:

- The writing is extremely entertaining, and the dialogue is GOLD (great tension!) with lots of hilarious and "right on" moments that had that me chuckling at many points of the story

- The MC is a spunky lady, I loved reading her internal dialogue and enjoyed her horny outlook on many situations, and also the fact that she's not taking herself too seriously

- I LOVED all the boys and their charact–µrisation (well written, authentic, and complex), how different they are and the dynamics between them, and the harem fan service was obviously some pure JOY

- Absolutely loved the art, each character has several poses! Quite a good number of CG which were made with love. The backgrounds are another gem in this VN, and they are WORKS OF ART all hand drawn (at least I think they are, they don't have that 3D feel you see in a lot of VNs)

- The romance route I've played so far turned out to be quite intriguing, dark and also very elaborate! It's been a while that I found myself staying up late at night trying to get to the bottom of it all. wow. just WOW.

Things I wanted the entire time I was playing:

- To slap Vincent and make that boy BEHAVE, and then pin him to the wall and have my way with him.
- The NSFW!!! If it ever comes out I'm purchasing it with my eyes closed!
- The voice over! This VN would be EXTRA PERFECT with some sweet VO

To sum everything up this VN has VERY high entertainment value for the money.

Recommend for anyone looking for some fun, dark and complex story with gorgeous boys, amazing aesthetics, awesome romance and rich dialogue! I will be eagerly awaiting the next installment!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am so glad I gave this game a chance! I'm officially in love with Degraman! The story shocked me! It was so fascinating to me, it seemed drastically different from what I used to play. I bought the DLC and it was worth every coin. If you like dark, story-rich, twisted plots full of mystery and romance then I'm sure you you'll enjoy Degraman as much as I did)

It took me over a week to go through all the endings without a walkthrough, and I can confidently say that this was one of the most interesting otomes I've played. Reach at least few endings and you'll understand that this game has nothing in common with stereotypical harem stories... It's actually anything but ordinary! Just don't jump to hasty conclusions because everything isn't as simple as it may seem at the beginning of the story.

This is a game with multiple choices and consequences. Playing a good/naive character differs greatly from playing an evil/feisty one... So keep in mind that your choices do actually matter.

Pros:
+ Very story-rich
+ Many choices and consequences
+ Beautiful character design and fabulous CGs
+ A very large quantity of CGs
+ Unusual character development
+ Amazing soundtracks
+ Many hours of gameplay
+ Customizable mc in the DLC version which affects the CGs
+ Dark atmosphere
+ Multiple endings(52 or 53 endings)
+ DLC has a number of routes where you get to know/flirt with other characters that will be LIs in future Acts of Degraman.

Cons:
- It's going to be really difficult if you decide to complete all the 52 endings without a walkthrough

But I highly recommend completing all the endings to unlock the true ending! And I can't wait to play the upcoming Acts with other love interests.

p.s. read carefully the warnings because this game is not for everyone!!\t\t\t\t\t
