A very tight entry into the 2D Retro pixel styled Action Roguelite Bullet Hell genre, with an active developer making the EA aspect appealing for a change

Circle strafing like an insane person used to entertain me no end when this genre opened up. The popularity of the likes of Vampire Survivors confuses me though due to the insanely repetitive and cloned nature of all the titles on offer. Finally though, we have a true contender to the throne which in my opinion was held by Crimsonland until now.

The demo release during a recent Steam Festival had me hooked.

Using a clever integration of a tower defence aspect, one protects a convoy truck while using your XP upgrades and money from said truck to improve both your player character and your 4 towers lining the deadly passage these brave (mad?) truck drivers need to endure.
  • The action is clean and satisfying.
  • The Rogulite upgrade aspect is clear and has meaningful and immediately effective choices
  • Graphics style and sounds/music aesthetics are unique and quite lovely
  • Frustration vs Reward is a good balance with that very moreish sensation, just...one...more.
  • Works well as a pickup and go game
  • Early Access developer actively pays attention to and discusses suggestions.
A fun and easily enjoyable addition to your Library, EA affordability make this an unavoidable choice.
