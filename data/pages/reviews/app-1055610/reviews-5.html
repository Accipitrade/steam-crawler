\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts a great game, and cheap to boot for what it offers!
You're going to play a crew member on a capital ship, and fight against enemy capital ship to take it down. You do this by decreasing the health bars which represent different parts of the ship - bridge, docking bay, etc.

Before the battle, each team equips their capital ship with various turrets and comm dishes (they give you data about enemy capital), purchase attack fighters for your hangars (light, heavy, interceptor, bomber, etc), and capital perk upgrades. Then you're thrown into the battle.
You have multiple roles, each with its own special ability. For example, engineer can repair doors and place a teleporter. Soldier can fire an insta-kill rocket. Basic crew grunts get extra resources.

Your job is to either help keep your ship alive, or help bring the other ship down, by shooting down hangar doors, turrets, repair drones, or eventually boarding it, then sabogating engines and generators in it.

There are also frigates, a mid-sized ships (between capital and fighters), and you can steal opponent's frigate. There is a mission type with a space station, which you can occupy to gain extra resources - which are used to replenish destroyed fighters and other things.

Lastly, the real joy comes from cooperation - for example, to fire torpedoes - someone has to actually physically go into the torpedo bay, pickup a torpedo and load it into the launch tube, before the pilot can fire it. If engines are damaged, you have to pull different levers in specific order to shut everything down, before you repair engine and reboot everything back. Of course, you're a sitting duck during that time.

Options and strategy are immense, it is honestly insane this game costs just 12$. I would expect a game like this to cost maybe 20-24$, even if there's some polish it could see.


There is only one negative thing about it.... playerbase is really, really, really small. I've never played a match with more than 3 people in it, and this game would _shine_ in a proper 12 people match.

Here's to hope there'll be more people joining - get this game. Even offline with bots, its fun enough.\t\t\t\t\t
