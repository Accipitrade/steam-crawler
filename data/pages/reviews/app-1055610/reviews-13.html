\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDeep Space Battle Simulator is about managing a spaceship in combat. You have have a lot of options to play with: drones, cannons and you even have the possibility to board the enemy ship. For a simulator this is amazing and made me enjoy a genre I always found too boring in the past. There are tons of ship customization possibilities, which modify what kind of combat you perform: close quarters to using a freaking laser.

It being in Early Access means it's a little rough, but there are updates every day and it seems that the dev will continue that trend for a long time. The biggest plus of this is that there are no game-breaking bugs, although there are some minor bugs, especially with the UI and some visual effects.

The music can get a little tedious, and the sound effects are repetitive or too simple, or even non-existent sometimes. Although the AI is great for first playthroughs, the fact that it doesn't adapt to what you do makes it kinda easy to figure out a way to defeat it every time, especially after a few hours of gameplay.

As of the time of the review, the developer has added a class system. I haven't fully tried it yet but will update once I do. But it looks promising, and will definitely make role distribution a lot easier.

Being of South America makes browsing servers a little difficult but that's always been the case so I have no problems with it. Just a minor warning. Also achievement didn't work for me but that may be a local issue.

Do I recommend this game? Of course. If you like space ship combat simulators that is. If you've never tried anything like this before, but you enjoy RTA or even strategy games, I recommend you give this a try. Playing solo will keep you busy, but that also means that boarding the enemy ship is difficult and requires a lot of thinking.\t\t\t\t\t
