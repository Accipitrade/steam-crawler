Crew based 6 vs 6 space combat
This game really should drop the "Simulator" word from it's name. It's not really a simulation. You play with a crew and each member of the crew can choose their own class. You have classes like Ranger which are set up for PVP. Each class has it's own perks. So far I have enjoyed playing as a Pilot, where you can take fighter ships out and engage in various types of action. Pilots have a perk of cheaper fighter ship purchase. There are classes like Engineer, which must fix various issues going on with the ship.

The crew aspect reminds me a little of Blackwake, instead of pirates we get spaceships.

Ship customization
The game gives you a budget to customize your ship at the start of every round. You can place turrets, communication towers and other things. You can buy droids, drones and other perks for your ship as well. This is all to customize your experience and tailor to your gameplay style.

Full blown spaceship foot invasion
You can land on the opposing enemies mothership by blowing the shields to the entrance. You can then proceed to wreck Havoc on the ship. Destroying the generator, the engine, setting fire to things and generally causing problems. Unless the security defenses on board find you and kill you. The game can also turn into a full blown first person shooter PVP experience in this situation as well. Players are invading your mother ship. You have guns. What are you going to do about it?

There is a lot to this little game that I just bought for $5.59. Great deal. Great fun. This is a good early access game with a ton of potential.\t\t\t\t\t
