\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet's get one thing clear immediately. The game is very much in early access. There could be a lot added, and improved. Despite that, the experience I had was still enjoyable especially since I'm very much into simulators and games set in space. I have always wanted a game like this, where people work together on a spaceship, controlling different areas of the ship, as well as smaller fighters. It even lets you board the enemy to deal some damage from the inside, something I was looking for in Guns of Icarus, a game where you also controlled a ship with other people (although the gameplay mechanics themselves differ very much, and that one is set in a steampunk setting). The developer is also very active in Discord, and replies to his growing community. That right there is the most important part for me as many early access games have a good start but fail due to developers not listening to feedback. That's why although right now the game is a bit bare bones, it's safe to say that I'm very excited for what this game will be in just a few months. Set your expectations right for this game in early access, and know the genre you're getting into, and you'll quickly learn to love what this game has to offer.

+bonus for a very nice community\t\t\t\t\t
