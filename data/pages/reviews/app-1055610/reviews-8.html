\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat multiplayer game!!!

Here are some details: There can be 6 people on each ship, so 12 players total in PVP matches, or just 6 people in one co-op session vs the AI. The graphics do what they are suppose to do: Be there and convey all the actions going on. There will be times where it looks like to super ships battling it out with all the laser fire going on. That is about it with graphics. The game has pretty in-depth combat with variety of strats you can use to win against both computers and players. Want to focus on defense and getting your pilot ships to do damage? Dooooo it! Want to focus on moving your Capital Ship in for some crazy maneuvering with Gauss Cannons/Laser weapons and the super laser - Go for it. Want to break open a hangar and steal enemy ships or blow up some systems/people? Viable and fun.

Variety is key and this game has a decent amount of it: You get to choose what class to play, what items to buy for your ship, and you can customize enough of your capital ship (such as placement of turrets and what type of pilot ships to have in the hangars) that it never feels stale and can match your team's play style. My favorite strat is messing with their engines via internally invading their ship, then getting my teammates to blow up sections of the enemy's capital ship Death-Star-Style.

That said, it has minor kinks that need to be worked out. The AI is pretty good in co-op, though rarely there will be times when the AI is about to win and then suddenly it can't decide what section of our capital ship to attack next and stalls. First person Combat can be a bit clunky at times, though it is workable and fun to a degree (On the other side of the coin - the ship combat is pretty stellar). The Key system to unlock doors on enemy ships is great, but there will be times where I only find one key card and I will never see the rest for the entire game, limiting the options on attacking the enemy's capital ship internally.

Overall, if you were looking for the next game to get a lot of people involved or create that small LAN party experience, this is the game. There is still more I haven't said about this game, but it would be best to buy the game and figure it out yourself! Have a great time blowing up ships! :-D\t\t\t\t\t
