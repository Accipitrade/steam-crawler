\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really wanted to like this game but it suffers from some major flaws, the big one being that the only times I played there was one online server but no people. No one plays online indie game unfortunately so unless you have a lot of friends willing to play you’re going to be playing a very clunky single player.

The Good
(+) Should run on most computers.
(+) Game is as presented as shown in previews with actual game play footage.
(+) All achievements can be obtained in single player.

The Meh
(/) Controls feel stiff and clunky when moving your crew member outside of controlling a ship or fighter.
(/) On capital ships you can assign AI bots to various systems, but I never actually saw them doing much – not even firing back at the enemy.
(/) Never got to see what loading torpedo was like since some game features are restricted to having multiple people in your game.

The Bad
(-) No online community. Unless you have friends that play it is a single player experience.
(-) Capital ship combat is slow, boring, and clunky. Too many time I saw the enemy moving faster than I ever could. Also, should not have to rotate a ship so much in all directions just to line up most your guns on the enemy.
(-) Tutorial does not really give clear directions or a proper chance to learn the controls, learn class rotations and abilities, or give instructions on ship functions.
(-) Fighters say they have homing missiles but they need to lock on – meaning onto enemy fighters not parts of the enemy ship. Not fair. Also, some ships have special abilities as indicated in the fighters interface, but clicking said button does nothing at least not that I could tell.
(-) Clustered UI – too many things going on at once and not enough learning curve to get used to it.

Wanted to like it but just didn’t. So sad. Maybe in time we’ll get something better.\t\t\t\t\t
