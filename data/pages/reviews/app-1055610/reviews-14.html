\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs this game is in Early Access, I will review it as a early access game.
Some opening notes, its somewhat bare bones and it sounds like the audio is put through a text to speech, however for an Early Access game, this is pretty damn good.

Things to be kept in mind:
From what I see right now, this is being developed by one person, or at least very few people.
Its a brand new game without much marketing, meaning it'll have a low consistent player base.
At the time of writing this review, I had done one round.

Seeing from the discord, the game began having updates by May 20th, 2019, meaning its been around a little while.

THE GOOD:
Massive variety of weapons, upgrades, and aircraft.
Active, Passionate developer
Decent Graphics
Customizable energy dispersal.
Want to shout full power to the deflector shields, and then actually divert more power to the shields and make them
stronger? You can do that! Want to charge up all laser cannons in order to prepare for a huge burst? You can do that!
Low Cost

THE BAD:
Not necessarily bad, but in order to find a match, you should have one arranged on the discord, as you will likely not find anyone on the servers.

Some bugs.

THE UGLY:
Repetitive voice lines which are text to speech.
If fighting the AI (on easy at least), they do not move their own ship which makes t hem extremely easy to kill.
Bare bones game, not much audio for anything besides TtS and laser fire.



Is it worth it?
Yes, I believe it's worth it. Not only are you helping fund the game, its already made considerable progress for its current state.
With a larger community, this game will only get better, as that is what it depends on.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDeep Space Battle Simulator
==============================

Deep Space Battle Simulator is a cool Team ship battling game, When I played there wasn't many people on so its a little rougher with just AI or a couple players, but with a full lobby this game looks like it can be an awesome game, the raiding enemy ships and destroying their ship from the inside is cool, Battling Robots, Very nice, I think the one thing I would work on is flight controls, The 3D shooter part is good, but wasn't a fan of the flight controls personally, Overall, I think this game has a lot of potential to be something spectacular. I would pick it up now while its still in Early Access, as the Dev plans on raising the price when the game is fully done.
I would Definitely reccomend this game if you like space games, and team battle games. Look forward to what the Dev does to improve it.
Will hop on discord and hope to catch a full lobby soon!


Postives:
+ Good idea at a team battle game
+ Decent gameplay
+ Decent amount of stuff to mess around with
+ Walking Controls are decent
+ Interesting Art Style
+ Battling Robots
+ Raiding Ships
+ Key Cards are Placed elsewhere when you die, making it more of a challenge.
+ Hydroponics Room lol
+ Was able to Teabag a Guy getting a Cat scan in the tutorial

Neutral:
= Hard to kill AI When playing alone
= Tutorial
= UI is ok but could be better

A couple Minor Negatives:
- Couldn't get my controller to work with it
- Wasn't a fan of the flying controls
-Entering ship through hanger, kept trying to fly in it, didnt realize I had to press a button for a while. (I realize it was on screen now but didnt catch my eye right away)

Suggestions:
+Make Flight controls seem more natural
+More Space races beside robots?
+More Guns
+Adjust AI Difficulty down when 2 or less players.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDeep Space Battle Simulator is an interesting game. At first glance, you simply think it is a game where you control a ship around. Then you get into the game and realize that you can also fly spaceships into the enemy ships and sabotage the enemy ship by turning off the turrets or destroying the generator. You think everything is going well until an absolute unit of a robot walks through the door and slaps you back to the medical bay in your ship.

Don't get me wrong, this is a very new game and has a lot of work do still, but that is the best part. Seeing the polished graphics and good controls make me believe that this game will come a long way.

Unfortunately, I have not been able to play this game with my friends, but I think it would definitely be a good time.

I would recommend anyone that likes these kinds of co-op games (especially if you have other friends that are interested). The gameplay and pacing still need work, but that is reasonable to expect from such a new game.

Overall kudos to the devs for an interesting and already polished experience
