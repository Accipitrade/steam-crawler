For me there are too many frustrating aspects to enjoy it at the moment, I'll come back in a year or two, hoping it will still be in development then and not abandonware.
The most prominent issues that I encountered were:
- in fighters you don't have visibility of enemies outside of main view, so you're constantly rotating around yourself looking for enemies while they're shooting at you. Of course, the very second you finally spot that enemy interceptor he's flying over your head and gone again.
- tutorial asks you to destroy communications array on two enemy ships. The ships are star destroyer sized. the communications array is the size of a phone booth. It is not highlighted or pointed out in any way. Have fun.
- you can hack enemy ships so their hangar doors open and you can get inside. Once inside you need key cards to open any relevant door. You move with the speed of a lame turtle, or you can put your gun away and move slightly faster. Good luck finding a key card. One the star destroyer sized ship.
