\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDeep Space Battle Simulator is exactly what its name suggests as a simulator of space battles. Players divided into two teams of a maximum of 5 people play as crew members of a space ship trying to destroy each other. Before each battle, we configure our ship by selecting its weaponry (5 types of turrets) fighters in hangars (also 5 types) and special options such as reinforced armor, faster engine or fire fighting droid. Already at this stage you can see a large variety of strategies to choose from. Do you prefer to keep your distance from your opponent? Take single lasers, their firepower is not staggering but all other types of weapons are out of reach. Do you prefer close combat? Reinforced armor and dual lasers are what you need.

At the moment Deep Space Battle Simulator is a very promising title. The gameplay is intuitive, we have a lot of strategies to beat the opponent, and the space battles are dynamic. In a group of several friends, even fight against artificial intelligence is exciting. The initial weapon selection only creates strategies - a certain framework for our capabilities when the battle comes. During the battle, the number of tactics increases as the situation develops. The simplest tactic is manning the bridge and turrets, however, boarding with a fighter jet and sabotage of enemy engines can also have good effects. Of course, the opponent is not idle, it is worth having an efficient mechanic or a fire-fighting droid on the crew. A ship without a functioning engine and shield generators is an easy target for the enemy. It is extremely important to control the energy distribution of your ship, strengthen the shield section under fire or divert energy to the weaponry.

Already at this stage of development, Deep Space Battle Simulator can pull you in, but it is worth pointing out some shortcomings of the current version. In addition to the occasional fairly corrected errors by the creator, the biggest disadvantage of early access is currently only one ship and one "arena" of the battle. While the same "background" of each duel is not a big problem, a larger selection of ships would significantly increase the replayability of the game. It is a pity that getting on a fighter jet is limited only to the first-person view, which makes maneuvers difficult.

The advantage of the game is undoubtedly the low price of only 25.49 PLN, but it is worth checking in advance whether we will persuade several friends to play, because the current player base is not impressive.

Unfortunately, at the moment, unlike, for example, Star Trek Bridge Crew or Icarus Starship Command Simulator, we do not have in this title either a free game mode with random events or campaigns, and the gameplay focuses exclusively on battles (though epic cast) of spaceships.
