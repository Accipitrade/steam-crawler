\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm no expert. Let me say that first. I enjoyed this game, but it didn't have quite what I was looking for. It's wholesome but it feels very hollow. 5 in game days to choose a waifu. You get exactly one date with each girl and how you respond on each date determines your ending. That's it. There's no real relationship building or character development past the introductory stuff. The characters in every ending feel like they didn't learn or grow at all. Each character was effectively a one-note with a cute girl visual design tacked on. If your'e new to VNs and just want something very light then it's good (and I do mean VERY light) but it's not really for someone looking for something more intricate or involved. I was hoping it'd be slower and have some build up but there's none. It's just a straight shot to the end and the "correct" choice for each girl is incredibly obvious.

But I paid 2.50 for it and I got about 2.50's worth of game so I'm not complaining. Just know what you're getting into. I wouldn't recommend this at the full 5 dollar price though.

Quick edit: The "Sexual content" tag is a bit of a lie. It's nothing more than ecchi. There's nothing exposed. So if you're looking for porn, this ain't it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMinor spoilers ahead:
So I bought this game without really any expectations. But I was kinda blown out the window. So heres a little review:

Story:
The story is nicely written for the length of the game. It is nothing extremely amazing but its nice as a backdrop for the characters.
7/10
Characters:
This is in my oppinion the very best things about this game. Even though you only get very little time to know each of the girls, each of them is so nicely written in their own personal way. I was actually torn when I had to choose one of them and when the game did it FOR ME I was shocked and genuenly sad for the other two. I played to every ending and of course I have a favourite but all of them are nicely done! Good job to the writer here!!
The only complaint I have, is that the side characters are never visualized. I personally wouldve loved to see other Monstergirls as well, not only the three we know. But thats just a minor complaint ^^
9/10
Gameplay:
Well, there is nothing much to say about this. Its a standard visual novel. But the UI is very clean and fits the style of the game. The music is well done and fits the tone of the story and the game at variuos points in the game. Over all it seems very well polished and done with love.
8/10
Summary:
In general this is a very nice visual novel for people like me, who like anime girls in more... extra shapes c:
They are all very cute and the art is... good, if you know what I mean.
This game might not be worth the full price of 4Euro because of its quite short lenght and limited replayability but if its on sale, I would defenetly grab it!!
8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
