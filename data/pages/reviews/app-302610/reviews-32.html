\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA brilliant, fun and simple game which takes up alot of your time......alot....

I downloaded the demo weeks ago and found myself constantly replaying the 6 levels availible for hours.So when i picked up the full game i was spoiled with even more levels to play. This platformer's unique art style is great as the geometry looks simple but is far from it. As the game progresses the levels get faster,more detailed and more complicated. My mind was never at ease YET it was relaxing to constantly die, death after death, and continue playing. (The menu is nice and quick if you are easily frustrated and need to rage quit quick. I did this constantly but i kept re-opening the game because it was to addictive to stop.) The soundtrack complements the game well with it dynamically changing to the levels style.

Should you buy this game? Yes.
I got incredibly lucky and got it on sale for just 75 cents.... 75 cents for a game you spend hours on, with great replayability, especially because of its competitve leaderboards. Its Normal price is 3 euro but even then, its a bargain. If the game devolpers objective was to create a simple,classic and fun platforming game, it has done its job rather well.
~4/5
