\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI remember playing this game on and off during my university studies 8 years ago back when it was just a flash game...I really sucked back then. Still, it is a quick and simple game that you can play whenever you feel like daydreaming, or perhaps just testing your mental focus. It provided the perfect 5 or 10 minute break I needed whenever I was studying for long periods of time, whether it be for exams or even while doing some assignments.

The levels start off simple and get progressively harder and they can certainly be quite frustrating at times. This is the type of game where you'll play for 5 minutes, rage quit and then come back to it an hour later.

A lot of people say it's just about memorisation, where they believe it is more about mastering each randomly generated reused part of the level and that it basically doesn't require sharp reflexes or quick thinking...Well I can tell you that it absolutely does require them. You'll even begin to appreciate the fact that the levels unfold in randomly generated but consistent segments, as you may be fortunate to reach 100% without facing too many difficult segments...You'll know what I mean when you start to play the game.

I'm happy with the music and graphics experience. I would definitely recommend it at the price of $4.50, even though I got it on special at $2.25. Now that I've beaten the game (I have 18/18 achievements), I know that I'll still come back to it time and time again as something quick to play where I don't have to invest much time or effort.\t\t\t\t\t
