\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA challenging and fast-aced runner with a gradually ramping up level of intensity, heightened by music and an increasing sense of speed. Despite looking fairly different and having slightly different gameplay, it feels reminiscent of Super Hexagon due to the fast turns and quick moves you need to make to not meet a quick end, coupled with an easy to restart nature that allows for many consecutive runs in short succession. Unlike Super Hexagon, which relies on a combination of visuals and music to disorient the player, Boson X focuses more specifically on fast animation and changing colors to make the game feel faster and harder, which is arguably easier to play for longer without a break, but still provides a hard challenge. The levels are decently varied and introduce new elements fairly often to keep things interesting, as you try to break old records and eventually learn the tricks of each level to beat them. A fun and frustrating game for anyone who likes a feeling of speed and intensity.\t\t\t\t\t
