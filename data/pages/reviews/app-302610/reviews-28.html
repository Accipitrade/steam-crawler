There are three buttons involved (jump left, jump right, and jump), and online leaderboards. That's it. No gimmicks. No 'XP'. No collectables. Just you, three buttons, eighteen increasingly difficult levels, and a few thousand other people who are better than you at all of them. Sure, the music is ♥♥♥♥. Sure, the graphics are nothing special. Sure, the stages do start to get a bit artificially difficult towards the end (the Dark X Boson, where everything is in wireframe to make it deliberately difficult to tell what's going on is the worst offender here).

But Boson X is an absolute joy to play, and in my book, that's all that counts.\t\t\t\t\t
