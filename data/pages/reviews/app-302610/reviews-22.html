\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoson X is a relatively interesting concept on building a simple yet fast-paced endless runner. Sadly the game suffers from poor pacing, a painfully gaudy color palette , a very steep difficulty curve and somewhat badly designed levels that will frustrate Boson X players more than challenge them. While the game states that you need quick reflexes to succeed, you will find yourself relying blind luck (and a fair bit of patience) to hit that '100% discovered' meter. The only real reason I am positively recommending this game is that it does offer a fair amount of value (in terms of gameplay) for what it costs to buy. Also, there is no 'Average' rating.

P.S. If you or anyone near you will be playing with suffer from photo sensitivity, there's a very real chance of triggering a seizure as there's a lot of flashing lights and fast moving objects.
