\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDepending on how good you are, this game could last only ~4 hours. To beat everything, it took me ~5 hours.

The game itself is like 12MB. Must be the cel-shading, but it's incredibly compressed. Runs quite smoothly, music is great, and level design remains interesting throughout. Not overly drawn out, and getting the jist of each level as you run through it feels great. Simple but very fluid mechanics make for a very addicting experience. For it's price, it's a lot of fun.

When you have an incredibly intense run, you'll feel like an absolute badass. I love that feeling. Recommended, it's a quick, well-executed, arcade-like experience that is quite difficult, but not impossible. It's not cheap difficulty, when you lose, you know you made a mistake, not that you were cheated out by RNG or something. (RNG can occasionally make your run impossible to complete though if you are in the wrong place at the wrong time.)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a very lighthearted free-running type of game very similar to something like Subway Surfers. There are 18 levels here of varying difficulty; from Normal Geon to Anti-Boson X. I have spent 4 hours collecting achievements (beating the game) and the rest of my time shooting for top 1000 (then 500) scores on each level.

I played the free version before this, but the Dark and Anti versions of each particle were a pleasant new feature. They are NOT retools or reskins of one another. Each stage usually retains the same core structure (gravitron likes moving/falling platforms, Y boson is a big fan of electric beams, etc.) but otherwise each level is fresh and unique.

Players with slow reaction times WILL have trouble with the later levels. This game gets pretty darn rediculous, especially once you take on the Anti particles. Anti Gravitron had me stuck for a good 30 minutes (1/8 of the time I was playing).

This is a very good game in my opinion. While it may not be a genre-definer like Super Meat Boy or a masterfully crafted experience like Limbo, it's nice for good quick fun, a coffee break or a reflex test.\t\t\t\t\t
