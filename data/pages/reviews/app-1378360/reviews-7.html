\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game but has some problems. Pathfinding of the AI needs serious work since enemies are frequently too stupid to "understand" that they have to go through a door to get to me and not through a solid wall. At least on higher difficulties (haven't tried lower) enemies are bullet-spongy and ammo pickups are scant (at least in the early levels), which is a really bad combo. Boomer shooters like this should be made tougher mostly by increasing the enemy count, not by forcing you to resort to your melee weapon. Also, it seems certain environment changes don't get saved. For instance if I break a computer screen or explode a prisoner body, save the game, and then load it, the computer screen is no longer broken and the prisoner body is back.

There's some weird lack of consistency between the knife animation and the crosshair placement. I need to get the crosshair over an object to break it with my knife, but when this is happening, the knife stabbing animation is far below the crosshair, so it looks like I'm not stabbing the object but am somehow breaking it anyway.

The game seems to lack any sort of autoaim, unlike, say, DOOM 1 and 2. So sometimes shots miss enemies in a way that feels annoying.

Those problems aren't enough to stop the game from being fun though. Overall it is a solid old-school shooter. The amount of content is pretty good for just entering early access too.\t\t\t\t\t
