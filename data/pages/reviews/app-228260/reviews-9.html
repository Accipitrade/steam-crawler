\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs is becoming a tradition with these negative reviews, I do actually like this game. I put over 10 hours in in one sitting across 2 campaigns. The first one as a pregenned faction cause I just wanted to jump in. Set up my first city, found out research is split into 3 categories which govern your cities, unit, and spells/magic units respectively. So in times of peace or when you vastly overpower the other factions you can focus on increasing your output for example, while if you are constantly being pressured you can ignore grain farming to get stronger units to set up a buffer. One of the better research systems ive run into honestly, no longer do you need to understand pottery to get arrows. So im plugging along get into a few wars which go nowhere cause the enemy is 3 empires away and I dont have open borders with anyone. No units ever show up to my doorstep so its just kind of saber rattling. Game crashes. Game crashes every time i try to load either auto save. Game DOES NOT crash when I load my manual save from about 2 hours ago. Game crashes 5 turns into that save. Manual save is also now corrupt and wont load.

Oh well fluke right? And anyway now that I have the hang of things I wanted to try my hand at making a custom faction anyway. Its simple with a bit more robustness than the custom unit generator. I make a race of old time mercenaries. They infight a lot but selling themselves to anyone who needs soldiers gets them information from far and wide so a science bonus and they are the best of the best so better rewards on quests. Cool. Put another 4 or 5 hours into a game. CTD. Again both autosaves corrupt. Dont even bother loading up my previous manual save cause yeah.

Games a fantastic cross between an RPG and a 4X. Almost feels like a sandbox campaign you might run in pathfinder with the kingmaker rule sets for managing a city. I mean that in the least sweaty basement nerd way possible. The game is fun, the tactical battles are fun, the game is surprisingly pretty for its age. But speed run it if possible. It doesn't handle long games well. At some point I think theres just too much stuff going on and it just fails. And as far as I can tell its been abandoned by the devs.\t\t\t\t\t
