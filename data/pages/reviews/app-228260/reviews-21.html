Custom factions, custom sovereigns, custom cities, custom units, and the appearance of everything updates to reflect these customizations.

Bought this two years ago and I've been kicking myself for not installing it sooner.

Addendum: I have come across a couple issues since I've started.

I've had a couple soft-locks so far. I was able to kill the process in Task Manager and restart without any problems. I do recommend setting Auto-Save to every turn, just in case.

Sometimes a city's area of influence does not render. This is apparently a known issue that was never fixed. Work-around is to exit the game entirely and restart.

And your units/armies do not block opposing forces. So your border guards do nothing as enemy mounted groups charge through to ransack your farms and mines. A bit annoying, that.

Still recommend the game though. And I'm still considering buying the DLC.\t\t\t\t\t
