\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlthough this game is starting to show its age and is 32 bit, I feel that this was a well designed game that corrected many of the previous flaws of the previous Elemental series.

The heroes system is very interesting and there is a surprising amount of depth to it. Each class has its own unique tree that can come in handy.

I especially liked the unit customization and the ability to add equipment to my troops as I saw fit. Some troops needed certain equipment that suited a particular situation.

There is also a large and pretty decent modding community. Many of these mods can add a lot of depth and make the game at times, feel like a new expansion was added.

I hope that a full on successor bigger than Sorcerer King is made.\t\t\t\t\t
