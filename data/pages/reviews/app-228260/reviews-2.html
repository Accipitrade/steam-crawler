\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy the original Fallen Enchantress although it has some slight bugginess (notable highlighting and water disappear sometimes on reloading). This version, however, is hot trash.

The game worked just long enough for me to play it once for a couple hours... and now crashes completely 80% on start up or load. So basically, it lasted just long enough to not allow refunds. I've spent a few hours trying figure out the reason... but at this point the game is just more work than play.

If the game actually worked, the gameplay might be a moderately interesting take. The battlefields are more varied, which can lead to more interesting combat. It can also lead to enemies populating right next to squishy units and immediately dispense them. The idea that attacks get stronger with more allied units surrounding the unit was a worthwhile addition.

There are a few extra hero/sovereign abilities, although most are just a rehash. The skill tree is a toss up. In way, you can have more control over leveling up your character. However, having some abilities gated behind other, previously unrelated abilities imposes limitations of its own.

Probably just going to play Master of Magic again.\t\t\t\t\t
