\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs 4x tactical games go, this one is fairly simple. It doesn't do anything particularly well, nor does it do anything particularly badly. Some features found in other games of this genre are missing entirely, such as any kind of water action whatsoever, and most flying monsters. However, the ability to customize your soldiers is a significant feature that deserves merit. The ability to play as an undead empire is a plus, though it requires a DLC (at three bucks it's a bargain). The undead faction also does not pay maintenance upkeep on it's soldiers, which makes this the first game of it's type that i've seen get that right! Sporadic crashes to desktop add some spice to an otherwise mediocre title, but don't do much beyond throwing off the groove.

All-in-all, this game is not going to particularly "wow" anyone, but it is a good bargain for it's price point, which makes it a more solid choice than many other titles in it's class.\t\t\t\t\t
