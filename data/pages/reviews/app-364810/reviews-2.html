\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYeah so 21 years after playing this to excess I'm back at it. Forget WoW, something that hooks you in decades latter deserves recognition. Only picked up the steam edition as my CD's finally died a death and its nicet not having to do the dosbox work.

Gamewise its still ballachngly hard (that last Ventri mission anyone!?!) and thats a good thing. We've been hand held for too long of late. Story is still solid and engaging if feeling a little short and naturally the FMV sequences look fairly dates and if the master copies are out there they would suit a revamp very well.

Gameplay wise it is preficly viable to play this with a Keyboard on it own. God knows I did in the 90's as the sidewinder felt a bit too bulky to play with. The gameplay itself and mechanics still feel good after all this time and feel more natural than later ST "Bridge Sim" games such as Bridge Commander and ST:O in some ways.

Now find me a mod team that can import this faithfully into a modern engine!!!!\t\t\t\t\t
