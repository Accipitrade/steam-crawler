\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYET ANOTHER CRAPPY PC PORT BY PLATINUM GAMES. WHERE THE HELL ARE THE CONTROLLER BUTTON PROMPTS?????

LEt me get this clear, I'm still angry for the Nier Automata PC port and how little effort and care was put into it. You can't play it in 1080p resolution, and it has several other graphic issues that make using a THIRD party tool a necessity. They didn't even fix it with time, notr when they re-launched the game as a Something Something Edition. I still don't forget it for that.

That's where I come from to this game, plus having seen it ages ago and thinking the concept was fun.

Now, this game is probably fun, in its wacky japanese way (which is already a bit hit and miss). But in this PC port, this turns against the game. The unfamiliarity and rushed nature of the action are severely hindered by the LACK OF CONTROLLER BUTTON PROMPTS.

So you're given instructions to use KEYS and mouse motions while holding your controller, and you have NO CLUE to what to press in each moment. Granted, with time you'll learn the basic stuff. But the game is about throwing new stuff at you all the time.

So for a game launched in 2020 it's unforgiveable to not have button prompts for the defacto standard controller for PC which is XBOX 360/One (Xinput, that is).

I mean, they DID have to code the keyboard + mouse prompts, right? those are new to PC. Why not do it for controller too???? It makes me mad.

Otherwise the game has potential. The visuals are a bit crappy, but it's an old game. The perspective is confusing and the action is really really hard to follow. But it's okay.

To be honest, I'm holding off to decide whether I return it or not to see if they implement the controller prompts. Believe me, knowing what to do with the controls at any given time is a BIG part of the game flow.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can see how this game would be wonderful on its original platform, the Wii U, but on PC, it's tough to recommend. The story seems compelling enough, and the bright colorful cast is accompanied by well-written and funny dialogue. The music and level design look great too. However, the controls simply AREN'T GOOD when using either a controller or mouse. Using the right analog stick to draw shapes in this game isn't a good experience. In my experience it was fairly inaccurate and led to me dying a couple of times.

As for the combat... it's a mix of Pikmin and Platinum Games' action. On paper it sounds like a good combination but in execution, since you control a crowd of 100+ characters and the camera is pulled back quite a bit, it's hard to tell where you are or whether or not an attack is gonna hit you, save for if you're fighting a big enemy 1-on-1.

Really wanted to like this game :(\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUntil Further Notice, I can't give this game a recommendation, and this is strickly from a PC port perspective. Look, I get that this is an underrated game for its time, and I really want to try to get to see what it has to offer, but the amount of baffling decisions made makes me wonder how much the QA team was ignored for this port. Let me count the ways:

  • First of all: Whoever thought that R was a good default button for the Menu: you're wrong. Seriously. But worse is the fact that in fullscreen mode, ESC, the normal menu button, removes you from fullscreen. Which means the first several times you play it, you're panickly trying to figure out how to get to the pause/menu and just can't. Any PC player would have told you this was a problem. A major problem.
  • Further more, Menu Navigation on the PC with a keyboard and mouse is.....well its ♥♥♥♥. To select options on the menu that you have selected, you use the mouse wheel. So if you're changing resolutions, you can't just hit the arrows that are literally right there, you have to use that. Mouse wheel? Really?
  • That's on top of the fact that menu navigation can be inconsistent. Sometimes, the left and right click are allowed to be used for navigation....but sometimes it's not. Like starting the game, you can hit left click for confirm repeatedly....until the last prompt. Then it just stops. Which leads to...
  • I swear, especially when attempting to draw in game, certain keys are just ignored entirely at times. Like I'll sit there and hit the key repeatedly, and it won't do it at times. I'll wonder if I'm in condition that doesn't allow me to do it....then all of a sudden it'll work. It's maddening.
  • This is debatable, but in Fullscreen/Borderless mode you don't have a cursor for your drawing, and it always assumes that you're starting on your character. Now look, I get that is similar to play with a controller on the Wii U (if not using the stylus), but the key there is perspective and the user base that's coming in here. Using a joystick to do the drawing is much less sensitive then the base mouse controls, and so when you're first trying to draw, you're probably going to suck with the mouse because it's REALLY HARD TO GAUGE THE SENSITIVITY. It doesn't help that the first few attempts have time elements to it.
  • So, ok, it's basically telling you to use a controller. Problem is that at that point when you switch, it doesn't change the prompts of the active control scheme to show you what buttons to use until your reload a save/restart the game.
  • The game can disappear from the taskbar despite still actively running. This happened during a stream and it was infurating.
  • In windowed mode, the mouse doesn't stay with the window. This is pretty important for a game with quick drawing mechanics, and so expect to click off it repeatedly if you do try it.
  • The tutorial telling the zoom mechanics not being mentioned until the second mission on the PC is a little bit of a problem.

Now I know that a couple of these have fan patches for the game, but I'm looking at the base game and the port delivered here, and until major problems here are fixed, I can't give the game any sort of recommendation. It's a game that deserves to be played and yet the game fights you at every turn in its current iteration. I can't in good faith recommend it until a majority of these problems are fixed.\t\t\t\t\t
