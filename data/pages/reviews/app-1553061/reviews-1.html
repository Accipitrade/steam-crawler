\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMythos is kinda bland for what you pay. $25.00 seems a tad steep for, basically, a new Mythological mode that just adds a few unique units (flying units, Cereberus, and Hydras basically, with a couple Centaur units as well) and a quest line to capture one mythical monster.

If you really enjoy Troy for what it is, Mythos is probably worthwhile for expanding on the game, but Mythos doesn't really change up tactics or campaign mechanics much.

Honestly, the main tactical thing from Mythos is having griffins or harpies or whatever they are. And after that, capturing one of the three mythical monsters adds a lot of firepower to your main army.

But in the end, I had hoped Mythos would be a more significant overhaul than it is.\t\t\t\t\t
