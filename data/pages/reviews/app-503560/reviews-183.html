\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a simulator for one to act as a 911 operator. It promises different scenarios including answering different 911 calls and providing answers, and directing emergency vehicles to the correct location.

This game has a very promising beginning. It actually has the option to download popular city maps with streets and play them with familiar surroundings to the player. It is quite addicting for the first few hours, however, it gets quite repetitive after awhile. For one, the 911 calls in which you have to make a decision only have a few different ones. Scenarios don't seem to get much more difficult, and the overall gameplay becomes too simple. The concept is good, but the replay value is poor, and gets old really quickly.

While I am aware there are downloads to enhance the game, I don't feel as if I should have to pay extra to keep the game continually exciting and challenging. This seems like a fun game if it was a phone app perhaps, but not as a challenging simulator.\t\t\t\t\t
