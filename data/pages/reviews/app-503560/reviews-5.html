\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a good game for anyone looking to put their strategy and quick thinking to the test.

As for what I liked:
- the first aid information is presented in a way that is easy to understand;
- the career mode while short still has variety and is able to keep the player engaged;
- the free play mode offers a ton of cities to experience and you have the option to download more, even relatively small ones;
- the game mechanics are relatively easy to get the grasp of;
- the calls you receive are a big strength of this game, both in keeping you on your toes and checking your knowledge of first aid.

What I didn't like:
- sometimes it feels like you're put in situations where it's close to impossible to actually resolve them without at least making some mistakes, especially at the harder difficulties in free play mode;
- by the time you reach 20 hours you would have seen almost everything the game has to offer when it comes to calls, though there is a call editor if you want to have some fun with that.
