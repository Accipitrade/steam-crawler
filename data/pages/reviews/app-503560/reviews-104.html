\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt 90% off, I'd strongly recommend this game. While fairly basic graphics and story (in campaign mode), the title does a very good job of pulling off the dispatching mechanic in realistic city layouts.

There are some bugs, and certain UI issues that need fixing. These include: stacked units sometimes fail to "fan out" so you are unable to select the one you want, maps have spots that units are unable to get to (but will try until the task runs to timeout), unclear gameplay modes (it turns out you can and probably should do the single city run through first).

With all the DLC for about $4.50, my 20 or so hours proved worthwhile.\t\t\t\t\t
