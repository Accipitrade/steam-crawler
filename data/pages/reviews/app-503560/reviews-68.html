\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI highly recommend this game! A bit pricey for 14.99, but goes on sale for a lot pretty regularly; I think I got mine for 80 to 90% off and then added all the DLC's for less than that.

Pros:
Hours of gameplay that you can easily do over and over again.
Educational in a sense; learning the signs of a stroke and heart attack, how to do CPR, est.
Exciting and thrilling. Probably gave my mom a heart attack of talking a mile a minute about this game XD
Can be played over and over again, searching up different cities to play in for freeplay.

Cons:
The calls can be repetitive after a while.
Can get stressful and overwhelming, sometimes redoing levels because of it.

And warning; there are mentions of rape, slurs and cussing, gun violence, riots, police brutality and things like that. It doesn't go into great detail and isn't too disturbing, but just something to note before buying.


But other than that, I highly recommend this game. Play with headphones and maybe a notebook; I wrote down a lot of the educational bits so I wouldn't panic along with the caller on how to stop a fire. I also bought the DLC's but they are of course not mandatory, but I would consider buying them if they're all on sale. One I believe is free to begin with, so don't forget to download it!
