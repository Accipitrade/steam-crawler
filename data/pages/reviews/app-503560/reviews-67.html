\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst that I wanna mention is that I have only experienced the game on normal difficulty and haven't had the chance to play it higher difficult levels.
It's a really good game to showcase how stressful and how limited the resources available are, calls offer multiple of different scenarios which all require unique approach (though repetitive calls start to occur after 5 hrs mark), and voice acting has been done really well and contributes a lot immersion.
Few complaints:
1. Add more scenarios for calls. Game does a good job at explaining how to help with emergencies and how to guide the caller, but on normal difficulty, you don't face that many cases (I have a total of 10 duties.)
2. Tools are not that necessary aside from boosting the time it required to help with situation, Fire Department can remove any obstacles, having tools just speeds it up. I wish there was multiple of tools with each of them being necessary to have on 1 member and not having them would punish the player.
3. There is really no benefit to buying a rifle, pistol does the job just fine.

If any of the points that I have said is wrong, feel free to correct me.\t\t\t\t\t
