\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis a game that I picked up and have played on and off, I really like it and love the concept. You play as an operator for 911 and take calls and handle emergency response units in your chosen city. That is it, sounds simple and it is in most cases.
But start taking bigger cities and going up the difficulty. Then it gets harder a lot faster. You start finding that you have no spare units free, or they may be on the other side of the map. Plus it goes from nothing to everything very fast, you have to think on your toes and keep up with what is going on at all times and try keep units spare and prioritise what emergency you go to first. Then there is the calls that you can take, some will make you think way the hell did you even all 911. Then there are some that when you start speaking to them, you are already making them priority one and re-tasking and send units straight to them.
This game can honestly be very stressful very fast, it will definitely test you, seeing if you can effectively manage a city and handle the pressure.
It is honestly a great game that I would recommend that everyone should have a go at, it is great fun and a really good game to do a quick round or two on a city, or play or a few hours and play lots of cities. Plus, there are tones to play on, maybe even yours.
