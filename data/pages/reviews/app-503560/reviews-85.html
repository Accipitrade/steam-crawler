\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIs this game blatant copaganda? Yes. Do I still recommend it? Also yes.

911 Operator is exactly what it sounds like. You take up the job of a 911 operator, answering calls and sending emergency services where they're needed.

This game has been on my wishlist ever since it came out, and I finally picked it up. And I'm glad I did!

Answering the calls is easily the best part of the game. Having a limited time to determine how to help the person on the other end, having to decide whether it's an emergency or not and what best you can do to help. And it's got full voice acting! I also like how some of these calls are recreations of famous 911 calls that happened in real life, like the kid with his math homework or the pizza order.

I will admit that eventually, after you've seen every call, and they just get repetitive and not as fun anymore, but even then, that's not enough to ruin the game. Deciding which people to send where under a time crunch is also a big part of the game, and even though the calls certainly carry the game, sending your guys out to reported incidents can be rather fun as well, as it takes a lot of strategy, and it's pretty easy to screw up and get negative points. The achievements also ascribe a lot of replay value to it as well!

Overall, definitely worth a good few hours of your time!\t\t\t\t\t
