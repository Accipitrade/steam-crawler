\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitly worth the money.

The game is really good and original. You can point out several minor defaults, but nothing spoiling the fun out of it. Originality is the major point, and you got it.
Pros and cons?

Pros:
+ The gameplay is fun, the gear and staff microgestion is working fine.
+ Scenarii are full of funny cultural references (several allusions to Breaking Bad in New Mexico map).
+ Voices are good, actors are acting like real comedians, every call you receive is believable, that's a major quality for immersion. You will never pause the game to say "what the hell, this voice is bad!" or "i don't believe it".
+ If you're getting bored with the phonecalls aspect and want to concentrate on strategy, you can deactivate phonecalls on menu.
+ You can elaborate different vehicule and staff affectation strategy, or specialize your staff with different gears. Not infinite possibilities, but enough to make you want to try different approches (more lethal weapons for cops to deal with crimes quickly, but with occasional death? Or more incapacitating weapons to control crowds and riots without hurting anyone? Quick and fragile vehicules to intervene quickly, or heavily armored vehicules to prevent injuries and death, but much slower interventions? The game isn't pushing one above another, it's your choice entirely).

Cons:
- Getting pretty repetitive for two reasons: 1) the theme itself implies routine stuff, so you'll always gonna have a "my stomach hurts" call, every night... But developers got it right: it's never for the same reasons and you must investigate everytime. 2) There is too few scenarii, maybe 4 or 5 (music festival, earthquake, social protest turning to riot). Extending those scenarri to 10 or 12 could have been useful for players.
- You can't see stats properly once your staff is affected to vehicules, except for cops. For firefighters and first aid paramedics, only the driving stat stay visible: you must get them out of vehicule to see their character's sheet properly. That's a waste of time between two missions.
- Available vehicules are limited, depending on maps. That's fine, but limitations doesn't really makes sense.\t\t\t\t\t
