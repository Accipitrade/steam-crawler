\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOn a whim I decided to try this game and after 8 hours I'm pretty much hooked. Even if this only simulates a 911 Operators job by 10%, that's still a pretty rough job. I love the challenge this game brings, it keeps me constantly thinking of location to place units, the type of units I should have and what gear they should be using. The first thing I did after the tutorial was the story and I felt with each progression to a new city that the challenge went up, also since each city could present a new unique challenge. After finishing the story I've now moved onto single maps, and so far I've been able to find every city I've looked for. It's at a great price point right now with the Steam Summer sale. I do recommend this game, for those who might be interested in a game of this style.\t\t\t\t\t
