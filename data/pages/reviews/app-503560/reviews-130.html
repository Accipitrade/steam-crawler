\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNeat casual game with neat concept but feels short and underdeveloped. It’s cool to play with your own hometown but that’s not enough for a positive review. The main mechanics could be much better. In game management of the city is messy and lacks proper management tools. It’s hard to micromanage (if you’re into that) because and specially when a lot of events are happening in the same place, can be impossible to understand which vehicles are doing what or where some of them are going. Developers: please update this. Some tiny changes can go a long way. Example: if I assign 3 units to respond to an event I should be able to click on the event and see (with a different color, for example) the 3 paths of the units. It’s easy to mess up assignments due to stuff like this. At least make broader zoom in and out. Mainly zooming in should be able to distinguish 2 different units that are in 2 different streets close by without overlapping each other.
Conclusion: room for improvement.
