\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Neat and immersive, but sometimes disappointing and definitely pricey (at full)

The hours I spent to complete every city in the career mode as well as play some custom loaded cities, and every scenario this game could throw at me-- earthquakes, explosives, accidents, threats, robbery, and even a 911 call reporting someone who ran a red light was just shorter than I expected. However, the game itself did present as unique and original, and in the first 10 hours of gameplay, I was dedicated.

Overall, I would only recommend this game if you can get it at least a 30% discount or more, as $15 for a game offering a total 20 hours of gameplay (I had to grind a few extra for an achievement or two) is not worthy, especially since some 911 calls have no audio whatsoever or partial audio.

I was a bit iffy about upvoting this game, but I do feel that it was worth the deal I got it for, as the game does go on sale pretty often + some mods for this game are just plain scary and amazing.\t\t\t\t\t
