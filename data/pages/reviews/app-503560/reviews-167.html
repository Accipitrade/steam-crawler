\r\n\t\t\t\t\t911 Operator is a mangement sim where you play the role of the 911 dispatcher, answering calls and sending (or not sending) the appropriate emergency vehicles. There are a few canned scenarios that after a few playthroughs, you start already knowing how they will play out (and if you should be responding to them). However, one nice feature is that some of the calls are similar, but just slightly different. For example, a man calls with stomache pain. I've had scenarios where he ate poisonous mushrooms and others where it was long term pain and should just be told to see a doctor . I haven't made use of the workshop yet, but I suspect that there are mods there that can help expand some of the scenarios you can encounter.

One thing that I would like to see is an ability to direct teams to meet up before approaching a scenario. There are several scenes that I encountered where it was obvious that one team arriving before another would be dangerous (for example a gang war that involved 5 known suspects, and my largest available team was a 3 person police team).\t\t\t\t\t
