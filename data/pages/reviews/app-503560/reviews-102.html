\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNice game idea, but sadly very poorly made. Fun at first, but shortly after it starts to get massively annoying.

The game "crashes" where your units won't do anything, no matter what you do. So you have to start the map over again...until it starts to "crash" again...rinse, recycle, repeat...
Nothing helps, even manually assigning the tasks won't work.
Another time you are flooded with emergencies to deal with. Not a chance to handle that.
Incident markers often overlap so you cant really see oder manage them very well. Same with vehicles.

The equipment system is very unrealistic. You have only got two slots per employee to assign equipment to.
Here a mix of vehicle and personal equipment slots would have been much more realistic.
For Example a police officer can carry a handgun and a bulletproof vest. No Pepperspray, secondary weapon, first aid kit and so on. All the stuff they would have on or near them in RL.
Very unrealistic...same with paramedics and firefighters.

After some time the game gets very repetitive with the tasks offered.

Some of the calls are poorly scripted (for example you have got two identical options to answer with different consequences. Or when you have to ask the mother, who lost her child, where her dog was last seen.

The most annoying feature is the placement of the "send all units" button. In the middle between "ignore call" and
"wait (close window)". Have fun cancelling every one of your units manually one by one.
Or accidentally ignoring a call because your mouse pointer slipped a litte.

All in all i was very dissapointed with this game. Really sad...it was promising in the beginning.

My advice: hands off as long as you don't get it for under 5 bucks including all dlc.\t\t\t\t\t
