My biggest problem however is the politics of the game. The amount of violent and dangerous calls is amped up for no reason given that it is not an action game and what you are doing could just as easily be responding to fender bender or low level items. The prioritization of activities is cartoonish, and the over the top heroic tone is somewhat disturbing. In real life dispatchers have interesting strategic decisions - this game strips these away and replaces them with boring decisions designed to remind the player what a hard job emergency workers have - rather than exploring the constant moral minefields that make these positions difficult.\t\t\t\t\t
