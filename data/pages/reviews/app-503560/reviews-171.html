\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPositives: Overall, this game is a fun and realistic depiction of a 911 operator's job! I played through the campaign on normal mode and felt the pressure as the difficulty curve did seem to spike at times. Although, it did lend to the realism when there would be several big incidents, in need of multiple emergency services each, all at the same time. With a limited amount of those services to actually work with at any given time, it was a challenge to juggle what I should send where.

Negatives: Although I enjoyed the experience, it felt short for the price. I personally purchased the game when it was on sale for $5.99, which feels like a decent price for what I got out of this. However, I would have been very dissapointed to reach the end of the campaign after having paid the regular $14.99. It's just not worth that much given the play time, in my opinion, even with the ability to download your own town's map added on.

Verdict: If you find the game's premis intersting, you'll probably enjoy it! But I would defintly wait for a sale before buying.\t\t\t\t\t
