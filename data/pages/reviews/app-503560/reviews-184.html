\r\n\t\t\t\t\t911 Operator is quite remarkable.

I boot up the game and it recognizes the little town that I am living in IRL. I am literally using that map to play the game. It knows and uses the real street names. This is awesome. There are tips in game with how to deal with real emergencies, and you will use this knowledge to field 911 calls in-game. The voice acting is more than passable and your choices feel meaty. Like, some dude calls with a stomach ache and you need to decide if it's worth sending an ambulance or not. It's all very cool.

Video games designed around real-world occupations always fascinate me. I think this evolution in modern gaming was one step (of many steps) that is further propelling video games into the art category.

Pros:
+Highly replayable and highly enjoyable occupation sim
+Voice acting is a nice touch
+Use the map for where you live in real life
+Choices feel heavy
+Steam Workshop really adds some diversity to the calls
+RPG-like upgrades are great; you manage a squad and buy new vehicles, gear, and staff

Cons:
-Calls will get repetitive, but you can disable them
-I wish there was some better UI design; the time control buttons could be larger and the drag-and-drop staff management leaves something to be desired

Yeah, we are having fun with this one. Definitely an interesting title to say the least.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty solid game, kept me in for the three hours straight, but I don't see myself ever returning. It's like the Emergency series, but without being able to see your little cars and trucks racing around the city. The Real life city thing is really neat...if a bit lacking in certain features. I like the idea of teaching players important information throught games, especially things like lifesaving, but after my third load screen I was sick of the First-Aid Wikihow articles. Repitiion is definitely a killer in this game, and a lack of ability to intervene in crucial situations, like when your officers are being slaughtered at a home invasino.

Is it a well-made game? Absolutely, 100%.
Is it a good game? Yep.
Is it fun? Well.....not terribly, no.

Also as a side note as it's quite important to me currently - the game does run great on low end systems.\t\t\t\t\t
