\r\n\t\t\t\t\t911 Operator isn't a bad game. It can be a enjoyable to unlock new units and try to micromanage individual vehicles and see how situations play out while under a lot of pressure.

That being said, how much fun can being a 911 operator be? Not much.

The game loses a lot of its charm after a few hours, as there really isn't much content or replayability. Sure, different cities and all that, but why should I keep playing this game after a few hours? It's so monotonous, and there are only so many different recorded phone interactions, you can probably memorize all of them after a few hours.

I haven't played the dlc, but I cannot understand why you would add more content into the game as PAID DLC when there is already so little replayability and unique voice lines anyway. If you want another game about micromanaging and resource management, and are willing to pay 3 dollars for a few hours of fun, sure, try it. Otherwise, find something else.\t\t\t\t\t
