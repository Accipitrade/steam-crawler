\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoyed the game a lot. I found some of the scenarios frustrating....well really just one. The blizzard scenario seems ridiculously hard, especially on expert mode. I doubt there is a way to get 100% efficiency, you're always going to miss at least 1 call.

Some things to note. If you finish a day, and don't do as well as you'd like, there is no way to go back and redo the day without starting the entire campaign over. This affected me when I got to Chapter 7, the music festival when I was just feeling my way around the game. By the time I got to Chapter 7, I had figured out little tricks...however, due to the overall efficiency requirement, my previous bad days came back to haunt me cause though I was able to get to 100% for Chapter 7, Day 1....It was still giving me a 67% overall efficiency so I was unable to move forward to the next day. To fix my it, I had to start the entire campaign over again. Then when I was in expert mode and events were popping up so fast, if I missed a call and lost 30 - 40 efficiency, instead of playing the day through, I would quit the day and go back and start over as I was afraid I would screw myself later on and have to restart the campaign. I recommend giving players a way to redo days as starting the campaign over can be tedious.

I'd also like to see a way to access the daily reports from within the scenario. Often I wanted to refer to the report but once you close it, I couldn't see a way to go and view it again. Not imperative to gameplay, but a nice to have

It would also be nice to have a way to control the sound from within the scenario. I like the radio chatter as it gives a realistic air to the game, but I couldn't see a way to lower or mute the volume of the sound from within the game
