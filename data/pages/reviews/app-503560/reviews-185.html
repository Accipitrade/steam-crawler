\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will preface by stating that I am a 911 EMT, but I have no Dispatch training.

This game is pretty true to life in the situations encountered, for instance: Suspicious call details for a medical call can usually mean a fight may break out, so having your Medic stage before making scene with PD may be prudent. The developers did their research, and the instructions you can give to bystanders for medical emergencies or traumatic emergencies can be what saves or kills the patient; Remember, never remove an impaled object.

I do have several small criticisms however.
-The game severely understaffs and underequips you, making putting multiple units on a call difficult to justify, even when a working fire in reality will require more than just one Engine company to handle.
-Being able to give your units name designations and posts would help organize your service area’s system better.
-There should be the ability to have your units automatically stage until PD clear a scene, possibly by context menu. It is difficult to manage a call with various units obscuring the incident location.
-Roaming and Posting, you should be able to assign units to post at a designated location or roam around the area. (EMS and Fire currently Post while PD Roams by default.)
-City maps: these should respect the actual cities’ borders I think, as opposed to being rectangles of various dimensions.

Overall a pretty great game. You can experience the frustration and stress of being Level Zero, just like in real life. The only thing missing is having your supervisor come over the radio to urge units to clear calls faster!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off I really love the concept of this game, it's simple yet challenging at times. I like that you control all the forces. (Medical, police & Fire fighters) Can be hectic at times, but very fun.

Little story: I went straight to the "free game" mode when I started, I picked a city near me and went to work. was SO ADDTICED. Then on my 32nd duty I had protestors... I wasn't ready... lost a ton of money... Had protestors again the next day... wasn't ready didn't call National guard either, instead they came on their own accord... and as a result was 276k in the hole. LMAO But the next day I grinded out some duties and now have a force I never fail. now on duty 72! I was so determined to get my city's forces back on top and I did. We are now ready for anything. I kind of think we are over prepared.

In saying all this, the game is great, I saw it the other day for just under $10 with all DLC, sangged it. I now have 25+ hours in it and I am hitting the wall... The wall of repetition... sadly the calls repeat so frequently it's making me not want to play it much. And I'm sad about that cause I love this game. Yes they have workshop, a lot fo the mods are very sub par, some are very good. But those some will not add that many hours to your gameplay. i wish there was something to add 100+ different unique calls to this game. But all in all a great game. I say if I get a game fot $10 I'd like to get 10 hours out of it, so $1 an hour. So I'd say I definitely got my monies worth.

8/10 NEED MORE CALLS!\t\t\t\t\t
