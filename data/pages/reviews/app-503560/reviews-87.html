\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGrade: 8/10
My first intention was to give 911 Operator 7,5/10, but I must recognise the originality of this game. Simulators often refer to other routine aspects, but the idea of playing as a 911 operator is very innovative and cool. Except for standard missions, which have got default maps, free matches have almost endless possibilities. You can download the map of almost every city on Earth and see them adapted to the game fonctions.
This game offers a number of various possibilities: different emergencies, lots of different phone calls and (why not?) some funny anecdotes too! Especially with DLC, you can place different types of units (for example police cruisers, police bikes, police van etc.).
Unfortunately, the game can become easily repetitive. It'd be nice to contact other emergency workers (such as lifeguards, soldiers etc.) according to the kind of emergency. Another defect is that you can play only as one 911 operator, there's no possible choice of voice or person. But actually the game is very good and realistic!\t\t\t\t\t
