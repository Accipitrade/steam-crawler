\r\n\t\t\t\t\tQuite fun and addictive gameplay. Lots of maps - and I love that you can use your hometown that's a really great feature.

Beyond that though my only real complaint is the progression seems kind of muddled. There's different game modes that aren't clearly defined and there is seemingly no pattern to when you get promoted to another map. There doesn't seem to be any clear finish line of completing a given map beyond your own personal goals.

Anyways the gameplay is solid but the campaign could have been put together a lot better in my opinion. Worth picking up you can probably get 4 - 10 hours of fun out of this before it gets stale. Though the workshop may even extend that.\t\t\t\t\t
