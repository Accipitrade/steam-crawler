\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile not extraordinarily realistic, I've found 911 Operator to be fun and rewarding casual gaming experience. The voice acting and script is done well, and it touches on some of the darker sides of life without fully immersing the player in overwhelming traumatic events.

Much of the game-play is centered on an adapted point-and-click style with mild real time strategy elements. Decisions must be made based on changing priorities while balancing limited resources and personnel. The only major gripe that I would make is that the game tends to get "grindy" and repetitive the longer you play.

Overall, it's a fun game and I've enjoyed the 40-ish hours I've put into it. 6.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
