\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoyed the resource management concept of this game and I really think with a bit more effort this game will be much better than it's current state. I feel like they had a great concept, executed it, and then ran out of time when it came to making content and features. There's a few hours of game here but after that you are doing the same thing. Every deployment in the same; nothing really stands out. Typing in any larger city and having it converted into a game map was a brilliant idea. Playing on your home city, seeing streets you know pop up was fun. But you get the same handful of calls and scenarios that have a few tiny variables that don't really change the outcome or your actions. You simply spam the mouse button to clear the call and get back to dispatching units.

I almost bought the DLCs as I was craving more content but the price of two DLCs is more than the full game and honestly I agree with other reviews--the DLC content should have been in the main game to begin with. There simply isn't enough content to be asking us to pay more.

I hope the developers reconsider this game and turn it into what I know it could be. There's plenty of untaped potential.\t\t\t\t\t
