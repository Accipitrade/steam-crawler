Do not pay the full price, wait for sale!

I played about roughly 2-3 times with a total of 8 hours.

The game contains quite interesting contents and themes of being 911 operator and resource management. However, I find it boring over time because it is quite repetitive. You don't have a luxury of experiencing the first-hand sight of the incidents. I guess this make sense because it is about the operator.

You will see a map of city that outline where the incident is occurring, where the incoming caller is located and how to assign your resources in the city so that they can reach the site in time, when needed. This is about it, no other interesting function in game.

I give a thumb up because I luckily bought this game with huge discount. Do not get it with the full price. \t\t\t\t\t

911 Operator
911 Operator is a Simulation of being a Emergency Service Dispatcher with being the Head department managing staffing, equipment and vehicle purchase of all three departments. Your job is to ensure that all incoming incident are handle properly with the correct respondent. From handling calls to handling incoming reports.

Pros
  • Calls have to be listen carefully to the background from the callers end for information
  • Staff do require days off for recovery meaning not everyone can be worked to death
  • Not every report and call can be made in time to the area.
Cons
  • Starting Cash is a bit hard to work with if your not playing story mode
  • Game can get really punishing if the previous day did poorly
  • Not much of a custom starting options if your using mods (Custom Calls)
Workshop
The amazing thing about this game is that you can have custom calls done by community via steam workshop spicing up your game with custom scenario's from school shooting, to terrorist attack and much more that can happen in the real world rather the having the same scenarios that can get old at times. I wish that the game would let you have custom set ups as this can prove a large difficulty depending on the workshop addon getting something like shoot out in the mall on the first day can prove a huge difficulty not having the right enforcement.

Conclusion
The game is amazing and the story mode is a really good interesting way getting into the game i just wish there was unique scenario you could add in for the custom maps. Being able to have the option to play a map of your real life city bring a unique experience as you can look and point at location you know in real life. The amount of customization could be improve on as it does feel a bit limited as well.\t\t\t\t\t
