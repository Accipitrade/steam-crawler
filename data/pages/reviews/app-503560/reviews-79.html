911 Operator is a good management game with a bunch of missons, whose appears to have a realistic flavour.
For full disclosure, I have only played the campain missons and they were quite good and short. There is a pause function, which is in my opinion essential for a good overview and playability. You're a dispatcher for the ambulance, fire department and federal police. The player has to assign the different vehicles to the calls and sometimes there are bogus calls, where people calling you and asking about the ordered pizza.

The main tasks are seperated in 2 ways and the first one is receiving calls and the second one is to assign the right vehicle to the call or not. The calls are usually questions, where you have to listen and answer in a multiple choice way. For example, check out the screenshots above.
Sometimes there are callers for example, they are so excited, they don't tell you where they are and it requires to keep them on the phone for the triangulation of the location.

Afterwards, it's necessary to allocate the vehicles to the calls. In some maps there are blizzards or other obstacles for the vehicles to ensure their arrival. If you stuck in the snow with one car, then you need the fire rescue ar for releasing them of their miserable situation. That's the gameplay loop and it's fun and not to long for some hours as dispatcher.\t\t\t\t\t
