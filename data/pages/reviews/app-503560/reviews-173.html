\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you've ever wondered what the life of a 911 operator is like, this game is fantastic. Like the trailer shows you have to juggle first responder dispatchs all the events ongoing in the city, from mundane cat in a tree situations to domestic violence to airplanes crashing into houses.

Each basic service (police, medical, fire truck) has it's own specalities and strengths, and your real life intuition does work - every force can treat a broken leg, but medical teams do it faster and are the only ones who can take them to a hosptial, and only police forces can intercept armed robbers or speeding cars.

As you process through the game, you have to decide what sort of reponse you can justify with your current available units. So far I've only missed a handful of calls you will find yourself wishing you had just one more unit available.

One thing the very tutorial doesn't do well is explain that you can buy equipment for your forces to help them. These range from police rifles and bullet proof vests, to first aid kits and technical tools. I allocate a first aid kit to my fire trucks and police forces so they can back up the ambulances and stablize victims while awaiting an ambulance for medical transport.\t\t\t\t\t
