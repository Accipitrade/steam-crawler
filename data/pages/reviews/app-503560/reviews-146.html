\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pretty entertaining if you love managing units or love the atmosphere of 911 situations. I live in Canada so there are many similarities of conduct between Canada and US 911 services. It truly does bring an almost realistic simulation of how most 911 situations go and it educates you well on it. There are some negatives like how cops almost always, no matter if equipped with stun guns and riot gear, end up killing the suspects (maybe it be more realistic to US Cops, JK). However, the calls are one of the other main features of this game. The voice acting does sell it and even the story situations like the pizza women and the leg chopped off are based off real 911 situations. The price is okay but it would be beneficial to buy it when the game is at sale, especially if you want to add the DLCs. Having the DLCs kinda feels necessary as it expands the game's playability time. Overall, it's a nice game and I would recommend this game.

Pros:
- Good Voice Acting
- Mostly Realistic Situations
- Educational

Neutral:
- The Price (Better if on sale)

Cons:
- Cops follow American Doctrine (which is kill them all, jk)\t\t\t\t\t
