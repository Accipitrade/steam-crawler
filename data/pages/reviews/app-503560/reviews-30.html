\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI liked the idea of the game and grabbed it during one of the sales. However, after playing 15+ hours, It lacks re-playability. Once you finish one duty on expert level, the others are pretty much all the same. The game could have been polished better, for example units, after finishing their task, could return to their original position. Also, when you have overlapping units, it can be difficult to sort through them. In fact sometimes when a new event pops up behind a unit, it may not be visible (especially in a congested area). The controls to move around the map could be improved too.

Moreover, when creating units the layout can be more user-friendly. For example to assign multiple equipment - it's quite tedious to assign the same equipment to every staff. There should be an "assign all" option. The equipment's effects could be enhanced, because overall during a duty there isn't much a difference with them, i.e. the effect is negligent. There can be more diverse selection of equipment as well. You should have the ability to chain instructions to units. It takes an age for a staff member to be promoted. And it takes a lot of time for a staff member to recover from injuries.

Good potential, but poor execution. Would not recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
