Great game:
Gameplay - 7/10 Fun but not completely action packed
Graphics - 3/10 Minimum even though the game's pull is gameplay
Story - 1/10 Doesn't really have one

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a really fun game, if you are into the whole micro-managing challenge genre. You can play in most cities around the US, from what I can tell even small towns are available (my hometown of 13,000 people is included). It is really enjoyable to help some of the callers, and the calls are honestly really cool the first couple of times you hear them. All in all, the game is a really fun idle kind of game, and you can get a lot more than you'd expect out of it.

Pros:
*several different locations
*voiced calls with different outcomes
*takes skill, but is easy to get used to

Cons
*some of the calls get old, and there aren't a huge amount of them (yet)
*occasionally the missions can feel a little annoying/repetitive

P.S. full disclosure here, I have not yet played the career mode, I've just been having a lot of fun in the free play lol, so I cannot give a description of that aspect.\t\t\t\t\t
