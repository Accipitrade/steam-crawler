Overview

Ever wondered how the 911 operators work? Well, now you can play as a 911 operator in this game! This game permits you to direct the police, fire, and paramedic forces around the city to solve the cases while attending the constant 911 calls. The game gives you the feeling of how hectic the work of a 911 operator is as they have a lot of work and the lives of the people are in their hands.

Gameplay

The base gameplay is the player taking on the role of a 911 operator and manage the police force, medical services, and firefighters, and dispatch them to the crime locations and to whoever needs help. The game has two Game Modes:
  • Career mode: This is the shortest mode in the game. This mode comprises several maps of popular American cities and these maps can be unlocked by racking up reputation after a few in-game days of work. This is basically a superfluous mode since the Free Mode offers more content and gameplay time than the Main Mode itself. This mode can be seen as a Starting Point for new players, but it still lacks the complexity to be considered as a mode by itself. A separate tutorial would have been better.
  • Free mode: This will be the mode that most of the players would invest the majority of their time in. The game swanks about having over a thousand cities to choose from and play in, and trust me, they are not joking. You can play in almost any known city around the world. They also have a lot of Not well known cities!

Understanding how to play is pretty easy, but playing the game is challenging. The game starts with you managing the units of the Police Force, Medical Force, and the FireFighting Department. You have to man each of the units with employees. Each employee would have their own Stats that determine whether they finish the job faster than others or reach the Emergency Locations faster. Each and every employee have two slots where you can equip them with boost items like weapons and armor for the cops, Fire Extinguishers for Firefighters, etc. There is also a variety of vehicles to choose from. Some vehicles are cheaper and faster but lack in transport space while others are pricier and have sufficient transport space for bigger crews but lack in Speed.

After setting up the forces, the player gets some time to spread out the units on the map which permits the units to get to a location faster. They will eventually group up later on, but its better to start with an advantage. As you start, you would have to solve small emergencies like help people with broken bones, put out small fires, save a cat stuck on a tree, etc. You will have to choose the right vehicle and direct them to the location of the emergency. The unit will head over there and automatically solve the case. Sometimes, more than one unit would be required at an emergency location, so the player should pay proper attention to the requirements of solving each case. These small cases would eat up most of your time, but you would also get special calls and you need to handle the situations. Not all calls will be emergencies, some of them would be just people trying to waste your time. But pay attention to each call as some calls which seem like a random wrong number call are actually people talking in a code so the people around them dont find out. You are given a few options on how to respond to each statement made during the call. You can gather facts, give instructions, or refuse to help them.

Pros
  • The calls are based on Real Life situations and the voice acting has been very well done! I loved how each call carried the perfect emotion in the voice.
  • The player has over 1000 cities to choose from to play in.
  • The player has a choice on how to respond to calls and whether to solve the case or ignore it.
  • The boost items and Stats feature helps the player to create the perfect team for each day.
  • The game has a unique concept and is really interesting to play.
  • There are no issues graphically and the game has a simple graphical layout, but it still gives us the 911 Vibes.

Cons
  • The Career mode seems unnecessary.
  • The game advertises other games made by the same developer inside the game. I feel its wrong for them to do this in a game that we pay to play.
  • The game becomes repetitive later on as you will be getting the same calls as before. Not recommended for people who want to invest a large amount of time into a particular game.

DLCs
  • 911 Operator - Special Resources: This DLC adds 8 new Emergency Vehicles: Bicycle, Unmarked Police Car, Sports Car, Armored Van, Large Police Helicopter, Medical Bike, Large Ladder and Mobile Hospital; and also adds 6 Special pieces of equipment: Police Dog, Taser, Shotgun, Defibrillator, Heavy Fire Suit and Bomb Defuse Robot. It also adds 15 new Reports.
  • 911 Operator - Every Life Matters: This DLC adds a new REINFORCEMENTS function which lets you summon additional units during your duty, four special EVENTS during the Free Game mode, two new cities for the career mode: Miami and Boston, 12 new conversations, 18 new reports, two new pieces of equipment: pepper spray and riot gear and two new vehicles: the prison van and the water cannon.
  • 911 Operator - First Response: This Free DLC adds two new vehicles: Firefighters quads and police horses, Answers for new calls, New Equipment, New incidents, and a lot more.
  • 911 Operator - Search & Rescue: This DLC adds the SEARCH mechanics along with new related reports, Special events: Fire Streak and Snowstorm which will be available in Career and Free Game mode, 2 new cities in the Career mode: Detroit and Anchorage, 12 new calls, 24 new incidents, 4 pieces of equipment: DRONE, THERMAL IMAGER, NIGHT VISION, and SAR VEST and 3 new vehicles: SAR Helicopter, Pick-up Truck, and Water Bomber.
Conclusion

The game tried to make it as realistic as possible to copy the daily life of a 911 Operator, and they did do a pretty good job. Hats off to the voice acting as its the aspect of the game that I really loved. Its a unique concept that requires recognition. I do recommend this game to people who have time to spare. Get it during a sale as I dont think its worth its full price. The DLCs are optional, they arent that pricey, but recommend the whole package during Sale.
