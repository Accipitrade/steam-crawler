\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend it, but I do think there should be a few changed/ additions.
1 would be a "slider" system that would change frequency and severity of calls/ incidences instead of basically getting the same missions repeated on each play through.
2 would be the ability to only answer calls. Similar to the checkbox to ignore calls, another checkbox should ignore incidences so you can focus on just being an operator.
3 would be to be able to pass calls on to another operator. Similar to ignore call, another option should be to pass calls/ incidences along because it's not very realistic that a city such as New York only has 1 operator and being punished for not stopping a speeder while dealing with 6 other major crimes.

Other than these issues, it's a basic but relatively fun game. I'd recommend getting it on a deal because it is very repetitive and understandably lacking in content.\t\t\t\t\t
