\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery impressed with the game. I had only seen the positive scores before picking it up and did not know of the cool hook that it download's your town and lets you play there. The game feels like "Rescue X-Com" you build up your team, hire random first responders, and train them up with experience while building more teams. The mix of random events and 911 calls is really good. I actually LIKE how 80% of the 911 calls are generic or mistakes. Every once in awhile you get a creepy, serious, and sometimes very dark call that sends chills up your spine.

I do think the DLC adds a lot especially to free play. Having major events such as fires, earthquakes, and rock concerts adds a lot of depth as do the new vehicles. I strongly recommend just starting with the base game + DLC and if the game feels too difficult, turn off the DLC and it will be easier and more straightforward.

If you're reading these reviews and seen the trailer or screenshots, the game is probably for you. The type of person to be interested in this is who the game is for. It's not hiding anything.\t\t\t\t\t
