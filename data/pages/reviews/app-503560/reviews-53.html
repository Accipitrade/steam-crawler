\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk to pick up during a sale I guess. But to really recommend it some things are missing or wrong.

Example:
Second hardest difficulty

Operator: sooo like, how big is the fire?
Caller: it is huge it is so big the flames shoot out 10 to 15 meters high
Operator: ok, we'll send the fire brigade

- 5 police officers nearby. send them there first to secure the scene/evacuate/clear the road, whatever.
This stuff isn't really modeled but i role play it that way anyway. And they do have some base stats to deal with fire so you can see it that they shave off a little bit of time of the fire counter (they measure it in seconds needed to dowse, e.g. 999 seconds).
Yay right?

Well yes, yay and nay.

Yay because they do shave off some time.
Nay because they shaved off ALL the time....

By the time the FIRE PICK UP TRUCK and the FIRE ENGINE arrive that i scrambled, the fire is gone.
Police just dowsed a fire in a building with flames that shot out 15 meters into the air.... yeaaaaaaaaaaah noooo that shouldn't happen...

Similarly the FIRE PICK UP TRUCK should also not be able to completely dowse LARGE FIRES by itself.
A trashcan fire? Sure. Maybe even a burning Car. But an Appartment building fire?


So what I am saying is:

1) it shouldn't be possible to dowse a LARGE FIRE with just any random vehicle...
because 2) what is the point of specialized vehicles? Any time saving these specialists (more expensive, you will have fewer) give you, is lost by the time they take to deploy.

You are better off having many cheap vehicles around.


I mean similarly why am I not getting penalized for using the MEDICAL HELICOPTER, an expensive Vehicle IRL, on some incidents that can actually be resolved by any other vehicle.
Be it POLICE CAR, FIRE CAR, or an actual AMBULANCE.

IRL these choppers are only deployed on the most urgent, most pressing, AND far away incidents.
Meantime here i am zipping my chopper from block to block because why deploy a slow ambulance when you only have 1 injured to transport and apparently you can just land on every street.....

So while i had fun getting into this little chill game, simple common sense mechanics are missing and making the game more simple than it has to be.\t\t\t\t\t
