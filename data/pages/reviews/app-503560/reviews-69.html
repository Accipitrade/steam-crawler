\r\n\t\t\t\t\t911 operator is a very unique game that presents players with a one-of-a-kind experience that you cannot find anywhere else to the same calibre in which Jutsu Games, the developer of 911 operator, does it.

You, the player, are put into the role as a 911 operator (a first responder for the North American Emergency Telephone Hotline) who must make quickly-thought and executed decisions in order to save the lives of civilians. As you progress each level the map also changes giving a more fresh experience while you get better at managing your emergency crews such as police, firefighters and ambulance.

Part of what makes this game stand out from the rest is the astounding voice acting; since the entire character-to-NPC interaction is based solely on audio rather than visual aid it was crucial that it was done right and it was. Every character you interact with feels unique, special and experiencing their own problems in the world whether it be relevant to your calls or not. Some have been smoking and call you thinking you're the local pizza place, some call you asking whether you can come handle their child having a melt-down and some, the most heartbreaking of all, have real problems which you cannot help but feel crushing, gut-wrenching sympathy for. This, on top of being able to choose your own dialogue from an array of different comedic and serious options, brings you a gaming experience you will never forget.

A word of warning: This game can be very heavy. Even if you try to take comedic approaches to certain situations, and try to lighten up the mood, you will still hear people scream on the phone, you will hear grown men, fathers, howl in anguish as they lose their wife, son or daughter and it will feel real. This game is a one-of-a-kind experience because of how real it is.

Take your time. This is an intense experience.\t\t\t\t\t
