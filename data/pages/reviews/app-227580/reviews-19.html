+ Addictive gameplay
+ Easy to get into for everyone
+ 'Resource-gathering' and upgrades that cost gold

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tName of the game? 10,000,000. Your goal? 10,000,000. Yep, it's interesting.

Even though we have a strangely-named (or numbered) game title, it does seem to fit within the main game itself as you simply need to score 10,000,000 points in order to win the game. How do you score that, you may ask? Well, the game is rather simple in a way that it mixes match-3, endless runner and RPG-like elements all in one game. You match tiles in order to either gain resources, build up your shield, unlock loot, or even just kill some monsters that'll simply attack you in order to push you to the left side of the screen, which will make you lose. As you gain resources such as XP, gold, wood and stone, you use them to improve your dungeon that'll bring in many status upgrades ranging from dealing more damage when matching swords and staffs to gaining certain buffs such as gaining time bonuses when matching wood and stone.

Throw in some objectives, ranks, and some score and resource multipliers, you'll be well on your way to reaching 10,000,000! It's a fun game for those who like match-3 RPGs.\t\t\t\t\t
