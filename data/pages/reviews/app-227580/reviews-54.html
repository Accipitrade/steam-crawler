\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10.000.000 is a game about numbers. You wake up in a locked up area and have to fight your way through the dungeon full of monsters. Along the way you can pick up items to aid you in the journey and upgrade your skills through Experience points and Gold gained from playing.

The gameplay consists of mixing tiles all over the place. That may sound boring, but is actually a quite interesting take on a game like this, and requires you to think about your moves and executing them as fast as possible. The game will reward you for playing well and mixing and matching the right tiles to kill the right enemies and gain resources to upgrade your buildings, which in turn allows you to upgrade your character even more.

The game is somewhat lengthy with some hours of gameplay and features no stops in the flow, unless you get matched up against a boss monster, in which the game heavily punishes you for not being prepared nor fast enough to match the right tiles. The game can feel cheap sometimes shoving a boss monster down your hallway with only a few points to go to the next rank.

The soundtrack is alright, but with only 3 songs, which may seem repetitive to some. Sound effects match the visuals nicely and fits the retro theme nicely along with the soundtrack.

All in all, the game is quite fun, not very pricey, has an above average soundtrack and will get you at least a few hours of entertainment.\t\t\t\t\t
