\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt surface level, the gameplay is simple match three before time runs out. The added twist of needing to match specific blocks to pass obstacles in engaging and implements well. As was mentioned in another review, you will occasionally have several of the same obstacles in a row, which will see you run out of the blocks you need. This doesn't seem incredibly common, and may just be a fluke of the radomness of the obstacle generation.

It will take a lot of runs to get the resources you need to escape. This isn't much of a problem, as it's incredibly nice to have an actual objective in a game like this.

One gripe with the game is that it seems to be perfectly geared for playing on tablets or other touch screens, and that using a mouse handicaps you. It's a minor problem, and one that might just be because I'm terrible at games.

There are currently some bugs with achievement completion, but that only matters if you care very much about achievements.

In all, it's a charming little puzzle that is definitely worth the price.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10000000 is a match-3, dungeon runner. Overall, the gameplay is consistent and entertaining as you kill enemies with matches, gain upgrades through loot and missions and collect weapons and aid items to help you survive longer in the dungeon.

The game works well and is fairly entertaining. If you need something to play on the side while waiting for matchmaking lobbies to load your matches in or some mind-numbing action, then pick this game up discounted.

If you are thinking about picking up You Must Build a Boat, then go ahead and pick this game up instead. The mechanics work better throughout your playtime than in YMBaB..

TL;DR

Simple match-3, dungeon runner that gets the job done and will keep you busy while waiting for programs to run. Get it discounted. Avoid their pseudo-sequel, You Must Build a Boat, for the initial purchase as this game is more solid mechanics-wise.\t\t\t\t\t
