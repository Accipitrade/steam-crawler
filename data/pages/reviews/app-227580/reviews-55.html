\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlthough it has a lot of things going against it (Match 3 puzzle game, iOS port, etc) this game is loads of fun. Don't be fooled by the store page, this isn't actually an RPG, but simply a match-3 puzzler with RPG elements.

While most M3 games have you moving one symbol around in one of 4 directions, this one has you rotating columns/rows to match 3. It takes a bit of getting used to, but well worth it.

Most gameplay is spent on the dungeon crawling. You look up on the screen, and match the symbol needed above. eg: If theres a chest, match 3 key symbols.

You cannot pass until you match the correct symbol, and theres an advancing wall that kills you (Keep ahead of it!). There are also monsters that you must kill using either Magic or Physical matches (Different monsters have different weaknesses). There are different items you can use too. Overall, a great timewaster/puzzler, but not really a puzzler.

17/20\t\t\t\t\t
