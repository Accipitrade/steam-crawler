\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is very simple, it's your average 3 tiles match game but with some more depth. You got ressources tiles, Woods and Stones, which let's you gain ressources for upgrading your shops. You got keys which let's you open chests and doors. You got bags which will give you a item from time to time. You got attack tiles, Sword and Staff, which make you attack the enemies in front of you and depending of the enemies you're fighting it might be better to use physical attacks (Sword) or magical one (Staff). Shield tiles let you absorb a damage from the enemies. There's also a star tiles which basically can match with any other tiles.

With the shop you can upgrade your gears, like your swords so you do more physical damage with sword tiles. You can upgrade your armor so enemies take more time to attack you. You gain experiences so you can unlock skills, like more chances for your magical attacks to make a abnormal status to the enemies. There's also potions that will handicap you so you can gain more points for exemple.

The graphics are simple but got charms, the really big selling point to me is the music which is fantastic in this game. So if you like 3 match tiles like I do, this is definetly worth a buy.\t\t\t\t\t

This is a Fast paced puzzle game.


Interface/Menu/Settings


Video Settings information.

  • You can play this game in fullscreen or windowed mode.
  • There are no Resolution sizes available.

Volume Settings Information.

  • There is a music volume slidebar available.
  • There is a sound volume slidebar available.

Miscellaneous Information.

  • You are able to delete your save file.
  • There is no Menu available.


About the game.


There will be a bunch of tiles on the playing field but you will have to make sure that you are swapping the tiles that are making a match together. 3 tiles or more of the same tile to make a match. Sword and staves tiles will danage the enemy, Key tiles will unlock door and chests. Wood and ore tiles are needed to unlock new things at the hub. Shield tiles are needed to reduce damage and make the run last longer. The backpack tile has a chance of giving you an item. You will have to reach 10M points in a single run and your run will end if you hit the left side of the screen.

Ingame Information.

  • Unlock new things.
  • Addictive.
  • Game is easy to understand.
  • Make your character stronger with upgrades.
  • The game isn't super long.
  • Nice Retro music.
  • Has challenges for rewards.



Miscellaneous Information.

  • The game is somewhat simliar like "Tetris attack" on the SNES or "Pokemon Puzzle league" on the N64. It's not 100% the same though. Just an idea.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA relatively simple match-3 style game where you match tiles to execute various commands while adventuring through a linear dungeon hallway: attack, block, magic, get item, use key (for doors and treasure chests), and a few resource gathering things. Get cash, XP, and resources, upgrade your character, and repeat. Get a score of 10,000,000 to beat the game. Nowhere near as forgiving as something like Bejeweled, but the hallway really just boils down to a timer mechanic that forces you to match certain types of tiles in order to add time to your game.

Worth skipping a soda with lunch to buy, but not enough here to recommend further.\t\t\t\t\t
