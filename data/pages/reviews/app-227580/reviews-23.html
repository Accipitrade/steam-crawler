\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot ambitious, nor very pretty or polished, this puzzle game is still a surprisingly addictiv experience for the genre lovers.
The puzzle time attack kind of game isn't generally what I like, but I must say it got me in the ressources collection, the unlockables and the challenge of missions and overall progression.
The game is simple to understand and has a satisfying difficulty curve without being hardcore or time eater.

Which gets me to the negative point : it's short!
And while i did a lot of grind on purpose, finished the game in 10hrs, I'd not be surprised if one could beat it faster.
I hoped for a system of several floors and many more unlockables but nope.

So despites its shortness, the game is good, you can still try to break the record after beating the "story".
I would take it only on sales (which I did) considering the game depth. The concept is nice anyway and could benefit a huge developpement imo.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a 'Match 3' style game doubling up as a dungeon crawler. It's deceptively simple in its artstyle, UI and objectives but it has a 'Just 1 more go' feedback loop that makes it maddeningly addictive. Match swords for physical attacks, Wands for Magical attacks, Shields for defence and resources to build your home base which unlocks upgrades.

Each run only lasts a matter of minutes but with every failure you earn a little more to upgrade your character and eventually hit the magical 10,000,000 which wins the game.

It's a perfect budget game. Easy to grasp premise and concept, mechanically excellent and a fun little diversion.

82/100\t\t\t\t\t
