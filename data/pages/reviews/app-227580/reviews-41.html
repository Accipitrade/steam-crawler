\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat is 10,000,000? It sounds like the beginning to a terrible essay but I've had friends constantly ask me while I play.

Enjoy Bejeweled but are terribly bored of rolling in riches and crushed gem shards? Puzzle Quest quests just not instantly gratifying enough? Well welcome to 10,000,000!

You are an explorer trapped in a dungeon and you must fight your way through hordes of monsters and dragons. STORY. Not really, that's pretty much all you get. You match tiles to use magic or melee attacks. Match keys to open chests and doors. Oh, but that's not all. You need to match lumber and stone to gather resources for your home base. Home base, well that's where you get all beefed up for your next expedition.

But what happens when you finally reach 10,000,000 points?

Farther you get in the dungeon, the more points you get along with experience and other such wonders to help you in future journeys.

It's a nice time killer when you are waiting for friends for other such online gaming shenanigans or just when you really don't want to think and maintain some level of sanity.\t\t\t\t\t
