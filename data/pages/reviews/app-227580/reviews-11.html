\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is hard to review the game because it is great and terrible frustrating at the same time.
Pro:
- The concept is awesome. If you are into match 3 games, you will love it. You can gather resources, upgrade your gear, learn skills, upgrade buildings up to level limit 3.
- The game play is easy to learn, you can re-run levels anytime to gain resources just like in plain Diablo

Con:
- Unfortunately it is nearly impossible to beat the game. The luck factor is just like playing poker by not knowing your hand.
- Your upgrades are limited, you can't gringd out our outgrow the luck factor. You need 10M points to beat the game. I can run 8M+ points 4 out of 5 runs, but never ever go over 9M.
- Your frustration will go over the roof at the end game because no matter your skill, only luck matters.

Feedback to the developer:
- I would recommend to add possibility to outgrow the luck element. Either by having bigger stash, or have lvl4 buildings to upgrade over the current end game limit. It is a great game, but rely on luck only to beat the game is terrible design.\t\t\t\t\t
