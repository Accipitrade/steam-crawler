The progression system is fun, and the goal is to become stronger with better weapons and armor until you are finally powerful enough to score 10,000,000 points in one session. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat Game but can be a little short. The puzzles are fun since you need to be making combinations based on what is in front of you, keys to unlock doors, weapons/magic to kill enemies and wood/stone tiles so you can level up your character between runs. I like the idea and additional complexity vs other more simple FB type games, I didn't want something simple like Blitz.

The short; Great game puzzle game with XP system and leveling. Down side; can be a little short, after you have fully leveled your character the stone/wood tiles don't really matter any longer and it is just you trying to get your 10,000,000.

Me: runs well on my laptop, game can be quickly picked up and put down. can feel short due to fully leveling character but I still play the game on and off.\t\t\t\t\t
