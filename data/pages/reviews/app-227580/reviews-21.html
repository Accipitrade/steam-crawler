\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10,000,000 was a game i found out about completely by accident, browsing through some new games to pickup I saw this interesting dungeon looking pixel/puzzle game and it looked interesting. With the low cost to buy in I took the plunge and I couldn't be happier with the decision.

The game is primarily match-3, matching the tiles allows you to send your nameless dungeon crawler hurdling through the stone corridor towards his inevitable defeat to some combination of the hands of a nearby monster or a clock depending on how well things go for you. The simplicity of the gameplay makes it very interesting to play and progress through as you become better at finding and making matches.

The game features RPG mechanics in the form of resource collection to boost the effectiveness of your matches. Matching swords and staves vs monsters will produce different results depending on what that monster is weak or resistant to as well as improving the overall power of each match. You can also unlock perks to be given to your hero permanently by spending earned experience, and temporarily through the use of the toggleable potions which change how some of the game mechanics work.

Overall, if you like puzzles, racing against the clock, and mashing monsters, you'll likely enjoy 10,000,000. The only thing I wish was that the name was a bit more memorable!\t\t\t\t\t
