\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInteresting little tile match game with RPG elements. Unlike some games with additional RPG elements this game makes them necissary, your weapon and armor upgrades allow you to get further in the game and without the RPG elements it would be nearly impossible to get to the victory condition of 10,000,000 points in a single run. The graphics are visually uninteresting but that is not that big of an issue as you can allways tell the difference between the different tiles and enemies. It bearly takes up any space on your hard drive so it is definately good for weaker PCs. my only real issue with it is the controlls. The laptop i am using utilizes a nub mouse so i have a bit of trouble quickly moving it around the board. This game almost needs a touchscreen to be able to reach it's fullest potential. On a basic PC 5/10, on a Touchscreen 7/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is one of those that suprises you by how fun it actually is. On the cover it looks like just another match three game, but once you actually get into action you'll notice it's just so hectic that it's super fun and addictive. And then you notice that you can actually upgrade your character. To do this you must combine some specific blocks like wood. The upgrades generally provide you more time. So in the beginning a round might take just 15 seconds, but once you're upgraded to the max you'll be in the dungeons for minutes.

There's actuallyl not that much grinding if you know what you're doing. Just use the right potions and you'll get an extra amount of wood and stone. Complete the required objectives and you get more skill points. The game never gets frustratingly hard, nor is it too easy. It's just more down to luck of which blocks the game gives you. But once you learn to manage the field properly, even that shouldn't be a big problem.

But in the end, the game is just a match 3 game, so if you don't like them, this might not be for you. Although I'm one of those people who doesn't like them, and this was just a blast from the beginning to the end.\t\t\t\t\t
