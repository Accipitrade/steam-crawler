However, the further you get toward the finish, the harder it is to get there - as much as I enjoyed the gameplay along the way, I was honestly relieved when I beat the game because it took several sessions of aggressive play to really hit the scores I needed. End vote: thumbs down, because the game's fun factor and unique approach just doesn't quite outweigh the grind and weak design.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMatch-3 meets procedurally generated, forbiddingly difficult dungeon delving.

If you like spending hours matching 3 while bobbing your head to a kickin' tune, you will probably enjoy the core mechanic that this game gets you to grind for a good handful of hours to get all its unlockables and have a good chance of actually achieving the objective.

There's a lot of character here, from your little faceless protagonist's enthusiasm when he flip-jumps out of bed every morning to go dungeoneering to the array of monsters you face, although I have to admit I was slightly happier with them when I thought the "goblin cannon squad" was actually a diseased cat coughing some kind of deadly hairball at me.

You won't spend long with this game, but what time you spend will be spent doing more or less the same thing. Don't buy it if you don't already like match-3 puzzlers at least a little bit.

You will definitely be frustrated by bad luck when you just can't get enough swords, keys, or staves at the right moments, or realize you were desperately looking for a match when you could have just used the handy standby you had in your pocket and forgot about. However, if you're up for a little frustration in the search of fun and you like puzzles and old-fashioned Nethack-style dungeons, this is the game for you. I bought it at full price and had a lot of fun with it. It was a little like Super Hexagon in its ability to keep me playing for hours just trying to get that one run that does well enough.

If you're still not sure, go check out a YouTube video for some game footage. There's no plot to spoil, so you can still enjoy the game for yourself after watching someone else play it a bit, and that will probably tell you whether you want to try it or not.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith such an odd name, you would expect an odd game to go along with it, and you'd be correct. The game mixes RPG elements into a match-3 game that requires quick reflexes.

Your character awakens in a dungeon and the only way to escape is to enter a dungeon and survive until your score gets to 10,000,000. But you cannot do this right away as your score multiplier is too low, so you need to complete mini-challenges to unlock harder dungeons with higher multipliers so you can reach your goal.

Along the way you earn gold, experience, and wood which you use to upgrade your weapons, learn new skills, and upgrade the individual stores you purchase these things from. You get a real sense of progression from all this as you do progressively get stronger as you progress.

The game itself involves your character running across the top of the screen, and you needed to react by completing the matches. If there's a monster, you need to match swords and staves to do damage; locked doors and chests require you to match keys to open them; match other blocks to get resources to upgrade equipment. The game is simple, yet very addicting. The game itself features 8-bit retro graphics and a soundtrack that is very catchy. For the price, the game is absolutely worth it.\t\t\t\t\t

You create combos of certain items in your playspace to deal with what your character has encountered above your play area.
Locked door? Combine some key blocks to unlock the door.
Skeleton? Combine some sword blocks or magic staff blocks to attack it.
Going to be fighting monsters soon? Combine some shields to improve your characters armor and survivability.
You also collect resources via completing combos of wood, gold, and stone that you can use to upgrade your skills and equipment.

Great time waster if you like puzzle games or RPGs, I heard this game is on android/IOS which might be the better place to play it since I could see the touch controls being alot of fun for this type of game.\t\t\t\t\t
