\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun little match-three game. Sliding the entire row to make a match is a little more awkward than swapping tiles like you see in many other match-three games. If you don't grab the row at the right spot, it can be easy to run out of room to slide it over and make the match you need, forcing the row to snap back into place and making you try again. Once you get used to it, it's not too bad. The item system is also a little clunky -- if you aren't right up next to the monster when you use an attacking item, you run the risk of wasting it entirely. Aside from a few QoL problems, the game is fun and easy to sink a couple hours into. 7/10.\t\t\t\t\t
