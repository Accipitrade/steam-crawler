\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA rather simple match-3 with simple Rogue "lite" elements and a simple plot, Notice the pattern yet?

You awake in a stone chamber with a bunch of doors, all but one is locked off, entering the big door causes the game to begin. The game follows a runner format type of game, up top is your dude running from left to right, he'll run until he hits a obstacle (Monster or a lock) and you match what you need from the bottom puzzle board.

Sword & staves do damage while the keys unlock locks, also in the board are armor tiles, item tiles and resource tiles. You keep this up until you lose by the screen catching up with you, which then causes you to awake back in the stone chamber.

You can spend the resources you find to open and upgrade the rooms which in turn boost your abillity to play the game,
rinse & repeat until you get 10,000,000 score and you win the game, simple.

There are milestones and challenges to complete that give you some resources and multipliers adding your end goal.


All in all it's a short but addicting match-3, nothing amazing but simply solid in its execution.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly bought this game because one of the reviews said it was like Huniepop without the babes. It's not really the same other than the matching moves side to side, but it's the entire row or column instead of a single piece. Nonetheless the game is pretty challenging to get all the achievements, especially "The Jose." I was able to do it twice, but the first time did not register (others have had this issue as well), so it took me another 12hours to get it again. The game can be easily completed in seven hours to get all of the achievements. A drawback is that one does not realise they get their achievements unless they log off the game and then receive them on Steam.

The game gets repetitive and leveling the weapon, staff, shield, and skills gets tedious. But it gets challenging when monsters get stronger along with you. Chance of getting good drops is also a large factor in success for this game.

I would recommend this game if you like to get achievements and enjoy matching puzzle games. Definitely just get it when it is on sale.
