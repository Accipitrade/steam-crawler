\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10,000,000 is another puzzle game on Steam, with an interesting RPG-like twist. Funnily enough when I got started on Steam, this was the first Steam game that caught my eye, and I'm glad it's one of the first Steam games I've completed.

The main goal of the game is to score 10,000,000 points in a single dungeon run, and these points can be gained mainly by simple "match three"-like moves. There's only a few tiles in the board: Sword for Melee attacks, Wand for Magic Attacks which have a chance to cause special effects like poison or freeze, Shields for... well shields, keys for opening doors or chests, and so on. You have to match quick though. You won't get 10,000,000 points easily on your first playthrough, but along the way you will gain wood, stones, gold and experience every time you run some dungeon runs. These can be used to fix rooms (and upgradable) to level up and boost your character to make beating monsters easier.

Soundtrack despite having 3 music tracks is pretty good to listen to although can be repetitive, the pixel graphics are pretty good for its time. The game can be completed pretty easily once you get used to it and it's pretty short.
