\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10,000,000

10,000,000. An odd name for a game, yes. But it is also the purpose of the game. To reach 10,000,000 points and escape the dungeon is your ultimate goal. So is the run worth it, or should you just pass this one up and find a new number to satisfy you? Let’s find out.

This one will be short and sweet, like the game itself turns out to be. Well, maybe not so sweet as it does tend to end with you dying. Quite a lot in fact. The gameplay is the tried and true match-3 formula we all know and love. With some minor combat elements tossed in, of course. Match three or more swords or staves and you will do damage to your enemies. Beat them and you continue along your merry way and earn more points. Along the way, you will collect gold and various resources, which can be used to upgrade your weapons, magic, and armor. Upgrading these will in turn allow you to get further in your run, collect more gold, upgrade more, and so on. The end goal is to reach 10,000,000 points and earn your freedom.

If you’re a fan of addictive match-3 games and enjoy some RPG-lite gameplay, you won’t go wrong with this one. The game clocks in at around 5-10 hours, and is well worth the small price tag.
