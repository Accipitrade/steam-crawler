\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe quint-essential Match-3 game for a short break or 20 minutes of fun. You see your little avatar running from left to right on the top and depending on what they encounter you need to match a certain symbol. E.g. you run into a door or a chest you need to match keys.

Runs are short and broken up by very light management in which you decide how to level up your character. E.g. matching swords now does more melee damage. Each run also comes with three side objectives like "Match 5 blocks" or "Reach score X" - that sort of thing.

The overall goal is to reach a score of 10000000 hence the name. Good luck!\t\t\t\t\t
