\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWorth the 99 cents for an easy perfect game.

The game itself was interesting. I love the idea just not some of the implementation.

Far too often I was not able to do anything because the game doesnt provide you with the tools you need when you need them. If you typically hate the RNG you will hate this game.

Oh sweet the super-mega ultra combo just destroyed everything I needed to complete the level, awesome. The bonus is YOU LOSE! Stupid design choices hurt what would have been a really solid game.

I would only buy this for achievement whoring purposes, but its not the fastest title for that either. 4 hours in and I only have 2 achievements. This isnt my genre of choice, so you may be faster.

I recommend this only because, despite the mentioned flaws, I have not been annoyed to the point of rage quitting. This is mostly because the game is not hard, you are pretty much at the mercy of the random number gen. You get ♥♥♥♥ on for three levels, then you manage a level thats endless chains and easy level up. Just have to take it as it comes, Skill is rarely a factor.

Plenty of player upgrades to make the game worth playing. I do not regret the ~1 dollar I wasted on this. I will take it to perfect game status and then uninstall forever, satisfied.\t\t\t\t\t
