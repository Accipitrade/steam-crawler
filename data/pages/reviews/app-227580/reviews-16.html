\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA really fun and short little game (disregard the 220+ hours, most of that is from the game being left on in the background over several days.) in the match three style of gameplay. Essentially the top fourth of the screne is used for showing monsters, chests, and doors. The bottome three fourths of the screne is where all of the tile matching takes place. There are 6 tile types. There is the sword tile, which does the most damage and has the highest chance to crit. The staff/wand icon which is a ranged attack and has a percent chance to inflict some kind of status aliment. There is the armor icon which allows you to take more hits before your run ends. The chest icon can give you useful 1 use items that do damage, or swaps tiles for others. The key icon is pretty obvious...it unlocks things, chests take two keys (can be changed through an upgrading system) and one key for doors. Finally there are the resourse icons (stone and wood). You can use the resourses to give yourself premanent buffs, like making chests open in one key. There is also a quest system per "floor" of the dungeon. The quests yield rewards like gold, which is used to upgrade your sword, armor, and staff icons, among other things. Quests also provide you with purple essense which is the currency fro the permanent buffs.

All in all there is actully quite a nice little bit of depth, and for the price it is well worth it. I would say the average completion time is maybe 8-12 hours depending on hour good you are at match 3s.\t\t\t\t\t
