\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: the game does what it says it does. If you like the trailer, you'll probably like the game, since they show you the entire game in the trailer.

Pros:
+ interesting spin on match-3 game play by incorporating the dungeon elements
+ fun pixelated style
+ very catchy and appropriate music
+ replayability (it's match-3, so pretty endless if you want it to be, but also has an "end" with the gain freedom at 10mil goal)
+ some interesting acheivements available to encourage replay and offer additional goals/challenges, if you're into that sort of thing

Cons:
- can feel a bit grindy at times to get what you need to reach the 10mil goal, but I didn't mind since there was a goal/end that was not too difficult to get to
- while I personally like the music, in the spirit of fairness, it can get repetitive. it might have been nice to have different dungeon music as you level up, since they did take the time to make the monsters different as you go\t\t\t\t\t
