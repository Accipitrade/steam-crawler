\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely fun and unique pictures which satisfy my need for complex pictures, but not in the way that most of the other images do so. It's not complex in that there are a lot of colors right next to each other, but complex in the way that the pictures are shaped, which I find to be equally as engaging as the other pixelated images in the other packs.

I especially love the last image in the pack, being the largest image that they have released to date, which is exciting.

The only downside is that the actual number of pixels isn't accurately said in the size of each picture because many of them have a lot of squares cut out of the image, leading to less pixels than is actually said in main menu when you select the image.

Definitely worth the money, so buy it!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe rumors are true, this is one of the best packs yet. Not a single image here that was unfun or frustrating, and even the smaller images can be decently engaging. The last three pictures here all look great and while they're kinda pixelly at points it's in the "meshes of 3-4 colors" sort of way instead of "every single pixel is a different color" kind of way and they're all broken into smaller chunks of the image anyways, so basically, yeah, ends up being a pretty good time. I was worried this was going to devolve into blandness like some of the Coloring Game images did, but this game's art is generally better-looking at its best and this pack is one of the two so far I could recommend without any hangups whatsoever.\t\t\t\t\t
