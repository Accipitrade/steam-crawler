\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't buy this game, at least for now. The current state of the game is so bad you can't imagine.

Discovered after playing for 5 seconds that walking into any wall let's you see through it made my expectations very low for this game.

Anyway let's dive in, so this game is full of :

- terrible map design (stretched textures everywhere, holes in the map that makes you fall
into the void)

- goofy monsters that don't see you unless you go in front of them, they look so bad and
destroys the ambiance)

If you thought playing with your friends was a good idea, this game is full of :

- need to restart the game everytime the game is hosted and bc we get softlocked a lot it's
annoying

- spectating mode let's you choose between playing again or headache type camera on
your friends bodies

- game is not synchronised with all players, some events are only seen by the host for example the long ass pipe level where only the host sees the entity. Or even sometimes doors are opened for my friend but not for me and he can't go in

- some levels by the time your friends are joining the game you get killed in repeat

- someone being killed = all player's camera to go in a direction

If you care about the mechanics of the game, this game is full of :

- awful hitboxes

- you don't see ♥♥♥♥ the whole game, sometimes the flashlight is flashlighting nothing
sometimes my screen is whole white at 2 am

- moon type gravity jumping cause you to hit the ceiling

- this game features a unique mechanic : dying for no reason

If you care about the design of the game, this game is full of :

- some areas of the map gives you an instant fish eye effect for no apparent reason

- you can't change sensitivity and sound bar is poorly made

- menu buttons work when they want to

- background music that restarts itself without transitions breaking the little immersion you still had


This game has great potential, for real they were times where immersion was kinda nice and the game was good looking, but for a short period of time unfortunately.
For me it's a steam refund type backrooms game\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst level had promise but it goes severely downhill from there.
The warehouse level requiring you to crouch to avoid the monster becomes extremely tedious while you search for the key card. The key card can also vary its position depending on player POV.
The pipe dreams level is extremely long for no good reason. While backroom levels being extremely long is lore accurate, it still makes for awful game-play.
The chase level gives you moments after spawning to run, combine that with player collision, you tend to die before you can get your bearings. However, the death trigger is insanely loud, and can repeatedly spam on the same person, requiring us to turn the game down.

The office level is okay-ish, the doors do take the piss though. Having a door swing towards you when it opens can be so annoying while you're being chased. The parkour is so janky, sometimes you slide off the object you're jumping to, nearby players who fail the jump can somehow suck you into the hole with them. You can also survive the fall, softlocking the game and having to start again.

The hotel level is also just okay. The doors here are even heavier and require you to sprint into them to get them to open, and monsters path in and out of the rooms, opening up the chance you die without actually seeing what happened. You can also walk up again the walls to look through them rather than go through the door.

The water level is the last one we attempted. So many sections of this area had spots where water hadn't been placed, so you have these hard cuts at the edge of the water. There are parts of the map that simply do not have walls, as if they had been forgotten to implement, there are also tiny gaps in the floor that can cause you to fall out of the map. There is no death, so you once again get softlocked.

Overall, this game feels like it wasn't tested for potential problems that can arise and levels were rushed out and made excessively long to make it feel bigger and more fleshed out than it is.\t\t\t\t\t
