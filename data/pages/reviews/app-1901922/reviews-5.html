\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI see a lot of people who are complaining about the price of the map, and I can't help but feel that these are people who are newer to the game who haven't watched them release several new maps for free over the past few years. I am totally fine with the developers releasing some new content (for money *gasp*) if it means that they will continue to add to and improve the game. And yeah, it's $3. Big whoop. I paid $5 for the game on sale originally so I'm up to $8 spent for 250+ hours of game play. Besides, if one person has the map, then all of their friends can play it too. Something that they didn't have to do for a paid DLC but did anyway. As for the map itself, my group of friends agree that it's one of the better maps that they've released in awhile. It feels more polished than previous maps and seems overall to be less janky, once you learn the holes.

Overall, I'm really happy that they released new content and I hope that they continue to do so, even if it releases behind a paywall. Developers deserve to be paid for their work.
