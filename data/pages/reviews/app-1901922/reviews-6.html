Golf With Your Friends - Bouncy Castle Course,
is a multi themed medieval styled golf course.

My Review
I found the course to be quite immersive, taking me into the world of ancient kings within the 400 AD-1400 AD era. Including such historic accurate references to kings (His Majesty Ginger Bread Man IV) residing in his colorful castle adorned by golf balls. (I don't know if they played golf back then, but I'll ignore this inaccuracy)

Verdict
Overall, I'd recommend this to my friends, co-workers, family, dogs, fellow history lovers, the boys, my girlfriend, the golf community and in general - the general population.
