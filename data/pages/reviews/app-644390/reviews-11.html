\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLords of Waterdeep is a well-designed worker placement / resource management game. It is accessible enough for new players, but deep enough for experienced players to develop complex strategies. It also has a clear turn-limit mechanism, which means that games never go on too long.

This is an excellent computer version of the game. In some ways, it works better than the board game version, because it manages the many small administrative details and Plot Quest effects for you. There are also excellent tutorials, which are a must for those who are new to the game, and very helpful for veterans who need to learn how to use the interface. That interface is well-designed, and I have encountered no significant defects.

Highly recommended!

--- Dev Rossik
