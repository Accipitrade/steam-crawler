My one complaint is the UI. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun board game. Play as a masked lord, running the city from behind the scenes: send out your underlings to complete tasks, and hire adventurers in order to complete quests and schemes to score points. At the end of the game, the player with the highest score wins.

Simple concept, and easy to learn: was ready to play after completing the in game tutorial (which walks you through the first half of a game).

The game keeps its variety from randomly assigning each player a lord, and randomly distributing quests, and action cards at the beginning of the game. This ensures every game will be different, and that your strategies will have to adapt to the board. Dice free. Nice concept.

Single player works well. Pick this up if you want to try the game before purchasing the actual board game, want to get in some practice between sessions with friends, or have long distance gaming sessions with friends from afar.\t\t\t\t\t
