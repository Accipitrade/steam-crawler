\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed the board game a lot, seems like a fairly true implementation.

They did make a mistake: FAQ says you can complete a quest any time on your turn, not just after you place a meeple, but that's a small issue.

If you like worker placement games like Caylus or any number of others, this is a good one.

Note: The undermountain expansion is reasonably balanced, but some of the lords from SKullport have a hard time winning (little control over their own fate in MP due to the nature of the Corruption Track) and some of the rules for playing with both expansions together (ejecting some buildings and quests) can make a very uneven playing field if you happen to need things that were ejected. Those flaws exist in the board game though as well.

I'd recommend this. My wife and I both like it.\t\t\t\t\t
