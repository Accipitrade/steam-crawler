\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood digital translation of the physical game.

Found a few bugs and the game seems basic on features (like missing customising the intrigue or hero deck or even designating heroes). The matchmaking/lobby interface is terrible but once figured out it seems fine if the intention is to play amongst friends. I prefer to play this with friends, or hot-seat rather than solo-queue/duo-queue to avoid the tedium of finding and starting a match. This is not a game to be played over days (like the system allows) and shouldn't be designed as such.

Mimicking the atmosphere of a board game session is up to the player and their mates and I'd always recommend physical over digital due to tactility. Still a decent port of a great game.\t\t\t\t\t
