\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNice implementation of the board game. They managed to get a lot of game elements like cards, tiles and cubes arranged in a way that makes it relatively easy to see everything. The only complaint I have is that it is very easy to think you have finished your turn while you still need to click (another) confirm box. This means everyone can be waiting, all thinking someone else is holding the game up.

The game itself is a worker placement type game with limited player interaction. The ability to buy new buildings (worker spaces) is interesting and it's fun to see people agonize over whether they should use the best space for them at the cost of helping the person who built it. The Dungeons & Dragons theme is well done with lots of background material on the cards (which you just ignore, only looking at the icons, if you wish.\t\t\t\t\t
