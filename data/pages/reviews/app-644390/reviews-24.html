\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLords of Waterdeep is a game that I can recommend.

For those familiar with the actual board game, this PC version as a very faithful recreation of it. For those that aren't familiar with the game it is frankly easy to pick up and learn.

The only real issue I had were at first the wonky design choices for the interface. The interface mostly is a push button that will bring up your resources, cards, etc. but to play things requires a movement to a designated space in the middle of the board that is not at first clearly defined. Further, you do have the ability to see similar things of your opponents (as you would in the real game) but the areas to click for them were just oddly done.

In all it is a fun competitive game that is easy to pick up but difficult to master.\t\t\t\t\t
