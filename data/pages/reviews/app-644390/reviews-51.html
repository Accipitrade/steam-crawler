\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game works fine on my old PC, and was a quick download.

The game took a while to learn, but once I got the hang of it, there wasn't much more to it, than play a few games until I learned how to beat the AI.

The online game works well, with plenty of opponents, but some online games require the expansions. If you delay in completing your turns, you may forfeit the game. So pick a game with a long time span, such as 45 days, and aim to finish it over a few days. The turn based system works well.

I chose this game due to an interest in D&D and I was disappointed that the game didn't really have the flavour of D&D. The "adventurers" are such abstract pawns they are unrelatable, and the lords are really not a big part of the game.

The game feels like Monopoly. It works well in this format, but as the computer does most of the thinking and organising, there's not much to think about once you've got the hang of it.\t\t\t\t\t
