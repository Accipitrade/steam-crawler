You have a finite number of actions and some of those you want to take might be taken by other players.

Take resource units / play an action card / take a new quest are amongst the actions.

I have played a lot of these type of games over the years - this one I think is a very good example and even better at time of writing on sale!

After playing 2 games I thought this game was great and purchased both of the expansions (again on sale at time of writing)

Those expansions add further options to the game and with the Skullport expansion the addition of some great actions - which come with the trade off of gaining corruption - which in the main will impact negatively your end game score.

If you like this type of game I think this one well worth a look.\t\t\t\t\t
