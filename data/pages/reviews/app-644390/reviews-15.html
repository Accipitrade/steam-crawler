It's a great card game even against the AI. I have sunk plenty of time so far and can say with certainty you won't regret playing. Get all your friends to buy it and play some online matches or just play alone it's a lot of fun.

Game play - You build up your randomized lord and try to assimilate a strategy of earning the most victory points. Do you want to focus on 1 type of quest? Buy all the buildings? Do all the intrigue and try to just put a stop to your opponent? So far I have not found any "insta" win strategy, you don't know what Lord your opponents have so they could be just trying to take your cards you're going for or have a similar strategy.... All in all very good game.\t\t\t\t\t
