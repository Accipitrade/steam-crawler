\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn excellent adaptation of a great board game - the addition of sounds, music, and auto point tracking is really nice.
Update circa 250 hours logged: This game is a blast to play with my wife and I love not having to setup, takedown, or store all three volumes. One thing I have noticed that I find, NGL very distasteful, is the whipping sounds in the slave auction area. Busy market sounds would have been okay but this was just in bad taste on dev's part. The AI also has a particular weakness - pretty sure it was developed around the base game but not tuned enough with expansions, particularly relating to the 40 point quest cards - IMO these should be an autograb by even easy AI - all AI should hatedraft even if it can't do the objective unless its the 8th round. Reason being, the 40 point quests are by far the easiest way to win. I can safely say that this game while it has enough randomization to keep it interesting for MANY years, it is a highly skill based game that requires constant recalculations when buildings drop.\t\t\t\t\t
