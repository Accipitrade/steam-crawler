\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great board game, and the adaptation is good looking, but the user experience is horrible.
* The interface is inconsistent about when you have to click a green button to advance or clicking on something has an instant effect.
* Sometimes a green button is for switching what card you are looking at.
* There is a player total turn limit timer, which could be nice, but if a player disconnects, their timer continues to countdown with no way to pause it while you wait for them to reconnect.
* You have to click on many things for example, to zoom in on a card to read the description, where it would make more sense to simply hover over it.
* There is very little contextual help in the game, so you will spend a lot of time staring at the screen before you figure out to click on your player icon to view your lord.\t\t\t\t\t
