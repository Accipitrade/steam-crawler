\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood oldie point and click adventure - puzzle case solving nostalgia game.
Better have your puzzle solving senses high on this one. Because in this humoruous point and click adventure puzzle game, Sam & Max Hit the Road ,Jack (and no, the song is not included) brings you the toughest case to crack, SPOILER ALERT -> In hunt of a missing Bigfoot Bruno and his girlfriend Trixie the Giraffe-Necked Girl.


Not very long game to beat, for average 5-6 hours or more, if you're struggling with some parts.
Some parts are well made, funny and good humour also hidden discoveries are included. I personally liked interesting things I can remember are The wishing well and end credits, typically end credits are boring, but this one surprisingly wasn't, atleast for me.

Mini-games were included to allow players to take a break from solving the main game's puzzles.
Music is well suited for the game.

I remember this game many years ago when playing this on old PC with no sounds and did not get far because lack of english and no internet back then to check guides in case of getting stuck.
Now being able to hear audio, it felt amazing and something that was sadly missing back then.

Fun fact: The game was one of the first to incorporate full voice talent; the two title characters were voiced by professional voice actors Bill Farmer and Nick Jameson.
