Old School Point & Click with all the bad and good that entails

Gameplay: 4 (It is old school in every sense of the word. Obtuse puzzles galore! If that is your thing, you won't be disappointed. I personally found it tedious.)
Story: 9 (Humor is very funny in my opinion. Right up my alley. Probably the strongest part of the game.)
Graphics: 7 (I like the old art style though there is some crustiness to it at the same time.)
Content Depth: 6 (If the game didn't have obtuse puzzles it would be even shorter)
Replay Value: 5 (If you've played this, you've seen anything worth seeing.)
Voice Acting: 9 (It's Sam and Max! Excellent voice acting! Great delivery.)
Music: 8 (Well suited for the game IMO.)

Overall: 6.9/10 (Back in its day it would probably rank much better. I would recommend this if some of the puzzles weren't so annoying and obtuse by modern standards. I would say maybe try it on sale and, if the puzzles bother you, look up a guide if you want to experience the excellent humor, or watch the banter on YouTube or something.)\t\t\t\t\t
