\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFallout 3 - Broken Steel Review

Finish what they started in this all new Brotherhood Vs Enclave DLC.

Please note that this Review may contain spoilers from the Base game

Some key points that this game add on has to offer:

1.\tLevel Cap increased to 30.
2.\tNew areas.
3.\tContinuation of the Main Story.
4.\tNew Perks and Achievements.
5.\tTesla Cannon.

Replayability:

This DLC continues the main Campaign: At the end of Project Purity your character sacrificed themselves if you choose that option, but with this DLC that ending was altered, and instead of dying you were merely knocked unconscious, waking up later with a continuation of the story line.

MGR Gaming’s Conclusions:

Fallout 3 - Broken Steel continues the story brilliantly in this new campaign add on. With this new story you finally get to deliver the death blow of your choice… the game is all about choice, and you get to make a pretty big one here.

Explore some new areas with the story line, with these new areas comes a few new perks and a level cap increase, giving you the opportunity to level up all the way to 30… Now that alone makes this little DLC worth it.

The Tesla Cannon is just there to sweeten the pot… Have fun.
