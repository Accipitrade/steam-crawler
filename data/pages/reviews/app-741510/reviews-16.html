\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEnjoyable game. Like hotline miami with better graphics. Soundtrack isn't quite on par with hotline miami but it's decent all the same.
Gameplay is your standard run and gun, activating lunges and bullettime to make your way through the increasingly challenging levels filled with ever more enemy soldiers, that get better equipped as time goes on. Get hit by one bullet and you're out, much like your enemies, but the penalty is only that you have to replay the mission. And when they're so short there's not much of a loss (2-3 minutes tops), which encourages you to keep battering away at a mission until you make it through unscathed, and that feeling of achievement once you do clear a tough level is definitely there.
Story is pretty basic. There's a crime lord. He's bad. You need to shoot him in the face.
The game provides a decent challenge over the ~4-6 hours of gametime at hard difficulty. The one slipup is the fact that boss levels are so much easier than the standard missions - I don't understand why. The game's second last mission is a masterfully crafted gauntlet of fearsome machine gun toting foes, and then the last mission is a 30s dance off with slightly more bullet spongy standard enemy who goes down after a few blasts from your, at this stage, fully upgraded uzi. Bit of an anticlimax. Especially as its more or less identical to the 3 boss battles before!
But I'm possibly being too harsh. This seems to have been a one-man effort which is very commendable. Definitely recommended on sale!\t\t\t\t\t
