\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExcessive lighting and bloom don't actually set a tone here, they only excuse otherwise generic visuals, straight from an Unreal Engine template.

Constantly trying to be cinematic with slow-motion rainy scenes and face close-ups, only to come off as an amateur, Hollywood knockoff.

Gameplay can be fun, when Max Payne meets Hotline Miami and only then. The rest of the time, its less precise controls make it feel like a washed down version of the latter.

Soundtrack feels more like a deep-web-queer-rave (/respect) than whatever other form of bad*ssery it was shooting for.

15 minutes is all I could take! It doesn't seem that bad, if you can ignore all that it's trying really hard to be.\t\t\t\t\t
