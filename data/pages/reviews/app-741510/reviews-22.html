The game doesn't generates various situation, but the game has repetitive levels and repetitive BOSSes, generic music that you you will forget. But the game almost masterpiece, when low iq guy review it after playing 0.3 hours. So if you are goning to play it only 0.3 hours, you can buy it without doubts.

It's OK to play the game with slow-mo 1 chapter per day without trying to unlock achievements and do challenges. But if you try to do it, it will be pain in the @$$. I had the problem with "Perfect aim". It means 1 shot = 1 kill. And it's not easy even with slowmotion sometimes, which makes game unfair hardcore.

Here the problems I noticed while completing the challenge:
1 - You can't aim through the all sight area, only in the circle around you. IT makes harder to shoot the enemies on edge of your screen. They shot perfectly as soon as they appear on your screen and sometimes behind it. IT'S NOT HARD TO COMPLETE LEVELS WITH SLO-MO HARD FOR "PERFECT AIM"

2 - AI aims likes cheaters and can shoot you in the pixel. And that;s why this game doesnt give you chance to make mistake at all. For example, in Hotlime Miami 1 or Mr.Shifty you could miss the enemy, but instantly throw the weapon in him or quickly take another and shoot or just have time to run behind the corner. In this game you don't have the time if you miss. The AI shoots so quick and so perfect that you will not avoid bullet. IT' MAKES HARD TO COMPLETE LEVELS WITHOUT SLO-MO HARD AND NOT MUCH FOR "PERFECT AIM"

E.g. you stand in one room with dual UZI (has the highest fire rate in game) and enemy in the other room. There is a straight line between you. You start to shoot through door. The 1st bullet opens it, the 2nd kills enemy...BUUUUT!!! Between this bullets there is another one. And it adressed to you by enemy. So if you stood when started shooting, it kills you. I won't say what usually happens with 4-6 enemies in the room

3 - Visually the game is not good. There are too much bloom sometimes, so you can't see bullets. Some objects on the map looks like you can shoot through them or above them. There broken cars and some boxes that you can shoot above. And there are boxes that are tall and bullets stall in them. IT'S NOT HARD TO COMPLETE LEVELS WITH SLO-MO HARD FOR "PERFECT AIM"

4 - There enemies which can roll like you, and they are invisible like you. It generates some gameplay problems even with slow-mo, because:
a) They do it randomly - you can kill 25 ememies with 25 bullets, and the last one will suddenly roll...the result is missing shot...or not...sometimes I completed level with "perfect aim", sometimes it didn't count (shoot in the begining of roll - you missed, shoot in the middle of roll, the game forgive your BAD AIM). I restarted the level in 30-40% of all times bacasue of roll to complete challenge;
b) they cancel "roll" animation in 50% of times. The animation itself is ragged (sudden start\\sudden stop). Without slow-mo at gives you another sudden death;
c) Bullet dissappears when you you shoot in rolling enemy. It make Very very very very nervious is situations when you activate slow-mo, there is one enemy that rolls and another one behind him, you aim at him, but you won't kill him, because the game thinks you shoot at rolling guy...and dosn;t matter that reticle is NOT ON HIM.

5 - There is no any "feedback" or "impact" (or call it whatever) on your roll. Sometimes you finish the roll, make one step and want to roll again, but in fact you can't cause of delay.Plus there are little gaps in the start and in the end of the roll when you can shoot. And AI takes any chances to catch you.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has some cool aspects that remind me of games like Hotline Miami, but falls short of being a recommendable game. I would give this game a 5 or 6 out of ten. Here's why:

Pros:
+ The Music by Professor Kliq is bumping
+ The dodge mechanic makes the fast paced action unique and new
+ I love the aesthetic of neo noir/ Hong Kong noir; this game pulls it off well
+ The level design match the aesthetic as well; the rooftop levels are the most dandy
+ Different color of jackets dictate which weapons they are carrying which is useful for planning your attacks ahead

Cons:
- Only four weapons and they are very simplistic
- No melee ability or melee weapons
- Boss fight levels are mostly the same thing each time, no variation other than the look of the level, but not the design of the level
- The slow mo and dodge used at the same time makes you an unstoppable force, breaks the game to make it easier to complete the hardest levels
- The rifle and shotgun are under powered compared to the pistols and sub machine gun
- The challenges are the same for every level, I wish that maybe there were 5-6 different challenges that were alternating for more variety
- Rigid aiming

Get this if you love Hotline Miami and this is on sale for $5-$10.
