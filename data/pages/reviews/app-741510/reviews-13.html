However, THKM is focusing on gameplay, rather than story or narration; and it delivers this smooth!

The "slow motion" feels like the old Max Payne games and a huge life saver.

The most frustrating thing (other than dying 100 times in every level) is the bullets. You can't rely on your "favorite" weapon since it's going to consume your bullet to zero in a second and you're going to start collecting pistols or shotguns like a mushroom on the ground.

If you find this game in sale, don't miss it. Cheers.\t\t\t\t\t
