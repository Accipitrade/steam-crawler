\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHotline Miami meets John Woo's Hard Boiled.
Does that sound cool? That's because it is.

Hard Boiled -
Leap through the air like Chow Yun Fat blasting bad guys with duel pistols and dodge bullets in slow motion. When the shooting stops everything in the room is either destroyed or covered in blood. It feels like you're directing your own 1980's Hong Kong action flick, it's super chaotic and stylish.
Like Hotline Miami everything dies in 1 hit including you and you will die A LOT but the level restarts in seconds so you can try again.

Runny Eggs -
It's pretty short at around 6-7 hours so i'd say try and pick it up in a sale.
The occasional short cinematics look cool but the dialogue is pretty ♥♥♥♥♥♥ like it was a bad translation or was an afterthought.
The boss fights are all the same, it would have been cool to have each one be their own unique thing but whatever, they only last about a minute.

Now i wish Stranglehold was on Steam.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: I would recommend it only when its on sale with discounts and for genre fans.

Graphics: looks good but optimization is very bad.
Gameplay: its usual like in other hotline-miami-like games. Its ok and I played it for some hours but there are no original ideas, just shooting and diving in slowmo in small levels. Only four weapons to choose but you can upgrade them a bit though. At the end of story you have lots of points for upgrading but all 4 weapons are already upgraded so those stars look useless. Very poor secondary tasks (time goal, aiming goal and no slowmo goal) and poor reward for them (see upgrades).
Story: nothing interesting, cutscenes are boring and terrible — models are just sitting or standing and do nothing and "talk".
Controls: it's ok but hero makes dives some weird directions from time to time and you die because of it, that's annoying.
Other: a lot of bugs. Terrible bugs when you are in window mode. You can't click Skip, Resume and other buttons from time to time so you have to restart game. Every second game launch I press 'Continue Story' button but it freezes at some info screen and you can't press anything and have to restart the whole game. Idea with roof jumping is nice but its realization is bad. Also lots of small items at level you stuck in and you die because you stuck in some chair you didn't notice.
Wanna 'perfect aim' for this level? F U, get an invisible wall behind enemy.
Wanna weird ragdoll enemies whirling around like tornado? YES.
Wanna crosshair you won't see in most levels? YES, gimme two!
Wanna stuck in objects and die bc you can't move from bullets? YEAH!
Wanna 25 same levels which have only 2-3 patterns of design? Nice.
What about same bosses every time. You got it.

And the cherry on top: if you restart a game to get some achievements for 1st and 2nd day (bc it doesn't clear if you should beat em with no single death level by level) it zeroes your total kill counter so you have to kill 10000 enemies from start. FUNKIN GREAT.\t\t\t\t\t

I really recommend this game, VRESKI made a pretty damn good job.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGeneral
This is a challenging but fair game in which you are outnumbered and 1 bullet can kill you. you have slow motion which drains with use and fills when not. but its not impossible to defeat stages without using it.

Content
each day (related to story) there is 6 stages and a boss battle with 5 days total. so there is a good amount of content to experience.
you have 3 difficulties to choose from. easy,normal and hard. an issue i have with this is you cannot switch difficulties AND keep your stage/upgrade progress on other difficulties. you have to each it separately on each difficulty.
the story has cut scenes, in 3D. not much to say about them.

Weapons
you can upgrade your weapons (there are 4 weapons in total) while this is a nice feature. at the same time, because upgraded weapons are likely a little better than non upgraded weapons. you may find yourself using some more than others instead of freely picking early game.
weapons are upgraded by using stars you obtain in each stages challenges. all stages have the same 3 challenges.
-time
-no slow motion
-only hit enemies.
there are more than enough obtainable stars to max all weapons (20 extra to be exact)

Overall
if you like something that takes some effort and is intense and you are fine with failing. then i recommend this.\t\t\t\t\t
