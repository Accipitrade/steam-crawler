\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really like this game, but Ubisoft's practice of awful DRM makes this game a terrible value. Every time you start the game you need to wait for Ubisoft to load into their server and download some annoying update. I dislike but can live with DRM on AAA brand new games, but this sort of DRM on old $15 games is ridiculous. Either do the thing they're trying to prevent, or buy this from a seller that does not require awful DRM. If I had known how intrusive the DRM for this game is, I would not have bought it.

I am 11 hours into a game, just got home from work, and am currently waiting a few minutes for the verification code needed to log into my Ubisoft account so I can play a game I bought and downloaded.

This game is a lot of fun. As someone new to the series, I will definitely check out more of this series and will buy non DRM versions (unless they're new and really good). I recommend playing on a very easy setting if you're also new to the series. It's really fun watching your city grow and learning how to manage all the different resources is pretty fun. From what I've played so far, there seems to be a pretty high skill ceiling too. Playing on hard would be much harder and would probably play way differently than easy.\t\t\t\t\t
