\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best Anno games and probably a good starting point for those who are completely fresh to the series, as it's not quite as deeply complex as the later ones. This is quite a detailed game though with a whole lot of content to go through, it's the best series of games if you want your citybuilding to be a little more than just that. Acquire resources, set up trade lines between islands, set up your resource lines and so on so forth. Go through the population tiers and fullfill their needs step by step, ever expanding and growing further with more complicated needs. It's very satisfying, but build with efficiency in mind for your industry.

Beyond this one i'd recommend Anno 2070, as well as probably the best one now; Anno 1800. Anno 2205 is a little different, i'd look into it more if you're thinking about that one.\t\t\t\t\t
