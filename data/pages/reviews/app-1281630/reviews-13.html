\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEasily one of the best strategy games one can play these days (don't look at my hours, I've played this game before steam was a thing). If you are interested in city-builders, this is a must play!

Gameplay is exciting, challenging, and you can set your games up according to your wish, alllowing you to play the way you want. For example you can choose if you want other AI players, and what diffculty they should be, as well as choose your own goals (like building monuments, defeating enemies).
The only issue i can think of is land combat, which is quite slow and IMO boring, but that's only a minor part of the game, which can be almost entirely ignored, with the correct settings at game start.

The campaign is decent too, serving as a long tutorial, and giving some background on most of the characters you meet, but you don't have to play it.

It also looks and sounds great, even by 2022 standards.
Bonus points for being afaik bug-free and having a good german translation.\t\t\t\t\t
