\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have found this revised version of Anno 1404 a lot of fun and a really good way to relax.
The soundtrack is beautiful and the graphics are so much better than the original games, which i both own.
The added Multiplayer does work well, but after a few hours of gameplay, there is a current bug that causes your game to become unsynced. i really hope the devs find a fix for it.
Singleplayer scenarios will keep you busy many hours as you can either play a continuous scenario or a campaign scenario. Campaign scenarios have certain targets you need to complete. Continuous scenarios are a little different, as you can edit the scenario by changing settings that either raise or lower the difficulty of the scenario.
Lots of strategy to learn where building is concerned and managing tax rates so you can support your colony. The map is made of islands which have pre allocated resources and a ? means you can allocate a seed to plant on that island by buying it from the relevant NPC.
I have 50 hours of gameplay so far, mostly multiplayer and look forward to many more.\t\t\t\t\t
