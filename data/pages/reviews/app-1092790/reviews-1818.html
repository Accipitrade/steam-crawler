(longer version) I'm not a fan of turn-based games, especially card games, but, this game makes it fun and interesting.
Yes, it is hard sometimes, yes it is frustrating sometimes, but that's the beauty of it. The fact that you have to rely on a random assortment of your choosing makes it feel like you're really the one in charge, while simultaneously making your failures seem like the fault of someone else. The interactions you have with the figures of the game might seem linear, yeah, but, they matter. As you play through the game, more and more things become apparent, and clear, and distinct.

Long story short; this game is really something else. One of the best games I've ever played, let alone a game I like that's within the category of games I generally hate. (side note: the soundtrack is also fantastic.)\t\t\t\t\t
