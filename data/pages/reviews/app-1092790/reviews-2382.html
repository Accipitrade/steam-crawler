\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood lord, this game is just incredible from start to finish! From its captivating plot and atmosphere to its deeply satisfying gameplay, Inscryption had me hooked like fresh fish the whole way through.

While it would be easy to sing the praises about this work of art, I DO have one problem with the game: its lack of postgame content. While the gameplay is most certainly great, enough to justify a second or even third playthrough, the story is just such a large part of the game that makes you wish there was something extra, like unlockable modes focused purely on gameplay. It's so satisfying to play, I just wish there was more incentive to keep playing after the credits roll.

With that aside, I can't recommend this enough; however, try to stay as blind as possible: the story is an amazing experience, so please don't spoil it for yourself.

Edit: With the completion of Kaycee's Mod, my complaints about postgame content have been addressed. :)\t\t\t\t\t
