\r\n\t\t\t\t\tive had several people tell me that this game's lore and mystery hunting aspect is like, the top thing about this game that draws them to that, and honestly? i disagree - while i think the mystery hunting aspect is incredibly fun, the lore wasn't really the most interesting thing about this game. i'll be honest, the story and the midway section's gameplay tended to lag for me (metaphorically - its not that heavy on my computer) personally, with the gameplay in the middle part feeling like it droned on and was just.. clunkier and less balanced overall - though, that's really part of the lore in itself, to show how disharmonious they were... still, the middle part made the ENTIRE section INCREDIBLY unreadable. INCREDIBLY hard to read, where the first part's was visible and clear, the font they chose was incredibly difficult and headache inducing.

that isn't to say i disliked the game or the lore or the middle sections, though! it was just... incredibly hard to get through for above reasons - i just sincerely don't think the story and the lore sold me enough on this game.

what did sell me, however, was the entirety of the gameplay and roguelike aspect of the card game.

SOOOO many times throughout the latter parts did i wish that i was playing the first part repeatedly. the atmosphere, the setup, the buildup - all of it was just INCREDIBLE, with a challenging card game with plenty of options as a roguelike, that could have me playing forever. when it ended, i felt sour that i couldn't go back! how many times could i play that over nad over again? apparently for hours at a time, it was just one of my favorite parts and if this game didn't have the lore and story, i'd still recommend this game solely because the deckbuilding roguelike was just my favorite thing about it!

i'm SOOO glad they added kaycee's mod, because honestly, i could play that again and again for hours. the lore is shortlived and it doesn't really make me think too much - it's not something incredibly thought provoking or intriguing to me, but the fun of discovering what each segment means is incredibly fun! i have to give it credit for that, but overall? this is one of those games where i'd solely recommend the gameplay aspect, because the lore in itself doesn't hook me in too much.

though, i will say? the characters in here are memorable, if short lived as well - what i would give for a whole new fleshed out gameplay aspect in their hands, they have such different takes on the card game that it'd be nice. though, it's completely understandable if it never gets made lol :P i get they all had their flaws too!

all in all; the lore hunting was fun, though i don't recommend this game if you're looking for a deep story or the like, and if you enjoy the roguelike aspect as much as i did, definitely boot up the kaycee's mod with the buttons shift+k+m! wink wonk :P (if you dont find yourself enjoying the middle aspects too much, you can always shoot back to the first part with this code! just use it on the main screen,) but i still very much do recommend going thru the game at least once; the different takes and aspects of each gameplay experience will make u appreciate the mod soo much more! id rate it a 8/10, i can find myself playing this repeatedly for soooooooo long\t\t\t\t\t
