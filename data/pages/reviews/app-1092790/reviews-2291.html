\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter completing this game, all I gotta say is: wow. The story and characters you meet are interesting enough just by themselves to make you stick around, AND having a mix of the irl aspect of the story always makes the game feel like I can engage more with it. A beautiful blend of different game themes that all surround the core card mechanic gameplay. People who say the game changes 1/3 through aren't wrong. However, it's the same game at its core! They just introduce it to you differently each time so you don't get bored right away. Speaking of, I never got bored of the new combos/decks I had to learn, or the way the boards switched up, especially in boss fights. Addicting gameplay + puzzle solving + hidden lore never ceases to entertain & intrigue you. This game has a bright future if it wants it, and I highly recommend more than one play through of the game. Ending I got was so melancholy and just raises more questions that I want to play more! 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
