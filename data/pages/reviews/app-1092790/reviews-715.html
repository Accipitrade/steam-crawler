The card game is great both in and outside of it's fantastic yet eerie narrative. It's hard to talk about this game without spoiling it because the events that take place are integral to the presentation, atmosphere, and mystery. All I can say is, if you can get this game on sale, get it and just go into it blind the best you can. I believe this game is for nearly everyone in terms of difficulty, but be aware of two things: puzzle solving ranges from simple to intermediate difficulty, you *should* not have a problem to a point where you need to look anything up; and lastly there is a fair amount of horror between the sounds and events that take place, but it's mostly presentation, and nothing hardcore.
