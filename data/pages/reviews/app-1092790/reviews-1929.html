\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is important for everyone checking out this game to know it is not a roguelike deckbuilder!!! If you want a dark twisted version of Slay the Spire this isn't it! This game is a dark and twisted mystery taking on the form of a deckbuilder. If you play the demo and have a strong desire to know what is going on in the world of this game, you'll love it. If you don't care much for the mystery and are just here for the deckbuilder, you will be disappointed and I'll explain why in the spoiler tag below. Its only a light spoiler, but I wouldn't recommend reading unless you are really unsure about the game.

The game consists of 3 Acts and the finale. Only the first Act is a Roguelike Deckbuilder and it has by far the best game play. It only lasts about 4-6 hours depending on how quick you are to figure out the game and the mystery. The other Acts change mechanics and are not nearly as fun mechanically but tell a riveting story full of unexpected twists. None of the other acts are shown in the trailer or demo in order to keep the twists a surprise. Some people who bought the game for just the roguelike in Act 1 find this disappointing and misleading.

Pros:
Some of the gameplay is amazing.
Dark, twisted, and well written story.
Many secrets to uncover.
Story melds well with gameplay.

Cons:
Some of the gameplay is a little weak.
Not much replay value.\t\t\t\t\t
