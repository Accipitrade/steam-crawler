\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game.

I just finished it a couple of days ago, and... wow. I don't even know what I could say about it in recommendation that wouldn't be a spoiler.

So I'll try to be as generic as possible. My two main pieces of advice:

1) Go into this game as cleanly as possible. Try not to read a ton about it, and if you get stuck, try to make your google searching as specific as possible, to pull just what you want, and nothing more. Attempt to keep your self-spoils to a minimum. In fact, if you've decided to buy this based on the trailers and such, stop reading reviews, and go play it.

2) That said, as games sometimes do, there come points in this one where it... shifts gears. I will be no more specific than that.

Let it be known that some people (it feels to me like mainly younger ones who have often had a linear, comes-with-little-surprise A to B journey in much of their media) can have a visceral, I-immediately-hate-this reaction when the game does this.

I say to you: if this ends up being you (it may not be, you might be like me, and love every solitary moment of this game! But if it IS you): I implore you, DO NOT walk away. Hang with it, even if you find yourself as one of those people that has that rough reaction to those moments.

Because if you do walk away without giving this game the time it needs... I'm telling you, you're absolutely cheating yourself. Do yourself a favor, and PLEASE disregard the people in the review space here who clearly didn't give the game time to explain WHY it's shifting those gears.

I have played only a handful of games, ever, that are as good as Inscryption is at dropping delicious plot crumbs on your entire journey through it. That whole, "Oh, THAT'S why that happened." "Ah, okay, THAT'S what that means." A pure dopamine trail as you travel though the plot and connect the dots, over and over and over again. It's a fantastic journey, IF you keep following along to its conclusion.

That's why I recommend this one. The game play itself is fun, sure. But the plot and its crumb dropping... pure gold!\t\t\t\t\t
