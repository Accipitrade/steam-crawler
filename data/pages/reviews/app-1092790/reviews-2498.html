\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm streaming this on Twitch (no I won't use this to advertise my channel) and I'm having a blast getting wrecked at every turn. This game is creepy, fun, intriguing, frustrating, evil, and I love it. I haven't beaten it yet, and I'll add more to my review once I do.

Some people will find this easy, I am not some people. There's videos on YouTube that reveal all the secrets and I advise against watching them. The puzzles aren't that hard to figure out, but sometimes you just have to guess and take risks.

There are consequences to some actions as "Sacrifices Must Be Made." Yet you can make mistakes and still do well.

The game does a good job of balancing knowledge versus secrets. You never know what you're getting yourself into until you can recognize game board icons by sight, but the card keywords can be right-clicked for more information, which is helpful and appreciated.

I think this game is a buy at any price. I can't wait to finish it and try a speed run. I also hope there's future DLC or a sequel in the works. There's a demo you can try out. I played it months ago and have been chomping at the bit ever since. Now I can play this and I'm happy to do so, regardless of how much many mistakes I make, or consequences that can't be avoided.

So give it a try. The demo is at least worth your time.

EDIT: Just finished the game. OMG. I can't even call this a game. This is the experience of the year! Highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
