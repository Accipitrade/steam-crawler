\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGO IN BLIND.
One of the most breathtaking games I've played. Brilliant storyline. Eerie and witty, much deeper than The Hex while retaining all the qualities of its predecessor. It's a narrative tactical card game with satisfying deck building elements and real opportunity to bend gameplay to your style while maintaining a coherent challenge.
It was creepy, mysterious, and gave me massive chills - it felt personal, almost intimate, in the way it knows players and the rules and language we use within interactive spaces. Moments of jubilant success were closely followed by unforeseen feels and the difficulty curve mimicked the narration in the most perfect way. It traumatised me and I loved it.
