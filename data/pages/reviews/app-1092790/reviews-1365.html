Firstly, I've just noticed it says I've played for 5 hours and completed it...Ok, I'm shocked for real thought it would say like 20 hours - IT FEELS LIKE THAT. It's an EPIC journey, so different, it somehow draws you in deeper and deeper. There's puzzles all over the place - at the board (your cards talk to you) , around the room , the bosses require you to adapt and plan ahead and use the various pitstops wisely...

Deck creation - So you mould your cards into better ones whilst trying to figure out what everything does (which takes time to master, trust me) - You can power up things like health, power or try to combine card abilities to suit your needs.

The Story - from my other reviews, you will get the gist that I'm old and have played a metric ton of games, so stories all feel the same in the end. Sometimes being old means you just want to sit down and play something rather than sit through a story, because you only got like an hour a day :(
Anyway...
This game is insane. Seriously awesome story and I do not want to spoil it for you - I figure you will only like this if:
A) You like a challenge (like slay the spire)
B) Want a more immersive experience than slay the spire - This has A T M O S P H E R E !!!
C) Enjoy mind bending reality fiction cray cray wtf oh yeah really, huh? uh ohhhhhh feelings.

I would absolutely pay for a followup or dlc, free is nice, but for this level of fun I'd happily fund more ideas.

Remember, probably not for you if you don't like card battling deck games ok? If you're like me, a bit of a bored veteran who wants something different, play this for the challenge and out of the box story :)
