Inscryption is primarily a singleplayer rougelike card game combined with an escape room mechanic and a very meta narrative.

Additionally, a few days ago the "Kaycee's mod" update went into beta, which expands the game massively and allows you to ramp up the difficulty if you felt it was too easy in the main game.

I highly recommend this game to anyone that enjoys card games like Magic: The Gathering and Hearthstone.\t\t\t\t\t
