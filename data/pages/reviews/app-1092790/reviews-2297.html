\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is one of the most unique games that I've played this year. It is very difficult to describe without spoilers but in its most basic form it is a card game with a great atmosphere and a great story. The actual card game mechanics are fun and make you think about what move you need to make without being too difficult and hard to understand. The game is split up into three distinct acts as well a finale. The first act is probably my favourite and while the third act probably goes for a bit too long that doesn't make it bad by any means. I won't say anything on the finale or ending although they were both really good, even if the ending happened a bit quickly. I'm sure this game has many secrets, and although I was left with more questions than answers, telling the player everything would definitely detract from the experience. If you want to buy this game, go in completely blind, because knowing just about anything about it could spoil the experience.\t\t\t\t\t
