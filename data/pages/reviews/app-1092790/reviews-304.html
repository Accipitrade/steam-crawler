\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rarely ever write a review for games. This is why all of us love this game so much (i think) and why everyone is saying to go in blind is the best experience. If you're the type to read reviews religiously before playing/purchasing a game im hoping you'll be convinced to try after you read this review.

2 hours in: this is fun asf but wtf i playing?
10 hours in: ok im really confused and i feel like i might get bored really soon, wtf i am playing?
13-14 hours in: ok this just got really interesting...but wtf am i playing!?
14-20 hours in: HOLY WHAT I HAVE DONE!?
20-40 hours: at this point you never thought a game would make you feel like and think the way you are rn and you're starting to have a existential gaming crisis to whether or not this story can even be topped by bigger/top tier game developers and publishers.

This game borderline haunted my psyche (in a good way) for a few days after i finished it. When i say its next level story telling, i mean it. I don't know a single game that comes close to having this much of a grip on me to keep playing through my absolute confusion to wtf is going on. Do not look at any FAQs/walkthroughs or anything because you will ruin the literal once in a gamerlife time experience. After that experience (if you loved it like majority of us) then use FAQs/walkthroughs.\t\t\t\t\t
