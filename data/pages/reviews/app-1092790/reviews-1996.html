\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwesome game! Breathed completely new life into my passion for gaming which has been long dead for years.

The art style was absolutely fantastic and versatile in its applications. Used perfectly as a storytelling mechanism in subtle and extreme changes over time. This game rewards attentiveness both in terms of progress and lore.

The gameplay is slightly challenging but also not completely punishing. I think what drew me in especially was the dungeon-crawler building of your deck. I didn't enjoy Act 2 as much because I was encouraged to build my deck entirely on my own. Still, luckily, the auto-complete function mitigated this issue for me. I'm just not good at strategy.

The sound design was heaps of fun; I especially loved the Magnificus track. The minute I heard the characters voices, I was in love.

So pleased to see that captions were automatically turned on; as a hard of hearing person, this means the world to me. There is nothing more jarring than having to sit through a cutscene, understanding absolutely nothing only to turn on captions, close the game and reboot.

The only downside of this game is that I want more, and it left me in an absolute depression knowing there wasn't any more. I had no reason to sit at my computer anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
