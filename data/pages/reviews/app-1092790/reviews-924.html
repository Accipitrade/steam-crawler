\r\n\t\t\t\t\tfantastic experience, really great gameplay to accompany it, sound design and visuals are as good as they get it, love the insanity. This game isn't just cards, go into it knowing nothing and you'll have a great time. I am literally the worst at card games and I still had a great time trying to strategise and build decks despite sucking immensely. Since the actual gameplay is only a part of what makes Inscryption great there's plenty of broken loopholes/exploits that feel awesome to discover and break the game in your favour without ruining the experience - the game is constantly changing and progressing so breaking it only makes the game more satisfying and fun. Would recommend this to anybody, one of the best games I've played in a while. Even the ARG stuff was kinda fun.
