\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDang. THIS was an experience. Go in without spoiling yourself if possible. It really is THAT good.

What begins as a creepy occult card game turns into a greater puzzle. That puzzle connects just about every single aspect of the game beyond just the card game, and it all wraps itself around an enigma to which the answers are always hidden in just enough shadow to be tantalizing, without giving a direct answer.

Sometimes, by following clues, you will come across tools to make the card game easier for you. Other times, you will get strange and unsettling scraps of information that will let you get a clearer picture of the enigma at the heart of this puzzle constructed around a frankly phenomenal card game.

...And once the game is over, you can even play a fully Roguelike version of the card game if you really enjoyed it for the gameplay.

What a phenomenal experience, worth all the hype I'd heard, highly reccomended.\t\t\t\t\t
