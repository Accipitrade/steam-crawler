Now imagine that they try to kill you in the most atmoshpheric way, with secrets sprinkled left and right along the path.


I'm not gonna talk about the story because I was going in without prior knowledge and was having a blast.
I can only recommend to stay away from spoilers if you intend to enjoy the game to its fullest.

I've encountered no bugs in my playthrough and the performance was stable.
