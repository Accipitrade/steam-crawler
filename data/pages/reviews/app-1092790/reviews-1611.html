\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt its core this game is a mix between a puzzle (15%), adventure (15%), and trading card game (70%). It does all of these with some grace, but the element that truly captures your attention is the story. The real secret to this game is that it's 100% about the story. The other parts inevitably turn into grindy filler. Don't expect to play this game more than once.

For the first 2-4 hours I was very intrigued with this game and wanted to uncover its secrets. After the first "act", things gradually became less polished and interesting.

While I appreciate the artistic direction and story this game displayed, I ultimately came out of it feeling like it was shallow and anti-climatic. Was it a fun ride? Sometimes, but unfortunately the pack just wasn't worth the crack.\t\t\t\t\t
