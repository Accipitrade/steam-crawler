\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a wonderful game oozing with creepy atmosphere. My playtime is generous as I was AFK for about half of it, but there's a truly compelling and fun card game here. There are tons of secrets to discover, and pretty interesting storyline. Also the 4th wall breaks and found footage sections are reasonably well done and add value.

The only downsides I can think of are a few nitpicks on the gameplay. The first act is much more difficult than the following acts, which feels somewhat anti-climatic even if it fits story wise. Also there's a point in act 3 where almost all of the game's mechanics can get shoved into your deck, and it overall feels a bit messy. You can also tell there was going to be additional content, but the developer called it at a certain point.

Overall I'd give this game a solid 8/10. Very fun for fans of card, retro, and creepy games. Just expect to lose a few times at the beginning.\t\t\t\t\t
