\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPut simply, Inscryption is a masterpiece unlike anything else Ive ever played. If youre even remotely interested in any of the genres or subgenres Inscryption is listed to have, I strongly recommend you play it.

Im honestly not even sure where to begin talking about Inscryption and why its so good. I could write paragraphs and paragraphs about its masterful blending of a deck-builder with an escape room, all built on the underpinning of a mysterious almost horror-like story and lore. I could write about how the game is really fun to break, with many unique and interesting card combinations that are fun to discover. I could write about the narrative and how it kept me glued to this game from start to finish. I could write about any number of things, and I cannot stress enough, that in every corner and crevice of Inscryption, you find nothing but an incredibly fun and well designed game coupled with a unique twisty mystery of a story.

Now, Im the type of person who enjoys writing long and drawn out multi-paragraph extended novels of near nonsense for my game reviews. But I loved going in blind with this game, I feel like thats a core part of the experience. So I just cant bring myself to write in detail about anything else. Consider that a MASSIVE recommendation from me! I really think this game has something to offer the vast majority of players out there, and I really think the experience is worth the price and the trip.

If you are curious, and this review didn't quite sell you on it, I am doing a DEEP DIVE spoiler riddled podcast episode on it in the near future. Search for VOID ARCADE anywhere you listen to podcasts to see if its out yet (will try to retroactively update this review with a link). But more importantly, PLEASE dont pass this one up! Its a Masterpiece! NameBrand.\t\t\t\t\t
