\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave you ever wanted to play Dnd, except instead its a Yugi-Oh style card game featuring a wonderfully disturbing DM who may or may not be completely insane? WELL THEN maybe this is the game for you!

Inscryption basically starts out as this rougelite where you need to gather cards, power them up, and try to make it as far as you can through the various encounters and bosses the DM has set up for you, all the while solving secrets and uncovering the overall plot by exploring the cabin you are trapped in. Tensions are kept high in every encounter since you only have 2 lives; the stats of the cards are kept small so even one point of damage feels like a lot, there is no easy way to draw cards so bad starting hands can be a death sentence sometimes, and in order to play more powerful units you need to be willing to make some sacrifices.

If you manage to beat the DM, the whole game flips on its head! I won't spoil anything, but there are other "phases" to this game that tie in with the big plot and completely switch up the gameplay by introducing new mechanics, characters, and environments to explore. It may seem a bit weird at first but I seriously recommend you give it a chance since there is loads of passion in this game and even though it isn't really super its really fun to play through.

TLDR; buy game because spooky and fun <3\t\t\t\t\t
