\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic game.

It's difficult to get into the details of why it's so great, because the less you know about the game when you start it, the better. If you like card games, puzzles and horror (the creepy, not the jump scare are overly gory kind) you should try it.

Few games had me on the edge like this. I don't know how many times I yelled a confused "What the ♥♥♥♥". Inscryption has so many twists and turns. Just when you think you know what the game has to offer, it surprises you again. It's wonderful.

There was one point though where a bit of frustration set in and i had to willfully bring myself to push on. As with other puzzle / exit style games, if you're stuck on a puzzle or don't realize you have to do something, progress can grind to a halt. It is very rewarding to keep going, though.
Would recommend even to people who aren't that into the genre, must buy for people who are.\t\t\t\t\t
