It's a wonderfully-crafted game with many twists and turns, but it's not something I'm going to dive back into unless I'm feeling particularly masochistic a few years down the line. That being said, it was an absolute capital-E Experience to play through for the first time.

Inscryption is a game that's driven by narrative and characterization, and the more you put into exploring its secrets, the better your eventual payoff will be. Easily one of my 2021 Game of the Year contenders.\t\t\t\t\t
