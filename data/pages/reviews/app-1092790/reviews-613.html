\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a card horror game where sacrifices must be made from the creator of Pony Island and The Hex.

Inscryption is a very clever game that clearly displays it's creators passion and is so good I was sad to finish it. The game is an amalgamation of deck-building, escape-room style puzzles, psychological horror and roguelike games with a sense of humour that genuinely made me laugh out loud as a natural reaction.

Inscryption is that rare game that everything I can think of typing here doesn't feel worthwhile. I truly do not want to spoil anything in-game, it'd be a crime to do so. Instead of prattling on and spoiling a game that's best played 100% blind I'll end by stating, I cannot recommend this highly enough.\t\t\t\t\t
