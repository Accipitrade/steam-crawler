\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh Inscryption, too many good things to say about this unique horror-esque game. First of all, this game's biggest pro is its unique and stylistic gameplay. I guarantee you will not a play game that has a constantly shifting mechanic yet still keeps its core ideals. The gameplay is arbitrary but refreshing as it puts a twist on the classic battle-style card game (such as pokemon, yu-gi-oh, and Magic) while including new mechanics that give the game its 'Breath of fresh air" one mechanic I would like to note is the "scale" mechanic. in order to win a battle not only do you need to do damage to the enemy but you have to overwhelm the enemy's damage with your own by tipping the scale in your favor. Another mechanic I love is the Sigils. certain creatures you attain during your journey will have certain abilities whether it's attacking the sides or evolving after a turn, these sigils bring a common concept and it flips it on its head by making sacrifice these animals to put it on others. this mechanic can create some powerful combos that can easily make this game fun for anyone.

Another aspect I love about this game is the atmosphere. The feeling of claustrophobia and uneasiness as you are stuck in a cabin forced to play this cruel game of kill or be killed while the solemn and melancholic track called "Deathcard Cabin" paints a picture of hopelessness as you the character playing the old man's game. it is simple yet unique in its sounds and plucks of the strings on what sounds like a slightly off-tune guitar of sorts. The game uses a lot of orange in the game which gives the room a closed-in feeling.

Minor spoiler alerts for the plot down below so don't recommend to continue reading but please just play the game. overall this game refreshing with an outstanding plot and brilliant mechanics and I would give it a 9.3/10 only because its difficulty is not high and it is rather short compared to other games but I think this game is for anybody.




Finally the plot. In games, the plot of the game is a make or break for me and this game does not cease to amaze me with its metaplot as you play a game that explores a malignant game that wants to spread like a virus and it feels so real at times but I do not wish to spoil anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
