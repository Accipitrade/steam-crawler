\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't think there are any spoilers here, but I do mention some character names that aren't revealed at first. Not sure if that counts as spoiler or not lol, so avoid this review if that might bother you. Absolutely LOVED this game! However, there were some things that I wasn't wildly fond of. Without spoiling too much, the parts where the game is actually a game were wonderful. They were atmospheric, they were engaging, especially the first act. Like, wow Leshy's cabin blew me away with the design, the mask changing, the ambiance, everything. On the other hand, I didn't find myself caring about what I believe was supposed to be the "real" story. The game company, the videos, I just didn't find it very interesting. I'm sure there is much more to the lore that makes it all come around, but after looking it up online, it seems like one of those things were it would require effort OUTSIDE of the game to fully appreciate/understand, and that's just not what I want to do with a game. That said, everything with Leshy, Grimora, Magnificus, and the other one (whose name is escaping me right now) I really enjoyed. Like others have said, Daniel, if you're reading this, I would love to play a game that just focused on them and their gameplay elements. I would gladly pay another $20 just to get that. The video stuff, while interesting at first, struck me a little too much like "Lost Tapes" or a knock off X-files to enjoy next to everything else going on. It took me out of the wonderful atmosphere every time he suddenly spoke over the actual gameplay. Would I recommend this game? Yes, absolutely. it took me like 10 hours to beat, but I'm going back to play through the first part again just because of how much I enjoyed that part. It's incredibly atmospheric and ominous, and I think anyone who can get behind card games will find a gold mine in this game. I only wish the game itself was allowed to be more of a game at times. If you beat it, you'll know what I mean.\t\t\t\t\t
