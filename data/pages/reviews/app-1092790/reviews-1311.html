\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHands down one of the best games i've ever had the pleasure of playing. The characters, style, gameplay, story, it all works beautifully. Do yourself a favor and go in COMPLETELY BLIND. Like another reviewer said, "let the game work it's magic". I'm serious. Not even the trailer. It's unlike anything i've ever seen before, and it would really suck if you spoiled yourself of the incredible journey Inscryption takes you on.


SPOILERS AHEAD





There are three acts in this game. All of the promo material is from act 1, which is, in my opinion, the best act of all of them. It's perfect. Act 2 was a huge change in tone, focusing more on a pokemon like vibe that the original Inscryption would be if it were real. It expands and explains so much in terms of lore and the story, and it brings a lot of cool new ideas to the table. After that is Act 3, P03's game. It is very basic, uncharming, and has no artistry to it. And that is exactly the point. Leshy was the best because he knew he was a video game character, and he just wanted to make the best out of his game. A master of storytelling and atmosphere. P03, however, is a cold robot so self-indulged in his mechanical improvements to the game he forgets the very thing that makes Leshy's game so great. The story, the atmosphere, the presentation. Through the dialogue all of this is revealed. This game is a master in storytelling and plot.
Edit: Kaycee's mod exists for those who just want a whole game's worth of chapter 1. Although it removes the whole experience of the game as a whole, it turns the game into a fun roguelike to hop into once and a whole.


TL;DR: This game is masterful with it's gameplay, themes and lore seamlessly blending together. 12/10 recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
