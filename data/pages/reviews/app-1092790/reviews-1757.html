\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnsettling, moody, captivating and sometimes poorly paced game with impressive audio (both effects and music). It is, hands down, the best game made by this dev, with gameplay more polished than in previous games made by this dev. The card battling as the main thing that you do in this game may seem to be more boring than serveral games that hex consisted of. But battles are enjoyable and there is not that much focus on deckbuilding - it's pretty straightforward and you don't have to spend hours staring at your cards thinking whether you should grab a mole man rather than a kingfisher hoping that you'd have a duplicate to maybe fuse it later or whatever. Gameplay seems polished enough to draw you in for several hours, but it may get a bit tiring after playing the game for a few days straight, therefore I recommend taking brakes between longer playing sessions.
Bottom line - I've had plenty of fun with this game, it's better than hex and battles in the cabin are kinda charming.\t\t\t\t\t
