\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHard to review this game without revealing too much of it. The only thing I knew was it was a card game with some twist to it. I have been pleased with both the card game mechanics and the twist. The most absurd thing to me was the fact it could have been a really solid game even with only the card game, but the devs didn't stop there and pushed their concept way further than it needed to be thus creating a really unique experience.

If you like card and puzzle solving games, you should give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a clever and captivating roguelike with a very intriguing story, an expansive and diverse set of mechanics, and an incredible atmosphere. The shocking twists and turns the player encounters as the story progresses ensure that every moment leaves you unsure of what will happen next, and even a bit unsure as to what has already happened. This game incorporates a variety of genres, including puzzle, horror, and classic RPG, yet none of the game's moments feel out of place, and instead everything blends together in a uniquely perfect way. Inscryption is easy to learn, but hard to master, creating a very high skill ceiling and allowing for boundless replayability. All-in-all an excellent card-battling roguelike at its core, with a dark, immersive, mysterious story surrounding it.\t\t\t\t\t
