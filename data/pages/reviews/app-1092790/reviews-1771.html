\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe highly positive reviews about this game speak for themselves. The game is divided into 3 acts. The first act is in a 3D pixelated fashion, the second act is a very retro 2D style and the third one is like the first one. It takes 5 seconds of gameplay to realize that this is really not your standard deck building PvE game. The atmosphere is unsettling and you quickly get the feeling that is a lot more going on than just playing a card game in this game.

As you'll progress through the different acts, you'll get to watch some clips which will unfold another story outisde of the game that were all shot in real life action and i have to say the actors did pretty good job. The whole story becomes more and more intriguing and unsettling as you progress. Couple that with the very fun gameplay and the very intriguing universe and you get a remarkable experience.

I've always prefered indie games to games made by big corporates because they have a lot of passion and soul that you can really feel when you're playing them. Games made by big companies can be very fun to play but they're always a bit lacking in terms of passion and while someone would say that a lot of indie games are very awful which isn't wrong, you do, every once in a while, get a diamond in the rough like this one.\t\t\t\t\t
