\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've seen a lot of people knock this game for changing drastically between acts, when in the trailer it only shows act one. It truly takes sticking it to the end to experience the full majesty and mystery of this game. I adore this game, each stage while using the same method of play, brought new ways to view and understand its challenges. I highly recommend this game as each act has its own flavour and if you prefer a certain stage, just complete the game once and you can choose from which act you begin.
I implore those who refunded this to pick it back up with a fresh face and open arms. The story is rich under the surface and the unique mix of graphics took my breath away.

I understand why those who played the demo aren't pleased with the rest of the game, but things evolve and develop overtime. Back then it was called 'Sacrifices Must Be Made' and was going to be about a starving group who play cards to earn food. (I think the campfire segment in act one is a nice nod to that demo)
It must be respected how the developer stepped away from that to make something bigger.

Course there is some downsides, but every game has its flaws and to me they are nitpicks if anything! I love love love this and will replay it in its entirety!\t\t\t\t\t
