\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI usually don't like games because I'm not very good at them. Even though I know I wasn't the best at this one either, it was very well-designed to the point that I had a lot of fun anyway! I liked that it could have a feeling of urgency but actually gave me all the time I needed to read things or plan things, and when I failed/died it didn't feel pointless since there were things like the death cards as a consolation prize. The story was very interesting, loved the meta elements, and the mechanics kept mixing it up enough to be interesting without completely drowning me.
Act 1 was the spookiest, and I feel like I might have missed a lot in Act 2--though I do like that there was lots of secrets available to find! Act 3 might be my favorite. It made me laugh out loud multiple times, and I was really proud of my deck at the end...the only thing that was regretful, since I don't think there's an arcade mode for Act3, lol.
The ending was very satisfying and tied everything up so well! I was surprised at how nostalgic and sentimental it made me feel (*the ending proper, not the denouement lol. That was more "??!??"). I highly recommend this game even to people who aren't usually good at games like myself.\t\t\t\t\t
