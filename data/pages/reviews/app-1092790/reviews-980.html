\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is superb with its unique art style, silent story telling and inky black texts. It starts as a creepy cabin in the woods style card game and turns into a pixelated 2D dungeon crawler to 3D projected robotic amalgam of a card game. Very surprising, deep, strategic and engaging puzzles similar to a chess game. Excellent injection of roguelike combined with good story telling make it a decent overall game.

Inscryption is an excellent card game. Borrows from other brilliant predecessors and create an engaging gameplay. There's a meta progression inside the story and dying only makes you better through death cards. You need to escape the cabin by solving numerous puzzles and learning the card game truly.

Where this game shines for me though is when you beat the story, you unlock this new game mode "Kaycee's Mod". This mode turns the game's best part into an endlessly replayable truly roguelike card game in which you unlock new cards and starter decks each time you climb the challenge tree. It's very thought provoking and challenging. Probably the best deckbuilding system, it pushes you to consider every single card or path that you have taken as they are vital for beating the mode. This mod has the potential of making you a better card game player in other games as the skill is transferable.

Definite purchase for anyone who is willing to get into card games, mysterious story telling and a chilling atmosphere and for who is already a card player looking for a new concept.\t\t\t\t\t
