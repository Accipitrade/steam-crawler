Now that i got that out of the way, id love to say i enjoyed every part of this game. From the challenging start where i had to find clues and figure out every little bit for the lore. Ive spend ~20 hours playing this game. I know it says i commented this around 'two' something went wrong with the counter. My personal favorite part of this whole game is the videos. I would watch the videos and when the binary came up i would translate it. (meaning google) This game is truly astonishing and shows a story well. It has amazing graphics, (the 2d and the 3d were so well detailed, and during the ending my eyes were sweating for a bit) When i had to go thru my own files on my computer to find the biggest and the oldest files was something i havnt really had in a game. It is truly worth the money its for.
A personal message to the devs:
Thank you for making this game. Every peice is so perfected and smooth, i know some bugs needed to be fixed (the tower part of the 3po where you had to climb ladders to the top of the tower and click the recevers, if you go on top of the tower, quit the game (it crashed for me because i had other stuff running in the background) and come back, the character would be stuck in the tower.) I look foward to buying you games in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
