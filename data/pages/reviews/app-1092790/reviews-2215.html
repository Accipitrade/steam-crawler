\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEDIT: Since the release of Kaycee's Mod, my negative points are mostly invalidated as you can now have access to a game mode that's centered entirely around what you see on the store page.

+ The game has a lot of interesting mechanics which allow you to better plan your deck and moves, with a lot of synergies to discover and utilize. From things like different card resource requirement (certain types of card require sacrificing something on the board, while others require bone tokens which you gain from losing cards on the board), to sacrificing a card and applying its modifiers to another, to collecting powerful cards just to create an overpowered card which you could obtain in future runs.
+ Exploration not only progresses the story, but also rewards you for solving optional puzzles and interacting with the environment.
+ Lots of encounters with unique gimmicks to keep you entertained (even after the first act)
+/- Solid core game loop, but it changes fundamentally twice throughout the game
- It constantly reminds you that you're playing a game via random quips from the guy recording
- Game loses a lot of what you'd expect from what it advertises after the first act
- Game slows down to a crawl without much challenge after the first act

Overall I'd say its a good game, if somewhat misleading, and while the first act nearly perfect, the other two just don't feel nearly as good.
There isn't much of a challenge after the first act, which may disappoint some hardcore CCG fans, with some unintuitive changes and mechanics here in there.
And while I understand the idea behind what they tried doing with act 2, it still felt like a huge downgrade. (Act 3 is way better, but still not as good as act 1)\t\t\t\t\t
