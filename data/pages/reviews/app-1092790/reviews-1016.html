I want to write a really big review, but I can't, anything I say is going to lean very dangerously into spoiler territory. I am going to touch on some very important things though, super quick.

  • Go in completely blind on this. Don't even watch the trailer. It spoils the first little twist in it and it's a tone setter. Just dive in.

  • Don't bother trying to guess or anticipate what happens next. The game is all about subverting your expectations and wants to take you on a journey. So let it. You won't guess what's going on, it's too out there.

  • If you dislike card/board game styled play, don't worry too much about it; it's quite simple and intuitive. Takes maybe 30 minutes, possibly an hour to get the gist of it. I think that covers the bulk of it. There were some dips into the experience, namely the second act, but the third act dwarfs it to the point it doesn't even matter all that much and is hardly a negative. Then....then the fourth act, which is essentially the ending sequence is just...master class. Absolutely fantastic in every way.

This is going to be a game that's on my mind for quite a while afterwords. Quit reading my words though, go play it.\t\t\t\t\t
