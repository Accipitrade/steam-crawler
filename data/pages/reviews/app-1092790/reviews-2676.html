\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI very much enjoyed this game whether it be the game play or the story/lore I found myself to be having fun the whole way out. (Except for a certain robot puzzle)

This game has a cool mix of being a story driven game while also having a fair amount of replay ability to it which I am fully looking forward to.

I've only slightly dipped my pinky toe into the fun mysteries and secrets this game has to offer and may update this review following those discoveries. I rarely ever leave (serious) reviews for many games at all but this deserves all the overwhelmingly positive attention that it is getting and a big props to the developer for another fantastic gaming experience.

Downside - If you're a hardcore fan of the rouge-like/lie genre then you may have an issue with the game. It is not developed nor formed to be a experience based rouge-like game (to an extent). It mainly focuses on its (quite fun and intriguing) story which you have to leave the repeatable loop of game play to progress. While some might be sad to hear this news I promise you that this is still a game worth buying and playing through regardless of how you might feel at first, I promise it will be worth your time and your money.

Give this a try and feel free to post your own review about the game to maybe encourage others to not only buy the game for a fantastic time but to also support the devs in order to get more awesome content like this. I can't wait for the next released game and am looking forward to it. Until then I shall play this game and discover what lies ahead.\t\t\t\t\t
