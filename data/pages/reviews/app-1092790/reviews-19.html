\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, one of the best games I've played in a while. Definitely try this out if you're into card games and strategy! I was hesitant to buy this game since it's unlike any other games that I've previously played. 100% worth it!!

!!SPOILERS!!
The different acts were super cool. I was surprised that I didn't get bored at all even though it was the same card game over and over. But I think all the random changes to the rules between acts, and trying to get the best cards + items + totems was the part that kept me interested. Deckbuilding was somewhat easy and getting though the game was semi fast. (if you're not slow like me-) But then there's KAYCEE'S MOD to have more fun with.
It took me a while to understand that I needed to die in the first act a few times to actually make progress, which threw me off. Figuring out different secrets in the room was a great addition.

Story was almost like any other horror found footage games/series out there. It wasn't that special, but they did a good job when mixing reality and the game together. Everything made sense when certain plot twists were revealed (not that there were many). The horror itself wasn't that scary, at to a horror fanatic like myself. I think people who do not really like horror could enjoy this as well

KAYCEE'S MOD, perfection. It took the first act (my personal favourite) and different modifications to the game. Beating skull storm is tough, but it's doable! Setting the difficulty, trying out different challenges and starter decks were fun. The Grizzlies were horrible the first 10 times lol. Scissors or the clock from the painting are the key! Get them ASAP

Overall a great game!\t\t\t\t\t
