Summary

Inscryption is an incredible, unique experience. It's hard to explain without spoilers, but here's a minor one. The game starts like a regular roguelite, then it changes as the story progresses. There is also a game mode after you beat the story that is just a roguelike. It is a really incredible game like no other. It reminds me of Outer Wilds, in how unique and spoiler sensitive it is. The game has very well made puzzles and the core gameplay is really enjoyable. The deck building mechanics are the best in the genre in my opinion.


Category Ratings

Gameplay: Very good. The core gameplay loop for act 1 of the game and the extra mode unlocked after beating the story especially are really good. The puzzles are also very well made. The deck building mechanics are incredible as well.

Graphics: Incredible. The art style for this game is beautiful. It's stylized and looks exceptionally good. Nothing more to say.

Story: Really good. This game has a story that makes it worth sticking through the slow parts to experience the ending yourself.

Innovation: Nothing else like it. Really no more to say. Play it and you will understand.

Sound Design: Great. The music is pretty high A tier.

Replayability: Sort of. The story isn't worth replaying, but the endless mode exists.

Spoilers: Very sensitive. Avoid spoilers.


Should You Buy

Yes. Inscryption is very much worth buying.\t\t\t\t\t
