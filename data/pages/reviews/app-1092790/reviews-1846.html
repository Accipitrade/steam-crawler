This game is one of my favorite games to have come out in 2021 and I fee like I don't see enough people talking about it. Spoiler alert for games like DDLC and for this game but a fantastic horror element is that they do stuff with your computer and files similar to how DDLC while alter the game files. This just adds the eerie feel of the whole game and it is fantastic. It doesn't do cheap jumpscares but chilling bits that just creep you out. The gameplay in act 1 is one of my favorite parts of the game though. I would play an entire Inscryption game with just an extended act 1 and nothing else with a better final boss. Fantastic card game, fantastic deck builder, and fantastic horror. Absolutely adore this game.\t\t\t\t\t
