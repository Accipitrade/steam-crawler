\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEasily one of the best games I’ve played this year, with the perfect balance of engaging plot and enjoyable gameplay. I didn’t expect to become as obsessed with the card playing mechanic as I did, but I’ve spent most of today playing Kaycee’s Mod virtually nonstop. Suffice to say, I’m in deep.
Streaming this game over discord with my boyfriend forced me to slow down and savor my first playthrough, and I’m glad I ended up doing it that way. Inscryption will get you hooked. The second time I played it through to completion, I was glued to my computer until I finished it for the second time. It’s rare that I go in for a full second playthrough so soon after I’ve finished my first, but Inscryption is just that good. I’m not even joking when I say that this game was the reason I finally replaced my slowly dying laptop. It was the first thing I played upon setting up my new PC, which just another reason it has a special place in my heart.
In conclusion: 11/10, would cause chaos with a 460/460 Ouroboros again. Get this game, and get The Hex and Pony Island while you’re at it. Daniel Mullins never disappoints.\t\t\t\t\t
