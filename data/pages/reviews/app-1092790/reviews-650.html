\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk... mixed feelings here and *minor spoilers ahead*, if you want to avoid that (and it IS better whitout spoilers) the TLDR is:

5.5 out of 10, wait for a discounted version, hard pass at full price

Long Version (WITH MINOR SPOILERS):

Ok, it is a 3 part game that feels incomplete in many aspects, and at the same time it would be a better game if had less of it, let me explain with scores:

Part 1: 8 out of 10 (AWESOME)
Part 2: 3 out of 10 (TERRIBLE)
Part 3: 6 out of 10 (GOOD)

[Part 1]

Is AWESOME, great pacing, presentation, puzzles, mechanics, if that was the whole game it would be better than the mish mash of the final product (even though the storytelling behind the game is good), this is the part that sells you the game, that is first presented to you, and is great! A roguelike cardgame awesomeness.

[Part 2]

Is TERRIBLE, suddenly you recieve a lot (and I mean A LOT) of new stuff that is not explained, it has (I kid you not) the worst possible interface, exactly when you need it the most, the mechanics are hard to understand on your own, and some are simply bad that cannot be justified. You start to compare with the [Part 1] that was so good, feeling sad with the nostalgia.

Hiding behind the aesthetics are a lame excuse for bad.

From a pure gameplay perspective, cut this part and you have a better game, and tell the story parts of it differently.

[Part 3]

Is GOOD, we go back to the nice parts, leaning to the [Part 1], the mechanics and gameplay are solid.

The bad part are some things that you MUST do on [Part 2] to access on the third, and some of the liberty of the five colums fall short when you have so much obstacles like trees, stones and bridge rails.

The puzzles are less polished (some are funny though), having to dig a little too deep to find proper answers, hinting a puzzle that is not (glowing chest I am looking at you) or recycled ones.

Honorable mentions to the awesome network boss and the card printing sections.

[Post game]

Concludes the history BUT leaves the player hoping for 2 aditional content with Magnificus and Grimora. And I understand that some cuts have to be made to ship a game, but feels incomplete, and it is not a hint of a second game or anything.

Unlocks Kaycee's Mod, that cuts the BAD and focus on the GOOD and polished [Part 1] of the game, having additional challenges, BUT requires you to finish the game first (and survive the [Part 2])\t\t\t\t\t
