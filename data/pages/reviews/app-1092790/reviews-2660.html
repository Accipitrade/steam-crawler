\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame was overall pretty enjoyable, however not recommending for these reasons (slight spoilers):

1. Game is more of a story game with a card game backdrop than the other way around.
2. The cool spooky cabin atmosphere with escape room like elements and rogue-like card gameplay is actually only 1/3rd or so of the game, while the game trailers make it seem like it'll be the entire game's theme. I would liken it to going from playing slay the spire, to the pokemon trading card game on gameboy, back to playing hearthstone afterwards. All are decent games, but might appeal to different audiences.
3. Having said this, the parts after the cabin feel a bit cheaper, you can basically brute-force your way through them. I finished part 2 with basically the same deck after getting a few cards, except for 1 boss annoyingly forced me to rebuild from scratch to beat his gimmick, and there were no extra deck slots to save your decks.

Seems to be the game developer's quirk, to make games that appear one way then evolve into something else, which I was not aware of.\t\t\t\t\t
