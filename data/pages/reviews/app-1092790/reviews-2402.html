\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a card game with some horror elements, where you playing an old game with the same title and making videos about it.

The gameplay is really good. If you like deck building and card battles with some rouge like elements, like making a better deck meanwhile advancing in the game, doing some big risk big reward activities to make a creature of yours stronger or use items to change the battles balance, then you will enjoy this one. Also, the game is quite addictive in my opinion. Not even mentioning, the game has enough room to explore and get more items to beat your opponents or know more about the game's lore.

In general, you won't notice much, if you just play through the game, however this product has a deep backstory, which is really good to explore. The characters in the game are pretty well made and it's also nice to see a game which breaks the fourth wall. I also this part of the game, just like the sound design.

Tracks in the game fit for the events or characters perfectly, not even mentioning the sound effects, which doesn't feel overused and boring, after hearing them a lot, but it adds a lot to the product. What really adds into the game, is the graphics.

Man, it's perfect mix of nostalgic pixelated styled graphics with modern technology, sometimes is literally just nostalgia to looking at the screen, meanwhile it doesn't feel or seems outdated or bad looking. Effects looks really good, characters models are nicely made and the shadow effects are very good in the game. Another good thing in the game, is the controls, which is really simple. You mostly need to use your mouse, however you need to use the directional keys to move around or have a better view at the table or do certain action in the card battles, but this are as I said, simple enough and well made.

I waited a lot for this game, at first I thought it will be some kind of survival card game, where you need to do certain actions to stay alive and playing cards, but thankfully the game is not that. It's really fun to play the game and a bless to explore it, especially looking into the lore. Sadly the game is not that long, but to be honest, one of the best indie game I played for a long time... hell, maybe this is even the best game I played recently I played. I can totally recommended to you to give it a shot, even for the full price. This one is a one true gem, and I hope the developers will give more depth to the game later. Masterpiece!\t\t\t\t\t
