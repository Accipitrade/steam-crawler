\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is so much fun and refreshing. It's a lovely card game with a story full of surprises and a mysterious horrifying delightful world to discover. It has a lot of quotes from other card games and graphically is a piece of art. Characterized by an old style of 3D, similar to what ps1 had to offer, it still brings you in the scary mood it wants to present. The gameplay is fun, not hard at all, but still the difficulty doesn't ruin the narrative. I really felt the game was "finished", and wasn't slave to the constructs that videogames usually tend to use. Everytime you finish a part of the game you feel like saying "nice! It was cool!" but then another part jumps right in. I also liked the longevity of the game: I liked the fact that there are different "levels" of the game and that each level doens't take too long to finish or isn't that redundant.

Tens tens TENS ACROSS THE... BOARD!\t\t\t\t\t
