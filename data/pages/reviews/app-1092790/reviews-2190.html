- It tries to have a story. A quirky, nonsense story that you expect from all the indie games with stories. This ends up just getting in the way of replaying.
- Too much abuse of loud static and flashing light. I guess LOUD = SCARY LUL. Sound was leaking out from my headphone on some scenes even though the game was dead quiet for 99% of it.
- Terrible UI to create a false sense of immersion. Just ends up getting in the way of the game: oh hey you have to count your HP, oh hey your fingers are covering up the numbers on your cards (totally realistic btw, very relatable devs), oh hey your left mouse is used for everything so misclicking is part of the game I guess.

It's enjoyable if you enjoy the developers' quirky sense of humor, but it's definitely a step down from all its inspiration (like Slay the Spire).\t\t\t\t\t
