\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the first game I have seen that truly innovates on the roguelike deckbuilder subgenre, and it capitalizes on that untapped potential incredibly well.

I am a big fan of deckbuilders, but this game is still very welcoming to newcomers to the genre. You don't have to be an expert or even a fan of deckbuilders to enjoy this game to its fullest. There is far more content than meets the eye here, and the game is always switching things up on you, keeping gameplay fresh and exciting, while also being far simpler than most other deckbuilders.
However, if you are experienced with deckbuilder mechanics, the game still absolutely rewards you for your skill. It feels just as satisfying to craft a powerful deck in this game as any other. But just like many parts of this game, the rules and mechanics themselves are not just given to you in full. The game does a fantastic job at encouraging and rewarding experimentation, even for someone like myself that enjoys game knowledge and optimization. So your ability to learn quickly will be tested, and it feels so good to hit that Eureka moment!

This is also the first roguelike deckbuilder I've played that has a meaningful story to go with it. The story is more than just a frame for excellent gameplay, it's a genuine motivator to keep trying. I wanted to succeed more than just to win, I was genuinely on the edge of my seat to see where this game would go next. And yes, I thoroughly enjoyed the ride that this game took me on.
However, the gameplay is not a simple frame for the story to be told either! The gameplay borders on genius in multiple instances, and is one of the most impressive deckbuilders based on its gameplay alone! Inscryption genuinely holds up as one of the most dynamic and innovative card games I've ever played even outside of video games. All while being newcomer friendly, which just blows my mind.

This game is one of the most impressive games I've ever played, and is easily on my list of all-time favorite games. I cannot recommend it enough even if you've never played a deckbuilder before, and even at full price.\t\t\t\t\t
