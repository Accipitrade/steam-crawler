\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think this is probably one of the best deck building games I have ever had the pleasure of playing. What makes it great isn't just the deck building aspect but the amazingly deep story that goes along with it. Truly a masterpiece.

There were some moments where I felt like it might become grindy, but it honestly never overstayed its welcome. The moment it felt like it could become grindy, they introduced a mechanic that sped the game up significantly. Mainly, introducing new abilities for cards that suddenly turned something weak into something fairly overpowered. I think that's what this game does best is introduce mechanics in a smart and methodical way so you feel yourself becoming more powerful over the course of each act of the game through just the acquisition of more knowledge. I would actually sort of compare this game to The Witness in a way.

Each card battle was its own puzzle and as you play, you learn more about the inscriptions on the cards and what they mean and how they interact with other cards. You quickly discover what is really good and what is really bad. Much like The Witness, you have the ability to know the answers from the start if you look up mechanics online, but part of the joy of this game is learning about it yourself and making discoveries as those discoveries directly correlate to the story beats of the game.

Highly recommend this game, and I believe its probably my game of the year easily.\t\t\t\t\t
