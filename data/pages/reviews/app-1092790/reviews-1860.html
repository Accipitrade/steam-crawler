\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe less that is known about this game...the better.

Allow me to be kind of vague but kind of informative: Inscryption is a rougelite deckbuilder featuring an assortment of beasts, birds, and insects. You will sacrifice lesser creatures, such as squirrels, to play greater ones, like wolves and bears, to damage your shadowy opponent. Along the way, you can encounter different events that grant you cards, augment your cards, or gain items that will help ensure victory. There are also a few boss battles with some very interesting mechanics.

And that's all I can and want to say. I highly recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
