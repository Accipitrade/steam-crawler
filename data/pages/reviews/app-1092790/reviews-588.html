Everything in this game is purposeful. Losing is purposeful, using various items is purposeful, even the things you think be counter against you is purposeful. I think a better way to describe this is that its a story/puzzler first and rouge like/lite secondary. I do not know what else to say but I have beaten the game and I kind of want to go through it again and see if I can figure out the secrets i missed. We shall see.\t\t\t\t\t
