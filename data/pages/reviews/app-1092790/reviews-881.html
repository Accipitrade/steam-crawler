Although considering how the game features tons of secrets and hidden lore bits, I can only guess I have seen but one ending.

Great game that shows what a feat a small team can archieve with passion and enough time. And Inscryption has way, way too many small nods, ideas and secrets to be anything but a labor of love.\t\t\t\t\t
