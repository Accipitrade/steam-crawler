\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is will one of my first ever review of a game that I have 100% achievement. From the game play I've seen on Youtube, the game is supposed to be a mystery/thriller story based game as opposed to the typical rogue-lite deck builder games such as slay the spire. However, the game mechanics of the individual games types in Inscryption is very fun to the point of me almost being disappointed that it isn't one of the infinite deck builder type games. I am a huge mystery and horror game enjoyer so finding all the story based events in the game were fun. I only wish there were different finales on parts where I think choices should be. I enjoyed the entire game and had a fun time throughout the game play and going back to find out some lore. I hope the game has a sequel or a prequel looking more into the GameFuna company and all its mysteries.

5/5 or 95/100 individual review of the game

Update (12/14/2021)
There is the beta gameplay that has been implemented to address the replayability issue I talked about before. As a stand alone game, this now has the beta mods in place to allow for act 1's infinite gamemode along with challenges to make the game mode harder. This update does not give you any aid as it throws you straight into the battle without the supporting puzzle bonuses you would normally get while trying to pass through act 1's section.

5/5 and 100/100 I can not find any flaws to this game besides me having bad luck, but hey that's what I'm signing up for in a roguelite deckbiulder type game.\t\t\t\t\t
