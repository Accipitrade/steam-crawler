TL:DR:
With multiple interesting story lines built into one, a while seemingly unfair card battle system in place that relies on more RNG than skill in alot of cases, many different boss type battles, numerous card types, good music and a semi solid horror theme, I'd say that the Developer lives up to former games with this new title.

My Thoughts:
The game play itself is one of struggle at first, but as you learn the cards and strategies the game actually becomes quite the cake walk. I will say, at first I was quite annoyed at how the opponent does not have to follow the same rules as you, your cards require sacrifices and payments to be summoned while the enemy can just drop cards of high caliber on the field for free. It feels unfair even in late game when you can wipe the floor with every opponent, seeing the enemy drop a bear on the field for free still feels quite broken. The bosses all have some what unique abilities that change fights up after you defeat/deplete their "health bar" which in this games case is a candle system. The opening of the game is rather HARSH, showing you by force that you WILL die in this game more than once with a couple battles that feel hopeless, infact I noticed after the first 2 deaths that felt forced, the game got way easier as though this was scripted which, if you play through, it might be. So far Ive played this game for alittle over 6 hours due to the fact that after the first portion of the game you unlock even more game much like the Dev's past games. All in all, it feels well built, the music is GREAT, I do feel like while theres a lot of cards in the game, theres not enough variety while at the same time, theres some cards that dont need to exist.... *coughs* Zombie... All in all for a game that costs less than $20, this feels like a passion project that took on a life of its own. Like one of those game jam games that ended up as a full release. I hope to see more from this developer and look forward to future releases.\t\t\t\t\t
