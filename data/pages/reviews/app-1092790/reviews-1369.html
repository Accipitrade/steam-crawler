\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRokk my friend, you were so wrong about this game.

Inscryption may be my favorite game in the past couple years. As a lot of the reviews say, just play the game blind. Go.

After the cabin you have a change to a 2D game, I initially did not like this but I cared enough about the story to continue on. And I'm so glad I did. I put in 8 hours straight one day because I was so into the game, it becomes oddly emotional near the end, and I found myself literally talking out-loud thanking the characters for their games. This is not an exaggeration. Never has ANYTHING made me feel so attached to characters, yet this silly little card game out of all things resonated with my core.

All in all: 10/10 game
