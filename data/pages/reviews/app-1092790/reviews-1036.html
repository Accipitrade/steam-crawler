\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a really fun game that incorporates both card games mechanics, rogue-like mechanics, and escape room mechanics.

You advance through a game board (similar to Slay the Spire) and play card games against the opponent and bosses. You can use items or knowledge you've gathered to unlock various parts of the room you're in in order to try and escape it. I don't want to spoil the game too much since it is important to play and experience it on your own, as there are plenty gameplay and story twists.

I recommend the game, however, some mechanics will either surprise you for the better or annoy you.

I haven't yet finished the game, as there are some parts that have stopped me from being interested in continuing, however, I still recommend the game as it is really fun!\t\t\t\t\t
