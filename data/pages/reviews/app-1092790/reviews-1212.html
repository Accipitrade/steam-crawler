\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo this game is great, plot twist after plot twist.

But there are two things I really disliked about it and one minor gripe

-The fact the first stage of the game is stupid easy if you go squirrel totem and get one of these
-Card reappears in your hand when it dies
-Card counts for three blood when sacrificed
-Card can be sacrificed endlessly

And I disliked the part where they forced you to use magic, after having not used it the entire game and being forced to learn/understand it just for one encounter was not fun, needless to say.

Other then that the game was great, the minor gripe I have with it is just that I wish the game was a bit less... Oh figure these secrets out to unlock the full story, I am sure the community will and probably already has done it but now I have to sit here and wait for a YouTuber to put all the pieces together into one coherent video.

Kinda just wish I could have gotten the entire package in my play through rather then having to look outside the game afterwards.\t\t\t\t\t
