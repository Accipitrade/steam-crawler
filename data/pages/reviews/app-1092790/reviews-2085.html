THIS REVIEW CONTAINS SPOILERS, IF YOU DISLIKE THAT THEN DO NOT READ.

Yes, the game is good but I'll go into more detail here.

If you enjoy card games, deck building, RNG and rogue like... Then this is a game for you. The game graphic wise is really good. It's not that spooky at least to me. The issue I have with the game is you are beset with a bunch of rules. You must solve puzzles outside the game in order to progress which is fine and dandy and fun. The enviroment is amazing. What I do not like is the RNG of the map and of course the normal RNG of deck building/drawing cards.

Too many times have I built a really awesome deck having a Urekl(7/7) into say a 14/14 by merging them together. Too many times have I gotten my previous death cards that are 7/7s that can fly/block flying, etc etc that cost 0 cause I always keep a pelt just in case. Too many times have I kept my deck small so I can top deck like a champ and still -lose.-

Too many times does he break rules because it is his game. He doesn't have to pay costs cause you are over coming his challenges. Nothing like having 4/4 bears come on down(Which I found out is because I was going too far too fast and the game basically ***** you because you didn't solve some obtuse puzzle in the corner).

The game is enjoyable but I am 6 hours in and I'm just done bending over and receiving the RNG's God's giant spiked dildo up the butt. So if you like slamming your face into a concrete wall over and over till you over come then you will like this game. For me, the game is enjoyable but just frustrating.

The game is beautiful and the people who made this game deserve every penny coming to them much like Pony Island but I just can't fully endorse it. I'd give it a 7 out of 10 overall.
