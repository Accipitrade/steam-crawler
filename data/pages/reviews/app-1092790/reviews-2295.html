\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm going to be completely honest, I didn't think I would like this game so much. I'm not the best when it comes to strategy and card games, so trying to figure out how to beat the different sections was incredibly difficult.

Despite this, I'm a huge fan of the game. I absolutely loved the plot and what the game was centered around. It was very entertaining, especially being a fan of any sort of suspense and horror. I just loved the theme of the game.

I enjoyed the gameplay so much that I basically finished the game in one sitting. I really liked the fact that the game came in levels of increasing difficulty. There were a ton of mechanics to learn, but they weren't too complicated, which made the game that much easier to keep playing.

The atmosphere and audio of the game fit very well in my opinion and the developers did a really good job. My only complain was the art style of the game, which at later parts seemed questionable. Although I did enjoy my time within each section of the game, I definitely enjoyed the earlier sections a lot more.

I definitely recommend giving this game a try. It didn't take too long to finish, but I was fairly enticed the whole time.\t\t\t\t\t
