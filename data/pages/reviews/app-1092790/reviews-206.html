No spoiler review

I'm not too into card type games, but this games story, music, character design, and overall atmosphere was so captivating for me that it sucked me right into all of it. Even though all of those aspects I just listed aren't ones I normally give too much attention, they were just that good. There's so many little puzzles and mysteries you can solve while working on the main story that make learning the game's mechanics much more enjoyable. The only thing keeping me from giving this game a 10 is the lack of replayability I felt after completing the story. There was an attempt to remedy this problem when Kaycee's Mod was introduced, and it does a decent job at keeping the game fresh if you want to continue on after the story. But I still found myself not playing that much more afterwards. Overall though, this game was well worth the $20 price tag for me. I'd likely put it in my top 5 games I've played, I would love to be able to play it for the first time again. If you can play this game without having any of the story or other secrets spoiled for you, do it.\t\t\t\t\t
