Out of all the characters I'd say that the robot guy (I forgot his name) is definitley the most interesting out of the 4 so I'm glad they chose him, it was cool to see another character in the Act 1 art style. At first the gameplay was boring and annoying to me but I progressively got used to it and sort of enjoyed myself, but by the time that happened the game ended. The bosses were fun and unique and I especially enjoyed the boss where you create your own boss, it was a highlight for me. Anyway the end of the game was honestly super cool, but the story going on in the background was interesting at first but got more uninteresting as the game progressed. I just feel like this game had so much missed potential with the 2nd and 3rd Acts, not that they were awful or anything but they're just kinda disappointing compared to what could've been. TLDR: The 1st Act's gameplay and story were very fun, the 2nd Acts's gameplay and story were alright and the 3rd Act's gameplay and story were better than Act 2's but nowhere near the 1st.


7/10\t\t\t\t\t
