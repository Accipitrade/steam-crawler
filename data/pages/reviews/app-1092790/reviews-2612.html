\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLoved the whole game, it's brilliant and was completely worth the pre-order.

It's a very well done card game, the gameplay is both simple to get hang of, and very complex, with the infinite possibilities and the player having a lot of impact on the game with the decision making and little rng during the rounds

However, as much as I loved the story and gameplay of all the in-game "worlds", it's a real shame we can't get to see more of the last 2 scrybes' worlds, especially when the game gives you just a taste of what you could be having with those. But even more important, for a game marketed as a roguelike/lite, its sadly not a one with a whole lot of replayability value. Sure, you can play the story mode again from the start (I definitely will), but sadly it's lacking its own game mode of just being a f**king amazing roguelike you can play over and over. And it's a shame, because it already has everything it needs to be that

EDIT: With the addition of Kaycee's mod update, this game has become exactly what I was hoping for, and now it's an absolutely awesome roguelike I can put my free time into. 10/10
Only way this game could become even better is if the other worlds/gamemodes from the story mode got their own roguelike mods as well. Overall great job, this game is a masterpiece\t\t\t\t\t
