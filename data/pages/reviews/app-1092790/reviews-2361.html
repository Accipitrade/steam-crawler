Not quite the card game you're looking for?

Inscryption is an incredible card game at first, it might remind players of other classics like Slay the Spire. After a few rounds, you may start to understand that there are some abilities which are quite OP and will end encounters in a couple of moves - what gives?

It's by game design! You'll progress through several parts of the game all while playing cards, but the rules and visuals will keep changing, significantly altering the way you play and interact with the game world. Inscryption encourages lateral thinking, and figuring out the puzzles gives off great satisfaction.

This game may not be for all card game fans because of how the game morphs over time, but it is certainly recommended for those that enjoy puzzles and card games.
