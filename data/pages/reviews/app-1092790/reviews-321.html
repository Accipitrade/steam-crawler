\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSTEAM DECK REVIEW: Plays very well on the steam deck, recommend binding Y to space bar for quick bell ringing. D-pad is great for moving around but sometimes there are issues with getting the right movement command to register when looking around the field, i.e. trying to look specifically at the scale or at your items or expand your hand.

Overall, this is a quirky and wonderful game that starts like a roguelike before a few surprises and a story hits you in the face out of nowhere then settles back into a roguelike. Highly recommend it for those who enjoy deck builder roguelikes! The ambience, underlying story, and twists make this game truly special. The game is also not afraid to let you make incredibly overpowered combos and combine broken mechanics, it's part of the allure! The final state roguelike is more true to a deckbuilder roguelike, which can be incredibly punishing and/or rewarding with some replay value but not as much as other roguelikes in the genre like Slay the Spire unless you really like restrictive challenges.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tthis game is a masterpiece, genuinely one of the best games ive ever played. so first of all, the atmosphere. ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ incredible. each of the scrybes has their own little setting and they all look amazing. generally dark and mysterious which i absolutely love, and they nailed it. next, the story. i was constantly interested and excited to see what happens next. then theres the characters. they are all good. luke carder was definitely favorite, Kevin Saxby did such an amazing job as an actor. Kevin's acting was seriously next level. of course, the gameplay was very good too. many cards are in the game and there's plenty of builds you can go for. this is especially true for act 2. i really dont have much bad to say about this game. in act 2, there is an enemy that you can use to infinitely farm foils, which can also be used to make an infinitely powerful card without even needing to pass a turn, if you have the right build using ouroboros. aside from that one oversight though, the game is easily a 10/10. actually, this goes beyond a 10/10. this is like at least 20/10 with how insanely good it is.\t\t\t\t\t
