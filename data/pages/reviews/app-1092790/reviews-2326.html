One of the best games I've ever played. Keep in mind that I've never liked deck builder and card games, but somehow after playing this, I was convinced otherwise. This game is filled up to the brim with unexpected plot twists, thought out comedy parts, a charming story and a remarkable ending. It is a narrative masterpiece and it includes amazing graphics to go along with it.

This game is worth its price tag and you should give it a try. You will thank yourself for doing so in the long run.\t\t\t\t\t
