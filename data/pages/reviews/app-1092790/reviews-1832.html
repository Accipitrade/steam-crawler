\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdit: Old review preserved below. Still mostly true, but the devs are really great and are hard at work adding a proper roguelike mode. This mode does fix the whole final bossed getting hard-countered, but I do hope they add some new intermediate bosses. The same three still get stale very, very quickly. Regardless, my faith is renewed; my hope, restored. With most of the previous issues being worked on by devs that listen, I am confident that this game will be both everything it promises to be and even more.

Let me start by saying this is a very good game. In a vacuum, I'd rate it about $16 give or take. I enjoyed this twists and the story, and even with all the switch ups, there was just enough nurtured familiarity with the systems to not leave the player hanging. That being said, I cannot just recommend this to a friend and say, "Yeah, take a look at the steam page, I think this might be up your alley." It would seem a safe assumption that the store page would give a potential buyer some idea of what is in store.

This game is not a roguelite deckbuilder, despite what the store page would suggest. That is indeed contained in this game, but it is very shallow and grows stale in about 5 hours. What the game actually is: two fairly simple escape rooms with some minor interactions with the card game aspect and a bunch of busywork in between them. Quite frankly, I just want Act 1 properly fleshed out with any variety of bosses at all, more than 25 cards, and an end boss that doesn't die instantly and utterly to a card you start every run with.

I cannot, in good conscience, recommend this game through Steam, even though I like it. I am still recommending it to my friends, as I can then explain that what they see on here is not the part that will be fun for very long, and that they should buy this game for the story and cool meta-interactions.
tl;dr objectively good game, just not what is promised.

p.s. stoat is the worst. a backseating backstabber. still a really solid card though.
p.p.s. wait, is This game actually stoat?
p.p.p.s. the whole trapper/trader thing is really good tho, I didn't even notice it until the cool flip\t\t\t\t\t
