A couple random highlights:
- One of the cool things I enjoyed was the game letting you design cards as part of the story and it being relatively easy to design busted stuff.
- Card mechanics were also unique and interesting, in line with the dark horror theme.
- The horror story that is occurring is pretty well written, but most importantly, while I was never 'scared', there were numerous moments that sent chills up my spine. The ambiance is perfect and the unknown opponents's shifts through the bosses creates some spooky feelings.

I went through the 10 hrs I have on record in about 2 sittings and was able to complete the story. While there is certainly continued re-play value, the game was easily worth full price to me through this point and I'd highly recommend it to anyone.\t\t\t\t\t
