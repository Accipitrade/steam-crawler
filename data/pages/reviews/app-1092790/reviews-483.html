\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy son told me to try this game, so I did.
At first, it was interesting. Good idea, interesting mechanics, excellent story, decent visuals, it had a lot going for it.

Then, I started getting past the stage of exploring and actually trying to win at it and then the fun drained right out of it. The random nature of the card draws makes it so you can play a perfect game with no mistakes and still lose because of RNGesus. You cannot even edit your deck, just take whatever you randomly get and try to build something out of chaos.

I was frustrated, more than enjoying myself, for most of my playtime in this game. Even after beating what I thought was the main game, it added another layer where I had to essentially start over. Now, I will grant that those secondary games / levels were actually way more fun than the rest of the game I had played up to that point. You could control your choices way more. Unfortunately they were quickly beaten and now the only way to get back is to reset everything..... which would be self-torture in it's ultimate form.

Then, after beating the main game, after much cussing and swearing, I took a stab at Kaycee's mod, hoping to play those middle stages again. Nope. The mod takes the original frustrating game and makes it even harder, meaning more frustration when the cards you need stubbornly refuse to be drawn. At this point continuing playing is more about getting an achievement than actually enjoying the game, because it stopped actually being fun a long time ago.

So, if you have a high tolerance for frustration, you will probably enjoy the hell out of this game. If you enjoy getting all the achievements, you will come to want to murder the developers for creating this game. For everyone else, play the main game for the story and avoid Kaycee's mod unless you are a masochist.

-Phicksur

----------------------------------
Second review, after another 25 hours of achievement hunting.
----------------------------------
While my original complaints about the game's random nature stand, I have learned that it is possible to start the game after the initial point if you want to play just the middle parts of the game. That causes my initial negative reaction to the first part of the game, which is beyond frustrating, to be tamed down and give this a good review. It is a good game, since even the creators recognized that the players might enjoy levels other than the main one.

Kaycee's Mod, however, is still evil incarnate. Just saying.\t\t\t\t\t
