~Pros~

- Very stylised, and the variety of aesthetics involved with this game kept it fresh
- The card game play starts off slow enough for you to grasp the basics easily
- The character design gives an array of different styles, ideas and nuances to different sections of the game, which i rather enjoy
- The puzzles are tough but doable, making you think hard about the logic behind them

~Cons~

- This is definately a game for those well versed with certain game knowledge, people new to gaming in general might have a hard time understanding certain aspects of the game
- Shorter than i was hoping, i wanted to be able to do a full section of each of the main cast of characters, whereas some unfortunately weren't as impactful

I am yet to play the new rogue-like mode added recently however i'm very excited to try it out!

9/10 well worth the purchase\t\t\t\t\t
