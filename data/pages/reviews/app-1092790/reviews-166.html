\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGod this game is incredible. The story, the gameplay, the characters and their relationships\\personalities, the VISUALS, everything is genuinely perfect. Very very high on the "replayability" scale too lmao. It's such a fun, fun game; very difficult at times but it was never enough to where I wanted to stop playing. Lots of trial and error. You can't just brute force your way through everything; it takes foresight and managing your options. MANY secrets, most of which I never found and would have to play again to find. Sound design is also incredible, from the musical elements to the sound effects. Lots of items make soft, satisfying sounds when you click on or move them, which give a very relaxing vibe to the whole game. When it wants to be calm, it's calm. When it wants to be stressful, IT'S STRESSFUL. Theming is spot on.

Spoilers? Not really?: Even after you're done with the main story, you get an extra little "mod" that you can play, that is less story based (on the surface level), and more of a thing where you try over and over to beat increasingly tough challenges.

My review doesn't do it justice, you gotta play it for yourself. Extremely compelling.\t\t\t\t\t
