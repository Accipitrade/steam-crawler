\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like roguelike deckbuilders and have patience for them even when they rely very heavily on luck, then you might like this game. There are some wacky twists and turns along the way.

Unfortunately, the card game (which is central to the gameplay) is just too unpolished and reliant on random chance for me. I'm not sure why this game has such a highly positive rating.

Edit: this does change quite a bit after the first act of the game. I still don't care for it personally, and since this is just my recommendation, I wouldn't feel comfortable telling people to buy the game.\t\t\t\t\t
