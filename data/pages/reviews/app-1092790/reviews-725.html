This game is not over-hyped, its overwhelmingly positive for very good reasons:
It is not overly expensive, the story is fantastic, the game play was challenging but not so much that you don't want to continue.
It took me 13 hours to complete the game and that may not seem like a lot to spend the money, however, it has got other endless options to keep you playing.
It is the first game I have enjoyed playing on my own (rather than multiplayer games) in a very long time.
Inscryption is perfectly executed in my opinion.
