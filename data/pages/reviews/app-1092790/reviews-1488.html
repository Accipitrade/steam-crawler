\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo first off...
If you plan on playing or are interested in playing Inscryption, do NOT spoil anything because if you don't you'll have a better time playing the game.

I'm not sure if anything bellow is considered spoilers so continue at your own risk.

Inscryption is a game that I would recommend that you play if you enjoy deck building games or if you like to play some roguelikes. The strongest part of the game is the ACT 1 which can be turned into an endless mode with the Kaycee's mod which I recommend trying at least once.

In my opinion ACT 2 was the weakest just because it felt just a little bit like I was playing something else, BUT I do think that the ACT 2 was a great experience and that it could have been expanded upon. And what I mean by that is I think it would be great if Inscryption were to get a DLC it would be about expanding or something similiar to the ACT 2.

ACT 3 was another thing entirely. While playing I felt that i wasn't being told enough information and hints toward the puzzles but I still managed to complete the "campaing" without looking up a guide. The factory was a nice change from the cabin but it just wasn't on the same level.

Conclusion:
Inscryption is something that surprised me by how good it was and I think it is a game you should at least put on your bucket list.\t\t\t\t\t
