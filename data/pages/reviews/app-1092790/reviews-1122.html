\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNARRATIVE: 8/10
- Becomes slightly repetitive and redundant but considering the gameplay format, this still contains one of the more surprising and unpredictable stories of the last several years in gaming.
+ Spooky but not scary. No jump scares or frightening moments.
+ The format of the game and story COMPLETELY changes on you in a way that the advertising does not show at all. Go in as blind as possible!

GAMEPLAY & FUN FACTOR: 9/10
+ Genuinely one of the most creative, addictive, genre-bending games I've ever played. If you are a fan of horror ANY card games you need to give this a try!
+ The card mechanics are a simpler combination of Hearthstone and Yu-Gi-Oh, but my first full playthrough took 25 hours and it managed to keep throwing fresh mechanics at me the whole way through. Throughout the campaign you will encounter and unlock 4 deck types, and each of them have long, dedicated story and campaign sections.

TECHNICAL FIDELITY: 10/10
+ The bleak, oppressive horror aesthetic is just fun enough to make you want to continue deeper despite how authentically disturbing the antagonists are.
+ Contains many convincing and fun "glitch" effects and 4th-wall breaking sequences.

OVERALL SCORE: 10/10 -- A Modern Classic\t\t\t\t\t
