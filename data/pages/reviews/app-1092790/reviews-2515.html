\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I really just wanna say meh for this game. Im not good at reviews so please keep that in mind

Let me start with the good:
The 1st act was just. Mmmmmm perfection. It brought the spook, it brought the rougelite, randomly generated map, puzzles. Very good. The demo sold me and had a blast playing the rest of act 1.

Act 2: Oh boy, I was concerned to be honest I skipped this as fast as possible, it never appealed to me. A complete change to what I expected. I was hoping this wasnt going to be like this for the rest of the game. I figured when you are in the overworld you would be able to choose the next scrybe to go up againts. With similar effects to Act 1. My hope were brought up when entering act 3 thinking "Pefect! Another level just like act 1.

Act 3: Just like Act 1 with a different rule set and fixed routes. No more random generation. This game practically turns into a linerier story.

Overall: I feel robbed and unsatisfied. I was under the impression the Game would go like XYZ based on what the store page was showing and the ads and what not. But it just feels unfinished. Unfinished to the idea that After defeating the beast scrybe, and the tech scrybe, I was expecting to be able to face off the other two with there own twists and rule sets. EVEN after defeating PO3 you get to see the jist of what the other two could have been and I was all for it. Very disappointed the other two did not recieve that much care. \t\t\t\t\t
