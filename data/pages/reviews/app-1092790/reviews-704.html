\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf I had known what this game was really like I wouldn't have bought it. It looked like a roguelike deck builder with some "escape the room" elements, and that seemed cool. I read so many of the reviews that said "don't look up more information, just buy the game," so I decided to try the demo first. The demo again seemed cool, representative of what I thought the game would be like, so I bought it.
The first segment of the game is fine, a little RNG heavy, but fine. If that had been fleshed out into a full game I would have no problems. Unfortunately, after you beat the first segment in the game things really fall apart. You get stuck in a black 4x4 room with nothing to interact with, I actually had to look up what I was supposed to do next because there was nothing to give direction (I had to quit the game and start a new game.) Then you get presented with these horrible, corny live-action videos of the developer pretending to be a youtuber, it's so hard to watch. It's cringy. If you've been around long enough to remember when pornos had stories you'll know exactly the level of acting on display here.
Anyways, afterwards you are taken into a much different game, a 2D top down adventure game that has you collecting cards to play a remixed game similar in concept to the first, but without permadeath. When you beat that you get another iteration of the same game. You essentially play through four or so iterations of the same game, it's like every concept of the game was slapped together back-to-back with some crappy videos and partial story (which feels thematically reminiscent of Haque, a very good roguelike game btw) to try to hold it together. It's very disappointing.\t\t\t\t\t
