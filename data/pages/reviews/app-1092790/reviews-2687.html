\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is what I would call "an experience", very much like other Daniel Mullins games. I got a solid 12 hours out of it, and enjoyed most of that greatly. I'll go through it at least one more time eventually, but will go out of the way to not break the game by using the OP cards and effects I now know exist.

If you're here for a roguelike card game that will be highly replayable, look elsewhere. If you are looking for just pure horror and nothing else, this isn't really that either. This is a game that is significantly better if you go into it without spoilers, but expect some very good atmosphere most of the time, lots of good card game mechanics and gameplay, lots of little puzzles, and absolute tons of lore and secrets to possibly find. There are lots of great jokes and taking the time to stop and explore a bit, read a bit, that sort of thing will help the game last longer and get you more out of it than rushing to the end.

The visuals are usually extremely good and immersive, though some sections are better than others. The audio is absolutely fantastic, great music and sound effects all around. The gameplay is solid though it really is designed around playing blind and not knowing much, as if you do know the solutions to the puzzles, and what the mechanics and cards do, it is far too easy to cheese the majority of the game.

The simplest way to give this a glowing rec is to say that if you want a game where you will not know what it coming next, that might keep you on the edge of your seat, and that can help you reclaim a little bit of that child-like wonder of gaming that is so hard to get as an adult, this is one of the best games I have ever played and could ever suggest you play. I honestly wouldn't even suggest watching the trailers for the game, they give you a tiny glimpse into what's here but in my opinion whether or not you will love the game has nothing to do if the trailer seems appealing to you. If you want some really cool twists and turns in a game, and enjoy card game gameplay, pick this up as it is worth the price and if it gets spoiled for you it will never be un-spoiled.\t\t\t\t\t
