\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIncryption sets a new standard for quality and gameplay variety in its genre and I'd happily recommend it to anyone, even those who don't usually find card games appealing.

The core card game could easily stand on its own, as it's fun, satisfying and complex enough to stay interesting throughout without becoming convoluted. Finishing Incryption has me yearning for more, and I eagerly await whatever comes next from these developers!\t\t\t\t\t
