\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is easily one of the more interesting card games out there. I would clarify that most of that 'interesting' is coming not from the card gameplay itself but how the game presents itself. Inscryption is a decent or pretty good card game with out-there presentation/story which probably outshines the actual card game.

I came for the cards but really I stayed to see what happens. This dev has a great habit of doing whatever the heck he wants, being weird and original, and it provides mystery and surprise throughout.

The card game isn't bad but I was mostly not amazed by it generally. The UI while interesting kinda sucks. You can't see your HP/opponent HP while choosing what card to draw which is a big weird yikes. There are a couple similarly dumb decisions in this vein. Beyond that, the card play does manage to be occasionally exciting and it's still satisfying building up your deck as you go through a run. As you get further, there's a good amount of variation in cards/decks to keep it fresh.

Again the game's strength is very easily in how it tells its stories and weaves in snippets of mystery regularly. Pretty good/okay card game, awesome curveball-filled lore/characters etc.

Easy to recommend this game despite my sort of meh attitude towards the card play.\t\t\t\t\t
