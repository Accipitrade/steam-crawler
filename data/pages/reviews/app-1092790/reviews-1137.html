\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I had fun at times, the advertisements are misleading; When the entire game suddenly changed into something not even resembling what it was previously, I *hated* it and the creators with a passion for taking away what had been such an intriguing game up to then, and to some degree, I still hold that grudge!

Chapter 1 was amazing, solid aesthetics, excellent mechanics, interesting hints at what's to come and chapter 2 ruined all of it for me for quite a while by changing the aesthetics and stripping down the mechanics to the point where this tense, bloody, fight-for-survival card game became akin to a pokemon tcg. Chapter 3 managed to just barely bring me back in, but the middle has almost entirely soured my opinion of the rest of the game, not even to touch on how the story reaches for metanarrative notes that it has absolutely not earned.

I think it would have been a far more enjoyable experience if I had known what I was in for, but instead the game tries to be all smug and "oh, I bet you've never seen this before, how cool, right, guys?!" about it and it's excruciating.

Learn the "twist" first and then buy it on sale.
