\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can't explain to you how much I LOVE this game!! I've played it for around 145 hours so far and even though I've completed all of the game and Kaycee's Mod, which is not a mod but another game mode, and gotten most of the achievements I'm still playing it just because I love it so much!
I've never played a deck building game and only am familiar with them because I saw Yu-Gi-Oh a couple decades ago.

Just so you know this game has 3 different Acts with slightly different game play and different art styles and locations in each. The first act tends to be peoples favorite and the second can throw people for a loop because of how different it is. In each act you have to relearn how to play. There are different cards and different game mechanics in each act. But enough similarities that you aren't totally lost. If act 2 is not to your liking just push through because chances are act 3 will be!

This game is chock full of lore which depending on your interest level you can choose to ignore some of, while still enjoying the game. If you like deep diving into lore then you have many hours to play and many things to try to find out, off the beaten path!

Kaycee's "Mod" Mode is separate from the main story but does have key points of information relating to the lore of Inscryption. It looks like and has the same play style as the first act but is longer and much more complex and challenging. It levels up in difficulty as you play it by adding challenges on top of challenges until it is extremely difficult. But to me it is always very fun and every time you fail you learn something new and your strategy gets better.

This game can have a lot of trial and error as you get familiar with the cards and the mechanics. You have to look at it as a learning process and embrace the fail!

!!!!Dying is part of the process and part of the story!!! You have to die to open up new abilities so don't be sad when you fail just keep going. The more you play the better you get and honestly the more you like the game!
