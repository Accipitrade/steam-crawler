\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEverything about this game is amazing, the story, the gameplay, the characters, everything.

First, the story. To keep a spoiler free description, in the game at the start it seems like you are simply playing a board game with a strange man. Certain areas on the boards give you cards which you use later in battles (I'll explain that in gameplay). However, upon the first death you find that the man will kill you if you lose, and you play as another "Challenger". The seems to have much more to him that it seems. The game does a wondrous job of keeping that undertone of creepy the whole time as well. I'm gonna end the story bit here because its hard to do this without spoilers.

Next, the gameplay. The game is similar to Yugioh, or Pokemon, in the sense that you are given cards that fight each other. Each card does a certain amount of damage and has a certain amount of health. Some cards can only be placed if you sacrifice other cards. And then there are "Sigils" for the cards that can grant them special abilities, for example, the ability to hide during your opponents turn so it can not be killed. Along the board you'll find more cards and different areas where you can buff your cards. In battle, you can either attack an opponents card, or attack the opponent directly. The goal is to have dealt five more damage to your opponent than he has to you.

Last, the characters. All of the characters are perfect, and they have an amazing synergy together. The characters feel human and you get attached to them so quickly. Again, hard to go into this without spoilers, but I'll try my best. Throughout the game you worry for certain characters, you fear certain characters, you enjoy the company of certain characters, but you never hate any of them. All of the characters, in my opinion, are absolutely impossible to hate.

This game is a solid 10/10, my only complaint would be the price, but because of the amazing mechanics, I can see myself replaying this game even after 100%ing it. Even then the sales for this game take it to 10 dollars I believe, if you decide to wait for that.\t\t\t\t\t
