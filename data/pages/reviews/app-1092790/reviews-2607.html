Because now even tho the for me personal bad stuff in the story is still there and i still dislike most of the game after act one but now you can just play the story (would recomend doing this first) and then play the apsolute amazing first act rougelike like a real rougelike. Wich is just chef's kiss.

(Will keep the rest of the review for every one intrested in why the story is not good in my opinion so they can decide if they wanna play it or just jump in the rougelike)

Wow this game is something.
TLDR: If you want a good rougelite game. Leav this store page this is not a rougelite game. Its only for rougly 4-5 hours a roguelite and then for 6-7 (depending on how you play) its a completly diffrent game with a completly diffrent premis.

For evry one else who has more time to spare here my normal explanaition:
(english is not my first language so sorry for typos)

I tried the demo on release day and i loved it. I enoyjed it so much that i played the demo for roughly 1 hr, and coulnd get enough. So then after beating the demo after getting used to the game, i went to the store and had to buy it.
And wow i loved it the at the time for me unknown first act was perfect. For me it was the most enoyojeble Deckbuilding rougelite i had played, and spezially in Oktober with Halloween around the corner it was a good setting as well. And from here on this review will have some spoilers. I would still recomment to read it because other wise there is a good chance you do a miss buy. Like others have pointed out this game as 3 act's. And again the first part is so well done (game play, setting, atmosphere, visuals and oh man the music is so good).
After beating the first act i was still really itressted becaues i thought this could lead to an intressting story. But oh boy was i wrong. The second act also started intressting but, its not like the first act any more and nothing of that is shown any where on there store page. I understand why they have done it, but this alone can be a deal breaker for a lot of people. But any ways i was still intressted and it still looked fun. Until the moment you realise how easy the second act is.
The second act is not a roguelite game any more its closer to nomral card games where you have a hub world you run around to get mini quest/puzzles done to unlock boss fights, and collect new cards throu a shop keeper or/and booster packs. I beat the entire second act with nearly (only a couple of changes) the beggining deck you chose at the start. Wich is why im saying its way to easy. What is the point for me do get coins to trade them in for boosters or cards when the free stuff you get is more than enough and you need to do only minor changes on your starter deck. Compared that to the boss fights of act 1 where you had to think what your doing and play around intressing mechanics. All of that is gone in act 2. Add on top of the realy cool chilling antmosphere dissapiering and its basicly a walk throu the park. But hey atleast the music was still pretty good for the most part. The humor implemented in act 2 is also preatty good. At least for me. It was just a few jokes here and there in good and fitting moments.
So act 2 was wrapping up i didnt enoyjed it as much as act one (i was hope a lot during act 2 it would go back to act 1) but it was still fun to see all the stuff from act 1 in this new world. And it didnt went on for to long right when it starts do drag a bit its wrapping up.
So here i am watchin the new unlocked videos and hoping to go back to the roots of act one. And oh boy was i about to get dissapointed. It starts relativly prommising going back to the game play of act one, with you sitting on a table and playing it basicly. BUT you are not in this mysterious dark creepy cabin in the woods, with a mysterious being, any more. And the chilling antmosphere is also gone. Instead you basicly sit in a abandon factory and the mysetious levitating eyes in the dark and creepy "voice". Is replaced with a robot with a giant screen as a face wich illuminates a lot of the space. Also the whole astetics are overall completly diffrent, as an exaple you dont have cards any more you have floppy discs. And the weights that tip the scale are not teeth any more, no its screews....
And even tho it returens a little bit to its roots its just bare minimum. Its still not a roguelite any more. The intressting mechanics like the sacreficeing and death cards are gone etc. You still have the chance to create 2 cards but its like 30 mins befor the end so you will barley use them. And the music is just bad at this point as well, there like only a couple of good sound tracks left. Wich is a shame because in act 1 i loved evry single one. And now its less than 3 good tracks left.
Also with the one boss that whants you to upload data from your hard drive to use it in the fight (pictures) im never a big fan of that because it copletly ruins the mood. I would assume most people dont have fiting imigies on there pc just in case a game whant you to use them. But the one thing that nearly made me alt+f4 is when they tell you hey the next picture is made it to a card and if its destorey we delet it from your pc. Like wtf is that yes its easy too "cheese" it by just not playing the card.
But why the ♥♥♥♥ they think they can just delet pictures form peoples pc is mind blowing in my opinion. It also adds 0 fun too the boss because you either risk losing data on ur pc or you have a usless card in your hand. Wow good desingn. And i can understand this is not as big of a proplem for evry one but i saw no review menting it. And this alone will make me probably avoid futur games from these developors.
Well at this point i was just done with the game evry thing that made me love the demo/act 1 is gone and replaced by a worse verions of itself and i mean litrely evry thing. On top of that the plot if you wanna call it that, act 3 is just act 2 all over again go and defeat 4 boss enemys.
Its even the exacte same map just looking diffrent like something out of a sci-fi movie form the 80's or 90's.
And to top evrything for some reason they had to but in so much bad and really cringey "humor" or "jokes" if you can really call them that wich evry time it happens (wich is quiet often in act 3). Just ruins the mood completly. Spezially at the end where all of a sudden youre basicly playing Yu-Gi-Oh. Like wtf. The tldr of the end is normal stuff into mood ruining joke into a quiet sad and epathetic moment into a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Yu-Gi-Oh joke wich ruins it completly.
Haha sad moment now use a duel disc to duel this god like creature. Like come on who had this idea.
I get it humor is subjetiv. But you can still critic it.
So at the end i just sad there fealing annoyed by what i just witnessed, being frustrated that such a good for me personally even perfect rougelite deckbuilder game got ruind, to force a story wich was just unecessery and also just went no where. And ultimatly feeling like i got bamboozeld to waste 17,99€.
If i could i would refunde it. Im awear games or movies that hook you with one thing to subverse youre expectations exist but in this case its just so so so bad made, and sadly ruins a pottentialy good rougelite for nothing. This game even made me want to write a review wich is the first time that happend (the other 2 or 3 times where in steam event moments for badge stuff etc so i dont count them).
So my only hope is that they will releas a free dlc that makes the rougelite part a bit more fleshed out and playeble as stand alone. (if it would be a standalone rougle lite right now it would be just way to short, as said in my tldr on top.)
As for now i will deinstall it and regret buying it sadly.\t\t\t\t\t
