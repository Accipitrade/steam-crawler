\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have beaten the game at this point and gone back to get all the achievements.

1. Atmosphere:
This uncanny valley type of game comes with details that will take your card game mind to the next level. The surrounding cabin will make you uncomfortable, yet, photogenic. The details on the character's designs are done extremely well. The music fits perfectly, truly encompassing you in this trapped adventure. There is nothing that I didn't think fit the game. It was designed perfectly and uniquely to its own story. It also breaks through the fourth wall, which can make a break game ambiance. In this case, it did so perfectly that it enriched the experience.

2. Story:
I will save you from the spoilers and just tell you that the story is written with great care and detail. The story is surprisingly much deeper than you would think a card game would provide. The writers wrote a beautiful tale that I commend them for and hope that other game writers can be inspired by it.

3. Gameplay:
Playing a board game with this mysterious person as you try to win to escape makes for a weird yet perfect game. I found this game to be extremely addicting as, during my first play-through, I was glued to this game for two straight days till I beat it. The board game is well put together, yet it's not hard to catch on but requires a strategy to win. I rarely care about achievements but this game made me want to go back to get every single one of them.

4. Price:
This game is well worth the full price of $30

-Conclusion-
This game is a masterpiece. Truly something that isn't just a game but also a piece of art and an experience. I purposely didn't write much into this review as I don't want to spoil anything. It's best to go in knowing little to nothing about this game and just be taken away by how wonderfully put together it is. This is one of those games where you will tell yourself, "I wish I could play it for the first time again".

Score: ! 10/10 !
