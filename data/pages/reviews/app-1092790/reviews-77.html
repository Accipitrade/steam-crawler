The card game has a dynamic number of ways to enjoy winning or losing..
until you get to Kaycee's mod.

To be clear the base game is superb. The mod/challenge mode portion of the game...
RNG decides way too much, even for a card game, and the game has been balanced around people who have 1000 hours of theorycrafting in inscryption discords. It's clear that your decisions within the card games are extremely simple. Not much complex going on here. 95% of your value in decision making will come from how you build your deck. I would get it if it became punishing later on, but it is punishing immediately. How many excellent games are ruined by loud minorities that complain about balance. Don't balance games around 1% of people who have no life. Balance games around your first time players, if someone is playing 1000 hours of inscryption and asking for a harder challenge, they are mentally ill. Don't change based on mentally ill opinions.

Main Game: 5/5
Kaycee's Mod: 2/5
Overall: 4.5/5\t\t\t\t\t

One of the best Card Games I've played since HearthStone!

The Game play is seamless, Fluid, Satisfying and easy to learn.

So many synergies and damn near limitless strategies to try and beat The DM.

Doing Rituals to combined cards and their abilities was probably my favorite part of my playthroughs.

The Cards are well balanced and the further you go down the maps the DM's cards along with your own, scale accordingly.

High re-playatability

Amazing and unsettling atmosphere, I felt on edge during my entire time with this game.

If you're looking for a new game to try out I highly Recommend Inscryption.




I absolutely love this game. 10/10
