\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay... to be fair. The game you get when you buy this is amazing! The card game you get to play for the first little while is fun, amazing, and pretty much infinitely enjoyable. I loved playing the game, and trying to discover the secret that exists in the game.

Then I did... and the new game happens... and it sucks. The decks that were so fun to make, and cards that were so good to play, and the graphics that felt great for the kind of game this is all went away. Once you get to the "RPG section" it's not even the same game any more. There's no more custom cards to play around with. The graphics go away. The game play that was once fun to do, look at and play with are gone and replaced by 8-bit gameboy RPG garbage. Sure the card game itself is "The same" but the way it changes makes me wish I didn't make it as far as I did.

IF... IF the game goes back to the way it's played in the first act I would love it again, but I just can't slog through that "RPG section"\t\t\t\t\t
