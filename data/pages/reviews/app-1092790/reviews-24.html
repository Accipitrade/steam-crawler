- Game switches up a lot as you go through the different Parts/Acts
- Great retro looking graphics.
- Great OST, high quality and atmospheric, fitting the game's graphics perfectly
- Good story
- Pretty long, took me about ~20 hours going kinda slow.


Cons :
- Difficulty is a bit all over the place, as Part/Act 1 is the most difficult.
> I can imagine that a lot of people stopped playing before Part/Act 2, if you aren't a fan of roguelikes but like the rest of the game, keep going!
- You will lose progress while switching Parts/Acts, forcing you to spend not TOO much time building your deck.
- Parts/Acts play differently from one to another, while it can be a deterrent at first, when you beat the game you can go back to any Part/Act and replay them again.
- Sorry roguelike fans, you cannot beat this one without dying at all.

This game is a straight-up masterpiece, it is a CRIME that I took this long to play it.
I cannot recommend it enough.\t\t\t\t\t
