This game grabbed me with its gameplay but kept me in for so many reasons.
I am not going to spoil anything about this game because this experience is something you should experience blind. The game bursts with creativity and really got my attention the whole way through.
This is something I had not experienced in a long time. Most games I stop playing after like an hour.
Inscryption saw my free weekend and said: "Well, this is mine now"

A great game that really inspired me as a Game Design student. This is the type of creativity I want to be able to put into a game.\t\t\t\t\t
