I picked up this game after it being recommended to me by Steam. As I have a love of card games and horror movies. As it was on sale, I thought I should pick it up for ~$18.

I was not disappointed. It didn't lean too hard on the horror side of the spectrum but what it does it does perfectly. Every system implemented was done so with so much care that the they could have been their own $20 standalone. The early gameplay loop on I could (and will) play over and over again. The only sad part is that I can't continue with my overpowered decks (Get the Mantis God. Best. Card. Ever.) I will not spoil anything as this game deserves to be played, not watched.

Pros:
+Extremely fun
+Easy to learn
+Makes you want to keep playing
+Price < Fun
+Great story!

Cons: *Spoilers*
-Short-ish (~13 hours to beat the entire game)
- You show me 5 systems to play the game and only really let me use 3?! I wanna use the board and duel-disk!
-Sometimes gets a little loud. Turning down in-game volume doesn't fix it.\t\t\t\t\t
