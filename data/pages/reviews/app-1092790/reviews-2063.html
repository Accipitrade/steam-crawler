\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(Pre-emptive: This review will be, for the most part, spoiler-free. It may hint towards spoiler content without saying it outright.)

Inscryption is a fantastic game and an incredibly strong choice for my game of the year.
From the moment you first open it and see that the 'new game' option isn't available, only 'continue', you know you're in for a treat of a format-breaking story. And this comes as no surprise; it's by Daniel Mullins, who made Pony Island and The Hex.

Now, Pony Island and The Hex were okay experiences with some cool self-aware moments, but their gameplay was, frankly, lacking. This is not the case for Inscryption.
The card game it presents is fun, interesting, and practically begging for you to break it and make overpowered combos of whichever type you see fit. You can tell it's clearly made by someone who plays, knows, and loves card games, and it's a delight to play.

As the game goes on and certain spoilery things happen, the gameplay reinvents and changes itself, but (unlike The Hex) never loses its core focus and mechanics of the card game, simply adding in new facets to it that keep the game fresh and interesting. It's a joy to play, and never feels like it overstays its welcome.

In addition to the card game itself, you also get to wander around, solve puzzles, and interact with the environment - a friend of mine described Inscryption as "a puzzle box in the shape of a card game", and I'd absolutely agree with that. There's a pleasant amount to discover and solve. It's great.

But that's not all - the atmosphere of the game is just wonderful. The lore it builds is intriguing, the cabin you begin in ominous, and the characters are just pleasant to be around. The soundtrack varies between ominous ambient tracks and Absolute Bops and helps excellently to set the tone.

I honestly have few to no complaints about the game. It feels paced at exactly the right amount to make the game interesting without ever becoming stale, and it was a ride that had me hyped until the very end.

Play Inscryption. 11/11.\t\t\t\t\t
