However, I will say that the strongest part is the beginning. While the game as a whole is great, the latter parts require different strategy and don't feel as great from an atmosphere perspective. Like most players, I'll find myself replaying the beginning over and over more than playing through the entire story.

Is it worth the $20 asking price? Of course. After playing through the whole thing, you can play through the beginning part as much as you want and still get a ton of enjoyment. Just interested in the gameplay in trailers and not the extra story, lore, and ARG stuff? Then I suggest getting it on sale. Worth it either way.\t\t\t\t\t
