\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game seems well crafted as an experience. I'm sure that hardcore card-based roguelite lovers will find flaws in its systems, I've only beaten the games a couple of times and it never once felt repetitive. Difficulty seems well balanced. Some runes I get wrecked instantly, sometimes I get an strong card and still get destroyed. Sometimes I have what seems like ♥♥♥♥♥♥ cards, but certain circumstances emerge that allow me to abuse them in ways I didn't foresee.

I really like this aspect about the game, It pits you against opponents that often look ridiculously OP at first, but then you get wise about their tricks and still end up having different choices in how to beat them. I can't imagine how much work it takes to craft a system like this.

The meta narrative keeps the game fresh. The death-card system is really cool. Although in my case, it kind of trivialized the game because I ended up with a card that did 5 damage to 3 spaces. The enemy has 10 hp, so doing 15 damage with one card was an instant win.

I have to admit my two victories were carried by that card. I suppose I should take some credit for collecting the powers that allowed me to create that card, but still, it left me wondering if I got lucky or if this is what happens to everyone.

The only thing I can think of to make this card more OP is if I had been able to make it free to play, but that wouldn't make a big difference.

I'm not sure if this is a 100+ hour kind of game. Maybe it will be for some people, maybe it's not replayable enough for that. I don't know if it can match up against the OG card based roguelikes.\t\t\t\t\t
