This game is way more than what I expected, with some great gameplay (That might have you pulling your hair at times) incredible story, heavy atmosphere, amazing world building and, at times, catchy OST.

Now, at the time of writing the review. I've played the game for about 9 hours and got done with most of the Secrets that you could find on an initial playthrough, but, just like the core gameplay, the game itself is a Rouge-Like, offering what seems to be multiple branching stories that one can embark in.

Honest score: 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
