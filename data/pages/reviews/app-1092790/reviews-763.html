\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI beat this game in a total of 9 hours. It was amazing.

I believe I paid $20 for this game, and only got 9 hours out of it.
But those 9 hours were... enlightening, magical, intoxicating.
This game is a breath of fresh air in an era of recycled reskined games and AAA disappointments.

The game is some strange combination of Card Battler, Escape Room, with minor Horror elements and some FMV (full motion video) thrown in.

The story is unique and different, gripping you right away while keeping you interested in exploring, sniffing about for more information or a route of escape.

The characters vary. Most are well fleshed out and you really connect, or at least enjoy them. The only character I really didnt care for was The Wizard Magnificus, he just never hit right for me, though the effort was there.

In the end, this game hits that craving for quality that is so lackluster in games these days.
Buy this, play this, revel in its glory.

Oh... and don't die.\t\t\t\t\t
