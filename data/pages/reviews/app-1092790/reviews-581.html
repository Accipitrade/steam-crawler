\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow, what a game. This is probably one of the most unique, frustrating (in a good way), and fun horror games I have ever had the chance to play.

A quick rundown: Inscryption is an Indi-horror/card game. You first start the game by solving puzzles and playing a sick card game against a creature to escape its wooden cabin. This part is ridiculously hard and took me the longest out of all the parts. I got frustrated many times, but when I finally got past this stage, it felt amazing. This part obviously contains a plot but I will not spoil it. The second part involves playing a 2D adventure style game, still including the card-play. You must fight all the different bosses to progress to the final stage of the game. This part was probably the easiest and didn't take me too long to complete. The final stage is hard to explain without spoiling the plot, so I will try my best to be as vague as possible. You are found are found to be in a similar predicament as in the first stage, but not as punishing. This stage is probably the most plot rich of them all.

Pros:
This game is very well thought out, plot rich, and unique. I don't believe there are any other games out on the market quite like it. The graphics are appealing and the card battles and puzzles are fun. The game gives the player a constant sense of unease and dread which definitely helps for immersion.

Cons:
This game is very difficult and strategic. This makes it sometimes hard to enjoy if you are stuck in one stage (usually the first) for long periods of time. This is because you may be getting bored (as I did) or frustrated, (also as I did). The plot, although interesting, can be sometimes hard to understand. The puzzles as well can also be confusing.

My opinion:
Overall, I believe this is a great game. The pros greatly out way the cons. I had a blast playing this. I couldn't recommend it enough to seasoned horror gamers and/or card gamers. However, if you get easily frustrated and aren't good at strategics or solving puzzles, then maybe this isn't the game for you.\t\t\t\t\t
