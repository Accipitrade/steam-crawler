\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was a fantastic game. The core game play loop is a rogue like deck builder. You are given the choice of cards to add to your deck with a variety of different effects. Along with the possibility of creating your own cards. Each run allows you to try different combinations especially since you get access to more and more game objects. There are creepy elements throughout the story of the game, but even as someone who does not like horror, I was fine with those elements.

Huge spoiler for the end of the game.

The game is split into 3 parts. The first part was very enjoyable and is definitely worth playing a lot. I am glad that they are introducing a mod that allows you to repeat this along with new content. I do struggle with some of the death cards. They can be either incredibly overpowered (such as a 0 cost 3/2 with flying) or really bad. These cards never seem to go away, but it is random when they appear.

The second part is fine once you have a deck that is functional. I did not have the cards that I wanted for a while, and created a different deck than I thought I wanted. I personally thought it was fine, but it was probably the weakest in my mind.

The third part I enjoyed quite a bit as well. It felt like the basic resource they give you (the empty barrels) were going to be useless. But then it felt great that they were upgraded to being somewhat relevant.

And once you beat the game, there is the epilogue. I enjoyed seeing many of the characters again and playing a few more rounds where it didn't matter. However, I thought the Yugioh dual at the end was over the top. I understand it fits with the goal of the game, but I personally didn't want to play it. It also seemed like there was no reason to try during that. Everything was glitchy (on purpose) and you just had to get to the end of it.

All in all, this game was fantastic and I would recommend it to anyone that can deal with the creepy aspect of the story itself.\t\t\t\t\t
