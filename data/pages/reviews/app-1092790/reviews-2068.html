\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is definitely the best game i've played this year, maybe ever. If you like card games you will certainly enjoy this epic journey! You should also go into it as blind as possible because their are a lot of mysteries, puzzles and other things that you will not expect. You also have much more gameplay than you expect, at 14 hours I just finished the game and I just want to start it again. The card making mechanic is tons of fun and works very well in this rogue-like card game. it allows your deck to get better and better in every iterations. Finally, the bosses were so unique and all with original mechanics, I've never seen anything like it in recent years

The one thing I feel is lacking is a higher difficulty, I found myself winning very quickly sometimes, I believe I died 4 or 5 times in total including the 3 planned death and killed the final boss on the first try without any of the OP boons you can get.

Now for the spoiler section, which is more of a message for the devs. Has much as I loved the game act 2 and 3 were much weaker than the first one. The ambience and the rogue-like style worked so well together that I want to start the game over just to be able to replay this part. I really wish that a future update brings a game mode where you can play ad nauseam ! During the deletion of the game you get to play against the last two scrybes and I must say, the settings for those two looked so cool! I feel really hope you get to play those in a DLC. It's a shame they made all these new assets and we barely played with them. Finally, and this is probably just me being stupid, if so I would like having it explained, how come in act 2 the NPCs mention events that happened in act 1 ? The act 2 is suppose to be the start of the game but Leshy and the other scrybes talk about being stuck in cards and other events from act 1. Those this serve to show that the scrybes are sentient ?

Hopefully this review helps some of you to pick up this game, because it for sure is deserving of your attention.
