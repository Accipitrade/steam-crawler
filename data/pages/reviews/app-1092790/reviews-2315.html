A definite recommend from me, especially if the trailer's caught your interest. At the very least, check out the demo

This game is very easy to spoil
I think this game is at it's best when you know nothing about it, so I'll try my best not to give anything away beyond the very opening of the game.

I love the atmosphere of the game. When you start the game, you find yourself sitting at a table in an eerie cabin. Across from you, an ominous figure opens their eyes. They say it's been a while since anyone's been here. It's been a while since there's been a challenger.

The ominous figure proceeds to reveal a game-board and figures, and passes you a deck of cards. The figure sets up deck-building rouge-lite, with him as the Game-Master. In this game, you find yourself making yourself making your way through the wilderness, battling creatures and bosses the mysterious Game Master along the way. You will struggle, but you are not alone.

Very early on, one of your cards, the Stout, talks you. They offer you advice, and imply that with the help of others, you may find a way to defeat Mysterious Figure, and escape. You'll find yourself exploring the cabin, finding locked boxes and other puzzling objects, in hopes of finding a way to put an end to this madness.\t\t\t\t\t
