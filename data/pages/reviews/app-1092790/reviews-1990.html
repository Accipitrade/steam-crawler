\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo this game gave me many mixed feelings.

I raged, I laughed. I got happy, I got sad. However that was during the first part of the game, the best part.
After I got past the "continue" game it fell apart for me and became something I did not enjoy or want to play.

I do not enjoy 8 bit games. This game turns into one.

There is no doubt this is a good and polished game. However I was expecting a 3D card game with horror elements and the premise starts of so well. You are stuck in a dank cabin with something. You have cards that are alive and talk to you and appear to want to escape this current predicament. This was super awesome to me. When I beat the first section of the game it the turns out. He is just the first boss. The game completely changes.

The first act should have been the entire game. It was super entertaining and I could not put it down.

Once the second act hit. It was like going backwards in gameplay and execution. It was so bad that I could not bring myself to play it. I have tried to get a refund 3 times because I am so dismayed at the completely different games within one game.

Had some weird video section after the first chapter, which made no sense to me and still does not. It seems like its trying to do something different but that rarely works. Should have stuck with the opening premise and just ran with that. I hope someone makes a card horror game like the first chapter. That is the game I wanted. What I got was half a game for £16 pound. Total ripoff.

I do not enjoy games like this. If you want a 3D quality card game with a path system then you will have to look elsewhere.
Shame I really enjoyed the first half of the game.
I can't bring myself to play the 8 bit game, I can't stand that sort of graphical style. Not for over £16 quid.

Just my opinion, I will look into games in future rather than buying them on a whim or watching an Influencer play them.\t\t\t\t\t
