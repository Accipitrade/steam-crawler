Looked up spoiler, even though I really didn't want to.

EDIT:
Beat the game. I think my problem with this game is that I was expecting a difficult tactical game, but this is instead a story-based game, and you aren't supposed to be trying your best to win here. This is evidenced by the easily abused mechanics. Sometimes the game will reward you (temporarily) for doing things like "going infinite", but the problem is that it gets really boring, especially with the deliberately clunky UI. When you remove the difficulty from this game, you also remove the tension. I don't consider it my "fault" for ruining my own experience, when the game incentivizes it.
Some great music, though.\t\t\t\t\t
