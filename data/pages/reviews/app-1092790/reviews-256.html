\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo you like M. Night Shyamalan? Is the 6th Sense or Old your favorite movie? Do you think these movies are awesome because of a TWIST? Are you so tired of good story telling and decent gameplay that having something come out of left field that was mildly foreshadowed makes you feel the tingles? Then this game is for you!

Listen, if you like unexpected, then I get it. Sometimes I like finding out that the dog took a dump in my shoe, or that grandma dated Sadam in high school, or that i'm having a stroke. These are all unexpected TWISTS. I get it, you like cheap thrills over good hard earn characterization or gameplay. But this game sacrifices everything for the TWIST.

The gameplay is not good... like at all. It's an inferior card battler. In fact, it's not a card battler. It's a rouge-lite where every time you lose you get much, much stronger. To the point where there is no challenge and it's super tedious. Major "Gone Home" vibes, you know a game that lacked much game but got a ton of love. This has game in it at least, but it's not very good.

Call me crazy but if I wanted to do an escape room, I'd... you know, go to an escape room? Not play a game that pretends to be a card battler but is really an escape room/adventure game in disguise. If the card battler elements were fun, then this would be good, but they aren't. Mechanics are really basic, nothing to keep you hooked.

7 hours deep and this game is not fun, weak. But hey, I SEE DEAD PEOPLE - AM I RIGHT.\t\t\t\t\t
