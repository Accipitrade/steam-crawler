\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first I wasn't feeling it. It kind of felt like the boss/game were cheating, so I tried it for a bit, didn't feel the whole card game genre, and left it alone for a bit. I got into the card game genre again thanks to the fantastic Fighting In Tight Spaces, great game. So, went to this after that and seeing the GoTY announcement.

Anyway, so, at first it feels like the boss/game are cheating. That's largely because, uh, they are. Those bits made me want to quit just because I'm a frequent card game player and I don't enjoy when deckbuilder games feel like the boss/game cheat to win. After a while the game essentially allows you cheat with a minor "Death Card" function that allows you to sometimes unleash OP cards into the mix.

The game is creepy, I just started and had a busy weekend and still managed to get over 10 hours as I yawn after a day at the zoo, which was great, winning the game today twice was also cherries on top. We saw tigers like 5 feet away from us, walking and playing, rattle snakes moving (huge ones), had multiple birds land on each of to feed (Lorikeets), saw penguins and bears very up close, saw 5 otters playing in the water and having a great time (adorable), a bald eagle like 5 feet from me, a lot more, and then went to my sister's to house to make gingerbread houses with my 2 nieces + nephew + my mom + GF = great. The entire time, was thinking about this game while also having a blast, then came home, played before bed (now), and won a match, gonna go binge before bed time.


Great day, great game, great experience, fun time. Very addictive, very replayavle, very deckbuilding, very hard, very unique, very good to jam out to deathcore/metalcore on, frustrating at times, very rewarding at times. 9.75/10, I'd love to see the boss selection extended and varied + more card variation. The cards are all pretty cool, mechanics are unique, when you're down you feel down, when you're up you feel very up.

Updat 1: So, the game has a drastic change in it randomly, a very interesting and unique one.\t\t\t\t\t
