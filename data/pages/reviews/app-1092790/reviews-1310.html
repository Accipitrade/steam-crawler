Art's great (even if you don't like it at first blush, you'll see some seriously dedicated artistry as you progress to the end), music's perfect, writing is very good at leading you through its mysteries, and it's not worth spoiling the best parts of the design of this game just to say I was nearly shouting at my screen in joy at some of the reveals.

If you do finish this game, take some time to look up secrets / spoilers on YouTube because there is some really clever stuff here and I missed a whole bunch of it.\t\t\t\t\t
