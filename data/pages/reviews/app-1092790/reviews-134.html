I will keep this fairly short as I do not want to spoil much.

I recall seeing Markiplier play this game and I was instantly captivated by it, I am a sucker for a good card game and this one got me good. After watching his first video, I purchased it on Steam.

There is an interesting mystery around the game Inscryption, which I will not spoil here. However as you delve deeper into the story something more dangerous lurks in the shadows

The art style is simple but affective, with great lighting and use of colour.

Use of sound and music really emphasises the horror elements of the game.
The gameplay is a turn-based card game where your cards must be played at the cost of your other creatures It has a nice dark element to it.

I have put a lot of hours into this game, it is a real gem that I am so glad exists and the fact there is a mod for an endless run is a lovely free update.

Check it out, play it and I am sure you will love it.
