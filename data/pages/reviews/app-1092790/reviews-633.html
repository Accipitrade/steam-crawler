\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThey tried to tell an interesting story. I respect that. One of my favorite games is Omori, which has mediocre gameplay in exchange for its story. What I don't respect is having great gameplay and then ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ it for the story. Without delving too deep into spoilers, the first act of the game is absolutely incredible, building tension and flow state masterfully. Then it is followed up by hours upon hours (3.5 at my count with no sign of stopping) of far weaker gameplay that tries to imitate what was so great about the first act and fails utterly, just becoming repetitive. Elements of the game that I found really cool the first time get the point where I don't even want to do them the 4th time, despite them giving me advantages. The story, which had me engaged, slowed to a crawl with this. I pushed on for 3.5 hours, so I really did give it as much of a chance as I could, but I was unable to bring myself to continue playing this game.\t\t\t\t\t
