\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt all began when I had encountered the stream of DISEMBODIED SKULL on steam. After 30 minutes of watching, I was hooked and bought the game. It is one of the best games I have ever played. If you love card games, unique story and hidden secrets then this game is definitely for you.

Story
At first, I thought it was just a regular card game, but it was a lot more than that. It has tons of secrets and puzzles. The story is very unique and dark, with a lot of hidden lore to explore.

Gameplay
In terms of gameplay, you get to play with many different kinds of cards. As you progress you build the deck that most suits you. The game has roguelike elements and is randomly generated each time you start over. In addition, the mechanics themselves change as you progress (especially the bosses), so it's never boring. The game isn't easy, so you have to make your actions carefully.

Artstyle
The visuals and the soundtrack give the right atmosphere. Sometimes it's spooky, sometimes it's funny and sometimes it's touching. They are absolutely beautiful.

Conclusion
I have completed the game in 32 hours doing all the achievements and was very satisfied. This game really touched my heart. If you have played tons of games and want something original, then this game is for you. I really recommend getting the game even at full price.
