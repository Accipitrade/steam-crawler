\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll I can say is incredible. The atmosphere created in this game during the 1st act is probably the best atmosphere I've ever played in a game. Every second of it just completely drew me in. Art style was great. Music great. Characters great. Loved everything about the 1st act. Again, I cannot express enough how awesome the feeling was when I was playing the game the first time through.

That being said, the 2nd act was definitely a big step back. Still felt pretty good but nowhere near as god as the first. Honestly I just tried to get through that part as fast as I could. Did help a lot with the lore of the game and building the whole world of Inscryption, but it wasn't my personal cup of tea.

Act 3 brought it back to something a lot more fun. Still not as good as Act 1 but still felt really good and was really fun to go through. The ending of this game, especially with Leashy brought me to tears. Loved it so much.

Went back through and got all achievements including the ones from Kaycee's Mod and that was a lot of fun too. Overall I truly loved this game. I'd probably put it in my top 10 of all time just based off of the first Act. Truly one that should not be missed. 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
