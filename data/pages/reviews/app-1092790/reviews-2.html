\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is one of the best I’ve ever played,

Before I say anything else DO NOT SPOIL YOURSELF, the mystery the game lays out for the player is well crafted and captivating watching the story play out is a truly unique experience you don’t want to miss out on. Including a (solved) ARG for those of you who really like to dig into the game’s story. There are a lot of horror elements to this game which are expertly done creating a feeling of unease and dread through out specifically the first section of the game. For those horror adverse fear not this game does stay away from jumps cares but there is some gore so do keep that in mind. The game has a unique art style blending pixelated graphics with a semi 3D setting allowing for a unique look and feel to the game and it looks great. The music also really helps sell the vibe the game is going for and while not out of this world provides a nice soundtrack to the setting. The gameplay absolutely complements the rest of the game with a rogue like card game which has you gathering cards over many fights and defeating powerful bosses all headed a mysterious game master who does his best to immerse you in his tale. It’s challenging and takes a while to master forcing you to make tough decisions especially with how you build your deck. The gameplay is great and if you’re like and can’t get enough of it theres a true rogue like mode unlocked after completing the story where you can play through again with additional challenges and cards providing hours more gameplay for those seeking it out.

10/10 phenomenal, if you haven’t played it yet don't deprive yourself.
