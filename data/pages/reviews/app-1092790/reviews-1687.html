\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDaniel Mullins has made a number of fantastic "what you see isn't all you get" mystery games with tons of other genres built in. But this game? This is a masterpiece. CCG, rogue-like, escape room, slight-horror, mystery... this game has it all. Even once you've solved the mystery (sorry, no spoilers here!) it's tons of fun to replay. And with Kaycee's Mod free DLC coming out, the objectively best part of the game is getting expansion.

I kind of wonder if Daniel set out to make a fun mystery-esque game and wound up creating amazing card game/s. Really really hope some of the mechanics can be even further expanded. When dedicated digital CCGs come out, they never draw me in. Yet this? I find myself replaying it multiple times, even with the mysteries all solved and no surprises, the card games are just way too fun. Simple, addictive, strategic.\t\t\t\t\t
