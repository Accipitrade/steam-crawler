Inscription is...an experience. Often experiences are best well experienced with no prior information on the experience. When a game gets popular like this one its hard not to have spoilers that otherwise ruin this.

However this game is kinda of a stand out at it is still worth experiencing for yourself. Some moments are way more enjoyable when playing even knowing they are coming. Many of the set pieces are just works of art and while this is most present in the first chapter it does help to set up many character dynamics down the road.

My only complaint is that this game got so popular. Much like doki doki a lot of the shock value that comes from this kind of media is best experienced in the moment. At least this game doesn't have a big red sign on the title screen announcing it. So I say if you have seen game play play it, if you have not stop reading right now and buy it next sale if it looks interesting, and if your someone maybe give it a go.\t\t\t\t\t
