\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game not only has an amazingly designed card game as its focus, but also a very engaging world built around it. This is definitely a game that's jumped right onto my favorites of all time.

The cards themselves have simple to understand mechanics that just get deeper and deeper as you play on, building on itself over and over to build a whole league of depth. It is simple enough that I think less-seasoned card game players can do quite well with it, yet with enough nuance for the veterans.

As a pretty serious UI/UX snob, I dont really have any real complaints. All the settings you'd want are there, and the flow of everything plays out well once you get over a little hump at the start. There are ways to quickly view abilities and see everything that's going on while in combat.

If the trailers catch your interest at all, definitely pick this one up!\t\t\t\t\t
