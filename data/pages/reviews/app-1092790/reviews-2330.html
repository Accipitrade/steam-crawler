Introduction:
This game is absolutely incredible, an easy 10/10 for me and one that I would say is a must-buy. The storyline, the sleekness, the gameplay variance and the surprises/experiences along the way that will have you raise eyebrows, laugh or even question reality - no spoilers, but do check it out.

Spoiler-Free Key Notes:
  • It is challenging to say too much about this game without spoiling it - however, it is based on a dungeon-crawler, roguelike deck building principle. The gameplay is very sleek, smooth and intuitive.
  • The innovation, immersion and storyline in this game is unlike any other and is quite simply incredible - one to check out to understand what I mean by this, It will keep you hooked big time. The developers have done an incredible job.
  • It does have some replayability, as the gameplay itself is quite satisfying, but not a lot of replayability. I would say that this element is the games only downside.
  • Just... you need to check it out. It is amazing!
