\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's good. The escape room aspect is fun and adds another layer to everything. However, it does take away from the replay value of the game.

The actually "Rogue-like" nature of the game is a little weak, and the deckbuilding is good, not great. But again, devs did something fresh here and it's not just another STS clone, which is a big deal.

Pros:
--Unique escape room mechanic
--Actually challenging bosses, with engaging mechanics
--Board-based card battles make for more strategic gameplay.
--Usable items are interesting and strategizing when to use/save them is fun, too.
--Resource management (and item management) plays a central role in the game.

+/- Story is fairly creepy, may not be for everyone.

Overall:
8/10 , Decent Deckbuilding Roguelike that is at least unique. Would be higher if game had better replay value. It's a fairly short game.

Con:
--Lack of replay value /additional content.\t\t\t\t\t
