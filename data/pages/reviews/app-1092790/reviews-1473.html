\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is easily the best game I've played in the last year. The most important thing is that you don't look too much into it before you play. Here is what you need to know: the game is (mechanically) a card battler, a la Magic: The Gathering, Hearthstone, and Slay the Spire. If you enjoy those sorts of games, do not hesitate to pick this up. (Note: thematically its a bit dark, so it may not be suitable for younger children, however).

Without going too much into the game, I'll just say that this does some very interesting stuff with the cardplay and deckbuilding mechanics which could not be achieved without the digital format. If I'm going to play a videogame deckbuilder/card battler, this is what I want it to be like. I also really dig the atmosphere/narrative and can see myself replaying it in the future just because of how addictive the gameplay loop itself is!\t\t\t\t\t
