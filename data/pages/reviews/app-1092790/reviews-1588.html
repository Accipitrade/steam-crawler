The entire game is built on the card game foundation, which is tons of fun (even divorced from the story and puzzles of the game.) These solid mechanics enable Inscryption to have more freedom with its plot, which twists and becomes super meta. If you enjoyed Slay the Spire, Champion Labs from LoR, Dungeon Runs from Hearthstone, or Banners of Ruin, you will want Inscryption.\t\t\t\t\t
