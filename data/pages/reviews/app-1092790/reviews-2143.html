\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo sum up my whole experience: Wow.
I have next to no complaints, the story was fantastic and involved, the card game itself is awesome (and I want irl please devs), graphics were pleasing and charming, and the meta elements really helped sell this whole 'evil program' idea.

I only have one complaint, though it contains spoilers, so I recommend you don't read this if you haven't played the game yet, plus it's more a personal complaint, and may not apply to all players: During act 3 (when PO3 takes over the game) you end up fighting a boss called the 'archivist'. The first gimmick of this boss is pretty neat, it accesses your real computer files. Every time you kill a librarian, you are allowed to pick a file from your actual computer, and the size of the file will decide how much damage you deal. In the later part of the game, you must pick a file which becomes a card in your hand, which the game threatens to delete if the card is destroyed (However it won't, this is a troll). While in retrospect this mechanic is very cool, it also made me extremely uncomfortable, especially the threat it made to my files, even though no threat to my security and computer are really present. I wish there was an option to prevent inscryption from accessing your real computer and that it would just use a fake desktop instead.

Other than that, good game.
10/10 would shake their hands again.\t\t\t\t\t
