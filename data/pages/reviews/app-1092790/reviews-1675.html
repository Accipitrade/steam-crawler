\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI seldomly write reviews but this game is particularly bad.

Visuals: the retro style (think games in the 90s, Pokemon GBA) pixel art style is definitely not for everyone.

Gameplay: it is meant to be a card game. The mechanics are simply variations from Magic the Gathering/hearthstone/Gwent and gets boring quite quickly. There are clearly OP mechanics to build OP decks and just ignore the rest.

The escape room element is quite gimmicky imo, but necessary for advancing the game, even when you can readily beat the card game.

The story telling is almost an bait and switch in the later part of the game, as described by other negative reviews.

Bottom line: DON'T go into this game blind as suggested by the positive reviews. Do your research and see if the game suits you. It's definitely the WORST game I've bought to date.\t\t\t\t\t
