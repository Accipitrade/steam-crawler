POTENTIAL SPOILER WARNING
---------------------------------
One negative aspect of this game is that once you "beat" the game, there is some "bonus content" which somewhat implies that the game was originally planned to be much bigger than what we get here. I went from the high of beating the challenge to the low of realizing what we didn't get. I went from feeling like this was a great game with great value, to feeling like I got ripped off from something I didn't even know existed until five minutes ago. I can't believe I'm asking for less content, but if the developer would have left it out of the game then I wouldn't know what I was missing. The "other version" is charming but inadequate in comparison, so even though you get to experience the other "bosses" it's not as good of a game and is visually more confusing and I found it harder to understand what's happening in a battle. I essentially beat Inscryption in one sitting and was rewarded with a lesser game that I have little interest in playing.\t\t\t\t\t
