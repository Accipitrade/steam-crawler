\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption, where to begin...

The story in this game is amazing, it may only have been made by one guy but it made me FEEL more for some of the characters than I did in games that were 10x bigger. It's well paced, and nothing is ever forced or feels like BS, it all makes sense with context.

Gameplay, well this game is amazing, it focuses mostly around the cards and deck building aspects, but even for someone like me, who doesn't really enjoy that type of game this was amazing. Sure, sometimes you might get an OP card, or you might use the mechanics to make a card unbeatable. But that feels EARNED, YOU put a sigil on that card that made it attack in 3 lanes for 3 damage with a flying ability. YOU found the perfect combination that can win you the round before you hit the bell twice.

Lore, there is so many secrets, hidden Easter eggs, recordings of the person "playing" the game. I mean, someone like MatPat would take 5 episodes to get through it all and STILL have questions left.

And the ending, well let's just say that I honestly felt more for Le- Him, then I did for Luke, even though Luke is the human actor, and the other one is the guy you died to over and over. But still... When he told me at the end that I had a good deck, and said "Thank you" for picking him, that really hit hard.

All in all, an amazing game and highly recommend playing it, and also, if you do plan on playing it then PLEASE don't look at a youtubers or streamer playing this game before... Trust me, you want to see this yourself\t\t\t\t\t
