But the game is very easy, an entire portion of the game have awful music and sound (opinion, but I usually don't dislike 8-bit music), expect at best 15 hours for the main story if not replaying for achievements like I did.

I like the game, it's great, it has its flaws though. The card play in the main game seems secondary to the "outside" story, the game lose most its value once you play it once. However, the dev are currently beta testing a free content update that adds a challenge mode for the first part of the game, allowing to replay it with more variations and increasing difficulty, adding tens of hours of playtime.

It's better to go in blind for the main story, that's what makes this game unique.\t\t\t\t\t
