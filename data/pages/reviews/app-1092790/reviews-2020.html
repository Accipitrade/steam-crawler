\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally promising and unique premise. Kind of reminded me of Petscop.

The first part of the game, a combination of roguelike deckbuilder and escape room, is really interesting. The learning curve is steep, and there's a lot of obnoxious RNG, so it can get extremely frustrating...but it's still fun overall.

Unfortunately, things break down after that. The next part takes the roguelike deckbuilder aspects and transplants you into a Pokemon-like game, where the cards just have 8-bit graphics. The visuals are fine even though they're fairly bland and not remotely as interesting as the first part of the game. The real problem is that the mechanics of the card game change, and what was already a difficult and frustrating experience gets much, MUCH worse.

I was interested in the game's meta-level plot hook, but the actual game itself became an obstacle to the story. I just ended up watching it on Youtube.

Definitely not worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
