Without spoiling too much, I really liked the direction of the game itself (music, art, and atmopshere is fantastic, I even like the gameplay across the acts while others might not), but I don't think I really cared for the ARG content that is sprinkled in and drives the overall content/story.

If you like ARGs and secrets, you might enjoy the game more, but to me, it's just not something I care about in a gameI'll just search it up later anyways.

Maybe the official Kaycee's mod gamemode will change my mind as it opens up the first act to just be about gameplay, but I still have to say I was really dissapointed. If anything, I'd just put this in as a "play with Kaycee's Mod" if you want just gameplay even though I personally enjoyed the gameplay in acts 2 and 3.\t\t\t\t\t
