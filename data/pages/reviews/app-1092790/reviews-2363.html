\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'd say this game is a solid 8/10. It's a great doki-doki-esque storytelling game and I loved most of the time I played it. Fair warning though: it's not quite what it says on the box.

Gameplay: 7/10
Everything controls nicely. Some mechanics aren't explained and have to be figured out, but everything is easy enough to understand and fun to use.

Asthetics: 8/10
The whole game looks great, but isn't graphically impressive. The moody and dark atmosphere of act 1 is *chef's kiss*, and the art really helps sell the story and create interesting environments.

Characters: 10/10
S T I M U L A T I O N

Story: 9/10
Naturally, for a story-focused game, there's a lot of story to be had. I quite liked it. My only complaint is that is was a bit predictable; no major twists except at the end of act 1 and act 3.

Replayability: 1/10
You'll only play this game once, maybe another time to try out some cool combos or solve a puzzle. It's a story based game, and the story really only hits on point once.

Spookiness: 6/10
A few bone-chilling moments and a generally spooky atmosphere. The spooky bits doesn't hit particularly hard. Minor blood is present. No jumpscares.

Potato-friendliness: 8-10
You don't need a particularly good PC to run this game. Any old personal laptop will do, but you probably won't be running it on an actual potato anytime soon.\t\t\t\t\t
