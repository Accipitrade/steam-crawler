\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing this, I feel Daniel Mullins is honestly one of the best Indie game devs out there right now.
Much like Pony Island (which is also crazy good and you should play btw), Inscryption is an innovative, amazing game, that's quite unlike your typical card game.
It's got everything: good gameplay, interesting mechanics, a very unique art style (or maybe I should say multiple unique art styles), and an interesting story.
You can really tell the amount of heart and hours of work that went into Inscryption, and it's absolutely worth the price.
Before this game, I had played the proof-of-concept game "Sacrifices Must Be Made" Daniel Mullins put together (in under 48 hours, might I add) for Ludum Dare 43. Back then I was already in love with the idea and knew it had potential, but I had no clue he'd been hard at work at expanding it into a full-fledged game until a short while before Inscryption was set to be released. The demo was solely based on the section in Leshy's Cabin, and even then I thought it was amazing.
So you can imagine the surprise when something like Inscryption took this theme and improved on it massively in so many interesting ways I couldn't have fathomed.
Truly, it's an experience that, if you like card games, you must have. Go ahead and give it a try, you'll enjoy it; thank me later.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm still not entirely sure what I just played, but I'm pretty sure "INSCRYPTION" is a fantastically creative, totally surprising (if somewhat uneven) dip into meta-fictional psychological-horror...by way of a YouTuber playing a computerized collectible card game. Act 1 is perfection. Act 2 is charming. Act 3 is really neat. The finale is a little bit underwhelming, if only because, while the writing is top notch, and the main actor was great, the two other human actors are...er...disappointing. And the abrupt genre shifts are often unwelcome. Though i love that they added a rogue-like endless mode modeled after Act 1!

Oh, and bonus points for making a game I couldn't stop talking about but which made my wife say "This is too meta! Just HEARING about this game is stressing me out!"\t\t\t\t\t
