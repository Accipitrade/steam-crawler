\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you love card battlers you'll probably love this game. If not, I wouldn't recommend it. I'm neutral on them, but I heard from lots of people that the game is really good whether you like card battlers or not, but I heavily disagree. There's definitely an interesting atmosphere and quality to the game, but you have to slog through endless card games to get there, and they're often unfair and/or boring.

The game really doesn't respect your time, every boss has unique mechanics that can easily instantly kill a run when you encounter them because of how punishing they are, but you have no way of knowing about them until you trigger them. Then you have to restart and hope you learned enough mechanics to beat them the next time (and that you're lucky enough to get a good deck along the way). You have to play through every zone and boss every time, compounding the time you're wasting just trying to make progress. So again, if you love card battlers, all these repeated playthroughs might not bother you.

The game also forces you onto its rails if you do too well. I got to the second phase of the second boss on my first run and the game literally said "no, you got too far too fast" and instantly killed me. I found that irritating.\t\t\t\t\t
