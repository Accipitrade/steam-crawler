\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am so very mad.
This game looked promising, new, enticing, mysterious.
But then: roguelike. I get that some people like them, but I don't. I really don't.
I don't want to replay the first level over and over again, it's just plain boring, even if it is different every time it is still the first level.
I had very high hopes for this game, but when I died the first time and got sent back, it was an instant refund.
I am so sad that so many brilliant indie game devs have gotten into this megatrend just for the sake of replayability (and no, I dont think that going through the same enemies with a card instead of another qualifies as new and enticing content)
I would say "focus on shorter, better games and dont make any bloated 100+ hour infinite Isaacs", but that is just not going to happen, is it?

Sono furioso.
