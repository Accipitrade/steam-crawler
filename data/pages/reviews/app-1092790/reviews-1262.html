\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I want to start off by saying that if you are considering getting this game, it is definitely worth it.. But do understand that the preview images don't tell the full story.

SPOILER HERE. DO NOT READ IF YOU DO NOT WANT A VERY GENERAL SPOILER.

This game consists of three acts. The first act, which is depicted and shown in the screenshots and videos, is FANTASTIC. It feels super cool, super atmospheric.. Spooky but exciting at the same time. The game evolves and grows with each cycle, getting better and better. You can pull of some real wild stuff with the right planning!

But Act 2.. The game changes entirely. It's not nearly as good, but not terrible. I do like the ability to build different decks with weird but useful synergies.. And seeing the minions of each Scrybe is kind of rad. Act 3 is genuinely really bad and changes the tone and environment of the game completely. It drags on, it isn't fun to play and it's just a slog.

Starting out, Act 1 was a 10/10. Act 2 was a 6/10, it lost a lot of it's charm and fun.. but.. still worth playing. Act 3 brought Inscryption down to a 3/10, to the point that I can't bring myself to care to finish it.

My advice? If you want this game? Just play Act 1. MAYBE Act 2. But absolutely avoid Act 3. It genuinely turns this amazing game into a really boring, lacklustre experience.

I understand if the Dev has a story to tell, a vision, but.. You sell the game based on Act 1 entirely, which is why I bought it, and I feel a little bamboozled?

Eitherway. It's a lot of fun, a fantastic experience, for Act 1. Act 2 slows down, changes completely, but it's still worth playing. Avoid Act 3 in my opinion.

Perhaps consider adding an infinite mode for Act 1? That'd be wonderful.

TLDR: This game is definitely worth it for Act 1 and maybe Act 2, but, avoid Act 3 at all costs. Goes from a 10/10 to a 6/10 to a 3/10.\t\t\t\t\t
