\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy thoughts overall:
A very good story driven card game.
I love the art style of the game, as well as how mysterious the characters at first are. I love the 1st and 3 part of the game. 1st mostly for the sense of discovery as you figure out the puzzles piece by piece. The 2nd part was likable enough I would say. Just the massive change of art style is a bit of a turn of for me personally. But the 3rd part picks it back up. Not as many puzzles as the 1st part, which i missed. But it has some good and fun surprises which I'm not going to spoil. To finish the game takes about 10 to 13 hours. So not a very long game but very much worth the 19.99 box price.

Gameplay: 7
Game play was solid. Nothing to flashy. but for a mostly card game it is very well done. lots of different combos you can use.

Graphics: 8
Nothing to grand, If you like this type of pixely dark art syle you will love this. And as bonus your potato PC's will be able to handle this game :D. like I said previously in act 2 the art style changes which is not really my preferred art style but a decent one none the less. act 1 and 3 is where this game shines in my opinion.

Sound design: 7
Music was fine. Nothing special. It did change quite often so you did not get bored of it. The miscellaneous sounds where quite well done. they still had the 8 bit kind of sound design but did not feel to old school.

Story: 9
The story was believable and very well integrated in the game and gameplay it self. I don't want to talk too much about it because it will spoil the game. this game relies heavely on the story and the surprise elements in the game. So it's best not to watch any guides or videos on this game before you play it.

Replay ability: 4
I don't really feel like this game has a lot to offer for a second play through. maybe some puzzles here and there if you missed them. but if you did all those then there isn't really any reason to come back. tho that said the gameplay it self is fun enough to maybe play this game again in a couple of years.

Overal: 7
I think this score is a bit unfair because of the non replay ability of the game. so if you do not take that part into account then the real score would be 7.75. I think this game is well worth the box price. It surprised me quite often with new elements. But did not become to much either.

I would say buy it!\t\t\t\t\t
