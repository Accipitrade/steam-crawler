Hopefully going forward we can have this silly Dan Mullins ARG put to rest seeing how it tainted this otherwise legitimately perfect game.

Edit 03/21/22 : Kaycee's mod adds a interesting varient to the first phase of the game that creates an endless ascension mode. I am somewhat glad the developers are leaving the project, as iteration made it worse not better.\t\t\t\t\t
