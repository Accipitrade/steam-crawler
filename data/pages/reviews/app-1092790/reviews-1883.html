\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game started out great! I just wanted to keep playing and learning about it. The story was fine, but to be honest i didnt really care about it and just wanted to play the card game.

And then came act 2, it was ok. felt very disjointed but i assume that was the intent. I liked the fact that you could actually build a deck and figure out probabilities and how many hands you would need to play etc. the only real downside to act 2 for me was the fact it felt too linear which was taking out a big part of the enjoyment for me.

But then came act 3. i hated it. all i wanted to do was get through it so i could hopefully get back to act 1. the sounds were very harsh compared to the hushed mood of act 1. The card game was simply, an entire different game that spirals out of control and became a "die over and over until you get a one-turn win" for each fight. then after finishing it just to try getting back to act 1. the game just resets. i dont get it. playing with an oro that has 105 attack/def with trisplit damage might be op but it was fun. now everythings undone and even if i wanted to try some fun builds or odd ways to play again, id have to replay the entire game just to unlock what ive already completed?

id say the game was overall really cool while having some super nice artwork and atmosphere. but all of that feels moot if the good parts are erased just by going through other parts you may not enjoy. dont punish players for playing more of the game. maybe i missed something and theres some hidden mode i just dont see. So if in the future i could just play the actual card game parts of it, id be entirely sold on all of it. until then, was fun while it lasted i guess.

edit: after trying out kaycees mod, id say the overall experience is much better. if you want story you have it, if you just want a card game, you have it. i havnt finished it completely yet, but so far the challenge and builds seems to be surprisingly varied! however, the first run seemed to be quite a bit harder than subsequent for me. maybe i was just rusty i dont know. theres only a few small downsides i can find and most are linked to progression. maybe im just not good enough but it seems like uber buffing a few cards is more consistent than playing with synergies. i hope that changes with more runs though, since unlocking new cards feels nothing short of checking if you got a new OTK. but with that said, every time ive won so far has been with a different type of deck, so props for making a wide range of viability.

the only other thing i miss is death cards. would be interesting to have some alternative like picking one card each time you win and starting with it next run, but if it dies its gone for good. im sure someones got better ideas than me though.

all in all, great execution and follow-up on an already great game.\t\t\t\t\t
