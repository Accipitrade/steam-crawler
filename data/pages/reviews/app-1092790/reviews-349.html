\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite some minor stuck and google-ing, I managed to pass the game under 20 hours.
I would 9/10 recommend this game to someone who loves building their card decks and enjoys strategic board game.

Spoiler alert....


First, I wish the ending of act 1, when Leshy is captured by the camera, there is more hint or instruction to start a new game. I have stuck not knowing what to do next.

Second, some hints on how to solve the puzzle would be nice. I have a hard time understanding how to reach 5/5 on the balance board puzzles.

Third, when we are supposed to throw three nameplate in the tombstones. The lighting and angle on the third tombstone for P03 was really hard to see and easily missed. Maybe make the nameplate glow a bit in the dark?

I like the 3D art and effect with act 1 in Leshy cabin and act 3 in PO3 trapped room. I am not really a fan of cartoon pixel art for act 2 but that is my personal tastes. It felt out of sync.

Otherwise, I really enjoyed playing this game. It has offered so much experience more than just a strategic deck building game. There is a storyline and the storyline is well incorporated in the game playing. The game gives off different tastes/vibes when act 2 and act3.

I really like act1 play style and I hope the developer can make it an option to battle other srybes like in the ending. It would be nice that I can choose to battle PO3/Wizard/Death God like Leshy in Act1.
