\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was definitely far more than what I expected from what I saw initially on the store page and trailer. That's about as far as I will go in terms of content due to spoilers, but definitely worth getting for the in dept story that lurks in the dark unknown of this game.

I died a lot at the beginning trying to figure out the deck building, the mechanics and abilities, but once I got the hang of it I became an unstoppable force. This game allows room for experimentation and finding a style of play that suits you. I went with the "how broken and OP I can make my deck" strategy and managed to achieve a virtually "infinite damage in first turn" build but limited it to 1006 as I can't go infinitely because I did want to finish the game. Even just going to 6 damage is enough let alone 1000 more.

So yes, if you like a deck building game with a story attached to it, you'll definitely get your money's worth and on top of the story. You can even torture yourself with a challenge mod which has even more additional content.

I just wish I had more time to play with all the Scrybes but maybe some day.\t\t\t\t\t
