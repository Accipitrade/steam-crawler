\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGOD TIER CARD GAME!
BASED AF STORY MODE!

MINOR SPOILERS :D

My play time is closer to 20-25 hours in playtime atm as I've left the game open as I get busy or leave it on overnight so I don't lose battle progress, but I've recently finished the games story mode and it was great through and through.

I think the initial card gameplay is the best but thats good because Kaycee's Mod is top tier, sadly they did nerf a couple sigils but the game is still relatively easy to go through even with challenges. One of the few problems I find with Kaycee's Mod and ultimately the game as a whole is its too easy. I legit had to look up a guide on this because I was unable to progress the game for my first 15 hours of gameplay. Kaycee's Mod is also pretty easy but its a lot less "every game is insta-win." It makes a couple changes and multiple challenges that can be toggled to fulfill a point criteria to unlock new mechanics but I still have not lost a single run that has not been intentional and I know I'm not that good at games so I'd like to see a difficulty increase that isn't just one of those toggles for "Bigger Numbers, More Enemies."

Disclosure about me saying the game is easy I'm not saying it to brag or be like, "Hahaha, I'm so great." It unironically is a problem because as I was in Leshy's Cabin I was 14+ runs in without losing and I just could not figure out what the knife does or how to progress the story. Sadly this resulted in me growing frustrated and having to lookup a guide on how to progress as I really didn't want to have to do that. It TURNS OUT that you NEED to lose ONCE in order to play the cinematic of Magnificus and his painting of the knife which would only then finally spawn the Runic Eye to progress further in the game.

After that though there were no unclear progression issues so no guide is needed.

The synergies you can create at all stages of the game are amazing. Also full disclosure part 3 when you can create your own card is NOT balanced, you can make a 0 cost card that has detonator and unkillable and just hammer it on repeat to destroy all cards on the board. Other then that god tier mechanics, creativity, innovation, presentation, and story telling. Definitely shoulda been GOTY for almost every category for 2021.

This is one of the games I really encourage avoiding guides and spoilers as much as possible in order to get the maximum enjoyment and satisfaction of completing this game.\t\t\t\t\t
