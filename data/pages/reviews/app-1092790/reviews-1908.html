I saw this game when it first came out and I loved Slay the Spire, so it looked appealing to me. I remember reading the reviews and I went to the negative reviews and they turned me away from the game saying its "misleading" or "underdeveloped" or "short" and whatnot, so I never bought it.

I then watched others play the beginning of the game and was like, "holy crap!! this is a really cool and interesting roguelike card game!" and bought it immediately. I spent a day or two having fun playing the game and learning all the cool mechanics and solving the escape room puzzles and holy crap...

Just play it. Don't listen to others, if you love card games or story games just play it. I get that it is a bit misleading but it's still amazing.

I do hope the devs make fully fledged card game(s) out of each one though! They were really good.\t\t\t\t\t
