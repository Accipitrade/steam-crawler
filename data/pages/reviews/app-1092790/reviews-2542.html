\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has a very strong start (everything shown in promo and trailer) but after ~3 hours the twist happens and with that everything just goes down hill.
(The twist is a complete 180 turn and the only thing in common with the first part is, that the main gameplay loop still revolves around a card game)

The premise is that "you" found a game in the woods and are now playing it and, who had guessed, there are spirits in the game, besides that, the game has some ARG elements.
The Developer is know for this kinds of games... sadly I didn't do my research and didn't know that.

All together the base card game that you play at the start is very strong, sadly the moment that part is over everything just gets mediocre.

So if you don't like irl lore stuff in games (e.g. ARGs or that """you""" found a game in the """real""" world and the game is cursed or smth) then I don't recommend this game.

There is an official mod that enables you to play only the first part with challanges but this was sadly just an after thougth by the Dev and it shows, there are no rough-like elements like in the normal game (first-half only).\t\t\t\t\t
