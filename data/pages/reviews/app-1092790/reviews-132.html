Music: 9/10 i love the boss music, and much of the game's atmosphere is tied to its music. I don't get tired listening to it, which is strange for me. i end up singing along.

Game play: 9/10 The core mechanics are fun to play and use, and is very intuitive. Plus the game is not to harsh with punishing the player as there are work arounds to being close to death. There are portions of the game more boring than others, and saying that they're supposed to be boring is not an excuse.

Story: 10/10 The story is expansive, thought provoking, and emotionally involving. The characters have their unique quirks, and the story makes the player question their involvement.

There is one issue with the game I fear it will most likely be a one shot, and that is terribly sad. Even though they added more mechanics the thought that these characters won't be expanded upon further upsets me. The opportunity for a huge franchise is there.

TLDR: Near perfect game, enthralling story and characters, Plenty of end game content.\t\t\t\t\t
