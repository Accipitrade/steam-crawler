\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption. Man what a game. Without spoiling too much, this game is incredible and tells an amazing story that I assume changes slightly depending on some choices you make. My only gripe is that there is not much replayability once you beat the game, and I would love a DLC with the game that allows you to ***SPOILER INCOMING*** play with the 4 different Scrybes in each of their environments and game settings. An endless mode of the original game with Leshy would also be a great addition. But for what it is right now, with its aesthetics, deck building, music, story, gameplay and detail that rings true to Pony Island before it, it deserves my utmost respect. Good Game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the weirdest experiences I have ever had with a game. Similar to the Hex, a game from the same developer, this is a creepy, almost horror game about deck building. Until you get through it, you will be addicted. It is one of the best thought through games, with everything supporting everything. Heck, even if I understand correctly, this has an ARG inside it with all the videos and finding the stuff for completion. This is a game you should play. A short one, but a replayable one for sure. With bosses that actually have really good and interesting mechanics, and the fact that the game has literally 3 different parts with different graphics, types of cards, and even a fancy epilogue with an unforgettable ending(in the creepy meaing). I recommend playing this while you can, because this might just be a GameFuna mistake that makes it an illegal game. Anyways, just play it. It's fun.\t\t\t\t\t
