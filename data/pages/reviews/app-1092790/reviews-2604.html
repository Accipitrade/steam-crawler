If you watch the trailers and played the demo, you know that there is more to this game that meets the eye but I will not spoil it. What I will say is go into this with an open mind, the game is built for the story and the story is built for the game.

There is a meta and that's all I can say without spoilers.

Mechanics are simple enough but combine to give complexity but it throws several curve balls... that's all I can say without spoilers.

Solid cohesive graphics for the world it is in and adds to the story... advanced warning, there is an element of horror in this game; not the jump scare type, but the uneasy tension that comes when the world is slightly off and the 4th wall isn't entirely solid.

This game does have FMV and Acting and performances are great and reflect the world it is in.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to start by saying this is a really fun game, building a strategic deck while over coming different obstacles and limitations that spice up the game play. The escape room element of the game is interesting, but feels less optional and more mandated after the first part of the game. I should mention that its better to play this game without watching other people play the escape room parts, as I was able to pass the first portion a lot faster than I think I should have. Also, the game feels short, I was able to beat the game in about 8 hours. IDK if I was lucky in terms of beating difficult challenges or if I skipped content. And there isn't a lot in terms of post game content. In the future I would hope they include a challenge mode that lets you experiment with the full extent of the content with increasing difficulty with challenges.\t\t\t\t\t
