\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most interesting games I've ever played.

Likely the best sound design, second maybe to SSBM.
Incredibly immersive gameplay, with this low, crunchy OST accompanied by effects that are so weighty you feel like you are there. Not to mention the absolutely beautiful visuals stretching across a couple different styles.
The gameplay is fantastic, coming from someone who usually finds deckbuilding a bore, I found Inscryption's to be very rewarding with some unique as well as tried and true mechanics.

I'm just floored honestly, I finished some degree of this game and put it down then a month later completed it in one sitting. I was absolutely taken by this game.

Get it if you're on the fence and just go in sight unseen. And if you've seen some streamer playing the game, don't worry because the gameplay loop is not as basic as it seems. There is a LOT that went into Inscryption.\t\t\t\t\t
