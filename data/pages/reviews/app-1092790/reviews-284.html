\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAllow me to start this off by saying: I have not finished the game's storyline, nor do I plan to.

The game is fantastic, the card game system of blood and bones to use cards of varying power and ability, it's just great. The horror theme of fighting for your life with the aforementioned cards to delay your end at the hands of your captor is just great. I enjoy the boss characters and the event characters immensely, with my favorites being the Mycologist/s and the Woodcarver. Now from this so far, you likely question the negative review, and I will now explain.
All of the footage, gameplay, and story you see before buying the game is the first of 3 "Acts". This initially seems like an interesting concept until the very moment you realize these Acts dramatically change the mood, tone, and gameplay. If the entire game were nothing but Act 1, with the masked characters being elaborated on in that setting, I would continue playing. I am aware of the existence of Kaycee's mod, which is just endless Act 1, but it lacks any advances in the story.
If you like the promo content and are aware and accepting of the dramatic shifts in gameplay and overall vibe, I'd say to pick it up. If you only like the "battle for your life in a cabin against a psychopath with masks", I'd hold off on the impulse buy. Unfortunately due to how much I enjoyed Act 1 and played it thoroughly, I am not eligible for a refund, but that is something I am willing to accept.\t\t\t\t\t
