\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI suck at card games, and I despise roguelikes, but this game is just different. Without spoiling the unforeseen twists in story and gameplay, I cannot give away too much about the content of the game. Just know this much, there is not a lot of grinding involved. You constantly make some sort of progress, sometimes, without noticing it. The graphics are crisp, the sound and music design are captivating, and the story is full of surprises.

Similar games that come to mind are definitely "Slay the Spire" and "Loop Hero" but some puzzles are closer to "There is no Game", and the plot twists are more akin to "NieR".

I started the game out by myself, but my SO asked to play it together. And it turns out, this game is great for backseat gaming. Each battle and the path of the run can be tackles as a puzzle together. I'm always on the lookout for games to play together, but this one caught me by surprise. I would never have considered it as some kind of Co-Op experience, but here we are.\t\t\t\t\t
