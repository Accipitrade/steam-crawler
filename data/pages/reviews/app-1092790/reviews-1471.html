So, overall I think i heavily enjoyed the game, I will however say that act 1 is the best atleast fun-wise, I enjoyed act 2 but throughout the entirety of acts 2 and 3 i was thinking "ok when do i get back to how act 1 was?" and then the game just kind of ended.
