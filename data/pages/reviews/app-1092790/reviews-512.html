\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10/10 -- HIGHLY Recommend!!!

TL:DR This game is stellar in almost every way-- mechanics, story, puzzles, and sound design.

Ho-Ly !@#$. This has to be the best game I've played since the pandemic began. It's an engaging mix of escape room-style puzzles, deck-building board game, and (light) horror suspense!

I never thought a card game would have such an engaging story! But the journey we go on as the player leaves so much room for wondering and mystery! The fact that some things are burried in the game (or only get shown once you beat a level in Kaycee's Mod) makes you want to replay to make sure you didn't miss anything.

Sound design-- A+
Graphics-- Spooky AF (and vary between levels!)

I !@#$ing love this game, I'm pretty sure you will too!\t\t\t\t\t
