\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell, It's a damn fine deckbuilding game.

Card game mechanics......Good, but simple if you play MtG. In fact I think the game punishes you if you start combo'ing too early. Haha. Still a lot of fun though.

The complexity is in the meta game, where your opponent keeps hitting you with new things, or outright throws 8 bears at you out of nowhere. And the rogue-like features are really fun.

Only beat it once, but so far, I can say the story is a 5/10. The game story is cool, but all the meta-meta-stuff (yes, more meta than meta) trades style for sense.
The example is analog filtering as if video taping a CRT screen; so the youtuber is doing a modern style video (theme is less than 7 yrs old yet CRTs were last produced in 2007 and at 480p) , streaming to a CRT, then analog recording the CRT with a camcorder, and upscale digitizing that to YT. Or so the filter tells us, it emulates recording a CRT screen at a 20deg angle. I could be wrong and the developer only chose a random analog filter on the live action stuff because analog filters are lit.... Should have just made a clear YT video, and kept the game as a WIN95 PC. But even then, floppy was on its way out. But who know what DOS 5.1 is anymore.....

But that's really my only complaint. Remove that and its a solid 9/10; worth the $20\t\t\t\t\t
