Tier AB

A unique approach to roguelike deck-builders, resembling Banners of Ruin x 5 Nights at Freddie’s. The addition of out-of-run progression was an interesting touch, unlocking new cards through puzzles scattered around the room. Cards feature an interesting diversity of perks and resource types, and items effectively feel important and powerful. Card buffing and merging helps build up decks in your favor without too much randomness that other of the genre suffers from. Some abilities, items, and stages being shrouded in mystery adds an interesting element to the game - most deck-builders are explicit in functionality, but the mystery meshes appropriately with the game’s atmosphere.

Overall, the well-combined mix of atmosphere, difficult gameplay, and flow added up to a noteworthy game, bringing a unique artistic vision into deck-builders.\t\t\t\t\t
