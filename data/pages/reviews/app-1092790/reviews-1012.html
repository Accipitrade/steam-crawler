\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCute game, neat concept. It has some major flaws that keep me from recommending it though.

First, some minor complaints: The music is bad. The "story" sections with live action cutscenes are execrable and should have just been cut entirely. But the big problem is that you'll get absolutely stuck at certain parts and there's no way through except trial and error. It's not fun and it makes the rest of the game not worth it.

Minor spoilers here: For example, in Act 2 I got stuck at a place where I needed to buy more packs to progress but there was no way to earn foils to do that. That was annoying. Eventually I just had to keep retrying the boss battle until I managed to get through with the under-powered deck I had. I should also note that in my case winning this fight depended on an undocumented card interaction found through trial and error. (A card says it does one thing, but actually it does something else if you repeat the action enough times.) Some people enjoy that kind of thing but I did not.

Another example was Act 3, where some of the "puzzles" are not puzzles at all but more like "guess what I'm thinking and click everything and wander around in frustration for hours before looking up the answer". The worst.

Apart from that, the game was entertaining. Especially Act 1. It's not bad... but yeah it's skippable. I'd say don't play it.\t\t\t\t\t
