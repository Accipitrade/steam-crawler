\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame was fun for a while, then it stopped being in the vein of Slay The Spire and started being like the Pokemon Card Game on the Gameboy Color. At that point, the shallowness of the gameplay becomes super apparent when you simply spam concede till your hand is good and then beat the opponent. And because I realize that now I am playing a narratively driven game (rather than a gameplay driven game), beating the first part (that was run based) feels like it was an inevitability rather than any interesting challenge I overcame(which is the single best feeling Slay the Spire invokes as you beat harder and harder Ascension runs). This shift would have been fine if the narrative Inscryption decided to weave was quality. The problem is the FMV Youtuber story line is painfully cringy to watch. I imagine a defense for this drivel from the developers would be that the cringe angle is intentional, but frankly succeeding at being cringe isn't a particularly glowing (or frankly worthwhile) achievement.

After beating the second "Gym Leader" in the second phase of the game and being subjected to more truly dreadful cutscenes, I said this. Inscrpytion is a game that unfortunately gets worse and worse the more you play it. It's downward trajectory leaves such a bitter taste that I wish I watched vods of people playing this game instead of spending money on it. Not because the cost was high, but because had I'd known I was supporting a developer baiting and switching the nature of the product I purchased, I'd have stayed the hell away.

What starts out as a deeply atmospheric spin on Slay the Spire, ultimately loses all possible momentum and becomes a shallow, uninteresting spin on Pokemon GBC (that also decided to wear the skin of Undertale and increase its focus on terrible storytelling).

I think I would be less bitter about this game if either the gameplay didn't become shallow spam/concede trash or if the increased focus on narrative wasn't also shallow trash. But with both of them stinking up the experience, this game gets a hard pass from me dawg.
