I really, really wanted to like this game.

But the longer the game went on, the more it squandered all the goodwill it earned from everything I mentioned above. I won't mention the details, because I don't want to spoil it for people, but the game's entire last 1/3 basically felt like a slow, long, wet fart, to the point where it made me regret having played the game at all. And at the end of the wet fart, it ended in the worst kind of rejected-from-the-SCP-Foundation generic creepypasta.

I cannot in good conscience recommend this game to anyone.\t\t\t\t\t
