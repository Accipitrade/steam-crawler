\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery mild, vague spoilers ahead:

Just completed my first playthrough and I (mostly) loved this game to bits. One of the most unique outings I've seen all year. The visuals, the music, the sound engineering... the atmosphere and presentation in general, it's all immaculate. Loved the puzzles and the secrets and the overall mystique of everything. It kept me guessing and had me wondering what all the possibilities were. The gameplay is very addicting. The difficulty, the progression system, and the rogue-like aspects all combine to create a gameplay cycle that is hard to put down. Well... for the first act.

My biggest complaint by far is the story. I can see how some people would find it to be very appealing, but I thought it was messy and dated. Or perhaps just not executed in a way that is compelling for modern standards. It's got a creepypasta way of feeding it to you with some somewhat awkward pacing and timing. I wish I felt that it hit its mark, but for me it just missed. Increasingly so as the story went on. Not sure how else it could have been done, however I almost feel like I would've preferred more of it to be buried in mystery with massive craters for plotholes for me to fill up using my own imagination.

The game was also longer than I first anticipated. I was impressed at first by the length until it started to drag towards the end. Again, the unraveling plot line was not doing any magic on me. And additionally, some of the gameplay mechanics were being switched up as I progressed. Not in ways that added layers of depth, necessarily, but just changed how some card battles were played fundamentally. I don't know, some would say it keeps things fresh but it actually started to feel less fun and satisfactory as I pushed on.

Overall, I was so impressed with the first act that I will continue to do at least one more playthrough... I will also keep a close eye on updates or DLC in the future. I would recommend this game to nearly anybody because of just how unique and endearing it is. Clearly a ton of love and care was put into it, and I certainly got my money's worth.

Upon first playthrough, my feelings go as such;

Act 1: 5/5. Phenomenal. It has my heart. Started off SO strong that the lackluster ending doesn't even irk me as much as it should.

Act 2: 4/5. Pretty neat. Kept me curious and on my toes.

Act 3: 2/5. Yeahhhh wellllll... I don't know, it just fell apart for me.\t\t\t\t\t
