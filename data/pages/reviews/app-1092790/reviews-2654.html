\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, it surprised me several times. Enjoyed it quite a bit, although I have a lingering criticism of it that is just spoileriffic.

The game requires a complete blind playtrough so dont spoil for yourself with my review.


Inscryption starts off very well and the game is quite fun trying to get behind all the rougelike elements and the cardgame itself, but when you finally beat Leshy (Beast Scrybe), you are suddenly revealed the story of a youtuber Luke guy who collects cards and found the Inscryption video game. The story behind that itself is not that engaging or intruiging, but its fine for those who want to dig further. Anyways, the game just changes into an RPG maker esque visuals and now you have cards with other kinds of mechanics available. This was cool, although I felt like it overstayed its welcome because I was so used to the 3D of Leshys Cabin and the mystique behind it. Now im in this videogamy 2D world that is pretty easy if you let the auto complete make a deck for you.

The new game style just felt a bit out of place and over stayed its welcome, having me playing the entire thing. And then, suddenly when completing that, Po3 takes over and you are in his world now. Complete with 3D again with puzzles and stuff. But Po3s world is probably my least prefered world to be had because its all so very blue, computery and does not lend well with the atmosphere.

Leshy had atmosphere and it had quirky and horror elements while Po3 is just gimmicks and un assuming atmosphere. It does not help that Po3s card gameplay overstays its welcome as well, taking a good chunk of the game again. Its no longer like a rogue like like in Leshys cabin, and it just feels odd to play.

During which the story takes left and right turns that dont make much sense to me, I mean they really dont make sense to me... we get a glimpse at the Necromancer and Wizard worlds as well, how those would be. But only as a taste.

And you know... that sucks a LOT! Ide rather have Po3s world be reduced in order for the other 2 to have more game and spotlight to them. Instead, we get teased some awesome gameplay of the necro lady. Although, the overproduced Yugioh world of the Wizard is definetly too much, but with Po3s world reduced, I would like even the wizards gameplay to have some depth.

The game pulls me out of one gameplay (Leshy) into another (2d and Po3) but also completelly skips out on the others. I dont get it, why do it this way? I found that awefully dissapointing actually. I was waiting for the next set of surprises, but instead I was stuck in po3s world... \t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am 42.

My ex-wife and I have a daughter together, and adopted our son together. They are now both 4 years old.

When we were going through our separation, I found myself lost and miserable. I was self destructive. I got so mad one day from everything spiraling out of my control that I punched some concrete in a moment of overwhelming emotion. That caused me to break my 5th metacarpal in my right hand... my working hand... my games hand.. the hand that I held and carried my children to bed with.. The hand I desperately needed to make sure I could continue to provide.

After learning of the severity of my self-inflicted damage, I was borderline suicidal. Keep in mind that just a few months before this, I was the happiest man with no history of depression or anxiety. I have never had fits of rage, or been one to break down and cry, but I was in a low spot that just really buried me from being able to see the light on the other side.

Having nothing better to do, I searched for a game I could play, ONE HANDED while I recovered. I somehow stumbled upon this game and read some of the reviews. I decided that it had to be worth a shot... I must admit, I didn't beat the game, or play nearly as long as some of you. In fact, I may have only played this game a day or two.. With that being said, after doing so, I had a new found joy and hope for life. I was able to put behind me the pain and suffering that had been cast over me. I was able to experience other peoples joy and happiness. I was able to see the fruits of my "labor". I relaxed for 5 ♥♥♥♥ing minutes to this music long enough to realize that I was going to be okay.

After coming to that realization, I turned the game off, and I went back to work. It hurt my hand like hell but I was motivated. I stopped feeling so damn sorry for myself, and I became the father I needed to be in that moment, not the weak boy I was behaving as.

Today, I am close friend with my kids mother. We don't fight, or argue, or say hurtful things to each other. We are parents, and friends.

I now have 3 kids. My third child is, wait for it, ALSO 4 YEARS OLD. The woman I am with was going through a very similar situation at the time of my own separation, and we just stumbled in to each others life unexpectedly. We have been in a relationship for a year now, and are very happy together.

Moral of the story, you never know what life holds in store for you, and if I would have given up when all the odds were stacked against me, I wouldn't be where I am today. This silly little game helped me realize that.

That's all, thank you.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow! Beat this in one day. 7 full hours but it could easily been done in less.

I actually had to change my review retrospectively. I think this game does a bait and switch, and IS NOT a roguelike deckbuilder TCG with a cool artstyle, unfortunately, the game changes about 3 or 4 hours in, and a lot of people have been saying it's for the worse. Sorry devolver, and i'm sorry to the people who put a lot of talent and effort into this game.
The music, the setup, all of it is great... until you're left like this.

---SPOILERS---

\\

The story is so corny! Feels like a generic creepypasta from the mid 2000's, and not in a good way! The really cool themeing of the hunters cabin gets trashed for generic pixel art that IS NOT ERA ACCURATE!! But i digress.. the bottopia area was cool, but it dragged on, and the unique card gimmicks of conduits and gems had no time to build.

Unsure if there's more to this game than going through, i think there was one box in P03's hub that i didnt open. Was hoping for more content or an actual roguelike, not so much a one and done story.

Hire better actors! Please.\t\t\t\t\t
