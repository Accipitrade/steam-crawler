I do have a couple problems with it though.

There's a bit that goes on all throughout the game with live-action video cutscenes that I really hated, and I feel like actively takes away from the game. Luckily, you can skip all of it if you aren't interested, but the end won't make much sense. (Avoiding spoilers, the end would have been 1000x better without it)

Easy fix, please add a new game+ that allows stuff from later in the game to be used in the beginning, and is a more dedicated rouguelike. I would love that so so much.\t\t\t\t\t
