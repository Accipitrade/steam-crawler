I'm not typically a card game fan but I saw five minutes of gameplay from YouTube and had to try it out. I played through the game with pretty good expectations but I did not expect such an immersive and fun experience. I didn't expect to get as gripped into the story as I did.


I think anyone who gets a chance should play this game. Even if you don't like card games, give this game a try. It has something for everyone. If the "horror" aspect is holding you back, just know Horror is not a good way to describe this game. It is a creepy atmosphere, but as far as horror there is none. Don't expect jumpscares or anything or the like.
