The twists and turns with the narrative are fun to witness but can be a bit hokey especially when you know what's coming on repeat playthroughs. Kaycee's mod nearly triples the replay value and time, if not more than that. Still diggin away at the game and don't plan on stopping any time soon.
I would recommend two main story playthroughs, maybe even three. The first playthrough I'd recommend not looking anything up, and just letting the game take you for the ride; as that will make all the cool twists and turns as effective as they can be. Your second and possible third playthroughs of the campaign should be with guides or the like giving you all those juicy exploits, cuz those are extremely fun to play around with once you know they're there, but they do make any encounter completely trivial with a little luck.
Once your playthroughs are done, jump into Kaycee's mod and keep the game going and have a blast!\t\t\t\t\t
