\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can not recommend this game enough.

Card games are not usually my thing, in games like The Witcher I would actually avoid the card game and even install mods just so I could skip it. This game on the other hand throws in so much mystery and new mechanics every 10 minutes that I kept getting pulled back into the game.

The mechanics work so well together in each setting, and the constant underlying horror/scary part works really well too.
The game is also very meta, the more you progress, the more you notice that nothing in the game is what it seems.

And I don't want to spoil anything, but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, the ending of this game is the best I've seen in a while.\t\t\t\t\t
