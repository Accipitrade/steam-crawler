\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe best game i ever played, or better sad, like my first game on pc.

It was "Ballerburg" and than "monkey Island".
Everything was new and you don´t know what happens next.

This game is like you enter a new world and you know nothing and get surprised every time.

But there are things i don´t like too.

The first point for some people are the riddles. I got the most, but only in Part one i tryied.

Part 2 is such an anying part of the game - i would skip it. I think there are different stories When you choose another way, but its boring, and i quit every battle suddenly, when my cards was bad.

Another Bad point of the game - the only challenge are the riddles.

So it is more an adventure than a stragey game, because the strategy is such simple, a kid could win this game, but the atmosphere isn´t for kids.

So what me get in this game was the freshness.
It is like water in a desert, more like an oase in the desert of the gaming world today.

But i wouldn´t play it again after i beat the game ( i´m finish at this moment, but have things to do - but i will not)
because when the story is over, why play a game with no strategy, no difficulty and no surprise.

So it is like a one time wonder. But when you get the things, you can´t be surprised two times from the same,
so i paid the price vor 16,5 hours, but this time was much more worth than 1000 hours rimworld or anything like this.

Nothing can be so good, like something really new.\t\t\t\t\t
