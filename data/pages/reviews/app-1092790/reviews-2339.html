\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(NO SPOILERS REVIEW)

First off, i am a big Deck-Building Roguelike/ Card game player and i also am a fan of Devolver Digital so i decided to pick this game up.

I went in totally blind and i suggest if you purchase this game, you should too. Devolver Digital always adds lots of secrets and challenges to their games so it's very fun to see them unravel when you find them yourself.

If i could describe this game in a nutshell without boring you i would call it a cool twist on Hearthstone-type gameplay with the creepy feel and art of Undertale. In all honesty this game is as creative as it gets, i really am impressed on all of the features that i don't wanna spoil... But what i can say is that there is a TON of card combos and abilities (more than i anticipated) and gets crazier and more in depth the longer you play.

It's not just a card game though, there is a story and lets just say there's more than meets the eye here... I really can't say much else about it without ruining it for you.

I personally, beat the game and it takes you for an INSANE ride. If you love card games and a wicked story, i would definitely give this a try!\t\t\t\t\t
