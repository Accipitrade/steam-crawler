\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI liked the game a lot for the first act. Card gameplay is engaging, and exploring the cabin and solving puzzles is rewarding. Second act of the game essentially becomes a different game entirely, where instead of a rogue like deck builder it is more akin to hearthstone + pokemon. You have to collect cards, and now have 3 additional deck types with their own unique resource systems. Not really interested in learning 4 different mechanics in a less appealing visual medium. Plot has similar issues to the gameplay. At first you are being held hostage by a magic woods hobo, and then it sort of loses all its engagement once the world changes. With such a strong start, it ends up leaving a bad taste in my mouth.\t\t\t\t\t
