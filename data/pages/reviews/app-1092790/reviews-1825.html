\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first glance of screenshots of the game I wasn't very interested, but after seeing my favorite youtuber (John Wolfe) play it just seeing the first few minutes of it had me hooked. I could wait no longer to see more of this game, bought it and started playing it immediately. Even playing it over a few times was still really fun to play, all the different ways you could build your deck and strategies you can use, as well as secrets to discover.

The game has one of the most charming visual designs ever, and is constantly showing you new ways that it can be creative. It's no surprise that it comes from the Pony Island devs once you start getting into the twists and turns of the story and design. The soundtrack is amazing, with a very fitting and atmospheric design for each boss or unique encounter. The story gets more interesting as you go with much of it hidden to discover.

As I saw someone else say the only downside about Inscryption is that you can only play it for the first time once. The quality of the experience is magnified by how blind you are going into it. Definitely recommend not watching more than the early part of the game if you look it up online and you want to play it.\t\t\t\t\t
