\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a multi-part narrative deckbuilder. The bulk of the plot is told via text chatting with characters and sections of fmv focusing on 1 character attempting to discover the secrets of Inscryption. The card battling is simple: Start with a deck of one kind or another, and then add cards as you play to the deck until you win or lose. If you lose, you start over with a basic deck (for the most part, this changes later), and if you win you progress to the next section.

If you're a fan of deckbuilders, you'll probably like this game though you may find it to be kinda easy. If you've never played a deckbuilder before, that's ok. This is a good introductory game in that you're going to win eventually so long as you pay attention, are willing to explore, and don't give up. About the only people I wouldn't recommend this to are:
A- People who have no interest in card games, for whatever reason.
B- People who don't like thinking.
Everyone else is probably safe to give this a shot.\t\t\t\t\t
