\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a horror game that doesn't take it self to seriously. It's a grand time while doing so. There there very few dull moments and those moments are used to prepare you for the next. at the end of my first session I was hanging on to every word and laughing at every joke, gasping at every twist and on the edge of me seat for the next encounter.

For the main gameplay loop, you be collecting cards (Monsters in one form or another) and using them in a puzzle like form to out damage or out last the opponent's deck. The bosses mix this up by adding extra phases and their own special abilitys. I haven't seen one yet that I would label as 'poorly designed'. They are all absolutely wonderful and work perfectly to break up the main gameplay loop.

For the hard truth, I don't thing there is much if any replayabilty of the game. I just simply doesn't offer that kind of encounter. This isn't too bad. I also don't see this game being for everyone. I enjoy puzzle games and this is no different and while this has something special to it, at it's core people who don't enjoy the thought process of a card are going to have a hard time.

I had to let this game mull overnight to give it a fair review I went to bed well past my bed time and even then I had to tear myself away from the screen. I hope that the story doesn't end too soon. I can't wait to see what creative event happens next. I can't wait to update this review with more thoughts on how it does end (spoiler free of course).

Bang up job Devs, I am having a blast, I'll be seeing this title come the steam awards come this winter,
