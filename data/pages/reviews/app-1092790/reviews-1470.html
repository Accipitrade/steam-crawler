\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing marriage of mechanics, story, music, and visuals. The only challenge here may be for players who have low literacy with card game mechanics (a la Magic, Slay the Spire, Griftlands, etc.). However, this is not insurmountable and the game does a great job of easing the player in to these mechanics, rewarding continued learning with tangible progression elements. For those familiar with such mechanics, the reverse may actually be true - it can become easy to find dominant strategies. Any nitpicks aside, this game is well worth your time and your money; easily a personal pick for one of my top games of 2021. Bring film.\t\t\t\t\t
