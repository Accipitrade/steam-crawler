first off let me say that this game is great and probably one of the more unique ones iv played in a while, so for 20 bucks this game is a steal especially for how unique it is. so this review is more for if theres gunna be a second game or spin off.

ACT1 is the strongest portion of the game by far only weakend by not enough puzzles in my opinion i woulda liked to do a few more tbh. and i woulda liked a lil more deck building or a lil bit easier time making a themed deck like a rat deck or ant deck.

ACT2 was a surprising twist but after the initial surprise u come to realize theres not much to do on the map aside from battling people and thats it, it very linear and coulda used some puzzles of some sort, cuz it had almost none. surprisingly though this portion of the game brought in more of the deck building i wanted which was pretty cool. it also had easter eggs and lil hidden things that gave this portion of the game a lot of charm. i also really enjoyed the simulation of pack opening that was really fun.

ACT3 is the weakest portion of the game by far. they turn ur resources deck from act1 into nothing but body blockers, or taunts in hearthstone terms and when u do upgrade it, its still not very good. they again remove the deck building aspects and actually make it worse than ACT1 because u dont make a death card or even reset ur deck and build another when u die/lose ur deck is permanent basically. no decision making when moving around the map such as, deciding to get buffs or new cards. they brought the puzzles back but werent quite as good as act 1 imo.

over all the game is damn good and even tho ACT3 is the weakest, its still so unique u cant help but praise them for thinking outside the box. if they decide to do a second game or something similar i would really like to see more deck building, maybe some more actual card synergies and more puzzles.\t\t\t\t\t
