\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinally beat this game. I think, can't even know for sure. It can't be done justice in a review, you should stop reading reviews and just try it. If you're into single player stories and love feeling curious you seriously CANNOT go wrong with this game.

One of those games that just leaves you thinking of nothing else, as you wonder what is actually even going on and what will happen next. Strange occurrences happen a lot and it just gets weirder the further you get through it. Questions about what is actually happening beneath the foreground always lead to more questions when answered. Even after the game ends I'm left wanting more from the all the very memorable characters!

Visual style is amazing, never even realised there isn't a typical UI, it's all 3D interactive to start with... I said it's weird didn't I? so the game just feels so immersive, and atmospheric.

Storytelling goes from quaint and intriguing, to becoming a full on conspiracy with several other characters that aren't what they seem. Almost nothing is what it seems at first, and then eventually it explodes into just a total fustercluck, with the game not even being recognisable anymore, and bizarre plots and characters breaking the fourth wall. This game in it's entirety is so creative, it's worth every penny of the asking price.

Soundtrack is brilliant too, incredibly memorable characters and story twists. It's brilliant. Just buy it. The demo doesn't even give you a fraction of the full experience. I love this lil game.\t\t\t\t\t
