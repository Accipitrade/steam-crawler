\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is honestly one of the best games I have ever played.
From the story to the huge mix of different aspects (Creepy card game, found footage, 2D RPG) to the different features I didn't even think were possible, the game constantly had me surprised by new revelations.

The card game play itself is very fun and well polished, in frustrating moments you can hear the actual "player" getting frustrated, with an effect to emphasise that it is a recording of game play.

The story was very interesting and without sounding like a broken record I am yet to see anything similar (the closest I can think of is 'FNAF: Help Wanted' in terms of general plot) and the ending was definitely worth all of the effort.
The game also runs very well, and doesn't take much power at all to look stunning graphically even on an old
gaming laptop like mine.

There aren't really any negatives I can think of about the game, only really that in certain areas it isn't clear that you are supposed to lose in order for the story to progress.

10/10 cannot recommend this game enough.\t\t\t\t\t
