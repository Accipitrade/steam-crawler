\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJumanji for the modern age. A genuine love letter to gaming. And dying.

I did not expect this game to be one of my favorite games of all time. Absolutely mind blowing game experience. There is nothing like it. A masterful, hypnotizing tone arrests your senses, fully engaging you in it's cryptic plot. The soundtrack/sound design is key, creating comfortable soothing auras that will betray you; one of the few pieces of music I've ever been able to describe as comforting but anxiety fueling. Leave the volume at 100% and turn the lights off. Certain imagery draws comparison to David Lynch, even in the editing and pacing of the scary bits too; it takes its time building the atmosphere, and in at least one moment, takes its time making you suffer. 10/10 this game is a timeless indie classic that will be talked about for years to come. Unbelievable, unexpected journey.


Oh yeah and the card game is out of this world too. I wish it never ended.\t\t\t\t\t
