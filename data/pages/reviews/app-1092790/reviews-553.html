Inscryption has quickly and easily become one of the best games I have ever played. The mystery, the lore, the gameplay; all of it is incredibly addictive. When I was playing it for the first time, it was all I was thinking about. When I wasn't playing, I was thinking about when I would get to play more, and what was going to happen next, or how to solve certain puzzles. The atmosphere sucks you right in, the soundtrack is wonderful. The only complaint I have is that I'm sad the game wasn't longer. (Devs, I am incredibly thankful for Kaycee's Mod, btw, I'm addicted)

Please, give Inscryption a try. I can promise that you won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
