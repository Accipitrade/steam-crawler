\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm in two minds about this game, but overall it's a fairly good game, Kaycee's mod makes it especially worth playing.

I absolutely loved the first third of the game- the artstyle, the gameplay, sense of progression, and the puzzle-solving were all amazing. I loved the "game within a game within a game" style it had going on with the card battles and escape the room style puzzles. I really wish the whole game was more like that.

SPOILERS FOLLOW:

Unfortunately, it's not. While the general mechanics for the card battles remains the same, nothing else really does. I *get* that this game's trying to tell a story, but the story just isn't that well presented, original, or interesting. It features the kind of "plot twists" you can see coming from miles away, and each new hub-world you get dumped into seems more dull and lifeless than the last one. There's also some live-action shots in between each chapter with acting that I can only fairly compare to an amateur YouTube channel- that might have been the point, but those parts were just painful to endure.

Also- as the game progresses- the puzzles start to disappear , or get more and more simple, or just straight up repeat themselves. The charm and variability of the first act all seem to vanish, and it feels like you are just trudging through to see what the ending is (but, really, you already know how this is going to end, as mentioned before). The card games remain interesting- and there are a few new mechanics thrown in (as well as some old ones disguised as new ones!), but the new hub-worlds and maps will have you yearning for a return to Leshy's cabin.

So, yeah- this has been a pretty damning review for the most part, but I'd still recommend this game for the first third of it alone. Just don't expect it to get any better once you've completed that part. Check out the demo, and if you enjoy that- you'll definitely like the first part of this game. If you don't enjoy the demo- it's definitely best to avoid this altogether!

A really good idea for a game that sadly ends up feeling more-and-more poorly executed as it progresses.

7/10- an enjoyable ( but short) game with replayabie aspects thanks to the mods.\t\t\t\t\t
