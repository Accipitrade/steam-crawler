\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe graphics and gameplay are awesome. You're basically encouraged to make your deck as broken as possible, and the "final" boss even gives really good buffs before the fight.

However, this is a game by Daniel Mullins, who made Pony Island and The Hex. Those games, and this one too, have big meta story lines and ARGs. So this game isn't just a dark fantasy deck builder, that's only the beginning portion really.

That's not to say the rest of the game isn't fun to play, there is still deckbuilding and they expand on it quite a bit. A lot of the complaints I've seen seem to be people upset that this wasn't a normal game and think they were deceived. It's a ridiculous complaint in my opinion, because there are a lot of hints (mainly the developer's previous games and trailers) that shows its not just a rougelite card game.

Also once you've beaten the game, you can go back to the beginning and basically play the "regular" game over and over again.

Even if the 4th wall meta stuff sounds uninteresting, the actual card battles are still really fun. There is one mechanic later on that I found less enjoyable than the others and you're forced to use it. But other than that it's a super fun game. And if you're a fan of Daniel Mullins Games' stuff, this is probably their best so far.\t\t\t\t\t
