If the game had a "real and complete" roguelike mode, that wouldnt be a problem so much, but as the focus is more on the story, I think a satisfying ending should not be locked behind finding almost game breaking mechanics and secrets (even though thats kinda the point of the game). I mostly enjoyed my time with this game and I very much support the growth of these kinds of indie games, so I do recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
