\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm genuinely shook.
Before I get deep into the review, let me just state the essential notes for my review:
10/10. Oustanding game, outstanding experience. Perfect for a card game fan (e.g. Magic).
Took ~10 hours to beat, although I had seen some videos of the first couple hours of the game so I had knowledge going in. I'd say 16 hours is a fair time frame for beating the game.

Now to describe the game without giving too much away:
Inscryption is an absurdly fun and interesting PVE asymmetrical deck-building card game. That is the essential framework of the game. Three factors affect that players ability to win: rng, exploration, and their own ingenuity. The latter is the most important, but the other two can be limiting. Through trial, error, and exploration, the player is quickly taught and gently encouraged to use and abuse the mechanics of the game. The game uses a rogue-lite system of generally unlocking more content after subsequent player deaths or exploration. If the player makes honest attempts and fails and explores enough they will eventually find the tools they need to win. Through every failure the game slowly nudges the player toward winning.

Style. The game has an extraordinairy sense of style. The visuals remind me of the Dread X Collection 2 but that rough finish doesn't clash with the game's fantastic overall design. The framing of the story takes away a little bit more than it adds and often feels more like an interruption than an integral part of the experience. However, that framing was what left my mouth agape in shock at the end of the game so I'll not speak ill of it any further.

Subversion of expectations. Without giving much away, just when you think you fundamentally understand the game, its rules, and what to expect, the rug is pulled out from under you and you are cast into the murky waters of the unknown. Despite this, it builds on the essential rules of the game and managed to remain fairly consistent.

There's a lot more I could say say, but honestly just play the game
It's really good.\t\t\t\t\t
