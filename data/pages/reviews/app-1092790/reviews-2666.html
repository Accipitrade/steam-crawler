\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn excellent Rogue-Like Deck Builder blended with puzzles and a creepy atmosphere. The game is lengthier than you would expect and does manage to surprise you in the way only Mullins game can.

Pros:
Value: The price is more than fair for all the content this game has.
Story: It's interesting, although nothing extraordinary. Lots of lore and speculation to be had.
Presentation: The game looks good and sounds good.
Gameplay: Variety of builds and decks to be made. It's simple to understand and plays smoothly.

Cons:
Repetitive sections: Certain sections of the game can feel quite repetitive. It's not to say they are terrible, but you wonder why you're made to play said part rather than the best ones.
Underwhelming payoff: While being it does leave a lot to the imagination, the payoff at the end just wasn't on par with the rest of the game.

Overall, I rate this game a 8.5/10. Fans of deckbuilders will be the ones to enjoy it the most.
