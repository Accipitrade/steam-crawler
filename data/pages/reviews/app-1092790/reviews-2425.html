\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably one of the better game experiences I've had recently. From the roguelike elements to the deck building, I really like this one. I beat it in two sittings, totaling 6.9 hours (hehehehe) and even lost a couple god runs to bad RNG and bad decisions. I definitely think this game needs an online versus mode or an endless mode at least, because I want more. I would even pay for a real pack of cards to play with.

To bring everything back to a relevant point, this game is pretty dope and the story is nice.
Gameplay 10/10 Not often do I praise card games, and not often do I thoroughly grasp strategy games without becoming bored. This game blends perfectly the puzzle elements of survival horror and the unfairness of roguelikes to create a very suspenseful game that feels like playing a thriller... kind of
Story 8/10 Original, quirky and sinister. There are definitely times where the player is left feeling like all efforts are futile, but that only serves to make it a better game.
Sound Design 10/10, makes up for the lack of music with excellent sound design and a lot of thought and intent into every sound, giving every action weight
Music 5/10 I can't give a great or terrible rating because there is very little music\t\t\t\t\t
