\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdated after beating the full game and Kaycee's Mod, plus playing much more just for fun.
This game is really good, but it could have been and all-time great. The advertised game, the dark rogue-like deck-building card game escape room is an amazing concept and I wish the developer could have let go of his artistic vision and let the game be what it could have been instead. Kaycee's Mod gives us a bit more of the great card gameplay, but actually *less* cabin escape room. The dev could have easily put his meta-game story into the game with acts 2 and 3 being playable as sub-games within the cabin - like a floppy disc you can find to put into a computer within the cabin. Alas, it doesn't live up to the potential shown in the trailers but it's still one of the best games to come out recently and well worth the money.

Unbelievable, absolutely magnificent game. Played the demo and immediately bought the full game. Play the demo!

I've played much more now and Acts 2/3 are not as good as Act 1 - it would have been much better if they were meta-games to earn more progress within the cabin. That said, while I'm still going to finish it all (partway through Act 3 now), you can just play Kaycee's mod if you want to play the best part of the game for longer!\t\t\t\t\t
