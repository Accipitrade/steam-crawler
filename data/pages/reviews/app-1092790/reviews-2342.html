\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI came into this game thinking it had a cool aesthetic and that it was a regular old deckbuilder.
In broad terms, it is indeed a deckbuilder roguelike.

If you look more closely, it's actually a card builder and a damn good one at that.
The atmosphere the game paints for you, the puzzles you have to solve and everything in between fits so well.
When I bought this game I was like, yeah it looks like it's worth the 22$.
Having actually played it for 4 hours in one sitting, this game is worth much more than that, to the point that I almost feel dirty having purchased this at such a low price.

This game is easily worth twice that price and I would recommend this to anyone who is a fan of dark and gloomy atmospheric rogue-likes.

10/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnother gem from Daniel Mullins.

All of his games are very interesting takes on familiar types of other games.
Pony Island was a "platform" game. The Hex was an "Adventure" game. This one is a "deck-building" card game.
Please for the love of all that is holy, go into this game WITHOUT seeing any information/spoiler about it.
Finding some easy secrets and hard secrets inside the game is HALF the fun of all of Daniel's game.
Learning how everything works by trial and error is the other half.

Regarding the gameplay, do not expect Slay the Spire but instead play for the fun, for the experiments, there are no bad answers nor results and even though it could seem frustrating, after a while you can easily master the game.

It provides lots of fun and the mood is excellent, I think I got more immersed inside this game than when playing Pony Island and The Hex. Maybe it's the first person view and the more complex graphics, but this game is a pleasure for the eyes and the ears.

It's really good sometimes to play something "different" and have an enjoyable experience. If you want to experience a game and be played, I highly recommend it.
Think of it as going to a fancy gourmet restaurant and asking to be surprised by the chef. You won't know what you will get but it will be varied and top notch quality.\t\t\t\t\t
