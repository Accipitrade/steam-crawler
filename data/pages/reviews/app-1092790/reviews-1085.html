Despite the game's simplicity, it has just as many "lightbulb" moments and just as much room for strategy as the big-name trading card games.

Beyond that, I can't tell you any more. Just go play the game. If you're even a little bit of a fan of trading card games, I can safely say that you will like Inscryption. And if you like dark, weird, unconventional storytelling...then you're in for even more of a treat. Seriously, go play this game before you get spoiled. I promise It'll be worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
