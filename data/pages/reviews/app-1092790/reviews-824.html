I think there can be very little argument that this isnt a GREAT game.

I personally, cannot recommend it. I really enjoyed the scary and mysterious atmosphere cultivated in the beginning. The design, the open ended info. It all really made me fall in love. After passing a certain point, the game becomes a "retro" style game. With a very reduced palette, flat 2d graphics, with an expanded story.
The old classic snes era style is not for me, not even a little. I dont enjoy it, the game entirely lost me once we moved away from how it was presented as a more luck based, choices matter almost horror style game. Lots of people find the blending of generations of games and homages to the earlier days of games like super mario/pac man.
I am not one one of those people.\t\t\t\t\t
