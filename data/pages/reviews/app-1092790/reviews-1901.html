\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most interesting and intriguing games I have played in a while. The card game is deep enough to allow tactical fun while not having a steep learning curve. The writing is fun and witty when it wants to be and dark and threatening to build an atmosphere, it's perfect. The world building is interesting and the twists neat! As of now the game is a little bit on the shorter side if you are good at card games, but I really hope that some updates or DLC will expand on it. , especially the other two Scrybes' games.

Would definitely recommend this to anyone even remotely interested in cards and mysterious games!\t\t\t\t\t
