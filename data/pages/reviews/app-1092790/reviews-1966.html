\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a game that's better played when you know absolutely nothing about the game. It's honestly one of my favorite games I've played this year. It's story is enthralling, the card-based combat was fun and didn't feel repetitive to me, and the art style is wonderfully spooky at parts where it needs to be.

Edit: There's now an endless mode and it is now infinitely better than it was. The endless mode is more challenging than the base game, so don't go in expecting a cake walk, but it adds a new layer to the combat that I didn't experience on my playthrough of the story. This game is absolutely phenomenal.\t\t\t\t\t
