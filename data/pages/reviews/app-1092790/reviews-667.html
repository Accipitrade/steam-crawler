\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is by far one of the best games I have played in a long time, though to call it a game is missing the point a bit.

SPOILERS AHEAD

Where to begin, the gameplay, the story, the score, the art, all of it is excellent without a doubt, however this is something I don't see myself returning to which I will get into.

The closest comparison I can see would be a game like Undertale, in that it subverts expectations whilst still containing enough of the anticipated to keep everyone happy. If you go into Undertale wanting another RPG you'd be disappointed arguably, similarly going into Inscryption wanting a deck-building card battler roguelike you may feel the same. The gameplay in both these works serves as a means to deliver an engaging, mind bending story. As a result, they do lose out on replayability, not least of all because all the twists are now known, but because sometimes the gameplay takes a back seat to the story.

On the gameplay, it is a deck-builder obviously. drawing inspiration from everything from MtG, Yu-Gi-Oh, and many others I probably don't know. however the style of deck-builder changes and swaps between different styles, then mashes them together in various ways. At times it feels clunky and overburdened, but the effort behind it all is remarkable.

The overarching story can be a bit cheesy and B-movie at times, not to say the actors didn't give it their all, and deserve commendation for doing so, but perhaps this kind of blending of reality is better left strictly in text. there are a great deal of game that mess with the player in ways that blend fiction and reality like Inscryption. It is even shown in the game that it has the capacity for it, using your own files, and steam friends list.

That all being said, the solid if quirky deck-builder and odd but intriguing story is wrapped up with graphics, environments, models and a soundtrack that will entrance you. The presentation of it all definitely sets this game apart. Act 1 and 3 excel more than the rest in this regard in my opinion.

In brief, very good. 8/10. Stoat moment
