\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBorderline Masterpiece

Great story/immersion, great gameplay, and great artwork. The base game is a card-dueling game with simple enough mechanics but there is an entire deck-building aspect that adds replayability/strategy into the mix. The art style/story/gameplay all go so well together making this game feel much bigger than it actually is. Overall a must-play if you are into card games, puzzle games, or story-rich games.

Only complaint honestly is the third act. It draws many parallels to the first act thematically but the gameplay just falls a little short even though it did introduce some very unique and fun bosses.

Don't listen to anyone complaining about the second act. It plays a very important role to the story and is also pretty short. The gameplay isn't even bad and it has it's own appeal.\t\t\t\t\t
