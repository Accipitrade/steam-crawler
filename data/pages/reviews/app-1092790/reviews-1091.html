\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am not often a fan of card games, but the mechanics of this one are incredibly engaging. Beyond the card game(s) itself, too, are the abundance of puzzles and secrets encouraging a replay. After completion of the game, you are allowed to return to the game's other acts, both enabling the secret-seeking and letting you replay whatever parts were particularly fun.

My only complaint is the rather frequent freezing - especially prominent in act three. Throughout my playthrough, I had to restart my game more than twenty times. During the first and second act, there was usually at least thirty minutes between freezes, but act three (particularly the final battle before the credits) froze more frequently. The suggested solutions for this issue that I've been able to find online haven't worked - I'm not running any other programs and steam overlay is disabled, but it still freezes repeatedly.

It should, perhaps, be a vote of confidence for the game's quality/engagement that I have continued playing this game (and completed it) despite the aforementioned issue.\t\t\t\t\t
