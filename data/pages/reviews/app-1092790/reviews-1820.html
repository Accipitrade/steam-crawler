\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever did I think that a card game would be a contender for my top game this year yet here we are. Where do I start? I suppose I'll start with one of the most amazing things about this game; the atmosphere. It's breathtaking. Seeing dark eyes before you as you scheme behind a sadistic man's back in order to escape, going through a short rpg section with a tone similar to the darker parts of Undertale, to having everything you know about the game be mixed around with an entirely different aesthetic while still keeping that horror tone, this game oozes with character. Second, the creativity. This game mixes together genres perfectly in different and unique ways. You have to constantly puzzle over your cards, the room you're in, the characters you meet and even your own freakin' computer to solve it's many mysteries. Lastly, the gameplay is awesome. You can really see yourself improving and outsmarting your opponent is ever so satisfying. The rules change in slightly different ways to keep you on your toes constantly so you never get tired of the gameplay loop. If you're debating on this game it is 100% worth the $20.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis right here is a game you should play if you're interested in card games. At all.
Inscryption has a wonderful atmosphere that could only be experienced by going into it blindly.
If you like being rewarded for investigation and puzzle solving, there's also good amounts of this to be found all throughout the game.
If your interested in a narrative, not only does this game stand well on it's own, but it's heavily connected to the previous games made by this developer. This doesn't mean you need to play the previous games to understand what happens in this game, but if you're interested in the over-arching story, it may be a good idea to take a look at The Hex and Pony Island as well.
If your concern is that this game may become reptitive or boring, then a (At the time of writing this review) healthy modding community should help relieve that burden after your first playthrough... Maybe two. or ten.
This is a quality game that I cannot recommend enough.\t\t\t\t\t
