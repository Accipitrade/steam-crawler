On to the actual review...

There are no words in this green Earth at this current moment that could describe the exact experience of Inscryption. Having just recently beat the game (and I do intend to go back and look for more secrets), this is less of a game and more of an experience. The storytelling is superb, game play constantly evolves and changes in a way that does not impede the story's progression, rather helping the world around it flourish. Those changes- though minuscule- offer so much to the table that I genuinely felt fear, even though the game just reads some data from your computer and Steam friends list.

This is not just a game.
It's art.
Go buy it.

Inscryption awaits.\t\t\t\t\t
