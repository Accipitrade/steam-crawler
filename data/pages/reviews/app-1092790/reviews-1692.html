\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI pretty much just bought this game, after watching many people play it. Normally I don't care for deck builders like this, but seeing the tag "Rogue-like" my brain went off in a mess of wonder and excitement, and I started watching gameplay. I was hooked very quickly. This game is up there with (in my opinion) the best rogue-likes out there. Think Binding of Isaac, Gungeon, Risk of Rain. And despite the glaring differences between this game and the ones I've listed, I still really enjoy this game. Building your deck with unique cards with special attributes, called "Sigils", you can power up your weak cards and make them useful.

One thing I love about this game and the ones I've listed below, is that the game doesn't care if you're stupid busted. I had a run where I made a card, (yes, you can create custom unique cards, and even name them.) with 7 attack, 7 defense, and 1 cost. every time I drew that card, I knew that the round was in my hands. You'd think it gets boring, but trust me, the customization, endless ways to play, and fun escape room/lore makes this game worth many, many replays.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a tough one to review. I enjoyed it and had a fairly good time playing. If I had it to do over I would buy the game and play it again! Well worth my time.

That said, it is really short, far shorter than what I'm used to. The horror aspects are also a bit cliche and simple. At first it had my imagination racing in anticipation of where things were going but when I got there I was like "oh, more of this kinda thing... meh." As others have pointed out the first act of the game is fantastic but it drops off hard after that. If the whole game was just more of the first act and at least twice as long it would be almost perfect.

This is not Slay the Spire! This is story first and card game second as the card aspect is incredibly simplistic. There is basically zero replay value once you get through the story, although I do think there might be more coming in patches.

Again, I do think it is worth playing! I just wish there was more of it and more depth to what is there.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the most fun I've had in a game in a LONG time. It reminds me a lot of Hearthstone, but without the min-maxing meta that runs rampant in any strategic or competitive card game. You don't play against other players (although that would be fun), so you dont HAVE to stick to a meta. There's tons of different strategies you can employ and a lot of surprising effects you can mix together based on what you find.

A lot of people say they dont like the ending, but I ADORE the ending. I went through the entire game listening to the story but remaining relatively indifferent to it, all the way up until the end. There's a specific scene where I realized I suddenly cared about the characters and it stuck with me HARD. Stupid card game and its stupid characters that I care about. >:(

Well worth the price, especially with the mod that's in beta as I'm writing this. It takes the first Act (the one advertised and the one everyone seems to enjoy the most), and turns it into an endless mode with a higher difficulty, so even after you beat the game you can go back and play as much as you want.\t\t\t\t\t
