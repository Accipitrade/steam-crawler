There's a lot of times when I played around with a particular mechanic or card combination because it made sense conceptually, only to find that there was some easter egg or mechanic that would happen from it. I'm not massively into card games, (Hearthstone is really the only other card game I'd played) but this game really scratched that itch and kept me hooked throughout.
