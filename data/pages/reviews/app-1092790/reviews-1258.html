\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCome for the game play
Stick around for the story

Fun for those who enjoy deck building, roguelikes, and a bit of puzzle solving.

Cards & combos get overpowered relatively easily once you get the hang of it, but by the time you have a good hold on the game, the story is interesting enough to finish out the remaining hours the game has to offer.
It currently offers a beta with an endless mode with some challenges for anyone who still wants to continue the deck-building aspect post-game.

For those who aren't keen on the horror genre, but are still interested in picking this up - the game is more eerie than it is frightening. There aren't any cheap jump-scares or anything of that nature for typical horror games. Instead it uses horror as an accent to set the mood and tell a story.

Overall good experience
