\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game...hoo boy. Took me a bit longer to finish than most other people it seems, but I'm glad it did so I could spend more time playing it. This game blew me away. After having just finished it, I am 100% flabbergasted at how spectacular of a gem this game is. If you are interested in any kind of card game, I implore you to check this game out. The mechanics are deep, involved, and extremely fun. I hope there will be some kind of follow up. DLC, an update, a brand new game that shares the mechanics of this one, anything really. I'd even like to see them adapt this style to a competitive online card game somehow, it's just that good. Which is incredible, because the cards are only half of what makes this game amazing. Just goes to show how well-thought-out and robust this game was made. I never played the previous works of this dev, but I am certainly paying full attention to his future IP's. This is a masterstroke. Period.\t\t\t\t\t
