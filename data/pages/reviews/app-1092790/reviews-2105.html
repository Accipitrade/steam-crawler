\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game overall. I've been a huge fan of single-player card game video games since I was young, and this game does an absolutely fantastic job at delivering on card-based gameplay on that front. The game is divided into three acts, and although I enjoyed each part of the game in its own way, the first act (as many others are in agreement) is probably the strongest in terms of a balance of unique, challenging, yet fun gameplay. Every act of the game has a solid selection of cards you'll come to love as you build your deck and carve out your own style of play.

I personally felt the story aspects of the game were weak. Although I enjoyed the lore behind the in-game characters, I felt like the meta aspects of the game (especially the ARG) were weak and negatively impacted what could have otherwise been a very fascinating, mysterious, and even ominous story.

I'm excited to see if there will be any future updates or additional content added to the game. Every minute I spent playing this game was worth it, and I would gladly continue to play this game if more content is released.\t\t\t\t\t
