\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is probably one of my favorite titles of 2021, which is weird since I normally hate card games.

The game mechanics grow beautifully throughout the story and each Act does a great job of incorporating new play styles. You'll see in other reviews that the art style changes throughout the game, but it doesn't negatively affect the gameplay at all. The card game exists in a sort of escape room, so there are quite a few puzzles to solve in addition to the core deckbuilding. Really just need to give a hand to this Dev for putting together such a strong game and tying the surreal puzzle-solving of Pony Island into a very enthralling deckbuilding card game.

The only thing that's really missing is an Endless or Extended mode. There are rogue-like qualities you experience that would be great to explore longer than the game typically likes to allow - though it's definitely worth nothing that, as-is, after beating the game, you can absolutely go back and jump into any Act and replay it in it's entirety without having to necessarily replay the prior acts. But that being said, Act 1's gameplay would be fantastic if you could play in a mode that wasn't hard-locked to end after so many tables and Act 2's deck building mechanics were intriguing enough that I want to continue to go back and keep trying new builds.

Edit:

Well, they did it.

There is now a challenge mode, Kaycee's Mod, and it's fantastic. I don't know how, but I managed to sink in a few dozen of hours into it. It's really, really good.\t\t\t\t\t
