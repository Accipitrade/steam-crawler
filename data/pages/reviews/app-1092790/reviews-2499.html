\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is the best. It's the best. Do it right now. And maybe lose your mind in the process.

I think it's about pointless incentives. Or maybe it's about videogames being transitory art. Or about game development being a career path for control freaks.

Not sure. The game is surreal. It's going to take me a awhile to put together a meaning. But every fiber of my intuition is screaming that there's a good one, here.

Centrally, the game is framed as a card game. But definitionally, it's a puzzle game. Most of the puzzles you solve are resolving CCG scenarios. You're not trying to outsmart an opponent. You're trying to solve the puzzle the boss represents. You just play cards to do so.

And outside of that, there's a Myst-like point-and-click adventure that takes place every time you stand up from the card table.

Devolver Digital is now the polar opposite of Activision.\t\t\t\t\t
