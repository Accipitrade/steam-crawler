\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(SPOILERS) Beautifully crafted game, love the art style, sound design and I adore the morbid themes. My only gripe is the story went in a totally different direction than expected, which itself isn't a bad thing, but I can see where some might get turned off by the second act onward. Quite a few people I know played it up until then and the shift in direction made them quit playing as they just wanted more of the first act.

Although there is Kaycee's Mod to deliver on that, the story still remains as an ARG so unless you're into deciphering codes and digging through files the story kind of ends without much of a resolution and I find myself looking online for other people to explain all the secrets only a dedicated group would find. I personally would've preferred if the plot stuck to the first act in a contained story about the cabin and the mysterious man you're battling, not the overarching game within a game narrative where the antagonist in the first act isn't even the main villain - more like a stepping stone. Just a shame the first part shined so much the rest felt like a slog that I was obligated to beat cause otherwise I had no idea why I was even in the cabin.

So I still recommend it to anybody who loves card games and escape rooms. The first act alone is worth the money, I even listen to the OST sometimes while doing other things it's so atmospheric.\t\t\t\t\t
