\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you expected a proper card game then run, this card game is an absolute ♥♥♥♥ show.

The rules of the game itself goes against the player and it's really up to a luck of the draw on if you'll win.

The difficulty ranges from "He only put one card on the field for the past 3 turns" to "He just filled the entire board and killed all my cards". It's ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ insanity.

The biggest issue is the fact that every time your turn ends the enemy will move their cards up and attack your card, and your card doesn't attack back.
This is obviously a fundamental rule of the game I know, but this rule guarantees that the player will always be put at a disadvantage.

You're better off not putting cards on the field when you first start, let the enemy attack you (sometimes almost dying in one shot), then put your cards out so you can make some sort of strategy.

Clearly this game is rigged for you to die (and die A LOT) during certain points so it can drip feed you story/lore.

If I'm clearly supposed to die during certain parts then why am I trying to play in the first place? The game's rigged against me anyways, it becomes a matter of endurance.

Game is more a scripted story than a card game.

No thanks\t\t\t\t\t
