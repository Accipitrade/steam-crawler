\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA fun and spooky card battler! While some unexpected bits comes up in comparison to most card games, the card game itself keeps the same core rules throughout the game.

I will outright say; if we would get Inscryption side-games just focused on the card game and not the other lore, I would still buy them and be happy. Especially if it would be a rogue-like.

As for the horror, this is mostly without jump scares and gore, this is more about the unsettling atmosphere and the dark subject matters that are brought up.

Took me roughly 10 hours to beat, and I didn't feel like I were wasting any time. I highly recommend it for anyone which enjoys card battlers, and just anyone which like games with a less common type of story telling. While the card battling will take up most of your time, it is a very straight-forward game system that most should be able to pick up.

Update: Now with the official mod out, which is a challenge mode of the already fun roguelite, the game got far more playtime to go through. The beta of the mod had a built-in tool for reporting bugs and feedback, and all issues I had with the game and additions I felt required was in well before the official mod release.

At this point, majority of my play time have been within the mod, with all challenges completed, everything unlocked, and only thing left for me is to clear a run with a starter deck I never played with. This mod is worth the game's cost alone!\t\t\t\t\t
