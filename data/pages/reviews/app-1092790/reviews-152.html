\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou are here for the fancy bullet points so here, summary after.

+ *Amazing story behind this card game- I repeat, a CARD game where it's not your typical fantasy story snooze fest
+ *Immersive, listen closely, you will actually feel as if you ARE in the game. Literally. The atmosphere is great, you get this claustrophobic and unsettling feeling
+ *Top Notch Graphics, everything looks really nice and refined to fit it's deep immersion
+ Has simple to understand but deep inner workings of how the cards and their abilities/effects contribute to the board
+ Deck Building is SO ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ FUN HOLY MOLY, this game is BEGGING you to abuse it's mechanics and busted combos to your advantage. The game gives you several options on how to fine tune your deck to how you want

Okay okay, review. This game is a 10/10 example of how to show up one of the most popular deck building roguelike games out there, Slay the Spire. Inscryption has not as powerful deckbuilding elements as Slay the Spire but this wins over it from the listed aspects above with the * near it. I didn't elaborate much because this game is best played blindly. This game will throw you for a loop when you least expect it to, tickling your butthole when you think you stopped on the roller coster ride this game throws you on. This game progresses Story Wise rather than the whole game being one massive 'you die, you start over' which by the sounds of things, it would ruin the game. In reality, thats why this game surpasses others of it's genre. I can't reveal anything so let me say something else- you will find yourself equally or more invested in the immersion the game brings forth along with the story rather than the deck building itself.

Tldr, it has that really fun and strategic gameplay that Slay the Spire has but has the presentation and immersion that StS lacks and desperately needs while also having it's own amazing story line.

Should you get this game?

Yes, just Yes. It doesn't even matter if you are into deck building roguelikes (though if you have an interest in this, you will most definitely like this game.) If you tend to find the game to be too easy during the story, look up how to activate Kaycee's Mod AFTER beating the story. It's an addition inside the game that the Dev added himself with some hard balance changes, difficulty modifiers, new cards, and new starter decks. There is more but you can find that out.

Rare 10/10 game, easy W\t\t\t\t\t
