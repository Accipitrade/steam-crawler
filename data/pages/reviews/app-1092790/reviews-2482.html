\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHighly recommend this game, and any game by Daniel Mullins. If you know his previous work then you know what you're getting into. Puzzles, tons of secrets, incredible theme and art, creepy story, and really fun gameplay. This game will surprise you at every beat.

The roguelike aspect is a bit oversold. There is some replay value in finding secrets, but it's really more so a linear experience. Card battles are more so puzzles, not dueling. But there is still tons of deck building, customization, and a huge variety of experiences. It's very hard to talk about most of it without spoiling it.

Playing the demo really only exposes 1/5 of the actual game. It barely scratches the surface of the actual depth to the game. Game is probably 10ish hours if you rush, but could certainly be longer if you take your time and enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
