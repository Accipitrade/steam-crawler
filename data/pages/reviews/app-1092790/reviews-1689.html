\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor now, Id wait to see if the Kaycee's Mod for the First Act card game gets properly balanced or reworked (this will probably take some time). Otherwise, this game is the definition of wasted potential. After beating the first act, my smile and optimism was gone. Seriously I mean it, it gave me whiplash. Going from playing the game for 4 straight hours with a goofy smile on my face to full blown jaw dropped, shock and disappointment, couldn't carry on. Never has a game so fully grabbed my attention only to pants me when I least expected it too.

Theres a really neat card game in here filled with interesting mechanics and an intriguing atmosphere but Im afraid it just isnt fully realized. It may even take a full rework to become great, but theres something in there man, theres a diamond in that ruff.

The problem is even with an endless mode, youll never get that initial gripping feeling you originally got from first playing it. The mystery and escape room shenanigans combined with the creepy yet cozy vibe the cabin exudes is one of the greatest aspects of the game. To put it simply, an endless mode just wont satiate the player the same way it originally did. The ARG the game derails into isnt interesting or fun enough to play through compared to its first act and completely ruins the games atmosphere. At this time, it simply isnt worth the price for what I would consider to be the only part worth playing.

I will be keeping an eye on this game. I really hope it reaches its true potential some day.\t\t\t\t\t
