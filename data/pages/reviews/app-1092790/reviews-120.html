So I was looping the game for like the 20th time and felt stuck I tried everything looked everywhere, for example I killed the boss without killing the moon expecting something special to happen, I played the cards so that I had no cards on my side that I could hook the moon which didn't work and nothing happened.

Since I was at a loss and couldn't figure it out by myself I thought ok let's see a Guide what obviously thing did I miss, and it turns out I didn't miss anything the game just is too easy, let me explain.

Spoiler btw for those who care:
You get a dagger in the game and when you use it you poke out your eye sheesh, but you get an eye back after defeating a boss, so far so good, now the problem is I did that for like 7 runs, and apparently you should get a special eye that I never got, so no wonder I couldn't get the puzzle when the solution never got to me, if this is a bug or not I couldn't tell, but after reading some forum posts it seems that it's either RNG which would be pretty unlucky for 7trys or worse you need to die in game for it to appear, now why is this worse well because the game gets easier and easier not harder, so if you don't die early on which I did not, you probably never die. That being said, both options are pretty ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ game design wise, especially if you give the player a hint after completing the boss with a cutscene where you show the dagger just for the "special eye" not to show up. In any case, I refuse to die after I had to look up a guide just to advance the game, which is sad since after what I've seen the game changes or at least has more progression, but this put the wind out of my sails and I couldn't be bothered.
So all in all was fun in the beginning the style is pretty unique for a deck builder at least but way to EZ with ♥♥♥♥ game design, don't rely on death mechanics or RNG for puzzles, simple as.\t\t\t\t\t
