\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game because of the graphics and intrigue from trying to watch playthroughs (and falling asleep before anything meaningful happens). The first playthrough was great! I loved all the puzzles, the storyline, and also the sheer chaotic evil I felt while playing this game. It was decently challenging for someone that has never played card games before, but easy enough to learn and beat. No one seems to mention the twist in the second playthrough....


The art style changes and makes playing a whole different game. You can't choose your cards anymore, and you have to relearn the game. The art style isn't intriguing enough at this point to keep me interested so I'm afraid I won't be able to finish it, but I will try to keep trucking on. I feel like the developer is misleading people by only showing the first act of the game and NOTHING else. Please beware that there is Undertale like gameplay in here, and if that isn't for you then don't waste your money, just play the demo!\t\t\t\t\t
