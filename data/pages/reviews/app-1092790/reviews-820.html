\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is possibly the most difficult game I've wanted to review. I am not a card game or puzzle game enthusiast. This game looked interesting enough for me to branch out, so take that how you will. Let me start with the good aspects: almost everything. There isn't a single part of the game that doesn't look impressive, and the writing and cutscenes are stunning. The gameplay is interesting and the amount of cards and new game plus content will keep you playing for 10+ hours without feeling like it dragged on. However, the heavy rng of this game will make you feel cheated occasionally. Some runs would be fun, running smoothly, and then an enemy would send out nothing but the most difficult cards filling the board killing you in a few turns. If you have patience and a real drive to find new card combinations, then it shouldn't bother you too much, but I don't have enough interest in card game mechanics for it to feel worth it. As well, THIS IS A SPOILER, there is a "third act" of the game that feels very boring compared to the first two. The first act has lots of cards and events that could happen (as well as it being brand new to you), the second act has way more cards and multiple areas (as well as being slightly short), but the third act is long, empty, repetitive, and occasionally throws in cards that will just kill you (hence repetitive). I would say 60% of the game made me feel so excited and stunned with how good it is. The other 40% of the game felt boring and unsatisfying. I recommend though, though if you aren't a card game fan you might have to look up strategies or help (though get ready to feel like an idiot when everyone says this game is stupidly easy).\t\t\t\t\t
