\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust finished the game. Wow. Absolutely amazing. The card game itself is simple, but the interesting mechanics spread throughout the game give it more life. The bosses are just the right amount of challenging to engage you to keep trying but not completely turn you off, which is great. They all have different mechanics that give them more character and make them super interesting. There are some bosses I could play over and over again, given the chance. Also the story of the game is one of those "things are not what they seem" stories and I really love that. I think I need to go back for some lore bits I missed, but for the most part you get pretty much everything without having to look up walkthroughs about secrets and stuff. The secrets are not so hidden they're stupid, but hidden enough to where you feel accomplished if you find them. Overall, 10/10 game, everyone reading this review who hasn't already played it totally should.\t\t\t\t\t
