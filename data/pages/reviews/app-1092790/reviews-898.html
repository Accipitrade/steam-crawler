Edit: Finished the game after 9.2 hrs and I'm not great at card games. Main story was very fun to play through but I really wish there were a way to play the game outside of story mode. Edit 2: Turns out there's a mod (seems to be officially supported) that turns the best part of the game into an endless mode and that was the only thing the game needed to be 10/10.
