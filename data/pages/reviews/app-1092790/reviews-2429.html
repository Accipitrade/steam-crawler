\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSimply amazing.

Game starts off with great setting, mood and mechanics and then twists your brain multiple times with elements of ARG, RPG, Rouge like/lite(?).

Has a bit of slow/overwhelming moments, but you learn the mechanics quite quickly and it moves forward with story in an entertaining manner.

Lot's of attention to detail. It also creates a magnificent illusion of being a much larger game than it actually is, which is a pity but also shows how well designed it was from the get-go.

If you like horrors/card games/puzzles/plot twists/retro graphics and breaking 4th wall - this is definitely a title for you.

Approx 10-12 hours of solid entertainment.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou will notice that many players are referring to several acts for this game, the only one that realistically can be spoken about is Act 1, as to not spoil any of the experience for incoming players. My advice to you is to not get this game. With the only advertisement being the first part of the game, I feel heavily betrayed. I bought this game expecting gameplay similar to the advertisements. Instead I got a few hours of the first act and then several painful hours of the rest of the content. It becomes very easy to get through the first act as you play and eventually it just gets painfully tedious. The only reason I have such a long playtime of this game is because I am stubborn and want to finish out the achievements. I am not enjoying the game anymore, I stopped enjoying it about 4-5 hours in after getting increasingly frustrated. I stopped caring about the story at about 7-8 hours in and I genuinely just stopped understanding anything that was going on at about the same time. This is not a good game, and I do not like to be harsh with my reviews. If you go in believing this is a "roguelike deckbuilder" as advertised, you will be highly disappointed. I see all of the positive reviews and I am shocked. I believe that fans of the creators other games have slammed the review page. I don't believe this is a game for anyone, as I know I was disappointed. If you are having any doubts about this game, and only want to play it due to the gameplay, I genuinely recommend finding a different game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe first act of this game I thought was a 10/10 experience. Honestly felt like it was going to be on par with Slay the Spire in the roguelike card game world.

After the first act it felt like it was more focused on its Frog Fractions like gimmick than it was trying to actually be a well designed game. The second act felt way too easy and there was basically no consequence to losing any of the battles, you could just repeat them ad nauseam. I just started the third act and its feels like a less interesting version of the first act.

To be honest i feel like I was tricked into buying a different game than what is advertised, it would have been better if the game just stayed in the first act and expanded on its mechanics, number of cards, etc. Instead most of the games enjoyment is removed after the first act in favor of its story gimmick.\t\t\t\t\t
