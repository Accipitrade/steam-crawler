\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave not played Act 3 yet and not that deep into Act 2.

Having said this I enjoyed Act 1 a lot more. It was fun to learn the game mechanics and unravel the mystery while also naming the death cards the funniest names and making them completely broken to win turn one each game. It's so satisfying after a series of annoying losses.

Then Act 2 rolls around and it's a slight downgrade. The deck building feels pointless since auto-complete makes it so that the game plays itself. Trying to build your own deck makes the game WAY harder.

So far the game is not bad though. Not amazing, but pretty good. I really like the escape game elements and the creepy mystery, but in terms of gameplay it's pretty meh. Would still recommend if you like escape game puzzles and mystery stuff.

7/10
