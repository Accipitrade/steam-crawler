\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI keep coming back to this one. Its a card game consisting of three acts.

Act 1: This part has amazing atmosphere. A psychopath holds you prisoner in his log cabin and forces you to play his homebrew card game. It is somewhat like MtG or Hearthstone, but with deck-building and rogue elements. Your captor pulls out a map to use as a gameboard, and basically does a table-top roleplay of your character travelling through several places. You choose which places to go to, which net you different opportunities to adjust the cards in your deck in different ways. You are playing to survive, both figuratively and literally. If you enjoyed the deck-building elements and dealer from Hand of Fate, you'll especially enjoy this.

Act 2: The game transforms into a card RPG with 2D graphics in the vein of Pokemon TCG on gameboy, or Sacred Cards on gba. You are dumped into a card-themed world, and have to duel everyone using some of what you've learned in Act 1, but with some new mechanics added in. This is the most controversial part of the game, since it excises the atmosphere of Act 1. I ended up liking this part quite a lot, even if it is a pretty big departure from the previous act. I don't blame anyone who had trouble with this part, since being good at reading the board is more important than ever. I didn't have too much trouble thankfully - experimentation is key here.

Act 3: The game switches back to the Act 1 format with a different character - you are held prisoner in a technology themed place by someone who treats forgoes the atmospheric treatment of Act 1. Pretty good mechanically, and the atmosphere isn't that bad even if it doesn't reach the heights of Act 1.

Kaycee's Mod: An add-on was created for the game which has the player run through Act 1 with some optional handicaps. I beta tested this, and I have to say it's pretty terrible. Basically a russian roulette simulator. At best, it's just Act 1 over and over with few new experiences.

Custom mods: There are an awful lot of them on thunderstore. I haven't tried anything yet.\t\t\t\t\t
