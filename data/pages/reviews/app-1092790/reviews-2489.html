\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off spoilers although I tried to not be direct. Second if you like what you see on the store play the demo it has what you want. I can't recommend it anymore than that without feeling guilty.

So I have extremely mixed feelings on this game as for what is shown in the promotional photos and video you get what you're sold for a few hours. A card game and story with heavy themes around sacrifice and reward. The atmosphere and tone of this first act as I'm going to call it is astounding. The flavor it oozes is so nice be it sound effects to the somewhat weird encounters with the various "characters".

However once you finish this first act the quality of the atmosphere and characters plummet. The Second act felt like it should have been a precursor to all others as it has mechanics related to 4 major characters of which two get dedicated acts. The other two deck types are effectively wash as they only exist for the short time of you obtaining these new cards with new mechanics in what is a short actual deckbuilding act until it all goes away. Your collection is gone and your choices were meaningless.

Then the Final act feels hollow. There are no stakes losing only costs you time as you can easily just ram your face into it until rng favors you especially with some of these fights mechanics being just "haha random card stats and effects but they might seem familiar" It just takes jabs at it's own first act.

Then the story of the game, god why did it have to be some damn game within a game? I was so hooked until then and the plot lost all things that were interesting about it. It feels like it was phoned in because you didn't develop the last two major characters at all.
