\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a fantastic game, possibly the best of 2021.

Do yourself a favor if you're even half-way interested in this game. Read absolutely nothing else about it. The store page provides all of the information you need to know. It is an amazing experience I was not expecting this late in the year.

Buy it. Play it. It is a near perfect game.


Are they gone? Good. Don't reveal the black bars unless you genuinely do not care about spoilers. Please heed my advice though.

The gameplay in Part 2 (the 8-bit specialized deck builder part) is quite the downgrade from Part 1 in my opinion. There's none of that "win by the skin of your teeth" and the lack of items really mess with the flow. Maybe I missed something along the lines but the difficulty spike felt astronomical unless you found one of the (probably infinite) ways to create an insanely overpowered deck. Part 3 revitalized the gameplay for me but brought up a different issue. The difficulty spike was immense, which again directs you towards unorthodox solutions like crafting overpowered card(s) or using the Orobot (Oroboros worm from the previous parts). Looking back on it now that may have been the point, but it didn't feel too great gameplay wise for me. These are just nitpicks and frustrations however, it didn't really take much away from the overall experience. Which, repeating myself here, was phenomenal. \t\t\t\t\t
