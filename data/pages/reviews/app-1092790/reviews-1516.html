The game felt unfinished. After completing P03's section the last 2 sections just felt like the devs were like, yeah the hell with this its enough content and just had a ♥♥♥♥♥♥ way of ending the game. There honestly could have been twice as much content if we would have gotten a full Grimora campaign, and a Magnificus campaign. What made it even more of a slap in the face was the fact that you get to play 2 fights with Grimora and you can see by opening the rule book there is basically a whole rule book there for both grimora AND Magnificus. Why tease us so badly? even starting a fake boss fight I mean all the pieces were there, the 3 dead entities in act 2 could have made for an awesome set of bosses for a grimora campaign. and Magnificus, he had pupils in act 2 that could have made for bosses in a full campaign. I would have gladly spent 40 dollars on this game if all 4 Scrybes had a full campaign. I really really hope they go back and actually finish this game, have Grimora gain control, then magnificus, then have a last and final devil character take control, like a 5th unknown scrybe, now that would have felt complete. But to just end it after all that, end it like that? What a huge tease. I really hope either for a mod, or for some DLC to give this a proper ending.\t\t\t\t\t
