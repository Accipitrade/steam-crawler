\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: This game is disappointing.

I like the mechanics of the first act. The sacrifice mechanic coupled with being able to draw from two decks created interesting choices. I also really liked the creepy atmosphere. By the third time beating Leshy, I was starting to wonder if there was any more depth to the game, since it isn't really balanced. It is very easy to win with certain combinations. I was hoping that there would be something that would make the game more challenging or interesting to play.

Then the game got a complete makeover, and I was really excited at first. That excitement was quickly tempered by boredom as I slogged through the second act. The only reason I kept playing was that I was hoping it would get better again.

Unfortunately, the third act was just a worse version of the first act. Effectively a ♥♥♥♥♥♥ hearthstone clone. At that point, I was only playing to see how the story turned out. The game kept teasing that it would get better, and then... it was over.

I don't see myself replaying it. You might say, "but hoogamaphone, I can see that you have 16.5 hours of play time in this game. Isn't that worth the $20 that this game costs?" Normally, I'd say yes, but in this case, the fun was over a few hours into the game. Honestly, I think you will get more out of watching someone play this on Youtube than actually playing it yourself.\t\t\t\t\t
