\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to start off by saying that if I could give this game a yellow side-thumb or whatever then I would, unfortunately i cant so i have to say i don't recommend it. truth is: I do, but only the first third of it.

it starts off fantastic, that perfect bit of nervous horror combined with probably the only true rogue-lite style of gameplay in this so called rogue-lite game. It's great, the core gameplay loop there is fun and if the whole game was this I'd fully recommend it.

unfortunately the moment you complete act 1 the game just.... falls off a cliff in terms of quality, all the positives about the first act are completely removed and are replaced with frankly boring new mechanics that completely undermine the first five hours of the game. it becomes less a rogue-lite and more a card - story game.

That's not necessarily a bad thing, card story games can be great like Voice of Cards and to a lesser extent the Hand of fate games.

I also feel like they really shouldn't have gone (SPOILER) 2d with the second half, I never made it to the third so I won't speak on it but i've heard it's not that great either however take that with a grain of salt.

In the end sadly I can only recommend the first act of this game, that's truly special in gameplay and unique horror style elements. The rest simply is not.\t\t\t\t\t
