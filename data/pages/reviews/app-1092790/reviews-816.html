\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am bad at puzzle game, and I REALLY really dislike card game. I always thought all card games eventually boil down to doing math and become too intricate to be enjoyable for a simple-minded person like me. This game, is the EXACT opposite of that.

The gameplay flow is fast and fluid, and its system extremely easy to understand but also full of depth. The game keeps adding new mechanics on the board but you are never overwhelmed with them. No to mention the horrific atmosphere, and the very intriguing and ever evolving ways of storytelling keep me hooked all the time (except the midpoint of the game which is a slight miss but it was over very quickly).

So please, play this game, preferable blind. Don't worry for missing out something during your first gameplay, because all you could miss is just some story titbits and you would probably Google the explanation of whole story after the ending anyway. I am still getting ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ by the ending right now.\t\t\t\t\t
