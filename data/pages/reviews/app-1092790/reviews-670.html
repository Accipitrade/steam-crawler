Inscryption is more about the story and the atmosphere, but the card game itself isn't too bad either. Definitely more compelling than the gameplay in Pony Island (another great game from the same developer).

However, I do think that more could have been done with certain elements of the game. The first part was over way too quickly in my opinion.

Overall the game is a decent length, about 15 hours for the average player probably.

Definitely worth a look if you like weird games.\t\t\t\t\t
