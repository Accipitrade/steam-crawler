\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of my top games of all time. I came to this with almost no card game experience, but this game makes it super easy to get comfortable with the playing style and you get invested in no time. The card game portion allows you to experiment with different styles which all feel rewarding. The side puzzles are a perfect level of challenging where they require some brainpower without being frustrating, and there's huge incentives to continue figuring them out and discovering interesting lore and useful gameplay mechanics for the card game portions.

The base game is worth playing for the engrossing story which took me about 10 hours to complete and after that the free beta DLC mod (Kaycee's mod) is the way to go for a more challenging and balanced card game experience after the base game has been completed. Since I'm about halfway through the levels on the mod, this game definitely has enough hours and enjoyment to justify its $20 price tag. Would rate a 9/10, only detracting because a certain section of the game was less enjoyable (although still with its high points) and felt a bit dragged out.\t\t\t\t\t
