If you enjoy good atmosphere, dark Americana with monsters, cryptids and weird frontiersmen, meta-narratives, deceptively simple card games or all of the above, this may be right up your alley. The main game may be somewhat easy and it takes quite a few narrative and gameplay twists and turns after the first act, but it's great overall. The newly Kaycee's mod which is a standalone roguelite ascent with it's own unlocks and narrative secrets serves as an excellent companion for the main story and game, with a decent if unbalanced challenge (due to being in beta still, and not without bugs).

Leshy will always be one hell of a host.\t\t\t\t\t
