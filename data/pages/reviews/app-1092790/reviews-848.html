\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolute masterpiece of a game. Daniel Mullins back at it again, subverting our expectations in the best way possible. From the beginning of the base game, to the ending of Kaycee's Mod (the free expansion available after beating the main game) everything is just perfection.

Do yourself a favour and play this game without looking up anything. This is one of those games that is so much better if you don't know anything about it. Honestly you should stop reading reviews and go finish the game right now, if you haven't already.

It can get difficult at time, sometimes crushingly so, but if you fail, try again and again, you'll get it eventually. Learning is part of the experience and what an experience it is. Sound, art, game-play everything is top-notch.

I can't wait for Daniel's next creation!\t\t\t\t\t
