\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic game! I initially got because it looked like a creepy Slay the Spire, and it scratched that itch and more. You face off against a serial killer who won't kill you as long as you keep playing his game in a deck building/ escape the room style game play. Go in as blind as possible.

EDIT: They added an arcade mode called Kaycee's mod which is everything I wanted and more

PROS:
-Fun game-play loop
-Hidden things in cabin can alter the game flow and make permanent changes to future runs
-lots of stuff to discover
-deep lore
-Atmospheric horror, 0 Jump-scares
-Solid villain
-Many surprises
-High replayability
-Good modding community
-There is a little bit of fishing

Cons
My con's for this game mostly have to do with achievements and are negligible to most people. If you are an achievement hunter, beat the game normally then look up a guide.
-Most achievements are hidden and many are miss-able, I had to look up a guide to get the last few and I would have never stumbled onto them otherwise.
-One achievement I had to map a left click to a controller then leave my headset on the button and leave the game running for about half an hour, no guides suggest a more sensible way.


My level of experience with this game:
I beat the game with and without using exploits, and got 100% of the achievements. (had to beat it twice to grab some achievements I missed and used exploits to speed up some sections. It does not take the time I have on file to beat it twice, I often leave it running while working)

Edit: When the beta of Kaycee's Mod came out, I played it for hours and even reported a couple of bugs that later got fixes\t\t\t\t\t
