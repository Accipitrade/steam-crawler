There were about 4 segments of game with only a few of them being good. The first part was great and I loved it, there isn't anything I could think of that would make part 1 better. Part 2 sucked because it turned it into some lame 90's RPGMaker type thing that was supposed to provide this backstory about it all but it was very unimpressive and not fun. The third segment was very good as well, and the boss fights for the third segment were the best. Absolutely loved the mechanics and quirks of the boss fights for the third segment. The fourth segment was rushed to finish. Ideally you would have had every character with their own game with an entire segment for each dedicated to it. The nail in the coffin for this game was the Luke Carder subplot. It reminded me I was playing a game, and a bad part of it. It would have been far more intuitive to leave out that part and instead have YOU as the player and not this Luke guy. The computer could have just as easily read your computer's user name and use that in place of Luke Carder. At the very least, Luke Carder would have been a separate entity from you the player. Overall, good game with amazing premise but ruined with a false protagonist.\t\t\t\t\t
