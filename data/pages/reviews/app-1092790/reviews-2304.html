The game is good, great even, although the game falls off a bit after chapter one, the whole game and story kept me engaged all the way through and the secret hunting was fun.
The community is amazing and I really hope Daniel Mullin takes this game further.
Overall rating 8/10

Pros
This game has an outstanding art style and atmosphere, tying in with the eerie sound design and framing leading to a captivating experience.
I feel like the game has a perfect time frame for completion, somewhere between 10-14 hours if you play casually and all the meanwhile dotting story events around and alluding to a deeper story (including the short ARG that took place) and left me on the edge of my seat wanting more to do with the universe.

Cons
As many other people will attest, the first chapter is the best chapter, by far.
The mystique, atmosphere, sound, visuals and lack of story knowledge all come together to set the bar SO high in anticipation for what will come next, and truthfully? its a little bit of a let down.
I found that a majority of my play time was in the first chapter (about 6 hours), but within the next 6 hours I had gone through acts 2,3 and the finale.
The game kind of shot itself in the foot by setting the bar so high within the first act and then changing shift so quickly to a different style of game, the game and the feeling are still there but the gameplay and visuals took a hit.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is easily one of the best games I've played in quite a while.

The card game aspect of it is phenomenal. Its simple, clear and a heck of a lot of fun. The puzzles are mostly great, only one or two were confusing or frustrating and that really didn't detract from the game in any meaningful way.

The story is wonderful, not exactly a new idea, but its executed perfectly. The characters are, while fairly minor, pretty good. I found myself really liking all of them and wishing I could spend more time with them.

My only minor complaint is simply that there's no way to play this online or against the characters in a more friendly, casual way, like a post-game endless mode where you just see how far you can get or whatever. And frankly, this is more a compliment than an actual complaint because the card game is just so good I wish I could play more of it.

Overall, I'd say check this out. I love card games but I'm not much of a horror or puzzle game fan and still had a hell of a time with this. Its really well worth the asking price, if not more.\t\t\t\t\t
