I only wish there was an easier way to get your custom cards back in the beginning after a new run starts. I've only seen the same one repeated while playing the first part even though I've created quite a few.

The way the game progresses is pretty jarring, but each part or "act" of the game has it's own interesting flavor and charm. The less you look up before you play, the better. I'd also say this game is more creepy than scary or horror, so big babies are welcome to play lol. I'd give it a 3 (maaaaaybe 4)/10 on the scary scale if that helps anyone make up their mind about buying.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty good game overall, I'd say the best game of 2021 for sure. The mechanics and lore are both great throughout the game. Here is the review for each act (spoiler warning):

Act 1: Act one is probably my favorite. I love the atmosphere it builds and all of the mechanics. I also love the escape room/puzzle aspect of Leshy's cabin. Everything about it is awesome.

Act 2: This act was still good but not my favorite. I liked how you could build a mega deck, and the style change from act 1. Overall though, I think it could have been slightly more difficult, because each boss fight only took one try.

Act 3: Botopia is similar to Leshy's Cabin (act 1) with the gameplay mechanics and card system, but it has the same map as act 2, just bot themed. I like how it introduces the new energy mechanic and the runes. I also like how it continues the escape room aspect of act 1. This was probably my least favorite act to play in the middle because of how it can be confusing to find where you are trying to go, but the ending totally redeemed it. The Golly fight was great, and the Anarchist fight was pretty good.

Ending: I love the ending and all of the story it builds with the video tapes being shown throughout of Luke Carder, the player, discovering the game and playing it. I think it ties loose ends together very nicely, and I appreciated how Grimora, Magnificus, and Leshy all seem to be happy(-ish?) Leshy even compliments your skill.

So yeah overall I think this is a great game that is totally worth getting.\t\t\t\t\t
