full review:
like i said above act 1 was easily the strongest part of the game and arguably the best part of the whole game. the setting the creepyness of it all and the use of escape room elements where all fantastic
and then you get to act 2 and honestly you feel like someone just popped your balloon. thats not to say its bad but going from act 1 in to act 2 is underwhelming, as for the act it self, story wise its fun, the addition of 3 new deck types and mechanics where very nice the act of getting packs of cards after battles made me feel like i was playing the pokemon trading card game on the gameboy again it wasa good act but lackluster compared to act 2
on to act 3
we pick it up again back to the card game escape room combo. new deck system and mechanics fantastic. personally i thought po3's part was a lot of fun, the bounty system the items. the escape room stuff felt a little lacking but was still fun exploring and finding a couple things

kacees mod: honestly its worth getting the game just for this mod. if i could change anything it would be to of added more to the escape room aspect of it as its basically pointless for this mod.
i would love to see a kacees mod for act 2 and act 3 in the future especially act 2 i think there is a lot of untapped potetiol there\t\t\t\t\t
