It's a roguelike (lite?) deckbuilder set in a world with rich lore, puzzles to solve, secrets to find, and characters to interact with. Every single facet of this game is interesting, challenging, and fun. The soundtrack is awesome. It takes the typical deckbuilder experience and goes wild with it.

If you're turned off by the horror element, let me assure you it's much more a horror atmosphere than anything else. Aside from some lightly portrayed self-mutilation required for mechanics (just look away from the screen, that's what I did), there aren't any jump scares and the horror element is definitely in the "spooky atmosphere" category rather than going to outright terrify you. (I hate horror games but LOVED this.)\t\t\t\t\t
