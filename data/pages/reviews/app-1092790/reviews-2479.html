\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, one of the better indie card games I've played in a while. A very solid 7.5/10. A definite purchase on discount if you were ever curious about the game from videos. If you're a fan of other card games Gwent/Hearthstone/Magic/Yu-Gi-Oh, then rest assured as to the creator of the game grabs qualities from those games as well.

The issue for the missing 2.5 was the I thought the plot with the card collector was a bit unresolved with the game itself. Either I missed something, or I just wasn't in depth with the lore as much but long story short,
there were 4 scrypts that came with the earth to introduce cards to the universe(I'm assuming pertaining to the floppy disk) and after a series of beating the scrypts and replacing one, you start the process of deleting the game, and under the game is this OLD_DATA file that has been referenced by the scrypts time from time playing. However, at the end, you get to witness the whats within that OLD_DATA file which does not really explain much other than the fact that it has to do something with the devil(pertaining to the dates).

I don't know, I honestly hoped for a more rounded story and connection between the game itself and the card collector but that's just my opinion.

Overall the game-play is very smooth and very intuitive after an hour of playing or so.

Definitely one of the more
meta game plays that breaks the 4th wall alot. Would recommend for people who first and foremost enjoy RTS card battles.\t\t\t\t\t
