\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, strange, weird, but what horror?

This is a cool Magic the Gathering-lite game. Nice eerie atmosphere and a story that is served to you in small chunks. Leaves a lot to your own interpretation and imagination. However there is NO horror element at all. Not unless you are creeped out by a pair of eyes looking at you....
Anyway, the card games are ok, but not really complex. You can always retry if you fail. The later stages become a bit more difficult, but you can still easily beat the game without having to beef up your cards or get the 'best' ones.
Only real downside I found is the difference in style between all the acts. No spoilers, but I enjoyed the first act and artwork, but the other acts I found disappointing in art, style and pace (they became a bit of a bore/chore).
Depends on what you find worth for the price, but looking at it in enjoyment 'per hour', I found it a bit expensive. And I played the game leisurely. You could finish it in under 5 hours.\t\t\t\t\t
