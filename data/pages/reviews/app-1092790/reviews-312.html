\r\n\t\t\t\t\tthis game is an exceedingly fun experience dripping with style and depth. the visuals are top notch all the way through, the gameplay is simple and flexible while still being unique and deep, and the sound design is absurdly atmospheric and bassy. The story is interesting, with plenty of secrets to uncover and puzzles to unwind. i love the way the game plays. the lane based combat and simple abilities prevent the game from being confusing, while the infinite possibilities when it comes to deck building allow you to be creative and powerful with whichever playstyle you want to use. I like the way the various economy systems work, as for the most part, they create multiple unique ways to build your deck and pose an interesting tactical challenge when it comes to finding good ways to manage your economy.

unfortunately, the game has some flaws. the later stages of the game fall off a little bit, and there isn't much replay value. for those who like a challenge, there is an endless mode, but the lack of unique enemies and situations means that, eventually, you'll get bored of that too. you either can't stand the difficulty, or eventually beat all of the challenge modifiers and run out of things to do again. I personally enjoyed it, and if you enjoy deckbuilders and roguelikes this is definitely for you, but if you don't like those kinds of games then you may find yourself becoming bored.

TL;DR: Incredible presentation, and gameplay that perfectly strikes the balance between easy to grasp and incredibly fun, with an intriguing story, but you may become bored by low replay value. 8/10, would recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
