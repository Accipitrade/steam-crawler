\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is insane. 9/10.

I would avoid reading or watching too much about it as it's one of those so original that spoilers are worse than for your average game (or movie).

What you need to know: it's a card game (lots of MTG and Hearthstone influence), but also a puzzler. And it's storydriven. There you go, sounds interesting? Dive in, just trust me and go play it. Stop reading now.

Why did I not give it a 10/10 if I'm so passionate about it? Well, the story is a bit confusing and not as great as it could be. That's when compared to a great movie or some incredible game story like The Last of Us or To The Moon. For a game, it's above average for sure. The awesome part about the narrative is HOW it's told, though, despite the story itself not being one of the greatest stories ever told in human history. That's why I didn't give it a 10, it could have gone deeper. It also reminds me a lot of The Beginner's Guide, although the latter is way more philosophical, goes deeper into questioning what makes a game and who are it's characters.

Overall, a masterpiece and I wish there was more. Anxiously awaiting the dev's next games.\t\t\t\t\t
