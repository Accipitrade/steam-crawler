Lack of difficulty
Predictable twists
No risk of failure

This game lacks the joy of overcoming a challenge

Also, very stylish and sound is honestly quite unsettling at times.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a great game for sure, but if you're buying it for the roguelite aspects you'll be dissapointed after beating the final boss. You won't be getting hours of replayability, despite there being so much buildup with puzzles and unlockable cards. The experience is mainly centred around story telling. The roguelite aspects do continue past that point, but there's no long term goal to strive towards once you finish the story. You also don't keep any progress on any of the Acts once you finish the game (yeah there are like 3 different games inside this really).

Buy it if you're looking for a good and refined horror/strategy card game experience, but I'll suggest staying away from this title if you were looking for something with hours of content and replayability such as Slay the Spire or BoI.\t\t\t\t\t
