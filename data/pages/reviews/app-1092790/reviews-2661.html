\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you want to buy this because you played the demo and wanted more, walk away now. I won't spoil it for you, but the game takes a sharp left into "oh we're one of those games now" territory, which is purposefully omitted from the trailers, screenshots and demo.

I'm going to recommend the game for the asking price, but I walk away from it deeply wishing the game had just stuck with the initial few hours and added the extra rule sets it introduces in the 2nd act, to spice up the rougelike aspect. Or at least had that be added as a reward for seeing it through to it's conclusion, for those who enjoyed the core gameplay loop.
As it stands, this is a game you can't really experience twice, which is probably what they were going for - an experience wrapped in a game, rather than a game you experience.

Not my cup of tea, yet I binged the whole game in one sitting and felt strongly enough about it to write this...So you win this round dev's.\t\t\t\t\t
