\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game solely because Justin McElroy and Brian David Gilbert said it was good - the former in a tweet I happened to see, the latter in a video where he made cookies. And then it was on sale, and I was like, well, I have sixteen dollars, let's go.

First, the important thing: this game does have horror elements and is definitely creepy at times, but there are no jump scares (at least, so far. I just finished Act II). Secondly, this game is GOOD. One of the best I've played, ever? It's a strategy game, it's a puzzle game, it's an escape room game. It's a deckbuilder, often. It's a roguelite, kinda. It's a mindf*ck consistently. It has a bunch of elements that should not go together but somehow work together perfectly.

I'm very glad I went into this blind, and encourage you to, as well. Trying to figure out what's going on makes the game really engaging. Shoutout to the internet for not spoiling anything when I tried to Google why I couldn't leave the dark room with the candle once I "beat the game". (I didn't beat the game.)

10/10, play this immediately.\t\t\t\t\t
