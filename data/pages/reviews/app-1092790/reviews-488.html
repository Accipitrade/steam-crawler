\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm sure I'm not alone when I say that I enjoyed the first Act of the game the most (this is the part seen in trailers). The music, the atmosphere, the way the player interacts with the environment beyond the card table for the first time.... it's just magical. While I did enjoy the other Acts, Act 1 was the only time where I felt entirely immersed in the world of Inscryption - something I think the creators agreed with, considering the existence of Kaycee's mod.

I think the devs have created the best card game ruleset I've ever seen, and I love this game for introducing me to it. It probably wouldn't work competitively, of course, but that's not really the point.

Inscryption is a narrative, gameplay and auditory masterpiece, and I urge anyone even marginally interested in card games, immersion or stories to give this game a try. It's a thing of beauty.\t\t\t\t\t
