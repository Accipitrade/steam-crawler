It also has some puzzle elements in the meta-game. I'm not really big on collectible card games, so it's not something I would go back and play repeatedly, but I recognize that it is a good game nonetheless. I found the first chapter to be the hardest, longest, and most unforgiving. The later chapters were much easier and over quicker. There are also a good number of hidden things to find if you care to look, which can give you boosts in your card battles.
Note that there are some videos in the game that contain strong profanity, which I don't care for. Otherwise it's a cool game.\t\t\t\t\t
