\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I do recommend this game, it leaves me wanting more game play wise. I played the demo and found it to be an amazing experience. Even throughout the game itself I felt wowed the whole time, but having finished it I think back and see things I wish were there. While the game itself isn't super short, I feel it isn't long enough either. It was rewarding yet also hurtful to get good cards that would expedite the battles that used to be tough, that became a joke. Certain mechanics of the game, in the card battle area, need more explanation. There is only so many times a person can read a card before giving up ,and then being punished for not understanding it if it needs to be used. While I do have these complaints I love the game. I feel the story was very fluid and mysterious letting players connect the dots as they progressed. Seeing the game itself change form into something new to keep it from going stale. The abundance of card types to give many different ways to play the game, even if some need more explaining. The puzzles were also very unique, with an array of different problems to be solved by wits and game play it kept me intrigued. As I've said the game it great, it just needs to be given a bit more. Something like this felt like it could've taken 20 or 30 hours instead of 10. I hope whomever reads this finds it useful and encourages them to buy the game.\t\t\t\t\t
