This game is fun and you should buy it. More importantly, and I feel like most gamers understand this sentiment at this point: If you own this game, please dig it out of your library, and play it.

Every time I sit down to put an hour or so into this game, I'm left salivating for my next opportunity to sit down with it. At its core, beyond all of the uncomfortable spooky stuff this game has to offer (and it offers a lot), the card game mechanics are solid, and I'm legitimately surprised at how good they are, when the horror aspect of this game had such a clear emphasis. If this game was literally just a card game, it would be marketable and fun to play. But then they made me actually care about the puzzles surrounding it, and added multiple layers on top of what was going on in the game. Bravo.\t\t\t\t\t
