My game of the year.

First act is a masterpiece of atmosphere that I wish had more depth.

Update: Kaycee's mod is an excellent idea that unfortunately suffers extreme balancing issues in it's current state. If you fail on the first challenge run, there is no reward for making it far. This leaves no way to upgrade your deck outside of the run until you get lucky and beat a run using the weak starter deck. The grain of salt is that the mod is in it's early stages so this will likely change when the developer fine tunes it. Also, big kudos to listening to the community and attempting to add more depth to a stellar game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you love other RNG card games like slay the spire this will ring pretty strong with you. Interesting subtle story, great visuals, great audio adds to a good immersive experience. The fights almost always seem fair, and after a few deaths you really get the hang of things and tactics really come into play.

Have to say the character designs really pulled me in here, and the audio also is satisfying. I have 5 hours into it now, no doubt it will increase.


Edit:
after some time there has been a drastic shift in game play and theme. The game changes from the initial roguelike deck builder and takes a different turn. I cant much talk about the rest of the game because it's become far more grindy and less rewarding that the first section. I still reccomend it but my initial enthusiasm for the game is pretty much extinguished.\t\t\t\t\t
