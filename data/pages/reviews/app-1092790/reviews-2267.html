\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever written a review before but this game demands something to be said about it...

What I first thought was a simple card rouguelike quickly became one of my most favorite games ever in a matter of hours. Not only is the game-play fresh and exciting and the world building and plot was completely unique. If someone were to ask me if they should try Inscryption, I'd say that you are missing out on one of the best games in the industry if you choose to pass it up, especially considering the quality we are settling for nowadays with Triple A releases. So much so that I even went back to 100% the game's achievements. As much as I am content with, and wholly appreciate the game for what it is, I would die for some additional content for this game or something new from the devs entirely.

You'd miss out not buying the game

10/10 IT IS A MUST BUY
