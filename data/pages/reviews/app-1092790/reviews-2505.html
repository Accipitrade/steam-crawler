If you do decide to buy this game, don't bother winning until you finish the escape room puzzle, you get zip for doing so. You actually still just lose.

First two runs are auto losses. You can win on the third one.

The idea of getting to grow creatures as you lose and 'die', the idea of souls trapped in cards? Amazing premise. Someone go make that game. That'll be fun.

This game? Don't bother. It wastes it's clever premise. It stops being fun when you realize the game will start to cheat in the card game section to make you win. There's a few very easy seams that are rng and once they exist you've won the game.

Brilliant idea. So close to perfection. Practically shovelware in execution.

Dev, what were you thinking? You had such a good idea. To throw it away? God. I wish I could return this.\t\t\t\t\t
