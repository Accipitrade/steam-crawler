\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm going to go ahead and recommend this game but only for the first part. Once you have done that, just assume you've won the game and move on. I slogged my way through the 2nd and 3rd act and hated myself for doing it. Even after reading the negative reviews I figured it couldn't be that bad because of how awesome the first act is. Seriously, it feels like 3 different complete game teams designed this game.
The first act is sublime. Absolutely dripping with atmosphere and fantastic mood. The game-play shines here and everything about it just screams for you to play more. Then... unfortunately you beat that and act 2 starts and you go... WTF? It's a complete different atmosphere and really distracts from what the game should have continued to be. Honestly, it feels like the developer made this 2nd act first as one of their first games they ever made and decided to toss it in to this game "full" game to give the player more time to play.
I would have been completely happy if only the first act were the game and it just ended there. I know there is a mod that allows this but it probably should have just been that way from the start.
Either way, I wouldn't pay more than $7-$8 bucks for this game because it is really only the first act that has any enjoyment to it. Anyone saying the latter 2 acts live up to the 1st are just fooling themselves to try and make the time they played worth it. Trust me, don't bother after the 1st act and you'll be happier for it.\t\t\t\t\t
