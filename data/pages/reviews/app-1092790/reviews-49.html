\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe best parts of this game are the secrets and ways to interact with the world. When the first act ended and I started playing the classic rpg style game I was super impressed. I really enjoy all the things you can find and the ways that you are able to really stack the game in your favor. The game has a lot of brilliant moments where it messes with you and makes it feel super unfair, until you find ways to bend the rules and overcome it. There are also plenty of interesting mechanics that are explored in act 2 and 3, and some that are just introduced in boss fights and never seen again.

Also the sound and visual design is amazing, every item shuffles and clicks and taps in such a satisfying way.

I liked this game a lot, but I don't want to play it over and over again (which I can do thanks to the Kaycee's Mod addition). My biggest issue is that I felt like the first act overstayed its welcome. I think there's a lot of cool depth to the card game, but I am not a huge card game fan. I enjoyed Act 1 for a while, but I was very relieved at the mixup in Act 2 and 3 and that is what allowed me to keep going and finish the game.\t\t\t\t\t
