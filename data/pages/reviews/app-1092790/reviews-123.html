Garage Sale Card Game - Inscription
I expected a half baked card game with a well crafted horror atmosphere and breadcrumb trail puzzles all on top an interesting story concept.

I was pleasantly surprised when the card game has some real depth to it. There is synergy, strategy, and the introduction of different mechanics to keep things fresh enough that you can shake things up. The horror is also presented differently to your typical horror themed game, instead using the the overall consistent style and sound to present an unsettling but gripping environment without resorting to cheap jump scares and LOUD NOISE.

Though I had a great time and had moments of strategic elation, and made broken combos, and cards.
I am not sure when I would replay this. I guess the downfall of a story that pulls you in offers a rabbit hole of lore and intrigue is it will take me a while to forget.

For now this can chill rent free in my head for a while and I will recommend this to some of my tcg friends as I think there is a solid card game here, AND a great game as well.\t\t\t\t\t
