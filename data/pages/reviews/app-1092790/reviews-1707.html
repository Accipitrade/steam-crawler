\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSpoiler free:
I feel like this is a game everyone should at least try. I have my reservations about it but there is a lot to love here. I recommend you go in blind. You will be playing a deck builder for pretty much the whole game. There is more that meets the eye here but be prepared for that if deckbuilding is not your thing. See my light spoiler review for elaboration on these things. I wont spoil what happens more so the context in which things happen if you are on the fence and want some more info.

Light spoiler review:
Ok now for the spoilers. This game is broken into a few sections, each being different. I won't say what causes this or how you get there, but know that the promotional images for the game are only a small part of the experience. I did not at all care for the second section of the game. The first is immaculate, which makes this all the more disappointing. The game gets kind of up its own ass about its lore and kind of throws away what makes the first part good. There is less focused game design, less fun card play, and worse atmosphere. I am only slightly tolerant of deckbuilders so this was more frustrating for myself than it is likely to be for fans of the genre. But I was so offput that I uninstalled. Part 2 of the game also kind of stunts you from the beginning. It gives you a choice on what core deck to build for the rest of the section, you get more cards, but some of the starter decks are blatantly worse than others. Whole thing just left kind of a bad taste in my mouth even though I initially loved it.

Conclusion:
Definitely try if you like deck builders, be cautious if you aren't. 6/10 personally.\t\t\t\t\t
