\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am not a fan of card games but Inscryption somehow manages to make it fun for me. All of the cards are so simple, they're based on real animals which you can imagine not some crazy zoo of new animals created for the game and they are given a simple stat system to reflect what you imagine them.

That's the ace this game has. Only after you've learned these simple surface-level mechanics, does you're mind starts racing about all the cool stuff that can work together to create some overpowered monstrosity of a deck. Its all tied together with an escape room type setting about trying to escape from a card game a serial killer is making you play. There is such a cool style to it all.

All of this is only the first act. The rest is great in its own right but I hope this was enough to convince you to give it a try even if you don't like card games, like me.\t\t\t\t\t
