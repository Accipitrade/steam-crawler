\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI beat it because I was hoping it would get better.

This game had insanely high potential. Act 1 was an actual modern masterpiece.

Then, (honestly I truly believe the following needs to be disclosed to buyers before purchase, but I will put a spoiler tag because I did not know when I started) Act 2 DRASTICALLY changes the game design, mechanics, and the art style. Eventually, Act 3 returns you to a slight semblance of the glory of Act 1, but it has annoying mechanics like powerful cards are limited to being played on later rounds, because on round 1 you can only play 1 cost cards, round 2, 2 cost, etc... and that leads for extremely tired and repetitive battles.

Once the game introduced The Scrybes, I was so excited and thought we would get 3 more acts very much like Act 1. Instead we got something so self-obsessed that it was truly a let down.

Also the human actor guy is cringey as ♥♥♥♥.

Anyways... If what you see in the trailer excites you... maybe wait for an update becuase the game is only partly THAT. If that intrigues you to no end... Well maybe this review is just reverse psychology and now you can't help but want to buy the game. Who knows?

I don't.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is by far my favorite game of all time. I would happily pay $40 for a game of this quality and length! To break it down without spoilers, here's my review:

Pros:
- Amazing sound design. Each sound is incredibly unique and smooth (especially character "voices", creating an immersive game. Headphones highly recommended, but it's great without too
- Smooth gameplay with great graphic design and low computer requirement. It isn't often that you find a semi pixelated game with such smooth and easy movements.
- Great character designs! Each character has a unique personality and appearance, and the animated movement of them looks great too. Their little snippets add so much depth.
- Unique gameplay design. I'm not usually one for digital card games, but this blew me away. As you learn from the tutorial, the cards each feel pain, leaving you with moral decisions for each card you play. The additional aspect of moving around and investigating your environment is fun too!
- Unique rule set. This is a card game that allows and even encourages a little bit of cheating and rule bending on both sides! Despite this, once you learn the mechanics, it becomes perfectly clear how the game works.
- Death doesn't ruin the game. It doesn't become repetitive and annoying to die, and it doesn't set your progress way back. Dying is a part of the game, which makes it much easier to accept the loss.
- Endless combinations. Every single card game you play has the potential to be unique depending on the cards and sigils you choose and infuse into cards, as well as totems. This makes the endless onslaught of card games always fun and exciting, even before you make your strategy
- Multiple strategies to win! This isn't a game where you have to look online to discover the exact strategy to beat a hard boss. You can experiment with the cards and use different strategies each time depending on what you have!
- Intuitive gameplay. Some parts may leave you confused, but there are always hints about what you need to do to progress in the game. They're actually pretty obvious at times.
- Not every puzzle and secret is necessary to win. There are a lot that you must find, but certain things just make gameplay easier.
- Constant saves. The game is constantly saving for you! You never have to worry about losing your progress because the game has you covered. After each encounter, it autosaves!
- Amazing scares!! This game does NOT rely on jumpscares to remain terrifying. The premise itself is spooky, characters look creepy, sound design and music is chilling, everything is just really unsettling and weird. Some items you can use might have you closing your eyes!

Cons:
- If super long games or card games aren't your type, this game isn't for you. Just know what you're getting into
- There are some battles that are just impossible to win. Sometimes you didn't get the stuff to beat them, sometimes it's meant to happen. It can be frustrating, but there's always another chance.

I could honestly go on about this game for hours, but I'll leave it at this; PLAY IT!! Seriously, you won't regret it, this is the best gaming experience I have ever had, and I hope it gets the popularity it deserves. I really hope we can get more games like this!\t\t\t\t\t
