\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSpoilers-
The game is so meta and fourth-wall breaking it is unreal. It breaks expectations so well, while still having competent gameplay mechanics throughout. What started as a unique deckbuilder rogue-lite game with weird mechanics changed into something I could have never anticipated. Never in a game have I had to attack a boss with actual files on my pc (attack damage was based on size), and later have that boss literally threaten to delete a file. Never have I played a game where the enemies are directly pulled from my steam friends. Never in a game have I been scraping tooth and nail to get by, only to get a card named "Trolled!" made by another player with crap stats, only to die. No matter how mangled the mechanics get, it still remains competent, and enjoyable. Hard recommend if you enjoy meta games or card games at all. Definitely one of the most unique games I've played in a long time.\t\t\t\t\t
