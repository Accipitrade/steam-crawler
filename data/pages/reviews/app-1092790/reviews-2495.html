\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI absolutely adored this game, but I feel something should be made clear in the interest of onlookers.

This is more of a Narrative Experience compared to your traditional rogue like. The card game itself is easy to break it's balance (To the point I'd say it's designed that way to be more fun. Infinite 0 cost cards are a hell of a drug in a game based on Tributes) which means that mechanically you would "Solve" it faster than say One Step From Eden or Slay the Spire. There is a definitive finish line and with my 17 hours I don't feel an interest to return to it aside from finding some missed secrets.

But for those who want a spooky narrative that wields the Deck Builder genre well, there's nothing quite like this. And the less you know about it, the better. Enjoy the ride.\t\t\t\t\t
