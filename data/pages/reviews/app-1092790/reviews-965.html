\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty fun game and the community around it is great, but the RNG is absurdly frustrating. It's always a major player in roguelikes, but good roguelikes know how to build a system of incremental improvement to mitigate RNG and prevent a game from becoming a repetitive slog. Great roguelikes know how to make a loop that plays the same but provides a different enough experience to keep the player engaged. This game fails on this note. In the first act, you can easily find yourself repeating the climb multiple times after failing because you didn't get the cards you needed to overcome a boss (while there are a variety of strategies, the bosses require a bare minimum of card quality that, if you don't meet, you have no chance of advancing).

If you enjoy the experience of running at a brick wall until it starts to crack, this game is for you. If you get frustrated at watching 20-30 minutes of your life get wasted because you encountered a random encounter that happened to have a counter to your cards or lose to a boss because you couldn't find the cards you needed, it should be a hard pass.\t\t\t\t\t
