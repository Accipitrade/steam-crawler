\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHoly Crap. I got this as a fun little time waster. What a rabbit hole.
Pro's:
Light weight card mechanics. there is an awful lot of variety and customization in the cards. you can do really crazy, and yes, broken stuff with it. And rather than feel broken it fits the world and the game, it feels right.
This game is so much more than it seems. Exploration is rewarded. Try new stuff out, some weird actions have unexpectedly awesome outcomes.
The atmosphere is fantastic. This game sucks you in Hard. The world is compelling, far more than i thought a little deck builder would be.
Cons:
If you like meta, your screwed. broken is normal in here and the game will flat out cheat. Mind you the cheating is almost always for a story beat, and infrequent enough it doesn't get annoying, but it is not about tweaks and balances.
It is designed to feel like an old floppy disk game. and whether intentional or not some boss fights have some signs of old game development. Sections that feel drawn out, or unessceissarly complicated, not fun complicated, more like "i have a good idea" complicated that just slows things down. Very infrequent, but i hit it twice so far.
TL:DR Get this game. Even if card games aren't really your bag, get it, so much here. This game is good enough, that if i ever meet the development team in a bar, drinks are on me. Seriously, Get This. Game.\t\t\t\t\t
