My god, I am so so so glad I bought this game. The style, soundtrack and atmosphere were amazing from the start and immediately had me invested.

the gameplay is simple and easy to get into at the beginning while slowly getting more complex and complicated as you progress, which genuinely made me feel like i was somehow becoming smarter by playing it haha.

If like me you know absolutely nothing going in, keep it that way, and just get started straight away.

Long and short, Inscryption is the first game in a long time to surprise me and completely change the way i feel about a genre of games, and the first game in god knows how long to actually keep me invested and still wanting more after 8 hours straight of playing. Weather you're a long time fan of interesting card game systems, or somebody who honestly could care less about the genre, you should definitely give this game a try.\t\t\t\t\t
