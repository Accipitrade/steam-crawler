\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is maybe the best deck builder game I've ever played.

I'm a huge fan of Slay the Spire, and I've sunk tons of hours into that game. While this game doesn't have *quite* the same level of depth, there's plenty here mechanics wise to keep you happily playing with different card combinations and the like. The story is surprisingly good and has some good twists and turns. The music is great. The graphics are just awesome, I love the style of this game so much.

There's a couple things I wish I had known going in to the game. When you first start the game up you're forced to hit the continue option, not start a new game. This is by design, just run with it. There's nothing wrong with your game. Also, this game has three distinct sections, each with their own version of the basic card game at work. When you finally beat the boss at the cabin in the first area *you are only 1/3rd of the way through the game*. I didn't know that, and so I found myself fairly fatigued of the whole thing when the 3rd area loaded up. If I had known that I think it wouldn't have been an issue, but when I was surprised by a whole new section of the game my reaction was "oh seriously I'm not done yet??? how long is this game!"

All that aside, this is an amazing game and you should absolutely give it a whirl.\t\t\t\t\t
