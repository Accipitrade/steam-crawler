The cardplay is pretty fun and somewhat innovative, but where the game really shines is in its "other" sections. The story really gets you involved and by the end I was awestruck. The characters are great, the lore is beyond great and at the same time most of it is completely optional: if you don't really care about the game's story you can basically skip 90% of it and get back to the gameplay immediately, although I strongly advise against that. I'd also say to savour acts 1 and 3 because they're truly like nothing I've ever played.

Overall this game is probably a 9/10 due to its (very sporadical) reliance on RNG at times. It's worth your money no matter if it's on sale or not. Do yourself a favor and buy it if you like story driven games because there really aren't many games like this around.
