\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay so, this is a very interesting take on a deck-building game. Frankly, I love card games and have always played them as a kid. THIS however is a wild ride. It has secrets, 4th wall breaking, an ARG aspect, and addicting card play. First off, the overall game play is great, the mechanics of the cards are thrilling, and the story is mysterious yet deep. Its general atmosphere is really good too; The graphic fidelity and grittyness really does add a sense of dread. The game certainly kept me entertained for a while and I had fun.

Is this game for you? While its a wonderful experience for me, I have read some others thoughts and seen some flaws. Firstly the rules and the vibe change vastly when you "beat" the game. I personally thought that it was thrilling to have a completely different approach thrown at you when you thought everything was over, but, not everyone sees it the same way. To be fair, I really did enjoy the whole the mysterious ARG aspect. Going deeper in a rabbit hole of lore as you just try to play some dang cards until your brain can't function anymore was exciting for me as well, but, its not for everybody. Game play wise give the demo a try. But if you are in it for the story I recommend going in blind. There are a lot of secrets and fun to be found just make SURE you are ready for it. I will remember this one fondly.\t\t\t\t\t
