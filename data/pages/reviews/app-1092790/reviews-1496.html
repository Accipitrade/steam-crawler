However what I do like are roguelikes; and if there was ever a game to combine those two genres in such a well translated and intriguing concept it would be this game. Such a fantastic card game, it keeps you strapped in with its deep strategic gameplay and provides the player with so many options and freedom to experiment, with the random elements of a roguelike (the only limit being you can't choose which cards to play at the start of a round - go figure). The aesthetics are one of the main reasons this game caught my attention, but the story and progression will keep throwing you curve-balls with delicious surprises and new mechanics around every corner to keep it fresh. I won't spoil anything - but make sure to keep on your toes and expect the unexpected.
This one is a fat yes from me, chief. Even if you aren't familiar with card games of this caliber I'm certain you will find yourself having a great time with this one. Go play this game!\t\t\t\t\t
