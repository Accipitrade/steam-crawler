\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst and Foremost: This game is an ARG. If you are looking for an atmospheric Slay the Spire, you aren't going to find that here.

The game is split up into 3 parts: The spooky cabin from the demo, a retro-collect a thon fighting 4 masters and a factory. The cabin is easily the best part of the game, while short it gives you the choices on where to go on the board and puzzles to figure out to progress the story.

The retro game where you buy/find cards and packs to fight 3 underlings before a boss of an area. Repeat through 4 areas and move on to the last area.

You then play in the factory, which is almost like the cabin-lite. A few puzzles outside of the main table and a few lore secrets to find, but clever players will notice you are playing almost a combination of the first two sections done not as well. The shame is that the third section is actually my favorite gameplay wise.

Overall: If you are the type of person that likes digging into a game to find out all the secrets hidden for you, the game is good. There is a lot of neat stuff to find and the game creator is known for putting more in as time goes on. If you are looking for a new creative card game, this will help fill the void for a few playthroughs, but unless and endless mode is added or modded in, the replayability of the game is fairly low.\t\t\t\t\t
