\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIve always been a xbox gamer but saw this game being played by a couple youtubers recently and fell in love. Outside of Minecraft, this was the first game I have ever played on a pc and it is incredible. I am blown away by the level of creativity and design. I cannot thank the team enough for making such a spectacular game that has brought me so much fun and excitement. I just finished Act 1 in about 12 hours, and I was so thrilled to hear about the new update because the aesthetic was what really pulled me in. I spent all that time learning the mechanics and... Wow. it goes DEEP. There is so many efficient strategies and combinations of cards but the best part was finding the most overpowered run possible without cheating or researching. Ive heard alot of people complain about how strictly linear the game is as you cant restart checkpoints or remake a crucial decision but I think thats what creates the fun. Without the pressure of screwing up a god tier run, there is no need to make critical decisions as you can just go back if you dont like it.

Overall, this game isnt a 10/10. No, its more than that for me. It is a game that wont just be enjoyed, rated, and sit in my library, but something I will continue to enjoy for many years to come and hopefully share with my kids when they ask about fond memories. Thank you to all of you guys that worked so hard on this masterpiece. If theres is one gift I could give in return, its that I hope you all feel a sense purpose knowing that youve made some stranger in the world forget about their dark, miserable, existence and feel a childlike wonder and excitment. Thank you, for making such a beautiful game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPartial spoilers below!
A board game and a card game all rolled into one. 3D design, pixel art and live action all bring this game to a different level than most I have played in a while. The story is intriguing, cryptic and a plays with your emotions and feelings towards the characters. This game is practically 3 different, yet intertwined games combined into one amazing adventure. The game encourages you to replay for the achievements but also replay to find secrets and extra, creepy bits, behind the story. Certain levels can be quite challenging and frustrating but as you learn the game, it becomes easier to navigate. I would highly recommend this to any avid gamer who is not only looking for a challenge but a story that sucks you in on almost a creepy level.\t\t\t\t\t
