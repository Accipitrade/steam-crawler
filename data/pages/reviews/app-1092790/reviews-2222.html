\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI haven't had this much fun with a card game in my life. As someone who used to play Magic the Gathering in highschool, I found this game to be highly stimulating. I don't want to spoil anything for anyone so I won't go into detail. This game has some sharp twists and turns that will leave you wanting more and more. The story is riveting and the gameplay is challenging, if you like deckbuilding, roguelikes, and exploring dark stories then you will probably agree that this game is more than worth the mere 20 bucks. This game got it's hooks in me for quite literally 16 hours straight on my first sitting. I have now reached 21 hours and counting and I have only owned this game for like 3 days. That's how good I think it is.\t\t\t\t\t
