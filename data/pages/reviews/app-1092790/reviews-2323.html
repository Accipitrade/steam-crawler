\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat this game is NOT. It is NOT the newest roguelike deck builder in which you can dump countless of hours into. I went into this game expecting it to be a neat spooky card game that I could repeatedly play to my hearts content.

I was disappointed to find this wasn't the case, but boy was it a wild ride nonetheless. A finite story that will likely take you somewhere between 10-14 hours to complete, it was an absolutely excellent experience that left me filled with dread and anxiety but a desire to continue digging into the secrets and mysteries of this game.

At the risk of spoiling too much of the fun, trust me. If you like card games and spooky things, you deserve to give this game a shot. The card play is actually pretty fun. If they end up coming up with an endless mode or something, I'll be swinging right back to this game to sink even more time into it. A must buy if on sale\t\t\t\t\t
