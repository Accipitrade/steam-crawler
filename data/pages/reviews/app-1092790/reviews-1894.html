\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs always, Daniel Mullins knocks it out of the park. I've been playing his stuff since Pony Island, and he always seems to do a great job investing you in his games. For someone who is the sole developer on a project like this, as well as the ability to get top tier publishing (Devolver Digital), this game deserves all the positive reviews it gets, as does his other previous titles.

That said, I'd say that, like most of his games, this one is super short for the asking price. Definitely longer than most of his other games, but not by much. You should play the other games Daniel Mullins has made before jumping into this one, if only to curb expectations of a 20-30 hour game with endless progression and grindability. There was definitely more room for this game to grow, and it seems like he cut it a little short for no reason other than to meet a due date. I definitely would've been okay to spend full asking price if there was confirmation of DLC, but as of right now, I would wait for a sale on this to get your money's worth

TL;DR: good game! Short, but sweet.\t\t\t\t\t
