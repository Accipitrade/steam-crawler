Without spoiling anything all I can really say is that there are multiple games inside Inscryption, all in that roguelike card game style but with extremely varying art styles and gameplay elements. On top of that once you have had your fill of the main game you can play the alternate mode "Kaycee's Mod" which expands on the first phase of gameplay from the base experience with challenges and new content.
