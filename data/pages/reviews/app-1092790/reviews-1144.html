\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPhenomenal first "Act"

Stopped playing once I reached the second "Act"

Originally purchased the game under the pretence that it was going to be a spooky, rogue like, puzzle card game with a dark and mysterious atmosphere. I got exactly that and then some with the first few hours of the game and was blown away, it had me hooked.

Once reaching a certain point in the game, there is a huge change in art style, music, game play and theme. For some they will still really enjoy the changes and might accept them happily. But for me it ruined the experience and felt like a completely different game.

The first Act of the game was incredible and am glad I played the few hours I did, after the first Act I decided not to continue as it just wasn't for me at all and I think other people will have the same thoughts on this and might be disappointed with their purchase.

It's hard to give this game a solid yes or no. The part that I did play was 10/10 the other stuff wasn't necessarily bad, in fact many have said after Act 1 is good, it's more that it was unexpected and not what I wanted out of the game and I feel other probably have the same thought.\t\t\t\t\t
