\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR - it's worth the money and the overwhelmingly positive reviews.

--- No spoilers section ---

1. It's worth full price
2. The demo gives you a great idea of what you're getting into if you're not convinced. It's not too long (i think for me it was <1.2 hours)
3. It's worth playing through to the credits for the experience.
4. Go into it blind for the best experience

--- Minor spoilers section ---

I beat the game at 12.9 hours, so the story part isn't too long but worth it for $20 IMO. There's some replayability if you're into that and the dev has a mini-expansion mod (work in progress at time of writing) which I haven't tried yet, but extends the replayability.

--- Bigger spoilers section ---

My one gripe about this game is act 2. I enjoyed everything about act 1 and being forced to go through the slog of act 2 almost put me off the game entirely. Act 3 is better but still doesn't come close to act 1. The last 10 minutes or so before the credits were incredible but way too short. I felt a bit cheated knowing what acts 2 and 3 COULD have been if they replaced all of act 2 with an expanded version of the end. I loved the deathly cards battle and I'm so disappointed we didn't get more of that.\t\t\t\t\t
