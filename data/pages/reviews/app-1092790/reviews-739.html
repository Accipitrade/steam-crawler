\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Inscryption is a deckbuilding/card game with neat puzzle sauce and one holy motherf'in meta fiction story.

PROS:
+ Art and general atmosphere. Everything was good. Color palette usage was genius level. Everything looked unique. Later game parts done great, everything had its unique feel.
+ Sound design. It was phenomenal. All glitchy, noisy effects done superb, music was unique.
+ Gameplay. I finished the game in 14 hours, and still in awe of all its parts. Not that it throws something new and challenging constantly but all of the mechanics were also so neatly planned out you never get bored, stuck, not even sigh...
+ Story. This is a great example of meta fiction. Im sorry now that i missed the game on its launch and never watched any creator broadcast. Now i adore the guy and what he accomplished here.
+ Pricing. It is a steal for the content and the concept.

CONS:
- None!

FINAL:
10/10
It is perfect. Game of the year for me surely. Brilliant use of meta-fiction with unique mechanics blending. Full with surprises, i respect the creators choice for not putting any late game screenshots, so i will leave it at that. Fun gameplay. Intriguing story. Unique art. What else one can expect from a game really? Go buy it and put it on the top of your playlist.\t\t\t\t\t
