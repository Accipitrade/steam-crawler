Spoiler free:
Jumping into a new world where rules are explained and then changed and then modified is a really fun premise for any game, and a card game is not only no exception, it's a welcome change to the format.

I wished that there were some later parts that lasted longer (the whole game is about 8-10 hours depending on your deck building strategy) and the lack of New Game plus that retains progress of your cards doesn't make me want to invest further time beyond the few remaining achievements. That being said, it was a great game and well worth the money. Genuinely a great time.
