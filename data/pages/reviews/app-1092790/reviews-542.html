Acts 2 and 3: Boring, frustrating, actual chore to play at times. Throws a lot of extra game mechanics at you that just aren't explained very well and aren't worth actually using for the most part. Graphical style and setting radically change but not for the better.

Edit: Came back to this game after a few months to play the endless mode. It is a lot of fun, even if you don't care that much about the story and puzzles in the main game. I also want to say that despite it having been a few months I still think about the characters and the story and stuff so it clearly left a positive impact on me despite the bad taste left in my mouth at the time.

Changed my review to positive from negative to reflect the fact that, despite its flaws, it's well worth experiencing.\t\t\t\t\t
