\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNo spoilers here. Bottom line: Inscryption is a fun experience and a middling game. Which is to say, I enjoyed myself thoroughly but was never, at any point, remotely challenged. There are no difficulty settings and--to be blunt--Inscryption is an easy game. If you have any experience with deck builders or roguelites, this is not going to furrow your brow or demand your best.

If you go into this expecting an experience closer to a visual novel, you'll be thrilled with your decision. If you go into this expecting a challenge...well, don't. You won't get it. Disclaimer: the Spirit of the Cards do not speak through me, and I'm not a Pro Gamer. This just isn't hard.

But if you want a great story and 10-15 hours of entertainment, Inscryption has my highest endorsement. Just know what you're getting.\t\t\t\t\t
