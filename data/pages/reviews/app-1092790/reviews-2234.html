\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter finishing the game, I was absolutely in the feels. Buying the game originally I thought it would be just a basic cardgame like hand of fate. Boy was I wrong. When the game kept going after the end of the first act I was absolutely surprised and thrilled!

As with reviews are, a warning for some spoilers but I didn't include anything to drastic.

-Pros-

-Absolutely phenominal sound work: With surround sound or even headphones, your thrown into an immersive card game where bosses feel like your up against something sinister.

-The many graphics in this game is phenominal: at first it was a bit confusing to be thrown into a sudden 'pokemon' style pixelated version of the game but once understanding my new barings it brought a different light to the tale to better fit the narrative.

-There are fun little puzzles! But nothing immensly to difficult where you need a walkthrough!

-Heavily challenges you to think carefully about your card placement.

-T H E S T O R Y ! ! !: Absolutely phenominal where the ending has you heavy in your feels.

-Cons-

-Progressing 'too fast' aka for example. My first round playing the game I got to the Angler (aka the 2nd boss of act 1) before the systems 'anti speedrun' mechanic kicked in an was wiped by a swarm of murder bears. While I completely understand its need to have it. It can be saltifying for some. My tip? Enjoy the game, don't try and RKO the first act in one go. It won't let you lol.

-No way to backtrarck previous acts, would require a full playthrough again. : After exploring what I thought was everything. I was able to get through act 1 completeing most of the achievements (Some I have yet to wrap my head around what they could be. But thats all the more fun) Act 2 however it seems I missed some things, causing me not to be able to fight hidden bosses in act 3. With no way to backtrack I finished the game but now require to go through it all over again instead of replaying campaign in parts.


-Overall?-

I'd give this well deserved game a 10/10 for the storytelling, use of musical notes and sound to convey deeper meanings in the game and of course the graphics that are unique and wonderful in their own way!

Personally I would LOVE to see an Endless mode of act 1 with the bosses randomly popping up again and again (kind of like hands of fate does). Would love to see just how high and dumb cards can get!\t\t\t\t\t
