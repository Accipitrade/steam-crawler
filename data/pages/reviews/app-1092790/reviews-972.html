\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was introduced to this game when I saw Markiplier play it. And once I realized how good it was, I knew I had to stop watching and play it myself, which I'm super glad I did! This game truly is best played blind, since it's full of twists and turns that I could've never seen coming.
Along with the twists in the story being great, everything about this game is fantastic. The artstyle is beautifully gritty, the mechanics are easy to pick up and hard to put down (at one point I actually considered not progressing the story just because I was having so much fun with the game loop), the characters are entertaining and mysterious enough to keep you intrigued, the audio provides an amazingly creepy atmosphere, and the puzzles around the cabin are really well crafted. And this is all only for the first act! This has quickly become one of my favorite games, so if you can, I highly recommend you pick up this game!\t\t\t\t\t
