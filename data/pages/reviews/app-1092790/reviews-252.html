the first act of this game is one of the most memorable parts of a game I have ever played. i don't think i'll ever forget how it felt to first lose and start over again. the visuals are amazing and create a perfectly unsettling atmosphere.

however, i found the second act boring, to say the least. it felt like something to get through, rather than a continuation of the game i had loved. my opinion on the third act is similar, and i just couldn't bring myself to finish the game. whenever i would boot up the game to try to drag myself through it i just could not bring myself to care/want to play it.

i would consider the first act great enough to justify buying and playing the game, especially if you can pick it up on sale, and maybe you won't experience my dislike of the second and third acts.\t\t\t\t\t
