However I made the impulsive decision to buy the game one night after watching a youtuber play it and get through most of the first bosses and I was enamored by the art style, the music, the atmosphere, the mystery of it all, AND IT WAS ALL RNG?

needless to say after filling leshy's table with figurines, i now, after 38 hours so far, can at least get through most fights without losing. Next is to try Kaycee's Mod, which I hear makes the game more challenging. Oh boy!

A game that's different every time you play it is something very attractive to me as it has replayability. this game is all RNG so everytime you start it again, new decks, new carvings, new things to try again. generally there's always at least one secret to find in every run if you havent found them all yet.

I recommend using as little guides as possible unless youre really stuck. this game is best going in blind i think.

I have to say this game may be my GOTY as I have fallen in love with it.\t\t\t\t\t
