\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best games I own, in my opinion its place should be somewhere between such games as Undertale or Doki Doki Literature Club as far as mechanic, plot and fourth-wall-breaking goes. Inscryption deserves to be an internet phenomenon and it is totally worth your money.
As far as the gameplay itself, it is still suprisingly well build. Every time you seems to get to know your mechanics games changes, throws something new at you, makes you learn something more. You think you know how to build your deck but then - nah-hah, here's something more you can do with your cards that make you reevaluate every choice you make, have fun! Each encounter may have different rules and yet changes are not as drastic to make your experiance unplayable if you make some mistakes within building your deck.
My only real beef with this game is highly spoilarable, but there is something we, as players, experiance very late in the plot, for only half a game each and this is not fair, I demand my full necro-arc and how dare you stop right after watercannons and pirate boss, this is unfair and cruel and I took it personally.

Also, with the Kaycee mod you can freely experiance both the plot and later the rouge-like feel of endless mode, so it's basically the best of two worlds. Just pls gimme my prite boss and more Grimora :C\t\t\t\t\t
