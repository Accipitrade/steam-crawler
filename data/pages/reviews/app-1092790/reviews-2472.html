It's got some really cool ideas but they never really get fleshed out and I have to admit that near the final third of the game I was chugging through the card portions just so I could see where the plot goes. Spoilers: Nowhere. Very interesting premise that falls flat. All the intrigue and mystery does not culminate to anything. Still, it was fun for 8.5 hours. Go in as blind as possible if you plan on playing. The less you know before hand the better.

Now the question... do I recommend? Not for full price, wait for it to come on sale, it's worth sinking two separate evenings in but you won't have anything to return to after you finish the story.\t\t\t\t\t
