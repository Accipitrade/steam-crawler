\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is such a great example of a well polished experience.

Very solid from start to finish! The game play never stops evolving, adding in new layers of complexity at a perfect pace to keep things interesting but not overwhelming. The art and lighting set such perfect moods for each part of the game and do an incredible job of delivering everything the player needs to know in very nontraditional (but very functional) ways. The story is difficult to say anything about without spoiling much but it is told well and paced expertly. I was constantly looking forward to finding out what happened next!

Great game play, Great story, Unexpected levels of depth to everything. Definitely worth playing through!\t\t\t\t\t
