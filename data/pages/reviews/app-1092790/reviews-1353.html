Act 1 used to be my favorite, I mean it had it all. It was charming, it looked great, it made me question everything in the game and I loved the experience personally.

Act 2, however, personally took the rating down a lot for me. It was not fun, and I was almost ready to quit altogether, but I stuck through, and I don't regret it.

Act 3, without giving any spoilers, is like Act 1, but with new sigils and new mechanics that, honestly, made me like it a lot. I'd go as far as to say I personally enjoyed it as much, if not more than Act 1. I don't know if others feel this way, but I do. I enjoyed it a lot.

And the way the game ended.. just, wow. I didn't think a card game could make me feel bad for losing characters I met throughout the entire game.

Inscryption was definitely worth the money I paid for it. It was a great experience, and I look forward to playing again and again in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
