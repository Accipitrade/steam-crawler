\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely worth playing, the overall review of this game is overwhelmingly positive for a reason.

Go in completely blind and definitely don't look anything up.

It explains itself rather well so even if you feel a bit lost, just keep playing and you should find the answers you're looking for. The game does a good job of leading you along while making you feel like you're stumbling in the dark. It also gives you the feeling that you are barely scrapping a win together which really added to the theme of the game- win or die. Also, lots of twists that took me by surprise which is saying something since I've seen/played a lot of different games from a lot of different genres.

Also, for you guys who have played some in, don't you wish that the first section of the game was its own standalone game on the side? PLEASE make this happen. It's probably one of the most brilliant cardgame videogame experiences I've had. Which means a lot since I'm a pretty die-heart fan of the original Yu-Gi-Oh.\t\t\t\t\t
