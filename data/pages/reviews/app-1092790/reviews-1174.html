Part 1 (same as demo) is the best and most intriguing. Part 2 and 3 feel pretty different but have their own appeal. The overall story is great and there's more to the story than where the game ends. A (already solved) breadcrumb trail will lead to a video giving the final part of the story so you should check that out post completion.

The best part of the game (Act 1) can be replayed as a roguelike using Kaycee's mod.\t\t\t\t\t
