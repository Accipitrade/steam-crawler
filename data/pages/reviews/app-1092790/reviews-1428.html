\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's so hard to appraise Inscryption. Only thing people will say "just play it" without giving you any context. As you can guess, there is a reason for that. Don't watch let's plays, don't read more reviews, just play it! Definitely one of the top games released in 2021!

I came for deckbuilding roguelike. The core idea is so simple and still unique, even there are thousands of roguelike deckbuilding games out there. The game introduced new mechanics in almost every run, which makes you keep playing.

I usually stop playing roguelike games after beating them once or twice. I am so glad that I didn't do the same for Inscryption.

If you are not sure about Horror aspect of the game, I can say there is nothing to worry about. The horror is just a theme, Inscryption doesn't focus on thrilling you with any mechanic like scare-jumps or visual effects. Definitely not a horror game

If you are either of the below, just give it a shot:
  • Roguelike deckbuilding fan
  • Likes Pony Island or The Hex
  • Likes unique storytelling
  • Want to join into Fight Club and cannot talk about it

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has to be one of the most overrated games I've played in a long time. The card portion of the game is incredibly shallow and easily broken. It's possible to win a match in a single turn if you draw the right cards. The rogue-like elements were just frustrating and felt tacked on. You'll be making smooth progress and then all of a sudden, whoops you died and now you lose all of your cards and have to start over from scratch because the RNG just decided that you lose. As for the horror elements, there are none. The game isn't scary in the slightest. Do not buy this game if you're looking for horror.

The M. Night Shyamalan-tier story has to be the worst part of this game. A lot of praise has been heaped on it and it does have an interesting setup and premise but tries way too hard to blow your mind with stupid, unnecessary twists. WOOOOOAH YOU'RE ACTUALLY SOME GUY RECORDING A LET'S PLAY OF A SPOOKY CREEPY PASTA GAME HE FOUND IN THE WOODS! SO WACKY AND ZANY. MIND BLOWN ETC. I cringed so hard when I finished Act 1 and realised what this game actually is. In short, the actual game is nothing like what is shown on the store page. If you're buying this because of the screenshots and the description given, you will be disappointed. Especially since the gameplay and presentation are so much weaker in the second part of the game. I don't understand why the devs thought it was a good idea to cheapen the experience by adding a stupid twist that brings nothing to the game. I will probably still try to finish it and maybe my criticisms will be answered by the end but I can't help but feel disappointed by how the story developed itself.

All in all this game isn't bad per se, just massively overrated and definitely doesn't deserve the overwhelmingly positive rating it currently has.\t\t\t\t\t
