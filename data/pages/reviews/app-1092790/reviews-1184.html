\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI loved this game, HOWEVER, before writing this review, I checked the negative reviews of the game, and have come away with a viewpoint I had not considered.

My initial opinion: The game is very unique as far as I am concerned. It has story, strategy, and the different acts allow the player to adapt to different playstyles. The characters drew me in, the gameplay made me addicted, and the is something I have never been introduced to until now.

My opinion now: While all the my statements still stand, I do understand how the trailer and demo can be seen as misleading (I have seen some saying it was purposefully a bait and switch, to which I think they are being extremely narrow minded). This was true when the game was initially launched, however, there is now a beta called Kaycee's mod (which will be released soon as a part of the main game). This allows for more of the rogue like gameplay that the demo and trailer depicted.

Final thoughts: While I can understand why there was some confusion, I very strongly feel that most of the negative reviews are now out of date, as the game has added more of the rogue like gameplay, with less gimmicks, and an added difficulty (with a little bit of lore included).

So, if this seems interesting, definitely get the game. Bye my fellow "card gamers"!\t\t\t\t\t
