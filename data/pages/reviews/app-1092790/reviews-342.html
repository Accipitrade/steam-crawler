If you like a deck-building game, I really don't understand why you haven't bought it yet.

But if you're not the kind that digs an infinite loop of thinking, calculating some odds and such, this still is a great game for you.

It's complex but very easygoing. The atmosphere is intense, the mystery especially intriguing and you can't hope to stop, once you started to play.
