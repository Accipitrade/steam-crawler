I don't usually write negative review and even tho I enjoyed the game, I can't finish it and there is a big think that for me is killing it, let me explain.

The game is in 3 act, the first act is what you see in the screenshot of the store page basically and this part of the game is awesome. It's been a long time since a game didn't hooked me up like that. I'm not really into deck building but since it's kinda rogue-like, you build up your deck with what luck bring you and you adapt your strategy of survival with whatever you are dealt with. It's really great, a ton of fun...

The problem is that you get used to that, you learn the rule, you learn what strategy work the best and then you finish the first act... And you get throw into an almost completely different game. The art style change, the vibe of the game change completely, the only thing in common is the basic rule of the card game. But the game throw at you so many different card at once it's impossible to keep track of, it's just a classic deck building game at this point. Plus the rule of how to use the new card is barelly explained and really confusing.

I tried to keep pushing trough the second act but I just couldn't, it's way to confusing and just meeeh compared to the first act

Just make a full game of the first act seriously\t\t\t\t\t
