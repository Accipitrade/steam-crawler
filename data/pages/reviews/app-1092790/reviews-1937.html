\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI... I have to write a review for this.

For starters, above all else go into this game blind. This game is mildly "meta-horror" and it can be spooky and somewhat grotesque.

If need more, think Hand of Fate crossed with Slay the Spire and Doki Doki Literature Club. Seriously DO THIS GAME BLIND.

I don't even feel that physically can be objective with this game.
1. Its super engrossing you might just play through the whole game without taking a break.
2. You think you've won and you'd be so wrong it just keeps going.
3. I actually feel depressed that the last two Scrybes didn't get the full playthrough.
4. There's a decent amount of secrets to find/miss.

Honestly my only two issues are:
1. Early progression is a little to forced. You can't beat the first three bosses to fast, since they will for you to fail at least twice. (Though depending on your luck you might be able to win, given certain items and cards)
2. I NEED MORE! \t\t\t\t\t
