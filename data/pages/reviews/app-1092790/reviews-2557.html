\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has got to be the best game I've ever played. What a must! I was sold solely from the demo to buy this and I'm so glad I did. Daniel Mullins is a master of what he does, when I realised this game was from the same genius behind Pony Island I knew the game would go way deeper than what the first hours had to offer and was not dissapointed. The card game aspect is made in a very approchable way and is very inticing by the original concept as well as the gloomy artstyle. And what a concept! The whole story is so thoughtfully crafted, I trust that nothing was left to chance. The fact that we start the game in so much uncertainty about the world also just makes it extra creepy. You can't give yourself a better experience than jumping in this game with no real knowledge about the story. Mostly everything is planned for you to discover on your own, and all the small details just makes you want to check every crevasse for some secrets.

I truly have no words for all the hours of pure excitement, emotions and fun that this masterpiece has brought me, and honestly to a cheap price considering the experience. I simply cannot wait to replay this game and 100% it, I'm currently fighting myself to not restart it immediately after playing it for a day straight. Genuinely hope Inscryption becomes a staple in the world of videogames as it deserves it.
