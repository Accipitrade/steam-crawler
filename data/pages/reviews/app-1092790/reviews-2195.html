\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, hear me out...

It's really good and a breath of fresh air from all the other indie games which are pretty much a copy paste of eachother.
Visuals good. Gameplay good. Story good.

BUT!!!

It is absolutely dishonest about what you're getting into. The first Act is a masterpiece. The atmosphere is great and the gameplay is captivating, as you walk around trying to find out where you are and what you are.

However, this all comes to a screeching halt at the end of it and you are introduced to a subpar experience during the second Act and third (final) Act. Don't get me wrong they are not bad but in ALL of the presentations and videos ALL you see are parts of the first Act and if you don't search a Youtube video about the rest of the game you'd think "Oh, this is what I'm getting." IT'S NOT!

It is advertised as a spooky card game and it is... for three hours. After that you get thrown into a pokemonesque map to roam around and fight enemies in an completetly different atmosphere.

And then here comes the third Act to mend some of this like a drunk surgeon on xanax.

Sorry fellas that's a NO from me because of the dishonesty. NEVER ONCE DID I SEE GAMEPLAY OF THE FULL GAME IN ADVERTISEMENTS. It's like buying DOOM ETERNAL and then three hours later you're playing Borderlands. NOPE!

So even though I enjoyed it, I'll have to say that I do not recommend this game for the mere reason of its dishonest advertising...\t\t\t\t\t
