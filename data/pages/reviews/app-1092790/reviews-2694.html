\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played pretty rigorously these 6 to 7 hours I played this game. No breaks, barely blinking, and I haven't been this engrossed in a game for a long, long time. The eerie atmosphere is perfect for the Halloween season as well! And just went I thought I was done with the game, it opens up even more so I am pretty sure I have at least another 30 hours of content here. It's great.

PROS:
Eerie and consistent atmosphere
Fun escape roomy type puzzles (you won't get the witness or secret world level ones, but you feel good when you figure something out)
Story line is intriguing
Card game is fun, but basic
Price!

CONS:
* Card game has the potential to blow out either you or your opponent so don't expect a lot of polish like hearthstone or MTG. I made a deck that won on the first turn like 40% of the time.

All in all, a great title 9/10.\t\t\t\t\t

Inscryption is probably my favorite game of 2021.

It is hard to properly review the content because every minute detail could potentially be a spoiler.

This is not your grandma's deck building game

This is not Slay the Spire

What it is , however, is an innovative and fresh take on a genre that desperately needed the fresh air. It is dark, it is thrilling, and ultimately it is has a narrative that is engaging and pulls you in.

The Breakdown
Graphics - 5/5 - Stunning, and rich with atmosphere
Gameplay - 5/5 - Well explained, innovative, and does not get stale
Story - 5/5 - I have no words
Difficulty - 5/5 - The game will make you second guess all of your choices, in a positive way.\t\t\t\t\t
