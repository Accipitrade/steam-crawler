This game has a really cool concept, and after seeing a lot of buzz around it I decided I would try it. The first act is really fun, I love the art style and the gameplay is challenging yet rewarding. I love all the side puzzles too.

Act two is... not so good, it really just feels like filler but again I dig the complete change in art style and as you re-adjust to the mechanics it was still pretty enjoyable.

Act three is... terrible. It strips the game back to a point where it just feels like a soulless grind, the interesting and engaging art style disappears and I was left the whole time wondering why I was even bothering to play it anymore. Couple that with a mediocre ending and I just can't reccomnd it myself.

TLDR Strong start weak finish.\t\t\t\t\t
