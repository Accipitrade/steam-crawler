\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI feel almost lied to.

It's a really fun game when you play what you see in all the promotional material, and all the videos talking about this game. I had about 10 hours of absolute fun with this game, especially considering I'm not a fan of this type of game usually.

Full of surprises, well made gameplay, and an air of suspense that had me coming back over and over.

Aaaand then the 'fun' metagame in a game that everyone including myself was waiting for starts. And it just gets worse and worse from there. The gameplay in these acts is just not made as well or as tight as the first one, and drags on for so much more than it should.

I understand why this part of the game isn't usually shown. It's the grand reveal or whatnot. But it's so dissapointing that it almost seems as if it was just to hide that really the rest of the game sucks. I will say, if you get the game on a deal, it's a fantastic 5-10 hours. Do with that as you will.\t\t\t\t\t
