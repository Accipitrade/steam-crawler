\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have never been a fan of card games before, its just not my thing. The aesthetic and gameplay that comes with Inscryption is above anything I've seen in a long time. The story is intriguing and very mysterious, with puzzles that don't feel too hard by any means. The game is fair and balanced, if you're having a hard time, it's likely your fault with you card picks. Sometimes you're pretty much screwed and you have to accept that which is upsetting but you realize it's fair because you made the choices. If I could vouch for any one game this year, Inscryption is top of the list. I absolutely recommend this masterpiece.\t\t\t\t\t
