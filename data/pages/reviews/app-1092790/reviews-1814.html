\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is super creative. I haven't played a card game that has given so much attention to atmosphere, character, and plot. In Inscryption, you actually get up from the table and explore the room in which you're playing. A lot of the gameplay revolves around deck-building, creating OP cards, and 1v1 matches. The card game has some interesting mechanics, but it is not as developed as Slay the Spire or Monster Train. Although it gets somewhat repetitive, exploring the world, feeling the atmosphere, and solving the puzzles that exist outside the card game makes it all worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
