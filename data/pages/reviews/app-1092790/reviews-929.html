\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust finished this game and holy hell was it an experience. 9/10
-Literally just PLAY IT NOW, if you enjoy roguelikes, puzzles, horror, or just flat out fun and an engaging narrative.

The game is divided into 3 acts and the first act is absolutely perfect. The final boss in the first act freaking pulls the moon out of the sky and uses it as his final weapon. I have never played any online card games before and this just felt so unique.

The only reason I am not giving this game a 10 is because of the second act which completely changes the gameplay, and the unsettling eerie nature of the game is just wiped clean. I also thought that the game could have done without the live action bits, but thats just my opinion.

Overall this is one of the most imaginative and immersing experiences I've had with a game. Ending was fantastic, and I felt some emotions I haven't ever felt from other games.\t\t\t\t\t
