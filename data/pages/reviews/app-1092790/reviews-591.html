\r\n\t\t\t\t\tbefore delving into the review, one thing should be made very clear. This game is an experience best gone through without prior knowledge of what it is about.

to write a brief piece on what I think. This game is brilliant. The atmosphere is immersing, the story is great, and the gameplay while it can be rather unforgiving if you get a bad draw. overall i find it rather fun, despite never really being one for card games. The game contains heavy puzzle elements, with some being required to progress and some to earn some bonus rewards of some sort, even including a secret boss fight.

MINOR SPOILER---

In some parts of the game you may be required to die a few times to unlock new mechanics, this can be frustrating if you're unknowingly attempting to continue without a mechanic or item that is required. However it is very unlikely that you will be affected by this as its highly likely that you will slip up at some point and be unable to complete the whole game without dying.

The game works in several acts, and the player is thrown through a bit of a loop as each act comes to a close.
Many people will be upset that most of if not all of the advertising is towards the first act and doesnt mention the later ones. I do understand that it may irritate some people who really did just want a simple card game with a bit of lore mixed in, but I personally think it makes the game all the better due to it allowing the game to catch the player by surprise.

In conclusion I would say that Inscryption is a game that many people wont be interested in. But if you want to solve a mystery and discover the secrets held not only in Leshy's cabin but in the game as a whole, while playing an excellent card game along the way? I would 100% say that Inscryption is worth the cost.
Inscryption has quickly rooted itself as one of if not my new favourite game due to the gameplay, the worldbuilding and amazing storytelling. overall I consider this game to be a niche game that's not for everyone, and because of that it does what it attempts to do so excellently. many will consider this game a waste of money, many will consider it a masterpiece. I am a part of the latter. And if what I've described so far sounds good to you, then chances are you will be too.
I'd rate this game a 10/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh man, this game is just incredible.

Graphics are pretty good for what the game is, i don't think they need to be amazing anyway but it was definitely capturing. I don't really want to give too much away, let's just say it isn't what it might seem at first. I know basically all other reviews have said that too but you will know exactly what I mean if you played it. It is definitely worth finding out.

Best part for me on a personal level is that the character has the same name as me ^-^ that helped make me feel more involved in the story but i understand that won't apply to everyone.

Only gripe I can think of, if any, is that the story-line and acting is a little bit unrealistic and tacky, but honestly I don't care about that. It didn't have to be Hollywood material, I still enjoyed the hell out of it. There are just so many minor details about the game too that make it so enjoyable such as the humour and the characters who really capture your attention. It is also not too difficult that its frustrating but not too easy that its boring. I mean, I completed it in 16 hours but it felt like much, much longer. If I'm honest, I probably won't be playing it again anytime soon but it is a game that will stay in my thoughts for a very long time.

So much thought has gone into this game and all I can say to Mr Mullins is very well done!
