\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, yeah. I dont write reviews, normally. However, this? This game deserves it.
It has been a while since I bought a game and rushed through it in the manner of days. I got it three days ago, and have already 12 hours in (and finished it). This game accomplished to make me feel like a 11 year old again, who just got permission to play their daily 1 hour of gameboy.

So. To the game. Do I recommend it? Hell yeah.
It has interesting mechanics, tons of surprises, you have to use your brain, it can be easy at times and challenging at others. The Artstyle and Musicchoice are amazing and engaging, and even the little soundeffects are there to immerse you even deeper into it.

Now, one word of warning: Ive seen a bunch of negative commments, mainly from people who expected a pure cardgame.
This, however, is a mixture of many things. Me, finishing the game after about 12 hours, should be proof enough that this is no "grindy, forever repeatable" cardgame. Its more of an experience of different genres, perfectly blended together to create a masterpiece that honestly deserves GOTY.

So, enough talking. Should you play this game? Yes.
Does it matter if you like this genre? Barely. If you go in without any expectations, I am sure you will be blown away (like me, who bought this on a whim after looking at one screenshot, without even reading any descriptions whatsoever) as this is for everyone who enjoys an engaging story.

Well, thats all I had to say.
Have fun, Folks. Enjoy the game!
