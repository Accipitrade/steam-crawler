\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a narrative-driven... something. Definitely a game and a deckbuilder throughout, it goes through 3 main acts that each have you playing with new mechanics on the cards and even new mediums to play the game through, making it hard to pin one genre on it. In the first act, the game is played as a roguelite; you die over and over, slowly building experience and powerful death cards that allow you to finally triumph over all the bosses. BUT there's also a whole escape room/puzzle portion of the act where you can walk around the room and interact with all sorts of mysterious devices. And as soon as you think you've figured it out and make progress, the entire game changes for the second act. It's a fantastic game that has an interesting story, intriguing card mechanics that continue to innovate and change throughout the experience, and an amazing style that brings the whole thing together.\t\t\t\t\t
