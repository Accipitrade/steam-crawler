\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a really cool, mainly deck-drafting card roguelite, but also has elements of puzzle solving/escape room gameplay, and has a significant story behind it. This sets it apart from most other card games.

The game itself is divided into several Acts, which play quite differently, but all are based around card gameplay. The game's high diversity and focus on more than just the raw gameplay make it quite interesting, and it has a lot of atmosphere, but at the same time, finishing an Act means the gameplay of that Act stops and you have to do something else, which is both a strength and a weakness, because sometimes you just want to keep playing the game rather than have the story cut it short. I also really didn't like the art style of Act 2, which is only there really to illustrate some backstory anyway, but it takes too long.

Therefore I'd really like a DLC where you can just play things like Act 1, 3 or 4, except expanded, longer/endless, because sometimes you just want to play the game more, and the main game as it is has relatively little replayability.

That said, Inscryption is very much worth getting and a great experience! Cool gameplay and great atmosphere, well-worth the time and money. Looking forward to more!\t\t\t\t\t
