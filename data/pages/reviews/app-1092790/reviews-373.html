Inscryption is very much as if creepy pasta met a card building game. The game is split into 3 acts, and honestly the first act alone feels like its own game (I spent an unfortunate amount of time stuck in this act because it can be difficult to know what to do to progress, which is a bit of a downfall). The gameplay is fantastic and addicting, and there are many different ways to play, giving you reasons to revisit the title. The lore is a bit convoluted and intense at times, but the ending leaves you wanting more. I hope to see a spin-off or sequel to this game in the future!\t\t\t\t\t
