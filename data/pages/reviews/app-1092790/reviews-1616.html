This game OOZES character. From the start there's a creepy feel that almost never goes away, with the setting being mysterious yet unnerving. There's a great story to be found here with plenty of surprises and secrets that kept me hooked as I wanted to see where the story would go.

The game play is easy enough to follow. You summon small creatures which can be sacrificed for stronger creatures as you attempt to tip the scales in your favour. However, the game keeps building upon these established ideas to keep things interesting, some which even allow for crazy combos. There were a few mechanics later on that weren't thoroughly explained (which led to some confusion), but I was able to understand with a bit of intuition.

So much passion has been put into this game, and I would love to see more! If anyone has a love for card games or immersive experiences, then this game is for you!\t\t\t\t\t
