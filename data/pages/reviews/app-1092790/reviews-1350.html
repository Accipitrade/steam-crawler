\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI cannot in good conscience recommend this game

Had the entire game been the same art style, gameplay, and general aesthetic as the first Act, this would have been a no brainer must buy.
But as soon as you finish the first "Act" and move onto the 2nd, the game becomes a top down pixel style deckbuilder, entirely removing the rougelike elements, items, and mystery from the first Act. The difficult spikes out of nowhere, while the UI changes so drastically you can't even figure out basic info from the first Act, like how many lives the boss has left. Additionally, after spending the entire first Act teaching you the gameplay based on Sacrifices (and bones to a lesser extent) through an actual tutorial, Act 2 suddenly just tosses in 3 new "currencies", NONE of which are explained to you.

If this game had been bad from the start, I think this would be an easier pill to swallow. A real shame.\t\t\t\t\t
