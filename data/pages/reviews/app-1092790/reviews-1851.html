The rest of the parts, though not always as interesting or as good as the first, had interesting elements in their own right. I don't know, I don't get why people are going wild in the reviews saying it's scamming because the other parts aren't exactly like the first. Honestly if they were it would have been boring. I would love some sort of DLC or something that allows you to play against the other two characters like you do in the first and third part. You get a really short time with them in the last part, but I thought it would be so fun to get to play against them more in depth.\t\t\t\t\t
