This is a difficult game to review because, it being primarily a story-driven experience can ruin a lot of what people expect out of card battlers, but that's just the nature of Daniel Mullins's games, so be warned if you didn't buy the game yet and plan to do so. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't often write reviews, but this game deserves one!

What a fantastic little deck-builder, with a creepy, compelling and atmospheric story wrapped around it.

The gameplay is simple and elegant, the mystery of the game is intense - I feel fully immersed.

Do yourself a favour - don't watch any playthroughs of this game. Playing it in itself is a masterclass in discovery, all the while engaging the player in a challenging battle against wits.

Downloading the demo gives you a great insight into how the game plays, with the beauty of not giving away any of the secrets.

I bought this game for around $27 AUD and would happily pay $40 for this.\t\t\t\t\t
