\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was one of the most unique gaming experiences I've ever had. I did not expect so much from a card/deck building game. The beginning is weird and feels a bit overly directed at time with the game telling you what to do, but once you get past the first section of the game (which does require some RNG to do) it just opens up and 100% stops holding your hand. It has options in the game to just brute force it with a couple cards if you want. This way you don't have to dive into the mechanics if you don't want to, but I really recommend it because you can make some really fun synergy decks that are super strong and don't require a lucky top deck draw on turn 1.

If you have any interest in card games then do yourself a favor and play this. Go in blind and just hold on tight to that chair because it's gonna be a crazy ride that'll send you for a couple loops. IT ACTUALLY HAS A STORY. When was the last time you played a good and fun card game that had an actual story and not just a story, but a legitimate story with substance, intrigue and mystery? Man I freaking loved playing this game!

My only complaint is in the early game. Failure isn't held against and the game actually tries to help you when you truly fail, but this helping hand is also coated in RNG grease. This means that the cards the game makes to help you from your failures might be amazing and broke beyond belief, but frequently they'll be just bad and when you see them later you're reminded of how terrible that run was or how far you may or may not have made. The cards you get are RNG of course, but the main ones you'll want to get won't always show up when for you to have in every attempt.

After the first section I really started to love this game, ya know, once I had passed the above mentioned issues. And it just kept getting better from there. I did find it difficult playing for long periods of time... some lady kept knocking on my door when I would play this game...\t\t\t\t\t
