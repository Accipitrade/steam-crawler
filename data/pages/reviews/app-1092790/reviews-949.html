\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely amazing game..
It will not stop surprising you and completly blowing your mind...
If you like card games, or horror games, or games that break 4th wall well... it would be a sin to not give this one a shot. and even if not, you should definetely give it a chance.
After some time, I thought I already know what is this about, but no.. there is more, the game will keep giving you new content, and the ending was absolutely splendid and unexpected

Difficulty

The game has really good balance of difficulty, and you learn mechanics easily as you go, the mechanics are quite easy to understand and the deckbuilding is really simple, so even if you are not good at card games you shouldn't have a problem, because there are lots of ways to make the game easier for you.

Storyline

It is nearly impossible to go too deeply into the storyline without mentioning any spoilers... but the story will surprise you.. definetely.. and then again. and again.
There are aspects that I am not the biggest fan of, but the lore on its own is amazing. and there are lots of hidden bits and dark secrets.

The characters are also amazing, even though I wish I could enjoy them a bit more and see more of their personalities (Having a game with.. the other ones for example. if you saw the ending you know)

Amusement / Fun

Obviously, if you enjoy playing card games, you will definetely enjoy this game too. It has surprised me how many mechanics and twists this game has in terms of gameplay.. it has really innovative gameplay and it always changes the playstyle. and the bossfights.. the later stage of the game has the most original bosses, I did not expect any of that.

Music / Soundtrack

The soundtrack is really well composed, it often changes genres and it builds the atmosphere perfectly, it gives amazing jams during bossfights, it messes with your feelings.. and the sound/audio design is also absolutely incredible

Graphics / Artstyle / Artwork

Making a more detailed review on this section would also.. bring some spoilers. but every part of the game has wonderful graphics. The card design is also very well made, the style of it is also amazing and iconic...

9,5/10 game, seriously, I wish I would see a sequel, or more content, but I doubt that will happen because of the nature of the ending.. that being said, having more endings and outcomes would be great, it is unfortunate this game only has one (Perhaps getting a DLC where you will spend time with the other characters would be nice)

This game has its own originality and style, and while it was inspired by some other games (slay the spire, or the other game from this developer, pony island) you will not find something like this anywhere...
So definetely give it a shot, it is not just "another" a card game, it definetely has its own soul (...quite literally)\t\t\t\t\t
