BEST PRACTICE: Don't watch ANY CONTENT of this game, just go into it blind and play it!

(*) Non-Spoiler Part (1)
Let me just start off by saying I usually don't write reviews, post comments, or just talk about my gaming experience for the most part, unless it's your popular online videogame like CS:GO or what not.

I've never played a card game before, and I went into Inscryption blind, it surprised me in so many ways.

I'm not even the type to enjoy singleplayer games, card games or any game that is a story based game.

I'm just your typical online PvP/PvE competitive gamer.

Never in a million years could I have predicted the true scale of this game.

Just wow.

As of the time I'm writing this review, I haven't finished the game yet, and I put well over 26 hours into it by now.

Everything about this game just screams a masterpiece.

If this game didn't win any awards, I'd seriously shocked, GOTY 2021 in my opinion as well.


Pros:

-> The Music 🎶 -> PERFECT

-> The Gameplay 🎮 -> SURPRISINGLY FUN! LIKE REALLY FUN!

-> The Story ✍ -> Brilliant, scary, cute and funny.

You could easily sink countless of hours into this game.

Cons:
(*) Contains some spoilers (2)

After Act I, the game changes completely, I've noticed some people didn't like that, and preffered to stick with Act I.
I personally enjoyed the whole game throughout, so it wasn't really a con for me.

However, if you did love Act I of Inscryption and you've finished the game, you could look up Kaycee's Mod, and turn the game into and Endless roguelike Act I game. :)


TL;DR: BUY THE DARN GAME, IT IS JUST PLAIN AWESOME!! \t\t\t\t\t
