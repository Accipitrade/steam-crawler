Without giving any spoilers away, let me share some of the hesitations I had on purchasing and playing for anyone that felt the same way as me. I'm not really into horror games - this is in no way a horror game. There is an overall dark theme to the game, but it's all mystery. No jumpscares or anything like that. If anything, it's a little creepy because of the darker story that develops and the way the characters behave, but that's all. What you see in the trailer on Steam is as creepy as it gets.

I'm also not into card games. This is a masterpiece of a game that goes far beyond just playing cards. Again, I don't want to spoil ANYTHING, but the mechanics are absolutely beautifully designed. This game never feels like just a card game. The story and evolving mechanics make you never want to stop playing.

This game is so underrated that it blows my mind that it took me until a few days ago to hear about it. I would recommend this game to EVERYONE. It truly is worth every minute of playthrough and is easily my new favorite game of all time.

Finally, my last little note to anyone reading this that took me a minute to realize: When you think you've beaten the game, exit to the main menu and start a new game. You'll quickly realize this was just the beginning. ;)

1,000/10 in all areas that matter in a game.\t\t\t\t\t
