\r\n\t\t\t\t\tINSCRYPTION allows for the player to go on a several hour-long journey of exploration, puzzles, and lorecrafting. I don't know if 12 hours is a super long time to have been playing for the first time, but it took me personally a while to grab some of the later mechanics down as I am not a card game player and struggled with some of the concepts based on that fact alone.

The game itself is beautifully made. Visually appealing and stylistic in a way that hits in every crevice, the player is able to explore a developed and fleshed out story. There's choices everywhere that can matter later on in the game. Nothing is as it seems.

Having just finished the game, I think everyone should take a crack at it. The story is incredible and I am thirsty for more of it, so I'm thinking of trying to get the remaining achievements and going for it a second time. All four acts of the game are so, so cool visually and gameplay-wise as they're each unique and truly hit a different audience each time while maintaining the core game mechanics throughout. I would have loved to see more fleshed out versions of the later boards (Finale Act) because I think they're sick, but I understand why things happened the way that they did.

Something else I will never forget is the website for this game. Devolver and Daniel Mullins games truly made something special for promoting this game. The website, as far as I can tell, is still up if you google it. The attention to detail and the super cool interactivity of that web page will stick with me for a really long time due to how absolutely awe-inspiring it was to just click around and explore new lore tidbits, the functionality of the desktop, and little quirks that made the game genuinely feel like it was made with the utmost care. I highly recommend checking out the game's website before the game itself if you want to get a small glimpse into what you're heading into (of course, there's no gameplay on the website) but I also highly recommend people just buy the game, so, there's that.

I give INSCRYPTION a 100/10 pumpkins. Truly a wonderful experience through the thrills, chills, and friends we made along the way.\t\t\t\t\t
