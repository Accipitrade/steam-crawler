Without spoiling it i'll say this, this is a 3 part game with part one being i would say the hardest. For me personally part 1 is the best part of the game.

Part 2 and 3 change up how you play and how the game functions in some way and in different styles but with the core mechanics still in play.

FYI if you finish the game you can choose what part of the game you would like to go back to so if you think that you wont be able to go back to say part 2 without going though part 1 you're ok.

The ending is ok and i like how the inbetween parts work.

I would recommend this game if you like deck building roguelites and a couple of puzzles however i found after i found a specific card part 2 and 3 just werent as good as i was abusing 1 card to get though about 80% of my battles and how easy it actually is to get the card in 2 and 3.

Other than that i really enjoyed the characters and how they interact with each other. And the mechanics of the game and how part 1 handles teaching it to you.

8/10\t\t\t\t\t
