\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wish i could give a thumbs up and down at the same time,
The game is great especially the first act which is displayed in the pictures. A really good card game creepy enviroment, good sound design and great art style. The scenario is great and scary and feels kinda like an escape room where you are hold captive by a serial killer or some sort.

So why the thumbs down?

When Act 2 hits everything i just mentioned is thrown under the bus, the game turns in some kind of old 8bit Game boy game and the game tries to tell a 4th wall break like story.
Everything from Act 1 is gone, the mystery where you are, who your antagonist is and so on. The new mechanics and cards in Act 2 and in later acts which also have complete different styles are confusing and frustrating which made me almost refund the game.
It gets a bit better later on but never recovers from this extreme style and gameplay turn. It plays like a complete different game and i felt robbed because this is not at all what i bought the game for. For me its a huge loss of potential to create a really good an unique card game which i would love to play with friends with physical cards, but sadly that oppurtinity was lost to tell the story of the game.

There is still a lot to enjoy here and i think a lot of players will disagree with me on these oppinions which is fine

I saw this game in lets plays and i think thats why i was so dissapointed because i expected the gameplay from the beginning and how its portrayed on the store page\t\t\t\t\t
