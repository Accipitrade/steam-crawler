\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is an excellent game full of innovative ideas and surprises that will keep you on the edge of your seat. It absolutely lives up to the hype.

The card-battling mechanics are always fresh and fun, something essential for a game with rougelike elements. I never found myself feeling frustrated with the game; in fact, even when I had to start over, I was always excited to learn and try something new.

However, my favorite part of the game has to be the incredible atmosphere it provides. The enigma of the game itself is complemented masterfully by the soundtrack. If you take nothing else away from this review, just know that this game is 100% worth a buy for the immersive experience alone.\t\t\t\t\t
