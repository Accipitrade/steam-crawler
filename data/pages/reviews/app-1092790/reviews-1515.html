Finishing with more restrictions gives lore, starter decks and also more cards so theres a massive incentive to continue playing. I found it pretty balanced although some of the card types are a little unbalanced. I find that runs where I get bug cards are just kind of busted. The amount of rare bug cards available from the start is nuts and they are all very strong (mantis god, strange larve, even the tape worm can become a god or clear out the camps so you can power things up risk free). The other main issue is the boss AI. They are at least in my experince, very limited. They play the same cards so you more or less no what to expect and so it can be a bit boring doing runs back to back. The final boss especially needs some tweaking imo (maybe there are challenge things you unlock later that make it harder but i haven't unlocked all of them yet), his first phase is quite challenging, but once you get him into the moon phase its pretty much gg for the poor guy, it is kinda nice that you can kind of relax at the end but another thing that would be cool is if he used other rare cards. I don't recall him using the strange larve or daus rat guy etc.

Overall really fun in its current state, in the future I'm gonna revist once the mod/expansion is in a better state. Thanks for a fun game!\t\t\t\t\t
