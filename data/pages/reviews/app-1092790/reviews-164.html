\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPositives

An amazing game that gets you hooked on the mystery of what's going on. The mechanics are incredibly interesting and even multiple runs in i'm still finding new cards. The third act has some of the most interesting bosses i've seen in years.

Negatives

While the first act was amazing the second act was merely good and the third act turned into a bit of a slough. While having very interesting bosses the rest of act 3 is mediocre compared to the rest of the 3. The first peak into other possible systems in chapter 4 was far better and would have been much more enjoyable. There is a bit too much use of the white noise sound effect which is too loud even on the lowest volume level. The ending is lackluster compared to how the rest of the story is. There is also a point where the only way to progress is to leave the game to go into the main menu and go back into the game, with no clear indication that you need to do that. When I get truly stuck I tend to leave games for a week to a month if I ever go back so if I hadn't looked up a guide I would have stopped the game for a while which isn't good.

Conclusion

Great game but definitely has some bad aspects\t\t\t\t\t
