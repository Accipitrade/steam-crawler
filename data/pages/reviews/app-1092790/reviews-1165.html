\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaving played his previous game Pony Island, I had a feeling that this was going to be a "more than meets the eye" type game.

However, it feels like he really took those ideas and amped it up to 11 here.

The card gameplay itself is actually pretty well thought out, and makes the moment-to-moment gameplay quite nice. The 3D visuals are also really really unique, and I want to see more stuff in this style now.

I do not care for card-based games terribly often, but I enjoyed this quite a lot. If you want to play something unique, I highly recommend it. The story is pretty entertaining as well.

Pros:
- Very cool aesthetic/vibe.
- Card gameplay is pretty solid.
- Story is entertaining enough.

Cons:
- Beat a couple bigger bosses by getting absurdly lucky with a build, felt like I kind of cheated. (Maybe this is intentional, not sure.)
- I get that finer details in the story/lore are kept vague, but the game seems to go back and forth on this. They give some finer detailed names, etc, but then never bother fleshing it out.

All in all, I'd give it an 8.5/10. I'd suggest just going in blind, and if you aren't interested within an hour, refund it.\t\t\t\t\t
