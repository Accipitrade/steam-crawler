\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm the type of person who never even completes many games. I collect them and keep letting my library grow, which is why I have an ever-expanding backlog, but I never actually get to playing games. I don't know if this is because I just like collecting things or I just don't have the desire to play games like I used to or I feel that the quality of games has just dwindled over the past few years. It's probably all of the above.

This was different. You think you have everything all figured out, but then everything is just turned on its head. It grips you from start to finish. The soundtrack, characters, mechanics, and overall theme of this game is just excellent. Definitely one of the most unique games I've played.

While we are only a few months into 2022, I can easily say this is the best game I've played this year. This is one of the better games I've played over the past few years as well, if we expand out a bit further. I actually got around to completing it, which is a very rare thing for me to do nowadays. I enjoyed this so much that it led me to buying other similar games, such as Slay the Spire, Monster Train, and Dicey Dungeons. That's the power it had. I never gave a second thought to card and deck-building games and this just totally changed everything.

I suggest going in blind if you're going to buy this. I watched a few minutes of this game on YouTube before buying it and I'm so glad I stopped myself from watching any further. Without a doubt, one of the best games I've played and I can wholeheartedly recommend buying it.\t\t\t\t\t
