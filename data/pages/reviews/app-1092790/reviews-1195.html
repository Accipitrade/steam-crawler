\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo here's the thing: I am bad at this game. I am really bad at this game. I am so bad at this game that I cannot get past the first Act after 8+ hours of trying. And this really sucks! It seems like the game gets more interesting -- for better or worse -- after the first act, yet it feels like I will never find out. If you are like me, someone who is an idiot, someone who is terrible, terrible, terrible at deckbuilding games, then this game is not for you at all. Don't think, "Hey, I can just struggle through Act 1 and see what comes next." No. If you are a truly bad player like myself, you will basically be doing nothing more than slamming your head into a brick wall for hours on end.

Here's hoping the developer adds an easy mode so I can one day see what the rest of the game is like. Until then, iunno. Maybe I'll hop once every other week, bang my head against a wall once or twice, and then go back to playing something else.\t\t\t\t\t
