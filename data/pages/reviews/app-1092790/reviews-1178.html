\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI cannot recommend this game. The first act is incredible, and had the game continued along that trajectory, this would have been a fantastic game experience. Unfortunately after the first 3-4 hours, the game "pulls the rug" out from under the player, and the experience changes drastically for the worse. At first it seems like this will be a roguelike deck builder (a la Slay the Spire). This is what is advertised in promotional material, as well as the playable demo. Once you beat that chapter a couple times, the game's art style, as well as many of the well-balanced mechanics literally disappear, and you'll find yourself playing a card RPG, with obtuse mechanics, and boring puzzles. I genuinely enjoyed the game's first act. Everything after that felt like a chore to play.\t\t\t\t\t

I've never really played "full games" such as this, I mostly play games that wipe or I end up restarting, but despite that this game hit me like a truck. This was one of the best games I've played in a minute, the story and the "glitches" really pulled me in and left me wanting to learn more when I beat the game. I really wish I could've shook Magnificus's hand though, that ♥♥♥♥ hit my heart. I hope there's some other game to this, doubtful but it would def be a purchase. Loved this game.\t\t\t\t\t
