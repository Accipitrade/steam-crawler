I saw someone else playing just the first act of this game without much progressions, and as a little gift to myself for finishing a rough semester, I bought this for myself. Needless to say, I fell in love with the game and finished it in about two days. Progressing past the first act, I genuinely found myself yelling in shock. There were many moments that just made me laugh in awe, or yell out in shock, or even just left my mouth hanging open. It was such a roller coaster of a game- and at the end I found myself even tearing up, my heart full of emotion and sadness.

If you are looking for a reason to buy this game, this is it. The price is beyond worth it as I have now amounted like 80 hours just going back and replaying the game- even delving into the beta mods, which is something I never do. This game really reached out to me and touched my heart.

Even if you aren't a big fan of deck-building, rogue like games- which I really am not- I recommend this game so strongly. It was beyond fun, full of twists and turns, and just such a crazy, amazing game that I will recommend to everyone.
