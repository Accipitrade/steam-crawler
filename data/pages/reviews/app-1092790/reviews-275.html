\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really felt betrayed by the marketing of this game.

I got so hyped up to play this game from what the store page showed and I had it wishlisted for a while before I bought it on sale. A friend personally recommended it and even watched me play through most of it, giving me hints if i got stuck and helping me better understand gameplay.

Now I play MTG and the Binding of Isaac so Im very accustomed to the on-the-fly mechanics and principles of trading card games as well as your standard roguelite rules. That being said, I think I may have been over prepared for Act 1 because I beat it on just my 3rd attempt. In spite of that, I got tired of Stoat (aka Asshat) constantly telling me that I "misplayed" over and over again (SMFD Stoat). I probably missed out on hours and hours of potential interactions with Leshy and all sorts of cool cards, mechanics, and other stuff. I figured it was gonna have a rug pull like Hades where you get some rewards for finishing a run and then I would just start over.

Boy was I ♥♥♥♥♥♥ wrong.

Dont get me wrong, Act 2 has its charm (except the painter, ♥♥♥♥ that guy) but yeesh what a disappointing fever dream in comparison to Act 1. I kept asking my friend when I get to go back to Act 1s playstyle and he just kinda laughed.

I shouldve taken the hint.

I literally uninstalled the game after spending more hours in Act 3 than I did in 1 and 2 combined and have gotten absolutely nowhere. I know the battery mechanic was an option in Act 2 but hoo boy, theres a reason nobody uses it in any of the Act 2 meta builds ("WeLl AcKsHuAlLy" shut up, I bet you play blue/black in MTG). The fact that getting more cards actually winds up ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ you over more than it helps you is obnoxious. The battery consumption mechanic is so bad when Asshat can just DUMP critters on the field and kill you on turn 2 or 3. Knowing that this is the last act has ultimately made it just not worth playing. I had fun earlier, but now Im done.

Btw, Im totally aware that you can use Oroboros to money farm and shell all of said money on card abilities to over saturate your decks with a grab bag of abilities.

We do not care. Ive already lost interest.

The 'secret' mode is kinda fun I guess but its more roguelike than the original roguelite element and your decks are just too damn weak to make any actual progress.

The ARG part was kinda interesting but didnt really feel like it was going anywhere. I expect theres more after Act 3 but if I care enough to find out what it is, Ill just look it up. Also thank god for the fast-forward so the player can skip the rambling parts of the longer videos lmao

Keep scrolling Slay the Spire fans. This only looks promising, but it aint it chief. Many thanks to the store page for keeping it real :)\t\t\t\t\t
