\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is deeply astonishing.
I myself am a huge fan of deck builder games, this however is a whole other level!
In the beginning you are obviously confused of why you're not able to start a new game. Thus the mystery commences! Continuously intruding your thoughts on what is going on.
I really like the way foreshadowing is used in the little snippets with The Lucky Carder and the way everything unfolds later on just feels right.
But even if you don't focus on the story, this game is still lovely. I could spend hours playing without adressing the story and when I read the files of Kaycee in the mod expansion, I could relate so much to what was being expressed.
Especially the way I was able to upgrade my cards was of interest to me, unfolding almost an unlimited amount of possible combinations to make your deck perfect. Sometimes I would just try to achieve a single card combination to test it out on the battlefield.

TL;DR:
Great deck builder game you can play if you...
1. want to dive head first into a mystery
2. want something you can play with ease while watching or listening to something
3. like games with multiple layers
4. like deck building or card collecting games\t\t\t\t\t
