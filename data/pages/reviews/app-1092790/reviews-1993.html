\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI chose this game over Age of Empires 4 (They released about the same time).
I did this even though I grew up loving the AoE series.
I have NO regrets with my decision to purchase this game.
I have found myself to be one of those gamer's drifting away from mainstream video games such as CoD, Battlefield , .....
and am becoming more and more drawn to Indie games.

To other players who want to give this a try, please do.
My only advise is (like many others have suggested) , is to go in as blind as possible.
Also, don't get infuriated like me to start over when dying. Even though it may feel like you aren't progressing when you have to start over, trust me, you are making progress.
You will enjoy the game the most once you understand the game mechanics.
Playing the game TWICE since it's release has definitely helped in this regard. (I have spent most of my time playing OFFLINE, hence lowish recorded playtime).

This game is an unforgettable experience. It has made me feel incredibly rewarded as well as infuriated me at times.
The atmosphere, characters, graphics, music , story are all brilliantly woven together to form this masterpiece.
There is opportunity for variation and to try different tactics, winning combinations and very awesome surprises (Super rewarding!! -- WARNING -- Dopamine Overload!!)

All I can say to the incredibly talented individuals who created this is WELL DONE.
I applaud you and praise your work.
There is HUGE potential for more games like this!!
I am a huge fan and I CANNOT wait to see what comes next!

Sincerley,

Another Happy Card Gamer\t\t\t\t\t
