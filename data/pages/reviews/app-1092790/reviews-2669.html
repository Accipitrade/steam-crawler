\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has three phases. The first phase is everything shown in advertising and the demo. The second phase is DRASTICALLY different from this, becoming very "pokemon esque" in style and mood while still keeping the card game mechanics. This is where many of the negative reviews of the game come from with many disliking that the game changes this way, but what those players didn't realize it that the first and third phase are extremely similar in tone and gameplay, with different game mechanics. Essentially, many people quickly give up on the game instead of enjoying themselves and going along with the game. Knowing that this isn't your average rougelike, and should be considered more of a story/adventure game will allow you to know if this game is for you. That being said, I believe that the game can STRONGLY be improved with some sort of an endless system, for phase one or even phase three. I agree that this was HUGE missed opportunity and the game would be enjoyed much more if this were the case.\t\t\t\t\t
