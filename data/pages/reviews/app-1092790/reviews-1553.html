After getting past the cabin, I was greeted with the "delight" of a 2D pixel art type game. Not at all what was advertised. After I was the style change my immersion broke and the fun seemed to run off as well. The battle system became way more complex for me, making it not as fun as it was in the cabin. If it stayed with the theme of having the markings and having the special events maybe I would've liked it more. But as it stands I am just heavily disappointed with how the store page shows NONE of this. If I knew the game was going to switch I wouldn't have bought it in the first place.


Edit: After legit skipping to Act 3 I enjoyed the game again. I didn't care much about the story or game-play in Act 2. As I said before, the switch completely breaks the feel of the game. If it was fully 3D I would recommend buying the game. As it stands, it's still a eh for me. I thank the fact I can just skip the second part entirely so I say try it out. The deck-building part of the 3D sections is so fun. Just, be prepared to sit through a few hours of fun then a slight disappointment in the middle. \t\t\t\t\t
