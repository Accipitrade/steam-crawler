\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a roguelike indie card-based horror game, but that doesn't do it the justice it deserves.

The tone setting and attention to detail that the team put into this game shines at all turns, best being seen where I could easily wish for the smaller parts of this game to be a full game in themselves. The main campaign is a spooky romp through Leshy's various trials, and this is only made better with Kaycee's mod, an endless mode with various challenge/ascension levels which offer deepening difficulty and replay value. While there's some certain flaws which stop this being a perfect review (the top-deck is too variable and powerful to ever have a multiplayer element without serious re-balance, for example: getting a Black goat, geck and uyaruli top-deck is an immediate un-blockable win) this game is still an incredibly addictive experience which deserved the many awards it won upon the year of release.

My personal rating:
8/10

Would I recommend to others?:
Yes!\t\t\t\t\t
