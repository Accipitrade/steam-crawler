\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think this game is praised too highly for what it offers.

I loved act one. Gameplay was enjoyable and so was the story. After act one, things go downhill.

**SOME MINOR SPOILERS BELOW**



In act 2, the game turns into another 8-bit game but with the card games on the side. There's puzzles and characters you can talk to, but the game felt much more bland. I have just started act 3 (where people say it gets good again). Although the twist and visual change is interesting, the story I feel is overrated for what it has to offer.
I'm not a big reader in games. No I don't play a lot of indie games (although I really liked Undertale). It seems lots of people really enjoyed this game and I wish i could feel the same.
