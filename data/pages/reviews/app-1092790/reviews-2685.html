\r\n\t\t\t\t\t----[Warning spoilers]----

Game is pretty short and the story went pretty stale after a few runs. essentially, you're playing slay the spire with cards and a host, acting as the antagonist that attempts to sabotage you during your runs. managed to finish the game in 2 hours and 15 minutes in a very normal matter of phasing. But the main problem was that you get the chance to create a "totem" which lasts throughout your whole run and you can simply add one permanent buff to it, that will make it impossible for you to lose. I figured this out in my second run without any thoughtful thinking, and simply rammed through the game as if the difficulty were non existent and i didnt have to start over anymore. After completing the game you're placed in a game within the game where the same mechanics and principles are applied. This however didn't take me long to beat as it acts as a more simplified version of the game. I still haven't tried playing the game over again to see if any extensive story exists within the game. Overall the game is very appealing and intriguing but it simply lacked complexity and difficulty to me. As someone who loves deckbuilding games, i found it way too easy and lack in the puzzles that are presented in the trailer. They are automatically done when you get clues from a few of your cards telling you what to do and interacting with each other as a part of the story. Please feel free to leave your thoughts as this is simlpy my input for the game.\t\t\t\t\t
