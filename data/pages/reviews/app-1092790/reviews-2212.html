\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was amazingly fun, I started off totally fresh, so I had no idea what to expect besides what had been shown in the trailers and such, just act one stuff. Leshy's act alone was great fun, introducing you to both the universe, game play, and puzzle elements that were to come. The second act was a totally unexpected shift, but a welcome one, as it fit lore wise, and the addition of oh so many cards was definitely welcome. For the third act and beyond, I was happy to be back to playing the game as I had been when I first met Leshy. That, along with the cut scenes that we saw every boss or so made me even more invested in the game than I thought I could. And the finale I got was insanely interesting.
If you enjoy card games, retro style graphics, horror and storytelling, I can't not recommend this game to you, after finishing my first full play through I know I will be coming back for more, trying to find all the secrets and achievements I can.
I would love for some more point shop items too, anything from mini profiles to avatars would most definitely be bought by me to fuel my current craze for this game.\t\t\t\t\t
