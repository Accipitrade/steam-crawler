\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a full-package game, similar to Portal 2 in all honesty. The initial game is for all intents and purposes, a series of puzzles that the player must solve to progress while being provided a very compelling story. {Don't get me started on the story, its a rabbit hole that is the size of a quarry, but it really is best to go in blind}

However unlike Portal 2, instead of a silly multiplayer set of chambers and a level editor, you get the REAL roguelike experience as your reward for completing the story, which keeps me coming back for more, even 35 hours after completing the main story.

If you like any of Daniel Mullin's previous games, this is his most polished experience yet, go play it.
If you like MTG, Imagine if they made green decks into their own game. That is what this is. Go play it.\t\t\t\t\t
