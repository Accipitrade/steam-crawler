\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSomewhat mixed feelings about this game, will still recommend

The first act is fantastic, between the story, the puzzles in the cabin and the gameplay, it's all great. However when that part is done you move on to the second act, and the mechanics change. Only slightly, and you get cards that belong to the first act, however it feels like you have to learn the game anew, and it starts feeling like work. You gotta build a deck and adapt it to whatever boss you encounter and it's alittle more tedious. I wish the cards had been the same between these 2 acts.. I haven't yet beat the second act. Still a fantastic game. \t\t\t\t\t
