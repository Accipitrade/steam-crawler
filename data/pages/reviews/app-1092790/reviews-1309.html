\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI loved every second of this game! I'll talk about act 1 first, then give a gap before talking about the further points.

ACT 1: Leshy's Cabin
Act 1 is an amazing and immersive experience. It was truly amazing to see the dark, horrifying aesthetic only grow as the act progresses into a truly compelling game.

SPOILERS FOR THE REST OF THE GAME BEYOND THIS POINT.
Act 2: The Original Game (idk the actual title)
I love the retro aesthetic of this act, and the meeting of the Scrybes, both the one we met before and the ones we hadn't seen outside of their card forms. In particular, I enjoyed Grimora's area, but Magnificus, Leshy, and P-03 were masterfully represented.

Act 3: P-03
I loved the Botopia area and how it managed to evoke Act 1 while still being its own experience. I could honestly see Acts 3 and 1 being swapped and the overall story still being a compelling narrative. Act 3 also has many secrets that rely on past acts, as opposed to Act 1's self-contained secrets (by necessity) that help tie it into the rest of the game and not make it a jarring leap.

Finale
The finale was amazing! Getting to have "proper" duels with each of the Scrybes just made me wish we could've gotten full acts of their stories as well! I thought Grimora's chessboard approach to the map was an inspired touch, and seeing each Scrybe have their own method of combat play out in 3D was amazing. I also loved the obvious Duel Monsters/Yu-Gi-Oh! reference and do wish we could've seen a proper 3D battle with both of the other Scrybes.

ARG elements
I definitely think it was cool to see these ARG elements sprinkled throughout the game, and I've seen summaries of the ARG experience, but those kind of super-puzzle games aren't generally my cup of tea. The lore of this GameFuna and the developer is certainly interesting, though, and a compelling narrative that I would love to see expanded.

Closing thoughts
10/10 pls give me a full Grimora experience I need this\t\t\t\t\t
