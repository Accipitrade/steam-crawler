If you're into meta, 4th wall breaking, genre bending games, this is the one for you. It especially helps if you're into deck building games. I do especially enjoy how deceptively simple the card play is, though it can be a little frustrating at times. I wish you could have 5 cards instead of 4 on the board, but that's a very minor critique. With only 4 cards on the board, there are a lot of mechanics to play around with and master.

The game is very much about how it blends so many different elements to create a very unique and rewarding experience. This is not just ONE type of game. It's an adventure wrapped around a card game.

Highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
