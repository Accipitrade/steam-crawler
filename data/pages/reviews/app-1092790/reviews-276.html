It's a fun roguelike deck-builder which sacrifices some aspects of the core gameplay for story reasons. Luckily, there's a challenge mode of the core game after you beat your first story run.

I see a LOT of people talking about how you should go into it knowing as little as possible about the game, but TBH I think that applies to all games, even those that don't have elaborate stories or twists. A solid work of art and a fun game, though the third act starts to drag (esp. if you build an OP deck quickly).
