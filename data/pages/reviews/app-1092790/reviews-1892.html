\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have never written a review before so bare with me.

A general Synopsis of my thoughts and I will try to avoid spoilers:
I really thoroughly enjoyed this game and the various mechanics that came at different points throughout it. The atmosphere in the beginning was very successfully creepy and atmospheric. I could not stop playing and just had to keep going, to see the next boss and the next surprise for the story.

So in general if you like card building games with a fun creepy atmosphere and an underlying mystery with puzzle solving mechanics? I high recommend this finely crafted game and keep an eye for DLC with hopefully some sort of endless mode or an easier way to play the card game without the story attached.

More Detailed Pros and Cons with some "huh" feelings at the end.

Pros:
- Well thought out game mechanics that were interesting and adapted throughout the game
- The art style is interesting and fits the mood of the game
- There is an underlying hidden story that is very easy to miss, but is super interesting.

Cons (spoilers):
- The sudden change in art and game play mechanics between the acts was jarring
- I wanted to experience the other Scrybes' and their interesting boards.
- Could be a bit overwhelming with new mechanics if you got unlucky with spacing of new cards

Middling / Not bad just a "huh" (spoilers):
- I wish there was a way to play against the Camera Man and Death Scrybe because their card mechanics were fun and while I enjoyed P30's ACT, I didn't really...find it as enthralling because of the art style difference. That's more a personal preference. Currently there is no endless mode against the Camera Man or Death Lady.
- Felt like there were less puzzles in Act 3, but that might just be because I knew how to solve most of them.
