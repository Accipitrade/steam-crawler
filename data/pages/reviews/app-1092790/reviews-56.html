\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe actual card game is decent, just not as impressive as its contemporaries - Slay the spire, Monster train. But what the game makes up for the gameplay department are the story, the twist, the atmosphere and the fact that it doesnt stick to one card mechanic for too long. The switch between 3 acts keeps the game fresh, notwithstanding the whiplash with the transition. I would rate act 1 > act 3 > act 2 but as a whole they add good experience to the game and if you like the spooky act 1 only they provide improved act 1 mode after you beat the game anyway.\t\t\t\t\t
