\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, This game is amazing. Best game of the year.

One thing to love about this game has to be the way that the story is being told. Having a Dungeon Master, while you travel around in the map, is such an awesome take on the play style. The story is both terrific and horrifying at times while some parts actually got me a bit emotional.

I was honestly sad when the game ended; I did not want the game to end... Either way, the ending was well expected and it even gave me time to accept what the ending ended up on.

Again, This game is my pick for best game of 2021. If there's something worth your money, is this.
Thank you to everyone that worked on the game.

PS: Please Please Please Make a Dungeon Crawler out of this game style!!!! Such a good form of card game is rare nowadays! I would love for you guys to do that! Also, a physical game board/card packs is a must! Just take my money!!!
