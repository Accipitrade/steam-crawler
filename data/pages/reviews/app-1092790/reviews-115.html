The card game element is pretty good and even quite funny at times, and the escape room style puzzles are a decent mix between challenging but still rewarding/fun.

The game even has a whole new huge element later on which is not advertised on the Steam store, and it was a really cool surprise.

Now I was seriously considering giving this game a rating of 10, but it does the horrible video game thing where it pretty much throws you to the start (more or less anyway), when you fail. Devs need to realise that in 2023 video game players don't want to play games that kick you back to the start like it's the 1980s again!

Will for sure come back to this game at some point though
