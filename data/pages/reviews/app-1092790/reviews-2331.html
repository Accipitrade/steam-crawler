The story overall is fun and creative but if you ever played Danial Mullins games like pony island, or the hex the story could fell tiring. This would not apply if you never played those games but the format to the story's in those games are quite similar.

Pros -
1. Great card Rougelike like since Slay The Spire, Hades, etc (in my opinion)
2. Escape room twist breaks up the pace in a good way.
3. Design and look of each "act" is stunning (especially the 3D ones)
4. Fantastic world and character's
5. Reasonably priced

Cons - Very minor
1. Story while really good does seem like retreating old ground with Danial other games (which this doesn't apply if you didn't play his other games)
2. And the ending which I wont spoil but I think the last scene wasn't really necessary when it could have ended with the main character smashing the thing and then cuts to credits leaving a bit of self imagination to what was on the thing but that's just me.
3. Low replay value

8/10 This is a game you play for the card game and (while retreating old ground) fascinating story with great twist and turns. Danial Mullins you did it again great job cant wait to see what you do next.\t\t\t\t\t
