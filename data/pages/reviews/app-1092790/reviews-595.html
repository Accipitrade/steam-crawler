\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA adventure card game when you boil it down to it's base elements but why would you do that when from these base elements so much has to be discovered! Not only is it employ excellent mechanics with about 4 different deck types of cards(Sacrifice, Bones, Energy, and Magic) but endless combinations, it has a intriguing story that opens up as you play. Much like Hand of Fate in the same genre you have your own character but unlike Hand of Fate you making it out alive is more predicated on your deck building. Enhancing card through sigils (special abilities for cards) and power ups can make or break your run. The underlying story of this game can be described as a swirling mass that wants you to come deeper with it, see it's vast vistas just to see how far the rabbit hole goes. Your goona enjoy this one if your into a admittedly a game with a slow burn at first but then it eventually releases with a explosion of content and your definitely gonna enjoy it if you like dynamic card games. 10/10 would buy twice if I could\t\t\t\t\t
