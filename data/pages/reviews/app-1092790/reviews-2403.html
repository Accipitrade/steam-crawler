here's what you need to know, with as little spoilers as possible:
- inscryption is a deck-building card game, and that remains consistent throughout its entirety, though new mechanics and variations are introduced. You will probably have to enjoy card games at least a little to enjoy inscryption.
- the story is creepypasta, and only really flirts with horror.
- the game got a free expansion that adds some substantial replay value*, so if you like inscryption, you can stick with it for a while
- when it comes to design, inscryption fluctuates from genius to baffling, and if you have any interest in the topic there's a ton to learn from, in both the great stuff and the terrible garbage!
