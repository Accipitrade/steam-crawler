\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHowwwdy! I don't usually take the time to write a review (even for games that I really like-- but that's mostly 'cuz I'm lazy), but Inscryption is gonna be an exception here. I played over 40+ hours of the demo, played through it once, played through it twice with a friend, and played through it thrice just to unlock all the achievements as well as discover all the easter eggs you can find, and along with the countless hours I'm spending on the DLC, I think I can say I'm way too invested into this game now.

Inscryption is a MUST-PLAY imo. My only advice for this game is to go in as blind as you possibly can, and play it before you get yourself spoiled somehow. All you need to know, is that it's a creepy card game mixed with escape room, with TONS of secrets lurking in the dark. And don't expect the game to be predictable... this game is far from it.

Story: 10/10 (VERY mystery-driven, still going thru it via the DLC, but so far it keeps you invested asf)
Music: 10/10 (Y e s)
Gameplay: 9/10 (Early on it's creative, and later on it's even more creative. Expect to think outside-the-box when playing through this game. I would normally rate this 8/10 due to the fact some parts are easy because of it, but the DLC solves that if you're looking for a real challenge.)

ANYWAY, stop reading this and heckin' buy it and play it!!! I promise you, it WILL be an experience. And it WILL be worth it.

In honor of Ouroboros my beloved. <3\t\t\t\t\t
