\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo cut a long story short, if you're undecided on whether or not you should buy this game, do it.

I'm not really an avid fan of card games, but the mysterious shroud surrounding this game along with the resounding support that you see in reviews tempted me to to buy it. And boy-oh-boy am I glad I did. I can't really talk much about the things that make this game so great without going into great detail, but just like every other review you see for this game, I can agree that you should go into it as blind as possible.

If you like card games, this is very much so worth the full $20 dollars, especially more-so if you can catch it on sale.

If you like games that have an atmosphere that enhances the experience, this game does it better than any I've ever seen; the soundtrack, the sounds, the animations, the artstyle, everything coalesces into a wonderful game that will leave you coming back for more.

If you are a fan of the gameplay, there is a mod released by the developers that expands upon the gameplay which I highly recommend checking out after you've beaten the game.

All in all, one of my new favorite games of all time, and a must-play for anyone who likes card games, or anyone who wants a truly unique experience.
