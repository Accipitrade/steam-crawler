I would honestly give this game a 9.8/10
Here are a few things I liked about it.
The mechanics were nice.
The graphics are awesome.
The story is pretty good.
The gameplay is very, very, very fun.
The deck building with difficult decisions to make, just made the game all that much better.
The game is in all honesty very well balanced, you may get an overpowered card here and there, though what did you have to do to get it? Sacrifice a card? Go through multiple fights to upgrade it? Defeat a boss and get lucky and pull it?
The game makes you work for what you get and that just makes it all the much better.

What I didn't like about is.
I kinda didn't really like the second part of the game (Three parts in total) each amazing. Though the second part felt kinda meh to me. Changed the graphics, turned it 2d pixel style. Wasn't really too into that. It was still cool though, don't get me wrong. I just liked the 3d parts more.
Honestly, the first part end boss is uh... it's kinda stupid easy to beat. You only need one card to beat it. No seriously, one card. (Figure it out for yourself)

Honestly, I don't really have many complaints about the game. The game is honestly really good, well made, fun to play, and just is great.
9.8/10 would indeed play again.
Only two things I would do to make the game better.
Add an endless mode to the first part of the game.
Make the First part last boss harder. (I shouldn't be able to get an easy win condition with a single card)\t\t\t\t\t
