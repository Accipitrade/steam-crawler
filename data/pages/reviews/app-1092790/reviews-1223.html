\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNo spoiler review:
Super fun and really well balanced, especially early on. The storytelling is phenomenal, my jaw dropped multiple times both due to subtle things and big story beats. However, I would say it falls off at about 9-10 hours in while it is about a 13 hour game from both my own experience and what I've seen from the averages online. The ending itself is really really REALLY good, but the 2 hours I spent getting to it were just... boring. Overall, if you like turn-based combat games, super meta games, or creepy games with extremely interesting stories, then I'd recommend it. It is definitely a good game.

Fairly big spoiler:
Honestly Act 1 was like perfect and I am slightly sad that the rest weren't just more of it, I wish it was 5 bosses or something (might play that mod for this reason). Nothing worth pointing out about it. Act 2 was perfectly fine, though the wizard starting deck was incredibly weak, especially compared to the other starters as I've discovered online. Made my experience much more frustrating. I still really like deckbuilding games, so when I was able to just make a skeleton deck, I started steamrolling and was having some fun. Act 3 was where the game just got monotonous. I am already not the biggest fan of sci-fi, so I was a little bias going in, but wow was the combat SO repetitive. Getting to the bosses was just... boring. However, the boss fights themselves were cool and really well done, as well as the story beats and especially the ending. I'd rate Act 1 9/10, Act 2 7.5/10, and Act 3 7/10.\t\t\t\t\t
