\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI used to really dislike card games, not even giving them much of a chance. Thronebreaker changed my mind and ever since i've been on a card game run. This game is good. I have a feeling I've only skimmed the surface of the depth of this game. The mystery of what the hell is going on is the driver for me. Our host is an interesting, creepy, and quite fair and even generous. He'd almost be likable if it wasn't for the wanting to kill us part. Overall, it's a unique take on deck building. The game is dark and challenging. The only down side for me so far has been the overall lack of locations and places to see. You're in a small room that has been brownwashed in color. It's not awful to look at, just gets a bit dull. After 5 hours of gameplay I'd give it a 4 out of 5 whatever that's worth. For sure you're getting good value for the price of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
