TLDR

If you like a fairly decent card system baked into a linear puzzle game with a story full of 'wtf is going on' ending in an 'Oh! Wait wot?' leaving more questions than answers well this sure is that kinda game. You start out not knowing what the poops is going on and end up in more questionable poops of unknowningness. This is an ARG and has been solved so for those looking to jump into that aspect of it, there are parts of that experience that are no longer... experienceable. All in all, it's still pretty enjoyable for the 10-15ish hours of weirdo card game with the rules constantly being flipped around while keeping the same core. There's not much replayability but it's still damn enjoyable.
