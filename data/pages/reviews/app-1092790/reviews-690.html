It's easily one of the best--and more importantly, most interesting--games I have ever played. Top 10, at least. Every swing it takes is for the fences, and nearly all of them connect. Incredible.

Hell yeah.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was an interesting buy, I saw many different gameplay videos. Each of them showing the game bit by bit. Early stages, to the most recent. The game has quite a bit to offer, and has a rather, intriguing story line that is pretty difficult to follow if you're not actively listening and looking for it. So it is more of a, get your cards dealt, and fire away sort of thing. There are cool systems, like sacrificing different cards you placed to place better ones, with different sigils. The sigils allow your cards to perform different actions after the turn that can do a variety of beneficial things. I strongly recommend that before you just dive right into that you look at the handbook that you can find by exploring the room you are in. But definitely complete the integrated tutorial that they've put in place. Definitely worth the money, and worthy of coming back to every once in a while.\t\t\t\t\t
