\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis developer took a game genre that has already been quite swamped with titles as of late and still managess to provide a fresh and innovative experience. You begin the game in a cabin wherein a mysterious figure shrouded in darkness resides. It bids that you sit down and play a card game with it. You quickly realize that this is no ordinary game, and your host is perhaps not all sane, if the knife and pliers and loose teeth being measured on a weight scale are anything to go by.

First you have to beat it at its own game. As for what happens afterward? Let's just say there's far more than meets the eye.

A wonderful, compelling game that provides that "just one more round" rush we all want in a card battler. This game oozes creativity and simple fun, with twists and turns and rule changes that don't come across as unfair so much as an organically-added addition challenge to test your strategy and skills. Sometimes it's the luck of the draw and you get a bad run, but the end never feels unattainable. Highly recommend!\t\t\t\t\t
