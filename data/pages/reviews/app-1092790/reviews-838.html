I ADORE this experience. Calling it anything other than an experience feels weird, as it is so much more than just a simple card game. Words cannot describe how fantastic playing it was. The story is captivating, the gameplay is beyond enjoyable, and the twists the game presents caught me off guard, despite having base knowledge of the game and how it goes.
This game is a MUST PLAY. I fell in love with it. It instantly pushed itself into my top 5 games of all time, simply by how tight it's grip on me was when i played it. I beat it in like 2 days, I simply could not put it away for a second.
I was unfortunately late for the ARG aspect of the game, but following along to the crumbs that were left over afterwards was also very fun. I cannot imagine the amount of effort that has gone into this game and everything surrounding it.
I am a fan of unreality/real life bleeding into fiction, so this game scratched every itch I could have possibly had; not only was the game itself fun as all hell, and as mentioned before, the twists were genuinely unpredictable, but the acting was impressive and there were some moments where I held my breath before giggling to myself and literally jumping out of my seat so I can scream about how amazing what I just saw was.
Replaying the game now, I see so many little hints as to what would have happened later - I couldn't have possibly known that there was a reason there were unobtainable items in the cabin, seemingly important but not usable. I did not expect to become attached to the characters, either, and yet here I am.
I could go on and on, detailing every single little thing that made me fall in love with this experience, but not only do I want to avoid spoilers, I also am not in the mood to get a wrist cramp from writing too much.
Play Inscryption. I promise it's worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
