One look at the store page and the trailer video and I knew I needed to play it. A spooky deck building card game where you wager your own life? Perfect for halloween which was just around the corner.

From the very start it was clear there were more things going on with the game than met the eye. As I played farther it only became more intriguing. Every time you think you have a handle on the game, it switches things up and throws you new twists and surprises.

I realize all of this has been pretty vague on details of the game, but that is only because Inscryption is best the less you know going into it.

If you like card games, unusual means of storytelling, or games that play with the fourth wall, Inscryption is a good game for you, and its my 2021 game of the year. For what that is worth.\t\t\t\t\t
