\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt started out as a really awesome experience, but i feel this game is a crescendo of risks that end up draining the magic of the game in such a impressively short time.

gameplay
Its started off really good. seems as though the card play had a lot of depth while also being super simple to understand. but after a few runs you naturally come upon broken card combos that just delete the enemy. And i gotta emphasize "naturally" i like to try to break card games but this game breaks itself for you.

Theme
the overall art style and theme is truly amazing and i almost want to recommend the game for its opening hours. Its so well done that its a shame that the second part of the game is so assaultingly bad looking. I struggled to play the second card game that gets established because its just such a ugly and cluttered screen to look at.

Story
its cringey. like its pretty bad.

I feel for many this is a 1-3 hour game, i think once people get to the second part of it i feel many will drop off.
