\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a journey this game has been. Much like Pony Island you will find yourself getting quite invested within the entire story and accidentally playing it in one loooooong sitting. Part of me wished there was acts for the crypt woman and wizard but the game would have maybe overstayed it's welcome if it had twice the length to it. While the gameplay is quite fun I do hope for some sort of dedicated roguelite run mode in the future via dlc or post launch update. Even without that though as an indie game experience this title is able to tell a unique and interesting story and its use of mixed media definitely help fill the atmosphere it was going for. I can confidently say I got my money's worth and then some with Inscryption. Also that mock up duel was about as kino as it gets.

Edit: Steam was down during part of my playthrough and I now have noticed I have next to no achievements. AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA. Second playthrough imminent.\t\t\t\t\t
