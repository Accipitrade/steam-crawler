\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm writing this review off of just finishing and thinking back on the 2-3 days I have spent playing this game.

This game defies any and all expectations you may have. A standard card game, easy to pick up and play, but beautiful in it's theme and execution. The art style is unique and the story is wonderful. When I haven't been playing this game it's because I couldn't, and the last 2-3 days I spent on it is amazing. I wish I could talk more about it without spoiling anything, but this game is EXTREMELY worth playing. I played the demo before the game came out, and didn't watch any videos before playing. This is the first time in a long while I went into a game almost completely, truly blind, and it was extremely worth it. The appeal of the slight horror, on top of a tricky, but well thought out game structure is super appealing and kept my interest way longer than most other games I have played in a long time have. I don't typically enjoy single-player story games due to short attention span problems, but this held my interest long enough for me to play it all the way til 4AM where I actually had to sleep cuz i had dozed off twice.

Brilliant story, brilliant execution, everything is wonderful. I only wish I could experience the game blindly, again. You might never find a game more creative, more fresh, and unique as Inscryption. It's probably my favorite single-player story game I've ever played.

If you see this review, please buy it. It's entirely worth your time.\t\t\t\t\t
