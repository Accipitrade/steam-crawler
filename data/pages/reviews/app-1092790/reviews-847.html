I bought this game because it looked like a really cool rogue-like and I am a die-hard tabletop card-game fan. Then I played it, and got far more than I bargained for. Nothing more to say, absolutely worth the buy, and one of the most exciting and engaging games I had played in a very long time.

It's like magic or hearthstone with an occult/satanic overtone, but something just doesn't sit right...






(READ NO FURTHER IF YOU DONT WANT MINOR SPOILERS)

If you like being deceived, being kept on the edge of your seat, then this game will floor you.

The implementation of an endless mode has just made this my favorite game of 2021. (Even while playing it in 2022.)\t\t\t\t\t
