I understood the mechanics and was immersed almost instantly, so if you are new to this genre, don't worry, you'll do great! My one criticism or even just feedback would be the lack of post-game content. I loved the content surrounding Leshy and it felt super well done, but Act 2 dragged on too long from my perspective and the entire time, I just wanted to go back to Leshy's segment. Once the game was finished, I hurried back to Leshy, beat him instantly, and was in Act 2 again...
