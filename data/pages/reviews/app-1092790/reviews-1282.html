The mechanics were very fun to figure out and play around with, and the rules kept changing and evolving throughout the game which kept it fresh and interesting. I especially enjoyed all the boss fights that all had their own unique twist. Outside of the card games there are still some light addicting exploration, secrets and puzzle segments that reward you with story pieces and gameplay boons.

Very slight spoilers: It was way more story heavy than I expected, and I didn't LOVE how meta it became eventually, and the found footage elements were a bit weak. It was the atmosphere and mystery of the cabin you play in Act 1 (and the demo) that was the best part of it for me. I didn't dislike the story, I just wish it took a different route.\t\t\t\t\t
