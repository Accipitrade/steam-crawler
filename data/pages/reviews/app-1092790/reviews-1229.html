Upon starting up, I instantly got Pony Island vibes (it turns out it was made by the same guy) except this game is so much more. More than a simple rogue-like card battling game (though there's a mod for that), Inscryption incorporates characters, horror elements, escape-room puzzles, and a mysterious backstory that will keep you hooked. And if you're worried about the challenge associated with mastering rogue-like games and don't know if you can actually win in order to beat the story, there are a number of sneaky puzzles and mechanics that can be solved or exploited to get the best of your shadowy opponent.

Definitely a worthwhile game if you have any love for card games and horror.\t\t\t\t\t
