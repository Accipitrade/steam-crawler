\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn general, I found this game and the runs I got within it to be more frustrating than enjoyable. For reference, I have played a lot of Slay the Spire, Monster Train, and other deck builder "rogue like" style games and generally enjoyed them very much.

After ~4 hours, several puzzles solved, lots of card building, and a few wins... I just don't see myself enjoying this for much longer.

The Good:
- When you lose you get to create your own custom cards each time. This allows you to create some insanely powerful cards that completely unbalance the game and win you runs easy!
- The game has a progressing story and "escape room" style feel... and I LOVE escape rooms


The Bad:
- The custom cards you get to create are difficult to actually get into your deck (pretty rare chance to gain access to them). They are often very unbalanced, but the fact that you can barely engage with this mechanic makes it feel bad until the rare run where you get 1-2 custom cards.
- The graphics are done well, for what they are, but honestly the highly distorted pixel style just wasn't for me
- I wasn't able to (almost) ever create an actual build or theme for my runs. There are different types of animals or sets that go together and I only ever got a mix of all of them vs being able to focus on synergy. Compare this to Monster Train, where I feel I have a LOT of control in how my run(s) build out.\t\t\t\t\t
