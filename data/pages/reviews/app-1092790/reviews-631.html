\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn all time great indie game that blends card building with excellent story telling and world building to become one of the most memorable gaming experiences on the market. What is shown on the few screenshots from the store page is incredibly misleading, but in a *very* good way. There is so much depth and creativity behind the mechanics of this game's card systems and its battles that I can't even fathom how the developers came up with some of these ideas. Just as fun as the card fights are the bits of lore and story the game weaves into it. Don't read too much on this game. Honestly for its price point I'd even go so far as to call it a steal. It's one of those experiences where the less you know the better it is. Similar to things such as Undertale, Doki Doki Literature Club, and The Outer Wilds.

Inscryption is incredible and I highly recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t

In addition to being a savagely good core gameplay mechanic, there's an insidious and far deeper meta to the game that has twists and turns that will surprise you. There are puzzles beyond the basic gameplay that provides a neat story that carefully ties in all aspects of the game, and I found it very entertaining and well done.

The gameplay is fantastic, the hidden story elements that become major plot points offer meaningful surprises as you progress, and a clear devotion and dedication to making a great game all shine through - it's a work of love and worthy of note for almost that alone.

I would 100% recommend this to anyone - go in blind and be pleasantly surprised. Avoid spoilers and embrace the mystery.\t\t\t\t\t
