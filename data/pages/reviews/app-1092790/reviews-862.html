\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnd here I thought these guys peaked with Pony Island

Inscryption is a card game with horror elements and a story about a haunted floppy disk with sentient video game characters on it. Nothing that we on the internet haven't seen before through those ♥♥♥♥♥♥ creepypastas that we religiously read as children, but Inscyption serparates itself from that dumpster fire by actually being creepy.

On top of that it's also a genuinely fun card game with roguelike elements and challenging gameplay that has more layers to it than Magic or Yugioh could ever hope to have. At least Leshy's version does. P03's version of the game is much more difficult because you have to rely on the stupid energy mechanics as well as Mox cards. If I wanted to play resource management I'd just play Magic, and anything that makes me want to play Magic must die. So it's a good thing P03 DID![spoiler/]

If you liked Pony Island, you'll definitely love this. It's pretty much the same thing except much shorter and more focused on gameplay.

So what are you waiting for? That Floppy Disk isn't gonna play itself.\t\t\t\t\t
