\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI went into this game not knowing much about it. It was described to me as a "puzzle game with a card game in it" which was most definitely not the case. It's a card game that has some puzzle aspects to it. It's actually very fun! Frustrating for sure, as a lot of act 1 is totally luck based, and I died a LOT, but that also meant I had a lot of OP death cards available to me. The characters are a interesting and complex. I like them as characters a lot even if I definitely wouldn't like them as people. This game does have ARG aspects to it as well, so if you're not into that kind of thing, you may not get the full experience or understand the ending much. That being said, it's a great game in and of itself, and I enjoyed playing it and plan to replay it. As frustrating as it could be, it was also very relaxing and easy to get immersed in.

Also, for first time players, don't freak out if you choose the wrong thing or merge the wrong cards. It's all about experimenting and you'll probably get to start over again anyway and everything (in the card game) resets.

All in all, a great game that I very much enjoyed playing. Very difficult to sell it to my friends though without spoiling anything as "trapped in a cabin with some guy who wants to play a card game with you" makes them all ask me if it's based on Yu-Gi-Oh.\t\t\t\t\t
