This game is amazing but after 3 hours of beating the first ACT 1 boss, I thought the game was over as it brought me back to the beginning after that cutscene change and I thought the game was just like Hades and the whole game was just working harder each time until you actually defeat the boss! I stopped playing the game thinking it was quite a fun Inde game but was surprised by the little play time until my friend told me there were still act 2 and 3 and that I just missed finding the *DEVELOPER PLEASE NOTICE THIS PART* 3rd wolf card and the battery. It was not obvious after beating the boss the second time around that I needed to find the battery and search the cabin a little bit harder. It would be nice to have an update after the 2nd kill where the voice in your head gives you a small hint or something that you need to have the battery when defeating the boss to continue the story in some way or another. (For those of us not as bright to listen to the talking cards) Because I spoke to 5 of my other friends who only had 4 hours on the game and they all told me the exact same thing, wait that's not the ending!?!?! They all semi completed act 1 and thought that was the end of it without giving a second thought that there might be more to the game! Other then that, I really enjoyed the little twist at the end and I can say the mechanics for ACT 1/3 were my favorite and I know act 2 had more diversity but for some reason I just enjoyed the way the cards were played in act 1 and 3. First act 10/10 2nd act 9/10 3rd act 10/10!

Can't wait for a 2nd game and looking forward to more games coming from this developer as this has put my expectations up really high!\t\t\t\t\t
