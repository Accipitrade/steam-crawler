\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn incredible rogue like deck builder and rpg. This is the first time I have liked a game in this genre even more than Slay the Spire.

It has an amazing, eerie, meta, and existential aesthetic with a fascinating story. It has the vibes of if you mashed STPs mechanical genius, sense of mystery, and secrets, with Undertale's Meta quirks, and Darkest Dungeons horror together.

Mechanically it starts out much like slay the spire with a fun and simple set of combat rules that will continue to throw fun twists at you, and than it mixes in mystery click adventure and rpg elements as well. I don't want to get to much more into detail due to not wanting to give anything away.

Writing this upon finishing the campaign portion of the game, excited to dive back into the rest of its secrets and post game content. I enjoyed my first play through largely blind, but it looks like it will be fun to do with a guide to. Not sure if it will have as much replay value as STP or Monster Train, but I definitely enjoyed my short time with it so far even more than I did those.\t\t\t\t\t
