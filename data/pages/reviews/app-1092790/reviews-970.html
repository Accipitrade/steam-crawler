\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have not yet written a game review in my 31 years as a gamer, but I will for this game. Out of many of the games in my Steam library, I have never felt so compelled, so driven to finish a game and a story as I have this one. I didn't expect the beginning to hook me, heck- I'm not even a fan of card games really, but something pulled me to finish this game, and it's disconcerting that I cannot put into words what exactly that is. All I can say is this is a game worth playing. A masterpiece? Potentially. There are campy moments and times when the game feels unbalanced, you will face futility at points when you feel you have won and then discover, you certainly have not won. This game gives a wide range of emotions, and I cannot say that for many games. The story is truly something that makes me think, and I hope it will make you think.\t\t\t\t\t
