\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-- Written before Kaycee's Mod --

This game does a lot of things at the same time.
And all of them are great on their own BUT they are kinda holding each other back.

The card game, rougelike elements and deck-building are all amazing, but the game is too easy.
I played a demo a lot and after that I was able to completely break the game's balance.

The ARG and lore is fantastic, but not everyone will be willing to play through a very specific kind of game.

-- After Kaycee's Mod --

I wasn't expecting to come back to this review, but Kaycee's Mod fixed all my problems with the game.
Having access to Act I as a full-fledged card-building rougelike that is actually fairly challenging and smaller focus on story and lore, allowing it to fully realize it's gameplay potential has me hooked on this game again.

-- Summary --

If your expecting a challenging card-building rogelike, play this game for the Kaycee's Mod.
If your interested in the story You will still be satisfied by the main game.

Either way I highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
