The closest thing I can compare the game-play to is a rogue-like/GWENT/MTG kind of experience. If you enjoy deck building type's of gmaes, you'll fit right in here. The story was a bit confusing at first for me but it quickly ramped up and played out beautifully. They call it a 'horror' game but I didn't really feel it deserved that title. More suspenseful than horrifying or scary.

Here's my tip: Play the game blind, accept the mistakes you will make, learn from them and most of all have fun.

9/10 The ending left me wanting more from it.\t\t\t\t\t
