\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPhenomenal game. This game is great from the superb gameplay to the in-depth story, with many surprises, and subversions of what is to be expected. That's not even to mention the amazing look and atmosphere of the, through your time in the cabin there is a lingering feeling of unease as you battle against the enshrouded man in front of you. The gameplay and structure of runs in this game are well designed, almost allowing you to break it in ways that feel intended rather than bugs. Its super satiating clear encounters through the good decision making and a bit of luck in your draws. There are so many possible "decks" that you can make with the cards you choose along the way and so many possible strategies to utilize. The puzzle-solving is possibly the most engaging part, in which you explore around the cabin to unlock new cards items and more for use in your runs. The story is pretty great as well, with it not being forced in your face every chance the game gets. Instead, you are slowly fed bits of story through the various acts that reveal the truth about the game and its makers. Oh and uh don't be deceived there is so much more to this game than the store page would lead you to believe there is truly something up with this card game. Overall I cannot vouch for enough for this game Daniel has done a more than a perfect job on this game, and that's not even to mention the free endless mode DLC that he created solely from the request of his players. This game was on my radar since the demo for the game dropped and had me enthralled instantly, and I'm glad to say that it in no way disappointed me. This is all coming from someone who really doesn't like card games either, as I have never played hearthstone or anything similar although I do find slay the spire slightly enjoyably. All in all this game is amazing, it has perfect gameplay, pristine puzzles for you to solve, and the nonintrusive story behind all of it. I really think that nearly anyone can enjoy this game even if they don't like card games or rogue-lite. I give it a solid 10/10 :)\t\t\t\t\t
