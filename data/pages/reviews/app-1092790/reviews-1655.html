\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a deck building game that has a lot going for it. The randomization, the story, but most of all the replay ability. With the new beta feature allowing you to replay the first sequence with added challenges it draws you back in. For $20 this game is a fun and enjoyable experience. The mechanics are fair however you can set yourself up with some unbeatable decks. Props to the game developer and I hope they continue to make more games with the well thought out story and gameplay that this masterpiece has. I would also like to add that I am not someone who enjoys card games in video games much. I saw this game being played on Youtube and was sucked in by the story. However due to how well this game plays I fell in love with the deck building style. I wasn't even aware there was a demo for this game however seeing it now as I write this review I must say at the very least give the game a try. Its honestly a good time.\t\t\t\t\t
