\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOhh... where should I start?

I wasn't expecting anything more than just a "good card game" when I launched Inscryption.
This feeling stayed with me as I watched various streams of the game from time to time.
But during my first run, I quickly realized how wrong I was.


If I had to sum up what I think about Inscryption in just a few words, I would say:
Inscryption is a perfect card game with a well-written story (that constantly surprises) and multitude of puzzles to solve. All of it amplified by an eerie atmosphere and music that sounds like it's being performed during some kind of ancient rituals.

It was such a pleasure to go through this game but even after completing it, I still haven't uncovered all the secrets it holds beacause the lore of "Inscryption" is so vast that it extends far beyond the game itself.

Although the story itself is easy to beat, there is also a mode (unlocked after completing main story) for more demanding players called Kaycee Mod, which significantly increases the difficulty level by throwing tougher set of challenges at the player.


If you even slightly enjoy card games, you should definitely give Inscryption a try.\t\t\t\t\t
