\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDAMN this game came out of left field and blew everything else out of the water. This was easily my favorite game of the year.

Music? Killer. Art? Amazing! Plot? Cool AF. I can't gush about this game enough.

This is a game you will play over, and over, and over. The trading card aspect is easy to pick up, and fun af to break with cards like Ouroboros. The game wants you to cheat a little and see how OP you can make things. Just as you start to get comfortable, the format gets shaken up just enough to present a new challenge and keep you on your toes. It's also no secret that it's supposed to be hard - a running plot thread is the main characters being narcissitic cheaters. This adds an extra level of satisfaction when you kick their a**. This game is addictive, and engages the nerdiest and most competitive parts of my brain simultaneously.

The game also has a really interesting style - somehow it successfully switches between TCG, old school fantasy adventure, found footage, and even survival horror. It never feels disjointed or jarring, and does a great job balancing all the moving parts. It's something I rarely see accomplished so successfully.

As a pixel artist myself, this game scratched an itch that you rarely get to have satisfied. Generally speaking, in both art and music, this games straddles the line between lo-fi and modern in a way that feels fresh and exciting, as opposed to derivative like some other games that use vintage-inspired aesthetics.

The characters are charming and you will be a little bit sad when it's all over. I find myself prolonging each stage of the game each time I play it so I can spend more time with them. They each have a lot of personality, and none of them feel generic. You are interested to learn more about each one, and you feel real emotions when you're interacting with them. It's rare that I get this engaged with game characters, so when I do, it stands out.

The game also has some mind-blowing easter eggs with a great community element to them - it's great to see a situation where the creator encourages people to work together to dig up clues and crack codes, and you can tell the developer loves his fan community.

I don't want to make this too long, so if nothing else, your takeaway should be this: This is a truly unique game with wonderful characters and worldbuilding, a great concept, and a true challenge that is extremely satisfying to overcome. You're missing out if you don't play it.\t\t\t\t\t
