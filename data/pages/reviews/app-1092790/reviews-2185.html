1. See new game on steam
2. See "Overwhelmingly Positive" Reviews
3. Think hmmm...I like card battlers....I like roguelikes
4. Think - Devolver Digital does publish some cool 'outside the box' games
5. Pull trigger on purchase
6. First Hour - Ok this has kinda a creepy vibe to it, but I don't really get the hype....it seems very vanilla
7. Second Hour - I've died a couple times, I don't really get what this game is about or why it's reviewed so well
8. 2.5 hours in - I think I'm getting the hang of the card mechanics - ok, got that 'one more run' itch
9. 3 hours in - making some progress, ok Boss mechanics are cool
10. 3.5 hours in - ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ - I GET IT NOW! I AM A BELIEVER!
11. Multiple "Sixth Sense" Uno Reversals to my Brain occur (but not in the way that you think)
12. Finish first playthrough and immediately start a new one
13. Pray that Daniel Mullins expands on this universe

THIS IS A LABOR OF LOVE.

In a time of broken game releases with multi-million dollar budgets, this reminds you of all the great experiences you had playing video games growing up as a child.

THE HYPE IS REAL.\t\t\t\t\t
