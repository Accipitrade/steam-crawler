DISCLAIMER: REVIEW CONTAINS SLIGHT SPOILERS!

I bought this game exclusively because of Leshy. Leshy's just awesome. Everything, from the atmosphere of his cabin to the cards' art style scratched the horror itch I've been seeking for so long. Although the card game isn't balanced, the mechanics are an absolute blast to mess around with. There's a fair bit of variation and strategy in Leshy's game that helps keep things feeling fresh and exciting. However...

Your enjoyment of the game may vary, depending on how you feel about the game's story. Truth is, Leshy's cabin only takes up one portion of the game. After you beat him, you'll be transported to areas with completely different settings and atmosphere. Unfortunately, I struggled to maintain interest in the story mode because the changes weren't my thing. If you enjoy ARGs and fourth wall-breaking narratives, then I think you'll get a much bigger kick out of the story!

That said! If you're like me and just wanna chill with Leshy, then I HIGHLY recommend playing Kaycee's Mod! It's a free, official expansion that turns Leshy's cabin into an endless rouge-like. Normally you have to beat the story mode to unlock it, however you can access it earlier if you're playing on PC. The expansion includes optional challenges and unlockables, as well as slight variation between runs (ex. the order of the boss fights are randomized). As of writing this review, most of my hours have been spent playing this specific mode.

It's a cool game!\t\t\t\t\t
