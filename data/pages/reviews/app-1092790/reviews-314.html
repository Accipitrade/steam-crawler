\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is better experienced blind so all I can say spoiler free is:

Great card game Mechanics, sometimes a little convoluted but still great fun. Spooky atmosphere, the sound design/music/effects are designed to make you uneasy which were 100% on point, definitely one of it's strongest aspects alongside it's artstyle.

But if you're expecting a fully rogue-like card game, this is not entirely it. The game's trailer is somewhat deceiving.
The game keeps changing.

Major Spoilers Below:
Act 2 made an harsh shift from an Original Artstyle and vibe to a boring 2d Pokemon Trading Card game(Gameboy) knock off which made it kind of hard to read anything, also added bunch of mechanics that you need to figure out how they work by trial and error if you choose the "wrong" starter pack.

The Acting was meh at best, the IRL writing was weak and felt crammed in, wasn't believable and felt random. Simulacra did a much better job(still a little cringe at times though). The finalle build up was feeling pretty awesome until the final scene completely blew it.
The game breaks the 4th wall and doesn't at the same time, which was jarring. You're playing through the eyes of the actor but at the same time your own steam friends show up on boss battle. Also, Luke(the Actor) randomly emotes some lines when something out of the ordinary happens, kept knocking me off the immersion.
The finalle build up was feeling pretty intense until you get pulled out from it for one of the weakest final scenes I'ver ever seen.

In short it had potential to be much better. Still a good game.
7/10\t\t\t\t\t
