Undertale takes bullet hells and makes them easy and pretty relaxing. Inscryption takes deck builders, a genre that's usually meditative, and turns them into nail-bitingly brutal crucibles. Undertale uses retro art styles to draw you into an inviting and colorful world. Inscryption uses a retro art style to make its grotesque and inhumane world even more alienating.

Undertale is a story about learning to see the best in fundamentally good people who sometimes make mistakes. Inscryption is a story about learning to acknowledge that you're surrounded by fundamentally bad people and how even their acts of kindness are ultimately motivated by selfishness.

And when the game makes these misanthropic bastards suffer, it breaks your heart to see it. Because like Undertale, Inscryption has the balls to ask you to be their friend in spite of everything.\t\t\t\t\t
