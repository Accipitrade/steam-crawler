Inscryption does exactly what a great indie game should: it subverts expectations, turns on its head at the most unexpected moments, and expands the boundaries of the genre and of video games as a form of art. You don't have to love it, but give it a spin nonetheless, at least for the sake of being able to say that you've played it. Inscryption is certainly worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
