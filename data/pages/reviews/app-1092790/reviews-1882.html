\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game did so much. It was fun. Never felt bored, even when RNG ruined my runs. Also threw me back right to 1999 playing Pokemon TCG on the gameboy and building decks. The most fun part is learning to break the game, because it allows for a good amount of experimentation and creativity. It also introduces and throws away mechanics for the sake of narrative and I think that's something to be made aware of, although I personally loved it a lot. At the same time the ride was so fun, I'd almost wish for ways to explore or play more with its later core mechanics.
And game design aside; the characters, writing and presentation were an absolute treat.\t\t\t\t\t
