\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI didn't know what to expect when I installed this game for the first time. I purchased it over a year ago, I can't remember why, but since Halloween was coming up, I figured I would try this game out to get into the mood. So happy I did! When I first started playing, the first thing I thought was it was a simplified version of Yu-Gi-Oh, and the more I played it, the more I fell in love with the game. It is very similar to Yu-Gi-Oh, and considering the ending, I don't think the game is hiding that, but this is definitely a good thing. I advise going through the game once normally, and then checking out the mods, which are extremely easy to install. Honestly, I think the modding community makes this game a real treat.
I will warn, that the game does not only take place in a cabin, which surprised me. The game has four different acts. I won’t give it away, but the game changes drastically from the first act, style and everything. I wasn’t really pleased with this at first, but if you give it a chance, you will find a lot of enjoyment in it.
Just as a side note, I noticed in the description and other reviews people mention this has “escape room elements,” I don’t see that in this game. There really isn’t much you’re trying to escape from, other than traversing through the game’s story, which doesn’t really play a massive role, at least not for me. There are things to explore, but none of it is mandatory, and most of it is very minor. The meat of the game is in the different card games.
I kind of feel the game could had been longer, and maybe they planned for it, but then decided to cut it short. This is where the mods come in to improve on where the game falls short. Honestly, recently, I haven’t been able to get enough.
Anyways, if you want to enjoy a quick, and easy version of Yu-Gi-Oh, or a simple card game in general, with rouge-style like elements, this is an excellent game to check out!\t\t\t\t\t
