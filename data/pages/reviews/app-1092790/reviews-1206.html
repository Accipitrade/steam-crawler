\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnfortunately I cannot recommend this game. It has fascinating "story" and genuinely unexpected twists which are great, however the actual card-based gameplay is just bad in my opinion. I was bored with the gameplay after a couple of hours, and I really love card games. Slay the Spire and MtG are among my all-time favorite games. Here, I had to slough through the card game because I was really fascinated by the plot. There is a ton of different card gameplay mechanisms in the game, but most of them seem either not balanced at all, or just half-baked. There is little challenge here, especially in the later parts of the game.
If you are interested in a game that is basically a great short story, this might be for you. But if you are looking for a card game, I'd say just play some more runs of Spire instead.\t\t\t\t\t
