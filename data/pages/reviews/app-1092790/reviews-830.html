\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun card game with a creepy atmosphere, but I don't recommend it as a whole because of the lackluster, over-hyped narrative. Plus in the second act of the game, that well-done atmosphere from before is completely gone with a tonal shift that will give you whiplash. Thankfully act 3 gets back to the good stuff, but act 2 made me want to quit the game. What's more, you won't even be able to understand much of the plot without delving into the developer's other works and the Inscryption ARG. The plot is just a jumbled, uninteresting mess if you play this game on its own.

Funnily enough I only wanted to try Inscryption because I was excited for the going-in-blind experience, but in the end the surprises were a disappointment and I just wanted to play more of the card game.

My Rating: 2/5
