\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere do I even start? The game is a very elaborate and fun card based expedition where you play very entertaining and unique fights. If you plan to play this game, avoid spoilers at all cost.

The constantly evolving gameplay was a breath of fresh air, and kept me hooked for hours. The story was interesting, and found myself perplexed on to what was going on.

If there was/will be DLC or any sort of expansion, I will 100% be first in line for it

This was a great game and a fantastic experience. The only thing I would ask for is to have some sort of endless mode to see how far you can get.

This game was a great card game and great adventure game, and one of the few pieces of media where I tried avoiding spoilers, which is saying something.

Do yourself a favour, and buy this game.\t\t\t\t\t
