Its up there with Undertale for me in terms of pure creativity with respect to both the story telling and gameplay. This game never stops introducing new twists, new mechanics and is overflowing with good ideas.

Also word of caution, please do not look up anything about this game. Theres too much to ruin and you'll miss out on so many amazing moments.\t\t\t\t\t
