\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption, just like its role in the story, seems like your normal game stepping out of the comfort zone in a card game format. However, this game not only steps out of the comfort zone, but all zones in general, and creates the most unique experience I've ever had as someone who religiously plays turn-based card games.

Every time you've think you've expected what there is to expect out of this game, it pulls the rug out from under your legs and sends you back to thinking on your feet again. Its not only immersive, but its story has that interesting ARG hook to pull you in while its setting and puzzle-solving portions are a breath of fresh air after you've spent a good 4 hour chunk fighting for your life against bee hives and shark flip cards.

If you want not only a grounded card game with good mechanics, but a good time and genuinely cool experience, this game is right for you.\t\t\t\t\t
