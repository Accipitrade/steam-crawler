\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is by no means perfect, but it is still a good game.
The real issue of the game is that the devs decided to sacrifice the entertaining parts of the game for the sake of the story of the game.
Game is set in 3 acts and a prologue.
The Act 1 is basically the part where everyone falls in love with the game and act 2 is where everyone hates the game and act 3 is where everyone is just wishing they were playing act 1 again.
So in general the game really falls off the trail by the act 2 and doesn't get any better really.
The story which is a meta story like a bunch of other indie darlings is not bad or good. But it is not imo compelling enough.
the game has a bunch of characters and while it tries to by the end of the game make you feel attached to them none really stand out and feel important except the act one character.
Sound effects and music are all nice.
Visuals vary from awesome and clever design to boring 8 bit gfx to eye gauging annoying.
All in all the act one is so strong I recommend the game based on that alone.
