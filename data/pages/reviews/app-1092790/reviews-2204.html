If you like puzzle games, play this game. Behind Outer Wilds, this is definitely one of the best puzzle games ever made. It oozes with passion and creativity, bends and grows with its own rules as you slowly crawl deeper and deeper. The genre is definitely horror, but (as someone who is terrible at horror) the horror is kept away from jumpscares, but rather moody atmospherics and horrifying thoughts; the horror, in a lot of ways, comes from your own imagination and processing of the events of the game.

In short, this game is a masterpiece, and the more blind you can go in the better off you will be. At first, it may seem too difficult mechanically or too robust to ever get around, but I assure you that 14 hours later you will be left with an experience you will never forget.

Buy this game. Just do it. It is a shame if you do not at least try this game.\t\t\t\t\t
