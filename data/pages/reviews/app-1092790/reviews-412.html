One of the best game experiences ever!

This is one of the best games I've ever played, if you can play this game without having a SINGLE clue about it just DO IT. Inscryption enters in the realm of games you should play with 0 spoilers, just like Outer Wilds, Disco Elysium, Doki Doki and many others.

I bought this game because I saw like 1 minute of a stream and was like, "Why is this card game so eerie, I love it, I'm buying it now" and I can assure you will not regret doing so.

This is the perfect experience you can ask for a card game, without going into too much details, the story is just TOP TIER, the mystery around it is really incredible. The soundtrack is JUST PERFECT, there are songs that are just ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ incredible. The gameplay is really enjoyable. The game replay-ability is good too, you end up mastering the card game, that is so well designed, I love every single piece of it, the characters, the environment, the development of everything featured in the game.

And the rest, really you can only understand it by playing, it is an experience, not just a game, you are immersed into a world, just so imaginative, so fascinating.

The only part I might say is a real bother, is the Completionist and fully optional Kaycee's Mod , but to be fair it is designed to be this way, differing from the base game, you have to grind it to the point of mastery, and then you may attempt the Master achievement (Spent almost 28 hours on just this part of the game).

In all, this is the perfect Story Card Game 10/10.\t\t\t\t\t
