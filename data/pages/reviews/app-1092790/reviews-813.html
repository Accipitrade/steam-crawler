\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell.. the game is a kinda a scam.
From the marketing material and playing the demo i got the impression that this was going to be a card based roguelike with a story that unfolds as you play it.
The end result wasn't quite it.

The gameplay is just a vessel for the plot. It is interesting but not meant to last for more than a couple of hours. It took me about 10 hours to finish the story.
Also the theme and gameplay elements change a lot as you progress the story. It makes the story more interesting but gameplay wise i didn't like it.
The story was ok but nothing special. I got most enjoyment from the act 1 of the story (the initial cabin).



Recently the game got an official mod/mini expansion. It addressed the community demand for more refined act 1, something that i also had hoped for.
The mod feels like one step forward one back. It expands the game with more cards and a challenge system.
The good thing is that now the act 1 is an independent game without the story aspects of the main game.
The downsides are more numerous.

1. The mod got rid of some of the most interesting aspects of the act 1. death cards and preparation for the act 1 final boss, there are no boons in the mod
2. The mod has some balance changes i don't agree with
3. The challenge system is mandatory for progression in the mod (more cards and story elements), it wouldn't be an issue if it was implemented better, currently most challenges are more annoying than fun
4. The mod is grindy. You need to finish the mod 10+ times in order to complete it and it just gets more annoying with added challenges.
5. The mod can't do miracles. The initial implementation of the act 1 was imperfect (it wasn't meant to last more than couple of hours) your runs are somewhat rng dependent even more so with many challenges active. The mod doesn't add more maps or bosses, that is fine but hurts the replayability of the mod.

Overall i have mixed feelings. Some elements were very fun, especially when playing for the first time. On some fronts the game was annoying and disappointing.
I feel like the act 1 of the game had wasted potential that the expansion wasn't able to harness. The act 1 was also the reason i bought the game and i feel disappointed that the game wasn't what i had been lead to believe.\t\t\t\t\t
