Act 1 was fun, and had it continued beyond the 4 hour mark I would've recommended it to any and all people that looked for a fun card-game to pass the time. Complete with secrets, a dark and gritty story, and methodical pacing... And come Act 2 it all turns to utter crap. Everything is removed for a much more simplified version of itself. The graphics turn into an 8-bit RPG-maker wannabe, the talking cards removed, the random stuff like sacrificing cards for powerups, or merging cards together, or trading sigils, all gone. All the secrets disappear to be replaced with you slowly wandering around a 2D plane to slowly play a much worse version of Act 1's card system.

Act 1 was a blast. But unless you want to spend full price on just one quarter of the game I suggest you run away, because that's the best part and it gets seriously WORSE from there. To the devs, I really loved the first Act. But once I got to Act 2, grabbed the undead deck and then slammed my head against a brick wall of two bosses and their stupid re-carded counterparts I went from enjoying myself to wishing I could refund the game.


***SPOILERS OVER***
TL;DR- First 4 hours was fun, after that was AWFUL. Like I'd rather player Battlefield 2042 levels of awful.\t\t\t\t\t
