\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is visual outstanding and absolutely a joy to play. I have a problem when it comes to rougelikes, and it seems I cant get enough out of the genre as a whole. This also combines my love for card games and rougelikes. Its simple and no overbearing mechanics and no complex elements (there are puzzles but it takes your knowledge of the game to figure it out). I do hate the "IRL" scenes a lot and its quite jarring in my opinion. I'm not sure how they would have worked around it, but I do see why they did it. You can skip these scenes and It's optional so I'm not truly upset by it. If I was forced to watch it then I would have a problem. It can feel unfair but later on in the game you can literally win in one turn if you play your cards right. I just hope that you enjoy this game and at most play the demo. I can't recommend it enough. This game isn't perfect but no game is.

There rest of my review will just be spoilers just a heads up.

Act 1 is phenomenal and it starts out very strong. I can see how it wouldnt hold up if that was the whole game, so thats why there are 3 acts. I wish that there were different areas for each Scrybe like how they have it for Leshy and P03 . There could have been puzzles for each Scrybe unique to them, and we also do see how that would kinda work in act 2. There is a lot of room for lore are exploration of this digital world.


Act 2 is still pretty good and explores the other Scrybes domains. All Scybes have their own types of decks and I love them. Each one has their own strengths and weaknesses, but the best thing is you're not tied to one deck in this act. You are free to build as you please. Balancing problems are prominent but hey its technically an old card game so that's normal in nature. You can create a deck that can destroy anything, and it feels cheaty. Each area contain their own set of puzzles and riddles. Be it I hate P03's section a lot. The battery puzzles sucks start feeling absolutely tedious there is just too many of them. This is while we are still in act 2 before we transition into act 3. P03 is meant to be hated and god do I hate them.

Act 3 pretty much plays like Act 1. P03 doesn't care about you or create an enticing story like Leshy. Leshy is a DM champ and P03 is just here to mess with you. Just like Act 1 you can get up, explore, find puzzles, ect. I will not be going into endings for the sake of people who did read this despite a spoiler warning. Also P03 got upset because of his fursona.\t\t\t\t\t
