\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all - This game is AMAZING!

I love everything about it. I didn't play ''The Hex'' or ''Pony Island'' (I've seen couple of videos on YouTube from ''Pony Island'' tho and this game looked awesome, so I bought it too while buying Inscryption) and for everyone, who likes games like ''Slay the Spire'' or such, this is something for you. The game isn't that much about horror, but it sometimes really gave me the shivers, for (in Germany) 18 Euros you can't do anything wrong with that, the acting of the main protagonist is awesome and everything is really well made. Each character really feels different and they really give you hundreds of options to hate them. Or to feel bad for them. That could also be. :D I really enjoyed playing it, sometimes the battles were a little difficult because of bad luck while drawing cards, but that didn't hold on too long, so everything is fine with that.

I give this game a 9,5 /10 - Just because of the card drawing luck you sometimes need to win card battles. Everything else is just wonderful. Keep going on making these wonderful games, Daniel Mullins, you are awesome! <3

And for everyone else, who have read this so far: I wish you the best and have a wonderful day! <3\t\t\t\t\t

But this narrative gets interrupted sporadically, and severely undercut in the end, by a found footage conspiracy thriller about... Wizards of the Coast? Its strange.

But all in all, the deck building puzzles are intriguing and open ended with a lot of room for self expression. The morbid tone and atmosphere of the game has a tendency to become disarmingly cute on a whim. Its a great game to jump into blind.\t\t\t\t\t
