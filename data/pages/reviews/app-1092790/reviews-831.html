\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow. I've never reviewed a game on Steam before, but I just beat this today and wanted to show some love.

Just when you are getting comfortable with the core system of the game, it pulls the rug out from under you in such a great way, and does so more than once. The multiple curveballs that the game throws at you feel imaginative and creative. I couldn't help but smile.

This game totally exceeded my expectations, and is so much more than just another deck-builder. You can really tell how much heart went into making this, and how much the developers care about games in general. All the small details and homages to card games and video games as a whole are part of what makes this game special.

While the overarching narrative could feel cheesy at times, by the end I was sad that it was over.

Don't research too much if you're thinking of buying. Going in completely blind is definitely the best method. If you are looking for something unique and refreshing in a short but very worthwhile experience, I'd highly recommend picking this one up.\t\t\t\t\t
