\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShort Review: Just buy it

Long Review: Roguelike Cardgame with impossibly simple mechanics but they build on each other to form complex interactions. Same with card effects; deceptively simple but the game doesn't shy away from letting you combine them into singular cards which in almost every case ends in a card more than thrice as strong as the sum of it's components. Seriously, most Keywords have really interesting or strong synergies with each other and there's a ton of things to experiment with. Inscryption is a game that isn't afraid to let creative players get overpowered, you are rewarded for thinking up insane combos every single time.
The story wasn't my cup of tea, a bit dragged out but the flavour and atmosphere more than make up for it. Gives me old time DnD vibes. The narrators descriptions add a whole lot to the feeling of the game and you are instantly immersed.

Lastly the game has a TON of secrets, in and out of game and it's really fun to find them. For once the ARG part of a game is actually solvable by a single person except for one specific part.

Lastly, if the game is over but you yearn for more there is an official beta named Kaycees Mod with tons of extra cards, challenges, mechanics and secrets that throws the story out of the window in favor of extra depth.

9.7/10\t\t\t\t\t
