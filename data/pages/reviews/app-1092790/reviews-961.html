\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMediocre and predictable story. I hoped Kaycee's mod would make it more like a regular deck-builder, but it's terribly balanced at the moment.
The way the game works, you get more and more bad cards, since there is no way to skip cards and there are very few possibilities to remove cards. The goal of a deck-builder should be building a deck, and not "having a good deck in the beginning and then trying desparately to not let it get clogged up by all the crap cards the game keeps shoving in your face".
I would also like to point out that the game punishes you for giving up on a run by taking away your options at the beginning of the next run. I think this is completely unnecessary, since I could also just go into a battle and lose on purpose with the same result without the penalty. The difference is that giving up is just faster. The penalty is just annoying and a waste of time.\t\t\t\t\t
