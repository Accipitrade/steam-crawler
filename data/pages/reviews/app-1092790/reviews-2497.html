As someone who plays games in the multitude of genres that are contained within this single game, the deck building mechanics were the most interesting. The other genres, most notably the escape room aspects, were fairly basic executions of the form.

The second half of this game felt like a chore, and I didn’t latch onto the meta narrative. The final scene was a bit too heavy handed for my taste. Still I played the whole thing in a day because I had to see where it all was going.

The price as listed seems higher than I am comfortable to recommend to everyone, but I think it’s not too high.\t\t\t\t\t
