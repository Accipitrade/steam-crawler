\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll start this review by simply stating that the best bit about the game is exploring it and it's twists and turns yourself, without any preconceptions from other people's opinions... However. $23 NZD is kinda a lot for someone to take on faith for an indie game so its understandable that some people will need more assurances.

Inscryption is a deckbuilding card game, It also has a lot of imagination and prizes atmosphere over gameplay balance occasionally, meaning that players who go into it who want a truly balanced and competitive cardbuilding experience like Slay The Spire, will be upset by this. But I personally think that Inscryption's value comes outside of it's pure gameplay depth or balance but more as an engaging single player experience overall.

I personally love Inscryption, and I invite the hesitant player to love it too, I sincerely doubt you'll regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
