\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game on sale on Wednesday, it's Saturday as I write this and I've basically been playing nonstop. Just finished it. Amazing experience all the way through. I mean phenomenal.

From an aesthetic perspective, this game is beautiful. The 3D graphics are stunning, as well as the 2D. The music and sound design as well. The tone of the game is definitely dictated by the gorgeous sound design. All the people you meet feel solid and distinct, though there's very little true "voice acting". The colors are warm and vibrant, but also distinctly unsettling.

The gameplay is riveting and fun as well. I like that this game is creepy without being scary, if that makes sense. It is unnerving and unsettling. It also gives off the vibe of the old one-room escape room flash games I played as a kid. I'm not usually one for this style of card game--I've never played MtG or Hearthstone--but I found it immensely fun and rewarding. It's easy to mess up, but it's also easy to learn and do well, so it's balanced well.

The story arc was genuinely moving and I was always wondering what would happen next. Won't say much here for spoiler purposes, but let me just say. A bit funny at the beginning, but definitely gets fleshed out and was just genuinely so good.

10/10, I'm so glad I never caved and watched someone play this. Going into this blind was one of the best decisions I've ever made.\t\t\t\t\t
