\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game had a whole hell of a lot of hype when it came out, and a lot of my friends were playing it, telling me it was great and I do remember picking it up around then, but I didn't touch it until recently, so I was kinda removed from the whole train surrounding it. I'm not exactly very good at card games, or anything involving logic honestly, but this game started real promising, it was simple enough to get into, though frustrating considering it's not a fair playing field, and I had a lot of fun as I beat the first part, I kept going back to it while I was in bed on my Deck.
Then I hit the 2nd part, and it just kinda removed all stakes and introduced some new mechanics. There's no random elements besides card packs, and if you're not really interested in experimenting you'll soon realise that the 2 new decks, and even the bone deck are just not good compared to the normal deck, require too much set up to mess with outside of the gimmick cards (shout out to the random damage bot who seemed to always roll 6), I got lucky and had a great first few packs and was set, sure I lost fights here and there but what does it matter? You can just bash your head against them until you win or die trying. The game also tried more story based stuff in this second part, exploring the lore, and I kinda just couldn't get myself to care? It felt very bare to me, maybe I'm stupid.
Then came the 3rd part, and oh boy, it's where I lost absolutely all interest and motivation to play the game and just gave up, if you're not having fun why bother playing? It's like a mix of part 1 and 2, a lot more battles in a shorter timespan, new mechanics and such, but the amount of battles is over the top and sometimes you just lose for not placing a specific card in a specific spot, or so it feels. It just dragged on so long and I get no joy out of banging my head against enemies till I win.

Overall the game does have fun parts, but that third part just kills the whole thing for me. I know there's a like, mod, or gamemode that extends the whole first part and adds new stuff but I'm so burnt out after the second and third part I can't even will myself to give it a try. If it's on sale and you like card games? Or just cute macabre drawings of little critters then sure, pick it up, maybe even pick it up for some good possum representation, but it wasn't for me.\t\t\t\t\t
