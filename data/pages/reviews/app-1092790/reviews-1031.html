\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic game! Easily worth more than the ~$20 dollar price tag. If you love any kind of TCG or other card game, chances are you'll love this. If you're a fan of horror/mystery, you'll love this.

Gameplay is incredibly solid, story telling is interesting and extremely compelling, and the visuals perfectly ride the line of impressive high quality and older game nostalgic vibe.

I haven't beaten the game yet, but without spoiling anything, I got 10+ hours out of the first act of the game and would have been satisfied if it ended right there just based on the quality and replayability.

This game tests your ability not only to understand the fundamental aspects that most card game battlers do, but also your ability to think outside the box and find non-traditional ways to solve in-game problems. Again, without spoilers, listen carefully to what characters say and read every piece of content you're provided because the developer has done an excellent job leaving bread crumbs to alternative paths, cool secrets, and hidden lore/story developments if you are paying attention.

Additionally, I would HIGHLY recommend going in to this game blind. There are a lot of twists and turns that are much more satisfying if you experience them yourself, and the secrets are very satisfying to find. As someone who often misses hidden game content, I found that they were not too difficult to find yourself as the game often will provide more than one nudge in the right direction for any given hidden element.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMechanically and aesthetically its a treat. Mileage will vary on the plot.
Mild plot spoilers ahead:
It's an ARG. It's solved so you don't have to go down the entire rabbit hole yourself if you don't want to (and in fact for certain parts you literally couldn't go alone on it). The plot outside of the ARG is pretty amazing, inspired even. The more the ARG parts creep in though, the less solid it gets, culminating in a wholly unsatisfying pay off likely leading into another project. For ARG fans this is great, they love that sort of thing. For people who wanted a satisfying pay off for this one game... it lacks a lot to be desired. I haven't played the mod the creator made yet though, and that supposedly will have a bit of plot in it, so my opinion might change on that in the future.

This is definitely my favorite game of 2021 by a landslide and I cannot recommend it highly enough. The creator has even made an official mod that is undergoing active development, just to cater to the fanbase who wanted even more of the game to enjoy. I'm going to start playing it sometime after posting this review, and might add to the review later with my thoughts on it. Regardless though, this game is definitely one of my favorites of all time, and I have annoyingly high standards for that sort of thing.\t\t\t\t\t
