\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm really torn on this one.

I bought it because I was intrigued by the trailer and I wanted to play this kind of card game.
Like some other comment mentioned, the game has 3 arcs and only the first one is like the trailer shows. I really liked the first arc and had fun, but the second arc completely changed the atmosphere and tone. I felt like all the mystery build up was suddenly gone and I'm playing a different game now. That part just wasn’t fun for me. The game didn't recover for me in the third arc either. Unfortunately, at this point the ending was lackluster too.

I know it's made by the Pony Island creators and they are known for shifting the tone but that doesn’t mean that this style will always work. The second arc shouldn’t be in the game imo and I also wasn’t a fan of the live action scenes and the whole meta thing, as they kind of took me out of the experience and where too predictable.
So, I decided to not recommend the game. It did have a lot of potential, but once the (good and fun) first arc is finished, it just becomes messy and not fun anymore. The other arcs just are a letdown to the first one.

So, if you just want the mystery card game then unfortunately it’s a pass.\t\t\t\t\t
