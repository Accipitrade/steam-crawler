\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is very atmospheric and in general really well made, from the graphics to the game mechanics. However, the game gets fairly repetitive and dull later on, I vastly prefer the combat and game style of act 1 to the other acts. Act 2 was kind of bad, but thankfully it was shorter than the other two. The issue with part 2 being that somehow everything felt more random, even though you had control of your deck your deck had a lot of filler cards and there was no differentiated deck for your "sacrifice" cards. Act 3 was cool but dragged on, but at the same time it felt as if everything new that was added along the way such as the transform mechanic of leshy's domain was completely forgotten and irrelevant as soon as you left the area. It kept me hooked, but I felt that the game tried to do too many things without fully fleshing out any of them. A longer standalone version of Leshy's game would be nice, with more lanes, longer tougher fights and more health. But don't get it twisted, this game is super cool and the devs really put a lot of care into it.\t\t\t\t\t
