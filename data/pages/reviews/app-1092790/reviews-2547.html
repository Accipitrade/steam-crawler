\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is what would happen if Slay the Spire and a point and click puzzle game had a baby and that baby was a deranged murderer in a room escape. I cannot emphasize enough the incredible presentation here, i've played alot games over the years, but i think those eyes and those hands coming towards me will remain in my aging brain for my remaining years.

GAMEPLAY! Right, forgot this is a game, its a very well made card game with standard roguelike elements. Where Inscryption differs from others, are the things you do OFF the table. Explore the room, discover puzzles and secrets as you progress, and new items and cards to help you in the maniac's game. If you like Slay the Spire or Hearthstone, the general mechanics are the same, but with more mechanics being introduced the further in you go.

Im not into card games anymore after Hearthstone bankrupt me, and i initially wrote this off as generic and not my kinda game. Watched a streamer play it and was instantly in love with the graphics and theme. All i ask of the devs, is PLEASE make it Steam Deck verified. I look forward to playing more and more and maybe even an expansion possibility down the line.\t\t\t\t\t
