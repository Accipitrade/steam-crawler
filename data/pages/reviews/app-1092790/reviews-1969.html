\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Where do I begin?"

Lets be honest: At the time of writing this, that would sound like the intro to a list of all the problems with Battlefield 2042...
Thankfully, this isn't that game, and it definitely isn't EA.

Let me get to the chase:
Graphics - Mwah! (Kissing sound basically) Magnificent... if you are into indie style/more crude art styles.
Gameplay - Engaging - Even when I wasn't doing so well in my attempts to progress through the story, I was still so determined to see how it played out.
Mechanics - 100/10 - Basically, it is so hard to explain how fluid the mechanics are, and the card-playing tactics...
Character Progression - 8/10 - There was certainly a bit to be desired on the character progressions, but each does separate itself from the next well-enough.

Overall: Buy it. Like now. Even at regular price it is far too short of the actual value it provides. It took nearly 20 hours of just regular playing to beat the main story for myself... not even including any extras, etc.
