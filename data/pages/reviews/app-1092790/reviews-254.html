The first two hours of the game were incredibly gripping and I was in love with how atmospheric the game was, but the longer it went on, the more irritations came up in the gameplay loop, though some of the moments were still great.

The last 8 hours of the game have significantly less interesting gameplay, and though I was interested in the game's story, it was a slog to get through at times. Others can recommend Inscryption, and it's likely you'll like it more than I did, but the game didn't quite do it for me.\t\t\t\t\t
