Inscryption is great. Definitely worth looking into for something a little different and/or you're into Puzzle games or card games.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is the kind of game that is best played knowing as little as possible about it. All you should know is that it is a horror-themed card game, there are elements of rougelikes and escape rooms, and that it is amazing.

Pros;
-Excellent card gameplay that allows for cool strategies and build diversity.
-A deeply compelling story that subverts expectations in a good way.
-Impressive graphics that mix realistic and retro styles almost perfectly.
-A haunting soundtrack that perfectly sets the mood.
-The above two points combine to make an unsettling atmosphere akin to The Room (not that The Room).
-Cool stoat.

Neutral (Informal);
-This is a short experience (roughly 10 hours ). If you are looking for a deck builder that will give you hundreds of hours of gameplay, then you won't find it here (I'd recommend Slay the Spire for that).
-There are no jumpscares. I know a lot of people hate jumpscares (myself included), but I'm sure there are a few people that like them. Just know this isn't that kind of horror game.

Cons;
-Not much replay value. There are a few cool secrets, but they're not worth another full playthrough.
- The ARG stuff was a bit lame. It was short, unneeded, and anti-climactic (at least in my opinion).

- The stoat is a lie.


Overall 9.5 out of 10. GOTY material here.\t\t\t\t\t
