\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI used to be a hater of card games, and the last game I played of this gender was Yi Gi Oh Forbidden Memories. First thing you need to know is that Inscryption is not a normal card game, it is a rogue like with a terrific story line within, therefore this game is friendly to every kind of player. At early game the mechanics may feel a little bit confusing, specially the card abilities but trust me, you'll master the mechanics quickly with every run you play.

This is the kind of game that the less you know about the game, the better the experience will be, so please stay away from spoilers and don't look up guides. Every puzzle or mechanic is well explained, and you'll solve it eventually. Forget everything you know about card games!

In conclusion, this game is an absolute masterpiece that every gamer should play, specially if you're into rogue likes and cards.
