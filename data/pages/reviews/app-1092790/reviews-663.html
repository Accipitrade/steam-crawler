\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is unreasonably good, the type that you can only find in an indie project made from the heart. I cannot recommend it enough. Warning: Stay as spoiler free as you can as the story relies on it.

The game mechanics are fun, engaging, and so ingeniously ingrained with the story in a way I've never seen before or after. The visuals are wonderful and very well done. The story is extremely well written and really lets you piece things together yourself while not feeling convoluted. The music is something very special. It's wonderful on it's own but in context they make the scenes come to life; fulfilling the exact emotions the player is experiencing. It leads to some downright spiritual moments that are some of the most engrossing moments I've had from a game in recent time.

The only reason that I could see someone not enjoying this game is if you don't enjoy deck builders or rogue likes. Which if that's the case that's fair but I still highly encourage you to give it a shot, it's easy to get into, loads of fun, and engrossing from cover to cover. With the addition of the free dlc it also has great replayability and even more story to uncover.

A 10/10 must have in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
