In its current state I cant recommend it at its price for whats advertised but if half off you would get the half you paid for.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn incredible game that will keep surprising you with what it can do. Really fun roguelite deck builder mechanics and lots of good twists. If you don't mind a relatively short game that morphs over time to eventually be something the store page didn't imply, buy this and don't read on.

Spoilers below.



















Unfortunate circumstances lulled me into the belief that there would be double the amount of content that there actually was. I picked the one out of four choices that makes the continuation look "natural", and was looking forward to there being 4 completely unique escape rooms when in reality there were only 2. Completing the game and looking at the achievements instantly killed all remaining hype.

If finishing the game unlocked the implied "complete" roguelite deckbuilder, i would be satisfied.

Some minor nitpicks, the escape rooms themselves are very simple and the meta narration/live action cutscenes detract from the experience. The plot itself is crud creepypasta bad.\t\t\t\t\t
