\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is a mysterious, atmosphere-rich experience who's unique presentation might be enticing enough to keep you around through what is essentially just a horrifically dull and shallow card game. Unfortunately, you spend far, FAR more time playing the very low quality card game than you do engaging with the story or atmosphere. For me personally, I felt like I was pulling as many teeth as the player character trying to slog through this one to see the mystery unfold. I genuinely don't understand what people see in this game, unless they've just never played a remotely interesting card game before.

There are times when I don't recommend a game but still feel like I got my money's worth. This game feels like a giant waste of $20 to me and I'm sad I wasn't able to refund it.\t\t\t\t\t
