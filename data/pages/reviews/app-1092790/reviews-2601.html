\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDo some research on the developer before you buy this.

The game is not what it advertises itself to be. It is not a rogue-lite card battler like Slay the Spire or Monster Train. It's a shame because the first 1/3 of it is done pretty well and could have been something great with some more work into it. Instead you get a generic creepy pasta story with some lukewarm and boring card fights with way too many mechanics for its duration. The mechanics would make sense,,, were this game actually a rogue-lite with multiple runs and all that, but the quality takes a nosedive after you beat the first part twice. Everything afterwards is just ok, but the advertising makes you think you are getting into a more traditional rogue-lite and it all feels a bit half baked.\t\t\t\t\t
