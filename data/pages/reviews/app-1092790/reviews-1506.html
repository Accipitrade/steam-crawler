Inscryption emphasizes atmosphere and story-telling, the deck-building and the core gameplay mechanics are shallow and don't hold up for more than a few hours. This isn't a bad game by any means, but I got pulled in by all the comparisons to StS...and it just doesn't hold a candle. Avoid if you're looking for depth, complexity and "bang for the buck" in terms of how many hours you're going to get out of it relative to how many dollars you put into it.\t\t\t\t\t
