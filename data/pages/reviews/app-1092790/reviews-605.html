I love it when a game subverts expectations and goes in a completely different direction. Just the base beginning couple of levels has been already worth its price tag IMO. It's a very clean base game that allows you to sacrifice and summon stronger and stronger minions. The cards feel pretty good to use and very satisfying when you "Overkill" the opponent to get even more cards. This brutal way of playing a card game and the ominous text, music, card design, atmosphere,and characters, really make you wonder how ♥♥♥♥♥♥ up our captor is (Then again, he is making you play a card game that if you lose you get sacrificed so i think it's kinda on the nose.) thinks It gives you enough tools and allows you to feel very powerful which i thought would be problematic for repeat playthroughs. You see, I don't like looking at trailers and reviews for games (1st review btw) I like to go in as blind as possible and the most i'll look at is if it's positive or negative. SO IMAGINE MY ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ SURPRISE WHEN THERE'S NOT 1 BUT LIKE 2 MORE FULLY MADE AND PLAYABLE BUT COMPLETE DIFFERENT CARD GAMES AFTER YOU BEAT THE LEVEL. They even had like 2 more that had the groundwork laid out for them. That blew my mind on how hard these developers and designers worked and unfortunately had to cut. They however get my fullest praises and commendations for making a game that was able to use them in a natural and completely believable way.

I think this game is the epitome of doing a lot with a little. The presentation of this game is phenomenal and every single new area getting new UI and UX. I would love to be able to play the other modes that got cut at the end and future card games made by this developer.\t\t\t\t\t
