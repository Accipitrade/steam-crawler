\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInscryption is more story based than it would seem, but the base gameplay is still very good. I was immediately pulled in with it's atmosphere, I think I played the demo for maybe 2 minutes.

Inscyption will last somewhere between 6-10 hours on the first playthough, depending on how thorough you are and how much you died. Personally I'd felt I was finished after playing the game twice, and going through a third time for some achievements I felt were easy to get. I may go back in the future though, the base game is quite fun.

Once you get to know the game and the cards, it becomes pretty easy to beat, for better and worse. There were few times that I felt that RNG had screwed me from the outset due to the cards I was dealt, which is obviously good. Though, once you know the type of decks that win, the game can lose it's feeling of challenge.

I would say though that that demerit isn't any kind of deal-breaker, since the story of the game is pretty interesting and draws you in. I only realised the ease of winning after beating the game and going back, this isn't something you'll notice on your first play through, and the base game is still fun when you know how to win, imo.

I won't comment on the story of the game since as many other reviews state, it's best to go into this game knowing nothing. If you look at the store page and you feel interested, I doubt you'd be disappointed by the story.

Overall, 10/10, A game so nice I played it thrice.\t\t\t\t\t
