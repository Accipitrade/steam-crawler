\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA good game, but for me personally, it felt massively overrated. The card game mechanics are fun, but I did not feel like there was anything particularly exceptional about the gameplay or the meta-narrative. Additionally, I felt that my enjoyment with the game fell off hard after (without spoiling anything) my first "successful run". I'd recommend it on a sale. If you enjoyed either of the studio's previous two games, you'll like this one. If you're looking for a deckbuilder roguelike / roguelite, they've recently released a beta of a mod that turns this in to one, but honestly you could do much better even at this price point with e.g. Slay the Spire, Monster Train.\t\t\t\t\t
