\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty fun card game. Basically if they took the movie Saw and decided to slap it together with Yugioh or magic the gathering.

Pros:
1. New mechanics being introduced further into the game made you rethink your whole build and kept it fresh too

2. Loved how every time you died you can basically build a bear your own "death" card. Renamed the first closest attempt of mines to "beating" the game, "Death king" with revive and 4 bones when it dies. Alongside the stats of big boi beefy elk. Then used it to kick his ass when I finally rolled for it.

3. Antagonist that I actually want to beat the ♥♥♥♥ out of. He sometimes changes your name for said build-a-bear death card to add insult to injury making it more satisfying when you beat him :D

4. More than meets the "eye." I refuse to elaborate further and suggest you to play it to find out.


Cons:
1. Can get mentally draining when you roll for bad cards and die earlier than you did previous runs kind of wishing there's a "can I just die and reset" button somewhere rather than just waiting for him to murder me.

2. Difficult to streamline your deck since most of the time you'll be adding more cards in than upgrading or sacrificing them even if you don't want to. This forces you to be stuck with a +1 in your deck making it more difficult to find the actual cards you want. (Unless you can search your deck)\t\t\t\t\t
