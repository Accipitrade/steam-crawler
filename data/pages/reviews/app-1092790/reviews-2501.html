\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI usually hate card games, but this one does something so unique and different that i couldn't help but love it. The rules of the game are super simple but allow for some complex strategies, The art style is charming, and the blending of several different game genres is perfectly executed. I was fully enjoying the game through out my time of playing it.

That said, I really wish this game was longer. The beginning of the game was so good and i loved the idea of the game rules constantly changing, and you get a taste of that at the end of the game, but it's cut super short and I was left really disappointed. I'm hoping that there might be more to come out of this at some point, but i won't hold my breath.

I think the ARG thing kinda bogs it down a bit and makes the core gameplay lose focus in favor of a story that isn't that interesting. I like the angle, but if it dropped the ARG story shenanigans, there could've been an act for all four of the scrybes, instead of just the 2 we got. \t\t\t\t\t
