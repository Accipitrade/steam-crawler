\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game after watching Markipliers first video on the game. At first the concept striked me as a roguelike card game with a RNG map and card system mixed with wonderful visual and audio design. And I was digging it a lot. But that was Act 1. Act 2 is fine in terms of varied card and deck building but the design overall for the act was, while fun, a complete downgrade from Act 1. Act 3 does return to form in a way, building off of the foundation laid out by Act 1. Semi-Open world, multiple options to build your deck (Not as great as Act 2 but better than 1) and the design overall is pretty good. The ending made me wish there was more to the game with the other Scrybes. The design for the 2 other Scrybes looked cool and I would love for the developer to add a Endless Roguelike mode that builds off of Act 1 and 3, and add in the other 2 Scrybes. If that can be so I can see myself investing dozens even hundreds of hours into it. I can only hope for now. Otherwise, it's a great card game. Great mechanics and design overall. Story is pretty good.
9/10.
It would be a perfect 10/10 if there was more endgame options.

Edit: With the addition of Kaycees Mod, the game now does have a solid endgame. A repeatable Act 1 with custom preset decks, modifiers to make the game more challenging, and more lore; all solid for an update to the game and I love that. I still wish we could see what kind of game we would have with the other 2 Scrybes but in the grand scheme of things, I love being able to sit down, pick a deck and some modifiers, and just go to down wasting away hours just playing what is essentially my favorite card game on Steam. Also its so good my sister, who loathes card games, bought the game and actually enjoys playing it. So a solid 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
