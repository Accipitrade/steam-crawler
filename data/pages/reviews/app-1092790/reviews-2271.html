Introduction
Inscryption is a deckbuilding game, but it is also not. Inscryption is more than what you see in the trailer and screenshots. If all you are looking for is the gameplay shown in the previews, I would highly encourage you to look into other games by Daniel Mullins to set your expectations first. You should probably get up from the game and stretch your legs every once in a while, too. I can't say much more than that without spoilers.

Conclusion
