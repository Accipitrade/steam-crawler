\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm still not sure what actually *happened*, story-wise, but I did enjoy the game. Card game with some meta elements and several significant gameplay twists that require spoilers to explain. Certain story elements require a ridiculously convoluted ARG to uncover, eg. finding and translating a secret code from a different game, so you'll miss out on some of the story unless (like me) you look at other people's story-hunting. And even then apparently there's a major thing left ambiguous.

For much of the game you're really only playing a subset of the larger in-universe "Inscryption" card game. It'd be interesting to have a mode where you get to play with the entire four sets of mechanics and not a strict limit to your card collection, but at no point do you get the chance to do this freely. The closest you get is also by far the worst-looking part of the game. So I'm left wishing I could explore the game mechanics a bit more. Even so, I did enjoy the experience.\t\t\t\t\t
