\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWent into it with some spoilers but it's great to playthrough. Not really interested in the meta stuff but the game design of the card game and looking for ways to break it are very interesting. MtG has been letting me down with too many complex mechanics or having to play someone elses broken game, and forget about Yugioh, but this game encourages you to play a rogue like deck builder and look for ways to turn your cards into winners. The game is meant to be broken, it wants to be solved, it wants to be found. Act 2 gets a little simple but by then you're being pushed forward to beat it quicker, find more secrets and learn some things that will be relevant in the later parts. There's also work on a "mod" to extend the first part as just a card game and less of a kidnapping. It's such a dense game and story I'd love to see spin offs or even just have people interested in card game development take notes, even just how games work or how characters try to build the same product and world from different ends.

There's a lot to talk about, I don't want to spoil it, I'm also just not interested in all of it. As far as the game is concerned it's a 10/10 masterpiece, but I'm not going out into the woods for it.\t\t\t\t\t
