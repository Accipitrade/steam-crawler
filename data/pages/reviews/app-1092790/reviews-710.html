\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery intresting game. It is a horror game mixed with cards that you would think at first glace not fit very well together. But I was so wrong!!

The first person POV from the start makes it more personal as you are learning the card game Inscryption and face many challenging puzzles, to go out of the 2D board game itself and into a 3D escape the room scenarios, along the way you gather clues from the creatures you encounter as you progress... and things get WAY more difficult. The mood and dark themes in the atmosphere when you die and face off against the ambient masked boss is a staple and keeps me coming back play more. The card game itself is addicting and random seeds allow for an infinite game play loop.

The story is compelling as well. Lots of twists and turns and perfect transitions from start to finish. A lot of thought and care went into this game and it defo worth has a longer story than most 60$ games. Highly recommend to anyone into a new type of horror/escape room card type of game.\t\t\t\t\t
