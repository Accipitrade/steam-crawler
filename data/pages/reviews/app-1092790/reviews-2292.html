\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI purchased this game almost immediately after launch and played through it over the next week and i gotta say, this is my favorite card battler easy.

The gameplay is so fun, almost every mechanic introduced works the way you'd expect it to. If you have any card game experience you wont even need the tutorial.

The escape room portions of the game are icing on the already perfect cake. So many card games before this have made it lore cannon that the players are just playing a card game, but this is the only one I've seen that capitalizes on that fact and makes it important. Which by itself as a concept is a huge flavor win.

If you're on the fence about getting this game because you're worried about how it holds up or because you don't typically like horror games, then trust me and buy and play it yourself. We all know you're scrolling through the comments for some validation, so take mine. Buy. Play. You're welcome.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the games out of 2 that I've been pretty obsessed with, the other being Darkest Dungeon 2 Early Access... I will start by saying that I am already a fan of card games, but don't let that deter you from checking this game out if you're not! It doesn't actually have super complex card game mechanics, it is more like a puzzle that you are trying to beat before they beat you! The game is very atmospheric and dark, one of my favorite things about the game. From the location, the music, or the little touches here and there to add that extra spook, the game is very well made. The game can start off pretty hard, even as a card game player I got busted up a couple times. However, as you play, you understand the mechanics more and more, and there are built in mechanics to help. I don't write a lot of reviews, and there's a lot I haven't covered, but definitely check this game out!
