+The cards talk to you
+Great Atmosphere
+Interesting Characters
+Great Soundtrack, (not too much, just what it needs, when it needs it)
+Cool Secrets
+Gameplay is quite interesting and unique, it doesn't get boring/stale because it changes in a way thorugh the acts
+You can sew together creatures and do a human-centipede-like creature! Fun!

Cons:
+The full game is too short, but it makes sense
+ There's a few things that the story could've done better, but it's not a bad one by any means
+There's no ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ pirate battle, (trust me, you'll get it by the end)
+♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Stoat

Kaycee's Mod is fun and all and i really hope it keeps getting updates like that because it's been some time since i've had this much fun with a short straight to the point game\t\t\t\t\t
