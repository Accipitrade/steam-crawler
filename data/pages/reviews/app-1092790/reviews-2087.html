Mechanics were straight forward and fun to mess with. While this is a deckbuilding game, it is simple to figure out and I imagine people who don't normally like deck builders will still enjoy the game. It's beyond simple deck-building when you can alter the cards dramatically and turn a useless card into a useful one as the game progresses. I actually felt sad when I beat the game. I wanted to keep playing. The story was a rollercoaster. I enjoyed it thoroughly. For the cost, I highly recommend for a week or so of play after work on on occasion! I could see this being fleshed out and made into a competitor to Hearthstone, should it come to that.\t\t\t\t\t

My Vote for Game of the Year!
Inscryption is short and sweet, depending on who you are, but it is an absolute masterpiece. Subtle yet strong story telling, a multitude of brilliant art styles with a superb soundtrack to match. All backed up by creative game play that puts a spin on an incredibly underrated genre, this is a game that deserves recognition. The plot twists are written spectacularly. The game is full of secrets and mystery, the hints and small details hidden throughout are a delight and a stroke of genius to those that pick up on them. 10/10. If only there could be more. A sequal, DLC? - whatever it is ill take it. My only con, would be wishing all Scribes could be as fleshed out and as detailed as the 1st. With a longer, more narrative driven approach.\t\t\t\t\t
