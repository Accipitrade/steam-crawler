\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of, if not not the best deck building games of all time. There is a VERY deep system involving 4 deck types that each have special mechanics, surprisingly complete for a game worth less than $30. But that's not even the main feature of the game. Like all classic indie games, Inscryption realizes it is a videogame, not simply a story being told through the analog of one. This means that much like Undertale, expect sudden genre swaps and curveballs around every corner which play with your expectations from a computer program.

The lore is great. An interesting story told out of order, which lots of red herrings to keep you guessing as to what actually is happening. The devs even have a total ARG that is in like 5 phases which involve you using ciphers and digging around in the game files.

A highly rewarding and at times genuinely baffling ride. One of the freshest experiences I've had in a while. EASILY a 9/10 9.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI haven't finished it, but this is definitely worth a buy at the price-tag, even worth more tbh. It's very in depth and from the reviews it looks to be about 16 hours worth maybe, but I can't really comment on that till I finish.

But damn is this fun. I don't think I've played a card game on my computer before, and this was definitely a good way to do it. It's funny because I ignore "retro-styled" games usually, but Devolver has always been my one exception. Broforce and Hotline Miami are such good games, but this is definitely an extra step out of my comfort zone. I like strategy games but deck-builders never seemed that compelling to me. I also am not a fan of retro-horror games, and this is another element that this does really well. Playing this stoned at night in my room during certain sections actually got me a little spooked.

I will say that the card game element may still need you to be interested in strategy/turn-based games in order to stay with the game throughout, it's basically the main mechanic of the game. If you hate turnbased games or strategy games (even Pokemon), then this may still not be for you, but if you have ANY interest in something like that, then definitely get this game. I haven't come across many games really worth more than the price-tag.\t\t\t\t\t
