\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start off by saying, I wanted to like this game, and I had high hopes for it. I'm a huge lover of strategy games, and I feel the best when I win a game based on my prowess and not being given pity cards for losing.

This game is literally "Die until you unlock enough good cards to win". A genius wouldn't do any better than a 5 year-old child. It's the illusion of "getting better" provided by the game giving you better cards from losing. IDK about you, but I'm not a fan of losing til you get to win, wish I didn't have over 2 hours so I could return it.

Don't buy this game if you find any merit in winning from any sort of skill or logical thinking. You win solely from the cards you "earn", and you get those cards from repeating the same crap story line and losing to the broken NPC getting 10x as many cards as you and 3x as many lives. Hard pass.


TLDR:
This game isn't "beat Dark Souls 3 unarmed with no weapons and no lvls cause you're good" This game is "wait until the game gives you enough material to actually play it, because no sort of skill will ever let you win."\t\t\t\t\t
