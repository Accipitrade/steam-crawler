\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust an absolutely amazing game overall, I think.

I can't really say much about what I love without spoiling the entire game but...The gameplay is solid and actually pretty fun, despite being "just" a card game.

Despite the game being a "horror" game, the game doesn't seem to do any cheap tricks like jumpscares (maybe a few small things can be considered one, but they aren't huge to me), or intense gore, instead it uses the atmosphere, lack of lighting, and character personalities to drive the "horror" aspect, without attempting to try really hard in making you feel uneasy. and i think thats what a good horror game should do.

Not only that, but the mysteriousness and the atmosphere the game throws you into at the start really sets the mood for the entire game...and once you finish "Act 1", well...the game is nothing like it led you to believe, though the core gameplay is still there throughout all 3 acts, with some new stuff thrown in to spice things up.
I nominated this game for the "Most Innovative Gameplay" during the 2021 Steam Awards, because I truly feel they went all out to make as a unique experience as they possibly could.

I really enjoyed playing through this game & as of this review, I have 100% of the Steam Achievements.

I heavily suggest buying this game and playing it at least twice. There are a lot of secrets to be found. My first playthough took me about just under 12 hours I think.\t\t\t\t\t
