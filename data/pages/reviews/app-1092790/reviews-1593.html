Inscryption is a fantastic card game unlike any other. It brings it's own mysterious personality to the realm of digital card games today. It has a very unique and unforgiving way of teaching its captives players the rules of the game; as well as how it shows players glimpses of the story beneath the cards. I believe the game is paced perfectly, as it allows just enough time for its players to absorb the rules of the game as well as piece together the story. Highly recommend to all players who are thinking about buying the game or what to dip their toes into the card game genre.

Death is a powerful teacher. \t\t\t\t\t
