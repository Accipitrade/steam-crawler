Considered the graphic change initially positive, but got stale and not pleasant to look at for long. I like the different take on the world and expansion of story. The new card battling was fun at first, but I preferred the animal aesthetic compared to the emphasis on death, magic and technology. I did not care for the FMV story elements at all. Act 3 I was starting to wear on the story, art and card game variety. The game lost most of its charm, started to come across as overly mean to player and cynical. Enjoyed portions of the end game. Story overall is okay but underwhelming, unclear what the story or meta commentary is about. The music and sound design is excellent...incredibly well done. Graphics/Art direction: 1st Act is top notch, mixed after that. Gameplay/puzzles: clever and...fun but eventually broken and simplistic.
