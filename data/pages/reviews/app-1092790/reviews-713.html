\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCurrent Location in the games story: ACT 2 defeated Magnificus, Grimora and Leshy.

Let me begin by saying I do enjoy the card game and mechanics. I actually love the old style that ACT 2 does (I love the old Pokemon TCG games with the similar style as well, I still actively play those). Was what I played up to this point fun and interesting? Yes. But the change in the art style and atmosphere was jarring when I bought a game for it's "Dark" looking story and atmosphere. I played the demo and from was I saw it was great and interesting, I then bought it and started playing the full game.

Gameplay was enjoyable and so was the story until the game decided to change styles and atmosphere that was advertised this also made it not feel like a roguelike anymore for ACT 2. It's like this game gut punches you because it drastically changes the game experience after ACT 1. After the first few hours the game changes and then makes it so half of the appeal the game originally had is just gone now. It feels like they put on the great atmosphere and story in the first part so it's too late to back out after you get past it. With barely any indication except 1 second in the trailer of the changes in the game on the store page it almost feels like false advertisement.

I am going to continue to play and finish the game. Hopefully the story and later parts make up for the differences.


EDIT 1:
Finished the game and it was a great experience, fun mechanics and a decent story. The return to the same gameplay for ACT 3 as in ACT 1 lessened the disdain for the jarring sequence of ACT 2 but still took away part of the Roguelike elements. I wish it was 4 ACTS with each of the scrybe's stories all keeping the similar style of ACT 1 and 3.

In all the game is a good game but I still feel slighted for ACT 2, it felt out of place (I understand that it makes sense story wise). If you understand what is coming for ACT 2 and not expecting a full game like ACT 1 then go for it, well worth your money, but I came in expecting a full game of the same style of ACT 1 and 3.

EDIT 2: Changing to thumbs up I would recommend.

After playing quite a few more hours with Kaycee's mod (The free mini-expansion) . I can say that it has in my opinion made up for the "bait and switch". It was a good addition to the game and a great redeeming feature. It feels like exactly what I wanted from the game, I would also recommend "P03 In Kaycees Mod" a mod by "Infiniscryption", it has great quality and effort put into it.\t\t\t\t\t
