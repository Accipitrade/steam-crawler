\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave you ever had that moment when you were playing a game, where you noticed something so small, that it filled you with pure joy when you connected those two little dots? Maybe it was some line of dialogue said by a character that comes back later in a new context, or some specific chain of events you did "just to try it" that actually resulted in a unique outcome, or perhaps you triggered something in the overworld, then the ramifications of that trigger actually showed up in the in game battle environment. This is the level of passion and effort that is absolutely bursting at the seams from this game. Throughout the entirety of my playtime, which is not a small amount mind you, I was blown away by the sheer amount of love, passion, and care for the player exuding from this game. There was no shortage of the amount of times I was surprised by cool little details that so clearly showed the hear of the developer, that many players are sorely missing in games these days. On top of these things, the gameplay itself never ceases to just get deeper and deeper. Once you think you've figured all the mechanics and you can't go any further, more and more elements get added, or expanded upon. This game is like the game we all dream of making, with massive, almost unending scope, it feels like the devs came up with a wild idea and nothing from the game was cut. If intense attention to detail and extremely in depth gameplay isn't enough for you, there's even an extremely deep and well thought out story (in game and ARG to research if that's your thing), and strikingly unique visuals to consider. All in all, this game hits every mark you could possibly think of, and is by far the hallmark for what a good game can be, and what a game should be.\t\t\t\t\t
