\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the best games I have ever played.

Its presentation and design alone are enough to sell it, but the gameplay is also really well done, and its all tied together into a very well knit story that fits into the subersive genre.

Im also happy to say this game is much longer than I predicted. I know mullin makes unique games that are realetivly short, but this game took me about 20 hours to complete.

My only critique of the game and something I hope to be added to the future, is sortof a heavy spoiler so im going to space it out.






I wish after the game was fully beat you could play through each of the 4 scribes and their campaigns just like Leshy and P03. I want to still be able to treat this game like a real rouge-like even after the story is over. I also really want to play through Grimora and Magnificus's own unique boards. Along with returning to Leshy and P03 for many more runs.




I dont think the game as it is needs to change, I think it should just have this content added at the end. Ive seen others who enjoyed this game have a similar sentiment and its only because this game is so good that we want more of it - more of what feels like is almost already there : )


"Masterful"
