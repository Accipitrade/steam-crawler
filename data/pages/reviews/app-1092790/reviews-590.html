My absolute favourite thing about it, though, is the lore. The actual storyline in the game is both terrifying and exciting because of how immersive and captivating it is. The ending also was a bit unexpected but very fitting.

There is a whole New Game+ mode with challenges and new things added, though it operates as permadeath runs. It lets you keep playing the game after you beat it, so I'd say if you're worried about replayability, the game will keep on giving.

TL;DR: Yugi Oh + D&D + Escape rooms + horror movies = this game. It's fantastic, and has a great story and a NG+ mode. You won't be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
