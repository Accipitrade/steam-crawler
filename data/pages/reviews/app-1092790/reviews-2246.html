Inscryption is a rouge like card game with a horror asthetic. Since this games is made by the same people that made pony island, and the hex I knew their was some type of twist comming, but man it is a good twist. The game itself is realy simple, being half a simple but fun card game, and a excape room style game. Fun card game
Good story
that just one more turn mentality game


cons:
no endless mode (edit their is now an endless mode)
sometimes to cryptic
somethings only make sence if you played pony island, and the hex


in short
a horror card game with a twist, but needs a true endless mode (edit: their is now an endless mode)
