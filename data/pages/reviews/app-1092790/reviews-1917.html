\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Pros:
- Fantastic art-style and ambience.
- The card game 'Inscryption' is original and interesting to learn.
- Fun to build different strategies and 'break' the game.
- The (meta) narrative is very intriguing and kept me hooked until the end.
- Great sound design.

Cons:
- Some of the puzzles are quite difficult or hard to find however never too difficult that I needed to look online for guides.
- There are 3 Acts to the game. Some players expect Act 1 (rogue-like deck-builder) to last the whole game but it doesn't. Personally I didn't mind this as each Act revolves around the 'Inscryption' card game whilst developing new mechanics.

Conclusion:
This was my GOTY for 2021. I fell in love with the art-style and ambience instantly and was gripped by the story. I enjoyed being fed crumbs of the narrative to keep me guessing. I especially enjoyed the interactions between the characters and their remarks/quips. Surprisingly touching towards the end of the story. The gameplay tied all of this together into an addicting and fulfilling experience. I'm eagerly awaiting a supposed Endless mode of Act 1.\t\t\t\t\t
