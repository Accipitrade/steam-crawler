Pros:
- The difficulty slider made the game more challenging. Clearly the AI needed an edge.
- The game is enjoyable to play but it is held back by an antiquated game engine and persistent memory errors.
- It is a 'beer and pretzels game like risk, easy to learn. Don't expect a lot of depth or historical accuracy.
- Graphics are colorful but nothing spectacular. Some of the unit models sport unique uniforms, but it is a shame that this wasn't done across the board.
- Research is varied and nicely done but it is too easy to rapidly build it up.

Cons:
- Music remains total crap and painful to hear.
- The game engine remains problematic and out of memory errors are constant. The game is 32 bit, so it can't use that 32 gig memory you have in your 64bit OS. This has been a problem with all their MH games.
- Trade allows some massive stockpiles and allows you to vastly overbuild your forces. This allows for some unrealistic almost impossible outcomes. Unfortunately this allows you to easily steamroll the poor AI.
- It is too easy to build up your economy quickly. In a year or two you can have a huge economy and supercharged research.\t\t\t\t\t

The devs of the game don't fail to deliver something unique in the world of map games. I do not think it is easy to think of something rather new and brave, and yet they managed to create quite a different system, where politics, economy+trade, industry and military power DOES MATTER.

-> If you want to beat everyone, you need military -> If you want good military, you need food, resources and money -> if you want any of these things, you need to conquer/play a strong nation

In my opinion, the game is worth it whether it's on sale or not. The graphics are okay, the AI is significantly better, the mechanics are also on level with games like HoI4, CivV. There are plenty of achievements which you can try and do.

Probably a downside is that you will stay weak if you choose to play small/minor nation, just because you will have no army or good economy and resources (which is just how real life is) unless you are a puppet of great nation and decide to claim the whole of Germany and let your master get it for you :D

Quite often you will get "Out of memory" issue if you play with fog of war off and spawn grand armies, but the devs said they are working on fix of this, which I genuinely believe will push the game over and beyond.\t\t\t\t\t

If you like a game that gives you many challenges at the same time across many areas this is a game for you.
