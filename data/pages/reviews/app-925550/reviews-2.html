\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCRC Extreme is a Patched and Fixed Version of CRC 2005.
If I have anything to say about this title, it is that this game is one of the first original games that has helped model the current generation of racing games as we know them today.
This title feature's also the game mechanics that was later used in the online racing games that I loved back in 2007~2014 (Level-R / Project Torque and Heat Online)

Pros:
- Can be an easy game for newcomers as a challenging one for experts.
- A realistic and tunable car handeling system
- A good physical deformation damage model (you wamm too hard, your a pancake)
- A decent amount of cars that you earn from completing your career
- A multiplayer mode to play whit your friends.
- An ajustable firstperson camera view in cockpit
- Possibility to add your own .mp3 music files in teh game
- Support for the H shifter (work whit mouse also and its funny AF)


Cons:
- The multiplayer servers are not showing up (you can still connect directly to your friends IP adress or using hamachi or whatever similar program you like)
- DX9 (its a bit outdated)

Overall Score: 9/10

A must play for any who like's and want to learn racing basics !\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat can I say!!! I bloody love it!

I had doubts about this game as it was released in 2005 and I thought nope will be blurry with horrible physics! Its exactly not that!

1920x1080 everything on max and it actually looks pretty good! Infact very good when you consider nothing has been remodelled. I suspect the textures have been updated and new shaders have been applied but its so much fun! The tracks are highly original. One lap can take you along tarmac, gravel, along a stream, back to gravel, back to the stream and then back on the tarmac. The ai are great fun and you can shove over a wall or for a good barrel roll and the replays just show how good the crash physics are. What more can I say? I love it!!!\t\t\t\t\t
