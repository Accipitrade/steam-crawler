\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have a love hate relationship with this game. It's fun for sure, but wow they really goofed.

The Gameplay:
Pros:
- Generally fun on its own, as a game should be when it has DLC. This includes the fact that the default available weaponry is good too.
- Combat and movement feels pretty good, and the game has a lot of different things you can do in that regard. The graphics are pretty nice, but the interactions are really punchy and it's always neat to be in combat.
- Has online leaderboards if you are the sort that likes competition.

Cons:
- Paid DLC. Paid DLC everywhere. Why spend $60 for a game when you can spend another $60 for some virtual goodies that you can only use in one niche scenario?
- Not really in the game's control, but you all know how Nofriendo is with their IPs, so if you were hoping to cop some StarFox content from Steam, sorry,
- Replayability is sub-par. The stuff that's exciting at first is also the same stuff you do over and over and over again, so it gets old quick, and that rare special interaction is reduced to just another common part of the game. Replaying it will just have you wanting to go back to your completed save, because it's not that much different.
- Denuvo, which totally doesn't affect your game's performance negatively in the slightest, nooo :^)
- ^ No mods! Want to make the game fun after you finish it? What are you, insane?! Go buy some DLC or something, stupid caulksucker. Can you believe this guy? Thinks games are for fun! Pah!
- What's this? You have a VM enabled on your machine completely separated from the game?! You monster! Disable that right now, or else you can't play!\t\t\t\t\t
