\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFairly bland environments and story with uninteresting level design and puzzles. Doesn't really encourage you to engage with the advanced physics system (They hand you a gun and nearly infinite ammo, the enemies are not a major threat, and they aren't fun to fight in melee, so just shoot them in the head).

This game will make those who don't usually have issues with motion sickness feel the hurl, and not just due to the below average performance. Don't let people gaslight you into thinking you just aren't "experienced" enough: I have over 1000 hours of VR playtime and rarely experience issues in games with more movement.

I would very much like to stress that the motion sickness is a downside of the game itself, and not a 'skill-issue' of the players, since I would hate to see a trend of newer VR games ignoring motion sickness relief because we let them get away with it.\t\t\t\t\t
