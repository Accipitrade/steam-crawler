\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has become a VR classic that will remain in the canon whether it's up to your taste or not. Despite it's fame, I don't think this game has aged well given titles that explore it's individual mechanics with better comfort/depth. Where Boneworks shines is in bringing together every VR mechanic into one unified playspace, a love letter to VR enthusiasts. It's a rite of passage and at some point I think everyone will at least come to consider whether they'll want to try an experience like this.

I didn't have as much fun though as I've had in Alyx or The Climb, or Lone Echo. This game is very optimized. We also need to move past analog stick locomotion, walk in place mechanics are genuinely good.
