\r\n\t\t\t\t\tScore: 8/10

After recently completing the campaign, I feel I am now qualified to give this game a quick, honest review.

Firstly, I thoroughly enjoyed playing through Boneworks roughly 8-10 hour campaign and at its current price I would recommend it to any VR user. The environments are amazing, the huge array of weapons are a pleasure to test, and using them against the (somewhat limited) variety of enemies in Boneworks is a blast.

Where the game loses a few points is in its physics based puzzles and climbing, both of which are often more annoying and rage inducing than they are fun. Whilst the physics based gameplay is what makes this game fresh for the VR audience, it is also what holds the game back. When completing physics puzzles or climbing around to often would my VR body get stuck on objects within the game and this ultimately led to moments of fustration.

Overall, Boneworks is fun and inivative VR game that should be picked up by every VR user. The environments are amazing to explore and the gunplay is a blast, however, the physics puzzles and climbing mechanics hold the game back.
