\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy Two hours in Boneworks (On the HTC Vive):
-Visuals of the enviroment, enemies and music to set the tone are on point
- collision is a far sight better than anything ive seen thus far, and less prone to freaking out like blade and sorcery
- Puzzles are easy and relaxing and force you to take time and explore the enviroment around you (breaking boxes) looking up in rafters etc.
- Gun mechanics are refined all around, gripping the mag feels great and the drawing of items and guns is a sharper and more snappier version of Pavlov's
- Ai Collision at least with the nullbodies and headset monsters are great, the shooting officers could use a lil work tho
- The game runs great but is very CPU heavy in physics or AI heavy areas
- all of the interactables (buttons, levers, and doors) feel great
- and the weight of all objects, including yourself, feels great
- And the story is immersive and flows well thus far
- I only have crashed twice due to what i think is lack of good system hardware, specifically my CPU\t\t\t\t\t
