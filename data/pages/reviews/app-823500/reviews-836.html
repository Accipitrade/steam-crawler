\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wanted to like this. I spent €25 on it so I at least wanted to have a bit of a good time.
I had seen some youtubers play it and it looked fun.
Youtubers cut away the bad parts to improve pacing, so that the viewer doesn't get bored or annoyed. This game does not.

For every fun firefight there are about 9 horrible physics based puzzles. Nearly everything in this game is a physics object, including yourself. You bump into everything you walk into including the fallen enemies.
Climbing or jumping makes the camera shake violently every time you do it.
You can't even walk into a straight line without the camera shaking at least a little bit, causing you to get motion sickness easily.

Also a large amount of the environments in the game have bad lighting and just look ugly. If there was a rulebook on how to create environments in VR, this game breaks all of them.

There are fun moments in this game, sure. You will just have a very hard time reaching them because most good content is found very late into the game. And the start of the game is completely horrible.\t\t\t\t\t
