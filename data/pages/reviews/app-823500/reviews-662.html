\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game overall. Story was quite bad (doesn't matter, I don't think anyone is interested in this game for the story). Great combat, mediocre soundtrack (again, doesn't really matter). Only mechanical issue I had were some of the physics puzzles were incredibly annoying and glitchy, sometimes objects would fly off and bounce around randomly, and I often had things I needed get stuck in a wall or something. My only other complaint is with one of the final levels, where you have to pick up cannonballs using a staff, put them into a cannon, and shoot them at another big ball in a very specific spot. This one level is the least enjoyable experience I've ever had so far in VR, because the cannons do not tilt high enough on their own to reach the target the ball has to land in. For anyone struggling with this, I fixed it by using my left hand to push the cannon down far enough so it doesn't go back up on its own, and then using the staff you're given to push the button to the right. I had to do this for all four cannons. It's a pretty inexcusably terrible level, but I can look past it as the rest of this game is pretty fantastic.\t\t\t\t\t
