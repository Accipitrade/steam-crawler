\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally fun VR game!

The Good:
First and foremost, it's very fun! The action isn't overwhelming and there's a lot of exploration. Even if the levels are linear there always feels like there are at least 2 routes through any level (except the last level but, eh, nbd). Loved the ads (aim-down-sights). Story was a fun play on an existential question... I'll leave it at that so as not to spoil anything.

The Bad:
First: the standard VR complaints I guess? Because of the mechanics in play there is no short range TP, only turn angle snapping, so if you get VR sickness from fluid motion, brace yourself.
Second, if you're not familiar with how to fire a RL gun, best learn what a "sight picture" is.
Third: the physically rigged player model (if you're not full body tracking) can leave it in some odd angles. And, as neat as physically climbing is... it's jank AF.
