i recommend getting this game with R2modman and looking at what's been changed already, adjustable strength, a change of playermodels, including features limited to other levels ingame, you know the drill. oh and you can rest assured there is plenty of gun mods ready. this game gets turned into the dream physics sandbox we all wanted in VR.

my recommendation is explicitly based on your intentions of downloading the mod manager with it. if it wasn't for that, i wouldn't be recommending. not to say this is a bad game, but it desperately needs this to truly unlock it's potential.

--this was the important part, the following just covers more details for anyone interested--


the game itself needs more fine tuning, that much is obvious, and without workshop support there is a huge user input element missing entirely. however a lovely third party mod manager perfectly emulates the workshop we needed by one-click installing mods and having their structure not actually interfere with the main folder, so your game will never need reinstalling/back-ups in the event your mods mess up.


i still stand by the belief that this game should just offer all its secrets as an optional choice cause most of them are hugely expansive on the VR platform and are hidden away in a long winded campaign that turns off most people from wanting to continue.

i also believe the control they want to give us playing as Ford by making us raise/lower his legs and judge ourselves whether leaning over a drop throws us down it or not is taking away more control that it wants to give, it wouldnt be bad to have a few helping mechanics like a bungee mechanic that rubber bands us away from edges should we try to walk off without jumping or climbing assists that don't require us to fine tune where we're trying to stand and actually find a safe point to position us to.

and one more gripe is there is a lot of missing optimisations by the fact ill look over to certain areas where buildings we just came out from start to impact performance, possibly because it's trying to render where we just were even though we ourselves cannot see that.\t\t\t\t\t
