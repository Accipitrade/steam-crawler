\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a necessary framework for what is to come (Bonelab). The physics and locomotion are groundbreaking, but the gameplay, variety, pacing, and story are not.
The game self-satirises this with neon wall scrawlings of "What if this is just a tech demo?" in various locations across several levels, which greatly misses the mark, and only serves to devalue the player experience.
The physics sometimes freaks out, causing the player (and whatever you are holding or standing upon) to get completely YEETED in a random direction, not unlike a collision in Blendr that cant resolve.

Great tech demo, empty levels, vapid gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
