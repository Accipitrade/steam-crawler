It pushes the limits with VR and even has proper parkour mechanics with your person, as if you're actually there.
Admittedly however, this game will spoil you with VR, I will warn you with that. I cannot look at any other game the same as this functionally is the most superior (mechanically) VR game. Alyx is rather limiting mechanically wise, but very bar pushing with VR story telling. Something of which most of VR games lack as of currently, so it wins there.

So alas, get this game if you're seeking a truest to its form VR game that fully utilizes all that VR can offer with little to no limits.\t\t\t\t\t
