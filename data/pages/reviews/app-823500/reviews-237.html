\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIncredible game! I jumped in and figured I'd last for an hour at best, this is usually due to either motion sickness, or just "how fun could a sandbox game be?". 3 hours straight later, I can say that through my 100+ hours of general VR, this is by far one of the best engines out there in its respective category/genre (VR FPS)

Now keep in mind, the niche here (imo) is smooth mobility and gun-play in a playground of physics. Weapon-based melee is a tough gig, and I've found that other games capture that a lot better (ex Sword & Sorcery). Having said that, their next game will probably change my mind completely (Bonelab) as bone breaking, debilitating enemies by hitting them in key places (like the knee) adds such a cool aspect to game-play.

You cannot experience this game by watching videos or thinking "you've been there done that" already in vr. Hell, if you only buy this for the Zombie mode, it's still more then worth it. I will admit, I am bias towards the developers as I've followed Brandon and his general group of people since the youtube channel was called "Freddiew".

Lastly, with default settings and going with the flow of it, I did not suffer from motion sickness over my first 3 hours gameplay (standing), which is really nice. They explain why in-game how they've done this and it's genius.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCauses motion sickness in me and more than a few VR veterans in threads I've seen in steam discussions and on reddit, and there's no demo so buyer beware.
I honestly don't understand it myself because I've got thousands of hours clocked in steamVR and I play other games of this type - no issues. I own two different HMDs, dozens of VR titles, it is only this game that makes my lunch reverse direction. My rig is specced so I get the 90 or 120fps out of my HMDs constantly (outside of loading screens).
Consider this a positive review though, for those immune to the effects. I've managed to progress simply by keeping an eye shut which is antithetical to VR. Sitting down makes no difference to boot. I saw one guy on reddit who said EVERY game gave him motion sickness EXCEPTfor this one. It's odd stuff, and probably beyond the realm of the dev to understand or remedy. Sure is easy for me to say 'make it like other games' but hell if I know what the root cause is here.\t\t\t\t\t
