Boneworks is a buggy messy content filled nightmare fuel adventure that was 100% worth the price for me. The best way I can put it is if you aren't someone who normally goes for early access games I probably wouldn't buy, for how fun it is, and how great the sandbox mode is, it's still a buggy mess with an unbelievably frustrating save method (saved after every level, no mid level saves NO MATTER WHAT) with levels that can take someone who is inexperienced, or just likes to explore, hours each, not to mention that crashes happened pretty frequently to me (probably 6 in my campaign run) no mid level saving option is BANANAS! If more patches get released and they add some quality of life updates (playing sitting down is the absolute worst, even though the game tries to tell you that's what it recommends) and input a better save mechanic this game would be one of the greatest VR games to exist.\t\t\t\t\t
