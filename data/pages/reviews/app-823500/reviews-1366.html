I find this extremely annoying, especially with guns. You have to hold the trigger to grab it, then grab it with grip, then release trigger, then press again to shoot. This is obnoxious. I've tried to change this, but I struggle to understand how inside of Valve Home settings.

Any help would be greatly appreciated. I just don't understand why SLO would make the trigger the grab button and not the actual built in grab button.\t\t\t\t\t
