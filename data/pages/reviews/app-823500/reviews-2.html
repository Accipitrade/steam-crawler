\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(PRE-TEXTURE FIX REVIEW) It WAS great, absolutely loved it, barely any issues with lag, until I finished the Museum level and tried to load the level Streets, and it just never worked. Tried multiple times, even tried increasing the amount of memory that my computer could use, and still nothing, SteamVR would just crash after a couple of minutes of trying to load. If you want to play the game, make sure you set your Texture Quality to HALF (in the options in the Main Menu level) to heavily reduce load times and prevent the game from crashing, unless you have a VERY powerful PC.

(POST-TEXTURE FIX REVIEW) The crashing issue was fixed by setting the Texture Quality to HALF in the settings, thanks random steam comment section from a few years ago! Now that I can actually PLAY the game, it's friggin great. After coming fron Half Life 2: VR MOD this is a completely different experience, like the physics and combat of Blade and Sorcery mixed with the large maps filled with secrets in Half Life 2. I spent about 5 hours in the second level, Streets, looking for secrets and BONEWORKS boxes for items to use in the sandbox, probably the most fun I've had with a VR game in a while, even if climbing with or without a grabbable ledge can be a bit jank, I managed to get everything I could find (including the Balloon Gun!)
I also played with some wrist weights on for some extra exercise, and my arms were sore by the time I stopped playing but I still had fun. Great game, if you plan on buying BONELAB I recommend buying this game first (mostly because it's cheaper and has more Story Mode content)\t\t\t\t\t
