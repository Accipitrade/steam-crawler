\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a good game, 8/10. The polish is top notch, there's a lot of detail on all the objects in the world. I didn't play any other VR game until I had finished this one. **NO SPOILERS**

PHYSICS: It's the selling point of this title and damn are they good. Half the fun of this game is just messing around with the physics engine. It's integrated into the melee combat and the puzzles, so it feels more "real".

GAMEPLAY: Mostly combat as you progress through the linear story. You're on edge during a lot of the game because even the boring enemies (basically zombies) can mess you up pretty easily. There's a number of puzzles as well which were good although I wish there were more.

STORY: Pretty wack, although good. I wish there was more of it, but I think part of the appeal is that there is a lot unknown to the player at the beginning.

COMBAT: Smaller guns are amazing. Bigger guns are still great, just sometimes I'll hit the stock up against my shoulder ruining my aim during ADS, although that might just be from the non-physics of Pavlov that I'm used to. Melee is a little crazy because you and your weapons can get tangled up with the enemies. Slashing/ hacking is a little weird because your weapons often get stuck inside the baddies, but stabbing is awesome and satisfying. Anything can be used as a weapon because of the crazy good physics.

I played through the entire story at a reasonable pace and ~30 mins of the arena at the time of posting this review.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter 2 hours of play I can say at least for me this game was not a winner. Certain aspects of the game are highly enjoyable, but I found the level design tedious the levels were...boring and I cant say I enjoy the physics. For a game that is not in pre-release it did not feel polished. I do not like the force grab nor do I enjoy having to use the menu to constantly grab my gear since the locations on the body are awkward, it pulls you out of the experience, at least in my opinion. I wanted to enjoy my experience but I found It severely..lacking. Climbing was a chore and while again certain things were enjoyable like throwing knives or stabbing an enemy, most other things just felt blah.\t\t\t\t\t

(Be sure to not have a ceiling fan above you while playing, as you may experience hitting it quite often)! I haven't even beaten the story as of now, but cannot explain the fun you get to experience.
