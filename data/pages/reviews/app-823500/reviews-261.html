\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is hands down the best VR game currently on the market. It's the first game to take full advantage of the VR medium and show what's possible.

Not only that, but it has a full-sized game (The type you might expect from a AAA 2D game). Usually VR titles are short and/or gimmicky. BONEWORKS has a long story campaign, a whole lot of unlockables, arenas, a zombie mode, sandboxes, and more. The world is dense and fun to explore. All this isn't to say it's without its problems, but whatever faults it does have get vastly out-shined by the pure magic that this game is.

As of right now I have completed the entire story campaign on the Meta/Oculus Quest 2 using Plutosphere. And though that came with many hurdles (tech issues and occasional bursts of latency and resolution drops), I'm lucky enough that I have such a high tolerance for motion sickness, though I imagine for a lot of people, especially those unfamiliar to VR, playing BONEWORKS, using Plutosphere or other, will make them sick. I'm extremely glad that, because of Plutosphere, many people like myself who don't have access to the necessary hardware, can experience the joy of PCVR gaming (and especially BONEWORKS).\t\t\t\t\t
