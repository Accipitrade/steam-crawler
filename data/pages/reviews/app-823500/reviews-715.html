\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYeah, BONEWORKS is fun but it has a lot of problems. VR has a lot of limitations and BONEWORKS doesn't really work with it's limitations and chooses to do things that shouldn't be done anyway. The janky physics is what causes most of these issues. Weapons sometimes don't cling to your back, limbs getting stuck in objects, jumping sometimes can do nothing or fly you to the moon - it's very inconsistent. I think the worst part of the campaign is few sections of platforming in the story which can lead you to getting stuck in objects, which is very annoying.

The "story" is pretty abstract but doesn't have much depth to it. The world you are in isn't very engaging. A majority of the game just looks like testing textures, it's ugly. Enemy AI is very dumb. I feel as though there wasn't a lot of play testing of the story missions because of how dull most of the game really is. Of course, being in VR makes it a ton of fun by itself and BONEWORKS shows that you don't need a lot of polish to make a VR title fun - it's just not going to be memorable.\t\t\t\t\t
