Boneworks' gameplay is SO unique in comparison to other popular VR titles out at the time that i'm writing this. Everything in Boneworks is interactable to your body, the objects you are holding, even the environment around you! It really sells the idea that you are Arthur Ford, Entering a dystopian, seemingly unfinished vr game trying to figure the mystery behind what Monogon is doing in the background. Also, i should mention that it is extremely addicting when you replay the campaign and try to find different ways of going about completing the levels. By far my favorite level in the game has to be Warehouse because of the amount of options you have at your disposal are close to endless.

Mods are a completely different beast when it comes to this Physics based, mind-♥♥♥♥ of a game. The mods add so many layers or replayability that honestly make the base game's strengths shine even more! From mods that add an almost endless amount of guns, to mods that add new maps for you mess around in. There are even mods that add new functions in the game like being able to webswing like spiderman and a mod that adds MULTIPLAYER to Boneworks!

In conclusion, if you haven't bought this game yet i have one question for you: WHAT ARE YOU EVEN DOING WITH YOUR LIFE?
This game is an absolute masterpiece of game design and physics based tomfoolery. So if you have the money to buy this game and have a strong enough pc to run it with addons, please do so because once you play this game you'll be immersed in one of the best vr experiences ever.

Congrats to Stress Level Zero for making such an amazing game! I can't wait to get my hands on BoneLab when it finally releases!\t\t\t\t\t
