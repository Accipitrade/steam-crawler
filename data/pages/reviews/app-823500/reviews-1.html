\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile the game is good, it's not worth 38.99CAD anymore. Maybe at launch but not now.

I personally didn't enjoy the story/campaign and only ever really played the Zombie Warehouse and Fantasy Area modes.

Zombie Arena: The most enjoyable game mode in the game in my opinion. It's just waves of zombies and headcrabs that come at you.
- This game mode allows for the melee system to shine and it really is the best mechanic in the game. The only issue is guns suck in this mode. The only time you will really want a gun is to quickly deal with the headcrabs, but you can avoid then easily or block them with your melee weapon so they aren't difficult to deal with. When you're getting swarmed you don't have the time to aim for the head or reload making guns unideal, especially because you can't reload while holding another weapon like a sword or knife.
- Another issue with this game mode is any difficulty below harder (or even hardest) is so easy they are interchangeable. Harder is a alright difficulty for just messing around but it is still very hard to die unless you accidentally throw away your melee or your arm glitches through a wall. Hardest can be a good challenge if you wait long enough to get to the later waves, but for the most part you can just stack a bunch of healing orbs in one spot then run back through them when you have low health, and if you don't have anything other then 1 melee you can keep 5 full heals on you at all times.
- The largest issue with this game mode is you can 1shot every enemy with the katana that you can find on the roof of one of the zombie spawners. This removes all challenge from all game modes other then hardest because if you hit 0hp then you have a few seconds to kill a enemy, if you do then you wont die. Meaning solong as you are somewhat near an enemy you cannot die. You could argue to just not use the katana, but the katana just 1shots anywhere on the body, every other sharp melee can very easily 1shot if you just stab/slash at the side of a enemy's neck. Even stupider is that you can 1shot melee with the pistols if you hit a enemy there with the end of the slide/the back of the gun, and holding an ar with both hands and bringing it down on a enemy's head will also 1shot them. All enemies will die in 1 hit and pose no serious threat, meaning if you just isolate enemies and don't get swarmed (not hard to do) you won't take a lick of damage ever making the game mode very boring.

Fantasy Arena: I played this alot less then the Zombie Warehouse, still better then the story mode though.
- I only ever played the main part of this game mode once and got about 2 hours into it before getting bored, it's a good challenge.
- The main thing I used this mode for was 'The Ford Fight', I set all the entrances to spawn 20 Fords and I would just use my hands to kill them all. Very funny to do while high but it takes a while.
- It's overall a okay game mode, but it suffers from the same issues Zombie Warehouse does with melee being overpowered, It's just you'll need a gun sometimes when facing another opponent with a gun. I wish you could reload while holding a melee so you don't need to holster/drop your melee, reload, then pick it back up.\t\t\t\t\t
