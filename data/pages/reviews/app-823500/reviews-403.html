\r\n\t\t\t\t\tcan get a little janky but it's a very good vr game. probably my second favourite placed right before half life alyx. the only things i don't like are:

- the story: i don't get how the devs expect us to understand anything that is going on through words drawn on walls.

- melee: it's very janky but not terrible. an update improved it a little but i'd honestly even prefer Blade And Sorcery's melee physics. the key problem is the fact that you can't really slice, only stab.

- gun recoil: its really weird. the gun goes up and immediately comes back down at light speed. the recoil literally only looks normal if you press the slow-mo button twice.

- death system: similar to gorn. you heal very fast and if you 'die' you have a few seconds to kill any enemy to instantly revive yourself. the combat is easy enough in this game most of the time.... this makes it really difficult to die.

- sandbox: the whole idea of basically grinding the campaign for weeks to unlock all the presets for the sandbox is honestly stupid imo. some people don't have time for that and just want to play sandbox. this isn't that much if a big problem as you can just download someone else's save file but I still think that it isn't necessary.\t\t\t\t\t
