\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely awful and feels overrated as hell.

The story is just boring, after 3 chapters I've completely lost interest as there's nothing happening.

The level design is chaotic, almost feels like the design choices were an afterthought

Physics are glitching out making a lot of puzzles extremely annoying and frustrating, causing a lot of motion sicknes.

Navigating menus and features seems very unintuitive, I had to google up how to do a lot of things because the ingame tutorials aren's substential enough

The currency system (ammo) is stupid, as you can't use the other ammo type (either rifle or pistol bullets) if you do not hold that type of the weapon, for example, if you have a pistol and you want to buy a rifle, that costs 300, but you have 150 pistol bullets and 150 rifle bullets, you can't buy it, because you can't use those rifle bullets to pay unless you hold a rifle in your hands,

At this point I gave up.

Shooting seems cool, Melee combat is ok-ish, Sandbox is pretty fun too, but unless you get a 100% save, the game feels like a chore to play, you're better off playing Half-Life Alyx.

In the end I'm disappointed, despite hearing so many positive things about the game, I will give Bonelabs a fair try as well, hoping it's better than this.\t\t\t\t\t
