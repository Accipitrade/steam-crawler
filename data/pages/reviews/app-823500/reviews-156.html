\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really wanted to like this game, but it's janky and frustrating AF. The controls in general suck -climbing in particular- and you keep clipping into physics objects (and/or vice versa).
Climbing and physics feel so much better in (admittedly newer) games like Blade and Sorcery, and gun play in games like Pavlov... and both of those games have far superior graphics and are... fun. This is not fun. It's frustrating and kinda boring. It's not as clever as it thinks it is either.
So after taking 2 breaks of several weeks and giving it a third chance today, I ragequit again and am going to uninstall this. 20 Euros wasted if you ask me.
Then again, maybe I've been "playing it wrong". Either way, I can't recommend this.\t\t\t\t\t
