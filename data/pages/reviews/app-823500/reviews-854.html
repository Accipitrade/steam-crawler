Never before have I screamed out loud playing a VR game and thought it was fair.

This was impressive in every way, fluidness of gameplay, graphics, believability of the areas... I had to go put shoes on a couple of times because I got confused looking at my characters feet in the early game.

Pros: This sold me on the future of VR. I want to see more from them.

Cons:
I beat the game before I saw in a trailer you can put ammo in the vending machines to get things?
Climbing is awkward at first but is second nature by the end when you pick up the tricks.
I hit my low ceiling light bulbs a few times
The puzzles can interrupt the flow sometimes.
On oculus DK2, the time slow button is nearly impossible to use while maneuvering

Overall: They're under charging for what they offer in the base game alone.\t\t\t\t\t
