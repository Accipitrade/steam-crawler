\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic game, best enjoyed at a slower pace.

This game is absolutely the very best moderate-advanced VR experience out there. This game absolutely nailed the firearm "feel" when shooting. Physics are a blast to have fun with and the polish of this level is incredible in a VR game.

I see a ton of complaints about the problems in this game, and all of them a valid. The inventory management system is horrendous, and a ton of time is wasted trying to holster and retrieve weapons. Physics engine can be goofy at times and I don't think the game truly nailed the virtual body. However, none of the issues have stopped me from enjoying this game a great deal, and make no mistake that it's absolutely worth the asking price.

Thank you to the devs for releasing a game with 90% polish without it being a Early Access title. It's a breath of fresh air in a space thick with in-development experiences that ultimately ruins the final experience.\t\t\t\t\t
