\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is amazing. If you have a VR headset you NEED this game.

I will say, don't go into this game expecting it to have a really entertaining or interesting story, the game revolves less around its narrative and more its lore. If you want to understand the story of this game you should buy stress level zero's other game Duck Season.

But you don't come to Boneworks for the story, you come for the gameplay, and in that aspect it delivers. I've spent countless hours and will spend even more time just mucking around in the sandbox. There is so much to do with the small amount of tools they give you and with mods (which I would highly recommend getting) the possibilities are nearly endless.

Look, if you like SLZ, Half-Life, Gmod, or VR games in general then you NEED this game.
