\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is really fun when it's fun, but very frustrating at times.

Full disclosure - I am playing this on a Rift S - so that may be part of the issue. Because the Rift S uses integrated cameras to track movement, when your hands are not visible tracking is lost. This translates in Boneworks to very odd behavior, limbs being entangled or re-appearing at odd angles. It also makes carrying objects very cumbersome, as it needs to be done in view of the camera (which is very frustrating for some puzzles in the game).

The combat in this game is pretty great, love the manual action associated with all the firearms - a great touch of realism. Physical items are just ok.

The most frustrating part of this game is the ending - I won't include spoilers but after several levels of gunplay/combat with puzzles, you essentially are facing rote menial challenges (many of which involve climbing). The throne room fight is really aggrevating because of this.

All-in-all it's a bunch of fun, just be forewarned there are parts that will really really frustrate you.\t\t\t\t\t
