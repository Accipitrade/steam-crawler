Jumping, Climbing, Arm-Banding it just all feels rough. Grabbing onto something to try to pull yourself up a ledge doesn't ever work properly. Carrying larger objects is at a crawl pace or your arms are rubberbanding everywhere. Overall its a fun little test world but until mechanics are placed into real games with narratives, thats all this really is. It's worth checking out to see some decent ideas for future VR titles but as a game, I don't find it fun at all.\t\t\t\t\t
