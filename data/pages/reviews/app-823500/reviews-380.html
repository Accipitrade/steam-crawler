\r\n\t\t\t\t\tthis was one of the few games I pre-ordered. looking back at the experience, and replaying it with a better PC, I just feel disappointed. concepts, physics and maps were great, but other things really hindered my experience.

initial playthrough: Game ran terrible when i first tried to run it on my Ryzen 1200 + gtx 1050ti, upgraded to a ryzen 1600 AF and a gtx 1060 3gb, but the game still had issues running.

Gunplay: I thought the arsenal was going to big, but they still haven't even implemented a shotgun. I've been spoiled by H3VR, so i was disappointed to not see a mag release button, slide release for a pistol, a bolt release for the ar-15, or the ability to HK slap a mp5. Everything involving firearms, H3VR does better.

Melee and physics puzzles. was revolutionary when it came out. but now, Half Life Alyx does physics puzzles better, and Walking Dead saints and sinners does melee better.

headset specific: was using a WMR headset, and it hurts to hold down the grip button for +2 hours at a time. you can make it toggleable, but this is not officially supported, and it is cumbersome to use when you're just picking up and dropping non-melee items. Also, the slo motion button uses the same thumb as the joystick for moving, So I can't use slo motion while moving.

replaying it now: i now have a Ryzen 2600x + RTX 3060ti. played through the first 3 levels, 3rd level still dropped resolution at the outdoor location. and overall, it just didn't seem fun for me anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
