\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had fun playing playing this game. The game physics are the best I have experienced in VR. A lot of people say it went too far, but honestly I feel all the decisions they made on how the physics behave make a lot of sense when you think about how it would work in the real world. I would say most people will find this game to be realism heavy and story light. There is a story/lore in the game but unless you are detective and take your magnifying glass out, in the game and also in the Boneworks discord, you are not going to find much of a story if you are not the type of person to to dig in like that. For example, the lore of Boneworks is partially connected to Stress Level Zero's other games. All of Stress Level Zero's games take place in the same universe. I personally would recommend the game as you will have fun smashing enemies with various objects and using the guns. Maybe buy it on sale if you only like games with a good story.\t\t\t\t\t
