\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is hands down my favourite (non-social) VR game so far. It really goes above and beyond when it comes to virtual immersion in graphics, sound, music, game play, and world building lore.

Everything is physics based with extreme detail to your virtual body. It's a little experimental as its a clearly a work in progress engine, so often things get wacky in the precision department when it comes to certain actions like climbing or moving/interacting with certain objects, but it's part of the fun. The game mostly consists of these elements of interaction/exploration alongside combat involving guns and blunt/edged weapons.

There is a story mode, an arena mode with two different levels with multiple types of challenges, and an awesome sandbox game mode that works a lot like Garry's Mod. You have to unlock every game mode and items for sandbox by fully exploring the story mode and collecting capsules and ammo which sort of works like money.

The music and sound effects are completely awesome. This game has one of the best and varied soundtracks in a video game, for real. It goes hard and amazes with mysterious ambience. The sound effects are both scary and hilariously appropriate. Just hit someone with a frying pan. You'll love it.

The graphics are great for a VR game, and only comes second to Alyx in that regard. Every texture is bootiful and the shaders and bump/normal maps are no exception. The aesthetic of the game is very meta and liminal, and creates a very strange, mysterious, and almost creepy atmosphere.

The lore and story are very abstract and sprinkled around as hidden rooms and graffiti but gives enough info in a straight forward manner to help everything makes sense. The story is going to be expanded upon in later games so dont expect every answer by the end.

If you have a VR headset, you owe it to yourself to play this game. It's hopefully the future of VR gaming and immersion.

Can't wait for Bonelabs :)\t\t\t\t\t
