\r\n\t\t\t\t\t*Spoiler Free Review*
8/10. This game is a great showcase of vr physics and how to make a fun and intuitive VR first person shooter

Pros:
-Guns feel amazing to shoot and reload
-melee weapons are incredibly fun to use
-Physics engine is just realistic enough to be immersive while still being fun to mess with
-Inventive visuals
-Pretty much anything you want to do you can do (explained in more detain below)

Cons:
-Story and Lore are almost completely unexplained and extremely vague (Don't play this game if you are looking for a good story)
-Only five enemies, one of witch is basically a reskin of another
-level design is bad at some points

I would only recomend buying this game because of its amazingly fun gameplay which i keep coming back to again and again because there is almost no end to the number of things you can do. Want to fist fight a hoard of zombies? go ahead. Want to throw someone into a dumpster? do it! Want to jump off a roof and headshot ten enemies before you hit the ground? Be my guest! This game lends itself perfectly to all the inventive and fun ideas that you come up with. The only thing i find myself wishing this game had was more stuff, which the developers happily deliver with regular updates.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really can't recommend the game in its current state. There's numerous issues and strange design decisions that just make for a frustrating experience. The lack of saves (and the lack of a warning that your progress will be lost if you quit) is the most glaring, but I've had the game crash on me mid-level (the Sewer level specifically), and in Warehouse the warp to the next level just straight up didn't work, leaving me stuck and forcing me to restart the whole level.

I really think the game has the opportunity to be great, but right now the single player is just unplayable. The kicker is the sandbox would be great to at least play around with until the single player issues get fixed, but unlocking all the sandbox has to offer requires you to find secrets in the campaign. I like what I've played, but I just can't keep replaying levels that I shouldn't need to replay because it didn't save or crashed.

One last thing I forgot to mention: Please allow the ability to swap between ammo types without needing the specific weapon that uses that type. Every time I get to the dispenser in the intro of a level I'm forced to use only one ammo type because I haven't found any weapons yet, so even though I have hundreds of the other type of ammo I'm out of luck unless I happen to find the weapon I need and run back to the dispenser.\t\t\t\t\t
