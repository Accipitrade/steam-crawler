\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSPOILERS AHEAD

Just beat the game, and let me say, it was fantastic. Now I got the index controllers near the end of my play through and the Vive controllers were a little annoying at first but I got used to them. Once I got the Index controllers it was way better but it was still playable with the Vive wands. Anyways, the physics are great. Being able to hit a guy in the face with my pistol and then grab his legs, throw him to the ground, and shoot him in the face just feels amazing. It also has some of the most cinematic moments. Like when you walk into the room full of guys with headsets on and the music from the trailer is playing. Or when you battle the king after beating the arena. And when you open the vault thing, enemies everywhere, you frantically run to the building, climb up the ladder, and you see the cut-scene, they found you, all of a sudden the void monsters pop up. I can't recommend this game enough. Also being someone who is a fan of FNaF, cryptic lore linking across multiple games is a welcome addition.
Also as the game says, this is for expert VR users, and they mean that.

The REDACTED update: This update is amazing. Before I get into the content, I'm going to talk about a major change they made. The melee combat. Before if I was holding a heavy weapon with one hand, it would flop all over the place and be impossible to use, but now they've changed that to where it doesn't completed flop around, and it feels great. I also noticed that my hands feel super good/ not as floaty as they used to be. Now onto the main course: The new levels. The first level you play is the REDACTED chamber. This is a fun little level that shows off a lot of the cool content in the game (as well as an easy way to get the flash light and spear). If there was a demo for BONEWORKS, this would be it. Next up, the hand gun course. Well, it's more than a handgun course. You go on this cool little adventure to get there. Climbing on pipes to avoid toxic ooze right below you, battling zombies and not headcrabs, it's fun. But then you get to the handgun course, and oh man oh man oh man. The song that's playing before was great, but this is just so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ good. Your jumping around corners is slow-mo, guns a-blazin', while you got this kick-ass song playing. And then near the end you ride on this zipline and you can shoot people while your on it. In Markiplier's video of BONEWORKS he was on the zipline in the museum level and he said "I could just imagine shooting people on this" and ever since I saw that I really want that, and sadly the one in the handgun course is a little short, but it still feels really cool. Anyways, you get to the end of the handgun course, and there it is, the Tuscany level. Once you beat the handgun course you are sent to it, and man what a blast from the past. I remember when I saw that BONEWORKS oculus trailer and I was like "Wait, that looks really familiar." The level itself is more than just a throwback to the classic level, it's a unique sandbox level, and level in general. You got a house, an awesome song, and bright colors which you don't see often in BONEWORKS. There are also some new weapons, like the new pistol, or those cool blade things that are like brass knuckles but the knuckle part is a frickin' BLADE. This REDACTED update is so frickin' amazing, If you haven't played it yet I can't recommend it enough.


When the game is going great, and you just are doing things with no thought, just real life muscle memory, well, as Todd Howard said (not referring to Boneworks but to a likely buggy and unfinished game), "It just works."\t\t\t\t\t
