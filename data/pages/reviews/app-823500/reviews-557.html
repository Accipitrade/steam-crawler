My up Front Thoughts
I love it. I would have said "loved" because i finished the story, but there's still so much to do that i think ill be playing many more hours in the sandbox and arena modes. Furthermore i'm thinking of going for all achievements, I've never once been an achievement hunter, but this game is just all round fun.

Where The Game Failed Me
First of all, the pros well outweigh the cons. This game is a masterpiece, but no artwork is ever finished.
The games few quirks where, the inventory, its probably no fault of the devs, I'm sure getting the parameters on such a thing is very hard, and they did it very well, but its when you think you put something on your back and then run off unaware its on the ground that interrupts otherwise smooth gameplay and can annoy one when they get to intense moments. Secondly there's the story, now don't get me wrong, it was good, but in juxtaposition to the gameplay it felt a little lackluster.
That's it, that's all i had an issue with, the occasional inventory mishaps and the story being just good, other then these two minute(and admittedly petty) things the game is amazing.

9.9/10, if you can, get it.\t\t\t\t\t
