\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
Great physics, you literally can interact with almost all of the environment and everything reacts in some way.
Great gunplay, it is made and developed by a gun guy, guns have weight and actually work like guns. Reloading feels good, you don’t just press a button, you need to manually reload.
Amazing sandbox levels, try Pistol Range once you get it, it is amazing, you feel like John Wick. All others are fun and allow you to just do stuff.
Puzzles are amazing, they’re actually fun, and exciting and only sometimes give you mild headaches trying to get out just so you can save and exit,
All the pros are the stuff you heard before, the game is the best, buy it.
Cons:
Enemies Attacks are always random (due to the physics, I’m pretty sure) meaning melee just gets funky sometimes.
Not many guns, just please add a shotgun and I’ll be happy.
A lot of the sandbox and all of the arena levels are locked behind a long playwall, where you have to complete a very long (but fun) campaign to play.
The saving is still clunky, they fixed the no save point issue a while ago (before I even had the game) but still save points come in at undistributed points, and when you reload a save, the entire level’s environment reloads respawning all the creatures at the previously defeated points.
There is only one armed enemy, there is only one armed enemy with only one weapon, a rifle for every single armed enemy.
Robot Crabs that jump onto your face put you in vr (in vr, in vr) and leach your blood

Statement: Buy the game, and don’t think twice about it, the cons are more of warning
