\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWonderful VR-Game with a lot of sandbox/puzzle/parcouring elements and a rather (good) strange story and funny easter eggs to discover. Somehow for me its the first consistent singleplayer VR experience i played so far.

In short: I really like it and totally recommend it!

Pre HL-A update:

If you are lookig for an intense singleplayer VR experience -> HL:A

If you want good and intense gunplay -> H3VR

Boneworks is somewhere between... the most disturbing thing in boneworks are your bodyphysics. They kill the immersion as they lack of intuitive and normal bodybehaviour.
Everything from climbing to jumping, yet even walking feels like you are stuck in spastic, epilleptic clonky body of a human in its mid 80ies. The physics of the objects in general feel quiet well but are also clunky, but thats rather a problem.
A patch which would set you free from your virtual and uncomfortable bodie would be imho the most needed thing for this game. I can appreciate the efforts of trying out to give you a sense for your virtual body, but in this case I would say it doesnt feel right and reallymakes the game frustrating and unenjoyable to me.

So with better alternatives out there (story-graphics-gunplay) I cant recommend this game anymore.\t\t\t\t\t
