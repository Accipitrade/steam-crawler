\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been waiting on this game to drop for a long while. I am mostly happy with it.

+ The physics are pretty sweet to play with.

+ Its optimized pretty well too. I'm running the game on medium with my rig
HTC Vive
Valve Knuckles
AMD Ryzen 7 2700X
EVGA 1070TI
32GB RAM

+ No fps drops that I have seen outside of just spawning in.

I've been sticking to VR gaming for the past couple months mostly playing H3VR or Blade and Sorcery.
I know the Devs played both of these games and was really hoping they would follow their lead.

- My biggest disappointment in this game is the locomotion.
Not including an armswinger mode for running. (This is a crucial part of combating motion sickness for me. Tricking my brain into thinking my body is moving via the motion and a bent knee stance.)
As such this is the first time in a while I've had to stop playing because of motion sickness. Also not being able to swing arms to run breaks the immersion for me. I would prefer to be active while gaming such is why I've been sticking to VR.

- The item holding system is a little annoying when it come to ammo. The game is strictly setup for a righty. I can shoot ambidextrous IRL but my dominant hand is my left. with the ammo layout ONLY being able to be pulled from the left is frustrating as all hell.
You can eat ‚ô•‚ô•‚ô•‚ô• if you tell me to shoot the 'right way' only

- The handling response for melee needs some tweaking for sure. I'm a big guy with a good amount of muscle. I can swing a sledgehammer hard and fast. When doing it in game it feels way too sluggish. So I slow down the movement to compensate. That in turn drops off the force of the blow.\t\t\t\t\t
