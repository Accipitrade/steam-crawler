Glitching while climbing, your arms getting stuck on things, and carrying cubes around that are heavy in game but have no physical weight - which devolves into your arms being much higher IRL then in game or you not lifting your arms enough to actually carry this object and glitching around on and inside of it over and over again. Jumping on boxes or chairs or trying to lift yourself up onto ledges feels horrible and just in general the game feels miserable to play. Some of the puzzles require precision thats not possible with how janky it all feels and how bad it controls. If they fix the "no save in 2 hour + levels" thing, and can iron out some of these janky controls than maybe I would recommend it and repurchase it. But at the moment I'd steer clear unless you have money to waste, and nothing to play.\t\t\t\t\t
