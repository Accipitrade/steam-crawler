\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend this game with a rating of 3/5. That's a pretty abstract rating, so let me explain.

The single player story is playable. I finished the story. I remained interested in the game for the whole time.

However, I didn't really understand the point of the game. What were the collectibles that I found throughout the levels? Part of the tutorial explained how I can permanently save items that I place in the special bin, but I never found any way to "browse my collection" or anything like that.
Was the tutorial incomplete? Or perhaps not enough instruction was provided in-game? I never learned how to use the vending machines I saw everywhere. I figured i was missing some bonus currency that would be unlocked eventually. Only after completing the game and reading a guide did i find out you're supposed to put ammo into those to get out the goods.

The main feature of the game seems to be the physics engine. But this seems to mean you spend a lot of time climbing and falling.
