\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks is easily the best VR shooter/story game I have ever played. The amount of time you can spend sitting and admiring the way your in-game body matches your real life body is endless. The story is intriguing and leaves you wanting to play other SLZ to discover secrets for yourself. The guns are incredibly fun to use and react how you would expect them to.

The base game of Boneworks has plenty of content for you to enjoy. This includes the main story, 2 arenas, and 4 or more sandbox levels. Each of these levels have their own guns and secret items to discover. Once I had finished the main story, I was surprised to see that there were so many things that I could still do. After completing everything that the game had to offer, I discovered a multiplayer mod that allowed me to experience the game again with friends.

I took a break from the game for months. After the announcement of the new SLZ game "BONELABS" which looks to be an even better Boneworks, I had to retry the game and start getting used to VR again. Coming back to this game gave me so many things to love about VR. The strong community that this game built expands on the already large content that is avaliable. There are hundreds of maps that you can download (and play with your friends), custom weapons, npcs, and player models.

Words cannot explain how much this game has changed VR for me and for a lot of people. I have a lot of hype built for this new game, and I can recommend a playthrough of this game to help you see what the hype is about.\t\t\t\t\t
