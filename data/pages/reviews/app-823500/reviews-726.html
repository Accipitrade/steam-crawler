\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is definetly my favorite vr game of all time. The controls took some getting used to but after awhile i got completely used to them. It took me awhile to learn to crouch down and stand up while jumping to jump higher but once i learned it, it was pretty easy, the gunplay is extremely good, and makes me wish i wasnt cross dominant, the melee weapons arent as good as a game like blade & sorcery but after a while you get used to it. If you like games that are involved with the story for most of the game, then this is not really for you, the story seems nonexistent, but if you look extremely close you can find some stuff out. I especially love how a lot of the levels have multiple paths, like how in one level you can skip half of it by shooting a button that you normally have to walk a long distance to press. I've heard a lot of people get sick from playing this game but apparently im the messiah or something because this game (or any vr game really) has never made me sick. I love this game and will continue to play it for a long time. sadly my left oculus touch controller has joystick drift that stops me from moving directly forwards and left, and forces me to move right and sometimes backwards. this also causes me to walk off ledges or knock things over, but that isnt a problem with boneworks, thats a problem with my controllers. overall i reccomend this game to people who are experienced with vr, (especially smooth locomotion because thats the only form of travel) dont get sick from vr that often, and are okay with a lot of physical activity, i forgot to mention earlier but after playing this game for a while i get very tired and because of the high jumps ive probably done more squats in this game then i have in real life. I hope you all have a great day!\t\t\t\t\t
