\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA lot of fun, physics are definitely a must experience. Gunplay and combat is pretty decent too.
Visually, the game is a mixed bag, a few areas look pretty stunning, while some look a bit lacking.

However it doesn't take away from the fact how awesome the game feels, especially once you have mastered the movement to the fullest.

Even after unlocking all the weapons, sandbox items, and more, I still keep coming back.
I simply can't get enough of the movement in this game.

I sincerely look forward to seeing more games by Stress level zero, and hope it includes this level of interactivity or better!

Thanks for creating such and awesome game, and pushing the VR medium forward.

I can no enjoy older VR games the same way as I used to, after playing Boneworks.

Floating hands, Teleporting, no jumping, and phasing hands through objects just doesn't cut it anymore, and Boneworks shows it! So let's hope future VR games catch on with that.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBasic Review: Thanks for the best VR game on the market today. With the exception of there not being a multiplayer, which we knew.

Graphics: 9/10 (skin looks weird to me... but if you minus the hands, 10/10 lighting and colors are on point)

Sound: 9/10 (sound is great, but the gunshots on max volume for Index causes a popping sound on the audio, just dropped it to 70-80% and problem was gone. which is literally fine.

Movement: 9/10 (locomotion is smooth, but you will sticky finger some objects in passing by accident or have your arms get stuck in geometry like a fence, and panic as you're getting mauled by half sentient tangerine people.)

Combat: 8/10 (some melee weapons just don't feel like they are hitting strong enough? A battle axe to the head will hurt them badly and get wedged in their orange head. But an M1911 pistol whip to their wireframe teeth kills them right off the bat. a stock m16 has significantly better accuracy in slo mo than the m16 with a fore grip and green laser sight. the modded m16 with said components wobbles Everywhere and makes follow up shots a nightmare. Shooting in real time is just fine though)

Story: ?/10? Gonna say 10 so far.. I am only 3 hours in and i took my time with the dozen of hidden puzzles. Tutorial was naturally blended in to the story in a way that seriously impressed me and made me chuckle multiple times. The setting of the game has Valve game (portal and half life) vibe. You can see the inspiration. But also the Originality. So far it's very impressive. And from the amount of care and love they put into this, $30 for this title feels like I'm robbing them. Get this. You will not be disappointed.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlaying on the Index and I cant complain.

Game feels great I love the gameplay and it feels really immersive.

What suprised me is that the puzzles are actually difficult Im usually always sceptical about puzzles in games cause they are just too easy/obvious and get boring fast but not here.

Actually I would love to talk about how natural it sometimes felt for me.
I picked up a hammer and had to go throu a boarded up fence gate so I naturally turned my hammer so the hooks pointed forward and hooked them into the wooden boards pulling them off the fence and behold it worked exactly like I thought and actually suprised me.
And the other time there was a turret shooting at me while beeing obvious to some people I paniced and grabbed the first thing that came to my mind, a trashcan lid and used it as a shield again worked perfectly and made me feel good.

Also worthy to point out I have a couple of hundret hours in VR across all sorts of games and I developed a "feeling" for my cable so I dont stand/trip on it but this game pulls me in so much and is so immersive to me that I often trip on my cable. Damn I wish I could get a wireless adapter now.

So 100% buying recommendation. Game is really fun and the price is suprisingly low.

Only thing that bothers me is that you get handed alot of guns without doing anything. This in itself doesnt sounds bad but there ar evending machines that sell guns and I really love that feature but you will find so many weapons it doesnt make sense to buy them, sadly.
For example there was an axe laying around one or two rooms before the vending machine that had the same axe in stock.
Or I bought 2 suprise boxes that gave me two machine pistols soon after I found a gun and an assault rifle.\t\t\t\t\t
