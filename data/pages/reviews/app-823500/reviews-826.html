\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game gives me anxiety, you're in a city where an Operating System runs everything including the AI robots and holo soldiers and everything goes on the fritz when your character (the head of security) plugs in a hard drive with a virus from a rival tech company, so your job is to make it through the virus infested city fighting these now hostile robots and holo guards to make it to a tower to reset the OS so you get info to a rival company or something. (apparently there is a lot of hidden lore and easter eggs as well hidden in the basic story i just explained)

It has Half Life/Portal vibes to it, with puzzles and physic systems that do make you rack your brain for a little bit. combat with melee weapons and fists can be incredibly wonky but the gunplay is a ton of fun, there are some things i think they could do better like saving and stuff like that, having to put everything onto a pad is a bit of a pain but you get used to it the more you play.

This is one of the "must have" VR Games if you're a fan of anything valve has done or looking for games for your VR headset. It's a great way to get introduced and sucked into VR, it even has a horde mode and some other side stuff you can do to mess around in if you're bored or haven't had your fill after the main story like going back for collectibles or a horde/scenario mode where you can do bar brawls or zombie survival etc etc.

It's a solid 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
