\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start by saying that I’ve been following this game since way before its release. And I’ve been very excited to play it for a long time, even considering/expecting it to be another landmark in VR evolution. Then why can’t I recommend it? First, the game is VERY motion-sickness inducing. This, by itself, would not be a reason for me not to recommend it. But the devs insist in only allowing you to save in selected places which are too far away from each other. There is no “save at any time” nor “save and quit”. The consequence is that, if you start feeling sick you would have to decide to either endure the sickness or lose all your progress. Now, is the game’s motion sickness that bad? Yes! It is THE worse game I have ever played (motion sickness wise) – and I’ve been playing VR for more than a year now. If you don’t believe me, the Internet is your friend. So, devs, please allow us to leave the game whenever we feel sick or you’ll be left with a good game that not many will be able to play.\t\t\t\t\t
