\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's kind of unofficial mix of Half Life, Portal and Garry's Mod and it's very well done.
The game took me about 14 hours to finish on the first play through, but there's still some replay value in the story levels, regarding hidden bonuses I started to intentionally skip, so I can finish the story first.
The game is not perfect. Everything is physics based and you will experience physics glitches (lots of them) but just like in G-Mod that's usually part of the fun.
You can solve puzzles and battles in very creative ways using physical objects, often in ways you feel like it wasn't intended to (but then maybe it was?) - The game is great in this regard.
The story is good enough to keep you interested, but confusing at times and well, thats it. You should play this game because of its gameplay and not the story.

The lack of a saving system within the levels was the biggest bummer for me, because this way I could only play it when I knew I have a few hours of time (Especially the first levels took me 1-2 hours each!). But they announced a checkpoint system coming soon, which should help future players with this.

I experienced no motion sickness at all, but then I usually don't get motion sick. The game is definitely intense in this regard and due to the lack of a save system (for now). You can only take a break while leaving the gaming running, without loosing progress.

Anyway it's definitely a must-play for everyone who can play it.
