\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI want to like this game, but find it difficult at the moment. A few things to consider if you are thinking about buying this game.
Graphics and physics are great. It has a Portal type feel and environment. This is very well done.
The arms, the arms. They feel and act like deflated tube balloons. The grip mechanism is broken when holding onto longer objects, like a sledge hammer or baseball bat. Your wrists tend to bend backwards a defy the laws of anatomy on so many levels. This has to be fixed.
No save option? "Why Craig?"
Had to restart the training after stepping away AND after restarting and finishing the training, game not only crashes but completely shuts down Steam along with it.
Another issue, I experienced at least two instances where the haptic feedback on my Vive controllers (both at separate times) would not shut off after a trigger event for quite some time.
Again, I want to like this game and maybe one day I will after said issues are addressed, but currently I'm afraid to relaunch it after the crash for fear of having to walk through the training all over again.\t\t\t\t\t

Apparently they have put in a checkpoint system now, so my old review is null and void, but I will keep it there as a reminder that at some point it did not exist when it should have been a part of the base game.

Overall, it's brilliant. The physics are incredibly fun and interactive, the combat is extremely fun if overall a bit basic, the world is interesting, music is great, and I'm running out of things to compliment. The puzzles, while not my cup of tea, are objectively good, and I cannot deny it. I'm terrible at puzzles with my caveman brain, but they are satisfying and fair (most of the time), so big plus to the devs for that.

In terms of what could be improved, it would be great to add a larger variety of weapons, though I understand the limitations and not wanting to turn this game into Horseshoes and Hand Grenades.

-----------------------------------------------------------


I love it, but-...Why can't I save?

Seriously, why? Each level can take an hour or more, especially if I'm a bit slow with puzzle solving. I get tired and then what? I can't even save and pick up where I left it off? Not even a checkpoint?

It's genuinely the only thing I can't stand. At LEAST a checkpoint system.

When this feature is implemented, I will recommend it. Until then, it's beyond stupid how I have to repeat the whole level if I get tired and stop or if the game crashes.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have never written a game review before but feel compelled to for reasons that will become obvious.

First, this game is mind blowing. I’ve been into VR for about 4 months, long enough to get used to VR and enjoy this to the full.

I invested in a high end 2080 super i9 having never owned a gaming PC before. The main reason for this was Node videos of Boneworks.

I started with PSVR then a quest. Oculus link (and the prospect of Boneworks) convinced me to shell out on the PC.... and a rift S as I could not wait. So you can tell how hyped I was for this....

And the highest praise I can give is that I was not disappointed. I was in fact surprised that my expectations were exceeded.

Brandon and the team - just wow..... You’ve definitely shown the triple A brigade how it can and should be done. My sincerest thanks and amazement have been well earned.

I look forward to VR games moving in the right direction, without Stress Level Zero this may not have happened.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPro: 10/10 quality game
The weapon handling is perfect.
Music is epic AF
Felt like i was playing HL2 again when the combine soldiers we're chasing you in the beginning.
Everything collides with you in the game so u can be flippin tables and smackin ♥♥♥♥ around with melee weapons.
The way the little rollie pollie guys die is like how enemies die in FF7/8 such an awesome touch.
Not gonna lie having two fully automatic weapons fighting enemies in full slow mo felt like the scene in the Matrix 1 from when Neo was fighting the guards in the lobby in slow mo. That was ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ sick.
Overall a really clean game for only $30 is almost a steal.

Cons:
No checkpoints

Small critiques:
More collision with smaller objects (chairs, small foam squares, ect.)
Remove AR reload where you reload it like an AK and us another mag to change if going for realism.
Pulling the mag out of pistols by accident gets kinda of annoying when trying to use two hands for pistol stability.
The way the pistol snaps when you fire it when not in slow motion just looks kinda funny maybe smooth that out.
Add a drop mag button besides having to open menu for Oculus controllers..(not so bad though)
More guns please lol.
Also let me destroy the damn radios in the game or put different songs on them!! lol\t\t\t\t\t
