This game was absolutely breathtaking from visuals, to gameplay, and to the story which might not be a favorite for some people. The story is very flat on first glimpse but when you start learning about the lore and little secret messages hidden throughout the world the story opens up into something really great. the environments in this game make you really feel like you are there. the world is designed so beautifully to make you feel alone but that there's always someone watching you. After you complete the story you unlock Arena mode which is like Blade and Sorcery only better and is actually optimized to work. It's really fun to time your shots, grabs, and punches just right to get some John Wick style moves on your enemies.

All in all this was a fantastic game and experience and is definitely on my top 10 games of all time. play this game right now if you haven't and have a VR headset\t\t\t\t\t
