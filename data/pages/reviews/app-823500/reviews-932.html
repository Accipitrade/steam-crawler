\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHalf Life & Portal had a baby and SLZ adopted it and raised it on a diet of Mellon Belly and NOODLES.
Boneworks is fantastic, and if the trailer looks good to you, you get what you see and so much more.
SLZ hits almost every nail on the head in creating a lighthearted but dark (and sometimes scary) world for the player to romp through and uncover its secrets.
Aside from HL: Alyx, (which came out after BW), Boneworks sets the standard to for high quality, large scale/scope narrative VR adventures.
The only weaknesses (post 1.4 patch) are the 'jank' that drag down some puzzles, which feel like they were designed for a system with better polish, and also the melee combat. After playing games like Blade & Sorcery, the melee combat here feels similarly 'jank', with everything but the slow-mo stab or headcrab beatdown feeling too unreliable to pull off.

P.S. Dont listen to the people who say that they story is weak. The story is like an Iceberg. It's GIGANTIC, but 90% is below the surface.
P.P.S. I know that style for story telling is not to everyone's liking, but the story is there.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t15 hours put in, finished the "story" left with a feeling of disappointment. The entire story feels like a tutorial and has no real cohesion. It felt dragged out and too long because of the listed opinions. Arena mode is locked until completion of the story, which is awful if you'd like to jump into some fun fighting without committing at least 8-9 hours to the story mode. Sandbox mode which lets you test out all the cool and quirky mechanics of the game is almost completely locked as well since all the items need to be unlocked by individually finding them throughout the different story maps and bringing them to bins. Understandably, some people would enjoy hunting through the game to find everything. Other people however, can find this to be a very painstaking task especially since there is no option to have everything unlocked without going through the whole game. No game is perfect, but after waiting for this game for so long, you can't help but walk away somewhat let down. Personally I give this game a 5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
