Its a somewhat fun but ultimately mediocre vr game and is in no way an "breakthrough vr experience" or "secret half life game" that all the shills on all the gaming boards and websites and youtube videos and comments keep parroting out. And if you criticize the game at all, say in the steam forums, the devs and white knights will bombard you with shaming comments. This makes me dislike the game even more now to where I don't recommend this game to anyone.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rarely leave reveiws but going out of my way to write this one. At the time of writing this, if you own a VR system, you need to play this game. I truly believe it is next generation of VR games. Most VR games I've played feel kind of gimicky or more like they are minigames rather than an actually fully fleshed out project. Boneworks is the first VR game I've played that feels like a real game, I played through the game at a pretty regular pace while still going out of my way to explore and find secrets, but the game still took me 11 hours to finish the main story, which is about on par with most FPS games outside of VR. My only real negatives about this game is kind of feels like there are a lack of weapons, the whole game I was waiting for an opportunity to find a shotgun. The fact that there are multiple ways to take on each encounter makes up for this however. The other negative was some of the physics based puzzles late into the game, the physics still feels a bit janky which can sometimes make puzzles very frustrating. The other part of this statement is that the game doesn't seem to work as well on older software. I don't know how many times I would be climbing a ladder with my original Oculus Rift and the left hand would lose tracking making my lose my grip and fall. At one point late game it got bad enough that I almost quit the level because there was just so much climbing involved.

TLDR: Good: Inovative, multiple approaches to puzzles, true freedom, quality world interaction.
Bad: Janky controls on older software, occasionally janky phyics, slight lack of guns (plenty of melee though).\t\t\t\t\t
