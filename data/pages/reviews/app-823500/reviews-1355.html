My only complaint would be the walking with Vive touch pads, I personally hate having to hold it down when ever i walk would much rather have it so i can press once and rest my thumb on the pad like in VRchat or something.
Also with the Vive wands the vibrations make my controller sound like its buzzing/beeping, I think it might be drawing too much power to make it vibrate since its more set for the index, Makes it feel like my wand will burn out eventually. other than that all the game play is super solid and alot of fun, even with the little complaints i still give this a 10/10\t\t\t\t\t

Great graphics, some textures seems low res but overall the graphical experience is pretty great. There is an Arena, Sandbox, and Story mode obviously. I have not yet tried anything but story. Well worth the purchase price and exactly the quality you'd expect from Stress Level Zero! Great Devs and a great VR experience. I have spent roughly 4 hours ingame granted I did restart.
