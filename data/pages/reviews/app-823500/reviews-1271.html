\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is the gold standard for single player VR shooter games, they have nailed the sense of presence and the mechanics of how to do a game like this.

This might not be a Half-Life game but it very much has the feel of a half life/portal inspired universe.

Doesn't have the same polish as a triple a game and there are bugs but that is understable for an indie team, i really hope they continue to refine this and perhaps release their code on the unity store so others can use this to build great VR games.

Caveats, you need strong VR legs for this game. The climbing and the way your player can be moved by physical interactions can be unsettling. I recommend taking breaks after any sort of climbing/physics puzzle as that sick/sweaty feeling tends to grow and then slowly fade.
