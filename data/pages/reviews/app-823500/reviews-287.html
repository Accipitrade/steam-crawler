In the Top 5 of my favorite games of all time.

Michael Wyckoff made some of the best soundtracks in a game of all time with his 80s "Hackerman" Synthwave. The music is down to a science in this game, capturing every moment with unique soundtracks. It really captures the feeling of the game.

Story is there if you actively pay attention and seek it. It is easy to play this game and completely miss the purpose of Arthur Ford's adventure.

The gameplay can be quirky, as everything is physics based. Movement will take a moment to get used to, even if you have experienced 'VR legs.' However, simply put, if you ask yourself "Can I do this?" in Boneworks, there is a solid 99% chance that YOU CAN. Whether it is beating enemies to death with a coffee mug, breaking a lock with a brick, or going full Street Fighter and tackling enemies, YOU CAN. Unleash your inner John Wick with GunFu.

Between the Soundtrack, Gameplay, and the "Aftermath" feelings of completing the game, this is a highly recommended VR game. The interaction is definitely "next-gen" for Virtual Reality, and has set the bar for interacting with a VR environment.\t\t\t\t\t
