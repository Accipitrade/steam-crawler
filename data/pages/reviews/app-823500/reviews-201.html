\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell I guess it's not my cup of tea looking at the positive reviews of the vast majority. This game is disturbing for me, the sounds the environment, the type of how the player moves. I mean on a flat screen it probably wouldnt matter, but in VR everything is more immersive so it feels disturbing. It's not only the fact that this game induces motion sickness but the goal of the game is just exploring, which I find personally boring.


Also the game praises itself to be super physics based, but really I didn't find too many differences to other games. You can interact with the world, move stuff, throw it away or break it. Well nothing extraordinary. The only difference is maybe that you can grab enemies and other creatures and climb, but even that feels weird. Climbing for example feels very weird and not realistic. So to sum up, for me this game is boring, I just bought it because it has good ratings and I saw a few cool videos on Youtube.

Also I can't understand the praised graphics. For me its meh, nothing like Half Life Alyx for example.\t\t\t\t\t
