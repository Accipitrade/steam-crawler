I only played this game for 2.8 hours and only finished the first real level, but the amount of content & length is already worthy for a Review. Visually this game is stunning, it contains realistic & futuristic furniture which are all moveable. You can mess around with every single object in this game, it gives you the freedom to do anything. Gameplay wise there is no better VR game around currently. Boneworks combines many games their great gameplay and makes it one. Its gameplay shows what VR can be & its limitations. The Climbing can be really annoying because of your Bodys physics and mass. Gunplay feels really good and satisfying. Its Atmosphere is great by the music and ambient sound design. Even on the "VR Legs overdrive" it didnt make me motion sick once, but may differ on you. There is no teleportation option understandably, so this may not be your game if you get motion sick easily. I may update this Review when played through the entire game, but for now this is it. The story and lore is also there, but not shoved in your face. The mod scene for this game is also amazing, its a bit small compared to anothher big VR combat game, but it's still rich with new weapons, and content for the game. Overall Boneworks is an excellent game\t\t\t\t\t

Graphics: 8/10 Great, the simplistic visuals play very well to the gameplay as well as storyline.

Gameplay: 9/10 Amazing, amazing Gunplay and exploration, however due to the nature of the game being as realistic as possible the un-realistic stands out (Examples, sometimes if you swing a bat at an object like a bucket it will get stuck in the matter of the bucket, fell off a ledge but stick your arms up? Get ready to break your in-game wrists)

Storyline: 6/10 Not the best but not the worst, connects with other games by Stress Level Zero with THE VOID story line. An understanding of the story isn't essential to play but it enhances the experience. If you don't fully understand it just look it up.. or don't idc.

Soundtrack: 101/10 PHENOMENAL, Michael Wyckoff is an amazing composer and it really shows. Whereas the soundtrack in Half Life Alyx, added to the experience but didn't get me to actively listen to it. BUT MICHAEL KNOCKS IT OUTTA THE PARK. Sometimes i would just stand in awe at the world in front of me, or just start dancing cause of the music. The music in Time Tower, The Museum AND LITERALLY EVERYWHERE ELSE IS FANTASTIC.

Locomotion: 9/10 Great, although can be disorienting and nauseating when you begin playing at the start. Climbing is great although can be a bit buggy at times. (Look at gameplay)

Replay Value: 8/10 You can always just mess around in the sandboxes, try to 100% it, mess around in the arena's

Overall: If you like VR get it, just do it. It's great, Perfect get it. Why haven't you already? do it
