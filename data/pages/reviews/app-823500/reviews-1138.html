\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks is a amazing game! The mechanics of the game surpass anything I've ever played and the vastness is amazing.

The Bad:
- I Never get motion sickness but once I started playing this I started getting dizzy and felt a little nauseous
- There are no comfort settings just zipping around in VR
- The controls treat this game like it's just on a monitor, I don't think it's ready for VR
- No way to save in this game so say goodbye to breaks

The Good:
- Great mechanics
- Always interesting
- Really fun when the nausea does not set in
- Well thought out game

Of course I'm relatively new to VR and if I play this more maybe I might get used being whipped around. This game's controls do not feel like their ready for VR. As of now I absolutely cannot play it. I don't recommend this game just for the lack of a ability to save and no comfort settings and horrible controls. I'll update this review as this game gets more updates, Thanks!\t\t\t\t\t

- Arms getting stuck when climbing
- Arms getting stuck on and in literally everything
- A R M S
- oh and hands too

- The chin hitbox rivaling that of GradeAunderA.

+ The first encounter with a headcrab thingy was absolutely terrifyingly hilarious.
