\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI expected a lot from this game. I was disappointed.

People call this game a masterpiece, and in a certain way it is. You can interact with everything, you can climb things, you can move things specifically. The game is very well made in that way. It has brought VR to the next level, it really has. But oh god was it boring. I near completed the game, I am on like the second to last level, so I can probably assume that the next level is going to be similar. The 'combat' is boring. Enemies can be slightly challenging but that doesn't really change my opinion. You just walk through this gaint map, completely boring puzzles and fight boring enemies. There are not that many weapons in the game, hower the mechanics for the weapons are great, but they're not customisable, they're always the same ones in every level. If you want to find out what VR can do, If you want to experience amazing physics, then sure this game is for you. If you want to play a game with a good story, and with fun and engaging combat, with challenging puzzles and levels, then the game is not for you. The puzzles weren't always easy but the times they weren't they were just quite boring. Maybe this is just not the game for me.

Games I did enjoy a lot:

- B & S
- Pavlov VR
- Echo VR
- Arizona Sunshine\t\t\t\t\t
