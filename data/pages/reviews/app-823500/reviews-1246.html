\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery good if not best VR game, took me 11 hours to get through once with lots of hidden secrets left.

Pro:
-Best physics interaction of any VR title
-Best controls of any VR title (I am moderately susceptible to getting nausea and I didn't get any)
-All the weapons are really fun, epic fighting feeling
-It's fun to explore and search for secrets
-Lots of Half-Life and Portal parallels/references

Con:
-No save games, you'll play for an hour and when you die you have to start the level from the beginning again... but it'll take you 5-10 min to return since you know exactly what to do. When you close the game to get a break you even lose all your inventory :(
-Some puzzles are needlessly annoying and disrupt the flow of the game
-Sometimes the physics interactions are glitchy; very annoying in melee fights
-a lot of experience needed until you master your movement skills; prior VR experience helps
