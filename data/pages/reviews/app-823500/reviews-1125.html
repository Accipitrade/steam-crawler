\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off this game has some serious flaws, the physics are the worst of them. it's advertised(by Node) like having physics on everything is great. it's really not! it's the biggest issue in the game, limbs constantly being stuck in objects sometimes resulting in you not being able to move until you free the limb.

Full body physics without full tracking is a mess, I never want arms in a game that aren't tracked as it's just another disconnect from reality, floating hands are always better in this scenario.

that being said I refunded this game and then changed my mind within 2 weeks to buy it again. It's got some great features and a decent length and while there is the "joke" of is this a tech demo, I do think of it at one but a very good one and worth the money, there is some serious jankyness to the whole thing. it's something I would expect to be Early access rather than a finished game but I expect the physics issues aren't something SLZ could solve as if they could, I don't think they would have released it like they did.\t\t\t\t\t
