Firstly let me begin by saying this game was my first take into VR bar Half-life Alyx so bare that in mind. However this game in my opinion is a must have like Alyx. Unlike like other VR games, it feels like you're actually getting a quality experience and tho the story is a little questionable? It's worth sticking through, as you collect so many different guns and weapons in the campaign for use in the sandbox mode. Aswell as extra modes like brawl.

I will say this however. I see people making massive posts on the forums about how to do all this crazy stuff just to unlock sandbox mode and let me tell you. It's not wroth it. without doing story you'll have no weapons or baddies to spawn in sandbox anyway so why bother? It upsets me that not many people bother with the story, while yes i agree it's not the most amazing story, but having all them cool little extra's, ina a game that offers so much replay-ability. It's just saddening to see people just skip the story, given how much the story has to offer, especially the end. Stress level zero put allot into this, just sucks some people can't be asked to see it.

So is this game worth your money and time, to that i say yes! Honestly trust me, this is one of two triple a experiences VR wise and is worth the money and time, Oh p.s, you can get zombie warehouse from the main menu, open the locker that says "don't scream", thank me later aye!\t\t\t\t\t
