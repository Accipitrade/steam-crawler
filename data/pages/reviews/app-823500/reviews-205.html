You have 4 enemy types, and 3 of them don't even have guns, because of this, the game is rather easy, a breeze even... The story? Don't even get me started, it's awful is what it is. It tries to be deep, but fails hard. Overall the game is worth a playthrough, but nothing more, it did not in any way come close to Half-Life: Alyx. I have to give a brief mention to mods which are the biggest reason to buy the game, they're great. I can't, however, rate a game based on the mods made by the community. A very solid 5/10... could've been better, could've been worse\t\t\t\t\t

This game. This game right here. Is such a mind-blowing VR experience. is this the best VR game.

(Minor spoilers.)


Security Director Arthur Ford inserts a Gammon USB drive into what is presumably the BONEWORKS system and barricades himself inside an old office. He opens a Sabrelake case and puts on a VR headset, dead-set on achieving immortality. (u/DellCruiser)

The whole entire "Semi-Linear First-Person Virtual Reality Action-Platformer-Puzzle game with a heavy focus on physics and full-body immersion" is apparently not even supposed to be a game, but a tech-demo. Yet everything in this game is so immersive which makes it feel like a game. Man oh man does this get you hyped up for what VR is going to be like in the future.

The music is whatever so nostalgic. It makes you love this game even more. And makes it more memorable. Whenever you get a VR-Ready PC and a VR Headset. Pick up this game. You will not regret it.
