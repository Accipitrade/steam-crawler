\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest Experience I've had in single player VR. There are always multiple ways of advancing and solving puzzles. If you like the feeling that you're using the environment or crouch jumping into somewhere you shouldn't be yet, this game is for you. There are always things to grab and climb, usually leading to rewards in the form of unlockable props or weapons. It takes the VR principles used in most high quality VR titles and expands upon them. Beware though, it may make other VR experiences seem lacking.

I use smooth locomotion in any VR title I play, and I play on a Valve Index. I can't speak to the quality of the game on other headsets/controllers or with teleport.\t\t\t\t\t
