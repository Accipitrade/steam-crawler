\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks is some of the most fun in VR you can play, and it's a really fun experience.

I don't have an amazing PC, and I've encountered moments of bad frame drops. Really good fun, bogged down by my awful setup.

Is it good? I would like to think so, though some bizarre decisions in the writing are probably the worst of it. I recommend it on the basis of it being a pretty decent sci-fi VR shooter where combat is fun and fluid. Telling your story through random infodumps with characters dropping exposition isn't something I'm a fan of. The ending is bizarre, and in a way, kinda stupid. It ends an hour after it should have, and it feels really lacking.

I enjoy Boneworks, I really do, and it's a good holdover until Half-Life Alyx releases, but I would have liked a bit more player engagement on the lines of making Ford an actual character. He says one line as you play as him, and it clashes with the tone they're going for.

Pick up this game knowing that you're going into it with little expectation of story. It's got neat level design, but really bizarre choices in writing, and the gameplay is some of the most satisfying I've ever played in a VR game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis unique game is very fun.
I have some problems with it:
- Lifting yourself up over a ledge can be yank and frustrating
- You can't always tell what is climbable and what isn't
- Sometimes I drag stuff out of my inventory even though I just wanted to pick up a nearby object or I'm climbing.
But you shouldn't look at the problems thinking it's all negative.
I'm very happy that climbing and the inventory system is a feature and it actually works really well otherwise!
The Inventory is actually quite intuitive, it's just rather limited.
The climbing makes you feel like a pro and sometimes like spiderman. It's a really fun mechanic and sometimes straight up a work out.
These were just 2 parts of a game that has much to offer and it's all quite amazing.
I wouldn't recommend this game to anyone who easily gets motionsick, because it doesn't have the teleportation movement and you will be tossed around (or dropped from high places)
Anyone who doesn't get motionsick easily and has some VR experience should be able to comfortably play this game and enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
