\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI COPIED THIS REVIEW BECAUSE IT IS SO SPOT ON.


This game is perhaps the most frustrating game I've ever played due to the astounding number of inconsistencies and bugs with the ui. It has the potential to be a good game if the devs spend an enormous amount of time fixing them, but I somehow doubt that that will happen.

Items get stuck in each other constantly. The ammo pouch regularly gets mis-triggered as the armpit holster, and force grabs near your feet regularly get mis-triggered as the ammo pouch.

Numerous gameplay elements also just don't work. Melee is unsatisfying. Puzzles, especially ones that involve climbing or tightrope walking, are inconsistent and by far the worst part of the game, made even worse by the horrible checkpoint system. In the middle of a level and planning to go to bed soon? Better finish it otherwise you'll start at the beginning instead of where you would if you were to die. The thought process behind this idea is unfathomable.

It's just not a fun game, though it definitely could have been.\t\t\t\t\t

Numerous gameplay elements also just don't work. Melee is unsatisfying. Puzzles, especially ones that involve climbing or tightrope walking, are inconsistent and by far the worst part of the game, made even worse by the horrible checkpoint system. In the middle of a level and planning to go to bed soon? Better finish it otherwise you'll start at the beginning instead of where you would if you were to die. The thought process behind this idea is unfathomable.

It's just not a fun game, though it definitely could have been.\t\t\t\t\t
