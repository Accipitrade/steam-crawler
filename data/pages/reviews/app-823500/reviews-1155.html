\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBONEWORKS
is exactly what I expected almost in every way before it came out, except for a few things.

BONEWORKS is probably the best VR game you can get right now, its not only a great new representation that will blow the minds of new VR players but even surprise some experienced VR player like myself in some of its physics and overall ideas. The game is extremely immersive, you aren't gonna be putting your hand in the wall in this game. It's immersion makes you feel like a physical character in a physical world that is in a VR game that's IN A VR GAME. (which is literally the story line, but more on that later...) This game does everything in its power to show truly everything a VR game is capable of in this current generation of VR. Using the greatest graphics, physics, gun play and sword play showing you just how realist it can be. Time to do some crazy parkour in this city like you could imagine doing it in real life! Time to swing your crowbar on a ledge you cant quite jump across and then pull yourself up with. The guns feel great. Makes MOST guns in other vr games look like a joke. Its consistency with weight or strength is amazing. In a game like onward where I would reload a gun would feel stale, unrealistic in a way... In BONEWORKS as I move my hand slowly backwards on an smg cocking back the pin to prepare to fire feels like I'm actually pulling it back. When carrying a heavy box my character struggles to move it so easily with one hand, Insanely so for lifting it. Its representation in the beginning truly feels like you are about to experience something crazy, in depth no one has done before. In many ways, they haven't. THIS GAME MAKES MOST VR GAMES LOOK LIKE A TECH DEMO.

GAMEPLAY AND THE ENVIRONMENT

Best shooting I ever felt, makes H3VR look only slightly in the lead when it comes to gun simulation, and that game IS a gun simulator! Cocking the weapons feel real the recoil feels real the physicality of breaking object with other objects just feels so real. The physics puzzles are amazing dealing with weight movement and can be tricky.

The enemies are just really cool. This game feels like a zombie game quite a bit. I feel zombie lovers will feel like they are fighting zombies very often, as the most common enemy are these corrupt beings inside the system that a mindless masses of corruption within the system that will scratch and chase after you to kill you. The headset crabs much like head crabs from the Half-Life series will jump on you and try to inception you to another world, which you will have to pull them off your face with force and quick force-full movement of your hand. They get stuck on you really well! They are really intimidating, and there's no better feeling than pulling one of them off and head bashing them to death. WITCH IS A THING YOU CAN DO! You can legitimately swing your head forward because like I said your character is a physical being and you can head butt your enemies to death. I wont go much in to enemies anymore because the plot does deepen a bit.

Climbing, weird main thing to point out but, I was amazed when a game like zero caliber had me climbing on anything I wanted. NOW THAT LOOKS LIKE A JOKE. This game has realistic climbing where you'll need to grab every crevice, edge, and corner to find your way up or over. You could always use pipes or cylindrical object though as well, maybe even a rope. I just have to say its the best climbing system ever and its actually pretty impressive.

The game has good replay-ability. I'm still not done finding all the secrets and unlockables through the campaign or even doing whatever I want in the sandbox mode, or fighting waves of enemies in the arena mode. Things are quite open on what you can do and its not even done yet. More update for end game content will come out over time in the future. The arena mode I feel should have a bit more work done for it but according to Stress Level Zero that's exactly what they plan to do. After hunting down all those unlockables as well its nice to be able to spawn them in in the sandbox to do really whatever you want with them.

STORY-LINE

I've said enough and I WILL NOT go into too much detail on this but the STORY, its very interesting I will just say I was said to feel that it was an interesting ending but, it felt so sudden and not very cool or good as it could have been. It could have had SOOO much more potential but it was alright looking back now it isn't amazing but its and interesting concept the ending is just kinda weak. The final mission is amazing though very well finishing it off. Also whats with the "what if this is only a tech demo?" comments? Is this only the beginning? Also pretty ironic being they have done things that make other VR games look like a tech demo. I will say the game did feel like a full game but in its atmospheric way a self aware test. Story line is quite meta and confusing so at the end of the day I feel there are things we don't know or haven't found yet. Possibly haven't even seen the end to yet. Also, whats with the melons?

CONCLUSION

9.5/10 Its a nine but I expected so much more from the story line, I also feel only slightly empty from the sandbox and the arena. HOWEVER, they will be adding more so I can't complain. Its one of THE best VR games you can get right now. I heavily recommend it as your first VR game ever, or a new amazing addition to VR game you already have. I think it will hold so many experiences you will remember for a while, and potentially become a new favorite of yours. Also, the devs are very compassionate and would love to even hear suggestions for future updates and weapon additions.

I have to say Stress Level Zero, insane job. You hyped a VR game and it didn't get over hyped like every game ever does. That is an accomplishment in its self.\t\t\t\t\t
