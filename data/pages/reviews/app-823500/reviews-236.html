\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEverything in this game makes me so nauseated. I have over 800 hours in VR but it is still too bad. This game has too much platforming and not just simple jumping but where you have to pull yourself up in ledges and manipulate your centre of gravity. When you push a wall, the game pushes your center of mass back, which is accurate but makes me feel really nauseated. All melee weapons tend to get stuck in geometry. Also, since everything is physics based, when I swing the hammer and when the player actually swings it is often a second or two late. It really throws me off. The ranged weapons have too much recoil.

Overall I think Half Life Alex did a better compromise for comfort over accuracy. For me this game is unplayable.\t\t\t\t\t
