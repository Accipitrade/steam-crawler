\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is more than a tech demo, and it’s absolutely wonderful!

If you own a VR headset, this is simply a must have. When we look back at the history of VR, we’ll look back at Boneworks in the same way we do to truly innovative games. Defining Boneworks as the true pioneer VR gaming and the one all other VR games should strive to be like. Stress Level Zero has truly raised the bar for all other VR titles.

You can expect solid gun play, rewarding melee combat, and very interesting physics based puzzles. This game really rewards you for thinking creatively, and there is almost no limit on how you can approach getting around certain obstacles in your path. You set your mind to it, and the game allows you to do it.

The only aspect of this game that is a bit difficult to grasp, at least for me, is climbing over fences, or pulling yourself over ledges. I likely just need some more practice to understand how to do it properly. However, I think the devs could have spent a little more time showing the player how to vault over objects.
