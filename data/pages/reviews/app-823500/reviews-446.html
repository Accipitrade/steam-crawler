\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pretty bad. The game design is like something from games from the 90's. Find a key to the door in a bunch of rooms. Can't find it? too bad, keep looking. No story, no management system.

The mechanics are bad, I don't know why holstering a weapon is so difficult, or why my hands keep on getting stuck and stop me from moving forward. Its probably the most unnatural feeling VR game I have, and I'm including free titles. Not to mention not as fun. FYI do yourself a favor and turn off adaptive resolution in settings. Adaptive resolution is good, but not how they have it setup.

I feel like many of the reviews were made by people who were still struck by the novelty of VR, and had little comparison to base their reviews on. This is an older VR title, and while I've seen it get alot of hype for pushing gameplay forward, I think it is clunky, especially compared to modern VR titles.

In short, this game feels older than it is, and I don't think anyone buying a headset in 2021 should consider spending their money on this game. It may have been a better game when there were very few VR games out there, but I've seen free games that are more fun than this.\t\t\t\t\t
