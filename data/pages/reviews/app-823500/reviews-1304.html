\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA real shame. This is the first game I have ever bought on release and it is disappointing to say the least.

1) Jumping. I have no idea why they thought it would be a good idea to have your player model crouch before jumping. I have played many vr games with button press jumping but none have given me motion sickness like this.

2) Jank. Grabbing the "workers" felt like they were made out of rubber and the whole way props interact with one and other is just irritating to say the least. The amount of times my pistol did not holster or I started dropping ammo on the floor when trying to grab props was too many to count. Hitting boxes with crowbars and having apart of the box get stuck inside of it and glitching out is just sickening. Also trying to open a draw and pulling the ♥♥♥♥♥ desk over is so annoying

3) Guns. NO SLIDE RELEASE and NO MAG RELEASE. If you are used to using guns in vr this is the part that will really annoy you. Having to physically pull a mag out of a gun that in real life would drop on press of a button. Why?
4) Bland. This game feels like it was made on gmod. the reuse of props and environmental details makes it feel empty and bland.

5) No saves=depressing.

Id like to say some good things, optimization is really good and has a good setting menu. Accuracy of guns felt really good. You can see the amount of effort they put into this game its just a shame it has these issues with motion sickness and with the guns. I will happily play it again if it goes down in price and play it in small bursts.\t\t\t\t\t
