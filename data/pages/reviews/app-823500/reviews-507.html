\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile this game is very fun, it does suffer from some serious problems for me.

The hand to hand combat is very poor. Every time you are required to or decide to not use ammo, you are always better off grabbing the nearest object (practical or not) to kill the enemy.
I’m not sure if the hand to hand combat is based on how fast your hand is moving or how well your form is and I just hit like a girl in real life, but it would always take me so long to kill the nullmen using only my fists.

The movement in this game was a new to me. The way you move in this game is very slippery. When you stop pushing forward on your thumb stick, your character will slow until a stop, instead of stopping immediately.

Climbing in this game is okay, but it’s kind of unforgiving. When attempting to climb through window or something else that doesn’t allow you to grab onto it, you’ll quickly find out that everything but the palm of your hands does not have any sort of collision. When your hands slide off (and they will) it will feel very unfair because of that.

Gunplay in this game was actually very good. Shooting most weapons in this game feels great and there isn’t any real issue with most of them. The smaller sub-machine guns however have a weird hitbox when trying to grab them with your offhand for stability. I often find myself grabbing the bolt of the weapons. There is also a very limited selection of guns in this game (roughly 8 non-gimic (dev/sandbox) weapons) There are plenty more melee weapons than guns. One problem I have relating to guns is that enemies with guns can easily shoot you clean though walls and physics objects. This makes every encounter with them (and there are a lot) a pain because you can not take cover anywhere. The only breaks you get are when they stop shooting at you.

Modding in this game seems to be small but healthy. For how popular this game is I expected more mods, but the limited amount does not upset me too much. The mods that are available do seem to be quality and not just a rush, dime-a-dozen, stolen and then re-uploaded product. Most of the mods are reskins, but there are plenty of custom weapon mods including lightsabers, pistols, and shotguns.

The story in this game is at least told very poorly. This is one of the few games where I felt I had to put in a significant amount of effort to just follow the story, not even to understand it. Half way though the game I just gave up trying to follow it and just started shooting things to get to the end of the level.

The first hour of the storyline (excluding the tutorial “scene”) was very dry and bland. I nearly stopped playing it after that first hour because of how boring it was. After a few scenes the game picked up and I was starting to have fun.


Here are my final thoughts:
Is it fun?
\tYes it is.

Is it worth $30.00?
\tAbsolutely not! Wait for a sale. I would say this game is worth closer to $20.00. It normally goes on sale for $23.00, and I guess that will be the best we will get for a while.

Is this a one-and-done game or will I come back to it after I beat it?
\tOnce you beat the game, you’ll probably want to come back to it, but only in 10-20 minute intervals.

Sandbox?
\tThere is a sandbox to this game, but you have to manually collect everything in the story line to have anything show up in the sandbox menu. Do you want an M16? You’ll have to toss it in the reclamer. The night vision goggles? Reclaimer. A coffee mug? Reclaimer. It will take days to get everything you want, and that’s just collecting things off of the ground.\t\t\t\t\t
