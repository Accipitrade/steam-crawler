\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWORTH A SORE FOREHEAD AND TIRED EYES

PROS;
Atmosphere
Weapon/device handling
Intuitive level design
Good Replayability

Cons
Live action cutscenes hurt
Lack of continued support
Somewhat difficult for new VR players

This is easily my favorite purchase for a full featured VR game. The atmosphere is GREAT. Portal-esque with a degree of self awareness off the bat. The story was a bit odd and tricky to follow but the magnitude and quality of the stages did alot of heavy lifting and helped me forget about the out of touch TV sequences that reminded me of recent need for speed live action cutscenes. The weapon and gadget handling is fantastic. The Level design holds true to the atmosphere and is awe inspiring at many points in the game. The alternate replay focused arena modes are alot of fun and ive dumped hours into each one. Sandbox modes are also very cool and each have neat easter eggs to discover. I was hoping to see mulitplayer support at some point or even just a feed of continued support with more gadgets, stages, and features but i understand that a small studio created this and im quite happy to enjoy this for what it is. Its my top recommendation for a VR player\t\t\t\t\t
