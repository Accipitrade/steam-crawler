\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBONEWORKS is arguably the best VR game that I have ever played.

As an ambitious indie title, it's can be rough around the edges, but the level of immersion and freedom that the game's complex physics system provides remains completely unmatched by every other VR title on the market. Every puzzle can be solved in an infinite number of ways, every combat interaction can be approached in however the player sees fit, every level has a multitude of secrets an alternate paths to explore. How you move through each level is completely up to your imagination, and BONEWORKS is more than willing to reward the player for experimenting with it's systems. There are a wide array of collectibles scattered through each level, and the more you're able to find, the more options you have for traversing stages and defeating enemies. As such, BONEWORKS has an insane amount of replayability, bolstered even further by the game's multiple sandbox and arena maps, which give you even more to do with the game's systems.

This is already a masterpiece, and I am so so excited to see how Stress Level Zero builds on the foundation they have created here with their next project.
