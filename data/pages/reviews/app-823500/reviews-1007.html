Save points are a little scarce, and there aren't enough body slots to carry a weapon and the sandbox items, so you'll have to go through each level a couple times with a fine-tooth comb.
Just an FYI, when I say the physics are frustrating, I really mean it. I've got my hands caught on the other side of a wall several times. This straight up immobilizes you until you can get it out. Items are finicky, your hands don't always do what you want or need them to do, your legs are a difficult detriment, constantly holding you back when you're trying to climb things, and it's difficult to reach ammo and use body slots when sitting (I usually laze back in a chair, you have to sit straight up for better reach).
Body slots are also a little finicky. I turn a little to make sure I can put the gun in an arm holder, but that instead turns my body and moves the holster farther away. The ones on the back aren't as bad, but it usually still takes a couple tries to put a weapon away.\t\t\t\t\t
