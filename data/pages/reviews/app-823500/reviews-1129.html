\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBONEWORKS is a fantastic step in the right direction when it comes to VR. Pretty much everything aside from the level geometry is a physics object, including your playermodel. The enemies are physics based which makes them look and feel very real. The level of immersion in this game is incredible. It feels great on the Oculus Rift, which is something I wasn't expecting as it was built for the Index. It will definitely spark your deja vu from games like Portal and Half Life. It has a very convoluted story, and I would recommend paying attention to the bits of lore it throws at you, because I was very lost at the end of my first playthrough. There are some slight issues, like the full vr body can feel very clunky at times, especially around all of the physics based obstacles and while climbing. Combat was somewhat difficult for me, especially when trying to aim down sights, as the gun seemed to be fighting with my ragdoll simulated shoulder and I couldn't line up a shot without holding it way out so it wouldn't collide with my fake body. Other than that, the gameplay is very polished and all of the secrets hidden throughout the layers of this game can keep you going back for more. I really do wish the story was longer, from a writing perspective, the ending seemed rushed and wasn't set up very well, but that's only for those that really care about plot. Definitely check this out if you have a decent vr setup.\t\t\t\t\t
