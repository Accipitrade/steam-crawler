\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks is a brilliant game. There, I said it.

You want more?

Well, boneworks is a mix between a shooter, platformer, and a puzzle game, however it uses experimental physics to achieve this, using a full body presence to its advantage. Because of this however, it means that people who are prone to getting VR sickness best not play this game until they develop a stronger stomach. The shooting feels absolutely amazing, using physics expertly to give the guns a proper sense of recoil, and the melee combat feels much the same, with the ability to stick sharp objects, such as knives, into the enemies, and putting real weight into the heavier weapons, such as the sledgehammer.
There is certainly a story here, however it is a little complicated to explain in one review, so i recommend you watch MatPat's videos on boneworks.
But, even if you don't want to watch the video. You'll still have an excellent time, pre and post endgame.\t\t\t\t\t
