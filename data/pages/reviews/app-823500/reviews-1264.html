\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed with HTC Vive and HP Reverb WMR.
This game is basically a VR puzzle platformer with some targets to shoot at and introduces a lot of innovations on VR game play along the way, kudos to developers. It is also trying to provide similar experiences over different VR platforms but unfortunately there are some serious short comings with game play and saving player's progression.
Here is my issues with the game:
- Jump mechanics with HTC vive wand is horrible. You will find yourself frustrated with not being able to jump between platforms and play same stage over and over again. It is bit easier with WMR controls.
- There is no save option. I have many times played a level, which is big enough to spend an hour and solve puzzles comes with it, but not came across any check point / save spot / next level etc and had to abandon game due to various reasons and came back for the same frustration and spend another hour but still no check point. The reason might be there aren't many levels to keep player entertained long enough so here comes no save and big space between check points. Very frustrating.
If you are ok with no save and play same level for hours and get frustrated with jump mechanics, yes this game is for you.\t\t\t\t\t
