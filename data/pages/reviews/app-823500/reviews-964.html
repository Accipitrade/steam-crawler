A significant amount of the physics puzzles are creative but feel bad and clunky. The melee combat is also nothing to write home about, you're probably just going to break a controller if you're not careful.

This game has a lot of cool stuff in it, but at the end of the day it's a little sad because clearly Valve took the concepts they liked and were able to refine what felt fun while leaving out what felt clunky.

I can't leave a negative review for this game since it's revolutionary, but at the same time if I could leave a neutral review that's what I'd do. It has a lot of funk you need to push yourself to get past.\t\t\t\t\t
