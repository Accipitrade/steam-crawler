\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe greatest VR experience I have had so far., runs flawlessly on my system.
i5 2500 @ 3.3ghz
Nvidia 1060GTX 6GB
16GB DDR3 RAM
Windows 8.1
Oculus Rift with Touch Controllers

And that's barely minimum but it looks and runs great.
The physics system makes it all feel so real, I feel a lot less sick playing this game than other VR games due to the physics-based player model, I can manage a good couple of hours play session with this game before needing a break, whereas other games I can barely push half an hour.

The only real downside is some of the level design isn't the most friendly, especially the more vertical oriented levels and the save system is pretty poor, levels are very long but the game only saves at the very beginning of a level, so if you quit, you start that level from the very beginning.

Additionally, the exit to the first level (The Museum) isn't really obvious at all and there was an anti-gravity room in there that was (I assume) bugged for me as I couldn't get away from it without restarting the entire level.


Other than that, best VR experience yet, buy it immediately.\t\t\t\t\t
