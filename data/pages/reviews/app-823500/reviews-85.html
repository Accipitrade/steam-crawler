\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks promised to be an evolving sandbox of a game. They never even made workshop support.

Just like hover junkers, this game has ended up forgotten by the devs, with more empty boxes than checked ones.

Don't support poor development practices. SLZ treats steam like its a game jam, except they can charge full price for a "most of an idea" game, then turn around and use the assets on something with 10% more polish, i.e. bonelab.

Graphics: Passable. This game is middle of the line here. It doesn't push itself therefore it doesn't push your fps.

Story: Awful. Think MGS5's "lets delegate all story telling to a vocal recording". Without them, there isn't one.

Gameplay: Disappointing. Gunplay is good, but theres only 4 guns. Melee has many weapons, but they all handle poorly.

Stability: Subpar. I had more than a couple of crashes, considering the milquetoast events unfolding when they happen, there's no excuse.

Overall, I'm really pissed that this game's potential, and previous games potential, was delegated to creating new, half-baked, recycled games. SLZ used to be the passionate new kids on the block in game development, now they are just hacks, and their example is a stain on this fledgling genre.
