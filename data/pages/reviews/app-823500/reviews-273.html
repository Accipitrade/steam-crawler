\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBasically a musthave for any VR player. The way you can interact with almost everthing in the world is amazing. The Story/Lore of the game is pretty confusing and not really the selling point imo, because the characters (which only appear on some computer screens or in cutscenes) are very forgettable and is a pretty bland way to tell a story. Despite the boring storytelling I had a fun time with the story mode and the other post-game content like the arena mode. What I can also recommend is playing this game with some mods (If you plan on doing this I recommend using a mod manager), because it brings brand new guns, features and maps to the game.\t\t\t\t\t

It will ruin your VR experience in other games, cause OF ITS UNPARALELLED FREEDOM
That sentence basically sums it up- has great interaction with world and basically anything you see will be moveable and/or draggable.
On the other hand, since its almost 3 year old and VR has made some big leaps since then, its graphics are sometimes simplified and world can feel bland, but lots of enemies and weapons to use will greatly make up for its cons. Also, it has much bigger maps (and I mean BIGGER) than for example very linear and "corridorrish" HL:A - there will be big halls and decently sized areas which look great in VR.
Plot could be described best as "Its complicated" - I didn't care too much about it, but its there somewhere.
Enemies give you chills and make you run around, trying to take them down, which is good in VR (Quest2 FTW, with its freedom of movement). But killing them has "IT"- the thing that makes you happy with every body hitting the floor- be it an axe, 1911 or M4- all of the weapons give you that nice "ahaha! pwnd!" feel. They also don't feel like made from styrofoam and shooting is nice, as well as hitting stuff with melee. Combat is generally very well done.
Climbing and grabbing (yourself to..)stuff could be made better, cause it can be very troublesome sometimes, but still - its one of the first games with continuous movement, which I love, rather than this dumb teleports in other games.
Overall its a strong 9/10, mostly for the overall freedom of movement and lots of ways to eliminate enemies
-1 for the sometimes obvious emptiness and some quirks (i.e. "plot, anyone?" and that sometimes crappy grabbing of stuff), but these are the things in this game you won't notice too much to ruin your fun.\t\t\t\t\t
