\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've completed the campaign, and I do quite enjoy the game. Some of the physics mechanics are a little awkward and don't feel great, like picking up and manipulating certain larger items. The gun play is fantastic. My main complaint with the game is how short it is. They begin to establish characters and story and hint at really cool backgrounds, then the game ends. I was hoping the game wouldn't leave so many questions unanswered. I had them answered by an awesome series talking about the lore on youtube, but I wish I wouldn't have to rely on youtube to be able to understand the story. The game also leans pretty heavily on the portal 2 influences to the point where it was a bit distracting at times. For instance, some of the humor and posters on the walls looked exactly like the style of the posters on the walls of old Aperture in Portal 2. This game did it well, I just wish there was a little more originality with certain story-telling elements. Another huge example are the rat-man esque drawings and writings giving the player hints exactly as it's done in Portal. All that being said, the story is very different from that or Portal, and once I learned more about the story and context, I was able to look past these things more. I would recommend the game, it is super cool and I hope for more in this series. A Boneworks 2 would be awesome.\t\t\t\t\t
