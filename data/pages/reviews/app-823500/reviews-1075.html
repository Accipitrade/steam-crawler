They should have let somebody else play the game and get some feedback before publishing it.

- Motion sickness because i feel dislocated to my representation of the body.
- Getting frustrated because of bad climbing mechanics
- Getting frustrated because of the boring riddles which are heavily based on the jumping, climbing and physics simulation. Jumping and climbling are a pain in this game though. Physics are great, but only if your own body isn't involved.

- The ugly as hell graphics (They should've gone either with a more textureless environment / Would've increased performance too or they should have hired someone who knows a thing or two about design and art direction)
- Can't say much about the story since i can't play through the game. Seems like the story is told through real live cheap videoclips though. And those story games are always great, right?


I can now understand why people say its a tech demo.

I was so hyped after seeing the first demo from Brandon on corridor and node.\t\t\t\t\t
