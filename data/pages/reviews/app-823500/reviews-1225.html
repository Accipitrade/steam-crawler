\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst, for anyone playing on HTC vive wands, its possible and not as bad as people say. Playable but not the best, kinda awkward at times. I would give it around 7/10.

Second, game is really solid and super fun to play. Could have a couple of improvements to areas, but the core game is a 10/10.
The menus are a meh, around 7/10, I was at the end of a level and was ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ around in the menus and sent myself back to a different level and lost all progress.
The lack of a save button really sucks, it would solve a bunch of the issues. I have to finish a level before I stop playing for the day unless I feel like leaving my vive running while I'm AFK.

Gun play is amazing, puzzles are fun and engaging. Sometimes I need to really scratch my head with some of them but I've seemed to managed thus far.
Game does well to teach you the basics before putting you into the actual game.

This game is the solid title to put VR into a new era, titles in the future should aspire to live up to this type of quality. Tons of replayability with secret puzzles that can only be done later once you've found the right secret tools/weapons.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pretty legit. Even on WMR (which is really showing its age)

Run speed feels good, motion is snappy, I play with constant motion on the right stick, instead of snap turn, but that's just my preference. Feels a bit nicer to turn exactly to reset my direction.

Game is somewhat spoopy. Guns feel outstanding. Some collectibles hidden throughout. Buy.

Anyone reviewing this game and mentioning anything about motion sickness should be ignored. From the steam page - "ATTENTION: This game demonstrates advanced VR mechanics and concepts, players are recommended to have previous VR experience and understanding of common VR gameplay principles before proceeding." and "BONEWORKS is an Experimental Physics VR Adventure."

If you are not tolerant of extreme+advanced VR motion for long periods, this game isn't for you. There are warnings all over this. This game is great for most people because they DIDN'T tone it down for the people that it isn't aimed at.

The reason we get 10 call of duty clones a year is because of this culture of complaining about everything that tries to be original not just being a product that already exists. This game is an experiment. Get over yourself.

And it's a damn good experiment.

EDIT - Having upgraded to a wireless Vive, this game is even more worth it. I can't recommend it enough.\t\t\t\t\t
