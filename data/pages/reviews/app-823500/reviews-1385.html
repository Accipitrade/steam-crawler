\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpdate:

First of all this game is fun, innovative and breaks some new ground with it's level of object interactivity.

It is more action/adventure with puzzle elements. I enjoy the guns and combat but the enemies don't give much a challenge so don't expect a hardcore shooter. I believe I'm midway through story mode so things could change later?

I definitely recommend the game at it's price, although I can't help but feel that most of the positive reviews are blown out of proportion. It is NOT vr's best game, there are issues that will hopefully be addressed over time. ( I feel the devs were pressed to release and could have used more time to tweak)

Some of the negatives:

We need more graphics settings. The settings are very basic and the anti-aliasing set high to get rid of flicker can be a touch blurry.


There are bugs - objects stick and you can't release without a struggle, I got stuck in spots that were very difficult to get out of, Sometimes you can reach through solid objects (I could put my arm through a solid door and break the boards off the other side).

I have been experiencing nausea more so than any other game recently. I believe it is because of a slight lag in in movement and also being a little floaty - kinda like the rag doll physics are slightly applied to everything if that makes sense?

In real life when you pick up something heavy you stiffen up to control the object and your movement - in the game you and the object are in rag doll mode and just flop from one place to the next, which makes some situations very frustrating and way more time consuming than they need to be, it can break the flow of the game.

It seems save points are quite a ways apart so it would be nice to know when you reach one, if you want to take a break and return later and not lose your progress.

I do wish it lasted longer than 6 hours - that's ok considering the price but double that would have been so much better. Hopefully there will be dlc? free or paid would be great!

The Index double tap forward to run sucks - I guess this is to get around the hardware issues with the joystick press down? I would have prefered it mapped to a button - don't need 2 inventory buttons?

As I said above I do recommend the game but it's not perfect.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Not having finished the tutorial yet, I still wanted to post my thoughts. I'm using the Index. Will update later when deeper in :)

No motion sickness as some have reported.

Graphics are clear, colorful and detailed where they need to be.

Interactivity is very immersive, maybe the best yet in any game.

Physics are mostly amazing. Some things like griping objects with two hands (sledgehammer positioning) or climbing (hands are sticky and don't release quick enough) can be a little clunky. Overall impressed though.

Haven't figured out the story yet but it has my interest up, curious to see where it leads.

I definitely recommend at this point.\t\t\t\t\t
