The many levels are super fun to explore, and the puzzles are the perfect amount of tricky. You can interact with almost anything in this game, so the number of solutions to pass a certain area is endless. Objects are fun to use and the game is fun to play. The only issue I had with the game was the lack of mid-level saves, but in the end I found that added to the experience. But mid-level saves were just added so I can say I'm very happy with this game, and the 11 hours of gameplay it gave me; and that's just for the main story. I love this game so much, and whilst it doesn't have an epic story, it is truly a massive step in the right direction for singleplayer VR games. Boneworks is groundbreaking, and I hope it helps to pave the way for what VR games will be in the future.
This game is not an introductory point for VR players. It is a proper game for VR Gamers, and they feel very rare currently. This game is worth the price and I recommend it in a heartbeat.
If you like VR and you like Games, then you will LOVE Boneworks.\t\t\t\t\t
