-Music 8/10 (Some really awesome tracks. Both fast paced and mellow)

-Graphics 7.5/10 (Nice minimalist style, works well in the games favour, More realistic parts look amazing)

Also you will need quite a powerful PC to run this game smoothly.
Overall a must have experience if you have a capable PC and headset.
