\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe levels and puzzles in this game are dull, uninspired and lifeless with no sense of cohesion. The first few maps were some of the most boring, grey and empty maps I have ever experienced in an FPS game. I struggled to even continue playing. Many will tell you the "real fun" is in the sandbox mode, which has to be unlocked by beating all those monotonous levels and the entire game. Unfortunately to unlock items for use in the sandbox you are forced to play these levels over and over again to find the items you want. Yeah, no thanks.

The physics system gets in the way of itself. Getting to the top of your climb and having your own immobile legs preventing you from mounting the platform directly in front of your eyes is something I experienced dozens of times. There is a reason most VR games do not use full IK rigs. They just don't add anything of value beyond an initial fleeting novelty. This title was not worth the price of admission.\t\t\t\t\t

I would really not recommend this game just for one reason: you can't play it. Only way to go with Boneworks is struggle. While this games is physics-based, you don't really feel your character.
