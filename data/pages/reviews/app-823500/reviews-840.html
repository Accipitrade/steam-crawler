\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe physics can be wacky sometimes but wow, I've been blown away by probably every aspect of the game. You really need VR legs for this game as if you don't, it may ruin your experience as you might get motion sick from how your body physics interact in the game.

The game's story is kind of confusing and the game's mechanics (such as how the ammo is used as both currency and a resource, how to actually use the reclamation bin and unlock other game modes) can be really confusing in the beginning.

Other than having wacky physics from time to time and needing VR legs to avoid motion sickness, this is easily one of the best single player VR game ever, gameplay wise in my opinion. With high quality physics engine, texture, graphics, sound design, sound localization and spatial audio (you can clearly pinpoint where and how far the sound is coming from around you), the game delivers a crazy immersive experience.

As a side note, I use the Valve Index headset and controllers so the audio experience may vary depending on what headset you use to play this game. As for the controllers, I believe that this game is much more immersive with the Index controllers as this game requires you to grip and let go of things (which is probably the only VR controllers you can actually "let go" to drop things more naturally) constantly. This is especially true when you have to physically interact with objects for puzzles or obstacles, and to just simply hold, switch, and reload a the many types of gun and tools. Basically, it is more comfortable and natural with the Index headset's speakers and controller for a game designed with physics and sound quality like this in my opinion. Highly recommend at least using the Index controllers for this game.

Props to the amazing music this game has too. Really raises the atmosphere and feeling of the game by a ton!

Highly recommend this game to anyone looking for a high quality polished VR game as the experience is insanely immersive. If you are worried about replayablity, do not worry as it has plenty of secrets, exploration, game modes, such as sandbox and zombie mode to keep you playing for hours after beating a pretty long campaign. Definitely worth the purchase!\t\t\t\t\t
