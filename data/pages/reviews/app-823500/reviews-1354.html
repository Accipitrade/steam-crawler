\r\n\t\t\t\t\t+ Fantastic physics-based gameplay

+ MOVING music <3

+ SLZ and Corridor Digital have been some of my favorite creators for years. To see Brandon and co. make something like this brings a tear to my eye

+ Easter eggs and attention to detail!

- Movement is a little wonky. The acceleration/deceleration feels off, so I can't move exactly where I want to. A tradeoff with having a physically simulated body. I also would like an option where neither the head nor the controller direction feeds into input. Just let forward be forward. I hate how turning my head or my hands will ruin my direction and usually makes me fall off whatever platform I'm on.

- The slow motion button is next to the movement stick, so I can't move while in slow motion. Maybe have a click to activate, then either a timer or click to deactivate?

- Climbing needs a lot of work. If you've played games like WAR DUST or STAND OUT, you'll know what I mean. All too often I'll try to climb up something or over a ledge, and the game doesn't think I should be allowed to climb there. Makes the "freedom" this mechanic should provide feel a lot less free.

- Similarly, grabbing things needs tweaking as well. It takes me a good minute to grab a melee weapon where I want it, and grabbing items from shelves or tables usually results in me grabbing and knocking over said surface.

- YOU LOCKED THE SANDBOX MODE?!?!?!!!?!?!??!? That was the mode I was looking forward to the most! The fact that I have to play through the campaign first makes me so frustrated.





TL;DR An incredibly innovative physics VR game, made by gamers for gamers. However, the mechanics are finicky and need some tweaking I think. That, along with locking out the sandbox and arena modes puts this game just a hair shy of perfection.\t\t\t\t\t
