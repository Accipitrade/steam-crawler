It's no Half-Life: Alyx but it's still a fun game to play either for the sandbox mode, it's wave mode, or even for it's weird yet intriguing story mode. Everything in the game feels grounded in terms of scale and size. If you have a VR headset and wanna goof around and dont have the money for HL:A then get this game.\t\t\t\t\t
