\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic VR
Best guns in VR
Best melee combat in VR

Great graphics

Good story

I'm hardcore VR and can take just about anything.
It makes gives me some nausia after about 3 hours of play in a day. Most get sick earlier.
I can't play at all sitting down. That was much worse for me. But I love the mechanics.

The grab can get a bit sticky and finicky.

I don't like the initial grab point when you grab many weapons. It's not in a combat ready postion so you need to use both hands to get the right grip. The axe is that way.

Minor gripes for a 10/10 game for me.

Loving it!!!

By the way I'm playing this using the oculus quest instead of my HTC Vive pro using Quest link and a 26 foot cable by extension. It works flawlessly!!!

I may sell most of my old hardware and get an index setup but I tell you the quest does everything you'd need and I don't see any visual artifacts. It's just awesome. The room space setup is better than my Vive.

The hand tracking is great but not as good as the Vive or index.
Still I never had a problem playing this game.\t\t\t\t\t
