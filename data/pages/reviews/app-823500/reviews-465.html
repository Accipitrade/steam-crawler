\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA really great game that pushed forward the investment into immersive stories and environments for VR games.

Though the story is a bit...Short, aside from plots and lore. Not to say that ten hours is bad but the ending left me kind of underwhelmed all things considered. The only other major gripe I have with the game is just the body you play in, it's a shame you can't get full body tracking for them, it makes trying to bend over areas or interact closely to the environment feel burdensom and clunky, try bending over a desk or anything and your character's body would forcibly push against it keeping you stuck in place. It's an interesting simulation and I know this was made before full body tracking was even commercially viable, but i don't know it hurt a bit for me to feel like I was confined to the floor instead of being fully connected to the world. The weight being independant from controller movement too made me move outside of my playspace a bit too much compared to other games too as I felt I had to move vastly further than my character would bother to, if there's anything I'd want outright changed for Boneworks it's to completely rework how the bones work.

Sorry for the nitpicks and spoiled rants, here is the good stuff!

The sandbox is amazing! The fact you can interact directly with enemies is fun, with a lot of interesting emphasis on playing around with the environment. Speaking of, the environments too are gorgeous, pulling a sort of matrix aesthetic with visual story telling all over the place. Areas look 'lived in' and some places are just a delight to be in. My favorite moments were in the sewers, walking around with a flashlight attached to my gun finding interesting places under the otherwise pristine looking cities. All in all, for the ten hours I've spent on this game they were well immersed aside from some conflicts with the physics engine.

Ultimately, it was worth the money I spent, and it was a great starting point for vr games to be taken more seriously. Though I wish some things were tweaked a bit and that the story missions were more drawn out, I had plenty of fun.\t\t\t\t\t
