\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks is a very technically accomplished game and pushed the standards for VR gameplay but I feel it is a poorly designed "game". The narrative is weak and uninteresting and it feels like it is lacking a compelling gameplay experience which actually puts the excellent engine and mechanics to good use.

The enemies aren't particularly compelling to fight and the environments are mostly quite unexciting. For me these negative elements outweigh the excellent technical and mechanical accomplishments of the engine.

If they added a compelling game to go along with the engine it would be a solid recommendation but after playing the campaign I couldn't see any reason to keep playing it as the "sandbox" elements just didn't hold any appeal for me.\t\t\t\t\t
