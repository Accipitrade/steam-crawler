\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have to make a review. I barely make reviews but I HAVE TO MAKE A REVIEW FOR THIS.
Ok, I beat it twice. First time when I first got my VR, it was cool, and fun and new experience for me. I didn't know thoroughly of how you are supposed to play the game besides solving puzzles and shooting stuff. That's the only fun part.
After beating the game I didn't play for a few months cause I didn't think about it. I started to play again and redo the entire story because it didn't have my saves.
Now the ACTUAL way to play this game is by literally collecting everything in each level by all AMMO and items you can put into the reclamation bin. This makes it so you are eligible to use those said items and props in the sandbox (Spawn). The reason you need to collect all the AMMO you can possibly get is because it is Collectibles and Currency. You get to the last level where the developers show you some of the "Fun Developer tools/Weapons" they made and those cost behind a paywall of 12k+ bullets up to 25k. It implies they want you to keep playing their levels till you find all the ammo. It becomes really tedious and annoying.

The enemies are so unbelievably bul***** that I feel like it is the reason why I don't play this game much. It's not that they are too difficult, they have certain things about them, that just makes me turned off from the game. I'll start with the NULL enemies, first type you encounter, they are slow, only melee attackers ranging from different colored neon colors. They need to flinch from gun shots more from where ever they are hit, it becomes a chore when you shoot a guy in the chest and he just walks up and smacks you when he should be recovering for a second from the bullet shot I made.
The gunners (I don't know their actual names), of course have aimbot for the player, its understood to be getting shot at and hit sometimes, but when I get hit with EVERY shot, even when I just peak around a corner from a higher, or lower point of view, I get shot with 100% precision and occasionally die from being around a corner.
The zombies that shoot Magic. These Mother******* are top of the line broken. As previous, they got aimbot, it isn't hard to move around their spells, but when you are being tossed 8 of them at a time, and a sword and Shield which the Shield does NOT block their magic attack and instead kills me, I tend to insult the developers for this idea.

Oh and the Climbing in this game is nasty as all hell. Compared to Blade and Sorcery climbing, this games climbing is the Arbys of climbing in VR. You literally jitter around with your entire VR PHYSICAL body which hits stuff while climbing around. The climbing only works on certain pipes, woods, and walls and its pretty difficult cause it doesn't feel good at all.
I don't normally make reviews, and my format of my review is more or less just a summary of what I experienced. The game is trying to keep you playing the game but with the worst layout reason to play. (Play, Collect, Replay) is basically the theme of Boneworks. The shooting of NPCs and other things is completely irrelevant to the actual way the game works.\t\t\t\t\t
