\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a massive step in the right direction for VR. With an amazing physics engine, and a story that I quite don't fully understand but is cool nonetheless. The campaign took me around 9 hours to complete but I skipped exploration and more focused on finishing the campaign. I plan on running through the campaign a couple more times to unlock items for the sandbox mode. This is an amazing game that I believe redefines the capabilities of VR and set an industry standard. Many VR games feel like party tricks, way overpriced, and don't offer much. This game offers much, and will keep you highly entertained. My main problem is the saving, or lack thereof. You cannot save midway through a mission. If you want to leave and come back, you either need to finish the mission or restart it. There are checkpoints for if you die so it is not that annoying. But these missions can sometimes be long and if I have something I need to do and leave the game, it can be quite frustrating. Regardless amazing game, Good job to SLZ. Im excited to see what they do next!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis will be short but this feels like it needs to be polished. Gun mechanics are awful unless you use the handguns which are honestly the only viable weapons to use, the puzzles are unrefined as it takes 20 minutes just to get a box across a walkway that isn't even stationary so you have to fall in-between the cracks 50 times, and the fact that you can't save during the levels just adds to the frustration. There are plenty of other things about this game that make this game feel like it was rushed, but overall in its current state i'd give it a 4.5/10 as there are things about this game that give me hope for the games potential but were just executed poorly. Can't help but feel disappointed after all this time of waiting but maybe these teasers of "What if this is just a tech demo?" throughout the game isn't actually a joke.\t\t\t\t\t
