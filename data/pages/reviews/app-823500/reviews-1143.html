Good:
+ Sandboxy but with some linear progression so it's not just another arena thing-a-ma-doer
+ Physics work well for the most part
+ Guns and weapons make for some creative combat
+ Music score is very nice
+ You have a physical body!

Bad:
- Your body blocks and bumps into a lot of stuff too
- Your legs don't do anything when you're climbing, making it clumsy to climb
- Gunplay isn't that great
- Some environments are a little bland

Overall a very solid experience, but with some frustration here and there. Highly recommended just for the experience, especially in the relatively early age of VR gaming.
