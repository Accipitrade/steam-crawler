\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright, So There's A Thing About Boneworks That Makes Me Love It, Ill Go By The List One By One
MUSIC
The Music, There Is Some Feel To It, A Relaxing One (For Example, Ambient Travel) Or Tense Ones (Example,MythOS). Music Makes Up A Big Part Of Boneworks, Music Can Be Used To Spice Up Any Sort Of Situation, Like A Big Fight Which Was Being Hyped Up For The Whole Game(Opera De Ossium) Or Just General Exploration Of The First Early Levels(The Museum, Analog Sunrise, Ambient Travel). Boneworks Has Beautiful Music And Uses Them Very Well (Fun Fact:Im Listening To Boneworks Music During Writing)
PHYSICS
Boneworks Uses The Marrow Engine, Same One As In The Newer Release Bonelab. Being One Of My First VR Games, The Physics Were Especially Impressive, Most If Not All Parts Of This World Are Collidable. You Could Even Play Getting Over It If You Have The Time. My Point Is That Boneworks's Physics Engine Are Very Well Done And Also Done Well For Bonelab
LORE
The Boneworks Lore Is Part Of The SLZ Universe, (From What I Know) Duck Season,Bonelab And Boneworks Are All A part Of This Universe (Not Sure About Hover Junkers). (Kinda Spoilers) What Happens In Boneworks Matches Up With What Happens Afterwards In Bonelab. MONOGON And GAMMON Are Both Competitors Who Have Access To The Void. (Spoilers Kinda End Here) Its All Very Cool
GAMEPLAY
Boneworks Has Probably The Best VR Gameplay Ive Ever Seen, Also Earning Its Spot As My Second Fav Game. All The Guns Work Very Well, The Swords And Knives Are Amazing.The Enemies Are Very Cool And Fun To Fight Against, The Pain And Torture To Ford Is Yes
REPLAYABILITY
Boneworks Has Alot Of Replayability, I Finished The Boneworks Story After 24 Hours Of Playing And All The Extra Hours Are From Sandbox Or Replaying The Levels To Find Gachapons(Capsules). Gachapon Are The Goofy Capsules You Keep In Your Inventory Until The End Of A Level To Put Into A Reclamation Bin Then Use In Sandbox(Or Campaign If You Have The Dev Tool). The Capsules Keep On Making You Replay The Level To Find All Of Them. Very Fun Replaying.
OVERALL RATING
The Music, The Gameplay, The Physics, The Music. They All Make Boneworks The Perfect VR Game, Landing This Game Definitely A 10/10 Rating. Would Play Again, Will Play Again\t\t\t\t\t
