\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: One of the best games I have played in years.

Played this using the Valve Index.

From the developers of the amazing game Duck Season they bring you a frolic through an immersive world with the most fun combat I have ever seen. I have finished the story mode, and I have been left wanting more. MORE! I cant say this one game made VR worth it, but by the end I was ecstatic to have had the chance to play it.

Pros:
The combat is fun and innovative (As innovated as realistic physics can be).
The enemy variety is fun and varied.
The guns provide for your choice of game play.
Being surrounded by a swarm of enemies gets my hear racing in the best way.
You can duck under the "head crabs" jumping at you.

Cons:
The story is fun and gives some glimpse into the world, but many things are left unexplained.
I wish we had more background as to why some enemies exist.
I wish the enemies were better at pathinding. If they patch this I would play it again.
Need a larger variety of enemies. Maybe more with guns. Maybe one I could climb on (Why isn‚Äôt that a thing?).
