\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wanted a game with plenty of weapons to pummel enemies with. Instead I got puzzles and walking with a few enemies thrown in there. I decided to give it one more shot and play sandbox instead, but found out you have to go find sandbox mode in the tutorial and plug it in. That's when I decided on a refund. Maybe the game gets better as you get further into it, but it shouldn't take that long for the game to get fun and interesting.

I do have to admit that the physics of items were really cool. I was able to pull a door open with the hook of a crowbar and reloading guns was nice. I used a trash can lid as a shield to keep an enemy back while I smacked his head with a hammer. I just wish there was a bit more action in the first 1.5 hours I spent playing it.\t\t\t\t\t
