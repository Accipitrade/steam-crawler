Stress Level Zero is crafting a universe and this is just a small story in within it.

The game isn't perfect, but what it is is fantastic.

This game brings back the mystery and wonder of my younger gaming experiences.

I've played for 30 hours and have so many ahead, I have things to find out.

SL0 has quickly become my favorite developer.\t\t\t\t\t
