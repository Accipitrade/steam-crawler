\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith how often this game is recommended to VR players along with Half Life: Alyx, I went in expecting Boneworks to be pretty comparable to it. Story is a big factor of appeal to me in most games, and this game is advertised as having a narrative story. However I was very underwhelmed by the attempt at narrative. I felt I could barely engage with the periodic notes and FMV-style video displays that you come across. The game felt more like a string of arena-style sandboxes than a progressing story.

On a gameplay level I was impressed by the combat and physics system. However, climbing can be pretty wonky especially when lifting your character's feet up on top to make them stand up. The save system uses fixed points to save, and saves do not retain your previous interactions inside the level. Both of these aspects felt opposing to the style of gameplay and ended up being pretty frustrating to deal with.

For someone like me who expected an engaging narrative, I would not recommend this game. However if you are more interested in physics systems and arena-style combat then you may still enjoy the game.\t\t\t\t\t
