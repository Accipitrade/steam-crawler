Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking pan with cooking spray or butter.

In a medium-sized saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the sugar and stir until combined. Remove from heat and let cool for 5 minutes.

Add the eggs one at a time, whisking after each addition. Add the cocoa powder, salt, baking powder, espresso powder (if using), and vanilla extract, and stir until the batter is smooth.

Add the flour to the batter and stir until well combined.

Pour half of the brownie batter into the prepared pan, then drizzle half of the caramel sauce over the batter. Use a knife to swirl the caramel into the brownie batter. Repeat with the remaining brownie batter and caramel sauce.

Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the brownies comes out with only a few moist crumbs attached.

Let the brownies cool in the pan for at least 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
