\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10/10, This game is the current king of Singleplayer VR.

Boneworks is hands-down my new favorite singleplayer VR game. Every second of it feels like you're in a Freddie Wong video, and the physics are just amazing. I love how far this game pushes the medium of VR, and I wish more developers would commit to making more "hardcore" experiences like this.

If I'm being honest though, I couldn't take an educated ♥♥♥♥ about what is going on in this game story-wise. But what this game lacks in story, it makes up for in pretty much every other category.

With that being said; DO NOT play this game if you are prone to motion sickness. Your body will be catapulted between skyscrapers in real time within the first few levels, and if that sounds too nausea inducing, you might want to steer clear of Boneworks (or at least work your way up to it if you're new to VR).

If you can stomach a high speed full-body physics fueled action adventure in VR, you absolutely need to give this game a try. You'll know relatively quickly if it's not your cup of tea, and Steam's refund policy is forgiving enough to basically let you get a 2hr trial before committing.

Buy.\t\t\t\t\t
