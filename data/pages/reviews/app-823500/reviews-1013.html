\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is super fun, offering something different from other VR games. The gunplay is also very nice, making reloading feel satisfying, especially in the heat of battle. It pulled off interacting with the environment in a way that doesn't compromise the immersion, like your hands and head not phasing through walls. However, that being said, for those who can't handle your head not phasing through walls, do not play this. It's designed for VR experts, so anyone who easily gets motion sickness should look for a more casual VR game, like Job Simulator. If I had to complain about something, it'd be that when using the Oculus Rift Touch controllers and resting your fingers on the triggers and thumbsticks, the hands look really awkward. Also, I kinda wish there was more gun variety, like shotguns or maybe sniper rifles, as well as an expanded variety of the already existing gun types, like a desert eagle or AK-47. I recommend standing up to play, as sitting down makes it difficult to quickly reload your weapons and reaching around your back for your waist inventory. Just be sure to turn off your ceiling fan before you climb a ladder :,)\t\t\t\t\t
