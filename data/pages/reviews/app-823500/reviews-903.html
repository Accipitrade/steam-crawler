\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTl;dr - Boneworks is an amazing game worthy of your time and money, provided you don't get motion sick easily.
Sometimes the physics are wonky, but I loved the fact that you can cheese any puzzle as long as you think outside the box with the physics. Many people complain about the story, or lack thereof, but Stress Level Zero have created a very interesting narrative provided you get into the lore and details (yes, there is lore). Let's just say that all of SLZ's games are interconnected, that there is the Void, concepts of immortality and SCP warning signs.
Another thing I really enjoyed is the soundtrack, especially that one track that plays near the end of the game, really sparked my imagination.
Overall, great game I recommend to any VR owner that has the stomach for a whole lot of vertical and horizontal locomotion.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was the best, and most violent VR game I've played. The world was interesting and it always made me want to know what was behind each corner. The enemies are fun and challenging to face too. I also love the physics in the game, it was so cool.
The only problems I had with the game is that the story was a bit confusing, I just thought the writings on the wall in the beginning was just the ramblings of a "Rattmann" like character, and the writing on the walls from the nullmen (which I did not know that until I watched videos) I went into the game blind and didn't really understand what happened until I looked more into it.
I would suggest having Ford either think or talk to himself, to give the player a bit of insight as to what's going on. I would also like to suggest adding shotguns and grenades, I feel they could make the levels even more enjoyable.
Overall a great game, love the physics and the gameplay, hope you guys at Stress Level Zero keep up the great work!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best VR experiences out there. You have to be an "advanced VR user" meaning, having VR legs, not getting sick easily when it comes to jumping and falling from heights, and played multiple VR titles. This game does not hold your hand. The puzzles are fairly difficult. You can cheat a lot on the puzzles, the game complements thinking out side of the box. If you have played many VR games and want a industry pushing game this is for you. If your are fairly new to VR, I would not recommend this game, I have seen many post on forums about new VR users struggling with the games mechanics. Stress level 0 never disappoints. This physics as you have likely heard are tier one. The lore of the game seems very bland and simple to the average player, but if you actually dig into the story it is quite interesting (e.g. looking up theory's about the ending) This game is a mile stone for the VR industry. If you don't purchase this game your missing out.\t\t\t\t\t
