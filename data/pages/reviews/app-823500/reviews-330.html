\r\n\t\t\t\t\teasily my favorite VR game of all time. every part of this game was great, almost every challenge was puzzling but it was SO rewarding when you finally got through it, the guns are amazing to use and the enemies are really fun to right against.

I have a few complaints about the game though.
1. the story mode ending was a little bit anti climactic, i felt like all of your work to get ford out of his own company didn't end with a resolution. not only that but its kind of confusing to grasp what actually happened at the end
2. having to walk around every single lever trying to find ammo and secrets for sandbox is kind of a boring and repetitive task that could end up taking upwards of 5 hours. its much easier to just download a full save file and skip that whole mess.
3. the whole idea of having ammo as a currency left me believing that ammo was a scarce resource and i needed to use as little of it as possible, and by the end of the game i had 2000 ammo left to spare with nothing to really do with it. i felt like i would have had so much more fun if i knew that i could use much more ammo without worrying about it.
Those are my 3 main concerns about the game, dont get me wrong though that is nothing compared to how much fun the game already is. 10/10 game a couple small fixes and its perfect\t\t\t\t\t
