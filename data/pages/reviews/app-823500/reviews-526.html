\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell... It's really cool for a VR game, The story mode is moderately long and quite alright. Yet there ain't really that much of as story.

The good parts:
Graphics and environments are all pretty pimp.
The gunplay is totally decent!
Pretty immersive overall.

The less good parts:
THE PHYSICS ENGINE! Woooo wow i can move stuff around. much cool!!!! such realism.
The physics parts of this game is frustrating to no end at worst and gimicky at best. The friction is entirely off and it feels like all the objects are just sliding around. The lifting heavy objects part is just horrible and annoying. And some of the puzzles are such a phase killer. The melee combat is annoying at worst.

But still all in all a decent VR game. But if you have played Blade and Sorcery you'll find the physics engine a pain, the Gunplay is okay but to where neare Pavlov or into the void.

3/7\t\t\t\t\t
