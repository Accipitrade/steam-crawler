\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, BONEWORKS is one of the first games to achieve an innovative VR movement system with a physics simulated player body that feels natural in both movement and world interaction. It has set a precedent for future VR titles and for this alone I recommend the game,

BUT.
Many people view this game as a sandbox and nothing more due to the physics-based nature of the game. Yet if you keep an eye out as you play, you will find the start of a rather interesting story that spans across multiple Stress Level Zero titles. A story about time travel, the pursuit of immortality, the many secrets (and dangers) the Void holds and the corporations and individuals seeking to bend it to their will.

It is primarily told through the environment, through clipboards and writing on the wall in hidden alcoves. The story of BONEWORKS may be rather unclear even to people who are paying attention, but it is by design. The story actually began in Duck Season and Duck Season VR and will continue forward through BONELAB and other yet-to-be-released titles. The developers have confirmed that the story in BONEWORKS is meant to be part of much larger puzzle that we don't yet have all the pieces to.

if you do decide to purchase this game, I suggest you keep an eye out as you fight your way through MythOS.\t\t\t\t\t
