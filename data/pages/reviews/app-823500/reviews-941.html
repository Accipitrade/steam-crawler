\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
- Robust exploration of VR physics
- Half Life vibes to the gameplay
- Arena and Sandbox modes basically render a number of other VR games centered around Arena/Sandbox environments redundant
- Gunplay is fantastic, decent variety of weapons
- Melee is basically a free for all, if you can pick up an item you can probably kill an enemy with it if you hit them hard enough. If you can't pick it up, bring the enemy to it.
- Gives a good all round experience to highlight the very capabilities of VR gaming

Cons:
- Not much of a storyline to make you feel engaged
- Levels can feel a little bit same-y even at a later stage
- Body physics and climbing are a bit janky and under utilised in the main campaign (with the exception of THAT ladder)
- [Variable] The intense nature of the physics elements in this game can mean people with motion sickness will suffer a bit (thankfully I didn't although my fear of heights was thoroughly put to the test)
