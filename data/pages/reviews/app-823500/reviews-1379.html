\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMediocre.

First up, the game doesn't have basic left-handed support. Which is made even more annoying because swapping hands means you'll be walking around cross-armed.

Vive wand support is pretty awful, having to double tap a direction on your left touchpad while holding the down quadrant on your right one to charge a jump is incredibly convoluted, especially for a game with such a focus on janky physics puzzles.

And speaking of physics puzzles, if you ever thought the only thing missing from HL2 was an option to spend agonizing minutes slowly pulling heavy objects across a map instead of picking them up with the gravity gun, then this is the game for you. You'll also really enjoy watching your arms get hung up on everything, although not in the one place it would actually help you, hoisting yourself up onto ledges. You'll just flounder around on them.\t\t\t\t\t
