\r\n\t\t\t\t\tso i thought this game would be an ultimate win. it seemed so... even felt somewhat like a more physics-based feel with combat way cheekier than Alyx (i mean, i literally was scared to DEATH of those Combine, but the zombies of Boneworks are more just a random hazard). it's got physics puzzles, decent physics (okay, climbing onto ledges are still kinda shaky) but the big meat is missing.
Physics puzzles: sure, that can be part of a VR game provided it isn't to physical *glares at Pavlov competitive players*
Weapons and combat: massive L. the pistols are horribly modeled, smaller than my controller (not to mention smaller than they should be in general...) and the weapon damage feels inconsistent and wasteful. melee weapons are the biggest jank i've ever seen and are exceedingly hard to use or simply not accurate (supposedly, the Kunai have throwable physics... but they kind of don't...)
Story: decent idea, but HORRIBLY DONE!! i legit despise the storytelling in this game. reality is questioned, there is practically no lore in this game whatsoever and feels more and more like a poorly designed ripoff of Half-Life 2 with physics puzzles.
Other random stuff: well, they made a sequel called Bonelab. i don't own it but that apparently has no story either and i have seen reviews describing the game as "not Half-Life 3, but Garry's Mod". also, the zombies' AI make me feel like i'm playing TABS. if you don't know what that is, that's fine, but basically the zombies are jank too.
overall: the devs need to pay attention to quality, and you should not buy the game until they actually give literally any attention to the game in general..\t\t\t\t\t
