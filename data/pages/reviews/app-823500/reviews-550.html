1. It is everything you could want in vr (mostly). I love the guns simply because they work well.
2. The game is a good way to get rid of stress.
3. It isnt hard to mod.
4. You may run into glitches and lag spikes but its based off your specs (the flashlight and near the end of the level show this, for me of course.)
5. Ragdolls.
6. It can be looking awsome but its performance heavy

Of course, there are draw backs.

1. Performance heavy, so lots of lag sometimes.
2. Its not perfect. Weapons and enemys glitch in walls
3. Duel wielding weapons are hard.
4. Dont expect everything all at once in sandbox
5. Im using the rift s but never ran in major problems but the grabbing is wonky along equiping.

All aside this was my problems and upsides.
Its a great game with little flaws or problems\t\t\t\t\t
