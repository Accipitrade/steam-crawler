\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI give up.. the game is just no fun at all!
This is a bit of biased review as I had vr for many years now and might of expected too much but..

The movement tries to be "realistic" but in reality it's just slow, clunky and feels nothing like the real life.
On one hand you are a weak nerd with grease hands that barely can lift something and hold it straight, your melee weapons keep slipping in your hands all the time, every object you hold shakes violently like your grease nerd is having a seizure from all the nachos he just ate. But then on the other hand when climbing you transform into a ring gymnast, holding his whole body in mid air, or when your hand "gets stuck inside an object" you can send everything flying with your psychic powers.

NO CHECKPOINT
Played a level for 3 hours until I wanted to go to bed, just to find out there is no save..
Played two more hours to finish the entire level.. f my sleep schedule -_-

The story mode is just long and boring.
If you are lucky you get to fight something, but then you spend the next hour "solving" "puzzles".
By solving I mean moving boxes and other object with your shaky grease weak limbs, accidentally forgetting to "remove" your hand out of the way and ruining your build having to start over.
And puzzles are just meh.. one puzzle I had to control the elevator to come pick me up just to get the elevator clip through and stuck inside another moving part thanks to the "advanced physical simulation".. so I just climbed the wall like I could of done to begin with..

Even after all this time playing I couldn't figure out how do you sprint on the vive.
It's either random, or you have to press harder to run faster, and then keep squeezing it tight to maintain the speed.

TL;DR
Very frustrating game I would not recommended playing, especially to people new to vr.
Sure it's a cool engine I guess, but I would very much like to remove all that "realism" from it, vr is not there yet, make everything less squishy and plain normal vr weightless.\t\t\t\t\t

I CAN'T TOLERATE A GAME WHERE MY HANDS GO THROUGH OBJECTS. What this game does so well, is gives that interact-ability.
