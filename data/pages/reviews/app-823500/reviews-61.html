\r\n\t\t\t\t\t10/10 (Review by Cyborg_Lucas)

BONEWORKS by Stress Level Zero is one of my favorite games, and probably my favorite VR game on the market. I recently got into it after playing the sequel, BONELAB on my standalone quest. Both games use the Marrow Engine, allowing for the most realistic feeling physical interactions you can get in a game.
In the game, you play as Arthur Ford, on his quest to illegally reach the void, after shutting down the metaverse simulation, "Myth OS City" so no one can stop him. Along the way, he fights many different enemies such as: Rogue AI that wants to escape slavery, Rogue VR headsets that latch onto your face, and even virus protection that takes the form of men with guns. There are also many physics puzzles that make you stop and think.
The best part about the game, has to be the sandbox gamemode. You can spawn whatever you want, after unlocking items in the story. You can make scenarios, do trickshots, shoot a crowd of clones of yourself! The possibilities are endless. Especially when you add MODDING into the mix. You can add mods to the game very easily, allowing for custom avatars, guns, npcs, and even maps!

BONEWORKS is easily a favorite for anyone in the PCVR community. I recommend anyone to get it.

For anyone who can only play via standalone Quest 2, I recommend the sequel, BONELAB.
In BONELAB, you play as an outcast from the Fantasy Land village, "Heavens Reach" after illegally modifying your brain. You go through many challenges trying to make it to the void so you can join the Lavagang, a group of modders and chaos causers uniting to take on something big.

I would recommend these games to anyone new or old in the VR gaming community. Spread the word about this awesome game. This is why YOU should get BONEWORKS or BONELAB.\t\t\t\t\t
