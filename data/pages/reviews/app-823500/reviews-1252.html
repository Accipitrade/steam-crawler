the connected arms and body are great they really add immersion, your arms are never in the right position and your legs are often not in the right place either, however your brain seems adjust for this. this is always preferable than the shortcut of floating hands and heads.

Jumping is great even though you wont be hitting your spot every time, but its still fun, its seems that other developers just don't want to take these risks. The spaghetti arms are a bit annoying but the concept is appreciated. the climbing isnt perfect but it really adds a lot to the game. gun play is near perfect. music was fantastic. what separates this game from almost any other VR game is a story has been told to a extent and the length of the single player is decent (13hrs) probably 5hrs more than a typical COD game.

It has a few bugs, but they are being worked though. (negative reviews where their at the release but have been reduced significantly over time. this game is now the new bench mark for all VR fps's now. I really hope there is a second one and the dev team are very successful.\t\t\t\t\t
