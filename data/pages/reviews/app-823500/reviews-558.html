\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can recommend this to anyone who owns a VR headset and is comfortable with it. From a gameplay perspective I can essentially say that it plays like an alternate reality in which you can be a a hitman like character.

The physics are awesome and from what I can tell, both the graphics (running on a 1060 6GB) as well as the rest of the game (on a ryzen 1500x) runs really well.

As for the VR headset, I was using a windows mixed reality from Lenovo. There are a few gestures that are a problem, like grabbing behind you, since the inside out tracking of this particular unit is probably the worst you can get on the market, I can still say that even after playing the story mode twice, there are a few more modes that are constantly being updated with new content.

Combat feels natural, while still retaining a sort of coolness factore like no other game, even though the lack of enemy diversity can sometimes be a little bit of a downer.

Other than that I can definitely recommend this game and supporting these awesome developers. A shame this didn't make it into HL Alex though :D\t\t\t\t\t
