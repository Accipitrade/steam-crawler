\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's good demo, but that's it.
Good parts of this game such as hunting for treasures or gun mechanics have been done as good or better in other games (HL Alyx for instance).
Physics on other hand though, seems to work at beginning, falls apart by end of story and was main reason I'm just happy this is over - not touching sandbox or any other module.
Here is what's wrong with physics:
1) Your virtual body will wiggle all around when climbing, making climbing sickness inducing, though I have pretty good VR stomach;
2) Hands get stuck in objects when climbing or grab inventory items when trying to climb or carry larger objects;
3) Large objects are still considerably lighter than your virtual weight, but when you step on those objects, they don't act as if your weight has been added. This makes climbing and traversing movable objects anywhere from twitchy to rage-quit-nightmare. Made me quit on secret hunting - I value my nerves more;
4) Melee is nice on paper, but in reality melee weapons get stuck in enemies and breakable objects all the time. The way that happens is that even using other hand to dislodge those weapons from enemies / objects doesn't work and just wiggling weapons around helps them dislodged. This makes one of last levels (Arena) that has no saves a highly stressful and unenjoyable experience.

My verdict (due to twitchy physics alone): 6/10... if above physics problems would be fixed then 7-8 / 10\t\t\t\t\t
