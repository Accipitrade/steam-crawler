\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPC specs at bottom. Score 8.5/10

Absolutely fantastic, a great representation of what vr can do. This game runs fantastic, the shooting and movement mechanics generally feel fantastic (Rift S) and the physics based world is amazing to play in.

This IS THE GAME you show off vr with. The devs are open to feedback but the team is small (about 6 people) so updates will take time. That being said the devs genuinely care about the project and won't abandon it. There's tons of YouTube videos showing all the development progress, it's been so open its great.

Visuals are good and the game runs smooth across a wide range of hardware. I have no problem running at Max settings with 2x super sampling on top of the in game AA & the Rifts built in AA so on the Rift you essentially can run 3 types of AA which makes the game look clean and sharp unlike many vr games from small studios.

This game is a genuine achievement and passion project. I highly suggest buying this and Walking Dead Saints and Sinners vr.

PC specs...
i7 5820k (6 cores @4.4ghz)
32 gigs ddr4 quad channel
2080ti ftw3 ultra (EVGA)
Intel pcie nvme SSD\t\t\t\t\t
