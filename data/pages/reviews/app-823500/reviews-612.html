After severely upgrading my pc and spending an hour or so testing different settings I finally have this game running smoothly. Yes, it can be a beautiful game. But for most players it'll appear passable and throttle you so hard randomly. I'm not sure what reviews I was watching before purchase but this is entirely a puzzle game. Sure, there's gunplay but it's more sprinkled on rather than anything near a focal point. The area, puzzles and everything else here that holds up progress are nothing shy of annoying. I think that's the best way to describe the game. Physics can be great, but often aren't. Gunplay can be fantastic, but often isn't and I've lost every inventory item that I have ever dropped because they just disappear off the ground. The puzzles could be good but my god they just aren't. More time was spent moving and messing with trying to find that perfect tracking location to avoid the "superior" physics from stepping in and running amok. Can be a good game, but often isn't.\t\t\t\t\t
