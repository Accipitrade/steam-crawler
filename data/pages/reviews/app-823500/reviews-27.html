\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is quite honestly one of, if not THE best game I have ever played in my entire life, for context, I have a LOT of games, but I mean it when I say that out off all of those amazing games this one shined brighter than the rest. Now it did likely help Boneworks's case with the fact that it was the first full length VR I had ever played, but even know, after I have seen the farthest reaches of video games, I still cannot say that any other game has beaten out Boneworks in terms of story, immersion, characters, enemy design, action, gun play, Ect. Essentially all that I am try to say is that as a learning game dev myself, Boneworks changed how I saw video games for the better, so if you have the equipment but your still hesitating to buy it, get it, right now. It is amazing.

(One small note I must make is just that if you do decide to play this game as your first ever VR game, #1: You will get motion sick, but after sometime this game will make you become impervious to motion sickness, and #2: Playing it as your first VR title is great, though it does make it harder to enjoy a lot of other VR titles on the market simply because after playing this your expectations can become so high that its hard to find another VR game out there that matches its quality. But still play it.)\t\t\t\t\t
