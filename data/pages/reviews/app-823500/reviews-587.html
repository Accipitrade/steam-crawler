\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBig fan of the company but not of this hot mess.

I've attempted this game a couple times now, couldn't play more than an hour or so before uninstalling again. The problem is you see the problem, you know the solution but they dumb controls of everything is awful and makes it difficult to do simple things (both vive and now quest 2) Without physical feedback, the "accuracy" of weight becomes an annoyance. Climbing is the worst thing they could have focused on here too. Your hands feels sticky weighed down laffy taffys that get stuck on everything around you. I had high hopes for this game but going through the forced experience just isn't worth getting to the "fun" stuff.

In closing, I don't think we're at this point yet. Software is evolutionary but the physical limitations are what break the experience.\t\t\t\t\t
