\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSynopsis: You are Arthur Ford, a man entering a virtual reality who desires to explore a forbidden space. What is this space? Who knows? It's ambiguous (for spoilers sake). Throughout the world, you'll notice graffiti drawn on the walls hinting your final destination. While you make your way deeper into the world to reach some profound conclusion, prepare to fight the many denizens of this corrupted world.

This game includes Story Mode, Survival Modes, and Sandbox Mode. Can be unlocked after completing portions of the game. Keep an eye out for them and deposit them into things called Reclamation Bins.

Controls: Valve Index Defaults
Left Analog Stick = Locomotion
Right Analog Stick = Rotate[Left/Right]---Stand/Crouch[Up/Down]
'A" Button Right = Slow Time
"B" Button Right = Show Menu
"A" Button Left = Jump
"B" Button Left = Open Menu
Triggers = Fire Weapon/Close Index Finger
Grip = Close Fingers
Sensor Pads = Close Thumbs

Mechanics:
Use any objects to attack enemies. Grasp anything, swing it, throw it, jab with it. Climb surfaces like spiderman or a monkey. Jump super high with the press of jump button in tandem with real life jumping (watch out for the ceiling). Tap the slow motion button multiple times to shift into a slower matrix/bullet time modes. Experience near death and recover by defeating any enemy. Catch enemies mid air or mid punch, toss them down/pummel them. Throw random items into things called Reclamation Bins to unlock neat things.

Verdict: Has great potential as a physics based game. If given official workshop, a community could make impressive maps or custom levels. Would be cool (I heard that unofficial mods exist within the community).

10/10
Would play again.\t\t\t\t\t
