Experienced on the Oculus Rift and Samsung Odyssey - WMR. Updated Review.

You can view my video full review here: https://youtu.be/vkYvgIcueC4

So is Boneworks Steam's VR GOTY 2019? I think it's a close call between this and No Man's Sky. Technically, I think No Man's Sky is a better game. However, if you're talking about pushing the medium forward and showing what's possible in VR, then I think there's no doubt that Boneworks should be Steam's VR GOTY 2019.

However, it does have several flaws and my video shows that. There are moments of absolute bliss while playing this game. You just didn't think some of the things this game does were possible in VR in 2019. There are also several moments of sheer frustration.

The physics are the best of times, the physics are the worst of times. Shooting with a pistol is absolutely perfect in this game. Shooting with a stock Assault Rifle is frustrating. Wielding a two-handed sledgehammer is weighted and impactful, yet it still feels at times like it's made out of spaghetti.

Enemies are interesting and combat can be a lot of fun. Enemy AI can be very dumb as well. There's this awesome moment where you grab a handle on a giant moving wheel and end up on a platform far on the other side of the wheel. Then the end of the game has this long wall ladder climb. You look up and it looks daunting. You start climbing and go pretty high and you fall because your hand collided with the inside of the handle and bounced your whole body away. You curse the devs.

At the end of the day though, I had a blast most of the time. It was like I was playing Super Mario Bros. for the very first time. It is next level gaming. No other game has come close to bringing that to this medium. That is why it's my Steam VR GOTY 2019.

Rate 8/10. My original review is below.

I spent the first hour of this game just in the tutorial. I messed around with stuff including sweeping the floor with a broom. I already died because I used a balloon gun to lift this platform up and I ran right into the ceiling fan, dismembering my whole body. The physics are incredible!

Rate 10/10. VR GOTY 2019. I paid $29.99 USD for this right as soon as it was available and feel that I got an absolute steal!

Seriously though, I am working on the review and I will have a full review & video incoming. I may even stream the game later. \t\t\t\t\t
