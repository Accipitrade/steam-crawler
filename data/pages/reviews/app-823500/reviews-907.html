\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs the game suggests, this is intended for advanced VR users only. Being a pretty experienced VR user, I had great fun playing this game.
I'm going to be honest, the game is a little janky, but for good reason. The controls and interactions can be clumsy and potentially frustrating, but that is only a result of the super realistic physics interactions. You may have heard that this game is just a tech demo, and to an extent, it is. This game demonstrates that full body physics interactions and an (almost) entirely non-scripted, physics-enabled interactive world can be a huge immersion factor in VR games, especially games that include melee combat.

Also, the OST in this game is one of the best OSTs I have ever listened to. The story is extremely complex, being that it is linked to other SLZ games. I had to read up on the story, because I didn't exactly pick up on it the first time through.

9/10, awesome VR game, highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
