1. The motionsickness is real and ruins the experience for me.
2. There are no story or sense of adventure to the game, and it is hard to care or feel anything going forward. It just feels like one of those higher quality story community maps in CSGO.

Often your magazine drops to the floor, or you try to grab something and suddenly you have a mag in your hand or thrown away your pistol.

I see the potential of this game, it is just sad they decided to make it so unpolished and "empty". There is NOTHING this game does better then Alyx.\t\t\t\t\t
