\r\n\t\t\t\t\tProbably the best immersive and realistic "modern themed" action VR game with a story. You feel like "John Wick" executing "Gun-fu/kata".

Pistol mechanics is good. Rifles/SMG mechanics are alright. The game does support you by assisting reloads. Rounds are only spent by firing or ejecting. Discarding filled mags doesn't "expend" your rounds. Thus you can reload as much as you want.

Melee mechanics is fun. Blunt weapons seems to be weaker but more consistent in hits because bladed weapons tend to slip and lose edge alignment. You can half swording with claymore. Katana is the easiest and probably most effective weapon to use with it's easy edge alignment and high blade damage.

Climbing is fun but it takes some time getting used to it or may cause motion sickness. When moving over an edge, you crouch with your VR body crouch which I think feels more immersive.

Story is alright, it is interesting and cryptic. Although most people might missed the plot/story entirely.

The music is awesome.

The game mechanics are solid and I do feel this game could have longer playtime if it has coop or pvp or improve zombie/arena/other modes. Imagine a Parkour/racing mode? Imagine PVE/PVP zombie survival where you can build stuff and buy weapons/ammo?

Mods are fantastic add-on.\t\t\t\t\t
