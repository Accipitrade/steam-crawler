There are about fifty different things it tries to do all at the same time, and absolutely none of them work as well as they should. The full body physics are only ever cumbersome and annoying, the combat is a mix of middle-of-the-road gunplay and absolutely terrible melee weapons, there are only five enemy types in the game and two of them end up being just pure annoyances while the other three are complete pushovers, every area in the game except for one is just a variation of incredibly boring gray corridors, the controls (for the vive specifically) are absolutely awful, and the story is about as interesting as watching paint dry.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great techdemo and an awful game. First two hours of the game is awesome, you learn new mechanics, trying awesome climbing for the first time, aaand after that evolution of the game has stopped.

Ranged Weapons

Animations of reloading looks like character is having a siezure and sometimes when you want to grab your pistol with second hand character will unload magazine - leaving you unarmed, cant they just look how this things is done in Pavlov? Reloading while crouching is a dissaster. In first two hours you will probably test all weapons in the game:
- Rifle - they're all the same, maybe some will have lasersight but that doesnt make any significant difference
- Pistol - M1911 and Glock - same guns, but with different ammo capacity
- SMG - UZI and MP5 - same as the pistols
And thats all folks.

Puzzles

Puzzles are boring, tedious and sometimes frustrating, because someone at SLZ thought that its a good idea that your character is not jumping high enough while you stand still to reach most of the boxes and other junk you trying to climb, while sometimes you have to build a stair from boxes and you dont have enough room to comfortably gain speed.
And i hope that creator of puzzle in castle where you have to throw heavy box from one swinging platform to another and EVERY TIME YOU FAIL YOU HAVE TO WALK LIKE 100 METERS BACK CARYING BOX AND CLIMBING STAIRS BACKWARDS CAUSE YOU DONT WANT TO DROP YOUR BOX will burn in hell, really who the played it and said "Yeah, thats a good idea"?

Melee

It feels good man, but some weapons feel so weak (especially twohanded) for christ sake in real life i grab a weapon with a best grip i can think of, TAKE A BIG SWING ACROSS HALF OF MY ROOM AAAND it just *POP* you dealed 10 damage, while you can just spam with short sharp weapons and kill most of the enemies in 2 seconds. Swinging and cutting someone is weak and awful, the only effective way to kill is to stab enemies. And the most frustrating part of melee is trying to break boxes with twohanded weapons in most cases it takes 2 or more hits and if youre not lucky enough for some reason you will deal 0 damage, at second half of the game i said " it" and just started shooting.

Enemies

Zombie - braindead HL2 ragdoll with 3 different reskins
Zombie that throws fireballs at you ! - same braindead zombie that can sometimes oneshot you
Headcrab - just headcrabs that cant harm you
Shooting zombie - respawning mfckers that love to shoot you through the wall

Pacing

Where is the saves ?
Last third of the game is really really bad

!! LIGHT SPOILERS AHEAD!!

For some reason after ok level - Tower SLZ thought that its time for tedious and after that

First: With boring music you shoot some balls from cannons and hit the target, but every time you miss you have to walk and collect the ball again sometimes at compeletely different part of the room, thats just boring. After you hit your first target you will be attacked by some headcrabs then zombies and more zombies repeat four times. Thats just boring as . Then you will wake up in a strange dark room with a crowbar and flashlight - i thought "Wow this will be awesome and probably scary", but you will break TOO MANY planks on door ahead and will drop in to the void immediately after that, finishing the level - WHAT?

Second: You will wake up in some medieval castle and face the worst puzzle in the game that i wrote about before. When you complete the puzzle you will have fight with zombies on arena using sword and shield, and again music is dull, why SLZ used punchy and cool song in small section on dance floor that you will stay in probably for 1 minute, but on an arena you have close to nothing. Next level

Third and final: You will do some climbing, fistfight dude, AND FINALLY YOU WILL GET GRAVITY GUN THATS SO AWESOME aaaand you will need it for like 2 minutes, after that you will have an opportunity to test it on some zombies, but i got oneshot in that section AND I WAS SENT ALL THE WAY BACK TO THE BEGINNING, no progress was saved at all, again climb, again fistfight. And because i dont want to do all of that again, i just cant test gravity gun this time, im too scared too loose all progress again. You will walk through nothing for 3 minutes and climb a ladder from MGS3, midway you will find some magnets that allow you to climb any wall, thats one of the best things in the game. AND AGAIN YOU WILL USE THEM FOR 30 SECONDS and even if you try to climb with them ontop of the build, higher than you have, you will face invisible wall. So you climb again for 30 seconds and the game is over. WHAT ?
Developers forced you to use same weapons through entire game and gave you the best tools in the game IN LAST 15 MINUTES OF THE GAME, BOTH OF THEM. Why they cant just let you experiment with these tools for entire level, they are so fun to use. In conclusion this game is very badly playtested, i sometimes think that before releasing only developers played the game.\t\t\t\t\t

His impression, and quote, "Did Valve make this? I'm genuinely being serious." He fell for it immediately and told me one more level before taking a break. The game itself, however is a 7/10 because it lacks out on its assortment of weaponry and reloading fluently takes a little to grasp for first time VR players.
