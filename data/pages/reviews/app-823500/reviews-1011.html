\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbout 5-6 hour campaign. Really nice gun play. Actually the best parts of this game are the parts where you get to have guns. So because of that the beginning is a bit slow. They recently added save points to the levels so you don't have to finish a level in one session to save your progress. I didn't ever really need them as I never ended a session while in middle of a level. The update also improved climbing stability. On my first session I played this update was not out yet and I was getting very nauseated by climbing as your character will be wobbling around a lot while grabbing the walls and pipes. After this update I never had any issues with it but it might also be that I simply got used to it. I have noticed that I get used to VR very quickly.
At the end of each level you will lose all of your weapons. So remember to toss them all in the reclamation bin near the end of each level to unlock them in sandbox mode. I didn't bother with sandbox mode or the arena mode myself so I can't give you any opinion on those.
The main campaign is fun and varied. Not a fan of the dark sever level. I noticed that it was causing me some eye strain.\t\t\t\t\t
