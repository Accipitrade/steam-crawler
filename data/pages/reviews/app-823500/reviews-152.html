\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rarely give bad reviews, but I feel I have to here only because this game is so hyped up and to me it totally failed to meet those expectations.

It really does feel more like a tech demo than anything else. The story is pretty much non-existent and I found this game quite boring. It is just a bunch of frustrating puzzles and repetitive combat with very janky controls and physics. Climbing, pulling and jumping anything feels like a chore. It is super annoying to have things fall on you or get stuck constantly.

On the other hand, the Walking Dead Saints and Sinners (which came out about a month later) does it much better. Has awesome physics, a compelling story and even beautiful graphics.

Unfortunately it is too late for me to ask for a refund, but yeah did not feel this game at all. Hope that for Bonelab it is more than just a 'physics' game.\t\t\t\t\t
