my current summary: Jank, drab, dry & uncanny.

Positives
* index controllers work well with hand representation
* the games concept of player physics is unique and interesting
* guns feel fun to shoot - equivalent to pavlov, but they are a bit more noob since you dont have to unload them.
* the 'sandbox' being what you load into before the story mode is a nice touch.
* the ability to adjust height and floor is quite good for aligning everything

Negatives
* sense of uncanny valley or unease
* drab colour scheme makes world feel dead and doesn't draw me in
* 0 storyline
* player model head moves when your hands push down on something (so you can climb on things) i personally find this sickening.
* player model arms and legs is a weird experience - feels like you're a head and hands attached to a foreign body
* the 'unique physics' hand models often grab things in janky ways, this is certainly a negative as it just makes the experience worse to have to re-position how im holding, or when I'm still holding a box even though i thought i let go - maybe this just takes getting used to.

Other notes:
* loading screens - please make these more comfortable. most games loading screens in vr make me feel dissoriented and sick, this game doesnt fix that.
* Game performance doesn't seem amazing, seems to stutter, maybe this is my pc performance, or maybe its a lack of smoothing on the player model, i don't know. - this makes this game high up there on the sense of feeling sick.
* audio isn't amazing
* you can jerk items\t\t\t\t\t
