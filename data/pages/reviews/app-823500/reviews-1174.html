\r\n\t\t\t\t\t9/10

BONEWORKS is arguably one of the best VR games to be released thus far. It's linear progression and physics is akin to that of Half-Life 2, but the physics interactivity extends to almost anything. Want that ammo box off the top shelf but can't reach it? You could literally jump up and grab it, shoot it to break it open and get the contents inside, fling a melee weapon up to knock it down... Or you could just do the simple thing and take the whole damn shelf down.

It's physics system rewards outside-the-box thinking with cathartic destruction and puzzle solving, and some of these platforming sections can be almost entirely mitigated with skill and knowledge of how to move within the world. It's perfect for replayability, especially if you're out to speedrun the whole thing.

Gameplay is reported to last 20 hours on that initial run, but it's really down to how you approach the world. If you're taking your time to explore and appreciate it, you'll find that levels can take up to an hour and a half. Gunplay is some of the most intuitive while feeling realistic (mag wells aren't H3VR realistic, but they're not as bad as Pavlov's. If they were that finicky, it wouldn't be as fun for some).\t\t\t\t\t
