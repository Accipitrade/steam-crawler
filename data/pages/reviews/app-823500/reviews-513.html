\r\n\t\t\t\t\t!WARNING!
I have not got everything, I have not found each and every secret, I have not beaten zombie warehouse on a mode harder then normal. According to some friends of mine it can very much give you motion sickness, So if you get motion sick at all, avoid.
!WARNING!

That aside, Killer game. control can feel a bit funky at first, but after maybe an hour or two of gameplay, it feels fluid. The first level in museum can feel handholdy, don't get used to it, you get thrown into the first "real" level with nothing to go on.
Following this you get to do anything you so desire. Really, I found it hard to find things that could not be done. The game has the best combat I think I have ever felt (minus maybe blade and sorcery) And guns feel good to use, the MP5 is my personally favorite. The slow time mechanic is the best of out any game I have played. And I think the bit that sets this game apart from everything else is that you feel like a person, Not only does Ford (the player character) have a fully body that interacts with everything, but it tends to feel like its mimicing what you would expect it to do.
And Finally, the Devs, Stress level zero (SLZ) is a small team, yet they are very invested in there games, and and you can feel it, the game feels like the dev's care about it, because they do. They dev's also feel like they play video games, and as such add elements that feel at home into the game, what they are is not something I can describe, as you have to feel it for yourself to really understand.

All in All, this is my favorite VR game to date. And I highly recommend it to anyone that has a PC+VR headset and does not get motion sick. Also, the Dev's are working on a game in the same universe, which should be awesome, as I think that BONEWORKS is just the tip of the iceberg, it shows what VR can be, and I expect both the next game that SLZ comes out with, and Just future VR in general to be heavily inspired by BONEWORKS, which I think is a good thing.


The game runs well with my PC specs:
B450 tomahawk motherboard
Radeon RX580
Ryzen 5 2600x
16 gigs of ram
Is on a SSD, recommended, as it can have some painful load times.

Oculus Quest 2 with the official link cable

GL HF, also there is some cool ass mods, and adding them to the game is all around an easy process, would recommed.\t\t\t\t\t
