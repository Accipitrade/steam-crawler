\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me openly warn you of the amount of Jankiness this game provides. It's pretty harsh.

Overall, I watched CorridorDigital since their inception and have adored the work they did. Seeing Brandon step into game Development and start making this game, i was in love. I've seen it from the earliest Dev sneakpeak, all the way to the wacky mods circulating.

The story is a bit wild, a little hard to get on with at some points unless you pay good attention. The gameplay is fun, if it is Janky at times. The game itself is still a universal love in my book, as it's my third VR title and my third favorite. (H3VR taking 1st, Blade and Sorcery being number 2) I purchased a new PC not two months ago, and have loved replaying all of these games in a much more consistent environment. I would recommend this game if you don't mind the jank, and want a genuinely good physics sandbox to mess around with.\t\t\t\t\t
