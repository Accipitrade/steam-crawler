\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay here is my take on BONEWORKS,

I've got to admit that after initially buying this game it took me a good while to actually play it. I tried to play it just a few days after release when I was still very new to VR but it just made me motion sick, the developer team has since released new updates making the game a lot less prone to give me motion sickness.

The game is set up in such a way that it doesn't give you too much information or story in the beginning, instead of getting overwhelmed with what your task is you get to play around with the mechanics of the game and get comfortable with your new virtual world. This could be either a good and bad thing depending on what sort of VR user you are, some people may argue that it takes too long before you get to see some action while others feel like its a good way to get used to moving around in VR.

After the somewhat long introduction you will gradually be faced more action packed and intense gameplay.
The puzzles in the game vary a lot in how much physical work is needed to complete them, some require you to pull a lever whilst others will make you carry around heavy objects to progress further.

All in all you can feel that the developers are constantly trying to push the boarders of that is possible in VR and this is something that I'm a huge fan of, I wish to see more titles like this game and hopefully more of Stress Level Zero.\t\t\t\t\t
