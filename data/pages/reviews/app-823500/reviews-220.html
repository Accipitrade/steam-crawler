\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very nice concept, but not for those who easily get motion sickness.
I've been using VR products for a year now, and I did not have motion sickess in a long time, but this game brings it back.
I like the gunplay, the melee the physics, everything, BUT the movement.
I get it, everything is physics based in this game, but it's taken to a point where it's unrealistic.

Superglue Hands

Why does resting my hand on a table with an open hand elevate me, while doing it with a fist does not?
I'm not grabbing the surface, i just put my palm on it and it lifts me up. great.
At this point if i want to lift my hand up from the table, my hand stays there while my character does a squat and now im 15 centimeters from the floor and my hand is still on the table, because f*ck logic (still not grabbing it, i simply touched it by accident). It seems like the game prioritizes the relative movement of the body to the hand, ehich would come in handy in the process of climbing things (more on that later), but maybe some rules could be set up to only do this when you are ACTUALLY GRABBING THINGS TO CLIMB and now when you touch any flat surface. Now after this nice little trip if I somehow successfully let go of the table, my character stays crouched. why?

Why do I have to correct my posture every time I accidentaly come within planck lenght of any object in the game?

Opening doors is difficult, pulling on the door handle brings me toward the door as well, so i just slam the door into my own face.
Pulling a level also pulls me toward the lever, like we are in space, exerting our gravitational forces on each other. things like this are incredibly disorienting and unrealistic.

Climbing

Imma keep this short, if you grab something to climb the character starts to have a seizure and moves around like a ball in a pinball machine, so by the time you climb up a pipe, your head actually travelled the distance between the moon and earth, but in a circural path around your own body, which feels really nice.

I'm sadly asking for a refund, I really tried to like this game, the combat is wonderful, the atmosphere is great, but the movement issues alone are enough to leave me with resentment towards the thought of playing this again.\t\t\t\t\t
