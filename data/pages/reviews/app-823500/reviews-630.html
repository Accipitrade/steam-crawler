\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe most boring VR game I've played so far, both gameplay and storywise. Melee feels like you're hitting people with a pillow that weights as much as a brick since you have the weakest arms and wrists in the history of mankind. And I won't even mention the guns.

But above all, the physics are a constant struggle to fight against. Which wouldn't be as much of a problem if the game wasn't almost entirely based on physics and puzzles that require some kind of precision, but instead you get inconsistency and frustration.

I'm sure this was a very impressive tech demo at some point but that's about it.


Edit: I'm playing it for the second time around now to see if anything's changed but it's all the same. It really helps to play with a 100% game file so you don't have to focus on finding ammo and hidden stuff, which is what I couldn't stop myself from doing the first time\t\t\t\t\t
