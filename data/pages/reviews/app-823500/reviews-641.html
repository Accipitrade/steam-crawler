BONEWORKS is a must play for any VR user.

I seriously loved every second of this game. The platforming, the combat, the puzzles, literally everything was not a dull moment for me. This seriously is a must have for anyone with a VR headset. There's lots of stuff to see, and lots of hidden stuff to discover.

One thing I'd like to mention is the platforming. It sometimes can be a bit wonky. It may take a few times to jump to a certain area or climb up to a certain place just because your hands didn't register a grab or you missed a jump about a few inches. More towards the hands though. Your hands can sometimes clip through a wall, and you can't progress without getting your hand out. It can sometimes be annoying, but it shouldn't be a major issue.

Another thing: Sandbox Mode! Sandbox is great. Anything you find in story mode can be reclaimed and used in Sandbox mode. I like the reclamation mechanic because it gives more of a challenge to get every single item in the game to be used for later. It can sometimes be tricky getting certain items though, and a lot of them are hidden, so you need to literally search every place possible.

All in all, this game works great and it runs fairly well on my HP Reverb G2. I may not have the most best computer ever, but it still runs well and operates at a fair advantage. This game is easily one of my favorites, hands down. It really is worth the full price.\t\t\t\t\t
