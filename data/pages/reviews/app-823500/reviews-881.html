\r\n\t\t\t\t\t^^ This was my first experience on the Oculus Rift S ^^

That was a mistake because it gave me heavy vertigo when you walk/run because it's not like other VR games where you *point&click* BUT after 20 minutes or so I was fine. This must be the most physics based game I've ever seen and you can only experience this on VR which was a good move. It's a great starter game for new players, hours of fun on this sandbox where you can climb,destroy items,shoot weapons,fight zombies/robots .

Climbing feels abit clunky at times because the headset acts as your body and sometimes your "head" goes above the ladder animation but it's a minor annoyance.
Boxing with the robots,grabbing them feels like you're actually there, fluid animations and no drop frames at all!
Shooting/reloading feels organic , the guns have realistic reloading (sometimes you'll find yourself being attacked by a horde and try to reload as fast as possible which adds to the realism)

TL;DR If you have a VR headset. Purchase it without hesitation.

Try the zombie warehouse it's extremely fun and a nice change of pace from the puzzles in the campaign!\t\t\t\t\t
