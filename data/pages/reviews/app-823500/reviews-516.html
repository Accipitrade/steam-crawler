\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe guns are absolutely wonderful. The recoil is nice, the reloading can't be better. I don't know what to say next about the guns. I've already covered everything about using a gun. :p

The level design is also very good. most of the levels aren't 100% linear. I mean as far as I know there's only 1 area where you can start and 1 area where you stop in the level but the way how you traverse it can always be different. There are also a ton of challenges scattered around the levels that reward you with a special box with something really good inside. Often a key to a secret area with goodies or a collectible which you can use for later in sandbox mode.

I like how the game incorporates physics objects into puzzles and ways to progress through a level. Everything is physics objects. The enemies, props, giant heavy containers that are too heavy to move with only your body strength, your own body, etc.
Also a thing to note. If you damage something/someone's joint enough, their ability to move that joint decreases which can lead to pretty funny results. You wouldn't be able to do that in for example Half Life Alyx or Super Hot.

The game also looks very nice. It's the most immersive game I've every played (I haven't played Half Life Alyx because I run my games on a potato) and the graphics are pretty decent. It most definitely doesn't have like RTX but it does have some form of global illumination because when I shine a light on a wall in front of me, it lights up the wall behind me a bit too.
It's surprisingly very easy to run. I'm playing this on a GT1030 graphics card using Virtual Desktop and a quest 2 and it's entirely playable if you can resist playing 35~60fps (without that frame interlacing program that runs on the quest 2 while playing. I believe it's called SSW or something in short.)

My only complaint is that sometimes your virtual body sometimes gets stuck on things
(like against things but not stuck in things) but having a virtual body that interacts with other physics objects at all, outweighs it.

In conclusion;
Very good game with a lot to do. It's probably the best game I have ever played\t\t\t\t\t
