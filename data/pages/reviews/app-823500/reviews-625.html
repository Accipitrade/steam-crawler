\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're a die hard puzzle enthusiast, you might like this game.

But i've given this game two tries on different times and i can't bear myself to like it beyond the combat level.
I don't mind puzzles, Half Life Alyx was the exact scratch on that i needed. But the amount of puzzles in Boneworks just aren't fun in my opinion, they are in fact just annoying.

Early in the game i couldn't progress because the physics had broken and i had to reset the game.
The next time i booted it up, i just *barely* managed to pull through the puzzle (which required me to climb into a vent above me) after 10 minutes of struggling.
After that, i thought it would be smooth sailing from there on. Which it was for about 20 minutes until i was forced into a puzzle that i just got mad at and quit the game.

Combat is pretty good, firearms seem a bit yanky but the melee ones are more refined and i like the fact how they work.
But i can't bear to continue for the sake of it because of the puzzles.
The climbing works when it wants to and sometimes your hands or arms just clip through things and lose grip when the game says so at random.


Do i recommend it? If you got a crap ton of patience and have a puzzle fetish, you'll get a kick.

Personally, i'd recommend Half Life Alyx if you want a good combination of Combat and Puzzles, with puzzles that are more for the enjoyment and satisfaction. Boneworks puzzles are more chores if you ask me.\t\t\t\t\t
