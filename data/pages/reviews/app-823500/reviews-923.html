\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will preface this review by stating that I ran this game on a ryzen 5 2600 paired with an RTX 2060. The game was played on an SSD with medium-ish settings. I also played on an Oculus Rift S, so my time with the game likely didn't have everything that would be gained from having better hardware (A faster PC, knuckles from the Index, etc.). I don't have nearly the amount of time invested into this game, however I will say that I wholeheartedly recommend it. I have completed the entirety of the campaign and played around with some of the side content. While the sandbox is enjoyable for short spurts of time, I found much more entertainment in the story levels themselves. Even at full price, the experience you get with this game is the most enjoyable for the current selection of VR titles, even slightly beating out Half Life: Alyx. The combat is jank, the physics are jank, but all of this jank makes for a supremely fun and interactive VR title. I am confident in saying you get the best single player VR gameplay in Boneworks, not even including the content that will be available in the future. Even at full price, I would heavily recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
