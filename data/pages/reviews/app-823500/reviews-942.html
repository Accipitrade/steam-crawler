If you touch something, hold something, grab something, push it, shoot it, hit it... you get the reaction you expect. The weight of things feels balanced. if I hit a dude with an object, you get the expected reaction based on where you hit him, how big the thing is you're holding, how dense it is, sharp, pointy...
And the guns, I don't even know what to say. There's a few of these VR games that work this way now, but when you reload, it's not a button press, you drop the clip, grab a new one, slap it in, and charge the chamber, almost like you would IRL. Pro tip: always leave one in the chamber. It's fine to empty clip, but always leave one in for the reload.
I love that they did an extra pass just for the knuckle controls. That really pays off with the index.
