stress level zero's horrific track record even shows that they just wing it with their designs and expect people to buy them. the ONLY reason people purchase this is because they want to feel even more immersed in this simulated world when control bugs and awful bindings butcher that thought before they even have it

i just cannot recommend this game in its current state. to be honest i despise giving games bad reviews because i truly know how hard they are to create and perfect. but this isnt a game yet. this is just some tech demo slz made to make people say "WOW i can lift boxes above my head and climb ladders" that is fine, you've reached some kind of stepping stone in vr evolution but unfortunately with every other gameplay mechanic it just isnt as impressing.

the one real proper thing slz has done at this stage is create the idea that you can implement source-level physics and movement mechanics into vr. you did good on that. but the implementation and satirical amounts of bugs and the very lacking "everything else" makes this a game that i cannot recommend to anyone\t\t\t\t\t
