\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm somewhat new to VR and just today new to Boneworks and despite it's positive reviews I feel the need to give a negative one.

I got this game after watching the cool "Youtube vids" of the combat system, thinking this game was going to be some sort of combat based game for the most part I decided to get it instead of StarWars: Squadrons. Imagine my surprise when this game is primarily a platformer puzzle game. Yeah, I was pretty surprised too given the amount of videos about this game and how 10% of those videos actually represent the game as such.

The controls? Maybe I'm doing something wrong but it's janky as all hell. Trying to lift yourself onto a platform? Pffft, forget about it, it ain't happening even though it clearly is supposed to work. Trying to hang from a platform and reach to the next? Nope. Maybe I'm just bad at the game but my God does the experience not seem at all like the actual gameplay videos I seen.

If you love platform puzzle games then this is the game for you! If you came here thinking it was a combat based game then do not waste your money, it isn't the game you think it is from the Youtube videos and it being represented as such is why I'm giving it a negative, to warn off others who may make the same mistake.\t\t\t\t\t
