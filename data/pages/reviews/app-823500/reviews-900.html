\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game could honestly be a lot better. It has a lot more cons than pros. I found myself getting more frustrated with the games mechanics than having any actual fun.

"Enhanced" Physics:

They tried to implement "enhanced physics" which seems to cause more bugs than anything. I found myself getting stuck inside objects CONSTANTLY. There are times during the levels where there are small and big objects cluttered everywhere. You try to do a simple thing, and end up getting stuck inside a cardboard box or your characters movement stutters because you walk on a pop can which can be very frustrating while trying to do puzzles. Or you try to pick up a object, and it decides to fling around and get stuck inside another object. Don't even get me started on the climbing. A lot of VR games cannot execute climbing real well, but this one was absolutely horrible in that aspect. Trying to climb or jump on objects was like trying to jump on a leaning jenga tower.

Puzzles:

They tried to implement puzzles similar to those in Half Life and Portal. Most of the Puzzles are designed horribly, and don't really have any creativity whatsoever. You try to do a puzzle and end up getting frustrated by the games crappy mechanics either by you getting stuck on objects or objects getting stuck on each other. Usually in good puzzle games, you spend a lot of time trying to figure the puzzle out. But in this one, you spend most of your time trying to fidget with the games awful mechanics.

The Combat

The Combat is actually pretty fun. The guns are cool and work decent. My biggest issue with the guns was when I would try to grab a gun with 2 hands, and instead of gripping the weapon my person would pull the magazine out. Other than that they work fine. Melee weapons are really annoying to use. They are really only effective like 50% of the time. Sometimes they work good and can be fun and other times it's like trying to beat somebody with a pool noodle.

The Story

The story is garbage and doesn't really make any sense. There is a lot in the story that doesn't have any explanation or anything. I guess that's what happens when people from YouTube try to develop a Video Game.

I do recommend this game to because there aren't a whole lot of VR games with a lot of content out there, but some people might enjoy this game. I did enjoy the sandbox and survival, but the campaign was aids with all the garbage puzzles and map design. I do not think this game is worth $30. Buy it on sale.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\ti really want to like this game but after some of the experiences i had with it, i decided not to, the game is physics based and thats really cool, however said physics engine is far from enjoyable, the game uses a reverse kinematics skeleton to see where your limbs and body are supposed to be, with that said, alot of the movements that i was able to do in real life are very restricted in game, either i cant aim properly or hit things because of the characters paralysed wrist that makes your arm look weak holding a crowbar, did i mention that your wrist wobbles and bends around when you hack enemies. On to the firearm combat, its very moderate, sure the physics is a fresh aspect, but you have to reload with another mag, i meant you hold a mag next to your gun for the empty mag to fall out on to your fresh mag clipping the physics and thats 3 more seconds wasted in intense combat with holograms balls. Also dont forget the mag holder is right next to your left armpit holster, that means when your rifle runs dry, your emergency secondary pistol would instead be a fresh mag * might appeal to some*. Forget about vaulting things, because your legs are crippled as well, making scaling over objects slower than my grandma does it. Oh and my hatchet clips through the â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥ floor once in a while. Now on to the story aspect, it has no clear storyline, why? because the in game world called MythOS is a work in progress, the developers jammed random textures and objects in to the game, its a literal Gmod server / halflife / portal wannabe. It feels like the dev decided to used prototype object and ai to build the world while adding more detailed objects as their skills in CAD improves.

after my rant, its still a fun experimental game, but i cant take neither the story nor the characters nor the physics seriously, because its just, unsatisfactory and frustrating\t\t\t\t\t
