\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is a self aware tech-demo that sets out to provide you with the best "VR body" possible. Their entire design model is "If you think you can do it, then you can".

When you're first playing you kinda feel like the QWOP Man . On my second time around though I was super comfortable in my new body and I now hope it becomes the standard in VR games.

The puzzles are designed with the intent of no right answer, letting your imagination run free with them. Playing the game a second time around was actually far more fun for me. Solving puzzles in a quarter of the time with methods I didn't even think possible the first time.

The "Null Body" Enemies are so much fun to poke around with and test their limits. Trap him in a trash can and watch him try to break free. Or break his leg and watch as he struggles to still approach and fight you.\t\t\t\t\t
