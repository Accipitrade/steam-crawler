\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun VR game which has a good mix of puzzles and intense combat situations.

I haven't played much of the arena or the zombie mode so I can't comprehensively comment on them, although I did have fun with it when I briefly played it. Campaign level design is very well thought out and interesting with places for the player to explore of the beaten path and find easter eggs. The campaign is a little short, but I put this down to the developers putting time into creating a stellar atmosphere and great level design rather than putting time into padding the game. I would say that you can definitely go back and replay the campaign, especially if you want to get all of the items to be available to use within the arena. The music in this game is amazing which is another plus.

The gun play in this game is truly amazing. I would say that it is just as good as Half Life Alyx's gun play. The guns reload very easily and it's quite satisfying. There is also an abundance of highly detailed firearms and weapons out there for the player to use.

The physics can be a bit... difficult at times with things just being out of reach or having problems getting onto a ledge. I feel like this happened at a few memorable moments rather than being a continual problem within the game and I never experienced motion sickness when playing this game (unlike when I player Skyrim VR).

If you want a great VR adventure this game is for you. Buy it.
