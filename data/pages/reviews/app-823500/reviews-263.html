\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks, in my humble opinion is the single most influential and content filled vr game of all time for a multitude of reasons. First the story mode, this mode offers as many hours as you want, as all modes in Boneworks do. but going fast you can complete it in 3-4 hours however taking your time it's about 7-8 hours The story starts off with the museum of technical demonstration(after a very short level before it that really only has the basic story explained), which is my personal favorite level, it is meant as a tutorial but stays really fun and even has a few secrets for returning players. While I would love to talk about every other level in the game, I would like to leave the story and puzzles for people reading this review to find. All I can say about the story is that it contains some very fun physics based puzzles, some very intense combat sequences and even a level in the sewers and a few other areas that genuinely terrify me. Now moving on to the arena modes. These modes are where you may spend a lot of your time after the story, fantasy arena for me is a fun more general and melee based experience where you can survive and kill a variety of enemies, it is very enjoyable and a ton of fun after you think you have nothing else to do. Zombie warehouse is an experience that is available from the beginning and requires nothing to use it is extremely fun with a variety of modes and I highly recommend it for anyone who has this game or wants it. Now sandbox mode, in the basic map you can do whatever you want. It is fun for as long as you want. The other few sandbox levels almost count as extra story missions, having a large open map to explore in most cases(excluding the outside map that I forgot the name of), and to end off this review I would like to tell you to download an 100% save file after you finish the story, it will be worth it.
