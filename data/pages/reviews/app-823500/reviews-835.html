\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR; Didn't have the best time, HTC VIVE has the worst controllers on earth.
I can not stress enough how bad these HTC Vive controllers play with this game.

The game's kind of short and has a lot of pointless content; the "physics" are meh and kind of sub-par !IMO!. Combat feels tame, copy pasted and predictable, towards the end of the game you just get swarmed to a point where you can't reload ranged weapons, and the melee combat barely worked for me. I could hardly grip the weapons with the controllers and lots of my swings would connect but the impact was like a 2 year old had threw the punch. Swings and stabs with sharp weapons worked but they'd get stuck like a mouse trap. I'm using the HTC VIVE Cosmos Elite VR and the controllers are- just do yourself a favor and look up a photo of the controller for yourself, I beg you. I had the hardest time just trying to keep grip of- anything, climbing had me physically sweating bullets, my arms hurt, and the games tracking of my arms was a joke and often had me face-planting walls.

I could literally nitpick every single aspect of this game and tell you much I hate it, but I won't because I actually like Stress LvL 0 so here's what I did enjoy;

The levels were full and well developed, lots of alternate routes, secret paths, and hidden rooms. The puzzles were challenging enough to have you scratching your head, but offered alternatives and even encourages you to find your own solution- even if it means cheating. LOTS of weapons, collectibles (pointless IMO), and other items to find.

That's all for now, might edit this as I think of more to say.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat an insult this game is! If you've already played Alyx then stay away from this game. Alyx set my VR expectations way too high, then I bought this "gem" to scratch the Half Life itch. The physics don't behave consistently, the two handed weapons behave like they weigh a metric ton yet can't break a box without wailing on it for 2 mins. Also, good luck if you want to climb, jump, run, and explore in the game. I'm not a religious person but there were a few times I begged all the Gods I could just to let me pull myself up on a $%^&#$R% ledge!!! Good luck if you think you can freely traverse the environments in this game. I am so frustrated with this game because it has so much promise. I want to enjoy the combat and puzzles but when nothing behaves it becomes a slog. I enjoyed the combat arenas until I got near the end. All of a sudden my guns wouldn't let me slide the rail back to reload and my hatchet flew off into outer space! This game is not worth full price or even half price. If this game is given away for free i'd still recommend uninstalling it. STAY AWAY from this frustrating mess. I wish I never made this purchase, I saw a few reviews and thought it might be a little wonkey but still a lot of fun....don't get fooled by the reviewers, save your money and play another VR game.\t\t\t\t\t
