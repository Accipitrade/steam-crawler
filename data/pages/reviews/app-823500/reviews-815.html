\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, I'm very new to VR in general so the "It is recommended that you are an experienced player" kinda brushed over my head, which was the wrong idea since I was nearly vomiting hour after hour when playing... but that didn't stop me from enjoying this wonder of a game. (This is first game that introduced me to VR)

After finishing the main story; the atmosphere, the physics, the soundtrack, oh god the physics and music are the two best things about this game. The physics with gunplay is amaaazing, it really makes you feel like you're using each weapon as if it were real, and the soundtrack has been a joy to listen to while playing through the game, every area has it's own unique theme. Even the story is very immersive, you feel alone in this unknown virtual world, given set objectives, battling and fighting your way through tons of enemies that are all unique in their own ways. Some of the puzzles are especially fun, not too easy and not too difficult but definitely engaging.

Even after the main story, you can go back through, looking for secrets which unlock different game modes in the menus. (I still need to do this). Boneworks is a must have, it's a great way to get introduced and sucked into VR, it has so much content, the immersion and realism of the game is well designed, and it can even be played with friends via mods. I am so glad that this was my first VR experience, it is definitely worth every penny.\t\t\t\t\t
