The physic is like no others, melee is great but gun play is a bit lacking. You do not have mag release or slide release feature, meaning you have to pull the mag and ‚ô•‚ô•‚ô•‚ô•(if it is empty) the gun every time you need to reload. I still would recommend this game to every VR headset owner. Very good concept and carried out nicely. I just wish that I could have stronger VR legs.
