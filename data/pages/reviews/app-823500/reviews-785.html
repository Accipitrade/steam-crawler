\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game really gives you a taste of what the future holds for VR. It's kinda hard core in its implementation of how you move, jump, climb, and interact with objects - not easy would be a kind way to describe it. I played this on the Oculus Rift S and the tracking worked ok in almost all cases. I would guess that you need pretty solid tracking because sometimes you find yourself in situations where you need to reach out with your hands in pretty unorthodox ways.

But that's what I like about it! Yea it's wonky but I get a real sense of inertia and weight moving around, climbing, and dragging objects around. It takes some real practice to get good at it so that can be a barrier.

You get a full body and this is really something to introduce to your friends as the reaction is usually exhilarating gasps and wonder.

There is a story and it does its job well enough but the game feels more like a big sandbox with some puzzles and enemies thrown in.

I highly recommend you give this a try but be ready for some difficulties!\t\t\t\t\t
