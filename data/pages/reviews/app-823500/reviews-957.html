\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEven after HL:A (which is one of the best games ever released) I still come back to Boneworks and will for a long time, they are about to release 1.4 as I write this review and I am excited for more content and expect more will be released as time goes on.

The game has a really impressive physics system that works really well, I love being able to climb the levels mostly any way I want, the game is also full of neat secrets.
If you are prone to motion sickness this game may not be a good fit for you until you get more resistant to it, just play more VR games and over time it will get better. I am very lucky as I dont suffer from motion sickness at all so I am able to play the game as "intended".

While HLA is a polished masterpiece this is more of a rougher but still polished physics sandbox with story, the melee combat is fantastic and the guns feel powerful and meaty.

If you have a VR headset you should buy this, if you dont have a headset you should get one and then buy this!\t\t\t\t\t
