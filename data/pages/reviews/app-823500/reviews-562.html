I just realised that I never reviewed this game... oooups better late than never I guess.
(I experienced this title with a rift s + ryzen 3700x and rx580 8gb)

The good : Everything around you, including yourself is a physic object and the game does it well. I heard a lot of people complaining about this game being janky because of this and while I seen a few bug I can't say that my experience was that janky. But honestly even if there is a small percentage of jank, it's totally worth it. I never been as immersed in my vr headset as boneworks did. All of the guns feel just right and the melee combat beat everything for me (yes even blade and sorcery).

The less good : The story feels like an aftertought, dont get me wrong I did watch some youtube explanation of the story and it's link with duck duck season and all. but at the end, you don't really interact with anyone through this whole game and it make the story feel shallow.\t\t\t\t\t
