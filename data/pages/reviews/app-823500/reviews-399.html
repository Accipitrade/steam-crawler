So, without further instructions I just loaded into the game and started messing around. I figured it was some combination of crouching, slowmo and jumping. After immersing myself long enough in the task to forget that I can almost touch my ceiling, I decided to add RL movements into the mix.

So, IRL, I crouched down and LAUNCHED myself up, throwing my hands up in the motion! OWWIE! I hit my hands into the ceiling so hard that I saw stars in my vision.

Aftermath: All my knuckles still hurt. My right pinky finger hurts the most, doesn't have full range of motion and is quite swollen. I think I have a minor fracture in it.

TL;DR: Immersive game. Would buy again 5/5.\t\t\t\t\t
