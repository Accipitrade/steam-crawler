\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you haven't played boneworks really what are you Playing on Vr?

Boneworks, you've heard time and time again how it's one of the best, if not one of the best Vr games so far, but why?

Boneworks, physics based story shooter with puzzle elements and some mind boggling time travel implications.

The boneworks story is around 15 hours give or take and then theres at least another 15 hours to go of side stuff depending on if you're a completionist or not. There is also very high replayability.

Why do I like the game?
I think boneworks is the best vr game because it really makes you feel like you're in control. Example, theres a puzzle you cant figure out that would lower a platform so that you can climb up to a ledge, but you can't figure it out? you can move around some objects and simply climb up, skipping the whole puzzle.

Your biggest weapon in boneworks is your creativity to use your twisted mind in finding new ways to do things. Everything in boneworks has multiple ways to do it, think how you would do it in real life.

The side content (Arenas and sandbox) is as high quality as the main game. I am a huge fan of fantasy arena, the whole hover junkers update kind of blows honestly, wasn't a huge fan of mindless searching, but hey, still more content.

Tediously, you must collect ammo which also becomes your currency. If you want to mess around with Dev weapons (such as the stick hand thing and the rocket glove you see in the trailer) you need a lot of ammo. Watch some guides and buckle up to find every piece of hidden ammo.\t\t\t\t\t
