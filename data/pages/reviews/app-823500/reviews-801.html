If this game had less stupid features, I would recommend it to everyone
. I do hope the developers will fix them one day and the game become perfect! Meanwhile,
the list of stupid features
  1. The weapon is lost when you die. When you die you respawn at the latest checkpoint, but... you've lost the weapon you had in the hands last time. Do you want to try it again with that weapon? Then you must go to the "preferences" menu, then to the main menu, then push "continue" and here you come. Why is it so difficult? Developers, do you hate your players?
  2. Laser sight works worse than regular one. The beam is not clear enough at the distance or at the enemy and for some reason I miss a lot using it. But when I use regular, simple sight I can giveaway headshots even on very long distances! How come?
  3. Insufficient place to carry all balls As far as I understand, the point is to collect glass balls with different pictures inside and drop them to the blue well. But I had to carry guns! I don't have enough space for everything and it sucks!
  4. Drop of the empty magazine is too complicated. You have to pull it manually or go to the menu and select upper option. Come on! A lot of pistols have BUTTON. One push and the magazine is gone! Don't you play Alyx?
  5. Exit to the main menu is hidden in "preferences" menu. What? Where the hell have you seen it? Exit is crucial and must be a separate menu option! Period.
  6. Exit from the game as VR helmet is funny only for the first time. When you have to strugle with it to put it on every time, you develop a gift of cursing the developers for this and prefer force quit from the game.

So I RECOMMENDED this game only for those, who want to experience new level of VR interactivity despite all that indie style stupid features. I hope in future games all developers will use all that advanced features as 'must have'. I will not describe them. Just try the game and you'll see!\t\t\t\t\t

The level of interactivity and physical presence in the game world is unparalleled. Each combat encounter and puzzle can be accomplished in a seemingly endless number of ways based on how you like the play the game. This is some revolutionary stuff right here.

And the soundtrack. Holy hell the soundtrack. Absolutely fantastic.

This is a must play for VR fans. It definitely isn't the most accessible VR title, as many of its mechanics can induce motion sickness and it is recommended to have previous VR experience before jumping in, but if you can get passed that or are lucky enough to not have those issues there is an incredible experience here.\t\t\t\t\t
