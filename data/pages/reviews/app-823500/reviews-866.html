\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile I haven't delved into anything much outside of the story mode, which is nowhere near all the game has to offer, this is definitely my #1 VR game. There were minor problems related to the poor tracking of my WMR headset, but this game still has what I feel to be far better controls than its competition, and once you get used to the in game body, it feels very natural. Playing HL:Alyx after this game was quite the letdown, especially with that costing me double what this did. If you have not yet, pick this game up. The hand to hand combat is satisfying, the gun play is second to none, and the amount of content you get for the money is unparalleled.\t\t\t\t\t
