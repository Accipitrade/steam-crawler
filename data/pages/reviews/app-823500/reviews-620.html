\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo start off with this game is not for new players, and not for people wanting to have an easy time, it's a game that has relatively challenging puzzles and makes you think. You need to use your head. The physics simulation rivals the best games out there and it's by far in my opinion one of the best, the simple act of sliding your hand across a crowbar to get the correct grip is what makes me feel so immersed.

I have seen various reviews from new vr players or maybe the player not lucky enough to be able to say that they have "vr legs of steel" leave this game a bad review, and yes in all fairness I was slightly put off the game when I first started playing, It is the only game that has ever made me motion sick from playing normally, I have 25 hours in the game and I have completed the game through once, After only playing 20 minute session every so often I now can play hours without any sort of nausea, it's one of the trade offs for playing vr.

Nevertheless this game is not only a game, but a tech demo and a damn good one at that, it's filled with funny and cool little easter eggs.

One feature which I think makes this game that much more playable and enjoyable is the fact that you have Slow-motion as a feature, this means that in those tricky situations you start to feel like John Wick, and that feeling of hitting an enemy with a brick is surprisingly satisfying.

Everything about this game is well thought out, the gameplay is fun and I found it a great experience.\t\t\t\t\t

My favorite part of the whole game is that there is no rule on what you can do, And the physics are just like REAL. You can interact with pretty much anything you want in the game. My favorite thing to do is do trick shots in slow-mo while doing fun tricks with my guns.

Anyways I would thoroughly recommend this game to anyone who wants to have fun with VR physics. The one thing that I don't like about the game is that you cannot play the games with your friends, and there is no Online Multiplayer. I hope they add that into the game sometime soon.
