\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll I can say is wow. They really out did themselves with this game and honestly, after playing Half-Life Alyx and this. Boneworks wins. For me at least. Story isn't really explained all that well, and left me with a lot of questions at the end. There is some replay value and they are updating it and adding new features which is great. The sound track is outstanding and man does it add to the game play.

I did have some small issues here and there. Physical combat was kind of wonky at times. Swords and throwing objects didn't do much for me. I don't like reloading in this game but the gun play is stellar overall. Just wish there was a mag and slide release for quicker reloads. (I'm on the OG vive so maybe it's different with knuckles which this game was really made for.) Tracking for reloads cause some problems for me as well. Go to grab a charging handle on the mp5 and pull out the mag, go to holster a weapon and drop it on the ground instead.

Don't let these small issues I had back in the spring stop you from getting this. I would say it's one of the games worth it at full price.
