\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere's a lot of good things about Boneworks but it doesn't really feel like a finished game. I get that it's the aesthetic they were going for, but it just feels lazy and incomplete. The physics, which is what the whole game is about, can get extremely wonky and don't always feel fun to deal with. Some of the puzzles are horribly obtuse while others are very easy or quickly bypassed through climbing or some other method. The combat is ok, but melee weapons honestly don't feel that satisfying to use and it's a huge disappointment for a physics based game. There are mods, but the lack of Steam Workshop support is a huge negative.

I think some people would enjoy this game but it just feels like a bad imitation of Valve game with a physics system that only mostly works. It's an ambitious game that just falls short in many areas\t\t\t\t\t

Finally, I am tired of fighting my own body or the poor ui. Why is my ammo constantly grabbable even if theres no weapon in my hand and nothing to buy? Saints and sinners solved this problem already.
