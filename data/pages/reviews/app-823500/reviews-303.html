\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGives the best feeling of presence in a VR game that is possible right now given the limitations. Unfortunately a lot of the time you feel like you are actually rigged up to a voodoo doll that you must control through contorting three specific points of your body. Also requires the strongest of VR legs to get through this game. The actual game design is quite fun, lots of fun little ideas and environments that keep things fresh. The game mechanics are very solid but also might require a specific type of person to enjoy all of them together. For example, the guns are modelled quite realistically despite most of the game being quite fantastical. I could see someone struggling with the guns at first if they haven't learned how to handle them before. Feels like an alternate reality Half Life game. I don't know if this has changed since I played it at release but it was extremely tedious unlocking sandbox levels and the objects you could use inside them, requiring replaying entire levels and backtracking through them.\t\t\t\t\t
