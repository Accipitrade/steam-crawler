\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great VR game. You can see the work that went into it the deeper you delve. The story is easily forgotten and takes a backseat to game play. You ride the story so that you can do cool ass John Wick-esque shooting. I haven't finished the game but I already forgot what I'm doing in the story at this point I just press forward and try to kill enemies in cool ways using the guns they give me.

That is the biggest problem with this game. It doesn't have an interesting story that draws me in and makes me want to finish the game. If Alyx is true Next Gen experience of VR games then Boneworks is a framework for next gen games. With such a weak story you would hope this game had some sandbox fun game modes, AND IT DOES!

That is where the most fun lies to me. You can replay any section after you beat the game with better weapons or come back to a level with a flying machine to look at secret portions you couldnt find your first time around. Then there are the actual "arena modes". This includes a hand gun course, an arena where you fight waves of enemies like "gorn" or "blade and sorcery" and zombie game mode. This isn't everything but it is a start and they have added a new "hover machine arena" as well where you get to drive a big ol hovership around an open dessert area based on Stress Level Zero's first game.

All in all this game feels like a start and what comes after it has the chance to be a solid 10 out of 10 experience in every way. In its current form it is a start. A great start that is fun as heck to explore.\t\t\t\t\t
