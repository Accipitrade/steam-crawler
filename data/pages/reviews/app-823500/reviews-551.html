\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely one of the best VR games, and one of the best games in general I have ever played. Even after completing the story multiple times and sandboxing for hours, there is still so much to do. Where many VR games just give you a gun and make you shoot the enemies in a slow-paced manner, Boneworks gives you a large level filled to the brim with physics objects, firearms, melee weapons and puzzles, giving you all the freedom to get creative. Just shooting the enemy is boring? Well, drop a brick on them from a four-story building you just climbed onto. The possibilities are endless. Especially the physics aspects as well as the overall "atmosphere" of the game really gives it that Portal / Half-Life feel we all know and love. The levels are so detailed that even after playing them multiple times, I still keep finding new things, collectibles, hidden areas, new routes, etc. The arena modes are also endlessly entertaining. Combine some gunplay with the easy-to-use slow-mo button and you got yourself a true John Wick feeling. I could go on for hours naming everything thats great about this game, but all in all, it's a perfect game that I can definitely recommend, 10/10. Do note though that the game is meant for expert VR users, meaning that players who are newer to VR might get motionsick from this game easily, but you'll usually get used to it quickly.\t\t\t\t\t
