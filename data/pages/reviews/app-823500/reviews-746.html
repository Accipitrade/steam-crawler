\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a freakin' masterpiece, simple as that.

The physics-based VR gameplay is revolutionary, easily the most immersive set of mechanics I've experienced so far in VR. I truly felt like I was discovering video games for the first time again. That's how amazing this experience is.
Gunplay is physically arousing. Reloading a weapon in a video game has never, EVER felt better.
Melee combat could use some work, it's a little weightless, but still better than any hand-to-hand/melee combat I've experienced in any other VR game.
The story and world is mysterious, ominous and complex. You won't discover everything the first time around, and honestly, it seems like the community is still trying to piece together all of the details, even a year after release. Filled to the brim with mind-♥♥♥♥♥. Fun stuff.
The soundtrack ROCKS, easily one of the best game soundtracks of the decade.
On top of all that, there's just a crazy amount of content. A 15 hour story, multiple unlockable wave-based combat arenas, free-form Gary's Mod-esque rooms to have fun in with no particular objectives, fun Dev Tools you can use to spawn in random objects or just fly around like Superman... I'm going to be playing this game for a long, long time.

If there's ever a sequel, you can bet your bones I'll be there in a heartbeat.\t\t\t\t\t
