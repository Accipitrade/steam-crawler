\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally good physics and controls (except on the G2). "Story" is more of an adventure mode.

On my note about the controls. They are work as expected on the quest. On the G2 (using custom bindings as the default ones don't work), grabbing guns off your person is really annoying as I would have to use the trigger which would also shoot the gun.

Small gripes:
Questionable save point moments as I died to an enemy and had to go through a whole obstacle again. Some physics is still a bit wonky, e.g. climbing a pipe and hand wonkin out, a shield got stuck on a wood spike. The game is a bit dark which is a shame since I would have liked to see more detail in their maps if the lighting was just a bit brighter at the dark points.

This is still one of the best VR games because of how interactive it is with the world. Shooting is fun. A tiny bit scary.\t\t\t\t\t
