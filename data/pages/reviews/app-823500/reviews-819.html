\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter hearing about the game being made in the works way long ago, I did all my best to avoid learning anything about the game other than it's a physics based VR game.

Fundamentally, this is an excellent VR experience. Primarily, the physics is what sells the game.

However, that's all it is, a good VR experience. As a game, moreso focused on the story gamemode, I cannot say I enjoyed it. To unlock more components to the game, you have to look in these areas that seem more bizarre than smart to smash boxes and take these collectibles into your valuable limited inventory space, all the way to the end of the game. Having to do this forced me into feeling like I was only limited to one or two weapons instead of discovering what Boneworks had to offer me. Moments such as puzzle solving or climbing sections felt more like a hassle than fun gameplay. The story isn't straightforward, while not necessarily a bad thing, it failed to captivate me and keep me interested at all. There's a whole lot of other nitpicky things I could point out but I feel these would be my main points.

I personally can't recommend Boneworks for it's gameplay, but there is plenty of potential.\t\t\t\t\t
