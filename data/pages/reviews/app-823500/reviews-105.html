No actual story, just a bunch of areas that you blindly move through with no clear goal or reason to keep playing, and the puzzles are completely uninteresting. This is nothing more than a tech demo, not the halo VR game that everyone thinks it is.

This was one of the first VR games I picked up and I was quickly very bored, so if even the excitement of a new way to play games couldn't keep me entertained while playing, then that shows just how uninteresting this game is.

I don't recommend this game, save your money and buy something else to justify the huge price tag of your new VR headset because this isn't it.\t\t\t\t\t
