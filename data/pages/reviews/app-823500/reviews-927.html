\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gunplay is fun, snappy and realistic, but:

-Combat scenarios are few and far between, with only one enemy type that actually shoots back at you.

-The puzzles are annoying and are plagued by wonky physics.

-Dropped items disappear on death, which turns a late-game scenario into a boring melee-slog even when the entire level gets reset.

-The plot is garbage with Youtuber-vlogger level acting.

-Gamemodes, items, and enemies outside the main story are initially unavailable, and are extremely tedious to unlock via crappy easter egg hunts throughout the exhausting campaign.

Most of the trailers emphasize combat, when in reality it takes a backseat to the tedious story, "puzzles", and token collecting. 5/10, would only play for glorious gun/melee combat. Feels like a forgettable Garry's Mod custom map with a depressingly high budget.
