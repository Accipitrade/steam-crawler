\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is awesome. The graphics are great, the game mechanics are good, and the atmosphere is very immersive. I ran this game on a gaming laptop, so you don't need anything super good to run. Overall the puzzles are challenging, but not to challenging that you need to search up how to do them. The weapons feel real and the guns work well. I was surprised to find out that this game was made by like, 20 PEOPLE. Which is just insane for a game that is this well made. If this is your first time playing VR, do not get this game YET. The controls are physics based, meaning that your character slides around a little when you use the joystick. Along with the heavy physics based enemies and weapons. It can be a little hard for new VR players. BUT if you have played VR before and are ok with the mechanics, then this is one of the best times in VR you can have.\t\t\t\t\t
