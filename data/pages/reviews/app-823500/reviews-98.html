\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this game when it dropped, I've played with a number of different controllers including WMR, Quest link, and the Index, the game looks great on all the headsets. The controls on the WMR seemed to be a bit wonky.

I only recently finished the game as you need good VR legs. Most times I'm pretty good but some of the climbing sections especially when trying to climb scaffolding its a bit too much with your body jostling around so much. As I said I've used pretty much all the popular controllers but I really struggle with climbing no matter which system I'm using. That's really my only gripe on the game.

That being said the gun play in this game is sooo satisfying! I feel like an absolute bad ass at times! I mean who thought reloading guns could be so much fun! I love that there are so many different ways to complete a goal, like you can go down kill all the dudes and call an elevator down, you can shimmy across on the pipes, and climb your way over, or you can just like shoot the box down because its a physics object and you realize you were a dumb ass the whole time!

Definitely something you'll play through more than once. I wish the story was a bit more, hold your hand like. I got what was going on but it was VERY light, I watched a youtube synopsis and I had already picked up on everything that was said, I guess I wish there was a bit more in game lore around the end goal and how that all works, I get its supposed to be mysterious and ties in with Duck Season and such but it just feels like there should be a bit more bite to it.

All in all a great game with great ideas and something that's going to set the stage for what VR really should be. All items can be interacted with which I think it so important at this stage, items feel weighty and more "real." The guns are just amazing!

Again you need good VR legs, but this is is the definitive world that all VR games should strive for.\t\t\t\t\t
