It's fully polished, and though some don't think the story was all that amazing, I personally loved it. It was never all that relevant but sometimes that's okay. The Combat in general is the main hook of this game. It's by far the best game to make you feel, and act like John Wick, while looking like a complete moron in real life. The guns? Amazing. Melee? Amazing (if you don't count slicing that was a bit iffy). The fact you can manipulate the enemies allowing you to block, tackle, knock out, head-butt, charge them into a wall. All because of the full body mechanics. Kid you not every time I saw a null-body I wanted to tackle him headfirst into a wall (I did) then proceed to figuratively decapitate it. Plus the slow motion? The guns and melee are enhanced by 10 fold.

My general score? 10/10 a must buy if you have a VR headset.\t\t\t\t\t
