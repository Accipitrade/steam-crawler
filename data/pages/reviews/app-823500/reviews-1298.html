Most games with a free movement system give me nausea pretty fast. In this game I get only slightly dizzy after quite a while of playing. I have some complaints though. I will list some good and bad points below:

Good
+ physics, physical objects and interactions are great
+ guns and shooting feels amazing and natural
+ good controls that feel like second nature very fast
+ mix of combat and puzzle feels good
+ seems like a big game
+ played seated and standing and it works pretty well for both modes. Immersion is higher when standing

Bad
- no checkpoints. Levels are pretty big and you have to repeat the level when it is not finished
- no rebindable controls
- no "left handed" mode (which would be less of a problem with rebindable controls) : slowing time, shooting and walking is all on the same hand for me and that makes fighting sometimes annoying)

All in all still the best vr game I have played so far and the price I would rate as pretty low.\t\t\t\t\t
