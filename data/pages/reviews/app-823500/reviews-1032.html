\r\n\t\t\t\t\t+ A very immersive VR game.
+ The physics can get in the way if you're not acustomed to it, but for me it adds a new sense of "physical depth".
+ The weapons feel very good.
+ This game realy push VR forward.
+ Enemy variety is a little limited, but each enemy type behave in it's own way and is crafted with a lot of love.

- Nausea inducing, use all the tricks in the book to keep yourself comfortable.
- No multiplayer.
- Not very short but not very long either.
- FPS stability isn't well guaranted, it's important in VR and altough I managed to make the game works great, I think not enough efforts were given here.
- While self-conscious, the level design severly lack polish in too many instances.

Overall, the game succeeds in offering a much stronger interactive and immersive VR experience. And with a couple less rough edges, a more ambitious campaign with a multiplayer mode, it lets me imagine VR could bring video games to a whole new level.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've played this game for 2 hours now and I'm about to uninstall it. Here's why:

- The environmental interactions are clunky and frustrating. I think this is a direct consequence of the physics-based system, which doesn't really map well to VR hand controllers. It might be possible to get used to this with time and practice, but I don't think it's worth the effort; other games with more abstract interaction mechanics (Red Matter, Lone Echo) are much more intuitive and work perfectly well with a much lower barrier to entry than this game.

- Same goes for the locomotion system: climbing and jumping simply don't work.

- The context and story is extremely poorly conceived and executed. It's a confusing mish-mash of ideas mostly plagiarised from Portal.

- There is no way to save manually, and automatic saves are so rare that you inevitably have to repeat huge sections of the game. This is the main reason I won't be playing again - I don't want to suffer another 2 hours of boredom to get back to where I was.

I can't find a single positive thing to say about this game - save your money and get Red Matter, Lone Echo or Robo Recall instead!\t\t\t\t\t
