\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pretty incredible and is a step forward for VR. I am a fairly seasoned VR player and this game is intense. There are frequent puzzles that are simple yet force you to use the games physics to your advantage, it’s really cool having to discover them. The gun play is alright. I’ve seen people say the story is really generic but it fits with the game and it works. The main allure to the game is getting to think outside of the box and be creative to solve puzzles. Also the immersion.

All of that being said, it has its flaws. The arms can get really wonky when using certain items and when climbing. There are no checkpoints so you are forced to finish a level, sounds like a future update is going to solve this.

Final thoughts: The game initially warns that it is for experienced VR players only. This is 100% true. Don’t play this as your first or second VR game as it will probably ruin your experience. But for the price, this game is absolutely worth it and a totally new experience that will pave the way for more games like it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile it takes some getting used to, this game is still amazing. Once you get used to the more advanced types of movement in this game it can be a blast just to wander around maps and climb random things. The physics objects in this game are also some of the better feeling physics (best in VR as of now) that I have dealt with in a game. There are also a variety of ways to solve the puzzles in this game which I love, the game even encourages you to "cheat" on them by doing them in ways you didn't expect. I didn't even realize some of the intended ways in a couple areas and instead resorted to stacking a box or two and jumping. Overall this game is probably the best VR game out there for most people, and for the few it, you can probably still enjoy the game for a little and acknowledge some of the great work that SL0 has put into this game. In my book its a solid 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
