\r\n\t\t\t\t\t With Bonelabs seemingly releasing very soon, and with an addiction for new VR experiences, Boneworks as a physics simulator/puzzle game almost hits the nail on the head for everything you look for in VR. I will admit the price does seem a bit much for something that is more of a proof of concept and what VR games could achieve if there was more time and effort put into development, but overall the overall experience is enjoyable.

The ability to interact with enemies and environments is superb especially with the variety of ways you can fight an enemy. From headbutting and landing on enemies from a height to using fire arms and fighting like John Wick is very satisfying. And the ways that you can travel around the environment are only restricted by how you play or think. No one way is the only way to get around an environment. To put it simply playing Boneworks VR is like playing the Turing Test and People Playground in one. Though Boneworks does have an apparent lack of guns to play with. Granted you can get mods to increase the firearms from 3 Pistols, 1 smg with varients, and 2 Automatic rifles with variants to whatever you want. The distinct lack of other firearms really hits hard. Especially when it comes to a desire for shotguns. Melee is fun though the lack of dismemberment mechanics as well as neck breaking interactions is a bit of a let down. Hopefully in Bonelabs they will add in so much more including Co-op play. Even though in Boneworks it is only a mod, the co-op experience adds in an even better aspect than when you first play. Though because it is only a mod atm De-sync can really be bad.

Overall Boneworks is really fun and a must try. And while it may seem a little much to ask but I do hope that they will have the H3VR amount of guns added to Bonelabs, as well as improve environment interactions as well as enemy interactions and hopefully take on the challenge of making enemies destructible and dismember-able. It doesn't have to be entirely realistic but it will add more when fighting enemies like Nulls and Zombies. But most of all, because of how boneworks is you could almost recreate any game you can think of in VR using this as a starting point.\t\t\t\t\t
