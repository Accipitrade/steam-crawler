Second, it's not a "shooter" per say as much as it is about puzzle solving (Think Portal VS HalfLife)

So the game is good, everything works well enough to have fun with, there is a bit of a learning (re-learning) curve though, as things dont work like they do in most other games.

Few downsides are: No manual save. Climbing physics take some time to get down. Some minor glitches like your arms getting stuck through stuff (that's why a requirement for manual saves)

If I had to rate the game right now as it stands it'd be around the 90% mark.

My wishlist: Co-Op, Manual Saves, Guns! More guns! Level editor/mod tools (I would love to see original HalfLife/2 in this engine)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn the first version of my review I complained about the Vive Wands controls, but I just wanted to update and say that I have tried with other keybindings, but they didn't really work out for me unfortunately. I've gotten quite used to the controls as they are per default, and it doesn't bother me too much anymore, but still makes my hands sore after longer sessions of using for example melee weapons and such.
I now have 4 hours played, and they've all been quite entertaining. Pretty solid title overall.
When I look back at older VR games like Pavlov VR, I actually start missing BONEWORKS, it just handles well.

Some things I just wanna point out quickly from my experience:
- I can get motion sick, but this game doesn't make it worse, but it seems to happen to some people.
- Heavy objects are a bit odd in VR, more specifically this game, but if you think about the object being heavy, it is not that bad imo.
- I really appreciate the introduction setup with adjusting height and choosing standing or seated. It feels quite polished.
- Is it worth 25€? - Well I thought it would be more expensive, so hell yeah, go for it. Worst case scenario, just refund it again.

There will obviously be some people who just can't play this game for whatever reason, may it be motion sickness, lack of physical space or other things, but I really recommend it either way. It's great.\t\t\t\t\t
