\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks has always been and will probably stay one of my favorite VR games of all time.
If you ever played Half Life 2 and liked the physics based puzzles/combat, this only expands on that in every way imaginable.

I also like the fact that there's hardly any predefined/limited grab boxes on items. Anything in the world, you can touch and pick up from any angle without restrictions. Alongside that and the previous point, this game rewards you for solving it's puzzles in any way you deem fit. There's no rules when you have the ability to climb, push, shove, and smash everything around you.

The story of the game is a bit confusing, but playing through the main story line and the extra content guarantees hours of exciting VR fun. Just keep in mind, this game only supports sliding locomotion, so I'd recommend some other games that include a toggle, so you can figure out if this game will make you sick before spending the money.

My only gripe with the game is that it's achievements are mostly hidden for just about no reason at all. Once you unlock them, you'll see what I mean. But hey, that's what the internet is for, if you care to figure out how to obtain them all.

This game really set the bar in terms of high quality VR games that include a strong focus on physics and gun play. I recommend anyone who won't get sick give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t
