\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy favorite VR game yet to date. Once you get used to them, the controls and mechanics become second nature, with very few slip-ups. You'll start out feeling clumsy but once you get used to accounting for all of your virtual body, you'll be a lot more comfortable and confident than you realize. The story is pretty solid and has inspirations from Half-Life, the soundtrack is one of the best soundtracks I've heard in a video game, and the gameplay is fantastic. There are a million easter eggs in the game so you'll want to come back to unlock everything. Pro tip: be sure to put every new item you find in the reclamation bins at the end of each level - you'll unlock that item in other game modes.
I am also enjoying the various sandbox levels such as Hover Junkers and the Basement, as they add entirely new experiences, now complete with new songs on the soundtrack.\t\t\t\t\t
