\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYeesh I was really hoping this would be better than it was. I don't know what it is with game's that have full body presence but it just never works.

Puzzles are just awful. Made worse by back tracking when the physics don't work. Got stuck on a gravity puzzle because the item I was supposed to use didn't break correctly and there was no way to get it back up so I had to drag other items from other areas which took way too much time and was a hassle because the body kept getting in the way.

The Vive Controllers aren't very good with this either. You have to push a button to release your hand from the gun which you have to hold on to in order to steady the weapon and then if you are crouched good luck reloading if your "fluffy" like me.

Rifles feel better in the than they do in other games I feel but still aren't quite there. Reloading is awkward. I don't like having to pull the mag out.

And then their is the pacing. Shooting is what you wanna do and there seems to be too few fights that act as any kind of real challenge. So far the only issue I've had is the controls.

I will say when it works it works pretty good but it's a let down for sure. Doubt I'll complete this one. The puzzles are horrific.\t\t\t\t\t
