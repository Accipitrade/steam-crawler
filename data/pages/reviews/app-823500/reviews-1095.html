EDIT: This was written at launch and many of my complaints have sense been fixed, and I am boosting the score up to a 9/10 with these issues resolved.

THIS IS NOT A GAME CHANGER. Well for technology it is, but in terms of gameplay much is left to be desired. And before you think Im blindy hatin on the game let me say that I thoroughly enjoyed it as a whole. Which is why I recommend it for any vr player that can handle smooth location. However there are some serious issues with this game that I don't see many people talking about. The major one being the campaign. The core gameplay is great, however the full body physics interaction and be annoying when doing parkour, climbing or using rifles. Your legs and arms will occasionally get stuck on unintended geometry, and rifles are janky when trying to comfortably ADS. This won’t happen often but when it does it kills the immersion. I think doing something like H3VR does where your hands and body are able to interact with geometry, but everything else isn’t would be much less annoying. This isn’t a super big deal but it’s worth mentioning. Moving on to the story, it is almost non existent. All you know is that your fighting things and that you gotta do something. It never builds up any tension, the whole time your thinking "when this ♥♥♥♥ gonna get crazy" but it never does. Just when you get the coolest items and think it's going somewhere it just, ends. It's pretty clear they tried to finish as many levels as possible, but right in the middle of one they where like "well, times up!" This compared with relatively bad level design just leaves you with a lackluster experience. It's such a shame too, you can really see the potential for an incredible story and campaign here. The other two modes, area and sandbox are excellent though. You'll have a blast messing around with them. Overall, like I said it’s really just the campaign that’s dragging the game down, and while it’s not bad by any means it’s also not really good ether. It’s definitely a unique experience however and if you can handle to locomotion then I highly recommend you try it. Also the soundtrack ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ SLAPS, worth the price of the game alone in that department.

8/10\t\t\t\t\t
