i highly recomend this to someone who loves VR and also loves puzzle solving and shooting.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great game. There was a lot of experimentation done here that I think we will see echoed in VR games for many years to come. It is by no means perfect, but I am excited to see what the future holds for this game, or for the ones that come after from SLZ.

The fully present avatar and it's ability to interact with everything in the game world make this feel like the most grounded VR experience yet. Shoving yourself into doors to open them, using your crowbar to lift yourself over a ledge. That is where the game truly shines. The climbing is a lot of fun, and the melee system is very satisfying.

Some of the downsides are the later levels of the story mode which lose steam after the first half. The shooting is okay, but I am spoiled by H3VR so I might not be the best opinion to rely on there. I wish that the sandbox mode had more things to do, as my favorite parts of the game involved platforming and climbing.

Definitely worth the purchase though. If you are new to VR, I wouldn't jump right in to this one though. It is very intense and relies on smooth locomotion which might have you hunched over.\t\t\t\t\t
