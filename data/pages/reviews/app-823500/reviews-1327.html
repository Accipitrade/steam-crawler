\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuper fun. The most interactive VR shooter I've played. That said, avoid if you get motion sickness easily.

It's got some roughness to it. Holster placement is odd and the story is strange. Not really enough enemy variety. Falling off of big climbs (there's only a few of these) can be downright depressing. Reloads can require some fiddling.

But other than that: Awesome music. Had an absolute blast through the whole thing. Shooting enemies is dope and the weapons feel good. The environment puzzles are a lot of fun. And of course, the physics here are incredible.

I also adore the art direction for most of it. It really harkens back to the golden age of Source. Feels like I'm in this weird dystopian blend of orange maps and Aperture Science service routes. I love it.

Hopefully they find out a way to support community content, but I'm not holding my breath.\t\t\t\t\t
