\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuperb game. It honestly feels more like an evolution on half life 2's physics based combat.
(Note: I experienced NO motion sickness with this game but I can definitely see how others might find this game nauseating. Best way to find out is to try the game out yourself)

I'll start with the few negatives:
- storing and retrieving weapons/ammo from the body is a little janky. I often found myself dropping my guns or ammo accidentally or not being able to pull out ammo from my pack when crouching because my in game waist was to awkward to reach.
- pulling myself over a ledge was sometimes frustrating as getting your legs over the edge took some finicking.
Other than that,
It's amazing to now embody a physics based character. Meaning you can climb whatever you like, wield all weapons and have them behave as the weapon should, and it allows for endless creativity in combat and puzzle solving as genuinely the limit is your imagination. All forms of combat are effective if you're skilled enough mainly because it's based on your physical skill. If you want to barelnuckle brawl everyone go ahead and do it. Or jump from high places and assassinate with two tiny blades far above, go for it. Or just avoid everyone and climb and parkour your way to victory. The game allows it all. It's a superbly immersive experience that has very few limitations. For example, one of my first realizations of what this game could do, is when I walked into a room and a giant statue stood before me and in my head I thouht " man I wish I could climb this" and so I go up to it and grab the base of the statue and climb up. And soon enough I'm at the top. Not only that but I start to swing around the statue and realize I can knock it over, which i do. It all behaves as you'd expect and because of that the game becomes so much fun as it lets you tackle situations exactly as youd like.
I love this game. Despite some of its jankiness.
So many times while playing I kept thinking how this game is like a spiderman climbing game, amazing physics based melee action game, an awesome VR fps shooter, and an excellent sandbox game allllll in one.
Highly recommended!
