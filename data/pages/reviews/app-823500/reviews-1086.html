\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best games I've played this decade.

Boneworks is one of the greatest combinations of game mechanics, story, gameplay, and music I've seen in a LONG time.
Not only that but Boneworks is a groundbreaker, a forefront in its genre, VR gaming.
I think Brandon and his team at Stress Level Zero have done a fantastic job, as well as Micheal Wyckoff for one of the most fitting and groovy OSTs out there.
Man i can't wait for the nostalgia trip in 15 years...

Pros:
-Amazing gameplay mechanics
-Jaw dropping atmosphere
-Fantastical OST that helps the whole Jaw-dropping
-Fun Story with hours of content
-Extra gamemodes such as sandbox and arena for re-playability
-Weapon collection system which encourages exploration of the fine crafted levels.
-ONLY DRINK ULTRAMERSION

Cons:
-Levels are sparse in checkpoints, this only becomes a problem after extensive use. (The game is addictive)
-When you die the level reverts to the last checkpoint with the weapons in your inventory and drops the weapons that where in your hands. You can loose crucial level equipment by dying and having to play through the level again without it. An example would be the flashlight in the sewer level.

Really the only bad things with the game can be avoided by the player but no game is perfect. This game however is nearing the point of perfection. The only issues i could find where more comfort than actual gameplay issues, though i'm sure other players have encountered issues along the way.

So everything considered.
10/10.
Yup.\t\t\t\t\t
