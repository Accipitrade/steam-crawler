\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis one makes my 11th gen intel i7 cry and the janky physics puzzles gave me aids.
Overall 9/10 the physics will not stop being fun to mess with, and you can collect stuff in the story to put into your sandbox item collection to make something like Garry's mod VR: extra physics edition.

The great thing about this game is that everything is interactable, whatever you can think of can be done, your body and everything you hold get true physics presence in the game. After playing this other VR games don't feel right anymore, when your body clips through something in the world, you gun floats through an object, things don't react to impact, doesn't feel right after this.

I will put this on spot number two for the best VR games I think, after HL Alyx. It's a weird game in terms of story and world design but it's one that knows how to exploit VR for all its got. It has no rivals in the physics sandbox department, not even Alyx comes close. For being an actual complete full length game including missions and a sandbox mode with endgame content it's already leagues ahead of the competition. It won't win over the number 1 because of this game's very janky feel, but it comes dangerously close honestly. For its price this is an absolute must buy.

Very nice.\t\t\t\t\t
