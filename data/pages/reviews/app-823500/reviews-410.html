\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks: The best VR action game revolving around modern-ish physics.

When buying Boneworks, you are basically purchasing it for the combat and strategy it offers. If you don't know what that is, look up a Boneworks highlight video on Youtube, and you will get the jist.

Boneworks combat, in comparison to other VR games, is very liberating. The game gives you a body, tools, and enemies, you have to make out the rest. Either that being you punching some people, shooting them, stabbing them, or better yet, mod the game to do more cool stuff, you can feel like John Wick in the comfort of your own home.

Boneworks lets people play in a lot of different areas as well. Want a free expanse? you got it. Arena with set rules? Yep. Arena with customizability and a zombie mode (Similar concept to a COD style Zombie game, but very simple and different in its own way)? They got that too!

For the story-seekers out there that want to know if there is purpose to the game, the answer is yes. To unlock things and items, weapons, and areas, you must beat the story. The story is a 20-ish story if done correctly with lots of secret areas and lore to uncover. It is also fairly hard and requires some thinking in the terms of puzzles and combat strategy. (You can also import a save file off the internet to get everything if you want to skip to the sandbox, tutorials are out there).

To conclude, if you are on the fence about Boneworks, or if its on sale and your debating on it, take my advice and grab a copy of this game. This game is what you will rate other games by when it comes to fun and free combat.

10/10 Well worth the buy\t\t\t\t\t
