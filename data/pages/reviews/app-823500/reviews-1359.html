First of all big thx to Stress Level Zero for making a VR-Title that feels like a real GAME. Especially like one I see myself playing for hours with no urge of refunding it immediately after shutting it down. And another big thx for not going "exclusive" with this. This title needs to be on steam!

The game feels like clever mix of Portal and Half life. I don't wanna say too much about the Story, just see for yourself.

In general i have to say that the introduction left me in awe, though. The tutorial felt great, the first (boxing) fights with that creepy dudes were challenging (it never felt so good to find a "cement block" to have my revenge on one of them) and don't get me started about my first encounter with a crawler, 10/10.

I am in love ...

Cons? Nothing bad to say at this point.

So, if you have an index and were in Love with Half Life and Portal, just go for it.

Performance:

Runs solid on my 1080ti at 150 % ss while looking crystal clear on the index hmd. There is reprojection here and there, but overall a fluid experience!
