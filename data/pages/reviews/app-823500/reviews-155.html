\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBONEWORKS is truly one of the greatest, hell best currently, VR game out there. It is my favourite as aside from this game being a story game, it is also a sandbox making the possibilities for what you do in game and create endless giving you so much to do. I should of reviewed this back in 2019, but didn't care about reviews then but you now get to hear my opinion from the perspective of someone who first played in 2019 up until now across multiple VR headsets!

The story/campaign in this game is very interesting. I will not say much as you should experience the story, but you play as Arthur Ford who is a Security Director for Monogon/MythOS who secretly works for rival company, Gammon who tasked Arthur with destroying MythOS. The story begins Arthur entering a panic room containing what supposedly is the system containing the MythOS simulation. Arthur goes to the computer and inserts the Gammon USB drive releasing the Gammon Virus attack on MythOS causing it to go on lockdown. He then barricades the door, then puts on the Virtual Reality headset in the room... Play to find out what happens next.

The physics in this game are very interactive and has some realism. There are puzzles revolving around this games physics making them very enjoyable, like having to smash glass or throw objects around with a gravity gun which thinking about it are just like the Half-Life 2 puzzles, but the physics are realistic.

Firearms in this game are very fun to play around with and are very accurate to the real life weapons they are replicating. Like hell, you can even load an MP5 by doing the HK slap which is awesome and shows how much detail were put into the firearms in this game. For melee weapons, for the love of god please don't use them if you play with Vive wands. One big glaring issue is that there is no toggle grip and that melees were clearly not tested for long periods of time using the wands as you are forced to hold the poorly designed grips which after holding down for a few minutes, feels very torturous and painful. You can just enable toggle grip via the SteamVR dashboard binding settings, but it should be noted that if you are going to add vive wands in a game, please playtest with them and add toggle grip!

There is also a collectable system in this game which is great for players looking to get more out of the sandbox and story mode puzzles. You can either go around finding all the collectables and depositing them, or you can swap out your save file for a complete dev one which is what I did so I had everything in sandbox straightaway after I completed the story.

I absolutely love the sandbox mode in this game. You could play just in sandbox forever as there is so much to do with the only limit being your imagination. You are given a utility gun if you finish the game (or replace your save file with a complete one) which allows you to spawn in any of the items you have collected in the story (or again, got from a complete save file). It is a shame though because the capabilities of the utility gun are very limited as all you can do is spawn items in and remove them, that is it. However, there is a huge and dedicated modding community for this game which adds so much more content to play around with increasing playtime significantly such as Custom Maps, Custom NPCS, Custom Guns, Custom Objects, modifiers, the list goes on. There is even a mod which implements multiplayer support which is what in my opinion was always needed in a game such as this.

I know that BONELAB is bound to release somewhere this year, but if you haven't please still give this game a shot, it is a surreal experience you will never forget. Can't wait for BONELAB to release!

(also please don't play with vive wands for the love of god)\t\t\t\t\t
