\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNope, sorry. Wrong. This is not how you make a VR game. Full stop.

I understand what they were going for, but this is just painful.

It is not fun. It is just not fun. You can't make a game centered around Unity physics and hope something fun comes out of it. Your character is the weakest guy ever existing. Yes, realism, but I am fairly sure that any average sized guy can handle a heavy hammer better than this guy.

Every second is a struggle with the glitchy gameplay. Every. Second. Wanna punch that guy? Now your hand and your enemy are one thing. Good luck stopping him from clawing your face.

Also, the lack of saves. Coupled with the tedious gameplay and LONG levels make for an experience that is comparable with one of the circles of hell.

The setting is interesting and at some points it seems really fleshed out. Some visual effects are cool to look at, but overall looks horribly Unity.

Disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
