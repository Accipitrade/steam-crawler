Easily my biggest pet peeve in games is when developers lock a TON of the game's content, usually extra modes & maps, behind story progression or, in Boneworks' case, behind specific objectives you have to complete in different maps or modes.

Like, for instance, to unlock Tuscany, you need to complete the handgun time trial in the handgun range. Okay, let's ignore the fact that within this map, every single time you boot it up, you have to redo the entire map in order to actually be able to get to that handgun range. Some people might not see that as a big deal, I think it's really ridiculous. If I already completed the level and want to attempt the time trial or go to a handgun range to test out some guns, why do I have to go through all this other bullsh*t over and over again just to get to it? Either way, I completed the handgun time trial (which didn't even feel like it was made with handguns in mind, more like SMGs) and went through the portal at the end of the level near the handgun range (which doesn’t say anything by the way, just a blue portal with some stairs inside you can see [???, these stairs aren’t even in the f*cking main menu]. When you go into it, it will take you back to the main menu and reset your level progress. WHY??? I legitimately feel like you are making things harder just to troll at this point), and Tuscany was still not unlocked. Really? You're going to not only implement a mechanic in which I have to complete parts of an extra map/level in order to unlock another level that looks cool and fun to mess around in, but you're also going to NOT give me completion when I've completed the f*cking time trial?

The only thing I can possibly think of as to why exactly the game did not actually let me unlock Tuscany after I completed the time trial (killing all 45 enemies and getting a time) is that I didn't hit reset after to save my time or whatever. It's not like it says that anywhere on the reset button, it just gives you a warning that it will reset the level, and lord knows I definitely do not f*cking like the sound of that. Not like I actually went back into the level to see if that was the case, because guess what? In order to get back to the time trial to even check I need to do the entire f*cking level and kill like 2 dozen enemies.

“Oh, but killing enemies is the whole point of the game! That sounds like fun!” I’m going to be honest, this level in particular is unbelievably boring and uninspired – there is nothing cool or original about an inch of the level. You have such an amazing engine and physics system, and you obviously have very cool and fun ideas for VR, and you F*CK it all up by putting this bullsh*t in your game.\t\t\t\t\t
