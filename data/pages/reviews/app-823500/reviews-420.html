\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks is THE VR game that every first person virtual reality shooter should aspire to be and hopefully go beyond. Gunplay, mechanics, physics, and general design make this game superb. Left handed play is not switched or inverted like many other games change movement for southpaw gamers, but you can make it that awkward right stick movement locomotion opposed to traditional if you so desire.

You can see that serious thought went into how everything in this game works. As an individual that's had a fair amount of real world firearms experience, I can definitely say that the firearms in this game feel and manipulate how you'd expect to be able to maneuver them. Albiet you can't "feel" weight in VR - Boneworks does a superb job of trying to actually simulate it, and it does a fantastic job with everything else.

If you're just getting into VR or are looking for an awesome shooter and puzzle type game with basic, but clever little puzzles, this is for you. Scratch that... This is for ANY VR FAN. Get it!\t\t\t\t\t
