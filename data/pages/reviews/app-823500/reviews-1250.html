Your gun collides with your arms, messes up the aiming sometimes. Your forearms and shoulders get in the way a lot when climbing, and you cant lean over things because your legs are pushing objects away. WIth that said, the physics works great in this game, I just believe that not everything should be physical.

Also, it's not the best looking game, or an amazing story, don't get me wrong, it looks decent, and so is the story. But if you're going to purchase this, you're doing it for what it technically achieves. And the final question, is the game worth the price? Yes totally (and that is without me even having tried the Sandbox or Arena mode).\t\t\t\t\t
