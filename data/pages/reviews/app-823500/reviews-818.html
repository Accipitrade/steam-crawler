\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTl;dr: it is good, but not recommended to players "new to virtual reality".

The main points:
* Although a bit janky, it is the most interactive "physics integration" in a VR game to date, by a BIG margin.
* Gunplay is good, above average.
* Melee combat is a bit more towards the janky side.
* The story is very cool to scifi fans and computer engineers, but less interesting to other groups.
* Has a big amount of "post main campaign" content to play, a lot of collectibles and secrets.

The big problems:
* this is one (if not THE one) of those games that give nausea to inexperienced VR players. It REALLY does not avoid involuntary camera motion and other bad practices on VR games.
* The progression of difficulty on the physics puzzles is bad. The jankiest of whole game is at the first hour of gameplay.
