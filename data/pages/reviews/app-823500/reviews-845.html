5/10 - Janky, boring tech demo

I am re-visiting my review having given this game another try with my Quest. My initial playthrough was with a HTC Vive Cosmos and the tracking issues of that headset combined with the generally jank caused by having a physical body made the game an absolute chore to play.

I initially gave this game a 4/10, I had performance issues and didn't find it at all interesting. I am giving it a 5/10 as they seem to have fixed a few performance issues, but the game is still janky and frustrating.

There are no cloud saves it seems, so the game wants me to go through and unlock everything again. No thanks.\t\t\t\t\t
