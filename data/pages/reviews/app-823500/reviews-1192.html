\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright, so this is the first review I've ever done for a VR game. If you want a TL;DR to gloss over, Boneworks is a game that pushes the medium of VR to its absolute limits, even if it starts tearing at the seams. It comes packaged with impressive physics-based puzzles, Half-life inspired gunplay, and it's packed with competent level design that complements exploration and experimentation.

If there's two BIG issues that I'd have you acknowledge before buying the game, you should know that quitting the game during a level will NOT save your progress for that level. It will start you all the way back at the beginning. Also, this game is prone to some rather aggravating and game-breaking bugs. It can range from full crashes, to restarting a whole level due to glitches with your character model or the level itself. Had this happen to me three times over the last 3-5 hours, and its something that can make a person feel justifiably sour.

If you don't have the time for those issues, hold off on buying this game for a month or two after 2019. If, however, you can work through these problem, Boneworks is a game worth the asking price.\t\t\t\t\t
