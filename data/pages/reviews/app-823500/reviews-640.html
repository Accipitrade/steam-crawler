\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrustrating for all the wrong reasons.

I'm refunding (at 113 minutes, just barely quit out in time) because it's clear this game is 'challenging' for all the wrong reasons. I see the solution to a physics puzzle, but moving the boxes, controlling the pallet jack, even just jumping is all made absurdly difficult.

In game menu system is obnoxious, and requires weirdly precise placement.

Some controls are impossible on oculus - you cannot hit A and pull the joystick down at the same time without breaking some fingers.

Upon startup you have to configure 3 things EVERY TIME. Then it loads the main menu.

I felt like I was fighting the game every step of the way to do even the simplest thing, including have fun.\t\t\t\t\t
