\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is the cheaper alternative to Half Life: Alyx, not saying that its bad. I love this game the physics engine is really impressive, the soundtrack is a BOP. The gameplay is the best part, everything in this game feels satisfying, flinging an empty mag out of the rifle with a new one, putting the new mag in and cocking the gun. Feels great. The puzzles I found fun. The story is a bit vauge, but game theory will cover you in on everything. Boneworks was an enjoyable game. I do recommend having a cpu with at least 3.0Ghz. I ran this game on a 2.8Ghz cpu, and except for the last level everything went smoothly. The last level, its big, but all i got was a lag spike every 10 seconds (which im lucky to have good vr legs.) The expert vr users note is no joke, this game does not have teleporting, personally I never got motion sickness. I had a good time, and these are one of the games that will be nostalgic for me in a few years (mainly cause of the soundtrack.) This game is up there with Half Life: Alyx, but I honestly perfer this one more. I liked the story of HLA, but I like the story being vauge. I also find the gameplay more fun. In conclusion, this is one of my favorite vr games, and I highly recommend it to people with decent vr legs. If you don't have good vr legs, go play HLA first, then try this one. 9.5/10 would get my bones working again.\t\t\t\t\t
