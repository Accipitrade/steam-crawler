\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, I do not review very often but this 'game' warranted one.

I cannot recommend this tech demo as that is all it is, the physics are far too janky, objects wobble when stacked meaning you cannot build any kind of platform to stand on, just walking across beams you have laid down has them wobble about making them fall.

Your hands will get stuck alot inside objects or on the other side of walls.

The weighted objects are just awful, it works with some things but most of the time it just feels terrible to handle, as if your playing a janky PS1 game.

The guns are OK, not amazing but not awful, I cannot really complain about the weapons as they work just fine.


My biggest gripe with the game is the lack of diversity, literally EVERY level is a large open area with little to do, broken puzzles and the same copy paste enemy over and over, when you have played one level you have basically completed the game, it gets no more difficult just mind numbing.

The only positive that I can give the game is the dark sewer level, that level made good use of atmosphere and enemy placement (despite them being braindead) I also limited myself to melee weapons for most of this level to make it a bit more fun, but you will find yourself using the crowbar almost all the time as its the fastest swinging high damage weapon, anything else feels sluggish and unsatisfying.


Conclusion: This game is not worth the money, even with the HEAVILY OVERDUE save points as its repetitive and feels awful to play, terrible puzzle design that you can work out instantly but takes ages to solve due to the clunky nature of the game and boring gameplay through repetitive, oversized areas full of the same enemy types with no challenge.

Most of the time you will just be climbing out of the map to quickly skip stupid design, at that stage whats the point?\t\t\t\t\t

It claims to have the best physics in the business, but if you have H3VR for shooting and Blade & Sorcery for melee, Boneworks just feels mediocre at best..

So should i have kept it for the story maybe? The game just didn't feel fun to me, and also made me really nauseous at times (I don't have this in any other title). I really wanted to like this game, but it just didn't blow my mind like it is advertised to do.\t\t\t\t\t
