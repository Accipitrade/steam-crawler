\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a very good game, but I suspect that many won't get past the first puzzle room, so never get to see Boneworks strutting its stuff. (Hint: you don't have to completely rely on jumping!)

Now that I'm a good few hours into the game I can now see where people are coming from with it being better than HL Alyx. Though to me, they are two completely different games and each offers something very different.

Boneworks is a physics sandbox with physics based puzzles with a wee bit of combat thrown in as the icing on the cake. If you approach it as a physics based puzzle game you will like it.

In many respects it is more adventurous in its VR implementation that most games including HL Alyx. Having a physical body in VR makes a lot of difference to the immersion and pretty much anything and everything is interactable in some way. Boneworks sets the puzzles, then it is up to you to use your imagination to complete them with everything that you have to hand.

I'm pretty sure my solution to the first real puzzle room was pretty unique because I'm pants and jumping. But, it is so rewarding being able to complete it in such an unorthodox manner. The fact the game lets you do this speaks volumes.

Highly recommended. Just try to get yourselves past the first puzzle room - it's worth it, trust me!\t\t\t\t\t
