The story is really the only bit i've played but there are a few additional game modes that you can play by finding them throughout levels. The campaign was fun but imo started to really drag on towards the end and after a while I just found myself blazing through the levels not really exploring them because I simply wanted the game to be over but that doesnt change the fact that I really liked the first handful of levels which outweigh the later ones. Other than that though I have had nothing but fun on Boneworks, the guns work great and were easy to pick up and learn, the map design for the most part is pretty cool, lots of puzzles, and even interesting lore if you really look into it. Overall I would definitely recommend Boneworks to any VR players out there new or experienced. 7.7/10
