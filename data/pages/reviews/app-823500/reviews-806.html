\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the best VR game out right now.
The Soundtrack, the Atmosphere and the full body physics interactions with the world make this game feel very unique and extremely fun.

I'm not really into searching every corner and looking for secrets in other games but Boneworks is something different. I check every room multiple times to find secrets, constantly try to climb everything and get to otherwise unreachable spots to find new places to explore, and there are lots of them to find, too.

Combat is another very good aspect of this game, using Slowmo and fighting off 10 Headsets at once is awesome.

The Story is also very interesting even though there is not a lot of it directly in your face, you have to look for additional information in the world and try to make sense of things, which I personally liked a lot.

And then there are the Sandbox and Arena modes which are also packed with places to explore and secrets to find.


I recommend this game a lot.\t\t\t\t\t
