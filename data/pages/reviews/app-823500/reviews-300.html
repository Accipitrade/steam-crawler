\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI normally don't write reviews but in this scenario i feel like i have to inform people of what exactly what there buying. Boneworks currently is the gold standard for VR as long as you follow what it says.

Let me start out with this, If you own Vive wands you will have a bad experience with this game just because i know because i own them. however i have also tried it with a Oculus touch,mixed reality, and index controllers and it works flawlessly. It really is just sad when you see people dislike a game because either there computer specs are too low, or have the non-recommended equipment. Face the facts guys, the vive wands are outdated, deal with it. The whole picking up things is bad in literally every game where you have to pick or grab stuff often.

Now the actual game:
the game is a physics based puzzler with some satisfying combat mixed in. Notice how i said Satisfying and not challenging. The combat is really fun with the enemy's with all the rag dolls, however It is not necessarily challenging foray. However when i say satisfying and fun i mean it is the best combat out there. it easily has the most fresh feeling Guns, Miles ahead of half-life, and Pavlov. so much so that its a put off for me to go play any other game now.

The best part of the game also is the puzzles. i personally loved the climbing aspect of the game with all the secret areas to explore. there is a **** ton of Easter eggs if your into that and a pretty deep lore if your into that stuff. the puzzles feel unique and there are usually a ton of ways to answer them. the devs even said that if the way your solving a puzzle feels like cheating, chances are, its the intended path. like i replayed it twice and got all the dev weapons. Plus there is a TON of replay-ability. there are arena game modes, sandboxes, and even now a flying level.


A in depth explanation of What makes the puzzles so great:
eventually the puzzles are using your common knowledge of what you would actually do. for Example: there is a high window to get into the building, a button, and a ton of pipes going around. you decide to take the boxes, stack them to reach the pipes, then using your crowbar to reach the high pipes, then parkour to the window and break it with by shooting at it, then getting inside to move onto the next puzzle which is super immersive. you can climb stuff because you have a full body with functional legs. WHICH IS AWESOME.

in summary, if you have played enough VR games to where you are positive you will not get motion sick with smooth locomotion even if jarring, and have controllers that have a joystick, you will enjoy this game. I personally tagged this as masterpiece as i view it as the gold standard of what VR is capable of. Hopefully people will realize to not give a game a bad review because they have outdated hardware or gets motion sick. THANKS FOR READING!
