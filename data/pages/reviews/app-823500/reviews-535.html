\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinal Verdict: A Physics Sandbox Tech Demo
After completing the game, I felt that the jokes written on the wall were... more than just jokes. The only thing I here from people that praise this game is "bUt ThE pHySiCs!" and that is debatable if that's a good thing or not. And no, Im not prone to motion sickness so that wasnt the problem. Since it is all physics and this game was made in unity, its very CPU hungry and you'll have to set the game to high priority (in task manger). I still have mixed feelings about the game because it does do quite a bit right, but with its over reliance on physics made simple movement a chore and a pain. A good step in the right direction, but its more or less a tech demo experience rather than a game.
If you already have a headset, the game is on sale, and you are looking for something else to play, then sure why not. Who knows, you might fall in love with the game and that's great, but go in more curious and less excited than I was. Below was my mid-playthrough notes and experiences...


Shooting guns feels pretty good and sound fine, the slow motion shooting was a nice touch. Objects got nice weight to them and hitting stuff feels satisfying. Having a full body is so nice and needs to be in more games. But the game has slight problems that people should know going in...
Movement is probs the worst, I know the game is completely based on physics but it feels more akin to Gang Beasts movement which is pretty floaty. Oh and to play the other game modes you have to beat the story which kinda sucks because... the story is pretty bad. The pacing is to slow and the save feature (though very creative) bogs down the progression.((edit) The game picks up in the later half, but still feels like the story overstays its welcome) FYI as soon as you start the game, turn down or off the music because as it get annoying after 30 seconds.
Note: I have a 2080 super, intel i7 9th gen, 32 gb of ram... using a Rift S
I have major performance issues with this game and its laughable because the graphics are as good as a VRchat world. Idk why this happens because have nothing open besides the game and the frame rate drops in random spots or in combat (which makes sense). Tried fixing it but to no real success, but its an indie team they get a pass.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter my zealous purchase, my first impressions were stunning. The game presented me in a very unconventional story mode, that actually was quite the immersive one, and dystopian too. Having previous experience with this game on YouTube, I had some predictions, but alot wasn't covered; Like the lenghth of the story line (Which was WAAY longer than I initially thought). The absolute gem of Boneworks is definitely the physics engine. A VR game is one thing, but attach a great physics engine to it, oh god. IT'S AMAZING! You can do stunningly alot of things, and brute-force some puzzles too. I would be a liar if I didn't admit to have repeatedly smashed a Nullbody's head into a wall, heh! The great physics engine is also accompanied by an interactive, virtual body. It was quite interesting to say the least. I really enjoyed shoving a weapon stock into my shoulder, gave me a feeling of realism and speaking about the weapons, the handling is great. Like, really. Although, the gun mechanics are simple, tiny abilities like having multiple grips were really great additions. The gunplay is really enjoyable and definitely worth a rememberance. The numerous enemies are really, and I mean, REALLY, fun to fight against. There are so many ways to dispose of one! You can body slam someone, come on! However, the pathfinding, or walking to be exact, might be sometimes a bit clunky. The soundtrack is also worth a mention, it was bonkers. The other game modes, mainly sandbox, are very fun to play, considering the big amount of mods available! I think I covered all the bases now, so I want to give a mention to how amazing the melee fighting is, It really is immersive. My hand and wardrobe would like to think otherwise, but who cares. Also, bashing someone with your gun stock is hell-of-a-lot-fun. That was it, my humble review of the game. If you're thinking about getting it for yourself, WHAT ARE YOU WAITING FOR!\t\t\t\t\t
