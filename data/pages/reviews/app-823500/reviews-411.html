\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI went into this game thinking it was similar to Half Life, a shooter game with a little bit of puzzles that are easily solvable to someone like me, a person who is HORRIBLE with puzzle games. Immediately got stuck on the puzzle that had boxes falling on the bridge.

At first, I thought you solved the puzzle by running really fast from the side bridges to get to the end, didn't work. Then, after like 30 minutes, I thought "Since this game has good physics, I might be able to stop the bridge from falling all the way down, then climb up at the end". That was a PAIN to do. The height of the end platform wasn't high enough, and I couldn't find any tutorials on how to do that specific puzzle, so I was forced to grab a box, jump on it, and jump on the platform, which is super hard to do believe it or not.

Then after that puzzle, you needed to put something on a button for a vent to open up, but you can't get onto the vent by jumping, so I brought another box with me, which took 15 more minutes because of the bad mounting on objects, then I climbed the box to reach the vent, and that is all I remember.

It was then I was like "Okay, maybe I could do some cool stuff in sandbox, like in GMod!" Behold, the most empty Sandbox experience. I loaded the house map and it was very small, with barely anything to do. I summoned some enemies, some weapons, and just shot at them, but that itself got boring very quickly. That ONE puzzle made me not want to play this game anymore, because if that's one of the first puzzles you go through, I DO NOT want to see the rest.

The game has good physics, and the gun play is really awesome, however, the puzzles and lack of anything to do in Sandbox really set me off. If you don't like puzzles, don't get this game.\t\t\t\t\t
