\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame Physics- As far as the physics of this game go, it delivers just like promised; the one complain I have is that climbing is a giant pain in the butt.
Combat- Melee combat is hot trash, it takes multiple hits to kill an enemy and just hitting them is more difficult than in other VR games due to them jerking forward at you the second they are within smacking distance. On the other hand ranged combat feels very satisfying.
Story- If you came here for the story, don't. The way that the story is mainly told is through people talking to you using TVs from time to time. From what I could gather you are stuck inside some kind of virtual world while everyone else is locked outside and you are trying to open a way in for everyone else, except you never do anything of that sort. Almost to the end of the game it turns into some medieval fever dream that comes out of absolutely nowhere, and after that it tries to go back. On top of which the ending is something an elementary school kid could write better.
Atmosphere & Immersion- This game will suck you in even with it's s**t story line. There have been many times in which I played past midnight without realizing. Halfway through it gets reeeaaaally spoopy; not for the easily scared.
Enemies- At first I thought it would be just fighting crab robots and people here and there and oh boy was I surprised. The enemies in this game never failed to send chills running down my spine and the "hey" a lot of them say when first detecting you is very disturbing.
Extra Stuff- The game has some weird obsession with watermelons. There's a system for unlocking weapons and items in sandbox mode, and it is horrible; in the levels you will find "reclamation bins", anything that gets thrown in there disappears and gets unlocked, but they only appear at the end of levels and you only have so many slots to put items in your inventory- and thus I recommend carrying around a trash can to put items in if you hope to unlock all items on your first playthrough.

All in all 8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
