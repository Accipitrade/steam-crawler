\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks is a masterclass in VR Gaming. Rarely do you see a game so built around its core controls in as fun and seamless of a way. It and Half Life: Alyx currently stand alone as the kings of VR done right. I used to be put off and disappointed with the lack of locomotion options, but after having "gotten my sea legs" so to speak, I understand why they chose to be firm in their decision to use smooth locomotion, having your character as a physical model in the world that interacts like any other ragdoll adds a sense of realism and presentness severly lacking in most other VR titles. Not only that, the post-game is pretty good too! Between unlocks for the collection-inclined to a built-in noclip gun and spawning system for those who just wanna ♥♥♥♥ around with the engine, theres something for everyone here. Cant recommend it enough.\t\t\t\t\t
