\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is coming from a 'fairly experiences' vr player with the original HTC Vive and Vive Wands

Physics are awesome. Of course there are many times where things can get a little strange with wrists being wobbly and climbing causing your body to hand and sway around. However, I have gotten used to motion sickness and didn't feel sick at all while playing.

Although some games may have better melee or climbing, the key with Boneworks is that it combines so many vr mechanics all into one experience which feels great when you go between levels and have to used them.

The soundtrack is incredible and I love even listening to it outside the game. Although it does add a lot of atmosphere to it.

The story I thought was a little too scarce. Watching and reading about the lore after the initially disappointing and confusing ending made me understand how deep it can really get but I think the minimal approach was taken a little too far and a little more communication with the player could've cleared up a lot of things.

Overall, if you are fine with motion sickness, this is a must play. this is like a $60 experience and for $30, it's a steal for a 15 hour story and incredible mechanics, not to mention it also has arena and sandbox modes to keep playing
