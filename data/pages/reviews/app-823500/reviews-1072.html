\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had been following this game before I even had a VR headset and upgraded my computer in order to play this game. Some lengths to go for a game perhaps but after playing it, I was only slightly disappointed. Boneworks is a single-player VR game that was thought to be the one to set the bar for what VR should be and managed to turn out as a fantastic title. There are a few glaring flaws with it (getting limbs stuck and that tedious Snake Eater-esque segment in the endgame) but for the most part, Boneworks turned out to be everything I had hoped for. This game is like Gmod tiers of creative with how you can solve a problem. Breaking the game by thinking outside of the box is encouraged through the options you are presented with. You can't even delete your save data without investigating the spawn room and using some puzzle solving. Shooting feels perfect although the selection of guns is limited (zombies but no shotguns seriously?). The controls are smooth with the inclusion of being able to use a stick to adjust stance being an excellent choice. I barely used the slow-motion mechanic but it's an appreciated inclusion as well. Taking the time to learn the ins and outs of Boneworks is recommended as well as retaining a sense of patience while you play. The physics can sometimes be a little odd but it doesn't affect the overall experience too much. I'd still highly recommend this game for its replayability and the price being perfect for the amount of content provided.

Me ranting but I really hated the endgame even though it doesn't take away from the rest of the game being phenomenal. Enjoy your time with Boneworks :)\t\t\t\t\t
