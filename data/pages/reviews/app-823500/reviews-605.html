\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHANDS DOWN THE BEST VR GAME!
If you are not a new comer to VR that gets motion sick, this is the best game there is! It does not have the story that HL-A will provide, the tension in Walking dead or the shiny graphics in Asgard's Wrath but this is the most fun I had in a single player VR game ever.
The story will probably take you less than 10h to finish but there are challenges and sandbox arenas to keep you busy for a lot more than that.
I think that steam did not include this game in the VR game of the year because it would have beaten HLA.

p.s: If you are just starting out, Half Life Alyx is a better game because you have the option to teleport and there is a lot less chance of motion sickness. Furthermore HL-A does have better voice acting, story and graphics but once you finish that and are ok with a little movement, come back to this gem because it is a must play!\t\t\t\t\t
