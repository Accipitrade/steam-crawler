\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is quite simply fantastic.

I've had my fair share of Vr games, but this one stands out. For the sake of not boring you I will organize this review, but to give a general perspective, I love this game for its physics and general fighting.

Physics:
The physics in this game are quite simply awesome. You can not only grip onto object to climb but use your arms to put yourself up. You can utilize weapons and items to climb. The melee combat is the best for me personally because it feels like you are a complete badass (best description I can give). You can punch, grab and throw your enemies with your bare hands and are given a large amount of melee weapons throughout the game, even utilizing things like a glass bottle.

The Combat
The gun combat is great. Some argued it felt uncomfortable, but this did not apply to me, its different, but works great. Reloading is rapid and effective and fighting with a pistol makes you forget that you aren't John Wick.

Soundtrack
The soundtrack in this game is great. Although I can't say its the most memorable one, its definitely fun and fits the combat well. Along with this, you can replay some of the pieces in the sandboxes which adds a level of "coolness" when you fight. Its exciting, fast, and makes you forget that (once again), you aren't John Wick... though we all want to be. In terms of general combat, the game likes to put you in areas where you can fight your enemies creatively (I might have smashed a glass bottle onto some enemy heads a couple times...) and likes to make you (again) feel immersed and like an action hero.

Graphics
The graphics aren't "outstanding" since that isn't the main target of the game, but they are definitely great, with the game keeping a level of immersion and confusion with its trippy, beautiful, and creepy locations.

Story
Personally, the story for me is alright. Throughout the game I didn't particularly focus on the story because I was just having a blast with the game itself, but apart from that it was decent. I didn't really comprehend it until after when I looked back, but in the end, if you play this game for story, maybe you won't be fully satisfied, but if you play this game for combat with some story, you'll have quite the blast.

Puzzles
The puzzles in this game were quite fun. I've never been one for puzzles, but these were pretty enjoyable for me. From what I recall there aren't really that many puzzles, its more of difficult situations that have some puzzle aspects in them like getting over a wall with small platforms that go down when you walk on them. Generally, there are a fair amount of puzzles without overdoing or underdoing it.

Cons
I can't really come up with anything that particularly bothered me, this game was quite fun and really set up a bar pretty high for other vr games. I guess the only con would that I would love to see more weapons, but then again that's more of something I want and not really a con

In summary
This game is great. I fully recommend it and once you finish, the sandboxes and arenas are awesome, and I still play them till now (as I write this review). I hope y'all purchase this game and have fun!\t\t\t\t\t
