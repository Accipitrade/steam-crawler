\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is the best VR has to offer right now in terms of content in my opinion. I have beaten the story in my first play though and it was quite entertaining. Although we are still a ways out from a AAA title here it truly is something new and exciting for the VR community and I would have to recommend to any experienced VR user. However, there are some downsides and climbing is my first complaint. There is quite a bit of climbing in the game and the grabbing feels solid however the movements do not especially when trying to pull yourself over things. I have had multiple cases where my body freaks out a little and I think this is the main reason that this game causes some motion sickness which I have never experienced in the many years of owning my Vive. Sometimes I just feel like it’s too much physics. Also, the game really needs some auto calibration settings. I messed around with my height and the position of the floor and could never get my body to line up properly.

Overall, even with its flaws which I feel like can all be fixed this game is a must have for any serious VR user.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first I found this game a bit of a disappointment. But I stuck with it and now I'm really enjoying it!

The tutorial is pretty long as an experienced VR user felt like I didn't get much from it. There is some questionable level design, like the turret in the first level and the slippery slope that leads to a dead end. (Seriously, how the ♥♥♥♥ did that get approved by QA?)

The first few levels have some rather slow, boring shooter sequences, then a puzzle in a little room, then another little shooter sequence. It's not bad, just rather boring. Being able to climb on things is pretty nice, but I haven't found many opportunities to use it yet, and am not sure if you can even climb up on surfaces when hanging on ledges.

Once you get to the sewers though, things open up. Some new enemy types, better level design, better atmosphere, basically everything has improved. I found myself experimenting more and just having a much better time. Holding a knife and pistol like snake in MGS, cracking people with giant axes, flinging them to the ground with crowbars around their neck to shoot them on the ground etc. I'm not montage worthy, but it feels great to do

After getting past the first few levels I'm definitely happy with my purchase and am really looking forward to seeing whats next and unlocking the new modes. Kudos to the devs!\t\t\t\t\t

would I recommend this game to people?
maybe, if they want a strange story (in a good way) and are used to VR, as well as having a literal good grip on the vive controllers.\t\t\t\t\t
