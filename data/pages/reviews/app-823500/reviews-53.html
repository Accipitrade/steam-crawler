\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat gunplay. Fun melee combat. Fun puzzles.
BONEWORKS is a must-have for VR.

My biggest problem with this game would be: climbing with both hands. I tried so many times to climb up things using both hands. Most attempts failed because my feet were a few centimetres below a ledge but the game refused to raise me onto the ledge - regardless of how low I put my arms (to move myself up) or leaned forward. There were many moments where I thought "I am basically on this ledge now, why won't you acknowledge that?!".
Blade & Sorcery dealt with ledge climbing much better: raise/press right thumbstick while clinging to a ledge to jump onto the ledge. Climbing is much easier in Blade & Sorcery.

Another problem with this game is the sense of progression. There are several times throughout the story where your weapons are removed. So you'll gather new and fun weapons to use but eventually you'll lose them. The constant resetting to a pistol or a hammer was annoying. Don't reset weapon progression; instead give me more challenging experiences with the new weapons.

4 enemy types was eventually boring. Orange techno zombie, blue techno zombie, techno headcrabs, and holographic soldiers. The soldiers all use the same assault rifle. More enemy variation would have been better.\t\t\t\t\t
