If the devs were able to figure out and fix or make some adjustment updates to help easy motion sickness prone players, this could easily be one of my favorite VR games. I'm not sure what Valve did with Half Life: Alyx but i never got motion sick in that game and could play it for hours on end. Maybe Boneworks could add a teleport option or emulate whatever that HL game did for movement to help out us "movement sensitive" players. For anyone that doesn't get motion sick, i am sure they will enjoy this game a lot. I wont give it a thumbs down just because i cant currently fully enjoy it due to my own limitations but it would be nice if the devs would consider a 'sensory friendly' mode for this game .\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks is probably the most fun ive had in VR. Hands down.

I own this game on both steam and oculus store, however most of my playtime has been via the oculus store with an oculus quest link setup.

Boneworks is such an incredible VR experience, and i would totally reccomend it to anyone who wants a unique and in-depth VR game to play. While there is alot going for this game, there are also a few issues i have. ill list the pros and cons here.

Pros:
- incredibly cool visuals and environments. I think SLZ has done a fantasitc job with the environment and world for this game.
-Michael wykoffs incredible soundtrack leads to some awe inspiring and intense gameplay moments.
-incredible replay value, especially for completionists. The game has a huge amount of things to do.

Cons:
-while the physics works very well in alot of places, i found heavy objects to be especially clunky. Small melee weapons are tons of fun, however.
-The game isnt well optimized at some points. While I personally ran the game at minimum specs pretty well, levels would start to lag a bit more near the end.
-This game is definetly NOT for people who get easily motion sick or have a fear of heights. I found myself getting quite burnt out after my play sessions.

If i had to give one line of advice to future players of this game: PLEASE run this game on the reccomended specs. While i got the game to run well most of the time, it cost me an incredible amount of time messing with VR settings, as well as a PC upgrade. I wish i could have enjoyed this game without all that hassle- so please make sure your PC is able to handle this before picking it up.

Other than that: this game is awesome. check it out. I cannot wait to see what else stress level zero does with the rest of their games!\t\t\t\t\t
