\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo just to get this out of the way, this game is pretty good, I have minimal problems with the core aspects of the game, but my main gripe is that if you are going to give your game a story mode, the story should make sense. The story is to put it lightly, a complete mess. I was over half way through the game and still didn't know what the f*ck was going on plot wise, I was just shooting enemies and moving forward. What little story is told to the player is told through far too quiet monitors scattered seemingly randomly throughout missions. If a player has to look up an explanation to understand your story, then it probably wasn't a great story. Next, i'd like to talk about the environments, which in my opinion are just boring to look at. Fighting through matte gray streets and corridors constantly is draining and makes everything feel the same. I get what they were going for, with that design direction, but to me it just comes off as generic. But besides the plot and environments, the mechanics, gun play and physics themselves are damn near perfect, and i'd say are almost on par with Half Life Alyx. Puzzles take great advantage of the physics, and are fun to solve, enemies are designed well and are fun to shoot and slash through, and guns themselves feel great. Overall great game but don't buy it for the story.\t\t\t\t\t
