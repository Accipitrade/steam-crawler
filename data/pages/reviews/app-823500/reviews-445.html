\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDamn, I was so excited to play this after Half Life : Alyx. But oh boy is it bad.

The first thing you will notice is how staggeringly ugly the game is. But I was ready for that.
What I wasn't ready for is the ridiculus walk cycle of your body. Looking a 'me' run made me laugh for a solid minute. Then I tried to crouch, and I was suddenly eating my own knees. Having a body in VR doesn't improve immersion, in fact it's completely breaking it in BoneWorks.

The game seem to revolve around physics ... aaaaand, it's completely broken. Carrying things is a pain. When you try to climb your body jerk around like crazy. After climbing you get weirdly stuck in the ground for some reason. Throwing objects is always off. Lucky I'm not prone to motion sickness, so it was just weird.

They don't use the 'grip' function of the Index Controllers correctly. Why do I have to grab AND press a button to pick something, like my "weapons". (Bricks ... deadly bricks.)

The puzzles are a pain, I didn't really understood the first one, so I glitched through the door instead.

I'll keep playing it because the gunplay look decent. But I give it a solid Janky/10.\t\t\t\t\t
