\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's alright? I'm glad I played it even if it was just to experience the hype first hand, but I can't in good conscience recommend it. TLDR; It's kinda boring, is filled with poor decisions, doesn't live up to its own potential and is very short.


Very Janky because everything is a Physics object. In some places this works and is implemented really well, in other places (like climbing) it's awful.

The gun-play is fine, but there are only a few weapons with a couple of variations, that if you come from the likes of H3VR will leave you mostly unsatisfied. The game is filled entirely nonthreatening (beyond the headcrabs which are just one of the many elements ripped completely from Halflife) and unvaried set of enemies, so you're constantly left with an expectation that something bigger and more dynamic must be right around the corner... There are hints are interesting physics based items but ultimately you have to work the grind to get them, with the game ending the moment you're given access to them within the story.

I don't think a single one of the puzzles was satisfying to solve. Between constantly fighting the controls/physics and overly simple puzzles with laborious solutions, holding the feeling of "I get it, but I now wish this was over" the entire time.

The plot is delivered through portal-esque "writing on the wall" and the occasional Screen that talks at you. The main story itself is incredibly short, has a few moments of intrigue and atmosphere and then forces you through an extremely poorly built "finale" that fails to deliver entirely by just ending; Typically of this game, at the literal second it starts to get interesting leaving you watching the credits with the words "is that it?".

Ultimately this is a tech demo for a game that desperately wishes it was made by valve, with a campaign that seems to have been developed without the question of "is this fun" built intrinsically into the puzzle and level design. If you're really into grinding laborious climbing and "exploration puzzles" (fighting the controls and playing hunt the item and item reclaimer), then apparently it has a good sandbox system that, if you listen to the die hard fans, is the main game.\t\t\t\t\t
