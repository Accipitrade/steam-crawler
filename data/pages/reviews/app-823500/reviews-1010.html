\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInteresting concept that reminds me of other popular games such as half life or portal. There's plenty to do in the game, even when you complete the campaign you can go back and relive the moments or explore parts of the map you previously skimmed past. Additionally, exploration in the campaign can lead to exciting guns such as a katana or a viking battle axe in special BONEWORK's crates where you can recycle in the reclemation bins scattered throughout the campaign to use in sandbox or the arena. Another interesting concept is the points system in the campaign is actually your ammo, where the more you have the higher the score/ammo to spend on the dev guns near the end of the game and also contributes to exploration. There's also the arena where you can fight off enemies in a gladiator pit and use weapons you've collected in the campaign. Probably the most interesting part of the game is sandbox that has real Garry's Mod vibes that I'm down for as that game was a huge influence in my childhood. The sandbox allows you to spawn in weapons, enemies, props and even a vehicle that acts like a segway of the future so you can traverse the large, open area with ease.\t\t\t\t\t
