\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEverything about this game feels wrong.

The physics are janky. Moving objects like boxes is the least enjoyable experience I've ever had in VR. They put a "virtual rubberband" between you and heavy objects to simulate the weight, but it just feels like ♥♥♥♥. Character movement is super delayed and scuffed. Walking feels like you're drunk. You can't reach over a table because the ragdoll body is just a sandbag hanging down from your VR-Headset, restricting movement. Climbing ledges is impossible and if there is a way it's not intuitive whatsoever. Reaching for your items on your body doesn't work half of the time, depending on the loadout slot it actually never does.

The Singleplayer mode is the most boring, cold and sterile ♥♥♥♥ show I've seen in quite a while. Nothing is going on except the disgusting ragdoll enemies and budget headcrabs. The puzzles are lacking in design and execution. They're just bland like the rest of the world. The horrible movement and physics make them even worse. Also this is the first game that actually made me nauseous. No other game managed to do that so far.

You can't even play the sandbox modes if you haven't finished the singleplayer or did some other stuff. I can't get to the pistol shooting range unless I do some unfun box carrying and acrobatic jumping puzzle garbage in a different level beforehand.\t\t\t\t\t
