\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing physics, great gameplay, a wide variety of weapons, and TONS of replayability. I recommend this game to everyone who owns a VR headset and a computer powerful enough to run this game. The only gripes I have with this game are things that might not come up on a normal playthrough, but still got in my way. 1.There is no collision on arms. This only came up 2 times on my 2 playthroughs of the game but I managed to get my hands behind a wall, and because there is no arm collision, I had to turn off my controllers to free my hands. 2.There are some secrets that you unlock for the sandbox mode where it feels like you would have to play the game getting almost every ammo box to buy the gun that lets you fly, just to unlock 1 new enemy for sandbox. 3.At pretty much the end of the game, I got the suction cups and I was using them to move across a wall to a platform, but I fell all the way down 4 times until I got up there, and the only thing on the platform was a crate that seemingly had nothing in it, effectively wasting 10-15 minutes of gameplay. But other than those three things, it is a great game that I am going to be playing for a long time.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe very positive average review score for Bone Works is baffling. I put this game down after I played a few hours when it launched and I returned to it the other day to see if I could finish the game. I should also note I beat Half-Life Alyx before I started to play Boneworks again. Needless to say after playing for another hour I will be uninstalling Boneworks.

Pros:
- Guns feel fantastic.
- Unique interactions due to physics.
- Some excellent SFX.

Cons:
- Bloated tutorial.
- Terrible A.I. Enemies either don't react or will frequently get stuck in the environment.
- Combat encounters are minimal. Instead the focus is on puzzles and not the best part of the game which are the guns.
- Nauseating climbing, still not fixed.
- Character will frequently get stuck on something in the environment.
- Uninspired and boring level design with extremely basic physic puzzle design. Sewer level is particularly a drag.
- Graphics. Nothing terrible but Boneworks didn't look good at launch and looks even worse now. Awful textures and lighting.
- Boring story.
- Melee combat works half of the time and it feels sloppy compared to gun handling. Even the gun handling isn't perfect. Often your hands are stuck when trying to reload certain weapons like the SMG.
- Pretentious easter egg hunting hide the fun content in the campaign.

With the talent and hype behind Boneworks I guess my expectations were too high. Overall I believe Boneworks is an admirable effort offset by a lack of direction during development.\t\t\t\t\t
