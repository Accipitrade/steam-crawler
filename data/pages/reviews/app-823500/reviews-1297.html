\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I took my time and played for about 4 hours before I had to go to work. I love this game, the puzzles are great and you can solve them in many ways. You could either stack the blocks on top of each other to get to a platform that has a key which seems like the obvious solution or you can shoot the floor boards of the platform and have the key drop down to you. I don't have room scale so melee combat is a little tricky but still fun.

So after getting about half way through the game I took a break and when I came back I was exploring the Main Menu and found a secret hatch that protected a button. Well in my excitement I didn't read the buttons label, yeah sure there were some writing next too it that said don't touch but any button labeled don't touch in a video game is worth seeing what it does. Well, it deleted my save profile. Several labels said "deletes save profile" but i was too hasty to read that ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥.

On my second attempt through the game i discovered the archive room which will unlock sandbox mode and to use items/weapons and what not in the sandbox mode you need to reclaim them throughout the campaign at the end of the levels. I remember the devs saying something like as you play the game you'll unlock more items with each play through. Well I'll update my review when I find out if thats the case.

TLDR: 10/10 Would delete my save profile again.\t\t\t\t\t
