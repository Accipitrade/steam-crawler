\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is literally the best game I've ever played.

The physics make everything an incredible experience. Like the combat, for instance, is SUPER fun. Being able to put a blade into an enemy and just push it through the other side is an experience you can only get with Boneworks. It is so satisfying.

I loved the puzzle aspects of the game too. There are so many ways to do the puzzles that sometimes when solving a puzzle I feel like I'm cheating, even though I'm fully aware that you can't cheat. Like in the museum at the beginning, I held open the door in front of the Reclamation Bin with a trash can and threw all the weapons from the impact physics testing range into the Reclamation Bin. (Seriously though, do that. 'Cause it makes sandbox mode way more fun when you have all those weapons)

I was also pleasantly surprised with how well it ran on my computer. (CPU: Intel Core i5-6400, GPU: NVIDIA GeForce GTX970) There was almost no lag, despite the incredible graphics and physics.
With most games, my computer will only be able to run for like 3-4 hours and then crash. Maybe even less. But on Boneworks, I've played for 86 hours, and I've only opened it two times. That means that each time I opened it, it ran perfectly fine for about 43 hours.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow! Boneworks is one heck of a game that gives me serious Portal/Halflife vibes! I would say without a doubt that this is the best all around VR title I have played yet and advances VR in multiple ways.

+ The Music is great and fits perfectly with the gameworld and lore. I will defnetly be getting the sound track!
+ The physics are great and offer so many ways to acomplish tasks or solve puzzles.
+ The full VR body and arms are tons of fun and I can see this influencing many games in the near future, It's so fun just to set up a parkour section with benches, chairs and trash cans... I know I'm a dork!
+ Gun play is wonderfull and the best I have played to date of any VR title.
+ Story and lore are the kinda thing I really like and can see this expanding and growing!
+ Performance is decent on a Ryzen 2700 & RX580 combo but I look forward to upgrading my GPU for Supersampling.
+ The body slots system is great, love it!
+ Game length and value are amazing! Thank you and I look forward to expansions of any kind.

- Although the Physics are a joy I can see room for improvement regarding grabbing things, arms and legs catching on things, plenty of room for tweaks.
- My most wanted feature is more then one player profile for height because my and my daughter are constantly having to adjust from taking turns.

Overall This is my VR Game of the year and I look forward to any updates or expansions, Thank you for a great game at a great price!\t\t\t\t\t
