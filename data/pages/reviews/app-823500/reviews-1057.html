\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks, a game about shooting guns, solving puzzles and beating the ever loving **** out of anything you see. This game has a lot of love put into it from the mechanics, the gun play, and the level design. The story isn't really that important and you'll just end up breaking all the tv's where people try and communicate you with you.

What the game is great at:
* The physics in the game make for creative puzzle solving. Being able to use a crowbar to smash through a window or actually pry open a door was always fun. Your able to interact with almost anything in the game. I once used a small gate as a bridge to cross a platform I was stuck at which can be frustrating but feels so rewarding once you finally cross that gap.
* Simple controls for players to understand IE: A well organized weapon inventory system. Being able to see all my weapons in my menu is great for quick access when in a fire fight. No need to remember where you put that one gun you love. Being able to slow time and force grab objects is so satisfying especially when throwing a blade into an enemy and force grabbing back to your hand.
* Never feeling as if your just doing the same thing over again with new levels and different puzzles to solve as well as finding all sorts of neat goodies laying around hidden through out the level. This makes exploring the games maps fun and rewarding.
* The Soundtrack in this game is by far some of the best I have ever heard. You feel like an absolute badass in combat no matter what your doing. Even if your just wandering around no where there usually is some beautiful melodies following you which really makes the player (at least me) want to stay around and play more.
* The Visuals: This game is BEAUTIFUL. The models on enemy's, guns, and other objects make you feel like your actually there. You get so immersed into the game with all the well made models that it just makes the experience ten times better.

What the game is not so great at:
* After you complete the story there isn't that much else to do besides the arena where you face off against waves of enemy's.
* Getting another magazine from your hip while crouching can be a pain in the ass. This is especially true if your getting shot at and need to find cover to reload. Your better of just swapping to another weapon when empty.
* Dieing in this game can be pretty frustrating especially in arena. When you are dieing you will hear your character say "I'm dieing" follow upped with your eyelids starting to close which is a clear indication that yes you ARE dieing. If your able to score a kill or two you will get out of this effect for a short time. In story mode when you die you must start all the way back at the start of a level. The only checkpoints you get are when you beat the area you are in to move onto the next one so death can be very frustrating to deal with when it happens.
* Lack of guns. Don't get me wrong I love the gunplay in this game but however there is not that many weapons to choose from. The only weapons are Rifles (2), pistols (2), and SMG's (2) (were not counting the high tech weapons that cost 30,000 or something bullets because that's just outrages.) It does kinda make up for it by allowing ALL blunt objects (including guns) to do melee damage but the small variety of guns is disappointing non-the-less.
* Telling a story. Holy **** are the other "characters" in this story are annoying as ****. The pop up on TV's talking about your in danger and trying to pull the cliche "It's just a simulation" (which it is) but I disliked all the people that showed up on the TV's, especially the lady with glasses that just gives you this disapproving look at a certain point in the game. I feel no emotional attachment which is fine for a game to not have but this game try's by making you talk to your TV buddies. The only thing I knew is that I had a gun and things needed to die which is honestly ok for me but in my opinion the story could of been much better because even after beating the game I have and still don't know what the **** the game was about.

Gameplay: 10/10 Seriously. I was having an absolute blast running and gunning with no restrictions.

Graphics: 10/10 This game has top notch model quality and makes every model in the game as beautiful as can be.

Controls: 9/10 Controls are easy to use and learn for anyone. The only issue I see is attempting to reload while crouched but overall nice work.

Story: 3/10. This game is not for the story, but that doesn't mean it's bad. If your looking for in-depth story telling and character development this game might not be for you. However all the other redeeming factors really make up for the games lack of story.
Replayability: 8/10 This game is so much fun. I would gladly replay the game over again because to be honest I don't want it to end. Some people may argue that there is no point in playing the story again but I must disagree. All the gun play and puzzles make this adventure so much fun and is definitely worth another play through

Overall I love this game and it honestly is one of the most fun adventure/shooter games I have played. It has it's flaws but what game doesn't? Sure the story is nothing to look at however there is so much more good in this game than bad. The gunplay is fun, puzzles are challenging, and the creativeness of the map design makes exploring worth it. Other developers should look back at this game and realize that even if the story isn't really there the experience can still be a lot of fun because that's what games are supposed to be, fun.\t\t\t\t\t
