\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is now one of the best "story" based single player games available for VR. I was skeptical to say this at release.

At release it was a bit janky, the physics were very springy, inaccurate and frustrating, and there were no checkpoints which led to the experience other than killing enemies with a gun to be more frustrating and more work than it was fun.

Now however, they have done significant improvements, particularly to the physics in game which made me replay the entire game and want to keep playing and get all the secrets. The first time I went through the game, i just wanted to get it done, because the secrets made you want to tear your hair out they were so frustrating. Some of the boxes are still this way to be honest, but it is actually plausible to get them now with the updates. I was originally left with a bit of a biter taste and wasn't ever going to revisit Boneworks. Now, with these improvements, I can't wait to get back in. I just wish there was more content. Seeing mod tools that would allow custom levels and multiplayer or even full blown story overhauls would make this game absolutely next level and I hope this becomes a reality.

TL;DR:

This game is worth buying, and it has improved greatly since it's launch.\t\t\t\t\t
