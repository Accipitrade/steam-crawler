\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great, yet flawed game. This is by far the most advanced VR game gameplay wise but the story is very weak. However, the story is not really the most important aspect for a game like this. Puzzles can range from decent to unpleasant. Melee combat and gunplay is very fun, though I do wish that the pistols can be a bit more accurate. I do not recommend this for people who experience motion sickness very easily as this game sacrifices comfort for these gameplay mechanics. I also heard that some people have issues where their in-game body glitches with in-game objects or parts of the map/level. This isn't a huge issue to me even though I do experience this time to time but it can be very bothersome to some people so keep that in mind. Overall, a very interesting and fun game but with very notable flaws. Still worth playing if you have VR and you can stomach a game like this. If you can play Half-Life: Alyx or some other VR game with the continuous motion option, then you are likely able to handle this game.\t\t\t\t\t
