\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest VR game by far. In my opinion, is much better than Half Life: Alex.
The gameplay is amazing. You can literally climb on basically anything. Anything you'd expect to be able to do in real life you can do in this game and more. For example, there were some pipes that I saw on a wall on the second stage of the game. I spent, say, 10 minutes climbing all over these pipes just because I could. Sure, my tracking was slightly buggy and glitchy at some points because my headset. The Samsung Odessey+ doesn't have upper cameras. Any issues I had with the game were because of my headset rather than the developers.
The soundtrack is awesome, I seriously love it. It instills a good atmosphere to the situation you are in, it kinda gave me goosebumps combined with some of the areas in the game.
The graphics aren't going to give any awards, but they work well enough, but it has a distinctive art style and it accomplishes the goal the developers had by far. It's hard to make really good looking VR games right now, because the PC hardware simply isn't there.
The only disadvantage to this game is definitely the fact that it kinda ruins a lot of other VR games for you. It's so advanced and well done that every other game feels floaty, weightless or very static and lifeless.

TL;DR- This is a must play game, and exemplifies everything VR is good for. Whether it's amazing physics, great level design, a great story, or even fun combat, this game has them all. Buy the game, and support the developers. Because this was and is easily the most ambitious and crazy VR game to be released.\t\t\t\t\t
