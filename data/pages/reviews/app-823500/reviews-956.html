\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm only a couple hours in, but this game does a pretty good job of being atmospheric, challenging, and fun. My biggest issue is a type of motion sickness that I've never experienced in other VR games before. I've long gotten used to the continuous movement style, so that isn't it, but this game, way more than others, has an issue of my hands/arms not matching my IRL body, and it really turns my stomach after 30 minutes or so. I mostly notice it when picking up an object that the game has decided is "heavy." You try to interact with it, and your in game arms refuse to move above a certain height or certain speed regardless of what your meatspace body is doing. It's hard to describe, but you'll know it when you see it. There are also some small controller inconsistencies with the Rift Touch controllers where some grabbing actions require just the trigger finger button and others require the trigger and grip buttons. You do get used to it, however, and it isn't that big of an issue. Also, the increased inventory space over most other games allows you to carry like six different guns, which I liked.
Finally, the highest praise I can offer this game is that it honestly gives you a vibe that makes you feel like it takes place in the Half-Life/Portal universe. Something about the quirkiness and atmosphere feels very Valve-esque, and I'm nothing but impressed that they pulled it off so well.\t\t\t\t\t

Pros:
The physics, these physics are so realistic after playing the game I felt the urge to actually throw random objects in my house..

The hidden story and lore, this game reminds me of fnaf where the story isn't up front in your face, it takes a lot time and exploring to find the story and there's still a lot we don't know!

Arena mode, this part of the game HAS TO BE MY FAVORITE, I love starting little wars between FRIENDLY_FORD's and ENEMY_FORD's.

Updates, I love how the game updates like the new one coming on April 9th

Cons:

Glitches, I get quite a bit of glitches where my arms get stuck in objects all the time! it's only kinda annoying because most of the time it's easy to get them out.

Lag, So usually the game runs at a nice 60 frames a second but when I hit the steam menu button, HOLY CRAP, The game starts to VIOLENTLY lag running at 3 frames a sec and sometimes shuts down my entire Oculus rift S, it even changes my entire boundry settings and breaks my tracking causing me to unplug my VR headset and having to log out.

Dizziness, this game makes people sick and dizzy but me personally I never felt it, but i still think it should be called out.

This game is Fantastic and I can't wait to see what Stress Level Zero does with this game, even with some problems I still fully recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
