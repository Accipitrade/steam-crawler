\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think I'm just about close to the end of the game and this last crash was my final straw. It just crashes every hour and I cannot figure out the issue. I lower the gfx settings, close all other programs, raise VM as they mentioned. But this is the ONLY game that causes my PC to crash. Beat HL: Alyx and use some other VR apps that simulate physics in an extreme way like this game. Nothing seems to cause the same crashing.

I was having a lot of fun with the game and then made it to the final level. No new guns or weapons, no new enemies, a pain in the ass looking puzzle. The game has 4 enemies. Zombies (in 3 colors), Combine riflemen (1 type) and head crabs. That's it. There are a good amount of weapons, which is cool. But there are games with WAY more. The physics play is fun, but other games do this, (or at least something similar) without being as taxing on my PC. For example, Zero Caliber has great gun play, has the ability to swap stuff on the weapons (almost all attachments work on all guns which is amazing) and has climbing and a full player model.

A lot of the Parkour in this game is frustrating AF and while it's certainly fun to be able to figure certain ways to solve a puzzle, the climbing can be frustrating (though it's still very cool) the jumping puzzles are just tedious.

I think the game is pretty neat. However, I've had just as much time being frustrated with it as had fun. And in the end I just gave up cause it lost it's charm toward the last puzzle (I guess it was) and I just can't imagine playing the sandbox in this if I have to go through all the levels over and over to unlock everything. I'd rather just play gmod or another game.

For a VR based physics sandbox, I think I prefer gmod with the VR mod. Just so much more to do in that, plus it's multiplayer. It might not be designed for VR, but the VR mod allows for motion controls and everything.\t\t\t\t\t
