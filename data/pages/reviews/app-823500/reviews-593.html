\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI cannot recommend this game enough.

- Outstanding physics and interactability that, in my eyes, is a great example of the standard that the VR industry should be known for
- Story is lackluster, but the varying amount of environments that you progress throughout the plot makes up for it
- Gunplay is just... Wow.
- You are rewarded handsomely for exploration, with any (hidden, or otherwise) items you bring to the end of the level becoming unlocked for use in the "sandbox" mode.
- Only drawback is that this game, if you are prone to any level of nausea whilst in vr, is probably not going to be very fun for you - I personally have not experienced anything of the sort myself, but a good majority of people who I showed the game off to had issues to varying degrees.

Now while it probably isn't what the quality of game the VR industry really needs to take off, its pretty damn close. If you're the kind of person who enjoys to mess around with physics-objects and prefer titles that just exude interactability, this is it! :P\t\t\t\t\t
