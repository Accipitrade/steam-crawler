I wanted to like this game, but the jank is real. Unreliable holstering, unreliable reloading (especially for the SMGs, tends to grab the mag instead of the charge handle), unreliable ladder dismounting at the top, and sub-par melee for a physics-based game. It definitely doesn't help that you feel like your playing as the weakest person in existence; kind of lends itself to the usual nightmare feeling of punching with all your might, only for hits to feel like they have no weight.
