üçí Additional personal opinion
This game was simply amazing to play, it's definitely up there in my top VR-games, however the tutorial part is actually the worst, it feels slow and a bit overkill in terms of learning. They should've spaced it out within the game instead of shoving everything at you right away, takes awhile until you get into actual gameplay/combat. And if you would replay the game it feels like a chore to do it all over again.

With that said, the physics are awesome, it's fun to take cover and blindfire when needed, the melee weapons feel responsive, however the two handed weapons feel a bit too sluggish, but one handed is great.

If you're used to VR-movement I highly recommend you give this game a chance, because it's actually quite fun!\t\t\t\t\t

5/5

Every system has it's "Staple" game, the game that makes people buy the console, that people will play generations later for nostalgia. That's this game for VR if you have friends that don't have VR, have them play this and they'll go out and buy the Index. This is the HL3 we all want HL3 to be.

Edit: I'm not docking a point for this since it was probably the best game I've played for VR so far. That being said, the ending of the game feels a little rushed, and the last level gives a very insignificant feeling, like nothing actually happened.

I feel like they could have done much more with the levels of this game. For example it would have been great if they added a few with different "VR" Themed levels like Wild West, Steam Punk, a few more Fantasy levels, maybe even played with a farm-like level where you're only given farm tools as weapons.

To that point, they could have played with the weapons much more. I know I saw a shotgun in the previews that was absent in the game. As well as they could have added in a revolver, a sniper, and had levels that co-related to that.

Overall the experience of this game is as I said in the original review - the Half-Life of VR. The game you show people, and it makes them go out and buy a VR set just because of how awesome it is. This is a Must have - Especially if you have the Knuckles.
