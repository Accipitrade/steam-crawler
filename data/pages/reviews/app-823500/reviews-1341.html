The firearms also have a nice feel to them, you can easily reload a gun, just placing the mag near the mag well automatically ejects the mag for you so you don't waste time pressing an extra button, which just makes it feel a lot smoother (but you can pull the mag out manually if you want to). The puzzles are just difficult enough so you have to give each one thought, but not enough so you're stuck on it. It does have a few bugs here and there but it just launched so that's to be expected. Boneworks has some aspects based from half life and it shows. If you are a fan of half life or just a fan of realistic physics in vr, you should definitely get this.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Pros:
- Awesome physics to play around with.
- A cool story mode with fun levels.
- Good story.
- Great soundtrack.
- Fun gun play and melee combat.

The Cons:
- Motion sickness. This is the one game it really kicks in on; I can only play this game for about 30 minutes before wanting to throw up.
- No saving. The levels can be very long, and combined with motion sickness it's very difficult to get through a 1 hour long level. Saving would be a god send.

Overall, I think it's a great game. It really shows off what the future of VR holds with the new physics that are unseen in any other VR game. My only problems with it are how easy it is to get sick in this game, and how there currently is no saving system. But other than that, it's a great game.\t\t\t\t\t
