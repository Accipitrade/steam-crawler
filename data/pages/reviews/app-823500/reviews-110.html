\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow is this a jankfest. The physics are bonkers. I truly do not understand how anyone enjoys this. There is barely any story whatsoever. It's a lifeless void with a few janky toys.

The melee is a complete and utter joke. Attacking with the Crowbar feels like hitting something with a balloon. Guns are meh. Pavlov nailed it.

Climbing is ridiculous, your own body is your largest obstacle. Moving in general feels like a chore. You get caught on everything and a simple touch will either send you or the object flying around.

One of the worst games I have ever played.
