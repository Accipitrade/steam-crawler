\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely the highest quality VR game I've played, although I haven't played some of the biggest budget ones yet (kinda working my way up). Don't start your VR experience here, you need to build up some tolerance on some easier games. The climbing and free moving/'falling will trigger some serious VR sickness if you don't have any tolerance yet. The campaign is short: 8-12. if you are a completionist you will have fun time hunting some of the collectibles and finding all the ammo hidden everywhere, they really reward exploration and attentiveness. Overall I'd say pretty fantastic and insanely immersive. Great pacing too, only this game dips SUPER hard after it's peak moment, which is the 3rd to last mission. I thought both mechanically and plot-wise, everything went downhill hard after that, culminating with an ending that is intentionally nonsense. The developer has a habbit for hiding the actual plot behind secrets, but I don't have patience or care enough for that. The zombie arena is also a fantastic experience. For the price, it's a no brainer, you've gotta check it out. The feeling of climbing and handling weapons is just so awesome.\t\t\t\t\t
