\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen it comes to VR games. Boneworks is probably the best in terms of physics as well as combat fighting to which it feels great at times. This games also does well for tracking the human body with height and proportions towards buildings and structures as well as maintaining what feels like a actual place to which you can imagine being stuck in with. However this game has some issues and these were the biggest issue for my experience.

One of the biggest issues that many people who also reviewed this game with both positive and negative reviews was that each checkpoint throughout the game are way too far to get to and there isn't a save option. This is an issue for a lot of people as what can be 2 hours worth of getting to one place to another can be lost the second they close out of the game. in addition the time it takes to get there can be too long for some users if they only have a short amount of time to use the computer making it more challenging for those who want to complete the game.
While on the topic of time. The time period in which a person can play this game for is the second biggest issue. Many people (including me) have said that most were only able to wear their headset for 1 hour to 1 hour in a half before feeling nauseous and in some cases throwing up. From my experience, Every checkpoint that I got to I had to take a half hour break before I can put the headset back on as I would feel nauseous towards the end. Theres something in this game Vs all other VR games that I've played that makes you feel nauseous. every other game played, I didn't have this issue.
The third issue is that some points in the game I found to be nearly impossible to complete in order to progress foward
