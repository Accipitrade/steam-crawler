\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is truly the definitive VR experience and one of the best sandbox/action-adventure games I've ever played.
Set in a physics-based deliberately crafted, uncanny computer simulation world you're left to your own devices to piece together your own story using the small clues scattered throughout the environment in the mysterious MythOS simulated world.

Each playthrough of the game's campaign and other missions is never the same as you learn more about the physics mechanics of the game that have a seemingly endless ceiling to master and have fun with as you play. You learn what you can climb and grab and what you can't, what you can move and how you can shoot, and how to use all of this to your advantage when exploring the world; Particularly vertically, where the game makes the most use of its space.

Explore the world thoroughly and uncover any secrets that may aid you on your journey. Look in every filing cabinet, trash bin, desk, and on top of every shelf or seemingly out of reach ledge that you can find and collect enough ammo and guns to use for filling the bad guys with holes. Collect enough ammo however, and you may be able to get some items that will help you greatly in your future playthroughs of the story and further amplify your sandbox experience.

Gather collectibles from the campaign and unlockable missions in the form of almost anything you can imagine from guns, to melee weapons, to objects in order to further enhance the sandbox portion of the game where you're free to create or destroy to your heart's content.

If you have your VR legs and a decent computer, there is literally no reason to not play this game. It is an immersive and mastercrafted experience waiting to be unraveled that has endless replayability.\t\t\t\t\t
