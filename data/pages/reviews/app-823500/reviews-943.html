\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo start things off BONEWORKS is one of the top 3 games currently out in VR in my opinion. That being said its not a perfect game.

The gun play and some of the physics are amazing and really push VR to new limits. But there's some things that really set it back. The main 2 things that got me were the melee combat and the legs.

With the melee combat what confuses me is that for a physics based game the melee combat feel so weightless. Punching a enemy or a object feels more like im tapping them lightly. Makes for really lame melee combat scenes. Wish they did more of a blade and sorcery style.

The other thing that drives me crazy is the legs. Climbing is hard enough in this game with your elbows fighting against you but the real issue for em was the legs. In my entire time playing I did not once pull myself up over something. Every time you need to pull yourself up over something even if you can push you legs stay in the way and dont allow you to climb up.

All that aside its a good experience I recommend to anyone with VR.

7/10\t\t\t\t\t
