\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere's a lot to like about this game. The gunplay is fun, and I can appreciate what they're trying to do. However, there's just way too much reliance on platforming puzzles to see everything on offer. Sandboxes are locked and you have to find them hidden in the massive levels, so you really have to play through it to see everything, and I just can't do it, physically.

I find platforming in 1st person games to be generally pretty awful with the exception of games that give you mantling. With Boneworks, the platforming is janky and vomit-inducing for me. Combined with a non existent save system, it makes for a pretty bad experience.

I was not able to stick with this game long enough to experience all it has to offer. I couldn't even bring myself to get past the first level with multiple tries. It just leaves me feeling too nauseated with lasting effect even after leaving VR. Not worth it.

I played through all of HL Alyx and never experienced this level of VR sickness. Boneworks broke my brain.\t\t\t\t\t
