\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere do i begin, This game is an absolute masterpiece and will forever shape vr and will be a benchmark for the quality of all vr games to come.
BONEWORKS is a narrative, vr physics game with its primary focus to make the vr world feel like the real world - and they did. Everything can be touched, moved, thrown as if you did in the real world. The team also put in effort for a weight system, for instance a sledge hammer is very hard to throw and move where as a kunai is easy.
The physics is obviously why this game is so good. But is also why the game is sometimes frustrating, because everything is physics based sometimes its so hard to do a simple task and not to mention how many complaints their are about motion sickness in this game (i personally dont feel motion sickness in vr so this wasnt a problem for me!)

To conclude i feel this game is a must buy for anyone with a vr headset, however the game at the moment is quite laggy in some open areas so if you have around minimum specs id hold off for a little while.

9/10 - Amazing game but needs some optimisation\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game, great physics, great design. I can (and have) played the same mission over and over again.

Why did I do that? Well, my VR headset is prone to blackouts, especially in this game. As in, the screen completely goes black, the device crashes, I have to restart. And, well, I'm not trying to speedrun the game. I want to actually enjoy it. So, after over four hours in the first real level (called Streets), I finally finished it. This is over a 4-day period of trying to complete it before my headset crashes. Thankfully, this time, my headset crashed about 2 minutes into the level afterwards, so I was able to completely get through Streets.

So here's my feedback, and what I ask of the devs: Please, please, please, could you fix the saving? Or, at the very least, allow us to decide whether we want continuous/prompted saving or at the end of levels. I doubt I'll ever get to play in the arena, and that's one of the main reasons I bought this game.\t\t\t\t\t
