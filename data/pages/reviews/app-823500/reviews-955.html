\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoneworks in my opinion, is probably one of the best VR games out there. I wanted to wait until I had finished the game before I made a review and wow, it did not disappoint me! The whole experience from start to finish was a blast. I feel like one of my favorite parts which really shows what I love about the game, was this one point where there was a wall and you had to find a way over it. Instead of doing a puzzle or whatever was necessary, I pulled out my axe and pulled myself right over it. The way that puzzles are done and how you do them is left upto you as the player for the most part, and I love that so much!

There were problems I had with the game. Sometimes my body would get in the way and it'd be hard to climb in certain ways. I don't know if I had missed it or if it just wasn't there at all, but at no point during the tutorial was I told how to pull myself over edges. So for the first few hours I had to struggle with learning how to pull myself over things. After I did find out though, pulling myself over objects became very fun and satisfying. Another issue was that there was no hand-holding whatsoever besides some hints from the environment itself (graffiti, signs, ect) I'm just a bit slow so in these large open areas I would get lost/confused on where to go next, but I can mostly chalk this upto my own incompetence.

All in all, I wholeheartedly suggest this game. The whole story experience was amazing and I was completely immersed in the universe and what was going on. I love this game and if you choose to buy this game, I hope you do too.
