\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has potential, but the "physics" are a glitchy mess. For reference, I play on Valve Index.

- Weight of objects is weird. You struggle with a hammer, but you can drag huge boxes and objects around.
- A lot of large objects feel like they are made of paper, bouncing around, falling over and hard to place.
- Carrying things is hell, because the character loses 2 handed grip randomly all the time. Tried carrying planks at altitude for a puzzle and the stuff just kept falling down due to grip issues, so I had to keep taking it back up in an elevator (which made them bounce out half the time due to the glitch above). This is not a controller issue, never lost 1 handed weapons from either hand.
- Climbing is a horrible experience and you will need it a lot. It is very slow and spazzy, it doesn't feel smooth and for whatever reason you can't properly pull yourself up on objects, so even when you finish your climb, the character either won't grip the ledge or won't pull himself up so you just fall anyway.
- Speaking of falling, you fall through the weirdest, smallest gaps that wouldn't fit a body.
- Objects have pre-defined grip points. This isn't a physics sandbox, you will grab it where you are told. If you try grab a handle lower, your hand will snap higher where it is meant to be. This stops you from pushing out of reach buttons etc. because objects are shorter due to forced restrictions.
- For whatever reason, they decided that you need to define your personal height, floor height and whether you sit/stand every single time you run the game, as if those things are going to change from day to day. Just create a player profile and save it like every other game does?
- Crouching in this game isn't a toggle, it is progressive. You can do it in reality or with a button. The problem is your character randomly crouches half the time you try to lift or drag stuff and gets stuck in that position, which is annoying because it limits your movement and you have to keep fixing it.

I want to play the game. I don't want to spend the majority of my time fighting the controls and screwed up physics.\t\t\t\t\t
