\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL/DR: Amazing game, do da buy


GAMEPLAY: Unlike Alyx you have a full physics body, allowing you do do such things as: headbutt a headcrab to death (how ya like me now), elbow people in the face, get stuck on simple object you should be able to easily mantle, feel incredibly immersed, feel incredibly unimmersed, Get confused as to whether your legs are moving in the game or in real life, etc, etc. This along with an incredilbe physics engine makes the game feel surreal. The enemies are fun and terrifying at first, the guns feel amazing (far better than any other VR game ive played), and the gameplay is smooth and fleshed out (tho a little buggy at times, nothing to horrible though).

STORY: In order to truly understand the story you either have to be paying a ton of attention, or watch Game Theory. Either one works, but if youre really only in this for the story. Play Half Life Alyx, youll like that better.

MODS: On top of all the pros with this game. once you have finished it, there is a very active modding community on bonetome.com and in their discord. most of the mods are amazingly high quality and if you arent sure which mods to use watch xDBluCZxD. he has a lot of good mod showcases.

BOTTOM LINE: Truly the most innovative VR game to date. Personally i would say i enjoyed it more than Half-Life Alyx, but that's just my opinion.

FINAL WARNING: this game is very intense for those with motion or VR sickness. I've been told you can get over this by playing in small sessions until you are used to it. However, i have no personal experience and cannot verify. Do some more research if you're concerned.

happy gaming, go kill some headcrabs for me (tho little suckers are scary as heck)\t\t\t\t\t
