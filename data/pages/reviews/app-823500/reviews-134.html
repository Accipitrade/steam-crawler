\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm fighting the controls every step of the way.
It is a constant struggle to utilize the positional inventory. It often takes multiple attempts to place weapons in the lower back slot, and too often do I grab ammo when trying for the left breast weapon.
Half-gripped weapons don't respect gravity, only physics, so when I retrieve a melee weapon from inventory and the default handling is way near the striking end, for some reason, I have to awkwardly pull it into the correct handling manually.
Mantling ledges is awful and inconsistent, full stop.
The double tap to run system is awkward and inconsistent, especially when L3 is sitting there unused.
Save system is not frequent enough for someone who gets VR sickness readily.
Recycling system is poorly explained or infrequent? I've found several things intended to be recycled, but the only explicit deposit for them is much much farther in the level, which would be incredibly tedious to relay them to; if there is another solution, it was not made apparent.

These are just some of my present issues, not to paint my opinion as exclusively negative. The game has merit, even just by having actual content; I am just disappointed that such a well-received game is equally if not more jank than Blade & Sorcery.\t\t\t\t\t
