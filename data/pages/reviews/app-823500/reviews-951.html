\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, this is the best VR-Game out there right now.

Some are asking themselves if they should get a VR-Headset for the new Half-Life. I think you should get one right now and play Boneworks. This game is worth it.
I am also currently playing HL Alyx but Boneworks...is just better.
Sure, many people will disagree with me on that point, but just from the feeling that I got, playing Boneworks as my first VR-Game, I would recommend playing Boneworks first.
The overall feeling of controlling an actual body was just much better in Boneworks. You can see a body when you look down, no bloody teleporting, touching things and handling a melee weapon (like a sword or knife), not having a wall when you but your head into a hitbox, the gunplay. These things just feel more real in Boneworks.
Of course the unbelieveble landscape that HL created cannot be compared to any other VR-Game (incredible visuals), but Boneworks is just better as a physical experience. You will sweat and smash bodies with a huge sword and it will fell just more real.
This is the future of VR-Gaming and I love it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Stress Level Zero" is a hilarious irony to the gameplay within Boneworks.
If you're intimidated by weird surrealist figures hobbling their way to kick your ass, you're going to be stressed while playing this; which isn't a bad thing.
9/10.

This game combines surrealism meme culture and great combat, and interesting physics-based puzzles to achieve a great experience. If you're confused about surreal memes, check out r/surrealmemes.

you're dropped into a virtual world (within a virtual world?) and make your way, killing N U L L M A N to pass each level, combined with climbing and parkour-ing over obstacles. The nullman are quite creepy, as they shambles towards you and make weird groans, killing them is rewarding; and frankly important for your survival. This game contains all sorts of gadgets and puzzles for you to use and figure out, to get secrets and explore the terrain.

Really fun and great experience.\t\t\t\t\t
