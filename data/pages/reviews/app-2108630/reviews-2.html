\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInto the Dark is a campaign expansion for The Riftbreaker that adds a new biome, more weapon/tower variety and a new challenge for players to progress through. When it comes to the new biome it is definitely unique much more so than the previous biomes, it will have you thinking how you are going to plan out a base, how you are going to create a path to ambush the unfortunate local fauna who come to attack your base oh yeah at the time of release they launched a free update, you can actually use the entirely new trap system to accomplish this too, just don't forget to reset all your traps after they are destroyed.

You may want to take some time craft some weapons in the base game first, maybe explore 1 or 2 other biomes get some additional resources and have at least blue tier weapons, you are going to need them.

My recommendation is to buy this considering how different this biome was to how I expected, with a game like this you definitely what the new biomes to all have unique aspects to them, oh and if you want the game to grow even further this is made by a small independent team based in Poland and they keep delivering some awesome content, buy the DLC.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot worth it. I got bugs like other negative reviews, but that doesn't impact my impression. It's just not worth it.

Like the other DLC, this just adds a different map type available from the orbital scanner. It doesn't change the game, it doesn't add much, it's just another tileset to play through. You know exactly how this will play out. First map you sense some stuff. Then another map you refocus your orbital scanner. Then the third map you face the big bad. Then that's it, you're back to the main mission with nothing new added. This is maybe 1-2 hours of additional gameplay that feels like all the other gameplay.

I don't like the design direction of these DLC's. It's short and unnecessary. I love the base game, I just wish the DLC's augmented it or extended it in a totally different way. If I could see the future/reverse time, I'd want my $10 back.\t\t\t\t\t
