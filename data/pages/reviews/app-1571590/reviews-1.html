OH YEA THEY GET SNIPER SQUADS/INDIV TOO

its lacking very much in its tank area, but you definetly more than battlegroup toulon, anti air is a joke, makes up for it with a lot of planes, spitfire fighter-bombers, mauraders and hellcat HE/SAP rockets, which might compensate for your lack of anti tank (you have M10s and 57mms, and bazookas too), i dont use recon too often, but they have light tanks and some recon squads

overall for its price, you get a unit that excels in the defensive with powerful infantry/aa and a versatile battlegroup good for dealing against other infantry battlegroups with good air power, think its quite worth it, though im quite sad that theres no toulon map :(\t\t\t\t\t

Not my cup of Tea
As always You get what you read
So if you think you might LIKE what you see... be my guest alright? This is a personal opinion on the two new divisions.

Verteidigungsbereich Toulon
The German division is rather boring and leans so heavily on defense that you almost need to play a 2v2 just to get some sort of mileage out of it. You got great AA and good Infantry with some AT but thats about it. The artillery, Recon and Tank tab lack in Fun and useful Items and the Air Tab is mostly fighter bombers with decent-ish payloads. All in all it is the better of the two divisions although the Flakzwilling is quite underwhelming for its lack of availability.
What I do have to mention is the terrible voice acting
The italian troops seem mostly alright with decently picked voice actors but the Kriegsmarine Infantry has some of the most boring, lowercase acting I have ever witnessed. This wouldn't be too bad if it were ALL NEW voice lines but many of them are old voice lines done in the style of the new voice actor. Where the old one would scream "ICH WILL NACH HAUSE LASS MICH GEHEN ;-;" the new one would simply state that "ich will nach hause lass mich gehen :)" which is going from screaming about how ♥♥♥♥♥♥ your situation is to a simple lowercase statement. All in all, the voice acting makes me sad.

1e Division Française Libre
The French got no good tanks. no good AA. No good AT. You could make a case for the recon and the infantry but it is mostly just ok with not being able to carry the division at its best. Where the French really suck is in the fighter department. While I do enjoy the bombers, the fighters are so horrendous, especially the 125 point Kobra knock off which LITERALLY yanks in the air, unable to decide if either going left or right is the better choice, coupled with low damage output and lack of speed, it cant kill anything above 30 points. I do believe this plane is either bugged or cursed but I don't got the time to micromanage that ONE plane.

So, in conclusion: I dislike the new Divisions. They are neither competitive nor fun to play and don't offer enough uniqueness or variety to be a "once in a blue moon division". If you enjoy sitting still for the entire match, with barely any movement play Toulon and if you enjoy being french, then play the Division Libre. I love what Eugen puts into its divisions but these ones are a miss if you: Enjoy tanks. Enjoy Rocket Artillery. SDKFZ or similar Recon and a strong airforce.\t\t\t\t\t
