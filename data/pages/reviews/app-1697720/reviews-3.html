\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game that does a lot that no other games do, there is so much tragedy and nuance to this deceptively ocean deep story, hitting very close to home for many.

The game faces you with a horror countless people face in real life, it doesn't rip you miles away from civilisation, or put you in an alternate nightmare world separated from your own with aliens or demons, instead the horror comes from being under the control of real people who aren't in control of themselves. Evil shows its tragic face through laziness, fear, and complacency, caretakers abandoning their oath for one reason or another.

I expected to fall in love with the game and its aesthetics and tone, with dark, slow, brooding waltz and oompah on the soundtrack throbbing in the grimiest heart of 20th century Germany, I didn't expect it to make me cry.

A solid 10/10, unique and fantastic visuals, outstanding sound design and music, and the gameplay is well varied, well paced, and very cleverly set up to teach the player the entire way through, a truly cohesive and hard hitting experience with a staggering depth for few hours of playtime and a borderline free pricetag.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow

That was a mindfu{k.

What started off as very frustrating experience blossomed into a fractal rose, with each "loop" exposing a deeper and more complex game than first thought.

When I began, the game wasted no time into immediately putting on the pressure. Dont be ashamed if you look for a walkthrough every now and then. Im not very good at puzzles and was able to complete pretty much everything except a couple, so the puzzles Id say are benign and kinda fun. Time and space seem to be fluid in this game and there is a story to be told, but the game isnt going to hand it to you.

Technically, the game runs amazing. Of course it should because of the graphics, but the game is extremely tight and polished. Never hit a glitch or a bug. The atmosphere created with such limited visual fidelity is truly a marvel. The setting doesnt ever get boring because before long youre in a new area with its own gimmick.

I cant say too much without giving spoilers, but this was to me more of a cosmic horror. Definitely unsettling. Definitely gave me something to ponder. The type of game that you think about for days afterwards.

8/10\t\t\t\t\t
