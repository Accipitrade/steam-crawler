\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very fun indie horror game. Its around 3 hours long (if you don't try to get any extra content). I tried going for some achievements which can add some time. The stealth sections aren't too hard (which isn't a bad thing, just something to note).

The environment itself and the characters/enemies are the best part of this game in addition to the fact that it very rarely relies on jump scares. The enemy in the library is by far my favourite.

I did feel like some content like the cafeteria was a bit hard to find and I only managed to get to it by following a tutorial after I beat the game. It also kinda messes up a section later in the game as I didn't meet the enemy in that section until after I beat the game although this issue is extremely minor as the game is still fully beatable even if you miss that section.

Overall a very fun horror game. I am very glad I gave it a go.\t\t\t\t\t
