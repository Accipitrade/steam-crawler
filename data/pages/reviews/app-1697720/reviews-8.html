\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I'm going to recommend this game. It's very creepy and unnerving, and there's a lot of crazy surreal stuff going on that gave me no small number of Lost in Vivo vibes. If you like pixel shaders, if you like surreal and tense, if you want to explore a interesting game using time loops, I can recommend this whole heartedly.

It does need a little bit of work, in my opinion. Keeping track of certain elements and discovering the multitude of paths in the game does take a little bit of tedious back-and-forth work. I would highly recommend you play the game with the "hints" active that tell you when something is interactable, otherwise you'll spend a lot of time clicking on thin air.

Overall though, great experience and I hope the developer keeps making games. They have some good skills and good ideas of how to make a creepy environment. It gave me minor Lost in Vivo vibes, and if you enjoyed that, you will likely enjoy this too.
