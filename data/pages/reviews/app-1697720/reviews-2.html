\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a little gem this is. I don't understand how it isn't more popular. While it has a few cons which I will get to it has so many pros.
This was my first horror game i've played, which might be weird to some, but I do not regret it. The story is really unique and I find the end of the game worth it, though short. There was never a time that I thought that the gameplay loop was boring, with having to backtrack and all. I 100% the game at the time of writing this, and while it's a relatively short game, it didn't feel short. If you're reading this, yes, you should buy this game, and at least try it for an hour.
But a few cons: I am someone who's REALLY bad with horror games and thus I never played any except for some free games youtube etc. but at the end of the game it wasn't really... scary anymore. Which happened due to one thing (Might be spoilers? Do not think so.) Each time you get caught by a different enemy you get sent to a room with a certain lore item in it, but this means that there will never be a moment that you can fail an area or get caught if you do not think he would send you to another room to do another loop (Have I explained that correctly?) because that would be tedious and... boring. So at the end of the game in the longer rooms (and you might have all lore items already) there will never be a moment you can be truly scared. Because... you won't get caught anyways.
That was my biggest con as the game is a horror game. My last con is the bugs that sometimes happen.
In the hospital area near the beginning of the game the lady got stuck for me.
In the hospital area near the end of the game another lady got stuck for me.
And the trash collector who you give trash and he gives you something is a little buggy. If you give him trash again he goes to the same or another area and a text flashes on screen and then he immediatly starts walking away again.
But. That never really took away from my experience. And I really like this game. (Thank you Markiplier for making me find this game)\t\t\t\t\t
