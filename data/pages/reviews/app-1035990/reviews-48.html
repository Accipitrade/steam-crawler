\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe humor in this game is so spot-on and always such a joy to play. It's hit the perfect sweet spot of "absolutely chaotic and ridiculous" and "snarky sarcasm and witty comebacks." The MC is a feral cryptid that lives in a tent by the park and scuttles around Dialtown on a righteous mission - a mission that no one else can accomplish.
The music is so wonderful too, and quite catchy! I love that all the characters have their own theme songs that play when you encounter them and in a particular case, the distortion of their themes reflect their mental status, which is very clever!
The visuals are so wonderfully weird and I have no idea how, but you've managed to give literal PHONES expressions without altering the phone at all. Absolutely wonderful that you had to get creative with the body language. Also, the backgrounds are amazing.
There are some text that gets cut off, but I haven't personally ran into any bugs or anything like that at this time of this review.
I've also spent HOURS playing the demo before this game came out and it was also such a joy to play. I'm really happy that there has been improvements from the demo that made it to the final game!

Overall: A fantastically weird and hilarious game that separates itself from traditional dating sims with it's visuals and it's ridiculous/witty writing! Cannot recommend this enough!\t\t\t\t\t
