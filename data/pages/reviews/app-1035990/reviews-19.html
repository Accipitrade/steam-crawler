Holy crap man, this knocks it out of the park. Day Shift at Freddy's ain't this mans magnum opus, he can somehow magically create the most crazy crap ever, with all layers of filth, and still make a beautiful and coherent story. I loved this game. The characters, the humor, the unstoppable amounts of references(especially to DSAF, I see the purple tone of Mingus and immediately think she's gonna call me old sport).
