\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWent from making the best trilogy of fan games to bless the earth to then make another comedic and surprisingly emotional masterpiece. It may be exaggerating immensly, but these games where my late childhood. Which made me incredibly pleased to see references pop up who i recognised immediately.

Just like in the dayshift triology that DirectDogman made, each character, whether being a main or side character, oozes in their own charm. Whether it be loveable cluts Randy, the incredibly chill god or poor POOR Jerry. Spoiler: The best and funniest character is Norm (Aside from my beloved bigfoot)

And the jokes, OH THE JOKES. They are as great as ever, this developer has a great talent... no... he has a inate character trait to make people laugh their eyes out at the smallest of things, such as bolding or colouring specific sentences to increase their effect or tension in the most mundane of scenarios.

In all seriousness, this game is great. It has a great story, with deep and hilarious characters filling the wacky and somehow stable world. From humble beginnings of the dayshift at freddy's series (Linked bellow, please do play it) to now creating his own game which he can now make money on. To anyone reading this, do give this game a go, it's a lot of fun

And if DirectDogman ever sees this, thank you for all the games you have made, they have made me smile even when I was in my lowest. Please keep doing what you love, you have a real talent for it. Most of all, I hope you do well in the future.
