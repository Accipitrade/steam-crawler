\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a true masterpiece. I am irrevocably changed and will never be able to fill the gaping void in my chest left behind by these abhorrable fictional characters. I no longer know how to face the harsh reality where these characters don't exist. Thank you, Direct Doggo and team for this experience, this distraction and descent into true art and astounding writing. Karen was relatable, Oliver gave me gender envy, Randy makes me feel better that I'm (maybe?) a bit better than him, and Norm gave me renewed hope in the future of space-cowboys. Beyond that I am entranced by the combination of character writing and honestly somewhat inspired that perhaps someday even someone like me could be part of making a great story to move the hearts of other fools like me (unlikely). It astounds me how the writing and setting can be both so down to earth and off the rails. You can see the love and passion throughout this project and I will eagerly await a sequel. 10/10, wish I could experience it again. Also, for any potential buyer who read this entire review, despite steam trying to say I've played for two hours at time of writing this, my playtime through the entire game was more around 8-9 hours with my fast reading and accidentally skipping dialogue. The ending achievement says "Thank you for sharing my dreams" and I hope to thank all of those involved in this project for making this wonderful, deranged. unhinged dream that I was disappointed to awake from.\t\t\t\t\t
