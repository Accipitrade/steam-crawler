\r\n\t\t\t\t\tV cute and fun. Well written, with what feels like lots of opportunity for replay. Simple and intuitive, and the around two hour length for an entire run feels good. Mostly a dialog game, and while it is quite well done, there a few places where the preview of what you might say does not have the same emotional feel as what is actually said, but it's minimal. Art is lovely, reminded me of visiting Switzerland. Some of the transitions could be a little tighter - there's a repeated pan up that is unnecessary - but overall this is a good and enjoyable game in the text/dialog/choose-your-own-adventure genre that I would recommend to anyone who enjoys Phoenix Wright, Long Live the Queen, or intrigue-based visual novels.\t\t\t\t\t
