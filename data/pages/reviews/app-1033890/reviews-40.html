\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm afraid I found this dlc mostly slow and a little dull - except for the ending to Johanka's quest which was very good and totally surprised me. But by the gods, it was a chore to get there. They have made this dlc longer than the others (there were lots of complaints of those being too short, some justified) but to do so it feels like they have just made every quest take longer to complete. Like escorting somebody from Sasau Monastery to the town at what must be the slowest walking pace possible.

The dog mechanics are interesting but I found he got in my way more often than not - just like a real dog in that sense!

If you really want to see Theresa's story of how she escaped Skalitz then this shows it, but most of her tasks are so mundane and pointless (she's recalling something that already happened, so it doesn't matter what you do as it doesn't effect the outcome) I wouldn't want to play through it a second time.

IF you get this, don't get it for Theresa's part - get it for the ending to Johanka's story which is easily the best part of this dlc. But be warned, there is some slow and steady gameplay to get there.
