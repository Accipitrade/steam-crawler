It gives background, shows what happened during events from the perspective of Theresa rather than Henry. To be honest, the reviews giving this DLC negative marks are really made by people who are clearly lazy in their mindset- and frankly probably have issues with women regardless of how they are portrayed. This DLC shows Theresa as a strong woman, without making the men around her appear enfeebled by their "manliness" or promoting some modern garbage sense of third-wave feminism. Still, she was a hero; not because she was a super killing machine ninja ultra Buffy-type ... but because she managed her fear and did what was right despite it. That is bravery. I loved this DLC and her character. This is a must have DLC, as they all are- for KCD.\t\t\t\t\t
