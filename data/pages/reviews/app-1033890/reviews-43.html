\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA story of women without Mary Sues... I congratulate Warhorse Studios for this; for the courage to do so. We all know that the studio was ostracized by all manner of soyboy journalists, who have accused this game of being racist, anti-feminine, and alt-right. Knowing this, I was really curious about what this expansion would be. The last one for this Kingdom Come (at least acourding to the dlc-map). An expansion about strong and important female characters in the game.

A "damage control"? Not really. This piece keeps the same Warhorse Studios mentality of authenticity, realism and respect to the real history. As a woman, you have physical limitations, but here is where it became interesting. The conflicts are even more challenging, more than with untrained Henry at the beginning of the main campaign. In conflicts, you have to think more strategically, use stealth, be aggressive at every opportunity you see that can result in killing your enemy. It's very funny.

This is my favorite of the 4 expansions. It is quite long comparing with the others, I spent 15 hours to finish the content and I will have to dedicate more time to search for all the achievements. It's more cinematographic, I love it, you do not have much cinematics in the other dlcs, obviously not the case of this one.

It's amazing to see the story from Theresa's point of view. It's very beautiful, heartwarming, brutal... Damn, the story is so good. I absolutely recommend this to you, even without a sale price.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all i'd like to point out that kcd is one of my favorite games, and to see this come out from warhorse is just frankly dissapointing. I've been excited for this dlc to much and it is so under delivered. You can have fun playing as theresa for i'd say 30 mins in total, the rest is cutscenes. I did like the story but it felt more like a short book not a whole dlc. The company overhyped it. When the story begins for theresa i was enthralled expecting it to be like henry's a weak and hard start to a great adventure. Not to grinding flour for the rest of her life and having that knocked out. It says i've finished woman's lot so i assume there's nothing else. If that is true this will have been the biggest waste of my time and money since skyrim on the switch. If i'm missing something i'd love to know maybe you can play as her once you've fully beaten every quest or something of the sorts. Although as it's currently portrayed i wouldn't recommend this to anyone(just the dlc, not kcd). Finally a side note, all these positive reviews came out twenty minuets after the release(not long enough to actually start woman's lot). So i assume that people just gave it positive before even playing it because they were expecting a great product(i was considering doing the same thing in all honestly). So buyer beware, i would love to change this review to a positive, but i could only see myself doing so if there were some major updates that made it live up to what they promised, the biggest dlc. From the ashes has more content and i had a better time so if you want a dlc i'd go for that because it is honest with what it is.\t\t\t\t\t

So how is it?

Well I am about 2 hours into the new questline and I have to say I am loving my time in it.

The world in Kingdom Come Deliverance is beautiful and has some great characters. Its also fun to explore because the devs gave the player a lot of agency in this beautiful world and getting to explore this world through a fresh set of eyes is just what this game needed.

IMO this is the kind of thing RPGs should offer more of and I commend the devs on a job well done for both this dlc and the base game!
