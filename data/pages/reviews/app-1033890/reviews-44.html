\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a marvelous change in tone.
I played every single dlc as they came out since the game's release. All of them could be finished in a single play session. I loved From the Ashes, was slightly underwhelmed by The Amorous Adventures, and loved Band of Bastards. But they were all SHORT. Too short, especially for the delightful stories and mechanics they delivered.
A woman's lot fills in one of the only major plot holes in the base game: Theresa. Interactions were short-lived and ended abruptly. Now we have a long, dedicated questline. TRULY long, as in more than a single playsession, all new skills to level up, game-changing mechanics (i.e your very own doggo companion), and additions to the story that we haven't seen before/stories we have seen before from "a new perspective".
As I said in my review of Band of Bastards, if you were hesitant due to the mixed reception of the previous dlcs, this will not disappoint you.\t\t\t\t\t
