\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA two part DLC.
Theresa's story sees you playing through her experience of the initial invasion. You can accidentally start it far too easily by speaking to her very early in the game. It involves trying really hard to avoid combat and far too much utterly tedious fetch-questing in the dark while running around great distances with no horse or fast-travel. The dog is both wildly irritating and utterly essential.
I managed to 100% this and while I considered it totally worth the effort in the end, I *hated it* half way through cos it felt like it would never, ever end.

Johanka's story has you performing an extended series of actually good and fun missions as Henry to help her out when she confides in having dreams about the Virgin Mary. It ties directly into several other mainline and side-quests.
I managed to pull of the Good ending, and it was honestly heartbreaking.

While Theresa's part could and should have been a lot better (or just a lot shorter), I still recommend this overall.\t\t\t\t\t

I am going to try to continue now that I've escaped A Woman's Lot, but this DLC has pretty much ruined the game for me.
