\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll over a really good DLC with a good Story. Especially compared to my last experience with band of bastards.

In the first story replaying an already known timeline with Theresa was fantastic. With her own matters and thoughts and all this little events you know from your first hour with Henry.
The later part of this story was also very thrilling. Allover the Theresa-part could have been longer.....but its ok.

Cause, on the other hand, the second part of the DLC " The Madonna of Sasau" was even greater and surprisingly story(dialogue) -rich. Though you play Henry in this part..it is still a story about a woman's lot ;-) And it's also in a historical context a good story, including the end....no spoiler here of course :-)
I played it with a Herny that did nearly everthing else....I don't know how it plays with an "early" Herny. But with the "late" Henry this was a very interesting "mission"...cause you already know many involved characters and you have your very own sin-record with you :-)

What else? Of course, the dog is also a nice add on....for Theresa required and for Henry at least usefull and funny. I also did not notice any problems with the dog...if you want to sneak somewhere...just send him back. And complains about dogs of enemies, like in other reviews, are basically complains about the base game.. it does not matter if you have the DLC or not. Honestly....before the update of the game it was often too easy to kill a bunch of enemies in a camp.


verdict:
Both parts are very emotional...very cinematic and I have to recommend this DLC.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI HATE this DLC. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it.

The biggest sin of this DLC: it doesn't tell you that you're starting it.

I set aside 4-5 hours this afternoon to pick flowers, brew potions and fight bandits in this game. I spent a night in game doing that, I go back to my Mill to rest and eat and I see Theresa on the way. I decide to talk to her and I get the option to ask what the raid was like for her. She asks if I really want to know, I say yes. I figure it's just a few lines of dialogue.

NOPE.

It's a 3-4 hour DLC that is 80% walking and talking and 20% stealth missions.

I had to stealth around when the Cumans attacked. Then I get hit with a mission to walk around in the dark to find stuff for bandages for my idiot brother. I can't find ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. The map markers are useless and I can't find what I need. I've been wandering around for half an hour trying to find 5 marigold and 2 linen for bandages and I can't find anything.

But I HAVE to complete this story if I want to continue with my MAIN STORY with my ACTUAL character.

I HATE this DLC. I only bought it as part of a whole pack that was on steam sale. But it has completely taken me from the main game and put me in a DLC scenario without asking, and one that I find unbearably boring and awful to play. And it's such a shame too, because there are genuinely good parts of the DLC (like helping a girl find a date with dice).

I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it. I hate it.

Avoid this mess if you can.

OR.

OR.

If this sounds like fun to you, then just be warned: talking to Theresa and asking her what happened before the raid will trigger this DLC. Then you'll be in for 3 hours of talking, walking and stealth missions in a character who is even weaker than he main character and gets her ass kicked even more.

If that sounds like a great time to you then go ahead and buy it. If not, steer clear. I wish I had.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had:
3 dozen batches of bandages, 5 units of drinking water and over 10 Marigold Decoctions, plus 6 Lazarus Potions... all with 100 Condition mind you.

But Theresa, in the middle of the night during a foreign invasion, had me looking for:
3 pieces of linen off of dead bodies to make bandages with, unknown amount of water from the dirt in the mines to wash wounds with and 10 marigold flowers from the field next to... you guessed it more dead people to prevent infection and help healing.

Conclusion:
Theresa rebelled against her family. Whilst her Pa and big brother were killed, she totally finished off Samuel to be free and do what she wants with her life. Damn... what a plot twist it was! 0/10 would not marry.
Master Tobias was really lucky to not have made it on her list of names. He was close though, but he was necessary to the plot hence he survived. Damned plot armor, such terrible writing! :D\t\t\t\t\t
