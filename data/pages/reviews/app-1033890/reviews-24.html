\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt the time of this review, I have only done Theresa's quest.

Gameplay: 3/10
Story: 7/10
Social: -/10
Graphic and Animation: -/10
Audio: -/10

Conclusion: This DLC is only worth it if you care to explore the side character's background and don't mind the tedious progress you'll have to do to explore their side of things. Do I recommend this DLC? Yes, just because I'm a lore nerd and I can stand the painful quest. Otherwise, go get yourself a Starbucks' beverage.

Note: This review is my personal take on the game. I'm not a certified critic (if such a thing even exists), I'm just a fellow gamer who happens to enjoy writing and evaluating my games.


Gameplay
3/10

What is at least acceptable in the base game becomes tedious and painful in this DLC. You are given 3 Saviour Schnapps which means you only have three free saves besides the checkpoint saves. Beware, you would want to save your schnapps until the last main objective after Samuel died. The quests are the typical RPG pain. Fetch this to A, go back to B, talk to C, return to A. Note that going to A from where you started takes about 2 IRL minutes, going back to B means 4 IRL minutes. Then you go to C for another 3 IRL minutes. You get my point.
Without fast travel and horse (which makes sense to the lore, but still...) most of the objectives become more of an obligation to grab that sweet achievement with hardly anything to say fun unless you don't care in which case, just focus on the main objectives.
Contrary to some complaints about how godly the NPCs are during stealth. My experience was the opposite, the Cumans were so blind and deaf I went Assassin Creed killing spree on them.

Story
7/10

An insight into Theresa's POV around the time of Skalitz's raid. It adds depth to Theresa's character and other side characters including the treacherous bastard Zbyshek (if you do all the side quests). I didn't care for the refugees before this and Theresa's impression was pale enough to not attract my effort romancing Henry, but after playing this DLC it makes me sympathize and connect with the side characters (particularly Theresa, Matthias, and Johanca), the Skalitz refugees more and hate Zbyshek even more.

Social
-/10

Nothing new, look at the base game review.

Graphics
-/10

Nothing new, look at the base game review.

Audio
-/10

Nothing new, look at the base game review.\t\t\t\t\t
