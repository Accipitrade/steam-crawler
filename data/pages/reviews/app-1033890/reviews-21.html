\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic. I really liked it. The negative reviews here are truly nonsensical and should be ignored. It seems that most of them began this DLC not realizing that it is DLC as opposed to just a short conversation/side quest, and got frustrated that they got taken out of the main game. So now they are review bombing the DLC because of their own rigidness and ignorance.

It's a very well done DLC and a very compact mirroring of Henry's experience in the main game. You start of slow, doing some chores that let you explore the greater Skalitz area. By doing these chores and exploring, you build up xp in certain skills. For example, one quest lets you accompany a woman into the forest where she picks herbs and then gets attacked. You will have a bow and arrow during this part of the game and you will then kill your attacker, raising your agility and archery skills. All this helps build up your character for when the inevitable Cuman raid on Skalitz begins.

The voice acting is great, the various quests are all fun and immerses you into medieval life.

Please ignore the negative reviews. If you like the base game you will like this DLC. The negative reviews are from people who got this game on a sale and then accidentally activated the DLC and got upset.

****EDIT:

I should have mentioned that Theresa's escape from Skalitz is only one half of the DLC. The other half focuses on the character Johanka who is experiencing visions, which she believes are sent to her by the Virgin Mary. You help her by investigating some of her visions (one revolves around a devil in a cave) which all turn out to be true prophecies. She then starts to believe that she has been chosen by the Virgin Mary to spread her message and she begins to preach to the villagers, gaining many followers but also drawing the attention of the church and the inquisition... which is not a good thing.

All in all this DLC is absolute fantastic value for money. Theresa's part is a very refreshing and almost a survival horror type experience. The second part about Johanka and her visions from Mary has great writing and interesting quests.

Please don't pay heed to the bad reviews. They fall into two categories: People who are upset about being taken out of the main game to do Theresa's DLC after asking her in the base game about how she escaped from Skalitz. This is a stupid complaint, as they can simply load a saved game to get out of the DLC and back to where they were. If they failed to save after hours of progress, well, then it's their fault for being stupid and they should not take it out on the game.

The second reason people give the DLC a bad score is because they don't like how they go from their powertrip action hero Henry game play to an immersive experience where you play as a weaker female character and they can't just Rambo their way through everything. The escape from Skalitz part of the DLC indeed has a slow build-up, but it's called world-building. The game tries to immerse you into what life would have been like for a village woman at the time.

Please don't listen to these people review bombing this DLC, it's fantastic.\t\t\t\t\t
