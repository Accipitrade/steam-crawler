But the seperate segment where you play as theresa is the WORST experience i have had in the game bar nothing. It consists soley of the worst quest design elements from every quest you have ever seen, including fetch quests, forced stealth segments and more. If you decided to start your game in hardcore mode, god help you because saving becomes a fully limited resource as you cannot sleep, and the number of shnapps you can aquire is finite, and even then you need to grind money to buy extras. After completing a series of 'go here do x' and then a stealth mission, you are put into the most frustrating thing I have ever seen. A segment where you have to sneak past a literal army of cumans - apparently there is a specific path you are supposed to take but the game does nothing to signal this. Further due to the AI's random decision making, cumans can decide to wander onto this path leading to effectively an unwinnable situation. And of course, you're facing numerous groups of multiple cumans while armed with a bow and a shortsword. You can't even stealth kill many of them due to the strength disparity, and your dog will sit there watching them stab you to death. It can take 3-4 bow headshots to kill them through their helmets and about two minutes of dueling to stab one to death with a sword. And if you die, you do it all over again.\t\t\t\t\t

1. It's poorly made: missions are totally linear, so in order to ensure you don't leave off wandering into the open world, they just built invisible walls all around the place to confine you to the designed area. Frustrating.
2. It's not fun to play: the DLC has you leave your main character and play as a girl instead, and Theresa is so annoyingly weak that you keep dying and dying, trying to complete all the basic chores that comprise this quest line. Realistic? Maybe. Frustrating? Very.
3. It's not even very realistic history-wise. A lone villager chick during a foreign invasion wouldn't be a huge heroine, stealth-killing a Cuman in her house, and then bravely escape the siege to save her brother. She would be immediately captured, beaten, abused and killed. The only realistic part of this DLC is the first quest in which you feed the hens and weed the garden.\t\t\t\t\t
