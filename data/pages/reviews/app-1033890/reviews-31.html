Edit; I have since the original posting played the second part of this DLC.

When everyone sins, how can one be expected to lead a life of virtue? And just what is a good christian? A long multi-staged quest involving religious affairs with Johanka at the center of it, and the looming authority of the holy church. Many familiar faces come into play, offering plenty of callbacks to the players past sins. You'll meet with other sinners, to either inspire them to lead a more virtuous lfe or take advantage of their situation. But even Henry must stand for his actions and repent for his sins at some point. Overall a fantastic and a unique quest, with the caveat that some situations could be more morally ambiguous.\t\t\t\t\t
