\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of those products I wish I could give a "meh" rating for.

The underlying games are good. Exceptionally so, back in the day. But the present-day implementation/emulation is technically sloppy. I experienced two different and recurring game-breaking bugs when using this product. At the time of writing, one of those bugs remains unpatched, but thankfully the community has found work-arounds which (eventually) made all the games in this collection playable for me.

If you're inclined to buy this, BE FOREWARNED. Be prepared to trawl through the Steam tech-support forums, and be aware of your rights regarding the Steam refund policy in case things turn belly-up.

EDIT: In the wake of a recent patch, I'm going to change this review to a tentitive "thumbs up", but I would still advise caution to any potential customers.\t\t\t\t\t
