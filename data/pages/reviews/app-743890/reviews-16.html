\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk. It took me a while to say anything about it because I was really giving it a chance.

It is a nostalgia bomb, indeed, filled with massive fps drops, just like it was on the original consoles back then. Yes, SNES/PSOne with slow downs on a modern PC.

This collection isn't optmized or have meaningful extras. It's just a compilation with some basic options and a museum section that, being honest, it is interesting... But doesn't do much beyong saying "this existed!". I can imagine the headache that it would have been to do it, but they could at least try and incluse some more extras, like the comic's complete issues or something. At least we have Age of Sigma included... BUT NO MAVERICK HUNTER X... For some reason. This is a Legacy Collection and one of the best remakes is out of it.

I use an Xbox Series controller to play this, but every damn time I have to start the game, close it and then start again just so it would recognize the controller.

Really, folks, there may be better ways to play this, either via emulator ou in the original consoles (if you're lucky to have one), because it's way too much of a headache for not so much of a collection. It's even split into two, for some reason that I can't make sense of. (Yes, money, but... Really?)

The slowdowns are a known issue for quite a while now and no updates or fixes has been made. So... Yeah.

The games are really good and very fun (and rage inducing, well.. Mega Man), but this collection IS NOT WORTH THE MONEY.\t\t\t\t\t
