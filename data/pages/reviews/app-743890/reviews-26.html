\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBought it for Mega Man X1 and X2. Mega Man X3 is a TERRIBLE game for a lot of reasons (see below), and Mega Man X sounds like a girl in X4.

Reasons why Mega Man X3 is terrible:

- Mega Man X takes roughly three hits before dying
- Minibosses randomly show up and are just as difficult, if not moreso, than the robot masters
- You can destroy some of the minibosses permanently if you discover the correct weapons to use, but instead of being rewarded by this you're punished by having to fight a more difficult boss later
- None of the robot masters can be killed with the X buster, not because they're invulnerable to it, but because you need to beat all of them to find the air dash, but you need the air dash to jump over them as they charge you, and you can only take three hits before dying, so you're just kinda screwed
- Whoever composed the music phoned it in, this is shoddy workmanship especially compared to X1 and X2
- The level designers also phoned it in, the same 5-6 enemies are present in most, if not all, stages, so yeah pretty repetitive and dull

Buy the game for X1 and X2, skip X3, maybe check out X4 but mute Mega Man X whenever he's talking. The X Challenge that pits you against two bosses at a time is pretty interesting, but it's a bummer it's in MMX4 style.\t\t\t\t\t
