\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've just spent the past 2 hours doing the X-Hunter stages on X2 and eventually found myself at the final boss. Took a 5 minute break, came back and the game had froze. Only solution was to turn off the game, meaning I have to replay the last 5 stages all over again, fighting every X-hunter, and having to re-fight every Maverick, Zero (because I didn't collect the parts), and Sigma twice. Sadly, I fail to see why one would purchase this collection instead of emulating the game. If I had access to save states, I would not need to replay all these stages again and there would be no need to sit through the pain of having to beat these bosses for the second time. Moving on from the game freezing, (which is crazy for 30 year old SNES games), the collection also suffers from horrible slowdown which makes some parts of the game a struggle to get through. I probably wouldn't have wrote this negative review, but the game crashing at the final boss was the last straw and I can't be arsed doing it again. I've only played X and X2 as of writing, and I know at some point I'll come back and finish the other games, but right now I am deflated.\t\t\t\t\t
