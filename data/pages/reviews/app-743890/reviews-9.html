\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMega Man X is one of the best series from the SNES. If you like classic Mega Man, you will love the X games.

The gameplay is similar to the original NES games but with better movement (dash jumping, wall jumps, weapons that effect your movement, and amazing physics) and the ability to charge EVERY WEAPON. The X series also has many more secrets, taking inspiration from Mega Man 4's secret items. Every stage has a heart tank to increase your max life, and most have either a sub tank (a rechargable E Tank) or a piece of armor that greatly enhances your abilities.

X1 is an absolute joy to blast through. The levels are simple though some have gimmicks. There are a ton of secrets, including ways to permanently change certain stages and weaken certain bosses with specific weapons. Bosses are also fair and really fun to fight against. X1 also starts off the story of the X and Zero games which is actually extremely dark and gritty.

X2 starts you off with certain abilities that you would normally need to obtain from armor in X1, which means it is a bit better feeling at the beginning of the game. The bosses are also just as good as those in X1, though they are a bit more difficult. Especially the X Busters.

X3 is basically just X2 but even better. In my opinion it is the best X game there is. Everything that I said about X2 is exactly the same with X3.

X4 is the first game where Zero is playable. Zero plays completely differently from X, utilizing more varied techniques as opposed to what are essentially elemental projectiles. Though X also gets some unique powers in this game. X is generally easier to play as than Zero, having more health but dealing less damage. X also gets armor upgrades, which Zero Doesn't. Once you beat the game, I recommend playing through it again using the Ultimate Armor code for X. It gives you a full set of even stronger armor as soon as you get your first armor piece, and it completely breaks the game. Zero has a similar code which changes his red armor to black, though in this game it does not have any gameplay effect.\t\t\t\t\t
