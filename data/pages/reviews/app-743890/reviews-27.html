Conclusion
It's ok to buy this collection just to support the franchise, but it's a terrible way to enjoy these games comparing to original hardware or emulation. I can't recommend this collection to whom want to play them seriously, especially experienced fans (unless you only wanna play X4). Get an emulator if you really want to play them on PC!

Cons
1. Horrendous Optimization:
You really need a high-end PC to run these 3 emulated games plus a 90's PC game, it will drain the half of your CPU and GPU power, like you're playing some new hot 3A games.

2. Noticeable Input Lag in 3 SNES Titles:
If you are used to the original or properly emulated form of these games, the input lag would be significant to you.

3. Censorship in X4:
Like seriously? Nobody had problems with those themes before, why now?

4. Unnecessary Tweaks:
Pseudo save feature with unfitting Y button icon in SNES games, why not just gives us save states without interfering the original game?

Pros
1. Games:
Undoubtedly, the original games are really good even to these days.

2. Additional Bonus (museum, challenge, gallery etc.).

3. Convenience:
This is the only way you can buy these games on PC so far.\t\t\t\t\t
