All four games in this collections are bangers. X4 being my personal favorite for its awesome graphics, fluid gameplay, and hilarious cutscenes. I'm sure I don't need to say anything about X1 since it puts the legacy in legacy collection. X2 and X3 are basically just the classic Capcom "if it ain't broke" mentality.
