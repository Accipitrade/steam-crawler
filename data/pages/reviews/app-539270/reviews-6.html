\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a basic Mahjong game, meaning it doesn't have a lot of options apart from different backgrounds and tilesets.

- It's decently made with a fair bit of challenge (some of the layouts do require a bit of luck with tile-order to pull off).
- It works fine in fullscreen for me (and I'm running at 4K).
- On passing a level, there's an option to submit it to online leaderboards (not a requirement, despite another review stating otherwise), though the online leaderboards might be bugged.
- There's no undo and the "how to play" documentation doesn't include pictures, so if you've never played Mahjong before this probably won't be the easiest entry for you.

I've enjoyed the game, but at $10 I think it's a tad overpriced. I got it at a steep discount though (part of a bundle), so I'm fairly happy with the purchase.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLuckily I bought this game for 90% off, asking $10 for this is just wrong.

First thing I noticed when I started the game is that it was french... I'm not french, nor did I ever set anything to french... also the translations are really bad

So I changed the language and noticed that there's an option to turn the music off, however, there's no option to change the volume so I had to manually turn it down in the windows settings

Next thing I noticed were the suuuper long loading screens, even just browsing the options and the menu took forever

The effects sound really cheap and distorted, they made my headphones crackle, you also only get 4 backgrounds and 4 themes for the tiles

About the gameplay: The first two levels went well, I was kinda confused because I never saw mahjong where you wouldn't have access to some tiles even tho they were right next to some that you could use, but I managed to understand it quickly

Once I was near the end of level 3 I went game over after using one of the hints... the bot made me go game over, so I tried again, this time without hints... and I went game over again and I tried it one more time and I went game over again

Isn't the game supposed to re-roll all the tiles when it's not possible to get a pair anymore? Well, after the third try I gave up

TL;DR

This game has nothing that hasn't been done better in other games, in fact, most free mahjong mobile apps are better than this game\t\t\t\t\t
