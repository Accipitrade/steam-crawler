\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't go into this game thinking you'll whiz through a bunch of mahjong boards for an easy 100%. This game will challenge you a bit and take a good deal of time to finish. But this IS a puzzle game. You'll need to use your brain and that's fun, right?

I haven't completed the game but I believe there are 70 different boards to play. I'm sure most of you are familiar with mahjong. Many tiles are placed next to each other and many are stacked and you have to match two similar ones to clear them off the board. But you can't clear blocked tiles - those with tiles on either side of them. So if there are multiple symbols of the same kind to match, make sure you clear the ones that unblock other tiles first. You can get stuck pretty fast if you aren't careful. And herein lies the challenge of the game. Because if you run out of moves, you have to start the board over from the beginning. There's no shuffle option. And each board (other than the first one) is locked - you must clear the one before it to unlock it. So it might take you a few tries.

I've found that I've gotten better after playing a few hours. Knowing which matches to create is key to not having to redo a board multiple times. Also, the game gives you a couple helping hands. First, if you can't find matches (and there are still matches to make) you can click on a hint. You can use this 3 times per level. Also, there are seasonal tiles. Flowers and branches and sun and clouds. Season tiles match up with any other of that season which gives you a few more options. You'll have to mess around with it for a bit to figure out which season tiles match up, but there aren't too many so it shouldn't take long.

If you don't get too irritated by having to redo boards sometimes, this can be a relaxing game. The music is soothing and matching tiles is therapeutic. Plus, some of the boards are in fun shapes like a skull or a teapot. I recommend this game to those who like puzzles involving strategic moves but ones which won't hurt your brain too much. :)\t\t\t\t\t
