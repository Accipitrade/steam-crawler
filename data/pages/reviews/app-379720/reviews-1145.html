\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile it has been about 4 years since DOOM came out and you should probably know if you want to play it or not based on just that, I can say, without a single doubt, that this game is worth your money and should definitely be played if you enjoy fast paced 1st person shooters. But what sorts of things should you know going in? Well, I'll tell you that right now. :)

PLOT: You are the Doom Slayer, a high-octane marine who hates demons with the burning passion of 1000 suns. Having just woken up within a strange tomb and the knowledge that nearly everyone in the Mars base for the UAC has been either killed or turned into more of the demons unwilling forces, it is now up to you to shoot, blow up, and rip and tear through every single creature that gets in your way with the tools at your disposal.

Pros: The game is fairly intuitive and easy to pick up for beginners, but it does get harder and more challenging as you meet and fight other demons within the wrecked remains of the UAC base and, later, the very bowels of Hell itself. Furthermore, the game sets itself up with a very simple and easy to follow story without looking into the backstory and extra elements scattered around you. However, should you choose to look at these, you will find a bevy of information and fun tidbits of knowledge and lore for the world around you.

But of course that isn't what you're here for, you're here to kill demons, and boy howdy, does the game let you do that! Everything from shotguns and plasma rifles to grenades and chainsaws are at your disposal to dispose of the residents of Hell. If you use the chainsaw, you get extra ammo and if you manage to daze them, you can perform a finishing move called a Glory Kill, which also gets you more health. The controls are tight and responsive on keyboard and mouse while I have found controller is a little more loose. But overall, it is a quick and extremely satisfying system that will easily allow you to obliterate the forces of Hell.

Cons: Truly, I can't think of any for myself. That's all. ^_^

Final Verdict: A must-have game if you are into bloody and satisfyingly violent games. If you like these sorts of games and don't have this yet, I'm honestly shocked.\t\t\t\t\t
