\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-- GAMEPLAY --
Combat:
Combat is really fun. This is what FPS games should be. It has 2 main problems though: not enough enemies in the combat arenas, and a lot of weapons become obsolate over time, because of better ones. For example, the pistol is just there to take up an inventory slot.
Exploration, secrets and upgrades:
This is a really good part of the game. Looking for secrets, exploring the map is really revarding. The maps look and feel amazing, like how they were in the original game. And with the unlocks, upgrades you can find, they gave a really good progression system to the game.
Arcade Mode:
It is a really fun mode to play around with, but only for a limited time. After a while, it gets boring.
Multiplayer:
Multiplayer is fun in this game. It is like the old multiplayer FPS games (like Quake), for which there is no modern equvivalent... yet. It has it's own gimmicks with demons, which are not the best, but whatever. There is a bigger problem. It is dead. So good luck playing it.
Snap Map Mode:
This is a really great addition to the game. There are tons of amazing player made content in it. We need something like this in more games.
Achievments:
There is only one problem here. Multiplayer achievments. They are the bane of mainly singleplayer games. And since the multiplayer is dead, it makes it less and less possible to gain these. It is annyoing if you want the Perfect Game badge.
10/8

-- GRAPHICS --
Well, it looks amazing. And the gore.. they put a lot of attention to detail there. This game is a constant visual orgy.
10/10

-- SOUNDS --
The sounds really fit the game. They put a lot of effort into it. And the music, that is just awsome.
10/9

-- STORY --
Minimal amount of story, not that we care much. We are just here to make demons into blood pinatas.
10/7

-- WORLD & WORLDBUILDING --
Yes, we have that here too, in the form of data logs and some exposition.
10/7

-- REPLAYABILITY --
There is not too much. You can replay the game on different difficulties, but that is it.
10/4

--TL;DR--
It is a great game, and fun to play. So go for it.
10/8
