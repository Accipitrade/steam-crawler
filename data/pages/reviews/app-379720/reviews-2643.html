\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR version:
PROS:
1. Fast game play, very brutal, much gore
2. Soundtracks are pretty good too, can be preview in iTunes which the music are composed by Mick Gordon.
3. Stunning visuals, if your hardware can support it, the game is highly optimized.
4. Exploring around the map will reward player from ammo recovery, to weapon/Preator suit upgrade.
5. A lot of challenges/runes to further upgrade Doom marine passive ability/weapon upgrade.
6. Fun and Fast paced MP, you will just respawn instantly after died.
7. No ammo? Chainsaw a demon, Low on health? Glory kill. Too much annoying demons? Big ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Gun!

CONS:
1. Doom marine is too over-powered, with those arsenal/abilities of his and plenty of ammo/health lying around the map. Player experienced in FSP should play at least at "Ultra Violence" difficulty.
2. High hardware requirement, At least Quad Core and mid-high end GPU is required.
3. Some area will not be accesible after moved to another area, which means backtracking sometime is impossible.
4. Limited enemies, they dont respawn which means you have to relaod a checkpoint if you missed the chance to complete a challenge.
5. Checkpoint save system.
6. Sometimes feels like controlling a Mario instead of Doomguy.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016 is the Dark Souls of Hell Based Shooters That Take Place on Mars.

The reason Doom 2016 is similar to Dark Souls is the difficulty that it may present and the disturbing imagery.

In Doom 2016 you take control of the iconic Doomguy donning his new and improved Praetor Suit. The Praetor Suit gives multiple abilities including a double jump and the ability to not die . Without this vital suit your character is just a man that got excavated from the depths of hell on a bloody quest for revenge.

On your adventure you have many tools in your arsenal from the amazing rocket launcher to the iconic Doom II double-barrel shotgun. Yet the main gimmick to this entry in the series is the glory kill, which can be used to kill an enemy with low health as well as gaining health yourself. This feature promotes fighting fiercly and staying near your enemies instead of hiding behind cover. The glory kill is also used as a mechanic for platforming. If you are around 10 feet away from your enemie and perform a glory kill it will proppel you to the enemie. There is a moment in the campain in which there is a jump you cannot clearalone but there is a stationary Imp on the other side. So you shoot the Imp until the glory kill is available, double jump across, and then try to glory kill the Imp. It is rarely used in a sense like that in the game but presents many ways you can you the mechanics in the game to your benefit.

It is a very fun Action FPS that really makes you remember the original Doom style where you just run around and Rip And Tear.

I give Doom 2016 a Dark Souls II/Souls-Like Games. I would suggest waiting for a sale, I would say that it is not worth the $30 price tag but if you can get it for $20 or less it should be on your watch list.\t\t\t\t\t
