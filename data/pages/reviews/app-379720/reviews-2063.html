\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom offers a gameplay similiar to the original Doom and Doom 2 with good graphics. There are a bunch of eastereggs included reffering to the original games. There are also a lot of secrets you can find on your way through demon infected mars and hell, if you search all the corridors and rooms to the left and to the right of the straight way to the end of the levels.

The weapons can be upgraded and most of them offer an alternative firemode. For each firemode you can get a mastery which needs you to complete some special kills with the specific firemode. The mastery makes the firemode even more effective.

There are several difficulty levels you can choose from. If you have already played several shooters "hurt me plenty" will be a good difficulty level to start. Completing all the challenges and weapon masteries the game offers, makes the game even more challenging. Don't get me wrong. The enemies aren't intelligent but they come in masses and sometimes in smaller areas where there isn't much space to escape.

Doom offers all the things the old games offered and is a nice trip back in the old days of shooters.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tViolent, bloodpumping and truly fast paced combat. They've taken every enjoyable aspect of an action FPS and crammed it into a beautifully bloody mess. Health regen comes through "glory kills", over the top melee executions that you perform once the enemy is at low health. This makes the action never stop and even if you're at the brink of death a glory kill or two gives you a fighting chance. You don't reload, instead you're encouraged to switch weapons constantly meaning you'll never even have the chance to thinking about getting bored.
The soundtrack is the best video game soundtrack ever in terms of fitting with the gameplay, hands down!
This game is for anyone that enjoys a good fast paced FPS with a good challenge and great and gory visual design.

Most prominent Pros:
- Every weapon feels good to use, gunplay is 10/10.
- No health regen. Instead you punch (or rip) every demon apart as it becomes your personal health pi√±ata.
- No reloading, instead you're encouraged to switch weapons and keep plowing through demons
- The most fitting and well made soundtrack of all time.

Neutral:
- Campaign is "only" at about 7-9 hours but it ensures that it doesn't overstay it's welcome. The game offers higher difficulties and an arcade mode.
- The story isn't super engaging but it knows that it is mostly a back drop to slaughtering hordes of demons.

Cons:
- If you're squeamish or generally don't like blood and gore, this game isn't for you.\t\t\t\t\t
