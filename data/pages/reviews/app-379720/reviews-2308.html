\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe go-to game to play when you want to RIP AND TEAR

This game is great! The gameplay is fast paced, the story is solid, and the destruction of demons makes you feel like a badass! The score for this game is also very well done and is probably my favorite gaming music for an intense FPS setting. Lots of weapons, perks, and powerups to use to add variety while playing, along with the intense up close and personal GLORY KILLS that change depending on which angle you approach and where u aim. Bethesda did very well to honor and showcase the original doom games in a HD rendered engine with secrets and other hidden gems around each level. The only downside is the multiplayer being very laggy and needs server support for better connections and balanced matchmaking setups. Doom 4 has definitely become one of my favorite games to play and I recommend it to anyone that is into the FPS genre.

Rating: 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
