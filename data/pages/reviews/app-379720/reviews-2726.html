Worthy of The Name...

...just short of perfection.

Pros
  • Gorgeous and well optimized (especially w/ vulcan)
  • Fast, frantic, meaty and entertaining combat with meaningful situational weapon swapping (during the campaign)
  • A hellacious in-your-face campaign that doesn't hold back from gorey gibby brutality
  • Amazing atmospheric multi-faceted maps, both MP & SP
  • No auto-regen, Health/Armor/Ammo pick-ups instead, all praise be to the FPS gods
Cons
  • No real mod/map tools (snap map is NOT the same)
  • Major fights are "events" I would have prefered static spawns
  • Originally split the community between haves and have nots w/ the map packs (which are awesome)
  • Movement could have been faster
  • Loadouts in MP, should have been map spawns, unlimited weapon carry
