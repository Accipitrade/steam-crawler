The reason it took me so long to play was only lack of hardware; I simply cannot both play FPS games and enjoy them on anything but a PC, and I only recently decided it was time, after all these years, to see if the fire still burned for them after years of being a console warrior.

It does. This game is a pinnacle. So smooth, so rich, so engrossing, in graphics, in sound, in feel and AI and, most importantly for me: in spirit.\t\t\t\t\t
