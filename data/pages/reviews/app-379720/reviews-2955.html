Just keep running and gunning, ripping and tearing. Explore detailed environments while collecting bigger, more satisfying weapons and upgrades until there isn't a single demon left on Mars.

I wholeheartedly recommend it just on singleplayer alone, and I haven't even tried Multiplayer yet.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs I was mainly interested in the singleplayer campaign of this game I waited and waited for a big sale on this. Now with a -67% sale you should definitely pick this one up.
There're no overly long cut scenes, Doomguy doesn't give **** and just wants to rip and tear, there are hordes of enemies to kill, badass weapons to kill with, gory finishing moves, fast gameplay, large and gorgeous UAC and hell maps that are well lit and not 90% dark, key cards and skull keys, lots of secrets, bro-fisting plastic Doomguy toys, classic maps, community made Snap Map singleplayer/coop/multiplayer maps and a decent multiplayer that doesn't really need the DLC.
If you are a fan of the classic Doom games and were disappointed by Doom 3's tedious campaign and gameplay then don't worry, this is truly a great remastering of the old formula.

The game is quite large however. With my max download speed of 1.7 MB/s it took 12 hours to download this game, so I recommend you instantly create a backup via Steam and save the files to an external drive so you can quickly re-install this game when you feel like destroying some demons again. But the wait was definitely worth it!

Played with keyboard/mouse

Pros:
- Gorgeous graphics
- Intense and fast gameplay
- Badass weaponry
- Secrets
- Classic Doom maps to unlock

Cons:
- Story, but who really cares about that in a Doom game?
- Multiplayer portion gets stale really quick
