\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is possibly the most cathartic and fun game I've ever played. The combat is fantastic in that it's challenging enough to be difficult but fairly easy to master at the lower difficulties. The higher difficulties ramp it well and provide plenty of replay value. The story is okay at best, but it's only there to give you a reason to skull♥♥♥♥ an army of demons. The fast pace, excellent soundtrack, and varied action make it fun to play even after multiple playthroughs and mountains of slain demons.

What else can I say? This game is the equivalent of a stress ball, if you replaced the ball with a chainsaw and a large collection of sci-fi laser cannons, old west double-barrelled shotguns, and everything in between.

Oh, and the multiplayer is mediocre. But who cares? This game is all about the singleplayer Doom-Slayer experience.\t\t\t\t\t
