\r\n\t\t\t\t\tpros:
-very well optimized, maintains 144 fps very well with i7 8700k and 1060ti on high settings
-good level design
-atmosphere between mars and hell is quite distinct
-a decently long campaign especially if you play on nightmare
-many screenshot worthy moments
-actually a good aim trainer
-great soundtrack that really fits the pace of the game

cons
-the in game sound is almost completely non existent behind the player, enemies will teleport behind you body blocking and wrecking your day. this is happens in multiplayer as well, expect to get a shotgun up your ass and not hear a thing.
-multiplayer is dominated by high level players running combat shotgun with double barrel shotgun and kinetic mine loadouts.
-demon runes and shotguns have made any level of skill attained through aiming not matter at all in multiplayer

TLDR:
very great single player game with amazing atmosphere, full of adrenaline and good music. multiplayer has a lowered skill ceiling compared to other arena shooters\t\t\t\t\t
