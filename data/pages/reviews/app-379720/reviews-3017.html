\r\n\t\t\t\t\t It's fast, it's gory, it's chaos. The best shooter game of this year. DOOM go back to its root of what making it...DOOM: Fast-pace First-Person Shooter.
The game give you only one thing to do: Make anything stand before you into a bloody pile of goo. Although there's a story in the game, but Doom Guy (or YOU) don't give a damn. The only thing he cares about is how fast he can unload his bullet into enemies. And if he's out of ammo? Out come the good old chainsaw to rip them apart for bonus ammo.
Good weapon customisations that change the way weapons work, although quite limit, the game give you the flexibility of switching between 2 customisations on the fly to best suit for the situation. No reloading make the gameplay even faster and more chaotic, since you don't even have time to duck and reload like other FPS game, and stop moving is equal to getting yourself blown to pieces. NEVER STOP MOVING in battle.
The game pacing is good. When you're not in battle, you can take time to explore and discover health, shield and ammo packs, and sometime collections hidden all over the maps. When you're in battle, the enemies will not let you breath for a second, waves after waves will jump at you at any chance they're given, so constantly moving is a must.
Graphics and animations can be condensed into one word: GORIOUS. Gory Finisher is very details and satisfied, but after a while, thing can get stale, since there's a limit animations for each type of enemies (some has less than others): front, side, back and air execution.
The soundtrack is killer and very HEAVY METAL!
Multiplayer is average, with endless grinding to unlock new skins, weapons, demons, taunts,..., just a side project that added after the core singleplayer game is finished. But that's a good thing, since players can get more out of Singleplayer than Multiplayer, which lots of games have it backward.

Overall, best FPS game I've played this year.
