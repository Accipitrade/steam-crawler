\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI didnt play this gem until 2021.

This game is fantastic, and gets more enjoyable as you progress within. This is similar to the Doom of the 90's and it's a real treat to have such a well crafted single player experience.

Sadly they couldn't replicate the simple formula with the more recent Doom Eternal (which decided Doom needs platforming and a enemy weakness system...just why?), but that's a story for another time.

This game "Doom (2016)" is fantastic, and can be had quite often on sale for dirt cheap (<$10CAD) -- it's well worth the pickup for those days where all you want to do is kick back after work (or late at night) and just shoot demons without convoluted systems, micromanagement or plotlines weighing you down.

In an era of annoying micro transactions, in game currencies, and annoying DLC segmentation, DOOM 2016 is great singleplayer game that has none of that â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥. If you played Doom back in the day, you owe it to yourself to get this. You'll get a solid 10-18 hours out of your initial play through.

While one may not enjoy it as much as I have, it's hard to imagine any fan of this genre and IP won't extract good value for their time when purchased on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
