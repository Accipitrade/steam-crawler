\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a game about shooting demons, because f*** demons. There was also some stuff about a sickly-looking lady and ScienceBot 3000 and extracting energy from the souls of the damned. It's all well and good, but it kind of goes on in the background of the Doom marine smashing things up and force-feeding demons their own eyeballs.

I don't play a lot of first person shooters, I never played any of the classic run-and-gun shooters like Doom or Serious Sam, and I don't even particularly like heavy metal. But I love this game. I like that there is both a great variety and a great volume of demons to shoot. I like that you can shoot them without reloading. And I like that taking cover and ammo conservation are not concerns the Doom marine loses sleep over--these problems being solved by more shooting/running and chainsawing demons in half, respectively.

In short, this is a very fun, energetic game that I think lends itself well to replaying on harder difficulties. I highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
