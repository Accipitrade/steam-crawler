\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow, what a game. It doesn't really break any boundaries. It's not anything revolutionary. The original DOOM was, but this is largely just bringing it into the modern era. But they did such a damn good job of it! The music, the level design, the enemies, the story, everything just pops. But the gameplay is really center stage here, and that's a good thing, because they've done a fantastic job of it; this is some of the most polished gameplay you can find in a singleplayer shooter. Oftentimes I prefer games which are convoluted and hard to get into, because of the depth of gameplay that can bring. But sometimes, you want something which is simple, but well done. That's this game. It knew what it wanted to do, and it did it - nothing more, nothing less. There is no fluff, there is nothing which doesn't help you accomplish the end goal of smashing in demon skulls with style. The challenge was just right, for me at least, on hard difficulty. It was just difficult enough to force me to strategize and plan things out over several attempts, but not so hard that I'm about to tear out my hair. The powerups and weapon mods are tons of fun to play with as well. There are some really cool combinations you can get going between runes, and mods, and powerups, and you can switch them to fit the situation. I also really enjoyed the rune challenges which you have to complete to unlock a new powerup. They're not required, but it can help a lot later on in the game, and they're also great little one-off fights if you're looking for an extra challenge. I really can't think of a single bad thing to say about this. It does what it does and it does it stunningly. The only reason I can take off any points at all is because this isn't my ideal kind of game. But for what it is, it's damn near perfection.

9/10\t\t\t\t\t
