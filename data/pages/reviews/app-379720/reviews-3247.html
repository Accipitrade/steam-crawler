\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast paced shooter without unnecessary sneaky stealhy bull.
Runs smoothly. 1440p res with 980 easily over 60 FPS and looks good.
Occasionally textures stream in a bit late when entering a new areas.

Weapons feel and sound good besides the starter pistol that just makes you sad. The chaingun especially tickled my fancies the first time I got it.

Plenty of secrets and collectibles to find if you're into such things. Can replay levels already played when you've gotten necessary things to reach those places you earlier couldn't reach.
Levels seem straight forward enough so you don't get lost completely and even if you do the game provides you with a map system.

At first the glory kill system feels a bit tacky but it works with the game pretty well.
Keeps the pace up even if you're running low on health. Punch a demon in the ♥♥♥♥ and it plops out health boost and you're ready to rampage again.

Running low on ammo? No problem, shove your chainsaw up that rocket shooting demons b-hole and it magically rains ammo for your assault rifle. No need to bother with mundane things like reloading. If you have ammo for your weapon it keeps shooting until you run out.

Weapons and your suit have upgrade options. But don't worry, it ain't no RPG with elaborate skill trees.

Guess it has a story too. Something about demons, hell dimensions, mad scientists and punching stuff. Few cutscenes here and there.

Sound track is awesomish enough. Single player campaign is about 10-ish hours long if you don't spend too much time snooping around in the corners.

Edited for silly badge.\t\t\t\t\t
