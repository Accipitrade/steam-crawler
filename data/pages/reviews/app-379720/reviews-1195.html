\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is how you remake a game.

Doom 2016 keeps the feel of the core Doom gameplay while enhancing it to create a modern masterpiece.

The gameplay is fast, brutal and satisfying.
The weapons are varied and fun to mess around with even if you will eventually end up using only 3 or 4.
The level design is spot on and the secrets are fair and rewarding.
The enemies look better than ever and take just the right amount of tactical playing to get around.
The story fits right into the world building and the overall tone of the game, and is given to you through several means, so you can go as deep as you want.
Though some may find the game a bit short, I feel like the length is just right. It keeps the experience tight and the pasing on point.
The ending is a tad bit anticlimactic, but it doesn't detract from the overall experience.

Just a fun game overall that does its predecessor justice.\t\t\t\t\t
