\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this for the "Co-Op" play, and unfortunately it doesn't off a Cooperative Campaign experience. It has Team Deathmatch, and it also has some SnapMap function that forces you to build your own maps to play a demon killing experience with friends. My and some friends wanted to spend the Campaign experience together, yet we were met with a void to the experience. I'm sure the multiplayer deathmatches are fun, and the snapmap could be fun, but I have tons of other games with multiplayer (including the Original Doom series) and it would be better to SnapMap if you could play the campaign with your friends, see which parts of the maps you found challenging and fun, and then came together with each other building the ultimate experience that way. I'm sure the single player campaign is fun as well, but I solely bought this game for "Co-Op" as I have plenty of single player games already.

Team Deathmatch - Was ok, however the announcer sounds like a dork. For the game being Doom, and you slaughtering the crap out of each other, I don't understand why they didn't have an announcer that has some ballsy freakin in-your-face murder-face metal as frick voice.

Campaign - I enjoy the fast-paced monster slaying action, and the gore kills are fun.

tl;dr I do not recommend this game if you're looking for a true "Co-Op" experience. If you want some classic single player monster killing fun, go for it, or if you want some deathmatch action it'll be found here.\t\t\t\t\t
