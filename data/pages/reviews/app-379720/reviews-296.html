Battles are fast and frantic, demanding that the player constantly stay moving and assessing which demon to kill and how to kill it the fastest, since spending too long on one gets you killed by the others. Basically, there's no reason not to get DOOM if you like FPS games even a little bit.\t\t\t\t\t
