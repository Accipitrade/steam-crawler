\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen this game was announced I was cautiously optimistic. With good reason, given the past few releases by id software, I was worried about the reimagining of one of my most beloved franchises. Given the previous releases I was wholly surprised by the ability of the id team, and bethesda to bring Doom back to the same visceral feeling that the original had. The balance of combat, the smooth feeling of moving about the world were all quite impressive to me. I can't imagine this game being reimagined in a better fashion.

It feels how Doom should play, the high intensity visceral combat combined with open ended exploration with secrets hidden about each level. The plethora of new and old weapons reimagined into a package that is just as exciting and enjoyable to play as the original was when I was a kid. It has definitely exceeded my expectations and then some. There was even some of the original melodies of songs from the original mixed into the new soundtrack in a very tasteful way. Along with the new additions as far as the sound design, an overall excellent use of the source material. I would recommend this game to both fans of the franchise, and to newcomers who want some fast paced challenging fps designs.\t\t\t\t\t
