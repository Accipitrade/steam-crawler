\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has an arcade feel to it.
Which is not a negative.
The action is fast paced and the enemies(demons) can be killed without too much trouble.
Some are more challenging than others.
Really keeps you on your toes!
I got stuck in the foundry when I had to kill the fourth cell which also lends the game a solve the puzzle challenge.
Not a sim but this is what I like about it.
It's fun,it's fast.
I'm really enjoying hell(Mars)
From Lazarus labs I could no longer cut it at hurt me plenty and had to play the game at I'm to young to die.
Went through the remaining levels easily while the final bosses still gives you a worthwhile challenge.
Felt like a really long game,money worth spending in my opinion.
But since the game became so hard halfway through I don't think I'll be a good enough player to consider buying Eternal.
I had a lot of fun though.\t\t\t\t\t
