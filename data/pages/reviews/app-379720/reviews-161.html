\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGameplay wise, this is definitely top. Level designs are fun (Even if they do get a little repetitive at times), the variety of weapons allows for different play styles, as well as many mods between which you can switch to your need. Also a variety of enemies with different gimmicks that you have to figure out, still really fun to basically maul through demons.

There's a total of three boss battles. The first two have different phases, two stages, gimmicks, etc., spicing things up to keep them fun. Died more than a few times to these two. However the third one feels rushed from beginning to end. No second stage, no real challenge, attacks are easy to read, and you also get lots of health and ammo. Seems as if it was added in the last moments before release.

The story is okay, although it's clearly not the main focus of the game. Definitely leaves you in a bit of a cliffhanger, basically a scheme to buy the next Doom.

No significant bugs, the deathscreen may glitch sometimes where enemies or your body may be floating or dying weirdly. But nothing gamebreaking.

Music is 100/10, Mick Gordon is an absolute genius, hearing BFG Division while in combat is enough to get adrenaline pumping.

Lastly, a little personal ick I noticed, the pause menu always plays 'Hellwalker'. Don't get me wrong, it's a great song, but having it start over and over everytime I hit pause is weird, and also lacks continuity. Other games (DMC5 is at the top of my head rn) have the battle theme continue in the background even in the pause screen.
TL;DR
Overall 8/10. I would've given it a 10/10 if it weren't for (in my opinion) the extremely anti-climatic ending.\t\t\t\t\t
