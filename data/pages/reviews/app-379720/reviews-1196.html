\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMETAL MUSIC
M E T A L M U S I C

Seriously, this game will make you bleed from everywhere with how intense it is. No game has ever made me take a deep breath after completing one of the arenas in a stage from the amount of tension and violence going on around the player. Everything is intuitive, fast-paced, and absolutely no-BS. Where other games give you cutscenes that show you something you don't really care about, this game reserves those few moments where control is taken from you for moments that legitimately matter and aren't annoying. You scarcely notice them, even playing through a fourth or fifth time.

While I tend to be a casual game player, not really concerned with being the best, I noticed this game is easy to learn, and only a little harder to approach mastery. With only a short period of practice you can get good enough to play on harder difficulty levels and blitz through them like they're nothing. Is it easy? No f'ing way. Is it a satisfying, non-punishing challenge? Absof'inglutely.

Buy it, on sale or not. It hasn't aged a day.\t\t\t\t\t
