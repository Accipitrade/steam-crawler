\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is game that conjures memories for many of us. While most seem to remember it fondly, I was one that never thought it was anything special. I truly despised missing secrets and having to start a stage over. Also, the degree in which secrets were hidden that all but required a guide or friend who learned from a friend who lied about finding it on his own; no one believes you.

The good news is that Doom 2016 is a fun game if played on Nightmare. The combat is challenging but fair. The "challenges" per weapon/level/rune force you to variety your playstyle which is both healthy and amusing. The secrets were well implemented and findable with the proper suit upgrade with the majority being simply slightly hidden path.

Arcade mode feels like the way the game is meant to be played shifting the focus from explore/defeat/repeat to a mad-dash unrelenting slaughter to link kill combos.

My one complaint would be focused on the Multiplayer achievements where I need to unlock equipment by FIRST reaching a certain level and SECOND doing a challenge. While this doesn’t sound tortuous, the level needed requires grinding 40-60 hours depending on skill. Doom’s Multiplayer is fun but after 10 hrs you have played every variation at least once and the next 10 you have favorites loadouts and maps. After that each game is a bore as you stop caring about if your team is any good and just try to grab the demon powerups to feel alive.

****************

Solid FPS that is worthy of your time; achievement completion-ist be warned if you hate MP\t\t\t\t\t
