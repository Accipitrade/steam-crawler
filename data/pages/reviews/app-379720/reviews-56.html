\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBetween this and Eternal you have what is probably the best single player FPS experience of all time.

This one (2016) has the better story and atmosphere. Eternal has the better game play. I really can't say enough good things about it.

Neither game though has great multiplayer. I would suggest another game if you are looking for that.

Importantly, if you plan to play both, play this one first. Eternal adds mechanics and it's hard to go backwards once you are used to them. Also the story starts here, so things make more sense if you play this first.

You can certainly play either one stand alone, or out of order, it tis but a suggestion.\t\t\t\t\t
