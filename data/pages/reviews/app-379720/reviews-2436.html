\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is the reboot that everyone did not expect to exceed their expectations, including myself. When it was first unveiled officially during Bethesda's first-ever E3 Conference back in 2015, I was both excited and skeptical at first. Though it looked somewhat beautiful due to its emphasis on atmosphere in the Mars and hell setting and the introduction of the glory kills mechanic to the franchise looked brutal and added more to the character of Doomguy (or Doom Slayer, which ever you prefer).

A year later when the game was finally released, it looked different from its E3 footage and id Software completely overhauled the graphical look of the game! Instead of downgrading, they upgraded everything! It looked even better and it was stunning. And it was a shock that consoles can look great and run of 60 fps, but the PC was the best place to play it. It is well optimized and the amount of options to tweak your desired standards is a lot.

To sum it up, DOOM (2016) is an amazing game worthy of the name for both the long time DOOM gamers and newcomers to the franchise and every content and effort put into the game is absolutely excellent and deserves every award it got. It is the return of the fast-paced, first-person gameplay and killing demons never gets tiring.

10/10, the most religious game ever.\t\t\t\t\t

PROS AND CONS:
+ Incredible graphics
+ Fantastic effects
+ Lovely sounds
+ Great music
+ Although the graphics and everything is modern the gameplay is exactly like the old shooter games.
+ Really cool bosses
+ Good game duration. The campaign is 8-12 hours long and if you include all of the multiplayer content and everything else it goes well above the 80 hours.
+ Different difficulties that really spice up the game
+ Multiplayer
+ Most badass game ever ;)
+ Alot of content for just 20 dollars

CONS -
- Needs alot of space on your computer (60-80GB)
- May be a little bit repetitive for some people
- Has some bugs
- Long loading times

Overall: 9/10 Fantastic game that tries to go back to the old school shooters with a modern touch to it. Not expensive for alot of pure and balanced gameplay. If you want to rip some arms and break some skulls where are you waiting for?\t\t\t\t\t

Brutal, without mercy, rage, this is DOOM
This game makes a character in all of modern media so angry, so violent, so dangerous its a horror...not for the player, but for the enemies that are sadly locked in a room with you. I have tried to think of a character more violent; I was unsuccessful. HE is too angry. There is no match for him. This has broken, smashed, and set fire to the mold of the FPS genre.
Performance, graphics, soundtrack, gameplay; everything is fantastic. Played on a 970 and 1080 ti, r1700 16gb. I may have also played it on an fx6300 and it still ran fine. You could potentially run it on a literal toaster.

Do I suggest the game? Absolutely. Rip and tear, until you finish it. Then play it again. And again.\t\t\t\t\t
