\r\n\t\t\t\t\tQuake meets Metroid Prime to rock some tunes. After you nail the "dance" of the game, it becomes quite easy even on Nightmare difficulty, but it is still addicting. You are going to find it difficult getting used to slow paced FPS again after playing this and the sequel.

The brown environments gets a little boring after some time, but if you dial the settings just right the colors can look quite good. It needs a good dose of sharpening because of the weird blurry anti aliasing. It supports Vulkan API and takes advantage of 8GB VRAM cards with a hidden Nightmare graphics preset.

The multiplayer is a basic arena shooter with a demon power up twist. It still has a small dedicated community of Quake pros who seem to be online every weekend.\t\t\t\t\t
