\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're new to the Doom franchise, this is the perfect time and place to start. The level design is expertly crafted, the story is intriguing without disrupting gameplay, enemies are both simultaneously deadly and satisfying to kill, and there are many fun weapons & gadgets to play with. In addition, Doom has a ton of extra side content such as multiplayer, varying difficulties, arcade, and even a map editor! Overall, the game is super solid fun throughout!

The only "bad" things about Doom 2016 I could possibly think of were 1.) the underutilization of a mechanic in the level design where you shoot red lights to progress, and 2.) An imperfectly programmed glory kill system which could potentially get players stuck out of bounds. However, these issues are so minor that most average players won't notice! I look forward to playing Doom Eternal this year!\t\t\t\t\t
