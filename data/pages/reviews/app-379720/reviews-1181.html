\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBrief Overview
Doom has the player take the role of a space marine as he battles demonic forces from Hell. It is a reboot of the Doom franchise, thus you aren't require to play previous instalments before this one.

Story
While the story isn't innovative, it helps give a purpose to your play style and provides an additional layer to the game, separating it from some of its competitors in the FPS genre. Missions are semi-linear and have multiple secrets hidden in areas of the level; giving the game high replay-ability.

Gameplay
I found the gameplay to be flexible and dynamic. The game offers a wide range of weapons that help change the rhythm of each subsequent play through. You can find upgrades for your guns to switch what the secondary fire does as well as spend points to increase their effectiveness. You're also able to upgrade your predator suit, which allows you to decide on increasing your health, armour or ammo count. Multiple types of monsters attack the player in their own unique way.
Doom's soundtrack is undoubtedly one of the best in video game history. It motivates the player and creates an adrenaline rush as you blast demons.

Rating: 9.2/10
The only complaint I have for this game is that I wish there was a longer playthrough time\t\t\t\t\t
