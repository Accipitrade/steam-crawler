TL;DR:

Pros - Excellent gameplay, music selection & timing perfect, glory kills are glorious, arcade mode is perfect

Cons - No additional singleplayer chapters (so far)

This is one of the best games I have played in a long time. I would go from marathoning it for hours on end, quiting to take a break, then jumping back in after 5 minutes away because I needed more. The gameplay is very fast paced and will get your adrenaline going (especially in the late levels).

I found the story interesting though I know some others called it a bit thin. I would definitely recommend "grabbing" the random historical story bits you come across (green things) as they offer an interesting recollection of what happened previously. Not the best story I have experienced but definitely not the worst.

As I said the gameplay is some of the best I have ever come across. It is very fast paced and the music helps to amp you up even more in the high pressure situations. The guns fit the fast paced gameplay perfectly with many different ways of taking down the demons you come across (you will likely use all within the same situations at points). Which brings me to the glory kills: hell yes. The glory kills are great and do not slow the gameplay down at all. Instead they make it feel more smooth as you go from shooting every demonic thing you see to doing an epic kill then back to running for your life and trying to survive.\t\t\t\t\t
