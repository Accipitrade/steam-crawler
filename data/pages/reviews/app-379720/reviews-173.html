\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the real Doom 3. Don't play that 2004 piece of ----. While 2016 is a little annoying with the much burnt-out community memes, this is a fantastic modern take on Doom. There is next to nothing wrong with it. A couple enemies are overpowered and cause cheap deaths (like the Pinkies) and sometimes you can miss a platform, there is fall damage but you have to fall incredibly far. Outside of that you have a perfect game, it's fast, extremely fun, the guns are all good, the bosses are fun and challenging, the level design goes back to the good era of the 90s where you unlock doors and have to backtrack (ie not the bad modern take on level design which is corridor, cutscene, corridor, etc). The double jump is a flawless great addition and ledge detection works literally every time. All of the animations are quick, ie glory kills, getting up from a ledge, so nothing distracts from the gameplay. You feel powerful and like your moves and inputs matter, you have to learn enemies and their patterns. The skill of the player in this game matters.

If you meet anyone who doesn't like 2016 and likes Doom 3, turn and run full speed and block them. If you didn't like 2016 and liked 3 you are EVERYTHING WRONG with the FPS games industry. id went bankrupt because their flagship game was a failure and no Doom game was made for another 12 years. How do you go from inventing the FPS industry in one game and bombing out of it in the next? The answer is Doom 3. I've never met anyone who liked Doom 3 over Doom 1, 2, or 2016 that wasn't a complete and total weirdo. If you like 3 please kill yourself and get out of affecting this industry.\t\t\t\t\t
