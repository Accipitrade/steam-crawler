\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNEAR PERFECT----------BEST SHOOTER OF THE DECADE----------------BUY NOW
Going into this game, I was expecting another runn, jump, and shoot 'em up game, (similar to the latest Cods, Halos, and Titanfalls) but Doom took me by complete surprise! Starting off with graphics, this game is unbelievable (In a good way of course). The textures of every box, weapon, and space demon absolutely blows my mind. You can literally see the sweet dripping off them. Doom is incredibly optimized to where a not-so-good PC is able to run it. Its beautifally atmospheric landscape is just pure eye candy and the action is unbeatable. I have never played a game where I can blow a whole in a hell alien's chest and then tear its arm off and proceed to beat its skull in; and that's just the campain - which might I add is extremelyinteresting and fun. There is never a dull moment and is simplistic yet entertaining. When I say simplistic I mean that the developers did not try to force feed the player to many things to do in the game like some others.. *cough cough Call of Dootie*. Furthermore, the multiplayer is incredibly entertaining and lives up to its potential of being a good ole fashioned gory gunfest. Although it takes place in space, it does not defy gravity and allows you to run on wallls and spaceboost 100 ft. in the air like some games previously mentioned.. cough... the customization is heartwarming with all the color and patern combonations you can do to your weapons and self-armour. Although it is fast paced it does not feel TOO fast. Upon all these positive things the icing on the cake is the level maker, which adds a feeling of Super Mario Maker to the game but with tons of blood and gore. All in all, I give this game a solid 10/10. I recommend this game to everyone who wants to have a good time and take a break from the raging competitive scene, even though I am thinking about playing this game competitively!\t\t\t\t\t
