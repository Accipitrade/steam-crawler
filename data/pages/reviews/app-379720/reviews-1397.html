\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM 2016 has the best level design of a game i have ever played, the dark and gross corridors of a completely destroyed U.A.C base is where you are running through most of the game.

It feels AMAZING to move as Doomguy, he is much faster than your average FPS protagonist which is to be expected from a series known for it's speed and creating the idea of speedrunning.

The guns are amazing and fun to use, each have their own mods that you can level up from mission challenges.

The only thing i do not like about the game is that the roster of weapons is unbalanced and the Super Shotgun or the Gauss Cannon with siege mode outclass every other weapon in your arsenal.

Other than that, buy this game and play it UNTIL IT IS DONE!
9.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
