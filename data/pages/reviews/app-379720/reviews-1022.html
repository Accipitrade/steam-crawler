Skip ahead, past my college years, a failed marriage, first children, death of parents, birth of more kids....to now. Doom has been remade for the newer generation. Although I doubt it is as revolutionary as it's now ancient ancestor, it is a hell of an upgrade. The game looks great, has intuitive controls, nice gun play, a quick pace. It has the kind of sophistication one expects from modern games, while keeping the DNA of its ancestor.

Obviously I got little of the story by playing this game for 10 minutes. But who played the original Doom for it's story? Did it even have a story? Play this game when you want fast paced first person gun play. Who knows, you may stumble upon a story.\t\t\t\t\t
