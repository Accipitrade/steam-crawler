The story is much more in depth as we're used to for newer games and is briskly paced with very little slow down. Multiplayer falls short for me, but I can only play the most multiplayer games and enjoy them in most cases. Doom will be the death and doom of your weekend as you won't be able to stop playing.\t\t\t\t\t
