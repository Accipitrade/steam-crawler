How well it runs:
Runs very well, I tested it on two windows pcs, here are the specs for the two pcs:

PC 1: Nvidia 1050 GTX 4gb vram, 16gb ram and an intel core i7 (7th gen).
Average fps on ultra settings, 1080p and vsync enabled: 60fps.

PC 2: intel(R) 620 UHD graphics, 8gb ram, intel i5 (8th gen).
Average fps on low settings and under 1080p resolution: a playable 30fps.

Story: you shoot demons with heavy metal playing in the background, oh and you have to close the portal to hell.

Gore: the Doom series is known for gore, you kill demons in different ways, guns, chainsaws, grenades and melee kills. It's not very realistic gore and bodies disappear after about 5 seconds (other than human bodies that are already on the map).

Replayable? Yes, very replayable, I'm currently on my second run on nightmare.

Age I'd recommend: well the point of the game is to shoot things and tear demons apart while heavy metal music is playing in the background, I'd recommend 14+ but it really depends on how much violence and gore you can stomach.\t\t\t\t\t
