\r\n\t\t\t\t\tKilling hell spawns never felt as good as DOOM (2016), this initiative to revitalize the franchise managed to carry its own weight and to deliver an enjoyable experience through and through.

From the kick-ass soundtrack to the enthusiastically dismemberment of the foes that dare oppose you, nothing is short of awesome. I felt the story to be somewhat forgettable, but that was because I was having too much of a good time killing demons to pay attention to it.

What especially surprised me was the incredibly fun multiplayer, which made me all the more sad to discover that so few people play it anymore. You can see where Quake Champions got all its good gameplay mechanics from. DOOM is worth every penny you spend on it.\t\t\t\t\t
