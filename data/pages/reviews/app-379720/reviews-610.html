\r\n\t\t\t\t\t☼ Graphics ☼
- Stunning - 10/10
The Graphics for the game were almost perfect. Everything in this game is polished and there are no dull areas.

♬ Music ♬
- Perfect - 10/10
The heavy metal that plays gets your adrenaline pumping and keeps you fighting. The soundtrack is amazing and nothing could better represent the Doom Slayer.

☠ Difficulty ☠
- Hard - 8/10
I played this game on Nightmare mode, and most parts I did not have an issue, but I did have trouble in some areas that seemed impossible. The game does a great way of balancing difficulties.

§ Bugs §
- None - 10/10
Nothing much to say here, I do not recall running into any bugs in my play-through.

☯ Story ☯
- Amazing - 10/10
The story has twists and kept me interested the entire time. When in hell, there are these shrines that tell you of the Doom Slayers history, and I loved listening to that and reading up on the logs that you can acquire throughout the game.

⚔ Gameplay ⚔
- The Best - 10/10
Oh my god do not even get me started on the gameplay, there isn't much more to say than that it is perfect. The glory kills and the visuals wrap everything together...amazing.

۞ Game time / Length ۞
- Decent - 6/10
I would have loved to see more content in this game, but it was not meant to be a long game. The game would take normal players around 16 hours to complete, but I was playing on nightmare and tried to do a completionist run, making my game time longer.

$ Price / Quality $
- Wait for sale - 5/10
$20 is pretty steep for this game I would say, I bought this game and eternal on sale. This game was 5 dollars and I could not recommend it more for that price. I would say a good resting price would be 10-15 dollars.

- Overall rating - 9/10 - I had an amazing time shredding through this game, when on sale I recommend it to everyone. This has been in my top 5 favorite games ever....\t\t\t\t\t
