\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI didn't think I'd have fun with a new Doom game in 2016, but I was wrong. This game is an absolute joy and a nice change of pace from the general shooter format we've come to know. The world is immersive, the combat is fluid, and there's a surprising amount of exploration to be made. I haven't touched the multiplayer yet, but I can highly recommend this game for its single player experience.

Pros:
+ Fluid gameplay
+ Immersive world
+ Rewarding exploration
+ Boss battles
+ More customization than you'd expect for a Doom game
+ Proper ultrawide support
+ Vulkan if you have AMD

Cons:
- A few FPS dips not due to hardware
- Poor multi-GPU support
