Unfortunately what ruined it for me was the fact that between the action set pieces, it becomes a big puzzle as to where to go next on the map and this pulled me right out of the game. In frustration, I had to consult YouTube walkthroughs several times to figure out where to go next and how exactly to get there. I also found the overall difficulty to be a bit high and the checkpoint save system didn't help matters either. In the end, I gave up on this one after playing just under five hours (most of that trying to figure out where to go next) and really have no desire to go back to it. Uninstalled. This was the only time that I ever asked for a refund on a game on Steam, which was denied because I played for more than two hours. Will definitely be skipping on Doom Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
