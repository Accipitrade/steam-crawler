I can safely say that this is the DOOM we wanted! Fast paced, gory good fun!


CAMPAIGN:

It's fast-paced, gory, brutal, and challenging. Ultra-Violence is pretty brutal if you aren't able to keep up at times. Weapon upgrades/mods and suit upgrades can help quite a bit in certain situations when you've lost control of the battle. Powerups such as Berserk make a return in this game, basically turning you into Kenshiro from Fist of the North Star sort of, lol. Mainly in terms of making enemies explode XD. Archvilles make a return of course, and this time they have new tricks while keeping the same level of annoyance like in the original games. The Rocket Launcher takes care of them easily, and ever faster with with the lock-on upgrade.

This game was very challenging at times, but it's all about moving, and using your weapons effectively. It also helps to explore the environment to find secrets and extra armor/health to aid you. The chainsaw is limited this time around since it uses fuel, but upon using it, it'll make enemies explode lots of ammunition in the process. Plus, it's still just so satisfying to run up to a Baron of Hell and just f-ing MURDER it with the chainsaw. In fact, it's satisfying to kill any demon with the chainsaw regardless!

And the BFG...sweet jesus the BFG....it makes a return, and it's just as big and powerful as when we last saw it! Shooting the BFG at a horde of enemies and then watching them all explode into meaty chunks is still nothing but pure eye-candy.

On top of all that, this game runs pretty damn well right now. I have yet to dip below 60fps in pretty much every single situation i've been in so far no matter how many enemies and particles are on my screen at one time on ultra settings in 1080p! The game looks gorgeous as well. Not to mention that there's no more 60fps cap either. It's completely unlocked, provided you turned of Vsync. ID Software did a very good job with the technical aspect of the game, and it really shows while you play.

If you want to know my specs, just look at my profile. Other than that, i will keep updating this review as i play more of it, including the multiplayer and try out SnapMap. Do i recommend this game still? HELL YES I DO!

So far, this was absolutely worth the $60 for the campaign alone.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPROS

+ Adrenaline pumping gameplay

+ Very fun maps

+ Doing justice to the originals

+ 9 - 12 hours to beat

+ Good weapon designs

+ Demons looking badass

+ Metal soundtracks

+ Lots of customization (online)

CONS

- Can't skip the story

- Doomguy runs too slow for my taste

- Some weapons are a bit weak

- No workshop and SnapMap is way too limited

- Multiplayer mix of DOOM and Quake = GOOD, Halo with Cod = BAD


Conclusion

Level design in this game are godly ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ amazing, hell was one of the best I saw in a video game, looked similar to the atmosphere of Quake and old Doom, graphics are great, itech 6 is indeed a powerful engine. Boss battles are amazingly well made, creative, and for some, challenging. Secrets can be fun and can help you to survive, there's easter eggs well hided. Upgrades, runes, challenges can be a little weird at first but I think it was a good addition.

Weapon designs they all look powerful but some can feel a bit weak but they can still be useful for some situations. Hardest difficulty called Ultra-Nightmare, you unlock it by finishing the game. Basically if you die once, crash or quit, you start all over. You can see helmets from people who've been there and died, just like you, tells the date, time, how they got killed, which is pretty cool in my opinion.

There's some changes in the gameplay compared to the original. To proceed in the game you need to kill all demons in several arenas, like Painkiller and Serious Sam style. It's still really fun, though I would want to have a option to turn that off and the story, speedrun wouldn't be that great in my opinion as it is now, but hope updates could fix that.

played a bit with SnapMap and tried levels, often looked the same, dull and boring, I wanted to make my own. Did all the tutorials and it's not that great. SnapMap is limited, you can't make your map from scratch and adding stuff as you go. I wanted my map to be really dark, making it scary and atmospheric, having spot lights here and there but SnapMap doesn't allow it. You won't be able to make a map outside, walking on Mars or hell. If you ever played Fallout 4 and tried to build your town and thought it was limited, it's really similar to SnapMap.

I would had loved to play for many decades with this version of DOOM for a change, with all sorts of mods, millions of maps to be played, but they don't believe that it's necessary. Me, I do believe that the new DOOM will die very soon if we don't get new contents like we used to with the classics, and also if we don't get a better multiplayer.

I want this new DOOM to be as good or even better than the originals, it has potentials. I'll wait for updates for the time being, I won't touch too much the multiplayer and Snapmap because I have no interest in their state. Though if you ever played the original DOOM, there's a high chance you will love the Single Player, and maybe the Multiplayer and Snapmap if you are into Cod and Halo, and you don't mind the limitations of SnapMap.

I give it a 8 out of 10,

Single Player is what makes this game a 8, multiplayer and Snapmap is the reason why my score is lower, if it wasn't the case I would had given easily a 9 or even a 10. I'm disappointed by the fact I might not play it again and maybe return to the originals once more. The developers are wasting this game for not giving his full potential, a 22 year old game can make more stuff than Snapmap, seriously?

I just hope they wake up, it could make old franchises coming back, and that fast paced genre. The only reason I can see why they don't want to give us a real editor is because they wouldn't make any money with us. Because mods, maps, and everything, could be better than what they would sell to us. They know SnapMap is limited, so when they will release a expansion or a dlc, we would bite and give them money to get something fun again with this game. Anyways, all I can do is wait and see what happens... So far they ignore us.\t\t\t\t\t
