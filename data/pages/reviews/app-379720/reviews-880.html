\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was recommended to me by my friend. I initially thought DOOM 2016 was gonna be just your run of the mill shoot demons and win. Well after completing the game boy was I right! it does have you shooting demons but you feel so badass doing it!

I'll be the first to admit, anytime I play an FPS I would normally play from afar, snipers and rifles are more my speed but with DOOM, glory kill mechanics aside, I found myself just charging into the heat of things to smash demons in the face! its amazing how immersive this game makes you feel. DOOM Guy is the ultimate chad in gaming, let no one tell you otherwise! fall from really insane heights? "impact compensation" betchhhhhhhh...whats this? a cyber demon from hell trying to stop me? here have a chainsaw through your gut its ridiculously intense and the balancing in this game is top notch. Tie it up with some badass metal combat music and you really do feel like you're a demon slaying bad ass!

The level design isnt your usual corridor type shooter like your standard FPS and i like how the game lets you just explore places, of course its within a constrained area but its a big enough sandbox to let you stretch your feet up and about without making it feel like a limitation.

There is also a story to be had here, albeit pretty basic but it does it job well to keep you invested enough. Voice acting for DOOM guy is what I expected from a chad of his stature.

This game is fun, and really, the fact that the devs dont reset your gun progression if you die is just icing on the cake man. They want you to have fun with this game. get it when its on sale since its a 2016 game it'll go for superrrrr cheap but you'll get your value worth. I guarantee it!

Cant wait to play eternal soon! 10/10!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn regards to single player:
Exactly what a sci-fi, FPS, video game should be.

Absolute blast to play! Full of blood pumping action. Has one feeling like a total bad ass as one destroys demons here, there, and everywhere. Great metal sound track. Dynamic music which picks up during battles.

95% of plot is told while playing rather than while watching cut scenes. Within 10 seconds of starting you got a gun in your hand and start playing.

Each weapon can have 2 special attachments mods (eg. rocket launcher can have a 3x lock on charge or explode in mid air)

There are secrets to find, and they are cleverly hidden and sometimes hard to find, but that's OK because they are secrets. Secrets for armor/collectables/weapon upgrades are not required for progression in the normal difficulty, "hurt me plenty".

The path forward on missions is cleverly indicated with green hues on edges or green lights bulbs that make sense for the environment showing the correct path forward. There is also a compass helping to show distance and direction to objective in typical Bethesda fashion. And if all else fails there is a map.

Codex and lore entries are interesting and contain plenty of dark humor but are not required reading to get through the game.

No annoying XP system or "XP on kill/exploration pop ups" -it's soo annoying when games do that!

Not punishing when you die due to checkpoint system letting you re-spawn just moments before major fights.
