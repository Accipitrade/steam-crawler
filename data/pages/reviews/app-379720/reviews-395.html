\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is probably the best action oriented game out there. I would definitely recommend.
The story is very interesting, but I would not say that it is the best part. The music is also fantastic.
The main part of the game is in the action. First off, it is a challenge, but in a good way. Some games are so challenging, that only hardcore players can play. But I would say that most players would be able to play Doom. There are five difficulties from "I'm Too Young To Die" to "Ultra-Nightmare", so it gives a range for any player type.
I have no problem with the mechanics. I like the jumping, sprinting, etc, and it's super fast paced, which can be fun.
The weapon selection is great, and I feel like the game purposefully gives you different scenarios where different weapons work on different enemies and such. The process for upgrading weapons seems fair, and ammo pick up drops seem fair for the most part.
Basically, if you want to feel like a demon slaying machine, then Doom is the game for you. (Definitely a rated M game though.)\t\t\t\t\t
