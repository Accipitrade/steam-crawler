\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBuy it, it's excellent. If you don't enjoy it, you have bad taste (now objectively proven) and I'm sorry to be the one to break it to you.

Doom looks at a decade of gritty grey cover based linear shooters and does the opposite.

Besides reinventing a genre entirely, Doom does something games rarely do and the fps genre simply never does. Doom isn't afraid to be a game. You have the secret rooms, the collectibles, the health and armor just laying about.

They took the chores out of the game and left only the fun.

The world is colorful and full of details, the levels are huge and worth exploring every corner, enemies are all deadly, with amazing animations and even personalities.

Although I will say I'm tired of the art direction, it feels like it's been kicking around since Gears of War. Doomguy looks pretty forgettable, the guns are kinda over-designed tech blobs and so on.

I'm writing this after the Doom Eternal trailers have been around and you can see clearly side by side that Id thought the same, the new iterations of guns have way more individuality. Certainly no reason to miss this game. But valid for a review I guess.

Combat is hard. Nowhere in a level is safe, there's no crows nest, nowhere to cheese the enemies. No matter what size- they can get to you, and they are never far behind. You might get the idea of "You can't run or hide" but having that mindset is the mistake. In Doom, you are the one doing the hunt. Every encounter is deadly, but you are deadlier, and it's all the more rewarding because of it.

Anyway, that's enough reading. Just buy it already.\t\t\t\t\t
