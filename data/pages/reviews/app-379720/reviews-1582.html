\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen I was a kid, I got my very first PC from my dad. Doom (classic) was one of the pre-installed game inside it.

Of course I was just a kid, the dark atmosphere inside it scared the ♥♥♥♥ out of me so I just played it rarely.

Fast forward 20+ years later, a friend recommended me DOOM (2016) and I was in luck because it was discounted on Steam. So I bought it.

My first impression was, this game is amazing. The shooting pace was very very fast, and the graphics are brutal and yet glorious. It was nowhere like the Doom I remember, but I can feel the soul of it. Every demon is satisfying to kill, especially Cacodemons (because I hate them very much). Weapon selection is great. Sure the demons' population are quite rare at times, so you have nothing to kill. The puzzles are also sometimes need a bit of brainpower to solve. But overall, it feels great when you solve those.

Final score: 9/10.

I would totally recommend this to anyone who is into fast pace FPS and demon killing.
