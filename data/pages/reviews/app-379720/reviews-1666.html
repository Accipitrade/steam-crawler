\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is pretty fun, but the constant collecting and completing challenges in each map really slows down the game play. It wouldn't be so annoying if it weren't tied to the weapon damage and power ups for your upgrades. After beating a level and going back to collect things I missed I can fly through them once learning the layout. I played the arcade mode only once, so I can't really comment on that. The multiplayer is fast paced when you are playing, but there is a lot of waiting in between maps and many times they are over too quick. The menus feel very unpolished as well. They buried some achievements deep into the multiplayer which is pretty annoying. There are a lot of dropped connections and lag can be a real issue when you're playing. Getting dropped when you're playing requires a complete game restart for me, as the game disconnects from STEAM entirely. But when it all goes smoothly it works well and is very enjoyable. I've spent a lot of my play time in multiplayer but I'd say only 60% of that time was spent playing, and it seems like the last two achievements will never be gotten at this rate. If you don't care about those type of things then it won't bother you. Overall I've had fun, and would recommend the game. I'm maybe 50-60% through the game and after my first play through I'll probably spend more time in arcade mode, I don't think my opinion will change much from this point on. I will more then likely end up buying DOOM ETERNAL when it comes out.

P.S. - If you are looking to get the multiplayer achievements then now is the time to start. The majority of them can be gotten in a few days. The scene isn't that busy, and once the new DOOM comes out I'm sure it will die completely.\t\t\t\t\t
