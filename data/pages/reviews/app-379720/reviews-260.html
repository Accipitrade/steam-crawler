\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever been a fan of DOOM but I've always had a respect for it since it's original launch (the 90s were real, man.)

Game's been a blast so far. Even the "Easy" difficulty it's not as piss easy. Unless you're an ultra-violent nut job that ain't got time to bleed.

I can see that this game pays homage to original 90s predecessor. Giving you the fast pace action with some modern adjustments. Callbacks to the originals such as collectibles and secrets, but, not too much where it'd feel intrusive and doesn't distract from the overall scheme of the game.

Story is more for the player and not for the Slayer. He really doesn't care other than killing demons.

Haven't really come across any thing that hindered my gameplay. Like, I didn't get stuck/trapped on geometry or anything. Environments are pretty polished. Visuals are great giving you a story that's also not told, but, you can pick up on if you're willing to look.

I bought this on sale alongside DOOM Eternal (another game I plan on playing after I beat DOOM).

Enemy designs are great, but, I do feel Hell Knight's design is a bit of a downgrade compared to the 90's original. I dunno, something like add some "shoulder horns" or something. Kinda looks like a beefed up version of an Imp. But, I get they wanted to differentiate a Hell Knight from a Baron.

The OST is just perfect.

I like the puzzle element of some environments that make you do a little more outside-the-box thinking. Like those levers that'll open up an original DOOM room.

The Difficulty Settings are great catering to those who are new to those who have become seasoned and want to test their valor.

Overall, great game.\t\t\t\t\t
