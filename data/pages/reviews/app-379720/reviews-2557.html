\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMost remasters or reboots of various older games made are either plagued with gameplay problems, and hope you ignore that because of the nostalgia element, or are just not fun/not faithful to the original gameplay style. This game is NOT one of those.

I'm not a fan of the doom series, but I don't need to be to like this game. The classy gameplay of 'bad*** marine killing demons from hell with huge weapons' works perfectly. Your shotgun effectively deals with smaller threats, and is my main weapon of choice! The assault rifles and chainguns take care of long range threats and crowds of enemies! The heavy weapons such as the railgun, rocket launcher, and BFG effectively kill anything that looks funny in your general direction! The enemies are moving fast constantly, coordinating at times, trying to kill you just as much as you want to kill them! Your character moves fast, just like the old games, had all their weapons instead of the modern shooter 'two weapon' loadout, and even a chainsaw makes enemies dispense ammunition from their SEVERED NECK STUMPS! The gameplay is challenging and very fun, almost too fun. Seriously, I still get weirdly comforting warm chills run up my spine whenever I rip an imp's head off...

The graphics are definetly up to par with modern games, so be warned that older gaming rigs will not be able to handle this game effectively. I'm not sure what more there is to say. The graphics are really good, so much so that I don't notice them too much. I may have a problem in this area...Lets not dwell on that.

The soundtrack is the most amazing mix of heavy metal and techno I have ever heard in my life, and suits the fast-running-gunning-jumping-ripping-tearing-bowing-the-f***-uppy gameplay absolutely perfectly.

I'm having a hard time even coming with problems because this game nails every major aspect without any major issues. The campaign is very fun, the gameplay is fun, the graphics are superb, the music is perfect, and it even has snapmap modes which allow you to create your own scenarios/maps, and multiplayer.

Overall, this game is worth EVERY PENNY of it's asking price. If you liked the original doom, or have ever had any sort of fun with any shooter, no matter how old or new, you will like this game. 10/10 hands down.

This is what a triple A game should be!\t\t\t\t\t
