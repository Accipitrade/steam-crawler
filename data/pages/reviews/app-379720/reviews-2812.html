\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTestosterone Low?

Do little kids beat you up on the play ground?

Do you often wonder if you should be Vegan?

Does the answer to that last question worry you as a man and or woman?

Well then Doom is the game for you, it will solve all your ills, it will cure all your pains... Or youll die trying.

The hyper masculine doom guy returns to hop scotch his way through hell and slaughter (indiscriminately) the hordes of hell itself. If I was asked, “Jerky, do you think well get a good Wolfenstein and doom game in as few of years? Both harkening back to the originals?” I would have said, “Youre ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ insane!” And perhaps you still are, but Doom is real, and its so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ metal that for the first several moments of the game you will assume you’re stuck in the cover of a Iron Maiden album, forced to fight your way through the reanimated corpses of lesser men as you use their bones to pave your way across the surface of the red planet.

I give it 14 Disturbed before they sold out out of 11 Black Sabbaths.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast as hell action broken up by exploring really excellent environments.

The gameplay loop of go to an area, kill all demons, find secrets, repeat is finely polished and the stream of new guns and demons makes it always feel fresh.

Weapon balance is very good, and limited ammo means you'll be weapon swapping constantly as the situation changes.

The optional challenges I could take or leave. They make you change up your approach in interesting ways but are sometimes tedious. Fortunately you by no means need to do them, as most of your weapons upgrade points come from just fighting demons well.

Man this game looks good. Like, really good, and it's well optimized as far as I can tell. Run's way better than a lot of worse looking games I play.

The characters (all 3 of them) are interesting and well voice acted, not that they needed to be. The story is fantastic in that it's basically just "demons are here kill all demons", then "go to hell and kill the demons there".

Not planning on playing any multiplayer so can't speak to that, but the campaign is easily worth picking up on it's own.

Excellent game highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
