\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is Doom, as it should be. Old school FPS gaming with modern graphics and with just enough RPG elements to enhance gameplay without bogging the game down. Bravo, Id Software. I played the original Doom a LOT, and this game does an absolutely stellar job of bringing Doom into the 21st Century. The game is an absolute hoot, just like the original, with all of the necessary modern additions/enhancements, and no unnecessary/cumbersome baggage that so often comes with modern games. With only a few exceptions, I recognize every beastie in this game as a modern version of the old monsters, with similar tactics for dealing with them. The imps are there. The zombie soldiers are there. The Hell Knights, the Barons of Hell, the Cacodemons, the Lost Souls...they're all there. Again, great job, Id.\t\t\t\t\t
