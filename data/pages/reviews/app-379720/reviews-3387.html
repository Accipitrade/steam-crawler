\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay, I've tried all 3 modes, so I'm gonna give this my utmost review. I rarely review games, but this one I just had to...
Single Player
I cannot express how much fun I am having on single player, and learning the story of Doomguy (Doom Marine).
I started my fight on Ultra Violence, and I have to say, it's harder than a Barons dense ♥♥♥♥♥! I cannot wait to try out Nightmare and maybe give Ultra Nightmare a try.
The gameplay is a fast-paced, adrenaline rushed joyride of demon slaying joyfulness.
SP - 10/10
Multiplayer
I waited until I played campaign to go back to Multiplayer, and I truly had a BLAST in the Beta.
After playing a few matches and a few game modes (Warpath, Team Deathmatch, Soul Harvest), I found that some game modes have barely anyone playing. The only one [gamemode] people are playing is Team Deathmatch, which makes the other game modes harder to try. Either nobody is really playing or the matchmaking isn't much good(?)
Also, I do have to say I would like better team matching, I was in a team of Me (level 1), two level 2's, two other 1's, and a level 5 against 2 prestiges, and 3 other high levels. Obviously our team had barely any experience with the maps so they were picking up everything before us and dominated us.
Better team making is obviously what I'd look for, but other than that I enjoyed myself. Yeah, I have yet to be a demon, too. Every time I'd slay the enemy demon I'd die and miss the oppurtunity.
MP - 7.5/10
SnapMap
I messed around with making my own map, without learning first, and that's a HUGE mistake. LEARN THROUGH THE ACADEMY BEFORE TRYING TO MAKE YOUR OWN MAP.
When I went to make my own, I wanted to just see what I was able to do, and there are ENDLESS possibilities. The amount of tools, accessories, demons, and just large and small items to be placed is so vast. I can't wait to learn more about it.
SnapMap 9/10

My overall experience has been outstandingly beautiful.
I give this game an overall rating of a 10/10, because between the nostalgia, and the new-age, and all the easter eggs, this game is so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ fun and worth every penny that's been spent on it.\t\t\t\t\t
