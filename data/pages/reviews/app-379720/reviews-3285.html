Overall the game really is beautiful. It gives you that adrenaline rush and literal edge of your seat experience.
Definitely consider picking this up.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is a very fun game to play with friends, not as much fun without,

PROS:
- Pretty good campaign, very fun.
- No lag as far as i can tell in multiplayer.
- SnapMap is a great addition for the doom community.
- Very epic, action packed, and looks amazing.
- Customizable character and weapons
- Monsters are awesome

CONS:
- Multiplayer still needs work
- If you don't have a decent gaming computer, you might have a hard time running it
- Repetitive arenas
- Repetitive mission structure
- Could have added more to the story (even though i'm not done with the campaign, it could still use work)
- Could need some more work/updates orverall, and mabye add more DLCs to the game.
- Needs bigger community

CONCLUSION:
Overall the game is great, it's fun but it still needs some work in some areas, and in other areas, the developers nailed it and made me hyped for it just from a demo/sneak peak of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
