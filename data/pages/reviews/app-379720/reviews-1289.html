\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBEST GAME OF 2016

Doom has by far my favourite type of combat, fast paced action arena shooter, the only game i played that did this style of combat better is DOOM Eternal. It is a great shooter that surprised me when it came out, with its constant action and non stop demon killing. They made the story of doom not that important and heavily focused on the fun part of the game, the gameplay. The game is also very graphic, with lots of ripping and tearing happening. This accompanied with Mick Gordon's kick ass soundtrack you will feel like a unstoppable killing machine that cant be stopped. The graphics look great and there is barely any fps spikes.


However, the game is way too easy. On ultra-violence i barely even died, especially with the final few levels. i believe its because the player has way to much ammo, and not as powerful and dangerous demons. not to mention the runes can be quite powerful (e.g. Rich get Richer) which gives you infinite ammo if you have over 75 armour, that's if you have it upgraded. The weapons also are way to overpowered, you can clear out a whole room of demon with nothing but a super shotgun or fully upgraded assault riffle with micro missiles attachment. making the whole game a cake walk. The game is way more fun if you switch between different weapons constantly\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you liked classic doom, you'll enjoy the fast paced combat, satisfying weapons, and overall violence*.

If you liked doom 3, you'll enjoy the level design and (a bit) more focus on story than doom/doom II.

If you haven't played doom before, this is a reboot and you won't be lost at all. Gameplay-wise, combat is a bit like an aggressive halo while the opportunity for exploration gives it a metroid prime-esque feel at times. Various difficulty settings and optional tutorials makes it a very user friendly experience.


*The game is very violent and bloody even compared to previous doom games so you might get squeamish if you're not used to that sort of thing.\t\t\t\t\t
