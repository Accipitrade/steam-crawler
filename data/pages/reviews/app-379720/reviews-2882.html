I started off playing Doom 2016 slower, trying to save ammo and position myself for engagements. But the game does a great job of encouraging you to just run and gun - you'll die if you don't. You soon find yourself conquering hords of demons without even thinking twice before running into battle. Very fast and fun!

I think the story is even decently interesting, it kept things moving along without actually getting in the way of the fun. I also think the minimalist upgrade / inventory system helps keep things simple while also having a few bits of modern gameplay thrown in.

I did play this on a controller on the PC - due to some RSI issues with keyboard and mouse best kept to a minimum - and after a transition period found it to be a lot of fun. I was sort of crappy online, but not quite as bad as I'd thought I would be.\t\t\t\t\t
