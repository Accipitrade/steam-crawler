\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed the campaign first time in ultra-violence just under 13 hours. Combat was very fast and fluid felt like you couldn't stop for a second or you'd be killed. Powerups were also very enjoyable and they really gave brief feeling of ultimate power on you. I was hoping the campaign to be a bit longer and feel like co-op campaign would have make this game 11/10 for me. There is still more to play in higher difficulties and to get all the upgrades. Also haven't checked so much into snapmap system but looked like there is some co-op maps which we will try out with my friends. Haven't touched the multiplayer and probably wont so I can't give info on that.
If you are looking for some fresh, fast and very skill based fps with powerups for singleplayer I wouldn't hesitate to recommend Doom for you.
Waiting Doom to be continued with more campaign.\t\t\t\t\t

The new DOOM made me realise that my new GTX1080, which costed around the same as the GDP of a small African nation, is well worth the investment.

Gameplay 8/10
A little repetitive since every encounter is effectively a locked room in which you have to kill all the demons to unlock the next room. Still great tho.

Story 8/10
Plot is similar to most modern day SciFi horror.

Graphics 11/10
The new 1080 GTX allowed me to play DOOM in 4K with everything turned to Ultra. With Adaptive VSync, even tho the fps got locked at 30 fps, it's still mindblowingly pretty.

Stability 7/10
My only complain is the stability of the game. so far it crashed 3 times. Just suddenlty quit and back to desktop, no crash report, nothing. Also, a few places where textures seems to be missing. Instead of seeing the actual texture, all I saw was a patch of whiteness.

Overall 9/10 - Highky recommended
