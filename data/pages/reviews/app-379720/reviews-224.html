I loved the story, the horror and the graphics. Doom 2016 has amazing graphics, excelent sound design and fantastic music tracks. The gameplay is also really great, smooth and fun! The glory kills are also awesome! I was afraid they will feel forced or QTE-like, but not at all. Exploration, character development via runes, weapon and suit upgrades, secrets, oldschool levels.. An excellent game and a must play for FPS fans! On the negative side, I didn't care about the snap map and I wasn't able to find any multiplayer matches at all. I guess everyone moved to play Eternal, just as I will. :) Also, it's a AAA game, but has very badly set up collisions in many areas of the SP campaign. I was able to pass through many props several times. Some of the collision meshes are too small and the camera clips through the objects. There are also small gaps between meshes where you can look outside of the world. Cheap mistakes that would be easy to fix. Other than these I had no game breaking bugs at all. A well polished game!\t\t\t\t\t
