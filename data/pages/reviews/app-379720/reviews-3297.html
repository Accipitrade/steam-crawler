\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere's the situations in which you buy doom.
1)COD is too technical of a shooter for you
2)You want a game as story driven as Monopoly
3)You want to play a game with pretty, albeit bloody graphics
4)You're tired of shooters that require mechanics
5)You just bought a collection of metal and hard rock and when you listen to is when play CS:GO you die a lot

Out of all of these though, this is the most important
5)You need something to de-stress to and pass some time in a fast paced, fun, well made and crafted game

This game doesn't do much. It's not genre defining, it doesn't bring gaming to a whole new level, and it certainly does not create any katharsis with the players. What it does do is create one of the most fun to play games ever. It's simple, but that's exactly what Doom is supposed to be. The only other Doom game I've played is the origional doom and let me tell you, this is exactly like that. you rarely aim up or down, you just side strafe and run backwards around bosses and run through smaller enemies and trample them under your steely boots.

Really, this is what classic games are made out of. Games like the origional Doom, Wolfenstein, Halo:CE, and even Mario Bros are still today considered classic games even though their mechanics were very rudementary and their graphics terrible by today's standards. Doom captures this same form. It's a simple game that you can have fun playing through every time like those older games, it just has great graphics.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere's pretty much no reason for me to write this review, but I'm going to anyway.

This game is what happens when a legendary developer does not become a crippled abomination over time. Growing fantastically into the new age of next generation gaming, id brings yet another one-ton diamond to the table with one of the most replayable, entertaining, satisfying shooters of all time.

The weapons are punchy, the executions are fast and brutal, and few games will give you the sense of classic joy that this game brings. Run and gun, blast demons back to Hell, all to the tune of sick metal riffs. If we were worried that this Doom would follow the path of Doom 3, we can set those worries aside.

Multiplayer is wicked fast and great fun, requiring speed, prediction, and coordination.

The Snap Map is brilliant, and I can't wait for updates or DLC to add new parts and pieces to play with.

That said, sometimes the formula gets a little bit stale in both visuals and set pieces. In general, you walk around, look for secrets, stumble upon this hellish portal looking thing, rip a flesh blob off to shut it down, and then fight the inevitable demon wave. This is basically on repeat. Despite the beautiful visual effects, there's so much red and orange that you'll actually notice when things are different colors. This can lead to occasionally stale environments, which don't combo well with the aforementioned "shut down portal, fight monsters, repeat."

Regardless, you'll be hard pressed to notice its few missteps when you're curb stomping a demon with his own leg (that is an actual thing you can do), or desintegrating the upper half of a huge demon with a two-handed gauss cannon.

Buy it. You won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMuch better than expected! Doom is back with gory vengence!

Doesn't seem to like my AMD FirePro graphics cards that are two years old too much (yes, I know they are NOT designed for gaming, but they game very well on other games at 1080p), but that seems to be an optimization that can be made in a future update to drivers.

UPDATE: Seems that lack of SLI/Crossfire support is the reason my cards are stuggling. Until an update is put out that enables SLI and Crossfire, the game may struggle for some gamers. I noticed there was a lot of talk across the web of poor optimization for AMD cards as well. This does not surprise me because if anyone remembers the launch of Fallout 4, support for AMD GPUs was not very good either.

Either way, this game is great so far! Would I like better FPS? Hell yeah, but for now I'll live. Buy this game!\t\t\t\t\t
