\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic game. I purchased it at the $19.99 price point and definitely got my money's worth, though I'm not sure I'd play through it again. The music is a great homage to the original Bobby Prince soundtrack....you can definitely hear the influence at times. The atmosphere is perfect. The weapons feel great. The story felt appropriate and does a decent job of building a sense of history around the world and character. The upgrade system is cool. At times this plays like a Metroid Prime, though with a much darker edge.

My only real complaint is it starts to get tedious after a while. Eventually you realize you are moving from one carefully designed arena to another, fighting off the demon spawns, rinse and repeat, onward to the end. This kind of design starts to kill the sense of exploration, and becomes a chore to get through. I got tired of the slog, looked up the cheat codes for god mode, and just plowed through to the end just so I could be done with the whole experience.\t\t\t\t\t
