\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEasily ranks as the top shooter I have ever played. First time I've finished an FPS and immediately wanted to hit 'New Game' and start again.

- Stunning visuals, great use of colour. Also well-lit, and I'm not a fan of games that are too dark to see.
- Highly optimised. Didn't think it would work so smoothly at 60fps on my laptop, but it did
- Great variety of weaponry which allows you to identify your playstyle and favoured weapon mods (super shotgun and precision-bolt gauss cannon are my favourites).
- The game dispenses with all the cutscene bs that has dragged down a lot of action shooters in the past, and jumps straight to the point: Non-stop Action and Violence all the way.
- The goriness of glory killing is oddly beautiful.

It's hard to think of any negatives, they got everything spot-on. But one criticism is that whilst the glory kill system was definitely a high-point of this game, I think it was possibly a bit dumbed-down, especially on higher-difficulties. When an enemy is staggered they glow blue/orange to make it super obvious - which felt like it was geared to casual gamers. And as soon as you are close enough, it is a bit too easy to glory kill. I don't know how to improve on this, but I felt they could have got rid of the blue/orange glow on harder difficulties, and somehow made it more difficult to pull off a glory kill, e.g. reduce the stagger time or the kill radius.

Overall, solid 10/10

Can't comment on the multiplayer, was only interested in the singleplayer.\t\t\t\t\t
