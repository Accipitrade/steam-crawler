\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs much as people think this is actually the DOOM formula. It's not the DOOM I remember, at all. This version is an Arena shooter, with double jump mechanics and kick ass large guns (that's not what the original DOOM was). The kick ass large weapons, is what I like..however, what I don't like, is the Arena style they went for. Where everything kind of just looks the same in DOOM 2016, with great graphics of course. Many people think that this DOOM is more like the Original DOOM. Well here's some experiences I remember from the original DOOM that i NEVER felt out of DOOM 2016, but i DID feel with DOOM 3.

There WAS loads of dark areas, in the original DOOM. There was slow Zombie like demons, with shotguns and pistols(like DOOM 3). There was night vision goggles, in the original(the flashlight in DOOM 3). ALL the areas in the original game, every single level felt COMPLETELY different to the level before it. Every level had a unique thing about it, in the originals. Whereas the new DOOM, feels like the same level , all the way through and to be honest, there's some annoying feeling levels in this new version, to me.

The original had sprawling , interesting, Toxic Waste levels and labyrinth style levels....The new one doesn't. The new one, feels like its one giant area after another.
The original actually felt (if you actually played it properly, the way it was meant to be played, with no brightness turned up on the moniter or anything like that or cheats) like an action survival horror game. Matter of fact, i found MORE things in common in DOOM 3 and the originals, than the new DOOM and the originals. Health didnt spew out in the originals, health spews out in DOOM 2016.

Its a fun game, lots of hardcore blood and gore action and lots of JUMPING, but it DOES make me wonder what DOOM 4, was going to be like now and ill never ever truly know, which sux.

I am actully one of the few people, that preferred DOOM 3, to DOOM 2016. I enjoyed the levels more, in DOOM 3, over DOOM 2016.
I even found many more comparisons with the original DOOM and DOOM 3 , more so than this..But if im in the minority, im in the minority. My test to see if i REALLY like a game, is can I replay it over and over and over? for me, the answer is no to DOOM 2016, all the levels just feel similar and a bit boring to me, played it once, uninstalled. But i CAN play the originals over and over and DOOM 3 over and over. Because i Feel all the levels in the original DOOM and DOOM 3 FELT different and interesting and unique to each other.\t\t\t\t\t
