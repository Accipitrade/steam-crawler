I mean the game is good I won't lie but I don't find it MY type of game. The multiplayer felt very Cliché cheesy FPS multiplayer I am not into, and the game gets boring quick. The reason I was so hyped for it was because I had a new pimped out GPU which was more than ready to play it and I played it and BOOM DOOM was GOOOD! Nice fast paced campaign that was quite satisfying but in the future I cannot find myself playing this but I can see why people love it. If you are into RPG open world like Fallout 4, Skyrim, Witcher 3, Metal Gear and those story rich games then this is not the game for you but if you don't really mind and like those games including the not so Story Rich enclosed games as well as open world games or neither or just the enclosed ones I can see you liking it with the majority of people. But this review is simply to tell people whether it's worth a buy or not and it is definitely worth a buy as I also supported great developers to continue to make great games like these for the lovely steam community! I may not like it but who cares about me if the majority likes it YES buy it it's still a great game!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest. Remake. Ever.

Seriously. The game is perfect. It keeps true to the Classic DOOM way by being very serious with subtle jokes, secrets and easter eggs, and even unlockable levels from the actual Classic DOOM games. If that wasn't enough, there's even an option to have the weapons centered, like the Classic DOOM had.

If you haven't played much of the originals, or if you played them over and over, this is probably the best game of the year.

I have played the entire story, and I only encountered two issues.
1) The pistol animation cannot work as fast as the weapon can fire. I'm not sure if this is an issue on my end, but I never used the pistol, so it didn't matter.
2) One time while doing a Glory Kill, the animation popped me inside a box I could get out from. I had to restart from Checkpoint, but the Checkpoin was at the start of the fight, so I really didn't mind either.

This is an awesome game.\t\t\t\t\t
