\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game sucks ass. It is so repetitive that it will make you lose actual brain cells. Shoot at enemy, wait for shiny animation, press super cool execute button and watch one of three execution animations that you will see around 500.000 times during your playthrough. Rinse and repeat this in Maps that have absolutely no charm and all look the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ same. The guns feel weightless and gunplay is incredibly bad for a game that only has gunplay going for it. The music in this game is laughable and is supposed to make you feel like the ultimate badass. In reality, it makes me feel ashamed for playing this. As you probably already know, the "story" is total garbage and can barely be described as a story, but I guess that is part of the franchise. Rarely was I so disappointed by a game, and I only had mediocre expectations going into it. Even if you are just looking for an FPS Game to shoot at some ♥♥♥♥, which is what I was doing when buying the game, then this will still be a disappointment due to the bad maps, cringey music and the unexciting weapon handling.

If you are not a DOOM fanboy and you like to have your brain stimulated at least a little bit while playing games, then this won't be worth your time.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game in 2016, but never ended up playing it. I recently got a new PC and decided to play it after seeing some amazing clips of the sequel. So glad that I did. This game is non-stop action and will often leave you breathless after a battle. The weapons are varied, runes provide multiple possible play styles, controls feel snappy. While the story is very simple, everything around the gameplay and combat are so well-imagined.

One gripe with the game is that the platforming is pretty awkward and kind of annoying at times. I also encountered a couple of glitches - an achievement didn't unlock and a glitch prevented me from ending the battle with the final boss and had to reload the checkpoint to try again. Not great, but also didn't ruin the experience or anything.

Overall, Doom is a fantastic action game and one of the best shooters I've played. I picked up Eternal when it was on sale last weekend and can't wait to jump into it.\t\t\t\t\t
