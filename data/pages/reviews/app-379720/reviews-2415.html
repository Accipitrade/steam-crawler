\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love almost everything about this game but it has one glaring and (in my opinion) game breaking flaw: the sound mixing. It's just horrendous. It's among the worst I've heard in any game. The levels and eq are just so off. Guns sound so quiet, while other sounds that are supposed to be quiet are loud. It is not my setup either, I have played it with my top notch audio setup, though a tv, through headphones, etc to no avail. Compare this game's sounds with Doom 3's, the originals or other games and they just don't add up. The soundtrack is good though!

This might seem like a nit-pick, but it's really not. A game about getting you pumped to kill demons should have exciting sound effects. These weak sounds really drain alot of the impact and fun out of the game. Like I said, almost everything else is good about this game.

One other gripe I have with this game is with the art style, it's not a deal breaker, but I didn't really like the more cartoony. playfull looking demons. The graphical fidelity is amazing, but I am not huge on the creature design. I prefer the dark atmosphere and demon designs of Doom 3. I think they took this one in a little too cartoony of a direction. If this game had better sound mixing and a grittier art style it would be flawless in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
