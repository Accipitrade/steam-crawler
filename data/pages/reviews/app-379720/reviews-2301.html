\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen I was younger I played Doom 3 and really liked it but Doom 3 is a rather slow pace game. Watching the trailers for Doom (2016) I hoped it would be a fast pace Doom 3 and this is exactly what we got. Even though one got to admit that this game is not as scary as its predecessor.

Soundtrack

The music feels really rough and this goes well with the gameplay. Blasting this music makes you feel like you are the coolest kid in town and puts you in the right mood to make daemons regret to have ever crossed your path.

Gameplay

Since this game is fast pace, it has to feel fluent and luckily this is what we get. the gunplay is something that you have to feel for yourself. it only takes very little time getting used to and once you get the hang of it, you will be enthralled.

Story

I actually hoped for a more compelling story but is not bad either. Is is okay but nothing special, let's put it this way.

Graphics

I have played the game at the end of 2018 and even though now we have a lot of games that look even better, I really enjoyed the graphics of doom. Especially the use of colour in this game is great.

Replayabillity

Although the gameplay feels smooth, the level design could be better. To be perfectly fair, the levels are great but they are lacking uniqueness. I was hoping to experience a more versatile level design. So the replayability value rather stems from the enjoyment of the gameplay and not so much because of certain levels

Summary

To put it in a nuts shell, Doom 3 is a great gaming experience. The gameplay will not fail you, the guns and glory kills are always fun. The story and the level design could be both more complex, however I am still satisfied with what we got in this game.

Recommended: 8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
