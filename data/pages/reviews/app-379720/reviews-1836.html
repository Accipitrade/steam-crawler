\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is hands down one of the best fps shooters of this generation. With it's smooth, fast paced and brutal combat it's a blast to play through and with one of the most fitting soundtracks ever it's something you can't get enough of.

Soundtrack:

I have never heard a soundtrack that's fits a game so good in my entire life. The music is so essential to the game in a way if you just but a normal sci-fi soundtrack over this game it just wouldn't work. Everything you do also changes the way the music plays if you do a glory kill it slows down if there's not alot of demons it plays a more calmer part of the song but if your right in the middle of all the chaos with a chainsaw it blasts the $h1t out of the heavy parts.

Gameplay:

DOOM's combat is simple to it's core easy to learn but hard to master. It's not like many other games you always gotta be moving you can't just be standing around but with the fast pace nature of the game why would you even wanna stand still.
Every time go in to a demon filled area it's all about strategy f*ck up once and your dead. The game is never boring it always keeps it fresh with new harder enemys and new big guns.
The combat is so good it just feels so nice to just shoot a demons f*cking face off or do a glory kill which means to just destroy a demon with your bare hands. But's let's just face it in what other game can you punch a hole through a demons stomach and tear it's guts out then shove it down it's throat and Watch it explode while having Heavy metal playing.

DOOM is a perfect example of that you don't need long tedoius tutorials or signs and lines that say were you have to go. "It's hard to figure out were i have to go the gamer say's. f*ck you figure it out DOOM replies can't find your way
through hell what are you a b!tch"
There's also diffrent ways to play if you like exploration you can search around for different collectables and the game rewards you with getting powerful guns earlier or other things that make you more powerful.

Story:

DOOM'S story is pretty simple it's your standard humans find an alien (demon) resource try to use it it goes terribly wrong and you have to fix it. To put it in a perspective the story in DOOM is like the story in p*rn it doesn't really need to be there it's just there to give the f*cking more sense but in this case ripping off a demons horn out of it's head and smacking the f*cker in the face with it.
This all lead to Doomguy he's always in control he wants revenge, doesn't back down, doesen't sneak, doesn't hide thats for p*ssies you hear that Solid Snake.

Overall DOOM is an incredible game with a badass soundtrack and killer combat 9/10
