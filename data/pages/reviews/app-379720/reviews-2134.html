\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't know if it's the DRM software or just an awful bug with the game but this game completely FUBAR'd a Windows installation for me. No matter what I tried my PC would not connect to my network. I reset network settings, reinstalled drivers, and I even tried the other NIC (I had a mITX board with two NICs at the time) but nothing worked until I formatted the drive and reinstalled Windows. That was my second attempt to finish the game after it crashed during a major fight and corrupted my save file. I left it alone for a while after that, then tried to come back to it recently and realized it actually wasn't that great of a game in the first place. I don't think I'll ever end up finishing it.

Update: I tried yet again to play this game recently. I have built a new PC since the last time I tried to play this game and still I am experiencing strange crashes and freezes in this game that I don't experience in any other game. It's extremely frustrating to have to restart the game every hour or two. What I don't understand is why this game crashes so much more often than others. I have put thousands of hours into other games including many hundreds of hours in to Fallout and TES games with hundreds of mods installed, and the only time I've ever experienced this kind of instability is in Oblivion with 300+ mods installed.

However, I do have to revise my earlier opinion of the game itself. It's not my favorite but it's a good game, if you can put up with all the crashes.\t\t\t\t\t
