+ The graphic is awesome. Polished, dark themed, realism-oriented colors. Not like Pongo:Eternal and its rainbow of colors.

+ Combat is fantastic without being frustrating. No infinite hordes of demons that overrun your ammo capacity, glory kills made to save ammo, for fun or just because you really hate that demon. Not because you are constantly low health.

+ Upgrades. While I upgraded only the protection against enviromental damage, being able to increase health, armor and ammo or complete a challenge in order to unlock a passive (runes) it is a fantastic way to promote exploration and improves the gameplay

+ Secrets. Some are impossibile to find for me, I was never that good at finding them but they makes you think differently. In fact from just walking and running you may find yourself jumping from a platform to another in order to reach a secret location. Not just jumping on a secret while desperately trying to complete the level after tons of poles and jumps.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI went into this game with a neagative mindset. After all the hype of it's realease and watching the teaser gameplay footage, I thought they had tried to market an old game to a younger crowd. Sure they added a new soundtrack, added bright blue health orbs, green highlights on ammo, had the demons glow orange when you hit them, and added red, yellow and pink neon lights on all the demons.....pretty much a "Hell themed neon rave". Just trying to take Bank on my childhood Memories.

Picked it up on sale and thought WTH (pun intented) and loaded it up. Right away I liked how smooth and responsive the gameplay felt but wasn't very impressed untill about 35 minutes in. After about 35min in the game really opens up into what I can only describe as semi open world arenas. Every demon has its own Strenghts and weakness adding heartpounding stratgey as you shoot and move to stay alive. I've only played for 6.6 hrs at the time of this review. but this game flipped my neagtive feelings towards it in about a halfhour. It takes everything good from the 1993 Doom, and improves upon it to beome it's own amazing FPS experince!!

If you're on the fence about this one take the chance and pick it up. because this game went from being 'oh look they ruined my childhood", to "Now imma Let you finish....................but Doom's the best shooter of all time....all time!'\t\t\t\t\t
