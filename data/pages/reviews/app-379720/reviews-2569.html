\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most satisfying shooters that I have played in 20 years. Fast-paced action, great visual and sound design, a nice variety of enemies and amazing movement/controls that feel very polished. The level design is very old school, linear with colored gates and keys that lead to more linear areas of the map, but even though modern games tend to focus a bit more on open worlds and exploration, the linearity is not a bad thing. It allowed the developers to created more focused encounters with great gameplay as a result. There are also 'modern' aspects though, such as 'runes' (skills), suit (stat) upgrades and weapon upgrades. These allow for some variety in playstyle and increase replayability.

Oh, and let's not forget the soundtrack. It's the thing that convinced me to buy the game.. go check it out!\t\t\t\t\t
