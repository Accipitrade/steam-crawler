It's a fun experience, and something that every FPS fan, old and new alike, should try. It blends the features of old and new well, and plays more like an older game than a tribute to one. Pros:

It's pretty refreshing to have a AAA single player shooter that requires some strafeing.

Don't have to reload any clips jus like the good ol days.

The hell levels are very nice to look at and explore.

The gun play feels natural and fun just like in wolfenstein new order

The finishing melee kills don't entirely break the combat flow (I still feel like the game would work fine without it) and the speed of the melee kills can be increased by completing a challenge.


Cons:

This game requires some hefty hardware to run at a full 60 frames at all times. I've been getting 100fps (nothing happening) - 40fps (in the middle of a mosh pit) with v sync off on my gtx 770.

There are a couple of sit-still-and-listen-for-plot-sake moments that are kinda annoying to me.

You get these perks through challenges (nothing wrong with that) but you can only have three perks working at the same time in a sinle player game.
