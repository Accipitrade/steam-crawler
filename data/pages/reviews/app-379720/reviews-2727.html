\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh boy, what to say. First of all, i didn´t pay almost any attention to the game when it did it rounds at E3, honestly, i wasn´t expecting much, nowdays i´m nothing but sceptical towards AAA titles and i never had any of the original titles as a kid, i played Doom 3 and thats about it, so no nostalgia aspect for me. But damn. This game is a great one.

Doom is a solid run of fast phased destruction, what i really love about this game is the consept of combat, the basic idea is that if you stay put, you will die, you just can´t take that much damage before dying, so what you do is you keep moving, you´re running, jumping, climbing and teleporting all the time, constantly shooting at demons. The game offers a nice little mechanic in which the enemies you gun down go into this stagger mode for a short time, that allows you to execute them, which rewards health. This idea i think is brilliant, it straigh up forces you to move around like a ferret on cocaine while slaughtering demons as fast as you can, avoiding combat or taking it slow is hardly rewarded, so to keep yourself alive you have to rip apart your enemies. To enhace this type of gameplay, the maps are obviously constructed with this in mind, the maps are big, multi-dimensional arenas with a lot of ledges and platforms to traverse around.

Theres a lot of ways to dispose of the demon invaders, the arsenal of weapons you have is good enough, not too many of them, but not too little, and each weapon has two "alternative fire mods" available, for example, my personal weapon of choice, the shotgun has a firemode that rapidly fires three shotgun rounds off and a second one that shoots a granade, you can upgrade each of the alternate weapon mods with weapon points, and once you have all the upgrades, you get a challenge, do this or that with the gun to a certain enemy or so, which upon completion, makes the firing mode even more deadlier.

And throughout the game you will find different rune challenges, bunch of challenges that award you with runes, you can have up to three runes equiped, they all have different effects (more ammo from dead enemies, move faster after executing enemies, able to execute enemies from further away etc) and each of the runes can be upgrading by performing certain things, so theres a lot of stuff to spice up the combat. Each map also offers three sort of like combat objectives, like killing three demons by executing them from the air, killing a huge demon with just the pistol and that sort of stuff.

The maps are full of collectibles, little Doom guys just for the sake of collectibles, but also data logs that offer lore and background info, armor upgrading points, weapon modules and so forth, so exploring is rewarded.

Doom also has one of the best fitting (and most definitely the heaviest) soundtracks i have ever heard in a game.

And you cant get past the attitude of "Doom slayer" its so satisfying when you get a voice message saying you need to carefully disable a certain component, only to have him smash it to pieces by kicking it, theres a company on mars basically harvesting the energies of hell for resources, but its apparent that the main caracter doesn´t give any ♥♥♥♥♥ about the wellbeign or existance of this company, nothing else matters to him then mercilessly destroying any form of demons and obstacles he faces.

Doom is exceptionally fun to play, its gruesome, fast and deadly, the most entertainging straight up singleplayer, non open world or rpg first-person shooters i have played in a while.


PROS:
+ Combat is incredible, fast phased with amazing mechanics to keep you moving.
+ Maps are well constructed around that idea.
+ A lot of variety in weapons and enemies.
+ Best soundtrack ever.
+ has its comedy moments.
+ Not a lot of voice acting, but whats there is done really well.
+ Ton of ways to execute your enemies, some of them are just as over the top as i was hoping to see.
+ A lot of side objectives and collectibles.


Can´t say anything about the multiplayer, i haven´t even tried that.

CONS:
-Sometimes, the maps can get a bit repetitive.
-The story is not great, this is one of those games that i play just because the gameplay is really fun.\t\t\t\t\t
