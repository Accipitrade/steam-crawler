\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA near masterpiece.

Super fun, gorey gameplay with straight forward objectives and a really interesting story if you choose to pay attention. Basically, this is the ultimate stress relief and opportunity to release your primal rage. Play the campaign by ripping and tearing into the vast amount of different demons using a varied amount of weapons at your disposal, each with different benefits and abilities to upgrade. Enjoy collecting (not so hard to find) collectables, uncovering the hidden story. Heck, why not also enjoy the stunning graphics and incredible soundtrack! My favourite part is stunning a demon then being able to use one of the many different animations to basically 'FINISH HIM' off. The game is self aware and knows it's silly, which makes everything more awesome.

However, the glory kill (FINISH HIM) animations can be glitchy in parts of the early game, resulting in me clipping into walls or falling through floors. Towards the end, the game can be a bit repetitive facing repeating enemies over and over again, but thankfully the creative building of each environment makes for fast paced parkour and agile dodging necessary to increase your adrenaline rate and think on your toes, especially at harder difficulties.

GET THIS GAME. There's a lot I haven't mentioned, but it's all worth playing it yourself.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
- Very good FPS gameplay with satisfying guns and and a very fast pace to every fight
- Lots of weapons and enemies to keep it from becoming repetitive
- Engaging soundtrack and fantastic sound design
- Good amount of challenge on normal difficulty once you get deeper into the game
- Tutorials ease the player in without being in your face or overbearing
- The action never stops as this game has minimal cinematics and very short loading screens
- Runs well with exceptional graphics and framerates
- Is a guilty pleasure of sorts, but at the same time has a layer of strategy that keeps it interesting - a balance which is very hard to achieve

Cons:
- It is annoyingly easy to die in certain areas by jumping or falling off a ledge
- The very first gun you get feels useless, but this is a minor complaint, because you unlock a shotgun only a few minutes in

9/10

Would highly recommend to anyone who is interested in DOOM, FPSs, or fast-paced gameplay.
At $20 now on Steam, it is an absolute steal.\t\t\t\t\t
