\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM has one of my favourite single player campaigns in an FPS and is one of my favourite games of 2016. I have only played the single player campaign and not multiplayer

Pros:
-Fast gameplay. You're always moving around and frantically shooting enemies to kill them. It feels awesome when you have zero health and you have to kill an enemy to get more health.
-Solid graphics
-It's lengthy
-Secrets are well hidden and are fun to find
-Many weapons to choose from, giving you a lot of variety for how to kill demons
-The game's difficulty curve is perfect and goes from really easy to quite difficult
-The tower level is awesome

Cons:
-If you like games with strong stories, you will feel dissapointed with the game's weak story
-Some of the guns feel useless compared to others. 3 or 4 of the guns were barely used by me the entire game
-The final boss could have been harder. It was easier than the other battles

If you are sick of stale and generic FPS campaigns, look no further. This game has the most fun FPS camaign since the days of Half-Life 2 and Bioshock. It really excels in fun, frantic gameplay

It has awesome graphics, very good optimization, awesome Singleplayer story with cool maps full of secrets to find and with awesome music. Moreover, you can play some Classic maps from Doom 1 and Doom 2 which is cool.

Also there is SnapMap, a tool that you can create and upload your own maps (Classic and Modern style) with different enemies, waves of enemies, weapons or even farm currency by killing enemies so you can continue further or unlock doors / weapons etc. So many options. You can play a sh!t load of custom maps from other people too.

Multiplayer is not bad. There are tons of customization options for your character to unlock and many maps with different modes to play. If you like that particular style of a fast pasted FPS game then you gonna love it.

10/10 Rip and tear.\t\t\t\t\t
