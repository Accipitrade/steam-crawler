\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a lot of fun. It is, however, very much a gameplay focused experience, and if you need an engagin story to get you through a game this may not be for you (I know this, because when i first bought it in 2016, i was bored as hell and didn't apreciate it.)
But let's go through why i now like this game.

This game has a really fun gameplay loop, especially once you start getting more guns and upgrades. It's really aggressive, really movement based gunplay. you do not stand still in this game if you want to live, which is a nice change of pace.
The variety of demons you get to shoot, blast, blow to ♥♥♥♥ or beat to death with their own ripped off limb is great, with each one bringing with a different behaviour, that works together to make enjoyable fights. They are also all killable in various gruesome and over the top way with glory kills.
The 2 things i will complain about is that 1: the "equipment" like grenades and such werent that useful, i ended up ingoring them most of the time, and the temporary powerups didn't bring much.

The levels are fun to run around in. the areas in which you fight are nice and open with a lot of verticality, and the rest is really cool to look at and travel around, it's filled with collectables, upgrades, tidbits of lore, so if you are someone who likes to scour every nook and crany to find stuff, oh boy do i have good news for you
. The plateforming can be a bit annoying in the beginning (I died more often to the pits than the demons), but you eventually get some things that make it better, and it's not that bad anyway.

The guns are... OOOOH BOY THE GUNS ARE ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ AWSOME!!! if you like to collect cool weapons, you are in for a treat. this game has a good dozen weapons for you to use, all of which are huge, with nice big sounds when you shoot them and are really satisfying to discharge into the ugly faces of hell critters. There are weapon mods for each and every single one of them that can be found, and then upgraded, which is always fun.
I will say tho, that due to some guns sharing ammo, i ended up just... almost never using some of the guns.
I used the chaingun 3 times, because the heavy assault riffle was just so much better for me.
After i got the gauss cannon, energy cells became too valuable to waste them on the plasma riffle, which is sad because that was fun to use.
And there is LITTERALLY no use in shooting the combat shotgun once you have the super shotgun, it is just worst.

The story and lore are very minor (the main character actually ignores most of what is said to him, which is pretty funny) but for what is there, it's interesting enough. The villain is nothing special, but very hate-able, which is always nice, the plot is simple, and it doesn't need to be more.
A lot of optional lore is around, which is interesting enough to seek out and read.

I hope this will be useful to someone, sorry for the gramma errors, i kinda suck.\t\t\t\t\t
