\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrom gameplay to music to environments and ambience, this is hands down the most exhilarating and immersive DOOM entry (I haven't played Eternal) I have gotten my hands on. Everything is so well done, it's clear that the team have put their heart and soul into this experience. The superb sound design gets you amped as you run and gun, laying waste to the legions before you. The gore-crusted environments perfectly encapsulate an infernal invasion. The hubris of humanity is on full display in the engrossing story.

A game so good I absolutely had to watch the credits to know the names of the talented and committed individuals who created such a spectacular horror-fest.

Masterfully done, id Software. Hats ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ off to ya.
