\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM might be the greatest game, that was brought kicking and screaming from the 90's. With games like Quake IV, and Duke Nukem Forever, I was more worried about Doom because of the attention the bad multiplayer beta was. And I played it and I defintiely didn't see the appeal. And then something happened; I started hearing about the single player, being this amazing adventure, with good story telling elements, and the best FPS spirit filled game many have seen. Everything in this game, feels like something you loved when you played it many years ago, but with an amazing graphics engine running this hulking demon beast of a game.

The Glory Kills never get tiring. The secrets never get tiring. Dying gets tiring and ♥♥♥♥ Id Software for Ultra Nightmare, but other than that, the game tells a rather nice story of a soldier that is being feared, by scary demon beasts...and they need to destroy him! But you are the Marine in the kickass Praetor suit! The skill advancement is really well done, as you get challenges for each level, masteries for weapons were you get in them challenges, Praetor tokens that you need to hire Sherlock Holmes to find in order to power your suit, and sweet nectar Argent to make you just more awesome. I love also how every single enemy I've known from the games look so amazing! So fearful and horrible to look at, as well as feel intimidated by. These ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ are also tough, and they don't group hug you with love and care, they ♥♥♥♥ rape you with Hell fire.

And speaking of Hell Fire ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥...the M-U-S-I-C.

I will buy a record player, and a set of speakers, and wear hipster glasses...if someone releases Mick Gordon's AMAZING soundtrack on that format. Please! Do yourself a favor and buy this game, and enjoy that sweet awesome hard rock/heavy metal/industrial/DJENT/electronic mishmash of music that was some from the old times, but given just an amazing (this word cannot be stressed enough) music.

Flaws include a weird glitch that cuts sound completely until the game begins, a few crashes during fights, a bit of a loading time thing, checkpoints in some areas that make no sense, and Ultra Nightmare can suck a dead goat's ballsack. But....

Nothing else I think. I played Multiplayer and it definitely feels better, and it's a fun diversion, and I have not played much SnapMap, but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, this one level was just a corridor, that would close doors bit by bit in a blue misty haze...creepy! I will dive more into that, and maybe make my own maps too.

I got this on the Summer Sale in a impulse buy because I wanted to wait. Don't be like me and don't be cheap! Get this game! NOW. And remember, God cannot save you, if demons are humping your dead corpse to the soothing sounds of Mick Gordon.

Ta-ta.

*UPDATE*
It's the near end of the year and this game is still as amazing as ever. They have added more elements on SnapMap, and then adding Arcade Mode and the 2 DLCs for the multiplayer. This game made my year and I definitely nominated this for the 5 More Minutes Award since...I CAN'T STOP PLAYING IT!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTHIS GAME IS A GOD DESEND, or the best thing that was brought from hell.

Before this game came out, I have never played the older doom games before. But with this amount of destruction, hell, and by the more amazing soundtrack, I just have no words on how this game changed me.

The music in this game makes me feel like I want to slaughter donald trump so badly...man, feels like I was sent straight to hell after playing this game. Plus, this ispired me to change my previous profile pic into a bloody mess >:) Edit: Now to doomguy, lol

The gameplay is great. You still move fast asf and its nothing tactical. This brings back the classic arcade but with beautiful detail and great content. In my opinion, arcadish games like is like the cure to all this gaming catastroy. Praise ID Software and Bethesda for this...

The campaign is jus the best out of the three modes. Even though there isn't much of a story, ITS JUST SO GOOD TO SLAUGHTER DEMONS AND FIGHT LIKE HELL. You can find many collectibles, upgrades, and the nostalgic classic maps. Feels like they really want to make DOOM like it always was in the past. The campaign will last you 11-16 hours depending on difficulty.

Multiplayer is the second mode you can try. It is sort of lacking/dying and sometimes it feels unfair when people use the rocket launcher. But thats the point really, games today restrict you from doing the damage and going on a rampage. And in my opinion, guns really don't matter. What really matters is how well you're good with them. Hell, even I aint great with the launchers. So everyone likes to use different guns sometimes. But I really like how its not just some gun action but also demon action and just complete carnage.

Snapmap is the third mode where you can create you own unique kind of maps and make it how you want it. Can be a TDM, Surivival, or come up with a new type of mode. (Btw, you can make a MOBA in the editor too, so LOL and DOTA fans should try making more of these modes.) Even if you get snapmap points from playing, you wont really need them much because the point of playing in other snapmap creations is to have fun.

One more important detail about this game is that this is one of the only game that doesn't make you want to grind or try to waste your time to get stronger. No, you have the power to rip and tear through demons and it already makes the game fun asf! Same goes with rocket league. Even if you started from scratch, you will already feel like a complete badass and will have a great time.

Now a few things I should also let you know about the slightly bad side of the game: Multiplayer and Snapmap could get repetitive, so grab some friends to keep the fun going. Playing solo would likely want you to just keep playing the campaign. But other than that, the game is pure fun and definately lets you keep playing.

If you want a game that makes you want to make you blood pump and make you feel excited asf, GET THIS GAME NOW. It is 666% guarenteed the worth of 60 bucks and you will love the living ♥♥♥♥ out of it.

P.S.
I wish I can recommened this game more than once because I just want more people to buy it so we can revive the multiplayer. It's dying plz help :(

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore you buy this game, understand that it's very different from games like Call Of Duty, Counterstike, or Far Cry. This entire game is a brilliant homage to the old school 90s shooter, with pickups to give you different abilities, no health regeneration, aiming down sights, and no reloading. It's not a game where you hide from enemies, or move around slowly, trying to trickshot enemies. You have to move. You have to be fast. Never stop. This is the kind of FPS I grew up playing as a kid. You can't go into this game, playing it like Call Of Duty. I only say that because I see so many other people doing it, and they just end up getting their asses kicked.

Moving along, this game is amazing. I found myself looking forward to every single wave of enemies, with disappointment everytime I cleared them all out. Because I just want more and more. I want the game to keep throwing everything it's got at me. And it does. The combat is extremely addicting, and you feel like you're a bunny hopped up on cocaine, running through levels, blasting away at demons with what is only the BEST arsenal I've seen in a video game. From plasma rifles, to a three barreled minigun, to even a gauss rifle, which is personally my favorite. You're going to need every weapon you can get. Don't get ready to play favorites or plan your loadout. Oh, and did I mention the melee kills? That's my favorite part! Do enough damage to ANY enemy, and you get to stomp or smash their head into the ground with your bare fists, or break their necks, or..Just whatever you want. There's even a pick up that lets you go through levels, just TEARING up demons with your bare hands.

This is honestly the best FPS I've played since my Quake days. This is exactly what gamers have been missing. There's a story to the game, but..It's not focused on story. This is an arcade shooter. And Doom guy even acknowledges it! He just breaks everything, and pummels through everybody. He doesn't care! He has one job, and that's to kill EVERYTHING, set to a fantastic metal-heavy score by Mick Gordon. It really makes combat just a thousand times better.

The game also runs fantastic on my PC. I have an i5 4690k, with a GTX 970, and I can run this game on ultra without a hitch. I honestly didn't expect that! That's why I didn't buy this game for a month..I was afraid that this game would destroy my rig. I was also afraid the singleplayer would have no replayability. I don't play a lot of linear games. I don't have the patience. But this game was an exception. Now, there IS a multiplayer, which is more of a bonus..This game focuses mostly on singleplayer or snapmap, which is basically like a map creator, where you can play usermade levels! My problem is that if you want to switch modes, you have to restart the game. Multiplayer has multiple modes, but the only mode you'll most likely play in is team deathmatch. I guess people just don't like the multiplayer...Though I never had a problem with it. I actually enjoy playing it every now and then.

Buy this game. I promise you won't regret it. Now if you'll excuse me, I'm going to try to beat the game on nightmare mode.\t\t\t\t\t
