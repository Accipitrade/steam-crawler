But its not even fun, its so retarded and unfulfilling for a Doom game, as it can be.
The pace is so quick (in every way) that i actualy get motion sick. And it takes al the strategy out of the game.
There is no time to think, only time to run shot and hit. And always in that order.
If you dont hit the enemy you wont survive, becouse its when you kill them by hitting them that they drop life.
And al to offen you loose track of them by just runing past them, instead of actualy hitting them.
So frustrating and irritating.... If I wanted a faststeped game i could go for Quake Arena (like this game feels like), but what I wanted was something like old Doom, dark, gothic, abit of jumpscare and a pace simular to Battlefield 4. In this game im flying around like a pinball shoting hitting, missing, dying and finaly, vomiting.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my favourite games of the past five years, DOOM is an explosive and pulse-pounding shooty bang-bang game about an angry space marine shooting demons in the face on Mars. A decently lengthy campaign with loads of hidden secrets to find, fantastic gunplay and smashing level design. You'll need your wits about you if you want to survive the intense skirmishes with the forces of Hell, and the game offers plenty of upgrades for your weapons which can be upgraded further for more shooty and more bang-bang.
The story's alright too, but let's face it, we're all here to shoot demons in the face. And punch them in the face. And explode their faces with micromissiles.
Basically, if you want some top drawer shooty bang-bang, DOOM is the game for you.\t\t\t\t\t
