\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-Single player only review-

♥♥♥♥ me this game is so satisfying and cathartic. Looks great, feels great, is great! It's so good to have an fps that focuses on movement and evasion instead of cover, it makes you feel so badass when you dance around the enemies slipping between projectiles and crushing the hellspawn underfoot.

Runs really well too, even on my ghetto surround setup, on max visual settings. The inital load time at game launch is a bit long (mechanical HDD), and it has a large install size, but level loading is nice and quick so that's not too bad over all. Gunplay is good, their's some nice meaty feed back when firing, though the shotgun and super shotgun don't quite feel as good as they should. Maybe a bit more visual feed back on use would fix this with more bloody effects so it feels more like they're doing a good amount of damage on a non killing hit.

Definitely worth picking up in the sale, especially if you're a fan of old school fps'. I have heard that the multiplayer is a bit mediocre and I probably will ignore it, but the game stands more than well enough even with just the single player.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed hours and hours of Doom, Doom II, modded my own levels. Suffice it to say I had lots of nostalgia for Doom as an aging gamer. When I saw it was on sale I jumped at the chance. Within 10 mintues of gameplay I was surrounded by mobs teleporting every 5 seconds and pogo-ing around throwing fireballs that soak up a ton of rounds running around like a Space Marine with his head cut off trying to fight to the next room with more of the same, and all the time wondering if it's just that I'm old and crotchety now, but I thorougly didn't enjoy it right from the get-go.

On a positive note, the graphics were spectacular especially when I switched to Vulkan on my AMD card. I suppose I could have given it a bigger try, but I wanted my refund and got it promptly. If you loved doom, and you're like me, it's on sale now, you can give it a quick go, but if in the first 20 minutes of gameplay you hated it as much as I did, I'd recommend stopping. It's not going to get any better :/\t\t\t\t\t
