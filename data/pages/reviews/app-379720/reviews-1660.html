The way this game handles movement is very disorienting for me, and everything from running to camera movement is actually *too* smooth for my eyes to deal with, which is a sentence I never thought I'd have to write. Fact is, the game for some reason just makes me dizzy as heck, to the point of actually feeling sick if I play it for more than an hour at a time. I'm an edge case and I don't hide that, but if it happens for me, it may well happen for someone else so I figured I'd at least put this in writing for those select few people.

This is a great game that is a genuine throwback to the best of 90's shooters, but if you're prone to motion sickness or dizziness, consider yourself duly warned.
