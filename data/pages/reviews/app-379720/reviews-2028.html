\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun, if a little bit repetitive. Variety of enemies is enough to keep you interested, and there are puzzles (mostly jumping puzzles but still) to add just a little more to the experience. Gun play is satisfying, and the chainsaw comes in handy in tight situations to help drop ammo for you (cut a demon up and it dumps ammo...idk). It's DOOM so of course it's a gorefest, and surprisingly relaxing for how over-the-top EVERYTHING is. Worth the $$ they want for it, but be warned: If you are trying to play this on a dual core system be prepared for some nice "old school lag". The sound will cut out and in during combat as the game taxes the system for every bit of computing power it can find. This won't happen a lot, but enough to be noticeable, and occasionally cutscenes will force a crash.\t\t\t\t\t

Previous fans of the series mock this one, and it's little brother (Doom Eternal) for being too over the top, and just being clickbait-like material with the finishing moves.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love the original Doom games. Doom 1 and Doom 2 are legendary, iconic, classic games. Over 20 years later, in a greedy gaming market with unfinished multiplayer games, Doom 2016 stays in it's own lane and continues to be a masterpiece.

Doom 2016 took some risks and is not a traditional AAA release, which partially why it's so great! No microtransactions, no season pass, no early access, no tricks, just simply a good game.

The Singleplayer/Campaign is amazing and keeps up in a good, modern way. It took me about 13 hours and it was fun looking for collectibles and secret. It's rewarding and adds a layer of depth.

The Multiplayer is pretty good but not outstanding. Nowadays its not too hard to find a game, but your limited due to player count usually. It can be fun but also feel unfair. It added some hours of life to the game for me. At least it's better than Tomb Raider 2013's multiplayer.

The Snapmap is a fun and cool idea I think that most games should have. Its great to see what the community makes and making something yourself if you want to. I appreciate modes like this.

Overall (9.7/10)
Doom 2016 is a modern masterpiece every FPS player should play. It's a great game in the Doom series, and it's just simply a fun, satisfying game\t\t\t\t\t
