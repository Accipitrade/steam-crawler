It's been a very long time since an FPS has gotten my blood pumping so quickly with non-stop fast paced combat and brutality. Every encounter feels unique compared to those that came before, new enemies, new positioning, new hazards, there's just so much that culminates into a cohesive and satisfying experience that'll suck you in and spit out your bones if you're not prepared.

What's more, there has never been a more well done version of Doomguy. He's still a silent protagonist, but as you progress through the story and uncover a bit of the lore you'll find yourself loving him and his seething hatered for demonkind more than ever. Just pay attention during the intro and you'll know exactly what kind of ride you're in for and it only gets better. Even the various death animations are rewarding in that regard.

A plethora of weapons, grenades, power-ups, runes, upgrades and enemy types will keep you on your toes the entire time. Every level brings fresh new concepts into the mix and combat becomes more chaotic and satisfying as a result. Just don't feed the Pinkies!

I can't say I enjoyed the multiplayer very much however, so if that's your bag then you might want to consider something else. That said, the custom map maker is pretty well done and there are tons of good community made maps for you and a group of friends to take on. Some even feature unique game modes you won't find in the main campaign or multiplayer. Definitely worth fiddling around with if you have the time for it, both as a creator and a player.

TL;DR

Super recommended if you crave that rush that comes with a chain of challenging encounters that only escalate the further you get. There's plenty of variety to keep you engaged. Not so much if you're into competitive multiplayer, but the custom map maker and the co-op options it has are fun to play around with.\t\t\t\t\t
