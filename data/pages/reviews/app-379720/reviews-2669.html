\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh boy it's DOOM. Honestly, considering it's success i doubt this game even needs a review anymore but why the hell not? Doom 2016 is a reboot of the legendary Doom from 1993 from the kings of FPS, ID Software. It's concept and design borrows heavily from the famously successful mod known as "Brutal Doom". Does it hold up to the original? Well, that depends on what's more important to you, Multiplayer or Singleplayer? Doom's Singleplayer experience can only be defined as sublime. As you rip and tear demons' faces off faster than a katana wielding ninja in a toilet paper factory, you will experience a variety of ways to forcefully relieve your stress onto the unaware denizens of Hell. Punching, Squashing, Ripping, Sawing, Blasting, Burning and slapping them in the face with your massive space marine♥♥♥♥♥♥are only a few of the things you can do in this singleplayer adventure. The plot? OH NO GUYS THERE ARE DEMONS IN THE BASE! I MUST KILL THE DEMONS! And then he did. If you enjoy completely obliterating helplesss AI goons while headbanging your face through your monitor you've come to the right place, because DOOM 2016's soundtrack is arguably one of the best soundtracks to listen to while TAKING THE LIVES OF YOUR ENEMIES! YEAH, EAT THAT SUCKER. POW, RIGHT IN THE BALLS. I'M DOOMGUY BABY, WOOHOO! Oh and it has an even faster paced classic-style "Arcade Mode" that's honestly even more fun than the campaign! It's a great time all around. http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1126969948
PROS
+ Holy♥♥♥♥♥♥it's fun killing things!
+ Holy flap jacks it's fun killing things!
+ Holy chicken sandwich it's fun killing things!
+ Holy hell it's fun killing things!

CONS
+ I personally can't stand snapmap compared to the quality material the modding/mapping community for the classic games has to offer. There's simply no contest!
+ The environments can all start to look a little stale after binging the campaign.
+ Spamming 'glory kills' get old after doing it 20 times to a revenant's face.

BOTTOM LINE
http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1126970006
If you love fast paced brutal FPS action, look no further than Doom 2016, truly an amazing feat of gaming development considering it's troubled history. Well done developers, let's see what you do for Doom II now!

--------------------------------
DOOM MULTIPLAYER
... The multiplayer on the other hand is an unusual creature. Caught between the trends of 90s gaming and modern gaming, this multiplayer experience is... confused, to say the least. They got some things right, like no regenerating health, no reloading animations, nice arena sized maps and it's real fun to blow people up with a rocket launcher, when you actually can that is. However most of the time you'll just be frustrated. At least i was. Be it the often terrible netcode, wonky matchmaking, infuriating bulletsponge health (That completely negates any fun, with any weapon) and a buttload of other crap, for me it often just doesn't feel as fun as it could be if they weren't trying so hard to appeal to Halo fans for some reason. Not to mention, it's really colorful. Like, it's so bright and hyperactive it kind of doesn't feel like doom at all. You could just slap another name on the multiplayer and boom, nobody cares. Granted they did try to rectify this this with a pickup that let's you turn into a demon during combat for a short period of time, but you usually end up dying so quickly after you obtain it, that it usually makes it's purpose seem unnecessary. But hey, at least Free-for-all (Mixed team modes) and Deathmatch (Last man standing) can be genuinely entertaining and fun at times with the right opponents.
http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1126970067
PROS
+ Character Customization can be fun
+ No stupid reloading (Thank god)
+ No regenerating health
+ Weapons are satisfying to use
+ Maps are varied and interesting

CONS
+ Looks/Feels way too similar to Halo
+ Bulletsponge health is unfathomably annoying
+ Repetitive announcer who never shuts up (Can thankfully be muted)
+ Runes/Perks were an awful idea, and you should be ashamed
+ No custom mod/map support or usermade content
+ They tried way too hard to cater to the casual crowd
+ Character Progression/Leveling is pointless

BOTTOM LINE
New Doom is like new coke, it will never be as good as the original.
If you want authentic Doom multiplayer action, you owe it to yourself to go grab the original doom wads, download Brutal Doom and fire up Zandronum, it's all you need and more.
