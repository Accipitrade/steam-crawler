\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM 2016 is a great game, and one of the greatest examples of AAA games. It is polished to near flawlessness both technically and gameplay wise, to the stark contrast with the various unfinished early access titles masquerading as premium games that have saturated the AAA market in the last decade or so.

It's fun, it runs really well while looking great, it'll get your blood pumping and the soundtrack is simply godlike. What more do you need to hear? Go play it.

To be fair, there are some minor issues, the game shows some problems with texture loading / streaming at times, which as far as I know is a bit of an issue with the engine itself. Aside from that, the rpg-lite elements could do with another balancing pass, especially the basic ammo capacity for some weapons. Oh and enemy sound sometimes breaks and does not play.

However, those are all very minor complaints, the game plays extremely well pretty much all of the time, it's challenging but not unfair, and I think it can easily be recommended to pretty much anyone looking for a good FPS to play.\t\t\t\t\t
