\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWas pleasantly surprized with this game. A nice little return to the old school shooters from the 90s and early 2000s. There are some faults to the game, like somewhat boring level design that doesn't bring anything new (what I mean by this is that you don't have those moments of "oh yeah I love this level, it's the one where there's ________"; levels just feel kinda same-y, there's definetly progression of enemies and of your arsenal, but it gets kinda old after a while), somewhat boring weapons compared to a lot of other arcade shooters (this game doesn't really compare to the variety offered by games like Serious Sam TSE , Unreal, Duke Nukem 3D, etc), and the multiplayer is lame as hell. However, outside of that it is one of the best single player shooters I've played in a while. Shooting feels nice, music is some of the best I've ever heard in any game, graphics are very good. I can keep going with praise for this game. I don't know if the game is worth the 60 dollar price tag, but seeing as it's on sale a lot, I can definetly recommend it. Right now the game is 67% off and at less than $20, it's a steal.\t\t\t\t\t
