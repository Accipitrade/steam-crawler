\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWords cannot describe the awesomeness of this game. It's like magic. Start the game. You shoot the bad guys. That's it.

And yet somehow DOOM manages to do it way better than any of the shooter games I've played so far. There's no staying in cover and waiting for your health to regenerate. It's mechanics are... Archaic. And yet they feel like a good modern game should. It's really difficult to describe. It's old school done in the best possible way. The storyline is... Optional. I don't usually like just plain shooters. I like a good story and I'm more of a fan of RPG games. Yet shockingly I like DOOM anyways. Probably because it's just really good. Also, the soundtrack was compiled by a metal god or something.

If you want to relive the old days of Quake 1 and DOOM 1/2 in the best possible way, this is for you. Don't even wait, just buy it already. If you're more of a modern gamer, this could still be for you. Just don't expect a lot of depth. Expect a lot of really good action.

Except if you want to buy it just for Multiplayer, then it's not for you. The shinning jewel of DOOM is it's singleplayer campaign, the multiplayer is quite lacking, sadly.

But if you want non-stop "metal" grinding action... Oh yeah.\t\t\t\t\t
