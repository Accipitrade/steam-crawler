\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a true example of mechanics, game feel, and fun all coming together in one gory, fun mess. This game is tough at first, but if you throw yourself at it, you will soon find yourself strafing, gunning, and running like all hell just to stay alive.
Pros
  • Really fun arsenal to upgrade and blow things up with
  • Fast gameplay
  • Fun and unique mobs to fight
  • Interesting level locations
  • Rewarding upgrades

Cons
  • Very steep difficulty curve. This game is known for being tought but the checkpoints are very forgiving at least.
  • If you are a completionist, I found myself missing a lot of hidden things and having to bactrack the entire level just for a single challenge.
  • Story is badass, but a little bland at times, as if there are all these levels but you havent really completed anything until about the very end.
  • Multiplayer scene is a bit dead from what I hear (have not tried it yet, but the game is good for its single player alone.
Also if gore is something you aren't into, I cannot reccomend this game for you.
Fortunately this game awoke the serial killer in me, so Im off to kill more demons.

-beautiful graphics (like literally gorgeous)
-ultra fluid gameplay
-Brutal fast paced combat system (you do not stop moving basically the entire game unless you want to die)
-full fast paced multiplayer, not the best i have played but definilty alot of fun.
-cool little custom map designer and a place to play other peoples made maps/games
-very fitting soundtrack that keeps you in the killing mood (basically a mix of demonic instrumental metal)

the game is bassically a hell bent brutal dance of Death from start to finish. run, shoot, kill, repeat. it is hard to get bored when you are constantly killing demons in more ways than you can think of, from blasting with your shotgun, to pulling out the chainsaw and sawing one in half, to running up to multiple injured ones and basically ripping them to pieces with your bare hands. and best of all while doing this, it flows together so perfectly, with such fluid controls its easy to catch on but hard to master. you wont be dissapointed with your purchase.\t\t\t\t\t
