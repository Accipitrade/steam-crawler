\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe starting of the FPS... The legendary... I always want to try this game as soon as I got my new system upgrade and it is not disappointed.

Pros
-This game has a smart level design with a mix of mazy and combat.
-Finding a secret in each level is also well designed, not too hard not too easy.
-Fast and smooth combat with a well timing great background musics.
-Monsters design make its classic and unigue.
-Excellent graphics and lighting design, especially the effect of stream water on a glass window.
-Collecting everything in this game is not so borring it's always give player a reward.

Cons
-I have collected all data pad and read all codex in this game and I don't like how it's wrote in the UAC advertising part, It's much like a propaganda and dose not make sense (thinking about all of UAC's employee must be very stupid working with this company), despite it's overall good story line. However, whos gonna read it all anyway.

Overall, DOOM is an another great game that's worth to buy.\t\t\t\t\t
