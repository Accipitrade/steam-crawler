\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPOSITIVES
Doom 2016 is a fantastic addition to the doom series, doing so many excellent things that make it a blast to play. The guns are so fun to use, and the gameplay makes you feel like an unstoppable badass. The powerups feel incredible to use, and the atmosphere and soundtrack are both beautifully designed. All the additional challenges to get 100% completion also feel mostly satisfying to complete.

NEGATIVES
If you're coming from doom eternal, these negatives will stand out quite strongly. Firstly, dying from platforming is a real pain, and it feels like an unnecessarily harsh punishment, especially before you have access to the in flight mobility rune, and platforming is not 100% reliable. Secondly, some challenges are just annoying, especially the glory kill and powerup challenges. If you don't manage to complete them first time round, you'll have to restart the level, and sometimes you miss a secret and cannot go back because of a 1-way level design. Lastly, the starting levels are also very painful to complete on the higher difficulties because of your extremely limited arsenal, and the start can feel very slow.

OVERALL
Despite all the flaws, I think that doom is an amazing game. While it can be frustrating at times, ultimately it provides a perfect demon slaying simulator that is definitely worth playing through.\t\t\t\t\t
