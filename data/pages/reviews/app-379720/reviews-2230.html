\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016 is a reboot of the original doom series that id could have very easily ♥♥♥♥♥♥ up, but I am glad to see they didn't.

The game is very well crafted and polished (but not overly-polished to the point it becomes bland like most AAA games nowadays). The game actually gives you the feeling you exist in its world, a feeling that reminds me of Portal 2, and it's not even supposed to be an immersive sim.

The weapons are punchy and the sound design is on point, you can feel the impact they have on the demons.

Every map has attention to the smallest detail, from the skybox to the lighting which really goes to show that the people working on this game have made it out of love for it and not profit.

All in all, a very solid game I recommend anyone to play.\t\t\t\t\t
