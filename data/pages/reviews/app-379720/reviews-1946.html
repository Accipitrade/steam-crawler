The weapons are amazing, the attachments you add to the weapons feel right and help a lot. It's not just cosmetic. Overall it is a great game for people with a strong computer.

I have just a couple complains with it:

- From time to time it seems to slow down at random. Sometimes it slows down with many enemies, sometimes it slows down in a small area with nothing in it. My PC is not optimal for the game, sure, but this slowdown seems really random at times.
- I don't know if this is an issue with my setup, but the sounds don't give you a good notion of location the sound was originated from. Sometimes it sounds like an enemy is right next you, but it is actually in the floor below, etc.
- Sometimes physics go crazy and a metal box or a rope keeps bouncing on its own.\t\t\t\t\t
