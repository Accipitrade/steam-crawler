\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the most optimized game I have ever played. It never drops below 80 pfs on ultra and 1080p, even on Phenom X6 or FX-8370, when I am using Vulcan API on RX 590. That fact alone is enough to support excellent work of developers and to buy the game.
And the gameplay is excellent. It forces a player to play aggressively and perform melee attacks on enemies (gory kills). I prefer stealth approach and sniping in games, but this game forced me to change gameplay style and I enjoy it.
Maps are big, and although the game is linear, player has a choice of multiple paths.
Graphics looks good, but it is hard to compare it to other games, because the game doesn't have any other live human NPC.
The only drawback is lack of quick save options, but autosaves are frequent enough, and it makes the player feel accomplished after cleaning the area from the enemies.
Replayability of the game is surprisingly high. There are various secrets and challenges scattered on each map, and somehow it is fun finding them as much as cleaning the map.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game has so many issues I'm forced to give this game a negative review.

It has nice graphics and decent gameplay, but a lot of times it starts with the wrong resolution, sometimes it doesn't start all (eg: I play the game just fine but when I come back to play again a few hours later the game doesn't start), sometimes some keyboard inputs don't work; I know it's not a problem with my PC because other games run fine and none of the fixes I tried worked for this game.
Gameplay has good things like being able to carry all weapons and fast pace but things like jumping puzzles ruin gameplay, rune trials are stupid and sometimes unfairly difficult even on the lowest difficulty settings, some levels feel repetitive, and certain aspects of multiplayer feel out of place in a Doom game such as restrictions on loadouts, the whole level up & unlocking stuff. Also the game requires constant internet connection to play custom matches against bots, which imo is a terrible design choice. Some achievements are glitched and don't unlock.

Unfortunately all the negative aspects negate any positives the game may have.\t\t\t\t\t

Overview
Violence. Speed. Gore. Destruction. DOOM is a fast paced first person shooter that is dripping in blood and guts. Playing the DOOM marine you are trying to stop the spawns of Hell from taking over Mars after things go wrong at a research base. Gameplay was fluid and nonstop action from start to finish of each level. Upgrades no matter how minor feel rewarding and add more to the combat. First play I went through on Ultra Violence; One stop to access what was happening or one mistake would end in my death. Little room for error or standing around.
