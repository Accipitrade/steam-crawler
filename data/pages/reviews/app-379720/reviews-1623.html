\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM !!! Truth be told the game is much more intense , gory and over the top than the bold lettered name of the game suggests . Its an omage to the old school areana based high octane scooter with addition of modern fps features such as weapon mods , collectibles and platforming . So long story short here are some pros and cons :

Pros :
1) Intense action
2) Glory kills ( because killing enemies by shooting is too mainstream )
3) Intricate level designing with amazing contraptions and platforming
4) Bada## weapons ranging from shotguns , assault rifles , pistols
5) Ability to carry all weapons and the same time which means players are not limited to only 2 weapons unlike modern fps
6) The chainsaw taking dismembering enemies to another level
7) Various types of demon enemies with their own strategies and attack patters
8) DOOMSLAYER
9) Level design and aesthetics


Cons :

1) Only 3 bosses
3) No story , I mean there is one for the sake of the game but its not worth mentioning
4) Niche featured multiplayer

Finally DOOM is a must play for both old school and modern players even if only to blow everything up . If someone is pissed or angry , playing DOOM is a great opportunity to vent their anger and release catharsis .

09 / 10 ( Campaign mode )

A must play\t\t\t\t\t
