\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThough it will never take the place in my heart that belongs to the original, this is the best game id has made since quake 2. For the nostalgic, the weapons work the same for the most part (and even have a classic mode in settings that centers the weapons underneath your view) and the enemies are fairly true to to the originals. For the modern, there is excellent graphics and and lots of violence and whatever else you children like, cause every one is playing this. As for the bad bits. The multi-player has grind. I hate grind in deathmatch type games. Maybe quake champions will be better, but I doubt it. And the weapons system is a bit clunky with the BFG and chainsaw being spectial weapons and all, nothing bad, just makes custom controls a bit chaotic. The weapons also look a bit too quake for me. That all said, great game, get this in your collection as soon as you can. And well done id.

10/10\t\t\t\t\t

Levels offer upgrades and challenges, and while the story itself is alright, the gameplay of it is the real meat. From blasting the demons with a big gun, to glory killing the most defenseless demons, it'll keep you in a rush! And the soundtrack too is also great and gets the juices going. Pros:
- Good music.
- Amazing fast-paced gameplay.
- Fun Campaign.
- Fun Multiplayer.
- Wide variety of enemy types and weapons to use.
- Doesn't feel repetitive in the story.
- Optimization is fine.

Cons:
- Low player count for multiplayer.
- An ok story.

I highly recommend this game to players who love bullet-hell shooters and want to have a good time!\t\t\t\t\t
