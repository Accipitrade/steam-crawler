\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI only played single player so keep that in mind.

One of the most fun game experiences I had in a long time.
Doom 2016 is fast (not quite es fast as the originals but close), brutal and at higher
difficulties very deadly. Doom guy is an angry ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ punching, ripping and tearing
through all 9 circles of hell and back with no remorse and no regret.
The story is actually kind of interesting if you want to dive a bit deeper, but you can also
completely ignore it and ♥♥♥♥ ♥♥♥♥ up.

Weapons handle great, and the upgrade system keeps things fresh and intersting without being too
overloaded.

The flow of the game in general is very good. The Glory Kill system is surprisingly robust even though
I was highly sceptical at first. Even trailers could not convince me. Ingame though it feels completely fluid.

Graphics are superb and partially downright amazing.

Leveldesign can be a bit confusing. Sometimes you need to run around a bit to find stuff, but this
is pretty close to the original.

All in all I was very impressed. S so get it - absolutely worth it :)\t\t\t\t\t
