\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing game so far! Beatiful sound track, intense and exciting gameplay keeps players under the effect of adreniline rush in order to prevent them from standing in place like other games (*cough cough* COD Ghosts) and instead keep them moving to keep their health from dropping to zero (which you get from preforming glory kills on enemies). The pacing and game mechanics are well balanced and the weapons and mods are programmed very good. The secret locations give the players thrill of discovering collectables and classic maps from the original Doom. You could argue that this is pretty generic since it is in most newer games but lets be fair: it is easier to make games more interesting and it works in almost all cases.

Despite the game being awesome, it still has some flaws. One thing is certain: most AI can be pretty derpy at times, not to mention that killing demons for a majoprity of the game gets pretty stale (however that doesn't happen until much later in my opinion). Also multiplayer is pretty generically average in my eyes, considering that a lot of the content is a part of the DLC. Speaking of DLC, the game now has a season pass with 3 other microtransactions, and not suprisingly: it is muliplayer content and none of three purchases are worth buying at the moment. I recommend that you wait till the DLC is less than $10 all together if you wish to purchase the DLC.

To conclude my review, I want to point out that this is MY OPINION and I do not mind if you disagree with me but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ do not rage at me PLEASE, if you do or do not enjoy the game thats fine! Also please respect each other's opnions! I give this game an 8/10.\t\t\t\t\t
