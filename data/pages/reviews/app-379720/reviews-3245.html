\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is back! A throwback to an era, long since passed, DOOM picks up where it left all those years ago. Graphically gorgeous, insane gameplay, suggestive themes, and gratuitous violence. Gone are the modern "perks" of regenerative health, taking cover, leaning, etc. It's now you, in an arena, fighting hordes of demons as you jump, duck, and scramble to stay alive. It's fast, frantic, and loads of fun. The Doom Slayer (Marine), says nothing but that's ok, you will most likely find yourself making snide comments as you dismember another one of Hell's minions.

It's an incredible game, that's worth your time and attention. DOOM does a marvelous job of keeping you hooked with secrets, creative objectives, and collectables. The single-player campaign, is estimated to be ~10 hrs but if you actually go back and unlock all the runes, weapon mods, suit upgrades, etc. You have a game that easily translates to 20+ hrs and no, you do not get bored.

If I had to describe how the game plays, it's DOOM coupled with Quake and Metroid Prime. It's fast, there's a lot of vertical fighting, and a ton of exploration.

id, I'm so happy to see such a stellar game! Can't wait for the sequel.\t\t\t\t\t
