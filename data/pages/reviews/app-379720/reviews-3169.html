\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNow the first question you may ask is this: How much like the original doom is this game like?

The answer: Less than you'd want but more than you'd expect.

DOOM is probably one of the best reboots to a old franchise ive seen since the new Wolfenstien. Its fun, it looks great and runs rather well (i crashed a few times during the campaign but otherwise got along fine).

If you're a fan of Doom and especially if you're a fan of brutal doom you'll love it. It's brutal, loud and fast. all the things we wanted to be brought over from the original doom.
Hell they even managed to throw a story in there (its there if you like...want to follow it or somthing but otherwise it just gives you excuses to go and kill things in specific places)

I found the controls to be tight and most of the weapons satisfying. you can modify and upgrade each weapon to give it that little extra edge. This is nice for two reasons, 1. it keeps the variety of gameplay up and 2. it keeps otherwise outdated weapons viable late into the game.

Ive found the multiplayer community to be rather entertaining as well even though ive only dabbled in it thus far.
There seems to be a rather good map making comnmunity providing both solo and CoOp Based maps and gameplay options as well as more traditional multiplayer modes (Death match, TDM, capture the flag etc...) These games can run the gambit from slow to frantic as all hell guranteed to set off any Quake PTSD you might have.
The addition of a demon pickup on the map makes things neat too though it largely depends on what kind of monster you show up as, Ive seen a player become an imp and get his pushed in almost immediatly but at the same time i've seen a player become a Revenant and clean house. So it varies...

If i had to give any downsides to this game itd probably be it's sound track. The soundtrack sometimes is spot on, amazing with its heavy metal and perfect with its timing (that goes out of its way to sync with executions)
Other times though the music is bad to the point of being annoying just going on with a single bass note or drawing out that ominous sound to the point of it getting old.
I dont know if this is what counts nowadays as heavy metal, maybe i'm just old now.

All in all if you wanted to get a new FPS game to scratch that itch you could do a whole lot worse than doom\t\t\t\t\t
