The graphics are amazing, the gameplay is intense, and the music is just as good as I remember.

Playing Doom again after all these years was a real trip down memory lane. I loved the old game, and the new version captures that same feeling of excitement and adrenaline rush. The enemies are just as creepy and challenging as I remember, and the levels are intricate and well-designed. I also love the new weapon upgrades and the ability to customize your character.

Overall, I would definitely recommend Doom to anyone who loves classic shooters or just wants a good, fast-paced action game. It's not for the faint of heart, but if you're up for a challenge, you won't be disappointed. The game is a true masterpiece that will stand the test of time.\t\t\t\t\t
