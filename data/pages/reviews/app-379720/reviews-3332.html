No manual saves. They make you just about start the entire level over each time you need to stop playing or get killed. After the first time that you go through a level, it gets very boring having to repeat the whole thing multiple times.

The cheesy way that the enemies glow and go semi-transparent when they start getting down on health is stupid. The same goes for the way that the semi-invisible, glowing health packs drop from them when you injure them.

I've stopped playing this.

At the very least, wait and see if they add manual saves. Either that or about ten times more checkpoints.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI vary rarely do reviews, but ill put in my two cents for the game.

As im sure many of you know, the story for this game is pretty straight forward, you are a BAMF that is on mars, an evil coperation in their greed unded up opening a portal to hell....again, and its your job to go into hell and take care of business. Whats really nice about this, for those who are long time fans of DOOM, this game actually adds a lot of lore to the game, as you discover your player charater, DOOM Guy, DOOM Marine, DOOM slayer, is actually a warrior who has been doing this for over a millenia so thats a pretty cool little bit of information they added.

The game play is very fast paced, in fact, its tireing at times. Do you ever go to the movies, watch a movie that was so action packed you get out of the movie actually tired because it took so much to watch it? Yeah thats what this game is like. Its non stop action, lots of fast movement, twitch shooting. Whats so awesome about this game, is it really makes you feel like a bad ass, you can pull off amazingly cool moves with ease.

For fans of DOOM, this is an amazing homage to the originals, its not a cover shooter, its not a puzzle game, its a DOOM game. The way the game works is you pretty much roam from ambush site to ambush site, fighting large swalths of demons in each location, with minimal demons between, mainly to keep you shooting. This can at times be repetative, but the changing enviorment, and the ability to chage the weapon load outs manages to keep it fresh.

All in all, this game is great, if you are a fan of the original dooms, get this game, the single player alone is worth it.

The following is just a quick bullet point of what i foudn was good and bad about the game.

PROS:
Fast action
Great throw back
Beautiful graphics
Well optomized, (For NVIDIA at least)
Lots of blood and guts
Chain saw finally put in a good place (Before it always seemed like an akward weapon as it was so dangerious to use, now its based off fuel and each enemy consumes x amount, but chain saw kills drop a crap ton of ammo.)
The secrets are awesome
Levels are great to explore
Single player campaign is amazing.

Cons:
Slight repetativeness
Not a very challenging game (Even on nightmare, not counting ultra nightmare because ultra is the exact same difficulty just only get one life, the game is really easy as long as you keep moving and jumping around.)

Neutral:
Story, i mean its good in that is very simple, kill demons, but at the same time, its so simple and almost cheesy, but in a good way.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPhew, the new Doom game is good! In fact, it's really good! I almost lost interest in fps's in the last couple of years, but this? This is something else. The combat is brutal, fastpaced and diverse. The exploration is engaging and the maps are huge!
Every time things are about to feel repetetive, a new type of demon is introduced, changing the battlefield. Or you get a new powerup you can play with. There's some really deep player stat progression going on at all times. And you are rewarded for finding secrets with points you can use to upgrade either your suit or your weapons.
There are a lot more tactics in this game than you might think. Powerups and the hugely varied types of demons make you pick different ways of approaching a battle. It's really rewarding when you find a tactic that works and you just kick major ass! Love it!
17 hours for my first play through. Now I'm going to replay certain levels where I missed a few secrets.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBottom line up front (BLUF): This game is to Doom 3 what Doom 2 was to Doom 1.

Pros:
-This game doesn't treat you like an idiot with tons of unwanted tutorials, you get straight into the action
-SP Campaign is suprisingly open and incentives exploring
-Constant moving is required to stay alive and keeps the action high speed and intense
-MP is fun enough, but it's not going to redefine anything (I would've prefered no loadouts though)
-Graphics are superb (though I'm playing on the highest settings for everything)
-SnapMap is pretty awesome and have already lost myself for hours making maps
-Codexes provide an interesting and funny look at the DOOM world

Cons:
-For some reason the control settings for SP, MP, and SnapMap are all different. Minor inconvenince
-As stated, MP should've been more like Unreal or classic Doom
-No server browser for MP or SnapMap (not an big issue in MP, but is kind of annoying for SnapMap
-It came to an end

Random Musings:
-Some elements in my SnapMap maps haven't worked correctly, still not sure if that's my fault or if there is another error going on
-Coop is only in SnapMap, not the campaign (apparently people are confused about the way Steam is advertising that)
-I wish SnapMap would allow for the SP ability to have all the weapons in the game (might be a way to do that, just haven't found it yet)

Summary: If you are already a fan of the Doom series, you won't be disappointed. If you are looking for a shooter that is a ton of fun, buy this game. If you are a fan of CoD multiplayer, I'm not sure I would recommend it. Hell, even fans of CoD SP might not like it, as you have to constantly be moving and there is no health regeneration (though Glory Kills provide a VERY fun way to recover health and armor, chainsaw kills do the same for ammo). If you are on the fence, buy this game. I give you my word you will not be disappointed.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlay time: 20 hours: Single player campaign: +- 19 hours.
Should take far less time if you just run and don't take in the views. :)

So I just completed Doom's single player campaign after a 19 hour trip through hell that was like heaven. I played doom as a very young kid, then I played Doom 2 and I also loved playing the custom maps! Unlike most people I really enjoyed Doom 3 as well. Although it wasn't like Doom 1 or 2, it experimented with so much and geniunely frightened me with it's well designed monsters and frightening atmosphere! It also had DoomEd
and I spent countless hours making maps of my own. Now, on to Doom 4.

Holy mother of barons, what a game! The format is very simple, get into a simple area, and move! Run, use pickups, get health, destroy those demons, tear them apart, run, get powerups, eviscerate more demons, repeat! It seems like a repetitive formula but that is what Doom has always been! Running and gunning! And it was so much fun! Finally, finally,
they even brought back the Baron of Hell, my favorite demon from Doom! Man guys, the demons aren't lumping, drooling muscleheads like in Doom 3 either! They run, dodge climb up everything and come after you to rip you to shreds! Such an improvement over Doom 3! There was this one stage where I had little health left and I frantically tried to get away from a hellknight and jumped down a path and as I looked back in fear I saw him jump after me in all his glory,
trapping me. That's when I knew, well done Doom AI team, bravo.

The only disappointment perhaps was it's lack of plot atmoshphere. Don't get me wrong, with Doom I'm not expecting much but I do feel that the voice recordings and videos of the PDA system in Doom 3 created a brilliant atmosphere. I feel they could have introduced all the cool sciency parts of the first UAC complex in a different way. The walls and walls of text the pda's provide you do feel kind of empty. I also feel the characters are extremely forgettable. Dr Betruger from Doom 3 genuinely frightened me in a 'This guy is satanic as hell'-way.

If the long single player campaign with secrets and battles abound isn't enough to satisfy you there's still the multiplayer and snapmaps! Especially the snapmaps, with it you can create your own multiplayer or single player missions, I can't wait!

9/10

TLDR version

An awesome old school shooter in 2016. That's NOT an easy task to pull off, well done DOOM team!\t\t\t\t\t
