\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt took 8 years for id Software to create this game and they have nailed the experience that they were hoping to give to players. Great gameplay, visuals and music.

Much like the original Doom, Doom 2016 allows its gameplay to be the peak of the game. From start to finish it is a complete shoot-fest. Not for one minute did I feel that the gameplay slowed down. There were no unnecessarily long cutscenes or dialogue. Just game. And this is what id Software have always done well. The gameplay is smooth, quick and easy to pick up.
It's such a great breath of fresh air in comparison to all other fps shooters out on the market right now. The abstract map layouts and weapon designs anchors this game back to the old-school style shooters rather than the "duck&cover" shooters we see today.
The visuals of the game are fantastic from its grand environments to the small scale details of horror and gore on every map.

Some missions I found much more entertaining than others and perhaps the story could have been a bit more interesting but in the grand scale of things these dont bring the game down at all. The only thing I would have perhaps liked better was if there were more bosses in the game. Maybe one after every 2 mission. You only face your first real boss around halfway into the game and I feel that's making the player wait a bit too long.

Doom 2016 was an amazing experience and I am hyped to see Eternal soon this year.

7//10
