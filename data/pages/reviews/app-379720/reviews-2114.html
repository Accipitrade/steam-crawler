\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWas it fun? Yeah.
Did I get to smash a bunch of demons? Yeah.
Did it look great? Totally.

I enjoyed Doom in the beginning. Toward the end, I just found myself asking, "Can this be the last level?".
The story was forgettable. The characters didn't make me feel anything.

At one point, you can get an upgrade that gives you infinite ammo as long as you avoid taking enough damage to drop below a certain amount of armor. This was fun, but trivialized the rest of the game.
I suppose I should have been playing on a harder difficulty, but I don't think that would have left me feeling any more positive about the game.

Doom promises demon killing, and it's great at that. It's just not a game I see myself coming back to ever.
I give it a "meh".\t\t\t\t\t
