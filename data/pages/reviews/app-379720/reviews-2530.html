\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA solid title overall, I was a bit skeptical at first, I was expecting an generic corridor shooter that had shamefully had taken DOOM's name.

However, what I got was tense, exciting and gory shooter, faithful to classic 1990's shooters for the most part.

There is no need to talk about the story much, since it's never been the main focus of DOOM, but it serves it's purpose.

In terms of negatives, I don't have many. The lack of a quicksave/quickload feature is quite disappointing. The multiplayer in all honestly is run of the mill, you may have fun for a few hours but will quickly get boring.

I will recommend this game to people who are itching for some good action.

P.S The music in this game is badass. It's worth listening to the whole soundtrack.\t\t\t\t\t

I found other issues along the way, a patch could easily fix but at this point the game came out 2016 I doubt they will bother.
