\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I got this game, enjoyed it, played it for about 15 hours. One thing the game does that I actually like is it has secrets and you can actually upgrade your character in the game to more easily find these secrets. This to me, encourages you to go back after you upgrade and pick up everything you missed. I did exactly that, took a break about halfway through Chapter 9 (out of around 13 I believe) and went back and collected all the missing secrets with my newfound abilities. This took me like 4 hours and I felt accomplished, logged off, and logged back in the next day to find all my progress missing.

This game has a crazy mismanaged save system and after backtracking, which I remind you the game encourages you to do, and returning to the chapter I was on it overwrote my save to the state where I first reached that point. A little searching and I found this bug has existed and Bathesda has known about it since June. This happened to me in October. This frustrated me to the point where I probably will not complete the game. I am a new father and game time is at a premium so when 4 hours of it is completely wasted I get very annoyed. There's no excuse to fix this known bug 4 months later or at least not warn people about it after your game prods you to do exactly this. Every time I log in I get a banner shilling the game's DLC but they can't stop me from wasting 4 hours on a bug they also haven't bothered to fix?\t\t\t\t\t
