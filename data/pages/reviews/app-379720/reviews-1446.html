\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat to say that hasn't been said already a thousand times over? Doom wipes the sour taste of Doom 3 from right out of your mouth, and rightly so. The narrative makes its presence known just enough but never gets in the way of what you're playing Doom for - the endless happy slaughtering of an unfortunate demonic presence who would have otherwise preferred the Doom Slayer not exist at all. Doom is a stress ball I was happy to squeeze for the 15 or so hours I spent in the campaign.

For as fast as the developers intended the game to be, it annoyed me a little that Doom had to take pause every so often for the Doom Slayer to open doors, pull levers, operate computers and listen to exposition. I get that loading screens were probably hiding behind each arbitrary lever pulling and door unjamming, but it kinda broke the pace every single time. That said, I'm nitpicking. Just play it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM 2016 is great. Many reviewers upon its initial release claimed it to be a revival or a resurgence of the FPS genre, and while I somewhat agree, DOOM 2016 is like waking up after being splashed with ice water while you drink a gallon of redbull and have adrenaline injected into you, it's a fast paced amalgam of guns, demons, heavy metal and raw fun. The demon designs are gross, but appealing in an unelegant way, the level design strikes a mixture of in-between narrow hallways and wide open spaces for the Doomguy to jump and shoot around on. The gmae poses enough of a challenge that you feel pushed by it, but it's enough that when you die you let out a sigh and get frustrated with yourself because these are the same demons you've been shooting, punching and chainsawing in half for the past 15 hours, and the game always remains a challenge throughout. Or maybe I'm just bad at FPS games, take your pick.
I was a little late on the uptake with this game, finishing it after the release of its sequel, DOOM Eternal, but I think it's an outstanding and grotesquely fun experience no matter when you play it.\t\t\t\t\t
