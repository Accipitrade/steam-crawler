\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlthough I was a moderate Doom fan in the early years I was not expecting to like this release very much. I have generally fallen out of love with most shooters. I was very very pleasantly surprised to find that this incarnation of Doom is fantastic! The soundtrack and sound design is top-notch. The only beef I had was a few times I loaded it up and the sound cut in and out as I was playing. I have no idea what caused it and it made me miss some audio-only storylines in the campaign for my first playthrough on some missions. I will have to play those missions again to find out what I missed. The graphics are great and I had no issues at all with FPS or dropouts/freezes - it played perfectly smoothly even when the audio was stuttering and dropping out some. In any case, I suspect it was something going on on my PC so I don't blame the game - it seemed to handle being played very well. The campaign seemed relatively short to me. I stink at games - even on the easy settings - but I felt like it didn't take long to get through. Luckily, there are loads of secrets and goals to go back and replay - and the levels are great so it is nice to have new things to go back and do. In all, I was expecting a solid 6/10 but this easily ranks a 9/10. Now excuse me while I go buy the soundtrack!\t\t\t\t\t
