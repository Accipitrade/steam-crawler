\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo start, I love Doom and everything about the series. So my review will most likely be very bias. I love the original games and still play them using project brutality 3 or brutal doom. I played Doom RPG 1 and 2 back on my old phone when they were out and would still play them today if they worked on newer phones. I even played the iphone rail shooter Doom game they made after Doom 3.

This game in a word is fantasticalwondergoreforia with a cherry on top. Theres, big guns, tons of enemies, well built levels, tons of secrets and unlockables, multiplayer, and snapmaps for those who want to try their hand and making levels. Theres enough to do in this game to keep you busy for hours on end. I've been trying to get everything in the game recently and it really does keep you going and loving the secrets. Recently I found a secret thats not even listed as one of the level secrets that had a birthday balloon and cake in a hidden room. Im amazed at how much side content there is to keep you coming back for more.

But more on the main game content. As for graphics, its beautiful, compatible with older devices while still looking good, and runs at great framerates. Its actually more difficult to make it look bad than anything else because of how amazing the engine is and what its capable of.

As for the gameplay, its old school Doom style with some extras thrown in for fun. You run fast, shoot things, do some glory kill finishing moves, brutalize someone with a chainsaw, and do some platforming to get to the next slaughter room. Simple process but it offers a lot of freedom in ways to kill things. Upgrading your weapons and getting new modes for your weapons spices it up even more.

As for replayability, theres a lot. You will like it and probably come back for more in search of the doomguy figures, weapon upgrade points, secrets, praetor tokens, and missions.

All in all I loved this game and it did a great job in doing justice to the series. Good job ID and Bethesda.\t\t\t\t\t
