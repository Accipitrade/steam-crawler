\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think the original Doom was one of my first games on PC back in 1993. It was revolutionary and I have so much good memories with that game that it's really hard to surpass it. In fact the Doom (2016) can't surpass it. Can't because it's a totally different game. It's good, visually nice, but today's FPS games can't really give back that old Doom feeling.
If we compare it to the old game, it's a completely different gameplay. Enemies are far fewer but more skilled of course.

In my opninion the developers did not even want a pure FPS game (in the the classes original meaning), they wanted some kind of survival horror feeling. Unfortunately for me it failed to give me the horror. You get too soon, too many weapons and never seem to run out of ammo. This means you always feel completely safe. That's not really good for a survival horror wanna be game.

On the other hand the shooting/combat part is fun. So if you take the game as pure FPS it's good. You miss the scary horror but in exchange you blast your way to the end with massive weapons, chainsaw enemies in half and rip apart demons with your bare hands in berserk mode in very-very gorey ways. Unfortunately there are not enough enemies. I would add much more swarms to this game.

Do I recommend it? As a horror game no. As an FPS yes :)\t\t\t\t\t
