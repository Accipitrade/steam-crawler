\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my absolute favorite remakes to release in years; keeps the classic quick movement and fast combat while still interjecting collectibles, secrets, and story. And of course, Mick Gordon is back at it again absolutely killing it with the soundtrack, that I even listen to on my own time on Spotify.

Some small problems I have with this make, however, is that the framerate in certain hell levels is very inconsistent, in the sense of I get over 120 in the level before, but once the next one loads in, it's under 20, and doesn't fix unless the game is restarted, and in some rare cases, the whole PC is restarted. A temp fix I found was to change the "Virtual Texturing Page Size" down to low. Seems to fix it for a while, but also sometimes causes the game to crash.

All in all, a fantastic remake, with it's hitches here and there--nothing's ever perfect, so I'll give it a small pass, but I highly recommend picking it up whenever you get the chance.\t\t\t\t\t
