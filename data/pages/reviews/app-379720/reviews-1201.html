\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a little late to this party but it's one hell of a party here. (Pun intended)
The gameplay is one of the best things this game has going for it. If you are a coward hiding in corners and lurking around, you will get destroyed. The game rewards aggressive play style and exploration.
The pacing itself is also very well done to the point where you are never overwhelmed or bored. As for the weapons, each and every weapon type feels comfortable and precise. There is never a moment where you feel that a weapon is underused as long as you have a diverse play style. (Or you could just kill everything with your chainsaw :P )
Momentum and inertia are a big factor in combat for me personally and I feel that this game makes the contact from point A (The point where you shoot your gun or make a jump/ movement) to point B (The point where your projectile/bullet hits or you land after jumping/moving) very smooth. The physics feel smooth as well and doesn't necessarily put forward the need to simulate realistic physics in order to make the game fun.
And the MUSIC... man oh man. The sound tracks are amazing and give you the rush of adrenaline you need to take down the army of demons waiting for you. The scores in this game have the perfect balance between suspension, intensity and horror. Each track has the right amount of reverb and distortion so as to compliment the gameplay rather than serve as a distraction for the player.
To be completely honest, I never played the OG doom games (I'm not that old, sue me :P ) but this 2016 release has been a fun introduction for me and I would definitely recommend this game for anyone looking to blow off some steam by killing some demons :D
Can't wait to finish this and buy Doom Eternal Later.\t\t\t\t\t
