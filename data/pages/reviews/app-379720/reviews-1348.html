\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt has taken me a very long time to get into the DOOM franchise. In fact, it has been a while since i played any FPS single-player game in a long time. In celebration of the DOOM eternal release, i had bought every Doom game available, DOOM 2016 being one of them. I am so ashamed that i had not gotten into the franchise sooner.

DOOM 2016 is bloody fun. (Pun Intended) The movement, quake-style combat, and music are very remarkable, and i find myself repeatedly playing again and again when i didn't even expect to. This game knows how to get the adrenaline running, your heart bumping to the rock metal music, and your fingers clacking away on the keyboard like never before. That's not to mention as well the many secrets and upgrades the game has, which thrives for you to explore the area and fight whatever's possible. If there's anything i have to point on as a con against this game, it would probably be the multiplayer, as it's not the best and pretty average, but i promise you will be satisfied just by the campaign alone.

If you want adrenaline to rush through your veins like a mad man, this is the game to go with. It's highly enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t
