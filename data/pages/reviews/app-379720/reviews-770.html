\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a masterpiece. From the animations of ripping through demons to the incredible variety of weapon choices to the powerups, this game exceeded my expectations. Being someone that prefers games to be on the less gory side of things, this still grabbed all my attention. Everything feels so fluid and fast paced, with music to match that feeling. The story is interesting, but mostly there to serve the incredible gameplay. The secrets littered throughout each mission are fun to snoop around for and the level design is fantastic in helping the fighting feel so fluid and unique throughout the 12 hours of playtime. There aren't many experiences quite like doom and I highly recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
