\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSimply great. Every single thing in this game is great. The graphics are amazing (on higher settings at least), pretty much every gun is satisfying to use in some way, even the small, weak, infinite ammo pistol that you are given in the beginning. The pacing is great : you spend the whole game travelling from one fight to another, and the further you go, the more intense it gets as the demons that first delt like bosses become frequent. The story isn't the greatest ever, but who cares in such a game, right? Glory kills are just amazing, and although they lose some of their "OH MY FU***NG GOD" effect over time, they always feel great to perform, especially when you can chain a few of them in a row. I highly recommend this game, altough I would probably advise to wait for it to go on sale, as it doesn't last too long (took me about 12 hours to complete with about 75% of the secrets found).\t\t\t\t\t
