\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is a fantastic game with a couple of surprising flaws. There is no bigger thrill than plowing through the demon hordes with a shotgun, or firing off the BFG in desperation and clearing out an entire room, but the game demands that you play it the way it wants to be played. If you've always been a sniper/assault rifle sort of person, this game is your crash course on shotguns and siege weapons. Assuming you can rise to the change in playstyle (or if you like me and played CoD with 6 different shotgun classes) this game will be a hilarious joyride.

The only thing I would change is the amount of time I felt I HAD to spend looking for collectibles, especially in the first couple levels of the game. This got a lot easier to deal with when I upgraded the part of the suit related to exploration.\t\t\t\t\t
