\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game. A great modern take on the classic. I didn't play the multiplayer or snapmaps but I did do the campaign. The spoken story is very minimal, but there are a lot of secrets and interesting things to find that explain more about the world. The main focus is gameplay.

I think the guns are cool and the weapon upgrade system is interesting, you get a few choices on how to use each gun. Combat feels good and the soundtrack perfectly compliments it. The main criticism I have is that the locations and enemy variety can get a little old over the course of the game. A lot of the locations, especially in the first half are a little bland. The second half is much more interesting. Fighting the same 6 enemy types gets a little old, and the flying demons are annoying as hell. Despite this, it is still a great game worth playing if you like shooters.\t\t\t\t\t

Maybe just a playstyle choice.

In the end, I don't really feel like the game is worth replaying, which, at 10h, is not the best hallmark.

Probably get it at a sale, but don't expect anything mind-blowing out of it.
I'll go for a neutral rating here, which, given the otherwise positive score, means a disrecommend.\t\t\t\t\t
