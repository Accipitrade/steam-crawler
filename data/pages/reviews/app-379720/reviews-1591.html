\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHey,
I am generally not a huge fan of FPS, but occasionally I like to shoot something.
Doom is just mindless obliteration of demons and that is why I liked it :)
I would compare to Serious Sam or Painkiller but way more brutal and darker.
Below Pros and Cons in my opinion:
- It definitelly look great
- Nice variety of demons and weapons
- Great performance and idea of Glory Kills
- Interesting challenge system, weapon/armor/rune upgrades
- Brutal and dynamic
- Great music and sounds
- Nice reference to old Dooms

Cons:
- I would say to little boses
- Too little enemies ...
- Plot could be a little more longer and less linear
- I would expect final boss to be a little (way more) demonic
- I miss option to start new game on new difficulty with all weapons / rune / upgrades etc. (or at least upgrades)

Game is definitely worth buying and playing, especially after recent discounts (new doom is coming).\t\t\t\t\t
