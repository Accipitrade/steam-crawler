Just go out there and blow some sh*t up was the only thing that the game was telling us. But that little story was great. It wasnt boring nor repetitive. I mean the missions can be seen somewhat repetitive, but new guns and new enemies made it fun to play. And talking about guns, they were fun to use. Deciding which one i should use and learning when and where was fun. Enemies were detailed and special animations to tear them apart was really really fun. Bossfights were enjoyable and didnt take hours to finish. It would take a couple tries before killing the boss but those were fun and enjoyable. And the secrets were perfect. Playing old levels of doom, and finding toys were too good for a game where our only mission is to kill demons. How our character acts and reacts was fun to watch. Him acting aggressive to robots who are basically helping us, and him hitting and destroying everything. This game was a fun experience. When i first finished i thought it was a bad thing for this game to be short. But no. It is actually a great thing for this game. Because this game has tens of special/secret levels and difficulty levels, people who want to play and explore more can just replay the game while searching for secret stuff and/or using different difficulties. Overall like i said, this was a fun experience. Gets a solid 8/10 from me.
Worth every penny and cant wait to play the next game.\t\t\t\t\t
