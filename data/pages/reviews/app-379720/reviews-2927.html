-The glory kill system is so simple but so smart. Instead of hiding behind cover and waiting to regain health, you regenerate health by jumping right into a demon's face and ripping it apart. The glory kills are just long enough to feel brutal but short enough to keep the action going.

-The graphics, music, and story all perfectly complement this.
