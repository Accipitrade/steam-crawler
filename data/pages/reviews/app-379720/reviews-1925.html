\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts an amazing callback to the shooters of old, with nothing but respect for the franchise. It is amazing. However I do have some nitpicks -- not deal breakers or anything, but it is something I wished fixed.

- Combat Shotgun: The Charged Burst seems to be redundant with Super-Shotgun, and once the Super-Shotgun is mastered there is little use for the Charged-Burst except for long-range use, and it is out-classed by the grenade-launcher and pretty much everything else.

- Heavy Assault Rifle: The Tactical Scope is nothing but disappointment, this is DOOM, not COD, and the mastery just making the scope mandatory due to damage increase is just awful -- if you insist the use of Tactical Scope, it should have consumed something like 5 ammo while dealing 4x the damage as a special snipe-mode for skill-shooting. On the other hand, the Micro-Missiles seems to be fun, the problem with it is that they are too powerful once you mastered them, with little point in actually using the standard shots especially when you got basically unlimited-ammo either by rune, or just by good supply of it. 6 shots is kind of fun, unlimited shots is kind of stupid, 12 shots would have been fine -- i mean there was a cut version of it that uses two racks.

- Chaingun: The Gatling Rotator is just rather redundant, i mean that is basically the standard gatling-gun at this point, and its only point of existing is that well it circumvents the completely artificial inconvenience of the spin-up which in most cases we actually wouldn't have to deal with, especially when the alternative -- the Mobile Turret when mastered could shoot constantly without overheating, and is arguably more fun to use. But at the same time, the Mobile Turret comes with the same problem as the micro-missile, as the mastery devalues the use of the chaingun normally -- at this point if you are low on ammo and need to be efficient, you might as well change to the gatling rotator, its just a press of a single button.

- Plasma Rifle: Boring as hell, its just shooting blue glowing balls, which is sadly even worse SFX than the trailer.

- Rocket Launcher: Eh, the Remote Detonator is fine, and its actually my go-to. The Lock-on burst though, the mastery is quite meh. I mean seriously, why would we want to split three missiles into three different targets? We want to pummel everyone.

- Gauss Cannon: The Precision Bolt is completely meh attachment, compared to the actual Siege Mode. As efficient as it goes, its basically the same ROF as with the siege-mode, but less fun and you can move -- but once you mastered the Siege Mode, you can also move. The zoom function is also COD-esque which we fall on the same problem as the HAR Tactical Scope, while it would make sense given that the Gauss Cannon is actually better suited for sniping, there is just little sniping use in the game -- the Push-Forward Combat coupled with glory-kills encourage getting close and getting dirty which the normal shotguns are quite suited for, this is just not useful.

- The Grenades are generally weak, seriously they are. There is little use for them, the siphon grenade is just unnecessary considering that you can just glory-kill demons one after another and get your health and ammo. The Frag-Grenade is the worse offender, demons keep moving and putting a time-delay explosion that stays on one place is just bad, because you will miss in most cases but it is at the same time weak as hell -- a sticky grenade with high damage but limited ammo would have been more useful, the limited ammo is especially necessary because it gives an excuse for the grenades to be powerful.

THAT IS JUST THE SINGLE PLAYER!

The multi-player is quite fun, but it is obviously not the centerpiece, its basically just a different Quake but with no rocket-jumping and is Doom-themed. It is not something you can have a good mileage unless you are quite competitive yourself.

ADDRESSING THE ISSUE OF DOOM 2016 OUTDATED DUE TO DOOM ETERNAL

I will admit, most of my weapon nitpicks would be addressed in Doom Eternal, like:
- No more chaingun spin-up time
- Gatling Rotator will now have a shield
- Plasma Rifle is now awesome
- Gauss Rifle -- replaced with Ballista now has a useful "precision-bolt" esque use
- Heavy Cannon -- formerly Heavy Assault Rifle, the Tactical Scope is now a sniper-mode that will deal high single-shot damage, for skill shots.
- Shotgun Charged Burst is now basically a Gatling shotgun that fires continuously.

I am well aware of those, but still could have been done on the 2016.\t\t\t\t\t
