\r\n\t\t\t\t\t'Ahhh! Chainsaw! The great communicator!' - Doom Guy

Doom took me a little bit by surprise. I simply couldn't believe that anyone would be able to make an honest old-school shooter. Glad I was wrong. It is really refreshing to just forget about everything and murder demons with impunity.

There are some things that irk me, though.
Fist, the download size is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ huge, so beware if you have data caps.
Second, the whole suit and weapon upgrade system is rather pointless. Sure, upgrades make stuff better, but it seems like this system is just there because all games have something like this.
Third, many combat encounters involve you being locked in an arena-style room and battling hell spawns until everything is dead. It would be nice to have some more roaming enemies.
The last thing is the fact that in few select parts of the game you are forced to waste some of your time and watch someone talking 'at you'. While it is definitely not a deal breaker, I would much rather have the option to skip this on my second playthrough and just go back to gibbing demons.
Apart from these four things, the game is 100% worth your time and money if you are into shooters.

In short:
1. Grab Doom
2. Install it
3. Rip and tear until it is done.
4. ???
5. Profit.
