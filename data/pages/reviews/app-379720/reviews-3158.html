Gameplay is smooth and fast, there are upgrades to unlock and challenges to complete in a very arcadisch style. There is a 3d map that can also show you secrets and collectibles and you can revisit each level and challenge anytime you want. The game took me quite a while to complete and I thought it would be the last leve theme when I was just halfway. At the end of each level you get a combat rating, it also shows you how much secrets you have disocvered and other stats.

Snapmap lengthens your experience as you can still play SP maps with it or make your own levels. I would recommend playing through the campiang in ultra-violence, it's hard but not impossible.
