\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM has been a franchise me and my Dad have enjoyed for years ever since we played the first on PS1.
By god this game brings all those memories back. iD made up for the previous game that we won't ever, ever talk about.

The gunplay in this game is ridiculous, the fact that it feels like the old classic DOOM but 100 x better is ridiculous.
Your movement, reload speed and general mechanical freedom feel just perfect.

The maps are great, they convey the simple 'get these things, open door and kill stuff' just like the old games, but they add the secret optional endeavors perfectly as well. This isn't just a linear story game that tells you go forward. Some secrets open up much better ways to enter a fight you might have previously struggled with for example. The powerups and Praetor suit upgrades for the most part, excluding a few certain ones actually feel like they make a difference in the game.
Pacing is a big issue for me, if i'm not hooked on a certain mechanic or story i get bored, but this was addiction from the start. Story was great, gunplay was perfect and the music.. Mick Gordon has created probably (in my opinion) the best game soundtrack i've ever heard. The way it weaves in and out of lows and mids then kicks in when it needs to deliver an impactful sound is unreal.

Get this game if you've ever thought about playing a DOOM game, get this game if you wanna lose hours to some of the best music you'll ever hear, get this game because Eternal is right round the corner no doubt and looks to be able to top this.

TL;DR - This games weaponry, sound and aesthetic make this probably one of my favourite game ever. Everything is so fast paced but you are always in control. Story sets up nicely and makes for an interesting sequel. BUY THIS GAME

Sincerly, the small kid in this guys head who has loved this franchise for years and has been waiting for them to bring something like this out that allows me to relive my childhood.\t\t\t\t\t
