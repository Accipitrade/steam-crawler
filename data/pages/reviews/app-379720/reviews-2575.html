\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall when i played doom, both the singleplayer and multiplayer, i vividly enjoyed the experience i had with the game, though it wasnt without its issues like most games.

Pros(singleplayer):
-towards the later stages of the game the enemy variety and count was incredibly enjoyable making many different fights enjoyable to run around dodging claws and plasma
-again towards around halfway to 3 quarters throguh the game the weapon variety left nothing to be desired, especially when unlocking the chainsaw and the BFG
-the modelling and texturing was on point for the game, making everything look great (or bad but in the 'good' bad way) aswell as the gore and dismemberment making the game all that much more satisfying when killing
-in correlation with the gore and dismemberment the different glory kills, mostly, felt satisfying and meaty, especially when later on you can make such things easier or faster meaning you cna just rip through crowds of enemies
-the variety of the challenges and secrets that were scattered through the levels were thoroguhly enjoyable to find and collect, if your someone who enjoys scouring every inch of a level for the last secret you cant find, its for you (though there are also upgrades that help with finding them
-checkpoints are fairly forgiving, so you dont need to replay an entire level if you screw up one jump or decide to give a mancubus a hug
-probably one of the best parts of the game that made it so bloodpumping and enjoyable, the sound design and music of this game made every shot feel ncie and meaty while the background music got me bobbing my head all the way through
-with the addition of arcade runnign through the levels trying to beat your own, your friends and other peoples scores is fairly enjoyable with a few different thigns making it a bit of a different experience to playing the regular game.

Cons(Singleplayer):
-the start of the game can be quite slow due to lack of weapons and enemy types, if it werent for secrets and challanges i wouldve probably had a very boring early game
-if you arent like me and replay levels to find missing secrets and collectables or try to complete all challenges n such, the game can be quite short compared to a lot of other similarly priced singleplayer games

Pros(Multiplayer):
-similar to old games feels very nice and fast paced, spawns being fast (based on gamemode in some cases), running around is a great pace and the maps are really nicely scaled for the pace of the game
-nice variety of gamemodes, though many of them are the basic ones you might find in most other online shooters
-nice variety of maps, in the time i did play th emultiplayer i never particularly got sick of playing any of the maps or got them time after time
-character and weapon customisation is really nice and in depth, though its ashame it uses basic characters for multiplayer
-they recently made the multipalyer dlc free for everyone, so youre no longer locked out of content, thoguh it didnt really give those who had paid for them before hand much compensation from what i remember (though i dont specifically remember everything)

Cons(Multipalyer)
-small playerbase compared to many other multiplayer shooters
