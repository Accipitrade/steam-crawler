\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is the first person shooter in which you play as some guy, who kills hordes of monsters. Even though this pretty much sums up the story of almost every shooter game, this game doesn't suffer anything from it. However, I lied and there's a little bit more story than this, there are a couple of cutscenes and information, which you can read in menu, if you're really interested in the world of DOOM.

The gameplay starts off without long introductions and tutorials. You awake surrounded by monsters, quickly grab the gun and get to killing. From the first munutes you get what this game is about and what it tries to convey. This game is all about emotions, every fight makes you feel like fighting for your life. DOOM gameplay breaks the tropes of modern first-person shooters by making a huge emphasis on movement. While it's impossible to dodge bullets in Call of Duty, every attack in DOOM can be dodged. The game also has no health regeneration, so if you don't have health, staying behind cover wouldn't do anything. But those things aren't enough to make the game interesting, the mechanic that really takes it to another level is "glory kill". When you shoot enough bullets at the monster, you can get close to it and perform a brutal takedown. It's not just brutal kill animation, glory kills give you health and ammo. This mechanic sums everything up: You have to constantly move dodging enemy fire, but you also have to get close and personal to recover your health and ammo. Every fight in this game fills you up with adrenaline. For me it felt like Devil May Cry, but from the first person perspective with guns instead of swords.

From technical point of view the game is also great. Graphics looking good, animations are smooth and glory kills feel satisfying. But what is the second best thing in DOOM? Soundtrack. It fits gameplay perfectly, you cannot imagine the game without it. When the music starts and you left alone against horde of demons, your pulse is rising and veins get filled with adrenaline. You just can't stop playing this game until you've ripped and tore each monster there is.

As for me this game is the best FPS ever. I think everyone should play this masterpiece, it's a revolution in the genre like the classic DOOM was in its time. Can't wait for the DOOM 2.\t\t\t\t\t

Single player is an instant classic, and on a techical level its a knockout.

However multiplayer just eh, it is nowhere near how bad it was at launch but I guess this game does need a deathmatch mode.

The forge feels like a glimpse of what it could have been. I wish you could host custom game with more than 4 people, and I can't help but compare the map making with the greatness that is Halo 5's forge.

Say what you will about Halo 5 but it got the job done in terms of map creation. in DOOM you can only select preset pieces meaning the maps will get linear after playing them for a while. However DOOM does have some major features over HALO, like more advanced scripting, spawnable AI, HUD text scripts (for objectives, lore if you're that guy, etc.) and overall it looks way better.

This game is absolutely worth its price tag. Good job bebezbu.\t\t\t\t\t

I can't stomach any gore or watch horror, and Bethesda's Wolfenstein was so sick it ended my purchase of their titles, so I was very happy Doom is tame.
