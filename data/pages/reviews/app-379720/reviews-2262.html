\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM 2016 is absolutely amazing!

There is so much content in this game and a lengthy fast paced, badass singleplayer campaign to go along with it.

Campaign: Amazing! its everything you could wish for as a DOOM fan, story is there but the shooting is where its really at in these types of games and DOOM has a little everything for everyone.

Snap maps: Okish, you can have some fun trying to explore community created maps and I personally have played some good ones although, there are bad maps too.

Soundtrack: Phenomenal! Mick Gordon absolutely delivered us a kickass soundtrack to boot with DOOM 2016 and I absolutely feel the award he won for best soundtrack in 2016 is deserved because it really kicks ass playing DOOM and hearing this soundtrack of Metal and agressive sounds company your journy.

Multiplayer: updated 07-06-2019.
I am sorry to say this but if you expect people to play DOOM multiplayer I am sadly gonna have to dissapoint you. Your best bet would be to find some friends to play multiplayer with because there is absolutely noone who touches the multiplayer anymore. A real shame, because I honestly really wanted to try it out but that's not gonna happen now.

Conclusion: DOOM 2016 is a neat package and comes with everything a 90s gamer could ever want with some modern day paint job to it. Just don't buy it for multiplayer\t\t\t\t\t
