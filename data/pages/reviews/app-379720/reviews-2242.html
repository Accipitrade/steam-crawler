\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a hell of a game this is. For those as yet uninitiated in the world of DOOM, you play as a very angry man with a large arsenal of guns. You are dead set on murdering an array of Hell's finest demons using your weapons. Upgrade your guns by using them more. Upgrade yourself by exploring each richly-- excessively, even-- detailed level. For those on the run, you can also blast through the game incredibly quickly on lower difficulties. But the game is ultimately good for its core element: shooting. They come in a few different varieties, but each and every weapon is uniquely satisfactory, from the starting pistol to a mobilized minigun and even the awe-inspiring, one-shot room-clearing BFG 9000 . The story is... well, it's there. Overall, DOOM 2016 is a fine purchase by any means...
