\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is hands down one of the best FPS games of all time

The map design is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ phenomenal, the amount of options the areas give are incredible. The secrets are also great, many hidden in clever ways. Even the more hidden ones i never felt like they were impossible to find without a guide.

For a silent protaganist who is just angry man shoot monsters, the game has several scenes where he displays character. Such as in the opening, Dr. Hayden says " I did what i had to for the betterment of mankind." AS the Doomslayer looks at a mangled and bloodied corpse of a man. The game has so many of these moments and i love them.

The weapon design is pretty standard stuff, but boy do they feel ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ great to shoot. Every weapon has 2 mods that give it an alternate fire mode which is where each weapon really shines. You can swap weapon mods on the fly. I never feel like the earlier weapons are useless in the later stages, the weapon balance is fantastic.
This also helps with the fast pacing of the game, as you're switching weapons and annihilating everything in front of you. Glory kills are fast and fun, they don't break the pacing of the game at all.

The game really sells the look of a demonic invasion on mars. The visuals are perfect. Blood, limbs, and satanic symbols litter the high tech Mars facility.

The soundtrack is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ perfect, I would go as far to say its one of the best video game OST ever. The blend of Metal and Electonica really set the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ tone. The OST standing alone is perfect and if nothing else Please do yourself a favor check it out. The OST sounds like Ultra Violence and i ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ love it.

Incredible fast paced, lots of map, enemy, and weapon variety. The game feels ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ amazing. Controls are tight and responsive. Each mission has challenges that are fun, none of them felt tedious at all.

I can not recommend this game enough\t\t\t\t\t
