\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is a true gem of a game. Bethesda managed to not only capture the vibe of all the original DOOM, but actually improved upon it by making it even more over the top and fast paced all while still giving you every bit of control you need. This is hands down the best single player campaign I have ever played in a game, and I can't wait to see what's next in DOOM Eternal.
Multiplayer on the other hand is a bit lackluster compared to singleplayer, but I have barely even touched it because I've been having so much fun playing the campaign over and over. This game's single player campaign truely has one of the best replay values out there, especially with the Arcade mode.
Playing DOOM truely makes you feel like you're in the armored suit of the angriest man on the planet, from ripping demons hearts out and feeding it to them, to smashing demons faces in with their own foot. And that's when you're only moderately angry; Rage mode is where the real 's at.

In short, if beating the everloving out of every demon on Mars and in Hell with your bare hands, and turning the finest warriors of Hell into a red mist with literal cannons sounds up your alley, DOOM is your game.\t\t\t\t\t
