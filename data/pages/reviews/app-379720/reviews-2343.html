\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't stand here reading this for too long. This game is all about action, you stop - you lose.

Pros:
  • Battle mechanics - reinvented old-school with parts of RPG elements; your survival depends on you actively engaging into battle, often in melee range
  • Scenery - gigantic planetary facility overrun by demons: blood, ruins, variety of spaces; secrets everywhere
  • Satisfaction - gory mess, chocking enemies with their own body parts, over the top graphic display of brutality in First Person
  • Level design - you won't get stuck in a dead-end mid-fight for sure; secrets everywhere!
  • Quality - sound, video, controls; also METAL
  • Self sufficiency - doesn't require you to know the original games to enjoy it (but knowing the original of course bring even more satisfaction)
Cons:
  • Brutality - your conservative parents would not approve
  • Metal - see above
Neutral points:
  • Not for those wishing to spend some time planning their next move during the battle - you don't have that time
  • Not for those preferring a lethal stealth / high range approach - you have to be in the middle of everything
  • You may find most secrets easy to find - this is not a detective game, what did you expect?

Now get into action and give 'em hell!\t\t\t\t\t
