\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM 2016 puts you in the shoes as the DOOM GUY once again. After a portal to Hell opens on Mars, you are summoned to do one thing: Rip and tear, until it is done.

Pros:
Very fast paced action.
New Glory Kill system.
Diversity in demons, weapons, and customizations.
Very fun, challenging, and memorable boss battles.
Lots of secrets and collectibles.
Lots of information on demons, weapons, main characters, and enviornments.
Lots of replayability.
Multiplayer, while isolated, is also very fun and fast paced.
Amazing soundtrack. Seriously, I hate heavy metal and this game has a great, heart-pounding soundtrack.
Very detailed and unique environments.

Cons:
Game can get repetitive.
Platforming feels slippery at times.

Mixed:
While story was never Doom's main focus throughout the years, the story in this game is non-existent.

Overall:
Overall, DOOM 2016 is a faithful reboot for the DOOM series. This game is fantastic. The combat gets your blood rushing and heart pounding, especially on harder difficulties. There are also lots of collectibles and secrets for all the completionists out there. While there are only 3 boss battles, they are challenging and very memorable. The environments are extremely detailed and unique, especially Hell. I'd recommend this to any hardcore DOOM fans, or any FPS fans.

9/10
