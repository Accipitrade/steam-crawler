SNAP MAP: 10/10 you must use snap map, best feature in an FPS for years, just like the original Hexen and Doom editors brought into the modern age.


Overall Impression: Abritrary rating id give it an 8/10 but I genuinely really enjoy the game and will continue to play it for hours on snap map after completeting the campaign.
