I average a few frames north of 100fps at 4k on max settings most of the time, which is very impressive, especially for a game this beautiful.

My older PC with a Xeon X5687 from 2011 and a GTX 970 ran this game maxed out at 1080p with almost no dips in framerate. That is very impressive for such an old PC where most of the hardware is either from 2009 or 2011 (with the sole exception on the GTX 970 from 2014).\t\t\t\t\t
