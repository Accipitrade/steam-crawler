\r\n\t\t\t\t\tInsane fun. I have played this game in several years (because my system could not handle it until Nvidia Vulkan driver update allowed me to run it).

First time was a couple years after it's release and I played until there was an update that didn't allow me to run it no matter what setting was set. I even downgraded everything and was willing to play it Low Specs.

Apparently they fixed it in the 2-3 years I had it installed. It worked! Currently started again this year and I recognized that everything was easier. The levels were familiar, but I must have matured in gaming to understand the mechanics a little better.

The story, enemies, weapons, abilities, level design, secrets all work in unison to give you a godmode feeling as the Doomslayer. Some skill is required to win some battles. There's always a way once you figure out which weapon is a demon's weakness.

If you're looking into the Doom franchise for some demon gut splattering gaming, this game is for you. For oldschool Doom fans, your love for this franchise will grow even more. The devs really thought this game through and have given the fans a jewel of a game to play through.

Get this game if you're on the fence. I can't wait to upgrade my 11 year old system and play with everything at maxed settings.\t\t\t\t\t
