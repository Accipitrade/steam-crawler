\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLined up several Barons of Hell in a row and let my siege mode gauss canon send them to a lower circle of hell 9/10 (EDIT: score updated below)

In all seriousness, I'm only 10 hours in and this is one of the greatest games I've ever played. I've never dabbled in the Doom universe before, but this has me engrossed and wanting to play everything else out there. As you start to learn what you should and shouldn't do in the big fights, victories feel so much more rewarding. DO NOT STAND STILL. Everything about this game is near perfect except that I wish I could buy the collectible figures in real life.

UPDATE AFTER COMPLETING GAME:
Wow. Great game. Had a minor glitch during one of the wraith battles on Argent D'Nur that was frustrating, but otherwise a flawless game. The lore, mechanics, gameplay, etc. are all masterfully crafted and balanced. 9.5/10 (slight bump).\t\t\t\t\t
