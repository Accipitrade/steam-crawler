\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM-ed: Hell Hath Taken Me!

- SINGLE PLAYER CAMPAIGN REVIEW -
I cannot place enough emphasis on how thoroughly impressed I am with this title. After previous dismal attempts to reboot this wonderful franchise, Id's 2016 offering of DOOM is decidedly the best FPS of the year, if not the past decade.

DOOM does everything right. All fat has been trimmed. This is the leanest first person shooter in years. No weapon reload. No firing from cover. Never stop running. Never stop gunning. Enemy deaths spawn vital health and ammo pickups. It is a very simple recipe and one the devs at Id have delivered flawlessly.

Other reviewers might be satisfied giving this 10/10. In my experience however, DOOM goes to 11!

This game is literally off the charts. The graphics are superb, the gameplay is crisp and agile, and the soundtrack scares the BuhKrishna, BahJesus, and Bohuddah out of me!

I've just recently reached Hell on Ultra Violence. Game is hard, I suck, or both. Regardless, I'm 17 hours in, bought the game on sale, and have totally received value above and beyond the paultry $30 spent.

When I play, my hands get sweaty, my heart races, and I wanna cuss like a sailor... or a space marine killing Hell spawn. Take your pick.

This game deserves a look and is worth every penny, be that $60, $10, or anywhere in between. Considering a season pass to further show my support and appreciation for such a brilliantly crafted masterwork.

Enough talk. Gotta blow up some demons!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore, I made a review saying don't buy this game. The only reasons were the annyoing glitches.
Now that I have these glitches sorted out, I would DEFINATELY reccomend this to anyone who's looking for a game with: Hours of fun, tons of action, and a great multiplayer.

I would like to add, if you want a game that has a great story that is so powerfull to make you tear up, DOOM is not the game for you.

Campaign Pros:
Is hours long
Full of action
Decent story.

Campaign Cons:
Has a glitch that didn't allow me to continue on in my game, as far as I know I'm the only one who's had this happen. (I had to start a new campaign)

Multiplayer Pros:
Action packed
Fast paced

Multiplayer cons:
If you are usingt Vulkan with an AMD card on multiplayer, there is a chance every 10 seconds your screen will flash green. To stop this lower the settings of your game, and switch to OpenGL4.5 for the multiplayer (AMD users who experience this problem only)

Out of 100 I give this game...
88/100

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am going to come out and say this right now...

I have never played any of the DOOM games. I know I know, im not a true gamer until I have played the origianl DOOM, well bah humbug. (I would like to try it though)

So with that as a side note I was skeptical of this game as I am of every game now-a-days, but after talking to some friends and hearing how "awesome" it was and the fact that it went on sale for 50% (i think), I bought it. AND I LOVED IT. This game brings me to my roots when it comes to FPS games. I have had my fair share of BF4 and COD style fps games, where they are fun. DOOM beats them in my opinion. DOOM gets to the point in an FPS game and that is you are given an arsenal of guns in your back pocket. and you have to run around like a crazy person blasting away whatever is attacking you. With a great deal of finese (spelling?). Honestly when i was playing this I was reminded of how crazy Ratchet and Clank for the ps2 could get where i am running around dodging bullets and blasting aliens with my electric shotgun thingy. only this game is a little more violent.

In a nutshell DOOM is everything I wanted in a shooter, AWESOME music for blasting away demons to, with awesome visuals and awesome guns do to so. And it gets your heart going for a good addrenaline (which I only ever feel in counterstrike and rarely BF)\t\t\t\t\t
