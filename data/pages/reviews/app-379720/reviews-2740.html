\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere is nothing more fun than shooting the hell out of demons, and then proceeding to rip their heads off with your bare hands. DOOM's combat is a blast, and each movement feels tight and accurate. Graphically, this game is gorgeous, and it game looks great on most PC's, due to the great optimization. The game does get challenging, but that only adds to the fun.

There is not much to DOOM deeper than surface value, so it can get pretty repetitive. Also, the gameplay can get very stressful, and I actually felt my blood pressure decreasing after I closed out of the game. Pick it up on a sale and you'll have a great time for your money.

8/10\t\t\t\t\t
