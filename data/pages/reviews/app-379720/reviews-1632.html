\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave you played DOOM yet? No? Start.

On top of it being a love letter to the fast paced FPS games of old, DOOM 2016 takes everything the original games created and refines it quite well. Challenging difficulties, a wide a variety of enemies and numerous ways in which to destroy them, and a great selection of weapons.

With the exception of the first two levels, every other level is open ended with a variety of options on how to approach them.

The story is there but it mostly takes a back seat to the gameplay. So if you're hoping for a rich story central experience, this isn't that type of game. There is lore and world building but they come in the form of data pads and collectables.

The game also encourages exploration as the more secrets you find, the more upgrades for your weapons, health, and armour you can get.

Oh yeah, and a soundtrack filled with so much bada**ery that it gives your ears chest hair.

Can't recommend this game enough.\t\t\t\t\t
