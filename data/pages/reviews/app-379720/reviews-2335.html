\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever been a true FPS champion, but always wanted to try it, so eventually I gave it a go. It has a lot to go for: runs really well on any machine, atmosphere is fantastic, there is a story (nothing too complex, but still), and lots of ways to build your hero. It actually captured me, and after 10 hours of playing , I can say hands on heart that this is a quality product, and I can't wait to explore more.

The only shortcoming is the stupid platfrom game type challenges: I really don't understand that in a suposedly fast-paced action shooter why on Earth do I have to bloody Mario myself through half of some of the levels?! There is no option to save, so if you miss that "perfect" jump and die, then off you go, start over from whenver the game decided the last checkpoint was. At times, this really goes on my nerves, and somewhat ruins the experience....or maybe I just suck at controls..Anyway, it is not enough for completely put me off, though it definitely erodes the value of what could have been a perfect game in the genre. If you are better than me (hopefully you are), give it a go, you won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
