\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best modern shooters HANDS DOWN!!!! I'm a HUGE Doom fan to begin with, The classic Doom games are my favorite games of all time and I was really disappointed in Doom 3 when it came out.... so when i heard wind of a new Doom reboot i was a bit worried... But ID Software delivered!!! While this game will never be better than the classics in my eyes, it's still a fantastic game with none of the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ of most modern FPS games. This game stays about 95% true to it's classic roots and even borrows a few things from the Quake series of games which is awesome too! i really loved the Monster designs, they are modernized while still hinting at their classic counterparts! the maps are fun as hell with lots of secrets and fun areas to mow down the demonic hell-spawn!!! the maps even added an element of parkour that I didn't expect to like, but it's done so well and fluid that it just works in this game. Weapons feel satisfying to use and have a vast variety of upgrades this time around which I actually loved! The soundtrack for this game composed by Mick Gordon is brilliant, i love the head banging metal mixed with electronic weirdness, it will definitely get you in the mood to rip and tear!!!! The thing i felt lacking for this game are pretty minor considering this modern age of gaming, and those are: The game feels a bit short... but hey, I'm all about quality over quantity. Snap-map, while they have improved it vastly in the last few patches, it doesn't feel like an adequate substitute for REAL modding support, and a few years down the line when the Snap-Map servers are inevitably taken down we wont have any of the long term mapping/modding community that the classic games have had for years and still have to this day. I cant speak for the Multilayer mode for this game as i have not touched it. over all i HIGHLY recommend this game!!!!\t\t\t\t\t
