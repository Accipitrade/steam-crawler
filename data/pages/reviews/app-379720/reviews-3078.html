Was a fan of the original and I am now a fan of this version too. To be honest, I think they nailed it. Here's what it was for me;

+ Totally bat-turd crazy waves of demon-slaying FPS goodness - as solid as you could want from a shooter. Some battles get extremely intense and challenging, but never to the point of fustration - if the skills are there... the game is fair. The melee component plays a strong role but works perfectly and was satisfying all the way through to the end.
+ The environments looked awesome and conveyed the various atmospheres perfectly; from modern labs and Mars' surface to hell's underbelly itself. Scavenging for secret areas and collectibles never got old.
+ Weapons were fun to use and trust me, you make use of all of them. The upgrades/mods make a huge difference to your death-dealing ability
+ A light story as can be expected, but it does a great job at sewing all the action together in a way that keeps you wanting to press on just a little further before turning off for the night.

- My only gripe is the sporadic performance issues I would experience, but very seldom. My system manages better looking titles without batting an eyelid, so not sure what the problem was, but I don't think everyone experienced this problem.

Picked this up on a 50% sale and it has been totally worth it. Can I recommend at full price? Probably still yes, but ideally wait for a discount of 25% or more.\t\t\t\t\t
