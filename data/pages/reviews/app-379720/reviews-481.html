\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty different than DooM 2016.

Story is much more prevalent here. It tells the story of how Doom Guy saved the world and gives some back story to him along the way. Locations all look absolutely amazing and grand. Imagery and scenery are very well done. Seeing a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Titan carry a huge ass castle on his shoulders was such a wow moment.

The game is pretty difficult this time. Enemies have weaknesses that you need to make use of so you gotta use every weapon type to exploit those. Enemies also spawn much more frequently, they are more aggressive, they deal more damage and they are tougher UNLESS you are making use of the said weaknesses. Ammo count is also incredibly low which also forces a certain style of gameplay onto players.

Doom guy is also a bit more agile and has more options for combat.

Combat design is now much more demanding thanks to the above points. Now you need to keep track of the enemies, your ammo count for each weapon, your essential resources (chainsaw fuel for ammo, flame belch cooldown for armor, blood punch meter for damage, glory kills for health and of course your dash meter), the arena. Not only the strategy but you also need the mechanical skill too. While it is not absolutely required, the things top players do with all the advanced movement and shooting mechanics (like quick switching) is absolutely insane.

Overall tho, I would say this game is a class above DooM 2016 for me just because of the fact that it is defintely an improvement in most areas. On the other hand I had more fun in DOOM 2016, I think this was a bit too challenging for me. It is honestly more difficult than Dark Souls 2 and 3 imho.
