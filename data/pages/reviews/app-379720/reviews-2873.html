\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBoy where to begin? The combat is super fun, glory kill system is an amazing addition to combat that was already pretty great on its own in my opinion. The variety of weapons is wonderful while not being overwhelming which was a big fear I had. Ammo was a little more scarce than I'd have prefered, but it simply forced me to switch weapons a lot so I really never ran out. Sometimes the game felt a little too easy even though I did die a decent amount, but I just came out of playing Dark Souls 2 so I'm spoiled with masochism right now I suppose.

I'm upset to see Bethesda has the publishing rights to this game, I don't like seeing them get credit for anything worthwhile to be honest. Id is amazing. I loved this game so much I've gone and checked out their past work on the Wolfenstein franchise which I also recommend.

Overall recommend this to DOOM lovers of yore, and anyone who loves mowing down hordes of cyber demons with gatling guns and double barreled shotguns, never has a game made me feel more like a badass.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPROS:
+At times you feel like a badass.
+Good graphics

Cons:
-repetative
-Horrible story
-Bad multiplayer
-annoying gun switching
-pointless upgrade system

I was disappointed. I thought I would like DOOM much better. The game to me feels lke Killing Floor with better graphics and a campaign. The story itself is forgetable and half-assed. Halfway through the game I just didnt care about upgrades anymore. It was mostly being lead around by green lights that determines where you go the whole game.Spoiler alert: if you see a path with green lights, and another path with no lights, the no lights one is probably a secret! Enter a new area, fight a wave a demons, enter another area, fight another wave of demons. Just felt pointless and a waste of time. The glory kills were fun for about 30 minutes.

I respect the devs attempted to bring the old style of DOOM into a new era of gaming. I just didnt find it funa nd thought it lacked depth or any meaningful reason to continue playing.

I wouldn't spend over $20 on it. Too overhyped imo.\t\t\t\t\t
