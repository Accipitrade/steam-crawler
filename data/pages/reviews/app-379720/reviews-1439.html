I like that this title is more like the original Doom (1,2,64) and less like Doom 3 which was more like a ScareJump puzzle. There are a lot of collectibles, some of them boost your survivability and damage output. I think it is not necessary to find all of them, but it sure it helps a lot.

I miss a story (background) told by more then text log entries - e.g. some movies, etc - but of course that is not what to expect from Doom anyway, so it is excused. If you need a story play the original series and read all the little text messages and logs in this game - If you dont, just go hunting.

Happy slaying ;)\t\t\t\t\t
