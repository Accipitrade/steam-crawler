\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCannot recommend this game enough! I love it on so many levels. Though I never grew up in the 90s and experienced the old Doom franchise I still felt welcomed into the series and was right at home. It's very easy to pick up and play, there's no complicated introduction needed for you to start slaying demons right off the bat. The difficulty can be described as not obnoxiously difficult, but still enough of a challenge to get your adrenaline pumping, especially during boss fights!

The weapon selection is varied and interesting accounting for all sort of play styles, such as playing as a sniper character, a run and gun character or even melee if you're daring enough. A feature called the glory kill mechanic is the most honorable addition as it can be addictive to see all the various ways the animations pan out depending on which angle you engage it at on the demon. Plenty of weapon upgrades to keep you hooked on trying to collect them all. In terms of sound track Mick Gordon hit it right out of the park with his use of heavy guitar riffs that match the intense nature of the game and act as a crescendo at the climax of carnage.

Rune trials are a new interesting mechanic. They offer power ups for the Doom slayer after completing certain challenges and can re invent you play style increasing the replayablity of the game as a whole.\t\t\t\t\t
