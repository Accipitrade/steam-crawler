\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pure fun. I picked it up after stumbling across the soundtrack while listening to music at work, and like many others have said the sound in this game will blow you away. That was what initially got em intrigued and so I picked up the game on a whim. I've been hooked and playing nothing else during my gaming time the last few weeks.

More than that, I just loved the feeling that "realism" is just totally thrown out the window and the game focuses relentlessly on just being a blast to play. I haven't had a rush like this in a single player campaign in a long time. No ducking for cover or slow, sniping battles. It's all headlong rush, close quarters strafe, double jump, and dodge. And holy cow does it feel awesome.

But it's not just the action either. I would say the game is also a surprisingly satisfying exploration game. Finding all the hidden secrets (which in turn upgrade your capabilities in fun but not really game breaking ways) in these incredibly designed and off the wall levels was so addictive. I found the environments and plot (the plot!!) to be immersive and really got an awesome vibe going.

Pick this one up, you won't regret it. Best gaming impulse purchase I've ever made.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe best fun I had with a FPS in a long time.

Among the many merits of the game like music or graphics, the really polished and fluent gamplay is the element which captivates me the most.
Combat is fast and vicious and every time you have an encounter the actions develops so fluent and nice it is very satisfying, The addition of modern touches like the collectibles to unlock power-ups and weapon enhanments serves to reinforce the gamplay; as well as give more options to face the enemy hordes. There are "touches" of lore which also enrich the game.

Recommended for the player looking for an action game. I find the main campaing replayable as I am running my third pay and the addition of Snap-maps and arcade mode adds even more fun.
Of course, if you are searching for an elaborate story, decision driven gamplay, careful story telling and such,... Better keep looking. DOOM is for the "rip and tear", fast pace, skull crusher player.

Extra point for including the legendary Double-Barrel shotgun.\t\t\t\t\t
