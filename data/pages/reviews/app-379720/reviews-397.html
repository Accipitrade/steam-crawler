\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood story even though it's basic.

Gameplay is solid with mouse & keyboard.

In terms of difficulties, it stays close to the previous installments and Ultra-Nightmare (Nightmare playthrough and first mission in Ultra-Nightmare mandatory to finish the game but it's doable with practice).
Due to the objective focus level structure, there's no Ultra-Nightmare unlimited respawning enemies which i'm equally sad and happy about (for an obvious reason).

Graphics are good and pertinent to the series. However, textures aren't going to age well but heh... i don't care. The design of some enemies strays a bit from previous game but stays okay.

My only complain is the MANDATORY MULTIPLAYER to finish the game. There are some good ideas but it's what it is : A number of cheaters, PvP, going to die out sooner than later (already hard to find a match in europe but there are ways if you despair).

Were it not for mandatory multiplayer gring, i'd give it an 8/10.
It'll still be a 5.5/10 for me.
I really hate when there is mandatory multiplayer (in an FPS) gating completion.\t\t\t\t\t
