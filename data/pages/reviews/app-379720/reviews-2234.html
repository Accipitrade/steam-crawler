\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat's to say, simply a great interpretation of the classic Doom formula. Just enough 'story' elements to be satisfying, but ultimately it doesn't get in the way of what this game knows you're here for:

Give you big guns and point you at hordes of demons in interesting environments and top-notch designed levels.

Since release they've added a bunch of great features, and while the user-designed level interface is clunky, the idea and content are good. The arcade mode with speed runs and point leaderboards is great.

Sadly the multiplayer was never great, and is mostly dead now. It's ok, and if you have a dedicated group of friends it can be a few nights of good fun, but I never found it anything to keep me coming back.

If you loved the original Doom, you'll probably love this game.\t\t\t\t\t
