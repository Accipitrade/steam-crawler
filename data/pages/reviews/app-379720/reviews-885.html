\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe original DOOM is one of my favorite games of all time. Easily in my top 2! I've put more hours into DOOM and DOOM mods than any other game in history.

With that said, DOOM 2016 is practically a perfect entry in the series. DOOM 3 way polarizing with it's slower gameplay and corridor levels. but DOOM 2016 goers back to it's roots. It manages to retain the fast visceral action that makes DOOM, DOOM, but switches up the gameplay for a modern audience with it's quick kill system, and various RPG elements that motivates exploration and achievement scouting.

I understand why some DOOM purists might not dig the new glory kill system that encourages aggression for extra ammo and health, or the arena like level designs, but they are made to work together in a very special way. I was skeptical too, but by the end of the game I enjoyed overlooking the next arena, planning my routes, and charging forward with the knowledge that these demons are meant to fear ME, not the other way around.

The story elements and cut scenes don't drag at all. DOOM GUY is still a voiceless hero who doesn't really care about anything expect killing demons, which is often played for laughs. This was another thing I was worried about considering DOOM isn't loved for the cinematic experience, but I think they balanced it out nicely.

The arsenal of weapons is very satisfying. All the classic guns are here with some new toys to play with. Every gun can be upgraded with alternate modes of operation. The cooldown these modes have encourages you to quickly cycle through your weapons, firing them off one by one for maximum damage output. You really do end up using every gun!

And you need to use every gun to defeat the various monsters in your way. Again, we have a returning cast of beasts along with some new variations. Enemy AI is incredible. They constantly shift, pursue, attack, and spawn in with amazing awareness that feels smart but not cheap. You can die in seconds if you're not careful, but it never feels like the game is being unfair.

Jaw dropping graphics - check
Badass soundtrack - check
Rip and Tear - check

What can I say that isn't already known by the gaming community at large. FPS lovers have already played this one to death and for good reason. Rip and Tear! You'll feel better afterwards.
