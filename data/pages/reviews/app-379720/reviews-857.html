\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI went into Doom thinking I was going to play a regular FPS with a cheesy horror scene here and there...boy was I wrong. It is non-stop "Grade-A" action from start to the very end. This game oozes of creativity!

Pros:
- some of the best graphics I've seen on a PC game. The worlds that you are emerged into are nothing short of jaw dropping. From the crystal clear whites of the mars facility to the fiery reds/yellows of the depths of...well you know where (maxed all settings @ 1440p/165Hz).
- soundtrack goes well with the game and if you are a heavy metal fan you will enjoy the tunes(I know I rocked out to a few of the tracks). Mick Gordon did a great job on this one!
- Frames, Frames, and more Frames! This game just eats up all of the frames your GPU and Monitor have to offer! If you want to test the refresh rate on your monitor look no further.
- The concept is one of a kind! Great story that is easy to grasp even if this is your first DOOM game (which it was for me).
- Boss fights are very well made and thought out. Epic, to say the least.

Cons:
- With the exception of the BFG ,the weapons are nothing special. They are pretty normal of what you would expect from an FPS.
- You have to wait until a checkpoint to save the game. you are forced into playing the game until you find a checkpoint.

Now that I've conquered the 9th layer, I'm ready for Eternal DOOM!
