Sure, it changes the DOOM formula from keyhunting around maze-like levels to a sort of arena shooter, but then again, is that really a downgrade? Maybe the level design isn't as original at times, but it's taking a dive so that the gameplay can be in the splotlight, and the gameplay really lives up to DOOM's name.

While the game still resembles the original DOOM, it takes some hints from games such as Serious Sam or the Brutal DOOM mod, with it's locked off arenas and glory kills. But I think that adds to the original gameplay in a good way, preventing you from retreating out of combat and adding to the over-the-top violence I associate DOOM with.

As is usual for DOOM, the story doesn't really matter that much. The most important thing about it for me is that the DOOM Marine is now the DOOM Slayer, looking like and being an absolute badass.

Really, I could go on with this, but I just urge anyone who considers themself a gamer to play this.

10/10 - One of the best shooters ever made.\t\t\t\t\t
