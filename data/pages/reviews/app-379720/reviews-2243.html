\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIntense and competitive: DOOM offers something new to the FPS genre. DOOM from the very start will be daunting as it only takes one well aimed bullet to end your round, there are no respawns in this game and every kill or death counts in it's personal 5v5 games. The game strays away from the normal beaten FPS path, it doesn't focus on large scale combat but instead streamlines close-combat firefights in between just a few players. You soon realize that to beat your opponent you have to outwitt your opponent. Luckily DOOM allows you to do just that with 40 operators that each contain unique gadgets at your disposal. With an emphasis on destructability each game truly feels different as their are countless strategies on each impressively well-designed map (besides yacht and Barlett U.).

Pros:
+Realistic graphics
+Competitive
+Destructability at its core
+Great core gameplay
+Constant updates

Cons:
-Plenty of hackers
-Hard to learn
-Frusterating
-The grind for DLC Ops is mental
-Cosmetics are completely overpriced and boring

DOOM is my personal favorite game of all time 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
