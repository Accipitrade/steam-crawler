Pros:

+ A meaty, lengthy singleplayer campaign ranging from 12 to 15 hours depending on difficulty setting
+ Technical performance is exceedingly polished all across the board and the game will easily run well on medium and low-end PC's
+ Sound design ranging from gun sounds to screams of demons is fantastic


Cons:

- Sluggish opening hours limit the game from reaching its full potential until a couple of levels further down the line
- Very limited amount of boss fights is a bit disappointing
- End game demon gauntless become repetitive

Overall Doom (2016) is an extremely polished quality game which any PC gamer should at least try. The over-the-top gory, viscious, gratuitous violence is a longstanding trademark of Doom games, and the new game delivers in spades.

I can wholeheartedly recommend this game to any FPS fan and both newcomers and veterans of Doom games.\t\t\t\t\t
