\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSingle player only.

Play on second hardest starting difficulty for the best experience imo.

Start off cowering behind corners trying to snipe things with your pistol and saving your pathetic 20 shotgun rounds for when you really need 'em.

Search for all the pick ups: upgrade your suit (health, armour, ammo capacity), find more weapons, find more mods for your weapons, get buffs to general mechanics and map tracking to find more pick ups.

At some point intense metal music is playing...you're suddenly up in the face of the toughest demon in the game with your triple shot mod on your shotgun - blasting it in the face. You rip off its horn and shove it through its head, then proceed to run and double jump off a nearby ledge, wipping out your chainsaw as you do so, and land on a giant eyeball shoving your chainsaw directly into its eye. Watch as the huge eyeball dies instantly. As you drop towards the sea of trash mobs below you - you wip out your plasma rifle and send out a huge shockwave nuking them all instantly. Realize you've developed no fear and are much much stronger than you were before. Feel like a badass.

For real tho:

The pacing of this game is amazing.
The collectiables are fun to find and give you power ups - but you don't have to be anal and find every one (you can replay levels if you missed something and are going for max power ups on everything).
The graphics are beautiful.
The gun play is insane when you get into it and find your favourite set up.
The music just compliments your epic moments.
The story fits.
The progression of your character into a murder machine is done so well.

On sale for 15 euros this game was a steal. Finding most of the secrets and pickups (and getting all the story fragments to flesh out the lore) - I clocked in at 12 enjoyable and intense hours.

I've never written a review before on steam, but had to do it for this game. It deserved it.\t\t\t\t\t
