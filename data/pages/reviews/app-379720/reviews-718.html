\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am notoriously not a 'shooting games' kinda person. Far Cry and Borderlands are probably my favorite shooter franchises, though I grew up on COD and Halo, which still sit near and dear to my heart. But boy oh boy does this game beat them all in the "let's have fun and shoot things" category. People call this a 'pure shooter' and that's so right. All the supporting elements rock: beautiful world design, A+ monsters, interesting lore. The collectibes aren't too arduous and help give the game a modern AAA feel. The upgrades are few, and help lean into the "youre shooting demons and nothing else" mentality. The combat loop works so well and each fight room pumps up youre adrenaline without ever feeling punihsing on the Normal dificulty. And the Glory Kills, incentivising a wild and reckless approach all lead into a game is pure "cool". What else can you ask for in a FPS? The mixture of sci fi and Hell asthectic is refreshing and brillaintly designed. It's a blast and when I wanna not focus on leveling up or discovering stuff or doing quests, I can come to DOOM and just kill things. Oh, and the music rocks. Can't wait to finish up this campaign\t\t\t\t\t
