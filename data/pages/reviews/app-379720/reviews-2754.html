\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA phenomenal reboot of a game that was largely responsible for creating the First-Person shooter genre over 20 years ago.

DOOM takes all of the old school aspects of FPS games and merges them perfectly with new age mechanics. Everything from the health and ammo packs, to the color key system will make the older gamers fondly remember a time when gaming was not over-bloated with needless features, and all the mattered was how fun shooting monsters could be.

Top notch level design makes going through the gory levels of hell a blast. The pacing is fast, fluid, and free from overbearing cut-scenes that so many FPS games rely on these days.

Instead of a 20 minute tutorial and a CGI cut-scene explaining to you who all of your ancestors were, DOOM supplies you a gun within 10 seconds of launching a new game. Some developers should take note on what DOOM is trying to accomplish here. It is not trying to be a story driven space romance, it will never be an open world FPS game with overburdened RPG mechanics. Not every game needs to be nominated for an Academy Award. Sometimes I just want to turn off my brain and shoot demons. And that is exactly what DOOM aims to be: A game about shooting demons.

The mechanics of the game are what make or break a project like this. If the fast paced combat was not engaging, and the shooting felt flat, this game would have tanked hard. Id Software knows exactly how to make an FPS game from the perspective of the player with the gun.


The only negative I could think of seems to be a tacked-on multiplayer. Unfortunately for Id Software, the amount of multiplayer FPS games available to the general public these days makes a game like this difficult to recommend as a substitute. It will never have the following of Counter-Strike, Battlefield, or Overwatch, but that does not matter. DOOM is largely responsible for the success of those games, and it still proves an FPS game can be designed using old-school mechanics and be loved by old and new gamers alike.

9/10
A must play FPS game.\t\t\t\t\t
