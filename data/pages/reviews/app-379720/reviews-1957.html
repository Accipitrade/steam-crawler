\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a massive Quake and Doom fan and decided to pick this up while on sale as I've heard a lot of good things about it and after trying it I can honestly say, almost none of the good things I heard were true.

This game has some of the most disappointing gunplay/combat I've experienced, none of the weapons have any weight to them so much so I felt like I was firing pop guns most of the time. There's also no feedback for when you get hit, the old id games had visceral grunts and screams when you took damage but there's nothing in this to indicate any sort of damage at all so you can end up with no health with no warning at all.

The audio balance is shockingly bad, guns make near zero noise, I can hear zombies screeching from literally the other end of the map through 9 rooms and the voice overs and mind numbingly loud.
To top that the audio is actually broken in some areas, sound will jarringly change where it's coming from when crossing certain areas. For example on the first mission while I was outside the audio jumped from sound like it was a way of ahead of me to right behind me, backing up and walking forward again across that area produced the same result.

The music is one of the most talked about parts of this game, yet for how good the soundtrack really is it never plays. Honestly the music feels like it's never on at all and that's a shame because it's the best thing the game has going for it from what I've played.

Now the performance is good, generally speaking, when actually playing I maintain a steady 200+ fps at 1440p max settings. HOWEVER, The game suddenly drops to 10fps and lags like nothing I've ever seen before during certain menu options like the suit upgrades.

Speaking of menus, there's no settings section on the main menu. You legit have to go to the multiplayer section just to change your basic resolution and video settings for some obscure reason.

All in all, I can't recommend this game at all. If you want to play a proper doom or Quake game then go and get one of the actual doom or quake games, hell Doom 3 and Quake 4 were more enjoyable than this honestly and they didn't take up 70 ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ gigabytes of my HDD either.\t\t\t\t\t
