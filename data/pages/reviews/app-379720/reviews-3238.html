Fast, visceral. Involves punching demons in the face in various arenas and the occasional corridor.

STORY:
Simple and self aware. Mostly revolves around providing context to punching demons in the face.

GRAPHICS AND SOUND:
Paticularly impressive particle effects and lighting as well as good sound design makes every punch to a demons face feel realistic. Soundtrack is not necessarily easy-listening but compliments the action of punching demons in the face fairly well.

MULTIPLAYER:
Old-School arena deathmatches somewhat hindered by modern gameplay mechanics such as levelling and perks. Considerable lack of punching demons in the face.

OPTIMIZATION:
Reports of varying performance - Personal experience throughout campaign framerate did not fall under softcap of 60fps, allowing for smooth demon-facepunching experience. Running GTX 970 and AMD FX-6300 4GHz.
