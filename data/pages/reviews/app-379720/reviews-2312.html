pros
+ gunplay
+ weapon variety
+ graphics
+ performance

cons
- plasma rifle is weak as hell
- gameplay: forced to use glory kills to get health and ammunition
- arena shooter sections

\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttl;dr: Go Buy This Game!

"Better late than never" a lot of people have told me over the years, and boy have they been right this time! This is one the most satisfying fps's I've played in the last decade. It's blend of past and present game conventions were masterfully done and is a blast to play! Take how the upgrade system [modern] works: aside from the combat challenges (most of which you'll complete over the course of action anyways), most of which is aquired through discovering secret areas and the loot that they hold [past]. The "glory kills", a recent mechanic, not only look cool but also helps keep you in the game and is implemented near seamlessly into the action. All this being said I do have one caveat: the action is cooled down a bit between areas and as such it lacks the visceral feeling of constantly dodging death at every step that the original Doom and other first person shooters of the era had, and I felt the difficulties below Ultra Violence were much too easy. Admittably though, I don't most players having this problem because they haven't grown up with the crazy hard video games of my youth and will be completely satisfied with their purchase.\t\t\t\t\t
