Dads Back and He Finally Brought the Milk

Doom returning to the classic form of their FPS formula with the advancements made in Quake and Rage is like receiving every birthday gift you could have ever wanted as kid all on the same day. This game is a prime example that you can take a series that fell by the wayside and bring it back in a major way. Not to say Doom 3 was a bad game, It just wasn't a good Doom game. I played this on Ultra-Violence and even when I wanted to tear out my hair and scream for John Romero to end my suffering because THE ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ PINKYS WON'T LET ME EVEN REMOTELY MOVE, I still had a wonderful time. I honestly just can't wait to dive into Eternal when I get the chance.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game so far, and have (nearly) gotten all achievements on PS4 since my PC crapped out on me for a few months. My overall positive experience aside, there *are* a couple of things that keep it from a 100% gem in my opinion.

Pros: The combat is very fluid/intuitive and makes sense even to new players to the series (like myself). The difficulty is brutal, but in a way that makes sense. There is very little in the way of BS mechanics that make deaths feel unfair, as well as plenty of tutorial tips for new players that aren't obtrusive to seasoned players. The world is gritty, but in a way that inspires awe. The level designs are gorgeous in their hellish (heh) way. The story feels very immersive, if you are thrown straight into the campaign without much in the way of "here's WHY". Being a Souls-series player, that's fine by me and almost more comforting to not have the entire story all fed to me at once.

Cons: If you're trying to get glory kill specific mission goals, be prepared for a couple of head scratches at how sometimes it won't give you the type of kill you need. I'm not sure if I just have the setups for the kill wrong or if the window or position is just so precise that I can't get it consistently. Chalk that up to me being a novice, but still a bit frustrating to reset due to failing the animation too many times. The ledge grabs are a bit janky sometimes as well, but that could just be my PC not quite coping with the Ultra settings or maybe its related to camera positioning (which might also explain the earlier mentioned issue with glory kills)

TL;DR -- Solid 9.5/10 other than a few quirky bits with movement and attack animations.\t\t\t\t\t
