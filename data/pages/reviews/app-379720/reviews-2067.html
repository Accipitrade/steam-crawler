\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I never really played DOOM in my life, including the original games. I got Doom 2: Hell on Earth and Ultimate Doom a few months back, then I decided to get this version. In all honesty it's one of the greatest games I've ever played, if not the best. The controls are smooth and responsive, the game play is fast paced and exciting.

The demons look absolutely amazing and the lore surrounding them has been absolutely awesome. Goes without saying the brutality and violence make for an epic atmosphere, glory kills are one of the most satisfying game mechanics I've ever seen in an FPS. It definitely has the look of an FPS but it doesn't feel like others, it is unique in it's story and characters, and lore as well. I would recommend this game to anyone really, fan of FPS games or not. It's also a breath of fresh air to have a triple A title with an awesome campaign mode with replay value, all the secrets and exploring add to said replay value and make every play through different.

Multiplayer is fun, it's an awesome game mode for those who enjoy playing with others, there's a variety of different game modes and some cool features included that change things up and break up the monotony that plagues most multiplayer FPS games.

Finally the snapmap editor is wicked, basically allowing you to create your own maps for either multiplayer matches or single player stages. You can use classic Doom elements or elements from this version.\t\t\t\t\t

How long did it take me to beat all 13 levels and get every collectible? about 32 hours. After that, you have 13 classic stages you can play, in addition to an Arcade mode, Multiplayer mode, and SnapMap (create your own maps or play others' maps.)

For $20? This is a steal. I reckon this game was probably $60 when it first came out, as DOOM Eternal will be, but trust me when I say it's worth the price of a full game because of the amount of content. Definitely get it, I'll for sure be pre-ordering DOOM Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
