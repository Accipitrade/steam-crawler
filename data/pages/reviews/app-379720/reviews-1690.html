\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery high quality first person combat and exploration gameplay with tightly designed levels.
To make the comparison simple, the feeling you get from playing this game is very similar to DOOM 1 and 2, even though the gameplay itself is quite different.
Bad parts:
- Levels are so heavily scripted that the scripting can glitch out at times, requiring a manual checkpoint load. this is rare, but it can happen.
- Most of the combat in the game takes place in rooms that are sealed until you finish the fight, which means that combat segments are very disconnected from exploration segments, even though both things are done very well.
- Instead of any kind of manual saving, this game relies entirely on unstable automatic checkpoints that don't work very well, since some but not all of the progress is lost on death it makes dying more confusing than anything else.

Good parts:
- Literally everything else. The developers knew exactly what they wanted to do, how to do it, and had enough resources to make it happen, easily one of the funnest games to come out of the 2010s.\t\t\t\t\t
