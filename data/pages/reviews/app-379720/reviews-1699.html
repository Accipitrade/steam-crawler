\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGenre:
-AAA First Person Shooter

Fit for me:
-I loved the original Doom games, so this should be a good fit

Pros:
-The soundtrack! Seriously, one of the best, most epic soundtracks for a game ever. It's badass death metal that fits the environment perfectly.
-Extremely responsive and crushes the basic mechanics. Moving, strafing, jumping, and switching guns feels so good. It sets the bar for the fps genre.
-Fast, fun, brutal, bloody gameplay that is fun and rewarding. You need to always be moving but it never feels overwhelming. It's a perfect balance of challenge, tension, and chaotic fun.
-Well designed difficulty curve that slowly ramps up the challenge. W/ enough practice, any one can beat this game but almost every one will feel their limits pushed.
-Excellent boss battles that get tougher and tougher. You will get better at proper target selection, and the most efficient use of your ammo. At first it will feel impossible, but you get closer to beating the boss w/ each try. It feels great to finally beat a boss battle after trying a dozen times; I always let out a sigh of relief when it's over.

Cons:
-There is a story here, but it's not the focus
-It may start to feel repetitive near the end of the game as they start to run out of new ideas or interesting environments to check out.
-I had a headache when I played for more than 2-3 hrs at a time. Not everyone will experience this, but this was my own experience.

Thoughts:
-This choice of a game from the Doom franchise was really risky but paid off big time. The safe bet would have been to offer a throw back retro style doom similar to Dusk but instead, they decided to go down a different path. Now, I love the old school Doom games, I love Dusk, but I also love this game. It is fun and chaotic and feels kind of like a fps puzzle. This is a game where you never stop moving. You will have to learn the timing and movement patterns of all enemies, and w/ enough practice, you will eventually clear a room. This isn't a perfect game, but you know what you are getting if you've watched the trailer, it's gory awesomeness!

Consensus:
-Solid Game (Tier 3 and makes up 35% of my reviews). You know what you are getting here. If you haven't played it yet, it's time for you give it a try. I recommend this to any fps fans out there.\t\t\t\t\t
