\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't think any game for years has nailed graphics, sound and gameplay this well for me. The progression in difficulty and player skill and gear is excellent, and addictive. I love a game with a good upgrade system to vary gameplay - DOOM has it. Graphics are solid, performance is excellent, playing on 970 Strix in 1920x1080, not all the way maxed, though.
It's also fun. 'A bloody good time', I agree. Combat flows easily, you feel like a bad ass: you're the scary-head here, not the enemy, and it feels and shows.
It's a standard length game, but you really want to check every corner for collectables, as they play a part in getting the upgrades you want. Exploration is surprisingly fun, the platforming elements on par with the rest of the game.
Story is minimal, but more than sufficient to keep you leaving a trail of demon entrails, broken goat skulls with the horns stuck into the eye-sockets, and general gore behind. You kill demons. That's the game, and it's enough!
There isn't much bad that can be said about this game. It's a gorgeous re-imagination of a legend.\t\t\t\t\t
