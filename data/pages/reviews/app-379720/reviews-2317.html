\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly the best Doom game in my opinion. Not as influential as the original, but if I had to pick one game to play right now, I'd choose this one because it's just the most fun.

They did a great job of changing what needed to be changed, and keeping what makes Doom, Doom. Graphics are great and the controls are intuitive. It's natural to fly around with a horde of hellspawn chasing you, and the gameplay is just good ol' Doom fun. I love the electro/metal hybrid music that plays when you're fighting. They also kept the old school secrets around the maps, and you can even find small sections of the original Doom maps. It's pretty cool to walk through a threshold, and they you're in 1993 graphics. Finally, the customization is really friendly to all types of players. It's a good way to reward exploration, but at the same time, if you're only interested in upgrading a few things you should be able to do that pretty easily.

It's a good, fun game, plain and simple. Definitely would recommend.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR 9/10 is a great shooter that's good when you can't get into other games. There's also a free demo. Keep in mind with the demo that the gunplay diversifies greatly slightly later.

I bought, started, and finished this game within 1 day. That's right, I played it 11-12 hours straight. That is the campaign length and that is the quality.

Doom is a violent, faced paced shooter which is one of the best badass simulators on the market. It has some good humor and an awesome soundtrack. The gameplay is basically killing demons to heavy metal, which is amazing. If you're like me and you want to get into a ton of games, but can't ,this is good because its just fast paced fun. There's no grinding, and mecahnics aren't difficult to figure out. My only complaint is that I got lost a few times in the maps. I highly suggest everyone who likes shooters to pick this up.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTHIS GAME IS AMAZING - GO BUY IT
9/10
GET IT AND PLAY IT

~~~~~~~

There are so many things that this game got right - I could probably praddle on about how the upgrade system is good, and the level design is both simple and smart - or how the story is quickly and easily conveyed without requiring depth. But let me summarize what makes this game so great: responsive, strong, brutal combat.
Blowing away enemies simply FEELS good in this game, and that's primarily what you do. While you're platforming and solving simple puzzles - you're anticipating that next fight - you're waiting for the amazing soundtrack to swell and for the chaos of combat to ensue.
The game is a bit short, and the multiplayer is lackluster at best. But the main came is more than enough to sate the appetite of those looking for a good game to play. It's easy to pick up, quick to master, and satisfying. There are few things in this life as cathartic as ripping a demon open with your hands - or beating one to death with their own leg.

If you don't own this game, buy it.

I'm removing 1 point for three reasons:
1. The campaign feels a bit short, I wanted more. Not that big of a deal - but come on ID Software!
2. Multiplayer is a pile of trash, it feels like it reduces from the experience - although it adds to the value on paper
3. There's quite a bit of legitimately satanic symbols here - so those of religious walks are going to be turned off - even though they of all people will be willing to smash demons to bits.

~~~~~~~

AGAIN:
9/10
BUY IT!\t\t\t\t\t
