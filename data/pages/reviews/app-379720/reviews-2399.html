Is DOOM for you?

if you are asking yourself this, you are reading the right review. I'm gonna start out with describing what the DOOM franchise is all about. In DOOM you play as a voiceless character know as "Doomguy" or "Doomslayer" you are thrown into a game without any backstory and the first thing you do after freeing yourself from your shackles is grab a laser pistol and blast the brains out of a demon. This sort of "gameplay first, shenanigans later" mentality is what had me hooked on DOOM from the beginning, its raw FPS carnage and this is a good thing. The feeling of complete control over your character in the beginning allows you to take on the role of Doomguy. This is reinforced by the story that develops, it manipulates your emotions and keeps you on the edge like any story should but allows you the freedom to rip apart demons unrestrained.

Gameplay

The most important part of DOOM is the gameplay. Overall, anyone who has ever played a first person shooter before will have no problem with DOOM, even people who consider themselves bad will still have fun. There are several levels of diffuculty that actually make sense (unlike other titles) they have a logical progression from "I'm too young to die" to "nightmare" (for people who really want a challenge) and this makes the game more accessable than ever. For me DOOM is quite a relaxing game. I know what you must be thinking, "wtf, this isn't relaxing! there are explosions and gore!" and for you, that may be true, but the gameplay of DOOM (especially for good players) I like to think of as a sort of ballet dance of destruction. The way Doomguy moves in the limited space you have and the level design make for a fluid and uninterupted experience. While nightmare diffuculty poses a great challenge, DOOM on any other diffuculty can be a relaxing and casual game, you don't have to worry too much about losing progress because checkpoints are positioned in just the right spots to make a "frustration free" enviroment.

Conclusion

I highly reccomend DOOM, its a must have for anyone interested in the first person shooter genre.

OVERALL: 9.5/10 \t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou don't quite realise how much you've been missing destroying assorted demons to the tune of hellish heavy metal until you've played this game, it fills a gap in your life you didn't know existed. ♥♥♥♥, I need to install this game again.

But for real, the gunplay is truly exceptional and the enemies are actually for the most part pretty intelligent so it doesn't feel like you're just demolishing a load of glorified mannequins (as is the case with many fps games). The visuals are very impressive and mostly polished well, with only a few texture problems here and there. However, it's the music where this game truly stands out for me. I highly recommend watching Raycevick's video on the composer and how the music interacts with the gameplay in real-time:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7X3LbZAxRPE

This game is such a fun solo experience (can't comment on the multiplayer tbh, never tried it). If you haven't played this game yet, absolutely do because you're truly missing out.\t\t\t\t\t

DOOM Review
Rip and tear!!!

Pros
+Has an awesome campaign that can take up to 10 to 15 hours to complete with tons of secrets and collectibles to find
+Intense and fast-paced gameplay that newcomers and veterans of the series will enjoy
+Very Fast-paced arena based multiplayer
+Includes a very easy to use yet very in depth map making tool to create nearly endless amount of content
+Insanely well optimized for PC
+Super Turbo Turkey Puncher 3 is GOTY
+It's f*cking Doom

Cons
-Once in a blue moon the game can be a bit glitchy
-Multiplayer can be a bit dull and uninspired

Conclusion
Rip and tear until nothing's left. Words to live by lol. This game is incredible in almost every aspect. The campaign is amazing, the multiplayer is fun, snapmap is very creative, the gameplay is very fluent and fast-paced and to top it all off it runs like a dream on PC. Anybody who enjoys old school FPS's will find something to love in this game. It's funny to say but even though this game maintains the spirit of its old-school roots, it's one of the very few AAA games that truly feels next-gen to me. The game looks fantastic but run so good and feels great to play! And on top of that the game isnt overloaded with pointless microtransactions or loot boxes which sadly deserves its own shout out nowadays lol. The game is amazing and I would highly recommend picking it up if you haven't already. Definitely worth every cent of its purchase price.

4 out of 5
