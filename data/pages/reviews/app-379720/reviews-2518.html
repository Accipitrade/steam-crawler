\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNow THIS is how you do a reboot. One of my favourites. Taking the ideas, enemies, settings and music of the original and enhancing it. I cannot remember the last time in over 20 years of gaming that I enjoyed a game so much. All the embelishments - story, glory kills etc work brilliantly for me. The gameplay is fast, but without the tedium of almost non-stop enemies such asthose in Serious Sam. So many games that I play have sections that feel like a chore. In this, completing a frustrating section gave me a a great sense of satisfaction.

The one this that come take this game to the next level is 4-player campaign COOP (I remember how good this was with the original) - ideally it should have a separate campaign as the single player is really well tuned for that experience.\t\t\t\t\t
