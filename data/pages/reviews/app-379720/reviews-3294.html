\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNostalagic at best!!

Fast pace FPS, no cover, no stealth, just pure nonsensical gunblazing gorefest!!

The game itself quite optimized, and the art direction from this doom are awesome, especially I can see the model from the collectibles that i collected within the game :D

The character development and story kinda meh, cause its DOOM!!, all you care is just tearing apart your foes limb from limbs!! And yeah, UAC f***ed it up again by experimenting bunch of hellish stuffs, and backlashes to themselves.

Multiplayer kinda okay, felt like Unreal and/or Quake Arena, but sometimes have balancing issues. Need to retweak the demon, so its not so powerful, make a limitation ability for the demon, not just one hit KO everything. So for the very least they are durable and powerful but need to play smart with it, not playing like a almighty stuff that just rampaging everything with it.

Snapmap, owh yea, its cool that they include Snapmap for this game, so the community can expand and customizing the maps and makes an interesting coop map that me and my friends can play along with it, the more the merrier!!

Okay that all so far, so my rating is a BFP9000/10

-Peace!!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere is a saying: "The Devil is in the details". And I can tell you, new Doom has a hell of a lot of Devils.

Small things are exactly what, in the end, make new Doom worthy of the name. The first ones that come to mind are things like:
1) Pinky's classic death sound
2) Visual references to the classic maps (MAP07: Dead Simple from Doom 2, for example, the one where Mancubi spawn on top of the platforms)
3) Doomguy's animations which add much more to his personality that any dialogue could have added.
4) Words of introduction in the very start of the game (SPOILER: RIP AND TEAR!

And there are many, many others that give the life to the already great and dynamic shooter.

New id Software team, you've made an "id"eal Doom game. My applause are for you.

10/10, got everything I was waiting for from this game and much more.

P,S. Multiplayer is still Halo, though, but who needs multiplayer in the Doom game?\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHey everyone and this is my review about the game!

This is a First person shooter game.


Interface/Menu/Settings

You can choose between Multiplayer, Single player or Snapmap (level editor).
Changing control options in one mode will not change the control options in other modes.
You can play with a xbox controller. (highly unrecommended when playing PVP).

You are able to change the sensitivity on the Mouse and Xbox controller.
There is a Master Volume that has a slidebar with a % bar.
There is a Music Volume that has a slidebar with a % bar.
There is a Sound Volume that has a slidebar with a % bar.
There is an announcer volume that has a slidebar with a % bar.
There is a Voice volume that has a slidebar with a % bar.

You can play fullscreen/ windowed mode or borderless mode.
There are many resolution sizes and aspects.
You can turn up Gamma and FOV(Field of view and i highly recommend to put this higher to move faster in PVP).
There are alot of graphical settings.

About the game.

There are 3 different game modes. I will explain all each of them.


Multiplayer

This is the PVP mode. You fight and co-op against other players. You are able to level up and unlock stuff! You can unlock more character and weapon skins, colors, weapons, Taunts and you can even make your own loadout customization! The PVP is a good " Pick me up " because it's simple to play and drop it. Each round is like 10 minutes or less. There are alot of game modes! You can find super weapons, Demon runes (where you will become a demon itself) and some other power ups!

Team Deathmatch

Pretty Known stuff. Just kill each other to win. First 75 kills win and there is a 10 minute time limit. Prepare for Chaingun Wars.


Soul harvester

Its an interesting game mode but could be very one sided. The Person that makes the first kill will become a Demon. The demon will never run out of a timer and will always be visible for the enemy team. So you always know and aware where he is. Killing the demon will grant your team the demon power. Killing an enemy will give you soul and then you have to collect a soul. Picking up the team mate soul will deny a point for the enemy team. So basically souls are the points in this match. Getting 125 Souls will be required to win a match and it also has a 10 minute time limit. Baron of hell and Revenant are good Demons to collect Souls Fast.

Domination

You can see this like, If you have played TF2 Control points, this is basically the same. But you can always take any control point that you like, There are 3 Control points, A, B and C. Chainguns are highly recommend and same goes for rocket launchers in this mode. Chainguns are a useful Tool for defensive, while rocket launchers are strong in the Assault.
Defending a control point or capturing one, will give you some XP and points. The biggest problem that i have encounter So far, that sometimes people forget to take the control points and rather just fight. Baron Of hell is a good defender for this mode. Controls that are contested, wont start capturing/defending cooldown if there is 1 unit of each team on the same control point. You need 250 Points to win and there is a 10 minute timer.

Warpath

Its somewhat similiar like "Domination", but there is only 1 control point that keeps moving around on the map. This mode could be super one sided. Area of effect weapons seems to be super useful here. Baron of hell is a good defender, because he has alot of health and has area of effect damage. Mancubus could be alright here aswell but he is situational because he is map dependant. Control Point that are contested, wont start capturing/defending cooldown if there is 1 unit of each team on the same control point. You need 250 Points to win and there is a 10 minute timer.


I Recommend taking grenades and rocket launchers here. It's super easy to make multiple kills at once and capturing the zone. I had once 4 kills with 1 grenade.

Freeze Tag

This is a true team-work game. Staying together is the key and solo wanderers will get punished. Players that are killed will be frozen on the battle field. To win the match, you need to freeze every enemy player.
You can "melt" frozen ally players by standing next to them. If there are 2 enemy players next to each other that are frozen, it is recommended that you stay here and defend it. Otherwise this could turn your game around. Fast demons are recommended here. But the chances are low that you will even see a demon because the matches are short.

You only need 3 Freeze tags to win a game and the game will not last longer than 3 minutes, There could be in total 5 matches. However this is a super short game mode but with a lot of fun.

Clan Arena

Its mainly focused on for clan purposes only. But you are able to play this mode without a team. Its not super popular and the rounds are short too. There are in total 5 matches (or u have to win 3 of them) with less than 2 minutes. It requires 4 players in order to start. You have to kill the enemy team. Once dead, You will not respawn until the round is over. DEAD IS DEAD. There are no powerups/supplies and no Demon runes. Hack modules are disabled aswell. Lifesteal grenades are useful to regain some health. This is the only way to regain Health. You start with 100 Armor and Health. Pure skill is required here because items won't save you. Rocket launchers are useful here.

Objective based mode

There will be 4 game mode played here. IF you have finished one game mode, you will return back to the lobby and the next one will start.
These could be - Warpath/Soul Harvester/ Team Deathmatch and Domination.

Round Based mode

Its not a super popular game mode, But Freeze tag and Clan arena will be played here.
The gamemode changes whenever you have completed your entire match. You will be send back to the lobby and then the next one will start.

There are a couple of weapons each with their alternative attack mod. (No super weapons which u can found ingame)

Chaingun.
Heavy assault Rifle.
Plasma Rifle.
Rocket launcher.
Super Shotgun.
Vortex Rifle.
Burst Rifle.
Hellshot.
Combat Shotgun.
Lightning Gun.
Static Rifle.

Single Player/Campaign

You will go through level to level. Killing enemies, finding weapons, Keycards and secrets! You are able to make execution to get some extra health back. Chainsaws are useful to receive more ammo out of an enemy. Its a story driven campaign. Whenever you have completed a level, you will see your stats. You are able to do and complete challenges to upgrade yourself or your weapons. There is Easy to ULTRA NIGHTMARE MODE. Gameplay is more focused on Doom 1/2. Lookout for nice easter eggs! There are checkpoints at each point and you can't manually save. Respawn and load games will select the last checkpoint you have reached.

Snapmap

This is the level editor mode. You are able to make your own levels, Semi story mode, Arena and many other things. You are also able to publish them online or download from other community members. Currently there is only 1 theme layout available (industrial). You are also able to place monsters and change their behavior or even their health. It is sure worth checking out!

Other things.

PVP matches are a good pick me up. They end fast so you can always stop or take a break.
PVP has some chaingun wars. Mainly in Team Deathmatch.
Secrets in story mode are harder to find compared in the old dooms.
Nice easter eggs and environments.
Campaigns feels ARENAish.



-------------------

I Give this game a 7/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is a perfect example on how to not judge a book by it's cover. After that horrendous multiplayer beta, I was ready to throw in the towel with this game. Luckily I didn't and the single-player proved to be mindless fun with an awesome soundtrack and masterful graphics. The gameplay is pretty straightforward, kill demons, and that's it. But that is what DOOM is about. There is no complex moral choices, no emotional storyline, no interesting NPCs. There is one man with a shotgun against the armies of Hell and that is all this game needs.

Rip and Tear!\t\t\t\t\t
