\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDumb fun. Those who like to backtrack a ton or inspect every inch of the map to find secret areas will find this satisfying. Went through it on nightmare difficulty and died a lot, also many secrets are hard to find (plenty are designed just so you replay those levels) -- those are the only reasons it took me 25+ hours but after finishing the story I have no intention of platying it any further.
The same enemy types just become repetitive and a matter if you have enough ammo on the weapons that you want to use.
The story was pretty interesting although nothing extraordinary.
PS: Rune challenges were pretty fun, I missed only 3 runestones (have no idea where they could'v been, I made sure to properly investigate every map) and upgraded all that I had.\t\t\t\t\t
