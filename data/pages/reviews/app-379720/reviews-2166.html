\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me be honest, I love the Doom franchise and there is one thing I can say for this one, buy it.

Now let's start with the details. It is fast paced with a lot of gore and smashed skulls. The game gives you an arsenal with awesome weapons, great level design and a really nice story and a promising sequel.

But there are a few disadvantages on the game, each ammo typo is used by two weapons so when you get the second weapon of that ammo type the first one is getting a bit boring, because usually the second is better. Also, on some levels as you explore them enemies spawn but on really small numbers which give no challenge at all and gave me the feeling that the designers just needed to add something there and not because they had something good to add.

The disadvantages' paragraph is a little longer but that does not mean that they are more or more important, I just didn't want to give many details about the advantages otherwise I would have been writing for days. I love the game and totally recommend it because it's really awesome. Also it gives you the opportunity to admire the demon you just cut in half while listening to metal, what else would you need in a game to call it great.\t\t\t\t\t
