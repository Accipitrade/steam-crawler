\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt the time of writing I have never played Doom Eternal. My plan was to play Doom2016 and see if it's any good. To be honest, I don't see how Doom Eternal could be that much better than this one... or any other FPS for that matter.
I am surprised that I even finished the campaign, these kind of games are not even my cup of tea. But it is that good.
There's little more to say, this game is awesome. I bet that 90% of us spent the dialogue with Samuel Hayden jumping around and trying to kill the guy. I think this is the kind of person that like this game.
The good:
Unbelievably good performance. What a game engine. Lots of action, incredible graphics. Crashes rarely if ever: had only one crash at the time of writing.
... and all this with the game running through Proton, by the way.

The bad:
A game like this should be a gaming benchmark, and should have a **built-in benchmarking mode**. It's a real pity that the developers didn't build this in.\t\t\t\t\t
