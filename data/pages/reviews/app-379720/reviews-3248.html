\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI apologize to any of you that were exposed to the hack 1st year grad student review that was posted by me earlier. I always write my reviews in Word and then cut and paste the finished piece into whatever medium I am working in, well this time I have no idea what happened but what was posted was missing paragraphs, punctuation, and all formatting. I have corrected the review accordingly and hope it helps you during your deliberation if you should buy this game or not.

I played so much of the original game Doom, that I could run levels entirely in reverse without touching any walls or hazards. I would deathmatch friends for hours at a time and got so good at the game that we used play where I would only use my fist but they could use any of the other weapons in the game. So all of this being said, I am what you would consider a doom professional.

So my Doom professionalism was a bit skeptical with the newly released 2016 Doom. I got flash backs of Doom3 which was horrible and was not Doom at all but more of a Fear meets Quake. Well I can tell you that Bethesda (whom acquired id Software), did a spectacular job of capturing the essence of the original Doom and did the original coders some justice.

This endorsement is not made as a Doom/id fan boi but as someone who played the original so much that I could almost play by sound alone and remember the emotions and energy the game created in a player and this new Doom has that. I think we have all said this before "if the game developer would only upgrade the graphics and sound to todays standards but leave the game play the way it was in the past, they would have an amazing game".

These guys have done that exactly, they have not tried to make Doom something it wasn’t, they did not try and reinvent what works, they kept all of the elements that made the 1993 Doom a success. I have over 33 HRS in the game so far and have unlocked 24% of the achievements and can say with a fierce amount of confidence that this game has earned every minute of play time I give it.

The game comes with a Map editor/Game mode editor which means that anyone can produce game modes or levels that they feel are the ultimate field of play. Anyone who has played the original Doom or Doom II, will agree with me when I say this games single player campaign get a 10 out of 10 rating/score. I can not say enough how great of a job they have done. The graphics, sound and overall immersion is second to none. While the game feels familiar with all of the monsters that existed in the original, they all received a substantial graphic upgrade. The levels feel large and size appropriate.

Combat is what you would expect with all the awesome weapons but the developers have introduced a fatality like melee finish that produces health or ammo for a player. This addition allows a player to get close and personal in order to gain assets eliminating the need to search for health packs and ammunition around the level.

Now on to the multiplayer portion of the game, my favorite game mode is Domination. Domination is what you would expect; there are points that you need to control to which earn your team score increases. Killing other players, assisting team mates and capturing points of control all gain you score increases just like typical domination/conquest modes in other games. The first team to 250 wins. There are six different multiplayer modes to play, there is Soul Harvest, Freeze Tag, Warpath, Capture the Flag, Team Deathmatch, Domination, and Clan Arena.

The feel of the weapons and the weight of the character in game all feel correct there is no input lag, there is not any delayed reaction time caused by poorly optimized code. If you get owned by your adversary and he didn’t use the BFG, you just outright got owned. Currently it is said that the Chaingun is OP and there are a few ammunition limits they are trying to put in place to reduce one class from having a distinct and unfair advantage but overall, I have never been owned by one class specifically.

If you purchase this game for no other reason than the single player campaign, you will not be unhappy. The one aspect of this game that I think is not getting enough applause, is the custom design and game mode multiplayer that allows a group of friends to play a their own game type in a private arena and just have fun. That was something the original doom never had.

My overall score for Doom 2016 is 90 out of 100 - Definately worth the jing!\t\t\t\t\t
