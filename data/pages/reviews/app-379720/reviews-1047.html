\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIncredible game. 9.5/10. One of the best single player FPS games I've played. It definitely stands out for its extremely fast paced gameplay which, unlike a lot of shooters, rewards a hyper-aggressive playstyle even on the hardest difficulties. The arcade mode is INCREDIBLE, and I had an absolute ball getting Slayer rank on all levels on the hardest difficulty.

My most substantial critique is that your first playthrough of the campaign doesn't allow you to select the harder difficulties, which makes it quite easy most of the time. Even on the second highest difficulty, "Nightmare", it's not very challenging, and the only real difference between "Nightmare" and "Ultra-Nightmare" is that "Ultra-Nightmare" sends you right back to the start of the campaign if you die. This makes for a frustrating way to experience the campaign, but a fantastic way to experience individual levels on Arcade mode.
Also, a minor complaint about Arcade mode - "relics" are an infuriating mechanic that are at odds with the spirit of the mode. It feels like an unnecessary element of RNG in a mode which is otherwise about mechanical skill and situational awareness. This is only a minor complaint though, and you learn to live with it.

Ultimately, I completely fell in love with this game in 2020, despite not seeing the appeal when I first picked it up in 2016. Once I stopped caring about the collectables, and just let myself kill as much as possible, as fast as possible, I understood why this game gets the praise it does.\t\t\t\t\t
