\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe singel player is a fantastic game, but I can't say anything about the mp or snapmap since I haven't tested them yet. This is everything I wanted in a doom game and more! I really liked the emphasis on upgrading weapons and runes, the challenges and secrets to find in the levels. The nods to the old 90's doom games was great as well! So glad they decided to drop the old COD-like build of doom they presented a few years back, and develop this instead!

The only thing that I can think of that detracts from it, is that it's a little bit buggy, at least for me. It has hard crashed my pc two times, to the point where my pc was non-responsive and I had to do a hard reset. And one time where it just crashed to desktop. I didn't loose that much gameplay time since the checkpoints are rarely far between, but it's an annoyance none the less. There were a few bugs in-game as well, such as falling through the level, getting stuck in geometry and so on, but overall I'd say the game is pretty well polished!

I used 23.5 hours to get through the singel player on hurt me plenty difficulty, and I took my time to explore the levels, search for secrets, upgrade stuff, read the lore and so on. I think most people will use about 14 hours or so to just get through the game.

Pros:
  • Fantastic graphics
  • Really good level design
  • Top notch character and monster design
  • Top of it's class shooter
  • Rune and weapon upgrade system
  • Lots of secrets

Cons:
  • A bit buggy

My Grade: 9/10 \t\t\t\t\t
