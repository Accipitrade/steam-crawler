TLDR: 9.5/10 One of the most fun, fast-paced campaign shooters you'll ever play.

Actual Review (No Spoilers): Updating my review after coming back to complete this game 100% after my first playthrough years ago (and introduction to the DOOM series!). This is one of the greatest comeback stories in gaming. After many people felt that Doom 3 wasn't a true doom game, years of waiting later and ID Software returns to it's roots for a classic doom-inspired modernization of the formula. Doom 2016 truly captures the essence of classic doom and turns it up to 11.

The only thing that really matters about this game is the gameplay experience alone and the game fully takes advantage of that fact. Story wise the game is decent enough and gives you enough of a reason to be interested in returning to Mars. But the game will riff on the tropes of a typical doom story and makes it apparent that you're only here to kill demons.

Let's talk gameplay. This is one of the most refined FPS experiences in recent memory if you're looking for a complete powertrip. Combat is fast and your movement is even faster. I remember this was the first game I played with the modern version of a mantling mechanic and MAN does it really open up the maps and aids in exploration. The weapon selection is great and each weapon has viable uses (yes, even the pistol) not to mention the weapon mods and upgrades you earn/find throughout your playthrough are so damn fun to use. Exploration is also a key factor in this game just like the classics.

Enemy variety is pretty fleshed out and each one makes you tackle an arena differently, and the introduction of glory kills just ramps up the satisfying nature of the combat even more. And of course I have to mention the absolute BANGER of an OST this game has. If you're playing this game without music then you aren't really experiencing it the right way. Music is dynamic and will flow in and out of combat and also around glory kills, so you basically get a slightly different version of the soundtrack every time you play.

Last thing I finished out where the achievements which aren't too bad aside from the multiplayer ones. The multiplayer is actually pretty fun if you know what you're doing but with Doom Eternal being the most recent game you're better off playing that if you want multiplayer. The achievements can be done solo with a little configuration and the only truly bad achievements are the cosmetic unlocking ones which require a ton of grinding your echelon rank which is extremely tedious. The multiplayer also has a challenges tab if you're really wanting to grind that out as well, but it's a TON of stuff similar to the insane amount of arcade mode medals in singleplayer. I had no qualms skipping all of it since I don't plan on actively playing the multiplayer and also I got a decent amount of them completed during the grinding process anyway.

If you're an FPS gamer than this is a no-brainer, but even if you aren't I'd still recommend playing this. It's an all-around great experience. 9.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
