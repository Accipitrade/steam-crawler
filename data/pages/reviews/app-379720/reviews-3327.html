\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe original Doom was the first PC game I had ever played as a kid so to say the least my hopes were high. Watching the original E3 gameplay secured my ticket on the hype train but given the state of most remakes and sequels made today I tried to keep my expectations realistic. Lots of games end up looking much worse than their original preview footage and although they can be fun, not often do they really hit that ideal spot that makes me feel like I am a kid again experiencing the franchise for the first time. With that said...

The campaign is absolutely amazing. The first thing I noticed is the game looked and ran so smoothly I couldn't believe it. There is something special about this engine, speed, design that just seperates it from all the other FPS I have played out there. It's FAST, I mean REALLY FAST! You feel like you are S#!T and on higher difficulties it's actually challenging. You need to know your weapons, learn the areas quickly, and react very quickly. I personally love this because as a kid I remember dying over and over in Doom trying so hard to figure out what I was suppose to do while avoiding endless hordes of demons being thrown at me and this game managed to take all the years of gaming experience I have since then and still manage to make me feel in that position again all while trying to find all the secrets and hidden areas which without the upgrade perk in your suit can be insanely well hidden. Honestly I could say so much more like how the soundtrack is insane, the hidden throwbacks & easter eggs made me fanboy hard, the gore & action was crazy but instead I will just stop here and recommend that you buy this game if you are a DOOM fan and let you experience it yourself.\t\t\t\t\t
