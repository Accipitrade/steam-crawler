\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhere to begin?! I was told to purchase this game on sale being told it was pretty decend. Wow, this was an incredible experience. From the moment I started on ultra violent Mode I couldn't let it go. I marathoned the campaign then and there.

I have to say after designing my first game and then playing this, this game schooled me good in how to make large fun level designs.

The single player for this looks absolutely incredible there is some really noticeable texture loading but it doesn't distract from the overall experience so much. The gameplay really keeps you on your toes with an arsenal of weapons and enemies that you will have to balance to survive.

Along with a pretty decent story line. This is probably the best single player FPS i've played all year. Not only that there's so many really great touches such as the sound design coming right from the original Doom, little things like doors opening that sound the same as the original. That coupled with secret levels from the original Doom, what a great touch.

The challenges are very run of the mill, unlock this, press this, go here, turn this off. But it's still really enjoyably and once again plays homage to the original. The boss fights are hard yet rewarding personally I feel the balance of boss fights was slightly off leaving me very slightly underwhelmed at the end but I can't really fault anything else. One one minor plot hole for me and that's me just being nitpicky. I haven't played the multiplayer yet so can't speak for that but really you should get this for the single player alone. I'd highly recommend on sale. You won't be disappointed.

Overall so far this is the only game that i've litterally not wanted to stop playing from start to finish in all of 2016. So for me its got to have a 8.9 / 10.

Reasons why this wasnt a 10?

- Two system hangs but doesnt bother me so much.
- Minor plot holes ( You might not even notice ).
- Texture popping.
- Slightly underwhelming ending, Might not be for you.

All in all, a great experience highly polished, very exciting, GREAT sound design, great graphics, fast and fluid gunplay. Do not miss this game!\t\t\t\t\t
