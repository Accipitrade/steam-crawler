There are two achievements in the game, one related to collecting all runes and another to completing all level challenges, that can be missed and require a second playthrough of the game in order to attain 100%. This game is NOT the type where you play the story mode more than once, lol. So make sure to look up this phenomenon and avoid a headache if you play DOOM.

As someone who isn't normally a huge fan of FPS games, anyone my age remembers and loved DOOM. And so I picked this up on sale, and I have to say it was WELL worth it. This reboot is true to the series and true to what you expect from DOOM. By the time I was done with the intro, I was hooked and have been sitting up late looking for all of the secrets and finishing challenges that in a lot of games, become boring quickly. Not so DOOM; every level is challenging and well designed without being frustrating and repetitious. ENTER DOOM.
