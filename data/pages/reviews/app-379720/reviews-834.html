Probably the best game i have ever given a thumbs down. But still a thumbs down. Since I finished Doom Eternal, this game seems incredibly half-baked. Combat is completely fine, but the movement is not enjoyable and not excusable for the time this came out. Couple this with the possibility to insta-die from environmental stuff (which was also removed in Doom Eternal because it doesn't make sense to interrupt a game about combat with stuff like this) and you got too many arbitrary deaths. 100% Exploration is unfortunately mandatory if you want to have all the cool weapons mods.

In other genres, it can be enjoyable to play older titles despite knowing the new ones, but definitely not here.

TL;DR: Want to play Doom? Play Doom Eternal. Want to go back in the series after starting with Doom Eternal (like me)? Don't. Rather, buy its DLC if you haven't already.\t\t\t\t\t
