The skill unlocks and rpg elements Bethesda has thrown into the mix make it soo much more fun + there are many secrets to harvest :)

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI so want to say I recommend this game. There is so much to like about it. I love the old style gameplay they went for. The developers did a great job capturing the feel and play of the original while bringing in some new mechanics I really enjoy. I love the dark atmosphere interspersed with fun easter eggs, like the little DOOM guys you can find on the maps. I like the way they do gun upgrades, it keeps all the various weapons interesting and relevant to the whole game. I could go on..but...

This game is a buggy mess. I even tried to be patient and wait it out and come back later and try again, and it's still a disaster. At launch, I had to screw around with all kinds of settings (both in the game and on my computer itself) to get it to even play. Now it will start and play for a little while without issue, but I still have to deal with random crashes for no reason whatsoever. When I can play, I will try to do things like a rune trial, a fun extra challenge to get a new perk. I complete the trial and come back, and all the textures are messed up and everything is flickering. Nothing makes any sense, there's no rhyme or reason to how screwed up it is.

I am desperately hoping someday these bugs will be gone or mitigated enough I can finish playing through it. Hopefully Bethesda/iD will one day do that, and then I'll happily change this to recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
