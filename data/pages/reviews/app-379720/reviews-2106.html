\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI never got into First Person Shooters. I just have a terrible aim, don't like taking covers, managing ammunition for my weapon of choice and following a simple path or a complex plan. Doom adress all this points with the gameplay. You move fast, all enemy shots are projectiles and you have big targets, so run around and find your oportunities. The glory kill mechanic is awesome and efective to the point I often find myself dealing damage from far away and running towards my enemy just to finish it of and regain health. The levels are full of plataforms where you can jump, at first over solid ground and later over hazards, with makes you prone to make risky jumps and complex movement to avoid damage. You will die many times the first time you walk in a room, but the game is full of checkpoints and usually on the second try you become aware of the threats and find a strategy. The ammo management is great, you have a simple unlimited ammo pistol and an arsenal with many uses and redundancy, the alternate fire mode allow you to supress a weakness of a weapon or make it more efficient on it's job, so even if you ran out of ammo, a simple switch and a search on the arena will do the job, since there's no reload in this game, you just shoot until you run out of bullets., and you can aways use the chainsaw to farm ammo too. If I would use one word to describe this game, would be "Fun". Every emotion, from the fear, the adrenaline, the badass moments, the nostalgia to the soundtrack forms one experience of joy, fun and freedom.

Last comment: despite some bugs, the game is very well optimized. You don't need to keep 60fps to make it fluid, but on my laptop, waaaaaay under the minimum requirements, I can keep a steady 60fps on 1280x720 just changing in-game configurations and the game looks beautiful even with low grafics, if you think your machine can handle it, it's a must have\t\t\t\t\t
