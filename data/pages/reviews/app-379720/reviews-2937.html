\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSpeaking in terms of the campaign this game is an absolute joy for anyone who's a fan of arena shooters and id Software. The stagger, glory kill, and chainsaw mechanics add some fun variety to the gun play. On my normal playthrough I never felt that there was any point where I had to exploit the AI to stay alive or run away from the fight. Very solid level design and well done boss fights that were fun and challengeing.

Now in contrast to the superb level design the weapon upgrade crap is just poorly implemented. Having the final damage boosting abilities switch to skill based unlocks IMHO is just a nuisance. I made a conscious effort to unlock the final Rocket launcher and Super shotty upgrades by the end of the campaign and was unable to do so. Also, what's the point in making the player invest in lower tier weapon unlocks if by the last half of the game they will be only be using the Gauss cannon, Rocket Launcher, and Super shotty. What gives with the Super shotty too? No mods for that one only why? A freaking laser whould have been sweet.

Overall its satisfying though. Hoorah for double jumps!!!\t\t\t\t\t
