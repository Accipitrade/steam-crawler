Series as old as DOOM usually does not age well, I'm looking at you Duke Nukem and Spyro, but iD Software and Bethesda made sure this demon was just as brutal and entertaining as ever. PROS:

- Action-packed gameplay
- Incredible graphics
- Glad I only spent $14.99 on it because of the cons.

CONS:

- Poor quality of software (very buggy, a lot of crashing and freezing, even on the single player campaign)
- No patches to fix these problems ever since the beginning
- Patch 6.66 only seemed to make matters worse in performance and bug issues
- Too large of a hard drive footprint (65+ gigs total!)

Until Bethesda / id gets this mess cleaned up, I have to recommend that you not invest in DOOM, even at it's low price.

I think I have never reviewed any game in my whole life but i had to review this one. This game will be remembered for a long long time. In other games involving demons and hell, you might get scared or play slowly and cautiously in order to proceed further. But in DOOM, you will find yourself wanting to kill more and more demons and you will get to kill a lot of them.
Play it on high sound, the music is really aggresive, gets you in the mood.

Remember, Super Shotgun is you best friend.... xD upgrade you ammo slot instead of armor..... you will never run out of your health.

Completed this game on highest difficulty. Involves a lot of jumping and strafe running.

Here are some tips for you guys:

1. Pistol upgrades are important. you can complete the whole game with just your pistol. There is even a video on youtube of a guy who completed the whole game with pistol only. lmao.
2. Killing Bosses is actually a lot easier than i thought. Just run around in one direction firing your gun. Thats it.
3. USE CHAINSAW WHEN YOU DONT HAVE AMMO. Yup you will run out of your ammo many times. just kill someone with a chainsaw and you will receive a lot of ammo pickups.


Yes, I would recommend this game to everyone. even minecraft players.
