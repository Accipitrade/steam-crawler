\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast pace fun arcadey style fps. Feels like a modern version of the original Doom without going to far into the cinematic experiences that a lot of modern single player focused FPS go into. All the guns feel great most of the time and the enemies are just challenging enough. The story is fairly competent and is there just enough that it doesn't detract from the run and gun style gameplay. Level exploration is greatly rewarded, and powering up weapons feels good. The level design is great and makes for great firefight arenas, platforming, beautifully grotesque set pieces.

Only a few gripes is the pistol and regular shotgun feel a little underpowered compared to the rest of the weapons even after fully upgraded. I also would have liked a few more suit upgrades that impact the way you approach combat like a dash, or upgraded melee. The game can feel a little repetitive by the last act of the game and maybe could have used 1-2 more enemy types.

You can really feel the love and hard work the developers put into this game. If you are coming off of Halo Infiinite, or enjoy the fast pace of Overwatch or Valorant you can't go wrong with Doom.\t\t\t\t\t
