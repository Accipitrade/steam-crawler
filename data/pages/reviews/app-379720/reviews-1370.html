\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(Keep in mind that I haven't played the original Doom series or any of its heirs until this title)

Having given that bracketed line, I don't really have much to add to be completely honest..
The game is good. VERY good, especially if you like exploriation/collecting games. yeah, that's right. You heard it correctly. EXPLORATION and COLLECTING stuff. I'd have never expected such a peculiar and peculiarly positive mix of genres. Ofc, most of the stuff you find are basically upgrades to your gear and weaponry, one way or another, but!
And it also has a plot? An ACTUALLY well (albeit simply) told plot that intertwines with the gameplay? Get out.

I have not finished the game yet, past 4th chapter as of now, but I can definitely recommend it to both old fans seeking a bit of nostalgy and rookies like myself that have not yet tasted what it's like to beat your way through hordes of demons as one of the most iconic characters in the gaming industry.

tl;dr and yadayadayada:

+ Peculiar mix of a bit of your classic arcade shooter with a collectibles (mostly intertwined with gear upgrading) system
+ Both a good nostalgic experience for the veterans, as a spark for rookies never yet drowned in the blood of the demon hordes
+ Great Graphics, setting up the the atmosphere without forcing the "real-like" vibe when it's not necessary
+ Actual plot?
+ So far haven't had any issues with the game whatsoever..

- .. idk, honestly, I'm gonna stretch it out unnecessarily now, but it lacks a bit of an environmental variety sometimes. As far as I've heard though, Eternal's dealt with that issue well enough already, so cheers!
- No boobs *dabs in 3D*\t\t\t\t\t
