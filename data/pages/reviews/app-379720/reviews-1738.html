\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is great.

The good: Fun game play, all guns are usable and upgrade able. Classic enemies. Great graphics. Just fun all around.

The bad: Please forgive me for the "bad" being much longer than the good, as I just tend to focus more on criticism than I do the fun elements in general. This doesn't mean I don't recommend the game.

My biggest gripe with the game is, while it seems to try its best to be an update homage to the originals, a vast majority of the game is hallway mode, with one clear direction, with any fork in the road being there simply for extras (secrets, items, etc). Instead of finding different ways to proceed, and figuring out where keys are to get to the next area, and solving a bit of a puzzle to do so, the keys are all in plain sight, and the only thing holding you back is stepping into a room, having all the doors close, and fighting a horde of demons until they're all dead. The game difficulties are artificial difficulty increases, meaning, the difficulty increases mean the monsters become bullet sponges, or just more waves of demons in general.

Overall though, if you can grab this game on sale, it's 100% worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
