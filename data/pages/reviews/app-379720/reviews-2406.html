\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like action focused fps games at all, then DOOM is a must buy.
The incredibly polished and smooth gameplay along with great weapon, enemy and map design throughout, makes for an almost flawless game that can be enjoyed by pretty much anyone. The soundtrack is kickass, blood pumping metal which is perfect for slaughting hordes of demons. DOOM truly makes you feel like a badass.

If you're like me and love a challenging game that demands utilisation of all the tools and techniques at your disposal to survive; chuck it on Nightmare difficulty. However if you're like most people and don't like dying over and over to the same thing and simpily enjoy smashing and pummeling tons of demons; the default and easy difficulties offer plenty of enganging fun and enjoyment for you the average player.

The basic story of the game is simple and easy to follow along with. But for those that enjoy some well written universe lore, there is tons of super detailed database entries that are discovered throughout the game, giving relevant backstory to the characters, places and events that take place prior to and during the game.

Also if you've ever played Hotline Miami then just imagine DOOM as a AAA, fps, heavy-metal, demonic version of HM.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM (2016) is in my opinion a very solid and recommendable game. It is pretty much the original game, but with some tweeks making it feel newer and more fluid. The enemies are great and very different from one another, the soundtrack is fits te theme and just pumps you up (You can even listen to it while not playing the game, only a few games achieve this in my opinion), the graphics are amazing and the game is running very stable. Overall, the 2016 release of DOOM is a worthy reboot of an amazing and very important classic, which wouldn't have deserved any less. If you're really into fast and fluid games and single player, I can only recommend this game. Just don't expect a good story like in Halo, Wolfenstein or Titanfall 2, as DOOMs single player attracts players with it's intense and somewhat strategic gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
