\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is by far the best action game I have ever played. The ledge grabbing and glory kills along with skilling and weapon upgrades makes it very entertaining. Graphics are very nice, I love how the original storyline has been colored and even though it's very straightforward, it's not boring. Engine is awesome, no glitches, no graphic issues even on my 21:9 monitor with wide FOV. I have literally felt my heart beating upbeat tempo after playing some of the levels. When you feel you cannot cope you can hold down weapon selection which slows down time so you can take a breath and choose the correct weapon. Monsters are great, very detailed and if you complete all of the challenges in game to get all of the upgrades, you will enjoy it even more. This game is not for slow playing action gamers, it is very fast paced, camping is not an option. It gives your brain a good kick when you're trying to figure where to jump and grab yourself. Don't do drugs, do DOOM!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a great game, a return the old school "simple" FPS, yet has just enough of a "newer" feel. The item upgrades work out quite well and are a ton of fun. The challenges are enjoyable too. I was very impressed with how beautiful the graphics looked. The new idTech engine is nice. On my 960m it runs great, so I can only imagine what it's like on a 1080. I've only played the single player campaign (have no interest in the multiplayer), so speaking only on that I high encourage giving this game a try to anyone who loves FPS.

The only real issue I have w/ Doom is the loading time on initial startup. It was horrible, but the most recent patch made it better, still feels long to other comparable AAA games (Far Cry Primal, Dishonored 2, etc.).\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe good parts:
*Very dynamic, fast paced and mobile action
*Good optimisation (unless it bugs out)
*Beautiful art style of levels
*Upgrades that can completely repurpouse weapons
*Plenty of secrets, side passages and room to maneuver

The bad parts:
*Awful weapon power creep. Once u get new gun it will probably be better than everything u had
*Awful balance. Grenades, railgun and 4 shot SSG make game extremely easy and some upgrades make no sense
*Bad level design. It looks good but in the end its just another corridor shooter with death pits at every single step
*Very short campaign. The only NPCs in game are kinda meh
*Lots of bugs. U may not notice them at first but then they will become really annoying
*Ugly glory kills. A few of them look cool, but mostly really stupid and ugly
*Too much depends on glory killing. Ammo, health, armor, hiding from enemy fire. Dont want glory kills? Enjoy walking through the game with pistol and unlimited grenades
*Tiniest amounts of ammo i ever saw in shooters. 20 shotgun shells without upgrades? Really? I guess they REALLY wanted to shove glory killing in your throat
*Co-op that was advertised so much is boring, childish and gimepd
*SnapMap that was heavily advertised is the one of the worst map makers i saw with LOTS of console restrictinos like maximum amount of objects in the map (128 or 256 iirc)
*No replayability

And the worst thing that was done to it is that its heavily multiplayer oriented. Much more weapons, monsters, buffs and game mechanisc in multiplayer. And their multiplayers sucks so much. Im veteran of quake, cod, battlefield, arma, teamfortress etc games so i really love any multiplayer games but this multiplayer just disgusted me. Boring, slow, laggy, unbalanced, bugged, with awful progression, alot of useless stuff, ugly looking, with tiny maps, most of the game modes play just like deathmatch. It is extremely disappointing how much potential the game had and how it was all wasted on nothing.

Doom is not the game to remember. Buy, enjoy for like 4 hours cuz its campaign is pretty flashy and nice looking then forget about this game. Because thats all it has to offer. 8/10 for this game's potential and 3/10 for how it was realised. Not as sad story as No Man's Sky but kinda the same type to me. This one is just louder and flashier. This distracts ppl from this game huge amount of problems and disgustingly bad choice of multiplayer orientation and post-release support. I really-really wanted to like this game but i couldnt\t\t\t\t\t
