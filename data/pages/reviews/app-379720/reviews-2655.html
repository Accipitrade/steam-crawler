\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCampaing - Long Story Short:

Pros:
- The Story is ok. You know what you need to know.
- Old-school FPS. Want to shoot? Just start the game.
- Cool Weapon designs and Mods.
- The easy way of controls.
- A nice GUI, which makes finding interactions much easier.
- Good optimization.

Cons:
- Silly/OP Gun Mods as well and some of the Runes.
- No hit indicator (Even if It will mess with the players head once the map is full with monsters).
- "Useless" Suit Upgrades.
- No way yet discovered to set Audio in one language, Subtitles in another.
- Once you get every Suit and Equipment upgrade, you will become bored of your difficulty (It simple becomes way too easy). And I'm talking about ultra-violence (Which is easy, If you know some basics).-- Difficulty Level is not what you think it will be.
- Campaing is just too short.
