\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is the best shooter on the market right now IMO, amazing gameplay with a well written story just makes this game a steal at any price.

Now i'm gonna go through gameplay, plot, Atmosphere and the look and finally the MP then tie it up in a nice little knot at the end with anything i don't like being there.

Gameplay:
DOOM is made by Id games who are know for making Quake and the OG Doom and it shows. DOOM is just so fast and so brutal that staying still is a death sentence. DOOM on the higher difficulty settings almost demands that you run around the room like a kid on a endless sugar high to even survive which makes this game so fun to play. Never a boring moment in this game. This game absolutely revels in its over the top brutality with its glory kills that spew viscera everywhere and are used to regain health and chainsaw kills that are used to regain ammo and would cause a conservative parent to faint in shock. The game also follows in the OG Doom games footsteps in that DOOM leaves secrets everywhere such as little dolls tucked away that unlocked in game models for demons and weapons or retro style levels that go back to the game that started it all. The demons you face are a real danger that will stomp you to powder if your not careful, if you played the OG Doom then you will have met most of them before but they may be a little different then you remember. The Weapons of DOOM are just pure fun to use, a energy cannon that blows demons to chunks of meat, a rifle that fires micro misses and of course the BFG which can clears rooms in seconds.

Story:
I hear you cry out "What story? The OG Doom had no story so why does this one have one". Well first, don't interrupt me and second of all, it has a story and it's amazingly done. The Doom marine or "Doom slayer" as he's called in this one, was released from a stone tomb by Samuel Hayden to drive out a outbreak of demons, which Doom Slayer does with pleasure. The Doom Slayer is a force of nature who just exists to beat demons into non existence and does not care about the main story BUT he is shown to be a bit deeper then that with very subtle hints which i'm not gonna spoil. I can't say much about the story since spoilers but it's amazingly done, it feels fresh and is actually pretty funny and is almost a parody of a FPS story.

Atmosphere and look:
The game nails this down to a T. It truly feels like hell on Earth... or Mars i guess. Blood and bodies everywhere, mangled metal and the general aura of disrepair and the taint of hell just make the environment truly amazing. Don't even get me started on hell itself, it's gorgeousness and just oozes the anguish of mortals and pure unfiltered evil.

Multiplayer:
To be honest...it's not bad. Can be fun for a while but it's not the best MP out there. It's similar to quake with some differences such as demon power ups and a loadout system. Not worth buying DOOM just for MP though, but it's not bad.

Final thoughts and criticisms:
The music also really is amazing, heavy metal for a metal game just makes sense don't it? Well then, onto my problems with the game. The higher difficulty settings can feel cheap at times when you get murdered by a single imp and the gore and guts may turn off people who are kind of squeamish about that stuff. Some glory kills can get a bit old and the MP is nothing to write home about but that really is the end of my problems with this game. It's that good that i really can't see much wrong with it.\t\t\t\t\t
