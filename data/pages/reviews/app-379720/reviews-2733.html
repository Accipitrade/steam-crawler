\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHadn't played a FPS like this since I was a very good player with Q1, Q2 and UT. Almost 20 years ago.
You know, life and stuff eventually drive you away and make you play Candy Crush and Farming Simulator with your family.
At first, I feared modern graphics and "washed down" gameplay to help console guys and hordes of players (back in time we were veeeeery few people playing this stuff at high level) would have produced a so-so game.

I was wrong, this game is top notch and brought me back to my twenties. Adrenaline, reflexes, even a bit of good story AND stunning graphics/design, in 60+ FPS glory even in the most gruesome battles with a mid-range PC. Back to Q1 rocketjumping and kill-on-flight times. Awesome.
Definitely not for campers or slow players, as in many games of this era. You *have* to join the fray to enjoy the game and earn the most from the mechanics.

Played on PC of course, and proper re-binding and FOV... can't imagine how console guys can appreciate this to its fullest, but oh well, to each his own.

Have a nice play and hooray to Doomguy!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM surely knows how to do one thing right - show the player a DAMN good time! This game is INSANELY fast and jumping around everywhere shooting demos and completely ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ ♥♥♥♥ up was an AMAZING time, and the soundtrack doesn't fail in absolutely any aspect - it is absurdly good, it makes all of the battles incredibly tense. The graphics are phenomenally good, but please get this over the console version! I played it on console and didn't have fun because of the limiting controls are somewhat blurrier graphics. The medium difficulty was perfect for my style of play, it offered a very tight, tense and gripping challenge that wasn't too hard or easy. The story and voice acting are also top notch and really there is nothing wrong with this game and it is a DEFINITE purchase for anyone! This is the most INTENSE and frighteningly fast shooter in recent years and I recommend it in absolutely every way! The boss battles are pretty fun too! The guns are all useful and you will find yourself switching between the weapons every 2 seconds (the game encourages this frantic style of play). The only bad weapon was the pistol, which was useless except for maybe the first 5 minutes. The story unfortunately leaves off in a cliffhanger, definitely implying a sequel (and the ending was predictable too). Not bad, and I absolutely CANNOT WAIT until the next one... it is going to be... superb. I haven't played the multiplayer all that much, but it's not anything spectacular, and at least you can come back to the game after finishing it.

Story mode: 9/10
Multiplayer: 5/10
Overall: (Ignoring multiplayer, and as a whole package) 9.5/10

Avoid the DLC.
