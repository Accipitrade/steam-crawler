\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the, if not the, best FPS-games to come out in the last 20 years. It's everything Doom 3 isn't, and it works out on all levels. Fast paced combat, quick finishers, nicely detailed maps, decent enough story (a very good story compared to the Doom movie). Everything comes together so beautifully, that it definitely makes you want to keep on playing,.

If there is one downside, it's secret-hunting. It does bring down the pace a bit, and there's no information about the "super secret" rooms and how to access them (and since it requires interaction with the world, something that you don't normally do otherwise, there's no way of knowing it's possible). These are minor inconveniences however, and doesn't bring down the overall score much, if anything.

Solid purchase.\t\t\t\t\t
