Singleplayer:
the singleplayer is soo good i mean the story it has is amazing unlike doom 3 and the gameplay the movement the weapons everything is soo good although i feel as your too op. i never actually died by and enemy it was mostly of me being stupid and falling of the map or something but it felt too op id suggest making the difficulty higher will be a little better who knows anyways its really amazing what they did i little searched for almost every easter egg they had and it just amazing there is literally no words for how amazing this game is, it brings me back to when i played the original because thats what it feels like. no bs slow movement like cod or bf games this is the type of game i like fast and scary. not too scary tho but after all i enjoyed every single thing about this game.

Multiplayer:
Now here comes the hard part... nah im just kidding the multiplayer is amazing just like the singleplayer its fun and exilerating (or however you spell it) i mean those moment when you go off on a killing spree with either the demon or just your normal weapons is so much fun, they also have really good game modes some are little hard but they are really fun once you get the hang of everything. the leveling system doesnt seem to bad i didnt think that those who played more/were higher level had that much more advantage over me. i believe it was mostly how you play, what decision you make, and how you execute those decision will decide if you win a 1v1 or maybe 1v2. the best part is as you start playing more and more youll find ways to get behind enemys and annhiliate them or also fail but none the less it still is really fun. i obviously havent played much but i think ill start getting more into it again im gonna make sure that i play alot more because honestly its way more fun than cod or bf and also less frustrating

Snapmap:
i havent really used this too much but i went just to see what people make and omg there is alot of option to choose from or even make your own there are alot of creative people out there. also this custom maps are super fun to play with friends.

overall i really enjoy this game not only because im obsessed with it but because it not as frustrating as all those other games its just a pure time of enjoyment i really recommend to those who dont know what to spend their money on. i know this is bold to say but dont buy cod or bf because tbh those games arent really looking at their fans anymore they are just focusing on who can make the most sales and who is better than one another well atleast thats what cod is like right now but honestly i suggest buy this game and even buy the original doom games alot of people are still making mods for those so they are really fun too. anyways i hope this review helped out someone.

10/10 - Doom 2016\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRating: 3.5/5 Campaign

Summary: Good game. Semi-repetitive fighting but fleshed out with various other objectives and secrets. Wood play again.

More stuff about the game: Played through the campaign. Very good, very solid game. Fairly easy, but overall still enjoyable. I found that going commando made for much more interesting gameplay. The secrets are very well hidden, the story was fairly well written, but quite predictable. I haven't experienced much on the multiplayer side of things, so no comments there. Overall 3.5/5 for the campaign, most gameplay was repetitve, but there was the occasional screwball that kept things spicy. The story was slightly difficult to grasp at first, but after a little while, things started to repeat themselves. Ending was slightly disappointing, but a predictable part of the plot. However, the numerous other objectives and secrets along with the complexity of the weapon upgrade and modification system gave a break to the incessant horde battles.

Will rate again after more hours spent in the multiplayer portion of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
