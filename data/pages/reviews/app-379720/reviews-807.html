Gameplay Features
  • Combat: The gameplay & levels are brilliantly designed around the chaotic, intense, & frenetic combat - you are never bored or stationary, constantly being forced to keep moving to stay alive. Agility & motion is integral to combat & survival, so move it or lose it.
  • Demons: In-game there are over two dozen enemy types, each with their own unique design & abilities to constantly keep you on your toes. From Demons who throw fireballs at you, to animal beast-like Demons who charge at you to ram you & eat you - there's plenty of variety. The Boss Battles as well are amazing, challenging & rewarding to overcome.
  • Praetor, Weapons, + Upgrades: There are ten different unique Weapons at your disposal, all of which will be essential in combat & upgradeable with different perks for your preference. Your 'Praetor Suit' Armor has several unique branching upgrades & perks as well - as you can tell there's plenty of options & customization to your preferred playstyle, but in the end nearly ALL of these upgrades & perks will be needed for the insane, fast-paced action.
  • Exploration, Secrets, & Hidden Collectibles: The game actively encourages exploration, with lots of Secrets & Hidden Collectibles. Chief among these are Praetor Tokens, Argent Caches, & Rune Trial Stones - all of which help you upgrade your perks & abilities. Definitely take your time to look around & explore as much as possible these upgrades will be needed. Additionally, there are Secret Levels (old retro style classic DOOM environments) to discover & Hidden Collectibles (mini Doomguy figurines/action figures).

    Oh, and if you're not playing on Ultra-Violence difficulty on your first playthrough, you're playing the game the wrong way.
