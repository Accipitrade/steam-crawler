PROS:

- The campain is awesome
- It doesn't take itself seriously
- Great balance in weapons
- The maps are beautful
- the fights take place in rooms where you can move a lot
- enemies very well adapted, some of them even emproved
- it's FUN, pure FUN, what it should be

CONS:

- It's not challenging enough bellow the ultra violence dificult level.
- There are missing Monsters from the original games and the new ones show a poor design.
- The multiplayer is trash.
- Some bugs can ♥♥♥♥ you really hard
- the final feels disapointing.
- some weapons feel useless at certain point
- the music is not as exiting as you would expect.

Conclusion: The game is worth the price, but i recomend to pick it up in the sales, also, play the originals if you haven't, you won't regret it.
