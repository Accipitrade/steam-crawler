\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is a game about killing demons. You are the Doom slayer a seriously overpowered killer of demons, and you need to be. Although it is the demons that fear the doom slayer, they are many and you are just one. This game is fast paced FPS action. No more cowering behind cover. You have to keep on the move driving the offensive. Standing still is death. The more you push that offensive the more you are rewarded with health, armour and ammo.
Doom is not your typical FPS. It is a game and it is okay with being a game. It is not trying to be an interactive, cinematic, story driven movie of a game. It has a story, and apart from a few places where it pauses the action whilst one of the games NPC's talks to you, those are few and far between. If you have no interest in the story you can ignore it but it is there for those who wish to uncover it.
It is a very good looking game. The game runs extremely well I get 4k 60hz on my gtx 1080 with dynamic resolution on with everything set to ultra.
I don't know what else to say other than if you enjoy fast and frantic FPS where you dance around demons blasting them back to hell, then this is the game for you.
