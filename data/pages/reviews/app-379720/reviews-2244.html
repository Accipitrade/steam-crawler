\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is simply a masterpiece.
The entire game is based on mobility and skill. Standing still will get you killed. The maps are amazing sometimes disorienting, exploration is challenging and rewarding, Special challenges (runes) allow you to customize your skills and weapons.
Beautiful bleeding edge graphics, intense music and action, great science fiction theme and ambience. Very challenging but satisfying to master. No bugs, microtransactions, telemetry, third party accounts and other garbage the game industry tends to excrete nowadays.
This is a fine example of a finished and polished game.
It even made me enjoy platform jumping (which I usually dislike).
The boss battles were so intense they left me shaking and exhausted and I needed several rematches to finally defeat them but what an immense feeling of accomplishment!
Highly recommended except for people with epilepsy, motion sickness or heart disease.\t\t\t\t\t

The game focuses on mobility and quick thinking. The ability to make a split second decision while under pressure. Unlike most shooters you don’t spend half the game cowering behind boxes or crates, and you’re not forced to run and hide. Instead you’re encouraged to take a more aggressive approach constantly attacking enemies while running at them, tanking hits while simultaneously picking up health. Also the game has an interesting atmosphere and environment that develops as you play. The only shortcomings I can think of is that there aren’t that many weapons, and that the game is kind of small. Still DOOM is a high quality enjoyable game.\t\t\t\t\t
