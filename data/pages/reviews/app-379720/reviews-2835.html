Doom is slowly climbing its way up to become one of my favorites in the genere. But hey, what can I say, sometimes after a bad day at work, ripping demon skulls in half and crushing enemies to a pulp is the ideal thing to do!

Bit of a warning though- it can cause motion sickness. I've found the best way to solve it is to turn the FOV way up (around 120%), lower motion blur (low/none) and disable chromatic aberration. Of course, the ideal settings would vary from person to person, as it is not guarenteed to cause motion sickness in everyone. However, in case this happens, I've found altering the settings in that way reduce any ill effects. Don't let this dissuade you; it's an excellent game, and I would reccomend it to anyone looking for a fun time- that's not afraid of gore!\t\t\t\t\t
