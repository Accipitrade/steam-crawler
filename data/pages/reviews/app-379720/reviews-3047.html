\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA challenging and fast-paced shooter with massive levels to explore and loads of secrets to uncover, adding tremendous replay value for completionists. Glory kills are just awesome and satisfying.

The only criticisms I have are the mediocre multiplayer (for what little I played), maps can get pretty irritating to navigate at times, and while the enemies are all great and different in their own right, it could've used a wider variety in both design and tactics in taking them down.

Nonetheless, this is an unapologetic, balls-to-the-wall gory and violent shooter that's an ABSOLUTE BLAST to play (no pun intended), even for those not normally fans of first-person shooters. To top off the fantastic gameplay is an AMAZING heavy metal soundtrack that matches the badass tone and mood.

It's one of those rare games that's every bit as fun to watch as it is to actually play. A VERY HIGH recommendation!\t\t\t\t\t
