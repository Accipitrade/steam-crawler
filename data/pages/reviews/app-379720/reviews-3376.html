\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this game on its lowest settings and still only made about 25 fps so if this reveiw seems a little negative, thats why. This game is fun, realy fun. its fast paced and the graphics look amazing, even with my settings as low as they could go, I was still amazed at the amount of details id managed to shove in this game. Every single enemy type is unique and different and each one makes you move and not stay behind cover and regenerate health. This game rewards exploration which can get you armor pickups and heath packs, yes you read that right thus game uses heathpacks, little figurines, weapons, ammunition and Maps from the classic doom and doom II. The rune system is great and lets you tailor your game to how you want to play it. i recomend playing this game on ultra-violence difficulty first because its going to put up a challenge you want to overcome. the level design fits beutifully in with the gameplay and push foward combat. The two things I disliked about the game is that if i want to change modes from campaign to multyplayer you have to restart the game, and that some enemies make the framerate drop so badly it becomes barely playable, then again this could just be my laptop. I still havent even beaten the campaign in my 11 hours of playing this game, and i still havent tried out this game's level editor, snapmap, and i wont touch the multyplayer because this game is hard enough at 20 fps. I highly recomend this game if you have a good PC and like shooters.
