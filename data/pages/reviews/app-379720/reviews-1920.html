The original FPS Demon Slayer is back and now with a bad ass soundtrack, with an intersting story behind it.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EeI3ApMn6eI
id Software does a great homage to the original franchise, with all the right monsters, guns and chainsaw. The gloomy atmosphere and map layout is great, they set a great pace(except when backtracking for secrets which lets face it is an homage as well).
  • Adult themed demon slaying simulator.
  • Great Campaign, of medium length.
  • Fast paced movement, of which only gets faster.
  • Double Jumping madness. No really, madness.
  • Nightmare difficulty level is awesome.
  • Dynamic music, level area and monster based, some good metal riffs in there.
  • Praetor suit upgrades through accumulated found chips.
  • Weapon upgrades through level accomplishments, secrets found, combat, & challenges.
  • Runes, gained through a glyph challenge always specific, and map placed.
  • Many many secrets hidden throughout the maps. Some of them a little more retro than others
  • Glory Kills! Typically 5 angles to execute foes when they are hurt, which in turn drops extra HP.
  • Glory Kills! Did I mention they are terribly violent in nature and a spectacle to behold.
  • Multiplayer, never tried myself, but it's there.
  • Arcade Mode, which allows replay of levels with all runes and upgrades fully upgraded and concentrates on a higher pace and score.
