As soon as I played E1M1, I fell in love with the series AND video games as a whole. Whenever they announced a new DOOM game after how DOOM 3 turned out, I got scared that they were going to put the final nail in the coffin. Sweet Jesus was I wrong. DOOM 2016 is the absolute best revival of an old classic that I have ever seen, and I fell in love with the series once again. Along with the fast paced gameplay and the amazing OST by Mick Gordon, this game lives up to its predecessors. This is one of the best FPS I have ever played and recommend it to anyone who loved the originals. 10/10 would relive my childhood again.\t\t\t\t\t
