\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere. Is. Only. DOOM. 2016.

There isn't a lot more to add really, I played this game at launch after several decades of generally being "over" first person action shooters, preferring my FPS games to be Dishonored or Deus Ex or the like. But my first computer game I fell in love with was Doom 2 back in the day, and they somehow managed to make me feel those feels again, packaged with even more fun ways to DOOM. Weapon upgrades, codex entries, amazing art, incredible backdrops and combat arenas, enemies that give no quarter, Preator suit upgrades and runes, there is a lot to dive into and the game never stops "giving" generously. Cannot recommend enough, for all skill levels (the "I'm too young to die mode" is still incredibly satisfying, even if you aren't very good at this kind of game - you'll feel like an unstoppable demon killing machine).\t\t\t\t\t
