\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is conflicting one, but I say it is not good enough 6.0/10

Pros:
- Most people love its fast paced shooter gameplay, and it is rather enjoyable, but might become repetitive
- Graphics are nice, but again eventually repetitive
- Exploring is quite satisfiying, platforming not so much
- PVP maps and modes are somewhat decent

Cons
- Forget about any cinematics, or any complex story whatsoever apart from you can grasp from reading tablets. It's plot even shallower than an action movie
- It is badly optimized, it is full of glitches, and it can be very laggy even in top PCs, specially the main menu
- I dont see too many combos or weapons at all, you can count them with your hands
- There are very few bosses, and some of them do not even count as that
- Replayability is limited to find hidden areas and compile the collectibles
- For a "badass" character, there are no really big epic moments other than crushing skulls

Conclusion: you will love it if you love pure FPSaction, if you expect any story to it, forget it
Take DOOM 3 for a decent Survival Horror game
or
DOOM (4) for a decent Action game.

Do not expect any lasting memories from DOOM games\t\t\t\t\t
