================Pros==================

Gameplay - Gameplay is extremely fun. You kill types of demons with a variety of weapons using brutal execution kills to create a glory spectacle. Its fun and satisfying.
Graphics - Graphics are crisp and environments are detailed.
Sound design - The sound design is brilliant. Some of the enemy sounds are genuinely terrifying and the metal music that plays during intense moments makes the game feel even more hardcore.
Levels - Levels are fairly varied, with around half in the demon world and half in Earth. While both are good, the demon levels are so much more interesting in their design and aesthetic.

================Cons==================
Repetitiveness The game does become a bit repetitive towards the latter half. Executions are cool but after doing it a hundred times, it starts to overstay its welcome and stops being satisfying.
Hitboxes Only a tiny issue but at times it felt annoying that to pick up ammo and health you have to be 100% touching them. Making the hitboxes a bit bigger for pickups would be nice in my opinion.

===========Conclusion================
Intentionally lacking any meaningful story, the game lets you jump right into the action and start killing hordes of demons. The gameplay is fun and rewarding even though it does grow a bit stale towards the end.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is easily one of the best fps games I've played. The combat is fun and intense, the soundtrack is fantastic and perfectly fits the gameplay, the lore and world building is fascinating. This game is overall a fantastic fps game and there's nothing out there that's like it. Out of all of the fps campaigns I have played this undoubtedly has one of the best fps campaigns ever. My only criticism with this game is that there should have been more bosses but other than that I do not have any major criticisms with this game. I definitely recommend this game if you like campaign based fps games since this has one of the best fps campaigns in existence.\t\t\t\t\t
