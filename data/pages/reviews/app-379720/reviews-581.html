\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI almost cried when the Pinky Demon made its re-appearance.

"Oh, Pinky, when did you get so big?" *pumps shrapnel from 24 shotgun shells into its face* "They grow up so fast..." *proceeds to penetrate its skull with one of its tusks*

__________________________________________________

Just as violent as the original Doom games from the early 1990's, only now with modern graphics and sound. And a story, now! I don't think most players of Doom who are fans of the game for what it is are into it for the story, but I really like a good story in a game (or in general, for that matter). The way this game's automated checkpoints work with only saving/loading for individual levels as a whole makes it just an absolute blast to play... I'm very grateful for it.

At time of review update I have beaten the campaign on Normal and was intimidated after trying it on Hard, but after pushing myself to really give it a shot, I think I can probably beat it on Hard. Definitely play this game again on a harder difficulty once you've already beaten it. It's practically built for that specifically. o.O\t\t\t\t\t
