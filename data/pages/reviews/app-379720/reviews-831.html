\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is boom. This campaign feels to me like Destiny 1 re-imagined in a more gory, aggressive, and fast-paced style. Unpopular opinion, but I really enjoyed Destiny 1, if you can't tell. Doom does really well to make the player feel truly powerful while still granting a sense of achievement when beating fights. The soundtrack is one of the best in video game history in my opinion; it adapts very well to what's happening in the game and gives you a shot of testosterone and rage. I'm a bit of a completionist, so it was annoying to find out I missed a secret after reaching points in the levels where I couldn't go back to get it without replaying the entire mission. Other small complaints are that you have to load a checkpoint if you fall of the map, and that the cutscenes aren't skip-able. Other than that, masterpiece.\t\t\t\t\t
