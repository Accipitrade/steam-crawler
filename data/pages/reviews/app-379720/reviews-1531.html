Doom is an old school fast paced 3rd person shooter. Shoot the demons before they kill you.
Not many of this type of games are still produced. The problem is the game play feels bland.

The good:
- Old school. Fast paced. Minimal fancy tricks.
- Great graphics.
- Runs super smooth on high graphics settings (after the Nvidia update in Jan 2020).
- Super pricing. ID had to be commended for selling this game at a steal.

The bad:
- The game just feels unexciting, at least it becomes better later on when the mayhem increases. It is hard to put my finger on exactly why the game feels unexciting. Maybe I have outgrown this genre?
- The maps are mostly unexciting. The Hell levels are great, but the rest are not.

The ugly:
- Nothing much.\t\t\t\t\t
