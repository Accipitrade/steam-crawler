Doom is a 2016 first-person shooter game developed by id Software and published by Bethesda Softworks.

Spoiler warning.

the soundtrack still plays in my head

DOOM 2016 is a FPS shooter game. Nothing less, nothing more. The only thing you do in this game, is shoot monsters. Are you mindblown yet?

No, but seriously - the story in this game is very much... not needed, *clears throat* because at its core, it's just about a bad girl that wanted to have a lot of power, so she opened a portal to hell, and made a mess. Thats where you - the DOOM slayer come in.. to fix everything - and kill her for the greater good. yea i told u bro it not the best

Gameplay on the other hand - masterpiece. Flows great, plays great, easy to understand. Even though this sentence is overused - DOOM 2016 does NOT have any BS. (except the story, which isn't well, bullsh*t, but it's just... it had to be there to not be a flat story-less game)

Weapons are found in the missions on dead bodies and such - they are made in a "linear" way when playing, so you get every gun. There are weapon mods for every gun in the game (except the BFG 9000), that are earned by finding a flying drone. Pick a mod for any of your weapons. punch the poor boi and steal the mod package from him. While playing, you earn weapon coins that you use to upgrade these mods. Every weapon mod has a special challenge IF you get all the upgrades before it, which makes the weapon stronger - and sometimes gives a gun a new ability (example: super shotgun mastery challenge makes it so you can shoot one more time before reloading)

Monster variety is INSANE. Small, medium, big monsters, demon dogs, flying demons - anything you can imagine hell would bring. The game does also have bossfights that are relatively hard, until you learn him which takes about 2-3 deaths. There are so many monsters, the game features a bestiary, so you can study on how to kill them efficiently.

Music (soundtrack) is phenomenal. Top notch tracks. Mostly made of rock tracks. They are perfect for this game's atmosphere. Placed well in fights, you'll be feeling like a crazy demon killer with memorable rock music to go by.

Sadly, at this time of reviewing DOOM 2016, I haven't played the old DOOM games (sorry, was too small back then!) so it's hard for me to compare if it's like the old games. One thing I can say, is that this game is f*cking amazing. A blast to play. Finished it in only 7 hours, never had a dull moment while playing. The game feels so polished, bug free, it's unbelievable. Everything in this game fits like a puzzle into itself. The music, the gunplay, the monster killing. This isn't A "ZOMBIE" GAME. This is DOOM.


9/10\t\t\t\t\t
