\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll be honest, when I first saw all the previews for this game i was underwhelmed. I thought "oh look another generic arena shooter" and "glory kills for health, what the hell is this?" I'm very happy that I was blown away by this game. Every step of the campaign was a ridiculously fun, ball busting, gorefest that didn't disappoint at any step of the way. The secrets are expertly placed and the classic levels are a really neat touch.

The level design is superb, the variety of locations is surprisingly varied for basically 2 primary locations, and overall the art direction the team went with was very well done and was eye catching at almost every turn. One thing I do have to commend above all else is the sound design. This game has the best sound package of any game I've played in a very long time. The guns are incredibly weighty and boom in your ears. The ambient sounds throughout each stage are perfectly designed to fit the specific area they're in and keep you on edge. Musically, I absolutely love what they did. The soundtracks for the original 2 games are among my favourites of all time, and while substantially different, the music in this game is perfect to a tee. I'm a bit of a metalhead myself so my love for the soundtrack may come as no surprise, but the way the thunderous drums and biting guitars makes your blood pump while you're knee-deep in the dead gives you a feeling almost no other game can.

Con wise I have very little to say about the game, mostly though I would say the pacing is slightly off towards the end. I'm someone who prides exploration,and after large battles I tend to explore and try to find everything before moving on to the next area. However towards the end of the game I felt like it was a constant stream of battles and found like I was fighting the game design of those specific areas by going and exploring.

Needless to say, this game is a masterpiece in my opinion, and I'm glad I ate my words and gave this a chance. Id have created a fantastic shooter and one of their best games, and hopefully it'll lead to more bloodshed in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
