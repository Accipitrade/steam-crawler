This bloodfest is the game that other shooters aspire to emulate. Decent writing, believable characters (including doom guy, himself), and top-notch gunplay.

What, exactly, makes it top notch? The player has an enormous degree of agency during fights with demons. Swarms of enemies, agile imps or bloated mancubi included, chase you under and over the terrain, yet you can still come out victorious. Combat is not a hit or miss here--it's a duck and shoot, jump and shoot, snatch that health, and shoot. It's an evolving slaughterfest that seems overwhelming at first. Shootouts will have you climbing and dodging between crates, up or down stairs, or diving for low ground to put some space between you and three hell knights. This isn't a twitch shooter--battles don't end before they begin. It's glorious.

Build up your reflexes as you play, and before you know it, you'll be snapping necks and shotgunning your way through thousands of em.\t\t\t\t\t
