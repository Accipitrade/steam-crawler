\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom (2016) went beyond a lot of my expectations in terms of gameplay. The frequent demon spawns kept the pace of the game high and it was satisfying to blow up and dismember everyone in my path. This review is only from the single player campaign. I have not played multiplayer.

Things I loved.
The multi-level rooms allowed for full movement to kill.
The world is beautifully created and each mission was well done.
The character models are fantastic.


Some things I didn't like.
The demon's snarls and growls were too loud and too frequent when they are just idling close to your location. There was no real sound fall off depending on where you were and it gave a constant sense that they were right next to you.

There is still a bug with the glory kill animation where you can get stuck under the floor or in a wall.

The story was simple enough to follow but there were some aspects that weren't clear. This got worse as I got closer to the end of the game. I'm being vague to avoid spoilers.\t\t\t\t\t
