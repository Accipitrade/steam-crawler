\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI already had this on PS4 but with the sale today I just had to buy it to play on PC.

Doom 2016 is a worthy succesor to the original. Like the OG Doom it's light on plot but heavy on arcade shooting action. It keeps the fast paced gameplay rather than the slow horror atmosphere from DOOM3.

While I liked the story driving campaign of Doom3 I also love the old school throwback of Doom4.

It really feels like D1 too but with some enhancements.
If youve ever played Brutal Doom the glory kill system seems heavily inspired by it. Which I think has been implemented into the game quite well.It's a pretty awesome reimagining of the punching from DOOM1.

It can get repetative at times (so did the original doom) but the maps and gameplay are varied enough to keep you interested.

The only other con is the Multiplayer. Its ok but pretty bland. However DO NOT GET IT JUST FOR MULTIPLAYER. The arcade mode and snapmaps are the real replay value.

The game runs great even on a lower end pc. I get a solid 60fps on High settings(with a couple settings like shadows, lighting, and reflections set on medium) at 900p with a FX6300 and GTX 1060.

TL;DR RIP AND TEAR! RIP AND TEAR!!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore I continue with this review: I have not played the original DOOM, DOOM 2 or DOOM 3.

Just a few years ago I watched a video about the DOOM franchise, before that I knew nothing about the franchise, even it's existence in the first place.

So when I watched the trailer for the new DOOM game I was almost immediately sold. On the other hand, like I said earlier in this review, I knew next to nothing about the franchise. Because of this I had a few doubts about the game, If I'd like the game or not.

In the end my doubts about the game instantly vanished when I finished the first 2 or 3 levels of the campaign. The Campaign is perfect, the Multiplayer is fun most of the time and Snapmap is a concept that keeps the game fresh and original at the same time. The soundtrack that Mick Gordon made is so good, even though I wasn't a big fan of this type of music before. Now I listen to it almost every day or week.

Basicly, this game is really good. There are a few games that I have played in my life that constantly get my attention, this is one of those games.

Yesterday I made a very very short review of this game. It was unprofessional and I apologize for making that review. I wanted to explain one of the main reasons I play this game so much: Stress relieve. When I had a rough day at school, I came back home and started DOOM. The gameplay is as energizing as it is relaxing.

I totally recommend this game.

Easily a 9.5/10 or higher.
