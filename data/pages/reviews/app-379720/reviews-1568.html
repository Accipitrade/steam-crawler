The game isnt bad, but there are some little bugs which are just annoying, one would be that you can kill yourself with a glory kill, when a enemy stays too close to a wall for example its possible to glitch throu thta wall with a glory kill and then you will fall down into your death.

Also, dont call a wepon "super" when its acrually more useless and more lame then every other wepon


Im glad that i bought this game for 6€ because of the told reasons and i also got throu the story mode within 5 hours.

I would recommend this game when you alredy know this genre ore want to try it and the game is on sale, thats probably also the case for eternal when that will come out.\t\t\t\t\t
