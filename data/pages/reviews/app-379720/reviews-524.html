\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a bit frustrated with this game right now, because I just checked the achievements and about half of them require playing multiplayer. I'm just not interested. Two of them have to do with a snap map and making one and playing 5 of them. How tedious. None of the achievements really have to do with ultra hard mode basically, other than completing the very first map (dipping the toe or something like that).

Outside of that this game has been a blast and I am probably about to get the next Doom very soon. I like the upgrading mechanic (thanks Bethesda) that is brought about in most of the games. The guns are so much fun. I rarely used the chainsaw or the BFG9000, because the other guns really handled their business most of the game.

The bosses were also a challenge the first time I really faced them. I thought the first boss was the end game. Surprisingly, it says I've only got about 12 hours in this game. It seems like it took much longer, but I really did finish it quite quickly, so maybe it's a very short game. I've been going after secrets and rounding out my guns, though. I'm still not finished with all of that and I plan to do it all and get a few more achievements, but the multiplayer just isn't in the cards for me. I guess the game will remain incomplete. Probably not all that inspiring 5 years after release anyway.\t\t\t\t\t
