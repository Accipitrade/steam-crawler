\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlot is over the top in a good way. It doesn't take itself to seriously, but it is also intriguing and there is enough of it there for people who want it.

Gameplay is smooth and fun. Lot's of ways to wage combat on the enemy in this game, which makes fights pretty interesting. There is a cool upgrade system that makes the player an unstoppable fighting machine (that is unless you play on nightmare then you are very stoppable because it is hard).

Level design is mostly good. Everything looks great and flows together well. Unfortunately some of the battle arenas have redundant layouts. This can make them feel a bit underwhelming. Don't get me wrong. Doom has always been redundant it's just that you feel that redundancy a bit more in some parts because of the battle arena nature of the game.

The mission select system that allows you to replay the game with maxed out stats gives the game great replay factor.
