\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwesome twitch-shooter goodness for when you want to just turn your mind off and kill baddies. Game looks beautiful and is well-optimized. Soundtrack is banging. Weapons are satisfying and can be adjusted somewhat to your playstyle.

A couple complaints though:
  • Ledge clambering is super unreliable at times.
  • The weapon upgrade challenges are distracting and force you to change your playstyle, which can break the flow.
  • The game doesn't seem to "click" until you've got the majority of your weapons and learn to cycle through them quickly, and by that point the game is halfway through.
  • The latter half of the game is A+ material, but the final boss is somewhat disappointing and the end is too abrupt.

Still had a ton of fun with it. I need some sort of "survival" mode where you can just kill endless waves of demons, because I didn't get my fill.

My Rating: 4/5
