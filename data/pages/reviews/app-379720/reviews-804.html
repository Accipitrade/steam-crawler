\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHands down the better game between Doom eternal and Doom 2016, is this iteration. The latter is a real first person action game, that hands you tools and allows you to use them. Doom eternal wants to prolong the show, to try and be more than what really made the series great.

As a first person shooter this game is top of the line. The main character Doom slayer, feels bulky but not slow, for this series and genre that is a refreshing feeling. You are nimble and reactionary in your movements; which allows your aiming and gun play to be aggressive. In defensive situations where you are moving backwards, you aren't clumsy or clunky, your movements are weighted and measured, even when enemies are racing around you.

Because the game play is so smooth it perfectly counteracts the feeling of your strafing. Your movements give you enough time to aim, and pick the best target, that allows you to strafe and engage all over again. It's really well done and gives a really satisfying feeling eliminating demons.

The atmosphere and movement have a level of tension that is also appreciated. It isn't suffocating because you are choosing your movements. What Doom Eternal lacks is everything that Doom 2016 is, player choice. We already give up any sort of open world feel. We give up on having a million abilities and weapons. Giving up the freedom of engagement in combat is a definitive downgrade in the Doom series.

With that being said this is Doom 2016, I have had this game for years, and I have to say it has aged well. It stands heads and tails above its would be successor. Even in 2021 this is a world class FPS game.\t\t\t\t\t
