Is it DOOM? That's such a dumb question but it seems to be the thing asked most "is it DOOM?". It's totally not DOOM. Because that game was made a very long time ago and the only thing these games have in common is the general idea and the genre. There's a lot of melee now and I like that, it makes the combat really flow as you gib enemies left and right with the shotgun as you're running from a Baron who desperately wants to pull your legs off your body. The combat really does flow. The level design is ridiculously good (in the singleplayer only), I mean some of the movement you can do to traverse above/behind/around your enemies is insane. There's a part (semi spoilers) where you have to use mining drones and the like to traverse up a very long shaft. All the while you're fighting demons and blowing â™¥â™¥â™¥â™¥ up, as you should be. This is DOOM! (This is not DOOM, though). I'm glad they pushed forward a made a totally new game, this is a moshpit of ideas and they come together extremely well in the singleplayer. The story is nice, there's tons of lore and secret spaces to go after if you're into that, or you want that next killer upgrade. The challenges that are dotted through missions are incredibly fun and some are near impossible. If you embrace glory kills, the chainsaw, and your entire arsenal, you will dance your way through the carnage like a balerina of death. It is insanely satisfying when everything comes together and glory kill a massive demon, like a Baron. Sawing through his leg and then his face after you jump off a platform where you just supershotgunned a revenant in the face is pretty great.

The singleplayer is definitely built for multiple playthroughs. I'm already looking forward to going through it on Nightmare, and eventually Ultra Nightmare. I played the entire thing on Ultra Violence so far and I've loved the challenge. Overall it wasn't too bad, but then again I've played a lot of shooters competitively so it shouldn't be awful for me. I know some people thought the weapons didn't feel powerful in multiplayer during the beta, well have no fear for they were built for the singleplayer! If you liked Wolfenstein: The New Order, you'll like this. It's 10x as gory and violent and has far faster paced gameplay.
