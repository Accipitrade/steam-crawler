The game contains lots of guns that are all useful in their own right (well, besides the pistol). The smooth gunplay and hellish level environments are accompanied by a heavy metal soundtrack that is one to remember. The game does contain multiplayer, but I didn't even bother with it since I heard it's not worth playing. Besides a couple of glitches and oddly hard to find switches, DOOM is a marvelous first person shooter that will be remembered as one of the greats, just like the original was over two decades ago.\t\t\t\t\t
