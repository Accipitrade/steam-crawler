\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEVERYONE MUST BUY THIS GAME! This game brings back so many memories not just of the old Doom games, but older games in general. Movement is such an important mechanic in the franchise I i like they nailed the feel and style amazingly. The music is top notch and it shines when you destroy a pod and the heavy metal starts whaling. The weapons and customizations are plentiful and deffinitely enough to get you entertained and there is enough enemy variation. THe AI is some of the best AI i have ever scene. The demons will constantly be moving and trying to dodge you making it a real challenge. Also the maps are perfectly designed creating verticality and even making exploration possible. The iteration of Doom feels like an open world experience almost, rarely did I feel like I was on tracks and I could only go where I wanted me to go. Also there are challenges and Rune mini-game events that add even more life to the game. The Graphics, Gore, and Effects are perfect. I am able to play on max settings at 1080p and this is one of the best looking games I have ever seen. I must warn those who do not have a very good rig, in this game, you need a high frame rate to truly injoy yourself. It is a twitch shooter and a lower fram rate would give you a headache. I am playing on the 3rd highest difficulty and I have to say that this game is very difficult, constant movement is key and I do get a sense of my own skills progressing not just my charactor. I have never felt overpowered or helpless. The story does leave alittle to be desired but it gets the job done so that you can move forward and murder thousands of demons among the way.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou will be a Sinner 10/10

I played the second it came out and I will say it is bloody ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ fun to play. It is easily a very good successor to Doom 3 that we have been waiting for some years for. The core mechanics, graphics and gameplay is fluent and bloody brilliant. No doubt when you jump into the game after purchasing you won't be dissapointed. The multi-player is fun for what it is, and a good little side game to play with friends that acts like any other regular PvP shooter/arena style game. That being said, the real element to the game I honestly play DOOM for is the main game and it's guns, and it is really good. It has sort of a Halo meets Metroid Prime feel to the surroundings with secrets, passageways and areas of monsters to defeat, and nothing feels more satisfying than to break the neck or limbs off a monster while blowing ♥♥♥♥ up around it. That being said a well rounded game that definitely shows its trademark. I do have some problems with this game though.

1. The Campaign is not as long as you may think. The main game does have a decent long storyplot that goes through several areas of the UAC installation and inevitably Hell. But I found the world to be smaller, somewhat linear unlike the older game of Doom 3, which always had you going through endless passageways, installations and areas and constantly discovering something new as you go deeper. In this DOOM it is all pretty easy to travel and discover everything the game has to offer first time through.

2. It is not Scary. My time playing it I have yet to be genuinely scared from the atmosphere, environmental elements or monsters themselves. Unlike the old Dooms and Doom 3 where the game was genuniely scary, dark and grimy. The new DOOM does not have this all that well. Most areas are open, bright, and so action packed that the monster's are not scary and it is tough to be freightened when you are constantly blasting away at enemies left and right while ripping through them with a chainsaw. Which is not bad considering, but its tough to be scared when the action drowns it out.

3. The Multiplayer although fun is very bare, it is nothing new but the usual kill or be killed team up match battles. The unlockables are not all that great, and it's just your usual Player vs Player arena style junk that we see in hundreds of games now. It is fun for a while but it will get boring with the same stuff everytime. If have friends to play it is can be very enjoyable and that is about it. The mix between having demons and switchups to the teams makes it refreshing, and with the added DLC's to it I can say keeps the interest going, but than again it's just another multiplayer.

4. No Main Game Co-Op or Co-Op Mods. This was somewhat a huge deal for me personally when waiting so many years for the next DOOM to really build into the campaign and survival with friends. I thought maybe finally developers of such great games would come to be aware how fun and successful main game co-op stuff could be. A big intricate main game with your friends is something many fans and my friends wanted to see. But instead there is no real co-op but little side mission/PVP Co-op junk that falls short of what we really wanted. When I first heard about this game and saw the Co-Op in its tags I was so hyped and hoped for a real main game campaign of Doom filled with Co-op survival action, similar to the Doom 3 co-op mod for the main game. But it became a letdown and became just another Multiplayer action scroeboard game.

All in All the game is still worth its title and a great game overall that kept me entertained and still entertained with its time. So until than I am going to chainsaw some more of these monsters!\t\t\t\t\t

I was anxious when purchasing a 60 Euro title because the multiplayer reviews were so negative, but I bought it on impulse
because its DOOM, and the original DOOM's have serious nostalgic value to me. Never the less, heres my opinion.

Pros:
1 - The pacing of New weapons and Mobs: I wasn't sure how'd they go about things, seeing there was limited guns in doom and there were only so many monsters, but the paced the game great! Evenly spreading everything out so it wasnt too soon to get a new gun that it wouldnt be satisfying and that you didnt have to wait too long to add another weapon to your glorious arsenal of death.

2 - Descriptions for mobs and weapons: Any game I play, whether its open world or a linear mission-based game I always loved to read about the enemies and items, especially in doom. When I played the original doom, I didnt know their names, Identifying Pinkys as "Pink Lions" when I was younger. Found it interesting to actually know their names and their origins, same for the guns.

3 - Weapon and Armour Upgrades: When a minigun isn't powerful enough, stick three barrels on that f*cker! Enough said. I won't go into too much detail because I seriously want you to experience the incredibly satisfying gun mods for yourselves! And all I'll say about the armour is, praise the lord for the double jump mobility upgade!

4 - Pacing of the action: There isn't a dull moment in doom, unless you like exploring for hidden items and unlockables like myself. Roughly around the second mission they introduce you to a hell night (The monster from the DOOM 3 cover) and you get caught by surprise. They then shove 4 more in your face because ♥♥♥♥ You! Millions of mobs to kill and at a quick pace, nothing more satisfying then seeing a room full of tough mobs later in the game and spotting a berserk at the corner of your eye. Suddenly, at the mere sight of the berserk powerup you feel like death, destroyer of existance!

5 - The right length: Thats what she said. And thats what im saying too, because the length of the game was spot on. DOOM, as I stated previously, only has so many guns and different varients of mobs, so it wouldnt have been longer then it was, Goldilocks moment for this game, it was just right!

6 - DOOM guys attitude: I was very amused the whole way through at the way your character reacted to everything. Literally in the very first scene he was getting barked at by Dr. Who gives a ♥♥♥♥, and shoved the monitor with force away like it was nothing, and smashing the voice comm in the elevator soon after.

Cons - None at all really, aside from the multiplayer, this game was a very enjoyable experience and a great nostalgic throwback.

Not that my opinion means anything, but I'm giving this a 10/10. They couldnt have done it better with todays technology, loved it!\t\t\t\t\t
