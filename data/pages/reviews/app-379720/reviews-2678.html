This is the run and gun mayhem many have been waiting for, with gory melee kills and tons of exploration elements between arenas. Beside the fights, the game has scores of secrets to find that makes it very rewarding to replay levels over and over again. Additionally Id added a very sophisticated snap map editor to create your own maps and a very enjoyable multi-player that very much reminds me of Halo.
Doom is the best shooter in years, seriously!

Pros:
- fantastic graphics
- that run with a constant 60 frames on my ASUS R9 280 with max settings
- supports Radeons Vulkan driver
- familiar enemies that look better than ever
- all the weapons you dreamed of
- plus a chain saw and the big fat gun
- tons of secrets and unlock-able upgrades
- exceptional good voice acting
- great music and sound effects
- perfect single-player level design
- emphasis on single-player
- enjoyable halo like multi-player
- extensive map editor
- arcade mode
- made me sweat like hell

Cons:
- very long loading times
- long time multi-players have advantage over noobs because your equipment improves the longer you play
- two crashes in 50 hours play time (thankfully in single player)\t\t\t\t\t
