Dammit. I left the tap on in the kitchen. Ah, that's alright. When I get home I'm gonna have to tighten the valves. Back to the matter at hand. Yes, you, demon imp. Mr. Matter-at-hand. Just had to enlist in hell's army didn't you? Your little devil sergeants and devil colonels probably told you your post in this weird gothic cathedral was going to be uneventful. Well guess what? You done messed up. Probably not best to find yourself under my boot next to some very clearly god-forsaken church. Because we're in Hell. Heh. That was funny. Seriously though, you have to question the wisdom of your commanding officers when everyone knows who I am. You all call me the Doom Slayer here in your sacred texts. I'm not much a Doom Slayer as I am just a Doom Guy. Ever since the first invasion so long ago, I've only cared about making sure you guys don't kill anyone's pet bunny again. Yes, imp under my boot, one of your demonic species kind killed my poor pet bunny rabbit Scruffles a long time ago while I was far away from home. All I've ever cared about. Ever. For twenty years. So here I am again, having to fight through actual Hell because Satan apparently hasn't heeded any past rampage through these halls. Stupid demons, my god. And I don't know who's running around lighting those stupid little candles and setting up altars, but I'll find him soon. Sure, here in Hell the architecture is nice and the ground has a neat red-rust Mars thing going on, but it can only get prettier after I'm finished ripping and tearing. A moment ago it almost looked like you and your buddies were going to put up a fight until you saw the power armor and realized that I was the traumatic event so many of you have PTSD from. You threw a few fireballs at me from across the clearing while I tore your friends' arms off and used them to smash the other's head in. I can't remember now if I even cared to break their spines before tossing their bodies to the ground and blasting something else with my shotgun, and I already don't know what's next to get my hands on after I'm finished up dealing with you. I've been talking about this for a while, but you ARE the 87th demon under my boot today. You're special. You're the only numeric demon that has had an 8 AND a 7 in your title since number 78. That was a minute or two ago. Imagine my delight when I had number 83 in my hands and realized you were coming up. Number 87. And until I get to number 178, I won't have another ticket quite like you to punch. Your armies should have ran when I arrived at doom's gate. Not when I was in the foyer. Oh well. Better crush your skull like all the rest. Better get back to the matter at hand, and dammit, I have to get home to tighten that valve under the sink. Maybe Scruffles II will be waiting for me on the couch. Alright, imp, time's up. Flex the abdominals... tense the right leg... aim that power armored boot at your head... This won't hurt a bit.\t\t\t\t\t
