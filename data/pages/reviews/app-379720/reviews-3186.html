\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI honestly enjoy playing this game to a high extent.

Pros:
The campaign can be summed up into two words: F@#king Awesome.

-Glory kills are amazing to watch and the weapons are so destructive! This game has been a thrill to play and the final boss (Don't worry, I'm not going to spoil anything) has such a satisfying death. In other words,the ultimate glory kill.

-The multiplayer has extra weapons that aren't in the campaign but are still fun to use. You can spawn in with a different loadout each time you die. Gamemodes can get crazy very fast when the first rune comes in.

-Challenges and continued exploration in the campaign give a lot of End-Game content (If you can actually find them).

-The sheer difficulty at "Hurt Me Plenty" and above puts players on the edge of their seats. If you pay attention to your surroundings, then progression will be enjoyable. If you think this is your average Call of Duty on easy difficulty, then buckle up and prepare for your body to be made up of 97% salt.

-Upgrade system is fair and balanced. You have to save your points in order to get better upgrades instead of getting little and spending all of it at once.

-Blood. Lots of it.

-Collectibles. Nostalgic collectibles.

Cons:
-The multiplayer customization however is very much similar to Halo. This isn't a big problem, but I expected something different.

-If you want to play this on max settings, then you will need a beefy computer, or at least a good graphics card and 1/10 of your whole hard drive dedicated to this game. This is NOT a game for slow comptuers.

-Slightly annoying that in order to play multiplayer or use the map-builder... thing, you will have to wait for the game to restart and load all over again.

Conclusion:
It's a great game, but like every game to exist... ever, it will have it's downfalls. This game however is beautifully horrendous and can/will take up a majority of your day. Yeah, it's that fun.\t\t\t\t\t
