\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA little late to the Demon-killing party, but man is this game brutal! I like the faster move speed quite a lot; it's a breathe of fresh air to be constantly on the move taking potshots at imps with the super shotgun vs. waiting behind cover constantly waiting for health to refill.

The secrets were a fun nod to the original Doom, with hidden levels scattered through the campaign resembling the original textures. I am loving the campaign levels so far. There's a great variety to the landscapes, and the atmosphere of Mars comes through really well, with plenty of time to take in the sights between the sections of rage-fueled carnage and blood. I'm not much of a multiplayer guy, so I couldn't tell you about that.

I really enjoy the soundtrack, so much so that I ended up buying the CD. Metal is my primary genre, and this soundtrack blends industrial techno styles with modern djent style riffing. My roommates and I quote the opening intro track to each other constantly.

Get this game.\t\t\t\t\t
