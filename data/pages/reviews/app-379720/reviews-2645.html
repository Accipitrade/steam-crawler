\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhy is this game a 7/10 (some might say lower than that) ?
And why it is not for everyone ?

Pros:
1. Staying true to the original concept of "Shoot first ask questions later", basically a "No brainer Game" Ment to challange you skill wise at killing monsters. This PRO is also a CON cause it will fit certain player and certain gaming purpuses.
2. Graphics are excellent, very atmospheric and very true to the original art concepts of DOOM.
3. Awsome animations for mele attacks
4. Mele is crucial part of combat, and ofcourse the CHAINSAW is awsome certain kill.
5. Active multiplayer community.
6. Community mods provide a whole range of cool maps starting whith novelty maps to really thought out ones.
7. cool multiplayer modes, and team based play, where you can also become a monster (it is cool).

Cons:
1. Gets boring after a while - Basically the 1st paragraph from PROS, I mean... for me it got boring when I reached the latter levels, pretty much doing the same thing over and over again, kill a bunch of monsters (Awsomley) then find some key to some door, rinse, and repeat. I get how its importent to stay true to the original, but at 2017, some content would be appriciated, maybe some story, which bring me to the next CON.
2. No story... Ok I get it the originall didnt really have a story eather, your mars station is invaded by demons and you need to kill em all, then your sucked into hell and you keep killing demons.
3. Multiplayer having LAG issues. I explain: many times you are in a game lloby and all players are on red ping, which you could say is balanced but still...
4. For some reason that I cant put my finger the game lacks satisfaction facotr... maybe something with the lack of XP purpose... maybe the "rinse & repeat" nature of the game.

I gave it 7/10 because basically it was much fun at first but it could not hold that level of fun for much.
I need more story... was somewhat disapointed by the shalowness of the game.
However for devout shooter fans and doom fans, who like fast paced multiplayer this is a sure buy.\t\t\t\t\t
