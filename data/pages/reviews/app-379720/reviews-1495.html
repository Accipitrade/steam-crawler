\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNow when Doom Eternal came out and I finished it I looked back to Doom 2016...

And honestly? I feel it is much better game. Sure Eternal did some things better. But few improvements does not change the fact that Doom 2016 is more Doom.

It is darker, more sinister and more badass. What I loved about Doom 2016 was Doom slayers nature. Using severed hands, torso with head to get access, taking weapons from dead personels, his clear anger and lack of interest for what is going on. He woke up and started to rip and tear. No time to listen no time for some tutorial. You wanted to smash the enemies with your fist? Sure thing. It is like a horror game where you are the monster. Setting is creepy. Backstory as well. But the demons are afraid of you. I love to punch all the zombies to death just for the giggle. While Eternal is supposed to be more fast paced in reality I still feel the Doom 2016 was more fluid and better at it. Also the maps were still linear but I felt like I am in a real place.

To sum it up.
Doom 2016 is darker, more brutal and you feel like a wrecking machine. It is definitely a great game and has a nice multiplayer with lots of mods. Oh and snapmaps. So you always have something to play.
Honestly Doom 2016 deserves its 96/100
Rip and tear my friend
