\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gameplay is awesome, the level design is awesome. Honestly what made DOOM was not just great gunplay but it was also the replay value. For instance I played E1M1 probably 40 times or so a bit less or more. I can already feel that I actually want to play certain levels again and build my character slightly different.

After hearing Stratton say "After first playthrough you can change some upgrades that you have on your second play through" I thought that was just rubbish and is really not significant enough to have any real impact on replay value. However I was wrong and it really does change the experience enough to warrant multiple play throughs. Also Ultra-Nightmare is the ultimate challange, until you have beaten Ultra-Nightmare technically you have yet to exhaust all the game has to offer.


Pros:
-This has to be the one of the biggest points, picking up certain iconic items feels very satisfying. The amount of detail and the sound really adds to the feeling of accomplishment. To list a few I really like picking up the Praetor suit upgrades, key cards and key skulls.
-Awesome gameplay
-Surprisingly quite deep lore and very unique character building techniques, which does not detract from the game.
-Awesome difficulty, me being very good at fps I actually found that the gameplay is challanging and I am only on Ultra-Violance. Can't wait to get rip and tear in Nightmare.
-Hell levels are actually more fun to play then they were in the Old DOOM, they are much more coherent yet carry the same awe and hellishness.
-Awesome sound track, they really took the best from the old DOOMs and made it even better. Whoever says I prefer the old E1M1 midi music clearly can't tell when something has been made better.
-Multiplayer is actually its own game and is not trying to emulate others, I saw all the hate from the beta and all I have to say if you want to play Quake so bad, then go play Quake and don't try to shove Quake down DOOMs throat. I am sure that is what id thought too, they did not want to create a DOOM game with Quake multiplayer, that is just wrong.


Cons:
-Quite a few FPS issues however there are some fixes that actually give you +20 fps here is the link to the steam forum topic: http://steamcommunity.com/app/379720/discussions/0/357286119106178993/?ctp=3
-Don't expect too much help from support as I feel like they are currently overwhelmed by the hordes of people emailing them bugs and other issues, thus it decreases the quality of the replies you get.\t\t\t\t\t
