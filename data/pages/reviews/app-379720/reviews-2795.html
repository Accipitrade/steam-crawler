\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're the TL;DR type of reader, I'll sum this up quick here:
BUY THIS GAME.
Imagine if Metroid Prime had a satanic orgy with Doom, the modern FPS, and a plush toy...
Yeah, the spawn of that would be this game!

Story/Lore: 7/10
Graphics/Performance: 9.8/10
Audio: 10/10
Gameplay: 9/10
Replayability*: 6.5/10
Immersion: 8/10
Bad*ss Factor: 9.9/10
Joy Factor: 10/10
Challenge Satisfaction: 10/10

*I have not yet tried anything outside of campaign, so regard replayability not yet definitively rated.


Now for the "TL;DR" section:

This game is pretty fun, but if you're easily triggered, get a barf bag and man-up!

What is truly impressive is how incredibly well optimized they made this game!
On my ever aging rig that's been given a 2nd wind by jamming a 8GB GTX 1080 up its tail pipe, I'm cranking out frames faster than a frisky squirrel in heat, averaging - AVERAGING - 120fps with ultra settings across the board, even with 8xTXAA, on 1920x1080 16:9 resolution... and somehow this is a Bethesda game? I suspect the team in charge of optimization may have consorted with the Devil himself for this project, because it's optimized as well as H*** is hot!

Kudos on Bethesda for finally publishing a game that DOESN'T run like a sticky turd.


That's my initial impression of this game.
Having played several missions now, I feel I must add more to this review:

This game is AMAZING. It's a perfect blend of old and new, with something blue... more like blew the f*** up!
Truly, this game will give you a pleasant nostalgia hit (especially with the classic Doom maps), yet it'll give you the immersion and fluidity you've come to love in modern gaming.
The game is full of all the classic elements done right; getting your s*** pushed at times even on medium difficulty (can you say, "runic trial where you have to melee/glory kill He** Knights from above?"), gunplay, carrying every weapon in your inventory simultaneously (seriously, I friggin' miss this totally unrealistic yet fun feature), ammo and health management, exploration, time trials, and platformer elements to level design.
The modern stuff is done near perfectly, too; there's gear customization (I'm quite fond of the combo of shotty with explosive mastery and the .50 cal LMG with scope mastery), OUTSTANDING audio (both soundtrack and effects), silky performance that churns out massive frames per second, and of course collectables/Easter-eggs.\t\t\t\t\t
