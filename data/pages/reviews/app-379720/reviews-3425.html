\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBethesda and ID managed to bring this gaming back to it's roots where room, speed, maneuverability stand above HP+armor. If you stay still you will die. While this game lacks a Witcher 3 quality story it has just enough to make it work, because this is doom, here is a shotgun and a machine gun, those guys are statding between you and next level. Level it out. The combat is fun, weapon do feel heavy and glory kills are well paced that do not break combat even when you chain them. got a decent 10h of singleplayer campaign(without hardcore must complete everything 100%). The bosses are nice as well as the enemy variety.

Now on to the bad parts of the game. First and foremost the music. I didn't really feel any of the tracks, there are very few times where I heard the music and went ''♥♥♥♥ Yeah!''. The next are the bosses, without spoiling I will just say that they need to be harder and not just more HP+DAM. Get creative Cyber Demon was the hardest and most fun of them all. Chainsaw feels more like a cheat that you actually do need to survive. Then there is an issue that went thought whole campaign, not enough demons to kill. Seriously, while it can appear that there are many you can still maneuver around them quiet easily.

Lastly, but I do believe one of the most crucial one is add Music in multiplayer because it feels empty without it and rather unsatisfying.

But overall game is very well made, action is good altho might feel limited at times, multiplayer is fun, cannot wait what will snapmap bring over time. I'd give it a solid 9/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been an avid fan of iD software since the Wolfenstein 3d days, and this game is a prime example of why I do not doubt them in the least. I will 100% recommend this game to anyone and everyone.

I have not yet finished the game, as I have also been messing around in MP and SnapMap, but the campaign to where I am has been nothing but intense, fast, very fluid and well animated combat, alot of exploration (must...find...secrets....) and very well designed levels, coupled with not seemingly pointless objectives (some of which are actually pretty cool)

The weapons look and feel incredibly powerful, even the measly pistol with its charged shot is quite fun to kill with.
The Glory Kill system is awesome, though I may find myself doing a playthrough without (if it's possible)
I have played a few matches of MP and I have to say, it's better than I was expecting, and I feel it would be quite awesome if I was actually good at it. :P

TL:DR
Buy now. Your modernized childhood friend awaits you. If you want classic DooM with a modest twist, insane amounts of gore, and more ways to kill than you can shake a stick at, this should be an immediate purchase for you!\t\t\t\t\t
