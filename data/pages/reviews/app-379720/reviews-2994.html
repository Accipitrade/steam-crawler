Pros/ Why it needs to win the "Whoooaaaaaaa, Dude! Award"
- Glory killing is really good.
- Game runs extremely well.
- Campaign is pretty long.
- Guns feel powerful.
- Music gets your blood pumping.
- Bosses are pretty hard.
- Good atmosphere.
- Level design is amazing
- Graphics are gorgeous.
- Extensive gun customization
- Extensive graphics, audio, and controls settings.

Cons

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBUY THIS GAME.

Many people have written at this point about how awesome this game is. Better than I can. So you should just know that what they've said is so true. The maps are big, full of secrets, and beautiful in their own twisted and nightmarish ways. You're the meanest and toughest sonuvabitch to ever walk the face of Mars, Earth, Hell, or any other planet or plane of existence. The shooting is more elegant than a movement of Mahler, faster than a Dragonforce song, and more brutal than a Cannibal Corpse album cover. The first time I grabbed a berserk powerup and then literally ripped an imp's skull in half with my hands I spat out a sadistic, gleeful laugh that would have scared Lucifer himself. For my money it's the best shooter of the past decade, Wolfenstein was close, but shooting hasn't been this fun since the original F.E.A.R. came out.

Oh yeah, and there's multiplayer. It's fun in a sort of mindless way. The campaign is where it's at though. The true epitome of what video game power fantasy is all about. So yeah, don't even wait for a sale, just get it and skip the season pass.

DOOM is back baby.\t\t\t\t\t
