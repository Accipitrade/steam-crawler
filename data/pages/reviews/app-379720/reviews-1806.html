\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is a high-octane demon killing game that is a ton of fun to play. After a very brief prologue the game throws you into a horde of demons with a shotgun and has you get to killing. As you progress you find more powerful weapons and weapons mods to help you kill demons faster and fight more powerful demons. The fights are by far the best part of the game, the game doesn’t slow you down with long dialogue speeches or slow game sections, you aren’t even slowed down by needing to reload your gun so you can focus on slaying the demons. The gameplay has also been developed to fit perfectly with the game, when you kill enemies with the chainsaw they drop a ton of ammo since you’d resort to melee weapons if your out of ammo and killing enemies with a “glory kill” which is a fast and violent cinematic kill scene that normally last 1 to 2 seconds has them drop lots of health packs since they are a fast way to “instant kill” enemies. There is a story to the game, but it takes a backseat to the gameplay, however they do offer several “database entries” for people that are interested in the lore of the game.
Doom also features an online multiplayer mode which features a variety of game modes like team deathmatch and some special modes where one player turns into a giant demon and the first person to kill it gets to become a demon themselves and try to kill all the other players and while I played it a little bit I couldn’t see myself getting too into it. They do offer lots of challenges that when complete unlock different armor and weapon skins, but it didn’t seem too much different than standard multiplayer games that are offered.
One of the biggest features Doom has are the “snap maps” which are maps that players have made for other to go through similar to Super Mario Maker, now at first it seemed boring because it was mainly big rooms full of bad guys and weapons that could clear the room in a few shots but then I checked the “best of” list and it contains a ton of maps that obviously have had a ton of work put into them and were a lot of fun to play.
Doom is flat out just a fun game to play. It’s very satisfying to kill enemies and the weapons you get feel like they have a lot of punch behind them and you aren’t just mindlessly killing enemies. The fast-paced gameplay keeps the game interesting up until the end and the game is rewarding for the different playstyles and I’d highly recommend playing it.
