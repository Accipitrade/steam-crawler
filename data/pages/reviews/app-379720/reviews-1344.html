\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game on release. It held my attention for about 2 hours, and then I did not touch it again. With the release of the new Doom, I decided to see why it did not hold my attention to the end. I finally made it to the end of the game, but I remember that this was frustrating because I constantly could not figure out where to go. I would recommend on a sale price because many features are top notch, but it definitely lacked the fun factor. Too much attention went into polishing certain features, while I would run into game breaking bugs still happening four years later, and the fact that no matter how I adjusted the sensitivity on the controller, it was still flying at 100 making for many missed shots. I don't understand how anyone plays this twice, but it was still worth the money overall for one run through just to see the show.\t\t\t\t\t
