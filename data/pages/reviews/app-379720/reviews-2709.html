\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun and fast-paced, it's a pleasantly mindless shooter true to the spirit of the originals. It has good controls and graphics. The player character feels fast, nimble, and powerful, right off the bat. Sound could be a bit better. Weapons and explosions lack punch, even on a big sound system, and monster noises become distractingly repetetive at times. I'll criticise the in-game cutscenes as well: they're a boring, witless, unwelcome, and unskippable break in the action. At least they're infrequent. Maybe they're hiding mid-level loading or something.

The game has some features novel to the franchise: a couple of melee combat mechanics that affect ammo and health spawns, an upgrade system for your weapons and suit, and a passive, optional quest system that'll earn you additional upgrade points. These add a bit of depth to the old run-'n-gun formula, but they don't get in the way of a good time. You can probably make it through without even using them, if you want.

I recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
