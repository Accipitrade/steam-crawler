\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI completed the Campaign!

As my first DOOM game, I enjoyed this a LOT. I didn't pay too much attention to the story as I had no idea what people were rly talking about, but as for gameplay, it's by far the best FPS I've ever played. Especially with how amazing the movement feels. Platforming, however, was a pain in the ass. This game left on a cliffhanger, too, and it's gonna bother me a lot. I'll be getting DOOM Eternal, for sure

As for completing the campaign, I thought the challenges were kinda boring. They were far too easy for a game I've kept being told was very difficult. The weapon mastery challenges felt more annoying than anything, but I'm glad they were easy enough to get them over and done with. The mission challenges were pretty basic, too. I was able to do them fairly easily. The collectibles were frustrating to find. There were some that I would've NEVER been able to find without any help. I'd say they were very well hidden. Same with the levers.
There were also only like 3 bosses. And I mastered them very easily

Other than bugs and glitching thru the maps and jumping off the edge of errything bcuz platforming kinda sucks, I breezed thru the game with ease
