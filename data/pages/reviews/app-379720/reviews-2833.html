Doom is good.

If you didn't grow up playing Doom, you have to play this. If you grew up playing DOOM as a kid, you definitely have to get this. Forget everything you knew about first person shooters today, forget the regenerating health, the taking cover, the one type of humanoid being your enemy, and having two weapons at a time. Forget all that ♥♥♥♥.

There is no sprint button, there is a walk button and you will never use it. The soundtrack is phenomenal and dynamic, ramping up when the big demons come out. There is a Glory kill mechanic, where when you wound a demon and they start glowing, you do an extremely brutal murderous takedown, and it's just quick enough so it never gets old.

Not only do you carry every weapon you earn in your pockets, these weapons now have special add-ons you can find that make it much more powerful in it's own unique way.

The Singleplayer is what shines in this game, even moreso with the addition of Arcade Mode. You can now go through the campaign with all add-ons and upgrades (except the first stages) with only one objective, to beat the stage as quickly and efficiently as possible. As simple as it sounds, it turns the game into a total non-stop adrenaline fueled murder-fest, figuring out how to keep that multiplier going, and barely ever stopping.

I didn't even play the Multiplayer, I don't care, this game is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awesome.
