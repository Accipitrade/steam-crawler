This iteration of Doom stays true to teh original in teh sense of running and gunning without cowering behind cover, but simultaneously bringing the game forward to current standards.

PROs
- Fast paced
- Interesting level design
- its really fun being doom guy and hearing the lore about yourself (feels like you rarely get lore about your own character in games)
- Varied difficulties making it playable for any quality of player

CONs
- Massive download (as in 50Gb+ download, if you have a poor internet connection, I would seriously consider not purchasing this game)
- Multiplayer falls a bit flat
- Didn't find it very replayable

Overall, this game is pretty good, and if its on sale I would definatly recommend purchasing.\t\t\t\t\t
