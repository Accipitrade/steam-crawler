\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is one of the few franchises that I can consistently rely on to give a great FPS experience.
(Also, quick note I have Platinumed this game on Ps4 and played it through on the Switch several times)

The gameplay in the Doom series is fairly simple. When compared to many FPS games released in recent years it seems like it doesnt have much under the hood in terms of its mechanics. However, dive into it and you will be surprised by alot. Each weapon has a primary and secondary ability. Some of these abilities may make a more precise shot, or shoot more rounds faster, or even firing micro missiles to dismember a horde. Plus, chaining different weapons to quickly dispatch the spawns of hell feels great, and the weapon wheel isn't stuffed with fluff, everything can be useful. Not to mention the Glory Kills. They just feel brutal.

Story is actually deeper in this game then previous entries. They didnt even have to make the Doom Slayer say anything to give you any details or to explain whats going on. Tons of logs/entries on various enemies, locations, and characters to give the world some depth.

Visually this game is beautiful. It is crazy to me that they can make Mars or Hell look so damn good. Textures look fantastic, and the effects just help sell the vibes.

Doom is something I would rate a 4 out of 5. The only thing I think this game could benefit from is a slightly better multiplayer, but thats also not why you should buy Doom.\t\t\t\t\t
