With that, I have to say this game is actually worthy of the Doom name except the last boss battle where the designers seem to have completely missed the point. Apart from that, it's almost exactly what a doom game should be like. Still, with the horrendously linear tunnel-like level design that characterises modern shooters this is a breath of fresh air and a bit of a gem.

Oh and there's very little time-wasting cutscenes so you can actually focus on the action rather than watching some boring movie.\t\t\t\t\t
