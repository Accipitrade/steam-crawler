\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you like tactics and well thought strategies. Leave Now!
If you have a history of epilepsy or seizures. Leave Now!
If you like a captivating story and interesting characters. Leave Now!
If you love explosive and gory combat and slaughtering enemies by the hundreds. Buy The Game!!!

This game has no decent story at all. But its DOOM, It all about the combat. And it never disapoints.
You start the game waking up chained to an open casket. You immediatly break free from your bonds and already, You have begun your demon slaughtering rampage. There is little else of me to speak of other than my battle expieriences. Which often consisted of me charging into groups of demons, killing them with bullets as well as my fists and their own limbs. As you progress, you are slowly granted an extended arsenal which is made even more versatile using various mods. And introduced to more and more deadly opponents. By the time I made it half way through the game. I had acquired each weapon except my Big ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Gun. (BFG) At that point, I found myself charging into combat and explosivly killing and eventually having my legs ripped off by a Baron. That is playing Ultra-Violence difficulty. I played trough the campaign in about 10 hours, and thats racking up death after death and spending no time looking for secrets. If you are better than me and don't care for secrets. Than you could probably play through in closer to 8 hours. But thats still a healthy length.

If You have played Unreal Tournament, then you are prepared to enter DOOM's Multyplayer. Each player is portrayed as a fully customizable marine. Customization includes various UAC, Bounty Hunter, and even Demonic armor sets. Each piece can be placed seperately, allowing you to have half demonic, half human characters if you please. My only complaint is the progression. Which seems more of an effort to be like modern shooters.Requiring you to lvl up to unlock custom loadout slots and certain weapons. But luckily, Progression is very quick and didn't take away from my full enjoyment of this game. You have access to every weapon from the campaign and more in multyplayer except the gauzz cannon and pistol. And you can evem find the BFG and chainsaw as powerups in game. Allowing you to drum up some quick kills. But the true gem of multyplayer is the Demon Rune. This powerup allows you to transform into either a revanant, Baron of hell, Mancubus, or Prowler that can grip walls. When the rune spawns, the first player to touch it is transformed into the demon they chose. Demon choice is selected when you pick loadouts. These demons often have large amounts of health and deadly attacks. Turning the player into a almost indestuctible juggernaut. That is unless you pick the prowler.

DOOM=10/10

Do not expect a thrilling competitive game like for example quake 3 and unreal tournament.
