\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI remember way back when when the first episode of DOOM was released on the internet as shareware, and it was a life changing experience. Before that the best FPS was wolfenstein, and boy oh boy was this an upgrade. I anxiously awaited the following two episodes in the coming months, and eagerly twiddled my thumbs as they downloaded on my trusty 56k modem (which was pretty sweet for back then)

Since then very few games have captured the constant action and frenetic pace of that game. Sure, graphics got better, gameplay for the genre in general advanced, but only really QUAKE managed to capture the feel, but even that was different(although equally awesome)

This game captures the feel of the original perfectly, while still managing to tickle my eyeballs with amazing graphics. This is by far the most fun Ive had with a first person shooter in over a decade.

For those that didnt play the original, this game is FAST. MOVE MOVE MOVE all the time, the AI is smart and moves too, stand still and you are dead. Once you hear that music start, it's on. In between the intense gibfests you are given a chance to explore, find secrets, do minigames to get upgrades, nd generally apreciate the very well crafted levels.

11/10, would cleanse hell again.\t\t\t\t\t
