The Good:
- The Guns, not much to explain here, just typical DOOM style guns.
- Them Graphics, even on low, this game is amazing looking.
- The Character Design, Oh God, the character design. The Demons are terrifying, more so than the originals.
- The Secrets, Secrets are now either upgrade tokens, weapons, classic style maps, or mini-figures that give access to models.

Neutrals:
- The Story, remember, this is DOOM, there isn't much of a story, but; what we do have is pretty good.

The Bad:
- Higher Tier Graphics Slow The Game to a Halt, this means you may get FPS rates between 0-25 without changing the game's advance settings.
- When Someone Messages You The Game begins To Freeze Constantly, this is pretty much unfixable, just be patient and ask your friends to not message you while you're playing.

Final Rating: 9/10, has some things that can be worked out, but; overall, great game.
