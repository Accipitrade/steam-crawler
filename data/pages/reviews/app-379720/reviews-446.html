\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recommend this game to anyone who likes the games from that era before every FPS was perverted by the ideals of modern games like Battlefield and Call of Duty. The shooty parts have a great feel to them and weapons feel impactful and strong. The story isn't really to be praised and it mostly acts as a backdrop to all the action. My single nitpick would be that the last fight felt really anticlimactic considering other boss fights were in my experience harder and longer. It also felt like the last boss had no true purpose other than to give the game a somewhat impressive ending. How was that monster summoned when it clearly had robotic parts? All in all a great game with a killer metal soundtrack.\t\t\t\t\t
