Then they go ahead and make one of the most ridiculously fun shooters ever. A shooter that goes back in time to get back to basics. No regenerating health, no cover shooting, an entire arsenal of weapons (because you're an adult who knows how to handle more than 2 guns), exactly one gun in the entire game that reloads, you run as fast as a car drives, and you have a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ chainsaw.

Despite having amazing gameplay, the story is also...good? Yeah inbetween running around and making demons explode, there's an actual decent story to be found.

This game shouldn't have been so good, but I'm glad it turned out amazing.\t\t\t\t\t
