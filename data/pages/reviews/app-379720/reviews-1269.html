\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was my first Doom game, and it was a lot of fun. To illustrate how much I knew about Doom: I thought I would be fighting zombies or something. No, turns out, it's demons.

The good:
- lots of different weapons
- great music and graphics
- fast-paced but with small breaks in between battles
- good level design, with lots of secrets
- generous with the ammo and power-ups (I only came close to running out on the final boss)
- up close and personal with the short-range super shotgun and glory kills, both of which are very satisfying to use

The bad: nothing really. I mean, there is lots of stuff to read with the lore, information on demons and UAC, etc. scattered all over. But it's optional. It's there for people who want it. It can be ignored.

I'm not interested in the multiplayer so I didn't try it, but I hear it's pretty dead, so keep that in mind if you are buying it for that reason.

Overall, this is a fun game that knows what it is and delivers what it promises: a loud, fast, violent and entertaining FPS.\t\t\t\t\t
