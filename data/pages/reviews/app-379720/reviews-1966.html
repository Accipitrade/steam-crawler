\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally cool game.

The story is not the best but together with the awesome atmosphere and all environments you feel as though you are in an epic place with awesome weapons in the middle of plenty big monsters that are all after you.
The music in every situation really elevated the combat for myself, its just plain epic.
The combat system is also quite cool because you get rewarded when you get close to your enemies and standing still is a really got death sentence which is why you are "forced" to move all the time.
There is also replay value to find further 3D models or upgrades for your armor or weapons.

Negatives:
I died like 6 times through falling down something without any monsters in my sight which was pretty annoying (you could also say that this was more my fault than the developers but it was pretty annoying :).
I didn't know how to knee down which is why I was stuck for 30 mins in the second level or so.
There was two times a glitch where I flew through walls during combat but landed both times in the same map in another place which was annoying but no problem because I could just walk back to the fight.
There was like two times a glitch where I just died for no reason at all.
One time the game just crashed when I replayed a mission to get the last big "argent" upgrade and I needed to replay the whole level (5min to get back to the end of the level doing just the things to get through without exploring or else).

Linux:
I played this game completely on Linux (Manjaro 18.1.1) on Vulkan with an GTX980ti and it was really smooth everything maxed out (1080p @60fps).

Conclusion:
Although there were some negatives the game itself was running most of the time as smooth as it can get on my system, I really felt like I was in the environment and in the fights (using the medium difficulty was hard enough for me to die very often, especially in boss fights though I would not recommend playing on easy) and thus can only recommend this game, especially for Linux users (using the official Steam Client with Steam Proton selected by the Steam Client).\t\t\t\t\t
