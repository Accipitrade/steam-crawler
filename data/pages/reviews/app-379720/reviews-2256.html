\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros: Killing demons is a ton of fun! The visuals and sound design are excellent, and the game play is very rewarding. A bunch of replay value with collection and secret finding. Id Software did a terrific job making you feel powerful as the doom slayer.

Cons: Weapon upgrades are not as fleshed out as can be. Some enemies like the Pinky (Ram) and Shield demons can be really annoying to deal with. There would also be sometimes where I miss (an obvious) skull or lever required to move on, that's partly my fault for not snooping harder, but I would have hope that the devs make Doom Eternal's puzzle solving a bit more easier to keep focus on the gun-play action. On a similar note, I did find myself dying to platforming sections a little bit more than I'm embarrassed to admit. Thankfully there aren't too much of those sections; and I get it, it's to break up the game play and to not keep the action too stale, but I get my fix for platforming from Mario Bros.

Notes: I played on the normal difficulty for about 75% of the way through, then around the second boss encounter I found myself struggling. Honestly, it wasn't something that I couldn't handle given the time and patience, but given that I there aren't enough hours in the day for me to play, and that I'm trying to clear my massive backlog of games from Steam and HumbleBundle sales, I had to throw out my pride and play on the easy difficulty just so I can say that I completed the game. (I even went as far as to tell myself that the game wasn't fun after dying so many times around the end, but I still kept pushing through). Overall, I don't think I'll be revisiting this game, but I did have some fun experience while it lasted!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell let me tell you I've been on this game for now 5.5 hours i'm not nearly done but i had to get on here and highly recommend this masterpiece. This is a game that's makes you feel like a bad ass. i'm on plenty of hurt difficulty with maxed settings and everything is perfect. The graphics game play mechanics the level design is superb. The enemy characters are fresh with a few new ones, I love seeing the classics all revamped. The story is cool and engaging. The combat is so fluid and i love that the weapons have upgrades that can be applied seamlessly and abilities in form of flying orbs are a nice touch that you simply walk through to engage. Also you'll find little collectible Doom Guy dolls around the levels. The arenas offer a vast array of ways to engage the enemy swarm which is really fun. Nothing felt bland so far again I've only been on for 5 hours and still have a while to go. So ill complete this review once i finish the game. But i can tell you i'm sorry i waited this long to take the plunge. It was so worth it and i'm definitely getting Doom Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
