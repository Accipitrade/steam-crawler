\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI spent 1000's of hours playing the original Doom when it came out back in 1993 and since then have played my fair of first person shooters. I don't care for CoD or the military style games but lean towards Quake, UT, Painkiller, style games. When I heard they were remaking doom I was not happy, don't ♥♥♥♥ with something so sacred because it's probably going to suck then I am going to be salty about it. What happened was iD software made one helluva game that is so much damn fun that it really gave me a warm fuzzy feeling and that sensation that I got when I first played Doom over 20 years ago. I won't go too much into details because you can find reviews all over youtube that will articulate better than I ever could as to why you should buy this game. The Single player game is as old school as it gets, strap on some weapons and blow some ♥♥♥♥ up. ♥♥♥♥ the story, ♥♥♥♥ the regenerating health, and ♥♥♥♥ standing still. Move, murder, and survive is the name of the game. I enjoy the multiplayer but it has it's issues. It was developed by another company and it shows. no Deathmatch, no anti cheat, no server browser, no bots to practice on, no voting, is it fun? yes, but they sure screwed the pooch letting another company make it. That being said I still play it everyday and find it enjoyable, it just could have been so much more.

Buy this for the single player experience and see if you like the MP, if you do it's just and added bonus.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played all the classic DooM games many years ago and enjoyed them then, this one does not disappoint. plenty of guns and explosions and of course (and much to my joy) the B.F.G is here to play.

The gameplay is nice and smooth and easy to get to grips with quickly, thankfully the designers didnt do what alot of games have been doing of late and flood the game with complex and confusing key requirements to do the most simple of things. here its run, jump, aim and shoot & shoot & shoot & shoot......well you get the idea.

the campaign is long enough to keep you interested but not so long as it loses the plot along the way, the story is one that will keep you interested and actually makes sense.

I liked that your standard pistol had unlimted ammo and was useful throughout the game, I wont bore you with my personal choices of weapons as everyone is different but needless to say there is something here for everyone.

the maps are huge, all multi levelled and take some exploring if you want to find all the secrets and upgrades dotted around. getting lost tends to happen but you can normally find your way back to where you started and start looking for that missing item again and figuring out where you took the wrong turn.

not sure i would pay the full asking price but i picked this game up for £19.99 (half price) and have been left with some fond memories and an enjoyable experiance. recommended for die hard Doom fans and newbies alike, you dont need to have any idea what happened in any of the other games to jump right into this one and enjoy whats going on.\t\t\t\t\t
