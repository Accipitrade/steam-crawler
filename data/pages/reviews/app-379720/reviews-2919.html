\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCampaign :

12 Weapons, 13 Levels, 3 Boss Fights, And 1 DOOM Guy.

Each Level Will last a New Player 30 min - 1 hour, Average Campaign Length is about 8-14 Hours, Even Speed Runners say the game takes at least an hour and thats using shortcuts and exploits.

Pros : Long Well Done Campaign, Lots of Secrets and Hidden Areas to Find, Satisfying Ending.

Cons : A Few Bugs that aren't a big deal but sometimes they catch you off guard. You can sometimes clip through a door/wall, sometimes Demons get stuck outside of the map.


Multiplayer : It has a huge learning curve from games nowadays.

Pros : The Community isn't toxic due to its passive level gain/rank system, There are great team based modes and they are great to play with friends such as : Freeze Tag(A Mode where when you kill an enemy they are frozen and thier teamate has to stand by them to unfreeze them.)
Clan Arena(It's like Search and Destroy from COD, Each player starts with full health/armor and you get one life each round, there is no objective but there can't be survivors on the enemy team.)Infernal Run(in this game mode you need to get a ball to the enemy spawn to score a point, it's like uplink from AW, I recommend using lateral thrusters(3rd DOOM Dlc) they can help you go around the map, if you cant get that then use a teleporter.)

Cons : The Servers are kind of messed up and everyone sometimes has high ping.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed it only for the campaign, so I've got no information about the multiplayer.
Took me about 20hrs to beat it and find almost all collectables. Only some challenges are not completed now.
I have to say I quite like the game.Nice oldschooly FPS feeling. No stupid cover system. No auto-regenerating life.
Just shoot hordes of giant (and some smaller) possessed mutants from hell. The story is okayish, but I still enjoyed it until the end. Upgrading your weapons/suit and searching for the secrets was a strong selling point and kept my motivation high throughout the game.
The graphics are really good, even on my somewhat older computer it ran on medium high at a constant 60fps and looked great.\t\t\t\t\t
