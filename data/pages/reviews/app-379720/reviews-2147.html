\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPicked this one up on sale for <$20CAD. Easily worth this much for the SP campaign. I do not have any intentions to play the MP so I am reviewing this solely on the basis of the SP campaign as well as taking into considering the cost being quite low (at the time I purchased it).

I initially held off on this game as while I did not hate the Wolfenstein reboot. I was still somewhat underwhelmed by the game play. And Bethesda is really seeming to put in a lot of effort to piss off it's fan base of the last 30 years moving forward. Dooms game play and the fluidity blow any similar fears away with ease (ID as always does very well with the gun play). The upgrade systems are enjoyable, it's gory and violent, and all rolled together extremely satisfying to play.

I saw it described elsewhere in this manner, In Dark Souls you are trapped in a room with demons. In Doom demons are trapped in a room with you. This in a nutshell, describes what to expect. And if you do play this title, DO NOT miss out on the glory kills! Melee is not in anyway lacking compared to the guns for this title.\t\t\t\t\t
