\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who is awful at FPS's, finds the game disturbing at times, and is lucky to beat the first two levels on Normal, Doom surprised me. The combat is a ton of fun (even if I can't hit things right in front of me with the shotgun), and makes you feel really powerful even if you can't actually aim at all. But what really makes this game special to me are the secrets. It is so much fun to hunt them down; they feel deliberate and clever, rather than frustrating (a problem that many game's secrets struggle with). Definitely going to enjoy the rest of the game and, if I can strengthen my stomach for it, Eternal as well. Even if you aren't a big fan of FPS's (I certainly am not), you can still have a ton of fun with this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game. Definitely recommend it to anyone who likes FPS, action, blood & guts, and some classic gaming elements (power-ups, portals, hidden areas, platforming, jump pads) with a modern twist.

Pros
- There was so much attention to detail from the devs. I love looking at how gross the human entrails look strewn throughout the UAC station, as well as the textures and features on the bodies of the demons. If you get this game, look at the goofy names of the snacks left around the UAC station and have a chuckle.
- The sound is immersive really well thought-out across environments.
- The fast-paced movement and combat are fun and keep fights from being boring.
- The load times are super quick (my pc isn't that nice and it never takes long to get back into the fight after I get rekt or move to a new area).
- There are elements of this game that really stay true to the Doom experience, especially the hidden areas and power-ups. If you've played the original Doom, you might get some fun flashbacks.
- The weapons are sweet and include some classics that I won't spoil (even though this has been out for a bit).

Cons
- The glory kills get kind of stale after a bit. I wish there were more involved environmental kills.
- You can't really step it up after literally going to Hell. I wish that bit would've been delayed instead of plunging you in somewhat early on.
- More boss fights are always good. There are fewer than I hoped.

That's about all. Give this a try! I bought Eternal along with this one on Black Friday. Can't wait to dive into that next.\t\t\t\t\t
