\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm going to try to do this review without ruining the single player, which is the only thing I'll be reviewing for now.

First, a note on performance, both OpenGL & Vulkan in this game are buttery smooth, I don't know if it's my setup or settings I need to fiddle with, but SLi did not work in Vulkan, but worked in OpenGL, getting about 40-60% GPU load across 2 Titan Xp's @ 3440x1440 with 100Hz Adaptive Sync on, flatlined at 97-100FPS (seems to be a bug where the game will act like it's capped at 97 FPS at times... also, any cut scenes run at 60FPS, which is not a big deal, but worthy of note). Keep in mind, my system is not "new", 3930K + 16GB of 2133MHz RAM isn't exactly state of the art, but this game has zero issues at the stated resolution & 100FPS cap.

The game's graphics are amazing, and the gameplay itself is what I call "SUPER INTENSE" for a good lot of the game. The glory-kill mechanics are a bit silly, but awesome at the same time. Unlike Doom 3, there aren't a lot of "jump scares" and this doesn't really double as a horror-genre game. It's a shooter and not much else, which it does pretty darn well. Overall, I would give this game, just based on the single player, a 9/10. I got a good 8-9 hours of gameplay out of it, which for a game that is multi-player focused, is a pretty decent amount of hours in the single player. If you can pick this game up for $15 or less, I highly recommend it!

Some of the boss fights require specific tactics to avoid taking forever (or dying repeatedly), but you can probably figure most of them out without looking up guides or anything. This game is pretty much 50% movement, 50% shooting/weapon choice, if you're not moving a lot, you're probably gonna die a lot.\t\t\t\t\t

The story for what it was was great.
The graphics are fantastic, some of the very best out there to be honest.
The glory Kills, never got old. The gore was the best part besides the smooth movement.
Everything flows perfectly together.
I started with DOOM 64, never played DOOM 2 till DOOM 3 was released on the original XBOX.
DOOM 3 is still a fantastic game till this day. the graphics are still top notch, and its actually still a great survival horror game . Its not as nearly as action packed as this game unless you play it on the hardest difficulty then enemies spawn everywhere every few seconds. GG
But all in all i bought DOOM for just the single player, it did not let me down for a second. money well spent. Cant wait for DOOM II.
