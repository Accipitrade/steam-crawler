\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst things first.

This is not DOOM, it is a homage to DOOM and a good one.

The weapondesign is marvellous, there was not a single ugly or useless one.

The mapdesign is stupendous, tight corridors, open places, indoor and outdoor fun for big and small.

The enemydesign... well the enemydesing sucks. What have they done to the Pinkies? They were the most beautiful ugly things in DOOM and now they are shrivelled red lemons with stubby legs and arms. The Barons of Hell were cool though.

Horrible bossfights, they felt like they belong into another game.

The worst thing for me though was, that I (almost) never forgot that I'm playing a game (with the exception of the last two levels).

But it was fun and I would recommend this game.
