This is what shooters once were and were kind of meant to be. Like, I love my artsy, introspective, thinkin' games as much the next ludophile, but Doom is a kind of fun that we just don't get anymore in video games. No complex story (that the protagonist seems to care about), no dramatic cutscenes, just killing the literal forces of Hell as they invade Mars because... really? You need a reason why? How about because, you know, ♥♥♥♥ 'em. That's why!

Doom is something special; a remastery of the genre it basically started without any of the pretentiousness needed to use a term like "remastery of the genre". This is some serious fun that never takes itself seriously.\t\t\t\t\t

With the advent of Steam play Doom became more of an icon of what's to come. The game runs flawlessly on wine/proton with minimal configuration, as it uses Vulkan directly.
