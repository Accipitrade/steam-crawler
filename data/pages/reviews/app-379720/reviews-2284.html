It's a little faster when you're on the "I'm Too Young To Die" difficulty level.

However, other than the final boss, my personal gripes involved the main menu, even without Vsync on, has the worst latency I've ever seen in a video game on both APIs, and it looks like id isn't gonna care about making fixes like that. It is just generally sluggish, and the menu isn't entirely standard or simple to use: you have to go through a saved game just to adjust the game's and graphics settings.

If I could give this a "mixed" rating, this would be the case. Otherwise, for some demon-killing fun, hell yes, this is the game to get on sale. (By the time I got this, it was roughly $60. Glad to see the price dropped overtime, but on a sale it's worth getting.)\t\t\t\t\t
