This game is overall very enjoyable. It relies heavily on the players having adrenaline rushes, that's what makes it enjoyable, you feel as though enemies are always coming from every direction and stopping for just one second can end it all. I don't think it's very story focused, there's a linear story don't get me wrong, but it's just your basic arcade game demon-dystopia story. Going back again to the gameplay, the game has very well designed levels that really helps enabling the sense of im-going-to-kill-them-all tunnel visioned focus, be it through the amazing soundtracks or various weapons at hand. Everything just goes well together in this game. One thing I don't really like about this game is probably the outside-mars terrain, somehow it's just too damn bright.\t\t\t\t\t
