\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEliminate all doubts my friends, because this game is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awesome.

So after waiting all night I FINALLY got to play it. It's 1 past midnight so I was only able to play the first few missions and have about 90 min invested into the SP so far. And yet, I can safely say that this game has completely blown away my expectations.

One of the first things that stunned me was that there was literally zero time spent wasting my time at the start with story elements similar to Doom 3. I was expecting at least a short Doom 3 style intro level, but instead you are just thrown pretty much straight into the action right away. And as for the action itself, it ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ rocks. Guns feel awesome. Killing feels awesome. Glory kills are actually fairly easy to execute even if you turn off the highlights because the enemies animate differently when they are weak and in fact I would recommend you play the game this way. Oh, and the game is hard. The players we saw in the SP footage are probably very good at the game, because even at HMP the game is actually considerably hard. They weren't kidding when they said "move or die". Stand still for too long and enemies will tear you into pieces in seconds. Another thing that I like is that you actually need to be careful with your ammo which means that unlike the first game the pistol is actually useful since it has unlimited ammo. I dunno what I feel about the player having unlimited grenades but at least the recharge time is long so it's hard to abuse it. The level design is also quite nice though still a bit more linear that I expected. I haven't checked out snapmap or the multiplayer yet.

Overall, if you were worrying about the game being bad there is no reason to. Doom is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ back baby!\t\t\t\t\t

lots of gore

personally id recommend- even to new DOOM franchise players. it is the run and gun (and bunny hop and explode) game everyone needs. season pass on the otherhand- only if you REALLY want to play MP.\t\t\t\t\t
