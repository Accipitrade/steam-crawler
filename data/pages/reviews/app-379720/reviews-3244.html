\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Action Is Back!!!

After the different gameplay paradigm that was Doom 3 (not that I disliked it), it's nice to get back to a game that focuses on what made the original Doom games great. I can not overstate how well the action flows in Doom. Whether you're strafing around mowing enemies down with an automatic weapon, blasting them in the face as you dodge fireballs, or picking them off with precision before ripping their heads off with your bare hands... you can count on the rush, the speed, the explosions, and most importantly the heavy metal!!

With health and ammo in plain sight (and the ability to get it from enemies), you are encouraged to keep moving and fighting...very few things detract from the action. One such thing I find is the Secrets. Doom has a LOT of them, and finding them is IMPORTANT (this is how you get upgrades). Many times on my first run through a level, I find myself spending a lot of time experimenting with ledges and pushing map boundries looking for them. This can really slow down the gaming experience.

However, once you unlock what secret rooms hold, they stay unlocked. You can play the mission again (even on a different difficulty) and focus on the killing rather than the exploring. And, if you want to replay an earlier level with all the toys and goodies you've found from later in the game- GO ahead! You've earned it!!

I can honestly say this is what I've been waiting for!\t\t\t\t\t
