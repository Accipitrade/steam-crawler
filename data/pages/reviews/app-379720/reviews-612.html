Gameplay loop is as follows:
-Enter room
-Lockdown
-Kill every single thing
-Exit opens
-Move to next room, repeat

It nearly put me to sleep by the end of the game. The combat itself is not bad and is certainly the main selling point of the game. The parkour aspect was really underwhelming to me. I found out about half way through the game that there's a rune you can get to improve midair mobility, but I hate that this improvement is not just base functionality. I probably died to missing jumps about the same amount as I did to enemies.

Other than that, the soundtrack is pretty good, and it's nice that the game doesn't take itself too seriously. Kicks the crap out of Doom Eternal.

I don't really understand all the 10/10 reviews but maybe I'm a boomer. This one is a 7/10 from me. Also why is it almost 70 GB LMAO\t\t\t\t\t
