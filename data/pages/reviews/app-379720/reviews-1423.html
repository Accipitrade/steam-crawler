You can choose to upgrade all, some, or none, of everything. Some unlocks are by finding secrets, some by performing trick shots, or some just by finding the little droid to give you the alternate fire mode.

It's Doom, so expect a bit of the old ultra-violence, but it was a real treat to play through the story.

Multiplayer isn't the most active thing and new players are at a disadvantage to players with more experience. In addition to knowing the maps and game modes better, they also have access to better runes, weapons, and demon options when playing than the starting player does. There's a few different game modes to keep the spice of multiplayer fragging interesting.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA lot of people say this is one of the best games ever made. It's fine. I was honestly spoiled by the campaign of Titanfall 2, which is short and has a forgettable plot but has some of the best movement and level design in any FPS. DOOM too often falls into a sludge of arena combat, where every enemy is dealt with the exact same way, and can't decide if it wants to take its story seriously or not. It is a lot of fun, to be sure. Just don't expect the greatest game ever.\t\t\t\t\t
