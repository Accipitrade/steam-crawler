\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM looks gorgeous and runs super smooth, I'll give it that. They even expanded on the hell maps which was one of my main gripes with DOOM 3. But underneath the flashy surface it's dull, predictable and boring. Story? There's nothing new here it's the same old same old. It's like one of those slow Saturday afternoons where there's nothing to do outside and the only thing on TV is another re-run of Gilligan's Island. After playing the original DOOM games, then playing them again with the open GL Doomsday mod, then playing them again on various consoles, then playing DOOM 3 I have to say I'm burned out on DOOM. I got this on sale for like 99.666% off. If you buy this take my advice and do the same thing. Get it on a ridiculous sale that's like 1976 roll back pricing and go into it with seriously low expectations. Maybe you'll be like hey this isn't so bad sitting at the DMV for 7 1/2 hours after all. I kinda like hanging out all day at the DMV it's rad.

This time around the tension and atmosphere is completely gone. It's more like a goof off version of DOOM especially with the glory kill stupidity. Weapons all feel weak and ye olde demons are bullet sponges. The Chainsaw has been effectively neutered by requiring cans of gasoline that last mere seconds. Super dumb. Background music is lame- shut that off within the first few minutes of game play. Yes it's true, Not Even DOOM Music (NEDM) can save this turd of a game. The space janitors are back in full force working overtime cleaning up all my kills which annoys me. Running around in each map really feels like an upright arcade game- but wait there's even an arcade mode! It's like double-arcade time. Neato. I think I'm pretty much done with all these id/Bethesda retreads. They are chimp feces. I am going to finish this chimp feces but as soon as I do it's uninstall time. And forget it I'm not going to bother with DOOM Eternal or whatever they're calling it. Just looks like more of the same chimp feces.\t\t\t\t\t

If you like metal things, and hell, and brutal this is the most fun game I've played in a long ass time. You literally look at awesome satanic and blow demons apart who react in a satisfying realistic way. Running and gunning feels like tf2, ammo system is very forgiving, and its the only game I've ever consistently used ALL the weapons all the time. Usually you pick your best gun and go with it till you get a better one, the most satisfying aspect of this game is that it constantly encourages you to use your whole arsenal.

I'm old enough to have played the old dooms, and they stayed faithful to the old designs, making appropriately halloween brutal scary renditions in modern graphics.

I got burned by a bunch of over promised games so I bought this out of just wanting to have fun and salavge a weekend. I need a fun 8/10 game and got a 12/10.\t\t\t\t\t
