\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI completely recommend this game to any fans of the original DOOM franchise. This really hit home for me in that regard. The music alone was incredible and the stunning graphics were only a fraction of the beauty of this game. Game mechanics are flawless and the gameplay itself is enjoyable and extremely fun. While the campain is not the longest in the world for the experienced gamer it is action packed and suprisingly story driven (more or less, being DOOM and all its not like he cares about "story lines" and "logic". Because jumping 20 feet into the air, ripping the eye out of a flying demon, jumping OFF of it and another 20 feet into the air, pulling a rocket launcher out of god knows where in the process and taking out a horde of demons on a cliff side before you even hit the ground is TOTALY something you could do in real life)

oh but so fun...so so fun

The weapons are exjoyable as well and finding them is always fun. The first play through of this game I always got so excited when I found a new weapon, especially when it was one I was unfamilair with and had no idea what it did xD

Weapon mods are amazing as well, as a side note, really enjoyed that whole system.

The weapon sound effects are astonishing and EXTREMELY satasfying

we gotta love that super shotty eh? so good....

While I wasn't PARTICUALY a fan of the leveling system for the armor/character it was quite a small thing compared to the beauty of the rest of the game and content.

As arcade like as the original DOOM games were this one suprisingly still keeps that feel, at least a little bit.
Its fun, fast paced, incredibly adicting, and can be very random at times. Some things in the game just make me flat out laugh like "did that seriously just happen" but then you do your first glory kill and it goes something like...

"oh..OH....OH GOD HIS FA- OH JUMPING JESUS ON A POGOSTICK WHAT DID I JUST DO TO THAT POOR BASTARD!?!?!?"

That was about what happened for me xDDDDD

SO in conclusion ( I realize I didnt go into to much detail but your just going to have to play the game for yourself) This is game is incredible and will be one of my all time favorites for a LONG time to come for sure.

10/10 would chainsaw again\t\t\t\t\t
