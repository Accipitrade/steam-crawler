Genre: FPS

I have never played a game from DOOM franchise before, so this is the review from noob perspective.

This game is pretty easy to explain. You are on Mars, and you have one job, to exterminate every monster you see. There are some lore tablets to read, if you want to find out more, and those you can find in each level, but overall story is pretty simple, and almost non-existent.

Gameplay is fantastic. It's 10 of 10. It's really fun and really fast. I played the game on the hardest, so I needed to move constantly, cause if you stop for 2 seconds, you will probably die. At first it's really hard, but after 2 hours or so, you get used to it. You can execute finishers on every monster, and that's the very important game mechanic here cause while doing them you are invincible and also you get health that way. There is a big variety of weapons, that you acquire through game, pistol, shotgun, rifle, chainsaw, etc. You rarely don't have enough ammo, cause monsters drop ammo, and also you can find it everywhere. All you gotta do is be fast and precise. Smaller monsters tend to be more annoying than bigger ones, cause bigger the monster bigger the target. Pistol is the less effective weapon and I rarely used it, only in the beginning. Shotgun is the weapon that I used most, and it's really effective. Aside from shotgun, heavy assault rifle is amazing and really precise. Chainsaw can cut any monster in half with only one click, but you need fuel for it. While progressing through campaign you will acquire some tokens that you can use to upgrade weapons, dexterity, environment damage and other standard stuff. You can also find some collectibles in each level, mini toys from DOOM guy in different colors. Overall gameplay is fantastic.

Soundtrack is amazing, it's pure metal. Every time you start fighting monsters blood is rushing through you veins...or so. Yeah, it's fun, but that's all you do in this game, you kill enemies move a bit and then kill again and repeat. It's insane how repetitive this game is, I know that it's supposed to be arcade shooter, but damn, I didn't know that it's this repetitive. Sure gameplay is fantastic, but what's the good of it if the game is too repetitive. You may say every game is repetitive, well I completelly disagree. This game offers nothing except from fantastic gameplay and amazing soundtrack, which is great, but every area looks pretty much the same. Yeah you are on Mars so it must look same I guess, but then why did they choose this planet? I am not sure. Aside from everything looking same, you are fighting monsters constantly, and that's fun at first, but it gets boring after some time. Every room is monster arena, in 2 of 3 rooms you are fighting monsters. Story is boring, so fighting monsters without some higher goal gets boring after some time. Graphics wise looks pretty good and it's optimized damn good, worked great even on my old setup. There is multiplayer and I guess it's good, but I didn't try it, so I am not sure if many people are playing it.

Pros:
- Fantastic gameplay
- Amazing soundtrack
- Amazing optimization

Cons:
- Story
- Too repetitive

Verdict:
DOOM offers fantastic gameplay and the atmosphere, but unfortunatelly it's too repetitive, Mad Max repetitive, if not more. It's a good game though, I can't say it's bad, but it lacks depth.

Personal rating: 7/10
