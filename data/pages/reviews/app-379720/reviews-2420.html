--When you get any enemy to 40%~ of its health, it gets stunned and tells you to Press F to watch your character do a finishing move

-- Stunned enemies have so much health, that to do anything but use a finishing move is a huge waste, and stunned enemies need to be killed within a certain period of time, otherwise they start attacking you again

-- Collectibles are how you upgrade your weapons, forcing you to search for easter eggs after you clear each room, if you choose to ignore collecticles your guns will end up being even more weak, which in turn will result in you running out of ammo even more

--The music to me sounded of vague dubstep sounding ambience, which wasn't enjoyable to listen to. When i put on a Metal playlist I enjoyed the game much more than i had earlier.

So to conclude all these problems bog down the combat, and suck the enjoyment from it, finishing moves are fine, but they should be less common, the music doesnt suit the game at all for me, and collectible hunting shouldnt be a core part of the gameplay, all these things serve to pull the gameplay to a grinding halt.\t\t\t\t\t
