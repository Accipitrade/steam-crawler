Would have loved to have the ability to shoot off npc's limbs, but have them continue fighting, like in the E3 presentation. Also, if this isn't already part of the game, perhaps having a demon's legs still stand and stumble around for a few moments after it's torso is blown off by the double shotgun.

I really enjoy this game I hope they continue with it in the future. I was surprised how quickly I managed to get used to not reloading.

if you liked their new Wolfenstein you will like DOOM even more.

Positives:
+Great port. It better be it's ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Doom.
+great level design
+weapons and gun play feel good
+simplistic but sensible equiptment recharge mechanic
+Amazing art design
+some of the best variance in color in an fps
+the lore is actually interesting to me
+interesting gun alt-fires and alt fire upgrades
+good weapon selection and multiple ways to switch weapons
+the chainsaw has an ammo granting mechanic on kills
+powerups
+pickups everywhere. I love armor chunks, weapons/ammo, and secrets.
+rip and tear
+they managed to bring back the Doom guy suit without ruining it.

Negatives:
-booting up can be pretty slow and awkward switching between modes
-I don't think too many people play the pvp (which is a shame because it's pretty damn fun)
-The pvp server decides the game mode and I don't like a few of the modes.
-Horse armor esque DLC
-The Online servers are veryyyy bad. 1/2 games are laggy messes\t\t\t\t\t
