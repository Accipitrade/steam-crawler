\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has ridiculously fluid combat, but that's really the only thing it does right. The level design is absolute garbage for what they tried to do with it. The arenas are just right for combat, but they ruin it by trying to cram in collectibles and hidden areas. It is very difficult to really enjoy the combat due to the collectible system. There are numerous hidden areas in each level, but the player has to be very very careful if he wants to get them all in one playthrough. This is because the levels contain a lot of one-way doors that prevent the player from revisiting earlier areas. Thanks to the absolutely moronic addition of one way doors, combat comes across as irritating rather than fun because the player might accidentally walk through a one-way door and force himself to replay 70% of the level to pick up one little weapon upgrade. As a result, the game has abysmal pacing. When you wish you were smashing demon skulls, you're actually wandering around for an hour looking for a tiny lever that will open a secret door. When you wish you were enjoying the amazing graphics, you're instead constantly pausing the game to stare at the poorly implemented map to see if you've missed any collectibles. On top of all that nonsense, the guns get stale fast. I was 6 hours in when I realized I was bored of the guns. All you get is a machine gun, a shotgun, a sniper, and a rocket launcher. It's a very bland setup for a game all about hectic FPS action. (There are technically more guns than that, but having 3 different machine guns that all do the same thing is a little redundant in my mind.l)

Honestly, this game feels like a crappy remake of Shadow Warrior from 2013. It's funny because Shadow Warrior was a doom clone back in the day.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShort Review:
10/10
In terms of gameplay and how the game runs I've NEVER played a better game than doom. I mean it. I've played many great games but this game trumps everything I've ever tried. Do yourself a favour and buy this game. It runs incredible, it looks incredible and it plays more than incredible. Doom is what every fps game should be at their heart. I'm defo buying doom eternal at full price. This game is definitely one of THE games of this generation or any!

Long Review:
Now for the longer review, this game is an fps by heart. It is no ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ gameplay and the story is just there to give you some motivation to keep going, which once you start getting into the game won't even matter. The level design is incredible. Demons look scary but you know what's more scary? A guy with some incredible weapons and there's no shortage of ammo. The upgrades to weapons make them even more satisfying and how the combat rewards good movement is also amazing. I've played many fps games, both PvE and PvP but nothing comes close to this. I'm never uninstalling this game ever as it's so damn fun to play. Please do yourself a favour and play this. In an age where fps games have gone stale this is a gem and in all honesty when I say that this is one of the games of this generation, i mean it.

They did a great job bringing the series to the modern era, in a time where the FPS genre has been going down a different path from where the original Doom was. Most shooters are tactical, slow and honestly, pretty boring these days. There are some good ones out there, of course but everything seems to want to knock on that Call of Duty popularity.

Doom came along and changed all of that, it was a return to the retro style FPS and its done so well. You will have fun exploring, you will get a sweet sound track and you will be constantly on the move. This is how action should be played, they did a wonderful job with this game.

The second half of the game loses a bit in the level design though, it starts to feel linear paths to Quake arenas where each arena is a huge action section. This part is still a lot of fun, though I miss a lot of the exploration in the first half. This does help change the pace of the game though, and it keeps the game moving.

There are some great moments of comedy where it looks like you are going to get cutscenes and other slow down moments, but well.. Doom Guy has other plans.

Great game, worth it at full price, even more worth it on sale!\t\t\t\t\t

PRESENTATION:

Dark, bloody, gory, intense - it’s Doom! Graphics are amazing and sound effects creepily brilliant. Then when the rock music hits, you are jamming away while tearing the limbs off demons! The visuals are so good - nothing better than finding a new bad ass weapon and getting the animation of it in detail in your hands. Good voice acting but the Devil's voice in the game was a bit over the top and some of his lines meh. Sounded a bit like Christian Bale’s Batman voice.

The holograms that pop up to give the corporate announcements are hilarious:
“Weaponizing demons for a brighter tomorrow”. “Unlike everything else in your life, the work you do here matters.” :-D

Interesting story with harnessing the argent energy and the corporate greed taking technology to uncontrolled levels (like opening up a portal to hell!). The Samuel Hayden character was done very well, as was Olivia Pierce. Though you as the Doom Marine don’t care about any of them and just want to open up a can of whoopass and complete demon genocide. And you are, as the demons know, a thing of Legend!

GAMEPLAY:

One of the most fluid progression and upgrade systems I’ve played. Though it can be difficult sometimes to know when you have hit a rest point. But great tutorial system, very clear what you have just learnt with explanation symbols.

Having the Classic Doom portals open is just so cool. Brings back some great nightmares!

The fighting, once your skill set gets strong, can start to become repetitive. But completing little missions to get weapon upgrades is great. Combined with the challenge system and rune trials to unlock new abilities, and exploring and finding the little toy models, all helps create little goals to keep the game challenging and interesting.

Overall the game is fast and intense and there is no hiding behind items to wait for some sniper shot. You need to move your ass! Nothing like the adrenaline rush when ripping the heart out of a gore nest. How gloryful glory kills are!

VERDICT:

This game is very polished and great fun. It brought me right back to when I was playing DOOM as a youngster and now in my advancing years, I love it more than ever. Well done to id software for staying true to the original DOOM and advancing the series more than most could have hoped.

9/10 - Amazing!
