\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my favorite story-driven games I've played. I liked it so much, I had to replay it before playing the sequel Doom Eternal. It's one of those games that gives you constant progression with your character, and constant opportunity to upgrade your abilities and weapons. I love that they all come with their own challenges too, so you aren't just waiting for the next upgrade - you can actively challenge yourself to get upgrades by going after them.

And did I mention this game is really polished? Like, really well done. For a game released in 2016, it puts some other AAA games to shame. I have not run into a single bug, glitch, or otherwise that has hindered my experience. Level design is well thought out, and beautiful to look at. Enemies are incredibly dynamic, and should actually be feared at higher difficulties. The music score is top notch. One of my favorite soundtracks of all time for any video game. It enters at all of the most epic moments, and carries through each of the battle scenes to really make you feel like an epic demon hunter in hell.

I enjoy trying to complete games with 100% achievements and I enjoy finding all easter eggs hidden in games. This game caters well to that, with tons of opportunity for finding hidden gems and completing every achievement. HOWEVER; I am extremely disappointed that so many challenges in Steam are specific to multiplayer. I think that these challenges should be under a separate game/install for multiplayer only. The multiplayer player base quickly dropped off after launch, and if anyone were to want to get 100% achievements now, it's actually impossible. It appears that all servers are shut down and no longer supported. You can't grind them in private lobbies with a friend either, it requires public online matches.

The other gripe I have is that some achievements are glitched and will not complete. For example, I gathered all Runes, but "The Circle is Complete" achievement is broken. I even have upgraded every Rune to get that "IDDQD" achievement. Alas. I will mark this down as "100% Complete" on my own even though it's impossible in Steam.

But with multiplayer and achievements aside, this game is a 10/10 story that everyone should play.\t\t\t\t\t
