\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has an amazing singleplayer experience, I will not be reviewing the multiplayer as I have little interest in it. Please do not take that as me saying its multiplayer is bad, I have never played it myself nor will I.

Pros:
1. It feels like the old doom games(not doom 3), your character moves smoothly throughout the battlefield cutting down enemies left and right. Powerups and health/armor pickups are plentiful with many secrets hidden throughout the maps. If you enjoyed searching every corner of the map for goodies then you will love this game.

2. Music. Normally I am not a audiophile for videogames, but the music that plays throughout the fight sequences fits the theme of slaughtering monsters and really added to the experience.

3. Combat. This comes back to point #1, the combat is very smooth, the weapons feel like they have power. You have a few bullet sponges here or there but even they can't stand up against the bigger guns for long.

4. Executions. This feature lets you instantly kill enemies that are low on life with a melee attack. The game has a plethora of gory executions for each enemy. If will get either health, armor, or ammo depending on which runes you have equiped, which can help you out in a pinch in the middle of a massacre.

5. CHAINSAW. I look at it less like a weapon and more like a emergency ammo refill. The chainsaw once found will instantly kill any non-boss enemy depending on the amount of fuel you have, bigger enemies need more fuel. Using the chainsaw causes a very satisfying execution that upon killing an enemy showers you with various types of ammo. This actions mixes in flawlessy with combat and is one of my favorite features.


Cons:
1. Story. I never really had a moment where I was emotionally invested in the story. The plot just felt like a means to send me to the next area to slaughter more demons. To be fair this game puts more emphasis on the combat then story. If you are looking for a in depth plot then look elsewhere.

2. Length of game varies. You will beat this game very quickly if you are not exploring the entire map looking for upgrades, hidden levels, addons.

3. Lack of good DLC. This is more of a message to the developers, you made a fantastic game. However you keep adding DLC that is entirely multiplayer focused, if you made DLC that added a mini singleplayer campaign then I would buy it. You made a great game that really captured the spirit of doom, you have potential to make alot of money with additional singleplayer content.\t\t\t\t\t
