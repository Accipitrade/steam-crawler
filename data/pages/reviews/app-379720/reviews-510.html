\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuch a great reboot of a beloved and influential series!
The Story revolves around the Doom Slayer and his mission to stop the evils from hell invading mars. The characters in this game are fine,but can be really boring except for the deep voice that you hear in hell which i must say makes him really intriguing when he either reads some lore about hell or commanding demons to kill the player. The story itself is very, very simple but for a game that mainly focuses on gameplay more than anything, i'm fine with how simple the story is as it does a pretty good job at moving the game along.
The Setting for Mars and Hell are really immersive with the Mars facility being filled with broken high tech equipment, dusty sandstorms and blood smeared on every wall imaginable due to the carnage that has taken place, to Hell looking ancient, ritualistic and endless. The game has so much to look at even when fighting enemies and its really impressive.
Visually, the game is exceptional. All the textures, lighting and effects are all on point and for a game that came out in 2016, it still manages to look absolutely beautiful. The way the two locations are designed is just perfect with blood dripping from everywhere possible, to glowing runes that contain powers, this game visuals are just really impressive.
Sound and audio are Fantastic. The Soundtrack that Mick Gordon did for the game is just amazing (on a side note i would recommend listening to soundtrack itself on Spotify cause its that great of a listening experience. As well as the audio, from the guns to the demons, everything in this game that is related to audio in some kind is at the highest standard.
In terms of gameplay, ID software completely nailed it. The main thing you will be doing is killing demons. Its as straight forward as that, but the way that the devs designed it to be an arcade like FPS, it makes the entire experience so much more enjoyable. The guns feel like their own character as they are all unique in their own way and have mods which can change the way you play which allows for some great interactions with the enemies. The demons are also very varied and are introduced naturally through the game. The areas that you fight the demons are set up like mini arenas and allows the player to traverse and experiment with the combat. There are upgrades for both the suit, guns and there are ever perks in the form of runes which can aid the player in combat. The final blow animations are really satisfying to watch and give the game more grittiness than it already had. Finally, boss fights that you encounter may be simple enough but are really nice to take on.
Overall, Doom 2016 is an amazing game that excels in all fronts and i would recommend this game to those looking for some intense, arcade madness.\t\t\t\t\t
