---{Personal Touches}---
To be perfectly honest, when I first heard about this game getting a reboot I wasnt excited about it at all. It seemed just another take on a dying breed of casual FPS gameplay. I bought it anyway, telling myself at some point I would play it. Well, I did play it, and I was extremely impressed. The art design, the soundtrack, the immersive settings, the integrated the game mechanics with the level design, everything about this game was a surprise. The developers cared a great deal about player experiance, and took their roles as artsits exactly as seriously as what was needed to make the game work. It's a voilent, self gratifying shooter that embodies that ol' nastolgic feel the orginal shooters made a player feel. I felt in control, I felt a mastery of the skills needed to play the game's challenges each chapter provided, and the reward for completing the game using the skills developed playing it. The creators hit every single mark they were striving for with positive results. The game not only embodies the spirit of the original, but made efforts to ensure the community felt welcome in adding to it with their own unique touches. Everyone who was invovled in creating this game, should be proud of their accomplisment.\t\t\t\t\t
