Metal, gore and horror elements in this amazing revival of an iconic game.

https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2650362120

One of the best games of the decade, the most entertained I've been in years, and the punch the gaming industry needed to finally takes us away from boring first person shooters. No hiding or taking cover behind a wall, just move, jump and shoot your way out of Hell.

Story: simple, straighforward, and at the same time engaging and with depth, Doom takes you to some decent worldbuilding and will make you feel part of it.

Gameplay: One of the best, I really enjoyed the game mechanics, and the weapons are great to gib demons. Highly replayable if you want to upgrade your weapons or find all the secrets.

Graphics: it's really good, and not that demanding, my 4GB GPU could run this with no issues. The character and level design is right up my alley.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2653038446
