This game one of the best games I've ever played, if I didn't love the characters in Portal so much, this and DOOM Eternal would be my favorite games of all time.

This review will cover single player, since I don't play much multiplayer, and snapmap doesn't need reviewing.

-----Story (Spoiler Free)-----

This makes the classic Doomguy look like a unicorn bouncy chair at a playground in comparison to the ultimate badass that is the Doom Slayer.

Even with all the mysteries frustrating you, this game's story is extremely investing, so much I could play it all day, and've come close to.

9/10

-----Graphics-----

Looks stunning, greatly optimized, looks better than most of my renders in Blender and it runs way faster, the lighting is great, pretty moody, fits the approach they take.

10/10

-----Gameplay-----

Combat:

People always say DOOM Eternal's combat is better, and I agree with them. But DOOM's is still great, a small problem I had with DOOM Eternal's combat is that the Slayer's punches were weak normally. But in here, Imp's are just playthings to keep your hands moving.

The regular weapon selection although similar, is better in my opinion to DOOM Eternal. There's a reason why onslaughts of demons are fun to fight. Combat is smooth and satisfying and gore nests get you excited.

7/10

Level Design:

DOOM's level design is mostly good.

But when it comes to secrets, it can UNFAIR! Looking at a random wall at interacting, I know it was in classic Doom, but it wasn't FUN! Having things blend in, especially the levers, oh god the levers, I found 1 lever on my own, 1! Most of the time, I think the solution for some secrets is just progressing, then returning, but it's barley that.

Let's go away from secrets for now, the level design adds some challenge to the game, trying to figure out where to go, but if you have the automap, it's kinda broken.

Also there's actual BFG ammo.

6/10

-----DiffiCULTy-----

This is based off Ultra-Violence (Basically Hard Mode).

DOOM most of the time is pretty easy, especially when you get the BFG and the rune that gives ammo for it.

There's a few segments that are pretty hard, for example when they introduce Cacodemons, Most of the time this is easy, since there's a quad damage, but at this point in the game, you're probably trying to get weapon masteries, so you worry about the imps, which you can farm masteries from, but the Cacodemons catch you off guard and deal a disgusting amount of damage.

8/10

-----Summary-----

DOOM is a wonderful game with everything being great, making you want more. The story balances minor unsettlement, and major badassery. The level design could be improved on in terms of secrets, but they are secrets for a reason. Combat is buttery smooth and is just making demons piss their pants.

9/10\t\t\t\t\t
