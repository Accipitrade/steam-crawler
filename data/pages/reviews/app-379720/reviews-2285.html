\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot bad, its overhyped IMO.

Default keyboard controls are super weird, feels like a console port.. after some rebinding I eventually got over this. No way to change FOV.

Mod support? where is it? We have SnapMap but if feels seriously crippled compared to any game with legit mod support. Very sad, much potential down the drain. SnapMap creations aren't uploaded into the main multiplayer either. Its a separate gamemode with 4 player limit...this is where the "co-op" lives by the way, not in campaign.

Multiplayer is arena fps BUT WAIT there are basically no guns to pick up on the map, and instead you get a CoD style level-locked progression system with loadouts. I would've enjoyed multiplayer a lot more without the CoD, it was extremely mediocre. Not bad but not worth more than 10mins of my time either.

The campaign is decent, higher difficulties are balanced like crap IMO and require way too many glory kills to keep your health up which locks you into an animation. This slows down combat, its cool at first but kills replay value. If youre going to buy this game Id recommend wait for sale and definitely don't buy it for the multiplayer if you were hoping for Doom2/Quake style multiplayer.\t\t\t\t\t
