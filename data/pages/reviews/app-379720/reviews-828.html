\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very generic shooter lacking almost all mainstream FPS mechanics. I'll list my problems below.
1. NO iron sights, I'm spraying bullets everywhere.
2. NO sprinting. The default run speed is faster than most sprinting mechanics in other games, but there's no sprint.
3. NO regenerating health! It's almost like they expect you to be good at the game.
4: NO cover system, there's barely anything to crouch under. They just expect you to run around in zags and circles, shooting demons with no iron sights and no regenerating health, and no sprint button.
5. This is the worst one, what an utter FAIL. There are NO quick time events! There were PLENTY of chances to have quick time events, several boss fights could have used them. Mashing random buttons in a particular sequence as complete control is taken from you would have heightened many of the boss battles and arena fights, but no, they utterly failed to include any instance of quick time events. The entire game is literally just you playing it, outside of cutscenes.
6. NO microtransactions... wtf?! They could have EASILY implemented an overpriced lootbox system into the multiplayer, giving access to utterly meaningless skins and unfair advantages but NOOO, they ignored possibly one of the best advancements in gaming history just to have the same old "unlock everything with your own skills" ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥. I want paywalls, I want 20$ skins, I want to waste ALL my money. But there's not a single microtransaction in this game, what a waste.
7. No singleplayer DLC. I don't understand this one, they could have cut the game in half and sold us the second part of the game as day one DLC, but they wasted a chance there too. They sold the whole game as one package, no dlc and no microtransactions.
8. The maps are way too open. Call of Duty keeps the pace rolling with linear, damn near on rails shooting, from closed off roads to claustrophobic hallways, but Doom seems intent on having gigantic battle arenas and levels you're expected to EXPLORE and find SECRETS. That's where all the problems come together, this isn't a linear shooter sprawling with set pieces, quick time events, cover based shooting or anything from the "cool" modern shooters. It's literally just fighting hordes of demons with nothing but pure skill and accuracy.

This is a satirical review. DOOM 2016 is actually amazing, for all the reasons I listed. Amazing game, almost as good as Half Life 2 and Doom Eternal. A pure GAME through and through\t\t\t\t\t
