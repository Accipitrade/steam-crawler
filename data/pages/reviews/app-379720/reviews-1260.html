The road to He!l is paved with good inten... Argent Energy?
I'm late to the party, as usual. DOOM 2016 re-vitalizes and modernizes the DOOM IP. At least for me personally, I'm sure for a lot of other people as well. I played a little of DOOM 3 but it never really clicked. The duct tape mod is its highest claim to historical significance now. And beyond that, the original DOOM is what I was familiar with.

The tranquility of benign chaos.
In DOOM, as opposed to more traditional, modern shooters — meaning open world, wherein you're able to choose your approach, plan and use different loadouts. DOOM remains closer to its roots as a classic shooter. Semi-linear mission/levels — singular maps with an objective replete with secrets to uncover. The difference being that DOOM 2016 (sometimes referred to as new DOOM) forces you to reside within the midst of battle. You have to become comfortable in being completely surrounded and outnumbered. Normally, I play shooters from a distance, taking cover and taking pop shots where I can. And DOOM is a totally different paradigm. Granted, not completely unique. Other twitch shooters exist such as Painkiller or Serious Sam. Even the glory kill system where upon weakening enemies, you're able to extract health and ammo by ripping and tearing them apart — can be said to have been inspired by Warhammer 40K's Space Marine, the third person shooter.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2135608002

But DOOM 2016 puts it all into package that is largely unparalleled.

Your affinity for guns is apparent.
All the guns in the game are really good and provide great visceral feedback. You can find upgrades for them to switch what the secondary fire does as well as spend points to increase their effectiveness. Zoom and charge functions on the Gauss canon, micro-missiles on the rifle, heat blasts from the plasma rifle — Environmental awareness and knowledge of what ammo you have left (being accurate and agile helps, too) will keep you alive long enough to close the He!l portal unleashed on Mars. You're also able to upgrade your Praetor suit. That's much more strait forward — increasing your health, armor and ammo count.
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2034357335

THE GIST:
DOOM 2016 is borderline flawless. It kind of irks me to say that, honestly. The only complaint I really have is... well... I wish there was a bit more of it. It does have multiplayer and user made 'SnapMaps' as well as rune challenges in the campaign. Plus arcade mode. I guess I'm just a bit more narratively motivated — even if that narrative is as vapid as closing a portal that stops a demon invasion. The idea of using Argent Energy as a power source is interesting, too.

If you've read this far, consider following my curation — Station Argus
