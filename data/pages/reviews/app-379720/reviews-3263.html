\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCampaign is amazing, except for that ending. I won't spoil story elements but expect a DLC pack to give you the actual ending...

I found the multiplayer to be pretty generic and disappointing. There were some nice game modes but for me any positives end there. The load out system was not as great as I had expected. I expected weapon mods to be interchangable even in multiplayer to give you a better customization experience to fit your way to play. Nope just color palletes and some patterns. It was kind of a let down since the weapon mods would have balanced out the game having less weapons than other multiplayer shooters.

So if you are a person who just wants the campaign I suggest waiting on a price drop.

Despite my negative comments I would still recommend this game. ID delivered on all promises about the campaign.
