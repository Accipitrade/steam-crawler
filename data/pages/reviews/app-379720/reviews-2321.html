It's not DooM.

I have a hard time giving this review. Let me start by saying as a game, it's not bad. It's playable and even enjoyable at times. But it's generic.

DooM always had an atmosphere to it: One of horror, one fear, one of grotesque beauty. It wasn’t a serious game, I mean Demons on Mars? It’s such an absurd concept, and it was never serious. Yet, when playing it, when fighting in it, you forget that. You fight and you survive, or you don’t. There’s always this fear in the background, this horror that embraces you. But this game, it just doesn’t have that.

Maybe it’s the health that constantly drops from the enemies. Maybe it’s the dumb challenges and hoops you jump through to upgrade your character. Upgrading your character, is just not a DooM concept. Maybe it’s the pistol that always has ammo, that you have no fear of having to hunt for.

This game, it’s generic, and I’m sorry, but it’s crap. If I want a generic shooter, I’ll play battle field or something similar. This is supposed to be DooM, yet, it just doesn’t feel like it.

If you want another shooter, it’s not bad. Graphics are ok, game play and movements are there. But if you want that DooM atmosphere, pick another game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll right, after playing it twice at Ultra-Violence difficulty this is what I've noticed.

> Most of the weapons are great, and their dynamic felt good. I'd have liked more long range ones besides the rifle with the Tactical Scope. My personal favourites were the Plasma Rifle with the Heat Wave aug since such augment wasted no ammunition and was awesome to deal with several enemies at once; and the Gauss Cannon with the Siege Mode.
> I barely used the grenades and holograms cause almost all the time I focused more on using my weapons to deal with the menaces.
> The enemies felt really annoying from time to time, can't count the amount of times I was busy dealing with a Hell Knight or a Pinky to give an example just to be killed by a Summoner or even an Imp with a fully charged blast. Specially on levels like Kadingir Sanctum, or the train room at VEGA Central Processor (Though using the BFG to pay them back for attacking me from all sides at the same time felt so nice).
>Dunno if it was due to bad luck or not, but sometimes the enemies felt really competent regarding to their strategies. I remember enemies being far away from me whenever I'd take a powerup, and they were really (if not too much) good coordinating themselved to attack at me.
> Regarding levels one common critic I've seen and I gotta agree is that the levels can be too small, several times I feel I have not enough room to freely move, also I'd like DoomGuy to move even faster. I had the most fun on the biggest maps.
> I'm not sure whether I like the RPG elements like the runes and the suit upgrades. Cause even when they can give you a really nice boost, it's annoying to have to stop the flow of the game to look around each corner to see whether I'm missing some of them.

Still a great game, hope that Eternal'll be way better since with the addition of the Meat Hook it seems we'll have several improvements regarding mobility

9/10\t\t\t\t\t
