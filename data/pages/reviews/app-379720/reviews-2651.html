Visuals : 4/5
Replayability : 2/5
Story : 1/5

Overall : 3

I wouldn't of got doom 4 if I didn't have extra money left over during the steam sale, but I don't regret my purchase at all. Doom 4 does a lot of things great, but it does them great too frequently. The only reason the gameplay, sound/music, and visuals aren't all 5/5 is because at about level 4 they've gotten boring. Everything just ends up feeling reused from earlier games with some exact copies thrown in such as dead simple near the end of the game. There's not many levels too which is a letdown but the levels in it are long enough to make up for that. The story seemed like it was going to be good at first meaning it seemed like there wasn't going to be one with some blatant examples such as doomguy going out of his way to shut up the “person” trying to talk to him but this gets thrown out pretty much into level 2 when the player must sit through a lengthy explanation of why they have to kill the demons. It’s just all unnecessary filler that they even made fun of in the beginning so i don't know why it's in the game, since everyone hated it in doom 3 anyways. Overall though I had a fun time playing the game and I probably won't play it ever again, unless I decide to try arcade mode.
