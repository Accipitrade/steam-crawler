\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI remember playing the original Doom on my dad's computer. We went to a technology convention and they had the first game (set of floppy disks) on sale for ten bucks. Since then, I never liked any of the remakes. It just didn't give me the same sensation.

This Doom feels like what the original would be like if it was released today. From the Imps to the Baron of Hell. The weapons are great, upgrades are great, maps are great and even the secrets are fun to look for.

Also, the storyline is perfect. Not just a marine who is stuck in the situation, but a true force of destruction. I've played through three times just to see the story again. With references to the old series, it feels more like a continuation.

Gameplay was smoothe and easy to follow. Took me a while to get use to the new glory kill system, but they have some upgrades that you can do that make it truly enjoyable to learn.\t\t\t\t\t
