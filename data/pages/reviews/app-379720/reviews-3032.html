The level design is beautiful and complex. You will get lost and have to use the map. This is not a linear corridor shooter.
The combat is great, it's a combination of classic Doom, Doom 3, and Q3A.

Bad
The character design is HORRIBLE. The demons look like they belong in World of Warcraft.

No mods.
No real editor.

While it's a game that locks you into an area to fight off demons from hell time and time again, it manages to stay fresh the whole way through by making it grizzlingly fast and amazingly well paced. The guns are varied and have welcome (but unnecessary) upgrades, though if you've played Doom before, you have a pretty good idea of what you're going to get.

My only real critisicm is that some of the environments could've been more visually interesting. Particuarly some parts of Hell. Though other parts looked awesome.

Runs great on most modern systems, too! Amazing optimization!\t\t\t\t\t
