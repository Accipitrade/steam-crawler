\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHave been playing id's games since Wolfenstein. Loved this entry and looking forward to replaying it again. I can't add much to the tens of thousands of other reviews. Note that I've only played single player.

Upsides
-Fast, visceral, fluid and rewarding combat.
-Ability to replay missions to pick up anything you missed the first time around.
-Not filled with the angsty teenage overkill of Satanic imagery as the older Doom and Quake games.
-More of an actual storyline and backstory (which you can skip through if you just want to rip and tear).
-Most of the weapons are useful and fun, with varied upgrades, giving you slight variations in playstyle.

YMMV:
-The checkpoint system can take a little getting used to, but unless you're playing on hard difficulty levels, you get used to it. They're rarely spaced too far apart.

Downsides
-There are a few challenges that are a little buggy and don't always give credit, requiring you to replay the level repeatedly.\t\t\t\t\t
