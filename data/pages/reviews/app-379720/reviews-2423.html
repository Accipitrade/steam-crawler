- Game takes too long to introduce core gameplay features like dash and double jump.
- Arenas are small and battles often too short. No enemies until you activate a gore nest.
- It seems the creatos wanted you to play like quake, jumping and dodging, but there are too many place to fall by mistake during battle that outright kills you.
- Little incentive to ultilize your whole toolkit.
- Visuals make it a bit hard to pinpoint which enemy is which with the fast pace.
- Little visual feedback after shooting a demon if it doesn't stagger or kills it.
- Repetitive visuals and battles.
- Battle music DRUMMING HARD on your ears after the battle is long over and there's one random imp alive somewhere you don't know. The music while your'e only exploring around is often too much also.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:

Well optimized. I technically don't have a graphics card powerful enough to run it, but it starts up and plays anyways. The graphics don't look pristine as such, but there's no lag or glitches which is great.
Super fun and smooth FPS action with good weapons that you can upgrade at a very nice pace throughout the game if your observant for upgrade stations and satisfying melee kills (melee cutscenes optional).
Straightforward level designs that are fun to manuever and create an engaging atmosphere.
Great voice acting.
Minimal, short cutscenes, good for replay value.
Level specific challenges are creative and make for more intentional gameplay if you're into achievements.
10/10 amazing soundtrack that enhances the gameplay right through the damn roof.
Interesting backstory that's unobtrusive to the gameplay.
Rewards investigating levels in a practical, helpful way.

Cons:

Can get a little samey in parts after dealing with a lot of the same enemies for several levels in a row.
Some boss battles leave a bit to be desired because of predictability.
Sound design for weapons can be a bit weak and generic (minor nitpick, not a dealbreaker, but for a gutsy game such as this, the weapon sounds could have used some more oomph).
It ends eventually. I want more. Now. Please release Doom:Eternal soon. Please.

8/10. A great action game that stands tall with the legacy of the original pioneering title.
