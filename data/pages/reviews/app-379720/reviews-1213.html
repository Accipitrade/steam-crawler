\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you played the original, you'll probably like this reboot. The action is fun, the gunplay is satisfying, the visuals and animations are fantastic, the music is good, and the optimized performance is really impressive on virtually every map.

Most people don't care about story (which is justifiable, it's never been the selling point of DOOM), but if you like that sort of thing you won't be disappointed. There are dozens of codex entries to find throughout the game that give context to the world, as well as environmental features like hologram audio recordings and diagrams littering the floors that add to the atmosphere. And just like the original, there are plenty of secret areas with items or easter eggs.

Alternatively you can ignore all the lore and just shoot demons. I can guarantee you'll still enjoy it. :)\t\t\t\t\t
