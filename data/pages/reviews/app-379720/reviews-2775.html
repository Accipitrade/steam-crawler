\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my 2nd review of this game. Deleted the previous one, and will keep this one short.

It's an excellent game, if you like shooters.

Except for a few bosses. They can be defeated, but battling them quickly becomes tiresome and frustrating. Don't hesitate to lower the game's difficulty if you start feeling bored, and aren't a completionist. Focus on the fun. I did.

Also, don't use god mode (available via the Console in single player.) I tried it once, it messed up my files and the game itself started regularly freezing (!), so I had to begin anew. (That's when I wrote my 1st, quite angry review, already deleted.) So just don't go god. Lower the difficulty instead. Lower it even more, if you wish to proceed and aren't entertained by the current challenge.

Right. So, it's a great game. Worth its price. :)\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is so so...single player it's great. A lot of fun and brings back the feel of classic doom all over again. The problem I have is the multiplayer and lack of replay value. I'm not sure I'm a huge fan of the levels either or how some of the bosses would spam high level attacks essentially giving you no chance. All in and all the game might be worth $20 in my opinion but the original price to me is not worth it.

PROS:
Stunning graphics
Interesting weapons
Glory Kills are just cool as ♥♥♥♥
A lot of different glory kills
Feels like the old game with a new style
Fun collectiables and cute music that brings you back

CONS:
Bosses seem glitched to the point of being unfun to fight
No replay value (in my opinion)
Short story
Multiplayer is majorly lacking and feels clunky to me\t\t\t\t\t
