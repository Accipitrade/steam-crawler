I LOVE THIS GAME.
I F***ING LOVE THIS GAME.

The Ultimate Doom was the first video game that i got my greasy little 3.5 year old mitts on, an accident when a nameless programmer at the Nuclear power station my father worked at passed it along to him having been "done with it". Not knowing what it in fact was, I was given free reign of the '95 Dell desktop for two hours. He returned to find me squealing in delight in response to the blood and gore.

Fast Forward twenty aught years, I'm five seconds into doom and the same f***ing noises are coming from my mouth. This game is like resurrecting your dead grandma to bake her signature meatloaf for you. Same nostalgia, same joy, only with copious amounts of gore and action. The graphics are spectacular (SUCK IT FALLOUT 4, YOU ARE MY FAVORED CHILD NO LONGER), the lore (while unnecessary) fits perfectly, the Soundtrack is like a masterful symphony performed by Slipknot. I had my reservations about the execution system, but after a few mintues of jaw ripping, neck breaking, and chainsawing, I was hooked.

TL;DR: I F***ING LOVE THIS GAME, 10/10, Would get dropkicked by to my childhood again.\t\t\t\t\t
