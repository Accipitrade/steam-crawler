\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is going to be abrasive to read, I rarely do reviews like this but with this one I am having my say.

Before Nu-Doom fans were created by Doom Eternal there was this. And this absolutely obliterates Las Vegas-Doom, I mean Nu-Doom. Oh wait, this is about Doom 2016, sorry Eternal left a really bad lasting impression on me, along with all its absurdities.

Out of all the Doom games since growing up with the originals in the 90's, this generated as much if not more awe as the long awaited Doom 3 before and after release.

If someone really knows what a Doom game is growing up with the games in the 90's, they know that this is worthy of the name, unlike the piss poor latest Bethesda interference.

I am sure this might have been even better if id software used a better publisher. Aside from being the introduction of Bethesda into id software, this is close to perfect. With Eternal? I swear they were licking Bethesda's schlong, come on ghost dogs and the Marauder 'Draugr Death Lord'? That's straight up Skyrim leftovers.

Don't even get me started with Bethesda's Doom 3 BFG Edition, which is a low fidelity train wreck coupled with the actual game difficulty being dramatically dropped to the neurological complexity to that of an amoeba.

Hate is a strong word but I have specially reserved it for Doom Eternal. And yes I am an absolute elitist gatekeeper and am mighty proud of it but only for the subject of Doom games. This is the last game in the series that was not a pitiful woe. I grew up during the inception of Doom and take great pleasure in gate keeping the gatekeepers.

I am convinced most the rabid Doom Eternal fans don't actually like the original Doom games leading all the way up to Doom 2016. Yes this is a real Doom game despite the stupid use of ammo and health shards dropped by kills.

Doom Eternal is absolutely without question superfluous and obtuse. This game is not.\t\t\t\t\t
