\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Doom" is special compare to recent FPS game. Its old school, remind me the good old time of playing Doom 3 in the past.

Singleplayer with arena style gameplay which is something i havent seen in the last decade. Yes, its a bite repeative but there is so much fun out of it. The game encourage player to keep moving and jumping while having combat, use melee to finish off enemy will get u extra ammo and heath pack, instead of "duck and cover" and try to find supply on the way.

Melee finish animation is very cool along side with different kind of weaponry, espically chainsaw, with its most bloody kill animation in the game. Level design r quite nice, with very different style between Mars station and Hell,

3D map with hidden secret improved the singleplayer along the way. Game music fits with environment, mixed with gun shots and screming makes the game even better. Like people always said, a lot of blood, a lot of screm, thats the good old doom, and the new one just getting better.

If u r looking for brutal singleplayer experience with a lot of blood and broken limbs, THIS IS YOUR GAME!
Dont purchase this game for Multiplayer, Singplayer is always the centre of DOOM.

11 out of 10 will recommend this game to my friends.\t\t\t\t\t
