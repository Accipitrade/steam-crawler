\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust pure fun violent action. Challenging on higher difficulty settings. None stop action that introduces new, bigger, badder demons as the game progresses. Great fun right from the start. Good pacing with lots of extra community made content, and a pure arcade mode where you play each level (you can choose which one) that awards points for various actions, with the possibility of big multipliers for good players. After the level is finished you can see your score and compare against a huge user base.
But, the main thing for me was the excellent campain.
With a decent PC and GPU the graphics are really very good. Very smooth and looks great on standard 60fps monitors, but butter smooth if you can get it running at over 100fps. Never experience a single crash in the 150 hours or so I have spent playing the campain and other modes.
If there is a negative it's the multiplayer, but never mind, well worth it for everything else which is very professionally implemented.
A classic to be sure. A must buy for all FPS action game fans, and one of the best there is.\t\t\t\t\t
