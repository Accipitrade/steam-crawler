The game revolves around spectacle. Ammo and health is gained through over-the-top cutscene kills that are gory and entertaining, but some may be frustrated by the interruption of gameplay. The movement is nowhere near as smooth or entertaining as its successor.

I enjoyed messing around in multiplayer - the skin customisations and demon abilities are particular highlights for me. However, at some point in the game's history, the multiplayer ranks were reset or smth and I lost a load of progress. Would not be a new issue for a new player as the game is basically dead + you'd be starting from scratch, but it was annoying at the time.\t\t\t\t\t
