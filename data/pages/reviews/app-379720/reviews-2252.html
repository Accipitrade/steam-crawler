\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's alright. I could see easily hating this game or loving it. It does nothing particularly well or poorly. Everything is pretty middle of the road honestly.

The damage feedback is pretty non-existent. I mean both the damage you receive and the damage you deal out. You can see faint hits of blood and get used to being able to tell if you landed a shot or not. However you don't really know your health or armor are getting low until you are in the threshold of one shot and it beeps at you.

None of the guns are memorable. They're fine but none of them have the oomph that so many people seem to praise from other reviews I've seen. The Assault Rifle sounds amazing but that's about it?

The glory kills get stale pretty quickly. They serve their purpose but I just got tired of the pause they put into the combat.

Speaking of pauses that break up the action, looking for collectibles can be so awful and I get you can skip the collectibles but some people just can't play games like that. I spent a good amount of time looking for collectibles and if I hadn't I feel like the campaign would have been about 6-6.5 hours.

I didn't try the multiplayer because the movement was pretty average and the gun play i talked about at the start.

Overall I got this game for $15 and if you can stomach playing through the campaign until you get to the stronger, better guns then I think you'd at least not regret picking this game up.\t\t\t\t\t
