\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is legitimately my favorite DOOM game. I've played every one of them and this is still at the tippy top!
This is also the first DOOM game that I ever played, but I'll try not to be biased.

SOME THINGS TO KEEP IN MIND:

The multiplayer mode -
This is a singleplayer game at heart. I personally really liked the multiplayer though, there's a lot of customization options that make it fun to keep playing, unlocking stuff in this game is really satisfying. Of course the multiplayer mode isn't without flaws, sometimes things seem very unbalanced at times.

The combat -
Beautiful. Amazing. I LOVE IT. The combat is obviously going to be one of the main aspects of this game, and it definitely doesn't disappoint. It requires skill, but doesn't interrupt the flow of adrenaline! I love that in order to get more health you have to get up close to the enemies for a melee attack. Which could be good or bad, it brings strategy to the table. Not to mention all of the awesome weapons, you can even customize your weapons, LOVE THAT.

The music -
Perfect. Absolute masterpiece. It kicks up to death metal when you are in an extreme combat section and simply MAKES you feel energized and ready to fight the hordes of hell. And once they have been defeated, the music tapers off to a steady eerie ambiance that encourages you to take your time to explore for secrets. Brilliant.

10/10\t\t\t\t\t
