\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast-paced, brutal action; beautiful game world (namely the hell levels); secrets, challenges, glory kills, collectables, easter eggs - what's not to love?? The music is alright, though I'd prefer that it were more metal (but then, there's Doom Eternal), I did love how they remade one of my favourite classic tracks - 'Sign of evil' - though. While the game is somewhat difficult in the beginning, once you reach a certain point in Kadingir Sanctum, it's pretty much smooth sailing (or maybe I just got used to the enemy AI and the weapons?).

What I disliked is some of the weapons: even when fully modded, they're rubbish (I'm looking at you, combat shotgun! Chaingun doesn't feel very satisfying, though it does pack a punch), the difficulty is a bit unbalanced (lost count of how many times those pesky imps got me! >.<), and for a game that's all about exploration and backtracking, you're sometimes locked out of certain parts of the level because either the door's scripted to lock behind you after you access it, or because you jump off a ledge and there's no way to return to that area (and there's no ghost/ flying console command either!), so you have to slog though the entire level again, just to reach the stuff you might have missed.

Anyway, this game is a worthy successor to the classics, even if it's more of an arena shooter than a classic one where enemies stand around or patrol until they see you.\t\t\t\t\t
