\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat stuff if you're a fan of the originals, like action FPS games, or both. It recaptures the best elements of the old and combines it with most of the better mechanics you see in today's games. No effort at realism or "fair fights" here - gameplay generally focuses on using your speed and arsenal to adjust to increasingly remote odds, overwhelming numbers, and seemingly unfair battles.

While on one hand the game is most definitely a mass slaughterfest, the fast paced battles depend on mastering a dance of sorts where every encounter is a massive game of cat and mouse, requiring you to evade withering amounts of enemy fire while you pick out and eliminate your next target until everything else has been reduced to piles of gore. The pace and "flavor" of the gameplay is closer to Half-Life or Unreal Tournament rather than what is seen in the recent trends of gritty realism or the survival horror helpless everyman archetype.\t\t\t\t\t
