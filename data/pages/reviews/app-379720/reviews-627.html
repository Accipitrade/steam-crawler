Rip and Tear those Demons! until you get Rich get Richer
"Local man just woke up in the middle of an apocalypse and too angry to die."

THIS REVIEW IS FOR CAMPAIGN ONLY. Finished the game in Hurt Me Plenty difficulty.
"Now I get it why they made DOOM Eternal more challenging"

DOOM (2016) is one heck of a FPS game, ever since I played the original DOOM and DOOM II, I was so excited playing this, and boy, they do not disappoint. This game is gore, blood, and shoot demons like no tomorrow. It's fun, and tense, and makes you keep playing, though cutscenes are unskippable and sometimes ruins the pace and mood, it's a decent plot of ripping and tearing. As I played this on normal difficulty, it is still challenging and fast-paced despite how broken abilities are or how most of the time you'll just use one or two weapons only, but it is very fun to experiment the different mods, runes, and guns. The amount of content of this 13-mission campaign is great in both terms of gameplay and visual. (Note: Best play the game in Vulkan than OpenGL, you'll thank me later.)

Overall, a great FPS game, and a must-try, and a good introduction to DOOM for new players and a great return of DOOM for old players.\t\t\t\t\t
