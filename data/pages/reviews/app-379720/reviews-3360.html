\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis isn't doom.

Enemies are bullet sponges, and the levels are arena fight after arena fight. There's no satisfying "mowing down hordes of enemies" because all your weapons feel underpowered. There's no tactically clearing out room after room.

It's like nothing but Dead Simple over and over and over again, except imagine your only weapons are the pistol and the BFG. That's not fun.

One of the things the original game got right was it makes you feel like your weapons are powerful, by giving you progressively stronger monsters to kill. When you've been killing pinkies with one double-barrel blast apiece, a mancubus feels powerful and dangerous taking 4. On the other hand, in this game, everything bigger than a chaingunner requires you to plink away at it for ages. Actually, you can't one-shot-kill ANYTHING in the game, because any time you damage an enemy sufficiently it goes into a stunned state for you to perform a Mortal-Kombat-Fatality style finishing move on. It's probably supposed to make you feel like an unstoppable bad****, but when you can't even kill the lowliest grunt in one blast it just feels like your weapons don't do anything.

So the game becomes very tedious. And for large targets it's pretty much the instakill weapons or nothing. That's not fun.

Neither is grinding cheevos to level up. F that console game garbage. I want to kill stuff because I'm getting better at a game, not because of toiling away at some nonsense so that my numbers go bigger.

So... I hate this game. Hate it hate it hate it. I still play the original, and love it. I don't know what this, but it's not Doom.\t\t\t\t\t
