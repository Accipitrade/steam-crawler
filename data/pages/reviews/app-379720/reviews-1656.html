\r\n\t\t\t\t\tworth $5, 7/10

it's mediocre until you get double jump and then it's only fun when you're engaged in the core gameplay loop, shooten dudes.

the weapon mastery challenges and level challenges are great 'side-quests' to do while you're fighting, and I would consider them the single greatest part of the game (even though the levels often insensibly cut you off from returning to previous areas, checkpointing and preventing you from achieving challenges you missed without replaying the entire mission).

as someone who was duped into buying this game at full price, I feel obligated to do a public service and warn about the critical flaws that drag the game down, which I was not informed of before purchasing:

CONS:
- audio design is absolutely terrible, and costs the game a lot. directional audio is completely unreliable. when a single basic Possessed zombie enemy is in the area, they scream and sound like five enemies. when five of them are in the area, they sound like a horde, coming from every direction. this is just baffling
- platforming sucks, I still can't figure out why there's such an emphasis on it and why bottomless death pits are so prevalent in a game that game-overs when you fall in them (as opposed to just putting you back where you fell from, with reduced health). 'fast ledge climbing' should have not been one of the last available upgrades
- movement speed is too slow. changes the sprint button to a walk button as a "♥♥♥♥ you" to anyone who presses it, as well as the reload button to a lengthy weapon mod swapping animation to punish anyone who has played a shooter in the last decade
- the weapon selection method sucks: pressing 1-8 for weapons is not good design. scroll wheel and weapon wheel are even worse alternatives. the best implementation for games with this many weapons is Half-Life 1's, from 1998: 1-5 correspond to weapon categories/lists and you tap the number multiple times until you get to the weapon you want.

otherwise pretty solid; good fan service, decent-if-dull environments, good music, great enemy AI and enemy variety, good bosses\t\t\t\t\t
