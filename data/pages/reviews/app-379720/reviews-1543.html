Introduction
Doom 2016 was a game I picked up and I ended up putting it down the last time I played it but I ended up picking it up again and I have to say I am really glad I did. I did not play the old classic Doom but I know about it because I am one of those people who knows about old classic video games, I did, however, play Doom 3 and that game I also enjoyed despite it being more of a horror game then what everyone thought Doom should be,

Story
The story starts out with Doom guy waking up and all hell has broken loose, these scientists decided that it’s a great idea to experiment with the possibility of going into Hell it’s self and of course trouble starts. Doom guy is, of course, pissed and wants to kill every single demon out there, Doom Guy’s favorite manga series to read is Goblin Slayer and Berserk as he can relate to both Goblin Slayer’s and Guts personality and character very well. I even hear a rumor that Doom Guy is Goblin Slayer just reincarnated. Doom Guy also loves listening to Five Finger Death Punch as that is playing on his earbuds at max volume while he is out killing demons on repeat.

A woman named Olive Pierce wants to make a portal that can lead to hell without a power source and it’s all up to Doom Guy to stop her as everyone has either turned into a demon or have been killed. Doom Guy has the weapons and the arsenal to fight the demons of hell and stop Olive once and for all. At some point, you even walk into hell and Doom guy is just killing any demon that crosses his path and he acts like he owns the place and he eventually gets the title of The Doom Slayer and all demons fear him.

The story does not have a whole lot to really talk about but that is fine to me, Doom shows us more gameplay than story and that is fine. That’s not to say that the story is not good, you will find logs of information on the world and other things to get a better idea on the story of Doom and the game does a good job on a world-building standpoint.

Gameplay
Now gameplay for Doom is the bread and butter and it has a lot to offer, it brings back the old classic Doom gameplay that everyone who played the original knows and loves. You can find all sorts of fun weapons to use, power-ups, and upgrades to your suit to help fight the hordes of Hell.

All the weapons that Doom has are fun to use, I found no weapon useless in the later part of the game as all of them have their own uses and are just awesome to use. The weapons have their upgrades you can get at these drones and you can get a weapon mod after that you have to kill demons to get the upgrade points. All upgrades are fun to use and they help spice up combat, if you get all the upgrades to a certain mod, you can get this 4th powerful mod for that weapon if you meet the certain requirements.

When certain demons are at a certain amount of health you can perform a melee kill on them and that will drop some health for you to grab, you get more health from doing a melee kill so try your best to do that when you are low on it, unless you die first of course which will happen sometimes. There is also a chainsaw and that will instakill some demons and when that happens you will get extra ammo for all your weapons and it’s so cool to use, you only have 4 uses until it runs out so try to use it cautiously.

There are three powerups that you can find, there is a haste power-up that makes you move fast, this one was my least favorite as all it does is make you move faster and that’s it. The next one which is quad damage makes your weapons do double the damage and you can kill even the hardest demons with 2 or 3 shots with the missile launcher. The last power-up and my favorite to use is the Berserk power-up as that will make Doom Guy go into a rage and he rips demons in half and it feels so nice to use.

At some point on your adventures on Mars and into Hell you will come across these runes, these runes will give you perks like you being able to absorb items at a certain range or making demons stagger for longer. The runes can get upgrades to them and they can get more effects, you can carry more runes but you need to beat these challenges to get the ability to carry more runes, plus you need to beat them to get the runes.

Honorable mentions
The graphics for Doom 2016 are really impressive, possibly the best I have seen in an FPS thus far, every area was impressive to look at and felt alive. The challenge was also something I enjoyed as well and for anyone looking for a challenge then this game provides it and I enjoyed it.

Conclusion
Doom 2016 brings back the classic gameplay that everyone who played the original Doom knows and loves. If you love the original Doom, the Doom 2016 is a treat for you as it has amazing gameplay, a great challenge and the classic Doom charm that you will love.
