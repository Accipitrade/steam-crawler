\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHad low expectations when they showed the trailer and had the melee combat stuff, thought it was just a cheap gimmick and there wouldn't be any substance in this game. DOOM was always a classic game growing up, and spent many of hours in that and DOOM2. I was under the impression this was just some kind of tech showcase, somewhat like what Doom3 turned out to be. Heard a few things about the game when it came out, and the types of things I heard were that the classic feel of DOOM was back in a big way. This got my interest so I decided to check it out. So far I am only 3 missions in the single player, but so far it has far exceeded my expectations. Classic DOOM gameplay is back, and a reasonable amount of difficulty as well. Can't wait to finish the rest of the campaign. Multiplayer also looks to be interesting although I've spend minimal time so far. The level editor has an online map browser that allows you to group up with friends for co-op style games and beyond. So far worth the money, if you were a huge fan of the original DOOM games, check it out!\t\t\t\t\t
