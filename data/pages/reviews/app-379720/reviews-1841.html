\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCan't believe this game is from 2016.
The grafx are partly as poor (blurry textures, pixelated decalls,...) as in the 90s! Even on ultra settings.
Then all this infotext! Is it meant to calm the player down from 100 to 0 instantly after some litte shootings or what?! Or should it just pretend the game would be as komplex that you would need or even want to read?

But what really kills my interest in playing this bloody gem are the invisible walls.
In the age of "open map" (whatever you definitions-fetishists may mean by it) it is more than ever a sign of cheap maps, quickly punched together by lazy designers.
MAYBE it would be okay for a 100% streight shooter like known from the very first playstation. But in a game where you have to explore and search for secrets??? I spent MUCH more time dying because "a gap next to the abyss" was behind a magic wall instead of being killed by the annoying little monsters.

See, I did not expect much from a game "already 4 years old". But I surely can expect more than graphics reused for the last 20 years, even less freedom or creativity than in the original game from 93 and sh!tloads of blown up text for "stuff" more complex than pressing a trigger!

Seriously, you have lost my respect id Soft. :(\t\t\t\t\t
