DOOM has always been known for fast movement, quick reflexes, weapon arsenal, monsters and demons ranging from standard to special, and an long and strong story fulled with lore. This game has a main story goal to progress to, however in each mission there are several secrets hidden throughout the level that rewards you with power ups that upgrade your gear and weapons with mods which gives you a better fighting chance against the demons. There are also challenges in both the missions and certain weapons that rewards you with either weapon upgrades to weapon special perks helping you fight. You should take your time exploring the levels and missions and makes sure you have all you need as things may get nasty in your next big fight.

This was a great return of the DOOM series that introduced new mechanics and elements but still respected the series roots and what made DOOM awesome from the very beginning.\t\t\t\t\t
