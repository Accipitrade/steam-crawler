The most seamless action videogame I ever had the honor of playing.

The game is built around completely utilizing all of its mechanics, and encourages you to kill demons in every way possible. The transitions between different kills not only feel smooth, but the execution leaves you with a bloodlust that can never be satisfied. The question is never "Why are there so many gоddаmn demons" but always "WHERE are the gоddаmn demons". The game never fails to remind you: You're a predator.

The only 'downside' I found mainly affects completionists: This game has no manual saving whatsoever. It only uses an autosave function, and on all difficulties but the last, works on a checkpoint system. This is a double-edged sword. On one hand, you can just focus on playing the game without worrying about saving every few seconds in fear of either forgetting collectibles/secrets, or being softlocked. On the other hand, if you go back to a certain level to complete challenges or get collectibles, YOU HAVE TO TRIGGER A CHECKPOINT OR YOUR PROGRESS WON'T SAVE. And if you (most likely) do that, DO NOT DIE, you get sent back to the last checkpoint on the level, but that also means your challenge progress gets reset. I had the misfortune of finding this out on the very last level.
Apart from this, I found no bugs or glitches whatsoever.

This game is a MUST PLAY. 10/10.\t\t\t\t\t
