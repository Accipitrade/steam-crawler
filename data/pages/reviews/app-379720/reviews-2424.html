\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is an incredibly fun game and one of the few "triple-A" FPS that has significantly different gameplay from others.
What makes this game so fun is that you have to move all the time, because the huge variety of enemies will get behind any cover you seek so you just need to move. Additionally, no enemy projectiles are hitscan, so you can dodge their attacks most of the time. The game also incentifies getting close to your enemies through the glory kills, which will quickly kill enemies, while giving you many health drops.
The great gameplay is complemented by the great level design, which sometimes contains some platforming sections, or searching for keycards to open locked rooms. Additionally the different combinations of enemies gradually make the game more difficult. By far the best thing about the game design, in my opinion, are the secrets. I am not the kind of guy that wants to 100% every game, but I found it very fun to try to get all secrets and collectibles in the levels. By giving you weapon and armor upgrades, the game makes exploration also pretty rewarding.
The Campaign is about 12 hours long, but you can still play community-made maps or multiplayer. While the multiplayer is not that great, some community-made maps are pretty good.
Despite being relatively short, the amazing gameplay holds this game up so much that I would definetly recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
