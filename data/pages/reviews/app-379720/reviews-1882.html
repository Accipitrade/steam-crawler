\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust to preface this, I've only played the campaign and haven't touched the multiplayer.

Coming from primarily playing Overwatch this game has been a completely refreshing experience for me. It's just mindless fun. However I do agree with the negative criticism of this game being repetitive. The campaign is basically follow the compass and do what you did a minute ago: which is killing the baddies.

If all you want to do is to point and shoot then this is the game for you. There are additional challenges in the game that can make doom guy even more fun to play and I highly recommend completing the rune which gives you infinite ammo if you have above 100 armour (thought this comes in the second half of the campaign).\t\t\t\t\t
