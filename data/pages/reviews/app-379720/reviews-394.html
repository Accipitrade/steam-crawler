Have you ever wanted to shoot chicken in space while also eating drumsticks and burgers while doing so? Then this game is for you, this and every other chicken invaders games are easily one of the best space invaders clones/parodies you can find, this one is also seemingly the hardest of the 5 (if you don't count universe since that game is more of a spinoff), the food isn't the only collectible in this game though, you can also collect coins (small (bronze), medium (silver) and large (golden), the smaller ones give you 100 points, medium ones give you 1,000 and the large ones give you 10,000, but that's not all, you can also collect various power ups in this game (they are basically presented into these differently colored present boxes), the ones you'll be able to find are the ones that follow:

- Red ones: Ion Blaster; It's one of the weaker weapons in the game but the wide range it gives you is definitely useful for stuff like purple comets, asteroids, chests and certain chicken waves that are probably useful for the Ion Blaster
- Green ones: Neutron gun; It's one of the strongest weapons in the chicken invaders franchise period, the range is not like the Ion Blaster but the damage it gives is definitely insane, it's the best weapon when you use it against pretty much all of the boss fights this game has to offer, especially the yolk star
- Orange ones: Laser Cannon; It's also pretty useful just like the neutron gun, it shoots instantly and as the name suggests, it's basically like a laser of some sort, I will say though, you should only get this weapon when you're at least around 4fp or 5fp because the weaker versions are really weak and not at all useful
- Light blue ones: Lightning Fryer; It's objectively one of the first weapons you'll use in this game, on the prolouge and it also start at 10fp, you will use it to murder the big chicken, and then when the game truly starts, you'll sadly start with a 1fp Ion Blaster, it's a really good weapon when locking onto the chickens and stuff, especially when you reach max power with 20fp where you'll get three lightnings instead of two.
- Dark Blue ones: Vulcan Chaingun; Sadly probably one of the worst weapons in the game, it sure is fast but no matter what, the weapon is weak as ♥♥♥♥ and really unless you're aiming to get more points with gift collecting, you should probably just try to avoid it, as it's not really all too helpful, the fourth and the fifth entry have their own versions of the Vulcan Chaingun and are in my opinion basically better.
- Pink ones: Plasma Rifle; This weapon is pretty strong, it almost insta kills all of the chicken at max power no matter what, but when using it, the cons are very obvious, it overheats faster then it should, meaning that you have to use the weapon very carefully, chicken invaders 4 also has a different version of the weapon that is pretty much better and overheats less.
- Black ones: Utensil Poker; What do you get when you combine the Ion Blaster's wideness and Neutron Gun's straightness? You get this wonderful weapon, it's definitely not as wide as the Ion Blaster but since the forks are almost as powerful as the neutron gun, it basically does not even matter, this is also a secret weapon too that you can only unlock if you beat the game twice in the rookie difficulty, though you can still use it without being an unlockable, yeah, on the final boss of the game.

Now we're gonna rate them all, warning this is pretty subjective so if you happen to have your own list, rate it in the comments, if this review gets found anyway:

Vulcan Chaingun: 2/10
Plasma Rifle: 5.5/10
Ion Blaster: 6/10
Lightning Fryer: 7/10
Laser Cannon: 8.25/10
Neutron gun: 9.5/10
Utensil Poker: 9.9/10

Now let's get on with the graphics, they are absolutely breathtaking, and I mean it, you might not find anything special about them or maybe won't even care since you're pretty much just shooting a bunch of chicken in space, but if you stop and look, you'll notice how detailed this game's graphics really are, from what I've heard there was barely any CGI or 3D used to make these and that they were all drawn, a lot of 3D touches have been placed in as well, such as how the meteors are animated, the way the chests were drawn, the amount of ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ directions the muller was drawn (the spaceship you control) and so on, and yes this was all for the most part made by a single person.

Now let's go onto the boss fights:

First of all, the big chicken:

All they really do is just move around and shoot eggs, they basically function like normal chicken only way bigger and definitely have way more HP, you'll fight the first one on the prolouge, then you will fight the duo of them, and then lastly you'll fight four of them, but don't worry they are not really that difficult, just don't bring an ion blaster or a vulcan chaingun and it's all gonna be alright.

Mother Hen Ship (Blast from the Past):

For those who did not play chicken invaders 2, let me explain real quick, this boss fight was basically the final boss from chicken invaders 2, but no worries he was not at all easy in chicken invaders 2, the final phase that is, all he does in chicken invaders 3 though is just shoot lasers and move a little bit and that's it, he is probably the easiest one to defeat.

The big chicken that wear a lot of coats (it gets cold in space):

Definitely one of the funnier bosses in the game, I mean hey, who hasn't laughed at this boss fight when they were a kid, but of course he isn't really that hard, you can easily avoid the eggs he shoots across the screen, certainly not something as unbased as touhou project.

The Giant Metallic Egg:

This boss fight is pretty easy once you know what you're doing, but that doesn't mean that he isn't cheap, because he is, when you encounter him for the first time, you'll probably just try to shoot him down as quickly as possible, but then he just ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ launches a cross like laser that basically cheap shots you without almost any warning, other then that he is a pretty easy boss fight, just always make sure you're in the right corners before he attacks.

The UFO unidentified chicken objects:

If you don't count the prologue, it's pretty much the first boss you'll fight in the game, you fight him 4 times in the game, each time he brings something new to the table, like the first time he'll only have those 2 green lasers and that laughable red laser that never properly aims at you, the second one has those pink lasers and again, the red laser, then he'll have those egg cannons that get wider each shot (you can defeat him easily if you just stay in a single spot) and then the final one is fairly tough if you panic a lot.

And finally, huge spoilers, the yolk star himself:

He is a pretty hard boss fight, especially if you panic a lot, those lasers he has shoot rapidly and always shoot at different spots, so you'll have to move a lot, it's also panicking when those terminator chicken come into the play and you have to avoid them as well at all cost, when you finally destroy the yolk star's ship, you basically just fight a remaining of him as a huge yolk, as long as you don't always stay on the lower path he'll never get you.


Now for the sounds: better then chicken invaders 2, no needed details.

The entire game is pretty great and I think you should give it a try, it has a big impact on myself and is basically a huge part of my youth, play it at least once, you won't regret it, I rate it 8/10 IGN, Good job iA, and thank you for making all of these wonderful space invaders clones that we all have come to love.\t\t\t\t\t
