Description: Everything has gone to Hell on Mars, quite literally. Demons infest everywhere and threaten to destroy all that is good. The only way to stop them is to RIP and TEAR until there's nothing left. Let nothing stand in your way!

Pros:

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't think there's anything I can say that probably hasn't already been said a million times, but hell, let's try.

Great music, graphics, environment, art, weapons, upgrades, level design, enemies, action, good game length, etc. Well paced, difficulty scales well with your upgrades and just a little ahead perhaps to make sure the action and violence intensifies and keeps you on your toes. Far too many games give you upgrades and turn you into a god stepping on bugs by the end of the game but Doom does a good enough job avoiding that (with at least one weapon/rune exploit I found myself avoiding so as to not spoil the game experience).

However, Doom is never an easy game but never frustratingly difficult either but instead rewarding at all times. I suppose if I had to complain I wish there were more boss fights and they were a little more original. Entertaining definitely, but strategically easier than the numerous battles you are faced with in every level.

Doom broke new ground 25 years ago and that was awesome. This game doesn't, but it doesn't need to. If you are expecting a new game to reinvent the FPS genre you are looking in the wrong place, and frankly, you may be looking indefinitely. Doom needs to live up to modern expectations of an FPS and do what it did 25 years ago and do it very well. This new Doom does just that, and that's all it needs to do.

Overall, fantastic, and I can definitely see myself coming back to play it again. Not that there is more for me to discover, but because it was just that much fun. There's not a ton of games I'll play twice, but I'm sure Doom is among them.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had first played this game on my PS4 last year and loved it. When I transitioned to PC I was always wanting DOOM for the PC, but never got around to it. When I finally picked it up I could never go back to playing it with a controller. With it's tight controls and fast paced gameplay makes this game one of the best FPS games I've ever played. The sound track is killer with it's metal music screaming as you slay demons with shotguns, RPGs, Plasma Rifles and much more! However, the multiplayer is lacking a lot. It feels like Quake and Call of Duty had a baby, and it didn't turn out so good. I'd say don't play the multiplayer only if you're really bored. Another feature is the Snapmap part of the game. The community makes maps that you can either play or make your own. But there are limitations to this, modding is not allowed and getting around making maps takes a little too long, but can be fun when you get the hang of it. With it's arcade feature allows you to play even more campaign after the game is over. It is like a speedrun with having a point system, it wants you to kill demons the best ways like glory killing them or using an explosive barrel to take out a group of demons. Even when you've done that you're not done with it yet, if you got the secrets by the old levels in levels activated by levers in the main campaign allows you to play old maps and levels from the first Doom and Doom II. There are some things you need to check on when running this game. If you have an older graphics card or if you see your frames being in the 30s or lower than that, I recommend switching from "Open GL 3.5" to "Vulkan" in the advanced settings. This can change the game from being stuttery to a much smoother game. I would recommend AMD users to use this setting while playing.
Overall this shooter is amazing to play. The controls are tight the game is a blast and even though it has it's downfall in multiplayer and snapmap, the game is amazing otherwise. The variety of weapons demons to kill and it's after campaign content is makes an amazing game. Doom 2016 gets a 9.5/10 from me and I hope Doom Eternal turns out as well as this game did.

-Cosmos\t\t\t\t\t
