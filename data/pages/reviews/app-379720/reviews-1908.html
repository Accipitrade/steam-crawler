\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest FPS since HL2 imo. I've been looking for an AAA game like this for a very long time - one that isn't scared of butchering as the main goal of the game. You're thrown into action immediately, and you're in it till the game ends. There's a large variety in types of guns and enemies, and thanks to well constructed gameplay loop, you learn which gun works best on which enemy, furthermore leading you to make really cool "combos". I love the fact that it's one of very few games that made me feel encouraged to use all types of guns throughout the game. Sure - I've used some more often than the others but still - it wasn't a level of useless pistols in CoD.

I also liked the weapons improvement system which was easy and made real changes to your guns power. What I definitely did not like, was a skill tree. I gave away points randomly without carrying much. It was useless to me tbh.

Great game with aggressive gameplay, just what I was looking for. Hope to see more FPS games going that way instead of CoD/Batllefield way.\t\t\t\t\t
