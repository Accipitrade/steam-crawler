\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has everthing that you would expect there to be, fast paced, in your face, and gore galore. If you are thinking about getting it, do it, you won't regret it. The variety of demons and guns is more than enough for you to either experiment and find out what guns work best against what demons or just go in literally guns blazing. Lets get a rundown of some of the features you get with this game.

1.The Campaign
First things firts, you can't have a game like doom without a campaign, although I would not recommend this game if you are buying it for the story because its basically non-exsistent. You play as DoomGuy, an unknown yet deadly marine that chooses guns over words and was born to kick demon ass. You wake up on a UAC facility on mars, where they are researching ways to use argent energy from hell to solve the resource crisis back on earth. Instead of solving their problems, this unleashes a deadly swarm of demons from hell upon the facility and it is your job to find out whats going on while kicking ass. Other than the generic story, the gameplay is good enough to keep you occupied for quite a while, combined with the gunplay and badass music this is one hell of a ride. If you're a completionist, this is a collectable wonderland filled with dolls that unlock 3D models of the characters and levers that unlock secret levels from the original Doom and Doom II. The graphics are beautiful and the audio is ear candy.

2. The Multiplayer
If you like killing people more than demons, than fear not because you got multiplayer. Others might say that the multiplayer is leaning more towards a Halo like experience which is parcially right. The multiplayer is as fast paced as the campaign and is smooth like butter. There are 4 playlists to choose from; Team Play: a 6v6 where you battle in a variety of game modes, Free-For-All Play: a 1vAll game mode where its kill or be killed, Team Deathmatch: you get the idea, and Deathmatch: where all you have to do is kill everyone. With every multiplayer you have a class system. with the class system you can create a unique loudout with two weapons and a grenade. The weapons in multiplayer are more varied than the campaign, you still have the original weapons but you get 4 more weapons. Along with the class system you can customize your weapons to your liking. This system is somthing simillar to Black Ops 3's customization system, but you have more options like color opacity and weapon damage. this multiplayer mode will keep you sated until Quake Champions releases.

3. SnapMap
SnapMap is a Forge from Halo type mode where you can play other peoples created maps or if you feel like it you can create your own. This mode is the icing on the cake and completes this game. Some of these maps are very good and have a unique taste to it. This is a creative minds playground where you can build tower defence type games, survival games, hell you can make a harvest moon game (and yes that exsists).

Doom is a great game if you just want to lay back and kill some demons. It combines the old with the new and suprisingly in 2017 doesn't have microtransactions. if you got spare change in your wallet, pick this game up, I'm sure you wont regret it\t\t\t\t\t
