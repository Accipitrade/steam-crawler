\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo be honest, I am simply writing this review for a Steam Nomination Badge.

So I've nominated this game for The "Whoooaaaaaaa, Dude!" Award.

Now the reasons why this game are great are several.. The nostalgia of playing Doom after 20 years of doom was #1 personally!

For others who maybe newer to Doom, the single player campaign is simply the best single person fps campaign game I've played to date. Barr none, the graphics, story line, oh guns :), and other characters (BIG BOSSES!!!!) you meet are intriguing and help immerse you in the game. You seriuosly will not be able to play any other game once you open DOOM AND begin the campaign. It just commands such attention and there are moments that garner GREAT player satisfaction.

One moment I will never forget was finding the minigun! The gentle hum and rumble on my controller as it mowed down demons was just enough to put a tear in my eye... and a smile on my face (but not enough for me to nominate the game for the "tear in my eye" award.. I think I'll officially nominate Walking Dead in that category).

Final words.. This game is awesome. Wait for a steam sale.. and get it.. oh.. there's one on now! :)\t\t\t\t\t
