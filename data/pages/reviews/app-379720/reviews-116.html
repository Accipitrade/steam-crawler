\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly it wasn't as good as i had hoped it would be. I think this is largely due to the fact that i had audio issues such as enemy noise spam (even when there's only a few), weapons lacking punch and music cutting out (or not playing at all). This is unfortunate because the music is brilliant. The game also had a hard time booting up sometimes. Technical issues aside level design was also a bit convoluted at times with the way forward being unclear. I think metal hellsinger was better in this regard, because of its streamlined levels where the pace was always high. DOOM fell flat sometimes, when you're looking around a room for that one touchscreen you missed to open the way forward.

Combat was fun but enemies hit like a truck and without a dash it was hard to outmaneauvre them sometimes. The double jump is also pretty weak so falling from high ground could be a death sentence in some cases. Pair this with a buggy ledge grapple and you'll have some unfortunate deaths that feel pretty lame. I think doom eternal is better in this regard with its combination of a dash & grapple hook. Visuals were great, and i barely had any lag when sprinting through the large setpieces. The story was unfortunately pretty basic, but that's not why you play DOOM, you play it to slay some demons. Despite my complaints slaying demons was indeed visceral and exhilirating. so in the end it was still a mostly enjoyable experience.\t\t\t\t\t

There are various weapons which can be "fired" and which cause damage to enemies when fired in their direction, with the goal being to kill them, at which point they stop moving. You can also play this game over the internet with other players, but playing this way is different, as the story is unclear and there are different weapons which you mostly "fire" at people, rather than demons (though there is sometimes occasion to fire them at demons). Theoretically, you could play this game in a tent, but many conditions would have to be met and this could require equipment you may or may not have. A demon is a creature that is upset.\t\t\t\t\t
