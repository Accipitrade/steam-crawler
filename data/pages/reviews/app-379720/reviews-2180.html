DOOM 2016
with Doom 4 Id set the bar for high octane action fueled FPS. it truly captures the Spirit behind the First 2 Doom games and does them one better, the graphics are Decent and the Price is right at $20 though i would pay more for such an experience as this. the Single player is short but with replayability that extends the longevity of the game substantially. the gameplay is smooth, bugs are few but somethings may catch your eye. "story in a game is like story in a Porno, its expected to be there but its not that important" Doom keeps to this quote. the story is just a catalyst for the action, as that's all that Doom is about. The Vulkan API also allows it to be run on mid range PC's. i once had a R9 380 and got 60 FPS on it, and that isn't a high cost card. this game is really a Challenger for best Doom in the series and is certainly a worthy addition to the Series. overall 9.3/10 this game is awesome and a must get for any Doom fan and and FPS lover\t\t\t\t\t
