\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA really good Doom game, continues on with the legacy of the first two.

Singleplayer is really nice. Not story-driven nor does it have a straight level. There's no reloading, tons of secrets, and ways to make your weapons more stronger by simply killing more in a specific way.

Arcade Mode is just DOOM's singleplayer but with scores and such. You have to be faster than you normally are in this game mode as you get higher score multipliers by killing.

Multiplayer in DOOM is just simply not the absolute best. It looks nice, but tries to achieve the feel of how multiplayer felt back when Quake was released, and the feeling of modern multiplayer shooters. They don't mix very well.

A love letter to the modding community of Doom, Snapmap is an easy way for anyone that does not know how to mod to create the best of the best maps. You could make deathmatch maps, co-op maps, singleplayer maps. The only limit would be your imagination.

It's really hard to find an enjoyabale shooter. Every now and then, I'm sick of what Halo or Call of Duty has to offer. Before this came out, I resorted to playing Ultimate Doom and Doom II. I had no problem with this. It was a better game than what existed now.

Overall, a fantastic game made by a fantastic company. Thank you ID and ♥♥♥♥ you Bethesda.\t\t\t\t\t
