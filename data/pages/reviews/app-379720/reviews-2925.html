\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016 which we shall reffer to as Doom 4

Where to begin....ah thats right

DOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOM!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!

I only played the original game once but playing this was such a refresher compaired to so many other shooters out there.

Doom 4 went back to the base aspects that made the original fun, no more "atmosphere" no more silly ♥♥♥♥, just a gun, the MUTA ♥♥♥♥♥ DOOM SLAYAH, and non stop rip and tear!

The modern era mechanical touch was just what this game needed, I feel more like Doom guy chasing down demons as they run from me in terror when I managed to kill enough of them and overall the gameplay is so smooooooooooth.

The only problem I have with this game is that it does have some optimization issues, granted they are few and far between and at times random problems that might be just steam issues.

As a generally new Dev to the industry myself I can say with a degree of familiarity, occasional frame rate loss can be the result of the memory cash not clearing out fast enough due to VFX LOD overallocation, asset loading having issues due to unoptomized sections of the levels/environments, or steam's server communication just being a pain again.

My overall opinion on this game?

BUY NAOW!! MUTHA ♥♥♥♥♥ DUUUUM GUY!!! *cue themesong*\t\t\t\t\t
