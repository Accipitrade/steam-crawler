\r\n\t\t\t\t\t9/10 Arguably the best game of 2016
Got a 20$ steam card and thought to give Doom a shot and it was worth the money. With its addicting gameplay, Constant fast-paced action, Insane graphics, And the Amazing optimization (Full vulkan support). Makes it one Hell of a game. (Also the music is just insane and totally off the charts). The only thing that's holding it back from being a perfect 10 is the multiplayer (which is dead and on launch was a little lackluster) and no progression to be made. Which should just be skipped all-together. But the snapmap feature is an Awesome addition and something i haven't seen in a AAA game nowadays, (including Scriptable AI!) and gives you full creative freedom. For 20$ you shouldn't have an excuse to not give this game a go (Other than not being able to run it). So go on and give it a try i swear you won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
