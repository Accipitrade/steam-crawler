\r\n\t\t\t\t\tId software doing what Id software does best. Mindless, gory, fast pace, intense and brutal fps action with a decent amount of exploration thrown in.

When I first heard reports of the multiplayer beta having loadouts and other elements of the bland, generic shooters that the industry churns out every few months I feared the worst. My fears were unfounded. The single player game, while a little short at some 9 hours, is an absolute blast from start to finish. No pausing behind cover to play out glorified turret sections every 100 yards, no cutscenes in your face every 2 minutes, no alpha charlie bravo in your ear constantly, no 2 weapon limit and regenerating health. Just you, a load of guns, sprawling multi tiered levels and a ton of hellspawn for you to shoot in the face.

If you enjoy the old school, run and gun shooters with huge weapon loadouts and no reloading then strap yourself in and enjoy the ride because you're going to love this. It's DOOM with a 2016 coat of paint. Bravo Id, keep it coming.\t\t\t\t\t
