\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast-paced, gory, intense FPS action. Heavy metal blasting in the background as you crush skulls and break bones. If these things sound even mildly interesting and enjoyable to you, DOOM is the game to play. A refreshing, loud and bold new take on the 1993 classic.

10/10 graphics (Great character, environmental, weapon and level designs.)

10/10 gameplay (Performing a Glory Kill is probably the most satisfying thing you will ever do in a video game.)

8/10 story and lore (Story wasn't quite memorable, but DOOM never was, isn't and never will be about the story anyway. The lore is quite interesting, but not forced upon those uninterested in it.)

11/10 soundtrack (May the headbanging commence. Absolute banger by Mick Gordon.)

One of the best FPS games in history (genre reinventing, you can say) and my personal favourite FPS game. Highly recommend.

"Rip and tear, until it is done."\t\t\t\t\t
