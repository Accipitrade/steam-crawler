\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't normally get crazy into FPS games (I'm not the best at them) but DOOM has one of the smoothest difficulty curves in a game I've ever played. The enemy variety is great, and despite some people's qualms with the Glory Kill system, I personally never got tired of it. The environments are richly crafted and the game rewards you for poking around a bit, which I appreciate! Despite a few slowdowns where I got a bit lost in the levels, the pacing of this game is pretty well crafted, balancing the big combat moments with smaller encounters and such. I can honestly recommend this game to anybody interested, even if FPS games are outside of your comfort zone. All caps no spaces! 9.0/10.\t\t\t\t\t
