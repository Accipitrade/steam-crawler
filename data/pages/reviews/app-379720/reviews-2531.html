\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not gonna lie, I almost returned the game after playing the first 1-2 hours. But I decided to stick with it, and it paid off.
At the beginning, the environments are a little repetitive, and the demons are kind of boring and simple, and you don't have much in terms of guns. Also, the fights are so short you barely get to hear the really cool soundtrack.

But then everything scales really nicely. Firefights get longer, weapon mods come in to allow for some switching it up, demons get bigger and meaner, and the adrenaline finally kicks in. The double jump boots make the gameplay a lot more fluid and hectic too.

If you're looking for any sort of half decent narrative, Doom is not it. But if you want to plain and simple kick some demon ass, with some great graphics, then Doom will scratch that itch nicely.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEver wonder what it would be like to RIP and tear you way through an infested martian outpost? If you have then this game will wholly fulfill your fantasy.

This game is an absolute thrill to play, the entire way through you will feel like a complete bad ass as you fling gore all over this desecrated martian outpost. The game mechanics aren't anything unique classic run and gun like any other FPS but the combat is amazing. Traditionall FPSs usally have you cowering in fear peaking corners to slowlly shoot at enemies. In DOOM you get right into the action, you confront enemies head on and you blow their brains out without a second thought. The hyper violence is incredibly satisfying and each time you tear through enemies you feel yourself craving more.

The arena type combat allows for some amazing fights where you often feel challanged but not to the point that your charachter feels weak. You feel as if you'll need to use all your skills but your power is enough to defeat anyone who comes into your path. The selection of weapons is refreshing as you will have many choices and will quickly choose favorites helping to influence your play style. Weapon upgrades allow you to boost the power of your most used weapons and the choice in features will allow for enhanced combat.

The soundtrack in this game is next level, it continually pushed you forward as you terroize any demons who cross your path. The intensity of the music drives you forward and really puts you in the mood. It's really beautiflly done and you never feel tired of hearing that music pickup as you enter into yet another battle arena.

The lenght of this game may lead some to think you will grow tired of the same battle royale arean type matches over and over. However the game manages to keep things fresh each time and you feel challenged as you progress through even with upgradding your weapons. The weapon upgrades are ballanced so that your guns feel more powerfull but you do not feel unstoppable by the end of the game.

The story is a bit wattery and to be honest was not my main driving force through the game. I did not have a major attachment to any of the charachters and the ending wasn't all that suprising to me. I felt like the story added context to your rampage but it really was secondary to the games combat. It certainlly wasn't the worst story in any game but it is easilly overshadowed by the rest of the game.

Overall I really enjoyed my time in this game and have recomended it strongly to friends. I even went so far as to purcahse 2 additonall copies whcih I passed on to two of my friends. This game hits all the right spots for me and so I easilly can give it a 9/10.

TL;DR Blasting, Slicing, Stomping, Squishing, Stabbing, enemies is extremelly satisfying and will continuelly keep you wanting more. Story is secondary to the refreshing arena combat that never seems to grow stale. Weapon upgrades are balanced and makes you feel like you are making progress. This game easilly gets a 9/10 from me.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, i don't take the time to review games very often, if at all (can't remember).
But this is in my honest opinion a masterpiece, they nailed it on how "DOOM" was supposed to look in modern times.

I play the game on Ultra-Violence (wich is 'Hard' mode if you want), and it's very challenging, the pace of the game is meant to be very fast, as i learned a bit later in the game where you see there's rooms where you clearly enter the area and you're like "Ok this is quite the arena (Quake style) and there's gonna be alot of action here whenever i press that switch"

I need to take break every few minutes as this thing is rushing me with adrenaline (no kidding, it makes my heart beat really fast) and i yell every now-and-then "Aah ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥! This game is craaaaaazy!"

Awesome game, awesome gameplay, awesome graphics, awesome sound -- All awesome.

If you're a old-school shooter fan, this one here is a beast.
Quake players (like i used to be back in the days) will feel right at home, control the map, keep moving, be aware of where your powerups/ammos/med kits/armors are, and you'll be victorious.

And please, dont play this game on easy, this is meant to be hard, like demon tearing you in pieces hard - At least Ultra-Violence
