\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeautiful and faithful reimagining of the Doom setting, everywhere you turn you recognize set pieces from the original games. A snow level was the only atmospheric misstep in my opinion (Yes there's can be snow on Mars, but no I don't want to see it), otherwise Mars, Hell, and the UAC base were all gorgeously realized. Monster design and mechanics were amazing and most of the classics make memorable appearances. Only boss battle health bars seemed jarringly out of place. Sound design and the pulse pounding soundtrack were excellent. Movement and combat is fast and fluid. Shooting feels great and customization/progression elements are very satisfying. The pace is perfect and the story is mostly enthralling... only the immediate ending was a letdown. A nearly perfect game, and it truly does feel like DOOM!\t\t\t\t\t
