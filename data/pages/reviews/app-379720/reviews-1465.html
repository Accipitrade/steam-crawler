\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot sure why someone would look at this review when this game has been out for so long, but here we go.

Secrets and explorable things unlock actual game upgrades. Almost everything you do in the game unlocks upgrades in a fun and very organic way. There are so many fun secrets and explorable things that unlock things it will turn you into someone who runs around trying to find everything. And if you can't find everything?

That's fine. Finding half the stuff in the game is enough to superpower you and make you a murderbot. Since you spend your rewards for finding secrets at your own discretion you can create the build that works for you. Got an affinity towards just offense? get extra ammo or the superpower that increases damage. Got a propensity towards collecting drops? Grab the superpower that lets you vacuum stuff in from afar. Like zooming in for headshots? There's a weapon that levels up when you get 50 headshots.

All those superpowers are good but they increase in value after activating their super super power requirement which makes them even more powerful.

All these things feel good to do in addition to the great storyline. Give this game a try, then pick up Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
