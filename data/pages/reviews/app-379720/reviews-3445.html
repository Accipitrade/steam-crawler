DOOM - 666/10

This game is HELL fun ! having only played 6hrs of single player and a good amount of the open beta MP modes, it is still new.. but as they say first impressions last. From the first moment playing the campaign I was as excited as I can remember playing any game, including the original Doom. As revolutionary as it was, this game feels even more special. Words don't do justice the feeling playing as Doom guy.. if the price tag is steep for now, surely there will be a demo of sorts for you to try for yourself, soon. Whether you care about the genre, or Doom at all, I urge everyone to give it a try ! personally it is a bargain, I can see myself putting countless hours into playing SP through on ultra violence, playing many of the default MP modes, and custom creations, along with learning SnapMap and making something myself ! good times.

id games have always been the forefront of 3D engine technology amd innovation, sometimes with a disappointing game but a fantastic engine. DOOM is certainly a big deal in engine development. A new key moment in 3D gaming evolution ? id tech 6 is a masterpiece of coding and design. Scalable as hell with megatexture support, it will take future hardware to push its limits. This new engine is the result of generation upon generation of 3D engine design, to the point of immense power without design flaws often the result of coding tradeoffs.

SnapMap is a revolution in content creation, other games licensing id tech 6 will have an all powerful platform of both next-gen engine code, along with a system to allow flexible, complex and deep user created content in a closed source system to avoid the range of problems associated with the previous open content systems. Countless other games suffer from the same problems with the open source mod structure. SnapMap is the new standard for mods. With promised ongoing improvements and support for mod development within the SnapMap system, limitations will be negligible.

The big question for many is what about the gameplay ? how much fun is the game that first runs on the new engine ? Going back to their roots, while embracing some accepted modern standards and flair, id have hit gold with the new DOOM. Clearly a lot of passion has gone into the campaign experience, the feel, the atmosphere of the single player mode.. recreating DOOM in the modern landscape is a huge task, they have done well. For any negative comments about not being the same as the original DOOM, that is not the point. The graphics updates available on the original DOOM game is there for that. The new version had to be a step forward, while retaining a lot of the feel, and of course the lore. The original Doom created a unique, new, epic sensation when playing, full of OMG moments. Gameplay mechanics are not the only ingredient to creating a new defining moment in 3d shooter evolution, but they are very important. The new DOOM has just enough of the old, combined with just enough of the new.

Expect a NEW game, with the unmistakeable feel and intensity DOOM exploded into the world with, so many years ago. Get to know it as it is, don't try to put specific expectations on gameplay or story based on an old, at the time revolutionary product. Play, learn, and understand all that there is, and see how the game makes you feel, how it challenges you, and how much fun you have as time goes on. It is the only fair way to judge this new beginning.. to me it deserves 10/10. I cannot complain in any way, it lives up to the hype and all I hoped for, in many cases exceeding what I could have hoped it could be ! just remember to let the old Doom go, come in with open mind and as a noob.. when you have played a ton of hours and kick ass at the game, then it is fair to judge it. Coming in and judging it for how it compares to the old Doom or other shooters is putting far too much emphasis on minor or unimportant factors. Be a noob, take it in.. enjoy it !

First sitting I played 6 hours straight of the campaign. Yes, it's a lot fun ! confirmed.\t\t\t\t\t
