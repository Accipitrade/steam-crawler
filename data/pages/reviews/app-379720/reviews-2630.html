\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: Singleplayer is amazing. Story is very well done. Multiplayer is too much like CoD but it's still great.

This game is what CoD wishes it was. The singleplayer mode is probably better than any other singleplayer game I've ever played. This is including all of the Half-Life games.

The gameplay is amazing, but the part I respect the most is that the story is subtle. It's not something you just have to deal with like in CoD. It's not so involved that it annoys me, but it's there just enough to keep me interested in moving forward.

As for the multiplayer mode, it's a huge improvement in some ways and a disappointment in other ways. One thing I always hated about CoD was having to unlock the guns and make loadouts. That system doesn't even work well for CoD, let alone for Doom. At least in Doom the weapons are unique so it seems worth it, though. Other than that, it's a lot of fun. All the guns are pretty well balanced and I've never had problems with matchmaking. It's a great break from going balls to the wall in CS or OW :)

I kind of wish there were community servers, though. That would be really nice. Having a set of famillar people to play with was always nice and I'm sad that that setup has gotten replaced over the years.\t\t\t\t\t
