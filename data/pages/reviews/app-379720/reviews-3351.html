\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI would certainly recommend this game to anyone who used to play first person shooters in the 90's. It brings things back to a time when shooting things, making things explode, and gratuitous violence were mainstays of FPS gaming. While keeping a toe in the modern age with some exposition and story elements thrown in, the core gameplay is just mindless shooting and fun. If that's what you're looking for, DOOM is up your alley.

While the multiplayer is a little lackluster in comparison, DOOM has always been known for it's solid single-player. If you're buying this for its multiplayer thinking it's going to be COD or Battlefield, you might be a little disappointed. However, if you're also expecting an old-school arena shooter, you too might also be disappointed. The multiplayer is a hodge-podge of the two melded together as some sort of bastard love child.

Overall, I would recommend this game if you enjoyed the original DOOM games.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t[CAMPAIGN REVIEW]

This is the DOOM game I have been waiting for. Phenomenal in every way.

Graphics are downright amazing and runs very well. I played on a 980ti and i7 5820k at 1440p and it rarely dipped below 90fps. Looking forward to SLI support so I can run it even faster.

Gameplay is a real treat. It's incredibly tight and fast as hell. If you've played the originals, this game comes naturally and imploves upon its predecessors it every way. Glory kills (the melee finishing moves) are great. It doesn't slow the action down a bit and there's even incentive to do so. Guns are everything you'd come to expect in a DOOM game. They nailed the customization, a feature some worried would make it too modern and bloated. I feel it didn't in any way.

Story is surprisingly good. You don't really think of expostition when it comes to DOOM, but they delivered a well made plot.

All and all, this is a fantastic shooter that truly brings back an immense amount of nostalgia in a new violent way. This game had me smiling all throughout while on the edge of my seat. It's downright fun as hell and feels good to be back in Hell.

10/10, would recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
