\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game rules. I've been playing id's D00M and Quake series since the beginning, and this game is the culmination of all that good stuff. The graphics are awesome, the gameplay is super fun and just the right amount of challenging to keep you engaged and not frustrated, and the audio is incredible (in particular the soundtrack). If you like fast-paced first-person shooters with lots of action, jumping, lots of guns, and lots of enemies, you can't go wrong with D00M 2016.

This is quite the departure from D00M 3, which I consider more of a horror game than a straight-up action shooter like its predecessors. D00M 2016 definitely sees the series return to its roots. Now, I thought D00M 3 was insanely good and one of the scariest games I have ever played, but like I said before, it went in more of an almost survival horror direction and focused less on the mindless slaying of endless hordes of baddies. D00M 2016 is the latter. Right from square one you are crushing skulls and bathing in gore. Rip n' Tear!

The story of the game is actually pretty cool and interesting, something I wasn't really expecting to say about this game, but they definitely went there and I think they pulled it off. Intriguing characters, some of the plot elements you can see coming from miles away, but when they happen you are still like "holy sh*t" lol. The atmosphere and environments are insanely awesome too. Looking out the windows of the various Mars stations and seeing the vast installations stretched before you, demons running amok, Argent energy blasting into a portal in the sky - it's really quite breathtaking. They also did a fantastic job on Hell in this game. It literally looks like what you would think Hell would look like lol. Awesome job.

All in all, I enjoyed the "hell" out of this game. Would highly recommend if you are an FPS fan or a fan of the D00M series, or just a fan of cool action games in general. The graphics, gameplay, soundtrack, atmosphere, everything are all solid. Can't wait to try D00M Eternal!\t\t\t\t\t
