\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThough it is fun no doubt ...

And what is up with the black screen. Can't even play the game properly ,

It may seem ridiculous to mention that not being able to sprint makes the game less fun but that is what made the older previous Doom games fun . The ability to go fast and blast your way through your enemies . The ability to dodge and zig-zag around your enemies made it super fun .

This isn't the older games so you can't compare as it this is a modern take on Doom but it does make gameplay very boring and unexciting .

But sadly , the big part about Doom is its satisfying puzzles . In the previous Doom games the puzzles had a certain flow to them and had a silky smooth flow to it and was fun to figure out . In this Doom game , the puzzles feel uncreative and tedious , the flow is broken multiple times and it just lacks the fun it should have in completing a puzzle .

The reimagined enemies aren't very memorable . They don't look very distinctive from each other and there's nothing good or bad . They are OK .

Back to the metal soundtrack , it compliments well with Doomguy's anger but it gets old fast and painful to listen to as it goes on and on in repeat .

The glory kills are cool but gets old fast . It makes the gameplay EXTREMELY linear and streamlined , you shoot a few times and then glory kill the enemy . Rinse and repeat . If you don't you will be punished for it , you will easily lose health and ammo fast as enemies drop health and ammo when killed by a glory kill . It will be cool to look at the glory kill a few times though but soon it gets repetitive .

And... dealing with unfixed things in-game , within only in an hour of the game when Doomguy falls out of the map , he falls through the map awkwardly in this weird buggy way which hasn't been fixed . With his body lying flat on the ground as he falls and you can actually see the map from the bottom view . Why hasn't something as simple as this been fixed ? I don't know.

It is crazy how linear this game is , what made Doom fun was you being able to run around exploring the map . And exploring the map fast ... Now you have to clear a specific area before moving on to the other . This utterly destroys the freedom you feel in the previous Doom games .

It is clear how much this game desperately wants to please the newcomers to the Doom franchise by making it a sterotypical FPS but it forget its history in what made it special therefore destroying what made it such a memorable franchise .

There are some cool stuff like upgrading your weapons . There quite a bit of positives but its overshadowed by so many of its negatives . The positives such as pretty graphics , also the cinematic is pretty as well to look at . The music is great but it gets very repetitive though as said before. There's more positives to mention but right now , lets focus on the big elephant in the room which is its negatives , hence why this review is negative as this was the main purpose to address this .

This game is a mixed bag in summary as it still delivers in its own right but it doesn't capture what made Doom , well Doom .

This feel like a generic first person shooter . If you play the older Doom games , the spirit of Doom in this game isn't here clearly . People liked Doom because it was unique , this game doesn't set itself apart from the likes of Serious Sam or Duke Nukem in this modern age . Yes there are differences in Doom 2016 compared to other titles but not as much as before and not as much as it should ... You mainly shoot and glory kill to loud blasting music which is fine but the previous Doom titles had much more to offer than just that .

What were they thinking ...
But it still is a decent enough game but all in all it isn't exactly as fun as the older Doom titles .\t\t\t\t\t
