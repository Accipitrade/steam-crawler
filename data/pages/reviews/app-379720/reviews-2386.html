\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start out by saying this game is pretty bad ass. It's an action packed first person shooter with guts and gore galore. They even kept some aspects of the original doom games in this new and defined version, which i loved; the levers you use to open up doors for you to explore the old doom maps, ect..

I was going to give this game a postitive review until i ran into a bit of a game breaking bug/problem today which has happened to numerous people that own this game, and the fact that this game was made in 2016 and it's 2018 and this is not fixed is appalling. I was on mission 11 fighting the last 2 bosses when my computer suddenly says it has to restart because it ran into a problem. Ok, so my PC restarts i fire Doom backup and it says that my save file is corrupt. So pretty much i have to restart the whole game over after plugging in about 20 hours in this game. This hasn't happened to everyone, but it's happened to enough people so the devs should do something about it. Thumbs down.

Pros:

- Visuals and graphics are great
- Intense gameplay
- Guides and index
- Climbing
- Challenges
- Many weapons

Cons:

- Sound is too low
- No manual saving
- Health and ammo respawns making it too easy
- Getting stuck in doorways
- Frame drops
- Big game breaking bug with corrupt files\t\t\t\t\t
