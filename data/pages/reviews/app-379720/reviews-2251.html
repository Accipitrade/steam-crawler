\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR; Buy this game.

I couldn't be more satisfied with what I got to experience for 20$. Doom's superb gun-play, fantastic level design, swift movement, and dynamic enemies make this a damn-near perfect game. ID's capability of maintaining the core mechanics and nostalgic aspects of the original Doom without feeling repetitive and aged is more than commendable. Any FPS player *needs* to buy and play this game asap.

As for the more negative criticisms: the singleplayer's plot is lackluster in some areas, the first hour of gameplay does not do service to the rest of the game and is noticably slow, and the multiplayer is, in all honesty, a worse version of Quake Champions. However, it's hard to hold these faults against Doom as a whole considering how insignificant they compare to what Doom does perfectly.

For an Added Bonus, the SnapMap feature is impeccable and exponentially amplifies this game's replayability, especially since, with the time that the community has already spent creating, it is already full of community featured maps that give way to awesome Halo-esk custom game nights with friends. And the whole thing is only 20$ when I would pay over 60$ in a heartbeat.\t\t\t\t\t
