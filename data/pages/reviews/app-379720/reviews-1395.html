\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was amazing. I knew about the classic DOOM games prior to playing this, but I never had enough interest to actually go back and check them out, or appreciate how they revolutionized gaming, but this game drove me to do that.

The most apt way to describe this game is that it's pure testosterone, start to finish. The gameplay is extremely fast paced and keeps you on your toes, there is a variety of weapons to choose from so the combat never gets boring, the character designs for the demons are all fantastic and super unique, and fully embrace the "demon from hell" aesthetic.

The story in this game is subtle, but not non-existent. It is there but doesn't impose itself enough to interfere with the gameplay. For people who are into lore, the codex in the game has longer, written entries that elaborate on the backstory in the game. You don't have to read them to finish the game, but for people who want them, they are there.

This game makes you feel like a complete badass. As much as reboots usually suck ass in film and video games, this game executes it flawlessly and perfectly honors the game that created its genre.\t\t\t\t\t
