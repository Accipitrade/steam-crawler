Overall: ◼◼◼◼◼
one of my favorite games of all time

Graphics
◼◼◼◼◻ pretty good, even at low settings

Gameplay
◼◼◼◼◼ i wrote an english paper on it I got an A

Audio (SFX)
◼◼◼◼◻ nothing jaw-dropping, but pretty nice overall (and kills and gunshots are super satisfying)

Music
◼◼◼◼◼ fits perfectly, gets you right in the mood to smash some skulls

Audience
◼◼◻◻◻ dont show your parents

PC Requirements
◼◼◼◻◻ you cant run it on a potato, but like, it runs acceptably on lower-end rigs

Difficulity
-variable- anywhere between harder Dark Souls (ultra-nightmare) to like fallout easy mode

Grind
◻◼◼◼◼ theres not really anything to grind for... (unless you're getting all the collectables)

Story
◼◼◼◻◻ whats there is ok, i guess, but as the main character doesn't give a crap, neither should you.

Game Time
◼◼◼◼◻ the only thing i ask for is MORE, MORE, MORE!!! (its pretty good, above 20H on normal, and great replay-ability b/c of the higher difficulties)

Bugs
◻◼◼◼◼ barely noticeable during normal play

Workshop integration
~◼◼◼◼◻~ well its not really steam workshop, but whats there is pretty good

Collectables
◼◼◼◼◻ don't give much (if at all) advantageous stuff, and usually arent hard to find (also give you cool stuff to look at)

extras:

◼◼◼◼◻ map maker: pretty expansive, but there are a few things lacking
◼◼◻◻◻ multiplayer: its just quake, but doom (not super amazing or revolutionary)
◼◼◼◻◻ co-op: probably fun with friends, but i have not been able to test it 😥\t\t\t\t\t

As a simple shooter, you can spend your time walking around ending up necessarily in one of the numerous arenas where you have to kill, cut, destroy, smash or whatever, any demon like you see. You have many gears to do that and they are all more or less useful given the enemy. The graphics are rather nice and the textures simple without being ugly. The game runs nicely at high FPS with a 1080 Ti but I am sure this is also a case with a middle-end card. In the end, give it a try few hours and make you own opinion. Cheers.\t\t\t\t\t
