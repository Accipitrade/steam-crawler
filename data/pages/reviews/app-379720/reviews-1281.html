\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are reading these reviews to decide whether or not to get this game or DOOM Eternal then you should know that this game is easily comparable to Eternal and even somewhat better in certain aspects. It is a game I have always been able to come back to and replay even 4 years after release and still have as much fun as the first time I played it. It is a visually stunning, high speed, action packed game that is a perfect fit for the DOOM series. While definitely not as plot heavy as Eternal, DOOM 2016 is able to perfectly blend exciting and energetic gameplay with just enough story to keep things interesting and cohesive (not to mention a little mysterious).
The game controls incredibly well and I personally love the freedom you have when taking on hordes of demons of being able to choose exactly where and how you want to take them down.
The art direction, aesthetic, and general feel of this game is absolutely phenomenal! Whether it be the designs of the environments, enemies, weapons, or anything else, this game always manages to hit the proverbial nail right on the head! Every level, even ones that take place in the same settings like the earlier UAC missions manage to provide a familiar yet fresh experience and feel even when played right after one another.
Also, this may not be a very important aspect to you depending on how you like to play/choose your games but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ this soundtrack is amazing and I don't think I could be doing this game any justice in a review without mentioning it! Mick Gordon did an exceptional job on both the soundtrack for this game and Eternal and while it is unfortunate that he will not be working on music for DOOM games in the future, I am really grateful that he left us with such incredible soundtracks that haven't grown old even after almost 4 and a half years of listening.

TL;DR
Get this game, and for the hell of it get Eternal too!
You wont regret it!\t\t\t\t\t
