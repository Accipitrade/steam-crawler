\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat value for $5. Almost feel bad for not paying full price. Almost.

Doom looks incredible and the game-play reminded me of Quake 3 with the various power-ups. It is fast and frenetic and a lot of fun. And totally evil.

There is so much variety there was never a dull moment. I like how the limited ammo in the start of the game forces you to use the different weapons to get familiar with them. Each weapon has two up-gradable alternate firing modes, including challenges you have to complete with them. Same with the Runes which augment your combat ability. Fun to try various combinations. Usually in a game like this I won't like a few of the weapons, but in Doom they are all satisfying to use. I love the gatling-gun turret thing.

Sometimes I get bored with a FPS because the bad guys are generic or similar. Doom is the opposite and there are plenty of unique demons to fight. And the models and animations look terrific. Again, so evil.

The levels look amazing, the lighting is varied with all sorts of hints of color and I stopped to take a lot of screenshots. I also like how the game uses green lights within the environment to hint at the path forward.

A couple things I didn't like:
- No quick save ability
- Environment sounds were underwhelming. I play without the music on and sometimes the levels were almost completely quiet.
- The main menu is a store-front for other content. Lame-o.

Highly recommend if you like Doom, Satan, or bloody meat chunks.\t\t\t\t\t

DOOM
The first half an hour of gameplay will most likely leave you speechless in the best way possible. Visually DOOM is absolutely stunning. Environment art is also exceptional (in a good way). Gameplay felt mostly pretty smooth, there definitely were multiple occasions where i just kept getting stuck on stairs and just overall random stuff that definitely did frustrate me a bit. Sometimes i also fell through the map when fighting the residents of the underworld (which also irritated me). Like don't get me wrong, the gameplay is actually phenomenal and there is nothing bad to really say about it, except some minor inconsistencies with clipping. Story, i think we can all agree, i didn't care about at all, it honestly just felt like a distraction from the bloodbath. Overall the whole game is absolutely magnificent and different, in my opinion, as there really is nothing similiar available on the market at the moment. Of course i can't forget to mention, that this game is surprisingly good at promoting christian values.
