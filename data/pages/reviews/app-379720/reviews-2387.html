Its not going to win any awards for "best story line" but thats not what Doom is about. Its about walking through the depths of hell ripping appart any demon that gets in your way. I envy anyone who has never played this game before because I would pay good money to experiance this campaign again for the very first time its that good.

Multiplayer is here but its not the main focus. Can be fun but nothing compared to the current Quake

Snapmap is a way you can play other peoples creations in the doom universe. Custom levels and game modes can keep you entertained for hours.

all in all I would pay AAA money just for the Campaign alone. If you like FPS campaigns there isnt many that come close to this.\t\t\t\t\t
