\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest PvE shooter I've ever played. Story mode is surprisingly well-done, arcade mode is an absolute blast.

Gunplay is close to perfect, and every weapon feels great. The enemy design, both aesthetic and on a gameplay level, is fantastic, and violently blowing them up and "glory-killing" them in different ways never gets less fun. The gore is satisfying, the sound design is orgasmic (though, if I did have one criticism, it'd be that occasionally some sound effects cut out or are oddly silent) and the soundtrack is PERFECTLY composed for the game.

Level design is some of the best I've seen in an FPS as well, with lots of replayability owed to the many secrets in each mission. And, as evidenced by the absolutely massive file size of the game, the textures and models all look phenomenal.

But above all, it's good to finally have a fun, Christian game that I can play with my kids. Gotta teach them to kill demons while they're young so it really sinks in.\t\t\t\t\t
