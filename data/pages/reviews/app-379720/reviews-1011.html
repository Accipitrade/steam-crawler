\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is absolutely amazing. I just finished it and it’s one of the best, if not the best, game I’ve ever played.
It’s an amazing FPS and definitely my #1 FPS game that I’ve played. It’s honestly just super fun running around the place killing demons brutally
I’ll list down some Pros and Cons

Pros:
In this game there are Glory Kills which are kind of like, cinematic kills.
Great storytelling, and isn’t too in your face about it.
The weapons in this game do tons of damage, and are really fun to use and slaughter demons with.
The gore is amazing. Probably one of the goriest games I’ve ever played, and it’s amazing.
Absolutely badass.

Cons:
No use of iron sights which can be annoying if you have a hard time aiming. But really, you don’t need them.
It does get quite hard near the mid-end point of the game, depending on what difficulty you’re on.
No cutscenes. It has a sort of Half Life way of storytelling where are the scenes are in first person perspective, but this is not a bad thing.
No mid battle checkpoints or anything, you always start right before the battle. But this is DOOM we’re talking about, so that doesn’t really matter.

Overall, despite the Pros and Cons I listed, this game is amazing. You have gotta play it. It’s probably the most badass game ever if you ask me.
To sum up this entire game:
Rip and tear, until it is done.
