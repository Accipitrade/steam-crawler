\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM.

I have loved this franchise since it begun, and to see it now is incredible.
This game is a lot of fun and is one of those games that you can go back to again and again.
DOOM Eternal may have the edge on most areas, but this version looks like that corrupted mars installation I remember from The Ultimate Doom.

Snapmap is incredible, and the music is one of the best game compositions i have ever heard, since games like Minecraft or Wipeout.

I got this game for £4.50 in a sale, but the standard price of £14 is so worth it.
In an age of pay-to-win, cover-based shooters, something that goes at you and doesn't stop is what I've been looking for. The return of DOOM is possibly one of the best game returns I have ever seen.

Id, you did great.\t\t\t\t\t
