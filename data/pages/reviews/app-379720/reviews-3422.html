\r\n\t\t\t\t\tObviously from my Avatar you know what my favorite series of all time is; given that I can either be a totally blind Fanboy or a stubborn nostalgic blowhard. I'm neither but maybe a little mix of the two combined, you be the judge. With that out of the way I have always been skeptical of how Doom was going to be back when it was announced 8 years ago because even though I love id software they haven't exactly been hitting it out of the park since probably Quake 3 Arena or Doom 3, Rage although underrated had it's glaring flaws in the Campaign.

Well about 1hr into the Campaign I can already tell you, this one is going to go out of the Ball Park. If this is your first FPS you have won the lotto if it isn't you are going to be treated to the best looking and playing FPS since the original Doom. If you like shooting hellspawn and lots of gore with an amazing soundtrack this is your game. Everything oozes polish here from all the detail in the textures, screams in the distance to the ambient sounds you can tell they put their heart and soul into this title and wasn't about to F-UP this iconic of iconic franchises.

I highly recommend this game and I still haven't even touched the two other major parts that are being offered. Multuplayer and SnapMap (level creation tool)\t\t\t\t\t
