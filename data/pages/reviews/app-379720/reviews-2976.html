\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBy far one of the most enjoyable single player campaigns I have had the pleasure to play. DOOM is an arcade shooter through and through, focused on run 'n gun rather than a cinematic experiance. It harkens back to the old days where carrying around all the guns in the game, and having a health bar along with constant pick-ups were the standard thing. That doesn't mean there isn't a story, there is one, however it isn't focused on it and because of that the game is much better for it. The upgrades in the game give every weapon a purpose throughout the entire game, making certain that each weapon is always useful in every situation (even the pistol is useful).

The only negative thing I could talk about for DOOM is the multiplayer. It's lackluster at best, taking almost none of the great ideas from the singleplayer campaign and implementing them. The MP plays more like CoD and/or Halo, so if that is your thing you'll probably enjoy it. I couldn't say how many people are constantly playing it, since I don't play it myself, so there might be issues finding a game.

Overall, I completely recommend this game for anyone who loves playing FPS games. At $20 dollars (writing this review during the steam sale) it is a STEAL, definitely worth picking up.\t\t\t\t\t
