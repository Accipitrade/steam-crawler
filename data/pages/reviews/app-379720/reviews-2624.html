\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaving played all the previous incarnations of DOOM all those years ago on pc then on the greystation. I can’t say I’m one of those who dislikes this version. I love it! It is fast paced and optimised very very well. I run it with all the bells and whistles on and get solid frames on an ageing i7 2600k GTX 980ti system @ 1440p. It looks and sounds amazing. It is a visceral heart pounding experience, visually it is an eye busting feast. The sound is crisp and full of depth the ambient sounds really immerse you. I really don’t think there is much in this game that you cannot and should not enjoy.

Haven’t delved into multiplayer but did finish the SP campaign and thoroughly enjoyed it, if you are in it for an intellectual plot and depth then look elsewhere but if you want hoards of demonic creatures to blow holes in then this is the game for you. I think it is faithful to the original DOOM to some degree but you don’t play it for that right? You wanna shred demons and feel overwhelmingly powerful. There is a nice addition to being able to unlock upgrades for weapons if that is your thing. Also there is a perk system there for those that like that sort of thing. I’m aware some people criticised this but I guess this is the way games are going these days. There is the usual Bestiary there too. I though t some of the boss fights were a bit naff and where more about systematic timing and jumping than anything.

I’m not sure I would do another play through, not because it is a bad game but I don’t usually do that unless a game offers me something that drives me too. Plus I just don’t have the time for it with so many games filling up in my library. However I could be tempted to do some COOP stuff, now that would be cool.

If this was my first experience of DOOM as it was all those years ago then I would have been blown away, as I was back then.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReview: DOOM ( 2016 )
Graphics:
The Game looks simply amazing and if you would ask me its one of the best current games graphics wise. Textures look sharp with amazing level design. Its huge that they were able to capture the original DOOM feeling with current gen graphics. The Characters / Enemies all look great and even better in glory kills ( close combat ). Overall i do not have anything else to add.
[]

Gameplay:
The Game is built like an older game which sometimes can go wrong but not with this one. No Regenerating health, no quest marks and a decent challange creates an amazing experience. Its one of the Games the more you play them the more you want to play them. Aiming is smooth as is movement and enemies are not easy to kill or immpossible to kill which is just flawless. Soundtrack is a straight A too.
[]

Story:
There will be no spoilers or not any major ones.
To be honest its basically a classic DOOM story, doom guy comes to earth or hell and kills everything, the end.
Might not sound amazing or life changing but you really do not play DOOM games for the Story.
Still the Story keeps you entertained.
[]

Performance:
The Game runs easily on High End PCs and Runs Great on the Nvidia GTX 760-AMD FX6300 so mid range PC will be able to play it easily. Probably doesnt run on Low End Laptops.

Multiplayer:
It is still active and in my opinion i had a lot of fun playing it.

Price:
The Game is extremely underrated for its price on key sides or on sales which is great for us.

I Just have to Recommend this game and have no idea why it did not win GOTY 2016\t\t\t\t\t
