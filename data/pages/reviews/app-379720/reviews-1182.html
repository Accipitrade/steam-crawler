Mick Gordon made this music PERFECT
it fits every mood in this game
PvP, I didn't play - I generally don't care about PvP the older I get.
Story, Oh man, this has got story, this game is more than worth getting
the gore levels are over the top, which I like
the guns feel like guns, except the Pistol (so glad this didnt make its way into Eternal)
Challenge mode is fun too, and the varying levels of difficulty mean anyone can enjoy this
easy is super easy, normal is easy, hard is normal, and then Ultra Nightmare, the only correct name of a difficulty in this game I can remember, cause it is all I will play on.

Atmosphere of this game is great, each area has its own unique vibe, the enemies repeat themselves with barely any new faces, so it gets a little repetitive.
This game is a linear FPS that has some exploration to each level, as well as a plethora of collectibles to find, including some levels from the Old DOOM games, DOOM and DOOM II.
The music is more metal than ever with Mick Gordon playing (mostly) on a Mayones Regius 8 Pro in E A E A D G B E tuning, and a Schecter Omen 9 (or Damien 9) so all the guitar riffs are LOW and Punchy.
The atmospheric noises are also really good. SFX in general are all really well recorded.

All-in-All this game gives a good FPS vibe, puts most other FPS games to Shame
doesn't have a bunch of Fortnite Children Playing it, Music and SFX that are top quality,
Linear gameplay with exploration and plenty of collectibles, upgrades and demons to gore.
10/10 - one of the VERY FEW games I will ever give a 10/10 to. BUY IT.\t\t\t\t\t
