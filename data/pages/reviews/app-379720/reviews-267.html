\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best First-Person-Shooters you can buy. Simple and straight forward; RIP AND TEAR UNTIL IT IS DONE! Fun and challenging with unique weapons and with plenty of intricate secrets in every level. Movement can be slow and methodical or fast and chaotic. Story is a bit weak, but your goal isn't to understand why but move forward until you've won.

MULTIPLAYER
Multiplayer is a bit generic but fun nonetheless. Servers are abit wonky; deciding to work for hours and to just stop working the next, but it usually resolves itself fairly quickly. Cute emotes and a plethora of more weapons not found in the base game can entice you, as well as a CRAP TON of cosmetics to create a cool, edgy, cringe, badass, walking meme of a character; including customizable weapons(changing the color of different parts of the gun).

SNAPMAPS (CREATE YOUR OWN MAP FRO OTHERS TO PLAY)
The 'Snapmaps'(creatable worlds) are really fun if you know what you want to create. Most of the assets build themselves but you are able to build your own room via a bit of creativity. Levels can be whatever you want; multiplayer, singleplayer, capture the flag, clear the level of enemies and the ability to create you own unique story out characters and monsters.
