DOOM I think I can say is a very welcome addition to the franchise. The previous game being Doom 3 which strayed a bit from the game play. THIS game however goes back to its roots baby.For starters the maps are very loyal to the original map making style being maze like full of monsters weapons and goodies hidden off in obscure places. The weapons are very diverse and the fact that you can upgrade said weapons to have brand NEW features such as a heat wave from the plasma gun and the mobile turret mod for the chaingun. The arenas your trapped inside always seem to be different and almost like those of Quake, Wide open but no shortage of places to hide, and flank routes. The sound track made by Mick Gordon is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ AWESOME the soundtrack really sets the tone and pacing with what your currently doing weather its the droning tunes as you explore or the guitars/synthesized chainsaw sounds screaming as you blow up ♥♥♥♥. Finally we get to the lore without spoiling it I'm going to say that it dives more deeply into Doom-Guy and the monsters and the places that you visit which makes it feel more Immersive than previous doom games. with Doom Eternal approaching the corner once more this game makes buying it twice worth it\t\t\t\t\t
