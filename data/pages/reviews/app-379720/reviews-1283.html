\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts an old school FPS action game that you rarely see these days. Unafraid of its own charm, it delivers a "brutal" experience with enough contemporary mechanics that make it one of the very few remakes or reboots that I actually enjoy and a unique FPS experience. This arena combat is so good you forgive its mild repetitiveness. Its enemies and their A.I. and variations are fantastic! It makes you remember when shooters had a variation of enemies (most military FPS games have bland enemies with dumb A.I. if they even have a campaign). Each gun has its own character and variation as well but the pistol feels out of place. The game encourages you to invest in it as much as you want in each gun, your avatar, or the story for that matter. The value on display feels downright generous compared to other "AAA" games; SNAPMAP caters to fans and user created maps feature content only available by modding communities from the original doom. Multiplayer is hit and miss (feels like a Quake 2 and COD mixture), probably the weaker feature of the title but i do appreciate the optional mode. Its Brilliant! If your shooter experience consists of COD or GOW this is almost required gaming, it's a type of unfiltered action shooter that outgrew the original games shadow and best present FPS competition. Oh, the checkpoint system is ass though.\t\t\t\t\t
