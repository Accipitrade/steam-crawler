\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can't believe Doom 2016 exists. There is no tutorial or long story cutscene to start, just this single phrase:

"They are rage, brutal, without mercy. But you, you will be worse. Rip and tear, until it is done."

It's everything I dreamed it would be. Amazing atmosphere, brutal soundtrack, hard hitting guns, absurd amounts of ultra violence, no regenerating health, no 60fps lock, full FOV slider, no holds barred. I can't believe it is the current year and this exists. There is nothing more cathartic than decimating hell and its residents.

What a time to be alive.

EDIT: Having just completed the campaign I have to say, this the absolute BEST singleplayer FPS campaign I have played in years. There aren't any goofy backtracking segements, absurd fetch quests, or long annoying cutscenes. There is a good sense of progression throughout as you unlock weapon modifications and the rune trials and secret areas add for replayability. I also highly recommend people to play on the "ultra-violence" difficulty as it adds a great deal of challenge. No parts ever felt excessive and I honestly think that is the level the game is meant to be played on. I am utterly astounded. After seeing the multiplayer in beta (still dislike the multiplayer), my hopes were abysmal. I am so glad ID had the opportunity to make a fitting tribute, here's to hoping for a fantastic sequel.

Oh! And I totally forgot to mention "Snap Map" a level and game mode builder built in. I haven't used it all that much yet but playing through the classic levels people have remade has proven to be a ton of fun. I will for sure be playing more of this. Especially due to the fact all of these levels can be played with a buddy online.

WELL DONE ID SOFTWARE , (and Bethesda), WELL DONE

EDIT 2: Just editing my review for the Steam Award Nominations and this game definitely deserves a "Whoooaaaa Dude!".\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSome people are lucky and have no performance problems with this game. I personally was unlucky, and had to spend hours doing very technical things to get this thing to run at more than 0.2 FPS in the MENU. Not mentioning the absolute unplayable 1-2FPS ingame performance. Even when you get it fixed, it loves to come up with new and annoying ways to screw your FPS and showcase very inconsistent optimization. But once you get through that grumble..


The game is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ amazing.
Absolutely silly, how good the gunplay is. The reboots coming out of ID soft and bethesda, like Wolfenstein last year - are incredible. The shooting is 10/10, very fast paced, all the guns (all of them!) are useful in their own way, and I never finished a big fight without using all the weapons in my arsenal at least once during the fight. Tons of secrets, some meaningful to your power, some fun collectibles. Story is simple, which is what you want from a doom game. Gives you plenty of reason to kill demons, and that's enough for me.

Enemy design is very good, each enemy has a different power level, and abilities and traits to watch out for. Frequently in the game you'll be faced with a new enemy type, and think "♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, this guy is strong. Must be a boss." only for the game to throw 10 of the same enemy at you in a later sequence, when you've powered up with new guns and upgrades. This is a great design choice to show the player their progression in terms of power and skill.

All in all, the game is very well made (if you can find solutions to potential performance issues you may face), and I'd strongly recommend picking it up, even if you've never played DOOM before, and you're in the market for an extremely solid shooter.

(Don't bother with multiplayer, it is sadly trash. Only buy this game for the singleplayer experience.)\t\t\t\t\t
