\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game overall is an 8/10.
Not as good as the old Doom Games imo Buuuuut its really really close so I would recommend this game to everyone who has every played Doom or is looking to get into the series.

Pros:
They nailed the fast paced movement and shooting in this game.
Story is very well done.
Monsters are very well done.
The music in the game is AMAZING and carries the game imo. (Make sure to crank your volume to 11 while playing cause it jacks you the **** Up!!!)

Cons:
Collection/level up system, though really good, sometimes is a little too tedious because I felt like I was forced to spend too much time searching for items rather than just going out their having fun surviving and slaying demons.\t\t\t\t\t
