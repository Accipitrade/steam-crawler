This game realy feels like how a Doom game should be.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast-paced, punishing but not unfair, metal soundtrack, demons, doomguy

DOOM is back.

After playing the first few missions in the campaign, I feel like my opinion on this game is pretty solid. This was a Singleplayer-centric purchase for me. The oil-slicken speed you are handed in this game almost comes as a shock after so many years of "gritty and realistic" shooters slowing us down more and more within their environments
(with recharging healthbars and cover-shooters becoming the standard). DOOM wastes no time getting you into the attitude of its pedecessors (barring doom 3), handing you a pistol from the get-go and telling you 'those are demons; you kill them'. From that point on, it's a classic FPS styled with a shiny coat of paint and a couple of short cutscene-lites to thread a story together. I'm having a whole lot of fun with this game, and I'm glad they didn't turn DOOM - the grand daddy of the FPS genre - into another COD-clone.

I am not all that interested in multiplayer combat, so I haven't checked that out yet, but I probably will sooner or later. If the online videos are anything to go by, though, it will probably be a fun and interesting experience for those looking for it.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has it all: Gore, loud guns, difficult enemies, atmosphere, fast pace, the best game-soundtrack I can think of, it is perfectly optimized, so even players with lower specs PCs can enjoy it, and, and that's a huge plus, it is easy to understand the inventory, mechanics etc.

This game is just as every game should be, yet very few are. And considering that this is a "remake", it makes it even better. How many remakes were just rip offs that you bought because of the title? Exactly. Doom isn't that. It takes the good things and makes them better (e.g. the iconic soundtrack). It adds a lot of features, without overloading it.

This game doesn't tie you infront of your monitor with it's heart-breaking story, or with the nostalgia that comes with the title. It does by doing the one and only thing perfect that a demon shooter has to do: It let's you shoot demons. In a perfect way. When Doom was good (and it was!), then this Doom is the real deal.


PS: You want to set +set m_smooth 0 in your launch options, because mouse acceleration is on by default and that's the only way to turn it off.

Have fun. And good luck.\t\t\t\t\t
