\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best FPS campaigns I've ever played. Fast paced, move forward combat with no reloads or taking cover.

The soundtrack makes you feel like a GOD, as it is controlled by your actions in the game. Adrenaline pumping hardcore metal when you're in combat, and very atmospheric while alone. Gave me the chills.

Would recommend this game to everyone who's into first person shooters as this has gone back to the roots of the genre, and challenged the cinematic, cower-behind-cover combat seen in many newer games (looking at you COD).
