\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great, fast-paced shooter that retains the spirit of its predecessors while updating the run and gun formula for the current gaming generation. The weapons and abilities feel weighty and have great sound effects, and there is a wide variety of enemy types to use them against. Combine this with some fantastic environmental storytelling, and you've got an awesome single-player experience.

There are a few minor elements that hold the game back at times. Most of the levels are very well laid-out, but there are a few that are confusing to follow on the map, necessitating some annoying backtracking. While the weapons are great overall, your upgrades only affect their secondary modes, and some of those modes are not particularly useful throughout the majority of the game. With that said, these are minor annoyances, and easily forgotten when you're in the heat of a fierce gun battle against the hordes of hell.\t\t\t\t\t
