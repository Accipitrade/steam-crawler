\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPeople talk about cognitive dissonance a lot in in video games. The protagonist being considered a good person, and yet they kill hundreds, even thousands of people in-game. DOOM solves this by making you a silent, uncaring and effective demon killing machine. Is the DOOM Slayer good? Well, he's pretty damn good killing demons, and that's all the game is about! To back this up the game gives you a variety of badass weapons you'll need to cycle through as needed in order to effectively mow down waves upon waves of unrelenting demons. The game's combat is fast paced and satisfying, the controls are responsive and accurate, a solid shooter all around. Enemy variety keeps on your toes and you can always test your skills by raising the difficulty and seeing just how effective you are utilizing your arsenal. Full of secrets, collectibles and littered with all kinds of gory challenges, DOOM is all meat and no fluff, don't expect a nuanced story, the substance is all in the combat system. You'll be busy finding the most effective ways to shoot demons in the face till the credits roll. If you like the sound of that, you'll have one hell of a time with DOOM.\t\t\t\t\t
