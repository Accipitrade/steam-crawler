\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM (2016) is an amazing game. The absolute RUSH I feel when playing this game is insane and literally no other game gets me this focused on pure MURDER. I have played a lot of FPS games in my life and none of them come close to making me feel as good as DOOM. If you haven't bought this game then now is the best time seeing as the sequel has been recently released, which I am waiting to get my hands on.

You want some more detail? Okay, lemme praise this game some more why don't I?

Gameplay

DOOM (2016) manages to keep the player entertained through amazing enemy and level design, keeping the player on their toes throughout their MURDER adventure. The guns that you are given are all amazing in their own right. Except the pistol, it's actual garbage. Go to HELL, you stupid pistol. The shotguns have amazing impact, the heavy assault rifle is meaty as hell, the plasma rifle being a stream of pure fun, the rocket launcher making enemies into red mist, the rail gun absolutely decimating enemies, the chaingun removing them from existence and the damn BFG making all of those guns look like toys compared to its destructive power. You also get a chainsaw. Nice. What are the enemies like? Well, they're all evil demons so you will feel absolutely zero remorse in murdering the red outta them. The enemy variety in the game is almost perfect, even managing to give some of the same enemy type different tactics to keep you moving and thinking and KILLING. The further you progress, the more enemy types the game throws at you with a couple bosses in their too. One of my absolute FAVOURITE mechanics is the glory kill mechanic. What does it do? Well dear reader, it allows you to RIP AND TEAR the enemy apart in glorious kill animations that Space Marines wish they could do on xenos scum. Whittle down the enemy enough and they'll flash blue from afar, indicating that they are ready to die by your hand. When you get close, they turn and orange, indicating that you are close enough to rip them apart. It's what they deserve, after all...

Story

HAHAHAHAHHAHA story? The Doom Slayer SPITS in the face of the story, for all he knows and wants is to RIP and TEAR.
Okay just kidding, there is a story. A company (The UAC) that is located on Mars decided to tap in to the vast and infinite energy exuded from Hell. Good idea, right? Absolutely not because it backfires so hard pretty much every one either dies or gets possessed and Hell is unleashed on the facility on Mars. Your objective is to get rid of these pesky demons by any means necessary and to stop them from invading any further.

Music

OH my GOD the music in this game. It's a mix of the heaviest and sickest of metal, with industrial themes mixed in, some electronic sounds and a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ OPERA. I actually find it hard to describe the music. There is only one word for it. DOOM. Believe it or not I'm listening to the soundtrack as I write down this review that I randomly started for no reason.

I think that's all I wanted to say about the game really. I can't think of anything else other than I genuinely love this game and I hope you do too.

Thank you for coming to my TED Talk.\t\t\t\t\t
