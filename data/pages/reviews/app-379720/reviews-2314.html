\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGot this game coz it was so highly rated and supposedly one of the top for 2016.. However, after playing it myself, I honestly have no idea why this is so highly rated...

Bad points: The story is stereotypical not much plot. The graphics is average at best. The mapping system is HORRIBLE! The worst i have ever use in any games I played. Took 18+ hours to complete this game in Normal mode and for most of the time, I spent running in circles, navigating and map reading than actual killing monsters...

Good points: The monsters' AI are quite good and varied. Most of the fights are high paced high tempo which I very much preferred. However, end of the day, AI will still be AI. Just need a couple of retries to figure out the pattern, tempo, weaknesses and you are good to go, much like old school platform games. So any decent online competitive shooters vs humans can easily top that experience.. The soundscape is immersive, so playing the game with headphones in a dark room can be rather eerie and suspenseful. But again there are plenty of better immersive horror games out there if that is what you are looking for..

Conclusion: If i have a time machine, I would definitely skip this game. To me the game is just decent and nothing special. The plot is below average. The graphics is below average. Only the monster fights, AI and tempo is good. So if this game came out in the 90s, before any online competitive multi-player fps, it might be highly rated. But for me, end of the day, that TERRIBLE mapping system is just too much to bear..\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst person shooter + gore = D00M. 'Shot and go' if you are into that kind of stuff Doom is the way to go.

Overall score from me to D00M is a 8.5/10.

Pros:
  • A lot of weapons , a lot of weapon mods .
  • Many demons.
  • Huge maps with some exploration.
  • Fast paced , very flashy.
  • The glory kills are very satisfying.
  • Almost nonexistent story-line (it's good that it exists).
  • The graphic is good (for early January 2019 standards).
  • Arcade mode! I love it.
  • Snap-map a cool feature.

Cons:
  • The campaign is the same every time you play it. Once you finish it, there is no point in playing it again.
  • There is nothing to explore. Exploring ... more like searching for hidden stuff. Who plays doom just to 'explore'?
  • Almost nonexistent story-line (where are the details?).
  • Snap-map is too complex. I can't figure it out.
  • Multiplayer is very boring after a medium while. Always the same people and some game-modes are just horrible.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is my personal game of the year for 2016. This game feels so fresh in comparison to many other FPS' of recent years in that it takes a different approach to level design and shooting to offer a more visceral, brutal, fast and relentlessly fun gameplay experience. I don't mean to criticise the design that other FPS' have taken, every game has it's unique design and it must be respected if done right. And thankfully, DOOM executes this perfectly. Mechanics typically found in other games of the genre are changed or removed. For instance, there's no sprinting or reloading. Your character always moves at his fastest possible speed and gameplay isn't interrupted by reloading, and it works fantastically for the game. Level design is excellent, soundtrack is also great and fits the games tone (heavy metal mixed with some techno here and there), the games looks stunning as well (which explains the game's 80GB download size...). The story isn't particularly deep, but it doesn't need to be, the simple story works very well with the game. I also want to touch briefly on the different modes, multiplayer and SnapMap. Multiplayer was often criticised by people, and while it's not perfect, I found myself having fun with it. And I feel SnapMap is extremely underrated. It's a level editor for the game where players can create and upload their levels for the world to try out, and It's excellent, offering lots of possibilities for cool ideas for levels. There's one guy who made FNAF with this editor, how cool is that?! However, I imagine most of you are buying DOOM for the campaign, and that's well justified, because It's excellent. My only gripe with the game is that the campaign might get a bit repetitive, but keep on going and there's always something to enjoy. 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
