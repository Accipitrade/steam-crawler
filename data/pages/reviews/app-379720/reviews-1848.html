\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM 2016, also referred to as DOOM 4, is the a soft reboot/sequel of the series. And the prequel to DOOM: Eternal.

The UAC researcher, Olivia Pierce, finds herself tempted by the demons of hell into opening a portal from Hell onto Mars, this naturally goes against the orders of Dr. Samuel Hayden, who wishes to exploit Hell and the newly-found Argent Energy. Before the events of the game, they found the Doomslayer's sealed tomb and brought it back to Mars. As the demonic outbreak on Mars occurs, the Doomslayer re-awakens, his goal simple: To "Rip and Tear, until it is done."
DOOM 4, like it's DOSBOX predecessors before it, is a movement based shooter. You WILL die if you do not learn how to move and shoot. If you find yourself low on health, simply weaken a demon and you can perform a Glory kill, which rewards bonus health and a satisfying sense of bloodlust. All the old weapons are back, from the trusty Shotgun to the BFG9000. New to this game are weapon mods, each weapon (excluding the Super Shotgun) has two mods that can enhance the weapon in different ways. The chainsaw also returns, albeit as a ammo gain mechanic rather than an actual weapon. You also have powerups, ranging from the Berserk powerup, which is essentially glory kill rampage. the Haste powerup, which speeds you up tremendously, and even Quake's Quad Damage, which boosts your damage by 4 times. New powerups include Demon Runes, which can be fitted onto you to have increased Glory Kill time, faster Glory Kills, a Glory Kill gap closer, and so on and so forth. Your armored suit, also known as the Praetor suit, also can be upgrade by finding tokens in the level from Red Armor corpses, which allow you to upgrade your grenade abilities, become more dexterous, survive explosive barrels, et cetera.

You may notice that I haven't talked about Multiplayer yet. And that's because I think Multiplayer in DOOM 4 is completely garbage for one reason, Loadouts. I personally hate it when old FPS series try to copy loadouts from something like Call of Duty. DOOM is no exception, it was never like this in Quake, which was essentially 3D DOOM. and I'm glad to say that Eternal will fix this issue. Please do not bother with DOOM 4's multiplayer.

The music in Doom 4 is outstanding. BFG Division, E1M1's remix, Rip and Tear, to name a few. Seriously what else do I say? Heavy Metal Demon Slaying.

Overall, if you are looking forward to DOOM Eternal but have somehow not played Doom 2016.. what the hell are you doing looking at my review? Buy it, play it, embrace the bloodshed. For Doom is Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
