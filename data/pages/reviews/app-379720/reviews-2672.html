\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA rare example of a reboot that manages to maintain the essence of the original games whilst appealing to more modern FPS sensibilities. The guns are meaty, there's a great variety of monsters and parts of the level design are spectacular, both visually and in terms of gameplay. Thankfully narrative takes a backseat to the action, but there's just enough of a story to keep you going. The first half of the game is definitely better than the second as certain battle scenarios start to become a little repetitive. The whole campaign could do with more dynamic sections akin to the Argent Tower level. Boss battles are a big disappointment as they just throw you in to a single-atrium arena and make you fight a bullet sponge. There are occasional flashes of creativty in the boss monsters attacks, but they would definitely benefit from some more interesting level design.

Don't bother with multiplayer or snapmap. The former was shopped out to a different development team who took all of the soul out of the game, and snapmap is an incredibly limited tool that just comes across as a lazy apeasement to the modding community. It might be exciting to console gamers, but PC gamers have come to expect more versatility from modding tools.

All said and done, this is the most fun single-player FPS since HL2, Wolfenstein coming in a close 2nd.\t\t\t\t\t

I wasn't so big on Doom 3 but this one is truly a return to the older style of running and gunning with some awesome metal soundtrack. The graphics are amazing and the campaign sends you through some cool environments. I was disappointed a few classic DOOM enemies did not show up in the game, including Pain Elemental. Arch Vile is in the game but they changed it to "Summoner" and gave it some new abilities. There is a high level customization in the gameplay that allows different character builds for different playstyles. Multiplayer allows for online gameplay but I haven't tried it out yet. Ultra Nightmare is the hardest difficulty and is essentially a Hardcore mode where you must start over if you die. If you enjoy shooting demons in FPS and like a good campaign this game is for you.\t\t\t\t\t
