\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA brilliant reboot/sequel to one of the most important FPS games of all time.

All of the weapons from the original games do return (Super Shotgun, Chaingun, BFG etc.), but obviously there are new additions to the arsenal.
All of the weapons (except Super Shotgun, BFG, Pistol and Chainsaw) have two weapon mods that change their alt-fires.
But there's always the one weapon mod that is lame compared to the other one.
What would you select?
Ability to spin up your Chaingun in an almost redundant way or have a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ THREE BARREL mobile turret mode?

Almost every enemy returns also, but almost all of them have new bizarre models, like the Mancubus now has only one eye and has some sort of organic armor.
Too bad the Pain Elemental, Arachnotron and Arch-Vile don't return. Arch-Vile has a watered down replacement, but it still sucks.
Obviously there are new enemies too.

EDIT: After trying multiplayer for the second time, I realized that it isn't quite as bad as most people make it out to be. It's well, something, I guess.

The SnapMap is ok, kinda, and it is the first ever map editor brought to consoles, but it is limited compared to previous entries' modding freedom because of open source code and consoles, hurr durr.

Recommend this even just for the singleplayer.

Also this game is very optimized, especially if you run it in Vulkan renderer instead of OpenGL 4.5\t\t\t\t\t
