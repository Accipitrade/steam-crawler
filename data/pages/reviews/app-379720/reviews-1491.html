I beat it on the PS4, so i have significantly more than just just the 7 hours noted by Steam. I bought in on the PC because i owned all the other ones. The game maintains its simplicity but has an up-to-date feel. I would recommend that anyone who is a fan of the franchise purchase this game.

Speaking to the multiplayer... if you are not a fan of all the new style of FPS multiplayer pay to win and other types of games that don't really seem to keep a level playing field, then this multiplayer is great. It is set up so you never really feel at a disadvantage. To keep going you have to keep gathering ammo/item/armor pick ups. There is no camping. If you know the mechanics of the weapons and daemons then you will be fine. I suggest playing the single-player first and reading... yes reading... the codex items you find while paying attention to the AI movements.

As an added bonus, this game is paired with one of the best soundtracks. The music gets you pump before, during, and even after the gunfights. So much so you don't want them to end.\t\t\t\t\t
