\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBrutal Doom, a free program any of you can go download and play right now, is everything this game is trying to be, but without... THIS

My average time playing Doom 2016: I bring up the holo map. I stare at the armor upgrade icon. I put down the map. I look around for a way to go up or down a level. I bring back up the map.

Woo! Sound exciting? Its not! Its really not! Collectibles are tied heavily to the STUPID rpg lite elements they added. So do you want your machine gun to shoot missiles? Do you want your shotgun to shoot exploding grenades? Of course you do! Do you want to upgrade your armor to be immune to explosive barrels, hold more death dealing ammo and take more hits? Obviously.

But... do you want to scour the level for longer then you ACTUALLY KILL THINGS with the said weapons your upgrading? For me the answer is a definitive NO. NO I do NOT want to scour every level looking for white shapes on the map. While this isnt a bad game, it is highly overrated, and ultimately, very disappointing. Do yourself a favor, save 60 dollars... download Brutal Doom.\t\t\t\t\t
