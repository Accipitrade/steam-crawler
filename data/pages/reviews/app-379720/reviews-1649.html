The combat shotgun is a quick way to deal with smaller demons, quicker than the pistol, close up.

The Rocket Launcher is how to deal with Caucodemons when the timing is right.
The Plasma Rifle is how to dispatch pinkys with an express ticket back to hell.

The Heavy Assault Rifle is the answer to DOOM 3's machine gun, and can fire mini rockets or be used like a sniper
The BFG / Big-[REDACTED]-Gun 9000 is the way to weaken the bosses or to clear roomfuls of demons at once.
Glory kills are what it sounds like - killing animations that are gory - like killing a demon with the soggier end of its own arm, or shoving a (cyber) mancubis's heart down its own throat - do not do if low on health, though.
