\r\n\t\t\t\t\t9/10

DOOM is a damn great game which, even though I still personally have a preference for the two classic games (partially from nostalgia, but also because they're amazing games), was still incredibly fun and enjoyable to play.

The game is equal parts true to the classics and a fresh new take on the series. Gameplay is pure fast-paced FPS action that's a lot about being constantly moving and shooting. Weakened enemies can be taken down with brutal kills that can actually be pretty damn brutal in some cases and also grant a little health to the character as well. Upgrades can be found in the shape of runes, gun mods, armor upgrades and argent cells. Argent cells will grant direct upgrades to max health, armor and ammo, while the others enhance the character's abilities and guns or grant special effects like acquiring armor (besides health) on brutal kills. Certain challenges can also be completed to improve runes or grant the final upgrade to a certain weapon mod. The game has a nice balance between being complex and simple: there is some complexity among all the upgrades and choosing which ones to use, but the gameplay itself is simple, fluid, action-oriented and straight to the point. The gunplay is amazing and there is a lot of variation on brutal kills depending on where on the enemy you aim, which is something that takes some getting used to but later becomes a lot easier.

Levels are not only very well designed, but they also look amazing. I was running the game on Low (was having framerate issues on higher settings) and even then the game still looked incredible. Levels also include a decent ammount of exploration as there are secrets to be found (including pieces of classic levels, which then unlock the respective classic level to be played later on) and additional challenges to be completed for extra gun upgrade points.

The difficulty levels are very balanced and allow for anyone to be able to play the game, as the game can be very well played on Normal without too much hassle, while Ultra Nightmare is the ultimate challenge for those who really like to be challenged.

The story is also well done, going far and beyond into really making players feel how Doom Slayer really is a badass with an important mission, with other characters feeling like they're equally important to the story rather than just NPCs on the background. There is some discussion on whether it's a reboot or not, but some things in the game (which I won't spoil) hint towards this game being a possible continuation of the previous games - which is something I want to believe, as it makes the story far more interesting.

I will say that I didn't play the multiplayer (because of a lack of coop, as I'm not a fan of PVP), but from what I've seen and heard from others, the multiplayer seems to be very well done, specially after some recent updates and also with the use of Snapmap for creating new maps.

It's also worth mentioning that the soundtrack is absolutely incredible.

My only real complaint with the game is the lack of a coop mode, which is something I really think should have been added to the game instead of just a pure PVP multiplayer. Also, not really a complaint, but it would be interesting to have guns having "base upgrades" instead of upgrades that are specific to each mod they have. The only gun in the game that can have its base stats upgraded is the Super Shotgun, since it has no gun mods.

Overall, a fantastic game, highly recommended and definitely keeps me eager for the sequel.\t\t\t\t\t
