Maybe it was the first mission / tutorial that didn't really hook me in, maybe it was because I was playing so much Overwatch all the time that I forgot that I had wanted to play this game.

Fast forward to 2023...
I have decided to at least try to go through my backlog of games I have in my steam library, and I decided to hop back in and see what I was missing about DOOM. I enjoyed it! Some missions I kind of felt *eh* about but overall I liked getting upgrades and trying to find secrets and such.

Speaking of secrets... I also have been wanting to set a goal for myself to 100% finish games this year...
This does not mean all of them, but I think after spending my time with this game I might try to hop back in and at least get all of the achievements that I can for the story mode. Though, I did play some games tonight and it seems to still be vibrant with some players, so who knows. Will DOOM 2016 be a game I 100%? I guess we will have to see!

I recommend this game for anyone who wants to have simple and destructive fun. I have a bit of a chaotic life working two jobs in the food industry right now so most of the time I do not have the energy to handle all of DOOM for long sessions, so if you are anything like me, maybe do a level or two everyday to keep yourself from feeling overwhelmed. :)\t\t\t\t\t
