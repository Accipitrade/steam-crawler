Rip and Tear, until it's done

I played this game after watching Korone (a Vtuber) playing it. To all fans: buy the game. It's that good.

Longer review
This game is like a fresh wind in the stale world of FPS.
It stays true to the original form while changing just enough to feel new.

It's more of a test of skills a-la Mirror's Edge than a tactical shooter. Every fight is like an arena where you run around avoiding enemy attacks while killing demons. It requires you to use only some spatial reasoning, but no more.

I also liked how they put a story in the game but don't really care to tell it to you. Are you interested? You can read it, otherwise the doomguy is not interested in it.

If you like FPS or fast paced games give this game a try, you won't be disappointed.\t\t\t\t\t
