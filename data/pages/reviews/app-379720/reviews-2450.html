\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt says I've played four hours, which I suppose is true. But I have played the rest of the game through on console so take my review with a grain of salt if you wish or simply take it for the story and gameplay.

DOOM is amazing. Having never played the previous Dooms, (yes, I'm sorry) I didn't feel like I was missing out on anything. It's simple enough, there's Demons. They're bad. You kill them. In beautiful, amazing ways that only demons should die in. One of, if not my favorite games of all time. Seriously. The combat is fast, fluid, brutal. The way to survive is to keep moving and charge forward. The health system implented is pretty darn good as well.

P.S. The soundtrack is amazing too and available for purchase if not through Steam than through other mediums and you should totally pick it up like I did and Rip and Tear your way into work every morning.\t\t\t\t\t
