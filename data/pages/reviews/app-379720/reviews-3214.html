\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI know im late with saying this but this game came out really good.

Story mode: It really does hit the theme it was going for being like the old doom's but still adding new stuff into the mix.
It's a blast from the start to almost the last part but there is a few bumps in the road it happens around the time of one of the second boss it was not really as hard as the two big name bosses in there. Then at kinda close to the end they ran out of demon types to throw at you and it started to is boring that was till the last boss fight where it picked up again.

The weapon mods where mostly useful but some where not namely the scope for the rifle i never even needed to get it.

And all the hidden stuff gives you something to replay it about.

Online: It's just there nothing really awesome about it. I am fine with the load out thing it's not like it breaks the game like some load outs there is no one " PvP " Load out that can be made which is good.
I would like the run speed to be a little faster because it's hard not to get hit by attacks ( And no bunnyhopping wont save you. ) And to my shock the Baron feels the more useless of the demon's Namely because you lack fireballs and he feels a lot slower then what they are like in the story mode.

And this next part is not the games fault it's just the playerbase.
Really you need to get some friends if you want to play this and have some one that can work with you because most the time you will get into a game with nothing but stupid people that barely seem to understand how to play a game. The thing that will happen a lot more then it should is.

1: You get in a game with people that think hitting you with there gun is smarter then just SHOOTING you. It's only a good thing if it's the enemy team is full of those people because that means you win but mostly it will be your team acting like that.
2: And most the time in Domination your team wont even try to cap the points leaving you to be the only one really capping which is not easy to do.

So really play with friends if you want to have any fun with this, because random's like most online games are going to be stupid unless you get that rare game in which the players are smart but that is rare.

Snapmap: If you played any of the timesplitters games and messed around with the Mapmaker mode in it then you know what you will get it's like an updated one of those. The space max in it is not to my liking but thats because i always try to make bigger maps, so being forced to work in a small box is not something i like but thats me.
Anyway it's fun to make your own maps if you are into that, And if you just want to play maps others made watch out for bad ones as those seem to be made alot. But you can still find some good ones in there. And again friends make it all the better in co-op maps because goodluck trying to find any random's. But then again would you really want to play co-op with randoms?\t\t\t\t\t

and iv been playing ever since.
So i ask my self the question is this game good? Is it like the old DOOM?
Short ansewer Yes Its good.
And the long ansewer is you will have to decide for your self as you cant compare this game to a 20+ year old god in the gamming industry. just from looking at the game play you have to live it and be it to under stand this what i would call the best series of games ever.
I loved the classics dont get me wrong but this game is different.
I don't want you to think im looking through my nostalgia googles as its doom so heres my thoughts on it.

so heres my thoughts on the SP campagine
DOOM guy is a demonic Slaying BAD A$$ with a whole truck load of fire power to kick some hellspawns A$$
you run gun rip tear repeat. (optional are secrets and collectables) like in the old doom you can look for weapons and extra ammo etc if you wish this extends the games play time.
Just like the originals if you find the secret rocket launcher or bfg your time beating back the hell spawn is more smooth and bad A$$.
The single player is Just as action packed and faster then the original.
Its also a lot longer then most AAA titels in the genre.
also you can carry more then 2 weapons.
the achivements names are fun and clever but you could of had more of them with names releating to the classics.

The graphics are beautiful and not as demanding as the time when doom 3 came out i rember my med end pc couldnt really run it back then and it med the min specs even on the lower settings it struggled
but thats the past i can run it on my 750 ti at 720p on a mixed med/high settings at over 60fps with dips to 45 55
but i could get it to run better if i didnt max out the lighting and shadows as they to me make the game look better :)
The only down sides are i would of like to see the bodys pile up like in the older games but i gusse its too demanding for the consol crowds consols sowe the pc players have to suffer because who didnt like looking around a room full of bodys piled up in the classics? and the worst tradgerdy that ruins this game is im hungry for more!!! SP whitch is where snap map comes in.

Snap map ok its not quite mod tools like we wanted but there are some fun and intresting modes here theres a mode where you pick up the flag and try to get it into the enermys goal wilst trying not to be shot.
I made a sniper map where you are on 2 sides of a void and you can spawn barrles and shoot them to flush the enemy out, or spawn demons to distract them so you can get them wilst there backs turned.
you can also creat your own mp maps and modes like 2 players v 1 demon, or hell why not all of you be demons lets call it hells mutany. theres still so much to do and its being updated with hell sectors we can put in to it and we are about to get a weapon weel in snap map so creating the old school style arena dm will soon be happening.


Multiplayer
pros ok...... hard to think of much good as the game stands but il tell you whats good ok the customisation of armor and weapons are good theres lots of variaty. You can really stand out from the crowd the only down side is alot of the guys look like power ranger turds and i would at least like to have doom guys armor not these halo rip offs.
at least i got to dres my self up as a demon.
Taunts are fun to do.

now for the game play
It's a nice mix of old school arena style fps with the mordern influances such as LOAD OUTSSSSS!!!! i know arena fans not what you wanted to see from a classic such as doom hopefully quake champions wont have them.

Tbh the worst thing about the MP in my opinion is The very unballanced weapons and cheats
now i dont use it lightly but there are a few i have encountered who litraly 1 shot killed my whole team with plasma even if you had 100 hp 100 armor at the end of the match as i played on he had 50k 0 d i know no ones that good but prove me wrong il be happy to veiw the footage :)
tbh iv only encountered 2-3 over my 100 hours of playing.

But back to the unbalanced weapons it was at first the chain gun over powered... ok at least you had to aim but since the small 68mb patch the rl is the only weapon any one uses as it kills you very quick and its way over powered and litraly you get into a match and every ones using it and im spawning runing out the door dying i mean even the splash damage is high so you cant even jump from it
So you dont have to realy aim the rl i tryed a match with it got 20 kills to 2 deaths and lots of assists i didnt even go for mid air shots with it like you would in quake just shows you how unballanced it is your only defence is 2 shot guns in a load out a hologram or cyber wall thing as it screws there plans giving you time to counter.
another good defense from it is vortex rifle from long range picks them off nicely.
tbh i like the weapons you can get theyr all fun to use.

tbh there needs to be changes to the RL
like have it as a pick up and give you 3 shots or somthing like that or give the rocket launcher a diffrent ammo box and scatter them randomly.


now to the controlls i love the controlls very smooth and i even play this with my xbox 360 controller witch is excelent for sitting on the sofa and playing some MP.
the steam controller works just as well if not better 24k to like 5D (and no not with the RL) im not cheap shot kinda guy.

well that rounds up DOOM in a nut shell a fun fast paced gory game with tons of action and exploration.
I would recomend you play it 9.5 out of 10 for the campagin 8/10 for snap map 6/10 ffor the MP fix the MP and the weapon ballancing and i will give you 100/10. for the whole game.
i hope you enjoyed my lengthy review of DOOM and if you decide to get it and you in the UK or europe feel free to add me and we can DM and CO-OP together thats it for now see you on the fiery battle fields in hell\t\t\t\t\t
