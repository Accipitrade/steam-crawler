\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever in my life have I needed something so bad and not known until I recieved it.
For the last few years, I thought I had simply outgrown the FPS genre, as I just didn't find any of the ones I'd played or seen my friends play very fun. Games like CoD and Battlefield were simply no fun for me to play, and I thought the change was in me.
Given that this is a review for DOOM, you can easily guess that I was mistaken. I bought this at the recommendation of a reviewer I trust, and was sporting a grin wider than a cacodemon's within three minutes of starting the game.
The graphics are amazing, the gameplay is fast paced, the weapons are wondefully cathartic, and the sprawling arenas in which you slay demons let you really feel like an unstoppable demon slaying warrior.

I will end my review with my sole nitpick/warning. My computer is hardly top of the line, and I experienced long load times and chuggy framerates on even lower graphical settings. However, after I turned the resolution down, I found I could jack all the graphical settings back up to maximum without slowing anything down, so try that if your game is a little slow.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe gameplay doesn't feel sluggish at all, the action is well-paced and there is never a lack of it. Rather than hide behind cover or be otherwise tactic in engaging your enemies, DOOM invites you in a more hands-on approach to killing them. While I did miss the dark and scary feel of Doom 3 sometimes, I quickly came to accept this is a completely different game: rather than the demons hunting and scaring/surprising you, you're hunting and scaring them on their own turf.

A beautifully composed soundtrack of heavy metal tunes accompanies and complements your killing spree. The composers did a great job at creating both slower and faster paced tunes to fit the different scenes and battles in DOOM.

The graphics are amazing. All sceneries are thought-out and filled in very nicely. The lighting and other special effects are really sublime, making both rooms and fights even more dramatic.

Pros:
- Amazing OST compliments the gameplay and key game moments very well
- Great variety of weapons, including your bare hands
- Gameplay is fast and smooth
- Great variety of enemies

Cons:
- Might not be much of a challenge for seasoned FPS players

I really don't have anything negative to say about DOOM. Even if FPS isn't really your thing, this game could be enjoyable. Even if more tactic or skill-based FPS is your thing, this game could be enjoyable. This game is simply the best and you should just buy it!\t\t\t\t\t
