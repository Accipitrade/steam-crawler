\r\n\t\t\t\t\tImagine a huge budget Action packed, Sci-Fi, Horror movie come to life as an FPS where you get to play the main role: A high level elite space marine/demon slayer who is pretty much all that's left standing between the survival of mankind and total annihilation. Unlocking secret areas, climbing walls, performing cinematic glory kills, being completely outnumbered and often having to think on the fly it's brilliant. Upgrading your gear and selecting specific mods allows you to create your own build, this (for me anyway) created more of a role play and care factor as I not only became absorbed into this world but invested in my slayer.

I've only just scratched the surface but I already know I am going to play this out multiple times and get all of the achievements and I can do this knowing I won't get bored because the re-playable aspect of Doom is outstanding, you can start off basic "Hurt Me Plenty" come back and do "Nightmare" and again with even more challenges to complete and this is just the campaign. 10/10 Will also be pre ordering Eternal!

Edited: After further review, this game is a bit too much for my PC to handle. Might have to put this on ice until I can get a better tower.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn short - it is a very good game! It took me 10 hours to go through the campaign (was not interested that much in other content tbh, although I heard it's good too). The best thing about this game is it's dynamic - you always have to move or u DIE. And you never run out of armor, you can replenish ur health quickly and continue the onslaught with a metal background music. It extremely intense on harder difficulties, but u have BFG "get out of jail" card. The gameplay never slows, I never found myself hiding or looking for ammo mid fight, the kill animations are great, weapon upgrades are game changing: rocket launcher can be more of an AOE clear or a lock on mini boss shreder... It has good old classic bosses, where you have to learn their patterns and animations in order to win. However, I did find that by the last mission I started to get a bit tired, but the game ended before it became a problem so it is not too long to bore you. I do recommend this game to anyone liking shooters, high movement speed, demon insides turning into health and ammo and those who are tired of average shooter (hide behind cover) gameplay and want to bust some demons while listening to the amazing soundtrack, without caring about story or plot, like the main charachter you only want to kill demons - the game gives it to you, and that is all that matters!!!
P.S. I liked the elite guard's red armor more than doom slayer's one :)\t\t\t\t\t
