\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI can't believe how much I enjoyed this game. I usually don't enjoy games that are very light on story. However, there is enough story in Doom that it was enough to drive me forward. Combine that with the missions just being a whole lot of fun, and I often forgot that there wasn't much story going on. I am usually ready for a game to be over at about the 10-12 hour point, including games that I am really enjoying such as Rise of the Tomb Raider. However, I never felt that way at all during Doom, and it took me 21 hours to complete the single player. This is all concerning the single player, since I didnt play the multiplayer as multiplayer is not my thing. It was beautiful, with enjoyable weapons and great sound. I highly recommend this to any FPS fan.\t\t\t\t\t
