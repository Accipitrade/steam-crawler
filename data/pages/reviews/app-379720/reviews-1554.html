There are weapon upgrades for every weapon and there are suit upgrades and even rune that improve things for you such as faster glory kills, glory kills at further range, armour from glory kills, and 9 more that add other things. A++ on this part

Sound: Fantastic, All the weapons with the exception of the Plasma Gun sound great and the impacts feel good. Then there is the music. That alone should make you play this game, and honestly, Mick Gordon nails it out of the park. A

Environment: The world, though red, brown and metallic colours, does feel like what is should be. Though it could be better, the environment of the world is explorable as well, with lots of secrets hidden about the world. B+

Story: Story is there, but not in your face. You are here to slay daemons and that is what you do. The story is there just as a background and lore is the same, everything outside of slaying the daemons are optional.
