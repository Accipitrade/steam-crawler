Soundtrack: ripping apart demons' skulls with your bare hands while heavy metal is blasting in the background is so fitting. Visuals are stunning, they will hold up for another decade. Long campaign with replay value. Frantic and intense arena FPS combat in multiplayer that reminds me of Halo 2, Unreal Tournament and Quake.

This game is truly a gem and I am glad so many people appreciate and enjoy it. If you haven't played in a while I urge you to go through it again. Check out singleplayer, check out the extensive SnapMap collection (a ridiculous amount of varied high-quality (I don't use that term loosely) user and developer-related content). The multiplayer is so great for blowing off some steam for a half hour or hour. Just pure mental stimuation lol. There are even bots in this game for offline play or if you just want a relaxing solo experience.

The FPS genre has been so stale for so long. You've had the copy-paste titles of Call of Duty and Battlefield for a decade now. Buy this game and you'll have more games like this. Buy less of the other crap titles and you'll have less games like that. You won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
