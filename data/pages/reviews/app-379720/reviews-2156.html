\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Good:
- Extremely good story
- Great looking, varied environments
- Great gunplay and movement systems
- Amazing soundtrack

The Bad
- Multiplayer isn't incredibly varied, although it has some replayability it ends up feeling like a less engaging Quake with demon powerups
- Snapmap isn't heavily utilized by the playerbase so there aren't too many maps to play on, and it is hard to get into mapping with the tool imo

Overall, I would definitely recommend this game as the devs at ID absolutely nailed making blasting away hordes of demons satisfying as hell.\t\t\t\t\t
