Want to de-stress but that one hacker dude keeps following you each game and ruining your fun? Look no further than the torn to gore imp you just brutally murdered! DOOM 2016 is not a kiddie game: your mechanical skills will be put to the test as you rip, tear, saw, shoot, smash, stomp, and a few other words for genocidal rampage that haven't been invented yet! Each weapon up your astronomically large hammer space is a unique addition to your living gun storage. Shoot skeleton zombies with your pistol, imps with your handy combat shotgun, and large horned demons with the classic BFG. Be warned; there is no game journalist mode. Even the easiest difficulty has more bloody tears per square foot of UAC floor. The music soundtrack? It's not heavy metal, it's heavy violence.\t\t\t\t\t
