..I'm glad i came back to try it again. About 2 hours into the game it started to really gel for me.

It's not like playing the original Doom games (which i still prefer over this), however this game manages to be it's own thing in a good way.

The combat and exploration are both done very well. I was originally unwelcoming to the idea of the game pausing me everytime i had to do a melee kill, but after some time i even grew to like this system.

I'll also say while i'm here, i'm not enjoying Doom Eternal, and wishing it was more like this game. That game added too much platforming and too many things to keep track of, as well as creatures looking less serious and intimidating, more cartoony almost.

I expect i will return to play through this great FPS again one day because it was a lot of fun and I can't think of another FPS quite like it.\t\t\t\t\t
