\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom has it's ups and downs.

The guns are cool, the enemies are plenty. However the movement ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ sucks, you feel like a potato on wheels.

Despite this I got to 89% achievements in less than two weeks, so clearly I had fun (also I was stuck at home due to corona).

The multiplayer however is super frustrating since it is basically "Noob-tube, the game! No skill required". The players who dominate basically abuse rocket launchers and power-ups. Of course you can do that too, but considering the cool modes and weapons the devs designed for this game, that is just such a waste.

The playermade maps are ok, but for the most part nothing special. I honestly feel a bit bad for the people who put in a lot of work to create their own content for this game, but the tools seem somewhat limited (when you compare it to real modding like in skyrim or other games) so most of the community maps don't provide alot of quality gameplay (even the ones with alot of upvotes)

In conclusion: If you want this game, get it for the campaign. If you have the game, at least try the multiplayer, since some of the gamemodes and weapons are pretty cool. And if you feel up to it, go ahead and check out the community section ("SnapMap") it has a couple of gems.\t\t\t\t\t
