\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR is --> Single Player = AMAZING, best ever. Multiplayer = pay for DLC to win, trash, don't buy this game for the multiplayer because it isn't worth a hot sh*t.

Quick PSA: I have a GTX 750 Ti with AMD fx 8300 processor and I can run this (not overclocked) at 1080p with high textures, rest of the settings low or off with vertical sync at 40-60fps in the majority of the areas and battles. The min and rec specs are misleading. However, the GTX 750 Ti has proven to be a fantastic mid-spec card and has power to run most games, even high power ones decently. Definitely worth on sale but it is a must-by for DOOM fans and the singleplayer. So give it a shot, play with settings. Loads of spec set up and ability videos out there to refer to based on your build.

On to the review:

Single player is amazing. Just like the old DOOM games. Gorey, intense, hard, fun, amazing level design, lots of places to explore, lots of demons to kill. However the multiplayer (which I played in Beta and now the finished version) is utter trash IMO. It could be good but the DLC's=pay to win because everything you get from it is stupidly OP. You become unmatched vs someone with vanilla DOOM MP. I put as much time as I could bare in to it and I can say that it has stability issues in the form of many lag spikes and server stability. Matchmaking throws you in with anyone from what I can tell so you are out matched there and good luck learning to get good at a game as chaotic at DOOM when you have vet's who have had it full-time since release and/or beta/alpha who are out there completely destroying everyone.
