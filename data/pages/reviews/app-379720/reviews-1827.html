\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am not sure how this won awards,. I’m sad I wasted my $$ & time on it. Ok, It looks better than any of the previous "doom"s but beyond that it brought nothing new to the table. Over all I found it quite repetitive, and boring to play with nearly zero story line. While I normally like a little bit of hidden retro map integration, here it just felt over done, and completely broke any sliver of flow to the game, maybe if it had been done is some cool slick way to enter “retro land” but no, you just open a door or find a secrete passage and you find yourself in 486 land for no apparent reason. its a shame, I remember really enjoying the doom franchise, but this new one just fell flat on its face for me. I think this franchise needs to evolve into something better than what it once was, rather than holding on to something that was cool, for its time.
If running through linear, poorly designed maps (layout not graphics) and mindlessly killing everything in a shell of a character with no real plot is your thing, then yah maybe, go for it, this game was designed for you!
