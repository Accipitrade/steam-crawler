\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best single player FPS experiences ever created, and still the best Doom game to date. While Eternal is deeper, more complex, and more challenging, there is a brutal, efficient simplicity to Doom 2016 that makes it eternally memorable (pun not intended).

Still visually stunning in 2022, with a surprising amount of lore to dig into and a gameplay loop that simply can't be beat, especially when factoring in weapon mods and their upgrades, which are so brilliantly done in that they constantly encourage (not require) you to change up playstyles and approaches so that you can master each weapon's various ultimate modified attacks.

Throw in a hard-hitting, hardcore metal soundtrack and a suitably laconic main character who doesn't waste time with words when a shotgun to the face will do, and Doom 2016 is as good as it gets. If this game isn't in your library already, it's time you rectified that mistake.\t\t\t\t\t
