Intro
Technically, the retro inspired FPS trend started with Hard Reset, which was then followed up by Painkiller: Hell and Damnation, Rise of the Triad (2013) and Wrack. But when Doom (2016) came out, it was followed by a lot more retro inspired FPS games. Like Amid Evil, Dusk, Hellbound, Ion Fury and so much more and for good reason. Doom (2016) in a way, laid the foundation for those great shooters with how well rounded it was and still is.

Positives
+ Great soundtrack.
+ Good replay value.
+ Great enemy variety.
+ Great weapon variety.
+ Great aesthetics and graphics.
+ Good amount of character and weapon skill trees.

Negatives
- All the grenades share the same ammo pool.
