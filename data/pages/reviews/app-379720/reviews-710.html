\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016 is a really solid first person action shooter i will give it that. And if you are interested in this genre of the game this is for you. But from my experience the game got incredibly boring after around 6 hour mark. Nothing new after that was introduced and the game stayed the same with level design and enemy layout. Whole game can be summed up to this:
>enter new area
>2-5 waves of enemies spawn
>you kill all of them
>next area
>end of lvl
>repeat 16 times
I am aware that the game was advertised to be a action shooter and nothing besides that but this still felt uninspired and boring.
The story was uninteresting and i never felt the need to actually look into the uninteresting world the game is in.
All these things considered this game is good at what it does and what it advertised to do. Just nothing else besides. If you want to shoot around 15 types of enemies in the formula i mentioned above. Feel free to.\t\t\t\t\t
