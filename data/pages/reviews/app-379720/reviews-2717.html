\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is simply fast paced FPS intensity. TLDR= Buy it if you want to smash demons in the face and then chainsaw any monster in a satisfyingly gory mess, all in the name of survival and conquest.

It contains all the classic weapons and the level design, gameplay, pacing, and fun factor are all there. It's violent and visceral, with stunning graphics that ran beautifully on my system, and sound design and effects put me there in the thick of battle. The story never gets in the way, thankfully just there to usher you to the next room of death. There is no stealthy, creeping segments, it is all-out-war all the time. Every weapon had its purpose and I found myself utilizing each one in their most apllicable situation, more than other recent fps game relies this heavy on tool-for-the-job mentality, and it is refreshing rather than frustrating. I didn't encounter many glitches, just took a fall through the floor once or twice. Controller support for a PS4 pad is better than average.

The multiplayer is fun as well, though I have trouble connecting and often I end up getting dropped, but the many matches I have played gave me all the thrills I remember, in updated fashion. The community feels inviting and I died many deaths. I wish the community was larger however, or that you could play specific game modes with bots, to practice, I feel there is far too much waiting around, and several of the game modes don't seem to come as often, since I need to find new servors almost every other match. The are many rooms in the single player campaign that I wish made into the multiplayer as maps, so maybe the community will come up with that. When I am playing the multiplayer though, it is a great time for sure.

If you like running around and killing, listen to the other million reviews like mine and buy this.\t\t\t\t\t
