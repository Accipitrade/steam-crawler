\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat's there to say? It's DOOM. Updated, refined, reborn.

While players were initially wary due to some warning signs leading up to its release, DOOM absolutely put those worries aside the second people heard those chilling words:

They are rage, brutal, without mercy. But you, you will be worse.
Rip and tear, until it is done.

You wake up, get your armor, and get to work.

Doom 2016 is a fast paced, fast-killing rampage through the legions of hell, and it's amazing. One really struggles to think of a game with a more fluid combat system; the Slayer is fast, his vengance swift, and that comes across perfectly the way you're able to zip around battlefields of demons, weaving or dodging or simply getting up close and personal with your chainsaw. The system of "glory kills" even gives you a way to dispatch weakened demons for health/ammo pickups, so the pace never falls flat. You're not wasting time looking for health packs or waiting for your life to regen in the middle of a battle. If you want healing, it will come from the nearest Imp's rib cage.

There's a kind of sublime efficiency to this game; combat wastes no time with long action sequences, there's never any dialogue ever interrupting a firefight, and the game is really really good about giving you moments of downtime, or even just putting a few demons around to not really threaten you, but still keep you on your toes.

My only two true nitpicks are that the "upgrades" you get form Praetor tokens are kind of useless and like one or two of the guns kind of become useless once you get some of the later ones.

But these are iceubes of complaints sitting next to a glacier of praise.

Seriously, get this game. 2016's GOTY, absolutely no contest.\t\t\t\t\t
