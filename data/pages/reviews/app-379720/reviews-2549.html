\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first I wasn't sure if I could even run this game. Every part of my pc is good enough except from the processor (AMD-FX6300) which I thought would be a problem. However, I bought the game to find I could run the game at high settings on constant 60fps. It's smooth gameplay and easy to learn combat system enables you to really enjoy the game rather than tr harding just to be able to get over its learning curve.

The campaign is enjoyable filled with pretty much none-stop action and when you aren't ripping demons in half you can be searching for secrets to upgrade your Praetor suit or find cool little items like toys of your character. One of my issues was that it felt like there wasn't enough boss battles and there is only really one fairly difficult one, which is the final boss.
The perk system is easy to use and th weapon upgrade system is great, including the fact that you can hold a key to change your mod in seconds just for a situation it may require.

The multiplayer is one of the best I've played in a FPS. Its chaotic style and a range of fun game modes make it fairly addicting. You can play as a grenadier blowing everything in sight up with a rocket launcher just to switch the a grenade launcher when your done. There is a huge amount of weapon, rune and demon combinations to choose from, each changing the way you play. There is nothing more satisfying in the multiplayer than going on a huge killstreak with the epic announcer voice saying what killstreak your own whilst rampaging as one of the 6-7 demons.

It is definately worth every penny. Highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
