There's very little to this new DOOM other than the best gunplay in any FPS ever, and certainly recently.

Just like with Doom 1 and 2 there's little reason to keep playing other than to keep kiling demons. It's fun as hell, but shallow to the point that if I died I just quit to desktop instead of replaying the 60 seconds from the last save point.

Graphics are really nice, but so ORANGE. Good lord. The whole game is ORANGE, except for a couple blue parts. Levels are good overall, but hunting for keys and doors can be annoying because nothing stand out and everything looks the same. You can walk by a dead guy holding a key you need 10x and not notice. The vertical aspect of levels is good and bad. It's fun to jump from 50 feet up and blow a demon away on your way down, but man the platforming and mantling up over and over feels so dated and just isn't fun.

Oh, and everybody says the MP sucks, but fundamentally it seems OK, except there is NO SP DEATHMATCH. Weak.\t\t\t\t\t
