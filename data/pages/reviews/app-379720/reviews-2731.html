\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood campaigns are hard to come by now a day, but Doom has proven that you can still find a game that has fantastic graphics, great gunplay and a fun story to experience. So far I have only played the campaign in depth, but with the four multiplayer matches I have played I have concluded it is not worth your time. The matchmaking of Doom already takes forever but on top of that Doom's PvP just isn't fun. Doom's campaign makes you feel unstoppable, from shredding Demons apart with chainsaws to using fully upgraded weaponry, you just feel like a total boss, but in the multiplayer you get guns that don't feel particularly special and slower paced gameplay. It's a very generic PvP excperience that doesn't introduce any mechanics that make it a blast to play. A new feature added to Doom is Snapmap. Snapmap is a custom level creator that's easy and fun to use. You can create your own custom Doom level or explore the crazy maps the community has created. Overall, Doom is still a fantastic game that is definatly worth you time and money, sure the multiplayer is pretty bad, but you play Doom for it's campaign, and it has the best campaign I've seen in years. I would reccomend this game to anyone who enjoys a perfectly crafted singleplayer excperience .\t\t\t\t\t
