\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI honestly didn't think my computer would run it.
But it does work.


Now for the review itself

The game takes place many years after the events of Doom64 for the Nintendo 64. The giant base on mars is crawling with monsters thanks to an experiment gone horribly wrong. As luck would have it, the head of the facility Dr Samuel Hayden has discovered you laying inside a sarcughagus and brought you back from hell to once again show the demons what for and dress up like a Lego astronaut.

One of the good things abouth this shooter compared to others, is that at the beginning you're not giving a long tutorial. Instead the game gives you a pistol and start shooting right when you wake up.

This game is extremly intense. Theres always a situation when you are locked in a room and dozens of monsters come at you and you can't leave until you've killed them all. On top of that, when an enemy's health is low enough, you can gruesomely destroy them with your bare hands. This is called "Glory Kill".

Even though this game was made for modern computers, it represents the classic FPS games from the 90's. Not to mention there are a TON of easter eggs hidden throught the game. Some of which are refferences to other games, some are to movies while others are mere jokes left by the developers.

Bottom line: Buy It Now!
Just make sure your computer meets the recommened PC specs.\t\t\t\t\t
