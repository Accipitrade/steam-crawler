\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA True Throwback to the 90's when shooters focused on the flow of the game.
This game does everything right from the fluid motion of gunplay to the intricate level design, with the little pieces of story that have been written into a well crafted game in such a way that the story isn't in your face all the time, only for small brief moments, enough to to keep you in the know with what exactly is causing what to happen within the game. The idea of the silent protagonist just wanting to do one pure and simple thing, simply to Rip and Tear. The music adds so much to the gameplay, the heavy metal ballads that play as you rip and tear through your foes adds so much to the gameplay, to the ominous sounds during the short breaks between the slaughtering zones. The game is well polished and worth it just on the single player alone. DOOM was never about the multiplayer, while it has this feature, it was never the focus for any DOOM game including this one, this one was all about the Singleplayer Campaign which has untold amounts of replayability in the different angles that you can replay the game through different weapons that are as equally crafted as the levels themselves and the demons you face. Well worth it just on the singleplayer. The Multiplayer is just icing on this amazing cake, you can choose to ignore it and scrape off the multiplayer icing or eat it with the rest of your cake. It's just an added feature that is there if you want something, and with the recent updates, it isn't actually terrible, almost harkening to the Quake style of Arena shooters.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best "old school" FPS "monster" shooters - outclassed IMO only by Shadow Warrior 2 and on par with KF2. So in other words - a great game.

Pros:
+ great singleplayer campaign
+ cooperative multiplayer mode
+ built-in level editor
+ support for community maps
+ satisfying, brutal, dynamic, old school gameplay
+ fun and engaging enemies
+ beautiful graphics
+ great soundtrack
+ nice landscapes & levels
+ customizable character in coop \\ community maps
+ customizable weapons in coop \\ community maps
+ support for "classic Doom" (DOOM1&2) textures
+ easter eggs and secrets, like in old school games

Cons:
- "meh" optimization at best, especially in campaign
- still not enough brutal "melee" and chainsaw kills animations
- only PvP MP DLC's for now, need more cowbe... SP \\ coop goodies

Haven't tried PvP MP yet, but people say it's not that good.\t\t\t\t\t
