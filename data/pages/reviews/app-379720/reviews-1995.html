\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm lucky, because I was at my teens when the original Doom game has been released back in 1993, so I went through all the series since then. I really love this incarnation, special thanks for the very good optimization of the engine, as I was able to play on 1440p at ultra settings without any frame drop. Big thanks for Valve and the Wine developers, as the game runs flawlessly on my linux machine using Proton.

The game's atmosphere and attitude is just fantastic. All of the enemies are well designed, and most of them are really hard to defeat. I like the idea, as some demons, such as the Cacodemon or the Pinky are much much stronger then they were in the original game. Glory kills are awesome.

However, I didn't enjoy the Rune trials that much. Most of them are way to hard to finish, so after three or four success, I just tried one, and if I fail, I just went back to the campaign instead.

But the function I really really missed, is the online cooperative mode, which we very loved in the original series in the 90's. I hope, in Doom Eternal this will be available. Or I would pay for a DLC, containing this feature in Doom and Doom Eternal as well.

All in all, I'm highly satisfied with the game, it is in the same league as the originals or the Doom3.

Highly recommended\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's ironic, but this game feels fresh and new. In this era of modern tacticool shooters filled with loot boxes, short and dumb (if not full of SJW culture) campaigns, pay-to-unlock stuff and a fast paced action that doesn't go along the mechanics of a modern shooter.

Why Doom feels fresh and new?
1. No health regeneration. You can't play dumb and expect your life to recover over time by running from the action (e.g. run-n-stab);
2. Player is rewarded for exploring the maps;
3. Well conceived maps. The Player will dominate the action the game by mastering the maps;
4. Minimal mechanics (e.g. no reloading, no sprint, no stamina) don't interrupt the action;
5. The glory kills (e.g. health), chainsaw (e.g. ammo for common weapons) kills and Rune abilities help you develop a personal strategy (e.g. using Rich gets Richer Rune combined with Equipment Power Rune and Siphon grenades against the Mastermind Spider);
6. A feeling of domination.

I would definitely recommend this game. It feels like a modern "Rip-n-Tear Unreal Tournament 99". Doom Eternal better not disappoint me like GTA 5 did.\t\t\t\t\t
