The combat is insanely brutal, blisteringly fast-paced, and unflinchingly unforgiving. If you quit moving, you're demon chow. DOOM is extremely well optimized technically- I get a solid 80 FPS on my laptop, and I only got 3rd degree burns on my thighs! The lore is great, and the character and level design are awesome. There's tons of things to do- collectibles to find, rune challenges to complete, and codex entries to find. But let's be honest... you're here because you have the same mission as the Doom Slayer.
