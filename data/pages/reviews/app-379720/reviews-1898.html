\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts DOOM. Need I say more?

If you don't play DOOM or know of DOOM's story, you don't know video games (particularly FPS). It is the spiritual rebirth of an era of quality arena based shooters. DOOM 2016 pays homage to John Romero and John Carmack's passion project from 1993, which launched ID Software into the mainstream. It is the game that made it to debates on the US Supreme Court for violence and graphic content. DOOM birthed a new era a gaming and inspired millions to frag each other at LAN parties and more. It paved the way for more stellar FPS titles such as Quake, Half Life, Heretic, and most FPS shooters we enjoy today. Its legacy and impact has no comparison.

DOOM 2016 gives a big F*** you to narrative limited FPS games. It tells a story at the upmost simple level. You are good, they are demons, you have weapons, now kill in any way that suits you. It is visually stunning, gory, and rewarding to play over and over again. You will enjoy the environmental storytelling, inspired much by how DOOM 3 created a UAC station for its 2004 addition. The demons are diverse in combat and design. It is a reminder of what really matters in FPS games: fast rewarding gameplay, simple feedback loops, and guns... big f******* guns.

DOOM MP is also a great addition. It was created by former Halo developers and is awesome. I argue that DOOM has better MP maps than Quake Champions. Jump in and have fun. We have a good handful of dedicated DOOMERS who play almost everyday. You will get wrecked... but you will learn.

In sum, DOOM 2016 is one of the best FPS titles released this decade and reminded us all who the grandaddy of the FPS genre is.\t\t\t\t\t
