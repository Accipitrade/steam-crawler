\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast paced and action packed as you carve through hordes of demons... Beautiful game visually and super entertaining.
The only criticism I have is of the battle style. The hordes of demons only really appear in certain areas of the map and come in waves. As you play through the game this becomes more and more predictable.
In the old Doom games your enemies were more evenly distributed throughout the level and you never knew what was coming around the next corner, which was sometimes extremely frightening and super exciting.

Having said that, this game is still excellent at giving you adrenaline rush after adrenaline rush.
