\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn exciting action shooter you can just turn your mind off to and enjoy for hours on end.

I'll start by saying this game isn't perfect, but it is pretty close. This game doesn't have much of a story, we all expect that from a Doom game, but that doesn't matter because the exciting game-play more than makes up for it. The engine, the lore and the weapons were all handled perfectly.

Everything about this game's campaign feels polished and extremely well optimized. The new idTech 6 engine is one of the most impressive engines I've seen (besides Frostbite). Everything from the lighting to the visuals is absolutely stunning. Each level is perfectly crafted to give off the vibe it was intended, from the decaying world of hell to the technologically advanced UAC labs. You feel invested in this world put out in front of you, making you want to explore each location and learn about the lore of the land.

Kudos to whoever was in charge of writing the codexs for this game, because each one you read brings you closer into this world. You are able to learn about each individual demon and every location in this world as well as the Iconic Doom Marine's story. They even leave many aspects open ended for fan speculation.

Don't even get me started on the weapons in this game, for they are absolutely perfect! Each weapon (except for an exception of two) have two weapon upgrades which you can rotate through on the fly, allowing you to have an extremely diverse arsenal at your dispose to eradicate demons with. Every weapon feels powerful and feels like there is a kick behind every bullet fired. You will never grow tired of seeing a demon explode into giblets at the power of a double barrel blast.

Ultimatly if you love First Person Shooters you will not regret this purchase. There is multiplayer and SnapMap but at the moment those are rather bare and average.

8.5/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts been 23 years since 1993 when Doom the original released and blew people's minds. Doom 2 was also hugely successful so there were high hopes for Doom3 which was less than critically acclaimed. Needless to say there was a lot riding on the return to hell in this newest iteration, simply known as Doom.

Doom 2016 feels like a return to its roots. It focuses on a run and gun combat style similar to the first 2 Doom's with high intensity battles followed by periods of exploration. The gun selection includes a wide variety of weapons to choose from and an option to mod the guns is a nice addition. Although I do wish some of the guns had more impact in the audio dept.

The graphics are superb especially onsidering the game runs like glass. I was shocked at the smoothness of play. The game focuses on combat but there is a storyline if you are interested in finding it. All in all this is a superb return for the Doom franchise and maybe the best shooter of 2016. Still the best in class.

Can't wait for Doom Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
