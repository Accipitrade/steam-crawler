\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet's start with the good stuff. Doom 2016 is a brutal, fast-paced shooter that doesn't waste your time with pointless cutscenes or exposition. It's all about the action, and boy, does it deliver. The gunplay is fantastic, with a wide variety of weapons to choose from, each with their own unique feel and purpose. The glory kill system is also a standout feature, rewarding you for getting up close and personal with your enemies in the most gruesome way possible.

But while the gameplay is certainly a highlight, it's not without its flaws. The level design is somewhat repetitive, with many of the areas feeling similar to one another. The story, while not the main focus of the game, is also fairly weak, with a predictable plot and forgettable characters. And while the graphics are generally impressive, there are occasional texture pop-ins and other visual glitches that can be distracting.

Overall, Doom 2016 is a solid addition to the series that offers plenty of adrenaline-pumping action. It's not perfect, but it's definitely worth playing for fans of the genre.\t\t\t\t\t
