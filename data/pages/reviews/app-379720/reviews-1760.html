\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game on a whim (it was on sale and promised to be a refreshing change to civ 6) , but I've been enjoying it a lot more than I expected.

The fast-paced FPS action is fun, with interesting enemies, maps, and weapons. There are a few map climbing puzzles, but a restrained degree of map complexity makes for a game that keeps up its momentum through the lulls in action. The jumping and climbing mechanics work well, and so the arcade elements of the game are fun and not frustrating.

I expected a story that was, at best, an ironically terrible excuse for otherwise unmitigated violence, but actually the story's pretty cool, and I think there's a decent chance of it coming together by the end of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
