\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom (2016) is a modern shooter with a classic style. On one hand, your weapons and armor can be upgraded like in any modern game. On the other hand, the combat kind of feels like the classic Doom from the 90's where you fight rooms full of enemies, and you fight out in the open rather than from cover. Some enemies will charge you and force you to face them head on, while a few can be handled from a bit of a distance. If you liked classic Doom, you will probably enjoy this newer entry into the franchise as well.

A new addition to the franchise is collectibles. There are many items to collect, ranging from those little Doom Guy dolls to objects that give you upgrade points for your weapons and armor. Unfortunately, it can be easy to miss a collectible, and some levels will lock you out of early sections, so it can be frustrating if you are trying to collect everything in one play through.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game isn't the best but it's great. IT's not supposed to be played for 20 hrs like me.
It's a fun game where you shoot demons on mars, simple as hell.

PROS:
It has some great voice acting by Samuel Hayden's voice actor. He sounds menacing and cool.
IT also has amazing animations shown in the first person cutscenes and the glory kills with all of their glorious brutality. The first time you pick up a new weapon, the doom guy lifts it up and shows it off to the player.
The music is spot on. It gives the game a relentless bgm to go along with the relentless shooting.
Boss fights are cool but all of them aren't hyped up well. "Close to me" from Just shapes and Beats did it best.
First Person Platforming gives a tingly feeling in the pelvis :)
Enemies are cool except for the possessed shield guy.
Enemies aren't hitscan, they shoot projectiles which can be dodged. Strafe skills matter now!!
There's so much more i wanna add. I should stop now........

CONS:
The Super shotgun is op as hell and just gets more op as you upgrade it. Kinda made the game a little easy for me. I will probably try playing it on a harder difficulty next time.

STUFF I CLEARLY DON'T HAVE OPINIONS ON:
There are side objectives in a mission to nudge you into a certain play style but it kinda gets finicky at times and you need to start save load spamming to complete them.
The second last boss fight wasn't hyped up at all imo unlike it's predecessor "THE CYBERDEMON"

I don't know what else to say. Simple good fun. Play it. Can't wait to see how the sequel works out. Leave this franchise alone Lordd Todd :[\t\t\t\t\t
