\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is great. I had zero expectations for this game, and picked it up during the new year sale. However, I was positiively surprised with how good it is. Go and buy it, good games like this deserve our support. Id Software did an awesome job.

The graphics look great and the game engine runs like a well oiled machine. It's fluid, fast and responsive. The game has a very clear, colourful and good looking art style. The gameplay is probably the most fun I had in an FPS since a long time. You shoot, run, jump, punch and kick. Non stop, lightning speed action. The maps are well made with many secret areas, powerups and perk points. The weapons are well done, each has its own unique feel and weight to it. The soundtrack and general sound design is just great. One minute it's ambient music, next it's demons running at you with bombastic heavy metal music.

This game felt like one of those games in 90s, when I was a kid. I feel like games back then were made with passion, not with greed. This is one of those games. If you want some good old fashioned, fast paced FPS which makes you feel like an absolute badass, just go ahead and buy it. Don't think twice. This is a great game.\t\t\t\t\t
