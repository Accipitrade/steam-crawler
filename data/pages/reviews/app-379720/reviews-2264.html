Overall, the game was so enjoyable that this is my second playthrough. I originally had this on PS4 but PC was a lot more fast paced and interesting. Cons: The gameplay can get a bit repetitive. Once you run out of new enemies, bashing the same demon skulls in can get a tiny bit boring. The game constantly keeps you engaged with its environment but it isn't God of War-tier to where I get a kick out of solving puzzles regularly. It's mainly running and gunning.

Regardless, this game is a must-play (at least once). I highly recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
