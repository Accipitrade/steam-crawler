\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA pretty good singleplayer shooter, by comparison with what's out on the market nowadays.

Enemy and gun variety is really good and my favorite aspect of the game. The story is surprisingly quite decent and the levels usually don't feel too stretched out.

The music, while so acclaimed by others, didn't really do it for me, being somewhat passable.

There are also all sorts of collectibles, runes, upgrades and so on. Some add a new way of playing with your weapons which is good, but others are just there to satisfy achievement hoarding craving.

I am not a big fan of the glory kill mechanic though, as I feel it interrupts the flow of the action during fights. It's also a bit silly seeing demons standing still at times or not doing damage, while you shove the heart of another fat demon up their throat. Sure ... they are visceral ... gruesome and fun to look at for a few times, but after a while they become repetitive and they ... stop you from shooting.

Solid shooter still.\t\t\t\t\t

Grew up with DOOM and Quake, this game recaptured a lot of the old excitement I had playing these as a kid. Great graphics even in 2022, intense gun fights that reward all out aggression, well balanced new mechanics for upgrading and powers that add to the fun without getting too much in the way, great level and monster design. The sound track kicks ass too. So, pretty much everything is good in this game, except that the lowliest monster is too ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ loud.\t\t\t\t\t
