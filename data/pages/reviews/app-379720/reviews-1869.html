\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe single player campaign in the best FPS I have played and is my favorite game of all time. This game is super refined and polished. It captures the spirit of the original game. Much to do and master in the game. I would recommend this game to anyone. Most of my time was spent with the single player campaign.

The multi-player is still somewhat active. This part of the game takes time to master. The rhythm of the game is important. Weapon switching, equipment, accuracy, etc. are all absolutely critical and you will die quick otherwise. Multi-player matches frequently are dominated by HIGHLY experienced level XI-50s. The balancing is downright frustration at times. Be patient and try to learn and it can be fun (at times).

Never really used SnapMap or the Arcade mode.\t\t\t\t\t
