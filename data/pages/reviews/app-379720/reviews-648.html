First-person shooters are one of my most favorite game genres out there. But in recent years they were becoming the same thing with different textures. Doom being the father of the FPS genre had to rise again. Gaming is meant to be played and not to be watched.

Doom 2016 was a reboot for the Doom franchise. It was a HUGE success. It revolutionized gaming once again. It showed that gameplay is the MAIN part of a game. Not some fancy cinematic thing. The gameplay was so much fun to experience. It was fun, which I didn't felt in other titles. Sure thing, I was late in the party, I played Doom 2016 in 2020! Always wanted to buy this game. Bought it on steam summer 2020. Downloaded it, Played it for a total of 30hours. And it was one of the best hours I have ever spent gaming.

The visuals were great, sadly due to my potato pc, I had to play it at 720p 75%, even then It looked pretty good. The textures were great. Slicing through demons was SO SATISFYING. It's a perfect game for stress relief. Hopefully one day, I will play doom 2016 and Doom Eternal on 1440p,144 fps (Some dreams right?)

Played it on normal and hard difficulty (that one below Nightmare). It was challenging but it was fun as well. In other games gives you instructions on how to do what, it's a good thing. In doom, They give you a gun, and boom now kills some demons. The arcade/arena shooter vibe takes the game on another level. Bundled with some of the best music. The gameplay combined with the music gives you a heavenly feeling.

In other games, you are in danger but in doom, YOU ARE THE DANGER. The value for money is superb. Every level every mode is enjoyable. It's a fast phase not like most other First Person Shooters. Where you have to wait to walk cut scene shoot cut scene reload end. Doom is like, take a gun and shoot until you are gone.

Overall IT'S A 666/10, HIGHLY RECOMMENDED. It's something you should definitely consider playing in your lifetime.\t\t\t\t\t
