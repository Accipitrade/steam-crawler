\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is just as fun and addictive in 2022, as it was in 2016. The game play, while much slower than DOOM Eternal, is still extremely fun. The music is amazing (Skullhacker is the best song in the soundtrack imo) and the snapmap mode is also extremely fun. The story is eh. Unlike Eternal, you can't skip cutscenes so be prepared to sit around for a minute or two to listen to Samuel Hayden talk. The game does admittedly get tedious near the end. By the time you reach the halfway point of the campaign you have already seen all of the demons available so it can be a bit of a chore to slog through the later levels. The atmosphere of DOOM also borrows heavily from Doom 64 and Doom 3 rather than Ultimate Doom or Doom 2. All in all I would recommend playing DOOM for a fun pick up and play FPS experience.\t\t\t\t\t
