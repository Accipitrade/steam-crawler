Imagine navigating team deathmatch arenas for the duration of a AAA shooter. This is Doom. And it's actually the best case scenario for a sequel of this type.

You got the guns. The demons. All the gore you could want. It plays beautifully. All the guns feel chunky. The executions are great. The boss fights are, well, boss fights, and not all that great, but they're something.

But the one thing that lets this game down from being a true classic is that it's just out and out the same.
