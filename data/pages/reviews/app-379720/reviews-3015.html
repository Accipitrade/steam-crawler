\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely an amazing game, worth whatever price it's at.
I bought this game for the campaign, haven't even played the multiplayer, so I cannot give any opinion on that mode.

id has done a great job at capturing the classic FPS feel, with a game that values enjoyable gameplay over anything else. Easily the most satisfying gunplay I've experienced in a while, with fast-paced and engaging combat. It's also extremely good looking, with plenty of graphical options, and optimized well to run on a wide range of hardware.

The level design is a little bit lacking (all stages generally fit one of 4-5 general themes), but don't let that dissuade you; The maps themselves are intricate and detailed, with plenty of secrets in each. The secrets and nods to the previous games add to the charm, and the well-placed moments of humor are always a welcome, if momentary, break from the action.

I cannot recommend this game enough. If you are a fan of the FPS genre, you owe it to yourself to play this.\t\t\t\t\t

As soon as that demo landed, great marketing strategy by the way, I gave it a shot and was hooked immediately.
