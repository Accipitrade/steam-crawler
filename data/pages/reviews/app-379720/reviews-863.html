\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had this game lying in my inventory over a year and now recently I decided to install it. I have to say this game really exceeded my expectations. It has to be in the top 10 of best single-player games I've ever played. It feels like DOOM captures the spirit of 90's FPS-games but does it with a modern touch.

Firefights are fast paced in this game. Combat feels very nice and just enough challenging; it didn't feel frustrating but instead it motivated me to be tougher. You get good arsenal of weapons; there aren't plenty of them but each one of those are useful in some situation excluding pistol what is quite weak. You can also pick up power-ups and simply destroy bunch of enemies with only your fists! Fun as hell..

Game's events take place in Mars where the player character Doom Slayer is waken up and finds himself in the middle of demonic invasion of the facility. Our hero fights to return the evil creatures back to hell. Story is straightforward but interesting; you can also find small bits of information from the levels and read them. They offer lots of information from characters, enemies, places etc. what can help you to understand Doom universe. Levels in this game are amazing and fun to play; despite game being bit linear you get multiple routes to choose from and I never felt bored. Also you can spend more time in the levels if you want to find all secrets, it can be quite fun but its purely optional. If you get lost you can check your location from a map what is clear and easy to read. Graphically this game is great; devs really nailed the looks of orange-toned Mars planet and lighting looks pretty convincing. Game felt really stable without any bugs and I got constantly 60 fps without any hiccups.

Towards ending the game puts your all skills to test and there will be few challenging boss fights. Along the way you can upgrade your weapons and choose some mods to fit your own playstyle. All of these are well thought and mods are easy to apply. In summary this game is really close to a masterpiece and I can't really think any big flaws with it. It took around 15 hours me to explore and complete the campaign. I definitely recommend this game! Game has good value for money; I think I paid 10 euros from it during sale and it's maybe my best 10 euros spent to a pc game\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPrefer Doom 2016 to Doom Eternal any day for 5 reasons.

One, Specifications. Doom 2016 runs on older machines, runs on open gl also whereas Doom Eternal only runs on Vulkan. Not much lost in detail as both are action games and no one hangs around to admire the HD. The Post-industrial look of Doom 2016 is anyway astounding and the Hell landscapes unique and adequate. Doom Eternal looks old and worn out - a cheap copy.

Two, Artwork. The colors, fonts, boxes, demon's faces, are much much better in Doom 2016, the ones in Doom Eternal look clumsily designed in comparison.

Three, Action. You can actually kill the zombies and imps with melee attacks in Doom 2016 whereas in Doom Eternal you can just push them back and you have to use your precious ammo on each one of them. Aiming is really difficult on consoles with the demons jostling you about. Also the demons fight amongst each other in both games but in Doom Eternal its all playacting - no one dies and you have to do the job yourself.

Four. Platforming. The crazy mindless platforming.....there's too much of it and makes the Doom Guy into a monkey. And the Climbable walls and Dash Charge-ups hanging in midair are really bad design. One can rationalise that earlier DoomGuys left ammo, weapons and health packs scattered about. But who constructed those stippled walls and left the Dash Charge-ups and convenient Monkey-bars in mid-air. Makes no sense. Maybe platforming works on a PC but on a console it is a horrible experience with therapist-level damage done to us players when we fall for the 50th time. The maddening thing is (at the end of the Exultia level) if you fall you respawn in an earlier checkpoint with slightly less health, and you have to literally commit suicide to loose all health and respawn in the checkpoint you started from. Crazy.
