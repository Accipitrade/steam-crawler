You're a walking nightmare.. for DEMONS !

At first I really got trouble getting into the game because of the complex horrible map design and menu information. I felt that for an obviously fast paced shooter, it was taking me too much time to figure out that map and explore the menu. Personally, the worst part of it is that the map rotates around your position (arrow on map), and not you (arrow on the map) changing direction on a static map like in a normal map design. I put a lot of emphasis on that because it really made me hate game until I played it for a longer period of time. I would've also enjoyed a flashlight thank you very much!

Now past the negative, it is a really fun fast-paced shooter. You just can't ask for more action and guns really. Some people run away and hide from demons, while you, you just can't get enough of them, and that no matter how big they get! The heavy-metal style soundtrack just won't allow you to quench your thirst for more! You also get too find cool secrets, like short old doom maps that you get to walk into for a trip back into the past. Good way to feel nostalgic when you're old enough to have lived to play the original game when it came out.\t\t\t\t\t

An absolute joy from start to finish. The game is fast, fun and brutal.

The Good:
- The best boss battles in any FPS I have played
- Amazing music
- good level design
- Weapon variety
- Chainsaw
- Story with no cutscenes
- Interesting characters
- Enemy design
- Fast paced combat
- Custom level creator
- Easter eggs
- Animations

The Bad:
- Most multiplayer modes

The Other:
I experienced no bugs on my first playthrough, but on the second one some enemies didn't spawn and I fell through the floor twice.\t\t\t\t\t
