\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy Two old Reviews are below.
After beating the game on the hardest mode (from 1st play threw) I can finally say, my old reviews old true.This is not a game that I found fun. You might wonder why I took the time to beat it?

DooM, DooM2 and even Final DooM are important games to me. I have been playing them for over 20 years and still play them today. This game, I can never see myself playing again. I'm not saying it was bad, but I wasn't having too much fun at the same time.

The only times I would die are on the 1st and last boss and that was just learning their patterns.The game play is very simple and there is no thinking to it. Play the Older DooM on the higher levels and you'll see that there is a lot of movement skill and manipulation (such as Gaining and Maintaining speed). There is Level Memorization and unit Memorization. This had NONE of that. It was very Lack Luster.

You my think I am being nostalgic but I am willing to bet (if mankind makes it this far) that none of you will be playing this 20 years from now. It's maybe worth running Once. But for the FULL price (and i got it for $5) I would be very upset.

I could go on but it's not really worth it. The Beth Fanboys will be Beth Fanboys.
If you can get it cheap, try it. I kind of want my 20 hours back and I left it idle for maybe an hour or so. Its just very Meh.

----

Update: After 9 or more hours my below review still holds up. I will beat the game only to see if it can get any better. It's not a bad game! But it's not DooM, its a better Doom 3 for sure, but that too was not DooM or DooM 2 or Master Levels or Final DooM for that matter. That wasn't even GREAT but still DooM.

If you can get it on Sale, give it a try. It's really noting new or nothing old sadly. It's just that middle ground that will keep all the newbs and Beth fan boi's happy.

Almost a full 60Gb DEMO for your "Free Weekend."

From what I've seen these are bland and dull, I feel like I have already played them before somwhere o_O

But you only let me see that much and for that little time I'm not giving you $15 to find out if all the levels are this boring-ly thrown together. Also you start to see a very obvious horde theme going on, well, Painfully Obvious! "Oh this level seems dead, oh look this giant heart thing right in the center of the map, wonder if I'm about to fight a horde..."

Yeah, no thanks Bethesda. Your laziness seems to only be surpassed by your ego.\t\t\t\t\t
