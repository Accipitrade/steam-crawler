\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is a fun and captivating game and a solid successor to the originals of the 90s and early 2000s. The game is solid in its story, combat, sound and graphics. I bought this game on sale for $15 and I must say that is the appropriate price for a game like this. It isn't necessarily long and will probably take you around 10 hours to complete if you're exploring somewhat and trying to complete each mission. The multiplayer is fine unlike the criticism the game got years ago. I don't think the MP is breathtaking but it's not bad and it's not what you buy this game for. The story is adequate and the lore system in the game is very informational and fun to read. Level design in the game is also very solid with tons of secrets to discover. The weapons in the game are fantastic and the upgrade system is very fun adding variety to your guns. Combat with these weapons is extremely fun and the glory kills or executions in the game are super exciting. It's like Shadow of Mordor but a FPS, it's a dumb fun game. I recommend this game to buy even at it's base price of $20. That's a steal for the amount of time you will spend in this game and the quality of the game in its entirety.\t\t\t\t\t
