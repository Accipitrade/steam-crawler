\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI refunded this game and initially had a review as 'do not recommend'. At the time, I had played only 2 hrs and felt that those early parts of the game seemed too slow paced and somewhat tedious. I also got a bit of a headache playing and that only made it worse......

I never could really shake the feeling that I didn't give the game a decent chance, and off the back of hearing so many positive things and the excitement in the lead up to doom eternal I decided to go back and give this title another shot.

And I was so glad I did. First, to combat the dizziness I had i just fixed some of the motion settings and increased FOV. Secondly, i was pleasantly surprised to find my old save was still there so I didnt have to play the first 3 sections. It was at this point the fights actually started getting interesting with lots more different enemy types, weapons abilities, getting thrown into the mix that the combat was just a joy to play. Oh boy that destruction. It seems last time around I quit just before the action was about to get really good.

Another great thing about this game are the secrets. When ever you try to find alternative paths or secret locations, you are rewarded with something meaningful. Maybe a power suit upgrade, or a challenge room. The secrets reminded me of older games that actually had a reward waiting at the end of them and not just some cosmetic item or collectable (im looking at you jedi fallen order).

Towards the end of the game my PC crashed and unfortunately it corrupted my save file. I couldnt fix it even after trying out all the online suggested solutions. I didnt want to start a new save, and I didnt want to quit either, so I ended up borrowing a friends save file just so I could play the last few sections and it was worth it.

I can recommend this game, give it some time at the beginning, make back up saves, and if you are getting dizzy increase FOV and take breaks.

Enjoy.
