\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow, it's a completely different game when it's not pitch black! I've probably played over half the campaign nearly blind, not being able to see much more than the green and white indicator lights. After running around for hours looking for the blue skull on Titan's Realm, I looked up a video walkthrough, and noticed the game was supposed to have colors other than black and brown, you should actually be able to see textures, not just silhouettes.
Anyway, after determining I was experiencing a bug, I changed some settings and found that my anti-aliasing had to be set to regular FXAA or SMAA.
I'm on a 960M on Linux. Not sure if this is a Proton issue or what, but if for some reason you can't see a damn thing, check your anti-aliasing. My campaign just got a whole lot easier, lol
Other than that, there's good sound design, fun weapons, and decent story pacing. It's an overall great game when it works properly.\t\t\t\t\t
