\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHow to make crack cocaine

1: Mix cocaine and baking soda at a 4:1 ratio (4 parts cocaine to one part baking soda). Make sure they are chopped into fine powder and mixed well, then combine them into the glass container.

2: Add a few drops of water until the mixed powder becomes like thin mud.

3: Heat the bottom of the container, evenly, until the entire mixture begins to boil.

4: Once the mix starts to boil, remove from heat and let the mixture cool. After cooling, it should be a mass of hardened crack that still has bubbles of air-pockets in it from boiling.

5: Remove the air bubbles. To remove the bubble air-pockets in the crack rock, simply add more water into the glass container and reheat the mixture until you have a solid yellow crack-blob floating in the water.

6: Let the water and crack-blob cool, then remove the hardened mass.

7: Your crack is now ready to be packaged and sold.\t\t\t\t\t
