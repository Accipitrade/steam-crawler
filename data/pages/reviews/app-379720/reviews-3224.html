Good beginning, nice presentation and execution, fun and brutal game play.

Cons(the ONLY CON):
Bad ending. Let me rephrase that, ending that I didn't expect.

BUT! This game as a whole, secluding the story, is a really well reboot of the game.

I am a wordless avatar of machismo in power armor, murderpunching demons into geysers of kung-fu-movie blood spray. I will flood the inferno with testosterone and stand over the last drowning hellbaron and laugh, firing plasma charges maniacally into the abyss.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was a big fan of the original, Duke Nukem ea. Much later I went on with Borderlands, Bioshock etc. Just to say I'm not really a huge FPS-player.

This game was a welcoming - if that's possible - kick in the teeth for me. It plays so fast and fluid. Demons attack you every ten feet and it's great. If you have a moment of rest, you have a ton of secrets to try and find. The music is great, play it loud. The different glory kill animations are a nice tough and no they won't slow you down.

Gunplay is great, the exploring 'platforming' is more like parcours, really well implemented. Every weapon is very different from the next one and good because of it. Especially the chain saw and BFG of course.

The campaign is fairly long and, well, full of what you'd expect from a classic Doom game.

Definitely recommend this game if you like the genre, are a Doom fan or just want something else.

Note: I do not play multiplayer so you'll have to figure that out for yourself.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 4 (DOOM) is absolutely fantastic, It's everything i wanted in a new doom game.
Campaign is excellent, shooting the out of demons is fun. fast combat, lots to explore and get rewarded for doing so.
The very few times it locks me into a room and talks at me only get annoying when playing on ultra-nightmare, or replaying levels.
Snap map is cool, though clearly not designed for PC and lacks a few features like texturing and actual geometry editing.Nno real scripting tools, it's all gui with drag and drop elements, would of liked to see event viewers for things. (I want something like the hammer editor)
Multiplayer just doesnt work for me. I'll get into a game and then it sits at 00% loading forever, to the point i have to kill the process. The majority of everything else is about 2-3 second loading time.
Some serious performance issues, sometimes dropping below 20fps for no reason. (it's an AMD issue from what i can tell)

Overall it's a great game and any issues currently will most likely be patched out.
If you're a Doom fan you've already bought it, if you're new the the series this is a good mix of modern and classic.
Oh and the soundtrack is killer.\t\t\t\t\t

It has some good action elements like climbing and upgrading weapons but it does not compensates the lack of immersion. The Scenarios and the enviroments are well though but not well designed. Everything is so damn bright that doesnt even leave room for the curiosity to find out whats in the missing dark corners waiting for your blood.

The enemies are just patterns theres barely any enemy that can hit me or looks like a challenge.

This is a game for those who like dumbless empty straight shooters.
Feels like a multiplayer played against bots, almost like an unreal tournament game.

There are parts of the game where you just wander arround lost in the huge map trying to figure out how to get to the landmark.

getting to the end of the level you realize you forgot to pick a secret and you want to go back 10 miles just to get that boost and end up wasting another 10 mins of ur life without any action cause you already killed everything.

The game allows you to kill the enemies in unique ways... its fun for 5 mins then it gets boring as hell. as hell... get it?

There are better games that can achieve the feeling this game gives for half of the price and i bet many of them are in early access.

My guess is that they were trying to make their own "competive multiplayer game" that are the trend nowadays rather than a good single player.

can't wait for the high definition edition....

The game looks much better on the trailer by the way. And im playing with ultra settings.

Well... at least the weapons are good.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust a statement before I go any further. I LOVE DOOM i grew up playing it as a kid and I played the original before this one and like many people who started with the original I expected this particular installment to be half-assed. Fortunately I was quite impressed by what i saw. After playing through the quite satisfying campaign I wanted to do it all over again on nightmare difficulty. The music is superb the combat is basically a modernized blend of classic doom and Quake 3. There is NO reloading just like in the original doom and the speed of your character is just as fast as the original. You can pick up armor and health powerups as well AND play on a few of the original levels from DOOM I and DOOM II. The multiplayer aspect is where I really expected it to be terrible, but suprisingly its quite satisfying. Its just like Quakes multiplayer. Idk why people said it is like Halo multiplayer because IT IS NOT AT ALL!! The only real downside of the game is that the Spider-Mastermind and Cyberdemon are both Bosses and not major enemies like in DOOM I and II. Other than that the game is supurb and would recommend ANY classic DOOM vet give it a shot I promise you wont be disapointed.\t\t\t\t\t
