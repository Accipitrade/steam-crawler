\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust finished the campaign on Hard, and everything I'm writing it's with that difficulty in mind. No more, no less.

I've died between 90 to 140 times throughout the 14 missions.

It's an outstanding game, and it remains true and faithful to the previous two DOOM classics (1 and 2).

The fear, horror or terror that you could feel on the DOOM 3 and Resurrection of Evil are absolutely gone. This is a pure run and gun FPS, you haven't got any time to take a breath, and it's insanely fun.

PROS:
Frenetic Action all the time
Perfectly Optimized
Outstanding Graphics
Good Story
Keeps the art design of the Enemies and guns
Very Good Level design
It blends a lot of classic, old school things such as the combat with the RPG things that we're all used to in the second decade of the XXIst century.

CONS:
Boss fights are EASY compared with most mob fights. It feels like bosses needed better mechanics or design, because you might die only once or twice when you meet them.
When you've upgraded some guns the game becomes really easy, like the Precision Shot on the Gauss Cannon, or the Super Shotgun.
Secret/Collectibles/Codex wasn't polished when it came out. It's really annoying getting them and looking at what you just unlocked.

OVERALL SCORE:
91/100
