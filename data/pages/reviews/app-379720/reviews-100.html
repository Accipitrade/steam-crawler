\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016 is another great note in the Doom series, full price or dirt cheap, it's worth getting.
The community says it's some sort of reboot maybe, never can get a precise answer on this.

Gameplay
  • It's a bit faster paced than the build engine games and most of the demons you're going to fight will be in arenas instead of roaming.
  • You don't need glory kills to kill enemies, but it is very useful if you're low on health.
  • You find collectibles throughout each level to upgrade your weapons, armor, health, and ammo. Weapons are primarily upgraded by just killing demons, each level has five weapon upgrades you earn through killing demons.
  • You have a grenade which I barely used because your guns do the job fine, these recharge over time.
  • Each weapon mod has a final upgrade you obtain by completing a challenge for it.
