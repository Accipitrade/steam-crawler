10/10

Not done with the game yet, but 5 hours in and this game is utterly amazing.
The amount of polish, quality, and care for the game is incredible.

Pros:
Gunplay is a 10/10
Glory kills are the best thing on this Earth
The difficulty system is great.
The level design is some of the best I have seen.
Feels like an old game in terms of gameplay which in this instance is not a bad thing at all.
Like mentioned before, the animations are fantastic.
The amount of details is incredible.
Exploration is encouraged due to secrets and other goodies I wont spoil.

Cons:
The story is quite simple, but still really intresting and keeps you motivated. But then again its a DOOM game, your not buying it for the story.
It was hard coming up with just one con off the top of my head, I really cant see anything that bad with the game.


Should you buy?
Of course! This is one of my favorite games now. If you have the money, this game is a must buy from me.\t\t\t\t\t

Arenas and enemy waves were always over pretty quickly. I just ran around with the Super Shotgun the whole time and everything died easily. The game looks very very nice and runs extremely smoothly, which is critical for a very fast paced shooter. (Ryzen 2600 6 core, nvidia 1060 6gb)
