For a period of time I considered FPS games my genre. My ♥♥♥♥. Then they grew stale. New CoD after CoD, new attempts at realism from Battlefield, new tired Halo installations. OK. I get it. There are a lot of features that can be incorporated into a FPS game. Cool. Too bad all those features take away from the fun of the FPS genre. Until now.

This Doom is an example of what the FPS genre is at its core. Gamers are faster, so Doom is faster. Gamers are bored, so Doom brings 100% action.

The campaign is glorious. No FPS campaign in the last 10 years compares. Poor multiplayer aside, 10/10. Play and see for yourself. If you don't have fun, maybe you should pick up Chess instead.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game feels very blah compared to Doom 3. Doom 3 had a much better environment feeling and setup more like dead space. This doom feels a lot like a gear of war rip. I understand they kind of wanted to return to the classic setup, with simple map designs and lots of combat. The glory kills are cool but get old fast and the story line is kind of there but you're not a part of it feeling. It would be cool if you were given choices in the story line that affected the out come, instead you just go from level to level killing demons over and over again. Also the levels are very linear and aren't open world to explore in. The game even on ultra violence was pretty easy to beat and I had it conquered in just 2 days (about 12 hours). I understand what they were trying to do but this isn't the 90's anymore and FPS games have evolved quite a bit, more is expected out of them now.

I would not recommend this game until the 60$ price tag comes way down. This game currently is only worth about 30$. Save your money get it on sale!!\t\t\t\t\t

The only negative for this game that I can think of is that the story is weak, but meh, it's a FPS, not an adventure game, if I want a story, I'll read a book or watch a film!
Thinking about it, this is a positive as I find cutscenes or story dialogue get in the way, pause the action, drag the experience out, wasting my time while I could be running and jumping around demons, whilst aiming mini-guns at their heads and then ripping said heads off with my hands!
