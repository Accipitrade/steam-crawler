\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is definitely not old DOOM or DOOM 3, it is simultaneously it's own thing while honoring the classic very well. The gameplay encourages you get up close and personal with all but the most powerful denizens of hell and while it is possible to back off and slow the pace, it doesn't really feel encouraged. This game does have more of a narrative than the original's ending cutscenes but so far it hasn't really gotten in the way of the basic "kill all the demons" purpose of the DOOM series. One thing you will find in this game that wasn't really a thing in previous games is platforming. This game has a jump button, and you will use it! I have been having a lot of fun - and a little frustration - smashing, shooting, and chainsawing my way through the hordes of hell, and I think you will too!\t\t\t\t\t
