\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you've been waiting for a great new Doom game, this is it!

I've played doom since I was about 5 years old on my cousin's old DOS machine. It's always been one of my favorite franchises and I've played it regularly throughout my life. I'm not going to say I didn't like Doom 3, it was a good game. But it didn't feel like Doom. This game, it feels like Doom.

The action is fast paced, the monsters are plentiful, the gore is beautiful, and they didn't fuss with adding too many modern FPS elements like ADS. The game is well optimized and runs at over 100FPS on moderate machine.

On top of this, SnapMap is a joy to use and id has been updating it to expand functionality since launch. I've spent hours just building maps and using the logic to take gameplay to places the game doesn't originally support.

If you're on the fence about this game, just get it, you won't regret it.\t\t\t\t\t
