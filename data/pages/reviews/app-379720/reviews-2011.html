\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM 2016 is the long awaited follow up to the rather dissappointing (but still enjoyable) Doom 3. Doom 3 left a sort of empty spot for hardcore fans. it was a good game but didn't hit every check point. the other characters in the game lessened the "last man on earth" feeling the games are known for, and the emphasis on horror and story removed from the combat emphasis. DOOM 2016 restored that feeling. it brings back the feeling of a marine waking up as all hell breaks loose knowing only that he has to fight his way out.
the lack of cover forcing you to use movement as armor, the limited ammo reserves, the constant need to keep moving and keep fighting all make the game challenging and fun to play. With the way it forces you to dance around enemies in high energy fights and run in for glory kills, it almost feels more like a Gun based hack and slash than it does an FPS.
Definitely pick it up if you haven't played this yet.\t\t\t\t\t
