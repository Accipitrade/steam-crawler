Then I played Doom Eternal and I needed more.

The gameplay is quite good, however inferior to Doom Eternal. Some guns are enjoyable, some aren't. You will never run out of ammo which makes the game sometimes trivial. Death mostly come from cheap one shot fall, random spawning enemies and the many one shot environnement mechanics.

This is a solid 8/10. Get it while it's on sale for sure.\t\t\t\t\t
