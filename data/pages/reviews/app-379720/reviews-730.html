\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Kneel before the King!"

The Doom series retains the king of FPS moniker with the 2016 Doom. Took 12 years, and what a comeback!

Doom 2016 is incredibly fast paced and relentless. There are some quiet moments in game, sure, but it's just so the player can get a breather between the endless demon killing and exploration. The entire game is about rush. There's no cover, no health regeneration ability and if you stand still even for a second during combat, you're dead. You get health and ammo by killing demons, and the game throws a sh*t ton of demons with big f***ing guns at your disposal. Ripping and tearing time begins.

Game is also well optimised on the PC platform. I'm getting 100-140 fps on ultra settings on my G4560+GTX 1050 Ti combo.

P.S.: Mick Gordon's soundtrack is killer!

9/10, recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
