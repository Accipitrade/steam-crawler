It's a callback to earlier days of shooters; the classic romp of seemingly endless waves of enemies that each require a different strategy to kill.

Doom paints a bloody picture with each fight. New mixes of enemies in dense and novel environments create a new experience each encounter and only through a full understanding of the nature of each beast can a player overcome the onslaught. Forget the strategies ingrained in you from more contemporary shooters and instead embrace something between bullet-hell and smart platforming. You'll have a lot more fun.\t\t\t\t\t
