\r\n\t\t\t\t\t An absolute classic and loads of fun. I don't have much experience in the previous titles but this game alone is worth it. Filled with secrets, collectibles, challenges, and exhilarating combat, this game is a modernized blast from the past kind of shooter. It's violent as hell and fast paced. If you aren't on the move then you're done. The story isn't very exciting I suppose, since all it is "oh no stop the demons from destroying us." But it really doesn't matter as DOOM's premise and major selling point is, as stated, the combat. With multiple weapons to use and plenty of ways to upgrade them, you can approach slaughtering the waves of demons in any way you please. Just recently replayed this game with my new PC on Ultra-Violence and it was a lot of fun. At this review's posting, DOOM: Eternal is in about two days. 8/10. Rip and tear.\t\t\t\t\t
