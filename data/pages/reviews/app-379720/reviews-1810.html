Snappy, hectic, bloody cathartic.

It's going to be hard to play regular 'ol shooters after this.

The game goes like this: you're on a Mars facility that's been redecorated with blood, by demons. Oops, that wasn't supposed to happen. Oh well, good thing the higher-ups have an ace up their sleeve - you. An armored soldier wielding a smorgasboard of weapons with a hard-on for demon guts so insatiable, you will stop at nothing to pry apart every last demon skull, torso and stomach. Not even the end credits will stop you.

Granted, Doom isn't brilliant from the get-go. The first few missions are a little slow and dry. It's only after a while that you begin to really get dancing in the groove the game expects you. And what a spasmodic, frenetic dance it is.
You're on a lovely stroll through the apocalyptic environments of Mars, when suddenly you find yourself in an open space with plenty of convenient ledges to mount. But oh no! There's demons over there. You take care of them, no problem. But now there's three of them from behind! And now two big ones from your new flank! Then twenty more from all around! Boom boom boom! Your shotgun's empty! Switch to Machine gun! That one's empty too! Gauss rifle it is! BWZOOM BWZOOM! Powerup! Crash! Slam! Bam! Shglehght!

The animation and sound design are what sells it. You can almost feel the shower of demon shreds pattering down your metal skin.

You really ought to try it. Wait for it to go on sale for 7 smacks, it'll be great.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSingle player is one of the best FPS solo episodic experiences you can find, if you want to shoot things in the face till they pop this is the game for you. The multiplayer is dead, on a good night you can get a full lobby but on most days/nights you'll be lucky to find 4-6 people and hope no one is afk farming. The multiplayer modes and gameplay are wonderful but without people there isn't much point. Still one of the best FPS of all time so worth playing the single player, but I wouldn't hope for much in multiplayer at this point.\t\t\t\t\t
