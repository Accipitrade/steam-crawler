\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: This is what testosterone poisoning feels like. And it's bloody amazing.

The Premise:
You are DOOMGUY. You've walked Hell for who knows how long, just tearing a bloody path and killing everything unlucky enough to get in your way. You were eventually defeated (not killed!) and entombed by the demons. The humans found you, and unleashed you after screwing up they hell energy mining operation (called Argent Energy).
The Feel:
Pure unbridled RAGE in the best possible way. Even as someone who can barely shoot straight I really enjoy the gunplay, and the limited ammo is just limited enough to push me for the glory kills but plenty enough not to feel actually limited. The environment may SEEM scary, but during gameplay, you won't feel a thing. I cannot play Dead Space, because I'm so skittish, but this doesn't scare me in the slightest, which is a good thing.
The Gameplay:
You have to use your fast movement speed to counteract your extremely limited ammo capacity. It seems counterintuitive to limit ammo that much, but it makes sense with the Glory Kill ssystem. When you damage a demon through some way, you can press a Glory Kill, a brutal melee finisher which rewards you with health and sometimes ammo. These include but not limited to: tearing off a demons arm, and proceeding to beating it to death with said arm, punching off a demons head with a single uppercut, breaking a demons spine with one kick and smashing its head into the concrete, and many more. Think of it as Mass Effects Vanguard, except bloody, on 'roids and CONSTANT ROIDRAGE.
The Upgrades:
Each weapon has two different upgrades, and you'll need to hunt down floating robots to access said weapon. These are upgrades like now your shotgun can chamber 3 rounds and shoot them in rapid succession, or explosive shots. You can also upgrade your armour to various effects, like reduced explosive barrel damage, enhanced scanner and map, faster item cooldown, but to be honest, the entire upgrade system is totally missable and unnecessary.
The Verdict:
DO IT! JUST DO IT!\t\t\t\t\t
