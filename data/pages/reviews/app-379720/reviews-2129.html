\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn DOOM you play as the Doom Slayer, tasked with shooting every single demon that comes across your way in your path to ridding our Solar System of Hell's forces for good. And that's all you need to know. Right from the get go, DOOM establishes itself as the type of game that is very much aware of why gamers want to play it, and doesn't try to be anything but that. As a result, DOOM is a highly polished, incredibly entertaining shooter, one of the best to come in quite a while.

The Good
  • Badass gunplay - DOOM delivers a very fast paced shooter that is all about movement, positioning and getting into fights rather than playing more like a traditional cover shooter. Specific gameplay mechanics like getting health and ammo from enemies, large arenas and aggressive enemies encourage what the developers call "push-forward" combat.
  • Badass story - The self-aware story is little more than "Demons bad. Kill all demons" but the game uses this approach to facilitate the gameplay and enrich it rather than bog it down with needless exposition and complicated plot twists and turns. The lore is actually very interesting but is contained in optional codex entries. This approach ensures that players are constantly engaged and keeps things fresh.
  • Badass score - Mick Gordon's score is incredible and is an excellent companion to the fast paced and brutal gameplay. Each piece reacts dynamically to what the player is doing, and there are some phenomenal pieces like BFG Division and Rust, Dust and Guts.
  • Very well optimized - Smooth as butter framerate and hardly any drops, as one should expect from a fast paced game like this. Graphics are very impressive and polished, even if they aren't the most cutting edge.

The meh
  • Honestly nothing I disliked or even found meh. If there is one thing, the multiplayer is a fun diversion but isn't fleshed out enough to compare with the like of the amazing single player campaign

Final thoughts

An amazing action game that feels good to play. An adrenaline thrill ride of a shooter. Play this!!!
