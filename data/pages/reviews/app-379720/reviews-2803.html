\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game without a doubt is one of the most enjoyable shooters I have played in many years.

The story and plot are okay, it's definately not the same atmopsphere as Doom 3, but instead it feels like Doom 1, 2 and 3 had a threeway and this game is what came out of it. You move very fast which is great, and as long as you keep moving you can usually stay alive pretty decently. I really do like the built in challenge modes and weapon upgrade system, keeps the game fresh with many different killing possiblities.

I would highly suggest this for all ages, there may be blood and guts but you are killing demons and zombies! There is no uniform on uniform violence like with cod and many other shooters which is a great change of pace from the normal grind of just killing other humans.

I upgraded my videocard to a GTX 1060 6gb to play Squad with better fps but of all the games "DOOM" had the biggest jump in performance which has made this game way more fun.

Overall I would highly suggest this to any Doom fan who has played any of the previous games, or anyone who just wants to try out a fun single player story.

IDDQD FTW
