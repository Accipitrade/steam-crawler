\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm usually not one for fast-paced action shooters but still enjoyed DOOM a lot, likely because it does what it does incredibly well.

PROs:
+ Fast-paced action. I've never played fights quite like these before. There's a lot of split-second reacting here, enemies spawning right in your face, lots of jumping around and madly firing your guns.
+ Fantastic gunplay. All the weapons have their raison d'Ãªtre, with their very own strengths and weaknesses. Weapons can be upgraded and gain two different abilities (like a sniper's scope or sticky missiles for the heavy assault rifle, stun grenades or a heat explosion for the plasma rifle, remote detonation or guidance lock for the missile thrower, etc.), which is very well executed.
+ Excellent soundtrack. There aren't many games where the soundtrack doesn't annoy me in intense situations, so I usually prefer soundtracks that easily fade to the background (as was done in 2017 Prey, for example). Despite being heavy metal, the DOOM soundtrack didn't annoy me at all, and is quite memorable to boot. Mick Gordon truly did excellent work here!
+ Great level design. From hidden secrets to glorious fight arenas, the level design is top-notch. Graphically, I was a bit worried at first; the game's set either on Mars or in Hell, so everything was fairly ochre for the first few missions. The later levels are absolutely beautiful set pieces, though!
+ The best ending credits I've seen in a game so far. It's like the Marvel movies. Honestly nice to watch!
+ The campaign took me 13h to finish. There're 13 missions (though playtimes varied between 30 min to 90 min for me). I think that's a good length, not only in total but for the respective missions as well.

NEUTRALs:
~ The violence is perhaps a bit too gratuitous. But, I mean, this is DOOM, so... kinda obvious, right?
~ Story and characters really are only the absolute bare-bones (heh...) necessary to provide some kind of justification for sending you to various settings. Again, that's just DOOM.
~ I did have a crash to desktop. Since there were no real bugs and only very minor glitches, I think that's an isolated incident, though, so you can probably ignore this.

CONTRAs:
- I actually can't think of anything.

tl;dr
A fantastic fast-paced action FPS with marvellous gunplay, excellent level design, and a wonderfully stimulating heavy metal soundtrack. Characters and story don't matter; the violence is obviously very gratuitous. One single-player playthrough took me 13h (ca. 1h per mission). I recommend buying it on sale when it's 6â‚¬ (probably the same in $).\t\t\t\t\t
