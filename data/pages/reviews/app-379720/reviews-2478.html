And So It Rains Blood...

Doom starts the way it's suppose to, you wake up chained up to a demon altar, you break the chain, ha, funny 70's music reference lol you grab your first gun and start the action. Doom knows it's classic 90's shooter roots, and the only time you aren't murdering demons with no remorse is when you're upgrading your weapons and armor or when you listen to the occasional bout of story sprinkled across the game. I'm not saying story driven games are bad, christ I love the Bioshock and Half-Life games, but Doom was never about it's rich narative and the way it was told, it was about it's fast paced game play and doom does that so well. The art direction and graphics add to the gameplay and the whole atmosphere of the game. A nice, squeaky, clean science lab contrasts with blood stained hallways, and the desecrated corpes of the science team. You shoot a series of unholy demons with your arsenal of lethal murder machines, and there goes it's body, blood spraying as if it was some sort of hellish pinanta, covering the floor and walls with the red liquid. Not just drops, but a fountain. The fast paced retro shooter action and the take down moves blend suprisingly well together, with enemies despensing health like how your guns despens bullets. In fact the only thing I didn't like was the bosses, the boss fightes got a little tedious, but besides that the whole game and all of it's components fit so well together, you would have sworn it was a jigsaw puzzle.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is awesome. It is. Let me just say in few words, why I find it that way.

First of all, I've played a lot of the old fps games (like Doom, Duke Nukem, Shadow Warrior, etc.) and I've loved them. So when I play the new reboots of those titles there is always a question: did they do a good job at taking an old game concept and implement it to a new engine. And in case of the new DOOM I must say they did a stellar job. The game feels like a Doom game - you have to keep moving to stay alive, there are lots of demons for you to dispose of with range of weapons and power-ups, you need to look for health and armor pickups to survive, and throughout all of this you feel like a one man mean kill machine. That's it - you're a baddass who doesn't speak, just run around and kill every enemy in sight. And it feels soo good.

It must be said that the developers of DOOM really understood what made the original games so fun to play, and they just upgraded that with possibilities of modern game engines. I really love the map design - some where so satisfying to play I just replay them couple of times after finishing them, just for the fun of it - the levels are big and well thought, and it some times took me up to 2 hours to finish them, while finding all secrets and doing all the challenges.

The graphics are also stunning, the enemies and weapons look new yet feel familiar, since they are remakes of old sprites into 3d models. Whit new reboots I always fear that the new designs will have nothing connecting them to the old games, that's not the case with DOOM. The game feels like just a next Doom game, placed in Doom universe, with Doom main character. It does not feel like a completely different game with old title put on it to grab some more money. It is Doom.

So to finish up I'd like to say that if you liked old fps games, you should really enjoy playing this one. It's great. Oh, I almost forgot to mention that the music is perfect as well. Just play it and enjoy some good old Doom.\t\t\t\t\t

Now Free from the chains of hell you set out on a path of blood, guts and destruction while trying to figure out what has happened to the world around you. You explore large and difficult levels facing all kinds of undead and demons.

What to expect:
-This game is rated M for a reason. Dont believe me, chainsaw something inhalf.
-Large and difficult levels with lots of exploration and collectibles to find
-Weapon balences and effectiveness against curtain mosters (Pistol wont kill it? Lazer Cannon Will!)
-The B.F.G.
-Horde mode on steriods
-Able to create "Snap Maps" to create minigames of other levels
-Difficult multiplayer

Things wrong with the game:
-Multiplayer is not balanced, some weapons are too over powered (Get over it)

Other:
- They are rage, brutal without mercy. But you, You will be worse. Rip and Tear, until it is done!
