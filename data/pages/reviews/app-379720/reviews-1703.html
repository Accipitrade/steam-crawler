\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best return to forms I have ever seen in video game history. This game feels as though it is just a modern incarnation of the original. The frantic and gory combat, the simplistic story, and the epic soundtrack. Doom Guy has never been cooler and ripping demons has never felt better. The graphics are nice and the game runs buttery smooth. The controls feel great (on controller at least) and moving around and shooting is fun and intense. There are lots of weapons to collect and upgrades to find and that is just icing of the cake of the game. The only thing I can really complain about is that it really is just all about the combat because the story is alright but nothing to write home about. That is ok though as the story does not matter for this type of game, which is true to the original game. Also other than making it more to modern standards, it doesn't really add anything super new to the formula other than the glory kills, but it didn't really need to change much. This game knows what it needs to be. It is pure, gory fun. It can seems a bit short if you speed through it just killing, but like I said there is loads to find and it is well worth doing the side stuff. This is a game any shooter fan should check out.\t\t\t\t\t
