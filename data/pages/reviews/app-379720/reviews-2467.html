\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat soundtrack, love the shotgun so far, the weapons' mods and the suit are well-thought, the overall ambiance and graphics are great, hearing monsters i feel like i'm at a death metal concert.
Mars in the background is also really nice.
11/10 after 2 hours into the game and the first map completed.

Update :

Well, it took me 15h to complete the Campaign.

Almost every minute was worth it, i spent a little bit of time looking for all the secrets, certainly less time in the last maps, i think my completion would be 95% i missed only a few of them overall, and somehow i want to play the campaign again in the hardest difficulty.

Worth it because of the music, the level design, the ambiance, the weapons, the enemies, you will be caught irresistibly in the madness of DOOM.

The game is quite challenging, mostly at start because of the few weapons and the un-upgraded suit, and it asks a moment to understand how everything works, then it's a pure nightmare, i mean joy, to go through the game.
The attention to details is insane, in every level, every map, no ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ about endless dialogues, the story is quite simple but it has its own identity.
It starts slowly in my opinion but to find all the secrets and the weapons is really rewarding and you will play to see ''what's next''.

Id software made an amazing videogame and it's too rare nowadays in this industry. The next one will be an instant buy for me.

I haven't tried yet the multiplayer nor all the community maps but i guess there are dozens of hours of shooting and jumping and exploding monsters and players over there.

I have to remove half a point (sic) for the sound bugs from some objects in the environment, it is really annoying on the 2 last maps on Mars, and maybe the relative shortness of the game so
my review will be 10.5/10

Doom was one of the first game i played on PC, it doesn't make me young, at least i'm not scared while playing anymore :D.
I think i was afraid to be disappointed about the new iteration.
So... if you look for an intense, gore, filled with adrenaline gameplay, in first person, buy DOOM, i'm 2 years later to review it but i'm glad i finally jumped in.n.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLong story short, I can tell this game would be a lot of fun if it ran properly. I'm on an Acer Aspier E-15 with a 940m, 8 gigs of ram, and an i5 5200U and this game just barely chugs along at around 30fps turned as low as it will go. I'm looking forward to playing it when I get my next computer, whenever that may be, but realistically you cannot play this game with the stats posted above. I have experienced single player lag with poorly optimized games in the past on this rig, so part of it is almost certainly the build I have, but if you need 60FPS to enjoy a fast paced game like this, you ain't gonna get it without a desktop. All that said, I played up until the Hell portion before I just couldn't progress due to terrible FPS and it was fun enough, but I really can't fairly review a game I can't finish. Give it a shot, if it doesn't work on your rig get your refund and come back next time.\t\t\t\t\t
