\r\n\t\t\t\t\t9.5/10

This game deserves a proper review.

This game is one of the best games in a long time, and honestly one of the best 2016 hits. Everything about DOOM 4 is pretty good.

First, the music. It is amazing. One of the best soundtracks in video game history.

Second, the gun play. It is simple, and incredibly fun. There is no real reload (other than the super shotgun). Which makes the overall game play focused on bunny hopping around, and ripping your enemies to death. I must also say that the glory kills are incredibly satisfying. The same goes for the chainsaw kills. Though the gun play gets much better in DOOM Eternal in my opinion.

The only real complaint I have is the semi-rare bugs that you can encounter in-game. One that I have seen a lot is when you run out of ammo on every other gun and switch to the pistol, and switch back. The HUD states that it is another gun, even though that one is out of ammo. The only fix I have found is to trigger an animation to play, such as grabbing a ledge or falling down 30 meters. Another minor thing is how some areas you can reach aren't solid surfaces, and you just fall through them.

That's all, I seriously recommend it, and at 20 bucks, it is really worth the money. But it drops to $6 on sales.\t\t\t\t\t
