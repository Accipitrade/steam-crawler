\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun gameplay, some exploration to find secrets. I didn't find many my first playthrough but I enjoyed myself and it definately was a rewarding 15+ hour campaign.

(spoilers)
The combat is great, however towards the end of the game they dont introduce many new enemies. The waves will feel the same although each enemy can easily kill you. Once you have a preferred weapon alt fire for each gun, the additional points they throw at you feel unneeded.

It took me 18 hours to beat the 3rd difficulty and I feel like I will return to beat it at the next step up as well.\t\t\t\t\t
