\r\n\t\t\t\t\t350 hours since November of 2017, on Steam. The first game I played with my own PC, now surpassed by Eternal with more than 360 hours in only 3 months (now almost 750 hours in 7 months). It's hard going back, the difference is huge from one game to another... BUT, there's a feel that's still pretty unique to this DOOM, a very special spot inside me.

It's just so much RAW power contained within it's combat. You are an unstoppable killing machine, using your own hands to rip through demons, sometimes even with their own horns, fangs, and organs. You feel how tough they can be to destroy, how much strength it takes to tear them appart, and how crude their designs can be. The weapons now feel and look weird but their sounds are still impregnated in my memory. The Siege Mode of the Gauss Cannon is still the most satisfactory mode I've ever used. The glory kills, unforgettable. BERSERK it's even better than the one in Eternal, by far. The dialogue, the way the cutscenes are presented always in First Person, with YOU breaking things, either to upgrade yourself or to progress the story.

RAW power. Everywhere.

This game is still great, and I'll never forget what it means to me, what it makes me feel. It's similar and different to Eternal, in a way that makes them both complement each other to creat the powerful monsters that are the new DOOM games, to create what the Slayer is. And I'll be forever grateful.

"They are rage, brutal, without mercy... but YOU, you will be worse. RIP & TEAR, UNTIL IT IS DONE!"\t\t\t\t\t
