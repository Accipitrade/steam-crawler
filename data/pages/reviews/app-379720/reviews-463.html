\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn absolute banger FPS. As of this review, this is the second time I've played through Doom 2016 and I can say for certain that its fantastic. While the actual narrative might be light at times, the game play itself makes up for this and more. Doom 2016 has some of the most engaging and high octane combat that I've ever played to date. The only game that has surpassed it in my opinion is its successor, Doom Eternal. The amount of weapons that are available to your arsenal reinforces this fact. Most weapons you come across are truly a new addition, and not just a replacement. Besides weapons, are the demons of course. You'll find that there are all sorts of grotesque creatures just waiting to be slayed. They all have weaknesses and strengths that can be exploited. But that doesn't necessarily make it a cake walk by any means. If you're not careful enough you can easily get overrun.

Moving on of course is one of the most notable features of Doom 2016, the music. Mick Gordon honestly deserves all the praise that he gets for this soundtrack. Its everything that you want it to be: aggressive, gritty, and damn near rage inducing. To put it bluntly, Doom 2016 and Eternal really wouldn't be the same without the music. Finally, I just want to touch on the visuals and level design of the game. The environments alone are so intricately detailed and set the tone perfectly. I could try and describe them, but you're better off just looking up what it looks like. As for the level design, everything you see is there for a purpose. Its very much a no-frills kind of design. There are plenty of places to explore and items to find but you can of course just skip over this and truly just go guns blazing.

To say the least, Doom 2016 and Eternal has enamored a huge audience, and for good reason. Its a testament to what games can and should be: filled with passion, innovative, and of course, fun! Doom 2016 has done a great job at just being a kickass ride that sets the golden standard all around. If any of my ramblings above even sound remotely interesting, please do give it a try. And remember, rip and tear until it is done :)\t\t\t\t\t
