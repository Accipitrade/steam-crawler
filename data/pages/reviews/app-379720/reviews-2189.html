Past the campaign, there's plenty to do on the side, like look for collectibles, run levels as fast as possible, and enjoy the multiplayer (I thought it was fun, even though it seems to be pretty dead).

Enjoy the atmosphere, the gunplay, and the amazing soundtrack. I've been listening to the OST on Spotify pretty regularly for about a year now. My favorite levels were in Hell, because the atmosphere was phenomenal. I loved the color scheme, with the vibrant oranges, yellows, blues, and green.

I would say that the campaign and story is secondary to your experience as the Doomslayer, and you can tell that at some points the game knows this.
