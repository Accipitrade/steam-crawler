\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTen out of ten, would doom again.

Pros: Amazing art direction and animation, fun weapons and upgrades (even more satisfying than the orginal's weapons), good story (doesn't interrupt the action, but still adds to the game), good level design (never got hopelessly lost, no pointless backtracking), good length (not too long not too short, I'm at 27 hours after beating single-player), fun secrets (nothing is impossible to find, yet some are still very challenging), hits nostalgia in best ways possible (fun blasts from the pasts sprinkled into a brand new experience), good voiceacting, and heck-yeah good music.

Cons: Minor glitches: Certain item-collecting challenges will glitch out making them impossible to complete if you fail them the first run through the level. If you replay the level after collecting 50% of the items, the challenge counter is reset, making the challenge impossible since the items you already collected don't respawn. This bites for people who like to 100% things, but this doesn't effect being able to collect all the upgrades, weapons, and power ups, or being able to complete the game, so it's not that big a deal. (Probably, hopfully will get patched)\t\t\t\t\t
