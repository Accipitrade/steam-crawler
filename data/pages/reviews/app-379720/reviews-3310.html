\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016 feels like the kind of over the top fps i've been waiting for YEARS for. Doom pumps you full of adrenaline, gives you a studded baseball bat, points at the kid who broke your favorite toy, and unceremoniously tells you to not come back in the house until he stops moving.

Doom manages to be over the top gorey without making you queasy, the finishers make every enemies life ending satisfying and the game rewards you with health for accomplishing them. There are also several ways to accomplish them based on the orientation of your enemy and where you aim.

The game keeps your on your toes, not moving around is the best way to get yourself killed and every room is layed out for you to run and gun.

The guns and the power ups all feel satisfying in their own regard (minus the pistol), whether you want to perform dental surgery with the super shotgun or turn everything in the room to swiss cheese with the gattling gun there's a weapon made for everyones play style. Nothing feels more satisfying than the first time you use berserk.

For those looking for more out of the game there are PLENTY of secrets. There are mini doom guys layed out all over the place for you to find that unlock character/gun models as well as levers that unlock small rooms based off of the classic doom games. There are also plenty of data/lore tid bits strewn across the levels for those curious about the story and characters.

Overall, i love what bethesda has done with the doom franchise this time around, and i recommend it to anyone who hates their fat ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ boss, and needs to cave some skulls that WON'T get them thrown in prison.

I give Doom (2016) 9 cracked skulls out of 10\t\t\t\t\t

It still keeps the same story structure and backround that has been seen throughout the entire series. The single player is hands down the best part of the game and leaves you always wanting more.

The Multiplayer is fun but is nothing groundbreaking. It continues to use the same template/struture that most FPS's use today for multiplayer. It allows you to customize your character and weapons like most triple A games theses days. You unlock the armor types, weapon skins and Demon variants by playing matches. Demon Runes are what really makes the multiplayer stand out adding some spice to all the chaos. Demon Runes allow you to take control of a powerful demon (Of your choice after unlocking variations) that can tear through or blast through the opposing team. It may not be anything crazy new but Multiplayer is still worth playing and is done well.
