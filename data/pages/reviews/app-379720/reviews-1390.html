The gameplay is fast and satisfying, the lore is largely irrelevant and is only there present as either a excuse to keep you moving foward or a source of humor.

If I had to point out defects I would say that the multiplayer is apparently either broken or dead, the game has collision issues in later levels (very easy to get trapped inside geometry or fall off the map), the game is kinda short, challenges/weapon mastery can be very annoying to complete (requiring you kill yourself sometimes to retry if you lose your only chance), and it's way too easy on any difficulty below Nightmare.\t\t\t\t\t
