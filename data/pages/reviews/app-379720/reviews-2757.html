\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinally reviewing this game.. the single player is a complete blast and has all the elements that the original had plus much more, the game runs fantastic on modern hardware and performance is rarely an issue, gameplay is smooth and natural, and although linear, that doesn't stop you from wandering off and finding secrets within the level that can also reward you with more upgrades. Overall the SP portion of the game is enough to satisfy all your DOOM needs. Guns, Demons, and Bad ASS Music.. what else could you ask for?

Looking at the MP, It's also amazing, fast paced and tactical, not something to play if you are used to non arena type shooters.. there is one Huge downside though; The complete lack of any sort of anti cheat, makes it hard to tell who is playing legit and who isn't, Bethesda has completely turned a blind eye to the health of the MP, and although many have begged for a system to be in place, they have chosen complete ignorance.

9/10

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHere is how it goes: Bethesda remembered about DOOM which is a franchise you would most definitely buy with these visuals after near two decades. Everyone cried tears of joy and lined up including me as a gamer who played the old DOOMs on floppies and vcds.

At my first hour or two I was like "This is the best shooter I have played for a while". Sadly the feeling does not last long as you proceed you see the cheesiest level designs and Quake like level processing which is basicly "kill 200000 demons to unlock the door and proceed". It is not a hard game, it is just repetitive. As a matter of fact it is so repetitive that I had to put on cheats to go on easily without having to look for ammo, hp etc. I just wanted to see the games ending as for my moneys worth. Ending is also a sad little pathway to the sequel they will probably make as it kept the terrible little story at a hold.

I would totally dodge this title, no matter the price. Good visuals and sound do NOT make a good game. Bethesda should have known this but anyways. I suppose sometimes the best thing to do is to keep the retro games in their era and not torture them by forcebringing them to our era.

A solid 3/10.\t\t\t\t\t
