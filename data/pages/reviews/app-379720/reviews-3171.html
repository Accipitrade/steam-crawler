- Minimal cutscenes.

- You can carry more than 2 guns at a time.
- Enemies fly apart in to little squishy red gibs when you kill them (Yes folks, GIBS in 2016!)
- Hordes of enemies attack you and you have to use your skill to survive because there's no regenerating health or cover.
