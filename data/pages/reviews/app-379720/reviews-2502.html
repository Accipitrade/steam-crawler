\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPhenomonal game, brilliantly entertaining combat that is so visceral and fluid that you feel like you're on a rollercoaster of blood and gore. Amazing original soundtrack that is not only tonally perfect but endlessly satisfying. Fairly interesting story (although that isn't the draw) and as a bit of a lore nerd i had fun reading the cool and sometimes funny archive snippets that you can discover (this is purely optional though). Only real gripe was that sometimes the combat was stopped in order to search for some minor item in a fetch-quest kind of way, i found this did detract from the game but not in any significant way as it is rare, i just thought it was worth mentioning as my only real drawback.\t\t\t\t\t
