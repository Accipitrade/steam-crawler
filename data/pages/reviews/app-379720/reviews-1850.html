\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDooM 2016 is the Perfect Modernization of the Oldschool FPS Genre. No ADS, Ultra Fast Pace, It's just you, your guns and legions of demons ahead.

No gun in this game is the "Ultimate Gun" that once found, you'll stick with provided you have the ammo, every single weapon (Except maybe the starting pistol) is useful from beginning to end and really fun to use.

Controls are tight, you have control over every movement, run, jump, point, shoot, rip and tear. No QTEs.

Combat itself is superb, fast paced and it's a nice balance between a gory power fantasy and skill, not once did I get annoyed when I failed because not once was it the fault of some OP bullet sponge, QTE or dumb mechanic.

Graphically it looks awesome, tearing demons apart looks as great as it feels, hasn't aged a day yet.

The only real flaw I can point out is the secrets and challenges serving as a distraction if only because they provide upgrade points. It would be better if secrets were pure collectibles and fanservice or just gave a chance to get a higher tier gun a level or two early but as is, if you care about upgrading The Slayer they are distracting and sometimes trying to find them grinds the pace to a halt. However they can be ignored completely for a greater challenge.

DooM 3 tried something different and it kinda worked, the story is there to do that kinda thing, DooM 2016 is the return to basics in a modern eye pleasing getup.

And I don't think I need to comment on how awesome it is to play a silent protagonist that displays more personality than some Player Characters in effin RPGs...

I got it 3 years after it released, this game is worth the money, it was worth the money on release.

Unless you're more into stealth and slow paced tactical FPS games, in which case, you should still give this a try just to see if maybe, just maybe, there isn't a berserk side of you waiting for an opportunity to come out.
This game will provide that opportunity.

Also, I bet this game wouldn't exist if it wasn't for Brutal DooM mod for the original, say what you will about it's maker but the guy deserves a credit for modernizing and brutalizing the classic, DooM was pretty much in development HELL (haha) since 3 released until his mod got popular.\t\t\t\t\t

Doom is bland, generic and poorly optimized for PC. Lacks the survival/horror element of Doom 3. Beating up demons, really? Gets old fast and feels even more unrealistic than the premise of Doom itself.

I guess it's somewhat close to Doom 1 and 2 but it just lacks the soundtrack and weapon feel. Shotgun is boring, pistol looks really, really strange and the other guns just kind of lack oomph. Oh, and did I mention the autosaving? Have fun playing the same fights over and over again because you get killed during some jumping-puzzle after a big fight. How can a game actually be less user friendly than its predecessor some 30 years ago?

Multiplayer is so-so. Netcode is good but the 'snapmaps' are horrible, they make verything look alike and to top it off, it seems that the community is too stupid to rate maps properly and we end up with single player maps being tagged as co-op. Whose bright idea was it to allow the players to decide if a map was co-op or not? I

And what's with the Windows 10-inspired tablet interface when playing snap maps?
Some things take way too much time in this game, including switching over to snap maps.
Just like with everything else, more effort went into making it look good than actually making it run good.

All in all a let down. Not a bad FPS game but just generic. Get it when it's on sale for a fiver or lower.\t\t\t\t\t
