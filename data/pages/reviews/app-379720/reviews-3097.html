\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is basically two games in one. You have arena like spots were you have a bunch of ammo and weapons and try to kill all the badies before the badies kill you. And in between you get a surprisingly fun platformer.

Basically, DOOM is an FPS like we like it. Good amount of difficulty, various ways to murder stuff and mechanics based fighting (a cadaver exploding into ammo when chainsawed might not make any sense at first, but when you consider people will go melee when they are low on ammo it seems perfectly logical). All the fighting usually happens in a small"ish" arena and monsters come in by waves.

Once the fighting is over, you get to explore the maps. And with double jumping, it is anything but linear. You get to solve jumping puzzles especially if you are into finding all the secrets. Oh and like you expect from big titles, the graphics are awesome.

tl;dr DOOM 2016 is not DOOM 3 and it's actually fun. Give it a go, especially if it goes on sale\t\t\t\t\t
