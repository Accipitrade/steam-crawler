\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is excellent. I heavily recommend purchasing this game.

Fast, frenetic, straight-forward, brutal. Every gameplay element has a great coat of polish. Not a moment is wasted. The game doesn't wear out its welcome. The story doesn't interject into the action enough to be annoying, while also being engaging enough to keep the game from being a series of shooting galleries.

I haven't even bothered with the multiplayer, but I won't let that stop me from saying that Doom is easily my game of the year so far, and it's a great breath of fresh air from a genre saturated with military shooters focused on realism and "political intrigue". You won't find anyone more weary of FPS than me, but I had a great time with Doom, and it has renewed my interest in the genre.

10/10. Recommended. Play this game.\t\t\t\t\t
