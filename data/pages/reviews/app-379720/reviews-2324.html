\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016 is a welcome return from a dormant franchise. Doom brings back the classical formula and improves it in every possible way. It is action packed and great fun.

On the positive side:
* Action packed from start to finish. The game design does not allow youto stand still or hide but instead to engage in combat head first
* Gorgeous graphics
* A very decent story for a FPS, establishing the mythos of the "Doom Slayer"
* Plenty of things to do, objectives to accomplish, secrets to find, ...
* A basic but functional RPG system
* A nice collection of weapons (including the infamous saw and BFG)/ Almost all weapons have two mods of action, can be customized and offer special missions to get the best out of them
* Rune levels allowing you to acquire various bonuses that you can later upgrade through gameplay
* Hidden "Classic Doom" levels
* Visceral Glory Kills

On the negative side:
* The gameplay feels like an arena. You always progress through the level and then get locked into a certain area that you have to clear of demons
* All good things come to an end ...\t\t\t\t\t
