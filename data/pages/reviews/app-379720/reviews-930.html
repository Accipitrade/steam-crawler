\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's Doom. What more do I have to say?

You go around from level to level getting from point A to point B with the level design getting more and more labyrinthine as you progress, just to make sure you're paying attention.

Of course, in classic Doom fashion, you get a wide array of weapons to play around with, from the relatively weak but infinite ammo pistol to the almighty BFG-9000.

A good arsenal is, of course, nothing if the gameplay isn't thrilling and engaging. This game's thankfully got plenty of that, with monsters semi-constantly trying to swarm and kill and you, it makes for an incredibly intense and challenging ride (especially on higher difficulties).

All of this isn't even taking into account the insanely satisfying Glory Kills that the game always encourages you to perform.

Although I do find myself getting lost not that far into the game, that's just me. Besides the near endless amount of carnage I inflicted while getting lost more than made up for it.

I couldn't recommend this game enough if you're a fan of FPSs, especially if it's on sale (which it just so happens to be right now).\t\t\t\t\t
