-Glory (read:gory) kills. Just... PERFECTION. Whoever designed this is a genius. The cherry on the sundae.
-Upgrades gives you fun challenges to do on top of just making you stronger.
-Graphics are top-notch. It looks so GOOD it's still on par with today's games.
-Level design/environments are jaw-dropping. As in, there is a jaw of a giant demon skull opening the passage here.
-You get to visit HELL AND CHAINSAW SOME DEMONS!!!$#@$#*%
-BFG. Aka Big [REDACTED] Gun. Just so much YES!!! The VFX!!!
-Story is very decent without ever taking too much time. Lore stuff is optional but actually neat!
-A great variety of weapons+upgrades which are good against certain enemies but are all fun to use!
-Secrets! A ton of secrets! They are hard to find, too; I missed a lot.
-Literally tearing off the horn off a demon and stabbing it with it.
-Kickass music that actually ramps up when you glory kill.

CONS
-This game is BIIIIIIIIG. You're gonna need a big hard drive.
-It's completely linear and there aren't any choices to make aside from upgrades.
-Multiplayer seems pretty dead. Also there's no old-school lobby list :(.
-Ending was too fast and a lot of things were unexplained and left unclear.
-No sense wondering WHY you're doing x, y and z. Brains are for shooting. Ask questions after you finish the game.
-Not much replayability, unless you wanna do the campaign again on hard or find all the secrets. Took me 15 hrs playing pretty slow and exploring.

BOTTOM LINE
-I actually have a hard time thinking of anything negative in this game. Nothing really bothered me!
-Absolutely worth getting this one before Eternal! I bet it's even better (I hear the marauder is a pain in the a**).
-If you're casual, play it on easy. If you like FPS, play it on hard! Anyone can enjoy this (except grandma... probably?).
-Even the credits roll is awesome!\t\t\t\t\t
