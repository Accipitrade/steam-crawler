EDIT:
After spending some time on multiplayer and reaching level 40, I find that the team balancing in this game is NONE EXISTENCE. It can go very unbalanced like full high level player team vs full newbie or ameteur player team. It is a fun multiplayer shooter but with this kinda ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ it take the fun out of it when all the veteran players just destroy you whenever after you respawn for 10 seconds or less. I am never an experienced player in this kind of multiplayer shooter (Quake, Unreal etc) so I can't fully judge how the system works, but seriously it takes the fun out of it. If this game has some team balancing mechanic where both teams have high and low rank players, the game will totally be more balanced and more fun. Veteran players have more knowledge and skill in the game so their map awareness, item drops, vantage points etc is far more better than new or average players that is just starting to get into this game. The devs for this multiplayer needs to fix this. And as for hackers, I haven actually met one yet.
Overall, the singleplayer campaign is FREAKING AWESOME, and the multiplayer is just DISASTER.\t\t\t\t\t

One of the things I find very refreshing is the level design, Ive seen some people who seem anxious that Doom 4's level design would be just like the slew of Serious Sam and Painkiller inspired shooters that we have seen in recent years In which your just locked into room after room and fight waves of enemies, and while Doom 4 does have parts where your fighting enemies in a room that is in fact not all there is to the gameplay and there is a decent amount of big open areas were your just moving through corridors and exploring the level and killing enemies, the levels themselves have some of the most creative secrets I have seen in a long long long time In an FPS game and I hope that inspires more people to, you know, actually design levels for their FPS games, a seemingly lost art In this era.

If your interested I would say get it when its on sale.
