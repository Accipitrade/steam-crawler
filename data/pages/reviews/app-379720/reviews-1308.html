\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA amazing comeback for the DOOM franchise. I remember thinking this game has some of the best combat of and FPS, and I still think that but with the exception of DOOM Eternal having even better in my opinion. After my first playthrough of this game I was sad because I didn't want it to end. A few months later I replayed the game on Ultra-Violence instead of Hurt Me Plenty like my first playthrough. I had a lot of fun with that one but still struggled badly at certain parts of the game. But then once DOOM Eternal was getting closer to release I decided to do a 3rd and final playthrough. I played on Nightmare. This was probably my most fun playthrough. I had mastered the games mechanics. I knew which upgrades were the best. I knew the enemy patterns like its on the back of my hand. And I beat it. Some of the sections of the game that I once struggled really badly on were now easy.
My only problems with the game is that certain weapons are just really OP. The Gauss Canon is just really OP and the Siege mode upgrade just magnifies that because the fully upgraded version of Siege lets you move. The combat shotgun I know is kinda supposed to be good at everything but I think its a little too good at everything. Stuff like the Plasma Rifle are just not useful enough for regular usage. And lastly, the Super Shotgun is just way too powerful with the Double Shot upgrade. Overall this game is really good but the balancing is better in Eternal. Eternal gives every weapon a good use, while 2016 is fine with the same few guns.
TLDR: I had a lot of fun with the game. The combat is very very good. If you're a fan of singleplayer FPS games it's a must play. But as a game DOOM Eternal is a better game that is better balanced with its weapons. This doesn't mean 2016 is bad, because it isn't at all. DOOM 2016 is a masterpiece of a shooter and should definitely should be played. If you are interested in Eternal you could get 2016 on a sale then play that first. Then after you beat 2016 get Eternal and you can really see the comparison. Anyway yeah good game, must play, hahaha I have 69 hours (unintentional i happened to beat my last playthrough at 69 hours), and best singeplayer FPS combat along with DOOM Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
