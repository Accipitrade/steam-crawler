\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pretty amazing. I was out of gaming for several years and not active or even paying attention when this released. In 2019 talking with some younger co-workers and learning about a new Doom game, that finally piqued my interest enough to build a new gaming rig and finally get back into gaming. I played Doom:Eternal before playing this, which was also amazing and I highly recommend if you like games of this variety. Doom:Eternal was amazing enough it inspired me to step back and try 2016 Doom.

it's really cool to actually see the evolution of Doom to Doom:Eternal. Graphics are great, mood is very dark, , music adds a creepy ambiance, with some genuine scare moments (and I don't scare easily). Very run and gun oriented (fast paced) when things are hot and heavy, but then you can slow down and try to find secrets and that sort of thing. Maps are vast, very well thought out, dark and creepy, and the story is interesting enough to at least pay some attention to.

All I can say is, I can't wait for Id's next version of Doom, whenever that hit's the shelves I will be an early buyer.\t\t\t\t\t
