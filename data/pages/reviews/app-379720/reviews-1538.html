Seriously, this game is an amazing comeback for a classic series with something for players both old and new, the level design feels great with secrets to find, while the weapons themselves feel great. The demons are mostly from the classics, but all feel great and satisfying to fight, with glory kills that are quick, cool, and helps you survive.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been a fan of DOOM ever since the first one came out as shareware when I was a kid. At first I didn't really give this game a chance because I fell in love with DOOM 3 and I was hoping DOOM 2016 would be a horror game like 3 was, so I was disappointed. I recently however decided to give this one a go again, and it's awesome. I love this game. It's tense, fast paced, simple story so you can focus more on killing demons. There's things you can collect add flavor to the story and well as an encyclopedia that has info on areas, characters, and demons for those who enjoy reading stuff like that (I do).

If you like faced paced action and/or killing demons I highly recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
