\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is the latest addition to the famous DOOM fps saga. It is a worthy sequel and it stands very high by being an old-school shooter but with state-of-the-art graphics and sound. No strategic thinking here, very little o no sniping just charge ahead and kill one after the other demons with some very gory finishing moves.

I cannot comment on the multiplayer aspect as I played it for just a couple of minutes (too old for such fast-paced action).

Pros:
+ amazing graphics and animations with very nice performance even in high-res settings.
+ plethora of weapons with two different modes and numerous upgrades. All can be useful at different situations
+ good variety of enemies and for demons they seem to be intelligent.
+ amazing sound and music, fits perfectly fine with the gameplay.
+ lots of secrets which are nicely integrated in the game. Most quite reasonable to find.
+ frenetic action and some really difficult moments, this is old-school fps at its best.
+ finishing animations are impressive (some may find them too gory).

Cons
- the hell/necropolis environments are less impressive and inspired compared to the station maps.
- could have more variety in missions, with perhaps a couple more in open environment.
- The whole setting is not as horror-based as Doom 3, which I loved.

All-in-all an excellent fps game with over-the-top, awesome relentless action that brings back fond memories for all of us who have played the original doom when it came out and all sequels. Definite buy and I look forward to the next part.
