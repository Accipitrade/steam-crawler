Nightmare was a breeze. Eternal really did step up the combat loop.

That being said, 2016 is still super solid. The setting and atmosphere alone keep it relevant, though now nightmare is the only way to play if you want a challenge. Eternal really is that much more demanding. The best sequels take everything to the next level while somehow not rendering the predecessor obsolete. id pulled this off spectacularly.

While I'll be going to Eternal to satisfy a general need to rip & tear, I can see myself coming back to this one every now and then just to run the campaign for the original sights, sounds and vibes that reinvented Doom in the best way possible.

2016: Absolutely incredible.
2020: Still absolutely incredible\t\t\t\t\t
