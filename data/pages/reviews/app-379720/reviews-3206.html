\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen they first announced the new DOOM, I was more excited than I had been for any game. My expectations were high, I was all psyched up and had great hopes for what I hoped would be my new favourite game of the next decade. When I played the game, it went far beyond my expectations and so far I haven't been let down at all. The combat is completely outstanding.

I can really tell that the developers have devoted a lot of time and put a lot of love and care into developing this game to make it what the community wanted. I'd say that they did that and more. id Software have gone and done it again, they've taken the one FPS that started it all and have re-created it. DOOM is back, and better than ever. And I can happily say that I'm going to be playing this game even 20 years after it's initial release. And with the latest update, id addressed some of the problems that players had with certain aspects of the multiplayer, we finally have a developer that listens to it's community.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOld man review. Played the hell out of the original DOOM as a teenager in the 90s.

This is without a doubt the best modernization of any classic game I've played before.

The way the game balances weapon mechanics and ammo pickups is on par with Halo's "Golden Triangle". Glory Kills are fast, and the way it snaps you to the enemy makes it a way to get around a battle quickly. They also give you health packs, vital to surviving as the battle flows. Chainsaw kills drop ammo for your guns (limited fuel) and can be used strategically to take out troublesome enemies when your back is against a wall.

During all of this a soundtrack that evokes NIN (Broken era) rises and falls with the action, with seamless musical stings woven in during Glory Kills and chainsaw kills.

My personal favorite Berzerk Glory Kill: http://i.imgur.com/ChOCBbr.gifv

I can't recommend this game any more highly. It is so choice. If you have the means, I highly recommend picking one up.\t\t\t\t\t
