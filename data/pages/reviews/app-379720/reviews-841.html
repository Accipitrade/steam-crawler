\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 4 I guess. 7/5/10

The first time playing through this I felt like it was great. A new doom that was not Doom3, although Doom3 has aged better with me than others. I like doom3 almost as much as Doom2 but Doom was always the top. This Doom however has aspects that are a mash of all those. It is Doom, the level layout is very much a doom feel. the style is very much doom 3 and it plays nice. It doesnt play like doom it doesnt even play like quake. it plays more like doom 3. This almost feels like what doom3 was supposed to be. its grand and great and probably my 3rd favorite doom.

Playing this after playing Doom Eternal, because I wanted to recall the lore after playing DE I found this to be a fragment of a game in comparison. DE feels and plays like Doom. The layout is grander and the action faster. It is in every aspect Better than this Doom. The engine is scores better too, I barely run this Doom with my 2060, at 60-90fps, where as I can comfortably run DE at 170-200fps. The action is faster, the loads are faster, the deaths are faster, the nostalgia is more nostalgiay DE is the Ultimate Doom.\t\t\t\t\t
