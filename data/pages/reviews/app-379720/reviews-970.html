\r\n\t\t\t\t\t8.5 / 10
Awesome reboot of the glorious series. I completed it all the way through on "Nightmare Mode", which is not easy in many areas, but hardly extremely hard either compared to the original games where enemies respawn if you backtrack for retrieving spare ammo and items. There's no additional enemies during boss battles compared to the original games. I also think the holograph and flag grenade equipment should have been limited obtainable items as opposed to things that keeps refreshing in your inventory.

Although its exploration moments and story cutscenes can slow the gameplay experience a bit in places, this ironically works in the game's favour as it makes its chaotic shooting moments feel even more frenetic and hardcore in its own way by comparison, with huge amount of enemies onscreen in single areas and you go overkill in many different ways as you progress through the game. The glory kills in particular are awesome, they pack a punch that makes this game in some ways rival with the originals.

Graphics are fantastic, though if you play with your PS4 / XBox controller, you have to disable the mouse cursor tool on Steam as the wobbling effect of the controller made the initial experience trippy, lol. Also, I noticed a few minor bugs (one time, I killed every demon in an area, but the game objectives don't seem to respond. I had to restart the game and checkpoint to get it right.) Well worth the purchase overall, and I intend to play Doom: Eternal sometime in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
