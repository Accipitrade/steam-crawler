- each demon is unique and fun (my personal favorite is Hell Knight).
- Mars level design is nice.
- main protagonist (Doom Slayer full of rage).
- finishers animation.
- light parkour (chasing imps via climbing is hilarious).
- weapon & suit upgrades.
- soundtrack.

Cons:
- Boss battles are kinda boring, I prefer face hordes of demons.
- Hell levels are generic.
- Short campaign (8 hours).\t\t\t\t\t
