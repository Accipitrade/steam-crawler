\r\n\t\t\t\t\tREVIEW OF SINGLE PLAYER CAMPAIGN

Fast-paced, gory fun with lots of explosion, gnarly looking demons and gorgeous level design!
It's got a great horror atmosphere - not for the feint at heart and too scary for the younger players, I'd say.

Besides ripping demons apart in imaginative ways, you'll also spend a lot of time jumping from platform to platform in order to progress - if you don't like jumping puzzles, this game might not be for you!


I only have two problems with the game:

1) You can't save manually - the game saves for you at waypoints. This SUCKS, and should be banned from gaming altogether!

2) The map only shows floors, so you won't always be able to guess when and how to gain access to a certain area with a valuable resource. I've wasted a LOT of time searching, only to head on ("the entrance must be somewhere further on") and then get locked out from turning back.


TIP FOR CASUAL PLAYERS:
I found the game to be quite challenging - note that you can change the difficulty and just continue your campaign if needs be. I'm a casual myself, and the game took me 18 hours to complete (I wasted a lot of time exploring, looking for secret places, though)\t\t\t\t\t

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5)

DOOM is a high-octane and adrenaline-pumping first-person shooter that sets a new standard for fast-paced action and intense gameplay. With its ferocious combat, exhilarating movement mechanics, and heavy-metal soundtrack, it stands as a modern classic that pays homage to the franchise's legendary roots.

Likes:

Incredibly Satisfying Combat: DOOM's combat is a brutal and cathartic experience, emphasizing fast and fluid movement, allowing players to unleash devastating weaponry upon hordes of demons. The visceral gunplay and melee takedowns create an unparalleled sense of empowerment.

Engaging Level Design: The game's intricately designed levels offer a perfect balance of exploration and combat, keeping players on the edge of their seats throughout the thrilling demon-slaying campaign.

Variety of Weapons and Upgrades: DOOM offers an impressive arsenal of weapons, each with unique attributes and upgrade options. The weapon modification system allows players to tailor their arsenal to suit their playstyle.

Metal-Rocking Soundtrack: The game's heavy-metal soundtrack sets the tone for the relentless action, adding to the adrenaline rush and overall immersive experience.

DOOM Slayer's Unyielding Persona: The protagonist, known as the DOOM Slayer, is an iconic and stoic force of destruction, fitting perfectly into the game's action-packed narrative.

Dislikes:

Limited Story Focus: Some players may find the narrative to be relatively straightforward, focusing primarily on the action and demon-slaying rather than an intricate storyline.

Occasional Repetitiveness: While the combat is exhilarating, some players may experience occasional moments of repetitiveness during prolonged battles.

In conclusion, DOOM is an unabashed celebration of demon-slaying and non-stop action, delivering an adrenaline-fueled and satisfying experience for fans of first-person shooters. With its relentless combat, engrossing level design, and metal-infused soundtrack, it pays homage to the franchise's iconic legacy while introducing new elements that make it a standout title in its own right. Whether you're a veteran DOOM fan or a newcomer to the series, prepare to unleash hell upon the forces of darkness as the indomitable DOOM Slayer and embark on a relentless and explosive journey through the realms of hell in DOOM.\t\t\t\t\t
