\r\n\t\t\t\t\t- Fast paced.
- Detailed blood, gore and violence.
- Huge and detailed levels with tons of secrets.
- Weapon/Suit upgrades.
- Awesome graphics and good optimization, runs pretty smooth.
- No long and boring cutscenes.

They definitely got down the mars theme the previous Doom (and Quake) games had, I really like the atmosphere. The gameplay is pretty aggresive and the enemies can be punishing if you mess around. The monster design is really badass with lots of details, they look like they will ♥♥♥♥ your ♥♥♥♥ up, unlike some Disney cartoony ♥♥♥♥. Also, just like I mentioned above the maps are huge. So if you're the type who doesn't want to rush or 'speedrun' the game to explore stuff, then you can find a good amount of secrets. Some of them will help you beat the game easier too. I also like the fact that you can choose between weapon/suit/rune upgrades depending on your gameplay style.\t\t\t\t\t
