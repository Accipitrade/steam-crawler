This game makes you feel like an unequivical badass, from the music, visuals, and the sheer brutal glory of the gameplay, everything in this game makes you feel like a pure force of nature. The soundtrack is amazing, and adapts with beats and riffs with your playstyle.
I think that this game fully captures the spirit of the Doom franchise, and kicks it into the modern age with detailed environments and fantasticlly detailed visuals. The glory kill system adds a fun new dimension to combat and so do the runes and weapon mods, all of which feel useful and awesome. Bad game for anyone with high blood pressure, but it's great for releiving stress through frantic movement and shooting. Doubly with the bezerk power up. Fantastic Game.\t\t\t\t\t
