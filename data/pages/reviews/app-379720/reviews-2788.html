\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is incredible.
The first thing that comes to mind is satisfying. Every input from the player feels so good, and then the weapons magnify that by a million. The minimum viable product of this game got so much love. I could just jump around and shoot forever.
And the game isn't afraid to be comfortable with that, the rune trials are an excellent example of toying with mechanics to make things interesting.
I don't think I ever encountered a section I was disinterested with. The maps are freaking incredible, varying up enemies and settings to fight them, hell and mars are both gorgeous and complex, rather than scapes of sameness. I think my favorite part of the map design is the same as it's been for decades: the developers taunt you with secret areas just outside your reach, but if you're clever enough, you can get there, grab that health boost, and blow the heads off more demons.
Additions to the series, like the glory kills and the weapon upgrades are more than welcome. I can imagine this game coming out a couple years after Final Doom, and recieving immediate praise.
If you have any interest in this game, buy it.
