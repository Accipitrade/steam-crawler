\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNew Doom is… tricky topic.

It's full of dubious unnecessary stops like "Look where the cat dragged another dead" and butt-ugly introductions of monsters, but devs play many of such moments just right with Doom guy tearing monitors apart, interrupting comms, OMGWTFBBQing another "glorious" monster dropped at you in the most badass way.

It's full of "Yellow means go there" bs, but full of secret places to literally punish you for following the magic yellow paint, taking extra ammo, upgrades and earlier access to guns from you.

It's full of unconvincing guns (And pistol/HAR/Plasma gun are outright weak, peashooter level) with laughable sounds and monsters that somehow manage to give you that Power-Rangers level of disapointment. But then you choke on it while shooting off the limbs and gibbing these horrifyingly awkward abominations)

All in all, it boils down to what you actually wanted.
IF you wanted new Doom, you pretty much got it, complete with useless pistol and "why am I even bother with these small buffs" feel.

However, if you wanted a BRUTAL DOOM with current-gen graphic, you are out of luck.
No limb socker.
No sawing off revenant rocket launchers.
No cool berserker mode allowing you to throw barrells and make lost soul your b**ch.
No disturbing rip and tear action introducing the whole concept of HORRIBLE PAIN, UNAVOIDABLE FATE and MORTALITY to all the demons around. You know, no part that make you believe in the fact that you ARE their DOOM.
Not enough metal.
Not enough gestures of deep respect and extensive tender making out with their ancestors in the past.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLove the game. Has the same feelings as the classic, mixed with DOOM 3. There is story, but its not as important as the gameplay.

I am a little upset that DOOM guy doesn't move as fast as the classic, but it does feel a bit more balanaced and its a slower climb to feeling completely badass.

I get adreneline rushes and I'm also spooked easily when playing. I haven't had this kind of feeling in any game for a long time. It actually does feel like a challenge. I'm not punished too much for dieing, but it stings enough that I actually care about staying alive. A hard balance to achive for a game.

The campaign battles are a rush. It almost has a quake feel added in to it, but I also get the mini boss and boss battle feelings that I got from the classics.

This game was done right. I am very happy with it.\t\t\t\t\t
