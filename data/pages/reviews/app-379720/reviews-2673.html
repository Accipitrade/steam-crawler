\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you've only played shooters like far cry 3, you may like this game.
For those expecting an fps with some resemblance to those of the early 2000's or at least some
incredible and exciting new game design, you'll be disappointed.

Pros:
+The gauss gun is good addition to the classic lineup
+The detonation upgrade for the rockets is really cool
+Half of the old guns aren't ruined by the new upgrade system
+Chainsaw has been nerfed into a decent power weapon
+Some really cool redesigns
+In battle powerups are pretty fun to use (except berserker)
+Cyberdemon boss actually felt like a challenge (an easy one though)

Cons:
-Horrendously bland story that can't be skipped and gets in the way of gameplay
-Very floaty and forgiving combat that makes the player go through battles feeling like nothing happened
-Upgrades make a few guns blatantly overpowered (charged gauss canon can 2 shot a mancubi)
-Terrible finishing move system that allows players to go unpunished for taking ludicrous amounts of damage
-some enemies just dance around flinging projectiles that miss
-melee enemies would be a threat, but guns and mobility are powerful enough that they can't touch you
-locked off arenas that barely vary beyond platforms to jump onto
-free grenades can be thrown constanly (and they deal alot of damage)
-MG and plasma gun feel like airsoft guns and bring little interesting design to the game
-Extremely tedious challenges can be completed to make the game even easier than it already is

Conclusion:
Feels like a copy of the Shadow Warrior reboot but worse.
If you want a good arena shooter, play serious sam.\t\t\t\t\t
