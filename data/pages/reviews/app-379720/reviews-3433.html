\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have to say returning to a world were collecting ammo, health and armor matters actually is a plus for me. Having limited ammo encourages me to choose the right weapon for the job. In general, there is a common theme where your play style/actions have consequences. You can’t play dumb and then sit in a corner waiting for your “shield” and “health” and weapon “ammo” to return to max. I will say there are parts where because of my play style I have gotten slaughter but due to the checkpoint system once I revisit the same area I do better and it hasn’t’ been an issue for me.

As a baseline your tried and true weapon the pistol has unlimited ammo, anything else has a magazine that will run out. Introducing finisher melee moves and the chainsaw (an ammo pinata gnearting weapon at the cost of "fuel") also starts to lay what I feel is a solid foundation for fast combat that feel is brutal and merciless, just how I want Doom to feel. While there is ammo and health on a map, if you play aggressively you can recoup health and, to a point ammo, from killing monsters. I favor this approve vs. Halo or whatever it’s kin is in that DOOM prompts an aggressive playstyle vs hit and hide recoup play styles.

Also in solo play the combat suit and weapons upgrade system, coupled with the advantages of putting effort into exploring a map really brings me home back to the mid 90s. Putting the effort into exploring a map on finding its secrets will give you a nice edge vs. trying to straight speed run it so you can tweet/facebook/what social media millennial instant gratification cliché you can think off. I have to say so far the early impression I’m getting reminds me slightly of Metroid, in that there is a combination of not only kicking ass through the map but also looking for secret areas and the associated power ups that come with said areas.

The secrets on the map that are call backs to earlier versions of Doom and unlock new maps you can play through with the new engine are a nice tip of the hat for those of us that were back there in the day.

Overall all I do feel at home with this game and so far enjoy playing it.\t\t\t\t\t
