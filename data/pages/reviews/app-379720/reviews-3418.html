I can't give enough praise to this game, it is the best FPS I have played in a solid decade or more. If you are in to call of duty type games, you will be confused by this and its gameplay style. If you are into oldskool FPS this is for you. 13/10

Edit: I wasn't sure about the snapmap in the beginning, but to tell you the truth it has grown on me considerably. It is set up much the same way as Little Big Planet, you build it and publish it and if the ratings come in you get featured on the front page. I have played entirely new campaigns that people have built from the ground up, including new renditions of the Classic DOOM done in unbelievable detail. People have been fleshing out the story of the side characters and even the Elite Guards have complete campaigns. There is a limitless amount of single player story campaign and these modes are also available to play with others.\t\t\t\t\t
