without spoiling it the ending is a bit of a cliffhanger, so there will be more to single player campaign in the season pass, not just multiplayer maps and worthless vanity content.

gameplay
if you've played the old doom games (doom-final doom) combat in this is right up your ally. its old school with a twist of new school fresh squeezed onto it. combat is high paced with secrets scattered about each stage, some easy to find others you really gotta dig for em.

music and audio
the music always fits the mood perfectly stage to stage. the demons and weapons all sound excellent, even has some throwback sounds from the old games here and there.

graphics
as amazing as this game looks, it wasnt super intensive as i thought it'd be. i'll sit anywhere between 70-144 fps my monitors refresh cap. here is my loadout for reference:

CPU: intel core i7-4790k 4.0 ghz
RAM 16GB
GPU nVidia 980ti Hybrid
display Acer 2k display with Gsync
