\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat hasn't been said about this game already? Such a near perfect modernization of the classic title that put FPS games on the genre map. Not only does it pay homage to its origins and old school games in general with its level to level structure, but it also does something that not many FPS games did by the time this came out in 2016: REFRESH THE STANDARD FPS FORMULA.

For almost a decade, FPS games were nothing but hide behind cover to wait for your health to recover, two weapon limit, everything is basically an M16 carbine etcâ€”but then this came along and forced players to be constantly on the move to avoid really aggressive enemies that will easily flush you out of cover by rushing toward you.

I love that it rewards you for risky gameplay like taking the time to get UP CLOSE with an enemy and glory kill them while they're staggered so you can collect extra health off of them. Then of course the variety of weapons keeps the game really fun and interesting, and they each come with two alternative fire mods that change the feel and properties of each weapon.

The game really encourages you to discover your own playstyle based on the various tools they give you. And best of all, FAST weapon switching was probably first introduced in this game and other FPS's since have adapted similar formulas. This allows for constant weapon switching with the press of a button, resulting in devastating weapon combos.

My go to weapon combo to take down heavy enemies was:

Stun Bomb with the Plasma Rifle
Charged/Regular Shot with the Gauss Gun
Rocket Launcher blast

and if the enemies are still alive, I finish them off with an Explosive Shot with the regular shotgun, then swap between the Standard Shotgun and the SUPER Shotgun with regular shots until the enemy is either staggered enough for a Glory Kill or until they blow into red and gory bits.

Instant classic. I see myself returning to this game once a year to replay the campaign because the story is really good and straightforward, exploration is fun, and I look forward to every combat encounter to get lost in a flow of weapon swapping combos and death defying acrobatics.\t\t\t\t\t
