\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStarted off pretty slow and boring. After I got into my first level in Hell, it really started to pick up the pace.

I played the game on Nightmare mode, which looking back may have been a poor choice to start playing the game. The pistol you start off with is an absolute joke and feels horrible to shoot. The Shotgun / Heavy Rifle combo was my go-to and served me well until I found the Super Shotgun. After that I couldn't go back.

All of the Mars levels were a chore to go through. It was tedious finding the Demon beacons and dying just to checkpoint back to 20 miles away. This is why the Hell levels were much more fun. They looked amazing, and there was so much combat in between the objectives that getting set back an arena didn't feel nearly as painful. I didn't mind re-fighting enemies because I died; I mind having to figure out how I got to the arena in the first place to try again.

Lots of secrets to find and upgrade your Praetor suit; however I felt like the upgrades were underwhelming outside of the one that gave you the Automap by default. I REALLY wanted to be immune to my own explosions instead of just barrel-booms. I never used the grenades so I didn't find that tree useful. And the dexterity options were a little nice to have.

Rune trials felt stilted and boring. They were all easy to go through after 1 or 2 tries. Runes themselves on the other hand were OP. So they are worth getting and upgrading.

All of the gun upgrades felt useful and fun. I basically used everything except the charged shotgun and the pistol as a whole. Well worth grinding the mastery to get them maxed out.

And finally, the story was meh. It's a DOOM game, so I'm not expecting Half-Life levels of story telling here. It's really just "go here" -> "kill dudes" -> "unlock path" -> "get mcguffin" -> "kill more dudes". It works great for shootin' demons, although it seems like they are setting up for Eternal with that ending. I guess I'll see when I get to Eternal.

7/10 Definitely above average, decently long, plenty of fun so long as you stick it out for the first third of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
