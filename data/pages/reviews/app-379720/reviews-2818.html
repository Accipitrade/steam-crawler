\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEDIT: I have replayed DOOM recently in the hope of changing this review to a more positive one. My opinion remains the same. This game is soooo boring, The music is repetitive uninteresting and dull, the level design is the same dog s*** brown everywhere. The new demons are unceremoniously dumped on a map, no intro no atmosphere. The sound design is weak at best. Most voices this are trying to deliver story (supposedly) dissapear behind what should be background noise, most guns sound and feel feeble and you are forced to use these "amazing" (and by amazing I mean repetitive after the first three) glory kills for ammo and health. For a decent Doom experience play doom 3 or the original two.
This is boring, repetitive, uninspired trash. I wish I had go a refund on it whilst I had the chance.

How can DOOM be boring!? This was one of my favourite games growing up. I played DOOM 3 for hours on end throughout college and was so excited to see this coming out. I Played the BETA and enjoyed the crazy multiplayer. I am however more of a single-player gamer. I was so disappointed, the trailers looked so exciting and the game did deliver this experience. However it gives nothing else! Everything is the same colour, everything is so detailed (The graphics are great, but wasted because there is nothing but red metal to look at) I couldn't see what was going on as soon as a fight started I was just running in circles and holding the trigger until everything was dead.....except I died every time. The game FORCES you to use the over-hyped glory kills (most you will have already seen in the trailers) Glory kills are the ONLY way to get health or ammo as it is incredibly scarce. This forces you to only have one style of gameplay. You can cary very little ammo, health dissapears in seconds and armour might as well be wet tissues! My average gameplay experiance is a moment (barely seconds) of looking at the scenery before it is boring, then getting into a fight and the usual blur of motion blood and fire begins with little to differentiate and less than miliseconds of choose what to do but get it wrong and you are dead. If you want story, play DOOM 3 if you want multiplayer play this. I cannot recomend it for it's singleplayer, I wish I had never bought this as forcing myself to play just so I can get through it is painful.\t\t\t\t\t

Thank you so much for creating such an aweseom game. I am currently trying to beat it on nightmare mode (very hard but the most fun I've had so far.) This game was so much more than what I thought it would be. It's incredibly satisfying in every way. The animations are some of the best of any game ever. It's in my opinion the best singleplayer fps out there and I have played quite a few. I have no complaints.

I do however, have a suggestion:

The glory kills/chainsaw massacres/beserk mode animations are all done very well. However the player deaths can sometimes be quite out of place and i would love to see more monster killing player animations.\t\t\t\t\t
