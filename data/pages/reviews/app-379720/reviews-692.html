\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all. I never rly played any FPS this fast-paced and got Doom either on a sale for a few € or on humble bundle (don t remember anymore). I had it in libary for a long time and never played it since i didn t know if it was a game for me but always wanted to try it. So after a long time i finaly tried it... and boy it was a BLAST!!

I started it on "Ultra-Violent" because i wanted some challenge. At the beginning of my journey i was "slow" and just moved steady through the maps. After some time i adjusted to the speed and the fun began. Even as a total beginner in such fast-paced shooter i feeled the learning curve to move and react faster. It never felt awkward or something like that. Just the change in playstyle from moving slow through the maps to jumping and flying over the vast battlefields. I m rly happy i started on "Ultra-Violent" because it was kind of a challenge but more on the easy side for me. I died sometimes because of stupid mistakes or because i tried to go for the 100% (didn t get the challenge completed and started the fight again).
The only point i m kind of "sad" about is, that the early weapons lose a lot of value later on (at least at my playthrou) because most of the time i blasted through the hords of hell with my trusty chaingun (and the unlimited ammo rune) or the railgun and watched them rain in bits and pieces. I only switched to the super shotgun if my ammo for the other weapons ran out.

For the end i can only recommend everyone to play this game. I never rly was in these kind of fast-paced shooter or didn t even listen the type of music/soundtracks. But for Doom i turned up the volume and enjoyed the massacre\t\t\t\t\t
