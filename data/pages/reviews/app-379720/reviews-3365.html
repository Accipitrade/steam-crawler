Played this game at a friends place for about 5 hours, loved it so much I baught it on PC.

I gotta say, DOOM (the original) was my first shooter when I was a kid. The music, the fast paced unforgivable action..... It's all come back with pretty pretty graphics.

The only thing that got a little stale for me was the melee kills. They kept the action moving and didn't interfere too much, but after your 1,000th melee kill it got repetative.

Overall 10/10
