\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile most games have their quirks and random crashes this game has seveal annoyingly long and noisy videos which always play and are unskippable at launch. When you have just had a mid game crash you are likely already somewhat irked and this just adds to your frustration.

For my first run through I have chosen the 'Hurt me plenty' difficulty. As with may games the difficulty does not seem to have been balanced very well. In many cases I have not needed to use any of the power ups provided such as the quad damage or the additional health and armour. In other cases I have been easily overpowered and had to retry several times. Final summation, game seems to lack obvious balance and has random but significant difficulty spikes, which which are usually far more difficult than the end of map boss fights.

SPOILERS BELOW (Close your eyes now!):
Upon killing the final boss the user is presented with an action which they must complete in order to kill the boss. When this occurred for me an invisible wall blocked me from being able to get close enough to be prompted to press f and complete the game. While this may be acceptable shortly after a games release it is totally unacceptable to have bugs which require a complete reload more than two years after release. After a short amount of googling it appears that I was far from alone in having experienced this issue.

In conclusion I would say that the game is great but unpolished. Perhaps we should check back in another year to see if they have fixed all the bugs and annoying glitches?\t\t\t\t\t
