I learned to use a keyboard and mouse before a console. So I had VERY high expectations for this reboot. I was pleasantly sent beyond my expectations. It's fun, it's gorgeous, and it's lengthy. Meaning I've been at it for more than a week.

I'm running at 1440p at 60fps average. My rig is a G3258 OC @4.0, with 8GB of ram, using a RX480 4GB (Nitro+, OC to 1400Mhz). On OpenGL/DX it runs choppy, but with a bit of code for allowing the game to use both of my cores, I can run Vulkan on it with superb FPS.

I haven't finished it. I'm loving it.\t\t\t\t\t
