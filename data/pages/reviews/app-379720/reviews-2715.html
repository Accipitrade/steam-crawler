\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTried the free weekend ... loved it!

I may get some flack for this, but I really enjoyed the dark, spooky atmosphere of Doom 3. It stood out as a really terrifying experience, and one of the first games that truly scared me.

This game does not have the same atmosphere. However, it makes up for it with pure adrenaline, which I suppose is closer to the original Doom. Combat is really amazing, demons are running around and jumping everywhere. It is enjoyable to fight in this game, I think that's the main point. In Doom 3, fighting was frightening and had an "oh no ... not again" vibe to it.

If you loved the original Doom, you will probably love this one. If you loved Doom 3, you might still like this one, but don't expect the same kind of experience.\t\t\t\t\t
