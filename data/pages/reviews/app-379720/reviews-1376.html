With Classic guns like the Supershotgun and newer weapons like the Gaus cannon, there's plenty of guns to fill your demon ripping dreams. An problem I have with this game however is that some weapons just outshine others. For example, for pretty much the last half of my playthrough (in Hurt Me Plenty), all i needed to use was the Super Shotgun and Gaus Cannon. Weapons like the Shotgun or Pistol are thrown to the sideline and I never used them after the 4th level.

Overall, even with the problems I had, DOOM 2016 makes me want to replay it to find all its secrets and powerups. So for my rating, I'd give it a 8/10.\t\t\t\t\t
