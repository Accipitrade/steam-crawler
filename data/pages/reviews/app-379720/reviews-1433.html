\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all i want to say if you like fps this is a must buy, especially now that it's heavily discounted multiple times a year. This is a fps where you shoot demons and go through very simple platforming with some secrets and "rune challenges" sprinkled in, simple as that. The guns feel nice, the way the enemies stagger, the way their body parts fly when you blow it off with the super shotgun, the satisfaction of doing glory kills be it on smaller or big and tougher demons just cannot be found anywhere else. Runs smoothly even on hectic moments like multiple explosions or a bunch of demons running at you at the same time. This game has relatively quick load times too, thumbs up on the optimization department. If you're familiar with fps titles i recommend starting with "Ultra Violence" difficulty like I did, it is a bit hard in the beginning but when you get all the guns and power-ups it feels so good.

There are just so many things going for this game, there is a reason it is labeled a "masterpiece" and it is worth the full price-tag, and a definite steal on a discounted price.\t\t\t\t\t
