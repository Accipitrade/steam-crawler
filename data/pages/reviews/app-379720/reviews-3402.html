\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSilly and repetitive. TL;DR - The game is too easy on Nightmare.

Cons -
Story feels like Dead Space, you have Zealots that think the Demons are great and will ascend them.
~ This room has Demons in it, time to lock it until you kill them all causing arena death match.
Ammo and health everywhere, even on Nightmare it gets silly.
Chainsaw kills spew ammo drops much like how Sonic the Hedgehog spews rings when he gets hit.
The game is very into parkour and the level design is focused around it heavily.
Secrets... aren't. Easily found. (This means they are easily found and in the open)
It's not intense feeling since ammo and health are very abundant.
Combat feels slow aside from moving around to avoid being hit, there are many areas where nothing is going on.
No surprise elements, nothing to make you think "oh no".
Grenades are infinite, but on a timer.
Pistol has infinite ammo.
Playing on lower difficulty settings causes the "tutorial" screens to take up 1/3rd of the screen blocking gameplay.
Doom needs monsters with shields like Halo does.
"Gore" shrines, you must break those to "purge" Hell from Mars and I thought there was a dimension portal?
Chainsaw starts with three uses.
You don't reload, you pick up ammo and you're good to go.

Pros -
Death, dismembermant and plenty of gore.
Weapons are okay.
Level of detail is okay.

I'm probably too blinded by all the cons to see much good in the game.
