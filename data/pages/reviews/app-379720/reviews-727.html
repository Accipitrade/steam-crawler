\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI know I've only logged a few hours on this game, yet as soon as I started running around the world and had my feet wet killing demons I knew I would enjoy it. The arena style FPS mechanics have been modernized in this classic franchise to provide a near seamless transition from computer controls to the emulation of who DOOM GUY really is. This factor was the main reason I knew I would thoroughly enjoy this game almost immediately into gameplay.

The game provides an "Ironman" mode that it calls Ultra-Nightmare, which is a must-attempt for any hardcore FPS gamer.

Another common gamer quality I possess is the desire for 100% completion, which this game makes easy while still providing reasonable difficulty with completing. Excellent for my little pea brain that wants it all, but stubbornly doesn't want to look up how to get secrets from the internet.

Overall, the community rating of Overwhelming Positive that this game has maintained is very well deserved. An excellent choice for DOOM franchise aficionados, old-school arena FPS players, and those who enjoy thrilling horror-esque audio/visual immersion.

As a final note, shout out to the Bethesda team. I can tell from many elements that this game was spearheaded by the same individuals that created the RAGE franchise and did a helleva improvement on many elements.\t\t\t\t\t
