\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA must play for FPS fans. Only way to describe this game is with only one word. INTENSE. From start to finish its a nonstop ride. I really dont want to compare it to the original games as this game feels like a reboot or reimagining of the DOOM franchise.

You don't play DOOM for the story but there is one, not overally complex but good enough to get you at least interested enough to make it towards the end. Can't say if there is a good or bad ending but the game leaves off with a potential sequel(as of 2018 there will be a sequel).

Sound and music is well done with lots of sounds and riffs of electronic rock along with weapons that make all kinds of noises. The music itself must be heard as its hard to describe but overall it fits with the games theme and tempo perfectly.

DOOM 2016 is a well made well paced game and a must play for those wary of the FPS genre being in a rut. The game proves arcade style shooting can stand on its legs even in the modern game market. High production value and a well done soundtrack culminates into a sensational FPS experience rarely scene in these days. A must own especially the price its going for now you can find it for less then 10 or 20 dollars during sales.\t\t\t\t\t
