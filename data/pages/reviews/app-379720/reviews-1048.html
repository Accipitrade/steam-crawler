\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast, responsive and satisfying, this is what this game strives to be. And it works. It's made for any player looking to disable their brains and to cut demons in half. For casual players there are easier difficulties and for those looking for a challenge there are difficulties that will make this game a nightmare. Many levels with geniusly placed demons that are ready to be destroyed ; many collectibles to collect for the fun of it or to upgrade your Doomslayer. You might say it's a lame attempt at a progression, but i say that for the most part you'll find just enough by just playing the game normally that you will end up maxed out at the final boss, the most hidden collectibles are just there to be funny collectibles to pickup for the hell of it. The weapons are all perfectly designed to be used at a certain situation, even the first shotgun you use can be used at end game because of the attachements you can get. The attachements are what makes this game awesome, it makes guns all feel unique and multitools capable of both riping and tearing demons at the same time. The game rewards players for switching weapons constantly, and reward those who go for those sweet sweet glory kills.
Are there problems ? Well yes, the multiplayer might be fun but no one plays it because apparently it's too different compared to the campain. What a shame. And the campain story is... lack luster. I know Doom needs an excuse of a plot to just kill demons but i feel like Doomslayer just get puppeteered despite the intro making it look like he was just going to ignore the directions given to him. The plot could have had more interesting characters other than robot dude and evil lady scientist. I feel like the gameplay reigns supreme in this game but when the levels follow a story i feel like a bit more care to the story could have been given.
So do i recommand this game ? Hell yeah, the game is totally OK and is worth you money big times. If you think you can't run DOOM Eternal just get this game instead. I've not played DOOM Eternal (yet) but if you can run DOOM Eternal get it instead of this one, and if you've already played DOOM Eternal don't buy this game because the lesser amount of abilities and different guns might leave a sour taste in your mouth.\t\t\t\t\t
