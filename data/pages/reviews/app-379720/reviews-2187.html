\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA blend of old and new. Face paced, no reloading, plenty of gibs. Tons of video features to better customize how you want the game to run, though that may not be an issue as it'll run at ultra settings on a GTX 960.

If you're worried about the game being bogged down with story and cut scenes, stuffing and padding out the campaign time then don't be. The cut scenes are few and far between and the ones that do exist are short and simple.

Plenty of weapons to play with from a lowly little pistol all the way up to the BFG 9000, all of which can be upgraded in two ways with both upgrades being accessible and changed at will with the press of a button.

Demon count may be lower than previous titles and the number that will appear on screen is limited but you won't want for blood, and old favorites make a comeback.

All in all a fantastic game and worthy of the name Doom.\t\t\t\t\t
