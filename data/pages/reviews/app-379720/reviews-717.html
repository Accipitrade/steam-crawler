\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll in all a very well made game. If you are a fan of the original Doom series I think you will enjoy it. Even though the game is modernized it still has a semblance of what the original games were. The secret finding, searching for switches and key cards and arena battles all contribute to making this feel like a Doom game and not just another cookie cutter FPS.

The game itself is very fun. Killing stuff feels so good and you just want there to be more and more demons to slay. The progression system adds to the experience in a good way. Level challenges and secret upgrades make exploration and fighting all the better knowing you can soon upgrade your guns for even more demon slaying.

If you have played Doom Eternal first you might find this one to be a little slow in comparison. It is not as hectic or strategical as Eternal. The fighting is a lot simpler as well. There aren't weak points, chainsawing is almost not necessary and there isn't an alternate means to find armor like with the flame belch in Eternal (unless you equip a rune that you get later in the game). To me, Eternal is the superior game but that doesn't mean this one isn't still a lot of fun. Some might find the simpler nature of this game to be more akin to the original series which I would agree with. I've been playing this game on and off ever since it came out so that goes to show that it is very enjoyable even after this long and since Eternal was released.

Oh, multiplayer is pretty much irrelevant. Just look at the achievements and you will see just how few of players have actually played it. But that's not important. The single player modes are what this game is about anyway.\t\t\t\t\t
