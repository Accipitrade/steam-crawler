It's an adrenaline rush all the way through. Once you get used to the concept of glory/ chainsaw kills for ammo, health and armor (which took me a while), the flow is unstoppable. Hunting for upgrades, secrets ect is fun. From a technical standpoint, this game is a showcase of reliability, beauty and polish. Only the boss fights, which are very few to begin with, are a bit stale.

Just don't expect wonders in terms of story, characters or even atmosphere (although it's kinda creepy at times). This isn't that kind of game.\t\t\t\t\t
