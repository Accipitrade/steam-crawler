\r\n\t\t\t\t\t My review for DOOM after ~20 hours

After seeing and hearing so many good things about this 2016 reboot of a classic game, I decided to pick it up when it was on sale for $6. It was $6 well spent. Had I known I would enjoy this game so much, I would gladly have paid full price for it.

PROS:

-Combat that somehow never gets stale
-A compelling story that isn't too intrusive on gameplay, but will have you wondering what happens next.
-Actually hard to find collectibles.
-A sort of Creation Kit similar to that from the Elder Scrolls series.
-Variety of methods you can use to commit genocide on demons.
-BIG ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ GUNS
-BIG ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ DEMONS

CON(S):

-The multiplayer and the multiplayer achievements. I really wanted to 100% this game, but that would mean having to grind the multiplayer mode at least a little. However, either I am too noobish to understand what is happening, or there are a good amount of speed hacking, one shotting, max level players in this game. First game I was in, one guy on my team gets 56 - 2, in a TDM mode, where 75 kills wins the match. It's insane. Anyway, I knew I wasn't going to be able to grind MP after seeing that.


MP aside, DOOM is a great FPS-RPG game with a decent story and I recommend it to anyone who is slightly interested in it, especially if it is on sale for 75% off.
