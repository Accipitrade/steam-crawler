DOOM 2016 also has deathmatch-type multiplayer and custom maps called Snapmaps, two things that DOOM Eternal (as of december 2020) does not have. The cutscenes are unskippable, but besides that, a good majority of the plot can be ignored as much or as little as you want.
Overall, this is the perfect start for modern DOOM. At such a low price-point, it's more than worth the purchase.\t\t\t\t\t
