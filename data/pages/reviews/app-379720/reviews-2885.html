\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is DOOM. This game has DOOM Guy. He is like the original but with a more modern outtake on life and he can see more than he used to, oh and he also lost his middle arm. (But that can be changed in settings)
DOOM Guy likes to smash demons into pieces, tear them apart, blind them, chop them up, vaporize them, steal their life energy and blow the living **** out of them. DOOM Guy enjoys all of this and it also has the added benefit of saving the normal world from an invasion of demons that were released by some scientists.



TL;DR
DOOM is a good fun game that has an amazing atmosphere and combat mechanic and the singleplayer for this game is incredible.

DOOM 2016 is essentially just a remake of the original game using 3D level designs and adding more content like a story that explains why the player is killing demons instead of just becuase they can. Although this game hasn't exactly changed the way games are going to be designed in the future unlike the original, it does incorporate some incredible aspects to it.

The atmosphere is designed to feel close and almost oppressive, the level design keeps you on your toes ready for anything that could come flying at you. Everything has a dark feel to it or is covered in blood before you even start shooting. There are pentagrams everywhere with candles around them which adds to the feel that the things you are fighting are straight from hell and have been summoned. The music and sound design is also amazing because it takes the original ideas of the classic and embellishes and adapts to a modern audience.

The gameplay is superb, with everything being focussed around the combat and movement around the maps. This game was made with the idea of a free-moving and fluid shooter that is great fun and offers lots of interesting and gory ways to kill demons; whether with your bare hands, grenades, shotguns, high velocity projectiles or even a chainsaw. The combat is so good and well polished that I have to say it is the best first person combat engine I have ever played, the only thing surpassing it being the third person freeflow combat from Warner Bros.

This game has been very well optimized with my actually relatively outdated GPU (R9 280X) handling it fine on high settings exept for one scene with a large background of Mars through a window that causes my frames to drop to about 40 but that is it; the rest of the time no matter how many chunks and bits of demon are flying around my frames stay at a constant 60 at 1080p.

I will be honest and say that I haven't played the multiplayer at all because I prefer single player or more tactical multiplayer games such as Arma (when it doesn't crash) or Counter Strike (when it doesn't break and screw you over) and according to the beta from earlier in the year, the multiplayer feature isn't that good anyway; one of my only problems with this game.

The support and replayability for this game is just amazing, even now there are updates to the fundamental sections of the game such as team deathmatch for multiplayer and arcade mode for singleplayer. Both of these were large updates and have been well implemented. The arcade mode allows the player to have every single weapon and bonus unlocked from the start and so can be used purely for the fun of going around the maps only loosely listening to the story while smashing stuff to pieces.

Unfortunately, there are problems with this game but luckily this list is short. I already mentioned about the multiplayer but if you want to go and 100% the game in a single slot then you have to do each mission individually and if you exit to do anything, even one of the in-game challenges then you will have to start that mission again from the start which really sucks. The only other problem was a pretty underwhelming and easy last boss, I won't spoil anything but I found the mid-game boss harder even with pretty much the same weaponry, items and gear.

In conclusion, if this game is on sale and is less than the equivalent of 20 quid (GBP) and your hardware meets the minimum requirements, then buy this immediately.
