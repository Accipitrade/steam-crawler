\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't think I can accurately describe this game. Whenever I let someone play games on my computer this is one of the games I reach for. The opening level is so well designed, and the tutorial is so perfect that I've yet to meet anyone that didn't get hooked and kept playing into the next one.

Since Mick had a recent falling out from ID software after Doom Eternal, this may be the last time sound design and music is expertly done for a single player shooter. Ever. I cannot think of another soundtrack I've enjoyed so much. The respect given to the past composers from Doom 1993 and how his creative process morphed over time is just amazing.

Some may prefer the simplicity of Doom 2016 over Doom Eternal, I've yet to play it so I can't comment but I would guess Doom 2016 should still be played first to master the basic mechanics before they get elaborated on in Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
