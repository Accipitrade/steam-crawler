\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou know.. I would LOVE to review this game... But im probably too busy playing it so here's a recipe for chocolate chip cookies instead.

Ingredients:
1 cup of butter
1 cup of sugar
1 cup of brown sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
3 cups of flour
1 teaspoon of baking soda
2 teaspoons of hot water
1/2 teaspoon of salt
2 cups of chocolate chips

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
3. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Additional Notes:
*Prep Time - 20 minutes
*Cook Time - 10 minutes
*Ready in 1 hour!

Tip:
Use alluminum foil and cooling oil so the cookies won't stick! Happy eating!! :))\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is an amazing game for not just the obvious, i.e., that id Software came back to form (like its old days) and delivered a great game. No, my review is focused on the fact that with 4 year old hardware, the number of frames per second (FPS) in this game while leverging VULKAN (new graphics API) is through the roof. DOOM is a showcase of what might happen in the next 2-3 years when studios start utilizing game engines (third party or in-house) whose creators have mastered VULKAN and/or DirectX 12. Namely, performance that is likely to astonish you. In my particular case, I'm running at a resolution of 3440x1440 (21:9) with ULTRA settings and TXAA (1x) on an original nVidia GTX Titan GPU (2013). Even so, with all the dials set to "11", the game is doing about 90-100 fps -- a very big deal for a game where the action is extremely fast paced and input latency is best kept to a minimum. Contrast this my last two purchases on Steam where I requested & received a refund due to poor performance (Deus Ex:Mankind Divided & DISHONORED 2).\t\t\t\t\t
