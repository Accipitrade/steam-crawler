\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe thing about DOOM is that you'd think it gets boring but... No, actually. Even when you've seen the same glory kill for the billionth time, you still enjoy watching your enemy pop into a fountain of gloop. There's just something great about this game. It's fast paced, it's juicy with gore, and there's a progression to the weapons and upgrade system that feels natural, not crammed in because it's trendy. There is a story but it doesn't bore you with it like other shooters, it's just there in the background, hanging out, waiting for you to look into the lore before bothering to talk about it.

Not to mention, that upgrade system I mentioned? If you get all the secrets, if you hunt down all the challenges and upgrades, I've seen what that can do. If you do all of that, you earn the title DOOM Slayer, because you become practically invincible.

This game is proof that you don't need to be some emotional, meaningful snoozefest like COD to survive in the world of FPSs, that sometimes they don't need to evolve with the times. Sometimes, all you need is an angry stranger with a big gun.... And that's enough for us.\t\t\t\t\t
