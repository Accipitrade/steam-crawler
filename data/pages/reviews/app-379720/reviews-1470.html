Very Fast Pace action with tons of various enemy will keep you busy for a long time. Glory kill mechanics was my most favorite. Story is not that great but as a badass first person demon slaying game it was too good. Various weapon made this game more enjoyable with upgrade tree. I was expecting more boss fight but ended up disappointed. It could be more great if the devs gives more bosses. But except story and boss issue this game is worth every penny. And it has also arcade mode and many more challenges with multiplayer.

Let's Give Some Pros and Cons. Shall We?

Pros:

1. Good Visuals.
2. Glory Kills.
3. Satisfying FPS.
4. Various Demons.
5. Tons of Side Activities.
6. Various Weapons.

Cons:

1. Repetitive Enemy Spawn For a long time.
2. Less Bosses.
3. Mediocre Story.
4. Ending Not Satisfying.
5: Checkpoint Mechanism Sucks.\t\t\t\t\t
