\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic revival of the Doom series. Anything I could say to praise it has already been said by many other people.

The one thing I will say, is don't rely on Steam's cloud save function for this game. I've uninstalled this game a couple times to make room for other things, and EVERY time I've reinstalled it, my save file has been corrupted. There is (from what I understand) no fix for this. If you're going to uninstall, MANUALLY BACKUP YOUR SAVE FILE, otherwise, accept that you'll have to restart the games story.

This game is also filled, and I mean FILLED with minor bugs. Idk why none have been patched out, but most have been around for years. Doors wont open even though you have the proper key, getting stuck on walls, the floor, tiny little ledges, etc. They all happen frequently too. Not enough to make the experience worse overall, but enough to annoy the hell out of you in the moment.

I get that this review sounds negative, but I enjoyed 99% of my experience with this game. I could waste a few paragraphs saying the same things other people have said, or trying to make some unique take on it, but I won't. It's Doom, a bloody, gruesome, badass romp to hell and back. Just keep these overall very minor issues in mind when you do go into it.\t\t\t\t\t
