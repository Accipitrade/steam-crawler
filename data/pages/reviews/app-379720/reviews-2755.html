\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis Doom lives up to the original and perhaps goes much farther even if you discount the obvious technical possiblites 20+ years has brought. The orignal was signifigant and this is too. It feels right in a way Doom3 never did(not that Doom3 was bad). Fast, viseral, intense and all around badass. Still have not finished it but you can pretty much tell this is a masterpiece in the first 5 minutes of playing and it only gets better. Deathmattch, arcade and snapmaps are only put it over the top. Every time I play this game it puts a smile on my face, which is something few games have ever done.

This is 10/10.

I sincerely hope they continue on to make a few sequels. Perhaps something like a new hell on earth. The could probably release 10 more campaigns like this and I doubt I could get tired of playing them.
