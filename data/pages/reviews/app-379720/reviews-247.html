\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBack in 2016, this was the absolute best FPS you could play.

While DOOM Eternal certainly makes this game seem like a slow paced prototype, it still retains all what made it great back in 2016. The visuals are still stunning, the combat is satisfying, the soundtrack kicks some major ass and I adore the way most of the plot is told in the background through lore items. You can literally pick it up and play it and understand the story in-front of you but should you need more details, you can seek them out yourself.

If you have never played DOOM before, then you live under a rock but you can easily make this your first DOOM game and get what the games are all about. Every DOOM game is also on Steam, so you can easily pick them up and check them out at your pleasure.

A major surprise for me recently was discovering there is still an active but small player base still keeping the multiplayer portion of the game alive. To my knowledge and own testing, the multiplayer is definitely dead on consoles. Back in 2016 the multiplayer was criticised and I don't understand why. Sure, it was not built in house by id Software but it is still a blast to play. It is classic, fast paced arena fun and then some. But be warned, there is the odd cheater lurking in the multiplayer, so if you see them, do everyone a favour and get them reported.

I cannot recommend this game enough and my enthusiasm for the series cannot ever be understated. This is simply a must play game for any gamer!\t\t\t\t\t
