\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRip and tear.

DOOM 2016 is a beautiful reboot. It's a brutal, gory, fast-paced game that will continiue giving you uglier and uglier demons and bigger and bigger guns to fight them (and I suppose if you're not into this or you're squemish, this is not a game for you).

First let's talk about the most important thing - the combat. It's so amazingly fluid and fun you will be craving more. There is enough difficulty levels to adjust it so that it's difficult enough to be challenging but not impossible depending on your skills. Game equips you with variety of guns (though you will quickly stop using the starter ones as they do almost no damage to more advancd enemies) and encourages you to be mobile so you will be running, shooting and jumping from ledges with new demons spawning in. Overall the combat feels great and it keeps you engaged.

Out of other things - grpahics are beautiful (well, more like disgusting, but beautiflly disgusting and gory), the music is on point and it will have you feel the urge to make more digital bloodshed, the save system is fine (that is usually you don't have to backtrack). If you're determined the game is riddled with secrets and collectibles, including pieces of original maps from older Doom games.

I suppose if I was to find some fault it would be that some weapon mods are pretty useless, so most likely you won't even try them. If you want to collect everything in the first go it will also stall the game - but this is by design, so I'm just happy I can get the collectibles later (either new playthrough or replay of the missions I'm lacking them in).

Overall a really solid, fun to play game with amazing music.\t\t\t\t\t
