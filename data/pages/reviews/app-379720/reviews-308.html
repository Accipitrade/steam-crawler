\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game manages to capture the original games feel and then turns up the chaos to ten!

If you are a fan of the original doom series of games then you need to be buying this one, but if you are just a fan of FPS fast paced destruction then this will also fit the bill. The movement is fluid, your weapons pack serious punch and the variety of enemies is just like the original. You don't spend long fighting basic creatures in this game and it doesn't drip feed you monsters like Doom 3 did. You will be fighting against the worst that the bowels of hell can throw at you, but what this game does that other legendary titles have also managed (such as Halo) is that it makes you feel like the badass. You are the boogieman in the demons nighttime tales, you are cutting swathes through their ranks and brutalising any foolish enough to stand in your wake. I don't know why more games don't do this nowadays, for example Far cry started with you as this feral enhanced badass, but then they switched gears massively and you became the typical weak protagonist being hunted, knocked out and in some cases disease ridden so you are weaker than the average person....why? What kind of power fantasy is that in a game? I want to feel like the badass, not the one getting stomped on and barely making it out! Only horror titles and thriller games should make you feel like the prey, but action games should have you be the predator. I don't know, maybe that is just me? But if you feel the same way then this game will give you that feeling of being the apex hunter of an array of hapless demons that were too slow to get out of your way. This is how you reboot a game and modernise it!
