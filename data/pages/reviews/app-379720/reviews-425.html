\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSomething felt really strange about DOOM (2016). It was hard to put my finger on it until I got to first boss fight in Hell. That's when it hit me; this boss killed me 5 times in a row and I wasn't raging, about crush my keyboard or huck a controller across the room

... Because it was obvious what I was doing wrong.

See I've gotten accustomed to Modern Gaming (TM) where you feel more like it's the game screwing you over when you lose than being bad at the game. "Here!" says Modern Gaming (TM). "Let me give you 50 boring enemies to fight with the same AI; some are arbitrarily bullet sponges; some have indiscernible strengths and weaknesses which can never be fully covered by the 2 guns you can carry; and oh lets force you to reload all the time and not be able to shoot because to move quickly and dodge, you need to sprint."

DOOM (2016) has none of that. There's nothing in the game that's there for any other reason than to kill demons. Nothing to impede gameplay:
- Different guns are good for killing different demons. But you don't need to choose; you carry them all at once
- Each enemy is visually and behaviorally unique. The behaviors are quite varied, and yet are easily comprehensible. And exploitable!
- There's no sprinting which cancels your ability to shoot, because you can move awesomely fast all the time.
- There's no BS, one-hit KO attacks out of nowhere
- And no need to cower behind cover when low on health, praying to RNGesus a grenade doesn't appear out of nowhere and yeet you; PLAYING THE GAME, MOVING FAST, RIPPING AND TEARING is how you regenerate health, not cowardice!

I had almost zero rage moments playing DOOM (2016) on Ultra Violence difficulty despite dying a TON because it never felt like the game was screwing me over. All the tools are in your hands at all times. If you're dying, its because you're not using them correctly.

Also the graphics are amazing, the animations are even better, and the violence is cranked up to 11. I can't believe I didn't know about this game sooner. It's amazing fun -- easily the best shooter of the 2010s.\t\t\t\t\t
