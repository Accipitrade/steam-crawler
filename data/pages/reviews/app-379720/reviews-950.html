OVERALL: 8.5/10


DOOM is definitely back on track with this 2016 edition, it feels like a return to a more authentic experience compared to DOOM 3. One of the strongest comebacks we've seen in recent history, no doubt.

Only negative thing is that, still today, there's many little glitches and bugs such as occasionnal crashes, invisble menus, suddenly getting lauched while jumping onto objects, alt tabbing suddenly not working, some mobs gettings stuck somewhere they cant be killed thus preventing you from exiting lockdowns... All minor inconveniences, but stacked on top of each other, 4 years after release... Yep be mindful of that.

GAMEPLAY: 10/10

Flawless, perfect, fast, satisfying, nervous, fluid.... I could go on and on and on and on.

LEVEL DESIGN: 8.5/10

Great construction, great use of verticality, love this old school attitude toward secret zones etc. Clearly one of the most rewarding exploration in fps games.

VISUAL & ART: 8.75/10

Blood, sheet and guts mixed with great futuristic Mars space bases and an interesting take on hell. Quite brutal, intense and interesting.

SOUND & MUSIC: 6.5/10

Honestly I don't like the soundtrack, these mindless metal tracks are just annoying, I know it's brutal and violent and blablabla and is supposed to fit the theme but it just pulls me out of the game instead of dragging me in. I also can't stand the audio mixing of these possesed trash mobs, their faaking grunt is the loudest thing in the game, come on they're the least threatening lowest tier mobs there is yet they're the loudest most annoying mobs I've encoutered in years... Unbelievable xD!

On the other hand the gun sounds are exceptionally good and most mobs and sfx are great too.

REPLAYABILITY: 7.25/10

With DOOM Eternal out, I doubt the multiplayer is highly populated, wasn't too interested in it anyway. Regarding the solo experience, it's not a very lenghty campaign but it's good enough to play it mutiple times around, to unlock secrets/upgrades and/or on a higher difficulty. By all accounts it is decent.\t\t\t\t\t
