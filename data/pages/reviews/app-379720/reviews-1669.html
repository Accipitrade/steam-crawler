\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun game. Play in short bursts though as to not get burnt out as the game is very repetitive. There is not much to do other than kill demons, find secrets, and read lore if you are into that kind of thing. Also the game is very high velocity in general so that's enough reason to play it cool.

But the demon killing is very fun. It doesn't get old. The arsenal of weapons is small compared to other FPS but the guns that are there are all varied, different, fun to use, and simply put- iconic. Killing bad guys is immensely satisfying, and dodging their projectiles while making sure to land your shots means the action never stops. Glory kills are very fun and don't get old throughout a first run through of the game. They are quick, brutal, and creative, and it's always fun to get one. The only exceptions may be the Cacodemon and the Mancubus glory kills. There is basically only one way to kill either of them so they kind of get old, it's much more satisfying to blow them to pieces with a powerful rocket or double barreled blast. But the chainsaw kills are very, very cool no matter what. Don't forget to use the chainsaw!

The story is also fun to explore. Doom Marine is an excellent protagonist and his character traits, while few, really shine. In my opinion he's the best silent protagonist ever. Everyone else in this world takes themselves so seriously. They are so arrogant, foolish, poorly equipped and evil. Their long winded monologues are delivered to the player under the assumption that both the Doom Slayer and the player are rolling their eyes from behind their perspective screens. The Doom Marine becomes relatable and likeable because of this. Something I can't say for, say, Gordon Freeman or Link, who are quiet for no discernable reason and their awkward lack of silence makes them seem strange in comparison to the characters around them. It's easy to know why the Doom Slayer says nothing in this game: because there is nothing to say. There's no reasoning with these people so why waste breathe?

That about sums it up. In short: buy and play this game, and you know, rip and tear until it is done, yadda yadda etc etc.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is one of the best shooters of the previous decade. The gameplay is the crown jewel of the game. The gunplay is very rarely clunky and, almost all of the time, the shooting is great. As long as youre aiming at the enemy, your shots will hit the enemy. No janky hitboxes or targets that are easy to miss, just nice shooting mechanics. Theres a hefty variety of weapons and enemies too that make sure the game never gets stale throughout the games short 10-12 hour runtime. Weapons range from a basic pistol. Machine guns, automatic guns, a chainsaw, grenades, and many more different types of weapons. The game also features these really cool final blow kills where, if you get the enemy to low enough health, you can pull off melee attacks where it switches to a cutscene of you ripping apart your enemy into pieces or pulling their hearts out and forcing them the opponent to ingest their own heart. You can purchase upgrades to upgrade your weapons too to make them stronger or add on parts to the weapon such as a scope or an explosive barrel. The environments of DOOM are also great. They range from government facilities, to the rough landscape of Mars, to hell itself. My only problem with the story mode is the story. It is decently forgettable, though the story just seems like an afterthought as they know that a story really isnt needed for this game, it just seems like a vessel for them to show off the great gameplay and mechanics of the game. The online mode is pretty great, too. Its incredibly addictive. In the online mode, you face against other players in game modes that aren't the most original and have been used a lot in other games but are still fun such as Secure the area or team deathmatch. You get to choose 2 weapons out of a wide variety of the singleplayer weapons to use for your life and can switch up weapons when you die. The twist on these are the powerups where you can increase your shield which will protect or health bar from depleting or become a demon which can deliver much more powerful attacks than the weapons. Overall, DOOM is not only one of the best shooters of the last decade but one of the most unique and standout games to be released and a great revival of a great game series.

Score: 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
