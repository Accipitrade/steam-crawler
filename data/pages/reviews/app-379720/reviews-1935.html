\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM was the most fun i've had with a video game in a long time. Simple, fast paced action shooter, with very basic but very well done mechanics (Glory kills giving health meant you never spent any time hiding away trying to regain health, rather you have to do the opposite and throw yourself into more combat). It takes shooting stuff to a whole new level of fun. The story was better than I expected, but it's still just a means to put you in more rooms with more demons to shoot. Heavy metal soundtrack is awesome too.

My major issue is that it began to feel like a bit of a slog towards the end, as rather than introducing newer, tougher enemies the game just started giving you more and more of them to shoot your way through, and with the average story your only motivation was just to shoot more stuff and get to the end.

Still, it was great fun. Would absolutely recommend\t\t\t\t\t
