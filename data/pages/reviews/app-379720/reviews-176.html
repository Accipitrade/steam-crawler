\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn terms of gameplay, this was the best single-player FPS ever made, until they made Doom Eternal. Although honestly, having just replayed it, they might be on equal terms.

The difficulty in this game is *spectacular*. You will feel like a literal god in terms of controlling your character; the enemies are numerous and dangerous, but not bullet sponges; and yet it will defeat you many times, until you master your abilities. Few games rise to this level of satisfaction, and even fewer single-player FPS games.

Even aside from the gameplay, the production values are amazing, every single member of the staff of this game gave 110%; the artists, the voice actors, the coders, they put everything into this and it shows. Six years after its release, this game looks cutting-edge; if you've acquired a new video card since then, give it another look.

If you've never experienced this game, buy it. Play on whatever difficulty challenges you, I guarantee you will have a blast.\t\t\t\t\t
