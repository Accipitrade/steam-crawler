\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPurely as a singleplayer experience, this game is absolutely magical, and worthy of the legacy of DOOM. I have played, and replayed this game on PS4 to the point of earning the platinum trophy and I bought it on PC to test out my new rig. It goes without saying that I enjoyed this game immesely. From the varied levels, to the bosses, to the guns. This game does it all very well. The only real complaint I can level against this game is the gun sounds aren't as meaty as they deserve to be. Listen to the gun sounds of the original DOOM to understand where I am coming from. The chunkiness of the reports from the original guns are still to this day the best in the genre. From the slick boom of the Shotgun, to the loud clacks of the chaingun, to the whizz of the rockets. They should have taken more from the originals soundscape and tried to improve me. The guns sound so weak. They don't sound bad. They give you enough auditory information that you know what you're shooting, but it feels phoned in.

This is one of the best optimized games I have ever played.\t\t\t\t\t
