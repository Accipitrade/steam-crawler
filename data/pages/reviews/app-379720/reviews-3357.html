This game is a surprising return to form from id after their latest titles. Theyve managed to distance themselves from the typical whac-a-mole cover shooter we've been served since the rise of the xbox and delivered a modern take on one of the great classics.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I've been playing new Doom and I must say, game exceeded my expectations by a mile. If I had to describe game with only one sentence it would be "This is so oldschool and metal as f*ck".

First thing that happens when you press "start new game" is you dropped right in the middle of the game and only thing you see is demons, pistol and space marine suit. So you get your ass (literally, since you wake up naked) in space marine suit and start slaying hell spawn.

I had a a suspicion that sneaky f*cks from Bethesda just made such game intro to appease Doom fans and that it will go back to "modern" standards of gameplay and story. But no, they did it, they made oldschool hardcore shooter that is metal as f*ck, absolute MAD MEN.

No stupid hand holding, corny story, cutscenes, "modern" gameplay elements like ADS or QTE (press "F" to pay respect). The game is purely oldschool arena shooter. The only "modern" additions are gun mods, which actually completely transform your guns into something else. Like shotgun into grenade launcher, or assault rifle into rocket launcher. So I don't mind it. Adds lot of variety.

Last things - secrets are back, rooms that lead to "Classic Doom" levels and other bonuses, like access to powerful weapons before normal progression, like getting Double Barrel shotgun way before you normally would stumble upon it.

Anyway, from just play 6 hours, game is top notch. I would recommend it to both people who grew up in the 90s with shooters like Doom, Quake and Wolfenstein, as well as people who just got tired of "modern shooters" formula and want to try something different.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game with solid gameplay, fun single-player campaign (as far as I can tell, only scratched the surface).

Bought this game mainly for the MP, longing for the feel of the "old"-school FPS like UT and Q3.

This is not however a new UT or Quake, and for fans expecting to get the same experience you will get somewhat disappointed.

Sure it bears much resemblance and it might be the closest you can get this day and age but there are some things that keeps me from really loving the MP (because I do like it, don't get me wrong IT IS A GOOD GAME).

There are however some gameplay mechanics I don't think fit in a game like this.

I'm talking about the "loadout", instead of picking the guns from the ground you chose a loadout of two-weapons and a utility item (like a frag grenade or a personal teleporter).

This takes away the fun with learning where the weapon locations are on the map and learning how to get to them the fastest way.

It also takes away the fun that comes from seeing another player with a gun you really really really think oughta be yours instead (since it goes better with your armor colour, obviously).

In its current form the game reward "Praying" & "spraying" (chaingun and combat shotgun, two weapons that lets you aim with your screen rather then your crosshair, ok I might be over exaggerating a bit but you get the point) which makes about half the weapon pool obsolete in multiplayer.

Sure you might argue that there are indeed uses for the other guns (since these two weapons are mainly for mid-range to CQC).

But presently you are going to find yourself on the business end of either one of these weapons more often than not.
But this is nothing that a little bit of balancing can fix.

And lastly you have the demon-rune that spawn on the map, turning the player into a powerful demon.
If killed as a demon (before the time runs out) the other team will have a chance to take the rune for themselves.

The demon run is a fun and welcome gameplay addition at first, but in the end it does wreak havoc on the balance of the matches, so I personally would rather se a specific gameplay mode for the demon rune (there are some gameplay modes without it, but I don't really like it being in TDM).

So if you like fast paced MP-FPS games then pick it up!
If you are expecting to get a competetive and well balanced old-school arena shooter then I'd suggest that you wait for additional balancing and a old-school gameplay mode.\t\t\t\t\t

+ Secrets and reference to old doom games and bethesda games in general (found one from S*****)

+ If you love doom 1-2 you should pick up this game now. Everything you saw in those games, you will see it here but with a DAMN GOOD graphics upgrade. PLUS a huge amount of new stuff.

+ Laugted my a** off when i read the line "Kill everything you see" in campaign quest description. This game does not mess around :)

- The multiplayer wait time. Not a real problem tbh but FPS games should be: JUST GO!

- I will probaly not play longer than a week because of next weeks Fallout 4 Far Harbor release.

In general a very very good game that you sure want to add to your collection. And do respect the 18+ note on this game. It's VERY violent and VERY gory.

So far I've played around 11 hours of Campaign, and I've played around 8 missions of 13.

The multiplayer is fast paced and fun. Not the best MP, but certainly entertaining.\t\t\t\t\t
