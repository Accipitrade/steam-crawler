\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI tried the first two levels during the last major content update and was surprised at not only how pretty the game is, but how playable the levels were. My previous experiences with Bethesda warned me to expect bland levels and even blander characters, but this is a doom game--I don't care about the characters, I care about fun ways to kill demons. And this game has that in spades. It is *FUN* to kill demons again. Even up-close and personal.

There's just enough customization possible to allow for a few different play styles, if that's an itch you like to scratch. However, the game also includes a rating system (which is needed to improve your character. So the game is rewarding players who don't need the help with more powers...)

Overall, this is an excellent contribution to the Doom franchise and gives me hope that one day Bethesda will make other good, high-quality franchise games.\t\t\t\t\t
