\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM was one of the best games of First-Person style I have played in a long while and I couldn't escape the entrancing gore and violence that it entails.

The campaign is nice and long (about 8 hours if played without intentionally trying to find secrets etc.) and is just challenging enough to make you think hard about tactics to fight different bosses and demons.

I haven't had much experience with the multiplayer as I have focussed on the campaign but I must say it is rather fun to play - fighting against something other than just demons.

As far as snap-maps go, it is a brilliant idea and I will certainly play around with it in the coming days/weeks.

All-In-All; if you like gore, shooting the hell (literally) out of demons and bashing their heads in for hours on end with little escaping it then this is the game for you!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBest game of 2016 bar none! Graphically it's the best game I've seen all year, OpenGL is such a powerful API and I hope that Vulkan as its successor becomes a more widely used API, because the way the game looks and the way it runs as 1440p and 4k is beyond belief... It's not an open world game, but the amount of detail in each environment just beggars belief. The fact that an OpenGL engine looks as good as it does, with realistic GPU accelerated particles (WITHOUT PHYSX!!) just boggles my mind!

Anyway everything in the game is superb! From the gun play, the soundtrack to the visual composition of each level is on point all the time from start to finish! The glory kills NEVER loose their charm, and the boss battles are epic! Played the game on Nightmare from the start and I did die a lot towards the end, but that's because I wanted to get all the achievements and all the masteries, and that takes patience and the will to kill Mancubuses with the pistol.

Still need to play the game on Ultra-Nightmare, and honestly I can't wait to replay, die and start from the beginning again :D


The Multiplayer sucks, I mean it's fun but NOT as fun as it could have been... Literally remove the 2 gun limit... Give everyone a pistol at the start, and may the best man/woman win :D... I love the customization options on everything, but that's just about it... Also I don't actually like the fact that the Seasons pass is 40€ for just the multiplayer part and nothing else... That just sucks balls...

So yeah SnapMap is AWESOME!!! 10 hours from the 27 hours I've played the game were spent playing, learning and creating snap maps... Really good part of DOOM and one that will insure that people still play doom for years to come, because SNapMap isn't just multiplayer! it's CO-OP and singleplayer! Endless gory possibilities!!!


10/10 would burn in a puddle of molten rock to get the Terminator 2 Easter egg!


PS. It's also nominated for "BEST MOST BAD ASS GAME EVAR!!!" in The Steam Awards.\t\t\t\t\t
