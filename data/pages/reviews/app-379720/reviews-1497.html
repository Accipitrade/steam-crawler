Constantly requiring to move, you'll destroy enemies and feel like a badass while doing it. All the weapons feel beefy, but you'll probably end up using shotgun the most.

All in all, a very fun game that is enjoyable even 4 years later at the time of writing my review (I replayed it)\t\t\t\t\t
