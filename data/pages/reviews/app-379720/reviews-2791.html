\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPositives:
- Mostly everything. Combat, artstyle, soundtrack, enemies, story and characters were executed well or perfectly.
- Ultra Nightmare is my new Nuclear Throne daily run.

Negatives:
- Glory kills are disorienting and gets mundane after a while. I do like the gameplay implications of the glory kill system, but i wish it was possible to disable the cutscenes so that you are in control permanently.
- Speaking of which, even tho you are invulnerable while performing a glory kill, i found that i'd regularly die the instant the glory kill was finished, because enemies would keep doing their things for the, admittedly small, duration of the animation.
- The filesize of this game is grotesquely large.

Creating a worthy successor to the legacy of the original DOOM is a monumental task, but id software nailed it.
Verdict: 9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
