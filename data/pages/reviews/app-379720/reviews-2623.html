\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI finally got a new tabletop computer, so I got the chance to play some titles I've longed to experience... Like DOOM! And hell, was it a damned ride on a rollercoaster to hell and back, littered with ichor-dripping corpses of the foul demons.

The new DOOM captions the spirit of "Rip and tear!" extremely well, I would dare say even better than the old classics with the partition from old gameplay in favor of the new glory kill mechnic. But does it capture the feeling of the classics themselves? Not quite so, I think. The difficulty levels can be a bit confusing for those who come from the classics, since they are named in a similar manner, yet they are vastly different. Myself I played through the campaign on "Hurt me plenty" difficulty, and danced through most of the combat without much effort. I really should have picked the hardest available for first run, but the game was enjoyble none the less.

If you want some action packed adrenaline pumping unadultered ultra-violence, and be the man that Hell itself couldn't contain, go play DOOM.\t\t\t\t\t
