It's a good game but it felt like you had to aproach enemies from the correct angle or you would fail. Again that didn't happen until the seccond half, it felt like you weren't given as many options to dismantle your foes. When the combat works it really works, it just feels like they started to limit your options near the end. Also the multiplayer sucks! Why are there loadouts in a doom game!?!! It feels like they took quake multiplayer, removed the sense of speed, and took skill out of the equation by only allowing you to use weapons you unlocked. I'd give it a 7/10 over all, and yes I did take away points for bad multiplayer, Doom 1 and 2 had amazing multiplayer that you can still play today using doomseeker and there's still people using it every day, I doubt this games multiplayer is even still alive after 3 years, so yes I will take 1.5 points away for watered down mediocre multiplayer, bite me.\t\t\t\t\t
