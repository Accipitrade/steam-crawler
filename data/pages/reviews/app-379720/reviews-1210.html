DOOM (2016) is the polar opposite of a generic FPS game.
Imagine having an arsenal of weapons that are lethal to the point of having missiles, to just use them against demons in the must brutal way possible.
Ripping and tearing demons apart, breaking their jaws, smashing their heads, feeding them their own hearts and even annihilating them with the BFG 9000 right into their mouth.

DOOM (2016) is the very definition of a brutal and fun experience. Even if you're someone who can't stand gore, you might come to love this piece of work. Movement is extraordinarily fun and the best way to experience this game is playing it on the hardest difficulties, so it's more of a challenge.

Overall, DOOM (2016) might be one of the best FPS's of our generation.\t\t\t\t\t

🔥🔥🔥 DOOM 2016 REVIEW 🔥🔥🔥

Full action pack, awesomeness overloaded with tons of satisfactory moments. Every moment feels alive and overwhelmed.
You play is DOOMGUY. The literal definition of OP. everything in front of you whether it's imp or huge mancubus, feels so stratifying when you Slay them one by one.

💀💀 Story 💀💀

It's not bad. Decent.

😈😈 Gameplay 😈😈

This where the fun begins as you slaughter every single of them from pistol to literal BFG 9000. Glory kills to power ups it's all refreshing, You will never feel boring doing the same thing for 69420 times, every single time it's the same as the first one. You can be creative and choose your weapon variety, You can be normal people who only use Super shotgun fight till the end with that or you can be badass super doomguy who uses timing, right weapon to them right enemy causing some real fireworks and chain attacks.
Weapon mods: very important, upgrade to the max and complete the challenge, You will be on another level.
Exploration is another aspect of the game which fascinates me like how from killing waves of demons you stop and look around, use your big brain to get that secret location. Never have I regretted once exploring the map.

👀👀 Graphics 👀👀

10/10 nothing else to say.
OVERALL 10/10. Not a masterpiece game but a masterpiece game.\t\t\t\t\t
