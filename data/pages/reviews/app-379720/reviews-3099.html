Pros:
+Classic DOOM-like feel to the game
+Beautiful graphics (and Vulkan support, who can't forget about that)
+Awesome design of the demons and the weapons
+Open-map face paced arena
+Multiplayer (in my opinion needs some tweaking)
+Bring something new to the DOOM series
+Runes
+Suit upgrades
+Weapon upgrades
+Rarely buggy
+Quake feeling to Multiplayer
Cons:
-Lacks story, like most DOOM games
-Ending felt lustlacker but it's forgiven
-Sometimes it can be hard to find a multiplayer game
-Very demanding on PC hardware
-60 GIGBYTES in STORAGE

I would recommend this game unlike F4, I actually enjoyed this game and it was a very fun experince for me. Worth the $35 bucks I dropped onto this game. Defintely snag it during a sale, it's worthy in being in your game stash.\t\t\t\t\t
