\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn absolute must play. It is gory, demon-slaying fun. The guns and the music work together perfectly to create, in my humble homie opinion, one of the best gaming experiences known to mankind.

Upsides:
-The music is killer! Mick Gordon did a fantastic job with the soundtrack.
-The gunplay is just about as good as you can get, and in my experience beats 90% of the shooters I have played.
-The campaign is well crafted, and keeps you engaged in the story (even if you're just in it to frag the demons).
-The level design is very well done, and if you ever get lost there is always the PDA screen to help you orient yourself within your surroundings.
-The art style is very pleasing to eye, even when most of it is covered in guts. The weapons are crafted beautifully, the demon models are incredibly detailed and clean looking, and the environments are frankly stunning (if you can get past all the blood and guts covering just about everything lmao)
-Secret hunting! Being rewarded for exploration is always a win.

Downsides:
-Sometimes the riffs from glory kills cut into the music in a rather jarring way, which can bring you out of the experience.
-Rune challenges can be frustrating if you keep dying, but only because of the simple fact of having to confirm that you would like to continue each time.
-This is more of a pet peeve, but when you are in a loading screen there is very little to show you that the loading is complete other than a small bit of text in the top right corner that blinks. The text is small and in the same color as the loading tip or bits of lore at the bottom.

In conclusion, buy the game! I fully recommend it at full price and especially on sale. If you are reading this review and are on the fence about it, then wait till it is on sale and try it out. You can always return it if it is not your style.

I can't wait for DOOM Eternal!\t\t\t\t\t
