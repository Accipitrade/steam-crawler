\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016 is a great homage to the classics. I think it shines in 2 main areas, movement fluidity and sound. Your character just moves so fast and you have so much freedom to jump and bounce around the map as you slay demons. The sound effects and music give this game that meaty punch, and is why the gunplay is so satisfying.

Performance wise this game is optimized very well. I'm on a higher end rig, but it runs well and cool. No complaints there at all.

I think this game is best played on easy or normal mode (depending on your skill at fps games), with the sound cranked up really high, and your brain completely shut off. Everything around you just melts away and you can immerse yourself in fast paced action. I absolutely recommend this game, and it has tons of great sales all the time.

However there are a few glaring issues I have with the game, and I hope are addressed in future installments (or at least options to work around them). The progression system is terrible and in direct opposition to the gameplay. I'm not a collector, I don't want to go to every corner of the map and find all the secrets, I just want to run directly to combat and finish the levels as fast as possible. The progression system requires you to explore the maps in greater detail in order to level up your gear and character, and I feel this directly opposes what makes the game the most fun.

I also hate the glory kills. The only thing I enjoy is healing by killing enemies, as that encourages aggression, but the brief pause every time you glory kill an enemy really disrupts the pacing. I wish there was a setting to remove the animation entirely. I have no issue with the mechanic itself, just the animation time (and yes the perk to reduce it is not enough for me).

For these reasons I think arcade mode is the best way to play the game, but the numbers for earning score is so intrusive, I wish there were more settings to reduce that ui clutter.

These really are fairly minor complaints, this game is great. I just hope the next installment has more gameplay settings to adjust the issues I mentioned.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThat was excellent?? Really action packed with lots of Pantera like music. It felt almost like the days of Doom 2, it was that much fun.

I really liked the no nonsense approach with secrets, maps, grabbing ledges- most of the time wasting things are gone. Glory kills are obviously the highlight and necessity. The runes and most of the weapon additions are great help as well. You cannot survive without them, even in lower difficulties. After a point, it just throws all sorts of foes on your way. The maps are nicely designed as well, so you do not get stuck somewhere.

The homage to old Doom was nicely done as well - the door sounds, mini maps. It is certainly better than Doom 3 as that was a bit too dark and horror focused.

Good one, I will wait for Doom Eternal now.\t\t\t\t\t
