\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI saw the game on sale and thought "Sweet I can play this with out cutting off my arm to play." I am glad I got it on sale, but mad I even gotten the game. Doom is what happens when you take Duke Nukem 3D polish that terd with decent graphics, and skew the doom story from the game. First off get yourself a nice pillow and some snacks. This game is a grind. Maybe for others the story mode was fun but to me it felt like a chore always using my pistol because starting out you hold hardly anything for ammo. Your enemys are strong. Which is great for a challenge but they come at you in waves that really do not do much but repeat their AI. It drove me nuts chasing down the same 4 people for every secontion of the maps. Pro Tip, just take laps around the map and our enemy will get confused and the AI will just go back to its spawn point. The game's over the top cheesey violence made me feel like rifle butting everything was the only way to kill my enemies after shooting them with my pistol 50 times. I would be using my shot gun or other weapons but going forward I have to unlock ammo capacity. So starting out ammo is thrown at you but you only have 20 rounds. Ending ammo is hard to come by and you now hold 18 rounds out of 150. I would not recomend this game really to anyone looking for something of a story, or even anyone who wants to play the horrible multiplayer where no one knows how to run and gun and actually play. IF you want to kill time and get a hard on for goofy violence go for it. But for me, I want something that is more involing with me not something that was made for 12 year olds to play to feel like they are playing an adult game.\t\t\t\t\t

I prefered the mars base sections to the hell ones mainly because the on rails sections felt better in corridoors than caves, but overall the pace and action was pretty spot on. I thought I would hate the glory kills mechanic but it was actually pretty good. Beserk powerup is by far the most fun thing in this game btw! :)

FYI, I haven't played any deathmatch.\t\t\t\t\t
