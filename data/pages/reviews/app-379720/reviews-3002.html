\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFast, frantic and ultimately fun, you get to do what you really want and just blast monsters without endless cutscene interruptions ruining the flow.

Pros:
+ Campaign isn't a thinly-disguised commentary on current affairs and politics; you just go forth killing ♥♥♥♥ and it feels good.
+ Campaign maps are *really* well designed.
+ Features just work in favour of the gameplay. Nothing feels like it was shoehorned in for no reason.
+ It's a Doom game for the modern era done right. It really is.
+ Snapmap has some decent content to fool around with after you're done with the campaign.

Cons:
- Campaign gets way too easy if you take advantage of suit/weapon upgrades.
- MP is nowhere near as fun as the campaign.
- Snapmap is also loaded with some awful waste that you might end up downloading.

Whatever, if you want some good FPS action "the way it used to be" with a shiny new coat of paint then you'd be a fool to leave this one out. If you'd rather play LOL ARMY SOLDIERS then you'll have an aneurysm trying this so don't bother.\t\t\t\t\t
