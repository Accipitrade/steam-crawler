\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bet 5 minutes of DOOM in the morning would do a better job at waking you up than 4 cups of coffee and a line of cocaine.


UPDATE:

I've decided to give some actual substance to my review, because DOOM deserves it.

When the original DOOM came out, my family didn't have a computer powerful enough to run it. We had Wolfenstein 3D at least, so it wasn't a completely terrible childhood.

But this kid who lived across the street from me did. His name was Andrew, and I hated that kid. He had all the game systems and a gaming computer though, so I had no choice but to use him for his toys. I think Andrew's parents bought him all that stuff so he could coax kids over to his house, because I'm sure they also knew how much Andrew sucked. Yeah, Andrew sucked.

Andrew would never let me see the manual to Mortal Kombat (all the character combos were in there) and then he'd just freeze me, "get over here" me, and pull off all these fatalities. Boy, did Andrew sure like beating the crap out of me in that game! He didn't like it when I just started spamming the uppercut attack after I got him trapped into a corner.

Losing to Andrew was annoying, cuz the little ♥♥♥♥ wouldn't give you a chance to learn the game, but beating Andrew was a real horrorshow. He would rip cartridges out of consoles, throw controllers against walls, and one time he grabbed a pillow and screamed into it for five minutes. Yeah, Andrew sucked.

For single player games, Andrew would expect you to just sit there and watch him play the game for hours. It was never my turn, because it was Andrew's computer. He would actually say "No! It's my computer!" whenever I would ask for a turn. The only thing I could do was find his mom and tell her how Andrew is probably going to become the next Hitler if he doesn't learn how to share. Then she'd tell Andrew how much he sucked and that he should give his friends a turn. Andrew would start making weird pig noises and throwing a fit, but then he'd get sent to his room and his mom would then let me play DOOM for about half an hour before sending me home.

YEAH, ANDREW SUCKED.

Thankfully I have my own computer now, so I can play all the DOOM I want without that little ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Andrew. In conclusion, DOOM is awesome. It makes me feel like a kid again, but this time there are no Andrews.\t\t\t\t\t
