This game runs smoothly with a 980 TI. Plenty of secret areas and easter eggs. Takedowns are definitely inspired by Brutal Doom.

Music and audio design is amazing.

Story wise... Well, it's Doom. You're fighting hell, everything else is fluff.

By today's standards, a top notch game.\t\t\t\t\t
