Meh:

Story ended on a cliffhanger



Overall 8/10

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA very gory yet incredible game! Singleplayer is a non stop fight which requires lots of speed and using your environment to your advantage. It has a very fluid movement system so this is quite easy to master and the levels are large in design and have heaps of hidden rooms and easter eggs that reward you for exploration. Singleplayer can be challenging on the harder difficulty levels and I would suggest trying out the harder difficulties for maximum satisfaction.

Multiplayer I find is an absolute blast. With a huge amount of customization for your character plus a few multiplayer only weapons and great game types, it is an hours killer!! Again like Singleplayer, multiplayer requires lots of speed and environment use. It reminded me a little of the smaller maps in Unreal Tournament games with the pace! If you're quick enough randomly generated power ups give you a great advantage plus DEMON RUNES!! Playing as a Baron Of Hell and perfomring a glory kill on another player is so darn satisfying !!! Overall, I think Doom is a must have for anyone who likes shooters for both the Singleplayer and Multiplayer game types (If you can't find anyone online try the North America Server)\t\t\t\t\t
