\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:

-Feels like I'm playing the original Doom, definitley lives up to the expectations of DOOM fans
-Great Story, slowly gives you a few detail here and a few details there
-Gorgeous graphics
-REALLY well optimized for 1st day of release!
-Tons of graphics settings to fine tune the game for your specific pc
-Gun/Suit upgrade system is a nice touch for a Doom game
-Just enough innovation in this game that it feels refreshing and new, while still sticking to the original Doom's gameplay mechanics of "Run n' Gun."
-Fast Paced


Cons:

-Couldn't record gameplay with hardly any of the most common video software. FRAPS, OBS, Dxtory, none of them worked. Would only record audio, but not video. I had to use MSI afterburner to record.
-Although the graphics are amazing and the visuals look great, when you get close to walls, rocks, outside structures, etc. the textures get fairly blurred and lack detail. The monster model textures are flawless, but the environment textures are pretty disappointing. Don't get me wrong, they look great from far away, it's just I was hoping for DOOM to take the "Best Graphics" crown for 2016. It's not really con, just a silly complaint ;).


Oh, and "Glory Kills," they never get old, they just don't.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAh, Doom!

The first impression is that it is pretty loyal to original one. Even the start reminds a bit how it did start since then 1993.
The difficulty set is done well. Ultra Violence is this time absolutely as it says, totally Ultra Violence. You have to be very aware of what comes on next, as monsters are unpredictable, and sometimes just standing, idling on place will eventually kill you, thanks to A.I given to monsters, standing still is definitely a death.

Atmosphere is what is should be: Mars.... orange, but not too much. It is kind of mixed one with Mars and UAC environment, with nicely balanced details and even cool secrets. It also gives a feeling about this masterpiece is mix up of Classic Doom, Doom 3, and various cool mods, putted them in blender, and having a ready product called Doom 2016.

The upgrade system is also a new feature which is never seen in Doom. Instead of Backpacks, and Soulspheres, It will introduce Argent Energies, which will give possibility to have a choice of three, to upgrade your health, armor, or ammunition, all as capacity upgrades.

The only minus so far, as played three levels is there is no real straferunning. Pity, that was vital thing since then, and somewhat original method compared to other game brands.

Giving honest grade as being hardcore "Doomer" for first impression: 9,6/10

I recommend this for all Doomguys and Doom marines, and specially for those who are not never tried Doom. Here is your chance, go, get it now and start to rip'n tear all your enemies!

Thank You! It is now time to put it down some Hell Knights!

EDIT: I wanted to say a bit more about on first levels, keep an eye of some random events, such as trains. Traps are not only monsters, and there is even more than only map area. Your mission is to find out about what I do mean... :)\t\t\t\t\t
