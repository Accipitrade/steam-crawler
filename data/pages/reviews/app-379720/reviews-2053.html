\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter the initial reaction to the changes this game has over Doom 3, I enjoyed it a lot and have in fact 100%'d the achievements. I would say I liked the scare factor in Doom 3 and missed it in this version but I actually prefer this game. It flows nicely and requires good accuracy without being too punishing if you miss the odd shot. It does however, require a lot of dodging especially in the harder game modes (It is a good thing I like that aspect). I think anyone who liked the original Doom will like this one since it does seem similar play styles.\t\t\t\t\t
