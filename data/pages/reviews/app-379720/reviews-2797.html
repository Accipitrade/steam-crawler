\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM, aka Doom 4, is undeniably a must have game for its single player experience. The combat is beautiful and remeniscent of days far past, with a few modern touchups so that it is an enjoyable experience for everybody. The graphics are also state-of-the-art, which is definitely a selling point. However, the Multiplayer is kind of a mixed bag. While you will undeniably have a few games that feel great, there are also times when it will be the most torturous 10 minutes of your life with some ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ running around that always manages to get the kill. It's also extremely annoying to play against people who have EchXI Lvl50 if your brand new, because they have a leg up on you on everything and will pound you to dust at every corner. If Bethesda would just add a matchmaking system and make the BFG a little bit nonexistent in multiplayer, it would be a much more fair and enjoyable experience.\t\t\t\t\t
