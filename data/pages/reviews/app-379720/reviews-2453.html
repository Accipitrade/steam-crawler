\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom

Doom doom doom.

Let me ask you all a question. Are you a metalhead? Are you generally pissed at the world? Do you often imagine yourself murdering the ♥♥♥♥ out of demons? Well then this game is 110% for you.

This game may be short (not counting arcade, multiplayer, and secrets) but evey demon killing, skull smashing, throat cutting, face melting second is worth the money.

Now if you're into story games this isnt much for you.... Or isss it? There may not be a big story on the surface but through hidden datalogs and secrets you can actually find out there is alot to this game and its past then you'd think (like how the doomslayer is possibly from another dimension, who knows)

So in my opinion 10/10

Soundtrack 10/10
Gameplay 10/10
Story 4/10 until you dig deeper then 8/10 (just a dude ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ pissed off at demons and hell but I still find that badass)
Graphics 10/10
