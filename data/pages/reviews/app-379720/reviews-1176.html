\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is good overall. There is a bit much of platforming. At times you may feel like SuperMario with a gun.
Graphics are nice and gameplay is smooth. You feel strong and like a demon stomping machine since the beginning. Gory kills are really nice.
The only downside is that all monsters move using patterns. Attacks, movements, everyting is a pattern. Absolutely no randomness from monster movements or attacks. Once you learn the patterns, the game becomes much more easy. This happens with all ID games, so it's not a big surprise.

The soundtrack is nice and weapon upgrade system too.
You can finish the game in 20 hours of casual play.

Deffinitely a recommended game if you like Doom and quake series.
