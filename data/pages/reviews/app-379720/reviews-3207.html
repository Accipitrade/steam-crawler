Its been a while since ID brought anything out that ive really liked with virtually no exception. this is that exception.
Did you want to have some massive story arc with mulitple diverse charactors? yeah? well you are not going to be negotiating your way out of this one. grab a shotgun and dont stand still.

The imps are probably the most fun enemies to come up against. agile and surprising.

The only disapointment is at the end, when it finishes...

Well done ID for breaking your own conventions. no carmack didnt add anything new, and they hardly had any of the old team there. in fact this is a first in that it seems to be left up to fans of the game to breath the new life into it and they succeeded.

If you love a no holds barred, blast it out of a shotgun, rip and tear, chaotic descent into hell, you're covered.\t\t\t\t\t
