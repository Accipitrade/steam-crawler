\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, this is by far my favourite game. DOOM has to have the best singleplayer campaign i've ever played, my previous favourite campaign being MW2. There are a ton of collectibles and secrets you can find on every stage, which gives the player incentive to stray of the main path. My favourite secrets are the classic levels. It’s really fun to find the secret lever, so that you can experience some nostalgia. The models are really cool as well. But the best part of the campaign, is that even after finishing with everything found, discovered and unlocked, there’s still more to do. The arcade mode is a fantastic game mode. The relics, medals, point system and the extra life system make it fun to play through the levels over again. The multiplayer is pretty fun to play, with the glory kills and the way demon runes work. It feels really balanced, it’s more a test of accuracy and map knowledge than anything else. The demons don’t feel too broken, and no weapons feel better than others, not including the pistol. I'd recommend this game to anyone who likes action. My favourite part of this game though has to be the soundtrack. Mick Gordon did a fantastic job with it, and it really does feel like the entire development team put their heart and soul into this game. From everything to the soundtrack to the beatiful enviroments and designs, everything feels like it's perfectly constructed and put in the right place. The snapmap system is also extremely good. It's the best map constructing tool i've seen in years. The tutorials and references you get make making maps really easy for everyone. The maps that you find by others are also pretty good, most of them atleast. Playing them with friends is damn fun, and really enjyoable.

Fantastic singleplayer campaign with a lot of replayability, enjoyable multiplayer that's well balanced and fair and a map creating system that is just extraordinary.
I'd give this game a 10/10, which is extremely well deserved. They did a damn good job on this game.\t\t\t\t\t
