\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe graphics are clean, modern and the game runs very smoothly. A single player campaign is a welcome addition. The rock soundtrack is excellent too and much better than the ones in the Quake games IMO.

Unfortunately, the weapons aren't particularly satisfying and the levels and enemies are boring, repetitive and forgettable. Sadly, those are the things that made the original Doom games such classics. id Software forgot that when they made Doom 3 and unfortunately they've made most of the same mistakes again with Doom 4. Once again the levels are too dark in a lazy or misguided attempt to create atmosphere. Maybe the enemies are detailed and nicely modeled but it's impossible to tell because the levels are so dark with crazy sparks and flashing lights and the enemies zip around so fast that they might as well be just blobs of pixels. Everybody remembers the (well lit) levels, enemies and weapons from Doom 1&2 but that won't be the case with Doom 4.

The gorey "glory" kills have me in two minds. Part of me thinks they're a very cool addition that's never been seen before and part of me thinks they're a silly gimmick that borders on torture porn much like Mortal Kombat X.

Multiplayer is OK but is unlikely to become a staple.

I can't recommend Doom unless you're desperate for a modern, hectic FPS that you play through once and then delete.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGot DOOM for $30 on a Steam sale. If you haven't bought this yet, do it. Game runs like butter and has incredibly impressive visuals. And HOLY COW the soundtrack. Fighting demons to the soundtrack has been a blast. The glory kills were always satisfying and made me feel like a badass and constantly fighting and jumping from demon to demon.

Does get a little grindy towards the end, but it's DOOM - you know that. The boss battles kept things fresh and were actually somewhat difficult. There's a story in there, albeit a little shallow, but the combat makes up for it by far. The MP is interesting and I've read the reviews of the MP. It's still enjoyable in my opinion and good fun to just boot up and play for a little.\t\t\t\t\t
