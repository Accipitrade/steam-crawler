\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI didn't play this until 5 years after release but I'm glad I eventually came back to it. I had been seeing how well Doom: Eternal was doing and figured I should complete this one first. I very much enjoyed the 9.4 hours it took me to complete the story mode.

They added some modern stuff like weapon mods and ways to improve your character but they didn't over-do it. Somewhat scarce ammo forced me to become proficient in every weapon and reminded me to use the chainsaw which gives bonus ammo when used. Clever level design encourages running & gunning and well placed jump pads have you flying and gunning as well, combined with the finisher system it was just all around a really satisfying combat loop.

While the game might not be quite as scary, the evil vibes are there and its still bloody and dark. Top notch graphics, art design, sound design, no complaints on that side of things.

Having purchased the game for 50% off I didn't mind that it was over in 9.4 hours, it felt like a long game and that was just one play through of the story, I'm sure I'll eventually go back and play it again on a harder mode.

I would highly recommend checking this game out if you are considering it, especially if you are fan of the old Doom & Quake games from the 90's, this one really brought back some nostalgic vibes from the era while also feeling like a modern top quality shooter.\t\t\t\t\t
