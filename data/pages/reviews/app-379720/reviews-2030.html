\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite my attempts to remain stoic, I'm now a RIP AND TEAR fanboy. Wow, what a blast to play. While not the physics masterpiece of the original Halo, they share an emphasis on freedom of movement, fluidity, effortless experimentation, and plenty of things to smash and blow up. It even has a decent story!

GOOD
+ Fun: Run'n'gun fun! Easy to pick up, difficult to master. Light enough to get into quickly, substantial enough to provide plenty of depth and experimentation.
+ Story: It's a good one. There isn't a lot of it, but what's there is intriguing and well told. Hayden is particularly interesting. The Doom Marine's infinite hatred and disdain is SHOWN, not told.
+ Sound: Incredible sound design -- hope you've got bass!
+ Voice Actors: There are only two characters who really speak, but they're both interesting and superbly voice acted.
+ Length: Probably perfect. It doesn't outstay its welcome nor does it leave one unsatisfied.
+ Replayability: If you're really hungry for more at game's end, the arcade mode is a BLAST (often literally). The community maps/games/challenges can also be interesting to explore (although they didn't do it for me). Also, you can gear up your weapons, and while you'll get most unlocks with a thorough playthrough, you'd want to go through again or do arcade mode to try them all.
+ Weapons: They are beautifully animated, acoustically perfected, and SO satisfying to use. The game won't waste time giving you big, awesome ones very early. Gearing them up according to one's own playstyle/preference is also
+ Art: Frankly, many of my screenshots in this game could be paintings. The lighting and landscapes are lovely, but even the interiors are gorgeous and interesting (assuming you've got the graphics power). The armour, power ups, character design, etc., all solid! There are also little bits of flavour here and there on computer terminals, in detailed environments, and so on that all add a lot of character.
+ Exploration: NAILED IT. It's not a huge open world, but exploration is not just rewarding -- it's actually FUN to do. It doesn't feel like a chore. I typically skip additional content, but I couldn't help seeking it out this time.

BAD
- I wonder if it was a bit poorly optimised. Seems overly demanding, graphics-wise.

NEUTRAL
= The player-made content seemed uninteresting to me, but that's not why I got the game.\t\t\t\t\t
