\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all, wow. This is the kind of reboot all the others should've shot for.

For those who don't know (by the way, what kind of rock have YOU been living in?), DOOM (2016) is a reboot of the original 1993 Doom by id Software. Back then, the game pioneered 3D graphics and the ever-popular first person shooter genre. The reboot sucessfully brings back the running, jumping, circle-strafing fun of the original in the shiny new id Tech 6 engine with graphics so real you'll swear the bullets are passing right by your face!

The game starts on a odd note as you awaken shooting zombies, but after the intro sequence, the game remains on a level that will you thinking " What crazy thing will the Doom Marine do next? "

Long story short, it's a proper revival of the series, stop reading and Fight Like Hell!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ THIS GAME

I loved the original Doom series and I even liked Doom 3. So, when I heard this thing was released I kept an eye on it and picked it up during the Steam Summer Sale.

This game is intense and fun as all hell. It looks gorgeous and has the soul of the original Doom series. The music sounds like something Trent Reznor wrote (most of the time) and I often found myself exhaling after a large battle.

This game doesn't try and be realistic AND THAT'S FINE. It's refreshing to play a game that doesn't take itself too seriously and is just meant to be enjoyed.

Play this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have fond memories of the original DOOM games from my old 486 when I was a kid (a DX2/66 no less!) and I've always been a bit hesitent to see re-makes/ sequals of legendary we love; we've all been let down (Duke Nukem, we're all thinking it!).

Firstly it's every bit as I remember DOOM, all about twitch and speed but sadly at the age of 36 I'm not as fast as I was, and even though I managed to complete the originals as a teenager in Ultra-Violence (Nightmare was a bit much for me then); I stepped down to Hurt Me Plenty and found more than enough challenge.

Honestly game is visceral, fast and more importantly FUN! Even though I died... a lot, I still never found myself getting frustrated which I know is hard balance to get.

The nods to the original game through secret challenges I found fun, and I love the idea of upgrading your armour and guns. The upgrade mechism is simple and that I feel is a good thing, if you want an RPG then you will disappointed with this but if you loved games like Serious Sam and the original DOOM series, you will be in hog heaven!.

tl;dr

I'm an old fart who played the originals and loved them, I love this as well... go buy it.\t\t\t\t\t
