\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe first thing I noticed about Doom was the loose controls with really high sensitivity. I thought that something was wrong but then I realised that this game is different, this game is Doom.

Id Software have a list of seminal titles and this game has maintained its unique identity among the rest of their library. As the gameplay is frenetic and fluid just like it was in the classic Doom.

It’s great fun when you get used to it and the new design elements in this modern version compliment this fast paced style of gameplay.

This game will put you into a flow state where you are totally engrossed in what you are doing and it feels great. When the action heats up and the heavy guitar music kicks in it feels fantastic.

The boss battles are very well balanced and fair too, I never felt cheated or overly frustrated because these encounters are so well designed.

In fact the design in general is on a genius level in Doom, this game is hardcore and extremely exhilarating to play through.

I did try the Multiplayer briefly too and it seems like a mix between modern Call of Duty multiplayer with oldschool gameplay elements similar to Quake 3 Arena.

A lot of the game modes had no players but I could find a few people still playing Team Deathmatch. Everything seemed to work fine and I’d say it could be a good option if you are looking for an online shooter to get into.

For me though the main aspect here is the campaign and it is fantastic. I think the developers did a great job here and every gamer should give it a try.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe most kickass, over the top, awesome FPS I have ever played. Incredibly visceral action, complete with a large arsenal of weapons and the glory kill system, which allows you to rip and tear through all opposition.

Graphically, the game looks gorgeous. The environments are mostly desolate hallways or large swaths of Martian wasteland, but there are some truly epic backdrops to the action. All of these environments are full of secrets and collectibles, and there is a real sense of accomplishment to finding a secret weapon or armor. The demons which you fight are also incredibly detailed and some of them are truly terrifying to look at, such as the awesome Cyberdemon.

The game doesn't fall behind in the sound department either. The soundtrack gets you pumped up and really conveys how furious the game protagonist is at the demons. The guns mostly sound great as well, although some of them lack a punch that would make them feel even better.

If you haven't done so yet, play this game!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBetter than DOOM Eternal, without the platforming ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, the silly multicoloured pickups you get from chainsaw-whoring the enemies, and other crap that has been added to DOOM in DOOM Eternal.

This is real, brutal, visceral, incredibly fun DOOM. Not the compromised sequel that is DOOM Eternal.

Original review after years of playing it is below:

Incredible FPS gaming experience. Great visuals, smooth gameplay and a semblance of a story (someone here described it as the result of the worst business plan ever - channelling Hell's energy for humanity's good). Amazing things about this game - glory kills, health system, badass enemies, action. Not so great things - a few bugs here and there, slightly repetitive gameplay, easy enough to finish some levels even on nightmare. But excellent, heart-thumping fun and plenty of wow moments in the gameplay.

Parting remark: I wish they do a Quake single player reboot. It is bound to rock with idtech 7/8/whatever they have now.

11/10 Highly recommended\t\t\t\t\t
