\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM has got to be the only game that, upon completion, I actually deleted my file and started a new game on a higher difficulty and began playing again. This is unprecedented. This phenomenon represents the positive and negative aspects of the game. The only negative being THERE ISN'T ENOUGH! Hah! If you play through DOOM you will get good at the game. You have to. For some this will happen faster, and if this game happens to be up your alley, you will want more.

The story of the campaign is rather cryptic, but there are collectibles on every level that can fill in some of the details. I found the campaign to be shorter than I wanted, but that may be due to the fact that finishing a game is a rare occurrence for me.

DOOM is one of the best shooters I've ever played. It's fast, relentless, and badass. And the music UGH, so good! So, get out there and RIP AND TEAR UNTIL IT IS DONE!\t\t\t\t\t
