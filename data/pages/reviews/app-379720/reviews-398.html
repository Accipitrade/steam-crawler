\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe greatest Christian game ever made.

The Positives:
+ Godlike combat system: Everything related to the combat system is insanely good and addicting. Gunplay is fast-paced and engaging, Glory Kill is satisfied to do, and the chainsaw is f*cking badass. All of this (and more) makes the combat one of the best that I've ever experienced.
+ Godlike soundtracks: They couldn't have picked a better person to make soundtracks for this game. Mike Gordon is a f*cking genius when it comes to making great music.
+ Great variety of enemies: Lots of Demons to kill, and their AI is no joke either. They can be very dangerous in numbers, and requires you to move at all time if you want to survive. In addition to that, boss fights are epic and challenging, and it feels so good to finally bring them down.
+ Interesting side activities: They're not only there to further enrich the main game, but to also help you get better at a certain aspect of the gameplay (Finding Collectibles to greater understand the map, beating trial to concentrate more on a single gun,...). They're just as fun and engaging as the main game in my opinion.

The Negatives:
- Achievement glitch: "The Circle is Complete" can be completed by earning all Runes from beating every trials in the main story, but if you beat the rune trials outside of the main campaign and earn the Runes that way, the achievement can't be unlocked anymore, even if you try to replay the trial inside of that level. You have to create a new save and redo the whole thing for that achievement to be unlocked, which is a bit annoying. So just don't do the trials in the "Rune Trial Select" option, do them in the campaign instead.
- Some hidden Collectibles are "too" hidden: Sounds like a dumb critique I know, but since 95% of the hidden collectibles can be found if you pay enough attention and use some common sense, the last 5% of them are impossible to find without searching for their location on the internet, which is something I don't like doing, since it feels like cheating to me. It's obviously not a big deal or anything, but it's there, and it did annoy me quite a bit.
- Some maps are quite large and can get you lost
- The ending is a bit unsatisfied

Final Verdict: The best FPS Shooter I've played. Definitely play this game if you like the fast-paced gunplay and the nonstop Demon killing (or Mortally Challenged for a less offensive term). 9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
