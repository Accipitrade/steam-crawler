\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a single-player campaign-only player, I definitely got my money's worth playing this game. It was an exhilerating journey of hyperviolence. You run, you shoot, you jump, you flip through the air to land a double blast from your super-shotgun on a demon and finish it with a decapitating kick. Great fun.

Even the exploration system was pretty spot on, usually I'dd go for the walkthough to blitz this stuff ASAP but DOOM makes the exploration straightforward enough that I was happy to just blow through, find stuff naturally, and let missed secrets lie unfound.

My only gripe was the lack of "endless" battles. There was only one battle that I can remember which had unlimited enemy spawns until you finished the objective and this battle was a breath of fresh air. So, more of that please? Also there were a many many empty corridors and arenas which would have been so much better with just a light sprinkling of isolated enviromental mobbs.\t\t\t\t\t
