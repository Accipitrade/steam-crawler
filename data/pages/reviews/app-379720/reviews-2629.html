\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a wild ride. A fast moving, intense, and really fun one too.

I believe I got this game in a humble bundle. I was ready to go shoot some demons, but after 15 minutes I was ready to marathon the game to completion. I didn't do that, work, and other real life concerns objected.

My favorite part of this game is the level design. It's sounds cliche and buzzwordy, but it's more than a hallway. It's not an open world shooter, it's definitely a rails-based game most of the time, but it's got more than a 2d hallway. You can go up, around, do a few objectives in different order. There's tons to explore. It even gives you a checklist so you can see if you've found everything (some might say that's casual, but I like it. I like checklists.).

The game looks amazing too, and runs incredibly well on my system. gtx970, i73820 (3rd gen i7 still going strong!)

100% recommend on sale, recommend heartily if you've got the $30 to pay out for it now. I would probably buy it for $30 if I was in a good spot in life.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, Doom, back at it again with a great remake of the old classic!

Let me start off by saying that this game while fun as crap to play, it is very short. Being that it is 11/2017 and just now purchasing the game here is what I have found.

1. Like stated above, it is short. Though I could have stretched the Campain out some more and searched hard for every Secret and completed every objective, it only took 12 hours to complete the Campain Mode while collecting roughly half of all objectives in the game.

2. Multiplayer seems to have already died off in this game. While attempting to join several multiplayer matches, there was an average wait time of 5 minutes or more to find just 4 players needed to start a match. Then once in game 1-2 players would leave after being killed a few times. There are Multiplayer mates for both new players and veteran players alike.

3. The graphics in the game are amazing, Running the game on Max/Ultra settings I have no problems running 60fps (Having it capped usign Nvidia Inspector), never once did it drop below the 60 FPS mark. No issues with crashes except once where I received an error that resulted in me having to underclock my GPU by 50mhz, but no biggie.
*Running Ryzen 5 1600 and EVGA GeForce GTX 1070 SC

4. I would reccommend the game to anyone who loves just making demons explode! There are many great weapons to collect and use all with addition "mods" that utilize the Right mouse button.In order to obtain the mods for each weapon you have to find them in the game, and while you may miss 1 or 2 of the little machines serving up the mods I had no issues unlocking all the mods for each weapon. Each weapon has 2 mods that are easily switched using the "R" key, each having its own use in situations.

Give it a shot if you please, sure did help me kill a few hours and enjoyed every hour of it.\t\t\t\t\t
