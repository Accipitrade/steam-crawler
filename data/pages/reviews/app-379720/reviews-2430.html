\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has become one of my favorites over my time playing it. Doom is very similar to my all time favorite game Duke Nukem Forever in many aspects. The fast paced gunplay in both games tickle my willy and honestly gives me a reason to keep living. I believe the only reason this game turned out the way it did because the fellas at ID saw the masterclass in gaming that was Duke Nukem Forever. You can see where this game took some ideas from the Duke Dome such as Possesed Soldiers essentially being the Pig Cops from Duke Nukem Forever and the Cyber Demon being a carbon copy of the Cycloid Emperor fight from Duke Nukem Forever. I feel as if the only place Doom faulters when compared to Duke Nukem Forever is the locations the game takes place in. In Doom, you have a Mars base and Hell itself to gut demons in. In Duke Nukem Forever, you have Dukes giantr tower, LA, the Duke Dome, a desert, and the dam all while having interesting puzzles and platforming elements that adds to Dukes uniqueness. Otherwise, Dooms levels are all very well made and fun to play in if not a little baron sometimes when compared to the lore filled areas of Duke Nukem Forever. The story in doom also faulters when compared to Duke Nukem Forever. In Doom, the Doomslayer wakes up mad at demons and commits a genocide. Duke Nukem Forevers story is much more in depth. Dukes story is that of a redemption story for a man who hasn't had an alien bastard to kill in a while. Duke Nukem Forever also excels at enviromental storytelling. For instance, in Duke Burger, you can see what kind of star Duke became after maiming the aliens the first time around but the damage done to Duke Burger shows you Dukes increasing irrelevency. Doom on the otherhand has dead bodies showing "demons be bad" and just serve to fill the enviroment so it isn't too baron. Overall, I think Doom is a very well done game but faulters slightly when put against Duke Nukem Forever. Without a shadow of a doubt I can reccommend this game to anyone, but i must suggest you play Duke Nukem Forever first if not done already.\t\t\t\t\t
