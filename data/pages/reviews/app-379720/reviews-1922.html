\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinally completed an ultra-nightmare run and just had to write a review. This game is brutal, in every sense of the word. Punishing difficulty, relentless SWARMS of enemies, cool music, incredible graphics fun and interesting weapons (though I will say I feel like a couple of them should have been replaced with other ideas as they are totally overshadowed by their counter-parts. Precision bolt Vs. Siege mode or Mobile turret Vs. Gatling rotator for example. Can't even justify them as a difference in style like the combat shotgun's Explosive shot Vs. Burst shot because they do essentially the same thing just less good. xD). Throw in a beautifully detailed environment and an interesting modern re-imagining of a classic and it's damn near perfect. I wish I could see the number of Ultra attempts I made and failed at. Hundreds, I believe. Each time I failed though, regardless of my frustration or anger, I'd boot that ♥♥♥♥ right back up and start slogging through again and again and again. Because I enjoyed the journey. I had FUN every single one of the hundreds of time I ran through the familiar levels. Each battle was exhilarating no matter how many times I had done it before. Impending perma-death definitely contributed to that exhilaration. And that is one of the things I like most about this game. It inspires MASTERY. But it's not just a mundane chore. It's a goal to strive for. Frankly, I was a lot less excited about finishing it than I thought I would be. Because it's over now. Kinda like finishing a book you really love. Bittersweet.

I'm not a pro-gamer or a streamer or anything like that. Hell, I just finally got the hang of weapon switching (after reconfiguring my weapon hotkeys, shoulda done what waaaaaay earlier) on my long-awaited victorious run. I just played the ♥♥♥♥ out of a game I really love until I cleared the final absurdly high hurdle. This game was clearly a labor of love by an extremely talented team and I'll always be interested to see what they come up with from now on.

Well, this was less of a proper review and more of a me gushing about how awesome I think this game is and bragging about the 646 hours I've arguably wasted on it. xD Whatevs, I regret nothing. Time to get slayer rating on all of Arcade mode and see what multiplayer is like.\t\t\t\t\t
