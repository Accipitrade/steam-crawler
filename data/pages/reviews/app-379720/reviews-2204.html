\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: Buy this game, full price or on sale. It's wonderful.

Probably one of the best games of 2016. Bought it on PS4, bought again on PC, and it plays way better here.

Game play: 9/10

Sometimes, melee will cause you to teleport behind an enemy but I believe it's something to do with using Vulkan vs Open GL. When I swapped back to Open GL the teleportation was far less than what it was. Showing feels great, and so does the melee kills.

Soundtrack: 10/10
I've listened to the games soundtrack on PS4 and it's even better playing on PC (mostly due to better audio on my PC than on my PlayStation).

Graphics: 9/10

I haven't gotten far enough into the game to really say much about graphics as only into the second level. However, from what I have seen on YouTube, plus my experience on PS4, this game is still very good looking.

Mechanics: 7/10

Listen, I know this isn't a very high score, however the game itself is super simple. You shoot stuff, you melee them to break their neck/jaw etc. and that doesn't get too old. However, there isn't much more depth to that. Even with the power ups that you can get, and some of the weapons, the mechanics are fairly simple, which isn't bad. IT doesn't take away from the fact that breaking a demons jaw is wonderful, it just means that I wish there was a little bit more to it.

Overall: 9/10

This is probably one of the best games that you can buy, if you're killing time, or want to go on a massive killing spree against some demons. The soundtrack is amazing, gun play is great, and the only thing keeping this from getting a 10/10 from me is the simplified mechanics (though great, just too simple for me).

I highly recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
