\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is the bomb.

I'm not a serious FPS enthusiast or anything. I think the last FPS I completed was Half-Life 2, but I'm old enough to remember when Wolfenstein 3D and the original Doom came out, and how exciting they were at the time.

I feel like DOOM finally kinda nails the genre.

The game feels like a bunch of mad scientists sat down to genetically engineer the ideal balls-out shooter. Movement is fluid, and fast. Precision is overrated. Combat keeps you going (it's almost always the best strategy, due to the speed with which you'll get pinned or whittled to nothing by ranged attacks if you stand still) and allows for a huge range of tactics with a satisfyingly diverse arsenal. The "glory kill" and chainsaw mechanics also make getting in close a priority, and if you use them both as needed health and ammo drop fast enough that you can just relax back into your role as ultimate killing machine, instead of some bean counter nervously watching your status.

As an FPS dilettante, I played on the Normal difficulty ("Hurt Me Plenty") and had a challenging time with some fights, and fun with the easier encounters. Having beaten the game, I'm heading back in to try it on Hard. I'm not sure I've ever enjoyed the gameplay in an FPS enough to do that before. Awesome game, beautiful graphics, would journey to hell to undo the legacy of man's hubris again.

(The only real con I can think of is that the boss fights are all pretty lame, but I think that's a structural issue with FPS games more than an indictment of this one.)\t\t\t\t\t
