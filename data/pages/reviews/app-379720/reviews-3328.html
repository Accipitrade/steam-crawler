\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is totally worth the purchase: it's beautiful and the FPS gameplay is the definition of "solid". Combat isn't "floaty" or accuracy-based; the game's gonna test your twitch-iness. You're meant to thin out the crowds of enemies as fast as you can and also switch weapons on the fly. Basically, if you prefer the tactical, cover-hugging, slow-burn feel of the modern FPS you'll want to avoid DOOM.

I can't speak for the lower difficulties, but the gameplay on Ultra-Violence is pretty fun; the simpler enemies like Imps aren't bullet sponges and the bigger guys can be brought down with a combo of alternate-fire upgrades and good shooting.

I have very minor gripes...I think the opening moments of Doom 3 were better than this game's, but I'm a biased survival horror junkie. I also miss being able to chainsaw and strafe enemies to death; it's not entirely feasible to chainsaw all the time in DOOM.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh Doom how i love thee. This game man, this ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ game.

Lets just say that you played the multiplayer player beta like me and your hopes for this game were shattered. It was bland boring and uninspiring, oh wait bethesda outsourced the multiplayer to another company that wasnt id software, makes since now. Trust me i got my fill of the multiplayer and wont be visiting it again except for snapmap.

This game is everything i wanted it to be. The fluid fast combat, the guns feeling great and beefy except for the pistol which is fine. The glory kill system is a welcome new comer, it doesnt break up the flow of combat at all and is needed if you need health in a pinch. You have to be aggressive and fast when fighting the hordes of hell cause they will kill you quickly and with efficency.

Oh and the music is some the dirtiest, grindiest, and heaviest metal/electronic music Ive ever heard. If any of this sounds awesome to you then what are you waiting for? Dive into hell Supershotgun barrels first and blast your way through some demons.

By the way the demons, oh my god are they awesome. The variety alone is huge and they come at you in massive hordes. My favorite is the Baron of Hell XD. id Software did a great job going back to what made Doom revolusionary, and they did an amazing job. Now granted it's not perfect, no games is. There were a few times that I felt the pacing could have been handled better but not a real gripe. There is dialogue and alittle story sprinkled on top of this gory and very fast paced game but it takes a big back seat to the gameplay. Take Wolfenstien the New Order turn that dial up to 666/10 because your in for a wild ride.

9.5/10
One of the most refershing FPS games Ive played in along time.\t\t\t\t\t
