\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a good game, looks and feels great, and it doesn't try to be anything more than it is. The story is non existent (thankfully!), the mission is simple. The DOOM Marine has a great personality that comes through very clearly in just his actions. The Rune Challenges makes for nice breaks and helps the game from getting too monotomous.

The negatives Imho are:
* Bad music.
* Repetetive gameplay for the lenght of the game (it could be either a shorter game, about half, or more variations in map designs).
* Compaired to the classic DOOM and DOOM 2: Hell on Earth, both the maps and the enemy types were much more distinct and varied in the aforementioned. I couldn't tell many of the enemies apart in this one. Most of them looked like brown pieces of p u u, just of different sizes. I think they could have used more colors and been more bold with shapes to make them stand out from one another.
* The game starting with adverts and "disclosure agreement".
* It would feel more satisfying if the enemies didn't feel like they were all made of jello.
* Too many different types of upgrades to keep track of. You might end up finishing the game without upgrading quite alot because those info boxes pop up mid gameplay and you forget them.
* Rather busy menus.
* 2 weapons are not designated to the weapons wheel (the chainsaw & BFG). I guess because it should feel more like an event to use them by pressing their own designated buttons, but you may very well forget you have them, and just not use them basically at all, which is a shame, because the chainsaw is AMAZING! I never remember if I have fuel for the chainsaw or not and so I don't use it half as much as I would probably be able, and would like to! I'd need to have someone sit beside me and go "Don't forget the chainsaw!"
* Please, pleeease, put the guns names somewhere. I find it very difficult to recognice the muzzle in game FP-view to the menu descriptions, especially with such hectic gameplay going on. Cycling through my whole arsenal (that all looks like LEGO) several times, trying to make out which gun was the one with a scope, all while dodging demons left and right.

Other than that, great fun!\t\t\t\t\t
