\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNever played any of the DOOMs before and this one game highly (and repeatedly) recommended to me from friends. Picked it up on sale thinking it was just an hour or two but it turned out to be ~10 hours or single player campaign so was worth the purchase right there.

The game starts a bit slow and I usually get bored in FPS campaigns and I was already dreading it when I loaded in (speaking of, the loading times are gnaaaaaarly) and had to "wait" to get up, walk to activate a short scene, walk to door, get equipment, put equipment on watch ANOTHER longer sce-Wait, what? He smashed the monitor and ends the scene? :eyebrow raises: ok, you have my attention!. But then it is kind of slow until you get to Hell and the combat picks up and becomes far more fun. I want a demon puppy from the game even if he noms my face and craps my soul into hell; I wonder if there is a plushie...

There are a few bugs I've encountered (ie clipping; getting stuck in the floor, etc) but nothing really awful. What really makes this game is the freaking soundtrack; it's so freaking good! I'm definitely going to be picking it up after I leave this review. I found myself just going through the motions of "kill demon, dodge fireballs, run to another demon, kill, repeat"... and then the music kicks in and "OOOOOH, SH!!!!T! LET'S DO THIS; IT'S HAM TIME" mode gets activated. It fits so well and the glory kills actually "modify" the music pretty seamlessly and adds to the mayhem. SO good! This game is worth it on sale JUST for the music alone, not even joking.

If you're looking for a chaotic, run-and-gun mayhem-fest of killing demons game DOOM 2016 may scratch that itch. The story is a little predictable and bland that's not what we're here for.

If you wanna see livestream gameplay of the game you can watch it on my YouTube Channel. Enjoy!\t\t\t\t\t
