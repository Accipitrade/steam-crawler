\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, this review is from someone (aka me) who has not beaten the game yet. Just keep that in mind.

So, Doom. It is a classic first person shooter franchise, one that influenced so many other titles in the genre. Well how does this new-ish installment hold up to the original games that made Doom a big name? The answer is clear, it holds up very very well.

This game is absolutely incredible! It is the best FPS I have ever played! (granted I am, for the most part, not a first, or even third person shooter guy)

What I like: Almost everything. For example I love the combat, the map design, the weapons and their mods, the enemies, the secrets, etc.

What I dislike: The crazy long levels (but I am a noob, so this is mostly because I get lost sometimes. It's not a problem with the game itself, I just dislike it personally.)

Bottom line, buy this game. It's amazing.\t\t\t\t\t
