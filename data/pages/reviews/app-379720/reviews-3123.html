Intro
As a fan of the classic doom series and a fan of killing demons i was hoping this game would do the series some justice and make a game worthy of continuing or revivng the series. I was worried that this Doom would plunge ID software and this series down to the deepest part of hell, instead they fought back and came back from Hell to bring back a game that rivals the classics themselves.

Single Player
DOOM is a fantastic FPS that continues and builds off of the classic doom series from the story, gameplay, environment, and soundtrack. Taking on the character of DOOM guy in DOOM is so satisfying compared to Doom 3's DOOM guy, instead of being a lone survivor Doom 3 made him out to be DOOM guy in DOOM is an all powerful badass who destoys everything and dosen't give a ♥♥♥♥! (Tell me that one dosen't give you a bonner). DOOM's story is simplistic and satisfying like the classics with DOOM guy killing demons so he can push them back to hell and kill them there too. DOOM's gameplay returns to its roots with gameplay simular to the classic doom series but builds an even more fun experience with arena like areas, powerups giving DOOM guy the ability to kill everything even with his bare hands, finding secret areas including rooms with the classic doom levels, collectable DOOM guy bobble heads for unlocking artwork, easter eggs from their previous games like Doom 3 and Commander Keem, and weapon challenges that aren't tedious and can be achieved with multiple playthroughs. The atmoshpere in DOOM is spot on with levels showing signs of destruction, decay, and death. Being able to traverse up huge generator rooms, structures, and huge areas adds more depth to the levels. This is a welcoming addition to the DOOM series and one i hope they continue implimenting. Hell looks good this time around with death painted walls, ancient forbidden tunnels/structures, the Hell doors from the classics ♥, and demonic figures/ remains. Going to hell is fun and exciting (Dear god whats wrong with me 0_o') fighting off hoards of demons, hell spawns, and Bosses; two of them throw backs to the originals. The music is great and badass making players adrenaline rush to the awsome heavy metal of death! Mixing this soundtrack with the heavy encounters players face off with makes the experience all the better. A word of advise don't head band to the music if you are liabe to hitting your head on something, i leanred this the hard way Q_Q.

Multiplayer/ Snap Map
The Multiplayer and Snap Map were poorly made and is a cheap way to add more content and gameplay to the game. ID software did not develope them and handed off to another company instead. Multiplayer is crap and reminds me of the bioshock 2 multiplayer with the overpowered demon pick up being simular to the Big Daddy suit pick up. The power up would mostly one shot people and make for an unfair advantage for people who would stratigieze on abusing it. The Season Pass itself is only for the Multiplayer DLC which saddens me as i was hoping they would possilby put more towards the single player. Snap Map would have been awsome if it weren't so poorly done as it can be buggy and very limited to ones imagination. These two modes feel rushed and half baked to something that could have been great in adding longevity to the game. I honeestly see these two modes better off without being in the game as the single player of DOOM in itself makes for a great game.

Verdict
DOOM is a wonderful game that brings nostalgia to fans of the old classic doom series. This game to me was like porn that I enjoyed countless times and would do all over again (I need help). I hope to see more games like this from ID software because they did a fantastic job with the Singleplayer Campaign. I highly recommend this game for its singleplayer experience but don't recommend it for its Multiplayer and Snap map Mode.\t\t\t\t\t

Hi all. Started with PONG in 1972 or so. Haven't stopped playing games since. Truly a must for the lifelong gamer. Some cut scenes a bit long, but I'm old and impatient. Phenomenal game on a great system (just got a new computer!). Outstanding blend of tactics, bunnyhopping (arrrrggggh! Years of dreaming that 3D graphics would exist and VR was coming are finally here. This is that realization of my dream---how good gaming can be.
