The atmosphere (music, weapons, gore, environments) is spot on!
Multiplayer is fun to play with friends.

Graphics : 9/10
Environments are designed very well. Non linear map design with loads of collectibles.
You really get the feeling that you are in hell or on mars.

Optimization : 8/10
Good optimization, 120 fps on 1440p-medium on a gtx 980 (overclocked).

Story : 8/10
Main story line is awesome. Just enough cutscenes to tell the story. The rest is just pure action and destroying demons.

Length : 6/10
20 hours on medium. I did play it again for the collectibles and achievements.

Replayablity : 10/10
I have finished the game 4 times now. Enough said.\t\t\t\t\t
