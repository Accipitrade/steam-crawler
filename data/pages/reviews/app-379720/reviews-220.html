\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is by far one of the best modern FPS titles in recent years and a phenomenal return for one of the original classics. Everything about the game is designed for maximum, cathartic carnage. The game goes a long way to making you feel unstoppable from the powerful weapon loadout to Doomguy's penchant for ripping and tearing his enemies in the most gruesome fashion.

It also doesn't hurt that the campaign is the central and most effective part of the package. The length is middle-of-the-road but the levels feature a ton of hidden secrets, power-ups, upgrades and other collectables to find. Multiplayer is less graceful but still worth checking out. If you're fan of shooters, Doom 2016 is an absolute must-own.\t\t\t\t\t
