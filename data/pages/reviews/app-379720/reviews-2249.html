\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom (2016) is totally awesome. It is a great, super re-boot to the original game (some say it may be more of a sequel to the original), with improvements in every way, keeping the fun, run-and-gun style gameplay, with even better background music that fits the game, and seems to know exactly how to sound depending on the action and whatever is happening at the time. The game is so colorful and beautiful, makes you wonder if hell is really that pretty, LOL. Kidding of course, about hell being nice, but the graphics are so beautiful.
Doom ran so smoothly for me with my details all set to ultra, and never did I experience even one glitch during the whole play through the game. Also, it had a really good story line to go with it as an added bonus, extras also like the rune challenges, and secrets, just to add some extra goodness. Love the secret old style Doom maps for the nostalgia, as a secret in each level, and the codex gave detailed info about everything as you go through the game.
I am not a heavy metal fan, but it totally works here in this Doom, and not only fitting to the game, but actually really good.
The weapons were all very nicely detailed, fun to use, with even more fun mods you can add to each weapon that you can earn throughout the game. Loved the 'glory kill' melees, nothing more fun that ripping out a demon's innards, and stuffing them down his throat, heh heh, or jumping on one from above, and smashing his head into the ground with your foot. :)
Really got this 62 year old fart's heart pumping, Ha! Ha!
Can't wait to play Doom Eternal. Would love to see a reboot like this of Quake II, made in the same way.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's as good as everyone says it is. Was skeptical when it first came out because it just looked like it was trying to be edgy and didn't really look like it'd have a lasting appeal. I was wrong. It's actually damn refreshing. Ironically, i found that the edge the game is soaked in is it's best feature, among the other good things about it. Solid sense of speed, good accurate shooting, decent length, pretty awesome soundtrack, etc. Not that this is difficult to do with the technology and innovations we've had since the 90's, but this easily craps all over the original games (And i've loved Doom since i was young). Anyone who says those games are definitively superior are blinded by nostalgia. The reason i say this is because this game has essentially the same exact gameplay loop as those games, so it's much easier to compare them. Outside of the obvious "modern game-isms" like QTE's, upgrades, etc. this game just feels and plays so much better than those games. Again, this came out in 2016. I understand it's a bit overkill to compare this to a game that came out decades ago. But i've seen YouTube videos where the original Doom is posted as the best in the series, only by virtue of it being the first game in the series. And that's a load of BULL. This game is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awesome. And i feel like it's what a lot of people envisioned when they played the originals back in the 90's. I never saw those games that way, but i know tons of older people did. Doom 2016 definitely became one of my favorite shooters of all time. Not for it's complexity, but because of how cool it was.\t\t\t\t\t
