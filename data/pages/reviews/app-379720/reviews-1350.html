\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've never been a HUGE fan of FPS games, nor have I ever felt good at them, but DOOM changed that for me, at least a little. I'm not going to compare this to it's 1993 counterpart, as I feel the two are very different, and this game should be reviewed without it's origins in mind, it would be unfair to compare the two, especially given how different their engines are.

I am not a badass, I have never been one, but ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, this game makes me feel like one.

I had a blast (pun intended) with DOOM. It is absolutely phenomenal, with great, fast-paced gameplay, an astonishing soundtrack, and an engaging narrative. There was never a moment where I wasn't having fun, and there were only a few instances where I was met with an unfair death.
I do plan on re-visiting this game semi-frequently, whether it be to tackle the harder difficulties, or to go for the secrets I missed.

No matter who you are, fan of FPS games or not, you should definitely DOOM a shot, it's non-stop fun from start to finish.\t\t\t\t\t
