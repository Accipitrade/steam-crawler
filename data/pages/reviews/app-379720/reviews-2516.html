\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat a triumphant return to classc 90's FPS gameplay! As someone who grew up on the likes of Doom, Quake and Duke Nukem 3D, this is everything I look for in an FPS that ticks all my boxes and I've not had this much fun in YEARS. None of this CoD bull***t, no slow movement, no reloading, no chest high walls and regenerating health. Just fast paced, dynamic and extremely satisfying gameplay that makes you feel like a total demon slaying badass.

The game rewards you for being aggressive with its glory kill system. Low on health? Go smash a demons face in with your bear hands and you can get more health! And overall it balances perfectly between exploration and combat, you are highly incentivised to explore and it never gets old for me.

I can't speak to the multiplayer as I've not played it not have much interest in it but otherwise I very highly recommend this game. If you are my age and grew up with this stuff, you MUST play this game. If you are a young person who never played the classics, then you MUST play this game simply to see how good an FPS can be when you unburden it from mechanics that slow you and the pace of the game down!

TLDR: Buy this game :)\t\t\t\t\t

My main complaint, and possibly my only one, is that this game is simply too short! There aren't enough levels to enjoy and play through. The length of this game should be doubled, maybe even tripled, with an organized expansion pack or set of expansion packs. The 'Snap Maps' are of mixed quality and not equivalent to a sequel or expansion pack. I recommend Doom if you are a fan of the franchise and enjoy nostalgic references to the original.\t\t\t\t\t
