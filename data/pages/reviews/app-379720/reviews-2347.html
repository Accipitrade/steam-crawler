\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEven though I haven't finished this game yet,
(My FPS skills are really rusty. Like haven't played an FPS game seriously, since I was 10 rusty...)
it brings out so much nestalgia from the era of good FPS games.
I would recommend this game to any fan of fast paced action. I'd say the same for fans of classic FPS games as well.
I haven't touched the multiplayer.
(Never was a fan of competitive multiplayer)
The feature I like the most above all else, is the lack of a "feature".
The lack of a "feature" called health regen.
Ever since the day that "feature" was introduced, games in general were too easy.
I commend id software for sticking to their guns, and not adding it to the newest entry, to the father of all FPS games.
I've been bored of FPS games for 6 years, and this game alongside borderlands, have revived my desire to play this genre.

So long story short, if you have a rig able to run this game, buy it as soon as possible.
I plan on buying the next DOOM when it comes out, and suggest anyone who bothers to read this do the same.\t\t\t\t\t
