What ID did is a true comeback to the DOOM roots. Oldschool as you know it but polished in a modern way. It's a simple, fast paced, run and shoot everything that moves FPS. Turn some switches, find some doors, push forward, discover a secret location. But it can be done in all three dimensions, by jumping, climbing, combo executing foes, using simple but powerful augmentations.

Enemies are pure tribute to original Doom monsters. They behave and look familiar, but also fall in to the same modern rule as mentioned - they use environment, attack from all sides, react to player's actions - dumb ones in a dumb way, the smart ones - can nastily test your skills.

And the whole arsenal is there, with a modern look but well known power and style. Weapons differ from each other and can be modified in different ways, depending on playerâ€™s game style.

One of the most original things about DOOM 2016 is customization of armor - Praetor's Suit. It's now not only a good looking gear, but a useful tool combined with Doom Marine body.

Speaking of Doom Marine - he's still anonymous super-hero but the whole game is a tribute to him in a way. Doom is Doom Marine. Doom Marine is Doom. Hell Barons, Revenants, Imps etc. are Doom. Doom is Hell Barons, Revenants, Imps etc. BFG, super shotgun and chainsaw are Doom. Doom is BFG, super shotgun and chainsaw.

As simple as it is it shall simple remain. And then DOOM will be...Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t

A REALLY GREAT GAME IN MY OPINION!

What I liked about this game:\t
- It's like Doom back in the old days but there's modern stuff now.
- The graphics are amazing. Although some people might disagree. You can see the world in full detail and it looks amazing, the different aspects of level design they did looks really good
- I kind of like Gore stuff in video games and this game has a lot of it so I would recommend this game to people who are a fan of gore. The gore in this game looks really beautiful.
- The wide variety of weapons you can choose from is phenomenal
- The game is very simple, you grab weapons and you kill the demons.
- The audio is really amazing and strong. The song by Mick Gordan is great and a real interesting choice.
- There's a little challenge in the campaign but it makes for a great experiance.
- The demons look really cool and badass.
- The characters and weapons have really good details.
- Glory kills are satisfying.

What I didn't liked about this game:\t
- I'm glad that you can customize your characters and weapons when you play in multiplayer but the customizations look kind of similar to one another. I would prefer it if they would make it stand out from one another.
- I know that a lot of people really enjoyed the campaign mode but I found it to be a little plain. There are only a few bosses in the campaign mode and I felt like if they added more bosses it would make the story more interesting.
- The only thing in the multiplayer that's really interesting is that you can turn into a demon form which doesn't really stand out from other multiplayer games.
- For some people it might be a little repetitive.

Overall:\t
- A great game that still has the old school shooters feel to it but with modern stuff.
- It actually is worth the money.
- I would rate it a 8/10.\t\t\t\t\t
