\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI never played OG Doom and skip to play the modern remake / reboot of the game. I can say this is one of good fps game in shooting mechanical which is straight and no bull-♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ fps gimmicks. You are a demon slayer who trapped in Mars Oh no, Demon invade your company, quick sweep them all while you listen questionable character dictate what you should do and go to reveal whats really happened. And that's it. End of story.

Playing Doom is essential for FPS fans, its one of game that solidify fps genre. The mechanic of the game is pretty simple, you have a weapon to shoot and then will find more with little bit upgrade by slaying enemies. The variation of weapon is basic from basic laser pistol gun into super rocket launcher to use everytime you find new place infested with demon. Its endless shooter session and thats Doom exactly.

I am quite enjoying the game and after finished campaign which lasts 10-14 hours with separated chapters that I really like because it gives player to take a break before continuing the campaign for approximately one hour session for each chapter. The graphic is stunning with uncompressed assets that eats my PC storage 60gb for averagely short game and nothing else to offer except those metal music that played slaying enemies and satisfying animation for killing enemies.

Overall, this game is must-do for PC gamer, the movement is pretty sleek and being quite means instant death. A good game for people who have good reflex, not recommended for casual or relaxing game fans. Thats it.\t\t\t\t\t
