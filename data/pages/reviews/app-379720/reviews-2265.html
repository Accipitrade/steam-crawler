\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe enemy AI is horrid. They will teleport directly behind you (cheap tactic to make the game feel difficult), but as soon as you turn to kill them they will flee, either jumping onto a wall, hiding behind something, or changing elevation. It's just annoying. Not fun. Makes the shotgun (your only weapon for the first 1.5 levels) useless on half the basic enemies. The first 2 hours of the game should be fun and exciting, not annoying. I shouldn't think about the dev's making bad decisions, I should be engrossed in their game.

Making "secrets" required for weapon upgrades is just pathetic. Never liked secrets in original Doom games; 2016 took them and evolved them in the wrong direction for my taste. The terrible map doesn't help make exploration less annoying, it makes it more annoying. I don't want platforming in my FPS, it breaks the flow and is just annoying.

Fully endorse the sequel, Doom: Eternal. Much less annoying in all aspects. Has a great rhythm to the combat.\t\t\t\t\t
