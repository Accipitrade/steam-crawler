-For startes it has really fast gameplay, it seemed faster than the original for me.
-It has that Doom 3 feeling to it beacuse you are most of the time inside the UAC Base.
-For me, this game will never feel like when im playing Doom or Doom 2, it just won't.

Here is the real review:

-Glory kills. (For people that just want something like the classics, just disable the Glory kills highlights)
-Fast paced gameplay.
-Combined what made Doom, Doom 2 and Doom 3 great.
-My potato pc can run this game, with just some fps problems in a couple areas.
-Few new weapons.
-Some enemys look like how the did in Doom and Doom 2.
-Includes Multiplayer and Snapmap.
-Who wants story anyways.
-You can skip the story if you want, and they just put you to hear story in a couple moments.
-Big Gun.
-Classic Leves that you can play if you want.
-Each level has a secret classic level.
-Loads fast.
-New enemies.
-Just like your classic Doom... No reloads!, just search for ammo and fire away.

CONS:
-Confusing menu (once you get to know it, this problem is gone.)
-Multiplayer is not something to talk about.
-Snapmap can be really confusing if you don't see the tutorials.
-You can do much more with Doom builder.
-The game didn't really last that much. (took me 4 days to complete the campaing)
-Cyberdemon is just a boss, not a regular enemy.
-Same with Spider Mastermind.
-This games functions more like an arena.
-Enter area, close doors or destroy paths, fight enemies.
-Crashes sometimes.
-Developer mode can ruin your experience. (I got saved from developer mode)
-Can't play multiplayer with Developer mode on.
-DLC's for Multiplayer, which many people are propably not going to care about.
-Again, Snapmap can be very confusing at first.
-Not recommended to buy at full price.

Overall i give DOOM 2016 (Doom 4 pls) a:
8/10

Completely woth buying for the campaing, not so much for multiplayer or Snapmap. You can have the story if you want, if you don't just skip it, fun gameplay and a nice way to refresh your first person shooter experience.
If you are like me, you are probably going to prefer the classic Doom over this.
Also i think this game is a sequel to Doom 64.
