\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is an incredible FPS. It skips the cutscenes and Hollywood nonsense of many other modern shooters and jumps straight into helping you become the demon-slaying god you were born to be.

The levels are a revelation. So many secrets, satisfying arenas, and general replay value. The weapons are totally awesome - each one has its place, and if you’re like me you’ll be constantly flitting from gun to gun at top speed to murder demons in new and creative ways.

The soundtrack is sick, and exactly what you want to be jamming to while you slay demons.

The graphics are top-notch (although the color palette is a bit too orangey-brown for my taste due to the martian setting). Some serious wizardry was employed to make this a very well-optimized game. it runs at a smooth 60fps on my GTX 970 and i5 4670 with nice settings.

This lists me as having played it for about 4 hours. That’s not entirely accurate. My main play-through recently took place on my Nintendo Switch, where I very much enjoy the gyro controls and portability. While I love DOOM on the Switch, the game looks simply gorgeous on the PC, and mouse controls are unparalleled, so the game is probably best experienced on good old Microsoft Windows.

Please buy the game! I've seen this game often on sale for 10 bucks, by the way. It's a must-play.\t\t\t\t\t
