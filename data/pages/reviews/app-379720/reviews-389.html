Normally, I write reviews for games that I play when I complete them, but for this one it's a different story because I've actually played and completed this game multiple times, so here we go!

Doom 2016 or just "DOOM" is a Reboot to the Critically Acclaimed ID software game DOOM 1993, after the somewhat failure of Doom 3 back in the mid 2000s, the series had been on a really long Hiatus for quite some time, until people had seen what they were working on with the supposed "Doom 4" Which from if you looked it up, it was nothing like what we get now. It just looked like if Doom got morphed into yet another Generic "Call of Duty" style game which to me would've been an insult to the series' legacy as being the game that started the FPS genre.


But Holy crap I'm glad they changed it because Doom 2016 is the most badass, adrenaline-inducing and fast paced FPS game I've ever played.

Story!

So there is a story to this game, but it's very simple, you are the Doom Slayer, a Man who is like Ash Williams from Evil dead but bumped up to 100 and is a silent, brutal killing machine who feels nothing but pure unbridled rage. and he is so unbelievably powerful that Demons both big and small wet themselves from even mentioning his name. and you woke up in the UAC base that's been overrun by Demons and you gotta stop them. Basic stuff as Story hasn't ever been the forefront of any doom game.


Gameplay!

Gameplay however, takes the front stage as this is what everybody plays doom for, It is an exhilarating, fast-paced all out gore fest where instead of running away from the horror, you ARE the horror. What I love about Doom is that there's very little tutorial and introductions like in so many games today, Instead of having to sit through cutscenes and long tedious tutorials about the basic mechanics of the game, Doom puts you right into the action from the very beginning, they just hand you a shotgun and tell you to go murder the sh*t out of those unholy hellspawns. Not only that but throughout your time playing through the various levels you find secrets which can give you various things, including weaponry, suit points to upgrade your various skills and sometimes little fun things like Retro levels and mini-games. And sometimes you can also find power-ups in the level where you got to fight a lot of enemies. There's Invulnerability, which makes you temporarily immune to attacks, Haste which gives you a considerable speed boost, Quad damage which allows you to kill enemies easier with weaker weapons, and lastly there's Berserk which is my personal favorite. With that power up, you can insta-kill any demon both big and small. Doomguy's Brutality really shows when you do glory kills against demons, he shatters skulls, breaks an arm or a leg, snaps necks and even rips out demon's jaws or hearts. So to sum it up, the gameplay will make you feel all kinds of emotions. I can see this game being used as Anger management therapy, I usually play Doom when I have a bad day or get angry at another game.

Soundtrack!

Oh man, the Soundtrack. Believe it or not, Mick Gordon's work was what got me into Heavy Metal and made me into a semi-Metalhead. His work is like Doom's original rock songs but WAAAYYY heavier and adrenaline-inducing. The Electronic/Metal songs with the thundering bass and guitar riffs that play during the game have made this soundtrack one of my top favorite video game OST's, right next to Minecraft, Undertale and Super Smash Bros. My Personal favorite tracks out of the entire game are BFG division, Rip and Tear and Mastermind (The Final Boss theme). I highly recommend buying the soundtrack to this game because it is that good.


Overall, This is one of my all-time favorite games even though I came to it kind of late, and is the game that made me go from liking Doom to LOVING it. Play this game, you won't regret a single moment of it.

10/10\t\t\t\t\t
