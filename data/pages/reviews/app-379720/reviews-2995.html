\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh Boy. How am I gonna review this game without gushing endlessly. This game is the pinnicle of what a modern day Doom game should be. It's load, obnoxious, and violent, and it has no intensions on pulling its punches. Doom 4 (or DOOM) is the reboot of the fantastic Doom franchise, where you once again fill the boots of the head smashing, bone breaking, ball busting Doom Guy. He's back, and even more pissed off than ever before. As Doom Guy it is your task to kill a ton of demons, whilst finding a way to end the unending demonic invasion. You will make your way through levels killing, collecting, and discovering secrets as you go along. The combat in this game is fluid and fun, and the story, while not at the fore front of the game, is rather interesting. The single player alone nets this game a 9/10. The multiplayer is generic, and really the only reason I took a point off the score. It is a derivitive CoD Halo combo and really isn't all that fun. Snap Maps, while not the best, is a great way to keep a flow of content going, as players can make their own maps, and it really shows the creativity of the Doom community. BUY THIS GAME NOW!!!!\t\t\t\t\t
