\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is amazing. It has almost all of the classics sound, demons, music (even though it has a nice non 16bit touch to it :)). If you were a huge fan of Doom in the 90's then you will love this game in my opinion. I have no played Coop yet but me and my friend will be doing that soon. That is another thing that they kept from the old Doom. I remember in classic Doom using my LAN me and my friend would play for hours. I just started the game. I just beat the first mission and I have to say I am really impressed. Very face paced shooter. The glory kills (one button kill after lowering an emeies health enough) are another nice touch they have added. The weapon and suit upgrades. Pretty much it's classic Doom with a bunch of new features and amazing graphics. In my opinion always go for the hardest difficulty.

TL:DR
-Awesome amounts of Gore
-Good metal sound tracks
-Guns have mods and the basic pistol has unlimited ammo so you never have to worry about punching things like the old one.
-The glory kills (one button kill after lowering an emeies health enough) are just phenomenal.\t\t\t\t\t
