\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGameplay is fantastic, I'd get stuck on something, die a few times, put it down, but a few minutes late I'd find myself coming back from more. Fast paced, demon slaying goodness. I'm not going to lie, once I got about half way through, it started to feel a bit repetitive with the "door's locked, kill the waves of demons" but I still kept coming back for more. Most stages feel a bit claustrophobic, but still large enough to get lost in during the manic bloodshed.

Soundtrack is amazing! Damn good demon slaying music.

Runs smooth, like butter! The vsync implementation is amazing, kept noticeable tearing at bay without the feeling of moving my mouse through molasses that most games feel like with vsync on.

With everything said, 9.5/10, would recommend. Slay some Demons!\t\t\t\t\t
