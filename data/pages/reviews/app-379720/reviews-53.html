\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awesome. It's incredibly well optimized, looks and sounds amazing, the combat is the most satisfying out of any shooter I've played, and the story is very self aware and incredibly simple: kill every demon you come across. Exploration is a ton of fun too. This was one of the first games where I actually wanted to complete 100% of the secrets, side missions, etc. because even just the act of looking for them was enjoyable. Every weapon besides the starting pistol is useful, and you'll be cycling through pretty much all of them in the tougher arenas on harder difficulties as ammo runs low. The fast-paced movement and the fact that enemies drop health and ammo when you punch their head off or chainsaw them encourages you to be the most aggressive killing machine you can possibly be and it never gets old.

I can't really think of anything wrong with this game. I guess some of the weapon upgrades aren't as generally useful as others, but even those are useful in certain situations. ♥♥♥♥, this game's basically perfect.\t\t\t\t\t
