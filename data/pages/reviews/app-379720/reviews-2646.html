\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn awesome game from start to finish, I don't know why I waited so long to play this.. Definitely one of the best first person shooters I have ever played. This is really what Doom is supposed to be, playing this made me realize what Doom 3 was lacking..

The pacing in this game is practically perfect, It never once feels dull at any moment during the campaign - alternating between quieter exploration sections and all out carnage old school fast paced Doom/Quake/UT style combat. This version of Doom more than any other FPS I have played requires you to really think on your feet and keep moving at all times during combat. Different enemies and areas provide unique challenges - as well as every gun performing very differently with strengths and weaknesses. "Glory kills" make the combat in this game unique and a little easier but even the default "Hurt Me Plenty" difficult gets VERY hard at times especially in the second half of the game, although I never once died thinking to myself "That was BS." The game is brutal, fair and above all fun.

The graphics are incredible and the level design is excellent. Every chapter is layed out very differently, has a unique feel to them and is very memorable. Plenty of eye candy and secret areas/easter eggs in the levels as well. Lots of variety in the ways you choose to upgrade your suit and weapons that definitely affect gameplay quite a bit. I wish more FPS games would focus on making a great campaign like this that is memorable, challenging and fun. The multiplayer is awesome as well from what I played so far, definitely better than the current state of Quake: Champions. Even just based on the campaign I would give this game a 10/10.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo where to start. Doom is one of the best first person shooters I have ever played, in almost every way possible.

The story is very simple, "demons everywhere, kill them all and fast!" Atleast thats what the common player is told, there is a very detailed story hidden in the collectables scatterd around the game, for example where the energy came from why humans need it and other little things. Or if you realy dont care for first person shooter storys just ignore it and keep shooting the demons, and have tones of fun doing it!

As for the first person shooting part of this FPS its is very smooth and feels amazing. Every shot feels like your shooting a gun, not a pea out of a straw, which may I add is super satisfying. The animations of the guns, the switch of gun mods and the secondary abilities animation is spectacular on every level. The look and almost the feel of the animations is heavy and powerfull.

Now as for the multiplayer, this is not why you should buy this game. It has almost no one playing it and was not the main centre of attetion for the development team at all. And it shows, because the campain is amazing.

As for the length of the game, its is a normal length campain if you intend on only playing it once. To get the full DOOM experiance you need to play the campain muliple times, on every difficulty, and set yourself little challenges such as only using the shotgun, or if your a god, only use the pistol on ultra-nightmare difficulty.

All in all this game is F***ing insane and is worth your time and money.\t\t\t\t\t
