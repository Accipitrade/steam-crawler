I think it was worth the price though for sure, and it has stuck in my mind despite my limited hours played.

Doom Eternal was made as a sequel to this, but I couldn't enjoy it as much. Doom Eternal's soundtrack I found to be sub-par, and it felt more limiting in how they expected you to play the game. If you have played Doom Eternal you likely will still enjoy this, but keep in mind that it's a bit of a different experience - more about killing demons endlessly and less about platforming or solving combat puzzles left and right.\t\t\t\t\t
