\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI am well aware I am not the first person to say this. But this is what Doom 3 should have been, a truly 3D, fast paced, 'HOLY CRAP I JUST TORE THAT DEMON IN HALF!' bloody, explosive, shotgun blasting, FUN experience. Massive levels, large kill rooms, and a near endless way to kill monsters, and be rewarded for it, in both the literal sense of getting healt or armor pick ups out of them, or upgrade points for killing enough of them, but also to simply enjoy the utter almost ridiculous level of carnage you can inflict upon a army of demons.

And if you think its too easy, you can ramp it up to ultra nightmare, which is just glorious to behold.

If you liked doom, or like fps in general, you'll want to play this.

Edit: Just a small bit of a personal editorial to this.

There is nothing quiet so satisfying as being able to walk into any room of the game filled with varying demons, and think, 'I have a super shot gun; this is going to be fun'. No reloading, no 'stamina' just you, your gun, your health meter (which does helpfully beep at you when its low, but not annoyingly, just a gentle reminder you need to get up close and physical with a demon to execute it and get a little health back. If only Navi was this helpful.) and a personal arsenal in order to deal a wonderfully realized array of death to demons. Its just satisfying.

Also, Doom guy is a protagonist that plays the 'Strong, silent' type so perfectly well. All you know, is he is the angry marine with a shotgun and to stay out of his way while he does his job.\t\t\t\t\t
