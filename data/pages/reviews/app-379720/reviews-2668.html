\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm going to write a fairly long review here. Alternatively, you can skip to the end where I will put a TL;DR review.
This is one of my favourite games of all time. I'm not going to say it's objectively one of the best games ever made, even though in my opinion it is, as I don't want to get your expectations too high for something you may well not enjoy. Saying that, it is incredibly hard not to enjoy this game. One of the most fun parts in shooting games and FPS games is where you do something that makes you feel like a badass. Every single thing you do in this game makes you feel like a badass. You can die pretty easily, but because of the way the game is made, it rewards going balls-to-the-wall, as killing enemies and performing 'glory kills', a kind of finishing move, gives you more hit points and ammo to play with. The enemies are very well designed, balanced, and unique. Every one has different attacks and different characteristics. The way the enemies in this game are balanced with the guns is excellent. The guns themselves are very balanced. One thing I always find myself doing in shooters is, even though I have 8+ guns at my disposal, I will use 1 or 2 for the entire playthrough. Not this game, though. Because the enemies are so varied, it rewards you to use different guns for different enemies, and since the ammo for any given gun doesn't last forever, you can't use the same gun too much. Using the chainsaw to kill an enemy will cause that enemy to drop a lot of ammo, but has very limited fuel which I think it a great mechanic. The chainsaw will use more fuel the more health the enemies have, so it's good to use it wisely. It also kills in 1 hit.
Do not think that the game forces you to use all of your guns, because if you want to use 1 gun and 1 gun only, you could probably manage it. But I found myself wanting to use all of the guns, as the guns are so well designed and fun to use. This is not only because the guns at their most basic level are satisfying to use, have vastly different effects to one-another, and feel completely different, but also because of a game mechanic in which they can have 2 different secondary fire modes. You can switch between these 2 secondary fire modes with every gun, and both of those fire modes can be upgraded to give faster reload or charge-up times, and just generally be better. This makes every gun versatile, varied and fun. The gunplay is one of the best parts of this game. You can also upgrade your suit, giving it upgrades like reduced damage and more. There is also a system where you can equip runes that give you special abilities, or enhance abilities you already have. All of these upgrades and guns mean you can play the game very differently each time. Hell, even each level. There are so many ways to play this game.

The monster design is also brilliant. Every single enemy seems like it has had so much work put into it. There are lots of different enemies and lots of different tactics you can use to kill these enemies.
Another great thing about this game is flow. You movement, the gunplay, the enemies... Everything just feels so fluid. There's no jerkiness, everything is fast and fun, and the game just has a great feel. It'll make you want to replay tthe game again and again. The charm of this game and 'DOOM Guy' is unquestionable.

The last thing I'm going to talk about is the level design. It's fantastic. There are so many secrets to find and things to do. The levels are designed in a way that the 'arenas' that you fight enemies in are incredibly fun, fast, and fluid to traverse. You can run around jumping from platform to platform shooting people with a sawn-off double barreled shotgun, stand up high and snipe people with a gauss cannon, run around blowing people up with a rocket launcher, or simply just go balls-to-the-wall with a heavy assault rifle.

This game is all kinds of fun. If you're looking for an incredibly fun, well-made, no-nonsense shooter, buy this game. You will never regret it and you will find yourself coming back again and again to kill demons. This game made me love video games again.

One of the best FPS games of the decade. If not the best. PLAY IT

TL;DR - Great gunplay, great level design, tonnes of fun, great enemy design, excellent replayability. Buy it.


10/10\t\t\t\t\t
