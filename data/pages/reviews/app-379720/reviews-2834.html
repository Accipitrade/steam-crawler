\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a ton of fun for those of you who miss classic FPS games. The gunplay in this game is just top notch. When you start getting into the big rooms where the game throws tons of enemies at you, the gameplay goes from being good to transcendant as you race around, barely surviving from glory kill to glory kill in a ballet of death. Don't play it on the easiest difficulty.

The story is very straightforward, but it is also humorous and very self-aware. You play a silent protagonist who despite being generic by design infuses your actions with a lot of personality as he smashes precious tech and ignores characters that are giving him directions. The level design is solid, as is the art design, and it is a great looking game. Also a special shout out to the music. The music is a phenomanal mix of metal (an homage to the original) and electronic music, and is brilliantly implemented in the game as the soundtrack reacts to how you are playing the game. Not just in a binary fighting/not fighting way, but in a "reacting to glory kills" way, or a "reacting to changing my gun and attack style" sort of way. Really cool stuff.

All in all a must buy if you love run-and-gun shooters.\t\t\t\t\t
