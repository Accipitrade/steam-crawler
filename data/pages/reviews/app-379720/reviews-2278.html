\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo disappointed by this game.
I tried 3 times to play it and accept its idiosyncrasies but I just cant like it.
They tried to fusion old school FPS and original doom gameplay with modern shooters habits. It's a fail in my opinion.
From my point of view, the original doom was:
1/ great maps
2/ corridor parts where a sense of dread was creeping on you as you never knew what was around the corner
3/ huge parts with tons of enemies where you could move freely and make a lot of on the spot decisions during a long fight
4/ technical excellency

Quake I and II did keep this 4 points despite the 3D, the 3rd point seing less enemies because of the 3D but there was still enough depth of vision and freedom of movement to promote interresting fights. This excellency in movement is what lead to QIII pinacle of FPS fighting.

Here we have mostly the 4/. I love the game engine but the overcharge in visual effects prevents it from displaying enough enemies and letting you fly over the map. Also they want to cater to more players so movement has to be nerfed. To this day and to my knowledge serious sam 1 is the only game that capture the fun of the original doom in 3D. (I havent played painkiller, I should try that one).

I kinda got the original feeling in the hell segment but that was all. The mars base is a drag. The main design point is arena stages where you face a few enemy at the same time, with constant spawning and small enclosed spaces with bottlenecks. Enemies are rushing you from behind, you are forced to evade and shoot but it's not a decision you make like in the original doom, it's more that you're forced by the map to get them in the right order with right weapon with the right way in order to get ammos and health back while eliminating the right threat.
I sometime got a doom like or quake like rush in some wider area but then, there was almost no challenge.
The game forces the fight to be on close quarters because there is no lethality otherwise. The enemies are spares. So all this leads to closed and small map areas.

Because of this they had to justify the fact that the base closes itself around you during the fight sequences. So the base has to be aware of "demonic intrusion". That and the decision to make health and ammos come out of the killed monsters leads to the story of hell being known and exploited by the humans. It's such a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ story on the level of the midi-chlorians. Doom original story was paper thin but it was coherent. We open gate to hell. Hell comes in. It's bad.
Here the premice is that hell energy is usable. WTF ? Next installment is a cross over with matrix with imps being harvested and kept in the matrix by human robots.

On top of that,for the game to be more interresting and lasting, you have layers upon layers of achievements and upgrades. Secrets area, rune area, usb keys, puppets, gotta catch them all to upgrade your suit and move
Moving faster should be here from the beginning.

I want to move fast and freely and make fast decisions on where to shoot to maximise the fun not to solve a puzzle.
I want to play doom not pokemon.
THIS IS DOOM Kick the game in the pits of hells...\t\t\t\t\t
