This is a really fun FPS game that really channels the spirit of its predecessors. Admittedly this game takes a bit of getting used to because it is quite twitchy and fasted paced, and while both are trademarks of classic Doom games it can take a bit of getting used to. Once you get the hang of the mechanics though this game is incredibly fun and you can get really creative with working your way through the combat of the levels. There is also so much packed into these levels with challenges to complete and secrets to find that give this game replay value and are really satisfying to find/complete to test your skills. Overall I'd recommend this game because the mechanics are fluid and a lot of fun and the game just oozes appreciation for its source material and pays homage to the original games which makes the experience all the more satisfying.\t\t\t\t\t
