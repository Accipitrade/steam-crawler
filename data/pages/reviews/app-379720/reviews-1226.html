\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a good game.

Clean, polished, well-designed levels, good secrets, well-designed challenges, plot is cohesive but no more intrusive than necessary to tie together the reason the game exists (massive violence, and cute collectible minifigures). Progression of weapons+abilities feel awesomely overpowered each time you get something new, and yet death is still something to fear. While the enemies grow gargantuan and awful, they never becoming tedious or frustrating to fight.

Never stop moving.
Never reload.


I categorized this in my 'Relaxing Games' folder.\t\t\t\t\t
