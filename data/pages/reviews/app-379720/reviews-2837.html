\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMan alone in hell. Heavy gun in his hand. And the only way out is through hordes of demons.
Echoes of the massacre are still ringing in his ears when he takes a deep breath, looks at walls painted red, steaming piles of corpses and... reloads.
Loneliness. Desolace. Fear. Not only monsters are his enemies.

But... this was not about this game. This was about Brutal Doom :)

Because THIS game is literally the ugliest game I've ever played. Period.
Plastic monsters are spurting cherry jelly instead of blood.
Every model, every location, every item is yellow-ish-brown.
Everything is glossy, even the sand.
Blood and bodies are magically vanishing leaving sterile clean locations behind.
Monsters drop shiny gold pickups for Sonic to take.
Sometimes you find Ken dolls lying around with access cards or more plastic guns.
And if you are out of resources you can kill whatever yellow-brown enemy comes at you (impossible to distinguish)
with chainsaw to get extra coins and 11 liters of jelly.
If monster is tired it glows gold, you know, the usual thing mosters from hell do.

This is incredible how bad graphics can ruin immersion experience.

And I haven't even mentioned tons of other stupid things in this game like:
* Micro-management - weapon upgrades, suit upgrades, who needs this?
* Plot. Vega? Are you supposed to go back to DragonBall?
* Area lockdown, daemon spawn, repeat till the end of level. No monsters wandering around. Even in hell. They only spawn from nowhere to greet you.
* Collecting dolls.

This is not Doom that you remember from childhood.
It has no dark climate, no gore, no immersion, no creeps when playing during night, no nightmares.

You will get really good, really fast, realy synthetic ARCADE game.
Tons of fun instead of tons of fear.
If you like jelly definitely worth 60 EUR, otherwise fire Brutal Doom for true massacre.\t\t\t\t\t
