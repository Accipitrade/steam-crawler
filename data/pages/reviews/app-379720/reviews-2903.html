\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a horror game, no doubt. Only this time, you're the horror. You're the thing that goes bang in the night, the legendary being that makes the hellspawn quake (heh) with fear. You hunt them unrelentingly, gaining power through their terror and rended limbs. No gimmicky flashlights or monster closets; rip and tear. Rip and tear.

This is a truly fantastic game. Graphics are great, movement is quick, sound design is spot on, the cut scenes are rare and short, the sound design is exceptional, the music is so ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ rad, and all the weapons feel... good? to use. The levels are the best Doom levels since Knee Deep in the Dead. If you're on the fence, don't be: purchase this game and remember why Doom was the bulldozer that it was 23 years ago. It's really, really good. GOTY for 2016 no doubt; realistically, one of the better games ever released.

My only minor complaint:
1. It'd be cool if more demons were just sorta walking around the levels, but I know the engine somewhat limits that. As it stands, you mow down tons of demons for a lil bit and then not for a while.

PS: I originally didn't like the music. Then I realized I was being a big dumb music snob and accepted that it's actually really, really awesome.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen it comes to shooter games, I tend to like the ones with a good story or those that need tactics and/or strategy. I don't like playing mindless shooters. Doom appeared to be just that and I thought I would give it a miss. But I got it because it was on sale. I've played Doom for 18+ hours so far and I've been having more fun than I ever expected! I don't think I've played any other FPS that made me feel this bad ass!! This game is vastly more entertaining than it appears at first glance.

The first thing you will notice once you start the game is how amazing it looks and yet how smoothly it runs! The developers have done a great job optmizing it. (Of course you have to meet the requirements.) The sound and music of this game are perfect and really helps in immersion. I actually put on my headphones while playing this game and I highly recommend you do that too. The environment looks hellishly gorgeous and the music puts you right in the mood for kicking some demon butts.

If you like shooter games with big guns and lots of enemies to shoot at, you'll love this game.
If you like shooter games but with a good story, you'll love this too!
If you like shooter games that actually require tactics and strategy, hell, you'll love this too!
Basically, if you like shooters, you'll love this game. It is a ton of fun.\t\t\t\t\t
