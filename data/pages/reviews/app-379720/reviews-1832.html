\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt the time, this was my first modern PC game and I am very happy it still is.

From watching trailers and gameplay footage, I knew Bethesda & Id Software did a great job with it. The gameplay itself is very simple: rip and tear demons however you see fit. It even has "Glory Kills" which gives you additional ways to kill demons and it varies pending on the position. You have a wide variety of weapons and upgrades to use and yes, the double barrel shotgun gets all the love it deserves since it is my most favorite weapon.

The bosses give them a whole new meaning of badass especially the Cyberdemon fight. Feels like you're meeting an old friend just to kick their ass for the hell of it. As for the Spider Mastermind, that is a tough boss, I thought the big machine gun from the original was bad.

The game does have plenty of references from the previous DOOM games. Especially, when it comes to unlocking hidden levels & additional secrets. All of which you can play again, but to be honest, it feels weird mixing Classic & Modern DOOM. I didn't like how they remade the Cyberdemon fight, all he does is point and shoot.

Highly recommended for your collection, regardless of what console you own. I'm looking forward to what DOOM Eternal has to offer and please don't screw it up!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStory: 7/10
Music: 9/10
Graphics: 9/10
Gameplay: 10/10

DOOM is a great game. The story is good and believable. The combat flows smoothly, you are moving around all the time shooting enemies, perfoming 'glory kills' (finisher kills), and picking up health, armor and ammo.
You are a one man army but it makes sense that you're a one man army. You're not some hench pleb from the military academy. You're the DOOM slayer. A man given immense power by a demon he struck a deal with. However for this immense power, he now has a burning rage in him that is never satisfied. So basically you just go around brutally killing demons.
It's a linear level-based action game, and it's wonderful. If you think the campaign isn't enough, don't worry. There's multiplayer to fight other people and level up in. Still not good enough? It has user made co-op maps. Now go kill some demons while metal plays in the background!\t\t\t\t\t
