\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNeed more demon guts in your life? Of course you do! Well this game lets you feel like a total badass while tearing those demons a new one!

Pros:
+Several iconic demons are back to murder your face!
+New functions, such as glory kills for massive amounts of health and chainsaw for massive amounts of ammo really make you change stratagy to help survive the brutal onslaught of enemies
+The game really emphasizes speed and mobility, keeping it fast paced
+No reloading besides double-barrel, making it so you don't waste time reloading and spend that time shooting faces!
+Great Graphics and Sound quality
+Great level design, makes it very difficult to find the secrets at time
+Hidden unlockable Original levels
+Awesome weapons, each with their own unique weapon mods, 2 per weapon in MOST cases (not all)
+The difficulty level is very brutal, and helps make the game feel more rewarding the harder you play!
+The Snapmap is Mario Maker with demons and blood! Will be very interesting to see what people can make
+You can play as super demons in multiplayer!
+Most secrets are very awarding

Cons:
-I get some lag when several enemies spawn at the same time. Might not be an issue for high tier computers or after future patches
-If your a secret collecter like me, it can get very brutal to find them, and delays the combat for periods of time.

I can't speak about balacing issues in multiplayer because I have yet to try it, but it does look very interesting, and the demon runes look interesting to use! I hope they will add this into Snapmaps.

Overall: 9.5/10 I highly recommend to those who love brutal FPS who enjoy seeing heavy gore and fast past action. Not to those who are faint of heart.\t\t\t\t\t
