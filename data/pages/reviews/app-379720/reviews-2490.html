\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's 2016's undisputed game of the year!
Do the Lord's work and brutally slaughter countless demons at a refreshingly fast pace. No cover shooting, no force-fed boring cutscenes, and no DLC(this was an issue before but they fixed it)! If you've been massively bittered by the state of modern game design and still haven't played this, get it. It has been scientifically proven to restore faith in the gaming medium for the majority of disheartened game-players.
Gamplay is an absolute dream. Your movespeed is super fast, high difficulties are almost always fair, you get to discover rare powerups and equippable perks that switch up the mechanics, and you get to meet old friends like the chainsaw and Super Shotgun. Screwed up and got hit in the middle of combat, but can't retreat to find health? You can still recover by beating your enemies to death, making them drop health pickups! This system works terrifically well, as it encourages you to man up and keep fighting even if you're inches away from getting ripped apart, turning the tension(and fun) up to 11.
The music, character design and art are all top-notch as well. The music more than does its job in getting you filled with energy when you're ripping and tearing, and the character design and animations for Doomguy turn him into one of the top coolest characters in history, without him uttering a single word; Doomguy is sure to give you a long-lasting grin at every new thing he does.
Lastly, the multiplayer is alright. It can be quite enjoyable sometimes, but I personally feel that loadouts and demon runes have a tendency to ruin some of that enjoyment. If you don't enjoy the multiplayer, you can always play arcade mode and compete against your friends for high scores in each of the singleplayer levels, which is a whole lot more fun than it sounds.\t\t\t\t\t
