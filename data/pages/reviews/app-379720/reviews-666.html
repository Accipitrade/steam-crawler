\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat, unique pace to the combat. Low on health or armor during a fight? Perform special kills on the demonic horde.

There is plenty of story to be learned, if you want. Intel gathered throughout the game provides interesting and sometimes humorous backstory to the events of the campaign. Don't care about story and just want to kill baddies? This is your game, too.

The music is so sick. The heavy metal fits perfectly with the run-and-gun pace of the game. There is also good creepy ambiance at times, which I love. I listen to the soundtrack when I workout so I can really kick the hell out of that treadmill.

Con:
I am obsessed with finding all of the secrets for each level. There are some levels or situations where I realize I missed something but the layout of the level does not allow me to backtrack. A couple of times, if you backtrack, you will get stuck in an area and be forced to restart the level. This can be frustrating but it is the only major flaw I can think of.

There are so many great things about this game and everyone already knows it's a great game. I know my review won't mean much in the grand scheme of things but I felt the game deserved at least this much from me. Love it. Can't wait to move to Doom Eternal.\t\t\t\t\t
