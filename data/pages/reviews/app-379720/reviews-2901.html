TL;DR : Multiplayer that makes COD look like child's play, Singleplayer that is riveting and bad-ass, and a Map creator that allows you to use all assests in the game to create anything you want. Would recommend to anyone. Able to run multiplayer flawlessly on a GTX 540.

This is one of my new favourite games. The multiplayer is VERY, VERY, fun. It is extremely fast paced and gung-ho, and doesn't take long at all to learn how to play effectively once you learn that you have to be constantly moving and picking up health and ammo. Anyone who dislikes this multiplayer is probably just bad at the game and FPS in general. Any FPS lover, or DOOM 1 player will LOVE this game. Who doesn't like running around as a gigantic, hellish demon ripping peoples heads off, or finding the famed BFG-9000 and one shotting people into oblivion?

While I have not finished the campaign completely yet, and am not much of a fan of singleplayer, it is so far very immersive, and extremely bad-ass, just like the original creators of doom intended. Weapon and armor upgrades encourage you to stray from the beaten path, and some encounters with demons are actually suprisingly challenging. The maps in the campaign and multiplayer are vertically designed, encouraging you to keep moving and shooting, as there is no forms of cover in singleplayer.

I want to note the fact that ID Studios has optimized graphically so exstensively, that I am able to get 45+ FPS on a GTX 540, and It only dips a little bit on the most graphically complicated maps, but most of them are relatively simple. The extremely good graphics gives you even more of an immersive experience into the lovecraftian, hellish world of DOOM.

While I got the game at a 67% discount I would not object to paying the full $80. The amount of work put into this game is extremely evident, the optimization of graphics, inclusion of a map builder that is wholly open source, a singleplayer with a riveting and bad-ass story, and a multiplayer mode that makes Call of Duty look like child's play.

When you pay 80$ for a Bethesda game, you get everything you paid for. If you buy it on the sale, it's an absolute steal.\t\t\t\t\t
