\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThey really brought back the feeling of classic Doom in all it's bloody glory with this one. The flow of combat is extremely well balanced and by mid-game you really feel like you have all the tools you need to handle any situation. Boss fights are fun and pretty much all boil down to "shoot it until it dies" while avoiding its super attacks. Music is great, visuals are great, pacing is great.

Biggest things I don't like are the secrets. Most of them aren't worth the time to find and really take away from the fast paced feel of the game. Especially the classic doom secrets, where you have to find a hidden lever (this doesn't appear on the map) and a hidden room (you can guess where this is if you have the map revealed). Also weapon mastery seems like a not-so-thought-out kind of feature. The mastery requirements are kind of dumb for some weapons and there is no way you'll complete many of them on one play through.

Great game. True successor to classic Doom and better than Doom Eternal. 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
