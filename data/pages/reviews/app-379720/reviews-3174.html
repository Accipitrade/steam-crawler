\r\n\t\t\t\t\t-It doesn't live up to the original Doom and Doom 2.
-Music is forgettable. I still remember the soundtrack from old Doom some of the songs were very memorable and some amazingly creepy, i cant remember a single level soundtrack from this game.
-I just doesn't recapture the creepy atmosphere the originals had.

-Most of the combat is: you walk into a tiny room, the game spawns monsters in the room and you jump and run around the tiny room trying to avoid the lighting fast pinkies charging at you.
-In original Doom the enemies were already in the map and you could decide how you fight them.

-No manual saving system

-Boss fights in a tiny arena, why?

I seem to be in the minority with the opinion that this game is pretty bad as a Doom game. It's not a bad game by any means but disappointment it for sure is.

On the positive side the game engine is amazing, it is very well optimized.\t\t\t\t\t
