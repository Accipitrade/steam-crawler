Review:
Doom(2016) is a fast and frantic shooter which rewards constant movement and pits the player into big arenas to fight off hordes of demons which it never relents on sending the hordes after you. It offers a great variety of different weapons to fight the horde off and rewards you for quick up close deaths of the enemies. It however is wrought with pacing issues where you receive all your weaponry early in the game which quickly makes the more weaker weapons redundant then they necessary needed to. It also places all of its bosses in the end game instead of spreading them throughout the game. Still the game does a good job keeping the main gameplay loop interesting but the pacing could be improved.
8/10\t\t\t\t\t
