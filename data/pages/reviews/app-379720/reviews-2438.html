\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoyed the game alot it's fast pace feels great once you get a hang of it. And when you hit flow it's great you have that amazing soundtrack thumping along with you and it's a blast to play. There is a few things that prevent me from reccomending this game to everyone.

Pros
- Amazing Game Feel
- Fast Paced Gameplay
- Killer soundtrack that makes me want to pick up a guitar and learn how to play it
- Encourages aggressive play (A nice change from the norm)
- Neat Secrets
- Great variety of enemies that are easy to identify
- Minimal cut scene
- Good tension and release between combat and non-combat
- Snapmaps provide a ton of user created content that can even be co-op which is really cool.

Cons
- Some Achievements are glitched
--This won't bother most people but for me if I like a game, I do enjoy 100% most of them to see if the achievements encourage me to play in a new way or experience a new part of the game. However, I now have at least 2 glitched achievements that not even starting a new game fixed. Really annoying and sad to see that it hasn't been fixed in the 2 years since release.

- I wish there was another boss or that you fought another boss again, I liked the cyberdemon fight and would have found fighting it in a more diverse arena a greater challenge.
- Demon data logs are only audiable if you stand directly beside them, would have been nice to hear as I did something.

- While the combat and release cycle is good I wish it wasn't so predictable, a big open area is always a fight, narrow corridors may have a few enemies if that. Some more variety in play would have been nice.

Overall:
If you like fast paced shooters you'll likely enjoy this but don't worry about 100% the game as some are glitched even 2 years later. I personally am really excited for the sequel and how they'll improve and adapt moving forward.\t\t\t\t\t
