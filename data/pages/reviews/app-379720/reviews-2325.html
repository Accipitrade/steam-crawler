\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI never played original Doom, and wasn't quite sure what will I get. And what I got is pure awesomeness! Playing this game was a pure pleasure.

Pros:
+ Very fluid game with emphasis on movement
+ Variety of weapons and upgrades available
+ Killing enemies feels very satisfying
+ Surprisingly coherent story that is not shoved down player throat
+ Superb soundtrack and sound effects
+ This actually is a 3D shooter, it reminds me of good old Quake 3 Arena in the sense that you must move also up and down a lot
+ Very fluid and natural quick actions and if they annoy, it is possible to upgrade rune for quicker quick actions
+ Good variety of enemies that each has its own strengths and weaknesses
+ By the end you should be able to exploit weaknesses of each of the enemies and it just feels really good to notice improvement in yourself in such a short term

Cons:
- Map could be more intuitive
- If you have some form of OCD, God help you with collectibles
- Slightly large downtime between arenas
- I couldn't find where to go on couple of occasions\t\t\t\t\t
