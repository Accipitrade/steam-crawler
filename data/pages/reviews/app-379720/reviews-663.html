\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros: Tons of action, a wide variety of enemies, and plenty of achievements to give you an incentive to look in every corner for bonus items. The soundtrack is perfect, keeping your adrenaline pumping as you gun down hordes of demonic enemies. Also, the selection of weapons is the best in the series so far. The Snapmap feature adds to the longevity of play and allows you to make and publish your own levels for others to try.

Cons: The detailed backgrounds lack interactivity at times. In particular, all those vending machines are going to waste - it would have been nice to have a way to buy health power-ups from them. Also, if you want all the achievements, be prepared to grind a LONG time in multiplayer.

The verdict: The best game in the series so far with both a stellar single-player mode and a fun multiplayer mode as well. Recommended for FPS and horror fans alike.\t\t\t\t\t
