Fast paced, interesting level design to hop around in while fighting, and a great set of weapons, almost all of which have 2 secondary abilities to choose from. Plus there's lots of little secret things to find and challenges to do. I've played a bit of multiplayer and it has mostly pretty standard game modes but it's pretty fun too, with loads of character customization and things to unlock. I don't have the best pc right now, but I was able to play this game on low settings plus lowering the settings on a specific file at 720p, 60FPS so if your computer struggles to run it maybe modify that file. I had some minor technical difficulties, and the story was kinda meh (does anyone really play Doom games for the story?), and towards the end it got a little repetitive, but I had a blast and highly reccomend it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has everything I was looking for in a modern single player FPS.
Yeah it does feel a bit linear; demon engagement -> walk -> jump up some ledges -> demon engagement.
But man are those engagements fun. A rocking tune starts playing and suddenly it's hell unleashed. Fireballs flying everywhere, Hellknights charge at you, 'low ammo' pops up as you notice something even bigger spawning in the back. And it feels freaking great to blast away every single one of them, especially when you throw in some glory kills (yeah, punch the f*ckers). Really gets your adrenaline going.

I appreciate the fact that every weapon stays usefull throughout the entire game, and that's due to some nice gun modes that can be further upgraded. You also get armor mods and runes that can improve a wide variety of features of your character. A just enough rpg aspect.

It's a seriously well done shooter that does give you that badass feeling we all love, yet makes you work for it. Recommending.\t\t\t\t\t
