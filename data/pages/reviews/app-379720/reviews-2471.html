\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI will directly come to the point.The game is not bad. Steam only has 2 options yes and no. There is no "not recommended for everyone" option.Thats why for me its a no. But in reality I would say its good, nothing more nothing less. For me its too much repetitive, gets boring after a while. I enjoyed it for sometime then it got boring. Basically you go from one point to another and clear hordes of enemies to unlock the door, thats it. Story is not much interesting & is also not necessary for a game like this. But if you are like me then you may not be able to play this game for more than an hour at a stretch. The game overall looks good but at higher res if you check the textures they are low. To compensate for that the game uses lot of depth of field, floating embers & motion blur. Also the gameplay is fast which means there will be so much action on your screen you won't have the time to notice anything. Generally you can see these type of masking featues in consoles (ignore the gameplay part). Anyways those are not of any importance.
The levels are well designed & the default controls are good too. But the map is confusing. Sometimes you may feel totally lost and get frustrated. The map offers two modes 2D (Top down view) and 3D. The former is bad. Now suppose you have a door to unlock the map will only show you the door. It will not show where you can find the key to unlock it.
Anyways if you liked the older doom games and serious sam series then you will like it too. Again this is not a bad game but also this is not a game for everyone. If you are an arcade shooter fan then definitely go for it! If you are like me who don't like doing the same thing on every chapter,level,sublevel then stay away from it.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first, I didn't like this game. The level design is sort of a step down from the old games. In those, you would traverse across a sprawling maze while killing monsters. In this one, the level design is just arena-break-arena-break. The levels are very straightforward, and it is impossible to get lost.

However, the enemy design builds upon the old games and the combat is nothing short of amazing. If it wasn't such a short game, killing demons would never get old. I sometimes play through various levels just to get a quick thrill of punching and shooting demons in the face. The music, while some songs just sound like constant farting, is mostly good. BFG Division in particular is awesome. I definitely recommend this game even though its level design isn't as good as the old games.\t\t\t\t\t
