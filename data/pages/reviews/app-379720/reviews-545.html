\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love almost everything about this game, except...

The formula of going from one place to the next, entering a room, and killing all the baddies gets super redundant super fast. I think if anything, this game is really good upon a second play AFTER you are familiar with where everything is and what everything is, but first play through things are really overwhelming.

It's fun when you can speed-run through most of the game, and you don't die that often. But eventually, when you reach the highest levels, it gets frustrating to retry different kill rooms over, and over, and over again. What's your reward for killing everything? A new room with new and more things to kill at once. Wave after wave after wave. I'm exhausted. On the final level.

The graphics, the soundtrack, the sound design, the animations, the weapons, the upgrades are all great. I wish, though, that there was more to the collectibles like unlocking cheat codes for a second playthrough once you're done beating the game the first time. I also wish there were more weapons, or weapon modifications. Considering that some modifications are SIGNIFICANTLY better than others, you eventually reach a point where you're just like, "Well, I guess I'll get the stun bomb for the sake of getting the achievement."

Overall: 7.8/10. If you love first person arcade-style shooters, it's so worth it. Especially if you have a nice rig with a 144hz monitor, this is the game that lets you experience the best aspects of your monitor.\t\t\t\t\t
