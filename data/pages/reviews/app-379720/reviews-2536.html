\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI genuinely can't think of any ciriticism that isn't petulent whining. It's just damn good.

The ending is a brutal cliffhanger (spoiler warning) but nevertheless fullfilling. The jump is a little piddly but the level design and double jump you get later makes it so it doesn't matter. The loading times are a bit long. And that's literally all the negatives I have on this game.

The music is incredible; it matches the game perfectly and gets me properly in the mood to rip demons limb from limb.
The guns are neat enough, though a bit basic. A pistol, a shotgun, a machine gun, a rocket launcher... Nah I'm kidding they're fantastic. No reloading to break up the red mist and attachments that put a spin on the gunplay.
The enemy variety is awesome and gradually but quickly ramps up til you're facing a couple mid-game minibosses among the groundling hoardes.
The sound design is overall brilliant. The music gets me headbanging and brings down the red mist, the guns (especially the machine gun) make my balls feel big, the enemies make distinct noises when they spawn in and engage so you know what to expect in a fight...

(i didn't touch the multiplayer and don't plan to, but from what I've seen around YouTube and such it looks top notch)

Honestly, this is the best game I've ever played with the Bethesda name slapped on it. Props to Id for designing such a masterpiece and please make another!\t\t\t\t\t
