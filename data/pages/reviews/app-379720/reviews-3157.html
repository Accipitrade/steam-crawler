\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun experience. I enjoyed this game so much I can forgive the crashing. I wasn't too interested in Doom or FPS games until Goldeneye 64 so I missed out on the whole Doom experience when I was young, so I decided to give it's reboot a try instead and I was not disappointed.

The controls and action were spot on, but I was actually more impressed with the platforming. I found the platforming in this game to be very nostalgic for me because I was a huge fan of the Metroid Prime series. Between the platforming and bosses, this game felt very similar to the experience I remember from those games back in the day.

The only down part outside of the crashes is that the game feels a bit shallow. This is a trend in modern gaming that isn't excluded here. Sometimes the battles feel too repetitive and the game sometimes lacks soul. But I've come to expect that from FPS games in general.

As far as FPS games go, this game leads the pack for me today.\t\t\t\t\t
