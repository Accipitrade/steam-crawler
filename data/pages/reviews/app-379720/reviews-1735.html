\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI enjoy FPS games because I consider them a form of stress relief. Tell me of any other medium in which you can not safely only act out the violent, murderous fantasies we all have from day to day, but also get rewarded for them.

It's why I'm not a fan of games like OUTLAST and its type. "What's that? A game where I'm defenseless and hunted relentlessly by deformed killers and bloodthirsty monsters? Sign me up!" No, not for me, thanks. I play games to RELIEVE stress, not add to it.

I also enjoy games that have a good story behind them. And say what you will about DOOM...but those games never really had much of a story at all. "Kill thing. Find key. Use exit. Lather, rinse, repeat."

And when I heard about ANOTHER DOOM game for 2016, I pretty much ignored it. I can't remember exactly what was commanding my attention at that time, it might have been WOLFENSTEIN: THE NEW ORDER, but I thought if it was any good, I'd pick it up later.

You would think that I'd learned my lesson with how id revamped WOLFENSTEIN.

id found a way to not only inject an actual STORY into DOOM, but they made the anonymous 'Doomguy' into a FORCE OF NATURE. This is a game where you're fighting zombies and demons and cybernetic abominations...and yet somehow, YOU are the monster in a horror movie hunting THEM instead of a gaggle of brain-dead teenagers.\t\t\t\t\t
