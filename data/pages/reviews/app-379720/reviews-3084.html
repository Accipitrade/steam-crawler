\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom 2016.

Before buying this game I watched a fair bit of gameplay. I decided that I found full price a bit steep so I got it during a 50% off action.

Judging from that price point I was blown away. Not only does that game look beautiful (even on my meager GTX760) if you get the frame rate stable it's butter smooth in it's controls. Gameplay is quick and relentless. The highest difficulty before unlock is challenging but not frustrating. Level design is some of the best I have seen in a LONG time. Great vertical movement, clear location and really nice hidden areas.

Gun play is fun and with the upgrades there are some options to chose how you want to play. This combined with the "perks" gives some replay options.

The story is... eeh... well it's a Doom game. Don't expect to much. Some references to the old games and a few gags but nothing special.

8/10, great game\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI never played a DOOM game before (aside from watching some videos of it), and I don't really play many FPS games. But I find this as one of my favorite games. This is my thoughts on this game:

Pros:

-It runs around 50 FPS average with my low-end graphics card (a 750ti) in 1080p with medium settings. Even at that settings, the graphics still looks very nice.

-The medium difficulty (Hurt Me Plenty) is just about right for people who's terrible at video games like me.

-About 10 weapons to choose from (including the chainsaw and BFG). Each has it's own advantage. Even though I almost always use the Super Shotgun from the second I get it.

-The speed/pacing (or whatever it's called) is pretty fast.

-Enemies aren't too bullet spongy. The big demons and bosses can take many shots to kill, but it makes sense to me.

-There's a free demo for you to try it out. There isn't many new games that do this.

Cons:

-The glory kill animations can get repetitive and slow you down a little bit. I didn't find it as much of a problem though.

-At some point in the game, there will be some unskippable cutscenes. I don't mind it, but others may find this really annoying.

I don't know about the multiplayer stuff, but I don't think I'll ever try it either. I'm tired of getting shouted at for being bad at the game. It's kinda entertaining sometimes, but being shouted constantly can get tiring. Just like every other multiplayer game I've ever played.

Conclusion: I find it as a good game. If you like the previous DOOM games, or FPS games, this game might be worth playing. It's up to you to decide though, there's steam refunds and a free demo. This is just my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t
