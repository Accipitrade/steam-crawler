\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL:DR = It's great. Just buy it!

You might be like me. You got into gaming on Doom and Doom 2. You played Quake and Quake 2. You maybe skipped Q3 because DM only games... meh. You got Doom 3 and while an excellent game, it was a little too survival horror. This DOOM is probably what Doom 3 should've been. It's fast paced intense combat. I get headaches from clenching my teeth trying to back-peddle and shoot Hell Knights at the same time!

It's a bit over the top gory. When I first saw the glory kill thing I immediately put this game on my "don't bother" list with the latest Mortal Kombat. Don't get me wrong, you shoot a demon in the head you want to see copious blood and bone right? RIGHT! But it's this new trend of close up "let's pause the action so we can watch bones break and see individual blood drops hit the camera" thing I can't get behind. But I'm happy to report the glory kill mechanic is completely optional and has the benefit of giving the player small amounts of health for doing them, and they really kind of add to the fast pace gameplay, even though they're semi-bullet-timey.

The thing I really love though is how fluid the game feels. You glide from place to place, you can jump AND MANTLE to higher ledges, it's just very clean and smooth feeling.

There's also some great dark, though modest humor that harkens back to the good ol' days. Like if you jump into a pit of fire, you slowly burn, fall to your knees, hands out in front of you, slowly burning and just as you sink down to die your hand slowly turns a thumbs up just before the "load" screen comes up.

There's also a bunch of RPG type upgrades to find/earn/get, changing the functions of your weaponry, upgrading your suit's ability and armor, not to mention playable Doom 1 levels using old textures just like you remember, but DOOM (4) monsters in place of the classic ones.

Once the level is cleared (or mostly cleared) I have just as much fun running around trying to mantle up everything seeking out every last secret area as I do fighting the monsters. It's like two games in one.

I'm not much for public DM but I found I just had to try and it was presently surprised with how that all worked out. Except, F that guy with the lighting gun. Every kill I got was followed by a streak of lightning from behind and this guy running over my corpse! Still fun though.

All that being said, I HIGHLY recommend this game for fans of the original.\t\t\t\t\t
