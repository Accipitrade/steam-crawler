Hence I love Wolfenstein etc as blasting Nazis is more satisfying than killing a demon. In theory. But, DOOM made me change my mind. The gameplay is radically different from any other FPS, in that you get health and other powerups only by being in the thick of it. If your health is low, you gotta get back in the fight. There are so many bad guys, so much blood. The gameplay is effortless, great graphics, controls and so on fantastic. So many tweaks to your gameplay style, and yet not too gimmicky. Good story but no-nonsense. Also, the snapmap feature is brilliant and totally underrated if you ask me. Snapmap is a good place to play on multiplayer, as the actual multiplayer has some issues. Anyway, just f***ing buy this game and then join the rest of us waiting for the sequel. Hands down best game on Steam for FPS carnage.\t\t\t\t\t
