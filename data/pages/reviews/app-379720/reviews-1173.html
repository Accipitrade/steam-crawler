\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is great fun, amazingly atmospheric and completely not for me.

I am glad this game exists because fast-paced kill a minute thrills should exist, and if I didn't get so motion sick off the game I'd love every second of it. I can really only deal with ten to twenty minutes at a time or about three areas I'd say. Which is fine for me but I don't know how it will help you. If there was a way to fix the anti-aliasing maybe I could play it longer, but at the same time I can't deny it looks fantastic.

Every couple of minutes I feel like a brainlet for getting lost but after finding where I'm supposed to go I feel like they aren't design problems so much as my own intoxication or stupidity. The game isn't "idiot-proof" but it is "idiot-resistant," and as shooters go it's much better than your standard fare with every weapon feeling visceral and every action feeling like a form of accomplishment (even if silly in retrospective.)

8 out of 10 for me but the average gamer with love this as a 9 out of 10, or even a 10 out of 10 if they have no sense honesty.\t\t\t\t\t
