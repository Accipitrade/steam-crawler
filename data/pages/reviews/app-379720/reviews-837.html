\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game so f*cking good, not only did it bring the long dormant Doom franchise back into the stratosphere of shooters, it was also a giant middle finger to triple A shooter design and how it went against everything that was the norm at the time. There's no taking cover, reloading, two weapon limit or ads'ing in this game, due to this, the combat never slows down and you're never given a chance to breathe until all your enemies in a combat arena are dead. Fast movement, quick reflexes, enemy prioritization and knowing what weapon is best used on what enemy is the name of the game. It even took the rip and tear line from the meme-tastic doom comic and ran with it as the tagline for the new doom games and made the slayer into one of the most badass and wholesome characters in recent video game memory. It's not as adrenaline pumping, or intense as doom eternal but the best shooter of 2016 layed the foundation of what went on to be the best shooter of 2020.\t\t\t\t\t
