Once, we reveled in our dominance over countless dimensions, tormenting souls and spreading fear. But when the Doom Marine descended upon our realm, our arrogance crumbled before his relentless onslaught. His arrival signaled an age of unyielding retribution for our wicked deeds.

The cacophony of gunfire, the screams of the dying, and the thunderous explosions became the soundtrack of our demise.

We watched in horror as our towering monstrosities crumbled beneath the Marine's unrelenting onslaught. His determination was unwavering, his resolve unbreakable. We could not comprehend his insatiable thirst for our destruction, as he dispatched our brethren with ruthless efficiency.

Even the infernal lords, our most powerful and revered leaders, fell before the Marine's relentless pursuit. Our strongholds crumbled, our fortifications shattered, and our reign of terror was shattered. The game world became a graveyard, adorned with the lifeless bodies of our fallen kin.

In the end, it was the Doom Marine who stood triumphant, having carved a path of carnage through the very heart of our dominion. We demons cowered before his wrath, our dominion reduced to ashes. The game had become a testament to our defeat, a reminder of the indomitable spirit of humanity.

So, from the perspective of a demon, the Doom 1993 Classic Game was a cataclysmic revelation, a reckoning that shattered our dominion and forced us to witness the end of an era.
