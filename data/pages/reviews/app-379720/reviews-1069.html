The gameplay is where Doom shines, if you are looking for a deep story experience I'm afraid you will have to look elsewhere. The multiplayer is also pretty good but it is hard to find games later in the day.
The only thing I really dislike about Doom is how it bugs out almost every time I launch it. It changes my resolution to something abominable like 480p and often not even on my primary monitor. It is however, easy to temporarily fix. Just navigate to Campaign->Settings->Resolution (and monitor if you have more than one) and change it to your normal resolution.\t\t\t\t\t
