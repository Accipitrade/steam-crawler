\r\n\t\t\t\t\t+Highly dynamic combat that gets you circlestrafing, switching weapons, backpedaling, and rushing to every corner of the combat area.
+Light RPG progression through weapon upgrade points, health/armor/ammo upgrades, and perk-esque runes. All acquired through exploring the levels.
+Levels are loaded with branching paths, arenas, and hidey holes. Secret-hunting is well-rewarded with item stockpiles, collectables, upgrades, and even early access to weapons. Some areas will cut you off from backtracking though, which can put a damper on secret-hunting.
+Not a survival horror. Unless you're a demon.
+Glory Kills (aka killmoves/finishers) were a sketchy concept to me, but the implementation is decent. The animations are very short, and add to the gameplay by rewarding aggressive playstyle with quick kills and small piles of health pickups. Also, more than violent enough to scare your mom.

=Multiplayer's alright. Didn't play much of it, but It's Quake/UT-style arenas and weapons, with CoD-style loadout system, experience and levelling. Oh, and powerups that turn you into demons. Bots are only available for basic gamemodes, and don't give any progression, so socialize or screw off.
=Custom maps are eh. There's some good stuff in there, but seperating the wheat from the chaff is a bit of a pain. I haven't checked out the level editor. But judging from some of the maps available, the scripting/logic is pretty robust. Also, the only co-op gameplay is through custom maps.

So hell yeah, more of this please.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAmazing game!
So far I've played 5-6 hours on it and I'm hook. To be fair I was hooked from the first level. Great on my hardware, the cpu and gpu don't even break a sweat while playing this game which is great, I do wish more modern day games would follow DOOM's example of great optimisation for Vulkan, as far as I can see this helps most systems a lot.

Pros:
Great soundtrack (for those who love metal)
Fast, very fast pace
Plays great on pretty low end hardware with vulcan and OpenGL

Cons:
Non so far, though I haven't tried the european multiplayer server yet.

Just as a small note, you can't take this game like most modern day fps games, where you can slowly inch forward picking off enemies at a distance, if you enjoy playing games like that then DOOM 2016 is not for you. To truely enjoy DOOM you need to go in strategically guns ablazing.

Hardware used:
CPU - FX8350
GPU - Gigabyte GTX 1050 Ti Windforce OC 4Gb
Ram - 8Gb\t\t\t\t\t
