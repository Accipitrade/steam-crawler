\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe 2016 version of Doom has everything this boomer wants in the genre. Well, so did the 1993 version, but that's the point. Imagine if the genre continued to evolve on the Doom/Blood/Quake/Duke style instead of taking this huge left turn into cramped, linear hallways and overdramatic storytelling. What would you end up with? Fast paced action, huge levels that encourage and reward exploration, beautiful graphics, super sharp mechanics, and tons of kickass weapons. Doom has all of this. The combat mechanics encourage constant movement, use of all weapons, mixing up-close and far away combat depending on who you are fighting, and quick thinking to manage your health - this is top notch stuff and a masterclass of FPS design.

Now, the storytelling is there but mostly in the background to be ignored if you want (although it's actually pretty good IMO). Doom 2016 also has an upgrade system to augment weapons and suit characteristics, and it works pretty well especially how it ties into the exploration of the levels. The entire game just plays like it was designed by people who love the genre and wanted to deliver a kickass game. They did, this is one of the FPS games ever.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Doom Marine returns in this- *speaks with demonic tone* EPIC METAL REBOOT of the cult classic video game series: DOOM. As the band Dethklok from Metalocalypse says: "That's so metal".

Premise

You are the fable "Doom Marine" and the UAC Mars facility has been overrun by Hell itself. Run and gun through beautiful breathtaking levels filled with demons of all sizes and secrets large and small as you try to contain the forces of Hell.

Gameplay

To compare it to Doom 3 is like comparing night & day. DOOM 2016 feels totally different while staying true to the core themes of the franchise; Run and gun demons from Hell and slay them with satisfying executions (albeit a bit repetitive animations). The gunplay is fast pace and you feel like a supreme badass from start to finish. Not one moment goes dull as you play this game.

There is a multiplayer mode; while fun- in the grand scheme of things your not missing out much.

Lastly there are challenges for you to do and upgrades for your large arsenal and for the Doom Marine. And of course, secrets.

SnapMap

SnapMap is a tool that let's you create your own maps to share with the multiplayer community. And honestly? It's a fun little thing they got going with that feature; seeing all the cool maps coming out of there is fantastic.

Audio/Visuals

The fact that the opening credit sequence has metal music playing with the shotgun being timed to the final beat as you exit the elevator topside. You know you are in for a thrill ride from start to finish. The visuals and the audio backs this up so freaking much because ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ is this game ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ beautiful. The level of detail is insane! You just want to explore every nook and cram and just really get in on those close ups of the demons large and/or small. The developers did a fantastic job

And finally the soundtrack; If you don't own this soundtrack you are doing a HUGE disservice.

Outro

DOOM 2016 is one of the most successful relaunches of a cult classic video game series and with the sequel coming this November; best get in on this incredible game now.\t\t\t\t\t
