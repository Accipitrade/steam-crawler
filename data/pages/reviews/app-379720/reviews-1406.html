\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game FEELS like a video game. An old one, more specifically. Not some manipulative sleazy con like most games today. It FEELS like they put a ♥♥♥♥ ton of effort into the game. A ♥♥♥♥ TON.

Not only does it mix up the FPS style we have gotten too used to from call of duty, but it brings in old elements like picking up armor and unique level design.

The game literally encourages exploration. It literally tells you and shows you that there are secrets, and if you get close enough, where they are. BUT only on the map. You have to figure out how to get there. It uses all the lessons learned from map design from old school games but with HD graphics.

The storyline isnt played out entirely in dialogue. There is some here and there, the cutscenes are either in person or experienced via a hologram to explain an environment. But a lot are in the tutorials or loading screens for new levels.

*SCREECH* WHAT NO STORY??

No, i didnt say that there isnt story. There is. The tutorial actually is very useful and gives a lot of info. some info is also hidden too. So its not like Destiny where the entire story is online in the stupid collectible things (that are not on the site anymore). Its extra stuff.

Theres SO much encouragement to explore and collect the extra stuff, its bananas.

and the multiplayer OMG its so fleshed out. Im still getting used to it and the leveling system, but its a mixture of casual and competitive. People are 1337 on there, thats for sure. but its not about noscopes and quick trigger fingers. Its more about running and movement and fun.

This is a seriously refreshing recharge of the FPS genre.

and collectible toys? Thats ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ bananas too.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is sooo bo-o-oring. I played it two years back and couldn't finish. Now Eternal released and I wanted to recall why I haven't finished this Doom. Now I understood.
The levels, the actions everything you do is so similar all the time there is absolutely no diversity. Jump up the cliff, jump down the cliff defeat monsters, go to next cliff. That's all. Story is boring it is absolutely not engaging. Equipment upgrades are not interesting. Those challenges everywhere. Challenge to get this, challenge to get that, too many challenges.
Most awful soundtrack I ever heard.
The only thing I must admit - is a great optimization.
Played two hours today and deleting again. I think I need to put a mark somewhere - do not play in this again.
Of course there is no talk about buying Eternal. This Doom franchise is over for me.\t\t\t\t\t
