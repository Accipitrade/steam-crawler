\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM is a very well polished games that not only is well optimised for PC but very well planned out and executed. The single player is particularly well planned out as upgrades for equiptment are handled nicely and their are just enough unlocks to maintain a steady leveling process throughout. Most importantly it just continues to be fun as you upgrade weapons to unleash more powerful effects with extremely gorey consequences. The game is essentially a collection of corridors linking together arena's of carnage which at first sound repetative but ultimately doesn't feel that way. On the contrary, DOOM continues to excite throughout.

The multiplayer does struggle a little with it's identity but ultimately has lots of interesting content ans some very cool armour sets to be earned. Snap map is a brilliant addition and has a lot more going on than you might expect before trying it out. Ultimately however the game is at it's strongest by far in it's SP mode.\t\t\t\t\t
