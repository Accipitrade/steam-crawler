\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOG Doom was one of the very fist PC games i ever played and this has to be one of the greatest action FPS games I have ever played. You've been promoted from Doom Marine to Doom Slayer in this epic crucible of vengeance, carnage, and the godlike soundtrack from the legendary Mick Gordon.

The Good...

- The story 7.5/10 - is actually good and the fact they even managed put together such a tale is impressive in it's own right because whose playing doom for the plot anyway lol.

- The graphics 8/10 - aren't anything too special but are more than strong enough to hold up as 2016 AAA level and you'll be moving so much & so often that it wont matter especially on higher difficulties and even when you're looking for secrets after you've cleared the level.

- The Soundtrack 10/10 - literally hits you with adrenaline and fury. I have been off my metal days for many years and this one game threw me right back into the seas of rage. if you don't feel the power of a god flood your body when BFG Division comes on then you are too squishy to play this game.

- The game play 9/10 - is fast, ferocious, and fun when coupled with the glory kill mechanic making a completely visceral and empowering experience. weather its bullets, blasters, or your bare hands, you'll rip and tear until it is done. Also, The simplicity of the mechanics and objectives fits perfectly in any Doom game because Doom 2016 is all about killing demons in brutal but satisfying fashion.

- The Doom Slayer BFG1000/10 - Silent but deadly, you are the lone Doom Slayer, the one truly effective weapon against the hell spawn humanity had ever produced. scarred from perpetual torment and cursed with endless rage you will raze the pits of hell until they are no more. The Slayer Makes Master Chief look like Buzz Lightyear.

The Bad...

- Secrets 6/10 - Although it is nice to see homage being paid to the OG Doom titles the old school maps aren't that exciting and don't offer little more than a brief moment of nostalgia. also the collectibles are not as bad but again they don't offer much in terms of enjoyment.

- Upgrades 6/10 - There are some legit upgrades in the game but not all of them are good or useful for that matter, half of the runes are pointless, half of the weapon mods are lame, and the praetor suit upgrades are too basic.

- Inadequate Seizure warning 3/10 - They have one appear on screen in multiple languages when you boot up the game and that isn't nearly enough to warn you of the frickin laser & pyrotechnic display they throw at you for the entire length of the game. Even if You have never had a Seizure i am certain this could give you one.
