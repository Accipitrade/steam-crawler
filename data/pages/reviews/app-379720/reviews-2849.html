The graphics are amazing. The action is VERY fast and furious. The gore is quite entertaining. The levels are beautiful and sprawling in scope. There are constantly changing achievements to unlock, including special kills and lots of hidden stuff in each level, which I really like as it gives me more to do than just "shoot everything that moves". The whole experience is as badass and intense as any I've ever had from any videogame. Playing - or even just watching a friend play - always gets my pulse racing, my heart pounding. It's a crazy adrenaline ride.
As I said, currently my favorite shooter of all time.

Although the campaign is just a *bit* short, after I completed it I got sucked into Snapmap - an ingenious level editing tool that allows you to design your own levels and edit game logic in *tremendous* detail. There are thousands (!) of community-made levels to play for free, you can download them and then edit them yourself if you want. This makes the gameplay content virtually infinite. Always a new map to play online... always a new challenge.

This game is the tits!!!\t\t\t\t\t
