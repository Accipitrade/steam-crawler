\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely amazing game. One of my favorite shooters, the game is just a non-stop adrenaline fest. Even as someone who likes well-paced story-based games, role-playing games, and open-world games which aren't singularly focused, DOOM's singular focus on its combat means the game is exhilarating to play at all times. The weapon sandbox and variety of enemies is excellent, and the game's design to force the player into killing things at all times is what sets DOOM apart from so many other shooters.

One thing to be aware of, however. This game can be exhausting to play. This might be down to me being autistic and thus having greater sensory sensitivity, but compared to the likes of any Halo game (besides 5 which I haven't played), Titanfall 2, and Half-Life, I had to take way more breaks while playing DOOM than either of the above games. The exhilarating nature of the game I mentioned above is a double-edged sword, at least for me. This by no means is a massive flaw of the game, but it's something that I think is worth noting.\t\t\t\t\t
