\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of my all time favourite games. It is pure action from the very start, and it does respect your intelligence as a player. The game does not hold your hand with long tutorials, instead you are tossed into situations where you have to put what you learned to the test. The very first room of imps will teach you that this game does F**k around. Games like Cod do have their place, but it has become a generic trope among FPS games. Doom forces you to really get out of your comfortzone and be relentless and dive into the action.

Gameplay footage does not make the combat just, if it looks like a mindless slaughter it is because there is skill involved. You die in seconds if you mess up, and even the first enemy will be a real threat for the rest of the game. I could praise doom all die, but short story: gameplay is amazing, music is Godlike, story is actually really interesting and clever, but you can ignore it. A solid 10/10 from me.
