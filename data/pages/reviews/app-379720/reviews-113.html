\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"Against all the evil that Hell can conjure, all the wickedness that mankind can produce, we will send unto them... only you. Rip and tear, until it is done" is the quote used at the beginning of this game and oh boy does it explain it all. This game is a extremely good experience if you love the sight of demonic creatures, blood, gore, and fast paced shooting and cutting.

This game although all of its controversy is definitely a must play from players of the Doom series and just people who enjoy and good old fun first person shooter. It has various weapons that all have their own charm (if you could even use such a word in this kind of experience) and the glory kills just make you feel like you have a power above all others. This overall just gives you a enjoyable experience.

Now to talk about the story, which may not the best thing in the world... well it is nowhere near the worst. The story gets where it is going at a pace that somewhat matches the game-play that you will partake in during it whilst also feeling like it is just lacking that little something. So if you are purchasing the game for a story rich experience probably steer away from it.

Another part of the game that gives it it's own unique feel is that head bopping music that plays throughout everything that you do. This music truly makes you feel like an unstoppable figure. Mick Gordon did a wonderful job whilst composing this soundtrack and stating it once again it is just amazing.

Overall, this is a very solid game produced by Bethesda and id Software with fun to play around with mechanics, well balanced game-play and a story that comes along as an extra feature placed on top. So if you feel like ripping and tearing why not give Doom (2016) a try.\t\t\t\t\t
