\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI must say the Singleplayer is amazing, I played it on the easiest setting. It took me about 8 hours to complete. The story is ... well... LoL but it is only there to give you a reason to keep shooting things. This is non stop splatter exploding mayham. It feels so much like a 90s game with updates to bring it into a modern game time. No reload and mass damage, no health regen, no real puzzels, no feels. Just kill and murder.

--- MUSIC ----
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, the music makes you want to kill more and more. It is so perfect. I usually do not listen to heavy rock like that. But playing DOOM it just feels right and perfect.

The voices too are amazing, and with good headphones. Damn the audio experience is great!!!

There are upgrades to weapons and the suit, they are suttle but fun to change it up a bit. Mostly to increase the damage you are doing. You end up doing close combat the most, because its fun and satisfying. After doing "Glory Kills" (when you do a finishing move) 1000x you still dont get sick of it. You tend to watch to not shoot the demon to death, just so you can do a finish move.

Granted, they give you health and armor and even ammo when you do them. Depending on what upgrades you use.

But it makes you feel badass and like a tank.

The Bosses are insanely difficuilt at first, at least for me, though I did kill each one right away. Yet, that was on easy setting and just in the nick of time, health, ammo.
-- Note that is my personal opinion, because I never liked boss fights.

MULTIPLAYER:
Not much to say... its, well... meh
Personally, I think I am not a fan of the MP because you use upgrades and mods like in COD or something. I rather just shoot and not think about specials someone is using. It is more fun based on pure skill and chaos. I end up in games where I might be 3rd place, but 1st has 3x the kills and only half the death. Just because they are a high level with all these perks.
Not my thing.
