It has a simple story that ends on a cliffhanger.
You've been awoken from stasis to kill demons.
It does not really go into the past events much.

In my eyes, Doom 64 (1997), came before this.
Story-wise it would truly make the most sense.
So yeah, visuals from this reboot are beautiful.
The music is a masterpiece, truly just amazing.
I enjoyed the environments, very good details.
Gameplay probably couldn't have been better.
It just felt, like the natural evolution of DOOM.
The new mechanics, such as parkour, are fine.
I truly enjoyed exploring through all the maps.
Finding the collectibles and such was just fun.
A bit slow at times but overall truly enjoyable.
It could've been way worse, so I'm just happy.
Much better than the Doom 3 & Resurrection.
At least in my opinion, It's just a great reboot.
I have to give this my honest, positive review.\t\t\t\t\t
