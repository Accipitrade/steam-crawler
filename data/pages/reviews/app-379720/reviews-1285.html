DOOM (2016) is a 91/100

This iteration of DOOM is the greatest FPS to date. Fast action, unique and powerful weapons, and interesting arenas abound as the the return of DOOM surely deserves. In terms of positive facets there are so many I have trouble itemizing them. The combat is fun, the enemies are well designed, the arenas are of the appropriate size to offer challenge and fun in the same instance. Even the rune system is so well crafted as to give the player everything they may want. A melee and BFG only run? Why not?!

The few demerits are the later stages (think Wraith Lords) are a bit samey and a few areas after the tram are a little constricted. The secrets are kind of hard to find in a few places but I won't spoil any of them here. All in all it'll blend into a 'whatever, this is great' for most people.

It's most everything DOOM Eternal was not, a well thought out shooter which rewards quick reaction time and good decisions but does not demand either (at least short of Nightmare).

DOOM is worth your money
DOOM is worth your time
Give it a try, it's only $20\t\t\t\t\t
