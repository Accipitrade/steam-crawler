\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLove this game. The campaign is awesome, extremely fun, and truly gorey as one would expect with a Doom game.

The multiplayer is pretty good, it's what I play most and I have a lot of fun with it. It does seem that players tend to lean more toward the Team Deathmatch game mode and sometimes it's harder to find players playing the other game modes, which kinda sucks because the other modes are fun as hell. Capture the flag with a moving capture point, soul harvest where there is always a demon and you try to collect the most souls of killed enemies, and the domination style map with three "zones" that need to be in your team's control for a set period of time, all super fun.

The customization is pretty good too with a lot of different armor types and colors/patterns to use as well as weapon camo and colors. Overall, I would recommend this game to anyone who enjoys FPS multiplayer games or just loves the Doom franchise.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter Doom 3, I started to wonder and had many hopes for new game in the franchise and after couple of years we got "DOOM (2016 version)" and it's awesome
The campaign is soild,well made and very challenging especially if you decide to play on "Ultra-Violence" and above, The gameplay is similar to the older games (Doom 1/2/Final Doom) but significantly improved to the modern era such as customization for example and the soundtrack? AMAZING
I decided to try the Multiplayer and it was quite fun and personally I just love arena-style combat like in Unreal Tournament and Quake but I still feeling that something is missing compared to other games that based on the same type, For example when I played UT4 even though it's still on Pre-Alpha stage it still feels more appealing, It's not too bad but you would enjoy it for a short period of time
Snapmap : So I decided to check out that mode and see how it goes and to be honest, I'm suprised. As you know, Today modern games lack the option to let you to play on custom maps but here? I completely enjoyed from it and browsing around the maps just to find more and more campaign maps that worth to try, It really adds more value to the game, It's not the same experience as Doom 2 (1994) and source ports like Zandronum offers but indeed can be considered as a big step forward
Well it's going to be weird but I have to talk about the optimization, Since it seems many games doesn't have it anymore but Doom? Ohh finally an optimized game, It runs flawlessly on my PC without any issues or FPS drops and also on PC's that below the system requirements the game works well and don't forgot Vulkan support that also can boost your framerates (I personally got 20FPS boost with Vulkan - Even more when NVIDIA improved their Vulkan support in their latest drivers overtime)
But the two last boss battles in game can be too hard sometimes in the higher difficulty levels since you going to die pretty fast due to the leak of health

Overall rating : 9.2/10
Keep in mind that's the first real review I wrote and hope to improve, I know that my English is pretty terrible since it's not my native language but trying to improve it overtime
