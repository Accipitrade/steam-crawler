\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had this on my "not interested" list, but in a sale I finally just gave it a go......and its one of the best shooters I have played.

Pros:

+ the graphics are great, it looks so good
+ super smooth performance
+ the level and art design is superb, the hellish look, its amazing
+ so much detail in every model, decorative corpses look gruesome and detailed
+ the soundtrack is one of the best I have heard (I love metal music)
+ all the SFX are fantastic
+ a lot of references to the DOOM franchise and previous games
+ easter eggs that are clever and funny
+ a lot of secrets to discover in general, not only easter eggs
+ even the collectibles are fun to collect
+ fast combat, a lot of action
+ controls are fantastic, even with a controller it feels great to play
+ weapons are sweet and you are going to use all of them, I liked every single weapon in the game
+ upgrade weapons with additional abilities and gadgets
+ upgrade your armour suit
+ gain new abilities during the campaign
+ full singleplayer campaign with very good voice acting
+ a lot of checkpoints during the level
+ a 3D map to navigate
+ optional challenges on every level
+ levels have a lot of variety
+ plattform sections are enjoyable
+ various enemies that have unique behaviour
+ cool boss battles
+ BFG
+ the story is not that important, its about the fun in shooting stuff and the game fully acknowledges that
+ temporarily power ups
+ glory-kills, melee kills that you need to do to regain some health, a very good mechanic in my opinion
+ multiplayer
+ snap map, an easy to use map editor in which you can snap pre-build map blocks together to make your own map
+ arcade mode
+ play as a demon in multiplayer

Cons:

- I can see why people would not like the game as much, as it is a series of enemy-waves linked together, making it very predictible and monotonous


I loved the gunplay, it just feels good to shoot at things. It is more of a wave shooter, but its one of the best wave shooters out there, you have to traverse the map like in no other game and the battles are fast and action packed. It wasnt as monotonous to me, because I wanted to test out the new upgrades that you get frequently, keeping the fights fresh with additional upgrades and gadgets. Definitely recommend to fans of FPS.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is a decent game, though it's a bit of a upwards-downwards journey.
It's ALL about the action on screen. And I do mean ALL. The story is virtually non-existent.
There is an attempt of sorts, but it falls into a category of 10 year old writing a sci-fi after playing through the original DOOM. And if you want to get even that you have to do it yourself - scavenge notes and manually read through pages of fluff text. But there is no one to blaem there, as to expect a coherent story from a DOOM game, well, no one considers it even.

The gunplay is really just that: no relevant strategies, no cover shooting, just run-and-gun. And tbh it's the game's forte among the lines of Serious Sam and Wolfenstein (I will come back that one in a moment). But that sadly you really have to span it out to short play periods as the brain quickly notices the repetition: came into a room, ran around, killed everyone, next room. Rinse. Repeat.
Don't get me wrong it's fun, it's gory, it's SATISFYING as all HELL (wink wink, anyone?), but after you quench your daily thirst of mayhem, it's lacklustrer as it's the core gameplay element.
Last boss was a complete let down. Almost worth a not recomended in of itself.
The collectibles are a nice touch but, really, just prolongs your experience with an hour or two of unnecerssary prop-hunt if you're a completionist like me.

So, final verdict, it's a decent game, awesomely satisfying in dosages of 30min\\a day, as prescribed by Dr. Vee. Other than that - MEDIOCRE. I would suggest picking up Wolfenstein: New Order (created and released by the same company) or maybe even the newly announced Wolfenstein II: The New Colossus (finger's crossed for that one) for a truly awesomeness like you never thought would be possible done with a FPS game (the final boss still was a letdown, but the whole package just tickles my fancy that much).\t\t\t\t\t
