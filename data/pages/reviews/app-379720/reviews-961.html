The pacing of the game starts up slow, so if you are discouraged by the early cinematics and lack of fire power, keep playing. The game really picks up after 3-4 hours where you get access to the more interesting, definitely imbalanced weapons.

However, the exploration elements aren't the main appeal of the game.
