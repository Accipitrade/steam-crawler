iT'S FUN. Wish it were more challenging though.

Gameplay is well paced so far.
No obnoxious respawns- you can clear an area, and then track down secrets.
No regen!!

Grenades somehow recharge?? Total wtf.
Enemies explode into health... Not sure how I feel on this.
Wish there was a mini-map upgrade/unlock option honestly, would make it easier to track down the collectibles.
Upgrade system kinda requires finding the (often easy, sometimes wtf to find) collectibles\t\t\t\t\t
