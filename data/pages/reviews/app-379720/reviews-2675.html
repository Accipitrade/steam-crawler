\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is a lot of kill satisfaction. I was a bit disappointed that it wasn't quite as spooky as the original Doom or Doom 3, but the gameplay is very smooth and the combat is well done. You're forced to stay on the move, and your weapon loadout is actually relevant, as different weapons can be more effective on different monsters. The secrets and easter eggs are great. The story is meh, so if you're looking for an action-packed game with an engaging story go play Undertale.

My only gripes with the game are as follows:
- The clipping can be pretty bad. For a game that's been out this long, I'm surprised it's as bad as it is. There were a handful of times where I fell through the floor and died, or got stuck on a stair.
- Difficulty spikes. A lot of this game is fighting waves of monsters until you've killed them all. Sometimes those waves are much more difficult than others.
- Infinite. F*cking. Imps. In some waves, no matter how many Imps you kill, they keep coming unless you kill the bigger monsters. This can be problematic at times, as you can't exactly just ignore them or they'll kill you. I guess that could be a pro. But damn it pissed me off sometimes.\t\t\t\t\t
