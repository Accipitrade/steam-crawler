\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust finished DOOM after 18 hours and I'd say its not bad. Note: I didnt play the original game in 1993 so this wont be one of those biased reviews. The games appreciation comes from its straight up no BS style of play. I liked the game and couldnt help but cheer these devs on to keep it up as I was ripping my way through hell. I think thats what a lot of people felt even though the game can get overhyped.

The story was ok, the level design was medium, and the combat was badass. Its like Halo meets hell. I'd recommend picking this game up for its current asking price ($30 I think) as its a fun ride. If youre like me, you dont really like the big games because theyre trying to please everyone. DOOMs not about that and keeps it real. I respect that. I look forward to picking up Eternal when it comes out in a couple months. Im not saying make it full fledged rp but....spice up the story some. I liked the in-game tid bits about the Doom Slayer and would like to learn more. Oh yeah, great easter eggs too.\t\t\t\t\t
