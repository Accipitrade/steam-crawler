\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom II, back in 1992 or so, was the first FPS I ever played. Back then, connecting via my flying 14.4 modem was the highlight of the day, and running about with several friends killing one another, or the fiends of hell, was the highlight of the day.

Doom 3 was my next foray into Doom, and I hated it: The feel was completely wrong, and I don't think I ever got beyond the second level. Maybe I quit too soon... but Doom 2016 is the old feel of Doom II, updated and just as much fun. Challenging, but with the same "kill all the things" feel of the original game.

Layered on it is a great story, with wonderful voice work and well done characters. Definitely the best game in the Doom franchise (I'll be trying Doom Eternal next...) and highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
