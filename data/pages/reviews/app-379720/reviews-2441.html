\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall a pretty fun game.

I played the singleplayer on the hardest difficulty available to me when I bought it (Ultra-Violence) and it lasted around 10-12 hours. The game was still rather easy though, as it pretty much litters you with health/armor, ammo and powerups on every encounter. I felt pretty safe by just strafing, jumping and shooting all around. Nevertheless, the gunplay is satisfying and most of the maps are actually pretty fun to explore.

The Multiplayer is ok but didn't really hold my attention for too long. I'd rather play Quake or UT than this but for what it's worth, it's not terrible.

Overall, it's one of the more fun singleplayer FPS games to come out in recent years.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI truly believe that pc is the way to best experience this game. I denied myself the privilage to play this masterpiece until I got a pc that would allow me to run this game in 1080p, ultra graphics, and over 60 fps. After achieveing this, it took a measly 30 minutes to secure this game as one of my all time favorites. This game is a glorious statement that gives the middle finger to modern shooters to bless us with the blood-soaked exciting fun of the 90s that DOOM and DOOM II did so well. The glorykill system is incredible and made me jizz every time I initiated one. The entire game is beautiful, exilerating, rarely stopping to give the player a breather. The enviorments are convincing and emersive, the graphics are simply magnificent, and the enemies and boss battles are all balanced and challenging, each with their own styles and movesets that you must account for and adjust to in order to survive. Multiplayer is tight and balanced really well. Snapmaps are an endless well of creativity and fun.
Overall, this game is no doubt one of the best shooters of all time and is criminally underrated.
Highly recommended.
11/10. Well done ID software. Well done.\t\t\t\t\t
