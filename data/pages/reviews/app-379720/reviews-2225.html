\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM.

I played the original DOOM as a demo on a 3.5" floppy that was passed around my middle school like a cheap porn found in the woods. Every kid played the hell out of that demo, and it was the best. Gave chills. Made you jump. Nothing like it back then, a real work of genius.

This is about a close as it gets. This game is amazing, one of the best single-player FPS experiences I have had in years. Mic Gordon's soundtrack is a daily spotify listen, the energy was insane, and I loved the hell out of it. The little "doom guy" dolls were fun to find and fun to interact with, otherwise, it was the best Death Metal Slaughter I have experienced. Each level was a blessed mix of the old Heavy Metal magazine and pure violence set to a masterpiece guitar symphony.

This game is a must play.

Oh, I heard they had multiplayer too. I tried it once while doing an Extra Life 24 Hour Marathon. It was meh. Just meh. I pretend it does not blemish the perfection of the single player experience by existing, I suggest you do the same.

10/10 will DOOM again.
