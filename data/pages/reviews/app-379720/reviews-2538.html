Very fast paced, ultra-violent game with smooth movement. While Doom 3 was a scary game, in this new "Doom" its the game that is scared of you.
Ideal for venting frustrations and unleashing your murderous rage.

The game however contains a stupid system of unlockables to improve your weapons as you find secrets and complete certain gameplay tasks. This certainly breaks up the fast pace and slows the game down to a crawl, specially if you have OCD, and by the time you have unlocked it all, the game is pretty much over. It's one of those jarring mechanics that are in direct conflict with the rest of the game.

TL;DR
Great game.
-2 Points for the progression BS.
