\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNOTE: I only played this on Campaign. I don't like multiplayer games.

MY THOUGHTS

I played the original Doom, Wolfenstein, etc. when I was a kid. They were fun and intense for the time.

Now I'm quite a bit older and, frankly, I'm just not as into the whole "blood and guts", high-intensity, "kill em all and let God sort them out" games anymore.

HOWEVER, if you REALLY like that kind of game, then this is the game for you. There are plenty of opportunities to FUBAR the demons, etc. It is intense, the story is consistent, the grind is minimal, and it is entertaining. The keyboard/mouse controls are excellent is mobility and your options for gunplay. I can see why so many like it.

But, it isn't my kind of game. I'm more of a "sniper" player.

CONCLUSION

Anyway, for the genre, I give it a 9+. For the kind of games I like to play, I give it a 6. It was worth buying at 50%+ off, but I wouldn't pay full price unless you are a REAL die-hard fan of the Doom series.

And, no, I won't be moving on to Doom Eternal.

PS. Here's a hint. If you haven't heard anything about this game, FIND ALL of the Secrets, etc because it will really help you progress (there are plenty of YouTube vids to help you find/get to all of them). Also, you can go back to earlier missions to help you max out your weapons. It can be a lot easier to get the special kills needed for a max out.

PPS. Don't waste your time trying to max out your pistol. It is almost worthless after the first mission.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst, I want to say that I have been a gamer for many years but as I was growing up in the 90s, Doom was not approved by my folks so I never played the original that much. This review is not written by someone who played the original game and drank the kool-aid surrounding the popularity. That being said, I have played other FPS games, but it is not my absolute favorite genre.

This game was quite fun especially after getting a few of the guns. I did like how they gave the pistol a charge to the shot though, making it slightly more useful. The levels are designed to encourage some exploring without it being too difficult to find everything. The upgrade system has a few interesting alt fire modes and the upgrades are a mix of level achievements and finding secrets in each level.

The guns are unique--most of them match guns I have seen in other games. When unlocked, each gun has 2 alt modes, usually one for heavy damage and another for crowd control. This game has a glory kill mechanic where you can stun enemies and to a melee kill. The enemy is instantly killed and the player gets health or ammo or a mix of both. Ammo spews out if you get a chainsaw kill.

The game's story is okay. You basically uncover a lot of text and have vocal scenes where you talk to scientists about all the stuff they did on mars that made the demons show up--it's not Shakespeare but the original game was not plot heavy itself. The graphics are great for the engine they used and it ran fine on my mid-level powered rig. I can see this working fine on older systems if you turn things to medium or so.

I recommend this game to anyone who is curious or wants a good time with an action fest like this. The only downside is the gore factor is high. No sex, but there are a couple nude images in the game too. Make sure you are screening for age appropriateness.

The only real negative is that the single player game itself has 13 levels and by level 9 or so, you have all the guns and you've seen all the monsters. The remaining levels are okay, but at that point, it feels like a chore. After some time, the novelty or gore and horror wear off and you just want to see the end. I had fun, but it seemed to be repetitive near the end, so I finished and was happy to be done. The other difficulties ramp it up pretty well though. If you feel bored, crank it up.

Multiplayer is a bit dead but the map maker is decent for what it offers and I see a number of available mods as well.

At it's current price, pick it up if shooters are your thing, but this is not Call of Duty. This game is NOT KID FRIENDLY. Again, please be aware. It's fun but repetitive after a while but also not too long. Nice in my book.\t\t\t\t\t
