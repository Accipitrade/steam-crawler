\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt is a bloody good time. Unlike most modern shooters it consistently locks you in a room full of blood and chaos and forces you to murder everything and stay alive. Not the pop out of cover/headshot/regenerating health generic stuff most games put out nowadays.

The guns are excellent. Especially the good old shotgun. ID found away to give you three fun options in the super shotgun and the two sub versions of the combat shotgun. The BGF and Chainsaw gave me all of the nostalgia feels.

Fast paced, heavy metal, ridiculous story, surprisingly good platforming to find secrets and the level design overall is excellent. I thought the game menu map sucked but that is literally my only gripe. This game brought me back to my PC gaming roots and I highly recommend it. Especially if anyone played the originals.

Overall 9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
