-Fun from start to finish
-Decent variation in level design
-Bosses are fun
-Good graphics and framerate
-Good music
CONS:
-Weapons are mostly boring until you unlock mods for them
-RPG-style upgrade system in a 10 hour FPS
-It's 10 hours long
-Too much backtracking in such a short game
-Multiplayer sucks
-Takes up 75GB for some reason

If it's on sale for 66% off or more, or the price drops, then I'd recommend getting it. That's what I did. Even though it's a short game, I prefer games that are short and action packed, rather than artifically long and stuffed with boring filler (Metal Gear Rising vs MGSV for example). It's not a "must-have" by any means but it is a good timesink for a few hours.
