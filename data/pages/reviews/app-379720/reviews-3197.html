\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSimply put this is a very good game, I was able to 100% the game in under 60 hours; however, I did not personally have a preference to multiplayer. The multiplayer aspect -- be it snapmaps or the actual deathmatches -- is obviously a staple part in this game and for people looking for that type of play this game is a must have. I would highly recommend it still for those wanting a great single player campaign. I never had one moment where the single player campaign was not fun, and the collectibles within the game made it necesarry to replay levels, but it was never dry repetitive gameplay. To conclude, if you are wanting a great single player campaign while looking for a new fps for online play, or a map creator with the snapmaps Doom is for you, and it's worth picking up at full cost. If you're not so focused on the multiplayer fps, or the map creation aspect then the game is still one worth playing, however with a complete run through at 34.7hrs it would be hard to justify spending the whole 60 USD on the game. Still a fun experience that if possible everyone should get the chance to at least experience.\t\t\t\t\t
