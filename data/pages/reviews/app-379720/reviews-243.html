\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's hard to dislike Doom 2016.

After nearly 27 hours of gameplay, I have decided that Doom 2016 is a good game. The graphics are excellent for 2016 standards, the gameplay is consistently fun, and the music is fitting for the atmosphere and action. The enemy designs, weapons, movement, and new mechanics are all great.

However, not every aspect of Doom 2016 is perfect. I do not think that the game's story was very interesting, but I understand that gameplay was id's primary focus. I also may be one of few people to say this, but I encountered a few odd bugs and glitches while playing the game- none of which were performance related (the game runs buttery smooth on my higher-end PC). To quickly name a few, enemies would sometimes get stuck in walls becoming unkillable, I would fall through the map after a glory kill, and after replaying a level to collect a missed rune, I accidentally exited the level, making it impossible to complete one of that stage's challenges. These are quite trivial issues, but I found them notable nonetheless.

This was just my experience. Yours may differ greatly.

If you're not sure about buying Doom 2016 or Doom Eternal, I couldn't recommend Doom 2016 more strongly. The game is commonly on sale, for about $6 or less these days. Good luck, and keep killing demons. :)\t\t\t\t\t
