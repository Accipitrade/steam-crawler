The amount of detail and work put into this game is unspeakable, if you are looking for a high intensive adrenaline rushing experience this is the game for you. The quick-paced game-play gives an amazing rush; the game has an insane amount of things to collect and to discover.

The campaign during this game only goes for 13.5 hours but includes a lot to complete in which can make it take a lot longer.
