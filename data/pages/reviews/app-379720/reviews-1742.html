\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL:DR; All in all, great game. If you like the DOOM franchise, you already know what you're signing up for. 9/10

As you would expect, this was a really good addition to the DOOM franchise. This iteration is more akin to DOOMs 1 to 64 than it is DOOM 3 that tried to be a horror game. Solid combat mechanics, interesting upgrades for the weapons, secrets aplenty, gore, and astonishing visuals are what truly make this game great. Although, I found myself ruining the pacing for the game at times when looking for secrets. You don't have to find them, but some of them are directly tied to the gameplay, like upgrades for your weapons and suit. Additionally, all of the demons in this game are great and true to form of the originals (we were missing the Pain Elemental and added the laser soldiers, but I digress), but I absolutely HATE HATE HATE the shielded soldiers. Why anyone on the dev team thought that adding demons that carry shields in this masterpiece of a game is far beyond me.\t\t\t\t\t
