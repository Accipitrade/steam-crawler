\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst things first, if you felt disappointed with the Doom 3, welcome back to the slaughter. This is Doom is more like the original Doom on steroids. Fear? You do not need fear when you have your trusty shotgun and nearly limitless supply of imps to kill in the most gruesome but fun way. Scratch that, you don't even need the shotgun, if you get spooked by something, you can just punch the hell from it (pun intended).

So, why you should play this Doom? If you need to relax after a long day in work being yelled at by your boss, killing few hell spawns can do wonder to your mood. There are plenty various monsters to kill in the most imaginative ways. Hell, you can upgrade guns to wildly change the way they behave and switch on the move if you get bored with it. The difficulty is just right, there are few puzzles along the way to even help you use few brain cells now and again and the story feels familiar. I did enjoy the story being somewhat better explained than in original doom, but the essence of the game stayed the same. Kill everything before it manages to kill you.

As for the negatives, there are none that you don't expect while buying pure FPS. The game is a tad linear, there are no important decisions along the way and the story is very predictable. However, in this case, it is not the story that matters. It's the feeling that you saved the world by smashing all the hell to pieces.

Did I forgot to mention? CHAINSAW!\t\t\t\t\t
