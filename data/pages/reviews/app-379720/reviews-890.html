\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIts good, if you're into mindlessly killing lots of demons with lots of chunky gore and a variety of weapons.

PROS:
-Ran very smooth, no glitches encountered
-Felt strong but not overpowered as a character
-Graphics were very clean, lighting was done well and had a lot of attention to details on animations to weapons
-Tons of little nooks for secrets if you into that
-Glory kills/Chainsaw look and feel nice


CONS:
-Level design got a little funky and confusing midway through
-Bosses had uneven difficulty distribution, some used mechanics that felt cheap like invulnerability for most of the fight
-Sounds of most the guns didn't pack oomph, sound just fell short (shotgun, minigun, BFG) and didn't sound as strong as they felt or looked.
-Music wasn't really doing it for me, but it isn't objectively bad. I would recommend the Painkiller OST while playing this for a change if you're not liking it.
-Glory kills felt lacking in variety somehow, even though there were a number of variations

Overall the game is well made, they put a lot of effort into the polish and it shows. Difficulty isn't out of reach for causal play and has settings for people who are looking for some extremely difficult challenges.\t\t\t\t\t
