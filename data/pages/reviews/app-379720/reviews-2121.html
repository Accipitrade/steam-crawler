\r\n\t\t\t\t\t>That 30 y/o boomer who still thinks DOOM was the apex of shooting games
I'm that boomer. This game is loads of fun. It does away with all the trappings that break the pace of a game and forces you to be constantly on your feet. No reloading is definitely something I like. Reloading is a pause on your game, and does nothing more than make you wait until you either are finished reloading or die while doing it. You also aren't playing a cover shooter. Enemies will run towards you like a stoner towards a snickers, and slaughter you. You have to keep moving, and your weapons will give you the breathing space you need to fight. Also, in the options menu, you can set your weapon to be positioned in the middle just like old DOOM games, and I honestly prefer it that way.

My only complaint is that there's not enough. I know that DOOM Eternal is on the way, but I just hope that id realizes what kind of goldmine they've struck and improve upon it. No frills, no doilies, just pure DOOM.\t\t\t\t\t
