\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStraightup BRUTAL

The multiplayer beta and lack of review copies made me nervous this would be a terrible reboot that dumbed down what made the original Doom magical (see: Quake 4, Duke Nukem Forever). However, this went the way of Wolfenstein The New Order and turned out to be a great modernization of the original game.

Technically it looks beautiful and has a great art style. It runs like butter and maintains a good framerate on high with my rig. Love the sound design; guns sound punchy, the violence and gore are chunky, but the music

The music is this mix of electro-industrial and metal in the vein of later Skinny Puppy, some Meshuggah, Nine Inch Nails, etc. The original had clear ripoffs of stuff like Pantera, Metallica, etc. that were still great tunes (E1M1 rips) but Mick Gordon's effort here is a phenomenal work of music that's dynamic and adjusts with what's going on in the game. I'll never understand how, but there are many versions of a song that it will cycle between depending on the level of action. This is one of the rare soundtracks so good I find myself listening to outside of the game.

Gameplay is similar to the original Dooms but it's a little slower and there are those annoying "lock-in" arena bits you'll see in Painkiller/Serious Sam. The mod BRUTAL Doom is by far the greatest version of the original Dooms' gameplay since it's basically the original levels inside it, but this is still a good presentation. I've changed my mind on the glory kills since I started the game. First they felt like a distraction from the classic Doom run 'n gun since they more or less encourage you to NOT shoot them since they pop health goodies when you finish the animation. This mostly goes away when the game starts introducing bigger and badder baddies that you can't spam the melee button on for your glory kills.

Still, they were able to tastefully recreate the fast paced carnage of the originals. There seems to have been a resurgence of old school remakes and interpretations. Serious Sam and Painkiller are still going strong, there have been two new Shadow Warriors, a Rise of the Triad 'remake', Wolfenstein The New Order, another Unreal Tournament coming soon, and alot of others out there. Hopefully Bethesda and id's next FPS is either the Quake reboot or HeXen.

Solid title with a good length. You'd be doomed to miss out on this!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA great action game about ripping & tearing out the huge guts of huge demons. Doom 2016 is a fast paced action extravaganza with more guns than a human being can physically carry. I really like the fast paced arena style combat, the huge & multi-layered environments, all the guns that do interesting things, all the enemies that are fun to fight. As an action game, Doom 2016 doesn't dissapoint. I can honestly say that this is one of the best shooters of 2016.

But system wise, Doom is a handful. It requires a huge amount of drive space & utilizes ID's megatexture tech (seen in RAGE) & that really bloats the game, 45-50 gb! I get that you want the game to look good ID, & it does (as much as destroyed hellscapes & generic scifi facilities go) but I think the space requirements here are a bit insane. Also, no FOV slider (though you can force it in the ini), thats about the only problems I've got.

I can't really say much about the multiplayer, it appears to be a return of Quake/Unreal style arena matches, not really my thing so I have no opinion on it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNope. Most boring game I have ever played in terms of First Person Shooters. I will admit, I like FPS games that are FAST. This game is so slow I fell asleep in the multiplayer. I may finish the single player campaign only because the graphics are fun and the demons make me laugh when they die.

I did enjoy hearing the chatter in Multiplayer about how these so-called DooM players don't like to play Quake Live because everyone in Quake Live is too fast and good for them. Ya think?!?!

Then I muted all - a nice feature in-game, so good for that.

Overall, a good job by id software to have a FPS game for the slow style players. This game is defininetly geared for them. Not for me. I need to move fast, frag fast and live fast. Sorry, but true.
