\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM (2016 reboot) is a must-play for any fan of shooters, and obviously for fans of the Doom series. Unfortunately, the multiplayer, which was quite underrated and decent when it was still populated a couple years back, is dead now. Nevertheless, this game is the precursor to the now legendary Doom Eternal in both story and gameplay, and it is highly recommended to play it first before jumping into Eternal. Along with Titanfall 2 and Battlefield 1, Doom was THE shooter of the year in 2016, and for good reason. Innovative and fun, yet never far from its roots, Doom walks the tightrope between looking back and forward the way a good reboot should. I have replayed the singleplayer campaign many times over the years, and even going back now after having played all of Eternal several times over, Doom (2016) is still a great experience, albeit a more limited one by comparison. I bought it back when it still sold for around $15 on sale and it was worth it then. Nowadays you can pick it up for probably $5 on sale, so there's no excuse not to get it!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM. It was even better than I expected! There are a lot of aspects of this game that I liked and a few things that I didn't like about the game.

I'll start off listing the positives:
+ Fast paced, energetic gameplay loved the chaos in general. Glory kills never got old.
+ Graphics were amazing, looks great on ultra-wide monitors.
+ Mechanics and gameplay are smooth, not a single glitch during the whole play through!
+ Guns all have their own feeling to them, keeps the game fresh.
+ Collectables/Easter Eggs were fun. Didn't feel like it was a grind to get them.
+ Insanely good soundtrack.

Here are some negatives that come to mind:
- Multiplayer is very dead. Some achievements are tied to MP so I couldn't complete them.
- The actual story line of the game was meh, nothing special.
- Arcade mode fell short. Wasn't much to it other then points filling the HUD.
- Snap Maps are a great concept but many of the maps I played weren't very fun.

I played a ton of Doom 3: BFG back in 2013 I figured I'd pick up this game and see whats its all about. I didn't realize that the gameplay of this game compared to DOOM 3 was going to be drastically different. By different I mean better in every aspect...this game surprised me. I'll pick up DOOM Eternal soon without a doubt.

Overall this is a solid FPS and I do recommend it to fans of the genre!
My Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐✨ 4 / 5\t\t\t\t\t
