This is the most badass game i've played in a LONG time. The single player always keeps you on the edge of your seat. When you hear the music intensify and hear that first demon roar at you, you know it's going to be a good fight. The demons are well made and look awesome. They can hold their own in a fight and always deliver. At the end of every fight you are left breathless and wanting more.

The fact that the game rewards you for exploring the environment by giving you upgrardes to your weapons, runes in which to unlock extra added powers in which to enhance your fighting ability is awesome.

The voice acting is spot on, the character design is flawless and the weapons look, feel and sound as beefy as DOOM Guy himself!!

The music in the game is awesome and exhilarating keeping your adrenaline racing throughout the entire playthrough. The sound design has a very polished and detailed feeling to the game.

The graphics of the game are truly "Next Gen" worthy.

In conclusion, this game's single player is amazing and extremely fast pased. Id Software has made an amazing reboot of the classic DOOM franchise. DOOM Guy would be proud!!

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRight off the bat, this game feels like the original DOOM from the fast-paced movement to the hordes of demons. Everything is a solid throwback from the soundtrack, the sound effects of the doors opening and closing, and the red, yellow, and blue key cards to access different parts of the map. It isn't all old though, there are fun new systems that make killing and exploring extremely satisfying. The game does a great job of keeping you enticed to tracking down demons by providing a good breadth of challenges that are very rewarding. If you were a fan of DOOM 1 & 2, I'd highly recommend this one. It'll throw you right into the action without having to sit through some thinly veiled story. DOOM does a great job of telling the story as you play. You get a real sense of what's going on in a very concise and quickly-delivered way so you know there is a reason for doing what you are doing, but it doesn't stop or slow you down from doing it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNow this is what DOOM always was. It plays exactly like the original DOOM only with enhanced graphics.

DOOM 3 is still a really good game, but it took place in clausterphobic enviroments with many jump scares and enemies here and there while this DOOM is just like the original: Large open rooms with hordes of demons coming for you.

For me, DOOM 3 showed off how it could play as a tense, atmospheric, horror game where using a flashlight was always a must, dark rooms left and right and jump scares around every corner. DOOM ( 2016 ), all you need is a gun and some ammo because your DOOM Guy and he doesn't give a ♥♥♥♥ about what's going in. But the original formula DOOM had is what made it and this reboot so good.

The story is actually pretty good, you get to learn more about the UAC although this version of the UAC is vastly different from the UAC we learned about in DOOM 3. The voice acting is pretty solid given only a couple characters ever talk. The guns feel awesome, especially the Super Shotgun making it's return and becoming the staple weapon once again. Relic power ups are still here along with some new ones, love the new Rage power up, tearing apart everything from imps to Barons with just your bare hands is so satisfying.

Glory Kills, relic upgrades, weapon upgrades, weapon mods, armor upgrades, different grenades, all nice touches to an already well put together formula that is DOOM.

Was happy this game was on sale during the Steam Summer Sale. Bought it the moment it did. It does feel a bit nostalgic considering just how much it plays like the original DOOM - the 16-bit graphics.\t\t\t\t\t
