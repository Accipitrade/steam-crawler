9/10
12~35 hour story campaign, collecting toy dolls. Finding upgrade kits for armor and weapons. participating in rune trials to unlock new perks. Researching PDA documents to explore the lore. Do map Challenges to achieve high score in global leaderboard. Online multiplayer mode.

Hard
It is not the type of most FPS games in which players constantly take cover and shoot. Instead it encourages players to fight with extreme aggression. Low on ammo? Cut down demons with chainsaw to receive ammunition! Low on Health? Charge towards Demons and break their necks to heal your wound! Take cover and hide? YOU DIE!
