\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most badass and best FPS games I've ever played.
(note: I was born in 2000 so I was too young to the 1993 classic)
(note 2: I suck at reviews)

There is only one studio that can get anything DOOM related done right and it's id software themselves, the very same studio who made the original 1993 DOOM, a game that changed the world of gaming as we know it (sadly it happened before I was born, lol)

Right off the start, no annoying cutscenes or quick time events (you know that time when a creature jumps and you have to mash buttons?) it doesn't have any of that crap, just pure action.

Speaking of action, DOOM offers a BS-free fast paced action, you can't sprint, you can't reload, you can't take cover, and standing still is death, and the soundtrack: OOOH THE SOUNDTRACK: Testosterone filled metal to get your blood pumping regardless of difficulty, being a metal-head, this is one of the heaviest and most badass metal I've ever heard, it makes you feel overpowered, it makes you feel like you can 1v1 the sun, and you are, you are the most badass marine in any video game, one that can single-handedly wipe out the entire hell army.

The enemy design stays true to the classic, many enemies make a return some don't.
There are only 3 bosses in the game, but id made sure they are the most badass opponents in the game, with specially designed soundtrack that pretty much encourages you to keep moving and dodging as much as possible.

I don't have much to say, but oh boy, the fun focused action, and staying true to the original, making a game, A GAME, and not a stupid 'live service' is a breath of fresh air in the trashcore 'AAA' industry. that's it, no more words form me.

With DOOM Eternal on the way, I thought I better write a review for this game before Eternal makes it look average.

I've played through this game 1 1/2 times, beat it on Nintendo Switch, and am playing it through currently on both PS4 and PC, and despite the fact this game is almost 3 years old, it feels new and fresh every time!
