\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI tried playing this a year ago and remember getting stuck on a level and just dropping it. I decided to try again and complete the campaign and I was so glad that I did!

You play as the Doom Slayer (DoomGuy). You awaken in a sarcophagus and soon realize the forces of Hell are invading Mars. It seems some company was trying to harness energy from Hell so Earth could have a new resource and in doing so opened up a portal to Hell and now the invasion is happening. You have to find out who's behind it, close the portal, and kill every single demon that gets in your way.

Doom is a First Person Shooter that follows the spirit of the classic FPSs of yore. Very fast movement, intense action, overwhelming odds against you, powerups, secrets, etc. Now add some beautiful graphics and some kick ass rock music and you have a masterpiece in ultraviolence.

There are a wide variety of weapons for you to unlock and most weapons come with two different modes for you to unlock and enhance with points that you earn throughout the campaign. The different modes are fun and switchable if you find them and makes for some great customization overall. You're gonna need to learn which weapons are good against certain enemies, because if you don't take them out quick and effectively, you'll find yourself surrounded and dead...a lot.

I stated before how this is like classic FPSs and the first thing Doom will teach you is that standing still/hiding behind cover = death. You have to constantly move and shoot in this game. Enemies are fast, will chase you down, and swarm you if you're not moving. What helps is that you are usually fighting in huge rooms with plenty of platforms to jump to, hide for a moment to switch weapons, and glory kill the crap out of enemies.

The most satisfying mechanic in this game is the Glory Kills. If you damage enemies enough, they will stagger and glow for a moment, prompting you to move in and insta-kill them. Not only does it look and make you feel badass, but it gets you health and ammo for doing so. If you find secret missions and complete them, you can even unlock passive perks that allow you to have enemies stagger for longer, get armor when you glory kill, or more. Doom is filled with tons of customization so you can find the perfect perks and weapons to unleash destruction on anything that comes in your way.

As for gripes, I think I only have one. It's very easy to get lost in this game. Sure it has a great map system, but there were a few levels where I had no idea where to go and wound up going in circles looking for a key or something for 10+ minutes. It really put a damper on things. My second gripe is not a gripe but more like a warning. Doom is a game you have to be in the mood to play. It requires a lot of attention and adrenaline to play since it's hard, but satisfyingly fun. I often found myself needing a break after a very hard level. This is not a negative thing, just something to take note of.

Final verdict? This game is a masterpiece. If you're looking for a challenging, bloody, adrenaline pumping gorefest of an FPS, then you NEED to play this game. As a player who isn't amazing at FPSs I found myself having an amazing time with this game and could not recommend enough to anyone looking for a good story and gameplay. Put the music on high, grab your mouse/controller, and RIP AND TEAR! Thanks for reading!\t\t\t\t\t
