\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDoom is a very solid FPS game and one of the best I have played. I love the fast, frantic combat and variety of weapons, and just the fact that you are taking on literally thousands of demons by yourself (For reference, I did not play any of the old Doom Games). The level design is fantastic and there are lots of secrets hidden in the levels which makes good replayablilty. Also the variety of different enemies makes things interesting, because in some shooters it's just the same enemies over and over (Looking at you CoD). One problem I had with this game is I felt that the blood and gore was a bit over the top, but I know it's Doom, and I'm fine with it, I just don't feel it's necessary. Also, I find myself not using certain weapons because of a lack of ammo, and I understand them being powerful and all, I'd just love to use them more. Overall, if you are looking for an outstanding FPS with fast-paced combat, look no further than Doom.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHoly crap what a game!

An amazing action experience. The game was really fluid and the tempo of the action was just right if you didn't explore too much. As a wise man once called it "John Wick of video games". Maps were quite easy to navigate, but I did get lost a few times.

The playability was top notch. For the first time in a long time, it felt like I was in complete control of what my character was doing. Every time I pressed a key or turned my mouse, the game responded instantly without any lag. Aiming was ridiculously easy with how well the game was performing. I liked the mechanic that you got ammo and hp by making different cinematic kills. Made the combat more fluid.

Story was ok. Not bad, not good, but definitely not the main point of the game.

The music was also fit the game really well.\t\t\t\t\t
