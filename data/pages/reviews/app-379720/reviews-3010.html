\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCarnage Incarnate!!
Doom is a fast paced shooter that has you constantly on the edge of your seat.
It keeps you fixated on the gameplay with an overall interesting story. With lots of collectibles / Easter eggs to hunt for, as well as weapon challenges and praetor suit upgrades there’s a lot of things to do on the side as well. I personally enjoyed the single player campaign & I’m looking forward to playing it again on a higher difficulty. Most probably on Nightmare.

Multiplayer brings the same fast paced action with new guns & a lot of customization. My biggest gripe is the wait times. I once waited for 10 min for a Team Deathmatch. Matchmaking doesn’t really care if you’re a n00b or a pro... It’s a free for all Colosseum of Pain! You’d definitely get your butt handed to you a few times before you get the hang of it.

Overall, money well spent!! Especially if you get it on sale like I did!!! :D\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile DOOM has a great game at it's base, it falls into a problem of forcing you into what it thinks is great. While the story and cut scenes don't take up all your time and slow the game down too much, the glory kill system does. Combats are made to be up close and brutal --with weapons like the shotgun being almost useless beyond five feet-- just to make you get up close and glory kill so the game can show you how brutal it is.
Higher difficulties more or less just upgrade enemy damage. As fun as it is coming out of the uncontrolled glory kill I didn't even want to activate (tried to melee enemy in front of me, teleported to the wounded guy behind him for the kill) then getting killed from behind before I even regained control, it would be great to not have to constantly pull myself from the action just to gain a sliver of health to stay alive. Red explosive barrells litter the orange landscape and will kill you instantly if you from quite the distance if you accidently graze one with your shotgun. Meanwhile ammo is plentiful, ammo caps are not. Having to blow through three different guns in a fight because I don't have enough ammo for any given one is tedious, only to be compounded when i go into the next room and theres three times as much ammo as I can hold.

Overall, its a would-be-great game ruined by developers forcing what they think is cool into it. The only reason I wont be refunding it is I purchased during a sale and I think there is still some fun to be squeezed out of it.\t\t\t\t\t
