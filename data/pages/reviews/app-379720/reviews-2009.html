\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDOOM (2016) is a perfect mesh of old and new concepts, fine tuned into one tightly knit package. At multiple points during the game you have to find key cards, which is something I didn't think I'd see again in the 2000's. It implements a mostly well written story atop of fast paced, action packed fight sequences. The game contains enemies from both the original and Doom 2, along with subsequent Easter Eggs and references to both. the game is superb, and not playing it fully is doing yourself a great disservice. While the campaign is great, I can't say the same about the Multiplayer mode. It is very disappointing, feeling almost like a Call of Duty game, while adding few select things from the campaign, such as power ups, the voice of VEGA, and a few power weapons from the doom arsenal, such as the BFG, Gauss Cannon, and Chainsaw. The multiplayer also adds the ability to play as one of the various demons, while decimating the enemy. Overall Doom is a fantastic game. Go play it before Eternal releases.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a great game. It's on sale quite often so definitly buy it then because its well worth the price. I just finished the campaign on normal, took me about 16 hours. If i didn't spend time on gathering collectibles maybe I could have got it done in 14.
The pacing of the game is really good. It's starts slow so even for people new to pc gaming you can finish the first level without a sweat. You got 1 gun, no reloading and enemies take 1 shot. Then every level you get some new guns but enemies are harder and harder to beat. Still you steadily get better at the game. You "grow" with it. The gunplay becomes more fast paced and before you know it you feel like an unstoppable rollercoaster of death and gore. Every gun feels great, they all differ in feedback/ recoil that corresponds well with the effects of the gun. Even the story isn't half bad. Id software nailed it by showing and not telling. No long ass speeches, no boring cutscenes. Why? because you're DOOM guy. And you got 1 job : killing demons.
10/10 will play again.\t\t\t\t\t
