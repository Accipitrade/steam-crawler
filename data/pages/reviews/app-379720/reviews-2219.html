Also the SnapMap level editor a good thing...and fortunately the only way to play some levels coop together... that`s what I miss a lot in the storyline or in the classic multiplayer modes...

The multiplayer mode also good and interesting, but not to compare with Quake Live, faster more game modes and so on...

so all in all it is to recommend, Story (SinglePlayer) VERY GOOD, Snap Map level editor GOOD, Multiplayer GOOD and....this Arcade mode (acctually part of the SinglePlayer mode) AVERAGE in my opinion...

So have fun with DOOM.... but Doom1 and Doom2, will be my real favorites also in the future ! :-)
