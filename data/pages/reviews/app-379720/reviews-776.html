\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYo so this game is insanely good. I played it in late 2020. I know I'm late, but it was really cheap and on sale, so I bit and gave it a shot.

Amazing gameplay. Absolutely ethereal. You just rip demons to shreds, you're flying all over the place, switching weapons, awesome.

The soundtrack is one of the best in all of gaming. Hands down. The main theme is just dope as hell.

Only thing I disliked was the online and the platforming bits. I wish they wouldn't have padded out (and this is a rare thing) a section or two of the game with platforming. It really just didn't need it. More than anything, it made some of the levels more confusing and longer than necessary.

The collectibles and challenges are there too, if you want to do that. Upgrades and cosmetics to be earned, but are no means necessary to beat the game. Nice bonus rewards for those willing to seek them out.

The story was whatever but if you buy doom, it's for the run and gun gameplay.

100% worth the pickup whether or not you've played eternal or are new to the games. The arcade style FPS gameplay is just awesome. Don't expect much out of the online matches, but I hear some people are into it, but not my cup of tea. More of a bonus if you enjoy the main game.

9/10 - near perfect, with only a couple of gripes that really aren't that big of a deal.\t\t\t\t\t
