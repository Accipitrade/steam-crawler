\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a great FPS. Lots of weapons, mods, upgrades, etc. Lots of secrets to find, too.

The story is lackluster. This game feels like an endless series of rooms one after the other where you must kill all monsters to proceed. After a while it started to get boring.

Some of the weapon upgrades have very difficult requirements, but you can just reload a save and the requirements carry over i.e. if you need to kill 10 X then you can reload a room with 2 X five times and complete the requirement. But this seems like a hack and I think it would have been better if the requirements were easier but you had to get them at once.

Also optional manual saves would have been nice.

Would have rated this game neutral if it was possible.\t\t\t\t\t
