This game is f*cking awesome. It is the most beautifully disturbing game I've ever played, and made me feel more badass than I did playing the original Doom games as a kid. The lore you acquire as you progress through the game makes it that much more fascinating. Fighting through the maw and remains of fallen titans of the underworld, in awe of the detail and beauty of of it all, knowing it is my sole purpose as the "Doom Marine" to destroy the twisted, disgusting awesomeness before me, ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ I f*cking love this game. I spent hours just inspecting the the details and background landscapes of this incredible work of art they call a GAME.

Beautiful, fascinating, sick, twisted, bloody, gory, unsettling, unnerving, adrenaline pumping, badass enducing, awesome.

Bring it on Hell, I am the Doom Marine.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's DOOM but with jumping (double jumping!)

Awesome single player FPS - extremely simple formula, collect the red key to use on the red door, the blue key to use on the blue door - something I would nomally criticize deeply, but it's DOOM, it's THEIR formula. If anybody can get away with it, it's DOOM. Honestly, if it didn't follow the formula, I'd be annoyed.

The newly added arcade mode is excellent, makes a great game better.

The multiplayer is horrible. Not just a little bad, not 'not great', it's objectively, absurdly, ridiculously terrible.

It's a quick fun single player game, but do not expect any of the finesse of a Quake. There is no movement. There is nothing. There is no skill curve. Can you aim your mouse at the OK button on a dialog box with relative ease? Yes? Then you're good at DOOM.

Super fun, super shallow, but exactly what DOOM should be.\t\t\t\t\t
