8/10 - Second best DLC pack, just behind Utopia\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOrigins: the balance is A double edged sword. On one hand I love the balance single player because it gives me a role playing perspective and experience. On the other, With multiplayer a person needs to generally respect and not choose a super op origin. I like to have a head-start/handicap option that’s creative because I like to role play. On the contrary, people that like competitive multiplayer may find this annoying. My bias and what I would like to push for is less balance in roleplaying as weird as that sounds. It immerses me more. It’s not Starcraft.
Galactic Community and federations:
On the issue of balance and roleplaying Diplomacy was sorely needed. The economy in this game, for example, is one of the most complex and fun I’ve ever played but there was groundbreaking on the diplomatic front. Creating galactic laws change this. The sanctions are harsh and breaking law does have consequences. However to benefit from say a galactic market you have to play nice. I like these dilemmas this mechanic introduced. Federation mechanics make diplomacy with allies deeper where as envoys initiate this process. This not only adds roleplaying but balance and groundbreaking diplomacy systems.
Juggernaut: I’m actually building it right now. I expect it to allow me to be more aggressive but I have to use it first.
Overall great way to spend quarantine and I live happily in a pandemic 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
