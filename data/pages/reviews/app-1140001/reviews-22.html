*Actual review now*

Pros;

-Galactic Community is pretty cool tbh, can't wait to see modders take advantage of this new system.
-Late game performance boost is actually pretty amazing, I thought it wouldn't affect me as much as it was portrayed on the forums. I was very wrong.
-New Origins are cool as hell.
-New diplomatic options are really cool, way more in-depth (though it still is right click to befriend and it kinda just does its thing, but maybe modders will drop in to add diplomatic events or something more interesting than watching a number increase.)

Cons;

-Galactic Community proposals take forever to be voted on, maybe making it so that when every Member of the community is supporting something on the floor, it just expedites it and passes it. This would save many a years of waiting.
-AI don't seem to care for the new Origins. Many just have the default one, which is cool and all as I don't expect them to actually be able to take advantage of them, but still a bummer.


-more to come as soon as I can get my game to stop crashing with mods, because I can't play vanilla.\t\t\t\t\t
