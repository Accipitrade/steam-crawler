Stellaris: Federations
The DLC adds a new Federation and Galactic Senate system to the game. As for the Federations, they are regional alliances focused on one aspect of the development of the Empire (which we can choose freely from a large list), controlling the political and military aspects. The Galactic Senate, on the other hand, is associated with our every action in the game. We can bring policies, reforms, and general decisions into the political room that will affect every represented nation in the Senate hall. You can really mess around here, and really feel this dynamic political game between senators. You can even (as the new Emperor) create the first Galactic Empire for safety and security, as it should be. Always.
