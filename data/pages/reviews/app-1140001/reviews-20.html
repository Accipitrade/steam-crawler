Unless you want a background idle game that can ruin/kill your game while you're AFK, just don't.

The people that are reviewing this saying "amazing performance" likely bought the game after MegaCorp (i.e. the patch that brought 10% performance compared to the previous patch). Yes, it is far and above better than that patch. However, the largest default galaxies (800-1,000 stars) still aren't even remotely playable. I did a test myself and 2.1 performance at GAME START was still 10% better than this patch, and the late-game performance gets exponentially worse in this patch due to population calculations (i.e. it is still unplayable, 1/10 would not recommend).

DON'T BUY.

Edit with some actual numbers: In 2.1 in early-game (i.e. 2200-2201), average time per month was about 5.5s. In this patch (2.6), the average time was about 6.3-6.5s. If it's that bad just 22 years in, I imagine it'll be a lag fest before it even gets to 2300.\t\t\t\t\t

Ive spent the last several hours playing. I think the stuff they added to the game for free for everyone was pretty good alone by its self. Wouldnt say you really need this DLC. And as usual, its not worth the price tag in my opinion. But to each their own on that one.

It does add some pretty neat features though that you dont get access to with out the dlc. Though i think its kind of poopy that paradox added such a cool feature to the game as the origins are, only to lock almost all of them behind most of the games DLC, many of them being tied to the federations dlc its self.

But all in all. From the little ive gotten to play around with this DLC is actually pretty good. Definitely makes diplomacy vastly more interesting than it was before.

Havent run into any major or game breaking issues myself yet personally. So thats a good sign. I would definitely recommend the DLC but would also suggest waiting for a future sale price if you can manage to wait. If you feel you need it though, i give it a thumbs up.

Edit: at the time im posting this, i noticed it says i have no hours playing the dlc. which is strange since ive literally been playing since like 2 in the morning and its 6am now. Not sure if steam even calculates time spent with DLC. Just thought id mention it anyway.\t\t\t\t\t
