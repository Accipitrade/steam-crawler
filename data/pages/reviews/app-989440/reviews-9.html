\r\n\t\t\t\t\ti was too rude about this game i think in this review the first time around and i want to try to rectify that. the devs clearly read what i had to say and made great strides to improve the experience. i think some of them fall a little flat, but the trying is what matters.

at the beginning the concept was if someone reaches the end of a level, that level would be closed off for anyone else that might follow. now, multiple people can complete the first and second set of levels, while the third is the only one that can be beaten just once.

the first few incarnations of the game seemed to be focused on speed and on-the-fly problem solving. situational analysis, and how you adapt. with the invasion update the game's emphasis on movement screeched to a halt. some traps require you to just wait, which is rarely an option when the guardians are chasing you. one of which used to kill you instantly, but it looks like the devs knew that they screwed the pacing up because now that guardian only does a pitiful one heart of damage, like they were forgiving their own game design.

i'm editing this to remove what i said about the charge bars, as well as upgrades you get from shrines such as Wings and Double Jump. the devs have changed it now so that those are infinite use like they were before the powers update. it would be unfair of me to leave it where it was.

it seems as if some of the traps don't work off of a global timer as well. for example, some of them are axes that come from below and damage you on contact. sometimes they work as intended; coming up all in unison, in series so you have a window of time to run over them, or based on proximity. numerous times ive found myself in a situation where they're all completely out of sync with each other which makes it very difficult to pass. going over them is rarely a safe bet because the axes actually reach pretty far up.

i love this game. the devs seem like they're working WITH the community now. asking for feedback and such. i'll leave the review negative until the next update proves me otherwise. i really think it could.\t\t\t\t\t
