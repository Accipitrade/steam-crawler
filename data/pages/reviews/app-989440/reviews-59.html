\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlright, so... It's fun, but overall a mid-tier game imo.

+ Good concept
+ Fun
+ Fluid controls

- Game made my computer warm on MEDIUM settings when it should be able to take it easily on that. Bumped it down to low settings and it definitely made things better, just not perfect. Optimisation is definitely needed. AMD Ryzen 2700x, GTX 1660 ti, 16GB RAM, if anyone cares.
- Spiked (speared?) fences are tedious to deal with. Whenever you run up to one they'll instantly rise on you, so you either wait a second or two or jump over them and take a hit. It's annoying imo.
- Repetition will definitely be a big factor, that's for sure, since you're seeing the same s**t all the time. Because of that I'll probably play in moderation, rather than hours at a time.
- In REGARDS to the repetition, the price is a bit steep imo. Like idek if I'll be playing this much cause of the repetition, and if so £15 (discounted launch price) is way too much imo. Yeah... big variety is definitely much needed.

Let's see how well the development process goes.\t\t\t\t\t
