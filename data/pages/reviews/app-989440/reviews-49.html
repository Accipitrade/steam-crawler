\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith elements from rogue-lite, platforming, deathruns and a bit of parkour, Phantom Abyss creates a fresh gameplay with the use of 1st person camera, and puts you on the shoes of a whip wielding explorer as you run your way through trap ridden tombs in what feels like a 3D Temple Run. Watch as other people's phantoms run ahead giving you hints in where to go for hidden treasures and die in traps just so you don't. Progress enough and you get a chance to claim a Relic and get out, sealing the tomb forever, or delve deeper into other Biomes and try your luck at a chance of better Whips.
