\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery entertaining rogue-lite with an interesting twist! The ghost system is very intuitive and makes for interesting gameplay since you can watch other players pull off neat stunts that can help you traverse faster or find secrets you may otherwise miss.

There are different whips used for traversing the levels that give different stats, each level gives you the opportunity to use any treasure you collect to buy an "offering" such as a double jump or increase whip range, and any treasure you have by the end of the run you get to keep (or alternatively if you die you lose it all!). It's overall just super fun!

My only complaint is that at the beginning of the game, for me atleast, the ghosts have been very minimal (at most 2-3) and don't come even remotely close to what's shown in the trailers. However, ghosts will interact with the levels in real time, meaning that any interactions they make during their run will impact your own (ie any traps they set off will go off on your end).
