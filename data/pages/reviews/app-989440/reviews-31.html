\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt the time of this review, I'm afraid I'm going to have to advise against buying this game just yet.

Currently, there's a severe, game breaking bug that basically ends your temple run should you get to the final treasure of said temple or often times between loading screens.

There have also been reports of this game running PCs, decent rigs at that, incredibly hot--originally I didn't pay them no heed since after a few hours of game play, nothing has happened; suddenly, my PC fans began working overtime and being in an air conditioned room with a modest but solid gaming rig? Yeah, I'm uninstalling until these things are addressed.

I would however, keep this on your wishlist--when things were working properly (for maybe an hour of the six I've poured in) the game is incredibly fun and engaging; really scratching an itch I didn't realize I had.

So, I would really put this as a "buy at your own risk" sort of scenario. I do believe that this game will have a turn around at some point, it IS early access and the bugs are very few...but those bugs are incredibly rough.

I'll be readdressing the score in the future.

edit: So, from what I've been reading, the disconnection issues have all but been resolved at this point--however, the over heating issue remains for a number of people.

I've no doubt that a solid number of these cases involved things like over clocking or even just flat out bad cooling, but it's often enough that quite frankly, that can't be the case for everybody.\t\t\t\t\t
