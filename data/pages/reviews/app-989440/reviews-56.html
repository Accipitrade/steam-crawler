\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy hour count should show how much I love this game. Joining on Day 1 and still playing. The movement is satisfying and you always feel like you're getting better. There is a lot of progression when you start out and it'll take a number of hours to get all the relics and bless all the whips! There's even movement options to master, like ledge boosting and crouch jump storage that reminds me of the hours I spent in TF2 learning rocket/sticky jumping.

It's not without faults, some complain about the difficulty, others mention the guardians needing work. What I can say is that the game is evolving and the devs host a Q&A biweekly on the Devolver twitch channel and the discord has plenty of people to interact with and make suggestions.
All I want to point out is that this is truly an Early Access game. The core system is there, with plenty to love and dig your teeth into. As the devs roll out update after update, things are improving and changing. They are passionate about this, and want to see it succeed. But it is a small team who can only work so fast. I recommend you give it a shot and see how this grows!

Original review:
I can see this turning into something special! The whip adds so many movement options when compared to a standard platformer. The more you play, the more you're able to judge whip distance, platforms to skip, and ideal routes to get to the exit. Day 1 I was concerned when I saw many of the same sections repeated in the many temples I ran through, but coming back the 2nd day, I saw new variants and different treasure hiding spots! As long as the layouts keep changing, I'm sure this will keep being enjoyable. Not to mention, the different whips change your game plan. One lets you get more coins from chests, but if any phantoms grab the chest before you, you get nothing. So you have to be quick, or wait until later levels to get coins for perks.

I've really been enjoying this one and would recommend you give it a shot!\t\t\t\t\t
