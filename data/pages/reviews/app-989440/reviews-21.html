\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLoads of fun, you can do a quick run in 10-15 mins, a medium in 20-25 mins or a hard which I didnt manage yet but I know people are doing it in 30-40 mins.

There's not too much diversity in what you do, you are a temple invader attempting to reach a relic either early on in the temple or later on. The further you go into the dungeon the harder the traps and the "follower" are.

The followers are a cool feature, they each have their own skill to damage you with and they each travel differently.

The use of "whips" is great, each whip you get has a positive and negative effect and you have to cleanse the whip by completing a run.

Very well done game, well worth playing.\t\t\t\t\t
