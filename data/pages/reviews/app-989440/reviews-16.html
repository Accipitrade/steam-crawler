\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot a bad game but too many loading screens.

My deal breaker is that this game is a rogue-like/lite but the time between retries is painfully slow. I'll compare it to Fall Guys that's somewhat a contemporary game.

In Fall Guys you press start, loading screen, game, loading screen, game until you die or win.

In this game you start and are in a hub, select your weapon and move to the game to select the gamemode, loading screen, screen showing how many people died, game, fade to black some words and you have to PRESS CONTINUE to load the next level, loading, game.

Remember that Fall Guys is an ONLINE game where people are actively playing with you, this game has ghost that are server loaded but anything else is client-sided I tried it on an SSD WD SN550.

There are a ton of steps that shouldn't be there and ruins the experience in one hand the game is fast and you die faster and in the other hand the interface and the presentation is painfully slow.

Thumbs down from me, I'll gonna refund it maybe some day it will be fixed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is basically Indiana Jones as a video game. This is an Action Roguelike and 3D platformer. This game is not easy and the two times I beat the first chamber the green one I only had one heart. Sometimes I make errors to avoid this keep an eye on everything and take your time when you can. There are special whips that add to the gameplay and how you play. The phantoms are mostly going to be people who died or beat a chamber but not the last chamber. Only one person can ever beat a temple and each temple is randomly generated. If you die in a chamber you can never play that temple again. The phantoms are going to be either blue for not live and green for the person who is doing the temple at the same time as you. You will mostly not see green phantoms. This game is solo but the phantoms of other players will affect your game. You can pause the game and the game will pause. This game is very fun and I can not wait to see what the devs add in the future because this game is still in Early Access.
