\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYES! This is unique and engaging movement roguelike! I'm constantly looking for games with good movement but often times it's stifled by another mechanic I don't like. The movement here is un-stifled and amazing. It feels good to move around and you feel like a real badass when you pull off certain moves.

THE BAD: It's still early access, and has that level of polish. There could be more menu options etc, but nothing groundbreaking is missing at the moment. There's 3 out of 5 level tiers available and right now that's fine with me because after 10 hours I can't even consistently make it to tier 2 (called the caverns).

THE AMAZING: The meta balance is incredible. You move fast but not too fast and the monsters that chase you definitely apply pressure, but they don't feel oppressive. Luck is absolutely an element and you can get really lucky or unlucky -- but it doesn't feel like how far you get relies on lucky drops by any means, and with good fundamentals you can still go far with an 'unlucky' run.

The graphics are awesome and a nice blend of realistic/cartoony. I'm a huge fan of blends like this as anything trying to be real very quickly dives into the "Uncanny Valley" (if you haven't heard of that I encourage you to look it up) and anything too cartoony just feels like you're playing a game meant for children.

The way the temples are set up makes it have that multiplayer feel without it actually being multiplayer. I'm a huge fan of this. I can't tell you how many games I've been super excited to play only to realize it's strictly a multiplayer/coop game. Sometimes I just want to play by myself, but it's still fun to compare your scores and stuff to other people. This game has that.

The whip system is awesome too. Different whips have different bonuses / drawbacks, plus each one has a new aesthetic. I saw a video with a lava one! XD

The controls are super responsive and feel great. However I do think they should add an auto run

I haven't encountered any bugs or crashes etc, it's been perfect.

Tl:DR This game is definitely early access, but very fun and engaging already! I can't wait to see what the devs have in store for us.\t\t\t\t\t
