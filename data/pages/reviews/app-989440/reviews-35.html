\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPhantom Abyss is a wonderfully frustrating game. It has a very simple premise of being a first person platformer where you avoid traps and special guardians while attempting to reach the end of a dungeon and claim the dungeon relic. To help you do this you will use a whip to cross bigger gaps and try and avoid traps.

The big twist is that each temple is randomly generated and that once any player in the world beats a certain temple you will not be able to visit it again.

You can also see ghosts of other players that played the temple you are in and perhaps see their mistakes or discover things you wouldn't on your own.

There are three layers of temples with the last layer being the hardest compilation of traps and the guardian being in enraged mode.

Another important thing to mention is that you will be able to acquire different whips with different effects on them that can make runs easier.

Simple run, jump and avoid death premise is executed flawlessly and you soon get grabbed by the game attempting to get one more relic or go a level deeper. There is something special about you being able to complete a temple and from then on it will only you that ever completed it. You also release the resources of all the players that died in that temple before you.

It is very easy to get into, but to beat the third circle of temple hell you will spend hours and have dozens of attempts. You will learn in the meantime which whip suits you the best, learn room layouts and how to avoid specific traps and finally learn which blessings are the most useful to your particular playstyle.

You will be cursing and sweating. You will rage quit and yell at the stars. But, you will keep chasing that high of beating this, ultimately, hard game. In the end you will not regret a single hour you spent with it.\t\t\t\t\t
