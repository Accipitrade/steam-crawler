\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn Indiana Jones simulator where you have to run through levels of a temple to reach the relic, undergoing multiple different tasks. Currently, I have completed two of the temples and have thoroughly enjoyed my time with the game.

Pros:
- The game runs quite smoothly on my 4K monitor with rarely any FPS drops
- The movement is so fluid and fun, being abe to strafe around objects is rewarding when done well. The whip also gives the game a nice level of depth where if used correctly you can completely skip certain parts of the puzzles.
- This game is tense, getting chased by a guardian and then worrying about the obstacles is a real rush currently.
- The ghosts are quite fun and I enjoy when you do a run with multiple because as you progress and see others die it makes you feel like you're achieving something the others didn't
- Powers and different whips add to each run being different
- Sharing your failed runs with friends is super cool and will be great for games nights with all of us trying to compete
- UPDATE: The game is quite hard and challenging in the later sections. It is more on you to play controlled, because once you start to jump around like an dummy you take damage.
- UPDATE: Rewarding parkour
- UPDATE: Patterns have become much different with recent updates to the game, and quite a bit harder too

Cons
- Currently no true multiplayer (looks like it's getting added)
- UPDATE: The whip can sometimes glitch and cause your death by not grappling. I would say around 95% it works intended, and like 5% it doesn't.

My Hopes:
- The add in secrets to the hub world, and I mean like secrets outside the area because you can get to some really crazy spots by climbing around.
- The true multiplayer soon so I can run realtime with my friends. Right now we currently just share after death and watch each other over discord

I'm personally hoping that they have created a system where they can add new traps into their random generation algorithm to add more complexity. Currently the game is in a really good spot, and I think is worth the money and can see myself always coming back to play. Support the devs if you're on the fence about this, and enjoy platformers.\t\t\t\t\t
