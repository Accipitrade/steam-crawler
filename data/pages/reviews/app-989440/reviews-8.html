\r\n\t\t\t\t\t(Edited as of April 4th, 2022) - They've reverted the power system from movement abilities and have kept the health system in place as it is. This review is still negative however, because these changes are not conducive to the long term health of Phantom Abyss. While on the surface, starting at One Health and continuing to gain more via collecting the souls of phantoms along the way seems fun (And to be clear, it is), there is a glaring issue that has me concerned.

First and foremost, the entry to difficulty is still insanely high for new players now. Where once you could have a few mistakes and continue to play, you're now punished with your run being ended instantly by the lion's share of the traps. This can be frustrating, but more important, it means if a new player enters a new temple, they are stuck. There are no health ups to be gained in way, shape, or form beyond blessings.

In addition, the wording on the update is that this isn't set in stone. They have admitted they plan to revisit these changes, which means this could easily be reverted back to how it was at any moment. Without a genuine, commitment from the team to keep the movement abilities outside of the Power System, I can't recommend this product as I cannot trust they won't go back to the disastrous system they had before the community rioted.

And to be clear, I do not think the Power System as a concept isn't viable. The idea of having limited use, high power abilities is exciting and interesting. HErein lies the first issue again: Gaining power back is via getting a blessing (A rare room in the middle of the floor that has a shrine in it, meaning you have to be stockpiling coins to get a blessing mid-run), Completing a floor (Which means you have only the bars you start with for the entire floor, turning your blessings into a limited-use item), or by collecting the souls of Phantoms.

All in all, the focus on Phantoms is good and interesting, but it's ignoring the fact that 'New Temples' are now inherently harder, and are only going to get easier. Dying means your whip is captured, which means you're punished for playing the game outright if you get a new temple, and you get nothing positive out of it. This is not a sustainable design philosophy.



(Original Review, April 1, 2022) Unfortunately, Phantom Abyss is a game that was once an incredibly joyful experience. The ability to move through the air, skillfully avoiding traps and dangers and pitfalls was an amazing thing. The skill ceiling was high, but even low skilled players were still able to enjoy the game.

With this new update, I genuinely see no purpose to continue playing. This is now no longer the game I purchased and sunk over 100 hours in. I am sad to see it go this way, I'm not one to review things typically, but this can't stand. I want my fun back.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game could've been anything, but they slowly stripped down what made it unique and fun, and now it regularly has single digit concurrent players.

What a waste.


Powers Update Second Rework Edit:

Honestly, even with most of the reversions, you still start with 1 max health and phantoms still mess with specific switch puzzles. This game's direction is seriously confusing, and I don't know what the developers have planned other than 'we want phantoms to have a bigger impact on your run', which, sounds cool, but isn't particularly fun. They're mainly just Mario Kart ghosts that eff with you occasionally.


Adventure Update.
Again, they've decided that more stuff must be restrained- This time, blessing whips is on the chopping block. This was the best part of the game originally; taking a cursed whip that made your run harder and making it to the end of that tier of temple that it corrosponded to. Now, they always remain cursed. There isn't a reason to try out new challenges, and you should stick to whichever reskin of the stock whip that you think looks best. The game I love is under all of this, but I don't think it's really worth it to dig through. I don't wanna grind 'reputation' to get higher adventure ranks, I just wanna play a roguelike that's tough but fair with cool challenges that give you great rewards.
Also, the daily can be repeated infinitely, defeating the point of dailies.\t\t\t\t\t
