Rating
7/10 (B-tier)
A fast-paced and replayable parkour game with interesting mechanics. Because it's not finished yet there is a certain lack of polish and amount of content.

Details
It's fun and good! The whip system is cooler than I initially expected and the game is also more challenging than I thought. Also really like the music, it makes the game a lot more tense (though it does seem to play at unfittingly calm moments quite often).

While the variety of obstacles across runs isn't super large yet, the game is fun enough for me to make that not matter much.

Current issues:
- The game could use some more variety in the temple layouts, traps and guardian types imo but this will hopefully be one of the major things they will improve during Early Access.
