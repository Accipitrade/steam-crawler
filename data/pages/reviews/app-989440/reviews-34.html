\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA really good game with cool ideas and well thought out gameplay, I'm glad I put it on my E3-Top-List. It's a semi-multiplayergame that's got the benefits of a singleplayergame (being able to pause and play at your own pace, yet kind of competing with other players). Strongly recommend it to all who like Indiana Jones- and Dungeon Explorer-games.

EDIT: However, I fully agree with those who criticize that there is absolutely NO reward if you fail to complete a temple, even if you almost managed to get through, resulting in spending hours of playtime for absolutely nothing which heavily reduces the motivation to continue playing (which in turn is a pity because otherwise this game is really good). This NEEDS to be fixed. In my opinion it would suffice if you'd at least get one small or medium reward after each stage, for example a key of the rarity of the corresponding stage instead of all keys at once in the relic room at the very end.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is really fun, I love how much you can commit to the game by choosing to go deeper or finishing the temple run by collecting the relic in one of the earlier zones. As it is, I have not encountered a single bug, the controls overall feel great, graphics are good enough for this type of game, and the time commitment is completely up to the player. However, I have a couple of cons that I hope the developer fixes:

1. They should fix the slide-to-sprint controls. Right now, when you're sprinting & you hit the crouch button to go into a slide, the only way to get out of the slide is to either jump (spacebar) or press the crouch button again. Devs need to add a third way, by pressing the sprint button again - this is pretty standard slide-to-sprint controls across many FPS games out there.
2. Devs should add some sort of coop mode. They have done a pretty good job at balancing out the buffs/debuffs on the whips, and I think this concept could extend to a coop mode. Assuming it's 2-player coop & each player starts with standard 3 hearts, the total 6 hearts could be shared across both players (this would be a sort of debuff to having 6 total hearts). Of course the total number of hearts can vary based on dev. internal testing & balancing, but you get the idea.
3. I hope the devs have more Guardians planned for the future. The 3 right now are all different & pretty cool to go up against, but after playing for a couple of hours you get used to them rather quickly.
4. I think there should be some types of objectives, similar in concept to the ball that you have to place inside the door to unlock it, but much different in execution. Right now that's the only "puzzle", and calling it a puzzle is stretching it. I hope they add some more optional challenges that reward players with tons of coins. This would also help balance out the chests that get skipped either due to a Guardian or simply by running past them.

That's pretty much it. The game is still quite fun, I enjoy just booting it up to play for however long I want to play for. I'm not that great, but I've gotten to the red zone a few times now. I think the game would be alot more fun as coop with another friend. Right now with the amount of content in the game, I'd say it's a pretty solid fun time for $10, even at $15 it would be worth it but $20 is stretching it.\t\t\t\t\t
