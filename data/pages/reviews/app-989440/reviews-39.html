\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOnce you get the hang of the controls, it feels incredibly good to be flinging yourself around with the whip, manoeuvring through traps, and dodging the guardians. The tutorial was a bit slow and had me wondering if the rest of the game was going to be like that, but things start picking up pretty soon after, and some of my favourite runs where when I made it to the final stage and had a very angry guardian on my back all the time. Really gets the adrenaline going.

There are some cool whips to unlock that change things up a bit, and relics to collect if you're a completionist. You can hang out in the discord and help people reclaim their loot they lost when they died using their share codes, or try and tackle some of the super difficult ones people share that have claimed hundreds of players.

It's still very early so there are some things missing and bugs/optimisation issues for the devs to fix, though in my 40+ hours I only crashed twice, and the game ran smoothly on ultra settings, so I do feel bad for people that are having disconnects or frame rate issues that are preventing them from having as much fun as I am.\t\t\t\t\t
