\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a game in early access, I can say this is a very nicely polished game!
I have played this game for over 50 hours now and I am STILL thoroughly enjoying myself.
Below are my thoughts and feelings about this game in it's current development:


The Pros:
  • This game has simple mechanics but it is difficult to master all of the different traps it has in store for you. You can easily avoid one trap but you can easily get caught out by the different combination of traps. This makes it great fun and re-playable.
  • Nice variation of whips (different whips provide different benefits).
  • Nice variation of blessings (skills that expire upon leaving the trial - some I imagine may get reworked in future).
  • The added fear of guardians chasing you will get your heart racing, so it's not just about navigating traps! This is a great feature to keep you on your toes!
  • This game has an interesting element of "only one person in the world will complete an individual trial". So no-one will complete the exact same trial you completed. Kinda cool!
  • If you die in a trial, you can share the trial with a friend and they can try the same trial as long as no-one has since completed it. If the trial is completed then you will get your gear and loot back.
  • You can see that they are looking to implement a follow your friend feature so you can do trials together! (Not currently implemented as of yet).
  • I have only experienced a couple of trivial bugs in this game so far! Nothing that impacts me from actually playing the game.

The Cons:
  • I personally would greatly appreciate a bit of story progression after obtaining a new relic or maybe after (x) amount of relics. Even something small would have been nice, like the power of the big statue getting stronger. Just something to signify that you are progressing towards escaping the prison.
  • The url for sharing the trial is huge, I'm sure there is a way the devs can shorten this.
  • Some people say they get a bit motion sick when performing a roll in this game. Thankfully this doesn't trigger my motion sickness, but I can understand how it would trigger some people. Although I say this is a "con", the devs are already aware of this thanks to player feedback and they are soon implementing an optional change to the combat roll!

Future Expectations:
I'm looking forward to the new things they can introduce to this game like:
  • Tackling the trial at the same time with your friends
  • New levels to explore
  • New traps to navigate
  • New whips
  • New blessings
  • New guardians
  • Cosmetics for your phantoms
  • More story

My Conclusion:
Overall I highly recommend this game! Especially for the price!
Easily a 10/10 from me.
I hope you enjoy this game as much as I do!
