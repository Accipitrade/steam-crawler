\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEasily the best parkour running game I've ever played. It checks close to every single box I want in these types of games. I find it impossible to understand how the player base is so tiny. I feel like this is a game that almost every single person I know could find enjoyment in for one aspect or another.

My favorite aspect of the game is being able to have an entertaining "chill time" game. This is my go to podcast, TV show, audiobook game where I can listen to things in the background while still putting in hours of this. To me, it's the perfect combo.

After your first handful of hours playing, you start to understand the level design better and start pressing your luck a bit more. Which is a fair tradeoff to me since the first hours was filled with the excited nervousness of outsmarting the level and it's enemies. Now that I have more hours into the game, it's fun to aim towards much harder temples. Which are surprisingly very difficult most times.

My biggest fear is developer abandonment or server shutdown. Simply because current playerbase size. Which honestly, wouldn't shock me at all since it's not looking great. But my biggest hope is this game getting the love and attention it deserves for being a perfect genre game that can entertain a wide audience. Because I am looking forward to enjoying this for years to come, if possible.\t\t\t\t\t
