\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was fun and a bit more geared towards speed running, however after the latest update it's even more slow. You used to be able to open boxes in midair, now you have to wait until you land and your whip retracts (No clue how this makes sense).

The amount of coin boxes spawning now seems to be lessened. I used to be able to buy upgrades on the first or second level and sometimes now you don't even get enough. This one is understandable as it adds to the randomness, but it is a bit painful getting to the end and only missing 1 or 2 coins.

They added in this essence thing for leveling up but don't even have any mention of what it's for or how it helps in the game. What?...

The whole coin box thing is interesting in such that if you're the first to get to the box then you can just loot and run, however if you're not the first there you're stuck just sitting there waiting for the coins to come. How does this help out new players who don't have the speedy whip? They're stuck waiting behind on coins and getting hit by stuff. This is either discouraging or makes you have to go slow so you don't have to wait for the coins to "flip" out of the box.

The worst offenders are the guardians. They spawn randomly and sometimes you have no way to avoid them where they spawn. I've had the purple ball guardian spawn ON ME while I was in mid-air. How am I supposed to avoid that? Other issues I've had is where they spawn under you in mid-air and you have no ability to adjust to avoid them. The laser eye guardian also sometimes spawns and shoots and you get no audio cue or anything and get shot.

This game can be very fun, but I no longer have interest in the game as it stands. Maybe I'll come back to it in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
