You make the split second decision to jump into the seemingly bottomless pit next to you as apposed to the spikes that would surely make you into an adventurer shish kabob. Your stomach drops as you look into the abyss bellow you, you've got to act quick. You grab hold of your trusty whip, aiming for the ledge just past that damn'd spike trap. Your whip flies true, you pull back with all your might, launching yourself from the abyss onto the ledge. You take a moment to collect yourself, you're getting too damn old for this.
You're shocked back into action by the telltale rumbling as the reddish glow of that thing fills the hallway behind you. You can see that stupid Relic just ahead, only a few more traps to go. You just need to keep running, dodging and weaving your way through the never ending stream of arrows that are flying out from the walls. Almost there, you can just about feel the relic in your hands. You just need to get past this last pitfall, a few jumps onto these floating platforms and your're there. You make the first jump with ease, but you find yourself waiting on the next platform. The red glow behind you in getting brighter, the rumbling causing your legs to quake... or maybe that's just the adrenaline wearing off and the exhaustion kicking in. You've gotta keep moving, the platforms be damned. You gauge the distance, you should be able to make it... in theory. Backing up to get a running start, you pray to whatever is listening down here in this never ending nightmare. Your legs launch you forward and you soon find yourself without anything between you and an untimely demise. Your whip shoots forward towards the wall just bellow the other side of the pit, its going to be close. You begin to pull back before you're even sure that the whip has attached itself to the wall.
It seems like your prayers were answered, you find yourself flying towards the ledge. You stretch out your arm, feeling as though you are sure to dislocate it. The tips of your fingers just reach the ledge, along with a final push from your legs and a pull from your whip and you've got a hold of it. As you pull yourself over the ledge you can feel the last of your strength leaving your body. You want nothing more than to just lay here at this ledge forever, no more running and jumping, just resting. Sadly life is never that simple, you find yourself bathed in that damn'd red light. You stand up and begin to stumble forward, the rumbling growing stronger by the second. Your bones rattle inside you as you cross the threshold into the relic room, finally. You can finally catch your breath as you look behind you to get a closer look at that damn'd thing. Radiating head and glowing flames bathing the hall in that red glow. Burning everything around it, however it has stopped at the door. An invisible force stopping it from going any further, all it can do is stare at you with its unblinking eyes.
You turn round back towards the relic, its finally time to be done with this temple. You walk forward up the stairs and grab hold of your prize, a golden idol that is shaped just like the statues around the temple. As you grab hold and lift it from its pedestal the temple around you begins to collapse, seeming to have lost the thing that was holding it together. As the temple beings to seal itself all around you, that familiar tugging sensation in your chest reminds you of your purpose. The tugging becomes a had pull as you find yourself yanked from the temple and back in your relic room. You place your newest fine together with the others and dust yourself off, one step closer to freedom. You almost can't remember what life was like before you got trapped in this never ending cycle of relic hunting...
You shake your head, now is not the time to dwell on the past, first you've got the finish this never ending mission. You walk past the giant statue in the middle of the room, some sort of trapped god or demon. Your fates now tangled together, you no longer need to fear death but at the cost of your freedom. Until you've collected enough relics you're trapped here together with this thing. You look at the doorway in front of you, wondering what temple will lie ahead of you this time. Hopefully it will be an easier one this time around, or at least you could get a useful blessing. You let out a sigh as you walk through the door, back into the abyss you go.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is suuuper addictive! The gameplay loop is already solid and most of - if not all - the trap rooms are interesting and challenging. There are however a few issues (but seeing as this is an early access game, I would let them slide). First of all, most of the time the game runs smoothly, but in certain rooms/levels like the second relic room for some reason and in temples with a large amount of phantoms the framerate drops significantly (usually at 170, drops to around 27fps). There are also some issues with the whip not attaching at times, but this is relatively rare. Another point is that there aren't THAT many different trap rooms, and I feel there should be more rooms to keep things fresh (but I'm sure this will be addressed in later patches). All in all a great title and I'm excited to see the future updates!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRewriting this review, since I progressed further.

I blessed all the whips and beat all the challenges this game has to offer (Missing a few relics, but with the best whips it's only a matter of time). It's been so much fun, the kind that creeps into your schedule and demands more. This game is right up my alley and I find it to be one of the most enjoyable game this year. I hope they add more to this game.

I strongly recommend this game if you enjoy any kind of movement and platformer entertainment. The skills and special whips you can get can greatly improve your mobility and soaring through rooms in different ways is always a blast.

The game has a few problems, some connectivity issues and some clanky hit detection cost me 4-5 runs but compared to everything the game offers, it's very small.\t\t\t\t\t
