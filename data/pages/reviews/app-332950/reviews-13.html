I'm gonna talk mainly about the new (4th chapter here). Here's the vid of gameplay that I'm gonna refer to a lot:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S44OZGQrV4w
(I COSPLAYED nurses so you can also check that out btw)

+ The visuals are amazing! The levels are still fascinating to look at and to explore. I'd say that the visuals and the ambiance only improves with each new level. Absolutely amazing. The level design is smart, you rarely get in a situation where you get caught in a dead end and most of the deaths you experience due to poorly managing your power or pathing.
+ Powers are fun to play with and the new ones are pretty good. I think mechanic-wise, it's a job well done.
+ The voice acting and character models are impressive. I love how interesting each character model and menacing at the same time.
+ Bierce has big booba.

- Game breaking bugs and just a ton of annoying ones in the last chapter. In the video you can see I played through Torment Therapy and my game crashed 3 times and I died to recently stunned nurse. I'm pretty sure that the devs will fix all of that soon but that is very annoying after 2 (or is it 3 now?) years of development and waiting.
- The pacing get worse and worse with each level. It's a fast paced horror. You wanna run, use your abilities and get spooked. You don't wanna sit through 20 mins of intro scenes that aren't even cutscenes that you can skip. Like going around rooms and pressing f to get out of them repeatedly or sitting through a song about fun town. Oh joy. I'ts cool to see once, after that it's annoying. I can't wait to sit through it all again and again trying to get Rank S on each level to unlock lore. I'd suggest letting players skip through all unnecessarily long intro sequences when they replay the levels.
- Achievements are bugged too. I got the one for "avoiding dying to traps" when like 15/16 deaths were to traps (I'm exaggerating a bit but you get the point)...

Overall:
I'd say that the game is still impressive. The new levels are amazing, They get better, scarier, harder, pose more challenges and introduce better boss battles. BUT the pacing gets only worse especially if you want to 100% the game and replay levels for better score. I really don't know what to think right now after all this waiting. I'd say that the devs shifted attention to a side project really backfired as the more you wait the higher are your expectations, so after all that time getting barely playable (due to all the bugs) game is disappointing. I would rate Dark Deception 9 crazy monkeys / 10 cuz it's an incredible game but I'd rate the update 5 nurses / 10 until it gets fixed and playable (which I'm sure will happen very soon and I promise to update the review).
