\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've yet to finish the full game, but after finishing the first 3 of 7 available levels, with more to come, this is some pretty high quality stuff!
Atmosphere is effective, enemies are TERRIFYING, and the maze design of the levels will REALLY make you panic when anticipating where they are, how close they are, and if you can find an available escape route if they do find you.
Each level has its own gimmick and theme, each one being equally scary and cool, managing to make something usually generic feel fresh again!

What I wasn't anticipating was the level of accessibility. Easy and hard mode are obvious, but you can also have unlimited respawns (if you don't care about your final score) and your progress is always saved for how much you have picked up.
So if you want a challenge, this WILL challenge you. If you just want to play the game without too much worry about dying, this game will give you that.
But even with your powers and unlimited respawns, this game will never stop being terrifying, so all horror fans will be satisfied!

My only major complaint so far, outside of the mouse sometimes not working in menus, is that the boss fights are TERRIBLE!
I've only fought two of them so far, but I imagine the rest are designed the same. In that they're just you running around in an arena. Intense? Yes. Fun? No. The running in circles as you wait everything out gets tiring after the first minute, and it was never made clear that you should be waiting it out.\t\t\t\t\t
