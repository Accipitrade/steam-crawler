'You want to S rank? Can't use a checkpoint. That means you need to run down an empty hallway and wait for two elevator rides that have nothing to do with the gameplay, it's just to waste your time.'
'Hey, you got 20 shards! Congrats! To reward you, here's a giant pop up that takes up your entire field of view. Better hope nothing is coming down that hallway!'
'You want a warning when something is coming. Sure. Maybe. If we feel like it, we might change the music. Or might not.'

If you get the powerup that shows where enemies are, you can see how screwed you are a good long while early when you get into a large area and see someone heading up one of the only paths out.
