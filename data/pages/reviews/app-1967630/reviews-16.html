\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWow what a game! After Stranded Deep I have been just itching for another cast away crafting game and this is SOOO much more! TY soooo much devs for bring this game into my live!

To be fair Stranded deep was just hands down beautiful with regards to the ocean and underwater and has this game beat there but everything else is +++

Took me a few hours to get into it, used to the mechanics and get totally hooked (so stay with it people!) This game is a GEM, I cannot believe it was not more high profile on youtube for upcoming crafting games, I spent hrs on there looking for games like this! Quite annoyed on one hand but pleased it exists on the other, I found it in steam discovery queue! Took me about 10 hours before I got a foot hold on my survival (comfortable ish with food, water protection etc). Up to 30 hrs now and haven't even built a boat yet for the other islands lol.

I am loving all your mechanics; I see you were going for realism and I think is really pays off. I love the mapping, the spread-out resources, how crafting takes time and the day spins forward an hour or more etc. I love the rain (soaked) and sun (sun burn), all the other conditions, the item weights.

And holy hell! The amount you can craft is amazing! 34 work stations for example and that just scratches the surface with the other stuff going on.. weapons, boats, building, carts, containers etc. With regards to crafting this game P's all over stranded deep as does the story and lore! My hat is off to you Dev's sir's.

I've seen a review moaning about container storage weight limit... seems real to me too much weight gona burst a container made of sticks. AND you can just put stuff on the ground like Stranded Deep! (If you prefer not to put heavy stuff in your boxes etc.)

Also seen a review moaning about collectable stone only in certain points when you pass rock formations all the time... just think of it as in you need a certain type of stone. Makes it more real and having to trek for resources it's clearly part of this game as it should be in any survival. Plus, with mapping and a cart or even no cart it is a piece of P.

My base is coming on a treat after 30 hrs and have soo much more to do!

After 30hrs no bugs no crashes I would be happy if this was a finished game (so far... no idea how complete it all is and how far I can go etc) but I can't wait to check out their road map if there is one.

I have a small wish list but will put that in the discussion pages i.e., pin craft recipes.
