\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game so far. It's a shame it's flying under the radar of more popular content creators.
Going to start off by saying this game has great potential. The survival part of the game is top tier. Managing illnesses, along with food, water, energy and time, is very well done.
There are some places that need a bit more work though.
The building is very basic. Does what it needs to, but there's a whole community of gamers that like to build more then just the basic box, with a roof. It also lacks furniture. So can't really decorate. If you can craft all these "machines," you should be able to make atleast some basic furniture.
As for the resources you gather.. it needs some work. Things don't respawn or replenish as they should. Or slower then they should. But I'm sure they'll iron that out in future patches. But it's worth mentioning.
Then there's some other QoL things that I'd like to see implemented. But it's still early access, so those features may come down the line.
All-in-all, it's got great bones and I hope it only gets better with time.\t\t\t\t\t
