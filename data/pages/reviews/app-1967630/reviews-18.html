The only thing I can comment on is I notice frame drops and stuttering at times usually involving fire and high foliage but it doesnt happen often and doesnt last for too long. I used to hate survival games but I have recently become addicted and Im enjoying the grind so far. I do hope for a roadmap to know what to expect but regardless I will look forward to the devs updating an already S tier game with or without one! I made my wife buy this game too \t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter my first time playing, I immediately see a huge problem with this game: base management. One of the first things you do in a survival crafting game is survey resources available in an area and decide where to put your base. However, in this game, resources take a long time to regenerate, so you will either have to constantly move your base, or travel further and further for resources (and that doesn't include how much the story quests move you around the map). With the very limited and restrictive movement/carry weight system (you stop completely dead if you are even .1 overweight, which I would suggest a change to maybe 75% movement speed reduction and unable to jump or run, when you are overloaded).

An overlooked possible feature of this game, right at the start, is salvaging the debris from your ship... Like, you see pieces of it all over, but can't do ANYTHING with it.
It's a mountainous island that you start on right? WHY ARE ROCKS SO RARE?

I'm not even 2 full hours into this game so far, so my review will likely change. Maybe it's just the learning curve, but for at least the first couple of hours, I am not particularly enjoying this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tClassic survival game with great exploration, a unique mapping system, polished ocean navigating, and detailed yet rewarding food, water, fatigue, and disease/ailment systems. The game world is beautiful and each new biome/area provides a new survival experience. The crafting system is very well established with several tiers of weapons and equipment, specialized crafting stations, complex cooking, as well as fully fledged base building complete with wall trophies, lamps, and rugs. The gameplay does feel a bit grindy until your base is established. Fast travel with your boat to player-built docks makes returning to your base a breeze. For a survival crafting game that has only just entered Early Access, this game feels surprisingly whole.\t\t\t\t\t
