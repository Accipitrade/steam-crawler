\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was not a fan of the GUI / UX in this game. Everything felt very artificial - e.g. collecting palm leaves by pressing E when staring at the trunk to pop-up a container-like view, selecting the leaves I wanted, and then pressing "collect". I didn't feel immersed in the environment at all. I also didn't love the awkwardness of pressing RMB to start throwing a spear, then RMB to put it away, but LMB to actually throw it.

I want to address the badger combat that a few other reviewers have complained about.
I think the problem with the combat is two-fold.

1. It seems like the player's block is very slow, like pressing RMB has a delayed reaction / the actual block occurs a non-negligible amount of time afterwards. This applies also to the attack.
2. It didn't seem like the badger had any telegraphing before the attack, so as a player it felt like I was just pressing RMB at an arbitrary moment in time in order to block the attack that would come later.

The combination of those two factors made the combat feel clunky and non-responsive. That could be addressed by tightening up player animations (make them much more responsive) and making sure enemies have a clear "pause" period before their actual attack so that the player can recognize and block at that exact moment.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIve played quite a few survival crafters and feel i have a good base line to compare to.

Thus far, this one needs some work.

The harvesting is quite bland with no animations, just a pop up screen.
The crafting UI is pretty terrible and un-intuitive.
The durability on items is ridiculously low, almost 1 shot use.
The progression of crafting tech doesnt seem particularly streamlined.
Item quantity requirements feel really high in comparison to low harvested items.
Map shows icons where "resources" are - loses a bit of immersion.
Resources have recharge times listed meaning static spawns which is just meh.

I was hoping for a mix of green hell / icarus but at the moment this game is way off. Great concept just not particularly well implemented.

Everything seems laborious, from killing to crafting to harvesting - and not in a fun way.
General gameplay wise, it got boring quite quickly - plugged away to try and do more but its just...dull.

Worst part: You cant harvest whilst being hunted by an animal. (The hunted animal was a snake 10 metres away just looking at me....just meh)

4 hours on record otherwise i would request a refund.

May try it again in the future once some work has been done.\t\t\t\t\t
