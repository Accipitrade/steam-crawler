\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty good DLC (i mean the idea, release seems a bit too early) I really love to play it, seems interesting so far. However I can see some problems which might should be fixed in the future.

- sold wine and beer bottles does not show up as income at the report page under farm house, which is a pity because report page is needed to keep track of business

- Sold beer botttles show up as sold wine at the account history

- every blend seems to be different object... thus independently needs to be set for sale at farm house trade. But not clear what the price depends on

- this warehouse problem actually ruins the whole brewery game: industrial brewery with level 5 workers overproduces malt, aroma and bittering hops side products but stops producing if raw hops run out. Brewery should definitely run the #4 process, the actual beer production even if input material ran out for #1 #2 and #3 but there is plenty of material for #4.

- brewery piles up huge amount of raw malt and hops, thus small brewery actually takes up resource from common warehouse, blocking the industrial brewery. Moreover material is now stuck at the small brewery, and cannot be removed... production building warehouse resource should be removed with the shift click like relocating between warehouses.

- wine business looks a bit strange. It does not really seem to worth to bother with growing grapes, its value is simply too low compared to the workforce and machinery needed for it. Otherwise if I grow a lot of grapes, it expires too quickly (probably just as in real world)... So best way seems to buy grapes for production to keep winery running all year and meanwhile produce grapes to the market for fun. Same like base game animal food, simply does not worth to make own grass/silage/chops. Probably grapes need to be more valuable.
- not clear if grapes and hops are orchards or plants? Which profession of workforce needed?\t\t\t\t\t
