General Impressions

This pack gives you the Marksman Rifle, the Sniper Scope (7.5x), and the Remote Explosive GTK for Resistence Mode, as well as a reskinned pistol and motorcycle for Story mode. It would be best to buy this during a sale, as I have for a cheaper price. Otherwise, you could continue trying to go through missions and purchase the weapon and attachment crates.

I purchased this because I got lucky with receiving the silencer for the Battle Rifle & Variants, which only made this purchase an instant stress reliever from having to make long range encounters with iron sights. If you know you can complete some of the easier (Enemy at the Gates, Burnt Pieces, Breach & Clear..) resistence missions, then this will expedite the process.\t\t\t\t\t
