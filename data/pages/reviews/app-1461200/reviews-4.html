It's a collection of fun minigames and race challenges, with some light customization options for the ducks you can train. Maxing out all your stats will take about 2-3 hours max, so replaying the minigames is the best value here. That being said, the games are really fun and arcade like. Not limited to Flash, the game is in widescreen and looks incredibly sharp. The in game music is very boring but can be turned off. The change in engine does affect the minigames themselves, which veterans of the Flash version will note play different. There are a few minor changes as well, such as freely picking what mini games you can train with and EXP seems to be more generous here. Overall this is a fun and simple game, but fans of the original will notice how the switch to Unity affects gameplay.\t\t\t\t\t
