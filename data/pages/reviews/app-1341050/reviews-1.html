\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI hate to echo other bad reviews, but this game is an increasingly frustrating experience, to the point where I don't think I can muster the energy to finish it. There is potential for a good game somewhere in here, but it's drowned in a sea of questionable design decisions. You use costumes to do pretty much anything, you can have 3 of them on you at a time, except switching between them takes time, and if you fall or take any sort of hit you lose the costume and have to trek back to go get it... this isn't a problem for costumes that are specific to the level you are currently playing, but if you want to 100% complete every level, you need to bring in costumes, for either later or previous levels with you, and if you lose them, there is no way to get it back other than to exit the level, go back to the level the costumes is originally from, hope you remember exactly where in the level it is if it's a rare costume, and then probably farm multiple copies of said costume in the hopes of avoiding the tedium again. This could've been avoided in a number of ways, once you die your costumes refresh, makes costumes unlockable once but harder to actually unlock... have an actual health system.... there is also the fact that to get some of the collectables you need to sit through Balan Bouts, which are basically Quick time events that re-use assets most levels, where you have to time button presses copies of Balan lining up with him, in the earlier levels you have to do this 3 times perfectly to get a trophy and as the game progresses you have to do this 4 5 even 6 times, and if your timing is any less than perfect for any 1 of those 6 button presses you don't unlock the trophy and there is no restart option other than exiting the level and starting all over, add to that the fact that some of those are off the beaten path and they become increasingly frustrating to deal with. Add to all of this a story that's not actually explained in the game itself but a tie in comic.... and this makes for a very confusing experience.... I wanted to give this one a chance.....and while I did't completely hate my time with Balan.... I don't think I will be going back to it to finish it\t\t\t\t\t

This evil is manifested by creatures known as Toms, the polar opposite of the Tims.
A couple tips for new players: 1. there is an amazing jump technique where you jump after falling off an edge to get extra distance. 2. the platforming gets significantly better as you develop new abilities throughout the game. 3. be on the lookout for Tim eggs.
