\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeen watching this one develop for a while and decided to make the plunge and get the early access version (which almost never happens).

First impressions:

Visually: Absolutely gorgeous, I would be hard pressed to find a pinball game that looks this good and polished, definitely in the same league as Pro Pinball Remastered. The rustic looking ball is a nice touch. I play in 1920x1080 with all the eye candy on.

Audibly: Good music and speech, pretty good proportionate mix on volume levels (but customizable). Does have some lapses in music at times but it doesn't bother me enough to knock it.

Gameplay: Not bad at all, could use a little tuning and tweaking but the experience was very enjoyable. Physics are pretty on, but i'd like to see a little more bounce on the flippers (unless this was intended). Ball movement is nice and smooth. Does seem to fly into the outlanes at turbo speed at times but that's kind of what I'm used to with Pinball Arcade. Nudge is about what i would expect it to be.

Control: I use an XBOX 360 wireless controller. No lags whatsoever in the flippers and it's nice and smooth. Flipper power seems a little on the weaker side but it feels pretty good, not too stong like other games. The ability to use both sets of buttons is nice. I prefer the bumper buttons to the triggers myself.

Some observations: Default view was the camera moving with the ball location which was a little bit stuttery. I like a still playfield so one tap of the back button got to me the view i wanted with no camera movement. After that it was very smooth with no lags or hiccups.

My rig is a bit dated and custom built but played this game just fine.

My Specs:

Intel Core i7 3770 @ 3.4 GHz
Asus Sabertooth Z77 Motherboard
16GB RAM (DDR3 - PC3-10700)
eVGA nVidia Geforce GTX 970
Intel 240GB SSD
WD 1TB Velociraptor (10K)
Creative SB X-Fi
LG BD-RE Optical Drive
Corsair Wired Gaming Keyboard/Mouse
2x Dell 1080p Montitors
XBOX Wireless Receiver for PC
XBOX 360 Wireless controller (Chrome)

Overall Opinion: Very well done. Love the theme, all the bells and whistles, music and of course how beautiful it is to look at. Solid gameplay which could use a couple of fine tunings but this was a lot of fun to play. Nice smooth experience with no studders or hiccups (other than camera on mode).

Recommendation: Absolutely. I love pinball and I'll take it any way I can get it. I'm an average player who has been playing for 25+ years. Can't wait to see what these guys come up with next.\t\t\t\t\t
