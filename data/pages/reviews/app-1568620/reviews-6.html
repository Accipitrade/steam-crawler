\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA diamond in the rough. In a genre that is quickly becoming overpopulated you occasionally find that one delight, this would be it for me. There have been very few bugs that I've experienced, certainly nothing game breaking. The potential softlocking, due to running out of funds, is a problem but can easily be recovered. I've not struggled for cash, but if you do run out just hop into some missions and get killed by the ghost. So longs you grab a few artifacts and remember to enter the ghost that killed you into your journal you can make money. Not ideal, and should be addressed by the devs, but definitely workable.

I'm about 12 hours in, lvl 46 at the time of writing this, and have experienced most ghosts (been killed by them too). Each ghost has a unique model, interesting mechanics, secondary behaviours, exorcism (even if the exorcism can be simple at times) and has been enjoyable so far. I still have much left to unlock and I'm sure I will be coming back time and time again. For fans of the ghost hunting genre I would absolutely recommend this.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs far as ghost hunting games & this genre go this is a hidden gem.

Pros:
- All the ghosty goodness
- Decent progression system with expert levels
- Lots of different ghosts to hunt
- Class roles that you can choose from with unique specialties
- Up to 6 players

Cons:
- Due to it not having a lot of feedback there's still quite a few buggy things in game
- Voice system doesn't register sometimes
- There's only a few different maps you can play on as of this review
- Finding the ghost can often be too easy
- Audio bugs with repeating squeaky doors & ambient noises
- The different difficulties need a bit of tuning

There are a few other things I could mention, but they're not that important. All in all it's a really good game. It's not as polished as phasmaphobia if that's what you're looking for, but if you like phasma you will definitely like this game as well with some of the new twists, and extra little goodies!

The game isn't perfect, and can be slow at the start. I'm saying this because it seems a lot of people only play it for a couple hours, and then write a bad or negative review. Please for the sake of the game, and it's future give it a little more time than that. Get to level 50 to get your first role. Unlock the ghost expertise ranks, etc.. It's a fun game with a refreshing twist, and the devs are constantly active and responding in the discord to feedback. As of right now i'd give the game about a 7/10, but with some tuning, polishing, and a few gameplay mechanic tweaks over time this game could easily be a 9/10 if not higher in my opinion.\t\t\t\t\t

Was quite a pain to get multiplayer to work but finally after 30 minutes was having a blast. Pretty steep learning curve but if you take the time... underneath all the rough indie dev stuff.... There's a beautiful hidden gem here. Many cool features that can make this game 10/10 alone. Give it a shot it's worth it.

Nothing like having a team destroyed by the Nun then spawn on you, teleport you to the church charging at you and you BLAST off with a shotty. The sigh of relief and the words that came out... That moment alone made this game so worth.

(UPDATE)
This game is soooo good. Having nearly defeated every ghost and played way more, I find myself shocked at how good this game is. Don't get me wrong the bugs are so infuriating. Some even game breaking to where you have to restart. But when you're playing with a friend... Hours go by. Once you hit 50 and get a perk it changes the whole game. I picked The Medium and that allows me to find the ghost once and always tell when a ghost is going to hunt. 20/10 I love this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGhost hunting gamer perspective:
Easy to jump in, got the usual EMF, ghost box, salt etc but all the entities have cool lore, personalities and different ways to banish them to the deepest hells where they came from.
All the items you leave will vanish, so keeping items with you or taking them back to your car from time to time is a smart choice and brings a interesting aspect to the game.
Can be challenging and even in beginning recommended to just identify the entity and if you don't have the experience or tools to banish the entity just mark it in the evidence and GTFO. (Trust me). You will get your revenge when you are a true ghost hunter. Devs are really nice which is always plus.
8/10 - Great potential and great world building. Needs bug fixing and little adjusting but framework is there.

Supernatural fan perspective:
Its everything you dreamed of ever, since you jumped into that impala with the brothers to hunt ghouls and demons. Loads of references and familiar creatures and situations.
Just buy and experience, you won't regret it. Mind the early access bugs, but having it in the library until its bug free and you will have a blast.
9/10 - I can color impala and chill in the gas station looking for jobs with friends to hunt down supernatural creatures. I have rock salt shotgun and chalk for pentagrams, while friend has alcohol and katana. I love it.\t\t\t\t\t
