\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat Game! Normally, I don't really leave much reviews, but this game deserves it. It really captures that sweet spot of Paranormal Investigator. I love the progression of it, as you as the player are also learning how to identify the monsters properly, and get comfortable with the process before you add fighting them into the mix.

There is a great variety of monsters, and ways to kill them from exorcisms to guns and katanas. Really it has that sort of Buffy the Vampire slayer/Supernatural monster hunting feel where you have to learn about the monsters, their quirks and weaknesses before you can fight and destroy them. The monsters themselves are unique, with different abilities and gameplay functions that fit them.

Can't wait to see what the devs come up with next for the game, I'd love maybe a base you can store trophies as you grow and destroy more evil. Perhaps even more weapon types or even armor.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEdit: 5/4/2023 - More hours in and I'm relatively happy for the most part still. T for the flashlight was a game changer for me. I cannot wait until the flashlight is added to the book (maybe an led lit bookmark?) and gun (might I suggest a light attachment to the gun) Can reliably hunt on rookie now without constantly dying like some of the other games are. There's still some EA glitchy stuff with audio and graphics and that's fine for early access.

Edit: 5/2/2023 - Dev tells me the flashlight works with most tools! Thank you Andre. That makes a world of difference. I am a happy hunter. Still learning the ropes and glad to see excited and responsive developers. I still think there's some polish that needs applied, it is early access so that's to be expected. I really do like this game a lot.


First Blush Review: This is pretty good for an EA hunt and hide style game. I could see it becoming a great thing, with a little bit more polish. It's a tad video glitchy in spots... audio is in consistently too loud than not loud enough. The shadows are very wonky looking making it sometimes difficult to do one of the major hunting actions of finding the name and the effects sometimes are a bit much... like using the flashlight and getting too close to a wall or reflective metal door and it blinds me. I see it's trying to go for a gritty look, but really it kind of takes away from the whole thing. Easily could remedy this by adding a way to toggle off the gritty filter stuff and lessen the flashing light problem by bringing up the overall night time light. It doesn't have to be day, but it sure doesn't have to be darker than vantablack without a flashlight. I could get by all of that... you know... get used to it. Be a good little soldier.

My biggest disappointment is the flashlight. Currently you cannot hold the flashlight and use the tools at the same time. If you are trying to do something that requires light and to use tools and it's a dark area... you're pretty much hosed. The tutorial house comes to mind, where you have to go round back to cleanse the soul...but you can't see where you're digging because you don't have a flashlight. As a solo player, it's disappointing. I don't have a little brother in game to watch out for and hold my flashlight while I dig up a grave... if I can't have a little bro bro to hold my light... I'd accept a brightness/gamma setting to bring up the lighting just a wee bit in the dark. The game is just waaaaay too dark like impossibly dark and that can be good, but when it blocks my progress entirely because my game character is not smart enough to hold two objects at a time (ie. flashlight and salt..) or perhaps my flashlight could be on a bungee... or even have it stay lit when I drop it. Even on a dark night you can get some ambient moonlight in a window so I don't quite understand why it's so stinkin dark.

As it is... I'd say buy it if you like hunt and hide paranormal games, buy it if you're a man of letters. However,iIf you're new to the genre of hunt and hide type paranormal.. I'd say skip out or wait for a sale... there are better polished hunt and hide games... this one needs a tad more polish it to make it really shine bright.

Review will change as progress is made or lost. - I'm still gonna play, cause I paid for it. I like it, but can I recommend it? Not without some careful considerations on the above points.\t\t\t\t\t
