\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR; The game is amazing, affordable, fair, fun, scary, and every update increases my addiction.

This is every Supernatural fan's wet dream. You're not just finding out the unknown entity, but casting it back to the deep depths of the afterlife. There's comedic relief in the Gas Station's menu board and in the Newspaper Articles you can read as you're looking for your next hunt. Your impala is decked out with ample clothes to survive you and your friends for any occasion, gear for your hunts, and much more.

Why not ride off into the sunset, knowing your supernatural hunting skills are being used to make a bit of quick cash while saving one family at a time.

Now to be serious..

Pros;
Up to 6 player co-op (a couple maps have been designed with this in mind).
Highly active devs who try to push new content out while simultaneously mitigating and improving what's available currently. This is a passion project started during COVID and all they want is to see others enjoy it and love it as much as they do. They're super chill dudes.
Squashed bugs constantly.
FIVE inventory slots.
Consistent behaviors (i.e. The entity doesn't just do whatever. The entity is chosen by RNGesus and you get that entity's behaviors, not some other entity's behaviors), playing more means learning more (the more you hunt, the better studying you've done of its abilities).
Early access (which scares some away but I honestly forget that it's an EA 99% of the time, there's been so much progress.)
It's only 12$ something after tax. It's really not a lot of money for the content available right now and replayability it has.

Cons;
There's some bugs. No, not the ones I mentioned earlier. There's spiders and roaches. Burn the house with fire.

Bottom line;
Try the game out for yourself or do some research about it. Check out some streamers who play the game, if they're a veteran...Poke their brain. Ask them all the nitty gritty details. I promise, none of us bite, unless you want us to.

Much love to Paper Promises Programming for their continued hard work, amazing game, and the friendships made from such an incredible game! Love you guys lots!!!\t\t\t\t\t
