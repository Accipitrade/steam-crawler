1.) No 21:9 support planned
2.) Broken languages (French/German/English are mixed in the game)

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is so SO fun, our group of three can play for several hours a session and not want to stop. The gameplay loop is incredibly satisfying, and we can go from defeating ghosts in under a minute (usually Banshees) to spending around 15 minutes on one ghost. We love that we all have our own roles and masteries, though you don't have to necessarily play that way. And I LOVE the voice recognition system with exorcisms and the priest role, so freakin cool! The visuals are probably the weakest part right now imo, but they're honestly charming in their own way, there's some very goofy moments with the player/ghost models especially.

Tl:dr, if you often have game nights with friends, grab this asap! Support these devs, they have an awesome system here that needs more recognition and funding!\t\t\t\t\t
