Amazing Ghost Hunting game and so cheap. Definitely worth picking up and playing with some friends.


TL;DR: Probably my favourite ghost hunting game. Not as scary/creepy as other ghost hunting games, but you actually feel like a GHOST HUNTER rather than a ghostfacer.

Pros:
* Huge list of different ghosts to hunt
* Each ghosts has unique strengths and weaknesses
* You can actually defend yourself and fight back against the ghosts. Exorcising them feels very satisfying.
* Getting kidnapped by a ghost and dragged off into their dimension is spooky.
* Great fun playing with friends
* It's still very early in early access, but the Devs have implemented lots of improvements already, so hopefully it'll keep improving over time.


Cons:
* The tutorial is god ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ awful and incredibly buggy. Having a good tutorial is so important for any game, so I'm shocked it hasn't been fixed yet.
* It just doesn't feel that scary/creepy. Especically when compared to similar games.
* Occasionally crashes and disconnects in multiplayer
* Voice system often doesn't register, especially when using spirit box.
* You can tell a lot of the ghosts by their models, which kinda takes away some of the fun in figuring out what ghost it is.
* Early levels is a bit grindy to unlock all the items (feels bad figuring out what a ghost is, but having no way to exorcise it)
* Devs are probably gonna get sued by Supernatural. lol.

Room for improvement:
* A hunt starting and IMMEDIATELY getting one shot and losing all your stuff really sucks and is bad game design. - Maybe add a slight delay before the ghost can actually kill you.
* Some maps are insanely empty and boring (looking at you, Factory!) - really needs stuff in it to make it interesting.
* Needs more ghost events and creepy animations - like the Ring girl in Demonologist.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love the logic in this game, it might be janky early access but most of the problems are easily overcome for now and hopefully fixed long-term. If you plan to play multiplayer make sure you select the same region, you may also need to swap to a different region and then back to get it to work.
I don't spend much time waiting around for evidence, I find the game is very engaging in that respect. If you play on rookie and stand around doing nothing but waiting for an EMF reading then sure you might be just there waiting, but that's not how you do it. There are loads of different ways to locate the ghost and deduce what type it is, and different types have different defensive precautions you can take while you explore and look for evidence of the name, as well as to narrow your conclusions.
With at least 4 fully distinct different types of exorcisms for different ghosts, and how these overlap with the varying evidence types of ghosts, you start to see why I say I love the logic in this game. Journal evidence is shared in multiplayer, and there is friendly fire for the weapons so be careful. I think I remember hearing up to 6 players can join one lobby, though I have only tried so far with 2. Public lobbies are a thing but I have yet to see one so best for solo play or with friends.\t\t\t\t\t

---

I love the concept and there's plenty of neat ideas in this game but the execution of it feels lacking.

Evidence gathering just feels like a chore. As far as I've been able to tell there's no real pattern or way to reliably find evidence. All you can do is walk around the entire house with each piece of equipment hoping you get lucky enough to have one trigger because it doesn't appear to be related to ghost activity. I've had times where the ghost opened a door right in front of me and none of the evidence gathering tools show anything.

In the rare cases where I do find evidence it's very hard to tell what happened. A cold spot can appear, go below freezing, and disappear in less than 30 seconds so if I wasn't looking directly at the thermometer then it's easy to miss. The EMF is similar but at least it also makes noises to alert you.

I'm also not a huge fan of the creature-specific experience system. You only get experience for a type of creature if you can banish it and at low levels you can't banish most of them so you can't unlock any of their behavior information.

All in all, this is a game to keep an eye on but I wouldn't recommend it in the current state.\t\t\t\t\t
