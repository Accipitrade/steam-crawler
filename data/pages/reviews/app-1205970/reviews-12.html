I read the reviews and avoiding buying the game until it went on sale. So scared I avoided buying the game till almost the end of the sale lol! But with that said I gave it a risk. I played 20 minutes of the game it was a roller coaster of emotion but it's a good game (Except for the extra money for the DLC's). I recommend trying it. you have to play it for longer than 10 mins to get a feel for it! Do not let the reviews keep you from having a good experience with a fun game. Furthermore, there are toooooooo many add-on's. Way too many add-on's that it takes away from the base game. IN TOTAL THERE ARE 33 ADD-ON VEHICLES AND ONLY 24 IN GAME VEHICLES!

DEVELOPMENT is solid I have not ran into any bugs yet! So that is awesome. I know when the game first came out the map textures would disappear. The moment of the bike was laggish/sluggish the list at the time period goes on. With that said the game is Night and Day from launch it is a lot more cleaner and less bugs. This is not like the first couple of years like Super cross Encore where it was low key unplayable until the developers released a bunch of patches. Overall, the game is worth buying and checking out. The developers are constant releasing new patches to improve the game and make the game better and add more content.

BUGS so far I have found two. BUG ONE: I found a bug containing the AI. There are squids, there riding is all over the place and they are slow on the hardest level with no modifications done to the vehicle. Not too terrible but lack the neck and neck competition the other titles had. BUG TWO: This happened multiple times before I considered adding this to the list of bugs. When in freeride and driving up a steep hill or mountain, if you tap the clutch to get more power the bike jumps in the are about 10 feet. once you land you start rolling backwards slowly. you do not fall off the bike but what in the world is this bug/glitch.

GRAPHICS are clean and high definition but I was expecting a little more detail. For some reason it reminds me of farming simulator 2022.

PHYSICS are similar to Mx Vs. ATV All out. Especially, when landing from stunts it can be tricky. you must put the time into the game to understand how to ride with the default bike. This is NOT a simulator racing game. If you want a Simulator game get MxGP 2020 or newer. IMPORTANT!!!! The physics will change for the better over time because they are constantly releasing more patches for different aspects of riding. For example. the corning was a little buggy at first and then they released a patch recently to fix it. If there are issues with physics don't give up hope.

MUSIC is way better than expected. There is a wide variety like EDM, Rock,metal, Screamo (No pop/hip-pop). If any of you know who Scarlxrd is you will know is music cant even be put into a category and his music is in the game. BUT let be honest video game sound tracks are just a cherry on top it doesn't make the game better or worst considering we are all on pc we can just listen to music through a wide selection of music platforms.

MULTIPLAYER there are very little lobbies. I rarely can find any. I would like to since I have seen in reviews there is a race online (You Vs Real players online) and Squad Compound (Freeride with friends and play custom events)

CUSTOMIZATION is very good so good they make you buy name brand bikes. All jokes aside you can customize bike pretty heavy and your character as well. There are 6 different tuning levels for engine, clutch, chassis, suspension, traction, and braking. You can customize everything from the handle bars to the tires (Top to Bottom customization). Your character can be customized with actual branded clothing items without paying add-on prices (Already apart of the game). Lacks many brands and lacks styles like most modern gear have (retro Oneal colors or any thing of the such).

VEHICLES there are a wide selection. You have dirt bikes ranging from 125cc - 450cc, ATV'S only have 450cc, UTV's 1000cc only. In my opinion, there are too many add-on vehicles and not enough classes. Pitbikes 100% need to be added in. If we gonna have to buy Honda and Kawasaki add-ons give use the 500cc bikes. ATV'S should have at least 250cc class and UTV'S should have more classes as well.\t\t\t\t\t
