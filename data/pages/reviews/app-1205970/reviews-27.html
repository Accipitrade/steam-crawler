\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall really enjoying this game, but it still has its issues.
The game starts off quite frustrating on the 125cc, however once you begin upgrading the bikes and move up to the 250f it becomes really fun. This only takes about an hour of game play. Career mode has been fun so far with a progression system that isn't too difficult. Only done a few online races but these have all been really fun with good battles, and the squad system works well. The recent patch fixed a lot of the issues, and the game plays a lot better now. The gameplay itself feels a bit more difficult than previous games, but once you figure it out it is a lot of fun. Terrain deformation works differently, some tracks are only light and some get very very rough. The ruts from other players are railable without too much trouble which is awesome. Really looking forward to the release of the AMA national tracks. Biggest complaint now would be that the AI are just too slow, most races it is very easy to win by a min+ once you have a fully upgraded bike. Some higher difficulty levels would be a nice addition\t\t\t\t\t

I really want to support the devs, but the main advice I'd have for them is to focus on making the game feel as good as it looks (because the graphics are, to be fair, great).
