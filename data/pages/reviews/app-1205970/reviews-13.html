\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirstly, ignore the reviews with a few hours or less.. they haven’t played long enough to get the hang of the game and give it a fair review…

Secondly, I almost gave up in the beginning and refunded the game as I was cracking the sheets early on, trying to get the hang of it..

I am so glad that I kept with it!!! This game is awesome, the tracks feel like dirt bike tracks and ride like it too!
Plenty of difference tracks so you don’t feel like you are doing the same thing over and over.

Keep in mind the first bike with no upgrades is crap.. you will crash, you will flat land, you will under jump… BUT, once you earn some money you can upgrade the bike, get better and have a nice flow.

Change the settings!!
If you want a better feel for the bike and want to be in control, go to gameplay settings and change:
Airborne Throttle: User Input (use the throttle to control the bike in the air/ don’t hold throttle flat all the time in the air)
Pro Physics: Enabled (gives you full control of the bike)

Once you stop treating the game like a simple accelerator/ brake race car game and treat it like a dirt bike, it will get much easier.

I never use first person view in race games, but this is a must for this game, it feels like an actual dirt bike and is completely different than third person view.
In first person you can really see where you are going and spot you lines and use the track. (the game really feels like it was developed for first person view)
In third person I slide out everywhere and under jump, in first person it flows nicely and feels much better.

All the buttons/ controls have a purpose and you need to use them all!!
Mainly use Preload (right stick down) to get over jumps and use both Preload and seat bounce over the massive jumps until you get the hang of it.

Keep in mind there are two brakes, front and rear.
Front helps balance the front of the bike in the air and rear helps bring it down and cutting into corners.

Rear brake into hard corners and clutch tap out of corners!

Use your clutch, its there for a reason.. Clutch tap out of tight corners, clutch tap up steep hills, clutch tap on a slow landing.

Don't forget to lean into corners!
Lean back and get that front wheel skipping over the terrain!!

Just slowly get the hang of the bikes and then worry about coming first place and making the money to progress.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPROS-

-Really Awesome tracks

-the bike and rider customization is pretty nice, love that you can pick the color of tire tread, would like a bit more to the customization like creating my own livery on helmets suits bikes etc

-cool little story in career mode, its nice to have some character interaction in games to add a little bit of depth

-i love how it feels using both joysticks to get more air off of jumps, i play mxgp 2021 and i love it but its nice to have an arcade style dirt bike game with insane air time and tricks

-graphics are nice in races

CONS-

The ai sucks, they cant navigate the tracks properly, they basically dont exist for most of the races because they're so slow you smoke them and i even play on the hardest difficulty and even if i crash and get reset they still are nowhere in sight

-game doesnt support 3440x1440p so the menu looks like an n64 game, that includes the garage and customization screens, bikes are all stretched out and it feels like N64. The game itself in the compound a races the screen fits well and looks good but the menus are an eyesore

-like i said its nice to have an arcade style dirtbike game cause i play mxgp and its super realistic feeling so its nice to have a game i can do a 500 yard jump and land, but at the same time its still a bit lacking and i feel like the riders are a bit too stiff and some animations like ai riders revving at the starting gates looks jenky

-Having to pay extra money to have official bikes in the game seems a bit lazy and like a rip off to me. like are we really going to start breaking games down this much? its like if madden starts releasing with generic teams and you have to pay $3 per team if you want the real ones, its terrible. Ill gladly pay for dlc if it makes sense and its something you came up with after you release a full game , but these games are now being pulled apart and released as bits and its something ill no longer support.

Final thoughts it is a fun game, but the extra charge for brand name bikes is ridiculous and i have zero respect to THQ for that, like come on man people are out here struggling from inflation and you're just digging as deep in our pockets as you can for content that should be included, but on top of that your ai is terrible, how has this not been an urgent fix before release, your ai cant keep up on the hardest difficulty did nobody test the game out? or did they? and you just said aweeee **** it who cares...also no 3440x1440p, its 2022 you put other 21:9 sizes in why not add them all? its laziness...Ive been told they plan on doing more updates, if they fix all these things ill forgive them for charging me extra for honda suzuki yamaha etc and give it a recommend, but in its current state its a no from me\t\t\t\t\t
