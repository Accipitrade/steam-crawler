I haven't gotten to a point yet that allowed me to change to something else. If I'm wrong than whatever. i bought other bikes to race with other bikes. Not whatever this cluster truck of a game starts me on. I'm keeping this game around in hopes that it turns into something we all expected. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm not usually one to leave bad reviews on a game, especially for a series I love so much, but this game is possibly the worst game to ever hit the MX vs ATV series. The game as of its first week since launch has little to no bike customization, and very few options for rider customization. The on-ground physics feel like you're riding a dirt bike on a trampoline made of ice, and the in-air physics feel like you're trying to hit 350ft jumps with a 40mph cross wind blowing you sideways. The controls are by far the least satisfying and natural of all motocross games on the market. Bike sounds for 250 two strokes are all over the place, either basically muting in high rpm's or making four stroke noises. Graphics although high quality and pretty to look at, are glitchy and hitchy. For a game that had so much hype surrounding it, as well as a pushed back launch date, I don't know how this game is as awful as it is. I'm not asking for Reflex 2.0, I'm just asking for a polished game that isn't a downgrade from MX vs ATV Untamed. I'm very hopeful that more in game customization options are added to the game... quickly, as well as some extensive physics tweaks. I guess 2022 just wasn't a good year for dirt bike games.\t\t\t\t\t
