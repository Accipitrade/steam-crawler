\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAre you tired of feeling disappointed in yourself all the time? I know I was. That's why I bought this game and boy let me tell you, its been amazing being incredibly disappointed in something else for awhile. And for the low price of $39.99, you too can feel disappointed in something else!

For the real review..
Overall: I understand this isn't a Simulator, not everything is going to be "Realistic". However; this game is pretty terrible considering the amount of advertising and how they are literally sponsoring a portion of the AMA National Motocross series. I would expect a much more polished game.

CONS:
-Upgrade system is a joke. Parts are for show only, no reason to buy them for performance. I'd wouldn't mind the "Tuning" options if it was a combination of tuning and parts to increase performance. But its just tuning.
-Preload/seat bounce doesnt feel consistent in any manner. Sometimes you'll clear a jump, sometimes you wont despite taking the same line, at the same speed, doing the same maneuver.
-A 125 with some "tuning" is faster than a stock 450, yet still can barely clear the 700ft jumps unless you're a master at their poorly designed controls and physics.
-You cant really rev it out in the air to prepare to land and get power to the ground, it feels like each landing you just bog out for 1 second before there's power again.
-Audio makes no sense, noises hardly correlate to whats going on. Putting around a 2 stroke and it sounds like im blowing out the top end.
-Dumping the clutch for quick speed works randomly, sometimes you get a boost, other times you just bog out.
-Crash on the MX course thats nothing but 350ft doubles with 60ft take-offs? Might as well restart the race because you aint ever going to get enough momentum to hit the next world record sized jumps.
-I know all of these games are the same, but for the love of god can I just play all the stock bikes and all the stock modes without "earning" them for 5 hours in silly races and tutorials? Maybe I just want a have 450 so I can go mess around, maybe an "Arcade" mode versus nothing but Career.
-Last one, bikes look really stupid. This isnt a performance thing, I just hate how they look. I'm on this spaceship looking bike that can barely clear jumps at full throttle. I imagine the OEMs (honda, yamaha etc.) are probably contractually tied up with the ME Supercross / MXGP Games, and they probably have to avoid making something look similar but come on...

Pros:
-Trail mode on MX is actually really fun, I like mode quite a bit because its like wide open desert/dirt roads, then they mix in what is as close to single track type technical turning/gullleys/canyons as you can probably get in this type of game.
-I dont mind the UTV mode, in first person with custom camera it can be fun to feel like youre drifting the corners. Just turn the sound off because your UTV will sound like a dying whale the entire time.
-Supercross isnt terrible, its actually more fun than the Motocross modes. Still needs a lot of work though.
-The ability to explore the large ranch is pretty fun.
-Hydroplaning is fun once you have a good bike.


I dont recommend this game as it is now unless they drop it to $3.99 during a steam sale or fix all of the glaring issues that are outlined in these reviews.\t\t\t\t\t

I am honestly enjoying the crap out of the game, I think the freestyle mechanics are lacking and Reflex's system for backflips/360s was definitely a lot better.

As far as the racing goes, I think this is honestly the best it has ever been in any game they have released. They are clearly taking feedback and making big improvements every update, the tracks look amazing and are a blast to ride.

Biggest thing I noticed as far as the racing goes, tracks are NOT one line any more, there is a lot more opportunity to pass cleanly now when every track has multiple viable lines, instead of fighting over the one fast line in Reflex...

Looking forward to seeing where it goes, if they had some way to go back to the reflex style system for backflips/360's, and let you place your own ramps in free ride it would be a perfect game as far as I am concerned.

Hope they fix the clutch boost abuse, turning it into a chargeable thing like alive would probably help...

Overall I'd definitely buy it again.\t\t\t\t\t
