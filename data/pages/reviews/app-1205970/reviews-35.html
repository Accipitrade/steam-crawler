\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGood game. Just needs more polish.

Legends is without a doubt an improvement from All Out; but nowhere near as fluid as Reflex.
The physics are pretty bare-bones and bike handling feels rigid and imprecise, but gets better with upgrades and tuning.
The game is fun despite having some glaring issues that need fixing. Hopefully they get fixed with updates!

PROS
+ excellent track designs
+ impressive elevation and environments
+ realistic track roughness
+ good terrain deformation
+ excellent rider models and scaling
+ working scrubs and whips
+ more bike weight
+ adjustable camera view

CONS
- poor sound design
- bike sound is out of sync with rpm
- sound effects break immersion
- tires look like they float on the dirt
- limited rider animations
- unrealistic collisions
- can't change controller bindings
- too many micro transactions

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe best MX vs ATV game since Unleashed

Pros:

+Some of the best free roam tracks ever to be introduced in the series
+The trails mode is absolutely insane and incredibly intense. Playing with a friend is a real treat with this
+Nice quirky little story mode and characters introduced. You feel like you're onto something big as a new rider
+Excellent old school soundtrack. Happy Bowling For Soup made the cut. Reminded me of the early 2000s biking games
+Graphics are beautiful. Especially in first person.

Cons:
-Physics at times. Lands are sometimes quirky and you will go bouncing up like a pogo stick

Overall, this game is excellent. The handling took a while to get used to and the game (for some reason) starts you off in manual mode so you will need to change that in the settings.

Verdict: 9/10
