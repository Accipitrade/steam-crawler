\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame is a lot of fun in first person but feels ridiculously unfinished. Feels like a massive scam having a brand new game with little to no gear or bikes to choose from. What happened to unlocking cosmetics and upgrades through playing the game? Unfortunately the game feels as if they have taken away many of the great aspects from previous MX vs ATV games and just improved the graphics of the tracks, so that they can make a quick money grab from DLC which should be included in base game. There just isn't enough to the game. I feel as this should just be graphics and track update/ expansion to a previous game.

Positives
- First person is great
- Scenery and graphics look good
- Each level of bike feels significantly different (125 - 250f - 250 - 450f)

Negatives
- Tutorial is awful.
- Physics in third person look very unrealistic. Riders slide all over the place and lean awkwardly in air.
- First 2 hours of gameplay are very frustrating. NPCs fly past you on easiest difficulty, The starting 125 is gutless, can't clear any jumps. After you unlock the 250f it becomes too easy. "G.O.A.T" difficulty offers little to no challenge to win every race, especially enduro races.
- NPC animations are in 1 fps? Every time they change body position, twist throttle etc there is no animation it is instantly throttle on or off or going from seated to standing.
- DLC on release which should be included in a game of this price.
- Career mode has no value. There is no point to career mode as there is nothing to unlock other than few changes to your compound.
- Little to no customisation (without paying more money after purchasing a brand new game). Barely any gear to select from. I (stupidly) paid for the Husqvarna pack. You cannot change any graphics or number font on these paid bikes. Changing other aspects such as exhaust has no changes in bike sound.
- Sponsors and sponsor challenges are pointless with such little to unlock.
- No goggle straps???
- "team mates" in compound offer nothing other than uninteresting dialogue.
- No native ultrawide. Everything is stretched which looks awful, especially menus.
- Some items such as gloves and sprockets are ridiculously low detail, almost a complete blur.
- No qualifying races or picking gates.
- When casing a landing you often just bounce backwards.
- Crashing/ rag-doll physics suck. You often fly off ridiculously. Feet always bend backwards and go limp despite wearing boots.
- Landing rear wheel first causes you to crash. Somehow it's better to land front wheel first on flat or up ramp???\t\t\t\t\t
