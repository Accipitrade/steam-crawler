A lot of the negative reviews about traction, AI, and overall gameplay are likely to stumble through a minecraft world set-up. You can tune your bike according to your play-style, adjust AI difficulty in settings, and have an improved experience over previous titles. It's a fun Arcade-style MX/ATV/UTV game. there is no additional launcher, the graphics are a huge improvement from previous titles, and the compound is pretty sick.

My only cons...There was some serious rubber-banding when loading in. Almost like severe server lag, but on single-player. I'm sure it was assets loading in, but i do periodically experience it on lengthier trails. Another would be the bails on hard landings. Sometimes a hard cut on a berm can force you to bail. Mostly it's when you land just after the end of a long triple. Most times your suspension would hold it, but dude and his ankles just fold.

All-in-all, it's a good game. It has some quirks, but isn't enough to ruin the fun of it. I'd say it's a 10/10 on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
