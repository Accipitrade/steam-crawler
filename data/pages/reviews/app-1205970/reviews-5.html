\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly this game is fun and I highly recommend the first person camera for the MX portion (the only part I actually play). The tracks are better and there are big lines in the supercross tracks.. unfortunately that is all the positives I really have on this game.

The AI bots are actually horrible, there is no difference in difficulty between Novice (the lowest difficulty) and Expert (the highest difficulty). In a 30 lap race I can have a 9-10 minute lead and I am by no means a pro at this game. Basically having bots in the race is just something to mess you up every 2 laps when you lap them because they will give you no competition. You might as well do the race by yourself as your lap time is the only thing you will compete against.

Though that is specifically for Supercross AI as the National bots are slightly better but still really don't give a challenge.. even on expert but believe me when i say.. supercross AI is a joke.

Bike customization is not that bad but could be much better but unfortunately if you want to ride the factory bikes (Yamaha, KTM, Honda etc.) you'll have to pay $8.

i would recommend the game to my friends who I would want to play the game with me but not to someone who wants to enjoy the single player part of the game. At least for the MX part of the game.

Also, they have ever other supercross map except Daytona.. not a big issue just something that disappointed me.\t\t\t\t\t
