\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played every mx vs atv game that has been made. Reflex being my fav and Alive a close 2nd. I suck at Mx sim but it is one of the greatest games of all time and Mx bikes is just pure joy.

At launch Mx vs ATV Legends was one of the worst motocross game I have ever played. The bike would slide and bounce all over the place. The bikes felt under powered and the sound made me wish I was deaf. I didnt plan on giving it a 2nd chance because I didnt think it could be saved. I was surprised to hear that Rainbow had a update after just a couple days. Aafter hearing about the changes and how much it improved I had to give it a 2nd chance and I am sure glad I did.

Once I upgraded the bikes and played around with the setup I found the bikes to more than enough power. I recommend using first person view as it feels like you are going faster than the 3rd person view. The sliding was greatly reduced and the remaining sliding lends itself to the games physics and skill gap. The bouncyness was greatly reduced. After getting comfortable with the game the bouncing happens very rarely but could still be worked on. The whips are a lot harder to do in this game and that is something that the majority of the community had been asking for in their previous title. I liked the old whips better but they are still fun and a welcome challenge.

The bad...
-3rd person makes the game feel like you are going slow. Maybe allow the fov to go a little higher?
-The sound on the 250 2 stroke is unacceptable.
-Online lobbies end too fast after first place finishes, half the lobby gets a dnf and we end up in loading screens more than racing.

All in all this is a good game and with a little bit of work it will be a one of the best in the series.

I had looked forward to Legends for so long. I'm a big fan and yes, I know they have had issues and we all want another reflex, but Legends looked like it was going to deliver. I have and own all the other MX games and you can check my profile. I have 100% most of them.

To me, Legends feels like an unfinished game. The number of bugs is insane. Everything from the music randomly changing volume to the UTVs looking like they are floating. For a game that is supposed to be motocross AND ATV/UTV, they really only focused on the motocross side of it. I know most of us just play motocross, but then just make a moto game if you're not going to spend time working on the other vehicles. I truthfully can't even say that they spent much time on the motocross part either. Yes, the bikes are heavier, which is nice. Your speed in this game seems to actually matter compared to older titles. For the people complaining about slipping, the bike tuning in this game is much better and actually effects your riding style, so make sure you mess with the tuning. 

The AI... the AI is terrible....... they have like 5 animations??...  (enough said) 

Needless to say, I am so disappointed. There are some really cool features, such as the ability to build your own compound, but at this time and point. just not worth it. I really hope they fix these issues. There are more minor issues to list, but I feel like these are the main issues that are bothering me the most. 
I don't recommend at this point and time\t\t\t\t\t
