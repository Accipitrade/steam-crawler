Honest Review From Someone Who Likes This Game

Some context before my review starts, I have played every single MX vs ATV game and almost every dirt bike-related game. You could say it's one of my favorite game niches. I played a lot of all-out but never really liked it. I prefer this game more even with all the cons.

Gameplay

For the most part I love the gameplay even with some clunkiness. I will get into more detail but to sum it up, this MX vs ATV takes a bit more skill and practice to really enjoy. I am used to it from simulator games but people who are used to All Out will need some hours to adjust. The days of holding down the throttle all race is over, you have to think more and use brakes + throttle management. Also turning and leaning feels amazing with dual stick steering; a must-have setting for this game.


Pros

Main Points
-Best soundtrack out of any MX vs ATV
-Quads and MX feel really good at its core
-Rewarding and fun Gameplay (see cons about this below)
-2 stroke bikes! yay
-Tracks are very good (much better than all-out)
-Whoops feel very good and challenging
-The best freeride from any MX vs ATV (excluding naulstagia lol)
-Graphics are good but unoptimized
-Straight rhythm is basic but at least its there
-Trails mode is great and a big improvement over the waypoint races in other titles

small pros
-125 sound is great
-UTVs have their own tracks
-The UI looks good
-A basic story but happy to see a story


Cons

Main Points holy cow a lot of cons
-Bikes crash for no reason I can't even go through the enduro course without a random crash
-Quads glitch over some berms similar to Mx but worse
-Random camera glitch or something looks like a stutter but it's not
-UTVs are F***ing terrible, I've driven them irl and I don't remember the tires being made of ice, they should have incredibly good traction and power like real life.
-AI is god awful, constantly flying off the track like goons
-Lackluster customization (it's not that hard to add more colors to models guys c'mon)
-Terrible economy (I purchased max upgrade and gear within 1 hour of playing)
-Terrible settings. You cant even turn motion blur off. There are no control settings either. Also, the graphics settings are terrible; I have a 3070 and only get 70 frames.
-Still, occasionally get that All-out bounce when casing jumps
-No Freestyle Events wtf

More small cons that p*ss me off

-Charging $ for the worst cosmetic pack DLC I have ever seen (you can't even change the color in-game)
-No Map? You made massive maps but I can't see where tf I am (maybe there is a button but I can't find it)
-Music sometimes gets super loud along with bike sounds
-All Vehicles have the same stats
-Reused bad engine sounds from All Out besides 125
-UTVs are quiet
-Some achievements are broken (it thinks I did a backflip and stunt on a UTV LOL)
-No way to save camera settings to presets
-Cringy story but who cares
-Cutscenes are low res and low frames
-Terrain deformation is more realistic on some tracks and worse on others
-Mouse comes up sometimes in fullscreen
-Cant change name/number font or color

Stuff I Wished For

-Custom map support
-Track creator
-Pit bikes, trophy trucks, and buggies
-Enduro Races with logs, rocks, and hill climbs
-Customization
-Reflex 2 lol
-New bike sounds (more than just 125)
-Intelligent AI
