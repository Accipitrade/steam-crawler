\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't know where to begin.

One of the biggest things I dislike most about this game is how boring it is literally.

The career events are so painful and tedious to play, the customisation of your bike and rider etc. The menu could use some improvements and so could the UI, the camera angles are not very good and slightly odd. I mean the game presents itself as a sort of free roam, where you can go around on your bike and have some fun, the problem is the map layout is a little dull and it strongly feels like there is just something missing like maybe a touch of atmosphere, the music is horrible and around the map are interactable characters or NPCs, that act as brick walls when you go near them and talking to them is so uninteresting, I don't know the game world just feels empty and a bit lifeless to me.

Another thing I don't like is how difficult the game is. I always seem to find myself falling off my bike its not exactly what I would call fun, there is not like any sort of rewind or instant reset, the actual reset the game gives you forces you to press and hold a button, for like maybe 5 secs and then by the time you do reset, all the AI opponents just slight shot past you and the game will just respawn you like exactly where you were, so say for instance your climbing up a hill or something your not going to gain any speed and then it becomes annoying and quite frustrating.

Considering the cost of this game with all the DLC.

I would not recommend spending too much money on this, especially at full price its really not worth it in my opinion. There are so many better Bike games out there like Monster Energy Supercross or MXGP etc those games are awesome and lots of fun but this one is not it kinda sucks.

I think this game was a waste of money I didn't like it.\t\t\t\t\t

Pros:
- Supercross tracks are all good they knocked it out of the park with these
- Pro national tracks are very well done the ruts and braking bumps are placed in right locations
- Physics are an improvement you have to brake instead of pinning it on every corner
- Seems like the dev team is listening to feedback already and trying to improve game
- DLC content
- Online racing is intense you can bang bars and race close without crashing

Cons:
- Customization is lacking from previous games (No graphics kits, less parts, barely any gear)
- Nationals tracks leave a lot to be desired thrilled they went with more fantasy on some; however the roughness and where they placed it on the track just doesnt work well at all.
- No leaderboards for laptimes, no voicechat, and no custom lobbies online
- Tuning needs reworked I understand having pros and cons most people are running the same setup
- Game seems to crash quite frequently on PC
- Bike sounds could be way better not sure why this is so difficult
- Career mode is cool and how you have a compound and level it up. The whole thing just kind of feels empty you get new sponsors and they never really seem to do anything. Good idea with the career just bad execution.


This game has a good foundation and they have improved in some areas over the previous installment however core features of the franchise are just missing which is unacceptable in my opinion. I'm really looking forward to the content they come out with in the future for this game. At this time I wouldn't recommend picking it up unless you are a diehard fan. The racing online is a blast PC seems to have a small player base that is usually active at night.\t\t\t\t\t
