I was given a bike by the man who owns the compound as a performance award. I don't see it in my garage though. Everything in the game is already "owned" in there, could be the reason why. Maybe I already have it because I chose to buy all of the DLC vehicles! Don't make that mistake.

I was given one suit of leathers upon entering career. It's black, bearing the TQHNordic logo. Nothing else, just the jumpsuit itself.

This game, in full, costs $127.89. WTH?
EDIT TO ADD: Season two's DLC is 2 @ $20 each. That makes this perhaps the most expensive racing game on Steam.

You get 35 vehicles, all vanilla. You get two boring suits, yellow and red. *There is no replay so you only ever see the back end of the bike and driver.*

I am "fan gate locked" with too few fans on social media to progress. Yes, fan counts are limiting your advancement. - - - You MUST RE-DO "fail" races that did not impress the kids on Rainbow social media.

Just what is it with fan counts in racing games today? At the time I first posted this review I was several hundred fans short of breaking the stalemate with the game over just HAVING a Career. Now, at 19 hours in, I'm still 1,400 fans short of unlocking the tier TWO races.

Exhibition racing brings next to no fans. I got 43 today for getting 2nd place in an ATV race on an MX stadium track. I won first place and got 1000 credits. Exhibition is not a fan count paying or credit-paying way to play.

This game action is too rough, visually, for all vehicles except the motorcycles which are the easiest vehicles to control well. The engine sounds could not be any worse. The UTVs are floaty. The very UNsmooth so awfully twitchy riders' legs and the bikes' jerky turns make me sad. - - Turn your eyes away from the movement of the quads. OMG, so unnatural. Irritating.

They have forgotten how they dev'ed realistic smooth steering and smooth character (driver) body movements as can be seen done earlier on their game "All Out," four years ago.

EDIT TO ADD: They remembered how and this was progressed in February. A six month wait.

____________________________

Some good things about this game:

>>The compound looks great and is huge. A big plus there.

>>>The graphics are great-looking. The tracks are highly imaginative.


BUT, back to those tracks....The long cross-country tracks in the desert or mountains are laced with death jumps. You die and then even the squid! AI competitors leave you behind.
"You lose! You get nothing! Good day, sir!!"

*****Many jumps look wonderfully exciting when you are in mid-air, but they are not flatlandably possible. No motorcycle stuntman who was/is an expert in ramp jumping on a dirt bike (like Robbie Knievel) would SURVIVE these landings. They'd be FATAL.

That is SO so unrealistic. The devs screwed up. They screwed your fun on those tracks with the suicide hills thrown-in. Surprise!
You can NOT win those races *because of the CRAZYLAND hills.* No tracks are like this in any way in the REAL WORLD. The game is set on planet Earth, not in Heaven where you can do anything suicidal and still not die.

_____________________________________________________________

This is one of the most expensive racing games released this year on Steam...and it's gone downhill in sophistication as MX vs. ATV newest version in Rainbow's franchise.

Upon launch it cost 28 dollars more for it's deluxe form than Forza Horizon 5's deluxe form. That's nuts.

When your driver leaves a bike or quad he is instantly paralyzed, limp as a wet noodle, and hits the track already a completely unconscious ragdoll.

And if you do that (crash) a couple of times you are put way back. Three crashes and you know you have to reset-restart the whole thing immediately (especially if it's a long race through the desert).

The AI crash in spectacular style, but *they instantly respawn and keep their previous position within one or two spots. *You do not.

Why are there but two suits and 35 vehicles that are COMPLETELY STOCK having only appearance differences. At Christmastime they added two Christmas leathers. OMG, how dumb. Insulting.

The game's DLC costs $87.90. That's DLC, vehicles and the future tracks for season one.

There's no replay, the camera is pointed only and always at your back....with you in one of your only two leather's, and only it's backside is visible. Your jersey's back panel with whatever is your last name showing and the vehicles' rear tire and fender is all that you will see in the game. No replay, you see. None.

So, you might as well ride just one really boss-looking bike or ATV with some gnarly knobby tires and cool-looking back fenders for $8 (the DLC price for one) instead of paying for a garage full of vanilla DLC bikes.

I upgraded a motorcycle using my motocoin earnings. I select a next race and the upgraded bike it is not there among my choices. So, naturally, "WTF." The available bikes are only the vanilla DLC. Why bother. Where are my upgraded bikes when I need them? I will not upgrade another.

I will race in exhibition only.

Screw the career and it's fan count requirements. I'll get my giant wad of money's worth of expected fun times from Rainbow by racing in exhibition only.

And why won't some one in charge at Rainbow turn down the AI difficulty at least [at] the "squids" level?! The game that preceded this one (subtitled ALL OUT) had beginner AI that you could race with - stay up with even if you were not a great player. The squid beginner is alot faster. "Squid" AI, the beginners opponents are much harder to play against than ALL OUT's "beginner" AI.

The ruination of the beginner setting must be a matter of pride for them. Is their arcade LEGENDS game taken more seriously with faster beginner AI? Is pride why? Why else would they make all of the beginner AI much more professionally proficient than they were in ALL OUT.

The end of the review: A major game manufacturer that put out such a beta level experience initially, and it remained obviously unfinished for eight months from release, AND for so much money! ---> $127.89 for the entire game, season one, ought to be ashamed of themselves.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis games a "disasterpiece" -

the MX portion of the game is probably the best- freeride is kinda there i guess... not much to do FMX wise without wrecking every jump. ATV and UTV portion feels like it was added with little thought/effort with MX being the primary focus of the game.

MX bikes control great, similar to previous games- but...
ATVs and UTVs control like a fat chick on rollerskates going down a slip n slide. Racing with those flat out feels like playing one of those cheap Wii games with how bad they control. Every bike/atv/utv feels like it has no power to it. Bogging going up hills, cant make it up certain areas, cant make jumps, cant start off up a hill etc... and whoever designed the sound of the motors needs fired cause its terrible.

Your rider also wrecks over everything and anything. A little short on the jump? Wreck. Overshoot a little? Wreck. Hit a rock? Wreck. Lean back when hitting a ramp like you would to do a back flip? Wreck. And it takes FOREVER to get backup. Very irritating and honestly needs worked out... customization is meh, not many gear sets etc to choose from anymore but im sure theyll tap into that sweet DLC money like they did with the name brand bikes and tracks... (this is how a 40$ game turned into a 80$ game for me)

next thing is bugs in maps. Certain maps have invisible walls youll hit ON THE TRACK and flat out get stuck. Some say youre in 2nd or 3rd place at the end of the race meanwhile you finished first. Out of bounds is picky on when it decides to reset you. How the hell did you miss this during testing? If there even was any testing? Do you need testers? Im sure theres a lot of people who would gladly test your maps/tracks for you...

The game looks great graphics wise, soundtrack i found to be very great surprisingly. The issues i have with this game doesn't make me hate it, rather think it was released too early and needed more time to be completed.

If you're a die hard mx guy like me youll have fun, but be prepared to want to hulk out on your PC a few times... MX vs ATV has been around forever... i dont know how you screw up this bad after making a masterpiece like REFLEX.\t\t\t\t\t
