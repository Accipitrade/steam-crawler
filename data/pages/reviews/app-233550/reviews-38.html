I wouldn't recommend that you buy Another World - 20th Anniversary Edition.

On the technical side of things, there is Steam Cloud support (which is almost unnecessary considering the amount of content in this game) and Steam Achievements support. They're all rather easy to do, and shouldn't take over 150 minutes to finish 100%.\t\t\t\t\t
