\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis classic game is like a time capsule from the past, with clunky controls that make you feel like you're playing an old Prince of Persia game. It can be frustrating at times, but let's face it, what classic game isn't?

Despite the occasionally frustrating controls, I found myself sucked into this world. It's a puzzle-adventure game with short sequences of platforming and combat. The graphics have been given an HD reskin that stays true to the original look, and it's pretty neat that you can switch back and forth between the old and new graphics at any time.

Now, don't expect a long and complicated story with Another World. This game keeps things simple and straightforward, but that's part of its charm. The art style is also simple, but effective in setting the game's tone and atmosphere.

But here's the kicker - there's no true ending to this game! Nope, it's more like a first chapter. The game actually has a sequel that unfortunately isn't available on Steam, so you'll need to find other ways.

Overall, Another World is a great old adventure game that deserves a spot in any gamer's collection. It's short, sweet, and full of nostalgia.\t\t\t\t\t
