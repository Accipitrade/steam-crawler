\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most cinematic games ever, the story slowly unfolds as you solve puzzles in real time in order to progress or survive the current screen.

I first played this on my Amiga back in the early 90's. There was a TV series at the time called Another World so when it was released in the states it was called Out of this World, that soap opera ran its course so now they can release it as the original title apparently.

It was so much fun when it came out that I played it in my first home theater while people watched it like they were watching a movie. I must admit that this is the only game that I had a hard time playing on a keyboard, its like playing a whole new game. I suppose its because my muscles are too well trained at solving the puzzles with joystick moves.

So this game got my nomination for the "I Thought This Game Was Cool Before It Won An Award" award in the 2016 Steam Awards. Here's hoping others remember it as fondly.\t\t\t\t\t
