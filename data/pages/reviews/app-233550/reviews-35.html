\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnother World is a classic title that still holds up today. It's visual style remains unique and still looks good, and the gameplay is great.

It is a pretty linear sidescroller, with lots of puzzle-type sequences and gunplay. There is only one weapon, but it has multiple functions that you will discover as you play. The game has no understandable dialogue, so everything is learned by example or trial and error. You will die, a lot, but checkpoints are common.

The only drawbacks are that the controls can be a little tricky, and that the game is very short. You will be able to beat it in a couple of hours pretty easily, but the length is about right, and there is a lot of variety.

Definitely pick this up, especially if it's on sale for a few dollars. I loved it back when it came out, and it's still great today.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is also known as Out Of This World, it follows the story of a professor named Lester who gets transported to an alien planet after a particle accelerator test goes haywire. I still own this game on Sega Genesis and this 20th Anniversary remake is true to its predecessor.

Graphics: 10/10
I love the art-style in this game, it is unique and at the time of release was WAY PAST advanced. It was unique unlike any other game and still is to a large extent. The colors are vibrant and things that you need to notice stick out pretty well. Since you are on an alien planet the drawing and color of it really brings forth that imaginative environment. I recommend playing this on the highest settings it will let you, it looks a lot better than the original game. You can switch settings by pressing Y on your controller or setting it that way so you can see what it was like on Genesis. It makes it pixelated and takes away a lot of the background scribbles that really give the environment more life, I recommend playing on high quality.

Sound: 9/10
I only really noticed the music at the beginning and at the end, the game could have used a little bit of ambiance, but it is no big deal, not many screens are empty and there is 99% of the time always something going on that requires you to either sit back and think and analyze what is going on or, most of the time, requires you to have fast reaction time. Anyway, the sound effects are awesome needless to say, the game in my opinion could have used a little more ambient music but it isn't something you really notice.

Gameplay: 8/10
So, let me be honest. This game is TOUGH. Much like a lot of classic games from back in the day. They are unforgiving and only TRUE WARRIORS OF VIDEO GAMING can overcome it. If you beat this game without using a guide, kudos to you. I did it way back in the day when I was younger, today I needed it for a part that I ALWAYS get stuck at. I use an XBOX controller so keep that in mind as you read the rest of this section. You get your direction buttons, an action or shoot button, and a jump button which is basically your up button. Holding A makes you sprint while you are running or charges your gun. You get 3 gun levels, basic shoot by pressing A, holding it until a little ball appears gives you a shield for a brief time, and holding until a LARGE ball appears gives you a super blast to destroy shields and breakable walls. It isn't a hard game to pick up and play, it IS a hard game to beat however.

Story: 10/10
For what the game is, I think the story is amazing. There is no voice acting, but you do have your alien companion try and communicate with you in a tongue you won't understand. Your character tries to by friendly at first with a raise of his hand in attempts of "hello" but it falls on deaf ears. Without spoiling anything, like I said above, you end up on an alien planet, you have to explore a bit and outrun a beast, then you get imprisoned by the alien inhabitants. The story follows you, Lester, and his attempt to escape from this world. The story very much relies on the alien you befriend toward the beginning so making sure he stays alive is just as important as keeping yourself alive. The puzzles are challenging and you will have a tough time figuring out every little detail to move on, especially toward the middle of the game, but stick with it. This is one of very few games that really make you feel accomplished when you beat it.

Overall: 9/10
I'm debating between an 8 and 9, but I'll give it the 9. It is a unique game that has an original art-style and story that are really entertaining and well done. Like I said, it could use a little ambient music but that is okay, and just beating the game is a big challenge in the first place. If you pick this up, I recommend trying to grab it on sale, it'll be worth the money. At time or writing I think the default price is $10, which isn't bad and is about what the game was worth when it released anyway.

Also I would like to add, this version of the game comes with achievements. Most you will unlock with story progression, one or two of them you'll probably come across anyway but require a tad bit of extra "exploration" (or standing still for 30 seconds *wink wink*).\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSome people are being too harsh with the game. I don't think this game aged in a bad way. It still preserves its atmosphere. Forget about hard timing duck and jump, this game is more about the feel of exploration and a bit of puzzle. There are checkpoints all over the place, no imminent threat of rage quit. It has actually nice level design. Even you're not in a nostalgic mood, this might work as a great way to learn some bit from videogame history.

Only downside is the separate soundtrack. I would rather hear it in game.

All the controls you'll need are the arrows plus the action button, but it seems to me, dev has forgot to mention how the gun works. Press CTRL for fire, hold briefly to deploy a shield, hold longer for the plasma burst.

Also, achievements hunter alert, you will get all 100% of the achievements just by playing, while finishing the game easily under 3 hours. It isn't particularly long, but you'll be tempted to play the game all over again. It does worth the price. Grab it in the next sale without a second thought. Mycaruba.\t\t\t\t\t
