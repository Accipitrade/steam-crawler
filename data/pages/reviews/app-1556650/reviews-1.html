\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best shootemups finally gets a widely available PC port.

The new translation finally obsoletes the old, awful, meme translation that people only laughed at. Watch those cutscenes on youtube, laugh, and then come to appreciate the real work that went into meticulously localizing all the character dialog, weird and funny scenes, and the strange lore of the franchise that is strangely compelling the more you get into it.

The publishers and developers are keen on frequently patching the game and it has come a long way from launch. Bugs I have complained about on launch have since been fixed and more issues keep on being ironed out. This is a good sign that shows the devs are taking care of this release (unlike say, Cotton Reboot which was abandoned on steam with bugs).

The gameplay is addictive. Each of the 6 characters (plus one "hidden" character, Chibi Fumiko) plays wildly different from one another, each necessitating their own strategies and play styles. The scoring makes the game as hard as you want it to be - want lots of coins? Risk it all by getting as close to bullets or enemies as possible and use your shikigami familiar to wipe them out. Want to play it safe? Hang back and let your normal shot take care of things... But you won't get as many lives and bombs that way. It's a great trade-off.

On top of that you have unique and interesting stages and lots, lots LOTS of memorable and interesting bosses and minibosses. Each boss outside of the first midboss (more of a tutorial really) has its own gimmick, and some of the human characters who face off with you rival and outshine what you would see in say, a touhou game (in fact, it's quite likely that ZUN homaged, or copied, several of their attack patterns for his own games, as he is wont to do).


I love shmups with diverse rosters and excellent boss fights, so this game is tailor-made for me. The fact that its scoring system (the coin collectin' based on your proximity, aka the Tension System) is so fun is just another huge bonus. Don't wait on this, grab it, grab the first game, and even Sisters Royale (the unofficial Castle of Shikigami 4).\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShiki 2 rules, and this is a great version. The backgrounds and artwork looks fantastic given this game's age. The new arrange mode is a nice challenge as well.

The scoring mechanics are like being a fly surrounded bug zappers, who just so happen to be offering cookies. The light is your friend. You must hug it. Watch those huge 8x multipliers roll in, but get too close and zzap! Fun and functional risk-vs-reward that's easy to understand.

The varied cast offers a lot to experiment with, as they all have their unique weapons and game mechanics. And of course, the dialog is fun (and get better when you have two characters selected). There's plenty to try out for newcomers and experienced players alike.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCastle Shikigami 2 aka Shikigami No Shiro 2 is another bullet hell SHMUP that adds a unique mechanic known throughout the franchise as Bullet Tension. Basically, your character has a colored aura around them which turns red at maximum risk and your score multiplier and shot power are increased when grazing bullets with your character's hitbox. This is the ultimate form of risk vs reward in a shmup, and most bullets are slow so that you can do special setups depending on the character you are using. This is what I would personally consider the best in the franchise, and a really solid pickup overall if you never had access to previous ports of this game released back in the PS2/Xbox era. It feels pretty accurate to arcade as well based on what I have seen so far for those that are concerned about arcade accuracy.

The one thing that isn't a con for me but will be a con for others is that there is no presence of the infamous meme English dub here as there was on the NA PS2 release. This isn't a real issue for me personally, especially since I have uploaded ALL of the english dub to two Youtube videos. If you really need that horrendous meme English dub you can always refer to those videos. 1/14/2022 Edit: NeoStrayCat has put up a guide on Steam to mod in all of that audio if you want it per a comment left by them.

Otherwise, this is a really solid port of a game that nobody should ignore and people should play it instead of remembering it only for it's meme English dub.\t\t\t\t\t
