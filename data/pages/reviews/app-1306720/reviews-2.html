\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWolf mutants' dashing command grab beats your dash attack. Wolf mutants dashing command grab sucks you out of the air if you try to jump attack it. Wolf mutants' command grab interrupts you when hitting multiple enemies including the wolf that somehow still gets to executes the command grab. This all happens in the 2nd stage before you even get to the rest of the game.

Just had to get that big gripe out of my system before going into what I think about the game proper. I wish I could give this game score rather than a thumbs down, because it is an awesome game if you can get beyond the balance issues. The artwork and animation is nice, reminiscent of late 90's cartoons of old. The controls are tight and responsive. The combos that are there are nice for what few attack options there are, but are outclassed by combo options that are found in games such as "Fight and Rage".

My biggest complaint really, is that the grunts are more troublesome to fight than the bosses in that some have command grabs that take priority over an attack you might use to interrupt them. The boss fights tend to put you in a situation where you simply have to stick with a single strategy to beat them leaving little room for you to mix it up with the few attack options you do get. Overall it is a decent game with decent amount of jank/imbalance that you should be aware of before buying. I will begrudgingly finish this game because it is too late for me to return and mama says you should always finish you plate even if the meal is underwhelming.

My lord, the wolf enemy shenanigans does have me wondering how this game got such high praise though.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA good brawler.

Gameplay loop.
1) Pick from 3 characters (4 total. Fourth one is an easy unlock)
2) You walk left -> right.
3) You kill anything in your way
3b) Pick up weapons and turkey from garbage cans to assist you just like in the real world
4) Kill boss. 7 stages in total with a choices of path every level

Why the recommendation
* I never really about graphics unless it's effects gameplay. However this game is nice to look at and its UI is simple and well done.
* The game length is good. ~1 hour per run.
* The controls and hit box is good.
* The health of the enemies are not insanely long. This is a big sticking point with me for other game of this genre.
* The enemy design and animation is top notch. The Arcane Hoodlum makes me smile even when they're tearing me a new one.

Achievements takes ~4 hours if you really go for it.

It's a solid addition to the belt scroller (beat'em up) genre. It's solid but not remarkable.
I give this game 4 Weredogs out of 5.\t\t\t\t\t
