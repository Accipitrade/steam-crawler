\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall: I recommend this DLC, but maybe wait another month or so for bug fixes and/or more houses to be released. I could've waited a bit longer for the final release for these things to be polished up properly. Some of my points may be because I haven't picked up the game in a while, but commenting here since it's based on the latest (as of writing) version of the game.

Pros:
- Farm animals +100 points
- I love the architectural designing for outdoor houses as well as house expansions! I will be going bonkers with it.
- While I do own all the DLC content I thought it was nice that this one provides a Chainsaw for those who don't have the Garden DLC's axe.
- NEW SHORTCUTS. THANK YOU. THE SPEED AT WHICH I TAB->ITEM->SPACEBAR AND SUDDENLY CAN PLACE THINGS IS BRILLIANT. I think that's a base game update but I had only seen it after getting this DLC. It's the little things like that where I went "oh wow how did I get along without this before".

Bugs (or things that feel like bugs):
- Panels can now be put up in bulk (VICTORY!), but I can't always put the amount I want to. I can only put up panels in segments of 1, 2, 5, or 6 (not 3 or 4, even though when I put two sets of 6, I have 3 tiles left). The game sometimes won't even let me put up the 5/6-panel long segment and restricts me to a bunch of 2-panel segments for what seems to be no reason other than bugs? Additionally, the game wants to follow the walls to put up panels instead of following the corners/interior walls- which also is a bit annoying.
- Autosave makes my whole game freeze for a bit and I panic that I'm gonna crash (I have not yet crashed).
- I was chilling in my house when suddenly my dog no-clipped from the dog room to my bedroom across the hall. Made me laugh the first time, but by the third time I was a bit annoyed. I think Farm DLC broke a couple more Pets DLC things than anyone realized.
EDITS THAT I FORGOT:
- I can no longer place floor panels underneath objects. They look see-through, but I can't actually put the floor under something that's standing there.
- Trees no longer have the see-through effect when I use the lawn mower, but I can still lawn through them. Just makes it hard to see, but I guess the mechanic still works.

Cons:
- Not enough additional houses. I finished all 9 properties within a day. Overall, it felt more like this DLC was for the equipment rather than the gameplay, which made it feel smaller since there's not that much additional equipment. Garden and Pets still had you do fun things within the houses, but all of this focus was on building from scratch and decorating the inside. Additionally, since there's not enough houses, there's not enough stuff to do to get all those perk points, which I guess encourages you to flip more houses, but still.
- I do not understand the point of the drone.
- Leaf blower being a part of (1) property and not being used anywhere else. It's cool, but I wish it had more value elsewhere.
- I think it would be cool if certain houses had more use of other DLCs. I recognize it's hard to implement, but it'd just be cool if you had a job requirement of cutting grass or pulling weeds that depended on whether or not you had Garden DLC. If I recall that's definitely a thing in Pets DLC (I would go around pulling weeds even though it wasn't required), but here it's ONLY Farm DLC, and doesn't implement small things like that.
- EDIT: Just noticed the store page also says crops are a major feature of the game? Didn't have one house in the storymode that explained the tutorial of how to use them, so I likely won't be using them in my freeplay mode either, which is a bit disappointing.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter waiting for 3 or 4 bugfix patches, I have installed, & boy does this improve my overall experience. I created a new profile to start fresh & did nothing with my first office, because I wanted to see if the new foundation tool would work on it... & it does!

Dunno if it's a recent patch, or the farm DLC, but general improvements have been made to skills that allow you to hold more wallpaper, tile, & paint & do it faster. You can finally get chickens for the coop that was introduced with the Pets DLC. The farming stuff elevates the Garden DLC. You can ride a horse & jump over obstacles. If you want, you can now also add the soundtrack from The Sims & play it on the radio. At least one new room type has been added: Gym.

I did enough low level tasks to unlock the farm content. The first farm house unlocks the grappling tool that helps you reach the roof without needing the scaffolding or ladders. The second house unlocks the foundation that allows you to build bigger houses or second buildings, which is great for the tiny houses in the early game.

Currently this DLC is still unstable & many are reporting it having negative effects on Pets & Luxury DLC, so you might want to wait for a few more patches.\t\t\t\t\t
