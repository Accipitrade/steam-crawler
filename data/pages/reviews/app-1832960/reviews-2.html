\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a big fan of this game, I want to recommend this DLC, I really do. I like taking care of the animals. The farming is... okay, but it gives me money so I'm not complaining. I like all the new furniture, and I love some of the houses that were added. I like being able to build houses completely from scratch (even though it could use a few different roof shapes haha).
But I really cannot get over how buggy it was and still is. I bought it when it first came out and the game lagged so bad it made me motion-sick; I have a fairly good computer, and the game worked very smoothly before I downloaded the DLC, leading me to believe that is was just poorly optimized.
I waited it out for a bit and played again after the first big patch. There were still glitches in many of the jobs, some as small as the animals being a bit buggy, and some being a lot larger, like not letting me complete jobs until relaunching the game. Now that a few different patches have been released, it runs a lot better and there aren't any game-breaking glitches that I've found, but the fact that this DLC was released in the state that it was in really irked me. (And wasn't the Pets DLC like this too? I didn't buy it when it was first released, so by the time I bought it, I never had many issues, but I vaguely recall similar feelings and issues when it came out. I hope this isn't becoming a pattern.)
I feel awful leaving a negative review (I don't think I've ever even left a negative review on anything on Steam before). I love this game. I love its community, as well as how involved the staff is in it. In fact, it's probably one of my favorite games ever. Truly, the DLC isn't bad at all, and I actually quite like it (especially the cows! I love cows). But I can't help but feel disappointed and a bit upset with how laggy and buggy it was. I feel disappointed that y'all are doing the same thing all of the big game companies are doing, rushing out unfinished products for the sake of deadlines. I feel pretty safe in saying that the majority of players would have preferred the DLC's release being postponed to fix its issues. I'm okay with a few small glitches and whatnot (♥♥♥♥ happens, I get it), but I'm not paying fifteen bucks to do y'all's bug testing for you. I'm not sure I want to buy Honeycomb or House Flipper 2 if they're gonna be like this DLC.
TL;DR: The DLC is very good, but the state it was released in leaves a bad taste in my mouth.\t\t\t\t\t
