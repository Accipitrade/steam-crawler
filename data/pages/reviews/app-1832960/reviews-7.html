\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the first DLC I have regretted purchasing for this game. It has absolutely tanked my performance, it stutters and lags constantly making it very obnoxious. The ideas are good in theory, but in reality everything is so clunky, awkward to use, and has to be lined up just so otherwise it does not work. The green house roofs were the most frustrating things I have ever attempted to build. I found myself not being able to figure out where to build, or how, or how to line up the fences the majority of the time in the scenarios. I still have no clue how to place roofs on new builds. The drone is just constant lag. The scenarios really are lackluster too, disappointingly so. For a game about house flipping, there is not much of that here, it is like 90% picking up hay trash in barns or building fences. The animals are just as awkward as in the Pets DLC, half the time the interactions do not work, etc... This took my relaxing, fun, creative game to being a very stressful experience. I also have a bug where half the stuff turns into ghosts of itself, it is strange. I do not think I will use any of the new tools or features because it is such a hassle. I am optimistic that they will fix the issues, they are really good about improving their games and give a lot of additional content for free. I am hoping things will improve.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was not expecting much after reading the reviews posted. So far, the only issue that I've had is that my hands in the game look like a ghost's. I tried to test out a bit of everything and I had no other issues, experienced no glitches, and no noticeable drops in fps. It seems to hang for a second when it saves but that's not really an issue. I reckon that many of the people having issues are using laptops or lower end PCs.

As for the game play, I haven't gotten too extensively into it but I love it so far! It's like a completely new game merged into House Flipper. The room building tool is very simple to use and the animals are really cute. I'm struggling with the roofs but I struggle with that in The Sims too so I won't hold that against the game.

Many PC games have bugs when they come out. It's just part of catering to devices that vary so much. The developers have always fixed bugs in the past so if you do experience issues, I'm sure they'll be resolved soon enough.\t\t\t\t\t
