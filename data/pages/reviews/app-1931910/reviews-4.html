\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had given a negative review of this map in the past, and while some of my earlier comments still stand, I'll go ahead and change from a "No" to a "Yes". I think part of it is just because objectively, Revontuli is probably one of the good maps for both constant action, and more importantly, overall visibility. I still think that it lacks a bit in terms of number of bigger species, and if bird hunting is not your thing, then this map will feel a bit like a waste. There are however lots of white deer and moose, for those that want to grind away for a diamond etc. Like I said earlier, this map allows you to see quite well because of the type of forest. At the same time, bow hunting is still possible, something that's a bit harder on the African map, for example. I had complained before about having to shoot most of the birds from very far, and nothing has changed here. In fact, it may actually be worse, because of the big update that took place with New England. Small game is VERY skittish now. Anyway, it actually is viable to walk around with the .22 and scope from afar for birds (unless you're the type that of player that likes to set a bunch of decoys, I have no patience for this myself). They are usually plentiful, at least. And the capercaillie are just fun due to their aggressiveness. Every single session I've had on this map has been quite fun lately. One of the best maps, I recommend getting it over most of the others, save for Africa, which I think is still one of the best (old) DLCs (clear line of sight for miles, beautiful, and unique species).\t\t\t\t\t

Does the map look great? Yes.

Did they fix birds? Kind of but that should have been fixed years ago when they broke it and isn't part of a specific DLC

I could list the things I like and probably should but you've probably already read most of them in other reviews.

Here is what I don't like:

Ambient sounds: Everywhere you go it sounds like you're inside a nest with 50 baby birds screaming for food. These aren't game birds either. It's so loud a lot of the time you can barely hear your own footsteps.

Lazy Map Asset placement: All over the place you'll see things that were just thrown there. There are several vehicles that were just dumped on the map and are probably a good foot off the ground just hovering there. They either never actually played this map or their QA team did and the dev team said "Our players are too dumb to notice so we're not going to fix it"

Birds not being drawn: This was an issue in the playtest videos they did in the development build and during later streams as well though they just tried to play it off as "I must not be looking the right way".
