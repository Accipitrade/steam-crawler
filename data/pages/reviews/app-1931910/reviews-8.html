I updated and restarted the game from 0 to sense better the changes and my opinion about this game that I cannot qualify lack of similar game on the market.
I AM DISAPPOINTED in general and I give my reasons.
Game for me is the best way to get off the daily stress and RELAX, RELAX and RELAX by playing. What the purpose of playing anyway in this life but recently I THINK, they shifted their approach. Now I feel that the primary goal is to make the COTW HARDER than normal ( idea of challenge) by increasing penalties after each update.
My second opinion is that the CONTENT CREATORS are more listen than a normal player like me. Do you think that content creators are the ones making us buy your game?
They are very nice persons playing a lot giving tips and help BUT at the end their goal is to make money ( Algorithms Soldiers)
In this Updates what is upsetting me the most is the UNREALISTIC movement assigned to the birds. Example: Geese are species I've been observing in real life enough in the last decade, studying the impact of climate change on their migration behavior. Their take off and landing speed are so EXAGGERATED, it's just ridiculous. Forget my comments here, if you have those species in your area, take your time observe them you'll see.
But the idea is always MAKE IT HARDER FOR THE PLAYER. It's just a freaking game where you're supposed to enjoy. For the challenge and stress i left that to my Boss, Co worker, Traffic, Politics etc..... When I get home I want just relax not been pissed or challenged UNREALISTICALLY.
I can wrote a book about my disappointments from lack of class 3 gun, The smallest size of the harvest screen ( I am happy the background is dark now) you don't have to look up the sky, but still you cannot fully look at your harvested animal.

What is the purpose of the hunting log, latest harvest list, that you can't sort, search, I even don't know how it is sorted.

COST of ammunition and equipment still sky rocking. What is the importance of hunting structures on the map. Most are not useful anymore while tents

Lions are fixed, drinking times are changed for some species, Global illumination effect on animal, Less crashes, Price very affordable. Map with more species ( why not increase species on other maps as well)
