The biggest problem with this DLC update are the ducks, I liked them more before this patch.
They just in my opinion completely ruined the ducks.

Even though they were hard to find before this, I felt more immersed then now.

The ducks simply are moving way too fast, it is frustrating to hit them.
Further more the ducks keep flying in endlessly to the point it breaks my immersion.

I hunt ducks IRL I know what I am talking about here, they don't move that fast.

I felt way more immersed when I had to sit down and actually had to have patience for them to come in.
Now you're just getting bombarded with ducks, that move way too fast, and they even take longer to land.
They also seem to land everywhere except where my decoys are.

In short duck hunting became like an arcade game now.

The only good thing about this DLC is the map, it's a beautiful reserve filled with tons of animals
just too bad that it gets overschadowed by the wrong choices they made with the ducks.

Also the eurasian teal caller its too loud, you can literally blow your eardrums out if you're not careful.

This is my biggest disappointment of 2022 so far.
