\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was really a mixed bag. The main game was decent enough for a HOG. It has an odd, somewhat confusing story in which you play a detective trying to catch the phantom terrorizing the theater. Nothing is overly difficult; the HO scenes and mini-games are fun and pretty easy, an enjoyable way to pass the time. As with most HOGs, it has a silly story, but it was decent and I enjoyed the gameplay.

Then I finished the main game and started the bonus game and suddenly all conventions went straight out the window. Mini-games suddenly have no logic no clues, and incomplete or incomprehensible instructions, forcing you to guess what you're supposed to do and then brute-force and/or guess your way to the solution (or use the built-in guide to look up the solution).
I had no problem with the main game, but I couldn't solve even the first mini-game in the bonus game. For the life of me, I cannot figure out how the developer could make a decent main game but then make the worst bonus game I've ever seen.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDarK City: Vienna is a detective Hidden Object story that is good to play if you're a fan of the HO series. You start by investigating the mystery regarding a masked figure (a direct inspiration from the Phantom of the Opera) and a mystical disappearing muse in the bonus chapter.

PROS:
- Male protagonist!
- Good graphics;
- Good music;
- Journal entries;
- Fast travelling map;
- Varied hidden object games;
- Bundle of extras good to try;
- So many good mini-games;
- Two sets of collectibles that you can spot easily;
- Feels like Charles Dickens' novels mixed with the Phantom of the Opera and any detective story.

NEUTRE:
- It does not give the masked figure's sidekick's point of view.
- The masked figurine isn't a vampire, though he looks like one. :)

CONS:
- The story could be improved;
- A little bit of mystery and imprevisibility wouldn't hurt;
- Voice acting could be better, but it's good as it is;
- Some subtitles don't appear in the male detective's deductions.

Over all, it has been great fun to play this game. Opera houses, competitivity between artists, secrets, love triangles, orphans, opera singers, mystical swami, masked entities, you name it. It was fun and informative.
