\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst, I want to say, please take this review with discretion. It's only my humble opinion.

In the beginning, I was so angry about this game: "The girl does not say anything, so I have not much to work with." or "I would do this stuff very different, why do you only give me this options?!"
I really was about to refund the game.. until I understood how to play it properly. Suddenly discovering how to to go with the options I wanted the whole time.. This made up for a completely different experience.

The best advice this game is giving is: "Relationships should rely on mutual understanding, not changing who you are."
That means understanding, accepting, listening, working together and growing as a person and team.


But a bit of advice: Do never make yourself rely on anyone too much, is it a friend or a partner, as they can break your trust and when they leave, it is possible you break down.

This game does not teach anyone to find someone you cannot live without, but how to live together and enrich their lives, because they want and not need to! Sometimes this also means letting go, because working together just does not work anymore.

Still, I need to criticize it.. sometimes people can be nervous on their first date and say something, which might kill the mood, but in this case no one has the right to get upset about it.
Work with it, that is how this person works and do not expect anything. Some people might not behave like you want them to be, but that is the point - No one will be perfect.
If you are unable to work with their personality, then this might be an indicator that he/she is not the "right one" for you. Leave.

No one should play around/act, of course some people want to impress us, but if we fall in love with someone that only exists in our imagination, we are more than disappointed, once the feelings of first love disappear.

Also, stalking someone on Social Media to get to know them, is a no go. I would not advise it, but that is only my opinion, as it can be helpful to find stuff to talk about.
But remember: keeping a conversation alive is part of both sides. Ask them about their interests, keep the conversation going. Do not only talk about or do what he/ she wants.
