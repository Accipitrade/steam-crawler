\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSometimes sad, sometimes adorable, sometimes confusing, just like love itself , game with great arts and sweet soundtrack.

LoveChoice

First of all, this game is not just any other game which you can play whenever you'd like. To really feel the mood of the game you should log out from all your social networks, keep for later all your usual workday routine and hide from the others on your closet with your laptop. Put your headphones on and read aloud like all that is happening to your character is really happening to you.

The game has 3 separate stories: Love:Game, Love:Distance and Love:Detective.
First story is about a couple which has met at the game development convent. Game describe the events like first meeting, getting closer to each other, first date, first disagreement, life together and so on.
Throughout this episode you would have solve true mysteries like "What to watch on your first date", "What to say, when your opponent is upset about something" and others. At the end you will get a letter from your girlfriend which will tell you how good was your relationship and you will get one of two endings.

Second story is about a boy and a girl who met as a really young kids. And gosh, your relationship could never be more complicated then that. Throughout the whole story there will be more and more obstacles to overcome which is really hard to deal with even sitting in front of a monitor.

And the last story is about a married couple where you, as a wife, beginning to feel that your relationship is becoming more distant then is ever were. Futhermore, you think that your husband is cheating on you. And when he will go to work you will become a real detective to understand what is really going on.\t\t\t\t\t

There's always another story. There's more than meets the eye. - W.H. Auden

LoveChoice may be a short visual novel however the stories and interactive choices it provides are beyond than what they are visibly appear. Beyond the adorable and minimalistic illustrations, the message of this game that they are trying to relay is deeper than we thought.

Rather than saying it is thought-provoking, I personally see it as a great game that reminds the players to reflect upon our daily interactions and habits which we may taken for granted from and with our loves one.

I personally think that the stories in LoveChoice portray very well on how in this contemporary society and century, there are countless of times we may be too focus into our jobs and careers which we lost sights of how we usually interacting with our loves one. Sure, we are doing the jobs to pay the bills, but that does not give us any right to neglect their emotions and feeling either. What they need most of the time, is not just a shelter on their heads, foods on their tables - but an emotional support; the reliable feeling and sense of secure and trust that allows our loves one to be at ease wholeheartedly.

Some may think that this game is like testing their morality regarding how they will approach to the situations in relationship. However, with a right and firm mind, you will see how the stories in this game is actually reminding and enriching our views and opinions towards the value of life and relationship given in the various of situations and the interactive choices that we choose.

Rather than passing by this game, I sincerely hope everyone to give this game a try. It is truly a very beautiful masterpiece to experience. An absolutely fulfilling and worthy gameplay. This game is definitely one of a kind.\t\t\t\t\t
