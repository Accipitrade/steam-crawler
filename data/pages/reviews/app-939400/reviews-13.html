A game about love and relationships

Make sure to play it blind, without any guides. At least your first attempt, and see the result.

It is easy to get into relationship. But can you maintain it forever? Do not get discouraged if you fail on your first attempt to live happily together. And do not look for easy guides, just replay the game and look carefully at all of the solutions. Best ones, are not visible to the player :) Just think before you click.
LoveChoice will help you to understand some principles and the fact that every decision has consequences. Like in real life scenario.

Hard to say much more about this game - just act like you would in real life and see what happens.
Graphic is simple but beautiful, music is calming and suits the game well. There's plethora of languages to choose from.

It's easily a 10/10, well crafted indie game. Will force you to think - and if you are in relationship (even if you're married), about your own behaviour and impact on the other half. A short and simple title with very powerful message.
Try to get all of the endings, you will shed a tear or two.\t\t\t\t\t
