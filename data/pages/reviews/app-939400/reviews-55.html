\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAt first, I wasn't too impressed, I felt a bit confused and a bit frustrated at the bad ending after another bad ending. But it didn't take me too long to figure out what I was doing wrong, and I honestly felt so impressed after that. It is such a clever little game, that really hits you in the feelings, because you learn what you were doing wrong the entire time. (I felt so guilty afterwards!) However, once you figure everything out, you will get a beautiful happy ending.
I highly recommend this game, especially if you enjoy a short game with a touching story to it.

Pros:
- Adorable artstyle
- Beautiful story (that is potentially relatable)
- Nice music
- Cute achievements that are easy to get
- Unique visual novel over all

Cons:
- The English isn't amazing at some points. (But it is still good!)
- The mini-game is a little frustrating.
- My game glitched a little at one point. (But I fixed the problem by just pressing escape.)

Overall, as a visual novel lover, and a romantic story fanatic, I would give this sweet story an 8/10!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was a really cute, fun game, albeit short. Which is fine, it didn't really need to be long for it to make its point for me. I found myself thinking about it for a bit after the game concluded (i'm a romantic, what can i say?!). I found the presentation and execution of the message to be pretty clever and engaging.

I played through the first time not realizing at all that I could interact with the scenery, once I found that I could it did enhance the experience. My only very minor spot of grief was a part that involves a mouse movement to advance the characters level of intimacy, that didn't feel intuitive or in line with the idea of the player paying attention (the wheel scroll). But again that was really really minor.

One could argue that the relationship themes presented are simplistic, but I would also recognize that simple things do still matter. I felt the themes could be applied to both romantic and platonic relationships. We all want a friend or partner who pays attention to our interests, listens, someone we can connect with. At times people trivialize these themes in all sorts of relationships, even through adulthood (I myself can be guilty of this!). I found the game to be a cute little reminder of the little things that can matter a lot.

Overall I'd recommend the game and am looking forward to future stories.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA brief review based on first impressions. A complete one will follow up soon.

If you looking for a beautiful and touching love story, you should check this one game asap.
Believe me on this one, you won't be disappointed.

A big story starts from a friendly conversation on gamedev con, and you'll be given choices as it unfurls, as well as some kind of "hints" regarding to what to do or what to say. But it's not only about the dialogs, I was amazed that sometimes you'll need to look for clues or "active points" right inside of... action area, where our heroes are in the game's window. Please, do not use guides or walkthroughs, just try and find solutions by yourself. It's not too hard, and satisfaction is guaranteed.

Depending on your choices, this story might have a bad ending, a good ending, or a perfect ending. Nice thing is that you'll be told where you did a bad choice, and if you know approximately where was that time in the game, you can start the story again and try harder to find a better solution.

The game is quite short, a full playthrough will take you approximately 20-25 minutes. I completed the story multiple times and get all the achievements that are currently available in 118 minutes , so, technically, I'm still eligible to refund the game, but there's no way I'll do that.. Plus there are a great chances that more content will be added.

Beautiful and sweet love story
Nice gameplay.
Touching soundtrack.

And all this - for more than a humble price.

Get it now. Thank me later.

A word for Linux players: I was able to launch and play this game on Linux via proton, but couldn't get a perfect ending - at some point it just stop advancing through the dialogue... and one of achievements doesn't open properly. I was able to get a perfect ending and open all the achievements on Windows. Developer told me that there might be Linux version in the future, so let's just wait.\t\t\t\t\t
