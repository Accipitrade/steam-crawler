\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all thank you Ye for this beautiful game about love, and trying to spread the message of love.

Secondly I wanted to say that the song for the End Credits is beautiful.

And now for a brief review of the game or Visual Novel, or rather games or Visual Novels as this game comprises of 3 separate stories. Each game within the main game is not that long, but put them altogether and to get all the endings and achievements it took me about 2 hours. Each story tackles relationships from a different angle. And each one tries to teach something about relationships and love. The message itself is beautiful. If you start from the first game, and then play the second one, and then the third you will find it surely but steadily snowballing emotionally. Each story is unique, and beautifully told. And I think it could also be educational for people who have not had many relationships. But the core message itself is LOVE.

And honestly for the price I paid for it, it was definitely worth experiencing. I personally could relate to each one of them. And I found the stories to be beautifully told.

So to sum it up:

- This is a collection of 3 games.
- They're all about LOVE
- Each story is unique in it's own way, and well written. They will most likely have an emotional impact on you.
- The games employ interesting point and click mechanics

And last but not least, MAKE SURE you watch the END CREDITS in FULL or you will miss out. :)
