\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen I saw this game on a Steam sale, I was interested. Added it to my wishlist but forgot about it.
Later I ended up buying it not on sale, and even without the sale the game is a bargain. For the story and feelings it delivers, I think it is severly underpriced.

Although the came is very simply, it is made of puzzles, and has a lot of diverse and interesting mechanics. One of the funnier ones was the mouse running away. Some of the mechanics I didn't understand right away, but it's ok.

I have to say that every single one of these stories has made me cry and, as I sit here writing this review, my eyes are still watery. The narratives are straightforward but full of lessons and you cna't help but empathize with their feelings, pains and journeys.

The first and third story have possible happy endings. The second story could be argued that it has a possible happy ending, where both work hard and achieve their dreams, but it is not possible to end happy together in any of the paths. Which is ok, because life isn't always fair and you don't get everything you want.

I think the second story was the one that hit me the hardest, being in a similar situation.

But even if you are not in a relationship, the narratives were crafted with a lot of love and care, and you will find yourself crying on your keyboard.

This game is 5/5 and I can't stress enough that even in doubt, you should buy it. It's an amazing experience that you won't regret.\t\t\t\t\t
