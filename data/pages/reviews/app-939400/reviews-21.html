\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is by far the best romance game I have ever played, one of the best game ever honestly. I played trying really hard to 100% the game and I loved every second of it. The game phenomenal in portraying falling in love and maintaining a healthy relationship, because of that the game was very relatable.

Playing the game at first I got the break-up ending, because I suck at reading into things and always take everything at face value. This game has made me realize how to be better at relationships. I did better on Love Distance as a result, but I still got the bad ending. The second playthrough I got a better ending, but it was still not the satisfactory outcome.

Now the last ending of Love Distance, the whole reason I wanted to right this review. The long distance aspect I relate to so much, spending the days talking to your partner, connecting through mutual interests and certain things we both love so much. The ending where you break-up so because of the distance and both of you needing to have your future was bittersweet to me. It's how me and my ex broke up. The part where you have to click the box multiple times and keeps moving around is so accurate to hard it is to say "I want to break up", especially when you both still love eachother and don't want to. It happened to me and those words were the hardest to say in my entire life, we both loved eachother so much. I was devastated for a while afterwards, I felt like an import piece of me was gone, and it would take a very long time to get back. It took a while, but I got over her being completely gone from my life.

This game makes me want to reach out to her, but there needs to be more time for us to grow individualy. We're not ready to talk to eachother yet, I will wait until we are ready. There are still things I want to apologize for saying right after we broke-up, as I was heartbroken and didn't reflect my actual feelings. We probably won't ever be together again, but I hope in the future we will once again be friends.

I love you Valy <3\t\t\t\t\t
