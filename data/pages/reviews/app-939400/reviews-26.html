\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn LoveChoice, you get to experience 3 stories who cover various aspects of love. Young love, Distant love and a long time love that has been established.

The first story starts with a young couple that met at a Game Jam and bond over like interest, another story involves childhood friends develop a long-distance relationship. The last story covers a married couple who struggle to keep the spark in their relationship through the wife playing detective as her husband displays for her a scavenger hunt for their anniversary.

As someone who has played my fair share of visual novels, I quickly caught on to the choices and doing activities such as reading a Twitter feed to get information on another person to cater to them to get the best outcome. The little mini games and such that come with each story is something I found both cute and charming.

The game truly makes you work for the good endings as you have to interact with the backgrounds and clickables to find new routes that will ultimately lead to the best ending for each story if done right.

I love it when visual novels are more than texts and characters on a screen.

Interactable scenes that change the story? Mini-games? I love it.

A cute and charming game with a beautiful soundtrack that I would highly recommend if you're a soft-hearted person with a passion for love stories.\t\t\t\t\t
