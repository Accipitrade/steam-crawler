Though I was "lucky" in my first run and got mostly good endings, the game can be tricky as it doesn't teach you all of its mechanics and you have to figure things quickly or miss out on a lot of options. It's a common feature in these short story indie games, if you've played some of them before you'll likely figure things out quickly. If not, try clicking and messing around. Great art overall, though less than optimal recording for the piano.

Recommend it to everyone that like story games.

On a side note, I found it genuinely funny that in the second story, distance, there's a "right time" for you to let the girl go follow her dreams and get the "good ending". I suppose waiting 10 years is a reasonable timeframe.\t\t\t\t\t
