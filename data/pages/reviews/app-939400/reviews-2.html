\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's one of those games that deserves a "MAYBE".

If you're expecting a game where your choices influence the outcome of these relationships. This isn't really it, only one of the three stories actually has a choice. And as you'd expect, these morals and attitudes mirror the Asian Dating culture and some things might not make much sense to westerners.

The game is more like "I wanna be the guy" x visual novel. You're supposed to find hidden options instead of choosing the seemingly 2-4 limited obvious choices. Such as, instead of choosing any dialogue option, you're supposed to click on something in the picture itself or wait for the time to run out. Once you figure out that's the point of the game, it becomes straight forward since 90% of the right answers are these hidden visual choices. (I suppose the game is trying to say actions are better than words.)

Cute story, nice music. The second story turned up the "feels-metre", but I personally related to the last story and it floored me. So for that, I'll you deserve a like. Can't be too angry with a game that costs a 1$.\t\t\t\t\t
