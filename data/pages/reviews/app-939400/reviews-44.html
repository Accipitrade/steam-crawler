\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this one with my wife, ended up being a nice couple of hours!

The good: Cute stories with a twist on the gameplay.
The art style is pleasant and cute, perfect for this type of game.


The in-between: This one may tug at your heart strings in different ways but that's kind of the point besides giving you some good life lessons through each story.
You may have to replay a story, but it hits home more if you end up having to especially for the first one.

The bad: It's short but for the price (especially on sale) you'll be fine ;)
Some of the puzzles are not intuitive, had to look up a solution for one of the puzzles. Turns out a lot of people were confused/lost too!


Recommend or not? Why?
Do you enjoy visual novels or slower typed games with a cute art style? Yes
Do you have a soft spot for love stories? Yes
Not interested in feelings and slow gameplay? You can skip this one.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI reviewed LoveChoice in a full article on my website for those who are interested: https://thenewleafjournal.com/lovechoice-review-a-steam-visual-novel/

Below is the reader's digest version of my overall positive review.

LoveChoice is a short but memorable visual novel, and well worth the price. It contains three distinct love stories, each examining a different type of relationship. It takes somewhere in the range of two hours to complete if one is playing it straight through to the end. A couple of out-of-the way achievements add a bit of mileage to the gameplay.

The art and music are both terrific and two of the most memorable aspects of LoveChoice. The story-book look works very well, although I did occasionally have some minor difficulty figuring out all the things I could interact with in a scene. The three stories are well told, and the best choices make sense in the context of what is happening.

The biggest weakness in the English version of the game is that the dialogue often sounds unnatural and wooden. There is never a point where the story or characters do not make sense, but what they say often sounds awkward. To be fair, however, the developers are forthright about the translation process, and the issues are ultimately outweighed by LoveChoice's very reasonable price and all of its merits. With that being said, I hope that it eventually receives a more professional English translation.

It has my recommendation - even at full price LoveChoice is a good way to spend an evening or a weekend.\t\t\t\t\t
