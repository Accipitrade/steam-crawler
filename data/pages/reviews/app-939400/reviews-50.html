\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game's descriptions says that this game will give you an answer to love. However, after playing this game I found that not to be true as love in reality is different than how this game shows.

The game as a fantasy is fun and enjoyable, the music is warm and the game is touching. If you buy this game because you want a love simulation you will be disappointed as its not realistic. However, if you come to this game wanting 3 really cute stories ( some short some long) you will enjoy your 2 hours.

The reason i am giving this a bad review is because of the description of the game I had expectations different to what was given in the game. The game is fun, music is good,story is touching, but due to the description I thought i would be presented with realistic love stories than fantasy love stories which this game delivered. I wish I could give this game a mixed review, but unfortunately I can't.

tl;dr: If you expect realistic love stories you will be disappointed, if you want 3 fictional love stories to warm your heart you will be pleased.\t\t\t\t\t
