\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPunch Club with a Kairosoft twist, you train boxers up and fight them.

The gameplay is simplistic enough to pick up and do well and animations feel very smooth. The interface feels better to handle on PC than many of their other ports and you can always get a good sense of progression as long as you focus on one 'good boxer' rather than spreading out too much.

My only few complaints is the 'forced retirement' of your boxers. They should have done what they did with Pocket Stables, have their stats start to decline past their prime but let you pick when to retire them. At least it's easy to extend that requirement if you're able to keep progressing. I also wish they would have added a 'bulk train' option. Gets repetitive spam clicking

Regardless, I like it :)\t\t\t\t\t
