\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot the prettiest game around, that's for sure. The graphics do utilize a lot of pixel size mixing and matching, which at first is very distracting, but as you keep going through the game you will discover that it is very deliberate indeed. The game's sprite style also ises a lot of strange proportions on the sprites, and borderline pillow shading at times. The character designs can also be grotesque to some, but moreso the enemies. The sense of humor may be lost on audiences outside of 99Vidas's fan base, the series being obscure outside of its home country. But graphics aren't everything, and the gameplay is... okay I guess. There are a lot of techniques, but you'll need to unlock stuff like your heavy attack combo and various specials at the shop. Some bosses don't give you much of a hint on how to beat them. Controls are decent enough, but nothing special whatsoever; advanced maneuvers seem to be non-extant and the game is moderately shallow. I only reccommend this for beat em up genre fans.\t\t\t\t\t
