\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile not entirely what I was expecting, I like the VR edition of Lost Ember. It takes the same game as the original and adds a new dimension to it. However be warned, if you are not used to VR motion, this may make you very motion sick.

It's still a bit rough around the edges. To me everything seems slightly out of focus (don't know if that's just my headset. I did try adjusting a few things), although that doesn't really take away from game play at all. The biggest thing I've noticed so far is many UI items are not aligned in both eyes, such as the intro scenes and the popup that appears when you spread mushroom spores. While not actually taking away from the game play itself, this can quite literally cause headaches.

Overall, if you'd like to be in the world while you play, this is a good option. Definitely needs a bit of work, but I'll still probably end up playing the whole game again in VR.

As of right now 7/10 would recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
