\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is probably one of the hardest reviews I have ever written. Basically, it is a good game that just isn't for everyone. I wish they would trim and reformat the base Tower!3d as a demo. This would give people a decent chance to decide before jumping in to the way more expansive world of pro.

~The Bad~
- First off, the Devs are busy making content for MFS and other games, so more airports are not really on their radar.

- It is way too overpriced for what you get at full price. The deluxe would be more realistic at the $50 price point.

- There is additional DLC that is only available on their website.
(This is my biggest pet peeve with the game. The Real Traffic DLC/MOD really changes the dynamic of the game.)

~The Good~
- There is a good non-steam mod/support community for the game. They really help you develop and expand aspects of the game like custom flight schedules and additional voice commands.

- The Devs support the community involvement. While I was refining my game files I would come across posts from the Dev team applauding and helping out the community.

- Multiplayer fan-base. The MP aspect does have a dedicated discord server that is friendly and helpful. It is also a great spot to find people for MP sessions.

~The Meh~
- The graphics are not the best, but honestly when the schedule is getting packed I never really stop to look at the airport itself anyway.

- The voice recognition can be a bit buggy at times. There is some simple fixes before getting a "good" mic.

- Pricing of additional airports. My advice is to use a map to scout out the airports and wait for a sale.
(so this is more of a personal aspect you will have to figure out for yourself. Devs are people like you or me, and I know my time is worth money, and time has been spent making these airports. For me, single runway airports does not present a challenge and not worth the money. However someone may live near one and would think it would be worth it.)

To summarize it is a very niche game and hard to say if I would or wouldn't recommend. If ATC and airport ground control games are your thing than I would suggest trying out the standard version first. If that version leaves you wanting more features like voice command and enhanced aircraft instructions, then pick up the Pro version.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't be fooled by the negative recommendation, I will say in advance that the new version (Tower! simulator 3) is well underway and is supposed to be lots better. With that in mind I would wait for the newer versrion. And upgrading this will most likely NOT be possible.

I am a seasoned player of this game, yes, game! This is not a simulator!

What you get is an old game engine, crappy graphics and loads of problems if you don't do things right. Installation is straight forward but when you start adding DLCs it can get complicated. Not all DLCs are available on stream, the two most important ones (those that give you real aeroplanes and real airlines and liveries) you must buy from the developers website. These will not automatically install in the correct folder, so you must be careful to select it for yourself.

The best feature of this game is the ability to give voice commands. I have seen lots of people strugling with installation and getting the game to understand non American accents. I've never had any problems in these respects although I'm a Dutchman living in the UK with a slight Essex accent. Any problems I have had have been handled by the Feelthere Zendesk and have so far all been resolved.

The gameplay is great, you are ground and tower control for the airport you play, 3 included, St Thomas TIST has a tutorial. By way of voice commands (or you can use mouse) you clear planes for take off; landing; taxi. You assign runways and delegate planes to and from their stands. It is all very comprehensive but not like real ATC. You have a limited amount of commands, You can edit some of them to combine some of those or slightly alter them but that is it. Many aspects of true ATC are not present, which maybe a good thing because it can and will get hectic.

The developer has stopped working on this game but at this moment they are still releasing airport DLCs. As said the new version of this game is underway and if the developer is to be believed we are in for a treat.

Lastly I want to touch on the price, I bought this game when it was in a steam sale. I got it half price (around the 20 mark). This is the only way this game is worth it. It has been reduced in price somewhat since but the general opinion of players is that the games biggest fault is it's price. $20 (15) seems to be what most players think it is worth.\t\t\t\t\t
