\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
-Fun, chill and can be quite challenging.
-Voice recognition works pretty well
-Planes and GSE (Ground Service Equipment) look decent.
-Many different airports to add to your collection

Cons:
-Base game costs too much for the current quality.
-To get real (non-generic) liveries and traffic, you have to go to the developer's website and purchase these for each airport you own.
-Overall Airport graphics quality needs improvement (Hopefully this will happen in time)
-Point system needs to be reconsidered. 10pts for departures and landings, -1000 or more if 2 planes get too close or go unhandled, etc.- That's a bit much.
-No Emergencies
-Weather dynamics need to be redone.
-Multiplayer requires you to find someone somewhere to play with and then connect through the game. In 2021 that seems a bit antiquated. Game player matchmaking needs to better.

Overview:
If you're into aviation and like the ATC part of it, I would definitely get this game *ON SALE*. There is a learning curve and even bigger one if you don't know a lot about ATC. This game is decent and has the potential to be amazing. I know game development can be time consuming and expensive but I hope the developers have big plans for this one. I would hate to see this game go to waste.\t\t\t\t\t

Price and graphics: The $50 price tag is ridiculous so you should only buy it on sale and the graphics look something akin of Microsoft Flight Simulator X from 2006. The DLC airports are nice, but because Google Maps has better graphics for airports I think that leaves something to be desired in a game like this.
Gameplay and realism: The voice commands are good and easy to setup and are generally accurate however there are some commands just simply missing like wtf where is the command to give clearance to cross a runway? There is a workaround to that specific fault, but at $50 what quality are we expecting?
Summary: Just wait for a sale or see what BigFatSimulation's Airport Madness Pro (When it launches, their website said early 2020 and its 2021 so idfk) brings to the table if your on the edge on whether to buy this or not.
