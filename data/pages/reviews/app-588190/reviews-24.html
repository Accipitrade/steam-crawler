\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have spent a lot of time on this game, and I really enjoy the overall experience. I think the way you interact with planes, and the feel of actually being in an air traffic control tower is very realistic, especially with the voice recognition.
There are a few minor glitches that I sometimes experience, such as planes turning in circles on the taxiway, or refusing to taxi a certain way, but these events occur rarely, and using the "delete airplane" button makes short work of the issues.
It may also take a little bit of playing the game to learn what not to do, and what will result in a -500 deduction off of your score. The score does not really matter, but it is a good way to make sure you are following the rules a professional air traffic controller has to follow
The price is a little high at $50, but if you get it on sale, the game, as well as DLC are worth the cost.
Overall, I would recommend this game to someone who likes airplanes, or just wants to try their hand at ATC.\t\t\t\t\t
