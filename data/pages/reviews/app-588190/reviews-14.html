\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't listen to the naysayers about the price. If you LOVE Aviation and ATC, you'll love this game.
I don't mind paying a bit extra for a game that I'm really into. I have both X-plane 11 and ATC. I get to experience both sides of the coin of both pilot (via X-plane 11) and the ATC side.
Get the actual livery for the aircraft, it is worth it. It is about $6.00 or so but they often give you a discount. Same goes for real ATC even though that is a bit more.
I agree with the other reviews that the screen rendering is not that great but it is definitely workable. I do hope they are able to improve on the rendering as that will take this game to a really good level. I'll pay extra for that if that matters for them.
One other feature I would like is to be able to zoom in and out and circle around any part of the airport in 3-D view at any time I choose. I'm surprised I can't do that. The 3-D view with zoom comes in after you have chosen a specific aircraft. The screen then zooms into the terminal where it is parked (decent screen rendition), or if it is taxing or on the main runway, it zooms there but the screen rendition is not great for those.
I'm interested in the technicalities of aviation and mastering all the skills, so if you're main focus is that, the rendering won't bother you as much.
I just started recently on it. I only play LAX for now. Once I master the voice control / AI and also high traffic times of LAX, I plan on SFO.
I'm hoping to come back to this review after I've played the game for a number of months and see where I am at with the evaluation of this game.
