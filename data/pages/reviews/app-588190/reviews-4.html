\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have to add my 2 bits to the details about this game. Overall, this is a fun, yet stressful experience, just like I'm sure it can be in the real world (especially if you are controlling tower and ground without using a buddy for multiplayer in game, honestly how most will play). I use a really good mic, and natively speak English, so my experience, although not perfect initially, got better after some speech recognition training in Windows. My first 40 or so hours saw a lot of repetition of commands, but the last 20 or so have been nearly flawless, so not sure what changed there, but the speech training I did was 5 hours in so??? When the game works, the point system is tough, but I would say fair. Overall, unfortunately, I cannot recommend this game at its current state in early 2022. I don't believe there is any more production or updates being made, so this may never change, but I'll explain further below.

The graphics are meh, but you don't play this game for the graphics, and once you start to get busy, the only graphics you will see will be the radar screens and arrival/departure lists. 2/5 for graphics.

Play-ability is good (I have only ever used default aircraft and callsigns) and keeps you on your toes. Flexibility in traffic control seems fun, although depending on where in the world you are, there are so many different ways to say ATC commands etc, that not everyone will find this to be prototypical for them or their area of the world. Many other reviews state this, and I am no different. The game cannot be all things to all people unfortunately, so this is a limitation that you can't really set out as a con per se. Just understand that you may not enjoy the experience if you really are an ATC. There are in-game limitations as to the commands you can give to aircraft, while still being able to fully control the position and usually the routing of aircraft in-game. For example, you have 2 parallel runways L and R, and an aircraft gets routed to Right by TRACON director. You can reassign the aircraft using *callsign: enter final runway **L. So there is some flexibility.

Now, for the primary reason I would not recommend this game in it's current state. If you care about the points thing in-game, then there is a current issue in how the traffic is programmed. Currently, turboprops on final will slow down to a more prototypical speed on short final, but if a regional jet or faster aircraft is following, the game doesn't provide enough spacing to allow for the slower aircraft to slow enough to land without the faster aircraft behind them, overtaking them causing a rush of warning errors every second until the slower plane touches down. You can easily end up with between 3,000, and 7,500 points NEGATIVE (-7500) for this "infraction." Why isn't the TRACON Director leaving more spacing between slower and faster aircraft so as this doesn't happen in the first place. You as the Tower controller can't request the faster following aircraft to reduce speed, so you have to catch this issue before it happens, and tell the faster aircraft to leave the sequencing and go back to delivery to be resequenced, all the while dealing with ground, and departing aircraft. It's not as easy as you think when you basically never stop giving commands (sometimes talking over the replying aircraft just to get enough commands out). So based on this glaring issue that was apparently created with an update in '17 or '18, I cannot recommend this game in it's current state.

Unfortunately, this leaves this review with this final statement

Save your money, and skip this game if the points system is going to trigger you!

If not, then you should be able to get much more enjoyment than the negative points you will see whenever this issue happens (which is fairly often). Thanks for your time and hope this helps.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll start by saying that currently, I am so G D frustrated with this game, that I am writing my first review of a game EVER! I have a LOVE / HATE relationship with this game. When it is working as expected, I love it and could recommend it. However, I believe this is a partially completed effort. It could have been so much more.

What has my undies in a knot is the scoring. I have over 200 hours controlling and that is since resetting my stats due to being 30000 point in the whole.What happens is when there is an infraction, you should get dinged either 500 or 1000 points. In reality, you are dinged this every second. Today (and what has finally prompted me to review this game) is I was rolling along , had landed or taken off, a total of 13 aircraft (10 points per aircraft landed or taken off). In the space of 4 or 5 minutes, I looked up and I was 22,000 points in he whole.

Issues:
1. only a small text on the bottom of the screen (which you don't often notice as it flashes, especially if you are on two screens and being busy controlling)
2. No sound warning of my infraction, {since you don't often see your message)
3, continuous penalties until infraction is corrected (thus the -22,000 points in under 5 minutes (at 1,000 points per infraction, that is 12 aircraft....I only had handled or in the process of landing or taking off, 17 aircraft. So how did I sink from 130 points to -22,000 points in such a brief period of time?). BTW, a separation conflict should only be 500 points, not 22,000)

I have owned ATC programs since the Commodore 64 (Kennedy Approach), Tracon for Windows, Tower, ATC2 Simulator, London Approach and ATCpro. I am also a retired VATSIM Centre Controller for over 5 years. I am not new to controlling. Yet my Game Score (Net) is -65,000 points since my reset (same issue on the original scoring). I can live with tough scoring. But when it is almost impossible to score fairly, it takes all the enjoyment out of this game.

I had read in a forum that the developer was working on a new version of Tower. If that is the case, I WON'T be spending a dime on an incomplete, poorly completed game from Feel There until they put out a completed product.

Until then, I cannot recommend this game. My two cents worth!
