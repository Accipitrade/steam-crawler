\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExcellent game if you are into the ATC sim vibe. Is pretty realistic in many ways but of course is still a game and has some not so realistic functionality (like planes that sometimes stop at runway crossings and sometimes don't, I guess the AI is a little fuzzy on that). Keep in mind in single player you are ground and tower so it can get a little hectic at busy airports at busy times and if you are using the "real traffic" mod schedules can really get hopping! The voice command functionality is nice and works well if set up right on your PC and with a quality mic. If you have a little bit of modding/coding chops you can edit the schedules pretty easily in text files as long as you pay attention to the formatting and ensure you line up with relevant relational data (airline codes, parking, plane types, etc. from the related file data). Would like to see the developers put some more time into the AI so single players could just choose ground or tower and let the AI run the other, and maybe a UI based schedule editing option.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is okay, but it has many issues. For starters, it seems like a money grab. The game itself hasn't been updated in a while. The devs just keep releasing new, paid airports that are essentially just a reconfiguration of the runways with some google earth quality mapping and some assets thrown in.

Second, working airports like KSFO (My favorite) is impossible. The aircraft land on the runway and slow down to a halt while going down the rest of the runway. This makes it near impossible to depart any aircraft on intersecting runways without either getting negative points or forcing a go-around. Forcing a go around is what I usually have to do to get departures off.

Finally, the way the AI moves is just downright ♥♥♥♥♥♥. Any instruction you give them they have to completely stop what they're doing, sit there, and then go after like 20 seconds. Runway crossings are very clunky (Wish you could give crossing clearance in the taxi instructions).

There's many features I wish to see in this game but honestly, I don't think I ever will. The devs are focused on making money and not on making the game better. Real shame...\t\t\t\t\t

The Good:
Voice works really well. The visuals are ok. Multiplayer is an option with each person taking a position in the tower.

The Bad:
New airports cost $20 each. On top of that if you want real airlines in the game it's a 2 step process. You have to purchase a $10 addon for the callsigns to be in the game. Then for EACH MAP you have to purchase a $5-$10 addon so that the airplanes show the correct logos otherwise they are just plane white (not even the default game logos). So, if you want a new map and real airlines it will cost you $30 per map. Another bad part of this is that you have to go to their website to get the addons that are not maps. No auto install. No auto updates. You just have to check the link they send you in your email every once in a while. Don't lose the link.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tQuite a few missing pieces that make the game tough to manage when things get busy. For example, IRL a controller could tell an aircraft that had just landed on 24R at LAX something like, "Delta 289, turn left at Zulu if able, expedite crossing runway 24L, contact ground on 121.65." If the Delta jet couldn't make the Zulu taxiway for some reason, they would say "unable" and the controller would come up with a new plan.

I don't see that you can do that in game - aircraft slow way down and are not good about clearing the runway, and they seem to always stop at the hold short lines: you can't preemptively clear a runway crossing. Telling them to expedite is also not possible, and you can't ask a dawdling airliner if they need assistance.

The phraseology is also kind of a mess. You tell a departing plane to taxi to a runway, you don't tell them the route, so you can't move a plane to a parallel taxiway to avoid a nose-to-nose situation if you have another plane that needs to go to another part of the airport. It isn't too hard to get a literally unsolvable gridlock on the taxiways.

I had high hopes for this game, but it was very disappointing.\t\t\t\t\t
