Few thing's i'd like to see:
Some sort of unlock system, it would seem the dev doesn't want meta progression and that's fine, but something like Gungeon or NT where you unlock new characters and items from playing the game would be good.
Corpses dealing damage and flying around like NT.
Perhaps a unique ability for each character outside of passives.
I know the level-ups are clearly placeholder somewhat, but more variety and interesting choices would be nice.
Map generation is a little 'jagged' and weird in some respects and sometimes is annoying to move around. Also could be generally more open than it is currently.
Not sure how I feel about the dragon, I'm on the fence.\t\t\t\t\t
