The game is good. Really good. In fact, despite the fact the game seems to be finished, and no one seems to want to make mods for it, I think I may actually attempt to make mods for the game. I find the game to be extremely fun. It has some hiccups here and there, but it's a fun game and it's enjoyable. It's very fun, and has a lot of depth to it. I do wish there was more content, but I am still happy with what I have gotten out of this game, and I plan to play it more.

The game may not be perfect, but when compared to Game Dev Tycoon, this game is far more enjoyable honestly. It has the feeling of someone who is just trying to be hoenst with how it feels to make a game. It has lots of customization, and has many different ways you can play it.

Don't expect perfection, but expect something that is an enjoyable experience.

Now let me clear in all the game dev tycoon games I've played this is the most complex and fun, however what it lacks it's content, don't take this as an insult though, since it compensates in again its complexity. One of the things I love is that it's consistent, in comparison game dev tycoon is quite inconsistent, you can train your dudes and have the best combo, and have supposedly the best sliders and yet your game can be dog poo, in this game if you train the devs, and get a combo with good mechanics the game will be consistently good. Now when making the actual studio it is so realist in the sense that you have to worry about light, water and bathrooms, in comparison mad games tycoon is quite mindless, you can do whatever and the dudes just teleport and even the deffus from not putting certain things is quite easy overlookable, in here if it's missing the office doesn't work. Inicially I hated this games for the simple fact that it was too hard, however at some point I replayed it, and thought wait this is actually more in depth then I thought, I had just overlooked some things, and when I figured out that actually making a balanced team actually worked was quite amusing, the thing that makes me not play this as much though is that after 2010 is became kinda pointless to play, the "ps3" comes out and that's the last console, and that's my biggest problem, even if there's not many themes (and the theme selection is quite wierd to me) at least making consoles and fighting the other opponents was fun, but again once you get to a certain year there's no more competition, it's just you, if the dev ever comes back to this game I would love to have all the newest consoles added hell even some more themes wouldn't hurt, however I also understand that the game maybe wasn't that big of a success, either way i love it and it is my favorite game dev game, it has the most depth out of all of them, and if you ever make a second game I'll be the first to buy it, cause again the game is so good, even the graphics are quite nice better then maybe all the tycoon games I mentioned here, so best of luck to you developer and anyone of you that read this buy this and try it to yourself, the first few times it might feel hard and unfair, but once you get the gist it's so much fun to play.\t\t\t\t\t
