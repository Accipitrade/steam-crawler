\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo of the ~40 minutes I've been running this game, I've literally been stuck on the first tutorial. I can't even put down workspaces. You can see this is a long-standing issue, that the dev never fixed:
https://steamcommunity.com/app/773580/discussions/0/1642042464749050691/

The game asks you to build 7 valid workstations, but when you follow the prompts, the workstations aren't valid. You have to hover the mouse over the object to find out they require what I think means lighting but the tutorial doesn't tell you how to find out, and also I have no idea how to find those objects since it's the first step of the tutorial and I couldn't figure out where the object was. Everything I could find was grayed out. I actually restarted three times to figure out if I had misread or skipped something because it made zero sense the tutorial would give such incomplete instructions.

You'll also notice the dev's response to the feedback was quite negative. This was first brought up two years ago. People still have issues. Good luck finding a guide on Google with a generic name like "Game Dev Studio". Considering this is still a problem, I'm guessing the dev shoved this whole thing under the rug; it seems quite plain they weren't sympathetic to their design flaw in the first place.

I love building games, especially game dev ones, and this looked so promising. Unfortunately, the tutorial is complete trash. I'm not going to play a game with glaring issues at startup, especially when the dev responses to such feedback is so confrontational.
