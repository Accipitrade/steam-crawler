\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRelated is a Horror game where you play as a woman named Jessica. Jessica goes back to the old orphanage she lived in to figure out what went wrong all those years ago.

If you like Horror games like Fran Bow than you're in for a treat! Related is a 2D sidescroller that uses puzzles and point-and-click gameplay. It also seems to be set somewhere in the 20's to 50's. However, Related maintains its own individuality and is no clone.

PROS

- The artstyle is very nice
- The sound design adds to the mood and makes you feel on edge
- The story is very interesting and holds a lot of promise

Cons

- The gameplay mechanics could use a bit more refinement
- The game is very short and leaves you on a cliffhanger. (This isn't too bad since its pretty cheap though)
- I'm not too thrilled that its getting released in chapters instead of as a full game but I'm guessing this is the developers way of trying to gain interest while getting the funding they need.\t\t\t\t\t
