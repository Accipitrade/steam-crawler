Well what can I say, I played few hours already and I had a very good time.

The game is really well made, sure you have to like that typ(ING)e of game, but if you do, you will love it. Also if you never tried typing games before (What ? You never played Epistory or Nanotale ??? Unforgivable!!), go for it too. I can't find anything against the game to write about. Fishing Cactus released an awesome game again. What a great studio

You have to play this game, it's a must have :)

EDIT : Because it's damn good, i nominated this game for the best game of the year 2022.\t\t\t\t\t

If you want to challenge yourself and see how good you are at typing and moving at the same time then please do give this game a shot! It's quite unique as a game as it combines arcade style gaming with typing ad heavily relies on the gameplay.
