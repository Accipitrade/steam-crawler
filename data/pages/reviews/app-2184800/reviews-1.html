It also adds Glass Walls. Tracking is a bit difficult at first, add tracking devices, a monitor and a router. Have the tracking devices placed in the infirmary, same with a monitor and have the router connected to the monitor. The router doesn't need to be in the infirmary, it just extends the range of the tracking device, so it's advisable to place them thru out your prison; then go to the tracking menu to assign tracking devices to prisoners. The pack also adds Electric fences.

The only downside to the pack from what I noticed (which is less of design problem and more based on realism) is, glass walls used on Prison Cells will dramatically increase the desire for Freedom for your prisoner. All and all, it's a good DLC pack. Quite difficult at first at setting the tracking belt up if you don't know what you're doing, but once you figure it out; it works.\t\t\t\t\t
