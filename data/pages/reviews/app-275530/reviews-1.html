\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the most careless ports I have ever played.
- Does not properly work on modern displays. On my UWQHD display, I am forced to either play 1920x1080 in window mode (without proper ingame cursor and notchy scrolling) or with a large part of the game outside of my viewport. What's the point of this port then?
- Terrible new UI: Classic RTS sidebar with radar and unit display was replaced by two detached panels that block your view on parts of the map.
- Unit AI seems to be even dumber than back in the original. E.g. robot units just do not shoot at tanks even when ordered but rather approach them.
- The macro AI is dumber than in the original game. At the time of writing, I have made it to level 6 and I have already seen the "hard" AI fail to conquer a sector while some of its units stood beside the flag, Instead, it started attacking the factory while it would still have had a minute to conquer it.
- The remade menu does not fit the original artstyle. The only good thing about it is the option to replay a given level.
- Some of the original MIDI music is missing, especially the winning theme. For the rest of the ingame music, the MIDI tracks seem out of balance, as if the "melody" was too low and the rest too loud.

The original game was so refreshing back in the day, and while the gameplay could still be fun today, this port is just unworthy.
If you want to play the game properly, go get ZodEngine or ZED Online. They are free and worked fine for me.\t\t\t\t\t
