\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played the original Z for DOS in 1997, it was one of my favourites games. This Steam version has been remade, there are new features introduced in the IOS and Android port, which were ported over to Steam, like this new detailed map. The original cutscenes are there but the portuguese language ain't, too bad it was pretty cool. And Z95 levels ain't there unfortunately, nor any of its features like the Medium tank having a wider range and the tough unit having three instead of two units in it.

It plays almost like the original, there are some bugs like units refusing to enter vehicles, especially the APC, pathfind issues. Sometimes units forget orders, pyros can't attack buildings and bridges. And I experienced a black screen in my old laptop with intel integrated graphics which can be fixed by closing the unit stats window. I haven't tried other computer yet, maybe it happens with intel cards only which is odd cause they are supported.

Anyway, the bugs are not game breaking so I recomend this game!\t\t\t\t\t
