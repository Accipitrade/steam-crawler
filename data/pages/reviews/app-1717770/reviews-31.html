\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery enjoyable experience! While not as scary as Zeekerss' previous games (Dead Seater & It Steals), The Upturned offers tense situations throughout its 5 - 6 hour playtime.

The floors (i.e., levels) had great variety in enemies, mechanics, and environments, so nothing ever felt stale, and the timing of the humor shows that Zeekerss knows how to handle humor much like how he mastered horror in his previous titles! And like I said above, this game WILL have tense situations and fast-paced action that make you panic to avoid your death.

Personally, I never got tired of throwing items! Such a simple but fun mechanic, and I look forward to both additional challenges in this game and Zeekerss' future projects.

A great title for any horror fans (whether hardcore or casual) or anyone who wants a fun little adventure where you yeet boxes at crows. Oh, and the soundtrack slaps!\t\t\t\t\t
