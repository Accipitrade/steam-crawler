\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is, by far, the single best Horror game I have ever played. Not just that, but the writing and comedic timing is top notch, and I'd rank it as far up on the scale of video game funnies as more traditionally goofy-to-serious games, like Undertale and Omori (yes I am an indie RPG game fan how could you tell?)

The horror blends into the absurdity of each and every scenario perfectly, with little to no fumbling of the change between the two. Pure hysteria like what The Upturned provides is only capable of bringing out two emotions; fear, and the state of being locked into laughing so hard you can't see the screen anymore.

Me and a friend played through the game together over a few days, and we absolutely loved it. My proudest achievement was gracefully cobbling together an absolutely PEAK gaming setup for a lonely statue, followed only by finally beating (final boss spoilers;) Shrimp's level. Having your closest companion and ally (besides Ik) be the "final boss" of the game is GENIUS, and I loved the execution.

Finally, the story is vague, but presents frequent interesting oddities to explore as you go through the game, my favorite of which being the back hidden room in Ik's art gallery.

With all that said, I would recommend this game 10/10 days of the week!
