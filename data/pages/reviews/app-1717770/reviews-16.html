This is a good ass game with a few not so good ass flaws.
First of all, this game is probably one of the funniest games I've played right next to jazzpunk. The comedy here is top tier. Second, the level design is mostly good. Most of the levels here are solid and integrate comedy and horror pretty well along with occasionally having some clever puzzles. Although some levels go on for way too long or don't have anything to them.
Also, this game needed bossfights. You could consider some encounters as "bossfights" but i would've liked actual bosses with healthbars and stuff. Especially because there isn't a final boss even though it seemed like the game was building up to one (the ending itself was pretty solid though).
Overall, this is a solid (and surprisingly scary) game that you should play that you should play for sure
