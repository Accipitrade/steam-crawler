\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is probably in my top 10 games of all time. It's that good. Wonderful soundtrack, characters, gameplay, creativity, story, it's all phenomenal. It's a game that manages to have excellent humor while still having a really nice and touching story. One moment you'll be laughing, the next screaming, then sad. The gameplay is a physics based platformer where every level is completely unique from all the others. You never know what will come next.

This is also my favorite horror game of all time. it's not traditionally scary. There's no jumpscares, the ambience and tension can be good, but aren't what bring the horror, and there's no spooky visuals. The upturned's horror relies on one thing. Panic. Enemies move fast, you move fast. When there is danger, whether you're running or fighting, it is a mad scramble to try to complete whatever you're doing. It's an absolutely brilliant way to bring forth horror and the game uses so many different tactics to bring forth that panic.

At the price point of 10 bucks there's absolutely no way I could possibly not recommend this game. It's an instant buy for me no questions. The game as is will take you about 5-7 hours, and those hours will be stuffed to the brim with phenomenal content. Not long enough for you? Updates will be coming to add free bonus levels meaning that play time will only go up! This game is also an absolutely phenomenal speedgame. If you like speedrunning or are even slightly interested in it this would be a great option to go with! truly a 10/10 game, I can't think of even a SINGLE complaint.\t\t\t\t\t
