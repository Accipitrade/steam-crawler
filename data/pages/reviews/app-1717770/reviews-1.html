\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Upturned is a wacky and yet somehow surprisingly tense and scary at times puzzle platformer with a higher focus on exploration and puzzles and a bit light on the platforming part.

If you're looking for a really challenging platformer to test your skills then this is not for you as the game is relatively easy all things considered and the most difficult parts don't come from the platforming but dealing with the many enemies in the game.

The Upturned's story is not anything to write home about but it's good enough to keep you interested on going up the floors and find out more. What helps is that the character you'll be talking with the most during the game, IK, is quite a fun and charming character that gave me quite a few laughs during my playthrough.

Although the game doesn't ever really try to build a creepy atmosphere be prepared to get jumped during the game as it likes to have enemies pop-up from the darkness from time to time.

For some negatives the game lacks an FPS limiter so have fun having your CPU and GPU maxed out constantly while playing and the player character's walking sound can get a bit irritating sometimes but nothing bad enough to drive me away from the game.

Overall it's a very solid and well made game which doesn't get stale as each new floor of the Upturned Inn has some kind of new thing to play around with combined with a surprisingly long run-time of around 7 hours not taking into account any bonus stuff.\t\t\t\t\t
