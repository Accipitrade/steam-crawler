Suddenly this developer comes along, who scraps the unimportant things and focuses on the most important.

There are several things Upturned does right. Scrapping the glitzy-glatzy eye-candy that doesn't impress anyone anymore. Going with the low-poly, pixelated graphics instead of hi-res detail.
Setting the frantic pace (yes, the whole game is exactly that word: FRANTIC).
While presenting itself as easy, the game is pretty damn HARD, old-school hard.
Awesome level design. I was jaw-dropped when I found out this is a work of one developer, who is still a school student. I mean, way to go.

This game is a perfect companion for this current day and age: absurdity, tragicomedy, hectic, frantic pace, and - yes, the end of the world) Enjoy while you still can!\t\t\t\t\t
