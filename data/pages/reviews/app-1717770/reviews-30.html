\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game really is something special. The characters are well written and pleasant, and the narrative is strong, being significant in a way which vaguely reminded me of another title called Presentable Liberty, with its strong storytelling and the clever method of delivering the plot.

In addition to this, the level design is good. Each area is unique and fun, and for most of the levels it is difficult to get badly lost, meaning you don't always have to worry about going the right way so much. The layout as well seems to have been made with minimizing backtracking in mind, as there are many times that what could have been a long unnecessary walk is avoided by design, keeping the gameplay interesting and a smoother experience. I will note that I found a later level to be frustrating, but largely because I had opted to play through the whole game in one sitting and was anxious to see how the story would end.

The humor is very welcome and is charming, and it is evident that every thing in this game has had a lot of life and style put into it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI originally saw a video of the demo of the game before it came out, After watching I instantly knew this was a game I was interested in. It has a creative story, funny writing, likable characters and inventive game play, After playing through the game, I can still say those things about it, however it is not with out its faults. still though the game is like not even $10 so that a great deal for such a quality game.

obviously these are just my opinions, and I am no way saying that the developer did a bad job or anything, i had a lot of fun with this game, I just had some ideas for things that I thought it was lacking

I liked all of the Different Mechanics from floor to floor, it made for great game play and puzzle, but I was sad that these different mechanics were used only on one floor (i.e. the chucklers, Bird taming, giant skeleton man). I would have liked to see what other interesting puzzles could be made with these, (especially the bird taming)


I feel more could have been done with shrimp. when we first met him he has a fun little detail where you could throw objects and he would bring that back to you, I was thinking there would be some sort of mechanic later on that would involve Shrimp fetching something or using him in some way to solve a puzzle. I wanted to spend quality time with the goodest afterlife boy.


The ending, specifically when it comes to the True owner. While riding the elevator all the way back up to the lobby the True owner starts taking to you and gradually get more and more intense. My assumption was that this was leading to some sort of final showdown with him, but it was just an empty lobby. The floor before "your floor" which is basically last level, seemed like it was just throwing everything at you all at once, it didn't seem like memorable "last Challenge," it was more just random chaos. I would have loved to come face to face with the true owner in some sort of climatic puzzle battling thing, His design looked pretty cool at least in the short time we got to see his face,


I want to reiterate, I had a great time with this game I highly recommend you buy it and try it out for yourself. I can't wait to see if anything more will come out for this game.
