\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHats off to the developer, this was an great game. The ending left me hoping for sequel. It is definitely worth playing. The characters were quirky and unique with enjoyable dialogue that had me chuckling during my play through, with a few irrepressible facepalm moments.
Each level had the same theme of finding a key for a locked room to get the item inside in order to advance. To my relief, every couple of levels introduced a new hazard without becoming overly difficult, but, kept the game play from feeling monotonous.
The interactivity of some of the enemies was really a nice feature, (without to much of a spoiler) targeting the last contact made to the water, tossing objects into them as a distraction, and converting one of them into a tool.
Almost every object having physics gave me quite a few clenched toe moments while being pursued, only to realize the exit was blocked by furniture that was knocked around, forcing me to remain in a confined space while being assaulted from almost every side.
Will be checking for other title from this dev.\t\t\t\t\t
