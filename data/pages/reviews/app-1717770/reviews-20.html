\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is 6-8 hours long, and is exciting for the entire duration. Gameplay mechanics feel really great and the music is suprisingly good. The levels are almost universally great. Every level feels different and there's always something new to keep you interested. Most levels leave enough room for you to try different things out and feel clever/dumb (depending on the achieved result). The atmosphere of the game is very much comedy/horror (think of Courage the cowardly dog). If you don't like horror games, don't worry this is not really one of them.

The only part that could do with a little bit more work is the story, especially the ending. For the most part the story happens in the background, and you don't really get to interact with it nor with the actors involved in it. As mentioned above this really gets evident in the last act where (spoiler alert) it feels like it just happens to you, taking away any player agency. On the other hand, the tounge in cheek writing is good.

All that being said I can wholeheartedly recommend this game.\t\t\t\t\t
