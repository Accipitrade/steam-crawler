Has a SHOGUN oldschool player ( second game i played on pc ) this game is not worth buying despite me loving it, ill explain it in Cons & Pros why my mind made it so.

Cons :

1- Devs completly abandoned this game, has theyr first successfull game they should at least mantain the online servers for the multiplayer option dont just be a button that you press but dont do anything when your paying them for an old game, theyr making milions with the DLC sales in all titles they have and its not just a few, so they defenitly have the money to do so.

3- Some Sound Glitches & derpy animations are within the game.

4- No Dev Support over this game, heck they even removed the title Shogun ( even Shogun 2 ) from theyr main page already.
________________________

Pros

1- Best Total War tactical title, this new total war titles " strip " compared to this title options that make campaigns more interesting (religion & spies / assassinations dont only apply to governors or factions )

2- Best Total War Practicality & simplicity, this game offers the same 2 map mode but, easier menus to build use and research on buildings then even the newest title where all is confusing, you need to do all the tutorials the first time and even so, you might forget about where something is after a while, wich in this game doesnt happens.

3- No DLC's YUSSSSS ! since its abandoned 0 risk off extra charging iff anything gets fixed or implemented haha

4- Unique Shogun ambient and mini-videos from assassinations wich make you the day.

The only TW game I've ever had a consistently easy time finishing campaigns on (Rome 1 being second) makes it stand out among the titles on one of their biggest pitfalls as a game. I'd also rank it as more polished in many ways than any title but Shogun 2. For those new to the games I'd put it over Rome 1 and Med 2 for accessibility - really worth getting for new players or veterans.
