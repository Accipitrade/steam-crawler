\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn excellent re-release of the classic and innovative Shogun: Total War.
Besides a few occassional problems with cut off dialogue sound (but you can activate subtitles!) the game runs fluid and well.
It remains a good, solid game sticking to the core business of Total War and adding some interesting Samurai specific flavor.
The inclusion of the expansion is quite welcome.
There is a tutorial, a sandbox 'grand campaign' and several 'historical campaigns'.
Plenty of options!\t\t\t\t\t
