\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed on Windows 10. For several days, and at least two complete run throughs to total conquest, I had no problems. Features are obviously antiquated which I accept as fine. In some sense I like he simplicity better than the super complex modern versions. The biggest downside to the game (when it's working) is that it is short. I never got the hang of battles so I let the computer compute battles. Playing that way, I conquered the whole map in less than a day.

After two complete campaigns, I hit a technical snag that caused crashing and appeared to completely lose my latest saves which would have made the game unplayable if saving is required. The reason is because I saved after every season and eventually filled up the saved game folder. Don't ask me how that can happen, but it did. When I reinstalled the game and erased most of the out-of-date save files, the problem disappeared. If you save new files a lot, you must do housekeeping.

The short story - I enjoy this game for the simplicity.\t\t\t\t\t
