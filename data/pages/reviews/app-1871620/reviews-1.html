DISCLAIMER: This is a first impressions review, and NOT a full review

PLEASE NOTE: I recommended this game with some exceptions, overall if the game did not live up to just a tiny expectation, I would not have recommended it at all, so please read the review carefully if you are thinking of buying/playing this game

Covid Simulator is a game developed and published by Coldrice Games LLC

The game offers some nice graphics, although some ugly glitches here and there, it is pleasant all the same. The game offers some really haunting but great music which I actually enjoyed listening on repeat whilst playing

The game does suffer with some horrible UI and control issues such as not registering my clicks or initiating something else instead of what I wanted to select which was incredibly infuriating

The only problem with this game is, even though it is a simulator and it's trying to simulate the transfer rate of COVID-19 and how infectious the disease can be, the features that the game offers are just not enough to warrant it being a fun or fully-featured game

After two hours of playtime, I just got... bored. Nothing happens or something does happen and it's the thing you intended to happen. And if you do it in a certain way where you just place Pro-Vax posters everywhere, no one will be the odd one out and change the course of your decisions. And if you just plaster Anti-Vax posters everywhere, almost everyone will die after a while

The game unfortunately just doesn't feel feature complete and that's a shame because it has potential and I understand that the game is developed by one person, but a game like Dust: An Elysian Tale, which was also developed by one person, has so much more features and better gameplay mechanics and such than Covid Simulator has and I think it should have stayed a bit more in the oven before being released, regardless of what it's trying to portray or say with it's social commentary. We've all been through this pandemic for over two years (2022) now, I think (most) people get the picture

Covid Simulator overall, however, is an okay-ish game to play, and it does get incredibly boring if being played for a long time, but is an okay-ish to meh outbreak simulation game and to play in short bursts

This game does have potential, but it really needs a lot of work and improvements to make it even better than it's already meh version of the game

RECOMMENDED :)
