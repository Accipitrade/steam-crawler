\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSeveral very challenging scenario missions. To my great surprise, it was even somewhat difficult even after I unlocked Guts' Beast of Darkness armor/costume. Admittedly, I was only playing on Hard, but I'm lvl 99 with maxed gear so the scenarios were more challenging than I expected, but less than I feared. They are probably very difficult if you're playing on the Berserk difficuly level and aren't maxed out.

The very last level is great for farming in-game currency, if you need more to level up your less-used characters (*coughGRIFFITHcough*). I average anywhere from 600-700k gold per mission.\t\t\t\t\t
