\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a fun pretty mellow experience. It's Starcom, it's hardly a new IP, but the polish shows.

Some reviews seemed to complain about things that must have been patched by the time I got to the game. You can keep notes, but it keeps a pretty exhaustive log of what hasn't been figured out or is an ongoing "quest" or what have you.

You explore planets, do a bit of trading, fight space battles with loathsome aliens, and take it at your own pace. The pacing of the game is pretty chill, which honestly appeals to me. I've been going back and forth between this and Drox Operative 2 - this is more of the star trek style exploring and helping/harming the natives or puzzles or whatever with some combat, where Drox Op is an ARPG in an insane game of Stellaris.

If you want a laid back experience, go for it. Anything you think is bottlenecking you - relax. They will candy cannon research, materials, etc all in due course. You'll find asteroid fields/wreckage to essentially farm resources even if you're the worst trader in the world or you wasted resources. The game is pretty easy on normal, I'm going to crank up the difficulty at some point but right now, this is my favorite relaxation game.\t\t\t\t\t
