\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverrated somewhat. While not a terrible game it has (IMO).... gameplay design issues

I'll probably never come back to it nor finish it. It just really became not fun.

My issues:
- Travel time is tedious. More so when its travel to dead/void/dark space (I think, dark space is more correct, just empty). Depending on hardware and research it cane be a few minutes (I literally started pulling up something to watch).
- A race called Aroana (bird people) their ships teleport behind and far away every freaking ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ 2secs... it was not fun taking a fight that should be like 10secs into probably 2-5 minutes. They endlessly did it and regardless of damage (actual pieces missing).
- The map is only partially random, or whatever. There could at least be a random map mode.
- Its just you going place to place mostly for resources. This could have been done so much better and brought more interesting mechanics to the game.
- Attack Range = Zoom out = Ship Shrinking. As you extend your attack range you have to zoom more out (so you can see what your shooting at) and thus shrink your ship. Personally (cause i need to say that for the white knights) i just kept finding myself unable keep an eye on my direction while managing and watching: numerous missile fire, enemies and distance, incoming fire, managing my energy levels ... etc etc. *The game could really have used nearly 1/2 a attack range cut so you are actually NOT looking at some dinky small ship on the screen... etc etc. *be nice to see those visuals*

Honestly, i couldn't care to go into details. People only read the most upvoted and just look at total reviews. Every game that isn't riddled with bugs are all rated 90%+ even crappy Anime games.

The simply point is that it was partly tedious and frustrating enough to not care about playing it anymore. The game could have done better.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinally, finished playing this game....................... and WOW !!! WOW!!!

In a nutshell, this game has a beginning, middle and an end story wise. As events happens around you while you discover your surroundings, anomalies and races, all leading to a grand Opera finale like no other game. Almost 40 hours gameplay in total ! It is worth every cent this game!

The game is absolutely fantastic and the story is pure mind blowing. Whoever wrote the game story deserves an Oscar! Very captivating, intriguing, so many mysteries to solve. interaction with different alien species, exploring anomalies, etc... all were very interesting and plenty of differences.

Graphics were great, with consistent gameplay and pure fun. I love the research tech! Just wished there was a zoom in and out feature in the tech tree. Sound department, beautiful sound everything match!

I had more fun playing this one than Starcontrol Origin. What really captivated me so much besides the great story was also the seriousness of the graphics. The characters design, the concept Artists here really represented very well the world and everything with the story.

It felt like playing an interactive space novel literally.

I W A N T M O R E !!! I W A N T M O R E !!! I W A N T M O R E !!!

I hope future DLCs or Starcom Nexus 2 (anything you want to call it), will keep this line of art / design / story (lots mysteries), etc.

Congratulations to all involved in developing this game! Pure fantastic game! Also thanks to the community in the forum. I was stuck few times and a quick search in the forum, I found the solutions and managed to proceed with the story. Thank you all !\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat an incredible fun game this has been so far. It is not super complex but that is fine in this case. FTL wasn't that deep either and turned out to be great as well. I would kinda compare it to FTL in the sense of the management part of the game and how the story is presented. But in this one you get to fly around and explore and blow stuff up hard. And where most space games have you just roam around in a void of nothing. In this game you always seem to find something of interest in each sector. The research skill tree is also nicely done and makes it so the game gets even more fun the longer you play and unlock new content for your ship. The story is also very interesting and the different races you meet along the way are good as well.

In short if you like space games that lets you explore and have lots of things to do in it. But also has a good solid underlying story. Then you need to treat yourself to this one. Kudos to the developers for making it. and will keep my eye out for future projects of them.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game was pretty good up to the 29 hr mark for me, but after that it became incomprehensible. I had to go to discussions to see that in order to cure the "sleeping disease" I had to speak with a certain race and then I would be able to go to (a place) to buy something which I would give to the research guy...but you cannot figure this out in game. Nothing points to it. The game is great in the beginning and mid-game(ish) because you have some directions, but later there is no way to find a solution other than to blindly stumble onto it and you'll quit before that happens.
The end game needs to be rethought. The beginning and middle were fine, why put tedious and "no reason to have them", "no solid way to solve them except by brute force" puzzles at the end that you have to guess at? It's a very good game, but not always good at directing the player towards the next goal.
There are also game breaking bugs. You may not get them, but I did.\t\t\t\t\t
