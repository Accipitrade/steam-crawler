\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStarcom Nexus is a highly addictive, fun, old school type of game.

It is basically an exploration game full of trivia, popular themes, beloved concepts and mini riddles of various layers.

Ship building is great and suprisingly in-depth but not extremely penalising if you go for a more lighthearted approach.

Exploration is enjoyable and varies from the quite obvious to the well-hidden, creating an 'old school' atmosphere of uncertainty at certain points whithout overdoing it.

Combat is fun, with a good balance of ship building/tactics/arcade element.

It's one of those games which you log in for "an hour or so" and suddenly you realise its well past midnight.

Don't play it on normal its way too easy, as many have suggested, try it on very hard or insane settings.

I would highly recommend it even at full price. If you like teh genre, it is money very well spent.\t\t\t\t\t

I got tired of waiting for something to happen.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wasn't expecting much from an indie space game for 15$, but this game is addictive in all the right ways. It plays like a Star Trek simulator you go from planet to planet on away missions which give you loot with an RPG like mechanic. You use the loot (crafting material and research points) to upgrade your ship. I've only played about 7 hours or so, the story is good so far. I've only scratched the surface and I already feel I've uncovered multiple mysteries that will need to be solved.

This game probably isn't for everybody though. If your looking for an arcade style space shooter with non stop action this won't be for you. You have to be willing to read to get sucked into the story. Some negative reviews have been posted about travel speed but honestly I feel it's a non factor I'm already zipping along at 150 AU/s which is about 8 times the speed of my starter ship. Wormholes and gateways are everywhere and I have yet to unlock the warp drive which is probably another mobility tool, advanced turbo, and all the techs that passively increase speed in empty sectors. Getting from one side of a sector to another takes like 10 seconds max. Travel time in empty space was tedious in the first hour now is acceptable after only 7ish hours and will be very fast once I research the technology and slap even more engines on my ship.

TLDR: This game is amazing especially for it's super low price. I highly recommend it.\t\t\t\t\t
