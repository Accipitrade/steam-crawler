\r\n\t\t\t\t\tO my god, this game is perfect. I rarely ever review games, let alone having played only maybe an hour but this game is an instant favourite of mine. Top five I ever played, seriously. Controls: perfect. You can set your bloody mouse side buttons for actions (which you sometime can't do in AAA games). Map: perfect; not cluttered, everything is clearly marked, you know where you are supposed to go what you are supposed to do - without being "made" do stuff, you are the captain of your own ship (literally). Upgrade, science: PERFECT. You can build your ship bit by bit. Resources: just the perfect amount, not too few, not too many. Combat: WOW, very satisfying. Story: just the right amount; your are not drowning in text, it is still lovely and funny. Scanning planets: satisfying, a bit like FTL (you take a bit of risk when surveying a planet); nice! I can't list any negatives now, diving right back in. Thanks devs!\t\t\t\t\t
