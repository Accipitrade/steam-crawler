\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game. Totally worth the price. I'm shocked it's not more popular than it is.

The writing is very good, on multiple occasions throughout the game I did a double-take and was shocked at what was unfolding. They do a great job of leaving bread crumbs throughout the story and I found myself making what I thought were reasonable guesses based on the information provided. Aliens you meet along the way are interesting culture-wise and clearly have their own motivations. Well done.

Gameplay-wise, those echoing Star Control 2 are spot-on. This almost feels like a spiritual successor, but specifically, the combat is tight -- smooth, responsive controls, visual feedback on damage is good, it really feels like you're in a space battle. Modular ship designs mean you can build your ship however you'd like and also easily target weak points on enemies when you can figure out the location of their core components.

The music is entrancing, reminds me of 2001. Sound effects are good, I wish there was voice acting!

If you've made it this far, buy it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is an excellent game; I highly recommend it to anyone who's a fan of the Firefly series.

The story line is fantastic, and I love the humour, the dialogue, the writing, and even the achievements. Love the ship design and elements, too.

Kudos to the creator; this was worth every cent.\t\t\t\t\t

If you're a fan of Starflight or Star Control 2 or similar games I can definitely recommend this.

Things which could be added: a map filter to show only "unfinished" planets, autopilot to remote locations, and more in depth race relations.

Well worth the asking price, IMO.\t\t\t\t\t
