I found it by chance because I ran out of systems to visit, updated my scanner range and went to the nearest one.they gave me a couple of questions which I of course couldn't answer and started attacking me.

This brings me to another problem. Apparently you were supposed to know the answers by the system in which you found the the maps of the old scavenger teams. Well I found one. But in the log, it neither told me that it was par of the bird story nor where I found it, so being absolutely useless

All great games, this is in a similar vein and as I said, I'm really enjoying it. The tech tree is fairly comprehensive, ship building is also fairly open ended. Finding resources has proven difficult in these two hours, but that's the whole point. The game is just starting, so every time I manage to build an upgrade for my ship I feel accomplishment.

Great game, thoroughly recommend.

Update: I just finished the game and I did it with a smile. The ending was... well, once you actually got there, it was a little abrupt. But the unfolding story, the last hour or two before the actually curtain close (as we say) is brilliant. I can't recommend this game enough to anyone who loved the feel of exploration in games like Star Control 2, or Escape Velocity Nova.\t\t\t\t\t
