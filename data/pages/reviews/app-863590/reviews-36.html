\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReally enjoyed this game. Reminded me of an ancient game called "Starflight" that I really enjoyed when I was in high school. Exploration is fun and diverse enough that you want to keep seeing what's around the corner. Lots of mysteries that don't resolve until much later.

The biggest drawback in my opinion, is that if you play the game like I do, and prioritize exploration over completing missions, you'll have a lot of missions floating around in your log. The log is a bit of a challenge to use--some things put out map markers, some don't (and you have to find and use the coordinates), some quests don't update when you think they should, etc.

But overall fun. The ships have a good feel--fun to build and try, etc.\t\t\t\t\t
