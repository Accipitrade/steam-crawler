\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one hell of an addicting game. The gameplay is smooth and adapts regularly as you upgrade your technology and ship. The research tree is substantial and allows you to make steady progress as well as occasional breakthroughs from discovery. The shipbuilding isn't as robust as something like Starcontrol, and I would have liked to see a little more work there, but it's by no means inadequate.

I was impressed with how well progress is gated. Resources, research points, blueprint size, quests, and discoveries all serve to hold you back your progress while increasing difficulty of aliens necessitates that progress. You'll find yourself challenged each time you meet a new race, struggling to beat their larger ships, but not entirely overwhelmed, and with some careful decision-making you can get the research and resources you need to upgrade to match their abilities.

Honestly, the only complaint I have is that the scroll wheel didn't work in the research tree for me, so I assumed that the only research available initially was what I could see on the screen. So I spent all my initial research points upgrading my engines, thrust, and deep-space speed to the maximum before I realized I was only looking at like 5% of the tree. Despite that, I was actually still able to progress. The engine speed made exploring a breeze, thrusting let me escape to heal up during fights that I was under-powered for, and I quickly amassed a new stockpile of research to start working on batteries, structure, weapons, and all the other stuff I had missed.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust getting started with this game but my first outing was highly enjoyable and definitely reminded me of a game I played more than 20 years ago called Escape Velocity. At first the controls seemed a little janky since I was using a mouse and keyboard but once I saw that it was possible to use an xbox gamepad I switched and would never go back since it is much easier to fly and fight with twin sticks. It looks like I will get quite a few hours out of this game and it isn't nausea inducing like most 3d space shooters can be so those are both big pluses. Definitely recommend this title to anyone who doesn't mind reading a little bit since there is no VO and who played older 2d shooters like EV.\t\t\t\t\t
