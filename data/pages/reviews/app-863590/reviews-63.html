\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStarcom: Nexus puts you in the captain's chair of a small, but customizable starship. You explore a map with a mix of randomly generated and hand built solar systems.

Pros:
+ Highly customizable ship
+ Extensive upgrade tree to suit you particular play style
+ The universe reacts to your decisions and the AI ships interact with each other in the background (I.E. randomly show up and start battles)
+ Interesting anomalies on planets that let you make decisions that could go horribly wrong or reap great rewards

Cons:
- The game is very stingy when dropping technologies you can't research and must be found in enemy wreckage
- Certain planets don't have a 'return to the shuttle' option, and they almost always result in the loss of crew

Overall though it is a solid game and I personally found it to be worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
