\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is exactly the game that I was looking for when I found it. I am a huge fan of Star Control 2 and the Starflight games, and over the years there have been a couple that came close, but it has been a while since I got this sucked into a game. The story kept me hooked from start to finish and I always felt a sense of wanting to explore the next system to see what little story or interaction I was going to experience next. While not as humorous as Star Control 2, the alien species were still interesting enough. The length of the game was just right, it didn't drag on too long, nor did it end too quickly. Trading resources is not complicated or particularly important, but it can help you get certain types of resources when you need them. The storyline was intriguing and had enough unique elements that I would say this game sits right up next to Star Control 2 or Starflight as one of my new favorites.

Highly recommend to anyone who was a fan of those games, but it isn't for everyone. Flying through deep space, or in areas where there aren't any gates, travel time can take quite a while. Other than that, I had a wonderful time playing this game.\t\t\t\t\t
