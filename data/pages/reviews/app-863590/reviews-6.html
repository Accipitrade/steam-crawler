Trekkies as well as Babylon 5 fans should get a lot out of this one I gather, and those who liked Elite but desired something a bit simpler but also a bit more expansive should also feel right at home with this game. \r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has been a very interesting game. Reading through other reviews before I bought it, I saw it compared to Star Control 2, and Outer Wilds. I found the game through theXPGamers' video on the sequel, and I can't believe I hadn't heard of it before.

This game does remind me of Star Control 2 quite a lot, but it is wholly its own game. In 28.6 hours, I have dome a single playthrough. It was not completionist (even if I tried somewhat), but I finished the game with maybe 4 unfinished quests in my log. The game did not crash during my playthrough, and it rarely bugged beyond the occasional stutter (which I think was just the invisible loading screens/instance borders).

I greatly enjoyed my playthrough of this game, and I would strongly recommend it to anyone looking for a single-player game about exploration, space ship combat, and story.\t\t\t\t\t
