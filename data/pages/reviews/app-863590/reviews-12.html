A simple, but well executed, space exploration game
While Starcom Nexus has few systems and little complexity to it, I think those systems are well implemented. The story is text-based, which I ususally don't appreciate, but it was for the most part quite interesting. Some of the stories (quests) were a bit too unclear in my opinion, and I was stuck a few times wondering what to do next or where to go. Still, I found my way by checking the forums a few times. By exploring the universe you discover new technologies that help you improve and outfit your ship to brave new challenges. All in all I had quite a lot of fun playing the game. Looking forward to the upcoming sequel. All in all, I give it a 7/10. Well done, and thanks.\t\t\t\t\t
