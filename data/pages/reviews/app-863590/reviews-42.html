\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere is a lot to love about this game, and other than the final... 1.5? hours of it, the game is a lot of fun. The combat is interesting, the ship-building is fantastic (more games could take a look at how this is done), and the audio/visuals of the game are enjoyable. There's a few spelling mistakes here and there, but those can easily be patched out, and they weren't so bad as to cause me actual problems.

Pros:
- Enjoyable navigation, flying, and combat
- Interesting species to interact with
- Ship building is extremely satisfying
- So many things to find/explore
- Real feeling of power progression as you go along

The cons?
- Sometime's it's not super obvious what you have to do, so you end up wandering aimlessly at points. As a tip: when in doubt, just go back to home base and talk to everyone.
- The game spends a long time setting up the end boss/baddies and it just ... doesn't deliver. By the time I got there, the big bads weren't a challenge for me, and the story just sort of fell apart. I was so let down by the end of this game, given how much fun I had right up until then.\t\t\t\t\t
