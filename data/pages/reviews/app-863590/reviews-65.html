\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been searching for a 2D Space RPG for a long time. Nothing has scratched that itch as well as SPAZ 1 until this. The upgrade system is really good, but you have to WORK for the research points to unlock new stuff and the materials to build the components to use some of it. I think the icing on the cake is this game has a story, with mystery, and unique alien races that you can actually interact with. It has rudimentary trading, materials only, but what's there is much better then most space RPGs that try to shoe-horn the whole trader/mercenary/fighter/pirate/etc formula.

The only thing lacking at this point is more content. Even with the hand-crafted nature of the universe, it does seem like the developer is delivering on new content at an acceptable rate. This is one of those games that as a part time hobby developer makes me wish I could jump into the code and help out!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore I begin, I'd like to point out this game held my attention much longer than the RE2 remake and Shadow of the Tomb raider did. Pretty crazy I know.

I played the first Starcom 10 years ago! It's taken awhile, but finally the next has appeared. For those that enjoyed the original, you'll naturally enjoy this one. For those unfamiliar, go play the first for free. If you enjoy it, then this'll be just as enjoyable. Onto the game...

Pros;

Looks very pleasing, sun flares and all. Weapon effects look good, and shields look rad.
While technically repetitive, something always allures me to keep going. (Grinding for tech upgrades is always fun!)
Variety of enemies, which can pose a challenge.
Very little bugs, very good for early access.

Cons (even if it pains me to);

While the ship building is a nice idea, I was hoping to get actual "models" to play with (technically there are, just LEGOed pieces together)
Controller was a little iffy (Missiles being the most important) didn't work for me.
Traversing large gaps in space between solar systems felt more like a chore than fun exploration.

Otherwise a great game so far. Add some more content, add different proper ships, and some changes and honestly I have full confidence this game will be 10/10 when actually completed.\t\t\t\t\t

PROS:
Planets are beautiful to look at, and interesting to explore.
In fact, eye candy is everywhere in this game. From nebula storms to the way the ship lights up when you fire plasma and a lot inbetween!
Ships are fun/easy to build.
Upgrades have IMPACT. Even the non-weapon related ones where exciting to get.
Characters/Aliens had personality, and the dialogue was FANTASTIC.
Seriously, the dialogue was really good. More pls.
Combat and flight mechanics forced me to PILOT my ship, espeically when trying to take down difficult enemies.
Science actually passes the smell test enough to not break immersion.
A pulsar destroyed my ship.

Cons:
This game isn't 10 years old with a thriving mod community that would allow me to spend the next year lost in this universe.
There isn't a button on here that will let me send them more money. I looked.

I'm rooting for you Starcom! And I'm going to pay attention to Wx3 Labs from now on too.
