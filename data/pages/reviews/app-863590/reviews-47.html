\r\n\t\t\t\t\tStarcom: Nexus is an story driven exploration game that is very well executed. I found myself zipping from star system to star system chasing new ? planets to uncover what secrets it might hold. The story will gradually reveal its self through fragments you uncover while zipping around the galaxy. I often found myself heading towards an objective to only be distracted cause my officer informed me of a new signal detected or a new star picked up by long range sensors, and off I would go to explore this anomaly or system.

My only real issue with the game is towards the end of the game combat becomes a irrevelent and slow as your starship is just a giant almost unkillable brick. That is not to say you can't still be killed if you are dumb and pick a fight with the tree people.

Overall I highly recommend this game and I really hope to see more content come out for post game. The game is totally worth paying full price for the experience.\t\t\t\t\t
