It's a damn fun game, but it requires some thought, which today's players don't have much or are just too lazy.

I'd get it on full price, but if you're sceptical wait for a sale.

3 euros for 8-13 hours of fun (depending if you want to all side missions)

Get this gem.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDon't buy into all the negative reviews, this game is treated unjustly. Why? Because alot of people just could not grasp the concept.

RONIN is an interesting blend of platforming, stealth, turn based-strategy and action game unlike anything you have played specifically, but reminscent of many games you have. All of it comes together nicely with a simple yet effective art style, music and level design. The story has a thin narrative but it does the job well enough establishing a cyber-punk samurai tale of revenge.

The controls (I used Mouse+Keyboard) were responsive and tightly tuned and they really let me sink my teeth into the finer nuances of combat such as letting bullets graze me and threading those jumps.

The game poses a moderate challenge even for a veteran gamer as it is not a simple hack+slash stealth game, but actually requires you to think ahead and attack at the right time or even the right angle. The learning curve is real and you will die until you ~GET GUD~ but not as much as these negative reviews would have you believe.

The players have all the advantages in the wonderful combat of RONIN, they only need use them!

Highly recommended\t\t\t\t\t
