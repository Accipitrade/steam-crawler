\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNice game with great thought through turn-based fighting system.
I want to warn you about some fact about game (not spoilers).
Though it called ronin - it's not a game about ninja: there is little to no stealth. Usually you need to kill all the enemies and avoid civilian casualties.
There is not a lot of story in this game. Story is plain and simple and motivation of the antagonist is clear from the beginning.

This game is about finding your way in a room with a lot of enemies without fast-paced gameplay. You have your time to think you next step. All this with satisfying visuals and nice music.

If you are looking for moderate game with a sword killing to chill without mashing buttons on your keyboard, this is one to look upon.\t\t\t\t\t
