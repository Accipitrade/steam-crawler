\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFishy 3D is a fun little puzzle/exploration game, and a great sequel to Fishy. I thought that the move to 3D worked a lot better than I predicted, and I enjoyed the game a lot. It took me around 2.5 hours to get every achievement, but I did have to consult a guide for a couple of the moons. The collectibles are fun to try to get, but the hardest 2 or 3 are really difficult to find on your own. The speedrun achievement isn't too hard, I did it first try after finishing the game for the first time. Fishy 3D is a fun way to spend a couple of hours. I highly recommend it, especially for only $3, but I'd recommend getting in in the Fishy bundle as its only $1 cheaper. One of my favorite Sokpop Games so far!\t\t\t\t\t
