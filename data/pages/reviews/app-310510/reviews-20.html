\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBeing only able to build certain types of traps in certain places is odd, but I got used to it quickly and combining different towers and ground traps for combo-attacks is fun.

I only played the marksman so far (who maybe wasnt the best decision for a starting character?) and the overal tower defense mechanics are nice, but I like experimentation, so the 200 gold cost for resetting each separate trap- and skillpoint is quite a pain for me. It is possible to invest in a skill that increases the gold gain by up to 50%, but that reduces the effectiveness of the character, which is quite needed in the higher tiers of the maps.

There seems to be an unresolved issue about game crashes / freezes too (I only experienced this twice so far), and according to a discussion thread some achivements are unavailable without reverting to an earlier version of the game.

I got back to the game after writing a negative review about it at first and now I can recommend Deathtrap, at least if you pick it up on sale.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's fun until you've completed the first run of the maps. The decision made by the designer to stop you being able to replay old maps and still farm XP is a bad one in my opinion, it forces the player to continue to play on the next tier, rather than to go back and enjoy it at their own pace. Although I've played for 50+ hours, I end up hitting this wall where my time spent playing is no longer rewarded with the ability to level up.

I've played this 1,2 and 3 player, it's absolutely more fun with others- the design of the levels is such that it encourages it. This is fine, but the game is less enjoyable on your own.\t\t\t\t\t
