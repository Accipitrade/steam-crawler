\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUpside: well made, fast paced, very few bugs. The game runs smooth especially since it's a little old, but the graphics are still good. Decent playtime and progression feels fluent enough (do level 1 endless mode if you feel stuck/ are bired leveling up). The mechanics are great and you can use your own playstyle for both towers used and hero leveling (no one skill/ tower is 20x better than another, although some clearly stand out and are preferable).

And some downsides: old game, so the community is virtually gone. Getting in a party with someone to play a co-op is pretty hard; getting to play a versus mode is virtually inexistent (at least I never got to try it although I have 24h playing); the connection to other players is weird (some weird coding from the deva majes it so that even if you see a game, you can't connect to it). And finally when you do connect, the players kick you out of the party, are afk, or don't know English. You will find a few lone wanderers here and there which don't kick you out and you can play, but this doesn't mean they know English. Most posts on online formums are from years ago and many are not even relevant anymore since the game changed after multiple patches. The game's wiki page is kinda empty and lachustre (which indicates it didn't had a huge community even in its prime days). The crafting feels "off": even at max level if you wanna build an item, the RNG loot element of the game and crafting assures you that you should create that item a lot of times (5-20x depending on what stats you're looking for) if you really want some specific stats. Building a high quality and high level item generally gives you 5 random stats. So you're essentially "rolling a dice 5 times" when you're trying to build that item with some specific points in mind. The main problem with this is that even at max level there is no consistent way of gaining mats quickly, so you so have quite a bit of grinding to do. The RNG part assures you that the best way to get items is to keep grinding maps for items, mats and gold, keep checking the shop (it sometimes sells you item schemas or even legendaries), and keep trying to build an item. The bright side is that some maps give more chests than others, so there are some specific farming spots that are better than others.

8/10 in its prime.
7/10 now tha very few people still play it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe best tower defence game I've ever played.

Like many TD games these days, this is an action-TD game with has a character/hero in it too, because traditional TD games can get a bit dull after you've laid out all your traps and there's nothing left for you to do except watch the action unfold.

And of all the action-TD games I've played, this has to be the best by far. And that's not a criticism of its competitors, I like those too! This one just does everything right.

- The environment is excellent - the music, graphics, effects, story, etc.
- The action is requires skill and thought.
- The traps are diverse and allow for decent strategy.
- The RPG / level up elements work really well, are very varied, and compelling.
