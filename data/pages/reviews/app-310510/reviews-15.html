\r\n\t\t\t\t\tChallenging and very well balanced tower defense game.

PROS
* Lots of traps and upgrades, i liked the customisations available.
* Replayable with higher levels of difficulty providing a good challenge.
* Variety in the levels and creatures attacking.
* Shop is not pay to win, and weapon generation matches character level.

CONS
* I disliked the graphics because the colours are all too grey, hard to distinguish enemies and attacks (eg electrical attacks are hardly visible sparks).
* Very weak story, voice acting is repetitive after a while, could have had much better dialogue.
* Levels are linear and easy to predict/defend. Would be nice if there was a random spawning option, or maybe that's the later stages i didn't get to.
* Default key mapping is horrible. TIP: remap character movement to WASD. Use keyboard for character movement and spells, plus mouse for aiming and attacking. Spells can't be re-assigned mid-game, need to pick top 4 before starting a level. There is a lot of clicking for attacks :(

SPOILER TIPS:
* Place traps in the middle path and near the gate makes the game easier.
* For larger maps with lots of enemy spawn points, place enough (most) traps on one half of map so that you can focus on attacking on the other. This saves you running back and forwards.
* Cheat - collect equipment that has % chance of finding magical items even if they are weak. Put these in your character inventory (C) on the mid-right side. Once you are in the final wave, and the boss is close to dying, quickly press C and right click on each item to equip it. This allows you to boost the good equipment found in chests by >30% once the boss dies. \t\t\t\t\t

There is not much story, just an Intro and an Outro, after 13 stages. After that you can either repeat these 13 stages in 3 harder difficulties, (play scenarios, endless mode (you have for both finish "Story" first), or fire up the editor make your own lvls, or download lvls from the workshop.

You can choose between 3 classes and if i had to rank them: Sorceress > Marksman > Mercenary.
Unfortunately it's only possible to use 6 skills activly out of 9 or 12(?)

25 Traps to choose from, which is kinda lame that the 3 classes ain't had unique and special traps, which makes to replay the game boring and uninteresting.

The progress is rather slow-passed, some enemies are getting stuck at the gates and then you have to search them,....

Overall a smart idea, mix Tower Defense with Diablo-like.

Because of repetition and monotony, i'll stay with an "Meh"\t\t\t\t\t
