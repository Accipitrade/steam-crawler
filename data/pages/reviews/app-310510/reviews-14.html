\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun tower defence mixed with Diablo-esque movement and abilities, pretty simple to grasp a characters pro's and cons. Only complaint is that boss mobs are too realistic sometimes, (Some of them literally swag-walk past you and won't stop for a hug and/or spell to the face.) But others will happily sit hitting you for hours if you stand in front of them as if they are fuelled with a desire for deep revenge.)

Multiplayer is pretty good, though I am yet to grasp how Essence is distributed in this setting yet (I feel like it is scaled automatically for 3 people at minimum, however it is not impossible and the challenge is pretty fun.

Tried to play entirely in Hard mode from the start, found a block when Flying monsters got introduced, then returned and after 2 attempts achieved the three stars, I got hard mode complete in tier 1 on the first 5 Chapters after around 6 hours, definitely not a game to play for a full day as you might get frustrated, but certainly one of those games worth coming back to often!

9/10 in my books, from what I see so far I would have loved a little bit more narrative, but otherwise a charming game that can frustrate and entertain for a good amount of time both alone or with friends.\t\t\t\t\t
