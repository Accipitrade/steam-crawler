\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was part of the Van Helsing bundle I bought. I'd never played a tower defense game, mainly RPG games but I really enjoy this game while leveling and getting through the 4 tiers.
- The game is easy to learn, you have a skill tree for your character and a skill tree for your traps. You can swap points around all you want at the cost of some gold which you'll get plenty of. You can craft items which is pretty straight forward too.
- The first 2 tiers are pretty easy, tier 3 and 4 get a bit more challenging but not impossible to do. If it gets too difficult on tier 3/4 you can always do 1 or 2 runs of the first level on endless mode to give you a lot of extra levels.
- Maybe in the past this was different but getting a party for multiplayer modes is close to impossible so consider this a solo game. Fortunately the main game was designed for solo so it doesn't affect that part.

If you are a hardcore gamer that always aims to beat the hardest tiers, nightmare modes etc then you'll probably be bored with this game in no time. If you are a casual gamer I think you will enjoy this game at least while getting to max level. It's definately worth the 2 bucks it's on sale for at the moment.\t\t\t\t\t
