\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis has got to be the most underrated tower defense game on Steam. You like Diablo? You'll love it. You like (pick any good TD game) you'll love it. I don't know how I went so long without knowing this game existed. It's super fun. The characters are interesting and the archetypes are very diverse allowing for multiple playthroughs and new experiences. In addition to laying down traps, your character can directly engage the enemy using an interesting variety of skills which, much like your traps, can also be upgraded. I put a very high value point on replayability. I can’t stand “one-shot” games where you beat the main campaign and that’s all she wrote. Each map also has different modes. Scenario and Endless. I’m still working on the campaign maps but this yet another factor for replayability. Ladies and Gentlemen, if you like action RPGs and Tower Defense games, you have nothing to lose.

Even better still , there is a map editor. You can make your own crazy maps and share them right on Steam.

On a side note, if you like this you might like the Adventures of Van Helsing games, by the same studio, which happen to also feature various TD moments. The franchise is some very well constructed action RPGs with a wonderful amount of depth. For reasons I fail to understand, the games have a mixed review on Steam. If you’re a hardcore action RPG nut, it will absolutley satisfy the itch. I found the games a ton of fun and very engaging. Very much worth the relatively low price point.

At the time of writing this you can get the entire Van Helsing anthology, plus DLC, for a absurdly epic price of $7.50. Worth every penny and then some.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tImagine Diablo 2 and a tower defense game mashed together.

That's what this is.

I can say I'm very glad I put in on my wish list. This game won't win any awards, but if they make a sequel with a good budget, they absolutely will.

Pros:
+ Outdated but good graphics.
+ Variety of enemies
+ Coop online
+ 55+ levels (I'm level 9-ish at the moment after a few hours of play, so it seems like there's lots of content here!)
+ Visceral combat/traps
+ interesting traps/towers etc.
+ Decent (not amazing) talen specs
+ Bosses feel epic (sometimes spawn mini-bosses)
+ If this game has microtransactions, I haven't seen any yet.

Cons
- Outdated graphics
- Minimal story telling
- Liniar Progression.
