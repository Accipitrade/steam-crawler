\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've had this game for the past 4 years and had not played it. My mistake. Deathtrap is amazing. It is a near-perfect blend of action RPG and Tower defense. In it we control a character of on of three basic archetypes. In typical ARPG fashion, we can attack with our character, level up, find random loot, pick our skills and otherwise customize our play style.

Along with fighting the monsters ARPG-style, we also build, upgrade and tactically modify traps on the fly, as well as between waves. There are some quests per level that add a frantic, fun level of tension, as you are trying to complete these quests while defending your Ink Gate. Tense and fun. Push your luck, and try risky quests for better random loot or craft you own gear between maps. Each level is replayable which extends the game play time and adds to the fun in trying different characters, or the same character on the same level repeatedly.

No doubt, this is NeoCore's best, most polished game. Well worth playing if you enjoy ARPGs and/or TD games.\t\t\t\t\t
