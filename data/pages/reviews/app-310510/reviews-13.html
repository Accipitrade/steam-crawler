\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this game on sale a few years ago, and have just gotten around to playing it. I have to say this is a fun game, it is what you get when Diablo and Dungeon Defenders have a baby. It is a mix of Action/RPG and Tower Defense and it is put together impressively well. I have no complaints and I feel that I am getting my money's worth of entertainment. There are three different classes, a melee character (fighter), a caster character (mage), and a physical/poison (hunter), ranged character to choose from. There are many customization options, and the RNG aspect of the game, when it concerns gear makes it random and delightfully unpredictable.

The Sorceress and Mercenary are easy to play, but the Marksman is much more of a challenge to master...\t\t\t\t\t
