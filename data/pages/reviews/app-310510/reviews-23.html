\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI got this with 3 other friends in order to enjoy some tower defense meets diablo gameplay which sounded awesome. The game is pretty fun in co-op mode as well as dorking around in single player.

Unfortunately only 2 of us could actually play the game. Nvidia cards seem to not have the same issues but if you have an AMD card in your system I don't recommend buying this game. I've spent countless hours and multiple system configurations trying to tweak and solve whatever the issue is.

From everything I can test it seems to be with how the game interacts with the video card drivers. Its like they wrote a proprietary interface that works correctly with nvidia drivers only. It is really unfortunate but I've found no settings that make the game playable past around level 14. And the game play is rather crappy up until them most of the time.

** NOTE: if you have an AMD video card do NOT buy this game! **

I want to recommend this game, and if they ever fix it and I notice maybe I would, but for now it has left me with unpleasant feelings and so I would warn people away from it so as to save them the pain.\t\t\t\t\t

Imagine my surprise whilst playing i suddenly get a borgovian hat..Hmm borgovian I say, that reminds me of the van helsing games..Let me check.and tadaa, it is from the same devs.Great already since I love that series (depite some of the gnarly bugs).

I have yet to encounter any bugs in this one, aside from a may or not be design desicion that doesn't grant you chest if you didn't pick it up before end last wave.

Same with the skills for your adventurer/defender/.. smth
A lot of good passive skills, something I can enjoy, don't want to be micromanaging the traps or the toon, it's exactly the good balance that makes this mix work.Otherwise it'd be a td with action elements or an action game with td elements. It's not, it's a true hybrid.Fun to play in co-op or SP, can't comment on online multiplayer since i play with a friend.

EDIT:
Playing a bit more: HM with random people is crazy and fun :) and sometimes even with just two we really get our ass handed to us and have to try different strategies.Maps change depending on what tier and how many players, not just more hp and more enemies, maps and possible traps change.Atm at 56hr and nowhere near done :D

For this price, just get it already\t\t\t\t\t

I bought this game during the summer sale and it was included in a package deal because it would be cheaper to do it this way then to buy the games seperately. Anyway, even if I didn't buy the package for THIS game, a tower defense game is always interesting.

My conclusion? No. Skip this game, because I am certain there are better alternatives. F You developers for forgetting such a basic thing.\t\t\t\t\t
