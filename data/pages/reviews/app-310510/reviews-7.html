\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe good news is that game is good. The bad news is the mid-game economy. I will update the review after checking out end-game economy.

There are two types of currency: gold and gems. You earn gold from maps and spend in store. You earn gems from item dischanting and spend in craft.

But:
- You don't need gold. If you need to buy something - you will be able to do it: you can get 5000-6000 gold from map, and an average item cost is about 800 gold. You have just can't spend all the gold you have. Yea, you can buy all store and dischant it for gems, but
- You don't need gems. You have several (about 20) crafting recepies, all of crafted items are not better then items from store.

And you don't really need the items. Yea, it can buff you damage etc, but you will be fine using only dropped items.

Futhermore, there are weird mechanics: if you are hero A, you will be able to buy and craft items for hero B. Idk why)

I think crafted items should have unique modifiers that cannot be obtained by any other way. It will fix all the issues.\t\t\t\t\t
