\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf Grim Dawn and Kingdom Rush had a child, that would be it. A solid 20-40 hours of fun. Basically this is a tower defense game in which you also get to aid the towers success with your main character who can be one of three classes - ranger/warrior/mage. There are a lot of towers to unlock and upgrade and each character class comes with a lot of unique skills as well. That being said, if you are looking for a game with a great plot behind it, that's not it. This game is 0.5% story and 99.5% action and that's how it was intended in my opinion. All in all I had a lot of fun with this game and I would heartily recommend it to anybody who is looking for a fun tower defense game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoy this game!

At the beginning I was disappointed, because its not like Orcs Must Die (1-3) which are my favourite tower defense games. But just because the gameplay is different, doesn't mean that the game is bad or no fun.

After a short period of getting used to the fact, that traps can only be placed on specific locations and that they have to be upgraded, first with XP and then during or in between the waves, I was hooked.

I really like the gameplay and the mechanics. There are not that many different levels (13, I think), but since there are several difficulties per level and in each the spawn locations for traps and the type of traps available are different, there is lots of fun to be had.

The character also earns XP and gets quite powerful in mid and end game, but the traps still are important, especially if there are different routes and exit points for the spawns.

If you like tower defense / action rpg games, give this one a try. And keep an open mind and don't be annoyed if it feels very diffent than Orcs Must Die, like I was in the beginning. But I am really glad that I gave it another try and I had over 60 hours of fun so far and just have finished all the levels on hardest difficulty with full rating. At least with the melee char, two others to go..! :D\t\t\t\t\t
