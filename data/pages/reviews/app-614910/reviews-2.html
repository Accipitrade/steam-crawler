\r\n\t\t\t\t\t#monstercakes is a match-making puzzle game. You connect 3 matching blocks but instead of popping, they form a single higher rank block. Some blocks with higher rank have some abilities that affect the blocks around.

PROS:
  • There are different cute blocks from monster movies and more.
  • There is an album collection of awesome stickers. (I don't know if I can use those on other stuffs like steam aesthetics? It would be REALLY great if i can use the creepy alphabet on my steam profile!)
  • I got 55 achievements just by opening the game!
  • 10 minutes in the game and I already completed all the achievements!

CONS:
  • It gets boring after you achieve a very high score and no one else to do.

Overall, I really recommend this game for achievement hunters as it gives 118 achievements without a sweat!
I bought this game for $ 0.35 only so it is great for what its worth plus you get 3 Trading Cards for your steam collection!
