\r\n\t\t\t\t\t#MonsterCakes is a fun little puzzle game.
You have to match 3 or more adjoining tiles to clear them, which then leaves behind 1 extra tile of the next highest rank.

You can rotate the new tiles (those to be added to the board), by clicking on them, so that they can then be placed on the squares in a different alignment.

The achievements for the game are easily obtained. However, unlike some cheap games, this one is worth playing even after the achievements have been completed to 100%.

If you like simple board puzzle games, then #MonsterCakes is worth trying out.\t\t\t\t\t

It is cheap and cheerful and allows a vital step on the community badge - voting on a workshop item. Yeah sure you could do that with csgo but come on, have some class.

Seriously though, the workshop is great, lets you use pictures you have made. The game itself is that old format (1024?) - sort of match 3 thing but a lot more clever. You do not use numbers here but levels of monster(cakes) that level into each other.

Good gameplay, good use of colours, nice rewards system built in (you get stuff like stickers of letters, flags, all sorts of things) and honestly the sort of game that you should not even wait for a sale, its so cheap to begin with and quite fun.

My playtime is only this low on this because it is my third account.\t\t\t\t\t
