\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR: This is a simple mini-game, not bad, but not 90% good either.

This review it's to counter balance a little bit the current state of the reviews (Very Positive). I don't think this game deserves such good review rates (90% - at the moment of writing). So, this high classification may be disleading.

Let me explain, this is a very simple mini-game, and that's about it. Still, it has some problems and it's a minimum effort work. It could easily have some more game modes like for e.g. different sized grids. The interaction with the pieces could get some improvement too and a mini tutorial would be appreciated (I spent some minutes trying to figure out what I was supposed to do, this is...even placing a piece in the board wasn't obvious).

Besides all this, the reviews might also be this high, because of the existence of workshop and daily pack drops. The pack has one or more items; steam items that can be sold on the market to make a little profit.

Other relevant points:
- Achievements that aren't really achievements, even the worst puzzle player will finish all current achievements in less than 10 minutes;
+ Regular updates to add new items;
+ Fair price for what it is; but still, if you get it, get it on sales.

All-in-all I give it a 5/10. If the developers issue the things I mentioned above and if the game drops below 80% I might change my review :) Have fun
