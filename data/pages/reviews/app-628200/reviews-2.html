\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShort 3 hour game but worth every penny. As a financial nerd, this game was one of the funniest I've played in a long time. Would recommend to pretty much anyone looking for a funny short experience with commentary on the problems of capitalism. Only issue I have is I wish there was a hard mode. I know the game is mostly story-driven but making money is so ridiculously easy that I wish there was a way to play through the story with a harder difficulty. For example, make info and trades go terribly wrong sometimes. It happens irl sometimes even with insider info and I think that could add a little more excitement. Overall though great gameplay would definitely recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
