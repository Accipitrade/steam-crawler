I like game but this is a few reasons why i would not recommend for any one that like trains.

This game is a weird mix of simulation and arcade. I personaly think other than lvl up systems for a citys they take out the worst things from sim and arcade and mixed in to thise game. It looks like they were on path to do sim game but they failed. And anwser from dev. For all problems or things thay failed this is arcade game not hardcore simulator. Well the name says a railway simulator.
Why ecen bothered with realistic aspects and trains bring in some jet powered train and fun stuff then.

GPS:
It is confusing as f... it would help a lot if you can see tracks not just one or two fat lines.
And fore some reason all train games developers have a feeling train operator should switch millions switches per minute.
Railroad systems have lots of employs doing this and it will make sance in free drive mode, but if you make GPS route that should be made automatic and will even fix stupid speed limits confusing

COASTING:
Exist only in tutorial and even there is bad. It is throttle up or train will brake (even going down the hill), what make no sance in case with 10 wagons.

SPEED LIMITS:
Just like GPS it is confusing as f... specially when you need to switch tracks like crazy. On all map max speed limit is 120km/h. Lot of times next speed limit will show you 120km/h and you will hit 50km/h or other way around.

TUTORIAL:
Is nicely done but broken and i need to skip 3 steps of it.

WHEELS SLIP:
It is a joke and have no effect in the driving itself, funny when you drive 100 km/h put throttle from 0% to 20% and wheel slip light came on.

FINES:
You will get fines for not honking at railway crossing or at dear this is just stupid. Same as roads crossing tracks if there are barriers it is pointles to honk.

SWITCHES:
To be fair it is redicules that there is no straight option only left and right and you will get to wrong tracks way more than you would like.

SIGNALING:
It is a complete joke. If train is aproaching on the other line most of time you get red light on both tracks and warnings (if you get one) so it is no way you can tell on whitch track is ai train. And often you will get red lights meter in fron of you.

TRAFFIC:
What traffic? Wholle map is a wasteland with from time to time pop up ai train that you will need to stop and even rewerse, and some suicidal dears laying on tracks. I don't mind playing wastland map but thise pop ups don't make game more fun or more alive just frustraiting.

TRAINS:
Well they are some and looks nice. But it is pointles buying or much upgrading. All trains can carry max of 12 cargo wagons. And refueling is not a probllem there is only 1 diesel loco so you can move from thise point fast. And remmember 120km/h speed limit. First train can do 100km/h easy.

MAP:
It is not that big as you gett in ads (streams, youtube, developers,...) it's compressed alot. It is simmilar to ETS. Or maybe ecen smaller. You get impression it is big by low speed limits and not really open high speed tracks.

COMPANY MECHANICS:
Sorry but i didn't even touch those, tgis is boring staff for me.

BUGS:
Game still have wattermark beta 0.5. Did they send us wrong version or it's just a bait for people that don't want to be testers. The developers need to fix so manny bugs it's probbably years away update with some big changes.

SOUNDS:
I have heard a worse but far from good, imagine riding a train and not hearing gaps betwen tracks, you know that tup tup sound?

GAME GETS BORING FAST:
At first it's fun leveling up citys but after few hours getts really boring. Multiplayer or co-op will maybe work but with this aignaling problems it is just impossible.

GOOD THINGS:
Not realy much as i sed before idea of lvl up citys os greate and implementing something simmilar as ETS is awesome but there is pretty much end. Maybe it is easy to load and unload.

I know this is not a TS or TSW but some things just need to be followed and for some reason every train developer forget that trains do not have rubber tires.
Developers says that they want game that is not just for hardcore sim players, well that point they miss by a mile. But if they thing game is for full arcade players want to think wtf is going on while trying to find the way true massive rail yard they are spot on.

For now i can not recommend this game to anyone.
If you like trains and want train game just go play TSW belive me it is more easy game that this on and if you like company idea just go play ETS.

I really hope this game will be done great, i wait for release and now i just feel i did trow away 16€
And like the way this is going i have a strong felling that game will be abanded with some DLC-s trying to milk as much is possible with as little of work. Well we will see some fixes and some miner or bigger updates but the physics will probably never be fixed.

And for all that telling it's a steal with thise price it's not look TSW single route DLC is prox same price and single route is big as tgise game but it is done correctly with greate physics, sounds, details and feel alive.
And dont strart with bs like but it is a hardcore simulator, it is but with greate tutorials and huds it is way more easy to drive than people says.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnd another early access game that releases unfinished and full of bugs.
Bugs that were there from day one and never addressed.

Avoid at all cost.

But don't worry, the devs have an excuse, it's not a "hardcore simulation", it's just more aracady.
Nope, it's not, it's not even worth the word arcade, there is absolutely 0 physics.
There is a wheel slip system, but it can be easily abused by simply lowering your speed and going up again in quick succession, going from 0 to 50kph in less than 10 seconds even with a fully loaded train.

Sorry, I said fully loaded...

You are limited to 12 wagons. Apparently because of real-life restrictions and station size. Yeah, too bad the game is set in Europe and most stations are standardized and can have way more than 12 wagons.

Don't expect anything in terms of coupling decoupling. You just have to push a button, wait for the fade to black, and tadaa.
Same with the derailment, you'll just get a text warning, and suddenly, fade to black.

And finally, the biggest issue, the AI trains, they never are on a set side of the tracks, they appear way too close to you and are mostly unavoidable. That has been an issue since day 1 of EA, they even had to remove them for months, but when they came back, the issue was still there.

Well, I said finally, but there are plenty of other issues. As you can see on the live stream demo, there are two indicators about the occupied track segment. Ingame, they do not display the same information, and while most importants informations are displayed in the top right corner, it's actually the one in the bottom left corner near the minimap that you need to pay attention to.

The game also advertised some iconic lines.

Those cannot be played in the open world, they are just scenarii that need to be played independently. So... it's the same as any other train sim game, minus the simulation part. Some trains are also locked behind those scenarri.\t\t\t\t\t

The things I would like to mention:
- I'd probably enjoy some fictional country more than this Europe map because of lack of environment variety and lack of overall feeling that, for example, we left Germany and in Poland now.

- Safest way to start the career is to do passenger deliveries but you're forced into cargo train.
- I kinda want an indication when you get closer to the station you're not authorized to be at; when you get close to the place where you should honk the horn at (there was no mention of it in the tutorial at all, so i needed to go and check which button is horn); when there is a bad weather so you need to use high beam (not sure if it was mentioned in tutorial). All of those are poorly indicated and there is a fine for each of the situations.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really like the simplified concept of running your own train company, but it completely falls apart by its shoddy implementation of the main part of the game, namely the trains. There is no physics simulation whatsoever. You pick a speed, and stay at that speed no matter the weight or track incline. You get constant wheel slip because it doesn't correspond to weight, or track condition, but by a fixed speed to throttle percentage ration which can only be modified by buying a bogie upgrade. You can't even conserve fuel by coasting since there's no weight or inertia. The second you pull back on the throttle you start to brake for whatever reason.

The condition of your train is also a non-issue since it's only represented by health bar that depletes when you hit the emergency brake, which I find to horrendously silly. And last but not least, the only way to pick up cargo, and passengers is to drive into a glowing box that spawns you in the positions the game wants you to be and you're good to go.

I don't understand why anyone would put the least amount of effort into the most important part of their game. The only redeeming quality are the artists which created a beautiful environment, and very believable lighting.\t\t\t\t\t
