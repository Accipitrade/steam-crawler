\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLike the game concept which is similiar to Euro truck simulator except that the train driving is not exactly feels simulated but more arcadey. You have throttle and brake increment at 10% increase and there is not much feeling in term of accel or braking when lugging 1 wagon or 12 wagon. Both feel the same.

City level grow after you delivered whatever it need. And you can hire train driver to work for you but they can only work a route that you have previously run or from stations that you have unlock or visited. And seriously you make more money with solo drive as your employee will take their sweet time to go to start point, deliver, then rest while you can work like a robot without any rest.

The game have 2 train type, diesel and electric but I don't see any initiative to drive a diesel train as it need refuel. Other than the diesel train being cheaper, I hope that in term of operating cost, running diesel will be cheaper but not sure on this. Maybe someone can do a comparison?

You got deer on track, other incoming train event and broken train on track event which add some flavor while driving the train. There are still some bugs and in some part of the rail, you have a bad signal, eg a section of 120 suddenly cut off to a section of 50km/h and you unable to react, and got derail.

The derail or accident part cut scenes need to be added or improved as current one is just a fade to back screen with option to select last save or pay penalty to continue. I would love to see a cut scene where the both train exploded in a spectacular fire and splattered remains.

This game still need more work but due to the crashing bug that I found, I can't give it a thumb up yet as this bug should be easily found and fix in a playtest (glaring bug).\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot a Sim to be sure .. but if your looking to have fun with a polished train game you could do way way worse than Train Life .. Looks great even when put up against the big boys (Unreal Engine). Fun to play .. no bugs that I have hit.. and overall truly enjoy..

Would I love if you can walk around or get out of the train .. Yep .. does it matter ... Nope. I would like to see a weather map .. or maybe be able to select what weather events we want. Map needs a bit of polish but the developer seems to keep updating. The only other thing I really wish was that you could switch and see from a CCTV type view your employees and watch what they are doing.. or even just see them on the map as a little train going off on the rout you assigned.

I think for someone like me that does not want to worry about 300 things while relaxing with a game this is a good mid point between arcade and sim. Played a lot during Xmas in the snow while listening to Xmas music .. it got me through a few bad days for sure.\t\t\t\t\t
