\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSince Steam still doesn't offer an inbetween voting, I tend to go for 'not recommended'.

This game was awesome during a time when the playerbase wasn't filled with toxicity. Now however it suffers from exactly that. Unfortunately. Usually I would write a long review and describe the game indepth, but it's enough to say this: Purchase and play it if you have a good friend to play with. Or even an entire clan/group of people you can play with. Believe me, it can be fun with those around. Avoid it if you don't unless you want to come across the problem of players constantly using profanity, absolutely disgusting topics and try to swear at you at any time given.

Believe me, the game has potential, it is great, it's a beautiful concept and a nice rework of the flash versions. If only the community would be kept the way it used to be...

Small Checklist:
Is the game easy to understand? Most definitely, mastering it is a different topic however.
Does it run smooth? Runs even on a toaster.
Is it fun to play? Yes, but as I said only with mentioned and trusted partner.
Is it worth the price? It's very cheap and compared to the flash version much less of a P2W game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHi, this is my first review... my speciality is not critiqiung so excuse me if this is bad...
This is my review on ShellShockLive
SSL (ShellShockLive) is a 2D game in which u play as your tank and must destroy the other tanks. (Unless it is a different mode)
You have over 200 weapons to choose from! You can get weapons from competing campaign missions, doing challenges, or leveling up. Each weapon in the game can be upgraded through earning exp.
PROS:
Not Boring: if you get into the late levels (70s, 80s 90s) it takes a while to level up, but when u finally do... you will usually earn a great weapon
Customizable: You can unlock different tank gunners,chassises and tracks. While this doesn't affect ur gameplay, your tank can look pretty cool. Each time you level up, you recieve an upgrade point, which then you can use it to upgrade your: attack, increasing the chanse for a crit, armor, adding armour to your tank, tracks, making it easier to climb hills, and luck, increasing ur chance of getting better weapons
CONS: Sometimes Slow Gameplay: As I said before, it takes a while to level up in the late game, so you may get frusterated.
So there it is folks... my first review! Hopefully some of you bigger reviewers can review this! Thanks everybody\t\t\t\t\t
