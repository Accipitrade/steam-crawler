\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHOLY CRAP!!!!!!

I must confess when I saw the ad for the game on the updates window, I thought, "Great somebody has resurrected the genre again, lets see how crappy a job these guys did, ugh." I saw the price and figured, "ahh, what the heck". Holy crap, I was totally blown away.

I hate to date myself, but its almost impossible not to when giving this review. If you ever played the old Shareware Scorched Earth or the less exciting Apple IIgs game, Cannon Fodder, and loved either one, you cannot pass this up. It takes the basic concept of those games, adds some(200+) sick, twisted and at times quite silly weapons and allows you to dominate the battfield.

I have already sunk in a solid 4 hours into this game with absolutely no regrets. I felt like I was 12 again. The gameplay, like the originals, is repeptive. Its supposed to be. With leveling being a priority to unlock weapons, tank stats and skins, it makes the grind well worth it.

I really do not have any cons about this game. Well maybe just one: a better matchmaking system. When trying to quick join a multiplayer game, it will kick you back gametype select if the server it was going to connect you to, becomes full before it can finish connecting you. This is really the only issue I have about the entire game and a minor one at that.

If you are one of the very few people who are dissapointed with your purchase, it's because you simply don't get.
