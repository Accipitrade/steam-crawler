\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor starters, it's a tank game, like the kinds of tank games you might have found in the era of Flash.

But we've moved on to a new age. As such, the dev has crafted an arsenal including a slew of new weapons (at least to me); from snakes, cats, and fish to bouncy balls, black holes, and even the wrath of God. Their usefullness will depend on your skill--and a little bit of luck beyond that.

Friends on call make this game a joy to play, although you can still test your mettle against strangers across the Internet. From traditional free-for-all or team deathmatch, playing soccer (or football for everyone else), scoring points, or even testing your aim, there's a variety of ways to play with others.

If that's not your style, there's a bunch of missions you can play alone to get a feel for the game, and help build your knowledge. It can be a nice break between the pseudo-chaos of multiplayer, or as a way to bring you up to speed to friend and foe alike.

Don't expect everything to go your way; even the most seasoned veteran can be thrashed in an instant, the best laid plans upset in a single turn. But soldier on, and victory will be yours...probably.\t\t\t\t\t
