\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like this game. The different weapons you use are varied and cool to use. The way you shoot, you have to essentially predict the trajectory and that is cool in itself. If you don't shoot right, it backfires on you by bouncing in such a way that it bounces back to you or you just miss entirely which is pretty funny either way. The graphics are simple and good.

The different game modes on the multiplayer side are interesting and fun to play. The campaign missions are fun yet challenging in some aspects and they count towards your experience level. You can level up by completing the campaign missions and finishing a round in multiplayer whether you win or lose. You can unlock more weapons by leveling up and unlock more powerful versions of those weapons by actually using them. The leveling system is good.

I recommend this game to anyone of any age.\t\t\t\t\t
