In terms of story, ShellShock Live is minimal, with players competing in matches to destroy the opposing team's tanks. In terms of overall rating, ShellShock Live gets high marks for its intuitive and enjoyable gameplay, well-tuned physics engine, and excellent graphics and sound design.
