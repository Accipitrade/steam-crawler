\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst off, there are a lot of ups and downs for this game. Depending on why you want the game makes all the difference.

I purchased a 4-pack because I intended to play with my friends and my friends only. We had tons of fun! We skipped the tutorial and still learned how to play very quickly. There are over 200 weapons and learning to use them is half the fun.

As you level up, you earn more weapons and can spend more skill points to upgrade your tank. I understand if you play against other people and they happen to be significantly higher level than you, then you are at a decent disadvantage which can be frustrating. This can also be a problem is you've been playing for awhile and have tons of items and your friends just get it and are low level, lacking certain items.

There is a single player mode, but I believe the games intent was to play with others and if that's what you're looking to do (especially with friends) I would definitely recommend it. 8/10

If you would like to see some gameplay, you can check my video below of my first experience with it. I had lots of laughs as my friends and I figure out what the hell we were doing.
