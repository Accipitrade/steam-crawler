\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA modern re-imagining (in my eyes) of a classic couch multiplayer Tank Wars (1986).

Best played with friends but it's never a problem to find a lobby and have fun with some rando's and blow each other up. Several different game modes spice things up a bit with classics like Deathmath/Team Deathmatch and others such as Marksman (consecutively hitting targets), Points (most points wins), and more. Other game modifiers add obstacles and wind to bugger shots and offer a nice randomizer to the mix, negating people who sit still and make the same shots over and over.

Generally the community is pretty engaging, though you get some players who like to tell you how to play and will voice their frustrations loudly. Games are meant to have fun, and this one definitely pulls it off regardless if you constantly whiff shots like I do.\t\t\t\t\t
