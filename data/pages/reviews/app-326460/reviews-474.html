\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a decently fun little game that's great for killing time or providing something else to do while watching some dumb YouTube videos or a bad movie. I never would've played this much of it had I not had something else in the background while I was playing.

While it is fun, there is an annoying yet unavoidable meta of shooting at 100 power and only worrying about the angle. It gets mitigated by the High Wind modifier or Rebound gamemode but is present in every game where the objective is kills. People also use rulers but that's much rarer than most think IMO. Lag between players also allows you to seemingly move after the timer expires, which can be very frustrating unless you learn to anticipate it (or use it to your advantage).

For $7 you can't really complain, though. Buy it on sale, goof around for a couple of hours, and never touch it again. Or play 250 hours and wonder what else you could've done with your time besides playing this game.\t\t\t\t\t
