\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEasy to learn, takes time to master, but nevertheless a fun game if you want something less competitive. Higher level lobbies will look daunting at first, but as you get more into it, you'll notice that nobody really cares about your level (not in my experience, at least). There are also singleplayer missions to get you warmed up and to collect some XP, and the devs are constantly adding new content into the game (and if you like it and speak multiple languages, you can help with the localisation). Also has a straightforward interface and most players are nice in-game (though also quiet). Overall, a fun game if you enjoy destroying tanks with every weapon imaginable.\t\t\t\t\t
