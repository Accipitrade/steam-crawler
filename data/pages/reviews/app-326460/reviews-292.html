\r\n\t\t\t\t\tScorched Earth? Gunbound? Pocket Tanks? If you love these games and want to see them evolved and epically upgraded, then this is the game for you. With fun addition's like a leveling system, skill point system ( that you can reset weekly ), rating system, global rankings, and a shop where you can purchase other fun things! "Oh no, I knew it was coming a Shop..MTX inbound" but everything is earned through game play even the in game currency(at this point). In the shop, you can buy different cosmetic upgrades for your tank, maps, weapons, and victory music. What about weapons? How about 400+ of them from a digger bomb that will bury your opponents deep within the terrain to a flurry of fried chicken that explodes on contact! If you can think of a type of weapon you will probably find a variation of it here. The weapons are all unlocked randomly as you level up your Tank. You will have a blast playing with your friends in multiplayer, with a plethora of modes that I'm sure you will enjoy. My biggest con right now is if playing by yourself it can be trouble at time to find a good lobby.. not terrible but it does take some time. Hopefully as the game gets more popular it will be easier to fill a lobby and find some good people to play against. I definitely recommend this game, the price point is beyond worth it!
