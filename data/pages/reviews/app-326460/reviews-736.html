\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShellShock Live is a fun game. While many people might brush it of as just another 2d Worms-style shooter, this certainly is not the case. A huge variety of weapons and upgrades make sure there is always something new to discover - I have clocked about 90 hours and there are still weapons I haven't used or experienced.

The ability to play with up to 8 people in several different gamemodes assures you and your friends never ending enjoyment. There are loads of different maps and weapons - there are more than 200 different weapons and each one can be upgraded, most multiple times. You can also change the outfit of your tank for some personality or invest skillpoints which you gain by leveling into four different categories - chassis (armor), tracks (movement), luck (better chance of getting a better weapon or crate) and your gun (critchance/critdmg).

The only negative point I can think of is the lack of moderation - a lot of players will randomly insult each other simply because there is no moderation available yet.

But in conclusion - for the current price, this game is a steal. Especially so if you're playing with your friends.

See you on the battlefield!\t\t\t\t\t
