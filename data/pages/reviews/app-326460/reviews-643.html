\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI grew up playing flash games and TANKS! was one of the flash games that I've played. I'm really happy to see that they have brought it back and made it more modern. With tons of new weapons, maps, modes, and challenges to complete, this game certainly does keep me entertained when I play it.

However...

* I would like to point out that this game may be really grindy for you. To level up a weapon, you are forced to go to multiplayer when you have gotten as far as you can on singleplayer. Really don't like the fact that they had to force you to go to multiplayer when you can't complete anymore singleplayer campaigns.
* Another thing about the single player campaigns is that they will be irritating. You will come across some campaigns that might seem impossible. They are impossible, UNLESS you head over to the multiplayer and level up more to get better gear and loadouts to complete the campaigns.

This game is great. I'll say that multiple times because it looks and feels great to play. I just hope that the problems above could be addressed to some people before purchasing the game. All cons aside, I definitly would have paid this game myself because I do support the company that is developing this game.\t\t\t\t\t
