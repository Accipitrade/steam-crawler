\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've enjoyed the Worms series in the past, so when I saw this one being played on YouTube, it held my interest. Since I've played it for the last few weeks, I haven't been disappointed. The graphics are simple but bright and eyecatching, the physics are well-done, the weapons are fun and funny, and the upgrade system is interesting and encourages you to use as many types as possible rather than just stick with a favorite few. Tanks are also upgradable with armor, movement, damage, and luck, and can make for some interesting builds.

Along with all that, there's extras like shields, teleports, and jet packs, an in-game currency won during matches to buy goodies like bonus weapons, battlefield customizers, and victory music. And if you don't feel like fighting against real people, there a challenging (and occasionally frustrating) single mission mode to help introduce you to the game, various weapons, and concepts like bumpers and portals. It's also hard to believe this game is early access; it shows much more polish than some more expensive and bigger name games I've played in the last few years.

My one complaint with the game is one that usually befalls multiplayer games; there are the odd occasions where you have rude, stupid, or rage-quit players. This is pretty much expected, though, and to be honest I've encountered far more nice players than trolls.

Overall, its a fun game with a surprising amount of complexity for a simple concept. Definitely recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
