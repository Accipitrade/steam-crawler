\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's funny when you spend 6,99€ for a good game like this, and you play more than when you pay 59,99€ for some overpriced garbage, isn't it?

Here you have endless fun tanks to (lol) different game modes. And if you like unlockable things, there is a passive skill tree and hundreds of weapons; everything is upgradable by earning exp.
In a single day, by completing the Single Player campaign, i easily leveled up to 40; in addition, there are in-game achievements to earn more exp and extra weapons. Those 40 levels are so easy that's like if the real levels were 60. After 47 hrs played, i reached level 64; the exp curve is not so fast but you can still earn 1-2 levels per day by playing moderately, so you get close to level 100 within a reasonable time. After 93 hrs played, i'm level 75; at this point i'm out of achievements (some of them are pointless), and yes, you have to farm to level up. However, be aware that's playable even if you are not level 100, or even better by using the match settings in order to block low or high level players.

About game modes (all modes support 2-8 players, except Tournament):
- "Juggernaut" mode picks a player and sets him as Boss, so it's All-Players VS 1.
- "Marksman" mode every player turn spawns a dartboard at random position; don't miss it, or lose.
- "Points" mode is without death. You play for a limited number of turns, and you have to rack up more damage than your enemy.
- "Rebound" mode spawns bumper surfaces at random position; all your skillshots must bounce on them first.
- "Assassin" mode automatically marks a target: you have to destroy it.
- "Deathmatch" mode... you know this classic; 1 VS 1, All VS All, or Team VS Team.
- "Tournament" mode allows you to create a tournament for 4-16 players, but it's basically 1 VS 1, where the winner challenges the other winner.

In the past i played other games of the same genre, and i have to say that's pretty good, except the maps (every map looks like the others - there are no high floors, just a ground floor for all players), and the graphics... it's way too simple, with horrible phosphorescent colors, no terrain details, and even worse background, but i have to say that quality is appreciable as it's well polished in its simplicity.
