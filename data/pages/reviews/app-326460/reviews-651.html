\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you had a PC back in "the good old days," odds are high that you fondly recall the time you spent playing the ultimate computer game of that era: Scorched Earth.

For those who may not be familiar with it, Scorched Earth was (and remains) one of the best 2D tank-battling games ever conceived - think "Worms," but with less focus on mobility and more focus on being the first one to deploy a mighty weapon called the Death's Head.

All these years later, ShellShock Live manages to capture the spirit of classics like Scorched Earth while remaining unique enough in its own right to justify its existence.

The graphics are nice, the music and sound effects are high quality, and the game has an unusually high level of polish for an early access title. At the game's current asking price, it's a must-buy for anyone looking to recapture their Scorched Earth glory days.\t\t\t\t\t
