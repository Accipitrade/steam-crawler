\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSingle player review: There are dozens of levels, starting off quite simple (Shoot 1 enemy with a basic weapon twice to kill him or something similar), moving on to multiple enemies and a small choice of weapons. Near the end the levels get incredibly complex due to the enemy now being able to move like a real player, and you have to kill certain enemies with certain weapons in a specific way so you don't blow your aresenal too early and run out of things to shoot. All in all, great way to gauge skill levels before jumping into...
Multiplayer review: Multiple game modes (Standard DM and Team DM of course, but also Soccer goal scoring, Assassin, etc), each with their own little twist. Unless you play with a regular group all the time, you never really know who you will get as a teammate or as an enemy, and everyone has widely ranging skill levels. I've seen level 100s that miss half their shots at medium range, while level 20s land everything but have a hard time killing simply because they have weak weapons. The unlock by leveling system means higher levels will have things like nukes and level 3 or 4 items of which you have the first (or maybe even second level if you've used it enough). The more you use a particular weapon (or any in its leveling chain) the more xp both it and you gain so you can reach new levels of said weapon, while unlocking the next level. VERY VERY grindy, but if you get the right people, it starts to be really fun and in no time you've reached level 80 and get to fire your first nuke...if you've spent enough skill tree points in luck so you start off with it in the next game instead of getting things like the basic shot or rapid fire. That happens a lot. even a level 100 can draw the short straw and have weapons a level 5 would be using, or he could potentially have the most powerful weapons in the game and kill half the enemy team on the first turn.
My take: Enjoyable game for the price, easy to get dozens of hours play time out of it even if it's not really your thing. You might make some new friends out of it too. Get the game.\t\t\t\t\t
