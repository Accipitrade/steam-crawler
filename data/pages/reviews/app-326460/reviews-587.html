\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShellShock Live is a fun multiplayer experience. My friends convinced me to try it out with them, and I have to say I'm glad I did. While it can get quite boring sometimes, when I'm having fun, I'm REALLY having fun.

With the countless weapons and upgrades for your tank that are available, you just want to keep playing and leveling up to unlock more stuff. The weapons are all pretty unique, and powerful in their own right, you just have to worry about using them correctly and knowing their pattern of movement. If that wasn't enough, there's plenty of maps to choose from as well, and each of them make you plan out accordingly and differently to make sure you hit your opponent. Not to mention, the aiming makes it feel like it's your fault for missing most of the time.

The only real flaw I can think of when playing the game is how limited your movement is, as you can get hung up on small ledges, which makes being stuck in a hole a lot worse than it already is.

So, if you like playing games with your friends, this is a game to look into. It can be a blast with the right group.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShellshock live is a fantastic value. A simple game that has a lot of content to unlock in terms of new weapons, upgraded weapons, new maps, improvements to your tank such as better tracks, more armor and more critical hits. I have played the game for more than 200 hours and I am still playing. The only negative I can say about the game is that it can become fustrating at higher levels. Even though the game is fairly simple (just move your tank, aim and shoot) some players don't realize what they need to do to help their team at higher levels. At the begining of the game the area effect weapons are not very powerful. It doesn't matter if your teammates are close together. But at higher levels, as the area effect weapons become more powerful, it can really hurt your team if they bunch up. It can be very fustrating if another teammate moves close to your tank because the enemy will target you with their large blast radius weapons, knowing that they can hit two tanks with one weapon.

Overall I highly recommend this game, it is a great value for the price and alot of fun. Just be prepared for some fustration at higher levels if you don't have good teammates on your side.\t\t\t\t\t
