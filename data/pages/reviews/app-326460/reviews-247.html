\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNow I've been playing this game since 2017 and after having wasted 1,3k+ hours of my life with ShellShock Live, I figured it would be appropriate to finally write a review on this game and give my honest opinion on it. Now, I don't want to immediately give a feeling that the game is complete garbage right off the bat, so I'll begin with the positive aspects of the game, before i might eventually give you that feel.

=PROS=

-Lots of content: This game has a good chunk of content, ranging from an enormous amount of upgradeable weapons, which all have their own unique effects and twists to them, to a variety of challenges, a handfull of interesting gamemodes, a big selection of maps and a singleplayer mode. Unlocking every weapon alone will most likely take more than 500 hours of playtime, so if you're looking for a game to effectively kill time, this one will do the job i guess.
-very addicting: The gameplay is very simple: you have to aim and shoot. Now, this might sound very easy, but it's harder to pull off than you might think. Learning to master the skill of aiming will take time, but it's gonna be worth it in the end. For me, the most enjoyable time of the game was the time when I wasn't very good at the game and still had many things to discover and unlock, which was from Level 1-80, but you might still find it enjoyable after that point aswell, especially if you enjoy grinding your butt off for that sweet next Level.
-Low Price: For a game that costs 7 bucks, it's surprisingly good and yields a ton of playtime. There's not much more to say about this, but it's definitely worth buying if you have some spare cash and no idea what to spend it on.

Now all of this might sound completely awesome, but there's a reason this review is marked as "not recommended".

=CONS=

-Toxic Community: Oh boy. I've been around in the community for a while now and I've sure seen a lot of messed up people. So many times I've witnessed people throwing insults out at one another for no reason, and then kicking them out of the lobby afterwards, even though they may have done absolutely nothing wrong. But this isn't the only thing that makes me despise this community, there's more to it. The game is also pretty much overrun by Germans who are so immature that it blows my mind. So many times I've seen someone join a lobby and instantly ask: "aRe YOu GeRmaN??" and also writing in german in the middle of a match with people who don't speak the language, while also expecting you to understand them. But it doesn't end there. The amount of Level 100 players with no signs of even a grain of common sense is astonishing, spreading lies about one another to damage someone's reputation, making an enormous fuss about absolutely everything, etc. and 99% of the Level 100 community being incredibly conceited in general.
-Repetitiveness: Now, I listed the simple mechanics of the game as something positive, because its only moving, aiming and shooting. But in the long run, the lack of other mechanics makes this game get incredibly boring after a while. There's nothing more to it and it gets repetitive and insanely painful to make progress, because gaining levels after Level 80 will take an extreme amount of patience due to the massive grind for experience.
-Delayed Updates: When I started playing, there were a couple updates coming out every few weeks, if I recall correctly, but since around Mid-2018 a small update tends to take multiple months up to a year to be released. Sure, you might now say there's not much to add, since the game already has a lot of content, but I'd think the dev would add more content if he wanted, especially since he opened up suggestions for the game some time ago.
-bad "endgame": Ok so if you decide to play the game and pull through the painful experience that is the levels 90+ to reach that sweet Level 100, you'll realize that the gameplay starts to become very different in a bad way. For example, as a Max Level player you will find yourself most likely instantly kicked out of the majority of lobbies, except if you play with friends or people from your clan. You'll also have a lot of low level players begging you to let them win against you so they can get the weapon "God Rays", which you can unlock by killing a level 100 player. I've always found the lower levelled players to be the nicest, but guess what, you're not welcome in their lobbies anymore except if you drop that sweet sweet god rays for them. Also you'll soon see you still have to gain a total of more than 2 million XP to unlock everything, cuz, y'know, 100 levels just isn't enough.
-Only fun with friends: I think this point is self explanatory, but to actually enjoy the game in its full potential, you'll have to play with friends, because playing with some possibly toxic strangers just isn't very fun.

=CONCLUSION=

All in all, the game is, well, pretty okay, but it's mostly the community that makes this game unbearable, especially in the late game. Also please never grind 4.08x xp, it's a huge waste of time. Thanks for reading my review, have a great day/night!\t\t\t\t\t
