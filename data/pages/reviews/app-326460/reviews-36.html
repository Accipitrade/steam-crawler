\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThese are 671.7 hours that I won't ever see again

Jokes aside, I highly discourage you from playing this game unless you're just like me. A child who had seen his favorite YouTubers post videos frequently and really wished for the chance to play with them (never happened). Regardless, I used to frequently play this game daily and pretty much got to the point I am comfortable writing this review.

Shellshock Live is a game in which your success is highly determined by the numbers of your display. If your friends or randos you meet drastically overlevel you, you practically have zero chances of overpowering them unless one or more of the following happen(s):
1. The high level is in the middle of a lobotomy and cannot aim for you
2. You have astronomical levels of luck
3. The high level has gone easy on you
4. The high level just had to leave the game
If that doesn't happen, get ready to be whooped.

As a high level player, low level players will kick you out their lobby for daring to join. As a low level player, high level players will crush you while openly mocking your shortness of good weapons and experience (lord forbid you don't play this game as often or as hardcore as they do). Of course, a decent chunk of the playerbase is cool. The other chunk, however, makes people like you and me reconsider the First Amendment.

The game itself is fine, but it lacks heavily in some aspects:
1. The games Skill Rating system is entirely style, no substance. You have a number that goes higher when you win and lower when you lose and that is it. There's no considerations for any skill shots or displays of high damage, no consideration of the gamemode and no consideration of whether you've used up any items or not.
2. The entirety of the Shoccer gamemode is extremely poor and makes me question whether it wasn't just added because the devs just enjoy the IRL sport. One good example of this modes shortcoming is that shots programmed to go for the tank will do just that, contradicting the gamemode's goal of hitting that stupid ball that behaves as appropriately as the current Twitter CEO
3. Shoccer sucks, seriously. I've not met a single player who wasn't playing it for the achievement
4. The chatbox on the main menu is littered with absolute garbage, most of the time.

That said, this game has some redeeming qualities:
1. Many of the weapons are fun to use and look good
2. Your Rebound shots can be a display of the fact you can do more than the players who stick to 100 86 in Deathmatches as if their life was actually on the line
3. The music is really pleasant to listen to
4. More stuff that you might appreciate that I simply don't bother to count up
