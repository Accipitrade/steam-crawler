\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think I have played enough to give a review.
Elements in the game - the atmosphere, design and simplicity - are stunning. There are a huge variety of weapons to choose from, and you can upgrade them. P2W was never a thing in the game. But that's about all the good things.

The community is salty, especially when you hit lv 80 and above. This is when you unlock the Nuke, and everyone starts ganking up on you in fear that you'll nuke them, without whatsoever knowledge that you have other non-nuke weapons. It's frustrating when people say it's a strategic move. It may be strategic, but it's not fun. I try to play fair, I don't focus on one at a time, because I don't want to discourage people by eliminating them first. I play solo, and mostly without a team. Players will also gank up on you, based on your skill rating. Double standards everywhere.

I can't recommend the game mainly because of its mechanics. Shouldn't we get better starting weapons the higher our level are, since we have access to more weapons? Apparently not so, every round 50% of my weapons contains any weapon a level 1 can have. And crits. I have max crit in my build, but the rate I've been getting crits are similar to not spending any points into the crits section. To put it simply, RNG in this game is a nightmare.\t\t\t\t\t
