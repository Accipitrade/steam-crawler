\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is fun... when it works.
The game is unique, it has a great, colorful design that makes it look amazing without destroying your graphics card or processor.

But my big complaint is the synchronization. I find that I am always around 3 to 5 seconds behind everyone else in the game while I'm playing, and for some reason, if I desync with the other players too much than I am unable to resync in the match. While out of sync, I cannot shoot, move, chat with others, or even leave the match to return to the menu. What I have to do is Alt+F4 to close the game, reload and try again. But the frustrating part is that you receive 0 exp and 0 gears until the match is fully completed (and you receive nothing if you leave the match), which make it very annoying when it goes out of sync close to the end of a hour long game of rebound where you get the most exp. Playing for close to an hour in one match, racking up loads of exp on your favorite weapons and earning tons of gears, only to have them ripped away from you because you were forced to close the game. It really is a turn off.

Overall I would recommend the game, I hate to see such a good game ruined by poor server synchronization.

7.5/10 (would be 11/10 "must buy" if there wasn't an issue with synchronization)\t\t\t\t\t
