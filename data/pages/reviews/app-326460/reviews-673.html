\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought it as it reminds me of an old game which I believe was called "Pocket Tanks".
Mechanics are similar to aforementioned game, you have a tank, with random weapons out of the pool of possibilities.
You can move a bit each turn, yes combat takes place in turns. Gameplay focuses on adjusting position angle and power of next shoot while chosing besst bullet for this shoot. The thing is, ammo is limited, and before you resuply 10-20 turns can pass, meaning if you waste all best bullets on first-scaner shoots you will be left with utility or low dmg bullets in crucial moments.

The game is easy to learn due to its simplicity, but it gets more tactical in multiplayer (I play only team deathmatch) when you have to consider portals, pick ups, such as healing with chance of getting a high level weapon and barriers that will reflect your shoot - but can be used to hit enemies behind cover if used correctly.
Friendly fire happens, but it's fun...

My main concern once I bought it was that it is a MULTIPLAYER BASED GAME - keep that in mind before buying as you cannot earn more exp if you finished campain mode, and skirmish is just for practice not earning points (except ones for ingame shoop).
But, the community is great... I never before had a disscussion like"
>(me) let me feed you *shots bannana at player2 and it misses*
> (player 3) you missed now he will starve to death"
even though its not best example that should give you idea.

I mentioned exp - you use it to lvl up. Higher lvl means that you unlock more weapons and tokens to upgrade your tank in one of 4 dirrections - Survivability - Mobility - Luck - Damage.

Survivability gives you more armour and persk giving you chance to regain % of health/armour on begining of next turn
Mobility allows you to travel further for less fuell and travel on more steep hills and allows for perks increasing your fuel capacity,
Luck increases our chances for rare drops from containers and provides perks related to them (but I haven read description for those just yet so dont rely on me here).
Damage increases your chances of dealing critical hits and how much damage those hits deal to enemy.

So far, I really really enjoy the game, in my opinion it's must have if you have a lot spare time and like explosions and tanks.\t\t\t\t\t
