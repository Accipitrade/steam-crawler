"Gorillas from 1991, but now with tanks and lots of content."
The genius simplicity of QBASIC's Gorillas from 1991 paired with tanks and over 200 weapons is a great recipe, also in the year 2017. Choose a weapon, set the angle, set the strength, shoot - that is it, that's the whole game. Shellshock is a great simple strategic turn based game, especially with/against friends.

Pros:
+ great multiplayer
+ great fun, chaff & anger
+ over 200 weapons
+ a lot of features / modes
+ replayability / learning curve
+ many players online
+ no lags / no bugs
+ option for a 4pack
+ whole implementation

Cons:
- unlockable stuff is too expensive
- no gorillas anymore

Verdict:
Still in 2023, simplicity can make a lot of fun!\t\t\t\t\t
