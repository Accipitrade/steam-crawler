\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game itself is quite fun, it's like the new and improved "Scorched Earth" of the 90's. For more modern references think Worms games from Team17 but without the well, worms. Lots of weapons to choose from albeit a lot of them are just upgrades of the others. Game has a nice campaign mode that can take quite a while, and of course. Multiplayer.

This is where the trouble starts. The community as a whole is rather hostile. In my short time playing I was randomly kicked from 40+ games just from entering and saying nothing, was harrassed numerous times, and saw some people exploiting modes of games to benefit themselves but in essence break the core mechanics of the game. One other thing to note, I did notice that physics seem to be slightly off or rather completely nonsensical in some cases depending on the angle your tank sits at. It's rather insignificant but it bugs me a bit.

In general, great game to buy on SALE, not so much full-price. For those not into these kind of games that are turn-based it's probably a pass, for me i'm glad I got it but I really do hope they work on the community more.\t\t\t\t\t
