\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is a fun little thing to play with a group of friends or even some of the strangers playing it. It is fun, to build up your tank and unlock all those weapons, but the longer the game goes on, it will be more challenging for newer players to get into it.
To be fair: Even without the weapons in the beginning it is fun and you can get enough experience from doing the singeplayer missions, but there is a slight problem. You get your experience awarded after how much damage you have done in that round. And with enough people able to lock onto you immediately, that is hard to come by. Yes, you can move your tank out of their line of fire, but in the beginning your movement is limited to very straight parts of the map, but those are hard to come by after once being fired upon.

In conclusion: if you match against high leveled enemies, you will have a big problem defeating them, but atleast you can turn on a maximum level difference for your lobby, so you do not get screwed too bad.

Oh, and the community is freaking toxic. The general chat is full of insults and "kys" and whatever without even a report function or anything. Lobby chat can be disabled, so you are fine there.

In conclusion: Yes, the game is nice enough and does not cost too much, but beware of 10 year olds and a bit of balancing issues. As long as you play with a friend or friends and probably even start together, it is perfect.
(Worms is a bit better though.)\t\t\t\t\t
