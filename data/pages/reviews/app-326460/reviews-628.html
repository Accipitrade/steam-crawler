As for the early access status: There are frequent updates, but the game is more or less stable, I've yet to found an annoying bug.

There are a lot of different game modes that you can choose from, all are about hitting something in some way.

All in all, this game is a great way to kill an hour or two in a boring evening.

WARNING: If you are looking for a competitive game, look elsewhere.\t\t\t\t\t
