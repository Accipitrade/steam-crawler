\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn general demo is the same in terms of fire rate, reload speed, firing delay, etc.
The original debate was about tick triples vs no tick triples (see examples from asdf). The idea was to remove tick triples for the sake of simplicity and to not implement health for demo (since it doesnt exist in any other gamemode) but as it turned out people really like them.
So the best solution to this would be making it so you dont have health while also limiting the amount of stickies out at a time to 3. That way we get triples that are fairly close to what tick triples were while removing the aids/inconsistent element of using tick triples (no death/punishment for messing up a precise strat). In a speedrun environment double pres are still faster than triples in most cases so it doesnt affect those much. The times that a triple is faster could be tick tripled right now in TF2 anyways.
This solution both makes it as similar as possible to tf2 and also simplifies the game for new players a lot (simple rules, 3 stickies = max, 1st fizzles if 4th is shot). Some jumps could be ruined by this "3 sticky" mechanic so thats why we should also introduce triggers that change the amount of max stickies out at the same time (usually decreasing the amount to 2 but could also allow for an increased amount of stickies in future maps). The one side effect of this change however is that triple pres would be killed but the general consensus about triple pres is no one is passionate enough about them to fight to keep them. This also impacts a lot less than you would expect as currently triple pres are very rare on actual demo maps. The maps that do currently have them a triple is just as good if not better (for example over level 8).
Also, in the case of trying to triple catch with pogo and then shoot your 4th sticky before detting this would not be possible. However, this is how tick triples are in TF2 if you're tick triple catching you can't be prefiring a 4th sticky there is no time. This keeps things closer to TF2 by not allowing you to prefire a 4th.
mmod changes:
1. allow full power triples (no death from 3 stickies)
2. limit max stickies out at a time to 3, 1st sticky fizzles if 4th is shot (disappears, no damage)
3. add triggers that can change the max amount of stickies out
4. dont implement health for demo
TL;DR: Max stickies demo can have is 3 with no health mechanic\t\t\t\t\t
