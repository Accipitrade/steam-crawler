\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery addictive. I used to play similar browser games but Shellshock Live has darn near endless upgrades and weapons. And to some extent, you don't feel massively overpowered going against guys 50 levels below you. Its skill based. And contrary to what a lot of people say, I haven't found any toxic lobbies. No one randomly kicks you because your better than them. I'm sure there is the odd bad egg out there, but I haven't yet met them and I'm level 61. BTW you also don't feel useless going up against higher level players. I play against guys 20 levels above me and still have a blast. I highly recommend this game to anyone who likes these types of games.\t\t\t\t\t
