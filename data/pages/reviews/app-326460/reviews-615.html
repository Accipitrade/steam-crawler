\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShellshock Live is that classic 2D tank game you probably know of…but with online Multiplayer and a crazy weapon selection that surpasses even Worms. For the most part, the gameplay is turn-based where you move your tank a small distance each turn, then put an angle and set power into your shot, then hope you calculated it right and it hits the enemy. The huge weapon variety prevents it from being stale, though, as you may have to plan different strategies depending on what weapons the crates give you. There’s not much to describe, but I assure you it’s fun and easy to get into, especially with friends.
There are a few flaws, though. The biggest flaw is the upgrading system. As you play more, you can upgrade your tank’s health, movement, etc. This can create unfair discrepancies between you and friends if one decides to play alone or if someone’s new. You pretty much have to reset tanks at that point. Also, you unlock weapons and the player with the wider weapon pool is usually at an advantage. The game has a few ways to try and balance this, but I think it would’ve been more fun and easier to pick up with anyone if everyone had the same tank stats and all weapons unlocked.
Otherwise, I think this game is definitely worth a shot. If you can get your friends in on it, I think that’s definitely the route to go…as long as you all stay leveled near each other.\t\t\t\t\t
