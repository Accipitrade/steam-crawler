\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you want a game that is just fun to waste time in, this is the perfect game for you. The mechanics are extremely simple, the graphics are cool, and the challenges of multiplayer open up numerous possibilities of ways to destroy your opponents.

My only gripe with the game is that once you complete a certain single player mission, replaying it won't award you the weapon xp you gained in the match. It's only a small issue, as the main focus is multiplayer, but it at least should be noted once people start focusing on getting upgraded weapons.

Other than that, the game is amazing. Once you include the fact that the developers are constantly adding improvements to the game (I'm looking at you Bouncy Ball), this game will have plenty of staying power as a way to have fun alone or with friends.\t\t\t\t\t
