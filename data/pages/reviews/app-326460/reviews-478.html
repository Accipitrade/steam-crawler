\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game looks very simplistic, and yet, it's also quite addicting.

The main mode of the game has you along with others fight in tanks, and whoevers left at the end wins. There's a huuuuuge range of weapons, 411 as of this post, and they can range from a simple bullet, to a nuke (yes, you can shoot nukes at others. It's amazing). These weapons can also be upgraded the more you use them, making them even stronger.

While there's the deathmatch mode I just mentioned, there's also additional modes, such as Juggernaut, where one guy faces everyone else with a ton of health and armor, or Rebound, where you gotta hit your shot off a bumper first for it to deal damage. There's even a single player mode where you can do missions. There's 100 levels in the game, and upon each level up, you can power up your tank to become even stronger.

For $7, and the Summer Sale right around the corner, I would highly recommend this game if you're into tank shooters.\t\t\t\t\t
