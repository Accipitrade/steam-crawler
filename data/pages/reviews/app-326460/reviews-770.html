\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat modernized version of Scorched Earth without the mechanics being a direct clone, which is honestly a good thing.

+Multiplayer works beautifully for a game in EA. Plenty of others to play with too.
+The screen shaking and deep bass of the explosions makes combat feel intense.
+Randomized, limited weapons selection each round results in mixing your strategy up on the fly instead of just being able to sit there and adjust your trajectory with the same weapon over and over until you kill the other guy.
+NO MICRO TRANSACTIONS, you have to earn everything
+A level system that somewhat makes sense without throwing multiplayer way off balance when playing with others of differing levels. (At a 20 level difference, the higher level person will have a _slight_ edge, but nothing crazy)
+No mandatory chat filters. THANK YOU for not treating us like children!
+The $19.99 4-pack is a fantastic deal.

All of the negatives I have so far are minor.

-A means to kick idle players and replace them with bots would be nice.
-The shortest round timer @ 35 seconds is still a touch long.
-Randomized weapons effects on death would be a nice option to add. (SE players will remember the horror of a nearby teammate getting killed and suddenly becoming a nuke)
-Game needs to be advertised/pushed more so we can get even more players
-The gear shop (gears are earned in multiplayer, not bought) needs more stuff.
-Needing to mouseover gears to pick them up is a bit odd and results in missing gears after you've died and are focusing on something else until the match is over.
-No wind (yet)

Game looks to be updated extremely frequently as of this moment. My only regret is not purchasing it sooner.\t\t\t\t\t
