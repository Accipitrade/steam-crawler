\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is surely a nostolgia gem for anyone who remembers Pocket Tanks. There are some differences, and some pros and cons to this game that may or may not have any comparison to the previously mentioned game.

Pros:
New Game, Actively Updated
Enhanced Graphics
Online Gameplay
Unique Weapons that reward strategic thinking.
Possibly one of the best gaming communities online at the moment.
(I nominated this game for the "Best Community" award.

Cons:
You don't have access to all weapons at the start, so you may at times be at a disadvantage.
However, weapons are randomly selected for all players based on unlocks, so it isn't like the other players are ALWAYS going to have better weapons. Sometimes they have too many situational weapons to put up a decent fight. Which is another con in my opinion.
While the community is amazing, there are a few who will literally put a ruler up to their screen to get snipe shots with 100% accuracy. They're technically not doing anything wrong, but it is a little cheap. Changing winds could help fight that.

All in all, highly reccomend this game if you're looking for a relaxing arcade game to play in the office or living room.\t\t\t\t\t
