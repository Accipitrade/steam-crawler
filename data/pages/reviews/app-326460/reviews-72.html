\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUnfortunately, this game completely misses the mark as far as what was so much fun about Atomic Tanks and its predecessors (which are FREE by the way).

There is no picking your own weapons. All weapons are given randomly. You don't start with any good weapons unlocked either. Apparently they expect you to grind xp for tens and tens of hours to get cool weapons.

All games are played in single rounds. In the original versions of this game, a game consisted of multiple rounds where you would gradually be able to afford better and better weapons.

The whole fun of the original games was that you could jump in and play with friends for an hour, do a few games, get ridiculous weapons, and blow the snot out of each other.

Not so in shell shock. You'll be stuck with bad weapons and you can't even pick your own loadout. Big thumbs down for me.

No xp from local multi-player btw.

I wish I had played it sooner after purchasing as I would have requested a refund. Game is just not fun.\t\t\t\t\t
