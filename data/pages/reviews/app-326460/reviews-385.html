\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI grew up with games like Bang Bang and cannon's on old windows system. So this game is great both for nostalgia and being modern.

The amount of weapons available is...well.... a lot, and trying to master the technique of each or at least your favorites is worth quite a few hours of game play in and of itself.

Great game to play with friends be it a private free for all or death match, Or joining a team against randoms. It is also a very fun game to just jump in solo and play with randoms and meet new people. Plenty of funny moments between missed shots, witty banter in chat, and so forth.

One small downside I do find is that the "quick match" matchmaking can be a bit troublesome and at times gives the "cant join because games is in session" message often, when you go and look manually at the server list there are open lobbies. But its not a huge annoyance, just wait a couple seconds and try again.

Would highly recommend, can be a great time waster, just jumping on a playing a match or two, or even be a great game to spends hour at a time on.\t\t\t\t\t
