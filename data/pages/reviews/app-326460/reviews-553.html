Everything works wonderfully together to provide a fantastic and chaotic experience as all players struggle trying to get their strongest hits on their opponents. Only major complaint is that the levelling system makes for some one-sided matches if you do not restrict the levels that can join your room (Did not have too much trouble with finding other players, though, even on the most restrictive level search settings.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has its drawbacks. Progression system is flawed to greatly favor higher level players. It's unfortunate that XP farms are the most worthwhile way to grind weapon xp.

On the positive side, the community is great here. It's a relaxed atmosphere, and there are very few toxic players. Rebound mode is fun and separates skilled vs unskilled players to some extent, and is what keeps me playing.

This game has no microtransactions, I don't know what the other reviews are talking about. There are only two exclusive player banners, but everything else can be unlocked through normal play.\t\t\t\t\t
