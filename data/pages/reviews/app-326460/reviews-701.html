\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt isn't a bad game... but it really needs some polishing. The problems aren't things that will absolutely ruin the game but they will be irritating and annoying over time. To start off, when I click "Quick join" I don't want to be able to join games that are full or in progress. The only servers to be available for quick joining should be ones that haven't started yet and have space. Also, when I click quit, instead of just closing the game, the game stops working. As in, it brings up a tab saying that it's stopped working and it's looking for a solution. Thirdly, weapons will sometimes not work quite how they should. For example, today, I fired quicksand straight at a tank and it just disappeared off the map. From my previous experiences, quicksand is one of the weapons that is actually supposed to impact, not go into the ground like a tunneler or sub-sniper. Finally, some of the missions are really harder or easier than they should be at that point. For example, assistant regional manager took one try while some of the target ones take several days of effort. Overall, I can't say it's a bad game. However, there are issues that make the game annoying to play when they happen.

EDIT: Ignore what I said about the quicksand thing. I was wrong there. The "stopped working" thing also never happens to me anymore. The quick joining is still an issue though. Still also a great game.\t\t\t\t\t
