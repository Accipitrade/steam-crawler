\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game isn't for you if you hate to grind for things, or don't like playing the same game mode over and over to get through the grinding fast.

Pros:

Fun with friends.

Cons:

Incomplete Clans features.
Incomplete Tournament features.
Incomplete Weapons being added.
Unbalanced xp for modes.
Bugs that don't get fixed and that have been known for months.
The game Dev that never talks to the community.
Once you reach the highest level there is nothing to do game wise.

About the xp, the multiplyers for each game mode is a big difference between them.

Deathmatch-Normal-x2.04, One Weapon-x3.06
Points-Normal-x1.53, One Weapon-x2.3
Assassin-Normal-x2.3, One Weapon-x3.44
Juggernaut-Normal-x2.04, One Weapon-x3.06
Marksman-Normal-x2.04, One Weapon-x3.06
Rebound-Normal-x3.06, One Weapon-x4.59
{{All Modes based on single shot with High wind}}

As you can see the game modes have a big difference between them, from Points at x1.53 to Rebound at x4.59. Now which would you play? Pretty safe bet you would play Rebound for the xp multiplyer. Now most games have all their modes at the same xp level and have for certain modes added xp bonuses for different things.
My point is that the xp needs to be fixed anyone that is wanting to go for higher levels mostly only play Rebound. Which makes this game very boring pretty fast because then they only ever want to play Rebound.

A few points that the Dev has falsely said.

“ShellShock Live has been community driven from the beginning. Early Access gives us the opportunity to build the game while receiving feedback from players to ensure that SSL grows to fulfill its potential.”
This goes with my point of him never talking to us, community driven isn't even shown anymore.

“ShellShock Live is already a fully functional game jam-packed with content. There are currently over 200 weapons, 100 maps, and 80 single-player missions. There are probably a few stubborn bugs here and there but we'll iron those out as Early Access progresses. There are also several planned features that we haven't had a chance to build yet.”
Fully Functional is not true at all, the servers crash and now with the latest update the game is all laggy. 100 maps....not even 50 yet.

So if you want my opinion if you should buy this game or not. I would say no, unless you have a ton of friends to play with. And you like a 1000 plus hour grinding game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is overall a great game, but there are few flaws in the game.

Pros:

+ Great Graphics
Extreme improvement over the flash versions

+ Great Music
A cycle of calm and intense music with victory music makes the game much more appealing

+ Extreme Number of Weapons
Over 400 weapons to unlock and upgrade to destroy opponents with. More weapons are always in the making. (Although the average time period for new weapons could be better)

+ Addicting
There is a weird glitch making you go back and play over and over again.

+ Play with your Friends
Playing with friends makes the game more enjoyable than with some random jerk

+ 9 Different Game Modes
Be the last one standing in Deathmatch, inflict the most damage in Points, destroy 1 tank at a time in Assassin, steal other's voltage with damage in Charge, attack a ball to score in Shoccer, become the sniper in Marksman, team up or destroy all in Juggernaut, become an expert at imagining segmented parabolas in Vortex, and become the master of trajectories in Rebound.

Cons:

- EXTREME luck factors in game
Random starting weapons, weapons with RNG, one tank gets a close x2 damage, easy to impossible bumpers in Rebound the game is full of RNG. Luck has a good impact on who wins, however the more skilled a player, the higher chance of victory.

- Could be better Community
Many players of this game are toxic, but there are a good handful of players that are fun to play with.

If you aren't sure to buy this game yet, you can check out the flash version (ShellShock Live/ShellShock Live 2) which are both free. The Steam version has much more content, and is instead play to win, not pay to win.\t\t\t\t\t
