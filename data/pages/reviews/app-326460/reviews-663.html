Don't let it's graphics fool you. Beyond it's retro look it has a large amount of gameplay and physics to explore.
The great variety of weapons in this game is astonishing.
It keeps the game interesting as you discover new ways to obliterate your opponents.
The game also has a great sense of humor like shooting piñata's, pooping birds or even throw music notes at your enemies.
The multiplayer aspect is what really increases this game's value exponentially.
It's just that much more fun to play with real people instead of artificial intelligence.
You can play with up to 8 people at the same time and customize just about anything you want, from the terrain to the game mode and wind strength.
This game is a must buy for anyone who loves turn based strategy.\t\t\t\t\t
