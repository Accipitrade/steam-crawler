\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith over 2100 hours in this incredibly affordable and fun game, an honest and thoughtful review from me is long overdue.

First of all I have to say, the amount of effort that has obviously been put into this should have warranted a price of way more than 7. With also the option to buy this game in a 4 pack, making each copy another 2 cheaper, this is incredibly generous and for long time players like me as well as for literally anyone who played more than like 3 rounds the money was definitely well spent.
Basically what you are buying is the opposite of an FPS-Shooter as you get as much time as you need to figure out in which exact direction and with how much power to shoot best.

There is an owerwhelming number and variety of different weapons and modes, of which many require their own and unique strategies, making for a lot of small things that even I havent yet figured out, and I frequently encounter other experienced players who havent either. All that is to say, this game should not get boring anytime soon if your goal is to perfect yourself.
Some bugs that haven`t gotten a fix or even a fixing attempt in years exist, but they are very minor and can even somewhat be exploited. Updates with new content happen rarely but surely. A big plus is that even if your PC is a literal potato it will probably run this game rather smoothly. The dev used to be playing regularly too, but, well, at least neither I nor anyone I know has seen him recently.

Most important to know however is that while it is possible to play alone, having a few friends with you will improve your experience here by a lot, I cannot stress enough how much of a difference one or more conversation partners might make. With game sizes of up to 8 players, everyone should be accommodated. If youre like me you could also make some online friends, there are a lot of genuinely nice folks to find if you are lucky. With enough time and the help of discord or something comparable, it might start to feel more like a primitive social network than a tank game if youre a talkative person. Sadly, the other end of the spectrum, such as rude and obnoxious players or people who seem to enjoy throwing profanities around, also has its fair share. Youll meet players from all around the world, but for some reason, Germans (like me), Norwegians and Americans are the most common. Make of that what you will.

Before I end this review, there is one major downside here: The leveling system. There are a total of 100 levels to achive by earning xp from each round you play. This by itself is nothing bad, but after you reach level 85, each subsequent level becomes an unbearable grind. For reference, the xp requirement scales exponentially, so that level 90 is about a tenth and level 96 half of the xp it takes to reach level 100. For even further reference, getting all the way to level 100 took me exactly 785 hours and from what I gathered it is not uncommon for this number to be over 1000 hours even. Dedicated players generally sit at level 99 for months and it is understandably frustrating for them. Also, some game modes give way wayyy more xp than others, which leads to the least lucrative ones seeing little to no play at all, which is quite sad because they are just as much fun.

In conclusion, do consider spending your money here if you:
- are looking for a game to play with your friends or want to make new ones
- dont mind slow progressing
- have a crappy PC that cant run anything else
- liked the original worms game because this is basically the same thing with different design

If you read everything until here and meet me in game at some point, do tell me cause Id be really interested in how many people actually read these. Until then!\t\t\t\t\t
