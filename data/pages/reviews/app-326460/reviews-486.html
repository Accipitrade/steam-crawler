Cons:
In all honesty, this game is horrifically unbalanced with its RNG and almost all the changes made to the game in the past ~3 years have been negative changes to mechanics. (Hopefully one day Kyle will listen to the experienced members of the community for positive change to gameplay.)

Ever since the changes made to the "Dev Destroyer" achievement were made, there has been a high influx of harassment to level 100 players.

Having to spend a minimum of 600 hours to reach endgame content can be a drag and often scares newer players away from progressing in the game.

There is a lot of bugs, ranging from minor things like barrel length inconsistency to game breaking like ejection to menu with no tangible cause (though this is rare).


Pros:

Well I've spent over 1600 hours of my life in this game, it's a good game for when you don't want something high intensity or high focus to wind down with. This game has founded a real life friendship for myself and others, it can be highly social when it isn't toxic.

There is a lot of content for a game that is this simple.

It can be visually pleasing, the tanks themselves are quite detailed. The assets are simple but gives the game a clean aesthetic, and low system requirements are nice to have.

Overall I give the game a 7/10 in its genre, there is very high potential, but there is issues present that drag this score down.\t\t\t\t\t
