\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShellShock Live is basically Worms without Worms, with less movement and a lot more guns. And I mean A LOT. The game has lots of different gamemodes, but the lesser known ones won't be seeing the light of day unless you are willing to wait ages to fill up a lobby. That generally isn't an issue though, because the basic Deathmatch can be lots of fun for a long time on its own, and you have control over lots of tweaks to the settings when hosting a lobby of your own. For instance: Wind, HP amount, Teams or FFA, and so on.

The gameplay is rather simple, you select a weapon you want to use out of a randomly pulled arsenal of weapons you have unlocked so far, and point it where you want and lock in. After that, when everyone is locked in, all the shots are fired. As for the weapons, there are over 400 of them, with weapons being unlocked by leveling up or by leveling up the weapons itself, which unlocks upgraded versions of them. It should be noted that reaching max level generally takes around 800 hours of playing, so you won't have access to all of the weapons from the start.

Pros:
  • Lots and lots of different weapons.
  • Unlocks for days, and getting new weapons never gets old.
  • Lobbies for more popular gamemodes are easily filled and getting into games is fast.
  • Good community, very few toxic players.
Cons:
  • Weapons unlocked on higher levels are generally a lot better, which may cause some difficulties for newer players, if not limiting the maximum level difference for the lobby.
  • The weapons you get are random, so you could get really unlucky every now and then, but as matches are short, you will be in a new one in a blink of an eye.

Overall rating [8 / 10]

Personally I can not get enough of the weapon unlock screen, getting something new you've been anticipating feels so nice. The game is also super chill and endless fun, especially with friends.\t\t\t\t\t
