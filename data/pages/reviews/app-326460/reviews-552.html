\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI bought this full price thinking...."Hmm...this seems like a quite simple and entertaining game." Which...It is!

Already a week or so more into this game, it has a lot of playability, especially if you have friends to play it with. Very casual and easy to get into, I easily could let my sister have a go at this (I haven't yet tho), and be fine, it's that easy.

Pros and Cons......


-Pros:

-Simple and Easy Layout.

-Campagn Mode to keep you busy, and to help you level.

-Speaking of Level, you gain new weapons each time you do, and because the game is in Early Access still, there's more to be added on in the future!

-Colors and Graphics are very attractive to the eyes. At least that's imo.

I think that wraps up the Pros, that I can currently think of.


-Cons:

-Despite the game being in Early Access, that's a disadvantage in itself, because at times, there will be bug fixes and patches that you will come across from time-to-time. But nothing deal breaking.

-There IS some luck-bias in here. You could have the Perfect shot lined up, and it all go to waste, or spread weapons, like the Shotgun, could not end up where you may like them. It's not just the wind affecting your shots, keep that in mind.

-For a game this simple, the price is a little high, but I'd suggest to get it while it's on a sale of some sort, unless you feel like purchasing it anyway.

I might think of more Cons later, but for now, this is what I thought of.


Personal Rating: 8/10

It's a somewhat casual game that invites players of all kinds to enjoy the fun of destroying each other, blowing stuff up...And it has some Physics in there for those of you who feel a bit brainy. I would reccommend this if you are looking for some simple fun to enjoy, and by all means.....Get it on sale if you can. It's a great game!\t\t\t\t\t
