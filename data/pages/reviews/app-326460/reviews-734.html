\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is quite entertaining. Throughout the entirety of the time that I played, the majority of it was spent getting kicked from lobbys and waiting for others to ready up. But the time I truly spent playing the game? Priceless.
For a very small fee you can pick up a game with a kind community. Challenging and fun gameplay. And a huge variety of weapons that can be used to your own benefit.

Features
After every level that you grow you recieve a token that can be used to upgrade your tank in 4 main branches.
Luck, armor, movement, and damage.
If you feel unhappy with your current path you can reset your upgrades once per week and recieve all your tokens that you have spent and build a new path.

During the game there are gears that drop from doing damage or opening crates. You can click the gears to collect them which you may then spend in the shop in order to unlock maps, colors, songs, and other customizations.

Overall Rating\t
For this Game I would have to rate it an 7/10 due to the time it takes to save up for items in the shop and the time it takes to get into a game. It can sometimes take 5-10 minutes to get into a game. Especially if you aren't playing with other people you know.

Final Thoughts
For a cheap game it was a good idea and is worth picking up. It however is not a good choice for someone who has limited time to play due to time restraints such as work, school, chores, or appointments.
