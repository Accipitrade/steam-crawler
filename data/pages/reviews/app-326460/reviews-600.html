\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAbsolutely no issues with this game at all. You can find, join and start a lobby within 5-10 seconds, most people are very friendly and the games are always interesting due to the random elements, but never feel grossly unfair.

There's alot of choice and variants in upgrades, weapons and of course - how you play the game.

The XP and levelling starts off very fast, which is nice for new players, but does slow down dramatically once you hit around level 75, but that gives the dedicated players something to aim for.

It's both a game for casual players looking for a fun remake of old Tanks, and a fun game for players who love a challenge! Plus the price tag is insanely low. Would recommend to anyone.\t\t\t\t\t
