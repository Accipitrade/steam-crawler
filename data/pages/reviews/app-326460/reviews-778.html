\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh look, I did a review.
This is a game that's only worth it if you actually bother to spend the time to learn and improve in it. You know, like most games. You would not beat this in a day, or a month, or probably a year if you don't play THAT often. The road to level 100 is a long and winding road, even if the xp requirements were reduced.

Pros:
A reasonable amount of players to keep the game flowing.
Missions that WILL boost you up past the first 30+ levels if you complete them all before the main game, allowing you to get a head start.
No IAP, everything's free if you take the effort to make it that way.
The graphics are beautiful to look at, even on low quality.

Cons:
The grind!
Extreme learning curve at the beginning, especially with working out what each weapon does and how to use them.
Rather imbalanced community, if you don't work on those said missions to boost your level, you're not gonna find many low levelled people.

Overall, it's a great game. Definitely get it if you enjoyed the worms series, and if you're unsure, go try the free online versions before you give this a shot.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played this game constantly on Kongregate and couldn't help but purchase this version to try it out. Overall it's just as much fun, a bit of a grind to level up both your tank and weapons but I don’t mind it too much.

The game is constantly updated with new features every few days to keep it interesting and has received a surge of new players due to being featured on some popular YouTube channels. I recommend this game if you don’t mind that it takes a while to grind up in levels (to put it in perspective, I played 180 hours and I am level 88).

My only complaints would be the unbearably difficult single player missions that pretty much require you to be lucky or have upgraded weapons to beat (such as the piñata and boss levels). Also, the match default setting puts a difference in level of 20 in order to join. This is understandable as higher levels would have an advantage but it also makes players who have high levels not able to play in a lot of matches (for me it's around 6-8 games available out of 60-70). That diminishes some of the enjoyment once you reach a high level and can no longer join a lot of games. It would be better if the default setting was "all levels" for matchmaking but you could change it if you didn't want to play stronger opponents so that there isn't as much of a downside to ranking up.

Overall I'd say this is a 7/10\t\t\t\t\t
