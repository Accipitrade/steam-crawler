\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo this is a pretty solid game to play. It is really fun to play with friends by testing out multiple guns and tank builds possible. The large amount of weapons and customization contributes to the fun factor. The solid price at about $7ish helped increase the appeal. The only real critque I have about the game is that the guns aren't really well explained to the user. Some of the shots are common sense like stickybomb, while others are not exactly clear on what they do or how to optimize the gun shot. I understand discovering how a new weapon upgrade or shot works is part of the fun factor for it, but a slight guide or some tips would be appreciated for the less experienced players or just general tips. Overall the gameplay is akin to worms with more guns and memes. Price is really solid and would say grab it now. Prepare your self for tank memes. I give a casual reveiw of buy it/play it.\t\t\t\t\t
