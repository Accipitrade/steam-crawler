\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis could have been a fun casual game. There is decent choice of weapons and tanks built in. The gameplay, while it could use some tweaking, is good mediocre fun.

The game itself is ruined by a few things though. Super slow, unskippable animation sequences at the end of each round would be my first gripe. I mean I get it, some poor dev and/or designer has spent countless hours making those, it would be a shame if the animation went by before the players had a chance to bask in the glory of their particle effects and tweens; but come on, those things last longer then some rounds. Make them skippable or make them faster.

Another thing that bother me but may not bother others is the leveling system. I was expecting to just hop in and play with everything, like back in the day with Scorched Earth. Nope. You must earn your right to wield the circular tank cupola or shiny glowing bullet. Cant have those without getting some pride and accomplishment first.

Ungh.\t\t\t\t\t
