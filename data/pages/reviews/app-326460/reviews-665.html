ShellShock Live is the best "tank"/artillery game i know so far. I played some of those games, like mostly "Pocket Tanks" or "artillery" but Shellshock goes some steps forward which were missing in other games "like" this one.

- First of all, you have a level up system. You actually can Level up your Tank and give him some passiv abilitys. its not "very much" or "unfair" ones, its stuff like luck, chances of crit, more & faster movement and, of course, armor against enemy fire.

- The system and the kind plays so..."fluent". The music, the grafic & co, all fits perfectly together, the explositions and co, all look great!

- The weapons are crasy sometimes but it gives a WHOLE lot of them and nearly every update it is some more. Srs, from normal shots, fireworks, water shots, rocks, sniper, cluster bombs, beams and what not...srs, it gives a whole lot weapons.

...But is it worth the price and playtime?
ShellShock offers a lot for the lil price. you will get kinda a "action round-based artillery game with Level Up elements" - and that all at ones. It also has NO pay to win system, all you get is by playing the game and not by buying it. The game is Totally worth the price and i highly recomment it to people who wana try a alternative next to the "Worms" series or people who actually loved such artillery games. For this price tag, there is nothing you can complain about. a nice game which actually has this "just one round more" feeling and a whole lot of funny weapons, modes and settings.
