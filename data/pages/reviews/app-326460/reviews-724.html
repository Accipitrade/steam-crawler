\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been playing this game for 6+ hours straight, I love it.

I'm either a fan of survival games, and/or a fan of games which you can play to level up, unlock things etc.

The simplicity of this game makes it even playable for kids around the age of 6. ( I even think some of these kids play it cause please don't spend your time in the in build lobby.. that's hell on earth.)

The game has some awesome missions which of some may seem impossible but they're really do-able!

I really dislike the ruler cheat but really, every game has their toxic players, and if you don't want to take the risk to play against these people, invite your friends and have a tank battle!

One think I also dislike is the achievement of which you will have to defeat kChamp in a round, which is linked to unlocking a weapon, if you're a collector (Like me) you want to be able to get all the weapons, without having to bother to be a lucky person to end up in a match with 1 person in the entire world..

But yeah to sum it up.

Pros:
- Awesome graphics, I like the effects/light effects, etc.
- Smooth gameplay, I haven't encountered many bugs, so I can't complain (Sometimes resetting a mission when an enemy attacks makes me have to reset it again but meh.)
- level up system! I love those, I spend my points not realising I was creating a swastika.


Cons:
- Shizzle ingame lobby, I dislike how their toxic personalities effect my gameplay. I get forced to look at it, maybe have a setting to turn it off? (EDIT: You can turn it off, sorry, it's in options!)
- The achievement to defeat kchamp in a round, I want some other way aswell to being able to unlock this achievement, for example, defeating a person that has defeated kchamp.

That's it, for quesions feel free to ask me about my opinion on this game.

PS: I challenge kChamp to a duel.\t\t\t\t\t
