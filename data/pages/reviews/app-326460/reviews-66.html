\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game for true pacifists. Good game to casually play with your friends. Just kidding, I know you don't have any, so let me tell you about the campaign. The missions become more and more difficult and you will wonder why only your enemies have these OP weapons and you don't. Well, it's because you shall prove thyself, n00b! So stop crying and keep trying.

Gameplay is a solid 8/10, the obstacles could be a little more unfair in my opinion.
Graphics, well it's a casual 2D game. What would you expect?
If you believe this game is difficult you probably think 1*1=2 and clouds are made in factories.
This game gives you the unique chance to attack enemies with frightening weapons like cats, sausages, bananas, bell peppers, water balloons or seagull crap. I'm not making this up.
The physics are absolutely insane, I have never before seen a football stuck to a vertical wall.

Highly recommend it to everyone who likes tanks, 2D games, "strategy" or simply burying your "friends" in quicksand and throwing nukes at them.

That being said, you should definitely NOT play the game IF:
- you like pineapple pizza
- you haven't seen Star Wars
- you play Fortnite
- your favorite color is yellow (WTF?!!?!?)
- you pour the milk before the cereal (this should be illegal by federal law, pls get a brain MRI if you do this)
- you think this game won't be worth the money (it will!)
If any of the above apply to or offend you, I really don't care. Talk to your therapist. To the rest of you: have fun negotiating diplomatic differences with your enemies ;)\t\t\t\t\t
