\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is fun and reasonably priced. The game's mechanics are simple to understand for a third-person shooter and any player should be able to understand the game easily. I enjoyed the single-player challenges and they get more difficult in a fair manner. However, I have had times where these challenges appeared to have a slight spike in difficulty when it was not expected to have one. As a player who tends to play single-player games, I found myself gravitating towards the challenges more than the multiplayer mode. Also, the sense of progression with unlocking new weapons as you level up is satisfying and made me feel good about my skills in this game.

The soundtrack throughout the game is beautiful. The songs give a sense of majesty and portray the game as grand and exciting. Personally, this is my favorite part of the game and it helps make this game special.

The multiplayer component of the game is fun if you are with similarly-skilled players. I have had instances where I found myself engrossed with the multiplayer mode of this game and I have found my skills improved over time. However, there were also times where I tried to join a game lobby and I was unable to, even though it appeared that the lobby was still open. The multiplayer games I have been in were fun and engaging, allowing me to hone in my skills in this game.

Overall, this game is a fun game with its enticing music and engaging game mechanics. Its core game lies with multiplayer games and has options for people who prefer single-player games. I recommend this game regardless of if it is on sale or not. It is a fairly priced game that can keep players entertained for hours.\t\t\t\t\t
