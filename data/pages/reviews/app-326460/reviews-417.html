\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter playing 2 years in Shell shock Live in armor games and Shell shock live 2 i feel that this game is a huge improvement to those

Positive

-->More smooth Mechanics according to the movement of tanks and the tracing of shots
-->New weapons
-->Achievements that help you level up
-->Single player if you are new to the game and try to learn it (also it can boots you up to level 50 easily)
-->New game modes
-->You can play with 8 players
-->Coins are found in the game or by winning a game and spinning a wheel and not bought(not pay to win with better weapons)
-->Songs

Negative

-->Removing of some weapons (Holy Grenade)
-->Chain upgrade weapon logic thus it needs a lot of grinding in low level weapons to get higher tiers

After V1.1

I was so excited to see this day happen after 3 years but it brought many surprises.

Healing

I feel healing items were and are unnecessary for this game.
Sure it helps but its just stalling the game and in high levels the amount of heals are not even noticed since the chances of rolling a high base damage weapon are high especially with 30 weapons in the pool or with getting 5 new weapons in turns 6,11 etc if set to 10 weapons pool.

Weapons

If we exclude confuser which was buffed a while after the initial update to higher damage the rest are kind of low damage and based on randomness or way too expensive (2000 gears for a simple 25-30-35 life steal no matter how much it deals to the enemies

Daily Quests & Cosmetics Coins

I mean sure he made a micro transaction service to buy cosmetics thus not affecting game play but adding an achievement and dog tags related to it seemed impossible at first to get ,since we rolled rares and ultra rares that cost over 250 of them.
So it became a game of patience to collect those from the daily quests in order to buy commons and uncommons for 40 or less of them
But it gave a chance to try new modes when it came to those quests.

Portal Fix

If more than 1 set of portal appears they are numbered so we know where to aim and not have it based on randomness

Great work kyle keep it up\t\t\t\t\t
