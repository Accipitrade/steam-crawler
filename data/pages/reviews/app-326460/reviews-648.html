\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPROS -
- Bunch of different game modes (most play death match though)
- Enough weapons and upgrades to keep you busy for a long time
- A single player where you can fight ai or progress through a couple of different challanges
- A good mix of skill and luck to a point where you can still win if someone is reasonably above your level.
- The weapons have a unique feel to them (most of them anyways)

Cons-
- The muliplayer is currently descending into chaos where half the servers are there only for grinding instead of actually playing the game becaause all servers are player created
- There is an rng aspect that can REALLY screw you over
- If you are playing a server with out wind some people have found a way to make a "ruler" that makes it to where they always hit their target

Neutural-
- The Dev is kinda slow with updates and not a lot gets added when the update does come out but they are still updating this game

Overall-
I really like this game, the community can be umm really bad at times but I have also found people who just joked around and had fun with the game. However, if you are a new player, not a lot of people are going to like you and you will most likely be kicked from the majority of servers you join.

Recommendation- Wait until a day where a lot of people would buy this game (sometime like Christmas) so you can level up with people who are also new. Until then you can play a game called shellshock live (hmmm, now where have I heard that name before...)on armorgames.com and it is basically the same exact thing as this but it is more simple, there are less weapons and less players (come to think about it idk if people are still playing it over there)\t\t\t\t\t
