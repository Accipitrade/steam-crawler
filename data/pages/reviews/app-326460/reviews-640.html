ShellShock Live is a 2D Strategy shooter Turn based game. With 100 Tank upgrades, 350+ weapons, Tons of great maps.

Now lets go over What we ACTUALLY want to see, yeah?

Personal Experience & Thoughts
At first glance, ShellShock Live looks like just another "2d Strat shooter", Well yes this is the case.\nThere are a ton of features this game has compared to other 2d Shooters. \nI've had tons of fun in my own play time, Nearly putting in atleast 3 hours per day.\nI still haven't even gotten through merely 15% of what this game has to offer yet.\nThis game is a Blast-From-The-Past as well.\nDo I suggest this Game?\nYes.
I give this a 8.8/10 Rating for sure.

Now lets go over other things
Tank upgrades
You may be thinking to your self "Ugh, Microtransactions!? Not worth it." Before you click off this review.
The tank upgrades are 100% Free You get them as easily as playing!
Effecitvely there are four Tank tree upgrades.We will start with the Most important trees.

Upgrade
Stats
Mobility/Tracks
Upgrade your movement ability.
+You can go up steeper hills, Move further away, Also move much faster!
Chassis/Health
Improves your Vitality.
+ Survive longer,Get Regeneration for health and shields aswell as start with more HP!
Gun/Barrel
Improves crit damage & Chance
+ Have a better chance to deal more damage Aswell as doing more damage.
Luck/Supplies
More weapons, better starting items.
+ Better items from crates, More upgraded weapons at the start, Higher chance to get better gear mid game

The Good Stuff
++The dev is always updating the game, with lots of new content and lots of bug fixes. Only Early Access game I've bought from memory that has done a phenomonal job at getting out of Early Access and
++Weapons. They just keep coming.
+A report feature for misbehaving players or cheaters
+Multiple gamemodes and lots of options for each gamemode
+The shop is based on Play-to-win versus Pay-to-win, also doesn't feel like you're grinding in an RPG game getting the gears for the shop
+Upgrade your tank however you want, both aesthetics and features
+Weapons are random in each game, meaning you get a small selection of weapons that you have unlocked overtime
+Weapons are very balanced and vary quite a bit, from weak hard to miss ones to hard hitting ones that have a tiny chance of hitting your enemy
+Have I mentioned weapons?

=Juggernaut gamemode is rather unbalanced, the juggernaut almost always wins because he has so much more health

-A major downside is when you play higher level players. They have more weapons to choose from and many more upgrades. Once you start to play this game, you will get destroyed a lot
