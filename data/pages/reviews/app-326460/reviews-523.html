\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShellshock Live is a very fun game if you enjoy using some skill and having luck take over your every move. There are a few issues I have with the game such as very low update frequency as well as the in-game currency taking a little long to amass. There is a fair bit of level advantage at least until you hit level 40-50 then it kinda comes down to luck and skill but before that it can be a rough grind for new players. Overall I think this game is just fine as a great time waster and to just hop on and kill your friends before you head to work or go to bed. 8/10\t\t\t\t\t
