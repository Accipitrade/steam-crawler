\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you have fond memories of a little DOS game called "Scorched Earth: the Mother of all Games", you'll probably get a few hours of enjoyment out of this.

At the moment, though, it's still not quite as good. The only things ShellShock live does better is a much larger arsenal and higher-end graphics/music.

You will, perhaps, be more than a bit upset that you can't set up matches with friends AND bots.

You also cannot select what weapons you want to bring with you. As of the last time I played, every gamemode randomizes your arsenal selection for each match, only making it more difficult to level up your favorites.

You also cannot change your Tank color. It's all red or green, and the variety of tank styles is lacking, currently.

Perhaps most heartbreaking of all, you can't play around with physics such as wind, dirt suspension, and most importantly: wall types. The inability to set my games to wrap-around or bouncing walls forever haunts me...

Still, there's fun to be had on the utterly mad variety of available weapons. My favorites by far are the Snake and Sinkhole shots, followed by diggers, builders, and dead weight!

The firing range is the best game mode.\t\t\t\t\t
