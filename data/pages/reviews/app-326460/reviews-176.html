\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't play this game a ton, but when I do - it's a blast. Very easy to pick up and get into. Pretty quick rounds, large variety of weapons, and heaps of chaos. The randomization of what weapons you get can be either unfair or incredibly OP, but it is part of the game and part of the fun.

That being said, the amount of times I've come across pretty toxic behavior or poor-sportsmanship, is real high. People get spammy with kicking or being sore losers. Either way, mostly this does not happen and is a minority of people. Still, something to be aware of if you are particularly sensitive to that type of behavior.

Reminds me of the Worms series, but with tanks. Overall, great stuff for such a small game!
Worth closer to $5 or less than it is $7. I'd say wait for a small sale at a minimum.\t\t\t\t\t
