\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSingle Player: 3/10

IMO this is where the game is the weakest for me. The only reason to play the single player is to level yourself up and that is it. There are some fun levels, but most of them are EXTREMELY frustrating. Like if you have anger issue stay away from this mode or you will break something (I'm a fairly calm person and I needed to stop playing because I was actually getting really pissed). A lot of the shots you have to make need to be damn near perfect in order to advance to the next level and some you can't mess up once or you will have to reset and try again. Aiming is a huge part of the game and it's all based on angles. It's extremely unforgiving and is super hard to figure out to make yourself accurate. There are a few fun levels, but they're mainly the duels you have to do.


Multiplayer: 8/10

Now I haven't played this against randoms so take what I say with a grain of salt! Online with friends is where this game truly shines. The amount of chaotic fun to had in this is surprisingly endless. Just because one of your friends is a high level doesn't mean they will stomp you into the ground and everyone has a chance at winning (in my experience). It will also level you up, but not as fast sadly. There's not much to write about it since all it is to me is chaotic fun that makes me belly laugh and want to come back for more.


Summary:

If you have friends to play with I'd highly recommend this game due to the amount of laughs and chaos you will have. HOWEVER, if you don't have anyone to play this with I wouldn't recommend it due to unforgiving mechanics and a supposedly a toxic community.

Overall Score: 6/10\t\t\t\t\t
