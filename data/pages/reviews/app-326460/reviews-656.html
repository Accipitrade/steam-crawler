\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's an unigue fun game. A lot of small things improved after the initial release which makes it nice and has good UX too. Its good to see the game grow with its community, I can find the game type I want almost any time I look for. Weapons, progress and shop system all are great with a wide range, you can spend a lot building things for your tank. The visuals are sweet, every shot a tank fires will look cool especially when the battlefield is large with many tanks. The soudtracks are cool too. There's a good range of track and also victory tunes you can set among other things. Another thing I like is there is no micro-transactions anymore (like there was in the browser version). I recommend it to anyone looking for a casual action game that is unique.\t\t\t\t\t
