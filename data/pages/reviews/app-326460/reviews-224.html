Shellshock Live

ShellShock Live is a very good strategy game. There are countless weapons from bananas to atomic bombs, there is everything. All weapons can also be leveled up. In addition, you can upgrade your tank in 4 different categories (Chassis, Track, Gun, Luck) with skill points, which you get through level ups. You can fight in 9 different game modes on many different maps. There are 100 levels in total and many different tasks, some of them are very tricky. ShellShock Live is a classic in this genre. You can't go wrong for under 10$.\t\t\t\t\t
