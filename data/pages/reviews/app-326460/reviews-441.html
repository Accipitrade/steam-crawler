\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all let me say that I've played over 30 hours of this game in a week, so I think that my review is very non-biased and justified very well.

Let's talk overall game design and graphics. The design of maps and menu screens was well thought out, and it's a very unique and fun blend of realism and cartoon. I like the scratchy, blown-up designs on the buttons, and I think it's a nice aesthetic touch. Personally, I play games on a semi-decent laptop, and this game runs FLAWLESSLY on highest graphics setting. It doesn't take a lot to run this game, and I would recommend to anyone who just wants a really decent time-passer.

The Weaponry: Near perfection. all weapons are thought-out, designed, and executed beautifully. I wish that there were more weapons to be unlocked in the shop or for lower levels, because trying to beat higher levels with the same weapons over and over is a bit monotonous. I know you can raise or lower maximum level difference, but that's not much fun is it?

The Players: The player base, for the most part, is quite nice. Every once in a while you may run into the occasional D-bag, but 99% of the time people are nice.

Overall Thoughts: This game has a lot more potential then what it already has, and while that's a bit upsetting, I think if this game can get past a couple small glitches and hiccups, it could be an absolutely amazing game.

Thanks for reading my first review, and I hope you enjoy this game!\t\t\t\t\t
