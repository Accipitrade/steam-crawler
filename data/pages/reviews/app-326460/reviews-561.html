\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's pretty simple. Set an angle, set a velocity and hit space. If done correctly a red colored tank will explode. gg ez.

But these red tanks move around, and shoot back, and there are hundreds of different ammuntion in use. The landscape around you will change, either by bombardement or randomness. All is in flux at all times.

You found the right spot? You got the perfect angle? Your target didn't move turing its turn? Just hit spacebar again and watch your projectile gooooo ...directly into the black hole, that just emerged. or bump of some barrier. Don't even get me started on calculating the right shot through some portals!

So, it isn't that simple. Really. But it is fun. So much fun.
Even more so, if you find the right group of people, that like to banter and make fun of each other.
Just never forget to press f, to pay respect!

Besides having a hundred weapons that can upgrade with use, and some other unlocks to keep you enganged, the game has 8 game modes. Including the always popular Deathmatch (FFA & groups) and Assassin, where you have to destroy a certain target, there is the brilliant juggernaut mode!

In Juggernaut it's one against the rest. The juggernaut has multiple times the hitpoints than the others to stand a chance, but still its an uphill battle. Especially if its a sloped map...

In essence: if you want to have tons of fun and like to carefully aim where to shoot a dozen of frogs, this is your game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI found this game through Youtubers playing with their viewers. I saw what the potential desctruction of the weapons could be and that got me excited. My reason for not picking this up sooner was that my friends and I were completely dedicated to this one PvP for a long time (about 2 years). Now we expanded are window for games and play many genres.

I bought this as for myself with the intention of buying the four-pack for my friends, but I wanted to test the waters to see if I would actually stay with this game. No regrets!

I first started out with playing campaign to get the feeling of how to play and the mini games in the campaign have kept me going through my grind to get better weapons. After lvl 14 I tried out servers. The servers in this game feel limited and you can tell that this game is mostly dedicated to playing with a group of friends than Random players. But that didn't stop me to try searching for a match.

About 20-30 mins later I find a lobby that I could get in and we started the match. It wasn't the best first match because it a was grind server butit still had the positives. One being how the other players treated me as a new player: I felt welcomed. The community for this game I feel has such great people to have fun with in All Chat. Keep in mind that not everyone was like this. Other kinds of players that I meet are mostly quiet and don't want you in their games because again, this game feels better with a group of friends already prepared to join in. I don't have an hatred or sadness torwards people like that because it's their choice on how they have fun in ShellShock.

This weekend I'll will try to buy the 4-pack for my friends as a little Christmas Gift from Me to them.\t\t\t\t\t
