\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI loved this game in its prime on kongregate and similar websites and was happy to pick it up for only $7. The additional customization options for matches are wonderful and the skill tree is cool, giving you something to work towards. I do have gripes though.

The singleplayer missions are an absolute slog and boil down to either trial and error or a theme that gives you weak weapons that chip away at the enemy teams screenwide healthbar. (Tear Down This Wall, and Hot Hot Hot are particularly bad offenders) The latter provides absolutely no challenge and is BEYOND tedious. There are also bugs present such as when you attempt to restart a mission while inside it with the button on the top left of the screen the enemy will fire right as you are restarting causing the shot to continue into the next attempt and freeze your character when it gets to your turn. Another bug is one that occurs when exiting a mission after failing, it kicks you to the main menu. If this was intentional I am unsure as to why on earth it wouldn't just take you to the mission select. While I'm at it upon exiting a mission it is a gamble as to whether or not you are going to be kicked to the main menu or to the top of the mission select. (You have to scroll to the section you were on when this happens.) To top off this awful pie, this is the only real way to unlock more weapons and give you a fighting chance in multiplayer matches.

Still a more expanded version of the flash game you loved (or have yet to play.) A patch is desperately needed to greatly change the singleplayer missions or make weapons obtainable through multiplayer matches, OR make all the weapons available on launch.

**As it stands I cannot recommend this game.**\t\t\t\t\t
