\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor an early access game, its fantastic would highly recommend if you are an old classic tanks player (from the macromedia flash days).

The single player missions are a tad bit steep, some levels are pure luck and no skill is involved. So you feel unrewarded when you beat a level, but thankful its over. Only to be presented with 4 other extremely hard levels that pushes you into state of frustration. Which is one of the reasons I stopped playing, as it became a grind rather than a fun playful experience it intitally gives you.

The Multiplayer is fun, it can be unbalanced at times with Huge High level players dropping into your lobby. I can understand why they can. As the playerbase dies they have no where to go. The reasoning to return to Shellshock Live to play another round is pretty limited. You either get stuck at a nearly impossible Sinlge Player mission or you stumble upon lobbys with people setting the requirement of players too high for them to ever get filled. Once filled they are unbalanced uber players that reap all the points and are impossible to kill. The balanced challenge is pretty much removed and turns into a grind than a fun experience.

Overall ShellShock Live is a fun game, would recommend. Thanks developers.\t\t\t\t\t
