\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: Too slow. Singleplayer - meh; Multiplayer - fine, depending on the mode.
Buy only if you have nothing better to play while listening to podcasts/audiobooks.

There are lots of weapons here, game mode customization is great, leveing up is nice, but kinda slow (39h playtime - 56/100 lvl), some customisation, but not much.

But some fundamental problems are really bad. One of them is downtime - you can get killed in first few minutes of the game, and then have to wait 5-50 minutes (depending on game mode) until the round is over. The animations are fun to look at, but at some point it just becomes a waste of time. Also, since you get nothing if you quit the match, after you die you just have to leave it and do something else, or chat.

The community is also horrendous, since the game is a grind, half of the matches are just xp grinds (everyone gathers in the middle and shoot at the ground), while a lot of others are just toxic since the winning team gets way more xp then the losers, then add the ones that get killed first and have to wait while doing nothing (if you get killed without doing any damage and your team loses, you can get literally nothing from 10-60min match), and so you get the frustrated/toxic people.

Some weapons are so op, that if on standard 4v4 everyone gets them, the game can be over in like 2 min. But if everyone gets ♥♥♥♥ weapons, the same game can drag on for 20...

You can only really play this game casually (while watching a stream/podcast, maybe audiobook) or just chill with friends.

Ofc you can go against bots but the game is just too slow to put your 100% attention to.\t\t\t\t\t
