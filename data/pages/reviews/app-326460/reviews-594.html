\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pretty dang fun. It reminds me of a certain old-school Atari game that has the same kind of physics and destruction as Shellshock (Unfortunatly, I can't remember the name of it). The Objective is to destroy the Red tanks. Blue tanks are your allies, so don't shoot them! Pretty simple stuff, to be honest. However, you need to get a grasp of the POWER and ANGLE of your shots to get those skill shots and direct impact shots on your opponents for max damage, which can be kinda difficult if wind is turned on and if there is atmospheric shift turned on. But don't worry! Wind, Atmospheric Shift, and even things like health and weapons can be adjusted or even turned off for beginners or if you just wanna have fun with some friends or other players. It is still a pretty small game, but is being almost constantly updated with new skillshots, obstacles, and weapons. Presentation is amazing, with all the sounds and effects having a lot of "OOMPH" behind them, like your shooting real things from a tank. The weapons range from the straightforward, like the Sniper, to the wacky Banana Split. The gameplay is really basic, the "A" and "D" keys move you left and right, respectively, and the arrow keys determine your power and angle. The graphics, while looking simplistic, are actually really well done (And really shouldn't be an issue in determining if you want to buy a game TBH). Oh, and don't worry about weapon selection, since there is like over 100 weapons in the game. All in all, I'd give this game a 5/5. It is still a young game with a LOT of potential, a VERY fun game to play with friends, a $6.99 price tag that doesn't break the bank, and is getting almost at least one major update a month. I HIGHLY recommend this game! ((I know my review is on a game that I've only played about 10 hours total as of writing this review, but it is that much fun.))\t\t\t\t\t
