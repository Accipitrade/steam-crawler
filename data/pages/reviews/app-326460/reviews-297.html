\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkey, here is my short impression about this game. You should buy it, if you are playing with friends often (but ONLY from different computers, i.e. not using hotseat), if not - I wouldnt really recommend it. Now a bit more in details:
Pros:
1) Really cheap to buy
2) Fun with friends
Cons:
1) So many inadequate random players. This includes people, who constantly insult each others parents (10 year olds, etc.), trolls (intentionally sabotaging the game by just refusing to play or attacking allies), or just very pityful individuals (I dont know how else to call high lvl players who find it normal or even find joy in killing the ones who just got the game).
2) The most terrible report system ever. To be honest, I dont know why they've even implemented it, because it doesn't do anything. In combination with the previous point imagine how fun the game becomes.
3) Random. The chance of your win is about 80% random and 20% is the skill of players. It is a totally common situation, when x2 or x3 damage booster or medkit appears right in front of one of the teams, allowing sometimes to finish the game in 2 or 3 turns. The weapons that you will have in the match are also completely randomized, so you can get a really good stuff or terrible things. Wormholes and energy bumpers make it even crazier.
4) Just some nonsence. As I've said previously, this game is good if you play with friends, yet if you play it from the one computer, using hotseat you wont get any experience and you wouldn't be able to progress in any way possible in the game, which spoils the experience a lot. Also, if you are using gamepad instead of mouse and keyboard you wont be able to collect ingame currency during matches, which leads to the previous result. There is no logical explanation why it is done that way. The worst part about it is that players have been complaining about it for a long time already, yet developers dont seem to react at all.\t\t\t\t\t
