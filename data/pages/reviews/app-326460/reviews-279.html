\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAlthough I loved this game, I can't recommend it unless you get it on a sale or for free.

As it is right now, it has a nice predictable aiming system, but the wind only goes left or right, and has very low impact on low and mid strength, at least compared to similar games.

The weapons are cool, but it takes too long to unlock them all and that may discourage some players to keep playing and get better. Also, it encourages xp farming (more later).

The tank upgrades are alright, but nothing too interesting, you basically want to farm xp to max everything and you can reset them once a week.

But there is the problem, there are not so many people paying and there are at least 2-3 rooms on at all times for xp farming (basically everybody stands in the middle and spams weapons to get xp quickly. Although, there is always 1-2 donkeys trying to ruin it for everybody).

Additionally, the game has no matchmaking or quick play, so you always need to join or create a room. If you win a lot you will frequently get kicked from otherwise pleasant rooms unless you host. This also means that if there are 10 rooms waiting for example, that might be around 20-40 people waiting around instead of being matched together. If you host, you will find that people have very low patience and no desire to play the different modes the game has.

There is an in game currency for visual customization and small victory songs, as well as items, but its way too hard and slow to farm. There are way too few customization option, and they cost too much. Seriously, is it too hard to whip out photoshop or gimp and make some easy and quick vector graphics? Different colored tanks maybe?

The items can give you an advantage in matches but you can also turn them off as the host, and again they are too expensive for the amount of currency you can realistically farm.

Overall, unless you play some games with IRL friends or make high level friends on Steam/Shellchock, most rooms will be without wind, where it is easy to memorize where and how to aim, and basically if you start and have better weapons and/or are in a better position, you win.\t\t\t\t\t
