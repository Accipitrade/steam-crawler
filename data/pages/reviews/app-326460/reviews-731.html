\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was excited when I saw this game in the shop. A spiritual successor to scorched earth. Finally a replacement for pocket-tanks?

It could have been.

The leveling-system is doing more harm than good. I don't even know why anyone thought it would be a good idea in the first place - at least in the way it is implemented now. If it was only in place for online multiplayer games or even better as a completely seperate game mode for online multiplayer (an "open" online MP without leveling benefits being the other mode) maybe it could have been a worthwhile addition - but everything in the game is built around it. There is no offline-multiplayer and the single player vs. AI-skirmishes are hardlocked to a zero-XP-multiplier (why the singleplayer-tag in the shop? remove it, if singleplayer is not a focus!) to force players into online.

Was an instabuy for me. Sadly, now it is insta-refund. What a shame.
Will consider re-buy and change this review in case this changes at some point in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
