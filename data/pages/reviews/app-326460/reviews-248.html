\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is an amazing game right from the start. It takes the classic DOS trajectory games and pumps them up with over 400 unique weapons. It immediately took me back in time to 1992 and chucking my first banana as a gorilla across the screen at my friend's opposing gorilla. Or the time I played the tanks version of the game a few months later and he decimated me because it was his PC and he had the whole summer to practice and learn how the trajectories vs. power system worked. The simple formula has been tried and true through at least 3 decades now and the fun has not let up a bit.

It features Single player mode, Couch Co-op and Couch Competition with hotseat, and Online Multiplayer with Remote Play Together compatability. It is literally playable with anyone anywhere.

Anyone who has ever gotten into these kinds of games knows the basic formula, if you haven't given this type of game a try you are missing out on a bountifully rewarding game system. I would recommend this game for anyone of any age group, especially those who have given anything in the WORMS franchise a good amount of time. It seems a bit more simplistic, and its game systems are, but with a level up system that progressively makes your tanks more useful, ten times as many weapon choices to unlock, and an extremely vibrant and active online community, on top of having a much lower base price, this game is an automatic win in my book. Solid 10 for a score, This is a game that ticks every box and should be owned by everyone with a PC.\t\t\t\t\t
