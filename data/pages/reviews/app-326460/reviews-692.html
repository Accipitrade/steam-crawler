\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I got this game because I saw it on the store, and it looked amazing. And a bit of selling things on the Community Market and a few days crying because for a while I was 8 cents away from having the cash to get this (made worse by the fact that I don't even have a job) later, I got it. But is it as good as I was hoping, or was it complete trash (just like me)?

Well, spoiler alert, this game is amazing.

This game has a huge variety of weapons and weapon upgrades. And they are all useful (except the Shot)! However, some of them might be a bit too good (like the exploding Bounce Shot, which is nearly a direct upgrade to the normal Bounce Shot). Also, some of the weapons are references to other games (like Mad Birds), or are just hillarious (like a Cactus Airstrike or Chicken Fling), and those things are always nice.

The gameplay is simple, just move a bit, aim, and fire in either turn based combat or turn based combat where everybody goes at the same time. However, it works really well, even if I suck at aiming.

The campaign can be fun, however it is extremely challenging, and can sometimes feel unfair.

The game looks amazing, even on low graphics (which is what I use)

Overall, this game is amazing, and I would recommend it to anybody who thinks it looks interesting and/or has 7 dollars to spare.\t\t\t\t\t
