\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery simple and yet enjoyable game. Fun by yourself and even better with friends. I find that although I love this game and have played it for several hundred hours, I have two main issues.

First and foremost, it is highly problematic that the developer of the game (KChamp) is by himself in producing the game, and so updates are few and far between. After a few hundred hours of no update you can imagine it gets a little stale, and until there is an update I have decided to move on to other things.

My other issue is to do with the level curve. Reaching 1 000 000 EXP (a little past level 100) equates to a pretty much maxed account. Doing this, however, usually takes approx. 1000 hours in a high-EXP environment. I find this to be a little excessive, and I personally can't justify spending that long grinding out what is, at its core, a 2D tank game that has obvious limits to its replayability. Also, the halfway point to level 100 is level 95, making the last five levels extremely tedious to pass through.

All things set aside I have recommended this game to multiple friends, and it can be a great way to spend a hundred or so hours with its very laid back yet competitive gameplay.

6.5/10.\t\t\t\t\t
