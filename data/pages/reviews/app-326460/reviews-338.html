\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a very fun game to play, the upgrade mechanics and progression are what really sold the game to me. Loads of weapons to unlock, all with unique styles and damage values. There is some math involved in the game as well, as the player can calculate where the shot would land based on the angle, power and wind values, and while this may seem complicated at first, the more you practice the more you will learn.

That being said, the game gets very very grindy, I have played about 400 hours, and I'm only level 92, unlocked a lot of weapons, but I'm still nowhere near max level. The xp required to advance to the next level is exponential, and it takes the same amount of xp to get from level 90 to 100 as it takes to get from 1 to 90, which seems a bit too much for me. Also, some weapons like Counter 6k require way too much xp to unlock, for a weapon that does hardly any damage even when fired correctly. I feel like less used weapons like the Counter and Guppies should be rebalanced slightly, as their max level versions aren't exactly different from their previous upgrades, and not fun at all to unlock.

However, I am still enjoying the game, the "collect it all" aspect is very fun, and overall it's a very positive experience. I am really looking forward to the 1.0 update, I'm excited to see what kchamp has added to the game.\t\t\t\t\t
