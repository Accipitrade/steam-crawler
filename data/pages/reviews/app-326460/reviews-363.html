\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA decent game to pass the time, reminds me of worms from years ago but this requires a lot more skill and planning.
The main differences for this game are the fact your weapons are entirely randomised at the start of the game, you do not start with the same weapons as your opponents, and as you level up you unlock more weapons, and using said weapons then gives you upgrades. This truly is a game that you progress by playing, there is an in game shop that runs on gears (not microtransactions I might add). You can purchase cosmetics (tank parts, victory music) as well as a small amount of shop only weapons), but nothing is ridiculously OP.

Other reviews say the community is very toxic but bar the odd bit of swearing from someone who is stroppy because you sniped them through a 2x damage or instantly dug them into a hole at round 1 the community has been fine.

What I like about this game is it requires a fair amount of skill. It is not forgiving of those who do not learn. There is a very good map variety and with 200 weapons you have no shortage or variation game to game.
While it is not a game i can play for hours on end it is perfect for a chilled out 30 mins to pass the time.
At least until someone gets lucky and drops a beacon on your head!

P.S Make sure to get your daily, its worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
