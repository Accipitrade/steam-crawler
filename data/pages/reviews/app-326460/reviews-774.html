\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEvery battle is largely based on luck.

From what weapons you get vs. what weapons your enemies get, to how those very weapons behave is usually up to RNG. Even if you are highly skilled and can hit 90% of your shots, it does not mean you'll win or even necessarily do well in any particular match.

There are weapons which require 100% accuracy and do terrible damage, while there are AoE weapons that you can miss by a large margin and still end up doing significant damage to the opponent. Then there are those weapons which can do as little as 5 damage, or as much as 150 - depending on how the weapon feels like behaving (Area Strike, and Snake to name two).

Then there's crits. Why are crits in a "skill" based artillery game? I have no idea. It just adds another layer of RNG to the mix which makes things unsatisfying. There's nothing like outskilling an opponent for 5 minutes, only to have him land a lucky crit or two and completely take your off the map. Factor in the random 2x Damage buffs you can get in battle, and this problem becomes significantly worse (I've seen people do 100 damage with a 2x Damage crit, using weapons that don't require any aim whatsoever. I.e Deadweight)

The large selection of weapons is cool, but half of them are very gimmicky and some just down-right underperform. Many of the weapons you start with and unlock by level 10 are the most practical.

Which brings me to the leveling system. Why. Why is this here in a tank game? It's bad enough that everything is so random, that you have to go ahead and add a grind to it? What's even more funny, is that considering your weapon selection is random, the more weapons you unlock the worse your chances become of getting the GOOD weapons. Once you reach level 40+, your inventory is going to be filled with garbage on the regular.

I've seen level 50s lose to level 2s because the level 50s got nothing but GARBAGE weapons the entire match, while the level 2s were repeatedly given high damage easy shots (Sinkhole, Airstrike, Sniper) because their overall selection is so much more limited.

The game overall is okay for a match here and there, as there can be some funny moments, but if you're looking for a game you actually want to get good at and invest time in, you should look somewhere else.\t\t\t\t\t
