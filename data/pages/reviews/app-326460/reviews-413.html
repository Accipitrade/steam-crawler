\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs someone who went from SSL1 to SSL2 to here I must say that sadly the heyday of Shell Shock Live has come and gone.


Unlike its internet hosted counterparts this version has become a shell of its former self. They had a nice idea removing most of the paywall on good weapons by removing most of them from the pay exclusive pool, and made them relatively easy to gain with in-game rewards, but that's about it. The people in-charge got a little ambitious and did something to the aiming system, that I see as nothing short of a complete cluster♥♥♥♥. So if you were like me and maxed out your account on ssl1 and ssl2, on say Kongregate, and came here expecting to surprise higher levels with your aim, you will, and are sorely mistaken.

Unfortunately this versions greatest boon is also its greatest weakness. Taking away the ability to buy weapons and replacing them with weapons you unlock was great. Until your average player base was above level 50 and the community shifted from playing the game to grinding out experience. This has made the game not only unplayable as a new person (I get kicked from 80% of my games for being 'too low of a level' at level 54), but downright unenjoyable as you have a tank with no perks, no powerful weapons, against players that have as much shields as you have health, with weapons that can 1-shot you. Their workaround for when it became dead or just wasn't fun was a campaign mode that I beat all of, excluding the final boss, at levels 1-25 as they are all relatively easy if you aren't a rock.

No I'm afraid the game is dead for new players. If you want the satisfying tank experience, look elsewhere.\t\t\t\t\t
