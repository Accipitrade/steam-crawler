\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played shellshock live 2 on the browser for quite a while before I played the steam version. One thing I did not like in the browser version is in game purchases which they took out in the steam version. So I am really happy about that. It gives everyone an even ground and provides fair combat.

I love the steam version and would recommend it to anyone having an interest in artillery games.
Here is the pros and cons for shellshock live.

PROS:

1. Simple yet elegant graphics design. One does not get bored looking at it.

2. The massive amounts of weapons to obtain and upgrade. At the time I wrote this review, there are 367 weapons including the upgrades.

3. Active developer and community. The developer works with the player community to constantly improve the game with new content, new features or bug fixes.

4. Many game modes to choose from ( 8 I believe). With countless customization options that will satisfy every need. For example, you want to play with players your level, you can do that, you want a more fun and challenging game play, you can set mods to one weapons and wind to high etc.

5. 4 sections of tank upgrades to master!

5. And many more!

CONS?

1. Time consuming. Is it? I have played for 60 hours and I am already level 83. I know the game is a grind, but isn't every multiplayer game? For example, I played warframe for 360 hours, or grinded for 360 hours. Shellshock live in my opinion is a grind, yes, but compared to some other "popular" games out there, it is not a grind at all. I know low level is frustrating and everyone wants to level up, everyone wants to win. But be patient and you will eventually reach level 80 in 100 hours. Besides, the real grind starts at level 90. Unless you're hardcore and want to get every single achievement. Which I advise to not to do if you want a social life... Don't make such a big deal out of it!

2. The music gets repetitive after a while and sounds boring, but that's not a big deal. Just run some music in the background or something.

3. Hackers? This is also a big deal according to a lot of people. IT IS NOT! I have yet to see a hacker in the 60 hours i played. And besides, the latest updates reinforced security to eliminate the piratically non-existent hackers. And also, people complain about rulers......... yes, there are people who use rulers, but there are multiple ways you can take care of this. First, play a game with them, and lose, then set atmospheric nudge to ON, or set high wind, and then play the next game, and then accuse them of using a ruler, and they will be too ashamed to admit it and leave... and yeah.



Overall, shellshock live is a really fun and addictive game to pass the extra times you have. There is so much to do, so much to learn and so much rage when you miss a shot.... ayyyy.
GET IT! YOU WON'T REGRET IT!!\t\t\t\t\t
