\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has captivated my interests for many hours on end, so it goes without saying it's quite the time-waster. It's a highly enjoyable tank game and it's quite easy to gain XP and levels fast. More levels grant you better weapons, better weapons grant you even mightier ways to conquor your enemies. However, as you dwell deeper into this game you begin to notice more and more faults plaguing such a sleek game. Some of the singleplayer missions are so difficult it's almost completely up to luck and repetition to pass (Fiesta Forever, I'm talking to you!). On occasion the game glitches out and you lose your progress in a mission/game that you were currently partaking in, seemingly with no explanation at all. This problem is actually what prompted me to write this review, I was almost done defeating the final singleplayer mission when I was whisked away back to the homescreen to join the charade of protests from the other players. As with the majority of unmoderated games certain trolls lie in the shadows waiting to ruin your game (and your day, if you let them).

Even excluding the listed problems, sometimes this game just gets too annoying to handle. If you perservere through all the pain and agony though, you'll eventualy unlock amazing weapons like the bouncy ball. Which bounces around an excruciatingly painful amount of times before finally bouncing out of the screen or if you're extremely lucky (and I'm talking about a one in a billion chance, here) dealing a minuscule amount of damage. This game may have it's share of brain-bashing, I'm-about-to-jump-off-a-cliff moments, but it also has it's enjoyable ones. After dedicating countless hours to this game, even getting kicked out of my final boss battle (as previously stated) I'm still going to go back and finish it. I have that undescribable need to finish what I've started, even if It'll be my eventual downfall. So if you are also willing to invest in this game, be prepared to get mad. Be prepared to get angry. Overall though, be prepared to never hit anyone with that damn bouncy ball.\t\t\t\t\t
