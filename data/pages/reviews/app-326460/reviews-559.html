\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game is fun, but just prepared to be disappointted after a while. The game becomes a chore and you'll only get that feeling of accomplishment 1 of every 10 games.

Fun at first, then turns to cancer as leveling up isn't worth the effort. Past level 70 it takes at least three hours of game play to level up (with +2 hours for every level after). The community is utter garbage online. Consistent mockery at all levels, and yes I know online is cancer already, but considering you may miss a shot be mocked and get treated the same by teammates and enemies alike, it just gets under your skin eventually. (Reporting system is usless fyi, only just mutes reported players. Not abusive ones)

Useless or overpowered upgrade system. You can upgrade your tank's luck, damage, armor, and fuel. Only that luck is next to useless. Other than the grapple upgrade, luck has no recognizable effect on your tank or the care packages you may pick up. Crits are overpowered, doubling the damage of weapons that are already overpowered. For example, the meganuke does 80 damage (doesn't sound like much until you realize you only have 300 health), crits double that to 160 so that's more than half your health gone. If the shooter hits a double damage (double the crit damage to 320) you just wasted your time joining the game.

An unspoken issue is targeting. You will join a lobby for a fun match and be targeted by every player (either team or FFA) and die in the first 3 rounds with little to no xp. Generally, weaker you are, more tempting to kill you earlier on. Ruins the fun aspect of the game as you are forced to quit rather than sit through a game that will last 15 turns longer (30 minutes - 1 hour) but get little to no xp for your trouble.

Tournaments are pointless. There is no huge reward for winning them and there is no level playing field in them. You can join one and be paired against a level 100 as a level 10. Literally no point trying.

Matches are not based on skill but placement. If RNG spawning puts you in the center of the map, well just leave and join another game. If you are placed in the corners, congrats you have a high chance of winning. But again, its all RNG. Usually players dont mind stepping on you to claim the corner for themselves, which just hurts you as enemies have objection to shooting you both for more points (again disgusting community).

The greatest bummer of all is the 100 rule. You will quickly learn there is no skill to this game. Rather by using a 100 power shot and an angle between 66 (full map), 79 (half map), and 86 (target radius) you basically hit any shot you want without effort. So much for "may the best man win" eh?
