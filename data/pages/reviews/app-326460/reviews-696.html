\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA couple big issues that people are overlooking:
  1. As you level up, you can upgrade your tank and get stronger armor, do more damage, etc. This leads to unfair advantages and unbalanced gameplay because everyone's upgrade levels are not the same. You also unlock new weapons as you level up, making you do more damage than your friends if you are higher level.
  2. You earn XP from winning games with your friends. Yes, you read that correctly. This means you can easily boost with friends to level yourself up by telling them to purposely let you win. You might think "Hey, that means the level differences you said earlier don't matter then if you can just boost!" And to that, I say, I shouldn't have to grind XP by exploiting the system in order to have a fair and balanced game. I don't want the XP to be the decider of who won or lost before the game even starts. That's, well, unfair.
  3. Lastly, "bumpers" spawn randomly and asymmetrically at the start of each turn. This means your shot could be completely blocked off by a random obstacle at any time. Sometimes, they even spawn around you, ensuring you damage yourself no matter how you place the shot.

All in all, a very unbalanced game reliant on XP and grinding.\t\t\t\t\t
