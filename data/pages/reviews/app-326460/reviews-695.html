\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShellShok Live is like worms... with tanks.

Pros:
+ Interesting gamemodes
+ Some cool weapons

Cons:
- Most maps are boring as hell
- It's unbalanced. It's way too easy to get others in a spot where they're barely able to move
or aim. Now all you need to do is spamming all you've got in the some direction. Sometimes
the game itself may put you in such a spot right from the start.
- I've only been playing a few times and yet, I feel like I have seen everything already - no
longterm motivation

Conclusion:
If you play it with friends, it can be entertaining for a few hours. BUT your money is better spend on Worms.
