- the game is a total GRIND. After going through many campaign missions I didn't manage to upgrade even a single weapon.... Skirmishes against AI give almost no XP. Tank development is therefore no fun at all.
- It would not be so bad if the PvP was fun but... many players establish matches only to grind experience instead of having good fun or competition. Again - grind destroys everything.
- The single campaign is full of random/luck dependent missions - not having anything to do with skill. And you must finish every single irritating mission to progress further
- The weapons themselves are ok but seem to be less spectacular than in old Scorched Earth. The map ("dirt") physics is not spectacular either (it does not "collapse" properly and weapons dont do spectacular damage to it).

The game can be still rescued - first thing to do would be getting rid of this ridiculous grinding and giving players possibility to develop/XP quicker to have fun.\t\t\t\t\t
