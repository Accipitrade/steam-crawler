\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really love this game. The tactical element really makes the game for me, making it fun and relaxing to play at pretty much anytime. I find progression in this game to be extremely rewarding with weapons and map variations keeping the game entertaining despite some of the higher xp targets. I do think there is a bit of a steep learning curve that takes sometime to get past but as long as you don't mind playing some single player missions to practice it's not that big of a deal. I know a lot of people argue that the community is the biggest negative and I can't disagree. I haven't played in a lot of public lobbies since I play with my friends however the public ones I experienced haven't been bad. That said I would take the word of more experienced players over mine when it comes to public lobbies. In my opinion the game is perfect for when you and a friend want to play a non-serious game together and the low price really helps with this appeal. I genuinely recommend this game to all of my friends. It's definitely becoming one of my favourites.\t\t\t\t\t
