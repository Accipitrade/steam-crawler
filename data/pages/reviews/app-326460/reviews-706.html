\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShellShock Live is not just a simple 2D Tank Destruction Game, it's all about tactics and skill! Fighting with or against your friends has rarely been this exciting.. The huge variety of weapons, maps and game modes make ShellShock one of the most entertaining strategic Action Games I've played in a long time.

Weapons:
There are hundreds of different weapons which can be individually upgraded by using them and dealing damage.
Each weapon is different to the other, so you'll never have the feeling of monotony..
Every weapons has it's own behaviour, so it needs a lot of practice to master them all.

Modes:
Of course there's the simple Deathmatch where you destroy other tanks.. but there are a lot of other modes such as Marksman, Rebound, "Football" and more. All of them are pretty fun and special in their personal way.
There's also some Singleplayer Missions you'll have to master to get XP and cool weapons. ^^

Upgrades:
With playing you will level up your tank and while doing so you upgrade your weapons and with each earned skill point you can spend them on the skill tree to gain special abilities fitting your playstyle. The higher you level the more you can get obviously. :D

Gears?
You'll also gather Gears alongside your playthrough (you can also get them with daily wheelspins) and with them you can purchase exclusive weapons, maps, music kits or tank parts to increase the variety of your gameplay. :)


For the cheap price you get a action-loaded, strategic game which will entertain you for quite a loong time.. so go ahead!

Start destroying some tanks already..
