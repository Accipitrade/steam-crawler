\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCool game. Brings back memories of Scorched Earth, albeit with MUCH better graphics, but with one or two absent mechanics for the sake of streamlining. That's fine I guess.

There's online multiplayer. Yay, I get to piss off my friends in yet another environment, what fun. The community's slightly toxic, but you're on Steam, so that shouldn't come as any surprise, but it certainly hits you from the get-go since there's a public chat room on the main menu and the profanity filter is off by default.

There IS a single player campaign, but you have to be online to get to it anyway, so it seems to be more the thing that people do for the unlockables rather than the gameplay, which seems kind of silly as a design choice.
The mechanics are simple and intuitive, the weapons are too (for the most part), there's plenty of unlockables.
There's also a level up system that I cannot for the life of me figure out why it's here but to stagger weapon unlocks, which I suppose is an okay way of doing things, but there's a huge achievement list that also gets used to unlock weapons and maps, and it really just makes me wonder why that wasn't expanded instead of trying to have it both ways.

All in all, decent game.\t\t\t\t\t
