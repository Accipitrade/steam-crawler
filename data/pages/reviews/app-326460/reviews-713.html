\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEasy to learn, hard to master.

The first few multiplayer matches tend to be overwhelming to newer players since you lack upgrades and potent weapons, but it is circumvented with single player missions which grant xp.

Battles can be simple and straight-forward with turns on, or just a complete cluster♥♥♥♥ of explosions and bright flashes if turns are off.

Despite being a selling point, there isnt much you can customize. You can change treads and chassis, which all look the same at a glance. You can also get victory music and other stuff from the ingame shop.

I like the fact that you must pick up ingame currency, which spawns randomly out of explosions.

Overall very good and suprisingly well polished despite early access.

9/10.\t\t\t\t\t
