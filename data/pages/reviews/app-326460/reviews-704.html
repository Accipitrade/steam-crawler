\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy Shellshock experience thus far:

>Start up game.
>Friend says "Do the campaign first, you'll start off at level 40."
>Do campaign up until Fiesta Forever, Hot Hot Hot, and Final Boss (All of which are RNG dependant.)
>Go into multiplayer at level 34.
>Have a bunch of garbage tier 1 trash weapons. Basic shot, Boomerang, etc.
>Crate spawns next to me.
>Drive over to crate, pick it up.
>Get a Mega-Nuke, a Snowstorm, and a Cobra from this crate.
>Waste all three because I had no idea wtf I was doing.
>Chat is spammed with "Nice shot".
>Shellshock servers disconnect me.

11/10 would waste Mega-nuke again and have the shellshock servers spite me for it.

Seriously though, this game is great. Infinitely better than the flash version/s. Absolutely worth the 7$ for the hundreds of gameplay hours you can scrounge out of it. Shoot popcorn at people. Shoot sneks at people. Shoot MOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOOONS at people. There are even Mad birds (Yes, that's an angry birds reference, but don't tell anyone.) which just so happen to upgrade to Livid birds. They're liv-id.

I've only ever had one problem with this game. The servers... The servers are dreadful and will disconnect you mid-game before you can blink an eye. They don't struggle all the time, and the servers are always being added upon, but the problem still persists and leaves a sour taste in my mouth every time I'm 15 minutes into a game and I get disconnected.

All in all, I'll give it a 9/10 for replayability, being heavily polished, having beautiful particle effects, along with not needing an outstanding internet connection or computer to play (you can turn all those fancy particle effects off of course.) The community is generally pretty tame and enjoyable to be around. If only Kyle Champ would get on so I can get the stupid achievement for beating him, that would be gggrrrreeeaaat.

Buy it. Play it. Enjoy it.\t\t\t\t\t
