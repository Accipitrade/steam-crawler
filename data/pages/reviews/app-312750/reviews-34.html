\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the first Final Fantasy game I have played, and it was an exemplary experience! This port has next to no technical issues, and as a remake the game is preserved from what I can tell of the original SNES version. The characters are all interesting and have arcs, as well as some voice acting. The combat has an auto mode to help grind, and there are many tactical options for neutralizing enemies. The OST is phenomenal, it thoroughly add enough emotion to the scenes. There are some plot points that are lackluster in the middle of the game, but overall the plot is fascinating and easy to understand. Additionally, there is a new game plus mode and you can choose between wait and active combat. This game is the foundation that all Final Fantasy games are based on, and it is a strong foundation indeed!\t\t\t\t\t
