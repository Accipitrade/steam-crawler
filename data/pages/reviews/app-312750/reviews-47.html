\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEver since this game came out as Final Fantasy II for the SNES (in the US), it has been a favorite of mine. I have gone back to it time and time again. The music had me entranced from the first moment I started a new game. The story from the first moments on the airship of Cecil's return from Baron you get the sense that things began before you even started playing, and held me until the very end.

This version incorporates more story than before, explaining things that I had been wanting to find ot for quite some time How did Cecil come to be raised by the King of Baron? How did Cecil meet Kain? The name of Kain's father.

The difficulty was also increased, which is something I approve of, even if it did drive me nuts at times. Having played the Original release in the States and the Playstation and GBA ports it was satisfying to defeat a troublesome boss rather than deal with more of the same.

All in all, I'd rate this game as a 9.5 out of 10\t\t\t\t\t
