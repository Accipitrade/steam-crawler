\r\n\t\t\t\t\tServicable and easily accessible. The DS version is mostly better due to having the dual screen for the map and the Whyt content, but they're more or less the same. This is a bit janky for a port, but it also lets you select normal mode as opposed to forcing you into the DS difficulty. I was never much a fan for how much stronger normal enemies were in the DS/hard mode versions of this game.

FF4 is a interesting beast because it doesn't have a definitive version. The DS remake is the best version of the DS remake, but the GBA port is easily the best of the 2d variants. If you've never played FF4 I would recommend checking out the GBA port first. I don't think its better or worse than the DS remake but I certainly think the augment system really changes the gameplay overall. DS always felt like it was designed for players experienced with FF4, and that's fine and I like it. The augment system is great! But, this isn't really a review of FF4: it's a review of the steam port. The steam port is meh. Servicable and easily available, which alone is enough to recommend but, if you can, play the DS port instead. You'll have a better time.\t\t\t\t\t
