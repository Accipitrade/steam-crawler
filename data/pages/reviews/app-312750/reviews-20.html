\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt IS Final Fantasy IV, but this remake is pretty bad. There are no filtering options, and the game was very obviously ported from a cheap mobile game. The battles run at 15 fps. Fifteen fps. The character models are made with as few polygons as they could get away with using. I would have felt a lot better if they would have simply ported the PSP version of this game (which is by FAR the best version imho). HOWEVER...the music remains strong, and the new voice acting is mostly a pretty great improvement. But everything else is just extremely cheaply done, the menus, the models, the graphics in this game just make it feel like this project was sold to the lowest bidder instead of being the amazing game it deserves to be. That all said, the game itself is still quite challenging, especially on hard mode. The 3d graphics feel very zoomed in here, and it adds to the difficulty by introducing claustrophobia while exploring the caves in this game. There is a map system but if you play on hard mode, you still have to be pretty careful.

If this game ran at full 60 fps and had some smooth filtering and better menus, I could easily recommend it, but as it is while I am playing it, its honestly far too rough around the edges, at least on larger screens. It may be ok for smaller screens but I wouldn't recommend playing this on larger screens until they push out some better quality of life patches, if that ever happens. 15 fps voxel graphics in 2021 is laughable. I could add that the same team seems to have done the Final Fantasy 3 remake and that won was much better overall. They dropped the ball with this version as far as I am concerned.

If I could offer up fixes, this is what I would do, first make the whole game run in at LEAST 30 fps, 60 would be fantastic. Add filtering options to smooth out the sharp edges of the pixels. Clean up the character models and monsters, these are an absolute mess in my opinion and deserve more time spent on their creation. I almost would have preferred the original pixel art to these 3d models. Lastly, zoom out the screen a bit away from the land, things in this game feel REALLY zoomed in and it drive me crazy while playing this version.

TLDR:

This is a mobile port. If you do want to play this version of FF IV, its a good challenge, but definitely an experience tailored specifically for smaller screens.

If you have the chance to play the PSP version of FF IV + The After Years, I would HIGHLY recommend playing that one over this remake.\t\t\t\t\t
