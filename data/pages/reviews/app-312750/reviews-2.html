\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuch a beautiful FF game that got me hooked more then expected on its story and characters. I recommend this to any XIV players who played trough Endwalker post-MSQ and who are looking into older games in the franchise or simply newcomers or JRPG fans.

The journey itself make this more then worth playing and might be just me but the DS 3D graphics give it lot of charms.
Plot itself is well-paced, character-driven with many memorable scenes.

The combat being ATB (Active Time Battle) might be hit or miss for some but once you get the hang of it will make your adrenaline rush when you need to think in split second on what action to use for all of your party members.

OST is as expected for a FF game nothing more to add other then beautiful

There are also some cons to be mentioned.

While the game does not require any crazy grind you might need to go back and grind a bit so the difficulty spike wont hit you in the face later In my playthrough when I reach the moon I started to get my ass kicked pretty hard... so I went back did some side quest got myself some better equipment and more levels so everything was fine after, was not the kind of grind that felt like a chore and if you are into completionism well that might get a bit painful and a lot more grind also needing to complete the game three time in total...
Another issue is that the game is not really download and play... you might need to apply some sound fix so it does not sound bugged... also fps fix to run the combat in more then 15fps and there is a font mod that makes the game just better.

Still even with these issues is a definitely worth experience if you are a JRPG enjoyer.\t\t\t\t\t
