\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Good:
Action-heavy story which carries the intensity throughout the game.
Great voice acting.
Lots of interesting characters which make you emotionally invest in their stories.
NPC dialogue that isn't boring.
The bosses in the game, while sometimes punishingly difficult, even after leveling up, always had some sort of trick you could work out on how to beat them. But, not something super easy to guess, and can be beaten with or without using the trick if you always play smart, and use the right spells and attacks.
Extremely well hidden side quests which encourage exploration.
While this game's graphics were never top-notch for PC, (this was built for the DS). The models are all very individualized, and have a style which evokes the old-school nature of the game, but are cute and timeless.

The Bad:
Characters are locked into 1 job type and not very customizable
A bit grindy towards the end of the game.
You have no control over the constant times the party rotated throughout the story, which are all necessary to the plot, but I never felt like I had a cohesive team. And you never have more members than the 5 you can bring into combat.

The Meh:
The battle system is a bit basic for anyone who is familiar with final fantasy, but it wasn't boring either. Sometimes if felt a bit repetitive, but the battles were quick most of the time.
While the character models are well done, some of the textures are a bit boring to look at.
I wish they had done more with Rose and Kain's stories, they feel like they're just along for the ride.\t\t\t\t\t
