\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHello friends! I just beaten FFIV for the first time, so i will explain about the pros and cons of this game. NOTE: This comparision will be with the Super Nintendo version of the game, since this is the version i played last.

First off, this version has 3d graphics, which improves on the graphic style of the original game. They also added voices and cutscenes. The voices are not great, but there not the worst i ever heard either. The graphics are definetly a improvement over the super nintendo graphics version. They also have a bunch of achievements. Some are quite hard to get. In fact, some involve playing the game twice. There are also missable things in the game. Make sure to consult some guides before playing the game to make sure to find everything in the game.

Anyways, here is my pro/con list:

PROS:

~New graphics upgrade.
~Voice acting and cutscenes.
~Achievements.
~Hidden items and other things.
~Developer room.
~Airships

CONS:

~Slowdowns on fighting with monsters. This happens quite often. Even if the battle is set to auto, it still slows down to 30FPS.
~The voice acting is not great, but it is not the worst i have heard.
~Some of the achievements take a long time to get. There is even one where you have to play the game twice. Alot of the achievements require lots and lots and lots of grinding.

So in the end,

I recommend the game, but only if your a final fantasy fan.If not, i suggest buying when it is on sale and try it out to see if you like it. This game is not for everyone, and requires some grinding, so be aware of this fact.\t\t\t\t\t
