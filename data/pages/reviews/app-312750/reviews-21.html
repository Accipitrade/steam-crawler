\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the remake of Final Fantasy IV. It is the port from DS version with enhanced graphic, 3D Chibi-style, nice opening movie and voice acting during major scenes.

Pro:
The augment system allows you to assign abilities from friends and foes alike to your party members. It provides some creativity in game play along with adding replay value – you can replay one save file to collect everything up to three times.

Con:
There is the issue about difficulty some players complain about. Personally, it is more about how much you are familiar with the game.
But fear not, the game offers you to select game mode between normal and hard. The hard one will really test your knowledge about mechanic in this game. While the normal mode, from my experience, is player-friendly, so you can casually enjoy the game without worry about the difficulty.

Definitely a game worth playing. Also, if you are familiar or used to play the original version, there are some added scenes which complement overall story too. You should give this version a try.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy personal favourite Final Fantasy game. Most people view Final Fantasy 7 as the starting point for the Final Fantasy franchise mainly at the time for its graphics, game play and its timeless story and characters. I somewhat agree to this however, for me I believe the true starting point of when Final Fantasy took off was with Final Fantasy 4. The main reason I say this is because all of the Final Fantasy games prior to this, had laughably bad story telling. This game introduced lore, character development and genuine plot twists that kept the plot engaging. Consequently, this began the revelation of telling stories within video games which only got better as the franchise progressed (excluding Final Fantasy 5 & 8).

In comparison to other Final Fantasy games, it doesn't have the strongest developed characters or story (that is not to say that the story isn't good). What it does strive in is its game play. This is the only Final Fantasy game where I feel the game actually gives me a challenge through enemy encounters and bosses. For me, the difficulty is really balanced and I view is as being not too hard and not too easy. If difficulty is not your thing, there's an added feature to this version of the game where you can select an easier difficulty compared to the original difficulty which some may consider hard. Additionally, another added feature in this version is an auto-save feature which triggers when you enter a new area or before an enemy encounter trigger. What this means is that if you fail in combat, you can easily continue with the auto-save right where you were in comparison with the previous version (Nintendo DS) where you had to reload a save from your most recent save point.

Throughout the story your party will constantly change with different characters with different classes/roles. What I find intricate about this is that the game forces you to adapt and change your strategy as you progress in comparison to just having this character do this role, this character does that role, until the end of the game. This keeps the strategy of combat fresh as well make your party dynamic interesting as you progress. You may argue that having this design philosophy limits a character greatly compared to Cloud from Final Fantasy 7 who can be and do whatever what he wants. Each to their own I suppose, but I feel having someone who can do anything makes the other party members kind of redundant and doesn't show much of their identity.

The game is decently long and will take about 20-30 hours on your first play through to complete. You can do new game+ which allows you to carry some of your items to a new play through. Although, I wouldn't recommend it as the only new content you can access are the two super bosses which take a lot of time to prepare for (not worth it). From my playtime I spent 20 hours finishing the story, then 65 hours of repetitive playthroughs and grinding to acquire the best equipment and summons needed to unlock the achievements.

If you are looking for challenging but fun RPG, I highly recommend giving this game a try.

9/10\t\t\t\t\t
