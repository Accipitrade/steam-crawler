\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA classic in a somewhat crappy 3D coat. Played this version it on the 3DS too and I had no issue with this port's playability, either.

One of the biggest things I think I enjoyed about replaying this game several times, is the feeling of nostalgia it gives me, especially all the music. Back then this game was amazing for actually having more of a story, and to have characters interact more and all that stuff. But in today's day and age where RPGs have come a long way I don't think that it's really held up well in terms of the plot. I'd go into detail as to why, but I don't wanna give spoilers. I also don't think the 3DS's 3D graphics are aging well so far, either. I honestly much prefer the look of the original version of this game.

Still, it's not a bad game and still one I love despite the meh plot and this version's crappy 3D graphics. The gameplay and turn-based combat is a blast and I still think it's definitely a worthy title in the main series, no matter which version. it's just not the best one. But it's more than well worth playing if you're a fan of the Final Fantasy games and have never played this one yet. I have yet to check out the pixel remaster so I can't say which one should be your go to between the two though.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFinest example of the classic Japanese Role Playing Game genre - High fantasy medieval setting with magic, monsters, and an expansive world with side quests aplenty. The turn-based combat is challenging and rewarding to master, all of it especially enriched by the Active Time Battle system which introduces a sense of urgency to the strategic nature of battles making for a very fun experience. All of the characters have their own unique skills and set of strengths and weaknesses. The graphics are simply great; colorful world, characters and surprisingly expressive models that reflect the old-school sprite based roots while re-imagining and giving new life nails the presentation. The story is a fantastical tale of escalating stakes and unexpected twists. Although the plot can feel a bit trope-ish at times it is endearingly at home with the JRPG and Final Fantasy games we all know and love. Would recommend to any Role Playing Game enthusiasts or Final Fantasy fans wanting to experience a classic in the series!\t\t\t\t\t
