Great addition to my collection, and well worth the playthrough.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis really is a terrific entry into the FF series. While I've never played the original SNES version (and thus cannot state definitively what is the best way to play this game), I can wholeheartedly recommend this adaptation.

The story is quite strong. Yes, it has its silly and campy moments, but it is driven by characters with deep narratives and clear motives. The battle system is terrific, offering a unique 5-member party. Each character has a special ability that is unique to them in addition to regular battle commands like White Magic, Items, etc.

I was very pleased by the addition of Hard Mode. It is extremely challenging, but not to the point where you'll get frustrated and quit.

The soundtrack is phenomenal, though I would advocate for one of the mods that inserts the original SNES version. I don't hate the soundtrack this comes with, but I prefer the synth patches and style of the original.
