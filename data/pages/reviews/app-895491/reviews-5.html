\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had a great time playing this episode! You can tell within the first 30 minutes of game play that its going to be different, but in a REALLY good way. I actually felt like I was playing more of the game this time and not in a series of cutscenes, which was honestly the biggest win as a gamer.

Without going into spoilers, the episode shined in the character development department; connections I never thought I could get from LIS2 (given ep 1&2) surrounded me. I felt like I had an engaged investment in all the characters. Everyone got real, and that was really cool to witness and experience. The story flowed well, drifting from time with Sean and Daniel to time with their new family.

The environment was s p e c t a c u l a r. Being in the redwood forest of Cali really brings you into the feel of the episode quickly and grounds you there, even when moving from place to place. It was honestly erie, in the sense that I knew where I was at all times, but struggled with who I wanted big brother Sean to become. But that's the point, as you find out!

The episode deals with romance, sexuality, and ultimately who the young brothers discover themselves to be. And while DOTNOD still often prevents you from having any say in who they become, staying along for the ride is well worth it. How you make decisions in this game overall are of higher importance and impact than in the two episode prior. Some of your decisions actually branch apart, giving you access to different areas of the episode.

HUGE IMPROVEMENT DOTNOD! I tip my hat off to your team, and await my next ep3 playthrough! well done 9/10\t\t\t\t\t
