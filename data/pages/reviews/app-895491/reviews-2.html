Ah, episode three. Just like with the first game, I feel this is the episode where things really start picking up in the story, and the moment from which I couldn't leave my seat (figuratively speaking). After fleeing their grandparents' home so as not to be found by the police, the Diaz brothers end up in California, working alongside some new friends (some of whom they've already met in the previous episode) in an illegal pot farm. Not precisely the best situation, but Sean and Daniel both seem to be living a relatively good life, and Sean's even been earning money to help with the trip down to Mexico.

Of course, good things don't last, doubly so when they're illegal things. During the episode, some tension begins to build between the brothers, and it's up to you playing as Sean to try and see if you can reconnect with Daniel. This episode starts bringing some serious choices to the mix, and it's ending is quite a cliffhanger.

Obviously recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
