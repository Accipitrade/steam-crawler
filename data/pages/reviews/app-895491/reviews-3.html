Family

So the adventure continues. Episode starts with some flashback which shows how the life was before the tragedy. It was really nice to experience that. Although their family was broken, they were happy, just like a normal family.

Sean and Daniel found new friends which feel like a temporary family. But Daniel feels neglected, although he's older now but he's still a kid and all the others are much older than him so he feels alone. And Sean of course is tired of being a single father and want to spend some time with other people so Daniel is kind of starting to distance himself from Sean.

Brothers now even have a “job”. The working sequence was fun, quite difficult doing the task and at the same time keep talking.
You learn about each new character you met, about their life and how they ended up there. You learn that life sucks and you're not the only one with ♥♥♥♥♥♥ up life, it kind of make you feel better knowing you're not alone.

There's also possible romance option finally. I loved that depending on your choices you end up doing different things and miss some some gameplay/cut-scene parts.
Ending sequence was tense, especially with that music playing.

I really loved this episode, loved meeting new people, enjoying the scenery and life for a second.

Pros
  • Fun activities
  • Lots of new people to meet and talk to
  • Ending didn't disappoint as always

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game started off all right. Episode 1 was okay, but Episode 2 dragged on too long with too little happening, but Episode 3? I'm gonna say it. I hate every second of playing it. Maybe it's because I like the characters from the first two LiS games too much, but everyone in this episode is either really boring, really annoying, or both. And that goes for our protagonist "Sean" as well. I don't like him. Daniel's all right sometimes.
Another possible reason as to why I'm not enjoying this game when I really loved the other two is that in both the previous ones, there was a mystery we were trying to solve. We had a goal in mind that we were working towards and there was a lot of character development and interaction along the way. In this game, so far as I can tell, the only thing we're really working towards is getting out of the country. The biggest mystery is why Daniel has telekenisis, but I really couldn't care less, I mean, it's not like they explained Max's rewind power.
I also think the picks for the soundtrack were weaker in this game, but of course that's likely down to personal taste. All in all, I would not recommend playing this episode unless 4 and 5 are amazing.\t\t\t\t\t
