\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNot really sure where all the good ratings come from. This is neither humorous (unless you think random things such potatoes are hilarious) nor does it have a good combat system.

I passed the game with basically using the basic sequence of attacks 1,2,3,3 and 4 for heal until the very end (with an occasional reposition, guard break). No tactical decision making from my side was ever needed, despite the ton of factors such as: statues, bonuses, enemy proximity and traits. It all broke down to the 1,2,3,3, and 4 when low hp. Combat seemed "deep" but ended up being reduced to the 4 commands you repeat over and over.

Game just feels like a time sink with a lot grind, but with ASCI instead of regular graphics so yay?
Not recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
