Adding in numerous new locations, quests, gun rarity, enemies, and additional levels allowing players to get to level 70. The locations are just fantastic and are super cool and look amazing also no re-used enemies from the game are used as they are all completely new and the plot is quite entertaining wish it was longer tbh. Lots of memes and jokes in this DLC as you enter inside of Claptrap’s mind, a totally unexpected great DLC from this game.

+Can be fast traveled to whenever
+Only $10
+Adds 10 more additional levels allowing players to get from the original max (60) to now 70. Note that another DLC with the Holodome also adds 10 levels but these two contradict each other as the max rank is 70 so you can get either DLC to reach level 70.
+10 new locations which I’d have to say was almost my favorite part about this DLC each location looks amazing and is cool whether its a giant cyberpunk esc city inside of Claptrap’s mind or cool recreations of past locations from the previous Borderlands games just very stunning and awesome locations especially the Subconscious
+19 new missions (14 side quests)
+50+ new enemies
+New weapon rarity: Glitch (pink)
+Several other new weapons and legendaries
+3 new cosmetics for each character that look great! (besides Athenas)
+New slaughter dome where you can fight waves of enemies from the DLC along with new ability to add buffs and debuffs and even difficulty
+A racist hot dog gives you a quest
+Get to enter inside Claptrap’s mind
+Lots of memes and movie references
+Several throwbacks to previous Borderlands games allowing you to visit iconic locations, NPCs, and enemies
+Really loved the locations saying this again but talking mainly about the Nexus which is a great hub world full of flying robots, light bridges that take you everywhere, a giant Claptrap working, and you can even see Handsome Jack peering into the Claptrap in the distance
+Several meme Claptraps throughout the DLC that have funny meme quests
+I really like the psychology of this DLC as you can just see how Claptrap really feels about how he’s treated and even though people are ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ to him all the time he’s still nice to them :)
+Lots of plot twists and great ending
+Quest where a Claptrap threatens to murder you but forgets which gun is loaded and which isn’t for you to pick from. Shots himself by trying to see which is which.
+Volatile Bits which act as floating elemental barrels which went shot either heal you or release elemental damage that locks onto nearby enemies
+Enemies in general are very weak and majority are robots so bring a very good corrosive weapon as well as new elemental enemies that require you to kill them with a different element weapon making for interesting gameplay
+One time whilst fighting Shadow-Trap when he teleported away he glitched through the floor and died instantly
+Mutator is a good Moonstone farm

-Plot hole? So obviously at the end of the main game itself Athena states the left Jack’s employ after his clear insanity at the vault opening however, here she is working for him yet again after the events of Pre-Sequel but before she is captured by the Crimson Lance? So she decides to work for him again even after all the evil ♥♥♥♥ he has done? Apparently she had ran out of money after seemingly “throwing it all away” after the events of the game causing her to come back to him for more money lol tbh just sounds like bad writing
-Return of Pickles & Janey ughhh
-Several side quests are rather pointless and just straight up stupid but I guess they are for the memes lelelelelel
-So what was the point of the H-Source? just to make Claptrap the sole surviving robot of his kind? Jack states that it holds so many Hyperion secrets, weapons, etc. yet all he seemingly does with it is destroy all Claptraps...
-Honey-Pot Robot and the “Spyware Who Came in from the Cold” quest? Literally just a robot who asks for 200 ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Moonstones in return for a chest open with low-tier loot that can only be opened one time. Legit avoid this quest just a way to waste Moonstones you’ll never get a guaranteed legendary or anything good just a trash can for Moonstones.
-Not sure if this was just for me but kept getting FPS issues in the Subconscious location. Legit never had any FPS issues in the entire game but whenever I was in the Subconscious I kept getting lag spikes and freeze frames.
-Hate quests in the Borderlands series that are almost if impossible to find and causes me to die multiple time and lose millions just trying to find the quest only for it be completely useless, good example: “Corrosion of Dignity” and “Temple of Boom”
-Most annoying thing in the game is that certain enemies, especially the flying ones, will spawn invisible and never try to attack you. Simply just fly around the map invisible and can never be killed. Sometimes enemies even spawn in the ground half of them sticking out or other times completely phase through the floor. To make things even better doing the Mutator rounds of survival nothing is better than when this happened to me twice in a row as the very last enemy to kill was one of these invisible enemies flying outside of the map therefor causing me to have to kill myself and restart multiple times.
-Final boss is super… repetitive? I’d say it's just the constant “ok you beat me” “JUST KIDDING” about three times is a bit annoying. The final fight with the Power Rangers looking one is super OP and takes way too many hits to kill super beefy and not fun gameplay wise but looks cool.

In the end I loved this DLC. I thought the locations were some of the best in the entire Borderlands series. I also enjoyed the plot and personally wished it would be longer. New enemies all around, new rarity, tons of new quests, lots of memes and lols, and easy achieves for the most part. Annoying and repetitive new slaughterdome area and some stupid glitches and FPS drops. For $10 this is worth it especially with the addition of 10 levels note that buying the Season Pass gets you all the other DLCs and you save $10 so you could go for that instead. Sadly the last Borderlands representation for David Hennings the original Claptrap VA :(\t\t\t\t\t
