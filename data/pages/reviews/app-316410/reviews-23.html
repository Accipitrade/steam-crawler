\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty fun!

(To start it, go to a new fast travel location: Deck 13 1/2)


One of the best (though not necessarily the best) DLC for any Borderlands game.


I'd be hard pressed to say what order without re-playing them recently, but I'd put it on the level of the greats (Tiny Tina's Assault on Dragon Keep and The Baddass Crater of Badassitude).

I'd place it just above some of the best DLC of Borderlands 1 (I liked robot revolution best, but others place knoxx and zed above it...to each their own). But I'd say it's a bit better than all of those DLC.


It does a great job taking a pretty disjointed story and giving it coherence, and it not only "cleans up" TPS, it has some nice story elements and characters from all three games.




TL;DR if you own the main game, this will make it better, and is the only DLC I'd consider "mandatory" simply because of how much it adds.\t\t\t\t\t
