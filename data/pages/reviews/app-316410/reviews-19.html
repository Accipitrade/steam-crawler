\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI thought because of the other DLCs this one was going to be bad. And damn I was wrong. I mean it has minor problems here and there but still it's a challenging fun DLC. But not only was there minor problems, there was a big problem at the end boss that almost made me change to Not Recommended. But that changed when I realized that this DLC was uncovering backstory like The Pre-Sequel should. It took me 3.5 hours to beat it but holy hell it was fun. It's sad that this is the only big DLC that Gearbox was making for this game.


Of course if you're going to make a DLC, you need weapons to go along with it. The weapons in the DLC are called glitch weapons. They turn different colors sometimes and that means they get a bonus. When it turns green it's like a rapid fire shotgun. When it turns yellow you just have to click once and the gun will go ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ shooting bullets at an enemy. When it's red it does about ten times the damage but knocks you back a little.

I'd rate this DLC a 9/10\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm satisfied with this DLC. Now only if there was one more DLC like this to replace the Holodome DLC.

Pros:
- Finally a challenging boss fight. It took me over an hour to beat him, due to the immense amount of health. Nonetheless, it was fun.
- Lore revelation. I kept asking myself what happened to Fragtrap that he ended up like he is now. The end of this DLC answers that question.
- The new weapon category. It was fun shooting with those.
- Customizable arena. Way better than the holodome, plus you don't have to pay for it itself >.>
- Interesting quests and storyline.
- Whole new kinds of enemies.
- Truly mazing soundtrack.

Cons:
- 10 new levels? How am I supposed to level up? Only by using the arena? I only leveled up once (to 61), although I didn't leave out a single enemy on my way.
- It was a little short in my opinion. But nonetheless pretty fun.\t\t\t\t\t
