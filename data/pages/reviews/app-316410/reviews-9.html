\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a really good DLC. I started out playing this slowly, thinking that it wouldn't add much playtime, but there is a lot of content here. Didn't specifically keep track from start to finish but I got probably about 40 hours' worth. There are plenty of side missions to help fill out the DLC. The glitch weapons add a nice new element. The story is also pretty good and adds a lot of emotion for what is usually just shown as an annoying joke character. There are still lots of silly things, too, though, of course. Some of the visuals and environments you see as you go through Claptrap's mind are interesting, in a dream-like sort of way. The final boss is pretty difficult, I'd say the most in all the Borderlands series, and it's quite the endurance round. I was able to get through it as the doppelganger but imagine it would be much tougher for some of the other characters.

Overall, it's a solid recommend. They did good on this DLC, and it's worth playing to have a sort of epilogue to the main game.\t\t\t\t\t
