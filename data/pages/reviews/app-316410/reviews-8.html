\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAn excellent expansion and one of the best in the entire Borderlands series (it's certainly in the top 3).

Pros:
* A surprisingly excellent, heartfelt, and interesting narrative that really develops claptrap as a character
* Some really interesting and well-imagined environments
* Glitch guns are incredible additions. I have an ice-spitting lazer rifle which can transform in to a shotgun or a sniper rifle on reload...
* One of the best boss fights in the entire series
* Some great music (I absolutely love the Subconscious Battle Theme!!)
* A replayable arena with good rewards and customizable match attributes!
* Adbots and literal popup ads made me chuckle
* Some interesting new enemies, like the Adaptive Virus

Cons:
* Level design at times left some to be desired - a couple confusing layouts exist, and in TPS fashion there are a few too many maps that you revisit which force you to make a long trek to reach an objective instead of providing a shortcut.
* The new enemies have fun, clever names, but many of them don't do much in the way of changing gameplay (at least on the normal vault hunter mode).
* Handsome Jack is a great character but it's time to put him to rest.

Conclusion:
I love it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSummarize this DLC in one word?

Wow. Just, wow...

Before I tried this gem of an add-on, I would not hesitate to call Tiny Tina's Assault on Dragon's Keep the best Borderlands DLC of all time. But the Claptastic Voyage is a strong contender for that title at least, if not the new piece de rsistance in the series. What makes me say that? Let me list it for you:

1. Completely new and diverse environment.
Every area you go to is different from the others and most of them don't suffer from the horrible level design that some of the areas in the base game suffer from. Most enemies are pretty unique to this add-on which only adds more to the diversity. Plus 1 from me.
2. The story is well written
Not too long, not too short, and the build up to a great finale is excellent. The main antagonist of the story, SH4D0W TP is a memorable new member of the series. There are quite a few story gaps that get filled (mainly involving the birth and end of FR4G TP. Completing the main story left me with a feeling of satisfaction, need I say more?
3. LEEEWT
This DLC features new Legendaries and a completely new rarity type called 'Glitch', which is basically purple with added features (like E-Tech in BL2). And there is no shortage of good drops (like in TTAODK). After completing the finale by backpack was litterally full with glitch, purple and at least 2 legendaries. WHY CAN'T I HOLD ALL THESE AMAZING GUNS!?
4. A repeatable arena survival mission with CUSTOMIZABLE FEATURES.
I get to pick modifiers, mutations and dificulty levels from the start and get loot based from it when I complete it? That is amazing!

This is worth the full price. Seriously. I think it's that good.
