\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery creative and fun DLC for the BL2 and BLPS fans. Glitch weapons are interesting and fun, if you go in at level 30 as I did, you're right at the correct level with the bad guys.

This DLC is more creative, has better pathfinding and less confusing levels than BLPS. This DLC is better than BLPS base game in my opinion.

Pros:
-creative level design with a bit of TRON and a bit of BL and BL2 nostalgia. more creative than base game.
-glitch weapons are fun and different.
-claptrap is actually not as annoying as usual.

Cons:
-most bad guys in this DLC need corrosive weapons, BUT good corrosive weapons are somewhat rare. this DLC does not have an increased drop rate for corrosive, as it should, given the bad guys. you end up farming corrosive weapons and sharing them between characters.

I'm about 1/2 through the DLC, and will update as i go along.\t\t\t\t\t
