\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA really nice puzzle games.

I would say the level of difficulty is average. Not too easy, but not too difficult. The hints are quite useful when you don't know what to do, but I found the hint system a bit hidden and I did not know those were the hints when I first found them.

If you like puzzle games that really need you to think, write notes and take some time to digest the information. This would definitely suits you. It may not have that much visual or animation, but the story, puzzles and the gameplay are something really unique, and I love this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis noir thriller was able to really catch my attention. The story is good and actually interesting, though its ending is somewhat predictable. However, it is the way that it's told that manages to catch and immerse the player, messing with the text, audio and narration in multiple ways, all creating an amazing experience.
But, of course, it is not a simple book, it is a game after all. The gameplay comes with the interactive images and text, and it is incredibly fun. You search for clues and hidden pieces of information to progress the story. Puzzles vary form easy to hard, but they are all fun to solve.
Overall, I really recommend Unmemory. Had a lot of fun.
Plus, I might be wrong, but it seems as if Unmemory left some clues to solve a mystery outside the game...\t\t\t\t\t
