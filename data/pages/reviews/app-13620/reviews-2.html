\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIncredible expansion.
Enemies are clever and adaptive. Map design expands this great AI, as enemies flank you when you destroy mission objectives, forcing you to flush out out-of-way outposts beforehand. Staying behind predictable cover like rocks and buildings makes grenades magically appear near you, making the far and rare bushes even more valuable in this dry desert.

Buildings are designed to force interesting strategies from the player themselves. While you call your A-team to storm a building, you take control of your sniper and snipe the enemies trying to flank or leave the building. You might even use your demolition experts as a distraction to draw fire.

Some time-specific missions. These are awesome. Moving from the slow and controlled strategy to on-the-fly positioning really shows off the brilliance in the gameplay loop. Support finally gets a lot of love in this campaign as well. Enemies like to stay grouped, making the fast firerate of Support's weapons critical in many missions where firing single shot from a sniper isn't reliable enough against the enemies' overwhelming numbers and tendency to scatter around when taking fire. Most maps have less fog, making them a lot more interesting as both you and your enemies can spot each other from across the map easier.
