\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've only owned this game for... Around 48 hours? I love this game. So much.
I only have three factions unlocked, so far. Terran, Farmers, and Red.
I figured I'd play Farmers, to kinda chill out and, ya know, farm some space plants.
Five hours of straight gameplay later, I'm flying what looks like a weird popcorn machine, leading an army of rocket-laden boxes on a quest to purge the system of thorns, and *eating through entire asteroids* in the process.
There's something oddly satisfying about flying through deadly space in a defenseless ship, hiding in an endless shield wall of rockets, stronger than the eight shields on my ship combined.
In all seriousness, though, the Farmers are really good if you want to lead a fleet of super-warriors, but not fight, yourself. Like if you want to put together a plan and just watch it unfold, sort of. Maintaining an income in a combat zone becomes a mild issue, but generally, you don't need it. And I should point out - no part of what I've said in this is an exageration. Except the "army" part, it was only, like, a dozen... or two..\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis might seriously be my favorite game of all time.

Ok, I have scene a LOT of reviews for this game saying that it not worth getting. And I have to say, I kinda see where they are coming from. There are parts of this game that are kinda boring, the flying/shooting in this game isn't spectacular, and it's kinda buggy (sometimes the bugs are funny though). However, I can most if not all of this games faults becuase of its greatest strength:

The ship building system. OH MY GOSH is it good. You know how this game's building mechanics are frequently compared to legos? Well, let me tell you, that's accurate. The building mechanic are so good, it single-handedly upgrades this from a compitent but generic shootemup with pretty but equally generic graphics to one of the most fun games I have ever played.

Ok, I can't really do this game justice telling you how good it is. So let me tell you my experience with my first faction:

So, early on, I had a tiny, weak ship, as you alwias start with. I go about the game as normal for a while. Maybe an hour in, I come accross a huge station by another faction. WAY too big for me to handle by myself. But I didn't. There were a lot of AI ships in my faction nearby. And we were all teaming up on this big huge station. None of us capable of handelling it ourselves, but together, we beat it. It really made like I was a part of something bigger, like I was fighting for the greater good of my faction.

Fast foward a bit. I now have a huge ship, with a massive fleet fallowing me. I'm no longer a part of something bigger. I'm not longer fighting for the greater good of my faction. I AM the greater good of my faction. I have risen through the ranks and now I am leading my faction towards greatness.

I can't garantee that you will have an experince as good as mine. But if it's even half as good as mine, you are going to love this game. I have played as eight factions (all seven of the vinella factions and one modded faction) and as of writting I am making my own modded faction, and I'm still not bored of this game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReassembly is a unique, albeit somewhat confusing at first, experience. That being said, I mean that in the best possible way. Reassembly is a game unlike probably anything else out there, and I was quite pleasantly surprised by this. The game progresses naturally and seamlessly, with more to do than what initially meets the eye. The bottom right corner has tips and tutorials to goad a new player along, and seasoned players enjoy a multitude of creative options for ship building. The game has a wonderful modding community to go along with it. I'd recommend this game to anyone looking for a game that they can sit back, relax, and play. It's actually quite a calming game. There's little to no consequence for death, yet the player is driven to avoid it still, and all the while it's probably one of the most relaxing games I've played. A very good job by the devs. The soundtrack is also very good. 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
