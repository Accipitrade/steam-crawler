\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a good and fun game overall, but I'm posting a negative review to highlight a gaping balance issue that NEEDS to be fixed.

In my first run, I encountered circular Agent ships made entirely out of armour spikes, with some thrusters near the centre to make them spin. These spikes have so much HP and regenerate so fast that killing these ships, (especially with them spreading damage around as they spin) is basically impossible. They also one-shot anything they collide with, regardless of how much armour and shields it has. They just cut straight through any ship like it wasn't even there to begin with. The only vanilla weapon that works against these beyblade abominations is lag. You need to make a fleet of ships with so many drone launchers (and some torpedoes for damage) that the game lags to a standstill so you can damage the spinners, and even then they will still try to run when some of the outer spikes are gone. If one does escape it regenerates almost immediately. Suffice it to say that killing just one is a tedious process taking several minutes and the existence of an entire fleet almost completely ruined the game for me.

I've no doubt that half the people would quit the game entirely after encountering these low skill, low effort, stupidly OP designs that exploit a single block type. This needs to be fixed.\t\t\t\t\t
