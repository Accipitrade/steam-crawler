ok so.

As a person that almost plays exclusively space games and building games, this is my favorite game. By alot. Beautiful effects, a perfect soundtrack, and a building system that lets you make anything you want (but if you want something structurally sound, you'll have to do math xD). The combat and gameplay is fun and satisfying to watch and I love the way ships can slowly fall into pieces/get cut in half/explode into a million pieces. Not only is there infinite freedom in the base game, but the game is incredibly easy to mod as well. If you want to test your skill in min-maxing monstrous mobile machines, there are many active community-hosted tournaments as well. Most of the hours post-campaign (likely "won" after 30-40 hours) will either be modding or competing in tourneys.

However, its not without imperfections. First off, updates are very rare: the developer has his hands full with his half-grown human that he had in 2019. There is no tutorial for the sandbox, many of the controls, and many of the cooler features. There are bugs that, while workaround-able, new players will not know what is causing them or how to fix them (also some that no one knows what/why/or how they're there. The game is haunted man...). The ai sometimes decides it will do nothing and engages at strange ranges (mostly observed in tournament mode, campaign ai is fine) and also has trouble using some of the newer features. Lastly, as said before, the campaign part of the game is relatively short.

Now, does this make this game unenjoyable? Absolutely not. Any thing the game may be missing, the community can supply. Someone (identity unknown) has stepped up and is acting as a temporary developer to squash some of the bugs the game has in an upcoming update (all hail Bugnonimous). If you need help, there are dozens of guides on steam and the internet, and if you still can' find what you need, the community Discord Server is friendly and helpful. And regarding the short campaign, most of the endgame is building for tournaments and modding. Heck, I'm making a mod at a snail's pace because I'm having too much fun playing with what I've made!

So TLDR. If you're an engineer looking to make the best [2d] spaceship you can; if you're a competitor, a creator and artist with an unhealthy obsession with spaceships; if you want to blow chunks off battle-carriers with bombardment from cruisers while your buddy's crowd around and boo at your crappy boating...

get this game :)\t\t\t\t\t
