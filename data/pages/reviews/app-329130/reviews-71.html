\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor $15, this game is great. For less, it's a steal.
Build your own ship out of geometrically interllocking modules. Guns, shields, power plants, armor, missiles, mines, and a dizzying array of other parts are combined however you see fit. Make the most efficient design, and compete in the arena mode. Make a troll face, or some other silly phallic themed space vessel. Make a hopelessly flawed design, and enjoy the challenge of trying to overcome vastly superior foes. Whatever, I don't care - it's a free country, do whatever your dark heart desires. That's the point. This is a game about dogfighting, exploring a universe full of player designs, and evolving your own designs to meet new threats.

That being said, it does get stale after a bit. I sort of wish there was a story mode.
I still played for more hours in this game than I did in No Man's Sky.
