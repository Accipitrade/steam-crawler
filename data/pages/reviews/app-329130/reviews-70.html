\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll around a good time playing this game. Well worth the $15. Very relaxing to play. Simple but effective visuals. Even though it isn't technically multiplayer it is best to have other friends who play so you can face your ships off against theirs. In some aspects it feel like less of a game and more of a simulation. Mods always feel like a natural addition to the game, as the game is very open-source. It's a great top-down space shooter, alongside of a robust (but sometimes aggrivating) ship builder. It is possibly one of the most fluid and dynamic games I have ever played. Battles can be simple dogfights or huge fleets with capital ships and hundreds of fighters, and everything in between. My favorite thing about this game is building large capital ships to expand my territory into uncontrolled space and to engage in massive campaigns against other factions, but this game offers a variety of playstyles for different players.\t\t\t\t\t
