\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't remember much about this game, even though my memory is excellent, and I remember playing it for an hour recently. Still, what I can guess from my playtime and when I purchased the game, I was eight and really into commanding a fleet of spaceships and building a massive ship capital ship. I distinctly remember naming a ship the S.S. Chornobyl because of how explosive it tended to be; it was my capital ship, and from my hours played and a vast amount of achievements for this game which are eighteen achievements out of twenty-four, so not that many compared to to other games I own but still cool. When I played the game last, which was relatively recent, the magic that the game held was all but gone. I uninstalled most of the mods I had and tried to play through it again as the starting faction, but when I realized I had unlocked everything, there was no real point in playing that faction when so many others were available didn't feel right. Then I played through the basic tutorial for another fifty-six minutes and got off. That same day I also looked through the comments and reviews and noticed the developers last updated the game quite some time ago, and I mean a long time. But this doesn't bother me. Sure, it's sad, and it sucks that they didn't update it when people enjoyed it, but I moved on. I grew up, but I respect that some people still want to play it; that's fine, but its lack of updates doesn't bother me. It had its heyday in 2015, and it's not worth your precious credits anymore, but thankfully for many, this game most likely sparked their love of games that have similar mechanics or allow for an equal amount of creativity. I feel this game put a tiny seed in my brain that blossomed and made me enjoy playing a game like No Man's Sky™ so much, but sadly, my ability to build good-looking vehicles in most games is completely gone. But I recommend this game for gamers ages 6-10, and I think it perfectly fits that age range. It is so simple, yet from what I can understand from my playtime, it's entertaining for people of that age range. Although if you are older than that and still play this game, that is fine. However, I believe this game helps sprout creativity in little ones as it did for me, and as games become all the more commonplace as a leisure activity, it's a good idea to research what games are suitable for young minds and I think this one is great for that purpose. It also helped me form my obsession with space and my love for sci-fi. Sure, it's a pretty basic place to start, and there are probably better games for ages 6-10, but at least it's something! Overall 8/10, and fun for people ages 6-10.\t\t\t\t\t
