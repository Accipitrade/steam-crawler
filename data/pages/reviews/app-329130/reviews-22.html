\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat you need to know:
Requires a mega gaming rig for sure IMO.
Limit of 2000 blocks per ship.
Uses (C)urrency, (R)esource, and ship (P)ower metrics.
Free structural blocks, most available immediately.
Unlock (P) the ability to add more functional blocks.
Immediate access to build max size ships.
Each race has unique tech specialties.
There is no friendly fire/damage.
The AI is okay but not very advanced.
There is no complex galactic simulation.
Ships grow from one tiny block produced at a factory block.
There is a very simple mercenary fleet mechanic, only for you.
You can save and load your ships and entire fleet schematics.
Crowded areas will cause ships to overlap, causing massive game slowdowns.
Agents are copies of other players' fleets put into your game!
Go into a wormhole to add your fleet! Show off your designs!

Overall, the vanilla game leaves me wanting much more, but it scratches that creative itch. I spend much of my time trying to make ships that mimic the biological reproduction of cells that will multiply at maximum speed, making for very annoying Agents in other players worlds. LOL\t\t\t\t\t
