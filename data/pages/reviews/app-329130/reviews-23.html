\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is totally fantastic, 100% recommend to anyone that enjoys designing spaceships and controlling them. Most enjoyable to me is the aspect of the game where you design ships at various cost levels and pit them against ships of similar caliber. The game gives you tons of options for customizing the ships and everything looks and feels quite nice. It's especially interesting to try to design ships that the AI can utilize properly to assault other fleets, and to build a fleet with a composition to protect the mother-ship.

The cons of this game are far from deal-breakers, and are more things that I wish the developer would patch. Maybe there's an option to turn this off, but every time I begin the game with a new race, I have to click through a bunch of tutorial text that I already understand.
Additionally, designing a ship that encapsulates its weapons or various blocks can sometimes be difficult, because the blocks fit together only at certain key points. That being said, this issue may be more to do with my own ignorance of geometry.\t\t\t\t\t
