\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDesign magnificient 2D space ships in a great and efficient editor that's better than most of vector graphic editors I've used. Your civilization is using the ships you've designed so you can see the progress throughout the galaxy as you iteratively upgrade your ships to be bigger, better, faster, stronger and annihilate all opposition in an extremely satisfying way.

Thanks to wormohles (author calls it async multiplayer), you wage war against other players' creatinos and send your warships into other people's universes to do the same! This is a fantastic way to deliver user generated content directly in the game without hassle and reward you for contributing - sending your creations to wreak havoc in parallel universes. How cool is that?!

I love space games, especially 2D ones like Space Pirates And Zombies and I also love engineering games where I build vehicles myself for efficiency (Robocraft, From the Depths, Space Engineers) and this game brings so much to the table I sit here with my mind blown.

Did I menion one of the 7 playable races is using bloody penrose tiles? What the... just wow!

I think there is space for more user content awesomeness like player designed races (from the tiles already available) that would increase the already huge replay value to unseen levels, but you could call that a greed as the game is great already as is. And imagine downloading new races (tile sets) from workshop... yeah, greed... hmm...

This is a very special game and I deeply thank the dev for creating this masterpiece!

Few highlights:
* ships are damaged piece by piece
* ships reassemble themselves piece by piece
* ships with factories can spawn other ships (with factories...)
* maneuverability is decided by the placement of engines (finally)
* 7 playable races are unique and many strategies and designs are available
* you can test your ships against other ships and even against each other across save files
* galaxy feels alive, almost like Spore
* user generated content integrated into game with 0 effort to both consume and create (just fly through wormhole and even get reward, yay)

I bought this at full price and I regret nothing. I can't say that about many Steam purchases I did :-p\t\t\t\t\t
