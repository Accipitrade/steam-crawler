Build any type of ship you can imagine from agile fighters to lumbering Dreadnoughts and conquer a procedural galaxy before entering a wormhole and moving onto another or uploading your fleet online to allow other players to test their mettle against your designs.
Excellent graphics and particle effects and satisfying individual part destruction as you tear enemy ships apart piece by piece.
Plenty of different races to unlock each with their own unique parts, strengths and weaknesses and mod support give this game everything you could want.\t\t\t\t\t
