\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
-Appealing art style
-Great soundtrack
-Endless possibilities of ship design
-Numerous playable factions with their own unique play styles and ship designs
-Moddable factions with steam workshop support
-Moddable ship parts, potential for completely custom ships
-Player made fleets appear in your game
-Ability to upload your ship/fleet to the cloud so they can appear in other players' games
-Good graphics
-Newtonian physics, it's like you're actually flying in zero gravity
-Interesting AI differences in factions, some npc ships will actually salvage parts from downed enemies and use them to upgrade their own ship.
-A sandbox mode where you can simulate battles, take control of ships you spawn, and write scripts. Great for testing your custom factions and ships.

Cons/Suggestions to devs:
-The ship editor lacks a symmetry mode, which would make ship building a bit more streamlined
-When you run into stuff, you take damage. I feel like there should be an option to disable this for new players, however this feature grants the ability to create ships that ram when in combat, which is always fun as hell.
-There needs to be a better way to quickly check how damaged your ship is. Perhaps a bar on the hud that shows what percentage of your ship is damaged. Sometimes the screen gets so busy you can't see how much of your ship is left and when it would be a good idea to turn tail and flee if things get too hairy.
-Sometimes it's hard to travel quickly from one place to another because you can't see far enough in front of your ship to see obstacles like asteroids and stations. Space high ways or warp gates would be an excellent addition to the game.
-There is currently no way to tell how much of your shields are left. A way to monitor that would be very convenient.
-The sandbox mode lacks a tutorial on how to use the command line.
-An interesting addition would be factions that have more than just 2 colors in their color scheme. I sometimes feel limited when attempting to create more flashy and appealing ships. Maybe the ability to paint your ship or add decals would work.
-There are no planets. ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ it would be awesome to blow up a planet.

tl;dr

I bought this game after reading the reviews. They do not lie, this game is extremely fun and addicting. You will not regret spending your money on it. Amazing game and amazing community. If you're on the fence about it, I'm telling you to buy it. It's great and you'll really enjoy it. 9.5/10\t\t\t\t\t
