\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSeriously an amazing game if you're looking for something to stretch your creative muscles. You choose one of many playable factions, each unique in the parts and weapons available to them. Using said parts, you create a 2D spaceship and begin to pioneer your faction to victory.

Things to note:
-This game has no story. It is purely driven by you making a story yourself, or simply wanting to conquer the generated world.

-All movement in the game is physics based, and your ships are only as good as you build them. This means there is something similar to a puzzle/strategy aspect of the game when building your ships. If this is unappealing, I would be hesitant to recommend this game to you.
A possible solution to this problem is the implementation of the Steam Workshop. This allows people to upload entire factions/fleets for you to use, but in my opinion you are mission out on half if not more of the game if you aren't building things.

-Replay value is arguable, and very dependant on the player. If you are someone who likes experimenting with strategies, or challenging yourself by say, limiting your options by a personal rule, then you will most likely find it easy to sink hours into Reassembly.

In closing, I will simply say that Reassembly is a very rewarding game for those who enjoy top-down shooters, side scrollers, and creating functional and even beautiful things. If you find it difficult to find personal motivation with a game, maybe double check other games on your wishlist before buying.

P.S. If you're into systematically destroying A.I. populations, this game could be for you.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me just begin by saying this game is in early developement and it may have its bugs but still is worth playing through. the gameplay and concept are a jewel among other games. there are strategy and shooter elements along with casual elements that make this game just an all around excellent experience both for hardcore action players and more relaxed casual players.
The ability to make your own ship one block at a time and perfect it and then see other stations and ships spawn your design is a beautiful site. This is an excellent marriage of strategy and shooter along with a wonderful atmospheric environment. the price for this game may seem a little high but I personally think it is well worth the price for the experience you can have in the game.

pros
- modular ship building
- living ever changing environment
- unique playable factions each specializing in a particular playstyle.
- interesting encounters with other players ship designs.
- early access
- inifinite ways to play
cons
- early access
- lack of uniqueness with some factions
- weak factions
- lacking content\t\t\t\t\t
