\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game doesn't get enough love for what it has to offer - I could find relatively little information about it online aside from this page and the wikia for it. A highly stylized space game that's half ship design building and half arcade-style gunning, with no messy plot or other details to get in your way other than 'grow, kill, expand'.

The best part, for me at least, is the ability to see your ship grow from a piddly fighter that needs to run and gun against all but the smallest of enemies to a capital ship bristling with weapons. You can also be the commander of a fleet which is...well, uh, not that good TBH. The AI is fair, but depending on your skill with ships the others will lag behind your inevitable beast of a main ship and have a tendency to get themselves caught in you or pieces of terrain. Still, it's not too damning and there's nothing like a full on war between your ships and two-three other factions to show why the game is amazing!

Other cons to note: the Spiky Plants and Bees with MESS YOUR FPS UP if you piss them off, so don't. You'll also find sectors controlled by other factions and it's because there's literally one tiny ship flying around that dodges all your weapon fire. Minor elements, but still worth noting.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt has been quite a while since a game has held my interest for so long. This has become my new addiction in gaming.

The concept of this game is simple:

1. Build your spaceship using guns, armor, and various other parts depending on your play preference.
2. Pilot your spaceship in real time on a 2d plane against A.I. faction ships.
3. Kill other ships and collect what is essentially their XP orbs.
4. Turn in XP for money.
5. Increase your build capacity and unlock guns and blocks with your money.
6. Improve on your ship or make an entirely new one to add to your growing roster of ships for your current faction and watch the A.I. build your new creations to use against your enemies!

It is so satisfying to see your own ship builds being made and used by your own faction in just the ways you intended.

Make a farming ship to cultivate plants for resource orbs.
Create a ship designed with massive cargo holds solely to collect resource orbs to turn in for massive profits.
Create a ship with the ability to heal other ships using beams and drones.
Focus on shields and beams to burst your opponent down from close up.
Make high armored missle-clad monsters to take out your enemies from a distance.
Cover your ship in drone launchers and swarm your enemies with mosquitos!

You can do all of this and so much more, your imagination is really the limit.

The modding scene with this game is surprizingly great and the community is very welcoming and also surprizingly large.

Hopefully, this game gets much more attention. It certainly flew under the radar for me but once I picked it up I was easily hooked without intending to be. I've already put in 25 hours in just a few days and I guarantee that will rise steadily over the next few days as well.\t\t\t\t\t
