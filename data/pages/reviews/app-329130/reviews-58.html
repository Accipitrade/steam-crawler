\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReassembly is what you get when you put a space theme on the first stage of Spore. It's great fun to build highly customizable vessels and cruise around collecting stuff from "plants".

However, things tend to get tedious afterwards. Reassembly is not particularly immersive, you're just looking at basic geometric shapes that shoot other geometric shapes. I'm not sure I fully understood fleet control, I tend to just end up with 100+ ships that are painfully slowly following me and if I want to actively fly somewhere they can't keep up. The game's entirely battle oriented, no trading, diplomacy or anything that would make you want to go somewhere. Instead just let the bosses (which are underwhelming if you have 100+ ships) come to you.

Also note that development seems to have stopped. Whatever community's left seems to be because of the workshop support, which is always a plus.

All in all it's a nice game for the general concepts involved and I hope it inspires others to make something more fleshed out. At a slight discount, you can't really go terribly wrong with picking this up.\t\t\t\t\t
