\r\n\t\t\t\t\tReassembly fits into my chill-out group of games. You can mindlessly tweak your ships, grow fleets, etc, and just explore the region. Once that region gets boring you can jump through the wormhole to a fresh new one. Over time the game does start to feel a little bland - once you get to the big ships and the big fleets with them, the challenge seems to go away. For that reason, that's why it's in my chill-out pile. It's Flow, with spaceships and some neat mechanics allowing you to change your ships, share your ships in other people's games, etc.\t\t\t\t\t
