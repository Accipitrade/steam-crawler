It's not on rails and has a much larger degree of customization, so I find it an appropriate upgrade to the gummi's.

I could wish for online multi-player. The tournament/simulation function can make up for that lack to a degree, but it requires emailing around ship files and streaming to your friends. For this small of a player base it might be better to allow us to host our own games on servers.

Of course, it would be nice to have some semblance of a story, but I'm mostly grateful for the experience of building my own ships with relative ease, and seeing if they survive the ecosystem.\t\t\t\t\t
