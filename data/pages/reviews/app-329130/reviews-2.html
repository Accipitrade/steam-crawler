\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game will take you from piloting the smallest stock drone, mining your own business, to meticulously planning out ship designs based on physics (do I have enough thrusters to compensate for the force generated by my cannons?), to commanding an entire fleet of all your past and present ship designs (or entirely made up of hirelings found along the way), carving through anything.

A rare game, that creates with minimal mechanics, an emergent levelling up, where problems on a whole new level (i have so many vessels, that they can't all partake in battle) arise trough natural progression. There are no buttons "increase power, add armour", everything is built up from the same blocks and all flight and fight mechanics are tangled together based on the layout of your ships; the layout of your fleet.

By the end of your play through you command a massive fleet, but you know every single block that is used to build it. Unless you just hired your whole fleet, which is also a valid way to play the game.\t\t\t\t\t
