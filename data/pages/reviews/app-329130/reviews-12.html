\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of those games you can lose a lot of time in. There's an endless campaign mode for learning the mechanics, unlocking factions, and building ships, but the real replay value for me is in taking those designs into the tournament mode and pitting fleets against each other.

The building mechanics are interesting because the block designs are juuuust mismatched enough that you can't connect a block to every adjacent block. This forces you to make decisions and get creative with your builds, because blocks can only regrow from adjacent blocks. It's not trying to be a believable world, the whole atmosphere is kinda trippy. I'm pretty sure there are some binaural beats in the BGM. I recommend this one, but I wish there was more player control over AI presets for tournament mode.

Also, mods. hundreds of them. You can find them on the workshop and configure them in game. If there's anything you don't like, I guarantee at least one person has fixed it already.\t\t\t\t\t
