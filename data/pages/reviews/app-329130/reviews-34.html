\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is pretty fun. I would recommend it at its full price. The gameplay is pretty simple and can get pretty repetitive as the progression loop is pretty much the same across all factions including mod factions. The progression loop being kill ships, get R, turn to R into P, spend P on making ship bigger repeat.

There's also not a whole lot of strategy involved in this game it's more about having more gun than your enemy and at some point your ship is going to be so powerful that literally nothing can touch you. Even once you start building a fleet of your own you're not going to start pulling off tactical moves or anything like that you just point at a target and all your little dudes will move into range and start firing thoughtlessly until it explodes. Honestly I found fleet building to be a bit tedious and not as fun as just creating one really big over powered ship that can knock out fleets by itself.

Over all it's a fun little time waster with some visually pretty nice looking battles and a satisfying if not overly simple progression system revolving around customizing your ship. If you're like me and don't like playing videogames for more than half an hour at a time this game is probably a good pick up for you.\t\t\t\t\t
