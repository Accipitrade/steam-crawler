\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwesome little city builder, though to be more accurate it's more like a "city painter." There is very little challenge, though you can lose if you completely neglect your services. What few mechanics the game does have are most similar to SimCity4. It includes in-game mod support, called plugins, to add content but doesn't look like any plugins change mechanics.

Clearly, the bread and butter of this game is easily creating beautiful cities. Native to the game is the ability to manually place any residential, commercial, or industrial building, turning all "growables" into "ploppables." Not even city skyline mods can do that. There are also about two dozen real life, to scale, landmarks and skyscrapers to add which all look spectacular.

The mobile version is free, you can keep playing your city anywhere! Isn't that neat? And it's multiplayer, SC2013 style. Other players can claim regions in your map, and you can visit them anytime and connect roads and rails to them. I'm on a server with some discord friends, and its a lot of fun.

The only major downsides to the game are tedious terraforming mechanics and no diagonal roads or rails of any kind. Still an 8/10.

Would recommend for a chill, laid back "city painter" with just enough mechanics/challenge to keep you from falling asleep.\t\t\t\t\t
