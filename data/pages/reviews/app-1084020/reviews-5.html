\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR: Runs on a potato, yet plenty of complexity, very similar to Sim city 4 + Cities:Skylines

Things one may consider positive:
+ Similar to Cities:Skylines mixed with Sim City 4
+ can use plug ins
+ NO DLC Gouging (Like Cities:Skylines) Things like nightlife, boats, additional assets are all in base game!
+ Easy to use, but plenty of complex design and functionality to explore
+ Only takes a few minutes to start seeing a city come to life
+ Great world ambiance
+ plenty of control over traffic, taxes, zones etc.
+ large, connected, world map
+ lots of assets to build with

Things one may consider negative:

- The way the menus are set up can take some getting used to (Theyre kind of clunky but 100% functional)
- stylized graphics (personally I like it but some might not)

I feel like I got this for a great price on sale! TBH it's worth its full price tag.
If you like city builders, especially Sim City, or Cities:Skylines, but are on a budget you should 100% get this.
