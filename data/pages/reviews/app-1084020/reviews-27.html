\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTheotown is a retro/pixel style city builder game that was originally released on Google Play Store.

The game is well designed for being an indie game and gives that nostalgic feeling from the pixel style graphics. It reminds me of the old SimCity games. There is a lot of content in the game that will keep you busy long enough but also has a lot of replay-ability from the amount of ways you can design your city and also the generated world. The game also comes with the premium features and the removal of premium currency that were present in the mobile version. Theotown features modding support via their ingame workshop where the community can create custom content.

Theotown does suffer from some small amount of bugs,glitches and crashes but they do not appear frequently.

If you like the old SimCity games and pixel graphics i would definitely give this game a shot.\t\t\t\t\t
