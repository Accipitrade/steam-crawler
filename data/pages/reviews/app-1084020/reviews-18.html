\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI played the mobile version for two hours and uninstalled it to play on the PC and have a better experience.
This game has a huge potential but it's so underrated and "unknow". It's not just a game of city construction that you just need to click on the build and put it on the map.


About The Game


• The management system is very logical and "realistic", for example the question of the population's demand, if the residents ask for more schools you simply build a school near the region.
This works in any situation, like more police, firefighters, hospitals, etc;

• Following the topic above, the game handles economic and environmental issues very well. You can't build any neighborhood close to an industrial area and electricity generator because noises and pollution would be very annoying and cause a bad quality of life;

• You can create your own seed for the map, choose any feature and even the size of the map;

• It is possible to choose the difficulty of your journey, starting with little money and with a more demanding population, or to start a world in which money does not matter but the "aesthetics" of the map, aiming at beauty instead of caring for the requests of the population;

• The soundtrack is so relaxing that I almost fell asleep in the middle of the game;

• You really need to pay attention to your actions, checking out how much you're investing in areas that don't really need as much attention and cut off expenses to generate more money;

• This game has many different buildings, including houses, soccer field, baseball field, hospitals, police department, radio station, parks, many types of road, churches of various religions, etc. You can even buy the Eiffel Tower or an Egyptian pyramid.

Conclusion

In addition to these qualities, the game has many others that make it addictive as hell and different from the others city building games. It's worth the price.

★★★★★\t\t\t\t\t
