\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat game. Really looks like simcity while still feeling more modern.

BUT WARNING! The game is nearly a 1:1 version of the Mobile one, which means maps can be played on both, but you won't be seeing it taking advantage of your PC and it is limited in PC related options and support such as no control remapping and iffy UI in some spots. To the games credit you can run experimental mode and use console and such which is cool.

An example of PC specific issues, my PC pumped out so many FPS for the game that it crashed my GPU multiple times, with vsync not working until I reinstalled video drivers.

Also that when playing fullscreen, the game is set to always-on-top so you can't tab out to other windows at all. If I want to tab out to steam I have to close the game.

I would love to see PC modding beyond plug-ins in some way, especially since I don't play on mobile so I don't need save compatibility (and control remapping, as I naturally want to press SPACE to pause/resume the game). Though I don't know the full capabilities of plug-ins yet, they may prove to be all I need.

In the end the game does look good and play pretty well and aside from a few PC related hiccups.
Worth it full price if you enjoy other city building/simulation games.\t\t\t\t\t
