\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really enjoyed this game. The game is similar to the SimCity series as it allows zoning and construction of city infrastructure (i.e. roads, tracks and public services). It has a full region with different map sizes, and you can spend days and even weeks on each of the maps. The game is challenging and makes players work at constructing their cities with things such as traffic and fire safety in mind. My most favourite feature of this game is the ability to build individual zoning buildings; this making working on the aesthetics of your city particularly easy and I have made some really nice skylines with this feature. I hope to spend many more hours playing this game. Well worth the price.\t\t\t\t\t
