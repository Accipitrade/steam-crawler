\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDefinitely more fun than Cities Skyline.

It's a throw back to like SimCity 3 back in the day. It's pretty simplistic but it's fun and it's exactly what many people are asking for. You zone regions (res, com, ind, low and high density), you build various roads and services, you get special buildings, you marvel as your city grows. That's it. That's all of it.

On the downside: this is a port of a mobile game and the menus really feel like it. Some basic things are missing, like simple options to bind keys, as far as I can tell, a way to turn off auto road placement, changing views to higher or lower angles, these things really should be fixed.

Things that could be improved upon that are a bit disappointing: there's no advanced monetary controls or policies. Why doesn't any modern city builder have those anymore? Also, transport systems leave a lot to be desired. Why can I make individual bus routes but I can't for subways? Why can't I control fair amount and see how it's performing? Why is setting routes such a pain in the ass (as is more advanced road building).

But, overall, I have to recommend this game, especially if you're getting it for sub $10. Definitely worth it to people longing for and old-school SimCity game.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWonderfully cute pixel art city builder, in which you can spend many hours creating a variety of interesting towns and cities.

I would recommend trying the free version on the app store first as you get most of there features of the game there but it's definitely worth buying this version if you are a dedicated player, as you get the extra features in game such as instant build, unlimited landmarks and plugins, 4 x speed, not waiting 10 minutes for the game to load all 700 plugins you downloaded!

The only downsides for me are:
- There can be a limit to creativity on larger maps. You can only put so much detail before it eventually starts to lag.
-sometimes plugins can conflict with each other or break a city/region (so download at your own risk)\t\t\t\t\t
