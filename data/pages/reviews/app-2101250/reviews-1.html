\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAwful game.
"Projectiles+" does literally nothing, be it the passive or upgrades. Or if it does anything, all the projectiles get layered on top of each other, as well as there being a wide window for projectiles to register hits, meaning that two shots would only be counted as 1 effectively, due to one killing an enemy and the other being deleted along with the first.
The "auto aim" seems to always select the objectively worst target to fire upon, making it both tedious and infuriating to play the game, as the optimal strategy is to fly in the opposite direction of where the ship is firing as it slightly increases the odds of landing a hit, which is a very bad strategy for this genre.
Weapon upgrades do too little to have any impact on level 3 and onwards. Basically impossible to beat level 3 without grinding the shop upgrades like crazy, which is not fun to do when the game is extremely slow for the first 70% of any level.
Unlocking new items in the hangar is too difficult due to the in-game item pool being too non-biased - it doesn't increase the chance of letting you select weapons/passives that your ship has equipped - which means that it's close to impossible to max out 2 weapons in one run.
Thought this would be a fun game to play, but nope. Very frustrating experience. Have already requested a refund.\t\t\t\t\t
