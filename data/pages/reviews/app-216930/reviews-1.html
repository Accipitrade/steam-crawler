\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMore of the same enjoyable gameplay as the first Luxor game, except there's now a Scorpion powerup.

WARNING!
Because MumboJumbo could not be bothered to patch the following two bugs out of the Steam version of this game:

  • Stage 9 in Adventure mode ends prematurely (One of the levels is misread as another from the previous Luxor HD game)
  • The path in the Anubis Aegyptus map is not optimised for widescreen (You can directly see how the spheres come in)

I strongly suggest downloading QuickBMS and the MumboJumbo.bms script, and then extracting the data.mjz file before patching the bugs out.

After extraction, you should find where the two bugs are located:

  1. For Stage 9 to play properly, jump into the directory:
    Luxor Amun Rising HD\\data\\levels\\adventure_luxor_ar
    There, you will find "level_9_4.lvl". Make an edit through Notepad or whatever. replace "imhotepsfolly" with "crossingthereedsea" and voila! The stage should now play properly!
  2. To optimise the entrance in the Anubis Aegyptus map for widescreen displays, jump into the directory:
    Luxor Amun Rising HD\\data\\maps\\anubisaegyptus
    There, you should edit the path.obj file with Notepad in the following manner: Replace "v 820 -40 0" to "v 1020 -40 0"
