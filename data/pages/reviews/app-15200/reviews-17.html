\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is the best sub-sim I ever played. At first i played with the game on auto-targeting until I got the feel for the controls and the commands (if you bought the game on the DVD, which I did the first time I bought this game, it came with a card you could hold in your lap to tell you which keys did what). At first I gave up a couple times, but after I applied myself and played the training missions I got a feel for the game and it became a lot easier. I learned how to do the manual targeting, which is fun, but the most fun is firing your torpedoes and watching them leave the sub and swim to the target ship - you can also track them on your map - and there's nothing like the feeling of victory when you see it hit! The missions and campaigns require a real dedication to this game as they are not kid stuff - navigating and attacking convoys will really test your skills. And this is still a tough game, and not without glitches. Half the time you command your men and they ignore you, it seems like. There are cheats you can download to speed up your sub and give your torpedoes near nuke-like destructive capabilities... but they also glitch your sub so use the cheats with caution since they might have unpleasant consequences. Anyway, I recommend this game, still plays on Win 10 last i checked, and you can get it for a pittance - I say give it a try!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was hesitant to purchase a 12 year old game. But all indications and articles I read was that this game was the defacto best simulation game for a submarine.
I also wanted a WWII American side war game.

This game delivers on both, aged as it is.

The graphics are not 2019 standards by any means but in general they are pretty darn good.
The mechanics of the gameplay and submarine simulation are amazing.

There is plenty to keep you interested.

I have been playing for about 40 days straight and have gone through a WWII career from 1941 to 1945. Lots of fun.
The modding community is strong and still vibrant and all the really good mods still work fine, if you take the tim eto investoigate how the game works with mods. Available on SUBSIM.com
BTW that is also the defacto forum to ask questions on. The steam forums are woefully vacant.

Running on Windows 10 required me to make sure I had the latest version. To get the v1.5 version you must buy the base game and the Uboat mission pack DLC. Then after install make sure you go to SUBSIM and get the memory changer program called LAA (Large Address Aware) and force the binary to use more than the 2Gb of ram it was originally allowed.
Then make sure the game exe SH4.exe is set for compatibility mode, either Win 7 or Vista.

Works great, no CTD's and I have a dozen mods loaded and running fine.

Buy it, you will enjoy it.

About Silent Hunter 4 vs 5. I have read a lot about the differences and v5 is somewhat buggy and adds just a bit more gameplay and graphics. I only bought v4 + the uBopat mission and have been quite happy. I dont expect v5 in my future anytime soon.\t\t\t\t\t
