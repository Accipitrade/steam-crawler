\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo this will be the last edit to my review for quite a while. And finally turning on the comments for people who have questions. So for starters the difficulty of this game is a mixed bag. I have managed to sneak into Convoys and Taskforce without a problem. I have done with with full realism and sunk a carrier and lived to tell the tale. But this is me, I learned most the mechanics and such before I started to play. For those new comers please understand this game will drive your patience, it will make you want to stop playing for weeks on end. Its not a game you can simply play for an hour or 2 hours and then be done, at least if your going for the end of the game. So with that out of the way lets talk about the modding community and how its mostly turned this game around for me. The modding community for this game is massive and very committed to finishing their product. You kinda have to when the last published product of this trade mark was pretty bad. So my recommendations are to us SH4 U-Boat Missions and any of the recommended mods. My personal recommendation is Fall of the Rising Sun as it improves the game, gives more ships, and is a rather good for the small errors that exist in it. So with all that said, how much does this feel like a sub game, I personally can say its a good experience for those who dont want the nightmare of a multiplayer sub game, its has mechanics that can be learned rather quickly and the difficulty of it can vary if your doing vanilla or a mod like fall of the rising sun. I would recommend if you are to get this game do get it with its DLC/Companion game U-Boat Missions and play through that so you can get a hold of all the Mods that are in store. I personally would rate this 7/10 on vanilla and a 9/10 with mods as for a old game from 2007 it does not feel that much aged from other games besides its graphics.\t\t\t\t\t
