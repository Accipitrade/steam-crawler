\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo far i've played for (at least) 100 hours Silent Hunter 3, 4 and 5 vanilla and modded.
Of all the titles, Silent Hunter 4 is my favorite.

Focusing on vanilla first, Silent Hunter 4 have game mechanics that i personally think are better. Meanwhile on SH3 you're limited to one simple mission per patrol that are ALWAYS patrol a designated area for a number of days, on SH4 you have a wider variety.
The missions go to the already known "patrol the designated area for x days/hours" to sink x tons of enemy shipping, spy/special forces insertion and recon (even though the last ones can be extremely annoying sometimes).
SH5 also have these types of missions, but, vanilla wise, SH5 is worse on a lot of aspects, but mostly realism and how buggy the game is.
But to not say just the good things, one aspect of the game that annoys me is the repair system. SH3 definetly does it better than SH4.

Even though the vanilla game is good, it really shines when modding (just like SH3 and SH5). Mods like Trigger Maru Overhaul, Fall of the Rising Sun Ultimate and Run Silent Run Deep add a lot of realism (making the game harder) and content to the game.

Personally, i would pick up first SH4. It's worth mentioning that if you want to play the game modded, you'll need the only DLC of the game: U-Boat Missions. Most of the mods can be find on Subsim (a submarine simulator forum).

Good hunting captain. Sink'em all!\t\t\t\t\t
