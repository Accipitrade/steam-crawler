\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhen it comes to submarine simulations, the Silent Hunter series is the best IMHO. It really has raised the bar very well for future installment of the genre. Silent Hunter 3 is still the King. It is a masterpiece, even with very little modding. Silent Hunter 4 is also GOOD - but there are items that disappoint me to no end (which is why I give this a thumbs down). Overall the interface to the game and crew management is much better in SH4. But, that is where the leadership stops. In SH3 you are in command of a revolutionary German U-boat in terms of technology and use of minimum resources/manpower. In SH4, the American submarine is so much better than anything the Japanese had to offer, it is not even a contest. But the gameplay really drives me up a wall. The main point of the game seems to be fending off the endless attacks of enemy aircraft. You can only stay underwater so long. Then you will have to fight them off using your guns. Realistic? Perhaps. But not very fun. There are good moments in the game. In one particular mission, I was able to sink a Japanese Fleet Carrier off of Midway. If the game focused mostly on shipping and stealth, I would be more happy with it. Then there are the bugs. When the U-Boat Missions expansion is installed, version 1.5 is installed for EXE file. You would think that after all these years, the bugs would be gone. But, they are not. The most obvious is a sick graphics error that shows your command crew still on the bridge of the conning tower even after dive dive diving. When in external view, you can see them search for enemy shipping with their binos while under the waves. Maybe they will find the ships I sunk while I was in SH3.\t\t\t\t\t
