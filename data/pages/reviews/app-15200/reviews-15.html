\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA fantastic follow up to the amazing third game, this time putting you in the shoes of an American captain fighting the Japanese in the Pacific War right after the Attack on Pearl Harbor.

Like the previous game, you have a dynamic campaign, war patrols and quick missions. I'm not to fussed on modding, but there is plenty of base content in this game to make it worth a purchase. At the time of this review, the game is on sale for $5. That is a fantastic bargain.

Game has a steep learning curve, but once you master it....it's addicting and super fun to play. Watching a torpedo attack succeed never gets old, the damage physics were honestly pretty stellar for a 2007 game. You can adjust the difficulty to your liking as well.

Silent Hunter III and Silent Hunter IV: Wolves of the Pacific are some of the best WWII naval games ever made. There is no reason to not give these games a shot, they still hold up to this very day\t\t\t\t\t
