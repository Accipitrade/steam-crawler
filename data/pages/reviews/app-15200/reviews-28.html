\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game has a lot to offer for just $10.

The game includes the following features:

  • Multiple controllable submarines of different sizes, capabilities, crew count and more.

  • A huge amount of ships both enemy and friendly.

  • Enemy threats not only include ships but also reconnaissance and attack aircraft of varying
    types that pose a massive danger if surfaced.

  • You can play many roles (sonar & radar operator, navigator, captain, gun operators (deckgun,
    AAA gun), torpedo tube operator, etc..)

  • The game allows you to organize your crew to specific tasks based on their experience; if
    some crew members are sleeping you can assign any other crew member their to position,
    same thing if they're wounded.

  • Ability to prioritize what problems are being dealt with (i.e. damaged bulkhead, periscope,
    etc..) by the crew and situation.

  • Real-time gameplay with the ability to fast forward as much as you like (not always the best
    idea to go too fast)

  • The game looks fantastic and is not very demanding at all.

  • Ability to travel the entire Pacific and possibly further (never tried).

  • Destroying enemy merchants, naval vessels, aircraft and such can be very rewarding
    especially when you are on a patrol. The game automatically writes logs of time date and
    location of neutralization of enemy activity.

  • Game has a few different types of missions including rescue missions in enemy waters,
    searching to destroy specific vessels, war patrols, support missions and much more.


This game can bring hours of slow paced and intense experiences if you play it right.

The game is not hard as long as you know what you're doing. I highly recommend looking up some
tutorials on Youtube and Google right away because the game's tutorial isn't very good to be
honest. Once you accomplish that the game will make a lot more sense and you'll have a lot more
fun and struggle much less.

The game goes on sale often, but right now at $10 it is completely worth it.\t\t\t\t\t
