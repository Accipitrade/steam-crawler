\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFolks this game, in it's current state, does not deserve the mindless praise. I'd say its not worth the money quite yet.

The combat is shallow because you only command movement each turn.

The loot is shallow because items within the same category (skinny sword, medium sword, fat sword) are all the same stats.

There aren't a large variety of enemies or possible pokemon allies (19?) which comprises everything you will see or fight in the game).

One could argue the gear is what brings variety to enemies and allies but as I've said, the gear is largely cosmetic.

Theres lovely art and lovely humor. Maps are (edit: feel) procedurally generated and change (edit: relocate you) every few outings. But the game is simply too shallow to warrant the money right now. Hopefully they have more units in the pipeline, and are going to change the stats of items.

Right now: 5/10\t\t\t\t\t
