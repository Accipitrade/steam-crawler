\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA humorous and lovely game; fun for solo or with a friend. Although it belongs to the RPG category, you don't really need to grind experience for your characters. The leveling-up acts as a healing mechanic rather than a way to upgrade and enhance characters. Players can casually proceed the way however they wish without facing difficulty issues.
One minor downside of this game is that it has a short story. You can probably "finish" the game within a couple hours. However, there are a lot of items and characters to collect. Much effort is put into the minor quests, which gives a diverse gameplay and really reveals the full potential of its design. My wife and I really like this game.\t\t\t\t\t
