\r\n\t\t\t\t\tShould you buy this game right now? If you like strategy, good music, a genuinely funny narrative and don't mind keeping yourself entertained with online battles and side quests for now - yes.

I have played every single one of Behemoth's games and this one is my favourite.
It has the character, humour and art style you'd expect, along with added replayibility and deceptively simple strategic gameplay that's unlike any other they've made.

Just note however, that the story mode, which can be played co-op, is not yet complete (in case you didn't read the game summary) and I finished what currently exists of it in around 4-5 hours. There's a plethora of side quests though and when they eventually run dry for you there's always the fantastic PvP and 2v2 PvP to sink your teeth into and that alone (like other reviews have already stated) is worth the price tag. It has the potential to develop a meta-game where strategies are consistently developed and improved upon with age.

If you need another reason to support the Early Access:
Behemoth are actively working with, and listening to any community feedback they receive.

There you have it.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\ttactics rpg with some really interesting twists i haven't seen in the genre before and a pretty strong basis in collectable monsters. i sunk a pretty notable portion of my playtime so far just running around the open world and capturing opponents for their gear or to build new teams, and the gameplay surrounding the constant goal of collecting a lot of dudes is excellent.

pit people puts the sorts of elements into the tactics rpg genre that you would expect from the behemoth (battles are super chaotic and ♥♥♥♥ explodes constantly, you can't directly influence every single thing your units do to the same degree that you can in other games, etc) and it works shockingly well. i didn't really feel the gameplay (worth noting that this has only been in early access for like 3 months as of this review) growing stagnant, largely because the tone and pace are preeetty casual and laid back most of the time, and battles are visually entertaining enough that things consistently feel interesting and new.

the only current downside is the actual story is super dang short, but again, early access, and i believe the next update is focused on story improvements so no big deal there. there's also enough side content to keep you entertained for at least a dozen or two hours, w/ sidequests being available both at your hub and scattered across the world, and the whole appeal of the "gotta catch em all" kinda gameplay. the humor can be a bit hit or miss if you aren't into poop jokes, i guess, so that's worth noting. also, i haven't tried the coop or multiplayer in general yet, so i can't weigh in on that.

pit people's a promising as hell title though, i rarely play an early access game and immediately feel like i got my money's worth. i'm really excited to see this thing completed.\t\t\t\t\t

Edit #2 March 2/18

Great game, tons of ways to personalize your team (appearance and composition), as always The behemoth never fails to make a fun game. Amazing competitive play that lets you try and go againts many types of enemy comps also co-op it´s really fun and would definitely recomend getting a friend to play with.

A little bit on the down side, game it´s still on an early stage and while PVP/co-op it´s practically fully implemented the current campaign is very short, however theres A LOT of mini quests to play and they are fairly challenging and fun, theres a good amount of units to costumize your team and strategy but would love to see more units get added in the future.

See you guys in the pit!

Edit: I think i have played this quite a while now to write a fair review, while the game is very good considering how much of it we get to play, it becomes quite dull once you have completed all the missions and it becomes a game where you just farm until you get that cosmetic thing you wanted or capture that rare enemy on some mission, dont get me wrong, i like that but i wish that there was more to it, like ultra hard missions with unique equipment loot, the pit is also very easy to grind and that golden gear well i wish it was more of a challenge to get, the pvm pit side can be farmed really easy and the pvp side its empty 23hours a day, its almost impossible to get a match, i wish they would nerf the pvm rewards (pit points, money and loot) and add a little buff to the pvp maybe just like the pvm, win 3 in a row get a bigger reward or anything else i dont know just something that attracts more people to pvp rather than the endless farm.

Now my main issue with the game is combat, whiles theres many combinations and character you can choose from, combat gets quite repetitive and boring later on, i feel like theres not enough creatures to actually make combat diverse, also the little interactivity just makes it boring after playing it several hours i really hope a lot more creatures get added in the future as well as something that makes combat more interactive, maybe somekind of unique special ability depending of whos your team leader, this game need work but it isnt bad at all, i would have gladly payed twice the amount for it on its current state.

Edit2: Oh boy... i like this game but its boring later, like REALLY boring, just finished the campaign and it was pretty fun, there where some parts that introduced something diferent from other than taking down walls and killing everybody but after that the game becomes boring again, dont get me wrong, game is good and definitely worth the price however unless you are an achivementwhore (like me) or really want to see all this game has to offer, you will probably just play it around 10-20 hours at most before you get bored because the game is pretty much the same, i am disapointed because theres where no new unit added once it came out of early access, no real strategy needed tbh and probably the worst thing about it is that theres no one playing pvp or unfair co-op, i´d say you buy this if you want a funny and challenging story with plenty of good extra quests and stuff to do, if you want an strategy pvp game, look somewhere else because you wont find it here sadly.\t\t\t\t\t
