\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrom the creators of the amazing games that are Castle Crashers and Battleblock Theater, Pit People shares an iconic artstyle from Dan Paladin that has been gracing his games with for over a decade now. Despite this however, Pit People definitely holds its own from the rest of The Behemoth's titles. With countless amounts of unlockables this game will have a grasp on the completionists for a good while. Offering 18 or so unit types the team combinations are quite varied. It is unlikely you will see 2 team compositions back to back and even less likely that these team combinations will have the same gear. Strategy is up to the user and many troops have varying success rates depending on what their teammates are. Team synergy and positioning is the name of the game for Pit People and the game makes it quite obvious. Cupcakes can heal most units in the game but when it comes to the Electrobot unit type they cannot. The player learns that the Robots would do better in a team with Gnomes instead as the Gnomes can heal the Electrobots.

The game has countless hours of playtime. I played it in Xbox Game Preview and decided to get it on steam as well. Even as an Early Access title I would highly recommend this game. The main thing you don't have access to in early access is the complete story. You still get the first handful of missions but the final ones are not in it yet. You still have access to a large quantity of side quests and the online Pvp and Unfair challenge let you test yourself and recieve exclusive rewards for ranking high in the leaderboard among 99 other players. The game is great. The main things I hope for in a future update(s) is a that the story hopefully has more than just one more mission for the cutscenes are great. The game could standalone from its story without a problem, the story is just the icing on top. My biggest wish is hopefully after launch the game continues to update with new items (like a costume for the mascot that makes it look like the Raccoon from Battleblock if that can't become a new unit) and I cross my fingers for a potential new unit. We already have a good handful to select from but I have been playing for a while on Game Preview and I have played around with the units a lot. One more unit would be an awesome surprise. The game is awesome and the devs are just as awesome. They listen to the community unlike many developers nowadays and make good changes in their games. Keep it up The Behemoth.\t\t\t\t\t

This game is great! Takes me back to a game called Ogre for my Apple IIE (yes i am old)
you can customize the people in your party. you can customize the hats they wear. you can customize the weapons and/or shields they wear.
there are so many types(species) of characters you can choose from to create your war party. the weapon load out for them is amazing. Uzi's all the way down to "well i'll just throw cheese at you" 2 handed greatswords...or...well...a huge 2 handed bomb.
The narration throughout the story line is so funny. The mini quests are funny. The writers for this game know what they are doing.
The soundtrack is amazing. very catchy. one of few games i leave the music turned on for.
I have played multiplayer with a buddy via Skype. it was hysterical! we were shouting in anger and laughing the entire time.
I cant stress enough how great this game is.

however. after a few months of playing it daily, i felt burned out. I unlocked, what i felt, was 3/4 of the weapons and hats. I felt like there was nothing left to do. i still revisit the game. but it needs more content. maybe more story line, or a different character race to spice things up.
all in all. you are missing out if you dont try it. Im telling you, you will like it.\t\t\t\t\t
