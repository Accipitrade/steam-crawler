Fun story.
Unique setting.
Nice artwork.
Great voice-acting.

CONS:
Tactical combat is very, very limited and repeditive.
Poor interface and controls makes unit selection and clarity a real chore.
Vast majority of battles are cakewalks that require no strategy.
Poor itemisation makes vast majority of loot useless.
Characters don't seem to get perceptively stronger no matter how much you level them up.
Very limited selection of quest types.


Hopefully everything about the combat and loot is going to get overhauled in a future patch, but until then I'd discorage buying this unless you're purely interested in the story and comedy elements.
