\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game was basically a strategy game made for me. I was always a strategy game fan but realized most of the time that the systems in place were a bit too complex with loads of different stats to consider. While a lot of people may enjoy that, It really isn't the case for me since it just leaves me more tired than fulfilled/happy after engaging with it for too long.

The game wasn't like that from my experience. There were around 4-5 main stats with some minor stuff like occasional resistances, vulnerabilities and critical chance items. The creature choice was also diverse and was one of the main reasons for me to keep going back as I found a play style that I really liked (1-2 support units and then all the same copies of one creature).

Another big plus is the art style and the music. After the intricacies of the game started to wane despite the solid gameplay, what makes me keep coming back for the occasional hunt is just the way the game is presented. It's unique and doesn't take itself too seriously among the plethora of dark / more serious iterations of strategy games even if the humor can be polarizing.

All in all, I very much recommend it for those who might be interested in at least one of the things I mentioned.\t\t\t\t\t
