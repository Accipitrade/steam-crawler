\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's like Chess, but with more cupcakes.

It's very weird.

Visually it's outlandish enough to be interesting without being overwhelming. There's just the right amount of goofiness happening on-screen.

The soundtrack is perfect, for my taste. It deftly backs up the visual and game-play atmospheres without distracting from them.

There isn't much of a PVP scene, so keep that in mind if you enjoy pitting yourself against others in these types of games. It doesn't bother me but it bears mentioning.

I've never experienced anything I'd call a significant bug. No crashes, no lost loot or progress. No problems with multiplayer connectivity. The game provides a stable and simple platform and does it well.

Highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
