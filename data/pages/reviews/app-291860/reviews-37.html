\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is super good, don't get me wrong. There's a lot I haven't even done and I've played 100 hours. The combat is easy to get into. There's no one class that feels super op compared to others besides Cyclops. Even then those are natural carriers, while other two slot takers can be very useful if used correctly. Thought does matter, and the strategy can be pretty difficult in some scenarios. The only thing I've got to say is wait for the game to go on sale. 20$ for this game is a little steep for how much the main story offers. Not much replay value there, and if you replay for the quests on the open map, it's more repetitive than fun. Play if you're fine with 100% the game, and then leaving to expand your horizons. I give Pit People a 7/10.\t\t\t\t\t
