\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm a fan of games where you can build an improve your own team of characters in an open world enviroment. I'll go ahead and list some pros and cons like some people do to help give you an idea of what you could expect playing the game.

PROS:
- Tons of Items, allows you to customize the appearance of your characters
- A lot of classes. Lets you experiment with a bunch of different team compositions
- Good humor. Albeit there isn't story yet, but as of this review the game hasnt even been in early access for two weeks.

CONS:
- Poor menu and interface design. After an hour or two I got the hang of it, but it's quite confusing when you're first thrown into it.
- Low resolution assets. I don't know why its this way, but everything is slightly pixelated. I'm confused why the resolution of the assets in the game isn't higher? I don't see a reason why they wouldn't be. (And yes, I'm playing at 1080p with max settings, I've checked like five times.)
- Level scaling is irrelevant. As of update 1, they've partially addressed this but not really. Your characters can level up from 1 to 99, but you'll quickly realize it doesn't make much different. In reality, a level 99 character is barely ~25% stronger than a level 1 character. That's unimpressive.

Overall great game. Had it less than a week and nearly 24 hours. They're already addressing issues the community has expressed problems with, so go ahead and buy it and enjoy!\t\t\t\t\t

The music are so great i have to listen to them again on youtube.
The gameplay: recruit and loots are shining imo, that makes exploring the world map is not boring at all! One thing i dont like about the item system is that there are too many weapons/helmets with the same stats (which makes them JUST different by the appearance).

WHAT I THINK SHOULD HAVE BEEN THERE:

SHOULD have boss battles.
SHOULD have more quest type other than just go to some point and win the fight there.
SHOULD have more skills (only use 3 attacks all the game is not fun at all, should have a talent tree with at least 5 skill (one ultimate) for each character/recruit), this will adds more depth to the combat system.
SHOULD adjust ranged attacks: it is fine right now, but can be improve imo. I think there should be only one range enemy with decent damage that rushs player to kill him rather than multiple archers dealing 1/2 dam (which is not threatening at all!)
SHOULD continue the main quest, and make it a long and memorable journey.

So far i can't join any pvp match, but the co.op against bot is really fun.
Recommended this to anyone who up to fun strategy RPG game.\t\t\t\t\t
