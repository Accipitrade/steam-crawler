This game is difficult, the gameplay never got old (for me at least), and the story is silly but good. There is only one voice actor which is the narrator, the other characters just do these weird sounds and you read what they say, which is something I'm fine with for the characters still have unique personalities and enough distinction to be memorable.

The game is strategic, and very in how the player wants to play the game which is quite nice. The side quests are very fun and unique to be worth it, exploration is also fun and worth while.

The only negative being once you found a team that really works you see no reason to change it up, this happens either really early or about half way through the game.\t\t\t\t\t
