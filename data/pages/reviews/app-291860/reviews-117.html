Never before have I been hooked by a game so quickly. The Behemoth are now 4 for 4 and I couldn't be more stoked about it.

The gameplay is grid-based & turn-based RPG team combat ... but the real joy lies in the execution of it all. The art, the story, the characters, the hilarious way they speak, the vibrant world. It's fun in its purest digital form.

Will Stamper as the Narrator is sublime, following on from his role in Battleblock Theater. I just love his delivery - it gets me chuckling every time.

The soundtrack is top-notch as well and I've found myself looking it up on YT a few times. Patric Catani has really captured the chaotic cartoony absurdity of 'Pit People'. "It's Us" (i.e. the opening theme) is a personal favourite of mine.

Love the game, love what The Behemoth is doing, glad I bought it, wish more people would too.\t\t\t\t\t
