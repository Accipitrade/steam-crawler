It's been a while since this game was released (and its no longer in early access) so I wanted to add a few things to my review...

Quite a few things have changed since release, and pretty much all the changes were for the better, but the game doesn't feel complete to me, (I have no idea if the story is actually finished, I haven't bothered yet). I'm mostly talking about the lack of units in this game. There are 19 different units in this game which is a decent amount honestly. But I feel like more could be added. I would love to see cool units like wizards, aliens (still not sure about that one) and other stuff that would be cool to deploy in battle.
I also wish they would add armor to this game, it doesn't even have to do anything, I just want my dudes to look a bit more unique body-wise.

Those are some suggestions for what I think should be added. Doubt any of my ideas will actually be put into the game but you never know I guess.

If you like complex tactical turn-based strategy games you might want to pass on this one. This game is more for people who like a not-too-difficult, casual turn-based strategy game, which I don't mind.
I would recommend getting it on sale as well, like in the summer sale that's going on right now! How convenient!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst of all: I rarely ever buy into Early access games these days, to many times have I been burnt by games that were pretty much abandonned before they were finished. Therefore I was a little hesitant to buy this one, but ultimatily decided to do so, because the Behemoth is a studio that made 2 really good games already(battleblock theater beeing one of my favourit plattformers of all time).

I went in, expecting a casual strategie game, but I got was so much more!

The humor just makes me smile, which few games manage to do these days, but there is just so much detail and intersting stuff everywhere in the world, that I just can't help but aimlessly wander around, looking forward to what kind of adventure is gonna happen next!

The gameplay itself is a simpel, but solid strategiegame, with a unique approch to local and online multiplayer.

My personal favourit though, is the "Pokemon"-aspect of the game, letting you capture pretty much anything you encounter in battle. More often then not, I find myself doing quests in very difficult ways, just so I can save the "boss" of said quest till last, so I will be able to capture it and put it in my team(I especially like having Lady Ladylegs and Yosef in the same team), after outfitting it with a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ of cosmetics first!

You can blast through the 2 mainstoryquests that are already in quite fast, most of my playtime was seeking out sidestorys(which I love to do, because all of them are hillarious) and capturing whatever boss is at the very end, although I still haven't found all of them.

Of course, since this is an early access review, things might change drasticly at any point of the development process, but I see an insane potential, that just increases with every new capturable creature you put in this awesome world!\t\t\t\t\t
