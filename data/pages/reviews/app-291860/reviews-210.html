\r\n\t\t\t\t\t* Do remember this is Early Access, and as such the value of this title as I review it. My review is based mostly upon the mechanics and what is here, rather than a story that has yet to unfold.

Behemoth once again delivers a class A experience with their one of a kind over the top humor. A robust and expansive world, a myriad of memorable characters, and enough loot to make your butt fall off. Pit People has a diverse and simplistic combat style accessible to players new to the strategy RPG format. Yet still it goes so much deeper than that to the true strategist. Offering near limitless customization this game allows for any playstyle. From people who like to hide behind ponies, to those who crave the boom boom, to those who simply love rainbows. Whether you are a tried and true fan of Behemoth and their past works, or a newcomer to the party, I do believe this is shaping up to be one of the best games of the coming year.

That being said, if you are looking strictly to play a large story filled with conquest, glory, and pain, Pit People may not be the title you want...YET. As it sits you will not run short of things to do, amongst them being PVP, Co-Op shenanigans, loot hunting, side quests, and dying with laughter. This is not a typical gotcha early access. What you get right now, without the story, is well beyond the price value. Behemoth made the right decision to let us get the jump start on such a sweet game.


Pros:

+ Excellent Voiceover
+ Wonderful and Diverse Art
+ A+++ Soundtrack
+ Fluid and Simple Combat
+ Deep Customization
+ Fantastic Price Point
+ Contains the Color Teal
+ Fantastic and Lag Free PVP Servers
+ Co-Op Couch Play (Bless you Behemoth for keeping the old ways alive.)
+ Early Access (This game is well and far beyond what I expected to get at day 1 early access.)
+ Flawless Controller Play

Cons (Most Likely To Disappear by Full Release) :

- Early Access (This is only a con because my body is ready for the whole thing.)
- Difficulty Curve for The Unsuspecting/New Player (A.I.'s beyond story mode is a CHALLENGE, PVP garnishes more experienced SRPG Players)
- Keyboard/Mouse Controls Need Refined
- Can't Buy The Soundtrack\t\t\t\t\t
