A very good game, even in Early Access!
Funny and fun, here's some bullet points on why:
  • Extremely fun Co-op
  • Still incredibly fun Singleplayer
  • Really cheesy and hilarious dialogue
  • Lots of character-types to collect
  • Large variety of characters in those character-types
  • "Rock, Paper, Scissors"-esque style of gameplay in relation to weapons and powers
I've played this game both single- and multiplayer and have beaten the main story that has been released up to the time of this review. After 28 hours of playing, I still have yet to find and beat all of the Side-Quests, which is pretty incredible since the quality of these are very high.

Some of the enemies are inherently less fun to fight than others most of the time though.
