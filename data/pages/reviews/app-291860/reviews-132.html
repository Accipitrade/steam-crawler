\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA brilliant start to a possibly brilliant game, ive heard a lot of bad things about this early access game such as it has no strategy and the gameplay is rather shallow but I think Pit People is on its way to be a great strategy game.

The story is humorous and interesting and I'm loving the direction its taking.

The units already in the game have great synergy with each other allowing for multiple team options. The controls while a little confusing at first are really simple and easy to get used to.

A few things it still needs
1) More people to play PVP (Although the PVP is still really good for Early Access)
2) More different units for more diversity, honestly the more you add the more strategy the game will have.
3) PVP should set the levels of all units to a certain level if it doesn't already. This would mean anyone can play PVP at any level and point in the game.
4) Some Units may need some balancing, such as the Units taking up 2 slots may need a very slight damage buff, mainly Hair Troll as Gorgons seemed balanced with their spawn ability.

Overall a great game and one im looking to being finished.

8/10 so far
