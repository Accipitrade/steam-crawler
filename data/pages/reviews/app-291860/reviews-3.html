Hell, the image of a random dude sporting two SMGs while riding a giraffe propelled by balloons will be forever engraved in my mind! But the gameplay gets tiring very, very fast. It often feels like you aren't strategizing at all and just looking at things happening and hoping they go your way. It just doesn't make me play want to play more despite all of it's charm.

Maybe I'll give this game a fourth chance someday, but for now I'd just rather recommend Castle Crashers or Battleblock Theater if you found that Pit People's aesthetic and humor is something you would enjoy.\t\t\t\t\t
