\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGLANCE RECOMMENDATION: 83% YES / 17% NO

GOOD
  • Crazy, cute art that you've come to know and love from The Behemoth.
  • Music is snazzy and catchy. You can collect sound tracks to change your in town music, too!
  • There are way more unit types than I could have ever guessed. Fun units like the reaper, pixie, and the weird octoclops. It's a shame I could never make some of the odder units work, like troll mama or zombies.
  • Genuinely funny dialogue with an entertaining story I didn't expect.
  • There's a butt load of quests in the world and from the town, offering quite a few fun side missions. There's an abundance of stuff to do both between and after the story.

NEUTRAL
  • Map is ok. It looks impressive and has a lot to do, especially with the different resets the space bear does eveyr now and then, but the fog of war is almost TOO close.
  • I wish having the recruiter didn't feel so mandatory. For most of my gameplay I had a human with the recruiter equipped just so I could grab some of the last species I desperately tried to find, which wasn't good in my opinion since I really wanted her off the team. She could apply some utility with different elemental weapons, but I never did enjoy how often I had to have her.

BAD
  • The main cast of characters the story employs are often those you probably won't use. With more interesting units like hte troll and mascot, why have 4 humans on your team? It's a real bummer when they had to come with you on your missions.

SUMMARY
This is one of the few games I could safely support during Steam Early Access. It's a wonderful title from The Behemoth that, while not offering the replayability of Castle Crashers, gives you enough to see, hear, and do while battling a space bear or whatever host of zombie/troll/pixies you find.\t\t\t\t\t
