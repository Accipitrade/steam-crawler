This Game Rocks!

I have never been a fan of any sort of turn based games, but this one is a true gem. My skepticism was snuffed out by the humor and large amount of working content already available. The turn based combat can be rather amusing and I've even been on the edge of my seat in some of the battles, too.

The co-op is incredibly fun with good friends. Hours have been lost trying to build the right tag team combo with, of course, the coolest looking apparel for my troops.

I understand if you're not a fan of turn based games, but give this one a shot. It's way better with friends and the mindless exploring isn't too bad.

My one and only con for this game is the story block. At the moment, the story is a work in progress and seems to be releasing in increments. That's not entirely bad, but I'd really love to dig into it all at once and see where Horatio ends up.

Other than that, this game is great and would recommend to anyone!\t\t\t\t\t
