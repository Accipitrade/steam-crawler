\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlayed this game for almost two hours and I couldn't get really into it. Sure the beginning is simple and the narritive is hilarious, but it soon transformed into a game where I didn't really know what was happening or were I was going. At first I thought this would be a turned based strategy in the likes of Fire Emblem or Advance Wars. As it progressed you notice there are a lot of random elements, like your fighter chosing who to attack randomly, and your archer shooting at random people. You get a feeling you're not in control of the game and it almost playes automatically. Maybe this is what the creators wanted? Maybe thats the point of the game? I don't know. Try the game, maybe it's fun for you! Just a warning you might not like the game if you're a die-hard Fire Emblem fan.\t\t\t\t\t

The combat boils down to moving your units to their optimal positions, pressing space, and just letting the turn play out. Over and over. It's very shallow for this genre, seems like the type of thing someone would play on their phone to kill time. Recruiting units was fun for a bit, but it doesn't do enough to shake up the gameplay.
