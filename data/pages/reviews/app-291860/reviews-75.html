It's supposed to be a strategic game of positioning but position doesn't seem to do anything. People behind cover can still be shot with arrows, same if they're behind your shield man even though the game tells you to position the shield in front of other characters to give them protection from arrows. Rear attacks don't seem to matter either. Can't tell how powerful a group is on the world map so there's no way to strategize who to engage and who to avoid. So many things are also left unexplained that really should have a bullet point or some sort of explanation.

The same humor is here and I really enjoyed that, but this game really doesn't come together very well for a positional strategy game.\t\t\t\t\t
