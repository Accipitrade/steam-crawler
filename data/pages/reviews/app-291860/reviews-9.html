\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a fan of a lot of other games by this studio this game disappointed me. The core gameplay is fine, I guess, but everything else is just so boring. None of the characters have special abilities, none of them are interesting, and their designs are really boring. When you are the actual battle part of the game you don't even get to choose what enemies your troops attack, they just walk up to a group of enemies and choose one to attack. But one of the worst parts of it all is that the tutorial is really bad, and when it introduces a new mechanic it's does a really bad job at telling you how to do it. Also speaking of mechanics there barely is any at all to begin with. Overall this game is boring, it looks boring, and I strongly suggest you don't buy it. I wish I got a refund when I had the chance.\t\t\t\t\t
