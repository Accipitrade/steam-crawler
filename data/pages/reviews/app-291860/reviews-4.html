\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe game runs perfectly on Steam Deck, uses very little battery, has no technical issues whatsoever, it's not an early access, and has no microtransactions. You're buying a full game with no ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥.

It's a turn-based, Adventure Time style brawler. The game brings to my mind j-rpg: you find story NPCs you can recruit to your team, but you can also recruit normal NPCs. You can find or buy armour, weapons and fighters. You level up your characters, but you can't distribute attributes yourself, that happens automatically, like first ever old-school RPGs.

It can get pretty repetitive and a bit slow-paced, but the visuals, music and wacky story keeps me playing. It would be a meme-game if not for the fact that it's really good and really funny.

There is a number of activities you can take part in the game. You have PVP arena, co-op, NPC arena, NPCs to fight in the overworld map, mini-quests on the overworld map, multi-staged side-quests, and of course the main story.

I haven't finished the game yet and I played over 34h at the time of writing this. I fully recommend if you're looking for a light-hearted turn based battle game for your Deck.\t\t\t\t\t
