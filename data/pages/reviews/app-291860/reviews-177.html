\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a review for an Early Access Game. Opinions herein are subject to change upon advancements in the games development. This review will be updated as each new patch rolls out for the game.

Pit People.. Pit People.. This game is absolutely bonkers, and I adore it so for such. Whilst the storyline is nowhere near finished (Early Access) there are a plethora of missions and so on to embark on, as well as countless enemies to "Recruit" (Kidnap) into your party. My current party is a Hairball which regenerates health per turn, and hits for massive damage, a cupcake which heals my party members by slowly killing itself, your typical knightly party member but with an Elvis haircut, a Unicorn that fires exploding rainbow artillery horns, and a recruitment person. Party diversity is key.

Pros:

- Fantastic Soundtrack, which I've listened to outside of the game for hours.

- Easy to understand movement system, as well as targetting.

- Diverse enemies available to be recruited. From Mushrooms that fart on you and flip you off, to frogs that explode.

- Hilarious questlines, which take an hour or so to complete if focused on completing the entire quest line.

- Difficulty scaling is quite fair, with the ability to set the difficulty to "Insane" at any point.

- Easily organized co-op, as well as split screen co-op using Mouse+KB and a controller.

- Humorous "language" spoken by the characters within the game.

- Exploration where you can ignore any/all enemies if you like.


Cons:

- Minor Framerate issues whilst exploring (Though, once more as stated above, it is an Early Access title, and this is subject to change).

- Rather boring equipment system, where each weapon is generally cosmetic depending on their grouping.

- Lack of Micromanagement aside from moving characters to an area, without the ability to designate specific targets, though this is most likely intentional.


Overall: I'd rate the game an 8, however relatively close to being a 9. It lacks polish, as it is an Early Access game, however I am extremely excited to see what the future holds in regards to this game.\t\t\t\t\t
