This game is turn based strategy similar to X-Com or Final Fantasy Tactics set to a hex grid rather than the standard square
You get your standard Rock Paper Scissors element with various races being weak or strong to different damage types
It stands apart from the rest with one simple mechanic: you can't control who your troops attack
The battlefield is a rapidly changing mess of trying to position your fighters so they can ONLY hit who you want

The game has everything I wanted from a good game: strategic depth, good humor, cool art style, and a fun soundtrack\t\t\t\t\t
