\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuch a fun and fluid game, I was a fan of Behemoths previous games but to me this is the best one yet. Turn based strat games don't always appeal to me but here they have done away with a lot of the micromanagement whilst still retaining the depth and fun. Couple that with the "recruit" (basically kidnap your team members into slavery) mechanic, the mind boggling amount of team combinations, 2 player, The Pit where you can PvE or PvP and the ludicrous amount of loot and this is a no brainer.

I am trying not to burn out on the game as its in EA but I keep coming back for more, there is already a staggering amount of content with more to come, highly recommended.
