\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, having spent over a day on this game, completing the storyline, and multiple sidequests, I figured it was time to write a review. First off, keep in mind this game is in beta, and not nearly finished (at least I hope not.)

Concept
So this game is a turn-based rpg kind of deal. The main concept is that a giant space bear smashed into your planet and shattered it into hexagons. Throughout most of the story, there is a narrator who clearly dislikes Horatio, your character.
In essence, this game is you going around and fighting things, as well as capturing enemies and adding them to your party.

Story
At this point, the main storyline is fairly short, with a grand total of two missions. That being said, this is the third update, so that's definitely going to change. There are also quite a few sidequests that you can complete, and lots of random encounters of varying difficulty. Lets also not forget what gives the game it's name; The Pit. This is a gladitorial style arena where your team of characters fights against three other groups with increasing difficulty. The Pit yields many rewards, and is a good way to level up and pass time when you're not playing a mission.

Battles
In the battles, each character has a specific amount of hexagon tiles they can move per turn. You chose where they move, and they chose which enemy to attack (if you moved them so they can attack multiple enemies) Each party member can be customized to have different attacks. There are also different creatures that do different things, like healing cupcakes, mind-warping octopi, poison mushrooms, and massive cyclopses.

Anyways, I'm guessing there's gonna be alot more updates to come, so we'll see what the Behemoth has in store for us next.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnother favorite made by Behemoth. In its current state (Early Access update 3), it stands strong on an excellent foundation for what can be a masterpiece once finished. I'll list some pros and cons below for its current state.

PROS:

Artwork.
Voice acting.
Funny.
Turn-based made fun.
World exploration.
Co-op, both local and online!
Daily leader board with rewards (replay value).
World seems to have 'pick up or ignore' side quests.
Capturing new recruits to join your team is currently my favorite part.
The weapon and shield choices are great, and hilarious! Spatula for a sword, large piece of bacon for a shield, lol!

CONS:

Main storyline is limited to only one, really awesome btw, quest once the tutorial missions have been completed.
Sound track is VERY limited at this point, and music can get repetitive.
There needs to be about 20 more monster types added for capturing and battle variety.

CONCLUSION
Great game that's worth a buy already. Made by a team you can trust to see the project through to the end and finish with a great product. Highly recommended, worth it for the laughs alone.
