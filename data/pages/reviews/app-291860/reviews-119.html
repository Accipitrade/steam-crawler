\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFun. Just a fun game.
It's challenging but fair.
As will all of Behemoths games, this one is hilarious, has a great soundtrack, and controls fantastically well.
Would recommend 9/10 game, and it's only in early access

Would like to see more characters, more customisability on classes that aren't humans (but what's already there is great) and some more variety in what weapons and gear does. Maybe an option for a character to say, take off their pants in order to carry more gear, something funny and in keeping with the games atmosphere.
Possibly some kind of changes to the roster setup? Because at the moment having 6 character slots not be unlocked from the get go is a little redundant, seeing as you can unlock both of them in about 20 mins of play after starting the game. Just seemed a little odd how easy it was to get - there was no sense of progression. Maybe unlock them after doing quests for the town or something. Or they unlock by completing random quests that aren't one of the 4 options you get on leaving the city? Just an idea, so that there's an element of choice as to whether you want to rush the story quests and ignore side quests, but have less characters, in an effort to make things more challenging, whilst it also acts as an inscentive to do the other optional quests out and about in the world.
Just an idea.

What is already here is fantastic, and so much fun to play.
Eagerly awaiting all that they add in the future

Oh and 1 more thing, I think it'd be really cool if there was an alternate ending for dying in the tutorial when the bear tells you to die
also it would be nice if when you get the rewards for the Pit, it told you what your final ranking was on the leaderboard. Just a minor thing that would add to the experience a little\t\t\t\t\t
