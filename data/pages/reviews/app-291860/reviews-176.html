\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is an amazing game!


When I saw what type of game this was it was an automatic turn off. I don't like the turn based genre. After seeing YouTubers playing it, I decided to give it a try. I fell in LOVE! Behemoth turned the genre into a more simpler, less complicated game (which I love). This game is another masterpiece from Behemoth. The quests are fun, the narrator (I'm so happy they brought him into this game) is hilariously dark, and 'The Pit' is the cherry on top. Battleing 2v2 is so much fun! I'm so glad they made it 2v2 instead of just 1v1 because then I can blame my failures on my team mate. But seriously, it is much better working together than grinding on your own. The last game I played (in the pit), I was going to keep one of my characters where they were and didn't realize there was a way better spot, but my teammate shook his cursor in the direction of where I should move my character and I avoided a careless mistake. I can't wait to tell my friends to buy this game and play 2v2 with them!

Another feature I like is the "pokemon" feel when I recruit an enemy that looks super cool and then being able to customize them. I LOVE the ability to customize my team. I LOVE even more the crazy weapons and hats you can equip to make your characters look like whatever you want them to look like! I made my cyclops have Sofia's head. (looks so weird).

Behemoth, my hat's off to you for making another amazing, extremely customizable, and simple/fast to play. I spent only 28 minutes each doing two games in the pit and managed to get up to the top of the leaderboard. (I'm not trying to brag BTW). People are probably passing me right now :(. Anyways, I can't express how much joy this game has brought me! The gameplay is fun, interesting, and does require skill. (and good characters. obviously).

VERDICT:

Totally worth the $15! Buy right now! Even if you don't like the genre, you will like it after playing this game! Behemoth always turns an ordinary genre of games into an amazing, totally wacky, and hilarious one! Castle Crashers, Battleblock Theater, and especially Pit People are all examples of the genius of Behemoth! MUST BUY!\t\t\t\t\t
