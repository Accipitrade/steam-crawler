\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPretty good turn-based tactics wargame.
Build your party, equip them and go out brawling.

The game is extremely accessible (for a tactical wargame). Units have weaknesses and resistances. And a variety of attacks. But you only control where they will move. Their attacks are sort of "pseudo deterministic". At some range it always uses a certain type of attack. But, with multiple enemies in range, it will attack one at random.
Knowing the units and thinking about movement is the core of the game.

"Scary-cute" art style, with lots of humour. It ranges from pop-culture related stuff to social satire to toilet humour.
Fantastic soundtrack.

But, the favourite plus is that my non-gamer girlfriends absolutely adores the game. She loves the funny creature voices. And the game being so accessible helps too.\t\t\t\t\t
