\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA really good turn based strategy game. I don't usually care for TBS games so I was kinda iffy on it even though it's by Behemoth. After watching some gameplay I finally talked myself into buying it and I was not disappointed. All the funny jokes and gags are there that you would expect from a Behemoth game.

Pros
- Really funny
- Great Art Style
- You get to collect and capture multiple enemies to use on your team
- Story is amazing (What there is of it anyway)
- Multiplayer is really fun
- They have a auto battler installed if you aren't good at TBS games and just want to watch your team fight

Cons
- Story isn't complete
- Some of the menus are a bit buggy
- nothing else really the game is Really Really good so far.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Best game that is currently early access. Keep in mind that this is early access.

9.5/10

Pro:
+ Will Stamper a.k.a Quibs from Battleblock theater is in it as the narrator.
+ Funny Gameplay during the fights
+ Lots of Enemy/ Ally types
+ Great turn based system
+ "The Pit" is a fun way to test out team compositions, it is basically an arena to fight in.
+ Simple level system
+ Funny way to recruit new allies, you throw a net on the lone enemy and you take him back to town

Cons:
- Not much story, but that is because this is in ** early access. **
- only a few weapons that look desirable
- not that many healer types
- limited inventory space in the wagon which is how you travel around the world map, the space is about 10, fills up fast
- only customization options to characters are weapon, shield, and head. (We ned more gear customization, like shirts to get rid of that plain baige t-shirt)

Both:
+/- promotes grinding in "the pit" instead of the world due to small inventory space\t\t\t\t\t
