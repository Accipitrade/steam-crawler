\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I've had this game in my wishlist since 2016 i've been a huge fan of Newgrounds and The Behemoth for way to many years, I regret not getting this day one. The games got that classic NG humor, also Stamper as the narroator was a fantastic choice he's always funny, he could honestly say something normal and still make me laugh. The gameplay reminds me of Final Fantasy Tactics just a bit. The idea of recruiting new party members is fun. I will totally beat this game. Do yourself the favour and get this game even if you're not a fan of stratogy games it's still worth the play. I hope maybe in the future we can see a port of Alien Hominid HD on steam. but either way this company releases the best indie games i've ever had the joy of playing.\t\t\t\t\t
