\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPit People is honestly the one game I have played the most of in the last week or so - even though it's pretty obviously ment to be a more casual title.

I won't try to explain to you why I did this, instead, let me just tell you about the things you can expect within this title that may lead you to become the same irrational human being I am as of now:

- It's full of great, over the top humour. If names like Stamper and Psychicpebbles ring a bell, it's in the same vein as their stuff.

- The combat is extremely simple with very little decision making. You don't decide what abillity a character is going to do and you do not decide which enemy he is going to attack. All you decide is where he will move. The Game will highlight his possible targets at the position you're moving him. He'll attack which ever one he feels like upon arrival. If there is only one target, of course, he will attack that one.

- Another thing that adds to the combat is the variety of humanoids and monsters you can catch and add to your troops, with every race having different perks, passives and attacks. They all come with random names as well (which you will be able to see BEFORE catching them, so don't worry if you only want serious names.) Because, let me tell you, there are some real hitters in there. As of me wiriting this review, there are a total of 18 Races, not counting the different weapons some of them can use, which essentially makes them different units again.

- The Main-Cast of characters is charming as all hell. Sadly, the main-story is not, currently, very long - however that's going to be expected of an early-access title.

- Staying with the theme of early-acces: This is one of the few games that actually seems to use that term rightfully. It's very much playable and you can already sink a good amount of hours into it, however, it's still, noticeably, not done. Don't worry though, the devs said previously that you are going to keep all units, items and general progress during the developement of Pit People.

- Did I say Items? Yeah, for some people this is the biggest factor: You can find an insane amount of items in this game for every race that change their looks, sometimes drastically so. The same goes for weapons. Very rarely do these items have a bonus effect (the likes of: does fire damage, but less physical, etc.)

- Also, there is meme-gear. I'm sure our creators look upon us in pure hatred and disgust at this point. So why not enjoy the ride until the inevitable heat-death of the universe.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRecently bought this game after seeing a few Youtubers playing, I was not dissapointed. Although, it is currently in early access if you do the side stuff there is enough content to justify buying it and it's reduced for early access buyers. I usually don't play many turn based stratergy games, but this one is has quite a quick pace and good mechanics. I really appreciate the style, jokes and genral qwerkyness of the game. The game is quite easy to pick up and has good local multiplayer. My main issues are that when different chracters have buffs you can't find out what they are. For example in the equip menu buffs have icons, but they aren't very self explanatory. If when you put your highligted them you'd get a little box of text which told you what the buff did that would be good. Also, with the Lady Legless quest I didn't have Yosef on my team and entered the quest, this then wouldn't allow me to finish the battle as she wouldn't die. When I had Yosef on my team it worked due to story reasons, prehaps if the quest didn't start without Yosef this could be fixed. The addition of ranked online battles in the pit would also be appreciated, however I'm mostly nit picking now. I really like this game and strongly recomend it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo to cut it short, I love Behemoth's titles, & bought every one for Xbox. I had my qualms about them moving into strategy with game 4 (Pit People), despite the first 3 games all being different genres to each other & all being pretty good, especially the latter 2 (Castle Crashers & Battleblock Theatre).

I ended up buying it yesterday, even with the Early Access tag & only a couple story missions currently... And boy, I love it already, this game has the funniest voice acting I've ever heard in a game, Will Stamper who did the voiceover for Battleblock Theatre smashes it out of the park this time, so, so funny.

The game plays a bit like a blend between XCOM & Pokemon with a ♥♥♥♥ ton of loot. a strategy game where you can capture the last monster in each fight & recruit them to your mismatched group of heroes. Despite the game only having a couple story missions at the minute, it's rammed with content currently, I suggest you all check it out, even if you have no intention of buying, just Youtube the first 15 minutes. I can't wait to see what happens next but I'm off to recruit some new madness!

The big difference between this and other hex based strategy games are that you don't pick actions for your characters, you simply move them & they do the rest. This adds depth that you wouldn't think of beforehand, & brings a whole heap of strategies to the table that you wouldn't think of in other hex games.

The graphics and music are what you expect from a Behemoth title, sharp & colourful, with the music being a highlight, I think this may be the best Behemoth soundtrack yet & that's saying a lot. Behemoth aren't done with adding music yet & will be releasing an OST for the soundtrack. I think this may be the first game soundtrack I ever buy, the music is that good.

- I'm not funny or creative enough to write a review that would fully give the game the appreciation it's worth, but I couln't recommend this game enough for people with a sense of humour, or even slightly enjoy strategy, loot, Pokemon, wierdness, or if you like to smile while playing.

TL;DR - Youtube the first 15-20 minutes of Pit People, funniest game I've played in 25+ years of gaming.

I'd rate this game at 8/10 for the moment, as people who don't enjoy grinding or "catching them all", may get somewhat bored without the story being there. I'm sure this will be higher when the full release comes around.\t\t\t\t\t
