\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPit People is a wonderful adventure into the ludicrous and insane.
The game has excellent writing and humor that not many other games can compare to.
While the combat is simplistic in comparison to other games of the strategy genre, this is not necessarily a bad thing.
It lets you focus more on the fun wacky world, and is great for a beginner in the genre or those not good at it.
There is a lot of fun combinations that can be done with the various different unit types, and equipment types.
Sometimes your combinations work great together, other times you kill your own units or your friends if you are playing co-op.
On that note, it is a great game to play with a friend, with so many things to do to work together, or against each other, it can be really fun.
There are a lot of customization options to make your cool or edgy, or goofy fighter of your dreams.
Overall I highly recommend the game, its a fun and casual intro into the overbearingly intimidating world of strategy games.\t\t\t\t\t
