\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDivine Protection of Trisection
The Divine Protection of Trisection (三分の加護 Sanbun no Kago) is used by Mimi Pearlbaton, Hetaro Pearlbaton, and Tivey Pearlbaton. It enables the users to bear themselves or each other's physical and emotional burdens and can also be used to split damage between all users such as to block death blows. However, if they were to use it on themselves, they would have to bear a heavy burden on their body. As Mimi is the strongest out of the three of her brothers, they are often stuck with bearing her burdens. In addition, Tivey can launch a destructive interference wave using the Divine Protection and his siblings, though Mimi is limited to five times and Hetaro and Tivey are limited to two times respectively, so the maximum usage per day is four times.

Divine Protection of the Sun
The Divine Protection of the Sun (太陽の加護 Taiyō no Kago) is used by Priscilla Barielle. It increases the effects of buffs and the user becomes stronger during the day.

Divine Protection of Evaluation
The Divine Protection of Evaluation (目利きの加護 Mekiki no Kago) is used by Russell Fellow. It enables the user to determine if an item is of good or bad quality, and if the item is in front of them, they can evaluate its condition and quality with perfect accuracy. However, if the user is evaluating an item they've never seen before, they only know if it's of good or bad quality, and gain no knowledge of how it's used or how much it's worth.

Divine Protection of the Sword Saint
The Divine Protection of Sword Saint (剣聖の加護 Kensei no Kago) was formerly used by Fribal van Astrea and Theresia van Astrea. Now it is currently used by Reinhard van Astrea. Whoever holds this Divine Protection becomes the Sword Saint and inherits its powers which draws out maximum martial skill potential within them. The user is also able to see white lines which indicate the enemy's sword slashes, creating an optimal path for defence and offence as the vectors are drawn out by Sword God himself. Unlike the other Divine Protection, this one is passed down from user to user, and the inheritor doesn't need to be near the holder when it is transferred. Furthermore, the previous Sword Saint will instinctively know who their successor is.

Divine Protection of Empathy
The Divine Protection of Empathy (共感の加護 Kyōkan no Kago) allows the user to read the feelings of the person they touched.

Divine Protection of Insensitivity
The Divine Protection of Insensitivity(無神経の加護 Mushinkei no Kago) is used by Tiga Rauleon. It makes sure the user's body can't be interfered with by any outside influences, whether be magical enhancement or impairment.

Divine Protection of Mind-Changing
The Divine Protection of Mind-Changing (変心の加護 Henshin no Kago) allows the user to change the mind of the person they target with the Divine Protection. Due to its potency, this Divine Protection has been renown as being dangerous to the rule of nations throughout history. This protection was used by a four year old Reinhard van Astrea.

Divine Protection of Lake
The Divine Protection of Lake is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user becomes able to walk on water.

Divine Protection of Mist
The Divine Protection of Mist is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is able to keep their nature sight intact without mist hindering their view.

Divine Protection of War God
The Divine Protection of War God is used by Reinhard van Astrea. It enables the user to use any weapon masterfully.

Divine Protection of Cloud
The Divine Protection of Cloud is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is able to walk on clouds.

Divine Protection of Lightning
The Divine Protection of Lightning is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is unable to get hit by lightning.

Divine Protection of Magic Resistance
The Divine Protection of Magic Resistance is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is immune against curses, debuffs like yin magic. Though the user is able to get buffed by yang magic or any other magic.

Divine Protection of Fire Avoidance
The Divine Protection of Fire Avoidance is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is 80% immune against fire damage.

Divine Protection of Wind Absorption
The 'Divine Protection of 'Wind Absorption is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user absorbs 80% of wind magic.

Divine Protection of Earth Resistance
The Divine Protection of Earth Resistance is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user has an 80% reduced effect on earth magic.

Divine Protection of Water Reflection
The Divine Protection of Water Reflection is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user reflects 80% of water magic.

Divine Protection of Light Sharing
The Divine Protection of Light Sharing is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user shares 80% of yang magic with another target.

Divine Protection of Darkness Immunity
The Divine Protection of Darkness Immunity is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user nullifies 80% of yin magic.

Divine Protection of Swiftness
The Divine Protection of Swiftness is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user can move at superhuman speed. When riding an earth dragon or another mount, this blessing affects them too.

Divine Protection of Riding Mastery
The Divine Protection of Riding Mastery is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is able to ride any kind of mount. The mount doesn't have to like him, he is still able to ride it.

Divine Protection of Item Mastery
The Divine Protection of Item mastery is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is able to know how to use any item he holds.

Divine Protection of Unarmed mastery
The Divine Protection of Unarmed mastery is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is very strong without any weapons.

Divine Protection of Power Bleeding
The Divine Protection of Power Bleeding is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is not negatively affected by bleeding. Bleeding actually makes the user stronger.

Divine Protection of Illness Immunity
The Divine Protection of Illness Immunity is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is immune to all sickness and diseases. Though it is unknown if it protects the user against dragon veins.

Divine Protection of Poison Immunity
The Divine Protection of Poison Immunity is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is immune to all sorts of poison.

Divine Protection of Training Mastery
The Divine Protection of Training Mastery is used by Reinhard van Astrea. The user is able to train not only his body, but also other people, to their maximum potential.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall really good!

If you had fun playing Castle Crashers as a kid/teenager, you will enjoy this game :D

1) The game has its singature Behemoth style that Castle Crashers and Battleblock Theater have.
2) The humor is really good, even if you are over 20
3) The game has enough RPG elements to make it interesting but not so much to make you want to wiki the entire game (btw, the wiki is pretty bad)
4) If it makes enough sense to you, it will work, since using the wiki will get you nowhere, use team formations that you think can work and use them, have fun with the posibilities! But most importantly, be creative, you wont find much on the internet, so play the way YOU want!
5) The game can be played in 2 ways, the first one is just playing the main story (around 20 hours) or second one, which is to do all 100% (which includes the side missions, which are fun and funny to do)

Important note, I highly recommend to play with a friend!

Overall 8/10, will try to do a perma death run once I hit the 100% with my buddy.
