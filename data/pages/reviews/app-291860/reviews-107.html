\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor a Early Access Game, I completely forgot it was one since I found no bugs or never crashed, the only reason I remembered it was one was because the story isn't finished. It's a amazing turn base strategy game with your regular The Behemoth humor. The music is great when in battle and makes it more imersive imo. The only problem I had was that my main characters died from a mission on permadeath and I had to restart, but that's my fault for not believing what would happen if I failed. It's also interesting how you gain more units since you have to catch them when they're the last enemy alive. I personally can't wait till it's fully done and which will make it an even better game and the story since it is pretty funny. Also don't take permadeath mode too lightly since if you're main story characters die, you CANNOT continue the story.\t\t\t\t\t
