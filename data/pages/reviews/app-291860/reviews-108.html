If you liked Battleblock Theater, Castle Crashers, or even Alien Hominid you will feel rewarded for having enjoyed those games also.

Pit People is one of those games that looks like it was made with a $50.00 budget but still comes out better and more polished than AAA games. The sillyness in the dialogue or even just roaming around laughing at enemies costumes makes this game both addicting and entertaining.

CO-OP is smooth. Playing with your friends and making jokes seems to be the spirit of this game.
that being said, single player is still very engaging for those worried that the campaign would be too dry without co-op.

If you are new to Behemoth games, now is just as good of a time as any to jump right in to Pit People.
