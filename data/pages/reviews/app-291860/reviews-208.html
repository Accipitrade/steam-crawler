\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEven in this game's current Early Access status, I can easily recommend this to my friends, and to anyone who's played either Battleblock Theater or Castle Crashers. This game has all the charm and uniqueness as these past games, taking aspects of them both (i.e. The Narrator from Battleblock Theater, the stupid but well implimented poop jokes from Castle Crashers) and smashing them all together into a 4X turn based strategy RPG game that re-defines the genre entirely, continuing the saga Behemoth has left behind in their unique twists on the genres of games.

I don't know how to make a long enough or good enough review to give this game the justice it deserves. It's fantastic, and it could easily be released right now but the fact that the devs want people to bug test it, while also adding new things to the game while it's in early access is amazing.

Behemoth and Blizzard are definitely my favorite developers (Blizzard because they don't do DLC's for Overwatch, and Behemoth for their creative games and wonderful humor.)

Thanks a lot guys, and keep up the good work!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is great. I took a look at the negative reviews and the few there are complain that there is little story content. I just wanted to write a review to make it clear that that really doesn't matter in my opinion. Story is coming, this is an early access game. I don't think it should recieve any bad reviews for lack of main story content. The world is pretty large and you can go around and capture endless amounts of mobs. But for me where this game is likely going to shine is in the multiplayer. I could easily see myself dropping over 100 hours into the pvp system alone due to the amount of variety there can be. You have to go capture the monsters/get the weapons and that itself might be tedious as there aren't too many quests, but when you have stuff it looks like it's gonna be endless fun. Definitely up there for most amount of polish for an early access game.\t\t\t\t\t
