Pit People, like all the Behemoth Games on steam are essentially masterpieces in every way. None of them are pay 2 win and the rarest items you can obtain could be just by playing the games itself without forking up extra cash.

What makes Pit People special is the comedic writing and the large selection of bonus missions (which isn't needed to complete all achievements btw). So essentially like me, you could have gotten all the achievements but only complete 50% of the save file.

Gameplay wise it runs smoothly on my intel hd graphics works comp so there is no issue even with computers of low specs.

Multiplayer is very fun when you manage to find another player (personally inviting them to play), the multiplayer search button definetly works but the relatively low player base meant that it is very difficult to find other players.

As a game i would give it a 100% must buy rating

Join the Behemoth discord server to find other pit people players quickly
