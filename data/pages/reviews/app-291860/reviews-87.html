\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're a turn based strategy/tactics fan with a friend who hasn't played such games, you probably want a couple copies of this game.

I'm into XCOM, Civ, and all of that nerdy goodness. I've been playing Pit People with a friend who is not, and we're enjoying Pit People enough that I'm pretty sure Pit People will act as her gateway into deeper TBS games.

The combat is tactical and challenging enough to be interesting to someone like me, even if it's a little on the light side, but the HP values are so high that you generally have time to respond to units in untenable positions and get them back to healers without them dying so long as you stay on top of things. This is all clear enough as to be obvious to someone with virtually no experience in this style of combat. Unit variety and different team synergies are great, with plenty for more experienced players to sink their teeth into.

The tutorialing is absolutely awful. The game seems unfinished in this regard. There's so many aspects of the game that're never explained, or explained poorly, leaving you to gradually realise how things work. This problem is compounded by poor wiki support. Normally when faced with this kind of confusion I'd hit the wiki, but in this case I was dissappointed. This didn't prevent us from enjoying the game, but expect to be confused for a long time.

The humour is very lol random, penguin of doom absurdism. It'll be a matter of taste. We loved it. When Yosef did that thing he did, we properly cracked up laughing. Some people will find this intolerable, and I doubt those people could enjoy the game. Look at the screenshots. If people firing hotdog bows and wearing celery helmets, penguins dressed as purple lions, doge hats and fish swords seems like it might be appealing, give it a go, but if this stuff turns you off then you're probably not going to be able to power through it, because this style of humour is a constant feature in the game. The game has tons of character.

For us, this was exactly the sort of chill co-op game we were looking for.\t\t\t\t\t
