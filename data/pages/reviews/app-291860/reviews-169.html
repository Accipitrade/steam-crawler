Took Me Around 2ish Hours to Complete the Main Story Quests (EARLY ACCESS)
RNG (not terrible xD)


Overall Pit People definitely isn't a finished product hence the EARLY ACCESS, but there are still hours of fun to be had and plenty of laughs to well....laugh. If you are wanting a great game and are willing to wait a little while for more content then I would suggest picking it up. If you are into solo gamplay and want a story driven experience, I would probably wait until a few more patches or the full release some time this year.\t\t\t\t\t
