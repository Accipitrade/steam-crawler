\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI rarly if ever write reviews, but I blieve this games warrants one, as it is really fun, but has some major flaws.
I love this game, but the UI and some aspects are horrible.
The UI and how things work.
\tVarious examples:
\t\t- Equipping a character: It is tedious and I pretty much stopped equipping anyone, as most new equipment is cosmetical anyways.
\t\t- You fullfil an eperors quest. You enter the city, and a popup awaits you with the emperor holding a chest, and a flashing icon of a mouse with the right mouse button flashing. You right-click. The popup dissapears. No explanation, if you just refused the reward, or actually received it. I have no idea how this is still part of the game, after a prolonged alpha/beta/final release.
\t\t- No explanation of what exactly usable items, such as "healing camp" do. It heals, yes, but who and how much?
\t\t- Attributes are never actually explained. What does armor actually do, what does a high mark in dodge achieve (10%, 20%, no idea)?
\tLeveling up:
\t\t- I am about 20 hours in and have no idea what this actually does, other than heal the characte during a fight
Other things:
\t- There is a market that sells stuff. No information on anything is given other than the looks of itmes, for no reason I can see.
\t
Why I still like this game:
\t- This game is FUN! No matter how badly they screwed up everthing else, the actual combat, where you spend most of your time is actually really fun! Although the above mentioned issues are present, the game is still fun to play, and I will try to finish it anyways, which should speak to how good it actually is, despite its flaws.
\t
Notes: How to adress the issues in my opinion:
\tNot at all. This seems like a finished product, with all its flaws. But in the future, maybe hire someone with UI experience, that can combine the feel the creative team wants to maintain, and the Information, someone who has not worked on the title for years may desperatley need. The Behemoth is no longer a fly on the wall, but one of the most well known independent studios, and can certainly spare the change.\t\t\t\t\t
