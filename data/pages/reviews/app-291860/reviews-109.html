\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy buddy and I were looking for a co-op game to purchase during the steam sale and I sent him a link to this game to check out.

I saw the similiarties that this game had art style wise to Battle Block Theater and Castle Crashers and a matter of fact, I loved those games! However, I didn't know how Behemoth would handle the concept of turn based combat within this game because most TBS games are either a hit or miss for me. Also, I was slightly concerned that the game was going to be childish and marketed towards younger children.

But when I played this game for the first time, I was won over. A combination of the art styles, gameplay, witty humor, and combat style drew me in. Behemoth implemented story rich, funny cut scenes into Pit People that are always worth watching (seriously, watch them, they're worth your time). If you have someone to play co-op with, you will develop an instant addiction Pit People.

I purchased this game during the Steam Sale for $9.94, and it was worth every single penny. Even if you are reading this after the steam sale, the MSRP for Pit People is $15 and that price is also an absolute steal.

Pick it up, you won't be dissapointed 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
