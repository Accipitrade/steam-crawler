With 3 story missions, 5+ multi part side stories, over 60 world map quests and over 1,500 cosmetics collect this game has a ton of content. There are almost 20 unique recruits to choose to assemble your team including giant farting mushrooms, lazy rocket chair driving gnomes, and a floating octopus with one tentacle. You can play 2 player co-op or you can fight in the pit for a 1v1 or a 2v2. Keep in mind that this is early access, and there is more to come.

Overall, Pit People is an amazing game. It might be a little simple of a strategy game for some people, but the Behemoth never disappoints. After all, their other hit games were not too complex, so why does a strategy game have to be?\t\t\t\t\t
