\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a decent game but it has a major flaw: You must have a minimum of an hour and a half to complete just one mission and you cannot save the game after starting a mission. You must return to town to save your game progress and if you do before completing the mission, you are penalized by having your town location randomized on the map.

There REALLY should be a better way to save the game, as it makes playing the game impossible for me. I usually only have about two hours each night where I can play and at any moment it can be interrupted by a 30 minute break to deal with a crying baby. On several occasions we had to call it quits very close to the end of a mission which meant completely abandoning our progress AND occurring a penalty for the trouble. On top of that annoyance, a mission can randomly be longer or shorter than average due to story elements. So you might think you have enough time to run a 90 minute mission only to find this mission takes 120-150 minutes!

Otherwise, I find the game cute and fun. There are interesting elements to the combat and the dialogue is constantly silly. You never quite know what to expect from the game except silliness. One other minor annoyance is the fact that there seems to be only one song. I don't know if I just haven't unlocked more tracks but I'm stuck listening to the same electro swing hit every time I play. I love electro swing but that song may be forever burned into my brain.

Ultimately I would recommend against the game due to the poor save setup. However, if you've got more free time and less obligations than I do, check the game out.\t\t\t\t\t
