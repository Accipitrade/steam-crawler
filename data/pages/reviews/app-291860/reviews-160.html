\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPit People is a one of those 'Easy to play, hard to master' types. It's fast to finish your turn just a few clicks, that's right, there's no special type of attacks your characters can use. It's more about type type of charcters and types of equipment that just leveling up.

This game also features pvp, very fun and easy to start playing, there was not once i felt like i was under leveled or overleveled in pvp, but like i was outplayed or someone with more knowleage than me. You get rewarded with gold and points by completing these pvp (you can also play with the AI but you have to go though extra rounds) The points are your rank, there are three stages. Bronze, Siver and Gold. The higher the better the rewards are.

Not going to give out spoliers but i'll say this game has pretty good comedy with the story, it's not fully finished but it's getting there!

The charcters you get are fun and different to use, like humans, they can use different types of swords, clubs, bows and some throwable stuff. The Cyclops who can gaint weapons, clubs and send people flying! There is even cupcakes, wraths and other scary and funny allys (or foes)

I'll give this game a 9.0 A bit hard to get into, but theres is so many laughs and adventures to be had in this game! Have Fun!\t\t\t\t\t
