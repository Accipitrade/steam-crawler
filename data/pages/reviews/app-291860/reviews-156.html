\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is one of the best turn based games I've played in a very long time. Behemoth has brought along their typical humor (Castle Crashers and BattleBlock Theater style) and has really improved on their storytelling (albiet extremely short story currently in game). I'm not typically a huge fan of turn based games, but this feels a lot like a board game when playing with very large risk/rewards with every move you make that make the game exciting to play for hours on end. All hours that I have played currently have been co-op and everything seems fairly balanced and challenging as far as story missions go. Have not attempted PVP yet. Overall I would highly recommend this game. It's very addicting right from the start, and hard to put down each session. Devs seem to be updating this which is nice to see, however it is still very early in it's life. I look forward to continuing to play this game and any future updates Behemoth adds to it. Well worth the $15 asking price, and a must buy if it ever ends up on sale.\t\t\t\t\t
