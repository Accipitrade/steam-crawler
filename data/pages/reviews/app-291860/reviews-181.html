\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't write many reviews, because I really don't have the time or desire to be a part-time games journalist (for free). But, this is one of the most objectively FUN games I've played in a long time. Combat is a blast, the narrator is great and interjects at just the right time to add flavor to a kill ("That's fair.....I guess."), and there are so many combinations to make battlegroups out of. While the loot varies mostly as cosmetic alterations of core types, you still get a feeling that you're constantly winning something. The locations and character names are hilarious, and I've already gotten a warning from my wife that I have to make sure my son isn't around when I'm playing (apparently she doesn't want him saying "Rumptrumpet" at school).

I'm not great at adding funny things to reviews, but I'd give this game a solid 10/10, would recommend friends buy it again.\t\t\t\t\t
