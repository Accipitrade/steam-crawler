\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, keep in mind this is an early acces review!

I waited for this game oh so long! When I first saw the trailers, I knew it would fit my sense of humour. When it hit early access recently I was not disappointed.
You want to whack your enemies with an oversized lollipop or a nuke, misused as a club? Check!
You want to have the weirdest outfits for your heroes that you can imagine? Check! (And I am not exagerating.)
You want absolutely hillarious characters and missions? Check!

The only disadvantage from owning this gem in early access: The story line is not finished. After one Story mission the progress (for the story) stops. However, you have a ♥♥♥♥ load of side bonus quests and some side missions to do. And of course you have the map to explore and try to "hire" all the different types of enemies (meaning: You put them in a cage and drag them back home).
Combat system is easy enough, but leaves enough unpredictability (especially since a lot of your weapons will damage whoever is unlucky enough to be in their radius, no matter if friend or foe) to make it a real trial in every fight.
I love it and cannot wait for the game to be finished.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust to be clear, I absolutely love the game. It is the most fun I've had with any game in the past year, and I am definitely looking forward to the full release.

That being said, the one issue I do have is the zoomed-in nature of the combat screen. I find it to be incredibly bothersome, and it makes moving throughout the map a real nuisance, especially when I have characters spread across the combat map. If there is a feature that allows for that focus to be removed and I'm just being stupid I apologize, but if there isn't I would highly recommened adding that into the full-featured game. The zoom makes sense for console (which I'm sure is what this game is intended for), but on a non-console device it just feels like I have tunnel vision. I'm sure a lot of PC people would appreciate making this change.

Other than that, thanks Behemoth, this is the game I've been looking for.\t\t\t\t\t
