The TL;DR Version:
I'm only a couple of hours into this game and I've already discovered a Unicorn that doubles as artillery and a pair of gnomes in flying toilets as well as beaten numerous people to a pulp with a giant lollipop. Nuff said.

The Actual Review
The humor is crude in the most wonderful way and it suits the art style perfectly. Speaking of art style, it's the most adorable looking game I've seen in awhile, it's really dorky in all the right ways and it contrasts beautifully with the over the top violence that is the game. The overworld aspect is a weird mixture of the really old school Final Fantasy and Legend of Zelda games with Pokemon, a lot of people have compared this to pokemon on account of how you recruit new members for your roster.

The main villain is one of the best I've seen so far, and right on Par with Dusan from Watchdogs 2 (perhaps a controversial opinion but I really liked him as a villain) and his near constant interference in your affairs will keep you on your toes without being a pain. Not to mention his voice overs. I want to give special mention to the actor who voiced him because he is just wonderful.

The music is the other feature of this game that's absolutely stunning, it's an interesting line up of electroblends, from swing to funk and a few others in between, and if you get sick of the music in the city (which is pretty hard to do) you can always find records and unlock new songs to change it too. One of the best features is that the song will remain changed until you change it again, even if you leave the city and come back.

Pit People lures you in with it's charming villain, humorous story and adorable art style but make no mistake, it will not be an easy experience. The game is forgiving enough, as death only has consequences up until you return to the town, but the battles can quickly overwhelm you if you're not paying attention and often times you'll find yourself losing fighters without immediately realising why. This game is incredibly fun though and I don't think I could recommend it enough.\t\t\t\t\t

This "game" horribly lacks one thing, and one thing only: GAMEPLAY. If you pick a character "A" and a character "B" of the same race, the only difference it will have is their level (and i actually doubt that it even has any significance). You can wear same gear as your other character, so what's the point? And most of the gear has no difference exept the looks. You wanna crush your enemies with golden scepter? Sure. You wanna do it with a lolipop instead? Fine, it matters not. You can even pick the giant ice-cream cone and it will be still as good for crushing your enemies, as the sax or some actual mace (this is somewhat untrue, since really rarely you get the gear with additional effects, like fire resistance, poinsoned attacks and stuff. But still). My biggest porblem as of now is that on the Xth battle you get bored. You probably have same characters as you had X-1 battles before, so you just do the very same thing you did for X-1 battles before, and kill your enemies the very same way you killed them for X-1 battles before. WHY? I had 30 level ups already, can't i be somewhat stronger? Can't i have some new skills to test? The best way to describe this game would be "like heroes of might and magic, but without heroes, and all you have is your army - which is forever limited to N units per squad". But if, one day, having 2 cupcakes will mean more than x2 healing. If, one day, having hummie "A" will mean something different from having hummie "B". If, one day, Horatio will actually DIE. That day will be the day when this game becomes much better than it is now.

TBH, i cant say that this game is bad, or good. It is neutral. If you want some funnies, you will find this game better than neutral; If you want to play a game, you will find this game worse than neutral. But i will press the "not recommended" button, just for the sake of keeping balance with all those "recommended" reviews this game already has. Besides, this is Early Access. The game still has more room for improvement. So lets hope for the best, and watch events unfold.\t\t\t\t\t
