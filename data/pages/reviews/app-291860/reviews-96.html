\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWas eyeing it while on early access, decided to wait until release, bought for full price, was wondering if it's worth it. Boy, was that a rhetorical question, hell yeah it is!
So far just tried it for a couple hours, but can already see some parts of ingenuity it.

Alright what's there for a Behemoth fan(and just somebody looking for a blast):
  • Thrilling characters story with unexpected twists and turns - check
  • Unique narration that takes a crack at your fails every time - check
  • Connection with previous games, and awarding loot for owning them - check
  • Intuitive design made in same picteresque fashion as previous titles - check

What's there you haven't seen yet(from Behemoth):
Gameplay.
So take a team of characters such as gyant cyclops named Hashtag, couple rednecks with chunks of wood for a head protection, pick your favourite gear, and some quest. Make it out of the city to breath some fresh air and wonder on a hexagon map starting battles. Throw axes/nets/hotdogs, hit with clubs/swords/scepters, dodge, block and manuevre on all those hexagons while there's living wonderful hell on the battlefield. However, if you were to fall in battle, your city GETS GRABBED AND DROPPED on a random map tile, with you wondering why on Earth that had to happen.

And a short story: misunderstood one of the quest requirements, got swarmed at the end, last character got pushed to the corner by mushrooms that farted over him until he died.

Horrible death but 10/10 would get swarmed by farting mushrooms again.\t\t\t\t\t
