\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI like it
Great titles and fresh new genre just like you see from these guys.

(More fleshed out review past the cons0)

Pros:
Has that charm the other titles have, an amazing voice actor, fun openining.
Fast flowing turn progression in combat situations.
pretty balanced characters

Cons:
Havent played online co op or versuses.
Local Play co op camera is trash.
Combat is too long. (Made me avoid enemies and didnt want to engage them)
Equipment system (at least early game) needs work, starting weapons barely mean ♥♥♥♥, and there isnt really a ledgend to tell you what all the buffs mean, which is so ♥♥♥♥♥♥ needed.







Ive been playing these games from alien hominide up to battle blocks and enjoy how they always change genre and manage to keep the humor fresh, refined and unexpected. I welcomed this new titles and loved the opening which these guys have nailed but this game has a few issues that turned me off at the start of the game.

- The UI is very stylish but all over the place, transistioning is very slight and hard to see and the controls for the ui and very light meaning you can go flying all over the place. The Main Menu Could of been Simpled down without losing its charm. And the City Hub Menu is like castle crashers world hub, maybe pan the camera back or something idk it didnt look right to me.

- The combat is amazing, its free flowing and non turne based, the characters dont have alot of special abilites but you can recruit people who have one special ability. its amazing, and the fact that each character can only do one thing or two, the proximity targeting is a great balance for strategical planning. But the fights become way to long winded, as i said before i was turned off after the prolouge mainly cause of the combat, The co op pust a boat load of enemies on the field which at first looks exciting(i will get to that in a minute), but evtually become a turn off to me cause i know i will be here for more than ten minutes, both enemies and your team barely do much damage, i had flankers go for archers but it took 12 turns to get one and the flanker died, The slow crawling pace of the fights are leagues different from the fast paced action based game previously made, and again i havent played to much but if i was to suggest maybe quicker fights but add a few more enemies to balance it out, heck bring in reinforcements, The previous titles liked to try and overwhelm you before but the challenges are always fair.

- Lastly , ive played the co op and like all the other titles you share a screen, but that becomes a major flaw, the camera doesnt pan out for both cursors, sometimes forcing one to move, it doesnt try to center both and making descions without having to wait on your partner to finish making his choices is a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ bore, it either needs splitscreen or a better camera that pans out. i did not like waiting when i wanted to get right back in the fight, the enemy turns are instant but i had to wait for my partner so i could make my choices or vise versa. we ended up just meeting at the middle and forcing the enemy to come to use. the camera forces team play which isnt bad but im saying it again but its different the other titles, before co operation was not needed, hell you could obstruct and ruin your partner for the giggles but mechanics were also given so that you could do the opposite. Sometimes you are forced to co operate and sometimes its more fun to lone wolf. But this title, the camera is so bad for co op play that it some how forces you to work to gether so the camera wouldnt bother you.

Again i cant speak for the whole, game just my few hours of playing it. Im somewhat mixed on the early access, it lets me know the game is being worked on but i fear it may die out. I hope it improves and comes back well polished.\t\t\t\t\t
