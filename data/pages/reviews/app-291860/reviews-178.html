\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBehemoth never disappoints. A new refreshing game with great art and fun gameplay, Pit People is just like Behemoth's other classic games with the humor, voice acting, and ridiculous weapons and cosemetics to match. :) I usually don't get into strategy and turn based games, but Pit People makes those who aren't interested in that genre love it. Such a weird combination of genres comes together in Pit People, and it's great. There is so much to do in Pit People, and I can't wait to see what Behemoth has in store for the full release of this game. Since it is still in development, some things are not complete(like the story), but there is much to explore. They are very attentive to the community as many major improvements from Beta recommended from the community and I love that. Tutorial is much better now and so are the animations since beta. Current early access price of $15 is a good deal, considering how well developed and different this game is from other mainstream games. (After early access the price will go up to $20).

Some cons to the game are the controls for mouse+keyboard. The game was designed for Xbox, and is best played on a controller. It's still ok though, but if you don't have a controller, you may find it annoying. There is also no world map, and the screen makes it hard to see sometimes while traveling. It is different and a cool idea, but can be frustrating.

P.S there is a LOT of grinding(it is part RPG). If that's not your thing, then don't get this game.

Thank you for another wonderful game, Behemoth <3\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA game made by the people who made Castle Crashers and Battleblock theater, comes Pit People

Pit people is a hexgonal turn-based strategy game, the twist here is that your units attack units on their own free-will, meaning that positioning is extremely important.

The art-style is simple but incredibly effective, being extremely colorful, vibrant, and having everything and everyone popout, every item is different in terms of art design and every character is memorable.

The gameplay is deceptively simple, but heavily rooted in complicated strategies, team compositions and even gear distribution, luckily, the team at Behemoth were able to make this game pretty easy to learn and play, but difficult to master.

My main problem with the game are relatively simple ones, first, the inability to scroll out the map can be problematic sometimes, especially against a full team against a full team, another problem is that the gear that you obtain rarely differ against each other, I would like to have like very small differences, but differences none the less (I want my throwing straws to deal more damage than Yusef's default axes xD)

Thirdly, some of the non-human party members are limited in their apperal, I would like that to be changed, it would be awesome to have a whole group of kobolds wearing hats and wielding chicken drums hehe.

Besides that, I would reccomend this game for many people, Behemoth fans, hexagon-turn based Strategists, normal people to give this a try, despite it being an early access game, its well polished and no doubt will be absolutely amazing when it finally releases.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay so, let me say I don't hate this game. I've actually been looking forward to it since it was annouced, as I am a huge fan of everything Behemoth does along with one of my favorite actors/voice actors/people Stamper. However, this game really just isn't fun to me. Like, the combat itself is extremely weak and unsatisfying as nothing really feels like you're actually doing anything. You can't plan for any battle, you can't choose who you attack, weapons are often just reskins and stats don't really matter too much. I know no one really cares about my opinion, so I'll just be brief. I find it hilarious, like the portion that is done was really funny as Stamper always is. The humor is here as they are known to do, but gameplay wise it's the weakest game yet. I'm really hoping somehow they fix it since nothing really feels fun besides hearing Stamper make silly voices.\t\t\t\t\t
