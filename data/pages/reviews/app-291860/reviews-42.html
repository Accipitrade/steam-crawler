\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a nice game, but doesn't allow much co-op as previous Behemoth games. What I enjoyed about the last ones were how well they fit into a co-op scenario. Castle Crashers becomes a much more fun game with buddies, and includes a PvP mode where you fight each other after saving a princess, it adds flavor to it and lets you get revenge on that time a buddy stole all the sacks of gold before you got any.
Battleblock Theater had a 2 player co-op mode that changed the levels enough so that they can only be possible with a 2nd person, and came with a multiplayer mode of multiple different minigames. While having less people than Castle Crashers, it was clear they spent lots of thought and time into making the puzzles similar to their single player mode, while still it's own course that needs each other to work together.
This game, while it does have 2 player co-op, doesn't work as well. Both players make their own team and the enemy team has double the enemies. It feels less like a co-op game and more just playing while someone else is nearby. There is less teamwork required because each player already has their own team and is dealing with their half of the enemies.

So if you're someone who's into turn-based RPGs like this, it's a fine game with some fun jokes. If you're here for co-op like the other Behemoth games, give this one a pass.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a tactics RPG but it does not offer many customisation options. The monsters are limited, the weapons are limited and they mostly feel the same. The team at the start of the game is about the same strength as the endgame one and even 2 different compositions feel to be about the same strength as each other. THis means that no equipment choice really matters. None of the equipment really stand out from each other. They do about the same damage for weapons and same defenses for armour with no uniqueness. I was winning on every quest in the story + optional quests with the same set up. and there was absolutely no need to farm, or change my team compositions or do any important sidequests. This has to be the biggest let down if you want a good tactic RPG.

Verdict: would not recommend this game to anyone even tactic RPG fans\t\t\t\t\t
