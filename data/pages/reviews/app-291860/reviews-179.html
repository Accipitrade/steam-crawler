\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAfter loving the other games of Behemoth and my girlfriend showing me this game, I immediately bought it.
Since I love turn-based strategy games & play them a lot, I feel that I can give a proper review for what this game offered so far.

The game itself is punny as Behemoth tends to do, which I hope they never quit doing. The offered missions & stories are good enough at the moment to already play the game for several hours. Although it felt to me like a demo that they could've given out for free to promote the game when it's finished.
The gameplay is fun with the RNG implemented, as you only control their movement, not their actions. Which gives it a fun luck-factor where you could have a fun rage-moment when they hit the wrong target for the 5th time.

The local co-op is really clunky atm unfortunately, the camera moves too much since it tries to focus on 2 moving cursors at the same time, which sortof forces you to do your movements 1 person at a time. Basically you could play for 2 people on your own, if you have 2 seperate controllers that is.

I wasn't able to test the online co-op yet & will propably update my review after I tested that.

Something negative I noticed is that currently, being in Early Access, it feels like the characters their stats don't go up after a while anymore, having a lvl 30 character feels the same as having a level 10. The damage seems to lock at a certain point, even if the game assured that the stats would keep going up.
This does give an advantage on enjoying the 'demo' to its full extend, since else the level 5 missions would become too easy, thus ruining the gameplay.
