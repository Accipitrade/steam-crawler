They even implemented a work-around for this, if a plane gets stuck, the planes BEHIND it get a new button to push, it says "Traffic Jam?" and allows the plane to go through another one. Oh, and when planes get stuck, it hurts your score. When things work, I enjoy this game... but doing the 100 planes challenge without a bug ruining your game is 50/50. Also ,every now and then you'll tell a plane to go around and not land... but it just lands anyway. Bugs. Ug.\t\t\t\t\t

I'm not entirely sure how the collision detection works still, as wings and noses will cross yet no crash happens.
Not much guidance, though. It would be nice if there were a small tutorial, or an indication of how difficult each airport is. It's not too hard to figure out how to play, at least.
4/5 overall. If there were an indication of difficulty or a bit of guidance the first time you start up the game rather than just having written instructions, that would pretty much make this game perfect.\t\t\t\t\t
