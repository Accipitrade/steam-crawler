\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBugs galore, to the point that I'm surprised this is even out at all, regardless of it's EA status.

Everything feels incredibly clunky, the controls are terrible and to interact with anything at all is a massive pain because you have to be in extremely specific spots relative to what you're trying to interact with.

Movement is probably the worst of it all. The camera speed is really slow even maxed out so turning the camera is a nightmare, everything feels sluggish at best.

My biggest issue as someone who really loves farming RPGs: zero character customization so far. Your option is male or female and that's it, no other way to customize looks. Maybe this is something they'll add in later but for now, it's very disappointing for a farming RPG.

No tutorial whatsoever, no guidance, no info. You just go in and pray you can figure things out.

I can't recommend this game at all, at least in it's current state. It has potential but is not worth it right now.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really love farming RPGs such as STARDEW VALLEY (hundreds of hours over multiple platforms), HARVEST MOON, and the like. This game has so much potential, and it is super fun. However, there are quite a few things to iron out. Here's my feedback.

Pros:
- You start off with the standard 15 seeds to get started, but they're different types! YAY!
- It's great that you can get your cat/dog right away, and it costs so little (four coins for a cat!).
- The recycling element is awesome. You can spruce up your town and get a few coins along the way.
- I love the variety of crops, which makes things more interesting.
- There's a wide variety of NPCs, also a police station and other important things other games miss.
- The art style is a cool kind of almost vector art feel, which I enjoy.
- You get an electric scooter, and while it takes a bit of getting used to, you have access right away!
- It's an indie developer, and I like supporting indies.
- We get lots of wonderful space without having to clear a bunch of rubbish, which can be boring on the first day or so. Initial plots near the pond aren't blocked off by annoying obstacles.
- You have three steps to plant a crop, which seems more realistic.
- Plots of land stay tilled as far as I can tell, even if you don't plant anything on them, which is great. Not sure how that works per season, as I haven't gotten that far.
- The weather changes, which is more realistic.
- There are lots of resources spread across the map, many of which you can access right away.

Cons (constructive feedback to help the devs):
- It's odd that you have to stand right in the middle of a square for the tool to work on the field. So if I water/harvest slightly off of center, it doesn't work.
- The sell price listed in the general store doesn't match the sell price offered by the shipping bin. Please make sure they do so that people can make informed decisions on the profitability of relative crops.
- I'll echo what others have said: I feel like things are a bit too spread out for the time allotted. It's great that you have a rich/diverse map with different terrain and characters. Brilliant! But I feel like I never have enough time to travel from one place to another.
- We need a weather report. This is really important because I'll spend half the day watering my crops, only for it to rain. If I'd have known, I could have spent the day doing something more productive, like chopping wood or breaking rocks. Either have the weather be consistent all day, or have a way to tell the weather.
- If you have an issue with the scooter, and it gets stuck in the water, you can't get it out. Perhaps either prevent us from accidentally doing that? Or allow us to retrieve it? Because otherwise we get a penalty.

Other suggestions:
- I would love to be able to pet my cat. While it's super cute that he shows hearts when he's around me, I feel bad that I can't return the favor and pet him too!
- We get lovely strawberry seeds at the beginning, but I have no idea where I can buy more. Could you make that clearer, please? Is it at the market that happens twice a season?
- Could you consider allowing the watering can to water a few more crops? Ten is all right, but we start off with fifteen seeds anyway? Could you perhaps make it 15 or 20 tiles? I like refilling my can, but perhaps not so often...\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo the game isn't the smoothest right now, and yes, like other people have said it is a little buggy, but it is within 24 hours of early access release so that's to be expected. I've only played a little so far but will likely spend a good chunk of time in this game over the next few days. I really like it and think this game has all the foundations of a good farming sim. I love the concept and am really looking forward to where the dev team takes this.

Some things I really like:
+ The recycling mechanic (I don't know why but I really do)
+ Horses (I love horses and not enough games implement them in a meaningful way)
+The crops - some of what I'm already planting I haven't really seen in other games before (broccoli for example)
+ Art style (simple but cute)
+ Levelling system in general, I like being able to unlock/ improve things as I progress
+The large map, this will appear further down too but I love having a lot to explore and discover

Some things to note:
- The world is large and at the moment does feel a little empty
- Because of the above, the days feel too short, it takes too long to get anywhere
- The NPCs are not the most interesting, however for me this isn't an issue, I don't always love the social aspect of these types of games
- Some of the mechanics are not super intuitive (the drag and drop to move items and particularly the fact it seems you can only fill your watering can from one particular pond)
- It does feel like it needs optimisation, it felt a little slow to respond/ laggy for me (going to play with some settings to see if I can fix this)
- Some of the early game recipes seem quite resource expensive but I don't know how this balances out later

Overall, I definitely recommend this. I think as is, with a little optimisation it would be a great game so the fact they are adding more to it for me gives it the potential to really be excellent.\t\t\t\t\t
