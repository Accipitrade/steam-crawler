-weather forecast, when theres such little time in the game theres nothing more annoying then wasting your time watering all your crops for it just to rain!
-engaging options with pets (cats, dogs, other animals) where you can pet or give them treats/toys
- a way to unstuck the scooter that constantly gets stuck and then you get a penalty for
-fixing glitches with missions such as this: sometimes I cannot drop the item a specific character needs due to it showing previous character mission item
- easier way to pin point some items for missions, such as waypoints or a radius would be great. I have yet to find Marcuss building plans with the little time given and vast map
-more in game help for beginning to figure out the best ways to earn money to proceed in the game, which again is effected by the little time given in the day

Those are a few of my main wishes to be fixed or added, like I said before this game has great potential but as of right now it seems more stressful to me due to the short short days.
