\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI see someone gave this game a bad review after one hour of playtime. That does not really make any sense to me because the first couple hours of gameplay are pretty simple (if you're familiar with platformers). This game is really fun and the controls feel good. This is a game of trial and error. After dying a bunch of times you realize what you're doing wrong and you learn from your mistakes. I hate to give this game a bad review for one level but that's what i'm going to do. The 18th level of the 3rd world (Arctic) is unbeatable. This level is called "The Storm". Unlike every other level this level has a timed storm that freezes you and your friend before you are able to progress at all and therefore you will be unable to get to the 4th and 5th world which means you will end up missing half of the game. The last world is space and i was excited for it because it looks cool but there is no one to progress past level 18 of world 3 because the Devs decided to ruin the game with one level. If you want to buy this game for one dollar when its on sale like I did and you're ok with only playing half of a game, I would say go for it. The game is really fun. Co-op works really well. I played all the way to the broken level with my friend.\t\t\t\t\t
