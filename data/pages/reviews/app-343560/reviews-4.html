\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt looks crappy, but don't be fooled by the graphics, this is a real gem of a neat platformer!
The levels get really tight and difficult. It's very well put together as the jump distances and move timings are on spot. It's very fit for speedrunning. I now started speedrunning single levels. The levels are short are can be done in under 1 minute each. If you can do it to the end, every level also has a hard version with additional more diffcult obstacles. It really reminds me of Super Meat Boy with it's movement and excellence. If you like hard platformers you shouldn't miss this one!\t\t\t\t\t
