\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was a game I had seen a Youtuber play about 6 years ago, and I decided that I finally wanted to give this a try. It's essentially a procrastination game with voyeurism. These sort of games usually stress me out because I have to worry about not being caught (pairing that with some issues with my childhood where I never wanted to be caught doing anything except what I was told), but this eventually became a game that I enjoyed playing and got a chuckle out of.

I like the ability to play different games and get different software and videos to play (especially the special videos you can get as you play the game). The game is neither easy nor terribly difficult, though it can prove to be so as you gather achievements. The one thing I don't like is how little the tasks actually change. There is an achievement where you have to complete all assignments given to you, and many of them repeat. I was forced to write down all of the ones I had completed, and even then it got really lengthy just trying to find all of the tasks.

This game can be completed fairly quickly otherwise, and I certainly recommend giving it a try for some laughs.\t\t\t\t\t
