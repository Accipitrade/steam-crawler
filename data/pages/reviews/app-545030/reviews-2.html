\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL/DR: a "steam click bait" with very, very suspicious reviews.
Important: I really suspect the legitimacy of these raging reviews.
Seriously, this game cannot possible have this degree of incontestable positivity. It is on the grounds of Skyrim. Actually, it is much higher: as I write this, 92,54% of the reviews of this game are positive, while Skyrim has 79,6% positive reviews. Fishy, isn't it?
And yes, I really went the trouble to calculate this. And I even went farther: I inspected the guys that gave positive reviews to this game. Most of them gave it a raging review after 0.2 hours of playing it, some 0.3. Point is, most of them give a raging review after an abysmal low time, not enough for it is it? And it gets worse: If the game is so good, why it does not show that they played it more than 12 minutes? That is what 0.2 hours means: twelve minutes. And these reviews are months old, it is not like they just made it and then they will play the game hours, for months to end. No, they played it (or just executed the game file without actually playing it) for only twelve minutes, closed it, rushed to make a raging positive review saying how awesome this game is and then never again touched it. If it is so good, why not, you know, actually play it? It almost feels like the entire purpose was just to make a positive review...
Some of you may be thinking: "Maybe it is a niche game". It is not. It is just a collection of (few) mindless minigames, like old timer cell phones or worse, with a loose uninteresting story behind it. It looks like the work of someone practicing coding in order to make a real game someday.  Maybe some people may enjoy it a little, considering it a light time waster. But then these same people would never say this game is awesome, they certainly would be much more cautious about it.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh boy, this game knows how to make its player's salty - and that's not a bad thing.
I finished this game in about 5.5 hours, but you could pretty easily do it in one sitting if you really know what you're doing.
The character design is extremely charming, and despite the simple style and 'story', it still allows for dynamic and different characters.

There are some translation issues (the game was originally in Indo.), but this is commonplace for translated indie games. It's not an issue and overall does not impede understanding or gameplay.

Overall, the game takes a simple mechanic a long way, and it's way more than meets they eye. It's saddening that there is not a larger fanbase for this game! Easy way to lose some time; and yes, it is possible to get every achievement.

Totally recommended!
