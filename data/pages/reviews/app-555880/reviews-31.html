\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMan, I still remember the first day I played this game and how amazing just the first hour was with it. From that day forward I was looking forward to each and every update that came out from this amazing and personal team of people that really understood what a fun VR game was supposed to be like. Now that it is out of early access and all of the major changes fixed up, it is still my favorite VR game to date.

If you are looking for one of the best VR action games out there, then there is no better place for you to put your money in. Not only has the team been there from day one fixing and adding things that made the game better and better, they say that the little add-ons are not stopping anytime soon. This game is not only awesome, it will make you feel awesome. From bows, shuriken, and katanas, to SMGs, shotguns, and sniper rifles this game has it all. Do you like Super Hot? There is a mod for your suite for that to be a reality within this game. Do you like In Death? This game has one of the best bow mechanics out there.

If you are a fan of action VR games or just a fan of VR in general, I could not recommend this game enough. You will get your money's worth and more within just the first couple of hours.

I can't wait to see what this team is up to next because if this game has anything to show for it they have a bright future ahead of them.\t\t\t\t\t
