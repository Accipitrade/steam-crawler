\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI honestly think this is the best game on VR in terms of mechanics. The game utilize the third dimension better than any other game I can think of. You play as a ninja that can double jump midair, dash and slide on walls and ground, And because of the slow motion mechanic It is also very easy to control, extremely liberating.

The combat also feels great with a lot of different weapon types (swords, guns that you can duel wield, shotguns, rocket launchers, throwing stars, etc.) And because of the slow motion mechanics I feel like I am playing superhot with precise headshots and dodging bullets. Compounded by all the aerial movement mentioned above, you really feel like a badass ninja.

Lastly I tend to get very motion sick with VR titles but surprisingly I didn't feel much at all especially considering I spend more time flying and sliding on walls than on the ground. A must by imo.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOne of the best power fantasy simulators for VR available right now. By no means a perfect game, with certain areas feeling underdeveloped and showing some questionable design choices. The story feels present at best, and there is a definitely not a lot of variety in enemies to fight (Seven in total, counting the boss) and by the time you get to fight some of them in the campaign, you've already fought them a handful of times in the random missions, which are required to unlock some campaign chapters. But beyond all of that, once you actually get into the gameplay and get used to it's unique movement controls, this game absolutely SHINES! No game has better made me feel like a stylish action hero like Dante, Deadpool or Raiden (And i'm sure this is intended as his H.F. Blade from MGR: ReVengeance is a selectable sword in the game). The Time slowing, aerial movement, Wall running, and ground sliding give you freedom of movement and a combat experience unlike anything I've seen in VR, and the customization and loadout options promises tons of replayability and an experience tuned perfectly to your liking. This is a great foundation for something truly amazing and I so desperately want to see it refined and polished into the perfect action fantasy experience. Best of luck to Mixed Realms with the sequel!!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR; This game is great, you jump, shoot, get relics, upgrade stuff, increase challenge, use skills. It's 100% worth it. Had more fun than in doom eternal. 10/10.

What game does great:
-great weapon arsenal,
-shockingly good skill system for a VR game,
-RNG relic system to boost your gear
-with legendary variants that change the weapon itself (for example giving shotgun a "homing blades" ammo),
-decent story,
-a lot of game modes,
-lootboxes (the good kind, not paid in $$$ but earned in a game for leveling up and missions),
-the freedom of moving around and shooting,
-options to increase the challenge (higer tier enemies, random modifiers, etc.),
-soundtrack (The Heist, Dystopia and the one with female voice are the best),
-graphics.

What it lacks:
-objectives in all game modes are basicly: "kill all enemies" with a slight twist (don't think there is much to do about it tho),
-enemy variety, especially bosses (there's 1 "boss" type, and 8 types of normal ones)...
-...so the boss fights are kinda repetetive,
-optimalization isn't the best (but isn't the worst either),
-some bugs, ocasionaly missions can't be finished (happend to me about 2 times in 26hour gameplay),
-very short missions (3-5 minutes),
-story is a little short.

Not sure:
-Multiplayer (it exists, it seems pretty empty, but if you have a friend with VR and this game you can try it)
-Stealh, silent kill mechanics (so it's kinda like warframe, where the game is meant to be played as a quick and dynamic shooter, but it does give you an option to sneak and take some enemies silently with bonus exp and damage)
-it is pretty intense, so you may get a little dizzy when jumping around. i never get motion sick in VR but it may be a thing (but there are options to make it a little less intense),

Final Verdict: 8/10, but in my heart it's 10/10
Yes there are no perfect games, there is always a thing that could be better, but this one is just unexpectedly good and i had a hard time finding a cons. And what more could i want from this game... Well... just more of everything. It's like an amazing cake. And what is better than a cake? 2 cakes.

PS: I can't belive how underrated this game is, judgeing by the ammount of reviews, it deserves at least 10 times more. This is one of the best, if not THE BEST ninja-shooting, action, arcade, looter-shooter games i've played. Definetly at least in my top 3 VR games.\t\t\t\t\t
