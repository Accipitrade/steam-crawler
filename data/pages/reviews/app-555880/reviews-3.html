\r\n\t\t\t\t\t*Played on an Oculus Rift S*

Absolutely awesome VR ninja shooter. You can wall run, multi-jump and slide around various arena style maps. There are a ton of weapons to choose to slice, shoot, and throw. Most of them feel right in combat. And so satisfying to take down enemies with.

Game polish is about what I expect from indie games. There are a few bugs that consistently show up, like losing my ammo counters in mid-battle. But nothing I consider game-breaking.

I would not recommend this if you get the slightest bit of VR motion sickness. You are a ninja, in a shooter game. There is a fair amount of movement involved. Also, the store page lists Seated Play Area but it does not support that. You have to be Standing or using a VR mod to offset the camera.\t\t\t\t\t
