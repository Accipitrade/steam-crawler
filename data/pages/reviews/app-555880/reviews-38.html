\r\n\t\t\t\t\tExtremely fun, but you definately want to get your VR legs first. This can make you very motion sick. Backflips will nausiate the hell out of you until you acclimatize to it. After that, jumping and flipping is fine.

A very nice feature this game has that I've not seen in games like Dead Effect 2, but which could definately use it, is the ability to set your holster location. Sairento allows you to slide by crouching after a jump as well. The location of your holsters is configurable in game by simply selecting the option in the configuration menu, placing your hands where you would go to draw a gun, and pulling the trigger. Crouching is similar in that you just crouch to a comfortable height and then confirm it. This is an effective counter to a common VR problem.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMy first (I believe) steam review! This game is not just my favorite VR game -- it's one of my favorite games right now--period.

This game fulfills the promise of VR in a way nothing else even approaches; Mixed Realms has taken all the conventional wisdom about movement in VR, turned it on its head, and made something that is undeniably best-in-class. Take the best elements of Ninja Gaiden, Kill Bill, and Robo Recall and garnish with a dash of cyberpunk. Play this game to feel like a superhero.

In case you're one of the unfortunate souls that gets motion sick at the drop of the hat, they have included the ability to disable jumps or--for the truly tragic among us--even replace the dash with a pure teleport, so don't let the videos of high-flying ninjas dissuade you. I'd encourage everyone to give the default settings a go, though--they work surprisingly well.

The game is early access, but it is not a tech demo--the devs are just still adding a lot of content, removing bugs, and improving on what is already the best buy in the marketplace.

If you've read this far, you've wasted time you should have spent playing this game. Go buy it. Buy it now.

Mixed Realms, this is an amazing piece of work--I look forward to your always-excellent updates, and to any other game you decide to work on. Thanks!\t\t\t\t\t
