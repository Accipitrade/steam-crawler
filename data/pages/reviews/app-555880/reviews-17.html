But now with the steam summer sale, I found this game while browsing around in the store for new VR games and I found the trailer pretty interesting, so I ended up buying it and installing it the same day. Also saw this as an opportunity to try and setup 360 degree sensor setup and it was working as a charm!
But into the review:

Pros:
- Decent small tutorial to start off with!
- Missions that starts off easy and gets harder as the more you do (can also add more layers of difficulty)!
- Okay story (haven't played much of the campaign tho)!
- Fun and engaging combat (it really gives you the feel of being inside the game)!
- Nice reward system!
- Nice variation of weapons and all of them are fun to play, especially bow from the DLC which I ended up buying after a few play hours!
- Can do a flip mid air if you enable it in the settings (which made me almost loose balance a lot of times at the start)!

Cons:
- Some maps were pretty laggy and made it hard to navigate around and kill enemies.
- The Kusarigama weapon from the DLC could have had better animation when you turn them into an extendable sickle.
- Deflecting bullets doesn't always work (might be tracking issues on my part tho)

Now, as I haven't been playing much on the campaign, can't say much about it and I have also not tried the multiplayer since it seems pretty dead (was 2 players on Europe server last I checked). But overall, it's a nice game to kill some time with :)\t\t\t\t\t

Reviews should be Overwhelmingly Positive

Probably my favorite VR game I’ve played so far. I only have an hour played, but I just tried it out yesterday. It’s on sale right now, buy it, even if it wasn’t on sale, you should buy it.

Movement feels amazing, and being able to trigger slowmo as I wallrun, double jump to the floor, and slide on the ground, slicing enemies with my katana as I pass and loading them with bullets is some of the most fun I’ve had in VR.

For someone who does get motion sick occasionally when playing VR, this game didn’t make me feel sick at all. This is probably attributed to the teleport/jumping mechanic, which I normally don’t like in VR games, but it works in this one because it makes sense in the context of bein a cyberninja.

As with anything VR, watching videos doesn’t do it justice, you have to buy this one and experience it yourself. Worth every penny.\t\t\t\t\t
