When you start to chain moves together, the satisfaction is immense. Wall running, while firing arrows into multiple enemies in slow motion and ending with a pirouette of the blade to dice somebody into pieces; Deflecting bullets with your sword, then triple jumping into the air while firing your duel weird pistols off, then switching back to the sword for a hack-fest back on the ground - it's so good and so varied.
