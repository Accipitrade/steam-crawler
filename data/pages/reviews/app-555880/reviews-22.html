The good:
  • movement is pretty fun

The bad:
  • extremely glitchy
  • runs exorbitantly badly regardless of SDK, even though it doesn't look very good
  • reloading requires you to do absurdly hard wristflicks, almost painfully so, regardless of sensitivty
  • pistols on your hip are rarely actually on your hip, but just "somewhere" around your body
  • melee attacks while you are moving (like with a slidedash) have a 50/50 chance to just go through an enemy without doing anything
  • melee weapons feel like paper (seriously, when a game that's almost exclusively about guns like H3VR does melee practically infinitely better than you, you might want to reconsider your design choices)
  • midair flips that (according to the game) require you to lift both arms sometimes randomly activate when you only have one arm lifted, which is incredibly annoying when wallclimbing
  • walljumps have like a 25% chance to not reset your jump, which obviously is also incredibly annoying when wallclimbing
  • you can (and will) randomly fall through the ground, walls, ceilings etc.

Verdict:
While the movement is incredibly fun, the core gameplay isn't. Apart from the enemies, which mostly feel (bullet)spongy, everything in this game feels hilariously flimsy. The guns are flimsy and have practically no punch, melee is flimsy, melee hit-reg is flimsy, the walls are flimsy, the ground is flimsy, and the perforamce is flimsy.
It's completely beyond me how this game got the amount of positive recommendations that it has.\t\t\t\t\t
