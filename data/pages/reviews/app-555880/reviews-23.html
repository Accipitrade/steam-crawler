\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is quite buggy atm, but it's also super fun. I play it nearly daily and have no regrets. The only major things I have a problem with are:

1. I constantly have to reset my position because I get stuck in walls, or fall through the floor.
2. The sound effects are very compressed sounding.

Now for the positives:

1. Endless customization, and constant progression.
2. Crazy weapon variety (and for the most part they each feel at least good)
3. You can do slo mo flips! (You have to turn it on in the options menu last I checked)
4. The game never ends, and will auto generate missions for you infinitely. I haven't even touched the campaign yet because I enjoy the randomized missions enough by themselves.
5. The many manuverablity options have allowed me to experiment and find new fun manuevers like sliding backwards with my sword drawn, and firing at two different targets at the same time while doing a backflip with two pistols.
6. It can be a decent workout if you play on high difficulties, slide a lot, and use the bow and arrow mainly.

In short, this is probably my favorite VR game, and I've played quite a lot of them so far. The graphics and immersion aren't the best (likely due to the high number of enemies that load in at once), but the mechanics and replay value are way up there.

Played on HTC Vive\t\t\t\t\t
