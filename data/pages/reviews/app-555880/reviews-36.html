\r\n\t\t\t\t\tJust plain awesome. At this point the main reason I don't regret getting VR set. One of very few early access products that I would play almost after every update.

- creative movement system (wallruns, sliding)
- solid swordplay, bullet deflect, dual wield, even jedi-style dual sword
- unarmed combat
- rewarding for skillful play (complicated kills, killstreaks, etc)
- interesting character customistion via perks and "relics" (augments for weapons and clothes)
- nice atmosphere/design in general, kind of of japanese-cyberpunk pleasant mix, reminded me E.Y.E:Divine Cybermancy
- tons of fun in coop (some bugs though, but it's still EA)

There are few bugs, but they get stomped quickly (mostly in coop, but atm it's only abouth month since they rolled out the coop update). The devs seem to be passionate about their creation.

Gameplay: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A-KgX2NvyqA

Suggestions for devs:
- more fov/less zoom for sniper (and maybe some love for rifle relics to make it competetive in late game)
- perk for unarmed bullet deflection
- some kind of ragdolling enemies in unarmed/melee (they either die or don't react at all)\t\t\t\t\t
