\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFantastic game, smashed 2 cups while cutting a samurai to pieces.

I recommend messing around with the options and weapons for a while to find what's right for you, there's a lot of extra options that add a lot of fun and mobility but getting that "badass" feeling requires some practice if you want all the bells and whistles.

There's so many different ways to play the game, changing your weapon, relics and skill trees around a bit makes the game completely different so if you dislike the slo-motion you can just opt to never use it and not upgrade it at all in favour of other playstyles.

If you have motion sickness issues in other games maybe look up some gameplay videos on youtube first because it can get pretty intense. Some of the backflips I've done got me feeling a bit wobbly and I don't normally have that problem.

There are a lot of options to help with sickness but I've not really looked at them.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBought this game at full price, which I usually don't like to do. But, boy... an hour into this game, and I feel like I've already got my money's worth.

This game. Is. Amazing.

I was worried that I'd get extreme motion sickness, as I am unfortunately one of the suffers from it. However, the movement in Sairento VR is so smooth, that I experienced literally NO motion sickness while playing the Tutorial, and first 2 missions; and trust me, I was taking FULL advantage of the movement system in this game. Sidenote: I cannot praise the movement system in Sairento enough. It's a game unto it's own.

I think most of what I could say positively about this game has already been said, but I do want to say this:

I do not understand how this game isn't on every top 10 list. I bought Raw Data, and refunded it because it was so overhyped and repetivive. It was stiff, and suffered from way too much chit-chat, and standing around waiting for something to happen. This game is instant action, yet still has room for tactics. It's masterful. I can't wait to play more.

This will be my go-to game to show off VR to friends when they come over to try my Vive.\t\t\t\t\t
