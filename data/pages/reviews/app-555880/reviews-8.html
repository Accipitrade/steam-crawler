In response to the developer request for more details on the glitches I encountered; I will try to see if I can figure out more specific details the next time I go on a VR binge...

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI really want to like this game. The gameplay looks really fun from what I've seen on youtube, but the Index controls are terrible. If I so much as bring my hand even near the holster, I put away my weapon. Tried playing around with a few things in the options menu, and opting-in to the index beta, but still the same issue. I've played quite a bit of H3VR and almost never have any issues dropping weapons there, because its based on keeping the grip on the weapon, rather than squeezing the controller to holster / draw. Another game that does this right is Space Pirate Trainer. It has a similar "squeeze to swap" control, but the weapons are on your back instead of your hips, so its very unlikely for you to accidentally swap weapons. Maybe the Devs could put in an option to change grip control from squeeze to hold? Or maybe an option to disable the hip holsters (though I don't know how this would effect things in the later levels of the game).

I'll keep checking back every now and again on this game to see if this is fixed, because when I was not fighting with the controls the game is quite fun.\t\t\t\t\t

has a Good singleplayer campaign.
has Coop to play with friends
has a lot of movement features (Run and jump on walls FTW!)
