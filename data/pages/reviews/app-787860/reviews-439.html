\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFarming Simulator, what is there to say? Well,

The vehicle physics are about as functional and realistic as Desert Bus, and the game engine itself about as dated, merely carried over from the last games in the series with very minor visual improvements.

The environment mainly looks like hot garbage, with flat field textures, meshy trees, and a skybox that literally shakes, along with washed out colors that require a shader to look halfway decent.

The game performance is generally intolerable and horribly optimized for even the more powerful rigs out there, with most modded maps running like a fat kid with type 2 diabetes.

And to top it all off, Giants Software and their in-game ModHub continuously do their level best to provide their players and the general modding community everything they didn't ask for and ignore root problems like letting theft of other people's mods reach their official page uncontested.

So, all that said?
I freakin' love this game. The modding community is truly the backbone of this game and is the only thing that's given this Simulator series the longevity it maintains. Without them, well, look at Ski Region Simulator.. You'll get what I mean.
Keep doing what you're doing guys, and we'll all hope and pray that the next game in this genre will serve you better moving forward.\t\t\t\t\t
