\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFarming Simulator 19: The same old.

I have played farming simulator since forever. It's never been a game that feels complete, its raw and direct on what you do and what you get, still is pretty addictive and well aimed, until you eventually get tired of doing the same on its slow gameplay.

With the pass of the years you would expect plenty of changes and upgrades, but honestly, Farming Simulator seems stuck in time, basicly being the same game, release after release, with some minor changes that have been or could have been done with older versions and using mods. This latest version is no exeption, with the added problem that is really broken.

What people thinks: was rushed for christmas release, what i think: definetly. Not many changes in vehicles and placeables, in fact, feels it has much less options than it should. 3D Models arent close to the detail shown on promos and arts. You can take models from 5 years ago and will fit just right beside the "new" ones. Yea there are horses and there is cotton, but, thats about it with the new. I am guessing the plan for this release was adding a fair ammount of new features, but it has not really been done.

Key upgrades seems have been completely left out for this release and seems to get added over time, such as terrain modification, maps and vehicles, still, pretty slow at the moment. The ability to modify the terrain is basicly the only thing i can say is woth saying is new and clearly different from previous.

Game mechanics are exactly the same as forever. No upgrades, no new additions there. Graphics wise i dont see much change neither. A lot of low detail alphas, animations and meshes. Nothing outstanding with sounds, probably same as forever as well.

Bugs are all over the place. AI on the helpers is simply garbage and totally worst than older versions. They cannot operate machinery correctly on the easiest fields using the easiest equipment, random errors constantly that stops them from working, and sometimes they wont even understand the task unless you start doing it. They will leave random patches wherever they can. AI on traffic and pedestrians is dissapointing with clear use of simple loops everywhere.

There are also many other random bugs like world lighting issues, multiplayer lag and crashes, missing key information, random things that stops working.. its a mess.

In clonclution, this release is pretty much a dissapointment. Giants needs to realize that its already due to make a large overlaul on the series. The mechanics are simply too old in comparison to up to date games. The quality on visuals and sounds is no longer marginal with current displays and sound equipments. Much more can be done in favor of gameplay, but is being held back by recicling old versions.

If you havent played FS before, or atleast not for years, you will enjoy it, but its starting to be a dissapointment for those that knows how little this game has progressed over the years.\t\t\t\t\t

* There is no greenhouses
