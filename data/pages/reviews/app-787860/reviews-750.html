\r\n\t\t\t\t\tthis game has so much potential. It's such a shame that the ui is complicated, that it's riddled with bugs and that the devs clearly didn't put much thought into making a game that's easily understood, or at least playable to a new player.

The game is honestly amazing in concept- for an advanced player, the game is super realistic and really quite stunning. If you've played farmsim before then i'd recommend it, but before you buy and possibly waste money, please PLEASE understand that you'll constantly have to be consulting google and various forums to work out how to do simple tasks like using herbicide and weeding, for example. Even the core idea of selling your crops for profit has lost me. The very limited tutorial shows you the absolute basics, and without that, i wouldn't disagree with the thought that this game is next to unplayable. It took me about half an hour of actual playing to ragequit over something minor that would have taken seconds had it been explained by the game.

Altogether this is a decent game if you know what you're doing. But honestly, i'm not sure i'll keep persevering unless there's an update with some form of help guide that explains how to play the entire game. I understand people hate tutorials, but with a game this massive, it would be helpful to have one. It can be pretty buggy at points too and in the two or three days i've had this i've needed to restart from scratch as i've either been stuck somewhere or have had vehicles trapped on tools that they were supposed to be grabbing.

tldr, i'm giving it a 4/10. Difficult to use, impossible to learn, but aside from that, pretty visuals and well developed realism.\t\t\t\t\t
