FS19 is the ONLY modern multiplayer game that I have ever played that doesn't even support playing over LAN. On top of this, the expectation is that for a dedicated server, you either have to buy another copy of the game for hosting purposes, or you use an official server and pay a monthly fee. What kind of garbage business model is that? I even tried searching Google for help setting up multiplayer and even though my search terms were innocuous, about half of the results were piracy and game cracking sites. Wtf. Though, I suppose now I understand why.

If you only want to go for the singleplayer, I suppose it's a good game. However, do your research before attempting multiplayer, and if it doesn't seem worth the trouble, don't bother wasting your money.
