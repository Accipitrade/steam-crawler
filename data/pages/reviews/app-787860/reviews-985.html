\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI think this is one of those "love it or hate it" games. Most of the stuff I'm currently playing is FPS and vehicle shooter/sims like WarThunder (you can tell I'm a naval player by that comment). I've also played Farming Simulator since FS15.

I really enjoy having a game to casually derp around and build stuff without having to fight and defend. It does require a little strategy if you don't want to go broke without using finance mods. That said, this version has mod support built into it so you don't have to mess with unzipping mod packages into to game folders and that is really cool and convenient.

The mission system is improved. DLC stuff you bought for 17 seems to carry over or be available in the base game from the mod menu. You get to do a bit of vehicle customization/optimization when you buy or rent them. There are more crops and animals to manage. The cloud/smoke/particle graphics are a better, but still need improvement.

All said, if you are a fan of the series, buy 19 (especially while it's currently on sale). It's a definite improvement.\t\t\t\t\t
