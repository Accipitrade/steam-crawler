Since then Ive bought every one in the series but i usually wait a few months until it has plenty of mods. Many will tell you there is not much difference between each in the series and they are right but i like them anayway.

Why I love this game - It runs smooth on just about any computer (though older computers may have to turn the settings down a bit) and as for the game itself there is plenty to do. Its also a sandbox so you can litterally play it however you want. The mod support in this game is insane. Once a title comes out, there will be tens of thousands of official, fully supported, and even unsupported mods for this game.

My Cons - The mods kind of make the game. It's just one of those games you're going to mod and that's not a real con for this game just because if you can think of it, there's probably a mod for it, but the base game just doesnt stand well without mod support. [a side note] its getting pretty common with new titles to almost require modding, at least this game hands you a finished product to mod. We've all played titles that felt completly unfinished without mods.

Overall: This is a farming simulator but its not really a hardcore simulator, there is a good balance between realistic and game and that to me makes the fs series the top dog of farming sims.\t\t\t\t\t
