\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFull Price is $20.00. I honestly think this game is work at least $30 but I also got it on sale when I got it for myself but I paid full price so my husband could also have a copy of this game. The MP, I was hoping we could each own land in the same game but it turns out like you get helpers instead of partners.

Single player version, I am addicted so far. I'm at just short of 70 hours now. I've flipped my vehicles more times than I should probably admit. I've gotten my horse stuck in a tree, did a head stand with a tractor (bale fork attached). I've been hit by the train a few times from getting stuck on those tracks! lol Once I walked across the tracks and a train came. Terrifying! The flag post, by the way, also tends to startle me when my camera angle gets close to it. This happen to anyone else? LOL Scares me nearly every time. Placing things for your farm can be tricky with elevation, intensity, etc. so practice! It's not easy to fix elevation issues once you make them. Perhaps save first? But mine auto-saves every 15 minutes. :) Good, and not always good but works.

I never thought I'd enjoy farming before but this, without dealing with the beating heat, it's always a comfortable experience. :D And the experience of driving each type of vehicle.. Hearing the roar of the engines. Finding a good streaming radio station can also enhance your experience. Anything from Country to Justin B. I've found a few songs I added to my spotify thanks to the game. :)

I'm just now learning chickens and horses. The technology takes a little time to learn but ultimately you can get better! Looting the towns: You won't get to keep what you find. It resets but if it didn't, my roads would be so littered by now with signs and things I've hit. haha

I don't regret getting the game and I've laughed so many times. Sometimes outloud scaring anyone nearby, especially if they are sleeping. lol My poor family has to deal with my sudden bursts of laughter in their peaceful slumbers. That's another reason I love this game so much. It makes me laugh, it helps me focus and with time it helps me organize (somewhat.. it's tricky with these awkward devices and no mods installed).

I'm still vanilla game. I wanted an honest feel for the original game before I branch off trying other things. I confess I prefer to learn with some funds so I do grab onto the xml file for a game I really need to learn the machine parts with less worry of moneys lost while learning and experiencing. I also have one with no money advantage so I can learn to manage a small farm and the finances for it. But I like them separate, at least for now. I'd be bankrupt if I didn't do it this way.

Play it your way.. That's what this game offers for the most part. Choices, options, freedoms! <3
