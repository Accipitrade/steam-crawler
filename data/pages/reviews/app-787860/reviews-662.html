\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI dont have a huge amount of hours under my belt for this game but enough to know two things. It's both brilliant and not so in equal measure.

What I like:
Do (almost) whatever you want - There's a pretty good choice of farming to specialize in.
The farm machinery is sensational, the selection and realism is very good and just shopping for new toys is fun by itself.
Relaxation - Really is a game that soothes your soul as you learn about things like PTO shafts and yields.
Mods - To be quite honest, the game is nothing without them.


What I dont like:
It's all very 1 dimensional - Grow/harvest/sell and repeat. I would love a more enriched commerce feature. Like, adding value to your crops in a factory and selling to the open market with other players (Mods exists for this kind of play but they can be clunky)
Multiplayer - tried a few times, found it messy and buggy and laggy...all the y's
BUGS - I mean the game is around since 2008 and your tractor still ends up on it's side like your Granny with a bad hip
AI - it's very limited, you really should be able to hire workers to do the full harvest from start to finish


Overall:
The game is nice to play for a while but too reliant on mods and I fear the devs are happy to let the community do the hard work. I think it really needs to be taken to the next level in future releases with an enriched multiplayer, GPS, better AI plus more game mechanics that allow you to do more with your crops then just sell the raw product.

I give it a recommendation based on the fact I paid <€30 and the modding community.\t\t\t\t\t
