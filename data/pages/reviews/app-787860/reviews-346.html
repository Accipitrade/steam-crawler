\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAll in all, this is not a bad effort. The inclusion of new crops is much appreciated, and does help skew it away from "just" being a European farming sim. The UI improvements are nice, giving a "go to one group of pages for everything" method that is helpful. No more having to remember which key brings up financials, where to change settings, etc. That streamlining is very much appreciated.

Pros?
More universal crops, an easier method toward forestry income, and a more concise page setup for purchasing things.

Cons?
Bunker silos are simply buggy, it's way more hassle to use them than it need be. The map, despite giving more information, is a bit cluttered with too much information. There's still not much use for the hand-tools function and it feels like this is a big missed opportunity.

Would I buy it again? Absolutely. Will I keep playing? Of course. It's a fitting continuation of a franchise that is really doing what it can to stay fresh and vibrant. Is it a fancy first-person shooter? No. But as a farming sim it ticks all the right boxes.\t\t\t\t\t
