\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBefore I purchased this game, I read a lot of the reviews. So many made comments about graphics issues and how horrible the graphics were. So I watched some YouTube videos of folks playing the game and really could not see any of the problems mentioned.

My PC is, to be honest, a box of crap. My graphics card is kept cool by another fan laying under it, blowing up. I have just 4GB of ram. So I assumed the game would be sluggish. In some ways, yeah, it is. Some grass textures and groups of vehicles are two of the things that makes the game stutter until whatever it is passes out of the camera view. I use a lot of mods which are all loaded with the game. Other than that, no issues.

I'm loving it and have about 500hrs into it so far. I only play solo so have no report on multiplayer. Without mods, this game would be boring as heck and not worth it. The same was for FS 15 (which I played a LOT) and FS 17 (which I didn't like and didn't play much at all).

is it worth the cost? Sure. Catch it on sale like I did.
Are the graphics horrid? Things at a distance or whatever distorted? Problem with lighting? Nope. Not for me and my crappy PC.\t\t\t\t\t
