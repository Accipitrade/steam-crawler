\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly, I can't see why this game has the positive reviews it has. Not a lot has changed since 17, and the graphics aren't really that much better. The devs have stripped content out, greenhouses are gone, passive income buildings such as solar panels and wind turbines have gone. Granted these things don't necessarily make the game, but they were nice to have.

The modding community added to FS17 what the developers have missed, and clearly they have not learned from this, as a lot of these things still have not been added to the game.

My biggest irritation is that forklifts still operate as if the forks have grease on them and the objects have zero mass to them, after several attempts to try and load a pallet I was ready to give the game up there and then. I didn't even get on to logging but have been informed it as still as frustrating to try and move things about as it was in 17.

To me, the devs have just took FS17, upped the graphics slightly, made a new map, and added some mediocre extra content, like adding lime to fields, and released it. If you have FS17 in you library, you may as well just stick to playing that.\t\t\t\t\t
