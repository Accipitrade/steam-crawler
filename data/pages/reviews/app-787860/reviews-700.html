\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFarming Simulator 19 is a great game if you're looking for a no stress gaming experience, and you can sink a lot of hours into growing your farm.

There is a bit of a learning curve if you don't have much experience around farms and equipment, but this can be overcome quickly by doing the in game tutorials and reading the help screen. And if you need further assistance, the Farming Simulator players are some of the most helpful players I've run into.

Graphics in FS19 are the best in the series, with a newly revamped engine. Highly detailed equipment and realistic day/night effects and lighting are great. What's not great is the lack of optimization, with my 2080 struggling to maintain 80+ FPS with all settings on max @ 1440p.

A very active mod community really enhances the playing experience. You can customize your experience to replicate farming in your part of the world, or try farming in far flung places such as Australia or South America.\t\t\t\t\t
