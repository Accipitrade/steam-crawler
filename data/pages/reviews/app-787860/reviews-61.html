\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverview:
You control a plot of land that you must choose, purchase, and cultivate. You are given access to a shop where you can buy tools, vehicles, animals, and supplies. You can also get yourself a home and decorations.

---
Pros -
* Diverse choices for pretty much anything
* Hiring help is easy and does wonders while flying solo
* You can buy and PET a dog!
* Dog is indestructible
* Selling is about as easy as you could make it
* Most everything you need from maps, controls, and sale rates are in one menu
* There's a LOT of individual settings you can turn off/on and alter
* You can speed up time in the menu, find the rate of speed that works for your equipment and crew
* Has a small Character Customization menu when you start a new game

---
Cons -
* Originally felt insanely slow when trying to get into the game, can also be overwhelming
* Had to go into the controls and find a better bind to open the main menu
* Hired Help sometimes misses patches of product or dirt and you have to go back over it yourself later
* Changing the landscape is quite harsh
* Any building placement has a high chance of making weird hills and cliffs that are difficult for Hired Help and own vehicles to navigate
* Not a big deal, but like most Sim games, there's little to no story element that I could find (in case you're someone who's into story-based games)

---
This game is a lot more fun than I thought it would be. This is one of those games where you can mindlessly waste 3+ hours in the blink of an eye while still having the time of your life. As someone who hemorrhages their spending money on Sim games, this is a top favorite of this genre.\t\t\t\t\t
