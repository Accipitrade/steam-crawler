\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have played every Farming Simulator game since fs11 and I enjoyed all of them and put in many hours. I'm also not sure as to why there is hate for this game as some other reviewers have stated.... But the game will improve over time as Giants will work to improve the game more with updates/patches and there will be a lot of mods coming out for fs19 like in fs17.

Here are some of my pros and cons from my own experience of playing fs19 so far:

Pros:
- Updated graphics
- New brands and some updated tractors.
- New animals (Dogs and horses).
- New crop types such as oats and cotton.
- Better engine sounds for machines.
-Weeds and birds "hanging out" in your crops.
-Building your own farm.
- Ability to change most equipment color to a color brand of your choice.

Cons:
- Night and day cycle seems to take a while to appear and transition.
- Not many options for placeables. (although many mods will be coming to take care of this as well as in Giants own modhub).
-No option like in FS17 to open and close doors and other things with the click of your mouse like in for example, in the Estancia Lapacho fs17 platinum DLC map where you could open and close the doors of the house/garage etc. (in the fs19 version of Estancia Lapacho the doors of the house and garage are all open).
-Buying pallets of seed and fertilizer. Giants should have included standard in the game a seed/fertilizer tank like in fs15 and below. (But this has been fixed with mods that recently came out on various fs19 mod hosting sites).
- Placeables are sometimes buggy in not wanting to be put down in a specific part of an area and even next to each other.
-Not having roads to get to various placeables that are placed down.

All in all, fs19 is a good game. It still has some bugs to be ironed out. But Giants will continue to improve the game with patches and modders will be making/importing their mods to make the game better.\t\t\t\t\t
