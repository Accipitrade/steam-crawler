\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVAST improvment over the last installment. Especially with the livestock upgrade, which is fantastic for those that perfer more of a "Rancher" type experience. Is this game perfect? by no means. But in videogames, issues pop up. Most of which I'm sure will be dealt with in a timely manner, as people report them. I personally feel like there was an improvment to the engine (Because it IS a new engine, they've said as much...it's kind of a big deal to them).

The custom charactor is a nice change of pace, though not super important...because you aren't looking at your own face. The focus of the game is Farming...this isn't "Country Fashion Barbie". Not sure why people are complaining about that.

I have graphics maxed out on my slightly above average rig and the game looks gorgeous. no FPS drops, reflective surfaces are great, the sun shining through the trees is beautiful. the dog looks dumb. a Little more effort in the dog, please. You cant be a proper farmer without a loyal dog.

The field destruction is really cool, having Narrow wheels changes how you determine which tractor to use. Ability to turn all that off is there. the worker function never really worked great, but it is a bit better. Mods will eventually come out to fix all that. Giants has always been really good about supporting that community.

Cons? Vehicle controls are CLUNKY as a drunk nun. Cruise control is your friend here. The mod screen is just as much a pain in the ass as before. Navigating it really needs a serious overhaul. The inability to delete redundant buildings to make room is non-exsistant. So that annoying shed thats sittinf where your chicken coop needs to go (because waterfalls make happy chickens)? Nope... going to have to move it three inches to the left. MOTHERF$&*%#&!!!!!1

In closing. Is good...Wodka anyone?\t\t\t\t\t
