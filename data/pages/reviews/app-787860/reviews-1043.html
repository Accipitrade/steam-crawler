\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWith only 9 hours of gameplay so far, I can say I had enough. I've played FS15 and FS17 and both of them are resonably better than this. The "overhauled graphics" and "Brand new graphics engine" really isn't what GIANT has lived it up to be, as the game only looks good if you max the settings with only 15-24fps. The background scenery is a sloppy mess which they didn't put any effort into and the trees look like they have knives for leaves. Moving on, the building in the game is an utter disappointment and rarely ever works in your favour. The buldings deform the land in hideous ways and give you a headache as you can barely place anything in them if they are on uneven terrain. It costs a fortune to place a bulding anywhere but flat land and ruins the area around it. Furthermore, there are so few buildings, that i dont even know why we have the option to bulid! You have an over priced farm house that does absolutely nothing, a couple ugly silos, a few sheds, and little to no useful buildings on top of the few i mentioned. And the dog! Don't even let me start! It's a clumsy, poorly put together animation and model which is a pointless mess.

Now for the farming. The overall farming feels like any other farming simulator. It's choppy animations and ugly pop-in textures are all the same. The steering of the tractors is very slow but somewhat okay, and if you go anywhere over 40-50km/h, you end up doing a double backflip into a wall or fence somewhere. The first person view is slow and eats your frame rate, and the animations for operating the vehicles that GIANT praised is just simple. Nothing special about it. Now lets talk about the prices. The land that you purchase is way too over priced and makes the game a chore. You can barely make a living no matter what difficulty and the land that you think would work to put a shed on, is occupied by empty space that wont let you place any objects. Also, they got rid of the vehicle menu so that you cant find your vehicles or reset them. The only way to do it is to zoom in really far on the map and click on the icon. This way you cant find vehicles or equipment and check to see what you have if you're on a big farm. It is honestly disappointing

The game has major bugs and issues. If you tab out of the game at any point or even go to your toolbar, the game chooses a random aspect ration and basically streatches or shrinks your game to oblivion. Another great one is a sound bug that ear rapes you with one loud ass engine rev. There are so many that the list would go on forever. Honestly, its a piss-poor effort of a game and they should be ashamed to even call it a simulator. There are better FREE games out there than this $50 title. It feels as if they didnt even test it.

To wrap the whole thing up, its a huge disappointment. GIANT is just one big scam and puts no effort into making games at all. Oh and I forgot! It rains indoors still. They completely lied to us. Unless they update it or change how to whole game feels, stay to FS17.\t\t\t\t\t
