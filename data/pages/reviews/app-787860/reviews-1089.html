\r\n\t\t\t\t\tNow lets start of by saying that Giants did many things right, with the new installment of FS19. The expansion in animals and the dynamic land is something that was definetly needed in previous version.
It is a game that is nice to look at and promises almost the same experience as FS17 so if you liked that one and had a few bugs that frustrated you for example in timber and pallet movement, these are solved. A nice overhaul to the field floor texture as well as graphically overall improvements can be seen.

Now to the wonky bits, the building system......
While the thought of building your farm modullarly is absolutly amazing in practise it is something that will frustrate the player from the get go. In normal mode no farm buildings are given to the player and if you play for example the american map (not very flat), you may soon realise that the buildings will level out the ground.
While this is realistic as no buildings are build angled, Giants did not implement an option to level out ground or even change ground textures with a tool, the essence leads to huge hills that lead up to your buildings.
Also the idea was that you could buy land and create your own farm, well since we can not create roads you have random buildings standing in the middle of a grass patch.

In all honesty I belive that the modding community will help all players but this brings me to the next point.
With FS19 it seems as if giants got lazy and thought, the things that we dont do perfect the community will adjust, this is a mindset that will destroy a franchise. And this would be a shame!

There is many good things about FS19 but also still many bad things, i hope that Giants will solve this via patches and their own mods on the website if not it will be Farming Simulator 2017.5\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlease read before buying Farming Simulator 19!!!!

I've read through on alot of the reviews and came to a conclusion that FS19 is about average or above average. There is alot of good, but there's alot of bads too, I'll give some of my opinions and share some aswell!

Alright I'll start with my opinions
Good:

• The visual overhaul is great as it gives more life to the environment

• The HUD change is great as it looks clearer and looks more pleasing to the eye, as well as the additional options such as being able to change the keybind without having to go to the main menu is also good!

• The addition of John Deere equipment is great!



Bads:

• The new Platinum edition/expansion is in my opinion not worth the name Platinum, it's more like an CLAAS DLC since there is only equipment. No maps... NO MAPS?!?! I've followed you since 2015 and both 17 and 15 had new maps, and now I came across 19 where I only get pitty equipment with no fun map to play on, smh for sure.

• Physics is an enemy of mine when it comes to the farming simulator franchise, one example is that I cannot load bales manually because the bales are basically rocks that weigh less than a gram, seriously, where is the squishy grip?? I cannot grab a bale without it sliding off or flying to space. And while I managed to load 1 bale and about to place 1 more on the trailer, the slightes bump will make it slide off the trailer.

• The game is overall boring, whenever I feel like playing farming simulator 19 I hop on a map on either SP or MP and while I can easely play for 20 minutes as soon as I reach about 30 minutes of gameplay and I get workers doing most of the jobs because it is either too boring or it takes too long to do. And I'll just kinda quit because It's boring.


Overall conclusion on what this/next game needs to update on:

• Better gameplay experience
• Better physics
• Plan better DLCs (Overall poor)


Don't take this as hate, I still play the game, but I think I could get a better experience if the game was a bit updated with the things I've listed above. I totally recommend you to buy this game if you are a fan of farming or willing to become a farmer IRL. Personally I am a farmer myself and quite enjoy to play this game when I don't have anything todo other than to roll my thumbs at the office.\t\t\t\t\t
