\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIn short this game is a great and fun time killer. BUT if your computer is lower end you will have some lag "UNMODDED". The game generally runs from 30-60FPS either way at 20-30 FPS its still playable. The game is also stable and no bugs until you start adding mods. The mods won't necessarily break your game, BUT ADDING A LOT OF MODS CAN AND WILL SLOW LOADING TIMES, they can cause conflictions between other mods, and make your game crash on loading the saves. I see a lot of negative reviews for this game because of issues caused by mods or people who don't know how to set up basic optimisation. After that's off my chest now, I'm going to be talking about base game and official DLC. The base game is a fun and enjoyable experience with countless hours. All the differant DLC are not needed to have a full experience but add alot to the game for most of them. There is no terrain deformation because of the minimum system requirements you would need. Animal husbandry is fairly in depth in basic care. The crops yield system is fairly in depth with needing fertilizer, weeding, plowing, cultivating, and limeing. (All which can be expanded with free dlc.) You can hire basic workers to help with most field tasks. (You can also get a mod that takes time to learn but has a smarter AI system.)
