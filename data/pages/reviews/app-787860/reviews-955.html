\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHello fellow digital farmers,

After having played this game / simulation for more than 700 hours, i thought it was time for a detailed review of this so addictive game / simulator. And why so extensive? Because this game / simulation simply deserves it and it is not that easy to explain, why this is so attractive and becoming more and more popular.

To begin with, you see as a reader, that i find it difficult under which category i can place this game. It can lead a wonderful life as a simulation, but it is just as well a game with its limitations within the game engine. Of course it's just A game and you'd say it's done. But the "game" includes so much more than that.

The developers of this game have made a nice balance between: just a good game and a relatively realistic simulation. Elements such as invisible walls / hit boxes are still present here and there, but are not overwhelmingly present. A balance between an arcade game and a simulation is perhaps a bit too black and white in a comparison, but it certainly has roots that direction sometimes.

You notice that every time it is 2 aspects that mix with each other every now and then. They alternate well each time. That not only ensures a good gaming experience, but makes judging about this game / simulation quite difficult. Sometimes you get annoyed by the "woody feeling" that the game makes you feel and at other times it presents itself wonderfully smoothly.

A game with two faces.

Graphically beautiful, but again not really eye candy.
Sound is certainly not bad, but not great either.
The gameplay is pretty easy to do, but really well-arranged, no.
The tutorial is pretty good and teaches you the basics, but there is so mush more in the game, that you sometimes have to discover it by trail and error.

As you read, every time there is something good, but also a bit less good things.
A balance that is so well mixed that you can make every setback disappear again, by something good that the game / simulator will surprise you with.

What is the real power of this game / simulator than?

Can you drive around with big fat cool looking tractors? Yes! - Check.
Can you work with the largest and most expensive equipment? Yes! - Check.
Can you design and build your own farm? Yes! - Check.
Does it also support, keeping animals on my farm? Yes! - Check.
Oh, does it support MOD's? Oh yes and how! - Check.
Can i also use other landscape maps than the vanilla ones? Pff...yes! - Check.
Does it also support multiplayer? Thats almost the best thing about it! - Check.
Is there perhaps also online in-game radio? Yes! Yes! Yes! - Check.
But can i change the colors of my purchased verhicles?......yeah.
Is it also educational and do you learn somthing from it, in the field of agriculture? (the parent says) Yes ofcource.
Is it replayable than? That is the purpose of this game...lol.
Is it expensive to purchase the game? Not really.
Are there DLC's too? You're kidding, right? Bloody hell yeah.
Are there any bad things about this game?

No, not worth mentioning and now keep your mouth shut and make me money at the farm.
Just gooddamn buy it!...lol\t\t\t\t\t
