\r\n\t\t\t\t\t2020-01-17 REVIEW:
A lot better than it was at release, but still lacks basic quality of life improvements. My last straw: I accidentally swiped with the landscaping tool and no undo option is available. When I opened the menu to quit, the game auto-saved. I deleted the save game then uninstalled FS19.


OLD REVIEW:
This game is buggier than I would have hoped for from a big studio release.


1 - When trying to unload a harvester into a trailer, it doesn't recognize the main body as a dump zone, but might catch the very edge of the truck as acceptable. The result is it's very hard to unload, and if it does, it's unloading outside of the trailer.

2 - I just accepted 3 missions, leasing the equipment. A popup shows up saying that the equipment at the store. The equipment is not at the store, and I don't have any missions. I just accepted three in a row (you can only have one at a time).

3 - I drove over a train safety railing and got stuck. After the train passed, the railing lifted the harvester into the air. Quite unrealistic. I had to use a trailer to ram it down.

4 - The lighting is very flickery, even on my 1080 Titan,

5 - "Helper B has stopped unexpectedly - for an unknown reason" - Reason: Invisisble rocks on fairly flat ground between fields 25 and 26

6 - "Icons - Part 2" in the help doesn't have a #5, but instead shows #9 twice.

7 - The game should be called "Transport & Delivery Simulator 2019". I can hire workers for the farming tasks, but not for driving/transport/delivery. Therefore, I spend the vast majority of my time driving, not farming. I don't care for this. I wish it was like the mobile 2018 game, where I can order a vehicle to drive to B, or unload and return from site C, or auto-refill tanks, etc.

8 - I want the camera position to be remembered between play sessions, not just between vehicle cycles. As it is, I have to start every game session with gobs of scrolling out. This will only get more and more tedious with the more vehicles I purchase.

9 - The vehicles are super bouncy. It's very easy to tip them over. I recently spent a half hour getting a mission-leased semi unstuck from a tree by ramming it with a tractor, which itself flipped over twice in the process. This, combined with driving from A to B, was the vast majority of my play tonight. No bueno. I'd love a simply "Flip me right-side up" button.
9.1 - Update: It's getting very frustrating, during my forced transports (no auto-pilot), I'll bump a tree, or a sign, or a trash can, or a car, or a rock, and my tractor and trailor flip. Then I have to spend the next 15 mins+ getting another tractor there to ram me right side up (which did 5 mid-air barrel rolls before getting flipped itself after taking a sharp turn). This is a transport and tractor flipping game. I do very little farming. I am aware I can "Reset" tractors from the map, however, you penalize me hundreds (if not thousands) in cargo/fuel. That is unnecessary and uncalled for. Very frsutrating.

10 - I wish all of the icons would say what they are when I mouse over them.

11 - Looking at my Owned Items in the Garage, I expect the pictures to look like the tractor I have, not the stock, unmodified tractor (ie. one of my tractors has 8 wheels, but the photo of it in my garage only has 4)

12 - Again in the Garage, I'd love to see the tractors/vehicles coupled with the accessories/trailors they have currently equiped. This'd help me identify which one of many I am looking at, as well as which accessory. For example, if I want to sell one front-weight, which of my dozen do I need to sell? Or, which of my 8 green tractors is the one I want to identify?

13 - Why can't I use a helper to fertilize or lime?

14 - I'd like to have am/pm times for the clock. Not just military time.

15 - Let me sleep during the day. The only purpose of the house, otherwise, is to speed time only at night, which makes it much more tempting to just sell the house and pocket 175k.

16 - Can we get a x240, or even a x480, time speed? When I've nothing to do but watch as crops go from planted to grown, it still takes quite a while, even on x120.

17 - If I run into a car with my tractor/trailor, I go flying, and the car doesn't budge. I should be able to shove them out of the way...

18 - Why don't you post patch notes? I believe you've updated a few times since launch, yet no patch notes. Without knowing what you've fixed, I'm forced to assume nothing. Not communicating didn't work out for No Man's Sky...

I'm sure I'll run into more issues, and I hope Giants fixes these release bugs asap.\t\t\t\t\t

-Build your own farm is a joke. There are a few buildings, but placing them in a way that makes them look good and functional is almost impossible.
-Once again the game releases with only 2 maps. Maybe I am missing something, but it seems like each version of this game keeps moving towards mountains every field is on a hill of some kind. Can I please have just one map of nice flat farming terrain.
-One of the biggest problems I have can be seen with this game, but many other games as well. Mods are great but if we have to count on them to make the game playable then the developers are not doing their jobs.
-The game is playable and it is fun, but it's a dissapointment from what I was expecting.
-If you already have '17 save your money and just keep playing that, at least until this goes on sale. The current price is not worth the graphics upgrade. Thats all that is different here, all of the other smalle changes are attainable via mods for '17 If you don't have '17 buy that instead or wait. Hopefully they will fix this one.\t\t\t\t\t
