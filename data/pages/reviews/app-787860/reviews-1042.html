\r\n\t\t\t\t\tDespite what other people say about this game, I think this game great.

The "Farm Manager" mode is something that I have been waiting for for years; it's awesome. I was at the point in FS17 where I would just sell off all the stock vehicles and start my farm for my choice as much as possible. The fact that the construction was very limited always bugged me.

The new maps are awesome too. They look so much better than the previous ones.

The only thing that still really bugs me (as a person who is all about the little things) with the features aspect of this game is that there is no American-styled equipment in this game. Why go through all the effort to try and make an American styled map without American equipment??

Speaking of bugs, the only issue that I have come across so far is a little graphical glitch with the John Deere 6M where if you turn left, the JD logo on the front left fender veers off the fender.

Oh, and spreaking of graphics, the graphics in this game are awesome. A larger jump in improvement compared to the jump from FS15 to FS17.

If there would be anything to me that would make this game better to me it would be to lengthen out the 2 year deadline for another game to 2 and a half, or four years. That would get rid of the rush and people would be more happy with a less buggy game (according to others).
