\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI was trying to think of how I wanted to review this game myself in a way that would best reflect my views on this game. Despite the 45 hours I've put into this game on Steam, I have also previously put around 264 hours into this game on the Epic Games Launcher (yes, I know what others think of the launcher. I don't care, I just used EGS to get FS19 for free and to get free games in general, thats about it) which totals my hours of gameplay to over 309 hours across both launchers.

Anyway, down to the review.

I recall thinking and saying something to myself some years back, roughly when I think FS17 came out, but it might also be FS19, I'm not sure. That thing was this, "This game looks stupid, I'd never play this." Fast forward to this year when they released it for free on the EGS and I started playing it, now also owning it here on Steam.

I do genuinely like this game, with the models of different equipment types from vehicles to trailers being well modeled and accurately represented. The graphics are also pretty dang good even on my low end hardware, which I found to be pretty pleasing. I have also come to enjoy how the game works with mods without much issues, which is also great.

Pros:

+ Graphics look great, can't complain about how the game looks.

+ Equipment representation is well-done and accurate, down to very small details.

+ I like the brand variety in the game, giving a lot of choice for most uses.

Cons:

- The land editor could use some improvement and work, as I don't find it to be very good.

- The traffic AI isn't great and could use some tweaking.

- The game has some mods that I have used that I think could be actual features in the game. Mods like Additional Field Information, Passenger Mod, God Hand, and more quality of life improvements.

- Being on a server is miserable, because the game freezes for a moment whenever someone joins, which makes being in any public server not worth it.

8.5/10

Would farm and go into debt again.
