\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had never played any of the previous farming sim titles but decided to give this one a try, especially as I had recently built a racing setup and heard that Farm Sim 19 was wheel and pedal compatible. I was not impressed by the wheel and pedal support (no force feedback and poor control mapping), however I had quite a bit of fun with the game itself. It has a special appeal with friends to get in there and just get some farming done. The map we played on was neat, with a bunch of little easter eggs and was fun to bomb around in our trucks and explore. I do feel, however, that a lot of obvious features are missing from the game, some that are added via mods, however I almost feel like the developers lean on the modding community a little bit too much. I don't really know anything about farming outside of experience with this game, so the DLC doesn't really mean much to me but it seems a little overpriced. I would definitely recommend getting this game on sale if it piques your interest.\t\t\t\t\t
