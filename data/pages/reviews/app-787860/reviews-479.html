\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you're interested at all in agriculture or you and a couple friends want to grab a game to mess around with its janky sandbox then this is 100% the game for you.

Fair warning to everyone looking for an extremely immersive sim, however, as this game is not that. As I mentioned before, the game is quite janky, and while the bugs are amusing (like I managed to do good ol' prop levitation with a bag of seeds to traverse the map faster than any car could drive), they can definitely take you out of the experience if you're hardcore and care about that. Not to mention the farming is not entirely realistic. The game breaks the process down into it's core stages of tilling-nurturing-harvesting but doesn't go in depth into the science behind farming and the methodology each individual real-world farmer chooses to use. There is a partial fix to this, though, as the developers released a free precision farming dlc on their website that adds pH, nitrogen, and soil type factors that us farmers actually have to take into account and the amazing modding community for this game has also introduced seasons and planting time frames. There are still aspects of the industry that go completely unmentioned even with these addons, like irrigation and erosion, but they help add to the realism nonetheless if you so want.

With all that said, while not being the perfect window into the life of a farmer that people detached from that lifestyle might've wanted to have, the game is relaxing and educational, especially when it comes to equipment brands (even I was introduced to some mighty pieces of equipment I have never heard of before), and I personally love it for when I don't have the chance to be out there on the field myself.\t\t\t\t\t

What is the game
Farming Simulator 2019 is developed by GIANTS software, that have making this game from 2008 till 2019, and will most likely keep making them. Farming Simulator is all about farming as if it was real life. You will be able to earn money from animals, plants and placeables. You will be able to player multiplayer with other farming simulator players and help each other with farming, or invite a friend.

Technical Analysis
Farming simulator 2019 can be very easy for a pc to run or very tough to run, compared to the highest and lowest settings. The following min. specs for this game is:

CPU: Intel Core i3-2100T @ 2.5GHz or AMD FX-4100 @3.6 GHz.
OS: 64-bit Windows 7, Windows 8, Windows 10.
VIDEO CARD: Nvidia Geforce GTX 650, AMD Radeon HD 7770 graphics card or better
FREE DISK SPACE: 20 GB.
DEDICATED VIDEO RAM: 2 GB.
