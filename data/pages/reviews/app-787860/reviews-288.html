\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet's get one thing straight-- I love this game. It's one of my favorites on Steam when my friend can hop on with me. It's pretty boring by myself though.

This game is not beautiful. The graphics and mechanics are archaic at best. It also feels like there was quite a bit unfinished about the game. For instance, your customization options. It becomes abundantly clear there was meant to be a lot of character customization, but that never came to fruition.

That being said, this game is so much fun. Especially with the Seasons mods. There's a huge surplus of stuff to utilize. At its heart, this is an economy game. It's not a driving simulator (mirrors don't work very well). It's more about the rewarding feeling of working hard mentally and having the patience and dedication to produce a good yield to pay off your equipment and upgrade to better equipment or gain more land.

You can also be a logger, but the controls for that machinery are just terrible.\t\t\t\t\t
