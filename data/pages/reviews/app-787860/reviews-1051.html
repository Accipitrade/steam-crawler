\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAnother solid reiteration of Farming Simulator, there's absolutely nothing inherently wrong with the game.

Vehicle textures have improved along with field textures and richness of color.

There's several new features that add some welcomed depth to the series. I enjoy the option of being able to set up wherever I want as well as addtional field maintenance to acheive maximum efficiency.

( HINT : Yes, there are a handful obvious spots on the map where they are basically inviting you to build, BUT you can revert any plowed land back to grass and virtually homestead on any section of map, given it's owned land)

The US map is a bit stark and a bit odd for a "farm feel", I think they were going for a Northern California farming community feel with the map.....it is growing on me, albeit slowly. I trust there wont be any shortage of available maps once people have time to complete map mods.

It seems like people were hoping for more, that's understandable. Given Giant's trackrecord, the new releases have always been a bit "updatey" but, as far as I'm concerned....I'm happy with the game.

Seasons anyway???...... What am I going to do? Chop all the wood on my map while my animals starve with the game clock is set to 120x because I can't plant anything? eh, just my two cents. ( wait, maybe that would be cool lol )

~ I've not ecountered any bugs, glitches or crashes at this time. 11/24/18\t\t\t\t\t
