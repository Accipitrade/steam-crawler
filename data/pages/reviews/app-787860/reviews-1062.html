\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSignificant upgrade over FS17. Too many people are focusing on small bugs present at launch (admittedly annoying) and missing the large improvments made elsewhere.

The graphics may not look dramatically different on the surface but the change in the rendering process feel different in game. Add to that the dramatic level of detail on every object and the too-many-to count new animations and this really feels like a simulator.

The ability to place most of the useable objects around the farm really is a game changer. At launch there is admittedly a lack of variety in placeable objects, but just being able to determine where you store crops, park vehicles and most importantly raise animals allows for more freedom than any previous entry. You are no longer stuck with the map maker's idea of a layout!

Highly recommend to any Farming Simulator fan!\t\t\t\t\t
