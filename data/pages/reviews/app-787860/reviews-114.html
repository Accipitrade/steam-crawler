\r\n\t\t\t\t\tfarming, pure and simple. none of the mechanics are overly complicated or tedious and they're satisfying enough that it leaves you wanting to keep at it. perfect for just relaxing and working those fields.

one of the biggest positives for me is the insane amount of mods and mod support, it adds entirely new dimensions to gameplay. that said, if a game needs mods to make it fun....maybe not the best base game? i'll leave that for you to decide.
it also has extremely good controller support, (i had 3 devices hooked up at once and it was able to recognize and bind to all of them!) and there's a lot of available info online and ingame about game mechanics and machinery.

all in all this game is a very satisfying game to play and is very in-depth and well made. i would recommend it to any simulator fan. as for farming simulator 2022, i don't know how it compares, but for what it's worth i haven't purchased FS22 yet because FS19 still holds its own as a very solid sim game.\t\t\t\t\t
