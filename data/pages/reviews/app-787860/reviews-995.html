Don't get fooled by the positive reviews. This is my worst game experience so far...

You are going to use vehicles, aren't you? It's a farming game. You're gonna drive them in roughly 98% of the time.
Well, you are going to get annoyed by the constant revving of the engines, because the incompetent dev team can't fix a simple sound bug for 200 days.

Today it's 2019.06.30 and it's broken since 2018.12.12. That is TWO HUNDRED days of ignorance.
I say broken because there were no problems with the "day one" patch.

What's the issue?

You hop into a tractor and start the engine. The Steyr CVT has a base rpm of 850. Pleasant sound. It's great.
Now, you move forward, and all you can hear is a monotonous screaming of the engine. How is that possible?

Let's check it with cruise control:

Tractor at idle, the engine is at 850rpm.
Cruise at 1km/h on flat surface: 850rpm
2km/h: 1372-1390rpm
3km/h: 1892 rpm
anything more than 3km/h: 1892rpm

So above 3km/h, there is no ups and downs based on performance and load changes. All you can hear is one, monotonous and annoying engine sound. Imagine that you are in your car, and from the very moment you accelerate over 3km/h, you hear the maximum rpm, that can be 3000,5000,7000, whatever. It's annoying and not healthy, right?

Well, you can expect this in this game.

It WAS wonderful, I played literally hundreds of hours, let's say 300 hours with the 1.1.0 version.
But ever since I bought it here, and they broke it, I only install it from time to time, to enter, create a game, buy the machines, check the sounds, exit and uninstall. It's broken AF, unplayable for me and definitely unplayable for audiophiles.


HUGE potential, nice implementation, but ZERO commitment to offer a quality product to the audience and ZERO communication.

Needless to say, it's all about money, because they released a nice paid DLC in the meantime. Of course.
Priorities apply. Money over quality.\t\t\t\t\t
