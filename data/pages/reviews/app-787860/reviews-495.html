There are soo many different tools you can use! Harvesting a crop isn't just about getting a harvester. There are many tools that go into it and a process you have to follow. In my opinion, although realistic, it is extremely confusing and time consuming. There is very very very little guidance throughout the game, so if you're going to play it, I recommend having youtube open on another device. Also side note, when you order equipment, it doesn't go to your farm. It goes to the shop and you have to find a way to get it to your land or wherever.

CROPS AND LAND
So, I'm not 100% how to get specific seeding for the crop you want to grow, and in fact a lot of the land on the map already has crops on it. Idk. Again the game offers minimal guidance and I just recommend opening youtube (or saving some time and not getting the game). But besides that, taking care of your crops.... super duper time consuming and tedious for me. I spent 40 minutes just cultivating the soil. Then I have to sow and seed and ahhhhhhh that requires multiple pieces of equipmenttttt. (which still show up at the shop and you have to haul all the way to wherever your land is)

ANIMALS
I got this game for the animals, but they're a bit high maintenance for me. For example, if I buy a hay bale, I have to get farming equipment to pick it up. Okay, fair enough, but if I want to give it to my animals, I have to buy another piece of equipment for that. If I want to give them water-- OH! I need a giant water tank for that. What even are hoses? It isn't clear how to get their products either... I'm sure a youtube tutorial will clear that up.

GAME DESIGN
Okay, it's open world which I think is really nice! But it was just a bit under done for me. There are two places to buy things. The shop, and the animal dealer. I wish there were more places to buy things to make it feel more atmospheric but putting that aside,,, the shop and the animal dealer is not a shop and animal dealer. What I mean is, instead of entering a building and talking to a friendly NPC, you walk up to a floating emblem and view everything there. In fact, you never ever open a door in the game. If it's not already open, you cannot go through it. Idk seems a little bare bones and takes me out of the game. Also you only see people in certain places of the map and you can't interact with them in any way.

CONTROLS
I am playing on PC and the key bindings are pretty inconvenient. You can always customize them though. Besides that, holy frick is it hard to control the tractors! With practice you'd be fine, but I wish there was some way to auto straighten the tractor when plowing and things.

PHYSICS
you can't crash into cars or people :( I know its not gta but cmon let me crash into a car with my mega tractor :(((
Okay but in all seriousness, I accidentally took my truck flying off of a cliff and tumbled on the ground and left without a scratch . Yeah sure that's cool and all that I didn't have to pay for repairs, but as I was floating through the air pondering what would happen next, a little part of me wanted to see some demolition.

OVERALL
Look, the tractors and equipment. They're very specific and wonderfully made in the game. If that's your jam, freaking go for it man! Buy this game! Have at it! But that is it. It really is FARMING simulator. But it simulates NOTHING else. There are no relationships, no tutorials, nothing. You'll be closer to your horse than anyone else in the game. It is almost post apocalyptic feeling.

I wouldn't recommend this game, but hopefully with my other ideas you can formulate an opinion on whether it has what you desire. goodbye thankyou
