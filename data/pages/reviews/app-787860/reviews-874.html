\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAs a long time video gamer, this one stacks up there nice in the simulator world. Having just passed the 1900 hour mark, I will state that I have played about every situation available on this but the arcade style sim league. Not a fan of that, but to each their own. The maps that come with this game are real fun to play, or tack on some mod maps and see how you can do. I have a few game saves running. One I play from scratch and the other one I did the money cheat. Seasons mod really adds the realism one would expect as a real dirt warrior.

The graphics are fantastic on my PC's. I utilize twin 1080 GTI cards from 2017. That said, some of the glitches in the programming make the game a bit quirky, as in the dairy cows walking right through your character. If you can put that all aside, then game on, because this is pure fun. The addition of horses is a great feature, and you really do have to ride and care for them everyday to max out your profits. The timber industry side of the game is a lot of fun, too.

The equipment used in this game is far reaching, and again the mod world has only added to the huge selection one can play with. I haven't yet tried multiplayer and would imagine that could be a ton of fun, especially when a player has to clear those bales from the fields, the hired help can't do it.

My biggest like right out of the box is the in depth realism and play options available. My biggest dislike is the AI needs help in the hire a worker department. It seems most times they skip parts of fields, don't catch the end rows well, and usually find every way possible to collide with objects. In a game world this big, my gripe may be small, but if your paying help to complete a field, the AI should knock it out. Course play helps a little in that area, and there are helpful mods to speed up your working progress.

I could go on about my hits and misses in this game, but eh....pick yourself up a copy and kick them there tires. All the dust and mud you want to conjure up is in there! Farming Simulator 19 certainly kicks the llama's arse!\t\t\t\t\t
