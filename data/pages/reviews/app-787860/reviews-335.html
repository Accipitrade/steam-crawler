\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTo get this you really need to be into farming. Vehicle models are really good. Maps are atrocious, buggy as hell and minimal with graphics that leave a lot to be desired. Interactivity is minimal, very clunky and reminiscent of something from the 90's. I'm aware it's a sim but they really need to add something to enhance playability for gamers' who are not necessarily too into farming and would enjoy gaming elements rather than solely sim. The farming jobs are an attempt at that but in my opinion fall way short. Personally I can think of a hundred additions that could make much more playable and dare I say even exciting. It appears updates for this are a design of new vehicle models which to be fair are outstanding. However it seems a thin veneer is placed over existing mechanics and maps and they are just thrown out. One great thing this has going for it is its modability and the modding community for this game are second to none. They have stepped up and made a mediocre game good. As far as price is concerned, look at the store page DLC and so on see what you get for your money and judge for yourself. I myself think it is extortionate.\t\t\t\t\t
