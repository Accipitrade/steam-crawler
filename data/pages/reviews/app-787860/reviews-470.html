\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFirst, let's start with the good stuff.
The models (trucks, harvesters, etc.) are very realistic and beautiful.
You can have people work your fields while you do other stuff.

Now on to the bad stuff.
There's no way to have a worker drive your materials to the selling point. Neither can I send a worker to go pick up a newly purchased piece of equipment.
Doing these trips repeatedly back and forth is a tedious task to the point where it almost feels like a driving simulator instead. It adds no gameplay value. I'd rather spend my time farming.
I get that they want to implement a sense of real-time experience by making you go the distance but on the other hand they let you teleport to distant tractors and trucks instantly.

Time also doesn't make sense in this game. You can grow crops in a single day??
Phsyics aren't very good either. Trucks can turn and flip over like toys and some of your plows constantly flip over. Driving at full speed with a truck into a small tree, car or other obstacle causes you to come to an immediate stop without any damage to either object.

I remember playing SimFarm on DOS which was lots of fun. The top view & grid gave you a clear layout of your farm and spared you any tedious walking around. Selling was done with the click of a button without the need to drive ten miles to a dealer first.
FS 19 however feels very grindy in comparison because the costs of your equipment & materials + time spent driving is way too high considering the little profit you make.

The landscaping tool also leaves a lot to be desired so expect no precision work here.
The tutorial is way too short and I had to learn most by watching YouTube movies.
Your personal house, barn, garage are all useless and decorative only. They serve no purpose other than to waste your hard earned cash.

I saw the overwhelmingly positive reviews and had to see what all the fuss was about but I'm not impressed. If you love hardcore grinding you may enjoy this game.
Otherwise, stay away and spare yourself the trouble.\t\t\t\t\t
