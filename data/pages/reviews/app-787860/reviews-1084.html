I was a bit hesitant, is a game about farming really good, will it keep me interested?

A day or so before the release of FS19 I watched a playthrough on youtube on one of the channels I follow. It really got me excited and I decided why not, and pre ordered the game.
Haven't regretted it ever since.

I'm loving this, just running my small farm, doing the jobs it really helps to clear my mind.
The game isn't perfect, and those errors do make it frustrating sometimes (clearing my last field of trees so I could expand it was a real chore and not fun at all).
Luckily the core jobs all work smooth, sowing, ferillizing, harvesting, those al work great and doing that is really peacefull.

All in all, this is a great game and I'm very very happy I got it.\t\t\t\t\t
