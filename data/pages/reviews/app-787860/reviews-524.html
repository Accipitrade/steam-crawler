Farming Simulator
Farming Simulator isn't a simulator. It's an arcade game that has editable difficulties.
The editable options?
Lime, Field Plowing, Wilting. All concepts of farming that essentially make things take longer.
Non-editable things, however, include vehicle collisions, a lot of missing or even highly incorrect vehicle modifications.
Some useless features like trains that have been pushed into the game for some strange reason.

The Developers
GIANTS, they're well known for the series, and well known for their modding community.
However they're looked down on by the community for many good reasons. Through the years, modders have created hundreds of unique vehicles, maps, systems, and features. And dozens of those vehicles, features and systems have been taken, picked apart, and put into the next iteration of the game as a paid DLC with not so much as a mention to the original authors.

If you've read this far, I'm sure you've probably got your own counter arguments about legality and terms and conditions references. Honestly I'm not interested in all that.
I'm interested in a simulator. This developer has, for many years, put out 'Trailers' of illusionary tales of what you can do in Farming Simulator. Including vehicle details, animations, game engine features that are all teased but never fulfilled. Because every 'Trailer' is just a CGI render and never actual game footage.

I'd give the game a solid 4/10 for being excessively misleading, however due to the developer, I'd settle for a 3/10.

Not a 'Simulator' by any means, ideas and features ripped from the community of previous games, essentially just a product that promotes idea theft, and mediocre development.\t\t\t\t\t
