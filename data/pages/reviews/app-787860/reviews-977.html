Just you nature and the machines you own and run. You can't help but learn to admire the men and women out there that provide the food we eat after starting to see what's involved with farming in the modern age especially now with the Seasons Mod out for the Sim. The failed crops the seasons to deal with flucuating prices weather conditions and so much more. The plowing, cultivating, lime, fertalizing, bailing, seeding, feeding collecting and so much more.

And I see a lot more coming in the future to make it even more realistic. Time to forget your real life and loose yourself in a world few of us have a clue about but enjoy each and every day on our tables because of all their hard work! Come see what the farming community is all about by yourself or with others.

I am new to this franchise never owned or played any of the older versions of it before and I admit if it hadn't been for the YouTube video's I had watched that people posted I might never had considered ever buying this Simulation but now that I have I wish I had enjoyed it much sooner.

After a long days work with my machines I clean them off with the pressure washer making them nice again repair them for the next days work as the moon rises in the sky and the crickets start to cherp then head to the house go to sleep wake up at the crack of dawn ready to start another days work just like the farmers do in real life.

Work your fields or accept contracts on other peoples farms helping them with their work to earn extra money for new equipment or do both the choice is yours but have fun and enjoy growing something for a change of pace.
