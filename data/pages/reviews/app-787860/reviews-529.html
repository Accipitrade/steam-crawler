\r\n\t\t\t\t\t Why should you play Farming Simulator 19?

On the surface it seems silly to invest your hard earned money, on a game in which you don't shoot anything. It won't bring your latest "overpriced" PC build to its knees. FS19 probably isn't going to be featured on Gamers Nexus's latest benchmarks examining AMD's clear superiority over Intel.

However, this game is very worth your money. It's hard to explain, but it is extremely satisfying to operate farm machinery. Plowing and sowing fields, then watching crops grow. Try not to forget to fertilize,and spread lime. Maybe you'll have time to feed the pigs, or whatever livestock you choose.
The gameplay is extremely complex, the number of controls at your disposal is mind blasting. You'll have more controls to control than a flight sim with a bunch of controls. The modding community is solid as a rock. So you can improve on something that is nearly flawless to begin with.
In all seriousness, don't overlook this game. Or any from this series for that matter. FS is fun, engaging, and highly addictive. In my opinion, you really should play FS19!\t\t\t\t\t
