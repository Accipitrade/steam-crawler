\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a good game. 8/10 for me. Why just 8 points? Well...

- You are the only farmer on a map. There are no NPC vehicles doing farming like you despite there are dozens of fields which you can do contracts for. But you'll never see a NPC harvester doing farming so in the end the world feels somewhat lifeless.
- Why are there no real NPCs which you can interact with? The pedestrians you see have no collision so you can happily drive through them, nothing happens. You can't talk with them either so they're just here for decoration. Do a car crash, somebody calls the police? Hell no. No damage to your vehicle. Drive against a wall, same. Sucks. Why not doing a bit more GTA style??
- The AI helper which you can easily hire for fieldwork is a catastrophe. You basically MUST get the mod "Courseplay". Watch a basic tutorial (e.g. from "The FarmSim Guy"). In 30 minutes you'll get the hang on how to automate your fieldwork. Believe me, this mod is Godsent.
- Raising cattle / animals is very tedious. There is no AI help you can hire which means you have to provide food, water, sometimes straw/hay/manure every 1.5 days. The payoff is not bad but I hate the grind every other day. Wish the capacity in animal pens would be higher so you only have to provide food and water once a week.
- I recommend use the mods "Seasons" and "Precision Farming". They have some great features to make the game more realistic. Otherwise you can raise every crop everywhere despite of the climate and weather.
- Graphics look somewhat outdated but there is the new FS 22 coming up by the end of 2021.
- The mod base is big but it's tedious to download all the mods by the ingame mod manager. I have almost every mod installed, most are compatible but some not. You won't get any hint about incompatible mods. So called production mods which for example add factories with non-vanilla goods sometimes result in animal pens not available anymore on a map. Go in the shop (press "p"), try click animals pens, nothing happens. If this occurs delete every production mod and try again. Worked for me but sucks.\t\t\t\t\t
