\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverall a pretty fun game, but rough around the edges. Definitely recommend playing multiplayer with someone who has experience if you haven't played previous games.

The AI is by far my largest complaint. It can drive in a straight line and maybe turn around, but it has absolutely nothing beyond that; if two AI controlled tractors interfere with each other they simply stop and notify the player. Additionally, some of the larger sowers/planters/cultivators/etc will miss large parts of the field if your AI worker ventures anywhere near a boundary such as a road.

Other than that, it seems that running a dedicated multiplayer server requires having a second copy of the game (possibly only purchased from GIANTS website), which just makes me feel like I'm dealing with an old school software licensing company. However, you can still host from the game itself (non-dedicated) and there appears to be an option to rent dedicated servers.

Overall a tentative recommend. The price is pretty low for the amount of content, even if much of it is carried over from previous entries in the series, and the mod support really extended my enjoyment and desire to keep playing past the first 30 hours.
