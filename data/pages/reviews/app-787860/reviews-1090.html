\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'll keep this short, the game is amazing in all ways over FS17. I have no idea why people are complaining in some other reviews, but I've had nothing, but positive experiences.

Graphics - These are top notch and the improvements actually make you want to change to the in-tractor views now.

Gameplay - I like the change on steering to give some weight to it and responsiveness, it makes it more realistic. The approach to have to buy land instead of just buy a field is also a nicer approach. They also redesigned the menu systems and it made things much more intuitive with the new categories for the shop. Having Vehicles and Tools is so much easier now to find things than these huge lump categories before.

Bugs - I have only experienced two minor ones and both could be fixed on my end by simply restarting a harvester that got stuck trying to turn in a tight area and cultivating a small section mysef that was missed by the helper. The game came out two days and given that there have yet to be any showstopper issues, I'd say they have done a fantastic job here.

All in All, it is a great game and is definitely worth a buy. I will probably get close to 100 hours in no-time.\t\t\t\t\t
