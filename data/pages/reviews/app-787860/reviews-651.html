\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFS19 is inherently a good game, in almost all aspects. For one thing, the graphics and optimization are both fantastic, meaning that playing on lower-end systems and even laptops is a breeze. Of course, it's also available on console, if that's your thing. There is a wide variety of equipment for you to choose from in the base game alone, with much more content available from mods. Gameplay is a mixed bag, and which many people enjoy it, is is admittedly somewhat mundane by nature. However, FS19 does not seem to have the same problems with redundancy that plagues some of the earlier titles in the series, with a wide variety of crops to grow, as well as several options for animal husbandry, and even forestry. Overall, I would recomend the game, but I would also say that you shouldn't buy it unless you are sure that it is the sort of thing that you would enjoy.\t\t\t\t\t
