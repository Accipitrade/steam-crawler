\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have 14h of this game so far, while the concept is really good and could be fun, developers seem to enjoy making your life
difficult somehow. First of all the game lacks information by a lot, I understand part of the fun is to figure out how the machines work and how to use them, but I don't understand the point of not having any information at all and having to dig out everything from google. Secondly the game play I consider to be one of the worst experiences I have ever had in a game, most of game is driving around and handling machines, transporting stuff with trailers is just ridiculous, I see no point on getting stuck with the trailer on a postbox or a electric pole, or to loose visibility of the vehicle because trees are everywhere. Driving vehicles on the cockpit is unplayable and impossible to handle with a trailer, there wasn't any attention to gameplay when this was developed and no improvements after release either.
My main note is, could be a very fun game to play if they had put more time into making the gameplay better and also if devs had spent more time putting information available. Also the game is still quite buggy at this stage.

I wouldn't spend the money if I knew what I know now.\t\t\t\t\t
