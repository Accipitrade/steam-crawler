\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI do not reccomend this game , its an unfinished bug riddled game. This game has bugs that you find in alphas. Here are the negatives:
- I will start with the biggest negative for me, This game is soooo Grindy its not even funny i relate it to being as bad as MMO games. Even with having the game on easy, time at 60x and no withering its still extremely grindy. In my honest opinion they need to give you more money for your harvest, and make animals worth it like in F17 otherwise its not even worth having them.
-Workers are extremely buggy and must be babysat, they randomly stop working and just freeze in place. They also keep charging you wages even if stuck in place.
- The placeable double bunker silos are buggy, whatever material you put in it clips through the other side.
-Landscaping is a great feature but it doesnt work properly, you click on the option to lower the ground but it makes it go higher before going lower???!! like wtf. It would be great if they added an undo option , or even a flatten to level option.
-I love doing forestry sooooo much but the attachments that are used to pick up the logs do not grip properly, so you can only pick up 2-3 logs at a time making it a tedious task.
-Another extremely frustrating part about the game is the map, Like who tf made this map? I get nobody wants a completely flat map, but wtf did this take place in the mountains?? I feel like they developed this map but didnt even test it. Alot of vehicles bug out and stop working with workers due to the various elevation heights.
-When you purchase lands sometimes they with have a random house or decorations on them, But you cant remove them for whatever reason which can be annoying.

- It rains wayyyy to much, which can halt operations for a while add a feature to remove rain.

Maby the game can be more enjoyable playing with a group of people but as far as singleplayer i did not find enjoyment in this Farming simulator.\t\t\t\t\t
