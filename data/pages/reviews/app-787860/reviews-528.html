Trees looks bad booth close and especially from a distance. Rivers and mountains likewise. The whole environment is lifeless. Maybe it’s because of the old and unoptimized game engine, but that doesn’t help us customers. Animal animations; bad. No terrain deformation 2019; not ok.
Bugs. All games have bugs, we are all beta testers nowadays. But the difference here is that the developers do not listen to feedback (Remember: “Won’t fix”). A few things have been fixed, but it takes way too long. Hotfix anyone?
It’s too obvious they just release the games and do the bare minimum to fix issues. But e-sports (seriously?!) and DLC’s is very important for them. Sure, they are different teams/departments, but then you have allocated completely wrong. Hopefully we all have learned and won’t buy next game until its playable. I do my part and haven’t bought a single DLC this time.
This game could have been good, or at least acceptable. But lazy development took away the fun part. It has too many issues, bad graphics and horrible game optimization to be able to enjoy. It utilizes 1 core to 70 to 100% and the other 7 are almost idle. That screams terrible game engine.
Don’t want to be pessimistic, but I’m disappointed. And a little angry.\t\t\t\t\t
