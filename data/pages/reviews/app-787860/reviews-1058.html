\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFS19 in its current state simply isn't ready to be released. Yes, the graphics are stunning compared to the previous games, unfortunately that's the only good thing I can think of the game in its current state.

The physics are worse than ever, pallets either stick to the ground or flip in the air when you try to pick them up,
Logs are indredibly heavy for some reason, forcing you to use logging machines/forks to pick up the tiniest of logs,
The logs have the worst collision I've ever seen in the FS series ; they float when you put them on trailers, won't let you fully close the log fork when picking them up, they'll slide off the machinery at most random times,
The current mission system is so un-rewarding it's not worth doing 90% of the time,
AI is dumber than ever, won't finish half the jobs you start them up and give you "unkown reason" as an excuse to stop working,
Lots of reported bug/glitches with Controllers bindings,
The new placeable farm features is incredibly costly should you place something on the tiniest of slope, etc..

This game has a lot of potential and I'm sure will become one of the best Farming Sims out there, but in its current state (just released I know we should expect "some" bugs), there are way too many downsides for it to be enjoyable.\t\t\t\t\t
