\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Farming Simulator series of games continues to get better with each new version.

Having played since FS13 each version is more an evolution rather than a revolution.

While I think Giants does a pretty good job expanding and improving each new iteration of the game - but please understand it is the modding community makes this game worth buying and playing long-term.

If you like what this game has to offer - it's a no brainer recommendation from me. If you haven't played a farming simulator (or like simulator game) before watch some YouTube videos to get an idea what you are in for.

Plow, spread lime, cultivate, fertilize, sow, spray, weed, harvest, watch the crop prices, sell, buy new equipment - do over again...

Take care of your animals in between and plan your agricultural kingdom (or bankruptcy.)\t\t\t\t\t
