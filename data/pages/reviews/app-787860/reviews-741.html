Eventually you will hit some challenges or limitations with various tasks. Particularly collection of round bales. Mods will alleviate this greatly.

My favorite map mod is No man's land. - Why?

It's a map where you start bare bones, no contracts, no neighbors and untamed wilderness.

You even have to buy some of your sell points.

The game encourages you start with a farm. I decided sell everything and go all in on a timber company to start. Busy work but excellent Cash per hour.

Considering I climbed straight from timber to corn silage I'd say it worked pretty well.

There are a few ways to play this game that are very different. Explore and find what you like.

Highly recommended.\t\t\t\t\t
