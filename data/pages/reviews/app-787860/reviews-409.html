\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOkay maybe after 213 hours (at the time of this review) I should finally say that I like the game?

I enjoy this one. Particularly that the amount of work you put into a field is the reward you will get out of the field as well. Logging in this game is particularly interesting. I feel that they did a really good job with this game. However, the modding community is amazing, and there's all kinds of great mods that can enhance your game or refresh it if you're bored with the current stuff.

One major downside though is that DLC is prohibitive for players joining multiplayer. If a server is running the DLC and someone doesn't have it they can't play with their friends unless it is disabled. A much better option would be like what ARMA 3 or Payday 2 did, where you can still play together but if you don't own the DLC you simply can't use the equipment from that DLC.

TLDR: Great chill game, nothing tries to kill you, work in = reward out\t\t\t\t\t
