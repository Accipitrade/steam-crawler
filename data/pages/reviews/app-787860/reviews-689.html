\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI guess after 1,215 hours, I would recommend this game to other players. It is fun and addictive. Very customizable and offers good DLC addons of real industry equipment that don't break the bank. (Try that with FSX). There are thousands of mods to extend your gameplay; whether it is deeper immersion (Season or MaizePlus mods) or more fantasy (1 million liter harvesters or Wilson trailers. Maps large and small, with all the standard fruits, or 10 or more different fruits. Manufacturing and forestry mods also abound for a completely different direction.

There are other types of Farming sims out there and I own them all. They are each fun in their own way, but nothing comes close to a fun afternoon (or complete day and night) on the fantasy farm like Farm Simulator 19. I've been called a fanboy for my views. I guess I am a 53 year old fanboy with real farming experience, that really likes to get away and enjoy myself. But it is realistic enough that it does allow me to sample equipment that I might otherwise not try in the area of business I am in. That's just a small sweet plus. It's a firm buy for me. Happy farming!\t\t\t\t\t
