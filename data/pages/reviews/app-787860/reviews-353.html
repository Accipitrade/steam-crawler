\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL:DR
Yes, play it to kill time and to just enjoy a peaceful gaming session. You're not being forced into any part of the game.

Not going to lie, this was one of those games where I thought "How do people enjoy this?" when I first seen screen shots and gameplay. I still gave it a chance, and then I realized why...

It's a very relaxing game, can do things at your own pace. Easy to learn. If you're going to get this game, I'd say get it for leisure time as it will take a while to do things. There is a time skip feature that becomes available at 19:00 on the clock and is available for a few in-game hours after, but you're not required to use it if you don't want to.

The game also has mod support. Inside it has a built-in mod browser that is connected directly to the official modhub at the dev's main site for the game, and it supports mods that aren't on it. The only difference between the mods you'll find in the in-game browser and those from other sites is the devs have ran QA tests to ensure the mod is working without issues (Most of the time, mod conflicts can still happen) in the game for any found in the mod browser. If you decide to get any mods that are found on 3rd party websites, just keep in mind they might have issues with your game as it's a 'at your own risk' type of deal.

There is multiplayer so you can play with your friends, just keep in mind everyone needs the same mods if you play it modded and if you include any DLC then everyone needs the DLC being used as well.

The game is keyboard and mouse friendly, supports game pads and steering wheels (Sometimes they're a pain to set up though).

So play it? Yes I say give it a go with relaxation in mind.

Now given my first remark about how people find it fun, well you can say the same thing to me with train simulator games found in my game history. I just never had the 'how do people find this fun?' question for those, likely due to how early I got into them. The same principle applies, play it at your own pace for your own enjoyment and for relaxation.\t\t\t\t\t
