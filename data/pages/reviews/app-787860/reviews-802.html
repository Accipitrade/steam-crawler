\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are looking for the best farming sim on the market, look no further. What it may lack in graphics (mainly trees, but this might be a personal gripe) it makes up for with its versatility and community. Here are a few plus sides they might not tell you.

Modifications:
You name it, chances are, there's a mod for it- that is- if it's not already in the game. These mods include everything from machinery, to maps, and the game mechanics themselves. They're easy to install thanks to an accessible folder that comes DEFAULT with the game. There are plenty of websites to get mods on, and even a mod hub in the game itself.

Experience:
The Farming Simulator franchise has been around for a while. They know what they are doing. With many DLC for purchase, and the default vehicles and mechanics, you are getting a lot of bang for your buck.

Multiplayer:
Go out and run a farm with your friends! They make it easy and fun for you to do this, and there's always plenty of people online.

Joystick Compatibility:
Do you have 2 joysticks, a steering wheel, and a pedal that you want to use with this game? Use them all at the same time and customize all the controls with ease.

That's all I really have to say. I think it's a great game with plenty to do. As of writing this, I have 300 hrs. in this, and I haven't truly gotten bored once. Happy farming!\t\t\t\t\t
