\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBottom line up front: This is a terrible game.

Don't be fooled by the pretty cinematic trailer footage. That is not at all representative of the actual game.

With the recent release of FS22, having never played a Giants SW title before, I thought it might be informative to check out one of their earlier efforts before shelling out AAA title money on the new one. Picked this up cheap and gave it a go. Woooboy, what an eye opener. It gave me a good sense of the kind of things which could be expected from GS.

This game is so riddled with janky mechanics, glitches, bugs, nonsensical gameplay choices and utterly absurd control schemes... it's virtually unplayable. Having read and watched a number of playthroughs and reviews of FS22, it is apparent, to me a least, that GS has simply doubled down, re-skinned the same game, and carried over all the problems of FS19 into FS22.

Save your money, and save yourself a lot of aggravation. Skip this one AND FS22 as well.
