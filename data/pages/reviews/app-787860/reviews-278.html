\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWorst of the franchise so far.
I have left it till just before the next release to buy this, in the hopes that any bugs would have been ironed out...hahahaha NOPE!!
Have always played this on PS, first time playing through Steam, and had I of started with this version of the game I wouldn't have bothered with any of the others, and wont be bothering with any future releases either.

Helpers are unable to farm any sort of field. They used to only be able to farm square or rectangular fields, but alas this is no more. I have tried on both default maps, as well as "Woodshire" mod and they are unable to plant, fertilise or harvest ANY field without leaving half of the field unworked. Needless to say this makes running a farm impossible - yes you may be able to work all hours of the day and night, weather permitting, but the game becomes a chore continually switching between vehicles, and checking that your workers have done the tasks you have set them - Even Clarkson isn't as inept as the workers in this game! Any hopes of being able to farm all the fields at once are a pipe dream. You can't plant, fertilise, harvest, store produce, make bales, do weeding, look after the animals etc etc, without Helpers that can actually do their job without me having to run round behind them and essentially do their work myself.
I am completely astounded that this game has been around for years and this glaring issue has not been resolved. I guess it makes better sense to the devs to "forget the old and make more money with the new".
This franchise has a large following, but this is getting slowly eroded by the laziness of the devs and their continual refusal to make a game that works - the issue with Helpers not being able to farm properly has been in existance since they were introduced to the game (in 09 I think it was). Rather than make their product work, the devs would rather keep adding new things in to entice the unwary and count on the modding community to make the game worthwhile (and often to fix what the devs can't be bothered to).

Also, there are hidden unmoveable blocks dotted around the maps so when you try to resize a field, or create a field on "No Mans Land" these blocks not only prevent the land being worked - "Object blocking helper" but also causes issues just getting a vehicle near it - the vehicle suddenly wheel spins like its on ice and refuses to go anywhere near that block .

So what I have is a farming game where you cannot create fields and cannot work existing fields using any machinery...only thing I have managed to do successfully is chop down trees.....and anyone who has played any of the previous FS games will know that cutting down a tree is the easy bit, being able to load that log onto a truck is an entirely different matter.

I have tried to find workarounds for all the issues in this game (there are simply too many to list) and have realised that I am spending hours trying to make this game playable...when in reality its just another broken piece of software from a game developer who isn't interested in the gaming experience they are selling - they just want you to pay for something that doesn't work, was never intended to work, and hope that the Modding community will fix all the issues for free while they sit back, do nothing, and count the number of people they have duped into buying a faulty product.

I've just spent over 20 quid on something that is very broken.....and its the last of my money this company will be seeing.
This game should be renamed "Tree Cutting Simulator" or as the devs call it "The Cash Cow".\t\t\t\t\t
