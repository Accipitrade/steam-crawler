\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are into really realistic simulators, the VANILLA game may not be for you. There is a free mod called Seasons that makes the game play more realistic.

If you like "casual" simulators, you will probably like this game. Like the casual simulators, the game is relaxing, repetitive, and straight forward, but there is a lot to learn if you don't know the basics to farming. It is more than just harvesting fields, but also has logging and animal farming.

I will admit, out of the time I've played the game, only the first 20 hours or so was completely vanilla. After I had a better understanding of the game, I immediately added equipment and quality of life mods. If it was not for the Courseplay mod, I would not have nearly as many hours because the micromanaging of different AI units, and creating their routes is what makes the game enjoyable for me.

The Pros:
- Easy to learn
- Relaxing environment
- Plenty of equipment to try
- Plenty of crops to grow, animals to care for, and trees to cut down
- Strong mod community for equipment, quality of life, and maps

Cons:
- Heavy dependence on modded community for maps and content
- DLC is only beneficial if you want bug free equipment that will work perfectly with the game

I have not tried multi-player.\t\t\t\t\t
