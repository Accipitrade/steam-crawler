\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI've been playing since 13, and I was really excited about 19. It's... ok, I guess, but I was also very let down once I noticed the following:

-Graphics that DIDN'T improve. It looks exactly like 17 with brighter light sources and more contrast (This is with all graphic settings on maximum). People have mentioned there are better shadows as well, but I'm not seeing anything that looks better, or even "more realistic"

-Why do they (Giants) always remove vehicles and implements from the game? I feel like whatever was in the game in 13 should stay there in every future version while they simply add more. No one would complain about that. Furthermore, they revamped the store menu because there are now more types of implements. For example, you can now purchase 2 different small models of something called a "powered harrower". Cool! But it's really just a cultivator with a slightly different look. The same can be said for a few other implements. I find that very pointless, and it doesn't really add anything to the game when those implements only do 3-6 meters of work. The option of using different implements should INCREASE as you upgrade to bigger and better equipment. It seems like all of the choices are in the small implements, and you just keep making money until you get to that one best implement at the top of the price range.

-There doesn't seem to be any way to lock objects onto the ONE lowloader trailer. In 17, I thought it was ridiculous to only have two lowloader trailers, and now they've taken one away as well as take away the functionality to strap things down or just lock it to the trailer in general. I could be wrong about that, but I'm not seeing the option anywhere. The trailer in the game is the exact same trailer that was in 17, but somehow it doesn't work nearly as well. When you extend or retract it, the objects on the trailer do not follow with it. They just stay in place while the trailer slides underneath. If you have a load of pallets on the trailer and go around a corner at any speed, the pallets go flying and you're stuck cleaning up the mess Giants has created for you.

-They have removed functional placeable objects, like the windmill and greenhouses, in favor of adding non-functional placeable objects that look like garbage. Furthermore, the default maps have practically nowhere to put any of these objects.

I still recommend this game because of the mods that will be released over time. These are just a few of my biggest complaints. I know the modding community will always deliver the game we all really wanted. What really bugs me is that I feel like Giants has jumped onto the paid add-on bandwagon. They've given us a enough to have a semi-decent game, and they'll fix all of the bugs as they did in 17, but everything in the future will cost extra money. Just look at the add-ons for 17. This will be happening with 19 as well. Maybe they'll give us the placeable objects, but we'll have to pay extra for them. Maybe they'll give us bigger versions of the new equipment types, but we'll have to pay extra. I won't be doing that. I paid my fair $35 for their mediocre work, and now I'll sit back and wait until the modding community digs in and makes this game spectacular. They always do. Thumbs up for modders, thumbs down for Giants.\t\t\t\t\t
