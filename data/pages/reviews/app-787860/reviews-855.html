\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI keep trying hard to like it, I really do, but no matter how "modern" and "improved" the Farming Simulator iteration is, it keeps feeling a 10 year-old game to me.
Improved graphics ? Really ? wow, I am sure for the year 2000 these "improved" graphics would be terrific, in 2020 ...not really, no, not even close.

Same goes for the animations, interface and general mechanics, they just look totally amatorial to me, I don't know, I guess if it would be an indie game that would be great, but for a franchise that is on the market since forever instead, FS19 still has a very sloppy feeling.

The only detailed and professional level thing in the game are the machines and farming tools in general, which sure, are very important, since is a farming simulator, but if all the rest feels 10 years old, is like driving a Ferrari in a ghetto.

Besides, I had way more fun with FS17, it probably felt more user friendly, even if you are not a professional farmer I mean.
I will probably keep playing it, but I believe the franchise is far away to be triple A, probably a single A at the moment.\t\t\t\t\t
