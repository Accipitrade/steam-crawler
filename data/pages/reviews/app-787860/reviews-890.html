\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor every reason besides one this is the best game in the FS series. If you love farming or just messing around then this will be a fun game. Im not going to go into it very much because im sure others can explain it better. The biggest problem is lack of mods/community for this game. In FS17 there were new mods almost every week for the entire span of the game that continued to impress me. There was also a striving community, plenty of Youtubers were getting on and trying the game out and it just felt more like there was a community. Now that has mostly changed. It seems like almost no new gamechanging mods have been added at all in this game and its a year old. In 13 we had logging. In 15 we had Courseplay. In 17 we had Seasons. There has not been a mod that ive found that has seriously changed the game to where Giants might add it as a feature in future versions. There is also a problem with maps. This could be partly community, but there have been less than 3 maps that are brand new this year that i consider good enough to start a game on. The rest are all from previous games with small addons. I dont have a reason for this, but its very sad. This is one of my favorite games and i have to put up with terrible mods and nobody to talk about the game with. Wish we could go back to 13-15-17. Hopefully Giants can find some way to make 21 feel unique and exiting to get. Shoving more brands and better graphics in our face will not work and im sure they know that as there are still alot of people playing 17.\t\t\t\t\t
