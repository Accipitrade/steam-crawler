\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been playing Farming Simulator since 2013.
I find this series of games to be quite relaxing to play. The graphics of this game to be quite good and it does run smoothly. The detail levels on the tractors and implements does seem to be quite high (although I am not a farmer in real life so I can not say anything about the accuracy of them. The sound are quite good as well (again I don't know how accurate they are).

Unmodded there is not much of an improvement to the overall gameplay when compared to Farming Simulator 2017 with the exception of the horses and two extra crops, Oats and cotton. Oats are just the same (gameplay wise) as the other crops but cotton needs has a special harvester and trailer to move it around. But it is the mods that make this game much better than just the vanilla version.

Overall I think that this game has much more potential than the previous farming simulator games in the series.\t\t\t\t\t
