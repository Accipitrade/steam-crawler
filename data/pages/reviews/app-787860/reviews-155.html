\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWish there was a 'neutral' option to vote, because i both love and dislike this game.

The mods *really* carry this game's replay value. Which, i believe, is not a good thing. It's great they've embraced mods, but it's negatively impacted the base game and the content it has. And i fear this will be a trend in future games- not just those made by Giants software. Where there will be a reliance on mods made by players to 'fix' the game. Not that the game is horrible on it's own, just quite barren for a 2019 game. I mean, why would seasons not be implemented in a farming game by 2019? (modders thankfully made a seasons mod, but still)

Overall all i can really say is if you're gonna focus on animal farming, be wary about buying this game because it may disappoint you. If you're going to focus on crops or chopping trees, then i'd say it's a pretty good investment and you will likely be satisfied with it.

Although be warned that the wood farming can be quite difficult, especially if you're playing on a laptop or just don't use a mouse. Even then i think the logs have a tendency to fall out of your grasp randomly. Again, mods mostly fix these issues but people shouldn't have to fix a game they paid for.
