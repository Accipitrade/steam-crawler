I would love to see more hands on interaction like being able to pick things up with your hands, and more small time and vintage equipment. Giants should take a look at the Hof Bergmann mod map and take some notes that map has a ton of different hands on, and really cool features. I highly recommend this game and if you can try out the Hof Bergmann map if you are bored of just standard old FS19.\t\t\t\t\t
