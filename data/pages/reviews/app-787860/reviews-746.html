\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been a big fan of the series since I was a smol boi. Ten years ago, I first started playing the Farming Simulator 2009 in my old pc. I loved every moment of it, it was basic but fun. Then I played every other game that released after it. And now we have the FS19. Firstly, it is as fun and vast as the previous games. Refreshed graphics look good but might be a bit too much for the eyes, new equipment and crops, animals... horses are a great addition to the farming world. The ability to showcase the vehicle before buying is a vital addition in my opinion, since you actually see what you want to buy. Purchasing lands directly from the pause menu also makes the game easier and more mobile. The community mods such as Straw Harvest and Seasons are the cherries on top of the cake.

There are a few irritations in my opinion. There are not enough flat surfaces to place the animal pens, and for the people with severe OCD like me, that means hell. I mess up every time I place those pens. Also, I think Giants needs to move on, since the game engine and the game as a whole feels exactly the same as it did with FS2013, which I played the most.

Apart from that, nothing more to report so far. 8.5/10. Another great FS game, but time to move on.
