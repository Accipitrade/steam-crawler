\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was my first Farming Simulator game, so I can't say how it holds up compared to the rest of the Farming Simulator games. What I can say is that this is an okay game. It's very overpriced. If it wasn't $35, I might have recommended this as a fun time waster for people like me, causal players. I don't know how hardcore players who stress about having giant fields and tons of money can do it. I nearly throw my laptop across the room trying to harvest a small field because at least one vehicle gets stuck on the smallest incline.

There are a lot of problems with this game. First, the physics are terrible. I went into this knowing they weren't good, but didn't expect it to be that big of a deal. It is. It makes driving a pain in the butt, because there is a decent chance you'll end up getting one or more vehicles stuck somewhere. Although I've got to admit, driving my tractor off a cliff to get to my farm was the highlight of that save file. I took like no damage. And did this several times.

Another big problem is driving the vehicles. Without attachments its all fine and dandy. But the moment I added attachments, certain necessary maneuvers were a pain. Is it realistic? Yes. It is still annoying? Yes. For example, I had to back up against a barn to sell the hay bales I had. Took me like 30+ minutes. I would've left, but I'd already spent waaaaay too long getting the bales all packaged up.

There are also some annoying bugs. Or maybe the helper AI is just complete garbage. I've had them plow like half a field and say they're done. And yes, I had the money to pay them and there was nothing in their way. But when the helper AI does work, it's really useful.

Also, small note, the weeds are crap. I liked the idea, but in reality it was poorly implemented. I'd have fields at 0% weeds, turn around to go do something, and within minutes it was at 100%. I swear it goes from 0% to 100% randomly. The only time I saw it less than 100% and more than 0% was after spraying weed killer. A few spots were at like 1% or something. A little warning would be nice. Weeds grow fast, yes, but they still take time to grow, and you can see them growing before they're the size of your crops.\t\t\t\t\t
