\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWill you like this game? Beats me. If you like relaxing, strategy/time management games and get inspired by a seemingly endless array of machinery and other things to strive for, then you'll probably enjoy the game. But that's not why I'm bothering to write this review. I'm writing this review because the most remarkable thing about this game is how well it works. In a sea of games that struggle to provide what they promise, this game is amazing.

I have 68 mods activated, and the game takes less than 10 seconds to load and start. I have 6 fields, tons of equipment and placeables, co-op partners, half-a-dozen AI workers going at once, etc. and the game has never once dipped below the 60 FPS that I have it set at--and I'm playing on a laptop. I've had the game crash once and that was on a custom map-created by a modder-that conflicted with one of my mods.

Speaking of mods, the modding is incredible. You can install mods directly from the in-game menu, or you can go to their mod-hub website and download them to the mod folder. Super easy. There are so many great mods that it extends the playability by a considerable margin. Many mods are uploaded by the developers themselves, including bonus maps from previous versions of the game and many equipment packs. They could sell these as DLC, but they give them away for free.

I'm just very impressed. Big thumbs up.\t\t\t\t\t
