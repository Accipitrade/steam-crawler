\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's a funny little game.

It's not for everyone. Definitely has its limitations and quirks. I gave it a chance because of a good gamer friend wanted me to try it.

And after my first attempt at the game a long time ago, it left a bad impression on me, and the main reason for that was really because I got trapped into just working fields. And that just became extremely boring and tedious. And after requesting a refund they refused it and so I've been forced to keep this game.

So if you do find one thing boring and tedious like that, I'd say look at the mods. There are many mods that enrich the game and give it a lot of extra flavour and variety. The base game is very vanilla and bland you really need a lot of mods to bring the game to life

For a game that is supposed to be a simulation it is very technically lacking in many areas and many mechanics are just overly cumbersome and overly unrealistic. The games sometimes lacks the ability to cater to realism at all and turn it into a joke.

Going up and down fields for hours and hours at a time just isn't for everyone. I can do small to medium fields, but it has to be in between other activities. So consider that when playing this game.

The game looks nice, has really nice atmosphere, the night mode and lights are done very well compared to many other games. But at the core, it is a grinding experience. The game is very laid back though, so there is no rush.
This game is more of a place to hang out with people and work on your farms while keeping each other company. Or if you just love farming which some people just do. And even if you don't it can be very educational and enjoyable nevertheless.

Usually I would never get a game like this, because I know I won't be into it. But sometimes it's amazing what impact friends can have on you and what you will try for them. Love you guys.

Remember you only get two hours to refund, so if you try the game and change your mind, don't go over that. They will not make exceptions for you. And sadly two hours isn't enough to determine if you like the game, since you won't be able to really to do anything meaningful in that time. So make sure you research the game in depth elsewhere first.

---------------------------------------------

End of Review and beginning of my Rant about Steams Refund Policy and game devs complying with it:

They state 2 hours playtime within a 2 week period.

However I think this needs to change. I rather it was based more on game activity for certain games like this.

For example, how can you tell if you are going to enjoy a game or not in 2 hours in a farming game when in 2 hours you haven't even grown and harvested your first crop yet. I only truly realised how grindy it was, after doing my 3rd Harvest. And I realised it then. Now that is not something that can be accomplished in 2 hours. So it is a ridiculous policy that needs to be flexible with the game it pertains to.

I'm changing this review to a thumbs down just because of that. Had they been more understanding and reasonable, I would have been happy to give them a thumbs up, but stick it up your Acehole, this is the price of refusing me a reasonable refund. Guess that is the final end value of my purchase.

End of rant.\t\t\t\t\t
