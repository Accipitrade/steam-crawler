3) No more grabbing a stick and flying across the map :(
4) Feels like a well rounded game, but some of the pricing seems off esp for selling crops. This is a real world vs fictionary area thing though
5) Two maps? Really? There are SO MANY modded maps out there - I find it very hard to believe that you couldn't certify a few of them (and maybe pay the modder(s) for huge community approval? DLC anyone?
6) Very mixed feel on multiplayer. The synchronizing on server every time someone joins is horrific - why? As a computer tech I understand a need for a 'break in production' to bring in a new item into a live database, but why can we not smoothly transition like so many other games (read:ARK) The world there is almost the same size as ravenport with as much detail.
7) On the subject of servers. What the f. Why do I have to pay for an extra copy of the game (not to mention all the DLC I want to run) to run a dedicated server? I mean yes this is MUCH cheaper than renting from a worthless host and much more satisfying for experience. but WHY? Almost every other game out there gives me a FREE dedi app
8) Also on the subject of multiplayer - whawt the f is up with this freezing during play? Every 15-30 minutes. Yes I know there are save points - again as an IT person I know that databases deal with online saves that don't interrupt services. If this wasn't the case there would be 5-20 minute windows every 1-2 hours where hospitals would be offline. Not only that but games such as ARK and Minecraft have 0 issues despite huge maps and mods...
9) Oh! As a dedi server admin FIX THE UPDATE FOR THE DEDI APP. I don't need to download 4GB of data and run a patch every time when my client downloads 100mb and can update the client. Get your crap in a heap GIANT.

Overall - Great game, if you haven't hit another in the series I seriously recommend it. I would hugely say forget the DLC and go get the mods. As a community game for a streamer there are minimal issues. Check out the Modded maps. Have fun!\t\t\t\t\t
