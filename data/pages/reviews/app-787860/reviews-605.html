\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is by far my favorite single and multiplayer game. It's calming, challenging and exciting all in one. Love the new DLCs they come out with which makes game play that much more fun. Play with others or challenge yourself with different farming scenarios. Scenarios like building a farm up to 600 - 1000 cows without buying food or taking a logging map and building fields and farms from the wilderness.

The great thing is that if you don't see a tractor or mod that you like you can explore Giant Software Mod Hub: https://farming-simulator.com/mods. At their Mod Hub you can add new maps, tractors, equipment or game play scripts that allow you to modify the game to your choosing.

I play the game on three platforms: XBox, Mac OS and PC Windows 10 with each platform having their own nuances. XBox game play has the least ability to modify the game as you can not add Mods from the Mod Hub. However, its's the best way to experience the game as Giant Software designed it. Mac OS has great game play as well. Macs tend to crash however unless you lower your display setting, but you can still use the Mod Hub to customize your game. PC with NVIDA graphics is the best way to play if you want the most versatile game play available. I have a friend who has downloaded every game mod and uses the Mod Hub for his best game play. No matter how you play, you will have fun for hours setting up your ideal farm. I don't think there is another farming game out there that is this realistic AND fun to play.

If you want a real challenge, Giant Software has developed an editor (Giants Editor) for folks to design their own farming mods, (ie. tractors, implements, building and maps). Now that's a real challenge! Just started to work on this and will let you all know how that goes. :-)
