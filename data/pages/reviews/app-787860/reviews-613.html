\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI never cared much about farming before, but this game did get me interested. Not enough to consider doing it in real life, mind, but certainly enough to keep me entertained for quite some time. If you make it past the steep learning curve (the in-game tutorials and statistical informations are very bare-bones), you're in for a relaxing experience that nonetheless will keep you busy at all times, depending on what you're doing - agriculture, animal husbandry or forestry. Personally, I stick to working my fields and raising some horses on the side since these occupations are both the most profitable and the least maintenance-intensive by far.
The game is certainly not perfect (don't get me started on the incredibly dumb Helper AI), but most of its shortcomings can be worked around with a bit of practice and it is really pretty to look at, so in the end it provides an enjoyable experience that's always good for passing some time when I have nothing better to do.\t\t\t\t\t
