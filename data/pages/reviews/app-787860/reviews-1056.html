\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYa know, there is something in common for some or many (I`m not sure about number), or all German games. Or its simulation games only. The main thing - simulation, or core part of game, call it what you want, is done very well, but all secondary things - animation, optimisation, visuals, secondary missions, objectives are horrible. For example - Omsi 2, farming sims... They all missing quite significant parts.

And separately about farming sims - to play this alone without courseplay is hard, boring. And I`ve got nearly all games - 13, 15, 17 and 19. The red line goes through all of them - mods. Only because of mods these games still coming out, and I fear only because of mods they come in shape like they are - unfinished. And always we pay full price. With hope, something will be better this time. And yes, something gets better everytime. But just something. It`s like the path to the ultimate farming simulator - gets better with every new version. How many of these versions gonna be more? I dunno. And i feel pity for poor modders, who has their lives, jobs and all, but everytime they have rebuild their mods to be compatible with new version of game. What will happen when loyal modding community get tired of this? The farming sim will die or we will see the ultimate farming sim? The one we all been waiting for all these years... Hard to say...

Every new game with old illnesses - the help section is nearly useless, it don`t say what you can use to fight weeds for example. Well it`s obvious, you`d say - a weeder! Right. And what are my other options? Having some knowledge in real farming, i understand that using sprayer and herbicide also helps. Other topic - icons. Every new game, everytime a new guide appears with meaning of these icons. Say, does it really hard to add a line of text under the icon??? Will it kill the immersion or what else?

Giants releases only a barebone of game, admit that. With bugs and all. Everytime. Well, I don`t afraid call it a beta version. Modders and players hunting bugs drags it to "release". Though money drops into pockets of Giants only. It`s like DYI game - add your mods and play as you like.

Nevertheless all aforementioned, I`d recommend this version. On sale. Or after 6 months. When we have all needed mods and bugs fixed.

Ok, ok, besides all criticism, some good things. I like new graphic additions - tools loosing the paint; more detalised machines, tractors. But again, every FS game I tried it`s like player is in some sort of buble, where nice and cool textures inside, but low poly outside. And the border between both good and bad is so good visible! It was ok for games 5 years back, but today... when I look what breathtaking views are made with Unity... and this. Giants, it`s time to change. And community, it`s time to say we demand the true and only the ultimate farming sim. Enough to feed us with this unfinished "release" every few years. Enough to earn money on some enthusiasts free work you gladly take into game.

I like farming sim, I really do. But with every new game I play less and less. I`m afraid i will not buy the next one, if current develpment will stay as it is now.

Thanks for reading.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love the new game but I have found some issues, and I am confident Giants will sort it all out in time. Remember, it took time for FS17 to become what it is today and that is arguably the best farming simulator ever made. I think that FS19 will surpass the previous title in time. The mods, the maps, and all the scripting is what really opens these games up anyway.
The graphics are beautiful but need to be re shaded.. The colors are dry and should be more vibrant. Tractor physics are amazing! I love how the game plays, how they drive, and It really has met my expectations minus a few issues so far.

The issues I have had:

-Pallets just out of no where flying off the pallet forks into oblivion.
-Pallets of fertilizer just falling through the pallet forks all together when I tried to move them to tidy the yard.
-On perfectly square base game fields the helpers don't complete the field in its entirety. (Could be my error)
-The prices are really steep in this game. While I do like the new challenge in FS19, I do think it makes it harder to get into animal work early game, which is what I liked in the previous title.

Thank you Giants for another incredible game. I know you will iron out these issues with coming updates.\t\t\t\t\t
