\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me start off by stating that I am a diehard fan of this franchise. I started playing all the way back from FS 2008 and recently started playing FS19 coming off of FS17 last year; of which it became my top 3 most played game ever. With that said, I am quite disappointed with this installment.

There is nothing major or new for returning players of this franchise, if anything it feels like a nicer looking version of FS17 with a couple of tweaks & visuals. Having both FS17 & FS19 installed I literally feel no motivation to run FS19 when I can simply go back to putting in more time on my farm in FS17. That is how similar these 2 titles are to each other. It reminded me of Far Cry 3 & Far Cry 4, rinse & repeat strategy.

The only major change from the previous title is an improvement on the graphics. It is more realistic then ever before but but it is simply just not as good or different in any other aspect than its predecessor besides that one thing and that is simply just not a good enough change.

As one person pointed out perfectly; "... This game is Farming Simulator 17 repainted ..." and I could not agree more. The new players of this franchise will love this game, for all the returning customers and hardcore fans of the previous titles, you will be wasting your money on FS19!\t\t\t\t\t
