\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo at the time of writing, I have about 200hrs in the game. The game has been coming along nicely since release, especially from the modding community. I would like to say beginnig that this game relies heavily on mods to make it enjoyable (especially in replayability).

Animals: I love the changes they have made to animals, it really does make the decision making whether to be a completely arable farmer or a completely livestock farmer a harder one to make. It is arguably harder to get into, but that is part of the game experience in my opinion.

Land: I love that the plots of land are not just the fields anymore (like they were in FS17). It makes it more of an accurate look into farming. You don't just buy the land that you harvest from, you buy the surrounding fences/pieces of grass as well. Something that could be improved here is adding in a way to lease land, as I constantly hear farmers mentioning that they have a mixture of owned land and leased land it may be a bit more realistic... (the farmers I mention here are YouTube based so take that for what you will).

Machines: Now there isn't anything really ground breaking here, but as I said, this will be filled in by the modders as they continue to move forward over the next couple of years. Already a lot out there considering it has only been out for a short time and over the holiday period I would suggest that the modding community will continue to impress moving forward. (COME ON FOLLOW ME!!!!)

Overall, I love the game and I look forward to what GIANTS and the community holds looking further into 2019. At the $50 price tag, I would still recommend it to friends who find this sort of game entertaining.\t\t\t\t\t
