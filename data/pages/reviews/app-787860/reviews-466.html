I can't speak to what it feels like playing the game how it's supposed to be played, because my 115 hours (at this time) have been spent driving around in whatever vehicle i thought was cool on the mods website. I currently have 229 mods installed right now and I would say that 98% of them are working. It's super easy to mod the game, and that's perfect, because I am a complete moron.

It definitely takes some getting used to to try and control those vehicles, but after you're used to it, it's still hilariously weird vehicle handling.

That being said, I've owned farm sim 2013 for xbox, 2015 on pc and xbox, 2017 for xbox, and I'll probably end up owning most of the proceeding releases, because these games are so fun/funny to play, that it's worth it to me.\t\t\t\t\t
