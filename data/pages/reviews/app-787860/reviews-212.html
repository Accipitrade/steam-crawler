Let's start off with how buggy it gets:

America Map:
Have had to sell or get rid of leases of my car multiple times because the physics gird doesn't exist. This game is about farming and if the physics grid doesn't exist, there's no point in even playing this like a simulator. What does that mean you might ask...well, turn the wheel slightly to the left or right and your truck and trailer will flip at even the single digits speeds. Want to play something, well guess what, the planter doesn't stick to the ground at leveled angles forcing you to come in at different approach angles for the littlest patch just to plant something and to harvest it.

France map (I think it's in France):
Yeah, 30 hours in this save and the physics grid doesn't exist either. Not to mention, the game has issues even letting you play on this map as it acts like it's missing textures and/or files. More than just the physics grid, it barely runs. Had issues getting some machinery to work. So I saved the game and existed to reload the save. (This shouldn't be the work around every few hours because the game is so poorly optimized and they half *ssed creating the game). Guess what, my tractor is flying through the air and no mods will fix the issue. Can't run skinny tires on this map either because it has issues going in reverse. Same with the loader that has tracks. Barely playable map.

Europe country (I couldn't tell you where as it's 4 in the morning and I just don't give a sh*t at this point):
Yeah, game doesn't even want to load. So many more bugs on this map and no amount to try to verify files will fix this map.

I don't know how they can release the next game if this game is such a buggy pile of sh*t. You download mods to enhance the game, not fix it. These bugs are quite literally make the game unplayable.

What about non-bugs you might ask...Yeah, when the game works there are a few issues that just scream, why. Why can you lease a vehicle, but not be able to buy it for keeps? Why does the physics grid not exist for a simulator? Why is there a cap to how much you can borrow from the bank? Why are the DLC's add nothing of value except for different companies and most price vehicles from the base game? Why can't you sell land you no longer need? Why are the AI complete morons that can't even plow the field correctly? Why have the ability to have caution lights when only the AI can use it when they have an object in front of them? Why can't they figure out how to get past obstacles? Why are the AI cars tougher than your vehicles and why do the AI cars deal so much damage to yours when they hit you?

There's so many questions to this game that you shouldn't even have to ask. If this was an indie game that was in "early access" like a some that have been in for nearly a decade, you can understand. But This is a sequel to a game that has come very highly of fans of simulators/calming games. They are the EA sports games of simulators. But instead of being on their 50th game, they have 2 in the series with another on the way and they are producing half *ssed sequels as if they just gave up.
