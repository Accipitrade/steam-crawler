\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wrote a review earlier and I am adjusting it.
Previously, I wrote that I couldn't wait to play it on a gaming pc because I was convinced it would look better. It....doesn't really. It simply has more contrast and subtle details actually change. This game isn't as good as the last edition now that I've slammed some time into it but I still play it because I'm a disabled veteran that has plenty of time on her hands.

Let's add some stuff:
-There needs to be more character customization. Like....WAY more. The options we have are sad to say the least.
-Graphics need an overhaul. The plants need more realistic movement and way less choppiness.
-The tutorial needs a complete revamp. For brand new players it literally only covers 20% of the game mechanics and all the things you can do in this game.

I would recommend this for someone who likes to pass the time, already knows the franchise and doesn't mind a bit of a graphical rip off. I wouldn't recommend this to newbs or people who love graphic detail or people who hate watching grass grow.\t\t\t\t\t
