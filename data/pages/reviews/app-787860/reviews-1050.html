\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFor fans of the FS series, I do not recommend this game.
Many of the new features are buggy, poorly thought out or very shallow.

The core of this game remains almost identical to the previous release, yet somehow they managed to introduce enough bugs or bad design decisions to make this game extremely frustrating to try to play. Read through the Giants forum to get an idea before you buy.
Issues include:
  • Terrible support for controllers (doesn't support more than 4 input devices, resets controls constantly or totally disables them - the game removed the hire worker control for me so I was unable to hire a worker at all)
  • Poorly balanced or broken mission system, cannot complete some missions
  • Multiplayer control axes reversed from single player
  • They have added a delay with throttle inputs, making any work with a forklift or telehandler difficult
  • The placables system is basically unchanged from FS17 but is now compulsory for working with animals. This would be fine except that there are virtually no places on the maps where you can place the buildings without creating a ridiculous ugly ramp up to the edge of the building because there is no flat ground. And don't bother trying to delete existing buildings because nothing can be built in the space that they once occupied

Graphics look nice up close, but most of the models in the game are the same as FS17 with slightly improved textures. Improved foliage effects only served to make the LOD pop-in more ugly by comparison. Lighting is definitely an improvement, the menus are better and I like the new lime/fertilisation system.



Same engine as FS17.
Same models as FS17.
Same textures as FS17.
All new bugs.

Stick with FS17.\t\t\t\t\t
