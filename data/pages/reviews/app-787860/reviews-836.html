The single player aspect is what I love about the game with the occasional multiplayer experience. That being said, this is where the game is lacking heavily.

Without fundamental AI improvements, and in Single player being able to become a "farm manager" of sorts, its basically worthless to try and have a farm of more than 5 or so fields to work. It becomes IMPOSSIBLE to manage a farm of any large scale due to a crushing AI experience. The workers do function better in 19 than any other series, however to have the ability to park them in a shed and say 'Go to field 19 and do...x, y, z" would seriously enhance the single player experience.

Until this is done, this will be my last Farming Simulator game. It becomes extremely tedious and redundant.

Multiplayer is hit and miss, which can be a blast with the right people. However there are a lot of trolls in the game and they just try to break the farms. Or you feel like you're doing all sorts of work for someone else.

Overall Im just done with the game until the single player experience can become manageable.\t\t\t\t\t
