\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFarming Simulator 19 is a great game, to me. I love it but many probably wont due to the learning curve and the up front "hassle" it is to play when you know nothing about farming. There is no tutorial. Yes, there's a basic how to but it doesn't go nearly deep enough. Do not pick this game up blind with the intention of chilling, (until you learn how to play) because it will fail and you will hate this game. A lot of the mistakes you make can take you a literal play session (1-2hrs) to fix up.

With that out of the way, the graphics are fantastic (with reshade), though I dislike the lighting. I cannot get dark areas in bright daylight to stop appearing pitch black until I walk into the area. So so so many mods to choose from, there will never be a lack of content thanks to community modders. The tractors and vehicles sound great.

If you are ready to suffer for the greater good of learning farming, then play this game.\t\t\t\t\t
