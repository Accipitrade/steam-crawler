\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTL;DR at the bottom.

FS19 is the follow-up to the smash hit FS17. This time around, there are some new crops, a couple new maps, and, what feels like, less equipment. Would I recommend this game? it depends on what kind of player you are. Let me explain.

I think if you're new to the FS franchise or skipped 17, you should pick this up. The starting maps will give you tons of playtime.

But if you are a returning FS17 player (like me, over 300 hours) then it isn't that simple. It mainly depends on how you played 17. If you spent tons of hours on the stock maps and using stock equipment, then this game will most likely be fine for you.

Now if you played less than 20 hours on each stock and modded map on 17, I can't recommend this for you. Currently, as of May 2019, there are only a handful of really good modded maps for the game and not very much equipment. Though you can find more of each on other websites besides GiANT's own mod hoster, the good mods are few and far between.

TL;DR: If you're new to FS or loved playing the stock FS17 maps, get it. If you only played mod maps from 17, hold off until a sale or more mods are released. Overall, it's a solid game.\t\t\t\t\t
