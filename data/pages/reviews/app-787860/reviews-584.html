\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is my go to, no need to fire up my brain, just sit back and do repetitive tasks until I am just purely calm... SMR if you will game. Kinda obvious as as of this writing I have over 1300 hours into it (I have a stressful job). It isn't challenging in any way, relatively easy, not super realistic in a lot of ways (though there are mods out there if you want the realism), the tractors are gorgeous, some of the mod tractors are more so. Really that sums up this game. You come for the relaxation and enjoyment of completing a task and earning something for that task, you end up staying to try all the different mods and and mod maps for the different views they bring.

Make no mistake, if it weren't for modders this game would not have near the popularity or play time it does. So I would like to thank the modders for keeping the game fresh and enjoyable for years!\t\t\t\t\t
