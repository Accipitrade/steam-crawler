\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, let me just start off with that the developers should have called this Garden Simulator, not farm simulator. The reason being is that the plots you work are so small that it is not worth the time to get all of those fancy and large vehicles and tools. The economy of the game leaves something to be desired as you will not make much from your harvest at all. The developers also have left this game in a state where everything pretty much gets in your way of getting your work done. Trees and trashy useless buildings on your farm that serve no purpose what so ever other than crappy decorations that you cannot remove. You can sell some buildings off, but some can never be removed and if you try to use the terrain modifier, it just turns into a mess so bad that you can't use your own farm afterwards. Speaking of terrain editor, you cannot expand your farming plots or join them together. You are confined to these small plots. The roads that you will have to travel are NOT made from farming vehicles. They are too narrow and full of obstacles that some equipment, you cannot get it to your farm. So here is my suggestion for both buyers and developers. For the developers, maybe just maybe you should make a sandbox version where the player can create their own farm how they want it and with plenty of space to be a real farm. As for the buyers, I would wait for this game to go on sale before purchasing.\t\t\t\t\t
