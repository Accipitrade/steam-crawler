Overview
FS19 is a farming simulator game. With a focus on machinery and various forms of farming. Crops, silage, animals etc.

Story
This game does not have a story.

Gameplay
This is a sandbox game with no real "goals". FS19 puts you in the seat of a farmer and sort of expects you to know some things already. If you know nothing about farming at all, this game may be hard to start out with. But soon, you will learn. Even though it says "simulator" in the title I would not call it a simulator. But it's close enough

The gameplay is undoubtably very fun and addictive. The game is relaxing.

Additionally you have the option of co-op and multiplayer, both of which are great with friends. I however stuck to singleplayer. Mods such as courseplay allow you to simulate several works on the farm.

Graphics/Performance
The graphics are okay. Nothing spectacular. The game's engine is outdated and somewhat unoptimized. Having lots of machinery can cause FPS hits. You can improve the graphics with some reshade, and having a good PC helps.

Community
50/50. Games like this don't always attract the smartest people at time. A lot of people just see a tractor and think "wow big machine me like me buy". The forums and the subreddit are filled with low quality posts. The mods are plentiful and I really mean PLENTIFUL. There is an insane amount of maps, machinery and crop types. You can go crazy with it. Unfortunately a lot of mods are also with errors which overtime can cause crashes or FPS drops. It's important to not download crappy mods and stick to quality. The modhub is very limited in mods so using third party sites is necessary if on PC.

Additional info
This game is essentially a type of sandbox, there is no 'right way' to play or any real ending. You make of it what you will. Small farms are possible and anything else too.

Conclusion
For people interested in agriculture/simulators I highly recommend this. It is also a great game to relax with and even learn something new. If you're interested in the process of farming this game can give you a rough idea of how modern day farming goes about. Personally I enjoyed the game very much.\t\t\t\t\t
