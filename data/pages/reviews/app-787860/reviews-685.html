\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI love this game. Especially with all the mods for it.

My only complaint is the game is to euro. It kind of ruins the immersion of playing as a US farmer when I can't pronounce the names of half of my equipment. "Oh yeah.. I love driving rasabahabadubahdah!" Said the Farmer in total confusion.

Not slagging euro products but there is a lot of missing gear in this game. A lot more than I would expect from the 2019 version. Where is Kubota? Mahindir? Yes I know they aren't American but both are very common in the US.

There are a lot of great mods that add in more gear in this game. But it's a real chore to download the 900 or so different mods needed to make the game feel authentic. I wish they came as a pack. On the plus side downloading and installing mods is probably easier in this game than in any other I have played. Even if you use the mods that don't install through the game client. Just drag a zip file into your documents/mygames/fs2019 folder and boom. All done!

This is a great name and this franchise really needs to keep going. Hopefully somebody in the modding community will figyre out how to pack 10-20 mods into one big download.

P.s. Whoever made the f350 powerstroke mod, THANK YOU!\t\t\t\t\t
