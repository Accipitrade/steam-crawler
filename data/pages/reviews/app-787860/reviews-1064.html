\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell my initial review of this game was negative, but after a couple of updates and a small number of mods, I can safely say that there is hope on the horizon.

The main issues were the building placement system being just straight up broken and the economy being disastrously unbalanced.

The update that introduces terrain editing makes placing buildings in an aesthetically pleasing fashion much more doable now, and a mod that allows buildings to be placed anywhere makes positioning items much less of a headache and less cost to construct.

The economy still needs a tweaking in my opinion, as its far too much of a grind to be able to afford high-end equipment. Also that awful dog is just crazy buggy, and I'm not sure I like the idea of making all the animals a placeable-only thing. (Makes for a really ugly farmyard.) Hopefully the introduction of some mod maps will solve this as GIANTS are notoriously bad at map design.

Overall its a major graphical overhaul from FS17, and with the mods slowly trickling in, this game will probably reach the heights that previous titles did before. It just may be a couple months before that happens.

I'd recommend checking this out after you've finished squeezing the last bit of play out of FS17 as that game still represents better value with the mods available.\t\t\t\t\t
