\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo, I'll finally write a review for my most played game of all time, after almost 3 years.

I'll start with the pros and cons:
Pros:
+Alot of replayability, with new mods coming out all the time. As FS22 comes out later in November though I suspect that might stop.
+Amazing graphics. Machinery and tools are just beautiful and attention to detail in this game is amazing.
+An actually peaceful multiplayer, no toxic kids in sight (this might depend on what server you're on though)
+You can actually play this game on a crappy PC; however you can't play on max settings but that surprised me either way.
+If you take an interest in tractors you'll enjoy this game.

Cons:
-This game is very dependent on mods. which can be safely downloaded from the in-game modhub. But, they require alot of space, so you actually need to have quite much space free for this game, if you don't want to be limited by the in game items.
-Ping on multiplayer servers is very noticable, so driving is not as easy as you'd hope.
-While the general graphics are great, some things are just not top tier quality, such as crops, imo. Things like that drive me insane some times.

+/- Unlike most driving games, this game might actually be more difficult playing on controller.

What made me play this game so much?
Mods. As soon as I actually got tired of playing on a world, I'd download new mods and play on a new map (might explain the 100+ gigabytes I have spent on mods :P). I strongly recommend Old Stream Farm btw.

Final thoughts:
I've had so much fun playing this game. It's so peaceful and I play it sometimes while talking to friends, just in the background. With FS22 coming out soon, I don't know if you should buy this game, however, I have no doubt that that game will be just as great as this has been.

I hope this review helped anyone, enjoy your day/night y'all!\t\t\t\t\t
