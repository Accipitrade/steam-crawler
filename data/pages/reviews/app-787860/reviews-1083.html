The graphics are a small upgrade from Farming Simulator 17, especially with the revamped lighting engine. The wide variety of content in the game makes it very fun to play and explore. While many people are saying that it is just a reskin of FS17 with more tractors, the game is truly a step up once you play it for over 10 hours. If you have the time and money for this game, I would high advise you to try it out with an open mind.\t\t\t\t\t

For those of us coming from 17-there's nothing impressive about this game. Now I don’t hate it. I’ll be playing it all the time. The graphics are great. The ability to buy land and start farming how YOU want to is a great idea and hopefully they expand on it and refine it. They added lime and weeds which are both cool additions to say the least. JD is in the game but then it really has been all the while if you’ve been playing on PC (thanks to all the hard working modders that make farm sim great). So, really not a huge addition. The menu for buying and customizing equipment is cool if you just want to take a look at the machines. Farm Sim is a solid title but they need more.

Now to what bothers me.
Farm sim is great because of mods. How do they go from the library of mods in 17 to nothing or pretty much nothing? I get the game just came out but once again we are at this idea of incomplete games launching and that’s just bothersome. Maybe they expect the community to do all the work while they sit back and collect…….
Helpers are useless in fields that are not square! I don’t think there has been a single improvement since they were first introduced.
How are things like courseplay and gps not STANDARD in this game.
Where are my seasons? Yeah, sure I get they; be coming down the line but why are they not here now.
Can we get some new terrain physics, please?
So, it hails and my crops are completely fine still?
Once its done raining I can just hop right back in the field and keep at it?
--Expand on these things and give us morrrreee--
Some of these American maps they dream up are silly. Ravenport is a farm that’s inside the Grand Canyon (being a little over the top but I mean it) that’s inside the town. So, you want me to either drive up this cliff to get to all the nice big fields or roll through the middle of town.
There’s more but I’m starting to get a little annoyed at it all and I am late for dinner so ta ta…

I’d like to say thanks again to all the modders that make this game great and keep it alive.

New to farm sim-7/10
Coming from farm sim 17-3/10
Maybe this review will change in the future. Maybe.
Lastly, this is literally my first game review ever on steam so I have some passion.
