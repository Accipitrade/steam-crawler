\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI don't know why this game isn't as interesting as FS17. Everything is better in this game, but all the improvements are only graphical and if there are other things that are new, their excecution is just stupid or done extremely lazy. Best example is how the weed is handled. They will slowly take over the whole map, starting from the top right. They arrive at the same time, and you will know when you need to get rid of them. That makes it extremely boring feature.

Logging and sawing is not what it is in real life. Giants should really take a deep look at this feature, because players have asked so much more than what Giants have ever delivered. Basically you still just got the log and then sell it. That is boring!

Giants have never been able to create good maps, but these vanilla maps especially lacks the spirit. They should hire a real artist that can create realistic maps. You can do some landscaping, but it costs a massive amounts of money and the new feature of placeable farms are also executed poorly. I was excited when I first heard about that feature, but now I want the old farms back.

When FS17 got 10/10 from me, this game gets only 7/10 and I think the main reason for that is the lack of immersion.

Mods will save this game, but keep in mind that I'm only reviewing vanilla version of the game.\t\t\t\t\t
