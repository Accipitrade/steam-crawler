\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe mods turn this into a game for all eternity.

The game play is smooth, the engine is well developed, the little in-game physics quirks are forgivable and the sounds, graphics and environment allow you to immerse yourself fully into your virtual farm.

The base game is great. For a sim fan, the ability to make meaningful decisions at every level allow you to shape and own your ingame progression fully. Decide what you sow, what you buy, what you and when you sell and live with the consequences and reap the rewards.

What really, and truly, and absolutely elevates this game to immense heights, however, are the modders.

I play with somewhere around 200-300 active mods. 20-30 high quality modded maps, over 200 custom machines and implements from various generations and brands, all freely available on modhub or a variety of fan sites. Mods are stable, well developed and add immense value and longevity to this game.

Seasons, courseplay, Global company and over a dozen key mods that elevate gameplay to new levels and new dimensions.

If you look at my hour count you may get an idea of how immersed one can get in this game. It is the ultimate escapism experience. After the a long day at work, after the kids are in bed, the virtual farm is where I relax. All the relaxation, no pressure, zero frustration.\t\t\t\t\t

Statement

The game is really good but can be alittle bit glitchy at times. First of all when this game says it has MacOS support it means it, my cousin has an old MacBook Air, and this game on pretty damn good graphics runs fine. This game can be really fun when you're playing it with friends or even multiplayer servers. The only thing that I think is pretty annoying is that a lot of multiplayer servers use DLC's, yes some are cheap but not everyone can have the DLC's.

Graphics

This game has truly beautiful graphics which can almost run on any low end device. The graphics have a pretty standard AAA game graphics which is nice to see on an older type of game. Some things don't look as great tho... I find myself that the star admitted from some crops like soybeans, oat, etc, could of had more detail in the texture.

Content

This game has some good starting stuff with out DLC's, there are tons of vehicles and trailers and other items that all come free in the standard game that require no DLC's. This game could receive more updates but I do understand that the people at Giants Software are trying to release fs21.

Customization

I mean nothing in this game is really customizable except for your character and the landscaping. This game offers few choices for your character., for landscaping its basically a tool that lets you change the land throughout the map.

Rating

I would give this game a solid 8/10 just because that to access more content its hid behind a paywall and many multiplayer servers use DLC's. One thing that also is annoying is that you can't change graphic settings in game, and there are occasionally weird save slot glitches when you use them for multiplayer.
