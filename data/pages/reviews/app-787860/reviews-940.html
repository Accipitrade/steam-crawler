Little bugs that are not game changers but i can tell time was not put into detail (harvesters unloading into trailer and it shows harvest going through the trailer), those kind of bugs and lack of detail. Despite my little issues this game has me hooked! I have not stopped playing this game since I bought it!

I am excited to see more additions to this game in the future. Curious why a watering system was not considered as part of a farm simulation? Irrigation up to small straight line wheel watering units and later upgrading to the big round field units, seems like a no brainer to me. More home choices. Picking a contract and then selecting machinery you have to use and lease item you don't. Lease to own equipment. Natural disasters and seasons. Predators of livestock. I want to farm, not simulate equipment (though it is still fun). Farming simulator 20 could be pretty dang awesome if more of the "Farming" is implemented. Add more fields.
