\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhat draws me to this game again and again is that it's immersive work. I makes you appreciate the hard and complex work that is farming. Now, I play on the hardest settings and I'm still pretty sure this isn't even 1% of how hard it is to be a farmer, but I appreciate the challenge this game gives me.
It has a great variety of equipment I had never even heard of before. And each piece has its own properties and quirks I need to get used to and I find this variety fascinating. They add new equipment in each DLC.
But the game doesn't have to be all this hard - you can set it up to be very forgiving for a simple and calm farming experience to just chill out. For a calm evening, just driving a tractor and listening to a good radio (Simulator Radio is awesome, btw), I would *highly* recommend this game.
The gameplay is solid, still has a few small kinks to iron out, but enhanced greatly with mods. There are simple quality-of-life mods (like precise angles or more information), as well as grand mods with new vehicles and even entire maps when you've beaten the original two.\t\t\t\t\t
