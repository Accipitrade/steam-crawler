\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI had mixed emotions when the game first came out. There was a lot of things about the game that I really liked, but at the same time, some I didnt like. I have a list of some of those things.
Likes.
1) The graphics.
The graphics in this game were a huge step up from FS 17. And while having great graphics, the game is also fairly optimized. My Ryzen 5 and RTX 2070 ran this game beautifully, and I didnt think my other pc with an i3 4150 and gtx 1050 ti could match that. But to my surprise, the game ran perfectly at the same settings! So lets move onto the next thing.
2) In game brands.
There are many tractor, harvester, equipment, etc brands in the game. They have alot of really good brands when it comes to just about everything. From John Deer, to Massey Ferguson, you can choose what you want and how you want it. Though, it would be nice to have some vehicle brands aswell, like Ford or Chevy but those are just very small details.
3) Crops and their physics.
The physics for the crops are pretty good. If you accidentally drive over your crops the bend, but dont always fall. With small vehicles they pop back up, but if you are using a Semi or heavy tractor, you better be careful!

Dislikes.
1) Animal physics.
It was disappointing seeing the animal physics once the game actually released. In the game commercial they made having a dog, or riding a horse seem like something fun to add to the game. In the end, it was not. Riding a horse is clunky, and not very enjoyable. As for the dog, it just kinda snaps in the direction it needs to go and you cant do anything asides from give it food and throw the ball. No getting it in the truck like in the commercial. Livestock could also use some new physics. In the end, I believe they could hire someone else to deal with those physics.
2) AI.
The AI can be annoying depending on how you drive or what you are doing. It is very annoying when something falls off a trailer in the road and the AI keep getting as close as they can so you cannot move it or load it back on, or when you have a wide load that gets sideswiped and knocked off by an AI. Small detail, but depending on what you are doing they can be a real heartache.
I could write a lot more about the game, but it would take to long for me to write, and you to read. In the end I recommend the game as it can be quite addicting.\t\t\t\t\t
