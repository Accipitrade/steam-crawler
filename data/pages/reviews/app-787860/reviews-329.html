\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere are so many things wrong it's not even funny. For example, the US map has European road markings and the world is full of European (mostly Volkswagen) compact cars. I know this has been developed by an European (German?) developer but come on, really?

Also, all the income generating facilities like bee houses or solar panels that were available in previous versions have been removed, and with it to make more realistic use of your land.

There's other stuff like the steering behavior (if you thought it was poor on previous versions of Farming Simulator you'll probably be as surprised as I am that they found a way to make it even worse). And we still get bugs like grass and bushes coming protruding into the cabin when driving over them, or the creepy and awkward acting NPCs with their repetitive behavior, all which is pretty embarrassing in a game that came out in 2019.

Of course there also are several improvements, such as the graphics (which looks much nicer than on previous games) and the level of detail they put into the Austrian setting, the horseback riding and other stuff, but all in all it's not enough to compensate for the shortcomings over the 2017 version.

I recommend to get the 2017 version instead.\t\t\t\t\t
