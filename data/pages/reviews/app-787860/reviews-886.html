This game is weirdly clunky to start, the tutorial is weirdly confusing and not very informative. And yet, once you put an hour or so into the game, starting to figure things out by yourself, this game then becomes weirdly addictive.

I used to be a fan of the farm sim 14, but I've then dropped the series for other games. Now with the farm sim 19, 5 years later, I've grew strange to the controls and many of the concepts of the game, but once I got through the rookie stage, I have to give this game 8/10 (and if you're a fan of the series, then 10/10).

Also, the multiplayer is just amazing. Lag never seem to be an issue, everything is so smooth, and it would be perfect if you have a mate to play the co-op with you, you can just chill and chat and bond with your mate, and just plough through the day (lol), you would never want to go back to single player.\t\t\t\t\t
