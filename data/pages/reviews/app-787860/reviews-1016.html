\r\n\t\t\t\t\tMilktoast. Playing since 13 and this is the worst yet. I am a mapmaker and have 1000's of hours on 17.

This game was born on PC and now the inevitable shift has happened. That shift is when a PC game gets big, supported by community mods that make it what it is, the dev's switch focus to console where the money is at. This has unfortunately happened to FS. I am bored beyond belief. The new graphics are lackluster and cater to console performance. The draw distance is pathetic. Many objects do not render until you're about 50m away and there's no option to change this even with a high end PC. On release the economy was broken along with the outrageous cost to buy land. Some fixes have come but still finding it very uninteresting. I can't explain the graphics but they just look washed out. The equipment is bright with clean lines and no real definition looking out of place on the bland backdrop. Like everything is toy and made of plastic. All I can say is that to me it certainly looks different than 17, but not better at all.

The default maps are catered to the Euro farmers. Small, clustered, bendy, obstructed curvy layouts with tiny fields. There is an uninspiring amount of equipment for purchase. Nothing new and exciting as far as I can tell. Was excited to see Mahindra but the only vehicle there is, is a stupid UTV. Mahindra makes tractors but there aren't any in the DLC.....

Once the modding community fixes this lame duck I'm sure it will be fun, but for now I find it hard to talk myself into playing it. To each his own, this was a huge disappointment for me,\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWhile there have been gameplay improvements from FS17 to FS19 (inclusion of John Deere, land ownership, terraforming), and a graphical overhaul, I cannot recommend this game.

The game was released with bugs, some of which were quite annoying, and while they have made efforts to fix their broken release, there are still issues. Several well-known and respected modders within the community have left (their reasons being their own), and the handful of quality modders remaining refuse to work with Giants (their reasons being their own).

The maps are...a very real representation of Giants' opinion of those they deem to be lesser beings. Ravenport (the US map) and Estancia Lapacho (the South American map) are both covered in trash and generally look like run-down ghettos, while Felsbrunn (the SUPREME German map), is well-kept and beautiful - a model of neatness and good housekeeping.

This is insulting on a level that I cannot simply overlook. Coupled with the lack of attention to the community, I cannot recommend this game, nor any other Giants title at this time. I do hope that they will learn from their mistakes, but my business with Giants is done.\t\t\t\t\t
