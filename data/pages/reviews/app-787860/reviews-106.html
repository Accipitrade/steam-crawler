Without the mod Courseplay I wouldn't recommend this game at all. The built in AI helpers are useless.

The game only comes with two maps and Ravenport is garbage. Every mod map I've tried has sucked, either the fields are right next to rocks/rivers/fences so AI helpers can't work them, or the layouts are so bad they're tedious beyond belief or both. Which means the only map worth using is the second map that comes with the game - Felsbrunn.

Even to this day the game is pretty buggy, in my time playing I've seen some comical bugs which I'm sure will never be fixed as the company has moved on to Farming Simulator 22, which from what I gather is just Farming Simulator 19 with some mods built into the game.

There's no goals, no skill trees, no purpose beyond that which you create for yourself.

The character creator is laughable, there are next to no customisation options. E.g. under the drop down "Accessories" you can choose to wear sunglasses, or not wear sunglasses! Amazing!

Why the recommendation then? Well it's kind of relaxing to just watch your tractors go up and down ploughing, cultivating, seeding, fertilising and ultimately harvesting. I'd definitely recommend waiting till it's on sale heavily discounted though.\t\t\t\t\t
