\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm coming from playing several FS games on consoles in the past. This is the first time I've played a PC version of FS.

FS19 is very fun but compared to previous FS games I've played in the past, this one is by far the slowest. I've played this game for a few months on Medium difficulty and the most I've gotten done is buy one new field and upgraded my combine. I set it to easy recently and you make much more money, but it's still very slow. I just spent four hours doing contract work (the fastest way to make money early in the game) and made about $30k. The next goal I have is buying a field that costs $220k, which will let me grow about 25% more crops at a time. So yeah, this game is SLOW, although I think you can play with friends online, which would be a massive help. (One reason it's so slow is that the fields are ENORMOUS!)

(Protip: the people giving out contracts only expect about 85% of their contract to be completed and they let you keep the last 15% if you harvest it!)

I am nowhere near buying a barn for animals, so I have no idea if the horses are any fun to raise.

Another thing I would like to point out is that, if you've played previous FS games, FS19 does not have the gold coin Easter eggs in it, which is a real shame since it was my favorite part of the earlier games--it gave you incentive to explore the map.
