\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI wrote a review one year ago. Game was to raw for that time, I noticed some bugs for example "the harvester New Holland CR 10.90 stuck for example on the field number 12 on the map Felsbrunn, because it's header falls too low and clings to the soil and the harvester can not go". Developers fixed this one, one "+" to them. So, I was playing this game for one year and noticed some bugs more.
1. I know that it is "optimization" but shadows behave really wierd (I'll leave some screenshots).
2. Not a bug but it's really annoying, NPC cars... They're stony and very primitive, without them every map looks abandoned but when they are "on" they can really be a interference, they stop only when they almost touch you, you can't move them coz they are stony. I really want smart NPCs will be in FS21 to they can give you pass through, to they are not stony (like in ETS2 you can move them when you hit them but pay for it a fine), one "-" to devs.
3. Next disadvantage it's glitchy forestry process, when you load logs with manipulator, they (logs) can suddenly fall in epilepsy seizure (I'll leave link to video in youtube to prove my words), in FS17 everything was OK with forestry process.
4. Next issue it's shimmering elements of controls of technical equipment, this issue exist in FS17 for example in "Stara Imperador 3.0" arrows on joystick shimmer and one tiny element shimmers in FS19 too, it didn't in FS17, second "-" to devs. What more that besides arrows on controls mirrors in some tractors shimmer too now in FS19 (I'll leave a link to video). But now let's speak about good side of this game. FS19 much better than FS17, graphics improved, gameplay improved, physics improved with some issues described above but improved anyway. I really love FS games, I just constructively pointed out the shortcomings. So steam doesn't have "neutral" review button so I'll recommend this game if you can close your eyes to this little issues. But if you can't live without good graphics and you love to find fault with little details and everything should be almost perfect I won't recommend this game.
