\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have owned every Farming Simulator since 2011 till now. The Focus has continued to make what I would consider to be small upgrades but overall the game has not changed. They have added animals and new equipment which add to the overall gameplay experience.
This version has been even better than the last visually. Everything is shiny and the power washer is used very often to keep the equipment looking that way.
Along with that, the vehicles have been more stable. I have only tipped over the $400k sprayer a couple times to the point I couldnâ€™t put it back on the wheels. I do warn you though, if you reset the vehicles, sprayer, planter, ect., you will lose all the seed or fertilizer you had in there. I normally use a tractor and tip it back over to save the $9k worth of fertilizer or seed inside.
The Land editing tool I do like. It does cost a lot to use if you are flattening a large area. It will create better land to cultivate and harvest or make flat space to build an animal pen. I will warn you though, you have to remove any trees from the land and stumps as well. If you leave any piece of tree behind the Land edit tool will not work and leave spires of land where you were leveling.
The grind is what I would consider more massive then any of the previous versions. Cultivate, add lime, plant, fertilize, weed, fertilize againâ€¦ Ooftaâ€¦ Just for one or two loads of grain that gets sold at a small price. I would challenge to say real farming is easier. Even if you shut off everything and have the game on easy setting, making a good amount of money will take hours to accomplish. Iâ€™m 40hrs in on easy, and finally own my own large seeder, harvester, tractor x2, sprayer, and 4 larger fields. Hitting the 10 million achievement is going to take forever.
Would I recommend this? Yes of course I would. I am a simulation guy and this is one of these games that should be played if only for the experience.
