\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSo I had some issues with accessibility with this game, but when I tried to get a refund to my Steam wallet, I was denied because I had it longer than the return policy allowed. I spent most of the time not actually playing, but looking for ways to improve my experience or how to do a simple task. This was disappointing since I really just wanted to exchange it for something that was a little easier with my motor-skill level. I also find it hard that Steam wouldn't even refund when it was more about a physical limitation. About the game itself - it isn't great for beginners and is very difficult to get into and get started. In future versions, I'd like to see more accessibility and an enhanced tutorial mode. Honestly, with those issues, this game took all the fun out of the whole experience. Kinda felt more like a job, and less like a game - but go for it, if you're into that kind of thing. In the meantime, it will just be sitting unplayed in my library.\t\t\t\t\t
