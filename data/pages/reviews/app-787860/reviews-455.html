\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHonestly my favourite game of all time. So much to do! Here's my review.

Firstly, if you see the dlc's and think "Oh no! i should buy these or i wont have anything good in game!" wrong! While the dlcs are great (Personally the platinum i think is best) There are Hundreds if not thousands of free mods to be found on the ingame modhub or on other sites! Many of which just as good, if not better than the dlc packs! So when the game says 300+ vehicles there are hundreds more for free! A lot of them are big improvements from the base game models.

The game isn't too difficult to get started either, and there's WAY more to do than just harvesting and ploughing, it's really easy to spend hours and hours on the game.

You might think a game such as this required a beast computer right? wrong! I'm running on a lenovo Yoga with an AMD A9 processor. NOT made for gaming, yet i can still enjoy the game just as well! you will be surprised how well low performance computers can run the game.

Definitely worth your money and you will not regret playing it! You will see just how cool and entertaining agriculture really is. You may even be interested in going into real agriculture just by playing this game!

PLEASE Don't listen to people who say that it's unrealistic, or boring. You NEED to try for yourself and experience it. Guy on youtube called Sim Farmer inspired me to get the game with his "No Man's Land" series. Watch some of that and maybe you will take to the game better!
