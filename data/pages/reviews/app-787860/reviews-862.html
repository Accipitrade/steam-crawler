\r\n\t\t\t\t\tsaw this game on facebook being played by others and was intriqued; however, upon acquiring the game myself I wish I hadnt. I truly enjoy the graphics -- that's the good part. The bad part is it seems to be overly complex and if the regular player type person (me) does not know the ins/outs of the game ... totally lost. The tutorial ... hmmm does not help in finding other items necessary--the controls are too complicated (constantly pecking at the keybord) or pressing this button and that button on the controller and having to really study/concentrate taking away the actual "fun" of the game. I know, to others they just might fall right into the scheme of the operation of the game and enjoy the complexity--I do not.
There is no clear cut guide to show the layman where and what there is to do! The game is frustrating and it should not be.\t\t\t\t\t
