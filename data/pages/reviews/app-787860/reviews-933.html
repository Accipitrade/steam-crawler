\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI have been playing the Farming Simulator franchise since their 2009 release. For 10 years, their development team grows bigger and stronger. From listening to fans and upgrading game physics/graphics, they continue to better themselves each release. Each new game, offers new gameplay and better improvements worth paying the small one time fee. Honestly, the DLC packs are not entirely worth it unless you specifically are wanting material in that pack. Reason why I say that because the modding community is so large, you can get a massive piece of content from an independent creator for free. Which brings me to modding. It cannot be easier to download and install mods for this game. I do wish there was workshop support, but I feel that would become over saturated quick. There are thousands of websites out there with hundreds of mods, and each site only contains 30%-60% of the same mods that another site may have. This is a great game that I would recommend to anyone, who wants to experience the farming life.\t\t\t\t\t
