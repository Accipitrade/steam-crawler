\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFarming Simulator 19 is in many ways an upgrade from 17, and there's plenty I like about it, but there's many times while playing 19 that I miss things about 17.

CONS;

The maps in 19 are horrible. I've played both maps for many hours and I can never get used to them. They are plain, boring, small, and overall not fun to play.

Parts of the maps are unavailable with larger tractors because of how poorly they are designed. The selling locations often have very tight corners with random junk in the way making getting into the locations (such as the ports) a huge pain. Some of them even have entrances but no exits, requiring you to back your semi truck back out of the selling locations. They are just overall very bad design.

Some tractors in 17 are missing from 19 for seemingly no reason? You can get a Case IH Steiger with both wheels or tracks in 17, but in 19 you can only get a Steiger with tracks. There is NO OPTION for a Case IH Steiger with wheels..

Physics are wonky. I often run into invisible objects that make my vehicle glitch out and sometimes fling across the map.

UI in my opinion is worse. The white UI disappears when against the sunny sky, and you need to move the camera angle down into the dirt to see what time it is or how much money you have.

Giants have released many official "mods" on the Mod Hub that are great, but it can get annoying if you want to play with your friends and you have to download the same tractors. I don't understand why the official "mods" from Giants are not added directly to the game via an update. That way everyone can easily play with all of the tractors without having to download them all individually.

PROS;

  • Store UI is amazing. Being able to see the model and customize it before you buy it is incredible.
  • Graphics are much better and look less plastic than in previous games.
  • More crops and animals adds more to the game.
  • John Deere being official is great.

The game is good. Luckily mods can fix most issues and they are available directly from the modhub in the game. Hopefully Farming Simulator 21 improves on these issues and doesn't require mods to make the game more fun.\t\t\t\t\t
