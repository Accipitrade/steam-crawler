Let me explain. I like it but cant reccomend it because it simply would not be a good value for most people. Which really concerns me about FS22.

It is alot more like a hands on tycoon game then just a hop on a tractor and drive it around.

Calling this game "simulator" is drawing an incredibly fine line, Its too basic to be a simulator and too realistic to be a casual. Although the way it is, it is casual by nature.

It lacks consequences, you cant really go bankrupt. Nothing gets repossesed if you cant make the payment and it lacks direction on top of that. There is no goal you just set your own goal, which is fine sort of.

It has no in game instructions at all whatsoever, if you went to a equipment dealer and you asked if the equipment would fit on your tractor etc they would tell you before purchase, they make the lack of information so bad that it puts you out of the immersion. And they didnt want to program any text I imagine due to having to translate it, so everything is an icon which is incredibly annoying when new and even after having 200 hours in, at least at a "hover" tool tip its just lazy not to this is basic HTML stuff.

Bugs are so prevelant, I have no issues calling this game totally "incomplete". because it is, its lazy and unacceptable for anyone that paid full price. I got it for 20$ and only because I sold a csgo skin for like 150$ so basically free. I found at least 10 major bugs within 200 hours some are game breaking and found within 30 hours.
1. ghoast tree limbs broke logging entirely you learn to not cut a specific branch from a specific tree type.
2. lack of low loaders or say like a car trailer to move your tractors around. watch them catipult into infinity if you try a regular flat trailer.
3. need to buy or lease a forklift just to leave it at the equipment store to load stuff.
4. AI is really bad
5. logging forks are broken
6. landscaping does not work and can have session runing or entire career runing consequences.
7. Cannot unload a partial filled baler, so if you want to make straw bales and you have grass in there welp youll have to have a quantity of 2 100,000$ balers just because of that.
8. you can purchase weird swaths of land that have no benefit.
9. there is a finance variable called "propery income" that has zero vanilla use.
10. some equipment simply does not work right.
11. O yea cant clear bushes but you can chop down trees
12. O yea cant get rid of some buildings and decorations on property you bought. Like idk you bought land with a farm and a factory(factory does nothing in game) and you cant demolish it to put a chicken pen etc.

Lack of content, the game is virtually unplayable or idiotic to play without certain mods. One is a simple pointer telling you what your looking at, another is a simple mod that adds utility trailers. Otherwise you would need some dark web information hidden deep within the code to find out that you need to customize a particular trailer by taking it to a garage to "customize" it into a flatbed, o and it works like ♥♥♥♥.

It is astoundingly addicting, you wouldnt think so but it is. I just wish they completed the game to begin with. You NEED to WANT to like this game to keep playing it. I feel like after 500 hours youll do everything you felt like doing and it might get shelved. My goal is to reach the 10 million$ bank acount as I assume thats the only realy way to "win/beat" the game.\t\t\t\t\t
