The focus on leaders is one of those changes that you kind of don't expect to come but the depth it adds is welcome nonetheless. Leaders now get you emotionally invested more into who you hire and can also create some wacky specialisations that scratch the 'how far can I really take this?' itch you get. The one gripe I have with this new system is there's a lot more pausing and pondering that can take away from game pacing but I think it is a fair trade-off.

Paragons are really cool and they always improve your ethics too. A gripe with this is that if you get them early you can dominate everyone... Especially the admiral.

Armies are less of a pain to manage now, better science ship AI so you can turn on and forget (+1 because this means you shift more focus onto leaders now with less pause time lost).

The new UI is pretty great but I am personally more of a fan of being able to have maximal information on one screen so there are less tabs BUT it still is a reduction in needless clicking.

Also added was capital planet specialisations that are missing a few types but great to add to narrative about the ethics of your empire.

Another crucial note about this DLC is that this DLC will enrich ALL of your other DLCs which is what I have been wanting for a while now. You are rewarding people for being a repeat customer and I highly suggest that custodians continue down this path.

10/10 - Minor gripes are not enough to effect the rating. Please continue down this path. Buy this DLC to enrich all other DLCs.
