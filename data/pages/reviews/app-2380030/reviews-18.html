\r\n\t\t\t\t\tTLDR; DLC is good, except for the Origin, which is no longer bugged, but still questionably designed.

Overall this DLC is a great addition. The new leader mechanics are something that I've been waiting for, for a long time, and they work amazingly well.

I was also very excited about the new Origin. As others have said, it's a cool way to roleplay a "God-Emperor of Mankind" sort of character. Rushing for the Chosen One on your starting leader is really fun, so this Origin is perfect for that, right?

No. Your original leader doesn't unlock the Psychic trait. Everyone else does, but not the "powerful legendary leader."
The leader must have the Psychic trait in order to get the Chosen One trait, which excludes this "powerful" starting leader.

As of hotfix 3.8.2, the leader now gets the Psychic trait.

However if you pick imperial authority after the 40 year mark, the road ahead is full of events meant to throttle you. In its defense, it does warn you that there will be consequences to choosing the Imperial authority, but... why? You don't start out as a democratic government, you start out as a dictatorship. I've had less strife playing a fanatically egalitarian UNE and swapping it to an imperial cult devoted to a Chosen One, than I've had trying to pave the road to it with this Origin.

The Origin bombards you with negative events and traits, that to the unknowing might seem to be an interesting challenge, but to someone's who's tried it and played it, it just feels like you're being punished for not playing the Origin the right way.

Get this DLC for the powerful, interesting and fun leaders that you can discover - not for the Origin.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPlain and simple, this was an update that simply wasn't needed in this game. Stellaris already holds the title for most complex 4X game in existence, now it's trying to be an entirely new game. At first I liked the idea of slightly more control over leaders, but the changes are DRAMATIC to the point where once again it feels like an entirely new paradigm has been forced on us. We JUST got used to the changes from the last DLC, to change things this dramatically to the point where our save games are basically not compatible is....silly, to say the least.

The leader cap is WAY too low. And shouldn't even exist.

ADDENDUM: a much better system for handling leaders and a "cap" would be to have a cap on leaders with a certain type of perk; call it an "ascension" perk giving you "ascended" leaders or maybe even turn them into Paragon-types. So you'd still have unlimited normal leaders - you know, which makes sense, you idiots - but you'd have a cap on ultra powerful leaders with special bonuses.

Envoys didn't get touched, so one of the biggest remaining ACTUAL gaps in the game STILL hasn't been filled even though this would have been the DLC to do it...

The new council system is horrible. Councilors should not count against the leader cap. This is a NO-BRAINER, folks, a no-brainer.

The level up system is boring, un-inspired, and missing a TON of content. The available traits get boring and redundant after a handful of hours.

And still no new cities or shipsets? How hard is it to give us some more variety with the damn city pictures??? Ridiculous that it's still the same base 9.

AND WHAT THE ACTUAL F**K IS THIS "RETIRES" EVENT ARE YOU F*CKING KIDDING ME?! You create a DLC focused entirely on leaders then introduce a mechanic that makes investing in them pointless.....are y'all for real?

Paradox definitely missed the mark here, and in a big way. For the first time EVER I am rolling back to an old version via the launcher and ignoring the new content until some fixes have been made.

UPDATE:
I've sunk a few dozen more hours into the game. The new changes in the DLC just keep getting worse and worse. The majority of traits available are "+3 to x resource" and guess what? After year 10 those are totally worthless.

This is a HUGE miss from Paradox. Huge. I am absolutely infuriated I cannot return this DLC and go back to a previous game version. Totally missed the mark, DO NOT BUY THIS!!

DO NOT BUY THIS DLC, YOU WILL REGRET IT!!!\t\t\t\t\t
