\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSuperbly put together route. Really scenic and captures what I assume is the feel of the area nicely. Even though I have a low spec PC (lower than the min requirements to be honest), I still get a decent frame rate on default settings.

Included rolling is very well modelled, although the loco horn sounds seem a little muted.

Interesting to see a portion of the Inside Gateway route included too.

Included scenarios are very enjoyable. Would like to see a few more, but thats par for the course with current TS add-ons.

One notable criticism. I feel potential buyers should be aware that the route runs from Oroville to Quincy Junction (including the Quincy Railroad). The section of the route from Quincy to Chilcoot Tunnel is not included. I do feel that this is an important omission, as it means that features such as Williams Loop, Spring Garden Tunnel, Clio Trestle, Portola Yard and the approach to Beckworth Pass itself are not included. Personally, I woud have preferred to pay a little more (and probably lose the section of the Inside Gateway) in order to have these included.

Don't let this put you off though, its still an amazing route. I just hope they consider a 'part 2' route heading further east! (to Reno perhaps???)\t\t\t\t\t
