\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSimple looking old style adventure game with cliched horror plot and just OK pixel art graphics. There are some clever puzzles now and then, but the gameplay is overall poorly designed. Even thought it is a horror story with some disturbing imagens, the cute-looking character avatars make the game somewhat less scary, and the cheap jump scares don`t quite manage to change that. The playable character is controlled by using the joystick or the keyboard WASD keys, but this style of direct control actually adds nothing to the gameplay. In fact, the inventory and mechanic puzzles would look better in a point and click mouse driven adventure game. The joystick controls only manage to add tiresome backtracking and the ocasional irritating quick time events. The ending of the story unfortunately feels pretty rushed and lacks real conclusion. This is a regular looking game that could have been better.\t\t\t\t\t

at first i was having a good time but then you started to meet the friends of Saki (the female lead) and they are empty.
the only things told to you are you're all friends....and 4 of the 5 are going to get married...no hobbies,dreams or anything to actually make them interesting. saki is the best character not only getting the most screen time but changed over the course of the story. still...there isn't anything to her as the prior still applies to her.

oh and the steam achievements don't work. and i despise the complete the game without saving/ complete a second time. i DO NOT want to play this more than once!

OVERALL
Sigh,apparently this story will have a sequel at some point which will explain things not done here.
this game did do some things really well, but where it fell short. is too much for me to give this a positive rating. perhaps next time will be different.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tRe:Turn - One Way Trip is a good Horror, Adventure game. Okay what is the story.

Saki and her four friends are on a camping trip, a last hurrah before they have to enter the adult world after college.
But after a stupid argument everything goes to hell, her friends gone. But as Saki looks for them she discovers an old abandoned train, what Horrors will she find? All aboard for Pure Terror next stop evil.

So what is there to like about the game? The pixel art style/graphics is very good; it's pretty detailed and nice to look at from the old broken old train. To the new train.

Those picture cutscenes like when we first arrived at the train and see it for the first time are very good and nice to look at.
The levels in the game were good to see and to explore as you go from car to car of this decrepit old train. The atmosphere in the game was good and spooky as you explore the train and seeing the detail they put into it.

The game isn't scary per se but there were times where I jumped or some parts that were definitely uncomfortable.
The music and sound effects were very good and it helped with the game's atmosphere. Like that one scene with the pregnant skeleton, those sounds when Saki is cutting its tummy it's just so gross and uncomfortable.

The story's pretty good it had some good, sad, creepy and some Sweet Moments in it. Though there are some plot holes/unanswered questions. But I heard that a sequel is going to be made that's going to fill them up.

The characters in the game were interesting and cool; I really liked Saki especially that one moment where she's had enough of the trains . And lastly the puzzles were pretty damn good they were hard but fair, except two puzzles.

So what is there to dislike about the game? Well as you know if you go into a new room and you're still holding the walk button your character continues to walk or run. But in here it doesn't if you're still holding the walk button your character won't move you have to let go and then touch it again to let your character walk, that was just really annoying.

Now that I have said all that, Re:Turn - One Way Trip is a good Horror, Adventure game. With very good pixel art style/graphics, a pretty good story despite some plot holes and a good spooky atmosphere.

If you're looking for a pixel horror game with an interesting story and very good art this could be the game you're looking for.\t\t\t\t\t
