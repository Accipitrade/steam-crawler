\r\n\t\t\t\t\tAEW Fight Forever is a fun, arcade style wrestling game that has a good ideas which if built on could be great.

I've played through what Fight Forever has to offer and I've had fun doing so, I'd recommend watching some clips of the gameplay to see if you like the style, as the enjoyment does come from the style being a little different to the WWE 2K games series. However there is more that AEW has to offer.

AEW has standard exhibition modes, singles, tag, along with barbed wire death match, lights out matches, falls count anywhere, and casino battle royale. The match types are quite limited honestly, handicap matches do exist in the Road To Elite mode, but you can't play it outside of the mode unfortunately. The matches are a little too quick due to the pin system being a weak point. It's a button mash but you can't see how far you are from reaching the kick out point, usually you can't kick out of a finisher, if you do, you'll be pinned off of the next move anyway.

The gameplay is really easy to get into, hard to master reversals, but very fun to experience, especially the lights out matches due to the massive variety of weapons such as thumbtacks which make for some really insane spots. Along with the standard exhibition matches or speciality matches, you have the Road To Elite mode, this is the career mode which has "blocks" where you enter different scenarios based on match outcomes and character choices. This is very much like No Mercy on the N64, that's what they're going for.

The RTE is the main mode of the game as it's the only other mode besides the exhibition and the few mini-games you start with. There is an achievement for playing through the mode with 10 different wrestlers but by the 4th or 5th run you've likely done everything unless you haven't followed a specific story path. There is some fun dialogue and interactions here, it doesn't take itself seriously and gets some laughs, if you play Orange Cassidy for example he'll have some unique dialogue which was fun but it's also inconsistent.

There are some perks in the game that are OP, and if you want to play it online you can't turn off the CAW (created wrestlers) so you'll likely bump into spamming, but if you leave nobody wins so there is that, you can simply leave which is a problem in ranked obviously. But I don't think in this games state that a "ranked" mode works, it relies on exploiting and it's just CAW's.

The music library is good, it's massive. It's just odd that we have some new music and some very outdated entrance music, that might be a licencing thing.

Roster is good, but could do with more people, Uno & Reynolds for example should be on the roster.

Overall, fun gameplay but very limited on the good ideas the game does have. You can't help but feel like "that would be good if they added more" to a lot of the content in the game. You can't really edit the actual wrestlers attires which is really lacking. Some wrestlers have multiple attires but you wouldn't know because you have to actually go into "edit" on the wrestlers, create a new attire, and select it for it to show.

If you have multiple attires and you wanted to switch through them in RTE mode, if you go into "edit wrestler" you actually can't change the attire, despite it showing you all your attires and it showing that you can, this just seemed like an oversight on the main story mode. It feels like the game itself just wasn't finished, despite it having a lot of time to finish after all the delays.

I recommend based on gameplay if you're into the style, worth getting on sale. They are bringing out a Stadium Stampede online mode which looks very fun in theory, but they really need to keep updating this game to get it to where it needs to be.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is great if you have friends to play with, I hook up a few USB cables and on my PC I play a lot of match types with my brothers and it's a blast. The roster all plays almost identical to how they wrestle irl, certain wrestlers can't do certain attacks, some wrestlers get what's called buffs for doing certain things, they just nailed it with the fighting. It feels arcade-y but so fun, easy to learn the controls but after about 20 hrs in I got really good and can beat Elite difficulty AI most of the time.

CAW kind of sucks, movesets for CAW are great but customization in general is lacking. RTE is very VERY boring. Some people say they love it, I hate it, I only play it to grind my CAW's stats which btw I wish there was a different and less confusing way to do that. The roster altho I love it, it is still too small for a game made in 2023. They're missing like 10 MUST HAVE wrestlers and a handful of more. Match types need to be fixed a bit, you can't customize who you play against in Casino Battle Royal, you can't pick which number you get, nothing, which is a shame bc I have a lot of fun playing the Casino Battle Royal.

Again this game is awesome and worth it if you're playing it with friends. If you don't have anyone to play with I would wait until it goes on sale + wait until they add updates but sadly it looks like they're going to be pretty slow with those.\t\t\t\t\t
