\r\n\t\t\t\t\tUPDATED AFTER 13+ HOURS OF GAMEPLAY:

DON'T BELIEVE THE WWE FANBOY/TROLLS DOWN-VOTING THIS GAME.

IF YOU'RE ALREADY FAMILIAR WITH AEW: Here's your confirmation bias! This game is for you, or should I say US (because I am a definite AEW fan). You can remake your favorite matches, or create matches you WISHED had happened. The characters and arenas are obviously from the 2019-2021 pandemic era, so we get a heavier Jericho, we still have Cody Rhodes and William Regal, and we can even play as the late, great Brodie Lee. You will love this game, the mini games, and the "Road to the Elite" campaign. If you're an AEW fan, you are a fan of great in-ring wrestling. That's what this game is about - fun, over the top, wrestling, with a deceivingly deep and re-playable story mode (more on that below). You can play multi-player vs players all around the world, but across platforms at the time of review,

FOR THE REST OF US: This is a fun, old school wrestling game, like WWF No Mercy, Def Jam Vendetta, and WCW vs NWO: World Tour. If you know and love those games, you're going to love this game as well. Pure Nostalgia with updated graphics. DO NOT EXPECT a wrestling tv show simulation, like the WWE 2K series. But hey, you also don't have to pay $300 in real money to get all the content on this game. As of today, you pay $63 to get absolutely everything (the "Elite" edition). You won't have to pay anything else for any unreleased upcoming DLC.

STORY MODE: The story struck me as simple at first. And it was short. But then I realized that I hadn't actually played the whole thing. What you see depends on your wins and losses and maybe even which character you play it with - as you replay the short "story" to unlock hidden rewards, including hidden wrestlers (NO SPOILERS), and earn more in-game currency you use to purchase them. The story mode also allows you to develop and build your Custom wrestler's stats and attributes and test them out in matches. So the STORY MODE actually has infinite replay value for that purpose alone.

EXHIBITION MATCHES AND ONLINE PLAY: At this time both work and are LOADS of fun. You can eve use your Custom Wrestlers, which means that you should actually think and strategize about how you BUILD your custom wrestler, so you can create an absolute BEAST adapted to your play-style, or build an all around juggernaut. Plus, all matches reward you with n-game currency that you can use to unlock rewards.

MODDING SUPPORT: That's right folks. They've UNLEASHED THE KRAKEN! The devs announced support for modders, and set up modding channels on the AEW Games Discord server. The game already included move-sets and assets (like audible names) for all kinds of wrestlers that weren't on the initial roster. Now, modders will be doing God's work, and adding everything to AEW Fight Forever that we added to 2K23 to simulate the AEW experience. Only this time, with ACTUAL bloody matches. MOD SUPPORT makes this game a MUST BUY.

IN CONCLUSION: Yes, I recommend this game for any fans of wrestling games. If you love the old school wrestling games, or you're an AEW die hard fan, or you're just tired of 2K gouging you $300 per game to get all its content, then pick this title up now.\t\t\t\t\t
