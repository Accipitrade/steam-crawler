\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA solid first game for AEW. Is it perfect? Nope!
Does it return us to the old days? Yep!

Let's start with some of the negatives:

Lacking a bit in the CAW department
-----------------------------------------

- I could care less about the facial features but the small things such as facial hairs are extremely limited
- Needs more clothing options or at least variations especially when it comes to pants and shirts.

Match Types
---------------

- I feel as if they could have added just a few more options to this.
- More tag team options rather than a basic tag would be nice to see (Tornado as an example)


The Positives
---------------

- A fresh new engine
- Feels like a tribute to the N64 days
- Extremely fun to play
- Finally an AEW Roster!!!!


Hopes for the future
-----------------------_

- With the word being that this will be a supported game for the next 3-4 years
I'd love to see some rich DLC (Paid and Free)
- Upgrades to the roster
- More CAW parts
- New updates with match types
- Steel Cage
- Tornado Tag
- Trios
- Blood and Guts
- Some previous PPV Arenas
