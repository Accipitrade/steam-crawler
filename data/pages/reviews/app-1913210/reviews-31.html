But is it worth?

The combat are fun and more arcade then 2K games. Feel a bit more like 90s games like WWF No Mercy.
The character design is okay! All wrestlers look caricatured some look great, other awefull lol!
You have a tons of objects to use! like 30 maybe more..

The road to elite mode is a bit similar to what the smackdown serie does back in the day. You play one year with an actual wrestler or a created one. And you play each weeks, you can go diner, make activities, train yourself before next match. At the end, its all quick cinematic. You'll see the same cinematic again and again.You have story cinematic too. And can participate in many stories lines! After your training, you unlock point to spend in characteristics, passive etc. You have like 4 act to do. Road to All out, Road to Full gear etc. And have challenges to do in every Road to PPV.

Beside of RTE, you also have challenges to do daily, weekly and normal challenge to do. Like having a hundred victory for example. You earn money and can buy some creation stuff, move, entrance etc.

Talking creation.. it's the big mistake in this game, all the customization!
Not a lot of things to customize on your created superstar.. we have like 8 faces to choose. Maybe a dozen hair cut a couple of top. No slider to customize the height of you nose or the width. You don't have a lot to do with the appearance, The most of the shirt is national shirt.. Same for the pants etc. They don't put any community creation option ( with the little we have it's understandable..) I hope they'll add some stuffs in the creation section..

And Jim Ross dubbing is awefull.. The start of every show feel like he read his text with no emotion lol..

Conclusion, not a bad game! It' fun! You have a lot of challenge to do, the road to elite mode to complete with every wrestler in the game! Plus mini game to play with friends! But don't think you will recreated all wrestler you love and arena like in 2k series..

Is it worth it's price? At least he didn't come out at the price of 79.99 or worse 89.99..
I expected better. It's a Ok game, If you want another wrestling game to play it's not a bad one. Not as good as WWE 2K

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun game, feels like a spiritual successor to No Mercy in more ways than one. The actual in-ring action is a ton of fun, with my only real complaint being that matches are a little too short on normal difficulty. The moves look awesome and the wrestlers are fun to control. The "Road to the Elite" mode is deeper than people realize, it has multiple branching paths like No Mercy and it's the only way to level up created wrestlers; you're not supposed to play it once with an AEW wrestler and be done with it.

Less features than I would have liked, it's a shame there's no 3v3 match type or steel cage. This is also exclusively an arcade-style game, no simulation here. If you want simulation and want to spend 100's of hours making lifelike CAWs then this isn't the game for you. But this is an excellent base to build on and I look forward to what's coming next.\t\t\t\t\t
