\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI'm recommending this because at it's core it's fun and is a good game.

HOWEVER

This game is trying to be WWF No Mercy a little too much where the game feels like it has the same limitations that No Mercy had. The difference is that you could forgive those limitations for No Mercy because No Mercy came out in 1998.

Entrances are in short clips like No Mercy. This is terrible design choice in my opinion. It destroys the atmosphere of a good entrance which in turn kills some of the buildup to the match. In No Mercy, one could forgive this because full entrances weren't really a thing in wrestling games at the time. Now they are.

That same vibe is consistent throughout this game. These design choices would be fine... in a bubble. This game suffers from the fact that it didn't take basically anything from what WWE 2K was doing. It feels like 1998 in both the good and the bad. The arcade feel is fine. It needs something to distinguish it from it's competition, but you should still pay attention to what your competition is doing right. Things like community creations, large varieties in match types, extensive rosters, in-depth wrestling with highly varied movesets. This game lacks those and if you have played the WWE 2K games. Those missing features stick out badly.

I am in full support of AEW making more arcadey games than WWE. I really like the variety of weapons in the game and the fact you can play an exploding barbed wire deathmatch. I really like some of the mechanics like riding the skateboard or throwing the propane tank to explode your opponent. I also like that you can control the pyro and camera cuts during entrances. But they should still have full entrances and some preset pyro, they should still have a community creations, they should still have an extensive roster (hell because its arcadey they have even less of an excuse there because they don't need to have as much attention to detail or mocap wrestlers like WWE).

Overall, the game is fun and does have some great features. It just feels like it's lacking a lot when compared to its competition. They will be adding more wrestlers in DLCs and hopefully other new stuff but yeah definitely fell short of some of the hype.\t\t\t\t\t
