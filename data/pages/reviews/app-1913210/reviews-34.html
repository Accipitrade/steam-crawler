\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a good start, but is overall very incomplete.

PROS:

Announced wrestler names are very diverse, on par with WWE2k
Road to Elite offers a fun career mode with progression, and simple cut scenes to string it along
Different cut scenes play depending on win/loss, making each run feel new
(did the intro twice and got different cut scenes from the Casino Royal)
Option to set a wrestler's street clothes, as well as entrance attire
masks on outside the ring
Gameplay feels good, wide variety of action available
lots of weapons

CONS:

Character creation EXTREMELY limited.
Only a few choices for face/body.
Only a few masks, very genereic clothing (even ones you unlock from the in game store)
No progression for AEW wrestlers in Road to Elite
If you choose an existing wrestler, the progression portion of Road to Elite is locked off

These next things are update level small, but annoying to deal with.

no option for hats in you entrance attire because reasons
no ability to scroll menus, enjoy going through each and every name and move
loading screens require you to press a to advance. all of them. i get not wanting to throw you into a match, but along with the last point, it makes menu navigation super frustrating.

Graphics are... what they are. Big name wrestlers are recognizable, and everyone has big hands. It's not realistic, it's a style I'm on the fence about. Hopefully with AEW agreeing to update the game constantly, it can come to fruition. As of now, it feels like a game made to release last gen, or even 360/PS3
