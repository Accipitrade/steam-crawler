\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun thus far. When it comes to game play, I absolutely love it. The variety of weapons is a superb. Skateboards, pizza boxes, barbed wire bats, propane tanks, hockey sticks, thumbtack shoes, and so much more. You can utterly obliterate your opponents with a myriad of weapons. You can create some really fun sequences. Also, SO MUCH BLOOD (if you want it). The Exploding Barbed Wire Deathmatch also deserves a brief mention. It's a fun match type and should continue to be a staple. I hope Blood&Guts gets added eventually

The game play is not like No Mercy, contrary to belief before release, so don't go in expecting a No Mercy clone. The game is way more enjoyable when you don't even think about NM. Let those comparisons come organically. The game seemed inspired by the simplicity of No Mercy more than anything. The fighting works differently.

Road to Elite could use some more depth and a padded runtime but the ideas presented worked well. They provided an enjoyable career mode despite a short length. The story line for a created character was fun at times but I wish there were more branching stories and opportunities at championships. However, there is a small bit of variety. And the story does change depending on your wins and losses. I've also played a Yuka Sakazaki RTE and the story was different but, again, I would like to see some more depth in the story lines. Some all out wacky and insane stuff would be appreciated. Or, even better, have certain branching paths that can give you heated rivalries or mind-boggling supernatural stuff. It's just an idea. The stories are fine for now but some variety would be appreciated

Entrance customization is rather deep, however, the same can not be said for character customization. While there are certainly decent options and you can create an okay character, it lacks worthwhile options. This area better be improved in the future. The options can't be left like this forever. While there are certainly a lot of t shirt options, we need more variety in types of shirts, elbow pads, kick pads, boots, etc. I find the entrance attire customization to be the most atrocious aspect of the customization's low depth. Only tops and jackets are available at the time of this comment and that shouldn't fly in a time when customization is desired by most consumers. GIVE US MORE CLOTHING OPTIONS, GIVE US MORE TATTOOS, GIVE US MORE FACIAL HAIR, GIVE US MORE CHOICES

I'm hoping that since a new game won't be created in a long time, Fight Forever will be given new content. It's very fun, especially with friends, but it needs to be built upon. It's a solid foundation. Let's see where they go with it. As of 2 days after launch, i'd say I recommend AEW: FF. It has the potential to be a phenomenal game, it just needs to keep delivering fresh content\t\t\t\t\t
