\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPros:
Gameplay is fun and generally quick-paced. It's so good I can almost overlook all the glaring issues.

Cons:
I really wanted to like this game. I've been a fan of The Elite for a long time and AEW since it's inception. Character creation is way too limited for a modern game. I get the appeal of making it look more cartoony, but sometimes it looks like modelers/texture artists were given no direction for continuity. Sting looks very impressive, and Pac looks awful. Nick Jackson doesn't even begin to look like himself.

I can forgive the roster not being up to date since the game started development years ago and they have to build a lot from scratch, so I'm hoping that a patch will introduce the BCC and HOB. For a company with such a stacked tag team division, it's WOEFULLY underrepresented here with just a few tag teams included at launch.

Entrances are just really sad. They're too short. The trailer shows almost the entire entrance for any given wrestler.

Voice acting is almost non-existent. I don't know if it's better or worse than hearing the same three lines from Michael Cole or not.

Theres a lack of a constant body injury indicator along with a lot of other QOL problems that I hope are addressed via patches or future releases.

For such a fast-paced game the characters get winded VERY quick. The casino battle match lasts the longest and 5 min in even a high-stamina wrestler like Kenny Omega is already spending 30 seconds on the mat. Other matches last around 3 minutes and that's a bit too short.

I haven't played a 2K game since 16, but it looks like the best AEW game might be a 2k games with mods to skin it as an AEW game.

The community manager said mods are welcome for this game, but it's still a question IF the game can be easily modded. Most of the issues I have with the game can be fixed by the community. I hate that it would come to that, but I'm a fan of Bethesda games so I also am just accustomed to it.

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThey promised a game that will feel like No Mercy and they delivered.

If you're looking for a wrestling simulation like the WWE 2k series, this game may not be for you.

If you're looking for a fun, arcade-y experience, it's pretty great!

I was expecting more moves/animations and a bit more functionality, but overall I'm having a lot of fun with the Road to the Elite mode. I wish I had some choices on how the story advances (like being able to choose between joining The Pinnacle or The Inner Circle), but that's a relatively minor gripe for an overall fun experience.

I'm hoping the devs at AEW games can deliver on their promise to update the roster and as much as I'm enjoying Road to the Elite, I'm hoping they can update the mode with more current story lines and stables as time goes on.\t\t\t\t\t
