\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGot some real bangers in this pack.

This car pack has some real competitive vehicles in it. Decent all rounders which can get some extremely fast times around circuits. They can take a bit of getting used to but they reward getting creative with tuning. They are by no means the most durable cars but if your looking for speed and handling to get you an edge on the track then Id say definitely pick this up.

Onto the cars.

The Stellar (Blue car) is probably the fastest car in this pack. It has not got the most powerful engine under the hood but it makes up for it in handling. Its a very planted car to drive and if driven well can easily outperform most "meta" cars. It eats both dirt and tarmac for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The Raven (Black car) has the most power out of the bunch. It drifts around corners very well and can accelerate quite quickly but it can feel a little floaty at times. Its not a bad derby car either as its quite heavy. But it certainly can keep up and surpass other drivers and win races.

The Speedie (Red car) The smallest and trickiest car in the pack. A FWD car with an extremely high skill ceiling. Very nimble but also difficult to drive but can be a world record breaker if you learn how to drive it. Great for dodging other racers\t\t\t\t\t
