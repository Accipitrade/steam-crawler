\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA simple solitaire type game with a dungeon-crawling theme. It's great if you want to play something new but don't want to invest time learning a lot of rules, or want to play a quick game waiting for the tea to brew or whatever. The game design is good at not feeling like you're just playing a regular solitaire card game because of the little thematic details (sounds, animations) and the fact that card values aren't fixed (you deal damage to other cards, heal yourself, etc.).

The art is right on the border of creepy and cute, in a good way. The atmosphere in the game, the little things going on in the tavern in the background while you play, is what makes it interesting for me and I wish there was more of that. It feels like they put a lot of effort into the art and UX. It's a really good value for what you get.

And, for the long-term draw, there's a daily deck challenge, which is finally FIXED in the year of our misery 2022.

The gameplay and development decisions make more sense when you realize this was an Android game designed to draw people back on a regular basis to make ad revenue. But you'll still have fun for a few bucks and a lot more fun without the ads.

-----

I notice a recurring complaint from other reviewers that not all games are winnable and you depend on luck of the draw, but that sounds like more of a critique of solitaire in general than this specific game. I personally found nothing about this card game which makes it more or less reliant on luck than a standard 52-deck game. The special cards can help you overcome some bad luck, but they aren't a guarantee you'll win.\t\t\t\t\t
