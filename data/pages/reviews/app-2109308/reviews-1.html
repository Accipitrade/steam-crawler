\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThe Sentinel Nine is a crit-focused pistol, functioning as an alternative to Leon's default pistol. Both the default pistol and the Sentinel Nine benefit from increased accuracy, stagger and crit chance when using the laser sight, and their exclusive upgrades both increase critical hit chance by 5x. This critical hit chance also increases melee (roundhouse kicks etc) crit chance when equipped, and this bonus crit chance also stacks with the Illuminados Emblem charm.

So if they're so similar, why choose one over the other?

At maximum upgrade levels, the Sentinel Nine deals 0.10 less damage than the default pistol and has a slightly slower reload speed, but boasts a lot more bullets per mag and a much quicker rate of fire, which can make firing feel much less sluggish. So: choose the Sentinel Nine if rate of fire and mag size is your bag; choose the default pistol if you want more damage per bullet and a bit more accuracy. It's all preference, in the end. I like having it as an option.\t\t\t\t\t
