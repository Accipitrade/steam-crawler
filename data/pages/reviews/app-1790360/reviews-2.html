\r\n\t\t\t\t\tHaven't played much yet, but looks like a nice game to spend time. Gameplay base looks like Goat Simulator, you know, open world, some quests, secrets, do what you want. But with cats. You can create your cat too, even the chonky one like Garfield. You can crawl, meow, spit hairballs, knock stuff. You have mana, likely used on skills. You can level up your cat as it have XP. And coop of course.
Just wanted to write something, because developers looks like a good people, and game looks like a game, not just "yo we have a cat with no gameplay, buy our game because it's just cats". I will update when get more time with game.
Played more. I don't understand why people call that "early access", yes, the world could be more live with more NPC and cars, but it's not a ghost town. Tutorial done nicely, nothing hard to understand.
Nice humor, interesting little stuff like physics based minigames, a lot of secrets, try hold R on keyboard - you'll see something cool. Well, actually it could be better, but for that price it's a nice game and best in coop.\t\t\t\t\t
