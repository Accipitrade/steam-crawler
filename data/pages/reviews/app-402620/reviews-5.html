\r\n\t\t\t\t\tCute story and artwork. The calendar presentation of the story looked like it would be annoying or confusing at first, but I ended up enjoying it. The story gets a bit repetitive at times, but I wouldn't recommend completely skipping the scenes that are simply retold conversations from other characters' perspectives - even if you remember what was said, make sure to read the non-verbal commentary to get more insight into their psychology and character development. I wish some of the scenes that are unlocked at the end were available during the first read since they explain some of the more far fetched stuff and really add to the character and story development. Unfortunately, the most far fetched thing in the story remains a bit ridiculous even after all is said and done. Major spoiler: So you just confessed your love to someone and became their girlfriend - why not say something as insane as "let's let some ghosts possess our bodies and have sex!" Without even telling her about the ghosts beforehand or anything. And of course she would just agree with it! They could have done that premise without making it so absurd.

I enjoyed the story overall, though. Definitely took me back.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is probably one of the best yuri VNs you can ask for. Cute art, incredible voice acting (if you get Full Chorus), great story and very likable characters. I've honestly never played a yuri VN as good as this. What I liked especially was that the intimate scenes weren't rushed. You didn't get any until probably the second half of the game. They took their time to introduce the characters and build their relationship nicely before giving us any. It felt more realistic and it was satisfying to see them finally doing it. Besides that, they cover quite a few deep topics, like struggles being in a same sex relationship, overcoming trauma, and keeping friendships. There were also a few fun scenes and some really crazy ones, I really do enjoy them as well.

If you enjoy yuri, you will not regret getting this game.
Also, if you want the game to be fully voiced, I suggest getting the Full Chorus DLC. It's quite worth it.
