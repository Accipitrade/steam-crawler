\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEasily one of the best visual novels I have ever played. The artwork is beautiful, it's extremely well written and the premise is really fun. It is a linear story with no branching plot lines, there are a few choices, but they won't change the ending. If that bothers you, maybe you won't enjoy it as much, but the writing is so good it more than makes up for it for me. It's able to juggle multiple plot points at the same time without being overwhelming.

One of the main problems I usually have with visual novels is the pacing. When people go from strangers to lovers in a matter of days it really takes me out of it, but this vn takes about six months. The relationships have time to build realistically, and the game's calendar system attaches each scene to a specific date, so you know exactly how much time has passed between each event, you can also go back and reread previous scenes whenever you want, which is really convenient. But what makes kindred spirits really unique, is that it gives you the scenes from the perspective of multiple different characters. It really helps you get into everyone's heads, and gives you a complete view of the situation.

The characters are all very fleshed out and likable. I highly recommend the "full chorus" dlc which includes all the voice acting. It will cost you a few extra bucks, but hearing all their voices adds so much character. (And it will help you pronounce their names.) Though it is only in Japanese, so be aware of that if you prefer dubs. The character writing is amazing. These girls actually look and act like real teenagers. All the things they struggle with are very believable for their age.

And yes before you ask there are uncensored sex scenes. But honestly, they're more intimate and romantic than outright sexy. Unlike most visual novels I've seen, the sex is actually used to develop the characters and bring them closer. If you removed all the actual sex and just kept the dialog, it would still be great.

It's an easy 9 out of 10 for me. If you have any interest in yuri anime or visual novels you definitely can't miss out on this one.\t\t\t\t\t
