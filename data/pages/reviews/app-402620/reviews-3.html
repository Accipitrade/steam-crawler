\r\n\t\t\t\t\tKindred Spirits on the Roof is a really good yuri visual novel, with rather unique premise.
Creating our own yuritopia? Sign me in!

Story
I don't want to put too many spoilers here, so I'll keep it short.
Storywise Kindred Spirits on the Roof is just okay. There's a few choices here and there but they ultimately doesn't matter.
I wasn't a big fan of ghost-girlfriends and their storyline, but all other pairings were really good.
There's something for everyone, even if you don't like particular character.

Characters
Probably the best part of the game. Each character is different and have their own side-story.
Extra stories (you'll unlock them after finishing main story) are amazing. It's great to see the story from different POV.
Voice-acting was on point and it makes the characters even better - each girl is different. Hearing them makes everything even more immersive.

Visuals
High quality backgrounds, character sprites and CGs. I can't tell anything bad about it.
