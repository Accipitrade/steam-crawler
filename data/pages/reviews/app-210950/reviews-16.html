\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis was the best game ever made with the old Unreal Engine (you know, the first one). Unlike all the shooters, this is purely a melee game. You hack away with swords, axes and hammers, there is a beserker rage and also a little buffing magic in the form of pick-up runestones. Healing yourself is possible by eating whatever edibles you come across, or using the occasional healing rune. The story takes you across caverns, snowy mountain ranges, the realm of the Nibelung dwarves and even through Hel, the fiery underworld.
The game has pre-shader graphics, so expect no miracles, but the setting and artwork are in my opinion good enough to revisit despite aged graphics. Multiplayer is basic and not really well-balanced, but fun enough if not taken too seriously. Did I mention that you can chop off limbs in this game? This includes heads, and yes, some enemies only die if you remove their heads.
I daresay this is a must-have for all once and future friends of Norse sagas.
Hail Odin!\t\t\t\t\t
