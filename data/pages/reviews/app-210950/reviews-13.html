\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis jewel is the pinnacle of my gaming experiences. It is the love of my childhood, and at the time I thought it was a promise of the grand things games of the future had to offer.
Sadly, it was merely one of the best games I have ever played.
Of this genre I have found no games that surpass this one in deliciousness. The combat is chancy at times, but if you pay attention to its mechanics you can start using each detail to your advantage.
Rune includes magnificent storytelling, superb voiceacting, exploration-based linear maps and a fairly significant amount of clean platforming.
But Be Warned: If you're a graphics-nut, this game might be hard on the eyes.
To me, it's beautiful and everything it should be, but I am aware that some people aren't fond of such... classic graphic representations.

Vikings, Swords, Runepowers, Gods, More swords, and some hellish enemy creatures that you'll surely come to love/hate. Oh, and multiplayer. Lots and lots of delightful endless multiplayer.\t\t\t\t\t
