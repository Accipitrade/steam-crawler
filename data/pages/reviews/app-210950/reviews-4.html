\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is a perfect example of old but gold. This game is a great action platformer where you delve deep into Norse Mythology and use both magic and blades to work your way towards Conrack, the betrayer, who is Hel-bent on destroying the Runestones that protect Midgard from Loki's evil armies from destroying all of mankind. The downside is the multiplayer. No one is on there and if you want the achievements, you'll have to give the multiplayer a skip. The singleplayer is where most of the fun is. Besides, what other game can you play where you lop off a man's arm and beat him to death with it? Or a game where you turn a goblin into a pile of limbs with only one swing of a mallet? Other than that, this game is a hidden gem. I give it a whopping 7/10! If you want more info on the game, check out LGR's review of it on Youtube.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you enjoy eating lizards, traps or just beating your enemies with their own limbs, this may be for you.

This was just going to be a quick nostalgic blast to the past. I played this back around day of release after downloading it from Kazaa or the such (sorry btw). I will definitely be making this my somewhat go to game for multiplayer thanks to the community AND continuation of support from the human head dev team on their discord channel (crazy for a game made in 2001?!)

Now this game has a lot for everyone BUT is not going to be everyones cup of tea being that it is almost old enough to start leaving for university.

If you can look through the ageing graphics and have a bit of time to get yourself set up to play on the games servers (check out communities for help on this subject) then you have an AXECELLENT platformer, hack and slash, story driven adventure game on your hands.\t\t\t\t\t
