\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's You Don't Know Jack. Except it's better in nearly every way. The old question types make a return (with the exception of probably every new question from YDKJ3... I miss the impossible questions tbh), and the two new question types are stellar. Roadkill is just Jack Attack, but it has a less intense "feel" to it. It's also quite fun, you just use the clues to figure out what links the two, then at the end, buzz in when a category that fits all the answers appears for the bonus. Then there's BINGO. This time, you buzz in when the first letter of the answer appears on the screen to get that letter. Getting all five nets you the bonus. Simple, easy, super fun.
The main mechanics are totally different as well. First off, you only get 13 questions per game. You can't pick 7 or 21 here. Second, the question values cycle rapidly before each question, and one player has to buzz in to choose the value of the question. Third, the questions themselves are predetermined based on what's picked at the start (though some give you the same thing because it needs to play every single "floor"). And fourth... well, I know it's a very minor plot, but I'd rather not spoil the end, so I'll just let you all experience it yourself. Or you'll just look it up online anyways, but I can't stop you.\t\t\t\t\t
