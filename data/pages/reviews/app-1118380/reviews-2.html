- The bugs are as good as if the whole package was in Early Access.
- No serious doc. You have to guess how about everything works.
- Debugging is worse than with a regular IDE like Visual Studio Code. It ruins the very purpose of this language.
- The language doesn't makes sense. It will take almost as much time to build anything as if you did it by learning to code.
