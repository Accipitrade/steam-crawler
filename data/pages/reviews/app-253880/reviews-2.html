\r\n\t\t\t\t\tBlast from the past!

Game runs fine for me on win 10, just don't set the graphics to 1080 or higher - play in the old native resolutions, you really aren't losing much/anything as far as visuals go, as this game is OLD.

it's not a high speed RTS like you expect from Starcraft 2, researching different weapons/vehicles/ammo etc takes a few minutes longer than that. You can customize vehicles to a pretty extensive level, and create ridiculous machines that take 15 minutes to construct.

3 Factions with completely different vehicles/tech, including water and areal units. Each faction has a unique energy weapon that you can research, as well as cool stuff like cloaking.

Also includes the ability to modify terrain in-match, and tunneling is a unique addition.

May be difficult/frustrating to play at first due to clunky old controls, but you can rebind all the weird stuff like building rotation being NUM ENTER to something that makes it a bit more user friendly.

Very cool old CG "cutscenes" for research, much better quality than you see in stuff like Red Alert (sorry, no bizarre Tim Curry appearances)

I absolutely recommend this old gem - get in there and rebind everything before giving up.\t\t\t\t\t
