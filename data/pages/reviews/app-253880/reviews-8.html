\r\n\t\t\t\t\tEarth 2150 redefined the notion of having mixed feelings from a game on Steam.
Beeing a trilogy,it has the original game and the two standalones.
To review this game, as a whole package ill use a list of Pros and Cons,and I’ll leave you to figure out if its worth it or not.
PROS:
-\tYou can “create” your own type of units by combining chasis with addons
-\tStructures can be updated with turret defenses
-\tThe three factions are very unqiue and attractive to try out
-\tStory has an interesting premise that keeps you engaged initially
-\tYou can reshape the terrain and build tunnels/bridges as part of your strategy
-\tYou always have a main base and a mission base
-\tMusic is better than in Earth 2140
-\t360 degrees rotation and zooming on the game screen
-\tFeels like a “deeper” Command&Conquer initially
-\tGame mechanics are really well done (probably the best part of the game)
-\tEach unit has a depleteable ammo stack and needs to be supplied with more ammo
-\tThe research tree is quite impressive
-\tGame can be played with air ,ground and tunnel fights
-\tThere is a transport vehicle that moves units from main base to mission base
-\tDay/Night cycle and weather system
-\tEach faction has unique superweapons
-\tYou can queue unlimited units/research and resources will be consumed gradually for them
-\tUnits can be assigned scripts
-\tLost souls has an actual chronological story
-\tThe concepts were unique and breathtaking for its genre and quite ahead of their time
-\tThe news reports,build up slowly but they give a sense of danger regarding Earth’s fate
-\tBuildings leave debris after you destroy them
-\tEarth progressively decays with the advancement of the campaign
-\tWeather affects units and buildings

CONS:
-\tIt has not aged well at all, the graphics being really washed down
-\tTutorials are very tedious and not straightforward
-\tThe path finding is horrible
-\tThe voice overs are annoying and too cheesy
-\tThe story becomes forgetful because too many pointless gather x minerals missions
-\tLearning curve is too steep
-\tMissions seem too hard even on Easy difficulty
-\tMusic is too repetitive
-\tToo many missions are gather missions (especially with ED)
-\tThe unit limit seemed small
-\tFiguring what to do is too hard. I had no idea what to do half of the time I played
-\tGame play feels too slow and sluggish
-\tThe ammo sluppying while interesting,it gets boring really fast
-\tTo me the AI seemed too hard because of the steep learning curve.
-\tNo real sense of achievement after completing several missions
-\tEach mission is a big time sink and becomes boring
-\tYour army can be immediately killed if it is facing some type of enemies
-\tThe fighting targeting is horrible because of the path finding
-\tYour units will keep being hit and move around to position themselves before attacking
-\tThere is a lights on/off on buildings and units which seems completely pointless
-\tThe maps are too big and annoying to navigate through
-\tThe minimap layout is extremly horrible,rotation makes even more confusing
-\tThe minimap is basically uselss,as it doesn’t help you at all during missions
-\tThe multiplayer is obviously dead and its game modes are severely outdated
-\tThe story fails to deliver in the end
-\tThe cinematics are pointless and too cheesy. They don’t add any value to the story
-\tGame feeling bounces from really like it or really hate it too frequently
-\tThe expansion moon project worse than the original game in most aspects : especially story
-\tThe terrain and movement on the terrain is agonizing to look at and wait upon
-\tUnit selection is faulty when you have a lot of units on screen
-\tThe game feels too often like a chore than actual fun
-\tMissions rarely vary from gather resource/destroy enemy base/escort transporters
-\tThe fighting music is too irritating and repetitive
- The ending for each faction felt anticlimactic and non emotional.
-\tLast impression was that game missions take too long and don’t have enough to keep you wanting to come back\t

Overall,I cannot say that I would recommend this trilogy. It has its PROS but it also has too many CONS for me to really like it. You can make your own decision, but I for one,really forced myself to play and experience as much as possible from this game.\t\t\t\t\t
