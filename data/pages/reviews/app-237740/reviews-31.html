\r\n\t\t\t\t\tFrom an AVGN-themed game one would expect an unfair, ultra-hard platformer inspired by the failings of the classic crappy games reviewed by Nerd himself. This is what its creators tried to do -- but, unfortunately, they failed. The game is hard, unfair and frustrating, but not in a good or creative way: it's nothing like I Wanna Be The Guy, where every room screwed with you in a different way. This game relies on one mechanic to make your life living hell: insta-killing blocks, appearing and disappearing in particular intervals of time. This becomes old very fast, and the game has little to offer beyond that. Overall, it's a decent 2d-platformer with lots of jokes borrowed from the show, but there are tons of games in this genre, from Super Meat Boy to VVVVVV that would challenge, enrage and frustrate while also not forgetting to entertain.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tPROS: I've been an AVGN fan for nearly 10 years, so the game is fan and definitely true to the source material in terms of look, sound, and feel. Has a ton a references to the episodes whether that's the level look or enemies. Music is enjoyable. Controls are very responsive.

CONS: As a game, it is very frustrating. I wanted to like this game but I just can't. Its level design and cheap deaths ruined the overall experience. Idk whose idea it was to have a bunch of death blocks (seriously they are everywhere). The game does not have a good balance of challenge. The game is literally trial and error, and maybe that's the point to be the "Nerd" but don't feel that it needed to be this diffcult. Some people like the old school difficulty, but I don't because I find myself repeating the same parts of a level over and over again. The levels are ridiculously hard but the bosses are easy. Again, an example of not a good balance of challenge. I have forced myself to finish the game and that's not a good sign. Really hope the second one is not like this.

CONCLUSION: If you're a fan, you will like this for its old school difficulty. If you're not, you might not like this.\t\t\t\t\t
