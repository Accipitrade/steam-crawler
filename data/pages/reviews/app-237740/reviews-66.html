\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOh my God this game... let me just say that I was brought up with the N64 as a kid so 8-Bit side scrollers such as this weren't really known to me until only a few years ago, but this game was awesome. I watched the AVGN episodes regliously for a few years and this game most definitly does the series justice. The game mirrors the comedy that fans and viewers of the show are familiar with and honestly really well done.

Graphically speaking ... yes it is pixelated but thats the style and I found it quite gorgeous both everything in the foreground as well as the backround. The different levels have there own unique environment that is loaded with the color and atmostpheric look of the game it is trying to mimic.

The sound track is simply awesome ive played a little over 3 hours and it has yet to be boring to me. Also i found it quite helpful in executing platforming that required precise timing.

The challenge element of this game is incredible and lives up the the games of the 8-bit era. your given 30 lives and ive found myslef burning through at least half of them on one stage (this could be because im not especially good in 2D platformers). I would definitly compare the challenge to that of Mega Man... yes it is a difficult game even on a normal setting but practice makes perfect and after playing for a while I did definitly see improvement in my own abilities. The learning curve is perfect and it provides just the right amount of challenge. Since starting to play, ive only be able to complete 4 of the total stages.

All in all if your a fan of a good challenge, the web series or even classical platformers this is a must have.\t\t\t\t\t
