\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you want to say expletives that the Nerd says while you play a game then this might be for you. For me? I don't like the jank stiff controls and the lame difficulty. This game is filled with unkillable hazards and death blocks that kill you if you touch them, though sometimes I was able to somehow stand in their hitbox and not die so I guess they aren't implented very well. Easy mode in this game gives you 6 HP and unlimited lives and the next step up, Normal, gives you 4 HP and 30 lives. Apparently there are even harder difficulties past that (going by the game's achievements) that have restrictions and ridiculous as one life. I don't doubt that there are people who can beat the game deathless, but you and I probably aren't one of them.

Level design in this game relies on cheap enemy placement and unfair hazards. I understand this game is an "homage" to bad NES games but you can reference a bad game and not, you know, include what made it bad in the first place. It doesn't make the joke funnier, it just makes the game worse. This plays like a kaizo Super Mario World hack with a gun and I don't mean that in a good way.

I gave this game a chance, I really did. But I simply hate it because of how much contempt it treats the player with. The Nerd humor is funny, the death screen is amusing, and the game looks decent enough for what it is. But it's just not fun. It's frustrating and you'll find yourself making the same jump twice and getting two different results from it. I can't recommend this game in good faith. Just stick to watching the YouTube show if you need your Nerd fix because this ain't it.\t\t\t\t\t
