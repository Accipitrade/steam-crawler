\r\n\t\t\t\t\tA retro-style platformer that revels in the addictively obnoxious traits that NES fans love. Intentionally vulgar, utterly shameless, and strangely hypnotic. I found it very hard to stop playing once I started.

Difficulty is slightly overblown. Easy and Normal difficulties won't give very many people much trouble thanks to the abundance of lives, infinite continues, and frequent checkpoints. The higher difficulties, however, will scratch that masochist itch of yours if you have one.

Unlike most retro-style indie games, this one is completely devoid of pretentiousness. It's a good Mega Man game with a dirty mouth. All the enjoyment will come from the gameplay and the AVGN fanservice.

If you're not an AVGN fan then some things will go over your head and you won't enjoy the references as much, but the gameplay is still solid.\t\t\t\t\t
