\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis is a platformer that really puts your coordination and pattern-recognition skills to the test, all while paying homage to not just the AVGN or Cinemassacre, but more gaming and pop-culture in general. There are an abundance of cameos from different people in the YouTube reviewing/playing sphere, but I won't spoil too much for those of you who want to find them out for yourselves. Also, the killer soundtrack. Seriously, it kicks ass.

One thing that I would very much advise is that, if you really don't like games that are unfairly difficult and are designed to anger you intentionally, then AVGN Adventures probably isn't the game for you. However, I personally enjoy games like these since the accomplishment felt after beating them is unlike any other feeling. And to be completely honest, many of the hidden cameo appearances and references that appear in this game can get quite stale and maybe even tiresome after a while, so buying the game based on that solely will leave you with a disappointing experience. Thankfully, the gameplay more than makes up for the that.

In this exasperatedly frustrating bout, you pilot the Nerd through countless levels filled to the brim with spikes, fire bars, disappearing platforms, ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ crazy enemy placement and design, and deathblocks... ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥, are there ever A LOT of deathblocks. These are probably the only hazard in the game most people agree upon as the root of their frustration. They're ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ EVERYWHERE, and sometimes only serve as a way to get a cheap death out of the player. In fact, they are so bad in this game that they actually toned down how many deathblocks are present in the sequel to this game, AVGN Adventures 2: ASSimilation (great name btw). But I digress, as deathblocks were really the only MAJOR issue I had with the design of this game. Sure, it's hard as hell and will kick your ass up and down the street before you even get a chance to succeed, but that comes with any game of this specific sub-genre.

Get it if you really hate yourself that much.\t\t\t\t\t

Im not joking when i say this, i finished this game under 1 hour, just look at the time i played this, its 63 minutes according to Steam, and it supposed to be a hard rage game, im used to them, i mean i ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ finished Cuphead like 20 times, so it was not that hard for me, first, let me talk about what i like about the game

First, the soundtrack is really catchy, im always been into old style/retro games for platformers, like the good old days playing Sonic The Hedgehog or Megaman, Super Mario, and a whole lot more games that i could mention.

Second, i like the design of the enemies, especially those ghosts at the ice stage, and i laughed my ass off when i seen the Pain Elemental/Cacodemon Doom Reference at the hell stage, it was really funny to me.

Speaking of reference, this is the Third point i like about the game, the references to old episodes, i mean, the most iconic to me is the Super Pitfal one, or the Boss at the hell stage again, or my all time favorite, Fred ♥♥♥♥♥,

Fourth, and im probably bias because of this, but i just ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ ride on a FLAMING SHARK IN HELL!!! my life is complete.

Fifth, the boss fights, i loved all of them, and the unique design of some of them, like the unicorn, or the dr. jekyll and mr. hyde one in the Castlevania stage.

Sixth, the funny things when the Nerd talks, it always made me chuckle (i know, cheesy)

that's probably all the things i like about the game, keep in mind, this is a raw review after i just finished the game, that being said, lets go to the things i hate/Despise

First, the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥! DEATHBLOCKS! i don't know who in the right mind had the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ idea to put Deathblocks to every single ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ half of the stage! it wouldn't be a problem if its a few stage, but it became a ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ habbit in the game and i hate it with a passion, there were times when the enemy hits me, and i touch the deathblock and boom! Dead! It felt unfair to me because most of them were such a tight space that i coudn't even move! its not fun! its not challenging! its just un-♥♥♥♥♥♥♥-fair!

Second, the controls, i play Every game on my keyboard (love me or hate me for this, its my playstyle) even those games that requies a controller to play, because its more of a good challenge for me and makes it more fun to me, but the controls, those who programed the game must have designed for this for the XBox controller, because who in the right mind wants to put the JUMP button to Z, the SHOOT button to Y, and the Item (which i never even used, only like 3 times) to X?! and okay, my Keyboard is different, its an Hungarian keybind, and it really felt hurtful to my hands when i first played because i was too stupid to look at the controls and change it the first time, but if it wasn't for being able to change the keys to Q and W, it would have been a major design flaw for me

Third, the platforming, there were times when i pressed the button and didn't jumped, like. dude, what the ♥♥♥♥?! maybe im bias because of this, or maybe it has to be frame perfect but that just makes me hate a game when i die unfairly because my character didn't jumped. speaking of jumping, why not hit two birds with one stone?

Fourth, the tights spaces i have to jump in the air, again, BETWEEN ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ DEATHBLOCKS!!! ARE YOU ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ KIDDING ME?! its one thing i have to jump between ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ deathblocks! but in the ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥! AIR?! ♥♥♥♥ whoever thought AND implemented this idea! seriously! thats just ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ stupid and makes the game look like a wanna be rage game like Cat Mario! but even Cat Mario is better! its okay for a game to be difficult, its not a problem for me, because again, i finished Cuphead multiple times, BUT THIS?! This is just stupid again and makes the game unplayable for me.



That being said, do i recommend this game? if you are a hardcore AVGN fan, then yes, i do recommend this game for you, if you just want a challenging game, a game that is like the good old retro games, then no, i do NOT recommend this game for you, again, im a bit bias because of my experience, so don't take just my word, but again, half of the stages are full of deathblocks, and its just not "Challenging" its just unfair, its unplayable, and its infuriating


Overall, i have a mixed opinion of this game, i hope the second one is better, my overall score is 5/10, its mediocre at best, its like eating Cheetos, but even Cheetos is better.\t\t\t\t\t
