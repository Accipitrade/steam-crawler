\r\n\t\t\t\t\t"What a ♥♥♥♥ load of ♥♥♥♥. I would rather let a raccoon ♥♥♥♥ in my eyes than play this ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ for any second longer."

The Nerd sums it up perfectly, but honestly this game is pretty damn entertaining. It's a clear homage to 2D Side-Scrollers of the 80es and 90es and it plays like the good old NES-Games. The Humor is great and you will find a lot of references to other games like Castlevania, Megaman, Friday the 13th and Silver Surfer.

In total the game consists of 9 levels. Each is one is designed pretty differently. (An Ice Level, Lava Level, Fantasy Level, Christmas Level, Castlevania Level, Future Level, Candy Level and an Atari Level) On easy mode non them should be a real problem, if you are familiar to these kind of games. Dying a lot is part of the deal and progressing every time a little further and learning patterns and positions till you succeed. The last level on the other hand is definitely going to test your patience. Not only are there more death blocks and cheap deaths here than in any other level, but you also need to face Fred ♥♥♥♥♥, the last boss of the game and he is a real pain in the ass. At my first try I rage quitted after losing against him for 2 hours. So at least you get a decent challenge and are truly relieved after beating the game.

What I am trying to say is, that the easy and normal difficulty settings give you more than enough challenge for you money. How a person can beat this game on Oldschool or hard as balls difficulty is beyond me. And Impossible and Yolo difficulty have to be some sort of sick joke. Even a half-god couldn't do that.

And the end I got exactly what I expected of this game. Even if you are not familiar to the AVGN you will enjoy this game plenty if you like Side-Scrollers at least a little bit and if you are fan of the Nerd (like me) than this going to be even better. This game gets a clear recommendation from my side.

8.5/10
