It's an addicting, fun platforming shooter in the style of many of the great classics from the SNES and NES era. The controls come straight outta Contra and the level designs are very similar to the Castlevania and Mega-Man games. The game is just hard enough to keep it challenging, but fun at the same time. If you're a fan of the Nerd series, you will dig the ♥♥♥♥ out of the Nerd's randomized quotes and references to many of the things from Cinemassacre. Overall, it's a hilarious, geeky, and fun experience that every fan of platformers should play. It really is a perfectly balanced treat of a game. A little short, but there's lots of replay value. Highly recommended for everyone, honestly.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThere is a good game here. Somewhere.

Sadly it's covered by a great idea of making this game as annoying to play as possible on purpose:

-instant killing death blocks,

-limiting vision fog,

-cheap deaths everywhere,

-garbage designed boss fighs (like seriously, not a single good designed boss fight in this whole game).

Funny, I don't remember even the worst games on NES or SNES to be this bad in term of level design. Hmmmmm.....

Angry Video Game Nerd Adventures tries to make call backs to both the AVGN show and old school era of games but it fails miserable at it because it actually made the game that is constantly hindered by this to the point of more annoyance than fun.

Also making a sh*tty and bad designed level for a sake of making a joke that level is sh*tty and badly designed is not funny - on top of that you just made a sh*tty level in your game.
And you want me to play it?

Note for devs: you supposed to make a game inspired by 90's. Not make it as much annoying to play and cheap on instant kills like in 90's.

I finished the game on hard difficulty and I can very easy tell that developers had zero experience in good game design and how to make the game that actually feels not only fun to play but that is also challenging and fair at the same time.

Please, do better next time because this garbage is not gonna stick.

5/10

I recommend it only to big AVGN fans. Everyone else: just skip it. There is many other platforming games and many other challenging games on Steam that are far better than this.\t\t\t\t\t
