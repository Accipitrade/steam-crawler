\r\n\t\t\t\t\tSince AVGN 2 Is Going To Be Released In The Next Few Days, I May As Well Give My Thoughts On The First One. AVGN Adventures Is One Hell Of A Game.

The Story Is Almost Non-Existent, The Nerd Gets Grabbed By His Balls And Pulled Into The TV. He Then Must Beat 8 Levels And A Secret Final Level To Destroy Game Land.

The Gameplay Is Very Reminiscent Of Games Like Mega Man (Which I Love To Death Especially 2 And 5) Where You Have 8 Levels, And A Boss At The End Of Each Level. The Controls Are Very Smooth And Responsive While Running At An Extremely Solid 60 FPS With Absolutly No Slowdown. There Are Also 3 Unlockable Characters (♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Man, Guitar Guy, Mike) Along With Many Cameos. Each Character Has It's Own Speed, Control, Jump, And Weapon.

Now How Hard Is This Game? It's Not As Hard As It Seems. It's Extremely Trial And Error Though. This Game Is Made To Frusturate You. And I Mean Frusturate You.

Overall, This Is A Great Game That Deserves All The Praise It Gets. However, I Would Suggest That You Buy This Game During A Sale, As I Don't Think To Most People It's Worth $10.\t\t\t\t\t
