\r\n\t\t\t\t\tYou don't need to be a fan of AVGN to enjoy this, but it helps. You do need to love some 80's NES-hard style platforming however. The bottomless pits, the timed spikes and obstacles, the enemy patterns, the cheap deaths, it's all there. It's extremely difficult and yes, they are merciless with the Deathblocks, but this game is not unfair. The controls are perfect, there are no infinitely-respawning enemies just offscreen, damage that can't be avoided, none of the truly rotten things that ruined our weekend game rental. You'll die a lot at first as you incrementally learn the stages and enemy patterns, and just like when you rented that NES game on Friday and couldn't get past the first stage, by Sunday you're burning through most of it with only the occasional death and desperately trying to figure out the last boss before you have to return the game.

Also the soundtrack is seriously awesome.\t\t\t\t\t
