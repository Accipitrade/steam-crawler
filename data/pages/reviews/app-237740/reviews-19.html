This is a nice tribute to the 8-Bit games of old, both the good AND the bad. The music is amazing! Every character feels useful in their own way. The humor is on-par with the web-series it is based off of. Gameplay is also pretty fun.

Yes, it is difficult, but because of that, it is rewarding to say the least. Whether it is fighting cheap knockoffs of Freddy Krueger and Jason Voorhees (Bimmy isn't even a real name!) in a haunted house, or killing Dr. Jekyll and Mr. Hyde in a Castlevania-esque castle, this game is very rewarding when you beat a level. And when you reach the end of Laughin' Jokin' Numbnuts and kill Fred F*cks, you will be satisfied.\t\t\t\t\t

Been a fan of the YouTube series for quite a long time, first video for me in the AVGN series was the super Mario bros 3. Review. Playing the game after being a fan of the series grants an understanding of the playable characters, levels, weapons, and dialogues in the game BUT, all these things are able to be enjoyed without prior knowledge to the series and will even open doors to you that were once closed. The levels are enjoyable and are set around scenarios and episodes of the series for example there is a very obvious castlevania based level and a street fighter 2010 based level, there is even a level based off of the Atari porn games episode, there are different weapons like the NES zapper (a.k.a. the light gun) and the snes superscope, you can even get power ups like the glitch gremling, super mecha death Christ, and rocks from the Friday the 13th NES game. There are a total of 4 different playable characters which are able to be unlocked by finding secrets hidden throughout the different levels. Being a fan of the series, retro video games, and platformers in general, this video game is really great and offers challenge, and if it isn't challenging enough there are 3 different difficulty settings. 10/10\t\t\t\t\t
