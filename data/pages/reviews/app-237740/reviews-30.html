The game is a throwback to the 8-bit style of games both good and bad. The game also pokes fun of bad games and has many refrences to avgn and in those regards, this game is perfect. The problem is that if you strip those things out you are left with a game very frustrating and cheap.

Graphics 10/10- I have no problems with this game on this font...They're great and you know what everything is as soon as you see it. Some of the best 8-bit graphics i've seen.

Sound 10/10-Once again, amazing. The sound track is a real standout part of the game, with great remixes of the avgn theme and orignal work that would be at home with the nes tune greats. The audio mixing is great too, with every shot and explosion having a perfectly timed sound effect.

Control 9/10- Any problem you have with the difficulty will not be related to this. The controls are great, the only complaint is that switching characters in boss battles can be hard on the controller since there are four characters and only two buttons to switch between them.

Gameplay 6/10-Now for my biggest issue. The game is very cheap with it's deaths. The game is going for jokes about bad design choices, but the problem is it only works if you get the joke. Because at the end of the day a bad design choice is still bad. I know this was for a reason but that doesn't make the game play better. But to be fair, it isn't awful. There are very cool moments in the game as well. I just wish they cut back on those death blocks. They're funny at first but lose me the 50th time they instant kill me.

replay 8/10-If you are a fan of the show then this game has a treasure trove of goodies. Hidden characters and cameos, secret jokes, and every frame is a refrence to the show is some way. The only thing is that all this doesn't matter unless you are a hardcore fan. There are multiple difficulties but I think the game is hard enough on normal.

overall 7/10-If you love the show totally worth it. But as a game it's slightly above average.\t\t\t\t\t
