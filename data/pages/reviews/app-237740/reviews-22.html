\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOverview:

You play as the f*cking nerd in old school style 2D side scrolling platforming gameplay. You'll make your way through various AVGN themed levels with references abound. Every death is greeted with nerd style dialog which echos your frustration. Levels are short but challenging and unique.

Pros:

AVGN Nostalgia: Fans of the show will love all the references and nerd style commentary. It's everywhere. The names of the levels, the power ups, the death dialog, the rolling rock life meter, even sh*t pickle.
Challenge: If you play on the easiest difficulty it probably won't be too difficult to beat as you're given plenty of lives. Higher difficulties restrict your lives. Jumping is precise and solid. Levels are designed well and present plenty of challenging obstacles to overcome. The game is like playing old school Mario with slightly increased difficulty.

Cons:

Old School Gameplay: It's hard for me to put this as a con but the trend in modern games has moved toward instant gratification mixed with increasingly easier difficulties. I wouldn't say this game is particularly hard, it just has the right mix of challenge and forgiveness. There isn't much more to this game than putting your platforming skills to the test which for many people probably won't be enough to hold their attention.

AVGN Nostalgia: If you're not a fan of AVGN then you won't appreciate what this game is. Which is an homage to AVGN. Fans know this and will be entertained while they play. Non fans won't care and will probably loose interest quickly.

Conclusion:

AVGN fans will really appreciate this game and I would definitely recommend this to them. Non fans should probably just pass on this. It's a rewarding, well crafted, and short game for the fans.

Score: 7.8 / 10\t\t\t\t\t
