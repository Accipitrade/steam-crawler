\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game reminds you of the simple days of jumping and shooting. It mixes a lot of the good and the bad.

The level art design is great for the most part, it has awesome 8-bit chiptune music, it contains a lot of fanservice for people who watches AVGN, and it gives you tons of lives depending on the difficulty you chose. But then...

Your lives are going to drain a lot faster than you can say F****. The game is created intentionally to resemble the crappy games AVGN has to go through. The game is hard although a lot of its difficulty comes from the cheap death blocks, bad enemy placements and the clunky controls.

If the cons don't bother you too much and you're already used to the principle of old-school games, then you'll like the game.\t\t\t\t\t
