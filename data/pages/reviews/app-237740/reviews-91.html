\r\n\t\t\t\t\t2D Dark Souls! That's what I am calling this frustratingly fun game. Playing as everyone's favorite nerd is equal parts precise gameplay and controller tossing anger. The platforming in the game is damn near perfect, sometimes the jumps feel a little floatybut that may be just me. The levels are varied and interesting and the first time I saw a lava shark I was like oh yeah this game oh yeah. While the controls and Angry Video Game Nerd are done very well where the game excells the most is the difficulty, giving you a game where no matter how frustrated I got I kept saying to myself "oh come on you can do this" it has that old school hard Nintendo feel where it's hard but rewarding when you finally beat a stage that made you so mad. I had that feeling when I beat my first level in AVGN Adventures, Hell i believe. I died numerous times figuring out the patterns and planning my attack but when I finally beat it I sat back and let out a sign of victory and that feeling of beating something that kicked the crap out of you through let's just say 18 lives or so is a feeling I thoroughly enjoyed. If you want a challenge pick it up as it is well worth it and when you do beat it you will feel pretty good about it.\t\t\t\t\t
