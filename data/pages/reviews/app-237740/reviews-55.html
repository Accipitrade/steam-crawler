\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game. . . phew
I have spent many hours with sweat dripping onto my keyboard and my teeth grit in visible pain as I saw the infamous death screen pop up, reminding me just how bad I was at this game.

AVGN Adventures is the perfect homage to the "bad games" of the 80's. It takes everything that could possibly be frustrating about them: cheap deaths, unnavoidable projectiles, leaps of faith, and above all else: levels that can utlimately be beaten in a matter of minutes (But in the real world, can take you about an hour at least after dying about 100 times), and mashes it into 10 obnoxious levels. Even when it is making fun of the "bad games" it does it in good taste. It rewards you for your unreasonable dedication to beating the levels and has more quality to it than actual abominations from the 80's.

The plot is kinda obscene, but with the Angry Video Game Nerd involved, what isn't? The nerd gets grabbed (by his junk specifically) by his game and is yanked into "VideoLand" where he must fight his way out and defeat the game's creator at the very end. Wild, huh? Regardless, tight controls, kickin' music, hilarious and well animated sprites, colorful backgrounds, and the little things like the unique phrases that appear upon each death at the bottom of your screen and the pause menu loop (those of you that have played this know what I'm talking about), and the misspelling of "Congratulations" as you beat each stage takes this game from being another steaming turd to a diamond. . . shaped like a turd.

This is a satire of videogames done right. It is a painful experience, but is one of those painful experiences where you go through it on purpose just so you can show off your scars to all your friends afterwards. Because not many people out there can say "I have beaten AVGN Adventures!" Only 5.1% of game owners actually have the achievement of beating the game on normal mode.

This game? I would say a solid 9/10. It is not perfect to me, but it is soo good. I also give it a 9/10 simply because I am kinda biased with the AVGN and this cushion is designed to offset my bias :p. If this baby is on sale, you should definitely give it a play, but I can't guarantee that you will get very far. . . Nonetheless, enjoy!\t\t\t\t\t

8.5/10 to this game.
