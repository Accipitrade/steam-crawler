\r\n\t\t\t\t\t I feel this is a master class in 2d side scrolling action. The controls are very responsive, the 16 bit style of graphics are fluid and colorful. The level design is pretty good. The developers were trying to create a game that was sastifying and bite your own tongue off frustrating just like the old retro cartridges... they succeeded.

If I were to complain; the level design is really good but at times feels like it lacks restraint. What I mean by that, it's like they made the levels then kept coming back to see where precisely to make things more frustrating in order to trigger a brain aneurysm (soooo many death blocks). Though regardless of that, as you get familiar with the levels you eventually get better to a point where you start feeling like a gaming god. It's very satisfying in the end. Though I'll tell you, I had moments where if a smiling developer had been sitting next to me on the couch while I was playing, I would've stabbed them in the thigh with a fork. I love this game!\t\t\t\t\t
