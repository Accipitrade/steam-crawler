\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLet me be clear when I say I *love* the nerd. This game is great, and is fits the show pretty well. The humor is somewhat different at times, like when you die it gives you some random cussing which is totally off base from what the nerd might actually say. For the most part it's spot on, like for example when you kill santa and ride his body down the hill like a sleigh. That's something I could see the nerd doing. I binged this game for a while, hoping to actually complete it on YOLO. I did it on Hard as Balls, only by memorizing every enemy position and grinding up extra lives in the happy time stage. You don't get extra lives on Impossible mode though, and I have gotten pretty far but eventually gave up. The attack patterns can be slightly more random than you expect, so even though you know where and when they are going to happen, one pixel timing difference and you're brown bread. It's pretty much harder than Dark Souls for that reason. Anyways, I got my money's worth. Definately recommend.\t\t\t\t\t
