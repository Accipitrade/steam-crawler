\r\n\t\t\t\t\tLazy, frustrating design and very unfunny - AVGN Adventures unfortunately does not contain much of any of the sarcastic wit of the actual show/AVGN brand and instead focuses solely on the vulgar and crude elements. There's potential here with decent controls and above average spritework but it is quickly squandered in gameplay aside from the outstanding soundtrack and occasional fun reprieve. There's a heavy reliance on 1-hit kill tiles and trollish blind traps to create a crutch of artificial difficulty that takes the place of interesting and iterative level design. Enemies, jokes and references get recycled over and over through the game to the point of tedium. As a fan of AVGN and very difficult platformers I was very disappointed but played through to the end to see if it got any better, it did not.\t\t\t\t\t
