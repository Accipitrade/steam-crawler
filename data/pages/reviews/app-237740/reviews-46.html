\r\n\t\t\t\t\tWell here i am. I have been a fan of the AVGN for a couple of years now and i have only just got round to playing this game now. All im going to say is that this game is very (very) HARD. Even on easy this game is so brutal that it will leave you biting your controller in rage. I guess this is the purpose of the game (To Annoy You) but im sure they could of toned the difficulty down in easy mode. The amount of references is mindblowing. I particularly enjoyed hearing bits of the Halloween theme in the "Boo Haunted House" level's music. This game uses 4th wall breaking humor throughout its runtime and BOY does it succeed! This game is absoultuly hilarious and made me laugh so much I fell of my chair and couldn't control myself. If you are a fan of the AVGN then this game is a must buy! If you are not a fan of AVGN but you like games that kick your butt then this is a must buy! If you dont like either then go away. It wont take you long to finish the game asuming you have basic knowledge of retro platformers. I rate this game 9.5/10. (The reason im not giving it 10 is because of the extreme difficulty, even on easy.)\t\t\t\t\t
