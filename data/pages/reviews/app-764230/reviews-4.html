\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGreat little game with lots of potential. Can't wait for it to be a completed product! It's still a little clunky in both controls and camera (you can't just turn on the spot, you can only turn and move) and the game still feels a little...empty. Although, that's expected for an Alpha release.

Pros:
+ Lots and lots of cute clothes you can dress yourself and your girlfriend up in.
+/- Easy to increase favourability with your girlfriend (it took me two game days to get to above 100 favourability and I didn't exactly know what I was doing).
+ Easy to use interface
+ Great character customisation for player character

Cons:
- Easier to spend money than to earn it. Just buying your girlfriend lunch for a day may cost you 2500 yen at the minimum. I've had to forgo buying cute clothing just to keep my girlfriend fed and watered :<
- Dates could be less of an interrorgation of trivia questions fired off by your girlfriend. Maybe there could be questions which the player could ask their girlfriend to add to immersion? Sometimes I feel like I'm robotically choosing which answers my girlfriend would like to hear rather than having it be a two way relationship.
- Wording of requests could be less like orders ("I want...") and more like requests ("May I...?", "I'd like...)
- No option to be alone. I honestly feel like I'm dragging more poor girlfriend along while I go and hunt squirrels to be able to afford her next meal. I know the point of the game is to be in a happy romantic relationship, but space is important, too.

Suggestions:
> Different Crane game types, like the ones often seen in arcades which give cuddly toys or accessories as prizes.
> Part Time job system/minigame to help earn money to buy things.
> Ability to customise the look of your girlfriend as well.
