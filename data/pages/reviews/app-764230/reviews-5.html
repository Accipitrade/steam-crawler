\r\n\t\t\t\t\tGame just came out in Alpha release 0.5

In its current state, it is entirely playable but not able to be completed. This game has a lot of potential.

Shoujo City is an entirely 3D version of its mobile counterpart, a 2D dating simulator.

Currently you can visit 3 locations, school, a shrine, and your home. At the school you can go along the street outside of school and visit a large number of shops, including a hair salon, maid café, and market. There is also a few crane games you can play where you win and collect Yandere Simulator figurines!

At the shrine you can purchase fortunes (honestly don't remember the terms) that grant good or bad luck in skills. You can also collect squirrels and pine cones to sell at the market in the school area.

In your house you can cook, using items from the market, sleep, access your computer, and step outside on your deck. You can also place any figurines here!

DATES
Dates consist of two locations right now, the shrine and the maid cafe outside school.

The shrine you can play hide and seek or go on a normal date. The normal dates consist of a series of questions to find out your interests. Answer right and she likes you and your score, or reputation, with your girlfriend goes up and she will want to kiss you. Answer wrong and she will say a comment such as "I have a headache."

Pros:
-lots of interaction
-Free roaming
-cute clothes
-Small storage
-Talking pillow-Chan
-seemingly realistic to Japanese culture
-cute graphics
Cons:
-slow camera rotation
-no music
-can't jump
-mostly empty rooms

Remember: This is an alpha game, so you can't play this expecting a lot of content right now. Alpha is the earliest playable state a game can have.

Tl;dr: Great anime dating game where you can date 3D waifu or a talking pillow and make her even cuter. Needs bgm.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tThis game is EA, but at this point is a barren wasteland with nothing really to do or see. Also it controls like garbage. And no music. Or sound effects besides footsteps.
check out my day 1 video where I show you how to make your girlfriend happy here:
https://youtu.be/Qg8QnGlpRAE
Currently this is the gameplay loop:
1. Wake up, your anime gf wants food and a drink
2. Take her to restaurant for food, buy drink at vending machine
3. Go on a "date" where she quizzes you about random things (Do you like snow?)
4. Get or lose love points based on the date
5. Go to bed, and start all over.

Theres a few shops you can go to, and empty places you can walk into, but thats about it at this point. Maybe later on they can put enough content into this heap to make it actually "a game" but I have my doubts.\t\t\t\t\t
