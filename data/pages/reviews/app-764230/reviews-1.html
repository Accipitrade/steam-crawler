The world and characters are super cute. There are more activities than I initially realised and all are fun to play. NPCs that walk around interact with the world by entering stores, ringing the bell and buying food, which is a nice detail. Earning money is easy and there are a good range of things to spend it on like clothes, accessories, the crane machine and furniture. I like all the different areas.

It is a shame that it isn't being updated anymore as several changes would really help. One thing that would make the controls much easier is an option to have the camera follow from just behind the player so you don't have to keep moving the cursor while running. There are also very few NPCs and having more walking around would help it to feel more alive. The school in particular would benefit from more students attending class.\t\t\t\t\t
