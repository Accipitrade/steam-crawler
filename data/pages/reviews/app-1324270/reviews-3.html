\r\n\t\t\t\t\tVery fun game with a tight core set of tools and mechanics to provide some variety and challenge to building and managing your space station! As of today (Aug 2021) the game is in pretty good shape, though I've crashed due to what seems like performance issues when there are a lot of graphical effects (radiation storms) - they said next patch that should be resolved. Very active and responsive devs!

The one hurdle new players will experience in the current game is that it is VERY hard to get over the first big growth challenge in the game. It is doable but it doesn't make for good onboarding of new players. Here's my advice: don't just build docking platforms, build a cafe, max out your prices until people start complaining or stop visiting then drop them a tiny bit. Buy billboards as soon as you can get them. Take the buy / sell missions from traders. That will get you over the hump and the game only gets better and more fun from there!

If you're into management sims this has some really excellent challenges that will both test your creativity and your operational organization!\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\tI recently decided to pick this game up again. the first go around was horribly buggy and pretty much unplayable. this time the game seems to be running much more smoothly, Im not running into any issues unless something changes and the pirate attacks start freezing the menu again, my only complaints remaining is the micromanagement system.
-The turrets have been fixed, the fps is steady, and I would say over all its now a good game with annoyances that can be ignored or worked around.
-everytime you click to upgrade something it then asks you to confirm that you wanna do it. this slows down progress terribly, you spend more time in upgrade menus then monitoring sales or building. needs to be just one click done, just like the training of your employees.
-everyone still complains everything is too cheap or too expensive. there reaches a point where you have to stop monitoring customer demands and just determine the correct prices based on how many customers are at your station.
-if the docking prices are too high it will say the fuel prices are too high. so you decrease fuel prices to zero and they complain until you figure out its actually the docking price.
as it stands there's little annoyances but no game breaking issues with the new patch. I would recommend this to an experianced player who can work around bugs but not someone without any experiance with indi games or work in progress games because they would not understand how to work around the issues to keep the game moving forward. for example they would price themselves out on raising dock prices with messages telling them their fuel prices are too high and go into debt not understanding why. im looking forward to the next patch.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\ta fun, simple game. there is not much combat. and once you start going, you get going. a lot of reviews are complaining of mechanics that are really ... not that complicated?

Income - your first priority should be to reduce upgrade costs. don't build faster than you can afford. Even starting at 20,000, build 3 dock stations and let your money generate and as you can, buy upgrade materials and reduce upkeep costs for each building.

Research - start research with upgrade cost reduction and then move to increased research - you will not have any more problems once you max both of these out

Happiness - your workers need security. there is also a tab in the overview menu to give your workers social security, health insurance and vacation. use it. you will not have any more issues with happiness. in a 4 hour period, i am generating about $6k per month and i have trained 1 worker, and given 0 raises.

Pricing - the Default price is not the price you should run with. Build your docking station and raise your price by 10-20. Do not charge 50 for gas until you have the upgrade levels to do so. The default price for gas is 10? Raise it to 15 and then adjust your rates as you continue to improve. if ships stop visiting your station, adjust your pricing.


I will say that it is a good game. Is it a AAA game? It's a tycoon. combat is extremely limited - as in just defense really.
For the price, it is worthy of a play or three. I'd like to see more station designs, actual ship combat/missions, and a general variation of station to station. With 1.5 mil and 5000 excess research, there is not much to do in regards to end game content.\t\t\t\t\t
