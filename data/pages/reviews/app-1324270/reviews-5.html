\r\n\t\t\t\t\tOk, it is neat concept and all, since there are practically next to none for space station tycoon games, yet...

My very first and major problem is this game lags so much. -> Smh the game itself fixed itself and now the lagging is gone :thumbsup:

Second, is that despite its concept is good and all, the general gameplay has no unique or trademark features at all. Rollercoaster tycoon lets you build your own tracks. Zoo tycoon lets you manage animals. So what can space station tycoon offers you for appeal its features? Right now, this game is pretty much like any other tycoon games, that you build things, there are electronic grids, research tabs, customers come and go, they spend money at various establishments. But I have yet to feel or experienced that makes this game really stands out compared to the other.

(Of course, there are some things that are unique in the game, such as faction system)

If you basically change the background into the earth then it is normal B&B tycoon. Developers could've tried much interesting or black joke-ish concept applied in this game, even though it would just be just text-rich background lore. Those things may seem trivial but they adds the depth and immersiveness into the game, which makes the game much interesting.

Third, I know that this is still in development, but this game needs more various designs for space stations.

Fourth, There should be various scenarios, but again, it is still early access, so I will have to wait and see how things would go.

So, the game itself is not bad at all, but it needs more of its own trait to gather more players and quality. I will still recommend this cause I see some potentials in this game, but it would be safe to wait for to see what the developers will provide in the future.\t\t\t\t\t
