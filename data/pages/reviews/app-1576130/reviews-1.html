\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt's honestly not too bad, but they just don't do enough with it. The premise is interesting, and the characters have interesting base point, they just never develop it much and rushed through what developments there are.

Linux player note: the game crash on start because one of the logo is in .asf. I have made a report on ProtonDB with a config file that enables the compatible movie display mode, allowing for them to be skipped, but it's a shame that there's no auto-setting when they have the workaround, it's just that it's one of those "the key is behind the locked door," type of situation.

The Heroine is a tsundere delinquent who happen to be good at singing, but she has strong opinions about a lot of things due to her background, and it could be pretty interesting had there were more downtimes not involving her slowly becoming an idol. She's just no Tsujidou replacement.

The MC, at least, have a dream and have some personality quirks, and god is it depressing that that's enough to make him stand out. It's really not much, but it's something.

The story is interesting in the beginning but gets boring once the heroine agrees to be an idol as it just becomes a "Newbie somehow becomes winner" type of story without even the meat of, say, Love Live's progression.

And the H and relationship isn't great. They didn't give it enough time, and even if you're just a complete coomer, they only have like 3 softcore scenes before the ending and each of the 2 good ending only have 1 insertion scene each (bad ending not even having any) so they're just meh.

Overall, it's really not much, and I legit spend more time (3 hours, so twice the amount I actually played) trying to figure out a workaround for the game and then reporting to ProtonDB and the devs.\t\t\t\t\t

\r\n\t\t\t\t\t I recommend this game but first, just before you buy it, take in consideration these features:

* The story of the game itself is short. I would say that I would like more drawings from other characters besides Tsubaki.
* The story is very beautiful, but if you have just a little experience watching anime then you'll find a lot of clichés here which, maybe, would make you feel like you are watching just another random anime story.
* The drawings and the animations are very beautiful and well done, but hey, take in consideration that even patching the game with the R18 Patch on the game site, you will find some H scenes with some kind of censure (I didn't like that at all).
* I loved the music a lot. The singer of the introduction of the game is the same VA from the protagonist.
* I bought this game because I played Escape from Hotel Izanami and Prison Princess from the same studio Qureate, which both I consider to be a very nice experience overall, so comparing them with this one (which is not a puzzle game) I'd say that those titles seems to be more polished than this one.
* There is a part on a dialog that I didn't like. I'll mark it as a spoiler. So the thing is that the protagonist that you incarnate, in a moment, he has the feeling that he is not the only man with whom she has shared her intimacy and he feels dissapointed about that (he found out later that he is her first), and hey, in real life that doesn't matter at all; she is a super hot girl, awesome attitude, beautiful feelings, among other positive features...
