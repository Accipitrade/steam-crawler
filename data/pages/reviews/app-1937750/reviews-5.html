\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIt plays like a branching Fire Emblem game. The roguelite elements (level up choices a la Nuclear Throne, branching map structure that allows you to pick battles strategically or go full farm mode, permadeath with revive options) all fit the genre well. There are 3 clans with different classes, and 2 game modes. Story mode is your bread and butter for learning and enjoying the game, while Abyss mode is the endless run mode for, presumably, the hardcore players. I'm a sucker for Fire Emblem/ srpg game play so I'm loving it so far, but I will note that the game is full of translation errors and unintuitive language. This makes the tooltips for learning mechanics pretty confusing. and definitely breaks immersion in the story. But I mean, $10 is a pretty damn nice price and there is definitely enough here to warrant at least a demo download..\t\t\t\t\t
