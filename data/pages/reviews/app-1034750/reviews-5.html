\r\n\t\t\t\t\tIf you are like me and are fed up with looking out your real window during this pandemic you can now exchange it with a virtual one and even help solve a crime!

The Flower Collectors feels like a combination of the movie Rear Window and the game Firewatch.

You're an ex cop of the Franco regime in Barcelona and are witnessing a murder from your apartment.
During the investigation you have to face your own troubled past and pave the way for a transition to democracy.


Throughout the game the voice actors did an amazing job.

I love the art design and the refreshing setting.

The only downside is the rough animations.
But they didn't stop me from enjoying the game.

If you like a good crime story with historical context you'll love this game (more bonus points if you're a furry)
